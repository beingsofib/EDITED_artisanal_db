A Tharos imposter was a traitorous member of the Fraternity of Tharos who sought to undermine their evil schemes.

Activities

Such an imposter may have joined the Fraternity with the goal to infiltrate it or may have turned against the faction in hopes of atoning for the evil that the initiation rites forced them to commit. Either way, imposters tended to be driven by hatred and fury directed at their demon-worshiping fellows. Even so, they operated with subtlety, relying on their knowledge, guile, and lies to stop the Fraternity's plots and chip away at their power while maintaining the appearance of a loyal cultist. They were known to feed information to and even to join adventuring groups fighting against the Fraternity.

Prerequisites

Imposters were natives of Impiltur who despised what the Fraternity was doing to the people of their land. To successfully oppose the Fraternity required both the arcane abilities of a warlock and the guile of a rogue.

Abilities

In battle, imposters harnessed their great fury at the Fraternity as well as their great talent for lies and misdirection. They were also able to wield the spells and demonic magic associated with the cult, calling upon hellfire, demons, and burning ash to slay their foes.
