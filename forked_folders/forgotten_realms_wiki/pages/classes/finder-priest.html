Finders were specialty priests devoted to Finder Wyvernspur, the Nameless Bard.

Culture

Only halflings and saurials could be finders.

Abilities

Finders could cast the following spells once per day—charm person, find the path, Melisander's harp, and starharp. Ghost pipes, dispel silence, and slow rot could be cast twice per day.

Possessions

Finders could train with any weapon but the heaviest armor they could wear was chainmail. They never carried a shield.
