Druids were specialty priests devoted to Chauntea, the Great Mother.

Culture

Druids were both male and female and could be any race.

Abilities

These speciality priests possessed all of the skills and abilites of common druids. They were usually fluent in several sylvan languages.

Possessions

Chantean druids wielded a variety of weaponry including sickles, clubs, spears, darts, daggers, scimitars, slings, and staves. They wore non-metallic armors such as padded armor, leather armor, and armor made from animal products such as hide armor.
