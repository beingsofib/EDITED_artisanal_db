Thalornor were specialty priests devoted to Sharindlar, the Lady of Life.

Culture

The majority of thalornor were shield dwarves or gold dwarves, but members of all of the dwarf races, both male and female, could become a thalornar.

Abilities

Thalornor were skilled herbalists and healers.

In addition to their normal spells, thalornor could cast cure light wounds, aid, merciful touch, cure serious wounds, cure critical wounds, heal, flowstone, and word of recall on a daily basis. Additionally, they could cast detect dwarves at will and gate twice per tenday.

Possessions

Thalornor did not have restrictions on the type of armor they were allowed to wear. They were permitted to wield bludgeoning weapons and knives.
