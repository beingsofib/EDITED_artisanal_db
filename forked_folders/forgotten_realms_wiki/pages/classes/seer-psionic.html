A seer was a type of psion who specialized in the clairsentience discipline of psionics, honing their skills in precognitive and information uncovering powers. They were equivalent to the diviners of arcane magic.

Abilities

Seers almost exclusively utilized powers that fell within the purview of the clairsentience discipline. Their specialization in this discipline allowed seers to unlock a suite of powers that were unknown to other psions. These powers were as followed – anchored navigation, clairtangent hand, clairvoyant sense, destiny dissonance, escape detection, fate link, fate of one, hypercognition, metafaculty, object reading, precognition and greater precognition, reality revision, remote viewing, second chance, and sensitivity to psychic impressions.

Being specialists in clairsentience, seers often exhibited powers to glimpse into or predict the future, glimpse into the past, and uncover that which is hidden.

Notable Seers

  • Ormion Bowbreaker, a Chondathan human seer and soldier of Castle Krag.
  • Sythrilian Sybil, a Chultan human seer and uncarnate.
