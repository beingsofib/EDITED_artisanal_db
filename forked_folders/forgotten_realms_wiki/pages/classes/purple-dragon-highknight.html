Purple Dragon highknights were among the powerful and elite Purple Dragon knights serving in the Cormyrean army in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

These knights undertook the most arduous of tasks, from investigating suspected traitors within the Purple Dragons and nobility to safeguarding the royal family itself. They could be found in the Dragon Reach, Dragon Coast, and Cormyr proper.

Culture

The Purple Dragon highknights were recruited not only from among fighters, but also bards, rangers, and other individuals who did not fit neatly within the court hierarchy.

Highknights of Cormyr spoke with royal authority, representing the crown wherever they went. They were entitled to an audience at court at any time, though any abuse or unjustified use of this privilege was strictly punished.

These knights were regularly subjected to mind-reading by the War Wizards to confirm their continued loyalty and to ensure they had not fallen under magical influence or been replaced by a being in disguise or shapechanger.

Abilities

They were good at dodging, had high endurance, and above-average fortitude. They were highly trained in the use of all weapons and could fight proficiently with any arms and armor.

They had an easier time training their balancing, concentration, diplomacy, listening, sneaking, people reading, and spotting skills.

While defending a known smaller area (such as a doorway, single room, or passage) in accordance with orders, they grew stronger, more steadfast, more resistant to avoidable harmful effects, and found it easier to dodge blows.

As they grew in power, they became more resistant to poisons and magic (due to their constant exposure to the spells of War Wizards), and more adept at avoiding enemy strikes. These were supernatural abilities.

History

This rank was relatively new compared to other positions in the service of the crown as of 1371 DR. Queen Filfaeril Obarskyr and Royal Magician Vangerdahast Aeiulvana were the joint creators of this rank.
