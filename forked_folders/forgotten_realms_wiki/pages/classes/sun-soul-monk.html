Sun Soul monks were monks and other members of the Order of the Sun Soul who followed the discipline known as the Way of the Sun Soul.

Abilities

In the 14 century DR, Sun Soul monks progressing in their studies acquired several unique abilities, such as, the ability to radiate magical light from their bodies and clothes; a means of channeling their inner light into unarmed attacks, turning their hands into flaming weapons, the emission of dazzling beams of light.

In the 15 century DR, as monks progressed in their studies of the Way of the Sun Soul, they gained various abilities that included reproducing the effect of certain spells, such as: radiant sun bolt, searing sunburst, sun shield, mastery of death, and searing arc strike.
