A egoist was a type of psion who specialized in the psychometabolism discipline of psionics, honing their skills in the alteration of the psychobiology of their own body and nearby creatures, achieving incredible feats of healing and transformation. They were equivalent to the transmuters of arcane magic.

Abilities

Egoists almost exclusively utilized powers that fell within the purview of the psychometabolism discipline. Their specialization in this discipline allowed egoists to unlock a suite of powers that were unknown to other psions. These powers were as followed – animal affinity, chameleon power, ectoplasmic form, empathic transfer, fission, fusion, hustle, metamorphosis and greater metamorphosis, psionic restoration, psionic revivify, psychic vampire, psychofeedback, restore extremity, and thicken skin.

Being specialist in psychometabolism, egoists had a profound understanding of their own physiology. Harnessing the psionic energy of the mind, they could perform great feats of psi-biofeedback, altering physical properties of themselves and other creatures.

Beyond psionic powers, egoists were skilled in the art of autohypnosis.

Notable Egoists

  • Ovdana Xothcorlar, a duergar egoist and psychic theurge who led the Duerran temple Spear of Conquest in Dunspeirrin.
  • Selinah Varhast, a human egoist and assassin that hailed from Zhentil Keep.
