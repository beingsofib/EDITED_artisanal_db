Warriors of the Elven Court were elven fighters, whose ranks consisted only of sun elves, that guarded the woods of Cormanthor.

Activities

These fighters proudly protected their people's once-great legacies around Tangled Trees and Cormanthor as a whole. Such warriors remained in Faerûn both to protect their kinsmen retreating to the west and to defend any enduring settlements. They were found outside their woods only if they'd been relieved of their responsibilities and given a mission to learn more about changes in the world outside the woods. Warriors of the Elven Court were rare. Not many were allowed to range far from their protected areas, and most of them, if given the choice, would not have done so anyway. Having at least one warrior of the Elven Court in an adventuring party was a rare sight.

Culture

The elves that became warriors of the Elven Court were young and relatively isolated from the outside world, and thus had a tendency toward rashness, ignorance, and arrogance, as well as being secretive and not trusting of non-elves. They were even sometimes suspicious of other elves who were not direct members of the Elven Court. Their arrogant behavior often tended to alienate those around them.

In particular, they bore no tolerance for dwarves, for fear that they would try to rebuild Myth Drannor and fail, or else unearth something that should've remained buried that the warriors of the Elven Court guarded.

In addition, they saw half-elves as aberrations that polluted or embarrassed the elven bloodline. They felt half-elves ought to be kept concealed. Despite this, some elven communities in the area welcomed half-elves, so the stubbornly intolerant warriors would not acknowledge then, let alone respect them.

Possessions

They wore elven chainmail, often fashioned to resemble silver leaves, and cloaks of forest green. On their heads, they sometimes wore precious metal circlets bearing their house insignias.

Abilities

Warriors of the Elven Court could speak both Common and modern Elvish.

Such individuals were adept at fighting one or two of drow, flinds, gnolls, goblins, hobgoblins, orcs, ogres, tanar'ri, trolls, or yugoloths. Their upbringing prepared them for such confrontations, so as to avoid another fall of elven civilization like the one experienced by ancient Myth Drannor.
