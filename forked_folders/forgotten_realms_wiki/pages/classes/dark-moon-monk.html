Dark Moon monks were monks who followed the ways taught by the Order of the Dark Moon.

Activities

The monks followed their purpose of executing Shar's will across Abeir-Toril with single-minded devotion. They sometimes performed missionary work, attempting to teach and indoctrinate individuals they encountered on their journeys.

Membership

Being a Dark Moon monk was both a religious and martial tradition. While becoming a monk was possible through rigorous and sometimes cruel training in Dark Moon monasteries, there were also those who learned these traditions by themselves and, while having no contact with Sharran organizations, considered themselves Dark Moon monks. It required a certain type of discipline and because of that, fully immersing oneself into the Way of Shadow tradition was possible only for lawful evil individuals that wanted to follow it as monks (in contrast to shadowdancers, who were not limited by their alignment, to practice it).

Culture

These monks were prone to use deception in contact with non-Sharrans.

Abilities

Dark Moon monks were trained in detecting illusions. They learned the 'frozen fist' technique, which allowed their punches to deal freezing injuries. With training, they could learn the following spells: blindness, blur, vampiric touch, and mirror image. Because of their training or faith, they could not lay on healing hands or make a stunning blow.

History

The traditions and practices of the Dark Moon monks were established by 1369 DR.

These traditions continued for over a century, into the era of the Second Sundering.

Notable Members

  • Hemma, a monk who was encountered by Abdel Adrian when he was young and tried to indoctrinate him
