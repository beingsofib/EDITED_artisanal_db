Peacemen and peacewomen were specialty priests devoted to Eldath, the Goddess of Singing Waters.

Activities

These individuals were always half-elves, halflings, or sirines. Elves and halflings tended to view peacemen with a raised eyebrow, finding them quite peculiar.

Abilities

Peacemen gained a variety of magical abilities as they developed including remove fear, sleep, silence, 15' radius, and Laeral's aqueous column. These abilities could be used once or multiple times per day. Especially advanced peacemen had a form of sanctuary active on them at all times and could breathe underwater.

They could walk on water at will and were completely immune to the pain touch of Loviatar.

Possessions

Though they only fought in order to defend themselves, peacemen could wield a variety of weapons in combat including nets, whips, staves, clubs, slings, and staff slings. They were particularly adept at parrying attacks. They never donned armor.

Notable Peacemen and Peacewomen

  • Milissa of Ravens Bluff, a swimming instructor and a retired adventurer who sough to finance construction of a Temple to Eldath in the late 14 century DR.
