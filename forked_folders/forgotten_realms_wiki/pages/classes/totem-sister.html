Totem sisters were specialty priests or druids devoted to Angharradh, the Union of the Three, that made small charms inscribed with symbols, which could pass special magical abilities on to their owners.

Activities

They crafted totemic images for their tribe and gave guidance. These women attended births, blessed young children, helped with the harvest and blessed warriors going into battle.

Culture

All totem sisters were female moon elves.Totem-sisters were considered a great source of wisdom and comfort. They were often considered wise women and sages and also often consulted on important issues.

Abilities

Totem sisters possessed many of the skills and powers of common druids.

They were able to use a rare form of magic called totemic magic that was similar to rune magic. It allowed to modify objects in such a way, that they could pass special magical abilities on to their owners.

They were artistically proficient and also were encouraged to focus on fire-building, pottery, weaving, healing and herbalism.

Possessions

These individuals wielded a variety of weaponry but all were skilled in the use of the spear and long bow. The types of armor they were permitted to wear were limited to hide armor, leather armor, padded armor, studded leather, or chainmail. Some totem sisters also carried a shield.
