Wizards of Halruaa, while looking foppish to many, were among the most powerful spellcasters on Toril. Their special flavor of practicing arcane magic could also be learned by humans outside Halruaa.

Activities

Halruaan wizards were known to engage in trading magical items, and their knowledge in matters arcane connected to these items was nearly unrivaled when compared to other merchants.

Culture

The wizards of Halruaa are noted for their often flashy dress and richly ornamented costumes.

Halruaan wizards often believed that they could use magic to resolve any issue. They were condescending to warriors and knights, no matter how noble, seeing them as no different from barbarians and rogues. In contrast, priests, who practiced divine magic, were considered siblings in magic.

Abilities

Halruaan wizards were well-versed in spellcraft and ancient history.

The magic of these wizards was potent, so much so that it was much harder to escape their spell effects when compared to other arcane casters. They in turn had an easier time escaping from the effects of hostile spells.

Compared to the average wizard or sorcerer, however, they had a tendency to have even less physical gifts.
