A nomad was a type of psion who specialized in the psychoportation discipline of psionics, honing their skills in propelling and displacing both creatures and objects across spacetime. They had no equivalent among the specialist wizards.

Culture

Among the greater paths of psionics, psions of the nomad discipline often took interest in the agile ways of the elocater.

Abilities

Nomads almost exclusively utilized powers that fell within the purview of the psychoportation discipline. Their specialization in this discipline allowed egoists to unlock a suite of powers that were unknown to other psions. These powers were as followed – astral caravan, baleful teleport, burst, detect teleportation, dimension swap, dream travel, mass time hop, psionic banishment, psionic ethereal jaunt, psionic fly, psionic dimensional anchor, psionic dismissal, psionic levitate, psionic teleport, psionic teleportation circle, teleport trigger, and time regression.

Being specialists in psychoportation, nomads had an intuitive understanding of the structure of reality, allowing them to slip through the cracks in space and travel vast distances. The most skilled of nomads honed their study of the discipline to such a degree that they could achieve movement through time.

Notable Nomads

  • Driklret, a blue goblin nomad that ruled the warrens of Brikklext from behind the scenes alongside the telepath Gryxt.
  • Malaeragoth, a very old sapphire dragon who used psionics as though he was a highly skilled nomad.
