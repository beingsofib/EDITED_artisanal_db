Thay infiltrators were a small group of Aglarondan loyalists who utilized both stealth and divine magic to become expert spies and scouts within the borders of Thay during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Only a few Thay infiltrators existed, and were known for their bravery, speed, and efficiency. Their experience behind enemy lines made them experts at navigating Thay as well as natural leaders for any expedition onto Thayan soil.

A Thay infiltrator used their skills, magic, and knowledge to conduct reconnaissance for Aglarond's military as well as to complete dangerous missions into Thayan territory, such as to retrieve an important object, rescue an abductee, or serve as a guide for a team with a specific objective to complete.

Prerequisites

Becoming a Thay infiltrator required the skills of a ranger and also those of a cleric, or at least the ability to channel holy power to use spells such as healing word. Only those who lived in and were loyal to Aglarond would become Thay infiltrators.

Abilities

The blending of holy power with subterfuge was the hallmark of a Thay infiltrator. Their sacred stealth allowed them to quickly maneuver and hide away from foes, and they could call upon a veil of glittering divine lights to conceal themselves and their allies from sight. They favored ranged weapons and would energize their projectiles with radiant energy, which they could use to blind their enemies. More experienced infiltrators mastered the skills of simultaneously healing their allies while firing upon their enemies, and in some cases, firing a barrage of divinely charged projectiles all at once.
