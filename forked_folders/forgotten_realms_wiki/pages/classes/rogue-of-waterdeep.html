Waterdavian rogues were rogues from Waterdeep, renowned for their skill in evading the City Watch of the City of Splendors.

Culture

A trait universally ascribed to Waterdhavian rogues was a mild paranoia. This is understandable given their history of persecution by the Lords of Waterdeep. Other than that, each rogue could differ from one another as much as any two members of the same race differed from one another.

Because they lived in a city where the great tide of humanoid races washed through and left its mark on every citizen, Waterdhavian rogues often found themselves with both many friends and potentially some life-long rivals. Younger rogues were often taken into the apprenticeship of older and more experienced ones.

Abilities

These rogues had a tendency to specialize in a single skill to a greater extent than most other rogues. They were also proficient in a larger amount of skills than most other rogues.

Notable Waterdavian Rogues

  • Madame Beautiful, a comely but egotistical rogue.
  • Stalker, an ebon-clad rogue.
