Rogues of Evermeet were elven rogues from Evermeet that practiced roguish arts specialized to a forest setting.

Activities

These rogues engaged in patrolling the forests.

Culture

Evermeet's rogues were introspective and thoughtful. They were comfortable living in the wilderness and in handling animals. There were among the most rustic people in Faerûn, apart from druids and rangers.

Possessions

They wore buckskin clothing and eagle-feather adornments. They typically donned cloaks of elvenkind, which let them without through the forests without being seen.

Abilities

These rogues were proficient in animal handling, animal lore, hunting, and tracking.

They were very effective at sneaking in forest areas as well as detecting sounds in them.

However, their lack of contact with large urban areas made them less effective in them. They were more prone to making mistakes in such places.
