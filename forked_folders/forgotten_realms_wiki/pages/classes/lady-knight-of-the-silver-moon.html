The Lady Knights of the Silver Moon were famed swordmaidens who worshiped Lurue, the Unicorn Queen.

Activities

Lady Knights adventured throughout Northwest Faerûn in Lurue's name. Their reputation for martial prowess rivaled that of Silverymoon's defenders.

Possessions

These warriors wore either silver chainmail, or elven chainmail if they could afford it. Their shields were polished to a mirror shine. In combat they preferred a lance while mounted and the longsword for melee exchanges.
