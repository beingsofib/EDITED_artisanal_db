Diviner

Class
nameDiviner
abbrDiv, W(D)
symbolWiz symbol.png

A diviner was a type of mage who specialized in divination magic. They were typically worshipers of the god Savras and fortune tellers.

Notable Diviners

Alaundo the Seer was said to be the greatest of their number.

Gromph Baenre was a very powerful diviner.
