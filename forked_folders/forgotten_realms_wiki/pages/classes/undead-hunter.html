Undead hunters were paladins who trained to be especially effective against undead opponents, albeit at the cost of neglecting their healing capabilities.

Abilities

These warriors learned to more effectively target, damage, and defend against undead creatures. They were immune to hold person-like effects and their experience in battle could not be affected by the powers of other creatures.

Because of their training regiment, they could not lay on healing hands.

They could turn undead like clerics, but with less power. With training, they could cast protection from evil and detect evil as well as other divine spells.

The most powerful undead hunters could use a myriad of martial techniques, along with war cries and could even summon a deva for aid in battle.
