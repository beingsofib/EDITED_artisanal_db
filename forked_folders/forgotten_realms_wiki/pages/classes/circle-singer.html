Circle singers were specialty priests devoted to Angharradh, the One and the Three.

Culture

All circle singers were elves or half-elves, with the vast majority being moon elves.

Abilities

Circle singers cooperated with clerics of Hanali Celanil, Aerdrie Faenya, and Sehanine Moonbow to summon powerful magic.

In addition to their normal spells, circle singers could cast ring of hands, mystic transfer, sanctify, line of protection, unearthly choir, focus, fortify, uplift, meld, thoughtwave, the great circle , spiritual wrath, and spirit of power once per tenday.

Possessions

The types of weapons wielded by circle singers, as well as the armor they wore, varied greatly.
