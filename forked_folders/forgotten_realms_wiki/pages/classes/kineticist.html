A kineticist, also known as a telekinetic psion or a savant, was a type of psion who specialized in the psychokinesis discipline of psionics,

Culture

In some cases, kineticists became overly focused on particular elements of nature and developed themselves further into cryokineticists or pyrokineticists. Other greater paths of psionics frequented by kineticists included that of the elocater, which appealed to their strategic sensibilities.

Abilities

Kineticists almost exclusively utilized powers that fell within the purview of the psychokinesis discipline. Their specialization in this discipline allowed kineticists to unlock a suite of powers that were unknown to other psions. These powers were as followed – control air, control body, control object, dispelling buffer, energy ball, energy cone, energy current, energy missile, fiery discorporation, inertial barrier, null psionics field, psionic telekinetic sphere, reddopsi, and tornado blast.

In the years following the Spellplague, almost all telekinetic psions exhibited the powers far hand and forceful push.

Being specialists in psychokinetics, the arsenal of kineticist typically included the power to fling objects around or bind them in place, create defensive barriers,

Beyond psionic powers, kineticists were skilled in the art of autohypnosis.

Possessions

While traditionally psionics users such as telekinetic psions didn't need any physical tools or weaponry to practice their discipline, following the Spellplague they began using implements to enhance their abilities. Typically these took the form of orbs and staves, through which they would channel their psionic energy into powers.

Notable Kineticists

  • Eldenser, an amethyst great wyrm who used psionics as though he was a highly skilled savant.
