Bear warriors were crazed warriors who adopted the bear as their totem animal.

Culture

The majority of these warriors were barbarians and usually the champion of their tribe or village.

Abilities

Bear warriors tapped into the spirit of a bear, gaining immense strength and ferocity during combat. They could transform into a bear when enraged. Examples included black bears, brown bears, and dire bears for particularly powerful bear warriors. This could happen several times per day so long as the bear warrior was enraged. They had a heightened sense of smell in either their normal form or bear form.

Notable Bear Warriors

Hagar Hlutwigsson, a hagspawn inhabiting Waterdeep in the , was a bear warrior.
