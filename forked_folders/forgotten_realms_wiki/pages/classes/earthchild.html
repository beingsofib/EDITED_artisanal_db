Earthchildren were specialty priests devoted to Geb, the Father Under the Skies and Sands in the Mulhorandi pantheon.

Activities

As with most of Geb's clergy, earthchildren spent much of their time mining or working with blacksmiths.

Culture

Most earthchildren were human though some dwarves followed this path as well.

Abilities

All earthchildren, regardless of their race, could see with infravision out to at least . These individuals were incredibly strong and hearty, while at the same time being rather awkward in social situations. They were skilled in mountaineering and similar skills and could detect slopes, grades, traps, pits, and deadfalls like a dwarf.

Earthchildren could cast the following spells once per day: detect metals and minerals, dust shield, dig, conjure earth elemental, move earth, earthquake, and sink

Possessions

In addition to blunt weapons, some earthchildren used a pick in combat. Most favored metal armor.
