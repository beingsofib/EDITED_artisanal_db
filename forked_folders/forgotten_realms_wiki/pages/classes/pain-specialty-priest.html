Pains were specialty priests devoted to Loviatar, the Maiden of Pain.

Culture

Pains were both male and female and could be any race.

Abilities

These individuals could use a pain touch on any target, wracking them with intense pain for several minutes depending on the skill and rank of the pain who touched them. Pains could cast a number of spells on a daily basis including whip of pain, dance of pain, Loviatar's caress, kiss of torment, eyebite, and symbol. Particularly powerful pains could cast ensnarement once per month.

Possessions

In addition to either a whip or scourge, pains wielded bludgeoning weapons in combat. The heaviest armor they were permitted to wear was scale mail, preferably pleated ceremonial scale mail, and some carried a shield.

Notable Pains

  • Matthew Hopkins, a Ravenian whose memories of being a pain were suppressed after he ritually sacrificed his own wife.
