Hakimas, or wise women, were insightful clerics in Zakhara.

Requirements

All hakimas were female and possessed great wisdom.

Role

Hakimas did not provide martial aid, though they were capable of defending themselves if needed. Instead they served as tribal family protectors or even leaders.

Possessions

These women owned a wide variety of clothing and other equipment depending on their wealth.

Benefits

Hakimas excelled at reading the truth in others, extending to being able to detect magical illusions and shapechanging. This ability developed as the hakima progressed and gained wisdom.

Hindrances

Hakimas could not use magical items and scrolls as well as other clerics. They were only able to use universal magical items.

Hakimas could not turn undead like other priests.
