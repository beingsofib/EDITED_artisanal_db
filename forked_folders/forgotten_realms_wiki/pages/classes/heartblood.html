Heartbloods were members of the clergy of the Cult of Karsus dedicated to Karsus, the Momentary God.

Activities

The Cult of Karsus extinguished before any formal titles for the clergy's hierarchy could be devised.

Possessions

Heartbloods wore crimson robes with gold and silver filigree. The only weapons there were permitted to carry were daggers, darts, and staves.
