An Evermeet warlock was a warlock who, in addition to their pact, allied themselves with the almost forgotten gossamer spirits of Evermeet, thereby gaining additional abilities beyond those they already received from their patron. Their original pact was tempered by their alliance with the fey of Evermeet. The spirits of Evermeet had the most to offer to warlocks who were already bound to other fey, but any warlock, regardless of their pact, could discover the benefits of allying themselves with Evermeet’s pact-makers.

Abilities

Thanks to their ties with the fey, Evermeet warlocks gained access to spells unique to their practice of magic, along with other, more passive benefits, that were seemingly related to the capricious and mobile nature of some fey.

When they teleported, Evermeet warlocks briefly became invisible to all enemies near the space they left behind. They could also call upon the power of the fey to enhance their speed. Moreover, when teleporting a small to medium distance, they could bring along one adjacent friendly creature with them, and place them somewhere near the place they teleported to.

The unique spells they had access to were blinding beacon, feylights, and moonflower inspiration. All of these spells were focused on manipulating and creating light.
