Luckriders were specialty priests of the Church of Tymora who observed games of chance to ensure that no one cheated.

Activities

Luckriders were very familiar with all games typically used to gamble for coin. They often participated in the games as well, though they remained constantly aware if anyone was cheating.

Culture

Luckriders used chance in their daily lives even more so than the favored.

Abilities

They were extremely skilled at detecting if someone around them was trying to cheat. They would lose access to all of their spells for a full day if they ever cheated during a game of chance.

Possessions

They wore simple black robes with their holy symbol worn on a chain around their neck.
