Sangehirn was an unusual specialty of psionics that dealt with focusing the Invisible Art towards curative and healing ends.

Culture

These psionics used focused their efforts on their bodies, keeping them physically fit. They typically viewed bodies as patterns of muscles, bones, and blood flow. It was not uncommon for sangehirns to develop a selfless attitude, caring more for the wellbeing of others than for their own body.

In the late 14 century DR, the city of Gildenglade in Turmish was home to a group of sangehirn and aspiring sangehirns known as the Hands of the Glade.

Abilities

All sangehirns in time came to master the following psionic powers: adapt body, affinity field, biofeedback, body adjustment, body purification, empathic feedback, empathic transfer, energy adaptation, energy conversion, forced share pain, inertial barrier, leech field, oak body, psionic iron body, psionic restoration, psionic revivify, psychic chirurgery, psychofeedback, restore extremity, specified energy adaptation, suspend life, sustenance, thicken skin, timeless body, true metabolism, and vigor.

Through their mastery of healing powers a sangehirn gradually learned how to alter the targeting and distance of the psionic abilities that came naturally to them, allowing them to use on others powers that normally could only target the psion themselves. The powers that could altered in this way were biofeedback, body adjustment, body purification, suspend life, sustenance, and vigor. However, suspend life could only be manifested upon another target if they were willing.

As they continued to advance their psionic mastery, sangehirns obtained a supernatural healing factor, allowing them to recover from harm more quickly. However, this healing factor could not restore damaged caused by starvation or thirst, not could it regrow or reattach a limb.
