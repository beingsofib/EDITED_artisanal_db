Xiang Do

Xiang Do was a martial art taught at Xiang Temple in Telflamm in the mid-1370s DR. The monk Raidon Kane was a practitioner.

Leaping Tiger Stance

The Leaping Tiger Stance was a stance taught as part of the ways of the monks in Xiang Temple in Telflamm. Raidon Kane used a similar stance while fighting Chun in .
