Sacred fists were specialty priests devoted to Tyr, the Even-Handed.

Culture

The principles and techniques used by sacred fists were developed by a man named Hlam during years of living alone in a cave on Mount Waterdeep. A sacred fist combined the divine powers of a cleric with the martial prowess of a monk. Hlam organized his followers into the Order of the Even-Handed. Admittance into the order required a vow of devotion to Tyr and a vow of impartiality.

Activities

Most younger sacred fists desired to best a monk of Helm in ritual combat.

Abilities

In addition to the normal abilities of a cleric of Tyr, sacred fists excelled in unnarmed combat.

Possessions

As sacred fists practiced the art of unnarmed combat, they rarely carried weapons.

Notable Sacred Fists

  • Grand Master of the Even-Handed Hlam
