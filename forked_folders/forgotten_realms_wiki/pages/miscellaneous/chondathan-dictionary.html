Chondathan dictionary

A list of words in the Chondathan language.

A

Arauna: "Lady"

Araunna: "Queen"

Ardess: "Princess"

Ardyr: "Prince"

Aro: "Lord"

Astrel: "King"

Aszraun: "eatery"

B

Balaumo: "tobacco"

C

Culdur: maniacal slaughter or berserk bloodbath.

D

dael or daelin: "a year" or "years"

G

Garrathen: murderous pirate, mercenary, or outlaw raid.

I

ildael: leap-year

M

Mahrsar: "Marchion"

Maharsara: "Marchioness"

N

Naraebul: "satchel"

R

Rakrathen: the word for murderous pirates, mercenaries, and outlaws.

Rathrur: "a bar" or "a tavern"

S

Saer: "Sir"

Staerra: "Duchess"

Storn: "Duke"

T

Taen: "Baron"

Taenya: "Baroness"

Tahar: "Knight"

Talthaek: a type of gravy-filled pie

Tlarvelm: "Viscount"

Tlarvelana: "Viscountess"

V

Velm: "Earl"

Velana: "Countess"

villendem: "soul"
