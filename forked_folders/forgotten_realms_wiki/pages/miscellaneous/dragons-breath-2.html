Dragon's breath was an arcane spell.

Effects

Circa 1369 DR, dragon's breath created a disembodied dragon head that breathed forth an enormously damaging fireball that only affected enemies and could throw them backwards. Like similar spells, it could only be learned by a powerful mage or sorcerer, and no scrolls existed of it.

Components

The spell required verbal, somatic, and material components. The material required was a hot pepper.

History

Vongoethe used dragon's breath against the party of Gorion's Ward.
