Polyhedron 124

Polyhedron Newszine #124 was released in September 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The Border Kingdoms: Hawkgarth

By Ed Greenwood. p. 6–8

Hawkgarth: a pretty friendly place for such high undead content.

The Halls of the Morning Light: A Temple of Lathander

By Rob Nicholls. p. 21–23

A Temple of Lathander, god of spring and dawn, for Ravens Bluff or your own campaign. It's Living City! It's campaign source material! Two mints in one!

The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited. p. 31

Reports from the front. News of the battle for Ravens Bluff.
