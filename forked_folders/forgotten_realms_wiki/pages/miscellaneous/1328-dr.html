1328 DR

  • After a raid on Menzoberranzan from the Illithid of Phanlinksal, House Baenre retaliates. In exchange for not destroying the settlement, El-Viddenvelp agrees to serve Yvonnel Baenre as an advisor.
  • Drizzt Do'Urden flees Menzoberranzan and goes to live in the wilds of the Underdark.
  • The Harper Ashenford Torinbow is captured by the Zhentarim while on a mission in Daggerdale.
  • Zaknafein is killed by Matron Malice.
  • The two volume Lord Bhereu’s Manual of Standards and Procedure, written by Duke Bhereu of Cormyr, is published.
