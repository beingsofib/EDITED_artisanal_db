Polyhedron 102

Polyhedron Newszine #102 was released in December 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Notes From HQ - Into the Heart of Malatra the Living Jungle

By Jean Rabe. p. 4

From Tarzan and Bomba the Jungle Boy to a place south of Kara-Tur.

The Living Jungle

By Uncredited. p. 5–7

Beyond the Living City, adventure lives on the lost Malatran Plateau, a new frontier for campaign-style tournament play.

Tribes of the Nubari

By Uncredited, illustrated by Phillip Robb. p. 8–12

From the brave Simbara to the peaceful Saru, the Living Jungle is a land of new and wondrous peoples.

Heroes of Malatra

By Uncredited. p. 13–14

Create your own heroes for the Living Jungle setting.

Living Jungle Hero Sheet

By Uncredited. p. 15;18

Use this form for your Malatran player character.

The Malatran Plateau

By David O'Brien. p. 16–17

The first view of the lands of Living Jungle.

Malatra Monstrous Compendium Sheets

By Uncredited. p. 20–25

New hero races for the Living Jungle.

  • Korobokuru
  • Katanga
  • Tam'hi
  • Shu
  • Saru

Architects of Adventure

By Uncredited. p. 26–28

How you can create a part of the Living Jungle!
