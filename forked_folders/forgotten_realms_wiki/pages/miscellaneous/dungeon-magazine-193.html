Dungeon magazine 193

Dungeon issue 193 was released in August of 2011 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Tavern Profile: The Beached Leviathan

By Craig Campbell, illustrated by Steve Ellis, Cartography by Sean MacDonald. p. 1-5

This article provides an overview of the Beached Leviathan tavern.

Monster Manual Updates: Kuo-Toa

By Logan Bonner, illustrated by Jason Felix and Daren Bader. p. 30-33

This article provides a 4th-edition rules update for the kuo-toa.

Monster Manual Updates: Sahuagin

By Logan Bonner, illustrated by Christopher Burdett and Daren Bader. p. 34-37

This article provides a 4th-edition rules update for the sahuagin.

The Gauntlgrym Gambit

By Daniel Marthaler, illustrated by Adam Paquette, Jim Nelson and Eric Belisle, Cartography by Jared Blando. p. 38-55

An adventure for characters of levels 3-5. Set in Gauntlgrym.

Eye on the Realms: The Winking Eyes of Rhauron

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Julie Dillon. p. 59-61

This article provides an overview of the mysterious magical gems known as the Winking Eyes of Rhauron.

Shards of Selûne

By Andrew G. Schneider, illustrated by Craig J. Spearing and Sam Burley, Cartography by Sean MacDonald. p. 62-83

An adventure for characters of levels 1-3. Set in Neverwinter, related to the Shards of Selûne.
