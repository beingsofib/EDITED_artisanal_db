1395 DR

The Wailing Years

  • The Netherese enclave of Sakkors reappears above Daerlun in the night. In the morning, there is civil unrest across the city and King Azoun V of Cormyr dispatches elite swordmages to establish order.
  • A great plague known variously as the Putrescent Anathema or the Plaguebringer's Blight brings devastation to Goldenfields and Waterdeep, primarily to the poor as the supply of grain plummets.
  • Most of the effects of the Spellplague end during this year, Most arcane magic returns to a semblance of normality, with a handful of major changes to the way spells are cast.
  • Nogah finds the Dreamheart in a drowned illithid city deep under the Sea of Fallen Stars.
