Watcher (disambiguation)

Watcher could refer to:

  • The Watcher, a nickname for the deity Helm;
  • The Watcher, the southernmost peak of the Desertsmouth Mountains;
  • Watcher (sword), a greatsword wielded by Helm's chosen Kleef Kenric;
  • Watcher, a yak-man who lived in the city of Huzuz; or
  • A member of The Watchers, a branch within the Order of the Yellow Rose who patrolled the Earthspur Mountains.
  • Watcher (Specialty priest), a specialty priest of Helm.
