Shadar-kai

The term shadar-kai referred to any one of three outwardly similar peoples native to the Shadowfell.

  • Shadar-kai (elf), loyal followers of the Raven Queen.
  • Shadar-kai (fey), a cursed species of fey.
  • Shadar-kai (Netherese), a more recent race of humanoids that arose from Netherese affected by the Spellplague.
