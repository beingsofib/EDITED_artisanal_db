Mir (disambiguation)

Mir could refer to:

  • The Mir (tribe), people of the Tun Marshes,
  • Mir (warlord), a warlord from Coramshan,
  • Mir (kingdom), the realm he founded,
  • Mir–Jhaamdath War, the war they fought,
  • Monrativi Teshy Mir, the former heartland of the kingdom,
  • Forest of Mir, the later name of Darthiir Forest,
  • Magis Mir, an adventuring company based in the forest,
  • Mirkazar, the Mir Protectorate in Tethyr, or
  • Spires of Mir, the stony remnants of the forest.
