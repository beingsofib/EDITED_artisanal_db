Wyvern watch (disambiguation)

Wyvern watch may refer to:

  • Wyvern watch, the evocation spell;
  • Wyvern Watch Inn, the inn found in Wheloon, or;
  • Wyvernwatch, an inn and taproom located in Myth Drannor.
  • Wyvernwatch Castle, a fortification on the border of the Stonelands.
