Basil (disambiguation)

Basil was a type of herb. It was also the given name of several individuals, including:

  • Basil Arness, a Turmishan officer in the 14 century DR,
  • Basil Grem, a gnome musician in the 15 century DR,
  • Basil of Lyndusfarne, a verbeeg runecaster in the 14 century DR, and
  • Basil Willowbrook, a halfling proprietor of Bindle's Blade of Skullport in the 14 century DR.
