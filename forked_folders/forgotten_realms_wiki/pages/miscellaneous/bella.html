Bella

Bella is a name that could refer to:

  • Bella, a dwarfish member of the Flaming Fist in 1492 DR,
  • Bella, a human member of the Flaming Fist in 1492 DR,
  • Bella, a cousin of Fineon Morninglight of High Haspur in the mid-14 century DR,
  • Bella, the twin sister or Stella, both ancestral spirit guardians,
  • Bella don Delroy Harpell, a member of the Harpell family in the mid-14 century DR,
  • Bella Sunbane, a hag from the Domains of Dread.
