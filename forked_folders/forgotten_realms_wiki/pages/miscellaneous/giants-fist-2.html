The giant's fist was a massive maul carried in the Storm Horns by the forces of the frost giant Grablandurr in the mid-1480s DR.

Description

The maul was heavily decorated, including golden plating and large blue crystals or gems.

Powers

The maul was magically imbued with even more brute force, causing it to hit harder and more easily stagger and dismantle enemies.

History

The giant's fist was captured from the supplies of General Grablandurr's forces in Tumblerock Pass, when they were wiped out by Cormyrean forces while carrying supplies for their Shadovar masters.
