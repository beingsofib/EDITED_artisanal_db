A Dove at Dawn was a book written by Ardreth, the High Harp of Berdusk. It was published in the .

History

As of the year , a copy of the book could be found in the Leaves of Learning, an Oghmanyte temple in Highmoon, as well as within the halls of Candlekeep on the Sword Coast.

A Dove at Dawn would more properly be described as a ballad composed by Ardreth and no doubt referred to one of the Seven Sisters, Dove Falconhand.
