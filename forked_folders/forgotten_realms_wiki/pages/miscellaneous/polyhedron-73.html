Polyhedron 73

Polyhedron Newszine #73 was released in July 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Experience Preferred, Part Two

By Tom Prusa. p. 7-13

The Company of the Swan takes a whimsical trip to an alternate plane in their search for the wizard Exeter.

Bahamut and Tiamat

By Erlene Mooney. p. 19–22

Bahamut and Tiamat, these new versions of the platinum dragon and his chromatic foe present forces to be respected and feared in any Advanced Dungeons & Dragons 2nd ed. campaign.

The Living City: Enchanted Items For Your Campaign

By Vince Garcia. p. 25

Two rings, a helm, a tapestry, and a splash of water add a touch of magic to the game.
