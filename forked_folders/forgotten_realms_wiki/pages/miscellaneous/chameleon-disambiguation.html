Chameleon (disambiguation)

A chameleon was a type of lizard, but the term could also refer to:

  • Chameleon, a spell common in Kara-Tur,
  • Chameleon, a psionic power,
  • Chameleon leather, a kind of leather made from the skin of ophidians,
  • The Chameleon, a title for the elven god Erevan Ilesere, and
  • A chameleon creature, magical crossbreeds of chameleons and other creatures with color-changing skin, found in the Underdark.
