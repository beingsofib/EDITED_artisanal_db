Dungeon magazine 162

Dungeon issue 162 was released in January of 2009 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Winter of the Witch

By Stephen Radney-MacFarland, illustrated by Dave Allsop, Eric Deschamps, Izzy, William O'Connor, Wayne Reynolds, Amelia Stoner and Sam Wood, Cartography by Jason A. Engle and Sean Macdonald. p. 27-61

In this epic adventure, the Winter Witch, a legendary Archfey, has launched a campaign to send eternal winter cascading over large portions of the world. After a summons to Winterhaven, the characters find a land locked in ice, and an old ally in need of aid. A trek to find a artifact known as the Sun's Sliver ensues, the only force potent enough to defeat the Winter Witch and banish her from the mortal world once more. Module for 3-5 PCs of level 22.
