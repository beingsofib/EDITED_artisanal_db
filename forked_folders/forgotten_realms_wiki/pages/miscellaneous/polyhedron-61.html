Polyhedron 61

Polyhedron Newszine #61 was released in July 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bookwyrms: History's Greatest Role-Playing Adventure

By Douglas Niles. p. 18

The Maztica Trilogy draws to a close with the release of the third book. But the adventures in this new area of the Forgotten Realms can go as far as your imagination with the new Maztica boxed set.

The Living City: Port of Ravens Bluff

By Lew Wright and Terry Wright. p. 20–21

The Sunfish and the Eldritch, Lightfoot, Findrol & Company Worldwide Import/Export are integral parts of Ravens Bluff's docks and will yield an adventure or two.
