-166 DR

__NOTOC__

  • The Eye Tyrant Wars come to an end in the Battle of Eyesvale, when King Silvam Ithal of Tethyr slays the last of the beholders on Uller's Hill.
  • Following victory over the beholders in the Eye Tyrant Wars, the human nations of Calimshan, Iltkazar, and Tethyr maintain their alliance in order to hunt down rogue beholders in their lands for several more years.
