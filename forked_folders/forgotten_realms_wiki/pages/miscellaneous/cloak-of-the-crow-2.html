Cloak of the crow

Item
nameCloak of the Crow
typeEnchanted cloak

The cloak of the crow was an enchanted cloak worn by Dahlia Sin'felle.

Description

The cloak of the crow was a high-collared black cape that could transform its wear into a giant crow.

Powers

The cloak of the crow was also reputed to affect changes to its bearer. When worn by Kensidan, the cloak of the crow made the man appear more bird-like, giving him an awkward, stiff-legged gait and a long, hooked nose.

History

The cloak of the crow was worn by Kensidan the Crow, of Ship Rethnor. In the , the cloak of the crow was worn by Borlann, the grandson of Kensidan. Following the death of Borlann, Sylora Salm became the wearer of the cloak of the crow.
