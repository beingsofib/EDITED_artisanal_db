Polyhedron 36

Polyhedron Newszine #36 was released in July 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

On the Road to...The Living City

By Harold Johnson and Jean Rabe. p. 26–28

It won't be long now before the gates of The Living City are within sight. But there is still a section of road that must be traveled to reach it. And the road is not deserted. A short encounter for PCs of any level.
