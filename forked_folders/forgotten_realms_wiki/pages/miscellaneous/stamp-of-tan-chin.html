Stamp of Tan Chin

The Stamp of Tan Chin was a unique and rare curse created by Raja Ambuchar Devayam in the Hordelands.

Effects

This curse was carried by a dowagu, a dangerous creature created through magic by Ambuchar Devayam. When one of these creatures touched a target, they marked them with the curse that appeared in the form of a dark tattoo on their skin. The victim did not experience any symptoms that something was amiss, but when they died, regardless of their cause of death, they rose as a zombie and traveled to Solon to serve as a mindless participant in Ambuchar's dark army. Particularly skilled victims rose from their death as a stronger form of undead such as a vampire, wight, etc.

It was impossible to remove or cover the Stamp of Tan Chin by any means, including the use of a wish, short of killing Ambuchar Devayam.
