-1428 DR

__NOTOC__

  • The death of Syl-Pasha Violir el Cajaan IX in Calimport.
  • Calimport falls and Qyraaptir the Bloodeye, a beholder mage, claims the throne of the pasha. Beholders rule the ruins of Calimport, and all large settlements across Calimshan, Iltkazar, and the Lake of Steam.
  • Naturally, trade between Calimshan and Tzindylspar suddenly comes to a halt.
