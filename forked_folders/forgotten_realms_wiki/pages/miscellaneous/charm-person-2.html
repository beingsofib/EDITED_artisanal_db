Charm person was an enchantment spell that made the subject a friend of the caster.

Effect

The post–Second Sundering version of the spell could target more creatures if used at a higher level.

Components

Only verbal and somatic components were needed.

The arcane verbal component for this spell, in the Mulan spellcasting tradition was Dothax Mylax Heeroz.

History

The Netherese arcanist Keonid was given the credit for the creation of this spell in .

Charm person was a common spell in the Realms by 1358 DR.
