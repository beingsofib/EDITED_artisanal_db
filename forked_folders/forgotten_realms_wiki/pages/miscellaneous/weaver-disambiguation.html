Weaver (disambiguation)

Weaver may refer to:

  • Weavers, persons who weaves fabric.
  • The Weavers, also known as spellweavers, beings from an alternate Prime Material plane.
  • "Spell weavers", a generic term sometimes used to designate wizards.
