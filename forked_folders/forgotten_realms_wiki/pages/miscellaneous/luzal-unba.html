Luzal unba

A luzal unba was a tradition of Sauk's clan. In rememberance of a dead family member, a warrior opened a bleeding gash from the crown of their own head to their cheek, and let it heal. Sauk opened one scar on his head in response to Kheil's death; upon meeting Berun, he opened a second one to remind himself that Kheil was truly dead.
