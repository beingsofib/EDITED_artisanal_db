The Dalelands

The Dalelands could refer to:

  • Dalelands, the predominantly human region, also known as "the Dales", in north Faerûn;
  • The Dalelands (sourcebook), a printed book describing these lands;
  • Living Forgotten Realms/Dalelands, the region of the Living Campaign that was assigned to players in Northern Europe and Russia.
