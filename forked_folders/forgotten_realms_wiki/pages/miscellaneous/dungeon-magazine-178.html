Dungeon magazine 178

Dungeon issue 178 was released in May of 2010 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Backdrop: Chessenta

By Brian James, illustrated by Christine MacTernan, Cartography by Mike Schley. p. 68-77

Chessenta is a land of feuding city-states vying for the favor of ancient gods through blood sport and conquest ... yet its people have inquisitive minds and an infectious zest for life.

Eye on the Realms: Deadeyes

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Jason Juta. p. 94-96

A monstrous beholder in Waterdeep.
