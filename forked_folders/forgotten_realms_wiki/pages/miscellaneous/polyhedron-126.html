Polyhedron 126

Polyhedron Newszine #126 was released in October 1997. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Notes From HQ: Why Haven't I Gotten a Poly in...?

By Robert Wiese. p. 3–4

Why we've been gone so long, and other questions, answered at last: the buyout of TSR, Inc. by Wizards of the Coast, new Mission Statement, New logo.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The Border Kingdoms: High Emmerock

By Ed Greenwood. p. 6–7

Who's who in High Emmerock and the dreaded Water Witch in Faerûn.

Living City Interactive Guidelines

By Dan Donnelly. p. 15

Run one of these at your own convention—it's easy!

Dispel Confusion

By Uncredited. p. 21

Changes and expansions for the Network's Living Campaigns: Living City Campaign expansion: Procampur, Living Death Campaign update, and Living Jungle Campaign update.

Forces to Reckon With: Wayward Wizards

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 24–25

Using Tulrun of the Tent in your game; a continuation of the article from Polyhedron #125, November 1996.

A World of Your Own: Island Campaigns, Part 5 - Ecological Nightmares

By Roger E. Moore. p. 26–27

More about the Tears of Selûne.

Personalities of Note: A Collection of NPCs from Ravens Bluff

By Sherrie Miller and Erica Woollums. p. 30

A selection of personalities judges are encouraged to use as background characters in adventures.
