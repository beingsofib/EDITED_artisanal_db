Jasmine (disambiguation)

Jasmine may refer to:

  • Jasmine, the flowering plant used for perfume;
  • Jasmine Night, a perfume made from the above sold from Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue;
  • Jasmine Dream, an inn in Dihliz;
  • Jasmine dust, an aromatic perfume powder from Kara-Tur;
  • Lady Jasmine, a priestess of Neverwinter of the late 1400s;
  • Jasmine, a winged spelljamming adventuress; or
  • "Jasmine", the 11 issue of the DC Forgotten Realms comic series.
