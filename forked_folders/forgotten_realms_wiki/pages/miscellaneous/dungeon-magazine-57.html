Dungeon magazine 57

Dungeon issue 57 was released in January/February of 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Rose Of Jumlat

By Jeroen Grasdyk, illustrated by Jim Holloway. p. 28-47;65

In the desert, old hatreds die hard. An Al-Qadim module for 3-6 PC's of levels 3-7. Includes a Monstrous Compendium entry for a kada.
