Nightshade (disambiguation)

"Nightshade" can refer to:

  • Deadly nightshade, another name for the belladonna plant,
  • Nightshade, the elven leader of the Silent Rain,
  • Nightshades, undead creatures native to the Plane of Shadow,
  • Nightshade, a nightclub in the city of Scornubel,
  • Nightshade, a unique enchanted shield,
  • Skabatha Nightshade, a hag of the Feywild,
  • Nightshade, the human owner of The Nightshade's Caress in Skullport,
  • Rian Nightshade, a Zhentarim envoy to Waterdeep, or
  • Another name for wood woses, elemental spirits of poisonous plants like the belladonna.
