Polyhedron 138

Polyhedron Newszine #138 was released in October 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Lotus the Fire Blossom

By Mason Porter. p. 5

A character spotlight of the half-elven thief Lotus of Ravens Bluff.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Wayfarers Guide to the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 8–9

Our ongoing tour now introduces Oeble, a town that stands nigh the center of the Border Kingdoms, near many realms but a part of none...
