611 DR

  • The Everhorde orcs pour out of the Spine of the World and rampage across the North, plunging the region into war. Yarlith is ruined, as is Illusk, though the Host Tower survives. Neverwinter is also spared, with the help of Palarandusk the Sun Dragon.
  • On Evermeet, Amlaruil Moonflower is born.
  • The members of the Pentad Retreat become sharn after the traitorous precept, the vampire wizard Palron Kaeth, used the orcs of the Everhorde to attack the hidden enclave of Miyeritar.
  • The dwarf realm of Roldilar begins construction of Sarbreen.
