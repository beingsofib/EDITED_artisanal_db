Shoon Dynasty

The Shoon Dynasty was a family of rulers of the Lands of Intrigue. Specifically, the term could refer to:

  • the Shoon Imperium, which ruled over Calimshan, Tethyr, and what would become Amn, or
  • the Shoon Dynasty of Tethyr, which ruled as part of the Shoon Traitorum.
