Polyhedron 74

Polyhedron Newszine #74 was released in August 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: The Ill Eagle Inn

By Christopher Earley. p. 7–10;31

The Ill Eagle Inn in Ravens Bluff features fine cuisine, an unusual mascot, and several more services that don't meet the casual visitor's eye.

The Everwinking Eye: Words To The Wise

By Ed Greenwood. p. 14–15

This overview of Faerûnian terms and names will have you speaking Common like a native in no time.

Experience Preferred, Part III

By Tom Prusa. p. 16-20

The Company of the Swan finally meets its nemesis, but can they stop him before he carries out his maniacal scheme for vengeance?
