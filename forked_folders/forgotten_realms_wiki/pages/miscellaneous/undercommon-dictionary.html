Undercommon dictionary

A list of words and phrases from the Undercommon trading language.

A

abster: "memory"

al: "dead, death"

arint: "gourmet of, taster"

ator: "elder, revered"

B

Bob

"Bob".

bossk: "lord, master".

C

corian: "leader, liaison"

H

hion: "black, dark, darkness"

hoon: "abomination, outsider"

I

illi: "mind"

ilsen: "brain"

L

lugri: "fear, feared"

M

maanze: "creed, law"

malin: "arcane, psionic"

mious: "sage, scholar"

O

ordell: "golem, humanoid, mind flayer"

Q

quas: "honor"

S

sine:"great, powerful"

T

tharid: "devour, devourer"

thelid: "conquerer, eater"

thid: "flayer, ruler"

tor: "elder, revered" (see ator)

U

uli: "noble"

ullip: "thought"

urop: "servant, slave, thrall"

X

xalli: "disgust, pain"

Z

Zin-carla: "Spirit-wraith"
