The Shadow of the Avatar trilogy

The Shadow of the Avatar Trilogy is a novel series by author Ed Greenwood, the creator of the Forgotten Realms. The trilogy is set during the same time frame as The Avatar Series, which describe the events during the Time of Troubles.

Books

  1. Shadows of Doom (March 1995)
  2. Cloak of Shadows (June 1995)
  3. All Shadows Fled (October 1995)
