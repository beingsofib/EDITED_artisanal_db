1342 DR

__NOTOC__

  • The rumor of a coup against him causes Matasuuri Nagahide to exile his nephew to the Island of One Thousand Pines.
  • Goblins raid the small town of Termalaine in Icewind Dale. Were it not for the intervention of Clan Battlehammer, the town may have been destroyed.
  • The Hojo War begins.
  • The orphaned Catti-brie is adopted by Bruenor Battlehammer.
  • The evil wizard Cyndre arrives in the Isles from Thay and joins the Council of Sorcerers.
  • Irritym Wonderstar is killed by thieves who were looking to rob his house.
  • The town of Verdigris in the Dragonspine Mountains is freed from a magical glacier that had been in place for over 300 years. Colonists found New Verdigris in order to gain easy access to the area's rich gem mines, ignorant of the reason the glacier was erected in the first place—the lich Eldamar and his army of monsters.
  • The wereraven Anil Zasperdes visits Candlekeep and hides a map leading to Chalet Brantifax within the pages of the Book of the Raven.
