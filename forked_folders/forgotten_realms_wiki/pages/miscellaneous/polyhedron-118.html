Polyhedron 118

Polyhedron Newszine #118 was released in April 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Open Air Farmers Market

By Joseph Wichmann. p. 6–8

Shop for an adventure in the Ravens Bluff Open Air Farmers Market. Reprinted from Polyhedron #44.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: Dunbridges and the Duskwood

By Ed Greenwood. p. 13–15

Dunbridges and the Duskwood.

Forgotten Deities: Dendar; Kezef

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 18–21

A look at two of the elder elemental evils: Dendar the Night Serpent and Kezef the Chaos Hound.

The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited. p. 28

Society News, Belgard's Stand Falls.
