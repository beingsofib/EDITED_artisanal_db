Lantern rings were magic items that emitted a slight glow.

Description

These rings were quite plain and usually made from brass.

Powers

The light given off by a lantern ring was similar to that of the dancing lights spell, moving and changing based upon the wearer's will. These rings could provide enough light to appear like a beacon for up to two hours but would not function underground or indoors.

Notable Owners

Tessaril Winter, the warrior and wizard of Eveningstar, carried some lantern rings. The Purple Dragons stationed in Eveningstar also wore these rings.
