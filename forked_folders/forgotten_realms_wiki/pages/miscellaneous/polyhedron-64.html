Polyhedron 64

Polyhedron Newszine #64 was released in October 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Short People

By Tom Prusa and Matt Prusa. p. 8–18

A wagonload of Burrowville Blueleaf must be sold - for a reasonable price or the evil Zhentarim will take over the town. An adventure for 6 pregenerated 7th level characters.
