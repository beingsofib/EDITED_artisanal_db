Polyhedron 88

Polyhedron Newszine #88 was released in October 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Notes From HQ: Living City Growing Pains

By Jean Rabe. p. 4

The Network's Chunk of the Realms, Living Dilemma, and Summer Conventions are what the editor discusses this month.

The Everwinking Eye: Treasures of the Vast, Part One

By Ed Greenwood. p. 13–15

Some treasure tales of the Vast, from A to D.

The Living City: The Ravens Bluff Diviner's Guild

By Walter Roberts. p. 16,

A look at the Ravens Bluff Diviner's Guild, a few of its members, and its trade.
