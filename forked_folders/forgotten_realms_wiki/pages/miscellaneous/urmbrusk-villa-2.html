Urmbrusk Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Urmbrusk.

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward on Diamond Street in between the Street of the Singing Dolphin and the Street of Whispers.

Description

The walled compound contained four separate buildings located at each corner of the estate. Each building stood between two and three stories tall.
