Polyhedron 147

Polyhedron Newszine #147 was released in July 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Pillars of Ravens Bluff

By The RPGA Staff, illustrated by Kalman Andrasofszky. p. 14–23

In a city teeming with legions of heroes, it's difficult to say who's most important. The four dignitaries presented here, however, certainly vie for the title, and each brings something different and crucial to the government of the City of Ravens.

  • Lord Mayor Charles Oliver O'Kane
  • Guildmaster Colin O'Lyre
  • Lady Lauren DeVillars
  • Lady Penelope Norwood

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Wayfarers Guide to the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 42–43

Last issue we introduced the Owlhold, a Border Kingdom rife with those who would prefer to stay hidden. The wily Sage of Shadowdale continues his tour, with one eye on the best taverns in the region and another on his purse.
