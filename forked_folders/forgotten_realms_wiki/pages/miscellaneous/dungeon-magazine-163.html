Dungeon magazine 163

Dungeon issue 163 was released in February of 2009 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

House of Pain

By Jennifer Clarke-Wilkes, illustrated by Ryan Barger, Cartography by Rob Lee. p. 4-11

Someone or something has been grabbing eladrin from the Feywild. As the heroes pursue another quest, they have the opportunity to discover the force behind the abductions, which might lead them to more conflicts than they anticipate. This Side Trek introduces the Shadowfell and shadar-kai to your player characters, if they haven’t been exposed to either before now. Module for 3-5 PC's of levels 8-10.
