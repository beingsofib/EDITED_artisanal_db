-2047 DR

The Netherese Archwizard Quantoul is born, creator of dozens of spells and founder of the city of Jockteleg.

  • Netherese arcanists hunt down the forces of the ogre mage Storm. After a battle north of the Southfill River, the ogres are defeated. It is unknown if Storm himself is killed, though his people never bother the Netherese again.
