Sharandar (disambiguation)

Sharandar may refer to:

  • Sharandar, the capital of the ancient realm of Iliyanbruen;
  • New Sharandar, the city of elves and eladrin that existed in both Faerûn, and the Feywild; or
  • Neverwinter: Sharandar, the expansion module for Neverwinter.
