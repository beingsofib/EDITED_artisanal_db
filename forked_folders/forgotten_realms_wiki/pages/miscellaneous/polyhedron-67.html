Polyhedron 67

Polyhedron Newszine #67 was released in January 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Sigil of the Silent Night

By Ben Robbins. p. 6–10

The Sigil of the Silent Night watches over businesses in Ravens Bluff.

The Everwinking Eye: Who's Who in Mulmaster, Part 2

By Ed Greenwood. p. 22–23

A bevy of ambitious characters are ready to muscle their way into your campaign.
