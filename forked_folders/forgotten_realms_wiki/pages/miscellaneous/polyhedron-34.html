Polyhedron 34

Polyhedron Newszine #34 was released in March 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

On the Road to the Living City

By Michael Lach and Jean Rabe. p. 6–8,30

The first installment of Polyhedron's Living City column, introducing the project to readers and presenting two adventure encounters set near the city.
