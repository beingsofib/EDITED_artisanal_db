Dungeon magazine 40

Dungeon issue 40 was released in March/April of 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Khamsa's Folly

By J. Mark Bicking, illustrated by David O. Miller, Cartography by Diesel. p. 18-36, 51

He meant to reign a thousand years. The gods deposed him in a matter of seconds. Module for 6-8 PC's of levels 4-6.
