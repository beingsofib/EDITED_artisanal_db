Polyhedron 50

Polyhedron Newszine #50 was released in November 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: The Toysmiths

By Steve Morton. p. 10–11

The Brothers Gaewilder are two reasons why Raven's Bluff has some of the happiest children in the Realms.

The Living City: The Brothers Galgolar Pawnshop

By James Lowder. p. 12–13;38

Need a loan? The Brothers Galgolar pawnshop probably can help, depending on what you've got for collateral.

New Rogues Gallery: Raven's Bluff Personalities

By Ed Friedlander, Benjamin Pierce, Francois Poulin, Rick Reid and Rich Rydberg. p. 14–19

A bevy of magic-users and one unusual boy fill this gallery's offerings for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons (2nd ed.), plus a few new magic items and spells.

Counterfeit Dreams

By Skip Williams and Jean Rabe, illustrated by Angela Bostick. p. 22-36

This is a tale of misdirection, betrayal, and revenge in the Living City. Shrewd parties can get the gold, but the foolish will wind up in the quarries.
