Polyhedron 123

Polyhedron Newszine #123 was released in September 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The Border Kingdoms: The Grand Duchy of Shantal

By Ed Greenwood. p. 7–9

A visit into the heart of waiting splendor and danger, The Grand Duchy of Shantal.

A Wish for Temptation

By Glen R. Goodwin and Robert Wiese. p. 11–22

War gets close to home in this Living City adventure. Includes 6 pre-generated characters. An AD&D 2nd ed. adventure for 6 PCs, Levels 6-7.

The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited. p. 31

News in the Living City shared world: Belanor arrested, the moral fabric of society is shredded, and more.
