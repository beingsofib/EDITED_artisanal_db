Polyhedron 78

Polyhedron Newszine #78 was released in December 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Arts' Haven

By Randall Lemon, Cartography by Brian Blume. p. 17–20

Meet the businessmen who've banded together at Arts' Haven, an old brownstone in Ravens Bluff that is home to music, portraits, tall tales, and a tragic past.

;Magic

;Items: harp of healing

The Everwinking Eye: A Visit to Melvaunt

By Ed Greenwood. p. 24–25

A tour of Thentia and Melvaunt, two Moonsea cities with a lot in common.
