Shadowdark (disambiguation)

Shadowdark might refer to:

  • The region of the Underdark in the Deep Wastes below the Dalelands and Moonsea, specifically Shadowdale; "Shadowdark" in this context refers to "Shadowdale Underdark", "the Underdark beneath Shadowdale", or "the Underdark's Shadowdale region".
  • The Shadowfell reflection of the Underdark and Feydark;
  • Shadowdark ale, brewed in Shadowdale (town);
  • Shadowdark House, a magic shop in Daerlun; or
  • Thelbaerone Shadowdark, the proprietor of Shadowdark House.
