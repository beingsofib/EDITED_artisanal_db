Polyhedron 137

Polyhedron Newszine #137 was released in July 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Wayfarers Guide to the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 8–9

Our tour of the Border Kingdoms this month looks at Nether Mukshar, the last of the three Mukshar realms, a land often forgotten as a 'backlands' realm.

City Stories: The Temple of Mystra

By Carl Buehler. p. 10–12

The Hall of Mystery, a new Temple of Mystra in Ravens Bluff and its priests.

Airs Above the Ground: A Manual of Dexterity for Warhorses

By Patricia A. Jackson. p. 18–21

Combat maneuvers for war horses in the Advanced Dungeons & Dragons game.

Living City Campaign Update

p. 31

Rules updates for the Living City.
