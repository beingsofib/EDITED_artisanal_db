Iron Lord

The Iron Lord, or Huhrong, was the highest-ranking male ruler of Rashemen. He was expected to rule wisely in the best interests of the people of Rashemen. His duties included keeping travel between settlements relatively safe, securing the frontier lands, controlling raiding monsters from the wilder parts of Rashemen, and serving as supreme warlord during times of war.

At a local level, each settlement was ruled over by a fyrra who only controlled the armed forces under his command. A fyrra's rule could be compared to that of a mayor. Each fyrra reported to the Iron Lord.

The real power, however, lay with the Wychlaran. They picked the Iron Lord and could change him whenever they felt it necessary.

The Iron Lords of Rashemen

  • 1360 DR: Hyarmon Hussilthar
  • 1361 DR: Thydrim Yvarrg. Thydrim was slain by a Thayan assassin in 1371 DR.
  • 1372 DR: Volas Dyervolk "the Bear". Volas was a stubborn man who often argued with the Wychlaran if he felt they were wrong, but was ultimately respected by the witches. He was awarded his position after the death of Thydrim Yvarrg, his predecessor.
  • 1479 DR: Mangan Uruk and 1489 DR
