Polyhedron 55

Polyhedron Newszine #55 was released in September 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Easy Money

Jay Tummelson and Lew Wright. Illustrated by Jim Holloway. pp. 9–14

Adventurers are hired to capture alive a golden cave bear as a new act for the Mooney & Sons Circus. An adventure module of PCs of levels 4–6 and using the 2-edition ruleset.

Spelljamming Monsters

By Roger E. Moore, William W. Connors, and Dale A. Donovan. pp. 15–18

Three new creatures for the Spelljammer setting: chakchaks, oortlings, and magistars.

The New Rogues Gallery: Iron Maidens

By Costa Valhouli. pp. 19–20

A trio of female mercenaries.

The Everwinking Eye: A Closer Look at Maskyr's Eye

By Ed Greenwood. p. 24–25

The second part in an exploration of Maskyr's Eye, looking at the village itself and its inhabitants.

The Living City: Shrine of Honest Toil

By Thomas M. Kane, Cartography by Dave Conant. p. 28–29

A corrupt workhouse in Ravens Bluff.
