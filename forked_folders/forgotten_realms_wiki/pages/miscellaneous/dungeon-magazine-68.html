Dungeon magazine 68

Dungeon issue 68 was released in May/June of 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Al-Kandil

By John Baichtal, illustrated by Karl Waller. p. 52-54

A genie lamp is a rare prize, indeed. Now that you have one, what will you wish for? An Al-Qadim module for 1-6 PC's of levels 5-10.
