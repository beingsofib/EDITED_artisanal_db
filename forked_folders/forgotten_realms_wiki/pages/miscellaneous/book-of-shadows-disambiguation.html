Book of Shadows (disambiguation)

Book of Shadows may refer to:

  • The Book of Shadows, a necromantic spellbook by Nebt Bhakau of Zakhara;
  • Briel's Book of Shadows, a mysterious spellbook;
  • The Mal'eb'dala, more commonly called The Book of Shadows, which discussed the Shadowking and the shadevar; or
  • A grimoire of spells bestowed to warlocks who received the Pact of the Tome from their otherworldly patron.
