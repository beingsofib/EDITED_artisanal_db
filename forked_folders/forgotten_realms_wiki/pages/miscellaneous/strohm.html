Strohm

"Strohm" was the name of a dynasty of Tethyr named after the elven king who ended the Shoon Imperium. Individuals named "Strohm" include:

  • King Strohm I of Tethyr
  • King Strohm II of Tethyr
  • King Strohm III of Tethyr
  • King Strohm IV of Tethyr
  • King Strohm V of Tethyr

"Strohm" could also refer to designer Keith Francis Strohm.
