1067 DR

  • Emperor Lui of the newly forged nation of T'u Lung offers amnesty to secret societies formerly banned by Shou Lung in exchange for sending soldiers to help repel Shou invaders in the First Incursion.
  • The Black Leopard Cult is significant among the societies who accept the offer, though they are not the largest nor the most liked in the army.
  • The combined army of T'u Lung, named the Might of the South, successfully routs the Shou invaders at both the Battle of Feng Wa Crossing and the Stand of Ninto.
  • By six years of age, the love of the people of Tabot for the High Lord of Oceans, H'Dang Li, leads to him uniting the lamas and creating the Council Kashag. The nobles of Tabot, fearing his influence and the loss of their own power, lead armies to bring him down but H'dang Li's magic causes them to lay down their arms. He goes on to create many powerful magic items.
