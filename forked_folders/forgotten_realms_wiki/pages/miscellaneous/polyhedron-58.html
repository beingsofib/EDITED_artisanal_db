Polyhedron 58

Polyhedron Newszine #58 was released in March 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Everwinking Eye: Adventures in Maskyr's Eye

By Ed Greenwood. p. 25–26

The fifth and final part in an exploration of Maskyr's Eye, featuring two mini-adventure ideas: in "Ssuntyr's Revenge", an old enemy returns to the village for vengeance, and in "Carnage on the High Road", there are murderous brigands preying on travelers.

The Living City: Of Wits and Wizards, A Logic Puzzle

By Rob Nicholls. p. 27

A logic puzzle to a solve a theft in Ravens Bluff. Just sort through the wizards, their houses, their familiars, their drinks, and their favorite brands of tobacco. Simple, really.
