Shou-town

Shou-town was a name commonly given to city districts with high populations of Shou inhabitants. Some known Shou-towns were:

  • Shou-town (Bral)
  • Shou-town (Chavyondat)
  • Shou-town (Elversult)
  • Shou-town (Marsember)
  • Shou-town (Phsant)
  • Shou-town (Sheirtalar)
  • Shou-town (Telflamm)
  • Shou-town (Tharsult)
  • Shou-town (Westgate)
