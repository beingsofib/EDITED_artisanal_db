Maggie (disambiguation)

Maggie was a common name used in the Realms. This could refer to:

  • Maggie Luskan, a woman who ran illegal gambles in Waterdeep in the 15 century DR,
  • Maggie Ni Chuirc, a criminal in Baldur's Gate,
  • Maggie Rollins, a widowed baker from Arabel in the 14 century DR,
  • Maggie Tefoco, a resident of Baldur's Gate,
  • Maggie Terrens, a resident of Moonhaven,
  • Maggie Two-Fingers, a Sword Coast criminal, or
  • Mad Maggie, a night hag.
