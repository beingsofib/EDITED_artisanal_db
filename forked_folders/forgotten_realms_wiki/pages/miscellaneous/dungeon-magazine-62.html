Dungeon magazine 62

Dungeon issue 62 was released in November/December of 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Grimjaws

By Jennifer Title Stack, illustrated by Lorelle Ahlstrom and R. K. Post, Cartography by Michael Scott. p. 34–37

The Vast Swamp is a most unnatural habitat. Module for 2–3 PCs of levels 5–7.
