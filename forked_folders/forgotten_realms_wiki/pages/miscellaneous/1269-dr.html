1269 DR

  • Sahuagin ambush a Eadraal merfolk hunting party, slaying the Prince Aldem and Prince-Consort Kran, who die to save the king's heir Kosul. The merfolk of Eadraal are distraught with grief for the much-loved princes.
  • Flamerule 20: Bodvar and his Moor Eagle clan are nearly killed by monsters in the Bottomless Bogs of Vaasa, and are only saved by the intervention of Melegaunt Tanthul.
  • Highharvestide: Bodvar and the Moor Eagle clan accept the darkswords from Melegaunt Tanthul, and they and their descendants are bound to serve him.
  • Highharvestide: Bodvar's wife, Idona, is slain by dragonmen.
  • Thaurog's Keep is constructed by a half-orc bandit lord named Thaurog. It stands on the site later occupied by Nesmé.
  • The Granite Tower is constructed
