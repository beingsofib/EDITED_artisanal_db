Silverhand

Silverhand might refer to:

  • Alassra Silverhand, better known as the Simbul, the Witch-Queen of Aglarond between 1320 DR and 1425 DR.
  • Alustriel Silverhand, Elué Dualen to the Tel'Quessir, the High Lady of the Silver Marches.
  • Laeral Silverhand, the Lady of Waterdeep and wife of Khelben “Blackstaff” Arunsun.
  • Storm Silverhand, the bard of Shadowdale.
