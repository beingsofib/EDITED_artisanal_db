Inquisitor (disambiguation)

An inquisitor was an individual who sought out those who were considered to be foes of a certain cause or organization. In the Realms this could refer to:

  • Inquisitor or High Inquisitor, two ranks within the Church of Bane;
  • Inquisitors, those psionic individuals who knew when others lied; or
  • Inquisitor (creature), undead horrors that excelled in the art of torture.

It could also refer to a number of different character classes, or sub-classes. These included:

  • Inquisitor (kit), a paladin character kit from 2 edition; or
  • Inquisitor of the Drowning Goddess, a prestige class for Kuo-toan worshipers of Blibdoolpoolp.
