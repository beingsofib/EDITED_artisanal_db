1206 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Jorist Archneie, the self-styled Sealord of All Faerûn and high priest of Umberlee, dies a fat, blustering tyrant. A giant wave claims his corpse from the top of his tower. His many children and grandchildren fight amongst themselves over who will inherit The Orglara. A lesser priest steals it and goes into hiding while they battle.
  • Donder Cormaeril, head of House Cormaeril in Cormyr.
  • The fifth Emperor Chin of Shou Lung leads attacks on the pirates of the Celestial Sea, sacking their havens on Akari Island and elsewhere. Wa, a victim itself of the pirate raids, recognizes Shou Lung's claims to several small islands in the Celestial Sea, and shogun Eiko grants ownership to them after assisting the Shou against the pirates.
