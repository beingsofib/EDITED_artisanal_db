-3400 DR

__NOTOC__

  • The Ninth Rysar of Rystall Wood begins.
  • Hobgoblins and their dwarf slaves construct a gigantic statue to Nomog-Geaya, the hobgoblin god, in the Gorge of Nomog-Geaya the Warrior. The gorge becomes a gathering place for hobgoblin tribes for three centuries, though they are obstructed by the dwarves of Shanatar.
