Market Street (disambiguation)

Market Street was a common street name in the Realms and may refer to:

  • Market Street, a street in the Brampton district in Baldur's Gate,
  • Market Street, a street in Hillsfar,
  • Market Street, a street in Neverwinter that crossed Clockmaker's Way,
  • Market Street, a street in Ravens Bluff,
  • Market Street, a street in Thentia that crossed Keldon Way,
  • Market Street, a street in Waterdeep that crossed Sul Street,
  • East Market Street, one of the market streets in Westgate,
  • Southgate Market Street, one of the market streets in Westgate,
  • Westgate Market Street, one of the market streets in Westgate.
