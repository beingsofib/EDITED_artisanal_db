Scale

A scale could refer to:

  • A merchant's scale,
  • A type of skin found on some creatures, such as dragons or lizards,
  • Scale armor, a type of armor with overlapping pieces, similar to the scales of creatures,
  • A range of musical notes used in musical instruments, or
  • Scale (specialty priest), a specialty priest of the Church of Tyr.
