Torst (disambiguation)

Torst could refer to:

  • Torst Habilar, a Merchant Prince of Alaghôn in Turmish in the 12 century DR;
  • Torst Obarskyr, a king of Cormyr in the 3 century DR;
  • Torst Skydark, a hunter of Maskyr's Eye in the 14 century DR; or
  • Torst Urlivan, a fence of stolen goods in Waterdeep in the 14 century DR.
