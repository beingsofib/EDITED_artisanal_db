Francis (disambiguation)

Francis is a name that could refer to:

  • Francis XII, a queen of Graubünden in the Whamite Isles,
  • Francis, a merchant from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR,
  • Francis Peele, a priest of Tempus from Ravens Bluff in the 14 century DR,
  • Francis Tenraw, a nobleman in a leadership role who lived before 1372 DR,
  • Francis Westhaven, a retired warrior from Ravens Bluff in the 14 century DR,
  • Francis the All-Seeing, the adventurer who banished two child ghosts from the Merchant Quarter of Neverwinter,
