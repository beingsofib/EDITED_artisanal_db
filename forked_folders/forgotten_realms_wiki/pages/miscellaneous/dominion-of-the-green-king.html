Dominion of the Green King

Dominion of the Green King (or XGR-5) was supposed to be the final module for the Legacy of the Green Regent RPGA campaign but it was never released to Dungeon Masters.

Dominion of the Green King was supposed to have been played at the Winter Fantasy convention during February 23-26 of 2006.
