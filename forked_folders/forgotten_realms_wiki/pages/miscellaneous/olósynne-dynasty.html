Olósynne Dynasty

The Olósynne Dynasty ruled over Aglarond between 1065 DR and 1425 DR. It began with the half-elf King Brindor's victory over Velprin and ended with the apparent death of Alassra Shentrara, "the Simbul".

Monarchs

King Brindor : Reigned 1065 DR – 1128 DR. First king of Aglarond.

King Althon : Reigned 1128 DR – 1189 DR. Became known as Althon the Old.

King Elthond : Reigned 1189 DR – 1194 DR. First son of Althon.

King Philaspur : Reigned 1194 DR – 1197 DR. Second son of Althon.

Mage-Queens Thara and Ulae : Reigned 1197 DR – 1257 DR under joint rule. Twin daughters of Philaspur.

King Halacar : Reigned 1257 DR – 1260 DR. Son of Ulae. Killed by agents of Thay.

Mage-Queen Ilione : Reigned 1260 DR – 1320 DR. Daughter of Ulae.

Alassra Shentrara, "the Simbul" : Reigned 1320 DR – 1425 DR. Ilione's apprentice and named successor.
