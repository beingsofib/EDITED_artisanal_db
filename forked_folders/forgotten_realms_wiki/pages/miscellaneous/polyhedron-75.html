Polyhedron 75

Polyhedron Newszine #75 was released in September 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Eye Of Newt and Food For Familiars

By Carla Hollar and Nicky Rea. p. 6–9

The Kettle of Many Things and Mirigan's Complete Components will fill your player characters purses with spell materials and their stomachs with wondrous, warm food. This was the winning entry in our Network club Living City neighbors contest.

The Everwinking Eye: Sagely Secrets Made Known

By Ed Greenwood. p. 10–11

Elminster takes time out to find printing mistakes in products relating to the Realms campaign setting, including additional Spellfire rules.

Of Lamps and Logic: A Brainteaser From The Land Of Fate

By Rob Nicholls. p. 28

The Land of Fate is the setting for this brain teaser. Can you find the right genie in the right bottle?
