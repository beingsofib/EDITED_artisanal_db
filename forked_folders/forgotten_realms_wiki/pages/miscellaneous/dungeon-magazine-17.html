Dungeon magazine 17

Dungeon issue 17 was released in May/June of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Waiting Room of Yen-Wang-Yeh

By Greg Kramer, illustrated by Lynne Taylor, Cartography by Diesel. p. 31-41

You must recover the bones of a long-dead noble. Except he's not quite done with them. Oriental Adventures module for 4-6 PC's of levels 5-6.
