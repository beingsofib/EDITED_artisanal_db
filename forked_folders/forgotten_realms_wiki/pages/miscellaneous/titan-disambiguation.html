Titan (disambiguation)

"Titan" could refer to a number of creatures of epic size, including:

  • Titans, the largest of the true giant races,
  • The greater titans, a group of entities exiled to and imprisoned in Tarterus by the Greek pantheon.
  • The lesser titans, a group of entities living in Arborea that are descended from the greater titans.
  • The dawn titans, or primordials,
  • Titans, a sub-category of quasi-deities, or
  • Any of the largest specimens of the giant races, such as earth titans or fire titans.
