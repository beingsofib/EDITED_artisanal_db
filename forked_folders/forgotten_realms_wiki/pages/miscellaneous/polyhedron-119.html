Polyhedron 119

Polyhedron Newszine #119 was released in May 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Knights the Dove

By David Feest, Jr.. p. 6

Peaceful knights who knock heads.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The Throat of Talduth Vale

By Ed Greenwood. p. 7–9

Emrys, bottleneck of the Border Kingdoms.

The Druid Circle of Chauntea

By Claire Hoffman. p. 18–21

Strangely unaffected by the Ravens Bluff war.

Forgotten Deities: Finder Wyvernspur

By Eric L. Boyd and Kate Novak. p. 22–23

Finder Wyvernspur, the Nameless Bard.

The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited. p. 31

Nioga Named Commander Of The Bloody Hatchets, Reporter Killed In Gang War.
