Phylactery (disambiguation)

A phylactery could refer to:

  • phylactery (religious item), priestly items, often worn on the head, holding religious text or relics, some of which were magical and provided powers associated with morale and alignment;
  • A lich's phylactery;
  • Phylactery of bravery;
  • Phylactery of faithfulness;
  • Phylactery of righteousness; or
  • Phylactery of undead turning.
