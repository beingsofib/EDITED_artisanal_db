-1293 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Maldraedior, the suzerain of the blue dragons of Unther and Mulhorand, abdicates, retreating to his lair in the Smoking Mountains near Dalath.
  • The spells feeblemind, hypnotism, and mass suggestion are first introduced under the names Hamring's feeblemind, Hamring's hypnotism, and Hamring's mass hypnosis after Netherese arcanist Hamring.
  • The Crescent Court, a temple of Sehanine is constructed in Cormanthyr, with the aid of Coronal Miirphys Irithyl.
