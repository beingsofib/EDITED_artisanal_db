927 DR

  • Uktar 30: Red stormclouds engulf the Sword Mountains and rain blood for three days. This is the wrath of the orc god Yurtrus, and results in the Blood Plagues. The orc shaman Wund has prophesied this event.
  • Wund unifies the orc tribes of the Sword Mountains under the rule of the chieftain Uruth. They found the orcish realm of Uruth Ukrypt.
  • After nearly fifty years of excesses and war from several theocrat rulers, the new king of Westgate, Altarl Campion, bans temples from the city. Not long afterwards, the former temple residents build shrines on several hills just outside the city walls.
  • The thieves' guild that will come to be known as the Shadowmasters of Telflamm is founded.
  • Gostaraj, the King of Westgate, dies.
