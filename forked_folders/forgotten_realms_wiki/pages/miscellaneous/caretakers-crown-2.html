Caretaker's crown

The caretaker's crown was a unique enchanted crown crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Halbenet Soladonel in the late 15 century DR. It was one of many pieces of so-called "Silverhand gear" crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep.

Description

Silverhand crafted the caretaker's crown with careful consideration of Halbenet's plans and mission.

Powers

The Caretaker's Crown allowed Halbenet to protect others by projecting their great gift of calm onto their allies to help maintain everyone's composure in combat. This bestowed an effect similar to that provided by a ring of mind shielding.

Notable Owners

  • Halbenet Soladonel, an elven cleric of Corellon for whom Silverhand also crafted the sword of bright blinding and the seal of constancy.
