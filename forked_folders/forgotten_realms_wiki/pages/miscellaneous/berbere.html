Berbere

Berbere was a type of spice mixture unique to the people of Chult.

Uses

In the city of Port Nyanzaru, berbere was commonly used in two dishes. One was Asa wat, a preparation of tuna in a stew that had been spiced with berbere. The second was Doro t'ibs, a hearty meat dish consisted of chicken and spicy peppers sautéed in berbere.
