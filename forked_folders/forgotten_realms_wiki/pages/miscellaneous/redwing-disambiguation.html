Redwing (disambiguation)

Redwing may refer to:

  • Arnfalamme Redwing, a red dragon whose domain included parts of Cormyr and the Dragon Coast as of the mid-to-late 14 century DR,
  • Morgan Redwing, a fighter in service of the Flaming Fist in the 14 century DR.
  • Redwings, huge dragonfly-like beasts bred in High Imaskar in the 15 century DR,
  • Redwing (butterfly), a species of butterfly found on the High Moor,
  • Redwing blackbirds, a kind of bird found in the Heartlands, or
  • Redwing Tribe, an orcish tribe of 200 that emerged from a gate in Waterdeep during the Final Charge of the Redwing Tribe in the late 14 century DR.
