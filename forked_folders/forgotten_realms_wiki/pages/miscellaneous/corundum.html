Corundum

A corundum was one of a group of minerals and could refer to:

  • Sapphire, a blue or black variety,
  • Ruby, a red or pink variety,
  • Shou Lung amethyst, a purple variety, or
  • Shou Lung topaz, a fiery yellow variety.
  • Jacinth was also related to the corundum minerals.
