Simon (disambiguation)

Simon may refer to:

  • Simon, a half-elf sorcerer of the late 1400s DR;
  • Simon Dessino, Lord of Arrabar c. 1370 DR;
  • Simon Thrithyn, proprietor of the House of Good Spirits in Waterdeep c. 1361 DR;
  • Simon (stablehand), a worker at the Palace of Waterdeep c. 1371 DR; or
  • Simon (priest), high priest of Stronmaus in Hartsvale c. 1366 DR.
