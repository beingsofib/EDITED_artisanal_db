Polyhedron 103

Polyhedron Newszine #103 was released in January 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Forgotten Deities: Amaunator

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 7–9

The first of eighteen articles on lesser-known gods that hadn't yet received a full write-up in other supplements.

The Living City: The Horse Businesses

By Terence Kemper, illustrated by Chris Deegan, Cartography by Brian Blume. p. 10–16

A description of the services to be found in Anvil Alley in Raven's Bluff and some adventure hooks relating to them.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Treasure Tour of Turmish

By Ed Greenwood. p. 23–25

Descriptions of known, lost, suspected and well-guarded treasure troves of Alaghôn in Turmish, plus the most detailed map of the land available.
