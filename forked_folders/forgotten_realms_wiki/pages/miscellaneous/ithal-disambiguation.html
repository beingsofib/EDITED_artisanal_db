Ithal (disambiguation)

Ithal was a Tethyrian surname for members of Clan Ithal, which produced the first dynasty of Tethyr. It could also refer to:

  • Ithal Bridge, which crossed the River Ith,
  • Ithal Crag, a hill upon which the royal palace of Faerntarn was built,
  • Ithal Road, a road crossing Tethyr form east to west, or
  • Ithal Pass, a mountain pass in Calimshan.

Other locations derived from the name Ithal include:

  • the River Ith, a major river in Tethyr,
  • Ithalyr, the capital of the Ithal dynasty,
  • Ithmong, a later capital of Tethyr, and
  • Ithmonn, a county in Tethyr.
