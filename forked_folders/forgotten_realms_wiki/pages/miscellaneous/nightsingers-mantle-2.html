Nightsinger's mantles were enchanted Sharran cloaks worn by the servants of the goddess, her shadar-kai, and rogues of Returned Netheril in the 15 century DR.

Powers

A Nightsinger's mantle was woven with the necromantic magics of the Shadow Weave and embodied the song of Shar, giving the wearer's sonic spells the ability to blind victims and greatly improving their skill in performing and entertainment. Additionally, the wearer was made immune to being blinded themselves.

History

In the 15 century DR, Nightsinger's mantles were worn by the agents of Returned Netheril in their attempts to conquer the town of Wheloon in Cormyr.
