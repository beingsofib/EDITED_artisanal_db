Polyhedron 144

Polyhedron Newszine #144 was released in October 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Secrets of The City of Ravens

By Richard Baker. p. 9–11

A new tome, the Sarkonagael with new spells for Dungeons & Dragons 3rd Ed. From The City of Ravens novel.

  • Shadow bolt
  • Shadow simulacrum
  • Shadow sight

Forgotten Realms 2000 Survival Kit

By Jim Butler, illustrated by Matt Wilson. p. 12–17

A sneak peek at the changes in the Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting in Dungeons & Dragons 3rd Ed., including racial differences, multiclassing, deities, feats, magic items, and spells.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Wayfarers Guide to the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 18–19

Our tour of the Forgotten Realms this month comes to Oparl, capital of Bedorn.

Ecology of a Dungeon

By Matthew Hancock, illustrated by Vince Locke. p. 32–35

Guidelines for creating functional and rational layouts for your dungeons.

The Palace of Passion: Ravens Bluff's Temple of Sune

By Keith Hoffman, illustrated by Michael Kaluta, Cartography by Dennis Kauth. p. 40–45

Welcome to the grand opening of the new Temple of Sune, Goddess of Beauty, Love, and Passion. A greater work of art won't be found in all of Ravens Bluff.
