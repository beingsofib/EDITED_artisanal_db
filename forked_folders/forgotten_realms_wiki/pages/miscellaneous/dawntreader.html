Dawntreader

Dawntreader could refer to:

  • Gareth Dawntreader, one of the founders of the Order of the Burning Dawn
  • Roth Dawntreader, another founder of the Order of the Burning Dawn
  • Dawntreader Gap, a pass through the Greypeak Mountains
