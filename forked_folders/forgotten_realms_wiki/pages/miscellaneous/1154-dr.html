1154 DR

__NOTOC__

  • In Chessenta, the armies of Akanax and Soorenar lay siege to Cimbar. Unable to receive aid from allies, the ruler of Cimbar is forced to sign a pact that severely restricts his power. However, Akanax refuses to sign, instead declaring its own lord as the true king of Chessenta.
  • The Tokiryo War is fought in Wa, leading to the rise of the Matasuuri clan.
  • A mind flayer cult establishes the city of Ch'Chitl, intending that it be an adjunct to Skullport
  • Roadaeron Lorndessar II, a future King of Westgate, is born.
