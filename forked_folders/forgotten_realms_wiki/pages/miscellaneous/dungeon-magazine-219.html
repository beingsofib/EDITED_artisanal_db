Dungeon magazine 219

Dungeon issue 219 was released in October of 2013 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Star-Touched Grove

By Tyler Gelvin, illustrated by Christopher Burdett, Cartography by Jared Blando. p. 25-29

Visit a druid’s grove corrupted by the Far Realm. Module for 2-6 PC's of levels 8-9.
