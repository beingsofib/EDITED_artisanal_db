-189 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Tashalar is completely conquered by the lizardfolk and yuan-ti forces of Serpentes. The majority of wealthy Calishites there flee back to Calimshan.
  • Shyllisyrr Ithal, elven wife of Clan Ithal leader Corin is killed by a snake bite, triggering a diplomatic scandal that would last for years between the Tethyrian clans and the tribes of the Forest of Tethir.
