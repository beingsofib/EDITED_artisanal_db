Kingpin

Kingpin was a title used within the Baldurian criminal organization known as the Guild. It was held by the crime bosses that oversaw a gang occupying a specified region, typically a single district within the Lower or Outer City.

They all operated under their guildmaster, competing with one other for increased wealth and influence.

Notable Holders

Upper City

  • The Fetcher, who dwelled in the Undercellar.
  • Whiskey Lady, of Manorborn.

Lower City

  • Goblin Behnie, of Bloomridge.
  • Genamine Kopali, of Seatower.
  • Rakkath Glitterbeard, of the Steeps.

Outer City

  • Rilsa Rael, of Little Calimshan.
  • Straightstick, of Twin Songs.
