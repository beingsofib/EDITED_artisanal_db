Dungeon magazine 90

Dungeon issue 90 was released in January/February of 2002. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Critical Threats

By Dungeon Staff, illustrated by Jason Engle. p. 52

New Feature! This lycanthropic cleric savors the taste of infidels. A D&D Forgotten Realms Critical Threat.

Tears for Twilight Hollow

By Angel Leigh McCoy & Christopher Perkins, illustrated by Brom & Chuck Lukacs. p. 62-110

A paladin's demise brings pain and despair to the village of Twilight Hollow. A D&D Forgotten Realms adventure for 7th-level PCs but adaptable for levels 3-12.
