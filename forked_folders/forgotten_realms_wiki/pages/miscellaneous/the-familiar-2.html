The Familiar 2

The Familiar #1 was published by video game developer Beamdog on March 12, 2016. The digital magazine contains several articles intended to promote Beamdog's Dungeons & Dragons products, as well as a licensed short story tying in with Baldur's Gate: Siege of Dragonspear.

Dangerous World
