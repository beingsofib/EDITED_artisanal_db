Astrology

Astrology was the field of observing the movements of stars and constellations to predict events.

In any decent-sized city, a sage specialized in astrology could be found with a 30% chance, around 1358 DR.

Notable Astrologers

  • Galileus: The 14 century astrologer from the Sword Coast.
