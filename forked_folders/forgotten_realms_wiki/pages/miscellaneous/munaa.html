Munaa

"Munaa" was a common Alzhedo given name for females. Individuals named "Munaa" include:

  • Munaa yr Shunnari el Tarshaj, the second wife of Akkabar Shoon the Elder
  • Munaa yr Oma el Shoon, who would become Shoon V
  • Munaa yr Oma el Jhotos, a vizar of Almraiven
