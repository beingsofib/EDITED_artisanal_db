Halcyon

Halcyon may refer to:

  • Halcyons, specialty priests devoted to Aerdrie Faenya, the Winged Mother,
  • Halcyon (spear), an enchanted electrum-tipped polearm from Amn,
  • Halcyon (sword), an enchanted sword wielded by Piergeiron Paladinson of Waterdeep,
  • Halcyon (ship), a naval vessel from the Dragon Reach.
  • Halcyon, the command word used by the hero Shal Bal in the 14 century DR.
