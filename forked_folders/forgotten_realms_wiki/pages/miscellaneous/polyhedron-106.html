Polyhedron 106

Polyhedron Newszine #106 was released in April 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Notes from HQ: Another Winter Fantasy

By Kevin Melka. p. 2

The latest in RPGA news.

Forgotten Deities: Ibrandul

By Eric L. Boyd, illustrated by Phillip Robb. p. 4

Ibrandul, “God of the Dry Depths,” is one of the greatest reasons to be afraid of the Underdark.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: Endless Treasures in Turmish

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Michael Hearrold. p. 6–8

The treasures of Turmish grow ever greater... and ever more dangerous.
