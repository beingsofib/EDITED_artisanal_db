The Mouse Smiled Brightly

"The Mouse Smiled Brightly" was a Cormyrean lullaby.

History

In the , the short poem was recited by the devil Raphael to victims of a nautiloid crash who had been infected with illithid tadpoles. When asked, Raphael said that he was neither the cat nor the mouse in the lullaby; he was the fox.
