Dungeon magazine 37

Dungeon issue 37 was released in September/October of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

A Wizard's Fate

By Christopher Perkins, illustrated by Bob Waiters, Cartography by Diesel. p. 20-28

It took only the love of one good woman -- and the hatred of one evil familiar. An adventure situated near Secomber, designed for 3-6 player characters of levels 1-3.
