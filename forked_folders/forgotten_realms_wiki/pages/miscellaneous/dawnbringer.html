Dawnbringer

Dawnbringer could refer to:

  • Dawnbringer (title), this title was given to higher-ranked clergy of Lathander
  • Dawnbringer (novel), the standalone novel by Samantha Henderson;
  • Dawnbringer (sword), the sentient magical longsword;
  • Dawnbringer Company, a band of adventurers from Waterdeep
