Goblin Wars (disambiguation)

Goblin War or Goblin Wars could refer to:

  • The perpetual goblin wars that led to the fall of the dwarf kingdom of Xonathanur;
  • The Goblin Wars that led to the destruction of the city of Tor Mak;
  • The Goblin Wars (200 DR) fought in Cormyr between the kingdom's defenders and the goblinkin of Hlundadim around 200 DR;
  • The Goblin Wars fought in the North between the Uthgardt, the Covenant, and the goblins of the Valley of Khedrun in 753 DR;
  • The Goblin Wars (1358 DR) fought in Erlkazar between the kingdom's defenders, the Shieldbreakers ogres, and the Starrock goblin tribe in 1358 DR; or
  • The Goblin War fought in Cormyr between the kingdom's defenders and a force of Grodd goblins, orcs, and ghazneths under the dragon Nalavarauthatoryl over 1370 to 1371 DR.
