Polyhedron 97

Polyhedron Newszine #97 was released in July 1997. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Champion of the Games

By Harold Johnson. p. 4

Charles Oliver O'Kane has ruled Ravens Bluff for three decades. What makes him so secure in this previously ever-changing position?

Notes From HQ: Connecticut, Chicago, and Ravens Bluff

By Jean Rabe. p. 5

More conventions, from Danbury to Chicago.

The Living City: The Hand of Mercy and Marigold's Menagerie

By Nicky Rea, illustrated by Larry Smith, Cartography by Brian Blume. p. 6–12

In Ravens Bluff, the Hand of Mercy Children's Hospital and Orphanage and Marigold's Menagerie bear a striking resemblance to two of the Network's favorite causes.

Your Tax Dollars at Work

By Rob Nicholls. p. 18–25

Special Advisory Council Guards don't get no respect in this Living City adventure. An AD&D 2nd Edition Adventure for 1st-3rd Level PCs.

The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited. p. 32

Vol. 1, Issue 2 of a newspaper for the RPGA Network's Living City shared world.
