-206 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Corin Ithal becomes chieftain of Clan Ithal.
  • The last Coronal of Illefarn, Syglaeth Audark, is killed by drow raiders from Jhachalkhyn, a drow city below the Neverwinter Woods.
  • Venominhandar's mate is finally slain by Jassin Aunglor, a moon elf warrior, who caves in the dragon's lair on top of both of them.
  • Tethyr's first ever crown prince, Darrom Ithal II, older brother to Silvam Ithal dies of a fever.
