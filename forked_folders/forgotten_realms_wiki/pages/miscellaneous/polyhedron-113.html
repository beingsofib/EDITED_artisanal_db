Polyhedron 113

Polyhedron Newszine #113 was released in November 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Forgotten Deities: Shiallia

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 4

Shiallia, goddess of the High Forest.

Jungle Tales - Servant of Fire Mountain

By Tom Prusa, illustrated by Valerie Valusek. p. 5

Tales and legends for the Living Jungle campaign.

Raft Dwellers: The Zantiri Tribe

By Sherrie Miller and John Richardson. p. 6–7

A 1995 Decathlon winner and new tribe in the Living Jungle.

The Hungry Spirit of Fire Mountain

By Ed Gibson. p. 8–9

Huroola placate an awesome god in our Ruins of the Ancients contest winner.

The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited. p. 32

Red Wizards Kidnap Lord Mayor, Giants and Ogres on the Rise, Adamantite Shortage Worries Smiths, and House of Critters Fate to be Decided.
