Kamal (disambiguation)

A kamal was a nautical tool used by navigators. It was also a common name in Alzhedo. Individuals named Kamal included:

  • Kamal adh Nadjar, one of the Red Rysals, and
  • Kamal el Kitaeb, a Harper druzir in Calimport.
