Wail of the banshee was a necromancy spell that killed several creatures.

Effects

The caster made a terrifying scream that could slay a living creature that heard it, apart from the caster and those who had the fortitude to withstand it. The more powerful the caster, the more creatures in close range that could be effected by the wail.
