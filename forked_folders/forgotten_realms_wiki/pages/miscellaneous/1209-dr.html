1209 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Fylraun, the fourth son of King Imphras II is born, but Rebaera Osterhown, the king's second wife, dies in childbirth.
  • Eleint 11: The plundering of Urdogen's pirates is curtailed after the Battle of the Fallen, a massive confrontation outside the Dragonisle by the combined forces of Sembia, Impiltur, Aglarond, and Cormyr. Prince Lashilmbrar led the Impilturan forces in the attack.
  • Bandits, supported by supernatural creatures (perhaps tengu), slaughter a band of pilgrims on the way to Aru in Wa. The daimyo of Aru Province, Arata Matsutomo, receives a strong reprimand from shogun Eiko.
  • The prospects of a united Inner Sea nation are doomed when Cormyr and Sembia begin bickering.
