Dungeon magazine 48

Dungeon issue 48 was released in July/August of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Oracle at Sumbar

By Paul Culotta, illustrated by David Kooharian, Cartography by Diesel. p. 16-30

Cross the Sea of Fallen Stars to see old debts repaid. An adventure for 4–8 good or neutral players of levels 4–6.

Honor Lost, Honor Regained

By Paul Hamilton Beattie Jr., illustrated by Tony DiTerlizzi, Cartography by Diesel. p. 66-70

"Come into my parlor" said the spider to the paladin. An adventure for a party of 3–5 characters of levels 4–6 (18–20 total levels). The adventure setting is in the frontiers of Cormyr. The main NPC is a paladin of Helm, so no player characters should be overtly evil.
