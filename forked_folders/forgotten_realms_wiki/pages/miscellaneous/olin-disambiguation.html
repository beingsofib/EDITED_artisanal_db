Olin (disambiguation)

Olin was a name used throughout the Realms. Individuals named Olin include:

  • Olin, the retired adventurer who lived in Daggerford; or
  • Olin (black firedrake), the drake guard from Innarlith.

It might also refer to:

  • Olin Gisir, an individual who acted as a keeper of ancient elven lore.
