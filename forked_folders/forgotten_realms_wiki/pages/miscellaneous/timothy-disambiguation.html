Timothy (disambiguation)

"Tim" or "Timothy" was a common name in the Realms, and was used by:

  • Tim (Eagle Peak), a human resident of Eagle Peak,
  • Tim (thief), a halfling thief trapped in the Black Pits,
  • Timothy, a gnoll enslaved by the Absolute,
  • Tim Butterworth, a human resident of Ghars, or
  • Tim Goldenhand, an elven adventurer.

It could also refer to:

  • Timothy (plant), a common grain crop plant.

Real people named as such included:

  • Timothy Bailey, a writer for Living Forgotten Realms,
  • Tim Beach, an adventure writer,
  • Timothy B. Brown, a TSR, Inc. game designer and editor,
  • Tim Eagon, a writer for Dragon and Dungeon magazines,
  • Tim Hitchcock, a Dragon magazine writer,
  • Timothy J. Kask, a designer and Dragon magazine editor,
  • Tim Lasko, a Dragon magazine writer,
  • Timothy Lethbridge, a Dragon magazine writer,
  • Tim Malto, a Dragon magazine writer,
  • Timothy T. Murphy, a Dragon magazine writer,
  • Tim Pratt, an author,
  • Tim Richardson, a Dragon magazine writer,
  • Tim Seeley, an artist, and
  • Timothy Truman, an artist.
