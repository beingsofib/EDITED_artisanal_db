Polyhedron 131

Polyhedron Newszine #131 was released in August 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Pillars of the Realm

By Brian Miller. p. 11

A look at one of the religious orders of knighthood in Ravens Bluff, the "Pillars of Virtue and Civic Responsibility."

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The Border Kingdoms

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Steve Bryant. p. 12–13

The conclusion of our visit to the Land of Two Princes in Faerûn.
