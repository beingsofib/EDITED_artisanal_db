1218 DR

  • Elliira Obarskyr is born to Dhalmass and Jhalass Huntsilver.
  • King Coram IV of Tethyr is stabbed to death in his sleep, as plotted by his half-brother Kymer.
  • A red dragon is killed by the Red Wizard Maligor, an achievement he would go on to reminisce about later in life.
  • Kymer becomes king of Tethyr.
  • The bard Sephica of Ordulin is beatified as a saint of Ilmater.
