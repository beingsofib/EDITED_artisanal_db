Polyhedron 47

Polyhedron Newszine #47 was released in May 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Burnhart's Outfitting

By Rollin Ehlenfeldt. p. 28–29

Burnhart's Outfitting in Raven's Bluff stocks just what the well-traveled adventurer needs. But watch out for the proprietors.
