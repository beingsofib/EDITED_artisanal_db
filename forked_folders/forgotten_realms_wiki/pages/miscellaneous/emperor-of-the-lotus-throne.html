Emperor of the Lotus Throne

The Emperor of the Lotus Throne, also called the Lotus Emperor, was the traditional head of the Lotus Court and ruler of the Yikarian Empire.

Activities

The Emperor not only ruled the vast Empire, but also served as the High Priest of the Faceless God—the only deity worshiped by the yak-folk. The Emperor himself was even sacrificed to his god after 20 years of serving as the Emperor. Traditionally, a younger yak-man was chosen by the Faceless God for this position.
