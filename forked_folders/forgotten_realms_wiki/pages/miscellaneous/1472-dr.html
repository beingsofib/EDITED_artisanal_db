1472 DR

  • Elisa Nettlebee is born to Bertram and Jayne Nettlebee.
  • Elnantra, Paerevelle, and Ralagondar are born to Skie Silvershield II.
  • Erlend Brightcovenant is born to Amauntha Bryndfeather and Breszyn Brightcovenant.
  • Alorae Wanderwyrm is killed by an unknown assailant in Suzail with poison. Witnesses note her last gasping words to be "I die, but fail forever!", though later war wizards speculate if she may have actually said "Fael", referring to the Fael Threl cabal.
  • King Emerus Warcrown witnesses an angry Reginald Roundshield in a practice battle and promptly assigns him to Iruladoon's town guard in order to train him and teach him humility.
  • Brunzarra Hethcanter retires from the office of Lady of Graces in Cormyr's Royal Court. That same year she is succeeded by Lady Jalessa Windstone.
  • In Delthuntle, the halfling crime lord Grandfather Pericolo Topolino takes an interest in the young boy Regis after seeing him climb a building to escape a gang of much older and larger youths. Dubbing him the "Spider", he goes on to manipulate his father to allow the boy to work for him diving for deep oysters, which Pericolo then cultivates to produce pink pearls. Soon after, Spider is unofficially adopted by Pericolo and trained in the ways of the rogue.
