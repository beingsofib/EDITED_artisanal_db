Polyhedron 62

Polyhedron Newszine #62 was released in August 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bookwyrms - Spelljammers: The Cloakmaster Cycle

By Marlys Heeszel. p. 2

The Spelljammer campaign setting has moved from modules to novels: Beyond the Moons and Into the Void.

The Jade Monkey

By Carl Buehler, illustrated by Stephen Schwartz, Cartography by Dennis Kauth. p. 10–15

A visit to a Ravens Bluff wizard proves hilarious and dangerous.

The Living City: As The Vine Twines

By Gregory Failing. p. 16–17

As The Vine Twines, Ravens Bluff's choice vineyard and winery, caters to nobility and adventurers - who are old enough to drink.

The New Rogues Gallery: The Freelancers

By Sascha Isaac Segan. p. 20-21

The Freelancers, a brother-sister adventuring team, can provide just the right amount of muscle to help out the needy.
