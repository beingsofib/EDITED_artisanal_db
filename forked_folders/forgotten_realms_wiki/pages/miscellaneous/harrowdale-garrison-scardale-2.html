The Harrowdale garrison was a fortification in Scardale Town established by the Seven Burghers of Harrowdale during the occupation of Scardale in the mid–14 century DR.

After the second inter-dale war, the government of Mistledale originally sent a detachment of Grey Riders but soon recalled them in lieu of a different martial force. The riders were replaced with members of the large, Sembian adventuring company called the Sword of Yhaunn. Over time, these steadfast adventurers were supplemented by mercenaries that had been financed by Sembia outright. By 1367 DR, the garrison had become an open Sembian camp and the Harrowdale ambassador had to plead to the Seven Burghers for new, loyal soldiers.
