666 DR

  • The Mourning Days in Myth Drannor end with a Claiming Ceremony to choose the next coronal that will Cormanthyr. Chaos ensues, with many elves, humans and other people of Myth Drannor slain and the Rule Tower destroyed. Finally, the Srinshee claims the Crownblade and restores the Rule Tower, but vanishes with the elfblade.

Novels

  • Final Gate prologue.
