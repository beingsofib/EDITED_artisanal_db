Buster Bracer was one of the finer armorsmiths of Clan Battlehammer in Mithral Hall in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Buster had a blue beard.

History

While living in Icewind Dale, Buster crafted Drizzt's mithral chainmail. In the , Buster worked on several pieces of armor for the Companions of the Hall, including Regis. Around 1358 DR, Buster mended the figurine of Guenhwyvar after Catti-brie cut it into two pieces with her sword, Khazid'hea.
