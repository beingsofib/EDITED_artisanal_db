Dungeon Master's Guide (disambiguation)

Dungeon Master's Guide might refer to:

  • Dungeon Master's Guide 5th edition (revised) (2024), the revised core rulebook for the 5th edition of Dungeons & Dragons.
  • Dungeon Master's Guide 5th edition (2014), the core rulebook for the 5th edition of Dungeons & Dragons.
  • Dungeon Master's Guide 4th edition (2008), the core rulebook for the 4th edition of Dungeons & Dragons.
  • Dungeon Master's Guide 3.5 edition (2003), the core rulebook for the 3.5 edition of Dungeons & Dragons.
  • Dungeon Master's Guide 3rd edition (2000), the core rulebook for the 3rd edition of Dungeons & Dragons.
  • Dungeon Master Guide 2nd edition (revised) (1995), the revised core rulebook for the 2nd edition of Advanced Dungeons & Dragons. Note that this book's title contains "Master" rather than "Master's".
  • Dungeon Master's Guide 2nd edition (1989), the core rulebook for the 2nd edition of Advanced Dungeons & Dragons. This book is also sometimes known without the apostrophe in the title. There is a "Master" and a "Master's" version of this book.
  • Dungeon Master's Guide 1st edition (1979), the core rulebook for the 1st edition of Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.
