Dungeon magazine 67

Dungeon issue 67 was released in March/April of 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Witches' Brew

By Steven F. Johnson, illustrated by George Vrbanic, Cartography by Diesel. p. 8-27

Black cats, witches, ghosts and goblins. Module for 6-8 PC's of levels 3-5.

Training Ground

By Rick Maffei, illustrated by Rags Morales, Cartography by Diesel. p. 30-49

Enter the dungeon of the Netherese mage. Module for 3-6 PC's of levels 5-8.
