Firedrake (disambiguation)

A fire drake was a type of drake. The term "Firedrake" might also refer to:

  • Firedrake (county), a county of Tethyr
  • Firedrake Bay, the bay where Port Kir was located
  • Firedrake (wine), a Cormyrean wine
  • Firedrake (dragonet), a dragonet resembling a red dragon
