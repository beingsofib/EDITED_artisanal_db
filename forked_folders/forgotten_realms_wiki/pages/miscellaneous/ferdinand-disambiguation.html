Ferdinand (disambiguation)

The name Ferdinand could refer to:

  • Ferdinand, the czar of Bolgor in the Whamite Isles,
  • Ferdinand, a ju-ju zombie servant of Madame Narcosa,
  • Ferdinand, a young helper of Georborg Fireblade of Mintar,
  • Ferdinand, a thief and a moneychanger from Netheril,
  • Ferdinand, a beast keeper who served the Adventurer's Academy of Neverwinter.
