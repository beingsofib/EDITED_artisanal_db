Duncan (disambiguation)

"Duncan" was a given name in several languages of Faerûn. Individuals named Duncan included:

  • Duncan, a merchant who visited Cormyr in the 1370s DR,
  • Duncan (sage), a sage in Arabel,
  • Duncan Farlong, a wood elf of Neverwinter, and
  • Duncan el Ashnarti, the sultan of Keltar.
