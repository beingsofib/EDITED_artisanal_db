Blue Feather

Blue Feather can refer to:

  • Blue Feather (lizardfolk), a tribe in the Lizard Marsh.
  • Blue Feather (orcs), a tribe in the High Moor.
  • Agents of a Pan yakuza family who bore a small blue feather tattoo.
