Feather Falls

Feather Falls could refer to:

  • Feather Falls (village), the settlement used for mooring in Featherdale;
  • Feather Falls (waterfall), a waterfall in the same region;

It could also be confused with:

  • Feather fall, the arcane spell that caused a person or object to fall slowly.
