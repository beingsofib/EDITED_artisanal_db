1450 DR

  • Auril sends her vassals to battle the djinn and efreet of Calimshan. As a result, the desert of the Skyfire Wastes is frozen over for months.
  • The Second Era of Skyfire comes to a close in Calimshan as the efreeti Memnon and the djinni Calim mysteriously vanish, presumably to their home plane. However, their followers continue their conflict.
  • Vasen Cale is born to Varra, who dies in childbirth.

Novels

  • The Godborn begins.
