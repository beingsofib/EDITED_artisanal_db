Myrmidon (disambiguation)

"Myrmidon" can refer to:

  • Elemental myrmidon, a member of an elite group of usually chaotic evil elementals,
  • Myrmidon, a kit for the warrior class in 2nd edition.
  • Myrmidon, a species of crustacean in the Sea of Moving Ice.
  • Myrmidon, one of the ranks a Landless Knight could receive in the Warlock Knights of Vaasa.
