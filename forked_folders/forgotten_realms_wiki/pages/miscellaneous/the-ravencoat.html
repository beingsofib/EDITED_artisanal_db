The Ravencoat

The Ravencoat was a local office of the Heralds of Faerûn, based in the city of Ravens Bluff in the Vast and performing Herald duties there. This heraldic office was folded into the civic official position of the Regent of City Works.

As a herald, The Ravencoat was entitled to a maroon-and-gold tabard adorned with black wings, as well as a trumpet.

Known Ravencoats

  • Bundrigo Dalastarra (–1370 DR)
  • Lastraeya Indomurr (1370–)
