Frostbite (disambiguation)

Frostbite may refer to:

  • Frostbite (condition): A skin injury caused by exposure to extreme cold temperatures.
  • Frostbite (spell): an evocation spell used by the priests of Auril.
  • Frostbite (transmutation spell): a transmutation spell used by the druids of Chauntea.
  • Frostbite (white wyrm): a white dragon found in the Spine of the World.
  • Frostbite (winter wolf): a winter wolf working with the Church of Cyric.
  • Frostbite (sword): an enchanted short sword.
