Divine Seekers were those individuals who discreetly served a church dedicated to one of the various Faerûnian pantheons.

Activities

Divine seekers were called upon to perform secretive missions for their church, such as spying upon others or recovering items they considered sacred, without leaving a trace of their actions. Quite often, they performed deeds that overt agents or church armies could not.

Abilities

These agents of faith were trained specifically in the ways stealth, of thwarting magical traps.

To carry out their duties, divine seekers could readily seek out pathways, creatures or objects, and even obscure the latter if needed.
