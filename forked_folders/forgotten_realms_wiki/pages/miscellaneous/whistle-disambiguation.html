Whistle (disambiguation)

Whistle may refer to:

  • Whistle, a simple evocation spell.
  • Signal whistles, wind instruments generating a high-pitched sound.
  • Tin whistles, another name for a flute.
  • Whistlecanes, simple wind instruments cut from a reed.
  • Whistlers, halfling bards producing magical effects through whistling.
