Kinslayer (disambiguation)

Kinslayer may refer to:

  • Kinslayer, an epic from the ancient kingdom of Nog in Zakhara;
  • Kinslayer (prestige class), a drow dedicated to killing elves; or
  • Kinslayer (trident), a trident enchanted to harm the merfolk of Serôs.
