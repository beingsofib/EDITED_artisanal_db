Polyhedron 37

Polyhedron Newszine #37 was released in September 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Open Scalery

By Rollin Ehlenfeldt. p. 11–12

We visit the Open Scalery this month, a fish market in Ravens Bluff with two unusual owners.
