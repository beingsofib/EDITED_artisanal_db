Polyhedron 142

Polyhedron Newszine #142 was released in June 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Table Talk: Legendary Weapons

p. 6–7

Presenting the first and second place winners of the RPGA's Clubs Decathlon's first event, "Best Legendary Weapon": the Makah whaling spear of the Sixth World and the tools of the common man of Toril.

Putting Life Back Into The Undead

By Matthew Hancock. p. 15–17

Making encounters with undead creatures more frightening for your players.

Men of the Basilisk

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 20–27

A detailed examination of a secret society of merchants and titled nobility seeking to enrich their coffers by engaging in illicit and corrupt activities.
