Aggravate dracorage

Spell
nameAggravate Dracorage
inventorSammaster
school3eEnchantment (Compulsion)
level3eCleric 4 Sorcerer 4 Wizard 4

Aggravate dracorage was a spell that triggered the effects of the Dracorage mythal on a single target.

Effects

This spell countered, and was countered by, abate Dracorage.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.

History

Aggravate dracorage was created by the lich Sammaster.
