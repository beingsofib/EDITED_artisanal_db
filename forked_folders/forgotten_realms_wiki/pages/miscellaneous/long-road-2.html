Long Road

The Long Road was the inland trade route stretching from Waterdeep in the south to Mirabar in the north.

Geographical features

Rivers and streams

  • River Mirar

Travel Times or Distances

  • Waterdeep to Amphail, Three days ride
  • Waterdeep to Red Larch, Seven days ride

Notable locations

Inns and taverns

  • Harvest Inn
  • Swinging Sword Inn

Settlements

The primary towns on this road, from north to south, were:

  • Mirabar
  • Xantharl's Keep
  • Longsaddle
  • Triboar—where the Long Road and Evermoor Way meet.
  • Westbridge
  • Red Larch
  • Amphail
  • Rassalantar
  • Waterdeep
