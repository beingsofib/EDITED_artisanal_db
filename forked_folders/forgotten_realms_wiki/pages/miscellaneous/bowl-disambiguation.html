Bowl (disambiguation)

Bowl may refer to:

Culinary uses:

  • Berry bowl, a dessert dish;
  • Bowl, the everyday piece of crockery;
  • Bowl of hares, a meat stew dish.

Locations named after bowls:

  • The Bowl, an area of deep, open water;
  • Bowl of Loneliness, a region of Anauroch;
  • Golden Bowl of the Goddess, a temple of Chauntea.

Magical terms:

  • Blaenther's bowls, a spell that created tiny portals;
  • Bowl of blood, a magical blood-generating dish;
  • Bowl of commanding water elementals, an elemental device.
