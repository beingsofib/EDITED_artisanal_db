Willow (disambiguation)

Willow could refer to:

  • Willow, a type of mundane tree found throughout Faerûn,
  • Willow, a barmaid from the Beached Leviathan of Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR,
  • Willow, a child obsessed with fireworks from Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR,
  • Willow Brownbug, an hin apothecary druid from Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR,
  • Willow Tree, the nickname given to Sharlotta Vespers of Calimport.

It could also refer to:

  • Willowman Trading Post, a general store found in Harrowdale in the 14 century DR,
  • Black willow, a species of carnivorous trees,
  • Carston Willows, the Regent of the Guilds of Ravens Bluff in the mid-14 century DR,
  • Drill of the Slender Willow, a gnomish drill from the city of Silverymoon,
  • Greenwillow, a bodyguard from the ancient Netheril,
  • Street of the Golden Willows, a street in the ancient Netheril,
  • White Willow Monastery.
