Magda (disambiguation)

Magda is a name that could refer to:

  • Magda, the proprietor of the Veil Theater in Mulsantir,
  • Magda, the wife of Fendrake from the town of Ghars in Cormyr,
  • Magda, a cleric of Laduguer and slave of Baeloth Barrityl,
  • Magda, a wooden toy seller, or
  • Magda, an inhabitant of Netheril circa -339 DR.
