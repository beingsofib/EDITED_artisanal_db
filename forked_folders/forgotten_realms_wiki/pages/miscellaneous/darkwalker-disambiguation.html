Darkwalker (disambiguation)

Darkwalker may refer to:

  • Kazgoroth, a monster from the Moonshae Isles and the adversary in the Darkwalker War, as described in the events of the novel, Darkwalker on Moonshae;
  • Darkwalkers, the specialty priests of Ibrandul; or
  • Darkwalkers, powerful warlocks with a dark pact.
