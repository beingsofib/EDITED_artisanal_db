-417 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Lacking an heir, the ruling Kasada dynasty of Wa comes to an end. The regency finds a relative among the Goshukara family and crowns the first emperor of the Goshukara.
  • The Wa calendar commences.
  • Mornarth Talanthil I, who would later become the seventh Sorcerer–Arkhan of the Talanthil Dynasty in Raumathar, is born.
