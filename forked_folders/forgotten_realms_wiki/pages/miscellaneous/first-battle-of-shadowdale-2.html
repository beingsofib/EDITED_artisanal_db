The First Battle of Shadowdale was a battle fought in Shadowdale, in the Dalelands, in 1357 DR, between the defending forces of the Dalelands, and forces of Lyran Nanther.

One of the combatants in this battle was Lanseril Snowmantle, who played an instrumental role in the defeat of Zhentil Keep's reinforcements by casting the spell insect plague.
