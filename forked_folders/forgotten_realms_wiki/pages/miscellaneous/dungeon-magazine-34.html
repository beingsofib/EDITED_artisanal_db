Dungeon magazine 34

Dungeon issue 34 was released in March/April of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Euphoria Horrors

By Alan Grimes, illustrated by Steve Schwartz, Cartography by Diesel. p. 8-20

Can you find a little boy's missing friend? The description he gives you is very strange. Module for 4-6 PC's of levels 1-2.

On Wings of Darkness

By Craig Barrett, illustrated by Ken Widing, Cartography by Diesel. p. 54-71

Summer in Calimshan brings a "simple" monster hunt in the cool mountain air. What could be easier? Module for 4-6 PC's of levels 5-8. Recommended PC mix is two to four warriors, a wizard, and a cleric or druid.
