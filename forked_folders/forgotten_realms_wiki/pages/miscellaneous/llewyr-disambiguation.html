Llewyr (disambiguation)

Llewyr could refer to:

  • An alternate spelling of Llewyrr, the name of the high elves of the Moonshae Isles;
  • Llewyr, an ancient colony of Illefarn based in the Llewyrrwood (modern day Neverwinter Wood) and from whence the ancestors of the Llewyrr elves emigrated; or
  • Synnoria, the hidden kingdom of the Llewyrr on the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles.
