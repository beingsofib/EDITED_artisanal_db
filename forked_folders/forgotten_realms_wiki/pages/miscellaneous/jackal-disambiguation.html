Jackal (disambiguation)

A jackal was a desert canine. The term could also refer to:

  • A jackal, a wizard from Zakhara with the ability to steal spells, or
  • A member of the Jackals, also known as the Sultan's Own, an organization of salamanders in the City of Brass.
