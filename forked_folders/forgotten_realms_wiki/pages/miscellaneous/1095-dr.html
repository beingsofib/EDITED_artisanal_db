1095 DR

  • Imphras Heltharn, the War-Captain of Lyrabar, leads an alliance of the city-states of the Easting Reach against a hobgoblin horde emerging from the the Giantspire Mountains.
  • The realm of Impiltur is founded by Imphras Heltharn, uniting the independent city-states of Lyrabar, Hlammach, Dilpur, and Sarshel.
  • Edwin Carrathal becomes King of Callidyrr following the death of his father.
  • King Bertrand Carrathal of Callidyrr dies of natural causes.
