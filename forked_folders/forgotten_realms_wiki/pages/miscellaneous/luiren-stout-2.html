Luiren Stout

Item
nameLuiren Stout
typeAle
creatorSholapur
locationLuiren, Shining South

Luiren Stout was a dark, thick ale produced by the Sholapur family of Luiren in the 14 century DR. It was popular all throughout the Shining South region, even in the Underdark city of Underhome.

Avilability

This ale was produced in Sholapur breweries,

Luiren Stout could also be found in Dambrath, brought over from the Luiren trading hub Ammathluir by caravans that traveled the Great Trade Way.
