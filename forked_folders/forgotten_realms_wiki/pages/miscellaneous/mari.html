Mari

Mari was a female given name. Individuals named Mari included:

  • Mari Agneh, a tharchion of Priador
  • Mari Al'maren, a Harper bard
  • Mari bint Sadita, a Zakharan merchant
  • Mari, a looter of West Faerûn

Mari was also the name of an ethnic group living in the Utter East and the language they spoke.
