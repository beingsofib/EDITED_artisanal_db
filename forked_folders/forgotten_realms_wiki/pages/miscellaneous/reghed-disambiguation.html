Reghed (disambiguation)

Reghed might refer to:

  • Reghed Glacier, a frozen wasteland in northern Faerûn.
  • The Reghedmen, a collection of barbarian tribes that inhabited the Reghed Glacier.
  • Reghedjic, the language of the Reghedmen.
