Hamarch

The Hamarch was the elected mayor of the city of Urmlaspyr in Sembia.

Activities

The position of Hamarch was elected by influential merchants in Urmlaspyr. Once elected, a Hamarch would serve for life.

Known Nessarchs

  • Aluin Sumbrar was Hamarch as of 1358 DR.
