-1900 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Beholders from around the Lake of Steam area continually assault the Caltazar Hills.
  • The confusion spell is invented by Netherese arcanist Keonid with the name Keonid's confusion.
  • Citadel Felbarr, part of Delzoun, is completed.
  • Calimshan's rule extends as far as the Snowflake Mountains. It comprises four subkingdoms: Calimshan itself, as well as Iltkazar, Mir, and Tethyr. This marks the beginning of the Third Age of Calimshan.
