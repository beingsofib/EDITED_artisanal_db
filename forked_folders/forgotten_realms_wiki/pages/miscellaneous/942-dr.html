942 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Amaara "Goldentresses" Nharimlur, former High Mage of Silverymoon, dies on the island of Evermeet.
  • The Eleventh Serôs War comes to an end with the fall of the sea elf kingdom of Keryvyr, but Nyratiis, the would-be-coronal who triggered the war, flees and evades capture. Keryvyr's allies, Naramyr and Selu Maraar, remain intact.
  • Drow raid cities all down the Sword Coast, carrying many human inhabitants of the Dessarin Valley off into slavery. The drow pillage and raze the tiny realms of Harpshield and Talmost beside the Ardeep Forest.
  • Emurra Scaradath, the young protégé of the Moonseer of Waterdeep, Engalathae, rouses the Selûnite clergy of the city into sending a rescue mission into the Underdark to rescue her family and the Moonseer who were captured by drow slavers.
