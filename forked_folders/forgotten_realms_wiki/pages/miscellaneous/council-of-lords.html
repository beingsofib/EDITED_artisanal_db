Council of Lords

The Council of Lords could refer to many different governing bodies in the Realms, including but not limited to:

  • The Council of Lords of Uthtower;
  • The Lords of Imphras II that governed Impiltur during the 14 century DR;
  • The Grand Council that governed Impiltur in the 15 century;
  • The Council of Lords (Melvaunt) that ruled the city-state of Melvaunt;
  • The Council of Lords (Ravens Bluff) that served as legislators for the city of Ravens Bluff;
  • The Council of Lords (Zhentil Keep) that ruled over Zhentil Keep prior to the ascension of Manshoon;
  • The City Council of Zazesspur;
  • The council of the Lords' Alliance.
