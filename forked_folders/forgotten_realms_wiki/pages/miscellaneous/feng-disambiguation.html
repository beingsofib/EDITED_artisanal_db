Feng (disambiguation)

Feng is the name of several individuals in the Realms:

  • Feng, a bouncer at the Beached Leviathan tavern in Neverwinter;
  • Feng Ironhead, the shopkeeper in Red Larch; or
  • Lady Feng, matriarch of the Ginger Palace on the Dragon Coast.
