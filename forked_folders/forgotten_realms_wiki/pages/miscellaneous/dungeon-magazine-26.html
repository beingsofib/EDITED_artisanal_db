Dungeon magazine 26

Dungeon issue 26 was released in November/December of 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Inheritance

By Paul Culotta, illustrated by Ken Widing, Cartography by Diesel. p. 6-14, 36

A member of the party has inherited a keep, which is occupied by a militant band of hobgoblins. Module for 6-8 PC's of levels 1-3.
