Justiciar

Justiciar might refer to several things related to the Tyrran faith:

  • Justiciar (sword), the sword wielded by Tyr himself,
  • Justiciar of Tyr, the elite servants of the Tyrran church, or
  • High Justiciar/Lord High Justiciar, two of the titles held by those individuals.

Additionally it may refer to:

  • Justiciar, a law-keeping position present in lands such as Cormyr and Tethyr.
  • The Dark Justiciars, an order of sharran priests
