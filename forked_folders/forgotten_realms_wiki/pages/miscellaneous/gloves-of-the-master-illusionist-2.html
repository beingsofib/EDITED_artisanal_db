The gloves of the master illusionist were a pair of magical gloves found in the mid-1480s DR.

Powers

The gloves improved the wearer's mental sharpness and specifically enhanced the power of their illusion spells.

History

The gloves of the master illusionist were taken from the Netherese arcanist Tharno after he was defeated inside of Rungnir's glacier, prior to the raising of Archwizard Amskar's flying enclave.
