Meat Cleaver was a enchanted battleaxe created to slay orcs and orogs with terrifying efficiency.

Description

Meat Cleaver was noticeably heavier than similar one-handed battle-axes.

Powers

Meat Cleaver had a +2 enchantment. Additionally, the axe granted the wielder a boon of strength and effectively had a powerful +4 enchantment when used against orcs and orogs.

History

By 1369 DR, Meat Cleaver could be found in the rubble on the deeper levels of Elven Catacombs of Myth Drannor in the territory taken over by the drow operation, grave robbing the ancients elven tombs under Castle Cormanthor.
