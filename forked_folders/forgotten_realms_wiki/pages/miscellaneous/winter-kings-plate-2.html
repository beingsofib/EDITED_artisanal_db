The Winter King's plate was a set of enchanted plate mail made using the body of the Winter King, a ravenous and massive remorhaz of Gloomfrost glacier in Icewind Dale.

Description

The armor's plates were carved out of the tough and thick hide of the most powerful polar worm to have ever menaced the cold Frozenfar. Unlike other armor of a similar type, the Winter King's plate was surprisingly light. The set appeared very unusual and striking in appearance, colored with whites and light blues of the King's hide.

Powers

The Winter King's plate possessed +3 enchantment and protected the wearer from fire and cold, both mundane and magical.

History

The Winter King's plate was created sometime before the by a blind dwarven smith who inhabited the icy depths of Gloomfrost in Icewind Dale. Blinded by the Seer, Tiernon Denthelm managed to kill the dreaded Winter King upon his first arrival to the black ice glacier. Tiernon created and enchanted the strange white plate mail using the creature's body. As of 1281 DR, the Winter King's plate was still in Tiernon Denthelm's possession.
