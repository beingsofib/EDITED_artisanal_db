Energy planes

Energy planes was a collective term for the two Inner planes associated with positive and negative energy in the Great Wheel cosmology and World Tree cosmology models:

  • Positive Energy Plane
  • Negative Energy Plane

In the post-Second Sundering version of the Great Wheel model, the energy planes were seen as encompassing the entirety of the Multiverse and providing it with the fundamental forces of life and death.
