Kiira (disambiguation)

A kiira, (Elven for "lore gem",) was a magical gem used primarily for storing knowledge. The term could be used for a variety of different kinds of kiira:

  • Kiira, "lesser" kiira that primarily served as spellbooks for mages,
  • Tel'kiira, or greater kiira, which could only be worn by elves of certain noble houses,
  • Kiira N'Vaelahr, "shadow lore gems" worn by members of the N'Vaelahr, and
  • Selu'kiira, "high lore gems", even more powerful versions of tel'kiira.
