Noldonar

Noldonar was a special method of melting broken glass down, reshaping it into new bottles.

History

Noldonar method originate from the nation of Amn. Phonchandas "Toadroarer" Baerlbohun of Athkatla was a foul-tempered and impulsive man who recklessly invested his abundant merchant coin. By the late 14 century DR, many of Phonchandas's investments were failures or ploys to swindle Toadroarer out of money, however, there were exceptions. Noldonar glass melting was among the merchant's successes stories and resulted in significant profits for the Baerlbohun clan of Athkatla's Gem District.
