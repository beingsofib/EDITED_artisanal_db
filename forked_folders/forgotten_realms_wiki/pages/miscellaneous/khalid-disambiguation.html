Khalid (disambiguation)

Khalid is a male name in the Realms and could refer to:

  • Khalid, a Calishite Harper and husband of Jaheira in the 14 century DR,
  • Khalid (Muluk), a young porter from the city of Muluk in North Zakhara in the 14 century DR,
  • Khalid al-Karim, a rawun and member of the House of Al-Karim in Zakhara around 1367 DR,
  • Khalid al-Zayir, a great poet of Zakhara,
  • Khalid el Axash, a Pasha member of the Axash dynasty of Calimshan, responsible for the construction of Khalid's Wall,
  • Ahmed bin Khalid, a matrud from the city of Qudra in the 14 century DR,
  • Habib Khalid Achmed Allafif, a peculiar thief from Athkatla in the 14 century DR,
  • Hosan Khalid, the man in charge of the West Center district of Huzuz in 1367 DR,
  • Yahun bin Khalid al-Hahiri, a decrepit old man from Iram, the City of Lofty Pillars,
