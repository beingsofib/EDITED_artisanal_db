Dungeon magazine 95

Dungeon magazine 95 was released in November/December of 2002. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Critical Threats: Yu'thiol Mansecho

By Matthew Sernett, illustrated by Kyle Hunter. p. 58

The githyanki pursue goals alien to most on the Prime Material Plane. This super agent is prepared to betray anyone to further the Lich Queen's cause.
