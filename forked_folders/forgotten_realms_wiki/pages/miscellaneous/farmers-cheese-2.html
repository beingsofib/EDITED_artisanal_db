Farmer's cheese or whitecurds, also called pot cheese or cottage cheese, was a simple cheese produced by small farming communities across the Realms. It was available for sale through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Description

Farmer's cheese was little more than the curds of aged milk when separated from its whey with cheesecloth. It was a fresh cheese that did not travel well. For that reason, it had to be consumed locally, and a customer ordering farmer's cheese from Aurora's Catalogue would receive cheese from the closest location available, in order to guarantee a fresh product.

The cheese was suggested to be served with salt and pepper or with cream as a side dish.

Availability

The Maid of the Moors restaurant in Mornbryn's Shield served fresh garden salad awash in whitecurds.
