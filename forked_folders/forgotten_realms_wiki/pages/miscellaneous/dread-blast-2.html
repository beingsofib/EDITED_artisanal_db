Dread blast

Spell
nameDread Blast
deityCyric
school3eNecromancy
level3eCleric of Cyric 4

Dread blast was a divine necromancy spell only available to clerics serving Cyric, the Prince of Lies. It fired a beam of negative energy at a target.

Effects

This spell could be used to heal undead creatures or harm live ones. It had a range of at least 170 ft (52 m) and more experienced clerics could extend this range and cause/cure more damage. When cast, a black ray of energy from the Negative Energy plane shot out from the caster's hand at the intended target and had to succeed in making contact. A victim had a chance to resist the magic of this spell, and if successful, only received half as much damage as usual.

Constructs and inanimate objects were unaffected by this spell.

Components

Only verbal and somatic components were required to cast this spell.
