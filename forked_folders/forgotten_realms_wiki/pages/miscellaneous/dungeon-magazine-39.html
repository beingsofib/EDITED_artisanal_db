Dungeon magazine 39

Dungeon issue 39 was released in January/February of 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

By Jeff Fairbourn, illustrated by Jeff Fairbourn, cartography by Diesel. p. 6–16

Flowfire

By Steve Kurtz, illustrated by Steve Schwartz. p. 18–23

A series of nine mini-adventures set in the phlogiston for player characters of levels 5–9.

Last of the Iron House

By Jasper Jones, illustrated by Jim Holloway, cartography by Diesel. p. 24-28

Horrible, wet, squishy things invade from the deep! An AD&D Adventure for a party of 3-5 characters totaling 12 levels. Players work to thwart the machinations of a group of dwarven highwaymen and the sahuagin priestess of Sekolah manipulating them. This adventure can be adapted to the Forgotten Realms setting, with the isolated port city required of the adventure replaced with Murann in the nation of Amn.
