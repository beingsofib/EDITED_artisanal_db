Darklake stout was a brew made from mushrooms by the duergar of Gracklstugh, the City of Blades, named for the Darklake.

Description

Darklake stout's strong flavor could only be appreciated by the duergar. It was commonly stored in kegs.

History

As of the late 15 century DR, Darklake stout was brewed by the Muzgardt clan of Gracklstugh. Kegs of Darklake stout found their way to the Spine of the World and the duergar fortress of Sunblight during the Everlasting Rime of Icewind Dale in the late 1480s DR.
