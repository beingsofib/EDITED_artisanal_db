Azoun (disambiguation)

Azoun might refer to:

  • Azoun I, the first Cormyrian king of the name.
  • Azoun II, king of Cormyr and slayer of the dragon Thauglor.
  • Azoun III, king of Cormyr and grandfather of the more famous Azoun IV.
  • Azoun IV, king of Cormyr from 1336 to 1371 DR. Notable events during his reign include the Time of Troubles and the Goblin War.
  • Azoun V, king of Cormyr from 1371 to 1449 DR. His reign included the Spellplague and several political reforms throughout Cormyr.
