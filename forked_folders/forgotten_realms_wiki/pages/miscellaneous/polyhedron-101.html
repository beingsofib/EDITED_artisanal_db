Polyhedron 101

Polyhedron Newszine #101 was released in November 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Back in Black

By Bruce Rabe and Lew Wright, illustrated by Larry Smith. p. 10–12

Those who've judged this book by its cover are in for a surprise.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: Turmish Customers and Festivals

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Larry Smith. p. 22–23

Elminster continues telling Ed about the customs and affairs of Turmish.
