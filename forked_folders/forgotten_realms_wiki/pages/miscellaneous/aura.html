Aura

An aura, also known as a dweomer, was a magical emanation from a person or object that was supernaturally detectable. Many divination spells functioned by recognizing auras. Undead, liars, secret doors, and lawful, chaotic, good, and evil creatures

Clerics always had particularly powerful auras that matched the alignment of their deity. Many paladins were surrounded by auras of courage.
