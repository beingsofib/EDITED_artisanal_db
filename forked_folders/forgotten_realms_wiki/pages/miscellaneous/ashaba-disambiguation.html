Ashaba (disambiguation)

Ashaba may refer to:

  • Ashaba, the water wizard and first lord of Shadowdale.
  • Pendant of Ashaba, the symbol of lordship of Shadowdale.
  • River Ashaba, the river which runs from Daggerdale to Scardale.
  • Twisted Tower of Ashaba, the tower which houses the government of Shadowdale,
  • Ashabenford, the largest town in Mistledale,
  • Ashaba Dusk, a variety of red wine.
