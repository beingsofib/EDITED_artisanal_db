Mask (disambiguation)

Mask could refer to:

  • Mask, the Faerûnian god of thieves and shadows, or
  • Mask (clothing), the article of clothing.

It could also refer to one of several spells:

  • Mask, a cantrip in the school of illusion, or
  • Aura, a Wu jen spell, the reverse of which was mask.
