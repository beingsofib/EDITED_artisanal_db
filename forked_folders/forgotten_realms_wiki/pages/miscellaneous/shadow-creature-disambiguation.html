Shadow creature (disambiguation)

Shadow creature could refer to several different concepts related to the Plane of Shadow or Shadowfell:

  • Dark creature, a creature with a strong tie to the Plane of Shadow,
  • Shadowborn, a creature native to the Shadowfell,
  • Shadowborn stalker, a humanoid made native to the Shadowfell via ritual,
  • Shadow creature, a descriptor for creatures native to or influenced by the Shadowfell,
  • Shadow creature (template), living creatures that are native to or tied to the Shadowfell, or
  • Umbral creature, non-humanoid undead shadow creatures.
