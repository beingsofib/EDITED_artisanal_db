Pan (disambiguation)

Pan may refer to:

  • Pan, the common piece of cookware,
  • Pan (town), a settlement in Koryo,
  • Pan (deity), a hedonistic interloper deity,
  • Pan Chu, a wako pirate who sailed about Kozakura,
  • Pan flute, another name for bird pipes, or
  • Pan lung, guardian lung dragons.
