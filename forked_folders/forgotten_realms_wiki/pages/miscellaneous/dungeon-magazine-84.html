Dungeon magazine 84

Dungeon issue 84 was released in January/February of 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Harrowing

By Monte Cook, illustrated by Stephen Daniele, Cartography by Stephen Daniele. p. 22-64

One sacrifice could spell the end of Lolth's supremacy in the Demonweb and the beginning of a whole new order. Module for 4-6 15th-level PC's but adaptable for levels 10-20.
