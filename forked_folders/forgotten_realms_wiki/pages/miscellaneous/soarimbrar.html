Soarimbrar

Soarimbrar was the given name of two males in the Heltharn Dynasty:

  • Prince Soarimbrar the Elder, son of Prince Velimbrar, and
  • King Soarimbrar the Younger, his grandson, who was murdered just before he could begin his rule.
