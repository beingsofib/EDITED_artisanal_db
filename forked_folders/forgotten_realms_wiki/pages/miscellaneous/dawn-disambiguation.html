Dawn (disambiguation)

Dawn could refer to:

Events:

  • The daily rising of the sun, an hour of the day considered sacred to gods such as Lathander or Kossuth;
  • The Dawn War, a great conflict between the gods and primordials in the Age Before Ages;
  • The Dawn Age, a period of Toril's history lasting from circa −30,000 DR to −24,000 DR, when the empires of the Creator races disappeared and were replaced by dragons and giants; or
  • The Dawn Cataclysm, an attempt by the god Lathander to reshape the Faerûnian pantheon.

People:

  • Dawn, an elf from Westgate who ran Dawn's Dress Shop; or
  • Jaskir Dawn, a captain from Elturel.

Other:

  • Dawn, a spell used by clerics and wizards that created a searing beam of sunlight.
