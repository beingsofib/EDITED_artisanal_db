Polyhedron 145

Polyhedron Newszine #145 was released in December 2000/January 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Living City Conversion Guide

by David Wise. pp. 10–21

A guide for converting Living City characters to 3 edition Dungeons & Dragons

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Wayfarers Guide to the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 42–43

Our tour this month takes a last look around the curious town of Oparl, luxurious (some would say decadent) capital of Bedorn.
