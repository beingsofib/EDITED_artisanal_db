Chaos Curse

The Chaos Curse or Tuanta Quiro Miancay (Most Fatal Horror) was a potion discovered by the imp Druzil and brewed by Aballister Bonaduce that made the afflicted being's desires paramount over all other things.

Difficulty

Both gathering the ingredients and preparing the potion successfully was extremely difficult. The gathering took two years.

Ingredients

Mentioned ingredients included yote mushrooms from the Great Glacier, eye of an umber hulk, blood of a druid, and tentacles of a displacer beast.

Appearance

The potion glowed blood-red when finished. Aballister placed it in an eversmoking bottle, which would then steadily release the Chaos Curse's maddening fumes.
