  • Silmarien Silverleaf, a moon elf from Synnoria in the 14 century DR,
  • Silverleaf, an adventurer from Daggerford in the early 14 century DR,
  • Silverleaf, a thief and a mage from Ravens Bluff in the 14 century DR,
  • Silverleaf fern, a species of fern that grew in the Hullack Forest,
  • Silverleaf grimoire, a warlock's grimoire,
  • Silverleaf hatchet, an enchanted dwarven axe,
  • Silverleaf Halfmoon, an adventurer from Freedale in the 14 century DR,

  • Silverleaf Trail, a road in the Moonsea North,
