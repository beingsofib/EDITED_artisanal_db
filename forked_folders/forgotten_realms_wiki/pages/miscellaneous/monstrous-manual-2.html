The Monstrous Manual is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory.

Description

The Monstrous Manual is a comprehensive bestiary for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons. It consolidates and refines a wide array of monsters and creatures previously dispersed across the initial loose-leaf Monstrous Compendium volumes and various appendices, offering Dungeon Masters and players a unified, hardbound reference.

The Monstrous Manual organizes its entries alphabetically, each providing a comprehensive overview of a creature, including its physical description, behavior, combat strategies, and special abilities. It also includes the ecological and societal roles of a creature. These entries are accompanied by illustrations.
