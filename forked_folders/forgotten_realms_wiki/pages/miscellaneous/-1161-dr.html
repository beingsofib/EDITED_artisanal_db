-1161 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Ali el Samesaj rescues the daughter of a Drakhon sultan of Calimshan from kidnappers. Her grandfather, Syl-Pasha Adjam el Drakhon, rewards him by raising him to the sultan's court, where he becomes the unofficial court wizard.
