Dungeon magazine 8

Dungeon issue 8 was released in November/December of 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Flowers of Flame

By Jay Batista, Illustrations by Kevin Davies, Cartography by Diesel. p. 46-63

The PCs journey to a remote mountain valley ruled by an evil spirit to claim the legendary Flowers of Flame. For 4-8 Oriental Adventures PCs of levels 5-8.
