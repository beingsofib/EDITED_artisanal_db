Dungeon magazine 177

Dungeon issue 177 was released in April of 2010 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Deck of Many Things

By Rodney Thompson, illustrated by William O'Connor. p. 57–68

A look at the notorious deck of many things, with color images of a complete deck of cards ready for printing.

Realmslore: Vaasa

By Brian James, illustrated by Peter Tikos, cartography by Mike Schley. p. 77–83

A visit to Vaasa under the rule of the Warlock Knights.

Characters

Darlisan • Kaggen Entreri • Borigon Hillsafar • Grintalalch • Telvannah

Organizations

Stoneclub

Eye on the Realms: The House of Naerhand: Family Games of Power

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Christine MacTernan. p. 95–97

A detailed look the wealthy and influential Naerhand family of the Sword Coast and Western Heartlands.
