Polyhedron 114

Polyhedron Newszine #114 was released in December 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited. p. 19

Mercenary Army Gathers on Plains, Lost Adventurers Return, House of Critters Moved.

Forgotten Deities: Ssethh/Vaerae

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 20

Ssethh/Vaerae, lord of the Yuan-ti.

Jungle Tales

By Dr. M. Hilzenbauer, illustrated by Valerie Valusek. p. 21

Tales and legends for the Living Jungle campaign The World Rests on the Back of a Toad, and Why the Shu Must Not Kill Each Other.
