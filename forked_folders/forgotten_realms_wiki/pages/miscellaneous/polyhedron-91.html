Polyhedron 91

Polyhedron Newszine #91 was released in January 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Everwinking Eye: Treasures of the Vast, Part Three

By Ed Greenwood. p. 4–5

The treasure-tour of The Vast surveys six more locations for adventurers to make their fortunes...or lose their lives.

The Living City: The Swineherd's House

By Terence Kemper, Cartography by Brian Blume. p. 13–20

Just outside Ravens Bluff, mystery and the supernatural add excitement to an otherwise ordinary family.
