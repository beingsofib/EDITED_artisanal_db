Haroun (disambiguation)

Haroun might refer to:

  • Haroun, the Cyricist commander of the Company of the Ebon Spur;
  • Haroun Abd al-Jari, a dwarven sergeant stationed at Sarahin within the Haunted Lands;
  • Haroun al-Raqqas, the caliph of Wasat; or
  • Haroun ibn Abbak, the grand admiral of the navy of Huzuz.
