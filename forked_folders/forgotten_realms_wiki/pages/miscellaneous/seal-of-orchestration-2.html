Seal of orchestration

Item
nameSeal of Orchestration
typeMagic item
creatorLaeral Silverhand

The seal of orchestration was a unique enchanted bauble crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Calliope in the late 15 century DR. It was one of many pieces of so-called "Silverhand gear" crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep.

Description

The Seal was a small trinket made of highly reflective silver. Calliope hung it from the neck of her lute.

Powers

As Calliope played inspiring songs in battle, the seal of orchestration would occasionally manifest an illusory duplicate of her, similar to the effect of the mirror image spell.

Notable Owners

  • Calliope, a half-elf bard, adventurer, and former pirate for whom Silverhand had also crafted the sword of riotous revels.
