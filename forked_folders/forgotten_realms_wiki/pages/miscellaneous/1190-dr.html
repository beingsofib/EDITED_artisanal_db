1190 DR

__NOTOC__

  • The shogun Eiko officially recognizes the settlement around the Shining Temple of Bishamon and names it Aru after the Aru river. Furthermore, he decrees it the capital of the new Aru Province and appoints Arata Matsutomo, head of the completely loyal Matsutomo clan, as its first daimyo.
  • The mountain temple containing The Giving Star, a magical morning star, is sacked by giants.
