Calliope (disambiguation)

Calliope might refer to:

  • Calliope, the ex-pirate half-elf bard in service of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
  • Calliope (maid), a maid who served in the Twisted Tower of Ashaba during the Time of Troubles.
  • Calliope (naga), the dark naga consort of Pristoleph the Purple from Innarlith in the late 14 century DR.
  • Calliope (cheetah), the animal companion of Jack Mooney from the Jack Mooney & Sons Circus of Ravens Bluff in the mid-14 century DR.
  • Calliope Blingsdorf, a musician who performed with the Circus of the Last Days in the late 15 century DR.
  • Calliope's enchanted lute, the battered magical musical instrument that belonged to Calliope of Waterdeep.
