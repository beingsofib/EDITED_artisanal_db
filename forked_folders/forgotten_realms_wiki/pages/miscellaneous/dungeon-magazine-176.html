Dungeon magazine 176

Dungeon issue 176 was released in March of 2010 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Codex of Betrayal: Geryon, the Broken Beast

By Ari Marmell, illustrated by Spikytiger. p. 57–62

An account of Geryon, the archdevil who once ruled Stygia in the Nine Hells.

Creature Incarnations: Fomorian

By Andrew Schneider, illustrated by Dave Allsop. p. 63–69

The mad, powerful, and deformed giants control vast stretches of the Feydark. Discover more new fomorian variants in the newest Creature Incarnations.

Fantastic Terrain: Elemental Motes

By Charles Choi, illustrated by Rob Alexander. p. 70–78

This unique type of terrain, magically buoyed to float in the air, can make for compelling encounters and adventures. Learn how better to use elemental motes in your campaign.

Eye on the Realms: Tarmel Drouth, Outcast Noble

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Christine MacTernan. p. 79–81

Tarmel Drouth was outcast from the Ilance family in Suzail, but he has not taken his banishment lightly and seeks revenge against those who have ostracized him.
