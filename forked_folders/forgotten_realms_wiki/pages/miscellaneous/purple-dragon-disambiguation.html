Purple dragon (disambiguation)

Purple dragon might refer to one of the following:

  • A deep dragon, an Underdark-dwelling dragon;
  • A member of the Purple Dragons, the army of Cormyr;
  • A member of the Purple Dragon knights, an elite corps within the army of Cormyr.
  • The Purple Dragons of Cormyr (ballad), a ballad about said knights;
  • Lay of the Purple Dragons, a ballad about their involvement in the crusade against the Tuigan Horde; and
  • Suzale, known as Purple Dragon ale.
