Barth

Barth is a common name in the Realms and can refer to:

  • Barth (Daggerford), a fighter in the Daggerford militia during the mid 14 century DR;
  • Barth (adventurer), an adventurer in the Western Heartlands during the late 15 century DR;
  • Barth (Scardale), a soldier and resistance fighter from Scardale Town; or
  • Barth that appeared in the novel Elfshadow.

It can also refer to:

  • Barth (island), the largest of the islands known as "the Teeth" in the Trackless Sea.
