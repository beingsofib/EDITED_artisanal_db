Setara

Setara could refer to:

  • Setara bint Aasim, a money-changer from Hawa
  • Setara bint Farid, a sea captain from Gana
  • Setara min Jisan, a priest in Huzuz
  • Setara, a witch in Neverwinter Wood (from Neverwinter Nights)
