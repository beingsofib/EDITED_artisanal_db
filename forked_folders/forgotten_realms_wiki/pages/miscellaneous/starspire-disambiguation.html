Starspire (disambiguation)

Starspire might refer to:

  • The solitary Starspire mountain in Melabrauth on returned Abeir.
  • The Starspire Mountains, a mountain range in the land of Tethyr.
  • The Starspire Peninsula, the peninsula south of the Firedrake Bay, which contained the mountain range.
