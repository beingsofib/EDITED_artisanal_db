Polyhedron 125

Polyhedron Newszine #125 was released in November 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The Border Kingdoms: High Emmerock

By Ed Greenwood. p. 6–7

Our tour of the Border Kingdoms continues fair apace with a look at one of Elminster's favorite backwaters, High Emmerock.

Living Jungle Spells

By David W. Baker, Dave Blum, James W. Cameron, Bill Sheffield, Glenn Smith and Susan Threadgill. p. 20–21

New spells for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons 2nd ed. from the heart of Malatra: grassdart • water window • lizard limbs • quick vine • ancestral spirit • hippo walk • vampiric plants • quicksand

Wayward Wizards: Tulrun of the Tent

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 24–25

Tulrun, a reclusive archmage who dwells in the northernmost frontier of Faerûn.

A World of Your Own: Island Campaigns, Part 4 - The Wild, Wild Wildspace

By Roger E. Moore. p. 26–27

About Realmspace and the Tears of Selûne.

The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited, Wayne Melnick. p. 31

War Slows, But Still Relentless
