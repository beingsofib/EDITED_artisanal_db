Berserker (disambiguation)

A berserker was a warrior who fought in a frenzied manner. In the Realms, the term often referred specifically to:

  • Rashemaar berserkers, the famous male warriors of Rashemen,
  • Runescarred berserkers, a sub group of those warriors,
  • Berserker blades, weapons specifically wielded by them,
  • Berserker brew, a beverage that appeared in the Utter East in the 7th century DR,
  • Berserker (weapon quality), the enhancement that improved weapons when the wielder was in a rage, or
  • Berserker blades, specific weapons from Rashemen that had similar properties.

It could also refer to a number of specific character types in various editions of the Dungeons & Dragons rules, including:

  • Berserker, a subclass in 1 edition,
  • Berserker priest, a priest kit in 2 edition,
  • Berserker, a warrior kit in 2 edition,
  • Frenzied berserker, a prestige class from 3 edition,
  • Berserker, a barbarian paragon path,
  • Berserker, one of the "primal paths" of a barbarian in 5 edition, or
  • Berserker, a generic nonplayer character in 5 edition.
