Arcane Lore: Secrets of the Slime Wizard

By Lloyd Brown III, illustrated by Eric Peterson. p. 70–74

Dirty, nasty, icky, slimy, gummy, stinky, gnarly...a number of repentantly gooey spells.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: The Bare Necessities

By Jeremy Johannesen, illustrated by Ryan Barger. p. 76–80

Don't leave home without these useful, if not terribly powerful, magical items.

The Dragon's Bestiary: The Ghazneths

By Troy Denning, illustrated by Pete Venters. p. 114–120

Only creatures as powerful as the ghazneths could threaten the kingdom of Cormyr.

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Terry Dykstra. p. 128–133
