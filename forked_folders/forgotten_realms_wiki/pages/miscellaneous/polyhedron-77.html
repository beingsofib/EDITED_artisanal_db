Polyhedron 77

Polyhedron Newszine #77 was released in November 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Everwinking Eye: Moonsea Shores

By Ed Greenwood. p. 7–8

Dress warmly and keep your weapons handy as we embark on a tour of the chilly Moonsea.

Downunder the Living City

By Wayne Straiton, illustrated by Gary M. Williams. p. 9–13

Tackle this mystery and give your characters a chance to get their feet wet in Ravens Bluff. An AD&D 2nd Edition Scenario for 4-6 PCs, Levels 2-5.

The Living City: Elonia's Beauty Shoppe

By Jack Graham, Cartography by Brian Blume. p. 24–26

A few coins and a few hours at Elonia's Beauty Shoppe can turn even a modest citizen into a temporary star.
