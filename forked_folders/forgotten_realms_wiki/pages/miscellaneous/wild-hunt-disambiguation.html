Wild Hunt (disambiguation)

The Wild Hunt may refer to:

  • The Wild Hunt, a supernatural phenomenon of baying hounds;
  • The name for hunts conducted by firbolgs seeking to emulate the true Wild Hunt;
  • Wild Hunt (sahuagin), a savage form of sahuagin raiding party;
  • An alternative name to the Dark Hunt, a subgroup of the Unseelie Court;
  • The name given to the hunts this group carried out on behalf of the Queen of Air and Darkness;
  • An alternative name to the High Hunt ritual of Malarites; or
  • An alternative name to the Wild Ride, divine realm of elven deity Tarsellis Meunniduin.
