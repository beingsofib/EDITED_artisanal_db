Polyhedron 80

Polyhedron Newszine #80 was released in February 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Everwinking Eye: Temples, Cults, and Idle Gossip in Thentia

By Ed Greenwood. p. 6–7;31

Discover the details of Selûne worship in Thentia and what things lurk in the wilds of the Moonsea North.
