Dalai Lama

The Dalai Lama was the spiritual leader and ruler for the nation of Ra-Khati in the Hordelands.

Etymology

The phrase meant "ocean spirit father".

History

Tsenya Garbo served as the Dalai Lama for over a century as of the mid 14th century DR.
