Upper planes

The upper planes (sometimes Upper Planes) was a collective term used in the Great Wheel cosmology model to represent the Outer planes of Good alignment, located across the top of the Great Wheel. These were Arcadia, Mount Celestia, Bytopia, Elysium, the Beastlands, Arborea, and Ysgard.

The World Tree cosmology model rearranged, renamed, and reorganized the upper planes into the Celestial planes but some people still used the older term to describe the good-aligned planes.
