Rose of Sarifal (disambiguation)

Rose of Sarifal may refer to:

  • The Rose of Sarifal, a novel by Paulina Claiborne;
  • Princess Amaranth, the so-called "Rose of Sarifal" or "Yellow Rose of Sarifal"; or
  • The symbol of the royal leShay family of Sarifal, depicting a climbing rose. Members of the family bore tattoos of this symbol, including Princess Amaranth (in yellow), Princess Callia (in white), and Queen Ordalf (in pink).
