Leviathan (spell)

Spell
nameLeviathan
school2eHigh Magic
level2eWizard 8

Leviathan was an Elven High Magic wizard spell.

Effects

This spell summoned different types of large sea creatures to aid elven ships at sea. Potential creatures included a leviathan, several giant whales, or a pod of common whales.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components a gold ring and a small carving of a whale to cast.
