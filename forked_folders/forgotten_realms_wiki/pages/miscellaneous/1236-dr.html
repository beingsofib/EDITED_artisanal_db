1236 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Errilam, who would become King Errilam I of Tethyr, is born.
  • The Revered Counsellor Totep of Nexala is assassinated by his generals. Zomoc becomes the new Revered Counsellor.
  • The Harpers erect the stronghold of Moongleam Tower near Everlund. Construction is overseen by Storm Silverhand the dwarf master stoneworker Gwuildeth Throck.
  • Lectures to a Youngling, a book written by Arivant, sage of Ormath, is published.
