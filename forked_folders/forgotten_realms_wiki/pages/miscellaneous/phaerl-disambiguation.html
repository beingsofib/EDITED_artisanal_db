Phaerl (disambiguation)

Phaerl might refer to:

  • Phaerl, the first ruler of the phaerimm;
  • Phaerl Hawksong, moon elf priestess of Sehanine Moonbow and member of the Moonstars; or
  • Phaerl Godeep, the Rebel Mage, a drow mage of Menzoberranzan.
