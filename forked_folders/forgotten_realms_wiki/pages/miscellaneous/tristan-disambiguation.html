Tristan (disambiguation)

Tristan and similar names were used by several people, including:

  • Tristan, a Flaming Fist soldier, or
  • Tristan Kendrick, a High King of the Moonshae Isles, or
  • Tristan Silver, an early human settler of Cormyr, or
  • Tristian, a crusading cleric, or
  • Tristram Cymrych, a High King of the Moonshae Isles, or
  • Tristran of Daggerford, an adventurer.
