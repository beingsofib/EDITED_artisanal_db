-3332 DR

__NOTOC__

  • The Sunset Plague slaughters nearly the entire population of Calimport in less than a tenday, including more than half of the ruling Marekh Dynasty. Calimport lies empty for nearly a century afterward, out of fear.
  • Daud yn Khadil el Marekh survives the plague to become the final ruler of the Marekh Dynasty and of the First Age of Calimshan.
