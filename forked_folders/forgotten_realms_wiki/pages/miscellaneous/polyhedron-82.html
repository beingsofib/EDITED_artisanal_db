Polyhedron 82

Polyhedron Newszine #82 was released in April 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: The Sapient Sorcerer

By Jim Dawson and John Miller. p. 8–9

The Sapient Sorcerer, a magic shop run by an addle-brained wizard, could pose a threat to the Lord Mayor of Ravens Bluff.

Magnificent Magic: Serious and Whimsical Items From Network Clubs

p. 10-21

Page after page of weird and wonderful magic items for the AD&D game are presented for your gaming enjoyment.

amulet of Lathander • collar of protection • Mondasso's automated spell scroll • Oyori of the Unknown Warrior • pegleg of Immurk the Invincible • potion of curing lycanthropy • quill of Pyrophilius • shakuhaci of peace

The Everwinking Eye: Secrets of Zhentil Keep Revealed

By Ed Greenwood. p. 22–23

Stroll with us into Zhentil Keep - if you've the courage.
