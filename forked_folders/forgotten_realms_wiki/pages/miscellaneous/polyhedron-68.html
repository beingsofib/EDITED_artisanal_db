Polyhedron 68

Polyhedron Newszine #68 was released in February 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Everwinking Eye: Goodbye, Mulmaster

By Ed Greenwood. p. 27–28

Our sage bids goodbye to Mulmaster - but not before he presents a few dangerous plot twists.
