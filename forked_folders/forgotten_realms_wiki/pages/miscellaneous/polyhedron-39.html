Polyhedron 39

Polyhedron Newszine #39 was released in January 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Tym's Supple Leather Shoppe

By Steve Thearle. p. 9

The prices are low and the quality is high at Tym's Supple Leather Shoppe in Raven's Bluff.
