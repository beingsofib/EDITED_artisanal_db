-1964 DR

__NOTOC__

  • A prolific year for spell invention, this year sees the introduction of five spells by four separate Netherese arcanists:
  • Blindness and foresight are invented by Smolyn;
  • Chain lightning is invented by Volhm;
  • Mount is invented by Jarm;
  • Rainbow pattern is invented by Nalevac.
