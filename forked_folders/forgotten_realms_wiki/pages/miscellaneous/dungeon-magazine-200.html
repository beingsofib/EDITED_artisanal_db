Dungeon magazine 200

Dungeon issue 200 was released in March of 2012 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Backdrop: Skullport

By Matt Goetz, illustrated by Brian Valenzuela, Cartography by Mike Schley. p. 65-74

This article describes Skullport, the infamous location below Waterdeep.
