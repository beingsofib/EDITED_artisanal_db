Polyhedron 86

Polyhedron Newszine #86 was released in August 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Friar Cookpot

By Scott Douglas and Craig Petillo. p. 6

Friar Cookpot offers passersby in Ravens Bluff mouth-watering victuals and more than a little smooth talk.

The Everwinking Eye: Adventures in Zhentil Keep

By Ed Greenwood. p. 17–18

It's time to bid Zhentil Keep goodbye, but there's still time for an adventure or three. Four mini-adventures for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.
