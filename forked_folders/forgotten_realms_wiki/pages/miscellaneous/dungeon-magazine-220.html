Dungeon magazine 220

Dungeon issue 220 was released in November of 2013 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

King of the Wolves

By Will Doyle, illustrated by Beth Trott and Tony Foti, Cartography by Jason Engle. p. 31-53

A host of grisly murders in Icewind Dale has the people of the Ten Towns on edge. Module for 2-6 PC's of levels 4-6, tied to the release of Legacy of the Crystal Shard.
