191 DR

__NOTOC__

  • King Ornoth I of Uthtower dies suddenly.
  • To avoid a dynastic struggle between the twin heirs to the throne of Uthtower, the realm of Yarlith is established to the north of Uthtower, south of Eigersstor.
  • The Twin Crowns of Myrmoran are created by Iniarv, the Mage Royal of Uthtower, at the request of the Council of Lords of Uthtower. Upon the sudden death of King Ornoth I, the third monarch of Uthtower, and the decision to split the realm into two kingdoms for his twin sons, the legendary archmage was commissioned to fashion identical crowns for the two branches of the Royal House of Myrmoran, known individually as the Twinned Crown of Yarlith and the Twinned Crown of Uthtower..
