Leviathan (disambiguation)

Leviathan may refer to:

  • The Leviathan, a proxy of the Earthmother resembling a whale that inhabited the waters around the Moonshae Isles.
  • The Leviathan (elder evil), an aquatic elder evil.
  • The Leviathan (ship), a pirate ship that shipwrecked and was refurbished into the tavern Beached Leviathan.
  • leviathan (creature), an incredibly rare species of whales of immense size.
  • Leviathan (spell), an Elven High Magic spell that was capable of summoning an instance of the aforementioned species.
  • Leviathan (elemental), gargantuan elementals of water.
  • Leviathan ale, an ale that was served at the Beached Leviathan.
  • Leviathan's Chase, a whaling ship.
  • Eye of the Leviathan (adventure), a Living City module.
  • Eye of the Leviathan, a powerful artifact that was the namesake of the aforementioned module.
  • Kuo-toa leviathans, massive kuo-toans created by Blibdoolpoolp.
  • The Strait of the Leviathan, a channel in the Trackless Sea named for the Earthmother proxy.
