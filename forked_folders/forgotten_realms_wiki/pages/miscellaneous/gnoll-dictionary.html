Gnoll dictionary

A list of words and phrases in the gnoll language.

G

grakling

some kind of expletive

K

kaamak

"dowry", wife's gifts to the mate

kadtanach

adj. "too massive to see all at once"

kerrla

"vile epithets"

kryshtantel

broken slaves who serve gnolls willingly and dedicate themselves to Yeenoghu, literally "savage souls" in Abyssal

P

paavak

"place for evil spirits", a place of death such as a battleground

R

rrachk-kiah

"what is seen is owned", gnolls' sacred right to claim anything they see in the world

S

shoosuva

"returner"

T

tantekurash

slavers who specialize in breaking slaves, literally "spirit breakers"

V

veela

"the victorious"
