Dungeon magazine 18

Dungeon issue 18 was released in July/August of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Irongard

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Eric Hotz, Cartography by Diesel. p. 4-7

A curse leads to a statue and places below. Module for 4-6 PC's of levels 1-3.

Crocodile Tears

By Marcus L. Rowland, illustrated by Karl Walter, Cartography by Diesel. p. 32-41

What really destroyed the peaceful village of Tijiki? Oriental Adventures module for 5-8 PC's of levels 4-6.
