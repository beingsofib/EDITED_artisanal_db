Dragon (disambiguation)

Dragon can refer to:

  • Dragon, a powerful reptilian being.
  • Dragon (coin), another name for a gold piece.
  • Dragon (magazine), a former printed magazine published by Paizo and later an electronic one by Wizards of the Coast, but now reduced to individual electronic articles
