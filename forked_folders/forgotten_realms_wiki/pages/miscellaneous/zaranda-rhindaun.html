Zaranda Rhindaun

Zaranda Rhindaun was the name of at least two queens of the Rhindaun Dynasty of Tethyr:

  • Zaranda Star Rhindaun, the founder of the dynasty, and
  • Zaranda Rhindaun II, its third queen.
