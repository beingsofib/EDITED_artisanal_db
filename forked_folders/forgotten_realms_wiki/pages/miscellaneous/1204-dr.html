1204 DR

  • Queen Bethilde of the Forest of Amtar gives birth. What very few know is that the child is the result of an extramarital affair.
  • Queen Lasheela of Impultur suffers a wasting disease and dies. In a matter of months, her husband, King Imphras II, marries his longtime mistress, Rebaera Osterhown. However, he starts showing the signs of madness.
  • The pirate Urdogen the Red seizes control of the Dragonisle and over all the pirates of the Sea of Fallen Stars. The pirates commence raids against ports and coastal towns.
