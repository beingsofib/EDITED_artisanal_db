Sceptanar

Sceptanar was the title given to the ruler of Cimbar in Chessenta.

History

The position of sceptanar was usually held by a powerful wizard. They lived within the Palace of the Sceptanar. The sceptanar held total authority, though they were advised by a council of elected land-owners.

Notable Sceptanars

  • Aeron Morieth
  • Tchazzar
  • Melisanda
