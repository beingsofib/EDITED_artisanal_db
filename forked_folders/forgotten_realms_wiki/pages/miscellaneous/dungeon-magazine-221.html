Dungeon magazine 221

Dungeon issue 221 was released in December of 2013 in online format only. This is the final edition of Dungeon. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Vainglorious

By Daniel Helmick, illustrated by Zoltan Boros and Paul Herbert, Cartography by Sean MacDonald. p. 28-43

The theocracy of Elturgard has some unpleasant neighbors, including a Black dragon in desperate need of an attitude adjustment. Module for 2-6 PC's of levels 7-9.
