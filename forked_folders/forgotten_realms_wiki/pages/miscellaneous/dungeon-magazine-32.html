Dungeon magazine 32

Dungeon issue 32 was released in November/December of 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Changeling

By R. Nathaniel Waldbauer, Cartography by Diesel. p. 26-27

It's big. It's old. It's mean. But is it white? Not quite! Battle Jhannexydofalamarne, also known as Whitefire, an albino Red dragon. Sidetrek module for 4-8 PC's of levels 8-10.
