Dungeon magazine 29

Dungeon issue 29 was released in May/June of 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Nymph's Reward

By Jeff Fairbourn, illustrated by Kevin Ward, Cartography by Diesel. p. 8–24

First impressions are sometimes dead wrong. Module for 5–7 PC's of levels 4–6.

Ex Libris

By Randy Maxwell, illustrated by Tom Baxa. p. 26–45

A dungeon that offers a truly moving experience. Module for 4–7 PC's of levels 5–8.

'Til Death Do Us Part

By J. Mark Bicking, illustrated by Terry Dykstra. p. 50–57

They were joined for better or for worse—mostly for worse. Module for 5–6 PC's of levels 8–10.
