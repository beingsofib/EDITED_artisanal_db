Fang (disambiguation)

Fang could refer to several landmarks:

  • The Fang, a peninsula in Aglarond,
  • The Fangs, a pass in the Cloud Peaks,
  • Fang (Laerakond), a mountain in Laerakond,
  • Fang Rocks, a reef in the Pirate Isles,
  • Fang of Skulls, a landmark in Dead Orc Pass,
  • Fang Island, an island in Luskan, or
  • Fang in the Forest, a peak in the Earthfast Mountains.

It could also refer to certain items:

  • Fang (weapon), a Kara-Turan weapon,
  • Fang (sword), a magical shortsword from Chult,
  • Fang dagger, a small venomous blade,
  • Fanged mask, a mask that gave the wearer fangs,
  • Fanged Mirror of Yehcir-Eya, a set of dangerous mirrors,
  • Fang of Zehir, a yuan-ti blade, or
  • Fang scarab, a gift of Lolth.

Many beings used the name Fang:

  • Fang (dog), a fierce fenhound,
  • Fang dragon, a gray chromatic dragon,
  • Fangs of Night, a ninja group in Koryo,
  • Fangs of Set, a secret cabal in Mulhorand,
  • Fang of Talona, the priests of Talona,
  • Fang of Yeenoghu, the gnoll champions of Yeenoghu, or
  • Fangshields, a group of heroic animals.

Some spells also used the name:

  • Fang trap, which set a biting trap,
  • Fang fist, which gave the caster's hand a biting mouth, or
  • Fangs of retribution, a spell that inflicted vengeful wounds,

Published adventures also used the term:

  • Fang and Claw, an adventure set in Avernus, or
  • Fangs and Frogs, an adventure set in Waterdeep.
