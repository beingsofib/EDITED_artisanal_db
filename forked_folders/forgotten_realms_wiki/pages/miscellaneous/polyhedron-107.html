Polyhedron 107

Polyhedron Newszine #107 was released in May 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Forgotten Deities: Moander

By Eric L. Boyd, illustrated by Phillip Robb. p. 4–5

Moander the Darkbringer met its match in Finder Wyvernspur—or did it?

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: Well-Hidden Treasures

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by George Vrbanic. p. 6–8

Turmishan treasures are everywhere... but finding them is the trick.
