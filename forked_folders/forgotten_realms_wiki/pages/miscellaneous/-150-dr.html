-150 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Syl-Pasha Kamus yn Tasyn el Tarshaj of Calimport grants the Ankaram region (north of the River Memnon and west of the Forest of Mir) to Tethyr, in order to weaken the power of ambitious Calishite nobles. Many in Tethyr accept this as a reward in thanks for their alliance with Calimshan during the Eye Tyrant Wars.
  • The Great Conflagration, a war between Narfell and Raumathar, ends with both sides ruined. Raumathari survivors are forced from the Hordelands by barbarians.
  • An avatar of Kossuth incinerates the city of Kensten.
  • River spirit folk flee from Narfell and Raumathar, and settle the Lake of Mists.
  • Rheligaun Darakh, the Nentyarch of Narfell, disappears and is presumed dead, ending the Darakh Dynasty.
  • Vayloss Talanthil IV, the tenth Sorcerer–Arkhan of the Talanthil Dynasty in Raumathar, dies.
