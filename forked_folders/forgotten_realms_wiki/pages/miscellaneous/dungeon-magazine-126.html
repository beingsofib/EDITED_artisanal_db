Dungeon magazine 126

Dungeon magazine 126 was released in September 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Blood of Malar

By Eric L. Boyd, illustrated by Chad du Lac and Mike Schley. p. 62-77

A hunt for Malar that takes place in the North Ward of Waterdeep. Part one of Vampies of Waterdeep. Module for 4 PC's of levels 12-15.
