Clara (disambiguation)

Clara was a name used by several people in the Realms. This included:

  • Clara, an unfortunate thief of Athkatla,
  • Clara Makepeace, a clothing trader in Eveningstar,
  • Clara (Freedale), a serving girl at Fendrick's Fine Foods, or
  • Clara (Port Llast), a serving wench at the Alliance Arms Inn.
