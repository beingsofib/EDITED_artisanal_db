Angelica

Angelica is a name that could refer to:

  • Angelica, a species of fragrant flowering plant,
  • The Angelica, one of the merchant cargo ships of the E.L.F. & Co. company of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR,
  • Angelica, an apprentice from the village of Dark Hollow in the late 14 century DR,
  • Angelica, a vampire spawn from Undermountain in the late 15 century DR,
  • Angelica Garpingwel, a Lady and an amateur sleuth of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR,
  • Angelica Nereid, a high ranking member of the Wizard's Guild of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR,
