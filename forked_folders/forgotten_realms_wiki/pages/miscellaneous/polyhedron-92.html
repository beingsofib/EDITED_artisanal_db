Polyhedron 92

Polyhedron Newszine #92 was released in February 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Vast Brewing Company

By James P. Buchanan. p. 5–9

It's the first thing any adventurer demands upon arriving at an inn, but who brews all that beer?.

  • Beads of brewing

The Everwinking Eye: Treasures of the Vast, Part Four

By Ed Greenwood. p. 18–19

The penultimate installment of Elminster's treasure-tour of the Vast.

Arabian Wonders: Magical Items for the Al-Qadim Setting

By Gregory W. Detwiler. p. 20–22

A trove of fabulous magical treasures from the Land of Fate, Zakhara!

  • Potion of Begging
  • Bag of Flaming Stones
  • Beacon of Fate
  • Figurines of Wondrous Power
  • Agate Buraq
  • Amber Camel
  • Emerald Winged Serpent
  • Jasper Hyena
  • Jet Wasp
  • Gem of Genie Delight
  • Gem Crystal
  • Instrument of Legends
  • Wooden Horse
  • Weapon of Prowess
  • Bagh Nakh of Punishment
  • Apple of Curing

Jenette LeFleur: A Troubled Rose in Ravens Bluff

By Rick Mangekian. p. 25–26

One of the Living City's most beautiful women is not at all what she appears.

Living City Character Sheets

By Harold Johnson. p. 31

The Network's new and improved record sheets customize your character. Here's the first in a series of class-specific forms: the Warrior.
