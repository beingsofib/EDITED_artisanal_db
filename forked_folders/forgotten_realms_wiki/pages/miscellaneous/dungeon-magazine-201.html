Dungeon magazine 201

Dungeon issue 201 was released in April of 2012 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Traps of Undermountain

By Matthew Sernett, illustrated by Victoria Maderna. p. 1–10

As the largest and most devious dungeon in Faerûn, Undermountain has always been littered with traps of all kinds.

Far Realms Fiends

By Michael E. Shea, illustrated by Christopher Burdett. p. 1–5

To help you flesh out a campaign that takes characters into the Far Realm, this article presents you with several creatures and hazards, as well as advice and ideas for how to make the Far Realm your own.

The Ecology of the Vegepygmy

By Teos Abadia, illustrated by Nicole Cardiff. p. 1–8

Trobriand's Machinations

By Philippe-Antoine Ménard, illustrated by Christopher Burdett and Jorge Lacera. p. 1–6

A look at Trobriand, a former disciple of Halaster Blackcloak, and his menagerie of mechanical menaces infesting Undermountain.

Tavern Profile: The Old Skull Inn

By Brian Cortijo, illustrated by Jason Juta, Cartography by Kyle Hunter. p. 1–6

Locals use the Skull as their unofficial town hall, and the inn serves as a rumor mill for many outside the environs of Shadowdale as well as those within it.
