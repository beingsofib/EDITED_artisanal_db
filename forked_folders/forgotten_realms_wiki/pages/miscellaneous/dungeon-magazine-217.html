Dungeon magazine 217

Dungeon issue 217 was released in August of 2013 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Rats in the Undercellar

By Tim Eagon, illustrated by Kieran Yanner and Scott Murphy, Cartography by Mike Schley. p. 2-17

An adventure for characters of levels 1-2. Set in the Undercellar, below Baldur's Gate.
