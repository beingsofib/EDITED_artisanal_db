Era of Skyfire

Era of Skyfire may refer to:

  • The First Era of Skyfire, a period of history from to when the efreeti Memnon and the djinni Calim battled over the Calim Desert; or
  • The Second Era of Skyfire, a period of history from to when Memnon and Calim returned and renewed their battle.
