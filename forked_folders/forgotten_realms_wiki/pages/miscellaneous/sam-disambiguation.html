Sam (disambiguation)

Sam was a name used by several people. This included:

  • Sam the Farmer, a resident of Daggerford,
  • Sam the Nimble, owner of the Black Sam Tavern of Liara,
  • Sam Witt, a TSR game designer, or
  • Sam Wood, an illustrator for Wizards of the Coast.
