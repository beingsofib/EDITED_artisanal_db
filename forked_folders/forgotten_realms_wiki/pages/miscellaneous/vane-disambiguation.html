Vane (disambiguation)

The name Vane could refer to:

  • Vane, the ancient civilization of the Moonsea North.

Or it could refer to one of numerous individuals, including:

  • Vane (person), the mercenary of the Golden Legion, or
  • Agada Vane, the well-respected Waterdhavian shopkeeper.
