Rod of favorable inclination

Item
nameRod of Favorable Inclination
typeMagic rod
creatorLaeral Silverhand

The rod of favorable inclination was a unique enchanted rod crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Vajra Safahr in the late 15 century DR. It was one of many pieces of so-called "Silverhand gear" crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep.

Description

Silverhand crafted the hooked metal rod to represent Vajra's strong convictions, and to empower her existing gift for imposing her convictions and interests onto her adversaries to influence their actions in her favor.

Powers

The rod of favorable inclination allowed Vajra to dominate her enemies.

Notable Owners

  • Vajra Safahr, the Blackstaff of Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR, to whom Silverhand also gifted the seal of insight.
