1244 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Illusk is overrun by orcs of the Bloody Tusks tribe at the end of a nine-year-long siege. The orcs then base themselves out of the ruins, from which they raid neighboring settlements.
  • After being under siege for nearly a year, Shogun Matasuuri Shogoro gathers allies from southern Wa and is able to break the siege at Tomo. He strengthens his position during the rest of the summer and prepares to strike.
  • Prince Elphras of Impiltur constructs a castle near the Great Barrow. Soon after, the castle is abandoned for reasons unknown and the prince disappears.
  • Duke Suldrian of Baldur's Gate commissions a set of magic plate armor to stroke his vanity, unknowingly ensuring his demise in 1249 DR during the Bread Riots.
