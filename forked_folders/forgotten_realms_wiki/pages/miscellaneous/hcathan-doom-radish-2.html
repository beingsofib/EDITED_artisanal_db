H'Cathan doom radishes were a type of vegetable that grew on the planet H'Catha. They were considered a savory food and were particularly enjoyed by the githyanki.

History

In the late 15 century DR, crates of H'Cathan doom radishes were stored in Stardock for the consumption of the githyanki population of Crèche K'liir. They were regularly brought to the station by trading spelljammers and githyanki astral ships.
