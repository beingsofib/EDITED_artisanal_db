-200 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Falling victim to a magical trap set by his closest advisers, Syl-Pasha Kalil el Evyrtaan of Calimshan dies from thousands of viper bites.
  • Vizar Asraf el Majizar takes power over Calimshan, beginning its Fifth Age. Calimport is developed to becomes an organized city, with managed trade and guilds for skilled workers.
  • Circa: Candlekeep is established and the Calendar of Harptos begins.
  • Clan Bormul announces the completion of Ithalyr, the first royal palace of Tethyr on the seashore cliffs of the Purple Hills. By year's end, the capital of Tethyr is moved from Ithmong to the new site.
  • Circa: Humans from the Vast cross the Dragon Reach and settle in the southern part of Cormanthor, becoming the first of the Dalesfolk.
