Dungeon magazine 208

Dungeon issue 208 was released in November 2012 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Demonomicon of Iggwilv: Fraz-Urb'luu, Prince of Deception

By John Rossomangno, illustrated by Ralph Horsley. p. 43-48

Do not underestimate this demon lord’s devious mind and gift for self-preservation.

Elves with Demonic Designs: The Fey'ri, Faerûn's Hidden Threat

By Erik Scott de Bie, illustrated by Sean Murray. p. 49-52

An article on the daemonfey, including Sarya Dlardrageth and her minions.
