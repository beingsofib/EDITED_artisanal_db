Slayer (disambiguation)

Slayer might refer to:

Classes or factions

  • Giant slayers, warriors who specialized in fighting giants and other large creatures;
  • Holy slayers, the elite assassins of Zakhara;
  • Illithid slayers, psionicists dedicated to eradicating mind flayers;
  • Monster slayers, rangers who specialized in hunting monstrous creatures;
  • Serpent slayers, individuals who dedicated themselves to ending the threat of the yuan-ti;
  • Undead slayers, elves dedicated to hunting and destroying undead;
  • Vampire slayers, monster hunters specializing on slaying vampires;
  • Wizard slayers, barbarians and fighters who specialized in killing magic-users.

Creatures

  • Slayer, the monstrous form taken by the Bhaalspawn;
  • Slayer genie, tasked genies who served to commit murder;
  • Symbiotic slayer, a monster from the Underdark.

Individuals

  • Slayer, an assassin who once stood against the heroes of Phlan;
  • Slayer, one of the avatars of Bhaal, the Lord of Murder;
  • Slayer, an insane Northman from Cormyr in the 14 century DR;
  • The Slayer Ebon, the ancient black dragon named Khaasxarax;
  • The Slayer of Mephistopheles, the hero known as the Hero of Waterdeep and a student of Drogan Droganson;
  • Tariq the Slayer, a slayer genie active in the late 14 century DR;
  • Zygarth Saern, also known as "Slayer," the proprietor of Saern's Fine Swords.

Weapons

  • Flayer Slayer, an enchanted great axe;
  • Giant slayer, a variety of magical swords and axes that were enchanted specifically to slay giants;
  • Monster slayer glaive, an enchanted polearm used by monster hunters;
  • Orc-Slayer, an enchanted short sword that belonged to the hin named Harpo Bogglinn.
