1392 DR

The Wailing Years

  • Seeking protection from the Spellplague, Pros and Ilipur, two cities on the Dragon Coast, petition to become vassal-states of Cormyr. Although King Azoun V accepts their plea, they are already doomed by the receding Sea of Fallen Stars.
