Aurora (disambiguation)

Aurora may refer to:

  • Aurora polaris, also called the northern lights, a display of colorful lights seen in the polar night sky;
  • Aurora, the wizard and businesswoman;
  • Aurora's Emporium, the delivery business owned by Aurora;
  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe), the catalogue used for ordering from Aurora's Emporium;
  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue, a sourcebook of common items purporting to be the above catalogue;
  • Aurora's Realms Shop, High Road Catalogue Counter, the North Ward outlet in Waterdeep;
  • Aurora's Realms Shop, Singing Dolphin Catalogue Counter, the Sea Ward outlet in Waterdeep;
  • Aurora's Realms Shop, Slut Street Catalogue Counter, the Dock Ward outlet in Waterdeep;
  • Aurora's Realms Shop, South High Road Catalogue Counter, the Southern Ward outlet in Waterdeep;
  • Aurora's Realms Shop, Street of the Tusks Catalogue Counter, the Trade Ward outlet in Waterdeep;
  • Aurora's Realms Shop, Waterdeep Way Catalogue Counter, the Castle Ward outlet in Waterdeep; or
  • Aurora Gost, consort of Bhaedulph Gost of the Gost noble family of Waterdeep.
