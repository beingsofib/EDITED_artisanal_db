Throat (disambiguation)

"The Throat" may refer to:

  • The road between Yulash and Zhentil Keep.
  • The watertower in Luskan.
  • The intersection of the North Ride and Shind Road in Voonlar.
  • A path outlined by boulders leading up to the Bone Dance in the Border Forest.
  • The sinkhole or crater that is at the end of the River of Gems
  • The Throat was a bay located on the southwest region of the Golden Water.
