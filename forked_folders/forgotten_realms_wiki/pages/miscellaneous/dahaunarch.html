Dahaunarch

The Dahaunarch was the lowest-ranking and least-prestigious seat on the Council of Six, the governing body that ruled over Amn during the latter half of the 14 century DR.

At some point during the late 14 century or 15 century, the council eliminated the seat of the Dahaunarch, which transitioned the Council of Six into the Council of Five.

Responsibilities

The Dahaunarch was entrusted with the command of the Amnian military, which comprised about one half the forces at the council's disposal, with personal armies and individual agents taken into account.
