White Crane

White Crane was a style of martial arts practiced by the Order of the White Crane of Shou Lung.

Description

A style of kung fu,

It did not include weapons use, but sohei of the order were skilled in wielding two matching weapons at once: butterfly blades, jitte, nunchaku, or sai.

History

In , when Batu Min Ho and Batu Hsuang Wu surprised one another in the dark late one night and he mistook her for an assassin, Wu fought back with White Crane style. She tried to disable her husband with first a kick to the bladder meridian nerves, and then laid him low with the angry goose nerve kick before he could draw his sword. She tended to him when he awoke.

Notable Practitioners

  • Ch'ang Tui, "Long Legs", abbot of the Order of the White Crane
  • Batu Hsuang Wu, wife of General Batu Min Ho of Shou Lung
