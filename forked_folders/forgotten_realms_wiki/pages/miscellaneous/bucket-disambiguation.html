Bucket (disambiguation)

A bucket was a common form of container used to carry liquids. It could also refer to:

  • Bucket (archbishop), a political figure in the Whamite Isles;
  • Bucket helm, a type of helmet;
  • Black Bucket Hunt, an annual event in Waterdeep;
  • Bordryn's Bucket, a tavern in Athkatla;
  • The Leaky Bucket, an inn.
