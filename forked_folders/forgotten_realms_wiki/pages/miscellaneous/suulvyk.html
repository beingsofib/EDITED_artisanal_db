Suulvyk

Suulvyk was a drow egg dish occasionally served in the Laerazss, the fifth course in a drow banquet. Due to needing spices from the World Above, it was considered a rare delicacy.

Description

Suulvyk were essentially hard-boiled eggs, but specifically the eggs of pack lizards. After cooking, the eggs would be covered in saffron and flavored with cloves.
