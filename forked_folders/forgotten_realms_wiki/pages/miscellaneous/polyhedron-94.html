Polyhedron 94

Polyhedron Newszine #94 was released in April 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Everwinking Eye: Land of Merchants

By Ed Greenwood. p. 4–5

Elminster has something other than a wink for Sembia, land of merchants and schemers.

The Cult of Ao: Who Watches Those who Watch over You?

By Steven E. Schend. p. 12–14;31

Since the Time of Troubles, one especially troublesome cult has appeared to maintain the divine Balance in the Forgotten Realms campaing setting.

The Living City: Myriad's Fencing School

By Lee Sheppard. p. 20–21;31

Learn to fence, or improve your skill at Myriad's Fencing School in Ravens Bluff.
