Quasi-Elemental Plane of Salt

The Quasi-Elemental Plane of Salt was the Inner Plane at the intersection of the Elemental Plane of Water and the Negative Energy Plane. It was a place of endless thirst.

Description

Creatures and elementals of water, as well as creatures containing water, suffered rapid dehydration the longer they stood in this plane. This also affected elementals of water-related liquid planes, such as ice para-elementals, ooze para-elementals, and steam para-elementals.
