Dungeon magazine 9

Dungeon issue 9 was released in January/February of 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Golden Bowl of Ashu H'San

By Rick Swan, Illustrations by Richard Bennett, Cartography by Diesel. p. 35–43

An angry spirit can even steal the rain. This adventure is intended for 3-5 Oriental Adventures characters of 2nd-4th levels.
