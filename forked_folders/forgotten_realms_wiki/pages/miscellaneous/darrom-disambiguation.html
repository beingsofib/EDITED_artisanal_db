Darrom (disambiguation)

"Darrom" was a Tethyrian male given name. Individuals named Darrom included:

  • King Darrom Ithal, the founder of Tethyr,
  • Crown Prince Darrom Ithal II, King Darrom's first son, and
  • Revered Father Darrom Vallson, a cleric of Ilmater and member of the Knights Kuldar.
