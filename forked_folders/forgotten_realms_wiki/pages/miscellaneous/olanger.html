Olanger

The olanger was the traditional ruler of Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Suldolphor's olanger, who was also referred to as the "Heartflame of Suldolphor," had absolute power within the city and could issue decrees as they saw fit. The olanger's will was enforced by the Flame Guard who also served as their elite bodyguard.

The olanger was usually selected by Waukeen, but since the goddess' silence following the Time of Troubles, many within Suldolphor were left wondering who would select the next olanger.

Notable Olangers

  • Daeranatha Dorluu, a high priestess of Waukeen who ruled for nearly a century during the 14 century DR.
