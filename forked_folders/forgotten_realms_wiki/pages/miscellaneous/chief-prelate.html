Chief Prelate

The Chief Prelate was an elected position who served as the head of the Clerical Circle of Ravens Bluff. They would preside over the monthly council meetings, appoint committees to address specific issues and represented the circle on the Council of Lords.

Prelates

  • Sarastos Gilgalard, Lion of Tymora
  • Sirrus Melandor, Holy Justice of Tyr

1370 DR

  • Relarn Dayspring, Morninglord of Lathander
