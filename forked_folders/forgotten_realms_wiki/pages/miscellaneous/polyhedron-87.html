Polyhedron 87

Polyhedron Newszine #87 was released in September 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Mailing cover

Convention schedule and a notice from Lord Mayor Charles Oliver O'Kane of Ravens Bluff warning those who own or traffic in illicit goods.

The Everwinking Eye: Sevenecho, Then and Now

By Ed Greenwood. p. 8–9

A look at the life and loves of Beliost Sevenecho and at life in the hamlet that bears his name.

Eye of the Leviathan

By Randall Lemon and Ed Peterson. p. 10–19

A simple treasure hunt in Ravens Bluff - if you don't count the intrigue, timely rescues, and perilous chases. A Living City Adventure for 4-8 PCs, Levels 3-5.
