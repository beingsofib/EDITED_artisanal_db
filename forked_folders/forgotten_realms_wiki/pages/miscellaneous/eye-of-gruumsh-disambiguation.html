Eye of Gruumsh (disambiguation)

Eye of Gruumsh can refer to:

  • An eye of Gruumsh, an orc touched by the power of Gruumsh who has gouged out their own eye
  • The Eye of Gruumsh, a powerful relic
  • The Eye of Gruumsh, a magical orb kept by the orcs of the Orsraun Mountains
