Kadar (disambiguation)

Kadar was a former empire of Zakhara. It was also a common Calishite given name for males. Individuals named Kadar included:

  • Syl-Pasha Kadar el Tarshaj, the last Caleph of the Tarshaj Dynasty of Calimshan and
  • Kadar al Ilmater, the Abbot of the Cloister of St. Uzurr.
