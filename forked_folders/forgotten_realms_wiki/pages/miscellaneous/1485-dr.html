1485 DR

  • Eleasias: The Church of Auril begins operating openly in and around Bremen.
  • Hedrun the Ice Witch gains the loyalty of the Reghedmen Tribe of the Bear. They establish a camp around Ingeloakastimizilian's lair in Evermelt.
  • Eleint: The Harper agent Aarun received a shipment of silvered weapons to help fight the luskar lycanthropes that have infiltrated Bryn Shander.
  • Flamerule: Warlord Hartusk was killed by Bruenor Battlehammer.
  • Tos'un Armgo was killed by Drizzt Do'Urden.
  • Aurbangras was killed.
  • Wartsnak was killed by an adventuring party hired to search for the Delimbiyr Bloke.
  • Pallidor likely died when an adventuring party defeated Akar Kessell for good.
  • Nalifarn was killed by an adventuring party hired to search for Jekk Ironfist.
  • Noyerghu was killed when an adventuring party invaded the Phylund Lodge.
  • King Harnoth was killed during the War of the Silver Marches.
  • Franko Olbert was killed in a duel with Tiago Baenre. His corpse was then eaten by the white dragon Arauthator.
  • Balor was accosted by the goddess Lolth, who was then allied with Balor's greatest rival, the balor Errtu. Lolth destroyed his minions, poisoned him, and entrapped him in a web cocoon.
  • Darrondar Gweth was visited by "Alwern" (in truth, the disguised rakshasa Nadir).
  • The Kingdom of Many-Arrows is dissolved.
  • Eleint: A simple dwarven miner of Clan Battlehammer, one Baerick Hammerstone, was influenced to foment dissent in the Dwarven Valley using black ice.
  • Vaelish Gant, a member of the Arcane Brotherhood, attempted to become Speaker of Bryn Shander by undermining Duvessa Shane.
  • Hedrun Arnsfirth become the Chosen of Auril.
  • The War of the Silver Marches results in the destruction of the Orc nation Kingdom of Many-Arrows.
  • Hammer 30: The Battle of Midwinter occurred in the Cold Vale between forces of Citadel Felbarr and Many-Arrows.
  • Mirtul 18: The Battle of Silver Arrows occurred in Luruar between an orc caravan of Many-Arrows and a group of Knights in Silver.
  • Eleint 24, Bjami Tengervaald led an attack of Hedrun Arnsfirth's army against Elk warriors, killing or capturing many of them.
  • Orcs besiege Silverymoon.
  • Flamerule: The War of the Silver Marches ends when Hartusk was defeated and the Darkening was dispelled.
  • Flamerule: Gromph Baenre, with the backing of Jarlaxle and using Drizzt Do'Urden as conduit, dispelled the Darkening, covering the act as a manifestation of the power of Mielikki
  • The Bedine tribes rebelled against Netheril as the Netherese spread their forces thin against Cormyr and the Dalelands.
  • The Netherese trade ground with Cormyrians in Cormyr and Sembia.
  • The Dales descend into chaos and war.
  • Drought continued in South Faerûn.
  • The Great Rain begun over the Sea of Fallen Stars at the start of autumn.
  • Eleasias 28: The corpse of Akar Kessel was discovered, risen as a wight.
  • Eleint 3: A substance known as black ice - a remnant of the power of Crenshinibon - was discovered in Icewind Dale.
