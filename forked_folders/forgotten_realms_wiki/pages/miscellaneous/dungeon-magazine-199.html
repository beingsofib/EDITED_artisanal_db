Dungeon magazine 199

Dungeon issue 199 was released in February of 2012 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Lords of Chaos: Olhydra and Yan-C-Bin

By Michael E. Shea, illustrated by Tyler Jacobson. p. 44-52

This article covers two more of the group in detail: Olhydra, the elemental princess of water, and Yan-C-Bin, the elemental prince of air.

Bestiary: Dao and Marid

By Doug Hyatt, illustrated by Milivoj Ceran. p. 53-59

Few creatures in the Elemental Chaos inspire more fear and awe than the great genies.
