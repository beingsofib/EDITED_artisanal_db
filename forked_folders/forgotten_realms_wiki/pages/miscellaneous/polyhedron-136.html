Polyhedron 136

Polyhedron Newszine #136 was released in June 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Announcements: Living City Campaign Staff Change

By Erik Mona. p. 2

An announcement regarding changes in management in the Living City organized play program.

Internet 101: Living City Live on the Web

By Ed Gibson. p. 7

A guide to Living City websites.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Wayfarers Guide to the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 8–9

Our tour of the Border Kingdoms this month looks at the Neth Stand, a dark and deadly forest that fills the northwestern corner of the region in Faerûn.

City Stories: The Temple of Selûne

By Brien J. Miller. p. 10–12

The Moonflame, a place of healing dedicated to Selûne in Ravens Bluff.

Rockroost: Hunters & Gatherers

By James C. Alpeter. p. 14–17

A Living City business that provides fresh game for those unable to hunt, and rare components for spell-casters and shops.

The House of Spice

By Terence Kemper. p. 18–19

Come to the house of Yeffres Mehaut, wealthy Spice Merchant of Ravens Bluff, and enjoy his hospitality.

No Honor Among Thieves

By Terence Kemper. p. 20–21

"Blind Dick" Corder, a personality of the Living City.

The Happy Hippocampus

By Daniel Cooper. p. 22–23

The Happy Hippocampus, an inn and tavern in Procampur is a popular spot for sailors.

Hunger in the Harbor

By Lloyd Brown III. p. 24–25

Something is attacking the port of Iriabor [sic]. Will this menace be too big for you to handle? An AD&D 2nd ed. adventure for 4 players, 24 levels.
