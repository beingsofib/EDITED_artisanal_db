Tusk (disambiguation)

Tusk could refer to:

  • Tusk, the elephant-fiend pet of Kurgoth Hellspawn;
  • Tusk (boar), the boar from the Emerald Grove;
  • Tusker, a derogatory term for an orc; or
  • Tusks, the pair of isles found in Firedrake Bay.
