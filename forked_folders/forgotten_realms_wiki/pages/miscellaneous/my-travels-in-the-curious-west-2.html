My Travels in the Curious West was a travelogue book written by Samatcho Ahonu and published in the .

Contents

This book detailed the experiences of an inhabitant of Kara-Tur traveling in Faerûn.

History

A copy of My Travels in the Curious West belonged to Manshoon XII, a clone of Manshoon, around . The book was stored in the mage's library of mundane and arcane tomes in his lair in an abandoned temple of Amaunator underneath the City of the Dead of Waterdeep.
