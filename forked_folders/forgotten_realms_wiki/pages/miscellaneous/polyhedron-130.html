Polyhedron 130

Polyhedron Newszine #130 was released in June 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The Border Kingdoms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 10–11

An exploration of the Forgotten Realms Land of Two Princes.

City Stories

By Keith Hoffman. p. 12–15

Temple of Chauntea: House of Bountiful Harvest.

Keepers of the Mystic Flame

By Carl Buehler. p. 16

NPC knightly order dedicated to protecting Ravens Bluff from magical threats.

Magic for Mariners

By Gary Watkins. p. 17

A selection of enchanted figureheads.

Sea Monster: Crustaid

By Jeremy Reinhard p. 22–23

The crustaid, a sea monster living off the coast of Evermeet.

Tying One On

By Spike Y. Jones. p. 24–27

A practical discourse on some basic knots, and how to tie them. (Really.)

Strongwind

By Glen R. Goodwin and Doug Smith. p. 28–35

A Ravens Bluff sea-going adventure for character levels 3 to 5.
