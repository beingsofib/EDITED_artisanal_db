Daisy (disambiguation)

"Daisy" can refer to:

  • Daisy, a type of flower.
  • Daisy, a unique enchanted crossbow.
  • Daisy, a prized dairy cow in Phandalin.
  • Daisy Head, an out-of-universe actress who portrayed the Red Wizard Sofina.
  • Daisyeye and Daisyeye II, two horses that were owned by Giogioni Wyvernspur.
