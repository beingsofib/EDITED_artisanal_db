Polyhedron 89

Polyhedron Newszine #89 was released in November 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Everwinking Eye: Treasures of the Vast, Part Two

By Ed Greenwood. p. 4;30

There's treasure in The Vast - if your characters have the mettle to capture it.

The Ugly Stick

By Rembert Parker. p. 8–15

A resort for the wealthy and powerful becomes a very dangerous place. It's up to a band of stalwart adventurers to defeat a beauty who threatens to make the resort's guests homely and the resort's owners poor.
