Thorm (disambiguation)

Thorm may refer to:

  • Thorm, a once-powerful noble house based at Moonrise Towers;
  • Eldebuck Thorm Fendarl, self-styled High Knight-Emperor of Fendarl's Gate;
  • Thorm Sureblade, Guildmaster of the Independent Fighters' Guild of Ravens Bluff;
  • Thorm Ubler, a mill owner in Mistledale;
  • Thorm's Mill, the mill in Ashabenford owned by Thorm Ubler;
  • Thorm Ultigar, an innkeeper in Yeoman Bridge;
  • Thorm Arthauvin, a farmer in Shadowdale;
  • Thorm (bodyguard), a bodyguard to a Crown envoy in Cormyr; or
  • Thorm (dwarf), a crooked dwarf merchant.
