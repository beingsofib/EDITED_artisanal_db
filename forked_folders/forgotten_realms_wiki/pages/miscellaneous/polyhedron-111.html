Polyhedron 111

Polyhedron Newszine #111 was released in September 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Forgotten Deities: Grond Peaksmasher

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 4

Grond Peaksmasher is worshiped by the firbolgs of the Moonshae Isles.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: Black Saddle, Great Oak, and Bedorn, and then a break

By Ed Greenwood. p. 5–7

The sage continues his review of the Border Kingdoms.

Larger than Life: Not Quite "Giants in the Earth"

By Dave Biggins. p. 22-23

The first in a semi-regular series on NPCs, detailing two in the Forgotten Realms setting.

The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited. p. 27

Mystra added to civic religion.

City in Transition: The Future of the Living City

By Scott Douglas. pp. 28–29

Mystra added to civic religion.
