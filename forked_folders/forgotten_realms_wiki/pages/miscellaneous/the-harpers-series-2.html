The Harpers series

The Harpers series is a loosely-connected series of novels about the Harpers, published between 1991 and 1998. Several books were interwoven with other major series, such as Songs & Swords and Shandril's Saga.

The Harpers series

  1. The Parched Sea (July 1991) – Troy Denning
  2. Elfshadow (September 1991) – Elaine Cunningham
  3. Red Magic (December 1991) – Jean Rabe
  4. The Night Parade (June 1992) – Scott Ciencin
  5. The Ring of Winter (November 1992) – James Lowder
  6. Crypt of the Shadowking (April 1993) – Mark Anthony
  7. Soldiers of Ice (December 1993) – David Cook
  8. Elfsong (January 1994) – Elaine Cunningham
  9. Crown of Fire (April 1994) – Ed Greenwood
  10. Masquerades (July 1995) – Jeff Grubb & Kate Novak
  11. Curse of the Shadowmage (November 1995) – Mark Anthony
  12. The Veiled Dragon (June 1996) – Troy Denning
  13. Silver Shadows (June 1996) – Elaine Cunningham
  14. Stormlight (October 1996) – Ed Greenwood
  15. Finder's Bane (July 1997) – Jeff Grubb & Kate Novak
  16. Thornhold (August 1998) – Elaine Cunningham
