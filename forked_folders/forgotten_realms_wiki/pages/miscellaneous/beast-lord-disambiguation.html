Beast lord (disambiguation)

Beast lord or Beast Lord may refer to:

People

  • The Beast Lord, an alhoon operating out of Dekanter;
  • Beast Lords, wizards who served Malar; or
  • The Beast Lords, rulers of the Beastlands in southeast Faerûn.

Spirits

  • Animal Lords, powerful spirits inhabiting the plane of the Beastlands;
  • Spirit Lords, powerful primal spirits.
