Cloak of Adeamozs

nameCloak of Adeamozs
school2eAbjuration
level2eWizard 3

Cloak of Adeamozs was a defensive abjuration wizard spell that protected the target against weaker magics.

Effects

The spell guarded either the caster or someone they touched from lower-powered spells or magic items replicating those spells' effects. This spell did not grant immunity while active, but lessened the effects of those hostile spells.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components some metal filings from plate armor and a scrap of cobweb to cast.
