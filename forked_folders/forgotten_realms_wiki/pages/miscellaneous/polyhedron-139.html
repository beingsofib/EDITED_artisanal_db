Polyhedron 139

Polyhedron Newszine #139 was released in December 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

City Stories: The Hall of Luck

By Keith Hoffman. p. 13–15

A visit to the Hall of Luck—the Ravens Bluff temple of Tymora.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Wayfarer's Guide to the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 16–17

This month's instalment of our tour of the Border Kingdoms completes our look at the perilous but fascinating town of Oeble.

Glyphs of Devotion

By Gary Watkins. p. 18–19

A new aid for those in service to a holy order: glyphs that hold spells of divination, healing, or protection.
