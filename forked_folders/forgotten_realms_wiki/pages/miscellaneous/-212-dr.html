-212 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Tethyrian barbarian clans and elven allies, led by Chieftain Darrom Ithal capture Myratma for a second time from the Calishites in the Battle of the Purple Marches.
  • The white dragon, Ufloq, awakens in its lair in the Olyniak Crevasse of the Great Glacier. Several dozen Nakulutiun witnessed the event, attracted to the crevasse by the loud rumbling. Many were promptly devoured by the hungry dragon. This is the only recorded sighting of Ufloq.
  • Darrom Ithal becomes king of Tethyr, and Calimshan finally grants it its independence. It is the beginning of the Calendar of Tethyreckoning, known as the Founding Year.
