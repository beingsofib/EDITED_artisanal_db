Abigail (disambiguation)

Abigail was a name used by several people, including:

  • Abigail, a Gondar inhabitant of Baldur's Gate, or
  • Abigail (queen), the wife of High King Carrig Cymrych III of the Moonshae Isles, or
  • Abigail (Ravens Bluff), an inhabitant of Ravens Bluff.
