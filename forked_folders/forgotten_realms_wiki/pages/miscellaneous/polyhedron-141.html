Polyhedron 141

Polyhedron Newszine #141 was released in April 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Wayfarers Guide to the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 12–13

Our look at the sorcerous land of Ondeeme continues this month with descriptions of the rest of the Slee.

Watching the Streets: Ravens Bluff City Watch

By Christy Nichols. p. 20–25

Updates to the rules for the City Watch in Ravens Bluff, and benefits and duties of the position.

The Silent Network

By Cisco Lopez-Fresquet. p. 26-27
