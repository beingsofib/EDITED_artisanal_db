Pure thought

Spell
namePure Thought

Pure Thought was a sutra used to fortify oneself against evil.

Realmslore

In the Year of the Helm, 1362 DR, the Koja of Khazari recited the Pure Thought sutra when he was being led by a mysterious envoy.
