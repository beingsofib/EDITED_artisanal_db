1210 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Bandits once again attack pilgrims on the way to Aru in Wa through the mountain passes, leaving no survivors.
  • Shogun Eiko replaces Daimyo Arata Matsutomo of Aru Province with Sousuke Toda.
  • Shlanarnla Durovree, a future Queen of Westgate, is born.
  • Pryntaler Obarskyr, king of Cormyr dies.
