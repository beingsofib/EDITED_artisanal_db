Polyhedron 129

Polyhedron Newszine #129 was released in April 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The Border Kingdoms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 10–11

An exploration of the Forgotten Realms jungle of Jundarwood.

City Stories

By Wayne Melnick and John Richardson. p. 12–15

Tabernacle of Mirth: The Temple of Lliira.

Knights of the Hawk

By John A. Harnes. p. 16

A ranger/druid order charged with protecting Ravens Bluff.

Jungle Tales

By Stephen H. Jay. p. 17

The Simbara legend of the ancient enemy.

Jungle Tales

By Tom Prusa. p. 29

The outcast who became chief.

Agriculture, Invention, and Society in Malatra

By Stephen H. Jay. p. 30–31

Living Jungle setting guidelines for players and authors.

More Living Jungle Races

By Stephen H. Jay, David Morrow, Tom Prusa and Gary Watkins. p. 32–37

Four new jungle races.
