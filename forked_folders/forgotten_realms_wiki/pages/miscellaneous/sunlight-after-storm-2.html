Sunlight after storm

Spell
nameSunlight After Storm

Sunlight After Storm was a sutra that cleared illusions from one's mind, enabling them to see things as they truly were.

It could be activated with a sprinkle of kumiss.

Realmslore

In the Year of the Helm, 1362 DR, the Koja of Khazari met a mysterious woman who'd presented him with a magical feast and attempted to draw an oath from him. Fearing a trick, he uttered the Sunlight After Storm sutra and flicked kumiss at her. She revealed herself to be god Deneir.

It is unclear if the sutra pierced Deneir's disguise or if the god simply chose to reveal himself after the investigation.
