Polyhedron 135

Polyhedron Newszine #135 was released in April 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Wayfarers Guide to the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 8–9

The tour of the Border Kingdoms continues with Myrinjar, the capital of Adaerglast, the Land of Mages.

City Stories: The Temple of Gond

By Joe Masdon. p. 10–12

A visit to The Turning Wheel, the newly-opened temple of Gond in Ravens Bluff.

Wolflords of Malar

By Chadwick V. Simmons. p. 13

An extension of the Church of the Beast-Lord in the Forgotten Realms campaign setting.
