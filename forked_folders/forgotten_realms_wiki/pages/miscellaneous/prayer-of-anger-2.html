Prayer of Anger was an enchanted greatsword.

Description

The sword was inscribed with the holy symbol of Tempus, god of battle.

Powers

It was +2 greatsword. It gave its wielder an immunity to fear magic, and it would enhance any rage effects.

Value

It was worth 14,350 gold pieces.
