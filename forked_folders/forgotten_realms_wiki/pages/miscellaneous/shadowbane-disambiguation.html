Shadowbane (disambiguation)

Shadowbane may refer to:

A pseudonym used by a number of heroes who wielded the sword Vindicator, including:

  • Gedrin Thalvar, the original "Shadowbane";
  • Kalen Dren, the second Shadowbane;
  • Rhetegast Hawkwinter, the third Shadowbane; or
  • Vaelis, another heir to the title of Shadowbane.

Novels and short stories within the Shadowbane series about the above characters, including:

  • Downshadow (2009) by Erik Scott de Bie,
  • Shadowbane (2011) by Erik Scott de Bie; and
  • Shadowbane: Eye of Justice (2012) by Erik Scott de Bie

Other:

  • The Shadowbane War, a conflict between Netheril and the cities of the Moonsea
