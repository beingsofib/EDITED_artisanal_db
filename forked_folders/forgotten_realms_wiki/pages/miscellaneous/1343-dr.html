1343 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Rachel Wykos is born in the city of Neverwinter.
  • An orc horde sets out to attack Silverymoon and Everlund. In a battle that will come to be known as the Battle of Turnstone Pass, the Moonlight Men mercenaries manage to slay 4,000 orcs at Turnstone Pass, but they themselves are decimated. Only six survive, including Mintiper Moonsilver, but the mercenaries are lauded for saving both settlements.
  • Mintiper Moonsilver is blamed for writing a particularly offensive drinking song that becomes extremely popular with surly gnomes. It is titled "Ga Nomes" and has been the cause of bar-brawls involving the Forgotten Folk ever since.
  • Mibbet the Meddler casts stone to flesh spell at a statue atop a fountain in the center of Ithmong. Jondar the Valiant is turned to flesh after being petrified for more than a century.
