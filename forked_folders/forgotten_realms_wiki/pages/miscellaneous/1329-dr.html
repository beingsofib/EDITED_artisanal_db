1329 DR

__NOTOC__

  • On Eleint 2, Prince Azoun Obarskyr IV married Filfaeril Selazair.
  • The mining colony of Berranzo is founded on Gundarlun by a Calishite merchant consortium led by the wizard Hochmiraz.
  • Following the instructions of Great Arcane Aodk, morkoth kidnap Queen Wylla, Princess Diera, and Princess Aeda of the merfolk. They magically transform the mer-women into mindless monsters that attack Voalidru. To avenge their deaths, the merfolk later attack Olleth and slay Aodk and his Arcounts.
  • A Dove At Dawn, a book written by Ardreth, the High Harp of Berdusk, published.
