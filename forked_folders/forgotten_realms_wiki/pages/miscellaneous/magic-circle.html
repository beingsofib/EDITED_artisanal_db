Magic circle

A magic circle was an effect created by a series of spells designed to either protect against or entrap extraplanar beings of particular alignments. Such spells included:

  • Magic circle against chaos,
  • Magic circle against evil,
  • Magic circle against good, and
  • Magic circle against law.
