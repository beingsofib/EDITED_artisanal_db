Chosen of Nendawen trilogy

Chosen of Nendawen is a trilogy by Mark Sehestedt that tells the training of Hweilan as the Hand of the Hunter and her revenge on the demon lord Jagun Ghen.

Chosen of Nendawen

  1. The Fall of Highwatch (November 2009)
  2. Hand of the Hunter (December 2010)
  3. Cry of the Ghost Wolf (December 2011)
