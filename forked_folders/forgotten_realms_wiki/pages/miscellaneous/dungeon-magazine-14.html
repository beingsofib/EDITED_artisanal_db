Dungeon magazine 14

Dungeon issue 14 was released in November/December of 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Masqueraider

By Randy Maxwell, illustrated by Tom Baxa, Cartography by Diesel. p. 4-13

Near the town of Tyrluk, monsters prowl the hills. Adventure for 4-6 PC's of levels 2-5.

A Question of Balance

By Nigel Findley, illustrated by Jim Holloway, Cartography by Diesel. p. 14-18

Between Cormyr and Sembia, two travelers appear in a magic storm. One is a demon. Adventure for 4-6 PC's of levels 8-12.
