High Horn

High Horn might refer to:

  • High Horn (mountain), a peak in the Storm Horn mountains;
  • High Horn Pass, the pass through the mountains near High Horn peak;
  • High Horn (hold), a Cormyrian fortification at the top of the mountain; or
  • High Horn (Snout of Omgar), a stone statue on the northern tip of the Snout of Omgar.
