-946 DR

  • Shandaular, the capital city of Ashanath, is destroyed by the armies of the Nentyarch of Tharos.
  • Leader of Shandaular, Arkaius dies sealing a portal that allow his people to escape.
  • The people of Shandaular flee by means of the two-way portal around which their city is built to the Council Hills. They name themselves the Arkaiuns in honor of their fallen ruler.
  • Nentyarch Thargaun unites all the small, conquered Nar kingdoms into the Empire of Narfell.

Novels

  • The Shield of Weeping Ghosts begins.
