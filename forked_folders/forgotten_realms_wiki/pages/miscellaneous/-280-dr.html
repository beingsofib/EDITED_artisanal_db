-280 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Responding to piracy in the Celestial Sea, the sixth Emperor Chin of the Hai Dynasty of Shou Lung proclaims the Scattering of Winds, a demand that the warlords of Wa swear allegiance to Shou Lung emperor. Wa declines, and Wanese emperor Gokami gathers a fleet.
  • The city of Almorel is founded by the Raumathari.
  • Emperor Gokami executes all but one of the emissaries from Shou Lung, triggering the start of the Omi War.
