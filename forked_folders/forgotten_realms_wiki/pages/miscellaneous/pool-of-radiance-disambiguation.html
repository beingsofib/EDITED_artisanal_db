Pool of Radiance (disambiguation)

Pool of Radiance might refer to:

  • Pool of Radiance (game), the first Forgotten Realms computer game.
  • Pool of Radiance (novel), the computer game novelization by James Ward and Jane Cooper Hong.
  • Pool of Radiance: Ruins of Myth Drannor, the computer video game published in 2001 by Ubisoft.
  • Pool of Radiance: Ruins of Myth Drannor (novel), the novelization of the game by Carrie Bebris.
  • Pool of Radiance: Attack on Myth Drannor, the companion adventure to the video game by Sean K. Reynolds.
  • Pool of radiance, magical phenomenon that manifested raw magic in the form of a small body of water.
  • Pool of Radiance (tavern), the tavern in Phlan, named after the infamous pool of radiance in Valjevo Castle.
