Matilda

Matilda is a name that could refer to:

  • Matilda (thief), or Matty, a notorious thief from Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR,
  • Matilda (Mossbridges), the owner of Matilda's Boarding House in Mossbridges in the late 14 century DR,
  • Matilda (Selgaunt), the aging proprietor of the Winsome Wench in Selgaunt in the late 14 century DR,
  • Matilda the White Lady, a retired adventuress from Nellie Thursday Home for Experienced Adventurers in the mid-14 century DR,
  • Matilda Gemsight, a matronly priestess of Gond in the 14 century DR,
  • Matilda Thoraker, one of The Pride of Procampur's crew in the 14 century DR.
