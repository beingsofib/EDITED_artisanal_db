1467 DR

  • The Tempuswrath occurs. Members of the Order of the Steel Fang are hired to fight on opposing sides of a conflict, and many of the order slay each other in the fighting. Tempus appears before them on the battlefield and commands them to return to proper worship.

Novels

  • City of the Dead begins.
