Cassandra (disambiguation)

Cassandra was a name used by several people in the Realms. This included:

  • Cassandra (Stag Falls), a serving wench,
  • Cassandra Rulmac, an adventurer, or
  • Cassandra Thann, matriarch of House Thann.
