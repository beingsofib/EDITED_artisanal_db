Dungeon magazine 192

Dungeon issue 192 was released in July of 2011 in online format only. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Backdrop: Daggerdale

By Eric Menge, illustrated by Noah Bradley, cartography by Jared Blando. p. 60–66

This article describes the history of Daggerdale.

Creature Incarnations: Abyssal Plague Demons

By Michael E. Shea, illustrated by Kieran Yanner. p. 52–56

This article documents the various demon creatures spawned by the Abyssal plague.

Eye on the Realms: Masks of Living Flesh

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Jason Bennett. p. 67–69

This article provides an overview of the strange parasitic creature called a jaod.

Monster Manual Update: Gibbering Beast

By Logan Bonner, illustrated by Thomas M. Baxa and Pete Venters. p. 76–78

Updates and lore for the gibbering beasts.

Monster Manual Update: Chuul

By Logan Bonner, illustrated by Wayne Reynolds and Francis Tsai. p. 79–80

Updates and lore for the chuul.

Monster Manual Update: Grell

By Logan Bonner, illustrated by Alexey Aparin and Francis Tsai. p. 81–83

Updates and lore for the grell.
