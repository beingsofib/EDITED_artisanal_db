Red Sword

The Red Sword was a title within the government of Archendale, one of the three anonymous Swords of Archendale. The Red Sword was the champion and commander of the Archenriders, the standing army of the Dale. The army's headquarters, and seat of power for the Swords, was the fortress of Swordpoint in Archenbridge.

Identity

In the Year of the Gauntlet 1369 DR, the Red Sword was Dheren Ogresbane, an intelligent young officer in the Archendale army. Like all the Swords, he protected his identity with a number of illusion spells and a pair of bracers of misdirection.
