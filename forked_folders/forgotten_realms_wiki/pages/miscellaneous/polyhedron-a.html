Polyhedron A

Polyhedron Newszine A, a special introductory issue of Polyhedron, was released in March 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: The Sign of the Quill and Scribe Shop

By Fran Hart. p. 28–30

Hoathen Thee, a scribe with unmatched talent, will translate almost any document.

Ravens Bluff Rumors

By Matt Denzler. p. 31

Traveling by sea to the Living City can be dangerous.
