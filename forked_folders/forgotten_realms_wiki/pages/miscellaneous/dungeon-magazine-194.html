Dungeon magazine 194

Dungeon issue 194 was released in September of 2011 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Crafthouse of Inspiration

By Doug Hyatt, illustrated by Milivoj Ceran, Cartography by Mike Schley. p. 17-21

Show us the one reason why, a hundred years from now, the world will still remember your name. Show us that miracle, and your invention might—I said might—just grace the Hall of Wonders.

Eye on the Realms: Thormil's Secret

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Tyler Walpole. p. 28-30

Not all that far east of Wheloon in Cormyr is a small, backroads farm, the holding of one Nars Thormil, that has started to attract some local attention for the steady stream of fat thimdrors it’s turning out, all of them bound for the best eateries in Saerloon.
