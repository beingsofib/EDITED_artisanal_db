Daern (disambiguation)

Daern may refer to:

People

  • Daern, a notable acolyte of Ilmater who died in the early-to-mid 14 century DR,
  • Daern Claundon, a dwarven adventurer renowned for his hatred of orcs in the mid-to-late 14 century DR, or
  • Druth Daern, a lich who dwelt in the ruins of Myth Drannor as of the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Items

  • Daern's instant fortress, a magic item that allowed the owner to create an adamantine fortress.

Other

  • A Dwarvish word meaning "familiar" or "known" (place, feature, or being).
