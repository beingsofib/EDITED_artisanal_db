Nightsong (disambiguation)

Nightsong may refer to:

  • A common star elf surname,
  • Aylin, a daughter of Selûne, also known as the Nightsong,
  • Jax Nightsong, the half-elven founder of the Knights of the Shadow Sword,
  • Lerien Hollybow, a Squire to the Dove Order and a former Herald of the Black Bird Archery Company, also known as Nightsong,
  • Kavrala Nightsong, a mage from the silver elf settlement of Nimlith.
