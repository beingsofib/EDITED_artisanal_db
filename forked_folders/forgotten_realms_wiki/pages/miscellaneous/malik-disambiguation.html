Malik (disambiguation)

"Malik" was a common male given name in Zakhara and Calimshan. Individuals named Malik included:

  • Malik al-Samawat, a noble djinni living on Al-Zira,
  • Malik el Sami, the Chosen of Cyric, and
  • Malik el Quaahl, the son of the sultan of Quill Ward in Calimport.
