Empires of the Sands (disambiguation)

The "Empires of the Sands" was a collective term for the countries later referred to as the Lands of Intrigue. The term might also refer to:

  • Empires of the Sands, a Forgotten Realms sourcebook by Scott Haring
  • Empires of the Sands, an eleven-volume history of Calimshan used as a reference by King Haedrak Rhindaun III in his Historia Tethyra
