Seer (disambiguation)

The term Seer often referred to someone with clairvoyant or divinatory powers, able to foresee or predict future events. It could also refer to:

  • SEER, the codename of spymaster Yi Lao.
  • Seer (class), a spellcasting NPC class meant to represent the archetypal seer.
  • Seer (psionic), a class of psion that specialized in the clairsentience discipline.
  • The Seer, one of the twenty-two cards found in a Wroth deck.
  • The Seer (adventure), an Adventurers League adventure module.
  • The Seer's Eye, a storefront in the crossroads town of Two Stars.
  • Seer's Cove, a pirate haven.
  • Smolyn's seer, a divination spell.
  • Nature Seers, a druidic circle.
  • Star spawn seer, a type of star spawn.

In many cases "Seer" was used as a title by individuals, typically owing to their prophetic abilities.

  • Alaundo the Seer, a sage famed for his predictions.
  • Ankhat-Parameh, a pharaoh who was renowned for his prophetic abilities.
  • Augathra the Seer
  • Erathaol, a great tome archon prophet and patron of seers.
  • G'axir the Seer, a fortune teller in Baldur's Gate.
  • The Gloomfrost Seer
  • Lizabet the Seer
  • Lertis the Seer, a Banite priest.
  • The Mad Seer of Manshaka, a title held by Tahyr yn Savras.
  • The Sacred Seer, a mutated myconid.
  • Seer, the alias of an Eilistraean priestess.
  • Seer (Aru), the nickname of an elderly man in Aru.
  • Seer (Huroola), a Huroola witch doctor.
  • Seer of the Revealing Light, a title held by the sage Sambranna Highstar.
  • Xaxxinnar the Seer
