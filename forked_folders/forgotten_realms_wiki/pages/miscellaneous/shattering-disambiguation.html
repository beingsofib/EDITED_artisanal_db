Shattering (disambiguation)

The Shattering might refer to one of several events:

  • Shattering (holiday), the annual celebration enjoyed by the church of Valkur; or
  • Shattering (Calimshan), the destruction of Ajhuutal by Calim; or
  • The Shattering, the birth of the Abyss by the god Tharizdun.
