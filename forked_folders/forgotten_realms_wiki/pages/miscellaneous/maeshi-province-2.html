The Maeshi Province was a small and isolated province in Kozakura in 1357 DR.

Geography

Maeshi was on a small northern peninsula on the west coast of Shinkoku and was bordered by several of the highest peaks in Kozakura, among them including Two Peak Mountain.

History

It was famous for the cursed Ito-jo castle and the Doi No Fugu inn.
