Mhair (disambiguation)

Mhair was both a given name and a name associated with the a jungle on the Chultan Peninsula. It could refer to:

  • Mhair, a Tethyrian cartographer
  • Mhair Gulzrabban, the founder of the Company of the Cloak
  • Mhair Szeltune, the leader of the Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors
  • The Mhair Jungles, a rainforest on the Chultan Peninsula
  • Mhairshaulk, an ancient sarrukh empire
