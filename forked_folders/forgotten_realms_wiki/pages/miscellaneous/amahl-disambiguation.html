Amahl (disambiguation)

"Amahl" was a male given name in Zakhara and Calimshan. Individuals named Amahl included:

  • Amahl Shoon I, the first ruler of the Shoon Dynasty of Tethyr,
  • Amahl Shoon II, the second ruler of the Shoon Dynasty of Tethyr,
  • Amahl Shoon III, the first emperor of the Shoon Imperium,
  • Amahl Shoon IV, the fifth emperor of the Shoon Imperium,
  • Amahl Shoon V, the sixth emperor of the Shoon Imperium,
  • Amahl Shoon VI, the seventh emperor of the Shoon Imperium,
  • Amahl Shoon VII, the thirteenth and final emperor of the Shoon Imperium, and
  • Amahl Abd al-Muhlis, a mamluk from I'tiraf.
