Mime

Mimes were a variety of entertainer in the Realms notable for seldom speaking.

Culture

In Faerûn, mimes could commonly be found in Waterdeep, and the city of Zhentil Keep. In Waterdeep, the majority of licensed mimes were members of the Council of Musicians, Instrument-Makers, & Choristers.

History

Mimes could be found as far back as the Empire of Netheril, where on the Karsus Enclave they were part of the League of Noted Singers and Performers.

Notable Mimes

  • Harlequin, a skilled mime and thief in Ravens Bluff who combined illusions and sleight of hand in his street performances.
  • Swen Sigirdson, a traveling entertainer in the Vast, sometimes worked as a mime after having picked up a costume secondhand.

Trivia

  • The Laughing Mime was an alias of the mysterious leader of the rock gnome kingdom Songfarla.
