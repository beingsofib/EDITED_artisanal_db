Forgotten God

The Forgotten God was a mysterious deity worshiped at a temple in the Spine of the World mountains.

Worshipers

Nothing was known about the Forgotten God except that his priests, referred to as acolytes, gained priestly powers from someone. Giant statues of a robed hooded figure stood outside the temple and another sat in meditation inside the entrance to the temple. This figure could be the Forgotten God, or someone else.
