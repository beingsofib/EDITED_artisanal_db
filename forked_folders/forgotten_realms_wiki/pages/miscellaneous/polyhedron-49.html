Polyhedron 49

Polyhedron Newszine #49 was released in September 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: The For-Rest Inn

By Mike Selinker. p. 28–30

The For-Rest Inn, an unusual establishment nestled in a garden in Raven's Bluff, is filled with whimsical and magical personalities.
