Dungeon magazine 64

Dungeon issue 64 was released in September/October of 1997. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Grotto of the Queen

By Paul Culotta and Shari Culotta, illustrated by Valerie Valusek, Cartography by Michael Scott. p. 8-23, 47

The Lantanese have lost their magical ship, but they aren't willing to fight for it. That's where your heroes come in. Module for 4-6 PC's of levels 6-9.
