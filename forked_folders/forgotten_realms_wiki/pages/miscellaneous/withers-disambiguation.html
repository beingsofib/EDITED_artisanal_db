Withers (disambiguation)

Wither(s) could refer to:

  • Withers, the undead entity interred within Jergal's temple;
  • Gorra, the custodian of the Tomb of the Nine Gods;
  • Wither, the reverse of the regenerate spell; or
  • Wither (Myrkulyte spell), the spell assocaited with the faithful of Myrkul.
