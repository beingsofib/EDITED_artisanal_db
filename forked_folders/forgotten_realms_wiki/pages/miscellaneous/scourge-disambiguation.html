Scourge (disambiguation)

Scourge might refer to:

  • Scourge, an exotic variety of whip
  • Scourge (city), a city in ancient Netheril
  • Scourge (sword), a magical sword wielded by Gondegal.
  • Scourge warlocks, a tradition among the warlock class.
  • Scourge (spell), a potent necromantic spell that inflicted disease on a target.
