Teeth (disambiguation)

"Teeth" was a popular name used for locations:

  • Auril's Teeth, the northern tundra and glaciers of Faerûn,
  • Dragon's Teeth, the strait between Koryo and Kozakura,
  • Isle of Jagged Teeth, an island in the Sea of Fallen Stars,
  • Omgar's Teeth, a maritime hazard near the Snout of Omgar in Samarach,
  • Small Teeth, a mountain range in Amn,
  • Sword's Teeth, the coastal cliffs of the Sword Coast,
  • The Teeth (Trackless Sea), a cluster of small islands in the Trackless Sea,
  • The Teeth (Great Sea), a group of islands in the Corsair Domains of Zakhara,
  • Twin Teeth, the towers of the South Gate of Luskan,
  • Vault of Gnashing Teeth, a cavern in the Underdark,
  • Vishap's Teeth, a set of four pyramids in Zakhara, and
  • Wood of Sharp Teeth, a large forest on the Sword Coast.

Some people and organizations used it in their names:

  • Hupert Three-teeth, a master thief of Delzimmer,
  • The Teeth (mercenaries), a mercenary company of ogres, and
  • The Teeth of Calaunt, the army of Calaunt.

Various items also used it for metaphorical or literal intent:

  • Belchorzh's Teeth, a pair of claw-like weapons,
  • Flail of Teeth, a magical flail,
  • Ring of teeth, a magical ring,
  • Teeth of Dahlver-Nar, a set of magical teeth,
  • Teeth of Firemane, a set of magical darts, and
  • Teeth Taker, a magical mace.

Some spells also used it in their names:

  • Cone of teeth, an aquatic spell that gave water a cutting edge,
  • Gauntlet of teeth, a spell that created whirling teeth, and
  • Ice dragon's teeth, a spell that produced a burst of sharp ice.
