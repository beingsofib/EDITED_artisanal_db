Karen Wynn Fonstad

Karen Wynn Fonstad was a geographer and cartographer known for designing the atlases of a number of fictional settings, including and D&D's and Forgotten Realms setting. She passed away in 2005, and was subsequently the subject of a New York Times obituary in 2025 as part of a series about interesting individuals who were “overlooked” at the time of their deaths.

Education

Fonstad earned her B.S. in Physical Therapy from the , but then changed fields and continued on to receive her Masters degree in cartography from the same university.

Career

Fonstad was the Director of Cartographic Services for the and also served on the City Planning Commission for Oshkosh.

She created many fictional atlases, notably The Forgotten Realms Atlas, which was published in 1990.
