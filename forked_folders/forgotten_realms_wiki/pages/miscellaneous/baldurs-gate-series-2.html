Baldur's Gate series

The Baldur's Gate series of computer games by BioWare Corp and the now-defunct Black Isle Studios have become some of the most respected computer roleplaying games to date. A trilogy of novels adapting the video games were not nearly as well received.

Games in the Baldur's Gate series

Baldur's Gate

The original, released in 1998. Based on 2nd Edition ruleset.

Baldur's Gate: Tales of the Sword Coast

An expansion pack for the original game.

Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn

The sequel to Baldur's Gate, released in 2000.

Baldur's Gate II: Throne of Bhaal

An expansion pack for Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn, adding a large amount of gameplay, released in 2001.

Baldur's Gate III: The Black Hound (Cancelled)

A cancelled game, initially planned to be released in 2004. It was revealed that the game had more plot connections with Icewind Dale II than with Baldur's Gate series.

Baldur's Gate: Enhanced Edition

A remake of the 1998 video game Baldur's Gate and its expansion. It was released in 2012. It uses the Baldur's Gate II engine as a base, and thus, it includes many of its classes and rules tweaks plus new party members.

Baldur's Gate II: Enhanced Edition

A remake of the 2000 video game Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn and its expansion. It was released in 2013. It includes the new party members introduced in Baldur's Gate: Enhanced Edition.

Baldur's Gate: Siege of Dragonspear

An expansion for Baldur's Gate: Enhanced Edition set between Baldur's Gate and Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn. It was released in 2016.

Baldur's Gate III

A distant sequel to II released in 2023. Developed by Larian Studios and based on 5th Edition ruleset.
