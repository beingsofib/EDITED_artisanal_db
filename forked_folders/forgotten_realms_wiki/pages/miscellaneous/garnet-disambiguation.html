Garnet (disambiguation)

Garnet might refer to:

  • Garnet (gem), a crystal known for being red.
  • Iriador Wintermist, a half-elven bard featured in the novel Elfsong who often went by the alias "Garnet."
  • Garnetallisar, a red dragon of Myth Drannor, nicknamed "Garnet".
