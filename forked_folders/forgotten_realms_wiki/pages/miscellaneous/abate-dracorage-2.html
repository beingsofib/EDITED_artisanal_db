Abate dracorage

Spell
nameAbate Dracorage
school3eAbjuration
level3eCleric 4 Sorcerer 4 Wizard 4

Abate dracorage was a spell that helped calm dracorage.

Effects

When cast, this spell restored calm and wisdom to a dragon in the midst of dracorage. It also prevented the dragon from returning into dracorage for a certain number of days, which was determined by the caster's skill level. This spell was capable of countering and dispelling aggravate dracorage.

Components

This spell required only a verbal and somatic component.

History

The spell was rediscovered by Karasendrieth during her research at the Monastery of the Yellow Rose.
