Dungeon magazine 87

Dungeon magazine 87 was the 87th issue of Dungeon magazine. It was released in July/August 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Raiders of Galath's Roost

By Skip Williams. Artwork by rk post. Cartography by Craig Zipse. p. 12-46

The secret to the raiders' sinister plot lies amid the ruins of a forlorn keep.

Summary

This is an adventure set in Mistledale using 3rd edition rules. The adventure focuses on the Zhentarim's attempt to cause havoc in the dale under the cover of the recent drow raids.
