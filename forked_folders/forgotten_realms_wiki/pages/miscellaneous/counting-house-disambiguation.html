Counting House (disambiguation)

Counting House can refer to several locations across the Realms:

  • Counting House of Athkatla in the late 14 century DR,
  • Counting House of Baldur's Gate, a bank, money exchange, and a financial institution in the Lower City,
  • Counting House, the guildhall headquarters of the Guild of Money-Lenders, Coinsmiths, and Pawn-Brokers of Calimport in the 14 century DR,
  • Counting house of Delzimmer in the late 15 century DR,
  • Counting House of Neverwinter that was located in the Warehouse District in the mid-14 century DR,
  • Counting House, a brothel in Samora, the capital of Azzagrat in the Abyss,
  • Inner Treasury & Counting House, one of the buildings that comprised the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz,
  • Wyrmhoard House, the counting house of Ravens Bluff in the late 15 century DR.
