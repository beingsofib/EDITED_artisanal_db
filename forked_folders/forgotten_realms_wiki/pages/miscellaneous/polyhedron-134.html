Polyhedron 134

Polyhedron Newszine #134 was released in January 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Wayfarers Guide to the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 8–9

Our tour of the Border Kingdoms examines Middle Mukshar, a land infamous in ballads as the Land of Slaughtered Knights.

Living City: Character Creation Guidelines

By Uncredited. p. 26-29

The updated rules for creating Living City characters.
