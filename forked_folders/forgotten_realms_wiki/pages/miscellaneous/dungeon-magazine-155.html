Dungeon magazine 155

Dungeon issue 155 was released in June of 2008 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Keep on the Shadowfell: Forgotten Realms® Conversion

By Greg Bilsland, illustrated by John Stanko, Cartography by Mike Schley. p. 4-17

Itching to start your first 4th Edition Forgotten Realms campaign? Wait no further! This article not only shows you where the Keep on the Shadowfell is in the Realms, but includes alternate plot information and bonus encounters.
