Dungeon magazine 128

Dungeon issue 128 was released in November of 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Vampires of Waterdeep: The Fireplace Level

By Eric L. Boyd, illustrated by Chad du Lac, Mark Sinclair, and Gala Ferriere, Cartography by Rob Lazzaretti. pp. 72–93

As the party faces an ancient foe who has never been defeated, their hunt threatens to unleash the buried ghosts of a forgotten feud between two of Waterdeep's powerful merchant houses. Part three of Vampires of Waterdeep. Module for 4 PC's of level 14, scaleable for levels 12–16.
