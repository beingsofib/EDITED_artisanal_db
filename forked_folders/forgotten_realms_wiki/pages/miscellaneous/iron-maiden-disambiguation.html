Iron maiden (disambiguation)

"Iron maiden" can refer to:

  • Iron maiden, a type of torture device.
  • Iron maiden, a spell that summoned helmed horrors.
  • The Iron Maiden, a Ravenian ship that was captained by Captain Stern.
  • The Iron Maidens, an all-female adventuring company.
