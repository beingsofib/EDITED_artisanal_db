Dungeon magazine 10

Dungeon issue 10 was released in March/April of 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Artisan's Tomb

By Matthew Maaske, illustrated by Richard Bennett, Cartography by Diesel. p. 9-11

In Oriental Adventures, honor can reach beyond the grave. Adventure for 4-8 PC's of levels 3-5.
