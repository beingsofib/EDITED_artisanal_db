Polyhedron 41

Polyhedron Newszine #41 was released in May, 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Wedding Party: An AD&D Oriental Game Adventure

By Robert Farnsworth, illustrated by Gary M. Williams. pp. 9–22

This AD&D module for six Oriental Adventures PCs is set in Shou Lung and involves an adventure to escort a princess to here wedding to a khan of the barbarian steppe tribes.

The Living City: Volodar's Stardust Inn

By Christopher J. Allen. pp. 23–26

A description of an inn in Raven's Bluff owned by a man named Volodar Celdonmen.

The New Rogues Gallery: Raven's Bluff Personalities

By Hubert Phillips II. pp. 27–31

Descriptions of various persons from Ravens Bluff: Baron Thoden, Lady Debbie Griffin, Master Ladeshar, Lord Paeon, and Maria.
