Swordpoint (disambiguation)

Swordpoint can refer to:

  • Swordpoint, a large stone fortress in the valley of Arkhen Vale.
  • Swordspoint, a neighborhood in the city of Ravens Bluff.
  • Swordpoint, a distinctive mountain peak in the desert region of Anauroch.
  • Swordpoint Streams, a set of rivers in the Border Kingdoms region.
