Helena

Helena might refer to the following individuals:

  • Helena Anchev, mother of Orin, a 15 century DR Bhaalspawn,
  • Helena Nostrum, a giantslayer from the Savage Frontier in the late 15 century DR,
  • Helena Treskeden, the leader of the Treskeden merchant coster in the 14 century DR,
  • Helena (Ravens Bluff), daughter of Beckwith, who went missing in the late 14 century DR,

or locations:

  • Helena Gap, a mountain pass in Narfell.
