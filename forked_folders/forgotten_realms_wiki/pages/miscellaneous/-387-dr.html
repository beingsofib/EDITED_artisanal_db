-387 DR

__NOTOC__

  • The Tethyrian barbarian clans unite and launch a surprise attack on Calimshan. They capture and loot Zazessovertan before moving on to the Calishite city of Zazesspur, which they sack and burn. Two sons of the pasha are killed. This begins a decade of overwhelming retaliation by the Calishites, and the First Age of Tethyr.
  • The forest gnomes of Yeven are discovered by elves from Semberholme.
