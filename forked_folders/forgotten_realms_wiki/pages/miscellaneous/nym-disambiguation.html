Nym (disambiguation)

Nym was not a unique name in the Realms, especially amongst the drow. It may refer to:

  • Nym, the drow shopkeeper
  • Nym Khalazza, the drow from Ust Natha
  • Nym (Icewind Dale), the drow trader in the Icewind Dale video game
  • Nym (monk), a half-elf monk of Ilmater
  • Nym (storm giant), the daughter of the storm giant King Hekaton
  • Father Nym, monk of the Monastery of the Yellow Rose
