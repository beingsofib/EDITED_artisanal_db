Hillforts

The Hillforts of Amn referred to:

  • Hillfort Ishla, the westernmost fort in the Small Teeth.
  • Hillfort Keshla, the easternmost fort in the eastern Tejarn Hills.
  • Hillfort Torbold, the central fort in the middle Tejarn Hills.
