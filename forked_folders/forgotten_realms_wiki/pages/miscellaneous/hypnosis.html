Hypnosis

Hypnosis might refer to:

  • A power used to influence the mind
  • Hynosis, a minor psionic devotion.
  • Hypnosis, a hishna spell from Maztica used to charm an individual
  • Hypnotism, an enchantment spell used to fascinate several targets
  • Hamring's mass hypnosis, an enchantment spell later known as mass suggestion
