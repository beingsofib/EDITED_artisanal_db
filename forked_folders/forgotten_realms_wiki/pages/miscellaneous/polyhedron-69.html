Polyhedron 69

Polyhedron Newszine #69 was released in March 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Sea of Fire - Part One: River of No Return

By Robert Farnsworth, illustrated by Stephen Schwartz. p. 7–14

Water becomes more valuable than gold. An Oriental Adventures adventure for 4-6 PCs, levels 7-9.

The Living City: Ye Olde Bluff Jeweler

By Alan Zak. p. 15–16

All that glitters can be found at Ye Olde Bluff Jeweler in Ravens Bluff.
