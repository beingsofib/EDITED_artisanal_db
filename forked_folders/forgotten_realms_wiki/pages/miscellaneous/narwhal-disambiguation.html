Narwhal (disambiguation)

Narwhal could refer to:

  • Narwhals, whales with large horns protruding from their heads,
  • the Narwhal, a galleon that sailed between Baldur's Gate and Chult,
  • the Narwhal, a caravel that sailed out of Skullport,
  • the Leaping Narwhal, a pirate ship, or
  • the Narwhal, a tavern in Skullport.
