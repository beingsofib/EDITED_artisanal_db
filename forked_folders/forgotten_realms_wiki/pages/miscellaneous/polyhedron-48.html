Polyhedron 48

Polyhedron Newszine #48 was released in July 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Spelljammer: AD&D Game Adventures in Space

By Jeff Grubb. p. 23

The Advanced Dungeons & Dragons game is taking off for the stars; A preview of the Spelljammer campaign setting.

The Living City: The Glow Shop

By Fran Hart. p. 28

The Glow Shop in Raven's Bluff carries candles, lamps, and a variety of other light sources to keep your player characters out of the dark.
