Skullcap (disambiguation)

"Skullcap" can refer to:

  • Skullcap, a type of helmet that was worn similarly to a cap.
  • Skullcap, a common form of cap that was worn across the Realms.
  • Skullcap, a type of mushroom.
  • Skullcap treat, a customary food item and gift in Turmish.
  • The top part of a skull.
