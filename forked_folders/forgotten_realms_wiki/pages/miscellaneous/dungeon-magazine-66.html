Dungeon magazine 66

Dungeon issue 66 was released in January/February of 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Operation Manta Ray

By Paul Culotta, illustrated by Stephen Daniele, Cartography by Diesel. p. 38-56

Immurk's Hold is home to thousands of pirates -- and one Sembian spy. Module for 4-6 PC's of levels 6-9.
