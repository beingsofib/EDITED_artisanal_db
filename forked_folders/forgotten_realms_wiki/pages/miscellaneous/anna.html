Anna

Anna is a common name in the Realms. This name could refer to:

  • Anna (Sword Coast), the person from the Sword Coast who loved the fisher Sy;
  • Anna (Zhentil Keep), the server from Zhentil Keep; or
  • Tatyana, the Barovian woman who was the target of obsession of Strahd von Zarovich.
