1348 DR

  • The armies of the Witch-King Zhengyi invade Damara, massacring all they encounter and triggering a conflict that will last almost a decade.
  • Battle of Six Smoking Shields: The village of Dunbridges successfully repels an attack by the armies of Thuntar.
  • King Rolf Olafsson of Gnarhelm dies.
  • Eleint 13, a recent rash of cow killings in Mossbridges lead villagers to accuse resident wizard Alice Clarkson of witchcraft and burn her at the stake for her alleged crimes.
  • Ri'ola'kirk, the future heir of the shalarin empire of As'arem, hatches.
  • In Cormyr, Azoun IV charters the Swords of Eveningstar. By the end of the year, Queen Filfaeril Obarskyr knights them and they are renamed the Knights of Myth Drannor. Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun gives the Pendant of Ashaba to them.
  • The tiefling Bysera makes a pact with the succubus Metynoma, becoming a warlock.
  • Erevis Cale is recruited into the Night Masks of Westgate.
  • Doust Sulwood is selected to be lord of Shadowdale.
  • After killing his predecessor, Selfaril becomes High Blade of Mulmaster.
  • Svenyird Olafsson becomes King of Gnarhelm.
  • A rieldoxia word crystal attributed to Exarch Thelasand IV of Jhaamdath is recovered from an excavation outside the village of Urml. Within are recorded images from the very beginning of the Procession of Justice.
  • In Cormyr, the Arcrown or "Iron War Crown" is rumored stolen.
  • The ballad "Another Night in Arabel" by Thumbard Voakriss, Minstrel Mighty, is published in broadsheet.
  • Dissensions of a Chessentan, a book written by Yarynous Whaelidon, is published.
