Dungeon magazine 51

Dungeon issue 51 was released in January/February of 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Last Oasis

By Peter Aberg, illustrated by Karl Waller. p. 56-70

When your shadow vanishes, it's too late! An Al-Qadim module for 4-6 PC's of levels 1-4.
