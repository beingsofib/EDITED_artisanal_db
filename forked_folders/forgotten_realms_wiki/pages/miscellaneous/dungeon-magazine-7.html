Dungeon magazine 7

Dungeon issue 7 was released in September/October of 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Samurai Steel

By Dan Salas, illustrated by Jim Holloway, Cartography by Diesel. p. 37-47

Treachery, betrayal, and a flower of ice. Oriental Adventures for 4-8 PC's of levels 3-5. This adventure is compatible with Swords of the Daimyo.
