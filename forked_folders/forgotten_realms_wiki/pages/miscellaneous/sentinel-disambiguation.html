Sentinel (disambiguation)

Sentinel might refer to a person or thing that serves as a sentry, guard, or watch, or it might refer to:

Novels

  • The Sentinel, a novel by Troy Denning featuring Kleef Kenric.
  • The Sentinels, a novel by Geno and R.A. Salvatore featuring Maimun and the Stone of Tymora.
  • Sentinelspire, a novel by Mark Sehestedt featuring the Fortress of Sentinelspire.

Locations

  • Sentinelspire (fortress), a volcano and fortress.

Items

  • Sentinel, a enchanted shield forged by Huffum Fuiruim.

People

  • Arunika, a succubus known as the "Forest Sentinel" of the Neverwinter Wood.
  • Bleucorundum, a blue dragon known as the "Sapphire Sentinel."
  • Miirym, a silver dragon known as the "Sentinel Wyrm."
  • "The Sentinel," the wizard tending the temple of Mystra in Elventree.

Classes

  • Druid, also known as Sentinel (Heroes of the Forgotten Kingdom).
  • Moonsea skysentinel, a prestige class described in Champions of Valor.
  • Sentinel of Bharrai, a prestige class described in the Book of Exalted Deeds.
  • Storm sentinel, a paragon path described in the Player's Handbook 2.
  • Union Sentinel, a prestige class described in the Epic Level Handbook.

Spells

  • Unceasing vigilance of the holy sentinel, an alteration spell.
