Dungeon magazine 60

Dungeon issue 60 was released in July/August of 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Nemesis

By Christopher Perkins, Illustrations and Cartography by Robert Lazzaretti. p. 32–58

Heads will roll if our heroes can't find a quartet of stolen swords. Come to think of it, heads will likely roll anyway. A Planescape module for 4–7 PCs of levels 9–12.
