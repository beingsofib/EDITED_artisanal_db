Polyhedron 65

Polyhedron Newszine #65 was released in November 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Holly's Mill

By Gregory Failing and Wayne Straiton. p. 6–11

Holly's Mill, an establishment to delight thirsty player characters, provides an assortment of NPCs for your Ravens Bluff Campaigns.

Oceans of Potions

By Network Members. p.12-21

The Newszine's potion contest yielded a myriad of magical mixtures for the AD&D game.

Ponderously Puzzling: Potion Plethora Predicament

By Rob Nicholls. p. 22

A plethora of potions causes problems for Ambassador Carrague of Ravens Bluff.

Bookwyrms: Read the Book! Play the Game!

By David Cook. p. 33

Improve your game with novel ideas; examples using Horselords, Crusade, and The Parched Sea.
