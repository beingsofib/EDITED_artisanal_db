Polyhedron 132

Polyhedron Newszine #132 was released in October 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The Border Kingdoms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 12–13

Our Volo and Elminster guided tour of the Border Kingdoms continues with a visit to Luthbaern.

City Stories: Mother's Cupboard

By James C. Alpeter. p. 14–15

Injured or feeling ill, but don't have the money for a Priest? Come to Mother's Cupboard in Ravens Bluff for some tender loving care.

Audition

By Tom Prusa. p. 20–29

The local thieves' guild of Clarkswell has become too powerful. Though they maintain a reputation of robbing the rich to give to the poor, the city council wants them eliminated. The Harpers secretly sends you to infiltrate the band and gather evidence to bring it down. An AD&D 2nd ed. Forgotten Realms adventure for 6 PCs, 7th-9th Level.
