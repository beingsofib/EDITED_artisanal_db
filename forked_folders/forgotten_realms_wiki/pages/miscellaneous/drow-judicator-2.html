Drow judicators were servants of the drow demigod Selvetarm, to whom they made an unholy pledge.

Entry

Judicators were spellcasters that survived the vicious rites necessary to work with demons and Lolth-warped spiders.

While at least some schooling in spellcasting was required for a drow to become a judicator, only the toughest and meanest sorcerers and wizards could survive the entry requirements.

Activities

They often served, albeit grudgingly, high-ranking priestesses of Lolth as defenders and assassins.

Abilities

As Selvetarm's power came from war, the lessons of such were the very first taught to judicators. This culminated in an especially powerful "war strike" they could attempt once per day.

Selvatarm granted some of his judicators the ability of command over spiders.

At higher levels, judicators were capable of another powerful attack that could be used once per day.
