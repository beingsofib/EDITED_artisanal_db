Polyhedron 140

Polyhedron Newszine #140 was released in January 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Living City Judge Guidelines

p. 6–11

Rules and tips for judging Living City events.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Wayfarers Guide to the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 12–13

Our ongoing look at the bucolic and sometimes surprising glories of the Border Kingdoms brings us this month to the coastal realm of Ondeeme, the "Echo of Calimshan."

Llurth Dreier: City of Ooze

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 14–21

Details of Llurth Dreier, one of the largest Drow settlements in the Underdark, located beneath the grasslands of Shaar.

Underdark Campaign Journal: Gateway to Ravens Deep

By Heather Van Buren. p. 22–26

A visit to Ravens Deep, the outpost of Lord Charles Blacktree's expedition into the Underdark of Ravens Bluff.
