The Hunter's Blades Trilogy

The Hunter's Blades Trilogy is the fifth series of novels about Drizzt Do'Urden written by R.A. Salvatore. It follows the Paths of Darkness series and precedes the Transitions series.

Brief Summary

The Hunter's Blades trilogy continues the tale of the famous drow Drizzt Do'Urden. When he and his companions travel back from their latest journey, a dwarf named Thibbledorf Pwent comes with a contingent of his kin and tells Drizzt's friend Bruenor Battlehammer that the last king of Mithral Hall has died, and that Bruenor must now serve as king. Meanwhile, a powerful orc named Obould Many-Arrows joins forces with Gerti Orelsdottr, princess of a clan of frost giants. The story passes through the war created by these two against Drizzt and his friends.
