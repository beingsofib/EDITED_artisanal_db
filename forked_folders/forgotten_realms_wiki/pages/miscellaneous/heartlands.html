Heartlands

The Heartlands may refer to one of several regions in Faerûn:

  • Eastern Heartlands, the various nations, independent city-states, and surrounding terrain around the Sea of Fallen Stars, or
  • Western Heartlands, the lands leading up to the western coast of the continent including the settlements and nations within, or
  • The Heartlands of Cormyr, the forested and heavily populated central region of the realm.
