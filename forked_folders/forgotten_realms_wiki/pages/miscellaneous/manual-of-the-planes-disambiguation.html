Manual of the Planes (disambiguation)

Manual of the Planes may refer to:

  • Manual of the Planes 1st edition (1987), the 1st edition Dungeons & Dragons supplemental sourcebook, by Jeff Grubb.
  • Manual of the Planes 3rd edition (2001), the 3rd edition revision of the core cosmology, written by Jeff Grubb, Bruce R. Cordell, and David Noonan.
  • Manual of the Planes 4th edition (2008), the 4th edition revision, written by Richard Baker, Rob Heinsoo, and James Wyatt.

During the 2nd edition of Dungeons & Dragons, the ' and ' campaign settings filled a similar role.
