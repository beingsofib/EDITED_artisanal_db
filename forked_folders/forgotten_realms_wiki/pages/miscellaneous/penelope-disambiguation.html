Penelope (disambiguation)

Penelope is a name that could refer to:

  • Penelope (Foaming Sands), the chatelaine and manager of the Foaming Sands in the late 14 century DR,
  • Penelope (Halruaa), daughter of Procopio of Halarahh in the late 14 century DR,
  • Penelope (Ravens Bluff), a scatterbrained cleric of Mystra in Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR,
  • Penelope Harpell, a member of the Harpell family and its most powerful member during the late 15 century DR,
  • Penelope Heartbow, a cleric of Chauntea from The House of Bountiful Harvest of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR,
  • Penelope Lightsbolt, an enchantress of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR,
  • Penelope Nilain, a renowned courtesan of Suzail in the late 15 century DR,
  • Penelope Norwood, a Chair of the Ministry of Art in Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR, or
  • Penelope Ringwarble, the author of Adopters of Culture.
