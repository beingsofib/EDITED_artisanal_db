Radiant dragon (disambiguation)

"Radiant dragon" was the name given to two distinct types of dragons. The term could refer to:

  • Radiant dragons, wildspace dragons also known as star dragons or celestial dragons,
  • Radiant dragons, planar dragons native to Mount Celestia, or
  • The Radiant Dragon, a novel set in Realmspace.

It could also refer to:

  • Solar dragons, which were known to some as radiant dragons.
