Polyhedron 79

Polyhedron Newszine #79 was released in January 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Everwinking Eye: Inside Thentia

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Larry Elmore. p. 7–8;30

Stroll into Thentia and take a look at the city's politics.

The Living City: The Painted Boat Restaurant

By Terence Kemper and Eric Kemper, illustrated by Larry Smith. p. 15–20;31

Something fishy is going on with the owners of The Painted Boat, a restaurant surrounded by the water of the Dragon Reach.
