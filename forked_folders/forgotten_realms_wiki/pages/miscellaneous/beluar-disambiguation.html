Beluar (disambiguation)

"Beluar" may refer to:

  • Beluar, the elven warrior of the Vast in the 7th century DR;
  • Beluar's Hunt, an area of hills named for him;
  • Beluar's Hunt (inn), an inn also named for him; or
  • Beluar's Tomb, his tomb in Sarbreenar.
