Polyhedron 54

Polyhedron Newszine #54 was released in July 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The New Rogues Gallery: Adagio Jones and The Goodwinds

By Tim Beach. p. 6-9

Meet the activist musicians and their 'mock music'.

The Everwinking Eye: Elminster's Guide to the Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting

By Ed Greenwood. p. 16–17

Kicking off a new series of articles, Elminster tells Ed Greenwood about Maskyr's Eye, beginning with the archmage and his tower.

The Living City: Lyle's Fine Cloaks

By Fran Hart. p. 27–29

A purveyor of quality cloaks in the city of Ravens Bluff.
