War Wizard (disambiguation)

War Wizard might refer to:

  • War Wizards, the organization of mages in Cormyr;
  • War wizard (kit), powerful elven spellcasters from the isle of Evermeet; or
  • War Wizard (club), a private club for mages in Cormyr.

The term should not be confused with:

  • War mages, unaffiliated spellcasters who focused on offensive magic.
