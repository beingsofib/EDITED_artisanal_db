Maskyr (disambiguation)

Maskyr may refer to:

  • Maskyr One-Eye, a famous archmage of the Vast;
  • Maskyr's Eye, the vale and village where he lived;
  • Maskyr's Tower, his tower in the vale; or
  • Maskyr's Trail, a nearby trail.
