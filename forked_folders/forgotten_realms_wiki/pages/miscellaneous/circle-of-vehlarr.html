Circle of Vehlarr

The Circle of Vehlarr is a magical portal that was constructed to allow transportation from the city of Vehlarr to the city of Myth Lharast. Myth Lharast had been protected with a magical lure by Syri Mhaal, and the werewolf Vehlarr, with the aid of the deity Sehanine, began construction on the portal in 712 DR, so that an attack on the city could be launched. It took eight years to complete and the attack was launched in 720 DR.
