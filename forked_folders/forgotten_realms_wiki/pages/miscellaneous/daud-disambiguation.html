Daud (disambiguation)

"Daud" was an Alzhedo male given name. Individuals named Daud included:

  • Bakkal Daud yn Khadil el Marekh, final ruler of the First Age of Calimshan,
  • Pasha Daud el Vehmet, a wizard controlling much of the spice trade in Wizard Ward of Calimport, and
  • Sultan Daud yn Daud el Vehmet, the pasha's son and ruler of Khanduq Ward in Calimport.

It could also refer to:

  • Lord Daud, chief military advisor to King Samakay of Golconda in Turmish.
