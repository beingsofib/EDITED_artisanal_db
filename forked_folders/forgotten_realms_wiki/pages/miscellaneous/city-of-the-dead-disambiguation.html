City of the Dead (disambiguation)

City of the Dead might refer to various in-universe locations:

  • City of the Dead, a ward of Waterdeep;
  • The City of the Dead, in the Fugue Plane formerly in Hades;
  • The Land of the Dead, the expansive burial grounds in Mulhorand;
  • Mictlatepec, nicknamed the City of the Dead; or
  • Ysawis, a city in the Grey Jungle of Zakhara, was called "City of the Dead" in its ruined state.

Out-of-universe it could also refer to:

  • City of the Dead, a novel that follows the exploits of Sophraea Carver.
