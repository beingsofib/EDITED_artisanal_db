Red Dragon (Herald)

In the Heralds of Faerûn organization, the office of Red Dragon was one of the High Heralds. The sign of this office was a rampant metallic-red dragon with wings unfurled and mouth open with teeth bared. Traditionally, this office was given to a young Herald Pursuivant who was a bit more radical and had fresh ideas for the future. Youthful enthusiasm led to Red Dragon playing the role of diplomat and matchmaker, arranging marriages between the hundreds of noble houses and helping create the coat-of-arms for their marriage shield and those of any children. Another tradition was that Red Dragon escorted supplicants, heirs, and honorees to ceremonies pertaining to the transfer or attainment of titles, positions, or awards of honor. The residence given to this office was a fortification called Dragonrise, located south of Easting near the headwaters of the southern tributary of the River Chionthar.

During the tenures of Lady Anaethe and Lady Bluthba, this office gained a reputation of decadence and ardent self-indulgence, but a successor (Bellym Glarsh) struck a different tone by being either less licentious or more cautious.

Possessions

The wooden rod of office had an exquisite carving of a dragon's head on top that could generate flaming spheres. The rod itself was enchanted to be impervious to fire.

Office Holders

  • Lady Anaethe (unknown)
  • Lady Bluthba (unknown)
  • Bellym Glarsh (circa 1368 DR)
