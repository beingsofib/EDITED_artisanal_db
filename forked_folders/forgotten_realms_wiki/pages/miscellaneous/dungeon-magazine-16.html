Dungeon magazine 16

Dungeon issue 16 was released in March/April of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Necropolis

By Nigel Findley, illustrated by Margaret Palmer, Cartography by Diesel. p. 38-42

In Impiltur, a city of the dead is coming back to life. An adventure for 2-4 PCs of levels 2-4.
