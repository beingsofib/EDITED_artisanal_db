Polyhedron 43

Polyhedron Newszine #43 was released in September 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Angel

By Vince Garcia. p. 24

This irascible character wanders the streets of Raven's Bluff. Be careful if he wanders into you.

The New Rogues Gallery: Hodgepodge

By Dr. Edward R. Friedlander, Costa Valhouli, and Stephen Wales. p. 25–30

A potpourri of characters fill this gallery's offerings.
