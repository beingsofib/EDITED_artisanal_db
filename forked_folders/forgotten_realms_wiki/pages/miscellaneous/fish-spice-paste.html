Fish-spice paste

Fish-spice paste was a type of condiment found in Faerûn.

Description

This condiment was made from fish and spices. It had a salty flavor and came in several varieties.

Availability

Elgath's Provisions in Archenbridge, sold fish-spice pastes to add flavor to their traveling rations, in addition to their hot sauces.
