Magefire ritual

A Magefire Ritual was a ritual.

Rite

Magefire rituals could be underwent multiple times, presumably only by mages, and involved both a number of other faithful. The mage in the ritual drifted at least for some of the rite; it may or may not have led to the mage learning new spells.

They were practiced in Neverwinter Wood.

History

In , Dethaera Matchlass underwent her first Magefire Ritual in the Neverwinter Wood. While she was drifting in its grip, Mystra suddenly became distressed, and Dethaera soared high in the air, while spell after spell entered her mind unbidden and painfully.
