Transmutation

The transmutation school of magic consisted of spells that changed the physical properties of some creature, thing, or condition. They might turn an enemy into a harmless creature, bolster the strength of an ally, make an object move at the caster's command, or enhance a creature's innate healing abilities to rapidly recover from injuries. The school was previously known as alteration. The transmutation school had no subschools. A wizard who specialized in transmutation was known as a transmuter.

Transmutation spells included bull's strength, cat's grace, bear's endurance, fox's cunning, owl's wisdom, eagle's splendor, and the various polymorph spells.
