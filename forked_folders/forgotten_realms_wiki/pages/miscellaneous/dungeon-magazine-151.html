Dungeon magazine 151

Dungeon issue 151 was released in October of 2007. It was the first issue to be published in an online only format.

Iggwilv's Legacy: The Lost Caverns of Tsojcanth

By Ari Marmell and Edward Albert, illustrated by Howard Lyon and Francis Tsai, cartography by Rob Lazzaretti.

This article is the first four chapters of a revised and expanded version of the classic 1-edition adventure for 3 edition. It is notable for including the only 3-edition statistics for the gorgimera, a creature found in the Realms.
