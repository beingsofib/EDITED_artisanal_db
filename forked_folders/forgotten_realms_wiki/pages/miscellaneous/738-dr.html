738 DR

__NOTOC__

  • High Mage Ederan Nharimlur and elven princess Elénaril give birth to their first daughter Amaara, who will later succeed her father as the first High Lady Mage of Silverymoon.
  • High King Alec Cymrych II of the Moonshae Isles dies of plague.
  • Pendar Cymrych becomes High King of the Moonshae Isles.
