Mystic (disambiguation)

Mystic might refer to:

  • Mystic, a special kind of priest from Faerûn with close connection to nature.
  • Mystic (Zakhara), a special kind of priest from Zakhara who gained insight through meditation and dance.
  • Mystic of Nog, a special kind of wizard from Zakhara who learned to store magical energy in their bodies.
  • Celestial mystic, a special kind of spellcaster who gained powers by contemplating the mysteries of Celestia.

Related terms:

  • Mystic theurge, a special kind of spellcaster who drew power from both divine and arcane sources.
  • Mystic fire knight, a member of an order of paladins devoted to Mystra.
