-1145 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Orcs attack the Rengarth settlement of Coldfoot. The Rengarth are victorious in the ensuing battle but Coldfoot itself suffers serious damage.
  • The blue dragon Lightningdance performs the first of 262 annual surprise attacks on the enclave of Palter.
  • The settlement of Frothwater is established at the confluence of several small streams and the Hungry River by several ranchers and homesteaders led by the ranger Samual Frothwater who value hard work and justice over the decadence and control of the enclaves.
  • An archaeological dig is established in the valley known as The Hollow when it is discovered that the first Stone Age humans lived there. Unfortunately for the archwizards who funded the construction of the camp—known as Holloway—no ancient magic is ever found.
  • A mosque dedicated to the treacherous aspect of Tyche is constructed in the Shade Enclave. The clergy and congregation are all self-centered and vengeful, kept together only by a mutual hatred of Tyche's temple in Imbrue.
