Polyhedron 84

Polyhedron Newszine #84 was released in June 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The New Rogues Gallery: Lord Speaker Mellisa Eldaren

By James Alan. p. 7

Meet Melissa, a druidess who is the new Lord Speaker of the Ravens Bluff Advisory Council.

The Living City: The Bandaged Wound

By Steven Kydd. p. 8–10

The Bandaged Wound caters to the wealthy and the poor, adventurers and common folk.

The Living City: Traagor's Tours and Souvenir Shop

By Halina Adamski. p. 11–13;31

Traagor's Tours and Souvenir Shop holds tunics, steins, and adventure! Lose yourself in Ravens Bluff on a special guided jaunt.

Living City Magic: Enchanted Items From Ambassador Carrague

By James P. Buchanan. p. 14

Ambassador Carrague's creations are just the ticket for player characters wanting new magic items for their treasure hordes.

Living City Character Generation

By Uncredited. p. 15-18

Follow these simple guidelines to create your own Living City character. Then get ready to play him or here in Living City tournaments near you; includes official Living City Character Record Sheet.

The Everwinking Eye: The Schemes of the Zhentarim

By Ed Greenwood. p. 19–20

The evil schemes of the Zhentarim find their way into neighboring lands. Learn about the wars and plans of Zhentil Keep's devious residents.
