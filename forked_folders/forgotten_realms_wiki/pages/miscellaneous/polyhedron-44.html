Polyhedron 44

Polyhedron Newszine #44 was released in November 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

A Few Monsters For The Living City

By Vince Garcia. p. 7–8

A few of the strange & unusual creatures in the lands around the living city.

The Living City: Open Air Farmers Market

By Joseph Wichmann. p. 23–25

Shop for an adventure in the Open Air Farmers Market (Ravens Bluff).
