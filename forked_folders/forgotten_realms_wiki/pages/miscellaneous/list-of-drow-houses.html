List of drow houses

The Darkening

The house ranks at the time of the Darkening in the :

  1. House Baenre
  2. House Barrison Del'Armgo; remainder consolidated into House Baenre in 1488 DR.
  3. House Faen Tlabbar
  4. House Mizzrym
  5. House Fey-Branche, remainder consolidated into House Baenre in 1488 DR.
  6. House Melarn, Subsequently demoted to eighth house for a failed attack on House Do'Urden before being destroyed in 1488 DR.
  7. House Vandree
  8. House Do'Urden Elevated to sixth house before becoming House Xorlarrin again in 1488 DR.

Other houses present as of 1488 DR were:

  • House Duskryn
  • House Druu'giir
  • House Hunzrin

A ninth seat on the Ruling Council of Eight was awarded to the Mistress of Arach-Tinilith, Sos'Umptu Baenre, in 1484 DR.
