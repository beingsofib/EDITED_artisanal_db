Telepathy (disambiguation)

The term Telepathy often referred to a unique ability to mentally converse and/or read the thoughts of another creature. It could also refer to:

  • Telepathy (discipline), the psionic discipline that revolved around powers of this nature.
  • Telepathy (psionic), a psionic power of the aforementioned discipline.
  • Telepathy (divine spell), a cleric spell.
  • Telepathy (arcane spell), a wizard spell.
