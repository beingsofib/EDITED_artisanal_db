Sasha (disambiguation)

"Sasha" was a common name found throughout the Realms. Individuals named "Sasha" include:

  • Sasha (cat), a surprisingly intelligent cat belonging to the wizard Thusk Tharmuil of Glister.
  • Sasha (Council of Ten), the youngest member of Phlan's Council of Ten in the mid-14 century DR.
  • Sasha (Daggerford), the proprietress of the Lizard's Gizzard in Daggerford in the late 15 century DR.
  • Sasha (firedrake), one of the firedrake companions of L'riyt, a spelljamming arcane.
  • Sasha (horse), the horse of Fyodor's, the companion and lover of Liriel Baenre in the 14 century DR.
  • Sasha (Rashemi), the wife of Rashemi berserker Borovazk in the late 14 century DR.
  • Sasha Baddja, a chambermaid from The Old Skull Inn in the Dalelands, active in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
  • Sasha (fighter), a member of the Bureau of Dark Tables adventuring party in the late 15 century DR.
