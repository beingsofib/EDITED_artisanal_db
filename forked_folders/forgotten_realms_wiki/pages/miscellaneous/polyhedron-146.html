Polyhedron 146

Polyhedron Newszine #146 was released in March 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Magic of Ravens Bluff

By Uncredited. p. 12–19

The recent Living City - Ravens Bluff conversion left us with a ton of ready-to-be-used Dungeons & Dragons magic items just sitting around taking up space.

Taverns: Gateways to Adventure

By Stephen Radney-MacFarland, illustrated by Vince Locke. p. 20–25

Tired of adventures that begin in taverns? Read up on historical taverns in this handy article to find out why what seems cliché is actually the ideal starting point. Don't believe us? Read on!

Teamwork: How to get it and how to use it

By Matthew Hancock, illustrated by Vince Locke. p. 38–40

Tired of charging into friendly fire? Do your fellow adventurers keep their magic items secret, so you have no idea whose backpack to search for healing potions when you're the last one standing? You're not alone

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Wayfarers Guide to the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 42–43

Enter the Owlhold, where twisting forests hide deadly secrets and even deadlier adversaries. There'll be no maps to guide you, so heed well the words of the Sage of Shadowdale.
