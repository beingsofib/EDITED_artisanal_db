Rurik (disambiguation)

Rurik may refer to:

  • A common given name for a male dwarf;
  • Captain Rurik of Winterglen, a human rebel active in Sarifal in the late 15 century DR;
  • Rurik Silverhilt, a dwarven merchant active in the Northdark during the mid-to-late 14 century DR;
  • Rurik, a dwarf living on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR (note that this character was cut from the final release of Baldur's Gate III).
