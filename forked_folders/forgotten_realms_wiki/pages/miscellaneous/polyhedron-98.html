Polyhedron 98

Polyhedron Newszine #98 was released in August 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ministry of Art: Ravens Bluff's Newest Government Branch

By Dave Gross. p. 6–7

The city's master spy gets the inside information on the newest branch of the Ravens Bluff government - straight from the wizard's mouth.

The Ambassador and the King

By Vince Garcia and Dave Gross. p. 8–9

This Living City duo touches the lives of many of Ravens Bluff's adventurers. Take a look into their pasts.

  • Ambassador Carrague
  • King the Dog

A Handful of Dust

By John D. Rateliff. p. 10–17

An errand for an alchemist is no easy task in this fast-paced Living City adventure for fleet-footed PCs. An AD&D Adventure for PCs Level 3-5.

Fellowship of the Sable Feather: An Adventuring Fellowship Takes Root in the Living City

By Kevin Melka, Cartography by Brian Blume. p. 18–22

Join the Ravens Bluff's adventurers' guild, an organization filled with interesting personalities and neverending opportunities.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: More Hidden Powers of Turmish

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Jim Holloway. p. 23–25

A look at some secret societies and religious sects of Turmish.
