Coram (disambiguation)

"Coram" was a common male given name in the Lands of Intrigue. Individuals named "Coram" include:

  • Coram the Warrior, the Calishite founder of Coramshan
  • King Coram I of Tethyr
  • King Coram II of Tethyr
  • King Coram III of Tethyr
  • King Coram IV of Tethyr
  • Prince Coram Rhindaun, the son of Queen Zaranda Star and King Haedrak Rhindaun III of Tethyr
  • Master Coram Wheelarit, a wheelright from Amn
