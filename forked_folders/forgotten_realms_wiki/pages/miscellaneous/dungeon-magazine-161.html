Dungeon magazine 161

Dungeon issue 161 was released in December of 2008 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Campaign Workbook: Ruins of Starmantle

By Bruce R. Cordell, illustrated by David Griffith, Cartography by Robert Lee. p. 114-118

Campaign Workbook returns with an encounter adapted from the Forgotten Realms novel Plague of Spells.
