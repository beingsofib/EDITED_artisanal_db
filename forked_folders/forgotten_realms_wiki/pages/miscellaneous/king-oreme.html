King Oreme

King Oreme was the name given to the ruling sarrukh lich of Oreme.

Organization

Sixty sarrukh liches rotated as the leader of the city. Every four years one would awaken and assume leadership, taking on the title King Oreme, while the remaining fifty nine liches hibernated.

Notable King Oreme

  • Arthindol.
