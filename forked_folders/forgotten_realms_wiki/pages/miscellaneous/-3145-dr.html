-3145 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Led by Ioulaum, the Netherese assault a horde of orcs by the southern Plain of Standing Stones. They exterminate the orcs and Netheril expands without restriction into the south and west.
  • One of the Rampant Peaks is renamed Thunder Peak thanks to the conflict between Netheril and the orcs.
