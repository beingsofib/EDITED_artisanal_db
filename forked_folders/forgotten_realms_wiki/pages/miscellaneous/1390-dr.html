1390 DR

The Wailing Years

  • Filfaeril Selzair Obarskyr, the dowager Dragon Queen of Cormyr, dies.
  • Following Filfaeril's funeral, Alusair Obarskyr is seen arguing briefly with her nephew, King Azoun V, in private, before storming from the court. She is not seen again for years, though there are rumors her riding through Cormyr's frontiers.
  • Arilyn Moonblade begins training Azariah Craulnober at the behest of the latter's father, Elaith Craulnober.
