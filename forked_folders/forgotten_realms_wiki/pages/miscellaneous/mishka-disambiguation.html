Mishka (disambiguation)

Mishka may refer to:

  • Mishka, a child that was abducted by a hag.
  • Mishka (worg), the matron of the worg pack domesticated by the Urshani.
  • Mishka Belview, a mongrelfolk that lived in Barovia.
  • Myshka, a cat that lived in Baldur's Gate.
