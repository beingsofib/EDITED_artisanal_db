Fire falcons were small raptors that could be found around the Battle of Bones.

Description

These falcons were small birds.

Behavior

Fire falcons rarely traveled alone. Instead, they preferred to gather in flights of 2–6 birds.

Combat

These birds favored swooping attacks on their prey, where they would launch small fiery spheres from their wingtips. All fire falcons were immune to fire-based attacks.

Ecology

Relationships

Some efreeti were known to domesticate fire falcons.

Trivia

  • The spell flight of Remnis could summon a fire falcon to the aid of its caster.
