1297 DR

  • Drizzt Do'Urden is born in Menzoberranzan to Malice Do'Urden and Zaknafein Do'Urden of House Do'Urden. Harnessing the power of his birth, Malice evokes a spell to destroy House DeVir.
  • Kelson Darktreader is born.
  • Thoss Balein, future Master of the Solemn Order of Recognized Furriers and Woolmen, is born.
  • Joadath, Lord of Shadowdale, slaughters Tyrists on Watcher's Knoll in his attempt to force his beliefs on his people.
  • In Menzoberranzan, House DeVir is attacked and destroyed by House Do'Urden, which becomes the Ninth House. This marks the beginning of the city's Sable Years.
  • The nobles of Yulash begin to fight viciously for control over the city.
  • King Rilimbrar of Impiltur weds Ilbritha Eirlthaun.
  • The dwarves of Ironfang Deep in the Alaoreum Mountains end their self-imposed isolation.
  • Nalfein Do'Urden
  • Ginafae DeVir
