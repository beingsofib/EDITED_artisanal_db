Al

Al may refer to:

  • Well-Adjusted Al, the proprietor of Well-Adjusted Al's General Store in Baldur's Gate in the 14 century DR,
  • Al Dimeneira, a powerful angel who once tried to defeat the power of Crenshinibon,
  • Al'Aif, a warrior of the Mtair Dhafir in the mid–14 century DR,
  • Al-Hyam Dhazid, a magic-user from the city of Phlan in 1340 DR,
  • Al-Amo, a castle located in the Seven Heavens,
  • Al & Tal's Slurp Syrup, a spiked libation from Waterdeep,
  • Or an abbreviation for alignment.
