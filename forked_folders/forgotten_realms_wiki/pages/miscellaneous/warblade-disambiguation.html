Warblade (disambiguation)

Warblade could refer to:

  • Warblade (greatsword), the greatsword that originated from the North
  • Warblade, one of the elfblades of Cormanthyr;
  • Willem Warblade, a bloodforge mage of the Utter East; or
  • Warblade (class), a blade magic class from Tome of Battle.
