Polyhedron 115

Polyhedron Newszine #115 was released in January 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The House of War

By Daniel S. Donnelly and Angelos Kaldis. p. 3–6

The temple of Tempus in Ravens Bluff.

Bantam Knights

By Wayne Straiton. p. 7

Ravens Bluff's Knights of the Golden Rooster.

Mercury Limited

By Paul Pederson. p. 8–11

A personal transportation service for Ravens Bluff.

Say, Aren't You...?

By Uncredited. p. 12

Fame points for Living City characters.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The Sage of Shadowdale returns!

By Ed Greenwood. p. 18–20

He's back! The Sage continues his descriptions of the Border Kingdoms.

Forgotten Deities: Lurue; Nobanion

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 21

Nobanion and Lurue, the lion and the unicorn.

The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited. p. 32

Deputy Mayor Holiday Resigns! Attention Focuses on Winter Festival, Sarbreenar Attacked!
