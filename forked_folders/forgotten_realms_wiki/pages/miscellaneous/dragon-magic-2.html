Dragon Magic is a sourcebook using the 3.5-edition ruleset in the core setting. It presents a variety of dragon-themed magical options and ideas.

Features

  • Nearly a hundred new spells
  • 7 new prestige classes
  • 10 new monsters
  • dozens of new feats and items
