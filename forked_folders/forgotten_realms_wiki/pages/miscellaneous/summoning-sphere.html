Summoning sphere

The Summoning sphere was a group of spells granted to clerics, mystics and shamans. These spells could bring aid in the form of creatures from distant places and planes, or transport the caster to safety.

Spells in the Summoning Sphere

Many spells were available to those who had chosen this sphere.

Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast:

  • call upon faith

Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast:

  • draw upon holy might
  • messenger

Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:

  • summon animal spirit

Intermediate divine casters had the ability to use:

  • abjure
  • animal summoning I
  • call woodland beings

Those with a high level of skill had access to:

  • animal summoning II
  • dimensional translocation
  • dispel evil/good

Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast:

  • aerial servant
  • animal summoning III
  • animate object
  • conjure animals
  • wall of thorns
  • weather summoning
  • word of recall

Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast:

  • conjure/dismiss earth elemental
  • creeping doom
  • exaction
  • gate
  • mind tracker
  • spirit of power
  • succor/call
