Ginger (disambiguation)

Ginger in the Realms could refer to:

  • Ginger, a flavorful aromatic root often used as spice.
  • Gingerbread, a type of sweet baked goods made with ginger.
  • Gingershroom, a species of fungi that grew beneath the Cloak Tower of Neverwinter.
  • Ginger Palace, the Shou trade embassy in the Eastern Heartlands.
  • Ginger Lady, a Shou trade caravel, active ind the mid–14 century DR.
  • Ginger-prince, the title of a prominent merchant from the Free Cities of Parsanic – Rishi Saubhari.
