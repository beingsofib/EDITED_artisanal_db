Polyhedron 120

Polyhedron Newszine #120 was released in June 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Forgotten Deities: Jergal

By Eric L. Boyd, illustrated by Phillip Robb. p. 6–7;31

Jergal, Lord of the End of Everything.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The Border Kingdoms: Felshroun

By Ed Greenwood. p. 8–10

The independent town of Felshroun.

The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited. p. 26

Dargon Haras attacked; Potions, Lotions, & Notions closes; and more.

Citizens of Ravens Bluff: The Lord Mayor Needs You!

By Uncredited. p. 30

Only you can help save the citizens of Ravens Bluff from an invading humanoid horde!.
