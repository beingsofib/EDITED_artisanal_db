Baldur's Gate (disambiguation)

Baldur's Gate is a city in the Western Heartlands and along the Sword Coast. The name might also refer to:

In universe

  • Baldur's Gate, the passageway that separated the Upper City from the Lower City.

Out of universe

  • Baldur's Gate series, the series of BioWare computer games;
  • Baldur's Gate (game), the first game in the series;
  • Baldur's Gate: Tales of the Sword Coast, that game's expansion;
  • Baldur's Gate (comic), the standalone comic released in 1998;
  • Baldur's Gate: Dark Alliance, a video game released on PlayStation 2, Xbox and GameCube;
  • Baldur's Gate (novel), the novelization of the original computer game by Philip Athans;
  • Baldur's Gate: Enhanced Edition, an updated version of the original game, made by Beamdog studios;
  • Baldur's Gate: The Black Pits, another game released by Beamdog;
  • Baldur's Gate: Descent into Avernus, the adventure module released in 2019; or
  • Baldur's Gate III, a video game released in 2023.
