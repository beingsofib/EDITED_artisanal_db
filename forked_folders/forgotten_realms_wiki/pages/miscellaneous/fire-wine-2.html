Fire Wine was a wine exported from the Old Empires. It was also sold through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Description

Crafted in the Old Empires, this thick, dark, almost black wine burned the belly with its rich spices.

Fire Wine was rumored to have a few medicinal properties.

Prices

Fire Wine could be purchased in the following containers:

  • Hand keg (38 cp)
  • Cask (18 sp)
  • Barrel (46 sp)
  • Butt (15 gp)
  • Tun (38 gp)
