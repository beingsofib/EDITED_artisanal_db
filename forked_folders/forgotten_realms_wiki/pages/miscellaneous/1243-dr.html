1243 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Anaglathos, a blue dragon, begins his rule of Turmish.
  • Vance Carrathal becomes King of Callidyrr in the Moonshae Isles after the death of his father.
  • King Cedric Carrathal of Callidyrr dies of natural causes.
  • Matasuuri Shogoro secretly arranges for ninja to assassinate the leader of the Makino clan, Makino Mansaku, bringing full control of the daimyos of the Makino–Matasuuri alliance to himself.
  • Matasuuri Shogoro begins the Summer March and engages in battle against an alliance of daimyo in the plains around the town of Tomo in Wa, a conflict that would last for nearly two years.
