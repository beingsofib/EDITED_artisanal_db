Tyranny of Dragons (disambiguation)

Tyranny of Dragons could refer to:

  • Tyranny of Dragons, the ongoing 5 edition storyline related to the Cult of the Dragon, or
  • Tyranny of Dragons (comic), the comic series detailing some events of that storyline, or
  • Adventurers League/Tyranny of Dragons, the series of Adventurers League adventure modules related to the storyline, or
  • Neverwinter: Tyranny of Dragons, the module for the Neverwinter video game that details other events in the storyline.
