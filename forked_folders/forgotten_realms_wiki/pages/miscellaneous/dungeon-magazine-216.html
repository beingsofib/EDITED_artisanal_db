Dungeon magazine 216

Dungeon issue 216 was released in July of 2013 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Sun Never Rises

By Shawn Merwin, illustrated by Beth Trott, Chippy, Chris Seaman, William O’Connor, Tyler Jacobson, Howard Lyon, and Emrah Elmasli, Cartography by Jason Engle. p. 37-55

Drow have taken hostages in the temple of the sun god, and it'll take more than diplomacy to win the day. This D&D adventure was originally run at Winter Fantasy 2012 as a tease for the Rise of the Underdark series of D&D Encounters seasons. Module for 6 PC's of level 1.
