Carrion Fleet

The Carrion Fleet was a fleet of ships sailed by fallen pirates in the Sea of Fallen Stars. A legend told that these were pirates who were strung up on their own ships and had returned as undead. The fleet was spotted many times off the coast of Thesk between the River Eth and Cape Dragonfang. Seeing the fleet was considered to be an omen of death in Thesk.
