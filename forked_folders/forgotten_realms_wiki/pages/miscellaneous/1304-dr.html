1304 DR

  • In Seros the storm giant hero Volar confronted the Blue Kraken at the Bymmal Trench. Neither hero nor villain had been seen since.
  • The city of Multum is destroyed by evil-aligned outsiders. The outsiders conjured a massive amount of water from the Elemental Plane of Water and submerged the city, drowning its residents.
  • Lord Kimball establishes Cantrev Graystone on Gwynneth, and the windswept plain on which it is located comes to be known as Kimball Moor.
