Rapier of derobement

The rapier of derobement was a unique enchanted rapier crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Jarlaxle in the late 15 century DR. It was one of many pieces of so-called "Silverhand gear" crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep.

Description

Silverhand awarded this weapon to Jarlaxle in honor of his "gift of candor". She claimed that the relatively simple decoration on the weapon represented Jarlaxle's inclination to overwhelm enemies not through brute force, but by stylishly ignoring them.

Powers

A flourish of this weapon had a chance to bestow a random magical protection on Jarlaxle, including boons that provided him with death wards, shields, life drain abilities, and others.

Notable Owners

  • Jarlaxle, the drow leader of Bregan D'aerthe and the secret High Captain of Luskan as of the late 15 century DR, to whom Silverhand also gave the seal of amusement.
