Long Road (disambiguation)

The Long Road may refer to:

  • Long Road, a road linking Mirabar and Waterdeep in northwest Faerûn;
  • Long Road (Underdark), a tunnel through the Underdark linking dwarf communities and the Great Rift; or
  • Long Road (Northeast Faerûn), a road from Damara to Nathoud.
  • Long Road to Riches, the Zhentarim trade route from Zhentil Keep to the Sword Coast.
