Errilam (disambiguation)

"Errilam" was a common male given name in Tethyr. Individuals named "Errilam" include:

  • King Errilam I of the Lions' Dynasty of Tethyr;
  • King Haedrak Rhindaun III of Tethyr, whose full name was Haedrak Errilam Alemander Olosar Lhorik Rhindaun;
  • Haedrak III's son, Coram Rhindaun, whose full name was Coram Haedrak Errilam Vander Silvam Rhindaun;
  • Errilam Krimmevol of Ithmong, one of the condemned traitors of the Interregnum Tribunal; and
  • King Errilam of Tethyr.
