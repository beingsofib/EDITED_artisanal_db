The staff of office was a unique staff wielded by the avatar of Thoth, the Lord of Magic within the Mulhorandi pantheon.

Powers

If used as a melee weapon the staff of office carried a stout +3 enchantment. Thoth could slay any target touched by the staff at will. Additionally, it combined all of the magical effects from a staff of command, staff of curing, staff of the magi, staff of striking, and a staff of power. If carried by Thoth his staff of office had unlimited charges with regards to the various magical abililites contained within the staff. Anyone besides Thoth who touched the staff of office would be instantly feebleminded.
