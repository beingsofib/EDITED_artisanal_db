High Old One

The High Old One was the title of the ranking priest of Dumathoin in a dwarven church or kingdom.

In Thunderholme, it was last held by Dagan. Another who held this rank was the dwarven archlich Bandaerl, who guarded the tomb of King Melair I in Undermountain.
