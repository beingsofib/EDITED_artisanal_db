Laebos

Laebos was the elder rune of fire.

Usage

The Laebos rune was marked to indicate a place of fire or where fire was commonly employed, from the use of firewood and kindling, to the occurrence of lava and molten rock, to the presence of fire-breathing or fire-using creatures. It warned a traveler to take proper precautions against flame and heat. An enchanted weapon with powers of light, fire, and warmth might also be marked with Laebos.{{Cite dungeon

Laebos referred to natural fire or extraplanar fire, not to heavenly fire, which was indicated by the Ingolth rune.

Halaster Blackcloak, the Mad Mage of Undermountain, placed elder runes imbued with magical power around the dungeon as traps and tricks for the unwary. The Laebos rune would either blast explorers with fire or make their weapons flaming.
