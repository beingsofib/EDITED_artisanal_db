Draconomicon (disambiguation)

Draconomicon is, or is similar to, the title of more than one book:

  • Draconomicon (1990), the 2nd edition book of dragons, a Forgotten Realms product.
  • Draconomicon: The Book of Dragons, the 3.5 edition book of dragons, a D&D base game product.
  • Draconomicon: Chromatic Dragons, the 4th edition base game accessory.
  • Draconomicon: Metallic Dragons, the 4th edition base game accessory.
