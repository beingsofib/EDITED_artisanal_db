Ander

Ander was a male name in the Realms. It may refer to:

  • Ander (Imnesvale), a commoner from the town of Imnesvale in the late 14 century DR,
  • Ander (Netheril), an archwizard from the ancient empire of Netheril, later, a lich,
  • Ander (Phandalin), a clerk who worked at Barthen's Provisions in the late 15 century DR,
  • Ander (Port Llast), the men responsible for werewolf attack investigations in 1372 DR,
  • Old Ander, an elderly eccentric from Daggerford in the late 15 century DR,
