1396 DR

  • King Azoun V of Cormyr marries Nalara Marliir the daughter of Lord High Marshal Dauneth Marliir and Krystin Lhal.
  • Nogah was stripped of her whip status when she started to preach about an ancient power revealed to her by the Dreamheart. When the kuo-toa of Olleth attempted to stop her, she summoned the kraken Gethshemeth.
  • Nogah was killed by poisoned crossbow bolts fired by aberrant kuo-toa.
