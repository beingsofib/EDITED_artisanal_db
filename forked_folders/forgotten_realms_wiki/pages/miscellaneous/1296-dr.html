1296 DR

  • Charles Oliver O'Kane is born in Teshendale.
  • King Benjamin Carrathal of Callidyrr in the Moonshae Isles is assassinated by nobles from Ogden.
  • Alec Carrathal IV becomes King of Callidyrr.
  • King Rilimbrar of Impiltur returns from exile and retakes the throne. Regent Kuskur remains in a self-imposed exile in Velprintalar after the death of his son, the usurper Prince Thaum, while his grandson Imphras IV is kept in house arrest in the Tower of Filur.
