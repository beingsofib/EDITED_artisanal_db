Otto (disambiguation)

Otto is a name that could refer to:

  • Otto (Greyhawk), a famed wizard from the wold of Oerth, known for such spells as Otto's irresistible dance.
  • Otto (half-orc), a powerful half-orc criminal from Arabel in the late 14 century DR.
  • Otto (Ravens Bluff), a rogue also known as the "Mouse" from the 14 century DR Ravens Bluff.
  • Otto (Scardale), a womanizing gallery master from Scardale in the mid-14 century DR.
  • Otto (spider), a giant yellow stuffed spider mounted in an inn in Shadowdale in the 14 century DR.
  • Otto (Thay), a Thayvian rogue who operated from Teziir in the late 14 century DR.
  • Otto Hirehook, a Lord of the Hirehook family of Amn in the late 14 century DR.
  • Otto Marvelius, a puppeteer entertainer circa 1368 DR.
  • Otto Ott, a prisoner in the Wyrm's Rock of Baldur's Gate in 1492 DR.
  • Otto Otturka, an obese quartermaster of Darkhold in the late 14 century DR.
  • Otto Thripid, a master chef from Ravens Bluff in there late 14 century DR.
  • Ottolenszillasla, or Otto for short, was a faerie dragon who dwelt within Undermountain in the 14 and 15 centuries DR.
