1072 DR

  • Govan Irfandel uses his affinity with the plants, vines, trees, and wildlife of the impenetrable Brush of Barbs to build the druidic Stone Circle of Govan. The circle was intended to be the meeting space, shelter, and ritual site for goodly druids.
