Polyhedron 56

Polyhedron Newszine #56 was released in November 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bookwyrms: Building Troy Denning's Dragonwall

By James Lowder. p. 18

James Lowder talks to Troy Denning and takes a look at Horselords and Dragonwall, the first two books in The Empires trilogy.

The Living City: Black Dugal's Music Shoppe

By Jim Dawson and John Miller. p. 24–25

A visit to Black Dugal's Music Shoppe in Ravens Bluff and browsing its high-quality and magical instruments.

The Everwinking Eye: Adventures in Maskyr's Eye

By Ed Greenwood. p. 26–27

The third part in an exploration of Maskyr's Eye, looking in detail at local culture and characters.
