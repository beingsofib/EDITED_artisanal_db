Dungeon magazine 195

Dungeon issue 195 was released in October of 2011 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

That Which Never Sleeps

By Daniel Marthaler, illustrated by Craig J. Spearing and Héctor Ortiz, Cartography by Mike Schley. p. 1-23

An adventure for characters of levels 3-5. Set in Neverwinter.

The Ecology of the Banderhobb

By Steve Townshend, illustrated by Howard Lyon and Jim Nelson. p. 29-35

This article provides details on the banderhobb race.

Eye on the Realms: Spellslayer Wine

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Héctor Ortiz. p. 36-38

This article provides an overview of the Spellslayer wine.
