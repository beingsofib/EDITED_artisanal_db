Ironlord

Ironlord was the title of the ruler of Earthfast, a dwarven city in the Earthfast Mountains of north Faerûn, in the Earthroot region of the Underdark

Powers

The ironlord was the sole leader of Earthfast, and it was a hereditary position. The ironlord led the army and maintained law and order.

History

In the early 8th century DR, the ironlord was Barundar mac Idrin.

In the late 1350s DR, the ruler was Ironlord Torg mac Cei, but he died in battle in 1360 DR.
