Wand of essential adaptation

Item
nameWand of Essential Adaptation
typeMagic wand
creatorLaeral Silverhand

The wand of essential adaptation was a unique wand crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Coriolis Rillevay in the late 15 century DR. It was one of many pieces of so-called "Silverhand gear" crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep.

Description

The Wand was hefty and crafted of metal.

Powers

The user of the wand of essential adaptation could use it to steal boons and other beneficial magical effects from a target and bestow those powers upon themselves. This property was inspired by Coriolis's natural talent for imitating the strengths of others.

Notable Owners

  • Coriolis Rillevay, a gnome sorcerer from Waterdeep who wielded wild magic, and for whom Silverhand also crafted the seal of invention.
