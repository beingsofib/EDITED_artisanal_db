Shadow (disambiguation)

Beyond its mundane usage, the term shadow could also refer to:

  • The Plane of Shadow, later known as the Shadowfell,
  • shadowstuff, the material of the Plane of Shadow,
  • a shadow creature (template), a kind of creature native to or tied to the Shadowfell,
  • creatures with the shadow origin, which are native to the Shadowfell,
  • the shadowborn, those creatures natively tied to the Shadowfell,
  • shadow, a specific type of incorporeal undead creature,
  • shadow dragons, a kind of planetouched dragon,
  • shadow demons, the hungry vestiges of demons,
  • the Shadow Weave, Shar's version of the Weave,
  • shadow magic, a school of magic in its own right,
  • a subschool of the school of Illusionary magic,
  • the shadow-cursed lands, an area twisted by Ketheric Thorm,
  • Shadowheart, a Sharran cleric,
  • Telamont Tanthul, who went by the moniker "Shadow" or "Lord Shadow" before the Fall of Netheril.
