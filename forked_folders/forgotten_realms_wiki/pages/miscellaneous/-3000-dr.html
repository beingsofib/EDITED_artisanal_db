-3000 DR

__NOTOC__

  • The drow city of Sshamath experienced a civil war. All matriarchs who worship Lolth are killed and the house wizards take power.
  • Kuo-toa establish Sloopdilmonpolop, the City of Pools, off the coast of Tethyr in the Underdark.
  • Migrating from the island of Ruathym Illuskan humans settled at the mouth of the River Mirar. They displace the Ice Hunter tribes that dwelt in the area and build the settlement of Illusk.
  • The couatls seek allies against the yuan-ti in the western end of the Chultan Peninsula. They go to another continent, lying far to the southwest of Faerûn, and preach about the god Ubtao to the native human tribes.
  • This year marks the end of The Founding Time and the beginning of the Age of Humanity, in which human civilizations in Faerûn commence a marked expansion and rise to dominance.
