Scar (disambiguation)

Scar could refer to:

  • The Scar, the great gorge after which Scardale was named
  • Scar Keep, the castle in Neverwinter
  • Scar Pilgrimage, the pilgrimage taken by the Order of Blue Flame
  • Scar, a traveling bard from the Dragon Reach in the late 14 century DR
  • Hurbold Duethkatha, the Flaming Fist commander who went by the name of Scar
