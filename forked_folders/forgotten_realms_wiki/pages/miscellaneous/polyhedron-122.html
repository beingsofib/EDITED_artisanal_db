Polyhedron 122

Polyhedron Newszine #122 was released in August 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Larger than life

By Uncredited. p. 4-5

Meet your Network staff, Scott, Robert, and Jeff. Our only thought, day and night, is to make your role-playing experience easier and more enjoyable.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: Gallard

By Ed Greenwood. p. 11–12

Elminster continues his tour of the Border Kingdoms of Faerûn with Gallard.

Living City: Sanguine Remembrances of Ravens Bluff

By David W. Baker. p. 13

Why Dave Baker is worked up about the world's largest shared-world campaign, the Living City.

Malatra, The Living Jungle

By Robert Farnsworth and Steve Jay. p. 15

All about the Network's demi-human campaign - the Living Jungle.

A World of Your Own: Islands in the Multiverse, an Overview - Part 1

By Roger E. Moore. p. 20–21

Roger Moore writes a world-building article that is raw idea fodder every month in A World of Your Own. Check him out and see if you don\'t come up with a few bright ideas of your own.
