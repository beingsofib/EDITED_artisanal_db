1327 DR

  • On the 12th of Marpenoth, the army of a renegade al-Badia sheik attacks Zakhara's Capital, Huzuz, and is annihilated by a great force of different genies called by Grand Caliph Bahamal al-Assad, discouraging open attacks on the city for generations.
  • Having trained for ten years at Menzoberranzan's Melee-Magthere, Drizzt Do'Urden becomes first graduate of his class.
  • The mad wizard Yarash occupies the silver pyramid on Sorcerer's Isle.
  • The Concise History of the Chosen Seven, a book written by Cirian, Master Chronicler of Candlekeep, is published.

Novels

  • Daughter of the Drow prelude.
