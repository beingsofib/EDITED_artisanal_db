Dungeon magazine 103

Dungeon issue 103 was released in October of 2003. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Forest of Blood

By Wil Upchurch, illustrated by Niklas Janssen, Cartography by Rob Lazzaretti. p. 40-58

While relaxing at the Berlingford Spring Faire, the heroes stop a pack of wild dogs from savaging innocent revellers. But these are just the latest in a long line of unnatural animal attacks. Investigation leads to a wide-ranging plot hatched in the depths of the treacherous Forest of Blood. Module for 4 PC's of levels 5.
