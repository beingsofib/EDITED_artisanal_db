Dungeon magazine 28

Dungeon issue 28 was released in March/April of 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Pipes of Doom

By Kristofer Wade, illustrated by Jeff Menges, Cartography by Diesel. pp. 8–19

"The forces of evil are ready to attack. But first, a musical interlude." Module for 5–7 PCs of levels 6–10.
