Cruel Nobility was a powerful and deadly magical scimitar from Eberron, found in the mid-1480s DR of Faerûn.

Description

The scimitar was a sharp, jagged black blade of darkness.

Powers

The blade was tyrannically axiomatic and unholy, tearing apart those of chaotic and good nature. It inflicted especially grievous wounds that bled relentlessly, and could even disintegrate foes with a lucky (or unlucky, from their perspective) hit.

History

The scimitar was forged for a master of the Lords of Dust, the noble rakshasa Tahmael. It may have been found by adventurers who destroyed Tahmael's cult in Stormreach while investigating the Spinner of Shadows.
