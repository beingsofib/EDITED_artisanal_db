Dungeon magazine 20

Dungeon issue 20 was released in November/December of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ship of Night

By Wolfgang Baur, illustrated by Dave Sutherland, Cartography by Diesel. p. 6-25

An engineering marvel awaits you, as does a legion of "ghosts". Module for 5-7 PCs of levels 7-9.
