Cauldron (disambiguation)

"Cauldron" could refer to the following:

  • Cauldron, the common cooking vessel,
  • The Cauldron, a bay in Chult,
  • Cauldron of Blue Fire, a lake within the Chasm,
  • Cauldron of doom, a necromantic cauldron,
  • Cauldron of the Reach, the waters near Thelve,
  • Cauldron of Sapphire, an adventure module, or
  • Cauldron (Wyrmbones), a part of the Wyrmbones.
