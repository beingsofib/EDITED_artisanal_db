Dungeon magazine 27

Dungeon issue 27 was released in January/February of 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bride For A Fox

By Craig Barrett, illustrated by Jim Holloway, Cartography by Diesel. p. 40–52

A daughter has been promised to a hu hsien. Oriental Adventures module for 4–6 PCs of levels 4–8.
