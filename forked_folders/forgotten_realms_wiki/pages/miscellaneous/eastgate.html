Eastgate

Eastgate or East Gate could refer to:

  • Eastgate (Arabel), a city gate in Arabel,
  • Eastgate (Suzail), a city gate in Suzail,
  • East Gate (Almorel), a city gate in Almorel,
  • East Gate (Bryn Shander), a city gate in Bryn Shander,
  • East Gate (Huzuz), a city gate in Huzuz,
  • East Gate (Westgate), a city gate in Westgate, or
  • Eastgate Street, a road that connected to the East Gate in Westgate.
