1313 DR

  • Dhusarra yr Fadila el Abhuk claims the title of Huntmistress in Wyllowwood, Undermountain. She has served Malar for only two years.
  • The black dragon Valraxaxath emerges from the Great Swamp of Rethild to terrorize the surrounding countryside for four full days before returning.
  • The Uthgardt Tree Ghost tribe breaks from the Blue Bear tribe.
  • After proving his above-average agility and hand–eye coordination to Matron Malice, Drizzt Do'Urden begins training his martial skills with Zaknafein.
