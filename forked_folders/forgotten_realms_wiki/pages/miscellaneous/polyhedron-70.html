Polyhedron 70

Polyhedron Newszine #70 was released in April 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Everwinking Eye: Elminster's Eversmoking Pipe Revealed

By Ed Greenwood. p. 6–7

The secrets of Elminster's pipe are revealed...well, some of them anyway.

Sea of Fire - Part Two: The Sea Of The Problem

By Robert Farnsworth. p. 8–14

A band of adventurers hoping to aid their drought-stricken land is tossed into another realm. An Oriental Adventures adventure for 4-6 PCs, levels 7-9.

The Living City: Signs Painted

By Joe Littrell, illustrated by Laura Kagawa. p. 15–16

Signs Painted, an artful business in Ravens Bluff run by a man with an unfortunate past, could lead to adventure - if the Zhentarim pay a visit.

The Living City: The Red Ravens

By James P. Buchanan. p. 23–27

The Red Ravens, Ravens Bluff's Volunteer Fire Department, can heat up your Forgotten Realms setting campaigns.
