Dungeon magazine 73

Dungeon issue 73 was released in March/April of 1999. It contained the following article related to the Forgotten Realms:

Mere of Dead Men: Eye of Myrkul

By Eric L. Boyd, illustrated by Stephen Daniele, Cartography by Stephen Daniele. p. 22-53

This adventure is the final part of the "Mere of Dead Men" series started in Dungeon #69 and involves retrieving the bones of a dracolich to save a paladin. The adventure is related to the Ring of Myrkul and involves an encounter with the sentinel of Uthtower.
