1494 DR

  • The Order of the Stout Half-Pint acquires the Silent Sound Lighthouse as a base of operations.
  • Hardyn Glorjarn takes over ownership of the office building shared by Demra Samdro's Best Beds, Erro Alhandrar's Nets, Laskalan's Lamps and Lanterns, and Tantra Jaressra's Fine Gowns. That same year, she moves into its uppermost floor.
