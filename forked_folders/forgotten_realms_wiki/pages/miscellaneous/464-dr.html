464 DR

  • While journeying across Anauroch, Arun's son rescues three Evereskan elves from an ambush by phaerimm but at the cost of his own life and that of his father, Arun Maerdrym. Mystra raises him from death and names him her Chosen, "he whom magic, duty, and honor defines."
