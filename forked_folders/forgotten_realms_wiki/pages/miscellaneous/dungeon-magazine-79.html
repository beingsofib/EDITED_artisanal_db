Contents

The Akriloth

By Matthew G. Adkins. Artwork by Terry Dykstra. Maps by Craig Zipse, Dennis Kauth and Robert Lazzaretti.

One year after Iakhovas's rampage across the Sea of Fallen Stars, the mer-city of Voalidru remains in the clutches of evil. Can you free the city from Qol'in'taroq?
