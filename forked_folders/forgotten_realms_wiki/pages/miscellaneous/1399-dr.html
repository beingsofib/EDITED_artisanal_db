1399 DR

  • Tsarra Chaadren, the Blackstaff of Waterdeep, as well as her heir and many members of the Moonstars are killed by a coven of vampire wizards in the Stump Bog.
  • Caladnei, Royal Magician of Cormyr, is struck by the expanding effects of the Spellplague in a heavily warded spellcasting chamber in Castle Obarskyr and is killed. The Dragonskull Chamber, with its wards warped so that anyone with spellcasting ability can't approach without becoming violently ill, is ordered abandoned.
  • Despite the risk to her sanity, Kyriani Agrivar takes the Blackstaff back to Waterdeep. With Open Lord Caladorn Cassalanter at her side, Kyriani proclaims herself the new Blackstaff from on top of Blackstaff Tower, beginning a tradition.
  • War Hero Ishual Karanok merges the different coinages of the city-states of Chessenta into a single currency.

Novels

  • Wrath of the Blue Lady begins.
