-394 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Cham Ko Hag, a mariner from T'u Lung, discovers the island of Wa, making the place known to the empires of Shou Lung and T'u Lung. He is granted an audience with Emperor Tsuba and promises to return later with gifts from Shou Lung.
  • The grand Whitewater bridge over the comparatively tiny Dahla River is completed.
