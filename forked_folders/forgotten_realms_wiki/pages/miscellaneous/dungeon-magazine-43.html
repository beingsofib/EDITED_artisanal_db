Dungeon magazine 43

Dungeon issue 43 was released in July/August of 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Into The Silver Realm

By Steve Kurtz, illustrated by David O. Miller, Cartography by Diesel. p. 52-72

Never, ever steal a silver sword. A 20 page adventure situated near Mirabar and in the Astral Plane intended for 4-8 neutral and good player characters of 8th-12th level (about 60 total levels). The DM will need access to The Complete Psionics Handbook.
