Polyhedron 42

Polyhedron Newszine #42 was released in July 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: The Crescent Moon

By Rollin Ehlenfeldt. p. 7;29

Is there a fortune in your future? Step into the Crescent Moon and Kisandra will reveal all.
