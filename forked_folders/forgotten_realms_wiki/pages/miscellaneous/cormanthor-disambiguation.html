Cormanthor (disambiguation)

Cormanthor might refer to:

  • Cormanthor, the forest, the woodland area in north Faerûn;
  • Cormanthyr, an ancient elven empire sometimes erroneously referred to as "Cormanthor";
  • Myth Drannor, an elven city once known as "Cormanthor";
  • Cormanthor, the "Old City" district of the Myth Drannor; and
  • Elminster's Ecologies, a boxed set containing a book on the forest titled Cormanthor.
