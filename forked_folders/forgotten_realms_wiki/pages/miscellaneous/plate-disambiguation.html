Plate (disambiguation)

Plate could refer to a few different things:

  • Plates used for serving food,
  • Darvoro's Plates, an engraving business in Khôltar,
  • Plate of Eels, a seafood restaurant in Hultail,
  • Plate-of-gold, a fried food dish from Amn,
  • Plate of Silver, a seafood dish from Purskul,
  • Chauntea's plate, a salad dish,
  • Plate spinners, used for performance tricks, or
  • Message plate, a magical communication device.

In armor terminology, plate could mean:

  • Plate armor, the general term for armor that used solid plates of metal,
  • Breastplate, the cuirass worn on the chest,
  • Half plate, a variety of partial plate armor that focused on the upper body,
  • Bronze plate mail, an unusual form of heavy armor made from bronze instead of steel,
  • Plate mail, heavy armor that used both plates and chain mail to cover the entire body,
  • Dwarven plate mail, a rare and excellent form of plate mail made by dwarves,
  • Field plate, the basic form of plate armor that covered the entire body,
  • Full plate, an advanced form of plate armor, sometimes known as coat-of-plate, or
  • Heavy plate, an extra-heavy variety of full plate worn by extremely strong humanoids.
