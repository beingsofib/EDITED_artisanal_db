Polyhedron 108

Polyhedron Newszine #108 was released in June 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Forgotten Deities: Sebek

By Eric L. Boyd, illustrated by Phillip Robb. p. 4

Sebek, Lord of the Werecrocodiles.

Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited. p. 5

Discount Merlin's House O'Critters To Open In March!

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The End of the Road in Turmish

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Chris Deegan. p. 6–8

The end of the road in Turmish.

Living Jungle Q&A

By Uncredited, illustrated by Jim Holloway. p. 13–14

Clarifications and corrections for Malatra: The Living Jungle.

Jungle Lore

By Kevin Melka, illustrated by Jim Holloway. p. 15–17

New proficiencies for Living Jungle characters.

Weeds of Wonder

By Greg Ferris. p. 28–29

A collection of useful Malatran plants.

  • Stikricki bush
  • Tiki
  • Gualla
  • Magasorium
  • Billitri
  • Biseechee
  • Manriki bush
  • Manshooki tree
