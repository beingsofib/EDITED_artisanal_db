Bright Star

The Bright Star was a lovelorn character from a myth in Sildëyuir, an elder elf whose love for the Far Traveler was parted by events for centuries, but in the end they found the way back to each other. A constellation in Sildëyuir's sky was named after her. Nangulis called Kiril Duskmourn, his love, by the pet name of Bright Star.
