Valashar

The name Valashar could refer to:

  • Valashar (kingdom), a short-lived realm, which was a part of the Shoon Empire
  • Valashar (county), the county of Tethyr, which was located roughly in the area of the previous kingdom
  • River Valashar, a river in Amn
  • Valashar's Bane, a Cormyrian ship named after the fallen kingdom.
