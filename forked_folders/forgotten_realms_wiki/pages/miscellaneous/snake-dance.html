Snake Dance

Snake Dance was an unusual martial art known in Wa.

Techniques

Considered a soft style, the practitioner struck with both hands and feet and focused on the opponent's vital areas,

causing severe pain, stunning, and paralysis. They also employed feints, deflected projectiles, and parried, locked, and even broke opponents' weapons.

Notable Practitioners

Naga, a yakuza in Aru in , was skilled in the Snake Dance style.
