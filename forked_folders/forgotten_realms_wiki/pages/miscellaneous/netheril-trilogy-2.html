Netheril trilogy

The Netheril trilogy was a series of books set in the Forgotten Realms during the age of the Netheril empire (circa -700 DR). The trilogy was written by Clayton Emery.

The main character is the barbarian Sunbright Steelshanks of the northern Rengarth tribe.

Netheril Trilogy

  1. Sword Play (May 1996)
  2. Dangerous Games (November 1996)
  3. Mortal Consequences (January 1998)
