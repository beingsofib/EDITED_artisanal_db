Temple District (disambiguation)

The Temple District was a common name for an area of a city that housed a number of temples or shrines. It could refer to the following areas:

  • The Temple District in the north-east section of Athkatla;
  • The Temples District in the Upper City of Baldur's Gate;
  • The Temple District, one of the wealthiest of Ravens Bluff; or
  • The Temple District, the black-roofed district for temples in Procampur.
