Shadowdale (disambiguation)

Shadowdale might refer to:

  • Shadowdale (adventure) (FRE1), the 1989 2nd-edition adventure by Ed Greenwood.
  • Shadowdale (novel), the 1989 novel in the Avatar series by Scott Ciencin.
