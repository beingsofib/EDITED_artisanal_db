Olembarlar

The Olembarlar was the leader of the armed forces of the city of Tulmon in the Lake of Steam region of south Faerûn.

Activities

An Olembarlar commanded Tulmon's standing army known as the Stone Spears.

Known Nessarchs

  • Grymmon Borlethgar was the Olembarlar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
