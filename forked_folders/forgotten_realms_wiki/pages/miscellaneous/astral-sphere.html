Astral sphere

The Astral sphere was a group of spells granted to clerics that allowed access to other planes of existence via the Astral Plane.

Spells in the Astral Sphere

Several spells were available to those who had chosen this sphere.

Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast:

  • astral celerity
  • speak with astral traveler

Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast:

  • astral awareness
  • ethereal barrier

Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:

  • astral window
  • etherealness

Intermediate clerics and druids had the ability to cast:

  • join with astral traveler

Those with a high level of skill had access to:

  • plane shift

Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast:

  • astral spell
