Polyhedron 66

Polyhedron Newszine #66 was released in December 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Oljagg's Rag and Bottle Shop

By Gary M. Williams, illustrated by Gary M. Williams. p. 7–10

Stroll into Oljagg's Rag and Bottle Shop in Ravens Bluff for an odd item or two.

Caravan

By James Ward. p. 15–21

Getting to Ravens Bluff is easy if you join a caravan with guards and fighters. Right? Adventure for 5-8 PCs of Levels 1-2. Includes 8 NPC guards.

The Everwinking Eye: Adventures in Mulmaster

By Ed Greenwood. p. 24–25

Watch your back and count your friends! Adventures in Mulmaster await.
