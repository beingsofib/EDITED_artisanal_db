Meatbread

Meatbread was a Sembian dish consisting of mincemeat and the salted, pre-boiled leftovers of slaughterhouses, all baked together into a loaf of wheat bread. Its taste profile was that of savory and mildly spiced flavor. Its mincemeat fillings typically included leeks, old sharp cheese, and the herb dill.

Availability

This was an affordable handfood in the city of Selgaunt in the 14 century DR, considered a staple among those who lived in the Foreign District.

History

In the , when visiting the city of Selgaunt after a long absence, a hungry Erevis Cale was provoked by his growling stomach to stop by a small bakery on his way to the Gilt Lizard. There he paid a baker two ravens for a day-old loaf of meatbread. He began snacking on the meatbread as he sat in the public plaza, waiting for a bell to pass. Cale remarked to himself that the dish's taste was just as inadequate as he had remembered it, since the last time he had ate it years prior, yet by the time he was finished with it he wished that he had bought two loaves.
