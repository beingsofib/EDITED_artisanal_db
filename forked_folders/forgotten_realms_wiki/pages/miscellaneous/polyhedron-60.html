Polyhedron 60

Polyhedron Newszine #60 was released in June 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bookwyrms: A Force For Good in the Realms

By Marlys Heeszel. p. 20;30

The Harpers, the mysterious do-gooders of Faerûn, are the subject of a new series of Realms setting novels: The Parched Sea, Elfshadow & Red Magic.

The Everwinking Eye: Who's Who in Mulmaster

By Ed Greenwood. p. 21–22

The creator of the Realms is not finished with Mulmaster. This issue he takes a look at who's who in the city.

The Living City: The Dancing Bear Inn

By Eric Kemper and Terence Kemper. p. 23–25

The Dancing Bear Inn in Ravens Bluff is filled with fine food, fast-paced entertainment, and quick-fingered thieves.

The New Rogues Gallery: Two Heads Are Better Than One

By Eric L. Boyd. p.28, 30

Having two heads on your shoulders keeps you from getting lonesome and always leads to a good conversation.
