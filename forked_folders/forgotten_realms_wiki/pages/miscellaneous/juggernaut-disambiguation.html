Juggernaut (disambiguation)

"Juggernaut" could refer to:

  • A juggernaut or stone juggernaut, a huge stone construct that charged and crushed foes;
  • The Juggernaut (Wormskull), a huge mechanical construct created by Wormskull in the Utter East;
  • The Shipwrack Juggernaut, a huge creature created from shipwrecks during Halaster's Higharvestide; or
  • The juggernaut spell that created a huge creature from earth.
