Akkabar

Akkabar was a common male Calishite given name. Individuals named Akkabar include:

  • Akkabar Shoon the Elder, the founder of the Shoon Imperium;
  • Akkabar Shoon the Younger, the grandson of Akkabar the Elder;
  • Akkabar el Souvlak, the head abbot of the House of St. Liwan's Blessing;
  • Akabar Bel Akash, a wizard of Turmish; and
  • the father of Sultan Abbas el Quaahl of Calimport.
