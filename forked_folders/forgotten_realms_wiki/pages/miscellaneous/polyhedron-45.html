Polyhedron 45

Polyhedron Newszine #45 was released in January 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: The Friendly Familiar Pet Shop

By James Lowder. p. 7

The Friendly Familiar Pet Shop is a shop in Raven's Bluff that is filled with lots of furry and feathered faces looking for a good home.
