Viper (disambiguation)

A viper was a kind of snake. The word could also refer to:

  • Viper, a lakshu warrior;
  • Black Viper, an assassin from Waterdeep;
  • The Vipers, a group of bandits;
  • A viper dagger, a magic blade; or
  • Syluné's viper, a spell.
