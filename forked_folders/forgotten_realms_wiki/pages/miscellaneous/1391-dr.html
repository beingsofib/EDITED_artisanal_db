1391 DR

The Wailing Years

  • Auril's Winter Hall assimilates the Winged Mother's floating palace into her domain.
  • Randal Morn, the legendary leader of Daggerdale dies.
  • Athror Baergil, brother of famous Captain Darrask Baergil of the Riders of Mistledale, dies, aged 90.
  • The elves of the militant Eldreth Veluuthra, seize control of the Hullack Forest to use it as a base for their anti-human operations.
  • The Eye of Justice drives Orbakh and the Night Masks from Westgate.
  • The druid circle in Cedarspoke is taken over by Zalaznar Crinios, a human turned treant. Firemane, a lesser druid who can turn into a lion, also becomes prominent.
  • Sathrin Cormaeril arrives in Daggerdale upon hearing of his uncle Randal Morn's death and claims the throne.
