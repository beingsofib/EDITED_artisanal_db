Zakharan calendar

According to Zakharan sages, the planet Al-Toril had 360 days in its year plus five High Holy Days. Each year had twelve 30-day months, which roughly matched the orbit of the planet's single moon. The five High Holy Days followed the month of Qawafil and preceded the month of Safa each year.

The months and High Holy Days of the Zakharan calendar year were broken down as follows:

Taraq : Hammer

Masta : Alturiak

Magarib : Ches

Gammam : Tarsakh

Mihla : Mirtul

Qawafil : Kythorn

Ahad, Atnen, Salas, Arba, Yasad (also called Ascension Day) : High Holy Days

Safa : Flamerule

Dar : Eleasis

Riyah : Eleint

Nau : Marpenoth

Rahat : Uktar

Saris : Nightal
