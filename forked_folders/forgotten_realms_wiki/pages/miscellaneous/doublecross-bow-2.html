The doublecross bow was a drow repeating light crossbow found between worlds in the mid-1480s DR.

Description

The crossbow had sensible wooden furniture with a top-loading bolt magazine. It was based on the same classic designs as drow hand crossbows, and used a nightshade or hemlock venom to incapacitate foes with a single hit.

Powers

The doublecross bow was magically enhanced to pierce through armor and cause crippling wounds in the target.

History

The crossbow, once of Faerûn's Underdark, emerged unexpectedly in the city of Stormreach in Eberron following the destruction of the Spinner's Prison in Khyber, when Lolth tore open a huge portal from the Demonweb Pits.
