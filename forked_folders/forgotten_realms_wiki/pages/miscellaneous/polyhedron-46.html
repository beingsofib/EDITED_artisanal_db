Polyhedron 46

Polyhedron Newszine #46 was released in March 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Skully's Bar and Bait

By Halina Adamski. p. 16–18

Skully's Bar and Bait in Raven's Bluff offers hot meals and food for eels. But it is not a place for PCs with weak stomachs.

The Living City: Embrol Sludge's Eatery and Shell Shoppe

By Randall Lemon. p. 19–22;30

Embrol Sludge's Eatery and Shell Shoppe is a dining establishment located near the wharfs in Raven's Bluff serves up fine food, a friendly atmosphere, and lets diners purchase jewelry while they wait.
