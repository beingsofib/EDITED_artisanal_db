Dungeon magazine 45

Dungeon issue 45 was released in January/February of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

An Artist's Errand

By Steve Kurtz, illustrated by L. A. Williams. p. 8–31

A reigar hires adventurers to retrieve a stolen necklace. This adventure is set in Pirtelspace and is suitable for 4–6 player characters of levels 6–8.

All Things Nice

By John Baichtal, illustrated by Ken Kosowicz, cartography by Diesel. p. 32–33,49

Some scoundrels can rob you with a smile. This Side Trek adventure is set on the Sword Coast and is for a party of 4–6 player characters of levels 2–4 (about 15 totals levels).
