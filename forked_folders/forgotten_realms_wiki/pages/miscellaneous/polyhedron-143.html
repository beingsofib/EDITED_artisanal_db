Polyhedron 143

Polyhedron Newszine #143 was released in August 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

A Funny Thing Happened On The Way To The Dungeon

By Matthew Hancock. p. 8–11

50 encounters, adventures, and oddities to make your journeys to and from adventures a little more eventful.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: A Wayfarers Guide to the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 29–30

Our survey of Ondeeme concludes with a quick overview of recent history.
