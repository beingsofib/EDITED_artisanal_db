1393 DR

The Wailing Years

  • King Azoun V of Cormyr makes a formal objection to the Dales Council in Archendale over Sembian investors purchasing large tracts of land in the southern Dalelands. His emissary is rebuffed.
  • Many people with spellscars and those seeking to get one start traveling to the Plaguewrought Lands. The Order of Blue Fire welcomes them in Ormpetarr.
