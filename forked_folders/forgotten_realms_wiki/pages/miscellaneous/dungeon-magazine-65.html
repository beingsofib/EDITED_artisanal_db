Dungeon magazine 65

Dungeon issue 65 was released in November/December of 1997. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Beast Within

By Paul Hamilton Beattie, Jr., illustrated by George Vrbanic, Cartography by Christopher Perkins. p. 66-69

A lonely cottage hides a dreadful secret. Module for 3-6 PC's of levels 1-3.
