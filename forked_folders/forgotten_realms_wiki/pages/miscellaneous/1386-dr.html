1386 DR

The Wailing Years

  • A meeting of resistance members against Szass Tam's takeover of Thay is attacked by a group of skeletons and an ulgurstasta under the control of one of the lich's minions. Though the undead force is defeated, the group's leader, Chon Vrael, is slain. Upon his dying request, he is raised as a sentient, intelligent mummy.
  • Parts of Abeir, a 'sibling world' of Toril, swap places with parts of Chondath and western Chessenta, causing catastrophe.
  • Wide expanses of the Sea of Fallen Stars seabed collapse into the Underdark. The resulting water level is lower than previously. Waters of the Vilhon Reach empty out into the lowered sea, revealing several Jhaamdathan ruins..
  • The Arnrock volcano in the Lake of Steam erupts violently, wiping out the halfling settlement that had sprung up on its long dormant crater.
  • The land of Luiren is flooded and destroyed by rising seas, transforming it into the Gulf of Luiren.
  • Genasi displaced from the land of Shyr on Abeir immediately begin establishing their own kingdom on Toril.
