1344 DR

  • Maethiira, wife of Piergeiron the Paladinson, Lord of Waterdeep, dies.
  • Elves begin the Retreat from Cormanthor and other elven nations, owing to the increasing human dominance of Faerûn. Most, but not all, migrate to Evermeet.
  • The last of the moon elves living in Ardeep Forest leave to heed the Retreat.
  • The first Thayan enclave is established in Procampur, with the concession of the small Thayan Quarter arranged by Samas Kul, Thay's Master of the Guild of Foreign Trade.
  • While apprehending a suspected smuggler in Marsember, a trio of War Wizards are defeated when the smuggler turns out to be a powerful mind flayer mage, claiming to have been hired by seven prominent and rebellious Cormyrean noble houses. Vangerdahast narrowly manages to avoid a civil war in Cormyr by careful investigation, identifying the handful of actual traitorous individuals in those houses, and separating them from those who merely wanted some political reforms.
  • Mintiper Moonsilver is said to have scattered the three Nether Scrolls that were being held in the Hall of Mists to other locations.
  • Falcon Fun was published by Edwin Narlok of Waterdeep.
  • A Tashlutan Traveler's Day-Thoughts, a book written by Caladrahan Mhelymbryn, is published.
