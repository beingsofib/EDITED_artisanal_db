Triel (disambiguation)

Triel may refer to:

  • Triel, a village in the Western Heartlands.
  • Triel Baenre, the matron mother of House Baenre.
  • Triel Teienna, a priestess of Lolth in Bezantur.
  • Alustriel, a faerie dragon who went by the nickname Triel.
  • Baalzebul, an archdevil who was formerly an archon known as Triel.
