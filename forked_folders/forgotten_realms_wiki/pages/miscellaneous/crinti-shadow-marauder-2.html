Crinti shadow marauders were a group of stealthy, mounted combatants in Dambrath who excelled in causing havoc and chaos when hunting their prey. They relished in the terror and uncertainty they instilled when attacking from the shadows. They would ride across the plains in groups as small as a handful or as large as a few hundred.

Abilities

They were capable of riding between shadows as if traveling through a dimension door spell.

Members

The aristocracy of Dambrath was the source of these marauders, and the majority of their ranks were found there. Members were almost exclusively Crinti in race, though a few non-Crinti were allowed to join their ranks as long as they had official approval of the nobility of Dambrath.
