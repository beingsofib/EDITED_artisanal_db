Create ibrandlin was a divine abjuration spell used by priests devoted to Ibrandul, enabling them to create a fearsome guardian.

Effects

This spell created an ibrandlin by magically changing fire lizard eggs, allowing the creature to be trained. Since the Time of Troubles the ibrandlin began breeding on their own, making this spell less and less common given its expensive spell components.

The caster needed to prepare themselves with a tenday of fasting before attempting the casting. A subject fire lizard egg was then coated with a special paste in a ritual lasting seven hours where the caster danced vigorously around the egg. The spell would fail if the caster became exhausted and could not be attempted again for another tenday. Two failed attempts on a single egg would kill the fire lizard within the egg. If successful, the hatched fire lizard would hatch a tenday later and be amenable to training from the caster.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components Ibrandulin holy water and 3,000 gp worth of diamond dust to cast.
