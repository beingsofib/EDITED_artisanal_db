1400 DR

  • The Alliance of Freesailors is formally disbanded, swelling ranks in the Cormyrean Imperial Navy.
  • Sembia becomes entirely absorbed by Netheril.
  • Krehlan Arunsun takes on the role of Blackstaff of Waterdeep.
  • After Bryseis Kakistos turns against Asmodeus, she gives the Old Staff to Caisys the Vicelord to hide it so Asmodeus cannot use Azuth's powers to the fullest.
  • Caisys the Vicelord hides the Old Staff in a cave of the Dead Stone Mountains, on the continent of Shyr.

Novels

  • The Devil You Know: Bryseis Kakistos's story, part nine.
