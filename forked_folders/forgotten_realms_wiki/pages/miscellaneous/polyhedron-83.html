Polyhedron 83

Polyhedron Newszine #83 was released in May 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Man Behind Drizzt: A Chat With Author R.A. Salvatore

By Erlene Mooney. p. 2

Join us for a friendly visit with R.A. Salvatore, author of The Dark Elf Trilogy and The Cleric Quintet. Mr. Salvatore discuses work in progress and the merits of softball, among other things.

The Living City: The Lord Thief-Taker

By David Cook. p. 7–11

The Lord Thief-Taker of Ravens Bluff is not one to take lightly - if you value your wealth or your neck. PCs crossing his path can survive, if they have pockets full of gold and the good sense to spend it.

The New Rogues Gallery: The Rat Catcher of Ravens Bluff

By Gary M. Williams. p. 23

Aldo the Rat Catcher and his kobold apprentices help keep the streets of Ravens Bluff free of (most) vermin.

The Everwinking Eye: Something Is Rotten At The Citadel Of The Raven

By Ed Greenwood. p. 29–31

The treacherous and cunning Zhentarim and the Citadel of the Raven are explored for your gaming pleasure.
