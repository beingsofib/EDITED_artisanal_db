Rahimat

"Rahimat" was a common male Alzhedo given name. Individuals named Rahimat included:

  • Rahimat yn Fahd el Taisar yi Almraiven, a tailor and member of the Knights of the Shield,
  • Rahimat yi Manshaka, an alias for Grand Yrshelem of Caravans Rafi al Shuree, and
  • Rahimat el Cajaan, the father of Ynamalik Nadim el Cajaan, conqueror of the Netherese city of Tzindylspar.
