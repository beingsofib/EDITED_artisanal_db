Anders (disambiguation)

Anders was the name of several individuals in the Realms, including:

  • Anders, the fallen paladin of Tyr;
  • Anders (halfling), the carpenter and caravaneer;
  • Anders Aricssen, the barbarian of Ravens Bluff; or
  • Anders Beltgarden, the wizard who created the Figurine of wondrous power named Guenhwyvar.
