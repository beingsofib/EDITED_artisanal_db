-230 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Calimshan recaptures the communities of Ithmong (formerly Calimaronn) and Artrimmar (formerly Myratma) and enslaves over a third of their populations—5,500 slaves in total. Many are are put to work on the Pasha's Palace, while the 1,480 warriors among them are forced to be gladiators in the Djen Arena.
