Ironguard

Ironguard could refer to:

  • Ironguard, a "stonedelve" tomb in the Stonelands,
  • Lesser ironguard, an abjuration spell,
  • Greater ironguard, a more powerful version of the spell, or
  • Tenfold ironguard, another more powerful version of the spell.
