Polyhedron 71

Polyhedron Newszine #71 was released in May 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Everwinking Eye: Beshaba, Tymora, and Xvim

By Ed Greenwood. p. 20–21

Three avatars are waiting to trouble the adventurers in your campaign. Beshaba, Tymora, and Xvim.
