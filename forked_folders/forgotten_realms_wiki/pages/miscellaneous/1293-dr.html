1293 DR

  • The sixth Emperor Chin of Shou Lung passes away from old age. For his faithful service to his ancestors, he is given the deific name Ch'uan, or Dog. He is succeeded by his son, the seventh Emperor Chin.
  • The Book of the Coast, a book written by Mespert of Baldur's Gate, is published.

Short Stories

  • The Halls of Stormweather
  • The Burning Chalice begins.
