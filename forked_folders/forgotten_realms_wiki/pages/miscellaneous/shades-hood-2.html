The shade's hood was a magical hood made for a shade of Thultanthar, found in the Wheloon Prison in the mid-1480s DR.

Description

The hood appeared to be a piece of simple black fabric headwear.

Powers

The shade's hood was imbued with shadowy energies that broadly protected the wearer from threats. Once per day it could partially transition the wearer partway between the Prime Material plane and Shadowfell, causing them to become somewhat insubstantial and much faster for a time.

History

The hood was captured during the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon when Cormyr's forces attacked the Netherese recruiting center of General Marthir.
