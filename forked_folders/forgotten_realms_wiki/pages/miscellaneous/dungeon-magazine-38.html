Dungeon magazine 38

Dungeon issue 38 was released in November/December of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

A Blight on the Land

By Richard Green, illustrated by Scott Rosema, Cartography by Diesel. p. 4–18

Monsters are prowling through the farmlands of Tethyr, people are starving, and the government is on the verge of collapse. But why? A 15-page adventure intended for four to six characters of levels 8-12. A party including a wizard and at least two warriors, is recommended; a ranger or any character with tracking proficiency would be useful.
