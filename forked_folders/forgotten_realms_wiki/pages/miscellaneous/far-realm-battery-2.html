A Far Realm battery was a device that could store energy―including raw magic―that had been extracted from the Far Realm.

Description

It resembled an amethyst crystal, set within an elongated cylinder crafted from glass and metal. They measured about long and in diameter.

When activated, it would emit a -long beam of light.

Powers

The Far Realm batteries could be used to power permanent portals, and other devices that utilized one or more forms of energy, including powerful explosives.

History

Some time during or after the , Red Wizards of Thay established a hizagkuur-reinforced Far Realm battery factory in the ruins of Xorvintroth, beneath the snowy wastes of Icewind Dale.

Notable Owners

  • Jaan'vald, a githyanki freedom fighter, once adapted a Far Realm battery with "mimic protoplasm". This created a mimic battery that could power just about any device to which it was attached.
