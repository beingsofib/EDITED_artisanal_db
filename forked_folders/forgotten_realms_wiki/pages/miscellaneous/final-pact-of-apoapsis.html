Final Pact of Apoapsis

The Final Pact of Apoapsis was a part of the compact that the Traitor had with the Abolethic Sovereignty. Upon his death, the aboleths would be granted access to the Prime Material Plane of Toril; this forced the Keepers of the Cerulean Sign to hold the Traitor alive in Stardeep instead of simply slaying him.

One of the Well-born avatars attempted to call upon a final pact prior to the Telarian Incident of , possibly this one as well.
