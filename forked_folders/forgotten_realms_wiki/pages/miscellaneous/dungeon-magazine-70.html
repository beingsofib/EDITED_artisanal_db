Dungeon magazine 70

Dungeon issue 70 was released in September/October of 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Ssscaly Thingsss

By Kent Ertman, illustrated by Terry Dykstra, Cartography by Diesel.. pp. 34–47

This adventure is part 2 of the "Mere of Dead Men" series and involves an exploration of the Mornhaven Towers and encounters with lizardfolk. Module for 5-7 PC's of levels 3-6.
