Polyhedron 90

Polyhedron Newszine #90 was released in December 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Marvelous MaGuffins

By the RPGA Network Clubs. p. 9–12

The winners are in, and the winners are you. The prizes are these wondrous magical items fit for any campaign.

Magic items: anklets of sure footing • Aslyferund's armor • bag of many bags • bag of teleportation • Browdow's ring of utmost weapon harm • enchanted leaves (leaf of his tree • leaf of pajon tree • leaf of convik tree • leaf of kemes tree • leaf of mistree) • loquacious lyre • money changer • ring of fearlessness • sticky shield • scimitar of the sands • Spider's boots of stealth • Tunnelrunner's axe • rod of equestrians • utensils of the cultured palate

The Living City: Master Etcheen's Chess Shop

By Brien J. Miller. p. 19

The master player is also a master craftsman residing in Ravens Bluff in Master Etcheen's Chess Shop.
