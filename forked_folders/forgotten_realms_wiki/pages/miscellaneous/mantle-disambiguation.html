Mantle (disambiguation)

Mantle may refer to:

  • Mantle, a series of caverns around the main cavern of Menzoberranzan;
  • Mantle stone of Vhyridaan, an ioun stone;
  • Mystra's Mantle, a Mystran temple in Haramettur;
  • Psionic mantle, a form of psionic power similar to divine domains;
  • Sky's Mantle, a prestigious tavern in Yhaunn;

The term "Mantle" was in many cases used in the names of enchanted clothing found across the Realms

  • ''Deathstalker mantle, an enchanted cloak worn by deathstalkers;
  • Earthen mantle, an enchanted cloak from the Underdark;
  • Mantle of Hell's Furnace, a cloak with fire-based enchantments;
  • Mantle of Kossuth, an enchanted cloak work by Kossuthans;
  • Mantle of spell resistance, a cloak with defensive enchantments;
  • Mantle of the dwarven commander, an old dwarven accessory;
  • Mantle of the Holy Warrior, an enchanted cloak that could cast crusader's mantle;
  • Nightsinger's mantle, an enchanted Sharran cloak;
  • Orbakh's bone mantle

The term "Mantle" may also be used to refer to a wide variety of defensive spells in the Realms, most typically mantle or spell mantle. Others included,

  • Alustriel's mantle, a defensive spell developed by Alustriel Silverhand;
  • Alustriel's improved mantle, an improvement upon the former spell;
  • Azuth's alteration mantle, an Azuthan defensive spell;
  • Blood mantle, a Garagathan protective spell;
  • Crusader's mantle, a general divine defensive spell;
  • Dragonflame mantle, a fire-based defensive spell;
  • Mind mantle, a defensive spell used by alhoons;
  • Greater mantle of Mystra, an improvement upon the eponymous spell;
  • Guardian mantle, a Dugmarenite defensive spell;
  • Ilyykur's mantle, a defensive spell developed by Ilyykur;
  • Improved mantle, an improvement of the mantle spell;
  • Improved mind mantle, an improvement of the mind mantle spell;
  • Mantle (Cormathan), a variation of mantle that was popular in Cormanthyr;
  • Mantle of Baravar, A Baravarian defensive spell;
  • Mantle of Mystra, a Mystran defensive spell;
  • Mantle of the slime lord, a Ghaunadauran defensive spell;
