Farsann

"Farsann" was an official title in the Kingdom of Tethyr. A farsann was responsible for managing the domestic staff of a noble house as well as for seeing to the planning and transpiring of feasts and ceremonies.

A farsann was an inferior position to a sammafar. They oversaw the work of the corlanns.
