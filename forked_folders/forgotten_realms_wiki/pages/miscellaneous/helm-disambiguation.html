Helm (disambiguation)

Helm may refer to:

  • Helm, the god of guardianship and protection.
  • Helm Dwarf-Friend, once the leader of Mirabar.
  • Helm Kormallis, the noble of Waterdeep.
  • Helm Stoneblade, the knight-turned-king of Athalantar.
  • A spelljamming helm, the magical chair with which a spelljamming vessel is powered.
  • A helm or helmet, a piece of armor worn on the head.
