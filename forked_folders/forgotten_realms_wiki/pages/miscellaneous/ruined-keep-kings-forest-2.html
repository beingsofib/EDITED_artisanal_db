The ruined keep was a former Purple Dragons outpost in the King's Forest of Cormyr, destroyed during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar.

Description

Post-destruction the keep was little more than a foundation, a few partial stone walls, and ballistae in disrepair.

History

The keep was originally built by Cormyr to control the magic that ran through that location. The drow fell upon the keep early in their surface invasion, destroying it and then moving on without realizing its worth. It was then taken over by a group of bandits that included many werewolves, who used it as a base while performing their raids. In response to this, the War Wizard Arganon Pine took a group of Purple Dragons and seized the ruins once more, along with adventurers sourced by Yenward Chorster. They protected Arganon while he performed a ritual to create a powerful ward over the area to keep away enemies. The counterattack was fierce, with bandits joined by werewolves after the sun set, including Claive the Carver and Bladeback. The defenders were able to hold them off and even repaired the fort's old ballistae for the defense, letting Arganon finish the ritual. Arganon then left to further investigate these werewolf bandits on the High Road.

Zenyff Bravehand took over from Arganon, but then had to deal with malevolent druids emerging out of the forest, who sought to take the magic for themselves and use it to control the surrounding area. Zenyff attempted another ritual that aimed to protect the area from their nature-based magics, just in time for another battle to take place. Facing off against the forces of nature—wolves, winter wolves, bears, mudmen, water elementals, and vine horrors led by a druid shapeshifted as a dire bear and the wood woad Gordy—the forces of Cormyr was successful, safeguarding the ruined keep and the magic within.
