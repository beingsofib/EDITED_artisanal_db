Polyhedron 159

Polyhedron Newszine #159 was released in July 2003. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Incursion: Knights of the Lich-Queen

By James Wyatt, illustrated by Attila Adorjany, Niklas Janssen, Vinod Rams, James Ryman, Derek Thompson, Marc Sasso, Wayne Reynolds. p. 5–39

Take the role of the enemy in the Knights of the Lich-Queen Mini-Game, in which you play a githyanki invading a Prime Material Plane world. The rest of Incursion assumes you're the good guys. We know you better than that.
