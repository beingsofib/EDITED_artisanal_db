Clovis (disambiguation)

"Clovis" was a male given name. Individuals named Clovis included:

  • Chief Clovis Ithal II, the second Clovis of Clan Ithal and father to the first king of the Ithal Dynasty,
  • Prince Clovis Ithal II, the second Clovis of the Ithal Dynasty,
  • Clovis Greenteeth, a shaman of the Gray Wolf tribe, and
  • Clovis, a citizen of Selgaunt.

It could also refer to the Plains of Clovis south of Darromar.
