Maztica (disambiguation)

Maztica may refer to:

  • The continent of Maztica far west of Faerûn, called the True World by its inhabitants.
  • The central part of the said continent, located between Anchorome and Lapongo.
  • Maztica (goddess), a mother goddess worshiped in the continent of Maztica.
  • Maztica Campaign Set, a 2nd edition sourcebox by Douglas Niles from 1991.
