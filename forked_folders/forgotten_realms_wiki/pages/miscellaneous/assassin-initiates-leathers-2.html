Assassin initiate's leathers were sets of enchanted light armor worn by servants of Shar, her shadar-kai, and rogues of Returned Netheril in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The leathers possessed powerful defensive +4 and +5 enchantments and also made the wearer's sneak attacks more powerful, much easier to land, and with armor-piercing properties.

History

In the late 15 century DR, assassin initiate's leathers were worn by the servants of Returned Netheril in their attempts to conquer Wheloon Prison in Cormyr, including the rescue of the Sharran priest Dedryk Black.
