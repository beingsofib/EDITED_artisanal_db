Dungeon magazine 101

Dungeon issue 101 was released in August of 2003. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Prison of the Firebringer

By Richard Baker, illustrated by Greg Staples and Peter Bergting, Cartography by Craig Zipse. p. 16-58

This massive Forgotten Realms adventure takes the characters on a quest in the Silver Marches. A powerful evil force, long ago imprisoned by a cabal of wizards, seeks its vengeance against the Realms. Module for 4 PC's of levels 13.
