Ada

Ada was a female name in the Realms. It may refer to:

  • Ada (dryad), a dryad seductress from Arborea,
  • Ada the Unbending, an aging adventuring cleric of Tyr in the late 14 century DR,
  • Mistress Ada, a ranking priestess of Talos from Athkatla in the late 14 century DR.
