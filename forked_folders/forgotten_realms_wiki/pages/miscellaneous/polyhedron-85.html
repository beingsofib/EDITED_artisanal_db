Polyhedron 85

Polyhedron Newszine #85 was released in July 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Mailing cover

Convention schedule and a notice from Lord Mayor Charles Oliver O'Kane of Ravens Bluff banning wild magic in the city.

The Everwinking Eye: The Pride of the North

By Ed Greenwood. p. 6–7

Life on the streets in Zhentil Keep is more placid than you might think.

Silverwood

By Mark Liberman. p. 8–20

The elves are growing restless; strange dreams haunt their sleep. Silverwood is calling and you must answer.
