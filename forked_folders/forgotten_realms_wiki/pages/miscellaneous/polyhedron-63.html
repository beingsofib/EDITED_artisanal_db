Polyhedron 63

Polyhedron Newszine #63 was released in September 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Everwinking Eye: Who's Who in Mulmaster

By Ed Greenwood. p. 7–8

Learn about archpriests, lesser clergy, merchants, and accommodations in Mulmaster.

The Circle of Swords: A Ravens Bluff Logic Puzzle

By Rob Nicholls, illustrated by Dave Zenz. p. 9

A clear mind will give you an edge as you tackle this logic puzzle.

Chemcheaux

By Dale "Slade" Henson. p. 23-29

If your character wants to buy or sell magic items - look no further. The Chemcheaux chain of magic shops stock nearly everything.
