Polyhedron 59

Polyhedron Newszine #59 was released in May 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: The Two Brothers' Butchery

By Eric Kemper and Terence Kemper. p. 7–9

The Two Brothers' Butchery in Ravens Bluff offers patrons meat, cheese, and other fine provender at good prices, but the business houses secrets even its employees don't know about.
