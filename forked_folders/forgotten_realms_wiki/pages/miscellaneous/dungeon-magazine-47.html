Dungeon magazine 47

Dungeon issue 47 was released in May/June of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Assassin Within

By Paul Culotta, illustrated by Karl Waller, Cartography by Diesel. p. 60-71

The god of knowledge wants Professor Hakim dead. Learn why, or face the final examination. An Al-Qadim module for 4-6 PC's of levels 3-5.
