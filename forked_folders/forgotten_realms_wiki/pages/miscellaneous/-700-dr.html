-700 DR

  • Circa: Psiarchs and monastic servants of the deity Auppenser of Jhaamdath begun helping slaves escape Calimshan and Tethyr. This continues for three centuries.
  • Circa: The orc realm of Vastar rises to power.
  • Augathra the Mad's visions become progressively darker as she begins to see the Black Chronology woven in-between the prophecies that help her write the Roll of Years. She eventually finishes her work on the Book of the Black and begins to wander Faerûn, being driven gradually insane by what she now sees.
