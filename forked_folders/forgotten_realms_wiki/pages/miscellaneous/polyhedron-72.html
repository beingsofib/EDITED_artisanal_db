Polyhedron 72

Polyhedron Newszine #72 was released in June 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Living City: Swimming Lessons

By Tim Beach. p. 6–7

A peace-loving trio of Ravens Bluff residents will have even the worst landlubbers swimming in no time.

Experience Preferred, Part One

By Tom Prusa. p. 11-20

Join the Company of the Swan, a group of veterans well into their golden years, as they strive to keep an old friend from hurting himself—and a lot of other folks.

The Everwinking Eye: The Tears of the Dragon

By Ed Greenwood. p. 29–30

The Realms creator explains why a certain dragon's tears are precious and re-introduces adventurers to the mysteries of The Vast.
