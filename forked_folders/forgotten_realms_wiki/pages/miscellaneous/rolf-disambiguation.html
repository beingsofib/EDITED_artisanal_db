Rolf (disambiguation)

  • Rolf (smuggler), a smuggler in Ravens Bluff;
  • Rolf (wizard), a Halruaan wizard;

  • Rolf Trygveson, a notable inhabitant of Hawa; or
  • Hrolf the Unruly, captain of the Elfmaid.
