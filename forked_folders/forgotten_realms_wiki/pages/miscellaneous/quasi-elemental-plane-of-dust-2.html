Quasi-Elemental Plane of Dust

The Quasi-Elemental Plane of Dust was the Inner Plane at the intersection of the Elemental Plane of Earth and the Negative Energy Plane. It was a place of wind, sand, and grit, where living creatures ever decayed to dust.
