Dungeon magazine 129

Dungeon issue 129 was released in December of 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Twisted Run

By Wil Upchurch, illustrated by Eric Deschamps and Doug Williams, Cartography by Rob Lazzaretti. p. 70-88

The Beastlord Malar has had enough of the people of the Silver Marches trying to "civilize" the Northlands. Now, with the help of a powerful new weapon, his followers are set to cleanse the stain of Silverymoon from the land. Module for 4 PC's of levels 13-20.
