Garth

Garth is a common name in the Realms and can refer to:

  • Garth (Daggerford), son of the owners of the River Shining Tavern,
  • Garth the Gimble, a rogue of Sembia and Cormyr,
  • Garth of Suzail, author of An Ecology of the Planes, or
  • Garth who sired Athan and Ghleanna Stormlake.
