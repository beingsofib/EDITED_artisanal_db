Dungeon magazine 218

Dungeon issue 218 was released in September of 2013 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Dawn of Night

By Shawn Merwin, illustrated by Jason Juta, Francis Tsai, Wayne Reynolds, Eva Widermann, Scott Altmann, Tomás Giorello, and Chris Seaman, Cartography by Jason Engle. p. 61-75

As Lolth conspires to take Mystra’s place as the god of magic, a band of drow adventurers do their small part to ensure their place in the new world order. Module for 6 PC's of level 1.
