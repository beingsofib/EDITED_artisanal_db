Helm of disguise

Item
nameHelm of Disguise
typeMagical helmet

A Helm of Disguise was a helmet which disguised its user for many purposes.

Description

This helmet had the ability to give its wearer a different appearance, in order to conceal their true identity. This could change not only facial appearance, but also race, among other qualities.

Notable Owners

  • Victor Dhostar - A night mask who stole the helmet from the goddess, Leira.
  • An adventuring party from Icewind Dale may have found a helm of disguise in a lair of leucrottas. They may have used it to disguise the identity of elves from their drow enemies.
