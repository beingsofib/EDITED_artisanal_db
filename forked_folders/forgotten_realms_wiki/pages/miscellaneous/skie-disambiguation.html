Skie (disambiguation)

Skie was the name for an electrum coin from Halruaa.

It was also a feminine given name. Individuals named "Skie" include:

  • Skie, a young woman from the village of Toralth and
  • Skie Silvershield, a noblewoman from Baldur's Gate.
