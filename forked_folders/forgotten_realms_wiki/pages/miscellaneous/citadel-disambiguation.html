Citadel (disambiguation)

The Citadel could refer to numerous in-universe locales:

  • Citadel, the fortress-complex of Thaymount in Thay;
  • The Citadel (Arabel), a fortress within the city of Arabel;
  • The Citadel, the dwarven citadel in the Tears of Selûne; or
  • The Central Citadel, the fortress in Bezantur.

It could also refer to:

  • The Citadels, a four-part novel series written by various authors.
