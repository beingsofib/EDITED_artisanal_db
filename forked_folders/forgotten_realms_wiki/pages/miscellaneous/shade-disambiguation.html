Shade (disambiguation)

Shade could refer to several concepts, many of which related to the Shadovar people who were brought to Faerûn during the Return:

  • The Netherese enclave of Thultanthar, also known as Shade or the City of Shade,
  • The Empire of Shade, the greater holdings of Thultanthar once it conquered parts of Faerûn,
  • the Princes of Shade, the ruling family of the domain, made up of Telamont Tanthul and his sons,
  • shades, shadowstuff-infused mortals who comprised the elite of Thultanthar's empire,
  • the general population of Thultanthar's empire such as the Shadovar, shadar-kai, and krinth, simply called shades as a whole as well, and
  • shade hunters, treasure hunters who specialized in looting Netherese ruins from underneath the shades of Thultanthar.

Other uses of shade related more broadly to shadow magic and living shadows:

  • The Shade's Veil, an ancient Imaskari artifact,
  • Shadevar, ancient shadowy beings present at the dawn of creation,
  • the Shade Lord, a powerful living shadow,
  • various creatures related to living shadows in general, such as shade wolves,
  • shades, an illusion spell created by Lord Shadow that produced quasi-real imitation conjurations,
  • create shade, a Zakharan spell that produced a patch of comfortable shadow,
  • mirror shades, a kind of spectral undead that lurked in mirrors,
  • Aurgloroasa, a shadow dragon also known as the Sibilant Shade, and
  • King Kymer of Tethyr, known as the Shade King for his mundane skulduggery.
