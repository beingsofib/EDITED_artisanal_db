1325 DR

  • The Lords' Alliance is founded in Waterdeep. Composed of the cities and settlements of the Sword Coast North, its aim is to oppose Amn's aggressive mercantile activities and prosper from the North's growing trade to the Heartlands.
  • Beers and wines made in this year become famous.
  • Colin O'Lyre is born in the Crow's End district of Ravens Bluff.
