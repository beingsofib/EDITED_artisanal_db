Halruaan

"Halruaan" was a demonym for the people of Halruaa. The name could also refer to:

  • the Halruaan language
  • the Halruaan ethnicity
  • a Halruaan Skyship
  • Halruaan elder, a prestige class
  • the Halruaan Consumption, a wasting disease
  • a variant of mountain troll
