Dungeon magazine 121

Dungeon issue 121 was released in April of 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Secrets of the Arch Wood

By Skip Williams, illustrated by Jeff Carlisle, Cartography by Rob Lazzaretti. p. 66-79

A few years ago, the swords of Archendale went ahead with plans to log a section of the Arch Wood near their borders. Yet only a year later, the logging stopped abruptly. Archendale's rulers did not reveal a reason, but that reason is about to make itself known. Module for 4 PC's of levels 13-20.
