The term Al-Badia () referred to the nomads comprising the wandering tribes of Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Activities

These people were driven by the constant need for food, water, and suitable land for their livestock.  Life wandering the harsh lands of Zakhara transformed Al-Badia into a hearty people, most being able to not only survive, but thrive on meager sustenance.  Poor by most common standards, Al-Badia considered themselves to be the richest people throughout the Land of Fate because of their independence and freedom which they cherished above everything else.
