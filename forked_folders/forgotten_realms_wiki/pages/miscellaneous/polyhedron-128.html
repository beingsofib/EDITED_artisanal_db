Polyhedron 128

Polyhedron Newszine #128 was released in February 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The Border Kingdoms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 6–7

A look at the quiet village of Irl in the Forgotten Realms.

City Stories

By Bob Nichols. p. 8–11

The Silver Halls: Tyr's Temple in Ravens Bluff. A Living City feature.
