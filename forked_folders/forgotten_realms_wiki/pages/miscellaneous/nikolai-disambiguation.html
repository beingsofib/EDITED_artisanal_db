Nikolai (disambiguation)

"Nikolai" was a common name found throughout the Realms. Individuals named "Nikolai" include:

  • Nikolai, the historical guide of Baldur's Gate; and
  • Nikolai Nikopolis, a member of the Unclean from the Haunted Lands.
