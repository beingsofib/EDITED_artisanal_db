Khazark

A khazark was a title given to those Red Wizards of Thay who served as leaders of the nation's foreign enclaves. They held roughly the same authority as the autharchs of Thayan cities, but were held in high regard due to their membership within the Red Wizards.

Notable members

  • Tola Vrass, khazark of the enclave in Hillsfar after the year 1373 DR
  • Chathin Zurn, khazark of Proskur's enclave (c. 1372 DR)
