Captain's helmet

Item
nameCaptain's Helmet
typeMagic helmet
creatorLaeral Silverhand

The Captain's helmet was a unique enchanted helmet crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Tommus Tallstep in the late 15 century DR. It was one of many pieces of so-called "Silverhand gear" crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep.

Description

Silverhand crafted the helmet to complement and empower Tommus's desire to apprehend his adversaries and prevent them from fleeing.

Powers

The captain's helmet gave Tommus the power to command his foes to approach him.

Notable Owners

  • Tommus Tallstep, a halfling fighter to whom Silverhand also gifted the axe of great cleaving and the seal of patience.
