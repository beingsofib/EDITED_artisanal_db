Polyhedron 117

Polyhedron Newszine #117 was released in March 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Incantatrix

By Eric L. Boyd and Ed Greenwood. p. 3–8

The Incantatrix, a new type of mage for the Forgotten Realms setting. Based on an article by Ed Greenwood in Dragon #90.

Larger than Life

By Steve Miller. p. 9–10

The Incantatrix Verity Shanae.

The Citadel of Protection

By Rob Nicholls. p. 11–14

The temple of Helm in Ravens Bluff.

Feather and Claw

By James Alan and Brian Burr. p. 15

Ravens Bluff's Knights of the Griffon.

Forgotten Deities: Bright Nydra

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 16–17

Bright Nydra, moon goddess of the Marsh Drovers.

Mr. Whiplash, I Presume?

By Uncredited. p. 18

Fame and infamy in the Living City, part 2.

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: Dapplegate and Derlusk

By Ed Greenwood. p. 19–21

Dapplegate and Derlusk.

The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited. p. 32

War Ravages Military, Mercenaries Needed, A Fife In Time Saves Nine.
