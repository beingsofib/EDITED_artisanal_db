Imperial City (disambiguation)

The Imperial City was used to name several imperial capitals, including:

  • The Imperial City (Shoonach) of Shoonach in the Shoon Imperium,
  • The Imperial City of Ten Mor Shou in Chu'ta Te,
  • The Imperial City of Inupras in Imaskar, and
  • The Imperial City (Vodoni) of the Vodoni Empire.
