1394 DR

The Wailing Years

  • The Emerald Enclave starts trying to half the flow of spellscarred pilgrims traveling through Turmish.
  • Cormyr tries to retake the Hullack Forest from the occupying Eldreth Veluuthra, ending when King Azoun V turns his attention to the more pressing threats from neighboring Netheril and Sembia.
  • Daerlun is finally annexed by Cormyr, owing to its mixed Cormyrean and Sembian heritage and Netherese rule.
  • Rault, who will one day be known as "Rault the Wise" and rule as Jarl of Norland, is born.
