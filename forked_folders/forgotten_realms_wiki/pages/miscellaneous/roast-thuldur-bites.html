Roast thuldur bites

Roast thuldur bites was a drow meat dish commonly served in the first course of a drow banquet.

Description

The meat of this dish was derived from old pack lizards. The throats of large, old specimens would traditionally be sliced and then left to bled out, as pack lizard blood could be used for a variety of culinary purposes. The body would then be sliced into long ribbons and overcooked in a boiling broth of various mushrooms in order to soften the meat. Finally, the meat would be cut into small, bite-sized pieces and run onto backbone skewers.
