Polyhedron 93

Polyhedron Newszine #93 was released in March 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Adversaries

By Steven E. Schend. pp. 4, 6

Lady Aridarye Phylund schemes to gain control of the noble Phylund family in Waterdeep.

Novel Creations

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 13–15

From the pages of the Forgotten Realms setting novels come three deadly foes for your player characters.

  • Groundlings
  • Deathmirror Beetles
  • Shadevar

The Everwinking Eye: Treasures of the Vast, Part Five

By Ed Greenwood. p. 16–17

In this installment of The Everwinking Eye, Elminster's treasure-tour of the vast concludes with a wink and a warning.

A Fool's Errand: The Viper Comes To Ravens Bluff

By James Tillman. p. 18–20

A pair of travelers face a deadly task before resuming their trek to the Living City.

The Living City: Underdeveloped Real Estate: Tower Aqueduct

The Living City column by James P. Buchanan, Cartography by Brian Blume. p. 21–25

There goes the neighborhood! Help the landlord evict some unwelcome tenants from his unusual land parcel. But first you must find them!
