Polyhedron 76

Polyhedron Newszine #76 was released in October 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Babette: A Sword By Any Other Name Would Still be Sharp

Author unknown. p. 6–7

The results of a Polyhedron contest to name and detail the sword appearing in the cover art by Clyde Caldwell on Polyhedron #70.
