Polyhedron 95

Polyhedron Newszine #95 was released in May 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: Clandestine Company

By Ed Greenwood. p. 5–6

Hsst! Can you keep a secret? News of secret societies and power groups in the Forgotten Realms.

  • The Broken Sword
  • The Knights of the Storm
  • The Masters of the Cowl
  • The Gold Fist

The Living City: In the Compter

By David Cook, illustrated by Larry Smith. p. 7–12

Crime and punishment in the Living City are as horrific as the offenses. Here are the prisons characters can expect to visit if they're caught breaking the law in Ravens Bluff.

Four Legs Are Better Than Two

By Tina Brown and Carlo Anziano. p. 20

Add these cousins to the centaur to your gaming table.
