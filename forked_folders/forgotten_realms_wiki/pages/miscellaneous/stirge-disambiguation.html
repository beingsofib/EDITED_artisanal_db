Stirge (disambiguation)

Stirge might refer to:

  • Stirge, the flying magical beast;
  • Stirge (gnome), the baby-killing gnome who was a major criminal across the Sword Coast;
  • Stirge (ship), the pirate ship captained by Laskilar; or
  • Stirge (drakkar), the pirate ship operating on the Sea of Fallen Stars.
