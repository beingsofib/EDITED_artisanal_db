Fallen Kingdom (disambiguation)

The Fallen Kingdom may refer to:

  • Illefarn, the ancient elven realm of northwest Faerûn that disbanded in 342 DR, or
  • The alliance of Illefarn between the elves of Ardeep and the dwarves of Dardath from 342 DR to 523 DR, or
  • Phalorm, the Realm of Three Crowns that existed from 523 DR to 615 DR, or
  • Delimbiyran, the Kingdom of Man that succeeded Phalorm from 616 DR to 697 DR, or
  • The brief, second alliance of Illefarn between the moon elves of Ardeep Forest, the humans in the Delimbiyr river valley, and the dwarves of the Forlorn Hills in 882 DR.
