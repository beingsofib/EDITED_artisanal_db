Melair (disambiguation)

Melair may refer to:

  • King Melair I, also known simply as "Melair".
  • King Melair IV, the fourth and final clan chief of the Melairkyn.
  • Melairbode, the home of the Melairkyn clan of dwarves.
  • Melairkyn, an ancient dwarven clan of artisans, explorers and traders.
