Polyhedron 53

Polyhedron Newszine #53 was released in May 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The New Rogues Gallery: Phoenix Roses

By Hubert Phillips III. p. 18-28

The Living City: The Ice House

By Fran Hart. p. 28–29

The business climate is cool at the Ice House, but the establishment should send pleasant chills up your player characters' spines.
