Aerie (disambiguation)

An aerie is the nest of a bird of prey or a high and remote but commanding place.
In the Realms it was the name of several inhabitants and places:

  • Aerie, an avariel from Faenya-Dail;
  • Aerie, a temple-city in the High Forest;
  • The Aerie, the divine realm of Aerdrie Faenya;
  • Aerie of the Snow Eagles, in the 14th century DR the biggest settlement of the avariel on Toril;
  • Stormwind Aerie, a group of giant eagles belonging to the Fangshields;
  • Acid Aerie, a gorge near Blindgenstone; or
  • Storm giant aerie of Lake Woe mountain, a settlement of storm giants
