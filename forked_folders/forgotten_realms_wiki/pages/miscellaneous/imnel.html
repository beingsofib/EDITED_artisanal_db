Imnel

"Imnel" was the given name of King Imnel Torlath I, a famous early ruler of Amn, as well as his descendants Imnel II, Imnel III, and Imnel IV. It was also an element of the names of several locations in southern Amn around the Small Teeth, among them:

  • Imnescar, a town built on the site of Imnel's Scar,
  • Imnesvale, a village in the Umar Hills,
  • Imnescourse Trail, the road between Imnescar and Esmeltaran,
  • the Imnestream, a river from the Small Teeth, and
  • Gambiton, a town named for "Imnel's Gambit".
