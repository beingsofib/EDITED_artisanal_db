Rauvin

Rauvin might refer to:

  • Rauvin Gorge, a gorge in Moon Pass,
  • Rauvin Mountains, a mountain range in the Silver Marches,
  • River Rauvin, a river within the Silver Marches,
  • Rauvin Vale, a valley along the River Rauvin,
  • Rauvinheart, a set of caverns under Dead Orc Pass, and
  • Rauvin (dwarf), an ancient dwarven hero for whom the above are all named.
