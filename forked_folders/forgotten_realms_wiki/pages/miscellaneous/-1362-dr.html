-1362 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Some Ulutiuns discover the Glacier of Ulutiu and translate some of its writings. Those who chose to remain in Nakvaligach to worship Ulutiu become the Nakulutiuns. Those who reject Ulutiu and return to Alpuk become the Iulutiuns.
