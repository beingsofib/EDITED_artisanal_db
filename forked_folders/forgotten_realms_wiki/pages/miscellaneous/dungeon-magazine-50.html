Dungeon magazine 50

Dungeon issue 50 was released in November/December of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Felkovic's Cat

By Paul Culotta, illustrated by Bob Klasnich. pp. 48–70

A Ravenloft module set in Valachan, wherein players must confront Baron Urik von Kharkov.
