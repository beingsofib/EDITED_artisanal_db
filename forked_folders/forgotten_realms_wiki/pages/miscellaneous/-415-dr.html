-415 DR

__NOTOC__

  • The regent of Wa, Toda no Nagamori attempts to overthrow the new Goshukara emperor but his plot is discovered. His family is killed, arrested, and scattered. Because Wanese custom prevents the emperor from executing a former regent, his wu jen created the Cell of Perpetuity which keeps Toda no Nagamori alive in isolation for over a thousand years.
