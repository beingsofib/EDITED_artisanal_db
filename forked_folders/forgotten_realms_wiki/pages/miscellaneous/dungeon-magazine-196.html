Dungeon magazine 196

Dungeon issue 196 was released in November of 2011 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Court of Stars: Baba Yaga, Mother of All Witches

By Alana Abbott, illustrated by Goran Josic. p. 6-10

Baba Yaga, the hag who eats heroes and places their skulls on pikes, has a long history of being one of the most evil and dangerous old women in folklore.

Backdrop: Moonshae Isles

By Shawn Merwin, illustrated by Dave Rapoza, Cartography by Mike Schley. p. 11-28

The Moonshae Isles are in the western portion of the Sea of Swords, near where that body of water leads to the uncharted expanses of the Trackless Sea.

Eye on the Realms: The Ghost Knight of Galardoun

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Wayne England. p. 36-38

Throughout the Heartlands of Faerûn, word has spread of an eerie apparition that many folk have encountered in dark and lonely places.

Baba Yaga's Dancing Hut

By Craig Campbell, illustrated by Noah Bradley and Bernadette Carstensen. p. 52-107

An exploration of Baba Yaga's Hut and all its hazards and inhabitants.

Monster Manual Updates: Choker

By Logan Bonner, illustrated by Izzy. p. 108-109

The greedy chokers hide in caverns and among trees, attacking and strangling victims with their flexible arms.
