Aasim (disambiguation)

"Aasim" was a common name found throughout the Realms. Individuals named "Aasim" include:

  • Aasim, an elven sha'ir spellcaster;
  • Aasim (Sword Coast), the follower of the Bhaalspawn Sarevok;
  • Aasim al-Gammam, an administrator genie from Huzuz;
  • Aasim Karim, a desert rider who lived in the lands outside Hiyal; or
  • Aasim al-Nazi, a priest who served at the Golden Mosque.
