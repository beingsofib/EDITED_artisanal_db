Farseer (disambiguation)

Farseer may refer to:

  • Farseer, a wizard spell.
  • ''Farseer, a powerful Savrasian diviner who administrated the House of the All-Seeing Orb.
  • Farseer of Illusk, a magical telescope.
  • Darcassan the Farseer, a sun elf diviner.
  • Malakii Farseer, the mayor of Burrowville in the late 14 century DR.
  • Mattias Farseer, a traveling ranger in the late 15 century DR.
  • Temenu Farseer, a Nubari shaman who was active on the Malatran Plateau in the late 14 century DR.
