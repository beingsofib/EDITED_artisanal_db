Dungeon magazine 144

Dungeon issue 144 was released in March of 2007. It contained the following article related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Muster of Morach Tor

By Russell Brown, illustrated by UDON, Cartography by Robert Lazzaretti. p. 16-26

This adventure is set in the Evermoors and involves a failed attempt by the town of Nesmé to hire lizardfolk mercenaries to push back attacks from trolls and giants. Module for 4 PC's of levels 4.
