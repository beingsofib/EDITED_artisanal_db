Watchers

The word Watchers could refer to:

  • "Watchers", another term for the spies working under the Crownar of Cormyr,
  • Watchers, the specialty priests of the god Helm,
  • The Watchers, who guarded Aglarond while stationed atop the Watchwall,
  • Belt Watchers, an alliance of various power groups in Damara,
  • Watchers of Candlekeep, the warrior-monks tasked with the library's protection,
  • Watchers of the North, a landmark in the Forlorn Hills,
  • Watchers over the Fallen, the company of Helmite healers,
  • Watchers of Sevreld, a druidic circle who held their meetings in the High Forest, or
  • The Watchers, the spy organization in service to the crown of Bral.
