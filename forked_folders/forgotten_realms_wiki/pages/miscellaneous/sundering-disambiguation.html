Sundering (disambiguation)

Sundering might refer to:

  • The Tearfall, also known as the Sundering of Abeir-Toril
  • The First Sundering, a high magic ritual around −17,600 DR cast by the Tel-quessir
  • The Second Sundering, a period in the 1480s DR which saw much turmoil and the return of some deities
  • The Sundering (series), a novel series covering the events of the Second Sundering
  • Sundering (weapon quality), a weapon property
