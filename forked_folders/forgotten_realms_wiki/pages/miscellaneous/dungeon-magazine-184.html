Dungeon magazine 184

Dungeon issue 184 was released in November of 2010 in online format only. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Eye on the Realms: Rastigur Stornont

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Warren Mahy. p. 58–60

Meet Rastigur Stornont, an amoral swindling merchant known for his drunkenness and debauchery.
