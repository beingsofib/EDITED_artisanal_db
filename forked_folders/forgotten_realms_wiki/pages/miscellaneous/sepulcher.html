Sepulcher

A sepulcher was a small chamber used for burial. It could also refer to:

  • Brother Sepulcher, a priest of Jergal.
  • Sepulcher, a town of ancient Netheril.
  • The Sepulcher of the Goblin Baron, a goblin lair in Airspur.
