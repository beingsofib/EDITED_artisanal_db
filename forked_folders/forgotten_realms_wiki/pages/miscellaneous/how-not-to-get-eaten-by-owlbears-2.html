How Not to Get Eaten by Owlbears: A Survival Guide for the Forgotten Realms is a book by Anne Toole published in September 2024, by (an imprint of Penguin). It purports to be a guide to the Forgotten Realms and dungeoneering by Falgriss, a dragonborn ranger and assistant to Volothamp Geddarm.
