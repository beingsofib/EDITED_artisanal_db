1266 DR

  • Four of the lost Demonshields are discovered in an ancient barrow in the Fields of the Dead and brought to the School of Wonder for examination. Soon after, demons and power-crazed students destroy the school.
  • King Vance Carrathal of Callidyrr dies of natural causes.
  • Teldin Carrathal becomes King of Callidyrr in the Moonshae Isles.
  • Sememmon betrays fellow apprentice Alvagaerus Firlthond to become chief apprentice of Manshoon.
  • The Bright Banner Brandished are wiped out in subterranean crypts north of Mirabar by the lich necromancer Orvanath. Only Ashemmi escapes alive.

Novels

  • Obsidian Ridge begins.
