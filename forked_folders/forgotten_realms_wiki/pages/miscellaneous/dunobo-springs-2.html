Dunobo Springs

The Dunobo Springs were a group of small springs located in the forested foothills of the mountains surrounding the Pasocada Basin. During the spring, the small streams fed by the springs turned into the Danozi Wash, which was responsible for creating the Sun Canyon. The Azuposi often cut down trees from around the springs.

Performing a sacred ceremony while immersed in the pool at the head of the springs opens a portal to the Astral Plane along a mystical road called the Sun Trail, which leads to the demiplane of Wenimats and the layer of Brux in the Happy Hunting Grounds.
