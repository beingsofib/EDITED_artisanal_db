Dragonbane

Dragonbane may refer to:

Magic

  • Dragonbane (abjuration spell), a spell that protected from dragons
  • Dragonbane (divination spell), a spell that was cast by dragons

People

  • Gareth Dragonsbane, the paladin-king of Damara
  • Smerdiuk Dragonbane, the famous dragon-slayer of Cormyr

Substances

  • Dragonbane amber, a magical substance found beneath Laerakond
  • The Dragonbane poison that was created from the blood of several dragons
