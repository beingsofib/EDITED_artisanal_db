Dungeon magazine 180

Dungeon issue 180 was released in July of 2010 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Spiral Gate

By Scott Fitzgerald Gray, illustrated by Tyler Walpole, Cartography by Mike Schley. p. 4-33

The separatist faction of Bedine desert tribesfolk known as the Sand Kings have long opposed the iron grip of Netheril’s restored masters, the Shadovar. Now the Sand Kings have uncovered a dangerous and far-reaching Shadovar plot ... but whose side will the heroes join? Module for 5 PC's of level 16.

Characters

Kungeya • Murrai • Yeri

Eye on the Realms: The Hunter of False Nobles

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by McLean Kendree. p. 72-74

Heldran Rallyhorn is devoted to keeping the nobility of Faerûn ‘pure’, and his methods have made him both loved and feared.
