Polyhedron 57

Polyhedron Newszine #57 was released in January 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Notes from HQ

By Jean Rabe. p. 4-6

An overview of the RPGA Network and introductions to some of the staff.

The Living City: Burton's Bouncing Bears

By Eric Scott Vaughn. p. 8–12;30

A look at Burton's Bouncing Bears, a new act in the Mooney & Sons Circus performed by a family of werebears.

The Everwinking Eye: At Home in Maskyr's Eye

By Ed Greenwood. p. 20–21

The fourth part in an exploration of Maskyr's Eye, looking in detail at village business and village politics.

More to the Maze

By Tom Wham. p.28-29

Development, errata, answers to rules questions, and an expansion to Mertwig's Maze.
