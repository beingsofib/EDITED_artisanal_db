Dungeon magazine 94

Dungeon magazine 94 was the 94th issue of Dungeon magazine. It was released in September/October 2002. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Spiral of Manzessine

By David Noonan. Artwork by Wayne Reynolds and Jonathan Wayshak. Cartography by Kyle Hunter. p. 68-90

Few can imagine a prison more terrifying than one run by illithids. Welcome to Manzessine.
