Auntie Nibbletoes' Famous And Very Very Good-Eating Downhome Recipes was a cookbook of recipes of the Nibbletoes family of halflings from the Shining South.

Description

This book was bound in snakeskin.

History

This book was eventually passed down to Marcus Nibbletoes, who in the 14 century DR brought his relations and the book to the Vast to form the farming community of Burrowhome.

On either Tarsakh 12 or 13, in the , a group of adventurers from Ravens Bluff came to Burrowhome and put an end to the murderous rampage of the cursed wizard Lasuren Felpus. Elated at the threat to their community having been vanquished, the people of Burrowhome offered the adventurers a variety of gifts, including this book.
