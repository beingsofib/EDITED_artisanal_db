Plantman (disambiguation)

"Plantman" can refer to:

  • Plantmen that attacked the island of Ruathym during the Godswar in the 14 century DR,
  • Plantmen of Malatra, also known as mold men, or Malatran vegepygmies, a species of intelligent plant creatures found exclusively on the Malatran Plateau in Kara-Tur,
  • Vegepygmy, a common variety of moldman found in Faerûn,
  • The Gardner, the plant man master of the Garden of Souls in the Astral Plane.
