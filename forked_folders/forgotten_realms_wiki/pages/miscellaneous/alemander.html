Alemander

"Alemander" was a male given name among the royalty of Tethyr. Individuals named "Alemander" include:

  • King Alemander I of Tethyr
  • King Alemander II of Tethyr
  • King Alemander III of Tethyr
  • King Alemander IV of Tethyr
  • Prince Alemander V of Tethyr
