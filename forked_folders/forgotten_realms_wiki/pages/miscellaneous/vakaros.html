Vakaros

Vakaros was an elder rune for air, referring to the Elemental Plane of Air as well as the naturally occurring element on the Prime Material plane and everything that came from air including clouds, lightning, rain, and birds.

The presence of this rune often indicated flying creatures in the area or high winds.
