Gaff (disambiguation)

Gaff may refer to:

  • A nautical term, often used by pirates in the Sea of Fallen Stars, that referred to a horizontal framework designed to support a four-sided, fore-and-aft sail, Ships that had them were often referred to as being "gaff-rigged."
  • Gaff, a tool used for fishing.
