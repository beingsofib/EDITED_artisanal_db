Polyhedron (German) Magazine 1

Polyhedron (Deutsche Ausgabe) Magazine #2 was released in 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminsters Erzählungen

By Ed Greenwood. p. 4–7

A translation of the Polyhedron column The Everwinking Eye to German. The article translated was one of the Mulmaster articles by Ed Greenwood.
