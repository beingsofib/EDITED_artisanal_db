Touch of the Talontar was a divine alteration necromancy spell used by priests devoted to Talona, enabling them to infect targets with a touch.

Effects

This spell was an enhanced version of poison touch, but allowed the caster to touch as many targets as possible for the spell's duration. Just as with the poison touch, victims suffered temporarily disfiguring corrosive welts to appear on their skin. However, the harm inflicted by the touch of the Talontar was often enough to make victims fall into a deep coma.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
