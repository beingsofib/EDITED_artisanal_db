1441 DR

  • Cormyr sends troops from Daerlun and Highcastle to lay siege to Saerb and Urmlaspyr.
  • Sembia, with the approval of the Netherese, signs the Treaty of Griffonfang Bridge, which ends hostilities with Cormyr, Cormanthor, and the Dalelands.
  • House Huntsilver is wiped out in the war between Cormyr and Sembia. The only two surviving female members marry into House Crownsilver and House Truesilver.
