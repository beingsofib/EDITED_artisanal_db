1354 DR

  • In Hillsfar, Maalthiir becomes ruler.
  • A large naval battle is fought between Mulmaster and Zhentil Keep, and Mulmaster's fleet is wiped out.
  • Dragonspear Castle is seized by devils.
  • The Ride: Zariel leaves Elturel with an army of Hellriders to invade Avernus.
  • Tarsakh 17: The Four Mages of Felshroun successfully repel an attack on the town by the Red Wizards of Thay.
  • The village of Hayashiro in Kozakura is destroyed during a nocturnal raid.
  • The Arcane Brotherhood consolidates its control over the High Captains of Luskan.
  • Nouméa Drathchuld becomes the newest Magister.
  • Iakhovas finally escapes from Umberlee's prison.
  • A temple of Tymora, the House of the Lady, is built in Shadowdale.
  • This year is known as the Year of Great Reaping in the Black Chronology.
  • The Origin Myths—A Treatise, a book written by Dunkelzahn of Candlekeep, is published.

Novels

  • Maiden of Pain begins.
  • Rising Tide begins.
