-293 DR

__NOTOC__

  • After a month-long siege, the Calishite garrison town of Calimaronn on the River Wurlur is taken by the Tethyrians. They rename the town Ithmong and the river Ith, after the chieftain and the clan that led them to victory.
  • Warlord Karlag falls in the Battle for Calimarron.
