Easting (disambiguation)

Easting might refer to:

  • Easting Reach, a wide expanse of water at the northeast of the Sea of Fallen Stars.
  • Easting River, a river in Northeast Faerûn that fed what remained of the Easting Reach after the Spellplague.
  • Easting language, a language spoken by inhabitants of The Vast and Impiltur.
  • Easting (town), a town in the Western Heartlands.
