Polyhedron 96

Polyhedron Newszine #96 was released in June 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elminster's Everwinking Eye: Mysterious Turmish

By Ed Greenwood. p. 5–6

Over a friendly skull-full of roast snails, Elminster introduces Ed to the land of Turmish.

The Living City: Rose's Tea Room

By Bob Kindel, Cartography by Brian Blume. p. 15–16

A look at the lovely and relaxing Rose's Tea Room in Ravens Bluff.

The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter

By Uncredited. p. 32

Vol. 1, Issue 1 of a newspaper for the RPGA Network's Living City shared world, including a census of all the characters registered with the RPGA.
