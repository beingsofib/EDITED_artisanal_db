Rashid (disambiguation)

"Rashid" was a common male given name in Calimshan and Zakhara. Individuals named "Rashid" include:

  • Syl-Pasha Rashid el Djenispool, a caleph of Calimport,
  • Rashid al-Azzran, a dwarf holy slayer,
  • Rashid al-Mawli, leader of the House of Mawli, and
  • Rashid al-Niran, an efreeti pasha.
