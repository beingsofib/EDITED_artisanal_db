Mist caves

Location
nameThe Mist Caves
typeCaves
regionIce Spires, northwest Faerûn

The mist caves were a collection of caves in the Ice Spires in northwest Faerûn that served as the residence of Goboka, an ogre chieftain, and his forces in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

These caverns varied in size, but were uniformly flooded with a thick, mildly corrosive gas and the stench of death and ogre accoutrements, such as molded clothes, half-eaten animals, and rotting, or rotted, meat.

Geography

There were about 27 caves in all.

Defenses

The mist in the caves and the pervasive stench allowed its nearly 100 ogre defenders to operate in often successful stealth, surprising any attackers.

History

At some point in the 14 century DR, the ogre chieftain Goboka took these caverns as the seat of his government.
