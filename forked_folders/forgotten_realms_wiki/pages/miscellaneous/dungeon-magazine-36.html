Dungeon magazine 36

Dungeon issue 36 was released in July/August of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Sea of Sorrow

By Steve Kurtz, illustrated by L.A. Williams, Cartography by Diesel. p. 40–69

The wildspace monster is deadly, but its lair is the real killer. A 28 page Advanced Dungeons & Dragons SpellJammer adventure for 4-6 neutral and good player characters. In addition to a spelljamming mage or cleric, the adventure can be completed by a party of resourceful fighters and rogues with any type of ship or helm.
