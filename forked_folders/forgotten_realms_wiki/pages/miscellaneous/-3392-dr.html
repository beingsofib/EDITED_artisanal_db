-3392 DR

__NOTOC__

  • Netherese arcanists conquer Thaeravel. They steal a number of new spells from the minds of the sorcerers.
  • The duergar King Horgar Steelshadow II founds the Deepkingdom, and takes Gracklstugh as his capital. Horgar claims all duergar settlements west of the Buried Realms and north of the Sharnlands as the Deepkingdom's domain.
