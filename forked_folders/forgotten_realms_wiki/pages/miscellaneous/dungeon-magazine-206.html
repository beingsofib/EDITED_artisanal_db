Dungeon magazine 206

Dungeon issue 206 was released in September of 2012 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Xanathar: Crime Lord of Waterdeep

By Derek Myers, illustrated by Jason Juta. p. 38-43

Meet a monstrous criminal mastermind who has his eyes—all eleven of them—on the City of Splendors and its many riches.

Eyes on the Ball

By Derek Myers, illustrated by Kai Carpenter and im Pavelec, Cartography by Jason A. Engle and Mike Schley. p. 45-69

A beholder crime lord needs your help to thwart a common enemy, and the job pays well. It’s an offer you dare not refuse. A D&D Forgotten Realms adventure for characters of levels 6–8.
