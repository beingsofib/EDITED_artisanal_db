The harlthorn was a variety of bush that grew throughout the Heartlands of Faerûn. It was known for its use in the creation of a specific form of medicinal draught.

Uses

When the thorns of this bush were boiled down with equal parts of dried hoof-leaf, it formed a solution that relieved a number of ailments: it calmed the effects of rage, grief, and delirium and soothed itchy skin conditions. When drunk, it allowed an individual to have much more restful sleep.
