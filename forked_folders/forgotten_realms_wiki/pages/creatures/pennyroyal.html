Pennyroyal was a species of a flowering herb, which had medicinal and herbal applications.

Description

Pennyroyal plants had long stems with green leaves and tiny violet flowers.

Habitats

Apart from being found growing on Toril,

Usage

Pennyroyal plants were commonly used to make a herbal insect repellent. The special recipe produced oil of pennyroyal that could be rubbed on one's skin to scare hungry insects away. A single flask was sold for five gold coins via the Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue. Each flask contained enough pennyroyal oil for fifty applications.

Pennyroyal was a key ingredient in certain potions of healing.
