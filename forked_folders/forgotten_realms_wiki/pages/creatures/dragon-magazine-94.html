Dragon issue 94 was released in February of 1985. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

From the Sorcerer's Scroll: Official Changes for Rangers

By Gary Gygax. p. 8

New rules for tracking and identifying tracks, plus an expanded list of "giant-class" humanoids.

An Army Travels on Its Stomach

By Katharine Kerr. p. 10

Large scale logistics in a fantasy world.

Same Dice, Different Odds

By David G. Weeks. p. 18

An examination of the probability curves of different dice rolls.

The Ecology of the Chimera

By Ed Greenwood. p. 24-26

A scholarly treatise on the Chimera.

Creature Catalog II

By Ed Greenwood, Matthew Quinn, Samuel Offutt, Gregg Sharp, Lenard Lakofka, Roger Moore, Stephen Inniss & Kris Marquardt. p. 43-54

Eighteen new monsters.
