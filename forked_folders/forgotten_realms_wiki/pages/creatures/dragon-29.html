Dragon+ 29

Dragon+ issue 29 was released on December 13, 2019. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Previews: In the Works

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

The Dragon+ team held council at the end of 2018, and decreed that each issue would continue to offer early looks at products in development. And so, let's see what's in the works...

  • Dungeon Mayhem: Battle for Baldur's Gate
  • Dungeons & Dragons: Dark Alliance
  • Warriors of Waterdeep

Cut Out And Keep Slobberchops

By Matt Chapman, illustrated by Emi Tanji.

Some assembly required! Break out the safety scissors and a glue stick and build your very own Slobberchops figure.

Holiday Gift Guide

By Matt Chapman.

  • Icons of the Realms
  • Maps and miniatures for Baldur's Gate: Descent into Avernus
  • Baldur's Gate: Descent Into Avernus
  • Lulu plushy
  • Asmodeus, Minsc, Boo Funko Pops

Creature Feature: Fiend Folio

By Matt Chapman.

Extra Life: Mordenkainen's Fiendish Folio, Volume 1: Monsters Malevolent and Benign

The Imminent Light

By Mark Price.

A side quest set during the Candlekeep section of Baldur's Gate: Descent Into Avernus, where the characters make their preparations to journey to the hellish plane of Avernus and save the town of Elturel.

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll, Cartography by Dyson Logos, Lee Moyer, Claudio Pozas, Mike Schley.

This issue we're pleased to include maps from several recent adventures: Baldur's Gate: Descent Into Avernus, Eberron: Rising from the Last War, and Extra Life: Infernal Machine Rebuild. Whenever possible we also look to provide you with both tagged and untagged versions of these maps!
