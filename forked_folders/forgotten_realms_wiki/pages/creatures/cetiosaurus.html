Cetiosauruses () were a species of large, marsh-dwelling dinosaur.

Description

Cetiosaurs were noted as having somewhat larger heads than other sauropods, such as the brontosaurus and brachiosaurus.

Biology

Diet

Cetiosaurs were herbivorous by nature.
