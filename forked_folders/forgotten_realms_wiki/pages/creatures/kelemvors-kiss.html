Kelemvor's Kiss was a type of poisonous shrub that grew in the most wild forests of Shadowdale, in the Dalelands.

Description

The thorn of Kelemvor's Kiss secreted a potent and lethal poison.

Usage

The poison of Kelemvor's Kiss could kill a person in short order, anywhere from a matter of hours up to two full days.

There did a exist a treatment for Kelemvor's Kiss poisoning. A victim could drink a solution of boiled mugwort prepared in theriac, which had been blessed by the god Silvanus.

History

In or around the , the healer Nettie learned about Kelemvor's Kiss and inscribed it into her copy of Flora of the Dalelands.
