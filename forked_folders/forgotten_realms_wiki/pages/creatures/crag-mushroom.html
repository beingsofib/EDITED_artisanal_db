Crag mushrooms were edible fleshy bulbous fungi, highly sought after for their invigorating properties.

Powers

These mushrooms possessed minor healing properties.

History

In the mid-to-late-15 century DR, crag mushrooms could be found growing in the areas surrounding Neverwinter and the city's Neverdeath district. During the same period of time, Tarmalune Trade House traded in these fungi.
