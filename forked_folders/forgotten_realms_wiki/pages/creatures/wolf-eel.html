Wolf eel, or akoak as it was known to the locals, was a species of cold-water eel that bore a resemblance to wolffish. They inhabited the seagrass flats of the Sea of Moving Ice in Icewind Dale.

Description

Wolf eels possessed powerful jaws that allowed them to feed on sea barbs, woolly crabs, and oysters. The ice hunters of Icewind Dale claimed that wolf eels, or akoak, were curious and usually harmless species of marine life, allowing the humans to swim with them during the warm summer months.
