Dragon issue 391 was released in PDF format only in September of 2010. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

A Legacy in Shadow: Shadar-Kai in the Realms

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 12–17

The shadar-kai are new to Faerûn, and they have much to learn as they explore their new home.

Winning Races: Genasi

By Peter Schaefer. p. 52

Too many genasi lead lives of forced servitude in the Elemental Chaos.

Watchers at the Living Gate

By Paul Park. p. 62–74

A half-orc druid and an eladrin shiere knight close a portal to the Far Realm in the Feydark beneath Cendriane.
