Dragon issue 277, Future Fantasy, was released in November of 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: Kim Mohan

By Michael G. Ryan. p. 15

A profile of Kim Mohan, from 1979-2000.

Coming Soon

By Uncredited. p. 28

  • Legacy of the Drow Collector's Edition novel.
  • Pool of Radiance: Attack on Myth Drannor computer game.
  • Pool of Radiance: Ruins of Myth Drannor novel.
  • Shadow's Witness novel.
  • The City of Ravens novel.

Countdown to the Forgotten Realms: Heroes & Villains

By Uncredited. p. 38

This is an interview about the heroes and villains of the Realms and how a dungeon master should try to keep the focus on the PCs instead.

Unusual Suspects

By James Wyatt, illustrated by Darrell Riche. p. 62–67

Bards, sorcerers, and wizards from a variety of races.

Another Name for Dawn

By Paul S. Kemp, illustrated by Carlo Arellano. p. 73–80

An Erevis Cale related story. Tie-in for Sembia: Gateway to the Realms.

Vs.: Orcs

By Richard Baker. p. 84

Killing orcs.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Soargar's Legacy

By George Krashos, illustrated by Arnie Swekel. p. 86–90

This article introduced five new magical swords of the Forgotten Realms.

The New Adventures of Volo: Dragonwing Stew

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Ron Spencer. p. 92–94

This article describes how one can cook a tasty stew from dragon body parts.

Rogues Gallery: The Heroes of Shadow's Witness

By Paul S. Kemp, illustrated by Justine Mara Andersen. p. 100–104

This article outlines the heroes from Kemp's novel Shadow's Witness.
