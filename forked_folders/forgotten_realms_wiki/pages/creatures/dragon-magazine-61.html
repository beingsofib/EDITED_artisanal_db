Dragon issue 61 was released in May of 1982. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

From the Sorcerer's Scroll: Now you see them: Cantrips for the aspiring illusionist

By Gary Gygax. p. 6

8 new 0th level illusionist spells.

Or with a weird one

By Rory Bowman. p. 16-26

Scarce and scary new weapons

The Gnomish Point of View

By Roger E. Moore. p. 28-30

Expanded outlook and psychology for the diminutive forest dwellers.

The Gods of the Gnomes

By Roger E. Moore. p. 31-33

The gods of the gnomes.

  • Baervan Wildwanderer
  • Flandal Steelskin
  • Segojan Earthcaller
  • Urdlen

The Dragon's Bestiary

By Ed Greenwood, Jeff Brandt, Theresa Berger and Willie Carlson. p. 48–50

The firetail, light worm, umbrae and tybor.
