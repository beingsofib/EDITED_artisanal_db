Aegwyrt

Plant
nameAegwyrt
typePlant

Aegwyrt was a common weed noted for its mild medicinal properties.

Usage

The plant was used in a recipe for potions of healing. Faerûnian children were fond of picking them in spring and summer and blowing the seeds into the wind.
