Dragon issue 106 was released in February of 1986. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Laws of Magic

By Charles Olsen. p. 10

How AD&D's magic really works.

Casting Spells for Cash

By Fraser Sherman. p. 14

How AD&D's spell casters can get rich.

The Ecology of the Maedar

By Ed Greenwood. p. 16-17

A scholarly treatise on the Maedar.

Money Isn't Everything

By Carl Sargent. p. 18

About character starting funds, by class.

Open Them, If You Dare

By Ed Greenwood. p. 22

Dweomered doors.

More Range for Rangers

By James A. Yates. p. 33-35

New tables for ranger followers.

The Way We Really Play

By Tom Armstrong. p. 38

A three-stage development for DMs.

A Plethora of Paladins

By Christopher Wood. p. 45

Paladins of other alignments.

TSR Profiles: Steve Winter

By Uncredited. p. 61

A profile of editor Steve Winter.
