Dragon issue 96 was released in April of 1985. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

From the Sorcerer's Scroll: New Jobs for Demi-Humans

By Gary Gygax. p. 8

Dwarven clerics, elven rangers, and more.

What Good PCs Are Made Of

By Katharine Kerr. p. 10

Making a character more than just stats.

The Ecology of the Gulguthra

By Ed Greenwood. p. 20-22

The Otyugh and Neo-Otyugh.

The Handy Art of Forgery

By Keith Routley. p. 28

A new skill for assassins.

Getting In Over Your Head

By Craig Barrett. p. 56

Swimming rules.
