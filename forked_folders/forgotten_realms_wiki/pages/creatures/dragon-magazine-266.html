Dragon issue 266, Shapechangers, was released in December of 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: Mary Kirchoff

By Steve Kenson. p. 24

Diplomat and visionary, Mary Kirchoff helps steer the Wizards of the Coast book department toward the future.

Feathered Friends and Foes

By James Wyatt. p. 34

Give your next hero a pair of wings - and we're not talking celestials!

PC Portraits: Shapechangers

By Jim Crabtree. p. 39

Portraits of shapechangers, from werewolves to mimics.

Humanimals

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Eric Peterson. p. 40–48

Descriptions of a dozen hengeyokai.

50 Monster Maximizers

By Brian Corvello. p. 50

New facts about the classic monsters you only thought you knew.

The Innkeeper's Secret

By Troy Denning. p. 57–65

A story of Tanalasta & Vangerdahast after Cormyr: A Novel.

Arcane Lore: Unleash the Beast Within

By Brian P. Hudson, illustrated by Andrew Goldhawk. p. 66–71

New lycanthropic spells.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Limbo Draughts

By Steve Berman, illustrated by John Matson. p. 72–74

Powerful and unpredictable potions.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Giant Lycanthropes

By Brian P. Hudson, illustrated by Scott Fisher. p. 76–80

Really big werethings.

  • Shadkyn (giant werebat)
  • Polarwere (giant werebear)
  • Weregiant

Role Models: The Great Outdoors

By JD Wiker and Jim Bishop. p. 90

Using miniatures to model outdoor travel.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 108

New products for December, January and February.

  • Beyond the High Road novel.
  • Canticle novel.
  • Secrets of the Magister sourcebook.
  • The Dark Elf Trilogy Gift Set
  • The Icewind Dale Trilogy Collector's Edition novel.
