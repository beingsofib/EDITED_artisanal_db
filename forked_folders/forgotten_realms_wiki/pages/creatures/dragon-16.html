Dragon+ 16

Dragon+ issue 16 was released on October 31, 2017. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Art of Storytelling

By Matt Chapman. pp. 7

An interview with several authors, including Realms writers such as Ed Greenwood, R.A. Salvatore, Erin M. Evans, and Richard Baker.

Fiction: Qawasha & Kupalué Part Two

Main article: Fiction: Qawasha & Kupalué Part Two

By Adam Lee. pp. 10

The second part of a short fiction piece about Qawasha and Kupalué.

Click or Die! The Rise of Idle Champions

By Matt Chapman. pp. 12

A look at the video game Idle Champions of the Forgotten Realms, with two of the game's developers.

Character Spotlight

By Matt Chapman. pp. 13

Character sheets for several characters from various podcast Dungeons & Dragons games, but also one for the character of Gibbet from the short story "A Man and His Dog" (published in Dragon+ issue 9).

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll, Cartography by Mike Schley. pp. 20

Several free maps from the Tomb of Annihilation adventure:

  • Ataaz Muhahah
  • Camp Righteous
  • Firefinger
  • Heart of Ubtao
  • Hrakhamar
  • Jahaka Anchorage
