Dragon+ 30

Dragon+ issue 30 was released on March 2, 2020. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Imagining the Ampersand: Max Dunbar

By Matt Chapman.

Cover artist Max Dunbar talks (abyssal) turkey with Dragon+, as he celebrates 2020 and the Year of the Rat.

Previews: In the Works

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

The Dragon+ team held council at the end of 2018, and decreed that each issue would continue to offer early looks at products in development. And so, let's see what's in the works...

  • Baldur's Gate III
  • Laeral Silverhand's Explorer's Kit
  • Trouble in Waterdeep
  • Adventures in Waterdeep Team Pack
  • Zhentarim Team Pack

Creature Feature: Moonrats

By Oz Mills.

Seeing as 2020 is the Year of the Rat it’s time to uncover what these creatures are really capable of.

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll, Cartography by Dyson Logos, Lee Moyer, Mike Schley.

This issues maps provide a handy guide to Eberron and look back at Tolstoff Keep and Halaster Blackcloak’s Undermountain!
