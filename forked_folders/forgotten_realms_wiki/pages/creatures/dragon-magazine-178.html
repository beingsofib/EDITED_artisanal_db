Dragon issue 178, The way of the fantasy warrior, was released in February of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Follow the Leader

By Carl Sargent. p. 22

Followers for high-level paladins.

In Defense of Polearms

By Gregory Detwiler. p. 28-30

Why blades on sticks are the kings of the battlefield.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 38

New products for February and March.

  • Pirates of the Fallen Stars sourcebook.
  • Pools of Darkness novel.
  • Prophet of Moonshae novel.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 58

  • Review: Pools of Darkness.
  • Clue Corner: Pool of Radiance and Secret of the Silver Blades.

Novel Ideas.

By TSR Book Department. p. 79

Review of In Sylvan Shadows, Night Masks, Pools of Darkness, Prophet of Moonshae, The Coral Kingdom, The Legacy, The Night Parade and The Ring of Winter.
