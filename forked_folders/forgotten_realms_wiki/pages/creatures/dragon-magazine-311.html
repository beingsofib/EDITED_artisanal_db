Dragon issue 311, Spellcaster Classes, was released in September of 2003. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Arcane Alterations

By Christopher Perkins and Monte Cook. p. 42

Adding various templates (e.g. Concussive) to spells. Includes several new Ceremonial Feats.

Faces of Faith

By Ari Marmell. p. 46

Variant Cleric concepts.

Changing Nature's Design

By Matthew Sernett. p. 54

Variant Druids.

Musical Masters

By Jeff Quick and Patrick Younts. p. 64

Prestige Classes for Bards.
