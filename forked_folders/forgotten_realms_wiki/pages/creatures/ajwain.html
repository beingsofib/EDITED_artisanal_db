Ajwain was an exotic fragrant edible herb from Chult.

Availability

Ajwain was part of various spice mixes used and exported from Port Nyanzaru in Chult. Ajwain was mixed with coriander, ginger, varieties of curry powders, and brightly colored salts. In the 15 century DR, the Distant Shores trading company imported these Chultan spice mixes into the city of Baldur's Gate.
