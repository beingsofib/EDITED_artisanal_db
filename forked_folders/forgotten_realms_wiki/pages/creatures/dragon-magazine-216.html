Dragon issue 216, Hey-What the...!!!, was released in April of 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

First Quest: The ultimate act of defiance

By William W. Connors. p. 8

How William W. Connors's first sci-fi role-playing session got him hooked to more than a hobby despite some inital resistance.

Paths of Power

By Wolfgang Baur & Steve Kurtz. Artwork by Wm. Michael Mott. pp. 42–49

Paths of power are an alternative system for wizards to learn and increase in magic as opposed to the standard system of schools.

Spells

Close path • Seal path • Burn path

Elminster's Notebook: Kobold commotions

By Ed Greenwood. Artwork by Gary Williams. pp. 100–101

Elminster notes past and present kobold activities and holdings at a number of different places in Faerûn.

Index

Organizations: The Drinn • Selgryn

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 120

New products for April and May.

  • Once Around the Realms novel.
  • Ruins of Zhentil Keep sourcebook.
  • The Seven Sisters sourcebook.
