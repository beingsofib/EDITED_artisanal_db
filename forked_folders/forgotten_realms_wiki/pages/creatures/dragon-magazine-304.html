Dragon issue 304, Mercenaries, was released in February of 2003. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Mercenaries for Hire: The Nameless Legion

By Mike Mearls. p. 23

A transplanar mercenary league.

Monstrous Magic

By James Wyatt. p. 30

46 Spells for unusual spellcasters.

Saddle Up

By Rich Redman. p. 42

Riding and training more intelligent mounts.

Guardians of the Wild

By Gwendolyn F.M. Kestrel. p. 58

Seelie Court Fey, Unseelie Fey, plus a fey PC race and some templates.

Haunted: A Tale of Sembia

By Dave Gross. p. 69

A swordsman turned assassin battles a vampire in the ruins of a noble house. Tie-in for Sembia: Gateway to the Realms.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: Jongrath's Mill

By Ed Greenwood. p. 78-81

Jongrath's Mill, a waterless mill in Tethyr.

DM's Toolbox

By Johnn Four. p. 96

Roleplaying NPCs.
