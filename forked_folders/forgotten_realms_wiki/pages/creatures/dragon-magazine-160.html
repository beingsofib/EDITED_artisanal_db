Dragon issue 160, Who stalks the streets are stalked as well, was released in August of 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Last Call Inn

By Willie Walsh. p. 15

"You're all sitting in an inn..." And here is that inn.

Up, Away, and Beyond

By Bruce A. Heard, illustrated by Larry Elmore and Larry Smith. p. 38–40

Gravitational mechanics and planetary dynamics for a Spelljammer: AD&D Adventures in Space campaign.

The Ecology of the Gibbering Mouther

By Nigel Findley. p. 55–57

A scholarly treatise on the Gibbering Mouther.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 52

Clue Corner: Curse of the Azure Bonds.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 86

New products for August and September.

  • Black Courser module.
  • Homeland novel.
  • The Forgotten Realms Atlas

AD&D Trading Cards!

By James Ward. p. 117 (Insert)

Pocket-size pictures of 17 heroes of the AD&D Trading Cards game!
