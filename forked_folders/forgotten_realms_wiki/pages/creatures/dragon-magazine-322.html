Dragon issue 322, Darkness, was released in August of 2004. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Ecology of the Dark Ones

By Paul Leach. p. 30-38

A scholarly treatise on the Dark one.

Silicon Sorcery: Shadows of Undrentide

By Clifford Horowitz. p. 50–52

Adapting Shadows of Undrentide, the second Neverwinter Nights expansion, back to D&D.

Magic poison qualities.

Sneaky, Stealthy & Stylish

By Richard Farrese. p. 54

New magic items for assassins, rogues, and bards.

Patterns of Shadow and Light

By Jason Nelson. p. 70

Light and shadow spells for assassins, clerics, druids, and wizards.

Rogues Gallery: Heroes of the War of the Spider Queen, Part III

By Richard Baker. p. 76–80

A final look at the characters of the War of the Spider Queen.

  • Halisstra Melarn
  • Danifae Yauntyrr
  • Nimor Imphraezl

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: The Wyrmworks

By Ed Greenwood. p. 84-87

In Silverymoon, a shop specializing in dragon bits.

Faiths of Faerûn: Nether Hounds of Kiaransalee

By Thomas M. Costa. p. 88–90

Kiaransalee, the drow goddess of the undead, and vengeance, and her nether hounds.

Faiths of Faerûn: Deathstalker of Bhaal

By Thomas M. Costa. p. 92–94

Bhaal, the lord of murder and a prestige class for him.

Characterization, Part 3: The Sum is Greater than Its Parts

By Monte Cook. p. 96

More on developing NPCs.

DM's Toolbox: Reading between the Lines

By Marc Tassin. p. 100

Finding out what's fun for players.
