Redwater (lake)

The Redwater was a lake found in the Dragon Coast region of the Eastern Heartlands.

Geography

The lake was located south of the great city of Westgate, situated between the Grand Ride road and the eastern stretch of the Trader's Road.

Geographical Features

Redwater lake was fed by the Lake of the Snake by means of the River Reddan, while another waterway emptied out northeast into the western waters of the Sea of Fallen Stars.

Notable Locations

  • Redwater, the lake's namesake settlement that was found on its northern shore.
