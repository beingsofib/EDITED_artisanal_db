Dragon issue 79 was released in November of 1983. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ecology of the Treant

By Susan Lawson. p. 6-8

A scholarly treatise on the Treant.

Magic resistance: What it is, how it works

By Kim Mohan, Roger E. Moore, Penny Petticord and Jon Pickens. p. 17

When, how and why magic resistance works and how it is used in Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

Setting saintly standards: Friends of the gods deserve special powers

By Scott Bennie. p. 28–31

Powers and abilities of saints, the most powerful servants of a deity on the material plane.

Be aware and take care: Basic principles of successful adventuring

By Lewis Pulsipher. p. 60

Ideas based on common sense and cooperation to help your characters live longer.
