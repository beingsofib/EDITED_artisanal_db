Dragon+ 23

Dragon+ issue 23 was released on December 6, 2018. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Idle Chatter: Idle Champions on Console

By Matt Chapman. pp. 8

Having conquered the PC and mobile gaming worlds, the Idle Champions of the Forgotten Realms are battling their way onto consoles.

Catch A Falling Star

By Matt Chapman. pp. 11

When a teenaged Chris Perkins drew a map of a sailing ship, little did he know it would one day become an amazing prop. Dragon+ sets sail on Wizkids' upcoming Icons of the Realms release.

Fiction: Raven's Reckoning

By Mark Price and David Roomes. pp. 13

The tale of Braya continues as the young thief's search for Waterdeep's Vault of Dragons takes a new turn.

Video & Audio Highlights

By Matt Chapman. pp. 14

This article highlights a livestream game using Beadle & Grimm's Platinum Edition of Waterdeep: Dragon Heist and celebrates Spanish-language gaming.

Dragon Classics

By Bart Carroll. pp. 18

This article looks back at sailing ships and dangerous dungeons.

  • Report from Undermountain from Dragon #227 (March 1996) by Steven E. Schend.
  • Aurora's Undermountain Sale from Dragon #239 (September 1997) by Paul Culotta.
  • Bazaar of the Bizarre from Dragon #410 (April 2012).

Maps Of The Month

By Bart Carroll, Cartography by Mike Schley and Tim Hartin. pp. 20

Maps for The Falling Star and Waterdeep: Dungeon of the Mad Mage.
