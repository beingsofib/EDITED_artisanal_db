Shaggun were a wild species of bovine in northwest Faerûn,

Description

In appearance, the bovines that shaggun most closely resembled were bison.

Behavior

These wild cattle were generally more aggressive than the standard domestic breeds of cattle in the North.

Ecology

Huge herds of these creatures roamed the central plains and hills of the North, often competing with domestic cattle herds for pasturage.

Relationships

Shaggun saw domestication in the hamlet of Longsaddle and in the Talmost Lands, with crofters under the Talmost clan's employ watching over their scattered herds.

Beyond domestication, shaggun were hunted by the Uthgardt for food and fought as a test of a barbarian's courage.
