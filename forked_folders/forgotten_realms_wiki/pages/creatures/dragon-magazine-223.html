8Dragon issue 223, Beasts Fell and Terrible, was released in November of 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Lords of the Nine

By Colin McComb, illustrated by Tony DiTerlizzi. p. 10–18

The Lords of the Nine Layers of Baator have been revealed at last. Do you dare read about them?

The Dragon's Bestiary: Four Guardian Gargoyles

By John Baichtal, illustrated by Larry Smith. p. 20–23

Special gargoyles for your use.

  • Archer (gargoyle)
  • Grandfather plaque
  • Spouter
  • Stone lion

The Ecology of the Chitine

By Belinda G. Ashley, illustrated by Terry Dykstra. p. 56-59

A scholarly treatise on the Chitine.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Nine Rings to Rule Them All

By Robert S. Mullin, illustrated by Michael Scott. p. 35–38

Nine magical rings.
