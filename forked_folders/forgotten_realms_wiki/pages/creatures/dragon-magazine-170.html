Dragon issue 170, 15th Anniversary Issue: Our best friends, our worst enemies, was released in June of 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Crossing Dragons With Everything

By Gregory W. Detwiler. p. 19–24

Several dragon hybrid creatures: the mantidrake, the dracimera, and the wyvern drake.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Give your campaign some heavy metal—dragons that is

By Jason M. Walker. p. 26–32

The ferrous metal dragons.

  • Chromium dragon (chrome dragon)
  • Cobalt dragon
  • Gruaghlothor
  • Iron dragon
  • Nickel dragon
  • Tungsten dragon

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 71

New products for June and July.

  • Horde Campaign sourcebook.
  • The Drow of the Underdark sourcebook.
  • The Parched Sea novel.

The Pitfalls of Game Mastering

By Steve Maurer. p. 87

Where GMs can go wrong, how how to fix it when they do.

Endings and Beginnings: the Forgotten Realms series

By Marlys Heeszel. p. 96

The ending of The Finder's Stone, Dark Elf, and Maztica trilogies; and the beginning of The Harpers series.
