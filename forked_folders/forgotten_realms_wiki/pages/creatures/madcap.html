A madcap was a redcap that soaked its hat in demon ichor rather than the customary blood. These fey exhibited a more extreme version of their more common counterparts' temperament.

Combat

Rather than falling unconscious, when a madcap was damaged to the point of no longer being able to fight, it died in a burst of fire that rendered all but its boots and weapon into ash.

Notable Madcaps

The Knucklebones Gang, an Avernian gang led by Mad Maggie, counted 30 madcaps among its members.
