Asps were small venomous snakes often encountered in the warmer parts of Toril.

Description

They were small snakes around in length with a very dangerous venom, able to kill those bitten within a minute.

Ecology

Habitats

Asps were found nearly everywhere in the Chultan jungles, thriving in the warmth and humidity of that land. They were also found in the Badu al-Kabîr desert in Zakhara.

History

In the , in the cursed city of Medinat Muskawoon, the Temple of Ra's courtyard was protected by a throng of asps, preventing anyone from simply strolling into the temple proper.
