Dragon issue 275, Fight, Fight, Fight!, was released in September of 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: Monte Cook

By Dave Gross and Chris Thomasson. p. 10

The editors interview author Monte Cook.

Coming Soon

By Uncredited. p. 26

  • Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn novel.
  • Into the Dragon's Lair module.
  • Pool of Radiance novel.
  • Servant of the Shard novel.
  • Shadow's Witness novel.
  • The Nether Scroll novel.

PC Portraits

By Stephen Schwartz. p. 37.

Illustrating fighters.

How to Design a Feat

By Sean K. Reynolds and Jonathan Tweet. p. 38–41.

3rd edition designers discuss the do's and dont's of feat-making.

The Right Tool for the Job

By Andy Collins, illustrated by Kennon James and David Day p. 42–45

More exotic weapons for the D&D game.

Instant Heraldry

By Brian McLachlan, illustrated by Brian McLachlan p. 48–51

Random charts for creating random heraldry.

Historical Heraldry

By Bryon Wischtadt, illustrated by Barbara Barian. p. 52–61

A thorough description of real heraldry.

Hooked

By Robin D. Laws. p. 62–65

Ten ways for players to drive the story.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Armor of Virtue

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Arnie Swekel. p. 88–91

Magical paladin armor.

The New Adventures of Volo: The Crumbling Stair

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Barbara Barian. p. 92-95

A ruin in the Sword Hills.

Creature Codex: The Ecology of the Darkmantle

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Justine Mara Andersen. p. 98–105

All about the highly developed gastropods, the Darkmantle.

Vs.: Goblins

By Bruce R. Cordell. p. 106

How to fight goblins. How hard can it be?

Silicon Sorcery: Snow Job

By Johnny Wilson. p. 126–127

How Icewind Dale can teach you to DM better.
