Dragon issue 189, Fantasy from the rest of the world, was released in January of 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Other Orientals

By Tom Griffith. p. 28

Can't wait for Oriental Adventures to hit the AD&D 2nd Edition game? Here it is!

Rhino's Armor, Tiger's Claws

By Michael J. Varhola, illustrated by Michael Scott and David O. Miller. p.34-39

You'd better know your knobkerrie from your isiHlangu.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Something Magical for the Compleat Savage

By James R. Collier. p. 78–80

Magic items for "savage" player kits.

Completing the Complete Bard

By Jon Winter. p. 82

Supplemental bard kits.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 120

New products for January and February.

  • Assassin Mountain module.
  • Realms of Valor novel.
  • Volo's Guide to Waterdeep sourcebook.
