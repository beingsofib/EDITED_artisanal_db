Toucans were a colorful species of tropical bird that could be found on the Malatran Plateau, and possibly Chult.

Sub-Species

Giant toucans: Oversized cousins of the usually small bird species.

History

Sometime in the late 14 century DR, the lacerial shaman Starr crafted a technicolored feather cloak, primarily sourcing its feathers from parrots and toucans.

Trivia

  • Within the Papazotl's Shrine, one of the nine trickster gods of Omu, there was one room that featured six statues of bare-chested humans with animal heads. One of these statuary heads was a depiction of a toucan's.
