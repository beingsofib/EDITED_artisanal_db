Dire tigers were a monstrous variety of the mundane species of Feline known as tigers.

Description

Dire tigers were giant cats with a pair of huge fangs jutting from their powerful jaws. They grew to be over long and could weigh up to .

Combat

Dire tigers preyed on just about anything that moved, patiently stalking a potential meal, and striking whenever the creature let down its guard. A dire tiger attacked by running at prey, leaping, and clawing and biting as it raked with its rear claws.

Ecology

Habitat

Dire tigers were found in warm forests. They are known to live the Dunwood.. They also lived in the Cat Lord's Prowl.

Relationships

The demon lord Baphomet was known to sometimes have awakened dire tigers serving him.
