Die Laughing

The Spirit of Myrrth - Die Laughing was the eight issue of the Advanced Dungeons &amp; Dragons comics series set in the Forgotten Realms and published by DC Comics. It was written by Dan Mishkin, drawn by Jan Duursema, and edited by Barbara Kesel. It is the fourth and final installment in the "The Spirit of Myrrth" story arc.
