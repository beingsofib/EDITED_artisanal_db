Twice-pale alder

Creature
nameTwice-Pale Alder
locationFeywild

Twice-pale alder was a species of fey that resembled a stag found inhabiting the Feywild realm, who were considered guardians of "slumber and sylvan secrets."

Description

A twice-pale alder was a large, graceful fey beast with lilac-hued hide. Their backs were covered in blooming flora and heads crowned with impressive purple antlers. A pair of relatively small butterfly-like wings emerged from their lower torso. Most notably, a twice-pale alder had two eyes on each side of its head and one more right in the center of its forehead. All the eyes glowed yellow. Their movements were graceful, gliding like something from a dream.

Abilities

When a twice-pale alder was summoned into the Prime Material plane, it created an etheral vortex that pulled the summoner's enemies into it. Its master also received a boon of increased damage and reduced incoming damage.

History

In the late 15 century DR, some brave adventurers of Neverwinter, who traveled into the Feywild, emerged with twice-pale alders as their mounts and allies.
