Dragon issue 86 was released in June of 1984. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ecology of the Slithering Tracker

By Ed Greenwood. p. 8

A scholarly treatise on the Slithering Tracker.

Familiars with a special use: New 'pets' that match the magic-users who call them

By Stephen Inniss. p. 12

New 'special' familiars for all alignments of magic-users in Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

Dragons and Their Deities

By Alan Zumwalt. p. 36

Adding dragon clerics to your Advanced Dungeons & Dragons campaign.
