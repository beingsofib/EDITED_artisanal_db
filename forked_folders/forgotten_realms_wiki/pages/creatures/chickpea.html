Chickpeas were a variety of legumes found in the Savage Frontier of Northwest Faerûn

Usages

In the town of Red Larch, chickpeas were used as the primary ingredient in the local staple – savory crumblecakes. Chickpea mash was baked into loaves with roots, nuts, greens, as well as turkey and wildfowl scraps.

A smallbite called ruth-balls was made using mashed chickpeas. They were common across the Realms and, according to Volothamp Geddarm, the best ruth-balls were found on the island of Tashalar and in the city of Sammaresh of Lapaliiya. Ruth-balls served in Waterdeep, Westgate, and Selgaunt were also available.

Chickpeas were sold through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue, listed in "Aurora's Larder" valued at 3 gold coins per pound.
