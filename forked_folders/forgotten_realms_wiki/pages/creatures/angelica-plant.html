Angelica (plant)

Plant
nameAngelica
typePlant
usageFood, spice, medicine

Angelica was a plant harvested in Faerûn that was used for food, medicine, and certain magical purposes.

Availability

Aurora's Emporium sold angelica seasoning for 5 cp per , and candied angelica for 1 gp per .

Usage

  • Angelica was believed to be useful for treating illnesses in the lungs, spleen, liver, eyes, and ears. A relatively simple medicinal cure for respiratory diseases was made from angelica, acacia, knotweed, and coriander.
  • The floral crown of an angelica plant could be used for the special ink for recording the message spell.
  • Certain divination spells could be aided by a preparation of angelica.
  • Dried angelica flowers could serve as a mild insect repellent.
