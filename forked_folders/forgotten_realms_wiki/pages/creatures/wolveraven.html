A wolveraven was type of duhlarkin created by Duhlark Kolat himself using the Duhlark's animerge spell.

Description

These carnivorous creatures were a combination of a giant raven and wolverine. A wolveraven had a wingspan of .

Combat

Wolveravens attacked with their hind claws, strong bite, and wing spurs.

History

Duhlark created four different wolveravens and bred six others from those, so he considered this version of a duhlarkin to be a success. He gave one of them to Khelben Arunsun as a gift. Duhlark released several of his wolveravens into the mountains north of Waterdeep where they helped control the populations of mountain goats and perytons.
