Dragon magazine 17 was released in August 1978. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Vampires in the Dungeon

By Clayton J. Miner. p. 5

Advice on utilizing the vampire to its fullest potential.

Sights and Sounds in Dungeons and Dragons

By Michael Weisberg and Robert Dushay. p. 17

A pair of tables to provide random sights and noises in the dungeon.
