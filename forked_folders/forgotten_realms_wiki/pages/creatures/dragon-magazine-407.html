Dragon issue 407 was released in PDF format only in January of 2012. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Editorial (Dragon): Romancing the Stone Giant

By Christopher Perkins. p. 3

How romance and Cormyr came together for this month's issue.

D&D Love Stories

By Jeff LaSala. p. 4

The time's come to add a little romance to your game. Before you do, learn a few lessons from literature. Complete with piles of plot hooks.

Character Themes: Heroes of Cormyr

By Dan Anderson. p. 13–18

What does it take to defend the greatest kingdom in Faerûn? Become a Purple Dragon Knight or War Wizard to find out.

Crowns and Mantles: The Ranks and Titles of Cormyr

By Brian Cortijo. p. 19–27

Before you can rub shoulders with the nobles and military officers of Cormyr, you need to know how to properly address them.

Blades of Kings: The Cormyrean Swords of State

By Brian Cortijo. p. 28–31

Fall in love with a collection of swords that symbolize the honor and legacy of Cormyr.

Eye on the Realms: A Surprising Vintage

By Ed Greenwood p. 42–45

Uncork a bottle of treasure.

Confessions of a Full-Time Wizard: Outsourced

By Shelly Mazzanoble. p. 46

Community Feedback for D&D Next.
