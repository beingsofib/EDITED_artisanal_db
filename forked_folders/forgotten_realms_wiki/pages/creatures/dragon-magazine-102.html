Dragon issue 102 was released in October of 1985. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

A Collection of Canines

By Stephen Innis. p. 20

Game statistics for every kind of wild canine, plus hyenas both modern and prehistoric.

Nine Wands of Wonder

By Ed Greenwood. p. 30

New magical wands described by Elminster.

  • wand of armory
  • wand of banishment
  • wand of darkness
  • wand of displacemnt
  • wand of hammerblows
  • wand of magical mirrors
  • wand of obliteration
  • wand of teeth
  • wand of wings

Creating a Cast of NPCs

By Jim Button. p. 56

Advice for creating and using NPCS.
