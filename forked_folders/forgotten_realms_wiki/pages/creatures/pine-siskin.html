Pine siskin

Creature
namePine Siskin
movementFlying

Pine siskins were a mundane species of bird that could be found in the forests of Hartsvale.

Description

Siskins spoke in whistles that generally sounded like they were saying "sweet sweet," but when agitated their calls sounded more like either "bzzrreeee" or "shrick-shrick."
