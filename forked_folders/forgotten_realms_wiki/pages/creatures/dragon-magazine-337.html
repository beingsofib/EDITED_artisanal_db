Dragon issue 337, Evil Enemies, was released in November of 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Monsters of the Mind

By Eric Jansing, Kevin Baase and Oliver Frank, illustrated by Kieran Yanner. p. 24–35

Creatures created by the Mind flayers or that hunt in their lands.

Demonomicon of Iggwilv: Zuggtmoy: Queen of Fungi

By James Jacobs, illustrated by Kieran Yanner. p. 36–54

All about the infamous Zuggtmoy, queen of fungus and ruler of the Temple of Elemental Evil.

The Ecology of the Shadar-Kai

By Jesse Decker, illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 72–77

A scholarly treatise on the Shadar-Kai.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Magic Face Paints

By Troy E. Taylor, illustrated by Cara Mitten. p. 82–84

Magic face designs.
