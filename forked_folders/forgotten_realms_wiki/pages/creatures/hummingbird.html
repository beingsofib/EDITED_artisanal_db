Hummingbird

Hummingbirds were a type of small,

Description

Hummingbirds were counted among the smallest of mundane birds. had needle-like beaks and swift, light-weight wings that moved so fast they seemed nearly invisible to the naked eye. Their swiftness in flight was so remarkable that they were often cited as examples of creatures skilled at flight, capable of precise aerial maneuvers,

History

These birds were known to at least some of the people of Cormanthyr.

Ecology

Diet

These birds fed upon the nectar found within flowers.

Habitats

In the Savage Frontier, hummingbirds could be found in the gardens of Ivy Mansion in Longsaddle.

Outside of Faerûn, hummingbirds could be found living in the jungles of east Zakhara, in Tabot in the Valley of Lo Chi, across the ocean on the continent of Maztica, and on the island of Evermeet. On Evermeet, they were most notably found within the garden of the Moonstone Palace.

Beyond the Prime Material, hummingbirds could be found in the realm of Faerie.

Usages

  • The feathers of hummingbirds were among the most favored for feathermagic and thus they were often trapped by plumaweavers for their plumage.
  • A hummingbird feather acted as a material component for the spells foresight and mass.
  • The tongue of a hummingbird could be used as an ingredient in a potion of animal friendship.

Relationships

Like many mundane birds, these creatures were often on good terms with the forest gnomes that shared their habitats.

Trivia

  • It was an often-told saying regarding genies and their relations with Zakhara's giants that the two would never cooperate until a hummingbird hatched the egg of a roc.
  • The feathermagic spell bird charm I could summon up to three hummingbirds.
  • Some halcyons, specialty priests of Aerdrie Faenya, had the power to wild shape like a druid into a hummingbird.
  • Hummingbirds were among the favored animals of the Maztican goddess Nula.
