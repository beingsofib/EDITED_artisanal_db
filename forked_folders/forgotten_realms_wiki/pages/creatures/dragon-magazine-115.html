Dragon issue 115 was released in November of 1986. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Tools of the (Thieving) Trade

By Vince Garcia. pp. 34-36

Specialized equipment for thieves and how to use it.

Hammer of Thor, Spear of Zeus

By James A. Yates. pp. 38-40

Introducing the concept of favored weapons for 1st edition clerics of many different deities.

Sharper Than a Serpent's Tooth

By Ray Hamel and David Hage. pp. 42-45

This article presents a variety of constrictor and venomenous snakes.

Rock python • ram python • boa constrictor • anaconda • ringed snake • saw-edged scaled snake • king cobra • black mamba • Gaboon viper • rattlesnake • flying snake • sea snake • spitting snake

Airs of Ages Past

By Ed Greenwood. pp. 46-47

This article describes nine magical harps from Myth Drannor's long-lost time of glory.

Azler's Harp • Methild's Harp • Esheen's Harp • Nithanalor's Harp • Dove's Harp • Zunzalor's Harp • Jhantra's Harp • Rhingalade's Harp • Valarde's Harp

The Ecology of the Harpy: Songs of beauty...

By Barbara E. Curtis. pp. 50-52

A detailed look at the life and abilites of the vile harpy...

The Ecology of the Harpy: ...Songs of death

By Ed Greenwood. pp. 52-54

...and her appearance in the Realms and some more lore recounted by Elminster.

TSR Profiles: ROGAR of MOORIA

By Uncredited. p. 82

A humorous look at the history and activities of game designer Roger E. Moore.
