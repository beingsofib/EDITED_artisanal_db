Dlurn were a species of fish endemic to the Lake of Steam, most commonly found in large numbers in the harbor of Derlusk and other inlets in its proximity.

Description

These fish were small in size and had a green coloration to them.

History

In the 14 century DR, dlurn fish was commonly sought after by the people of Derlusk for the production of dlurn paste, a popular fish paste.
