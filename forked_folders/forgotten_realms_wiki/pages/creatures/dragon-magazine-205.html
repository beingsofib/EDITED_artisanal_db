Dragon issue 205, Born to be Wild in the Wilderness, was released in May of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Getting Back to Nature

By Jon Pickens. p. 26

Making the 2nd edition druid's spell list look more like 1st edition by rearranging the spheres of clerical magic.

Arcane Lore: New (And Old) Priest And Druid Spells

By Jon Pickens, illustrated by Jennell Jaquays. p. 34–36;96

Add these new (and old) spells to the priest's and druid's repertoire.

  • Crown of brilliance
  • Greater guardian seal
  • Imbue purpose
  • Inner sanctum
  • Lesser guardian seal
  • Precipitation
  • Seal of destiny
  • Shield of the Archons
  • Sunburst
  • Tsunami
  • Warmth
  • Whirlwind
  • Windbearer

The Plane Truth Part III: The Transformation

By David "Zeb" Cook. pp. 51–53

The final journal of the fallen paladin Ambran of Cormyr.

Eye of the Monitor

By Sandy Petersen. p. 61-62

Review of Dungeon Hack.

12 Secrets of Survival

By Spike Y. Jones. p. 84–87

Magic items to help adventurers cope with the more mundane dangers of the wilderness.

The Game Wizards

By Dori Hein. p. 89-90

Get the lowdown on the revised and expanded RAVENLOFT® campaign setting.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 124

New products for June.

  • Cities of Bone module.
  • Marco Volo: Departure sourcebook.
