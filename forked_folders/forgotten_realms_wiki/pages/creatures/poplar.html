A poplar was a type of tree that could be found in various places on Toril.

Habitats

  • In north Faerûn, poplars could be found in the west starwood area of the forest of Cormanthor.
  • In the Western Heartlands, poplars lined the canyon of Yellow Snake Pass.

Beyond Faerûn

  • On the island of Evermeet, poplars could be found around the Temple of the Seldarine.
  • In the Hordelands, poplars could be found in the Merket Oasis and on the slopes of the Katakoro Plateau.
  • In Kara-Tur, poplars could be found in the Hochi Province of Kozakura.

Beyond the Prime Material

  • In the plane of Hades, poplars could be found on the layer of Pluton, particularly in the divine realm of Hecate.
  • In Qui, one of the forests in sector five of the plane was populated with poplars during the Season of the Phoenix.
