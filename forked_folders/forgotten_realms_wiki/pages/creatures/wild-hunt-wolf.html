Wild Hunt wolves were a unique breed of fey dire wolves that served the Wild Hunt, sometimes being used as mounts.

Description

These creatures had black coats of fur, with golden glowing runic script running across it.

Abilities

Like other creatures unique to the Wild Hunt, these wolves had incredible stamina and strength.
