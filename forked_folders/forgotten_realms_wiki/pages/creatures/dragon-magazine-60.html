Dragon magazine 60 was released in April 1982. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Elven Point of View

By Roger E. Moore. p. 6-8

A look at the way elves perceive the world.

The Gods of the Elves

By Roger E. Moore and Georgina Moore. p. 9-12

Some deities from the elven pantheon.

Deities

Aerdrie Faenya • Erevan Ilesere • Hanali Celanil • Labelas Enoreth • Solonor Thelandira

The Half-elven Point of View

By Roger E. Moore. p. 14

A look at the way half-elves perceive the world.

From the Sorcerer's Scroll: More Cantrips: Mini Spells for Apprentice Magic-users

By Gary Gygax. p. 16

Minor spells for magic-users.

Spells

Bee • Belch • Blink • Bluelight • Bug • Change • Cough • Creak • Distract • Footfall • Firefinger • Giggle • Gnats • Groan • Hide • Moan • Mouse • Mute • Nod • Palm • Present • Rattle • Scratch • Smokepuff • Sneeze • Spider • Tap • Thump • Tweak • Twitch • Unlock • Whistle • Yawn

Firearms: Primitive powder weapons for AD&D play

By Ed Greenwood. p. 24

Details the use of firearms in fantasy settings.

Weapons

Basilisk (weapon) • Bastard cannon • Bastard culverin • Bombard • Cannon • Cannon royal • Cannon serpentine • Culverin • Demi-cannon • Demi-culverin • Falcon (weapon) • Falconet • Handgun • Minion (weapon) • Pedrero • Rabinet • Ribald (weapon) • Saker • Serpentine (weapon)

Alignment: A New View of the Nine Philosophies

By John Lees. p. 72

An attempt to clarify the alignment system in the AD&D game.
