Porpoises were a species of aquatic mammal.

Behavior

Porpoises were known for being friendly and playful. They were also helpful.

Abilities

Porpoises were capable of holding their breath for a significant amount of time. Porpoises used sonar to see, using high-pitched sound waves that many creatures could not detect to see objects and other creatures to a distance of .

Ecology

Habitat

Porpoises were found in temperate climates and warm oceans.

Uses

Porpoises were a common companion animal to druids and rangers who lived in aquatic environments.
They were also quite useful as mounts, as they could carry a medium sized creature on their backs even though they themselves where of a medium size. They could not, however, carry more than one passenger, and it was better for the rider to shed any unnecessary equipment to ease the strain of the porpoise. They could usually be bought for around 100 gp and it required an additionally 20 gp to train them.

Summoning

A celestial porpoise could be summoned by the summon monster I spell if one were in an aquatic or otherwise watery environment. With the summon monster II spell 3 celestial porpoise could be called upon
A single ordinary porpoise could be summoned with the Summon nature's ally I spell.Likewise the spell Summon cetacean could call up to 16 porpoises to the caster, required that they all were within when the spell was cast.

Magic

With the spell Cetacean form clerics of Deep Sashelas could assume the form of a porpoise.
