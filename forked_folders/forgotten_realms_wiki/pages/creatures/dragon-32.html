Dragon+ 32

Dragon+ issue 32 was released on June 23, 2020. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Imagining the Ampersand: April Prime

By Matt Chapman.

An interview with artist April Prime looking at the creative process behind many art pieces for Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden.

D&D Live 2020: Roll w/Advantage

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

A retrospective of the D&D Live 2020 event, with minor insights into the products announced there.

Previews: In the Works

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

A deeper look at several upcoming releases:

  • Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden
  • Icons of the Realms
  • D&D Dice Adventures
  • Relentless

Young Adventurer's Guides: Beasts & Behemoths

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

A brief look at Beasts & Behemoths, the upcoming fifth installment in the Young Adventurer's Guides series.

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman, Cartography by Jared Blando, Jonas de Ro, Dyson Logos, and Mike Schley.

Several new free maps, including a selection from Acquisitions Incorporated.
