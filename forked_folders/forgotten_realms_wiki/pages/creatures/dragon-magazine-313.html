Dragon issue 313, Races of Power, was released in November of 2003. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Brain Power

By Clifford Horowitz, illustrated by Dan Scott. p. 46–52

Secrets of the Illithids. How the illithids blend magic and psionics.

Eye Wares

By Mike Mearls, illustrated by Dan Scott. p. 54–59

Potent powers of the beholders. Equipment, feats, Prestige Classes, and advice for optimizing the beholder.

Faiths of Faerûn: Elder Serpents of Set

By Thomas M. Costa, illustrated by Karl Kerschl. p. 85–91

This article discusses the unique and deadly Elder Serpents.

Ghost Elves: Elves of the Ethereal

By Kieran Turley, illustrated by Dave McClellan. p. 70–73

Introducing the ghost elves race and how they came to live in the ethereal plane.

Strange Bedfellows: New Half-Monster Templates

By Wade Nudson, illustrated by Todd Harris. p. 92–97

New half-monster templates.

  • Half-doppelganger
  • Half-janni
  • Half-minotaur
  • Half-nymph
  • Half-ogre
  • Half-rakshasa
  • Half-satyr
