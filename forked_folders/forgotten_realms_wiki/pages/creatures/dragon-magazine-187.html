Dragon issue 187, Across the beautiful, savage countryside, was released in November of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Wild, Wild Wilderness

By David Howery. p. 11–12

Combat tactics for wild animals.

Deadlier Dinosaurs

By David Howery, illustrated by Bob Walters. p. 14–18

Tips for beefing up your dinosaurs. Includes stats for new dinosaurs as well as modified stats for a few old ones.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Magic of earth and wood

By Matt Posner, illustrated by Karen L. Wann. p. 20–22

Druids need a boost - and here are the magical devices to do it. New magic items for druids.

  • amulet of transformation
  • claws of the bear
  • mistletoe wand
  • ring of the hierophant
  • ring of the little people
  • sickle of the harvest
  • square-meal seeds
  • wooden armor of the east

The Ecology of the Dakon

By Nick Parenti. p. 25-27

The shabby apes of the Fiend folio tome get a facelift for the AD&D 2nd Edition game.

Arcane Lore: Additional spells for elemental wizards

By Richard A. Hunt, illustrated by Tom Baxa. p. 32–38

Earth, air, fire, and water - an elementalist's playground for new spells.

  • Airball
  • Deep delve
  • Flame chase
  • Liquid orb
  • Power word, liquefy
  • Searing serpent
  • Shark bolt
  • Steam blast
  • Stone drill
  • Stone sleep
  • Windwar
  • Wings

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 47

New products for December.

  • Gold & Glory sourcebook.

The Lonely Harpist and the Lady Rogue

By Ed Greenwood p. 48–51

Mintiper Moonsilver, half-elf bard; and Asilther Graelor, half-elf thief: Partners who roam the Sword Coast in search of adventure.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 63

  • Clue Corner: Eye of the Beholder
  • Clue Corner: Eye of the Beholder II: The Legend of Darkmoon.

The Dragon's Bestiary: The wilderness isn't as nice as it used to be

By Malcolm Bowers and Spike Y. Jones, illustrated by Tom Baxa. p. 76–80

Two new monsters of the wilderness.

  • Kruel
  • Pardal

Troubleshooting Your Game

By Tanith Tyrr. p. 82

Dealing with disruptive players.
