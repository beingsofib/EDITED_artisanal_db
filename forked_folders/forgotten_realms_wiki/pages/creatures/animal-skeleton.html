Animal skeleton was a general term for skeletons created from the corpses of small to large-sized vertebrate animals.

Abilities

Much like other skeletons, these creatures were immune to charm, hold, sleep effects and death magic. They were vulnerable to holy water and turned by clerics like any standard skeleton.

Variations

Revived fossil: A skeleton created from the remains of an animal that had been preserved in a petrified state.

Undead lion: Skeletal lions created by the Red Wizards of Thay.

Warhorse skeleton: An animal skeleton created from the corpse of a warhorse.

Notable Animal Skeletons

  • Squire, a skeletal dog belonging to Ketheric Thorm.
  • Toysmiths, a toy store in the city of Ravens Bluff, kept a skeletal war dog as a guard animal. Rather than having its bones exposed, the store's owners kept the animal skeleton stuffed inside of a plush dog.

Ecology

Animal skeletons were typically created by means of the animate dead spell to serve as minor guardians, and could be made from the bones of beasts of burden with the undead mount spell. Additionally, around one to twelve animal skeletons could be conjured by undead summoning I.

Members of the Church of Malar annually animated the skeletons of great stags and other beasts on the night of Eleasis 9 for a festival known as the Bone Dance.

History

Around 1370 DR, a number of whale skeletons roamed near Thordentor, defending an undersea lair belonging to the Twisted Rune.
