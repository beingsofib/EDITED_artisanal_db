Apparitions were undead creatures existing mainly on the ethereal plane, who induced heart-stopping terror in their unlucky victims.

Description

Apparitions were the rare result of people who died while in the Border Ethereal. They looked like skeletal creatures wrapped loosely in cloth bandages.

They had a detect thought ability that allowed them to locate potential victims from , and across the boundary between the material plane and the ethereal.

Combat

Apparitions would dart into the material plane from the ethereal, and attempt to frighten their prey to death. By having the victim experience powerful feelings of cold hands squeezing their throats, they attempted to cause massive heart attacks in those attacked by them. If the victims were not able to distinguish between illusion and reality, they were in real danger of dying. Should they be slain and no attempt was made to raise them within one hour, the body would itself become a new apparition, drifting into the ethereal plane.

When they made their attacks, they had to be on the material plane, and at this time could be attacked by any ordinary weapon. Otherwise, they could only be hurt by weapons that could strike across the planar boundary, or by beings physically on the ethereal plane themselves.

Ecology

Relationship

The deity Kiaransalee would sometimes act through apparitions.

Many an Uthgardt burial mount had apparitions guarding them, although these were far less evil in their disposition than other apparitions encountered elsewhere.

History

In the , Fell Pass were haunted by several apparitions, in addition to ghosts and haunts.
