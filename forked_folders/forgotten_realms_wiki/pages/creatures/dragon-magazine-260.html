Dragon magazine 260, Dragons & Dragonslayers, was released in June 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Greater Drakes

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Eric Peterson. p. 29–39

Draconic mounts for characters.

Creatures

  • Arsalon
  • Fumarandi
  • Kavainus
  • Retchenbeast
  • Silislithis
  • Vandalraug

Kits

  • Air Knight (Warrior)
  • Sky Wizard (Wizard)
  • Storm Priest (Priest)
  • Wind Scout (Rogue)

Spawn of Tiamat, Children of Bahamut

By Keith Francis Strohm, illustrated by Andrew Goldhawk. p. 48–54

Creatures that are the children of Tiamat and Bahamut.

  • An-Ur
  • Dhrakoth
  • Medrinia
  • Mordukhavar
  • Vanathor
  • Xathanon

By Any Other Name: Dragon Names

By Owen K.C. Stephens. p. 56

A dragon name generator.

The Herbalist's Shop

By Cindi Rice & Steve Miller. p. 60-64

Even after his disappearance, the good Doctor's work goes on.

The Honor of Two Swords

By Kate Novak. p. 66-75

West meets East when Priam Agrivar follows Ishi Barasume to the secrets of her past.

The Ecology of the Aspis

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Brad McDevitt. p. 76-85

A detailed look at the Aspis.

Arcane Lore: Dragondoom

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Bob Klasnich. p. 88–92

A compilation of spells useful in hunting and slaying dragons.

  • Body reunion
  • Chain invisibility
  • Dust shield
  • Earth magnet
  • Fizzlebreath
  • Lockjaw
  • Soften scales
  • Temporary youth

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 108

New products for June, July and August.

  • Baldur's Gate novel.
  • Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas software.
  • Sea of Fallen Stars sourcebook.
  • Silverfall: Stories of the Seven Sisters novel.
  • Skullport sourcebook.
  • The Silent Blade novel.

ProFiles: Richard Baker

By Steve Kenson. p. 112

Fun is the name of the game for author and Creative Director Richard Baker.
