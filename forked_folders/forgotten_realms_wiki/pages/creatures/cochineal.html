Cochineal

Creature
nameCochineal
locationMaztica

Cochineals were tiny parasitic insects, native to Maztica, notable for their red color.

Usages

Cochineals were crushed into a red dye, most commonly used in the production of makeup for reddening lips and cheeks, as well as fabric dyes.

History

By the late 14 century DR, powdered cochineal was imported from Maztica and sold all around the Realms via Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue. A vial of was sold for 4 silver pieces.
