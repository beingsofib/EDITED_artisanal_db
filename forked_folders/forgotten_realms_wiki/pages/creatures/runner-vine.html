Runner-vine was a species of edible legumes found in Cormyr and notably in its capital – Suzail.

Description

Runner-vine were easily cultivated. Some households in Suzail planted theses edible beans on their back yard annually. When grown, runner-vines formed thick hanging curtains, climbing up nearby surfaces.

History

As of the late 14 century DR, Bhaerusk Montalar, the proprietor of Montalar's Happy House in Suzail, planted runner-vines at his restaurant's back entrance.
