Golden Tree of Dawn

nameGolden Tree of Dawn

The Golden Tree of Dawn was a particular Forest Monarch tree in the Feywild.

Description

Per Malyanna, the Golden Tree of Dawn held the Sun in its boughs and split its light into creation's prism with its leaves.
