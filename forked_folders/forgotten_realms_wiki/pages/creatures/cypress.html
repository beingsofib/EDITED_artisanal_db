Cypress

Plant
nameCypress
typeTree

Cypress was a type of tree that grew in Faerûn.

Varieties

Black cypress could be found in the Shadow Swamp, the reflection of the Vast Swamp on the Plane of Shadow.

Habitats

  • Cypress was known to grow in the Great Swamp of Rethild.
  • Cypress was known to grow in the Lizard Marsh.
  • A grove of cypress trees was known to be in Anauroch.
  • In the Shining South one could find cypress trees in the Akhlaur Swamp, the Great Swamp of Kethid, the Long Forest, and the Wood of Dark Trees.
  • Cypress was known to grow in the Vast Swamp.
