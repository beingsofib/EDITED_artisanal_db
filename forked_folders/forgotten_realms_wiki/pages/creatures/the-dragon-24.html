The Dragon issue 24 was released in April 1979. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Keeping The Magic-User In His Place

By Ronald Pehr. p. 7

Some variant rules to keep the magic-user from overshadowing the rest of the party.

Another Look at Lycanthropy

By Jon Mattson. p. 10

More about the beast in all of us for Dungeons & Dragons.

From The Sorcerer's Scroll

By Gary Gygax. p. 17

The logic behind the melee system.
