Dragon issue 300, 26 Years of Dungeons and Dragons, was released in October of 2002. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Previews

By Uncredited. p. 14

  • The Sorcerer novel.
  • The Thousand Orcs novel.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Hellish Fangs on Abyssal Wings

By James Wyatt, illustrated by Sam Wood. p. 28–38

Fiendish dragons from the lower planes: howling, pyroclastic, rust, styx, and tarterian dragons.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: By the Hands of Hags

By F. Wesley Schneider, illustrated by Arnie Swekel. p. 40–43

New magic items crafted by hags.

The Minions of Darkness

By Eric Cagle, illustrated by Jonathan Wayshak. p. 58–65

Prestige Classes for cultists devoted to doppelgangers, kraken, kuo-toa, or rakshasa.

Class Acts: The Flesheater

By Monte Cook, illustrated by Rob Alexander. p. 68–69

A Prestige Class for a tribe of cannibal halflings.

The Ecology of...: Shrouded in Death

By Terry Edwards, illustrated by Wayne Reynolds. p. 78–83

The "ecology" of the mummy.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: Old Angrath's Smokehouse

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by David Day. p. 84–87

A local landmark in Red Larch that is also an important meeting place/supply dump for the Harpers.
