Total Eclipse

Total Eclipse was the twenty-second issue of the Advanced Dungeons &amp; Dragons comics series set in the Forgotten Realms and published by . It was written by Dan Mishkin, drawn by Jan Duursema, and edited by Elliot S. Maggin. It is the fourth and final installment in the "Phases of the Moon" story arc.
