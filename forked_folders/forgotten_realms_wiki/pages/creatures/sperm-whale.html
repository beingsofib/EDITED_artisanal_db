Sperm whales, also known as cachalots, were large carnivorous whales.

Behavior

Sperm whales lived in groups called pods. They communicated with each other via whale-song. They maintained positive relations with good-aligned sentient aquatic species. Sperm whales were curious about surface-dwelling humanoids but cautious around those who hunted whales.

Combat

Sperm whales defended themselves by using their massive tail as a weapon.

Habitat

Sperm whales lived in oceans and seas. They were known to inhabit the waters of the Sea of Fallen Stars.

Biology

Sperm whales were carnivorous and hunted large fish and octopuses.

Uses

Sperm whales naturally produced spermaceti. This waxy substance was used by wizards to craft white candles that were used in spellcasting. Additionally, sperm whales produced ambergris, which was very rare and could sell for up to 20,000 gp. Due to their habit of swallowing entire boats whole, treasure could be found within the stomach of a sperm whale.
