Dragon+ 15

Dragon+ issue 15 was released on August 31, 2017. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Tomb of Annihilation: Preview

By Bart Carroll. pp. 5

A preview of the Tomb of Annihilation adventure, featuring several excerpts from the book.

Fiction: Qawasha & Kupalué

Main article: Fiction: Qawasha & Kupalué

By Adam Lee. pp. 7

The first part of a short fiction piece about Qawasha and Kupalué from Tomb of Annihilation.

Betrayal at Baldur's Gate

By Chris Dupuis. pp. 8

Developer Chris Dupuis details the design process of the board game Betrayal at Baldur's Gate.

With Every Step Comes Annihilation

By Kiel Chenier. pp. 10

A preview of the Tomb of Annihilation Adventure System Board Game.

Tales from Candlekeep

By Matt Chapman. pp. 11

A preview of the video game Tales from Candlekeep: Tomb of Annihilation.

Neverwinter: Tomb of Annihilation

By James Pickard. pp. 13

A look at the Tomb of Annihilation module for the video game Neverwinter.

Travels in Chult

By Matt Chapman, illustrated by Jen Mei. pp. 14

An article describing the making of several "travel poster"-style advertisements for the Neverwinter video game.

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll, Cartography by Jason A. Engle, Mike Schley and Lee Moyer. pp. 19

Several free maps from the Tomb of Annihilation adventure:

  • Chult
  • Port Nyanzaru
  • Dungrunglung
  • A merchant's villa

Frost Giant's Fury Part 4

By Matt Chapman pp. 21

A free peek (the first six pages) at the latest issue of Frost Giant's Fury featuring Minsc and Boo.
