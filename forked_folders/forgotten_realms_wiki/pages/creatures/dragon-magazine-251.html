Dragon issue 251, Elvenkind, was released in September of 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Magic of the Seldarine

By Chris Perry. p. 28

Gods of the elves.

Knotwards & Woodsongs

By Steve Berman. p. 36

Sylvan elf magic.

By Any Other Name

By Owen K.C. Stephens. p. 52-54

Elf names.

Wyrms of the North: Olothontor, The Minstrel Wyrm

By Ed Greenwood. p. 70-74

Olothontor, another northern dragon.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Missing Links

By Johnathan M. Richards. p. 76–80

Monstrous evolutionary links.

  • Bloodstinger
  • Boneslither
  • Marble pudding
  • Shadow panther

The Ecology of the Wererat

By Kristin J. Johnson. p. 82-85

The Wererat, lyncanthropic ratlings.

Rogues Gallery: Thornhold: The Harpers at Twilight

By Elaine Cunningham. p. 86–90

NPCs from the Realms' Harpers.

ProFiles: Tony Diterlizzi

By Allen Varney. p. 120

About artist Tony Diterlizzi.
