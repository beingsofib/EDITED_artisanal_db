Large spiders were spiders considerable larger than their small normal cousins, yet mostly acted like them.

Description

Large spiders, despite their name were, around in diameter.

Behavior

They would scuttle about on walls, floors, and ceilings, weaving their webs and hunting for prey. They would build strong sticky webs, then lurk nearby waiting for prey to become entangled. Only the strongest of persons would be unaffected, as most would at least have some difficulty removing themselves from the webs, allowing the large spiders to strike.

Combat

Large spiders would bite and inject their prey with a weak, but deadly, poison.

Ecology

Habitat

Large spiders were found in the starwood parts of the great Cormanthor forest, although they would sometimes be encountered in the midwood.

Relationship

Both Lolth and Zanassu were capable of calling upon large spiders to serve and fight for them at a moment's notice. They often served as familiars to magic-users such as necromancers.

Summoning

A large spider could be summoned by the summon monster II spell, while several could be called up by the summon spider spell, depending on the caster's ability.

History

Sometime around a couple of large spiders were encountered in Ravens Bluff by a group of adventurers looking for a wererat.

Subspecies

Flying spiders were a rare subspecies of the large spider.
