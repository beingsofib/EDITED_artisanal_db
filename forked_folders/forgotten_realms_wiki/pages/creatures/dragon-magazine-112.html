Dragon issue 112 was released in August of 1986. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Dinosaurs

By Stephen Inniss. p. 10-14, 16, 66-77

Revised stats for dinosaurs.

Revenge of the Nobodies

By Joseph R. Ravitts. p. 19

Possible mass movements that the peasants of an AD&D-type fantasy world might be drawn to.

Cloaked in magic

By Ed Greenwood. p. 27

Elminster clues Ed in on magical cloaks.

Armor, piece by piece

By Matt Bandy. p. 32-34

A partial armor system, adapted from Oriental Adventures, plus hit locations.
