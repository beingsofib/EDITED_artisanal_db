Dragon issue 71 was released in March of 1983. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

From the Sorceror's Scroll: New druid spells...naturally!

By Gary Gygax. p. 5-9

15 druid spells for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

  • Changestaff
  • Cloudburst
  • Detect Poison
  • Flame Blade
  • Goodberry
  • Liveoak
  • Moonbeam
  • Reflecting Pool
  • Slow Poison
  • Spike Growth
  • Spike Stones
  • Starshine
  • Sunray
  • Transmute Water To Dust

Mind of the Monster: Give creatures the brains they were born with

By Bruce Humphrey. p. 14

A guide for DMs on how to role-play the monsters effectively.

Who gets the first swing? Attack Priority system offers more realism without more work

By Ronald Hall. p. 23

A variant initiative system that takes into account weapon length, weapon speed, and dexterity.

Sage Advice: Solid answers to astral questions

By Roger E. Moore. p. 47

Q&A about other planes in Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.
