Dragon issue 357, Wilderness, was released in July of 2007. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Demonomicon of Iggwilv: Demogorgon: Prince of Demons

By James Jacobs. p. 20-32

History, goals, and other background for Demogorgon.

Arcane Botanica

By Scott Noel. p. 52

Magic plants.

The Ecology of the Titan

By Nicolas Logue. p. 60-64

A scholarly treatise on the Titans.

Savage Tidings: Gazing into the Abyss

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 66–70

Dark pacts and the abyss.

Volo's Guide: Renegades of Darkhold

By George Krashos. p. 72-75

This article details the characters Sememmon and Ashemmi.

Class Acts: Divine: Plant Companions

By Hal Maclean. p. 90

Plant companions for druids and rangers.

Class Acts: Warrior: Monk Guide

By Amber E. Scott. p. 92

All your monk charts.
