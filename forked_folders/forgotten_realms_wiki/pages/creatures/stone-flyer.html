Stone flyers were magical beasts which hunted prey deep in the Underdark.

Description

These creatures looked like wolves with large wings. Most stone flyers were earth tones, such as black, brown, or gray.

Personality

Very social creatures, stone flyers were often moving together in packs of up to 10 members.

Combat

Stone flyers moved through stone as if it was air and attacked victims with surprise. These earth elemental beasts worked in packs while hunting for prey, using their combined effort to bring down those who either travelled alone or were lagging behind a group. Once prey had been brought down, they would use their unique ability of extending their capacity to glide through the earth to drag the dead or unconscious prey along into the earth where they could more securely consume it.

Ecology

They bore live young, which meant that those who wished to capture a stone flyer would have to risk both the ire of the parents and the resistance of the young.

Habitat

Stone flyers were most commonly found in the wilderness and desolate parts of the middledark and lowerdark.

Relationship

The social aspect of the stone flyers, combined with their ability to extend their earth glideing powers meant that they could be trained as loyal and very effective mounts, able to navigate in the realms of the underdark. Thus young trainable stone flyers could go for as much as 10,000 gp each, while the cost of training one to bear a rider where a mere 2,500 gp.

Figurine of wondrous power

Due to their great usefulness of riding through the earth, stone flyers were one of the more common, but expensive figurines of wondrous power, carved in sardonyx and going for upwards of 16,500 gp each.
