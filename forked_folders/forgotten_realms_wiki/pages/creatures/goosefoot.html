Goosefoot was a type of leafy plant that grew in Maztica and was among the main food sources for the Azuposi humans who dwelled there.

Availability

Goosefoot was known to grow in the Pasocada Basin of Maztica. These were cultivated by the Azuposi, along with beeweed, calabashes, juniper, mayz, melons, pigweed, pinyon pine, prickly pear, squash, and various beans.
