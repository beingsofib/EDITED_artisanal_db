The Dragon issue 38 was released in June of 1980. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Leomund's Tiny Hut: Rearranging and Redefining the Mighty Dragon

By Lenard Lakofka. p. 10–11, 40–42

New rules for subduing dragons, along with magic use and other things to beef up dragons.

Which Way Is Up? Well, It All Depends... Tesseracts

By Allen Wells. p. 14

Using tesseracts (4th-dimensional cubes) in your Advanced Dungeons & Dragons game.

From the Sorcerer's Scroll: Good Isn’'t Stupid, Paladins and Rangers, and Female Dwarves Do Have Beards!

By Gary Gygax. p. 22

Gary talks about how "good" actions aren't always cut and dried, nor are characters who do good blindly stupid, and once again avers that female dwarves have beards.
