Dragon magazine 410 was released in PDF format only in April 2012. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Howl at the Moon

By Robert J. Schwalb, illustrated by Beth Trott, Eva Widermann, Ben Wootten, Jesper Ejsing. p. 15–25

An article on lycanthropes.

Creatures

Werebear • Wererat • Werewolf

Eye on the Realms: The Talking Door

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Kai Carpenter. p. 33–36

A description of the front door of the Inn of the Dripping Dagger in Waterdeep.
