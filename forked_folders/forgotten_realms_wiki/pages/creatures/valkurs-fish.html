Valkur's fish

Valkur's fish were a spined species of fish that inhabited many of Toril's seas, from tropical to arctic. The fish was named for the belief that the eye-like dark spot on its side was left by Valkur's thumb. Fishermen thought the sight of a Valkur's fish brought good luck.
