Yellow Finned Oponi

Creature
nameYellow Finned Oponi
locationRiver Sundarra, Laerakond

The Yellow Finned Oponi was a variety of fish found in the River Sundarra, in Laerakond. It was a local delicacy in Ramekho, and was cooked in several ways there in the late 15th century DR.
