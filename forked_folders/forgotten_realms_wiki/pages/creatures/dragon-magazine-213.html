Dragon issue 213, Prospecting the Planes, was released in January of 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Godsmen, Bleakers, Guvners, & Takers

By Richard Baker. p. 12

Secrets of the Planescape setting's factions.

The Demiplane of Shadow

By Edward Bonny. p. 22-29

An exploration of the Plane of Shadow and its shade residents.

Series Magic

By Steven E. Schend. p. 96-98

Our chronicler gets a visit from Khelben himself — almost.

Elminster's Notebook: Aldreth Fireshar

By Ed Greenwood. p. 102

Aldreth Fireshar, a wizard with a variety of unusual spells and unpleasant associates.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 120

New products for February.

  • The Moonsea sourcebook.
