Ab'i was a plant that grew in the High Desert of Zakhara.

Description

Viewed from the surface, ab'i looked like tall brown grass. The succulent blood-red tubers of this plant laid around 3 feet (0.9 meters) underground. These tubers were edible and contained up to a gallon (3.8 liters) of fresh water.
