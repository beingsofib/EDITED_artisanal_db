Eclipse lion

Creature
nameEclipse Lion
size5eLarge
locationNeverwinter, Sword Coast North

Eclipse lions were a magical species of lion, said to be so powerful they blocked out the light from any sun.

Description

An eclipse lion was a large feline with a powerful body and a thick mane. The entirety of its body was almost pitch black, with the only spots of color being its yellow eyes. These lions had a constant halo visible around them, similar to that of an eclipsing sun.

Abilities

An eclipse lion could be summoned forth by its master onto the field of battle. While on the Prime Material plane, it magically boosted the master's physical strength and damage output, as well as offered them magical protection.

History

In the late 15 century DR, some of these majestic beasts were sold in the auctions of Neverwinter to the wealthiest of the city's adventurers.
