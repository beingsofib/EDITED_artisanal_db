Woodlily

Plant
nameWoodlily
typePlant
flowercolorPink and purple

Woodlily was a pink and purple flowering plant that bloomed in late autumn in the Savage Frontier and Northwest Faerûn.

Usage

The plant found use in strong potions of healing.
