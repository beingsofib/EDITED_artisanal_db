Dragon issue 150, Halloween magic and October dreams, was released in October of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Dragon's Bestiary: All life crawls where mind flayers rule

By Stephen Inniss. p. 12–16

Dangerous fauna from the homeworld of the illithids.

  • Cessirid
  • Bracer
  • Kigrid
  • Saltor
  • Amorph
  • Brown mold
  • Gas spore
  • Shrieker

Fangs Alot

By David Howery. p. 28

A corrected entry for the vampire, misprinted in the initial release of Monstrous Compendium Volume One

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 40

New products for October.

  • Avatar Trilogy, Book Three: Waterdeep
  • Spelljammer: AD&D Adventures in Space
  • TM4 Waterdeep
  • FRE3 Waterdeep
