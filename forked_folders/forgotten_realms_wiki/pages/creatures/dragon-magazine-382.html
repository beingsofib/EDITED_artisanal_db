Dragon issue 382 was released in PDF format only in December of 2009. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Swordmage Essentials: Art of the Blade

By Andy Collins. p. 17–24

Learn the secrets to building and playing the Swordmage, arcane defender.

Familiar Power

By Arthur Wright. p. 25–29

Familiars are more than just shoulder dressing for your wizard. These new powers feature kickers that activate if you have a familiar.

Class Acts: Druid

By Matthew Newman. p. 53–55

New options for the shifting druid in all of us.

Winning Races: Drow

By Bruce R. Cordell. p. 65–67

The drow are known for their treachery and viciousness, but some learn to tap these innate callings to serve a higher cause.

Winning Races: Dusk Elf

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 68–70

Twilight elves are Sehanine's chosen, and they have gained some of the patron's mystery, and much of her power.
