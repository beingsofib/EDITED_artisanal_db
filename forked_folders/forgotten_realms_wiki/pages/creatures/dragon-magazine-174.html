Dragon issue 174, You haven't a ghost of a chance in Ravenloft, was released in October of 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Cry Wolf!

By Tim Malto, illustrated by Terry Pavlet. p. 23–27

Three new AD&D game monsters that are a howling success at frightening heroes.

  • Dread wolf
  • Vampiric wolf
  • Stone wolf

Bugged About Something?

By Gregory W. Detwiler, illustrated by Dan Burr. p. 34–36

How to torment your players with giant insects.

Collect them all!

By James M. Ward. p. 36

A preview of the 1991 set of AD&D Trading Cards.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 63

Clue Corner: Curse of the Azure Bonds.

Defeating More With Less

By Gregory W. Detwiler, illustrated by L. A. Williams. p. 79–87

Make your monsters fight smarter, not harder.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 88

New products for November.

  • Canticle novel.
  • Endless Armies module.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Ioun stones: Where do you go if you want some more?

By Matthew P. Hargenrader, illustrated by Kevin Davies. p. 90–94

All about Ioun stones, including new varieties.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Who guards the ioun stones? Here are some candidates

By Matthew P. Hargenrader, illustrated by Tom Baxa and Matthew P. Hargenrader. p. 96–100

New monsters connected to the Ioun stones.

  • Chamrol
  • Crystalle
  • Energy pod
  • Glomus
  • Shard
  • Spined shard
  • Trilling crystal
