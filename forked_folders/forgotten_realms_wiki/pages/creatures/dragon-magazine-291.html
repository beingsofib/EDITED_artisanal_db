Dragon issue 291, Gnomes, was released in January of 2002. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: Robin D. Laws

By Michael G. Ryan. p. 18

Profile of RPG mainstay Robin D. Laws.

The Little-Lympics

By Matthew Sernett and Robin D. Laws, illustrated by Mike Vilardi. p. 36–41

Games gnomes play.

Abuse Your Illusions

By Rich Redman, illustrated by Justine Mara Andersen. p. 42–46

Using and designing illusion spells.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Good Things Come in Small Packages

By Brian Dunnell and Jonathan Richards, illustrated by Jeffrey Carlisle. p. 48–53

Magic items for gnomes.

Water, Water Everywhere

By Skip Williams, illustrated by Tom Gianni. p. 60–62

Underwater combat rules.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: The Skondarr

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by David Day. p. 80–83

The Skondarr, a cavern network near Zhentil Keep.

Class Acts: Gnome Giant Killer

By Andy Collins, illustrated by Scott Roller. p. 84–85

Gnomish prestige.

Sage Advice

By Unknown. p. 104-108

Your guide to the planes.
