Dragon issue 85 was released in May of 1984. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Here's to your health: Second thoughts on first aid in the AD&D game

By Kim Mohan. p. 9

Healing and curing magic.

Special skills, special thrills: Varying powers helps avoid cleric stereotypes

By Roger E. Moore. p. 12

A variant giving Clerics powers depending on the god they worship.

Clerics must be deity-bound: How one acts depends on who one worships

By Fraser Sherman. p. 16

Role-playing clerics based on the gods they worship.

The Ecology of the Ixitxachitl

By Ed Greenwood. p. 24-27

A scholarly treatise on the Ixitxachitl.
