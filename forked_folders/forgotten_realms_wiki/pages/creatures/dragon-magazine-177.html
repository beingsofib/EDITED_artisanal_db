Dragon issue 177, A Dungeon Master's work is never done, was released in January of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 60

  • Review: Gateway to the Savage Frontier
  • Clue Corner: Pools of Darkness and Secret of the Silver Blades.

Defenders of the Hearth

By Chris Perry. p. 82-88

Specialty priests of the halfling gods.

  • Arvoreen
  • Brandobaris
  • Cyrrollalee
  • Sheela Peryroyl
  • Urogalan
  • Yondalla

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 94

New products for January and February.

  • Monstrous Compendium Forgotten Realms Appendix sourcebook.
  • Pools of Darkness novel.

Think Big

By Thomas M. Kane. p. 96

Owning and riding an elephant.
