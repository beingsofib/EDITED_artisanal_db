Berronar's cap was a type of mushroom found in Faerûn and likely named for Berronar Truesilver. It was used to make healing draughts and salves when combined with vaundyr vine.

History

When the forces of Neverwinter attempted to secure the Blackdagger Ruins, Alinda Greenwood sought out Berronar's caps and vaundyr vines to make medicines with, in order to treat their many injuries. The mushrooms grew widely in the abandoned mines around the region.
