The Spirit of Myrrth

The Spirit of Myrrth - The Spirit of Myrrth, Part 1 was the fifth issue of the Advanced Dungeons &amp; Dragons comics series set in the Forgotten Realms and published by . It was written by Dan Mishkin and Jan Duursema, drawn by Duursema, and edited by Barbara Kesel. It began "The Spirit of Myrrth" story arc.
