Dragon issue 341, Constructs, was released in March of 2006. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Demonomicon of Iggwilv: Baphomet: Prince of Beasts

By James Jacobs, illustrated by David Bircham. p. 20–33

Baphomet and his cults.

The Ecology of the Inevitable

By David Noonan, illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 52–57

A scholarly treatise on the inevitable.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Animated Antiquities

By Jeremy Hogg, illustrated by Tom Fowler. p. 64–66

Magic items that make peoples' lives easier.

The Ties that Bind

By Matthew Sernett, illustrated by Attila Adorjany. p. 68–74

A preview of pact magic from Tome of Magic, including two original vestiges.
