Dragon issue 390 was released in PDF format only in August of 2010. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Winning Races: Eladrin

By Tim Eagon. p. 33

To eladrin, magic is Corellon’s supreme gift, but Corellon is not the only path to magical power.

Winning Races: Gnomes

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 37

Stealth and guile, wit and cunning are tools gnomes use to survive.

Winning Races: Drow

By Rachel Cirricione and Arnie Frank. p. 41

The bloodthirsty social order of the drow permeates every aspect of their society, including their institutions of learning.

Fiction: Lord of the Darkways

By Ed Greenwood. p. 55–77

Manshoon is determined to become Lord of the Darkways, no matter how many lives it costs.
