Trachodon (), also known as anatosaurus, was a species of dinosaur.

Description

A trachodon was a large, duck-billed dinosaur.

Combat

Trachodon's only defense against a predator was its long, powerful tail.

Biology

Trachodons were herbivorous creatures who lived in herds.
