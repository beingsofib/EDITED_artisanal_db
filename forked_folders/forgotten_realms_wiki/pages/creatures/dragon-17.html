Dragon+ 17

Dragon+ issue 17 was released on December 18, 2017. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Betrayal at Baldur's Gate: Holiday Haunt

By Matt Chapman. pp. 8

A "haunt" (scenario) for the board game Betrayal at Baldur's Gate, featuring Krampus.

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll, Cartography by Mike Schley, Robert Lazzaretti and Jason Thompson. pp. 13

Several free maps from the Tomb of Annihilation adventure:

  • Tomb of the Nine Gods (all 6 levels).
