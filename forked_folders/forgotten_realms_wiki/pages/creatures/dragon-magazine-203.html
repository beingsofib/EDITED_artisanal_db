Dragon issue 203, Science Fantasy will blow you away, was released in March of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Arcane Lore: The Blue Books of Othyisar Du'Morde

By Leonard A. Schmidt. pp. 34–37

Presenting lightning spells to spark interest in wizard PCs.

charge • electric bow • lightning shield • lightning strike • normal aura • staffspell • static field • storm

The Plane Truth Part I: Codifying Sigil

By David "Zeb" Cook. pp. 74–76

Excerpts from the Codex of Infinite Planes.

Role-playing Reviews

By Rick Swan. pp. 86

The Astromundi Cluster.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 120

New products for April.

  • Elves of Evermeet sourcebook.
  • The Complete Sha'ir's Handbook sourcebook.
  • The Ruins of Undermountain II: The Deep Levels boxed set.
