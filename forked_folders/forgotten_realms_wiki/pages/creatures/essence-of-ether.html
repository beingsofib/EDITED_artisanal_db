Essence of ether, was a somnolent drug and an illicit substance, inhaled to induce drowsiness and sleep.

History

In the late 15 century DR, members of the Guild worked with smugglers who brought drugs, weapons, firewine, and other substances into Baldur's Gate. Bowls of essence of ether were among such illicit goods.
