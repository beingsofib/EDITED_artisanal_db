Walleye pollock was a species of cold-water pollock that inhabited the western current of the Sea of Moving Ice in Icewind Dale.

Description

Walleye pollock was believed to breed excessively after stormy summers. They were a common catch among the northern fishers. Fishmongers were well aware of the abundance and took advantage of the fact that many confused walleye pollock for the more expensive haddock fish.
