Sea hydra

Sea hydras were a rare aquatic offshoot of hydras.

Description

These creatures largely resembled the common hydra of the surface, but had flippers in place of legs.

Ecology

These creatures were only found in the lower depths of the Sea of Fallen Stars, an area that was approximately beneath the sea.
