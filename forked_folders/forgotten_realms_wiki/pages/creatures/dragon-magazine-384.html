Dragon issue 384 was released in PDF format only in February of 2010. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Legendary Figurines

By Robert J. Schwalb, illustrated by Matias Tapia. p. 18–24

More figurines of wondrous power.

Winning Races: Gnomes

By Brian Yablon. p. 57

Gnomes make poor defenders… or do they?

Channel Divinity: Mielikki

By Matthew Sernett. p. 65

In the Realms, Mielikki is called the Daughter of Silvanus, but learn how her followers pursue her goals in the world.
