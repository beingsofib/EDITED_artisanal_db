Dragon issue 416 was released in PDF format only in October of 2012. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bazaar of the Bizarre: In A Vampiric Vein

By Walter Kammerer, illustrated by Jason Juta. p. 23–24

Vampires need more than blood to survive. Here’s a small trove of magic items popular among children of the night.

Eye on the Realms: The Awakener of Golems

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Patrick McEvoy. p. 28-32

A mysterious figure has the power to bring inert golems to life temporarily, but to what end?
