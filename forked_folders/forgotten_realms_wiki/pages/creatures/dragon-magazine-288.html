Dragon issue 288, Fear, was released in October of 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Previews

By Uncredited. p. 16

  • Oriental Adventures
  • Sea of Swords

Plots and Schemes: Designing Mystery Adventures

By Bryan Fagan, illustrated by Jeff Laubenstein. p. 38–47

How to design a mystery, with a fairly extensive recommended reading list.

Unhallowed Ground: Building a Fantasy Graveyard

By Rudy Thauberger, illustrated by Val Mayerik. p. 48–51

Different types of graveyard and their features.

Disguise

Uncredited. p. 52–55

Guidance for steering the players in an investigative scenario.

Class Acts: Lifedrinker

By Monte Cook, illustrated by Carlos D'Anda. p. 64–65

A vampire prestige class.

Forgotten Realms Poster Map: Second Part

Uncredited. p. 66

The second of four issues with part of the giant Forgotten Realms poster map.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: The Moonwyvern Inn

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by David Day. p. 74–77

The Moonwyvern Inn, the best meeting place in the middle of nowhere.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Vampire Slayer's Toolbox

By Joseph R. Terrazzino, illustrated by Arnie Swekel. p. 78–82

Anti-vampire gizmos.

Silicon Sorcery: The Legacy of Baldur's Gate: The Bhaalspawn

By Dave Gaider and James Ohlen. p. 100–103

Bhaal is apparently as promiscuous as Zeus, so there's a template for his many children.
