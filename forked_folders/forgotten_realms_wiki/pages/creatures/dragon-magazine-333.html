Dragon issue 333 was released in July of 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

From the Editor: The Remembered Realms

By Erik Mona. p. 8

Coverage about the upcoming Forgotten Realms.

Demonomicon of Iggwilv: Fraz-Urb'luu: Prince of Deception

By James Jacobs, illustrated by Andrew Hou, cartography by Chris West. p. 24-39

Fraz-Urb'luu, the Prince of Deception and Lord of Zoragmelok seeks to kill all humans—starting with you!

The Relics of Faerûn

By George Swan, illustrated by Eric Deschamps. p. 49–54

Religious artifacts of the Forgotten Realms.

The Ecology of the Behir

By Eric Cagle, illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 56-60

A scholarly treatise on the Behir.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Treasures of the Gnome Hills

By Richard Farrese, illustrated by Jeff Laubenstein. p. 66–68

Discover the magical ingenuity of gnomes.

Spellcraft: Visions of Fear

By Peter M. Ball, illustrated by Jonathan Wayshak. p. 70–73

Horrific spells.
