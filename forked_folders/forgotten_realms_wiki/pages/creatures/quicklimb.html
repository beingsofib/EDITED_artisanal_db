Quicklimb

Plant
nameQuicklimb
typeTree
locationThe Vast, north Faerûn

Quicklimb was a type of tree that grew in the Vast.

Alongside shrubs and creepers, quicklimb saplings were quick to take root on the fieldstone and earthen walls around the farms in Maskyr's Eye.
