The stench kow, () also spelled as stench cow, was a species of bison native to the Lower planes.

Description

The bodies of these creatures bore a resemblance to the standard bison, but were misshapen. They had humps over their shoulders, with bulls having the largest, and long horns that curved downwards. Their heads were huge and had pugged features, large round eyes, and lolling tongues. Their fur was largely a dull orange, with a matted greenish mane.

Abilities

These creatures were very hardy compared to the average bison. They were able to withstand severe cold, heat, and poison, and poisonous clouds of both a mundane and magical nature.

The name of these creatures was derived from their breath and bodies having terribly foul odors so horrible that being close to them could cause one to be poisoned, in a similar manner to the spell stinking cloud.

Behavior

These creatures had a basic animal level of intelligence.

Combat

Stench kine fought other creatures by charging at them and trying to trample them.

Ecology

Much like their Prime Material cousins, stench kine traveled in grazing herds.

Diet

Stench kine fed upon much of the noxious and poisonous growths throughout the Lower planes, such as bloodthorn and stinkweed.

Habitats

Stench kine were only found in the Lower planes, being most common in the Nine Hells. They could also be found in Acheron, Gehenna, and Hades.

Relationships

Many archdevils were known to possess some form of stench kow herd.

Usage

The horn of a stench kow could be used as an alternative ingredient for crafting a potion of fire resistance.

The Infernal Rapture restaurant in Avernus often served cheese made from the milk of stench kine. Stench kows also recieved some culinary use. Their meat was used to make shepard's pies and stenchburgers.
