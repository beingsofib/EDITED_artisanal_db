List of dragons

A list of dragons. Both true and lesser dragons are included.

Key

Name

The dragon's primary name, and most relevant aliases.

Kind

The dragon's specific kind. Dragons that have been altered, such as dracoliches or vampiric dragons, are marked as such.

Age

The dragon's age category. This field only applies to true dragons.

Sex

The dragon's sex, represented as F for female and M for male.

Area(s)

The dragon's most recent known habitat, in the format "Specific place, broader region" (unless only the broader region is known). In some cases, multiple areas are provided.

Status

Living Undead Dead/destroyed Unknown

As of

The dragon's most recent appearance or mention, represented as a year or time period. For dragons that are dead or destroyed, the year of death is provided if known. If the year of death is unknown, the year in which they were reported as dead is used, with the year of last sighting provided in parentheses if known.
