Dragon issue 199, Welcome to the wild world of monsters, was released in November of 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Crude, But Effective

By Derek Jensen. p. 16

Simple combat tactics for humanoids.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Those terrible trolls

By Alec Baclawski, illustrated by Mark Nelson. p. 23–28

Discussion of trolls in D&D with some new species.

  • Fire troll
  • Gray troll
  • Phaze troll
  • Stone troll
  • Trollhound

The Known World: Fantastic Heraldry

By Bruce Heard. p. 41–47

Suggestions for using heraldry in D&D.

Campaign Journal: The Black Pegasus Trading Co.

By David Montgomery and Jim Milner, illustrated by Jennell Jaquays, Cartography by Steve Beck. p. 48–54

An interplanetary merchant consortium.

Romancing the Stoneskin

By Compiled by the editors. p. 72–75

Forum readers give advice for dealing with players' who abuse the spell Stoneskin.

Ready, Aim, Fire!

By Bruce A. Heard. p. 96–99

Seige weapons for D&D.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 120

New products for December.

  • Player's Guide to the Forgotten Realms Campaign sourcebook.
  • Soldiers of Ice novel.
  • Volo's Guide to the North sourcebook.
