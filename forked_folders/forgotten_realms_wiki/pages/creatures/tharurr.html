The Tharurr, or Thar's horse, was a smaller type of the heavier breed of horses that were often used as warhorses or workhorses. They were commonly found throughout Damara, Impiltur, the Ride, and Thar.

Description

Intelligent and eager to please, these athletic steeds were easily trained and could easily traverse the mountainous terrain of Faerûn.

History

These horses were often encountered among the ogres of Thar. the breed found increased use among the inhabitants of nearby Realms, including the Erakan barbarian raiders.
