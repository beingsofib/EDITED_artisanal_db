Dragon issue 153, Whatever the immortals want, they surely get, was released in January of 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Following in their Footsteps

By Fraser Sherman. p. 26-34

Why clerics of Poseidon had better not get seasick, and other priestly tidbits.

Game Wizards: An evening (wasted) with Elminster

By Jeff Grubb. p. 48

TSR enlists a special consultant on the new Forgotten Realms tome.

The Ecology of the Manticore

By Spike Y. Jones. p. 56–59, 92–93

A scholarly treatise on the Manticore.

The Role of Books Review: Tantras

By John C. Bunnel. p. 51

The Role of Books Review: Waterdeep

By John C. Bunnel. p. 51

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 98

New products for December and January.

  • Kara-Tur Trail Map
  • Pool of Radiance novel.
  • The Bloodstone Lands sourcebook.
  • The Halfling's Gem novel.
