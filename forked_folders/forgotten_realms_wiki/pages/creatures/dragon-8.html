Dragon+ 8

Dragon+ issue 8 was released on June 30, 2016. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Volo's Guide to Befriending Giants

By Ed Greenwood. pp. 6

A Volo article on the subject of giants. 8/Volo's Guide to Befriending Giants

Power Leveling in Neverwinter: 10 Essential tips, tricks, and tactics

By Troy Whitney. pp. 14

A handful of advice for how to level up in the video game Neverwinter. 8/Power Leveling in Neverwinter

Forging the Forgotten Realms

By John Houlihan. pp. 15

An interview with Ed Greenwood on the origins of the Realms, and his novel Death Masks. 8/Forging the Forgotten Realms

Map of the Month

''By Christopher Perkins, Cartography by Mike Schley.pp. 20

A free map from the Vault of the Dracolich adventure.
