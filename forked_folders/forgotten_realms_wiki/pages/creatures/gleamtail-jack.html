Gleamtail jack

Creature
nameGleamtail Jack
locationElemental Chaos

A gleamtail jack was a type of fish known to live in the Elemental Chaos.

Description

Gleamtail jacks were albino colored. They were about the size of a human's thumb.

Abilities

Gleamtail jacks could swim through any elemental matter and could shift between planes.

Scholarship

Information about gleamtail jacks could be found in the Vault of the Sages in the city of Silverymoon.
