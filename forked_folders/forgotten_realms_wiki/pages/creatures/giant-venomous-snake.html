The giant venomous snake was a larger and often more deadly version of the normal venomous snake, being as large or larger than most humans and demihumans.

Behavior

Like their lesser cousins, these great venomous snakes would attack prey by injecting it with venom through their hollow fangs, but some species could also constrict lesser creatures like halflings. They would roam in groups of up to 6 individuals, biting their targets with deadly poison and afterwards swallowing their prey whole.

Species

Black mamba: A very aggressive snake that could grow to over in length.

Bushmaster: A powerful form of viper.

Viper: A common type of venomous snake that came in various sizes and colorings.

Ecology

Habitat

Giant venomous snakes inhabited the Starwood parts of the Cormanthor forest. They were also encountered deep in the Raurin desert in the Temple of Set.
