Dragon issue 279, Elves, was released in January of 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: Jason Carl

By Michael G. Ryan. p. 12–18

About Jason Carl, the author of Sword and Fist.

Up on a Soapbox: What the Heck is a Roleplaying Game?!

By Gary Gygax. p. 20

What makes an RPG.

Coming Soon

By Uncredited. p. 26

  • Legacy of the Drow Collector's Edition novel.
  • The Summoning novel.

Countdown to the Forgotten Realms: Realms of the Realms

Uncredited p. 28

The evolutions of the Realms up to 1372 DR.

Leaf & Thorn

By Robin D. Laws, Illustrated by Vinod Rams. p. 34–40

The secret life of elves.

Revenge of the Spider Queen

By Monte Cook, Illustrated by Carlo Arelano. p. 42–47

The Hand of Vengeance, a group of heroes of Lolth.

The Elven Marketplace

By Steve Kenson, Illustrated by Kennon James. p. 48–50

Things for elves to buy.

An Elven Lexicon

By Sean K. Reynolds. p. 56–59

Speaking elf.

Unusual Suspects 3: Monks & Druids

By James Wyatt, Illustrated by Damon Brown. p. 62–65

Monks and druids from unusual races.

Vs.: Fiends

By James Wyatt. p. 78

Fighting fiends.

The New Adventures of Volo: Lost Treasures of Cormyr, Part 2

By Ed Greenwood, Illustrated by Ron Spencer. p. 80–83

More on the riches and legacies of Cormyr.

PC Portraits: Elves

By Mike May. p. 94
