Quell were ghostly undead creatures that despised the mere concept of life and hated the connection the living had to the deities. In essence, they hated the act of worship and the very presence of a quell isolated a worshiper from the touch of their god.

Abilities

A quell could lash out at divine spellcasters of any faith, an act known as intercession, preventing them from accessing the Power. Several of them would work together and intercede against even notable powerful clerics.

Notable appearances

Because of their unique abilities, quell were often used by necromancers to protect themselves from divine powers.

A quell was sent by Pandorym against Thormud Horn in . Quell were among the armies of the undead commanded by the nighthaunt Ysval, when they assaulted the nation of Thay in the year 1375 DR.
