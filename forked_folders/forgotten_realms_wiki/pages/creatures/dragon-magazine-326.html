Dragon issue 326 was released in December of 2004. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

A Novel Approach: The Rite

By Richard Lee Byers, art provided by Wizards of the Coast. p.34-35

Meet the main characters from Richard Lee Byers' The Rite.

The Dungeon Delver's Guide

By Jayce K. Purvis and Mark A. Hart, illustrated by Ramón Pérez. p. 42–49

What every doughty underground explorer should know.

Down the Drain

By Chris DeKalb and Jacob Steinmann, illustrated by David Wahlstrom. p. 50–55

Adventuring in the city sewers.

The Ecology of the Rakshasa

By Eric Cagle, illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 66-70

A scholarly treatise on the Rakshasa.

Spellcraft: Cantrips and Orisons of the Academy of Apprentices

By Kieran Turley, illustrated by Mike May. p. 72–74

Cantrips. Minor League power; Major League cool.
