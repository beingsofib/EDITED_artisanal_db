Dragon issue 354, The Modrons March Again!, was released in April of 2007. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Return of the Modrons

By Ken Marable. p. 35-49

Everything you wanted to know about Modrons, but were afraid to ask.

The Ecology of the Kopru

By Tito Leati. p. 58-63

A scholarly treatise on the Kopru.

Volo's Guide: Cormanthor: War Amidst the Trees

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 70-74

This article updates details on Cormanthor to bring its history current up to 1374 DR.
