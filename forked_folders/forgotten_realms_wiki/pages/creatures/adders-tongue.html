Adder's tongue

Plant
nameAdder's Tongue
typePlant

Adder's tongue was a type of plant that could be used in alchemy.

History

While fighting for New Sharandar, Finderal Birchspear and Triste sought to enter the fomorian coven in the Witch Fen led by Magra Curse-Eye. Their lair was protected by magical shields created by alchemical rituals, but by adding adder's tongue, certain cauldrons could be neutralized, allowing the coven to be invaded and destroyed.
