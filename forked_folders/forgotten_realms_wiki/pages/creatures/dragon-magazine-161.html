Dragon issue 161, A little excitement never hurts, was released in September of 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Romance and Adventure

By Tom Schlosser. p. 15

Adding romance to your campaign.

It's sort of like a wand...

By Gary Coppa. p. 21

Bringing a sense of mystery and wonder back to your games.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 52

Clue Corner: Curse of the Azure Bonds.

The Role of Books

By John C. Bunnell. p. 64

Review of the novels Horselords and Ironhelm.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 85

New products for September and October.

  • Draconomicon sourcebook.
  • Homeland novel.

Sage Advice

By Skip Williams. p. 88-89

The Ecology of the Griffon

By Christopher Kederich. p. 90-93

A scholarly treatise on the Griffon.
