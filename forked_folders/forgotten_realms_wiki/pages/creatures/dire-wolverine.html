Dire wolverines, also known as Giant wolverines were a larger and more dangerous variety of the common wolverine.

Combat

These beasts were capable of becoming enraged while fighting others.

Ecology

Dire wolverines were known to attack settlements, killing livestock and raiding food stores in the process.

Relationships

Gray orcs often kept dire wolverines as guard animals.

Summoning

A dire wolverine ally could be summoned by the spell summon nature's ally IV, while a fiendish dire wolverine could be called forth by the casting of the summon monster V spell.

History

The arctic dwarf village of Frostrill in the Silver Marches had tamed a number of dire wolverines to assist in hunting game.
