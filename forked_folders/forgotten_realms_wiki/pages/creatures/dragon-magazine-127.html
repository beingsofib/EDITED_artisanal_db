Dragon issue 127 was released in November of 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

No Quarter!

By Arn Ashleigh Parker. p. 22

A set of combat maneuvers for fighters, including parry, feint, and feign death.

Two Hands Are Better Than One

By Donald D. Miller. p. 32-34

Divides weapons by size and provides a chart for how many hands are required to use a weapon, based on the wielder's race.

In Defense of the Shield

By Tim Merrett. p. 36

Increase the AC bonus shields provide, and has rules for different sized shields and using them as weapons.

In the Heat of the Fight

By Sean Holland. p. 46–47

Several new combat situations, and rules for dealing with them.

A Menagerie of Martial Arts

By Len Carpenter. p. 48

Twenty new martial arts styles, including twelve animal-inspired styles, using the new rules in Oriental Adventures.

The Ecology of the Yeti

By Thomas Kiefer. p. 56-60

The Yeti, presented by dialog, followed by game material in the footnotes.

Arcane Lore: A Legacy of Illusions

By Arthur Collins. p. 62–64

New spells for illusionists.

  • Anesthesia
  • Blandness
  • Color blindness
  • Echoes
  • Lapse
  • Numbness
  • Remove smell
  • Seizure
  • Tunnel vision

The Dragon's Bestiary: Slimy, Gross, and Yucky

By Scott Bennie and Thomas M. Kane. p. 68

Stats for two slimes; Xador's fluid and Quagmire.
