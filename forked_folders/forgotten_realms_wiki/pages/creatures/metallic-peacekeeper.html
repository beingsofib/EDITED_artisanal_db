Metallic Peacekeepers were the humanoid variety of metallic sentinels, constructs created by metallic dragons to watch over and keep safe weaker beings that the benevolent dragons cared for.

Description

Like all sentinels, peacekeepers were created from fine, filigreed metal, of the variety associated with their master. Similarly, they were powered by the type of energy associated with their creator's breath weapon.

Abilities

These beings possessed an innate, telepathic bond with their draconic creators. They could communicate in the common and draconic languages. This bond could be replicated with another being with the use of a mimic battery.

Combat

Metallic peacekeepers made simple attacks with their fists, but preferred an alternate method to subdue aggrieved foes. They could release a soothing mist in an area with a radius of centered upon themself.

Their physical forms could not be harmed by fire or altered in any way, even by magical means.

Like other constructs they were immune to charm spells and effects, did not succumb to exhaustion, and could not be poisoned or frightened.

History

The Abeiran dragonborn artificers of the Inquest maintained a collection of ten metallic peacekeepers within the Supreme Forge in the city of Xorvintroth.
