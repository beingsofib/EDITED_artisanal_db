Wooly crabs were a species of crabs that dwelled in the shallows and seagrass flats of the Sea of Moving Ice in Icewind Dale.

Description

Wooly crabs were small crustaceans that were found in the shallows and seagrass flats in the north and were often fished by the locals. They received the name after the vast quantities of tiny, pliable spines that grew out of their carapaces, resembling wool. These spines were the wooly crabs' main method of defense against predators.
