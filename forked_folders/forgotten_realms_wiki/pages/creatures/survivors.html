Survivors

Survivors was the twenty-sixth issue of the Advanced Dungeons &amp; Dragons comics series set in the Forgotten Realms and published by . It was written by Dan Mishkin, drawn by Jan Duursema, and edited by Kim Yale. It is the third and final installment in the "Scavengers" story arc.
