The Dragon issue 21 was released in November 1978. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Prophet Proofing or How To Counter Foretelling Spells

By David Schroeder. p. 11

Tips for DMs who have parties that rely heavily on Scrying magic to make adventuring easier.

Sensible Sorcery

By Ronald Pehr. p. 12

A set of rules for spell research to avoid abuse of the research system.
