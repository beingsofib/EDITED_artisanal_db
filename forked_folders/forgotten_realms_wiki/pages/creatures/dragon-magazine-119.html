Dragon issue 119 was released in March of 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

"Is There a Doctor In the Forest?"

By John Warren. p. 18–20

Variant rules that give druids non-magical healing powers.

On Becoming the Great Druid

By Robin Jenkins and William Volkart. p. 22

What to do when a druid is ready to start challenging his betters.

Cantrips for Druids--Naturally

By Rick Reid. p. 28

Zero-level spells for druids.

King of the Jungle

By Ed Greenwood. p. 30

The new beastmaster class.

The Ecology of the Korred

By Ed Greenwood. p. 42–44

This article details the korred, a little known fey race of the Realms.

The Dragon's Bestiary: A Walk through the Woods

By Allan Hopkins, Arthur Collins, Douglas Lent, Ed Greenwood, Lance Hankins, Loran Wlodarski, Mark DeForest, Robert Benedetti, Samuel J. Offutt, and William L. Bowman Jr.. p. 46–53

A huge collection of woodland monsters.

  • Anuchu
  • Giant capybara
  • Leshy
  • Luposphinx
  • Musical spirit
  • Sashalus
  • Wendigo
  • Whispering pine
  • Wild halfling
  • Wood giant
  • Wood golem

The Game Wizards

By Jeff Grubb. p.59-60

This article highlights the then upcoming Forgotten Realms campaign setting for 2 edition.

TSR Profiles: Dennis Kauth

By Uncredited. p. 88

Profile of artist and cartographer Dennis Kauth.
