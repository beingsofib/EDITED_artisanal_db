Dragon issue 73 was released in May of 1983. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ecology of the Catoblepas

By Richard Edwards and Chris Elliot. p. 22-23

A scholarly treatise on the Catoblepas.

When it gets hit, it gets hurt: A system for equipment damage

By Arthur Collins. p. 34

A system to figure wear, tear, and damage to arms and armor in Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

Non-Violent Magic Items

By Lewis Pulsipher and Roland Gettliffe. p. 36-40.

A collection of magical items of a non-violent nature.

Up on a Soapbox: All for all, not one for one

By Roger E. Moore. p. 74

Why gaming should be a cooperative activity, not a competitive one.
