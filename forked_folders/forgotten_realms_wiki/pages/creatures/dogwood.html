Dogwood was a variety of tree found in the Realms.

Description

These trees grew flowers.

Habitats

  • In Faerûn, these trees could be found in the high elevated areas of the Yuirwood.
  • In Kara-Tur, these trees could be found in the Maeshi Province of Kozakura

Trivia

Dried flowers from a dogwood were used in the making of the Edu'sascar.
