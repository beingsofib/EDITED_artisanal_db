Dyer's broom

nameDyer's broom

usageDye

Dyer's broom was a flowering plant found in Faerûn, notable for its use in dyemaking.

Availability

Dyer's broom was not native to the Sword Coast, but had to be imported from the east.

Usage

The flowers of the plant were a key ingredient in making dependable yellow dyes.
