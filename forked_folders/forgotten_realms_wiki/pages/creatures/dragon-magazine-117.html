Dragon issue 117 was released in January of 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Elements of Mystery

By Robert Plamondon. p. 10

Limited-information campaigns.

Feuds and Feudalism

By John-David Dorman. p. 16

Solving mid-level character problems by getting players involved in government.

Condensed Combat

By Travis Corcoran. p. 18

Streamlining the attack tables in AD&D games.

Dungeoneer's Shopping Guide

By Robert A. Nelson. p. 22

A list of items to buy for D&D.

A Touch of Genius

By Vince Garcia. p. 28

Putting intelligence to work in AD&D gaming.

The Ecology of the Ankheg

By Mark Feil. p. 33‒36

A scholarly treatise on the Ankheg.

Hounds of Space and Darkness

By Stephen Iniss p. 37‒39

Three unusual dogs for the AD&D game.

Fun without Fighting

By Scott Bennie. p. 40

Romance, business, and organizations as the basis of AD&D adventures.

The Forgotten Characters

By Thomas M. Kane. p. 43

A look at henchmen, hirelings, and followers.

By Magic Masked

By Ed Greenwood. p. 46–47

Nine magical masks for the Forgotten Realms.

TSR Profiles: Clyde Caldwell

By Uncredited. p. 84

Profile of artist Clyde Caldwell.
