Dragon issue 62 was released in June of 1982. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Faerie Dragon

By Brian Jaeger. p. 6-7

The Faerie Dragon: a colorful new dragon.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Evil Dragon Armors

By Roger E. Moore. p. 10

What to do with that dragon after you've killed it.

From the Sorcerer's Scroll: Everything You Never Knew about Spell Books

By Gary Gygax. p. 14

Specifics on spell books.

Pages from the Mages

By Ed Greenwood. p. 16-20

4 sample magic-users spell books from Elminster the Sage's records (pre-Forgotten Realms) for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

  • Mhzentul’s Runes
  • Nchaser’s Eiyromancia
  • The Book of the Silver Talon
  • The Chambeeleon

The Scribe Non-Player Character

By Ed Greenwood. p. 21

A new AD&D NPC class.

Point of View: Half-Orcs

By Roger E. Moore. p. 26-27

How do half-orcs end up "rude, crude, crass, and generally obnoxious"?

The Gods of the Orcs

By Roger E. Moore. p. 28-32

5 deities of the Orcs:

  • Bahgtru
  • Ilneval
  • Luthic
  • Shargaas
  • Yurtrus

Leomund's Tiny Hut: Magic for Merchants

By Len Lakofka. p. 56–60

Cantrips and other magics to deal with shop-lifters.
