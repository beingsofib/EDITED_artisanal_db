The gorgonocephalus was a strangely shaped, deepsea creature. They were only known to be found in the underwater trenches of the Sea of Moving Ice, off the coast of Icewind Dale.

History

These creatures were long believed to be some form of aberration. However, this was eventually disproven and they were found to actually be distant relatives of starfish.
