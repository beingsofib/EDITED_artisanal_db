Beneath the City of the Dead

Beneath the City of the Dead was the sixth issue of the Advanced Dungeons &amp; Dragons comics series set in the Forgotten Realms and published by . It was written by Dan Mishkin and Jan Duursema, drawn by Duursema, and edited by Barbara Kesel. It is the second of four installments in the "The Spirit of Myrrth" story arc.
