Dragon issue 344, Giant 30th Anniversary Special, was released in June of 2006. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Neverwinter Nights II

By F. Wesley Schneider p. 20–23

A first look at the upcoming Neverwinter Nights 2 video game.

Planar Dragons

By Mike McArtor and Tom Fowler p. 26–37

Four new planar wyrms.

The Wizards Three: A Dark and Stormy Night

By Ed Greenwood p. 56-61

A short story featuring Elminster Aumar.
