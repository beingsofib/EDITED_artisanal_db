Dragon issue 365 was released in PDF format only in July of 2008. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Playtest: Artificer

By Logan Bonner, illustrated by William O’Connor. p. 5–15

This article gives a preliminary set of class features, powers, and magic items for the Battlesmith build of the Artificer class prior to its official release in the Eberron Player's Guide.

Backdrop: Cormyr

By Brian R. James, illustrated by Randy Gallegos, Cartography by Mike Schley. p. 16–24

This article details the post-Spellplague geography of the Kingdom of Cormyr, including statistics for the capital of Suzail. It covers information on the history, society, government, military, commoners, and adventuring companies of that country. It includes maps and a timeline. It also provides rules for two new paths:

  • Draeven marauder
  • Luck bringer of Tymora

Ecology of the Dragonborn

By Chris Sims, illustrated by Brian Hagan and William O'Connor. p. 25–34

This article provides details on the dragonborn race.

Codex of Betrayal: Beleth, the Witch's Viscount

By Ari Marmell, illustrated by Ben Wootten. p. 35–45

New feature! The Codex of Betrayal brings the unique devils of the Nine Hells to life. In this article, meet Beleth, Prince of Imps and Spymaster to Glasya.

Roll vs. Role: Coils Below

By Bruce R. Cordell, illustrated by Efrem Palacios and James Zhang. p. 61–66

The snaketongue, human cultists of the yuan-ti. Includes a rumored chapel, adventure hooks, potential encounters, and a high priest.

Design & Development: Monsters! Monsters!

By Peter Schaefer and Rob Heinsoo. p. 72–74

The development team discusses how they approach the creation of new monsters, with many comparisons to 3E.
