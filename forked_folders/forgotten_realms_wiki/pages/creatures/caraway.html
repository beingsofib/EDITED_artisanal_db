Caraway was a flowering plant that produced a common spice seasoning, caraway seed.

Availability

Caraway seeds were sold through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for two silver pieces per ounce.

Caraway seeds were used for cooking in Cursrah of the Calim Empire.

Usage

Caraway-distilled alcohol was a potent drink sometimes carried by travelers the Great Glacier.

  • Elvish seedcakes were delicious treats imbued with healing magics, baked using copious amounts of crushed aniseed, caraway, and coriander.
  • Erase spell scrolls required a special type of ink to be penned with, created out of mixed giant squid sepia, acid, crushed caraway seeds, a chrysolite, and a black pearl.
  • Caraway had minor medical uses, possessing antacid and digestive properties.

Trivia

  • Patricia Caraway was a celric envoy at the House of Bountiful Harvest of Chauntea in the city of Ravens Bluff circa 1370 DR.
  • Spice Caraway was a curiously-named famous bard, active in the late 15 century DR.
