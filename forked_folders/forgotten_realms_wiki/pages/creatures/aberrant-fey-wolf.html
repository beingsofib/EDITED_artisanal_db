Aberrant fey wolf

Creature
nameAberrant Fey Wolf
size5eLarge
locationNeverwinter, Sword Coast North

Aberrant fey wolves were fey wolves imbued with foul magics of the Far Realm by the warlock named Nostura, who took over the Chasm of Neverwinter, afther the Spellplague ravaged the city.

Description

Unlike their gray-furred counterparts from the Feywild and Prime Material plane, aberrant fey wolves had a sickly green coat of fur, splotched with light-green markings that webbed their bodies, leaving only their underbellies untocued.

History

In the years that followed the Spellplague, in the late 15 century DR, Nostura of the Cloaked Ascendancy used fey wolves she altered with magic for the organization's evil plans for Neverwinter. Some of her wolves managed to retain will and intelligence and rebelled against their cruel mistress, seeking new, kinder owners among the heroes and adventurers of Neverwinter.
