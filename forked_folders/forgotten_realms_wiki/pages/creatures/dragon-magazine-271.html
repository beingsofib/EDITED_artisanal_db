Dragon issue 271, Diabolical Puzzles for Your D&D Game, was released in May of 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: Steve Miller

By Steve Kenson. p. 20

Steve Miller got into the gaming industry on a dare, but he always wanted to be a writer.

Up on a Soapbox: Maybe Even Dull Witted

By Gary Gygax. p. 22

Disparaging gamers who claim that those who don't like their games are unintelligent, and a call for a return to the dungeon crawl.

Countdown to Third Edition: Monks and Multiclassing

By Uncredited. p. 24

Power Word: Baffle!

By Mike Selinker. p. 26

Extensive look at designing word puzzles. Has a further reading list.

Arcane Lore: Spells of Defense

By Owen K.C. Stephens, illustrated by Carlo Arellano. p. 68–72

Spells of defense come in handy for every wizard.
