The diatryma () was an ancient large, flightless bird native to the Underdark.

Description

The gigantic bird was about tall.

Ecology

The drow used their hugely plumed feathers for decoration.

Usage

The mercenary Jarlaxle sported a diatryma feather on his wide-brimmed hat, as both an ornamental and a functional magical item known as a feather of diatryma summoning. By blowing into the feather, Jarlaxle was able to summon a diatryma bird. Although this feather was used up in the summoning, it would grow back.
