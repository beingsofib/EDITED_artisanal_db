Dragon issue 235, The Lure of the Sea, was released in November of 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Mage on Deck!

By Ted James and Thomas Zuvich. p. 31

The shipmage kit for AD&D.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Monsters of the Lower Deep

By Gregory D. Detwiler, illustrated by Stephen Schwartz. p. 43–48

Sea monsters.

  • Angler fish
  • Death minnow
  • Gulper
  • Hide
  • Octo-jelly
  • Viperfish

Arcane Lore: Sea Spells

By Brian Dunnell, illustrated by Amy Lusebrink. p. 52–56

Seagoing sorcery for nautical adventures.

  • Amgig's rowers
  • Buoyancy
  • Call wind
  • Eagle eyes
  • Fantar's reef
  • Fantar's shoal
  • Flame resistance
  • Foul rigging
  • Ironside
  • Land call
  • Leomund's lifeboat
  • Leomund's many life preservers
  • Map
  • Mentap's mine
  • Morning glory
  • Narcomb's battened hatches
  • Otiluke's screen
  • Plug leak
  • Raise vessel
  • Repair breach
  • Spectral navigator
  • True compass

The Ecology of the Troglodyte

By Spike Y. Jones, illustrated by Jim Holloway. p. 78-83;86

A scholarly treatise on the Troglodyte.

Arcane Lore: Spells of the Scaled

By Steve Berman, illustrated by Michael Scott. p. 89–90

Amphibious, reptilian, and piscine spells.

  • Barble
  • Camouflage
  • Moltings
  • Scale blade
  • Serpent tail
  • Skin of the salamander

Dungeon Mastery: Let's Get Physical

By Troy Daniels. p. 91

When your players fall asleep.

Role-playing Review

By Rick Swan. p. 113

Short review on Heroes' Lorebook and The North: Guide to the Savage Frontier sourcebooks.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 116

New products for November, December and January.

  • Dangerous Games novel.
  • Realms of the Arcane novel.
  • The Council of Blades novel.
  • Undermountain: Stardock module.
