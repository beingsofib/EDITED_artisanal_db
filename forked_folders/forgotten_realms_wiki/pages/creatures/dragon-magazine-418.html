Dragon issue 418 was released in PDF format only in December of 2012. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

History Check: The Tarrasque

By Jeff LaSala, illustrated by Craig J. Spearing. p. 4-8

Learn the secrets of this primordial, party-eating monstrosity.

The End Is Nigh!

By Dennis R. Johnson, illustrated by Craig J. Spearing. p. 9-12

Meet the new and improved tarrasque and its twisted cult following.

Inns in an Instant

By John Hasznosi, illustrated by Chris Seaman. p. 35-48

Creating an inn or tavern from scratch has never been easier or more fun.

Eye on the Realms: The High Priest of Beholders

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Phill Simmer. p. 55-59

Meet the eye tyrants’ number-one fan.
