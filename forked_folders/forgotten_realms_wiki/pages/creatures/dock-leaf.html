Dock leaf

Plant
nameDock Leaf
othernamesJewelweed
typeHerb, plant
locationWestern Heartlands

Dock leaf was a common plant with some medicinal properties.

Uses

Dock leaf was used by rangers of the Western Heartlands as a curative, alongside with some medicinal berries. It was used to elevate certain health conditions of a personal nature.
