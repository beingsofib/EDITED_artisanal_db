Dragon issue 145. A knight's home is his castle, was released in May of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

A Castle Here, A Castle There: Need a random fortress in your fantasy game campaign?

By Daniel Salas. p. 14

Tables for generating physical and political characteristics of fortresses, both western and those in Oriental Adventures.

Holding Down the Fort: Random events to enliven anyone's stronghold

By Matt Iden. p. 18

A table of random events that can occur in a castles.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: The Magic Fortress: Magical Items for Fantasy Castles

By J. Jasper Owens. p. 36–42

Magic items for fortresses, including several new spells for strengthening or shattering walls.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 44

A walkthrough of Pool of Radiance.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 58

New products for April and May.

  • Darkwell
  • Curse of the Azure Bonds module
  • Shadowdale module
  • The Great Khan Game game folio
