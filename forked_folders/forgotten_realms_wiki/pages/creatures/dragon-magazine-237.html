Dragon issue 237, Something Wild, was released in July of 1997. The first issue published after Wizards of the Coast purchased TSR, Inc.. There was a 6-month gap between this and the prior issue. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Wyrms of the North: Eldenser

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Storn Cook. pp. 32–37

This article details the amethyst dragon Eldenser of the Sword Coast North.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Venomous!

By Kevin Melka, illustrated by Valerie Valusek. pp. 42–48

This article details a number of venomous creatures: snakes (bushmaster, black mamba, boomslang, cobra, fer-de-lance, gaboon), lizards (gila monster, rough-skinned newt), and amphibians (poisonous frog, neotropical toad).

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Long Shots

By Brian Dunnell, illustrated by Stephen Schwartz. pp. 96–98

New magical items to set your archers quiver.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 116

New products for January, February and March.

  • Elminster in Myth Drannor novel.
  • Finder's Bane novel.
  • Four from Cormyr module.
  • Mortal Consequences novel.
  • Realms of the Arcane novel.
  • Undermountain: Stardock module.
