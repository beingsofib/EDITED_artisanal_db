Dragon issue 264, 10 Ways to Play 3rd Edition Today!, was released in October of 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Oeridian Lesser Gods

By Andy Miller. p. 46

Part 2: Raxivort and Sotillon.

Dungeon Mastery: Heroic Horror Campaigns

By Lester Smith. p. 68

Mixing horror and heroism, with a dash of humor.

Rogues Gallery: Planescape Torment

By Ray Vallese and Val Vallese, illustrated by Scott Fisher. p. 74-78

Four peculiar characters leap from the Planescape: Torment computer game to the pages of a novel - and into your own campaign.

  • Morte
  • Ravel Puzzlewell
  • Pharod Wormhair
  • Fall-from-Grace

Role Models: Terrain Tactics

By JD Wiker and Jim Bishop. p. 90

Incorporating the third dimension into terrain.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 108

New products for October, November and December.

  • Beyond the High Road novel.
  • Drizzt Do'Urden's Guide to the Underdark sourcebook.
  • Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas software.
  • The Temptation of Elminster novel.
  • Under Fallen Stars novel.

ProFiles: Brom

By Steve Kenson. p. 112

The fantasy artist Brom talks about monsters, the darkly beautiful, and Normal Rockwell.
