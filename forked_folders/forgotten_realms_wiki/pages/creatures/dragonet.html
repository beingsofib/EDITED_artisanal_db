The dragonets were a group of tiny lesser dragons.

Description

Dragonets as a whole were very small, with most being less than in length; only the mole dragonet was larger. They tended to be draconic in form.

Abilities

Dragonets were intelligent, possessed of differing magical abilities, and capable of becoming mages' familiars.

Types of Dragonets

There were many different types of dragonets.

  • The Crow's-nest dragon, also called the spiretop dragon or spiretop drake.
  • Draken
  • Faerie dragon
  • Firedrake
  • Geyser dragon
  • Mole dragon
  • Pavilion dragon
  • Pseudodragon

Ecology

Dragonets inhabited a variety of terrains in a range of ecological niches.
