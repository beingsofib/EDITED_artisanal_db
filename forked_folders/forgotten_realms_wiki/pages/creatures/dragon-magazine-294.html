Dragon issue 294, Deities & Demigods, was released in April of 2002. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Previews

By Uncredited. p. 16

  • Faiths and Pantheons sourcebook.
  • Realms of Shadow novel.
  • Spellfire novel.

Epic Level Countdown: Epic Characters

By Mat Smith. p. 20

An advance look at Epic Classes, Epic Skills, and Epic Feats.

The Windsinger

Uncredited. p. 52

A Prestige Class for bards who go to sea.

Adding Injury to Insults

By Robin D. Laws. p. 52

Arming your character for the battle of wits.

Words To Live By

By Robin D. Laws. p. 54

Creating catchphrases for your characters.

Class Acts: The Nightsong Infiltrator

By Monte Cook. p. 66

A Prestige Class for breakers and enterers.

Faiths of Faerûn: The Green Hunter

By Eric Haddock. p. 68–69

An organization of elite hunters in service to Thard Harr, with accompanying Prestige Class.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: The Leaning Tree

By Ed Greenwood. p. 70-73

All the secrets of a traveler's rest revealed.

Silicon Sorcery: Neverwinter Nights

By Aaron Loeb. p. 105-106

New magic items adapted from Neverwinter Nights, the D&D-based computer game.
