Dragon issue 184, Nonplayer characters have fun, too, was released in August of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Courts & Courtiers

By Larry Granato. p. 10

Expert hirelings to flesh out your game's royal courts.

Really Good Bad Guys

By Derek Jenson. p. 16

Some challenging ideas for making challenging foes.

The 7-Sentence NPC

By C.M. Cline. p. 23

The seven most important elements of any good NPC.

You again!

By Scott Sheffield. p. 26

Creating--and reusing--good enemies for your PCs.

The Referee's Code of Honor

By John Setzer p. 33

Six simple ways to earn player trust.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 39

New products for August and September.

  • Land of Fate boxed set.
  • The Coral Kingdom novel.
  • ''The Great Glacier sourcebook.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 62

  • Review: Treasures of the Savage Frontier. 184/The Role of Computers
  • Clue Corner: Curse of the Azure Bonds. 184/The Role of Computers2

Game Wizards: Secrets of a trading-card guru

By David Wise p. 66–70

A look at the upcoming 1992 AD&D Trading Cards releases. 184/The Game Wizards: Secrets of a trading card guru

Novel Ideas: Interview: Troy Denning

By Sue Weinlein p. 88

Magic With an Evil Bite

By Jason M. Walker p. 92-94

Nasty new spells and magic items for the Neogi, as well as a new monster, the undead Umber hulk. 184/Magic With an Evil Bite

Audible Glamour--Not Clamor

By Mike Shema p. 96

Fine-tuning your descriptions for clarity.
