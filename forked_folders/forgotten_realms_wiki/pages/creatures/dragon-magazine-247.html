Dragon issue 247, Heroes, was released in May of 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Wyrm's Turn: Forgotten Realms

By Dave Gross. p. 4

A tale becomes heroic in the retelling, not in the doing.

Wyrms of the North: Miirym

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Storn Cook. p. 56–60

This article describes the undead female silver dragon Miirym who resided near Candlekeep.

Reunification (Body & Soul)

By Jeff Grubb, illustrated by Rags Morales. p. 62–71

This short story continues the tale begun in the Forgotten Realms comics, and tells of the crew of the Realms Master.

Index

Characters : Vartan Hai Sylvar • Priam Agrivar • Ishi Barasume • Minder • Dwalimar Omen • Foxilon Cardluck • Labelas Enoreth

Rogues Gallery: Crew of the Realms Master

By Kate Novak, illustrated by Rags Morales. p. 74–80

This article details the characters from the Forgotten Realms comic series:

  • Dwalimar Omen
  • Minder
  • Foxilon Cardluck
  • Ishi Barasume
  • Vartan Hai Sylvar
  • Priam Agrivar

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Magic that Works

By Sebastian Dietz, illustrated by Jim Crabtree. p. 82–86

Look here for some magic that works. Magic items for alchemists, merchants, and craftsmen.

The Dragon's Bestiary: The Anurans

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Bob Klasnich. p. 88–92

Frogs and toads. Archer frog • Ghoul frog • Leech toad • Spined toad

ProFiles: Jeff Grubb

By Allen Varney. p. 120

About Jeff Grubb, designer of the Al-Qadim and Spelljammer campaigns; author of The Finder's Stone trilogy, and co-founder of the Forgotten Realms setting.
