Killer mimics were a less intelligent, more aggressive breed of mimics.

Description

They did not look noticeably different from regular mimics, though they were larger, approximating .

Personality

Though semi-intelligent, killer mimics were extremely voracious and aggressive, being single-minded in their pursuit of food.

Abilities

Other than being slightly larger and scrappier, killer mimics were not very different from regular ones in their abilities; they could not speak, and that was about it.

Combat

Their actions in combat were rather similar to regular mimics'.

Ecology

Killer mimics did not differ from regular ones in their ecology.

Notable Killer Mimics

  • Orokoth's library held a killer mimic in temporal stasis as a trap.
  • The Wadi al-Aqhaf, or Gorge of Skulls, was inhabited by killer mimics, amongst other creatures.
  • One lay in wait at Windsong Tower.
  • Another lay in wait at the Neirdrecrypt.
  • A killer mimic was guarding The Old Monster Shop in Waterdeep.
  • Another was in the Lanceboard Room of Undermountain, disguised as a coffin.
  • A door in Wolover's Keep had been replaced by a killer mimic.
  • The Halcyon, a crashed spelljammer, had a killer mimic aboard.
