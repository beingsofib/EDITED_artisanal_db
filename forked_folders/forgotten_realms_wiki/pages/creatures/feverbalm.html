Feverbalms were a type of flower native to the Savage Frontier region. They also were considered to be a type of a herb, as they had medicinal properties.

Usage

In order to access the medicinal properties of feverbalms, creatures were known to boil them into a tea. This tea had the effect of temporarily curing a creature of insanity.
