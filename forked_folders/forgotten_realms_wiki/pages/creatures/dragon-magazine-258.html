Dragon magazine 258, Mage vs. Machine, was released in April of 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Wyrms of the North: The Black Death Voaraghamanthar

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Storn Cook. p. 52-56

Voaraghamanthar, the "Black Death" sure gets around the Mere of Dead Men.

The Ecology of the Flail Snail

By Johnathan M. Richards. p. 58–63

A scholarly treatise on the Flail snail.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 92

New products for April, May and June.

  • Skullport sourcebook.
  • The Dream Spheres novel.
  • The Glass Prison novel.
  • The Silent Blade novel.

ProFiles: Todd Lockwood

By Steve Kenson. p. 96

From beer cans and satellite dishes to the Forgotten Realms, Dragonlance and Ravenloft settings; Todd Lockwood is an artist with big visions who loves his work.
