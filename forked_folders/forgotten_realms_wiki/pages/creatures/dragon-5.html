Dragon+ 5

Dragon+ issue 5 was released on December 15, 2015. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Baldur's Gate: Siege of Dragonspear

By Amber Scott and Trent Oster pp. 5

An interview with the developers of the video game Baldur's Gate: Siege of Dragonspear.

Amulet Fellow & The Regal Rose

By Andrew Foley pp. 6

A short fiction piece by Andrew Foley.

Tyrants of the Underdark

By Peter Lee pp. 8

A look at the board game Tyrants of the Underdark.

Neverwinter: Underdark

By Scott Shicoff pp. 12

An overview of the Underdark module for the Neverwinter game.

Community: Insanity of the N.P.C.

By Matt Chapman. pp. 13

Six community-created NPC profiles for the Rage of Demons storyline, illustrated by Richard Whitters.

Interview: Erin M. Evans

By Matt Chapman. pp. 14

An interview with author Erin M. Evans, focusing on her Brimstone Angels series.

Sword Coast Legends: Better by Design

By Matt Chapman. pp. 16

An interview with several developers of the game Sword Coast Legends.

Sword Coast NPCs for your Tabletop D&D Game

By Matt Chapman. pp. 17

5th edition statblocks for the Sword Coast Legends companion characters Jarhild Stoneforge, Hommet Shaw, Illydia Maethellyn, Larethar Gulgrin, and Bryn Lightfingers.

Tavern Tales: Adventurers League

By Matt Chapman. pp. 18

A look at upcoming Adventurers League content, and a free copy of the adventure The Occupation of Szith Morcane.
