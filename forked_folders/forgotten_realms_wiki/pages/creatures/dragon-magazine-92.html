Dragon issue 92 was released in December of 1984. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

First, spread the faith

By Paul Vernon. p. 8

Clerics need to keep thier mission in mind.

The more, the merrier

By Bruce Barbar. p. 12

How clerics can convert new followers.

Let the horse buyer beware

By Robert Harrison. p. 26

A system for seperating the stallions from the swaybacks.

The Ecology of the Ettin

By Ed Greenwood. p. 29-31

A scholarly treatise on the Ettin.

Pages from the Mages III: Four more magic books, courtesy of Elminster

By Ed Greenwood. p. 34-40

Elminster describes four magical tomes and the nine unique spells (and one unique monster) to be found within.

  • Aubayreer's Workbook
  • Orjalun's Arbatel
  • The Scalamagdrion
  • The Tome of the Covenant
