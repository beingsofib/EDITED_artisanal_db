Pecans were an exotic variety of nut in the Realms that grew as the fruit of trees of the same name, which were a species of hickory.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold pecans under it's "Aurora's Larder" section's "Exotics" category for a whopping 150 gold pieces per .
  • By contrast, in the Vast the shop Fruits And More in the city of Ravens Bluff sold bags of pecans for 5 gp.
  • In interior Faerûn, in the land of Turmish, one could find wild pecan trees growing in the bogs near the Alaoreum River, flourishing where the village of Morningstar Hollows once stood.
  • In west Faerûn, in the land of Tethyr. While traveling along the Zazesspur Road one could encounter a round mound of a hill, atop which sprang a lone, mighty pecan tree.
  • In Interior Zakhara, pecans were cultivated in the herb garden of the fortress Sarahin.

Beyond Toril

  • Pecans could be found in the Feywild.
  • Beyond Toril, pecan trees could be found on a world known as Earth. On that planet the tree's nuts were commonly baked into pies Both of these desserts were quite popular with the wizard Elminster Aumar.

Usages

  • Mraed, a type of nut-based desert, traditionally used pecans.
  • Nutbread crescents, a popular snack food in Halruaa, were made with a combination of nuts that traditionally included pecans.
  • Roast gammon pie, a Cormyrian dish, called for 3 cups of pecan nuts. The nuts all had to be diced and lightly roasted.

History

To celebrate its sixth anniversary in the , the Downunda Patisserie in Ravens Bluff whipped up a large menu of special dishes. One of these dishes were cinnamon rolls topped with pecans and raisins. The bakery sold these in groups of a half a dozen for just one silver piece.

Trivia

  • Beldargan groundworms were said to have a taste akin to roasted pecans.
  • The wizard Fannt Golsway owned a staff of thunder & lightning that was crafted of pecan wood.
