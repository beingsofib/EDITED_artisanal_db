Dragon issue 193, Evil creeps in caverns deep, was released in May of 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Dungeoneering 101

By Steven E. Schend. p. 10

A guide to survival for beginners down in the dark.

Live Statues and Stone Men

By John Power. p. 19-22

Golems of the Forgotten Realms Underdark.

  • Brain golem
  • Hammer golem
  • Spiderstone golem

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 63

Clue Corner: Eye of the Beholder.

The Druid Gets a Life

By Alan M. Clark. p. 65

What exactly do druids do? Meaning, what do they do that's useful?

The Game Wizards: Oh, no—it's the 1993 TSR collector cards!

By Thomas M. Reid. p. 71–73

Get ready for the real thing: the 1993 TSR Trading Cards!

The Role of Books

By John C. Bunnell. p. 91

Review of Realms of Valor.

The Dragon's Bestiary: They came from the Outer Planes!

By Randy Maxwell. p. 95–100

Three creatures form the worlds beyond.

  • Abyss ant
  • Giant nautilus
  • Incarnate
