Dragon+ 33

Dragon+ issue 33 was released on August 26, 2020. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

In the Works

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

A deeper look at several upcoming releases:

  • Tasha's Cauldron of Everything
  • Curse of Strahd Revamped
  • Adventure Begins

Icewind Dale: Art of the Frostmaiden

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

A look at several art pieces created for Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden, including interviews with artists Kieran Yanner, Tyler Jacobson, and Hydro74.

Maps of the Month

By Hal Howard, Cartography by Jared Blando, Jason A. Engle, Jason Thompson, and Mike Schley.

Several new free maps, including a selection from Tales from the Yawning Portal.
