Dragon issue 334 was released in August of 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Cities of the Realms: Crimmor, City of Caravans

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Vincent Dutrait. p. 22-34

None held more valuable secrets than the merchant lords of Amn.

Fires of Alchemy: Alchemist's Fire Explored

By Paul Leach, illustrated by Andrew Hou. p. 46–51

The secrets of alchemist's fire and other insidious incendiaries.

Drunkards & Flagons: Fantasy's Deadliest Spirits

By Evan Dicken, illustrated by Matt Dixon and Tom Fowler. p. 51–59

Dragon magazine does not advocate drinking and adventuring.

The Ecology of the Kraken

By Richard Pett, illustrated by Peter Bergting and Mike Schley. p. 60-65

A scholarly treatise on the Kraken.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Arsenal of the War Mages

By Scott Noel, illustrated by Attila Adorjany. p. 70–72

Unusual magic weapons and other accoutrements.

Spellcraft: Livre d'Aquatha

By Gregory Marks, illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 74–76

Water spells.
