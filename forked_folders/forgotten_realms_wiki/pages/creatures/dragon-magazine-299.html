Dragon issue 299, Knights & Paladins, was released in September of 2002. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Previews

By Uncredited. p. 16

  • City of the Spider Queen module.
  • Hand of Fire novel.
  • The Thousand Orcs novel.

Campaign Components: Knights

By Michael John Tresca, illustrated by Arnie Swekel and Wayne Reynolds. p. 25–45

Honor and chivalry in any D&D game.

The Dragon's Bestiary: The Horrors of Cormyr

By Thomas M. Costa, illustrated by Mike May. p. 52–59

Monstrous monsters in the Realms.

  • Blood horse
  • Catoblepas
  • Glamer
  • Shadovig
  • Tree troll
  • Veserab

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Wizard's Toy Box

By Christopher Coyle, illustrated by Matthew Mitchell. p. 62–66

Magical toys.

Feats of Personality

By Eric Cagle. p. 69

Using feats to define your character's personality.

Arcane Lore: Blessings of War

By Amber Scott, illustrated by Glen Angus. p. 74–77

18 new paladin spells.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: Moon Mountain Brewery

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by David Day. p. 80-83

Moon Mountain Brewery‎, an establishment two days' north of Secomber.

By Lisa Smedman. p. 86–94
