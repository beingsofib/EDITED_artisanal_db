Shadow wolves were powerful wolf-like creatures native to the Shadowfell.

Description

Shadow wolves were enormous in size, compared to their mundane counterparts from the Prime Material plane. They looked like creatures made out of shadows, surrounded by an eerie dark mist, with their eyes glowing with red malevolence.

History

In the late 15 century DR, shadow wolves were known to be summoned to the Prime by the mages of Returned Netheril. These summoned creatures were used for their savage strength on the field of battle and as obedient mounts. Some adventurers ended up using shadow wolves as their mounts as well.
