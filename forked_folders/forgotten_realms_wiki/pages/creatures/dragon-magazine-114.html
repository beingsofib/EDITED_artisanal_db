Dragon magazine 114 was released in October 1986. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Witch

By Bill Muhlhausen. pp. 8-23

A revision of the popular NPC class combining many features of both clerics and magic-users, with spells.

Grave Encounters

By Nick Kopsinis and Patrick Goshtigian. pp. 22-24

Random encounter tables for graveyards, with keys.

Ecology of the Remorhaz

By Kurt Martin and Ed Greenwood. pp. 36–39

A scholarly treatise on the Remorhaz.

Combined Generation

By Robert Kelk. p. 40

Various charts for use in character creation decision making.

Class Struggles

By Mark Kraatz. p. 44

New rules for between-levels training.
