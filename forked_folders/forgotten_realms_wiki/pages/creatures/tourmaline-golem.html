Tourmaline golem were a weaker constructs similar to the emerald gemstone golems found in the nation of Thay, created by the Red Wizard Marcos Flass in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Tourmaline golems were construct creatures fashioned using tourmaline gemstones, mined from the Termalaine mines in the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale. Despite them being weaker and overall inferior variety of gemstone golems, they were still, nevertheless, dangerous foes.

History

Tourmaline golems were created in the , by Marcos Flass, a disgraced Thayan Red Wizard. When the town of Termalaine was emptied out by an unnatural blizzard and an army of verbeegs, the town's famous mines were abandoned and Marcos Flass seized that opportunity, hoping to use the mine's tourmaline to redeem his good name in the far-away Thay. He managed to quickly develop and build a batch of golems, when the Hero of Neverwinter found their way into the Flass laboratory during their operation to free Termalaine from the invading force. Marcos may have had negotiated with the Hero and their companions, allowing him to finished the golemcraft work in exchange for loaning the Ten Towns his freshly-carved tourmaline golem in case there was a battle to come against the demigod Arctus and his army of creatures of the tundra. The battle did commence soon after, however, it was unknown if the deal was made, securing the gemstone golems' aid in the life-and-death battle.
