Dragon issue 306, Humor & Hellspawn, was released in April of 2003. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

House Rules: Druid Summoners

By Andy Collins. p. 18

Letting druids spontaneously cast summoning spells.

Betraying Your Evil Nature

By Eric Cagle, illustrated by Arnie Swekel. p. 20–25

Playing evil, fighting temptation, and enjoying redemption.

By Evil Bound

By Mike Mearls, illustrated by Wayne Reynolds, Jason A. Engle ,and Cara Mitten. p. 26–42

Elemental demons and battleloth: 18 outsiders.

Index of creatures : Battleloths (Arrow • Axe • Crossbow • Pick • Spiked chain • Sword) • Elemental demons (Air demon • Ash demon • Earth demon • Fire demon • Ice demon • Water demon) • Hellforged devils (Coal devil • Glass devil • Lead devil • Obsidian devil • Sand devil • Spiked devil)

Killing Cousins

By Chris Thomasson, illustrated by Jonathan Wayshak. p. 52–58

Githzerai hit squads.

Racial Variants

By Will Upchurch, illustrated by Todd Harris. p. 60-65

Enviromental variants of races.

Urban Traps

By Penny Williams, illustrated by Christina Wald. p. 67–70

Traps for urban usage.

Silicon Sorcery: Icewind Dale 2

By Clifford Horowitz. p. 76–82

Icewind Dale 2: The game of Battle Squares.
