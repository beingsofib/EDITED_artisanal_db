Widow's tear

Plant
nameWidow's Tear
typeShrub
locationBarrowlands, Impiltur
heightShort
leafcolorBlack

The widow's tear was a species of small shrub that was found in the Barrowlands of Impiltur. It was the only type of plant life that grew atop the barren, mound-covered landscape. They could be identified by their black leaves.
