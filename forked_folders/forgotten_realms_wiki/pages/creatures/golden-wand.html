The golden wand was a wondrous plant found only in the remote World Pillar Mountains within the Land of the Yak-Men.

Description

The reeds of this strange plant were made of solid gold.

Uses

Brave djinn traveled to the dangerous mountains to harvest the golden reeds for use in crafting the wondrous nay of the djinn—a special gift to the new high chieftain of the jann of the High Desert on his ascension day.
