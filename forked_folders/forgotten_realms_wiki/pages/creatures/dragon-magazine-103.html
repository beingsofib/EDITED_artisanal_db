Dragon issue 103 was released in November of 1985. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

From the Sorcerer's Scroll: The future of the game

By Gary Gygax. p. 8–10

A look at the changes being considered for 2nd edition AD&D.

A dozen domestic dogs

By Stephen Inniss. p. 26-28

Expanded stats and rules for man's best friend.

The Centaur Papers

By Kelly Adams and Stephen Inniss. p. 35-46

Everything you could ever want to know about the horse-folk for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

TSR Profiles: Gary Gygax

By Uncredited. p. 56

A profile of TSR's chairman of the board Gary Gygax.
