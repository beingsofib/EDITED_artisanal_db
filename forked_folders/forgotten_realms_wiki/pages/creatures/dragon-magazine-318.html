Dragon issue 318, Ninjas, pirates, dinosaurs, was released in April of 2004. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ninja - Masters of the Silent Shadow

By Jesse Decker, illustrated by Todd Harris. p. 24–28

Master the path of stealth and shadow from levels 1 through 20.

Oriental Adventures Update: Eastern Flavor

By James Wyatt. pp. 32–48

Oriental Advantures is updated for v.3.5.

Dungeons & Dinosaurs

By James Jacobs, illustrated by Marc Sasso. p. 61–69

New dinos for your game.

Compsognathus • Dimetrodon • Diplodocus • Giganotosaurus • Helicoprion • Liopleurodon • Pachycephalosaurus • Parasaurolophus • Pteranodon • Rhamphorhynchus • Stegosaurus

Under Command

By Jessie Decker and Michael Donais. p. 94

Unearth a drow campaign for d&d miniatures and the venomous arsenal of Lolth's chosen warbands.
