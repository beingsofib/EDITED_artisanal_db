Quinces were a type of fruit, grown from trees of the same name, found in Faerûn.

Availability

  • In Amn they were grown in the Purple Hills region.
  • In Cormyr, quince was grown in the farmlands along the kingdom's southern coast.
  • In the Moonsea region, the Bounty of the Goddess temple grew and sold quinces.
  • The Sword Coast.
  • In Tethyr, they could be found growing as a crop or in the wild all across the nation.

Usages of Quinces

  • In the town of Fendarl's Gate, it was often used to garnish thick slabs of bacon.
  • Purple Hills Cider, a type of cider made with a variety of different fruits grown in the Purple Hills region, included quinces in its recipe.
  • Quinces were an essential ingredient in the handfood quince sticks. Similarly, quince-and-crushed-insects sticks were handfoods from the Vilhon Reach, Shaar, Var, and Estagund.
  • The elven dish vaervren was sometimes seasoned with diced quince.
  • Quince preserves were a staple of a dwarvish pantry. Quince marmalades and butters were sold via Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.
  • Some believed that quince possessed medicinal properties that aided with various eye and skin diseases, as well as with dysentery.
