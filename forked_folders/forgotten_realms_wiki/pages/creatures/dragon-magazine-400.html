Dragon issue 400 was released in PDF format only in June of 2011. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Wrought from Gloom

By Claudio Pozas and Robert J. Schwalb, illustrated by Patri Balanovsky, Warren Mahy, and Milivoj Ceran. p. 1–3

The magic items introduced here have strong ties to the world’s darkest reflection. Some are relics of bygone eras, once held in the hands of powerful wielders of shadow magic, or forged by ancient masters in the shadows of Gloomwrought.

Dragon Anniversary: Return to the Nine Hells

By Richard Baker and Ed Greenwood. p. 1–32

A reprint of the two "The Nine Hells" articles from Dragon #75 and #76, with updated illustrations.

Dragon Anniversary: Dragonchess

By Gary Gygax, illustrated by Dennis Kauth and Russel Roehling. p. 42–53

A reprint of rules for the game dragonchess from Dragon #100. with updated illustrations.
