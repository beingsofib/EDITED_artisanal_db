Dragon issue 427 was released in PDF format only in September of 2013. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Editorial: Going Dark

By Chris Perkins. p. 3

Big changes are in the wind for Dragon and Dungeon. Read it here first. Announcing the end of Dragon magazine.

Codex of Betrayal: Levistus

By John Rossomangno, illustrated by Hector Ortiz and Anne Stokes. p. 17-23

If you thought being stripped of his title and entombed in an iceberg would slow down this archdevil, think again.

The Ecology of the Neogi

By Jeff Morgenroth, illustrated by Christopher Burdett and McLean Kendree. p. 24-30

Even in the far reaches of the Underdark, few creatures can match the boundless greed and patient hatred of the Neogi.

Eye on the Realms: The Sundering: To Rule Two Worlds

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Hector Ortiz. p. 36-38

As Abeir and Toril move apart, a few fell entities seek to rule parts of both worlds. A handful of heroes oppose them, but they need help—and fast.
