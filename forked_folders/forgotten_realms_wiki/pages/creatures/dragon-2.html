Dragon+ 2

Dragon+ issue 2 was released on June 11, 2015. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

D&D Videogames

By Bobby MacPherson p. 6

A brief history of D&D video games, including Forgotten Realms.

  • Pool of Radiance
  • Eye of the Beholder
  • Baldur's Gate
  • Icewind Dale
  • Baldur's Gate: Dark Alliance
  • Baldur's Gate: Dark Alliance II
  • Neverwinter Nights
  • Neverwinter Nights 2
  • Neverwinter Nights (AOL game)
  • Sword Coast Legends

Exploring Sword Coast Legends

By Matt Chapman p. 7

A preview of Sword Coast Legends, including tabletop character sheets for some of the game's characters.

Neverwinter: Strongholds

By Matt Chapman p. 9

A preview of the Strongholds update for Neverwinter.

The Winter King

By Chris Avellone p. 12

A previously unpublished short story by Chris Avellone, set in Icewind Dale.

Interview: Ed Greenwood

By Matt Chapman p. 15

An interview with Ed Greenwood.
