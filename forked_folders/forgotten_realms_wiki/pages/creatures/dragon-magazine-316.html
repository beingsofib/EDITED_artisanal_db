Dragon issue 316, Spies, was released in February of 2004. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Campaign Components: Covert Ops

By Evan Michael Jackson. p. 30

Running an espionage game.

Campaign Classics: Cloak & Dagger

By Eric Cagle and Evan Michael Jackson. p. 39

Gears, gadgets, and gizmos for those spies in your game.

Spies Like Us

By Dean Poisso. p. 55

Stealth for your non-stealthy characters.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: The Black Dwarf Mine

By Ed Greenwood. p. 74-77

Due east of Waterdeep, an endless succession of caverns.
