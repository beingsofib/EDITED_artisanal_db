Dragon issue 89 was released in September of 1984. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Survival is a group effort

By Stephen Inniss. p. 8

A look at population growth and it's effects projected onto demi-human and humanoid populations.

Six very special shields

By Ed Greenwood. p. 14-18

Elminster tells Ed of six unique magical shields.

  • Dzance's Guardian
  • Grimjaw
  • Hawkstone's Bulwark
  • Reptar's Wall
  • Shoon's Buckler
  • Thurbrand's Protector

The many types of magic

By Charles Olsen. p. 22-24

The meanings behind the AD&D spell schools.
