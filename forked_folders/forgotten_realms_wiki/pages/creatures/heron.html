Heron

nameHeron
terrainMarshes, swamps

Herons were a species of water-wading bird found in the Realms.

Description

Their beaks had a similar shape to that of a boobrie.

Ecology

Habitats

Herons were generally found in habitats typical of water wading birds. These included marshes, swamps, lake verges, and along the banks of watercourses.

In interior Faerûn, herons were quite common in the Farsea Marshes. In the Unapproachable East, they could be found in the nation of Aglarond in the Tannath Gap.

Relationships

Some swamp-based rangers kept herons as animal companions.

Usages

In the city of Ravens Bluff, herons were one of many birds that the Raven's Glory dining club would serve to its patrons.
