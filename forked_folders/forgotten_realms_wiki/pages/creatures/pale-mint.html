Pale mint was a rare herb native to the scorched sands of the Calim Desert.

Description

Pale mint leaves were of light green color. The plant was often called a culinary jewel and rightfully so. When tried and crushed, pale mint gained lemony sweet flavor, popular addition to the most gourmet dishes in Faerûn.
