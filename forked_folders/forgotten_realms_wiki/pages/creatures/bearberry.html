Bearberries were a type of edible berry in the Sword Coast North region that were named after the fact that they were most commonly eaten by bears. They were believed to possess some curative properties.

Varieties

Kinnikinnick: A variety of bearberry that grew in Hartsvale.

Usages

  • In colder climes, inns served bearberries alongside muskox soup.
  • Bearberries were valued by some as a tanning agent, typically being used for the tanning of bear and aberration hides into leather.
  • Due to their curative properties, some used bearberries in conjunction with honey and distilled water to brew potions of greater healing.
