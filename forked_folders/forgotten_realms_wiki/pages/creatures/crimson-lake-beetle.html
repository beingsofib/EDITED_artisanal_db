Crimson Lake beetle

Creature
nameCrimson Lake Beetle
locationAnauroch, North Faerûn
terrainDesert

Crimson Lake beetles were a species of desert insect found in the sweltering sands of the Great Anauroch.

Ecology

The crimson color in the beetles' name referred to the insect's use in making a red-colored dye, making them a valuable commodity.

History

By the late 15 century DR, Crimson Lake beetle-dyed leather helmets became a common sight in West Faerûn, the Western Heartlands, and the city of Baldur's Gate.
