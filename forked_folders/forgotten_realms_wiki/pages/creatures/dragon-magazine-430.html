Dragon issue 430, the final issue of Dragon magazine, was released in PDF format only in December of 2013. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Harpers Of Waterdeep

By Erin M. Evans, illustrated by Ralph Horsley and Matias Tapia. p. 33-38

The reformed Harpers of Waterdeep bring the skills of spies to the cause of right.

Ecology of the Kruthik

By Tim Eagon, illustrated by William O'Connor and David Griffith. p. 39-43

Eye on the Realms: The Three-headed Helm

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Zoltan Boros. p. 44-46

Not all secret societies are out to destroy the world.
