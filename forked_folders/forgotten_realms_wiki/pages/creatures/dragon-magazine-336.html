Dragon issue 336 was released in October of 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Birth of the Dead: Origins of the Walking Dead

By Ari Marmell, illustrated by Jeff Laubenstein. p. 36–47

Discussions and examples of how various kinds of undead rise.

The Ecology of the Spawn of Kyuss

By James Jacobs, illustrated by Peter Bergting and Mike Schley. p. 60–65

A scholarly treatise on the spawn of Kyuss.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Curios of Corruption

By Gregory Marks, illustrated by Vincent Dutrait. p. 70–72

Use these items of horror...if you dare!.

Spellcraft: The Demonomicon of Iggwilv

By Gary Holian and Owen K.C. Stephens, illustrated by Matt Dixon. p. 76–84

Page through Iggwilv's little black book, the Demonomicon.
