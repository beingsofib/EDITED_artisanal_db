Waxflowers were a variety of flowers found in Faerûn.

Description

Some waxflowers were speckled, some had bilobate leaves, and some expressed both of these traits.

Habitats

In the city of Waterdeep, speckled waxflowers commonly grew around graves in the City of the Dead.
