Snapdragon

Plant
nameSnapdragon
typeFlower, plant
locationCormanthor

The snapdragon was a flowering plant that could be found in various places in Faerûn, such as the meadows of the midwood area of the forest of Cormanthor.

Trivia

  • Snapdragons were among the favored plants of the goddess Lliira.
