Dragon issue 133, Berserkers & Spies, was released in May of 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ecology of the Carnivorous Ape

By David Howery. p. 8-9

A scholarly treatise on the Carnivorous Ape.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Magical quivers and unusual arrows

By Andrew Salamon, Ed Knight, and Gordon R. Menzies. p. 10–11

Q-&-A time again - unusual quivers and arrows that is.

Wards Against Evil

By Parker Torrence. p. 40

Boosting the Shugenja class's power against spirits.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 75

New products for May.

  • Black Wizards novel.

The Dragon's Bestiary: All Giant Worms are Not Alike

By Kent Colbath. p. 82–86

Statistics for giant versions of various real-world worms.

  • Diopatra
  • Eurythoe
  • Glycera
  • Nereis
  • Pectinaria
  • Sabella
  • Terebella
  • Vanadis
