Dragon magazine 122, 11th Anniversary Issue, was released in June 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

The Ecology of the Rot Grub

By Ed Greenwood. p. 16-17

A detailed look at the rot grub.

A Step Beyond Shogun

By David Bunnell. p. 18-19

A reader's guide to adventuring in the Orient.

And a Step Beyond That

By David "Zeb" Cook. p. 20

The designer's new list of Oriental references.

The Natural Order

By Arthur Collins. p. 32-38

Yet more spells for druids everywhere.

From the Sorcerer's Scroll

By Gary Gygax. p. 40

A few words - and good-bye - from one of the creators; the disassociation of Gary Gygax from TSR, Inc., and the forming of his new company, New Infinities Productions.

The GM's Ten Commandments: Ten dos and don'ts for game masters everywhere

By Rig Volny. p. 44

Ten helpful guidelines for keeping your game at its very best.

Marshalling the Martial Arts

By Wayne Goldsmith and Dan Salas. p. 46-48

Twelve martial arts styles for Oriental characters.

TSR Profiles: Jane Cooper Hong

By Uncredited. p. 69

Profile of editor Jane Cooper Hong.
