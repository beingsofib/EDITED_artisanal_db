Dragon magazine 307, Game Expansion, was released in May 2003. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Risen Dead II

By Gwendolyn F.M. Kestrel, illustrated by Joe Corroney.p. 39–45

Four undead templates.

Templates

Bodak • Ghastly • Ghoulish • Scion of Kyuss

Heroes of Cormyr: Adventuring in the Forest Kingdom

By Thomas M. Costa, illustrated by Todd Harris.p. 46–54

Detailing a number of classes from Cormyr.

Classes

Battlepriest of Cormyr • Council Mage of Cormyr • Moon drover • Noble adventurer • Royal scout

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: The Haunted Bridge

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by David Day.p. 56-59

A look at Ilikur's Bridge.

Locations

Black Maw Bog • Huntinghorn Water • Ilikur's Bridge • The Long Road

Vegetation

Crabgrass
