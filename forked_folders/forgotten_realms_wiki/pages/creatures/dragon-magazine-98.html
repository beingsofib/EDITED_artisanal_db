Dragon issue 98, 9th Anniversary Issue was released in June of 1985. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Tailor-made treasure

By Roger E. Moore. p. 7

Developing individual dragon hoards.

The magic of dragon teeth

By Gregg Chamberlain. p. 11-13

Dragon damage revised

By Leonard Carpenter. p. 14

Scaling dragon's attack damage based on size and age.

Knowing what's in store

By Dave Rosene. p. 28

Know what to expect when your PCs go shopping.
