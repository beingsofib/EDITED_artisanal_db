Dragon issue 296, 26th Anniversary Issue, was released in June of 2002. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Previews

By Uncredited. p. 16

  • Crown of Fire novel.
  • Dissolution novel.
  • Heirs of Prophecy novel.
  • Silver Marches sourcebook.

Dragon Hunters

By Keith Strohm. p. 28

Four fool-hardy prestige classes.

Wing, Fang, and Spell

By Andy Collins and Skipp Williams. p. 38

Dragon senses, new feats, and a dragon mystic class.

Worshipers of the Forbidden

By Eric Cagle. p. 46

Monster cultist prestige classes.

Guild Secrets

By Andy Collins. p. 54

Using and designing guilds, secret societies, and cults.

Class Acts: Acolyte of the Fist

By Monte Cook. p. 72

A new prestige class.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: Starfall Pool

By Ed Greenwood. p. 74-77

A pool south of the Moonwood.

Silicon Sorcery: Baldur's Gate: Dark Alliance

By Will McDermott. p. 110–114

Boss monsters for your D&D game from Baldur's Gate: Dark Alliance.
