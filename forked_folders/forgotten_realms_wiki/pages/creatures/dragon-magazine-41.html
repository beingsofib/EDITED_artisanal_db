Dragon issue 41 was released in September of 1980. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elves, Dwarves & Halflings: For Appearance’s Sake

By H. R. Lovins. p. 21

Tables for generating appearance and facial features of demihuman races.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Arbane's Sword of Agility

By Ed Greenwood. p. 32

A sword that enhances the wielder's movement capabilities.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Glowing Globe

By Ed Greenwood. p. 32

A luminous floating globe.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Singing Sword

By Ed Greenwood. p. 32

A silver bastard sword that sings constantly (and loudly) when drawn.

The Dragon's Bestiary: The Silkie and Tomb Tapper

By Ed Greenwood and Tom Moldvay. p. 57–58

A race of wereseals.
