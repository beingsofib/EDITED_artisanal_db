Catnip was a type of herb that could be found on Toril.

Availability

Places in the Realms where catnip was known to be cultivated included:

  • Cormanthor
  • The Old Empires
  • In the land of Zakhara, catnip could be found being sold and even exported from the Pearl Cities.

Usages

  • Among the Old Empires, it was sometimes used as a material component for the spell animal vision.
  • Catnip was one of three herbs that needed to be burned in a brazier for the spell find familiar.
  • Catnip could be used to treat colds, fevers, hysteria, and spasms.
