Snowberry

nameSnowberry
typePlant, fruit

usageFood

Snowberries were a general term for berries in the North that came from a variety of different bushes and vines, but all shared the same property of being quite pleasant and edible when frozen. This classification was mainly used by rangers and Uthgardt barbarians.

Habitats

Places in the Realms where snowberries were known to grow included:

  • The Fallen Lands.
  • Near the settlement of Glister in the northern Moonsea region.
  • Throughout Icewind Dale.
  • In the Luruar region, these could be found in the Ice Mountains.
  • All across the Sword Coast North.
