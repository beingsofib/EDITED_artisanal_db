Werecow

Creature
nameWerecow
buildHumanoid, bovine

A werecow was an alleged type of lycanthrope that presumably could take the form of a cow or a cow-like humanoid hybrid. They were alleged to exist by some in the Domains of Dread

Notable Werecows

  • Bedford, a fictional werecow in the book Dusk's Dark Desires.
