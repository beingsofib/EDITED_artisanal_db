Hazels, also known as hazelnut trees for their edible nuts, were a type of tree that could be found growing across many parts of the Realms.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold almonds under it's "Aurora's Larder" section for 3 gold pieces per .
  • In interior Faerûn, House Thalavar of Westgate dealt in the trade of various food goods, including hazelnuts.
  • In north Faerûn, one could find hazelnuts included in provisions in Phlan.
  • In Interior Zakhara, hazelnuts were cultivated in the herb garden of the fortress Sarahin.
  • In the Domains of Dread, one could find hazelnut trees growing wild throughout the lower hilled forests of Barovia.

Varieties

Witch Hazel: a type of hazel that could be found in the eastern rimwood area of the Cormanthor forest.

Usage

  • Diviner's staves were typically made from gnarled hazel wood.
  • Hazelnuts were a commonly used nut in fruitcakes.
  • Hazelnut oil, a type of cooking oil derived from distilled hazelnuts.

Notable Users

  • Hazel trees were among the plants considered sacred by some druids, who sometimes used parts of them as spell focuses or in the crafting of weapons.
  • One of the house specials of Umbrel's Hot Tarts in Ravens Bluff was a chocolate and hazelnut tart.
