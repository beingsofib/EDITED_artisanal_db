Dragon issue 188, Good advice for heroes: Watch it!, was released in December of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

That's "Role," Not "Roll!"

By George T. Young. p. 10

Tricks and tips for emphasizing your role-playing and increasing your fun.

Be Nice to your Referee

By Stewart Robertson. p. 16

What a GM wants from the players.

Roles For Role-Players

By David Wilder. p. 22

Jobs players can take over to reduce the GM's workload.

The Wizards Three: Sorcery from Three Worlds

By Ed Greenwood. p. 27–34

Elminster and Mordenkainen are joined by Dalamar of Krynn to exchange information and trade secrets. Includes AD&D game statistics for new spells, magic items, and monsters that they discuss.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Ten swords now lost (and up for grabs)

By Nathaniel S. Downes p. 50-54;119

A set of special swords from the Forgotten Realms.

  • Calathangas
  • Dyerwaen
  • Elquillar
  • Evithyan's Blade
  • Heart of Stone
  • Ice Claw
  • Neekar
  • Nightwatcher

Novel Ideas: Interview: R.A. Salvatore: Creating a Legacy

By Sue Weinlein. p. 79-80

R.A. Salvatore reveals how a hero and his "legacy" were almost never born.

Mind Over Matter

By Richard Baker. p. 98

How to play a psionicist.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 120

New products for January.

  • Volo's Guide to Waterdeep sourcebook.
