Storks were a variety of wading birds.

Ecology

Storks were preyed upon by eagles.

Habitats

Storks were found in the Sumber Hills, and would nest in such places as Gladestone.

In ancient Netheril, white storks were a symbol of the floating enclave of Eileanar.

Beyond Faerûn, storks nested among the trees of the Katakoro Plateau. Storks were especially common in Huzuz.

Across the oceans, storks also lived in Maztica, and were a feature of the pool at the Palace of Kukul at Tulom-Itzi.

Notable Storks

Circa the , Arcturia was served by a stork she turned into a stork-headed humanoid.
