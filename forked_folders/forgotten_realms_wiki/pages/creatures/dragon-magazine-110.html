Dragon issue 110, 10th Anniversary Issue, was released in June of 1986. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Cult of the Dragon

By Ed Greenwood. p. 8-14

The Cult of the Dragon and their creation, the dreaded dracolich.

All about Elminster

By Ed Greenwood. p. 30-36

Everything you ever wanted to know about the Realms' most notorious mage (or at least, everything he wants you to know). Includes stats and three new spells.

Dragon damage revisited

By Leonard Carpenter. p. 28

Expanding the modified dragon damage from the article "Dragon damage revised" to the dragons in Fiend Folio and Monster Manual II.
