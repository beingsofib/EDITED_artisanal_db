Spruces were type of coniferous tree, part of a family of trees known as evergreens.

Habitats

  • Together with pine, they were the most common type of tree found in the Silver Marches.
  • Spruce were known to grow in the Cold Wood.
  • In Cormanthor, spruces were known to grow in the central Starwood and on hillsides in the Rimwood.
  • They were seen to grow in the Greypeak Mountains.
  • Spruces were known to grow in the coniferous forests of the Moonshae Isles.
  • In the Western Heartlands, they were very prevalent in the Misty Forest.

Beyond Faerûn

  • On Evermeet, spruces were known to grow near Tower Reilloch.
  • In Kara-Tur, spruces could be found in the Hochi Province of Kozakura.
