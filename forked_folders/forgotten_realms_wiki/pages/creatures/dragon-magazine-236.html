Dragon issue 236, Faith & Hope, was released in December of 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Seldarine Revisited

By Chris Perry. p. 13-17;25

Forgotten members of the elven pantheon (supplementing Dragon #155, with references to both the Forgotten Realms and Greyhawk).

  • Araleth Letheranil
  • Kirith Sotheril
  • Melira Taralen
  • Naralis Analor
  • Tarsellis Meunniduin
  • Rellavar Danuvien
  • Tethrin Veraldé

Arcane Lore: Demihuman Priest Spells

By Robert S. Mullin, illustrated by Terry Dykstra. pp.34–40

Spells for the dwarven, elven, gnomish, and halfling priests of your campaign.

  • Abbathor's greed
  • Analyze contraption
  • Animal animosity
  • Assume gaseous form
  • Berronar's favor
  • Burrow
  • Call hounds
  • Crypt ward
  • Faerie flames
  • Faith arrow
  • Garments of elvenkind
  • Mantle of Baravar
  • Mark of brotherhood
  • Probe enemies
  • Reed staff
  • Segojan's armor
  • Shades of Rhondang
  • Stealth of Brandobaris
  • Weapon of the earth
  • Weapon shift

Children of the Night

By Paul Culotta, illustrated by David Day. pp.80-88

This article introduces three unusual vampire characters that can be found in the Forgotten Realms setting. They are Morg, Saestra Karanok and Saed.

Wyrms of the North: Deszeldaryndun

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Storn Cook. pp. 54-60

This article details the silver dragon Deszeldaryndun Silverwing of Everlund.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 116

New products for December, January and February.

  • Dangerous Games novel.
  • Elminster in Myth Drannor novel.
  • Errand of Mercy novel.
  • Finder's Bane novel.
  • Four from Cormyr novel.
  • Realms of the Arcane novel.
  • The Council of Blades novel.
  • Undermountain: Stardock module.
