Dragon issue 186, Halloween greetings, fresh from the grave, was released in October of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Dragon's Bestiary: Unique undead additions to your AD&D game campaign

By Spike Y. Jones. p. 20–24, 84

Three unique undead horrors.

  • Cariad ysbryd
  • Memento mori
  • Tymher-haid

Welcome to the Neighborhood!

By James R. Collier. p. 26

City locations more interesting than your typical inns and shops.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 58

New products for October and November.

  • Hordes of Dragonspear module.
  • Menzoberranzan boxed set.
  • The Ring of Winter novel.

Give Your Villains a Fighting Chance

By Kevin Troy. p. 63

Run your villains as intelligently as the players run their characters.

Game Wizards: Bill's guide to villainy unbound

By William W. Connors. p. 89

Creating bad guys to despise.
