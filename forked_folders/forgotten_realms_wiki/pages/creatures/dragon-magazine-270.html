Dragon magazine 270, High-Level Campaigns, was released in April 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: Sean K. Reynolds

By Steve Kenson. p. 20

Webmaster to game designer, Sean K. Reynolds is a nice guy. Everyone agrees.

Up on a Soapbox: Hack & Slash

By Gary Gygax. p. 22

Defending mindless hack & slash.

Hidden Faces of Evil: Fiends Who Walk the Prime Plane

By James Wyatt. p. 50

You might know better than to face demons and evils on their own plane, but they have no qualms about facing you on yours.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Armor of the Abyssal Lords

By Paul Fraser, illustrated by Shawn Sharp. p. 64–71

Artifact armors.

The Ecology of the Gorbel

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Jeff Miracola. p. 78-84

The Monster Hunters come to the rescue.

The New Adventures of Volo: The Ardeep

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Ron Spencer p. 92-95

Dare to explore the Ardeep Forest.
