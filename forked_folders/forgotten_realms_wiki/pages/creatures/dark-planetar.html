Dark planetar

Creature
nameDark Planetar
othernamesFallen Planetar
alignment2eChaotic evil

Dark planetars, also known as fallen planetars, were planetars, powerful celestials from the upper planes, who had fallen from the grace of their patron deity. Some were dragged down to the depths of the Abyss, while others languished in the Prime Material plane.

Abilities

Dark planetars had access to both arcane and divine magic in their spellcasting portfolio. Among the spells they employed, were: flame strike, symbol, dispel magic, heal, globe of blades, and unholy word among others.

Combat

Like their still-celestial counterparts, dark planetars possessed inherent powers that made them terrific combatants.

They had inherent protections from all forms of physical damage, held resistances against to the elements, and near-immunity to all manners of spells and magical effects. Dark planetars could not be charmed, confused, or petrified. They were permanently hasted and regenerated at double the rate of other beings with the same boon.

Possessions

Some dark planetars wielded powerful, +3 enchanted greatswords that had a chance to dispel magic, nullify illusory creations, or even decapitate foes upon a landing a successful strike.

Ecology

It was possible to gate a dark planetar into existence by use of the spell, summon dark planetar. This magic was only available to the most powerful, malevolent or neutrally-aligned arcane spellcasters.

History

In the , a number of dark planetars fell into the service of the powerful Bhaalist priestess, Amelyssan the Blackhearted.

Notable Dark Planetars

  • Fazrian, a fallen planetar who served as the master of the Terminus Level of Undermountain.
  • The Winged, the leader of the Planar Hunters and servant of Szass Tam.
