Dragon issue 349 was released in November of 2006. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Hitting the Bullseye

By Eric Cagle. p. 21

A comprehensive guide to bows and crossbows.

Demonomicon of Iggwilv: Dagon

By James Jacobs, Illustrated by Andrew Hou. p. 30-45

A CR 30 Dagon, his cult, his thralls, and his minions.

The Horde

By Brian Cortijo, Edward Bonny and Laszlo Kollerl, Illustrated by Ramón Pérez and Eric Deschamps, Cartography by Robert Lazzaretti. p. 46-64

A massive gazetteer of the Hordelands.

The Ecology of the Ogre Mage

By Tim Hitchcock, Illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 68-72

Background on ogre magi, their real history, and advanced monsters.

Class Acts - Poisonmaking Guide

By Amber E. Scott, Illustrated by Christophe Swal. p. 86-87

This article serves as a reference for characters who wish to make poison. It provides easy access to lists of poisons, rules for crafting them, and a few new optional rules and new equipment as well.
