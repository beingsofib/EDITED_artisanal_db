Dragon issue 428 was released in PDF format only in October of 2013. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Backdrop: Westgate

By Erik Scott de Bie, illustrated by Eric Belisle. p. 4-12

The city of Westgate is brimming with opportunity—for those who can handle the risk.

Court of Stars: The Wild Hunt

By Aaron Infante-Levy, illustrated by Beth Trott. p. 13-16

The Horned Lord was adopted into the Green Court, but he was destined to fall from favor.

Codex of Betrayal: Amon the Wolf

By Tim Eagon, illustrated by Beth Trott. p. 23-27

An introduction to Amon, exiled Duke of the Nine Hells.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Treasures from the Far Realm

By Eytan Bernstein, illustrated by Jim Nelson. p. 28-31

A magic item crafted in the Far Realm may be useful in mortal hands, but never let one fool you into thinking it's your friend.

Eye on the Realms: The Speaking Skull of Themtraver Hall

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Eric Belisle. p. 38-41

In the gloomy, rotten-floored Themtraver Hall dwells the Speaking Skull.
