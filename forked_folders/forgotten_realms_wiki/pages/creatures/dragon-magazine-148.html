Dragon issue 148. No one conquers who does not fight, was released in August of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Always Wear Your Best Suit

By Gordon R. Menzies. p. 14-17

Customizing armor with enameling, engraving, plating, and special materials.

Tracking Down the Barbarian

By David Howery. p. 18

A revised barbarian class.

Good Does Not Mean Boring

By Scott Bennie. p. 24

The different motivations of paladins.

Arcane Lore: Healing magic for wizards

By Bruce Kvam, illustrated by Janet Aulisio. p. 34–38

Wizards aren't clerics, but they, too, can cure wounds.

  • Accelerated metabolism
  • Arnvidís unseen limb
  • Dispel exhaustion
  • Empath
  • Exchange
  • Life force transfer
  • Vampire dagger

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 40

New products for July and August.

  • Cities of Mystery
  • Ronin Challenge module.
  • Tantras module.
  • Tantras novel.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 68

A review of Pool of Radiance.
