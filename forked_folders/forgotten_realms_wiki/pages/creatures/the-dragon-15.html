Dragon magazine 15 was released in June 1978. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Dragon Magic

By Michael Benveniste. p. 5

Benveniste gives variant rules for creating spell lists for dragons in the 1st-edition Dungeons & Dragons game.

Creatures

dragons

Pits

By Richard Morenoff. p. 7

This article contains random rules for creating traps.

Random Events Table for Settlements and/or Settled Areas

By N. Robin Crossby. p. 7

The article includes rules for randomly building specific events in different locations, such as plagues and blizzards.

Weather in the Wilderness

By David Tillery. p. 16

David Tillery has developed variant rules for adding weather into any 1st-edition Dungeons & Dragons game. The author gives detailed descriptions of the effects of rainfall and snow.
