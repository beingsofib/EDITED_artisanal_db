Death and the Dragon's Eye

Death and the Dragon's Eye was the twenty-seventh issue of the Advanced Dungeons &amp; Dragons comics series set in the Forgotten Realms and published by . It was written by Dan Mishkin, drawn by Jan Duursema, and edited by Kim Yale. It is the first instalment of the "Death and the Dragon's Eye" story arc.
