Kitsune kasumi, also known as mist foxes, were minor nature spirits that took the form of white foxes and lived in misty places. They were about in length and possessed human-level intellect. The were able to take on a mist-like form at will and breathed a pale blue fox fire.

They were known to inhabit the island of Miyashima in Kozukura.
