Dragon issue 76 was released in August of 1983. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ecology of the Beholder

By Ed Greenwood and Roger E. Moore. p. 6-8

A scholarly treatise on the Beholder.

Leomund's Tiny Hut: For NPCs only: the death master

By Lenard Lakofka. p. 10

An evil, necromantic NPC sub-class of the magic-user to foil your PCs in Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

The Nine Hells, Part II

By Ed Greenwood. p. 22-44

Detailed descriptions of the bottom 4 layers of the Nine Hells (and their inhabitants) in the Forgotten Realms.

  • Malbolge
  • Maladomini
  • Cania
  • Nessus
