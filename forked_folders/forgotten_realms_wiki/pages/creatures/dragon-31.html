Dragon+ 31

Dragon+ issue 31 was released on April 29, 2020. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Previews: In the Works

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

The Dragon+ team held council at the end of 2018, and decreed that each issue would continue to offer early looks at products in development. And so, let's see what's in the works...

  • Relentless
  • Warriors of Waterdeep
  • Baldur's Gate III

Playing D&D Remotely

By Matt Chapman, Chris Hislop and Oz Mills.

Maintaining safe social distancing doesn’t mean you can’t still gather your party. We check out virtual tabletop software and other online resources.

Accessibility & D&D

By Chloe Mashiter.

Chloe Mashiter highlights the charities, streamers, and advocates helping to build better awareness around accessibility in the D&D community.

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll, Cartography by Dyson Logos, Todd Gamble, Mike Schley.

As we hit the battlefield, we look at a variety of maps in support of military campaigns.

Dndspeak d100 list: Gargantuan Monsters

By Casey Willis.

Break out two d10 and inject a colossal threat into your game thanks to our massive list of massive beasts.

D&D Classics: Battlefields

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

This issue we consider the chaos of pitched battlefields as well as the stalwart defense of fortified castles in military campaigns.

Behind The Screens: Tactics for Mass Combat

By Sam Hanshaw.

Big battles don’t need to cause big problems with our pro tips for handling hordes.
