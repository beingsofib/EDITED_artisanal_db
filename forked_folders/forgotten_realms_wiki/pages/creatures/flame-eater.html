Flame eaters were a kind of magical spider in Eberron created by the Spinner of Shadows, a fragment of Lolth, to counter and defeat the power of the Silver Flame.

Description

In their "natural" form the spiders appeared to be a semi-translucent orange. After they had devoured a piece of the Silver Flame, they glowed with a bright blue radiant light from the energies inside them.

Abilities

Created for the express purpose of breaking the Spinner of Shadows out of her jail, these spiders could consume small parts of the Silver Flame Guardian (and thus, the essence of the Silver Flame itself), dimming and weakening the bonds put upon the Spinner. They also possessed a slow but lethal venom imbued with the power of the Abyss that resisted all conventional forms of healing.

History

When the Spinner of Shadows was partially freed by the Lords of Dust, she created and used these spiders to further weaken the Spinner's Prison and enable her escape to the Demonweb. The spiders scurried about the prison consuming the flames, only for heroes sent by the Templar Orrex and Brother Aurelius to appear and start sabotaging her efforts. They slew the spiders and salvaged the flames they had consumed, replacing the broken wards and restoring the bindings. Despite this effort Lolth was able to find the prison and shatter it entirely, freeing the Spinner to escape into the Demonweb Pits.
