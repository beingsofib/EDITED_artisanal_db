Alfalfa, also known as lucerne, It had a distinctive sweet smell.

Availability

Places in the Realms where alfalfa was known to be cultivated included:

  • In Interior Faerûn, alfalfa was a staple crop in the nations of Cormyr In Cormyr it was one of the grains that filled fields across the nation that spanned hundreds of acres, including the Kirinwood. And in Sembia, the typical farm had dedicated field plots to alfalfa.
  • In the Moonshae Isles, alfalfa was grown on the island of Snowdown.
  • Alfalfa could be found in the Vast, where businesses such as Norge Greenbank's Horses fed them to horses.
  • In the Blade Kingdoms, lucerne was collected and kept as cattle and horse feed.

Usages

  • Centaurs were known to sometimes eat either fresh or dried alfalfa.
  • Some used alfalfa in the crafting of alchemical powders for curing nausea or paralysis.
