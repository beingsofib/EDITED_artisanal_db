Dragon issue 125 was released in September of 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

The Ecology of the Greenhag

By Nigel Findley. p. 10-12

A scholarly treatise on the Greenhag.

Woodlands of the Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 14-16

Common trees of Faerun, including a number of unique species.

  • Blueleaf
  • Duskwood
  • Felsul
  • Hiexel
  • Phandar
  • Shadowtop
  • Silverbark
  • Suth
  • Vundwood
  • Weirwood
  • Zalantar

The Code of Chivalry

By Mark Easterday. p. 18

A how-to on creating orders of knighthood, with examples.

Armies From the Ground Up

By James A. Yates. p. 30

How to build and maintain an army, including taxes, mercenaries, auxilaries, expansion, and more.

Plane Speaking: The (Positive) Quasi-Elementals!

By Jeff Grubb. p. 68–69

A look at three natives of the positive quasi-elemental plane in Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

  • Radiance quasi-elemental
  • Steam quasi-elemental
  • Mineral quasi-elemental

Role-playing Reviews

By Ken Rolston. p. 78–82

Treasure Hunt.
