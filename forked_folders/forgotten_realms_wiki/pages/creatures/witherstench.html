Witherstenches, also known as skunk beasts, were strange, monstrous relatives of the more mundane skunk.

Description

These creatures had very little fur, with what little they did have being in the form of dirty patches. Their skin was a blotchy yellow and covered in tiny purple spots. On average their bodies grew to be in length.

Abilities

The tiny purple spots that covered the body of a witherstench constantly emitted a powerful, nauseating musk odor.

Combat

These creatures generally fought with their claws.

Ecology

Diet

Witherstenches were carrion eaters.

Habitats

Witherstenches were primarily found in areas that were dank and filthy.

Usages

A cup's worth of musk from a witherstench could be mixed in a broth with the blood of a green dragon to enchant the same type of dragon's tooth.
