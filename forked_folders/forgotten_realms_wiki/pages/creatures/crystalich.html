Crystalich

Creature
nameCrystalich
size2eSmall
patron deitySavras

A crystalich was a rare form of lich created when a powerful diviner or sibylite merged their spirit with a scrying device, transforming themselves into a sentient crystal ball.

Powers

Crystaliches could scry anywhere in Faerûn and on any plane of existence they desired. Furthermore, they could cast spells within any location they were scrying.

History

A trio of crystaliches was believed to inhabit the Hall of Pools and Mirrors buried deep within Candlekeep.
