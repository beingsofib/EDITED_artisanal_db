Alu-fiends (), also known as alu-demons or semi-demons, were female half-fiends born of procreation between a succubus demon and a human.

Description

They closely resembled humans, with only vestigial horns and under-sized bat-like wings to give away their origins. They were always female, and varied in height at .

Abilities

A gift of their succubus heritage, an alu-demon could drain life energy from another being with but a firm physical touch, and use half of it to heal themselves.

Alu-demons had an array of magical powers, giving them the power to charm mortals; read others' thoughts; make compulsive suggestions; and shape change to a humanoid form of around the same size, height, and weight as themselves, all up to thrice a day. They could also teleport a short distance once a day.

Moreover, a quarter of alu-demons were of genius-level intellect and could learn and cast spells just as wizards could. Some could also develop psionics, but only on an individual basis.

In turn, alu-demons were naturally resistant to magic.

Thanks to their natural demonic resilience against attacks, alu-demons rarely needed to wear mundane armor, as it interfered with their innate protection. However, they would wear enchanted armor or don other magical items when they provided better protection. Only enchanted weapons or cold-wrought iron could harm them.

They saw with a superior degree of infravision effective up to .

Personality

A majority of alu-demons were chaotic evil like their mothers, but around a fifth were not entirely bound to evil.

Relationships

Alu-fiends were known to serve the goddess of bad luck Beshaba, with two of the winged creatures being among the deity's closest attendants.
