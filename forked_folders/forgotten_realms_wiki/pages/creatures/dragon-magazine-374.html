Dragon issue 374 was released in PDF format only in April of 2009. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Creature Incarnations: Orcs

By Greg Bilsland and Mike McNerney. p. 34

Many more orcs for your 4e game.

The Ecology of the Deva

By Chris Sims. p. 41-52

A scholarly treatise on the Deva.

Design & Development: Familiars

By Logan Bonner and Stephen Radney-MacFarland. p. 78

How the 4e familiars came to be.
