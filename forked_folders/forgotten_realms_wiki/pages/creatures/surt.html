Surt was a vegetable found in southern and southeastern Faerûn.

Description

The root of the surt plant was a large, bulbous, tuber with a strong flavor. It was a main ingredient in mlael, a somewhat spicy brown gravy favored by the denizens of Delzimmer circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR.
