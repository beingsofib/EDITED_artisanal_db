An ochre jelly () was a dangerous variety of ooze.

Description

Ochre jellies resembled giant amoebas, dark yellow in color. A mature ochre jelly reached up to in diameter and around thick. However, it could compress its mass to fit through gaps of only . Moreover, it had a mass of .

Combat

They lurked in dungeons, slowly creeping along floors, walls, and ceilings and under and through doors and cracks looking for victims. When they were found, the ochre jelly extended itself and latched onto a victim, proceeding to constrict and engulf them. The jelly exuded pseudopods to slam and grapple foes in combat.

Ecology

If cut, an ochre jelly could split into two similar, but smaller, offspring.

Habitats

Ochre jellies were known to exist in the dungeon of Xûl-Jarak in Thar. They could also be found in the ruins of the Ulcaster school near Beregost.

In the Abyss, ochre jellies could be found in Shedaklah, the Slime Pits, the 222 layer of the Abyss.

Significance

They were favored by the drow deity Ghaunadaur.
