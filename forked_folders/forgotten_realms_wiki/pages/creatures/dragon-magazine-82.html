Dragon issue 82 was released in February of 1984. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ecology of the Peryton

By Nigel D. Findley. p. 10–12

A scholarly treatise on the Peryton.

Wounds and weeds: Plants that can help keep characters alive

By Kevin J. Anderson. p. 14

A dozen plants that can aid healing of characters.

Rings that do weird things: Thirteen pieces of jewelry not from the DMG

By Jeff Abbott, Bill Birdsall, Charles C. Craig, Ed Greenwood, Richard Lucas, Jerome Mayard, Craig Stenseth and Thomas Zarbock. p. 28

New magic items.

Spells between the covers: Details for delving into magical research

By Bruce Heard. p. 55–68

Rules for conducting magical research.
