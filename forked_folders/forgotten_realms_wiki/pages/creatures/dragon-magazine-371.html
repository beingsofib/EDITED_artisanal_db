Dragon issue 371 was released in PDF format only in January of 2009. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Playing Dhampyr

By Brian James.p. 32–38

These tormented souls have the blood of vampires in their veins. Learn what it means to be one of the tainted dhampyr.

Origin Stories: Incorporating Races

By Bruce R. Cordell.p. 39–42

This article suggests ways to incorporate warforged into a Realms game.

Creature Incarnations: Zombies

By David Marthaler, illustrated by David Allsop. p. 46–53

Zombie 'features' and over a half-dozen new types of zombies.
