Dragon magazine 272, Dragons, was released in June 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: Andy Collins

By Steve Kenson. p. 18

Editor and designer Andy Collins is the product of "a fairly normal gamer youth".

Up on a Soapbox: Hooray for the Dungeon Crawl

By Gary Gygax. p. 22

Praising the maligned dungeon crawl.

The King and Queen of Dragons: Bahamut & Tiamat

By Skip Williams, illustrated by Carl Critchlow. p. 28

Bahamut and Tiamat return for the 3rd Edition D&D game, exclusively in these pages.

How to Talk to a Dragon

By Robin D. Laws, illustrated by Dave Dorman. p. 42

One wrong word, and it’s time to make a saving throw.

The Secret Library of Vecna - Things You Weren't Meant to See

By Steve Miller, illustrated by Marc Sasso. p. 52–57

Don't get caught browsing these stacks.

Arcane Lore: Dragon Dweomers IV

By Robert S. Mullin, illustrated by John Matson. p. 68–72

As if dragons weren't deadly enough, here are even more spells with which they can crush those meddling heroes.

;Spells

breath control • breathmantle • claw aura • dimension trap • hoard cache • magnify breath weapon • wing razors

The Dragon's Bestiary: Diminutive Dragons

By James Wyatt, illustrated by Carlo Arellano. p. 78–82

From the familiar to the fantastic, these tiny dragons make perfect companions or pests.

  • Crow's-nest dragon
  • Geyser dragon
  • Mole dragon
  • Pavilion dragon

The Ecology of the Hydra

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by David Day. p. 84-90

The secrets of a classic monster, the Hydra, now updated for a sneak preview of the 3rd Edition game.

The New Adventures of Volo: Quotations of the Realms

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Ron Spencer. p. 94–97

A collection of quotes from around the Realms delivered by Volothamp Geddarm.
