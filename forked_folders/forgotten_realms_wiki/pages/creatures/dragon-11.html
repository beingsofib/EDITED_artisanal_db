Dragon+ 11

Dragon+ issue 11 was released on December 15, 2016. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Concepting the Creatures

By Kate Irwin, Richard Whitters and Shawn Wood, Illustrated by Tyler Jacobson, Richard Whitters, Tom Babbey, Chris Seaman, and Christopher Burdett.pp. 7

A brief look at the creation of certain creature illustrations for Volo's Guide to Monsters.

Expanding the Underdark

By John Houlihan. pp. 9

An introduction to the board game Tyrants of the Underdark and it's new expansion Tyrants of the Underdark: Aberrations and Undead.

Dungeon Mapping

By John Houlihan. pp. 11

An interview with map artist Mike Schley, featuring several samples of his work.

Lore You Should Know: Tabaxi vs. tabaxi

By Matthew Sernett. pp. 16

An article explaining the difference between the feline tabaxi race, and the Maztican tribe named Tabaxi.

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll, Cartography by Jason A. Engle and Lee Moyer. pp. 22

Several maps from the Storm King's Thunder adventure module.
