Aberrant drakes were magically-created species of drakes, imbued with the corruption of the Far Realm, sometime in the days of the ancient Dragon Empire.

Description

Aberrant drakes were large quadrupedal drakes with tough purple scales that covered their bodies. Their undersides were protected by a bright orange set of interlocking shells, and the snouts ended in a menacing orange horn. Their claws were black in color.

Abilities

These creatures shared many of the abilities common across all similar species of drakes. An aberrant drake's attacks were their sharp teeth and rending claws. When they were used as mounts, aberrant drakes exuded an aura that empowered their rider, both physically and by boosting their skills.

History

Aberrant drakes were products of magical creation by the ancient Dragon Empire, being twisted by magical energies of the Far Realm, long before the 15 century DR. Their original purpose was to act as guardians and pets. Despite being mounts and pets, they possessed some level of will and intelligence, allowing some of the aberrant drakes to rebel against their masters, the evil Kabal, and join adventurers and heroes of Neverwinter instead.
