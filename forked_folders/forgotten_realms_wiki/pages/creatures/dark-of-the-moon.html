Dark of the Moon

Dark of the Moon is the twentieth issue of the Advanced Dungeons &amp; Dragons comics series set in the Forgotten Realms and published by DC Comics. It was written by Dan Mishkin, drawn by Jan Duursema, and edited by Elliot S. Maggin. It is the second installment in the "Phases of the Moon" story arc.
