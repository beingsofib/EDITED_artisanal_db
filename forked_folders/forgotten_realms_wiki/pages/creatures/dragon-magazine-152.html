Dragon issue 152. In the darkness dwell things darker still, was released in December of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ecology of the Umber Hulk

By Tony Jones. p. 10

A scholarly treatise on the Umber Hulk.

In a Cavern, In a Canyon...

By Thomas M. Kane, illustrated by Jeff Echevarria. p. 18-27

Rules for mining and metallurgy.

The Wanderers Below

By Buddy Pennington. p. 28

Random encounter tables for the Underdark.

Servants of the Jeweled Dagger

By Eric Oppen. p. 40

The lives and habits of the gray dwarves, or duergar.

The First Notch

By R.A. Salvatore. p. 44

An early adventure of the dwarf Bruenor Battlehammer.

Through the Looking Glass

By Jim Bambra. p. 81-82

A review of Cities of Mystery from the miniature gamer's perspective.
