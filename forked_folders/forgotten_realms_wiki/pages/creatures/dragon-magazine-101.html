Dragon issue 101 was released in September of 1985. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Plan it by the numbers

By Frank Mentzer. p. 16

A system for tailoring chanllenges to your PCs.

Charging isn't cheap

By Peter Johnson. p. 29

Expanding the rules for recharging rods, staves, and wands.

Creature Catalog III

By Various Authors. p. 39–55

Two dozen more monsters for added Advanced Dungeons & Dragons game excitement.
