Dragon magazine 259, The Campaign, was released in May 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Stolen Dreams

By Elaine Cunningham, illustrated by Russell Walks. p. 52–58

A short story set at the Friendly Arms Inn.

Rogues Gallery: Dream Spheres

By Elaine Cunningham, illustrated by David Day. p. 60–63

In the City of Splendors, a word can cut more deeply than a knife.

  • Isabeau Thione
  • Jerome Diloontier
  • Myrna Cassalantar

The Dragon's Bestiary: Symbiotes and Parasites

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Brad McDevitt. p. 64–69

Hold still... you've got something on you.

  • Blood pudding
  • Powerslug
  • Skullcap ivy
  • Worry-wart

Wyrms of the North: Zundaerazylym

By James Wyatt, illustrated by Carlo Arellano. p. 70-74

Zundaerazylym. This steel dragon's secrets could spell the North's salvation—or its doom.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Sleep on It

By Drew Karpyshyn, illustrated by Jim Crabtree. p. 76–78

For the weary adventurer, a selection of the finest in pillows, blankets, and bedrolls.

  • Bedroll of the woodsman
  • Blanket of recuperation
  • Blanket of security
  • Cover of invisibility
  • Pillow of dream traveling
  • Pillow of magic restoration
  • Pillow of nightmares
  • Pillow of perpetual youth

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 108

New products for May, June and July.

  • Baldur's Gate novel.
  • Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas software.
  • Skullport sourcebook.
  • The Dream Spheres novel.
  • The Silent Blade novel.
