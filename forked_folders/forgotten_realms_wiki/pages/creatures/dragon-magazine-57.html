Dragon issue 57 was released in January of 1982. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Leomund's Tiny Hut: Shield and Weapon Skills

By Len Lakofka. p. 10–12

Rules for determining starting weapon proficiencies.

Random Magic Items

By Pete Mohney. p. 24

Tables for generating random magic items that are less conspicuous than the standard (e.g. a magic rock).

Up on a Soapbox: Dungeons Aren’t Supposed to be "For Men Only"

By Roger E. Moore. p. 52

Promoting gender equality in roleplaying games.

Up on a Soapbox: Play a Villain? An Evil Idea

By Brian Blume. p. 52

The downsides to playing evil-aligned characters.
