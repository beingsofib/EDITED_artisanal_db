Dragon issue 88 was released in August of 1984. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Physics and falling damage

By Arn Ashleigh Parker. p. 12

A new method for calculating falling damage based on velocity.

Kinetic energy is the key

By Steven Winter. p. 19

A rebuttal to "Physics and falling damage," which says that kinetic energy is the key.

The Ecology of the Rust Monster

By Ed Greenwood. p. 22-24

A scholarly treatise on the Rust Monster.
