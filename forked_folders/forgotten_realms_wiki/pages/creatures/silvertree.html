Silvertrees were a species of trees that grew in the courtyard of the villa of House Maerdrym in the ancient city of Myth Drannor.

History

Centuries after the fall of Myth Drannor in the Weeping War, baelnorn protector of the mythal Miroden Silverblade reminisced about the beauty of the old city, swaying silvertrees and the Windsong Tower touching the night stars. In the , a Wearer of Purple of the Cult of the Dragon – Kya Mordrayn used that memory to give the lich false promise of Myth Drannor's rebirth in exchange for his aid tapping into the mythal's magics.
