Almonds were a type of nut found across many parts of the Realms that grew on flowering trees.

Availability

Places in the Realms where almond trees were known to be cultivated included:

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold almonds under it's "Aurora's Larder" section for 3 gold pieces per , as well as almond oil
  • In nation of Amn, almonds could be found cultivated at Arnise Hold and served to guests.
  • Fruits And More, a business in Ravens Bluff that sold a variety of produce, offered bags of almonds for 7 gp.
  • In the Vilhon Reach, roasted almonds were a staple of Turmishan cuisine.
  • In the Sea of Swords, almonds were cultivated on the Moonshae Isles and the island of Mintarn.

Beyond Faerûn

  • Almonds were cultivated in some parts of Kara-Tur.
  • In Interior Zakhara, almonds were cultivated in the orchards of the fortress Sarahin and in the Garden of Delights within it. west Zakhara.
  • In Zakhara's Crowded Sea, almonds were among the produce cultivated on the Fortunate Isles through careful irrigation.

Beyond Toril

  • Bytopia, where they were used by the plane's inhabitants to make an aromatic nutbread.
  • On the world of Earth. During Elminster Aumar's many visits to the home of Ed Greenwood, he came to love the macadamia nuts and garlic butter roasted almonds that his host served.

Usage

Almond Products

  • Almond brittle, a kind of sweet candy.
  • Almond butter.
  • Almond cake.
  • Almond milk, a milk-like beverage.
  • Almond oil.
  • Almond tarts.
  • In terms of alcohol, these nuts could be distilled into almond brandy

Other Dishes

  • Almond liqueurs were used to fortify sluth into zzar,
  • Dolmat, a west Zakharan dish that included almonds.
  • Fryplate, a dish popular across communities of Northwest Faerûn and the Savage Frontier, featured generous helpings of sliced almonds.
  • Halomonde, an almond paste condiment.
  • Jack wyvern tail, a Cormyrian dish, sometimes used almonds.
  • Mraed, a type of nut-based desert, traditionally used almonds.
  • Wheels of redsharp were typically studded with almonds.

Notable Users

  • In the lands further south and east of the Western Heartlands, it was quite common for ground almonds to be found amongst the spice pouches carried by travelers.

Businesses

  • The Blackwater Stout, a tavern in Blackfeather Bridge (village), offered its patrons roasted almonds.
  • Donder's Dancing Masques, an unruly tavern in Tharkar, offered its patrons roasted almonds.
  • The Flying Stag, an inn in Voonlar, offered its patrons an almond and mushrooms soup.
  • Hriiat Fien Pastries, a bakery in the city of Waterdeep, sold almond-and-apple sugar tarts at a price of 1 copper piece.
  • The Lantern Inn, a tavern in Airspur, offered patrons a delectable dish of squid coated in slivered almonds, garlic, and red pepper.
  • Maercrossan's, a riverside restaurant in Ravens Bluff, served sea bass slathered in roast almonds and mushrooms.
  • The Moonstone Mask, a renowned festhall in Neverwinter, offered almond tarts and a blackberry-and-apple pie that was topped with cream and sliced almonds.
  • The Plate of Eels, a restaurant in Hultail, served roasted chicken with almonds.
  • The Raging Lion, an inn & tavern in Scornubel, served fried and stuffed snakes in a gravy of poultry stock and almonds.
  • The Sign of the Forgehammer, an inn in Mirabar, served almonds as part of its curry dish.
  • The Silver Taproom, a tavern in Essembra, used a dash of powdered in the simmering sauce they soaked their venison pies in over night.
  • The Stag Triumphant, a sophisticated inn in Mosstone, sometimes served a stew of almonds & oysters at highsunfeast.
  • The Swordsmith's House, a fancy inn in High Castle, served chicken cooked with almonds.
  • Volodar's Stardust Inn, an inn in Ravens Bluff, served roasted quail with almonds.

Individuals

  • The silver dragon Deszeldaryndun Silverwing loved to eat roasted almonds when in his human form.
  • Duara, a Telflammar mage, was quite fond of almonds.

Trivia

  • At the Cyrion Spice House, asking the proprietor if he had any ground almonds and offering to pay top coin for any he had, was the secret code for being allowed back into its illicit gambling den.
  • Oil of invulnerability was noted as having a strong aroma that resembled the smell of burnt almonds.
  • Tombworms and uttercaps were both said to have a taste similar to roasted almonds.
