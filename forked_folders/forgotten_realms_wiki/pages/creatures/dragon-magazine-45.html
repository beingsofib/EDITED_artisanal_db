Dragon issue 45 was released in January of 1981. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Gas 'em up and Smoke 'em Out

By Robert Plamondon. p. 6-7

Dungeon Ventilation Clears the Air

By Robert Plamondon. p. 10

Talks about different types of ventilation systems for underground lairs / dungeons.

Magic Items for Everyman

By Philip Meyers. p. 16

Tables for quickly outfitting higher (14+) level characters with magic items.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Box of Many Holdings

By John Beck. p. 23

A cubical chest that has 6 magical storage compartments.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Ring of Oak

By Roger E. Moore. p. 23

A ring that allows a dryad to leave the vicinity of her tree.

Up on a Soapbox: Ways to Handle High-Level Headaches

By Lewis Pulsipher. p. 23

4 things GMs can do when player characters reach higher levels.

Leomund's Tiny Hut: Missile Fire and the Archer Subclass

By Len Lakofka. p. 32–36

Expanded rules for missile fire in combat and the Archer / Archer-Ranger NPC class.

How to Have a Good Time Being Evil

By Roger E. Moore. p. 62

Ways to put together an evil-oriented campaign.
