Dragon issue 401 was released in PDF format only in July of 2011. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Class Acts: Assassin: Black Flame Zealot

By Chad Brown, illustrated by Peter Tikos. p. 19

Background information on the Order of the Black Flame, plus character options for playing a black flame zealot.
