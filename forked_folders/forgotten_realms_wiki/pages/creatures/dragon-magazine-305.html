Dragon issue 305, Urban Adventures, was released in March of 2003. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Urban Heroes

By Stan!. p. 21

Character classes and feats for the city.

Faith and Honor

By Skip Williams. p. 37

Piety for non-clerics.

Fear the Invincible Blade

By Robin D. Laws. p. 46

Combat flourishes and other special rules for fighters.

The Ecology of...Venom and Coil: The Secret Life of the Yuan-Ti

By Robin D. Laws. p. 54-62

A scholarly treatise on the Yuan-Ti.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Mirror Mirror, on the Wall

By Jacqui Smith. p. 64

Magic mirrors.
