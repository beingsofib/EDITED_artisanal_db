Dragon issue 370 was released in December of 2008 as a PDF. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Her Majesty's Secret Servants: Agents of Tiamat

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 5-12

Chromatic dragons aren’t the only servants of Tiamat.

Riven Justice: The Mercykillers

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 13-24

The Mercykillers return to D&D, complete with information on how your character can join their ranks.

Adventurers of the Realms: Ancient Magic and Present Threats

By Chris Tulach. p. 35–39

Whether you’re playing a Living FR campaign or not, you’ll want to take a look at these regionally inspired extras for your character.

Dangers of the Demonweb

By Logan Bonner. p. 40

New monsters fresh from the newest D&D Miniatures release, presented here for inclusion in your campaign.

Design & Development: Reimagining the Planes

By James Wyatt and Richard Baker. p. 50

Rich and James discuss how the cosmology of D&D evolved with the design of 4th Edition.
