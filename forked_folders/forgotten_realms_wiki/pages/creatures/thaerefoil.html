Thaerefoil was a plant known to grow in Cormyr and perhaps elsewhere in Faerûn.

Uses

When consumed as a tea or even by swallowing the paste of chewed leaves, thaerefoil diminished the physical lusts of men for a time being. To chew and swallow a single leaf would make a man impotent for days.
