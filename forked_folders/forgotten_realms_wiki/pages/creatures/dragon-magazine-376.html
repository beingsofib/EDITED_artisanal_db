Dragon issue 376 was released in PDF format only in June of 2009. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Demonomicon of Iggwilv: Turaglas, the Ebon Maw

By Ari Marmell p. 11–22

The Ebon Maw was born from the Abyss after the first demon lords rose to power, but it has since grown to rival many of its peers.

Adventurers of the Realms: Tarmalune and the Windrise Ports

By Chris Tulach p. 45–50

Information and rules about adventurers and life in and around Tarmalune.

Realmslore: Sarifal

By Brian R. James p. 59–65

Information about the region of the Moonshae.
