Slop Street

Slop Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Slop Street was located in the southwest section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward. The road curved through much of the east and southeast parts of the ward, originating in the north at the end of Fishwife Alley, curving east around the Adventurers' Quarter, and eventually ending on the southern end of the Way of the Dragon. Other roads connecting with Slop Street included the Boomgarden, Curtain Alley, Dancing Court, and Fillet Lane.

Notable Locations

Several businesses were located on Slop Street. These included the Medusa's Glare, Nueth's Fine Nets, and the Temple of Good Cheer.
