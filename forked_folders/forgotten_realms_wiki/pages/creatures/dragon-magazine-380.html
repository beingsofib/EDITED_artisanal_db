Dragon issue 380 was released in PDF format only in October of 2009. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Abyssal Genasi

By Peter Schaefer. p. 18–23

While most genasi are touched by one of five elements, some have a darker heritage.

Barbarian Essentials: The Way of Rage

By Rob Heinsoo. p. 24

Learn about the essential character building and play options for your barbarian character, and get some new powers and mechanical options as well.

Wizard Essentials - Secrets of the Spiral Tower

By Eytan Bernstein. p. 36

Character building and tactical advice for your wizard character, as well as some new spells and other options.

Character Concepts

By John Zamarra. p. 45

Check out builds for levels 1–30 for a powerful shaman healer and a glaive-wielding warden, incorporating material from the Primal Power supplement.

Playing Vistani

By Chris Sims. p. 75-83

An introduction to the culture of the Vistani.

Playing Vistani: Secrets of the Caravans

By Chris Sims. p. 84-88

Feats and paragon paths related to the Vistani.

Playing Vistani: Magic and Mystery

By Chris Sims. p. 89-91

Rituals and magic items related to the Vistani.
