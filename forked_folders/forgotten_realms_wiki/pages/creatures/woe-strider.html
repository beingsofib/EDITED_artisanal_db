A woe strider was a type of aberrant creature that resembled a giant yellow caterpillar.

Description

Woe striders had rounded yellow bodies. They had a number of spindly arms that connected to hands with talons.

Abilities

They had sharp mouths, from which projected a cone of antimagic while open. A bite from a woe strider temporarily suppressed magical properties of magic items. They also spoke with telepathy.

History

In the late 15 century DR, goliaths of Wyrmdoom Crag were transformed into aberrations by Far Realm energies brought from the city of Xorvintroth. Along with star spawn, core spawn and spectators, some woe striders appeared.
