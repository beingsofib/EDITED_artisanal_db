Dragon issue 252, Hauntings, was released in October of 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Dragon's Bestiary: Formidable Visitants

By Michael D. Winkle, illustrated by Mark Nelson. p. 64–69

Four creatures adapted from the works of M.R. James. Demonic sawfly • Living hair • Web-spectre • Death linen (Killer pillow • Flannel beast • Sheet)

Wyrms of the North: Palarandusk

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Storn Cook. p. 70–74

This article describes the ancient gold dragon Palarandusk, who protected a gnome village.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Jack-o-lanterns

By Brian P. Hudson, illustrated by Jim Crabtree. p. 76–79

Magical gourds.

Arcane Lore: Tomes of Forbidden Lore

By Matthew Martin, illustrated by L.A. Williams. p. 82–89

Filled with dark knowledge and forbidden magics, spellbooks found in the Demiplane of Dread are best left alone.

The Ecology of the Ghoul

By Wolfgang Baur, illustrated by Brad McDevitt. p. 90-94

All about the curious Ghouls of the Underdark.

ProFiles: R.A. Salvatore

By Allen Varney. p. 120

R.A. Salvatore tells us the true origin of Drizzt Do'Urden and discusses his new fantasy world of Corona.
