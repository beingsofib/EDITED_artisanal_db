Iron-vine was a plant notable for its unpleasant metallic, sickly-sweet smell.

Uses

The plant was used by gur hunters as a monster repellant. The plant was dried and powdered and could be detected by its foul aroma.

Notable Users

  • Gandrel, a 15 century DR monster hunter.
