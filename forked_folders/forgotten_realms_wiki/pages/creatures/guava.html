Guava was a variety of tropical fruit-bearing tree that could be found on Toril.

Availability

Places in the Realms where guavas were known to be grown or be cultivated:

  • In west Faerûn, guavas could be found in the land of Calimshan.
  • In Kara-Tur, guavas could be found on the isolated Malatran Plateau.
  • Somewhere in the Sea of Fallen Stars, upon an unnamed island where the pirate Ivar Bloodhand buried his treasure, one could find guava alongside many other varieties of tropical fruit.

Usage

  • Some juice stalls in Calimshan cities offered guava juice.
