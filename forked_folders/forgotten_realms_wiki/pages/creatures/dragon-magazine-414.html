Dragon issue 414 was released in PDF format only in August of 2012. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Editorial: De Planes, De Planes!

By Christopher Perkins, illustrated by Julie Dillon. p. 2

Is there room for the Feywild and the Shadowfell in the Great Wheel? Let’s peek behind the curtain and see how the D&D cosmology is shaping up.

The Ecology of the Modron

By Brian James, illustrated by Julie Dillon and Victoria Maderna. p. 7-15

A scholarly treatise on the Modron.

History Check: The Iggwilv-Graz'zt Affair

By John Rossomangno, illustrated by Eva Widermann. p. 20-24

Perhaps the greatest love story in D&D lore is the on-again, off-again affair between a demon prince named Graz'zt and the witch-queen Iggwilv.

Eye on the Realms: The Lost Dragon of Waterdeep

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Zoltan Boros. p. 34-37

There’s a lost dragon in Lord Eagleshield’s mansion. Can you be the first to find it?
