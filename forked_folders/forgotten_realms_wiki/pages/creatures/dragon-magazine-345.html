Dragon issue 345, Giants, was released in July of 2006. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Demonomicon of Iggwilv: Kostchtchie, Prince of Wrath

By James Jacobs, illustrated by Andrew Hou. p. 20–33

Everything you never wanted to know about Kostchtchie, the giant demon lord of winter.

Excursion

By Martin Rayla, illustrated by Jeff Carlisle. p. 44–53

Four ways to travel the world: by caravan, sail, dirigible, and submarine.

The Ecology of the Annis Hag

By F. Wesley Schneider, illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 64–68

A scholarly treatise on the Annis Hag.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: For and Against the Giants

By William L. Christensen, illustrated by Chuck Lukacs. p. 72–74

Check out what giants have in their bags besides just rocks.

Class Acts: Divine: Underdark Animals

By Ryan Nook. p. 86

Four creatures to fill the "animal" niche underground. Dekayi, Elgonn, Jagendar, and Tage'rivvin.
