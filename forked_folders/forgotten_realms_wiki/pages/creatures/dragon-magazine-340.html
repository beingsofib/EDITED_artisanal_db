Dragon issue 340, Astrology, was released in February of 2006. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Master Astrologer

By E.W. Morton, illustrated by Tyler Walpole. p. 36–40

A Prestige Class that follows the stars.

The Eternal Light

By Chris Tulach, illustrated by Jeff Laubenstein and Chuck Lukacs. p. 42–48

The Sun and its role in D&D.

Eye of the Night

By Craig Campbell, illustrated by Jeff Laubenstein and Chuck Lukacs. p. 51–57

The Moon and its role in D&D.

The Ecology of the Mooncalf

By Owen K.C. Stephens, illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 58

Everything you ever wanted to know about the malformed harbingers of disaster, the Mooncalf.

Spellcraft: The Twilight Codex

By Gregory Marks, illustrated by David Bircham. p. 72–75

Star spells and moon magic.
