Dragon issue 95 was released in March of 1985. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

From the Sorcerer's Scroll: Demi-humans get a lift

By Gary Gygax. p. 8

Increased level limitations for demi-humans.

The Ecology of the Cockatrice

By Ed Greenwood. p. 22-26

A scholarly treatise on the Cockatrice.

Credit where credit is due

By Katharine Kerr. p. 32

Elaborating upon the experience rules.

The many shapes of apes

By Stephen Inniss. p. 38

Primates packaged for use in the AD&D game.

Into the Forgotten Realms: A tournament adventure for the AD&D game

By Ed Greenwood. p. 43-54

An adventure for 10 pre-generated 5th-8th level PCs, to be completed in 4 hours. An expedition into the abandoned Myth Drannor School of Wizardry in the Dalelands, to keep a mad dictator of Scardale from gaining the magic within.

Battles above the dungeon

By Tim W. Brown. p. 55

Tactics and strategies for use in the great outdoors.
