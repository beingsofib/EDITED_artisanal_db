Dragon issue 136 was released in August of 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Taking Care of Business

By Anthony D. Gleckler. pp. 22-26

Merchants: An NPC class with cash.

A Room for the Knight

By Nick Kopsinis and Patrick G. Goshtigian. pp. 28-32

Inns and taverns in fantasy-game cities.

Fifty Ways to Foil Your Players

By Jape Trostle. pp. 34-38

50 NPC foils.

Arcane Lore: One Charge is Never Quite Enough

By Dan Snuffin. pp. 46–48

A new spell for recharging rods, staves, and wands.

  • Recharge

The Golem's Craft

By John C. Bunnell. pp. 54-56

Details on the difficult process of golem creation.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 64

New products for August.

  • Ruins of Adventure module.
  • Spellfire novel.
