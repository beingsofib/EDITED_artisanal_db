Dragon issue 138, Halloween Greetings from the Undead, was released in October of 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ungrateful Dead

By Tom Moldvay. p. 22

Beautiful ghouls and titanic zombies: new undead for your AD&D games.

Methods to Your Madness

By Ed Friedlander. p. 38

A new insanity system that lets a little lunacy go a long way.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 86

New products for September.

  • City System sourcebook.
  • The Savage Frontier sourcebook.

The Game Wizards: Empires of the Sands strikes back

By Scott Haring. p. 90–91

Sifting through the Empires of the Sands.
