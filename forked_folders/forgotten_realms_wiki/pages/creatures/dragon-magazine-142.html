Dragon issue 142 was released in February of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 40

New products for January and February.

  • Lords of Darkness module.
  • Mad Monkey vs the Dragon Claw module.
  • Streams of Silver novel.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 42

A review of the Pool of Radiance Clue Book.

Made-to-Order Clerical Orders

By Michael Harrison. p. 54

Religious hierarchies and temple design.

Arcane Lore: Spells of the Wild Cult

By Andrew Kasarskis, Christopher Krough, Craig Dudek, David Ulrich, Mike Rodgers, Nathaniel Downes and Tom Hazel. p. 62–64

15 new druid spells.

  • Animal/bird call
  • Coalstone
  • Druidsight
  • Hailstone
  • Sense direction
  • Sharpleaf
  • Shatter stone and metal
  • Sink into earth
  • Splinter wood
  • Stonewood
  • Thunderclap
  • Tracking
  • Warp stone or metal
  • Weather dome
  • Wind and rain protection
