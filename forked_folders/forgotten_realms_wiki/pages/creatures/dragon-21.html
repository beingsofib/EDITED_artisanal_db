Dragon+ 21

Dragon+ issue 21 was released on August 15, 2018. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Preview: In the Works

By Matt Chapman. pp. 5

Previews of most upcoming D&D works, including Waterdeep: Dragon Heist and the Endless Quest series.

Boss Battles

By Mark Bonington. pp. 14

A preview of the Vault of Dragons board game.

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll. pp. 18

Several free maps from several 1st edition sourcebooks, as well as The Rise of Tiamat:

  • Monastery Cellars & Secret Crypts
  • Ruins of Gygar's Castle
  • Geomorph: Basic Dungeon
  • Geomorph: Caves & Caverns
  • Geomorph: Lower Dungeons
  • Neronvain's Stronghold
  • Xonthal's Tower

Six Faces of Death

By Jason Bradley Thompson. pp. 22

An adventure where players must stop a mad skull lord from reactivating a flying battle station from Acheron that has crashed in the Sea of Swords.
