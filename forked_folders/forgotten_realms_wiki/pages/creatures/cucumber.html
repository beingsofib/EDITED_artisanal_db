Cucumbers was a type of cylindrical fruit that grew on a creeping vine plant of the same name.

Availability

Growing

Cucumbers were grown in the lands of Faerûn, and Zakhara,

In Sembia, cucumbers were a typical crop grown in a family-run farm, often grown in the same farm plot as other vegetables.

In Zakhara, crops of cucumbers and other vegetables were grown around the House Fajirik military camp on the Empty Glass Battlefield.

Selling

In the late 1300s DR, cucumbers were among the fruits and vegetables sold by the grocer Jack from a stall in the marketplace of Ravens Bluff in the Vast, at three for 5 sp.

Cucumbers were also sold by grocers in the marketplace of Westgate around 1486 DR.

Usage

Culinary

  • Cucumbers could be made into pickles.
  • Cucumber medallions in vinegar were a common feature of the eveningfeast menu in high-class eateries.
  • Feldepost's Inn, an old establishment in the town of Beregost, served cheese-and-cucumber buns.
  • Cucumber sandwiches were served at Rose's Tea Room in Ravens Bluff. They had just a little too much vinegar.
  • In Fendarl's Gate, cold cucumber soup was the current favorite of its lord, Eldebuck Thorm Fendarl, around 1366 DR.
  • A juiced and peppered cucumber sauce was available at the Old Stonebows inn in Archendale.
  • Turtleback soup included cucumber medallions; this was served at Blackthorn's Brew in Velen, Tethyr.
  • Quails in nut sauce with garlic cucumbers was a dish served to the wealthy in Cursrah during the time of the Calim Empire.
  • Cucumber ricotta torte with smoked salmon and caviar was on the menu at Umbrel's Hot Tarts in Ravens Bluff.
  • Zakharans enjoying fried crimson battan in a mixture that included cucumber juice.
  • In Sigil, complimentary bowls of cucumber soup were given to guests of the Slumbering Lamb boarding house.

Medicine

  • Besides being used as food, some used cucumbers in the treatment of inflammation.

Races & Sentient Creatures

  • Kappas, a race of creatures native to Kara-Tur, were quite fond of cucumber and melon and might be appeased by being given some.

Rumors & Legends

In the legend of Pariza and the Talking Bird told in Huzuz in Zakhara, the Grand Caliph Akbar al-Assad was served a cucumber stuffed with pearls as a demonstration.
