Dragon issue 293, World Building, was released in March of 2002. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Monsters with Class

By Rich Redman. p. 52

Using monsters as characters.

The Elemental Planetouched

By Sean K. Reynolds. p. 56

About the Genasi.

Campaigning with Class

By Andy Collins. p. 66

Customizing your campaign with prestige classes.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: The Missing Minotaur

By Ed Greenwood. p. 74-77

A new tavern in western Everlund.

Class Acts: The Nightsong Enforcer

By Monte Cook. p. 84

A new prestige class.
