Water moccasins were a rather common species of poisonous snake.

Ecology

Habitats

In Faerûn, they were known to be found in parts of the Sword Coast and in the Flooded Forest in the Vast. In Kara-Tur they were known to be found on Miyashima, a small lake-bound island on the larger island of Shinkoku.
