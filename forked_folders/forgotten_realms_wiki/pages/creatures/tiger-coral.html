Tiger coral was a species of coral named for its coloration that could be found across nearly all the shallows of the Inner Sea.

Description

This coral had a gold and brown coloration with striped patterns. It was a quite hardy breed of coral and grew at a rate comparable to star coral.

Ecology

Usages

Dead tiger coral was frequently used by Serôsians, particularly locathahs and merfolk, as a building material.

Notable Reefs

  • Three Gates Reef, one of two huge coral reefs that formed an ecosystem for the city of Myth Nantar. Measuring over tall and spanning many miles, it encroached upon much of the southern half of the city.
