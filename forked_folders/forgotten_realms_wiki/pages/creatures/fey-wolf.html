Fey wolves were a species of mundane wolves that dwelt in the exotic realm of the Feywild.

Description

Fey wolves resembled ordinary, large, and fearsome predator beasts of the Prime Material plane. Their gray fur bore glowing azure swirling markings that made them quite distinct from the regular wolves of the Prime. Fey wolves were typically wary of humanoids and stayed away from human settlements in the wild.

History

By the late 15 century DR, some of the fey wolves were captured at a young age and trained to be used as trusty mounts and companions. Some were found serving the Neverwintran masters in the Savage Frontier of Faerûn.
