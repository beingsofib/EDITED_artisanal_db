The Dragon issue 18 was released in September 1978. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Insanity, or Why is My Character Eating Leaves?

By Kevin Thompson. p. 9

This article gives 1st edition Dungeons & Dragons rules for developing rules for adding insanity into your game. Thompson has a nice chart for insanity saving throwing and creating madness behaviors, such as gibbering, catatonic, and kleptomaniac.

New Spells in D&D! Cure for an Ailing Campaign or Killer of A Healthy One?

By Paul Suliin. p. 13

Suliin writes an article containing new spells for a magic-user. Suliin creates a number of spells, including magic missile II, wall of water, and rust monster's touch.

The Chamber of the Godgame

By Mick McAllister. p. 19

Mick McAllister creates an interesting trap or encounter setup to challenge players to utilize their alignments to solve a puzzle.

Monkish Weapons & Monk vs. Monk Combat

By Garry Eckert. p. 23

This article gives new rules for monks to use various weapons, such as nunchakus and sai, plus a kedo combat chart.
