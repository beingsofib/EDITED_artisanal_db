Wildsage was a type of herb grown in Maskyr's Eye in the Vast.

Availability

Wildsage was cultivated by the farmers of Maskyr's Eye, alongside their other crops.

Culinary

Giving food a flavor not unlike buttered leek, wildsage was added to nearly every dish in the vale,
