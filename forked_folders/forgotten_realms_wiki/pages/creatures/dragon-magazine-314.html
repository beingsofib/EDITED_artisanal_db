Dragon issue 314, Elemental Magic, was released in December of 2003. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Brotherhood of the Burning Heart

By Clifford Horowitz, illustrated by Howard Lyon. p. 18–24

A fire group, with magic items, spells, and prestige class.

Masters of the Four Winds

By David Noonan, illustrated by Marc Sasso. p. 32–40

An air group, with prestige classes and spells.

Guardians of the Deepest Seas

By James Jacobs, illustrated by Jeff Laubenstein. p. 42–47

The Darkwater Guardians, a water group with prestige class and spells.

The Ecology of the Salamander

By Christopher Campbell, illustrated by Anne Stokes. p. 49–54

A scholarly treatise on the Salamander.

Channeling the Elements

By Spencer Kornhaber, illustrated by Arnie Swekel. p. 56–62

Four elemental prestige classes.

New Psionics Races

By Bruce R. Cordell. p. 64–68

A preview of the Expanded Psionics Handbook, plus the Synod, a new race.
