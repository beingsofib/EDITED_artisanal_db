Dragon issue 113 was released in September of 1986. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Welcome to Hades

By Bruce Barber. p. 10-26

All about the neutral evil Outer Plane.

A Capital Idea

By Vince Garcia. p. 28

How to run a business in the world of D&D.

TSR Profiles: Keith Parkinson

By Uncredited. p. 60

Profile of staff artist Keith Parkinson.

Clout for Clerics

By James A. Yates. p. 72

Expanded information on followers for high-level clerics.

Easy as 1, 2, 3

By Rick Swan. p. 74

Advice on creating memorable NPCs.
