Gnat

A gnat was a type of minuscule flying and biting insect found in many parts of Faerûn.

Ecology

These insects traveled in groups called swarms, described by many as "clouds". They were often seen in the company of mosquitos and other biting insects.

They were attracted to scents of death and decay,

Habitats

Known locations of these pestering bugs included the jungles of the Chultan Peninsula, in Cormyr in the land of Thay and the Yuirwood of neighboring Aglarond, in the Vast at a marshy bog within the vicinity of Ravens Bluff, in the Zehoarastria region of Amn, and in the Underdark.

Beyond the Prime Material, gnats could be encountered in Smaragd, the jungle-like 74 layer of the Abyss.

Predators

These creatures were preyed on by animals that typically ate insects, such as frogs, and spiders.

Trivia

  • Aril, a young halfling of the village Oakthorne, once took a dare by his friend Nem to run through a cloud of gnats with his mouth open. He spent a good time gagging and spitting out bits of gnats afterwards.
  • When the archwizard Serreg received the dagger of chaos, a blade that allowed him to polymorph himself, he contemplated the stealth capabilities of a gnat. He thought to himself that in such a form he could spy on any wizard in the Karsus Enclave.
