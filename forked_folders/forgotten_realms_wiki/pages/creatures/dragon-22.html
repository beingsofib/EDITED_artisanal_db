Dragon+ 22

Dragon+ issue 22 was released on October 9, 2018. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Previews: In the Works

By Matt Chapman. pp. 5

Previews of most upcoming D&D works, including Waterdeep: Dungeon of the Mad Mage, Guildmasters' Guide to Ravnica, and the board game Dungeon Mayhem.

D&D Classic

By Bart Carroll. pp. 17

Reprints of several undead-themed pieces from the Dragon magazine archives. The only directly Realms-related offering is the article "Daurgothoth, The Creeping Doom" by Ed Greenwood from Dragon #234. Also included is a series of articles by Tom Moldway: "Hearts of Darkness" from Dragon #126, "The Ungrateful Dead" from Dragon #138, "Out of the Shadows" from Dragon #162, and "Beyond the Grave" from Dragon #198. Finally, an excerpt on lycanthropy is provided from the 1st-edition Dungeon Masters Guide.

Treasures of the Yugoloths

By Jason Thompson. pp. 21

A selection of yugoloth-themed magical items.

Fiction: Final Exam & Best Friends

By Dave Gross. pp. 22

Reprints of the short stories "Final Exam" and "Best Friends", first published as promotional tie-ins to Baldur's Gate: Enhanced Edition.
