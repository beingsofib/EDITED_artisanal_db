Dragon issue 207, Treasures Magical and Mundane, was released in July of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Big-League Magic

By Chris Osborne. p. 10

Using artifacts in your AD&D campaign.

Trifling Treasures

By Halina Adamski. p. 31

Trash to make treasures that much more exciting.

Game Wizards: The Spellfire Card Game

By James Ward. p. 56–57

A preview of Spellfire: Master the Magic and a sort of checklist.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 120

New products for August.

  • City of Splendors boxed set.
  • Corsairs of the Great Sea module.
  • Marco Volo: Journey sourcebook.
