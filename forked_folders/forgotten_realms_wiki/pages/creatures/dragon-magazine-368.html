Dragon issue 368 was released in October of 2008 as a PDF. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Playtest: Barbarian

By Jesse Decker, Mike Mearls, Rob Heinsoo and Robert J. Schwalb. p. 19

A not-quite-ready for publication version of the barbarian. Includes a full set of evocations and the frenzied berserker paragon path.

Campaign Items

By Stephen Schubert. p. 36

Advice on utilizing artifacts, with some examples based on items from the Dungeon Master's Guide.

Fight! Battles in the Gladiatorial Arena

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 42

How to handle a campaign revolving around gladiatorial duels, from arena fights, to arena-related adventures, the battlefield, dealing with crowds, and some example opposition. Includes a number of monsters, skill challenges, and traps.

We Who Are About to Die... D&D Gladiators

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 56

Character concepts based on the gladiator, 54 new feats, the arena champion paragon path, and 9 new magic items.

RPGA Report: Dungeon Mastering Your Living Forgotten Realms Game

By Chris Tulach. p. 97

How to game in the Living Forgotten Realms.
