Dragon issue 263, It's Official -- Countdown to 3rd EDITION!, was released in September of 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Heaven's Trump: Celestial Messengers & Divine Intervention

By James Wyatt. p. 54-59

Advice on including agents of the gods in a campaign. Includes stats of a hollyphant and a 5th-level ranger aasimar.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Blessings in Disguise

By Lloyd Brown III, illustrated by Bob Klasnich. p. 76–80

Not every cursed magical item is useless. Some are blessings in disguise.

PC Portraits: Priests & Druids

By Tony Dykstra. p. 81

14 character portraits.

Role Models: Creating Terrain

By JD Wiker and Jim Bishop. p. 90

How to make terrain for your miniatures to play in. Hills, platforms, advanced bases, trees, cover, narrow crossings, and painting advice.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 108

New products for September, October and November.

  • ''Drizzt Do'Urden's Guide to the Underdark' sourcebook.
  • Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas software.
  • The Spine of the World novel.
  • The Temptation of Elminster novel.
  • Under Fallen Stars novel.
  • Wyrmskull Throne module.

ProFiles: Bill Slavicsek

By Steve Kenson. p. 112

Writer, game designer and Director of Roleplaying Game Design for Wizards of the Coast Bill Slavicsek has exciting plans for the future.
