Dragon issue 154, War travels on rapid wings, was released in February of 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

By Eric Oppen. p. 16

Paladins are made, not born.

By Jim Bambra. p. 59

Spelljammer: AD&D Adventures in Space.

By Uncredited. p. 98

New products for January and February.

  • Spelljammer: AD&D Adventures in Space
  • The Halfling's Gem novel.
