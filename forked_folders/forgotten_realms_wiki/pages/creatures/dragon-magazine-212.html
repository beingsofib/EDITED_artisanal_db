Dragon issue 212, A Holiday Gift of Advice for GMs, was released in December of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Reruns: Adventures that Improve with Age

By Thomas M. Reid. p. 18

Running your favorite modules--again and again and again.

Consider the Consequences

By Renee Stern. p. 24

What happens after the characters leave the dungeon.

Tricks of the Trade

By George T. Young. p. 30

Add these storytelling tricks to your GMing repetoire.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 120

New products for December and January.

  • Elminster: The Making of a Mage novel.
  • Marco Volo: Arrival sourcebook.
  • Realms of Infamy novel.
