Dragon issue 64 was released in August of 1982. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Featured Creatures

By Gary Gygax. p. 10–11

Planetars and solars.

Greyhawk's World: Redefining deities of the Flanaess

By Gary Gygax. p. 13

Introduces Raxivort, god of xvarts.

The Assassins' Run

By Ed Greenwood. p. 30–36

An adventure under Waterdeep.
