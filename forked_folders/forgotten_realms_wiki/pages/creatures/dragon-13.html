Dragon+ 13

Dragon+ issue 13 was released on April 28, 2017. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Welcome

By Bart Carroll. pp. 3

A word from editor Bart Carroll, followed by a piece by Volothamp Geddarm on Durnan and the Yawning Portal inn.

Fiction: The Sunless Citadel

By Bart Carroll. pp. 6

A short fiction piece related to Tales from the Yawning Portal.

Fiction: Forge of Fury

By Adam Lee. pp. 8

A short fiction piece related to Tales from the Yawning Portal.

Fiction: The Hidden Shrine of Tamoachan

By Adam Lee. pp. 10

A short fiction piece related to Tales from the Yawning Portal.

Fiction: White Plume Mountain

By Bart Carroll. pp. 12

A short fiction piece related to Tales from the Yawning Portal.

Fiction: Dead in Thay

By Scott Fitzgerald Gray. pp. 14

A short fiction piece related to the Dead in Thay adventure.

Fiction: Against The Giants

By Bart Carroll. pp. 16

A short fiction piece related to Tales from the Yawning Portal.

Fiction: Tombs of Horrors

By Bart Carroll. pp. 18

A short fiction piece related to Tales from the Yawning Portal.

Tales from Cryptic Developers

By John Houlihan. pp. 24

Several developers share their Dungeons & Dragons anecdotes, interspersed with art related to the The Cloaked Ascendancy module for the Neverwinter MMORPG.
