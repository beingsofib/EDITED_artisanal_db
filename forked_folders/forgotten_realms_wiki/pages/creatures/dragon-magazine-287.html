Dragon issue 287, Planes, was released in September of 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

When Celestials Attack

By Jeff Grubb. p. 32

How to use celestials as antagonists in D&D campaigns.

Vs.: Celestials

Chris Thomasson. p. 41

Weapons, spells, and tactics for fighting celestials.

Expand Your Mind

By Bruce R. Cordell. p. 54

New psionic feats.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: Shadowdark House

By Ed Greenwood. p. 70–73.

Shadowdark House, a building in Western Daerlun.

Faiths of Faerûn: Dreadmaster

By Eric Haddock and Julia Martin. p. 82–83

Dreadmaster, a prestige class for Bane's clerics.

Class Acts: Fiend Slayer

By Monte Cook. p. 84

A demon-fighting prestige class.

DM's Toolbox: 7 Steps to Creating Cunning Foes

By Johnn Four. p. 110

How to create a smart adversary.
