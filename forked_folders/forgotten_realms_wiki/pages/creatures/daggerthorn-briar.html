Daggerthorn briar

Plant
nameDaggerthorn briar
locationFeywild
usageProtective hazard

Daggerthorn briar was a mundane species of thorny plant that was hard to maneuver through and colorfully described by some as being "bloodthirsty". Because of its hazardous nature, some cruel nobles would plant daggerthorn around their villa grounds as protection.

Habitats

This species of plant grew in deep woods and parts of the Feywild,

History

Following the Spellplague, the inhabitants of the city of New Sharandar grew daggerthorn briars on their defensive wall, the Living Abatis.
