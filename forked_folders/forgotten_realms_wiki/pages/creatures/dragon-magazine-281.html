Dragon issue 281, Psionics, was released in March of 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: James Wyatt

By Michael G. Ryan. p. 12

Keeping the Faith: A profile of writer and game designer James Wyatt.

Up on a Soapbox: What the Heck is a Roleplaying Game?!, Part 3

By Gary Gygax. p. 15

Economics, exploration, intrigue, and politics as parts of RPGs.

Coming Soon

By Uncredited. p. 16

  • The Floodgate novel.
  • The Summoning novel.

Countdown to the Forgotten Realms: Organizations of Faerun

Uncredited. p. 18

More info on the upcoming 3rd edition Forgotten Realms.

Calm amid the Storm

By Bruce R. Cordell, illustrated by Carlo Arellano. p. 32–37

About Githzerai monks, a supplement to the Psionics Handbook.

By Any Other Name: Races of the Underdark

By Owen K.C. Stephens. p. 46–49

Names for Derro, Duergar, Illithid, Kuo-Toa, and Svirfneblin.

Instant Dungeon Tiles

By Ron Shirtz. p. 50

A sheet of wilderness and dungeon tiles for use with D&D.

Logjam Busters

By Robin D. Laws, illustrated by Jeff Laubenstein. p. 57–61

Helping the GM to keep games on track.

Vs.: Psions & Psychic Warriors

By David Noonan p. 74–75

How to fight psionicists.

The New Adventures of Volo: Lost Treasures of Cormyr, Part 4

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Ron Spencer. p. 76–78

An overview of many Cormyrian treasures.

Rogues Gallery: Heroes of the Summoning

By Troy Denning, illustrated by Glenn Harrington. p. 79–83

Characters from Denning's novel, The Summoning.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Imaskarcana: Faces of Magic

By Robert Sullivan, illustrated by Arnie Swekel. p. 86–89

Magical masks of the Realms.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Subterranean Scares

By Joseph R. Terrazzino, illustrated by Brian Snoddy. p. 90–95

Creatures of the Underdark. Deepling • Genocid • Inliving spawn • Jawg • Minwhelgo • Verx

PC Portraits: Psionicists

By Vinod Rams. p. 110

14 psionicist sketches.
