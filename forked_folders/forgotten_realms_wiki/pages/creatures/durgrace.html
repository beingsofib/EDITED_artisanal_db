Durgrace was a rare species of fish that inhabited the Lake Glorfindar in the Starmetal Hills and remained active through the winter months under the lake's ice.

Description

This fish was considered to be a lucky catch due to its growth. Durgrace could grow to a massive size, a lake monster and a fisher's dream.

Ecology

They were edible and were a part of a winter's hero's feasts served during the annual Simril celebration in Twilight Tor. The feast also consisted of cooked brown bream, blackjaw, and candlefish.
