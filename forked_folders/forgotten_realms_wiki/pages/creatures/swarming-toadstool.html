Swarming toadstool

nameSwarming Toadstool
typeMushroom
locationThe Underdark

Swarming toadstool was a parasitic species of fungus that could be found almost anywhere, clustering bigger species of mushrooms.

Description

Swarming toadstools appeared to be a cluster of smaller mushroom heads sprouting from a single stem of a biter mushroom. These fungi were of sickly green and yellowish color.

Found in many underground locations and the Underdark, these mushrooms were valued around 20 gold pieces per cluster.
