Dragon chicken

Creature
nameDragon Chicken
locationNeverwinter, Sword Coast North

A dragon chicken was a strange creature that resembled an amalgam of a dragon and a chicken, both majestic and terrifying, while extremely prone to chaos.

Description

Dragon chickens were large, bipedal avian creatures covered in feathers and scales. They had large, leathery tails and stubby, scaled wings. They could not fly but possessed powerful legs. Their diet was a mystery, but it was recommended for their owners to keep their dragon chickens away from bakeries.

Abilities

Unlike dragons, dragon chickens could produce small fiery nuggets that exploded, dealing damage to their foes and knocking them prone. Creatures affected by these explosions also temporarily suffered from a weakened state.

History

It was unclear who or how these creatures were hatched or where they came from. In the late 15 century DR, trained dragon chicken mounts were known to be ridden by some of the adventurers from Neverwinter, who participated in the festivities celebrating the Sunite Summer Festivals.
