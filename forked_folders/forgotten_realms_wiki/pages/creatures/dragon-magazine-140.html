Dragon issue 140, Healers and Holy Warriors, was released in December of 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 12

New products for November and December.

  • Azure Bonds novel.
  • Dreams of the Red Wizards sourcebook.
  • Kara-Tur: The Eastern Realms sourcebook.

Fantasy Clerics and Clerical Fallacies

By Robert Stockdale. p. 34

A focus on clerical healing.

Roll On!

By Len Carpenter. p. 44

Expanding Method V of character generation (from Unearthed Arcana) to multi-class and Oriental Adventures characters.

An Assortment of Knives

By Edwin C. Mason. p. 54

The Dragon's Bestiary: Creatures of light and darkness

By Ed Greenwood. p. 56

New monsters from the Forgotten Realms setting.

  • Glyptar
  • Magebane
  • Quezzar
  • Scythetail
  • Xantravar

Role-playing Reviews

By Jim Bambra & Ken Rolston. p. 82–89

  • Waterdeep and the North
  • Moonshae
  • Empires of the Sands
