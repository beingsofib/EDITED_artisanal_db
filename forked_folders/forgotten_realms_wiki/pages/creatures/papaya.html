Papaya

Tree

Papaya was a fruit cultivated as food. One might expect to find it served either as a side dish or as a tea at inns in tropical regions.

Availability

Places in the Realms where papaya were known to be cultivated included:

  • In the Chultan Peninsula, papaya were grown in orchards around Guthland and in Chult by the Tabaxi tribe in agricultural groves near Mezro.
  • In the land of Luiren, papaya were particularly known to grow in the tropical fruit plantations that stood between the cities of Beluir and Chethel.
  • On the continent of Kara-Tur, papaya could be found on the Malatran Plateau and in remote parts of Shou Lung, where fresh papaya was eaten as part of feasts. The Laska of Kilap Valley would give gifts of papaya as a peace offering.
