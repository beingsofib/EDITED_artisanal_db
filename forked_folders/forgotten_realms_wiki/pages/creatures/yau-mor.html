Yau-mor were lesser spirits found in Kara-Tur.

Description

Yau-mor appeared as freakish monstrosities. They had the torso of a human male and the head and legs of a small dragon. Their heads sported multiple eyes, ranging from a single eye to four. Their skin was a sickly shade of green.

Personality

Yau-mor relished combat and destruction, so much so that it often distracted them from their duties. They obeyed orders well enough but were easily manipulated by more intelligent beings.

Combat

Yau-mor despised any spellcaster and sought to attack them whenever possible. Due to their low intelligence, a yau-mor was only able to detect a spellcaster once a spell was cast. Once they choose a target, they ignored all other threats until that specific target was eliminated.

Abilities

The yau-mor's signature attack was being able to shout a stream of fire from its mouth. It caused considerable damage and ignited any combustible material. A yau-mor used this ability sparingly as it caused them great pain.

Society

Yau-mor were servants of the Celestial Bureaucracy, performing tasks for the evil-aligned members of the Bureaucracy. When encountered in Kara-Tur, a yau-mor was usually in the midst of a mission for the Celestial Bureaucracy. They were tasked with missions that were either tiresome and dangerous for a more competent being to undertake.
