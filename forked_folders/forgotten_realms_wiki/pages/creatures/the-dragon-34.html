The Dragon issue 34, special Divine Right issue, was released in February of 1980. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

For Your Minarian Merriment, Nine Ways to Change the Game

By Ed Greenwood. p. 11

In Defense (Once Again) of the "Poor" Magic-User

By Michael Dodge. p. 17

The author defends the 'Vancian' magic system in AD&D.

Leomund's Tiny Hut: The Segment of Action System Takes AD&D Melee One Step Further.

By Lenard Lakofka. p. 34-40

An alternate system for combat in AD&D.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Getting Into the Flow of Magic Fountains

By Tim Lasko. p. 41–43

Magic fountains and their effects.
