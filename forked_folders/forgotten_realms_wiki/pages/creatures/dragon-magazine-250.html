Dragon issue 250, The Deep, was released in October of 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Heroes of the Sea

By James Wyatt, illustrated by Bob Klasnich. pp. 26-35

This article gives information for player character races from the depths of the sea in emulation of the The Complete Book of Humanoids.

Races

Crabmen • Koalinth • Locathah • Malenti • Merfolk • Pahari • Selkies • Vodyanoi • Liminals

Warships of the Sea

By Keith Francis Strohm, illustrated by Roger Raupp, David Kooharian, and Diesel. pp. 44-52

This article expands upon Of Ships and the Sea with information about more modern sailing ships, rules for cannons and a glossary of nautical terms.

Ships

Brigantine • Carrack • Cutter • Fluyt • Frigate • Galleass • Galleon • Lugger • Pinnace • Sloop

Sunken Fortunes

By Steve Berman, illustrated by L. A. Williams. pp. 64-68

All about looting sunken ships.

Spells

Burnishing hand • Waterlight • Wreck compass • Thale passage • Fresh surroundings

Wyrms of the North: Nymmurh, The Wyrm Who Watches

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Storn Cook. pp. 80-84

This article describes the ancient bronze dragon Nymmurh, the mysterious Wyrm Who Watches, who had particular ties to a noble Waterdhavian family.

Spell

Jumpgout

The Dragon's Bestiary: Aquatic Creatures

By Johnathan Richards, illustrated by Stephen Schwartz. p. 88–90

A small menagerie of new aquatic creatures.

  • Black slime
  • Giant clownfish
  • Giant diving beetle
  • Sea cow

Arcane Lore: Secrets of the Arch-Geomancer

By Paul Fraser, illustrated by Steven Schwartz. pp. 92-94

This article reveals magical secrets of the geomancer Tisan Balshareska and her spellbook, the Kitab al-Asfr, from Al-Qadim's Ruined Kingdoms region.

Spells

Dusting the rock • Cone of earth • Entombment

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Magic with Class

By Lloyd Brown III, illustrated by Jim Crabtree. pp. 96–99

Magic items for specific classes.

ProFiles: Tracy Hickman

By Allen Varney. p. 120

A profile of author Tracy Hickman and his projects.
