Dragon issue 230, The Might of Dragons, was released in June of 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Dragonslayers

By Wolfgang Baur. p. 19

How to kill dragons.

Wyrms of the North: Arauthator

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Storn Cook. p. 37-40

Arauthator, "Old White Death", who lives on the icy sea several hundred miles north of Mithral Hall and Icewind Dale. Both large and rather clever, at least for a white dragon.
