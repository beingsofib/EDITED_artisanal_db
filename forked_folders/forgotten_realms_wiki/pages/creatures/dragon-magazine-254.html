Dragon issue 254, Giant Kin, was released in December of 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Wyrm's Turn: The Pitfalls of Roleplaying

By Dave Gross. p. 4

Low Wisdom and the perils of pits.

PC Portraits: Giant Slayers

By David Day. p. 28

14 potential PC pix.

The Bigger They Are...

By Skip Williams. p. 30

Giant NPCs.

...The Harder They Fall

By Paul F. Culotta, illustrated by Pete Venters, Cartography by Delfino. p. 36–46

Combat tactics used for and against giants.

Wyrms of the North: Saryndalaghlothtor, Lady Gemcloak

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Storn Cook. p. 84–88

Saryndalaghlothtor, the crystal dragon who wards the city of Mirabar.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Giants from the Grave

By Gregory W. Detwiler, illustrated by Stephen Schwartz. p. 90–96

Undead giants.

  • Barrowe
  • Cairn
  • Frostmourn
  • Firegaunt
  • Spectral cloud
  • Temperament

Rogues Gallery: Greendale MeadowField, a Lord of Ravens Bluff

By Jeff Quick and Robert Suriano, illustrated by David Kooharian. p. 98–99

Greendale MeadowField, half-elf bard and merchant of Ravens Bluff.

The Ecology of the Cyclopskin

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Matthew Mitchell. p. 102-108

A scholarly treatise on the Cyclopskin.

ProFiles: Jeff Easley

By Allen Varney. p. 120

TSR's senior artist Jeff Easley has painted the cover of all the AD&D core rulebooks, and he still hasn't tired of dragons. "There's nothing else I'd rather paint!"
