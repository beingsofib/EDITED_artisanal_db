Dragon issue 238, Humor & Villains, was released in August of 1997. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bazaar of the Bizarre: The Rewards of Villainy

By Wolfgang Baur, illustrated by Bob Klasnich. p. 28–32

Magic items for your bad guys.

Gangsters of the Underdark

By Keith Francis Strohm, illustrated by the Brothers Filbach. p. 34-40

Satirical Underdark personages.

The Return of the Wizards Three

By Ed Greenwood. p. 42–49

New spells, once more from three worlds.

Wyrms of the North: Felgolos, the Flying Misfortune

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Storn Cook. p. 51–54

Another new dragon for the Realms. Felgolos, a juvenile Bronze dragon known for crashing into things.

Nothing but Trouble

By Ed Greenwood. p. 63

The notorious Mirt the Moneylender finds more than he bargained for.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Spawn of the Sewers

By John Baichtal, illustrated by Tom Baxa. p. 68–73

Right under your feet.

  • Albino crocodile
  • Lich's blood
  • Necromantic sludge
  • Plague moth
  • Water cat

The Dragon's Bestiary: The Other Mummies

By Brian Walton and Richard Pengelly , illustrated by Tom Baxa. p. 84–87

Hard-hearted killers from bogs and glaciers.

  • Bog mummy
  • Call blizzard (spell)
  • Ice mummy
  • Ice shatter (spell)
  • Slippery slope (spell)

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 116

New products for July and August.

  • Finder's Bane novel.
  • Lands of Intrigue boxed set.
  • Passage to Dawn novel.
  • The Council of Blades novel.
