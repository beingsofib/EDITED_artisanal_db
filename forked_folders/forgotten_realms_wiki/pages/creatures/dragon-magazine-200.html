Dragon issue 200, Special Collectors Issue, was released in December of 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Magic from the Gods

By Michael John Wybo II. Artwork by Mark Nelson. pp. 14-18

Wizard kits for an campaign, who use magic given by the gods. The baule diviner can also appear in the Al-Qadim campaign setting.

The Wizards Three: Once more the three

By Ed Greenwood. Artwork by Dan Burr. pp. 20-25

The archmages Elminster, Mordenkainen of Oerth and Dalamar of Krynn meet once more in Ed Greenwood's house to exchange spells, this time surprised by an appearance of the Simbul.

Spells

Shadow bolt • Slowspell • Acid bolts • Mordenkainen's involuntary wizardry • Bonebind • Bloodstars • Lightning storm • Alamanther's return • Tempestcone

Familiar Faces

By David Howery. Artwork by Mark Nelson. pp. 30-33

This article expands upon the find familiar spell for the 2nd edition by providing new ideas for familiars for different kinds of wizards.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Fourteen Keys to Adventure

By Johnathan M. Richards. pp. 34–36

Fourteen magical devices to unlock a campaign's mysteries.

  • Key of inquiry
  • Map key
  • Courier keys
  • Keys of reunion
  • Saddle key
  • Shapeshifter key
  • Grave key
  • Key of silence
  • Spider key
  • Key of storage
  • Rogue’s key
  • Key of translation
  • Dowsing key
  • Wizard’s key

The Whistling Skeleton

By Ed Greenwood. Artwork by P. L. Wolf. pp. 43-46

A short adventure scenario in the northern Heartlands.

Characters

Aldegund • Ratharr • Belaerzyn • Yathruin • Rilithinn • Amaristee

Mentioned only: Sumbral the Mystical

Spell

Spelltouch

The Even More Complete Psionicist

By Jon Winter. Artwork by Karen L. Wann. pp. 97-101

Seven psionicist kits for 2nd edition.

Kits

Mountebank • Crystallite • Dowser • Empath • Manipulator • Psychic surgeon • Spiritualist

Non-weapon proficiencies

Body language • Camouflage • Pharmacy • Seance • Sleight of hand • Water divining

Sage Advice

By Skip Williams. pp. 104-106

Novel Ideas: A Brief History of the TSR Book Department

By Eric Severson and Will Larson. p. 111

Elminster's Notebook: Lord Kuldak Maurancz

By Ed Greenwood. Artwork by Gary M. Williams. pp. 144-145, 166

Elminster's recounts his visit of Kuldak Maurancz, Lord of Cragmyr near Beregost in his - according to rumor haunted - keep.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 168

New products for December.

  • Player's Guide to the Forgotten Realms Campaign sourcebook.
  • Volo's Guide to the North sourcebook.
