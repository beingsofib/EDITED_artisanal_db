Gingu

nameGingu

Gingu was a fruit-bearing species of tree native to the Malatran Plateau in Kara-Tur.

Habitats

Gingu fruits grew in abundance surrounding a scenic waterfall in the jungles not far from the saru village that fell victim to a strange wasting curse in the Year of Discovery. Gingu grew around other trees that bore edible fruit, such as coconut and banana palms.
