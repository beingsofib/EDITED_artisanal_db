Dragon issue 55 was released in November of 1981. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Dinosaurs: New Theories for Old Monsters

By Lawrence Schick. p. 12-16, 72-73

Revising the dinosaur stats in view of new scientific information.

The Many Ways of Getting Away

By Pat Reinken. p. 50

Methods and magic to keep your character out of the crypt.

The Dragon's Bestiary

By Craig Stenseth, Ed Greenwood, Erol Otus and Jeff Brandt. p. 59–62

All about the Devil Spider, Surchur, Dyll, and Poltergeist for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.
