Dragon issue 402 was released in PDF format only in August of 2011. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Rise of Bardic Beauty

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Eric Belisle.

A Spin a Yarn tale from Gen Con 2010.

Character Theme: Neverwinter Redeemers

By Erik Scott de Bie and Ari Marmell.

Sons of Alagondar are born on the streets of Neverwinter and, like the city itself, refuse to die without a fight.

History Check: Kas and Vecna

By Ken Hart.

A brief overview of the feud between Vecna and Kas.
