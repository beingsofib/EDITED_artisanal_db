Sparrow

Creature
nameSparrow

A sparrow was a small bird found in Faerûn. Ilmater, the deity of suffering and martyrdom, could cause the appearance of sparrows to indicate his favor or encourage his faithful.

Habitats

Beyond Faerûn sparrows could be found in the land of Kara-tur.
