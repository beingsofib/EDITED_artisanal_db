Flying squirrels were a type of squirrel with a furry membrane between their fore and rear legs that allowed them to glide rather than fly.

Behavior

Cowardly little critters, they only attacked when they had overwhelming numbers and always by attempted surprise. They would glide down from the tree-trunks and land on the heads of their prey.

Ecology

They built their lairs amongst the treetops, and would squirrel away small shiny objects be they gems, jewelry and the like.

Habitats

They were noted to live throughout the great Cormanthor forest, and the lands of Cormyr, Sembia, and the Dalelands.
