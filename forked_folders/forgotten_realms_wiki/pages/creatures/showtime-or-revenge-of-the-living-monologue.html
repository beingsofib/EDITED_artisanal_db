Showtime! Or, Revenge of the Living Monologue

The Spirit of Myrrth - Showtime! or Revenge of the Living Monologue was the seventh issue of the Advanced Dungeons &amp; Dragons comics series set in the Forgotten Realms and published by DC Comics. It was written by Dan Mishkin, drawn by Jan Duursema, and edited by Barbara Kesel. It is the third installment in the "The Spirit of Myrrth" story arc.
