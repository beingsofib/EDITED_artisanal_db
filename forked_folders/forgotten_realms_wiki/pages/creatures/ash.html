Ash was a type of tree found in Faerun.

Varieties

  • Black ash grew in the Trollbark Forest.
  • Water ash grew in the Vast Swamp of Cormyr.
  • White ash grew in the midwood part of the forest of Cormanthor.

Heraldry

A tiny brilliant white star balanced on an open lush green ash leaf was an old symbol of the goddess Mielikki.

Usages

  • The sap of an ash tree was one of the ingredients of the steel dragon Zundaerazylym's magical mixture longbite.
  • The Spear of White Ash was an enchanted Malarite spear used in High Huntss. The aptly named polearm was made out of white ash wood.
  • Some used the bark and leaves of this tree to treat fevers, inflammation, or to make laxatives.
