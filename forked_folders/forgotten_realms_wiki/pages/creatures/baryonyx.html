Baryonyx

Baryonyx were a predatory species of dinosaur that inhabited the jungles of Chult.

Description

These creatures were noted as having heads resembling those of crocodiles.

Combat

Baryonyx typically hunted by standing perfectly still, knee-deep in water while shielded by various jungle plants, and waiting until prey passed them by.
