Dragon issue 327 was released in January of 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Silverfish

By Richard Lee Byers. p. 38-46,48

A mystery featuring a character from The Rite novel of the The Year of Rogue Dragons series.

The Ecology of the Grimlock

By Mark A. Hart. p. 58-61

A scholarly treatise on the Grimlock.

Winning Races: Diaboli

By Mike McArtor. p. 62

A fiendish race for your players' use.

Animal Allies

By F. Wesley Schneider. p. 87

Three new animal companions for druids.
