Dragon issue 361 was released in November of 2007 as a HTML edition. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

A Fractured Family: Elvish Strife, Separation, and Rebirth

By James Wyatt. p. 8–12

Once they were a single race, and they would have stayed that way but for the help of Lolth.

Shadow of Shothragot: The Price of Survival

By Robert J. Schwalb, illustrated by Efrem Palacios. p. 13–17

An addition to Elder Evils and Exemplars of Evil, featuring a herald of Tharizdun.

The Allure of Evil: Dark Churches

By Jason H. Haley, illustrated by Ralph Horsley. p. 18–28

The evil deities of D&D have churches all their own, but not all their followers are villains. These affiliations give non-evil PCs all the information they need to join one of these dark churches.

Infernal Aristocracy: The Dukes of Hell, Part II

By Robert J. Schwalb, illustrated by Marc Sasso. p. 29–39

"You thought we were done? It's impossible to count the number of diabolical, unique personalities scheming in Hell. We've got five more here, ready to terrorize your D&D game." Agares and his companion Harbinger, Tartach, Lilith, Hutijin, and Adramalech.

Ecology of the Fire Archon

By Matthew Sernett, illustrated by Randy Gallegos. p. 40–48

Everything you never wanted to know about the elemental archons of fire.

Realmslore - Ironfang Keep

By Brian James, illustrated by Diem. p. 49–51

"Long reputed to be the home of dark power, this shadowy basalt fortress is held responsible for centuries of abductions and strange abominations unleashed to roam the countryside..."
