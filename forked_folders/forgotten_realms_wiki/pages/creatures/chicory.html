Chicory was a species of plant found in Faerûn.

Availability

  • Chicory could be found for sale in the markets of Waterdeep.

Notable Users

In the kitchen of Blackstaff Tower, Khelben Arunsun liked to served steaming cups of roasted chicory.

Trivia

  • According to some, the process to create a staff of command involved boiling the wood of an ash tree in an ointment made of chicory and the hide of a spirit naga.
  • In the field of candle magic, chicory root was a necessary ingredient to create candles that, once lit, gave off an effect similar to the spells blink, see invisibility, or shield.
