The Dragon issue 23 was released in March 1979. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Mind Wrestling

By Jefferson Swycaffer. p. 10

A variation of Psionic Combat for Dungeons & Dragons.

It Weighs WHAT?!!!

By Mike Mornard. p. 14

Some Notes on Armor and Weapons encumbrance for Fantasy Gamers.

From The Sorcerer's Scroll

By Gary Gygax. p. 15

Random Generation of Creatures from the Lower Planes; an advance look at the tables to determine inhabitants of the Abyss, Tarterus, Hades, Gehenna, and Hell that later appeared in the Dungeon Masters Guide.
