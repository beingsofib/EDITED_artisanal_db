Dragon issue 375 was released in PDF format only in May of 2009. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Gontal: Dominions of Nehu

By Bruce R. Cordell, Illustrations by Chuck Lukacs, Cartogragphy by Sean Macdonald. p. 77–84

This article describes the Dominions of Nehu, the core ruins of an ancient Primordial fortress located in Gontal.

D&D Alumni: Return of Demogorgon

By Bart Carroll & Steve Winter. p. 122–124

A short history of Demogorgon.
