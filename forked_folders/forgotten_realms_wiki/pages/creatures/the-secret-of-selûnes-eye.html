The Secret of Selûne's Eye

"The Secret of Selûne's Eye" is the third issue of the Advanced Dungeons &amp; Dragons comics series set in the Forgotten Realms and published by . It was written by Michael Fleisher and Jan Duursema, drawn by Duursema, and edited by Barbara Kesel. It continued the story begun in "The Gathering" and "The Bounty Seekers Of Manshaka".
