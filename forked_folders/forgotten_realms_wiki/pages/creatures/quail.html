Quail

Creature
nameQuail
subracesSand hen

Quails were a species of bird known in Faerûn.

Species

Sand hen

The sand hen was a rare type of quail that could be found in the rimwood area of the Cormanthor forest, east of the River Lis. It was notable for the tasty stock that could be produced from its carcass.

Ground quail

The commonly hunted and cooked species of quail found in Evermoors. They were used to prepare a satisfyingly delicious dish called bustards.

Ecology

Habitat

Quail were known to inhabit the North.

Usages

In the city of Ravens Bluff, quails were served at the Raven's Glory, Phoaraudo's, and Volodar's Stardust Inn. Also in that city, the establishments Sword Tables Dining Lounge and Nimber's Skewer Shop sold quail meat on skewers.
