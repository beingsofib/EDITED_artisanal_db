Cejor

Cejor was probably a metallic dragon who was caught by the silver dragon Azhaq in the act of brewing some useless potion as part of her experiments in mitigating the Rage of Dragons. She gave up Karasendrieth's name once questioned on her experiments, which led to Lareth ordering she be caught, per Llimark's reckoning, on Hammer 6 in .
