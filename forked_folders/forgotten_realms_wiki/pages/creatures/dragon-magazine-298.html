Dragon issue 298, Dark Elves, was released in August of 2002. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Previews

By Uncredited. p. 16

  • City of the Spider Queen module.
  • Hand of Fire novel.

Flesh for Lolth - The Secret Life of Dark Elves

By Robin D. Laws, illustrated by Marc Sasso. p. 24–32

An article on the life of the Drow and living in the Underdark.

Characters

Vazhan-do

The Dragon's Bestiary: The Punishments of Lolth

By Eric Cagle, illustrated by Anthony Waters. p. 34–41

When drow are punished, they are sometimes turned into a drider.

Crusades of the Ashen Compact vs. Drow

By Jim Bishop. p. 52

Information about a secret cabal of subterranean races that recruit surface dwellers to fight the drow. Includes several new Prestige Classes, spells, and feats.

Shades of Death

By Wade Nudson. p. 62

Necromancer class combinations. Includes several new Feats.

Preparing to Improvise

By Johnn Four. p. 94

Tips for GMs on how to improvise when running the game.
