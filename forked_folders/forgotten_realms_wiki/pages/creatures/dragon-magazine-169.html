Dragon issue 169, Heroes wanted–some risk involved!, was released in May of 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Editorial: The answers (sort of) at last!

By Roger E. Moore. p. 6-7

The editor gives the answers to an editorial test that was given last issue.

Creative Casting

By Joel E. Roosa. p. 10

New and interesting uses for low-level wizard spells.

Learn Something New Every Day

By Gordon R. Menzies. p. 20

New non-weapon proficiencies.

The Strategy of Tactics

By Jordan Clarke Hayes. p. 26

Melee tactics for warriors from both editions of AD&D.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 32

New products for May and June.

  • Horde Campaign sourcebook.
  • Nightmare Keep module.
  • Sojourn novel.

Role-playing Reviews

By Ken Rolston. p. 72–80

The Complete Wizard's Handbook.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: A dozen deadly daggers from the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 88–92

Twelve magical daggers.

  • Blade of banishing
  • Chill blade
  • Dagger of defiance
  • Dagger of doomwarding
  • Dragonfang
  • Fang dagger
  • Grimwald's dagger
  • Invisible blade
  • Jump dagger
  • Rust blade
  • Speaking dagger
  • Spider fang

Sage Advice

By Skip Williams. p.98-100
