Dragon issue 364 was released in June of 2008 as a PDF. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Demonomicon of Iggwilv: Yeenoghu

By Robert J. Schwalb, illustrated by Jason A. Engle and Brian Hagan. p. 5–16

Unleash Yeenoghu, the bloodthirsty Demon Prince of Gnolls—and his many loyal followers—on your unsuspecting PCs.

Ashen Covenant

By Ari Marmell, illustrated by Dave Allsop. p. 36–47

Devoted to the demon prince Orcus, the Ashen Covenant believes that victory for their dark lord will come only when he ascends...as the new god of death.

Creature Incarnations: Kobolds

By Mike Mearls, illustrated by Jim Nelson. p. 55

How to build a kobold lair and random crazy things for kobolds to do when they drop a PC.

Class Acts: Wizard

By Rodney Thompson, illustrated by Carl Frank. p. 68–69

Level 1 to 10 wizard powers with an illusory theme.

maze of mirrors

Design & Development: The Dungeon Master's Guide

By James Wyatt. p. 75

A chapter-by-chapter look at the new Dungeon Master's Guide.
