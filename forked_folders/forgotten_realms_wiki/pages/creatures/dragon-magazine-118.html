Dragon issue 118 was released in February of 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Fighting Circle

By Dan Salas. p. 8

Gladiatorial combat in the AD&D game.

On Target

By Leonard Carpenter. p. 34

Archery competitions for AD&D games.

Arrrgh!!!

By Christopher Wood. p. 38

A painful topic for AD&D game characters. (Pain thresholds and wounds.)

Sage Advice

By Mike Breault and Penny Petticord. p.58;69

Q&A about the Dungeoneer's Survival Guide.

Out of the Stone Age

By Jack R. Patterson. p.62

Neanderthal player characters in AD&D games

The Dragon's Bestiary

By Ed Greenwood, Jeff MacArthur. K.L. Campbell. p. 64

Spiders for AD&D.

  • Giant bolas spider
  • Opilionid
  • Phoenix spider
  • Polar spider
  • Spider cat
