A brontosaurus (), also known as Thunderbeast{{Cite book/Son of Thunder

|}}, was a massive quadruped dinosaur.

Description

Brontosauruses were so large that most predators did not dare attack them.

Behavior

These animals were not aggressive. They calmly lumbered through the jungles ignoring smaller creatures.

Combat

Brontosauruses only fought in self-defense. If necessary, they could stomp enemies and hit them with their enormous tails, which were powerful enough to even kill smaller predators.

Ecology

Brontosauruses were common to the jungles of Chult, near swamp areas and rivers.
