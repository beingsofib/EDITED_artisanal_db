Berrygobbler

Creature
nameBerrygobbler

A berrygobbler was a small, scurrying, -like rodent common in the Heartlands and other areas of Faerûn.

In warmer climates of Faerûn, such as Calimshan or Tashalar, there were black-furred and speedy berrygobblers called "slinks".
