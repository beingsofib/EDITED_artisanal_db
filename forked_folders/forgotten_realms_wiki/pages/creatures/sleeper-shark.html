Sleeper shark

Creature
nameSleeper Shark
movementSwimming

Sleepers sharks were a large species of shark found in the Sea of Moving Ice.

Description

These creatures were named for their sluggish movements, which were thought to be the result of poor eyesight caused by parasites.

Ecology

Sleeper sharks lived extraordinarily long lives.

Rumors & Legends

Though not proven, sleepers sharks were thought to grow slowly over the course of their lives and never stop growing.
