Dragon issue 286, Shannara, was released in August of 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Previews

By Uncredited. p. 22

  • Baldur's Gate II: Throne of Bhaal
  • Elminster in Hell
  • Magic of Faerûn sourcebook.
  • Sea of Swords
  • Temple Hill

Up on a Soapbox: The Final What the Heck is a Roleplaying Game?

By Gary Gygax. p. 26

Surveying the results about what makes up an RPG.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: Shattershree's Rest

By Ed Greenwood. p. 72–75

Shattershree's Rest, aka the Old Tomb, due west of Serpentsbridge.

Faiths of Faerûn: Nightcloak

By Eric Haddock and Julia Martin. p. 82–83

Introducing the favored priests of Shar, Nightcloak prestige class.

Vs.: Vampires

By Bruce R. Cordell. p. 84

Fighting vampires.
