Dragon issue 348, Horror, was released in October of 2006. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Core Beliefs: Vecna

By Samuel D. Weiss and Sean K. Reynolds. p. 18-33

All about Vecna, including clerics, temples, new spells, NPCs, and a secret history.

Bestowed Curses

By Jonathan Drain. p. 34-36

Curse variants.

Bloodlines

By Michael J. Montesano. p. 3947

Three variant vampires.

The Ecology of the Wight

By Graeme Davis. p. 60-65

A scholarly treatise on the Wight.
