Giant maggots were the larval stage for several giant insect species.

Description

Giant maggots looked like their smaller cousins. Some giant maggots had several appendages growing from their body, causing them to resemble a hideous caterpillar.

Abilities

Giant maggots were immune to all mind-affecting effects and spells.

Combat

Giant maggots avoided combat, only attacking a creature in self-defense. If a giant maggot was starving, they became predatory and attacked nearby creatures.

Ecology

Several giant insect species laid their eggs on or near the decaying corpse of a large creature or a large pile of dung, upon which the giant maggots fed. Shortly after, the giant maggot metamorphosed into an adult giant insect.
