Dragon issue 132, Humorous RPGs, was released in April of 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Resourceful Sorcery

By Michael DeWolfe. p. 24

How to play a sorcerer.

With All the Trappings

By Gregg Sharp. p. 28

How to create and use traps.

The Ecology of the Aurumvorax

By Mark Feil. p. 46

A scholarly treatise on the Aurumvorax.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 68

New products for February, March and April.

  • Empires of the Sands
  • The Crystal Shard
