Dragon issue 141, From nearly human to barely human, was released in January of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Dragon's Bestiary: Nonhuman creatures - with human form

By Gregg Sharp, Linda Hankins, Michael J. Szarmach, Ronald C. Morgan II, Scott Bennie, and Steve Sloane. p. 16–19

New humanoids.

  • Black troll
  • Caiveh
  • Cynamolgus
  • Diurge
  • Jor
  • Rock troll

Orcs Throw Spells, Too!

By Randal S. Doering. p. 22-33

Shamans and witch doctors must earn their magic - but the price is steep.

Boulder-Throwers and Humanoid Hordes

By Arthur Collins. p. 34

Battle tactics for giants and humanoids.

Hey, Wanna Be a Kobold?

By Joseph Clay. p. 38–43

Orcs, goblins, kobolds, and xvarts as player characters.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 62

New products for December and January.

  • Dreams of the Red Wizards
  • Lords of Darkness module.
  • Mad Monkey vs the Dragon Claw module.
