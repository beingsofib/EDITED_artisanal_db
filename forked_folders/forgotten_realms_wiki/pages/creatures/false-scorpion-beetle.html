False scorpion beetle

Creature
nameFalse Scorpion Beetle
locationShining South, South Faerûn

False scorpion beetles were a species of beetle found inhabiting Dambrath in the Shining South, where they were considered a delicacy.

Description

False scorpion beetles were big insects. Thy had glistening black backs and barbed tails, their needle-sharp stingers used for fighting off predators and pierced tongues and cheeks of those unwisely trying to eat the bugs from the wrong end.

Ecology

False scorpion beetles were farmed by the Crinti nobles of Dambrath to be used as food. The beetles were raised on a diet of honey and consumed alive, head-first.
