Dragon issue 295, Build Better Castles, was released in May of 2002. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Previews

By Uncredited. p. 16

  • Crown of Fire novel.
  • Faiths and Pantheons sourcebook.
  • Heirs of Prophecy novel.

Every Home a Castle

By Darrin Drader. p. 36

New rooms and castle layouts, including wall of force aquariums, ossuaries, and saunas with 4 sample edifices.

Mortar and Stone

By Mat Smith and Matthew Sernett. p. 52

Magic walls, moats, and repairing your fortress.

Build a Better Rogue Trap

By Penny Williams. p. 58

New traps.

The Way of the Fist

By Brent Phillips-Watts. p. 70

3 prestige classes: The primal rager, the fierce grappler, the brawler, and a few new feats.

Class Acts: The Master Siege Engineer

By Andy Collins. p. 76

The master siege engineer prestige class.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: The Bone Dance

By Ed Greenwood. p. 78-81

A haunted hill near Dagger Falls.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: And the Walls Came Tumbling Down...

By Eric Cagle. p. 82

New siege weapons and ammunition.
