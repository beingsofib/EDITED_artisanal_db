Dragon magazine 14 was released in May 1978. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Excerpt From an Interview With a Rust Monster.

By Michael McCrery. Artwork by David A. Trampier. p. 16

McCrery gives a wonderful story from a rust monster's point of view in this article.

Creatures

rust monster

Lycanthropy - The Progress of the Disease

By Gregory Rihn. p. 31

Rihn creates rules for adding lycanthropes into the adventuring groups with ways of controlling the disease. This article provides excellent tools for incorporating werebears into any 1st edition Dungeons & Dragons game.
