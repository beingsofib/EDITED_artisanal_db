Dragon issue 124 was released in August of 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Sailors on the Sea of Air

By Ed Greenwood. p. 18–20

The skyships of the Realms.

On a WIng and a Prayer

By L. Gregory Smith. p. 16

Gliders and gliding.

Flying the Friendly(?) Skies

By Thomas M. Kane. p. 26–32

Aerial adventuring.

The Wings of Eagles

By J.F. Keeping. p. 34–37

The Aarakocra as a PC and NPC race.

Kicks and Sticks

By Joseph R. Ravitts. p. 40

The Escrimador class for Oriental Adventures.

The Ecology of the Gelatinous Cube

By Ed Greenwood. p. 56–57

A scholarly treatise on the Gelatinous Cube.
