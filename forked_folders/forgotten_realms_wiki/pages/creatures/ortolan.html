Ortolan

Creature
nameOrtolan
locationFaerûn

Ortolans were a type of songbird.

Ecology

Ortolans were found in Faerûn.

Uses

They were considered a delicacy among the leShay of Sarifal, who ate them after they were imported, blinded, force fed, drowned in brandy, and then whole-roasted.
