Dragon issue 273, Swashbucklers, was released in July of 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Up on a Soapbox: D&D: The Next Generation

By Gary Gygax. pp. 22

Getting the next generation into the game.

Countdown to Third Edition: Sorcerers and Bards

By uncredited. pp. 24

About the final character classes.

40 Swashbuckling Adventures

By Malcolm Haymes and Michael T. Kuciak pp. 34–36

40 swashbuckling adventure ideas.

Swashbuckling Essentials

By Robin D. Laws, Illustrated by Terri Tiritilli. pp. 38–42

12 tips for the swashbuckling player.

Something Up Your Sleeve

By Gregory W. Detwiler, Illustrated by David Day. pp. 50–54

New equipment for AD&D thieves.

Shamur's Wager

By Richard Lee Byers, illustrated by Terese Nielsen. pp. 56–63

A story of Shamur Uskevren. Related to The Shattered Mask novel.
