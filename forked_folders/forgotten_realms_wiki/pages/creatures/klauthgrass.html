originMoonshae Isles

Klauthgrass was a rare herb used to make a drug that acted as a truth serum.

Description

It had a distinctive smell that could be detected when it was added to food or drink, although it was claimed not to affect the taste.

Ecology

Klauthgrass was only found in the Moonshae Isles.

Uses

In order to be used to compel truth-telling, Klauthgrass had to be harvested under a full moon and used to craft a poison.

History

As of the late 15 century DR, Klauthgrass was known on the Sword Coast as an effective but hard-to-get truth serum. Its use was favored by Jaheira of the Harpers.
