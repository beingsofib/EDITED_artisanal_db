Dragon+ 3

Dragon+ issue 3 was released on August 13, 2015. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Introducing Rage of Demons

By Christopher Perkins.

An introduction to the Rage of Demons storyline.

Ways to Play Rage of Demons

By Matt Chapman.

A short preview of three Rage of Demons products: Out of the Abyss, Sword Coast Legends, and Archmage.

Interview: R.A. Salvatore

By Matt Chapman.

An interview with R.A. Salvatore.

Unwrapping Sword Coast Legends

By Matt Chapman.

A more in-depth feature preview of Sword Coast Legends.

Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide

By Steve Kenson

A preview of the Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide, including an interview with designer Steve Kenson.

Community: Underdark Spell Components

By Matt Chapman.

A collection of alternate spell components used in the Underdark, selected based on reader suggestions and illustrated.
