Dragon+ 36

Dragon+ issue 36 was released on February 24, 2021. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Imagining The Ampersand: Clint Cearley

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

The illustrator talks menacing shadows and hidden words as he brings Candlekeep to life.

Candlekeep Mysteries: Introduction

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

Meet the anthology of adventure authors who have gathered to tell the latest fifth edition tales—many of them new alongside the existing team.

In the Works

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

A deeper look at several upcoming releases, and upcoming content for existing releases:

  • Van Richten's Guide to Ravenloft
  • Dungeons & Dragons: Dark Alliance
  • Neverwinter: Sharandar
  • Warriors of Waterdeep
  • Baldur's Gate III

Character Spotlight: NPCs

By Max Dunbar and Adam Lee.

Three intriguing NPCs ready to be dropped into any campaign.

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

Several maps for Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden, Ghosts of Saltmarsh and Waterdeep: Dragon Heist.
