Dragon Trail (Zhentil Keep)

The Dragon Trail was a small road that connected with the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Dragons Trail approached Zhentil Keep from the northwest, connecting with the Street of Dragons at the Dragon Gate.
