Lettuce was a leafy vegetable cultivated in the Realms.

Availability

Places in the Realms where lettuce was known to be cultivated included:

  • In Interior Faerûn, it could be found cultivated in the Dalelands, Sembia, and around Ghars in Cormyr. On the average Sembian farm, lettuce was often grown in the same farm plot as other vegetables.
  • The Shragga cultivar was grown in the Great Dale, where the citizens of Uthmere grew it and other vegetables and herbs on their balconies.

Beyond Toril

  • In Krynnspace, the temperate moon of Hiddukel was home to farmers that grew a variety of crops in incredible sizes, including heads of lettuce that measured across. They variously sold or traded these to visiting spelljamming merchants.

Cultivars

Marsh lettuce

A cultivar of lettuce presumably found in marshes.

Shragga

A leaf lettuce.

Notable Lettuce Products

  • Lettuce was often used in salads. For example, the Maid of the Moors restaurant in Mornbryn's Shield served a fresh garden salad of lettuce leaves, olives, parley, parsnip shavings, strong cheese and cottage cheese, for 1 cp. The Inn of the Dripping Dagger in Waterdeep offered a plate of cold meats with lettuce or endive for 4 cp.
  • Marsh lettuce or celery was an ingredient in roasted cockatrice.
