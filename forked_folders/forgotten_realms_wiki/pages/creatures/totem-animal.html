Totem animal

A totem animal was a spirit-like creature resembling an animal that was encountered by priests known as skinwalkers during their solitary meditations in the woods. These meditations, undertaken as part of their path to becoming skinwalkers, granted the enlightenment and wisdom necessary to assume the form of their totem animal. This magical power was known as skinwalking.

They could be sought out by others for different purposes. For example, they could be asked to show support or undertake a sort of patronage over an individual that tried to show their worthiness to stay on Evermeet.
