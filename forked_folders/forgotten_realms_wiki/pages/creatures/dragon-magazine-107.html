Dragon issue 107 was released in March of 1986. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Six Main Skills

By Jefferson P. Swycaffer. p. 16

What the six AD&D abilities mean in real-world terms.

Room for Improvement?

By Kim Mohan. p. 22

A complement to the previous article, here you will find rules for increasing a character's ability scores.

The Ecology of the Sea Lion

By Ed Greenwood. p. 34-36

A scholarly treatise on the Sea Lion.

For Sail: One New NPC

By Scott Bennie. p. 38

The mariner NPC class.

Economics Made Easy

By Ralph Marshall. p. 44

Economic theory as related to RPG campaigns.

When the Rations Run Out

By Paul Hancock. p. 60

Charcters don't live on hit points alone: Rules for starvation and dehydration.

TSR Profiles: Roger Raupp

By Uncredited. p. 62

About Art Director Roger Raupp.

TSR Profiles: Larry Elmore

By Uncredited. p. 63

About artist Larry Elmore.
