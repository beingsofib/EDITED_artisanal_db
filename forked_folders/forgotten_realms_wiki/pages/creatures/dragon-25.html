Dragon+ 25

Dragon+ issue 25 was released on April 26, 2019. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Previews: In The Works

By Matt Chapman. pp. 5

The Dragon+ team held council at the end of 2018, and decreed that each issue would continue to offer early looks at products in development. And so, let's see what's in the works...

  • Waterdeep: Dungeon of the Mad Mage Board Game: The newest entry into the Dungeons & Dragons Adventure System series sees players journeying though the Yawning Portal into Undermountain to take on the creatures and quests that lie within.
  • Neverwinter MMO: Undermountain: Undermountain is affectionately known as the "mega-dungeon" for a reason.

Adventurers League News

By Chris Lindsay. pp. 9

Discusses a new Adventurers League story arc titled Dreams of the Red Wizards and introduces the concept of seasonality.

  • DDAL-DRW01 Blood in the Water
