Dragon issue 257, Dark Ages, was released in March of 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Fighting with Flair: Beyond Hack & Slash Warriors

By Lance Hawvermale. p. 50

Make your warriors more than hackers.

101 Paladin Quests

By Johnathan M. Richards. p. 56

Plenty of adventure ideas to keep the party's paladin occupied.

Wyrms of the North: Valamaradace, the Dragon Queen

By Ed Greenwood. p. 76-80

Valamaradace, the Dragon Queen of the North.

The Ecology of...: Hammer & Anvil

By Paul Culotta. p. 88-94

The ecologies of the firenewt and giant strider.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 116

New products for March, April and May.

  • Evermeet: Island of Elves novel.
  • The Accursed Tower module.
  • The Dream Spheres novel.
  • The Glass Prison novel.

ProFiles: J. Robert King

By Steve Kenson. p. 120

A talk with novelist and game designer J. Robert King.
