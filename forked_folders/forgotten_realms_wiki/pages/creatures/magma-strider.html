Magma striders were one of the two more bestial species of magma beast.

Description

These creatures had a very insectoid appearance, sporting six long spindly legs.

Behavior

Magma striders were the most relentless of magma beasts, willing to pursue their prey for countless miles.

Abilities

Like all magma beasts, striders were immune to the effects of petrification, had some resistance to fire, and were particularly vulnerable to cold-based spells and abilities.

They uniquely radiated an aura of intense heat.

Combat

Magma striders fought their prey by charging and biting at them.

History

Following the Spellplague, many magma striders made their way through elemental vortices and planar rifts into volcanic regions of Toril's and the Underdark. magma striders could be found inhabiting Mount Hotenow.

Society

Languages

Like all magma beasts, striders were capable of speaking the Primordial language.

Relationships

Magma striders were occasionally bullied by magma brutes and magma hurlers into fighting alongside them. They were also often lured into serving fire archons, fire giants, salamanders, and even the occasional eye of flame.

Ecology

Diet

Magma striders could subsist on merely clay, ores, and even minor elementals. Though they preferred to hunt and feast upon organic creatures, savoring the taste of burned flesh. Whenever they killed a humanoid they would devour them whole, including their equipment.

Habitats

These creatures were native to the Elemental Chaos.
