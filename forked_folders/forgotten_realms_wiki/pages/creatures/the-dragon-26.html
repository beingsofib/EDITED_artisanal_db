The Dragon issue 26 was released in June of 1979. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

D&D Meets the Electronic Age

By Rick Krebs. p. 28

Talks about how computers are being used to help play RPGs by mapping, generating characters and so forth.

Hirelings Have Feelings Too

By Charles Sagui. p. 28

A discussion on hirelings: their motivations, potential problem areas, etc.

Blueprint for a Lich

By Len Lakofka. p. 38

Describes the process and material needed to become a lich.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Barghest

By Uncredited. p. 44

Stats for one of the most common denizens of Gehenna, the Barghest.
