Dragon issue 210, One strange wild dark long Halloween, was released in October of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Dead or Alive

By Michael Satran. p. 12

The economic, legal, and political aspects of resurrection magic.

Who's Afraid of the Big, Bad Ghost?

By Spike Y. Jones. p. 18–24

Advice on making players nervous in order to induce fear and terror in the game.

Too Evil to Die

By Tom Moldvay. p. 26

Basic undead covers variants for spectres, liches, and ghosts.

Primitive Weaponry

By Gregory Detwiler. p. 79

Primitive weapons translated to AD&D use.

Role-playing Reviews

By Rick Swan. p. 90-94

Player's Guide to the Forgotten Realms Campaign.

Elminster's Notebook: The Serpent Blade

By Ed Greenwood. p. 102

Weapons and rings used by the cults of Tiamat.
