Dragon issue 77 was released in September of 1983. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Tarot of Many Things

By Michael J. Lowrey. p. 6-12, 50-63

Using a Tarot deck in Dungeons & Dragons and other RPGs.

The Ecology of the Unicorn

By Roger E. Moore. p. 14-16

A scholarly treatise on the Unicorn.

Curses!

By Ed Greenwood. p. 18

20 good ideas for bad tidings in Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

Elemental gods

By Nonie Quinlan. p. 22-24

A four part approach to campaign deities based on the elements.
