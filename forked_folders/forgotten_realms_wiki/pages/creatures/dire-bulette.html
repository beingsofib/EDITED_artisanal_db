Dire bulettes were an exceptionally huge variety of bulette.

Combat

Dire bulettes were heavily armored and attacked with their sharp teeth. They preferred to strike at their prey by burrowing beneath their feet and erupting up from below, sending a spray of rock and dirt into the air and leaving the ground destroyed and treacherous. They could move at frightening speed while burrowing just below the surface.

Ecology

Like ordinary bulettes, dire bulettes generally made their lairs below ground and were often solitary creatures. They were said to sometimes cooperate with giants or nabassu.

Homelands

They could be found in the Highlands of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles. Local firbolg hunters could become celebrated for their skill if they successfully hunted dire bulettes.

They were said to be found in the Outer Planes within desert environments.
