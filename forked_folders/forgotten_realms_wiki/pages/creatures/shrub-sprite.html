Shrub sprite

Creature
nameShrub Sprite
movementFlying
locationUtter East

A shrub sprite was a tiny type of sprite known in the Utter East during the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

If caught, these faerie guides helped folk travel through brambles and thick undergrowth without hindrance or harm.
