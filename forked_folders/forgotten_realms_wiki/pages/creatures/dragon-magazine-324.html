Dragon issue 324, The Shadow over D&D, was released in October of 2004. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Exorcising Equipment

By Robert J. Hahn. p. 56

So you want to slay some vampires? Maybe toast some mummies? You're going to need some gear.

Chilled to the Bone

By Yury Pavlotsky, illustrated by Noi Sackda. p. 58–62

Icy enemies from arctic environs.

The Ecology of the Night hag

By F. Wesley Schneider, illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 66-69

A scholarly treatise on the Night hag.

Spellcraft: The Hidden Book

By Rich Burlew, illustrated by Mike May. p. 70–72

Discover new illusions—if you can.

Gaining Prestige: The Sworn Slayer

By Scott Fitzgerald Gray. p. 80

The Sworn Slayer: A new Prestige class dedicated to fighting a single foe.

Winning Races: Grippli

By Joshua Cole. p. 84

Ever wanted to play an anthropomorphic tree frog? Then read on...

Player Tips: Teamwork

By Tracy Taylor. p. 100

How to play a character who is a productive member of the team.

Adventurer Tricks: Magic Traps

By Joshua Cole. p. 101

Detecting and defeating traps with magic.
