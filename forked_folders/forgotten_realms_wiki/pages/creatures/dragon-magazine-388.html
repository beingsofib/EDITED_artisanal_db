Dragon issue 388 was released in PDF format only in June of 2010. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

In Duty Bound: Paladin Basics

By Jeff Morgenroth. p. 5–12

The paladin is the defender par excellence, and the options presented this month offer them new ways to be formidable.

Winning Races: Dragonborn

By Peter Schaefer. p. 38

Some dragonborn don’t develop their race’s characteristic breath weapon. Instead, they inherit the overpowering sense of fear that dragons instill in others.

Channel Divinity: Tymora

By Erik Scott de Bie and Matthew Sernett. p. 45–47

Tymora is the goddess of luck and the favored patron of adventurers.
