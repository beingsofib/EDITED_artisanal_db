Dragon+ 34

Dragon+ issue 34 was released on October 22, 2020. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

In the Works

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

A deeper look at several upcoming releases:

  • Tasha's Cauldron of Everything
  • Curse of Strahd Revamped
  • Dungeons & Dragons Annual 2021
  • Warriors of Waterdeep

The Art of Drizzt

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

An interview with artist Max Dunbar on the art he provided for a set of action figures of Drizzt Do'Urden and Guenhwyvar.

Frozen Offerings

By Paul Bimler.

A single-player adventure set in Icewind Dale.

D&D Classics

By Bart Carroll.

Several articles and excerpts from the archives themed around living brains.

  • Excerpts from Libris Mortis, Open Grave, and Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden.
  • "Alchemical Imbalance" from Dragon #364, "Face of the Moon" from Dungeon #201, and "Tomb of Meowers" from Dragon #393.

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

Several maps from Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden and Tales from the Yawning Portal.

Ice Out

By Greg Tito.

A short story set in Icewind Dale.
