River Sundarra

The River Sundarra flowed from the east out of the Windrise Ports in Laerakond and into the Dragon Sea.

Background

The River Sundarra ran through the city of Ramekho, offering easy fishing to the inhabitants of this city and nearby settlements. A common fish found in the Sundarra was the Yellow Finned Oponi.
