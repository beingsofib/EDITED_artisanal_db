Dragon issue 144, And now, for something completely different..., was released in April of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Still More Outrages From the Mages

By John M. Maxstadt. p. 32, 34, 37, 79

A parody of the "Pages from the Mages" column and the Forgotten Realms itself, with the wizard Elkwhisker presenting the Misbegotten Realms, the notorious Nimrod of Nump and four scrolls worth of spells that aren't quite right.

Role-playing Reviews

By Jim Bambra. p. 38–45

  • Mertwig's Maze
  • The Savage Frontier

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 56

New products for March and April.

  • Curse of the Azure Bonds module.
  • Darkwell novel.
  • Hall of Heroes
