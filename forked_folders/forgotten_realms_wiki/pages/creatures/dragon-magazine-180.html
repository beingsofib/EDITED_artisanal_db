Dragon issue 180, Bringing a little chaos into your life, was released in April of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Not Another Magical Sword!?!

By Charles Rogers. p. 14

Creating unique magical items, with a focus on swords.

Your Basic Barbarian

By Lee A. Spain. p. 24

Roleplaying stupid fighters.

Hot Night in the Old Town

By Joseph R. Ravitts. p. 28

Writing adventures for priest characters based out of their home temples.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Three peculiar beasts for all AD&D campaigns

By Spike Y. Jones. p. 70–74

A goat that knocks down walls, and a lizard that knocks down everything.

  • Battering ram
  • Gorse
  • Quakedancer

Novel Ideas: The heroes who refused to die

By Pat McGilligan and Will Larson p. 79

Review of the Druidhome trilogy.
