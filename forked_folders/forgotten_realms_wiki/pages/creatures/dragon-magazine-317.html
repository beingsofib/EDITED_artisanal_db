Dragon issue 317, Exotic Heroes, was released in March of 2004. It contained the following articles possibly related to the Forgotten Realms:

Urban Druids: Secret Voice of the City

By James Jacobs, illustrated by Dan Scott. p. 30–36

A variant druid adapted to life in the big city. Includes new spells.

Truenames and Fetishes: The Power and the Peril

By Philip M. Greeley, illustrated by Marc Sasso. p. 38–42

A new form of true name magic.

Eye of Newt and Toe of Frog: Using Power Components

By Jim Bishop and F. Wesley Schneider, illustrated by David Wahlstrom. p. 44–49

Extra uses and benefits of material components.

Body of Knowledge: Feeling it in the Bones

By Andrew M. Scott, illustrated by Wayne Reynolds. p. 50–57

Three new body-warping magic Prestige Classes: the bone-shaping osteomancer, the flesh-crafting flux adept, and the brain-enhancing cerebrex.

Magic's High Notes: 9 Magical Instruments

By S. Deniz Bucak, illustrated by Jonathan Wayshak. p. 58–61

A selection of magical musical instruments.

Faiths of Faerûn: Battleguard of Tempus: Soldier-Saints of the Lord of Battle

By Thomas M. Costa, illustrated by Jason Engle. p. 76–78

A Prestige Class for the soldier-saints of the god of battles. Includes new spells and a new domain.

Faiths of Faerûn: Holy Strategists of the Red Knight

By Travis Stout, illustrated by Fred Hooper. p. 80–83

The clergy of the Red Fellowship. Includes new feats and spells.
