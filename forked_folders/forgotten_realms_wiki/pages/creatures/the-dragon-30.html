The Dragon issue 30 was released in October of 1979. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Leomund's Tiny Hut: Good Evening - Are You Wild About Vampires?

By Lenard Lakofka. p. 7–9

Here's Something to Sink Your Teeth Into; a complete guide to vampires in Dungeons & Dragons.

The New, Improved Ninja

By Sheldon Price. p. 13–15

Rules for a ninja based on 4 fields: weaponry, tools, equipment, and poisons.

Dragon's Bestiary

By Ed Greenwood. p. 43

All about the Curst for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons. Ed Greenwood's first Dragon article.
