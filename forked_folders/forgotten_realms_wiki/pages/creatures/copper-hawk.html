Copper hawk

Creature
nameCopper hawk
buildAvian

The copper hawk was a unique construct used by Strasus Thingoleir.

Description

The hawk was made from burnished copper and imitated the mannerisms of a bird, moving and acting like a living creature. It had chips of sapphire for eyes cut from the same one used in the magic rapier Quick, granting it the same affinity for electricity. When it attacked, the wings crackled with energy.

Abilities

To attack, the copper hawk would pump its wings and launch a bolt of lightning out of its mouth. The blast was not exceptionally deadly, but it could launch many of them in rapid succession with great accuracy. When sufficiently aggravated, it could launch a much larger, more powerful blast.

History

Strasus Thingoleir obtained the hawk in order to guard his private study and workshop in Fourstaves House.
