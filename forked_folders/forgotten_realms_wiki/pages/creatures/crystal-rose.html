Crystal roses were extremely rare flowering plants found at the Garden of the Drow in Undermountain.

History

Crystal roses were beautiful crystalline plants. Their rarity and beauty made them highly sought after symbols of status and wealth. Acquiring the rose was extremely dangerous due to the location they grew in. In 1370 DR, Asborn Moonstar, a love-sick young nobleman from Waterdeep perished during the expedition for the legendary flower. He was trapped in the garden chamber by its drow residents, leaving him to starve to death. Subsequently, one rose was taken from the garden and returned to the noble youth's grieving fiancé, Elene Ruldegost, as a token of her dead lover's affections.
