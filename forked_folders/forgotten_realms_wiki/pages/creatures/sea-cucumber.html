Sea cucumber

Creature
nameSea Cucumber
locationCrystal Reef, Shining Sea
Kara-Tur

Sea cucumbers were a species of harmless simple mollusk with soft bodies, lacking the common hard shell among other marine creatures from the same group.

Ecology

Sea cucumbers could be found in the Shining Sea, and notably the among rich aquatic life around the Crystal Reef, to the west of Tharsult.

The products of seas, oceans, rivers, and lakes were a major food source in the lands to the east of Faerûn, such as Kara-Tur. Sea cucumbers were among the seafood consumed and were considered a delicacy.
