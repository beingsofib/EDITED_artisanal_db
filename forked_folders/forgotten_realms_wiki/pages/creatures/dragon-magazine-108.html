Dragon issue 108 was released in April of 1986. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Role of Nature

By Bruce Humphrey. p. 12

Systems for bringing the effects of the enviroment (cold, heat, weather, terrain, etc.) into play.

The Ecology of the Pernicon

By John Nephew. p. 22-25

A scholarly treatise on the Pernicon.

The Pernicon: A New Version

By John Nephew. p. 25-26

Revised game stats for the Pernicon.

Cantrips for Clerics

By Arthur Collins. p. 28

0th level spells for clerics.

A Different Design

By Lisa R. Cohen. p. 30

Tips for designing tournament adventures.

TSR Profiles: Douglas Niles

By Uncredited. p. 66

Author Douglas Niles talks about how he became a game designer.

TSR Profiles: Jean Blashfield Black

By Uncredited. p. 67

Managing Editor of the TSR Book Department, Jean Blashfield Black, talks about her history in publishing.
