Dragon issue 65 was released in September of 1982. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

From the Sorcerer's Scroll: Character Classes to Consider

By Gary Gygax. p. 9

Character classes under consideration for Unearthed Arcana

Leomund's Tiny Hut: Keep Track of Quality

By Len Lakofka. p. 13–17

A system of item quality.

Featured Creatures

By Gary Gygax. p. 25–26

The Baku and the Phoenix, two more upper outer planar creatures for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

Tuatha De Danann — A revised Celtic mythos

By Robin Emrys Atkinson. p. 47-53

A revision of the Celtic pantheon presented in Deities and Demigods.

Law of the Land

By Ed Greenwood. p. 56-62

Laws in AD&D worlds.

Up on a Soapbox: In Gaming, Your Style Will Tell

By Lewis Pulsipher. p. 68

Gaming styles in games.
