Dragon issue 335 was released in September of 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Waterdeep, Splendor of the City

By Eric L. Boyd, illustrated by Scott Rischer. p. 22-23

A listing of all the sources for Waterdeep lore.

New Olamm Bard College

By Elaine Cunningham, illustrated by Vincent Dutrait. p. 24-32

The music of Waterdeep.

Interview with Ed Greenwood

By Jeffery E. Thetford. p. 34-40

Volo's Guide to the Master of the Forgotten Realms.

Game of Chance

By Elaine Cunningham, illustrated by David Wahlstrom. p. 42–51

A Moonblade in Waterdeep.

The Ecology of the Lizardfolk

By Amber Scott and F. Wesley Schneider, illustrated by Peter Bergting and Mike Schley. p. 52-57

A scholarly treatise on the Lizardfolk.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Swamp Swag

By Amber Scott, illustrated by Jeff Carlisle. p. 68–70

Items you might steal from those Lizardfolk.

Spellcraft: Songsabers of Waterdeep

By Mike Mearls, illustrated by Attila Adorjany. p. 74–77

Bardic spells.

  • Animate instrument
  • Ever armed
  • Harmonic void
  • Loresong
  • Tune of the dancing weapon
