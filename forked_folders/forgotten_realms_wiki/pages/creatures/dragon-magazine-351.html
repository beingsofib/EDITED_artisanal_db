Dragon issue 351, Campaign Classics: Beyond the World Serpent Inn, was released in January of 2007. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The World Serpent Inn

By Eric L. Boyd, Illustrated by Andrew Hou, Cartography by Robert Lazzaretti. p. 20-26

An extradimensional connection to all the worlds of the multiverse.

Al-Qadim: Magic and Intrigue in the High Desert Tribes

By Wolfgang Baur, Illustrated by Andrew Hou. p. 28-31

The tribes of the Great Oasis.

Forgotten Realms: Kara-Tur

By Brian Cortijo and Edward Bonny, Illustrated by Brian Cortijo, Cartography by Christopher West. p. 46-49

Cham Fau and the White Tiger Monastery.

The Gatetown of Ecstasy

By Amber Stewart, Illustrated by Jeff Laubenstein, Cartography by Robert Lazzaretti. p. 50-53

Ecstasy: A gate-town a thousand miles from the Spire.

The Shadows of Sithicus

By James Lowder, Illustrated by John Gravato, Cartography by Robert Lazzaretti.. p. 54-58

The domain of Sithicus.

Volo's Guide: Lost Regalia of the North: The Toppled Thrones

By Eric L. Boyd, Illustrated by Andrew Hou. p. 72-75

Thrones of the Realms.

  • Bloodbone Throne of Uruth
  • Shining Thrones of Splendarrmornn
  • Soaring Throne of the Witch Queen
