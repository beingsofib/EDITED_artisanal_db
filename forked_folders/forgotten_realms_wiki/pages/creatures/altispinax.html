Altispinax

Creature
nameAltispinax

An altispinax was a species of dinosaur.

Description

Altispinaxes had sails on their backs and a coloration that gave the creatures camouflage in the jungle. They had hundreds of long, sharp teeth, and powerful jaws. They could create a sound with the deep bellow of a lion's roar and the trill of a birdsong.

Ecology

Altispinaxes signaled to others of their kind through their bellows, which silenced the calls of many other jungle creatures. They attacked in groups, and were excited into a frenzy by the screams of their prey.

Biology

Altispinaxes were carnivorous.

History

Little was known about altispinaxes, save for that they were often sighted in Chultan swamps.
