Dragon issue 323 was released in September of 2004. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Forgotten Realms: Demon Stone

By F. Wesley Schneider. pp. 54–59

A preview of the computer game Demon Stone.

Seven Deadly Domains

By Hal Maclean, illustrated by Rob Thomas. pp. 62–66

The new spell domains of pride, wrath, envy, sloth, avarice, gluttony, and lust.

The Ecology of Chokers

By Eric Cagle, illustrated by Peter Bergting. pp. 74–77

Details on the creepy choker.
