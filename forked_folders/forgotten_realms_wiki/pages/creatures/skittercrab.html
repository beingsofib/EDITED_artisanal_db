Skittercrab

Creature
nameSkittercrab
alignment1eNeutral
locationSkysea
firstDesert of Desolation

Skittercrabs were carnivores unique to the Skysea. They skimmed its surface in their hard shells, looking for soft food. They traveled in groups of up to ten.
