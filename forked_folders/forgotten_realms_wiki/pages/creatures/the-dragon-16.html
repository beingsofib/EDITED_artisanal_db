Dragon magazine 16 was released in July 1978. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ultimate NPC: Ninja — The DM's Hit Man

By Sheldon Price. p. 9

This article gives 1st edition Dungeons & Dragons rules for creating the ninja class. Price presents tables for ninja assassination probability, thief skills, and lists weapons, such as shuriken and ninja-to.
