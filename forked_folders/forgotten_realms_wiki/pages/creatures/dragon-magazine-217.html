Dragon issue 217, Creative Campaigning, was released in May of 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Boons and Benefits

By Larry Granato. p. 12

Giving the PCs rewards that are not strictly material, such as titles and privileges.

Two Heads are Better Than One

By Joshua Siegel. p. 24

A case for using a pair of GMs instead of just one.

Class Action!

By Peter C. Zelinski. p. 28

Designing AD&D adventures for one PC class.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 124

New products for June.

  • The Sword of the Dales module.
