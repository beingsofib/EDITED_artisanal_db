A dusk unicorn was a rare breed of unicorn. Some fey believed that the birth of one was an auspicious omen.

Description

Unlike regular unicorns, dusk unicorns possessed a dark coat and mane and a black horn.

Abilities

Dusk unicorns, like other unicorns, could heal other creatures by touching them with their horn. Additionally, a dusk unicorn possessed a further two unusual magical abilities: they could manifest a group of dancing lights that drew other creatures towards the light, and they could teleport themselves or a group of allies up to .

Combat

Dusk unicorns wielded their hooves and their horn as weapons; in particular, charging into an enemy horn-first was an effective tactic of theirs.
