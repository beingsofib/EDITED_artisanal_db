Fey flower was a variety of rare flowering plant used in alchemy.

Usages

After fey flowers were dried, they could be crushed and turned into ashes through an alchemical process. These ashes of dried fey flowers had a pleasant herbal aroma of honeysuckle, eucalyptus, crocus, and cyclamen.

Ashes of dried fey flowers were the main ingredient in creating hag's bane potion – a magic concoction that could force a pregnant hag to disgorge the child she consumed.

History

In the , several dried fey flower were assembled by the members of the Anti-Hag Support Group to be used against the green hag that called herself Auntie Ethel in the city of Baldur's Gate.
