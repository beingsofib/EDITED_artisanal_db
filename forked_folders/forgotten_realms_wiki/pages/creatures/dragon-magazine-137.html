Dragon magazine 137 was released in September of 1988. It focused on wilderness adventures. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

What's For Lunch

By David Howery. pp. 8–14

Detailed hunting rules expanding on the Wilderness Survival Guide.

Creatures

  • Antelope
  • Bison
  • Giraffe
  • Hart
  • Musk-ox
  • Okapi
  • Seal
  • Tapir
  • Walrus

Treasures of the Wilds

By David Howery. Artwork by Joseph Pillsbury. pp. 16–24

Treasures gained from the bodies of wild creatures and the value of their offspring.

The Ecology of Carnivorous Plants

By Gregg Chamberlain. Artwork by Jonathan Hung. pp. 26–32

A detailed look into the lives and ecology of carnivorous plants.

Into the Age of Mammals

By David Howery. Artwork by Jim Holloway. pp. 42–52

Mammalian (and avian) of the past.
