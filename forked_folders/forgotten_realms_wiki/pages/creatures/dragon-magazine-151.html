Dragon issue 151, May your life be full of surprises, was released in November of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ecology of the Kappa

By David Wellman. p. 14-17

A scholarly treatise on the Kappa.

Soldiers of the Law

By Dan Salas. p. 18

A look at law enforcement in Oriental Adventures.

Earn Those Heirlooms!

By Jay Ouzts. p. 22

Balancing the heirloom tables from Oriental Adventures.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 27

New products for October.

  • Avatar Trilogy, Book Three: Waterdeep
  • Spelljammer: AD&D Adventures in Space
  • TM4 Waterdeep
  • FRE3 Waterdeep

The Dragon's Bestiary: Never forget, never forgive

By Sylvia Li. p. 28–31

The Wang-Liang, a long-lived race of mischievious giants with a curious grudge against humanity.

The Ecology of the Yuan-ti

By David Wellman. p. 33–35

A scholarly treatise on the Yuan-ti.

The Beastie Knows Best

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 36

The best computer games of the year, as chosen by Dragon's reviewing team.

  • Pool of Radiance

Role-playing Reviews

By Jim Bambra. p. 38-40, 74

  • Lords of Darkness.
