Dragon issue 218, Wing, Fire, and Claw, was released in June of 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

First Quest: Play with Me, She Breathed

By Ed Greenwood. pp. 8, 54

Ed Greenwood's early love for role-playing games, without and within the Forgotten Realms.

Dragon Intrigues

By Gregory W. Detwiler, illustrated by Steve Schwartz. pp. 16-22

What dragons can do when working from behind the scenes.

Dragon Dweomers

By Robert S. Mullin, illustrated by Scott Rosema. p. 24-30

Spells for dragons.

Spice of Life

By Thomas M. Kane. p. 32

Running multiple plotlines simultaneously.

The Game Wizards

By Steven Schend, illustrated by Jeff Easley. p. 39-40

Presenting a checklist for the first Blood Wars Expansion, and a sample deck to help you conquer the City of Doors

Magic Resistance, Step by Step

By Skip Williams. pp. 56-57

How to apply the concept of magic resistance.

The Nature of Evil

By Michael T. Kuciak. p. 66

Making your bad guys really bad.

The Ecology of the Bird Maiden

By Paul Culotta, illustrated by Karl Waller. pp. 75-80, 94

A detailed look at the bird maiden, a Zakharan relative of the swanmay.

Role-playing Reviews

By Rick Swan. p. 88

The Deva Spark.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 120

New products for July.
