Dragon issue 120 was released in April of 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Spells for Everyone, Part II

By Alan Webster. p. 10

A sample of spells usable by any character class.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Three that ring true

By Bill Birdsall. p. 33–34

Three new magic items for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

The Ecology of the Gas Spore

By Ed Greenwood. p. 37

A scholarly treatise on the Gas Spore.

Plane Speaking: Tuning in to the Outer Planes

By Jeff Grubb. p. 42–43

Planar travel with special tuning forks.

TSR Profiles: Karen S. Martin

By Uncredited. p. 88

Profile of game designer Karen S. Martin.
