Spelt was a grain-producing grass found in Faerûn.

Availability

Spelt was cultivated for its grain in ancient Netheril alongside wheat, bran, timothy, oats, and rye.

Ecology

Spelt was affected by the wheat rust blight, which destroyed the plants and their grains during the time of Netheril. The spreading disease was cured in by the arcanist Candlemas, who used a complex combination of magics to propagate a cure and save the crops.
