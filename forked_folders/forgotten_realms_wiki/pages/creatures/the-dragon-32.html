The Dragon issue 32, "IDDC Winning Entry", was released in December of 1979. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Poisons from AA to XX

By Charles Sagui. p. 4

A new system for poisons to prevent player abuse in Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

The Druid in Fact and Fantasy

By William Fawcett. p. 21

A more historical look at druids as priests/judges of the Celts, and an alteration of the character class to reflect this.

The Dragon's Bestiary

By Ed Greenwood. p. 39

All about the Crawling Claw for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.
