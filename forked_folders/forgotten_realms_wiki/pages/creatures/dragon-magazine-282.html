Dragon issue 282, Show Me the Funny Issue, was released in April of 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Coming Soon

By Uncredited. p. 20

  • Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting 3rd edition sourcebook.
  • Pool of Radiance: Attack on Myth Drannor module.
  • The Floodgate novel.
  • The Shattered Mask novel.

Countdown to the Forgotten Realms: Magic of Faerûn

Uncredited. p. 24

A very brief overview of FR's magic.

Up on a Soapbox: What the Heck is a Roleplaying Game?!, Part 4

By Gary Gygax. p. 26

What makes up an RPG? Problem solving, questing, random chance, and role assumption.

The Outgoing Goblin's Guide to Gaming Etiquette

By Jeff Vogel. p. 30

DOs and Donts for roleplayers.

Logic Missiles

By Mike Selinker. p. 34

How to design numerous different types of logic puzzles for your game.

Possessions

By Elaine Cunningham. p. 61

What is a Necromancer's greatest desire? A ghost story. Reprinted in The Best of the Realms III.

The New Adventures of Volo: The Urge to Hunt

By Ed Greenwood. p. 71–73

Hunting dragons and buckets in the Realms.

Events

Dark Dragon Hunt

Class Acts: Blessed of Gruumsh

By Monte Cook. p. 84

A prestige class for orcs.

Vs.: Elves

By Steve Kenson. p. 94

Fightin' elves.
