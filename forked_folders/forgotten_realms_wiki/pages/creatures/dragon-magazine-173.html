Dragon issue 173, A dark sun casts the darkest shadows, was released in September of 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bazaar of the Bizarre: A Wizard's Staff is to Lean On

By Ed Greenwood. p. 30-34

Magical staffs, some with Forgotten Realms background.

  • Rilantaver's staff
  • Staff of battle
  • Staff of displacement
  • Staff of divergence
  • Staff of miracles
  • Staff of night
  • Staff of scrivening
  • Staff of silence
  • Staff of spheres
  • Staff of surprises
  • Staff of the moonglow
  • Staff of vision

Magical Mangling Made Easy

By Gregory W. Detwiler. p. 36

What to do when magic is too strong.

Get Your Priorities Straight!

By Royce Wicks. p. 50

Playing alignments as objectives, not rules.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 52

Clue Corner: Eye of the Beholder and Secret of the Silver Blades .

The Ecology of...: The Sociology of the Flind

By Spike Y. Jones. p. 77-84

A scholarly treatise on the Flind.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 95

New products for October.

  • Elfshadow novel.
