Aphelda's vendor golem was a construct creature what was capable of functioning as a shopkeep on behalf of its creator.

Description

A vendor golem performed all tasks that a real shopkeep did on a daily basis. They could purchase, sell, and haggle over the store's stock. Skilled wizard store keeps left these strange creatures in charge of their stores. Leomund's merchant elemental was a similar creature and a viable alternative. Some claimed that unlike Leomund's elemental, Aphelda's construct was a mercantile pushover.
