Stench ghouls, were an intelligent subtype of ghoul that existed in the period between the Spellplague and the Second Sundering. They arose from the corpses of humanoid cannibals that died from eating rancid humanoid flesh.

Description

The most distinguishing feature of a stench ghoul was the incredibly nauseating smell that constantly emanated from its body.

Combat

Beyond using their stench tactically, this subtype fought in the typical ghoul manner of biting and slashing with claws.

History

In the late 15 century DR, a group of Daggerdalesmen adventurers traveling up the Tower of the Void were alerted of the presence of ghouls and a particularly potent stench surrounding them by the brigand Madder Belebrix. After fighting through a number of groups of ghouls and plaguechanged ghouls the adventurers came to find the debilitating smell was coming from a stinking ghoul that towered over them in size.

Society

These ghouls were typically found in areas with rich amounts of carrion, such as battlefields and graveyards. Due to their great intelligence they were often found commanding packs of other ghouls.
