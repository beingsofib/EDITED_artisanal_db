Dragon issue 225, Building a Better PC!, was released in January of 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Secret Origins and Motivations of Player Characters

By Fraser Sherman. p. 18

Why PCs leave home for the uncertain life of adventure.

I'm OK, You're One-Dimensional

By David Clarke. p. 36

Using familiar people as the basis for your PC's personality.

RPGA Network News

By Scout Douglas. p. 42

Network coordinator Scott Douglas explains the LIVING CITY™ Campaign.

Campaign Classics: Three Greyhawk Grimoires

By Robert S. Mullin. p. 49-54

New spells from famous mages of Oerth: Vecna, Iggwilv, and Acererak.

Dungeon Mastery: What to Do When Your Party Wants to Split Up

By Paul Culotta. p. 84

The "Include Everybody" option for running multiple parties.
