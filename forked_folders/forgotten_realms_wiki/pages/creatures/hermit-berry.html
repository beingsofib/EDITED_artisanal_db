Hermit berry

nameHermit Berry

locationHermit's Wood, Cormyr

A hermit berry were a type of berry-bearing plant found in the Hermit's Wood in Cormyr.

Description

They were small yellow berries with bluish dots covering them.

Usages

Hermit berries were known for their pleasant aroma, enough that sprigs of hermit berries were given as signs of affection between lovers in the area.

However, the aroma of the hermit berry has a repulsing effect on kelpies, even causing them to abandon their prey if had even so much as a few berries on their person.
