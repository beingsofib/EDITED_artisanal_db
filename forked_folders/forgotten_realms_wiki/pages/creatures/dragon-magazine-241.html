Dragon issue 241 was released in November of 1997. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Modron Magic: The Sorcery of Structure

By Monte Cook. pp. 56

The Modrons are on the march again, and just look at their new toys.

Wyrms of the North: Hoondarrh

By Ed Greenwood. pp. 70-74

This article describes the infamous male red dragon Hoondarrh, also known as the Red Rage of Mintarn.

Spell

  • Mirror fireball

Campaign Classics: The Roof of the World

By Wolfgang Baur. pp. 88-95

This article describes the inner workings of the empire of the evil yak-men of Zakhara for the Al-Qadim campaign.
