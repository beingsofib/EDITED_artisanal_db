Dragon+ 27

Dragon+ issue 27 was released on September 19, 2019. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Previews: In the Works

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman.

The Dragon+ team held council at the end of 2018, and decreed that each issue would continue to offer early looks at products in development. And so, let's see what's in the works...

  • Baldur's Gate: Descent into Avernus and Baldur’s Gate: Descent Into Avernus Platinum Edition: The next major Dungeons & Dragons story-line takes a Hellish turn.
  • Adventurers League: The D&D Adventurers League will usher in the release of Baldur's Gate: Descent Into Avernus in glorious style—by literally paying a visit to Hell!
  • Baldur's Gate III: Larian Studios places the fate of the Forgotten Realms in your hands.
  • WizKids Figures: Some very cool figures are being released to compliment Baldur's Gate: Descent Into Avernus.
  • Funko Pop: Bow before the terrible and powerful visage of… Pop! Asmodeus!

Plotting the Descent

By Matt Chapman.

Artist Max Dunbar spills the secrets of crafting a Hellish landscape and its denizens as he shares his concept art with Dragon+.

Behind the Screen: Sign Language and D&D

By Matt Chapman.

Matt Chapman chats with interpreters on both sides of the Atlantic working to make D&D more accessible for hearing-impaired players and viewers.

Adventurers League

By Matt Chapman.

The devilish Chris Lindsay previews an Adventurers League campaign that sees players forge their fate in hellfire.

D&D Classics

By Matt Chapman.

A look back at vehicles of the clanking, rattling, and infernal kind. And the Single Class Ninja!

Maps of the Month

Maps for Ghosts of Saltmarsh and Barber of Silverymoon.
