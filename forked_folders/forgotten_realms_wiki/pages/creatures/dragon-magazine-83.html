Dragon issue 83 was released in March of 1984. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The many facets of gems: Stories behind the stones listed in the DMG

By Michael J. Lowrey. p. 10–19

Full descriptions and illustrations of all the gemstones from the Advanced Dungeons & Dragons Dungeon Masters Guide.

The Ecology of the Stirge

By Ed Greenwood. p. 20–22

A scholarly treatise on the Stirge.

The Dancing Hut

By Roger Moore. p. 31–52

AD&D adventure for levels 9+; An expedition into a bizarre artifact, Baba Yaga's Hut.

How to finish fights faster: A suggested simpler system for unarmed combat

By Michael J. Lowrey. p. 55

A simpler alternative to the weaponless combat system in the Dungeon Masters Guide.
