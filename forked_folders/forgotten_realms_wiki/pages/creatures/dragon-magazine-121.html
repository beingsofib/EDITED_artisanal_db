Dragon issue 121 was released in May of 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms. This issue featured the realms of Kara-Tur and Oriental Adventures:

Whaddya Mean, Jack the Samurai?

By Barbara Curtis. p. 16

Tables of Japanese names suitable for Oriental Adventures characters.

The Deadliest Perfume

By John P. Brown. p. 26

The properties of lotus blossoms and dust in Oriental Adventures.

The Life and Death of a Castle

By David "Zeb" Cook. p. 32

The story of a real-world Japanese castle to assist DMs.

The Geisya

By Gregg Sharp. pp. 38–41

A description of a new NPC class, the geisha.
