Dragon Annual 2 was published by TSR, Inc. in December of 1997. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Magical Cups and Flasks

By Oren Schnurr, illustrated by Chris Adams. p. 16-20

Here are some cups and flasks to hold that brew that is true.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Arctic Monsters

By Belinda G. Ashley, illustrated by Catherine Burnett. p. 32–38

Your heroes will find only cold comfort in the clutches of these monsters.

  • Crystal skeleton
  • Growler
  • Snow spider
  • Tundra lizard

Dungeon Mastery: Talk of the Town

By Lloyd Brown, III, illustrated by Susan Van Camp. p. 40

What happens between adventures is often the talk of the town.

Ecology of the Shambling Mound

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Valerie Valusek. p. 48-52

A scholarly treatise on the Shambling Mound.

Rogues Gallery: Finder's Band

By Jeff Grubb and Kate Novak, illustrated by Rags Morales. p. 54-57

The characters from Finder's Bane and Tymora's Luck.

The Magic of Myth Drannor

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Jim Crabtree. p. 88-94

Spells and magical items from the Ancient Realms.

Pox of the Planes

By Ed Bonny, illustrated by Chris Adams. p. 100–111

Night hags, their wares, and their servants.
