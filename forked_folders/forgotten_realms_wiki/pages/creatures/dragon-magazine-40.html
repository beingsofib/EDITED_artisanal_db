Dragon issue 40 was released in August of 1980. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Believe it or not, Fantasy has reality

By Douglas Bachmann. p. 10

A philosophical work on the nature and importance of fantasy.

The Other Were? Right here!

By Roger E. Moore. Artwork by Gail Gierahn. p. 32-33

Some lore about some rare and unusual lycanthropes.

Werelion • Wereleopard • Werejaguar • Weresabre • Weredire • Wereram • Wereweasel • Weresloth • Werebadger • Werebison

Giving the Undead an Even Break

By Steve Melancon. p. 38

An expanded Cleric vs. Undead table based on the Hit Dice of the undead being affected.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Oriental Magic Items

By David Sweet. p. 44

A dozen magic items for Eastern settings.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Nidus' Wand of Endless Repetition

By Ed Greenwood. p. 45

A wand that forces a target to repeat his actions.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Wand of Ochalor's Eye

By Ed Greenwood. p. 45

A wand that induces a very powerful Fear.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Wingless Wonder

By Ed Greenwood. p. 64–65

The Wingless wonder, a comical beast that resembles a walking egg.
