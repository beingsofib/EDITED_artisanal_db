Dragon issue 330 was released in April of 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Enter the Far Realm

By Bruce Cordell, illustrated by David Bircham. p. 20–40

The Far Realm, its magic, its monsters, and its madness.

Amoebic crawler • Cranial encyster • Kaortic hulk • Nightseed

Aching dread • Call amoebic crawler • Call cranial encyster • Call kaortic hulk • Call nightseed • Dimension rift • Far realm visitation • Finger of expulsion • Lingering raver • Soul blasting dread • Welcome the amoebic sea

The Ecology of the Chuul

By Mike Mearls, illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 60-64

A scholarly treatise on the chuul.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Tools of the Assassin

By Oliver Diaz and Robert J. Hahn, illustrated by Jeff Carlisle. p. 66–68

Magic items for assassins.

Spellcraft: Volume Veneficus

By Mike Mearls, illustrated by Chuck Lukacs. p. 70–73

Spells for the yuan-ti.

Gaining Prestige: The Jester

By Richard Pett, illustrated by Jeff Carlisle. p. 74–77

A jester prestige class.
