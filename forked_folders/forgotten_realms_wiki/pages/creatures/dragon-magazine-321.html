Dragon issue 321, Planar Adventure, was released in July of 2004. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Way of the Gun

By Michael Shortt, illustrated by Marc Sasso. p. 30-38

Bang! Boom! Rat-a-tat-tat! Pump your enemies’ guts full of lead.

Planar Dragons

By Edward Bonny, illustrated by Marc Sasso. p. 42–56

Dragons of the Outer Planes.

Adamantine dragon • arboreal dragon • axial dragon • beast dragon • concordant dragon

Silicon Sorcery: The Temple of Elemental Evil

By Clifford Horowitz. p. 58-61

Three new creatures great and sm—no wait, they are all pretty big.

The Limitless Light: A Tour of the Plane of Radiance

By Bennet Marks, illustrated by Michael Phillips. pp. 64–68

The Plane of Radiance, a new transitive plane opposite (in more ways than one) the Plane of Shadow.

Creatures of Brilliance: Inhabitants of the Plane of Radiance

By Bennet Marks, illustrated by David Wahlstrom. pp. 70–75

It's not all rainbows and lollipops on the Plane of Radiance. These critters want to fill your visit to the luminous plane with terror.

Faiths of Faerûn: Arvoreen's Keepers

By Thomas M. Costa, illustrated by Jeff Carlisle. pp. 76–79

Arvoreen's Keepers and Arvoreen's Warders, new prestige classes for the Defender of the Realms.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: Nurneene's Marvelous Masks

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by David Day. pp. 80–83

Nurneene's Marvelous Masks: A shop in Waterdeep.

Prestige Classes of Al-Qadim: Champions of Fate

By Dean Poisso, illustrated by Jeff Carlisle. pp. 84–90

If you had sand in your sandals every day, you'd be as bad-ass as these guys too.
