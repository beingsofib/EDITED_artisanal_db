Dragon issue 116 was released in December of 1986. It contained the following article related to the Forgotten Realms:

Children of the Deep

By Todd Mossburg. p. 28

Aquatic elves as PCs.

The Ecology of the Minotaur

By Anthony Gerard. p. 33-35

Everything you never wanted to know about your favorite anthropophagic cow furry, the Minotaur.

The Dragon's Bestiary

By Alex Gray, Gregg Chamberlain, Nick Kopsinis, Robert Maurus, and Spike Y. Jones. p. 38

The aquatic monsters Brain coral • Centaur, sea • Clam, giant • Grouper, giant • Morana • Porcupine fish, Giant • Ray, electric • Sawfish • Sawshark • Sea anemone, giant • Titan, sea • Undine • Weed giant.

Rogue Stones and Gemjumping

By Ed Greenwood. p. 54

This article details the magic of gemjumping using rogue stones.

By Tooth and Claw

By Gregory Detwiler. p. 58-64

Ordinary animals are dangerous in any game.
