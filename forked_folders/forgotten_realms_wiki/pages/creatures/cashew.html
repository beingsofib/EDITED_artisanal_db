Cashew, or krail, was a fleshy nut produced by a tree of the same name.

Availability

Cashews were sold by the pound through the famed Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue, priced at 20 gold pieces.

Usage

Cashew soup was a dish prepared in the city of Waterdeep in the late 1400s DR.
