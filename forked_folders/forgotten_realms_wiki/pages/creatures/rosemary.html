Rosemary was a type of plant in the Realms that was primarily used as either a spice or a herb.

Availability

  • In addition to selling pure rosemary,
  • In Interior Zakhara, rosemary was cultivated in the herb garden of the fortress Sarahin.
  • Rosemary could be purchased in the markets of Waterdeep.

Usage

  • Rosemary was a common ingredient, alongside onions and thyme, used to season a full leg of mutton, be it roasted, smoked, or simply stewed.
  • A sprig of rosemary could be used as a material component in the spell banishment.

Notable Users of Rosemary

  • In the lands further south and east of the Western Heartlands, rosemary was among the spices considered "base staples" and were often found being carried in the spice pouches of travelers.

Businesses

  • Rosemary was one of the herbs that the restaurant Infernal Rapture typically used to season deep fried, miniature giant space hamsters.

Races & Sentient Creatures

  • Rosemary was one of many spices that humans would often make into a powder and mix with other spices, then use to paint some meat.
