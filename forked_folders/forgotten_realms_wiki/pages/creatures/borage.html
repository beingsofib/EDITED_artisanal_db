Borage was a typing of flowering herb found in the Realms.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold borage under its "Aurora's Larder" section for 2 copper pieces per .

Usages

  • These plants could be used medicinally to treat coughs and various lung infections.
  • These plants were a common form of seasoning in the Realms.
  • All illithids had an allergy to borage. Like other vegetation that illithids had known allergies for, borage could cause nausea, a strong desire to withdraw from others, and if consumed in large quantities could induce debilitatingly painful internal cramps and convulsions.
