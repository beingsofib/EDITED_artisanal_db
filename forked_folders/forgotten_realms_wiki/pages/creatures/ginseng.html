Ginseng was a type of herb that could be found in the Realms.

Availability

Places in the Realms where ginseng was known to grow or be cultivated were:

  • In Faerûn, Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold ginseng in its section of wizard supplies.

Beyond Faerûn

  • The nation of Wa.
  • In Xiousing, a small Shou district in the city of Marsember, ginseng was grown by Shen's Herb Garden.

Usages

  • The process for creating a homunculus that was devised by the alchemist Askrim required a large leaf from a ginseng or alternatively the whole plant.
  • Ginseng was a material component for the shocking grasp spell.
  • Some used ginseng in the making of incense.
  • Some were known to cultivate ginseng for medicinal purposes.
  • Ginseng could be used to make tea.
  • The root was believed to possess aphrodisiacal qualities.

History

In the 14 century DR, ginseng was one of many goods transported along the Spice Road.
