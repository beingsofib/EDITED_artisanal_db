Dragon issue 269, Into the Wild, was released in March of 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: Rob Lazzaretti

By Steve Kenson. p. 20

Cartographer Rob Lazzaretti never really drew maps when he first started gaming, but that's certainly changed these days!

Animal Henchmen

By James Wyatt, illustrated by Susan Van Camp. pp. 26–34

Familiars aren't the only animal companions that might join your heroes. This article details intelligent animal and animal-like creatures:

  • Asperii
  • Talking bird
  • Giant lynx
  • Greater winged cat
  • Tressym
  • Blink dog
  • Dolphin
  • Pseudodragon
  • Giant eagle
  • Hippocampus
  • Moon-horse
  • Giant owl
  • Talking owl
  • Pegasus
  • Unicorn
  • Mist wolf

Herbcraft

By Owen K.C. Stevens, illustrated by Rob Alexander. p. 36–40

Expanding the herbalism proficiency.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Demihuman Pets

By Jack Pisker, illustrated by David Day. p.68–76

This article discusses the pets of different demihuman races:

  • Yllethyn (Elven horse)
  • Crayghe (Elven hawk)
  • Byut (Fey Deer)
  • Pennig (Halfling Pony)
  • Twill (Pocket rat)
  • Stoght (Forest Gnome fisher)
  • Brak Twan (Dwarven Tunnel hound)
  • Guttar (Dwarven Ox)

The Ecology of the Pseudodragon

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Tony DiTerlizzi. p. 78-83

Pseudodragon: The Monster ... Savers ... Association?

The New Adventures of Volo: Hin Nobody Knows

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Ron Spencer. p. 84–87

This article describes the halflings and gnomes of the Realms.
