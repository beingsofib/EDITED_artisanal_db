Barracudas were a type of carnivorous fish that inhabited warm salt water.

Description

They were slender creatures with long jagged teeth poking out from a noticeable underbite. Their bodies were shaped like arrows, able to move very fast in water, the better to dart into range, bite their prey, then disappear just as suddenly.

Behavior

Barracudas typically did not attack humanoid creatures. However, they occasionally would attack humanoids carrying shiny objects, out of a mistaken belief that said object was a small fish they could prey upon.

Combat

Barracudas were lightning-quick predators, attacking any prey that was injured or appeared helpless. Occasionally they would use their speed to launch themselves out of the water and bite at creatures on a boat.

Ecology

Habitats

On the continent of Faerûn, barracudas could be found in the Sea of Fallen Stars. Beyond that continent, they could be found in bodies of salt water in Zakhara.

Outside of the Prime Material plane, barracudas could be found in the domain of Draga Salt-Biter in the Domains of Dread.

Relationships

Some sea mages were known to keep barracudas as familiars.

Subspecies

Dire barracuda:A monstrous variety of the common lamprey.

Magic

Summoning

A small barracuda could be summoned, in a saltwater aquatic environment, by the summon monster I spell, Finally a large barracuda could be summoned by the summon monster III spell..
Those who specialized in sea-based magic could summon 2-8 small barracudas with summon monster I, between 1-6 medium barracudas with the monster summoning II spell, and 1-4 large barracudas with the monster summoning III spell.
