Shallots were a cultivar of onions found in parts of the Realms.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold large shallot bulbs under its "Wizards' Laboratory" section for 5 copper pieces per piece.

Usages

  • Blentra's cider and vinegar oyster sauce, a seafood sauce made at the Platter of Plenty in Marsember, required mincing two shallots. Alternatively, those making the recipe could substitute a handful of scallions.
  • In the Dalelands, depending upon availability, it was quite common to use diced shallots as seasoning on a dish of thinly sliced and roasted medallions of carrot, parsnip, and brussel sprouts.
  • Dragon soup, a long-simmered soup served at the Old Monster Shop in Waterdeep, was cooked with six shallots alongside two cloves of garlic Alternatively, those making the recipe could instead use fourteen hot onions.
  • In the mining settlement of Fireshear, salmon steamed with a shallot-and-wine cream was a specialty.
  • Thaevor potatoes, a dish served at Swordsfall House in Silverymoon, involved thinly slicing two shallots.

Notable Users

  • Heino the traveling gnome bard's specialty dish was a roasted wild rabbit, served with basil and shallots.
  • Maerend Skeldragon, an adventurer and chef who travelled across southeast Faerûn in the 15 century DR. One of his favorite dishes he cooked during these travels was a roasted hraztam in Ulgarth, which he stuffed with shallots and other vegetation he could source locally.
