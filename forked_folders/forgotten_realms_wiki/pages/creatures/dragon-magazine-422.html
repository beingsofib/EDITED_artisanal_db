Dragon issue 422 was released in PDF format only in April of 2013. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Hyrsam, Prince of Satyrs

By Mark Monack, illustrated by Hector Ortiz. p. 4-8

Hyrsam is so ancient that he remembers—and misses—a time before Corellon and the other gods discovered the Feywild.

Eye on the Realms: Barlar's Bucket Helm

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Hector Ortiz. p. 35-38

Barlar Belasko is a man of above-average height, handsomeness, and ego. But an annoyed wizard brought him down a peg.
