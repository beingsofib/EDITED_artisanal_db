Eagles were large, majestic birds of prey that were often seen as symbols of nobility or as divine messengers.

Description

They were generally larger than other birds, with a powerful build and heavier head and beak. They possess broad wings allowing for faster flight. Eagles tended to be around long, with an average wingspan of .

Combat

Eagles hunted their prey from the sky using their enhanced vision, which enabled them to see small animals from great distances away. They captured food with their long and powerful talons.

Species

  • Giant eagle
  • Osprey

Ecology

While eagles could inhabit a variety of terrains, they were more likely to dwell and build their nests on tall trees or high cliffs.

Habitats

Beyond the Prime Material, eagles could be found on the Krigala level of the Beastlands.

Summoning

An eagle could be called upon with the spell Summon nature's ally I,while a celestial eagle could be summoned with Summon monster II, and up to 3 celestial eagle could be summoned with Summon monster III.

In Culture

Eagles were often used in the names of places, families, and organizations. It was also used in spell and item names, such as eagle's splendor.
