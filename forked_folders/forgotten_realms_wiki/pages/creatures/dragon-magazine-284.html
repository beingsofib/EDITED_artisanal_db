Dragon issue 284, 25th Anniversary Issue, was released in June of 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Previews

By Uncredited. p. 16

  • Elminster in Hell
  • Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting 3rd edition sourcebook.
  • Pool of Radiance: Attack on Myth Drannor module.
  • Pool of Radiance: Ruins of Myth Drannor computer game.
  • Servant of the Shard novel.
  • The Shattered Mask novel.
