Dragon issue 67 was released in November of 1982. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

From the Sorcerer's Scroll: New magic-user spells

By Gary Gygax. p. 4-9

34 new spells of levels 1-4.

  • Alarm
  • Armor
  • Bind
  • Cloudburst
  • Deeppockets
  • Flaming Sphere
  • Grease
  • Melf’s Acid Arrow
  • Mount
  • Tasha’s Uncontrollable Hideous Laughter
  • Taunt

Featured Creatures

By Gary Gygax. p. 10–11

All about the Grugach (Wild Elf), Valley Elf, and Cooshee for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

  • Grugach
  • Valley elf
  • Cooshee

Souping up the spider

By Gregg Chamberlain. p. 15–17

Descriptions of 11 different "monster" spiders for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons, based on real-world arachnids.

The Astral Plane

By Gary Gygax and Roger E. Moore. p. 27-36

Rules for adventuring in the Astral Plane, with annotations by Gygax.
