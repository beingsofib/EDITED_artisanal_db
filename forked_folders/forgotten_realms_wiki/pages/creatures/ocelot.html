Ocelots were a species of wild cat that could be found in the Realms.

Ecology

Habitats

On the continent of Faerûn, ocelots could be found in the Shining South in the Ajmer Forest. Beyond Faerûn, they could be found in the land of Maztica.

Trivia

In the land of Maztica, hishnashapers were able to charm ocelots with the cat charm spell.
