Dragon issue 280, Sorcery, was released in February of 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

By Gary Gygax. p. 20

Building, business, character development, and combat.

Coming Soon

By Uncredited. p. 28

  • The Floodgate novel.
  • The Summoning novel.

Countdown to the Forgotten Realms: Deities & Demigods

Uncredited p. 30

A look at the deities and the Unpaladin of the third edition Forgotten Realms.

Your Sorcerous Life

By Robin D. Laws p. 33

Building a background for your sorcerer, complete with past history checklist.

By Johnathan M. Richards p. 39

Some new spells of change, and what to do with them all.

By Jesse Decker and Steve Kenson, illustrated by Brian Snoddy p. 50–53
