A wereape was a type of lycanthrope that could turn into an ape.

Description

These weretypes could be hill giants in their original humanoid form.

History

In 467 DR, a dire wereape known as Dermos the Proud claimed to be the deity of evil lycanthropes, Malar. His following quickly became large, filled with zealous members, who were ordered to kill all Arkaiuns who would oppose them. Him and all members of his beast cult would be killed two years later, in the , at the Ambush of Blackrock Ridge.
