Dragon issue 157, Welcome to the XXV century!, was released in May of 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Where There Is One Sumotori...

By Stewart Wieck. p. 32, 37-39

Stronger than a mountain: the sumo wrestler for AD&D Oriental Adventures games.

...There's Bound to Be Another!

By Len Carpenter. p. 33-37

And an alternate view of the mightiest unarmed fighter of all. Alternate presentation of sumo wrestlers for AD&D.

The Ecology of the Wemic

By J.F. Keeping. p. 48

A scholarly treatise on the Wemic.

The Dungeon Alone

By Tom Little. p. 58

Role-playing with a GM and a single player.

Next Stop: Eveningstar

By Ed Greenwood and Jeff Grubb. p. 64–67, 92

A crossroads village in Cormyr, cut from the Forgotten Realms Adventures book.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 86

New products for May and June.

  • Ronin Challenge module.
  • Storm Riders module.

The Role of Books Review: The Halfling's Gem

By John C. Bunnell. p. 88

The Role of Computers

By HHartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 101

Curse of the Azure Bonds hints.
