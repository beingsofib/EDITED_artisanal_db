Dunchow

Creature
nameDunchow
locationLake Esmel, Amn
terrainFreshwater lakes

The dunchow was a rare fish found only in the waters of Lake Esmel in Amn. Dunchow was served as a special dish at most inns in Esmeltaran and was a delicacy throughout Amn.
