Dragon issue 350, Mighty Magic Mayhem, was released in December of 2006. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Legacies of Ancient Empires

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 50–57

Planetouched races in the Realms. Includes stats for first level warriors, plus info on using them as characters. Azerblood, Celadrin, D'hin'ni, and Worghest.

The Ecology of the Clockwork Horror

By Eric Cagle. p. 58

A scholarly treatise on the Clockwork Horror.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Wizard's Workshop

By James Lafond Sutter, illustrated by Francis Tsai. p. 72–74

Some non-combat items for wizards.

Spellcraft: Chronomancy

By Christopher Wissel, illustrated by John Gravato. p. 76–79

New Chronomancy spells, and notes on time-travelling campaigns.
