Dragon issue 182, 16th Anniversary Issue: Dragons: born of imagination, was released in June of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Vikings' Dragons

By Jean Rabe, illustrated by Jim Holloway. p. 17-23

An overview of the Norse lands' great serpents, part 1.

  • Forest linnorm
  • Land linnorm
  • Sea linnorm
  • Frost linnorm
  • Dread linnorm

The Dragon's Bestiary: Some of the uglier branches of the dragons' family tree

By Gregory W. Detwiler. p. 25–27, 32

Two of the uglier dragonkin.

  • Lindworm
  • Swamp wyrm

The Wild, Wild World of Dice

By Michael J. D'Alfonsi. p. 45

A look at common dice beliefs, habits, and superstitions among gamers.

Kings of the Caravans

By Ed Greenwood. p. 48-53,60

Four unusual travelling merchants of the Forgotten Realms.

  • Llachior Blackthorn
  • Ondul Jarduth
  • Sharest Tanthalar
  • Emrock Uerngul

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 60

Clue Corner: Pools of Darkness.

Ready! Aim! Fire!

By Donald D. Miller. p. 68

All about crossbows, from the tiny hand model to the immense seige.

Psionics–In Living Color!

By Jan Berrien Berends p. 72

Engaging all the senses when describing psionic effects.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 78

New products for June and July.

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sourcebook.
  • Haunted Halls of Eveningstar module.
  • Monstrous Compendium Al-Qadim Appendix sourcebook.
