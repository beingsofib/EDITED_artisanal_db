Whales were the largest mammals in the world.

Types of Whale

Baleen whales

The baleen whales were the larger,

Toothed whales

Toothed whales were active hunters that preyed upon such large creatures as giant squids and krakens, though often relied upon smaller more accessible food. The known species of toothed whales in the Realms included killer whales, narwhals, and sperm whales. All could be found in the Sea of Fallen Stars, with the killer whale being the one most often sighted by sailors and scholars due to their hunting grounds coinciding with some of that sea's more prominent surface trade lanes.

Giant whales

The second largest variety of whale found on Toril. These creatures resembled sperm whales and devoted themselves to protecting the more common species of whales from sea monsters and whalers.

Leviathans

Also known as "Lords of all Whales", these fabled creatures were whales of immeasurable size. They were said to be intermediaries between whales and deities. Only one was said to exist within an ocean on any given world, if at all.

Ecology

Society

Whales were intelligent enough that they had devised their own calendar system, known as Whaletime.

Habitats

Whales inhabited Patzcoatl, the Western Ocean of Maztica. Over height different species of them could be found in the deeper waters of the Sea of Fallen Stars, beyond the northern coasts and the Pirate Isles.

Languages

All whales shared a common language. Referred to as Whalesong, it was a pure-sound language that was difficult for the majority of humans, demihumans, and humanoids to emulate or understand.

Relationships

Whales were sacred to the elven god Deep Sashelas and the deities Istishia and Valkur.

Usages

The bodies of whales were highly valued by surface dwellers for a variety of purposes. Whale oil was used as a type of lamp oil on the western shores of Maztica. that was used for creating various aromatics and perfumes.

In Maztica, whales were hunted by the Metahel tribes that lived along the shores of Patzcoatl.

Summoning

Sea mages could use the spells summon monster VI to summon 1-3 narwhals. With summon monster VIII they could call upon 1 or 2 gigantic whales.
Clerics of Deep Sashelas could summon a couple of whales with their spell Summon cetacean depending on what whales were within when the spell was cast.

History

In the 14 century DR, many scholars in the Heartlands insisted that very few whales existed within the Sea of Fallen Stars due to its relatively small size. Though those who lived within that body of water attested that many could be found within its deeper waters.
