River Tantulph

The River Tantulph flowed from the east out of the Windrise Ports in Laerakond and into the Dragon Sea.

Geography

The River Tantulph ran through the city of Imdolphyn, separating the docks from the upper city. Its delta was known for its plenty of fishing spots.
