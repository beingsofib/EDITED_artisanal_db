White helmets were a species of crabs that dwelled in deep underwater caves of the Sea of Moving Ice in Icewind Dale.

Description

White helmet crabs were a northern subspecies of helmet crabs found in far warmer waters. They were a cold-loving species that thrived in dark underwater caves, as was evident by their white carapace coloration.
