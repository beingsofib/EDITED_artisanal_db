Mussel

Mussels were a variety of simple s found in both freshwater and saltwater bodies, including marshes. They had soft bodies that were protected by a hard shell.

Varieties

Giant mussel: Some mussels could grow to monstrous proportions, becoming giant mussels. Some of these could be found beneath the Trackless Sea.

Lance mussel: A common mussel in the land of Kara-Tur, often found in the Mokan River, that were noted for having a pearly inner shell with long, razor-sharp edges. They were dredged up by the thousands for their meat and the ocassional large pearl, which could sometimes be as big as an eye and coveted for use as false eyes.

Unio: Unusually large freshwater mussels that were drag-netted from rivers. Their shells were commonly used to make gambling tokens.

Uses

Mussels were widely harvested by people across Toril for their meat or sold in open-air markets, such as Skullport's fish market. Some even pried them open with knives and ate them raw. or the spicy Calishite mixture emerald eyes, which had them diced.

The demand for mussel meat was such that some found it profitable to ship them to parts of Faerûn, such as the Ravenian business Ornagar's Smoked Eels & More as this helped them to maintain their shelf life for longer.

Beyond their use as seafood, mussels were boiled alongside other shellfish in alcohol to produce the perfume Sunrise. And some storm giants found use for giant mussel shells as tables, as was done in the fortress Maelstrom.

Ecology

Habitats

In Faerûn these creatures could be found in the Dragonmere, the shallow waters of Kilmaruu Swamp, Lizard Marsh, the Sea of Fallen Stars, and in the waters of Wave Echo Cave. Within Faerûn's Underdark, mussels could be found in Lake Donigarten and possibly the River Sargauth.

In neighboring Zakhara, mussels could be found within the Great Sea. Further across the globe, mussels lived within the streams that crisscrossed the island of Evermeet

Predators

In the wild, mussels were preyed upon by alguduir and starfish.

Notable Users

  • In the Pasocada Basin, mussels were regularly harvested by Metahel and used as trade goods with the Azuposi.

Businesses

  • The Adamantine Mug, a cheap tavern in Athkatla that served sea-salt and spice-flavored snail-mussel pâté.
  • The Blushing Mermaid, an inn and tavern in Baldur's Gate.
  • Copper Coronet, a large tavern in Athkatla, featured mussels in their crudely named harbor stew Sark.
  • The Cutlass, a festhall in Luskan, offered bowls of smoky-flavored mussels wrapped in salty bacon
  • Flying Stag, an inn in Voonlar.
  • Ornagar's Smoked Eels & More, a business in the city of Ravens Bluff that smoked their shellfish like mussels before sealing them in jars to be sent throughout the Vast and as far north as the Moonsea.
  • The Painted Boat, a restaurant in Ravens Bluff, served mussels at a cost of 10 sp.
  • Stargath House, an inn in Purskul that served mussels in their bowls of spicy vegetable broth.
