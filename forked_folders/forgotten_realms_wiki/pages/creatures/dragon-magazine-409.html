Dragon issue 409 was released in PDF format only in March of 2012 as a PDF. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Character Themes: Citizens of Splendor

By Andrew G. Schneider. p. 18

Waterdeep themes: Play a Blackstaff apprentice, a clone of Halaster the Mad Mage, or a Masked Lord of Waterdeep.

Eye on the Realms: The Merendil Gold

By Ed Greenwood. p. 31

Long after the Merendil family was exiled from Cormyr for attempted regicide, thieves and bandits continue to chase rumors of the gold that was left behind.
