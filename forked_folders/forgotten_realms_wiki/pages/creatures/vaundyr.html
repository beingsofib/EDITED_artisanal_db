Vaundyr was a vine that grew in southwestern Faerûn.

Description

The vaundyr vine grew thickly in wet, shady locations. Its leaves were small, green, and waxy.

Availability

The vine could be found growing in Amn, Calimshan, and Tethyr and in the Dragon Coast lands. It was also found as far north as the Blackdagger Ruins, growing on the sides of cliffs and rock faces.

Usage

Alarvaun was an herb made from vaundyr leaves. It was one of the ingredients in spellslayer wine, which was developed in the late 15 century DR.

The vine was a key ingredient of several basic but effective healing draughts and salves, requiring Berronar's cap mushrooms as the other main ingredient.

History

When the forces of Neverwinter attempted to secure the Blackdagger Ruins, Alinda Greenwood sought out Berronar's caps and vaundyr vines to make medicine for them.
