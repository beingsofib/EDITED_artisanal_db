Aberrant yeth hounds were a magically altered species of regular yeth hounds that dwelt in the Ethereal plane. They were created by Gyrion Farseeker, a mad wizard of Neverwinter, who was corrupted by the Spellplague.

Description

Aberrant yeth hounds were infused with the foul influence of the Far Realm, twisting their minds, abilities, and appearance. An aberrant yeth hound was a large canine-like creature with its fur colored blue, splotched with lighter-colored patches. Its eyes glowed with yellow radiance.

History

In the years that followed the Spellplague, in the late 15 century DR, Gyrion Farseeker used yeth hounds he altered with magic for his mad schemes. Some of his hounds managed to retain will and intelligence and rebelled against their cruel master, seeking new, kinder masters among the heroes and adventurers of Neverwinter.
