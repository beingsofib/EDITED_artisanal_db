Arkas

Plant
nameArkas
typeGrass

Arkas was a grass found on the fields of the ranchos in Dambrath.

Uses

This hearty grass was used as feed for local livestock.

Description

Blades of arkas could grow between 3 to 4 ft. (0.9 – 1.2 m) tall.
