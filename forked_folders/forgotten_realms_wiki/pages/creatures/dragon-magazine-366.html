Dragon issue 366 was released in PDF format only in August of 2008. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Mithrendain, Citadel of the Feywild

By Rodney Thompson. Illustrated by Anne Stokes & Rob Alexander. p. 5-16

The eladrin are some of the least understood creatures in the world, yet their culture is as deep and storied as any. Get a glimpse into an eladrin city with Mithrendain.

Wish Upon a Star

By Bruce R. Cordell. p. 17–25

Warlocks are a mysterious bunch, but few are as misunderstood as those of the Star Pact.

Ritually Speaking

By Peter Schaefer. p. 26–33

What caster wouldn’t want more rituals? Dig into this feature and discover a number of new ritual options.

Rose Keep: A Red Wizard Enclave

By Bruce R. Cordell. p. 41–49

This article describes a post-Spellplague Red Wizard enclave.

Gontal

By Bruce R. Cordell and Ed Greenwood. p. 50

This article tells of the previously unexplored region of Gontal.

Design & Development - Forgotten Realms

By Bruce R. Cordell and Philip Athans. p. 78

Phil and Bruce discuss the philosophy behind the Forgotten Realms timeline shift, as well as other critical decisions regarding the new Realms
