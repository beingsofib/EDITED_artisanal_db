Dragon issue 185, At war for a world's survival: Athas, was released in September of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Twenty Tricks for Castle Defense

By Fraser Sherman. p. 26

How to defend a castle against magical attacks.

The Wizards Three: Magic in the Evening

By Ed Greenwood. p. 57–63

A meeting between Elminster of Shadowdale and Mordenkainen of Greyhawk recorded by Ed. Also includes AD&D game stats for spells, magic items, and monsters mentioned in the conversation.
