Dragon issue 412 was released in PDF format only in June of 2012. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Character Theme: The Mariner

By Jeff Morgenroth, illustrated by Ed Cox. p. 2

The freedom of the open sea has great allure to adventurers of all sorts, from dashing corsairs to murderous pirates. What kind of mariner are you?

Channel Divinity: Chauntea and Silvanus

By Chad Brown, illustrated by Claudio Pozas. p. 22-27

Nature is a powerful ally, and the Oak Father and the Earthmother of Faerûn are very giving to those who revere them.

Eye on the Realms: The Thing in the Crypt

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Mark Winters. p. 31–35

Rhauntagar. Before the world ends, many adventurers will learn to fear this name.
