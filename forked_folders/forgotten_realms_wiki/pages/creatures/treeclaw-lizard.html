Treeclaw lizards were a species of lizard endemic to the Khopet-Dag mountains of the Hordelands.

Description

These lizards had mottled brown scales, with a small horn on their noses

Ecology

Diet

Treeclaw lizards fed mainly upon spiders.

Usages

The yaqubi people of Khopet-Dag raised treeclaws as guard animals, though they became unreliable in the rain.

Notable Treeclaws

  • Pech, a treeclaw lizard that was an animal companion of the druid Berun.
