locationFaerun

The fir was a type of tree that could be found in various places throughout Faerun.

Habitats

  • The Ashenwood of Rashemen.
  • The east starwood area of the forest of Cormanthor.
  • Near Tower Reilloch on Evermeet.
  • In interior Faerûn, fir trees could be found in the Winterwood.
  • In the Unapproachable East, fir trees could be found in the higher elevated reaches of the Yuirwood and lined the Rashemen shore of Lake Ashane.
  • Beyond the Prime Material plane, firs could be found in the valleys of Barovia.
