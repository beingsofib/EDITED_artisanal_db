Dragon issue 265, Lost Worlds, was released in November of 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: Wolfgang Baur

By Steve Kenson. p. 28

Feeling paranoid? Well then, designer and editor Wolfgang Baur has the game for you!

Earthshakers

By Jennifer Tittle Stack, illustrated by rk post. p. 34–45

Volcanic spells and monsters.

Primitive PCs

By Lloyd Brown III. p. 48

Monstrous races for your PCs.

Arcane Lore: Savage Spells

By Jeffrey N. Mendoza, illustrated by John Stanko. p. 72–78

Wizardry from the wilderness.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Jungle Magic

By Jeffrey N. Mendoza, illustrated by Jim Crabtree. p. 80–85

To native and intruder alike, these enchanted prizes are jungle magic.

Role Models: Pop-Up Terrain

By JD Wiker and Jim Bishop. p. 88

Building 3D terrain.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 108

New products for November, December and January.

  • Beyond the High Road novel.
  • Drizzt Do'Urden's Guide to the Underdark sourcebook.
  • The Icewind Dale Trilogy Collector's Edition novel.
  • The Temptation of Elminster novel.
