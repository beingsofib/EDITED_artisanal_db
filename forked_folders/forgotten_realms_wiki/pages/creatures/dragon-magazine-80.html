Dragon issue 80 was released in December of 1983. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The psychology of the Doppelganger

By Fraser Sherman. p. 6-8

An inside look at the insidious, telepathic shape-shifters.

How many coins in a coffer? Don't forget, all that treasure takes up space

By David F. Godwin. p. 9

A method to determine the size of coins and how many you can fit in different containers.

Five keys to DMing success: Make it easy on yourself and fun for your players

By Mike Beeman. p. 14

Tips on building a good campaign: continuity, character, competence, creativity, and cooperation.

Treasures rare and wondrous

By Ed Greenwood. p. 30

A random table of unique treasures to liven up your Advanced Dungeons & Dragons game.

Leomund's Tiny Hut: New charts, using the '5% principle'

By Lenard Lakofka. p. 48

Revised charts for attacks, saving throws, and experience point values for monsters.
