Dragon issue 253, Enchantments, was released in November of 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Wyrm's Turn: Little Heroes

By Dave Gross. p. 4

Miniatures.

Spontaneous Enchantments

By Lloyd Brown III. p. 28

Making magical items without all the messy spell-casting.

Random Magical Weapon Generator

By Gregory W. Detwiler. p. 34

Working Class Wizards

By Lloyd Brown III. p. 48

What jobs can wizards do?

Wyrms of the North: Raulothim, the Silent Shadow

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Storn Cook. p. 82–86

Raulothim, an emerald dragon of the northern Sword Coast.

Arcane Lore: The Mage's Secret

By Lloyd Brown III, illustrated by John Stanko. p. 88–93

Divination spells.
