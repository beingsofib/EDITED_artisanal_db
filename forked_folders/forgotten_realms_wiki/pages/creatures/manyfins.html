Manyfins was a name given to a type of fish found in the Vast in north Faerûn.

Description

It was very small in size, characterized by its number of fins, and had a sharp taste.

Ecology

They were called "manyfins" by Melicent, Meliors, and Melusine Mellicot. They stocked Swords Pool with manyfins and trout around 1370 DR. Although they discouraged fishing, some people did it anyway on dark nights.
