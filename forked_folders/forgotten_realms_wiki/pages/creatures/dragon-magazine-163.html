Dragon issue 163, Magic is to dream, was released in November of 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Hedge Wizards

By Gregg Sharp. p. 18

A look a low-level mages running businesses and providing services to their community.

Magic Gone Haywire

By Rich Stump. p. 26

Adding quirks and side-effects to standard magic items.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 48

Review of Secret of the Silver Blades.

A Hoard for the Horde

By David "Zeb" Cook. p. 56

Four new monsters from the Horde campaign and novel trilogy.

  • Dzalmaus Dragon
  • Manni
  • Morin
  • Sand Cat

Making Law Out of Chaos

By J.R. Renaud. p. 74

Analyzing the purposes and traits of the nine alignments.

Professional Monsters

By Matthew Schutt. p. 80–81

Analyzing the purposes and traits of the nine alignments.

The Dragon's Bestiary: A weaver of spells and disaster

By Ed O'Connell. p. 82–83

Get a job - for your NPC centaurs, orcs, and lizard men! Centaur cavaliers, orc barbarians, and lizard-man druids.

  • Spell weaver

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 85

New products for November and December.

  • Blood Charge module.
  • Dwarves Deep sourcebook.
  • Exile novel.
  • Halls of the High King module.
