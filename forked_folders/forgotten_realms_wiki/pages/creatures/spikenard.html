Spikenard

Plant
nameSpikenard
usageInk for scrolls of write

Spikenard was a type of plant found in the Realms.

Usages

  • The recipe for the ink needed to write a scroll of the spell Write required boiling spikenard alongside mistletoe and owl eye.
  • Certain conjuration spells could be aided by a tailored infusion of fenugreek.
