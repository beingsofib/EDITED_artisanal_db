Dragon issue 356, Dragons, was released in June of 2007. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Savage Tidings: Into the Abyss

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 66

About the Planes, and a listing of items you can buy.

Volo's Guide: Wyrms of the West, East, and South

By Brian Cortijo. p. 72-75

As an adjunct to Ed Greenwood's old Wyrms of the North column, some dragons from other places.

  • Acuakvacaesin
  • Rhyaexthadarsh
  • Svernvignarngix

Class Acts: Adventurer: Bard Guide

By Amber E. Scott. p. 86-87

A listing of bard rules.
