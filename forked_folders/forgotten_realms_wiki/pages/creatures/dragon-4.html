Dragon+ 4

Dragon+ issue 4 was released on October 16, 2015. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Discovering the Gold Box

By Susan Manley pp. 5

A look at several classic Realms video games.

  • Curse of the Azure Bonds
  • Eye of the Beholder
  • Eye of the Beholder II: The Legend of Darkmoon
  • Menzoberranzan
  • Neverwinter Nights (AOL game)
  • Pool of Radiance
  • Secret of the Silver Blades

Winning Races: Duergar

By Jay Turner pp. 7

A short article on duergar.

Travel Talk: Dare to Descend

By Melisande Calador pp. 8

An in-universe travelogue of the Underdark, penned by the scribe Melisande Calador.

Neverwinter: Strongholds

By Matt Chapman pp. 11

An interview with the developers of the Neverwinter game about their Strongholds expansion.

Demonomicon of Iggwilv: Belaphoss

By Matt Chapman. pp. 13

Information and game stats for the demon lord Belaphoss, featured in the game Sword Coast Legends.

The Thweem

By Adam Lee. pp. 15

A short fiction piece by Adam Lee.

Interview: Jay Turner

By Matt Chapman. pp. 16

An interview with Jay Turner, narrative designer on Sword Coast Legends.

Beyond the Board: An Introduction to Dice Masters

By Matt Chapman. pp. 19

A preview of the Dice Masters game Battle for Faerûn.

Tavern Tales: Adventurers League

By Greg Bilsland pp. 22

A look at the Rage of Demons storyline for the Adventurers League, and a free copy of the adventure Shackles of Blood.
