Dragon issue 312, Evil Classes, was released in October of 2003. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Among the Dead: Necromancer Archetypes and Abilities

By Mike Mearls. p. 28–38

Specialized necromancers focus their abilities; whether on consuming your soul or warping your flesh.

Holier than Thou: Celestial Monsters, Part I

By Christopher Perkins. p. 52-61

Making celestial monster characters.

Faiths of Faerûn: Prayers of the Frostmaiden

By Thomas M. Costa. p. 62–65

This article outlines the spells of the goddess Auril.

  • Auril's flowers
  • Cold fire
  • Cold snap
  • Corona of cold
  • Freeze
  • Frostbite
  • Frost breath
  • Heat leech
  • Ice axe
  • Ice gauntlet
  • Mass snowshoes
  • Snowshoes

Ecology of the Drider

By Paul Leach. p. 76-80

The Drider: Outcasts among the drow.

Rogues Gallery: The Heroes of the War of the Spider Queen, Part 2

By Richard Baker. p. 82

This article is a continuation of "Rogues Gallery: The Heroes of the War of the Spider Queen," as published in Dragon #302, and details the characters from the novel Condemnation:

  • Dyrr
  • Halisstra Melarn
  • Valas Hune
