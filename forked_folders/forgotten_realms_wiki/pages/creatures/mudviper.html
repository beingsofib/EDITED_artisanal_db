Mudviper

Creature
nameMudviper
terrainMarsh

A mud viper was a creature that dwelt in the Marsh of Chelimber.

History

In 1374 DR, the lizardman Sleyvas claimed that these creatures spoke to him. They apparently told him that the three adventurers, Kromlech Bruenghor, Vahn, and Adrianna, would enter the Marsh of Chelimber. As he later deceived the adventurers, it is possible that his claims were not true.
