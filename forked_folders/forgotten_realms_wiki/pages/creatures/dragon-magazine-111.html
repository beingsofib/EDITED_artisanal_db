Dragon issue 111 was released in July of 1986. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Good stuff, for a spell

By John M. Maxstadt. p. 8

A new type of magic item which is powered by the user's spell slots.

No campaign ever fails

By Joel E. Roosa. p. 22

How to identify an out-of-control campaign, and what to do to tame it.

TSR Profiles: Jeff Grubb

By Uncredited. p. 64

Profile of author and designer Jeff Grubb.
