Tongue of madness

Fungus
nameTongue of Madness
typeFungus
locationThe Underdark
distinctionsResembled a large human tongue

Tongue of madness was a fungus that grew in the Underdark. When mature, it resembled a large human tongue and was edible but somewhat poisonous. When consumed, any creature with the power of speech who did not have sufficient constitution to resist the mind-altering effects was compelled to speak his or her every thought for the next hour. This compulsion could be cured by a lesser restoration or similar spell.
