Werewolves were a type of lycanthrope who took the form of wolves at the full moon. Werewolves were the most common type of lycanthropes on Toril. They were found in all climates, both in the wild and hidden in civilization. Werewolf packs were organized similar to wolf packs, with a powerful alpha in command of the entire group. The moral and ethical outlook of the pack tended to follow that of the alpha.

Abilities

Werewolves were vulnerable to silver or magic weapons and could instantly heal most wounds not inflicted by such weapons. Infected werewolves transformed during the full moon involuntarily. Members of their race that were born with the curse could infect others and could control their shifting.

History

Selûne had a group of non-evil werewolves that worship her, though most lycanthropes were drawn towards the worship of Malar.

Notable Werewolves

  • Talbot Uskevren: the second son of a high profile Sembian noble, Thamalon Uskevren
  • Feena Archwood: A cleric of Selûne and the daughter of Rusk and Maleva
  • Rusk: A cleric of Malar and leader of a pack of werewolves in the Arch Wood
  • Maleva: Feena's mother, also a cleric of Selûne.
  • Sorcia: An elven werewolf, also a member of Rusk's pack
  • Darrow: A human werewolf that formerly worked for Stannis Malveen
  • Karnek, Morrel, and Brigid: Human werewolves in Rusk's pack
  • Bidderdoo Harpell: a member of the eccentric wizard clan, the Harpells
  • Urtos Phylund II: patriarch of the Phylund noble family of Waterdeep
  • Arnell Hallowleaf: a worshipper of Sêlune and father of Shadowheart
