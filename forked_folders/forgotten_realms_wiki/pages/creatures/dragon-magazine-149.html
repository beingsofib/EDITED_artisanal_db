Dragon issue 149. Treasures bright, rare, and wonderful, was released in September of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Dragon's Bestiary: Not quite horses--but perhaps better

By Kurt Martin. p. 22–26

5 equine creatures.

  • Av
  • Bahtel
  • Kiita
  • Vor
  • Wandega

Getting It Right the First Time

By Richard W. Emerich. p. 56

Don't be caught with your DM's screen down! Run your convention RPG event like a pro.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 76

Review of Curse of the Azure Bonds.
