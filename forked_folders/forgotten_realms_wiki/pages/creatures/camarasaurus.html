Camarasauruses were a type of herbivorous, quadruped dinosaur.

Behavior

These creatures were known to panic easily, especially in comparison to apatosauruses.

Ecology

These creatures were herd animals.

Habitats

They were typically found to inhabit marshes and swamps.
