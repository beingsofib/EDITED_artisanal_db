Dragon issue 208, SPELLFIRE game variant rules, was released in August of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Right from the Start

By Eric Noah, illustrated by Larry Elmore. p. 13–18

A seven-step approach to starting a campaign.

Get Motivated!

By Gregory Gliedman, illustrated by Brian Durfee. p. 26–27

Another approach to creating 'realistic' character background based on their motivations and goals.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Magical Items for Your Favorite Horse

By Gordon R. Menzies, illustrated by Erik Olson. p. 29–32

A plethora of equine related magic.

Behind the Scenes

By Dori Hein, illustrated by Dana Knutson. p. 42–46

The making of the Planescape setting.

Eye of the Monitor

By Sandy Petersen. p. 64–65

Game tips: Al-Qadim: The Genie's Curse.

Defend the Realms!

By Allen Varney. p. 69–72

Variant rules for the Spellfire CCG.

Through the Looking Glass

By Ken Carpenter. p. 121–122

Ogres of Dragonspear and Szass Tam figures reviewed.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 128

New products for August and September.

  • City of Splendors boxed set.
  • Corsairs of the Great Sea module.
  • Siege of Darkness novel.
