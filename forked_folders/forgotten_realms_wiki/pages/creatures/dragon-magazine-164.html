Dragon issue 164, Travel broadens the mind, if you survive it, was released in December of 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bonds of Brotherhood

By Tom Griffith. p. 18

Placing monks in their proper environment.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 55

Clue Corner: Curse of the Azure Bonds.

Arcane Lore: Pages From the Mages, Part VI

By Ed Greenwood. p. 59-63

More famous Realms spell books.

The Book of Shangalar the Black:

  • Bone Blade
  • Bone Javelin
  • Negative Plane Protection
  • Repel Undead

The Glandar's Grimoire:

  • Disruption
  • Fellblade
  • Immunity to Undeath
  • Melisander's Harp

The Tome of the Wyvernwater Circle:

  • Flame Shield
  • Mold Touch
  • Touchsickle
  • Wailing Wind

The Hand of Helm:

  • Exaltation
  • Forceward
  • Mace of Odo
  • Seeking Sword

The Mechanics of the Iron Cobra

By Theresa Hickey. p. 72–78

A devoted machine always follows its last order, no matter who gives it.

What Has He Got in His Pocketses, Anyway?

By Ed Greenwood. p. 80

The treasure hook table.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 86

New products for December and January.

  • Blood Charge module.
  • Crusade novel.
  • Exile novel.
  • Ninja Wars module.

Three Book, Three Writers, One Great Trilogy

By Theresa Hickey. p. 90

On the Empires Trilogy.
