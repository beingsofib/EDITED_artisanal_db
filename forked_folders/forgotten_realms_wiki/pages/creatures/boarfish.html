Boarfish was a species of deadly poisonous fish, if consumed uncooked, found in the Nelanther Isles of the Sea of Swords.

Description

Boarfish received its name from the fact that its cooked flesh tasted a lot like bacon.

Ecology

Boarfish was considered a snack prepared in the wilderness, rather than a full dish. When preparing, the fish was halved crosswise and grilled up over hot coals, allowing it to simmer in its own juices, until the skin was blistered and began to fall off. It was served with a sauce of sourmilk, mint, and cucumber. According to Ed of the Greenwood, cooked boarfish tasted like "Spahamm."
