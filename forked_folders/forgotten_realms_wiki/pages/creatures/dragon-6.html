Dragon+ 6

Dragon+ issue 6 was released on February 18, 2016. This Issue dedicated to the memory of Wayne England. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Jim Zub: Butt-Kicking for Comic Goodness

By Matt Chapman. pp. 4

An interview with Jim Zub, author of the comic series Legends of Baldur's Gate and Shadows of the Vampire. Also included is a free copy of Legends of Baldur's Gate issue 1.

Travel Talk: Volo's Visit to Barovia

By Ed Greenwood, Cartography by Mike Schley. pp. 7

A Volo travelogue of his journey to Barovia in the Demiplane of Dread, written by Ed Greenwood.

Top 6 Warlock Features in Sword Coast Legends

By Matt Chapman. pp. 8

A look at the Rage of Demons downloadable content pack for Sword Coast Legends.

Finding the Fun: D&D Board Games

By Matt Chapman. pp. 11

An article on the design of various Dungeons & Dragons board games, including Realms games such as Tyrants of the Underdark and Lords of Waterdeep.
