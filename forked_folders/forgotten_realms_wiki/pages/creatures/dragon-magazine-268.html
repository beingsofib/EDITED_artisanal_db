Dragon issue 268, Unearthed Arcana, was released in February of 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: Bruce R. Cordell

By Steve Kenson. pp. 27

Author Bruce R. Cordell relies on his background as a wrestler and debater when making adventures.

The Armory Annex: New AD&D Game Weapons

By Ryan Staake. pp. 42–44

This article provides statistics for several exotic weapons from the real world, some of which are found in the Forgotten Realms.

Secrets of the Brotherhood of the True Flame

By Paul Fraser, illustrated by Stephen Daniele. pp. 50–54

This article describes the Brotherhood of the True Flame, a secret society of Zakhara.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Power at a Price

By Simon Horton, illustrated by Shawn Sharp. pp. 74–79

Magic items with drawbacks.

The Dragon's Bestiary: The Lizard Folk

By Michael Kuciak, illustrated by Pete Venters. pp. 80–87

This article discusses lizardfolk and suggests new varieties of subspecies.

  • Agrutha
  • Crocodilian
  • Geckonid
  • Iguanid
  • Varanid

The New Adventures of Volo: Lost Princess Road

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Ron Spencer. pp. 88–91

Volo describes the mysteries of the Lost Princess Road.
