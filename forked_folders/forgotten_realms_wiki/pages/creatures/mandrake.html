Mandrake

Plant
nameMandrake
othernamesWitchroot
usageAnesthetic, material component

Mandrakes, also known as witchroots, were a type of plant found in the Realms.

Powers

Mandrakes could be used as an anesthetic.

Habitats

Beyond the Prime Material, mandrakes could be found on the plane of Ysgard.

Usages

  • A complicated extract of mandrake roots was used by fensir to brew a special restorative potion that could reverse petrification.
  • The roots of a mandrake were one of the ingredients for potions of skeleton control.
  • The roots of a mandrake were required to construct a rod of resurrection, which would be wrapped over its bound amethyst.
  • The roots of a mandrake were needed as a material component to cast the spells cacodemon, create homunculus, and ghost gauntlet.
