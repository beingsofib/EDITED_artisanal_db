Dragon issue 246, Henchmen, was released in April 1998. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Wizard's Companion: The Care and Feeding of Homonculi

By Lloyd Brown III, illustrated by Susan Van Camp. p. 24

Wizards are loners by nature. Whether young or old, they spend their time studying to become more powerful, learning better spells, and enchanting potent items. They rarely have time for making close friends.

  • Homunculus
