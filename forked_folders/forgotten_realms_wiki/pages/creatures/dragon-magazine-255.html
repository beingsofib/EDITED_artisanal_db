Dragon issue 255, Psionics, was released in January of 1999. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The New Illithid Arsenal

By Christopher M. Schwartz p. 28-34

New psionic items.

Psi-Kits

By Lloyd Brown III and Owen K.C. Stephens p. 37

New psionicist kits.

The Shadow Way

By Samuel M. Wright p. 50

Combining psionics and ninjutsu.

Candlekeep

By Chris Avellone p. 54-58

The locale from Baldur's Gate.

Dark Legacy

By Mel Odom p. 67-77

A prelude to The Threat from the Sea.

PC Portraits: Psionicists

By Bradley K. McDevitt p. 81

Psionic character pictures.

Rogues Gallery: The Threat from the Sea

By Mel Odom p. 82–88

Characters from the Realms event trilogy.

Wyrms of the North: Thalagyrt, Old Lord Memory

By Ed Greenwood p. 92-96

Thalagyrt, a mist dragon from the Sea of Swords.

Index

Chultan tree slug

ProFiles: Mel Odom

By Steve Kenson. p. 120

About Forgotten Realms author, Mel Odom.
