Ludluth Street

Ludluth Street was a road found within the city of Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan.

Location

Ludluth Street ran through northwest Suldolphor, originating in the northwest at the exterior city wall and passing southeast toward the center of the city.

Notable Locations

Emerald Dragon Way branched south from Ludluth Street's midpoint. The Griffonpost House Inn lay along the south side of Ludluth Sreet.
