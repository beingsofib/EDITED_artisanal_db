Dragon issue 406 was released in PDF format only in December of 2011. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Paragons of Fey Valor

By Claudio Pozas, illustrated by Claudio Pozas. p. 12–16

Out of the Feywild come three new paragon paths for heroes.

Secrets of the Fey: A New Pact for the Binder Warlock

By Rodney Thompson, illustrated by Jason Juta. pp. 17–21

Dark fey emerge from their Unseelie demesnes to tempt warlock binders, granting them the power to bewitch minds and summon sprites, satyrs, and displacer beasts.

Eye on the Realms: Xraunrarr Shall Triumph

By Ed Greenwood. p. 35-40

Descend into the depths of the Underdark to unearth the secrets of Xraunrarr, the beholder empire.
