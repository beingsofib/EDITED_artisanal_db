Tombworms were a species of edible worms found in the Dalelands.

Ecology

Tombworms inhabited foundations of castles and were gathered for culinary purposes. A dish simply called worms in sauce was a bed of rice topped with seared green pepper-like vegetable and still-wriggling tombworms drowned in brown sauce. The worms had a taste akin to roasted almonds, "but better."
