The Thayan blackwood was a type of tree unique to the plateau of Thay in eastern Faerûn.

Usage

These trees were harvested for use in the crafting of furniture and sculptures that were quite popular among wealthy collectors across Faerûn. They grew naturally and were also cultivated from orchards within the nation's many slave plantations.
