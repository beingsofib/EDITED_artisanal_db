Dragon issue 93 was released in January of 1985. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Life beyond 15th level

By Gary Gygax. pp. 8-9

Rules for very powerful druids called hierophants for 1st edition of Advanced Dungeons & Dragons, and a word about the role of druidesses.

Thinking For Yourself

By Gary Gygax. p. 10

A polemic against the anti-roleplaying crowd.

The Ecology of the Eye of the deep

By Ed Greenwood. pp. 18-19

A detailed look at the eye of the deep, an aquatic relative of the beholder.

Short Hops and Big Drops

By Stephen Inniss. p. 21

Rules for jumping.

Ay pronunseeAYshun gyd

By Frank Mentzer. pp. 24-30

A large list of proper pronunciations of D&D names.
