Dragon issue 352, China Miéville Super Spectacular, was released in February of 2007. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ecology of the Yrthak

By Eric Roelofs. p. 64-68

A scholarly treatise on the Yrthak.

Volo's Guide to the Forgotten Dead

By Brian Cortijo and Ed Greenwood. p. 78-81

3 undead of the Realms.

  • Charnel custodian
  • Inquisitor
  • Skuz

Class Acts: Adventurer: Criminal Specialists

By Hal Maclean. p. 92

Rogues who specialize in a particular crime.

Class Acts: Arcane: Sorcerer Guide

By Amber E. Scott. p. 94

All the rules you need to know to run a sorcerer in one place.
