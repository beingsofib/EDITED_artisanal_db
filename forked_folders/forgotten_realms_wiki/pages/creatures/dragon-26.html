Dragon+ 26

Dragon+ issue 26 was released on June 18, 2019. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Imagining the Ampersand

By Matt Chapman. pp. 4

Rogue-loving artist Fiona Staples steals our hearts with a double-rainbow as she discusses the heaven and hell images she's crafted for Baldur's Gate: Descent into Avernus.

Previews: In the Works

By Bart Carroll and Matt Chapman. pp. 6

The Dragon+ team held council at the end of 2018, and decreed that each issue would continue to offer early looks at products in development. And so, let's see what's in the works...

  • Baldur's Gate: Descent into Avernus: The next major Dungeons & Dragons story-line takes a Hellish turn.
  • Dungeons & Dragons Essentials Kit: The D&D Essential Kit boxed set contains everything you need to run a D&D game.
  • Dungeon Mayhem: Battle for Baldur's Gate: The first expansion to this wildly popular, easy-to-learn, family-friendly D&D card game comes later this fall.
  • Warriors of Waterdeep: Warriors of Waterdeep is a tactical dungeon adventure where players assemble a team of heroes to fight deadly enemies across the Forgotten Realms.
  • Infernal Tides: If you thought Minsc and the characters from the popular D&D comic were about to miss out on a trip to the Nine Hells, you are sorely mistaken.
  • Baldur's Gate III: Larian Studios places the fate of the Forgotten Realms in your hands.

D&D Live 2019: The Descent

By Matt Chapman. pp. 7

Welcome to D&D Summer Camp 2019.

The Experience of Youth

By Matt Chapman. pp. 12

Jim Zub offers a sneak peak inside the D&D Young Adventures Guides, before Chris Perkins lifts the lid on the Dungeons & Dragons Essentials Kit.

Video & Audio Highlights

By Matt Chapman. pp. 16

We expertly captured all five live games from the main stage of D&D Live 2019: The Descent.
