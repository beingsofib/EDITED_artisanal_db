Dragon issue 100 was released in August of 1985. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

All about the druid/ranger: A classy explanation, better late than never

By Frank Mentzer. p. 9

A long-winded explanation for why the "illegal" druid/ranger class combination works.

Pages from the Mages V: From Elminster, to Ed, to you — more lore

By Ed Greenwood. p. 12–20

New spellbooks and spells.

  • Sabirine's Specular
  • Glanvyl's Workbook
  • The Red Book of War
  • The Alcaister
