Dragon issue 211, An Underground Extravaganza, was released in November of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

First Quest: Mammals and Dinosaurs

By Jeff Grubb. pp. 8-22

Jeff Grubb's early experiences with role-playing games, some of which influenced the Forgotten Realms.

Sight in the Darkness

By Roger E. Moore. pp. 16-22

An in-depth look at infravision and its uses in a campaign.

Fungi of the Underdark

By Chris Perry, illustrated by Terry Dykstra. pp. 24-26

More grows in the Underdark than just dwarves and drow.

  • Askume
  • Nimergan
  • Ormu
  • Timmask
  • Trillimac

"I Sing a Song by the Deep-Water Bay"

By Steven E. Schend. pp. 29-34

Laeral Silverhand shares knowledge about the Harpers within Waterdeep with Steven E. Schend.
