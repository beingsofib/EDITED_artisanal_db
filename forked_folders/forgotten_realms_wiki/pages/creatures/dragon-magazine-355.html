Dragon issue 355 was released in May of 2007. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Seven Saintly Domains

By Hal Maclean. p. 24-30

New domains for cleric spells.

  • Charity domain
  • Chastity domain
  • Generosity domain
  • Humility domain
  • Patience domain
  • Temperance domain
  • Zeal domain

Creature Catalog VI

By Various Authors. p. 32–57

A dozen more monsters, old and new, including cave fisher and scarecrow.

Black beast of bedlam • Cave fisher • Giant dragonfly • Dungeon phantom • Rot giant • Cannon golem • Maedar • Obliviax • Scarecrow • Seedroach • Giant slug • Springheel

The Ecology of the Devourer

By Owen K.C. Stephens. p. 58-63

A scholarly treatise on the Devourer.

Volo's Guide: Demon Cults of the Realms

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 70-72

Cults of Baphomet, Dagon, and Graz'zt.

Class Acts: Divine: Druid Guide

By Amber E. Scott. p. 88-89

All your druid charts and rules.

Class Acts: Psionic: Power by Discipline

By Scott L. Gable. p. 90-91

A listing of all the psionic powers.
