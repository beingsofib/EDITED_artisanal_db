Fawn of Shiallia

Fawns of Shiallia were celestial, youthful deer and servitors of the goddess Shiallia. She sometimes had them manifest on the Prime Material as a token of her favor.

Description

These creatures had the appearance of youthful deer, sported green–hued coats of fur, and were surrounded by small motes of light. They were sometimes encountered with neck adornments such as bells and bows.

Behavior

These creatures had a gentle nature to them.

Abilities

The fawns of Shiallia emitted a magical aura that would heal the injuries and wounds of friendly creatures within their proximity.

History

Sometime in the late 15 century DR, at the festival of Simril, a number of adventurers in the Sword Coast were bestowed a fawn of Shiallia as a companion.
