Dragon issue 377 was released in PDF format only in July of 2009. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Tu'narath, City of Death

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 16–28

The githyanki city has been profiled before, but visit it for the first time in 4th Edition. This article supports the Scales of War Adventure Path.

Explore Airspur

By Matthew Sernett. p. 61-69

Airspur, city of genasi, is one of the Forgotten Realms' newest. Learn more about Airspur in the first of a multi-part series on the city.

Class Acts (4e): Ranger — Masters of the Verdant Silence

By Bruce R. Cordell. p. 70

The ranger is one of the most popular strikers in the game. Get some new options for your two-weapon fighting ranger, courtesy of the Verdant Silence organization.

Design & Development: Psionics

By Mike Mearls and Stephen Schubert. p. 73

Learn about the goals and design challenges involved with 4th Edition's newest power source.
