Dragon issue 158, Dragons are not to be toyed with, was released in June of 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

A Spell of Conversation

By Ed Friedlander, illustrated by Jim Holloway. p. 18–19

If you'd rather talk with a dragon than fight it, use this spell. Details the shugenja spell Speak with dragonkind.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Two almost-but-not-quite dragons

By Spike Y. Jones and Steve Fetsch. p. 20–22

The Gorynych and the common Dragonet.

Also Known As...the Orc

By Ethan Ham, illustrated by Jim Holloway and Daniel Horne. p. 30–34

Alternate names for common D&D monsters. Includes descriptions and stats for new subraces.

The Rules of the Game

By Thomas M. Kane. p. 36

Introducing new players to RPGs.

A Role-player's Best Friend

By Michael J. D'Alfonsi. p. 45

How to use a computer to enhance your gaming experience.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley, Patricia and Kirk Lesser. p. 53

Clue Corner: Curse of the Azure Bonds and Pool of Radiance. .

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 86

New products for July.
