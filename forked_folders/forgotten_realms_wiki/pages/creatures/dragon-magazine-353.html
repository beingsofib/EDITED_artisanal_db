Dragon issue 353, The Planes, was released in March of 2007. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Demonomicon of Iggwilv: Malcanthet: Queen of the Succubi

By James Jacobs. pp. 22-35

Malcanthet, her goals, her cultists, and more.

Multiple Dementia: A Guide to the Demiplanes

By Amber Stewart with Oliver Diaz, illustrated by Julie Dillon. pp. 36–42

Any of those thirty demiplanes might be just beyond the next portal.

The Archomentals, Part II: Princes of Elemental Good

By Eric Jansing and Kevin Baase, illustrated by Andrew Hou. pp. 43–51

Good counterparts to the evil archomentals: Ben-hadar, Chan, Sunnis, and Zaaman Rul.

Volo's Guide: Outsiders of the Forgotten Realms

By Thomas M. Costa. pp. 64-72

Some of the divine creatures of the Realms.
