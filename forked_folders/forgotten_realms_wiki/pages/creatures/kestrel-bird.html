A kestrel was a type of bird and one of several distinct species of falcon.

Description

Some, though not all, species sported a blue-crest.

Ecology

Diet

Kestrels were a predatory bird, often feasting upon various species of rodents, rabbits, and voles.

Habitats

The blue-crested variety of kestrels inhabited the continent of Kara-Tur.

In north Faerûn, kestrels could be found year-round in the land of Thar.

Beyond the Prime Material, kestrels could be found alongside many other predatory birds as part of the hunting parties of Remnis.

Trivia

  • The T'u Lung city of Ha'chou sported a blue-crested kestrel on its city banner alongside an owl and hawk.
  • Circa 1358 DR there was an adventuring company named for the birds, known as the Men of the Red Kestrel.
