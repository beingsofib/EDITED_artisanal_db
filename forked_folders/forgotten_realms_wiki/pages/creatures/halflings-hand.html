Halfling's hand

Halfling's hand, or oxhrel was a species of aquatic plant found in Faerûn.

Description

Oxhrel resembled a lilypad plant grew in freshwater. The plant could be distilled into a special liquid that, when mixed with dried and powdered horseradish, created a poison that caused violent convulsions when introduced into bloodstream.
