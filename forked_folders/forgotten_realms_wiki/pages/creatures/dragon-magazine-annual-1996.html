Dragon Annual 1 was published by TSR, Inc. in December of 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Ecology of the Wyvern

By Spike Y. Jones, Illustrated by Scott Rosema. p. 8-12

A scholarly treatise on the Wyvern.

Arcane Lore: Scrolls of the Ice Mage

By Anne W. Davis, Illustrated by Storn Cook. p. 26-30

Frosty new spells for all wizards.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Predators of the Underdark

By Keith Strohm, Illustrated by James Holloway. p. 35–43

The reason you should be afraid of the dark.

  • Chromatic mold
  • Elghonn
  • Gohlbrorn
  • Lukhorn
  • Varkha

Rogues Gallery: In Silver Shadows

By Elaine Cunningham. p. 41-43

From the pages of Silver Shadows come three new heroes.

  • Allain Kevanarial
  • Ferret
  • Hasheth Balik

Campaign Classics: The Scro

By Roger E. Moore, Illustrated by Mark Nelson. p. 44-52

The Chosen of Dukagsh are poised to invade worlds in any crystal sphere. A Spelljammer Monster for Groundling Campaigns.

Forgotten Realms introduction

By Ed Greenwood. p. 53

The new kingdom of the north deserves a grand palace.

Gem of the North: The high palace of Alustriel

By Steven Schend, Illustrated by Lorelle Ahlstrom. p. 54-60

The new kingdom of the north deserves a grand palace.

Deckbuilding Revisited

By Jim Butler. p. 109-112

Tuning your Spellfire deck for competition or just for fun.
