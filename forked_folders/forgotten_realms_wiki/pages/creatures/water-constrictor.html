Water constrictor

Creature
nameWater Constrictor
locationKelvin's Cairn

Water constrictors were a species of constrictor snake that could be found in the waters of Icewind Dale.

Descriptions

These serpents were monstrously long.

Combat

A water constrictor attacked by, as its name would suggest, constricting its opponent with a force strong enough to bring down even a verbeeg.

History

In the aftermath of Drizzt Do'Urden and Wulfgar's attack on Biggrin's secret base in Kelvin's Cairn in 1356 DR, a fleeing verbeeg was led into a mountain pool by Guenhwyvar, where the giant was attacked and drowned by a water constrictor lying in wait.
