Leatherjacket

Creature
nameLeatherjacket
locationChult

Leatherjackets were the larval form of the common Chultan cranefly.

Ecology

Leatherjackets were eaten by the freshwater fish of Chult's rivers.

Uses

As of the late 15 century DR, they were a popular bait for fishing, notably among those brave or stupid enough to fish in the River Soshenstar. Leatherjacket bait sold for eight copper pieces.
