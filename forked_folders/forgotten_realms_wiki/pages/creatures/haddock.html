Haddock was a species of edible saltwater fish that was often confused with a cheaper fish – walleye pollock.

Varieties

  • Northern haddock: a subspecies of haddock found in the far northern Sea of Moving Ice, commonly fished by the villages and towns in Icewind Dale and Frozenfar. Northern haddock was an important export out of the region, traditionally smoked, but also battered and fried in the Ten-Towns, as an imitation of southern dishes.

History

In the , the Baldur's Mouth Gazette published an article on the excess of haddock catch and recommended it to be cooked into fishcakes.
