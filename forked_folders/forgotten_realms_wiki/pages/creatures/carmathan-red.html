The Carmathan Red, also called the Carmathan Horse-Ox, was a breed of horse found in Carmathan in the Bloodstone Lands.

Description

The breed of horse was renowned for its strength and durability. They were favored for use as plowhorses.

History

Carmathan Reds were bred and sold exclusively in the duchy of Carmathan in Damara, usually during Ravensburg's horse market each spring. Their popularity and use had spread throughout the Bloodstone Lands as of the mid–14 century DR.
