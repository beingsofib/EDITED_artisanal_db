Dragon issue 206, 18th Anniversary Issue: The Incredible Majesty of Dragons, was released in June of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Dragons: More than Just Couch Potatoes

By Thomas Reid. p. 11

Using dragons as long-term foes.

Part Dragon, All Hero

By Roger E. Moore. p. 22–28, 30

Half-dragon characters.

The Dragon's Bestiary: The Good, The Bad, and the Clumsy

By Spike Y. Jones. p. 71–74

Faerie creatures.

  • Bramble
  • Dobie
  • Faerie fiddler

Elminster's Notebook: Tashara of the Seven Skulls

By Ed Greenwood. p. 76–77

A character from the Realms.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 120

New products for July.

  • The Chaos Curse novel.
