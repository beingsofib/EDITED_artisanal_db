Dragon magazine 346, Adventuring, was released in August 2006. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Three Dragon Readings

By Craig Shackleton. p. 34–41

An article on an alternate way of generating characters.

Games of Chance

By Seth Irvin Williams, illustrated by Attila Adorjony. p. 42–48

Dice and card games for D&D.

Supporting Cast

By Michael Trice, illustrated by Andrew Hou. p. 50–54

How various classes act in positions of leadership.

Impiltur: The Forgotten Kingdom

By George Krashos, illustrated by Cyril Van Der Haegen. Cartography by Rob Lazzaretti. p. 56-73

A detailed description of Impiltur.

The Ecology of the Rust Monster

By Nicholas Hudson and Nicholas Logue, illustrated by Peter Bergting. Photography by Tony Moseley. p. 74-78

A detailed look at the rust monster. A previous Ecology article by the same name appeared in Dragon #88.

Class Acts: Scout Feat and Options

By Stephan Happ with Paul Leach, illustrated by Kalman Andrasofszky and Andy Belanger. p. 86–87

Further feats and options for the scout class.

Class Acts: The Power of Truenames

By Mark Jackman, illustrated by Kalman Andrasofszky and Andy Belanger. p. 88–89

The power of discovering a creature's true name in its native language.

Class Acts: Sacred Enforcers

By Scott Fitzgerald Gray, illustrated by Kalman Andrasofszky and Andy Belanger. p. 90–91

Describes sacred path options for clerics.

Class Acts: Eastern Cataphracts

By Paul Leach, illustrated by Kalman Andrasofszky and Andy Belanger. p. 92–93

An explanation of cataphracts during the real-world Middle Ages.
