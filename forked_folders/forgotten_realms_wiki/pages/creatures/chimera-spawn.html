Chimera-spawn were mutant chimeras, known only in the Stonelands.

Description

These mutant chimeras were covered in serpent-scales and had a single central lion's head, flanked with two dragon heads.

Personality

Per eyewitnesses, chimera-spawn were recklessly aggressive.

Abilities

One of the chimera-spawn's heads breathed fire, and the other one breathed electricity. They were otherwise rather similar to existing chimeras, with only a few differences.

Combat

Besides its twin breath weapons, the chimera-spawn's serpentine draconic scales covered its whole body, making it that much tougher and giving it far fewer weak spots. The creature was also slightly hardier than a regular chimera.

Ecology

Chimerae of the Stonelands sometimes gave birth to chimera-spawn when both sire and mare were chimerae. The resulting mutants lived generally short lives, as they were too aggressive for their own good.

History

Per the adventuring wizard Maris Khorna Terrabin, these creatures were found only in the Stonelands. She'd only ever seen one personally, and it was dead after it accidentally collapsed a cave on itself during a fight.
