Dragon issue 302, Magic, was released in December of 2002. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Summoner's Circle

By Charles Dunwoody, illustrated by Todd Lockwood and David Wahlstrom. p. 25–28

Expanded rules for summoning animals and outsiders.

Class Acts: The Tainted

By Chris Tanner, illustrated by Brom and Kyle Anderson. p. 32–44

Noble heroes who have fallen to fiendish parasites.

Arcane Lore: A Clutch of Cantrips

By Kieran Turley and S. Deniz Bucak, illustrated by Jeremy Jarvis. p. 48–51

More level 0 spells.

60 Unstable Arcana Magical Side Effects

By Kieran Turley, illustrated by David Wahlstrom. p. 52–54

More things to go wrong.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Five New Golems

By Eric Cagle, illustrated by Marc Sasso. p. 56–64

Web golems • Alchemical golems • mud golems • Rope golems • Puzzle golems.

Silicon Sorcery: Neverwinter Nights

By Clifford Horowitz, illustrated by Mike Sass and Todd Grenier. p. 66–70

Glyph guardians and other monsters from Neverwinter Nights.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Arcane Armor

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Arnie Swekel. p. 72–74

A variety of magical armor.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: The High Flagon

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by David Day. p. 76-79

The High Flagon, a typical country gambling house on the Long Road.

Rogues Gallery: The Heroes of the War of the Spider Queen

By Richard Baker, illustrated by Brom. p. 80–87

NPCs from the War of the Spider Queen.

Dungeoncraft: Running Large Encounters

By Monte Cook. p. 102

How to run a big D&D combat.
