Dragon issue 74 was released in June of 1983. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

From the Sorceror's Scroll - Warhorses and barding: From light to heavy, leather to plate

By Gary Gygax. p. 4

The different types of warhorses and the armor they can wear .

The electrum dragon

By Ed Greenwood. p. 17

Another good metallic dragon for your Advanced Dungeons & Dragons campaign.

Seven swords: Blades of the Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 18-24

Complete history and lore of seven unique magical blades from the Forgotten Realms.

  • Adjatha, The Drinker
  • Albruin
  • Ilbratha, Mistress of Battle
  • Namara, The Sword That Never Sleeps
  • Shazzellim
  • Susk, The Silent Sword
  • Taragarth, The Bloodbrand

The Ecology of the Bulette

By Richard Edwards and Chris Elliot. p. 26

A scholarly treatise on the Bulette.

A player character and his money...

By Lewis Pulsipher. p. 50

Ways to (fairly) separate players from their treasure.
