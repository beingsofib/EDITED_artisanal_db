Dragon issue 204, American Gothic humor in gaming, was released in April of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bard on the Run

By das Fraulein. p. 16

More D&D song parodies. Includes "Love Me Kender," "Mister Sand Mage," "Simply Resurrectable," and "Drizzt's Lament; or (Wastin' Away Again in) Menzoberranzan."

The Plane Truth Part II: A Journey to the Outlands

By David "Zeb" Cook. pp. 28–31

The travel journal of a paladin from Cormyr as he explores the Outlands.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 124

New products for April and May.

  • Crown of Fire novel.
  • Elves of Evermeet sourcebook.
  • The Complete Sha'ir's Handbook sourcebook.
