Dragon+ 19

Dragon+ issue 19 was released on April 19, 2018. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Imagining the Ampersand

By Matt Chapman. pp. 4

Artist Aleksi Briclot on mastering a new style to bring R.A. Salvatore's Drizzt Do'Urden to life.

The Crystal Shard: 30th Anniversary

By Matt Chapman. pp. 5

An interview with author R.A. Salvatore on the origins of the Legend of Drizzt series. Also included is Salvatore's own suggested reading order for the series.

The Legacy of Drizzt

By Matt Chapman. pp. 6

An article on Drizzt Do'Urden's impact on the Realms and Dungeons & Dragons.

Mordenkainen's Mayhem

By Bart Carroll. pp. 7

A preview of Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes, focusing on the devil Moloch.

Fiction: Qawasha & Kupalué Part Three

Main article: Fiction: Qawasha & Kupalué Part Three

By Adam Lee. pp. 9

The third and final part of a short fiction piece about Qawasha and Kupalué.

D&D Classic

By Bart Carroll. pp. 14

Reprints of three pieces from Dragon and Dungeon: the adventure "Fedifensor" from Dragon #68, "Hounds of Space and Darkness" from Dragon #117, and "Adapting "The Lich Queen's Beloved"" from Dungeon #191 (itself an adaptation of the adventure "The Lich Queen's Beloved" from Dungeon #100). Also included are 5th edition statistics for Vlaakith.

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll, Cartography by Mike Schley and Jared Blando. pp. 19

Several free maps from the adventures Tomb of Annihilation and Out of the Abyss:

  • Kir Sabal
  • Wyrmheart Mine
  • Yellyark
  • Fort Beluarian
  • The Worm Nursery
  • Gracklstugh
  • The Whorlstone Tunnels
  • Mantol-Derith
  • Menzoberranzan
