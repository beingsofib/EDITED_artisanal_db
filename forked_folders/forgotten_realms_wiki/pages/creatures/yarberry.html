Yarberries were deadly poisonous red berries from Thay. They were one of the most abundant and well-known plants in Thay in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Yarberries turned red when ripened, becoming very poisonous fruit. The berries were so bright and distinctive, they were used to describe beautiful and desirable colors.
