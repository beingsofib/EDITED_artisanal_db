Telling Lies

Telling Lies was the thirty-fourth issue of the Advanced Dungeons &amp; Dragons comics series set in the Forgotten Realms and published by . It was written by Dan Mishkin, drawn by Jan Duursema, and edited by Kim Yale. It is the first installment in the "Rites and Wrongs" story arc.
