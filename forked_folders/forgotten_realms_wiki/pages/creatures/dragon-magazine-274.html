Dragon issue 274, Special Collector's 3rd Edition Issue, was released in August of 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Up on a Soapbox: The Perfect Dungeon Master

By Gary Gygax. p. 8

How to become a good GM ... according to the master.

ProFiles: Jonathan Tweet

By Steve Kenson. p. 10

Jonathan Tweet brings not only dignity and respect to the table, but also a career in game design that began over two decades ago.

Coming Soon

By Uncredited. p. 32

  • Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn novel.
  • Death of the Dragon novel.
  • Into the Dragon's Lair module.
  • Servant of the Shard novel.
  • The Nether Scroll novel.

One Roll to Rule Them All

By Jonathan Tweet. p. 38

The core d20 mechanic.

The New Adventures of Volo: The Stag Lass

By Ed Greenwood. p. 105–108

A hunting lass of Waterdeep.

Dungeoncraft

By Ray Winninger. p. 124

Style, voice, and pacing.

Silicon Sorcery: Everybody into the Pool

By Johnny Wilson. p. 128-130

Managing magic items in game, plus a look at the items in Pool of Radiance: Ruins of Myth Drannor.
