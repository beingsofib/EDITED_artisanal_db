Purple panacea

Fungus
namePurple Panacea
typeMushroom

Purple panacea was a type of mushroom found in Faerûn. Given its name by dwarves in reference to its color and medicinal properties, this mushroom had a mildly sweet taste and smell.

History

The mushroom grew in the abandoned fungal gardens of Gauntlgrym around the Fiery Pit. When the Lords' Alliance expedition arrived to fight the Cult of the Eternal Flame there, they used purple panaceas as a local source of medicine for their wounded soldiers.
