Dragon issue 139, Pages from the Arch-Mages, was released in November of 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 56

New products for November.

  • Azure Bonds novel.
  • Kara-Tur: The Eastern Realms sourcebook.

Hand-to-Hand - Against the Rules

By Vince Garcia. p. 58

Modifying the martial arts of Oriental Adventures.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Rare beasts of the Forgotten Realms setting

By Ed Greenwood. p. 70–74

Rare Forgotten Realms fauna.

  • Lock Lurker
  • Lybbarde
  • Metalmaster
  • Serplar
  • Thylacine

The Ecology of the Spectator

By Dougal Demokopoliss. p. 86

A scholarly treatise on the Spectator, and statistics for the marine spectator, aka the searechter.
