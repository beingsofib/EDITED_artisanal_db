Watercress was a variety of a green leafy vegetable found in some areas of Faerûn and primarily used as food. On the Malatran Plateau, watercress was known as waterfood.

Usages

The Stargath House in Purskul, Amn, served cream of watercress soup with hickory-smoked apples.

Some believed that watercress possessed medicinal properties and could be used as a blood tonic to combat anemia.
