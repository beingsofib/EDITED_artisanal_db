Dragon issue 196 Good advice for great game masters, was released in August of 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Organization is Everything

By Richard Hunt. p. 30

Organizing your campaigns.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 62

Clue Corner: Treasures of the Savage Frontier.

Novel Ideas

By James Lowder. p. 65–66, 73

This article gives official Dale Reckoning dates for all of the Forgotten Realms fiction written up until 1993.

The Wizards Three: Three Wizards Too Many

By Ed Greenwood. p. 82–92

This short story presents a meeting among Elminster Aumar, Mordenkainen, and Dalamar. It also details some new spells (bloodglass, Fistandantilus's firequench, Thultaun's thrust, barrier reaver, dragon breath), magic items (Balacer's Hand, helping hand, spell mirror), and some notes on monsters (friendly fungus, stelurge).

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 120

New products for September.
