Dragon magazine 54 was released in October of 1981. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Down-to-earth divinity—Creating a playable pantheon

By Ed Greenwood. p. 6–9

One DM's design for a mixed and matched mythos.

Ruins

By Arn Ashleigh Parker. p. 10

Making adventures in ruins.

The Dragon's Bestiary

By Craig Stenseth, Ed Greenwood and Roger E. Moore. p. 28–29

All about the Boggart, the Stroan, and the Incubus for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: More Feather Tokens

By Ed Greenwood. p. 67–69

More forms of Quaal's feather token.
