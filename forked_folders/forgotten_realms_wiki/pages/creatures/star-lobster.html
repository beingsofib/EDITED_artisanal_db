Star lobster

Creature
nameStar Lobster
locationFiredrake Bay, near Port Kir

The star lobster was a type of shellfish found only in Firedrake Bay, near the town of Port Kir in Tethyr. It was a prized delicacy in the city of Zazesspur and in the cities of Calimshan to the south.
