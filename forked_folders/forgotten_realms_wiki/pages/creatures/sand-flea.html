Sand flea

Creature
nameSand Flea
terrainSandy beaches

Sand fleas were tiny parasitic insects that caused extreme discomfort to creatures they infested.

Ecology

Sand fleas inhabited sandy beaches. An infestation of these insects often was treated as a highly infectious disease. Their bites and movements caused host creatures extreme itching and discomfort. These tiny insects caused misery and discomfort, preventing the host from resting and healing normally, causing them to become irritable and distracted. Sand fleas could jump great distances, allowing the infestation to spread quickly from one host to another.

Sand flea infestation could be summoned via the infestation spell.
