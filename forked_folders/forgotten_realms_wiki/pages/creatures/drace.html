Drace

Plant
nameDrace
typeHerb
locationFaerûn

Drace was an herb produced from the leafs of the bittergar bush, a small thistle-like shrub which grew all over Faerûn, with the exception of mountainous or desert regions.

Description

Drace was too bitter to consume raw, but when cooked it lost this bitterness.

Usages

  • Drace was one of the ingredients of Spellslayer wine.
  • When added to a pot, cooked drace could enhance the flavor of other foods.
  • The medical book Disorders of the Nerves and Mind: A Treatise features a recount of a man being cured of an "unspecific neurotic enthrallment" with the administration of a garlic and drace tincture.
