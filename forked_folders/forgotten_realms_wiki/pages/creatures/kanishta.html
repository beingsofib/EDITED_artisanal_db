Kanishta was a root found in the Cold Lands, specifically Narfell.

Description

Kanishta was a dark-colored root.

Uses

Though extremely bitter tasting, when chewed this root helped alleviate the effects of exposure to the cold weather of northeast Faerûn, infusing strength and warmth into the user.
