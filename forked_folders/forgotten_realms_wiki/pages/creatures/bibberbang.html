Bibberbang

Plant
nameBibberbang
locationUnderdark

Bibberbangs were one of several highly dangerous growths found in the Underdark. When a person drew too near, they would swell and if they went closer still or touched or attacked them, they would explode violently. Often, they grew in large clusters, so disturbing one could cause a highly destructive chain of explosions.

After exploding, bibberbangs left behind noxious clouds of poison that would linger for some time.
