Dragon issue 367 was released in PDF format only in September of 2008. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Epic Faerûn

By Richard Baker. p. 5–10

This article details three new epic destinies for the Realms:

  • Elf high mage
  • Harper of legend
  • Mythic sovereign

Ecology of the Genasi

By Rodney Thompson. p. 11

This article covers the culture, history, and society of the genasi of Faerûn.

Children of Darkness

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 11–21

This article discusses drow who refused to worship the goddess Lolth.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Mindless Monstrosities

By Greg Bilsland. p. 42–44

Information on golems and oozes. Azure jelly • Crystal golem • Sand golem • Shadow slime • Rimefire golem • Iridescent ooze

Playing Gnolls

By Keith Baker. p. 47–53

If you’ve always wanted to play a gnoll PC, now’s your chance. This article comes with plenty of information on gnoll adventurers, along with racial feats for your gnoll hero.

Class Acts: Primordial Paths

By Greg Bilsland. p. 55–57

This article goes over features of the swordmage class.

Spells

dimensional thunder • elemental aegis

Hall of the Frostmaiden

By Brian R. James. p. 58–62

This article explores the realm of Auril, the Storm-bringer.
