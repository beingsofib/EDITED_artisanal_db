Furnace golem

Creature
nameFurnace golem
size2eLarge
alignment2eNeutral

Furnace golems were a variant of iron golem used in Wildspace. By consuming magical items, they were able to spelljam.

Notable Furnace Golems

A furnace golem acted as a bouncer for the Honored Mages' Guild of Bral, barring non-mages from entering.
