Dragon issue 58 was released in February of 1982. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Leomund's Tiny Hut: Beefing Up the Cleric

By Len Lakofka. p. 5–7

A pile of new spells for clerics.

The Dragon's Bestiary

By Ed Greenwood and Roger E. Moore. p. 8–10

All about the Sull, Beguiler, and Magenta's Cat for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

The Blood of Medusa

By Michael Parkinson. p. 11

Legendary monsters and creatures spawned by Medusa.

The Dwarven Point of View

By Roger E. Moore. p. 24-27

Background on dwarves.

The Gods of the Dwarves

By Roger E. Moore. p. 31-35

A half-dozen dwarven deities.

  • Abbathor
  • Berronar
  • Clanggedin Silverbeard
  • Dumathoin
  • Vergadain

A Long Bow Isn't Always a Strong Bow

By Robert Bowling. p. 50

Bows of varying strengths.
