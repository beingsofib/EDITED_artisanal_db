Dragon issue 387 was released in PDF format only in May of 2010. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Winning Races: Shifter, Shifters of the Cities

By Peter Schaefer, illustrated by Tyler Jacobson. p. 47–49

Some shifters have embraced civilization, finding ways to blend into urban environments seamlessly.

Winning Races: Tiefling

By Peter Schaefer, illustrated by Tyler Jacobson. p. 54–56

The Blightseers, one of many tiefling bloodlines, brings ruin to any and all they deem as their foes.
