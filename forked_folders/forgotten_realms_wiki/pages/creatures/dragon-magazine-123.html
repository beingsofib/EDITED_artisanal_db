Dragon issue 123 was released in July of 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Music of the Forgotten Realms

By Ed Greenwood. p. 13–14

A brief discussion of the music of the Realms appended to descriptions of 6 unique instruments.

The Mystic College

By James A. Yates. p. 16

A guide to establishing, maintaining, and attending academies of the sorcerous arts for both players and GMs.

Fire for Effect!

By Richard W. Emerich. p. 32

A review of the effects of fire and electricity, with relation to magic that harnesses such forces.

Arcane Lore: A new column of spells and enchantments

By Harold Dolan. p. 36–40

The debut of a new column presents eight new fire-based magic-user spells, and one new related magic item.

The Ecology of the Giant Leech

By Anthony Gerard. p. 50-52

A tale of monsters and men and the thin difference between.

Time Flies...

By Lisa Cabala. p. 54

Tables to help track the passage of time in your campaign.

Just Making Time

By Roger E. Moore. p. 60

The joys and horrors of fantasy calendars.
