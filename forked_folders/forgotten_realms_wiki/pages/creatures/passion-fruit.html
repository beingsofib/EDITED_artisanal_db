Passion fruit was a type of delicate fruit grown in Zakhara.

Availability

The eatery called the Golden Oasis in the city of Tajar served passion fruits as a part of its feasts. The gardens of the Fortress of Sarahin in the Haunted Lands grew lemons, tangerines, oranges, pomegranates, and passion fruits.
