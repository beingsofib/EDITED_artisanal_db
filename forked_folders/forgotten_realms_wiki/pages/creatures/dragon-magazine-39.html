Dragon issue 39 was released in July of 1980. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Women Want Equality ...and why not?

By Kim Mohan and Jean Wells. p. 16

The role of women in gaming, both as players and characters.

Leomund's Tiny Hut: Starting from scratch

By Lenard Lakofka. p. 20–23

How to start a campaign in Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

Bows: Of staves and strings and other things

By Bill Fawcett. p. 26

A discussion of the many types of bows, crossbows and composite bows.

What Are the Odds?

By William Keely. p. 39

The probabilities for each number occurring when randomly rolling 3d6 for characteristics.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Greenstone Amulet

By Ed Greenwood. p. 41–42

A highly prized magic item that provides mental protection.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Laerl's Storm Armor

By Ed Greenwood. p. 42

A magical armor that provides protection from heat, cold, and electricity.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Mists of Rapture

By Ed Greenwood. p. 42

A magical gas that causes its victims to writhe in pleasure.
