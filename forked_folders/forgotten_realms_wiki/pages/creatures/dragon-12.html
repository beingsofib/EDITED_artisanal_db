Dragon+ 12

Dragon+ issue 12 was released on February 27, 2017. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Standing on the Shoulders of Giants

By John Houlihan. pp. 9

A look at the board game Assault of the Giants.

Behind the Screen

By Bart Carroll. pp. 13

A preview of Tales from the Yawning Portal.

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll, Cartography by Jason A. Engle and Lee Moyer. pp. 16

Several maps from the Storm King's Thunder adventure module.

The Barber of Silverymoon

By Jason Bradley Thompson, illustrated by Jason Bradley Thompson. pp. 17

A free 5th edition adventure for characters between levels 4 and 6.
