Dragon issue 131, Descent into Deepearth, was released in March of 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Folk of the Underworld

By Eric Oppen. p. 32-34

The svirfneblin point of view.

The Ecology of the Aboleth

By Brandon Crist. p. 36-39

A scholarly treatise on the Aboleth.

The Ecology of the Hook Horror

By Michael Persinger. p. 42–46

A scholarly treatise on the Hook Horror.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 59

New products for February and March.

  • Empires of the Sands
  • The Crystal Shard
