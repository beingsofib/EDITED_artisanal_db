Dragon issue 162, A Halloween guide is always welcome, was released in October of 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Bazaar of the Bizarre: The Book of Horrors

By Christian Bök. p. 10–13

A compendium of terrifying spells.

The Mind of the Vampire

By Nigel Findley. p. 15–20

Roleplaying powerful and "twisted" undead.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 51

Clue Corner: Curse of the Azure Bonds.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Two hauntingly good (but definitely bad) undead.

By Spike Y. Jones. p. 52

Two new undead.

  • Ankou
  • Spiritus anime

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 86

New products for October and November.

  • Draconomicon sourcebook.
  • Dwarves Deep sourcebook.
  • Halls of the High King module.
  • Viperhand novel.
