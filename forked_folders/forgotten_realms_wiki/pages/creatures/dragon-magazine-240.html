Dragon issue 240, Mystery and Suspense, was released in October of 1997. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Wyrms of the North: The Thief Dragon

By Ed Greenwood. p. 77–81

Gaulantyr, or "Glory Tongue", an intelligent, paranoid topaz dragon with a hunger for gems.

Dungeon Mastery: 101 Little Mysteries

By Steve Berman. p. 84

101 incidents and objects that can add a little spice to any campaign world.

The Ecology of: The Nymph

By Johnathan M. Richards. p. 70–74

A scholarly treatise on the Nymph.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 116

New products for October, and November.

  • Elminster in Myth Drannor novel.
  • Four from Cormyr module.
  • The Simbul's Gift novel.
