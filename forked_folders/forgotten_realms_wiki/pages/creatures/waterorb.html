Waterorb

Fungus
nameWaterorb
typeFungus
locationThe Underdark
usageFood and water

Waterorb was a type of fungus that grew in the Underdark in shallow water. When mature, it was bulbous in shape and bigger than a human head. If squeezed and wrung out, it yielded about a gallon (almost four liters) of potable water. The remaining husk was bland and chewy like a sponge, but edible, providing about of food.
