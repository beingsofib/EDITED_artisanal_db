Sorghum was a grain cultivated as food during summer months. It could also be used as a sweetener.

Availability

Places in the Realms where sorghum was known to be eaten included:

  • In Chult, where the Tabaxi tribe grew Sorghum in the Agricultural Quarter of Mezro.
  • In Kara-Tur, where sorghum was one of several grains—allong with barley and millet—seen as a poor person's alternative to rice. It was often eaten either as a thick gruel or pounded into cakes. Sorghum was cultivated in Ma' Yuan, such as by the monks of the Strong Claw monastery near Yen Ching.

History

During the invasion of Shou Lung by the Tuigan in the , a decisive battle in the Ma' Yuan Province came to be known as the Battle of the Sorghum Field.
