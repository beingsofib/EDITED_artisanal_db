The Dragon issue 29 was released in September of 1979. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Curses: Never get even - get ahead!

By Harold Pitt. p. 13

Tailoring curses for maximum effect (and cruelty!) to your PCs in Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

Rewarding Heroism in D&D

By Doug Green. p. 23

Getting your Dungeons & Dragons players to act more like Heroes.

Smaller Than Man-Sized Weapons Table

By Tom Holsinger. p. 30

A table listing damage for different sized weapons in Advanced Dungeons & Dragons.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: The Ring of the Necromancer

By Bill Howell. p. 43

A moderately powerful ring used to control undead.
