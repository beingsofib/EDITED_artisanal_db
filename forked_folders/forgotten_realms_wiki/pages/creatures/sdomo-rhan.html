S'domo rhan

Creature
nameS'domo rhan
locationNaktong Moors, Koryo

The s'domo rhan was a giant beetle that was said to dwell on the Naktong Moors of Koryo.

The s'domo rhan had the power to transform into a beautiful woman, in which form it would lure men to their dooms.
