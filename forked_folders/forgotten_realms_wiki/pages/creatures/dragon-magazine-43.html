Dragon issue 43 was released in November of 1980. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Brewing up a new NPC: The Witch

By Kim Mohan, Tom Moldvay, and Bill Muhlhausen. p. 6

A revised and refined version of the Witch NPC class.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Lythlyx

By Ed Greenwood. p. 30

A creature that resembles a spinning, rotating springs or corkscrews.
