Redwoods were a species of trees found in several locations across the Realms, known for their sturdiness and massive size.

Habitats

  • Redwoods grew in the High Forest and were used by elvish craftsmen to carve impressive furniture out of, including thrones.
  • The Forest of Wyrms in the Western Heartlands had a population of majestic massive redwood trees that reached several hundreds feet tall and to feet wide.
  • Cormyr's Helmlands had the Redwoods forest that perished during the Spellplague, but in the century that followed a new generation of redwood trees grew to maturity in place of their perished progenitors.
  • Hartsvale, the land of giants and giant trees, exported quality redwood that was used to fashion fashion masts and keels by shipbuilders of Westgate.

Usages

  • Orcs and tanarukka that served Daggerford's Devil Gorgomorz in the , were supplied with orcish-made redwood shields. each, they lacked in appearance but were very effective nevertheless.
  • Redwood was used in construction of doors in Tethyr. Redwood doors were in use even in the royal palace of Tethyr in the 15 century DR.
  • Redwood furniture was in use in the land of Zakhara. A huge table of carved redwood could be found in the Ivory Tower of Dar al-Bayda in the 14 century DR.
