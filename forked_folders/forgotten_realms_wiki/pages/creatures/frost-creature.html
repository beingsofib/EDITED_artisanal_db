Frosts, also known as snow fairies and winter folk, were distant cousins of pixies and sprites.

Description

Frosts were small, fey-like creatures with wings that looked an insects. Most had pale blue skin, although frosts who lived in subarctic climates had dark blue skin.

Personality

Frosts delighted in pulling pranks on others. Common pranks include creating slippery patches on ice in the path of larger creatures or shaking snow covered tree branches as someone walks under them.

Abilities

Frosts had the innate ability to duplicate the effects of cone of cold three times per day, frost fingers twice per day, ice hands and control temperature, 10' radius at will. Some frosts had the innate ability to duplicate the effects of the cold ray spell. Frosts were immune to the cold and any cold-based effects and spells.

Combat

Frosts hunted small animals by using bows and special daggers of sharp icicles. Frosts never engaged in melee combat with creatures larger than they were. If forced into combat with a larger creature, frosts kept their distance and used their innate magic abilities as weapons. Frosts avoided fire as it could instantly melt their wings, which did not grow back.

Society

Frosts were communal creatures and lived in small villages. They were nomadic so they made their villages within abandoned animal dens, hollow trees or small caves. Frosts rarely stayed in the same location for more than a year. Frosts spoke their own language as well as elven and the languages of their local allies. Frost villages formed small scouting groups, who went out to hunt for crystals and gems, which were highly sought after. Frost lived in peace their forest and had little negative effect on their environment.

Relationships

Frosts maintained peaceful relations with dryads, elves, halflings and woodland fairies. They often allied with them to defend a forest from those who wished it harm. Frosts harassed humans who exploited a forests, such as loggers and hunters. Frosts were hostile towards small evil creatures such as quicklings.

Frosts sometimes acted as servants of Aerdrie Faenya and Ulutiu.

Usages

Some evil sorcerers have experimented on frosts to determine if any part of their bodies could be used as material components for potions or spells.
