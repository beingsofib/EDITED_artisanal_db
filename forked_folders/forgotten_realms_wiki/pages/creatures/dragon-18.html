Dragon+ 18

Dragon+ issue 18 was released on February 26, 2018. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Preview: Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes

By Matt Chapman. pp. 5

Mike Mearls and Ben Petrisor discuss a preview of the upcoming sourcebook Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes, including a look at three planar monsters: the geryon, marut, and astral dreadnaught.

D&D Classic

By Bart Carroll. pp. 6

Reprints of two articles penned by Ed Greenwood for Dragon: "Magic in the Evening" from Dragon #185, and "Return to the Nine Hells" from Dragon #400 (which in turn was a reprint of two articles from issues #75 and #76).

Fiction: Rancor

By Dave Gross. pp. 8

A short story by Dave Gross on the origins of Dorn Il-Khan, a character in Baldur's Gate: Enhanced Edition, including 5th-edition statistics for Dorn.

Doing it for the Fans

By Mark Bonnington. pp. 9

An interview with 's Trent Oster and Jonathan Hill on the development of Neverwinter Nights: Enhanced Edition.

Neverwinter Goes TRPG

By Matt Chapman. pp. 11

A 5th-edition tabletop conversion of a quest in the video game Neverwinter, Crypts of Kelemvor is a free adventure suitable for characters between levels 3 and 5.

Dragonfire: Warlock's Crypt

By Matt Chapman. pp. 13

A free adventure (scenario) for the deck-building tabletop game Dragonfire, set in Warlock's Crypt.

Comic: Port Nyanzaru

By Jason Thompson. pp. 16

A comic map of Port Nyanzaru by Jason Thompson.

Maps of the Month

By Bart Carroll, Cartography by Mike Schley and Jared Blando. pp. 19

Several free maps from the adventures Tomb of Annihilation and Out of the Abyss:

  • Nine Shrines
  • Nangalore
  • Camp Vengeance
  • Wreck of the Star Goddess
  • Fane of the Night Serpent
  • The Oozing Temple
  • The Lost Tomb
  • Blingdenstone
