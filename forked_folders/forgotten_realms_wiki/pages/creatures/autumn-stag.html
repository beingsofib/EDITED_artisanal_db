Autumn stag

Autumn stags were a unique breed of deer, cousin to the sylvan stags, found inhabiting the depths of Neverwinter Wood in the Northwest Faerûn.

Description

These large stags were considered to be some of the most majestic deer. They had large, powerful bodies and a crown of awe-inspiring antlers on their heads; their bodies were covered in colorful autumnal leaves.

Ecology

Just like sylvan stags, autumn stags were possible to tame and train, and they were sometimes used as mounts. Autumn stags granted small amounts of magical healing to their masters.

History

Some tamed autumn stags were found used as loyal mounts by the heroes of Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.
