Dragon issue 331, Adventuring Gear Super-Spectacular, was released in May of 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Point of Pole Arms

By Ari Marmell and Jason Bulmahn, illustrated by Attila Adorjany. p. 23-28

A plethora of pole arms.

Alchemical Charms

By Yury Pavlotsky, illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 39-34

Crafting alchemical items.

Bedrolls, Bottles, and Bells

By Mike Mearls, illustrated by Ramon Perez. p. 40-44

You've been hauling around all that crap since 1st level. Isn't it about time you got some use out of it?

The Ecology of the Green Hag

By F. Wesley Schneider, illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 56-60

A scholarly treatise on the green hag.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Insidious Items

By Timothy T. Murphy, illustrated by John Gallagher. p. 64-67

Warped and cursed items.
