A redwing was a huge dragonfly-like creature bred in High Imaskar as of the late 15 century DR. They were used to transport both trade goods and passengers on massive gondolas strapped to their undersides.

History

Redwings were bred to facilitate trade

As of the , the Vengeance Riders kept specially trained redwings stabled on great balconies near the tip of the tower-city of Skyclave. At least four of these redwings were always on patrol around the tower on the lookout for threats.
