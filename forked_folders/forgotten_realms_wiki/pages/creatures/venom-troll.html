Venom trolls were a rare and dangerous type of troll.

Abilities

Venom trolls were immune to poison and poison-based effects and spells.

Combat

Venom trolls were dangerous in combat. Its body excreted poison and its sharp claws and teeth were coated in poison as well. Even its blood was saturated with poison.

Biology

Venom trolls were the result of a rare mutation resulting from consuming large doses of poison.
