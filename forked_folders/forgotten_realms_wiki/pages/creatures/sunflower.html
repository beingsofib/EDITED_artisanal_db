A sunflower was a flowering plant noted for its distinct yellow shade of its petals. They were typically found growing in meadows.

Habitat

  • In Faerûn, fields of them grew in the edgelands of Cormanthor. and around the town of Phandalin.
  • In the High Desert of Zakhara, the center of the city of Vahtov had a spacious sunflower garden.
  • In the Abyssal realm of Azzagrat, the city of Zelatar had an open-air arboretum noted for its remarkable display of petrified flowers that included the likes of daisies, daffodils, roses, and sunflowers.

Varieties

Giant sunflowers: An oversized variety of sunflower that grew to heights of and greater. They were indigenous to the Spiderhaunt Wood, but some specimens were brought to estates of Cormyrean aristocrats for aesthetic purposes in the 14 century DR. These aristocrats were initially advised against this, being warned that the massive flowers would likely require inordinate amounts of watering, but it was found that they actually required no more water than a mundane daisy.

Usages

  • Sunflower oil, a type of cooking oil, could be derived from their seeds. bottles of this oil were sold in Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue under its "Aurora's Larder" section at a cost of 3 silver pieces.
  • Sunflower seeds could be used in baking. In the city of Baldur's Gate, they were used in sunflower seed buns.
  • Sunflowers were a staple in the diet of bhukas and were among the meadow flowers that pack lizards were fond of.

Trivia

  • These flowers were the namesake for the Sunflower Inn of Dark Hollow, whose sign featured a painting of a sunflower.
  • Some mimirs took the shape of sunflowers. These were a popular choice among druids of the Outer Planes.
  • Sunflowers were among the favored plants of the deity Amaunator.
