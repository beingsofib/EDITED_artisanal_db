Dragon issue 320, 30th anniversary of Dungeons & Dragons, was released in June of 2004. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Dragon Player Characters

By Mike McArtor. p. 36

Playing dragon PCs.

Fostered Dragons

By Casey Loe. p. 46

Rearing a wyrmling.

New in Town

By Richard Lee Byers. p. 60-71

When cultures collide! Savagery meets the city in Richard Lee Byer's newest tale. Tie-in for The Year of Rogue Dragons.

Elminster's Guide to the Realms: The Roaring Dragon House

By Ed Greenwood. p. 72-75

Waterdeep's newest den of vice and victory.

DM's Toolbox: Fun Dungeons

By Johnn Four. p. 96

Making dungeons fun.

Characterization, Part 1: Bring Your Characters to Life

By Monte Cook. p. 104

Fleshing out NPCs.
