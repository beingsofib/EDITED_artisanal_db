Dragon issue 342, Divinity, was released in April of 2006. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Domain Power

By Jake Manley, illustrated by Tomas Giorello. p. 21–25

Alternative uses for divine energy.

Beyond the Pale

By Rodney Thompson, illustrated by Tom Fowler. p. 26–32

Six new ways to put the fear of death back into your PCs.

The Ecology of the Adventurer

By Tony Moseley, illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 60-65

A scholarly treatise on the Adventurer.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Healer's Touch

By John E. Ling Jr., illustrated by Tom Fowler. p. 70–72

Healing magic items.

Spellcraft: Alien Blessings

By Eric Cagle, illustrated by Warren Mahy. p. 74–77

Spells of monstrous deities.

Arcane Class Act - Subtle Spells

By Christopher Wissel. p. 86

Spells for Assassins and Bards.

The Wild Hunt

By William L. Christensen. p. 88–89

Options for worshiping the Master of the Hunt.
