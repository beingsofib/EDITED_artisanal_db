Dragon issue 325 was released in November of 2004. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

War Magic

By Joshua Cole, illustrated by Ramón Pérez. p. 54–58

Optimizing your magic in large-scale battles.

The Ecology of the Duergar

By Amber Scott, illustrated by Peter Bergting. p. 66-69

A scholarly treatise on the Duergar.

Spellcraft: Myths of the Shadow

By Rahul Kanakia, illustrated by Andrew Hou. p. 70–72

Spells for those who stalk the shadows.
