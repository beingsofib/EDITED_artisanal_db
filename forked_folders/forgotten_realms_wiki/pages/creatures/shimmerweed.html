Shimmerweed

Plant
nameShimmerweed
typePlant

Shimmerweed was a type of common cotton-producing plant found in Faerûn.

Description

It had blue leaves and produced fluffy round pods that contained the shimmerweed seeds.

Usage

It was used in dyes, potions, and shimmerweave.
