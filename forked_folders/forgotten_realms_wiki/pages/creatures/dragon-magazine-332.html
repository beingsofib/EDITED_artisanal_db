Dragon issue 332, Special 29th Anniversary issue, was released in June of 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Chromatic Dragons: Rage on Wings

By Mike McArtor, illustrated by Andrew Hou. p. 23–37

Rain fire and wreak havoc with all five chromatic dragons as playable monster classes.

Cutting up the Dragon

By Andrew Coleman, illustrated by Tom Fowler and Jeff Carlisle. p. 48–57

Rules for crafting items from bits and pieces of dragon corpses.

The Ecology of the Kobold

By Mat Smith, illustrated by Peter Bergting, Cartography by Chris West. p. 59-68

A scholarly treatise on the Kobold.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: In Defense of Home and Hoard

By Greg Marks, illustrated by David Wahlstrom. p. 70–73

Magic items for dragons who want a little extra protection against the PCs.

A Novel Approach: Realms of the Dragons II

By J. L. Collins, Kameron Franklin, Ed Gentry, Harley Stroh, and Erin Tettensor, illustrated by Matt Stawicki and Jeff Carlisle. p. 74–76

Explore Faerûn's Rage of Dragons with Realms of the Dragons II.
