Dragon+ 7

Dragon+ issue 7 was released on April 27, 2016. It contains the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Ten Must Play Quests in Siege of Dragonspear

By Matt Chapman. pp. 5

A list of ten notable side quests in Baldur's Gate: Siege of Dragonspear.

First Look: Tyrants of the Underdark Strategies

By Andrew Veen and Yoni Skolnik. pp. 6

Two developers of the board game Tyrants of the Underdark share tips and tricks.

Neverwinter's Massively Multiplayer Dungeon Master

By Chris Perkins. pp. 9

A look at Neverwinter's in-game "Marvelous Game" event.
