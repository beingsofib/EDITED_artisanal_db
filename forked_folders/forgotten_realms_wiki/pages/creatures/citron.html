Citron

Plant
nameCitron
typeTree, fruit

Citrons were a type of tree-grown citrus fruit found in the Realms.

Availability

Places in the Realms where citrons were known to be cultivated included:

  • In the Crowded Sea, citron was cultivated on the islands of Masud Jazayir.
  • In the Unapproachable East, citrons were a major fruit crop of the nation of Thay.

Variants

Mir: A species of citron that grew on Skyport, in the public park known as the Terrace of Tranquility. The fruit of this tree was delightfully sweet and a staple of poorer citzens' diets.

Notable Orange Products

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold candied citron imported from Thay at a standard price of 10 gold pieces per in its "Aurora's Larder" section. Dried citrons were also featured in the fruitcakes that the catalogue sold.
