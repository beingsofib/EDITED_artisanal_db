Dragon issue 68 was released in December of 1982. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Featured Creatures

By Gary Gygax. p. 4-5

Fungoid monsters for your campaign

  • Ascomoid
  • Basidirond
  • Phycomid

Be a two-fisted fighter

By Roger E. Moore. p. 7

New rules for fighting with two weapons.

From the Sorcerer's Scroll: Be the first to cast these new high-level spells

By Gary Gygax. p. 24-27,54-59

New magic-user spells of levels 5-9.

  • Avoidance
  • Banishment
  • Binding
  • Chain Lightning
  • Contingency
  • Crystalbrittle
  • Demand
  • Dismissal
  • Energy Drain
  • Ensnarement
  • Eyebite
  • Fabricate
  • Forcecage
  • Leomund’s Lamentable Belabourment
  • Mordenkainen's Disjunction
  • Mordenkainen's Lucubration
  • Mordenkainen's Magnificent Mansion
  • Otiluke's Telekinetic Sphere
  • Sending
  • Sequester
  • Succor
  • Teleport Without Error
  • Torment
  • Transmute Water To Dust
  • Truename
  • Volley

Leomund's Tiny Hut: The Cloistered Cleric

By Lenard Lakofka. p. 30-34

A new NPC class representing the non-adventuring cleric. Includes new spells.
