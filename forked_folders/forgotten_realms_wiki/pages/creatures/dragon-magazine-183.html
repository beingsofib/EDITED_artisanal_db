Dragon issue 183, Magic plus science equals wonder, was released in July of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Avast, ye swabs, and heave to!

By Richard Baker. p. 21

Pirates and privateers in the Spelljammer setting.

The Vikings' Dragons

By Jean Rabe, illustrated by Jim Holloway. p. 65-70

An overview of the Norse lands' great serpents, part 2.

  • Flame linnorm
  • Gray linnorm
  • Rain linnorm
  • Corpse tearer
  • Midgard linnorm

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 72

New products for July.

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sourcebook.

Game Wizards: When the going gets tough, the tough go shopping

By Anne Brown. p. 100

A look at Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.
