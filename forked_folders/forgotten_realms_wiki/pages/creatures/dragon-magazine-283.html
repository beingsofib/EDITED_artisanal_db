Dragon issue 283, Clerics & Deities, was released in May of 2001. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: Mark Zug

By Uncredited. p. 22

  • Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting 3rd edition sourcebook.
  • Neverwinter Nights computer game.
  • Pool of Radiance: Attack on Myth Drannor module.
  • Pool of Radiance: Ruins of Myth Drannor computer game.
  • The Shattered Mask novel.
