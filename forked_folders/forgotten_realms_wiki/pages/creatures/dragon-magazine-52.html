Dragon issue 52 was released in August of 1981. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Dragon's Bestiary: Rhaumbusun

By Ed Greenwood and Victor Selby. p. 48

A small, reptilian creature that can paralyze with it's gaze.

Up on a Soapbox: To Err is Human, to Repair Divine

By Lewis Pulsipher. p. 63

What to do when the DM makes a big mistake in a game.

Up on a Soapbox: The Best DMs Will Look Further Than the Book

By Tom Armstrong. p. 64

Advises DMs to throw curveballs at players who have read the DM Guide and Monster Manual and abuse this knowledge.
