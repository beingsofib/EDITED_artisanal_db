Dragon issue 309, War, was released in July of 2003. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Beasts of the Battlefield

By Andy Collins and Matthew Sernett. p. 24

Five monsters bred or built for war: crawling slaughter, death hurler, golem swarm, guardian steed, and wardrake.

Arcane Lore: War Spells

By Sean K. Reynolds, illustrated by Mike Vilardi. p. 44–51

Powerful new spells designed to effect the course of battles.

Paragons of War: The Ecology of Hobgoblins

By Terry Edwards. p. 52-61

A scholarly treatise on Hobgoblins.

Incursion: A World Under Siege

By James Wyatt, illustrated by Sam Wood. p. 70–104

Introduction to the Incursion campaign, where an army of githyanki invades and conquers the PCs' home-world.

The Incursion Begins

By James Wyatt. p. 80

Incursion: On the March

By James Wyatt. p. 88

Incursion: The Beginning of the End

By James Wyatt. p. 96

Incursion: Fighting the Githyanki

By James Wyatt. p. 104
