Dragon issue 415 was released in PDF format only in September of 2012. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Channel Divinity: Zehir

By Tim Eagon, illustrated by Beth Trott. p. 3-6

The Midnight Serpent’s hunger for death is insatiable, and as a Fang of Zehir, it’s your job to make sure the god of murder is well fed.

Heir of Shadowbane

By Erik Scott de Bie, illustrated by Raymond Swanland. p. 10-18

Read the new short story from the author of Eye of Justice.

History Check: The Many Deaths of Manshoon

By Brian Cortijo, illustrated by Chris Seaman. p. 19-23

How does the nefarious leader of the Black Network stay on top of things and thwart assassination attempts?

Songs of Sorcery

By Alana Abbott, illustrated by Beth Trott. p. 24-26

The next time your bard tries to soothe savage beasts with song, make the power come alive with lyrics!

Eye on the Realms: The Haunted Battlement

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Tyler Walpole. p. 30-33

An unspeakable monster haunts a lonely hilltop fortress in northern Calimshan. Three hired adventurers have already hurled themselves off its battlements to their deaths. Will you be next in line for such a fate?
