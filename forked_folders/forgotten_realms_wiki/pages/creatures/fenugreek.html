Fenugreek was a type of plant found in the Realms that was commonly used in cooking.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold fenugreek under its "Aurora's Larder" section for 3 silver pieces per .

Usages

  • These plants could be used medicinally as a stimulant.
  • Certain spells in the evocation and necromancy schools of magic could be aided by a tailored infusion of fenugreek.
