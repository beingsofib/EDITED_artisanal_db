Dragon issue 97 was released in May of 1985. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

From the Sorcerer's Scroll: Deities and Their Faithful

By Gary Gygax. p. 8

How deities draw power from their followers.

Sticks, Stones, and Bones

By Stephen Inniss. p. 10

Weapons to use when your enemy swipes your sword.

The Ecology of the Gorgon

By Ed Greenwood. p. 25-27

A scholarly treatise on the Gorgon.

Pages from the Mages IV

By Ed Greenwood. p. 30–35

More magical books from Elminster's memory for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons, along with new spells.

  • Bowgentle's Book
  • The Spellbook of Daimos
  • Book of Num "the Mad"
  • Briel's Book of Shadows

The Only Good Captive ...

By Lewis Pulsipher. p. 38

Saving characters who have been defeated.

Blueprint for a Big Game

By Jim Dutton. p. 44

Running a large-scale AD&D game.
