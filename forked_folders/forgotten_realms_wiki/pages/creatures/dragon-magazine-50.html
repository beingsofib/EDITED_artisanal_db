Dragon issue 50 was released in June of 1981. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Self Defense for Dragons

By Gregory Rihn. p. 7

Making dragons a little more dangerous.

The Ups and Downs of Riding High

By Roger E. Moore. p. 48

Flying mounts.

Up on a Soapbox: Good DM Can Cure Bad Behavior

By Thomas Griffith. p. 52

Characters, GMs, and friendships.
