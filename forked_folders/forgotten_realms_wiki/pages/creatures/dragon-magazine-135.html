Dragon issue 135 was released in July of 1988. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ecology of the Cave fisher

By Timothy Lethbridge. p. 10–12

Everything you ever wanted to know about the monstrous lobster-arachnid, the Cave Fisher.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Trouble comes on little cat feet

By Scott Bennie. p. 14

The tibbit (cat-were).

Bazaar of the Bizarre

By Various Authors. p. 20–24

Some sharp reminders that magical arrows are an archer's best friends.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 52

New products for June.

  • The Magister sourcebook.
  • The Forgotten Realms 1989 Calendar

Role-playing Reviews

By Ken Rolston. p. 70–77

Bloodstone Pass.

Review: The Role of Books

By John C. Bunnel. p. 79

Review of The Crystal Shard.
