A werepegasus was a type of lycanthrope that could turn into a pegasus.

Description

Werepegasi could transform into their pegasus form from their human form. A werepegasus could be used as an aerial mount. These shapechangers were seemingly very elusive, with only one werepegasus known in the Realms. The only known pegasus was swift-winged and appeared to have the same appearance as a black pegasus.

Notable Werepegasi

  • Thorn, a Cormyrian bladesmith, and husband of Iyrytharna Dantras.
