Wu t'ung were a type of ornamental tree found in the plane of Qui and particularly favored by feng huang.

Description

Wu t'ung were enormous trees that produced bell-shaped flowers. The color of these flowers was most often white or brown, though red wu t'ung flowers were also known to exist.

Habitats

Wu t'ung were primarily known to grow in the Celestial Bureaucracy's plane of Qui. Misikin, a feng huang officer of the Qui sub-committee that governed the affairs of the animals dwelling on the Fochu Peninsula in Wa under orders of the Celestial Bureaucracy's Ministry of Animal Affairs, took a particular liking to wu t'ung trees and had groves of them in her domain lining the golden stairway to her reception nest. Misikin's lieutenants nested in these particular groves.

Usage

Feng huang built elaborate nests of gold and silver thread upon the tops of wu t'ung trees, and they also especially enjoyed eating wu t'ung seeds. In particular, Misikin adorned her reception and meditation nests with red and white wu t'ung flowers.
