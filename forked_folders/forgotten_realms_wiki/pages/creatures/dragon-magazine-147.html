Dragon issue 147. When magic talks, everybody listens, was released in July of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Variety, the Spice of Magic

By Vince Garcia. p. 22-25

Caught without your spell components? Try a substitute.

Gaze into my crystal ball...

By Krys Stromsted. p. 26

How Scrying devices and spells work.

Spelling It Out

By Douglas J. Behringer. p. 34

Rules for scribing spells into books and scrolls.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 40

New products for June and July.

  • Cities of Mystery
  • Shadowdale novel.
  • Tantras module.
  • The 1990 Forgotten Realms Calendar.

Role-playing Reviews

By Ken Rolston. p. 69

The Magister.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 76

A review of Hillsfar.
