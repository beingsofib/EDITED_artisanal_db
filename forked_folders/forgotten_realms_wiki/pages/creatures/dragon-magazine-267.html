Dragon issue 267, The Underdark, was released in January of 2000. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

ProFiles: Miranda Horner

By Steve Kenson. p. 14

For games editor Miranda Horner, fun is the name of the game.

By Any Other Name: The Drow

By Owen K.C. Stephens. p. 28-31

Generating drow names.

The City of Sunken Spires

By Eric L. Boyd. p. 32-40

Underspires, an underground realm.

PC Portraits: The Drow

By David Day. p. 41

Denizens of the Underdark

By Johnathan M. Richards. p. 43

Three new PC races for the Underdark.

The Dragon's Bestiary: Designing Dungeon Monsters

By Gregory W. Detwiler, illustrated by Bob Klasnich. p. 62–66

Some underground monsters and advice on making more of the same.

  • Glitterworm
  • Stalking catfish
  • Tunnelmouth dweller

Bazaar of the Bizarre: The Eyes Have It

By Ron C. Poirier, illustrated by Terry Dykstra. p. 68–71

Magic eyes.

The Ecology of the Carrion crawler

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by L. A. Williams. p. 72-79

All about Carrion crawler and the Underdark.

The New Adventures of Volo: Onward!

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Ron Spencer. p. 80–83

Aumrauth, Elventree, Evereska, Greycloak Hills, Iyrandrar, Mlossae, Nindrol, Narbeth, and Ondroth, plus a new elven magic spell.

Silicon Sorcery: Review of Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas

By Johnny Wilson. p. 102–103

Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas: "The best way to acquire high quality maps"
