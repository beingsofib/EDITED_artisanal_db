Pineapples were a type of fruit, grown from trees of the same name, found in the Realms.

Availability

Pineapples were known to grow in various places in the Realms:

  • In southeast Faerûn, pineapples were cultivated in northeastern Dambrath, northern Estagund, the northslope foothills of the Toadsquat Mountains, and in Luiren. In Luiren they were particularly known to grow in the tropical fruit plantations that stood between the cities of Beluir and Chethel.
  • Southeast Faerûn also sported wild pineapples throughout Veldorn and in the woodlands northeast of Veldorn.
  • In southwest Faerûn, pineapples were cultivated in parts of the Chultan Peninsula and in Lapaliiya. In the Chultan Peninsula, they were particularly found in Tashalar

Notable Users of Pineapples

Businesses

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold dried pineapples in the catalogue's "Exotic" food section for 300 gold pieces per .

Regions & Settlements

  • A favorite dish among the people of Shou Lung's Ch'ing Tung Province was hot and spicy stewed snake served with pineapple.
