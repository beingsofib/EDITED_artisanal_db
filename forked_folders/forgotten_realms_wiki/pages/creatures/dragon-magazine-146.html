Dragon issue 146. 13 years–and still on top, was released in June of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Dragon's Bestiary: All dragons, great and small

By David E. Cates, Gregory Detwiler, Malcolm Bowers, Mark Mathis and Randy Johns. p. 22–28

Not all new types of dragons are bad - only most of them!

  • Cobra Dragon
  • Draken
  • Gray Dragon
  • Minidragon
  • Obsidian Dragon
  • Rainbow Dragon

The Ecology of...: The New Ecology of the Dragons

By Jean Rabe and Skip Williams. p. 39

A primer on the changes to dragons in the new edition, done in fiction with game stat footnotes "Ecology" style.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 58

New products for May and June.

  • Shadowdale module.
  • Shadowdale novel.
  • The 1990 Forgotten Realms Calendar.
  • The Great Khan Game game folio.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 68

A review of Pool of Radiance.

Arrows of the East

By David Kloba. p. 80

New types of arrows as well as armor adjustments for the arrows in Oriental Adventures.
