Dragon issue 201, Civilized Centers of Adventure, was released in January of 1994. It contained the following article related to the Forgotten Realms:

The City of Lofty Pillars

By Steve Kurtz. pp. 10-14

This article describes the legendary city of Iram, seldom glimpsed in the lands of Zakhara (the Al-Qadim setting).
