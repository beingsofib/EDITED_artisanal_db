Dragon magazine 13 was released in April 1978. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

How Heavy Is My Giant

By Shlump Da Orc. p. 5

This article provides a mathematical formula for determining the weight of humanoid monsters, such as giants and golems.

Creatures

clay golems • giants • iron golems • stone golems

Notes from a Semi-Successful D&D Player.

By James Ward. p. 30

James Ward gives his tricks for utilizing magic-users to their full potential while adventuring in the dangerous worlds of the 1st edition Dungeons & Dragons game.

Spells

light • permanency • plant growth • polymorph
