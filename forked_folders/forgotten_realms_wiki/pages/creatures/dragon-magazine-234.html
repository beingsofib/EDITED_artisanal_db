Dragon issue 234, Beyond the Grave, was released in October of 1996. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Draconomicon

By Jamie Nossal, illustrated by Jim Holloway. p. 14-19

The dracolich isn’t the only form of undead dragon, only the greatest. Here are the “lesser evils.”

Wyrms of the North: Daurgothoth, The Creeping Doom

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Storn Cook. p. 32-36

Daurgothoth: An undead wyrm of the north.

The Dragon's Bestiary: The Necromancer's Armory

By Rudy Thauberger, illustrated by Ray Van Tilburg. p. 52-57

The necromancer's weapons are walking nightmares.

  • Beltyn's Burning Blood
  • Imbue Undead

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Lich Magical Items

By Johnathan M. Richards, illustrated by Bob Klasnich. p. 79–82

Magic items for your favorite undead, the Lich.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 116

New products for October and November.

  • Dangerous Games novel.
  • Murder in Halruaa novel.
  • Netheril: Empire of Magic sourcebook.
  • Spellfire Night Stalkers booster pack.
  • Stormlight novel.
  • Volo's Guide to All Things Magical sourcebook.
