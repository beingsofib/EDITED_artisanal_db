Badger mouse

Creature
nameBadger Mouse
locationDuskwood, Border Kingdoms

A badger mouse was a mouse-like rodent roughly the size of a badger. They were native to the Duskwood and were most abundant during the spring, when ash wheat was in good supply. Badger mice were preyed upon by puff adders.
