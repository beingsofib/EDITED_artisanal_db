Dragon issue 194, 17th Anniversary Issue: Dragons: Good to the last bite - and beyond, was released in June of 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Dragon Dogfights!

By Anne Brown. p. 13

Using dragon miniatures for an aerial duel.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 31

New products for June and July.

  • City of Delights sourcebook.
  • Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting 2nd edition sourcebook.
  • The Jungles of Chult sourcebook.

Breaking Them In

By Neil McGarry. p. 47

Incorporating novice players into your campaign.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 63

  • Clue Corner: Curse of the Azure Bonds
  • Clue Corner:Spelljammer: Pirates of Realmspace.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Tenser’s Bottling Co., Inc.

By Spike Y. Jones. p. 86–88

A plethora of new potions to add to your game.

With a Bond of Magic!

By Gregory W. Detwiler. p. 95

Bonded weapons: an element, material, or essence bonded together in the shape of a weapon.
