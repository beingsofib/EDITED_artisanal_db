Giant eels were a monstrous version of the commonly encountered eel, often resembling morays.

Description

Like most other types of eels they were long snake-like fish, often around long when adult.

Behavior

Giant eels were more ill-tempered than other types of eels.

Combat

Lacking the electric ability of the electric eels and great marine eels, giant eels would instead bite with their pointy teeth, striking with incredible speed.

Ecology

Habitat

While they predominantly lived in saltwater, around 1-in-10 giant eels were at home in freshwater instead.

Usage

The skin of giant eels was used to bind certain books, the binding being smooth, glossy, and turning black with time.

Locathah would ride atop giant eels wielding light lances in combat.

Some also used giant eels for towing ships through water in the Sea of Fallen Stars, where the giant eels could tow up to each, thus often requiring multiple giant eels working together to tow the ships.

Summoning

A giant eel could be summoned in freshwater environments with the summon monster V Spell.

History

Giant eels fought as part of Iakhovas's forces assaulting Voalidru in the during the Twelfth Serôs War.
