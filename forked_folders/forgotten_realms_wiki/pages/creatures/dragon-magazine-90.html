Dragon issue 90 was released in October of 1984. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The enchanting incantatrix

By Ed Greenwood. p. 8–14

The Incantatrix, a variant magic-user specialized in countering other mages.

From the Sorceror's Scroll: Hold that person!

By Gary Gygax. p. 16

The definitive list of charm-able humanoids.

Bats that do more than bite

By Ed Greenwood. p. 20-23

6 species of monstrous bat.

  • Azmyth
  • Night Hunter
  • Gloomwing
  • Hundar
  • Sinister
  • Werebat

Playing the political game

By Mike Beeman. p. 31

Designing political adventures.
