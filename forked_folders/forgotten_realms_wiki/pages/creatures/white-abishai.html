White abishai were the lowest-ranking of the abishai devils

Abilities

The stingers of white abishai froze those they struck, contributing to their bestial claws and fangs.

Combat

White abishai were savage and reckless, attacking with their claws, if not with longswords or flails. They were fearless in battle and on the battlefield become incredibly dangerous.

Society

When Tiamat decided that a soul was worthy of serving her she transformed it through an agonizing process into a white abishai. White abishai reinforced the ranks of Tiamat's legions with their fearless attitudes and combat prowess.
