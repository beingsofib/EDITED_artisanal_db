Vampire moss was a rare parasitic species of moss.

Description

Usually pale green in coloration, vampire moss grew in net-like masses ranging from in length that hung off of trees and branches. As it fed the moss grew brighter shade of green, becoming a vivid emerald green once fully sated.

Abilities

Vampire moss preyed upon other creatures in an aerial manner, magically attaching itself to prey instead of making physical contact and draining them of life energy from afar. Any creature that came within of it might feel a prickling sensation on the back of their neck, though a vampire moss always targeted those closest to it.

If harmed by any means other than fire, this moss would slowly regenerate over the course of weeks.

Ecology

Diet

This species of moss fed upon the life energy of other creatures. It most preferred mammals, especially intelligent ones, as they tended to have more vibrant energy. Only when on the verge of starvation would they resort to feeding upon the life energies of nearby trees.

Habitats

Vampire moss was typically found in swamps or tropical woodlands.

On Toril, vampire moss could be found in the High Moor region and rarely in the forests and mountains of the Savage Frontier.

Relationships

This species of moss served no particular purpose in the ecology of environments it was found in.

They were a favored plant of the nature deity Rillifane Rallathil.

Usages

Vampire moss could be used as an ingredient for energy draining magic, such as rings of weakness.
