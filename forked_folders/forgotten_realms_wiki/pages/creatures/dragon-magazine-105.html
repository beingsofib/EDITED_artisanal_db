Dragon issue 105 was released in January of 1986. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Leomund's Tiny Hut: Tone down the demi-humans

By Len Lakofka. p. 10

Balancing the new demihuman races: Wild & Dark elves, Grey dwarves, and Deep gnomes.

Travel works both ways

By Paul Vernon. p. 14

Randomly determining the people you meet walking down the road.

Seeing is believing

By Geoffrey Meissner. p. 20

A "look" at the three different types of invisibility.

The well-equipped victim

By Ed Greenwood. p. 30-32,34-35

A "treasure type" system for ordinary people.

TSR Profiles: Kim Mohan

By Uncredited. p. 62

A profile of editor Kim Mohan.
