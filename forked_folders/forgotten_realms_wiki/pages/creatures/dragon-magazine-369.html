Dragon issue 369 was released in November of 2008 as a PDF. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Playing Races: Playing Minotaurs

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 5

Minotaurs as a PC race, with some background.

Demonomicon of Iggwilv: Baphomet, the Prince of Beasts

By Robert J. Schwalb. pp. 18–30

What good are minotaurs without Baphomet, the Horned One?

Creature Incarnations: Gnolls

By Ari Marmell. p. 40

More of Yeenoghu’s get are described in this article,

Dragon Slayers

By Ari Marmell. p. 46

The other side of the coin of the recently-released Draconomicon: Chromatic Dragons.
