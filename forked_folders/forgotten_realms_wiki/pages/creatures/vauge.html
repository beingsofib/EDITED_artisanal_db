Vauge was a plant that grew wild in the Shaar. It was known as voj-weed in Khôltar, south Faerûn, circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR. Vauge was used to make tea and was available at Munsrum's Ready Ladle in Khôltar, the Iron City.
