Vine horrors were murderous and semi-intelligent mobile colonies of algae or other plants that assembled themselves into a vaguely humanoid form.

Description

Vine horrors looked like green humanoids with rough features and smooth, unformed faces. Their bodies writhed, and because they were comprised of plant matter, they were highly malleable and could contort and squeeze themselves through openings as narrow as without even slowing down. They could swim and move through swampy or forested terrain at great speed.

They were created via a natural albeit rare sequence of events that could only happen in areas of wilderness touched by the Shadowfell. If an evil humanoid was slain in such an area and their blood came to saturate the surrounding plant life, those plants would twist into the form of one or more vine horrors.

Behavior

They were primitive and driven by cruel and malicious instincts to hunt and slay any living creature that intruded upon their territory. They likewise did not care about the life or wellbeing of trees or other plants. A given vine horror might also manifest aspects of the humanoid whose death caused it to be created, with some showing enough intelligence to coordinate with and even support allies.

When they spoke, their voices changed pitch seemingly at random.

Abilities

A vine horror's signature ability was to animate surrounding trees and vines, whether to create an assassin vine to fight alongside them or simply to ensnare and crush their prey.

Vine horrors might also manifest the abilities of the humanoid whose death caused it to spawn. Such was the case with so-called spellfiends, which were vine horrors created via the blood of a wizard which could manifest arcane magical abilities, notably the power to summon lightning, cause fear, or create clouds of acidic gas.

Combat

They preferred to ambush their prey, often laying in wait within algae-filled water where they were exceedingly difficult to detect. They preferred to start their ambushes by animating nearby trees to attack targets one-at-a-time. In melee, they struck with their claws or with slams of lashing vines, with their plant-like appendages serving to both bludgeon and pierce their foes.

As they were composed of plants, vine horrors could not by traditional means be put to sleep, charmed, stunned, paralyzed, poisoned, or polymorphed, nor did they have any fatal weak points.

Ecology

Vine horrors were elusive, but could be found haunting swamps and jungles, searching for things to kill. or in vegetation-filled caves. They shared hunting ground alongside shambling mounds, and would sometimes cooperate with them. Intelligent swamp dwellers were also known to use vine horrors as minions, including hags (notably bog hags) and lizardfolk mystics. Their disregard for other plant life put them at odds with treants.

They were known to cultivate predatory vines.

Homelands

Vine horrors could be found in Cormanthor, the Dalelands, the Fallen Lands, the Meirig Peatlands of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles, the Reaching Woods,

Beyond Toril, they were said to be found in Shedaklah (the 222 layer of the Abyss) as well as in the Feydark.
