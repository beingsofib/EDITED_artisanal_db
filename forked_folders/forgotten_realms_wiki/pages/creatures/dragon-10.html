Dragon+ 10

Dragon+ issue 10 was released on October 28th, 2016. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Delving into Dungeonology

By John Houlihan. pp. 8

An interview with Matt Forbeck and Adam Lee about the upcoming book Dungeonology.

An Unlikely Hero

By Matt Chapman. pp. 11

An interview with R.A. Salvatore about the upcoming novel Hero.

Volo's Guide

By John Houlihan. pp. 14

A preview of Volo's Guide to Monsters.

Neverwinter sets sail on the Sea of Moving Ice

By John Houlihan. pp. 15

A preview of the Storm King's Thunder module for the Neverwinter MMORPG.

Demogorgon: Prince of Demons

By Shannon Applecline. pp. 16

A short design history of Demogorgon.

Map of the Month

By Bart Carroll, Cartography by Mike Schley. pp. 19

Several maps from the Curse of Strahd adventure module.
