Dragon issue 75 was released in July of 1983. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Ecology of the Mimic

By Ed Greenwood. p. 6

A scholarly treatise on the Mimic.

From the Sorcerer's Scroll: New denizens of devildom

By Gary Gygax. p. 8-14

A partial preview of Monster Manual II.

  • Abishai
  • Bael
  • Belial
  • Glasya
  • Mammon
  • Mephistopheles
  • Moloch

The Nine Hells, Part I

By Ed Greenwood. p. 16-33

Detailed descriptions of the first 5 layers of the Nine Hells (and their inhabitants) in the Forgotten Realms.

  • Avernus
  • Dis
  • Minauros
  • Phlegethos
  • Stygia

Beyond the rulebook: Procedure and style tips for good GMing

By Lewis Pulsipher. p. 38

20 guidelines for being a good GM.
