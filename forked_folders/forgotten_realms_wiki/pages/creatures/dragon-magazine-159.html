Dragon issue 159, One world of adventure is never enough, was released in July of 1990. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Rough Times on Refuge

By Ed Greenwood. p. 10-14

Describes Refuge, a spaceport run by the Arcane in a crystal sphere close to Realmspace.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Magic from the stars

By Steven E. Schend. p. 15–18

12 magic items for spelljammers.

Items

boots of star striding • cabinet of air restoration

Voidjammers!

By Randal S. Doering. p.20-26, 101

Want to hitch a ride in the Astral plane? Here's how!

The Dragon's Bestiary: The call of the wildspace

By Harold Johnson, Timothy B. Brown, and Troy Denning. p. 30–35

3 new monsters from space, formatted Monstrous Compendium style.

  • Andeloid
  • Infernite
  • Metagolem

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 53

Clue Corner: Pool of Radiance.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 86

New products for July and August.

  • Black Courser module.
  • Monstrous Compendium Kara-Tur Appendix
  • The Forgotten Realms Atlas
