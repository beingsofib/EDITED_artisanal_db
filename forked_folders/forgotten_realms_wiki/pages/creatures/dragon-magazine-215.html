Dragon issue 215, A gathering of gaming guidance, was released in March of 1995. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Putting Evil to Good Use

By Johnathan M. Richards. p. 11

Good uses for bad weapons.

Gaming with Style

By Thomas M. Kane. p. 16

Taking advantage of various styles of play.

The Beginner's Game

By John Nephew. p. 22

Attracting and keeping new RPG players.

The Ecology of the Amphisbaena

By Johnathan M. Richards. p. 32-38

A scholarly treatise on the Amphisbaena.

The Role of Books

By John C. Bunnell. p. 70

Review of Elminster: The Making of a Mage.

The Game Wizards - How to build the Blood Wars deck from heck

By Steven Schend. p. 97-100

Get all the dope on the new Blood Wars card game — including a complete card list.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 120

New products for April.

  • Elminster's Ecologies Appendix I sourcebook.
  • Ruins of Zhentil Keep sourcebook.
  • Shadows of Doom novel.
