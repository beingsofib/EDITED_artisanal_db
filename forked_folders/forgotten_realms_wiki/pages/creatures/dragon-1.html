Dragon+ 1

Dragon+ issue 1 was released on April 30, 2015. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Elemental Evil: Unearth the Deception

By Matt Chapman.

A look at the various enemies and products in the Elemental Evil storyline.

Travel Talk: The Dessarin River Valley

By Melisande Calador

An in-character travelogue by the ranger Melisande Calador, describing various sites and sights in the Dessarin Valley.

Profile: Goliaths

By Chris Lindsay

A description of the goliath race.

Sword Coast Legends

By Dan Tudge

A preview of the video game Sword Coast Legends, featuring Dan Tudge, president of the developing company .

Player Factions: Our Heroes?

By Matt Chapman.

A brief description of each of the five factions players can align themselves with in the Elemental Evil storyline.

  • Harpers
  • Order of the Gauntlet
  • Emerald Enclave
  • Lords' Alliance
  • Zhentarim

Know Your Enemy: Black Dragons

By Bhric Kharas

An in-character guide to fighting dragons, narrated by monster hunter Bhric Kharas. Features the dragons Velathidros, Nurvureem, and Vartilingorix.

Interview: Rob Overmeyer

An interview with Rob Overmeyer, executive producer of Neverwinter, on the upcoming Elemental Evil module.
