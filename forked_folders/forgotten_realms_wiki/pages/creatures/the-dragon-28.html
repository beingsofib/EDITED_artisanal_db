The Dragon issue 28 was released in August of 1979. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

From The Sorcerer's Scroll

By Gary Gygax. p. 10

Evil: Law vs. Chaos; an examination of the evil alignments.

Fantasysmith's Notebook

By Phil Neuscheler. p. 14

A guide to buying miniatures for your Dungeons & Dragons/Advanced Dungeons & Dragons game.

Bazaar of the Bizarre

By Len Lakofka. p. 31

Several new magic items added to the AD&D roster.
