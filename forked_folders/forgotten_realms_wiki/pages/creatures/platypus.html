A platypus was a species strange animal that lived on a small island off the coast of Orlil, Lantan, alongside a village of intelligent and speaking wombats.

Description

A platypus was a natural beast that seemingly was cobbled together out of leftover parts of other animals. They had beaver-like tails and bodies, duck-like bills, webbed feet for swimming, and venomous barbs.

Ecology

Platypuses laid eggs and lived in the vicinity of water, riverbanks, and lakes. A sentient group of wombats and platypuses could be found inhabiting a small island off the coast of Orlil.
