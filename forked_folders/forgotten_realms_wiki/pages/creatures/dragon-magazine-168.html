Dragon issue 168, As the wyrm terns, was released in April of 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 52

Clue Corner: Curse of the Azure Bonds.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 64

New products for April and May.

  • Feathered Dragon novel.
  • Nightwatch in the Living City module.
  • Nightmare Keep module.
  • Sojourn novel.

Treasures More Real

By Scott Williams. p. 79

Making your treasure troves into more than just "300 sp, 213 gp, and 3 500-gp gems."

Harpers Bold

By Ed Greenwood. p. 84–85

Jhastar Belnold and Talantra Bowgentle, two stalwart Harpers.
