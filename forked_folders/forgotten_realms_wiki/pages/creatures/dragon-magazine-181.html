Dragon issue 181, Every great wizard starts with a dream, was released in May of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Sorcery & Strategy

By Thomas M. Kane. p. 12

A look at the strategic and tactical applications of magic in a fantasy battle.

Pages from the Mages

By Ed Greenwood. p. 16-20

Why you don't want to be hit by a blackstaff spell, and more. Another visit from you-know-who with four more you-know-what.

Galadaster's Orizon

  • Firestaff
  • Geirdorn's grappling grasp
  • Morgannaver's sting
  • Slumberward

The Arcanabula of Jume

  • Dark mirror
  • Prismatic eye
  • Shadow gauntlet
  • Shadow hand

Laeral's Libram

  • Blackstaff
  • Forcewave
  • Jhanifer's deliquescence
  • Laeral's aqueous column

Tasso's Arcanabula

  • Chromatic blade
  • Shadow bolt
  • Shadow skeleton
  • Tasso's shriek

That's Certainly Un-Familiar

By Len Carpenter. p. 24

Rules for improving familiars.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Celestial Fury

By Chris Hind p. 32

A katana from Kara-Tur with the spirit of a dragon that loves violence trapped inside it.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Dragon Bones

By Chris Hind p. 32

Magic bones that aid in auguries.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 59-60

Clue Corner: Curse of the Azure Bonds , Eye of the Beholder , Gateway to the Savage Frontier.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 64

New products for June.

  • Haunted Halls of Eveningstar module.
  • The Night Parade novel.
