Gurglewort

Plant
nameGurglewort
typeFlowering plant

Gurglewort was a type of flowering plant found in Faerûn.

Description

It had a beautiful flower, but its sap was sickening to the digestive system if consumed.

Usage

When correctly prepared, gurglewort sap had some use as a herbal remedy.
