A jubbrawuk was a type of dragon of fantastical appearance and a long neck. The name "jubbrawuk" was used by some sages.

Statues of jubbrawuks were among the statues of gargoyles, gremlins, satyrs, and wyverns that adorned Oldstone Hall and its walls, in Wheloon in Cormyr in the mid–14 century DR.
