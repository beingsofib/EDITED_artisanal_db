Dragon issue 303, Gladiators, was released in January of 2003. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Campaign Components: Gladiators

By Dean Poisso. p. 20

Everything you wanted to know about gladiators.

Class Acts: Blood and Fame

By Kolja Liquette. p. 42

The gladiator prestige class.

Saying the Right Things

By Eric Cagle. p. 48

Optional rules for character social skills.

The Iron Path

By Dean Poisso. p. 54

Martial arts styles for fantasy cultures.

The Undergroves

By Tim Hitchcock. p. 62

The natural dangers of the Underdark.

Guild Secrets: Academy Necromica

By Andy Collins. p. 68

A necromancer's guild.

Silicon Sorcery: The Lost Horrors of Neverwinter Nights

By Clifford Horowitz, illustrated by Mike Sass and Todd Grenier. p. 80–86

Presenting monsters from Neverwinter Nights.

  • Battle intellect devourer
