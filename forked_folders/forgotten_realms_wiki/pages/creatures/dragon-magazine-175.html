Dragon issue 175, What does it take to build a world?, was released in November of 1991. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 38

New products for December.

  • Anauroch sourcebook.
  • Red Magic novel.

The Role of Computers

By Hartley Lesser, Patricia Lesser and Kirk Lesser. p. 66

Clue Corner: Curse of the Azure Bonds and Secret of the Silver Blades .

Only the Best Will Do!

By Uncredited. p. 72

The winners of the 1990 Origins Awards.

  • Best Role-Playing Supplement, 1990: Forgotten Realms Adventures.

Rhyme & Reason

By Scott Roach. p. 87

The uses of riddles in your games.

The Answer Is...the Riddle!

By Mark Anthony. p. 91

How to create your own riddles.

Review: Elfshadow

By John C. Bunnell. p. 97
