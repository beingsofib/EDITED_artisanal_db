Dragon issue 126 was released in October of 1987. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms.

Dead on Target

By David Howery. p. 26

How to pick the right weapon to use against the undead.

A Touch of Evil

By Vince Garcia. p. 29-34

Advice on using the 31 types of undead published in Monster Manual, Monster Manual II, and Fiend Folio.

The Ecology of the Shade

By Dan Salas. p. 40-43

A scholarly treatise on the Shade.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Treasures of the Orient

By Gregory W. Detwiler. p. 50–51

12 new magic items with an Asian feel.
