Dragon issue 392 was released in PDF format only in October of 2010. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Class Acts: Beastmasters

By Stephen Radney-MacFarland. p. 23

Armed with this article, beastmaster rangers will have two new beast companions to choose from--the horse and the simian--and new powers for all of the beast companions.

Class Acts: Fey Wardens

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 26–29

Fey wardens are charged with preserving lands touched or otherwise influenced by the Feywild.

Bazaar of the Bizarre: Illumian Echoes

By Robert J. Schwalb, illustrated by Slawomir Maniak. p. 30–32

The illumian race is gone, but the words of power which led to their downfall still echo through the ruins of their dead world.
