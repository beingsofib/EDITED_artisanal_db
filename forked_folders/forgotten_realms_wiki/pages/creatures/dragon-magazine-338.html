Dragon issue 338, Magic, was released in December of 2005. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Schools of Magic

By Alfred O. Cloutier. p. 24

Philosophical schools of magic for your wizard specialists.

Staffs of the Magi

By Ben Vandgrift. p. 54

Imbuing your Wizard's staff.

Ecology of the Spell Weaver

By Tito Leati. p. 62

A scholarly treatise on the Spell weaver.

The Wormhunter

By James Jacobs. p. 70

A specialized prestige class.

Spellcraft: The Silver Hexameric Folio

By Gregory Marks. p. 76

Spell weaver spells you don\'t need six arms to cast.
