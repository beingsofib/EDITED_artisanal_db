Broken Pillars

Broken Pillars was the thirty-second issue of the Advanced Dungeons &amp; Dragons comics series set in the Forgotten Realms and published by . It was written by Dan Mishkin, drawn by Tom Mandrake, and edited by Kim Yale. It is the second and final instalment of the "Dwarven Gold" story arc.
