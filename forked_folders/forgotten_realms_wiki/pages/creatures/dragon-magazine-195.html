Dragon issue 195, The world belongs to the warriors, was released in July of 1993. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

To All a Good Knight

By Tom Griffith. p. 18

Background on religious knightly orders, with guidelines for creating your own.

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 24

New products for July and August.

  • City of Delights sourcebook.
  • Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting 2nd edition sourcebook.
  • Prince of Lies novel.

Overcoming Obstacles

By Justin Mohareb. p. 81

Playing hadicapped heroes.

Up Front, In Charge

By Thomas M. Kane. p. 98

How to choose a party leader, and how to take charge when you need to.
