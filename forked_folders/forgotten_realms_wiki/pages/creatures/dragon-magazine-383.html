Dragon issue 383 was released in PDF format only in January of 2010. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Devotees of the Gibbous Moon: Secrets of the Beast Form

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 35–39

Learn about these wild shaping druids and the primal spirits they command.

Class Acts (4e): Ranger

By Peter Schaefer. p. 49

Make your bow ranger even deadlier with these new ranged exploits.

Class Acts (4e): Wizard

By Peter Lee. p. 52

Learn the secrets of evokers, wizards who blast their way to success.

Class Acts (4e): The Bard

By Arthur Wright. p. 54

New options for bards to use when harassing their foes.

Class Acts (4e): Warden — Wardens of The Breach

By Robert J. Schwalb. p. 57

Wardens who combat intrusions from the Elemental Chaos, detailed here.

Class Acts (4e): Shaman

By Tavis Allison. p. 63

New options for shamans and their spirit companions.

Winning Races: Humans

By Stephen Radney MacFarland. p. 66

Humans have a knack for adapting and surviving any conditions. Learn more about their techniques.

D&D Alumni: Underdark

By Bart Carroll and Steve Winter. p. 88

The origins of the Drow and the Underdark.
