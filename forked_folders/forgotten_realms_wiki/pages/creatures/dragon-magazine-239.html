Dragon issue 239, Traps and Dirty Tricks, was released in September of 1997. It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms:

Sneaky Sea Devils

By Skip Williams. p. 8

Sahuagin secrets that didn't make it into The Sea Devils.

101 Dirty Orc Tricks

By John Baichtal. p. 14

Annoying things for your orcs to do.

Aurora's Undermountain Sale

By Paul Culotta. p. 21

Items for the spelunker to buy.

Magical Locks

By Charles M. ANdrulis and Robert S. Mullin. p. 26

Sorcerous security measures.

Wyrms of the North: Galadaeros, The Sunset Flame

By Ed Greenwood, illustrated by Storn Cook. p. 30–35

Galadaeros, a copper dragon of the northern Sword Coast.

The Ecology of the Stirge

By Tim Richardson, illustrated by Tom Baxa. p. 40–44

A scholarly treatise on the Stirge.

The Dragon's Bestiary: The Little People

By Brian Corvello, illustrated by David Day and Jim Holloway. p. 48–54

Four tricky little fellows.

  • Boggart
  • Clurichaun
  • Leshy
  • Wicked leprechaun

Arcane Lore: Larcenous legerdemain

By Jeff Dancey, illustrated by David Day. p. 57–60

New spells for the criminally inclined.

  • Appraisal
  • Audio enhancer
  • Disawareness
  • Feet of the cat
  • Forgery
  • Ghostly garrote
  • Instant lockpick
  • Lock inspection
  • Major disarm
  • Minor disarm
  • Muffling blow
  • Remote access
  • Silent saw

Dungeon Mastery: Mind Games and Other Dirty Tricks for DMs

By Ed Carmien. p. 78

Review: Netheril: Empire of Magic

By Rick Swan. p. 110

TSR Previews

By Uncredited. p. 116

New products for September, October and November.

  • Elminster in Myth Drannor novel.
  • Four from Cormyr module.
  • The Council of Blades novel.
  • The Simbul's Gift novel.
