Will-o'-wisp language

Language
nameWill-o'-wisp
speakersNyths, Will-o'-wisps

The Will-o'-wisp language was an entirely optical language used by will-o'-wisps, Nyths, and variants found in the Domains of Dread, such as will o'deeps and will o'seas.

Description

Described by some as merely a "flickering light" language, will-o'-wisps and its other "speakers" conveyed ideas through changing the color and or the brightness or intensity of their light-based bodies.

The alien and subtle nature of this language made it largely incomprehensible to the average human, demihuman, and most other creatures.
