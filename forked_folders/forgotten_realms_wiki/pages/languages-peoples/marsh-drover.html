The Marsh Drovers were a people inhabiting the Farsea Marshes.

History

It was believed that the Marsh Drovers descended from those humans of Anauria that fled the destruction of their kingdom in the . The Chauntean cleric Twick theorized that, prior to settling in the Farsea Marshes, this group lived in the Goblin Marches for a while, migrating south due to the combined pressure of the orc and goblin kingdoms, as well as the hostile climate.
