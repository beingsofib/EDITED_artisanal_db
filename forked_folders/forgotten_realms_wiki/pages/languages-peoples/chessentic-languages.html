The Chessentic languages composed a branch of the Chessan languages that included the modern language Chessentan.

Languages

The Chessentic languages were those Chessan languages spoken around the Sea of Fallen Stars near Chessenta. The group included two dead languages and two living languages:

  • Auld Chessic (also known as "Alambic", dead language)
  • Thresk (dead language)
  • Chessentan (also known as "Chessic")
  • Alambit
