Commani was a language in the Imaskari languages group, spoken in the western Hordelands.

Description

Like other languages in the same region, Commani had round and broad vowel sounds with rolled R sounds.

Speakers

This tongue was spoken by the nomadic Commani tribe.

Script

Commani did not have a written form.

History

It was considered to be one of the Northern Imaskari languages and was strongly influenced by the empire of Raumathar.
