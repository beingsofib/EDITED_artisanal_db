The Tayanulchi, also spelled Tayanuchi, were a tribe of humans living in the Ama Basin in the Northern Wastes of Kara-Tur. They split from the Issacortae and disappeared into the Land of the Snow Demons centuries before the 14th century DR.

Society

Having broken off from the Issacortae, the Tayanulchi likely spoke the Issacortae language.
