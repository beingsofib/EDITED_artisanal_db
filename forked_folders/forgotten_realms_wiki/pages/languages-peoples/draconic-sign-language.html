Draconic sign language

Language
nameDraconic sign language
scriptNone
speakersScalamagdrions

Draconic sign language was a form of non-verbal communication used by draconic creatures. It involved a series of movements of a dragon's maw, claws and wings.

Speakers

Scalamagdrions were known for communicating with this form of sign language.
