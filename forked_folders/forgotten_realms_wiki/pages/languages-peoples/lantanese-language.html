Lantanese, also known as Lantanna,

Speakers

Other regions where Lantanese was commonly spoken included the Nelanther Isles and Tethyr.

Script

Literate Lantanese speakers used the Draconic alphabet.

Related Languages

Some scholars classified Lantanna as an Imaskari language,

Examples

Common female names in Lantanese were Avilda, Bersace, Charissa, Melsany, Phaerilda, Ravace, and Umbrasy. Male names might be Eberc, Fodoric, Koger, Lambrac, Midoc, Norbert, Samber, or Tibidoc. Common Lantanese surnames included Angalstrand, Decirc, Lamstrand, SeKorc, SeLangstra, and SeMilderic.
