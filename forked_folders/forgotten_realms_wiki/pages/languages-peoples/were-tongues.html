The Were-Tongues or Were-Tongues of Lycanthropy was a general term to refer collectively to the racial languages of lycanthropes. Every type of lycanthrope had its own distinct racial language. These languages were not mutually intelligible, thus weretigers could not speak the language of werebears and vice-versa.

Notable Speakers

  • Sea druids, a type of specialty priest in the Church of Deep Sashelas, were known to learn various other aquatic languages. These included those of aquatic lycanthropes, such as weresharks.
