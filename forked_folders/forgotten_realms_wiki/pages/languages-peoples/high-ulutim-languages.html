The descriptive term "High Ulutim" referred to two closely related Ulou languages spoken in the northernmost ("high") reaches of Faerûn by the Ulutiun people.

Languages

The High Ulutim languages were Uluik and Ulutiun. The Ulutiun people could similarly be split into two distinct cultural groups—those living on the Sea of Moving Ice were called the "Ice Hunters" and spoke Uluik; those living on the Great Glacier were the "Ulutiun" and spoke Ulutiun.

Script

By 1372 DR Uluik and Ulutiun had only recently begun using the Thorass script, if writing was used at all.
