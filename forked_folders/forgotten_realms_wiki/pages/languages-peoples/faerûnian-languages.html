The Faerûnian languages were a family of languages spoken by the majority of humans on the continent of Faerûn. The Faerûnian language family encompassed roughly 50 distinct human languages, including Common.

Language Groups

The following language groups were considered by scholars to belong to the Faerûnian language family:

  • Illuski
  • Waelan
  • Ulou
  • Thorass
  • Chessan
  • Raumtheran

Script

The vast majority of languages in this family used the Thorass alphabet.
