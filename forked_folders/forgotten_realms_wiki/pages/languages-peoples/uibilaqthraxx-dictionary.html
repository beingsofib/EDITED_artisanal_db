Uibilaqthraxx dictionary

A list of words and phrases from the Uibilaqthraxx beholder language, an ancestor of the Quevquel language.

D

daeth

wise, far-sighted, thoughtful, or strategic.

H

haera

a gathering or alliance.

haeromm

the members of a gathering or alliance.

L

lurl'uk'lok'lahassarruin

"little-kin" or similar. Referred to small beholderkin such as gazers. Led to the Quevquel word sarruin.
