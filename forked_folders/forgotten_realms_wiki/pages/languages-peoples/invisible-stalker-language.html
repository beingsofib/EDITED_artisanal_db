Invisible Stalker language

Language
nameInvisible Stalker
speakersInvisible stalkers

The Invisible Stalker language, properly known as Sshai, was the racial language of invisible stalkers, a race of elemental beings native to the Elemental Plane of Air.

Description

The sounds of this language were reminiscent of the roaring and whooshing that could be heard during wind storms.

Notable Speakers

  • Mempter, a mysterious and powerful elementalist mage.
