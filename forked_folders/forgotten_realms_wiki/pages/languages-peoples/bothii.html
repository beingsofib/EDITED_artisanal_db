Bothii was the language spoken by the Uthgardt barbarian tribes and the inhabitants of Hartsvale.

Bothii was treated by some as a dialect of Illuskan. Bothii itself consisted of two dialects, one spoken by the Uthgardt, the other by the Hartsvalers.
