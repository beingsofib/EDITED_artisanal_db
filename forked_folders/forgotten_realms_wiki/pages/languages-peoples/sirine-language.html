Sirine language

Language
nameSirine
groupFey languages
speakersSirines

The Sirine language was the racial language of sirines, a type of aquatic fey, spoken by both those in Faerûn and Zakhara.

Speakers

Beyond sirines themselves, Sirine was a common secondary language among asrai, water sprites of the Upper Planes, and the specialty priests of gods that held dominion over water. and the waterwalkers of Istishia.
