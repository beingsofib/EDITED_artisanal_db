Selkie language

Language
nameSelkie
speakersSelkies

The Selkie language was the language spoken by selkies, an aquatic race of shapechangers.

Speakers

Beyond selkies themselves, Selkie was a common secondary language among arctic dwarves and sea elves of the Inner Sea.

Notable Speakers

  • Nimtu, one of a pair of humans who owned The Painted Boat restaurant.
