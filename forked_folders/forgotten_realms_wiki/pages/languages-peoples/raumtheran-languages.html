The Raumtheran language group was a small branch of the Faerûnian languages family that included languages spoken in northeastern regions of Faerûn, such as Rashemen.

Languages

The following languages were considered Raumtheran languages:

  • Halardrim (dead language)
  • Rashemi (also known as Rasallesian)
  • Allesian
  • Sossic
