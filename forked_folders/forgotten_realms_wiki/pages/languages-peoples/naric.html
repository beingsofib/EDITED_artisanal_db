Naric was a language spoken by the Nars, from Icelace Lake in the north to Rawlinswood in the south, and from the Giantspire Mountains in the west to the Icerim Mountains in the east. It was one of the Low Ulutim languages. It was presumably derived from the ancient language of Narfelli.

Most Nars also spoke Damaran.
