The Hill giant language, spoken by both hill giants and mountain giants. It was closely related to Jotunise, the language of frost giants.

Speakers

In addition to the hill giants spread across the breadth of Faerûn, the hill giant language was used among many of the merchants who did business within the Shining Lands of the Shining South.

Notable Speakers

  • Arkaneus Silvermane, the Archdruid of Mosstone.
