Gur was the language spoken by the Gur people of the Hordelands.

Description

Gur was a nasal language, with long E and I vowels drawn out.

History

Gur was considered one of the Eastern Imaskari languages, and was strongly influenced by the Shou Chiang languages of Kara-Tur.
