Kara-Turan languages

Language
nameKara-Turan languages
speakersHumans

The Kara-Turan languages were a family of languages spoken by the majority of humans on the continent of Kara-Tur.

Languages

The following language groups belonged to the Kara-Turan language family:

  • Amaese languages
  • Han languages
  • Shou Chiang languages

In addition, the following languages were also among this family:

  • Bavanese
  • Bertanese
  • Kuong
  • Laothan
  • Lidahan
  • Purang
  • Tabotan
  • Trade Tongue

The Chuchian language from the Imaskari languages family was also found among the languages of Kara-Tur.
