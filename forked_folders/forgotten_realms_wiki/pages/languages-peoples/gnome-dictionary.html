Gnome dictionary

A list of words in the Gnome language.

C

cosim camman denoctusd

"the settled ground before the earthquake", which in the Common tongue would be close to the phrase, "the calm before the storm".

E

estel

"rose"

F

forstneblin

"forest gnome"

G

Gond'ral thra'kazhul nesh

"May Gond be with you."

H

harnor

"monstrous spider"; plural is harnorl

K

karkolohk

"home"

kagu-svirfneblin

"stone-seekers"

M

magga cammara

"by the stones".

mustikka

"blueberry"

N

norl

"spider"; plural is norra

P

praed

"male homosexual" (slang)

S

steinneblin

"rock gnome"

storgin

"stone-eater"

svirfneblin

"deep gnome"

T

thoulal

"transgender"

tukkavaaskivo

"little mean one", a Vani word for a badger
