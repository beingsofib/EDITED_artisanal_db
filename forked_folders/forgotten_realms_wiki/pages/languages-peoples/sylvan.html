Sylvan

Language
nameSylvan
scriptEspruar
speakersFey

Sylvan was the language of the fey and woodland creatures. Its written form used the Espruar alphabet.

Dictionary

G

groef

Title of a grig prince in a local dialect of the High Forest; for example, Groef Feythrin.

H

hurbryn

"heavy-footed ones". Used to refer to humans, especially by brownies, korreds and satyrs.

hykyath (Hik ah YATH)

"Prance!"—A parting used by satyrs.

S

sabbas

"Run free!"—A parting used by centaurs.
