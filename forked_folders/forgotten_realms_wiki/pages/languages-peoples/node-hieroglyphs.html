Node hieroglyphs

Language
nameNode hieroglyphs
speakersSpellweaver

Node hieroglyphs were the writing system used by the spellweaver race. Far from a simple alphabet, hieroglyphs were closer to a complex cipher, the meaning of the symbols varying from one document to the other. Sages theorized that this was due to the alien way the spellweaver brain worked, encoding information in complex patterns incomprehensible to other creatures.

Alphabet

The spellweaver alphabet was made of at least 29 symbols.

Numeric system

Spellweavers used two different numeric systems.

They also developed a decimal numeral system to work with other races, such as the nerra.
