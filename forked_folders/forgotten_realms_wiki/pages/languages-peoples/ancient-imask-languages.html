Ancient Imask languages

Language
nameAncient Imask languages
familyImaskari languages
speakersHumans

The Ancient Imask language group was a linguistic pairing of two dead languages spoken in the region of the Old Empires. These were Rauran and Reian, which were believed spoken by races of people who predated the Mulan.
