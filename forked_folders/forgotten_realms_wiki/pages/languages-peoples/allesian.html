Allesian was a language spoken by the inhabitants of Thesk and Ashanath, the region southwest of Lake Ashane, from Kront in the north to the River Umber in the south and from Telflamm in the west to the Plateaus of Thay in the east.

It was a Raumtheran language, just as Rashemi, which was spoken to the northeast. It had two dialects, Ashane and Golden Allesian.
