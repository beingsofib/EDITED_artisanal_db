The Kozakurans were a human ethnicity who lived on the islands of Kozakura in Kara-Tur. They were similar to the Wanese people.

Notable Kozakurans

  • Ishi Barasume
  • Tamoko
  • Yoshimo
  • Kymiko Piiradost: Consort to Lord Humbraz Piiradost III of Waterdeep in the 14 century DR.
