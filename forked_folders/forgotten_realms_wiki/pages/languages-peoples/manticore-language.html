Manticore language

Language
nameManticore
speakersManticores, mantidrakes

The Manticore language was the racial language of manticores and mantidrakes.

Speakers

Beyond manticores and mantidrakes, this was a secondary language among many druids.

Notable Speakers

  • Arkaneus Silvermane, the Archdruid of Mosstone.
