High Wealdan

Language
nameHigh Wealdan
familyElven language

High Wealdan was an ancient elven language more akin to music than a spoken tongue. In written form, it appeared as carved glyphs that could be activated by running one's fingers along the relief. This produced a melodic rasp and haunting song.
