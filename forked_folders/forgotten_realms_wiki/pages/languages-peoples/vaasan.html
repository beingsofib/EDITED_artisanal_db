Vaasans, also called Tesharans, Tharrans, or Yûls, were one of the lesser-known ethnic groups of humanity. The term referred to the peoples of Vaasa as well as parts of the Moonsea and the Dalelands.

Description

Vaasan people typically had skin tones ranging from fair to olive with dark hair. and they grew thick beards.

History

Vaasan heritage traces back to Netherese ancestry. After the fall of Netheril's survivor states of Asram, Anauria, and Hlondath, Netherese refugees traveled east to the Moonsea to found new realms, beginning with Teshar in the , followed by Yûlash and Northkeep. These people would be joined over the following centuries by the second group that would come to be known as the forebears of Vaasan culture, Chondathans from the Dalelands, Sembia, and the Vast. The two peoples would go on to found the Moonsea city-states of Hulburg, Melvaunt, Mulmaster, Phlan, Thentia, and Zhentil Keep.

By the late 15th Century DR, Vaasa had seen an influx of Damaran immigrants from Damara, Impiltur, and Narfell who had integrated into Vaasan society.
