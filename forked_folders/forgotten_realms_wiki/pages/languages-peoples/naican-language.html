Naican was a language in the Imaskari languages group, spoken in the Hordelands.

Description

Naican had round and broad vowel sounds with rolled R sounds. Many of its consonant sounds were harsh, such as C and K. The sound of L was heavily aspirated. Its grammar had a subject–object–verb order.

Speakers

This tongue was spoken by the nomadic Naican tribe.

Script

Naican did not have a written form.

History

It was a hybrid language derived from both the Northern and Southern Imaskari language groups. It was influenced by the empire of Raumathar and also the language of Muhjuri.
