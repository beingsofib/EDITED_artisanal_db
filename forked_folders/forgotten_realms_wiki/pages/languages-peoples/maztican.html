"Maztican" was a collective term used by Faerûnians for any of the inhabitants of Maztica. When most folk in Faerûn used the term, they were most likely referring to Nexalans, as that was the only people group of Maztica for which individuals had traveled to the east. In truth, there were seven separate ethnicities of humans in Maztica:

  • Nexalans
  • Azuposi
  • Dog People
  • Green Folk
  • Metahel
  • Nahopaca
  • Payits
