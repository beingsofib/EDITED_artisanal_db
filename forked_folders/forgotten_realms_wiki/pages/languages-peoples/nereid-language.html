The Nereid language was the racial language of nereids, a type of aquatic fey.

Speakers

Beyond nereids themselves, Nereid was a common secondary language among the pahari of Zakhara, some planeswalkers, and the specialty priests of gods that held dominion over water. the waterwalkers of Istishia, and the waveservants of Umberlee.

Notable Speakers

  • Nimtu, one of a pair of humans who owned The Painted Boat restaurant, learned to speak a smattering of the Nereid language after befriending the nereid Anemone.
