Orc, also known as Orcish or Daraktan, was the common language written and spoken by most orcs. Daraktan used the Dethek script.

History

Orc evolved from the archaic Hulgorkyn language used by the orcs.

Orc names

The following typical names were used by orcs in Faerûn:

Given names

  • Male: Besk, Durth, Fang, Gothog, Harl, Kesk, Orrusk, Tharag, Thog, Ugurth
  • Female: Betharra, Creske, Edarreske, Duvaega, Neske, Orvaega, Varra, Yeskarra

Surnames

  • Dummik, Horthor, Lammar, Sormuzhik, Turnskull, Ulkrunnar, Zorgar
