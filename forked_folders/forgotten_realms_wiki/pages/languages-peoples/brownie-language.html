The Brownie language was the racial language of brownies, a type of fey.

Speakers

Beyond brownies themselves, Brownie was a common secondary language in Faerûn among forest gnomes, lightfoot halflings, rock gnomes, and tallfellow halflings. Beyond demihumans, Brownie was also commonly spoken by other fey creatures, such as dobies, grigs, killmoulis, and stwingers. And elven cats who attached themselves to brownie families typically spoke Brownie to a rudimentary degree.

Brownie was also common as a secondary language among druids devoted to certain nature deities. druids of Eldath, druids and druid/rangers of Mielikki, the druids of Silvanus, and the druids of Sheela Peryroyl. Other forms of specialty priests in the Realms for whom Brownie was a commonly known secondary language were the Eldathyn peacemen.

Notable Speakers

  • Alrina Skyeyes, a human wizard who traveled as part of the crew of the Osprey.
  • Jack Mooney, the renowned owner of Jack Mooney & Sons Circus, along with his sons Edan Mooney and Lockwood Mooney.
