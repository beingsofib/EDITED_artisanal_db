__NOTOC__

Old Illuskan was the language of Old Illusk and the parent language of modern Illuskan, Bothii, and Reghedjic. It also influenced the modern language of Dambrathan.

Script

Old Illuskan had its own alphabet. By 1372 DR, the only known samples of this script were located in the Green Library in Ruathym and in the Host Tower of the Arcane in Luskan.
