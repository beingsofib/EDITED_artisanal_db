The Untheric languages group was one of the two major branches of the Rauric languages family and included languages spoken in both Faerûn and Zakhara.

Subgroups

The Untheric languages included the Untheric language spoken in Unther and also those of two language subgroups, the Midani and Alzho languages. while the Midani and Alzho languages were shaped by the languages of genies.

History

By the late 15 century DR, the Untheric group had risen to become the third most active language group in Faerûn.
