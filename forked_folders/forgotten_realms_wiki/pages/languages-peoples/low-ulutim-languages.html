The term "Low Ulutim" described a grouping of closely related Ulou languages spoken by small populations of people in various Faerûnian locales.

Languages

The Low Ulutim languages were spoken at lower latitudes than the High Ulutim languages. The following were Low Ulutim languages:

  • Rengardt (dead language)
  • Narfelli (dead language)
  • Uloushinn (also considered a dialect of Midani, a member of the Rauric languages family)
  • Andt
  • Erakic
  • Naric
