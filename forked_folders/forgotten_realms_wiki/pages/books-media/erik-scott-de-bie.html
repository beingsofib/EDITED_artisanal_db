Erik Scott de Bie

Erik Scott de Bie (born July 18, 1983) is an American fantasy author and technical writer. He has a degree in English Composition and Literature from in . His wife is named Shelley and he has several cats and a dog. His hobbies include fencing, rock music, movies and computer games. He is a self-confessed geek.{{Cite web|
