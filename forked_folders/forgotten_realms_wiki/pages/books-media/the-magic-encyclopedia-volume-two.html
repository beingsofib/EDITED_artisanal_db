The Magic Encyclopedia volume Two is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory.

Description

The Magic Encyclopedia volume Two is the second of a pair of 2nd edition books containing a vast collection of mundane items, their magical equivalents, and some unique magic items from G-Z sourced from other AD&D products. It was published in 1993.
