Raiders of the Twilight Marsh

Raiders of the Twilight Marsh is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Tyranny of Dragons series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Phlan, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 5- to 10-level characters.
