The Big Book of Numerological Nursery Rhymes was an immensely popular collection of traditional songs and poems for children.

Contents

The book collected such greatly favored tunes as "Frickle Fractal Froo" and "Little Hector Vector."

History

As of the , the The Big Book of Numerological Nursery Rhymes was considered well known and first-edition prints were exceedingly rare. The Collector of Neverwinter used one of the poems in the book as a code for his safe that held numerous rare and priceless treasures.
