A Manyrealms Gallimaufry was a 12 century compilation of maps that was considered the most well-known of its kind among the scholars of the Realms. It was compiled by the renowned cartographer, Trammeth Anstrelgor.

History

When Anstrelgor died around the , the original copy of A Manyrealms was interred with his body. The final resting place of both Anstrelgor and his masterpiece was located somewhere in Tethyr.

As of the late 14 century DR, an incomplete copy of the original could be found within the library-fortress of Candlekeep.
