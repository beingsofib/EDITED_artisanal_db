Ray Vallese

Ray Vallese is an American writer and editor, who has worked on many Advanced Dungeons & Dragons products.

Works

Vallese mainly contributed to the Planescape campaign setting. He was the editor for accessories such as On Hallowed Ground and adventures such as Fires of Dis and Something Wild.
