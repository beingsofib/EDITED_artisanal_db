Ron Spencer

Ron Spencer is an American illustrator who has done work for companies such as Wizards of the Coast, White Wolf, FASA, Pinnacle Entertainment, AEG, Iron Crown, Dragon Gate, Holistic Design, TSR Games, Privateer Press, Toy Vault, and others. He is most notably known for his work on the Magic: The Gathering card game.
