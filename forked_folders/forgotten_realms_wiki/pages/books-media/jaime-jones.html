Jaime Jones

Jaime Jones is an artist who has worked on 4 and 5 edition Dungeons and Dragons material, and has provided art for Wizards of the Coast.

Works

Jones is primarily known for his cover art for novels and sourcebooks, particularly those of the Forgotten Realms. Jaime Jones illustrated the cover art for books of the Chosen of Nendawen trilogy of novels, as well that of the Dungeons & Dragons Starter Set.
