The Song of Druid's Deep was a song worked on by the Harper bard Tarim Inavule in the late 15 century DR.

History

The song was created to commemorate the events that occurred around the Druid's Deep of Thorngap, in the King's Forest of Cormyr, which was taken over by the druid Halsaime after he was corrupted and maddened by a cursed staff. Although he caused a great deal of destruction and suffering as he plotted to conquer the world, Tarim, Jussko Kwahn, Kirinel Zay, and some heroic adventurers managed to stop him and destroy the staff before he could do more than threaten Eveningstar.
