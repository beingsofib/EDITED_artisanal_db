Dungeonology is an officially licensed tome based upon the original tabletop roleplaying game, Dungeons & Dragons. Narrated by Volo, the author of dozens of D&D guidebooks, the book features an introduction from the great wizard Elminster, written by his creator, Ed Greenwood himself.
