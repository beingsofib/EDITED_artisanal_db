Wolfgang Baur

Wolfgang Baur grew up near Chicago and later began working with Dungeon magazine in a low-level position but worked his way up to become editor of Dragon magazine though he eventually left to work for Wizards of the Coast. He edited a lot of books and designed the Dark•Matter setting.
