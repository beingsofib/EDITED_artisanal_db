The Gilded Unicorn was a type of stage performance called "grand play", popularized in Athkatla of Amn in the late 14 century DR.

History

By the late 14 century DR, The Gilded Unicorn was considered recently famous play. The performance was penned by Raulivan Torthtra, a young playwright from the city of Zazesspur in Tethyr who had a knack for capturing realistic dialogue in his work. This "grand play" was in the repertoire of the famous The Crown Aflame theater in Athkatla's Temple District.
