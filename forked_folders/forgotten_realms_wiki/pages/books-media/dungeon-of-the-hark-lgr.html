Dungeon of the Hark is an adventure module from the RPGA Legacy of the Green Regent campaign.

Plot

Stedd Rein, leader of the Red Fellowship has declared war on the bandit lord known as The Hark. He, his Red Fellows and the party march on The Hark's lair in the High Moor. On the way, they are waylaid by a pack of worgs and Stedd asks the party to clear out their den so his army's flank is protected.
