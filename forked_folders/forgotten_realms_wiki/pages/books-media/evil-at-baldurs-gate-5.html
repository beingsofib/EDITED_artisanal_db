Evil at Baldur's Gate 5

Evil at Baldur's Gate #5: Hamster Quest is a comic published by set in Baldur's Gate. It is the fifth and final issue of the Evil at Baldur's Gate series. It was written by Jim Zub and drawn by Ramon Bachs.
