Rise and Fall of the Elven Empire was a three volume series of books that detailed the history of elves on Toril. These volumes had the subtitles "Rise," "Years of Glory," and "Decline."

Description

Each of these tomes had bright red covers.

History

One copy of this tome laid within Bard Keep, a temple of Oghma that stood within the North near Silverymoon.
