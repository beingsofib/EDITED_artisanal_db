Legends of Baldur's Gate 5

Legends of Baldur's Gate #5 is a comic published by set in the Forgotten Realms. It is a part of the Tyranny of Dragons storyline. It is written by Jim Zub, drawn by Max Dunbar, and edited by John Barber. This is the last part of the Legends of Baldur's Gate series.

Synopsis

In the legendary city of Baldur's Gate, the young moon elf wild mage Delina; the legendary ranger Minsc; his hamster, Boo and thieves Krydle and Shandie are all that stand in the way of a red dragon's destruction. They are forced to ground the beast, who just happens to be the recently-transformed cultist Deniak — who just happens to be Delina's brother! The Heroes of Baldur's Gate earn their name in this grand adventure.
