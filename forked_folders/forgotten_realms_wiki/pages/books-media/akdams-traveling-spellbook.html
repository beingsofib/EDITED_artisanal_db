Akdam's Traveling Spellbook was a spellbook written by Akdam. It was a thin, traveling spellbook containing several simple spells. The title was printed on the cover in an old, rare script.

The Vipers bandits took it from a merchant and their leader, Cedric Ravajar, kept it at the Vipers' Nest in 1369 DR. The spellbook contained the following spells: alarm, dancing lights, detect magic, light, mount, deeppockets, and fog cloud.
