War Chant of Sith was a powerful war song empowered by a bard's magic.

Content

The chant's powerful magics became apparent when a talented bard began to sing it in battle. The bard's allies who heard the performance received an improved defense; any damage dealt to them was reduced as if by magic, and they began to regenerate, slowly healing any wounds on their bodies.

History

By the , War Chant of Sith was performed only by the most experienced bards of the North. It was heard being performed in the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale and the village of Kuldahar.
