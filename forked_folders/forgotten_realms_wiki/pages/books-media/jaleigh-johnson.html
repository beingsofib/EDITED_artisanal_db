Jaleigh Johnson

Jaleigh Johnson is a writer of Forgotten Realms content.

Works

Johnson has written several Forgotten Realms novels for both 3 and 4 edition Dungeons & Dragons.

While there are no current plans for Johnson to continue her story of Icelin Tearn and Ruen Morleth nor plans for a third novel in the Unbroken Chain series, in 2022 she authored a prequel novel to the film Honor Among Thieves entitled The Road to Neverwinter.

In 2024, she authored a novel called The Fallbacks: Bound for Ruin.
