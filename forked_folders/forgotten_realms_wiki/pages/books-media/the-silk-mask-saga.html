The Silk Mask Saga was a series of fictional chapbooks in the mid–14 century DR.

Plot

The Silk Mask Saga was a melodrama following Lady Elradra, a slave from birth and secretly a princess of Cormyr, looking for allies and money in Sembia to reclaim her throne.
