The Shadowstruck Passages was a collection of Sharran doctrines compiled within a single tome. It was one of the most notorious and illicit books of the faith and was banned in many of the realms across Faerûn.

Possession of the Shadowstruck Passages in Sembia was illegal and punishable by imprisonment for a tenday within the merchant nation. A single copy was worth around 100 gp on the black market.
