Clyde Caldwell

Clyde Caldwell is an American artist and self-described fantasy illustrator. Originally a commercial illustrator for ad agencies, he gained fame working for TSR, Inc. from 1982 to 1992, illustrating many products at a time the game was at the peak of its popularity. Since 1992 he has worked as a freelance artist.
