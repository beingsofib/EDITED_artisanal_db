Races of the Dragon is an accessory for the 3.5-edition of ruleset. It explores dragon-descended races, focusing on kobolds and half-dragons and introducing dragonborn of Bahamut and spellscales, as well as dragon-related prestige classes, options, magic, and equipment.

Description

Races of the Dragon focuses on dragonborn, spellscales, kobolds, and half-dragons.
