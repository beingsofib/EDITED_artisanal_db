Heroes' Feast: Saving the Children's Menu is an adventure module using the 5-edition ruleset released by Wizards of the Coast on the D&D Beyond website on November 22, 2023. It is intended as a companion to Heroes' Feast Flavors of the Multiverse: An Official D&D Cookbook and is set in the Forgotten Realms in Daggerford and the Delimbiyr Vale. It also includes a recipe for macaroni and cheese.
