Jonathan Tweet

Jonathan Tweet is a game designer who contributed to the 3 edition of Dungeons & Dragons and the early design of 4th edition. He also acted as the head of Wizards of the Coast's miniatures division, heading the development of the Dungeons & Dragons Miniatures skirmish game.

History

At the end of 2008, Jonathan was one of many employees let go by Wizards of the Coast in a wave of layoffs. His layoff would be commented upon by Erik Mona on his blog as having been unthinkable in the prior two to four years, his star design work having made him considered close to untouchable.
