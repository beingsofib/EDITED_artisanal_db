A Walk in the Park

A Walk in the Park is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Tomb of Annihilation series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Port Nyanzaru, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1- to 4-level characters. It is the second part of the The Jungle Has Fangs trilogy.
