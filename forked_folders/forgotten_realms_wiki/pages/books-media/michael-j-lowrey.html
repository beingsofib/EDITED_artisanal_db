Michael J. Lowrey

Michael J. Lowrey (mistakenly bylined as "Mike Lowery" in one issue) is a gamer from Milwaukee, Wisconsin who wrote gaming articles, as well as book, television, and game reviews for Dragon magazine and at least one issue of Ares magazine. He is a volunteer admin for Wikipedia and monitors many RPG and specifically D&D articles in Wikipedia.

Works

Lowrey has articles in:

  • Dragon #77 (1983): "Tarot of Many Things",
  • Dragon #83 (1984): "The many facets of gems: Stories behind the stones listed in the DMG", "How to finish fights faster: A suggested simpler system for unarmed combat"
