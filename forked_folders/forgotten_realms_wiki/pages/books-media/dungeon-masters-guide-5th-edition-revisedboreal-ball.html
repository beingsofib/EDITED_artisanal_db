Boreal Ball is a short sample adventure included in the Dungeon Master's Guide 5th edition (revised). Of the five sample adventures included in the book, Boreal Ball is the only one with a connection to the Forgotten Realms setting (while the remaining four take place in the Greyhawk setting). The adventure is designed for 7-level player-characters.
