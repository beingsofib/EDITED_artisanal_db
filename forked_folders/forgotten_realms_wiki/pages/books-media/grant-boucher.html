Grant Boucher

Grant Boucher is a designer and author that worked on 2 edition Dungeons & Dragons material. He undertook a degree in English and Theoretical Physics before contributing to the design of several sourcebooks.

Works

He designed some of the books in the DMGR series. Particularly, he designed the The Castle Guide, Dark and Hidden Ways, Wild Things, and others, as well as the armor for the Arms and Equipment Guide.
