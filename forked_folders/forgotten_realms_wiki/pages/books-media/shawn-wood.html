Shawn Wood

Shawn Wood is an illustrator, interior, and concept artist for 5-edition Dungeons and Dragons. His official current position is an in-house concept artist for Wizards of the Coast. He has ran, as well as participated in, D&D sessions for D&D Extra Life in 2017 and 2018. As well as this, he has written and produced art for his own adventure.
