Paths of Darkness

Paths of Darkness is the fourth series of novels about Drizzt Do'Urden written by R. A. Salvatore. They follow the Legacy of the Drow series and precede The Hunter's Blades trilogy.

Paths of Darkness

  1. The Silent Blade (1998)
  2. The Spine of the World (1999)
  3. Servant of the Shard (2000) *
  4. Sea of Swords (2001)

* Servant of the Shard was moved to The Sellswords trilogy, which follows the adventures of Jarlaxle and Artemis Entreri.
