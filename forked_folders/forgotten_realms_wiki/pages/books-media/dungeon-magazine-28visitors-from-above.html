"Visitors from Above" is an adventure in the Sword Coast North for Dungeons & Dragons 2 edition, published in Dungeon #28, designed to introduce players and dungeon masters to several elements of the Spelljammer setting. It was designed for a group of 4 to 9 adventurers of 4–8 level.
