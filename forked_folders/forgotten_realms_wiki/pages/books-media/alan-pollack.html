Alan Pollack

Alan Pollack is an artist who has worked for both TSR and Wizards of the Coast.

Works

Pollack has contributed art for many Dungeons & Dragons products, particularly for those in the Forgotten Realms and Planescape campaign settings. Including interior illustrations, Alan has drawn the covers for many issues of both Dragon and Polyhedron magazines, multiple sourcebooks, and novels of many series.
