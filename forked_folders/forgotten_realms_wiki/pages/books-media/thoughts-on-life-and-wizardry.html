Thoughts On Life and Wizardry was a book written by Laeral Silverhand, writing as "Laeral of the Nine". It was written in the , while she was a member of the adventuring company known as the Nine. It was a collection of her observations on the world and on magic.

Availability

Around 1372 DR, a copy was in the holdings at Candlekeep.
