Ben Wootten

Ben Wootten is an illustrator, as well as a cover and interior artist, for several types of Dungeons & Dragons content. He has worked on art for sourcebooks, magazines and other media from 3 to 5 edition. Wootten has had an interest in role-playing games since he was very young.
