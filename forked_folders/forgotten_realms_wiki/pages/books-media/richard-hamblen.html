Richard Hamblen

Richard Hamblen is a board game designer whose contribution to the Forgotten Realms is co-designing the board game The Great Khan Game.

History

Hamblen was born on February 2, 1945, in , . In 1975, he became a part of editorial staff. His first two board games were both released in 1977: Victory in the Pacific and Victory at Sea. He won the 1977 Charles S. Roberts Best Strategic Game award for Victory in the Pacific.{{cite web

His sole known contribution to the Forgotten Realms or Dungeons & Dragons is co-designing The Great Khan Game with Tom Wham.

Works

Board Games

  • Victory in the Pacific (1977)
  • Victory at Sea (1977)
  • The Arab-Israeli Wars: Tank Battles in the Mideast (1977)
  • Fortress Europa (1978)
  • Magic Realms (1979)
  • Gunslinger (1982)
  • Bull Run: The First Major Battle of the American Civil War (1983)
  • Merchant of Venus (1988)
  • The Great Khan Game (1989)
  • Merchant of Venus – Second Edition (2012)

Board Game Expansions

  • Merchant of Venus: The Variant Mini Expansion (1990)
