Stone of Tymora trilogy

The Stone of Tymora trilogy is a trilogy of novels by R. A. Salvatore and his son Geno Salvatore intended for younger readers and published by Mirrorstone. The series follows a character named Maimun instead of Drizzt Do'Urden (who appears throughout the series).

Novels

  1. The Stowaway
  2. The Shadowmask
  3. The Sentinels

Omnibus

The trilogy is also available as the Stone of Tymora omnibus, released by Wizards of the Coast in 2012.
