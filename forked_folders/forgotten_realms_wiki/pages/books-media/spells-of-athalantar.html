Spells of Athalantar was a spellbook featuring in a vision sent by Mystra. It of course pertained to the spells of Athalantar, the fallen Realm of the Stag.

History

In the , Elminster Aumar, a Chosen of Mystra and survivor of Athalantar, was shown a number of visions and temptations by Mystra. One of them was a room full of spellbooks, including one labeled Spells of Athalantar. It was unclear whether specific books he saw actually existed outside of the vision.
