A Glimpse into the Abyss was a book written by Tahyr yn Savras, known as "the Mad Seer of Manshaka", and is presumed to be about the Abyss. It was written in two volumes in 1322 DR.

A copy was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep around 1372 DR.
