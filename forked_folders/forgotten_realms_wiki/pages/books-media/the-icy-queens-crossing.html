The Icy Queen's Crossing

The Icy Queen's Crossing is an adventure in the Living Forgotten Realms organized play campaign for Dungeons & Dragons 4 edition. As a Weekend in the Realms module, it was only released in a very limited quantity and exclusively for public events. The Icy Queen's Crossing ties in with the novel The Fall of Highwatch.
