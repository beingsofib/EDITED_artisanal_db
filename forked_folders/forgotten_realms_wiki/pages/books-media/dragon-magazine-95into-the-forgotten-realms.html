"Into the Forgotten Realms" is a tournament adventure for Dungeons & Dragons 1 edition, designed by Ed Greenwood and published in Dragon magazine 95.

Synopsis

A band of adventurers from across the Dalelands were sent to the ruins of the School of Wizardry in Myth Drannor to gather powerful magics lost in the destroyed City of Song, before the evil Lord Lashan of Scardale could claim them first. However, the ruins were not as abandoned as most thought. The School of Wizardry was still under control of its old Master, the now utterly insane lich Azimer.
