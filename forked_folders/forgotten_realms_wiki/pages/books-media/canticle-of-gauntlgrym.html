The Canticle of Gauntlgrym was a song passed down among the dwarves of the North in the generations prior to the reclamation of Gauntlgrym in the late 15 century DR, after which it served as an anthem of that city.

History

The Canticle was believed to have been composed in Gauntlgrym shortly after its original founding. For centuries after the city's fall, it was said that every dwarf in the North knew the song, and it held special significance for those who traced their lineage to Delzoun.

As of the , this ancient song could often be heard among dwarves traveling to Gauntlgrym to make a new life there.
