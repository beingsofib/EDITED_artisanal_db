Dungeons & Dragons: Builders of Baldur's Gate is a board game set in the Forgotten Realms setting and the city of Baldur's Gate. The game is centered around faction competition between the Council of Four, the Flaming Fist, and The Guild for building and controlling the city. The game is designed for two to five players.
