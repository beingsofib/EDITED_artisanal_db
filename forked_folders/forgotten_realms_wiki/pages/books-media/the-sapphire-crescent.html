The Sapphire Crescent, by Thomas M. Reid, is the first book in the Scions of Arrabar trilogy.

Summary

In Arrabar in 1362 DR, Xaphira Matrell, a noble, hides from the guards with a crossbow bolt in her leg. Earlier that night, she was attending the Night of Ghosts festival at the Generon when her nephew, Vambran Martell, and his friends, Adyan Mercatio and Horial Rohden, tell her they believe Vambran accidentally killed someone while playing with a crossbow. Dregaul Matrell, Xaphira’s older brother, comes on the group and learns what happened. They investigate, finding the dead body of Rodolpho Wianar, cousin to Eles Wianar, lord of Arrabar. Xaphira poses as a masked assassin to draw blame away from Vambran, then flees, stowing away on a ship and leaving the city, although she is wounded during her escape.

Eleven years later, Emriana Matrell, Vambran’s sister, sneaks out of the Matrell estate to meet Vambran, now an officer of the Sapphire Crescent, a mercenary company in the service of the temple of Waukeen. She encounters her other brother Evester Matrell on the way, but he does not stop her. Emriana meets Vambran, but on their way back to the estate, the siblings hear a scream. They investigate and find two bodies and some watchmen who claim that the dead were slain for falsely drawing dots on their foreheads to signify magic use, although Vambran quickly grows suspicious that the men are not really members of the watch. Real watch officers arrive, but with Emriana in the mix, Vambran does not want to risk a fight and just leaves. Emriana tells Vambran she remembers seeing one of the dead bodies, a woman, somewhere before, but she can’t remember where.

The scene is scried by Grozier Talricci with the help of his wizard Bartimus. Grozier tells Bartimus to take care of the situation. When Vambran goes to speak with the watch about the bodies, he finds that Waukeenar priests claimed they carried magical plague and burned them. After Dragaul scolds Emriana for sneaking out, Hetta Matrell, the head of the family, tells her about Xaphira, and reveals that Xaphira had to leave because of a fatal accident, although she does not reveal the details.

Grozier and Bartimus meet with an assassin named Junce Roundface and a priest of Waukeen. The priest read Vambran’s mind to learn that he was still determined to investigate, and the conspirators discuss ways to dissuade him. The priest arranges a distraction, Junce sets up an assassination, and Bartimus is told to kill the false watchmen who committed the murders.

Vambran meets with Kovrim Lazelle, a priest of Waukeen and his uncle on his mother’s side, and asks him to look into the priests who burned the bodies. Emriana is brought by her mother, Ladara Matrell, to the estate of the Pharaboldi family, where she is expected to court Denrick Pharaboldi, although she actually has little interest in him. However, while there, she remembers that the dead woman was a servant who worked in the Pharaboldi kitchens.

Vambran is ambushed, but is saved by Kovrim and a mysterious figure in red. Emriana asks the other Pharaboldi servants about the dead figures and learns their names were Hoytir and Jithelle, and that Jithelle had been sleeping with Denrick. Emriana and Vambran discuss their findings, and Vambran tells the story of Rodolpho's death. He gives her his present for her upcoming sixteenth birthday, a necklace that allows her to cast a Sending spell. Vambran sets out in search of the false watch members, recruiting Horial and Adyan as backup. Kovrim meets with Grand Trabbar Lavant of the temple of Waukeen, who confirms that he had the bodies burned. Lavant claims they were an unfortunate byproduct of an important deal, but that the guilty have already been punished, and warns Kovrim not to investigate further.

Vambran, Horial, and Adyan use magic to locate a dagger that the false watch members took from Emriana, tracking down their hideout. They find all the men dead and are attacked by a Leechwalker that nearly kills them all before the figure in red reappears, killing the leech monster with a bag of salt. Emriana, having followed Vambran, uses her necklace to contact Kovrim, who shows up with some trustworthy mercenaries, including the dwarf Grolo Firefist.

Vambran meets with Jithelle’s family and learns she was pregnant with Denrick’s child. Grozier meets with the heads of two other families, involved in a conspiracy with him, and plans to send Vambran a warning. Meanwhile, Vambran and Embriana plan to confront Denrick at her sixteenth birthday party. However, Grand Trabbar Lavant arranges for the Silver Crescent to leave the city on short notice to stop his interference. Kovrim sneaks into Lavant’s chambers and discovers that three families are plotting to gather a mercenary army to cause a war, including house Pharaboldi and House Matrell.

Vambran’s allies in the guard, including Grolo, Adyan, and Horial, arrange to sneak him away from the barracks and back to his family. Kovrim tries to warn the family but is nearly cornered by Waukeenar priests. However, the figure in red saves him. At Emriana’s party, Vambran and Emriana confront Denrick, who weeps and denies knowledge of Jithelle’s pregnancy or death. Before Vambran can use magic to determine the truth, an assassin wounds Hetta and leaves a threatening note.

Vambran sets off in pursuit of the assassin, while back at the party, Dragaul insists that Emriana returns to the party, where he announces her engagement to Denrick. While hunting the assassin, Vambran encounters the figure in red, who reveals herself as Xaphira and warns that members of house Martell are conspiring with houses Talricci and Pharaboldi to start a war. The two set out to find Kovrim to help stop the plot, only to find him surrounded by a group of men.

Denrick comes to Emriana in her room. When she tries to make him leave, he assaults her and her maid Jaleene and restrains her. The house guard witnesses this, but helps Denrick instead of Emriana. Elsewhere, Dragaul is finalizing the plot with Grozier and Anista Pharaboldi, Denrick’s mother. Hetta tries to stop the deal, only for the house guards to reveal they have been turned by Dragaul and take the other members of the family hostage.

As Vambran, Xaphira, and Kovrim confront their enemies, the leader reveals himself to be the one who shot Hetta, and also claims to have witnessed Vambran kill Rodolpho. Battle breaks out, and Adyan and Horial arrive with reinforcements to save the day, although the leader assassin teleports away.

Emriana frees herself briefly and warns Vambran using her necklace before being recaptured. Vambran’s group plot to retake the house and rescue their family. Vambran saves Emriana while the Sapphire Crescent platoon capture the treacherous guards, as well as Grozier Talricci and Barnabus. Vambran confronts Denrick and discovers he has killed Dregaul and Anista. Emriana tries to help, but is captured by Evester, who was in on the plot and the one who suggested sealing the deal by arranging a marriage between Emriana and Denrick. Denrick is defeated, but Evester takes Emriana hostage, demanding that they be set free. However, Xaphira strikes, freeing Emriana. When Denrick tries to kill Emriana, she kicks him out of a window. He catches onto Evester in an attempt to save himself, but both men fall to their deaths.

The remaining members of House Matrell gather to discuss the situation. Hetta admits that she allowed Dregaul control to expose that he was unfit to lead and measure who would step up and prove themselves, although she had not realized how far his plots went. She says that Vambran and Emriana have proven themselves worthy to lead the family when the time comes. She also reveals that Xaphira has been secretly working for her ever since Xaphira’s disappearance, and that the family holdings are far more vast than they all know because of it. Xaphira reveals that the crossbow bolt that killed Rodolpho Weinar matched a bolt that she was shot with that same night, and that it is likely someone stole both bolts from Vambran and shot Rodolpho to frame Vambran. She identifies that “someone” as Junce Roundface, and the family realizes they have become embroiled in a conspiracy spanning more than a decade. Ladara warns Emriana against sneaking out further, but Emriana and Xaphira share a knowing look. Finally, Kovrim arrives and explains that they were unable to prove Grand Trabbar Lavant’s involvement in the scheme, and that Grozier and Barnabus were broken out of custody, likely by Junce. Vambran and Emriana realize that this is far from over.
