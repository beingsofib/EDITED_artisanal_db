Jason Thompson

Jason Thompson is an illustrator and writer who has worked for Wizards of the Coast and contributed to the Forgotten Realms. He is notable for his complex, cartoony maps in a wimmelbilderbuch style.

Works

Jason wrote and illustrated the adventure The Barber of Silverymoon in Dragon+ 12 to showcase monsters from Volo's Guide to Monsters. He also wrote and illustrated the adventure Six Faces of Death in Dragon+ 21 to showcase monsters from Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes. He illustrated a walkthrough of Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden in Dragon+ 35. An illustration of the Yawning Portal by Jason appeared in Waterdeep: Dragon Heist.
