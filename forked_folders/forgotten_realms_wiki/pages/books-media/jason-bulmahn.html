Jason Bulmahn

Jason Bulmahn is a fantasy author who contributed to some 3 edition sourcebooks, some articles to the Dragon and Dungeon magazines, and coordinated the RPGA's campaign. He went on to join Paizo Publishing, becoming the managing editor for Dragon in 2004, and later in 2007 becoming a lead designer for Paizo and the 1 edition of the ''''.

History

Jason Bulmahn was born in Indiana on June 25, 1976. He began playing Dungeons & Dragons in 1985, playing with the 1983 revision. By the 1990 he began volunteering for the Role Playing Game Association and writing organized play adventures. He played in a number of the RPGA's "classic" events and Living City modules. He would eventually use this experience to become the managing coordinator of the Living Greyhawk campaign for the Wisconsin area and two years later a member of the Circle (campaign administrators).
