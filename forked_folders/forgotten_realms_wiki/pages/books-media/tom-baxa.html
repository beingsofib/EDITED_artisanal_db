Tom Baxa

Tom Baxa is an artist who has illustrated various role-playing games.

Education

Baxa was educated at , where he was taught by Mark Nelson, another artist who has illustrated Dungeons & Dragons material.

Works

In addition to many other projects, Baxa is known for his illustrations of many Dungeons & Dragons products, including many images for the Al-Qadim setting.
