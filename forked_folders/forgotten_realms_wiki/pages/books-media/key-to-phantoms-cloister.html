Key to Phantoms' Cloister is an adventure module by Ving Domanski set in the Forgotten Realms campaign setting using the 3.5-edition Dungeons & Dragons rules and designed for the RPGA's Legacy of the Green Regent campaign. It was released in 2003 and has interior art by David Esbrí and maps by Dorwin Cooper. Intended for four or five 1-level characters, it has the player-characters investigate a ghost sighting.

Synopsis

The annual haunting of a ghost in the streets of Loudwater is interrupted by a groundling attack. Their burrowing reveals a previously undiscovered reliquary containing a tangible shard of pure light. Following clues, the party makes their way to the ruin of Phantoms' Cloister, where they find Zhentarim battling creatures under the control of a vicious quasit.
