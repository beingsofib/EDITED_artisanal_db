The Nymph Said No was a book in the series of smutty romance chapbooks by Goldhallow of Cormyr, published before 1348 DR.

History

By 1348 DR, Goldhallow's chapbooks were popular in Suzail, and more rural areas of Cormyr, especially among women. The series of books that included The Nymph Said No was read by farmers' wives and Purple Dragons. The book featured some titillating naughty bits. The Nymph Said No was also released in a rare Blackcovers edition.
