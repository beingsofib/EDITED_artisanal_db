Volo's Guide to All Things Magical is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms.

Description

Volo's Guide to All Things Magical is one in a series of guides written by famed explorer and raconteur Volothamp Geddarm (under the editorial control of Elminster of Shadowdale).

Contents

  • Elminster's Introduction
  • The Magic of Guardianship
  • The Magic of Items
  • The Magic of Movement
  • The Magic of the Person
  • Artifacts of the Realms
  • Appendix I: Gemstone Tables
  • Appendix II: Spell and Magical Item Index
