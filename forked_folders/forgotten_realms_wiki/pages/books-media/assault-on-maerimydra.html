Assault on Maerimydra

Assault on Maerimydra is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Rage of Demons series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Maerimydra and Sporedome, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 11- to 16-level characters.

Summary

The adventure begins with adventurers gathering in Sporedome with the various factions that have rallied against the fire giants of Maerimydra.

The arrayed factions work together to concoct a plan to assault Maerimydra, splitting their forces across various objectives.
