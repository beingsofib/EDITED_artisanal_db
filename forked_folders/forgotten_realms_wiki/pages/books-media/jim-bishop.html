Jim Bishop

Jim Bishop is a game designer.

Works for TSR

Jim Bishop was one of the authors of the "Role Models" column for Dragon in 1999, between issues 256 and 266.

In 1999, he was the writer for the Crown of Salt adventure for the Adventurer's Guild program.

Works under WotC

He contributed an article to Dragon Magazine 298 entitled "Crusaders of the Ashen Compact VS. Drow", and another to Dragon magazine 317 entitled "Power Components". He also contributed "Head to head: Mini-Adventures" to Dragon Annual 6.

Works for videogame companies

Jim Bishop was working for BioWare as of 2001. He is credited for writing the manual for Neverwinter Nights, and was mentioned under "Wizards of the Coast and Approvals" in the manual for Pool of Radiance: Ruins of Myth Drannor.

Other Work

Jim Bishop's "Freeport: City of Adventure" supplement for Pathfinder won the 2002 ENnie Awards.
