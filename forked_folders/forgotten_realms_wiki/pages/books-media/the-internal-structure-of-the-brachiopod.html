The Internal Structure of the Brachiopod was a natural science book by Professor Linnaeus Gozzling of the University of Suzail in Suzail, Cormyr. It was about the . It had one volume.

A copy was kept in the small Ghars library managed by Phelos Marmwitz. The retired War Wizard and sage Benelaius borrowed it in the summer of 1367 DR and had his servant Jasper return it a few weeks later.

A copy was kept in Candlekeep around 1372 DR.
