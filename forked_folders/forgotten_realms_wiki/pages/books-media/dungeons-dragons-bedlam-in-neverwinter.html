Dungeons & Dragons: Bedlam in Neverwinter is a board escape room game set in the Forgotten Realms setting. Play occurs in the famed region of Icewind Dale, where character cooperatively solve mysteries, fight monsters, and attempt to escapade dungeons.

Contents

Includes:

  • 6 plastic figures
  • 11 gameboards
  • 4 secret envelopes
  • 1 mysterious object
  • 1 d20 die
  • 1 d6 die
  • 298 cards
  • 44 cardboard tokens
  • 1 rulebook
