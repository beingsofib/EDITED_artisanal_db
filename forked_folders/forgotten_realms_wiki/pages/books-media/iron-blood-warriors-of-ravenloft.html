Iron & Blood: Warriors of Ravenloft is a 3D fighting game set in the Ravenloft setting of Advanced Dungeons & Dragons. One-on-one fights took place in a 3D arena between heroes brought to the Domains of Dread from Toril and dark and monstrous inhabitants of the domains and servants of Count Strahd von Zarovich and the dreaded Lord Soth of Krynn.
