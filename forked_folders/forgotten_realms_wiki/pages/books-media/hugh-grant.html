Hugh Grant

Hugh Grant is an actor starring in the Honor Among Thieves movie, released in 2023. He plays Forge Fitzwilliam, a rogue, a former member of the thieves, a conman, and the new Lord of Neverwinter, in a villainous role.

Career

A star of many period dramas and romantic comedies, Hugh Grant has also appeared in fantasy and science-fiction genre movies, including , ', , and '.
