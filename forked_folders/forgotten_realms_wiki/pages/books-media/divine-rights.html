Divine Rights

Divine Rights is the seventeenth issue of the Forgotten Realms comics series published by . It was written by Jeff Grubb, drawn by Jerry Acerno and Mickey Ritter, and edited by Elliot S. Maggin and Kim Yale. It is the third installment in the "Fallen Idols" story arc.
