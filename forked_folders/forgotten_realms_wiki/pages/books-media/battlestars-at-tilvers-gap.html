Battlestars at Tilver's Gap was a ballad about a historic battle against an orcish horde that took place in western Cormanthyr and the Dalelands in the 7 century DR.

Contents

The ballad titled Battlestars at Tilver's Gap retold the events of the , when a horde of work from the Thunder Peaks swarmed onto the lands of western Cormanthyr and the Dalelands, threatening Semberholme. The titular Battlestars were an army of the Dalesmen and elves of Myth Drannor who stood against the encroaching horde and obliterated it in a fearsome battle.
