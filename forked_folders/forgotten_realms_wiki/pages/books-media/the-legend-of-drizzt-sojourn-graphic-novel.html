The Legend of Drizzt: Sojourn (original title: Forgotten Realms: The Legend of Drizzt, Volume 3: Sojourn; reissued title: Dungeons & Dragons: Forgotten Realms: The Legend of Drizzt, Volume 3: Sojourn) is the graphic novel version of R.A. Salvatore's novel Sojourn. It was originally published in three parts: The Legend of Drizzt: Sojourn #1–3.
