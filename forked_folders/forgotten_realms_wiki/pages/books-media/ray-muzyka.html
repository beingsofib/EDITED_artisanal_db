Ray Muzyka

Ray Muzyka is one of the founders of BioWare. He left the company in 2012 after almost 20 years of working in the video game industry.

Education

Muzyka was trained as a medical doctor at the and practiced as a physician before founding Bioware with Greg Zeschuk in 1995.

Projects

Forgotten Realms games on which he contributed include:

Lead Producer

  • Baldur's Gate (game) (1998)
  • Baldur's Gate: Tales of the Sword Coast (1999)

Executive Producer

  • Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn (2000)
  • Baldur's Gate II: Throne of Bhaal (2001)
  • Neverwinter Nights (2002)
  • Neverwinter Nights: Hordes of the Underdark (2003)
  • Neverwinter Nights: Shadows of Undrentide (2003)
  • Baldur's Gate II: Enhanced Edition (2013)

Producer

  • Baldur's Gate: Enhanced Edition (2012)

Joint CEO

  • Neverwinter Nights: Shadows of Undrentide (2003)

Credited Special Thanks

  • Icewind Dale II (2002)
  • Neverwinter Nights 2 (2006)
