When Love Fumbles was a book from the city of Baldur's Gate, printed sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.

History

By the , When Love Fumbles could be found in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. A copy belonged to Lady Firelia Jannath and could be found among the many books in her Lower City estate.
