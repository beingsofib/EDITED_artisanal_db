Princes of the Apocalypse is a 2015 Forgotten Realms adventure module for the 5th edition of Dungeons & Dragons, designed by Sasquatch Game Studio. The module was part of the Elemental Evil storyline, and revolved around stopping four destructive cults devoted to the Elder Elemental Eye.
