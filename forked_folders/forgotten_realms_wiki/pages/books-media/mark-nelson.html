Mark Nelson

Mark Nelson is a professor and artist who has illustrated role-playing games and comic books.

Career

Nelson was employed as a professor at , where he also taught Tom Baxa, another artist known for his Dungeons & Dragons illustrations.

Nelson later worked for as a concept artist, before starting his own art studio with his wife Anita.

Works

In addition to many other projects, Nelson is known for his illustrations of many Dungeons & Dragons products, including Dragon magazine.
