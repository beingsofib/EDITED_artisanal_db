Todd Gamble

Todd Alan Gamble is an artist, illustrator, and cartographer, focusing on fantasy illustration. He has worked for Wizards of the Coast and several other gaming companies. He lives in . He works on three-dimensional scale models and dedicates much of his time to volunteer and educational projects.

Education

Gamble has degrees in advertising design and fine arts with an emphasis on drawing and structure.
