Dungeons & Dragons Online/Web of Chaos

Web of Chaos is a free quest chain for Dungeons & Dragons Online where the player characters face strange drow cultists in Stormreach, only to discover Lolth and her plots for both Eberron and Faerûn. The adventure pack was added to the game on February 27, 2012 as part of Update 16, and served as an introduction to Menace of the Underdark and further Forgotten Realms content. It was also accompanied by the novel Skein of Shadows, which involves the cult of the Spinner of Shadows and Tarath Marad.

Plot

The Lords of Dust has the players be recruited by Brother Dectaran to help raid the cultist base in Stormreach, having identified it as a Lords of Dust hideout. They're joined by Inquisitor Gnomon and Novice Corin Dryden, and proceed to cut a swathe through the cultists inside. In a shocking twist, Gnomon turns out to be a rakshasa in disguise, having infiltrated the Church of the Silver Flame and sabotaged their efforts until then while working on a fiendish conspiracy. Though Gnomon and Karas are defeated, their ritual succeeds in opening the Road to Tarath Marad into Khyber, allowing their plan to proceed.

Servants of the Overlord has Felnis send the players into the blasted-open pit to fight through the cultists as they try to break into the Spinner's Prison. Along the way the players fight many drow cultists, as well as fiendish spiders, jariliths, hezrou, fire reavers, and hell hounds. They eventually find Brother Aurelius, the Silver Flame faithful assigned to protecting the prison.

In The Spinner of Shadows Orrex sends the players into the Spinner's Prison where they encounter the Spinner of Shadows herself. With the Silver Flame Guardian weakened by the flame eater spiders, the Spinner is freed from her bonds and immediately turns on the Lords of Dust that saved her. Before she can escape, the players and the Silver Flame Guardian manage to relight the wards and trap her once more, only for Lolth to personally intervene and open the Rift Between Worlds. The Spinner escapes with stolen dragonshards, leaving Eberron behind.

Beyond the Rift has the players return to the Spinner's Prison, where the Rift Between Worlds continues to grow and will eventually pull Eberron into the Demonweb Pits. Tavnar Kronnak of the Gatekeepers sends the players into the Demonweb, hoping to find a solution to the growing rift. Once in the Abyss the players are fortunate enough to meet Elminster, who was also investigating the strange goings-on with Lolth. He saves them from a bebilith and guides them for a time before departing, and the players fight their way to a portal exit that leads to Faerûn's Underdark. Encountering the drow of Sschindylryn amid their invasion of Eveningstar, the players eventually reach the surface in Cormyr and rescue a number of Evenor villagers from the Eveningstar Cavern, including Ana Brabener.
