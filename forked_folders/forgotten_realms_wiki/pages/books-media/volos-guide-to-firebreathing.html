Volo's Guide to Firebreathing was a book written by the famous Volo, with a self-explanatory title.

Contents

The book was filled with helpful instructions compiled by Volo, all to teach readers how to safely perform amazing firebreathing tricks. It was not based on his personal experience, but rather hearsay and rumors that he had heard.
