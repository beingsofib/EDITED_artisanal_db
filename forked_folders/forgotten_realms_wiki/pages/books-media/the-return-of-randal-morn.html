The Return of Randal Morn is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons adventure module for the Forgotten Realms.

Description

The Return of Randal Morn adventure module is part of a trilogy that focuses on the character Randal Morn. It is designed for four to six characters of levels 1-4. The module is designed to provide a mix of intrigue, combat, and exploration.

The Return of Randal Morn revolves around the efforts to restore Randal Morn to his rightful position as the ruler of Daggerdale. The region has fallen under the tyranny of the Zhentarim, and Randal Morn, the rightful heir, has been leading a guerrilla war against the usurpers. The players' characters are drawn into this conflict and tasked with a series of challenges that will aid Randal Morn in reclaiming his rule.

It begins with the characters embarking on a quest to find the legendary "Silver Standard," a symbol of legitimate rule lost in the Dalelands. This quest leads them through a series of dangerous locales, each presenting unique challenges and revealing parts of the overarching plot.
