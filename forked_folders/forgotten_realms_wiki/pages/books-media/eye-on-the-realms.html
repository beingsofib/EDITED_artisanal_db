Eye on the Realms was a column by Ed Greenwood published in both Dungeon magazine and Dragon magazine. The series debuted in November 2009 (Dungeon #172) and ended in December 2013 (Dragon #430).

The focus on the column was on various groups, locations, items and individuals of the Realms.
