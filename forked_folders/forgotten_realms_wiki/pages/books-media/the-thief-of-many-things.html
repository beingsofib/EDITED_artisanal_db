The Thief of Many Things

The Thief of Many Things is a comic published by in April, 2024. It is written by Ellen Boener and Jim Zub, with art by Eduardo Mello. It is a one-shot story set in Baldur's Gate and featuring the deck of many things as a tie-in to the 5-edition sourcebook The Book of Many Things.
