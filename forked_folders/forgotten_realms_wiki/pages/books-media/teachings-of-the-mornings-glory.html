The Teachings of the Morning's Glory was a holy text of four volumes, penned by Thorndar Erlin, the High Priest of Lathander in 20 DR.

Contents

This Lathanderite work was created in the early 1 century DR. The book's author, High Priest Erlin, talked about the importance of family for the Morninglord's faithful, improving the family's well-being from one generation to the next. In the same manner, the Teachings stated that the leader of a realm is responsible for improving the lives of its inhabitants with each passing generation.

By the late-14 century DR,a copy of the Teachings of the Morning's Glory was preserved in the library stronghold of Candlekeep.
