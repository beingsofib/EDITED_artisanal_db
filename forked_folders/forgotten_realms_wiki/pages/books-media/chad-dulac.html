Chad Dulac

Chad A. Dulac is a former serviceman in the U.S. Navy who has been working as a concept artist on the side since the late 90s. His work has been published in both Dungeon and Dragon magazines 14 times. He is also the COO and Art Director of SilverHelm Sudios LLC., a game design company. He retired from active service in 2015 to concentrate on his artwork.
