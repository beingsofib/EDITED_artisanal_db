Blas Gallego

Blas Gallego is an artist that has worked on Forgotten Realms cover material, particularly novels. Blas illustrated the cover of the Finder's Bane novel, as well as some anthologies. His art for Realms of Magic was reprinted for the comic, The Grand Tour.

Works

Blas has provided cover art for:

  • Realms of Magic
  • Realms of Infamy
  • Realms of the Arcane
  • Finder's Bane
  • The Grand Tour
