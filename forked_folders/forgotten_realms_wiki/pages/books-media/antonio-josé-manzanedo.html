Antonio José Manzanedo

Antonio José Manzanedo is a digital artist, concept designer and illustrator from . He made illustrations for the Forgotten Realms, the Dragonlance, the Star Wars and the Warhammer 40,000 settings, and the cover art for a Spanish tabletop campaign, Jernhest.
