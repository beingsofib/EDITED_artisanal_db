The Forgotten Vault is an encounter published in Dungeons & Dragons: Adventurer #3. The encounter was the third in the series of encounters comprising Adventure #1.

Summary

While transporting their mistress, the vampire spawn Etterine Tzavari, her servants were caught in a mudslide not far Phandalin. A sole surviving servant, Malet Crint hired a group of adventurers—who would later gain fame as the Champions of Neverwinter—to uncover the vault's secrets. to escort him and his mysterious "cargo" safely to Waterdeep. Their journey was interrupted by an attack from Zhentarim bandits, forcing the Champions to defend their charge and his dangerous secret.
