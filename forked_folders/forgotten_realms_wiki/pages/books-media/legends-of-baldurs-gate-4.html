Legends of Baldur's Gate 4

Legends of Baldur's Gate #4 is a comic published by set in the Forgotten Realms. It is a part of the Tyranny of Dragons storyline. It is written by Jim Zub, drawn by Max Dunbar, and edited by John Barber.

Synopsis

In the legendary city of Baldur's Gate, a young moon elf wild mage named Delina has teamed up with the legendary ranger Minsc; his hamster, Boo; and thieves Krydle and Shandie. After being separated from her companions, Delina is held for use for a dark ritual being prepared her cultist brother, Deniak. The gang has interrogates one of the cultists to learn his evil scheme and sets out to rescue their friend, hopefully before the cult's dark magic can be completed.
