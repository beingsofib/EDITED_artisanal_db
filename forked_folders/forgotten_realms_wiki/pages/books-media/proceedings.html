The Proceedings by Magus Firewand was a classic work of fiction published in Faerûn. It was acclaimed as a literary masterpiece. It had one volume.

The retired War Wizard and sage Benelaius had a copy in his library at his home near Ghars, Cormyr. His servant Jasper read it in 1366/1367 DR.

The original manuscript was kept in Candlekeep around 1372 DR.
