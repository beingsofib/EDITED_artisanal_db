Red Dragon's Tale: A Lego Adventure is an adventure for the Dungeons & Dragons 5-edition ruleset and set in the Forgotten Realms.

Description

Red Dragon's Tale: A Lego Adventure was written by Christopher Perkins with art by Patricia de Erausquin Llorens and Alison Titus Hughes. It was published on April 1, 2024, by as part of the Lego Insider rewards program, as either a free downloadable PDF or a booklet that could be ordered for a number of points, and ties into the Dungeons & Dragons: Red Dragon's Tale Lego set.
