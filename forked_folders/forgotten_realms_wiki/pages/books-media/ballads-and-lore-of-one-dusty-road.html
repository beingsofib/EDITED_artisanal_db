Ballads and Lore of One Dusty Road was a work of two volumes penned by Sharanralee Crownstar, a renowned adventuress and secretly a Harper agent in the mid-14 century DR.

Contents

Lost and Away was a traditional elfin fireside ballad sang by the Fair Folk recorded in Ballads and Lore of One Dusty Road.

History
