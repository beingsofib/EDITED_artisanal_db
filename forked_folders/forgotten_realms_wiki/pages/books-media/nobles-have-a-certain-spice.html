Nobles Have a Certain Spice was a bard ballad by an anonymous minstrel was became popular in 1254 DR.

Description

Nobles Have a Certain Spice was a song of somewhat gruesome nature and social commentary about a noblewoman being hunted by wolves in a forest.

History

Even though the song was popularized in the Year of Silent Steel, the ballad's story came true, in a way, a century later. In 1348 DR, a young ranger Florin Falconhand hatched a plan to teach an unbearably draconic and rude noblewoman of House Crownsilver, Narantha Crownsilver a lesson by scaring her, chase her into the woods and then "rescue," in attempts to change her attitude for the better.
