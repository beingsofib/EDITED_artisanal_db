Volo's Guide to Baldur's Gate II is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms.

Description

Volo's Guide to Baldur's Gate II is one in a series of guides written by famed explorer and raconteur Volothamp Geddarm (under the editorial control of Elminster of Shadowdale).
