Cauldron of Sapphire

Cauldron of Sapphire is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Tomb of Annihilation series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Chult, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 17- to 20-level characters. It is the third part in the Broken Chains series.
