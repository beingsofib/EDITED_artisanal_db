The Poison Chalice was a novel by an unknown author.

Description

A work of fiction, The Poison Chalice told a story of political intrigue set against the backdrop of Menzoberranzan. It was written in Elvish, and was not considered a valuable publication.

History

As of the late 15 century DR, a copy could be found in the guest quarters of Vlonwelv's base in the Muiral's Gauntlet section of Undermountain.
