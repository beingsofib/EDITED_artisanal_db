Genealogy of Giants and Giant-kin was a giant lore book bound in red and penned sometime in or before the mid–13 century DR.

History

Genealogy of Giants and Giant-kin combined information on races of giants and giant-kin and was a valuable tome. By the mid–14 century DR, undamaged copies of the book were valued at a hundred gold pieces. A copy of the Genealogy was preserved within the once-wondrous Bard Keep, a temple and library dedicated to Oghma that fell into ruin in the mid–13 century DR. The book was stored within the Reading Room and hid a page with the Dethek "G" rune, which was part of a set that was required to control the Keep's moving rooms. That copy of the book was also magically trapped, unleashing a red abishai once opened.
