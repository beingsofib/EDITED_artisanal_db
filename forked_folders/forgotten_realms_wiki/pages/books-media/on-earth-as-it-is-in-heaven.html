On Earth as it is in Heaven

On Earth As It Is In Heaven is the eighteenth issue of the Forgotten Realms comics series published by . It was written by Jeff Grubb, drawn by Dave Simons, and edited by Elliot S. Maggin and Kim Yale. It is the fourth and final installment in the "Fallen Idols" story arc.
