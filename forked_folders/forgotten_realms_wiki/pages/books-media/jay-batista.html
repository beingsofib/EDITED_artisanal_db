Jay Batista

Jay Batista is a game designer who has worked for TSR, Inc. He is known primarily for his work on the Kara-Tur setting.

Education and Career

Batista has a masters degree and has worked as a professor and an engineer for various radio stations. He became interested in Asian cultures at a young age, and worked on multiple sourcebooks involving Kara-Tur.
