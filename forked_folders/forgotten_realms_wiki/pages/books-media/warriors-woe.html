Warrior's Woe was a musical work known in Cormyr in the 1360s DR.

Hisotry

In the , Jasper heard Diccon Picard playing a song on his fiddle and thought it was either Warrior's Woe or Red-haired Lad, as he thought most fiddle songs sounded alike.
