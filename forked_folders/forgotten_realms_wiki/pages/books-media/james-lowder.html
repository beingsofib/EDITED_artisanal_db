James Lowder

James "Jim" Lowder is an award-winning editor and author of the fantasy and horror genres.

Work

Lowder has worked as an editor for TSR and has edited novels such as Elaine Cunningham's Elfshadow.

As an author, he has written several novels, including Prince of Lies and The Ring of Winter for the Forgotten Realms campaign setting. He was an editor for sourcebooks such as Hall of Heroes, and even comic anthologies, such as TSR Worlds Annual 1.
