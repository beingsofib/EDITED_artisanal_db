Sage of Shadowdale

The Sage of Shadowdale is a series by Ed Greenwood that follows the adventures of Elminster.

Books

  1. Elminster: The Making of a Mage (December 1994)
  2. Elminster in Myth Drannor (November 1997)
  3. The Temptation of Elminster (December 1998)
  4. Elminster in Hell (August 2001)
  5. Elminster's Daughter (May 2004)
  6. Elminster Must Die (2010)
  7. Bury Elminster Deep (2011)
  8. Elminster Enraged (2012)
  9. The Herald (2014) (also book #6 of The Sundering series)
  10. Spellstorm (2015)
