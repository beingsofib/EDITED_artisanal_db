Alexander Winter

Alexander Winter was a contract designer for Dungeons & Dragons. He is no longer in the roleplaying gaming design industry, and after working as a student researcher, is now a software engineer.

Works

Alexander was the designer for adventures such as Murder in Baldur's Gate and the The Rise of Tiamat. In addition, he wrote an adventure for Dungeon magazine 217.
