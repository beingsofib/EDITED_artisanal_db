Heroscape: Champions of the Forgotten Realms was a small expansion wave for the board game and its crossover with 4 edition Dungeons & Dragons, Battle for the Underdark, that was released in 2010. It consisted of four booster packs, each containing a treasure glyph and five miniatures. Despite the wave's title, the connections to the Forgotten Realms setting were tenuous, though each figure's accompanying card did list their home dimension as Toril.
