The Diamond by J. Robert King and Ed Greenwood is the ninth and final novella in the Double Diamond Triangle Saga series.

Summary

Piergieron is in a coma due to the death of Eidola and thought to be dead. His funeral is interrupted by the return of Kern and Noph, with Trandon and Entreri prisoner. The Blackstaff frees them both while faking their deaths. In the second funeral, Piergieron regains consciousness and is rescued. Miltiades, Belgin, Rings, and Aleena return with Eidola’s soul gem and release her soul from it. At the third funeral, intended to preserve her reputation, the gem releases the soul of Shaleen, Piergieron’s first wife and the basis for Eidola's human form. They are joyfully wed and Noph becomes a Masked Lord of Waterdeep.
