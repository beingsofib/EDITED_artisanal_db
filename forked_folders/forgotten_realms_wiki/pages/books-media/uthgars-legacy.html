Uthgar's Legacy was a tome that delved into the history of Uthgar the Battle Father and his mortal origins.

Contents

This book tales about the namesake and the creator of the Uthgardt barbarian tribes of the north Faerûn. The author talked about several historical theories of Uthgar's origins. Some believed that he was offspring of Beorunna's, the namesake of Beorunna's Well, and others claimed that he was the child of Tempus himself.

One thing was clear, however, the man who was believed to be Uthgar lived a life three times longer that a life of an ordinary human. He was a powerful and proud warrior who was elevated to divinity after dying in a one-on-onw battle against a frost giant Gurt. The legends said Uthgar conquered primeval best spirits in battle and shared their immortal gifts with his followers that became the Uthgardt barbarian at his death and apotheosis.

Scholars concluded what it was very likely that the fabled Uthgar was a Ruathym northman Uther Gardolfsson. His long life left historical footprints of many raids and looting of Illuskan, before assembling his barbarian dynasty. The author of the book argued that Uthgar's accession indeed happen but at the barbarian king's deathbed, sponsored by a deity of war who was impressed by his fighting spirit and accomplishments.

History

Copies of Uthgar's Legacy could be found around Neverwinter, and the general area of the Sword Coast North circa 1372 DR and the Moonsea region around 1373 DR.
