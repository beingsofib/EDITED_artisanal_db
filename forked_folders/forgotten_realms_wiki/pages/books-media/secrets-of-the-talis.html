Secrets of the Talis was a book dedicated to the art of fortunetelling using a talis deck, penned sometime before the late 15 century DR.

Contents

Some of the advice the book gave to aspiring seers included knowing their deck like they would know a long-lived and wise person. It stated that when one would attempt to interpret the cards and spend time with their deck, the cards would philter their arcana onto the seer. It was for the fortuneteller to discover the secrets of their decks through the powerful symbols and ancient wisdom hidden within the seventy-eight cards.
