Chris Perry

Chris Perry is a writer that contributed many articles to Dragon magazine. His earliest contribution was in Dragon #176 in 1991 and his latest was in Dragon #325 in 2004.

Works

Perry's works mainly consist of writing about the Seldarine, as seen in Servants of the Seldarine in Dragon #176, The Seldarine Revisited in Dragon #236, and Magic of the Seldarine in Dragon #251.
