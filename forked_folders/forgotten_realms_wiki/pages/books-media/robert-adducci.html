Robert Adducci

Robert Adducci was the community manager for the D&D Adventurers League from May 2014 through December 2017 and has written adventures for it as well. Robert is the founder of the official Dark Sun website, The Burnt World of Athas at Athas.org.

He was born in Phoenix and lived in the Denver area until a few years ago when he returned to Phoenix.
