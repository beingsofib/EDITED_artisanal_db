"Only a Woman Can Take this Sort of Abuse" is a short story by Ed Greenwood revolving around the Seven Sisters, based on a number of suggestions provided by attendees of 2003's Spin a Yarn with Ed Greenwood seminar, and published on the Wizards of the Coast website that same year.
