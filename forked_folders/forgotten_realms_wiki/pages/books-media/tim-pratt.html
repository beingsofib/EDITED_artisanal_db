Tim Pratt

Tim Pratt is a science fiction and fantasy writer from Dudley, North Carolina. He now resides in Berkeley, California.

Works

Tim Pratt wrote the well-received Forgotten Realms novel, Venom in Her Veins, in March 2012. Pratt stated that this was one of the best books he had even written.
