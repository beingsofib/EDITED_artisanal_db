Ray Winninger

Ray Winninger is a game designer who has worked on D&D as well as other roleplaying games. He was a competitive chess player during his childhood, and worked for TSR during the 1980s and early 1990s.

In 2020, Winninger took over as the Executive Producer in charge of the Dungeons & Dragons studio at Wizards of the Coast.
