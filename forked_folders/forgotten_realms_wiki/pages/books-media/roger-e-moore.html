Roger E. Moore

Roger E. Moore is an editor for magazines and writer of roleplaying games.

Background

Moore's interest in roleplaying games began in the later years of the 1970s. He wrote gaming articles for magazines while he was working for the Army of the United States of America.

Roger Moore was once a member of the Science Fiction and Fantasy Writers of America.
