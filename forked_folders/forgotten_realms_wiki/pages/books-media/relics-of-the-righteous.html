Relics of the Righteous

Relics of the Righteous was a book written by Thontoros the Learned, the Sage of the Just Gods.

Amongst other things, it detailed the enchantment of the magical sword Dornavver at Allyn's Anvil in Year of the Wrath Sword, 275 DR.
