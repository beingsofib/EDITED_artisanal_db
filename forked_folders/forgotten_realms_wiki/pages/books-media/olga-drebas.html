Olga Drebas

Olga Drebas is a concept artist and an illustrator. She has been a freelancer artist for Wizards of the Coast since 2015. In particular, she contributed artwork to 5 edition Dungeons & Dragons adventures, from Out of the Abyss to Baldur's Gate: Descent into Avernus.
