Saturday Morning Adventures II 2

Saturday Morning Adventures II #2: "Here There Be Pirates Part Two" is a comic published by in February, 2024. It is written by David M. Booher, with art by George Kambadais. It is the second of a second four-issue series following the characters of the 1983–1985 Dungeons & Dragons cartoon series.
