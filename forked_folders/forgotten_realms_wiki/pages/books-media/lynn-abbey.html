Lynn Abbey

Lynn Abbey (born September 8, 1948) is an American author born in Peekskill, New York. Lynn began publishing in 1979 with novels and short stories. She began writing for TSR around 1994 while continuing to write novels and editing anthologies. Her works for TSR include stories set in the Forgotten Realms and the Dark Sun setting.
