David Day

David Day is a Canadian comic book artist who has contributed to Dungeons & Dragons products and illustrated several articles for the "Elminster's Guide to the Realms" column in Dragon. His older brothers and Dan were also comic book artists.

David was born on July 28, 1965, and currently lives in Ontario.
