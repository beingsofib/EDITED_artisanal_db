Sayings of a Wise and Fat Merchant was a book penned by Guldoum Tchar of Mirabar in the late 13 century DR.

History

Sayings of a Wise and Fat Merchant was a collection of musings and thoughts put to paper in the . One notable opinion that Guldoum Tchar her was his dismissal of most spellcasters as con artists and that one could easily scare them away by pretending to cast spells, making somatic and verbal parts of spells up, scaring wizards off with ease.
