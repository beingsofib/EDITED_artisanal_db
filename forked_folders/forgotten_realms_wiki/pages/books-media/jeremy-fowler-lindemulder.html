Jeremy Fowler-Lindemulder

Jeremy Fowler-Lindemulder was a member of the Role-Playing Group Association (RPGA) who played-in the Living City organized play program and authored two modules for it. His only known player character was the salacious half-elf dancer DeVia Dirkdancer, who featured in both of his written modules.

The author also ran a website titled DeViations as part of the Streets of Ravens Bluff web ring, which doubled as a fan site for his character DeVia. The second of his modules notably included a (now defunct) web address to the site for those who were curious to learn more about her.

Works

  • Cat and Mouse (July 18, 1997)
  • Court of Thieves (July 17, 1998)
