Baldur's Gate: Enhanced Edition is a remaster of the 1998 video game Baldur's Gate and its expansion, Baldur's Gate: Tales of the Sword Coast. Released in 2012, the Enhanced Edition adds several hours worth of new content, along with numerous technological improvements.

New features

The Enhanced Edition brought 3 new characters, several new areas, and a number of new quests.

  • Rasaad yn Bashir - A Calishite monk on a journey to seek out inner solace after the death of his brother.
  • Neera - A half-elf wild mage who isn't in full control of her magic.
  • Dorn Il-Khan - A half-orc blackguard on a path of bloody revenge.
  • Baldur's Gate: The Black Pits
  • Cloud Peaks
  • Enhanced interface
  • New cinematics
  • Improved multiplayer
  • All Baldur's Gate II specific features included
