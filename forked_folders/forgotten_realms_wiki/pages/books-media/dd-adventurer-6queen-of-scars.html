Queen of Scars is an encounter published in Dungeons & Dragons: Adventurer #6. The encounter was the sixth in the series of encounters comprising Adventure #1.

Summary

Upon his triumphant return to Phandalin with treasure and tales, Mordred Wester was honored with a feast by his brother, townmaster Harbin Wester. The celebration was cut short when orcs laid siege to the town, demanding Mordred's life. In response, Harbin turns to a group of adventurers (the future Champions of Neverwinter) to resolve the crisis.
