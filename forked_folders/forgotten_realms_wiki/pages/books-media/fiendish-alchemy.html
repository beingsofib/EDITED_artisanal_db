Fiendish Alchemy was a book on the topic of the art of alchemy and the Part XIV of the Basics of Alchemy series penned by Haskinn Xhesilaphin, a dragonborn alchemist sometime before the late 15 century DR.

Content

Most notably, Fiendish Alchemy reatured a chapter called Fiendish Flora, which listed plantlfe found on the layer of Avernus in the Nine Hells. Most of them were stated to be born from the vital fluids spilled in the Blood War.

Notable Owners

  • Arfur Gregorio, a toymaker and a cultist of the The Absolute in the late 15 century DR.
  • Golbraith Stredivas, a late 15 century DR monster hunter from Baldur's Gate.
