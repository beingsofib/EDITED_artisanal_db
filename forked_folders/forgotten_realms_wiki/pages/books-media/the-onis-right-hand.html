Oni no Migite or The Oni's Right Hand is a Japanese-language adventure module released on July 28, 2025, on the Dungeons & Dragons Japan website for use in learn-to-play games in Japan, beginning in August. It was written by Masaki Yanagida, who also created pre-generated characters, and features character illustrations by Toshiaki Takayama. Intended as a starting adventure for Dragon of Icespire Peak from the Dungeons & Dragons Essentials Kit, it takes place in the Phandalin region and involves adventurers from Wa and Kozakura searching for an artifact named the Glove of Oni Claw.{{cite web
