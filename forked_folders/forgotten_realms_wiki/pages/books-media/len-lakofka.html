Len Lakofka

Len Lakofka was an author, editor and designer. He was an influential voice in the development of . He was one of the playtesters of the game as it was being developed. He was also an editor of early sourcebooks, including the Players Handbook 1st edition and Dungeon Masters Guide 1st edition. He wrote a regular column, Leomund's Tiny Hut, for Dragon magazine.
