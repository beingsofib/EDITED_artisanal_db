Last Charge of the Hellriders was a book about the Hellriders of Elturel. It had no real value.

In the , Duke Thalamra Vanthampur of Baldur's Gate had a copy in her library at Vanthampur Villa, but kept a key to her dungeons concealed within a hollow in its pages.
