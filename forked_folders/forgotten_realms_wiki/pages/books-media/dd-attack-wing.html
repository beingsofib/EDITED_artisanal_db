D&D Attack Wing is a strategy-based tabletop game released in October 2014. It is set in the Forgotten Realms setting

It can be played in three different forms. The first form is free play, where the players build their legion according to the basic rules of the game. The second form consists of playing scenarios involving good versus evil, with added neutral allegiances in certain modules. The third form involves tournament play; this form is usually played in a local store sponsored by Wizkids. In it, the players set up a battlefield with objectives. They are subject to time limits and must play in rounds. The winner gets a limited-edition prize figure.

The starter set comes with a copper dragon (Galadaeros), a blue dragon (Eshaedra), and a red dragon (Balagos). Some of the basic game tools are attack/defense dice, movement dials, objective tokens, character cards, upgrade cards, and custom movement measurement sticks.
