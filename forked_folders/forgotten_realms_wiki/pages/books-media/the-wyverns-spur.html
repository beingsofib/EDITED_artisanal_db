The Wyvern's Spur is the second book of The Finder's Stone trilogy by Jeff Grubb and Kate Novak.

Summary

The novel begins on 19 Ches, 1358 DR. Flattery Wyvernspur sends his apprentice Cat, a twin of Alias, to steal the Wyvern’s Spur from the crypt where it is kept, but Drone sends Jade More, another twin, who takes it first. Flattery sends a wraith to recover it, and Drone fakes his death. The spur falls into the possession of Olive Rustkettle, who eventually gives it to Giogi Wyvernspur, who defeats Flattery with the help of Olive, Drone, and Cat.
