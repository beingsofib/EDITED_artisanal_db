Roger Raupp

Roger Raupp was an artist and illustrator who has contributed interior and cover art to several sourcebooks, as well as several issues of both Dragon and Dungeon magazines. He was also an Art Director for Dungeon magazine and was on the editorial staff for Dragon magazine.
