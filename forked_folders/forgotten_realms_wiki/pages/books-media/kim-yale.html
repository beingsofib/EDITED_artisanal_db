Kim Yale

Kim Yale was an author and editor. She was an editor at and oversaw several licensed titles including the Advanced Dungeons & Dragons comic series and Forgotten Realms comic series from 1991 to 1993.

Advanced Dungeons & Dragons

She was editor of the following issues:
