Selvar's Ineffable Conjurations, Magicks, and Phantasms was an ancient arcane spellbook penned by the wizard Selvar. It contained 32 spells ranging from common to unique or rare.

Description

This tome was medium-sized measuring three handwidths wide by three handwidths tall. The book was enclosed with ash boards covered with stretched white-tanned cowhide. The cover was adorned with brass corner caps and the tome was bound in brass rings. There were 33 pages in the tome with each containing a spell save the first title page.

The first spells in the book were from the school of illusion and written in the secret magical language of illusionists called ruathlek.

Powers

Selvar's Ineffable Conjurations, Magicks, and Phantasms contained the following spells:

color spray • detect invisibility • gaze reflection • phantasmal force • blacklight • hypnotic pattern • invisibility • magic mouth • mirror image • plague • dispel illusion • fear • mailed might • paralyzation • spectral force • dispel exhaustion • phantasmal killer • maze • shadow door • demishadow magic • turnshadow • web • dispel magic • haste • water breathing • ice storm • remove curse • hold monster • wall of force • globe of invulnerability • power word, silence • Khelben's warding whip
