Treasure of the Broken Hoard

Treasure of the Broken Hoard is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Storm King's Thunder series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Parnast and Phlan, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1- to 2-level characters.
