The Wilds

The Wilds is a group of four novels written by various authors. Each novel was published by Wizards of the Coast in 2009.

The books are:

  1. The Fanged Crown by Jenna Helland (January 2009)
  2. The Restless Shore by James P. Davis (May 2009)
  3. The Edge of Chaos by Jak Koke (August 2009)
  4. Wrath of the Blue Lady by Mel Odom (December 2009)
