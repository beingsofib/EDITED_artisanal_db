Dragons Ye Should Know was a book published by the sage Myrindas of Port Kir in the year 1354 DR.

Description

This book detailed a number of notable dragons in the Realms, such as the white dragon Arauthator.

History

This book was later quoted by Volothamp Geddarm in his own chapbook detailing the most notable dragon rulers of the Sword Coast North region, Wyrms of the North.
