Carrie Bebris

Carrie Bebris is an American author, journalist, novelist and editor.

Career

Carrie Bebris worked as a newspaper reporter and English teacher before coming to fantasy publishing. As an editor for TSR, Inc., she helped develop the Birthright setting while sometimes working on the Ravenloft, Dragonlance, and the Forgotten Realms worlds. Bebris and William W. Connors wrote the Ravenloft novel Shadowborn (1998), and she wrote the novel adaptation for the video game Pool of Radiance: Ruins of Myth Drannor in 2001. Bebris also wrote three articles for Dragon, and did editing work and proofreading on several D&D game books for TSR, Inc..

As a freelancer, her work runs from fiction to articles in Better Homes and Gardens Special Interest Publications. Her other works include Regency novel mysteries which are based on Pride and Prejudice.
