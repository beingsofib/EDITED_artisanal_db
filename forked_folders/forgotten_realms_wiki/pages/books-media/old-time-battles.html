Old Time Battles was popular upbeat song in the late 15 century DR, often performed without lyrics, only accompanied by rhythmic grunts and finished by a "HOO-RAH!" shout.

History

By the , performances of Old Time Battles were common on the streets of Baldur's Gate and along the shores of the Chionthar Valley in the Western Heartlands. Bards and minstrels who sang or played the melody were sure to swiftly attract the attention of the crowds. The song's annotated sheet music could be found in the Chromatic Scale instrument shop in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate.
