Dungeons of Mystery is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory. The box set includes two paperback booklets, one containing mini-adventures and the other containing Monstrous Compendium sheets. The set also includes foldout set that allows the Dungeon Master to build their own 3D dungeon maps.

Contents

Books:

  • Dungeons of Mystery (64 pages, stapled, no cover)

Maps:

  • City streets battle map (front color, back monochrome)
  • Castle layout battle map (front color, back monochrome)
  • Outdoor sandy battle map (front color, back monochrome)

Other:

  • 24 pages of fold-up 3D buildings
