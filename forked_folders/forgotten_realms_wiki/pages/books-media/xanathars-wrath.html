Xanathar's Wrath

Xanathar's Wrath is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Waterdeep series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Skullport, and is an adventure designed for 5- to 10-level characters. It is part three of the Skullport Shakedown Trilogy.
