The Cormyte's Boast was a song written by Master Bard Chanthalas of Cormyr.

Contents

In the 14 century DR, The Cormyte's Boast was used as an example of a typical Cormyte in Volo's Guide to Cormyr by Master Traveler Volothamp Geddarm. The ballad illustrated them as being "honest, self-confident, and loyal."
