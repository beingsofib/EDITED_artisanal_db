Rrakkma

Rrakkma is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is an introductory adventure for Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes. It focuses on members of the Sha'sal Khou working to stop a group of mind flayers from reenslaving the gith race, and is an adventure designed for a group of six pre-generated 9-level characters.
