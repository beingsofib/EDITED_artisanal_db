Dungeons and Dragons Online: Magic of Myth Drannor is an expansion pack for Dungeons & Dragons Online released in August 2024. The expansion is primarily set in and around Myth Drannor during the Elven Crusade of Ilsevele Miritar in 1374 DR, as the elves and their allies must face the magical monsters that lurk in the city and an opportunistic Red Wizards of Thay expedition.
