The Annotated Elminster collects the first three of Ed Greenwood's classic Elminster novels: Elminster: The Making of a Mage, Elminster in Myth Drannor, and The Temptation of Elminster. Extensive annotations by the author will provide fascinating insight into not only the books themselves but the process of creation that made the Forgotten Realms world this generation's greatest fantasy setting.''

The Annotated Elminster is a collection of novels by Ed Greenwood with unpublished annotations by the author. It collects the first three volumes of the Elminster series:

  • Elminster: The Making of a Mage
  • Elminster in Myth Drannor
  • The Temptation of Elminster

It was released on November 27, 2007.
