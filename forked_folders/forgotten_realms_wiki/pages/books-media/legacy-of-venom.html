Legacy of Venom

Legacy of Venom is an adventure module released as part of the Adventurer's Guild organized play program in 1999, and a tie-in with Van Richten's Monster Hunter's Compendium, Volume One. It uses characters from Ravens Bluff in Ravenloft.

Overview

A ranger and a young boy become infected by werespider lycanthropy, and the adventurer begins to attack the town of Liara in Nova Vaasa during the nights. Adventurers are called on to put a stop to the murders.
