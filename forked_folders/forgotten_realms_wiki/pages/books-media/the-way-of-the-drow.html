The Way of the Drow

The Way of the Drow is a series of novels by R.A. Salvatore published over 2021–2023 and featuring Drizzt Do'Urden and Jarlaxle. The series follows the Generations series.

  1. Starlight Enclave (August 3, 2021)
  2. Glacier's Edge (August 9, 2022)
  3. Lolth's Warrior (August 15, 2023)
