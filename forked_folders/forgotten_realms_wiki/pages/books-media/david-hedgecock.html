David Hedgecock

David Hedgecock is a comic book editor and publisher for . He graduated from the University of California San Diego with a Bachelor's degree.

Works

Hedgecock has edited comics set in both the Forgotten Realms and Ravenloft campaign settings, mainly detailing the adventures of the Heroes of Baldur's Gate. He has been the editor for comic book series such as Frost Giant's Fury, Shadows of the Vampire, Evil at Baldur's Gate, A Darkened Wish, and Infernal Tides.
