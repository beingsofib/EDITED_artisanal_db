Legends of Baldur's Gate 2

Legends of Baldur's Gate #2 is a comic published by set in the Forgotten Realms. It is a part of the Tyranny of Dragons storyline. It is written by Jim Zub, drawn by Max Dunbar, and edited by John Barber.

Synopsis

In the legendary city of Baldur's Gate, a young moon elf (and wild mage) named Delina teams with the newly revived ranger Minsc, his hamster Boo, and thieves Krydle and Shandie. To find Delina's missing brother, Krydle makes a deal with The Fetcher – in exchange for the brother’s location, Krydle must deliver a letter to a member of the Parliament of Peers. Learning the Peer in question is his old friend (and Krydle's father) Coran, Minsc leaps into action – and hijinks are about to ensue…
