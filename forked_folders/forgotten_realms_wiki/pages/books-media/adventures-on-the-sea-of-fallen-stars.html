Adventures on the Sea of Fallen Stars was a collection of stories about legendary pirates of the Realms, published some time before late 15 century DR..

Contents

The stories in the book included one of a struggle between Gracie Gravehart and the evil pirate Lord Buckler. One of the scenes depicted the two crossing their cutlasses in battle over the freedom of the people of the isle of Everwind following the pirate's ship being sunk. Following the battle, Gracie Gravehart was crowned the queen of Everwind who continued sailing her ship, Liberty, until her death. She was buried on the island, and the key from her crypt was tossed into Umberlee's depths. Legends said that sailors could still see the Liberty's red said far in the sea in the early morning at the very beginning of dawn.

History

As of the , a copy of Night-Night, Pirate Ship belonged to Lora, a drunkard sailor from Baldur's Gate, whose daughter, Vanra, was consumed by a hag seeking to produce offspring. The book was a gift, evidenced by the writing on the inner cover that read: "Lots of love, Uncle Tim Twofingers."
