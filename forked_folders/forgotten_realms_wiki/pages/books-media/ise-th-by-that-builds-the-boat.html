I'se th' b'y that Builds the Boat was a sea shanty from the late 14 century DR.

History

In the , I'se th' b'y that Builds the Boat was sang at the Vulgar Monkey, a dockside tavern on the island of Brynnlaw in the Nelanther Isles. The shanty was a favorite of Pirate Newf's.
