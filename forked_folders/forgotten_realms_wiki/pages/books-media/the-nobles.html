The Nobles

The Nobles is a series of unconnected novels, each with a different author. The books all touch on the schemes of the nobility in Faerûn, and are a look into the politics behind powers such as Tethyr, Waterdeep, and the Vilhon Reach

The Nobles

  1. King Pinch (May 1995) – David Cook
  2. War in Tethyr (October 1995) – Victor Milán
  3. Escape from Undermountain (February 1996) – Mark Anthony
  4. The Mage in the Iron Mask (August 1996) – Brian Thomsen
  5. The Council of Blades (December 1996) – Pauli Kidd
  6. The Simbul's Gift (October 1997) - Lynn Abbey
