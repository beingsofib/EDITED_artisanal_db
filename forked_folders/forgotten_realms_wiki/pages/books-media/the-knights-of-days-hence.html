The Knights of Days Hence was an epic ballad about the ghostly knights of the Firewine Bridge, penned by the bard named Poe in the late 14 century DR.

History

In the , Poe ventured onto the Firewine Bridge, laid to ruins some two centuries prior. He strove to write an epic song about the ghost knights and the old dungeon underneath the ruins. He would recite the poem to any adventurers whom he encountered on the bridge's ruins.
