Damien Foletto

Damien "Puuk" Foletto is a video game design director for Lost Boys Interactive. Formerly, he worked for Black Isle Studios.

Video Games

Designer

  • Icewind Dale II (2002)
  • Baldur's Gate III: The Black Hound (cancelled)

QA Tester

  • Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn (2000)
  • Baldur's Gate II: Throne of Bhaal (2001)
  • Baldur's Gate II: Enhanced Edition (2013)
