Brotherhood of the Griffon (series)

Brotherhood of the Griffon is a series of five novels by Richard Lee Byers that continue the adventure of Aoth Fezim, the leader of the Brotherhood of the Griffon who was introduced in The Haunted Lands trilogy.

Brotherhood of the Griffon

  1. The Captive Flame (April 2010)
  2. Whisper of Venom (November 2010)
  3. The Spectral Blaze (June 2011)
  4. The Masked Witches (February 2012)
  5. Prophet of the Dead (February 2013)
