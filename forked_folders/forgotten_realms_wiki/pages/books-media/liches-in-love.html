Liches in Love

Liches in Love is the fourteenth issue of the Forgotten Realms comics series published by . It was written by Jeff Grubb, drawn by Rags Morales, and edited by Elliot S. Maggin. The story in this issue is concluded in chapter four in TSR Worlds Annual 1.
