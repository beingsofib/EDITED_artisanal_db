Karen S. Boomgarden

Karen S. Boomgarden is an editor and author who worked for TSR.

Trivia

The clothing and armor supplier Boom's Garden that is mentioned in Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue is an in-joke referring to the surname of Karen Boomgarden.
