Christopher Perkins

Christopher Perkins has written many supplements for Wizards of the Coast, first as a freelance contributor in Dungeon magazine, then as that magazine's editor before becoming editor-in chief for all Wizards of the Coast periodicals. He was also the design manager for the Eberron campaign setting before becoming the Story Design and Development Manager for all of Dungeons and Dragons in 2007, acting as senior producer for the game.
