"Volo Breaks a Hot Tale" is a short comedic story by Ed Greenwood set in Waterdeep, based on a number of suggestions provided by attendees of 2008's Spin a Yarn with Ed Greenwood seminar, and published on the Wizards of the Coast website that same year.
