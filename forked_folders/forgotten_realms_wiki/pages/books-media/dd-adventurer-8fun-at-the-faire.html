Fun at the Faire is an encounter published in Dungeons & Dragons: Adventurer #8. The encounter was the eighth in the series of encounters comprising Adventure #1.

Summary

Lady Keris Daunt visited Phandalin, organized, and sponsored a faire for the denizens of the town to honor the deeds of the group of adventurers who would later gain fame as the Champions of Neverwinter. The celebration offered the adventurers a chance to compete in games and earn renown. Lady Daunt's own knight, Sir Bertalot Malcer, was plotting to kill Lady Daunt. The adventurers had to navigate the festivities to uncover a treasonous conspiracy and defeat the servant of Dagult Neverember.
