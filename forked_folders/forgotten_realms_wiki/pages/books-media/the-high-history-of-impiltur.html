The High History of Impiltur was a history book penned by Amphyr the Venerable that chronicled the history of the nation of Impiltur up to its publication date, the .

Contents

Among the contents of The High History of Impiltur was a recount of how Ilmara, sister of King Imbrar, had taken elven scrying stones from the ruins of Myth Drannor and through them learned that the hobgoblins of the Giantspire Mountains were rebuilding their forces. An act which sent Imbrar to launch an assault on them with his Royal Guard and a host of armsmen, only to never be seen again.
