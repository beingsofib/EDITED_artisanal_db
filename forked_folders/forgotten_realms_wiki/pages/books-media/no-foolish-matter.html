No Foolish Matter

No Foolish Matter is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Rage of Demons series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on the hamlet of Hillpicket, and a travelling carnival. It is an adventure designed for three to seven 1- to 4-level characters.

Summary

The adventure begins with adventurers arriving in the hamlet of Hillpicket. However, when a strange carnival arrives with a reputation as the herald of some bizarre malady, they are compelled to investigate.

After battling through a deadly mirror maze, the adventurers confront a doppelganger masquerading as the carnival's owner. Upon defeating the creature, they release the trapped Doctor Jubal and end the sickness.
