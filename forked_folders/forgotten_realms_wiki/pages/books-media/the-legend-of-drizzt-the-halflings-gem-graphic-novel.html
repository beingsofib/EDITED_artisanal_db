The Legend of Drizzt: The Halfling's Gem (original title: Forgotten Realms: The Legend of Drizzt, Volume 6: The Halfling's Gem) is the graphic novel version of R.A. Salvatore's novel The Halfling's Gem. It was originally published in three parts: The Legend of Drizzt: The Halfling's Gem #1–3.
