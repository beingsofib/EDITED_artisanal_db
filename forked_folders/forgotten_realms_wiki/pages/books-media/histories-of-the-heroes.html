Histories of the Heroes was a history book that collected tales of great heroes, men and women of honor and action.

Contents

Histories of the Heroes was published sometime in of before the . A copy of the book found home in the private library of Taeros Hawkwinter of Waterdeep, in the noble family, estate – the Hawkwinter House. The nobleman treasured the tales of grand adventures and dazzling heroes, such as King Azoun Obarskyr IV.
