The Ogre's Paw

The Ogre's Paw was the first issue of the Forgotten Realms comics series published by . It was written by Jeff Grubb, drawn by Rags Morales, and edited by Barbara Kesel and Elliot S. Maggin. It is the first installment in the "The Hand of Vaprak!" story arc.
