Another Dart in the Minstrel's Heart was a play involving a minstrel and a heroine that they were romantically involved with.

History

Around the , the Ravens Bluff Playhouse put on a benefit performance of Another Dart in the Minstrel's Heart to support the Hand of Mercy Children's Hospital and Orphanage. Martin, the flamboyant recruit officer of the Silent Network, starred in the leading role and put on a dashing performance that won the adoration of many women in the audience.
