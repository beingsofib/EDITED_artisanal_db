The Forgotten Vault is an encounter published in Dungeons & Dragons: Adventurer #2. The encounter was the second in the series of encounters comprising Adventure #1.

Summary

Circa 1451 DR, when Daran Edermath located a secret vault beneath a ruined elven villa in the Neverwinter Wood. However, he subsequently lost all memory of this discovery. Decades later, around 1491 DR, he stumbled upon a note in his own handwriting referencing the vault, but it failed to jog his memory. To solve the mystery, Daran enlisted a group of adventurers—who would later gain fame as the Champions of Neverwinter—to uncover the vault's secrets.
