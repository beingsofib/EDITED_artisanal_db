The Legend of Drizzt Visual Dictionary is a guide written by Michael Witwer with the foreword penned by R. A. Salvatore which is set to be published by DK Publishing in accociation with Wizards of the Coast on March 14, 2023. The book will be a large reference book detailing the characters, items, and locations within R.A Salvatore's novels.
