The Life and Times of Grooble Stonepate was a work of fiction featuring a dwarf named Grooble Stonepate, written and published sometime before the late 14 century DR.

History

A copy of The Life and Times of Grooble Stonepate was kept in the library of Zerith Hold, Lord Purdun's stronghold in the city of Duhlnarim in Erlkazar, around . Lord Purdun's copy was notable for a doodle of Grooble Stonepate on the book's inner cover. The image of was questionable quality and evidently drawn by someone who'd never seen a dwarf.
