Todd Fox

Todd Foxx is a comic book artist. Fox worked on The Grand Tour, a promotional comic set in the Forgotten Realms. He penciled all aspects of the comic, including the famed (and now much older) characters of the Dungeons & Dragons television series.

Works

Foxx provided art for:

  • The Grand Tour
