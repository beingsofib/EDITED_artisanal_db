A Minstrel's Whimsy was a chapbook that collected lyrics of ballads, penned by Dezsmra Langrafarl of Iriaebor in the late 14 century DR.

Contents

One of the ballads recoded in the chapbook was the Waiting Grave, a song about brave galant heroes and backstabbing knaves. The collection was published in the .
