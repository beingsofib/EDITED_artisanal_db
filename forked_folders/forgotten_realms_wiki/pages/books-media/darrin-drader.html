Darrin Drader

Darrin Drader is a novelist and game designer who co-authored several Forgotten Realms and core D&D sourcebooks. He was employed as a writer at 38 Studios, a computer gaming company owned by Curt Shilling and R.A. Salvatore until its financial collapse.
