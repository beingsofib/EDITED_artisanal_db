On Educating the Faithful was a lengthy religious Lolthite treatise delivered by Grand Matron Tarrae in the drow city of Menzoberranzan and recorded by an unnamed spy of the Seldarine. The record was published sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , On Educating the Faithful was found in circulation with one of its copies making its way into the Underdark underneath the Western Heartlands on Faerûn. Another copy was found in Nansi Gretta's book shop, The Bibliophile, located in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, as well as another copy in her private chambers above.
