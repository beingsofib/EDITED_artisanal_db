Mark Liberman

Mark Liberman was a member of the Role-Playing Group Association (RPGA) who both played in and authored material for the Living City organized play program. He notably conceived of the House of Thud, a gambling establishment in Ravens Bluff that was a staple of many convention interactives. His known player characters included Carmen, operator of the House of Thud, who was first made back when the Living City was still operating under 1 edition AD&D rules.
