Song of Spears

Song of Spears is an Epic Adventures module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Plague of Ancients series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Icewind Dale, and is an adventure designed for 5- to 13-level characters.
