Scott Davis

Scott Davis is a designer and author that worked on 2 edition Dungeons & Dragons material.

Works

Davis worked on the design of many creatures in Monstrous Compendium Spelljammer Appendix 2, such as the gullion. He also designed the clothing for the Arms and Equipment Guide, and authored The Hill of Lost Souls section in Elminster's Ecologies Appendix I.
