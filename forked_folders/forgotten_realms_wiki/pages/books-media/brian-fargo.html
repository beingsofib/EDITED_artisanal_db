Brian Fargo

Brian Fargo is a computer game producer and designer who founded both and .

Career

Fargo began work in the video game industry in 1982. Only a year later, he founded Interplay Entertainment. He was the executive producer of the Forgotten Realms-based Interplay game Blood & Magic, which was released in 1996.

He remained with the company until his resignation in 2000. Two years later, he founded inXile Entertainment with many former Interplay employees.
