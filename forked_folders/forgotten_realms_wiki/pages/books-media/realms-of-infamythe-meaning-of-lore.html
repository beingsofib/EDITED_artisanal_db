"The Meaning of Lore" is a short story by Barb Hendee included in the 1994 anthology Realms of Infamy.

Summary

Chane Troiban, a young and charismatic Oghmite scholar, lies and cheats his way to gain access to the secret vaults within the Seat of Lore in Berdusk.
