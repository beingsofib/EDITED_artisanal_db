Uncovering Umgatlik was a research manuscript dedicated to the study of the ancient Netherese lich Umgatlik.

Contents

Uncovering Umgatlik was a collection of drafts, manuscripts, and sketches, some of which were done in charcoal. The work was compiled by a hapless Savant Gumbril, who signed his manuscript as Savant Gumbril the Great.

History

Savant Gumbril discovered lore about what he thought was a long lost god of the ancient Netheril empire. He assembled a team and, eventually, uncovered Umgatlik's shrine, buried within the deadly cliffs of the Crab's Claw Coast in the . Uncovering Umgatlik was what Savant Gumbril titled his notes and copies of writings and arcane symbols collected from within the lost crypt. As of 1372 DR, the research remained unpublished. The book's future remained unclear, possibly due to Gumbril's rumored death or the research being discredited or even abandoned.
