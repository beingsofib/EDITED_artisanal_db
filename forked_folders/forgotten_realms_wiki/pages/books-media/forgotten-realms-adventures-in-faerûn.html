Forgotten Realms: Adventures in Faerûn is a campaign sourcebook for the Forgotten Realms using the 5-edition (revised) ruleset published by Wizards of the Coast. It is set to be released in November 2025 and, alongside Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn, will be the first campaign setting book released for the Forgotten Realms in 5 edition, a full decade after Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide. Oriented toward dungeon masters, it presents adventure ideas, monsters, and magic items, and an introductory adventure, "The Lost Library of Lethchauntos".

Contents

It includes five detailed gazetteers on Baldur's Gate (described as dark urban fantasy), Calimshan, Dalelands, Moonshae Isles, and Icewind Dale (described as survival horror), with over fifty ready-to-run adventures, in addition to "The Lost Library of Lethchauntos". It also includes fifty-two maps, thirty-seven or forty monsters and villains, and seven or twelve magic items. It also includes rules for Bastions, allowing player-built strongholds.
