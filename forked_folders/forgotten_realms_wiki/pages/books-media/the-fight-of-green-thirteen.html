The Fight of Green Thirteen was a ballad penned by Gallow Deely in the early 11 century DR.

Contents

The song, originally performed on the enchanted Cittern of War, told of the heroic struggle of the Company of Frozen Trees, aided by Gallow Deely's own magical music, against a young green dragon in the . The song continued being a mainstay of West Faerûnean savers into the 14 century DR.
