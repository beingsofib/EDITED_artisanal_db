Of Ships and the Sea is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory.

Description

Of Ships and the Sea is the ninth and final book in the DMGR series. It focuses on ships, sailing, and naval combat, as well as underwater exploration.

Contents

  • Chapter 1: Ships
  • Chapter 2: Nautical Travel
  • Chapter 3: Nautical Encounters
  • Chapter 4: Nautical Combat
  • Chapter 5: Underwater Survival
  • Chapter 6: Underwater Combat
  • Chapter 7: Spells Underwater
  • Chapter 8: Magical Items Underwater
  • Chapter 9: Underwater Topology
  • Chapter 10: Denizens of the Deep
  • Chapter 11: Using Specialists

Also includes fifty-nine tables.
