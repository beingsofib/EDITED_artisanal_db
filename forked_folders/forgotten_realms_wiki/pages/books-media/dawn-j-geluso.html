Dawn J. Geluso

Dawn J. Geluso is a freelance editor who has been editing professionally since 1998. She has contributed to 4- and 5–edition Dungeons & Dragons magazines, adventures, and sourcebooks. Geluso has a degree in journalism from Pepperdine University, but only branched out into the gaming industry as a result of the interests of her partner. She has since moved away from technical editing and role-playing games.
