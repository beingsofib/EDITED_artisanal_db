Galagard's Compendium of Spells Netherese was a spellbook featuring in a vision sent by Mystra.

History

In the , Elminster Aumar, a Chosen of Mystra, was shown a number of visions and temptations by Mystra. One of them was a room full of spellbooks, including one especially thick volume labeled Galagard's Compendium of Spells Netherese. It was unclear whether these specific books actually existed outside of the vision.
