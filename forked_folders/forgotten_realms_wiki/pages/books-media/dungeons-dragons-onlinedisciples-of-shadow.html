Dungeons & Dragons Online/Disciples of Shadow

Disciples of Shadow is a partially free quest chain for Dungeons & Dragons Online where the player characters must investigate Sharran cultists in Cormyr. The adventure pack was added to the game on May 22, 2013 as part of Update 18, and serves as an introduction to further content involving Wheloon Prison and the Shadovar.

The final part of Disciples of Shadow requires the purchase of Dungeons & Dragons Online: Shadowfell Conspiracy.

Plot

Disciples of Shar has Abella Toth pursuing the Sharran Dedryk Black, who somehow escaped from Wheloon Prison. She's traced him to a secret hideout in the Storm Horns, where he's already assembled a cult of fanatical followers. Abella tasks the players with finding him and bringing him back. After fighting through the cave system, they manage to corner and defeat Dedryk, who allows himself to be captured.

Escape Plan features Barthold Moncrief, who hires the players to help guard the Wheloon Prison boat as it transports Dedryk to Wheloon Prison. While camped on the shore of the Wyvernflow one night, the escort comes under attack by a fierce force of lizardfolk led by Rynarr Nosryrr and Srass Marnanr, and they dimension door Dedryk away. Before they can undo his bindings properly and make a full escape, the players pursue them to a nearby campsite and recapture Dedryk, once again putting him in custody.

Shadow of a Doubt is from Celia Pendrick, who has the players take part in Dedryk's interrogation once they reach Wheloon Prison. Joining Cornelius Latrimor in the Magistrate's Office, they manage to get some information out of him but are interrupted by the Shadowfell Nexus bringing the Shadowfell to the Prime Material plane, and allowing a horde of shadows to emerge. While attempting to get help from Captain Hillard, the players find that the shadows are accompanied by a full-blown riot and prisoner breakout attempt. Though they manage to secure the area and end the crisis, the shadar-kai Teralyn takes advantage of the distraction to kill Magistrate Cornelius and vanish Dedryk away into the shadows.
