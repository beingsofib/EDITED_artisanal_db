Andy Collins

Andy Collins is a game designer for Wizards of the Coast.

Education

Collins received his bachelors degree in English from in 1994.

Projects

Collins has worked at Wizards of the Coast since 1996. He was the author of the "Sage Advice" column of Dragon magazine. His projects included the 3.5 edition and 4 edition revisions of the Dungeons & Dragons rulesets.
