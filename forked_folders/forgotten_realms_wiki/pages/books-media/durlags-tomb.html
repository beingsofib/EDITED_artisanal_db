Durlag's Tomb

Durlag's Tomb is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Storm King's Thunder series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Durlag's Tower, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 11- to 16-level characters.
