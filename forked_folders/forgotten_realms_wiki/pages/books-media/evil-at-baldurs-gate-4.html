Evil at Baldur's Gate 4

Evil at Baldur's Gate #4: Beyond Death is a comic published by set in Baldur's Gate. It is the fourth issue of the Evil at Baldur's Gate series. It was written by Jim Zub and drawn by Ramon Bachs.
