Whistling in the Dark

Whistling in the Dark is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the Living City organized play program and part seven of the Living City at War series of modules, and is intended for a party of adventurers of any levels. It was published in 1996 and later published once more as part of the Adventurer's Guild in 1999.
