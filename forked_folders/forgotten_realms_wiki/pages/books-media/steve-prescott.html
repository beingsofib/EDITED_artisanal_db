Steve Prescott

Steve Prescott is a fantasy . Steve Prescott is best known to fans of World of Darkness and Shadowrun products, but has also done work for Alderac Entertainment Group, Sword & Sorcery Studios, Hero Games, and more recently Wizards of the Coast.
