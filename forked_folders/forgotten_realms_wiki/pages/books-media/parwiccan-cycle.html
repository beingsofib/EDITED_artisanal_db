Parwiccan Cycle

The Parwiccan Cycle was a compilation of ancient elven myths regarding the origins of dragonkind and the first interactions of elves and dragons.

The myths compiled in the Parwiccan Cycle told of the Tearfall, among other events.

Many respected scholars, such as most of the sages of Candlekeep, dismissed the Parwiccan Cycle as just a compilation of fancy elven mythology with no relationship with actual historical facts.
