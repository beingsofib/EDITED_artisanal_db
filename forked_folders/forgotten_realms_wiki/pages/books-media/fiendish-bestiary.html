Fiendish Bestiary was a book on the topic of Hellish zoology that saw print sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.

Content

Fiendish Bestiary attempted to record the fiendish fauna and many creatures that battled in the Blood War. The chapter on devills took the majority of the book's page count.

Notable Owners

  • Helsik, the diabolist proprietor of the Devil's Fee shop in Baldur's Gate.
  • Mystic Carrion, the mummy dealer in dark magics who laired in Philgrave's Mansion in the late 15 century DR.
