The Everwinking Eye was a column by Ed Greenwood published in Polyhedron Newszine. It was written as a guide by Elminster Aumar as he toured various locations in the Realms.

It was titled Elminster's Everwinking Eye starting in issue #95 of the German Polyhedron magazine.
