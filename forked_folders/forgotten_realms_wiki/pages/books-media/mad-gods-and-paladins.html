Mad Gods and Paladins

Mad Gods and Paladins is the sixthteenth issue of the Forgotten Realms comics series published by . It was written by Jeff Grubb, drawn by Rags Morales, and edited by Elliot S. Maggin. It is the second installment in the "Fallen Idols" story arc.
