Katerina Ladon

Katerina Ladon is an Italian illustrator and concept artist. She has worked with companies such as Wizards of the Coast, Penguin Random House, Mana Project Studio, Paizo Inc., Ghostfire Gaming, and Monte Cook Games, among others.
