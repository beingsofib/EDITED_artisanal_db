Things a Knight Should Know was a book of musings penned by Myrintar Hasantar, Sage of Suzail, in the early 14 century DR.

History

Things a Knight Should Know was published in the . A copy of the book was preserved within the library citadel of Candlekeep on the Sword Coast as of the late 14 century DR.
