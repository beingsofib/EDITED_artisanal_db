The Escapades of Fundango Glovin was a scroll that contained a musical work, penned sometime long before the late 15 century DR.

History

A decrepit and decaying copy of The Escapades of Fundango Glovin could be found in the city of Baldur's Gate, sold by Thomas C. Quirkilious of Chromatic Scales, a music shop located in the Lower City of the Gate.
