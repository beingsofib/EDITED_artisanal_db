Lost Empires

Lost Empires is a group of four novels each written by a different author. Publications were by Wizards of the Coast from 1998–2000.

The books are:

  1. The Lost Library of Cormanthyr by Mel Odom (Mar 1998)
  2. Faces of Deception by Troy Denning (Nov 1998)
  3. Star of Cursrah by Clayton Emery (Feb 1999)
  4. The Nether Scroll by Lynn Abbey (Sep 2000)
