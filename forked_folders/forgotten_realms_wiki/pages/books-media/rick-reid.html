Rick Reid

Rick Reid is a game designer who has contributed to TSR products.

Work

  • Swords of the Iron Legion, an adventure anthology in which he was credited for "The Hand of Tyr" (April 1988)
  • "New Rogues Gallery: Raven's Bluff Personalities", a multi-authored article in Polyhedron #50 (November 1989), detailing a number of personalities for the Living City setting. In it he was credited for the character of Spunk.
