Translations from Kara-Tur Haiku was a book written by Lhaeo of Shadowdale in 1354 DR. In three volumes, it comprised translations of haiku poetry from Kara-Tur.

A copy was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep around 1372 DR.
