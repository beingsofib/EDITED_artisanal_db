Mama Needs Her New Pair of Shu

Mama Needs Her New Pair of Shu is a tournament module set in Malatra – Living Jungle using the Dungeons & Dragons 2 edition ruleset. It is part of the Living Jungle series of adventures published for the RPGA Network, and the third in the Shu series, following If the Shu Fits and The Soulless Shu adventures. It is intended for 1 through 5 Level characters.

Summary

In the Year of Great Change (1373 DR), two children of the Simbuki shu tribe were abducted by the dreaded servants of the Tamara – black leopard katanga. The children, named after the spirits of the jungle, Ekana, Huko, were to be consumed by akathasa. A group of heroes embarked on the rescue journey across the Rayanna Savanna, meeting a nature spirit Yato and a small army of Malatran mud-men, before confronting the leader of the leopard katanga – chief Many-Teeth.
