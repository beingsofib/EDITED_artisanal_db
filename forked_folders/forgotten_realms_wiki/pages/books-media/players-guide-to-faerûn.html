Player’s Guide to Faerûn is an accessory for the Forgotten Realms campaign setting using the 3.5-edition ruleset.

Description

Player’s Guide to Faerûn updates many rules from the 3rd-edition Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting and provides additional and update spells, magic items, and prestige classes; expanded cosmology; psionics lore; adaptations for Book of Exalted Deeds and Book of Vile Darkness; and more.
