Dale Donovan

Dale A. Donovan is a freelance author, editor, and game designer.

He was an editor for Dragon magazine for over five years.

He worked as a game designer for TSR, Inc., Wizards of the Coast, and Hasbro for over five years before being let go in 2002.

Dale has been a gamer since 1981.
