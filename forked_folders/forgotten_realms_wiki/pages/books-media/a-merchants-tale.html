A Merchant's Tale was a book written by Jarn Tiir of Lantan in the late 13 century DR.

History

A Merchant's Tale was a collection of life lessons and musings published in the . The work shared some insights into the nature of those who lived to travel and the lesson that a human was more dangerous than any monster waiting in the wild.
