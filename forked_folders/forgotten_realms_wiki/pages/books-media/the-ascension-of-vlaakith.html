The Ascension of Vlaakith was a githyanki book that contained a narrative of the ascension of Vlaakith CLVII to the throne, as well as a discussion on the importance of arcane magic to githyanki society.

Description

The book was written in Gith. Its account of Vlaakith's rise to absolute power emphasized her reliance upon arcane magic, arguing that she would not have succeeded without it.

The book's discussion on arcane power stated that wizardry was fundamental to the githyanki's plans of defeating their enemies and eventually ruling the multiverse.
