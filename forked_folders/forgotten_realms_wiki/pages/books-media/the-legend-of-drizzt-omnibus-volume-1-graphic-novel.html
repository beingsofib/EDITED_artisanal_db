The Legend of Drizzt: Omnibus Volume 1 (graphic novel)

The Legend of Drizzt: Omnibus Volume 1 is the first omnibus of the The Legend of Drizzt Graphic Novels. It was published in 2007/2008 by and in 2011 by .

It is a collection of the first three volumes of the graphic novel series.
