Legends of Baldur's Gate is a series of Forgotten Realms comic books published by as part of the Tyranny of Dragons storyline. It is continued in the series Shadows of the Vampire, Frost Giant's Fury, Evil at Baldur's Gate and Infernal Tides.

Summary

After accidentally bringing the Rashemi berserker Minsc back to life, the wild mage Delina and her newfound companion find themselves fighting to stop a Cult of the Dragon plot against the city of Baldur's Gate.
