Quests from the Infinite Staircase is an adventure anthology for 5-edition Dungeons & Dragons published in July 2024.

Description

Quests from the Infinite Staircase updates and adapts six well-known adventurers from earlier editions of : The Lost City, When a Star Falls, Beyond the Crystal Cave, Pharaoh, The Lost Caverns of Tsojcanth, and Expedition to the Barrier Peaks, suiting player-characters of levels 1 to 13. These use the Infinite Staircase as a linking story.

As adaptations, these adventurers are not considered canon for this wiki, though Pharaoh was previously adapted to the Forgotten Realms in Desert of Desolation, which is considered to be canon.
