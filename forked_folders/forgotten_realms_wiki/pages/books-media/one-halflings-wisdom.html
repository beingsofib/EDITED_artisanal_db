One Halfling's Wisdom was a book published by a halfling author Harbunk Jhelliko and published in 1338 DR.

Contents

One Halfling's Wisdom was a collection of thoughts and musings on various topics from Harbunk Jhelliko's perspective, notably the halfling's thought on the price of fame and glory.
