The World Beneath Waterdeep was a compiled collection of journals and notes by the scholar Wently Kelso.

Description

This book detailed the expedition of Wently Kelso and his team beneath Waterdeep, from the sewers to the inner sanctum of the beholder crime-lord Xanathar. After returning, Kelso compiled his journals into book form, which was used to help confirm the existence of the evil doings of Xanathar.
