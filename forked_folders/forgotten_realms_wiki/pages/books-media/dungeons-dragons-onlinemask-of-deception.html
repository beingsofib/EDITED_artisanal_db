Dungeons & Dragons Online/Mask of Deception

Mask of Deception is a free quest in Dungeons & Dragons Online where the player characters are sent by the Harpers to infiltrate a Cult of the Dragon safehouse in the Stonelands that holds the white dragon mask in their possession. The quest was added on December 11, 2014 as part of Update 24, tying into the Tyranny of Dragons storyline.

In Update 70, the treasure rewards from another quest, "Memoirs of an Illusory Larcener", were merged into Mask of Deception.
