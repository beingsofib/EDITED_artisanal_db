Kate Welch

Kate Welch is a game designer who formerly worked on Dungeons & Dragons.

Career

Wizards of the Coast

Welch became an employee of Wizards of the Coast in February 2018. She worked as a game designer on Dungeons & Dragons, and co-authored her first sourcebook in 2019. She also appeared as a personality in many forms of media, including live-streams – both as herself in her "Welch's Game Juice" D&D video game streams, and as the character of Rosie Beestinger in the Acquisitions Incorporated "C" Team show. In December 2019, she moved to the role of senior user experience designer.

On August 14, 2020, Welch announced on Twitter that it was her last day at Wizards of the Coast.

Other work

Kate Welch is also known for being a former designer of the popular MMORPG Guild Wars 2, as well as participating in the development of various other games.
