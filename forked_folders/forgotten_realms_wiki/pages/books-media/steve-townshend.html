Steve Townshend

Steve Townshend is a former actor and game designer who has contributed freelance work to Wizards of the Coast.

Career

Townshend began submitting articles to Polyhedron and Dragon magazines as early as the mid-90s and was first published in Dragon #288. He later was hired for freelance work on Monster Manual III and has worked on many Wizard's sourcebooks since that.
