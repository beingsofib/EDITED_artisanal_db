The Way of the Gods was a two-volume book penned by Thaldeth Faerossdar in the .

Contents

The Way of the Gods was a collection of musings on the price of power. The author voiced his opinion that individuals fueled by blind ambitions were harmful to Faerûn.

The original copy of The Way of the Gods was kept in the library-citadel of Candlekeep in the late 14 century DR.
