The Malady of Elventree

The Malady of Elventree is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Rage of Demons series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Elventree, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 5- to 10-level characters.
