Unbroken Chain is a novel by Jaleigh Johnson set in the Forgotten Realms. The novel tells the story of Ashok, a wild shadar-kai of the Shadowfell who discovers Ikemmu, a city at the crossroads of the planes and the heart of his own struggle between life and oblivion.
