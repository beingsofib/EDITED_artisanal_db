The Trail Tales of the North was a book penned by Jhalivar Thrunn in the .

Contents

Jhalivar Thrunn's book was a collection of tales and musings from North Faerûn. One of the points made was the idea that wizards were lonely creatures whose life was filled with competition and either ended in early death or in endless undeath.
