Strike the dragon with the sponge

Strike the dragon with the sponge was a game played in Cormyr where blindfolded participants attempted to hit an image of a dragon with a sponge. The game was popular in the Baths of Arabel, where it was often played by groups of adventurers or furloughed Purple Dragons, often scantily clad, naked, or wearing only the Baths' customary masks. Elminster described the game as being similar to the game of played by the people of Earth.
