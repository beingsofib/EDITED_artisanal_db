Come the Lion

Come the Lion is an adventure module set in Malatra – Living Jungle using the Dungeons & Dragons 3-edition ruleset. It is part of the Living Jungle series of adventures published for the RPGA Network. It is an adventure designed for 6- to 12-level characters.
