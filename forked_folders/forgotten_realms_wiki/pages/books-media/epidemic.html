Epidemic is an adventure module from the RPGA Legacy of the Green Regent campaign.

Plot

All over Loudwater, orcs are exploding! The cause is a disease that is spreading quickly through the city's populace. There isn't enough clerical magic to go around and an epidemic will soon strike if a cure is not found quickly.
