Monstrous Compendium Ravenloft Appendix III: Creatures of Darkness is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for Ravenloft.

''Something moves in the darkness. Your heart pounds. Death is upon you before you know it.
Inside this book are dozens of sinister beings waiting to strike terror into the hearts of role-players everywhere. From the dreaded scarab beetles of Har'Akir to the archer skeletons of darkon and the drow vampires of Arak, these Creatures of Darkness will provide hours of diabolical adventure. But be warned - if you buy this accessory, your AD&D® game players may never forgive you.

Description

Monstrous Compendium Ravenloft Appendix III: Creatures of Darkness is the third installment in a series of Monstrous Compendium volumes dedicated to Ravenloft.

Adhering to the modular design of the Monstrous Compendium series, it provides detailed entries for each monster, including descriptions of their appearance, behavior, and abilities, as well as their roles within Ravenloft. In addition to providing stat blocks and combat information, the appendix explores the lore and background of its creatures, offering insights into their origins, motivations, and potential plot hooks.
