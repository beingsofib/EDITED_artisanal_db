Master of Chains is a Forgotten Realms novel by Jess Lebow and the first book in The Fighters series, which focuses on heroes of the Fighter class and different combat styles. As such, it is likely inspired by the Master of Chains prestige class in Sword and Fist and the spiked chain.

Synopsis

The novel is set in Erlkazar and follows the brothers Liam and Ryder of the Crimson Awl rebels as they oppose the vampire Shyressa of the Twisted Rune and Lord Purdun of Ahlarkhem.
