Windsong of the High Ice

BookIU
nameWindsong of the High Ice
typeMusical composition
locationFaerûn

The Windsong of the High Ice was the longest musical composition in recorded history. It was an improvised humming melody created by wraith-like wind spirits that chased each other through wind-sculpted passages and holes in high icy peaks.

Rumors & Legends

According to the Church of Chauntea, the beings responsible for this music had been serving their goddess for eons.
