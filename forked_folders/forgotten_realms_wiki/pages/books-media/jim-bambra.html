Jim Bambra

Jim Bambra is a British game designer, writer, and TTRPG reviewer who did work for TSR, Inc's AD&D and game lines, primarily through its subsidiary TSR Hobbies UK Ltd, and wrote a number of reviews for Dragon's Role-playing Reviews column. He has also done design work for other games, such as ' and ', and was formerly Head of Design at video game company .
