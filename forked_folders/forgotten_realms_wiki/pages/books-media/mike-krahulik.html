Mike Krahulik

Michael Krahulik is an American artist and streamer. His games are the origin of Acquisitions Incorporated and Jim Darkmagic (his player character). He was the cover artist and a writer for the Acquisitions Incorporated sourcebook and adventure.

He is primarily the artist of the webcomic Penny Arcade, written with Jerry Holkins. Together with Holkins, Krahulik created the Penny Arcade Expo beginning in 2004, a series of gaming festivals held in Seattle, Boston, Australia, Philadelphia, and San Antonio. He is also a co-founder of Child's Play, a charity which organizes toy drives for children's hospitals. Along with Holkins, Krahulik was included on the 2010 Time 100 for their work on Penny Arcade.
