Tom Wham

Tom Wham is a game designer and illustrator has contributed to Dungeons & Dragons and the Forgotten Realms. He is notable for his light-hearted and cartoony style.

Trivia

  • The Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars are named after Tom Wham.

Works

As Game Designer:

  • Mertwig's Maze
  • The Great Khan Game (with Richard Hamblen)

Solely as illustrator:

  • Monster Manual 1st edition

Outside of the Forgotten Realms, various other board games designed by Tom Wham appeared in issues of Dragon Magazine.
