The Saturday Morning Adventures comics adaptation of Dungeons & Dragons is a four-issue series published by , part of their Saturday Morning Adventures line which also includes Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles and G.I. Joe. The miniseries is written by David M. Booher and Sam Maggs, with art by George Kambadais. It follows the characters of the 1983–1985 Dungeons & Dragons cartoon series.
