Behind the scenes

While Keep on the Shadowfell was designed initially for the "Points of Light" core setting for 4th edition D&D, alterations to the adventure made in an article in Dungeon magazine #155 made it possible to use it as a dungeon for characters in the Forgotten Realms. These changes included changing the focus on Orcus to that of Shar and relocating Winterhaven to a geographical location in Faerûn between Cormyr and Sembia.

In April 2009, the adventure was released online at Wizards of the Coast's official website for download.
