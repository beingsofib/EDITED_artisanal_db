Pillar of the Community

Pillar of the Community is an adventure module set in the Living City organized play program penned by John Terra, part IV of The Menagerie of a Raven Lunatic. It was published in 1993 for the 2 edition, and was later republished in 1998 as part of the Adventurer's Guild program.
