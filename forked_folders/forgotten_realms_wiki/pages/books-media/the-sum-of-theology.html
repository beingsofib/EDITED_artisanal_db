The Sum of Theology was a book written by the Oghman servitor, Saint Vetheera. As of the late 15 century DR, a copy of the book was available within the library of Candlekeep.

Notable Owners

The ogre scholar Little One was in possession of this tome for some time, studying its pages within the walls of the Hearth.
