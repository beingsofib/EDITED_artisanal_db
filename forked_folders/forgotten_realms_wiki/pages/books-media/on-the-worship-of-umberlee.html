On the Worship of Umberlee was a book that collected information on the practices of the Church of Umberlee. It was popular among pirates of the Sea of Swords and Nelanther Islands circa 1372 DR.

Contents

The first part of the book covered the malicious Stormgoddess of the Seas herself, her inconsistent and petty personality, and her love of flattery and power. The second part described the worshiping traditions, tithes, and donations to the Church of Umberlee and her shrines. The third part talked about the Church in general, it being disorganized and prone to violence, and the Umberlant priests often selling their services to ships. And lastly the fourth part described the clerics' and worshipers' holy festivals and customs.

History

This book about the worshipers of the Bitch Queen was often found in homes and establishments frequented by pirates of the Sea of Swords. It was often sold by vendors of Spindrift Town and Neverwinter's harbor circa 1372 DR.
