Dragonspawn (comic)

Dragonspawn was the sixth issue of the Forgotten Realms comics series published by . It was written by Jeff Grubb, drawn by Rags Morales, and edited by Elliot S. Maggin. It is the second installment in the "The Dragonreach Saga" story arc.
