Across the Peaks of Icewind Dale, Strode Fast the Sage of Shadowdale was a popular ballad about the Sage of Shadowdale, Elminster, with its lyrics penned by Volothamp Geddarm sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.

Contents

According to Elminster, the ballad was loosely based on the two weeks he spent in the frigid Icewind Dale, surviving annoyances of "frozen beard" and frostbite. The Old Sage was frustrated over Volo's lighthearted retelling of the tale, making it sound as casual as a walk across the Dalelands.
