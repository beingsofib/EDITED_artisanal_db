Angel McCoy

Angel Leigh McCoy is a writer and game designer.

Works

Angel was part of the Dungeons & Dragons design team for Wizards of the Coast from 2000 to 2001. Here, she worked on material for 3 edition content, particularly in the Forgotten Realms campaign setting. McCoy specifically worked on Magic of Faerûn and articles for Dragon magazine.
