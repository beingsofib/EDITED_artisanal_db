Beneath the City of the Dead (module)

Beneath the City of the Dead is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Waterdeep series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Waterdeep, and is an adventure designed for 1- to 4-level characters. It is part two of the Umbral Aristocracy Trilogy.
