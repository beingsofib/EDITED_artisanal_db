"The Topaz Dragon" is a short story by Jess Lebow included in the 2004 anthology Realms of the Dragons.

Summary

An egg is stolen from the lair of the topaz dragon Kraxx. Suspecting a nearby dracolich is the culprit, but knows that entering its lair is risky. At the same time, the Expatriate, a ship laden with adventurers also seeking to steal Kraxx's egg, is approaching the topaz dragon's island. Realizing their plan, Kraxx forces the adventurers into submission and bids them return her egg from the dracolich's lair.
