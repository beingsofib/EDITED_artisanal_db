Jeff Butler

Jeff Butler is an artist and illustrator. He has contributed artwork to 1 and 2 edition Dungeons & Dragons material. Butler drew the cover art for The Shining South (1993). He used pencil and ink to sketch and color humanoids and part-human creatures in the ''Monstrous Manual.
