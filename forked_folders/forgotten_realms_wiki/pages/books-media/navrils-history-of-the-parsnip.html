Navril's History of the Parsnip was a common book dedicated to the history of parsnips, a noble root vegetable, penned by Navril sometime before the late 14 century DR.

History

By the , the fortress library of Candlekeep had several copies of Navril's History of the Parsnip and many visitors continued bringing more copies as their entrance gifts. However, the library's monks always rejected "old Navril" in favor of some regional collections of songs and minstrels' sayings.
