Van Richten's Monster Hunter's Compendium, Volume One is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for Ravenloft launched as part of D&D's 25th anniversary.

Description

The book compiles and updates the complete texts of Van Richten's Guide to Vampires, Van Richten's Guide to Werebeasts, and Van Richten's Guide to the Created.

Contents

The book is divided into three parts, each of which updates the text of a particular previous sourcebook.
