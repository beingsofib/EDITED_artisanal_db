The Empires trilogy

The Empires Trilogy is a group of novels each written by a different author. Publications are by TSR, Inc. from 1990-1991. Beginning in the Endless Wastes and reaching to the eastern shores of Kara-Tur only to travel back to Faerûn.

The books are:

  1. Horselords by David Cook (Apr 1990)
  2. Dragonwall by Troy Denning (Jul 1990)
  3. Crusade by James Lowder (Jan 1991)
