Treatise Historical of the Dragon Tyrants

The Treatise Historical of the Dragon Tyrants was an ancient text penned by Reverent Dreamer Rhistel Laelithar. It was recovered from the ruins of Myth Drannor in 1374 DR.

The book discussed the history of the Time of Dragons, including the creation of the kobold, urd, and gnome races, and the origin of the deities Kurtulmak and Kuraulyek.
