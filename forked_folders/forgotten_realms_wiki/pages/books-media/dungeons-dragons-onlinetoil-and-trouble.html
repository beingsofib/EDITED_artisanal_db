Dungeons & Dragons Online/Toil and Trouble

Toil and Trouble is a paid quest for Dungeons & Dragons Online where the player characters investigate the King's Forest and make a deal with a hag. The adventure was added to the game on April 16, 2025 as part of Update 73's Tavern Tales quest bundle.

Plot

In the aftermath of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar and Lolth's schemes, the people of Eveningstar are troubled by yet another menace. Mielikki's Grove in the King's Forest is overrun by blights, and Jarra Morand suspects a hag is to blame. Finding Granny Gertrude nearby, the hag actually proclaims her innocence rather than reveling in the disaster, and blames a nasty Red Wizard of Thay who has sacrificed a blessed unicorn and despoiled the shrine. Gertrude in fact is a deal-maker and has a working relationship with Mielikki, and would be happy to deal with Dhovras if not for her agreement preventing her from harming the inhabitants of the grove—which includes the accursed undead unicorn now defending Dhovras.

In order to resolve the situation, Granny Gertrude hires the players to cleanse Mielikki's shrine and save the day, all with the approval of the goddess herself. Once the unfortunate unicorn is put to rest Dhovras is left vulnerable to the peeved Gertrude, who captures the Red Wizard and drags him off for some hag-style justice.
