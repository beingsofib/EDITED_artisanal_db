101 Garlic Recipes was a cookbook by a garlic-obsessed wizard Rab Bitnez in Faerûn.

Contents

The book contained one hundred and one recipes of a wide variety of dishes creatively made using its star ingredient—garlic. The recipes included The Bitnez Pavlova Extraordinaire!, Rab's Riotously Intense Ice Cream, and Garlic Guy Pie.
