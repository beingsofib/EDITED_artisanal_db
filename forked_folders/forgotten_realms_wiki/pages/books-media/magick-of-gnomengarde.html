Magick of Gnomengarde was a spellbook that was studied and shared by all gnomes of Gnomengarde as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

The leather-bound spellbook displayed the title "Magick of Gnomengarde" in both Gnomish and Common. It was stored on a wooden pedestal atop a ledge overlooking the workshop in which Gnomengarde's inventors crafted magic items.

Powers

Magick of Gnomengarde contained the following spells:

burning hands • detect magic • identify • mage armor • magic missile • shield • sleep
