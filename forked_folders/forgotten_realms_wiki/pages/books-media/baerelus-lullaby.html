Baerelus' Lullaby was a bardic song performed by the satyrs of Cormyr.

Content

When performed, the song relaxed the listener's body and potentially put them into a state of magical sleep, called Baerelus' Dreams.

History

The tune was named after the famed satyr Baerelus, and came to be performed by the satyrs of Cormyr in the 15 century DR, who lived in the King's Forest and Storm Horns.
