Regé-Jean Page

Regé-Jean Page is an English actor who portrayed Xenk Yendar, a paladin, in the Dungeons and Dragons film Honor Among Thieves.

Career

In the fantasy and science-fiction genre, he appeared in and an audio version of , and previously in Gimme 6 and Space Ark.

}} In an interview with Variety in May, 2021, Page explained he wasn't a D&D player but said "I've listened to a couple of Dungeons and Dragons podcasts in my time. I played a ton of JRPGs and basically everything that's come out of it. I played a ton of Diablo as a teenager, so I'm used to the fact that I play a paladin—that's just what I do, and I know what that means, to a degree. I watched my friends play Baldur's Gate, so I'm like a second-generation 'Dungeons and Dragoner'." He recruited friends to teach him about D&D, adding "There is nowhere better or bigger to learn new worlds from than D&D." He said he was attracted by the Dungeons & Dragons movie script, observing it had stepped up and raised the bar for fantasy movies and would be a "huge sigh of relief for Dungeons & Dragons fans everywhere." He described the part with "It's a brilliant job. I'm literally paying my mortgage by fighting imaginary dragons." Director Jonathan Goldstein mentioned Page's role "allows him to show a full spectrum of his talent" and "He naturally exudes a sense of dignity and heroism that is fitting for the fantasy genre, and is perfect for the role we cast him in."{{cite web
