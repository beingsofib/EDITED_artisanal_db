Steve Mitchell

Steve Mitchell is an artist who has worked on comics since 1972. Mitchell has worked for both DC and Marvel. Steve Mitchell, along with Steve Kurth, provided inks for the promotional comic, The Grand Tour.

Works

Mitchell contributed art (inks) to:
