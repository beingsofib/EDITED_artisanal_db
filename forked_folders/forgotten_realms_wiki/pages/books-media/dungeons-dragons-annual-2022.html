Dungeons & Dragons Annual 2022

Dungeons & Dragons Annual 2022 is a comic published by in August, 2022, set largely in the Feywild, as a tie-in with The Wild Beyond the Witchlight adventure. It comprised two stories, Lost & Found and Something for Everyone, written by Jim Zub and Ryan Cady, respectively.

Summary

In Lost & Found, a young adventurer joins an unlikely companion on a journey to the Witchlight Carnival and the Feywild beyond, in search for missing memories and lost family.

Something For Everyone sees two star-crossed lovers—a pixie and a sprite on opposite sides of the law—fight for their love despite the odds stacked against them.
