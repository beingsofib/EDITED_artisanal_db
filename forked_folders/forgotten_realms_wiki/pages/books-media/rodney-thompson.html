Rodney Thompson

Rodney Thompson is an writer and game designer. He has contributed several Dungeons & Dragons sourcebooks, as well as numerous books for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game. He was the lead developer for the 4th edition version of the Dark Sun campaign setting.
