The Radiant Dragon by Elaine Cunningham is book four in the Cloakmaster Cycle, a six-book series for the Spelljammer campaign setting. It takes place in Realmspace—including the planet Garden and the island of Evermeet—and Winterspace.

Synopsis

Teldin Moore has stopped on Garden in Realmspace to resupply. While at a tavern, a bar fight erupts. Teldin escapes the chaos and finds himself in the company of an aperusa man and two young dracons.
