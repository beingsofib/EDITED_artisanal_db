The Healing Power of Prayer: Curative Rituals of the House of Ilmater was a medicinally oriented religious text centered around various Ilmatari ritual practices.

Description

Copies of this book were bound in leather of an ivory hue.

Contents

Among the contents of the book was a chapter entitled "Lycanthropy and Other Magical Diseases and Curses".

Notable Owners

  • Father Steif, an Ilmatari priest in the 14 century DR, owned a copy of this book and was using it to help him in his experimental effort to cure himself of the disease of lycanthropy solely through prayer.
