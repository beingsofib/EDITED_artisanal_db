Description

Races of Destiny focuses on humans, half-elves, half-orcs, and illumians. Many of the resources in Races of Destiny deal specifically with urban settings and nearly all have racial requirements. It also includes seven new prestige classes, racial substitution levels for half-elves and half-orcs and sample NPCs and encounters. Races of Destiny has dozens of new spells, psionic powers, and feats and racial information for ten near-human or altered human species, including aasimar, tieflings, half-ogres, mongrelfolk and more.
