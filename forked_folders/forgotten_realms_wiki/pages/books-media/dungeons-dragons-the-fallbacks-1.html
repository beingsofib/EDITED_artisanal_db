Dungeons & Dragons: The Fallbacks Series 1 Issue #1 is the first of a series of four comics from published on October 15, 2025. It is written by Greg Pak and illustrated by Wilton Santos and Raul Angulo. Set in the Forgotten Realms, it features characters from the novel The Fallbacks: Bound for Ruin.
