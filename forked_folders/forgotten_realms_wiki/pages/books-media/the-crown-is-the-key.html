"The Crown is the Key" was a treatise on the Crown of Neverwinter found in Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.

Discussing the magical nature of the Crown of Neverwinter, it discussed how only a true heir of Nasher Alagondar could wear it, and argued that the crown was somehow a magical key.

A copy was in the library of Bradda's Sage's Shop in Neverwinter in 1479 DR.
