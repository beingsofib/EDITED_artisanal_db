Schools of Magic: Abjuration was a book dedicated to the basics of the abjuration school of wizardry, published sometime before the late 15 century DR.

Content

The book outlined several key terms associated with this school of magic, including "banish," "prevent," "safeguard," and "stop short." Its most common magical concepts included wards, counterspells, dispellations of magic, and magical resistances.

History

In the , several copies of Schools of Magic: Abjuration could be found around the city of Baldur's Gate and were stored and sold by the Sorcerous Sundries magic shop in the city of Baldur's Gate.
