Lands of Intrigue is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons boxed set for the Forgotten Realms.

Description

Lands of Intrigue specifically focuses on the Tethyr, Amn, and Erlkazar regions, exploring the political machinations, secret alliances, and covert operations of the region.

Instead of focusing solely on dungeon crawls and monster battles, Lands of Intrigue encourages players to engage in political maneuvering, establish and betray alliances, and navigate the complex social hierarchies of the game's setting.

Additionally, Lands of Intrigue includes detailed maps of the regions covered in the set.

Errata

  • Book 1 of the boxed set names the dragon-in-disguise Nyadnar as Zaranda Star's tutor, but the novel War in Tethyr makes it clear that Nyadnar was not her tutor but her employer, the one who hired her to steal the brazen head from Thay. Zaranda's tutor is said have been Alshayn on p. 135 of the novel.

Trivia

  • The cover art for "Book 1: Tethyr" is reused from the novel War in Tethyr and is by Walter Velez, not Todd Lockwood.
