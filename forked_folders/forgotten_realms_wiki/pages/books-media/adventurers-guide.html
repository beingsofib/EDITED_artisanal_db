The Adventurer's Guide was a multi-author book that shared its many insights on the trade of adventuring with the readers.

Description

The Adventurer's Guide was an old tome that showed its age through wear and tear. The book had mismatched dogeared and dirt-stained pages. Most of the book was hand-written by many contributors over the years, evident by a variety of penmanship inside.

History

In the , the Adventurer's Guide traveled aboard the Vigilant from the Sword Coast North to the nation of Samarach on the Chultan Peninsula, alongside the infamous bard and author – Volothamp Geddarm.
