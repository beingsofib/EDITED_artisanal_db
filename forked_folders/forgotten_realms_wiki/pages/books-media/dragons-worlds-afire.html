Dragons: Worlds Afire is an anthology first published by Wizards of the Coast in 2006. Its four stories are based around the theme of dragons and each is set in a different campaign setting: "If Ever They Happened Upon My Lair" by R.A. Salvatore in the Forgotten Realms, "Here Be Dragons!" by Margaret Weis and Tracy Hickman in Dragonlance, "Principles of Fire" by Keith Baker in Eberron, and "Unnatural Predator" by Scott McGough in Magic: The Gathering. Of these, "If Ever They Happened Upon My Lair" was reprinted in The Collected Stories: The Legend of Drizzt Anthology.
