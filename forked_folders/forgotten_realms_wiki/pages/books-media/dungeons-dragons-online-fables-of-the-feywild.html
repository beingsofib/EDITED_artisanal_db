Dungeons and Dragons Online: Fables of the Feywild is an expansion pack for Dungeons & Dragons Online released in November 2020 alongside Update 48. The expansion is set in an area of the Feywild, and primarily deals with the archfey Hyrsam and the consequences of his actions.
