Vance Kovacs

Vance Kovacs is a concept artist, illustrator and designer working in the film and game industry. Formerly, he worked for Black Isle Studios. He worked with Wizards of the Coast, , , , , , , , , , and many more.

History

Vance worked for Interplay as a concept artist and a lead artist in 1995–2003.

In January 2005, Vance Kovacs, Justin Sweet, John Dickenson, and Marshall Vandruff established the Carbon Canyon Studios.
