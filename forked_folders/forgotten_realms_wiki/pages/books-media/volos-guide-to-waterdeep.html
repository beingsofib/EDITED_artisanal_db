Volo's Guide to Waterdeep is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms.

Description

Volo's Guide to Waterdeep is one in a series of guidebooks written by famed explorer and raconteur Volothamp Geddarm (under the editorial control of Elminster of Shadowdale).
