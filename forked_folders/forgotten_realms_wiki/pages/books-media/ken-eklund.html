Ken Eklund

Ken Eklund is a prolific video game designer, author, writer, and programmer. He was highly involved in the production of some video games in the Forgotten Realms and other related settings such as Al-Qadim. Eklund also made large contributions to video games of other Dungeons & Dragons settings, such as Dark Sun.

Works

Eklund's works in the Forgotten Realms and related settings include:

  • Pools of Darkness (Encounter author)
  • Al-Qadim: The Genie's Curse (Writer and programmer)
  • Pool of Radiance: Ruins of Myth Drannor (Designer, co-writer, story & dialogue)
