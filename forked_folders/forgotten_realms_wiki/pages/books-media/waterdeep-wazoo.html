The Waterdeep Wazoo was a broadsheet periodical newspaper published in Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.

Description

The newspaper was published by Gaxly Rudderbust,

Gaxly authored some of the newspaper's articles himself, but the paper also accepted editorials from freelance contributors.

History

In 1492 DR, Jarlaxle anonymously submitted to the paper an exposé on the rising devil worship cults among Waterdhavian noble families. That same year, an article written by Gaxly was also published, speculating on the origins of the Black Viper and linking her to the Cassalanter family.

Reputation

The Wazoo's news were followed by noble Waterdhavian families and various organizations, such as the Order of the Gauntlet.
