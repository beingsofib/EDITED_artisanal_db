Saturday Morning Adventures: Endless Summer

Saturday Morning Adventures: "Endless Summer" is a comic published by in August, 2023. It is written by David M. Booher, with art by Jack Lawrence. This comic serves as an Annual, part of IDW's Endless Summer Annuals in several titles, and follows the characters of the 1983–1985 Dungeons & Dragons cartoon series.
