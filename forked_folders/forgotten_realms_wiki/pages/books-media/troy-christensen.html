Troy Christensen

Troy Christensen is an author and designer of Dungeons & Dragons material.

Works

Christensen contributed to 2 edition accessories of the DMGR series, such as the Arms and Equipment Guide and The Castle Guide. In particular, he and Jon Pickens designed the equipment and mount equipment for the Arms and Equipment Guide.
