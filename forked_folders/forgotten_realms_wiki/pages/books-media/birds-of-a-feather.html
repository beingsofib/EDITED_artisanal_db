Birds of a Feather

Birds of a Feather is an adventure in the Living City organized play campaign and the third adventure in the The Menagerie of a Raven Lunatic series. It was published in 1993 for the 2 edition, and was later republished in 1998 as part of the Adventurer's Guild program.
