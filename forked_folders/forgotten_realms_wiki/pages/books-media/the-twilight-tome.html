The Twilight Tome was a periodical chapbook published in the city of Berdusk of the Sunset Vale.

Description

By the late-15 century DR, The Twilight Tome was one of the longest-running and famous publications in Berdusk. The chapbook was filled with satirical poems, witty commentaries, song lyrics, and works of short fiction.
