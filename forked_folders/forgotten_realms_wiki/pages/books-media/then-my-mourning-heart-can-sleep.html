Then My Mourning Heart Can Sleep was a mournful ballad performed by a minstrel duo named Shymon and Scarbunkel in many taverns and inns across northwest Faerûn in the late 14 century DR.

Contents

The song was performed in gentle harmony, with the second voice occasionally slipping into echoing a line and the two voices switching leads.

History

Some time around and after the , Shymon and Scarbunkel, a pair of handsome young minstrels could be heard performing their songs, including Then My Mourning Heart Can Sleep across the Sword Coast North, including the Wandering Woodsman's Tavern in Conyberry, the Danley and Sons Inn in Phandalin, the Dusty Gnome Tavern in Leilon, the New Leaf Tavern in the hamlet of New Leaf, the Phoenix Tail Inn in the Crossroad Keep, the Seaspray Inn in Highcliff, and the The Hammer and Shield Inn in Thundertree.
