Triglio (shanty)

BookIU
nameTriglio
typeSea shanty
dateBefore 1485 DR
locationTargos, Icewind Dale

Triglio was a sea shanty known to the fishermen of Targos in Icewind Dale in the late 15 century DR. They sung it regularly while working on Maer Dualdon. The general store Triglio in Targos was named for the song.
