Daniel S. Donnelly

Daniel S. Donnelly was a member of the Role Playing Game Association (RPGA) who designed several modules for its organized play programs Adventurer's Guild, Living City, and Living Jungle. He also served as the answerer for The Trumpeter's FAQ section.
