Spell Compendium is an accessory for the 3.5-edition of the ruleset. It presents many spells and divine domains.

Description

Spell Compendium compiles spells from a variety of other books and updates them to use the 3.5-edition ruleset. Spell lists are included for all spellcasting classes in the Player's Handbook and Dungeon Master's Guide, along with a selection of cleric domains.

Sources include Draconomicon: The Book of Dragons, Manual of the Planes, and Savage Species, along with numerous issues of Dragon and articles originally found on the Wizards of the Coast website.

Spell Compendium was reproduced as a premium reprint featuring a new cover and including errata, released on April 16, 2013.
