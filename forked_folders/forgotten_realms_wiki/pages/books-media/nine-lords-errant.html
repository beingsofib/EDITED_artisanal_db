Nine Lords Errant was a play written by Narelra Manecandle of Athkatla in the late 14 century DR.

History

Narelra Manecandle, Playwright of Athkatla, penned the Nine Lords Errant play sometime before the . The play's first performance was held in 1380 DR. The play's protagonist was Amlaric Amend-All, who, along with several coconspirators, plotted a murder most foul.
