The Wonders of Magery, or Of the Wonders of Magery, was a book by Albaerum of Neverwinter. The book touched on the mission of the Magister. It comprised four volumes, and had been published circa .

History

A copy of The Wonders of Magery was kept in the library-citadel of Candlekeep in the late 14 century DR.
