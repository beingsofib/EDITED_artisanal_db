I Harp As I See It was a book written by the Harper Abranthar "Twoquills" Foraeren. It was published in 1365 DR.

History

As of 1372 DR, a copy could be found in the Leaves of Learning in Highmoon and the original rested in the shelves of Candlekeep.

I Harp As I See It referred to one of the Seven Sisters, Qilué Veladorn.
