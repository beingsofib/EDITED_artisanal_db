"The Unicorn's Blessed Crescent" was a musical epic composed by Isla Donrig in Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the early 1370s DR.

In it, she made reference to the ruins of Phantoms' Cloister, calling it "Ol' Fallen Man's monastery" and including the lines "it shifts from its prison, and into sight, luring others into its ancient plight."

Isla performed the epic in Loudwater sometime before she departed the city in Eleasis of the .
