Planescape: Torment is a remaster of the 1999 computer role-playing game Planescape: Torment, developed and published by in 2017. This re-release focuses on quality-of-life updates and compatibility with newer gaming systems.

New features

The Enhanced Edition brought a number of improvements and features to the game:

  • Updated interface
  • Wide-screen & 4K resolution support
  • Improved renderer
  • Numerous bug fixes
  • Quality-of-life updates (such as quickloot and item highlighting)

Unlike the other Infinity Engine remasters however, there has been little to no gameplay content added (such as new companions or quests), with the release focusing on improving the technical side of the game.
