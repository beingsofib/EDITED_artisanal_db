Mark Anthony

Mark Anthony (born 1966) is an American fantasy author known beyond the Realms for his The Last Rune series. His first novel was Crypt of the Shadowking in The Harpers series. His works include novels for the Forgotten Realms, the Dragonlance and the Ravenloft settings, as well as several short stories.
