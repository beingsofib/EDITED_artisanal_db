Sailing Back Home to Love was a famous ballad dedicated to a renowned courtesan Dayatharra of Luskan, penned by Breldur Arskitarr of Neverwinter befroe the 14 century DR.

Contents

Sailing Back Home to Love told the story of Dayatharra, a woman from the northern pirate city of Luskan who provided many sea captains of her time with companionship and sexual services. Dayatharra was immortalized in several other ballads.
