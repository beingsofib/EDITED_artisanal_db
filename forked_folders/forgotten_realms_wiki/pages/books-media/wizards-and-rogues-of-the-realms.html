Wizards and Rogues of the Realms is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms.

Description

Wizards and Rogues of the Realms is the 9 book in the FOR series.

It features character class kits for wizards and rogues of the Realms. The book also introduces two new character classes: spellsingers and shadow walkers. Each kit includes an overview of home area for the character and describes the clothing native to each area, guidelines for roleplaying, and special abilities and disadvantages for the kit, as well as statistics for racial and ability requirements, and also proficiencies.
