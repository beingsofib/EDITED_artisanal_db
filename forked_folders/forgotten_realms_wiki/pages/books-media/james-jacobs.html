James Jacobs

James Jacobs was the editor-in-chief of Dungeon magazine when it was published by Paizo Publishing. He has authored and co-authored several sourcebooks with fellow Paizo editor Erik Mona.

Works

Dragon magazine

  • "Monsters in the Alley," Dragon magazine 307 (May 2003)
  • "Sin Eaters of Eilistraee," Dragon magazine 315 (January 2004)
