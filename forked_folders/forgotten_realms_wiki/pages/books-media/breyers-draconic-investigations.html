Breyer's Draconic Investigations was a lore book that detailed arcane secrets about dragons and dragonkind. At least one copy remained in the possession of the renewed Cult of the Dragon of the 15 century DR.

Description

Full comprehension and command over the magical specifications detailed in Draconic Investigations required exceptional attention to detail and studiousness.

History

Circa the 1480s DR, at least one copy of the book remained in the possession of senior most agents of the Cult of the Dragon. It was brought to the lair of the red dragon Lostmauth on the isle of Tuern.
