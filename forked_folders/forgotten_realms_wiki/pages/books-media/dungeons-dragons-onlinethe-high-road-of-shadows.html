Dungeons & Dragons Online/The High Road of Shadows

The High Road of Shadows is an adventure pack for Dungeons & Dragons Online where the player characters walk the High Road of Cormyr, hunting for the pieces of a Nether Scroll. The adventure pack was added to the game on November 12th, 2012 as part of Update 16, and connects the two main stories of Shadowfell Conspiracy.

Content

Netherese Legacy

The Netherese Legacy quest chain takes place after the Shadow Over Wheloon quest chain of Shadowfell Conspiracy, in the wake of Oriphaun Huntsilver's escape from Wheloon Prison with the Nether Scroll. A team of Harpers arrive in Eveningstar to follow his trail and find the pieces of the Nether Scroll that he has hidden along the High Road west of town, and the Shadovar are also hot on the trail of their artifact. While the Purple Dragons and War Wizards are busy with the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, opportunistic bandits led by Theodas Riveron and werewolves from the King's Forest take advantage of the situation to run rampant. The leader of the Harpers, Rina Belostos, is responsible for giving the quest chain. In addition to the quests, the High Road is littered with Oriphaun's journal entries, describing his thoughts and experiences as he evades the Shadovar and hides the Scroll pieces.

Detour from Tibalt Dormen sends the players to meet Bensen Virandson at Greenweald Lane. They must escort him and his piece of the Nether Scroll past a small army of hired mercenaries (including the double-agent Nil) and reach the Harper Lodge for safe storage. The leader of the mercenaries, Dom, beats the Harpers there but is overcome.

In Rest Stop, Freda Gambosi sends the players to Beckside Castle, where Oriphaun has hidden another piece. The ruins are inhabited by a number of adventurers using it as a camping spot, unaware of the Netherese agents elsewhere in the structure. After finding the Netherese forces, the players overcome the arcanists Keldan Shadeskein and Gaston Wyrst, taking the Scroll for the Harpers.

Lost in the Swamp takes the players from Eradin Krall to the wisp-haunted Lost Souls' Swamp where the hermit Seamus lives, only to find the medusa Sshaastess has already taken over the swamp and seized the Nether Scroll piece, dominating elementals and corrupting the local dryads. After fighting through the creatures defending Sshaastess, the medusa is slain and the Scroll recovered. The players may also help the titular lost souls of the swamp—Callie Brennan, Evrim Whitehair, and Kyle Briarbuck, who were trapped by a powerful shadow.

In A Stay at the Inn, Kordo Sweetspire sends players to the Old Man's Face inn of Tyrluk. Oriphaun hid a piece there and although the Netherese have already taken over the inn, they are unable to find it. After eliminating the Netherese commander Tarys, the bartender William Windsphere reveals that the only thing Oriphaun left was a bar tab—actually the Nether Scroll, flipped over and scribbled on. Thus the players are able to recover that piece of the artifact.

The End of the Road has Olyn Araphyr dispatch players to meet Girmond Dyle, holder of the Harper's pieces, before heading to Granwell Keep in the Blooded Wood, where the Netherese have gathered up all the remaining pieces of the Nether Scroll. On arrival at the Keep, the players find Girmond dead and the Netherese in possession of all of the pieces. Before the players confront the arcanists, they accidentally cause the Scroll to open a portal to the Abyss, causing the glabrezu Razagnol and his dretches to invade. After a fierce battle, Rina Belostos and Elminster arrive to take custody of the Scroll and ensure that it doesn't fall into the wrong hands.
