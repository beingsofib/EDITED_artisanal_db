Dungeons & Dragons Online/A Study in Sable

A Study in Sable is a free quest in Dungeons & Dragons Online set in Cormyr where the player characters must use detective work to uncover the perpetrator of a brutal attack on Sir Ebermund Harker in Sable Manor. The quest was added on November 12, 2013 as part of Update 20.
