The Glory of the Dragon was a history book that recorded the glowing history of King Azoun Obarskyr IV of Cormyr.

Contents

The Glory of the Dragon was published sometime in the , postmortem, or sometime before the monarch 's death. The book's author was Danchas the Scribe. A copy of the book found home in the private library of Taeros Hawkwinter of Waterdeep, in the noble family, estate – the Hawkwinter House. The nobleman treasured the tales of grand adventures and dazzling heroes.
