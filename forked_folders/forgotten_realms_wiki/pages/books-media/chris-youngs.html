Chris Youngs

Chris Youngs was a magazine editor at Wizards of the Coast.

Career

Youngs first joined Wizards of the Coast in 1998 as an editing intern. He worked his way up in the company and joined the teams working on the Dragon and Dungeon magazines. Youngs was editor-in-chief for Dragon issues #312 through #315 and #360 through #388. In 2007, he joined the team. In 2018, he left the company.
