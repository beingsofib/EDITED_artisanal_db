Sarah W. Stocker

Sarah W. Stoker is a video game writer and designer. She was highly involved in story writing for a couple of the games in the Forgotten Realms campaign setting. Stoker was the lead writer for the final game in the Pool of Radiance series.

Works

Stocker's works in the Forgotten Realms include:

  • Pool of Radiance: Ruins of Myth Drannor (Lead writer, designer, story & dialogue)
  • Forgotten Realms: Demon Stone (Art manager, writer, producer)
