These Men of Zhentil Keep was a crude sea shanty popular among pirates and buccaneers of the Moonshae Isles in the late 14 century DR.

History

The song was brought to the southern wizardly nation of Halruaa by an infamous 14 century DR pirate named Old Bess who, in exchange for care and healing, taught navigation, tides, currents, and shared tales of fierce sea battles at the Jordaini College.
