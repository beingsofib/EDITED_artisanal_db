Darsson's Notes was the research tome of the famous Halruaan mage known as Darsson Spellmaker. It contained his personal notes on the history of magic, specifications of spells of his creation and research on magical items attributed to him as well.

Description

Darsson's book was tall, wide and about thick, bound in fine leather. It had 150 pages, about half of which were full of his notes, commentary and directions.

Contents

Darsson's Notes contained descriptions and the details of both the ring of magic detection and wand of portraiture.

It also contained information on the following spells:

Amplify • Distort • Know school • Darsson's cooling breeze (unique) • Darsson's fiery cube (unique) • Portrait • Safety net • Lessen gravity • Darsson's eye in the sky (unique) • Darsson's potion (unique) • Darsson's music box (unique) • Summon Darsson (unique) • Suspension
