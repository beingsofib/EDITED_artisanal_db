Baldur's Gate: Siege of Dragonspear is an expansion to Baldur's Gate: Enhanced Edition that takes place between Baldur's Gate and Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn.

Features

Siege of Dragonspear features new areas along the Sword Coast, more than 100 new items, four new NPCs, a new class (shaman), and new difficulty modes.
