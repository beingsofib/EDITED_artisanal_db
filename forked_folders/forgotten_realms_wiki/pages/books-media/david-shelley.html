David Shelley

David Shelley is a video game developer, designer, programmer, author, artist, and writer.

Works

Shelley worked for as a lead designer and producer, as well as other positions. During his time, he worked on the following Forgotten Realms games: Pool of Radiance, Curse of the Azure Bonds, Secret of the Silver Blades, and Pools of Darkness.
