In Sylvan Shadows is the second book of The Cleric Quintet by R.A. Salvatore.

Summary

Cadderly Bonaduce ventures into Shilmista at the bidding of the elven prince Elbereth to help the wild elves push back the invading force sent by Castle Trinity.
