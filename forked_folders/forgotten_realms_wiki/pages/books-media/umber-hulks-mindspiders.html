Umber Hulks & Mindspiders

Umber Hulks & Mindspiders was the second issue of the Spelljammer comics series and published by . It was written by Barbara Kesel, drawn by Michael Collins, and edited by Elliot S. Maggin. It is the second of four installments in the "Rogue Ship" story arc.
