James Wyatt

James Wyatt is an author of several sci-fi and fantasy novels. He has co-authored numerous Forgotten Realms sourcebooks.

  • Defenders of the Faith (2001)
  • City of the Spider Queen (2002)
  • Book of Exalted Deeds (2003)
  • Draconomicon: The Book of Dragons (2003)
  • Player's Guide to Faerûn (2004)
  • Cormyr: The Tearing of the Weave (2007)
  • Player's Handbook 4th edition (2008)
  • Monster Manual 4th edition (2008)
  • Player's Handbook 2 (2009)
  • Fizban's Treasury of Dragons (2021)
