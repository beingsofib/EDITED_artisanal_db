Hannibal King

Hannibal King is an artist and illustrator whose work has appeared in many TSR-era Dungeons & Dragons books and on the covers of Polyhedron Newszine. He has also illustrated cards for the Magic: The Gathering collectible card game, Wizards of the Coast's '''', books for White Wolf Publishing, and various mainstream and independent comic book publishers.
