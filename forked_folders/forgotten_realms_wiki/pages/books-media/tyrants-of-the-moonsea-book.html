Tyrants of the Moonsea was a book that recounted the events of 1373 DR in the citys of the Moonsea and the activities of the Hero of Daggerford after her departure from the said city and recounted the hero's struggle against the archwizard Maganus that culminated in Orcus laying waste on the city of Zhentil Keep.

Contents

Tyrants of the Moonsea was a book that recorded the very recent events at the time of publication, so recent that the author, by their own admission, was still tracking down records and eyewitness accounts of all the events that transpires that year across the Moonsea, but focusing on Zhentil Keep. The book stated that a vengeful archwizard, named Maganus, targeted Zhentil Keep and the entire region in a poignant attempt to destabilize the Moonsea, led by his youth's bitter memories and trauma. The archwizard created a cult led by a crazed dracolich – Order of the Crippled Fang, and formed a pact with Orcus himself to achieve that goal.

The book also recounts the best it can the hero who opposed Maganus. It stated that the archwizard's fate was unknown, but there were rumors of his survival and even some that claimed that he was searching for powerful Netherese artifact that gave him power to unleash the crisis on the region in the first place.
