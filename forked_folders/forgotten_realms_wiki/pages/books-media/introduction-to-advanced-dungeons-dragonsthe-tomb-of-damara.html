"The Tomb of Damara" is an adventure module for beginner players set in the Dalelands, where they must rescue an elflord and then retrieve an artifact. It was accompanied by a CD with recorded dialogue.

Synopsis

The players are called by the archmage Netheril to rescue the elflord Taran Goldenstar from the Tomb of Damara. After rescuing Taran, they are called on to defeat the ogre Bonegnasher and retrieve the orb of dragonkind.
