Adam Gillespie

Adam Gillespie, also known as Adam Danger Cook, is an illustrator who works with numerous clients including Wizards of the Coast. Adam's art is usually cover art as he works in full color.

His most recent work for Wizards was several images in A Reader's Guide to R. A. Salvatore's the Legend of Drizzt.
