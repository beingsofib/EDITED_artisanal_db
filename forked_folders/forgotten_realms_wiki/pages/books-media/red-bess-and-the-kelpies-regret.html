Red Bess and the Kelpie's Regret was a chapbook owned by the tiefling bounty hunter Havilar. It was about a kelpie.

Description

Havilar gave her copy to her twin sister Farideh, claiming the story reminded her of Farideh's relationship with Lorcan.
