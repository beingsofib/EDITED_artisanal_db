Raised of Spells an autobiographical song written by Alatelo De Bonito, a mage guard and a financial advisor from the city of Athkatla, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Raised of Spells written some time before the . Alatelo was an amateur singer and songwriter who attempted to perform his works at the inn named the Den of the Seven Vales, where he was employed by Patricia. Raised of Spells was among the laments Alatelo sang to little enthusiasm from the clientele, apart from an occasion polite dwarf's claps. The wizard's talents were lackluster enough to irritate Patricia provoking serious scolding.
