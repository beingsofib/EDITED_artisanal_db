The Shattered Throne Tumbles was a type of stage performance called "grand play", popularized in Athkatla of Amn in the late 14 century DR.

History

The Shattered Throne Tumbles was penned before the late 14 century DR by a blind monk of the Ilmatari faith. The "grand play" was regularly performed at the famous The Crown Aflame theater in Athkatla's Temple District.
