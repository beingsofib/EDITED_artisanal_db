Avatar series (comic)

Avatar Series is a trilogy of comics illustrating the Avatar series novels. It was published by in 1991. It was written by Barbara Kesel, edited by Elliot S. Maggin, and drawn by Dameon Willich. It occurs in 1358 DR, during the Time of Troubles.

Issues

  1. Shadowdale
  2. Tantras
  3. Waterdeep
