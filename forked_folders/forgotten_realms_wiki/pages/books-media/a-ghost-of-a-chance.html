"A Ghost of a Chance" is a short story by Ed Greenwood, originally an outtake from "Elminster: The Making of a Mage".

Summary

While crossing country between the Horn Hills trying to reach Woodsedge Inn, Elminster Aumar, who was a young bandit at the time, comes under attack by Undarl, one of the Magelords of Athalantar. Struggling mightily, he manages to flee north into the treeline of the Wyrm Wood; he discovers himself in a burial ground, where Undarl is waiting for him again. However, Undarl's attack is thwarted by a suddenly appearing ghost. The ghost fights off Undarl, who flees, overmatched; the ghost points a little east of south for Elminster, who goes in that direction.
