Of Fathers, Faiths, and Fortunes was a book of musings written by Aleena Paladinstar, daughter of Open Lord Piergeiron Paladinson of Waterdeep, and published in the early 15 century DR.

History

Aleena and her husband Savengriff became prolific writers. One of the books from under their quills was Of Fathers, Faiths, and Fortunes, published in the . In the , Renaer Neverember owned the couple's entire body of mundane works, which included Aleena's Of Fathers, Faiths, and Fortunes.
