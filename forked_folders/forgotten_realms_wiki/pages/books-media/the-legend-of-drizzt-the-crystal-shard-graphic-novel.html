The Legend of Drizzt: The Crystal Shard (original title: Forgotten Realms: The Legend of Drizzt, Volume 4: The Crystal Shard) is the graphic novel version of R.A. Salvatore's novel The Crystal Shard. It was originally published in three parts: The Legend of Drizzt: The Crystal Shard #1–3.
