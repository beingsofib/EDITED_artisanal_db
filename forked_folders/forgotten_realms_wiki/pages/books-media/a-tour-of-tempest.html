A Tour of Tempest was an Umberlant book that discussed the divine tempest domain, written by and published sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.

Content

A Tour of Tempest was a large tome the size of an encyclopedia. The book's writing style was wild with its content being sometimes hard to understand.

History

A copy of A Tour of Tempest could be found in the in Water Queen's House, a temple dedicated to Umberlee located in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate.
