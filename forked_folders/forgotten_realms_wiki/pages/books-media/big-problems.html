Big Problems

Big Problems, designed by Marcello De Velazquez, is an adventure in the Dreams of the Red Wizards storyline published for the Adventurers League. It is the first part of the Red Seeds series that takes place in the wake of Storm King's Thunder.

Synopsis

As the Red Wizards of Thay were working to dominate the Sword Coast under Szass Tam's command, Queen Serissa of Maelstrom sendt an envoy, a young vegetarian storm giant Trosius to broker an alliance with the city of Daggerford against the Rid Wizards. Knowing how a group of little ones had restored the Ordning, she wanted to ally with the settlements of the Sword Coast. Illan Darosi, a Thayan spy tried to sick adventurers on the storm giant envoy, trying to convince the group and the locals that the giant was behind killing of cattle in the Daggerford Farmlands. The heroes discovered the Thayan ploy and apprehended the spy, his Red Wizard apprentice and hired bandits. Subsequently, the alliance was forged and Alekka Golt, a retired city militiawoman, was assigned as an envoy to Maelstrom.
