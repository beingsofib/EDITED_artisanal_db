Test of the Samurai is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons adventure module. It is part of the Kara-Tur campaign setting.

Description

Test of the Samurai is intended for up to 9 player-characters of levels 6 to 9. The module follows on from Ronin Challenge, but has an independent storyline set in Wa.
