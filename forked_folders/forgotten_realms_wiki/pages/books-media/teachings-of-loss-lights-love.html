Teachings of Loss: Light's Love was a Sharran text, printed before the 15 century DR.

Content

The tome was dedicated to an important concept of the Sharran faith: the absence of love. The book preached a dogma of love being a destructive force in one's life, a type of corruption that can entrap and enthrall a person within a moment, a passing glimpse. Love's warmth and comfort were an offense to Shar's emptiness and oblivion, and as such, the Church commanded its devotees to forcefully extinguish any embers of love and affection in their hearts, leaving behind nothing by ashes and a hungry heart.

History

By the , a copy of Teachings of Loss: Light's Love could be found in abandoned and ruined secret temple to Shar underneath the town of Reithwin, the Gauntlet of Shar.
