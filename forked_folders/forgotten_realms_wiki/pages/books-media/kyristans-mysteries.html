Kyristan's Mysteries was an arcane spellbook penned by the lich and skilled necromancer Kyristan. It contained a plethora of notes covering Kyristan's detailed research into his exploration of undeath. The tome also contained four unusual necromantic spells.

Description

This large tome measured on each side and was thick. The pages were made from bone with the writing inscribed in blood. The writing in the tome could not be removed by any means. The tome's spine was in fact an actual spine, complete with short ribs encircling the perimeter of the book.

History

Kyristan's Mysteries was last seen around the Year of the Bow, 1354 DR, in Suzail where it was stolen from the Lark.

Powers

Kyristan's Mysteries contained the following spells:

undead familiar • create spectral wizard • evolve • lifeblend
