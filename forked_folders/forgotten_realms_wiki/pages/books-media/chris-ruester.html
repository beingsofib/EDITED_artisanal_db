Chris Ruester

Chris Ruester was a member of the Role-Playing Group Association (RPGA) who played-in the Living City organized play program and authored the Red Threats series of modules for it.

Works

  • Red Dawn (March 13, 1998)
  • The Underground (July 17, 1998)
  • Desperately Seeking Selûne (February 05, 1999)
  • What do you know?, an article about Non-Weapon Proficiencies in Polyhedron #132.
