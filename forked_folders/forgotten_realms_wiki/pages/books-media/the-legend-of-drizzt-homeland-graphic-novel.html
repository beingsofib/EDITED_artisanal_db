The Legend of Drizzt: Homeland (original title: Forgotten Realms: The Legend of Drizzt, Volume 1: Homeland; reissued title: Dungeons & Dragons: Forgotten Realms: The Legend of Drizzt, Volume 1: Homeland) is the graphic novel version of R.A. Salvatore's novel Homeland. It was originally published in three parts: The Legend of Drizzt: Homeland #1–3.

Reprints

The The Legend of Drizzt: Homeland Graphic Novel series was reprinted in May 2006 as a paperback omnbius and in July 2008 as a hardcover omnbius by . It was released as a paperback omnibus in February 2015 by .
