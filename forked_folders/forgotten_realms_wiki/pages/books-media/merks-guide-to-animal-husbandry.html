Merk's Guide to Animal Husbandry was a book on the topics of agriculture and farming, written and published sometime before the mid-14 century DR.

History

A copy of Merk's Guide could be found in the library of the dilapidated Wolover's Keep in the Veilstone Peaks. The fortification's past master was a mage committed to agriculture, whose library was filled with works on the similar topics in the mid-14 century DR.
