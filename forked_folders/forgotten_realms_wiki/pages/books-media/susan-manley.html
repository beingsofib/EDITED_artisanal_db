Susan Manley

Susan Manley is primarily an artist who worked on Forgotten Realms video games in the late 80s to early 90s. She also contributed to another AD&D campaign, Dragonlance.

Susan voiced a character in a vehicular combat simulation game and managed projects for other video games. She still works in the industry to the present day.

Susan wrote an article about discovering the classic AD&D games.
