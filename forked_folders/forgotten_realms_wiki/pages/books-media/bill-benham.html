Bill Benham

Bill Benham is a producer and manager that works for Wizards of the Coast. Since 2014, he has mainly worked on D&D Adventurers League material, but went on to become a TRPG producer for further types of Dungeons & Dragons content. He has a degree in Health Care Management from Washington University, and worked in the army twenty years prior.
