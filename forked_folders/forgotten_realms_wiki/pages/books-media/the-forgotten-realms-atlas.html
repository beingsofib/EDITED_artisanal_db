The Forgotten Realms Atlas is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms.

Description

The Forgotten Realms Atlas was produced by Karen Wynn Fonstad and provides detailed maps of the Forgotten Realms, as well as timelines of various novels set in the world.
