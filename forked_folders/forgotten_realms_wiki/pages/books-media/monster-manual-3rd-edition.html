Monster Manual 3 edition is one of the core rulebooks for the 3-edition of . With the release of version 3.5, the book was revised and republished as ''Monster Manual v.3.5.

From the mundane to the magical, from the meek to the mighty, the Monster Manual brings the monsters of the DUNGEONS & DRAGONS game to life with statistics, descriptions, full-color illustrations, and player character information. This book is essential for every Dungeon Master who wants to keep players on their toes!

Description

Monster Manual is one the core rulebooks released for 3-edition and was not intended to make major changes, only to update older monsters to third edition rules.

Monster Manual won the Origins Award for Best Graphic Design of a Roleplaying Game, Adventure, or Supplement 2000.
