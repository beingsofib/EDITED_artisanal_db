Here's a Health to the Company was a song about lovers and companions parting that was performed and enjoyed in Faerûn.

History

The song was performed as a duet by the bard Lunasa Eveningwood and her companion Merlin Fastfoot while they stayed at Beckside Castle in Cormyr one night during the late 15 century DR. They also sang The Crafty Maid's Policy and The Mermaid together.
