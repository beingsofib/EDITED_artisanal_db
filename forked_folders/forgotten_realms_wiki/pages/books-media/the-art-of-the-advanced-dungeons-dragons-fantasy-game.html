The Art of the Advanced Dungeons & Dragons Fantasy Game is an artbook published by TSR, Inc. in 1989. It features full-color artworks and sketches by Clyde Caldwell, Jeff Easley, Larry Elmore, Fred Fields, Keith Parkinson, and others from then-recent and other TSR publications, for Forgotten Realms, Oriental Adventures, and other settings.
