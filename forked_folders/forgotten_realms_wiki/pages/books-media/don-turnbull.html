Don Turnbull

Don Turnbull was an editor and game designer who worked for TSR, Inc..

Career

Turnbull began his career as a high-school math teacher, but eventually began working in the game design industry. He was one of the initial contributors to the gaming magazine , to which he regularly wrote articles as part of a feature called "The Fiend Factory". Gary Gygax was a fan of this column, and he hired Turnbull to join TSR to manage its operations in the UK and to be the editor of a second Monster Manual based on "The Fiend Factory". This book became the 1 edition Fiend Folio.
