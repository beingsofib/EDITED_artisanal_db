Saturday Morning Adventures 4

Saturday Morning Adventures #4: "Prisoner of the Deep, Part Four" is a comic published by in June, 2023. It is written by David M. Booher, with art by George Kambadais. It is the fourth of a four-issue series following the characters of the 1983–1985 Dungeons & Dragons cartoon series.
