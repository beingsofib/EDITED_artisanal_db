Dark and Hidden Ways is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory.

Description

Dark and Hidden Ways was a booklet that was bundled with the Dungeoneer's Survival Guide as the "Limited Edition" of that sourcebook, mainly as a way to get rid of unsold stock. The first scenario, Drow Magic-User and Dracolich, is set in Hooknose Crag.

Contents

  • Drow Magic-User and Dracolich
  • Mindflayers
  • Goblins
  • Pyrohydra & Xorn
  • Aboleth and Cloakers
  • Ankheg
  • Jermlaine and Rats
