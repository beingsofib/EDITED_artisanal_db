The Dark Elf Trilogy Gift Set is a box-set of novels collecting Dark Elf Trilogy: Homeland, Exile, and Sojourn by R.A. Salvatore. It is sometimes called the Dark Elf Trilogy Box Set or the Dark Elf Trilogy Boxed Set.
