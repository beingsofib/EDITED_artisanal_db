Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn is a campaign sourcebook for the Forgotten Realms using the 5-edition (revised) ruleset published by Wizards of the Coast. It is set to be released in November 2025 and, alongside Forgotten Realms: Adventures in Faerûn, will be the first campaign setting book released for the Forgotten Realms in 5 edition, a full decade after Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide. Focusing on player options, it includes new subclasses, feats, backgrounds, and spells, as well as details on membership of heroic and villainous organizations.

Contents

The book includes eight new subclasses, eighteen player-character backgrounds, forty-two deities, thirty-four feats, eight factions, nineteen spells, three magic items, twelve mundane items, two monsters, and thirteen maps, as well as overviews of ten regions of Faerûn.
