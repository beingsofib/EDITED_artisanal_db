Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue was a shopping catalogue produced by Aurora in order to facilitate her mail-order shopping business, as an extension of Aurora's Emporium.

Contents

The main body of the catalogue was taken up with illustrated listings of various goods, ranging from common everyday tools to odd and esoteric devices.

A Word from Our Founder

The catalogue opened with a message to the customer that explained how Aurora's business worked and how to place orders using the catalogue, including deposits, rush fees, and customizations.

Rangers' Field

A selection of hunting and fishing gear, as well as specialized ranger tools like tree beds.

Thieves' Corner

Questionably-legal tools for thieves to use in their mischief, such as tar makeup and caltrops.

Bards' Emporium

Props and instruments for bards, including stilts, bamsmacks, and zithers.

Priests' Alcove

Clerical equipment like incense, aspergillums, and a wide array of material components for rituals.

Wizards' Laboratory

Laboratory equipment for experimentation such as retorts and glass tubing, as well as various reagents for alchemical and magical processes.

Laborers' List

A wide selection of practical and useful work tools like awls, hoes, shears, and raw metals and molds for smithing.

Inn and Tavern

Common domestic accoutrements used in taverns and inns, from bowls and blankets to mugs and mops.

Scribes' Desk

Various materials for scribes such as paper, papyrus, and pens, and a rainbow of inks and paints. It was very popular with the sages of Candlekeep.

Storage Items

A generous selection of baskets, barrels, crates, and chests.

General Hardware

Miscellaneous useful tools, including paint brushes, shackles, and chain.

Tailors' Corner

Sewing needles, scissors, and patterns, and an array of fabrics, skins, and dyes.

Clothes and Shoes

Wardrobes full of slippers, capes, chemises, and purses.

Boom's Garden

Various pieces of improbable and impractical gear made by Kar, including armored corsets and low-cut plate mail bustiers.

Jewelry

Popular pieces of jewelery like torcs, brooches, and fillets.

Household Accoutrements

Domestic items including hand mirrors and water clocks.

Personal Supplies

Tools for cleaning and grooming, like hair brushes, soap, and perfume.

Illuminations

Every flavor of lamp and lantern imaginable.

Diversions

Toys and games, as well as nesting dolls, rocking mounts, and kaleidoscopes.

Aurora's Larder

A selection of rare and common herbs, spices, and preserved foods.

Breads

Various crackers, breads, noodles.

Cheese Shop

Cheeses like pepper cheese, Death Cheese, and yak butter.

Wines and Ales

Cellars-full worth of ales, beers, ciders, and wines, from Bitter Black to Westgate Ruby.

Exotics

Outlandish imported foods including tomatos, soya sauce, tabacco, and garum.

Wilderness Gear

Useful survival gear like tents, hammocks, rations, and canteens.

Priest in a Poke

A set of practical emergency medical tools, including bandages, splints, and tourniquets.

DaRoni's Workshop

Ingenious and unique creations of Nadul DaRoni, like the furnace wagon and underwater boat.
