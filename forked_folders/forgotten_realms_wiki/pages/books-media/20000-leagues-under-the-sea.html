20,000 Leagues Under the Sea was a book found on the Sea of Swords and Nelanther Islands circa 1372 DR.

History

The book was penned by an unknown author peculiarly named Jules Verne, sometime before the . Several copies of the novel could be found in the pirate-ridden Spindrift Town, notably, one copy belonged to the Willigam family of the pirate town and was kept in their manor.
