Bart Carroll

Bart Carroll is the producer for the Wizards of the Coast website, and general digital marketing manager. He has been a member of the team since 2005. He has also written some articles for Dragon+ virtual magazine and for an online panelled comic. Bart has also been involved in a handful of Dungeons & Dragons material, such as playtesting board games.
