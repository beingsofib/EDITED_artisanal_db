"Liar's Game" is a short story by Jessica Beaven that appeared in the Realms of Shadow anthology of stories from the Forgotten Realms, published in April 2002.

Summary

In preparation for their return, members of Shade Enclave find descendants still in Faerûn and begin to corrupt and command them. One, a sadistic wizard-druid woman, is sent to find some notes that may have power to help Shade Enclave. In the , she finds them with a man living alone in the woods. A type of love ensues.
