Raymond Swanland

Raymond Swanland is a science-fiction and fantasy artist who has illustrated the covers of several Forgotten Realms novels.

Career

Swanland began his artistic career as a graphic designer for the video game company and worked on all four of that companies games. During that time he began to illustrate book covers and comic books as well.
