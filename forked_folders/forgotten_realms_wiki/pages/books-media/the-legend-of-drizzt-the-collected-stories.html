The Legend of Drizzt: The Collected Stories is an audiobook of short stories written by R. A. Salvatore, read by a number of notable actors and musicians.

Contents

All stories appearing in the audiobook were previously published in other anthologies.

  • "Wickless in the Nether": read by Sean Astin
  • "Bones and Stones": read by Al Yankovic
  • "Comrades at Odds": read by Ice-T
  • "To Legend He Goes": read by David Duchovny
  • "If Ever They Happened Upon My Lair": read by Wil Wheaton
  • "Dowery": read by Melissa Rauch
  • "Dark Mirror": read by Dan Harmon
  • "The First Notch": read by Felicia Day
  • "The Third Level": read by Greg Grunberg
  • "Guenhwyvar (short story)": read by Tom Felton
  • "Iruladoon": read by Michael Chiklis
  • "That Curious Sword": read by Danny Pudi
