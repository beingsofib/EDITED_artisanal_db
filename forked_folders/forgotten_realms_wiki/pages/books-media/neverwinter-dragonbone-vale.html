Neverwinter: Dragonbone Vale is an expansion module for the massively-multiplayer role-playing game Neverwinter. Released in February 2022, the expansion continued the Cult of the Dragon storyline that started in the previous module, Jewel of the North.

''In Dragonbone Vale, the evil lich Valindra Shadowmantle has returned to threaten the Sword Coast alongside the Cult of the Dragon, who have come to possess an ancient artifact with the potential to amplify their foul rituals of undeath. Under the leadership of famed wizard Elminster Aumar, the brave Heroes of Neverwinter will need to work with the allied factions of Protector's Enclave, to reclaim the Dragonbone Vale and put a stop to the Cult's plans. Excerpt from the press release
