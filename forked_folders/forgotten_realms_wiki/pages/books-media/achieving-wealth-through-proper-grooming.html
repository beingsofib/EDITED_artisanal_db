Achieving Wealth Through Proper Grooming was a book published several decades before 1372 DR.

History

As of the , a copy of Achieving Wealth Through Proper Grooming was stashed on a bookshelf in Castle Exeltis's basement. The family's scion mismanaged the once-flourishing winery, and the bookshelves in the castle basement were covered in a thick layer of dust, evidence of a dire literary neglect. The shelf, however, hid a secret passage from the basement to the castle's second floor and the lord's chambers.
