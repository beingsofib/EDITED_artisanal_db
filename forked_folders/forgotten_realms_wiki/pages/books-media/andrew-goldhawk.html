Andrew Goldhawk

Andrew Goldhawk is a fantasy illustrator who was born in 1966 in Buffalo, New York. He graduated from the Colorado Institute of Art and from the early 1990s he has worked with RPG companies such as TSR, White Wolf, and Wizards of the Coast. He considers himself to be a science fiction and horror artist, and prefers to use traditional methods such as oil, pencil, and brush.
