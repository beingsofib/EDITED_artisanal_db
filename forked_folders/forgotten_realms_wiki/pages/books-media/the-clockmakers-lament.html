The Clockmaker's Lament was a poem in three parts written by Enki the Carver, a member of the Waterclock Guild of Neverwinter.

History

As of the late 15 century DR—long after the collapse of the Waterclock Guild—pieces of The Clockmaker's Lament could be found scattered throughout the abandoned Waterclock Guild's Crypts.
