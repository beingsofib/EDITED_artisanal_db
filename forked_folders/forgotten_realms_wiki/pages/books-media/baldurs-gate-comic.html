Baldur's Gate (comic)

Baldur's Gate is a standalone comic published by set in the Forgotten Realms.

Synopsis

A group of adventurers band together in the Sword Coast to escort a soon-to-be-wed woman and the accompanying dowry to her future husband in Amn.

After the group is ambushed by a band of monstrous humanoids, two of their company - Ajantis and Shar-Teel - must investigate in nearby Baldur's Gate and find the traitor that disrupted their journey.
