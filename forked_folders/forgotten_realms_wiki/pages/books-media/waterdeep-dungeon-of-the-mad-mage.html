Waterdeep: Dungeon of the Mad Mage is a 5-edition Dungeons & Dragons adventure published by Wizards of the Coast in 2018. It takes place in the dungeon of Undermountain, located under Waterdeep, and follows the Waterdeep: Dragon Heist adventure through levels 5‒20.
