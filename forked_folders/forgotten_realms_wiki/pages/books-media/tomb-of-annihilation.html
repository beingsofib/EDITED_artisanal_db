Tomb of Annihilation is an adventure module for 5-edition Dungeons & Dragons, published by Wizards of the Coast in 2017, set in the Chult region of the Forgotten Realms. It is intended for 4 to 6 players, taking them from 1 or 5 level to 11 level and higher.
