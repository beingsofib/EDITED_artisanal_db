Sembia: Gateway to the Realms

Sembia: Gateway to the Realms is a seven part series focusing on the residents of Stormweather Towers in Selgaunt. It was originally titled the Sembia series. The first novel, The Halls of Stormweather, is an anthology, and multiple authors wrote the rest of installments.

The series was first released between 2000 and 2003, with cover artwork by Terese Nielsen. Beginning in 2007, the series was reprinted with new cover art by Raymond Swanland.

An eighth entry into the series, Pride of the Lion by Ed Greenwood was cancelled in late 2002 due to the authors other work commitments.

Sembia: Gateway to the Realms

  1. The Halls of Stormweather (July 2000)
  2. Shadow's Witness (November 2000)
  3. The Shattered Mask (June 2001)
  4. Black Wolf (November 2001)
  5. Heirs of Prophecy (June 2002)
  6. Sands of the Soul (November 2002)
  7. Lord of Stormweather (March 2003)
