Shadows over the Moonsea

Shadows over the Moonsea is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5th edition ruleset. It is part of the Tyranny of Dragons series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on the Moonsea, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1- to 4-level characters.
