A City on the Edge

A City on the Edge is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Tomb of Annihilation series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Port Nyanzaru, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1- to 4-level characters.
