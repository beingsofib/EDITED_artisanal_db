Mark Buchignani

Mark Buchignani is a game designer and programmer that has worked on many Dungeons and Dragons video games. Buchignani has contributed to such games from 1991 to present.

He has had many roles throughout his years, such as: project manager, scenario and encounters designer, programmer, dialogue engineer, and story writer for games in the Forgotten Realms campaign setting.

Works

  • Gateway to the Savage Frontier
  • Treasures of the Savage Frontier
  • Pool of Radiance: Ruins of Myth Drannor
