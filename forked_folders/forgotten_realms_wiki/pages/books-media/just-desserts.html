Just Desserts was a book by an unknown author found in the Collector's Mansion in the Blacklake District of Neverwinter in the late 14 century DR.

History

The copy of Just Desserts found in the Collector's Mansion in the , was turned into a magical trap. When touched, the book felt unstable, and an attempt to open it resulted in a fiery explosion.
