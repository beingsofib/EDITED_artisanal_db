Rick Brown

Rick Brown was a fantasy writer who has worked for TSR, Inc. He notably contributed to the first set of AD&D Trading Cards in 1991.

Works

As part of his work for TSR, Inc., Brown wrote the text present on the back of the 750 cards included in the earliest of the Collector Cards yearly sets. However, later ones, such as the 1992 collection, would divide this responsibility up amongst departmental staff.
