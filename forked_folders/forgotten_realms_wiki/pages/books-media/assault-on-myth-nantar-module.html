Assault on Myth Nantar (module)

Assault on Myth Nantar is an epic adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5-edition ruleset. It is part of the Adventurers League/Dreams of the Red Wizards series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It is an epic adventure involving multiple parties of adventures, centered around an attempt by the Red Wizards of Thay to conquer the fabled city of Myth Nantar. It is the third part of the Sea of Fallen Stars series.
