Description

Tome and Blood provides supplemental information for characters belonging to the wizard and sorcerer base classes. This book contained tips for creating and playing characters of the aforementioned class, as well as a large number of prestige classes. Tome and Blood was not updated to 3.5-edition, although most of the prestige classes were later reintroduced in Complete Arcane.
