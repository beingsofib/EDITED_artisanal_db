Missives of Candlekeep was a collection of important letters written by the Avowed of Candlekeep.

Description

The letters collected within Missives were preserved in their original format, down to the handwriting of each letter-writer.

Content

Most letters included within the book contained esoteric scholarly information, such as the movements of celestial bodies or specifications of magical rituals.

One letter of note was that written by Trystan P. Shale to the famous Elminster of Shadowdale. In the letter, Trystan shares with Elminster the whereabouts of the sage Gorion and his ward Abdel, along with information related to the individuals known as Bhaalspawn.

History

As of the , a copy of this book was left in the Emerald Grove of the Western Heartlands.
