Jerry Holkins

Keith Gerald Holkins, better known as Jerry Holkins, is an American writer and streamer. His games are the origin of Acquisitions Incorporated and Omin Dran (his player character). He was a Creative Director in the production of the Acquisitions Incorporated sourcebook and adventure.

He is the original author of the webcomic Penny Arcade along with its artist Mike Krahulik. Together with Krahulik, Holkins created the Penny Arcade Expo beginning in 2004, a series of gaming festivals held in Seattle, Boston, Australia, Philadelphia, and San Antonio. He is also a co-founder of Child's Play, a charity which organizes toy drives for children's hospitals. Along with Krahulik, Holkins was included on the 2010 Time 100 for their work on Penny Arcade.
