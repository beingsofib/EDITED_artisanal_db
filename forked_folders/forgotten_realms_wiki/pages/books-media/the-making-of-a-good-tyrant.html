The Making of a Good Tyrant was a book that was available during the mid–14 century DR.

History

After the notorious wizard Elzid Natholin fell in love with Erilyn Windfellow and stopped performing evil, Elzid's imp familiar Zotzpox placed a copy of this tome under its master's pillow in an unsuccessful attempt to rejuvenate the hatred in Elzid's heart.
