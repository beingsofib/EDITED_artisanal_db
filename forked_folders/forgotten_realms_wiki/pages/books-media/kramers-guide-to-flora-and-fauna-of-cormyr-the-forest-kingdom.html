Kramer's Guide to Flora and Fauna of Cormyr, the Forest Kingdom was a book that catalogues species of plants and animals found in the kingdom of Cormyr, penned and published sometime before the late 14 century DR.

History

A copy of Kramer's Guide to Flora and Fauna of Cormyr was among the many books of Phileas Hobblebush, a 14 century DR Sage of Ravens Bluff, expert on the topic of fauna and especially flora, and a famous Cormyr-phile.
