The Heroes of Phlan trilogy

The Heroes of Phlan trilogy by James Ward, with Jane Cooper Hong and Anne K. Brown, follows the adventures of Shal Bal, Ren o' the Blade, Tarl Desanea, and their descendants among the mystical pools that plague the Moonsea region.
