Bryon Wischstadt

Bryon Wischstadt is a programmer. In 1997, he was the writer for the Vale of the Dragon Oracle adventure for the Adventurer's Guild program. He has been repeatedly credited in online communications for his contributions to the development of Cormyr.

He is also named in the special thanks section of a number of books from 1998 to 2001, including the Hellgate Keep adventure, Empires of the Shining Sea, Sea of Fallen Stars, Secrets of the Magister, the Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting 3rd edition, and the novel Blackstaff.
