What Came Out of My Mouth Too Swiftly was a collection of the jests of Jeremmer Hardree, the Mad Jester, a court fool to two kings and a queen of the city-state of Ormpur on the edge of Lapaliiya in southwest Faerûn in the 13 and early 14 centuries DR.
