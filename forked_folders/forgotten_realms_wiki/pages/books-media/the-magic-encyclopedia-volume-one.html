The Magic Encyclopedia volume One is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory.

Description

The Magic Encyclopedia volume One is the first of a pair of 2nd edition books containing a vast collection of mundane items, their magical equivalents, and some unique magic items from A-G sourced from other AD&D products. It was published in August 1992.
