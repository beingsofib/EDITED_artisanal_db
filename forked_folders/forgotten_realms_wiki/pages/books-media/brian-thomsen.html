Brian Thomsen

Brian Michael Thomsen was a writer and editor who worked for TSR, Inc.

Career

Thomsen worked as the managing editor for fiction for TSR. He also authored two Forgotten Realms novels, Once Around the Realms and The Mage in the Iron Mask, both of which involved the character Volothamp Geddarm.
