Gerald Brom

Gerald Brom is an artist and illustrator. He has a distinctive gothic fantasy style.

Biography

He spent much of his early years on the move, living in many countries such as Japan and Germany.

Career

Among others, he has contributed illustrations for the War of the Spider Queen series and reprints of The Avatar Series, and was a key illustrator of the Dark Sun campaign setting.
