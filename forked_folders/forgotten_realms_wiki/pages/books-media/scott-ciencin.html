Scott Ciencin

Scott Ciencin was an American author who sometimes wrote with Denise Ciencin. He is credited for writing two novels in The Avatar Series as Richard Awlinson, though later editions credited him with his actual name. He also wrote The Night Parade and worked with R.A. Salvatore on a couple of non-Forgotten Realms projects.
