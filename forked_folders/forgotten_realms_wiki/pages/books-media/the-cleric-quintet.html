The Cleric Quintet is the other significant series by R.A. Salvatore, after the extremely popular Legend of Drizzt. The entire series was reprinted in 2000. It is also available as a single collector's edition omnibus.

  1. Canticle (October 1991)
  2. In Sylvan Shadows (April 1992)
  3. Night Masks (August 1992)
  4. The Fallen Fortress (June 1993)
  5. The Chaos Curse (June 1994)
