Les Dorscheid

Les Dorscheid is an artist for Dungeons & Dragons material, particularly sourcebooks, accessories, magazines, and comics. Dorscheid has worked on both interior and cover art for the Forgotten Realms, Spelljammer, and core settings.

Works

Dorscheid provided art for:

  • Monstrous Manual
  • The Complete Paladin's Handbook
  • The Complete Ninja's Handbook
  • Dragon magazine 212
  • Player's Option: Combat & Tactics
  • The Grand Tour
