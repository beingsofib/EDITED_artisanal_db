Theskan Anthology of Goblin Poetry was a simple pamphlet-size chapbook that collected works of poetry by the goblins of Thesk.

I once met a wizard in Tammar
Who went and corrected me grammar
'e said I can't cast no spell
'til I enunciate well
So I beat 'im to death with a hammer. Excerpt from Theskan Anthology of Goblin Poetry''

Description

The Anthology was filled with many lewd sketches.

History

In 1492 DR, a copy of Theskan Anthology of Goblin Poetry could be found within the camp of the Cult of the Absolute that took over the Shattered Sanctum, once a temple of Selûne. The followers of the Absolute included numerous goblins.
