Tour of Duty is an adventure module from the RPGA Legacy of the Green Regent campaign.

Plot

As Waterdeep's city guard is crippled by illness, volunteers are needed to relieve a northern outpost. At the same time, rangers from the same outpost discover a dragon's hoard and help themselves to some choice items, much to the anger of the dragon.
