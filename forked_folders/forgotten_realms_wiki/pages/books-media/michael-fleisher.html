Michael Fleisher

Michael Fleisher was a comics writer who worked for DC Comics during the 1970s and 1980s.

In 1988, Fleisher wrote the first four issues of the Advanced Dungeons & Dragons comics series set in the Forgotten Realms and published by DC Comics, with artist Jan Duursema as co-plotter. Together they introduced all the heroes of the series: Cybriana of Shadowdale, Onyx the Invincible, Timoth Eyesbright, Vajra Valmeyjar, as well as Priam Agrivar.
