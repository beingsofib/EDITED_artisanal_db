...And Pearls Do Not Dissolve in the Mud

...And Pearls Do Not Dissolve in the Mud is a three-round tournament adventure module for the Al-Qadim setting.

Index

This adventure contains three rounds in three separate documents, which are indexed separately on these subpages:

  • Round One: The Tired, Hungry, and Poor
  • Round Two: Wealth and Riches Beyond Imagining; Treachery and Deceit Beyond Comprehension
  • Round Three: Into the Desert
