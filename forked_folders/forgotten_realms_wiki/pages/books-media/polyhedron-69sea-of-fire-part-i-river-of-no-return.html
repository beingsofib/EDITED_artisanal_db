Polyhedron 69/Sea of Fire Part I: River of No Return

Sea of Fire Part I: River of No Return is an adventure module set in Kara-Tur. It was published in March 1992 in Polyhedron 69 in 1992. It was followed by Sea of Fire Part II: The Sea of the Problem. The adventure was designed by Robert Farnsworth and is intended for 4-6 characters of 7-9 levels.

Synopsis

A powerful wu jen from Wa slew the chiang lung guardian of Lake Pakarsh and re-routed the Tokali River via a gateway of symmetry. This caused deadly draught in the Hordelands, harming the wildlife, the nomad tribes, and starting grassfires. The Khans tasked a group of adventurers to investigate the drying Tokali River. In a race against the clock, the adventurers were joined by Pok Luing, a chiang lung sent by the Celestial Emperor to replace the killed dragon as the lake's guardian. They discovered the gateway of symmetry and followed Pok Luing through the gate to Wa.
