Robert J. Kuntz

Robert J. Kuntz is a game designer and one of the first people ever to play Gary Gygax' Dungeons & Dragons.

Career

Kuntz was TSR's sixth employee. He was initially hired into the shipping department, yet he was still able to do design work, and he co-authored Gods, Demi-Gods & Heroes. Later, he was an author for Deities and Demigods, and he wrote for Dragon magazine as well.

After leaving TSR, Kuntz started his own publishing company, Pied Piper Publishing, which was in existence until 2010.
