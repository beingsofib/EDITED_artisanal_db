Luvander's Prime Enchantments and Illusions of the Heart was a two-volume spellbook that contained the specifications for a number of different spells.

Description

Most of the spells contained within Prime Enchantments could be readily learned and cast even by mages of middling skill or limited scope.

Notable Owners

  • Ansoain, a wandering mage that made her home in the city of Scornubel in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
