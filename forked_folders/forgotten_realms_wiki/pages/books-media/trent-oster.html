Trent Oster

Trent Oster is one of the founders of BioWare and the founder of , a downloading service, and IdeaSpark Labs.

Education and Career

Oster studied computer science at the University of Saskatchewan before co-founding Bioware in 1995. He worked on the Baldur's Gate game and directed the production of Neverwinter Nights and its expansions

He left BioWare in 2009.
