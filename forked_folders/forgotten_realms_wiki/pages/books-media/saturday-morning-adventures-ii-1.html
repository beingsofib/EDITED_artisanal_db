Saturday Morning Adventures II 1

Saturday Morning Adventures II #1: "Here There Be Pirates Part One" is a comic published by in January, 2024. It is written by David M. Booher, with art by George Kambadais. It is the first of a second four-issue series following the characters of the 1983–1985 Dungeons & Dragons cartoon series.
