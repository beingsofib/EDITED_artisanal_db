Karl Waller

Karl Waller is a comic book and fantasy artist.

Education

Waller graduated from the in 1987.

Career

Waller illustrated many Dungeons & Dragons sourcebooks between 1988 and 1998. He also illustrated for a large number of other role-playing and collectible card games.
