The Meanderings Upon the Friezes of Hotun-Shûl was a book penned by Skorm Caldril and translated by Triel of Baldur's Gate sometime before the late 14 century DR.

History

A copy of The Meanderings Upon the Friezes of Hotun-Shûl belonged to Manshoon XII, a clone of Manshoon, in the . The book was stored in the mage's library of mundane and arcane tomes in his lair in an abandoned temple of Amaunator underneath the City of the Dead of Waterdeep.
