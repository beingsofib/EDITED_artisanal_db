"Menace of the Icy Spire" is a short adventure for Dungeons & Dragons 4 edition published in Dungeon #159.

Synopsis

In Eleasis of 1479 DR, a group of adventurers is hired to investigate the unseasonably cold weather in the Gray Vale. Local leaders in Loudwater suspect this is a magical phenomenon emanating from the tower of the dwarven warlock Draigdurroch, who disappeared thirty years ago. The adventurers must investigate Draigdurroch Tower, overcoming frost goblins, ice warriors, and a spirit of winter itself along the way.
