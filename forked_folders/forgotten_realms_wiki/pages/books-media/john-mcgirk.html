John McGirk

John McGirk is a game designer, developer, writer and voice actor. He mainly designed video games for . He worked in the industry from 1993 to 1998.

He was the game designer for the Menzoberranzan video game, based around R.A. Salvatore's Legacy of the Drow series of novels. John also supported the development of Dungeon Hack.

He further contributed to the Dungeons & Dragons scene, working on the Ravenloft campaign. Later on, he went on to work for Warhammer 40,000 and some other projects.
