The Marionette is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms and Ravenloft settings using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Curse of Strahd series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It is an adventure designed for three to seven 1- to 4-level characters and is part four of Misty Fortunes and Absent Hearts.
