Faiths and Pantheons is an accessory for the 3-edition of for the Forgotten Realms campaign setting. The accessory detailed the mechanics of the system established at the end of the Time of Troubles.

Description

Faiths and Pantheons features the power levels and exact abilities of the various major deities of Faerûn as of 3 Edition, and has descriptions of the dogmas and churches of all of the intermediate deities, lesser deities, and demigods named in the core rulebook. It also features the names of various monster deities and others unmentioned in the core book, with descriptions of some, as well as 20 prestige classes for player characters and non-player characters alike.
