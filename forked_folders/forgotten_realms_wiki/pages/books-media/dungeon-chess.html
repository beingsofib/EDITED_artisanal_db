Dungeon Chess was a virtual chess game developed & published by Experiment 7, set inside the Yawning Portal tavern and utilizing various Dungeons & Dragons creatures in place of standard chess pieces. It was released first for Android and Windows in 2017, then later released to the Oculus store in 2018.
