The Treatise on Good Wifery was a book written by Miriam Buttercake of Ashabenford during the late 13 century DR. It was published in 1298 DR.

As of 1372 DR, a copy of the book could be found in the Leaves of Learning in Highmoon, while the original was located in the library of Candlekeep.
