Out of the Endless Prison was a book that discussed methods for escaping Carceri, the so-called "prison-plane".

History

The book was written long before the 15 century DR, and was highly valued by scholars who studied the planes.

Notable Owners

  • The Dolindar family, who were known for their study of the planes. Their copy of Out of the Endless Prison came to rest within the vault of their family tomb.
