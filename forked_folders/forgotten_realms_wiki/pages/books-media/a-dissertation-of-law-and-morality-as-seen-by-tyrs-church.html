A Dissertation of Law and Morality as Seen by Tyr's Church was a book about the church of Tyr and ethical and legal matters.

Adzerak, a lich and bibliophile devotee of Deneir, kept a copy on his nightstand in his lair in Cormyr around 1367 DR.
