A Sprinter's Guide to Goblins was a book that dealt with various types of goblinoids and suggestions on how to avoid them, penned by Colin Meringue and published sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , copies of Colin Meringue's A Modest Guide to Sword Coast Wine could be found in the city of Baldur's Gate. Nansi Gretta sold the book in her Lower City shop, The Bibliophile; a copy could be found in the library of The Lodge, the Baldur's Gate chapter of the Society of Brilliance; and it was found in the Crimson Draughts potionmaker's shop.
