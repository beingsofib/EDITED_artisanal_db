Kirk Lesser

Kirk Lesser is an editor from California who co-wrote "The Role of Computers" column for Dragon from 1987 until 1993 with his parents Pattie and Hartley Lesser.

After "The Role of Computers" column ended with Dragon #196, the three went on to write the "Mac Attack!" column for Mac Home Journal. Kirk currently runs an IT company in the San Francisco Bay area.
