Sophia Lillis

Sophia Lillis is an American actress who portrayed Doric, a tiefling druid who was recruited by thieves, in the Dungeons and Dragons film Honor Among Thieves.

Career

In the fantasy and science-fiction genre, she appeared in the horror movies ' (2017) and ', as well as the series ''''.

In an interview, Lillis said she had "played D&D in high school with my teacher as the DM" and "loved listening to D&D podcasts and Critical Role and all that." She said "I love D&D, so this is like a dream come true."
