Loren Coleman

Loren L. Coleman was the designer of the 2-edition sourcebook Chronomancer, which introduced time travel to Dungeons & Dragons settings.

Biography

Loren was born in Washington state in 1968. After serving in the US Navy, he began a freelance writing career. Chronomancer was one of his earliest works. His first professional novel was a novel titled Double-Blind. From that point forward, he wrote nine further BattleTech novels, as well as novels for other settings. He has contributed scripts for several video games and done editing work for dozens of game-related manuals.

Loren is married and has three children and lives in the Lake Stevens area.
