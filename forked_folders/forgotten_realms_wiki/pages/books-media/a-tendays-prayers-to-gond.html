A Tenday's Prayers to Gond was customary prayer dedicated to the deity Gond Wonderbringer, adopted sometime before the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , A Tenday's Prayers to Gond was used by the Gondar of the city of Baldur's Gate and the workers of the Steel Watch Foundry.
