The Citadels

The Citadels was a series of novels written by various authors.

  1. Neversfall by Ed Gentry (November 2007)
  2. Obsidian Ridge by Jess Lebow (April 2008)
  3. The Shield of Weeping Ghosts by James P. Davis (May 2008)
  4. Sentinelspire by Mark Sehestedt (July 2008)
