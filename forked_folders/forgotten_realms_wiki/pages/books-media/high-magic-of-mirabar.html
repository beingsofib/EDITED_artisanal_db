High Magic of Mirabar

BookIU
nameHigh Magic of Mirabar
typeBook

High Magic of Mirabar was a book that detailed the vast number of treasured magical items that dwelled within the city of Mirabar. This included a wand of armory, which the book noted had the command word "Skulpin".

History

When Elminster detailed wands of armory to Ed of the Greenwood, he made mention of this tome.
