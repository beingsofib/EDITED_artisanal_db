Curtis Scott

Curtis Scott was a game designer who had worked for TSR, Inc. He was also one of the organizers of the gaming convention.

Education

Scott had been accepted to to study computer science.

Works

Scott was the author of the Horde Campaign. He also worked on the gaming system.

Death

Scott was killed in a car accident in 1992 on the way to a gaming fair when he was 32 years old.
