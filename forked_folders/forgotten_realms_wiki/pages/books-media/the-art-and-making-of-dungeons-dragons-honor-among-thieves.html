The Art and Making of Dungeons & Dragons: Honor Among Thieves is a non-fiction book about the production of the Honor Among Thieves movie published on April 4, 2023. It is written by Eleni Roussos and has a foreword by John Francis Daley and Jonathan Goldstein.
