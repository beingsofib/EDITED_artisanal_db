In the Swirling Smoke of Abyss was a book written by an unknown author.

Description

The tome was black bound.

Contents

This book provided detailed accounts of demons, their lords, and even their true names.

Notable Owners

This book was in the possession of Gromph Baenre in 1485 DR.
