Dungeons & Dragons Online/Fall of the Night Brigade

Fall of the Night Brigade is an adventure pack for Dungeons & Dragons Online where the player characters return to Wheloon Prison to help the Riverside Rats gang against the murderous Night Brigade. The adventure pack was added to the game on December 11, 2024 as part of Update 71.

Plot

The Fall of the Night Brigade quest chain is introduced by Old Tarlow, a representative of the Riverside Rats. They've been driven out of their hideout and lost many friends, and want nothing more than to break back into the prison and return home. To do so, the Sharran Night Brigade gang must first be destroyed.

Rats in a Cage starts off with Ryndalla Cuttail sending the players to rescue captured wererats and deal with Agarta the Breaker in her arena complex. Using a ratty talisman, they are able to take a smaller form and sneak past the Night Brigade defenses and strike from the inside, beating up the gladiators Gregori the Broken, Bonegnasher the Cursed, Durvastra, and finally the bloodthirsty Agarta, putting an end to her murderous games.

The Wizard's Labyrinth has Wyshbryn Saldar target the insane wizard Darstil Vurusk for death. His tower has become a labyrinth of death and torture, with his test subjects doomed without aid. Once again sneaking in as rats, the players are dropped into his laboratory maze, which they eventually escape. The mad scientist is eventually cornered and slain, ending his reign of terror.

The Knife's Edge is led by Neese the Burrower, who wishes to see Master Thief Karissa the Knife fall. The greedy thief has laired deep underneath the city in a palatial hideout with all her stolen riches. Despite the many traps, puzzles, and guards defending her, Karissa is unable to escape her fate at the paws of vengeful rats.

The Darkness and the Light ends the quests with Garmor the Grim, who sends the players to take care of the Night Brigade leader, the priestess Storna Harnwood. She is fortified in the locked-down Temple of Shar, which used to be the Riverside Rats' own hideout. Meeting the auspicious Selûnite Brunwell Godber outside, the players' moon-blessed mission sees them dispel Shar's darkness and overcome Storna's defenses. With Storna and the other officers gone, the Night Brigade are no more and the Riverside Rats can return safely home.
