The Legend of Drizzt: Exile (original title: Forgotten Realms: The Legend of Drizzt, Volume 2: Exile; reissued title: Dungeons & Dragons: Forgotten Realms: The Legend of Drizzt, Volume 2: Exile) is the graphic novel version of R.A. Salvatore's novel Exile. It was originally published in three parts: The Legend of Drizzt: Exile #1–3.
