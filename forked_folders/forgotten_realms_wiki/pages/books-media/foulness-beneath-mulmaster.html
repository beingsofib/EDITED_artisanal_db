Foulness Beneath Mulmaster

Foulness Beneath Mulmaster is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Elemental Evil series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Mulmaster, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1 – 4-level characters.
