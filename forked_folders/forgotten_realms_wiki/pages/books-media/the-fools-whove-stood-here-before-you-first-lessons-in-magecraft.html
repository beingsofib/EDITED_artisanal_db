The Fools Who've Stood Here Before You: First Lessons in Magecraft was a book written by Malchor Harpell in the .

Description

The book included a section on spell inventors. Instead of focusing on well-known wizards, the author offered short accounts of several lesser-known spellcrafters, including Archveult Tattercloak, Caligarde, Grimwald, Nchaser, and Nulathoe.
