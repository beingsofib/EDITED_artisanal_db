The Fighters

The Fighters is a series of novels by various authors.

  1. Master of Chains by Jess Lebow (September 2005)
  2. Ghostwalker by Erik Scott de Bie (December 2005)
  3. Son of Thunder by Murray Leeder (January 2006)
  4. Bladesinger by Keith Francis Strohm (April 2006)
