"Her Love for It Was Like a Plum" was a crude ballad about the shadow-cursed lands of the Chionthar Valley, written some time before the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , a book that recorded the song was carried by one of the pilgrims that followed the new religion of the Absolute into the shadow-cursed lands that surrounded the Moonrise Towers. The pilgrim perished to the curse, becoming an undead creature of darkness, still clinging to the book.
