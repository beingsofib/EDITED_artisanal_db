Bard Dance was popular upbeat foot-tapper song in the late 15 century DR, often performed with and without lyrics.

History

By the , performances of Bard Dance were common across the streets of Baldur's Gate and the Western Heartlands, along the shores of the Chionthar Valley. Bards and minstrels were sure to sing or play the melody and swiftly attract attention of the crowds.
