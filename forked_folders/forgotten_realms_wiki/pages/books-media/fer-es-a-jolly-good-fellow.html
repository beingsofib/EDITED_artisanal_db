Fer 'e's a Jolly Good Fellow was rowdy sea shanty from the late 14 century DR.

History

In the , Fer 'e's a Jolly Good Fellow was sang at the Vulgar Monkey, a dockside tavern on the island of Brynnlaw in the Nelanther Isles. The shanty was a favorite of Pirate Paul's.
