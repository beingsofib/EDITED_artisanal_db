Stephen Radney-MacFarland

Stephen Radney-MacFarland is a designer previously employed by Wizards of The Coast and Paizo Publishing. He assisted with creation of many live play programs, as well as the DnD 3.5 ruleset, before moving onto Pathfinder content at Paizo Inc..
