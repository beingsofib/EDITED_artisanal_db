Exemplars of Evil is a sourcebook for the 3.5-edition ruleset of for the core setting. It presents concepts for villains in adventures; abilities, feats, and spells for villainous NPCs and PCs; and eight detailed villains at various levels of power, together with adaptation notes for the Forgotten Realms setting. It is not considered canon to the Realms, but is included here for reference.
