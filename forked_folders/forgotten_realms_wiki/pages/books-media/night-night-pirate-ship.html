Night-Night, Pirate Ship was a collection of thematic sea-sailing nursery rhymes, compiled and printed sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.

Contents

One nursery rhyme of note included in the book was called Umberlee Remembers.

History

Night-Night, Pirate Ship could be found in the city of Baldur's Gate and sold by Thomas C. Quirkilious of Chromatic Scales. Another copy belonged to Lora, a sailor who inhabited the Lower City of the Gate.
