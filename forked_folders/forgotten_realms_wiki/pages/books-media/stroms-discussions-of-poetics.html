Strom's Discussions of Poetics was a philosophical book written by Storm Silverhand and published sometime before the destruction of the city of Phlan in the beginning of the 14 century DR.

History

A copy of Strom's Discussions of Poetics was left in Mantor's Library in the city of Phlan as the city fell into ruins during a Rage of Dragons of the . The Heroes of Phlan came across the book in the library's philosophy section in the , during the campaign to liberate the Old City from its master – Tyranthraxus.
