Amber Stewart

Amber Stewart is a freelance game designer. She holds a doctorate in pharmacy and is best known as Shemeshka the Marauder, her preferred pseudonym and online persona. She has written several articles published in the runs of Dragon and Dungeon magazines.

Works

She is most known for her work on other games such as Pathfinder. A number of her contributions to Dragon and Dungeon Magazine relate to the Realms, particularly the greater multiverse:

Dragon magazine

  • D&D's Most Wanted; Preferably Dead, Dragon #359 (September 2007)
  • A Guide to the Demiplanes, Dragon #353 (March 2007)
  • The Gatetown of Esctasy, Dragon #351 (January 2007)

Dungeon Magazine

  • Dergholoth, Dragon #357 (September 2007)
