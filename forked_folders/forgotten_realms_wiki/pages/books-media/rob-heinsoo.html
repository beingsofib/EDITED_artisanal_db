Rob Heinsoo

Rob Heinsoo is a game developer for Wizards of the Coast. He helped write the Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting and the Forgotten Realms Campaign Guide, and is the designer of Three-Dragon Ante, a unique card game. Other Forgotten Realms works include the sourcebook Monsters of Faerûn.
