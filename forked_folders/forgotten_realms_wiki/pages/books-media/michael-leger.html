Michael Leger

Michael Leger is a Senior Design Manager at Blizzard Entertainment, and was a co-author of the contents of the Menzoberranzan boxed set. Along with R.A. Salvatore and Douglas Niles, Leger detailed much information about the various drow houses and their respective NPCs, of the subterranean city of Menzoberranzan. He was thanked in the Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting 2nd edition as a contributor to the game design of the Realms.
