The Animated Series Handbook is a sourcebook by Matthew Sernett that adapts the characters of the Dungeons & Dragons cartoon series of 1983–1985 to the 3.5-edition of . It was published by Wizards of the Coast and released with the Dungeons & Dragons – The Complete Animated Series DVD boxset on December 5, 2006. It presents stats of the main characters; a mini-adventure, "Beneath the Blade of Sword Mountain," designed to lead into the Season 2 episode "The Dragon's Graveyard;" and a map of the Realm (no relation to the Forgotten Realms).
