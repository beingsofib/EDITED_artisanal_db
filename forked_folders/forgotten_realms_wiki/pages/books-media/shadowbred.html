Shadowbred is the first book in the The Twilight War trilogy by Paul S. Kemp. Erevis Cale, struggling to preserve his humanity from a transformation into a shade, struggles alongside Drasek Riven to find and free their friend, Mags, from Sakkors. In doing so, the group slows Netheril's tightening grip on Sembia.
