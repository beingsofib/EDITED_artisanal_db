Shandril's Saga

The Shandril's Saga trilogy by Ed Greenwood covers the adventures of Shandril Shessair. The second book was originally part of the loosely-connected Harpers series, but as the story of Shandril Shessair became more fleshed out and the books became more popular they were joined together as a separate series.

Shandril's Saga

  1. Spellfire (August 1988)
  2. Crown of Fire (April 1994)
  3. Hand of Fire (September 2002)
