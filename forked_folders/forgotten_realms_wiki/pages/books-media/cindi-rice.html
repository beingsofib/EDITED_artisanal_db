Cindi Rice

Cindi Rice is a designer and editor.

In 1995, she began working for TSR, Inc. as a designer and editor for .

When Wizards of the Coast purchased TSR in 1997, she was promoted into the brand management team and put in charge of the D&D campaign settings. By the time Hasbro purchased Wizards of the Coast in 1999, she had also been granted responsibility over business development and licensing for the entire roleplaying game business.

In 2001, Cindi transferred to Hasbro's entertainment division in Los Angeles, where she managed the entertainment rights for all of Hasbro's fantasy, science fiction, action, and horror brands.
