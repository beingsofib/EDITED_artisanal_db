Matthew G. Adkins

Matthew G. Adkins is a contributor to Dungeon magazine, writing adventure modules for 2- and 3-edition D&D.

Works

For the Forgotten Realms, Adkins wrote the following adventures:

  • "Into the Nest of Vipers", Dungeon #75 (July/August 1999)
  • "The Akriloth", Dungeon #79 (March/April 2000)
