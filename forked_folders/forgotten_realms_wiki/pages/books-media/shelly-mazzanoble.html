Shelly Mazzanoble

Shelly Mazzanoble is an author and podcast host. She has two books published by Wizards of the Coast and several articles in Dragon. She was part of the D&D Brand Team for several Dungeons and Dragons board games and was part of the D&D Adventurers League Wizards Team. She co-hosts the official Dungeons and Dragons podcast, Dragon Talk.
