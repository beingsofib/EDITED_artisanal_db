Darkwell, by Douglas Niles, is the third book in the Moonshae trilogy. Niles would later continue the story in the Druidhome trilogy.

Summary

Tristan Kendrick, High King of the Ffolk, forges an alliance with the Northmen, defeats Bhaal and his servants, and marries the druid Robyn, although Moonshae suffers many losses, including the unicorn Kameryn and Tristan’s friend Daryth.
