Unique Mageries was an arcane spellbook penned by Nezram, a wizard who traveled the planes extensively. It contained 21 spells ranging from common to unique or rare.

Description

Unique Mageries was a large tome that measured four handwidths wide by ten handwidths tall. Polished ivory formed both the front and rear cover. Each cover was decorated with inlaid ebony. Each of the 21 pages was gilt-edged and held a single spell.

Powers

Unique Mageries contained the following spells:

magic missile • stinking cloud • wizard lock • dispel magic • feign death • gust of wind • infravision • tongues • water breathing • fear • fumble • polymorph self • remove curse • wall of sand • wizard eye • telekinesis • anti-magic shell • Nezram's ruby ray • spelltrap • power word, blind • spellstrike

History

Nezram left the tome behind on Toril sometime during the 7 century DR when he traveled to another plane and never returned. It was taken from Nezram's tower by the green dragon Chathuulandroth who held it within his lair for 600 years before adventurers from the Black Gauntlet seized it from him. The book was rented to Elminster who copied its spells.
