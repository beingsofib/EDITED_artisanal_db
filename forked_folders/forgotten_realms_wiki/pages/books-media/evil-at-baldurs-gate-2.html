Evil at Baldur's Gate 2

Evil at Baldur's Gate #2: Krydle & Shandie: Honor Among Thieves is a comic published by set in Baldur's Gate. It is the second issue of the Evil at Baldur's Gate series. It was written by Jim Zub and Steven Cummings, and drawn by John Wycough.
