A Song of Elturel was a ballad about the city of Elturel in the latter part of the 15 century DR.

History

It was sung in Elvish by the ghostly elf spirit of the Elfsong Tavern in Baldur's Gate in the , after the descent of Elturel into Avernus. Notably, this was the only time it was heard to change its tune.
