The Fall of Tiamat was a 15 century opera outlined the events of Tiamat's demise, and subsequent banishment to the Nine Hells, at her temple in the Well of Dragons. It was performed in the language of the giants, one of the most-hated foes of all of dragonkind.

Performance

The opera was known to have been performed at the Lightsinger Theater in Waterdeep in 1492 DR.
