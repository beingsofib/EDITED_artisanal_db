Why I Am a Harper was a book written by Belbradyn Tralaer. It was published in 1366 DR.

History

As of 1372 DR, a copy could be found in the Leaves of Learning in Highmoon and the original rested on the shelves of Candlekeep.

Why I Am a Harper referred to one of the Seven Sisters, Storm Silverhand.
