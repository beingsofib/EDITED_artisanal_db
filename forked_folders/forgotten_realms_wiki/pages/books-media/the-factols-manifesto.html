The Factol's Manifesto is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory. It is part of the Planescape campaign setting.

Description

The The Factol's Manifesto was published by TSR, Inc. in 1995. The book is a guide to the factions of Sigil, the City of Doors, and their leaders, known as factols.
