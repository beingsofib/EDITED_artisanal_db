Underworld Speculation

Underworld Speculation is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is an introductory adventure for Xanathar's Guide to Everything. It focuses on Sylgar's fish bowl, and is an adventure designed for 1- to 4 level characters.
