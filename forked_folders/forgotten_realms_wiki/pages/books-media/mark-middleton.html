Mark Middleton

Mark Middleton compiled the four volumes of the Wizard's Spell Compendium and the three volumes of the Priest's Spell Compendium, two series of 2nd edition sourcebooks released in the late 1990s.

Works

  • Wizard's Spell Compendium Volume One (November 1996)
  • Wizard's Spell Compendium Volume Two (January 1998)
  • Wizard's Spell Compendium Volume Three (March 1998)
  • Wizard's Spell Compendium Volume Four (September 1998)
  • Priest's Spell Compendium Volume One (1999)
  • Priest's Spell Compendium Volume Two (1999)
  • Priest's Spell Compendium Volume Three (2000)
