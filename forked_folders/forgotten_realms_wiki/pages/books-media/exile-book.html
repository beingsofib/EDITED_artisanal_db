Exile was a religious tome dedicated to Laduguer the Exile, a duergar deity, penned by the same author who wrote Gnomefather, sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.

Content

Exile was a work of bleak religious fervor, celebrating the god Laduguer, a slaver deity.

History

Circa the , copies of Exile could be found in the city of Baldur's Gate. Some were sold by Nansi Gretta in her bookstore called The Bibliophile in the Lower City. A copy was stored on the bookshelves of the non-denominational shrine – Stormshore Tabernacle, among many other books dedicated to a large number of deities.
