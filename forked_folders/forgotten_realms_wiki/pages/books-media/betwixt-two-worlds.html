Betwixt Two Worlds: A D&D 50th Anniversary Adventure is an audio book by R.A. Salvatore and read by Victor Bevine and released by Audible Originals on October 24, 2024. It focuses on Briennelle Zaharina, the daughter of Drizzt Do'Urden and Catti-brie.
