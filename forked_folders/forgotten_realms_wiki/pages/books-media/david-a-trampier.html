David A. Trampier

David A. Trampier (1954–2014) was an American fantasy illustrator, particularly notable for his contributions to 1st edition Dungeons & Dragons sourcebooks.

Biography

Trampier abruptly stopped producing art in 1988, choosing instead to work as a cab driver in . He passed away in 2014.
