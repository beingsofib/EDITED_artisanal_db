Tempo and Improvisation was a three volume series of reference books that detailed the music of different demihuman races. The first volume focused on the music of Dwarves, the second on Elves, and the third on Halflings.

History

One copy of this tome laid within Bard Keep, a temple of Oghma that stood within the North near Silverymoon.
