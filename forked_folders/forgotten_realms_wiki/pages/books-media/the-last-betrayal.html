The Last Betrayal

"The Last Betrayal" is the sixteenth issue of the Advanced Dungeons &amp; Dragons comics series set in the Forgotten Realms and published by . It was written by Dan Mishkin, drawn by Jan Duursema, and edited by Elliot S. Maggin. It is the fourth and final part of "Spell Games", which related Vajra Valmeyjar's past and relationship to Conner and revealed the mystery of the cat child.
