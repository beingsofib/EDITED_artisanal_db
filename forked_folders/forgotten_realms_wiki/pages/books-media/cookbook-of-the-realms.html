Cookbook of the Realms was a series of thick eccentric chapbook-cookbooks published in Faerûn in the 15 century DR.

Contents

Some of the recipes included in the latest issue of the Cookbook of the Realms as of the late 15 century DR included directions on how to cook cockatrice and a dessert that required self-dividing, swashbuckling gelatinous cubes for its preparation.
