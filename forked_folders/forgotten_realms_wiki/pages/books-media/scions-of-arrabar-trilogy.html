Scions of Arrabar trilogy

The Scions of Arrabar Trilogy is a trilogy of novels by Thomas M. Reid. The novels cover the deadly feuds between and among the powerful families of Arrabar in Chondath.

The Scions of Arrabar Trilogy

  1. The Sapphire Crescent (November 2003)
  2. The Ruby Guardian (November 2004)
  3. The Emerald Scepter (August 2005)
