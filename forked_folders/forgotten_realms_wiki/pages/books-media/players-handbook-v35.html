Description

In 2003, after two years of player feedback, the 3-edition rules were revised to the 3.5-edition ruleset. Revisions in the Player's Handbook v.3.5 included the classes becoming more balanced against each other. Player's Handbook v.3.5 was reprinted as a premium edition on September 18, 2012.
