Traveler's Guide to Silverymoon – Inns, Taverns, and Local Law was a guidebook to the city of Silverymoon, the Gem of the North, that came out from under the quills of two authors, Imgath Gland and Sagreth Kormun sometime before the late 14 century DR.

History

A copy of Traveler's Guide to Silverymoon belonged to Manshoon XII, a clone of Manshoon, in the . The book was stored in the mage's library of mundane and arcane tomes in his lair in an abandoned temple of Amaunator underneath the City of the Dead of Waterdeep.
