Dungeons & Dragons Online/The Haunted Halls of Eveningstar

The Haunted Halls of Eveningstar is an adventure pack for Dungeons & Dragons Online where the player characters revisit the classic Haunted Halls adventure. The adventure pack was added to the game on March 10, 2014 as part of Update 21.

The adventure is narrated by Ed Greenwood, the original creator of the Haunted Halls, and features optional voiceover commentary from him about the dungeon.
