Counterfeit Dreams

Counterfeit Dreams is a Living City adventure for Dungeons & Dragons 2 edition, intended for a group of 5 to 7 adventurers, written by Jean Rabe and Skip Williams. Originally printed for Gen Con '89, it was the third ever adventure in the Living City organized play program. It saw a reprint two months later in the pages of Polyhedron #50 and a third reprint nine years later as part of the Adventurer's Guild program.
