The Legend of Drizzt: Starless Night (graphic novel)

The Legend of Drizzt: Starless Night (original title: Forgotten Realms: The Legend of Drizzt, Volume 8: Starless Night) is the graphic novel version of R.A. Salvatore's novel Starless Night. It was originally published as a single issue.
