Penny Williams

Penny Williams was "Game Questions Expert" for TSR, Inc. in the 1980s. She served as RPGA Network Coordinator, Polyhedron Newszine editor, and Senior Editor and Coordinating Editor for the RPG R&D Department at Wizards of the Coast. She then became a freelancer, editing for several game companies, and running the online playtesting program for Wizards products.
