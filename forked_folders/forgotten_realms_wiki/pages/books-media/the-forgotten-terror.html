The Forgotten Terror by William W. Connors is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons adventure module set in the Ravenloft campaign settings. It is a sequel to the Forgotten Realms adventure Castle Spulzeer and continues the story of Chardath Spulzeer in the domain of Aggarath.
