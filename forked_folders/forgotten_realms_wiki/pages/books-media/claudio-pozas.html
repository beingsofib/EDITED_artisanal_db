Claudio Pozas

Claudio Pozas is an artist and designer known for his fantasy and science fiction illustrations.

Claudio has created several images for 4 and 5 edition Dungeons & Dragons material. His work has been shown in both Dragon magazine and various sourcebooks. As well as providing art, he was involved in the core design of a couple of 4 edition sourcebooks. Pozas has been a Dungeons & Dragons enthusiast since 1987.
