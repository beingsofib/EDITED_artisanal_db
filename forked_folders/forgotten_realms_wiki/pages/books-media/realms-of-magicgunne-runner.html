"Gunne Runner" is a short story by Roger E. Moore that appeared in the Realms of Magic anthology of stories from the Forgotten Realms, published in December 1995.

Summary

In Waterdeep, the wizard Formathio and watch captain Ardrun investigate the murder of the Yellow Mage and uncover a smokepowder-weapon smuggling operation. They find it's connected to a small scro spelljamming fleet that arrived in Realmspace and is and trading firearms to humanoid races in Faerûn through contacts in Waterdeep.
