And So They Met the Gargoyle was a type of stage performance called "grand play", popularized in Athkatla of Amn in the late 14 century DR.

History

By the late 14 century DR, And So They Met the Gargoyle was considered a classic. The performance was penned by Zarghar, Playwright of Athkatla. The "grand play" was in the repertoire of the famous The Crown Aflame theater in Athkatla's Temple District.
