Lawrence Schick

Lawrence Schick is an author and game designer who wrote for multiple Dungeons & Dragons modules originally published between 1979 and 1981, as well as one of the contributors to the Fiend Folio. He is credited with the creation of the aarakocra, the kelpie, the khargra, and the tabaxi.
