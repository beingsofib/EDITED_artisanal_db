Special Delivery

Special Delivery is an adventure module set in the Living City organized play program in 1994, then later published once more as part of the Adventurer's Guild organized play program in 1998.

Index

Index based on the Living City edition; pregenerated player characters are from the Adventurer's Guild version. It is assumed the events therein are the ones adequate for the characters as designed in the AG version, with alternatives considered to be referenced only. This procedure is subject to change upon further availability of information.
