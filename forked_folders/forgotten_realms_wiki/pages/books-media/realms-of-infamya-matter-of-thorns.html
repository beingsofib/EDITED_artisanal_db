"A Matter of Thorns" is a short story by James M. Ward included in the 1994 anthology Realms of Infamy.

Summary

After the newly-arisen young Lord Stone kills the long-serving gardener of Castle Stone, a massive and malevolent rose bush rises out of the castle's gardens.
