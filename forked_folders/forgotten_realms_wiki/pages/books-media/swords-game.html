Swords (game)

Swords was a four-player card game enjoyed in northwest Faerûn circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR. It was often a gambling game and was offered at the High Flagon gambling hall and inn just outside Waterdeep.

Set-Up and Play

The game of Swords used a 25-card deck made of four suits of six cards each, plus one card called the Throne or Castle.
