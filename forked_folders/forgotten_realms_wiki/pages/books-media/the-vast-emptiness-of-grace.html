The Vast Emptiness of Grace

The Vast Emptiness of Grace is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Avernus Rising series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Avernus, and is an adventure designed for 11- to 16-level characters. It is part three of the Call for Aid series.
