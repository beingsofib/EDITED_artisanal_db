The Spectrum of Multiversal Beliefs was a collection of six volumes of scholarly musings on the philosophies of several factions found outside of the Prime Material plane.

Description

These six tomes were all written in Common and discussed in depth the conflicting philosophies of various planar cultures.

History

The Spectrum of Multiversal Beliefs's last recorded location was on the Crystal Labyrinth level of the mega-dungeon underneath the streets of Waterdeep, Undermountain. The tomes were among the valuables of a githyanki named Urlon, guarded by his crystal golems in 1492 DR.
