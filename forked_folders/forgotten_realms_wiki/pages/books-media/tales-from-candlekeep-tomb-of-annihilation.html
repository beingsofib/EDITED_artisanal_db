Tales from Candlekeep: Tomb of Annihilation is a turn-based RPG by which is based off the Dungeons & Dragons Adventure System series of board games. It was released on the 11th of October 2017 on the platform. The game was withdrawn from Steam on the 20th of May 2022 and is no longer available for purchase.

Plot

Artus Cimber, Dragonbait, Asharra and Birdsong attempt to end the death curse by travelling through the lands of Chult and defeating Acererak and his ally Ras Nsi.
