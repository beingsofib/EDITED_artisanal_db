Newton H. Ewell

Newton H. Ewell is a designer, developer, editor, cartographer, and artist of Dungeons & Dragons material, from sourcebooks to accessories. He particularly focused on the Forgotten Realms and Spelljammer campaign settings, but worked on several others as well. Ewell is personally credited with designing many ships and maps of Realmspace. He also provided art for Dragon magazine, but not for the Realms or a related campaign setting.

He is a lover of tabletop gaming, and names Dungeons & Dragons as his favourite game of all time.

Works

  • Wild Things (developer, editor)
  • Monstrous Compendium Spelljammer Appendix 1 (designer)
  • Realmspace (interior artist)
  • Monstrous Compendium Spelljammer Appendix 2 (designer)
  • Maztica Campaign Set (interior artist)
  • The Drow of the Underdark (editor)
  • The Ruins of Myth Drannor (editor)
  • The Great Glacier (editor)
  • Pirates of the Fallen Stars (editor)
