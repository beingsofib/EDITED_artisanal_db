The Vilhon Reach is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons box-set for the Forgotten Realms.

Description

The Vilhon Reach focuses on the Vilhon Reach area and its nations. The book also details the Emerald Enclave, a loosely organized group of druids, clerics, bards, and others which exerts a heavy influence on the region.
