"The Canopic Being" is a short adventure module for 13–level characters, collected in the anthology Candlekeep Mysteries, where players delve into a tomb associated with a devotee of the god Savras.

Synopsis

In 1492 DR, a band of adventurers begin to investigate the disappearance of a missing sage of Candlekeep, Great Reader A'lai Aivenmore. Their journey takes them to the House of the All-Seeing Orb temple in the far southern city of Tashluta.
