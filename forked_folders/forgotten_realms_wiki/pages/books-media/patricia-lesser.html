Patricia Lesser

Patricia "Pattie" Lesser is an editor from California who co-wrote "The Role of Computers" column for Dragon from 1986 to 1987 with her husband Hartley Lesser and was joined by their son

After the column ended with Dragon #196, the three went on to write the "Mac Attack!" column for Mac Home Journal. Pattie then worked as an advertising editor for the magazine inCider. After that, she worked for a time for and then moved on to develop video game strategy guides for . Hartley and Pattie currently work as editors for Satnews Publishers, where they have been since 2006.
