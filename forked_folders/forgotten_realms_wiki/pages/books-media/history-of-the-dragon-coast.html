The History of the Dragon Coast was a book that detailed the storied history of the Dragon Coast region of Faerûn. It was written by a famously erudite pirate who lived near Nathlan.

Description

The single volume book went over a broad overview of the Dragon Coast region, detailing the various malevolent organizations that operated within its walls. It detailed some of the city's rulers, including pirate lords and kings in the 12 and 13 centuries DR.

Copes were bound in black leather.

Notable Owners

  • Oberan, the Halruaan wizard that lived in the city of Baldur's Gate in the mid—14 century DR.
