Neverwinter Nights: Darkness over Daggerford is a premium module for Neverwinter Nights: Enhanced Edition.

Development

Darkness over Daggerford was originally developed by as a premium module for Neverwinter Nights. While the module was in development, abruptly canceled its premium module program in 2006. Darkness over Daggerford was canceled as a result, but Ossian still opted to give the module an unofficial release by uploading it on a popular fan page. In 2018, nearly twelve years later, Darkness over Daggerford was officially published as a premium module for the Enhanced Edition of Neverwinter Nights.
