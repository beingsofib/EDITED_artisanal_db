Snow Dwarf and the Seven Wights was a type of storybook, copies of which could be found in Barovia.

Description

Copies of this book were bound in leather.

Notable Owners

  • A copy of this book could be found among the nonmagical books kept by the pharaoh Martek within his tomb.
