Dungeons & Dragons Online/Eveningstar Challenge Pack

The Eveningstar Challenge Pack is an adventure pack for Dungeons & Dragons Online where the players can face small combat challenges around Eveningstar in order to gain access to fantastical treasures. The adventure pack was added to the game on June 25, 2012 as part of Update 14, and supplements the concurrent Menace of the Underdark expansion.

Plot

Dryad Grove: The Great Tree involves defending the dryadic Grove of the Great Tree in the King's Forest at the behest of Ravanys Woodsoul, which is under heavy attack by malevolent drow forces. The grove must be protected while it builds up enough energy to blast away the drow and reinvigorate the land.

Dryad Grove: Defenseless has the player repeating a similar feat, though without any supporting defensive trees as the drow forces converge on the Great Tree.

Ruined Keep: The Sunset Ritual sends the player via Yenward Chorster to the ruined keep in the King's Forest, where the War Wizard Arganon Pine is performing a ritual to turn away threats. Before he can complete it, he must be protected from a small army of bandits and werewolves that seek to stop him.

Ruined Keep: The Daybreak Ritual has the player aiding the wizard Zenyff Bravehand as he performs a second ritual at the ruined keep, only to be attacked by waves of furious natural and druidic forces.

Underdark Arena: Fight to the Finish is a gladiatorial fighting pit represented by the duergar agent Djir Garpeth, run by Azena'ar the drider for House Cay'Vorym.

Underdark Arena: Ring of Fire follows from the previous, and has the red dragon Aptoryx get involved. Having taken an interest in the sport of death matches, Aptoryx got his own arena and filled it up with lava.
