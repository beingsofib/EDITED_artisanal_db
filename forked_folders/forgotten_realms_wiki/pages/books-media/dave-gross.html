Dave Gross

Dave Gross is a game designer, editor, and author of several fantasy novels.

Career

Gross was an English teacher and technical writer before he came to work for TSR, Inc., where he served as editor of . He worked on several gaming magazines for TSR, Wizards of the Coast, and Paizo Publishing. In addition to Dungeons & Dragons, Gross has written fiction for the and game settings.
