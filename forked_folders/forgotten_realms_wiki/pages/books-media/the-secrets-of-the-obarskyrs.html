The Secrets of the Obarskyrs was a Chapbook owned by the tiefling bounty hunter Havilar.

Description

Havilar's boyfriend, Aubrin Crownsilver, was the son of an Obarskyr bastard. She enjoyed reading about Aubrin's "crazy entertaining" family.
