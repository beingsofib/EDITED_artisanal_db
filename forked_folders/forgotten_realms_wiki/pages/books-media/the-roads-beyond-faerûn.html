The Roads Beyond Faerûn was a book written by the Sage of Tashluta, Iyritar Sarsharm. It was published in 1360 DR.

History

As of 1372 DR, the original could be found on the shelves of Candlekeep.

The Roads Beyond Faerûn possibly referred to one of the Seven Sisters, Anastra Syluné Silverhand.
