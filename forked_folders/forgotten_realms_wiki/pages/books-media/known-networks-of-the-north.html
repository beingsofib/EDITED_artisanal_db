Known Networks of the North was a book detailing the various thief and spy networks that operated in the North during the 13 century DR. By the following century its information was considered to be very out of date.

Description

This book had a red cover.

History

A copy of each volume in this series laid within one of the reading rooms of Bard Keep, a library and temple of Oghma that stood within the North near Silverymoon.
