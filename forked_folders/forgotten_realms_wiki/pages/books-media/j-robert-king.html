J. Robert King

J. Robert King is a novelist and game designer who has contributed to a variety of sourcebooks from TSR, Inc.

Career

King has written over twenty novels, including three of the books in the Double Diamond Triangle Saga. He also contributed to Monstrous Compendium Forgotten Realms Appendix II and Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue in addition to other campaign settings from TSR.
