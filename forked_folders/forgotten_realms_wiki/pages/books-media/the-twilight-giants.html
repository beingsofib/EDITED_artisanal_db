The Twilight Giants

The Twilight Giants series is a group of three novels written by Troy Denning and Published by TSR, Inc. from 1994–1995 and then reprinted in 2005 by Wizards of the Coast. The novels take place in a little known part of the North called Hartsvale.

The books are:

  1. The Ogre's Pact (September 1994)
  2. The Giant Among Us (February 1995)
  3. The Titan of Twilight (September 1995)
