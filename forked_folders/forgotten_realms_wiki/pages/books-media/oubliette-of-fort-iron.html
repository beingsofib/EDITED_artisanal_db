Oubliette of Fort Iron

Oubliette of Fort Iron is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Elemental Evil series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Point Iron, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1- to 4-level characters.
