Musings on Mortality was a book penned by Ulvryn Hamdarakh, Sage of Saelmur, in 1284 DR.

Contents

Musings on Mortality was a collection of ponderings and ruminations on the nature of life, the purpose of the said life, great deeds, and how they were achieved. All the things that are done to entertain the mighty gods.
