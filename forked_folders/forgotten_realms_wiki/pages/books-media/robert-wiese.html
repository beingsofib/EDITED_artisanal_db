Robert Wiese

Robert Wiese is a biochemist and a former game designer for Wizards of the Coast.

Career

Wiese was a long-time player in the RPGA who eventually worked for the RPGA for seven years. He has written over 60 adventures, several articles, and several web enhancements for Forgotten Realms sourcebooks.

He left the gaming industry and, as of 2005, was working as a biochemist for the University of Nevada.
