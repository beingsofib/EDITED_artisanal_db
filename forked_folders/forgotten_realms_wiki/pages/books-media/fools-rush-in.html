Fools Rush In

Fools Rush In was the fourth issue of the Forgotten Realms comics series published by . It was written by Jeff Grubb, drawn by Rags Morales, and edited by Elliot S. Maggin. It is the fourth and final installment in the "The Hand of Vaprak!" story arc.
