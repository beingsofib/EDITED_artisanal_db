Calimport is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms.

Description

This accessory provides a comprehensive guide to the city itself, featuring detailed maps, descriptions of its districts, notable locations, and key non-player characters. The intricate history, politics, and factions that shape the city are also explored.

In addition, this accessory presents a wide range of adventure hooks, quests, and plotlines tailored specifically for Calimport.

Calimport also includes new character options, such as unique kits, spells, magical items, and other resources.
