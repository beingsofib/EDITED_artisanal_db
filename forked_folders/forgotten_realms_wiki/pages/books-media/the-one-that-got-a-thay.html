The One that Got A Thay was a piece of ahistorical smutty romance fiction found in the Western Heartlands.

Contents

The book contained a slow-paced, enemies-to-lovers story between an acerbic Thayan wizard and his arch-nemesis, a musclebound pure-of-heart berserker.

History

A copy was kept in the library of the Sharess' Caress brothel in 1492 DR.
