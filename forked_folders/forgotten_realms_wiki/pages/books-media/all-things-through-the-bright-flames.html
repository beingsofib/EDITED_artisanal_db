All Things Through the Bright Flames is an ebook novella by Ed Greenwood, set in the Forgotten Realms, released in late 2016.

Originally released on the DM's Guild as-is, this novella was taken off the website with no explanation, only to be replaced six days later with a Dungeons & Dragons rules appendix included. Ed Greenwood commented, "Wizards wants it wearing the full glory of its game Realmslore! Stitching glitter, back up soon!"{{cite web
