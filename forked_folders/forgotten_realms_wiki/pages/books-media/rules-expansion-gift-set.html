The Rules Expansion Gift Set is a boxed set containing three sourcebooks and one Dungeon Master's screen for 5-edition Dungeons & Dragons. Two of the sourcebooks are updated versions of older rule sourcebooks, namely Tasha's Cauldron of Everything and Xanathar's Guide to Everything. The third, Mordenkainen Presents: Monsters of the Multiverse, collects and revises hundreds of monster stat blocks and thirty player race options from several previously published sources.
