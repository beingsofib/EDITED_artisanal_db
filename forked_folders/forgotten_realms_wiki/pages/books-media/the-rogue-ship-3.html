The Rogue Ship 3

The Rogue Ship 3 was the third issue of the Spelljammer comics series published by . It was written by Barbara Kesel, drawn by Michael Collins, and edited by Elliot S. Maggin. It is the third of four installments in the "Rogue Ship" story arc.
