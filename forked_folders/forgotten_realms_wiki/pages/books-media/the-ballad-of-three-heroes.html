The Ballad of Three Heroes

BookIU
nameThe Ballad of Three Heroes
typeBallad
locationTen-Towns, Icewind Dale

The Ballad of Three Heroes was a song that, when performed by bards, was imbued with beneficial magics.

Content

When performed by a bard, the song's magic guided the weapons of the singer's allies towards their goal. The swords cut deeper, and maces hit harder. Additionally, those who were inspired by the ballad received the boon of improved defenses and reactions.

History

By the , The Ballad of Three Heroes was very common among bards of middling skills. It was heard being performed in the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale and the village of Kuldahar.
