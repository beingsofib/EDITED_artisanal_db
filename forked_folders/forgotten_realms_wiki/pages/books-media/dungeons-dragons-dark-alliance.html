Dungeons & Dragons: Dark Alliance is a video game developed by and distributed by Wizards of the Coast, released on June 22, 2021. The game is set in the Icewind Dale region.

Story

After Akar Kessell's army is defeated 1356 DR, hordes of powerful creatures invade the northern region of Icewind Dale in an attempt to locate the Crystal Shard.

Gameplay

The game includes both single-player and multiplayer modes. In the single-player mode, the player can choose any of the four characters to control, and swap between them. The multiplayer mode allows for online co-op for up to four players.

History

In March 2019, Tuque Games announced that it was developing a Dungeons & Dragons game in partnership with Wizards of the Coast. The game was officially announced with a teaser trailer shown during The Game Awards 2019 on December 12, 2019.
