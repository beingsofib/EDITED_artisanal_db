The Standing Stone was a song about the titular Standing Stone, recorded in Myth Drannor sometime before the Weeping War, and later recovered in the late 14 century DR. It was separate from Standing Stone, the version of the song later produced by Finder Wyvernspur

Contents

The song described the bright future awaiting Myth Drannor thanks to Coronal Eltargrim Irithyl and the Dales Compact.

History

A written copy of the song from before the Weeping War was found in the ruins of the Myth Drannor House of Song when the Elves of Cormanthor and Eilistraee's Wanderers arrived to reclaim the city. The drow bard Zelliira in particular investigated the temple where it was kept.
