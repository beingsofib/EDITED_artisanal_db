Mary H. Herbert

Mary H. Herbert (born 1957) is an American author who wrote several novels for TSR's Dragonlance setting and five books in her Dark Horse series. Her contribution to the Forgotten Realms is limited to three short stories released in miscellaneous anthologies.

Forgotten Realms Bibliography

Short stories

  • "Thieves' Honor" in Realms of Infamy (1994)
  • "Thieves' Reward" in Realms of Magic (1995)
  • "Thieves' Justice" in Realms of Mystery (1998)
