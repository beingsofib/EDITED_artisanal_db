Caravan (adventure)

Caravan is the first tournament adventure module set in the Living City organized play program. It debuted at Gen Con 20, which ran from August 20–23, 1987, and was later published in Polyhedron #66, then later published once more as part of the Adventurer's Guild organized play program in 1998. Its story is continued in At Last, Ravens Bluff.
