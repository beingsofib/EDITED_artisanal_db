King under the Hill is an encounter published in Dungeons & Dragons: Adventurer #1. The encounter was the first in the series of encounters comprising Adventure #1.

Summary

The Stonehill Inn was assaulted by a swarm of cranium rats. The creatures were pushed by unknown forces to acquire the brightblade interred within the tomb of the human chieftain buried beneath the inn's foundation. The adventurers came in time to save the staff and guests of the inn.
