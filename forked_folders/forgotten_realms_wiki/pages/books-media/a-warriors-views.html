A Warrior's Views was a book penned by Galgarr Thormspur and published in 400 DR.

Contents

A Warrior's Views was a collection of musings of Galgarr Thormspur, Marshal of Maligh. He expressed his opinions on poor career choices among many adventurers in the Realms.

A copy of A Warrior's Views was kept in the library-citadel of Candlekeep in the late-14 century DR.
