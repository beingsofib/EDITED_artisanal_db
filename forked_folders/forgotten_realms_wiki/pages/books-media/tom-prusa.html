Tom Prusa

Tom Prusa is a game designer who has worked for TSR, Inc..

Description

Many of Prusa's works involved designing game material for the Forgotten Realms and Al-Qadim campaign settings. For a time, Prusa also ran Living Jungle, one of the RPGA campaigns.
