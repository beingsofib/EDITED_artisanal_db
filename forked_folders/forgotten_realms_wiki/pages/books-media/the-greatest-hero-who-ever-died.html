"The Greatest Hero Who Ever Died" is a short story by J. Robert King, first published in the 1994 anthology Realms of Infamy, and reprinted in the 2003 anthology The Best of the Realms.

Summary

A mysterious stranger travels to the isolated settlement of Capel Curig and shares the story of a valiant knight whose exploits seem too grand to be entirely true. When confronted by the wizard Dorsoom, the knight reveals the truth of the matter.
