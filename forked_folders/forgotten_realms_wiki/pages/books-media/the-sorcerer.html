The Sorcerer by Troy Denning is the third novel in the Return of the Archwizards trilogy.

Summary

Tilverton has been laid to waste by the Shadovar forces, but the Shadovar paid a steep price for their victory, as the Cormyrian war wizards obliterated many of their numbers. The twisted Seraph of Lies, servant of Cyric continues to plot to perform his master's will. The Chosen of Mystra and the elves of Evereska and Evermeet must make their final stand, and trust in a tortured and twisted former Tomb Guard and wielder of shadow magic.
