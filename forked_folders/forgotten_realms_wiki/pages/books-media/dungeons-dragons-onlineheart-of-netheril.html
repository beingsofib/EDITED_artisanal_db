Dungeons & Dragons Online/Heart of Netheril

Heart of Netheril is the title of a cancelled adventure pack for Dungeons & Dragons Online initially planned to conclude the Netheril storyline in the Forgotten Realms, introduced in Shadowfell Conspiracy. It would have seen players exploring the desert of Anauroch and the ancient Netherese city of Thultanthar, facing off with Netheril's strongest wizards, the alien phaerimm, and possibly even the dread Tarrasque itself.
