Shawn Merwin

Shawn Merwin is a freelance game designer who has produced and written material for Wizards of the Coast, including spending time as one of the Global Administrators of the Living Forgotten Realms campaign. He has written extensively in the Forgotten Realms in third edition, fourth edition, and fifth edition supplements and periodicals. He is currently the Executive Lead Designer for Ghostfire Gaming.
