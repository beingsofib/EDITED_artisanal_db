Five (Or Six) Heroes was a children's book published sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.

Content

Five (Or Six) Heroes was a book written in simple and easy to understand style aimed at the young. Its protagonists was a group of unlikely heroes tho came together in defense of a forest gnome village that fell under a marauding gnoll pack's attack. In the end, the heroes successfully repelled the attack, sent the gnoll forces fleeing, and a comedic relief – a group's sidekick, married a gnome beekeeper from the village.

History

By the , Five (Or Six) Heroes book was already known in Baldur's Gate. A copy could be found in Nortale's Hostel in the Lower City, no doubt brought to the Gate by one of the hostel's refugee guests.
