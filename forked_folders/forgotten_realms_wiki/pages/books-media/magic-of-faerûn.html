Magic of Faerûn is a sourcebook published in August 2001 for the Forgotten Realms campaign setting using the 3-edition Dungeons & Dragons ruleset and written by Sean K. Reynolds, Duane Maxwell, and Angel McCoy. It covers many magical matters of the Realms.
