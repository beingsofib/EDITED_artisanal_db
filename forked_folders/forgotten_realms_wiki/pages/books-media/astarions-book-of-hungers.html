Astarion's Book of Hungers is a digital-only supplement for the Forgotten Realms using the 5-edition (revised) ruleset published by Wizards of the Coast. It is set to be released in November 2025 alongside Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn and Forgotten Realms: Adventures in Faerûn. It covers vampires and Baldur's Gate III tie-ins. It is included in the Forgotten Realms Ultimate Bundle on D&D Beyond along with Netheril's Fall and Lorwyn: First Light.
