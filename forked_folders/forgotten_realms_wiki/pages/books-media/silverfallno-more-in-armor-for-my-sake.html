No More in Armor for My Sake is a novella by Ed Greenwood first published in the 1999 anthology Silverfall.

Summary

Mirt meets Dove Falconhand and warns her of drow infiltrating Scornubel disguised as surface dwellers. Dove investigates in the guise of a drow noble, finding a drow trader named Sarltan, but she is exposed and forced to kill a whole group of drow. After the battle, she finds Sarltan has been killed with a magic-dead dagger. Dove decides that a drow expert is needed to continue the investigation.
