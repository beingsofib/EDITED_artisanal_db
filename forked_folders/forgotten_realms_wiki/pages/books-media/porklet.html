Porklet was a play penned by a famed half-elven poet and playwright Archibald Ravenshade in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Porklet was a story about an irresolute man who wrestled with a decision to murder his evil uncle.

History

As of the , Porklet was immortalized in the form of a painting by Oskar Fevras. The image, painted in the late 15 century DR, depicted Archibald Ravenshade, surrounded by two of the Vanthampur brothers, as the half-elf unveiled a review of a theatrical production of Porklet. The painting, simply named The Vanthampur Brothers, was stored in Oskar's studio in Lady Wisteria Jannath's Lower City manse.
