Giantcraft is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms.

Description

Giantcraft details the giants of Faerûn and is the is the 7 book in the FOR series.

This accessory includes detailed information on giants as characters and as a race in the Realms. It provides background lore, cultural details, and specific rules for creating and playing giant characters. It covered various types of giants like hill giants, frost giants, fire giants, and others, each with their unique traits and societal structures.

Giantcraft adds new spells, magical items, and equipment relevant to giants, enhancing the options available to players and Dungeon Masters when incorporating these beings into their campaigns. Additionally, it featured adventure modules or campaign settings where giants play a central role, offering ready-to-use material for game sessions focused on giants.
