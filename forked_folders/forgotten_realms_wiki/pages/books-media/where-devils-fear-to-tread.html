Where Devils Fear to Tread

Where Devils Fear to Tread is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Avernus Rising series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Avernus, and is an adventure designed for 17- to 20-level characters. It is part two of the Red Hunt series.
