The North Wind was a broadsheet based in Waterdeep on the Sword Coast North. It was published during the late 15 century DR.

Contents

This broadsheet featured illustrations of voguish garments and attractive models. It also contained gossips covering the life of high society, reporting on the life of eligible bachelors, noble maidens, and wealthy merchants.
