Escape from Undermountain by Mark Anthony is the third novel in the loosely related series The Nobles.

Summary

The half-orc thief Artek Ar'talen, called 'the Knife', is hired by Lord Darien Thal to rescue Lord Corin Silvertor from Undermountain.

Here he meets the wizard Beckla Shadesar, who agrees to help him.

Together, they locate the young nobleman, and the three of them battle their way through the Halls of Halaster.
