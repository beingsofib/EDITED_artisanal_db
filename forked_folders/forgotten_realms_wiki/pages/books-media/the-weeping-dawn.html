The Weeping Dawn was a sad melodramatic ballad written by the tiefling bard Alfira and dedicated to her perished teacher in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Alfira and her teacher, Lihala, were traveling near the Risen Road when they were beset by a pack of gnolls. They did not hear the fearsome creatures begin their attack, and unfortunately, Lihala paid the ultimate price. Alfira managed to escape and return to safety at the Emerald Grove, under the protection of its druids. Distraught over the loss, Alfira wrote The Weeping Dawn to mourn her teacher.

Some time later, the Weeping Dawn was among the songs guests could perform at the Fortune's Wheel casino in the extraplanar city of Sigil. Aggy Farseer sang it during a karaoke performance.
