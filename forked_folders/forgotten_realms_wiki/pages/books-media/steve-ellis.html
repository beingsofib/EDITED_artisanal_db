Steve Ellis

Steve Ellis is an artist who has worked on 3 and 4 edition Dungeons & Dragons material.

Works

Ellis has produced art for comics, magazines, board games, and sourcebooks, many of which are set in the Forgotten Realms. Steve was the cover artist of the Cutter and Dungeons & Dragons: Forgotten Realms comics, an issue of Dragon magazine, and the Tyrants of the Underdark board game.
