The Truth of Hearts is an adventure module from the RPGA Legacy of the Green Regent campaign. It is the first of a four-module series called the Tides of War.

Plot

A spy who had been collecting information on the defences of Loudwater for the last two years has left town. He needs to be captured before he can inform the city's enemies.
