"The Mermaid" was a sailor's song about a mermaid and a doomed ship, performed and enjoyed in Faerûn.

History

The song was performed as a duet by the bard Lunasa Eveningwood and her companion Merlin Fastfoot while they stayed at Beckside Castle in Cormyr one night during the late 15 century DR. They also sang Here's a Health to the Company and The Crafty Maid's Policy together.
