Tomás Giorello

Tomás Giorello is an Argentinian artist and illustrator whose work has appeared in Dungeons & Dragons products from 3.5 to 5 edition. Outside of his work for Wizards of the Coast, Tomás has worked as a cover artist, inker, and penciller for various comic books.
