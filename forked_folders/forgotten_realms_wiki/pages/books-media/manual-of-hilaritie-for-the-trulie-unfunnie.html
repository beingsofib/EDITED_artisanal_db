Manual of Hilaritie for the Trulie Unfunnie was a reference book for aspiring comedians from the city of Baldur's Gate and printed sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.

Content

The book was flooded with misspellings, including its title. The handbook included a "A Thorough Indecks of Hilarious Topicks," which consisted of "Punnes, air emitted from the southernmost hole, coincidence, a funnie face, publick houses and their vagaries, pratfalls and other fysical prancks, funnie little creatures, the misfortune of the other," and lastly, "stinck."

History

In the , Manual of Hilaritie for the Trulie Unfunnie could be found in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, upon one of the Elfsong Tavern tables, during the Laff Riot, a stand-up comedy contest hosted by Harvard Willoughby.
