A Seat That Draws Blood: Ten Years a Guildmaster was a book of personal and professional accords penned by Jalaerho Kloskurmrundur, Guildmaster Emeritus of the Stationers' Guild of Waterdeep in the late 14 century DR.

Contents

The book was published in the . On its pages, the Guildmaster of the Stationers' Hall guild in the City of Splendors recounted his career and musings of ten years he had held the position. Jalaerho Kloskurmrundur showed his unfavorable attitude towards change and rebellious youth and stressed the importance of business and coin it brought.
