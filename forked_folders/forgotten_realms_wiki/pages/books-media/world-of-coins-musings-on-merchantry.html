World of Coins: Musings on Merchantry was a book penned by Tarth Ammarander, Sage of Athkatla, Amn, published in 1345 DR.

Contents

The World of Coins was a collection of thoughts and meditations on the mercantile profession and the elaborate but necessary set of skills required to be successful in that vocation. The book discussed the skill of weaving falsehoods and its possible dangers and downfalls.
