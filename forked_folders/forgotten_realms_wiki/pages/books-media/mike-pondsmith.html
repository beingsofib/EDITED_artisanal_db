Mike Pondsmith

Mike Pondsmith is a prolific game designer—including role-playing, video, and board games—who has written sourcebooks for both the Forgotten Realms and Oriental Adventures settings.

Background

Pondsmith graduated from with a Bachelor of Arts degree in graphic design and a Bachelor of Science degree in behavioral psychology. He started out in the video game industry before moving into pen-and-paper games. He founded , a role-playing company in 1985. He contributed to TSR, Inc. products for a brief period a few years after that.
