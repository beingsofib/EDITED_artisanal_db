Stephen Inniss

Stephen Inniss is an author who contributed to many Dragon magazine articles.

Description

Stephen specialised in writing about creatures, and was noted for creating the lillend creature.

In addition to his work in magazines, he contributed to the Greyhawk adventure, Fate of Istus.

Works

Stephen's articles were featured in several magazines, including:
