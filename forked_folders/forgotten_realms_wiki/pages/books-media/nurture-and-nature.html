Nurture and Nature is an adventure module from the RPGA Legacy of the Green Regent campaign.

Plot

Another caravan has been attacked along the road to Secomber. Four sun elves who were riding as passengers were kidnapped, one of whom is apparently the twin sister of a distraught young sun elf woman who begs the party to rescue her.
