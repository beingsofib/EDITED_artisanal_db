Mike Breault

Mike Breault is a game designer and editor who has worked for TSR, Inc.

Education

Breault was trained as a physicist from in 1980. As a freshman, he joined a role-playing game club. He attended graduate school at , during which time he also worked as an editorial intern.

Career

Breault joined TSR as an editor in September of 1984. He worked on several 1-edition sourcebooks and on 2-edition works as well. He was co-designer of the Pool of Radiance video game.
