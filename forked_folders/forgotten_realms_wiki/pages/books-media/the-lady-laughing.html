The Lady Laughing was a ballad penned sometime before the early 14 century DR.

Contents

The Lady Laughing was one of many ballads in Mintiper Moonsilver's repertoire. The Company of Crazed Venturers was present at the famed bard Mintiper's performance in Neverwinter sometime in the early-to-mid 14 century DR, during which he performed The Lady Laughing.
