An Old Warrior's Way was book penned by Dathlance of Selgaunt in the late 13 century DR.

History

An Old Warrior's Way was a tome that collected musings and life lessons of a warrior from Sembia, Dathlance. It was published in the . Another copy was kept in the War Library of the Abbey of the Sword in Battledale as of the late 14 century DR.
