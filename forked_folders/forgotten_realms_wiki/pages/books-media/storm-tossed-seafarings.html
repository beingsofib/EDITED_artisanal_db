Storm-Tossed Seafarings was a theatrical play penned by playwright Jostrin Zavalandur during the Wailing Years in the late 14 century DR.

History

The play was first performed in the city of Marsember in Cormyr in the . The play featured the character named Faernsar the Fair Knight and his Far Lady – naval vessel passenger.
