Waterdeep – An Elven Diary was a book of personal accounts penned by Malenya Brighteyes sometime before the late 14 century DR.

History

A copy of Waterdeep – An Elven Diary belonged to Manshoon XII, a clone of Manshoon, in the . The book was stored in the mage's library of mundane and arcane tomes in his lair in an abandoned temple of Amaunator underneath the City of the Dead of Waterdeep.
