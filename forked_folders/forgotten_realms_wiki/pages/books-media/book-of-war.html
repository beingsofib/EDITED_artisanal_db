The Book of War was a book on military tactics known in Shou Lung.

Scholarship

Considered a classic of Shou literature, the Book of War was required reading for applicants to the bureaucracy. The annual Civil Service Examination held at Ping Chow saw would-be officials tested on their knowledge of the Book of War, the Book of Heaven, and the Histories and other works over ten hours.
