Lost Princess Road was a ballad penned by an unknown author about the mysterious disappearance of Princess Darseera from the gardens of her fortified home in the Purple Hills sometime in the third century before Dalereckoning began. Contents

The ballad that commemorated the vanishing of Princess Darseera also lent its name to the network of portals into which she apparently stumbled on the night of her disappearance—the Lost Princess Road.
