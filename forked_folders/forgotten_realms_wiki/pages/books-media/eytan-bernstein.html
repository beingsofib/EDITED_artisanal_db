Eytan Bernstein

Eytan Bernstein is a game designer and writer of Dungeons & Dragons content.

Works

Bernstein has worked on several core sourcebooks, particularly for 3.5 and 4 edition Dungeons & Dragons. He has also worked on numerous articles for both Dragon and Dungeon magazines. Along with Eric L. Boyd, Bernstein was one of the main designers of Dragons of Faerûn.
