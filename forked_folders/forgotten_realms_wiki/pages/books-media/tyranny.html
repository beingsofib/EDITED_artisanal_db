Tyranny is an adventure module from the RPGA Legacy of the Green Regent campaign.

Plot

High Lord Kalahar Twohands orders the party to immediately return to the Fortress of the Eternal Despot from which they have only recently arrived back. New research suggests that a powerful artifact of evil lies underneath the structure and must be retrieved before Bane's clergy gets their hands on it.
