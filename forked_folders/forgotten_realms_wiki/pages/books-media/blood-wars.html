Blood Wars was a collectible card game (CCG) created by TSR, Inc. and based on their popular Planescape campaign setting.

Gameplay

Each deck has cards representing Battlefields, Warlords, Legions and elements of Fate. Players use their Warlords, Legions and Fate cards to seize Battle fields. The objective is to capture enough Battlefields to reach a preset number of victory points. Each set of cards contained Common, Uncommon, Rare and Chase cards, plus the occasional Ultra-Rare card.

History

Blood Wars was released in March of 1995 as part of TSR's 20th anniversary. Three expansions were planned but ultimately cancelled, Insurgents of the Inner Planes, Weapons & Warmongers and Hand of Fates. The game has been out-of-print since the late 90s.
