Pages from the Mages is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms.

Description

Pages from the Mages is the 8 book in the FOR series. It features an array of spells structured in a unique format that showcases several spellbooks of the Realms. The accessory not only catalogs these spellbooks but also explores into their origins, providing comprehensive backgrounds on the magicians who authored them. Additionally, it offers insights into the current locations where these spellbooks might be discovered by characters.

The majority of spellbooks detailed in this accessory are repositories of spells that are widely recognized within the Realms. However, the spells specifically outlined in this accessory are predominantly distinctive spells, many of which bear the names of their creators. These spellbooks have been historically sought after by various characters within the Realms.
