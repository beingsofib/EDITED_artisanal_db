Ambritur's Whip was a mean-spirited chapbook published in the city of Westgate of the Dragon Coast.

Description

Ambritur's Whip was an anonymously-penned hatchet job publication that relentlessly but eloquently criticized local establishments and events as well as served to gossip-monger about the prominent inhabitants of Westgate. It was wise not to use Ambritur's Whip as a guide.
