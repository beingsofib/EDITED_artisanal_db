Musings of a Witch-Queen in Exile was a book penned by Athaeal of Evermeet in 229 DR.

Contents

The tome, penned by the so-called exiled Witch-Queen of Evermeet, collected ruminations and observations on various topics, including thoughts on wizards being enslaved by their quest for power.
