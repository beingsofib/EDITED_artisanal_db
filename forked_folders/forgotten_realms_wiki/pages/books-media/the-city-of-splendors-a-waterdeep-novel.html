The City of Splendors: A Waterdeep Novel by Ed Greenwood and Elaine Cunningham is the fourth and final book in The Cities series.

Synopsis

The novel opens on 30 Ches in the Year of the Tankard (1370 DR) with the ocean-side siege of Waterdeep by the saughrim.
