"The Book of Inner Alchemy" is a short adventure module for 12–level players, collected in the anthology Candlekeep Mysteries, where players fight an order of unscrupulous monks in the Cloakwood.

Synopsis

In 1492 DR, several pages are stolen from The Book of Inner Alchemy, an ancient tome in the Great Library of Candlekeep. A group of adventurers is tasked with recovering the stolen pages. To do so, they must confront the monks of the Order of the Immortal Lotus, and defeat their legendary leader, Bak Mei.
