Hungry Shadows

Hungry Shadows is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Avernus Rising series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Baldur's Gate, and is an adventure designed for 1- to 4-level characters. It is part two of Betrayal is in the Blood.
