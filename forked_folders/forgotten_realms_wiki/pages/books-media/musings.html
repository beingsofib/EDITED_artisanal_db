Musings was a book penned by Helsuntiir of Athkatla in the early 13 century DR.

History

This aptly named book collected the author's thoughts and ruminations on various topics. Some of Helsuntiir's opinions of note included his thoughts on adventurers, and the value they brought to schools and society, despite being "dangerous idiots." The book was published in the .
