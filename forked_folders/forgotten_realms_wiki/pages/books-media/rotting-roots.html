Rotting Roots

Rotting Roots is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Tomb of Annihilation series of adventures published for the Tomb of Annihilation. It focuses on Chult, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 5- to 10-level characters. It is the second part of the The Rot from Within trilogy.
