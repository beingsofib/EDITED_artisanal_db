The Cookbook of Nemalas was an incredibly rare and legendary cookbook in Faerûn.

Contents

The Cookbook of Nemalas contained recipes for a variety of dishes that were once made by the Netherese.

History

At some point in time, Elminster Aumar gifted a copy of this book to the Harpers, who then kept it within Harpers' Hold.
