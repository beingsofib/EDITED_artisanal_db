Of Healthful Enchantments for the Body was a tome that collected crafting recipes for various enchanted belts and boots.

Contents

The book collected ingredients, recipes, and arcane formulae required for crafting and enchanting several magic articles of clothing. Some of the recipes listed included belts of agility that required use of elemental essence of air, star sapphire or rogue stone, and spells of cat's grace. The recipe for belt or frost giant's strength required blue diamonds or jacinths and a bull's strength spell. Another item listed in the tome was a pair of boots of striding.

History

As of the , several copies of the tome could be found across the Sword Coast. One copy found its way into the hands of Shadow Priests who worshiped the King of Shadows in the Crossroad Keep, before the fort was re-taken by the Graycloaks of Neverwinter and the Kalach-Cha.
