The Legend of Drizzt: The Legacy (original title: Forgotten Realms: The Legend of Drizzt, Volume 7: The Legacy) is the graphic novel version of R.A. Salvatore's novel The Legacy. It was originally published in three parts: The Legend of Drizzt: The Legacy #1–3.
