"So High a Price" is a short story by Ed Greenwood first published in the 1994 anthology Realms of Infamy, and reprinted in the 2005 anthology The Best of the Realms II.

Summary

In the , Manshoon is determined to overthrow the council of Zhentil Keep, and plots with Fzoul Chembryl toward this end. The great sage Elminster, disguised as a beholder, manages to stop the duo.
