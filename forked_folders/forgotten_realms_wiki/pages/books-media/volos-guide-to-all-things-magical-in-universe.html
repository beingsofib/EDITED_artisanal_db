Volo's Guide to All Things Magical was a pamphlet written by Volothamp Geddarm early in 1358 DR.

Description

Printed on vellum, and a grab-bag full of odds and ends about magic.

Controversy

Several powerful mages, including Elminster, All copies were hunted down and destroyed.

History

The initial pamphlet, "Volo's Guide to All Things Magical", was first published early in the . Notoriously, it included a great many spells that had been thought long forgotten and exposed these to folk across Faerûn. Several mighty mages, who would rather these spells had stayed forgotten, confronted Volo over this. In response, Volo switched his focus from writing books of magic to writing travelogues. Nevertheless, the spells remained known to the magical community, and even entered common use.

Re-Release

In the , a second pamphlet of the same name was released.
