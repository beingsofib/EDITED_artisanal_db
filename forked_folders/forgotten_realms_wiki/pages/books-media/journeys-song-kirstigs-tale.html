Journey's Song, Kirstig's Tale

Journey's Song, Kirstig's Tale was the first issue of the Spelljammer comics series and published by . It was written by Barbara Kesel, drawn by Michael Collins, and edited by Elliot S. Maggin. It is the first of four installments in the "Rogue Ship" story arc.
