Crown of Salt (adventure)

Crown of Salt is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 2-edition ruleset. It is part of the Adventurer's Guild series of adventures published for the Adventurer's Guild Retail Play Program. It promotes both the Sea of Fallen Stars and Of Ships and the Sea sourcebooks. It starts and concludes in Selgaunt but is focused on the Sea of Fallen Stars, and is an adventure designed for four to six 5- to 6-level characters.

Summary

Andeth Ilchammar sends a rival of his', Sturge Whitsunday, in a doomed mission to find Myth Nantar so that the city of Sembia can sign a treaty with them for defense against pirates. Once they find Myth Nantar against all odds, authorities in the underwater city require them to retrieve the Crown of Salt from Agenais before they accept any treaties.
