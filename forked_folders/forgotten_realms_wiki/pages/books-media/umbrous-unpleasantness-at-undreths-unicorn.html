Plot

Mirt "the Moneylender", acting as a secret agent for Laeral Silverhand, is gathering information in the town of Bucklegrim. He learns from two locals about Daerdrard Orglast, the Black Wyvern, the ambitious son of a powerful grand merchant duke, who is spying on the area and amassing an army to conquer local settlements and add them to his "collection" of castles.

The questioning attracts the attention of the Black Wyvern's agents in the inn called Undreth's Unicorn. A fight breaks out, which Mirt easily wins using trickery, brute strength, and a poisoned needle. After defeating the first group of "bullyswords", a second, larger group of the Black Wyvern's men arrives. Mirt provokes them and, using the same deceptive tactics combined with physical force, effortlessly defeats them as well.

Mirt's dry wit comes to a conclusion that he has underscored the dangerous and unpredictable nature of the Border Kingdoms, a place where he, despite his age, is more than capable of handling himself.
