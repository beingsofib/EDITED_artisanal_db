Champions of Ruin is an accessory for the Forgotten Realms campaign setting using the 3.5-edition ruleset.

Description

Champions of Ruin provides details on the role of evil in the Realms. It has information of various villains, evil-oriented organizations and introduces three new evil-aligned races. It also contains new spells, feats and prestige classes.
