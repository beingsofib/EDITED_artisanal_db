Dungeon Mayhem: Battle for Baldur's Gate is an expansion for the Dungeon Mayhem card game set in Baldur's Gate.

Contents

  • 56 cards split into two decks
  • 2 reference cards
  • 2 hit point trackers
  • 2 hit point tokens
  • 6 damage tokens
