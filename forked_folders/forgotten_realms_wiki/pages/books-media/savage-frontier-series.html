Savage Frontier series

The Savage Frontier series is a computer game series developed by and published by . It occurs in the Savage Frontier region of northwest Faerûn.

Games in the Savage Frontier series

Gateway to the Savage Frontier

The first in the Savage Frontier series. It was released in 1991.

Treasures of the Savage Frontier

The direct sequel to Gateway to the Savage Frontier. It was released in 1992.
