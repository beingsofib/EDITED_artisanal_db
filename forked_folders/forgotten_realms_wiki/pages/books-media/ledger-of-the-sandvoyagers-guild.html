Ledger of the Sandvoyagers Guild was a ledger book belonging to the Sandvoyagers Guild, which operated in the Raurin in the mid–14 century DR..

History

Adventurers might've found the book around ; it may have made it evident to them that the Sandvoyagers Guild had been taken over by a slaver ring, as its latter contents were faked by Thurnas Netmaster.
