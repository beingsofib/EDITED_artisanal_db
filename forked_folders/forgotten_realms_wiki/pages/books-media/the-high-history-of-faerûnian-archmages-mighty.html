The High History of Faerûnian Archmages Mighty was a four-volume tome penned by Antarn the Sage in the .

Contents

All four volumes of the book chronicled the history of several archmages of Faerûn. One of them was dedicated to Elminster Aumar– the Sage of Shadowdale and his early years. The mage's recorded journey started from being an orphaned prince of Athalantar in the and his fateful journey to Myth Drannor on behalf of Mystra in the .

A copy of The High History of Faerûnian Archmages Mighty was kept in the library-citadel of Candlekeep on the Sword Coast as of the late 14 century DR.
