Song's End, Tale's Beginning

Song's End, Tale's Beginning was the fourth issue of the Spelljammer comics series and published by . It was written by Barbara Kesel, drawn by Michael Collins, and edited by Elliot S. Maggin. It is the fourth and final instalment in the "Rogue Ship" story arc.
