Just Lookin'

Just Lookin is a 3-round Living City adventure set in Stag Falls. It was later reprinted as three Adventurer's Guild modules, separating and naming the rounds A Funny Thing Happened on the Way to Stag Falls, Stag Falls Knights, and The Pyre Down Below'.

Summary

Background

After Baron Vilhelm von Stag Falls accidentally unearths the sorcerous vampire Romanisk Vlaensk underneath his castle and is turned into an undead under the ancient wizard's control, Marcus Rubicon hires adventurers to investigate the nearby town of Stag Falls, as it is late with its taxes for the first time and a patrol sent there earlier has gone missing. He makes it clear that if the threat is one they can handle on their own, they are encouraged to take it on, but he will settle for a report of the situation.

A Funny Thing Happened on the Way to Stag Falls...

The adventurers get hired to investigate Stag Falls. They stop at Eaglton, a town halfway there; they learn of the bad condition of the road, and that no one has come traveling from the town for a while. While on the last leg of the road, one or more werebats attempt to infiltrate the players to feed; the players come across the missing patrol, and are then attacked by wolves. Upon reaching Stag Falls, they find the town to be deserted, and the villagers in hiding at nearby caves, led by a druid named Sidone.

Stag Falls Knights

The adventurers help deliver a child, and are given a ring by Sidone, which carries five charges of dispel magic. Then, they must make their way to the castle, where most of the guards are charmed, and many others have been raised as zombies after death, seeking to somehow stop the now-undead Baron. They may be captured during this process.

The Pyre Down Below

The adventurers seek the vampire Romanisk Vlaensk in his underground lair to slay him and so end his evil.
