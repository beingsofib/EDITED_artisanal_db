The Legend of Drizzt: Omnibus Volume 2 (graphic novel)

The Legend of Drizzt: Omnibus Volume 2 is the second omnibus of the The Legend of Drizzt Graphic Novels. It was published in 2009 by and 2012 by .

It is a collection of the Icewind Dale Trilogy volumes of the graphic novel series.
