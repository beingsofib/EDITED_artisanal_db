Ed Gentry

Ed Gentry is the author of the novel Neversfall, part of The Citadels series. He began writing with an entry to the open call for Maiden of Pain. His short story "Freedom's Promise" appears in the Realms of the Dragons II compilation.

Influences

Amongst Realms authors, Ed regards Paul S. Kemp as a tremendous talent, and regards Elaine Cunningham's characters as providing a lot from which to learn.
