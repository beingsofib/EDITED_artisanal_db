James P. Davis

James P. Davis is the author of several Forgotten Realms novels including his latest novel Circle of Skulls. He lives in Shreveport, Louisiana with his wife and a crazy cat.

Bibliography

Novels

  • Bloodwalk (2006)
  • The Shield of Weeping Ghosts (2008)
  • The Restless Shore (2009)
  • Circle of Skulls (2010)
