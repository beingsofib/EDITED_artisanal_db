A Life Lived in the Shadows of Death was a book comprising the personal memoirs of the Archmage Briel, written some time in the 14 century DR.

Notably, they covered Briel's time with the notorious lich Aumvor the Undying, and told was little was known about the Undying One and his activities after the fall of Netheril.

They were kept in his daughter Brielbara's library in Baldur's Gate around 1374 DR.
