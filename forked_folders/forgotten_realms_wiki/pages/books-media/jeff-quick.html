Jeff Quick

Jeff "Zippy" Quick is a game designer who has worked for TSR, Inc. and Wizards of the Coast, was a member of the Role-Playing Group Association (RPGA), and acted as the editor of Polyhedron Newszine from mid-1996 to 1999. He has also performed freelance work for and other games.
