Red-Haired Lad was a musical work known in Cormyr in the 1360s DR.

History

In the , Jasper heard Diccon Picard playing a song on his fiddle and thought it was either "Red-haired Lad" or Warrior's Woe, as he thought most fiddle songs sounded alike.
