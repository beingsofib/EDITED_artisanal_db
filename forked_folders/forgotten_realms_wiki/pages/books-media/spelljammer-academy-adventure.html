Spelljammer Academy is an adventure module using the 5-edition Dungeons & Dragons ruleset published by Wizards of the Coast on D&D Beyond on July 11, 2022. Set in the Forgotten Realms and Spelljammer campaign settings, it is intended as an introductory adventure for Spelljammer: Adventures in Space.
