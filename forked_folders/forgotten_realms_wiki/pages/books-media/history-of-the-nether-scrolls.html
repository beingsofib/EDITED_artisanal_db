History of the Nether Scrolls was a tome on the subject of the Nether Scrolls.

Description

The tome was completely indecipherable, both by normal and magical means.

History

A copy could be found in Castle Maluradek in Anauroch, around the .

In the , Duke Eltan of Baldur's Gate presented it to Abdel Adrian in order that he could gain admittance back into his home of Candlekeep.
