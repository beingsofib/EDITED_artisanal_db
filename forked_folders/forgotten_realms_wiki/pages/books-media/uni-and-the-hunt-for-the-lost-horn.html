Uni and the Hunt for the Lost Horn is an adventure module for the 5-edition revised ruleset, released on September 25, 2024, on D&D Beyond and for Adventurers League. It is based on the Dungeons & Dragons cartoon series of 1983–1985. As such, it is set in The Realm, not the Forgotten Realms, but is included here for reference.
