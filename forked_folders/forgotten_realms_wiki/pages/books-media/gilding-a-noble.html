Gilding a Noble

Gilding a Noble is a one-round tournament adventure module set in Waterdeep for the Living Forgotten Realms organized play program and the first part of the Restoring Splendor questline. It was published in 2010 and is intended for a group of 4 to 6 adventurers ranging from level 7 to 10.
