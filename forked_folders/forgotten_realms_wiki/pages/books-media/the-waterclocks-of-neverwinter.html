The Waterclocks of Neverwinter was a book detailing Neverwinter's famous waterclocks in the late 15 century DR, including their precision and rare pieces by noted clockmakers.

A copy was in the library of Bradda's Sage's Shop in Neverwinter in 1479 DR.
