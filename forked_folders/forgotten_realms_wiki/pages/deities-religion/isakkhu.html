Isakkhu was a male deity worshiped by barbarians dwelling in the Earthfast Mountains

Description

The deity took the form of a mountain.

Personality

Isakkhu was dependable and reliable.

Worshipers

At the end of each day, clerics of Isakkhu asked him to return the sun the next morning and he never failed to deliver.
