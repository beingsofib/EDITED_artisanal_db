Glory domain

This article is about the standard domain. For the prestige domain, see Glory prestige domain.

The Glory domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with spells and powers inspired by glory.

Powers

The cleric's ability to turn undead was improved.

Spells

Novice spellcasters were able to use the disrupt undead, glorious raiment, and searing light spells. Clerics of some experience could cast celestial brilliance, crown of flame, and bolt of glory. Those that were masters of this domain had access to sunbeam, crown of glory, and blinding glory.
