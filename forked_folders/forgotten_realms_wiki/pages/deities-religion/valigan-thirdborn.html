Valigan Thirdborn was a lesser deity of anarchy in ancient Jhaamdath, whose rise to power in the directly coincided with that of Exarch Thelasand IV, a bloodthirsty advisor who urged the emperor of Jhaamdath into greater conflict with the neighboring elves. After those same elves brought about Jhaamdath's fall shortly after, the region fell into lawlessness. It was pacified and Valigan was killed during the Procession of Justice ( to ).
