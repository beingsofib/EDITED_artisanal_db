Creation domain

The Creation domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with creation-related spells and powers.

Components

Their conjuration spells gained increased potency.

Spells

Novice spellcasters were able to use the create or destroy water, minor image, and create food and water spells. Clerics of some experience could cast minor creation, major creation, and heroes' feast. Those that were masters of this domain had access to permanent image, true creation, and either genesis or, more likely, pavilion of grandeur.
