Hatred domain

The Hatred domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with spells and powers inspired by hatred.

Powers

They gained an evil boost to their combat skills and resistance versus a single selected foe for one minute a day.

Spells

Novice spellcasters were able to use the doom, scare, and bestow curse spells. Clerics of some experience could cast song of discord, righteous might, and forbiddance. Those that were masters of this domain had access to blasphemy, antipathy, and wail of the banshee.
