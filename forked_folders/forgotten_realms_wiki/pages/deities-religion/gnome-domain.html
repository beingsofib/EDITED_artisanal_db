Gnome domain

The Gnome domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with gnome-inspired spells and powers.

Powers

Their illusion spells gained some potency.

Spells

Novice spellcasters were able to use the silent image, gembomb, and minor image spells. Clerics of some experience could cast minor creation, hallucinatory terrain, and fantastic machine. Those that were masters of this domain had access to screen, Otto's irresistible dance, and summon nature's ally (earth elementals and animals only).
