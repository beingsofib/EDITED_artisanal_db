Pag was a savage god of Zakhara worshiped among the isolated islands of the Crowded Sea.

Description

Pag was believed to inhabit every tree and flower within the islands of the Crowded Sea. Because of this, worshipers of Pag only ate fish and seaweed, leaving fruit to rot on the vine.

Abilities

Pag was a god of nature who provided rich bounties.
