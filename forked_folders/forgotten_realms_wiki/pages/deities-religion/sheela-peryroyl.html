Sheela Peryroyl (, sometimes misspelled Peryroyal) was the halfling goddess of nature, song, dance, agriculture, and romantic love. In the period between the Spellplague and the Second Sundering, during which Yondalla was interpreted as an aspect of Chauntea, Sheela was considered the prominent halfling deity.

Domain

Sheela Peryroyl tended to a thriving orchard and farmland within the Concordant Opposition.
