Amarok

Deity
nameAmarok the Wolf
power4ePrimal spirit

Amarok the Wolf was a powerful primal spirit venerated not unlike a deity in parts of Toril. He was a source of primal magic for his followers.

History

As of the late 15 century DR, Amarok was worshiped in the realm of Sarifal on the Moonshae Isles alongside many other powerful spirits and archfey.
