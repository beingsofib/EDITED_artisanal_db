Moon domain

The Moon domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with spells and powers related to the moon, perception, and lycanthropes.

Powers

The spellcaster gained the ability to turn and destroy lycanthropes as a good cleric would turn undead.

Spells

Novice spellcasters were able to use the faerie fire, moonbeam, and moon blade spells. Clerics of some experience could cast good hope, moon path, and permanent image. Those that were masters of this domain had access to insanity, animal shapes, and moonfire.
