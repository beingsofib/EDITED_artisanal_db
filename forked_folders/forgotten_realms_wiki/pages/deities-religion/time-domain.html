Time domain

The Time domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with time-related spells and powers.

Powers

They gained the ability to respond quicker at the start of a fight.

Spells

Novice spellcasters were able to use the true strike, gentle repose, and haste spells. Clerics of some experience could cast freedom of movement, permanency, and contingency. Those that were masters in this domain had access to moment of prescience, foresight, and time stop.
