Craft domain

The Craft domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with craft-related spells and powers.

Powers

One of their crafting skills and their ability to cast creation spells improved.

Spells

Novice spellcasters were able to use the animate rope, wood shape, and stone shape spells. Clerics of some experience could cast minor creation, wall of stone, and fantastic machine. Those that were masters of this domain had access to major creation, forcecage, and greater fantastic machine.
