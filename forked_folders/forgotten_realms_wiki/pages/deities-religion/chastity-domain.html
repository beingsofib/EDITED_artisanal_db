Chastity domain

The Chastity domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with chastity-related spells and powers.

Powers

Once a day, they could remove a spell effect that targeted their body as if they could cast dispel magic.

Spells

Novice spellcasters were able to use the shield of faith, lesser restoration, and magic vestment spells. Clerics of some experience could cast spell immunity, break enchantment, and bigby's interposing hand. Those that were masters of this domain had access to repulsion, antimagic field, and mind blank.
