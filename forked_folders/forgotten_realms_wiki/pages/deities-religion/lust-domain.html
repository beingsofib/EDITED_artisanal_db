Lust domain

The Lust domain was a suite of spells and powers for divine spellcasters like clerics that were granted by deities whose portfolios were concerned with passionate desire for sensuality, power, influence, or status.

Powers

Clerics with the Lust domain gained the ability to enhance their force of personality for several seconds through body posturing and movement designed to seduce and distract.

Spells

Novice spellcasters were able to use the charm person, invisibility, and clairaudience/clairvoyance spells. Clerics of some experience could cast lesser planar ally, scrying, and symbol of persuasion. Those that were masters of this domain had access to refuge, sympathy, and trap the soul.
