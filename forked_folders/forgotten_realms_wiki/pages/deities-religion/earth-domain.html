Earth domain

The Earth domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with earth-based spells and powers.

Powers

The spellcaster gained the ability to turn away and destroy air creatures the way a good cleric turned undead, and to rebuke, command, and strengthen earth creatures the way an evil cleric rebuked undead.

Spells

As they advanced, a priest with this domain could enchant stone projectiles with magic stone; use soften earth and stone to make clay, sand, or mud; sculpt with stone shape; create defenses with spike stones and wall of stone; protect an ally with stoneskin and themselves with iron body; and trigger a small earthquake. Finally, they could summon an elemental swarm of earth elementals.
