The Silver Flame was a deity local to the world of Eberron. An abstract being of law and good, the Silver Flame and its church were at the forefront of fighting against the forces of evil and chaos, particularly the fiendish Lords of Dust.

History

During the ancient Age of Demons, the world was ravaged by the fell Overlords, fiendish beings of immense power and cruelty. The Silver Flame trapped and held the Overlords in great prisons hidden in the underworld of Khyber, binding them with its own essence. The Lords of Dust sought endlessly to find and free the Overlords and usher in a new Age of Demons, but were countered by the Church of the Silver Flame.

Around the 1480s DR of Faerûn, the rakshasa Gnomon infiltrated the Church of the Silver Flame and managed to discover the Spinner's Prison, home of the great and terrible Spinner of Shadows. He put together a conspiracy to break open the road to Tarath Marad using a ritual and then undo the wards on the prison, allowing the Spinner to run free. Though his plans were only partially successful (the Spinner immediately betrayed the Lords of Dust), it was enough to overpower the Silver Flame Guardian and let the Spinner call out to Lolth for rescue. The Spinner departed through the Rift Between Worlds, leaving the forces of the Silver Flame to handle the aftermath while Lolth pursued the conquest of the Weave in Faerûn.

Notable Items

The agents and champions of the Silver Flame were equipped with various pieces of blessed wargear, including the Bow of the Silver Flame, Fury of the Flame, Luminous Truth, Silver slinger, Templar's bastion, Templar's bulwark, Templar's docent, Templar's Justice, and Templar's retribution.
