Peace domain

The Peace domain was a divine domain for clerics of deities whose portfolio included tranquility, arbitration, and friendship. Clerics who chose this domain were granted free access to these spells—they could cast them at any time without having to prepare them in advance.

Spells

Novice clerics of this domain could cast heroism, sanctuary, aid, and warding bond. Those of some experience were able to use the beacon of hope, sending, aura of purity, and Otiluke's resilient sphere spells. Master clerics of the Peace domain were skilled enough to cast greater restoration and Rary's telepathic bond. In addition, they were able to use balm of peace.
