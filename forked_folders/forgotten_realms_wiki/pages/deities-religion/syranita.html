Syranita was the patron deity of the aarakocra

Description

Syranita's avatar had the appearance of a female aarakocra with silver skin and pink-gold feathers.

Abilities

In avatar form, Syranita could see perfectly within a range of . She was very watchful and skilled at spying.

The opal necklace she wore protected her from evil. She was able to summon air elementals and djinn.

No natural bird or avian creature would harm her.

She was able to change the direction of wind.

Personality

She loved music and would sometimes appear in avatar form just to listen to bards practice their craft.

Relationships

Syranita tried to be friends with as many gods as possible, including human sky gods like Akadi. She was also an ally of Deep Sashelas and other members of the asathalfinare as she sought to create friendship for her relatively weak race.
