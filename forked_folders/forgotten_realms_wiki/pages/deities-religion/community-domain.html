Community domain

The Community domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with spells and powers themed around community.

Powers

The priest gained the ability to use calm emotions innately once a day, and they also gained a knack for diplomacy.

Spells

Novice spellcasters were able to use the bless, status, and prayer spells. Clerics of some experience could cast Rary's telepathic bond, greater status, and heroes' feast. Those that were masters of this domain had access to refuge, sympathy, and mass heal.
