Sss'thasine'ss, the Lord of Venom was one of the many deities that split off from the World Serpent.

Description

Sss'thasine'ss appeared to his followers as a writhing swarm of serpents coiled into a roughly humanoid shape, and spoke through the mouths of the countless snakes in the swarm. His symbol, similarly, was a swarm of snakes.

Cult

Sss'thasine'ss was worshiped by some yuan-ti and dark nagas.

History

Sss'thasine'ss was originally an aspect of the World Serpent that became a true deity when the World Serpent shattered; however, he was later destroyed by an elf god before the Crown Wars, and Talona subsumed what remained of him as an aspect.
