The Drummer was a savage god of Zakhara worshiped among the isolated islands of the Crowded Sea.

Worshipers of the Drummer called their god by beating large drums made from hollow logs.

Description

The Drummer had no physical form and flew through the air when called.

Abilities

Unbelievers were at great risk when the Drummer appeared for he could pass into their bodies and gnaw the flesh from their bones.
