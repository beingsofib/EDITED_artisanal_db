Suffering domain

The Suffering domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with spells and powers themed around suffering.

Powers

They gain the ability to perform a pain touch, which temporarily reduced the subject's strength and agility.

Spells

Novice spellcasters were able to use the bane, endurance, and bestow curse spells. Clerics of some experience could cast enervation, feeblemind, and harm. Those that were masters in this domain had access to eyebite (sicken), symbol (pain), and horrid wilting.
