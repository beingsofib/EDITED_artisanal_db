Chaos domain

The Chaos domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with chaotic spells and powers.

Powers

A priest with this domain could cast chaotic spells.

Spells

As they advanced, a priest with this domain could protect against lawful beings and effects with protection from law, magic circle against law, dispel law, and cloak of chaos, and assault lawful or non-chaotic beings with chaos hammer and word of chaos. They could break objects with shatter or give them life with animate objects. Finally, they could summon powerful beings of chaos to their aid with summon monster IX.
