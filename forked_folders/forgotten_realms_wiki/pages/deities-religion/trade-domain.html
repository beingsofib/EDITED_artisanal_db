Trade domain

The Trade domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with trade-related spells and powers.

Powers

Once per day, they could use detect thoughts innately for some minutes.

Spells

Novice spellcasters were able to use the message, gembomb, and eagle's splendor spells. Clerics of some experience could cast sending, fabricate, and true seeing. Those that were masters in this domain had access to Mordenkainen's magnificent mansion, mind blank, and discern location.
