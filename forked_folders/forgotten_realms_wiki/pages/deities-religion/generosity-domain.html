Generosity domain

The Generosity domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with generosity-related spells and powers. Once a day they could immediately cast a healing spell of the conjuration type at maximum effect.

Spells

Novice spellcasters were able to use the deathwatch, shield other, and detect thoughts spells. Clerics of some experience could cast discern lies, true seeing, and prying eyes. Those that were masters in this domain had access to guards and wards, teleport object, and foresight.
