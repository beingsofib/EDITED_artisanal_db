Law domain

The Law domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters such as clerics with lawful spell and powers.

Powers

A priest with the Law domain cast lawful spells at greater power.

Spells

As they advanced, a priest with this domain could protect themselves and others against chaotic creatures and magical effects with protection from chaos, magic circle against chaos, dispel chaos and shield of law. They could harm and debilitate chaotic creatures with order's wrath and any non-lawful creature with dictum. They could also pacify beings with calm emotions and paralyze them with hold monster. Finally, they could call an inherently lawful being of the planes to aid them with summon monster IX.
