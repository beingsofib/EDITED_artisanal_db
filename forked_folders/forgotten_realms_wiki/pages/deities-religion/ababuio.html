Ababuio was a god of luck worshiped on the island nation of Wa. They were one of the Eight Million Gods.

History

One of the mortal descendants of Ababuio in the mid–14 century DR was believed to be Osari Daizan, the daimyo of the prosperous province of Tifuido on the island of Tsukishima. In reverence to Ababuio, he carefully observed a longstanding tradition among the Osari clan of fishing for jellyfish daily, going out to the beach each dawn with a net.
