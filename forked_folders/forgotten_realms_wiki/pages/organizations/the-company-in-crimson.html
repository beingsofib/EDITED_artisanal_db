The Company in Crimson was a group of adventurers based in Cormyr some time before the late 14 century DR.

History

The company was betrayed and killed to a man by the wicked mage Shandaril during an expedition into the Stonelands. Shandaril had recently joined their group and slew everyone for their magic.
