The Red Fangs were the smallest of the three orc tribes that dwelled within the Rauvin Mountains during the 14 century DR.

Activities

The tribe earned its name from the ritual sacrifice they made to Gruumsh. They lashed their foes to altars and feasted upon them while they were still alive.

Base of Operations

They dwelled within the western peaks of the Rauvin Mountains.

Relationships

The Red Fangs considered the Heart Takers tribe to be their rivals and held deep-seated fear of the Tornskulls.

History

Around the , 400 orcs from the tribe left their ancestral home and ventured west to find new lands to pillage. They were not heard from by their fellow Red Fangs since their departure.
