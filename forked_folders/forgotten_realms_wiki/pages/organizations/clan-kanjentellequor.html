Clan Kanjentellequor was one of the dragonborn clans of the nation of Tymanther.

Description

Members of Clan Kanjentellequor used silver skewers through their jawline as their distinctive clan piercings.

History

As of 1487 DR, Clan Kanjentellequor has a sizable number of wizards among its members.

Known members

  • Kanjentellequor Biri, a hero of the clan in 1479 DR.
