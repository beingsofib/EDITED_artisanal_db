Duskrose was a prominent non-noble family who served the Crown of Cormyr up to the 15 century DR.

History

The Duskroses were long-time servants of the Crown in the Royal Palace and Royal Court, alongside the Bracetels, Lionwinters, Ormantrels, Saraclarrs, and Windstones. Additionally, several members of the family were knighted in the 12 and 13 centuries DR.
