Husteem () was a noble house of Waterdeep.

Base of Operations

The family owned a villa in the Sea Ward, on the east side of the Street of the Singing Dolphin between Delzorin Street and Sighing Maiden's Walk, across from the Tower of Luck.

Members

  • Boreas Husteem - Orbos' fourth son and heir.
  • Daervin Husteem
  • Luna Husteem - Orbos' consort.
  • Orbos Husteem - head of the house.
  • Orlpar Husteem - brother of Orbos.
