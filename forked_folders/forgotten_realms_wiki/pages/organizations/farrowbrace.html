Farrowbrace

Organization
nameFarrowbrace
typeNoble family
baseCormyr

Farrowbrace was a noble house of Cormyr. They held an estate on the Northbank region, on or near the River Starwater.

Members

  • Lord Hornsar Farrowbrace, father of the deceased Lady Shamril.
  • Lady Shamril Farrowbrace, daughter of Lord Hornsar.
