Seiveril's Crusade was an elven military expedition organized by Lord Seiveril Miritar of Elion, that was formed in Evermeet and fought at Evereska, Myth Glaurach and Myth Drannor. After fighting against the forces of evil across the breadth of Faerûn, including the sprawling Cormanthor War, it ultimately achieved the reclamation of Myth Drannor.

Relationships

In the final march on Myth Drannor, the Crusade worked with the Elves of Cormanthor, an alliance of local elves that in turn aligned with the drow of Eilistraee's Wanderers. These elves were not nearly as martially-minded as the Crusade, instead being mainly focused on the exploration and reconstruction of the ruins.

History

The Crusade was born on Ches 10 of 1374 DR when Seiveril Miritar resigned from the royal council of Evermeet, forming a volunteer army to relieve Evereska from the assaults of House Dlardrageth. The elves began arriving in Evereska as soon as Ches 16, joining in battle and driving the fey'ri off by Tarsakh 12. They then moved to assault the ruins of Myth Glaurach, where the orc and ogre allies of House Dlardrageth had been based, arriving by Tarsakh 19.

On Mirtul 19, Araevin Teshurr discovered the hidden portal network that Sarya Dlardrageth had used to move between Myth Drannor and Glaurachyndaar, and on Mirtul 24 Seiveril called a Council of War with his commanders that set the course of the Crusade. They used a portal to take the Crusade to Semberholme, and on Mirtul 28 Seiveril reaffirmed the Dales Compact with Battledale, Deepingdale, Mistledale, and Shadowdale, and then set marching to Myth Drannor on Kythorn 1. In response to this pressing threat, House Dlardrageth formed alliances with Hillsfar and Sembia that led to them joining the war by Kythorn 4, and on Kythorn 12 Hillsfar in turn allied with Zhentil Keep to bring them in as well.

This grand alliance against the Elven Crusade had shattered by Kythorn 21, as Hillsfar had only joined with the intention of luring Zhentil Keep into a losing war. The plan worked—on Kythorn 24 the Crusade defeated Scyllua Darkhope's army, the withdrawal of Hillsfar's forces sent the greater campaign into disarray, and Zhentil Keep began warring with Hillsfar instead. These conflicts drew attention in nearby lands, with House Sashenstar taking bids from every side of the wars for logistics and supplies.

By Eleasis 5 the conflict Sembia formed a truce with the Crusade, which turned into a new alliance against Dlardrageth by Eleasis 11. With the odds now reversed, the Crusade managed to assault the Vale of Lost Voices on day 17, reached Myth Drannor on day 20, and took the city on day 22 of that month. Though almost all of House Dlardrageth was slain, Seiveril Miritar also fell in battle.

Now called the Army of Myth Drannor, the far-from-home elves were put on a defensive stance from Eleint 3 as Zhentil Keep finished its brief war with Hillsfar and sought to prevent the elves from securing their new foothold. Facing not just the Zhents but their new drow Masked Brigades from House Jaelre and Clan Auzkovyn, both sides were bled by constant raids and strikes. Despite the arrival of the Knights of Myth Drannor and the crowning of Ilsevele Miritar as the new Coronal, the situation remained poor as the Cormanthor War dragged on, only ending in 1376 DR when Zhentil Keep and the drow cut their losses and accepted peace terms.
