The Glaezel family was a prominent business-owning family of Baldur's Gate in the late 14 century DR.

History

By the late 14 century DR, the Glaezel family was a prominent and a long-standing family that owned many businesses and buildings across Baldur's Gate. Manthuran Glaezel became the family's head some years before the late 14 century DR. The family's properties included Glaezel's Dock in the Seatower district of the Lower City.
