Guardians of Skuld

Organization
nameGuardians of Skuld
baseSkuld, Mulhorand
favored deityAnhur, Osiris, and Isis

The Guardians of Skuld were a religious order devoted to gods of the Mulhorandi pantheon and dedicated to protect the city of Skuld, the capital of Mulhorand.

Members

This organization counted priests of Anhur, Horus-Re, Osiris, and Isis (via the Sisters of Life) among its members.

Activities

They guarded the capital city against invasion and betrayal.

History

Previously unofficial, the existence of the Guardians of Skuld was formalized after the Time of Troubles of 1358 DR. They were still active by 1369 DR.

Leadership

Leadership of the Guardians cycled between the different priesthoods. By 1369 DR, it had passed from the clergy of Horus-Re back to the church of Anhur.
