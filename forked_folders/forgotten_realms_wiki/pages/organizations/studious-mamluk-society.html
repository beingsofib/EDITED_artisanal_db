The Studious Mamluk Society, also known as the Studious, was a military organization in Zakhara.

Activities

Contrary to most of the mamluk organizations in Zakhara, the Studious focused on espionage and communication.

Organization

The Studious were led by General Okin Abd al-Talib'ilm.

Base of Operations

Qudra was the base of operations for the Studious. They also operated in Huzuz, Wasat, and Utaqa.
