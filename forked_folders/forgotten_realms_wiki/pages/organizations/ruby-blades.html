The Ruby Blades were a Sembite adventuring company that operated circa 1369 DR.

History

In the , the Ruby Blades were summoned by their allies, the Wolfmasters, to join them in a dangerous expedition into the Vast Swamp to loot Orvaskyte Keep. The Ruby Blades were delayed, and by the time they arrived the Wolfmasters had been slain by a cornugon devil. Running into Cormyrean adventurers sent by Lord Partic Thistle to find the Wolfmasters, the Ruby Blades abandoned the Orvaskyte Keep mission, instead turning to hunting a local black dragon. Utterly outclassed, they fled with the dragon in pursuit, only to be saved by the Cormyrean adventurers. They were then reinforced by two-dozen Sembian mercenaries, and went on to secure Orvaskyte Keep for their own delving.

Known Ruby Blades

  • Ranni Stalkersdaughter, co-leader and ranger.
  • Pyrra Flamedancer, co-leader and wizard.
  • Arpeggi Trill, a dramatic bard.
  • Dendir Abbathorson, a dwarf rogue.
  • Zurk the Looney, an insane fighter.
