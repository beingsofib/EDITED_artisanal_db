The Knights of the Silver Horn were an adventuring party that built strongholds in the Dessarin Valley in the late 9 century DR.

History

A band of adventurers, the Knights of the Silver Horn re-discovered the ruins of Tyar-Besil in the Year of the Raised Sword, 893 DR. After returning again and again, they established strongholds over each of the known access points leading to the ancient dwarven ruins.

For a couple generations, the Knights and their descendants watched over the Dessarin Valley. Then, in the Year of the Sky Riders, 936 DR an army of orcs from Uruth Ukrypt swept through the Dessarin Valley on their way to lay siege to Waterdeep (then called Nimoar's Hold) in what would become known as the Orcfastings War. The Knights of the Silver Horn were driven from their strongholds. The Knights were sent into decline, and the keeps stood empty for centuries. The keeps were known by locals as the Haunted Keeps of the Sumber Hills.
