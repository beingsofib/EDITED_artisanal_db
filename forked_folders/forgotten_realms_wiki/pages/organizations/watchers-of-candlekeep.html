The Watchers of Candlekeep were the guardians tasked with the protection of the library-fortress of Candlekeep.

Organization

These protectors were led by the individual known as the Gatewarden. They were aided in their efforts by the Keeper of the Partal, who stood guard over the keep's main entrance from the Way of the Lion.

Each of the four Watchers led a contingent of a dozen well-armed monks.

Activities

They spent much of their time patrolling the grounds of Candlekeep and surveying the surrounding landscape for danger.

Possessions

The Watchers were said to have been equipped with magical rings and enchanted rods to aid them in their duties.
