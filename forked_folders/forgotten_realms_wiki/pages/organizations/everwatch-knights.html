The Everwatch Knights were an order of dedicated bodyguards affiliated with the church of Helm active around 1369 DR.

Temples of Helm hired them out to others in order to generate revenue.

Notable Members

  • Kelef Rhon, veteran of the War of the Shining Crusade.
