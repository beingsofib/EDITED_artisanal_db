The Industrious Brothers and Sisters of Carpentry, Cabinetry, Puppetry, and Toymaking was an honorary order whose members were devoted to Gond, the Wonderbringer.

Organization

Members of this organization praised Gond through their skill in carpentry, toymaking, and puppetry.
