Triumvirate

Organization
nameTriumvirate
baseOoltul, Northdark
racesPhaerimm

The Triumvirate were a group of three phaerimm who managed to escape Anauroch through a breach in the Sharn Wall. They conquered the beholder city of Ooltul in the Northdark.

Activities

In their continuing war against the beholders, the Triumvirate learned from the still-trapped phaerimm their ancient methods for creating a beholderkin known as a gouger. They also created miniature, mutated beholderkin known as eyeballs. However, the Triumvirate allowed their mutant creatures to stray out and across the Underdark.

By 1372 DR, they plotted to release the other phaerimm still trapped in Anauroch.
