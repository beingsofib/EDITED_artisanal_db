The Seven Traders were traders in Marsember as of the late 14 century DR.

History

After the spring of the , when trying to gain access to lady Joysil Ambrur, the bard and Harper Roldro Tattershar threatened Elward Daunthideir, the steward of Haelithtorntowers, her residence, with revealing the steward's involvement in the dispute between the seven traders and the tax takers of the Marsember port a season prior.
