The Amanir were a clan of cloud giants that existed during the time of Netheril.

Base of Operations

This clan lived in the northern Channel Mountains, where they acted as the leaders of a community of cloud giants.

History

In , this clan of giants began trade relationships with Netheril's waterborne city Harborage..

In , when Harborage was falling apart as a result of a volcanic rift created just outside the city's walls by phaerimm, the Amanir clan arrived with their cloud palace and saved around five thousand residents.

Members

This clan was made up of around forty members, who all had expertise in spellcasting.
