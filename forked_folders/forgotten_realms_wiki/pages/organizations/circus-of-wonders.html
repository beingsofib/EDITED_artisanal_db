The Circus of Wonders was a traveling circus run by the kenku Corvus Nightfeather in the late 15 century DR.

The circus traveled throughout Tethyr and other regions in southern Faerûn and was used by Corvus as a cover for his activities as a spy for various factions. The circus acts included clowns, historical reenactments, a strongman and aerial performances.

The Members

Prominent members around the included the clanless goliath Tobin Tok Tor, aged ranger Mattias Farseer, animal companion wyvern Trill, couple Melda and Whitey, and the twin halfling aerialists Shan and Cynda. In late spring of 1479 DR, the circus was joined by the earthsoul genasi Cephas. However, the troupe encountered a major loss of members later in a show at Argentor, prompting a temporary division of the troupe.

History

Cormyr

In light of Cephas being the target of a deadly chase, the circus temporarily divided into two, one maintaining the circus travel as scheduled and the other consisting of few individuals traveling to Calimshan. The main troupe of wagons led by Whitey and Melda traveled to Cormyr, reaching the Bridge of Fallen Men by the autumn festivals, to continue their performance. The Cephas-centered group led by Corvus teleported to the City of Spells near the Shining Sea to ward off future el Arhapan pursuers. Alongside Ariella, Corvus, Cephas, Mattias, Trill, Tobin, Shan and Cynda, the group visited this one of Corvus's factions.
