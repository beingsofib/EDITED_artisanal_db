The Broken Bone was a horde of Uruth Ukrypt orcs formed by the Brotherhood of the Scarlet Scourge.

History

In the , the Broken Bone horde attacked Waterdeep in retaliation for defilement of the orcs' sacred Ukrypt crypt by a Waterdhavian adventuring company, the Dawnbringer Company. The horde aimed to destroy Waterdeep, but they were defeated with aid from a bronze dragon, Lhammaruntosz.
