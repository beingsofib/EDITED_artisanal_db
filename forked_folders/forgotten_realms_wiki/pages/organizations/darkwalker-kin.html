Darkwalker kin

Organization
nameDarkwalker Kin
typeBloodline
racesShifters, humans

Darkwalker kin were inhabitants of the Moonshae Isles who were believed to be descended from Kazgoroth. They lived with the sensation that their souls were divided into a normal portion and a bestial portion, which did not mesh easily.

Abilities

Their wild soul enhanced their strength and gave them an intimidating presence, while the primal power in their blood could grant them regenerative abilities. As with lycanthropes, they were vulnerable to silvered weapons, which burned them if touched.

They could freely shapechange between their normal humanoid form and that of a wolf. Individuals with the experience to bring their divided souls into harmony were able to assume a nightmarish hybrid form akin to that of a werewolf.

Members

Darkwalker kin were most notably shifters, who were referred to as having the "blood of the Darkwalker". Aside from shifters, humans were most likely to be Darkwalker kin, although it was conceivable that half-elves or elves could also be among Kazgoroth's descendants. Regardless, they tended to avoid civilization and favored life in the wilderness. Such Darkwalker kin grew up hunting and fighting in the wilds, leading them to master pack-oriented tactics as well as to hone their senses and instincts as sharp as those of beasts. Those with good hearts came to revile their feral ancestry, and so secluded themselves even from their own kind as they sought ways to bury their dark lineage.
