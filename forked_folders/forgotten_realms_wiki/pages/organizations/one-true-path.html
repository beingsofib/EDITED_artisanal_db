One True Path

Organization
nameOne True Path
typeSecret cabal

The One True Path was a hidden cabal of conspirers vying for power in the mid–8 century DR.

History

In the , at a feast in Felmorel, Barundryn Harbright enthusiastically introduced the One True Path after Thessamel Arunder, a mage at the same feast, joked the reason for all mages committing the same mistakes over history was that they didn't have him to keep them from straying off the One True Path. According to Barundryn Harbright, they were a small sect of which he was a member, already holding power over a dozen towns, and with their sights set on a realm next.
