Telmark's Stalwarts

nameTelmark's Stalwarts

Telmark's Stalwarts were an adventuring company active in the late 15 century DR.

Base of Operations

They were based in Suldolphor in Calimshan.

History

Although they fluctuated in their activity, they were still active as of the late 1490s DR.

Members

The adventuring chef Maerend Skeldragon was a member of Telmark's Stalwarts.
