The Helm's Hundred was an army that operated out of Helm's Hold on the Sword Coast North during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

The group was some 100 soldiers strong.

Activities

During the spring months each year, the Helm's Hundred would venture out from Helm's Hold to Sundabar, in order to fight against the demons that infested Hellgate Keep.
