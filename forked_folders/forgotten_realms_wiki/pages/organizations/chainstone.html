The Chainstone family was one of the most powerful aristocratic houses of Athkatla, the capital of Amn, by the late 14 century DR.

Base of Operations

House Chainstone was based in Athkatla's political heart, the Scepter District. Like other powerful Scepternian houses, Chainstones' sprawling family compound was the size of a city block.
