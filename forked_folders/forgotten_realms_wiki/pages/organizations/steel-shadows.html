Steel Shadows

Organization
nameSteel Shadows
typeMercenary company
baseWaterdeep, Shadowdale

The Steel Shadows was a mercenary group originally based in Shadowdale, but by 1491 DR they had broken up after moving to Waterdeep and being unable to find work. Notable members were Jalester Silvermane and Faerrel Dunblade.
