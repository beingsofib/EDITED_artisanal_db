The Company of Frozen Trees was an adventuring band active in the early 11 century DR.

History

In the , the Company of Frozen Trees was joined by a bard named Gallow Deely. He played an enchanted instrument made by a hin master, Relias Blackseed, called The Cittern of War. It aided the adventurers with its inspiring magic in battling a young green dragon. This deed was commemorated in a ballad called The Fight of Green Thirteen, penned by Gallow. The song remained popular in the taverns of west Faerûn into the 14 century DR.
