Immerdusk

Organization
nameImmerdusk
baseArabel, Cormyr

Immerdusk was a noble house of Cormyr. It was based in Arabel.

History

The Immerdusks supported Gondegal when he tried to usurp the kingdom in 1352 DR.

During the Abraxus affair, the Immerdusks (like many noble families with country estates) favored Vangerdahast as regent instead of a ruling noble council if King Azoun IV died.

Members

  • Casplar Hundyl Immerdusk, bastard son of King Baerovus Obarskyr and founder of the Immerdusk family
  • Storm Silverhand, Marchioness Immerdusk, ennobled by King Baerovus for adopting Casplar
