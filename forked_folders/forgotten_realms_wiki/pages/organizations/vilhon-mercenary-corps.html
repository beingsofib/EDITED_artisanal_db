The Vilhon Mercenary Corps were a renowned group that traveled around the Forgotten Realms completing various contracts for noble lords and city-states.

History

In 1479 DR, it was rumored Lord Caleb Randol of Kovel Mansion commissioned the group to undermine Black Fist operations north of Phlan.
