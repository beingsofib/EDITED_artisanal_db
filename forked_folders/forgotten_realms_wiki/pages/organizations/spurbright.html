Spurbright

Organization
nameSpurbright
baseEspar, Cormyr

Spurbright was a noble house of Cormyr.

Description

The Spurbrights had a seat in Espar, and owned 20 or so miles of tenanted farms north of Espar along either side of the Way of The Dragon. Their heraldry contained a circle of four crowns at its heart, referencing the legend of the Four Floating Crowns.

Thaelder was the house wizard of the Spurbright family in the mid-14 century DR. Secretly, like many of house wizards that served Cormyrean noble houses, was a War Wizard who used his magics to monitor the family.

Notable Members

  • Aglara Spurbright, a family member who fell afoul of the Four Floating Crowns.
  • Delandra Spurbright, wife of Elvarr, mother of Torsard.
  • Elvarr Spurbright, the patriarch of the Spurbrights circa 1348 DR who conspired against the Obarskyr royal family.
  • Torsard Spurbright, Elvarr's son and avid falconer.
