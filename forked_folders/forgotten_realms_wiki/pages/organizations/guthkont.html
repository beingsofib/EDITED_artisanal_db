The Guthkont family was one of the wealthiest families in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Base of Operations

The Guthkont estate lay along the affluent Emerald Dragon Way in the heart of the Golden City.

Activities

The Guthkonts were the thirteenth-wealthiest family in Suldolphor, giving them considerable influence within the city.
