Fey guides, sometimes styled as feyguides, were individuals who made their living by helping travelers to use fey crossroads.

Activities

Fey guides usually specialized in finding and activating local fey crossroads with which they were already familiar, and their knowledge could be a replacement for needing to cast walk crossroads to locate a crossroads. They could sometimes provide additional information about the crossroads, and were often good sources of rumors or lore relating to local fey activities.

History

Feyguides in the Hordelands saw themselves as just one of many kinds of merchants dealing in the rarities of their land. They could be encountered along the Golden Way, offering to help travelers visit the region's numerous fey crossroads.
