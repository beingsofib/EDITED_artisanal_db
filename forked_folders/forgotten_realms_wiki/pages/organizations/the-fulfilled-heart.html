The Fulfilled Heart

Organization
nameThe Fulfilled Heart
baseBaldur's Gate, Sword Coast

The Fulfilled Heart was a matchmaking agency in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , master smith Gyldro Angleiron of the Rivington General smithy attempted to find a mate using the services of The Fulfilled Heart's Maestro Hartstrum. Despite Hartstrum's best attempts, the agency continuously failed to find anyone interested in a date with a simple blacksmith. In his letter to the proprietor of Rivington General, Maestro Hartstrum recommended Gyldro to commission a portrait that looked less like a blacksmith and more conventionally attractive. But even without a revised portrait, The Fulfilled Heart promised never to stop trying to arrange a date for the gruff melancholic man.
