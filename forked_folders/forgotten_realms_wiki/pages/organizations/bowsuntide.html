Bowsuntide was a wealthy human merchant family of self-styles nobles of Selgaunt in the 15 century DR.

History

In the 15 century DR, the Bowsuntide family was one of the patrons of the famed scuttling dwarf sculptor – Orgelna Highshield of Selgaunt, alongside the Ornwindsea family of human merchant nobles.
