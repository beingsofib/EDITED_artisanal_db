The Curious Eye

Organization
nameThe Curious Eye
typeBusiness

The Curious Eye was a publishing house based in the Moonshae Isles. Its owner and chief scribe was Immrult Dauntrael.

Activities

This business produced copies of many popular lorebooks and works of philosophy.
