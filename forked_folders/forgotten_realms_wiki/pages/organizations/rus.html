The Rus were an Illuskan tribe.

History

Many centuries before the 14 century DR, the Rus raided much of coastal Faerûn, with some bands even sailing the rivers further inland.

In , a group of Rus led by chieftain Yvengi were transported onto the eastern shore of Lake Ashane in Raumathar by way of a malfunctioning portal in the Whalebones. The Rus integrated with the native Rashemi of the region, and their presence was credited with sparking the insurrection that would displace Eltab in , led by the half-Rus and half-Rashemi warrior hero also named Yvengi.
