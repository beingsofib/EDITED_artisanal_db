The Dragoneye Dealing Coster was a merchant company in Faerûn.

History

This merchant company was originally founded by two brothers who did not want to ship overland to avoid pirates only to loose their goods to roadside bandits.

Base of Operations

The Dragoneye Dealing Coster was based in Cimbar, but they also had headquarters in Iriaebor and Elturel.
