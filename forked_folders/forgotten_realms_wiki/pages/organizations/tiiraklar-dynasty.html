Tiiraklar was a noble house of Tethyr that held the realm's throne. It was the fourth of the Shoon Traitorum vassals of the Shoon Imperium.

History

The family came to the throne by the marriage of the ruling Queen Vajra Korrunhel with Karazir Tiiraklar, leading to the rules of two kings. Another marriage later led to the crown passing to the Tornamn Dynasty.
