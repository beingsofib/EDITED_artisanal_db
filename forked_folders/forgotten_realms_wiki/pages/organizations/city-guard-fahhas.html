The City Guard of Fahhas served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City of Searching".

Organization

The composition of the city guard as of was 2,000 soldiers and 1,000 cavalry from the Sword of the True Gods. Other forces included 2,000 regular soldiers, 150 clerics, and three 300-man units of mamluks from the Devoted Mamluk Society. Fahhas' navy consisted of 5 large ships and 10 smaller vessels.
