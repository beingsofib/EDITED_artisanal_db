Circle of Rust and the Worm

Organization
nameCircle of Rust and the Worm
allegiancesTalos

The Circle of Rust and the Worm was a rumored organization that served the god Talos.

Known only in fearful rumor, the Circle of Rust and the Worm was a mysterious group. It was said to consist of insane sages and spellcasting mystics of a variety of religious and secular disciplines. They were entropists who sought to destroy the world. They sought the patronage of Talos the Destroyer, and received it.
