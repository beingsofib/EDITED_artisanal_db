Severed Fist

racesOrcs

The Severed Fist was an orcish tribe that inhabited the southern reaches of the Fallen Lands in the 14 century DR.

Possessions

Like most orc tribes in the Fallen Lands, the Severed Fist occasionally rode upon axebeaks. While doing so, they typically wielded lances or shortbows.

Relationships

More often than not, the Severed Fist were seen to be engaging in warfare with the other orcish tribes that inhabited the southern reaches of the Fallen Lands. However, they showed fealty to the Bloody Scar tribe and their leader King Ogrash, who ruled over the northern reaches of the Fallen Lands.
