The Iron Legion was a disbanded mercenary company by 1358 DR.

History

The Iron Legion was the combined army that fought the arcanaloth Yrkhetep in his home plane of Hades. Most of its members died in the decisive battle. Only two of the many groups that composed the Legion remained active: the Windriders and the Sisterhood of the Oaks.
