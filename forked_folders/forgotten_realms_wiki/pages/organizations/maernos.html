The Maernos () family was a human noble family in Waterdeep.

Organization

The family patriarch was Ultas Maernos in the . He was a widower, having lost his wife and children to the plague in Amn in the .

The Maernos family retained a trio of halfling adventurers that became known as the Defenders Three.

The family coat-of-arms consisted of a pair of praying hands with green sleeves and three silver spear heads on a royal blue field.

Activities

House Maernos was primarily in the moneylending and financial services business, and specialized in assisting the various temples in Waterdeep with money matters.

In 1368 DR, Lord Maernos gave significant financial help to the newly recognized prince Haedrak Rhindaun III in his bid to rescue Zaranda Star and take over the throne of Tethyr.

Base of Operations

The family villa and compound were located in the North Ward between Copper Street and the High Road about five blocks south of the Upper Towers. The north side of the compound faced Delzorin Street and the south side faced Sidle Street.

History

With no heirs, Ultas petitioned the Lord's Court in 1368 DR to be allowed to bequeath his holdings and wealth to the ten major temples and the Plinth, to be equally distributed by the Lord's Court. The family compound was to be converted into a temple specifically for use by the non-human residents of Waterdeep. After a closed-door session, the Magisters and the Lord's Court agreed to allow his request. By this time, Ultas had already started renovating his villa, buying paintings, carvings, and sculptures by elven, dwarven, and halfling artists appropriate for a place of worship.

After Ultas' passing, the Maernos compound became known as Holyhands House and was run by the Defenders Three. As of the 1490s DR, it was still in operation.

Beneficiaries

The temples that were recipients of Ultas Maernos' largess were:

  • The Font of Knowledge (Oghma)
  • The Halls of Justice (Tyr)
  • The House of Heroes (Tempus)
  • The House of Inspired Hands (Gond)
  • The House of the Moon (Selûne)
  • The House of Wonder (Mystra)
  • The Plinth (all faiths)
  • The Shrines of Nature (Mielikki and Silvanus)
  • The Spires of the Morning (Lathander)
  • The Temple of Beauty (Sune)
  • The Tower of Luck (Tymora)
