The Colchar were a tribe of orcs in the Goblin Marches in the 14 century DR.

Relationships

The Colchar had a good cooperative relationship with the Neidlig goblin tribe, and settled near Neidlig Citadel.

They were targeted for raids in particular by the Fenlis goblins, who sought to subjugate all other creatures beneath goblin tyranny.
