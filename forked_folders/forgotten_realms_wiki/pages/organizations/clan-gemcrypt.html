Clan Gemcrypt

Organization
nameClan Gemcrypt
baseSondarr, Shanatar

Clan Gemcrypt was an ancient dwarven clan of Shanatar in the sub-kingdom of Sondarr.

History

The clan emerged in the realm of Sondorr, and dominated the eastern end of the realm beneath the interior Starspire Mountains while Clan Ghalmrin and Clan Trollspear controlled regions to the west. The three clans' ardukes were the regents for the ruling Triumvirate for most of the realm's history following the death of King Ebersar. Gemcrypt lasted until not long after the disastrous Clash at Earthrift, when the accidental killing of two elven princes in the battle caused a deadly feud with the elves of the Forest of Tethir. This led to the extinction of the clan around the 50 century before Dale Reckoning.
