The Royal Vagabonds was a secret Shou Lung spy agency.

Description

The Royal Vagabonds were a secret clan in service to the Shou Emperor. All members of the clan were ninja. They were not an official part of the government but still reported to the Minister of State Security and the Chief Imperial Censor. They did most of the difficult missions that the Emperor ordered. Nobody outside the clan could positively identify a vagabond. The only positive means of identification was a magical sigil borne on the chest of each man.
