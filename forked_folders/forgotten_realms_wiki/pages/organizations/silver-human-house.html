Silver (human house)

Organization
nameSilver
aliasesSuzail, Cormyr

The Silver family was an ancient Cormyrian house that split into three separate houses sometime between 26 DR and 245 DR. These houses were Crownsilver, Huntsilver, and Truesilver.

Members

  • Jaquor Silver, twin brother to Tristan.
  • Tristan Silver, twin brother to Jaquor.
