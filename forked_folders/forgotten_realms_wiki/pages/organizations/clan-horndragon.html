Clan Horndragon was an extinct dragonheir clan of the Sword Lands of Laerakond.

History

Clan Horndragon was one of the oldest clans of Laerakond. Its members were descendants of the first human roostmasters of the primordials, who lived millennia before the Spellplague.

In the late years of the 15th century DR clan Horndragon was considered by most to have been completely wiped out by their enemies of old, the dragons.
