The Bordjias were a tribe of the Bedine that lived in the great Anauroch desert of north Faerûn.

Relationships

The Bordjias were known allies of the Qahtani tribe of El Ma'ra.

History

The sheikh of the Bordjias had 10 of his camels stolen by a young warrior of the Qahtani tribe in the . Only a few Bordjias warriors were slain in the raid, a number not likely to cause strife between the two tribes.
