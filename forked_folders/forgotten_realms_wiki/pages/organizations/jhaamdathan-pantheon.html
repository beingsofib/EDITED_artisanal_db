Jhaamdathan pantheon

Organization
nameJhaamdathan Pantheon
leaderAuppenser

The Jhaamdathan pantheon was one of the human proto-pantheons that eventually merged with others to form the Faerûnian pantheon. Its members were worshiped in Jhaamdath, around what was later called the Vilhon Reach and Dragon Coast.

Members

  • Auppenser, god of moderation, inner balance, self-reflection, and reason.
  • Murdane, goddess of pragmatism and reason. Lover to Helm.
  • Valigan Thirdborn, deity of anarchy.
  • Arrtyr Judge of All, god of justice.
