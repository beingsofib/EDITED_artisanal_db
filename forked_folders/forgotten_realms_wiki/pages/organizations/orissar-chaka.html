The Orissar chaka was a prominent chaka within the Shining Lands of southeast Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR and a member of the Chakas of 31.

Base of Operations

The Orissar chaka was based in the port city of Turelve on the Golden Water and River Sarren in western Durpar.

Activity

This chaka was composed of adventurers who eventually delved into the gemstone business and traded horses with Dambrath.
