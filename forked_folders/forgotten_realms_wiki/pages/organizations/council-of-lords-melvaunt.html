The Council of Lords was the ruling body of government in Melvaunt made up of the city's wealthiest merchants.

Location

The Council of Lords operated out of the Asberyth in the center of the city.

Members

A new member could join the Council by either replacing and old Lord or creating a new seat for the sum of two million gp. A new seat on the Council was permanent once created. When a member died a vote was held on who should fill the vacant seat.

All prospective candidates for the Council of Lords needed to meet the following requirements:

  • They must be innocent of causing the death of a Council member.
  • They must not associate with any of Melvaunt's enemies, particularly Zhentil Keep.
  • They must show their support of the Council by offering a gift of 100,000 gp.

The Council of Lords had 21 seats as of 1367 DR. They met on a monthly basis but were reluctant to intervene in the affairs of their members.
