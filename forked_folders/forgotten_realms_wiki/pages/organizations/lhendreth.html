Lhendreth

The Lhendreths were a cabal in Saerloon in Sembia during the late 15 century DR.

Rumors & Legends

According to the servant Osbur, his Lord Olgarth Oldbridle left him with a warning for his son, Elbert Oldbridle, should he die. Said warning concerned the presence of Sembian cabals seeking power by means of subverting courtiers and specifically mentioned the Lhendreths of Saerloon.
