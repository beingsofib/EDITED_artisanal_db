The Tuskar family was a prominent Amnian business family of Murann during the 14 century DR.

Activities

The Tuskars were a successful family in Murann that owned seven taverns that also served as gambling establishments.

Members

  • Lord Gerry Tuskar, the family leader in 1370 DR.
  • Lord Otall Tuskar, Gerry's second son.
