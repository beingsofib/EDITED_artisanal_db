Fnebish Armada

Organization
nameThe Fnebish Armada
typeMercenary fleet
baseWhamite Isles, Sea of Fallen Stars

The Fnebish Armada was an unaffiliated fleet of mercenaries from the Whamite Isles. Along with the Fnebish Legion, they participated in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles
