The Cult of Istishia was a small cult in Hawa who worshiped Istishia in 1367 DR.

Activities

Members of the cult, through their own choices or by magical coercion, were involved in kidnapping inhabitants of Hawa in an attempt to make converts out of them. The cult typically targeted children found wandering the market.

Base of Operations

The Cult of Istishia was based in Hawa within the Genie's Turban off of the coast of northwest Zakhara.

Hierarchy

The hierarchy for the Cult of Istishia was broken down as follows:

  • Faizah: High Priestess
  • Baccira: Lesser Priestess
  • Terrog: Lesser Priest
  • Jessiyah: Acolyte
  • Tuka: Elder Brother
  • Setara bint Aasim: Holy slayer
  • Sahlah: Holy slayer
  • Shadha: Holy slayer
