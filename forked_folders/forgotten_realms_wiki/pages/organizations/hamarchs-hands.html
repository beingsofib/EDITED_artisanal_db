The Hamarch's Hands were a group of adventurers striving to rid Urmlaspyr of all evil in 1358 DR. They were named after Urmlaspyr's elected mayor, the Hamarch.

Activities

These adventurer's often tangled with wicked organizations including smugglers from Westgate, the Zhentarim, and the Red Wizards from Thay. The would often intercede as a hit team when the Helms failed.

Organization

Destrin Chamaerlyn was the head of two dozen other individuals of the Hamarch's Hands in 1358 DR.
