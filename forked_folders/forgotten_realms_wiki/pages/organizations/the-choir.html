The Choir was a cult of aberrations based in Akanûl who worshiped a plaguechanged sirine they called the Blue Lady.

Members

Members of the Choir had the ability to influence others through their strange songs. The Choir used wolf-like spellplague changed creatures known as Oenaths to track prey in the wilds.

Activities

In 1479 DR members of the cult traveled to Airspur where they recruited a number of Airspur's residents. Those who didn't travel back to Tohrepur with the Choir were kidnapped. The Choir was disbanded in 1479 DR when a small group of adventurers led by Uthalion traveled to the cult's shrine in Tohrepur and killed the Choir's leader, Lord Khault.
