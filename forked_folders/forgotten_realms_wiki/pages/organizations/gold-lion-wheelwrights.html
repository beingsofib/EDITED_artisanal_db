Gold Lion Wheelwrights

Organization
nameGold Lion Wheelwrights
typeBusiness
baseSaelmur, Land of the Lions

The Gold Lion Wheelwrights was a wheel-making business based in Saelmur in the Land of the Lions in the late 14 century DR.

Though there were no outward signs, the business was directly controlled by the leadership of the Rundeen, a mercantile organization, known as the Yrshelem. The only clue was that it was named for the gold lion, indicating it had once been an independent business that had been taken over and renamed.
