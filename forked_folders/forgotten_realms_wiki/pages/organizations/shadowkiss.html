Shadowkiss

Organization
nameShadowkiss
baseLylorn, the Dusk Ports

The Shadowkiss was a criminal organization that operated across the Dragon Sea. Their base of operations was the city of Lylorn, in the Dusk Ports of Laerakond.

Activities

The Shadowkiss engaged in illegal activities such as drug and poisons selling across Laerakond.

Notable members

  • Nandrel Gostur
  • Zasheena Drethyl
