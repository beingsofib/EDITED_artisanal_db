The Company of Night were a group of Sembite adventurers who operated circa 1369 DR. They had a strong rivalry with the Stag Runners, another Sembite company.

History

In the , the Company of Night attended a party hosted by Lord Partic Thistle at Thistleflame Manor in Suzail. The occasion was King Azoun Obarskyr IV's birthday, and Lord Thistle hoped to improve the standing of adventuring companies in Cormyr by having them present themselves as a gesture of fealty to the King.
