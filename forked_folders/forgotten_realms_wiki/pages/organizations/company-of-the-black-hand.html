Company of the Black Hand

Organization
nameCompany of the Black Hand
typeAdventuring company

The Company of the Black Hand was a short-lived group of adventurers in the 14 century DR. Most of their members were killed by monsters while exploring ruins in the North, some time prior to 1357 DR.
