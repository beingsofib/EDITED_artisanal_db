The Ladies of Compassion were Lesser Immortals in the Celestial Bureaucracy who served the goddess Kwan Ying.

Description

These spirits appeared as dignified maidens wearing white robes. They wore their long hair loose. They were seen carrying white cloths of silk for the binding of wounds.

Activities

The role of the White Ladies was to ease pain and suffering among both mortals and immortals, doing the bidding of Kwan Ying.

History

In Shou Year 2606 (1356 DR), the kensai An Ching Wang was visited by a Lady of Compassion, who encouraged her to follow her lover on his journey of repentance, telling her, "Follow your heart and things will work out."
