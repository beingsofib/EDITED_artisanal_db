The City Guard of the Free City of Umara served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City of Knights".

Organization

The composition of the city guard as of was 3,000 elite warriors of the Astok tribe, 2,000 mercenaries, four units of mamluks numbering 500 soldiers each, 600 cavalry, 2,000 local militia, and a navy consisting of 6 ships. The Atoki warriors served as the palace guard.

Activities

The ships of the navy patrolled Al-Yatir River and the coast of the Great Sea.
