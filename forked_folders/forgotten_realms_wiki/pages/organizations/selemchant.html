Selemchant

Organization
nameHouse Selemchant
baseAmn
leaderThayze Selemchant

House Selemchant was a powerful family of Amn in the mid–14 through the late 15 centuries DR.

Lands

Along with the other noble merchant families, they were prominent and powerful in the capital, Athkatla. They owned land by the Tethir Road, between Trademeet and Murann, north of the Alibakkar Estates. During the Amnian occupation of Port Nyanzaru during the 15 century DR, House Selemchant operated as a trading company vying for financial and political influence within the colony.

Activities

Clarorna Amaerityl, a Wizardess of Athkatla, who was often hired by House Selemchant as a magically-inclined enforcer when mercantile frictions were in danger of erupting in violence in the late 15 century DR.

Members

  • Thayze Selemchant, Lord of House Selemchant (1332–1362 DR), Meisarch and founder of the Council of Six.
  • Sulima Selemchant, a young member of the family active during the late 15 century DR.
