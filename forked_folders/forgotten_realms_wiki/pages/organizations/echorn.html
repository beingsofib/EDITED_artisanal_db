The Echorn was an elven house of Myth Drannor that later migrated to Selu'Maraar.

History

House Echorn was one of the twelve First Houses of Cormanthor, the fourth by seniority.

The clan supported the School of Arts in Semberholme since its founding in .

After the laying of the mythal, the clan moved some of its members towards Semberholme. The rest moved to Selu'Maraar after the Fall of Myth Drannor.
