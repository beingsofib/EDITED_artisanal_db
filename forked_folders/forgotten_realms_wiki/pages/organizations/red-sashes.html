The Red Sashes were a vigilante group that was based in Waterdeep.

Organization

The individual cells of the Sashes, one for each ward of the city, each answered to an authority known only as "The One".

Activities

Not an impulsive or excessively violent group, the Red Sashes aimed to arrest the guilty and free the innocent, typically when the City Watch failed in these aims.

Membership

Prospective members were approached by the Red Sashes and were forced to swear a loyalty oath to "The One". These invitations were only given after approval of the member was reached after much observation. Members were not allowed to reveal any secrets of the group to outsiders and had to pay a 5 gold piece per-month membership fee.

"The One"

Only a few of the Lords of Waterdeep were aware of "The One's" true identity. These included Mirt, Kitten, Nindil, Sammereza and, before his death, Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun.

History

The Red Sashes were formed by Durnan in 1312 DR to fulfill his own personal needs.
