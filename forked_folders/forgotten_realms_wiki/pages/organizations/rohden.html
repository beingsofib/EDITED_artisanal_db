House Rohden was a minor merchant organization and a noble family from the city of Arrabar in the Vilhon Reach.

History

In the late 14 century DR, Horial Rohden was the scion of House Rohden. By the , Horial was the member of the Waukeenar mercenary organization – the Order of the Sapphire Crescent, seving alongside two fellow noble scions, Vambran Matrell of House Matrell and Adyan Mercatio of House Mercatio.
