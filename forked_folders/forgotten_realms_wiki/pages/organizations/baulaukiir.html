Baulaukiir was a powerful merchant family of Archenbridge.

Activities

It was said that the Baulaukiirs as well as the Ithrymm, and the Tantal families outshone Sembian merchants in trade.

Base of Operations

The Baulaukiirs had a grand family estate in Archenbridge.

History

In the late 14 century DR, the Baulaukiirs were considered one of the wealthiest and most powerful families on Archenbridge.
