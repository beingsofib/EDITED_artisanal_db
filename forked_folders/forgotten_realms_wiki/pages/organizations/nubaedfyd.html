Nubaedfyd

Organization
nameNubaedfyd
baseMurann, Amn
racesHuman

The Nubaedfyd family was a prominent family in the city of Murann in Amn in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

They worked in whaling, ship outfitting, and producing oil.

Members

Circa 1370 DR, the family was led by Lord Segar Eeyescee.
