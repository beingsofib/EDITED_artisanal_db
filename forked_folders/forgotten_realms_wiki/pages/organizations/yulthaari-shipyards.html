Yulthaari Shipyards

Organization
nameYulthaari Shipyards
typeShipyard
baseAlmraiven, Calimshan

The Yulthaari Shipyards was a ship-construction business in Almraiven in Calimshan in the late 14 century DR.

It was owned and operated by elves, but in truth was directly controlled by the leadership of the Rundeen, a mercantile organization, known as the Yrshelem, though there were no outward signs of this. The only clue was that it was named for the yulthaar, indicating it had once been an independent business that had been taken over and renamed.
