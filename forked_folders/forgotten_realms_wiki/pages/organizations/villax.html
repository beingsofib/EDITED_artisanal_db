Villax was a powerful Amnian mercantile family during the 11 century DR.

History

The house made history during the last days of the Torlath dynasty, when their archrivals, the Braethars, installed their respective puppet claimant. The Braethars had King Niskam the Doomed killed and replaced with King Rhor the Lackey in the , and the Villaxes responded in the , by slaying him and usurping the throne with King Dornak the Puppet King, who didn't last the year before being killed himself. The situation was ultimately resolved with King Erdhinn Wavegold I taking control of the realm.
