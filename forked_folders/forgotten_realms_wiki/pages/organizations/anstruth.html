The College of Anstruth () was a traditional bardic college in Sundabar.

Base of Operations

Even centuries after Anstruth's closure, the city of Sundabar remained famous for the quality of its musical instruments.

Possessions

The Anstruth harp, one of the Instruments of the Bards, was named after this college. It had been created by the legendary bard Falataer to test and reward his students who achieved mastery of the Anstruth level of skill.

History

The College of Anstruth was one of the seven elder bardic colleges, dating back to Falataer, who had established it as the penultimate level of his bardic college. The College of Anstruth ceased its operations sometime after .

As of the , the only remaining structure from the original college was a concert hall that had been converted into a warehouse.

Members

  • The half-elf bard Iriador Wintermist studied at Anstruth in the 11 century DR but dropped out to join the Harpers before earning the degree of Magnum Alumnus.
  • Sir Riordan Parnell was a member of the Anstruth College.
