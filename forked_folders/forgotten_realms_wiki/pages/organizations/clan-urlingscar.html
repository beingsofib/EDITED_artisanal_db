Clan Urlingscar was one of the prominent dragonborn clans of Relmaur, in Laerakond.

History

Clan Urlingscar was the traditional ruling clan of Relmaur. However, as of 1479 DR, the head of the clan, Harangor Urlingscar, allowed members of other clans to participate in the government of the nation, by appointing members to the Crown Council.

Known members

  • Harangor Urlingscar, the head of the clan in 1479 DR.
