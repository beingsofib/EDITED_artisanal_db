The Mandible was the most powerful gang operating in Skullport in the late 15th century DR. Next to the Skulls of Skullport The Mandible was the closest thing to a government the town had. The gang took its name from a sword known as Dragon's Tooth which was found in the ruins of the old town. The sword served as a badge of office for the gang's leader and, since gang members bickered frequently amongst themselves, possession of the sword changed hands often. As of 1479 DR, the Mandibles worked on a lift that was designed to ferry goods back and forth between Waterdeep and Skullport but additionally bypassing the dangers of Undermountain and the Lapel Sea.
