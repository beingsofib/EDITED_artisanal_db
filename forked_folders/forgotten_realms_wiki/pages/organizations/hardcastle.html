Hardcastle was a noble house of Cormyr. The family was loyal to the Obarskyrs.

Notable Members

  • Ildamoar Hardcastle, head of the family in the mid-14 century DR
  • Guldrin Hardcastle, elder son of Ildamoor
  • Rathtar Hardcastle, younger son of Ildamoor
