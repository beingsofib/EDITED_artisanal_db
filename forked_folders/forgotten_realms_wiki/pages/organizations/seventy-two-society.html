The Seventy-Two Society was a secret society in Shou Lung.

Description

The Seventy-Two Society (its name chosen only for feng-shui reasons) was officially a merchant club in Wa K'an. In truth, it sought the independence of Wa K'an from Shou Lung and was massively infiltrated by agents of the shogun of Wa.
