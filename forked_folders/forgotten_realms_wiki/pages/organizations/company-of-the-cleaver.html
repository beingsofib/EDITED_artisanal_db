The Company of the Cleaver was a moderately successful adventuring troupe active in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

The saw the formation of the adventuring company that gained renown in Cormyr – the Knights of Myth Drannor, but the same year birthed several less known organizations, including the Company of the Cleaver.
