Ezra (deity)

Organization
nameChurch of Ezra
symbolSword and shield
alignmentLawful neutral

The Church of Ezra was a religion in the Domains of Dread centered around the worship of a woman named Ezra. It had an order of priests known as Anchorites.

History

Around the s, a group of adventurers from Ravens Bluff encountered a werespider that had been infected alongside a young boy in the Domains of Dread. However, the young boy was protected from some of the affliction by the holy symbol of Ezra he carried, and thus was able to fight off the infection without becoming a lycanthrope.
