Brothers and Sisters of the Quill

Organization
nameBrothers and Sisters of the Quill
baseMulhorand, Old Empires
favored deityThoth

The Brothers and Sisters of the Quill were an order of wizards and scriveners of Thoth in Mulhorand. These priests and wizards were charged with retrieving lost knowledge and protecting the secrets of their nation in the lands beyond Mulhorand's borders.
