The Sultan's Own, also called the Jackals, were a vicious organized military force located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire.

Organization

This light infantry unit was a formation of 4,000 salamanders.

Activities

These security troops were infamous for their sadistic nature. They were used to violently quell unrest or riots among the poor citizens of the city when the Illuminated were not successful.

Base of Operations

The City of Brass served as the base of the Sultan's Own, specifically the Long Castle within Ashlarks.
