The Company of the Howling Harpoon was a mercenary band that aided the city of Hlondeth against Turmish invaders in 146 DR.

History

In the armies of Turmish under the command of Lord Dempster besieged the nation's old enemy – the city-state of Hlondeth. While the front assault was broken by the city's defenders, the Company of the Howling Harpoon flanked Turmite armies. The mercenaries destroyed Dempster's food supplies and siege mechanisms, forcing the invading army to retreat.
