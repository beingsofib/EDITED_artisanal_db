Ticklebeard was a dwarvish clan, famous for the Crazy Wilbur technique of fighting.

History

By the late 15 century DR, Clan Ticklebeard had developed the Crazy Wilbur. The technique involved the returning dwarvish weapon enchantment. These enchanted weapons, like returning pikes, were allowed or forced to be swallowed by their opponents. Then, the fighters called the returning weapons back, making the flight back as ready and messy as possible.
