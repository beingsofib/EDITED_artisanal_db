The Manticore Court was a body of government in the coastal town of Tulmon on the Lake of Steam in south Faerûn.

History

The court was heavily financed by many of rival Suldophor's wealthiest families, ensuring Tulmon's neighbors were represented. This usually came in the form of Tulmon's crafters producing what Suldophor wanted or needed.

Defenses

A pair of enchanted manticore statues guarded the entrance to the court building, giving the court its name.
