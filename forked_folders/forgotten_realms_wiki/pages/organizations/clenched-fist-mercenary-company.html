The Clenched Fist was a mercenary and adventuring company that was a subsidiary of the Rundeen mercantile organization in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. Active in the Shining Sea region, they served the Rundeen as enforcers and dungeon delvers.

Organization

The Clenched Fist, both the leaders, individual members, and the band as a whole, were sponsored and actively supported by the Rundeen. They were controlled directly by the First Yrshelem, the second tier of Rundeen leadership, but technically ranked below the Mitalibbar, their collectors and watchers, like their other subsidiaries. Although technically an independent company on a full-time retainer, the Clenched Fist were treated as employees and thus were effectively a semiprivate army.

The senior members of the Clenched Fist led the company and each commanded their own smaller bands or cells of 8–12 combatants. They selected their personnel according to capabilities, temperament, and suitability for a mission. The leaders understood they were simply muscle for the Rundeen but were happy with this arrangement, provided they and their underlings got their money.

The lower ranks were fighters and rogues, all lesser warriors and one-time gladiatorial slaves who'd all been bought by the Rundeen for the purpose. The company had well over 100 members as of 1370 DR.

Activities

The Rundeen leaders assigned the Clenched Fist a range of specific missions to carry out. These included such shady jobs pressuring creditors and members who'd fallen behind or fallen short in their payments; enforcing rules in Rundeen-controlled markets, both to maintain the influence of the Rundeen and limit that of others; banditry of caravans on trade routes; forcing "protection" on caravans; robbing the safe holds of rival merchants; and even exploring dungeons. For the latter, they were often supplied with maps, information, weapons, and armor, and would split the loot 50:50 with the Rundeen in exchange.

Tactics

They were renowned for their love of battle.

Relationships

The Clenched Fist were one of a number of mercenary and adventuring bands, along with the Fist of Rundeen and Many Long Arms, formed by the Rundeen with the express purpose of opposing the Harpers.

History

The Clenched Fist began as a dozen-strong band of thieves and warriors based in Schamedar, Calimshan. Rowdy and greedy, they took any job they came across just for the coin. That was until the Rundeen hired them on a permanent basis and started giving them missions as if they were salaried employees. The founding members didn't mind, as it meant a constant income.

They developed in experience and acquired more personnel, growing swiftly in only a few years. The original members, those who'd survived, worked as commanders of smaller bands of their own.

In the , the Clenched Fist fought a band of Harpers including the bard Mintiper Moonsilver, who had nothing good to say about them. They would go on to have several clashes, but were defeated several times by the Harpers, who saw them as no longer effective by 1367 DR.

Despite this, the Clenched Fist remained in operation as of 1370 DR. Nine members of the original twelve still lived and led the company.

Members

Known leaders and founding members of the Clenched Fist were:

  • Yusuf al Memnon, a human fighter and hairy bear of a man;
  • El Yestaadi, "the Huntress", a half-elf fighter/thief, graceful and deadly;
  • Daud, a human thief, an amnesiac and spy;
  • Astil adh Saida al Manshaka, a human fighter, a former slave of great strength and tactical skill;
  • Wagym, a wereboar fighter and former slave gladiator; and
  • Crymara al Rundeen, a human thief, a seductress and knife thrower.
