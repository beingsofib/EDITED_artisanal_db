The Braeder Merchant Collective was an organization of traders in Silverymoon during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Merchant collectives in Silverymoon acted akin to costers, except rather than forming caravans, they simply bought and sold in coordination. All of them worked in numerous trades, and none of them were monopolistic.
