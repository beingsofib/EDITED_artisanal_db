Red Sail Coster

Organization
nameRed Sail Coster
baseTurmish & the Vast
leaderHamil Alderheart & Geran Hulmaster

The Red Sail Coster was a small trading company founded by Hamil Alderheart and Geran Hulmaster in the late 15 century DR. The company dealt in trade between Turmish and the Vast.
