The Kimball family were the traditional Ffolk royalty of the Kingdom of Moray in the Moonshae Isles.

History

The Kimballs ruled Moray since at least the 11 century DR.

A lord of the Kimball family founded Graystone on the island of Gwynneth in the , and the surrounding moorland was named in his honor.

In the , King Dynnegall of Moray and all of his heirs were assassinated by Razfallow on the orders of Cyndre, thus ending the royal line.
