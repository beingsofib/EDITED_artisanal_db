The Twelve was an arcane order of wizards, artificers, and sages in the world of Eberron. They were devoted to researching and applying magic both modern and ancient.

History

During the mid-1480s DR of Faerûn, the fiendish Lords of Dust conspired to break into the Spinner's Prison and managed to partially free the Spinner of Shadows. This led to Lolth herself forming the Rift Between Worlds, connecting the prison to the Demonweb Pits and allowing the Spinner to escape. Worse, the rift continued to expand and threatened to eventually destroy Eberron as well.

The Twelve joined the Church of the Silver Flame and the Gatekeepers in trying to study and contain the portal, though they could make little sense of the situation. The Gatekeeper Tavnar Kronnak then decided to send a team of heroes through the rift and hopefully find a solution. The heroes ended up meeting Elminster, found their way to Eveningstar, and fought against Lolth as she attempted to conquer the the Weave.
