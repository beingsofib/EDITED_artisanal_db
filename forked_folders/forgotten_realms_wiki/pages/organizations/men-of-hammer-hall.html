Men of Hammer Hall

Organization
nameMen of Hammer Hall
baseHammer Hall, Delimbiyr Vale
formedBefore 1370 DR

The Men of Hammer Hall was an adventuring company hailing from Waterdeep that operated in the North during the mid—14 century DR. They operated out of a wooden cabin, of their own construction, that they called Hammer Hall.

Activities

For several years the adventurers led expeditions into the nearby dwarven settlement that fell into ruin, the Halls of the Hammer.

Between their forays underground, the company regrouped and rested in their outpost. On a number of occasions they fought off fearsome creatures, such as trolls, orcs, and even bugbears.

History

Sometime before the , the Men of Hammer Hall ventured north and abandoned their namesake home.
