Organization
nameKeskrel

baseMarsember, Cormyr

House Keskrel was one of the noble families of Cormyr. House Keskrel was one of the oldest noble families of Marsember, although a minor one.

History

House Keskrel were known for they loyalty to House Obarskyr, and were ennobled because of their martial prowess.

Members

  • Rordurn Keskrel was the first of the Keskrel lords.
