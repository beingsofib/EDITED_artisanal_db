Neddic Legion

Organization
nameThe Neddic Legion
typeMilitary
baseAl-Ubaid, the Whamite Isles, Sea of Fallen Stars

The Neddic Legion was a military group from Al-Ubaid in the Whamite Isles, in the Sea of Fallen Stars. They represented Al-Ubaid in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles
