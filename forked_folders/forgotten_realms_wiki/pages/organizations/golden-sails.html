The Golden Sails served as the navy of Suldolphor in eastern Calimshan during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Base of Operations

The Golden Sails were based out of Suldolphor, the Golden City, and were active along Talagath's Gauntlet.

Organization

By the , the Golden Sails were composed of a dozen well-trained quick cutters. The sleek ships were aided by members of the Flame Guard, as well as by special spells developed by Neirshyn Fallowfar that prevented them from capzising.

Activities

Ships from the Golden Sails protected Suldolphor's harbor and their trade interests on the waters between the Lake of Steam and the Shining Sea.
