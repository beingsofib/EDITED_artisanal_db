Vi'oontu

Organization
nameVi'oontu
baseManchar, Koryo
enemiesPanthers of Xi

The Vi'oontu were a ninja clan based in the town of Manchar in Koryo. Their name meant "evening swallow that calls death".

Around 1357 DR, the Vi'oontu waged an ongoing war with the Panthers of Xi, who dominated the Vorkani Mountains to the north.
