Rhinoceros Tribe

Organization
nameRhinoceros Tribe
baseThe Shaar, south Faerûn
racesHuman
sourceShining South

The Rhinoceros Tribe was one of the large groups of hunters in the Shaar. The roamed the areas south of the Chondalwood between the Landrise and the Great Rift. Their raiders would unleash a devastating attack when charging at their opponents.
