The Order of the Fiery Heart was part of the standing army of the efreet in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire.

Organization

This elite unit numbered 500 griffon-riding troops. Many of the members were efreet nobles, and even efreet priests or sorcerers.

Activities

The Order of the Fiery Heart was trusted with the most important missions for the Sultan.

They served a wide range of roles, depending on the mission. Great honors were bestowed upon members of this unit, up to and including marriage to one of the Sultan's daughters.

Base of Operations

The City of Brass served as the base of the Order of the Fiery Heart.
