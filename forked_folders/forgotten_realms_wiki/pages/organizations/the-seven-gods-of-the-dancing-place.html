The Seven Gods of the Dancing Place was the name of the divine alliance between Corellon, Mielikki, Mystra, Oghma, Selûne, Silvanus, and Tymora. This alliance was created when these deities met in the sacred glade known as the Dancing Place. There, they extended their blessings to mortals who vowed to battle evil, thus creating the first Harpers.
