The Queens' Dynasty, also known as the Queens' Era, was a period of Tethyr's history defined by its ruling queens of the House of Ithal-Strohm-Bormul.

History

The era began with King Strohm V's daughter, Queen Alisande of the Bormuls. She was followed by three more queens renowned for their wisdom and diplomacy, who were remembered for centuries to come by their fair laws and governance. The peace and prosperity was ended suddenly when their cousin Mhoaran slew King Teremir I and siezed the throne for himself, leading to the assent of the fractious Lions' Dynasty.
