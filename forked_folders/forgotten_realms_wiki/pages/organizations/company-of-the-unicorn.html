The Company of the Unicorn was an adventuring troupe that was active in Cormyr sometime before 1348 DR.

History

The history of the little town of Eveningstar, Cormyr, and its Haunted Halls was known for several great battles between adventuring companies and various unsavory organizations that used the Halls for their purposes. One such battle took place sometime before the . The battle was a bombastic fireball-filled struggle between the Company of the Unicorn and a group of nine mysterious levitating wizards adorned in black robes. The combat took place in front of the Haunted Halls' entrance.
