The White Pack was a pack of good-aligned, Selûnite werewolves that served under the network of altruistic beasts known as the Fangshields during the 14 century DR. They were distinguished by gray and white fur while in their bestial form.

Members

This group of lupine lycanthropes were led by the alpha female Brena Whitebrow. She had been grooming her son, Brenvol to take over as the pack's leader following her time as leader.
