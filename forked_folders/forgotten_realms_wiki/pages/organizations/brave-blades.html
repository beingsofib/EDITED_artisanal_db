History

The Brave Blades were an adventuring band in the early 3 century DR. In the , their wizard Lhangaern, accidentally killed himself with a potion he brewed; he was replaced by a priestess, Elmara (secretly Prince Elminster Aumar of Athalantar). On the , they fought and slew the Bright Shields band of adventurers in Baerlith, several of their numbers dying in the confrontation.

Later, they adventured into the Floating Tower.
