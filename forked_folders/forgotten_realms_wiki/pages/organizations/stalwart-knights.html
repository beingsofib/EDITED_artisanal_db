The Stalwart Knights was a Helmite order of warriors and priests that comprised an entire division of the Champions Vigilant

Organization

The Stalwart Knights were based out of The Home of the Stalwarts, a temple dedicated to Helm, located in the town of Velen on the Dragon's Head Peninsula. The organization consisted of 100 priests and 50 warriors circa 1370 DR. The Chevangard often exchanged its members with both warriors and priests of the Stalwart Knights. The Stalwart Knights performed numerous pompous rituals, one of which was the change of guard at The Home of the Stalwarts, done four times a day. Notably, one of these guard changes was called the Vigil, when fully armored knights lined the temple's battlements to watch the passage and the setting of the sun.

History

The Home of the Stalwarts was a historic structure in Velen and was considered by many to be "the anchor on which Velen was built." The Stalwart Knights served as Velen's guards and militia, protecting the city from the constant looming threat of pirates. This protection of the city allowed Velen to grow and prosper. By the late 14 century DR, the Stalwart Knights kept peace in the lands adjacent to Velen, protecting them from thieves, smugglers, pirates, and brigands. Locals believed that the Stalwart Knights were the only thing that stopped the thieves' guilds from taking over Velen.

Members

Circa 1370 DR, the Stalwart Knights, as well as the lay-members of the temple, were led by Overblade Orrdun Fistright, a warrior-priest of Helm, who inherited the position from the locally much-beloved Orm Talath.
