Clan Undurr was one of the dwarven clans from the Deep Realm of the Great Rift.

Base of Operations

The family dwelled primarily in the city of Glitterdelve.

Members

  • Barrowin Undurr, a cleric of Gorm Gulthyn and noted adventurer of the late 15 century DR.
