The Ysnam chaka was a prominent chaka within the Shining Lands of southeast Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR and a member of the Chakas of 31.

Base of Operations

They were based in the capital city of Heldapan on the Golden Water in central Durpar.

Activity

The Ysnam chaka was involved in construction, stonework, and shipyards.
