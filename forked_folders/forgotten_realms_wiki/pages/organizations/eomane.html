The Eomanes of Baldur's Gate were one of the rich patriar families that composed the city's wealthy noble-class. They earned their riches from the first-class perfumery they owned, along with manufactories that produced oil from fish and whales.

Their estate, the Eomane House, was located in the Manorborn district of the Upper City.

Notable Members

15 Century

  • Nysene Eomane, the entitled noblewoman often invited Baldurian status-seekers to the family estate for revels and cruelly tortured them, without causing any severe physical harm.
  • Sarken Eomane, a member of the Parliament of Peers in the 15 century DR.
