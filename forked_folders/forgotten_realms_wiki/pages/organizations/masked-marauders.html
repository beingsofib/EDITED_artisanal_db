The Masked Marauders were an evil adventuring company in Myth Drannor in 1370 DR.

Members

The four members of the group were:

  • Gaurldar Ravenhand, the leader;
  • Ilueth Dragonil, a wizard;
  • Irlon Shulstarr, a thief; and
  • Llurella Shulstarr, a thief sister of Irlon.

History

The Masked Marauders operated in Thay but in 1370 DR they quickly escaped from that country. After a journey through Impiltur and Calaunt, they diverted a ship to Myth Drannor, where they hoped to gain many treasures from the ruins or from other adventurers.
