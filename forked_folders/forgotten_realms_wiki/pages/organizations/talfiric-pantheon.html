Talfiric pantheon

Organization
nameTalfiric Pantheon

The Talfiric pantheon was one of the human proto-pantheons that eventually merged with others to form the Faerûnian pantheon. Its members were worshiped in the Western Heartlands.

Members

  • Tempus, deity of war.
