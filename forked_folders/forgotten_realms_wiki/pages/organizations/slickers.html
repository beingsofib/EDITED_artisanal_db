The Slickers was a secretive thieves' guild from Deepburrow in Great Bhaerynden that operated in the Underdark during the late 14 century DR.

Activities

The Slickers focused their efforts on exploiting traders and other foreigners for their money. They trafficked stolen goods through Deepburrow, and sometimes stored their wealth in the underdark benneath the city.

In Deepburrow, most considered the Slickers to be a fictional organization. Only the Captain of Guards Nemuel Fadinglight and Chief of Scouts Chalice Appleton were sure that the group existed. They secretly allowed the Slickers to operate, on the condition that the guild would not target the people of Deepburrow and would protect the city if ever called to do so.

Members

The Guildmaster of the Slickers was the lightfoot halfling Beldon Oftenhand. Other members were known to be bards, experts, and rogues.
