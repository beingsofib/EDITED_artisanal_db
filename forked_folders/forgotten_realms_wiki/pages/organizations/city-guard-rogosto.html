The City Guard of Rog'osto served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City of Spires".

Organization

The composition of the city guard as of was 1,000 infantry, 400 palace guards, and 200 jann. Additionally, each of the city's towers housed a personal guard of around 200 soldiers each.

Activities

The martial forces of Rog'osto were extremely weak compared to every other city in Zakhara. However, the presence of so many powerful wizards made potential enemies think twice before attacking the city.
