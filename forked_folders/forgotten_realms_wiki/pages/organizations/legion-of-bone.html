The Legion of Bone was a militaristic group of undead commanded by Szass Tam during the mid–14 century DR. It comprised "soldiers" of skeletal undead cavalry.

The order fought in battles alongside Cyric's Legion, another Thayan undead contingent, named after the ascended mortal Cyric.
