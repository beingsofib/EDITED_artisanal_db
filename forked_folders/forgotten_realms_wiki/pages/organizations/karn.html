Karn

Organization
nameKarn
baseArgent Hall, Selgaunt, Sembia

The Karn family was a noble merchant family of Sembia. Their family home, Argent Hall, was located in the heart of the capital, Selgaunt.

Family members

  • Lord Javis Karn
  • Lord Fendo Karn, Javis's son
  • Shamur Uskevren (born Shamur Karn), Javis's daughter
  • Lord Lindrian Karn, Fendo's son
  • Shamur Karn, Lindrian's daughter, with the same name and identical appearance to the previous Shamur
  • Lord Fendolac Karn, Lindrian's son
  • Diurgo Karn
