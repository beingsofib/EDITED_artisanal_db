The Wheloon Watch was a militia and law-enforcement organization that operated in Wheloon in Cormyr, in the 14th century DR.

Organization

The Wheloon Watch was a militia force of volunteers, all merchants and tradesmen by day, with basic fighting ability. They were led by a captain.

Activities

As well as being a militia force,

Base of operations

They were based out of the Wheloon Watch House.

Possessions

Members of the Watch were equipped with leather armor, maces, short swords, daggers, and crossbows.

Relationships

The Wheloon Watch worked with the local Purple Dragons contingent, and shared the Wheloon Watch House with them.

History

During the Time of Troubles in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR, in Wheloon, Kelemvor was framed for the robbery and murder of a local merchant, with Cyric's agent testifying to Lord Sarp Redbeard that they had seen him leave the house with a bloody sword. When the Wheloon Watch tried to apprehend Kelemvor, the group fled on stolen horses, pursued by the Watch and the Purple Dragons, and made a successful getaway.
