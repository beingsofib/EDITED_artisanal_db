Dulkaster and Sons was a wealthy and successful family trading organizations operating on the Sword Coast in the late 14

century DR.

Activities

By the 1370s DR, Dulkaster and Sons has been operating a profitable business managing trade across the Sword Coast for centuries.

Base of Operations

Dulkaster and Sons' main offices were located in Waterdeep and its family members often traveled for business purposes, sometimes staying away from the main office for months at a time.

History

In 1372 DR, Egbert Dulkaster the Third was visiting Daggerford. While he was conducting business there he stayed at the Chateau Elite.

Members

  • Egbert Dulkaster the Third, the pompous rich man who appreciated wealth, and fine fashionable silk clothes circa 1372 DR.
