Windrunners was a Athasian thri-kreen tribe of insectoid nomads who lived in the desert of the Ringing Mountains's foothills on the world of Athas in the late 14 century DR.

History

In The Year of Despair, Malatran calendar, late 14 century DR, a group of heroes of the Malatran Plateau were sent through an elemental gate to the far-away world of Athas. Created temporarily by Roshink, the elemental noble explained that he and his fellow nobles fled from this world long ago and it is what could befall the Malatran Plateau should the heroes fail to vanquish the dreaded Tamara. In order to return home, he instructed them to head for the mountains until they came across a great forest, where they would find an "oak tree that is too big for a saru to put his arms around". When they found such an oak, he would be able to bring them back to the Plateau through the Dreamlands.

Immediately upon arrival, the group was captured by Athasian elf slavers. A young Athasian thri-kreen named Ka'cha was among creatures captured by the elves, and he lamented being separated from his clutch. Subsequently, the heroes escaped when the elven caravan was attacked by a hungry sink worm. They seized the opportunity and freed Ka'cha, fleeing from the pursuers across the desert.

After two days of travel across the hot sands, guided by Ka'cha, the group stumbled across the Windrunners tribe and their leader, Ki'rik'ku. Ka'cha convinced his tribe the strange adventurers were friends, not food. Happy with one of their number returned, chief Ki'rik'ku gifted the aliens four large erdlu eggs (an invaluable source of nourishment in the Athasian desert) and two enchanted apples of healing. The thri-kreen explained to the Malatran heroes how to reach their destination—the Ringing Forest—the way back to Toril. After parting ways, Windrunners embarked on a quest of revenge against the slaver elves who'd wronged young Ka'cha.
