The Marines of the Seven Seas, who were also simply called the Marines, was a sect of followers of Valkur, the Captain of the Waves.

Description

The Marines of the Seven Seas formed a small navy of various war ships stationed at different ports within the Sea of Fallen Stars and along the Sea of Swords. The Marines hunted down monsters of the deep in Valkur's name. Many kills were credited to them including dragon turtles, krakens, giant octopi, morkoths, quelzarns, vurgens, and more.
