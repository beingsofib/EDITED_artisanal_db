The Tribe of the Tiger was a nomadic group of Reghedman barbarians living in the tundra of Icewind Dale.

Like their fellow Tribes of the Bear and Elk, the Tribe of the Tiger lived in relative peace with the folks of the Ten Towns.

History

By the time of the War of the Silver Marches in the late 15 century DR, the Aurilian Bjornhild Solvigsdottir had become queen of the Tiger tribe. She assumed the title after her husband, King Korold, was slain by a frost giant.

Notable Members

  • Bjornhild Solvigsdottir, a ruthless worshiper of Auril
  • Korold, slain king of the Tribe of the Tiger
  • Fritha Craegmar, a chieftain of the Tribe of the Tiger
