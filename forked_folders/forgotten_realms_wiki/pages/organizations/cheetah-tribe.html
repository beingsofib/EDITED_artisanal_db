Cheetah Tribe

The Cheetah Tribe were a group of hunters in the Shaar in the mid–14th century DR. They roamed in the region between the Uthangol Mountains and the Great Rift, southeast of Hardcastle, and as far east as the Council Hills.

The raiders of this tribe trained to run like cheetahs and charge into the fray.
