The Goblin lodge, who were also called the Goblin Berserkers or simply the Goblins, was a lodge of Rashemaar berserkers based in Rashemen's capital of Immilmar and the town of Taporan as of the mid–14 century DR.

Base of Operations

The Owlbears dwelt within the Goblin Berserker Lodge located on the north side of Immilmar.

Activities

Like many berserkers, the Goblins enjoyed competing against each other and strangers in contest of wrestling and drinking.

Organization

The leader of the Goblin lodge as of the , was Yuthrim.
