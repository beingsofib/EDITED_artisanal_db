The Uluevryn family was one of the wealthiest families in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Base of Operations

The Uluevryn estate lay along the affluent Emerald Dragon Way in the heart of the Golden City.

Notable Members

Dustras Uluevryn was a self-made merchant whose wealth surpassed those of all of his rivals within Suldolphor, placing his family above all others in terms of financial influence.
