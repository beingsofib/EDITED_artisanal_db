The Company of the Crushed Grapes was a mercenary company stationed in Nimpeth, the Free Cities of the Vilhon Reach, circa 1370 DR.

Organization

The Company of the Crushed Grapes was under the leadership of Duke Folkwain, who was a member of the group and was put in charge of all mercenaries hired by the city of Nimpeth. The Crushed Grapes were a special force that served Nimpeth's leader, Lord Woren, through his second in command, Duke Folkwain. In the late 14 century DR, the company consisted of more than 250 foot soldiers and 150 horseback riders.
