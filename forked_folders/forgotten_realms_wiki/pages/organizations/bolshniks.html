The Bolshnik family was part of the nobility of Nova Vaasa in the Domains of Dread at a time roughly corresponding with the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Organization

Their political leader was Prince Othmar Bolshnik.

Base of Operations

The castle of the Bolshniks was near Kantora.

Relationships

The Bolshniks received tribute from all the other noble families in the realm, including the Hiregaards, Chekivs, Rivtoffs, and Vistins.

History

Prince Othmar had come to power roughly twenty-two years prior to a time corresponding with the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
