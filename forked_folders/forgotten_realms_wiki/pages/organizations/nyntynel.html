Nyntynel

Nyntynel was one of the noble houses of Anauria, one of the Lost Kingdoms of north Faerûn. It was known for the skilled wizards that plied their craft for the good of Anauria's people.

Activities

In Anauril, wizards of House Nyntynel were known to utilize their spellcraft to enchant weapons and blades that were produced by the city's great weaponsmiths.

Members

It was one of two elven houses that made up Anauria's nobility, along with House Olyrnn.

History

Circa the Era of Upheaval, some traces of House Nyntynel could be found throughout the ruins of Anauria that were left within the sands of Anauroch.
