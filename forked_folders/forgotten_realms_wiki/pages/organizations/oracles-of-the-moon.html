Oracles of the Moon

Organization
nameOracles of the Moon
favored deitySelûne
allegiancesChurch of Selûne

The Oracles of the Moon were a group of female diviners. They worshiped Selûne, the Night White Lady, and were affiliated with the church of Selûne.
