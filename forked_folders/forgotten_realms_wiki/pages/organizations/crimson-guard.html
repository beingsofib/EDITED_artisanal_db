The Crimson Guard was a mercenary company, active in the Western Heartlands, Northwest Faerûn, and the Sword Coast as of the late 14 century DR.

History

One of the most well-known Crimson Guards was Astar Graysteel, an ex-Blade of Mulmaster. Astar spent several years with the company after leaving home in the Moonsea sometime in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. His stint with the organization ended long before the .
