The Olafsson family were the hereditary rulers of the Northlander kingdom of Gnarhelm beginning in the early-to-mid–14 century DR.

History

The family originally ruled the subkingdom of Olafstaad, where they reigned from a grand lodge filled with art and finery claimed during raids. They came to rule all of Gnarhelm when they united the kingdoms of Gnarhelm, Olafstaad, and Sunderstaad in the .

Notable Members

  • Rolf Olafsson, the King of Olafstaad who was named King of all Gnarhelm. He was a witness to the signing of the Treaty of Oman.
  • Svenyird Olafsson, Rolf's heir who was tutored by Elminster's apprentices.
  • Brandon Olafsson, Svenyird's heir who journeyed with Alicia Kendrick and ultimately supported her bid to unify the Northlanders and the Ffolk under the United Moonshae Isles.
