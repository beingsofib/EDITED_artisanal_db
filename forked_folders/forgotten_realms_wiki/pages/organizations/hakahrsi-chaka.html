The Hakahrsi chaka was a prominent chaka within the Shining Lands of southeast Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR and a member of the Chakas of 31.

Base of Operations

The Hakahrsi chaka was based in the coastal city of Sezilinta in central Durpar.

Activity

They were rare among the chakas of the region in that they were an organization of halflings. The Hakahrsi chaka generated wealth from grain and other foodstuffs. They were one of the newest members of the Chakas of 31 during this time.
