The Zalaths were a Calishite family based in Almraiven in Calimshan in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

They ran a shipbuilding business, but were partners of the Rundeen.

Circa 1370 DR, Achem el Zalath gained fame after proposing, with diagrams, a new kind of vessel he dubbed a 'waterwalking ship', which on Earth would be called a in Elminster's understanding.
