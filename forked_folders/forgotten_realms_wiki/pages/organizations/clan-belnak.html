Clan Belnak was one of the dragonborn clans of the nation of Tymanther.

History

Clan Belnak was a traditional ally of Clan Jalt. As such, Clan Belnak had representatives in the frontier village of Ruinspoke.

Known members

  • Belnak Vikom, the representative of Clan Belnak in Ruinspoke, as of 1480 DR.
