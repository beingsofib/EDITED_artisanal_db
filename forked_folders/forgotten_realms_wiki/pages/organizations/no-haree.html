No-Haree

nameNo-Haree

The No-Haree were the personal guard of the High Lord of Oceans of Tabot.

Description

They were zealous warriors who would die to protect the High One. Their dress was entirely white, with white robes, furs, and enamelled shields.

History

Circa , they served High Lord Ning D'Ahn, protecting him from such threats as ninja assassins.
