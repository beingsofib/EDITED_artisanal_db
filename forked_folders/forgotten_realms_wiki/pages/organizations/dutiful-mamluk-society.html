The Dutiful Mamluk Society, also known as the Dutiful, was a military organization in Zakhara.

Activities

The Dutiful represented the largest and most powerful mamluk organization in the entire Land of Fate. Despite this, they were bitter rivals with the Respected, who dominated the Free Cities of Zakhara north of Qudra.

Organization

The Dutiful were led by the dwarven Emir Hatit Abd al-Wajib.

Base of Operations

This large organizations had a presence in most cities in Zakhara, but most of their power was centered in Qudra, Huzuz, and Utaqa.
