Vale Guard

Organization
nameVale Guard
baseEvereska

The Vale Guard were Evereska's soldiers that patrolled entryways to the city.

Description

Contingents of Vale Guards were led by single kanqats, and were typically made of around 100 archers.

History

Most of the Vale Guard perished during the Evereska-Phaerimm war.
