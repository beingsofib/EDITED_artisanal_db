The Hallixes were a family based in Neverwinter.

Activities

Like other wealthy Neverwintan families, the Hallixes maintained a family mausoleum in the Neverdeath Main Graveyard. This mausoleum contained a secret connection to the crypts of the Waterclock Guild.

History

The family had been living and burying its dead in Neverwinter for a long time prior to the eruption of Mount Hotenow in the mid–15 century DR. Six members of the family died following that eruption.

Within forty years, the family mausoleum would become home to a cult dedicated to Vecna, and the remains of the Hallix family interred there were consumed by the cult's ghouls.
