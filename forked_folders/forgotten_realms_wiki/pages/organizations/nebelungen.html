Nebelungen

Organization
nameNebelungen
baseIsle Broddick, the Whamite Isles

The Nebelungen were mysterious individuals from Isle Broddick in the Whamite Isles. They represented Broddick in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Centuries prior, the Nebelungen created the Nebelshield, a magic shield which was most powerful when wielded by them.
