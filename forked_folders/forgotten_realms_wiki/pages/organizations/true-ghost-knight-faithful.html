True Ghost Knight Faithful

nameTrue Ghost Knight Faithful

The True Ghost Knight Faithful was a cult that venerated the Ghost Knight of Galardoun as an incarnation of the then-dead deity Helm. The group met in the upstairs rooms of the Ten Trolls Fallen tavern in Waterdeep's Trade Ward. The cult often quested to find the apparition that wandered the Heartlands.
