The Order of the Hammer were a Cormyrean adventuring company who venerated Torm, that operated in the mid-14 century DR. On 5 Alturiak of the , they perished on a mission on behalf of Cormyr. Their service was commemorated by a memorial plaque in the office of Registrant General Lord Partic Thistle in the Royal Court of Suzail.
