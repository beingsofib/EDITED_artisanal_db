The Friends of Blacklake Association were an ecologist group operating in the city of Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 century DR.

Activities

As of 1479 DR, the main goal of the Friends of Blacklake was to clean and restore the Blacklake to its former glory.

Known members

  • Eralt Gavin, the leader of the Friends in 1479 DR.
