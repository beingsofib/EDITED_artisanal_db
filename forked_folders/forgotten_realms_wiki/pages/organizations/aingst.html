The Aingst were a tribe of Nars found in western Narfell.

Activities

Generally speaking, the Aingst did not mind dealing with others, particularly farmers from Damara, so long as they did not tread within the borders of Narfell.

Base of Operations

The Aingst made their home in and around the Giantspire Mountains between the border of Narfell and Damara.
