Clan Nornglast was an extinct noble house of Eskorn.

History

Clan Nornglast was one of the most prominent clans of Eskorn, and its members were the founders of the "Royal Keep" of the same name. They had an incredibly fabulous wealth but expended most of it in a secret project nobody knew about it. Eskornar commoners called it the "Nornglast Secret", as a way to say that the Nornglast were different to other nobles but nobody knew how.

Clan Nornglast disappeared many years before the Spellplague.
