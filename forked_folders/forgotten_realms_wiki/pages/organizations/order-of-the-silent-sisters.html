The Order of the Silent Sisters were a Cormyrean adventuring company that operated in the mid-14 century DR. In Ches of the , they vanished without a trace in the Vast Swamp. Their service was commemorated by a memorial plaque in the office of Registrant General Lord Partic Thistle in the Royal Court of Suzail.
