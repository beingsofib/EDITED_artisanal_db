The Youthful Mamluk Society, who were also known as the Youthful, was a military organization composed of mamluks based in the Free City of Hafayah in north Zakhara.

Organization

Twelve hundred mamluks, broken down into two 600–man units, formed the Youthful Mamluk Society.
