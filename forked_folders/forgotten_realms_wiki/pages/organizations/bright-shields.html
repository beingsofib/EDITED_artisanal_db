The Bright Shields were an adventuring band of northwest Faerûn in the early 3 century DR.

Activities

They were known to be lawless.

Members

They had at least two wizards.

History

In summer of the , the Brave Blades clashed with the Bright Shields in Baerlith. The confrontation ended with the Bright Shields slain, and several of the Brave Blades dead.

Possessions

After their defeat, they were looted of coins, armor, horses, and a number of enchanted devices, including boots, belts, rings, and rods.
