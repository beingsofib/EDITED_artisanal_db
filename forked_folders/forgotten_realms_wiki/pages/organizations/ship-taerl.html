Ship Taerl was one of the five factions of Luskan ruled by a High Captain. It was named for and originally led by High Captain Taerl.

Relationships

Ship Taerl was allied with Ship Baram in 1376 DR.

Members

  • Throa Taerl, leader by 1489 DR
