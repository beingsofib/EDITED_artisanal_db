The City Guard of I'tiraf served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City of Confessions".

Organization

The composition of the city guard as of was 2,000 regular city guards, 300 clerics, and six 300-man units of mamluks from the Exalted and Devoted Mamluk Societies. An additional 8,000 soldiers and 3,000 cavalry from the Sword of the True Gods were also stationed in I'tiraf. I'tiraf's navy consisted of 16 ships.

Activities

Additional bahglahs, or large dhows, were under construction as well.
