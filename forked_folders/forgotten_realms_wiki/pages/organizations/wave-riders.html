The Wave Riders was one of the military organizations that served as the police force and core of the military forces of the kingdom of Akanûl in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

The Wave Riders were in charge of the naval defense of the city of Airspur and the rest of the kingdom. They acted as the guard of the harbor of Airspur during peacetime.

Membership

The Wave Riders were made up mostly of watersoul genasi.

The Wave Riders were under the leadership of the Steward of Fire. Circa 1479 DR, the Steward of Fire was Magnol.
