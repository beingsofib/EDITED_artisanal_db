The Company of the Fiery Fane was an adventuring company in the early 14 century DR.

The group was founded by Szuszalan, the Warrior Maid, who was formerly of the famous all-female adventuring company known as the Circle of Steel. She wrote of their exploits in her biography, Walking on a Swordblade, published in the .
