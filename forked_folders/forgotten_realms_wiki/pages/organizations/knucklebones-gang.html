The Knucklebones Gang was a group located in Avernus, first layer of the Nine Hells, in the late 15 century DR.

Membership

The Knucklebones Gang consisted of a night hag named Mad Maggie, two kenku mechanics named Chukka and Clonk, a fiendish flesh golem named Mickey, two conniving imps named Pins and Needles, a flameskull named Barnabas, and a host of murderous fey creatures.

Activities

Mad Maggie operated the gang circa 1492 DR. Many members of the Knucklebones Gang were located in Fort Knucklebone.
