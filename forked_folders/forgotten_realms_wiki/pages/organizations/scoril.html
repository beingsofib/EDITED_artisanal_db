The Scoril family was a noble house of Cormyr.

Bases of Operations

The Scoril family had holdings in the city of Marsember, and were the only noble family to be prominent there.

Activities

The members of the house were loyal to the Crown and often served as factors, wardens, and officers in the Blue Dragons.

History

They were originally nobles of the independent city of Marsember, before Cormyr gained the city, and encouraged House Marliir to join in the fight. In the , the Scorils switched sides to help the Cormyrean cause.

Members

  • Bledryn Scoril, head of the family and the king's herald for Marsember in the late 1300s DR.
