The Haldines were a family from Stag Falls in the Vast in the 14 century DR. Their lineage had been in Stag Falls since its last resettlement.

Activities

They were farmers.

Possessions

The Haldine family's holdings consisted of the Haldine farm.

History

The Haldine family had been with Karl von Stag Falls during his initial resettlement of Stag Falls. They moved into an older, stone home in Stag Falls that had become their generational residence, even adopting the device of a lion's head atop the front door as their symbol. When, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR, they learned of the transformation of Baron Vilhelm von Stag Falls into an undead, and his kidnappings of the townspeople, they moved to the nearby caves alongside the rest of the townspeople.

Notably, if the vampire controlling Baron Vilhelm was dealt with, the Haldines had an acceptable claim to the throne of Stag Falls.
