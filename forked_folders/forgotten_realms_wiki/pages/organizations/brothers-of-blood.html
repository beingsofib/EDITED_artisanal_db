The Brothers of Blood were a group of crusaders devoted to Garagos dedicated to crushing his enemies.

Activities

Much of the Brothers of Blood's time was spent attacking the faithful of Tempus, Garagos' sworn enemy. As such, the crusaders in the organization tended to live short bloody lives.
