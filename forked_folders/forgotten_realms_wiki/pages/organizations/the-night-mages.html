The Night Mages, also known as Felodar's Sendings, were the sorcerers sent by Felodar Aumar to seize his native realm of Athalantar in the early-to-mid 3 century DR.

Tactics

The Night Mages worked most often at night.

Relationships

The Night Mages worked for Felodar Aumar, and were opposed by the Magelords of Athalantar.

History

Once settled in Calimshan and established as a successful merchant, Felodar Aumar sent wizards to Athalantar to try and seize control of the realm on his behalf. These wizards were a threat to his brother, King Belaur Aumar for most of his reign, which lasted between and . These mages hid by day, combating the Magelords mostly during the hours of darkness.
