Paertrover

Organization
namePaertrover
typeNoble family
baseTaverton Hall, Cormyr

Paertrover was a noble house of Cormyr in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Base

They occupied Taverton Hall, on or near the River Starwater. It was small but the oldest of the great estates in Northbank.

Members

  • Lord Crimmon Paertrover, son and heir of Lord Eskult
  • Lord Eskult Paertrover, Baron of Starwater, Horse Marshal to the Crown of Cormyr
  • Lady Maeraedithe Paertrover, Eskult's wife

Servants

  • Immult Greiryn, seneschal of Taverton Hall
