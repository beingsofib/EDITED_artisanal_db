The Thousandheads Trading Coster was a merchant company in Faerûn.

History

Bharavan Bhaerkantos, a former adventurer, founded this merchant company. The Thousandheads Trading Coster only operated on trade routes from Waterdeep to Hillsfar via Scornubel, Berdusk, Iriaebor, Priapurl, Arabel, and Essembra.

Base of Operations

Bharavan's personal stronghold, located just east of Riatavin, served as the base of operations for this company.
