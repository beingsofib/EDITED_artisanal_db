The Sailors of the Mountainous Waves were a group of sea-faring dwarves active around on the island of Ruathym, which was then known as Arauwurbarak.

History

Originally from Haunghdannar, the dwarves constructed the fortress of Sonnmorndin on Ruathym to use as a base of operations for their sailing excursions.
