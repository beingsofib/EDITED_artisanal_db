The Soravian Talebringers was an organization in Damara in 1359 DR.

Organization

The Talebringers were a group of rangers and clerics of Ilmater focusing on helping the people of Soravia and keeping the various local communities in touch. The people of Soravia trusted them.

History

The Talebringers acted for some time but emerged politically after 1357 DR when the duchy of Soravia was at risk of falling into chaos with its ruler, a puppet of Zhengyi, killed in battle with Gareth Dragonsbane. Many of them joined Gareth in his quest to free Damara. In 1359 DR, they cleared the land of any minions of Zhengyi who remained and answered to all the citizens' requests.
