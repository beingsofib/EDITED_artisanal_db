nameTillerturn

The Tillerturns were a patriar family of Baldur's Gate around 1482 DR. They were relatives of one of the first four Grand Dukes of Baldur's Gate.

Activities

They were engaged mainly in property and rented out many of the buildings in the city.

Notable Members

  • Ernest Tillerturn, a musician noblewoman from the late 15 century DR.
  • Milon Tillerturn, a nobleman who attended Lord Enver Gortash's coronation in 1492 DR.
