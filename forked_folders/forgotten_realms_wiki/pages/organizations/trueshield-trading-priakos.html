The Trueshield Trading Priakos was a merchant company in Faerûn.

History

This merchant group built its own wagons and even trained and equipped their guards. No expense was spared, resulting in fine caravan guards that few bandits dared tangle with.

Base of Operations

The Trueshield Trading Priakos was based out of Telpir on the Dragon Coast of the Sea of Fallen Stars.
