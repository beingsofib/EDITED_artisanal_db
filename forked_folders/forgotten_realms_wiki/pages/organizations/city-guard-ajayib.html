The City Guard of the Ajayib served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City of Wonders".

Organization

The composition of the city guard as of was 8,000 regular soldiers, 2,000 cavalry, an elite unit of 300 farisan, and a navy consisting of 6 ships.

Activities

Ajayib's navy was focused primarily in thwarting western raiders along the coast of the Crowded Sea. The unit of farisan was commanded by Calipha Halima al-Wahsi.
