The Ring of the Unquenchable was an efreeti military force located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire.

Activities

Though they were a slave legion, the efreeti mamluks of the Ring of the Unquenchable were favored by the Sultan.

Base of Operations

The City of Brass served as the base of the Ring of the Unquenchable. Their barracks were located near the Charcoal Palace and the major mosques
