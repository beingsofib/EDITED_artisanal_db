The Weathered Dawn was an adventuring company of Cormyr that operated in the late 15 century DR.

History

Shortly after the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, the Weathered Dawn encamped at the ruins of Beckside Castle. With the paladins Tanya Brock and Vicas Oakleaf taking the lead, the group effectively put themselves in charge of the site and set about managing the accessible parts of the ruins in order to make it a safer campsite for travelers. While they were vigilant and watchful, they did not suspect that many of their guests were actually affiliated with Risen Netheril, who then sneaked away into the deeper ruins to locate the Nether Scroll that Oriphaun Huntsilver had hidden there.
