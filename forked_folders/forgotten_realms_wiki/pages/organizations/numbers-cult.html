Numbers Cult

Organization
nameNumbers Cult
typeSecret society
baseChessenta

The Numbers Cult was a secret society of mages and mathematicians in Chessenta in the mid–14 century DR. They worked to define magic in terms of mathematics. They believed the resulting equations would conquer the world! However, their basic premise was apparently based on a fallacy.

The philosopher of Cimbar, Heptios the Archmage, had been rumored to be involved in the Numbers Cult, but he disavowed any connection with them by 1357 DR.
