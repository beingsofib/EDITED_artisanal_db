Caladanter was a noble family of Cormyr in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

During the reign of King Azoun Obarskyr IV, Lord Rhallogant Caladanter worked treason against Cormyr's royal family and the War Wizards. He ordered his bodyguard, a man named Boarblade, to kill Lord Eldarton Feathergate of Cormyr.
