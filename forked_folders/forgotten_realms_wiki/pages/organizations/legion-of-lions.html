The Legion of Lions was an order of wemic and werelion paladins of Nobanion, who served as champions of the benevolent monsters of Faerûn.

Relationships

The Legion were sworn enemies of the followers of the Beastlord, Malar.

History

The order was formed just after the Time of Troubles by a Mindulgulph mercenary, Karrlon.
