The Yhliivast family was a human mercantile family of Amnwater in Amn.

Activities

The Yhliivasts concerned themselves mainly with caravan-related trades and businesses in Amnwater, including horse breeding, horse training, wheelwrights, and the Gilded Spur, a luxury inn that gathered coins and information from caravaneers. Together with the Ironseth and Wheelarit families, they had gained near complete control over Amnwater by about 1320 DR, with the Yhliivasts the wealthiest and most powerful of the trio.

Members

  • Lord Jhanos Yhliivast, the fearsome family patriarch in 1370 DR.
  • Krigar, the lover of Lord Jhanos.
