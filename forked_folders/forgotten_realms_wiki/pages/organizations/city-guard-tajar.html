The City Guard of Tajar served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City of Trade".

Organization

The composition of the city guard as of was 4,000 infantry, 3,000 cavalry, and 300 jann of the High Desert.

Activities

Tajar's cavalry was based around an elite group of 800 desert riders from the High Desert and House Bakr.
