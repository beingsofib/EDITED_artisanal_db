The Snow Tiger lodge was a lodge of Rashemaar berserkers active within Rashemen as of the mid–14 century DR.

Base of Operations

Snow Tiger longhouses were protected by telthor snow tigers.

Activities

Berserkers from this lodge donned the skins and claws of their spirit animal. The claws were used as weapons during combat and could even be enhanced further.
