The Cryptskulls were the general clergy of Velsharoon within the Crypt of the Arisen Army.

Activities

In addition to their duties within the temple, the Cryptskulls haunted the entire length of Skull Gorge and the Battle of Bones. They were aided by an undead army that guarded their territory from intruders.

Members

Baron Vjurn Blacktower was their high priest, or necrophant, and provided their orders.
