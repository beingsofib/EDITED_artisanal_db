Brotherhood of the Pick

Organization
nameBrotherhood of the Pick
baseMulhorand, Old Empires
favored deityGeb

The Brotherhood of the Pick was a society affiliated with the church of Geb in Mulhorand around 1369 DR.

The majority of miners in the country were members. Its representatives presented their needs and wants to the nobility and handled negotiations, so it functioned much like a union.
