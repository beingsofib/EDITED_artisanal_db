The Singers and Thespians Guild of Opus was a prestigious group of theatrical performers from Opus in ancient Netheril.

Activities

The Guild was the most famous band of performers in Netheril, and originated from Opus, the enclave famed for its culture and education. The next most famed group was the League of Noted Singers and Performers of Karsus Enclave, who performed more tours and were ultimately seen by more people.
