Havnaether's Fist

Organization
nameHavnaether's Fist
typeAdventuring company

Havnaether's Fist was an adventuring party of the late 14 century DR.

Activities

They were employed as agents by Othdil Mauneiyn and Roekandal in their search for the Crown of the Undying Flame.
