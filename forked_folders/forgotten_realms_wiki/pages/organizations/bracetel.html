Bracetel was one of the Palace families who served the Crown of Cormyr in the 15 century DR.

History

By the late 15 century DR, Bracetels were career officiers for multiple generations. Like other officers and chamberlains, the Bracetel family resided at Cormyr's Royal Court south of Castle Obarskyr.
