The Brotherhood of Skuld was a group of adventurers based out of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Base of Operations

In addition to Skuld, the Brotherhood had members in Gheldaneth.

Activities

Members of the Brotherhood of Skuld were determined to undermine the nefarious influence of the god Set and his followers in Mulhorand.

Members

A paladin of Osiris named Halcaunt led the Brotherhood in the . Another member of note was a wizard named Urius.
