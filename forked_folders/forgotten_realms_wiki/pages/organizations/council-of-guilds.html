The Council of Guilds ran the daily affairs of Daggerford in the 14 century DR.

Activities

Imitating the masked Lords of Waterdeep, the council members wore masks and shrouded themselves in robes to hide their identities at council meetings, but everyone in Daggerford knew who the council members were.

The council consisted of the guildmasters of the town's trade guilds, the Heralds' and Runners' Union, the Pleaders' Consortium (lawyers and attorneys), blacksmith's guild, Merchant's Guild, Jeweler's Congress, Whitesmiths' Guild, Tailors' Federation, Tanners' Council, Physicians' Order, Taverners' Confederation, Scribes' Friendship, Sorceller's Encapsulate, Farmers' Society, Animal Handlers' Affiliation, and Carpenters' League, and likely not the self-styled guilds of the Thieves' Brotherhood

Notable Members

  • Derval Ironeater, guildmaster of the blacksmith's guild
  • Maerovyna, chief priestess of the Harvest House
  • Liam Sunmist, Lightlord of the Morninglow Tower
  • Bando the Lame, priest of Tymora
  • Sherlen Spearslayer, the head of the Daggerford militia
