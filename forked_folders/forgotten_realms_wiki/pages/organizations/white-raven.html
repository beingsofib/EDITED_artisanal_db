The White Raven was an elven patrol in Cormanthor until .

History

In the , the White Raven patrol, led by Iymbryl Alastrarra, was completely eliminated by ruukha (hobgoblins). Iymbryl, the last survivor, was rescued by Elminster Aumar but died of his wounds, bequeathing upon him the mission to return his kiira to his family in the city.
