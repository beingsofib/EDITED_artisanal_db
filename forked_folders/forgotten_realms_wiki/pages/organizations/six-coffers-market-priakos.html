The Six Coffers Market Priakos was a merchant company in Faerûn.

History

Originally founded by six merchants, only four of the remaining founders remained alive in 1367 DR. Thelve Baruinheld served as the leader of the organization, operating out of Berdusk.

Base of Operations

This merchant company had bases of operation in Berdusk, Waterdeep, Silverymoon, Priapurl, and Selgaunt.
