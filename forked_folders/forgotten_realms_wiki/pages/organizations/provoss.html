The Provoss family was a patriar family from Baldur's Gate that made its fortune through cattle.

Description

The Provosses lived in a home located in the Temples District of the Gate's Upper City, north of the High Hall.

History

By the , the Provoss family was nearing ruination after heavy losses to their cattle herds.

Notable Members

  • Beatrice Provoss, a lady member of the Parliament of Peers of Baldur's Gate in 1492 DR.
