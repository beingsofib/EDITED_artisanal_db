Clan Wardragon was a family of humans who were dispersed across northwest Faerûn. Many of the Wardragons of Loudwater originally settled Waterdeep.

Notable members

  • Laroun, early warlord of Waterdeep.
  • Meloon Wardragon, descendant of Laroun.
