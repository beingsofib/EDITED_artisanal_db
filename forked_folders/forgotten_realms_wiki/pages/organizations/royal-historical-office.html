Royal Historical Office

Organization
nameRoyal Historical Office
baseSuzail, Cormyr

The Royal Historical Office of Cormyr was an extension of the Crown that dealt with the purchase and preservation of cultural and historical artifacts and King Azoun Obarskyr IV's personal collections in the mid–14 century DR.

Organization

The Royal Historical Office was under the direct control of the Crown of Cormyr and employed sergeants in charge of procuring.

Activities

The Royal Historical Office often worked with respectable adventurers and expeditions that operated out of Suzail to procure items of historical importance. The Society of Stalwart Adventurers was one of the organizations that the Royal Historical Office did business with.

History

In 1362 DR, the Royal Historical Office purchased the majority of items and artifacts recovered by Artus Cimber and Hydel Pontifax from their expedition to the Stonelands where they encountered a strange silver statue of Mulhorandi origin, along with ancient coins and spearheads.
