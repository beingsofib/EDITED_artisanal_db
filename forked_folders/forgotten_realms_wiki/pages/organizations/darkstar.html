Darkstar

nameDarkstar
baseVast Swamp, Cormyr
racesOrc

The Darkstar tribe was a tribe of orcs dwelling in the Vast Swamp of Cormyr.

Base of Operations

The Darkstar tribe had territory in the center of the Vast Swamp.

Relationships

Around 1367 DR, Darkstar territory was bordered in the north by the Broken Dagger goblins, by the Norgath hobgoblins in the east, and the Dragonslayer lizardfolk in the south.

History

The Darkstar tribe was known to live in the Vast Swamp around 1367 DR.
