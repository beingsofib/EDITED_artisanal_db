Kant Family

The Kant Family was a chaka of Vaelan, in Durpar. In , at least one of its members had become a plangent; while he was en route to meet a secret lover, Shaddon Datharathi possessed him and made him give a loud hoot for no reason. The man had no memory of this event after the fact.
