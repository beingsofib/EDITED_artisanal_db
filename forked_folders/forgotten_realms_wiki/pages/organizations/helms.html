The Helms were the city guard of Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR.

Activities

The Helms patrolled the streets of Urmlaspyr and maintained the general order of the city. They also policed the roads outside of the city walls all the way to the Cormyrean border marked by the River Lurlar. The waters of the Neck were patrolled with the Helms' 11 ships.

Organization

Approximately 4,000 individuals comprised the Helms.
