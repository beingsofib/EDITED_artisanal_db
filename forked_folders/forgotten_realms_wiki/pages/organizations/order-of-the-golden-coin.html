Order of the Golden Coin

Organization
nameOrder of the Golden Coin

The Order of the Golden Coin was an all-female martial order of priestesses dedicated to the goddess Nephthys of the Mulhorandi pantheon, active around 1370 DR.

Known for their vigilance, they worked as temple guards, caravan escorts, and bodyguards. They were quite skilled at dissuading criminals, although their fee was considerable.
