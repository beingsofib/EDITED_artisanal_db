House Goldfeather was a Cormyrian noble house whose last living noble was executed for treason by King Dhalmass in 1191 DR.

History

All of their property, including the hunting lodge called Stag Steads was forfeited to the crown.

In 1442 DR, the house was ennobled again by King Azoun V. The Goldfeathers further enhanced their ties to the crown when Prince Irvel Obarskyr married Ospra, the daughter of the House's leader.
