The Fist of Rundeen was an elite band of assassins that served the Rundeen mercantile organization in the mid-to-late 14 century DR and active in the Shining Sea region.

Organization

Sponsored and directed by the Rundeen, the Fist and Rundeen were controlled directly by the First Yrshelem, the second tier of Rundeen leadership, but technically ranked below the Mitalibbar, their collectors and watchers, like their other subsidiaries.

Membership

The Fist of Rundeen comprised only eight active members, no more and no less. All of them were dedicated assassins, killers, and warriors trained as fighters and rogues. They were entirely loyal to their paymasters and utterly merciless on completing their missions. But they were much less loyal to one another: el Scimitar slew three over 1365–1370 and el Yadhi plotted to be rid of el Dyram because she desired his position. Moreover, retirement was impossible; they were members for life, or at least as long as they survived.

Names held vital significance to them. Their real names were kept closely guarded secrets, never mentioned even amongst the Rundeen and very rarely amongst one another. Members instead adopted noms de guerre in Alzhedo based on their preferred weapons, and used these names with the Rundeen and one another. In contrast, they had a tendency to name their favored weapons.

Tactics

Each had an unique weapon of choice, one not wielded by another active member.

Activities

Forming a small but elite strike force, they were used to eliminate threats and traitors to the Rundeen when deemed absolutely necessary. They were dispatched on missions involving committing murder or causing havoc.

Relationships

The Fist of Rundeen were one of a number of mercenary and adventuring bands, along with the Clenched Fist and Many Long Arms, formed by the Rundeen with the express purpose of opposing the Harpers.

History

The Fist of Rundeen were formed some time in the 11 century DR. The following centuries would see over a thousand members join the band, but never more or less than eight at a time.

Circa 1338 DR, the man known as el Yadhi joined the Fist of Rundeen. For 32 years, he would outlive all others and rise to leadership of the band.

They had several clashes with the Harpers, but were defeated several times by them, who saw them as no longer effective by 1367 DR.

Members

Known members of the Fist of Rundeen circa 1370 DR were, in order of seniority:

  • el Yadhi: Called the Hand, a human fighter with a penchant for snapping necks and crushing skulls, he was the leader and longest-lived of the band, with skills in tactics and planning.
  • el Scimitar: Called the Scimitar, a human fighter and brother of el Jambiya; he'd killed three fellow members to avenge perceived insults to her.
  • el Jambiya: Called the Dagger, a human thief who considered herself an artist with the blade.
  • el Katar: Called the Knife, a human thief and giggling madman who delighted in terrorizing victims.
  • el Kholnar: Called the Axe, a half-orc fighter and former executioner for a pasha.
  • el Dyram: Called the Arm, a human fighter and former gladiator of the Arena Efreetum who earned her name there before joining the Fist.

Former known members included el Jikholnar (the Hand Axe), el Enaza (the Spear), el Nadim (the Club), el Cestys (the Cestus/Spiked Knuckles) and el Nataf (the Cord/Garrote).
