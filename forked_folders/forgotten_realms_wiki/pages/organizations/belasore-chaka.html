The Belasore chaka was a prominent chaka within the Shining Lands of southeast Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR and a member of the Chakas of 31.

Base of Operations

The Belasore chaka was based out of the coastal city of Klionna in Estagund on the Great Sea.

Activity

Their wealth was generated through fishing and the sale of pearls.
