The Three Scourges were a group of pirates operating in the Sea of Swords or the Shining Sea of west Faerûn in the late 14 century DR. They were a subsidiary of the Rundeen.

Activities

Circa 1370 DR, the Three Scourges pirates would steal cargoes from captured ships, pose as the actual crew of the captured vessels, and brazenly deliver the goods themselves to take the profits for the Rundeen.
