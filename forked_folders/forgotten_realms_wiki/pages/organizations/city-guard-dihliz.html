The City Guard of Dihliz served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "Gateway City".

Organization

The composition of the city guard as of was 1,000 soldiers, 1,000 cavalry, and a navy consisting of 8 river barges.

Activities

Dihliz's forces were commanded by Ebu min Hassana. The "navy" was active only along the mighty Nogaro River.
