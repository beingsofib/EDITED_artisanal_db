The Daggers of Truth was a group of adventurers who followed the teachings of Sharess.

Base of Operations

The adventuring band was active in Mulhorand during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

The Daggers of Truth worked to thwart the plans of Set and his agents in Mulhorand. They received support from followers of Anhur and Horus-Re in their ventures.
