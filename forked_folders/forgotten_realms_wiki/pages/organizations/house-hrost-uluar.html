House Hrost Ulu'ar was one of the ruling drow Houses of Menzoberranzan in the Northdark in the late 13 and early 14 centuries DR.

History

House Hrost Ulu'ar rose to its Ruling Council status sometime before the , when it held the seat of the fifth House of Menzoberranzan. By the , Hrost Ulu'ar took the fourth House's place after the fall of House DeVir.
