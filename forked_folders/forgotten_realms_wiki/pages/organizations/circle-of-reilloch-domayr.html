Circle of Reilloch Domayr

Organization
nameCircle of Reilloch Domayr
baseTower Reilloch, Evermeet

The Circle of Reilloch Domayr was a circle of mages of Tower Reilloch on the island of Evermeet.

Members

  • Araevin Teshurr
  • Kileontheal
  • Loremaster Quastarte
  • Yesvelde Shaerim, a diviner
  • Eaglewind, a wood elf sorcerer
  • Jorildyn, a half-elf battlemage
  • Faelindel, an abjurer

The following members were killed in early 1374 DR in an attack on the Tower.

  • Philaerin
  • Aeramma Durothil
  • Earelde
  • Olleile
  • Starsong
