History

The forces of the Everhorde were amassed in the , Eventually they besieged the kingdom of Yarlith, who could not hold back the Everhorde with its small army, leaving it too in ruins.

The kingdoms of Phalorm, Uthtower, and other allied realms further south rushed their armies northward to respond to the Everhorde. Though too late to avert the destruction of Yarlith, the armies managed to arrive in time to lift the Everhorde's siege of the independent trading city of Neverwinter. Backed by the might of the gold dragon Palarandusk, the Everhorde was at last repelled.

By Mirtul 26, 611 DR, the Everhorde had made its way down to Deepwater Bay and the vampire wizard Palron Kaeth manipulated them into attacking the hidden enclave of Miyeritar.

Over the course of the next year, the , the North was engulfed in countless battle after battle, its human and demihuman kingdoms and communities fighting desperately to defeat the Everhorde. Foremost amongst the casualties was King Lathlaeril "Leafspear", ruler of Ardeep,

The armies of Uthtower, greatly shrunken from the war, immediately made their way back to their own kingdom. This left the kingdom weak to attacks by two separate orc hordes, culminating in the pillaging of Dolblunde and the death of its king, Oskilar Ironaxe.
