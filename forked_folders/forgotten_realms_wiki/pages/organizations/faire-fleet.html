Faire Fleet

Organization
nameThe Faire Fleet
typeFleet
baseIsle Broddick, Whamite Isles

The Faire Fleet was a fleet of ships from Isle Broddick in the Whamite Isles. The Faire Fleet represented Isle Broddick in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles
