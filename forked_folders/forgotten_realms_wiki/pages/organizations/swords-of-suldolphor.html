The Swords of Suldolphor was the militia of Suldolphor the Golden City in far eastern Calimshan during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Organization

The militia was composed of 16,000 soldiers armed with spears, shields, swords, and daggers. The Swords of Suldolphor were paid by, and ultimately answered to, the elite bodyguard of olanger Daeranatha Dorluu called the Flame Guard.
