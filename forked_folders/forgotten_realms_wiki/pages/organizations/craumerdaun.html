The Craumerdaun family was a powerful human house of Amn based in Eshpurta.

Activities

The family had a hand in horse breeding and training, with an estate and accompanying ranches at Beregost.

Members

  • General Labak Craumerdaun, of the Council Army.
  • Marcos Craumerdaun, of the Eshpurta Shield Council.
  • Vimal Craumerdaun, family accountant.
