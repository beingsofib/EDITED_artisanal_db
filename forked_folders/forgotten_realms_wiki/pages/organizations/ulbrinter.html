Ulbrinter () was a noble house of Waterdeep. Their trade and interests included shipping and shipwrights.

Base of Operations

Their estate within the city of Waterdeep, Ulbrinter Villa, housed 24 guards and six servants

  • Arthagast Ulbrinter - husband of Remallia
  • Arthius - half-elf son of Remallia and Arthagast
  • Serenore - half-elf daughter of Remallia and Arthagast
