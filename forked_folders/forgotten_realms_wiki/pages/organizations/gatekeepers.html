The Gatekeepers was a druidic sect of orcs and half-orcs in the world of Eberron. It was devoted to protecting the world from planar invasions, aberrations, fiends, and other threats that emerged from uncontrolled portals.

History

In ages long past, the Gatekeepers were founded to protect Eberron from planar threats, eventually thwarting a cataclysmic invasion from Xoriat. In the years that followed the druids, rangers, and barbarians of the Gatekeepers continued to guard against invaders and hunted down those that slipped through.

During the mid-1480s DR of Faerûn, the Lords of Dust managed to break into the Spinner's Prison and weakened the bonds on the Spinner of Shadows. This led to Lolth herself breaking open a Rift Between Worlds from the Demonweb Pits and allowing the Spinner to escape. Worst of all, the rift continued to grow and threatened to destroy the world of Eberron in the process.

The Gatekeepers joined the Church of the Silver Flame and the Twelve at the scene of the disaster, and lent their efforts to containing the portal. Despite this, the portal continued to grow, and Tavnar Kronnak made the decision to send a small team of heroes through to explore the other side and hopefully find a solution. Most fortunately the heroes met Elminster and found their way to Eveningstar, taking part in a greater struggle against Lolth and saving the Weave in the process. With the guidance of Valana they also stabilized the rift, preventing it from growing any larger and thus saving Eberron as well.
