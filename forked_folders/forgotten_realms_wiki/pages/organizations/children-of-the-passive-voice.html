The Children of the Passive Voice were an organization of monks devoted to Oghma, the Lord of Knowledge.

Activities

These learned monks were dedicated to protecting libraries and abbeys spread throughout Faerûn.

Notable Members

Rowan Silvercrown was a ranking member and leader of the order in 1372 DR.
