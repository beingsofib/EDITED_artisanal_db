The Yaqubi were a group of people inhabiting the Khopet-Dag mountains in the Endless Wastes.

Activities

The Yaqubi earned a living by harvesting silk from the giant spiders inhabiting the mountains. Each man and woman had their own, jealously guarded gathering spot, hidden from everyone else.
