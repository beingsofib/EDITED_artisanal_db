The Lontles were a family living in Ravens Bluff in the Vast in the late 14 century DR. They owned a shop selling rowboat fittings.

Location

Their shop stood in the Burnt Gables neighborhood of the Crow's End district of Ravens Bluff, next door to the Downunda Patisserie. This was a middle-class business area, and nearer the upper-class area than the poor area.

Activities

They sold and other fittings for rowboats. By the , however, Jemima Chisolm of the Downuda Patisserie had recently hired some of the Lontles to run errands, buying and fetching ingredients such as flour, fruits, nuts, and sundry seasonings.
