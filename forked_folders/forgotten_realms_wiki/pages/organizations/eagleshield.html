Eagleshield () was a noble family of Waterdeep.

Their symbol was a spread-winged eagle. They owned a tack shop and farm for treating ill animals in Amphail, and they produced harnesses of black leather, which featured their family crest.

Base of Operations

The family owned a villa in the Sea Ward, on the northeast corner of Cooper Street and Feather Street.

Notable Members

  • Nuthos Eagleshield: Patriarch in 1357 DR, through at least 1372 DR.
  • Glenn Eagleshield: Consort of Nuthos.
  • Arundel Eagleshield: Eldest daughter and heir of Nuthos and Glenn.
  • Narrovan Eagleshield: Patriarch circa 1479 DR.
  • Embrelle Eagleshield: Consort of Narrovan. Died of unintentional food poisoning.
