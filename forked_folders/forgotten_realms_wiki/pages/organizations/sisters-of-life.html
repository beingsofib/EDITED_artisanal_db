Sisters of Life

Organization
nameSisters of Life
baseSkuld, Mulhorand
favored deityIsis

The Sisters of Life were a religious order devoted to Isis in Mulhorand around 1369 DR.

This order served with followers of Anhur and Osiris as members of the Guardians of Skuld, who protected Mulhorand's capital, Skuld.
