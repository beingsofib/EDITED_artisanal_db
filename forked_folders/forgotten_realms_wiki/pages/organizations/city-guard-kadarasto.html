The City Guard of Kadarasto served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City Most Sinister".

Organization

The composition of the city guard as of was 2,000 soldiers, 1,000 cavalry, and three 300-man units of mamluks of the Devout Mamluk Society. All of these forces were commanded by Prince Nari ibn Aman.
