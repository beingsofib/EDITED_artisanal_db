The Thorp family was a Waterdhavian noble family.

Activities

This family made its money through caravan mastering and mercenary fighting. The holdings of the family were administered by a council of lords-regent composed of Stelar Nesher, Hulameros Rhond, and Khorom Thorp.

Members

  • Hulmara Thorp, matriarch as of 1372 DR
  • Hulameros Rhond, third cousin and heir to the family
  • Khorom Thorp, disowned brother of Hulmara
  • Stelar Nesher, daughter of Khorom
