Dagohnlar

Organization
nameDagohnlar
typeNoble family
baseMarsember, Cormyr

Dagohnlar was a noble house of Cormyr, based in Marsember, in the late 14 century DR. They had a suit of ancestral armor.

Members

  • Durexter Dagohnlar, merchant of rather ill repute
  • Starmara Dagohnlar, wife of Durexter
