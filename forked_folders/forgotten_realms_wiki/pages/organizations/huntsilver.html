Huntsilver

Organization
nameHouse Huntsilver
baseSuzail, Cormyr

Huntsilver was a noble house of Cormyr. An "old blood" family, it was considered one of the Royal Houses, owing to its close ties to the Obarskyr royal line.

Base

The family lived in Huntsilver Palace, situated beyond the Royal Gardens in Suzail.

They also had holdings in Dhedluk and Thunderstone.

History

After the Silver family split, they formed the Huntsilver, Truesilver, and Crownsilver families. The Huntsilvers were established by 245 DR.

The Huntsilvers chose not to take sides when civil war erupted between Regent Salember and Prince Rhigaerd II in the . Instead, they equivocated when Salember demanded soldiers and aid.

In the event of King Azoun IV's presumed death in the abraxus incident, the Huntsilvers were among those families who preferred replacing the monarchy with a ruling council, rather than a regency, in order to free Cormyr from what they saw as Obarskyr tyranny.

The fortunes of the Huntsilver family wavered for a while, when their sons died in wars with Sembia. Their daughters had been married to the Crownsilvers and Truesilvers, and were still awaiting heirs. Eventually, their fortunes must have shifted, for in the , they were a strong enough force for Raedra Obarskyr to look to for acknowledgement of her ascension.

Personality

The Huntsilver family was seen as aloof in the mid–14 century.

Activities

The Huntsilvers were historically focused on managing Cormyr's forests and their resources. In the early years of the kingdom, the family organized the systematic development of forests for farmland. In the 14th century, Huntsilvers had a prominent role in clearing the Hullack Forest.

Members of the Huntsilver family rarely appeared at the Royal Court, except on important occasions or on issues regarding the forests of Cormyr.

Members

Like the other Silver families, the Truesilvers would often marry its members to the Obarskyrs and other Silvers, such as Crownsilvers and Truesilvers.

Pre–14 Century

  • Chanthar Huntsilver, head of family at the beginning of the 13 century DR.
  • Jhalass Huntsilver, married to King Dhalmass and mother of King Palaghard II.
  • Lareth Huntsilver, married Roatha Ildraen, an innkeeper's daughter.

14 Century

  • Agrast Huntsilver, cousin to Elvarin Crownsilver.
  • Cordryn Huntsilver
  • Elanna Huntsilver, Maestoon's wife.
  • Hundilavatar Huntsilver, rumored to have pursued Tanalasta Obarskyr for courting.
  • Jostlyn Huntsilver, Knight Royal and personal bodyguard of Filfaeril Obarskyr in the mid–14 century.
  • Maestoon Huntsilver, head of family circa 1367 DR, and game warden of the King's Forest.
  • Shalanna Huntsilver, Maestoon's daughter.

15th Century

One of the following was the leader of House Huntsilver, as of 1483 DR.

  • Belvedere Huntsilver, uncle of Oriphaun and an aged member of the house from the town of Wheloon.
  • Oriphaun Huntsilver, nephew of Belvedere and a relic collector and an adventurer who helped to push back Netherese invasion of Wheloon in the 15 century DR.
  • Pelarra Huntsilver, mother of Glorin and Roatha Huntsilver. She offered her house's support to Raedra Obarskyr's ascension to the throne.
  • Roatha Huntsilver, twin sister of Glorin, and daughter of Pelarra. She was a child as of 1486 DR, blonde, and possessed dark eyes. Her mother pushed her to converse with Raedra instead of nudging Glorin.
  • Glorin Huntsilver, twin brother of Roatha.
