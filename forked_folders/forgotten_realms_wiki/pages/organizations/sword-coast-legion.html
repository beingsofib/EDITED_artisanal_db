The Sword Coast Legion was a militaristic religious organization active in the early 13 century DR.

History

The order was formed some years before the . Its members were clerics and paladins who took arms against the increased attacks from orcs along the Sword Coast. To battle the multitudes of battles against the savage humanoids, the Sword Coast Legion was given a set of enchanted maces known as the Fists of the Legion. It was unknown how long the Sword Coast Legion remained active, but their Fists of the Legion carried the organization's legacy into the late 14 century DR.
