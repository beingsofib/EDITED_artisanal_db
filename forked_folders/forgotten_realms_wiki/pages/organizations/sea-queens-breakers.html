The Sea Queen's Breakers was an adventuring group that venerated Umberlee, the Bitch Queen, loosely affiliated with the Church of Umberlee.

Activities

The adventuring order included priests of Umberlee. The Sea Queen's Breakers dealt in underwater treasure hunts and cargo recovery from the sea depths. They were known to adventure on land and in the Bitch Queen's domain.
