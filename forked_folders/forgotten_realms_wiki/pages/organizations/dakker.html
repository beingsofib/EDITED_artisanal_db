The Dakker family was an old, wealthy, and influential clan of Archendale, which was still active during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

The clan operated a trading coster, the Darkwater Brand, which dealt in ore, fresh products and Moonsea metalwork. Some of their side-businesses also included bodyguard services, as well as escorting precious cargo (such as captured creatures) and wealthy travelers.

While the merchandises sold in their shop were pricey, they were usually of good quality.

However this was all a façade for much more sinister endeavors: the family actually sold poison and nefarious potions, and dealt in kidnapping, smuggling, and slavery.

Tactics

The family employed lot of well-equipped mercenaries to watch over their unlawful activities. They explained the presence of those combatants, as well as their ownership of wagons fitted with cages and small siege weapons, by their side-businesses.

Base of Operations

The Darkwater Brand was active in Archenbridge, Hillsfar and Ordulin.

Relationships

The family had a bad reputation outside Archendale, and various rumors hinted at their criminal activities. In the Dale, they were careful not to break any laws, and were respected with a healthy dose of caution.

Rumors

According to some Arkhenfolk, an invisible creature watched over the Archenbridge shop and hunted down would-be thieves.

Members

  • Vzoun Dakker was the Head of the family during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
