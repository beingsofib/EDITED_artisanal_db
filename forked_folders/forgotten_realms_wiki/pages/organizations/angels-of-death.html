The Angels of Death were part of the standing army of the efreet in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire.

Organization

Fully 35,000 efreet armed with composite short bows made up the Angels of Death.

Activities

The Angels of Death were the archers of the efreet army. The archers were both mounted and unmounted. Mounted archers rode nightmares, ghost mounts, or skeletal horses.

Base of Operations

The City of Brass served as the base of the Angels of Death.
