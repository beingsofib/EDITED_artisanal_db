The Pearled Harp, also called the Purple Pearls, was a trading coster based in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

The coster traded in both Calimport and Cormyr.

History

The coster was formerly owned by Pasha Samiir el Volahrn, who'd grown powerful off the business. However, when he passed away in the late 1360s DR, he had no heirs. Thus, agents of Syl-Pasha Ralan el Pesarkhal went to Suzail, Cormyr, to inform Samiir's daughter by a courtesan, Vanda Volahrn, of her of her father's passing and of her birthright. They offered her Samiir's status as pasha and ownership of the Pearled Harp, provided she used it to support certain trade alliances beneficial to Calimshan's ruler. This made her one of the only female pashas in Calimport and blocked rival merchants who'd coveted her father's coster for themselves, and made her a target for death.
