The Windriders were an all-but-retired mercenary company based in Chondath.

History

They fought during the war against the arcanaloth Yrkhetep and were decisive in defeating the yugoloth's army in Hades.

Membership

The Windriders were griffon-mounted cavalry.

Contracts

By 1358 DR, the Windriders worked only for the glory.
