The Paladins of the Hand, also known as the Twelve Champions of Torm, was an order of paladins and other holy servants of Torm that were active in the Shining Plains during the 12 century DR.

History

In the , the Paladins of the Hand fought in the defense of Lothtia in the Shining Plains against the orc army of Thoktor Ironfangs.

Notable Members

  • Harondath, the paladin that famously bore the Veloghon of Vigilance during the battle of the plains.
