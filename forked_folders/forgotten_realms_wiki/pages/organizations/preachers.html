The Preachers were a faction of the holy slayer organization known as the Everlasting in 1367 DR.

Activities

The Preachers served the Everlasting by being a direct representative to the moralist mosques of Hajama and their imams.

Members

Batina al-Shaat was the leader of the Preachers.The Preachers had several sub-sections listed below:

Courageous Honor Guard : Dwarven warriors

Eagles : Hobgoblin footsoldiers
