The City Guard of Sikak served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City of Coins".

Organization

The composition of the city guard as of was 2,000 infantry, 3,000 mercenary barbarians, 500 cavalry, and an imperial guard of 200 ogres. Sikak's navy consisted of 20 ships with 400 sailors trained in ship-to-ship fighting.

Activities

The imperial palace guard of ogres was broken into three different groups. The smaller ships of the navy patrolled the shore of the Golden Gulf and the Bountiful Shoals.
