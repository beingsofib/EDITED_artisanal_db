Ironhanded

Organization
nameIronhanded
typeGuild
basePhlan, Moonsea North

The Ironhanded was the local ironworkers guild of Phlan. It operated within the city as of the late 15century DR.

Yanna Hammerbound was one of the higher-ranking member of the organization.
