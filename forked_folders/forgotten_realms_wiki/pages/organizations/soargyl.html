nameSoargyl
typeNoble family
baseSelgaunt, Sembia

The Soargyl family was a noble house of Sembia. It was seated in the city of Selgaunt and was an old enemy of House Uskevren. Their family badge was silver swans on a backdrop of green, and their motto was "Always even in the end."

They were rumored to use necromancy.

Members

  • Lord Rildinel Soargyl, head of the family until his death during the destruction of the first Stormweather Towers
  • Lord Loargon Soargyl, Rildinel's first son
  • Blester Soargyl, Rildinel's son
  • Lord Saclath Soargyl
  • Alale Soargyl
  • Lord Boarim Soargyl, head of the family until 1371 DR, when a lesser dread invaded Sarntrumpet Towers and slew him and his wife
  • Mora Soargyl, Boarim's wife
  • Lord Rorsin Soargyl, son of Boarim and Mora

Properties

The family owned a mansion, Sarntrumpet Towers, in the heart of Selgaunt.
