The Many Long Arms was a band of thieves, spies, and other criminals that served the Rundeen mercantile organization in the mid-to-late 14 century DR and active in the Shining Sea region.

Organization

Effectively a private thieves' guild serving the Rundeen, they were controlled directly by the First Yrshelem, the second tier of Rundeen leadership, but technically ranked below the Mitalibbar, their collectors and watchers, like their other subsidiaries.

The actual leader of the Many Long Arms around 1370 DR was a masked man known as el Chaddalk; only the Yrshelem knew his true identity. He was aided by his vizar, Harun yn Riikaln, the Scarlet Sorcerer.

Members were typically dispatched on missions in threes or fours, so they operated similarly to many adventuring companies without actually being one.

Membership

True to their name, the Many Long Arms were a vast organization, the biggest of its kind south of Tethyr and second in size only to the Shadow Thieves of Amn. Their ranks included rogues, thieves, housebreakers, goons, smugglers, and even assassins and pirates. They were required to give a 35% cut of their 'earnings' to the organization. In return, they had use of safehouses, fences, moneychangers, and new false identities when required.

Activities

On behalf of the Rundeen, the Many Long Arms stole goods, framed rivals, and collected intelligence. It was said that if the Rundeen required information on any locale under from the shore of the Shining Sea, then el Chaddalk would have it for them before the month was out. Their agents infiltrated almost every ship on the Shining Sea to acquire information or other peoples' goods.

Base of Operations

Again true to their name, the Many Long Arms had a huge reach thanks to their extensive network of agents. Their heartland was the Chultan Peninsula, but they reached across the Shining Sea to northern Calimshan, which was the origin of their name, and from there they could send agents further north. They were primarily dominant in Almraiven and Teshburl in Calimshan, with limited presences in Memnon and in Derlusk and Yallasch in the Border Kingdoms. They had many other agents in all other Shining Sea ports, but these ones were more independent and only reported in once a year.

Relationships

The Many Long Arms were one of a number of mercenary and adventuring bands, along with the Clenched Fist and Fist of Rundeen, formed by the Rundeen with the express purpose of opposing the Harpers.

Although almost as large as the Shadow Thieves, the Many Long Arms were more discreet and little-known. Few folk even knew of their existence outside the Rundeen and their enemies.

History

Within four hours of the death of Pasha Pook in the , the Many Long Arms brazenly looted his grand villa, Pook's Palace in Calimport. The twenty thieves simply sneaked around in the background, overlooked by panicking guards and distressed family and all, taking away absolutely everything of any value.

They had several clashes with the Harpers, but were defeated several times by them, who saw them as no longer effective by 1367 DR.
