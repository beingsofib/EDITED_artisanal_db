The Channath family was a noble merchant family of the city of Tantras in the Vast in the 14 century DR. The head of the family sat on the High Council that governed Tantras.

In 1358 DR, shortly after the Time of Troubles, the Channaths were allied with the Elovear, Laranadda, Tithlin, and Vandover families.
