The Yevven Riders were the standing army of Yeshpek in the Lake of Steam region of south Faerûn.

Base of Operations

The Yevven Riders operated out of the Crownarch, a stronghold located in the southwest section of the city.

Organization

The Riders were led by High Rider Lassratha Nalorth, a giant of a woman with a fiery temper. Each Rider was a skilled horseman proficient with the scimitar, hand crossbow, and javelin.

Activities

The High Rider kept her troops is top fighting shape. Patrols of forty soldiers ranged around Yeshpek constantly. They carried a special powder that when placed on designated beacon fires, could be used to create signals and relay information back to base.

The Riders also trained all of the soldiers in Yeshpek's city watch called the Kardryn.
