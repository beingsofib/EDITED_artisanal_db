The Wilddwarf Brigade (also known as the Wilddwarves) was a dwarven battlerager squad in Citadel Adbar.

History

In 1484 DR, the Wilddwarves were led by a dwarf simply known as Crunch.

Tactics

The Wilddwarves drank liquor before battle.
