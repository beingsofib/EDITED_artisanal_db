The Surefeet Trademasters was a merchant company in Faerûn.

History

A council of merchants ran the operations of this merchant company. Activities included providing expert guides, guards, and escorts for caravans. Their prices were steep, but experience combined with a great reputation made the price worthwhile for many.

Base of Operations

The Surefeet Trademasters were based out of Scornubel.
