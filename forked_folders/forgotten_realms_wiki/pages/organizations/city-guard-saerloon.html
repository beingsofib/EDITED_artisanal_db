The City Guard of Saerloon served as the main line of defense and police force for the city.

Organization

Lady Merelith was the leader of the city guard in the . She commanded 7,000 guardsmen who were called "helms." Merelith was assisted by two Overcaptains–Burinta Shammarcron and Maerlus Balaern.

Activities

The city guard served as the standing army for the city, but they also policed the streets, surrounding countryside, farmlands, and waterways. The waterways were patrolled with eleven ships.
