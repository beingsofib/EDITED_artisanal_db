Ni'Tessine was a noble sun elven house of Cormanthyr.

Organization

The family's ruling line used heraldry colored in pegasus-related colors such as powder-white and sky-blue. Lesser lines used griffon-related colors such as dun-brown and gold.

History

Following the destruction of Myth Drannor in the 8 century DR, many members of the Ni'Tessine clan emigrated to the city of Selu'Maraar in the sea elven realm of Serôs. Along with other immigrant clans, they were looked down on by the "native" Serôsian elves even though the majority of the city's population originally hailed from other "surface elven" communities.

As of the 14 century DR, the clan also had a presence in Waterdeep. This branch of the family, led by Fenian Ni'Tessine, were elven supremacists who violently opposed the romance between Princess Amnestria of Evermeet and the human ranger Bran Skorlsun.

Notable Members

7 century DR

  • Cystenn Ni'Tessine, patriarch and Lord Speaker of the clan, and a powerful wizard.
  • Dannyd Ni'Tessine, heir to the clan and an accomplished bladesinger.
  • Rhys Ni'Tessine, clan elder and former aronor (officer) of Myth Drannor's Honor Spellguard.
  • Myrddin Ni'Tessine, clan archmage. A magical prodigy in training to become a High Mage.

14th century DR

  • Fenian Ni'Tessine, who assassinated King Zaor Moonflower of Evermeet.
  • Filauria Ni'Tessine, daughter of Fenian and a gifted circle-singer.
  • Tintagel Ni'Tessine, son of Fenian and watch commander of the City Watch of Waterdeep.
