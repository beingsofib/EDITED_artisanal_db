Lords of the Inner Reach

Organization
nameLords of the Inner Reach
typeMercenary company
baseOslin, Chessenta

The Lords of the Inner Reach were a mercenary company based in the village of Oslin in Chessenta circa 1357 DR.

Without other defenses, the people of Oslin hired mercenaries like the Lords of the Inner Reach for protection. However, they had also been employed by local farmers to push rivals off their lands so they could claim them.
