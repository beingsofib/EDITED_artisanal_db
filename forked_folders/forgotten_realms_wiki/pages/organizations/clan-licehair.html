Clan Licehair

Organization
nameClan Licehair
typeDwarven clan

Clan Licehair was a minor dwarven clan that inhabited Faerûn and neighbored Clan Stonehand sometime before 1372 DR.

History

Clan Licehair was known for the attention its bachelorettes gave to the young warriors of Clan Stonehand, following their defeat of the black dragon Zarkursk and inheriting its massive gem hoard. The Clan Licehair women's attempts to marry the Clan Stonehand warriors was so unwanted that Kurgin Stonehand, the leader of Clan Stonehand commissioned the creation of dwarven belts of charming to make the young warriors unappealing even by low dwarfish standards.
