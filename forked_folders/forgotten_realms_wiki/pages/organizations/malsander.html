The Malsander family was a dynasty of traders in Waterdeep during the 14 century DR.

Activities

The Malsanders celebrated their ships with portraits of them for having earned them their wealth, an almost certain indication that they were maritime traders.

Symbols

The Malsander crest was an eagle.

History

By , Mrilla Malsander was trying to get the Malsanders recognized as nobility. She was also involved as a go-between between Auvrarn Labraster and Brelma.
