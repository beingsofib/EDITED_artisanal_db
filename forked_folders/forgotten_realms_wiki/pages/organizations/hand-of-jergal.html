The Hand of Jergal was an elite order of priests devoted to Jergal, the Lord of the End of Everything.

Organization

The priests of the Hand of Jergal commanded groups tasked with avenging slights against Jergal or his church. The most common of these offenses was raising or resurrecting someone without paying tribute to Jergal. Another common offense was looting a tomb under the church's protection.

History

The Hand of Jergal was extinct as of the mid–14 century DR.
