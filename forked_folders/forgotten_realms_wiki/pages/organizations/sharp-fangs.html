The Sharp Fangs were a tribe of goblins that inhabited the Orsraun Mountains in the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

Their members typically wielded short swords and rode upon the backs of worgs.

History

In 1358 DR, the Sharp Fangs were allied with the Muktaran empire and fought for them in their Turmish war at the Battle of Wilna Pass.
