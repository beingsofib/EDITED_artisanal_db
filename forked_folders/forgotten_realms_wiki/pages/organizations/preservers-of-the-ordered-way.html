The Preservers of the Ordered Way was a monastic order devoted to Deneir, the Lord of All Glyphs.

Activities

The monks of the Preservers of the Ordered Way focused their attentions on copying and illuminating texts. As a whole these monks kept to themselves in order to focus on their work.
