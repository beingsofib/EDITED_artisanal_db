High Moor Heroes' Guild

Organization
nameHigh Moor Heroes' Guild
baseElturel, Western Heartlands

The High Moor Heroes' Guild was a guild of adventurers based in the city of Elturel in the Western Heartlands around 1358 DR.

The closest thing to a rogue's guild permitted by the High Rider, Lord Dhelt, it was a legal outlet for the poor, desperate, unruly, maladjusted, or just violent people of the city.

This adventuring band made regular expeditions onto the High Moor, "scouring" whatever they found. Guild members also tutored each other and other adventurers for a fee, with a discount for members.
