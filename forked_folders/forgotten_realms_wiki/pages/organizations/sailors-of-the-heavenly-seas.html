The Sailors of the Heavenly Seas was a sect of followers of Valkur, the Captain of the Waves.

Description

Originally from Faerûn, the Saiors of the Heavenly Seas decided to depart Toril in favor of exploring the endlessness of Realmspace aboard spelljammer ships. They viewed Valkur as more of a god of exploration versus a god of the oceans.
