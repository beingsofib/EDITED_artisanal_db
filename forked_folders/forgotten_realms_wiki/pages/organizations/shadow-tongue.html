The Shadow Tongue was a crime ring operating in Laothkund during the late 14 century DR.

Structure

The leader of the Shadow Tongue was the necromancer Sathra. A smaller section operating as the higher-ups within the organization took the name of the Shadow Cadre.

Base of Operations

The Shadow Tongue had a lair in Laothkund. There was a vault in the building, which served as Sathra's base of operations.

Activities

In , the Shadow Tongue were involved in protection rackets, traffic in infernal drugs, illicit slave trade in children, and a scheme to implicate the members of the ruling council in a conspiracy with Thay.

History

The leader of the Shadow Tongue, the necromancer Sathra, had called for the theft of the sword Angul by midwinter of the . After the thief Gage stole it back from them, Sathra decided to pursue the thief and the blade's rightful owner into the Yuirwood, where she was slain by Kiril Duskmourn.
