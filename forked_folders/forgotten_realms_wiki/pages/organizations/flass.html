The Flass were an old noble family from Thay.

Activities

As members of the nobility, the Flass family were among the rulers of Thay, and counted many prominent Red Wizards and at least one Tharchion among their name. As Thayvian nobles, even members without positions of formal power were referred to with the honorifics of "Daeron"/"Daeronness" (literally, "noble-born"), or with "Sir"/"Mistress", "Your Excellency", or "Your Honor".

Members of the family shaved their heads and wore tattoos or body paint, as per Thayvian custom.

History

The Flass were an ancient Mulan noble family from Mulhorand. They were among the most influential Thayvian families as of the mid–14 century DR, however they were clearly in decline by that time.

Members

Notable members of the family included:

  • Nyressa Flass, the Zulkir of Necromancy as of the 12 century DR, also known as "the Vampire Zulkir".
  • Dmitra Flass, the Tharchion of Eltabbar throughout the mid-to-late 14 century DR, also known as "The Tharchioness" or the "First Princess of Thay".
  • Marcos Flass, cousin of Dmitra and a Red Wizard golemcrafter, active in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
