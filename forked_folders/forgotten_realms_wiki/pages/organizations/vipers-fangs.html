The Viper's Fangs was a secret order of assassins, spies, and thugs devoted to Sseth, the World Serpent.

Activities

Members of this organization infiltrated noble houses, mercenary companies, trading costers, government agencies, and adventuring groups across Faerûn. Their primary role was to serve as critical information gatherers for the yuan-ti nation, but the Viper's Fangs occasionally eliminated threats as well.

Organization

The Viper's Fangs were composed of humans and yuan-ti purebloods. They referred to themselves as Vipers.
