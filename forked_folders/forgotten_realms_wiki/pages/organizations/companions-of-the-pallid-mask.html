The Companions of the Pallid Mask were a group of Jergal's priests who specialized in combating and commanding the undead. They eliminated the undead creatures whose existence was not sanctioned by the church or who had proven to be troublesome. They also were the supervisors of the skeleton and zombie work crews that the church sometimes ran to profit itself.
