Lords of the Tempest

Organization
nameLords of the Tempest
favored deityTalos

The Lords of the Tempest were a rumored organization of wizards that served the god Talos.

They were a mysterious group known only in rumor around 1370 DR. They were said to be wizards who specialized in elemental magic in exotic combinations.
