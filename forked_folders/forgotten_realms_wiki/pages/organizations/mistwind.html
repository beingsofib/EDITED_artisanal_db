Organization
nameMistwind

baseMarsember, Cormyr

Mistwind was a noble house of Cormyr.

Base

They had a seat in Marsember and a mansion called Mistwind Towers.

Description

This noble house was very old, and the family were private and few in number. The family coat of arms were a seashell crossed with a trident in gold upon a blue-green field.
