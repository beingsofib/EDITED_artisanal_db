The Warriors of the Star, also known as the Warstars, were an order of knights from Tethyr that served as bodyguards to the King and Queen of the realm. These 40-strong guardians were led by the Monarch's Champion, Vander Stillhawk, from their barracks located within the Royal Palace of Faerntarn.

Among their duties were to act as personal bodyguards for the monarchy, and screen all the court visitors for anyone who may be a threat or whose loyalties may conflict with the crown.

History

The group used to be Star Protective Services.
