Bloody Buggers

nameThe Bloody Buggers
typePirates
baseLand of the Beastly Brigands, the Whamite Isles
racesHuman

The Bloody Buggers was a group of pirates from the Land of the Beastly Brigands in the Whamite Isles. The Buggers represented the Land of the Beastly Brigands in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles
