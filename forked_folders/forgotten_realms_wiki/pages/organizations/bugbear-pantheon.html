The bugbear pantheon was an extinct pantheon whose deities were once worshiped by bugbears.

History

The bugbears once had a pantheon with deities unique to their race and culture. However, many of them were cut down by Maglubiyet, though the two brothers, Hruggek and Grankhul were spared. The original pantheon was absorbed into Maglubiyet's goblinoid pantheon. However, after Maglubiyet's conquest, bugbears still worshiped the two brother deities, even though it meant submitting to their conquerer's rule.

Members

Some known members of the pantheon were:

  • Hruggek, surviving member and tyrannical deity
  • Grankhul, surviving member and deity of duty

The bugbears also knew of a god of fear known as Skiggaret, though it was unknown if this deity was ever part of the original bugbear pantheon or even the reformed goblinoid pantheon.
