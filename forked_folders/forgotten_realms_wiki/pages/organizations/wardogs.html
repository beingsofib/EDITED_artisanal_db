Wardogs

Organization
nameWardogs
typeMercenary company
baseAirspur, Chessenta

The Wardogs were a prominent mercenary company operating in the mid–14 century DR. They were based in the city of Airspur in Chessenta.

Around 1357 DR, they'd recently battled in Chondath and suffered heavy losses.
