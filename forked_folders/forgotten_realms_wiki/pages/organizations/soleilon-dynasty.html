The Soleilon Dynasty was the first established dynasty to rule over the city-state of Westgate. It was established in when the righteous paladin Gen Soleilon slew the vampire King Orlak and claimed the throne. The Soleilon Dynasty would reign until .

Monarchs

King Gen "the Radiant King"

Reigned -137 DR – -91 DR. First King of the Soleilon Dynasty.

King Lemere I the "Merchant King"

Reigned -91 DR – -74 DR. Firstborn son and heir of Gen.

King Lemere II

Reigned -74 DR – -68 DR. Firstborn son and heir of Lemere I.

Regent Lharida

Reigned -68 DR – -55 DR. Wife of Lemere II and regent until her son came of age.

King Ryndarth I the "Boy King"

Reigned -68 DR – -27 DR. Firstborn son and heir of Lemere II.

Crown Prince Ryndarth II "the Younger"

Reigned – . Firstborn son and heir of Ryndarth I. Slain by a doppelganger before his father's death.

King Ryndarth II the "Masque King"

Reigned -27 DR. A doppleganger who assumed the form of the secretly deceased Ryndarth.
