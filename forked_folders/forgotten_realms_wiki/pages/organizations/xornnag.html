House Xornnag was a powerful human family of Amn.

Members

  • Lord Wulver Xornnag, a family patriarch who joined the Council of Six, becoming Dahaunarch in 1342 DR and Pommarch in 1345 DR.
  • Captain Amlos Xornnag, the commanding officer of Hillfort Ishla up to 1370 DR.
