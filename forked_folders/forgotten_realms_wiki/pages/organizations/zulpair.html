The Zulpair family was a Waterdhavian noble family.

Activities

This family made its money through shipping goods with their merchant ships.

Members

  • Olomar Zulpair, patriarch as of 1372 DR
  • Anyla Zulpair, consort of Olomar
  • Gerath Zulpair, eldest son of Olomar and heir to the family
