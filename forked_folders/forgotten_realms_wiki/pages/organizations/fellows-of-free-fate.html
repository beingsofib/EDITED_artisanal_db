The Fellows of Free Fate, known locally as the Triffs, was a sect of Tymoran clergy within the greater church who dedicated themselves to fight against the actions of Beshaban followers.

Activities

The group specifically their efforts to act in opposition of the Beshaban order of assassins known as the Black Fingers.

Members

Any priest of Tymora was free to join the sect so long as they received the blessing of a single senior Fellow.
