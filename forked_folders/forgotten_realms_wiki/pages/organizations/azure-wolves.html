The Azure Wolves were a mighty mercenary company active east of the Sword Coast in the mid–15 century DR. They were so renowned that any mercenary active in the region knew their name even a decade after they had ceased activity.

History

The Azure Wolves—and their leader, General Varnoth Wender—were widely feared. Their fame peaked around the , after which the injured and exhausted General Varnoth left the company to live a life of atonement at Dragon's Rest on Stormwreck Isle.

Notable Members

  • General Varnoth Wender, the company's feared and fearsome leader.
