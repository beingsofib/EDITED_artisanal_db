The cragwardens were defenders of the ancient dwarven stronghold of Highhome on Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles. These dwarves were accordingly known by the title of Cragwarden.

History

Following the return of the fey realm of Sarifal to Gwynneth in the , evil fey would subsequently attack the local dwarves, forcing them to all but abandon the island and flee through the Dwarvenhome Tunnels. By the late 15 century DR, only a small retinue of cragwardens remained in Highhome as the last guardians of their ancient fastness.
