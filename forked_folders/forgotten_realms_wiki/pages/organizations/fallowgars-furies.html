Fallowgar's Furies was an all-female adventuring band from Tethyr, active in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

By the , Fallowgar's Furies were believed to had been dead for a decade, perished in the Weldath forest of Tethyr. The band's name, however, was remembered thanks to stories of the women's treasures buried nearby the Hunter's Tree. Along with another group – The Striking Talons's treasures, it was believed that Fallowgar's Furies buried some elven magic items, coins, and gems the adventurers collected as they adventured in the Forest of Tethir.
