The Hunabar () family was a notable part of the nobility of Waterdeep, first gaining nobility status in .

Organization

As of the , prominent members of the house included patriarch Haskar, consort Amonra, and Haskar's eldest son and heir Haskar Hunabar II.

Activities

The Hunabar family were most notable for importing and trading in textiles and fashions.

Base of Operations

The Hunabar Villa was located in the North Ward of Waterdeep on the southeast corner of Hassantyr's Street and Ilzantil Street, and consisted of two-story buildings.

Possessions

As of 1368 DR, Horth Hunabar had in his possession a dagger of homing, a magical dagger specifically crafted as a throwing weapon that would teleport back to its scabbard.

Members

  • Haskar Hunabar: Patriarch and leader of House Hunabar.
  • Amonra Hunabar: Consort of Haskar.
  • Haskar Hunabar II: Heir of House Hunabar and eldest son of Haskar.
  • Horth Hunabar: A member of a group of Waterdhavian nobles who took to delving into Undermountain via the Yawning Portal.
  • Elquaern Hunabar: A wizard that disappeared after fighting the green dragon Galarrdratha.
