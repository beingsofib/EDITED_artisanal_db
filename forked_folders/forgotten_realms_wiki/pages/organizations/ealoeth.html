Ealoeth was an elven house of Cormanthor.

Livery

The colors of House Ealoeth were sky blue, violet and rose. Their symbol was a two-addorsed pegasi volant, which lay on a sky blue field.

History

They'd become slick manipulators with ties across the continent by the time of the Fall of Myth Drannor.
