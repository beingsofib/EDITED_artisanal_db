The Order of Cryptic Transformational Purity was an order of mystics who worshiped Istishia, the Water Lord.

Organization

The Order of Cryptic Transformational Purity chose for their symbol a human transforming into a fish. Since their symbol resembled a merman, members of this order were often simply referred to as "fishtishians" or the Water Lord's fishmen.
