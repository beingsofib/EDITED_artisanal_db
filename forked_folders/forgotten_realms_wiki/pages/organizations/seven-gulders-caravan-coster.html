Seven Gulders Caravan Coster

nameSeven Gulders Caravan Coster

The Seven Gulders Caravan Coster was a business based in Yallasch in the Border Kingdoms in the late 14 century DR.

Though there were no outward signs, the coster was directly controlled by the leadership of the Rundeen, a mercantile organization, known as the Yrshelem. The only clue was that it was named for the gulder, a Tethyrian gold piece, indicating it had once been an independent business that had been taken over and renamed.
