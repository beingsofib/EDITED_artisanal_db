Silvershield was a powerful patriar family of Baldur's Gate.

Activities

Fabulously wealthy, the Silvershields lived a life of extravagant luxury as they subtly worked their influence on the political scene of Baldur's Gate.

Base of Operations

Silvershield estate, located on the western edge of the Upper City, was considered the grandest of all the city's noble manors.

History

The Silvershield family rose to prominence at some point in the 1300s DR. Before long, they were represented on Baldur's Gate's Council of Four, a tradition that went on nearly unbroken until the late 15 century.

Notable Members

14 century

  • Entar Silvershield, Grand Duke of Baldur's Gate
  • Skie Silvershield, Entar's daughter who took up the life of an adventurer
  • Eddard Silvershield, the deceased son of Entar

15 century

  • Alana Silvershield, a member of the family in the late 15 century DR.
  • Torlin Silvershield, High Artificer of Gond, Duke of Baldur's Gate, and Chosen of Bhaal
  • An unknown Silvershield was the third husband of Olivine Ambergul, before she poisoned him.
