The Red Blade was a mercenary company of the mid-3 century DR.

Equipment

The company's men wore leather armor and were armed. They all had badges with a scarlet sword across a white shield.

History

In , they passed through the Herald's Horn in the Western Heartlands on their way to the Calishar, where they would seek guard employment. They briefly met with Elminster and witnessed an argument between two prospectors, Surgath Ilder and Karlmuth Hauntokh.
