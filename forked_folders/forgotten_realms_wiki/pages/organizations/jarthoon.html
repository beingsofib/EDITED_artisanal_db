Jarthoon was a noble house of Cormyr of the 12 century DR.

Base

They were based at Jarthoon, or Jarthroon, which lay west of Suzail on the northern shore of the Lake of Dragons. It was long lost by the 14 century DR.

Members

  • Ongmar Jarthoon, a rebellious young lord of Jarthoon
