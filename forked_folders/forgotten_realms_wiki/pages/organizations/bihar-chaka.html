The Bihar chaka was a prominent chaka within the Shining Lands of southeast Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR and a member of the Chakas of 31.

Base of Operations

They were based out of Kolapar in Estagund.

Activity

The Bihar chaka's wealth was amassed through the sale of grain and shipping services.
