The Theorists was an intellectual group in Mulmaster in 1491 DR.

History

The Theorists were a group of friends who indulged in arguing about philosophy, literature, art, and politics. The core members of the group were Thibauld, Eugone, Archill, and Lucianne. They lived off money provided by other members of the group and noble scions: Elred Korsinchev, Orym Illustriana, and Liticia Gos.
