The Mallorhen family was a noble house of Tethyr that briefly held the throne in the 9 century DR during the greater Ithal-Strohm-Bormul period.

History

Tibor Mallorhen came to the throne by marrying King Strohm V's eldest daughter, who did not inherit it on her father's passing in the . Instead, her spouse Tibor became king, followed by their son Samyte. Though they had several sons, the dynasty was cut short by the assassin Uthaedeol the Blood-drenched, who killed the king and his heirs in the . With the dynasty extinguished, the throne passed to Strohm's younger daughter, Alisande, who started the Queens' Dynasty
