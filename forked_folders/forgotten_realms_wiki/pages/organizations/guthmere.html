The Guthmeres were a patriar family of Baldur's Gate around 1482 DR. They owned many butcheries and tanneries.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Lady Eshvelt Guthmere was a member of the Parliament of Peers of Baldur's Gate and was a supporter of law and order brought forth by the steel watchers and their creator – the lowborn Lord Enver Gortash.
