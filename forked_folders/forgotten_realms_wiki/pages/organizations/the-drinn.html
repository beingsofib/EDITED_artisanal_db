The Drinn were a tribe of kobolds in northwest Faerûn in the 14 century DR.

Organization

The tribe was led by a kobold named Olgloth Ryndrinn.

Activities

The Drinn often slew any miners they encountered in their mountains. They also sometimes conducted attacks on Citadel Adbar.

Base of Operations

The Drinn made their home somewhere in the Ice Mountains north of Ascore.

Members

There were around 3,700 kobolds within this tribe.
