The Disciples of the Yielding Way, who were also called the Brothers and Sisters of the Open Palm, were a monastic order devoted to Eldath, the Goddess of Singing Waters.

Activities

The monks of the order guarded sites sacred to Eldath and traveled to collect information on other isolated groves. Though they were quite capable of defending themselves, the monks never instigated violence.
