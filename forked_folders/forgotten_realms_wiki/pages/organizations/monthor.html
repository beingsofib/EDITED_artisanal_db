Monthor

Organization
nameMonthor
baseMarsember, Cormyr

Monthor was a noble house of Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

Base

Circa 1373 DR, the family owned a mansion in Marsember named Monthorhall.

Members

  • Lady Baerdra Monthor, who opposed the Obarskyrs.
