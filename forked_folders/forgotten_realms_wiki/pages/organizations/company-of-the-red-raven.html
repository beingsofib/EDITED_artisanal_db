The Company of the Red Raven were a Cormyrean adventuring company that operated in the mid-14 century DR. On 12 Ches of the , they were burned to death by the Great Red Wyrm Uryiax. Their service was commemorated by a memorial plaque in the office of Registrant General Lord Partic Thistle in the Royal Court of Suzail.
