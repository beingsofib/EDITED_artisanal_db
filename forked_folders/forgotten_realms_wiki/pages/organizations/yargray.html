The Yargray tribe was one of the groups of frost giants that dwelled within the mountain domain of Tholvarr in the Spine of the World.

History

For many years the tribe was led by Jarl Orel "the Grayhand". By the late 14 century DR he had grown quite old. His daughter, Gerti Orelsdottr sought leadership of the tribe in his final years.
