Barelder (sometimes known as Bareldur) was a noble family of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

The Barelders held Windcrag House, a farming estate and mansion on the Tun Shores in the West Reaches of Cormyr. They also possessed the small urban mansion called Heldartowers in Suzail.

History

In the , Lord Montror Barelder was slain at the Council of Dragons by a blueflame ghost. His corpse was removed from the Hall of Justice to the Chapel of the Valiant.

Members

  • Seven Lords Barelder preceded Montror.
  • Lord Montror Barelder and his wife Lady Asmrae Barelder.
  • Lady Narnathra Barelder, who inherited from her father Montror. She married to Lord Varkander Heldruth, and had Lady Laeyalane Tantorn and Lady Cathone Ravenhill as mistresses.
  • Maereenra Barelder and Braeryl Barelder, the children of Narnathra and Varkander.
  • Two younger siblings of Narnathra.
