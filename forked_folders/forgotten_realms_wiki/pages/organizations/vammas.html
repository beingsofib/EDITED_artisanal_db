Vammas was one of the numerous noble families that composed the patriar of Baldur's Gate.

Activities

Vammas made its fortune from controlling the majority of trade with Chult.

Base of Operations

The family's city estate, known as Vammas House, stood in the Temples District of the Gate's Upper City, between two crossings: Belltoll Street and Wendserpent Street in the north and Sornbanner Street and Wendserpent Street to the south.

Members

A late 15 century DR painting by Oskar Fevras depicted two contemporary eldest sisters, scions of the Vammas family, conspiring to shake up their noble bloodline. Both sisters appeared be humans with light skin tone and dark hair.

Lord Randolf Vammas, a member of the Parliament of Peers, was one of the guests who attended Enver Gortash's coronation in Wyrm's Rock of Baldur's Gate in the .
