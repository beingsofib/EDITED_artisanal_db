The Scoured Legion (also known as the Scourged Legion) of Hellgate Keep, later Splendarrmornn, was a strong company-horde of tanarukks.

Leadership

The Scoured Legion was led by Kaanyr Vhok, a cambion who proclaimed himself "the Sceptered One and Master of the Scoured Legions." By the Spellplague, High Warchief Ghiirvox led the Scoured Legion.

Members

Vhok's Scoured Legion consisted of 2,000 tanarukk warriors with auxiliary troops: bugbears, ogres, giants, etc. They had their own forges and armories.

Vhok lost 120 tanarukks in the Siege of Menzoberranzan.

Vhok's tanarukks originally numbered 1,200. By 1479 DR, they increased to 7,000.

Base

The Scoured Legion was originally based in Hellgate Keep. After the fall of the keep, they settled in the Underdark's Ammarindar, where they remained until 1372 DR. After the failed Siege of Menzoberranzan in 1373 DR, they returned to Hellgate Keep. The Spellplague forced them to change base.

The Scoured Legion was based in Splendarrmornn of Ammarindar during the Spellplague era.

History

The Scoured Legion invaded the Nameless Dungeon in Alturiak 1369 DR, opposed by a band of snakelike creatures.

In 1372 DR to 1373 DR during the War of the Spider Queen, they participated in the Siege of Menzoberranzan alongside the duergar army of Gracklstugh with the support of the Jaezred Chaulssin, but withdrew just as the tide of battle turned against them, leaving their allies to be decimated. Vhok subsequently removed his headquarters to Hellgate Keep.

Later in the year they besieged Sundabar but were again repelled. Kaanyr Vhok and his consort Aliisza, fled for the Elemental Plane of Fire through a portal beneath the city, leaving the Legion fractured.

Before they completely fractured, tanarukk Warchief Ghaarzhvex took over and a century later, circa 1479 DR of the Spellplague era, they came under High Warchief Ghiirvox. By then, their official faith was the exarch Garagos. Ghiirvox's predecessor died because of the Araumycos dream madness.

Notable members

Kaanyr Vhok : Leader of the Scoured Legion.

Aliisza : An alu-fiend and Vhok's chief consort and advisor.

Ruinfist : A tanarukk captain in 1372 DR. His left hand was useless but was encased in a spiked battle gauntlet.

Rorgak : Vhok's chief aid and lieutenant during the Siege of Menzoberranzan. He was red-scaled.

Ghaarzhvex: A tanarukk Warchief and the second leader of the Scoured Legion before the arrival of the Spellplague.

Ghiirvox : A tanarukk High Warchief and the fourth leader of the Scoured Legion in 1479 DR. His uncommon violet-blue skin made him rare amongst the tanarukk.

Jervaan : The Grand Bloodreaver in 1479 DR who spread the worship of Tempus's exarch Garagos within the Scoured Legion.
