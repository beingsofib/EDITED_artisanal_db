The Wolf lodge was a lodge of Rashemaar berserkers based in the town of Taporan as of the mid–14 century DR.

Base of Operations

As with most buildings in Taporan, the Wolf lodge's longhouse hugged the waterfront of the River Tir.

Activities

The Wolf lodge was the largest of Taporan's many berserkers lodges. Most of these berserkers laid their enemies out before tearing them to pieces while they were downed.
