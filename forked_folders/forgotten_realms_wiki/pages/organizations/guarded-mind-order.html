nameGuarded Mind Order

The Guarded Mind Order was a monastic order dedicated to knowledge and observation that existed in the late 14 century DR.

Organization

The Guarded Mind Order was known for its strict rules and teachings. The monks of the order were tasked with studying, witnessing, and recording. Their targets were individuals, races, regions, and wars. Members of the Guarded Mind Order were not allowed to participate or interfere in the events they were recording.

History

In the , a half-elven monk of the order, Cyphus Ordinae, was observing the recent events transpiring in the City of Splendors, Waterdeep. He chronicled the series of assassinations performed by drow, who seemingly started appearing from the mega-dungeon of Undermountain. Cyphus observed heroes being assembled by Durnan of the Yawning Portal and the events that followed.
