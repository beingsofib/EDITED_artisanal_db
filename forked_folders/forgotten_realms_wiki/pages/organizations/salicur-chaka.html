The Salicur chaka was a prominent chaka within the Shining Lands of southeast Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR and a member of the Chakas of 31.

Base of Operations

The Salicur chaka was based in the port city of Huorm on the Golden Water in central Durpar.

Activity

They were involved in the copper and textile industries.
