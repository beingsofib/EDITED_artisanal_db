House Alenuath was a moon elf house on Evermeet.

History

The house was established by the legendary Haramara Alenuath, who reputedly killed the red dragon Skarra with a single arrow shot from her bow.

Membership

Its members were typically rangers, hunters, or others who spent a great deal of time alone, which their sun elf neighbors thought unusual. The house members were often to be found scattered across Evermeet on their adventures.

As of 1368 DR, the house was led by Thalanil Alenuath.

Base of Operations

Although the house had a small manse in Leuthilspar adorned in the family's colors, it was often unoccupied for extended periods. It was cared for by the mostly moon elf servants. As they could more-or-less do as they liked during these times, they thought the Alenuaths to be good employers.

Possessions

Haramara's bow was a non-magical bow that had great significance in the house. It was held by the house's leader.

The house's colors were gray and green.

Members

  • Haramara Alenuath, the legendary founder of the house.
  • Thalanil Alenuath, leader of the house in 1368 DR.
  • Adamar Alenauth, a moon elf warrior loyal to King Zaor Moonflower. He was the father of Shanyrria and Rhenalyr. Through Montagor Amarillis's maneuvers and threats, Adamar kept the conception of Rhenalyrr a secret. He committed suicide with the family's moonblade on the day Rhenalyrr died.
  • Shanyrria Alenuath, a moon elf bladesinger, daughter of Adamar and half-sister of Rhenalyrr. She learned of her half-brother's existence with Adamar's last breath and forced Lydi'aleera Amarillis to draw a moonblade, ending in her death.
  • Rhenalyrr Alenuath, moon elf half-brother of Shanyrria by former Queen Lydi'aleera. Unbeknownst to him, Prince Rhenalyrr was secretly conceived by his mother with a magical potion that induced Adamar to falsify a son of Zaor Moonflower to the crown. He died when attempting to bond with the King's Blade.
