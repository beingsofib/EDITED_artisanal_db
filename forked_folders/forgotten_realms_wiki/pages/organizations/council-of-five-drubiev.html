The Council of Five was the ruling body of government in the town of Drubiev in the Great Amber Steppes of the Hordelands in 1375 DR.

Activities

As the name implied, the council comprised five elected officials charged with making decisions about Drubiev's affairs, including the appointment of the First Contable. The five councilmen and women were prominent figures of Drubiev who were elected as a group during every Shieldmeet.

Members

Known members of the council as of 1375 DR included Emmezel Tersku, Pavel Tragosk, and Aevan Dragorovich.
