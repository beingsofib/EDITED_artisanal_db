Innkeepers Society

Organization
nameInnkeepers Society
typeGuild
baseBest Harps Hall, Emerald Ward, Calimport

The Innkeepers Society was a guild in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR. Needless to say, its members were those who managed inns.

They had their headquarters in Best Harps Hall, a quality five-story building located in the heart of Maz'ahl Drudach, Marekh Sabban, in the city's Emerald Ward.
