The Whip of Flames was an adventuring company from the city of Kelazzan in Ulgarth, active in the mid–14 century DR.

Organization

Like most powerful individuals in the region, members of The Whip of Flames were all-female or mostly female. The adventuring companies in the region were filled with dramatic schemes, plots, and feuds amongst the groups, and The Whip of Flames was no exception, feuding with the Ladies of Renown of Myrmyr.

History

In the mid–14 century DR, The Whip of Flames sent their agents to the lands north and west from the Shining South in search of new magics to give them the upper hand against other adventuring bands. The Whips traveled to Thay, Rashemen, Waterdeep, the ruins of Myth Drannor, and Zhentil Keep, among other locations.
