Thond

Organization
nameThond
baseArabel, Cormyr

Thond was a merchant house of Cormyr that had a seat in Arabel.

Activities

Though it was a quiet and almost reclusive house, the house of Thond was known for their fine jewelry and was one of the most influential merchant families in Cormyr.
