The Fallen Justice was a group of spellthieves in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

The organization fought old foes of Ramman, such as followers of Gilgeam and Hoar.

Structure

The leader of the organization was Erilon Amadar.

History

All the members of the Fallen Justice were originally scouts who became drenched in the blood of Ramman during his killing in the Time of Troubles in 1358 DR, gaining spellthief abilities. The organization remained operative until at least .
