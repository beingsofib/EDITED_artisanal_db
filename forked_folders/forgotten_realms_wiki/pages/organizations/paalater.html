Paalater was a noble family of Amn that held the Baron and Baroness titles as of the late 14 century DR.

History

As of the , the Paalater family included at least three members: Baroness Paalater, her husband, and their daughter, young debutante Alicia Paalater. The young Paalater woman was enamored with Count Claylan to disdain of Lady Lasalla, who experienced Claylan's amorous ways of using naive youths.
