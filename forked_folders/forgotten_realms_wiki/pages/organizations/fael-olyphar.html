Fael Olyphar was one of the common drow families of Menzoberranzan in the Northdark in the late 14 and 15 centuries DR.

History

The Fael Olyphar family was founded sometime before the Spellplague years of the late 14 and 15 centuries DR. Their estate stood in the Eastmyr neighborhood of Menzoberranzan, amongst other less successful drow families: Asbodela, Dlaen Dei'Amatar, Ilystryph, Uluar, and Zalyzryn.
