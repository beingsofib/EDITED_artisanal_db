The Striking Talons of Memnon was an adventuring band from Calimshan, active in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

By the , the Striking Talons of Memnon were believed to had been dead for a decade, perished in the Weldath forest of Tethyr. The band's name, however, was remembered thanks to stories of the accumulated treasures buried nearby the Hunter's Tree. Along with another group – Fallowgar's Furies's treasures, it was believed that the Striking Talons buried some elven magic items, coins, and gems the adventurers collected as they adventured in the Forest of Tethir.
