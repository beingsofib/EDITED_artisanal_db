Order of the Watchful Lion

Organization
nameOrder of the Watchful Lion
baseMulhorand, East Faerûn
favored deityAnhur

The Order of the Watchful Lion was an order of crusaders dedicated to Anhur. This organization served as Mulhorand's first line of defense. Its members often scout neighboring regions and report important events, areas, and people to their superiors.
