Cobblers' Society

The Cobblers' Society was a guild in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR. Its members made and repaired shoes, boots, slippers, and such.

They had their headquarters in The Dao's Step, a mudbrick rowhouse located on the western edge of Wahir Drudach, Axash Sabban, in the city's Emerald Ward.
