The Knights of the Forked Tongue was an elite military order in service of the Cathedral of Emerald Scales in Hlondeth, the Vilhon Reach.

Organization

The Knights of the Forked Tongue were crusaders who guarded the Cathedral of Emerald Scales and its clergy. The members of the order dedicated their lives to the serpentine deity Varae, an aspect of Sseth.
