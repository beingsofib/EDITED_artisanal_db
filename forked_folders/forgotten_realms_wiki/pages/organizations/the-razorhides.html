The Razorhides were a gang of street thugs in Veltalar during the late 14 century DR.

Organization

The leader of the Razorhides was Dherk.

Activities

The Razorhides traded in traveler's dust. They were known to steal, and were rumored to kill freelance thieves, cutting out their limbs and spreading them around the docks.

Base of Operations

The Razorhides made their base of operations at an abandoned brewery in Veltalar's wharf.

History

The warlock Japheth Donard strong-armed him into obedience in , becoming their leader for a brief time, during which he demanded a thirty percent cut of all their takings.
