Headless Serpent, Secret Venom was a gang from the port town of Nyanzaru on the Chultan Peninsula, southwest Faerûn, active sometime before the late 15 century DR.

History

Headless Serpent, Secret Venom was opposed by several monks, including Kimigha Poji, Roahton Mobar, and Strigga Morkoscavich, some years before the . The monks wore magical garbs known as The Graceful Cloth, The Mighty Cloth, and vest of soul rejuvenation, making them a serious threat to the Port Nyanzaru gang.
