Valwater

Organization
nameValwater
typeNoble family
baseVast Swamp, Cormyr

House Valwater was one of the noble families of Cormyr. It had an estate in the Vast Swamp, along the Way of the Manticore. It had a bad reputation due to one of its members trying to poison King Foril Obarskyr I.

Members

  • Lady Mersha Valwater
  • Jerrick Valwater, Lady Mersha's eldest son. Jerrick served a prison sentence in Wheloon for the attempted assassination of King Foril. He was the founder of the Lady's Marauders gang who controlled part of the town.
  • Lord Delshan Valwater, Lady Mersha's youngest son.
