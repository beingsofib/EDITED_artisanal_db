The Snobeedles were a family of halflings who owned and ran the Snobeedle Orchard and Meadery in Undercliff just outside of Waterdeep. By , they were Waterdeep's richest halfling family.

Members

  • Blossom Snobeedle, an old druid.
  • Dasher Snobeedle, a member who ran away to join a gang of halfling wererats called the Shard Shunners.
