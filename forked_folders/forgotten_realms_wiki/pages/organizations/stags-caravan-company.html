The Stags Caravan Company was a merchant company based in Scornubelin the 14 century DR.

Organization

The Stag was led by the trail-lord Black Tomm Bharhinn but effective control of the day-to-day operations gradually fell into the hands of three young Amnian merchants Storm, Hlevell, and Dindar Pel in the late 14 century DR.

History

Originally the Stag were an adventuring company but in time the original adventurer members were replaced by new merchant members and when all the original members were dead or left for other adventures, the Stag became a full merchant company.
