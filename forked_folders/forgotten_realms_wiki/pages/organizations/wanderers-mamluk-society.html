The Wanderers Mamluk Society, also known as the Wanderers, was a military organization in Zakhara.

Activities

The Wanderers were the only naval mamluk organization in Zakhara.

Organization

The Wanderers were led by Admiral Dus Abd al-Dawwar.

Base of Operations

Only a few supporters of this organization were found in Qudra.
