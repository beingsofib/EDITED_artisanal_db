Maquar

Organization
nameMaquar
baseEstagund, Shining South
leaderThe Rajah of Estagund

The Maquar were the elite warriors of Estagund who served the rajah as the most influential organization of the nation. They were valued for their dedication in a variety of areas from civil service, political and military council and domestic peacekeeping.
