The Dancing Dwarves was a religious organization in Zakhara whose members followed the teachings of Kor, the Zakharan god of wisdom.

The symbol and favored weapon of the Dancing Dwarves was a battle axe.

Activities

Members of the Dancing Dwarves tended to be extremely conservative.

Members

As of 1367 DR, the Dancing Dwarves had 200 members. The vast majority were dwarves, but membership was not limited to the stout folk.

All members of the Dancing Dwarves were dervishes.

Base of Operations

The Dancing Dwarves were based in Al-Akara Mountains near Huzuz.
