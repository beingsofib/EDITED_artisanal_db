The Stone-Cold Reavers were a group of reckless adventurers led by Syleen Wintermoon in the Sword Coast North in the late 15 century DR. When there were suitable jobs, they made their living as sellswords. However, when honest work was unavailable, they turned to criminal activities, resorting to brigandry and thievery.

History

The group tried to steal the presumed treasure hoard of the young white dragon Cryovain from its lair, Icespire Hold. They waited for the dragon to leave its lair so they could raid it in its absence. However, Cryovain hadn't amassed any treasure yet.
