The Society of the Jade Empress was a secret society in Shou Lung.

Description

The Society of the Jade Empress planned to reestablish the Hai dynasty on throne. For this, they wanted to kill all of the Chin dynasty imperial family, a very hard task. They were based in Hai Yuan.

History

The Society of the Jade Empress had been planning their assassination plot for a long time. They gathered allies in most of the monasteries of Hai Yuan and killed many of the lowest-ranking local Shou bureaucrats.
