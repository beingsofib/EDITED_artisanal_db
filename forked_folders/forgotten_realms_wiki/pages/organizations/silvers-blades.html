Silver's Blades was a band of eladrin and fey pirates operating out of the ruins of Caer Corwell's docks in the late 15 century DR. They were led by Captain Silver Silwyrt.

Relationships

They operated alongside many unseelie fey who had overrun the ruins of Corwell. Among them, the Blades had formed a tenuous alliance with Phillwyp the Wary, a twisted gnome operating out of the Caer Corwell castle.

History

As of , Silwyrt had turned the pirates toward the slave trade: they would capture fey and mortal creatures alike to sell to the fomorians of Oman's Isle.
