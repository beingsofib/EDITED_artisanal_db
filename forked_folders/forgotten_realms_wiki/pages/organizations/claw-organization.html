The Claw were a mercenary company based in Evermoors.

Tactics

Usually, the Claw had no true organization, with basic tactics but their regenerative abilities proved to be very effective.

Contracts

They charged 1000 gold pieces per month for their services.

Leadership

The Claw was a company of trolls directed by four human wizards directed by four mind flayers under the leadership of the alhoon Killithor, who ruled as a dictator.
