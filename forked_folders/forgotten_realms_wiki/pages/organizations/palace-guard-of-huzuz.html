The Palace Guard of Huzuz served as within the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Organization

All members of the palace guard were large individuals who possessed not only intimidating physical strength and presence, but enough intelligence and eloquence to resort to politely addressing visitors. They also possessed an eye for spotting potential trouble or anything that was out of place.

There were 1,000 members of the guard.

Base of Operations

The palace guard was active within the royal palace of Huzuz.

Members

Prince Cheddah al-Assad was the ultimate commander of the palace guard.
