Kraliqh

Organization
nameKraliqh
baseArabel, Cormyr

The Kraliqh family was a merchant house based in Arabel in Cormyr in the mid–14th century DR.

Base

The House of Kaliqh stood by the southwest wall of Arabel. They had a warehouse near the center of the city.

Activities

The Kraligh family made their income from real estate.
