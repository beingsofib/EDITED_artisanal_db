The Kindly Order of Silversmiths was a guild in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR. It comprised craftsmen who worked with silver.

They had their headquarters in Seven Chalices Trading House, a quality four-story building located on the edge of Farix Drudach, Oathl Sabban, in the city's Emerald Ward.

Notable Members

  • Janar el Vyrsatyr, third highest-ranked member c. 1370 DR
