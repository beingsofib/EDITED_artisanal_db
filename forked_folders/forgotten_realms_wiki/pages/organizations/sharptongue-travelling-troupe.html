Sharptongue Travelling Troupe

nameSharptongue Travelling Troupe

baseWestern Heartlands, West Faerûn

The Sharptongue Travelling Troupe were a bardic troupe that performed in theaters around the Western Heartlands.

Activities

The Sharptongue Travelling Troupe were known for being the actors of The Folly and the Fall.

Rumors & Legends

The troupe, along with thaumaturges, were said to have sent Elturel's Everlight Theatre into chaos.
