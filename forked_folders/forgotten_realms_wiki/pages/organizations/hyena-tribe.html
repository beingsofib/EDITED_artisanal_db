Hyena Tribe

Organization
nameHyena Tribe
baseEastern Shaar, south Faerûn
racesHuman

The Hyena Tribe were a roving group of hunters who were found in the Eastern Shaar, whose territory lay between the Riftwood and Sharawood forests, south of the Council Hills.

Like hyenas, they specialized in hiding and using tripping tactics against their enemies.
