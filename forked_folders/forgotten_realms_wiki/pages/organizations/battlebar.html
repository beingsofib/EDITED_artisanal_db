Battlebar

Organization
nameBattlebar
typeNoble family
baseNorthbank, Cormyr

Battlebar was a noble house of Cormyr in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Base

The family held an estate in the Northbank region of Cormyr.

Members

  • Lord Belophar Battlebar, head of the house c. 1368 DR.
  • Lady Chalass Battlebar, daughter of Lord Belophar.
