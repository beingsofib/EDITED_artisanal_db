Shield of Iriaebor

Organization
nameShield of Iriaebor
baseIriaebor, Western Heartlands

The Shield of Iriaebor was the name of the armed forces of Iriaebor in the mid–14 century DR. They acted to both police the city and patrol the surrounding lands.
