The Hand (organization)

Organization
nameThe Hand
baseVoonlar, Moonsea
favored deityBane
alignmentEvil

The Hand was an evil adventuring group based in Voonlar. They had at least one encounter with the Knights of Myth Drannor during the latter part of Doust Sulwood's time as the Lord of Shadowdale (1350 DR–1353 DR).
