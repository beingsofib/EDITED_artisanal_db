Tribe Quintax

Organization
nameQuintax
typeTribe
baseVeldergauttland, the Whamite Isles

Tribe Quintax was a seafaring tribe from Veldergauttland in the Whamite Isles. They represented Veldergauttland in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles
