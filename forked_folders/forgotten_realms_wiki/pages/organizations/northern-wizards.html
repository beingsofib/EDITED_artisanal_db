The Northern Wizards were one of the two spellcasting organizations in Unther.

Organizations

They were a group of wizards that considered themselves the protectors of the common people of Messemprar.

They mostly worshiped Mystra and Ishtar.

History

The Northern Wizards had always protected Messemprar and its people.

When a revolt started in 1357 DR, they found that their main enemy was the god-king Gilgeam's army. However, they tried to negotiate with the king.

After the Godswar and Mulhorand's invasion, the Northern Wizards were the de facto rulers of the remaining free Unther by 1373 DR.
