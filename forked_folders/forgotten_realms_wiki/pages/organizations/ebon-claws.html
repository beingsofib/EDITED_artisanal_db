The Ebon Claws was an organization that attempted to wrestle control of Westgate's criminal underbelly in the late 14 century DR.

Base of Operations

According to a mad beggar named Salty Jack, the Ebon Claws laired in the Sewers of Westgate.

History

The Ebon Claws seemingly appeared out of nowhere in the and started a guild war, giving a pretty good account of themselves. The infamous Night Masks quickly became the Ebon Claws' foes and the streets of Westgate began erupting into violence at night. The gang's alleged leader was Altama, a female wererat, however, the gang's true leader was a vampire named Tasheni.
