The red triumvirate was a cabal of red dragons during the Time of Dragons.

Territory

They possessed lands to the south-east of the territory of King Orchtrien.

Relationships

The red cabal was a foe of King Orchtrien.

Members

The red triumvirate was almost certainly made up of red dragons.

History

In , the red triumvirate lost a significant battle to the forces of King Orchtrien.
