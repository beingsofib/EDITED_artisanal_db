The Mist of Shadows was an elite elven band of rangers that roamed the great forest of Cormanthor sometime before the mid-14 century DR.

History

The Mist of Shadows was active before the , protecting the ancestral forest from outlaws and dangerous monsters. The group's second in command was Indeera Lakhan who wore a set of magic armor, later named in her honor.
