Paladins of Velen was a legendary paladin order, believed to be extinct by the late 15 century DR.

History

By the , Paladins of Velen were so old that most stories of them were thought to be just legends and hearsay. They left little behind, except for the enchanted shields of devotion that still bore their powerful divine blessings.
