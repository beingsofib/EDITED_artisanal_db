The Sisters and Brothers of the Ruby Rose were an order of paladins dedicated to the deity Sune, active during the 14 century DR.

Activities

These paladins of Lady Firehair sought to defend beauty and destroy those evil beings of Faerûn that were exceptionally repulsive in their deeds. They were particularly adept at destroying the undead.
