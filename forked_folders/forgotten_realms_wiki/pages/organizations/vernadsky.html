The Vernadsky family was a Raumviran noble family who inhabited the city of Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Base of Operations

The Vernadskys occupied the Vernadsky Fief—a large walled compound that was located almost directly in the center of Almorel along the Golden Way. Much of the estate bordered the promenade that led to Mechnik Nikitsky's residence.
