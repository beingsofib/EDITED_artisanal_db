The Watch-blades were the city guard of Yhaunn in 1358 DR.

Activities

The Watch-blades patrolled the streets of Yhaunn and maintained the general order of the city. They also watched the waters of the Dragon Reach near the city by using Sembian naval detachments.

Organization

Approximately 3,000 individuals comprised the Watch-blades. All wore plate mail and were armed with an assortment of weapons including halberds, maces, long swords and daggers.
