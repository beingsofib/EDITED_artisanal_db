The Red Thunder Mercenary Corps was a centaur mercenary company based in the Hordelands.

History

The Red Thunder Mercenary Corps was formed by the centaurs from the Chondalwood after they participated in the Battle of Five Crowns in the war against the arcanaloth Yrkhetep.

Later they fought in the Shaar, Vilhon Reach and Bloodstone Lands, recruiting more members. After the Horde Wars they established themselves in the Hordelands, where they recruited a local centaur tribe.

Organization

Tactics

The Red Thunder were three units of 50 archers.

Contracts

The Red Thunder charged 150 gold pieces per week for their services. they worked primarily as caravan guards.
