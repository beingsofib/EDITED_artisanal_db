Clan Tlassian was one of the dragonborn clans of the nation of Tymanther.

Description

Members of Clan Tlassian used their distinctive clan piercings on their left check.

History

Clan Tlassian was a small clan in 1479 DR. The clan gained status in Thymari society when Tarhun became the Vanquisher, as the Tlassians offered to him their staunch military support. As a reward, Tarhun gave to them the lands to the east of Djerad Thymar,

Aside from their role as defenders of the lands located to the east of Djerad Thymar, the Tlassians were known as one of the farming clans of Tymanther. They also specialized in metalwork, and were specially renowned for the quality of their armorcraft.

They were traditional rivals of Clan Vkriss.

During the Second Sundering, most of the Tlassian farms may have been lost when Abeir and Toril exchanged lands.

Known members

  • Tlassian Telina Silberhaar, the matriarch of the clan in 1479 DR.
  • Tlassian Talmar Eldwing, elder of the clan and second-in-command to Telina in 1479 DR.
  • Tlassian Antar Ludovic, the chief smith of the clan in 1479 DR.
  • Tlassian Trank Eldwing, a young smith of the clan in 1479 DR.
  • Tearn, a skilled warlock and a former member of the clan in 1479 DR.
