Mestel was an old and powerful merchant organization and a noble family from the city of Arrabar in the Vilhon Reach

Organization

House Mestel was one of the oldest and most powerful merchant families of Arrabar. The oldest families held nine-tenth of the city's wealth while the younger merchant houses, such as House Matrell, fought for the one-tenth share of the market. The estate of the Mestel family was a sprawling high-spired mansion surrounded by a grove of suth trees planted along the estate's perimeter. Like all other mercantile houses of Arrabar, Mestels housed a sizable mercenary and guard force.

History

Obiron Matrell was born in the to one of the lordlings of the great Mestel family of Arrabar. Mestels denounced the distant-born bastard and left him no choice but to change his name and establish his own merchant organization – House Matrell. Even after Obiron, along with his wife Hetta, built a small merchant empire, House Matrell was still looked down upon by Mestels.

In the , Falagh Mestel became involved in a plot against House Matrell.
