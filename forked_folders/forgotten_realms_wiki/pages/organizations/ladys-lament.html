The Lady's Lament was an adventuring troupe from the City of Splendors, Waterdeep, active circa 1310 DR.

Activities

The Lady's Lament was known for being a group of quiet, soft-spoken, and somewhat melancholic adventurers. They built a reputation of stepping up to help those in need and not being afraid of a challenge, even dangerous tasks other adventuring troupes declined.

History

The Lady's Lament was established several years before the . The group's first member and leader was Sheris Lyricist, a rich, spoiled, and willful daughter of a Waterdhevian noble house. When faced with a prospect of an arranged marriage organized by her father, Sheris ran away from home and took on a mantle of a bard. Later, she was joined by a half-orcish couple who were not accepted by either humans nor orcs. Geldin and Khatja became the company's muscle. The adventurers later were joined by Laeris, a quiet cleric of Ilmater, a drow sorceress Vraedna who abandoned worship of Lolth and wanted to build a reputation of being a good dark elf, and lastly, a cheerful hin rogue Kylie.

In 1310 DR, the Lady's Lament, along with many other mercenaries, traveled from Luskan to the besieged city of Targos in the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale.
