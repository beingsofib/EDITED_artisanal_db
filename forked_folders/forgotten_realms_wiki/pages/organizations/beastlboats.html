Beastlboats

Organization
nameBeastlboats
typeFleet
baseLand of the Beastly Brigands, Whamite Isles

The Beastlboats was a fleet of ships from the Land of the Beastly Brigands in the Whamite Isles. The Beastlboats represented the Beastly Brigands in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles
