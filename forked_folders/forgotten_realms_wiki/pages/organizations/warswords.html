The Warswords of Impiltur were a militia army that regularly patrolled the nation and defended the borders it shared with the eastern realms of Faerûn.

Activities

The Warswords often bolstered their numbers with groups of hired adventurers, called "swordpoints", who were granted additional freedoms when operating under the "King's Code".

Organization

A typical Warsword patrol consisted of twenty mounted soldiers, clad in chainmail and armed with lances and crossbows.

Notable Members

  • Haelimbrar: War-Captain and councilor of the Lords of Imphras II
  • Simgar: Another member of the Council of Lords, Simgar spent much of his time leading Warsword expeditions
