Starfall was an archaic noble elven house from the Time of Dragons.

Territory

The Starfall territory coincided with the heart of King Orchtrien's realm.

Relationships

The Starfall were vassals of Orchtrien, but they were not very loyal to him.

History

In , the house of Starfall set an ambush together with the Vilirith and Duskmere against some of Orchtrien's forces in a forest within the heart of his own kingdom. They nearly defeated the arriving force, captained by Rhespen Ash, an elven wizard; however, Prince Bexendral teleported into the battle and struck the traitors down before Rhespen's surrender.

After the rebellion was quashed, the heirs of the House were then taken to Dawnfire to learn and to serve as hostages, which would ensure the House remained on good behavior.
