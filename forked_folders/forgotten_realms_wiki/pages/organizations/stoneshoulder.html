The Stoneshoulders were a dwarf clan of northwest Faerûn and in the city of Iltkazar in Shanatar.

Members

  • Iirikos Stoneshoulder, a famed artisan and architect of Ammarindar around the 2 century DR.
  • Bettargh Stoneshoulder, high priest of Dugmaren Brightmantle in Iltkazar in the late 1300s DR.
  • Dunnabar Stoneshoulder, high priest of Gorm Gulthyn in Iltkazar in the late 1300s DR.

Bases

  • The Hall of Stoneshoulder, a guild hall in Iltkazar dedicated to martial training.
