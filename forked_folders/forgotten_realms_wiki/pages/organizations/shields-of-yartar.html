The Shields of Yartar was a group of mounted soldiers that defended the city of Yartar.

Organization

The Waterbaron of Yartar served as the Shields' highest-ranking commander. They were served by a number of officers known as Overswords.

Activities

In defense of the city, the Shield was often forced to drive off monstrous raiders, such as bands of orcs and groups of trolls.

Base of Operations

The group was headquartered in the Shield Tower located just west of the city proper, across the fortified bridge that spanned the River Surbrin.

History

By the , the ranks of the Shields had been infiltrated by agents of the Kraken Society.
