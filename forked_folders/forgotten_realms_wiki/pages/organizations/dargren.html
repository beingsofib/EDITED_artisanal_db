The Dargren family was a noble clan of Tethyr.

Organization

The head of the family held the title of Count, and control over the family's finances.

Possessions

Some of the Count's possessions included estates a few miles away from Zazesspur.

History

In , an individual claiming to be Count Kelmar Dargren, who had inherited the title after the death of his brother Lukan Dargren, sought the help of his sorcerous aunt in slaying a spree killer; he kept secret the fact he knew the murderer was actually the fang dragon Xexillidaulgrymm.
