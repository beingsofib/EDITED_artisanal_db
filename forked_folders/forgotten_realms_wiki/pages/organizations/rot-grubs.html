The Rot Grubs were a criminal organization within Ordulin as of the .

Activities

The Rot Grubs viewed themselves as revolutionaries who performed all kinds of criminal acts in the name of the "true king" of Sembia, though they certainly seemed far more interested in illegally acquiring wealth for themselves.

These criminals were secretly backed by the Zhentarim in order to promote civil unrest in the area.
