The Guild of Fishmongers and Fishermen was a guild based in the town of Tulmene in Tethyr in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. It serviced those who caught and traded fish.

Base of Operations

Their guildhouse loomed over the town's eastern docks. Naturally, its lower floor was fragrant with fish oils.

Members

The guildmaster was Tormos Ollday, a respectable merchant who'd brought prosperity to this guild for three decades by 1370 DR. Much of the guild were hardworking and honest folk.
