The Openers

Organization
nameThe Openers
baseWestern Heartlands

The Openers was a secretive band of individuals in the Western Heartlands in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

The Openers dedicated themselves to discovering, mapping, and unlocking the secrets of the ancient Netherese gates that were believed to be both underneath and nearby the village of Hardbuckler.
