Nymara Scheiron, more commonly known as Kitten, was a Masked Lord of Waterdeep, circa the . Few people knew her full name.

Description

Kitten had brown hair, usually unkempt, and a full, strong figure. She was fond of dressing up in rather revealing outfits, decorated in makeup and scented with strong perfumes.

Personality

She was known for her pragmatic disposition but particularly fierce temper. She refused any gift of wealthy or charitable donation offered to her by others.

Possessions

She owned a very useful ring of invisibility alongside an enchanted dagger, and leather armor.

Activities

Kitten often posed as a tavern dancer in the Dock Ward. She was quite skilled at stealthily stalking other individuals through the streets of Waterdeep, and was quite knowledgeable about both the city's skyline and its sewer system.

History

During her adventuring days, Kitten was known to have survived a few delves into the House of Stone. At some point she even fought one-on-one against the son of the god Bane, Iyachtu Xvim.

She was recruited to serve as Masked Lord by Mirt and Durnan in the 1340s DR.

Residence

Kitten rarely stayed in one place. She had a number of residences around the city.

Relationships

Kitten was one of the "Like-Minded Lords" as of the , along with Mirt, Khelben, Durnan, Larissa, and Brian the "Swordcaster", who frequently met up to enjoy each other's company.
