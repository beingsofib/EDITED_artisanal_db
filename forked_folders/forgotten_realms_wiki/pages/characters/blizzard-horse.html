Blizzard (horse)

Blizzard was a mare owned by Brianna Burdun.

Description

Blizzard had a black coat with white flecks and a tail that was snow white.

History

In 1366 DR, while stabled at the Weary Giant he was badly startled by Earl Dobbin as he tried to search her stable for a missing thief and ended up badly bruising him.
