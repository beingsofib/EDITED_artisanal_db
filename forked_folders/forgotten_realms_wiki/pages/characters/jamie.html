Jamie was the son of Elizabeth Clawthorn, an inhabitant of the town of Ghars in Cormyr in the mid–14 century DR.

History

When Jasper met with the elderly Elizabeth in 1367 DR, she mistook him for Jamie. Though Jasper did not know who or where Jamie was, he played the role somewhat for her benefit. Liz recalled Jamie was a good son, and as a boy he swung on a rope hanging from a tree his father later had to cut down.
