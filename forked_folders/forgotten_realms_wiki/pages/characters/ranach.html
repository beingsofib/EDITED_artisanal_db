Ranach was a human cleric of an unknown god in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

A hard-working man that always tried his best, he was however plagued by misfortune.

Personality

Ranach was a loyal yet gullible man. He wasn't the most intelligent and as such had been tricked several times by those who harbored ill will to reveal secrets he held or even go against his own faith. Unknown to Ranach, his fellow priests would always keep an eye on him.

Possessions

He wore padded leather armor when out and about, wielded a staff of command and bore a ring of sustenance.
