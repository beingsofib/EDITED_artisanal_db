Olmer Twiggins was a local halfling and customer of the Lonesome Tankard Inn and Tavern of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Olmer was a halfling-sized fellow with ruddy skin and short brown hair.

History

In the 1480s DR, around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar and Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, Olmer spent time at the Tankard with a tressym, Zilar Fenner, and Alyn Farrago, who told stories of the Haunted Halls of Eveningstar to his friends.
