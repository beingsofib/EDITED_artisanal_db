Valshee of the Black Blade was was a notorious pirate who plagues the Sword Coast in the late 13 century DR.

History

As of the , Valshee was already a feared menace in the Sea of Swords. She became the target of Gholdaunt's – a pirate hunter from Tashluta. He sent an open letter to the port cities of the Sword Coast announcing his quest to end Valshee's life.
