Brog was an orc warrior from Many-Arrows who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 century DR.

Activities

Brog was a regular of the Fallen Tower tavern. As of 1479 DR, he was the champion of the tavern's arm-wrestling and drinking contests. Brog was the only known orc able to withstand a full tankard of Vagdru One-Ear's homemade beer.
