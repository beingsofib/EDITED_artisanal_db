Zedir was a gnome wizard and student of the Neverwintran Adventurer's Academy in the late 14 century DR.

History

In the , Zedir studied under the instructor and a fellow gnome mage Jaroo, who readily assisted anyone facing difficulties in their pursuit of ancient wisdom. Zedir was diligent and committed student, yet her life was tragically cut short in the attack on the Academy that took lives of most of its students and instructors.
