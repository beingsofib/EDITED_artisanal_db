Cyrel was a wizard and a trader that operated out of a tollhouse situated along the Risen Road within the Sword Coast region during the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

She wore simple robes and carried with her a mundane quarterstaff, but offered a number of valuable items for sale or trade.

Relationships

For a time, she traveled alongside the fallen paladin of Tyr Anders, the rogue Rurik, and the ranger Trynn.

History

Cyrel and her fellows were present at the Risen Road tollhouse when it was assaulted by gnoll raiders loyal to the cult of the Absolute in the . The group managed to fight off the savage beast-men, but could not save the lives of some innocent bystanders.
