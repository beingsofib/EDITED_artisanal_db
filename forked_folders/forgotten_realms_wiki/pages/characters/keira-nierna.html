Keira Nierna was a human bard and wandering minstrel sometime in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

She had voluminous flamed-tressed hair that spilled down her shoulders.

Personality

Keira was the more serious and introverted sibling compared to her identical twin sister Kaele. She was fascinated by magic, and used it confuse those around her.

Possessions

She wore a set of leather armor and had a mandolin, longsword, and staff sling.

Relationships

She worked alongside her twin sister as wandering minstrels.

History

Once Keira and her sister played for the wizard Daltim Flamefist, and managed to dazzle him so well he, afterwards, would refer to them as 'the sirens'.
