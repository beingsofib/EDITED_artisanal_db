Gauntlet Dain was a solider of the Flaming Fist of that served during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

She was a determined individual who sprung into action during emergencies.

Possessions

Her preferred weapon was a flanged mace.

History

Dain was present at the Waukeen's Rest inn when it was set ablaze by cultists of the Absolute, the . She tried to help clear rubble away, to find a way past the flaming wreckage, but was largely unsuccessful.
