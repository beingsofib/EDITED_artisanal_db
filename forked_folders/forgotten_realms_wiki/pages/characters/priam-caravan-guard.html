Priam (caravan guard)

Person
namePriam
sexMale
raceHuman
occupationGuard

Priam was a caravan guard. He was a lanky man with a fierce trap of a mouth, admired for his courage. when he discovered a Flight of Dragons. Though frightened, he kept his wits about himself, and led the caravan to hide in the tall grass. Though most of the flight passed it by, they were then attacked by a hungry red dragon. Janx suggested they scatter, but Priam talked him out of it: both were slain by the dragon, which then killed the rest of the caravan. The only survivor was a badly maimed, 9-year old Dorn Graybrook.
