Hanaleisa Bonaduce was the daughter of Cadderly Bonaduce and Danica Maupoissant and twin sister of Temberle in the late 14 century DR.

Abilities

She and her siblings were trained by their parents in the ways of priesthood, martial arts, and sorcery. Hanaleisa followed her mother's footsteps and preferred her monk prowess.

History

In the , on a trip to Carradoon, the twins encountered hordes of undead sweeping the streets, which were summoned by a servant of the Ghost King.
