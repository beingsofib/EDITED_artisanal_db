Gundalin was a particularly outgoing and helpful wheelwright that lived in the village of Parnast during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

He spent his time helping locals and merchants with their carts or other means of conveyance. When he was not working, Gundalin was a regular at the Golden Tankard tavern.

History

When the Cult of the Dragon took over Parnast in the 1480s DR, Gundalin was stricken became stricken with fear. He desperately wanted the group out of his home but was too scared to take direct action. Over time however, he did learn of the two pass phrases that could be uttered to gain entry into the cult-controlled Skyreach Castle.
