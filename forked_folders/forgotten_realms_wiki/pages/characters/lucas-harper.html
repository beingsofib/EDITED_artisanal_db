Lucas (Harper)

Person
nameLucas
titlesSir
sexMale
raceMasculine
occupationHarper

Sir Lucas was a member of the Harpers and a frequent guest at Inn Plain Sight in the late 15 century DR. His heroic portrait hanged on the wall of the inn.

Personality

Sir Lucas was described as a brave Harper and a hero.
