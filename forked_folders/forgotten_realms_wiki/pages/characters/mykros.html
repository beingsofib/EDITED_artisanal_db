Mykros was the commander of the army of Arcata in Damara in 1359 DR.

Activities

Mykros was the right-hand man of Duke William Horgath. He was also in league with the Barony of Bloodstone.

Personality

Mykros was a quiet and observant man. His loyalty was directed to the kingdom as a whole. For this he supported King Gareth Dragonsbane.
