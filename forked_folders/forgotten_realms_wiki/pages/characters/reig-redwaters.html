Reig Redwaters was a member of the Targos Guard, active in the early 14 century DR.

Description

Reig was a brown-haired human man who wore the Targos Guard's leather armor and was armed with a dagger and sword.

History

In the , the town of Targos in Icewind Dale was besieged by the Legion of the Chimera. One of the Legion's surprise attacks herded a troop of goblins by magical means through underground tunnels into the Targos docks, where Reig Redwaters and his partners, Brohn and Honest Jon, stood guard. Reig and Jon survived the attack, though Reig received a slash from a goblin axe, and Brohn was killed by an arrow to the neck.

Shortly after the attack began, the Wicked Wench docked, and a group of adventuring mercenaries from Luskan arrived to aid Targos. Reig greeted the adventurers, warning them of another band of goblins nearby, and asked them to find the harbor master Magdar Shenlen‎‎ and acquire some newly-arrived healing draughts for his wound. Reig Redwaters remained guarding the docks against goblin arsonists, as its safety was crucial to the town's survival.
