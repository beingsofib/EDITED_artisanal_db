Resengar, also known as Resengar the Whitebeard, was a wizard from Waterdeep and a Masked Lord until .

Abilities

One of his cantrips created seven stars.

Relationships

He was a friend of Mirt, and the one fellow he liked the most amongst the other Masked Lords after Durnan. Resengar liked to show off his cantrips to Asper.

Possessions

His home was warded with magic. In his living room, he had at least one fur rug and a ceiling decorated with stars.

History

In , Resengar was slain by Amril Zoar just before he slew Tamaeril Bladesemmer.
