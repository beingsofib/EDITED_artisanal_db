Madarn Spiderhunter was an old gnomish trapper, merchant, and spider-hunter from Stormpemhauder in Shadowdale around the year 1369 DR.

Activities

He would trap and capture giant spiders in the Spiderhaunt Wood within Shadowdale to protect his village. Madarn was knowledgeable about herbal remedies that would negate the effects of the spiders' toxins.

Personality

Madarn was a fervent arguer and was passionate about his trapper lifestyle.
