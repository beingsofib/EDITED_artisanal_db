Zalazel Harauntan was a skilled wizard inhabiting the city of Yhep along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

As the most skilled practitioner of the Art in town, she used her skills to protect both Yhep and its valuable oyster beds. She also tutored a handful of students in the use of magic. She owned a large house in town, but actually spent most of her time within a large stilt house positioned directly over the oyster beds on the Lake of Steam.

Relationships

Zalazel was a member of Yhep's ruling family. Her most promising student was a wild tribeswoman named Yashiira.
