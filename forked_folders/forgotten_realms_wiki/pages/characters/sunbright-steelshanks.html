Sunbright Steelshanks was a Rengarth barbarian of circa , who caught the eye of the Netherese wizards Candlemas and Sysquemalyn. He was a polytheistic warrior, believing in the Earthmother, God of War and Lady Luck.

Description

The blond-haired barbarian was slender and quick with corded muscles. With his bright hair and strong legs, the barbarian was given the name of Sunbright Steelshanks by his mother Monkberry on his nameday.

Possessions

When traveling, Sunbright dressed in a worn linen shirt, and iron-studded boots, and a brown goatskin . Over his shoulders, he wore a longbow, a bedroll, and his sword, which was called Harvester of Blood.

History

During the Shadowed Age,, Steelshanks was born to Monkberry and Sevenhaunt. For many years, Sunbright lived with his parents in the Rengarth tribe called the Ravens until his father took ill and died.

Circa −700 DR, Sunbright killed his first Angardt enemy and was granted his father's greatsword, Harvester of Blood, by his mother. Envious, the Raven shaman Owldark prophesied Steelshanks would murder his people, so Sunbright fled in the night with the help of his mother.

A year later, the young fighter encountered a group of orcs within the Barren Mountains while being observed by the Netherese wizards Candlemas and Sysquemalyn.

Sunbright and Candlemas encountered a time traveling Karsus seeking their recently discovered star metal. The pair were temporarily brought to the arcanist's conclave centuries later until they, with the future half-elf thief Knucklebones, were sent back by the Fall of Netheril.

Sunbright and his group were later entangled by an orcs-elves incident related to the future path of the Rengarth tribe.
