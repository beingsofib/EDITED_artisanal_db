Dheren Ogresbane was one of the Swords of Archendale in 1368 DR.

Activities

Dheren publicly was an officer in the Archendale Army. In his Red Sword role, he appeared on very rare occasion but always pursued the army's agenda. He had started to hire adventurers to spy on the armies of Sembia and Hillsfar and to investigate the possibility of traitors inside the dale.

Personality

Dheren was always very serious in his duties, being very diligent, careful, and observant. His main goal was to train his army in order to be ready for any situation.
