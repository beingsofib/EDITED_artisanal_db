Ozgor Hyelvel was the Magister from to .

Abilities

Ozgor employed a number of black lightning spells, and could cast spells that brought down towers. He also could cast meteor swarm.

Activities

Ozgor's entire life was akin to a military campaign, where he continuously moved from fight to fight.

History

At the time he was offered the office of the Magister, Ozgor was in the process of attacking a town (destroyed by the late 14 century DR) in the Vilhon Reach, on behalf of its neighboring town's tyrant. By the turn of that year, Ozgor was slaying his employer at the moment, on behalf of one who had outbid him.

He continued to ravage and pillage as the Magister, building up stores of enchanted wares and weapons to which he returned when in need. However, his caches never contained money, for his campaigns always left him short on those.

Ozgor perished when he assaulted the keep of Velgorn Kataclath and his fellow adventurers. Velgorn employed magic to send a piece of the keep, brought down by Ozgor's spell, on a circular trajectory which struck Ozgor from behind while he was busy in a spell duel with him.
