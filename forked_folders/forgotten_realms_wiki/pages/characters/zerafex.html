Zerafex was a dangerous vampire who plagued Faerûn sometime before the mid-14 century DR.

History

Zerafex assembled a number of vampire spawns under her control at some point long before the . She attracted attention of a Waterdhavian paladin of Lathander named Ariabel. The paladin carried his enchanted blade Harrower into battle but Zerafex emerged victorious, ending Ariabel's life. Ariabel's corse and Harrower remained undisturbed for years within a Tethyrean crypt where the battle ended.
