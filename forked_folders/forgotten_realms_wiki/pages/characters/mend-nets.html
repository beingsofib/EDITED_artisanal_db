Mend-nets was a warrior of the Ice Hunters tribe that made their home on Oyaviggaton during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

He was a rather untrusting individual that held a distinct distaste for the monstrous creatures that laired within his tribe's icy island-home. He detested the kobolds that laired within the isle's cave network, loathed its community of revolting ice toads, and became completely frightened by its ravenous ice troll denizens.

History
