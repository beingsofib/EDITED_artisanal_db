Vesarius was a cornugon lieutenant in Dragonspear Castle circa the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Vesarius was a very brutal commander and believed Vooka and Vrag to be worthless inferiors.

History

In the past, Vesarius made a bargain with the ancient black dragon Maelestor Rex.

In the , Vesarius led one of the Hordes of Dragonspear, specifically the Storm, a group composed of 100 bugbears and 100 ogres, plus Maelestor Rex.
