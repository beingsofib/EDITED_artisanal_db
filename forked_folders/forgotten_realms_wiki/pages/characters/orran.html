Orran was a senior Doomguide and priest of Kelemvor who lived in Neverwinter as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

The cleric had blonde hair.

Possessions

When protecting the Neverdeath Graveyard, Orran carried a longsword and dressed in dark clothing.

Activities

As a member of the Eternal Order of Kelemvor, Orran's mission was to preserve Neverdeath Graveyard and cleanse it of undead.

Relationships

He was a contemporary and associate of Doomguide Volahk.

History

In the early goings of the events known as the Tyranny of Dragons, circa the , Orrin began investigating a group of Red Wizards that were exhuming bodies in the Neverdeath Graveyard. He worked with adventurers to clear the holy graveyards of undead abominations and cleanse the lands by means of Kelemvor's influence.
