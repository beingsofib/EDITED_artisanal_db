Isma Lecere was a corporal in the Thayan military in the late 15 century DR. She was the leader of an elite unit known as the Sunmasters.

Abilities

Lecere was a warlock who wielded radiant magic.

History

As of the , Lecere and her Sunmasters were stationed in Surcross to serve as a strike force against the Shadovar during the Shadovar–Thay War.
