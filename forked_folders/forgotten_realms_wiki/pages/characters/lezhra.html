Lezhra was a priestess of Lolth and the rival of Azurna in the mid-14 century DR.

History

In 1370 DR, Lezhra and Azurna headed the divine quest on behest of Lolth - the recovery of the fabled Flame Sword of Lolth, and the stone spider statue. Both items were powerful artifacts from the War of the Seldarine and wielded by the spider goddess. Upon her defeat, the Flame Sword was locked away by the gods and hidden within Undermountain, and the spider stature was broken and hidden by Halaster Blackcloak.

Lezhra and Azurna fierce fought for the favor of the goddess, with Azurna, eventually, winning.
