Buckweather, also known as Sprite-Heels, was a halfling thief and Lord of the Watch of Ankhapur on the Lake of Steam in the late 14 century DR

Personality

Sprite-Heels loved ale and cheese, and he liked his comforts. He was a very good gambler. However, Sprite-Heels often spoke before thinking, being an impulsive individual. Because of this, he often sniped at and insulted all members of the gang.

History

Sprite-Heels was one of the thief companions of Pinch in Elturel. He followed his boss back to Ankhapur and helped him to regain his body from the lich Manferic III. However, he was blinded by the lich in the fight.

Once Pinch became King Janol I, he appointed Sprite-Heels as Lord Buckweather.
