The Golden Master was the title of the mysterious leader of the Nine Golden Swords criminal secret society based in Phsant in Thesk in the mid–14 century DR. Around 1372 DR, the Golden Master was thought to be a male human rogue and sorcerer.

Supposedly, the Golden Master could call on hundreds of people if needed and was building resources to extend his reach across the city by the early 1370s DR.
