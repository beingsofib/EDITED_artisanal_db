Fiona was the mother of Ayana, who lived in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

Ayana died in the , and her body was buried in the Lower City Graveyard. Fiona, the child's mother, could not thank the mortician Valmorba for going above and beyond for the funeral in the letter, sending the Lower City Mortuary gold for the coffin and for Valmorba to burry the girl's beloved teddy bear with her.
