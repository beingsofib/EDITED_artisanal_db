The savior of the Lannon family was an individual of unknown gender and race in the late 14 century DR who probably helped Pitney Lannon and his family get rid of lizardfolk intruders from their home in the swamp near the Mere of Dead Men on the Sword Coast North. If so, Pitney granted them the family's reptile bane cudgel.
