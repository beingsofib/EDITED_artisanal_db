Thwitle Phaslebum was a halfling scout and adventurer who gallivanted along with his friend Vexter Master of Illusion.

Description

He had black curly hair.

Personality

Once a shy halfling and quite the homebody, he began to change once he met and befriended Vexter. After becoming an adventurer, he enjoyed hitting unaware foes with shots from his sling.

Possessions

He wore a set of enchanted +3 leather armor, and wielded a enchanted +4 short sword alongside his ordinary sling and dagger. He also owned a bag of holding.

Relationships

Vexter Master of Illusion, befriended Thwitle and took him along on his plane skipping travels.
