Tralia Falrith was a Crinti living in Ammathtar in northeast Dambrath during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

She was a third cousin of Queen Yenandra.

Activities

Tralia served as Duchess of the North for Dambrath. Though she was sixth in the line of succession, Tralia still had designs on the throne, including assassination attempts on the other duchesses.
