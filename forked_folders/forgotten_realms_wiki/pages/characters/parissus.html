Parissus was a mercenary and bounty hunter from Impiltur active in the Bloodstone Lands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Parissus had a large-boned and round face and short, light-colored hair.

History

Parissus was companion and close friend of the half-elf warrior Calihye.
