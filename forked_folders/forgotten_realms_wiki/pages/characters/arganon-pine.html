Arganon Pine was a human Cormyrean Wizard of War in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Arganon had light skin and back-swept red hair.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, Arganon joined the Cormyrean forces at Eveningstar as they fought back against the drow and their allies. In the midst of this, a forest outpost that sat upon a magical locus was destroyed by the drow, and was then taken over by werewolf bandits. In response, Arganon took a team of Purple Dragons and adventurers (hired by Yenward Chorster) to seize the site and performed a magical ritual on the spot to protect the valley from creatures like those malevolent werewolves. In the process of creating the spell ward, the bandits launched a fierce assault, including the fearsome Claive the Carver and Bladeback, but Arganon's efforts succeeded in the end.
