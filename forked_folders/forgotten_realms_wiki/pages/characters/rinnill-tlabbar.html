Rinnill Tlabbar was the house patron of House Faen Tlabbar, the fourth house of Menzoberranzan.

History

Around 1358 DR, Rinnill hailed from a destroyed House and was chosen by Matron Ghenni'tiroth Tlabbar to be her house patron.
