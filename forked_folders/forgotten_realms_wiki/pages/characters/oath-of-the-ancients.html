Oath of the Ancients

The Oath of the Ancients was one of the sacred vows that paladins could choose to take when they reached a milestone in their journey of faith. When they dedicated themselves to the tenets of this oath, they also were granted access to a set of spells.

Abilities

Novice paladins could cast the spells ensnaring strike, speak with animals, moonbeam and misty step. Adept paladins were able to cast plant growth, protection from energy, ice storm, and stoneskin. Those that were very advanced had access to the commune with nature and tree stride spells.

Notable Oath of the Ancients Paladins

  • Galea, an elf adventurer who traveled across the Sword Coast.
