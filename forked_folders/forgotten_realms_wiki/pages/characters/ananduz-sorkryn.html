Ananduz Sorkryn

Ananduz Sorkryn was a "Master Trader" of Scornubel who operated Sorkryn's Soft Hides during the 15 century DR.

Description

He had a sly, dashing persona matched with a pointed beard.

Activities

Ananduz sourced leather and live animals from Amn and Tethyr for his business. He was notorious for his lack of scruples in this regard.
