Ziatar was a half-green dragon who followed the faith of Cyric during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Ziatar helped High Priestess Akanna take over the temple of Bhaal in the Forest of Wyrms. After its conversion to the worship of Cyric, Ziatar assisted with the day-to-day operations.

Relationships

Her green dragon half-sister Morentherene made her lair in a cavern just outside of the temple.

History

In the , Ziatar became very concerned about Akanna when the High Priestess locked herself in her quarters for a long time. Unbeknownst to both of them, Akanna was being influenced by a powerful illithid named Darskhelin.
