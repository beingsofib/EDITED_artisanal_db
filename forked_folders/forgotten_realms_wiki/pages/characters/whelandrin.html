Whelandrin was a servant in Suzail, Cormyr, in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Whelandrin was an older man.

Personality

He gave off an impassible appearance, and took care to stay expressionless.

Activities

Whelandrin was a trusty under the nobleman Marlin Stormserpent's roof in Suzail. He was not above accepting money from elsewhere.

History

In Mirtul of the , after the blueflame ghosts that Marlin Stormserpent summoned completed their first assignment for him successfully, Marlin called on one of his trusties, Whelandrin, to find him a wench for enjoyment.
