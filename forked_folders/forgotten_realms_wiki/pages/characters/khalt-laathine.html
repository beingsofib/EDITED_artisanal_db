Khalt Laathine was a Trunalor elf from the Forest of Amtar and worshipper of Avachel who was briefly involved in the efforts to fight the Rage of Dragons in the late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a lean, leonine, tawny elf. Across his cheek, he had a tattooed silver dragon, with the tail going all the way down to his neck.

Personality

Khalt was perceptive and devoted.

Abilities

He was known to wield bows very effectively.

Possessions

He carried a dagger and several enchanted arrows; these arrows could be arrows of attraction, or a different kind that generated a luminous flare after impact, akin to the pyrotechnics spell.

Relationships

He was part of the same tribe as Ferla Treerunner, and a fellow Avachel worshipper together with the mercury dragon Trinculo, of whom he was a friend.

History

Khalt left the Forest of Amtar in to aid Trinculo, who was trying to find a solution to the Rage of Dragons; the two of them met with the copper dragon Chalintash in the Jovial Juggler inn of Beregost. There, Khalt felt singled out by Chalintash, whom he distrusted almost right away.

After their conversation, Khalt learned that Chalintash's bitterness was because he'd learned that elves were responsible for the Rage of Dragons, and that Trinculo had been tasked with looking for an answer to the Rage in Evermeet. That night, Chalintash succumbed to the Rage and attacked Beregost, necessitating he and Trinculo lure him out of the city and slay him to prevent a massacre.

However, when crossing the Trackless Sea, Trinculo appeared to be overwhelmed by grief, giving in to the Rage. Khalt quickly prepared to attack him, but the dragon dived; with the saddle separated, the elf plummeted into the water, from an almost certainly deadly height. Trinculo looked for him a minute afterwards, returning to his senses, but couldn't find him.
