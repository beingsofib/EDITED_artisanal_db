Shavryn Ilidar, known as Ilidar the Brave and the Healer of the High Roads, was a famed itinerant healer and a high priest of Ilmater at a temple in Arrabar in the Vilhon Reach in the 7 and 14 centuries DR.

Activities

In his travels around the Realms, Ilidar generously offered healing, food, and money to needy folks he met along the way. He seemed to have a habit of always passing by just when injured adventurers were most in need of aid, and this reputation lasted long into the 14 century DR in the lands around the Dragon Reach and on the Northern Sword Coast.

By the late 1350s DR, however, Ilidar was elderly and had settled down to managed a large and influential temple in Arrabar.
