Tu'ara'thal was a shalarin cleric of Oghma in Myth Nantar.

History

Born into the shalarin's Seeker Caste, Tu'ara'thal followed the call of Oghma and joined the Faiths Caste.

Around 1349 DR, Tu'ara'thal, with the unborn Ka'ioa'Tara still inside her, led her husband Po'ioa'nam to penetrate the Great Barrier after fleeing a group of merrow that had killed all his companions. Qos accepted Po'ioa'nam and his wife and permitted them to settle in the city.
