Ophinshtalajiir Shaushka was a dragonborn member of Clan Ophinshtalajiir, and the Vanquisher of Tymanther in the period of 1457 to 1467 DR.

History

Shaushka gained renown after a successful operation against the ash giants, and was acclaimed as the Vanquisher of her time almost unanimously.
