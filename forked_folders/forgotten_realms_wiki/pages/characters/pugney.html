Pugney was the proprietor of the Mithrest Inn in the City of Coin, Athkatla, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Pugney was a portly bald man with fair skin.

History

Pugney's exclusive inn was located in the grand market named Waukeen's Promenade in Athkatla's Center District in the . Pugney shared the latest news from Athkatla to his customers but rarely gossiped. His inn was a calm and quiet establishment and he liked to keep it that way.
