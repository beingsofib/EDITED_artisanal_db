Alysa was a stablehand at a tavern in Liara in the Demiplane of Dread who was killed by a werespider.

History

A stablehand at The Boar's Tusk tavern, Alysa was one of the people murdered by Sundian Silgathen before a party from Ravens Bluff was drawn into the Demiplane of Dread by the mists. All they'd know about Alysa's death and all the other victims was that they were human.

Rumors & Legends

The bite marks in Alysa's neck suggested a vampire, though one was not responsible.
