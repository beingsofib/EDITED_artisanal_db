Faltis was a merchant of Beregost who lived in the town during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

He was an associate of two other merchants, Glayde and Tessilan.

History

In the , Faltis, Glayde, and Tessilan were targeted by the scam artist Silke Rosena. The young bard arranged to meet the merchants at the Red Sheaf inn under the guise of purchasing a collection of gems. Silke then told the bard Garrick and some passers by the men were local thugs hired by Feldepost to collect gold after failing to perform at his inn, in an attempt to have them attacked so she could make away with the gems.
