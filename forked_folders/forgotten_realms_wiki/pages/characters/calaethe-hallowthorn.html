Calaethe Hallowthorn was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

History

On , the Waterdhavian thief Narnra Shalace's first assignment as a spy for Caladnei, the Royal Magician of Cormyr, was to tail a number of that country's War Wizards including Calaethe Hallowthorn.
