Faerdral was an assassin with the School of Stealth in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Faerdral was not given to speculation, almost to the point of dullness.

Activities

He was an assassin.

Relationships

His loyalty lay with the School of Stealth; one of his comrades was called Faerdral.

History

In Flamerule of the , Faerdral was hired from the School of Stealth to accompany Count Kelmar Dargren, who was obsessively investigating a series of murders. However, one evening, about a tenday from the murders starting, he was himself killed while searching for the killer in the area between Ivory Street (Zazesspur) and Temple Street.
