Ko Dun Kin was a Shou merchant in T'u Lung in 1358 DR.

History

Ko Dun Kin was a merchant, representative of the mercantile Ko clan in the town of Pe Nok in T'u Lung. In 1358 DR, he welcomed his cousin Ko La Ko and an adventuring party hired by Ko Ho San. He helped them with their journey to H'sin-to, at the mouth of the Fenghsintzu River. Possibly they were attacked by Dragon Claw followers trying to scare Ko Dun Kin.
