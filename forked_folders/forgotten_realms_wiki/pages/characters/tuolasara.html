Tu'ola'sara was a dukar of the Order of Maliirn and a member of the Nantarn Council.

History

Hailing from Ys'bhol, Tu'ola'sara was a shalarin of the Wanderer Caste. During a trip, a rogue current sent her against the Great Barrier. She was saved by the dukar and soon Tu'ola'sara joined them. She was sent back to Es'rath as a secret dukar agent in the area collecting as much lore as possible.

After the Twelfth Serôs War and the reveal of the dukar orders, Tu'ola'sara was granted a place in the council.
