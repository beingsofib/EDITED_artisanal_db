Xana was a cleric of Iyachtu Xvim in Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Xana was the leader of the cult of Xvim in Zhentil Keep following the city's destruction in the . She was a priest of Bane before Zhentil Keep's fall.
