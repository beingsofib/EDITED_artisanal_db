Kaedros was a guard for the slaver ring in the Oasis of the White Palm in Raurin in the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

He wore a dark robe. He also carried a potion of gaseous form and a wand of paralyzation, which still had eleven charges in it.

History

Shortly after the princess was kidnapped by Khalitharius in the , if he heard a noise from the bugbear guarding the cells, he would come to investigate within a minute or two, together with Abu Karesan.
