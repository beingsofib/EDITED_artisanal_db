Sha'Quessir Mirabella was a human and elf-friend of Myth Drannor reputed to have been an elven knight.

History

According to legend, Mirabell was offered a knighthood by the Elven Court at Myth Drannor. However, elves of Evermeet disputed this claim since all elven knights were pure-breed elves, and traditionally gold elves.

Possessions

If the legend was true, Mirabella would have worn elven plate, fought with a longsword, and rode a moon horse like other elven knights.
