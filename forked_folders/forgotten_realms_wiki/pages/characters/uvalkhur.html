Uvalkhur was a wizard of Sembia, and the once-rider of Malaeragoth.

Personality

He was a charming, expert guide with no head for intrigue. He didn't mind sharing his magic with his dragon steed.

Possessions

He had a manor in Northern Sembia, northwest of Saerb. He stocked it with much magic, which included a scrying mirror.

Activities

Uvalkhur was fond of riding the dragon Malaeragoth, and besides other activities, he gladly showed him many places.

Relationships

The son of a rich Sembian merchant, Uvalkhur was known to ride on Malaeragoth, a sapphire dragon.

History

Uvalkhur was a young man when he somehow secured the services of the dragon steed Malaeragoth; he also drew the enmity of the Cult of the Dragon. Eventually, when he was no longer young, he was slain in his estate, which was looted in turn by the surviving Cult members, and then by his steed.

Legacy

His manor remained haunted into the 14 century DR by phantom wizards and overrun by leucrotta.
