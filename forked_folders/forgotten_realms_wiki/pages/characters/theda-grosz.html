Theda Grosz was a lustful inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Theda Grosz was a small hin woman with short blonde bob haircut, blue eyes, and pale skin.

History

In the , Theda Grosz could often be found in the Lower City, not far from the Blushing Mermaid with her human friend, Fast Neddio. Together, they spent their days productively – drooling and lusting over passing Flaming Fist mercenary patrols. Theda was convinced one of the patrolmen, a woman who was not named, made eyes at her.
