Maugrim Korothir was Morag's second in command and the leader of the People of the Eye.

History

Maugrim unleashed the Wailing Death upon Neverwinter and, being one of Morag's elites, was the mastermind of the attack on Neverwinter. He was defeated shortly after he performed a ritual to bring his mistress Morag out of her magical stasis. This resulted in his banishment to Cania.
