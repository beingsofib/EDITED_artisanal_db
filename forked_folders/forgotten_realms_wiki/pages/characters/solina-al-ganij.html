Solina al-Ganij was a skilled flame mage and member of the House of Uqab tribe in the High Desert of west Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Personality

Solina was a greedy coward.

History

Once a member of the infamous Brotherhood of the True Flame, Solina turned on her brethren during a plot to assassinate the Sultana of Hiyal. Much to her surprise, the Sultana's men hunted her down as well after she turned on her companions. Solina fled Hiyal and disappeared into the expanse of the High Desert where she was rescued by Sheikh Hanjar al-Haqara.
