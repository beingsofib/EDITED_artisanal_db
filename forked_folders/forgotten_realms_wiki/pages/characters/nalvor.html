Nalvor was an inhabitant of Scornubel in the mid-14 century DR.

Description

His voice was cold.

History

In late , Nalvor at the Stormy Tankard sought the services of a certain Fraea, and a woman quoted a price of four gold pieces to him. He began to quibble, and was warned the price would rise to five.
