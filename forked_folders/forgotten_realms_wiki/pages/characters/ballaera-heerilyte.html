Ballaera Heerilyte, who was nicknamed Tentacles by some, was a wizard inhabiting Suldolphor in the far eastern reaches of Calimshan in south Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Ballaera was very flirtatious and enjoyed parties.

Description

She was a voluptuous and beautiful woman. Some believed she achieved her beauty through magic.

Activities

Ballaera developed a unique spell that allowed her to shoot strong and versatile tentacles from her palms. She specialized in gathering information for others for a fee.

Rumors & Legends

She sometimes adpoted different forms, some of which were quite hideous, for days at a time. It was rumored that one of these scary forms was her true one.
