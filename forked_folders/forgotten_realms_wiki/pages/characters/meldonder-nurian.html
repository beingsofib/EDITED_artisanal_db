Meldonder Nurian served as "Lord of the Waves" in Melvaunt around 1367 DR and 1373.

Description

Meldonder was an imposing young man who's hair had turned white prematurely.

Activities

As Lord of the Waves Meldonder was responsible for the welfare of Melvaunt's navy. In 1367 DR the navy consisted of twelve vessels with another four under construction.
