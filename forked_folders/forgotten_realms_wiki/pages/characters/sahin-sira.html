Sahin Sira was known as the Sheikh of the Great Ghuls in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

History

Sira kidnapped Yasmin, a mortal hakima, and made her his wife. She despised her husband and wanted nothing more than to hide from him and be left alone.

Rumors & Legends

According to legends among the jann and the Al-Badia nomads, Sahin Sira made for her the All-Knowing Eye, an artifact of great vision, divination, and detection, so that she might look upon the vastness of his domain and fall in love with him.
