Tracker Azak was a goblin devotee of the Absolute who dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

Description

She had vibrant orange skin and curly black hair she wore braided back in the center, and sheared along the sides.

Personality

Azak was wholly focused on her duties in the cult of the Absolute and did not abide any distractions.

History

Following the cult's raid of Waukeen's Rest in the , Azak remained stationed in the inner chambers of the Shattered Sanctum.
