Omik Twede was a renowned male gnome armorer living in the city of Karador, capital of Sarifal, in the Moonshae Isles as of the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Omik was famed for crafting and selling his signature armor of etherealness. His shop in Karador was Diamond Thimble.
