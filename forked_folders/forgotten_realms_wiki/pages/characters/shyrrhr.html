Lady Shyrrhr, born Larelda Shyrrhr Mallowdar, was a Lady of the Court inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Lady Shyrrhr's home was located on the south side of Waterdeep Way just west of the Street of the Sword in Waterdeep's Castle Ward.
