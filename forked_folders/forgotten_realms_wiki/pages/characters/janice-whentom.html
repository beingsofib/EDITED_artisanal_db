Janice Whentom was a soldier and a fighter-wizard who created the enchanted caster's shield, designed to be wielded by the users of the Art.

History

Janice Whentom created caster's shield to further protect herself on the field of battle. She wore heavy armor and caster's shield to fight alongside her battalion in the front lines and support them with beneficial spells. However powerful the shield was, it did not offer absolute protection, and eventually, Janice was killed by a stray arrow. After her death, caster's shield was returned to her family as a sign of respect by her fellow soldiers who survived the battle.
