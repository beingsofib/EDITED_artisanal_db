Wolg was the chieftain of a group of kobolds that fought as part of the Hordes of Dragonspear during the Second Dragonspear War of the mid–14 century DR.

He led about 500 kobolds as part of the Marauders, the group that was tasked with the least desirable and most dangerous tasks.

Personality

Wolg was exceptionally cruel, even for a kobold chieftain.

History

The chief and his kobolds fought in the war's final battle at Dragonspear Castle.
