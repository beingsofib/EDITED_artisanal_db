Ulreel was a bronze dragon who attended the meet called by Lareth on Marpenoth 14 of the . He was initially reticent to follow Lareth's plan, and almost got violent, but eventually caved in to the scheme involving Nexus' spell to sleep through the next year's Rage of Dragons.
