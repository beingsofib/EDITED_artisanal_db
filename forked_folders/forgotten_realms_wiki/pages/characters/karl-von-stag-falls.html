Baron Karl von Stag Falls was the pioneer who resettled Stag Falls centuries prior to the 14 century DR.

Possessions

Karl's shield had originally belonged to him.

Relationships

He had been accompanied by the Haldine family in resettling Stag Falls.

History

Karl von Stag Falls had been the one to reclaim the castle in Stag Falls and resettle the area, together with a number of settlers.

Legacy

Karl's barony was passed down his lineage, all the way to Vilhelm von Stag Falls, his great-great-grandson.
