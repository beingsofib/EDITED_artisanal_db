Ebon Lefash was a person in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

Ebon Lefash led a a mob of looters and scavengers who moved into the deserted Old Stone Keep. The leader of the Legendary Campaign, which sought to unify the wartorn Utter East through conquest, seeking to take back something they believed to be rightfully theirs, came to the ruins to defeat Lefash in a bloodforge battle.
