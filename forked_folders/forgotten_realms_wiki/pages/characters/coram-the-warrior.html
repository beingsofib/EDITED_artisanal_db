Coram the Warrior was a human leader who founded the realm of Coramshan in the 61 century before Dalereckoning.

History

Following the entrapment of Calim and Memnon in inside the Calimemnon Crystal, their realms fell apart while their surviving servants (mainly lesser genies) struggled to maintain their domains. Shortly after in , rebel humans slaves and servants allied with the dwarves of Shanatar, forming an army led by Coram the Warrior to fight the remaining genie overlords. His army ultimately ended the genie domination of the region by , and Coram declared himself ruler of the lands between the Calim Desert and Marching Mountains. They rebuilt Calimport and Keltar, naming this new realm Coramshan in honor of both Coram and Shanatar.

Despite his personal success, Coram's nine sons slew each other and ended their dynasty within 32 years, and the first 50 years of the realm saw over 300 rulers and heirs slain as they jockeyed for the throne.
