Daud was a human thief and a founder and leader of the Clenched Fist, a Rundeen-affiliated mercenary and adventuring company based in Schamedar in Calimshan in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Daud had amnesia, having no early memories apart from his name. This made him curious and driven to uncover secrets. He was also described as a "horror", given his evil ways.

Description

A long scar ran over his forehead and scalp—a likely cause of his memory loss. It turned red when he was angered.
