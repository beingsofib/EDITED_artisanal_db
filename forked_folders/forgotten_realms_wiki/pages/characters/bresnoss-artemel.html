Bresnoss Artemel was the patriarch of the Waterdhavian noble house Artemel during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

He lived within Artemel Villa located at the corner of Sul Street and Vondil Street in the northeast section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward.

Relationships

Bresnoss' consort was Lydda and his eldest son and heir was Ord.
