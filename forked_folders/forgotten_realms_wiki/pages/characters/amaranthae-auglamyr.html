Amaranthae Auglamyr was an elf of House Auglamyr in the 3 century DR.

Appearance

During a revel in , they wore high boots. They were tall for elves.

Relationships

She was terrified of her cousin, Symrustar.

History

In , she was a confidante for Symrustar Auglamyr.

During a revel, she helped Symrustar find some time alone with Elminster by conversing with Earynspieir Ongluth. However, she revealed later to the Srinshee and Earynspieir she believed her cousin meant Elminster harm.

She became concerned about her cousin after the latter disappeared.
