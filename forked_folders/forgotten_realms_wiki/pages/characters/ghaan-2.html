Gha'an was a githyanki child from Crèche Y'llek on Toril in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Gha'an had a shaved head and was dressed in leather pants and pauldrons of githyanki design.

History

Githyanki of what would become Crèche Y'llek, attacked and claimed the Rosymorn Monastery of Lathander in the Western Heartlands sometime in the late 15 century DR. Gha'an was hatched in the Crèche and, along with other gith children, attended military and combat training around the , during the Absolute crisis.
