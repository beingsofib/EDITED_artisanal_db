Aeristhax was a pseudodragon who became an ally of the Uthgardt sorceress Saskia in .

Description

The pseudodragon was the color of wine, and no larger than a cat, with delicate wings.

Personality

Referring to itself as "the Wyrm Aeristhax", it communicated with dignified solemnity.

Abilities

The pseudodragon's venomous stinger was operational until it was removed.

Relationships

Aeristhax introduced itself as heir apparent to Akilskyls, Wyrm of Renown. It appeared to join with the barbarian Saskia at some point, mind-speaking with her; he referred to her as "sister" while asking for its freedom, though it referred to her as a "southern" woman at a later point, possibly implying it believed her to be kin because of sorcery rather than being aware of her Uthgardtian Dragon Tribe heritage.

History

The dragon considered Saskia a southern woman, strongly implying it had spent part of its life somewhere north of Sembia.

Aeristhax was trapped by a Calishite and sold to Captain Tombli of the Company of the Chimera in late Mirtul of the , in the Inn of the Seven Silvers along the Dawnpost; the trappers were still injured, suggesting it hadn't been caught far from that place. While it was being haggled over, the pseudodragon noticed the Uthgardt woman, Saskia, whom it mentally communicated with, demanding its freedom. She refused, and Aeristhax stung and poisoned her.

Tombli bought it at some later point during that day, severed its venomous stinger, and cruelly taunted and tortured the creature throughout the day in the inn's stable. Come evening, Saskia heard noises and found Tombli still tormenting the beast. She attacked Tombli, who overpowered her; however, she was able to call on some arcane talents and unlock the dragon's cage. Aeristhax then repelled Tombli, making the dwarf escape. The dragon then led Saskia north towards Tasseldale as the two of them fled the Company of the Chimera.
