Buri was a homunculus inhabiting the ruined city of Ysawis in the Grey Jungle of east Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Description

He was about the size of a common squirrel.

Relationships

Buri was created by the powerful necromancer Sumulael to be his pet and companion.
