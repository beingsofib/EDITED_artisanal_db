Felaern Starym

Person
nameFelaern Starym
raceElf
occupationMage

Felaern Starym was an ancestor of the Starym of Cormanthyr in the 3 century DR.

Legacy

Glarald Starym retrieved an enchantment from his tomb, which allowed him to control wands and scepters from afar. This enchantment was forbidden.
