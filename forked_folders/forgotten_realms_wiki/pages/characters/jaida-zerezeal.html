Jaida Zerezeal was a half-elf ranger of the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. She held the title of Gauntlet of the Western Marches, with the duty of defending Loudwater's territory to the west, until she was replaced by Isyan Kiy'sisnos in Eleasis/Eleint of 1372 DR.
