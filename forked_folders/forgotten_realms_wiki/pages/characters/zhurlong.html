Zhurlong was a thief who lived on the Sword Coast during the mid−14 century DR.

Description

He was something of a kleptomaniac, and stole from others at nearly every opportunity he could.

Possessions

Zhurlong had a pair of boots of stealth, that were stolen from him by a band of hobgoblins that skulked around the ruins of the Ulcaster school.

History

At some point during the , Zhurlong took up residence at the Burning Wizard in the city of Beregost.
