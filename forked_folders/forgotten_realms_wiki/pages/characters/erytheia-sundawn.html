Erytheia Sundawn was a militant morninglord priest of Amaunator during the late 15 century DR.

Description

She had pale, pinkish skin and a bob of dark red hair, with bright green eyes. She wore a suit of resplendent plate mail and carried a a large mace.

Abilities

Erytheia was a skilled magical healer with heal and mass heal, and could even cast true resurrection if necessary. She could also defend herself with the implosion spell.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Erytheia joined various adventurers as a supportive hireling, lending her abilities as a supportive healer against the drow and other threats.
