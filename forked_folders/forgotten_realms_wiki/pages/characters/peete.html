Peete was an aged sailor from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Peete was a human with the weathered complexion of an older man. He had a light skin tone and short hair whitened by age.

History

In the , Peete could be found in the docks of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, where commoners could buy fresh fish. The docks were attacked by a band of sahuagin when the area was visited by the Absolute Adversaries. The fish-men jumped from the water all around Peete, terrifying the old man. It was unknown if Peete survived the attack. If he were to survive, he would express his gratitude and voice concern over the sahuagin bodies smelling up the docks.
