Sleam was the captain of a smuggling vessel that operated out of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Sleam was an aging hin woman with a light skin tone, crossed by many wrinkles. She had light blue eyes and dark brown hair.

History

In the , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, Dharin Brislen attempted to ship his wife and daughters away from Baldur's Gate. He attempted to negotiate a passage to Waterdeep with Captain Sleam, a smuggler with her own boat and crew. But Sleam asked for too much money, claiming it was a life and death situation for her crew too. Dharin could not afford to pay for four seats so he resorted to staying behind. It was unknown if the family and Sleam's boat escaped the Gate before the battle of the Upper City commenced.
