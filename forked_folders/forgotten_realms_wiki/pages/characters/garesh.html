Garesh was a lieutenant of the Cult of the Dragon circa 1369 DR during the occupation of the ruins of Myth Drannor under direct command of Overseer Sinod.

History

Garesh was one of the cultists who were led by Kya Mordrayn in 1369 DR into the ruins of the ancient elven city to take control of a pool of radiance in the basement of Castle Cormanthor. During that time, Lieutenant Garesh was tasked to hunt down a band of orog mercenaries who rebelled against their cultist masters, stripping souls off their kin, turning them into subservient living zombie-like creatures. According to the notes left behind in the Elven Catacombs, he was also the cruel prison warden who ordered the torture of Athan after his capture.
