Shay Morningstar was an advisor to Dagult Neverember in Neverwinter during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Shay helped to coordinate the efforts of adventurers in accordance with Lord Neverember's goals for Neverwinter, such as by directing them to dangerous dungeons.

Relationships

She worked alongside the likes of Drijm Stoneaxe, Sudbury, and Silwen Wiltfen in her service to Lord Neverember.
