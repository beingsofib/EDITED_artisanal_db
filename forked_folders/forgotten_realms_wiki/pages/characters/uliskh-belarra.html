Uliskh Belarra was a notable wizard inhabiting Yeshpek along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Uliskh was young but unhealthy. He was bald and rather ugly.

Activities

He was utterly devoted to the security and defense of the city.

Relationships

He used magic to alter his appearance when an amorous mood struck him and he wished to enjoy the company of Yeshpek's women.
