Nuial Treestrider was a half-elf bard of the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the late 14 century DR. He was also a Scion of the Green Regent.

History

At the ceremony for the choosing of the Green Regent on Shieldmeet of the , Nuial was among the volunteers and was accepted as a Scion.

Activities

A minstrel, he entertained at many taverns and other venues in the area.

Personality

Nuial loved the city and, despite his position as a Scion, had no taste for getting into any adventures in the wilderness. He tended to get lost in his music and fellow bard Teseryne Truesilver considered him a bit vapid and touched in the head, but liked him nevertheless.

Description

His eyes were hazel and his hair was sandy blond. He wore a tunic that was powder blue and hose that were seafoam green. He played the lyre and was armed with a dagger at his belt. While a Scion of the Green Regent, Nuial bore the holy symbol of Mielikki upon his brow, glowing emerald.
