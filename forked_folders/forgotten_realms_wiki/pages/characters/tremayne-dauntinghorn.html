Lady Tremayne Dauntinghorn was a member of the noble Dauntinghorn family of Cormyr in the 14 century DR.

In the , Lady Tremayne attended King Azoun Obarskyr IV's birthday feast, hosted by Lord Partic Thistle at Thistleflame Keep. She was accompanied there by Lord Alexi Dauntinghorn.
