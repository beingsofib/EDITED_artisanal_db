Yegora, the Maw of Myth Drannor was a great purple worm that laired beneath the ruins of Myth Drannor in the late 14 century DR.

History

The worm lived in a series of shallow caves beneath the city, accessible from the surface by great craters blasted into the ground. Among the various relics that accreted in the beast's lair were the band of manifest magic, the band of seasons' warding, and the band of faith and fortress, which the worm was too large to wear.
