Estimyra was a writer and a War Wizard of Cormyr, active in the early-to-mid 14 century DR.

History

By the , Estimyra had spent twenty years serving as a War Wizard with her latest known post at the High Horn keep in the Storm Horns. She published a collection of autobiographical musings titled Twenty Winters a War Wizard in 1352 DR.
