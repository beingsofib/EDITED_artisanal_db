Magroph Avithoul was a draegloth of House Avithoul in the 15 century DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, Magroph served at the House Avithoul estate in Sschindylryn. When Mirini Elemys sent adventurers to wreck the estate, Magroph likely fell in battle defending the household.
