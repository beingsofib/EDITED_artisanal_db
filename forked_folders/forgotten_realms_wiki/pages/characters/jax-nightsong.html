Jax Nightsong was a half-elf follower of Shaundakul, the Rider of the Winds.

Activities

Jax founded an order of crusaders, rangers, and windwalkers loyal to Shaundakul called the Knights of the Shadow Sword. He operated out of Shaundakul's Throne in Myth Drannor where he and his brethren helped rid the city of monsters.
