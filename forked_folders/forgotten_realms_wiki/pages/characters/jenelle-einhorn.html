Jenelle Einhorn was a priestess of Oghma at the Leaves of Learning temple and library in Highmoon, Deepingdale, in the mid–14 century DR.

After the Time of Troubles of 1358 DR, while still an acolyte of Learned Father Hasicor Danali, she wrote the Encyclopaedia Deifica, which detailed a range of gods and goddesses of various pantheons. It received a new edition in 1370 DR.
