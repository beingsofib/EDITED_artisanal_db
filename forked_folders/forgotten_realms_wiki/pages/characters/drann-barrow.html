Drann Barrow was a member of the Zhentarim who lived in Neverwinter in 1484 DR.

History

During the Tyranny of Dragons, Drann was in the Whispering Carverns and directed many adventurers to disrupt the actives of the Cult of the Dragon, including in the fight with Vilithrax.
