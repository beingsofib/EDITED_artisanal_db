Inrath Mirandor was the founder and first king of the kingdom of Impiltur. He started the Mirandor Dynasty of Old Impiltur.

Born the son of Ornrath in , he crowned himself king in the Year of Splendor, −74 DR, and founded the kingdom of Impiltur. His reign ended in and he was succeeded by his eldest son, Iranth II.
