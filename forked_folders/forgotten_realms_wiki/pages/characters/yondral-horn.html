Yondral Horn was an innkeeper in Rassalantar sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

Activities

Yondral managed the Sleeping Dragon inn. Secretly he was also a spy under the payroll of the Zhentarim, especially interested about City Watch (Waterdeep)'s and Lords' Alliance's agents.

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Yondral was an adventurer paid by the Zhent. After retirement he became the innkeeper of the Sleeping Dragon.
