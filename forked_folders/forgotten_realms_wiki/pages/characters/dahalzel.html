Dahalzel was a Bedine warrior of the Raz'hadi tribe who lived in the desert of Anauroch during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He was described as being rather "brawny".

History

Dahalzel fought in the vanguard during the Battle of the Fissures in the . During the battle, Dahalzel had a spell cast upon his jambiya by the witch Ruha that made it appear as if there were eight times as many Bedine as were actually present. This granted them a great edge as they charged the Zhentarim and their asabis.
