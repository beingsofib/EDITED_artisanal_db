Synnoria was queen of a group of elves called the Llewyrr in the Llewyrrwood.

History

Queen Synnoria and her people were forced to flee their homeland in because of the Vyshaan occupation. They crossed the Sea of Swords and settled in the Highlands on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles with the permission of the leShay who occupied the region. The home they founded, Synnoria, was named in their queen's honor.
