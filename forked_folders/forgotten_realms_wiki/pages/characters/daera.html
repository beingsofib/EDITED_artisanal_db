Daera was a coin-lass of Athalantar in the early 3 century DR.

History

During the , the outlaw Javal debriefed to his comrades, the Knights of Athalantar currently turned to banditry and fortified at the Lawless Castle, that a team he was part of had slain two of Athalantar's armsmen coming back from Daera's the night before. He referred to an old farm that had been converted to a house of pleasure a dozen years prior, circa the , which was frequented by men from Heldreth's Horn.
