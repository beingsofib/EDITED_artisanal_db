Erlathan Greycloak

nameErlathan Greycloak

Erlathan Greycloak was an elven sage in ancient times.

Personality

The wise elf had much to say on the drow, claiming that they were "cruelty personified" and that their talent was wasted. Greycloak also described them as "beauty lost in the darkness" and a "proud, graceful evil".
