William was the mayor of the halfling town of Waukeshire in Bloodstone Barony in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He had a waxed moustache and wore a waistcoat with a silver chain.

Personality

William liked to make long, formal speeches.

History

In 1357 DR, with the beginning of Zhengyi the Witch-King's uncontested rule over Bloodstone Pass, William formed the Alliance of Belt Watchers along with Tokan, leader of the dwarves of Clan Orothiar, and ultimately with Chief Kiros of the centaurs.

In 1358 DR, William and his people met Gareth Dragonsbane's party.
