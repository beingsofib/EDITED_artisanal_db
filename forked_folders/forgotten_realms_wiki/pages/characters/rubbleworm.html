Rubbleworm was a huge and hungry carrion crawler that resided in the Underdark beneath the ruins of Myth Drannor in the late 14 century DR.

History

The monster laired in an Underdark cavern with demons and other creatures beneath the ruined city. It was passed by the drow captain Niziira Xiltun during a raid on the surface.
