Haji bin Marat was a rawun living in Hudid in the League of the Pantheon region of interior Zakhara.

Activities

Haji was one of the leaders of a heretical sect of Balanites within the city.

Relationships

He was engaging in a secret romance with Princess Inara al-Munsif within the library while she was supposed to be researching ancient texts.
