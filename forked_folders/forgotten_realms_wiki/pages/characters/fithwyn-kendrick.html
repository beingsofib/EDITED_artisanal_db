Fithwyn Kendrick was a member of the Kendrick family of the Moonshae Isles in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Fithwyn was one of the clients who did business with the mummy lord only known as the Mystic Carrion of Baldur's Gate. The mummy procured a Bone of Abyssal Depth, a rare magic item for Fithwyn to be used for "soul settlement." The record of the purchase was kept in Mystic Carrion's lair in Philgrave's Mansion in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate.
