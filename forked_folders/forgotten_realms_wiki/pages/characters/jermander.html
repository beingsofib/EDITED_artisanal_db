Jermander was a tiefling swordsman and a member of the Bounty Hunters of Cavus Dun in the mid-to-late 15 century DR.

History

Jermander was a swordsman and the leader of the Cavus Dun squad tasked by Effron with finding Dahlia Sin'felle and Drizzt Do'Urden.
