Vur'din was a drow ritualist who specialized in summoning planar beings, who lived in a drow settlement beneath the Stonelands in the late 15 century DR.

History

Vur'din led a group of summoner ritualists at the outpost in service to Mistress Laerion'vyr, a high priestess of Lolth. Unusually for Lolthite drow they summoned a number of slaadi as minions, including red, green, and blue varieties, and the powerful gray slaad Il'tharis the Terrible, which he kept enslaved with a control gem.
