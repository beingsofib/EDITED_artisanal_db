Brynth Carrathal was heir to the defunct Carrathal Dynasty of Callidyrr in the Moonshae Isles as of the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

Brynth was the bastard son of King Reginald Carrathal and an Amnian woman.

He had a son, Lionel, who was born in the .

History

Brynth was raised in Amn by his birthmother. After his father's death in the , the Carrathal Dynasty fell out of power, however the line of succession passed to Brynth.

When Brynth was killed by wererats in the , Lionel became the Carrathal heir.
