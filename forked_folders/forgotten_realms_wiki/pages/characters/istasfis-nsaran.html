High Lord Istasfis Nsaran was a yuan-ti member of House Jennestas tribe that was the ruler of Mhairhetel on the Chultan Peninsula.

Activities

Istasfis inherited his title of High Lord when he married the widow of Mhairhetel's previous ruler, Lord Rhaunister.
