Govinda lived in the fortress of Luntse Dzong in Khazari during the mid–14th century DR.

Activities

Govinda commanded the forces of Luntse Dzong within Ahs Trango Shan. He utilized 150 warriors and 50 monks to insure the pass into eastern Khazari was secure.
