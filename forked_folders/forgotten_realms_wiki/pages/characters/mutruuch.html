Mutruuch was an orc veteran in 1484 DR.

History

In 1484 DR, during the War of the Silver Marches, Mutruuch was a member of the orc army that besieged Mithral Hall. She and some orc minions accompanied Szordrin Xorlarrin to secretly infiltrate the Keep. Mutruuch and her minions remained behind to control the entrance to the tunnel. However, they were discovered and defeated by an adventuring party.
