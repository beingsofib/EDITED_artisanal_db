Nandel Greenward was an Illuskan wizard inhabiting Waterdeep and Undermountain during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Nandel was a student of arcane studies at Halaster's Heirs, a school of magic established Trobriand and Muiral who were apt past pupils of Halaster Blackcloak.
