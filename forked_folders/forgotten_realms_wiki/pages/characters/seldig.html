Seldig was a deep gnome fighter/illusionist from Blingdenstone.

History

Seldig was the third svirfneblin who Drizzt Do'Urden would know by name. Drizz met him some decade after leaving Menzoberranzan, and to Seldig and his fellow youths, he shared the tale of how he battled and slayed a basilisk. Around 1357 DR, Seldig’s svirfneblin patrol encountered Catti-brie disguised as a drow female, with the help of Agatha's mask. In 1338 DR , Seldig and fellow gnome, Pumkato, played with Guenhwyvar, and they recognized the powerful beast as a friend, so they stopped the attack on Catti-brie.

In 1362 DR, the deep gnome finally, like his longtime friend Pumkato, become a burrow warden.
