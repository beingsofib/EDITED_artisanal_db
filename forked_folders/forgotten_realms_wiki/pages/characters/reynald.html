Reynald was a duck-breeder from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

Reynald was a beloved family man, a father and a husband, who perished in the , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldur's Gate. Reynald's tombstone was commissioned to be carved by Candulhallow's Tombstones in the Lower City. The freshly-carved grave marker was stored in Candulhallow's workshop. It read: "Beloved Reynald. Father, husband, duck-breeder. Gone too soon."
