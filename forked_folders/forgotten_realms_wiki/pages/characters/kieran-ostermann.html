Kieran Ostermann was a Cormyrean Purple Dragon knight who served during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the late 15 century DR.

History

During the war with the drow of Sschindylryn, Kieran worked as a hireling of sorts, traveling with adventurers rather than staying on regular Purple Dragons duties. Despite this, he remained utterly devoted to the defense of Cormyr.
