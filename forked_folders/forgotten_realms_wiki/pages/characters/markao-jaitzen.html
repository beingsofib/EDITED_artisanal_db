Markao Jaitzen was a sohei participant at the Komite in Shou Lung.

History

Markao Jaitzen was a sohei at the Temple of True Enlightenment in Suijeng, training under sensei Lu-che. In 1358 DR, Markao Jaitzen went to participate at the Komite. However, he knew it was not possible for him to win. He only wanted to honor his teacher and get practice against strong adversaries.
