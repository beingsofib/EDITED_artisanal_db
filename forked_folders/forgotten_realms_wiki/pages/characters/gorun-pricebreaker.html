Gorun Pricebreaker was a dwarf merchant of the Sword Coast North and the proprietor of the Broken Crown inn on the High Road in the late 15 century DR.

History

Gorun purchased the Broken Crown in the late 1470s DR and reopened the establishment as trade and traffic along the High Road near Neverwinter became active once again.

The inn became a safe haven for travelers along the road near Raven Cliff Beach in the , as activity by the cutthroat Blackdagger Bandits forced many of them to seek safety and protection. Gorun sold goods and supplies to those who came through.
