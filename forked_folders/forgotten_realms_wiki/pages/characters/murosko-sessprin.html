Murosko Sessprin was a pirate who sailed the seas of Faerûn during the late 15 century DR. He was the original captain of the Uncivil Serpent, before it experienced a change in leadership.

History

Around the , Captain Sessprin died when his first mate, "Dead-Eye" Cadavrus led a violent mutiny aboard the Serpent.
