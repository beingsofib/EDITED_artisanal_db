Alaine Greenleaf was an elven hero who lived some years before the late 14 century DR.

History

Alaine was believed to be the last wielder of the longsword called The Equalizer, sometime before the . She and the sword were lost during an expedition to the Underdark, where the The Equalizer was broken in three and remained lost until 1369 DR.
