Minsk was a werebat from the Demiplane of Dread that became trapped in the Vast during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Minsk may have looked like a young boy, or a young man, with a squeaky voice either way.

Personality

His strategy to get fed was to get in the middle of travelers' groups, isolate them, and then suck their blood when they let their guard down.

Abilities

Being a werebat, he had all normal werebat abilities.

Possessions

He had a crucible of melting, and a globe of purification; Minsk had since taken to melting silver weapons used against him in the crucible, making some interesting silver disks from them.

Relationships

He may have had a wife called Ursala, and he may also have had up to four kids: two sons called Marcov or Rensil, and two daughters, called Olga or Romona. That much was true.

History

Minsk had been drawn from the Demiplane of Dread after Romanisk Vlaensk, a vampire, summoned bats; he became trapped in the Prime Material plane, landing in an area near Stag Falls, unable to find his home again. He may have had the rest of his family with him at the moment, including his wife and up to four children, all of them were actually werebats like him.

About a month after Romanisk Vlaensk's release, Minsk had taken over a new cave, where he kept new treasure, perhaps up to a couple hundred bats, and up to ten giant bats. He tried to feed on a group of adventurers at that time; it is unknown whether he survived the ensuing confrontation, and if he did, whether they pursued to his lair, where they may have slain him.
