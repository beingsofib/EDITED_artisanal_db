Marigold was a dragon.

In Flamerule of the , Marigold slept in the Galena Mountains at a refuge for metallic dragons waiting for the Rage of Dragons to end. After the duel between Lareth and Tamarand, from which only the latter emerged alive, Nexus meant to wake Marigold up to tend to his wounds and Karasendrieth's.
