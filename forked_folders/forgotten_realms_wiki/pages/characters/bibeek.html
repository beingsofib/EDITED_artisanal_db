Bibeek was a musician inhabitant of Baldur's Gate who died some years before the late 15 century DR.

History

Bibeek died sometime before the and was buried in the Lower City Graveyard. His gravestone was etched with a praise to Oghma, and the musicians songs. The inscription read: "Bibeek, favoured of Oghma. Your songs live on in the streets and in our hearts." The musician's grave may had been accidentally desecrated, and the body risen as a zombie by Nina Dortmell, a talented child with a gift, who was trying to resurrect her recently deceased brother.
