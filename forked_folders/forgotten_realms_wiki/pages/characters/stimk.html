Stimk was a bugbear who was associated with the cult of the Absolute during the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

His preferred weapon was a one-handed morningstar.

History

In the , cultists of the Absolute launched a terrible raid on the nearby Waukeen's Rest inn. Afterwards, the cultists retired to the Shattered Sanctum temple to drink, carouse and otherwise celebrate their victory. Stimk was among the raiders who remained passed out, completely drunk, along the upper reaches of the temple's courtyard.
