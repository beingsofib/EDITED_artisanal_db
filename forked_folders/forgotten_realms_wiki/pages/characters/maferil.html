Maferil the Gravewalker was a necromancer in Luskan on the Sword Coast North during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Maferil's apprentices thought him cold and distant, but he still took great pride in the work of his students, and carried their memory with him in darker times.

Activities

Maferil dedicated many years of his life to studying the history of the Order of the Burning Dawn.

Maferil was notorious for writing everything down, and had a secret library in the ruins of Illusk. His Lyceum was hidden in the catacombs beneath Luskan's cemetery district, accessed by performing a magical ritual.

His Lyceum also contained alchemical supplies for concocting potions.

Relationships

Maferil was known to take pupils. His first and most promising pupil was Leonid the Reacher, the second was Taralin Felanamar, the third was Goriss Steinchaser, and his fourth was Kneeda Morningchild.

Maferil's last apprentice was Hommet Shaw.

History

Around 1487 DR, Maferil was murdered by the Arcane Brotherhood, who had attempted to recruit him to their order, but on his refusal had framed him for demon worship and had him killed. However, his shade rose again, and encountered the Champion of the Burning Dawn.
