Goodwife Thintagast was an inhabitant of Ieirithymbul in the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

She was married to the jeweler Thintagast.

History

In Uktar of the , there was an incident in Thintagast's home where one of his apprentices, Burlmarr, briefly passed out and banged his head on the ground. Goodwife Thintagast assisted in watching over the convalescing gnome until he woke up.
