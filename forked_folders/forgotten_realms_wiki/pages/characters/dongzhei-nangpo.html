Dongzhei Nangpo was the leader of the Yellow Mountain Sect in Manass in 1367 DR.

Activities

Dongzhei was the master of the Temple of the Yellow Mountain. His high favor with the governor of Manass allowed him to attack and harass the Yellow Mountain Sect's rivals in the Temple of the Red Mountain without fear of political interference.
