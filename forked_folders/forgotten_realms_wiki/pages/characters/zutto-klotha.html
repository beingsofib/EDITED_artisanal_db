Zutto K'Lotha was a Chultan alchemist for hire from the city of Neverwinter, active in the late 15 century DR.

History

Zutto was already an experienced virtuoso alchemist by the time she traveled from her home in Southwest Faerûn to the northern city of Neverwinter to secure some trade agreements. She quickly found herself beguiled by the Neveren alchemy and decided to stay. Since then, Zutto had been making a living by offered her alchemical services for hire.
