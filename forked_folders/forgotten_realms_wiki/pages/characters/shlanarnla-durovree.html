Shlanarnla Durovree was the ninth ruler of the Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate, and Westgate's first and only Queen.

Activities

Shlanarnla was engaged to King Ilphael. The throne passed to her when he died without naming an heir in the . Queen Shlanarnla would rule until the , when she abandoned the throne.
