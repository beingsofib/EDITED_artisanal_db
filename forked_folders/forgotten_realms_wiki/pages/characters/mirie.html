Mirie was an Attendant of Grief in the House of Grief temple that hid the Cloister of Sombre Embrace in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Mirie was a wood elven woman with light and freckled skin. She had blonde hair and green eyes. As an attendant and a clerk, she wore simple priestly tunic and carried a quarterstaff.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Mirie served the Mother Superior as one of her Attendant of Grief at the House of Grief. She welcomed guests and those searching for the temple's services in achieving sweet oblivion from grief, pain, melancholy, or treachery. She directed guests to the ritual of Mapping of the Heart that determined the supplicants' true thoughts and intentions.
