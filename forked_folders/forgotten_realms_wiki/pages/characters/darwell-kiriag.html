Darwell Kiriag was a descendant of Dunman Kiriag and the proprietor of the Lonesome Tankard Inn and Tavern in the town of Eveningstar in the 15 century DR.

Description

In the late 15 century DR, Darwell was a stout middle-aged human man with a light skin tone, brown hair, and well-trimmed facial hair.

History

In the 15 century DR, Darwell Kiriag could be seen welcoming guests and adventurers, whether they were there to fight against the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar or wanted to go look at the nearby ruins of the Haunted Halls.
