Abigail was wife of High King Carrig III of the Moonshae Isles during the late 5 century DR. Their son was Colin Cymrych.

History

Abigail became High Queen of Moonshae when Carrig III inherited the throne in the , following the assassination of his father. Carrig III was also assassinated just a year later, at which time Colin was only eight years old. Thus, Abigail named a regent, King Edmund Kincaid of Snowdown, to rule Callidyrr until Colin was older. Colin would assume the throne in the .
