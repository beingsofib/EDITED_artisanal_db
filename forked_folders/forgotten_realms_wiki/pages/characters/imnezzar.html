Imnezzar was a Zakharan inhabitant of Sokkar before the demise of the city.

History

Much to his eventual regret, Imnezzar was once the lover of Princess Ophidia. When she grew tired of his affections the princess slew him, earning her an eternal curse from Imnezzar's powerful father.
