Ghaliyah was a nereid living in Hawa in 1367 DR.

Activities

Enslaved by the Cult of Istishia, Ghaliyah was forced to guard the entrance to the cult's lair in Hawa.

Relationships

Ghaliyah was accompanied by Jsss, a baby sea wyrm.
