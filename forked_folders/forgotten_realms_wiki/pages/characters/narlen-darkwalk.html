Narlen Darkwalk was a member of the thieves' guild that operated in Baldur's Gate during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He had a very unique style of speech that was fairly close to Thieves' cant.

Relationships

Narlen operated under the leadership of none other than Alatos "Ravenscar" Thuibuld.

History

In the , Narlen had planned a heist to break into a noble estate in the Upper City of Baldur's Gate along with another thief named Rededge. The pair were in need of a trustworthy lookout.

Some time later he also heard rumors about a rogue stone being transported from Waterdeep to the home of a man named Gandolandan, and had the intent of stealing it.
