Torzhin Hulvesper was a human cleric of Chauntea in the village of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

He was the youngest son of Gundul Hulvesper, a local horse-breeder.

Activities

In the late 1350s DR, he served as an acolyte at the House of Plenty temple under Lharathuel Hesmyr. He was available to provide healing to the farmers.
