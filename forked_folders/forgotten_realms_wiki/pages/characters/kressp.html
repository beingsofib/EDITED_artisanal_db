Kressp was a skilled yuan-ti abomination fangblade rogue who dwelled in the Underdark during the 15 century DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Kressp worked with the yuan-ti forces near the Great Waterfall as they fought the drow of Sschindylryn, not far from Massrak.
