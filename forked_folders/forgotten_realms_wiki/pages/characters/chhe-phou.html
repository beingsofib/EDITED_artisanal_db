Chhe Phou was a human warrior from the continent of Kara-Tur in the late 14 century DR.

Personality

Chhe Phou wandered anywhere on the two continents of Faerûn and Kara-Tur where his services as an adventurer were needed. His focus was on destroying evil and he would, for a short time, band together with other adventurers if it proved necessary in order to win. However after victory was achieved he would once more break apart from his erstwhile allies, once more wandering alone.

Possessions

Chhe Phou had, alongside his dagger and leather armor, a scimitar of speed and a helm of telepathy.

Relationships

Both his parents died when he was still young and he sought no relationships with others apart from the short companionship of other adventurers.
