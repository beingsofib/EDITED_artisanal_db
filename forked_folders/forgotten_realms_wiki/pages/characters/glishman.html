Glishman the Annoying was a wizard who lived in Ravens Bluff in the mid–14 century DR.

Home

Glishman lived in a red run-down two-story house on Magician's Row with a shield over the door. It was the third of five houses.

Description

Glishman had a toad familiar, smoked Golden Sea tobacco, and was primarily a milk drinker.

History

Circa 1357 DR, the City Guard serving Regent of the Exchequer Vernon Condor considered Glishman the Annoying for the theft of two minor magic items of historical importance from the city vaults, after suspicions and divinations tracked the thieves to Magician's Row. However, Glishman was innocent.
