Diero Mivaldi was a trader who operated in the Sea of Fallen Stars in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Diero was a a dark-skinned man who wore a green vest.

Activities

Diero was an oceangoing merchant who traded between Yhaunn in Sembia and the distant Vilhon Reach.

History

In Eleasis of the , Diero attended a prestigious party hosted by Ammanas Aumleagarr in Yhaunn, meeting prominent individuals such as the sage Arthagus of the Miracles and Moonmistress-Designate Feena Archwood of Moonshadow Hall.
