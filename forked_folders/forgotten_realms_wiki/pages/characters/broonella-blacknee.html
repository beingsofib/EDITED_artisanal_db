Broonella Blacknee was a halfling woman who operated the Blacknee's Halfhappenstance inn of Halfhap alongside her husband Buzzrip Blacknee in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. They were assisted by their adult children Breena Blacknee, Billyup Blacknee, and Boldric Blacknee.
