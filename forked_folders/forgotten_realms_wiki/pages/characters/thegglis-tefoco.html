Thegglis Tefoco was a caravan guard and one of the older children of Oliver Tefoco, the proprietor of the Beehive Goods and Wares general store in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Thegglis was a child of Oliver and Kroyce Tefoco, and had a number of siblings. The oldest of them was Bubbles Tefoco, who lived in the Stonyeyes district of the Gate with her family and the newborn Dorimara. another older sibling was Dariel Tefoco who served the Flaming Fist. Thegglis' brother Crowell was a fellow caravan guard, and the youngest siblings Hemjax, Maggie, and others, still lived with Oliver and Kroyce as of the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , as the Cult of the Absolute threatened Baldur's Gate's safety, Thegglis and Crowell were stuck with their Seven Suns Trading Coster caravan at Boareskyr Bridge. The caravan's return to the gate was postponed due to the cult's threat and the city gates being closed. During their travels, the older caravan guards taught Thegglis to dual-wield a dagger with a sword, according to the letter home Thegglis penned for Crowell, who could not yet write.
