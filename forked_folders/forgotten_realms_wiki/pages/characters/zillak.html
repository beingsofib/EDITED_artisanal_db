Zillak was a member of House Baenre and an assassin in the service of the Archmage of Menzoberranzan Gromph Baenre in 1373 DR.

History

Zillak was one of Gromph's most trusted agents. During the Siege of Menzoberranzan, his master, after being blinded in a fight with Nimor Imphraezl, requested the assistance of Zillak and the wizard Jaemas Xorlarrin to perform an eye transplant from a prisoner who was a member of House Agrach Dyrr.
