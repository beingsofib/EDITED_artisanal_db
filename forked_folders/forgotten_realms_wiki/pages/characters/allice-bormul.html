Baron Allice Bormul was a Lord and a member of the noble patriar Bormul family of Baldur's Gate sometime before the late 15 century DR.

History

Some years before the , Lady Allice Bormul became a prisoner of the secret Sharran cloister below the House of Grief of Baldur's Gate. She was incarcerated in the Chamber of Loss and died before ever seeing freedom. Allice left behind her Last Will and Testament, written in her own blood, leaving possessions to her beloved daughter, Voebe Bormul. Her skeletal remains were still within the undergoing cloister during the Absolute crisis.
