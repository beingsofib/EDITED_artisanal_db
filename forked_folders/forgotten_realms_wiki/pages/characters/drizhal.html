Drizhal was a young soldier and lieutenant in the allied army of the Dalelands that lived and died during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He was a slender man with gangly limbs and long, vivid blonde hair.

History

In the , while less than 20 years old, Drizhal fought alongside his countrymen to defend their home from the Zhentilar in the second battle of Shadowdale. For a time during the battle, he served under the authoritative Kelemvor Lyonsbane,

Facing insurmountable odds and unable to execute a tactical retreat, Drizhal stood firm against the Zhent soldiers but was ultimately killed soon after the fighting broke out.
