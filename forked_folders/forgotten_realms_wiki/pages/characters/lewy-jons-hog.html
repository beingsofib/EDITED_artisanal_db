Lewy Jons' hog was a pig of West Harbor on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR, belonging to the farmer Lewy Jons.

History

In the Harvest Fair of the , Lewy attempted to win at the hog contest, cheating by using magic to enlarge his pig. Orlen had doubts and tasked the Kalach-Cha to investigate; Lewy was prepared to bribe them to avoid being exposed.

After the Harvest Fair, however, Lewy and his hog were killed by duergar and bladeling raiders.
