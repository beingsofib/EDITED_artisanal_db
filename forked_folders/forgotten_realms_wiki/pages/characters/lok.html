Lok was the leader of the Eagle Knights in Payit, Maztica, in the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

Lok was one of the most powerful men in Ulatos. He was always close to the ruler Caxal during times of war.

History

In the , soon after the arrival of the Golden Legion's ships, Lok was called by Caxal to a war council with the leader of the Jaguar Knights, Gultec, to discuss how to fight the strangers.
