Thiralan Farstep was one of the Cormanthor Elves during their efforts to reclaim Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Description

Thiralen had light skin, messy white hair, and pale white eyes. He wore an ornate suit of plate mail decorated with leafy designs.

History

Around 1374 DR, Thiralen served as the Sergeant of the Guard at the Myth Drannor outskirts camp, helping protect the residents and travelers there. One of his concerns was the ability for elves to safely reach the camp, particularly the paths near the Spiderhaunt Wood where a large amount of spiderkind dwelt. While the patrols were successful at first, the traveling elves then started disappearing entirely. Perplexed by the development, Thiralen guided a group of trustworthy individuals to the site of the disappearances, where they discovered that the local ettercaps were kidnapping people to feed to the hungry and lazy fang dragon Nylerian.
