Yeda was a high priest inhabiting the city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–10 century DR.

Activities

Yeda was the high priest to Pharoah Akonhorus I. He was once quoted as saying "Adventure? Ha! Excitement? Bah! A Mulhorandi craves not these things!"
