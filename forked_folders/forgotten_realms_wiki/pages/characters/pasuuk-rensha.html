Pasuuk Rensha was a member of the Rensha family and the ruler of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the early 14 century DR and the last of his line to reign there.

History

In the Year of Spilled Blood, 1315 DR, Pasuuk ordered a band of talented mercenary hunters to obtain for him a unicorn's horn. His reasons for this were not known, but some worried it was for a ritual of dark magic that would link High Lord's Hall with the Nine Hells, and he was later confirmed to consort with devils.

But, when they caught the unicorn, one of the hunters, a Nimbrali mercenary named Nanathlor Greysword, instead defended the beast, became the Green Regent, and rebelled against the rule of Pasuuk Rensha. He and his followers fought to overthrow the Renshas and liberate Loudwater, launching the War of the Returned Regent.

After two years of fierce fighting, the war culminated in the Battle of Tanglefork in the , when Pasuuk and his forces confronted Nanathlor and his ragtag band Nanathlor called on the goddess Mielikki for aid, and she awakened the trees around the battlefield and set them to attack Pasuuk's forces. In response, Pasuuk summoned a coven of devils to assault the trees. Ultimately, Mielikki's servants were victorious and the devils and Pasuuk were all killed in the fighting. The corpses of Pasuuk and his fiendish allies were scattered like filth, blackening the soil of Tanglefork Woods with their evil. Over half a century later, it was still as dark as midnight.
