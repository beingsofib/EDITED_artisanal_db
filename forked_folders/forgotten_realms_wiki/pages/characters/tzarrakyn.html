Tzarrakyn was an adventurer active during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Tzarrakyn was a member of the Company of Crazed Venturers. He perished one day while battling a vicious beholder. His young wife, Dartheema, died several months later while giving birth to their son named Tzarrakyn the Younger in honor of his father.
