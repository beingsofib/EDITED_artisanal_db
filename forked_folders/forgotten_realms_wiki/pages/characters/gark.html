Great Gark was the Goblin King of Nachtur in the Feywild as of the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Gark commanded a great host of goblins, worgs, unseelie fey, and other creatures. Gark attempted to expand his kingdom into the Moonshae Isles by sending his forces into the Dernall Forest in Callidyrr. This was initially a small nuisance for the scattered villages found within the forest, but Gark's minions had infested the entire wood by the late 15 century DR.

History

Great Gark's forces started by kidnapping people and livestock before their numbers grew so large that soldiers from House Kendrick, druids led by Antola Pilark, and even fey outcasts from Sarifal all joined forces to drive them out. As of this time, Great Gark appeared to be working alongside fomorians to defeat the druids aligned against him.
