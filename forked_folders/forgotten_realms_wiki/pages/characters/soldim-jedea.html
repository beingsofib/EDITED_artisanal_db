Soldim Jedea was a member of the Jedea family and the first ruler of the city of Mordulkin in Chessenta following its revolt from Unther in the Year of Flashing Eyes, 929 DR.

Soldim was the most powerful mage of the Jedea family, and it became standard practice for the family's best mages to rule the city.

Soldim later founded the Jedea Academy, a school for magic.
