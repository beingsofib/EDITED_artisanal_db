Mareak was a dwarf that lived in Baldur's Gate during the 15 century DR.

Description

The dwarf had a blond beard.

History

In 1482 DR, not long after Abdel Adrian's death on Returning Day, there was a large holdup at the Basilisk Gate. Mareak, along with several others, wished to pass through the gate, but some sort of "city shield" had been stolen from the Harbormaster's Office. The toll collector of the gate, Nant Thangol, demanded everyone be searched, and to acquire fees from them. Mareak was very unhappy about this, and planned to convince the Heroes of the Wide to have a word with Thangol. Specifically, he wanted to heroes to sympathise with him and help the poor folk home to their dinners.
