Galladel was the elf king of Shilmista in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He once believed in the magic of the forest, but lost his beliefs when he tried a ritual and failed his fellow elves. Over his many years of life, he became almost human-like in his thinking and planning during the war of Shilmista.
