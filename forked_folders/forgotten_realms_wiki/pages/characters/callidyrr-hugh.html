Callidyrr Hugh was King of Corwell in the Moonshae Isles in the late 2 century DR and the founder of the Hugh Dynasty. He was the namesake for the Kingdom of Callidyrr and for its capital, Caer Callidyrr.

Relationships

Callidyrr was a son of Ebenfar nobility. His first son, Cymrych Hugh, would go on to become the legendary first High King of the Moonshae Isles.

History

Callidyrr claimed the throne of Corwell in the after unifying the Ffolk kingdoms that had been engaged in civil war since the death of King Deric and the end of the First Dynasty of the Moonshaes in the .

He ruled for sixteen years before he was slain by trolls in the . Eight years later, his son would name the land of Callidyrr in his honor.
