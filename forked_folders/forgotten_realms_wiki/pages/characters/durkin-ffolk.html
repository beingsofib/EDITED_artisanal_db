Durkin was a Ffolk captain of a fishing coracle who inhabited the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Durkin was ferried Elminster of Shadowdale to Corwell on his ship during the mage's journey through the various islands of the Moonshae Isles.
