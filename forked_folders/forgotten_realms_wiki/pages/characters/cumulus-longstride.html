Cumulus Longstride was a storm giant proxy of the Giantish power known as Stronmaus and was one of his foremost proxies.

Description

He was usually dressed with nothing covering him from the waist up.

Personality

Cumulus was a very hardy and sociable individual, who reveled in good company regardless of their height.

Activities

Cumulus acted as a shepherd to the many petitioners of Stronmaus that resided in his divine realm of Stormhold.
