Rrosp was a hefty earth elemental who was controlled by the medusa Sshaastess in the late 15 century DR.

History

When Oriphaun Huntsilver left a piece of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll with Seamus at Lost Souls' Swamp, the medusa Sshaastess was drawn by its energies and proceeded to dominate the area with foul magic. While defending herself from Harper agents sent to recover the Scroll she summoned up Rrosp and Gruck as her bodyguards, only for them to all be slain in battle.
