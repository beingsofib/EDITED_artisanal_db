Ayana was a child of Fiona who lived in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

Ayana died in the , and her body was buried in the Lower City Graveyard. Fiona, the child's mother, could not thank the mortician Valmorba enough for going above and beyond for the funeral in a letter and sent the Lower City Mortuary gold for the coffin and for Valmorba to bury the girl's beloved teddy bear with her.
