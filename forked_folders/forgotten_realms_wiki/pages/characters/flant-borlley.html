Flant Borlley was a commoner from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in late 15 century DR.

Description

Flant was a human man with facial tattoos. He wore an azure-colored hood and commoner's garb.

History

In the , the city of Baldur's Gate was threatened by the encroaching army of the Cult of the Absolute. The crisis flooded the city with refugees, elevating tensions among its citizens and allowing a new power to take rulership of the city – Lord Gortash. Flant Borlley eagerly consumed the new lord's propaganda from the curbside preachers and from the pages of the Baldur's Mouth Gazette. Augur Harxkellan was a Gortash loyalist whose words attracted Flant's attention as he worshiped Enver Gortash, thinking him to be an underappreciated genius.
