Ortran was an apprentice of Alarashan Tlor in the mid-3 century DR.

Abilities

The man was inept at sorcery.

Relationships

He was an apprentice to Alarashan Tlor, one of the Magelords of Athalantar.

History

Ortran had been forced as an apprentice on Alarashan Tlor at some point prior to the . On that year, Ortran was slain in the jakes of Athalgard during the siege of the castle by the forces coordinated under Prince Elminster Aumar.

Rumors & Legends

Alarashan believed him to be a spy for Undarl Dragonrider.
