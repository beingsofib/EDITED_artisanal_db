Yauln was the leader of a stone giant clan in the ancient past.

History

Yauln led his clan until the duergar of Gracklstugh offered an alliance. He went to the mystic library of Gravenhollow asking for counsel.

It was known that a stone giant clan became a longtime ally of Gracklstugh's kings.
