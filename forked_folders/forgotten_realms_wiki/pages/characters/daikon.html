Daikon was a wizard with the Arcane Brotherhood of Luskan in the late 14 century DR.

Description

He wore a cowled robe.

Personality

Daikon's ambition was remarkable.

Abilities

One of his spells launched a missile of colored light.

Possessions

He had a stone through which he sent and received messages, most likely a speaking stone.

Relationships

He served Arklem Greeth. During his mission in Ieirithymbul, he had three accomplices.

History

In Uktar of the , Daikon intercepted a caravan of gnomes in Leilon that was on its way back to Ieirithymbul and killed them, interrogating the last before killing him, and taking on the likeness of the four dead gnomes for him and his accomplices. He did this under the orders of Arklem Greeth, the Archmage Arcane, who bade him confirm the identity of the Unseen Protector of Ieirithymbul.

Once inside Ieirithymbul, Daikon and his accomplices quickly captured Kaylindrra Gromann, wife of elder Froga Gromann, and forced him to take them to the foothills of Felrenden, to a place where the Unseen Protector, the gold dragon Palarandusk, could be found. Daikon knocked out the gnome once there, and summoned Arklem Greeth. Under the Archmage Arcane's supervision, an antimagic trap was set up to capture the gold dragon Palarandusk; Daikon himself acted as additional bait, disguised as a young gnome. Palarandusk turned up, and was soon caught.
