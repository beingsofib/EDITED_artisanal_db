Veoyh was a beholder inhabiting Veldorn during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Though he was certainly in a position to attempt to wrest power from Saed, an ancient vampire who ruled Veldorn at this time, he respected his rival's power enough to give the matter careful thought.

Relationships

Veoyh had a twin named Xaoch.
