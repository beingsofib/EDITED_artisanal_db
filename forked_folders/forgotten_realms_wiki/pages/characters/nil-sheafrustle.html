Nil Sheafrustle was one of the engineers of the Lantan-Samarach portal in the jungles of Samarlogh in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Nil was stout and too paunchy for a gnome. He was nervous and fidgety individual who continually checked and tested his equipment to ensure everything was in working order.

History

As of the , Nil Sheafrustle commuted from Lantan to the illegal logging operation in Samarach every morning. His tasks included camp's mechanisms maintenance. Nil belonged to a wealthy gnome family that invested into the operations, so he had personal stake in ensuring the portal stayed operational. In 1374 DR, Nil was authorized to pay bounties for killing nasty pests and more dangerous creatures that roamed the areas around the camp.
