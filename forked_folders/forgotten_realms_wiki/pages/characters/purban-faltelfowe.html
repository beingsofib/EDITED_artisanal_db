Purban Faltelfowe was a member of the Shadowmasters thieves' guild during the 14 century DR.

While the guild primarily operated out of the city of Telflamm, they maintained operations in a number of other cities in the east. Faltelfowe led the chapter located within the Thayan city of Escalant circa 1372 DR.
