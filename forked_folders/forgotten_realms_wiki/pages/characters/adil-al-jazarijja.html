Adil al-Jazarijja was a member of the House of Hanif tribe within the Haunted Lands in 1367 DR.

Activities

Adil was Sheikh of a smaller branch of the tribe near the Weeping Desert. His camp was attacked and put to the torch by raiders from Krak al-Niraan. Adil's daughters, Dalida and Aisha were kidnapped during the raid and taken back to the castle.
