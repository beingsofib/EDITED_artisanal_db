Ko Mei Kho was a Shou merchant in Kozakura in the 1350s DR.

History

Ko Mei Kho was a merchant and representative of the mercantile Ko clan in a town in Kozakura. In 1357 DR, on the request of his cousin the colonial governor at Akari, Ko Lei Kho, he hired an adventuring party to investigate an ochimo in Akari.

Personality

Mei Kho was honest to the point of tactlessness, as was traditional in the Ko clan, and honored any agreement struck.
