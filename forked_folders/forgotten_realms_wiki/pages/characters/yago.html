Yago was a wizard who lived in the city of Baldur's Gate during the mid−14 century DR.

Personality

He was an exceptionally vindictive and spiteful individual.

Possessions

Yago wore a knave's robe and kept a personal spellbook that was filled the specifications of various curses.

Relationships

He was married to the "witch" Brielbara.

History

Circa the , Yago's wife Brielbara had an affair with the adventurer named Coran. The two had a half-elven child called Namara. Yago cursed the baby and holed himself away at the Low Lantern gambling den in the Eastway district of Baldur's Gate.
