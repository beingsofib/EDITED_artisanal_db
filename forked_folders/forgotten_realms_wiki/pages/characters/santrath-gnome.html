Santrath (gnome)

Person
nameSantrath
homeAthkatla, Amn
raceGnome
occupationBaker

Santrath was a gnome baker of Athkatla, Amn.

Santrath made popular a sweetened bread with dough made with yeast and eggs, called santrath after the gnome.
