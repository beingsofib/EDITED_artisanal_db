Yestrel

Person

Yestrel was an assumed Sembian or Westgate conspirer against Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Rumors & Legends

In late Mirtul of the , upon his coming death, Osbur claimed to have orders from Olgarth Oldbridle in case of his death, calling on him to deliver a message for his heir, Elbert Oldbridle. In turn, the latter sent Osbur to lord Traevyn Illance to repeat the message, warning him to mind the perfidy of cabals from Sembian or Westgate, naming specifically Kormoroth, Yestrel, and the Lhendreths of Saerloon.
