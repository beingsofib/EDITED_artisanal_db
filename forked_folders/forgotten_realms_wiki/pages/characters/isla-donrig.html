Isla Donrig was a bard from the Moonshae Isles based in Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the late 14 century DR.

History

In the early 1370s DR, she composed an epic titled "The Unicorn's Blessed Crescent", which covered legends of the Delimbiyr Vale, including that of Phantoms' Cloister. Few knew from whence she received her information, however.

On Shieldmeet of the , she undertook the ceremony of the Green Regent in Loudwater and was accepted as a Scion of the Green Regent.

Soon after, in the month of Eleasis, Isla went on an expedition into the High Forest to investigate rumors of a group of wild elves who worshiped a dragon. A tenday later, she had not returned.
