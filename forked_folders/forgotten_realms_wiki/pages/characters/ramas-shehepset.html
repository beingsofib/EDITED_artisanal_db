Ramas Shehepset was a Mulan tavern owner inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Ramas owned the Angry Coxswain tavern located in the central section Waterdeep's Dock Ward along the waterfront where Odd Street joined Dock Street. He was secretly a slaver who worked with his brewer, Orth Ironchisel, to magically transport victims to Mulhorand via a portal found within the tavern. Ramas would employ Drejjd from the Unseen so that it would appear the victims had not disappeared.
