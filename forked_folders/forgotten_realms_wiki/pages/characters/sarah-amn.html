Sarah was an ambassador from Amn who served her nation in the lands abroad during the mid–14 centruy DR.

Personality

In contrast to her important position in Amnian foreign relations, Sarah had a rather poor opinion of the lands of the Sword Coast north of her home.

History

As rising tensions between Amn and the city of Baldur's Gate intensified during the Iron crisis in the , Sarah was dispatched to help negotiate peace. She arrived at the city some time before the attempted takeover of the Council of Four by Sarevok Anchev, staying at the Helm and Cloak in the Temples District of the Upper City.
