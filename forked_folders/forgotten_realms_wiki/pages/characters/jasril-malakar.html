Jasril Malakar was a carter inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Jasril was the guildmaster for the Fellowship of Carters and Coachmen circa 1357 DR. He held the title of "Master Carter" within the guild and worked out of the Road House—their headquarters on Carter's Way in the Southern Ward.
