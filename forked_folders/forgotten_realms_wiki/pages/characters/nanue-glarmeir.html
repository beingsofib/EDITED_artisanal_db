Nanue Glarmeir was a noblewoman of Hastarl in the early-to-mid 3 century DR.

Description

She had long hair.

Possessions

Her bridal gown was said to come from Shantel Othreier, and to have cost 1,000 gold pieces.

Relationships

Originally from the Glarmeir family, she married Peeryst Trumpettower.

History

During her bridal night in , Nanue Glarmeir and her newlywed husband, Peeryst Trumpettower, were attacked by clashing bands of thieves: the Moonclaws and the Velvet Hands, which led to a succession of shenanigans that ended in no consummation of the marriage, and the destruction of a unique bridal gift.

During the siege of Athalantar in , rushing soldiers injured her husband; trying to check on him, she was shoved with a sword, which damaged her arm, to the outrage of her uncle-in-law, Darrigo Trumpettower, who decided to join the fray. She was left with Peeryst, whom Perrigo instructed to bind her arm wounds.

Rumors & Legends

It was rumored later that her marriage had been calmly, heedlessly consummated while Darrigo Trumpettower fought off a dozen thieves for the full duration of the act, hurling them out of the windows to their deaths at the Trumpettower courtyard.
