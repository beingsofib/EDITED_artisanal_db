Breden Yesve

Person
nameBreden Yesve
sexMale
raceFey'ri

Lord Breden Yesve was a fey'ri with black fangs under the command of Sarya Dlardrageth. Sarya gave him command of the houses of Yesve and Ealoeth in order to keep Silverymoon at bay.
