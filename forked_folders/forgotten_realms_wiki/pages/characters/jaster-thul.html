Jaster Thul was a guild spokesman inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Jaster was the spokesman for the Guild of Watermen during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

His father Zzundar was the head of the Guild of Watermen.
