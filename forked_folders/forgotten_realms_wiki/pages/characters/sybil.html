Sybil was a Dark Justiciar and an Inquisitor of the Church of Shar, who lived sometime in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Sybil maintained a notorious reputation for her harsh punishment of thieves.

History

Inquisitor Sybil commanded Dark Justiciar forces stationed at Grymforge during Ketheric Thorm's war, operating from the Inquisitor's chambers in the Sharran dormitory. She was in charge of the assault on Moonhaven.

In her capacity as Inquisitor, Sybil extracted intelligence from detained Selûnites and Harpers, using a whip when interrogating captives. A Harper prisoner disclosed the position of a stockpile situated on a rocky cliff adjacent to Grymforge. Additionally, Sybil obtained the words "Lux Splenda", which was a passphrase for disarming the trapped chest within the stockpile.

Inquisitor Sybil possessed a sentient amulet of Lathanderite origin that carried a curse. When one of her subordinates stole the artifact from her chambers, they became afflicted with unceasing laughter. After several days, Sybil discovered the laughing thief, removed the amulet, and consigned it to the lava below, thereby silencing its victim's hysterics.

However, Sybil's assault on Moonhaven was fruitless. She had also mistakenly executed several loyal Sharrans under suspicion of espionage. Her critical failures forced General Thorm to personally assume command of the Dark Justiciars attacking Moonhaven, casting aside his disgraced Inquisitor.

Inquisitor Sybil's ultimate fate remained unverified. Two plausible scenarios included her execution by General Ketheric Thorm for her wartime failures, or death at the hands of Yurgir's merregons who continued occupying her sealed Grymforge chambers as late as 1492 DR.
