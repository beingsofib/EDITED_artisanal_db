Relona

Person
nameRelona
sexFemale
occupationAlchemist

Relona was the owner of the alchemy shop Relona's Potions in the market district of Luskan in the late 15 century.

Description

Relona had shoulder-length dirty blond hair.

History

Around 1487 DR, Relona sent her apprentice to fetch samples of the oozes that formed in the Luskan sewers.
