Tolar was a fighter of Bane in Shadowdale in the Year of Risen Elfkin, 1375 DR.

History

Tolar was the overseer of the gnoll mercenaries involved in the Castle Krag reconstruction work during the Zhent invasion of Shadowdale in 1375 DR.
