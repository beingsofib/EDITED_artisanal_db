Marsa Ferrentio was an adventuring paladin of Herm, a member of the Band of Iron, and the ruler of Mimph, in Sespech, circa 1370 DR.

History

Before his rise to power in the , Baron Aldorn Thuragar was the leader of the Band of Iron, an adventuring company of which Marsa Ferrentio was a spiritual guide. Along with fellow members Gavilon and Marcius, Marsa was established as the ruler of one of the Sespechan towns. Marsa Ferrentio of Helm became the ruler of Mimph. In the , she oversaw the construction of a grand temple dedicated to her deity – Helm.
