Izefia was a member of the Shadow Druids that dwelt within the Cloak Wood forest during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Like other Shadow Druids, Izefia demonstrated noted aggression against those who brazenly traveled through their woods without reason they found acceptable.

Rumors & Legends

He was aware of a local "woodsman", Peter of the North, who lived in a local cave capturing beasts for his own personal use.
