Miri Buckman was the resident cleric of Tyr of the Chapel of Resounding Justice in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

As the resident cleric of the chapel, Miri both presided to religious services, and provided magical support to the defenders if the temple was under attack.

History

Miri was of Tethyrian origin—her parents fled the kingdom during the civil war.
