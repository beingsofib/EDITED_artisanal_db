Saer Frunilla was an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

She was a human woman with a fair bronze skin tone, blue eyes, and a full head of red curly hair.

History

In the , Saer Frunilla was among those citizens of the Gate who were not convinced by the broadsheets and propaganda coming from the office of the new Grand Duke. The rulers promised safety from the encroaching army of the Cult of the Absolute, but Frunilla thought that they were as vulnerable as if they were outside the city walls. However, she was hopeful that when the time came, Lord Enver Gortash would fulfill his promises to the city.
