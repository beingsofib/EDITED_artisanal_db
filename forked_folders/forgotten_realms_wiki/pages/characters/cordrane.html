Cordrane the Recent was a necromancer in the employ of Sorcerous Sundries of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Cordrane was a gray dwarf with charcoal skin, shaved head, and a snow-white beard.

History

In the , Cordrane was employed by mage Lorroakan on the first floor of Sorcerous Sundries in the Lower City. He offered his necromantic skills to customers, selling reanimated creatures like zombies. Alice Althea, a wealthy Baldurian, was among Cordrane's customers. She was contemplating purchasing a newly created zombie of Cordrane's making.
