Pusavril was one of the many merfolk refugees in Myth Nantar in 1370 DR.

History

Pusavril was a weaver and netmaker. Her husband Traylir died in the last battle of the Twelfth Serôs War protecting the High Mages. Afterward, she resettled in Myth Nantar and started a net-making business there. She still felt the loss but the atmosphere of the city gave her hope and Pusavril decided to remained there for stability.
