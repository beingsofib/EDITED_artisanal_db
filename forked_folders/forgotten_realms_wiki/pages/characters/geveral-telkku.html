Geveral Telkku was a caravan captain from Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

An employee of the Red Boar Trading Coster, Geveral was the captain preferred by its owner, Deldron Rein.

History

Reportedly, on or near Shieldmeet in the , Geveral and a band of adventurers explored the ruins of Phantoms' Cloister in Southwood, either immediately before or immediately after the place "went phantom" and manifested its structure in full. Only Geveral came out again, and he was found wandering the woods north of the ruins. His mind was broken and he rambled incoherently of a "whispering light", a "dragon in the mind", "the Fallen One", and a "monastery of the Fallen One."

He died by his own hand three years later.
