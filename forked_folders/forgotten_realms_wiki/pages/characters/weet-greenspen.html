Weet Greenspen was an alchemist in the Moonsea in the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa 1486 DR, the doppelganger Tak, posing as Doctor Jubal of the wandering Doctor Jubal's Carnival of Curiosities, kidnapped Weet and forced her to brew alchemical items.
