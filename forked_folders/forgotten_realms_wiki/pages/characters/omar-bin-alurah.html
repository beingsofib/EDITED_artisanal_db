Omar bin Alurah was a powerful sha'ir and prince of Hiyal around 1367 DR.

Personality

Omar was a short near-sighted man who was obsessively jealous of his older brother Anjar bin Alurah. He enjoyed reporting Anjar's mistakes to his mother.

Activities

Though it was not common knowledge, Omar was connected with the Brotherhood of the True Flame. Both the Brotherhood and Omar desired to use the other for their personal gain then eliminate the other when the relationship had run its course.

Relationships

Omar was the second son of Sultana Alurah bint Asrah. His efreeti servant was Jalas.
