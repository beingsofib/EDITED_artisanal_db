Qarlegon was a pit fiend in service of Geryon in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

In , Qarlegon was sent by Geryon as the head of a sixty-strong force of devils against the Simbul while she scoured Avernus in search of Elminster Aumar, then a prisoner of Nergal. She slew the entire force by dropping a mountainside on them.
