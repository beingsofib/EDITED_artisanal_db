Winter Zulth was a half-elf living in Waterdeep and the regional agent of the Moonstars for non-Faerûnian locations in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR.

History

Winter was raised in Waterdeep and worked at the Horizon's Sails as a map-maker. He used his position for the Moonstars' cause by collating maps and logbooks from exotic places like Zakhara, Maztica, and Kara-Tur and producing unique maps that were extremely valuable to explorers and navigators. He could also provide a ship for the Moonstar's needs upon request.
