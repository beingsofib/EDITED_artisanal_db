Braeryl Barelder was a young Cormyrean noblewoman of the Barelder family in the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Her parents were Narnathra Barelder and Varkander Heldruth, and had her sister Maereenra. Her name was a old popular Barelder name for girls.
