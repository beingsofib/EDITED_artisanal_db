Turiel was a scourge aasimar cleric of Bahamut.

Description

A dark-skinned male with shoulder-length, white hair, Turiel's features were marred by gray-blue lines caused by his divine heritage. He wore ceremonial plate mail and a stylized mask representing his deity, Bahamut. His weapon of choice was a staff in the shape of a metallic dragon.

Personality

Turiel was an extremely devout and zealous adherent of Bahamut whose life revolved around holy missions handed down from his deity. He worked to rid Toril of lawlessness, chaos, and injustice.

Abilities

As a cleric in good standing with Bahamut, Turiel could cast several spells, including sacred flame. He could also channel divinity to produce effects such as order's demand and voice of authority.

Activities

When not carrying out crusades or inquisitions, Turiel resided in Candlekeep, where he conducted research and petitioned Bahamut for guidance.
