Maulygh was one of the magelords of the kingdom of Athalantar in the early 3 century. He was originally one of the Night Mages.

Personality

He was cruel and ambitious.

Biography

Maulygh was a master wizard from Unther who was hired by the prince Felodar Aumar in Calimshan to fight the magelords of Athalantar. However, Maulygh quickly joined the ranks of the magelords instead.

During a dinner in Hastarl in 229 DR, he was killed by the Magister after fellow Magelord Neldryn Hawklyn used a spell to fetch the man and tried to bind him, unsuccessfully, with the Crystal Chain of Binding.
