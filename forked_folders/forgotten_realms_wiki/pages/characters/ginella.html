Ginella was the chief of the staff of Helm Dwarf-Friend in Sundabar in 1373 DR.

Description

Ginella was a burly and severe elder woman.

History

In 1373 DR, Ginella greatly suspected the new companion of her lord, and she was right, because it was Aliisza in disguise. Ginella made it her duty to watch her.
