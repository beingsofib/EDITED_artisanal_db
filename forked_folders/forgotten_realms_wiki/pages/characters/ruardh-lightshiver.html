Ruardh Lightshiver was an elven Laranlor of Ardeep, and one of the founders of the Fallen Kingdom of Phalorm.

Relationships

Together with two other kings, Torghathar and Javilarhh Snowsword, Ruardh was one of the three kings of Phalorm. They had two dukes under their command.

History

Laranlor Lightshiver was one of the founders at the famous Council of Axe and Arrow that occurred in The Laughing Hollow, together with Torghatar blood of Bharaun and Javilarhh Snowsword. On the , this alliance with the shield dwarves of Dardath was forged; the combined region became Phalorm, the Realm of Three Crowns, in the .

Around the , his forces defeated the armies of Najara at the Battle of Blunted Fangs by assassinating the yuan-ti infiltrators sent south to the Reaching Woods by King Nejizar, dealing a defeat that forced these serpentfolk to retreat to the Serpent Hills.

He perished in battle

Legacy

The words of Ruardh Lightshiver during the founding were preserved in an enspelled king's tear gemstone kept in the Vault of Sages in Silverymoon.
