Starlaurynguldar was a wyrm black dragon. He slew the Magister Ohland Grethgar in , but died of his wounds in the same day.

Possessions

The dragon possessed an antimagic amulet as of his final fight.

History

Starlaurynguldar was known to have slain Ohland Grethgar, the Magister, in ; his victory was due to an antimagic amulet of sorts. However, Ohland's power was enough to destroy the amulet, and the dragon perished of his wounds not long after the fight.
