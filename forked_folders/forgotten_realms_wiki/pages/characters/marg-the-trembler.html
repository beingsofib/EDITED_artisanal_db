Marg the Trembler was a brigand from the town of Targos in Icewind Dale in the late 13 century DR.

History

Marg the Trembler was the second owner of the Lucky Knucky, a well-known, by the 14 century DR, lucky charm from the town of Targos. Sometime after the , Marg played cards against Young Ned, he first owner of the Lucky Knucky. To none's surprise, Marg cheated and won the game, claiming the young fisher's lucky charm. Soon after the game, the brigand changed the Lucky Knucky for supplies and fled Targos.
