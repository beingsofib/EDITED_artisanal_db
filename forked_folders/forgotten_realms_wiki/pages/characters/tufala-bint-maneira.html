Tufala bint Maneira was a half-elven rawun inhabiting the High Desert of Zakhara around 1367 DR who related the history of her desert tribe through her stories and music.

Description

Tufala has chestnut-colored hair.

Activites

Tufala inherited her duties as the tribal storyteller and historian from her elven mother. She took her responsibilities seriously. She was well-informed about current rumors of the High Desert.

Possessions

Tufala wore leather armor and carried an enchanted jambiya and a normal cutlass. She played her pipes of sounding to help relate her stories.
