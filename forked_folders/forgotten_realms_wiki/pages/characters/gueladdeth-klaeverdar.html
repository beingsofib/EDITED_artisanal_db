Gueladdeth Klaeverdar was the matriarch of the Klaeverdar family of Elturel in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

She bred goats that she trained to never ate anything containing paper, or anything at all indoors.

Relationships

Her most likely successors as of were Meldrethe Klaeverdar and Andrath Klaeverdar.

Possessions

She had six magically trained wolves, and dwelt in the ruins of Oldturret at Oldturret Farm, where she had stashed numerous spellbooks.

Rumors & Legends

Gueladdeth was said to scry for items up and down the Sword Coast, which she retrieved by teleporting. She was also said to transform disobedient family members into goats.
