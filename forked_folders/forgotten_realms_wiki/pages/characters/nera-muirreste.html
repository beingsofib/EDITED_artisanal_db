Nera Muirreste

Person
nameNera Muirreste

Nera Muirreste was the wife of Elvath Muirreste.

History

In 1374 DR, after Elvath's death in the Battle of the Cwm, Seiveril Miritar went to visit Nera in order to tell her how her husband died valorously.
