Taaki was Sauk's arumwon, or animal companion.

Description

Taaki had ochre lines painted into her flanks and the top of the head.

History

On Tarsakh 14 of , Taaki had been attacking livestock around the Shalhoond. After killing one shepherd and wounding another, Berun and Lewan were hired by the locals to stop her. They found lettering scratched into a boot track close to its own, spelling out "KHEIL". Berun continued alone, until he came across the tigress menacing a man. Taaki was distracted by Perch,

Taaki was slain on the night of Tarsakh 25. On finally swallowing Perch whole, the lizard slew her from inside.
