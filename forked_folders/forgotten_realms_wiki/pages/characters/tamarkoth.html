Tamarkoth was a Shadovar wizard of Returned Netheril who pursued the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll in the late 15 century DR.

History

When the Shadovar pursued the Nether Scroll that Oriphaun Huntsilver stole from Wheloon Prison in the mid-1480s DR, they assaulted and took over the Purple Dragons fort of Granwell Keep in the King's Forest. They then spread out over the High Road using hired mercenaries, contesting the Harpers of Rina Belostos for the scattered pieces. Tamarkoth and Azmagol were among those that studied and assembled the pieces, speculating on its function. Although they managed to finish the project, it was followed by the demon Razagnol being summoned and the Harpers arriving to kill everyone.
