Yampa

Person
nameYampa
raceWater buffalo
patron deityPadhra

Yampa was the name of a water buffalo that was considered holy in the Padhran religion.

Yampa carried the Enlightened One to "the realms of perfection"

By the mid–14th century DR, a room called the Hall of the Water Buffalo lay within the Horseshoe Temple in the Quoya Desert. It housed a large statue of Yampa.
