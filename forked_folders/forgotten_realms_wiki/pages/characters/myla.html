Myla was a winged kobold tinkerer in the Dragon's Rest cloister in the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Myla had two brothers, Mek and Minn.

Myla spent time with Laylee, who was serving as her helper. Laylee once handled Myla's alchemical fire and caused an incident in the cloister kitchen. Myla prohibited her from using it again.

Activities

Myla spent her time experimenting with alchemy, engineering, and magic. She often visited the cloister library and unlike most of the other kobold residents she was studious.

History

Circa the , Myla lived in the small monastic cell of the Dragon's Rest cloister cut into the cliff face. Her cell was cluttered with junk and tools. Just like all the other kobolds, she worked at night because of their sunlight sensitivity.

Once Myla, Mek and Minn were attacked by "hungry, icky, blood-sucking bat things" and her wings were severely maimed. The brothers left her for dead. Later, the elder Runara aided her recovery.
