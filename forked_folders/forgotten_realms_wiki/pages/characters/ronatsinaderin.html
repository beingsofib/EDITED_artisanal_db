Ronatsinaderin was an ancient green dragon who lived in Estagund. By the year , he had died of old age. In a conversation with the blue dragon Gerinvioch, the wizard Kinase Bronihim pretended to have slain him; Gerinvioch pretended to have been injured in a fight with him.
