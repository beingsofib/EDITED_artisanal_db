Ymezra was the daughter of Jessamine, one of the merchant princes of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

She was shy.

Relationships

Ymezra was born from a love affair between Jessamine and a sea captain from Amn. Her mother regarded her as the "fruit" of this relationship, and hired tutors to prepare her to one day take over the family business of overseeing the sale of plants, poisons, and assassination contracts within Port Nyanzaru.

History

Ymezra was born three years before Port Nyanzaru achieved independence from Amn.

She was twelve years old when the death curse struck and afflicted her mother, whose body began to rot.
