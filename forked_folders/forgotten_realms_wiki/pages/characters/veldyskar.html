Veldyskar was an intelligent basilisk living in Gravenhollow in the late 15 century DR.

History

A stone giant visitor to Gravenhollow donated Veldyskar to the keepers of the library to act as a guardian and general-purpose helper countless years before the 1480s DR.

Circa 1486 DR, the basilisk met an expedition led by some adventurers, who'd been hired by Bruenor Battlehammer to investigate the threat of a demon lord, at the gate of Gravenhollow.

Personality

Veldyskar's philosophy during fights was to petrify first and ask questions later. To avoid problems, the keepers taught him how to cast a greater restoration spell.
