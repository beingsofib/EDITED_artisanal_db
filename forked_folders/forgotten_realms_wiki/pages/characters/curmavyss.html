Curmavyss was a powerful wizard inhabiting Caer Callidyrr on the island of Alaron in the Moonshae Isles during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Curmavyss was a member of the Council of Sorcerers, whose primary task was to concentrate most of the magical power within the Moonshaes Isles into the hands of the High King.

History

Curmavyss was the first leader of the Council of Sorcerers, assuming the role when it was founded by the High King in the early 14 century DR. He held the position for about three decades before the arrival of Cyndre from Thay circa the , whom he invited to join the Council in order to keep an eye on him. However, Curmavyss would be dead by the , with Cyndre in control of the Council.
