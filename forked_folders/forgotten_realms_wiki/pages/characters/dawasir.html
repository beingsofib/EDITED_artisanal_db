Dawasir was a warrior of the Mtair Dhafir tribe of Anauroch that traveled the deserts during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He had a particularly deep voice.

Relationships

Dawasir was friends with Ajaman, son of the Qahtani sheikh.

History

Dawasir was present at El Ma'ra when the oasis was assaulted by asabi mercenaries in the employ of the Zhentarim.
