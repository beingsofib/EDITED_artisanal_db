Krenmore Toth was a gnome artificer in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Krenmore had pale pinkish skin and bright red hair, short on top and a beard with a chin curtain and forked braids. He wore a long robe and chipped breastplate for protection.

History

In the during the Elven Crusade, Krenmore joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp on the outskirts of Myth Drannor. He served as a trainer for other artificiers who passed by the camp.
