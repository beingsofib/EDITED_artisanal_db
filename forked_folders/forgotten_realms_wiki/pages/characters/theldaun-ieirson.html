Theldaun "Firehurler" Ieirson was an aging wizard, active in the early-14 century DR.

History

By the , Theldaun was an old man and experienced wizard. He harbored opinions that adventuring life was not a fun and exciting vocation. Instead, it was filled with danger, especially for wizards whose life could be ended with another mage's stray spell. Firehurler put his musings into a book – Teachings of an Angry Old Mage.
