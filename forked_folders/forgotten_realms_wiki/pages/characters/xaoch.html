Xaoch was a beholder inhabiting Veldorn during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

He was one of the most powerful creatures inhabiting Veldorn in the , but had a healthy respect for Veldorn's vampire leader Saed.

Relationships

Xaoch had a twin named Veoyh.
