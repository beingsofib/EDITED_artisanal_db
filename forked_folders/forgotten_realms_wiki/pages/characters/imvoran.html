Imvoran was a Zhentarim mageling of the mid-14 century.

History

By , Imvoran had been in service to the Zhentarim for a dozen years. Late that year, while in Manshoon's presence, he witnessed the spellfire-induced death of Drauthtar Inskirl, when the latter teleported to the High Lord's throne room, fatally injured by Shandril Shessair's spellfire. That was the first time he saw fear in Manshoon's face.
