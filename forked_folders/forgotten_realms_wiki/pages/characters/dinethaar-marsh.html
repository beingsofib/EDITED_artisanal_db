Dinethaar Marsh was a well-respected chef who worked for Duke Ulder Ravengard of Baldur's Gate in the 15 century DR.

History

Chef Dinethaar Marsh was the instructor of Chef Marjoram, who later would become the chef of Lady Jannath's Estate in the Lower City of the Gate. Later in life, Chef Marjoram would say that Dinethaar was an efficient but tough teacher.
