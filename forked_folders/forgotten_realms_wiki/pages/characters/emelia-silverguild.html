Emelia Silverguild was the daughter of a successful merchant inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Emelia acted like a spoiled brat.

Activities

She lived within southern Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter in Silverguild Manor.

Relationships

Her father was the successful merchant Malvred Silverguild and her mother was Eunice. Emelia's only sibling was her brother Meryn.
