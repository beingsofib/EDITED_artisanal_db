Belarian was a well-off elvish businessman and inhabitant of the City Core of Neverwinter in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Belarian was a proud and fair-skinned elven man with a full head of long, golden hair.

Relationship

Belarian held the Head Gaoler Captain Alaefin of the Peninsula District in high regard. He considered the inhabitants of the Beggar's Nest to be nothing but filth.

Activities

During the Wailing Death plague of the , Belarian could be found strolling the streets of the City Core, safe away from the dangers of the quarantined and zombie-infested Beggar's Nest and the convict-overrun Peninsula District. He was ready to offer advice and opinion on the city's districts to the Hero of Neverwinter. He also had some insight about the cure being assembled by Khelben Arunsun and disrupted by a recent attack on Neverwinter Academy, according to Belarian's trusted associate.
