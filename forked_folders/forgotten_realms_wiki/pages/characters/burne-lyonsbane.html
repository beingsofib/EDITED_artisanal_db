Burne Lyonsbane was warrior and a member of the Lyosnbane family that hailed from their familial keep.

Personality

He was known to be a thoughtful and caring man.

Relationships

Burne was one of the 6 generation of men born after Kyle Lyonsbane, the mercenary whose actions cursed the Lyonsbane family with lycanthropy.

He had a beloved nephew Kelemvor, son of his brother Kendrel. Kelemvor greatly admired his uncle, especially after being saved by him in youth.

History

When Burne learned of the death of Kendrel, he went searching for the teenage Kelemvor. He and his adventuring band found the boy isolated and alone after six months in his werepanther form,

After his death, Burne's exploits were known and remembered by for years other warriors and men-at-arms.
