Crown Prince Rythan of Tethyr was next in line to the throne of Tethyr. He destroyed the lich Jhaniloth Puiral of the Twisted Rune while she was in her lair. In retribution for this act, the Rune created Mulsparkh, and Rythan was killed in the Battle of Nightflames against its armies in 1334 DR.
