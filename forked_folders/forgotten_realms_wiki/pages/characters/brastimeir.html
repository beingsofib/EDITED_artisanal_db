Brastimeir, also known as Brastimeir the Bold, was a mage who challenged the Simbul prior to . She won, and Brastimeir perished.

Legends & Rumors

In , Elminster Aumar judged that the Simbul's slaying of mages, whether from Thay or not, had become excessive, and the feats of her power were tilting the balance of power around Aglarond. Amongst other mages slain, he counted Brastimeir the Bold.
