King Ugra Ngarl was an orc of the Skortchclaw tribe in the Spine of the World region of Faerûn around 1370 DR.

Activites

Around this time, he forced goblin slaves to mine for mithral underneath Fortress Eyegad which was previously a dwarven fortress. The mithral was being sold to a buyer in the High Forest.
