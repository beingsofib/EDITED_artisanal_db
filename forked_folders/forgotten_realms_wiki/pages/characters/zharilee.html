Zharilee was a sun elf citizen of Evereska

Description

The gold elf leader was stunningly beautiful, even for a member of the Ar-tel-quessir.

Activities

Zharilee worked alongside the Cloudtop Circle of elven high mages, coordinating the defense of the valley and lands beyond Evereska.

Relationships

She had a sister named Gvendor, who also lived in the city.

History

Zharilee was present in Evereska during a series of skirmishes that broke out between her people and the phaerimm that had recently been released from within the Sharn Wall beneath the Shaeradim in the .
