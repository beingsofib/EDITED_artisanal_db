Odamaron was a lich who kept his lair in the region of the Underdark underneath the Lands of Intrigue as of the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was supremely arrogant and believed himself much powerful than others he encountered.

Possessions

Odamaron was in the possession of several powerful items such as the Eye of Tyr jewel and the heart of the damned.

Relationships

During the Bhaalspawn crisis Odamaron allied himself with the drow spellcaster Sendai.

He was served by an apprentice lich and several skeleton warriors.

History

In the , Odamaron was destroyed by Abdel Adrian and his companions when they stormed Sendai's enclave in order to confront the drow Bhaalspawn.
