Fries was a powerful Netherese priest who taught at the Alchemical Seminary in Eileanar in Netheril shortly before its destruction in .

Activities

Fries was a master of the transmutation of elemental earth, meaning the conversion of stone and metal and similar substances from one form to another, and taught the subject at the Seminary under Quintus. Her last major project before the fall of Netheril was the conversion of gold to platinum, a process that turned much gold into little platinum, but one that she was steadily improving until her death.
