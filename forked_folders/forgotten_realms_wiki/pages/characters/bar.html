Bar was a clay golem inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the late–14 century DR.

Activities

Bar was a guardian within the Dancing Coin, a moneychanging house located in the Foreign Quarter on Zhent Avenue. He was controlled by Andalias via an enchanted ring.
