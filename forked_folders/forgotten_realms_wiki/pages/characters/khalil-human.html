Khalil was a desert rider and grandson of Sheikh Ibrin of the House of Hotek tribe in the Haunted Lands of interior Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Relationships

Khalil was a dutiful son who obeyed both is father, Jisaron, and grandfather. He made sure to stay out of the way of Jisaron's vizier Amene
