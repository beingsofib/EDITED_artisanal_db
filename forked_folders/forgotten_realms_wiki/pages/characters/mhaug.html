Mhaug was an annis hag shopkeeper in Skullport. She was the owner of Mhaug's in the Lower Port.

Personality

For a hag, Mhaug was cordial but her mind was focused on business.

Relationships

Mhaug was a close associate of Nightshade.

History

Around 1370 DR, Mhaug produced most of the undead labor force in Skullport for the right price. Her business greatly increased after the death of Shradin Mulophor in 1369 DR.
