Wilund

Person
nameWilund
sexMale
occupationAdventurer

Wilund was an adventurer who searched for the Magical Chessmen of Ultham-Urre some time before the mid–14 century DR.

Wilund came into possession of an antique spellbook called The Alcaister and gave it to the sage Ardagundus of Baldur's Gate in exchange for information on the Chessmen's functions and whereabouts. It is unknown if Wilund succeeded or not in his search.
