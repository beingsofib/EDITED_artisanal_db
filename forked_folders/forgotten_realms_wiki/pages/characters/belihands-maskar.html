Belihands Maskar was a stablemaster inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Belihands was the guildmaster for the Stablemasters' & Farriers' Guild circa 1357 DR. He held the title of "Senior Master" within the guild and worked out of the Guild Paddock—their headquarters on Walltower Walk in the Trades Ward.
