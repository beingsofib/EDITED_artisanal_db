Gorthrimmon was a member of the Cult of the Dragon in the mid-14 century DR.

History

In , while deployed to Orthil Voldovan's caravan from Scornubel to Waterdeep to shadow Shandril Shessair, Holvan and Brasker were under orders from Gorthrimmon to take Shandril alive.
