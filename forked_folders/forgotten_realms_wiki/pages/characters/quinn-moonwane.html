Quinn Moonwane was a druid in the Moonshae Isles as of the Darkwalker War. He was responsible for overseeing Llyrath Forest on the isle of Gwynneth.

Description

Quinn was a dark-haired and powerful druid who could see the true nature of evil creatures.

History

As Kazgaroth stirred on Gwynneth in the , Quinn was one of many druids who convened at Myrloch Vale to discuss how to counter the nebulous growing darkness in the Moonshaes.

Later, as the Northmen fleet of Khelgar Ironfist (secretly Kazgoroth in disguise) arrived to sack Caer Corwell, Quinn was one of three druids who came to the town's aid. He and his fellows first summoned gusts of wind to slow the Northmens' longships before relying on other spells to attempt to destroy the oncoming ships. However, upon locking eyes with the disguised Kazgoroth, Quinn's body exploded into a bloody mist, leaving only his clothes and boots behind.
