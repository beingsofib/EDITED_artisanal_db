Toraga was a human samurai who travelled the lands of Kara-Tur in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He traveled around mostly in an attempt to distance himself from what his brother Okuma had become.

Possessions

Wearing a robe of blending, a ring of regeneration, and a pair of bracers of defense he fought with two enchanted Sang kauws. In his possession he also had 5 potions of healing.

Relationships

His brother was once another samurai but their paths diverged once Okuma went mad and went on a killing spree during a fit of rage.
Unknown to Toraga he was being actively hunted down by his brother, as Okuma had put all his insane blame over his disgrace on Toraga.
