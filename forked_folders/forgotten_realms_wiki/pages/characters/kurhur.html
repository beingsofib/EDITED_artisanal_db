Kurhur

Person
nameKurhur
homeBralizzar, Dustwall Mountains, Durpar
occupationTent seller

Kurhur was a salesman in the town of Bralizzar who specialized in tents. They were known to be active in the mid-14 century DR. Their shop was a tiny one nestled south of the cloth shop on the eastern side of the city's bazaar.
