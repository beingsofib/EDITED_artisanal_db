Aerlyse was a half-drow psion and a member of Bregan D'aerthe as of the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Only those with intelligence and charm could impress Aerlyse.

History

Aerlyse was a student of Kimmuriel Oblodra, the powerful psionic second-in-command of Bregan D'aerthe.

In the , Aerlyse was sent along with other Bregan D'aerthe operatives to secure an encampment in the ruins of Castle Never, and from there to explore the crypts and spy on the city of Neverwinter. In addition, Aerlyse had been given a special mission from Kimmuriel to spy on the captain of the Bregan D'aerthe camp, Xalbyn, and did so dutifully.
