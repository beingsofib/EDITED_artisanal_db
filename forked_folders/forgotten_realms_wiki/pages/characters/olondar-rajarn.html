Father Olondar Rajarn was the proprietor of the Oak-Father's Boon, the best inn in Mosstone, Tethyr, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Olondar Rajarn was an elderly man, kind and friendly. He was beloved by all Mosstonians and he kept his inn filled with friendly and pleasant faces.

History

Father Olondar was a priest of Eldath. At one point, Olondar's inn was visited by Volothamp Geddarm, who wrote a raving review of the Boon in one of his published guides.
