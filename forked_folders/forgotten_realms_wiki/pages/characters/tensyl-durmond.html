Tensyl Durmond was a messenger that lived in the city of Arabel as of the mid–14 century DR. At one point he was employed by Iardon's Hirelings.

History

The young Tensyl had a brief encounter with the future-god Cyric, when the latter was still a mortal, in the . Cyric paid the boy one gold piece for the successfully-delivered message from his traveling companions at the Pride of Arabel inn.
