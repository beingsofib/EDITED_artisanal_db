Rhondethar Windrider

Person
nameRhondethar Windrider
homeWestgate, Dragon Coast
occupationMage

Rhondethar Windrider was a mage who was held in the dungeons of Westgate on the Dragon Coast in the early 14th century DR.

Rhondethar was forcibly extracted from the dungeon by the famed mercenary Brandon some time before 1356 DR, who cut through many city guards to get to him.
