Bunt Chugley was a common folk visitor to Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Bunt was a dark-skinned human man with brown eyes, He wore a cloth hat and a simple brown tunic.

History

In the , an army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in the Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldur's Gate, displacing countless refugees in the process along the way. It was during this refugee crisis that Bunt Chugley was visiting the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. Bunt's business trip to the metropolis left him overwhelmed and he could often be seen walking down the streets of the Lower City whilst mumbling to himself reminders not to make eye contacts and not to engage with the locals. During his time in Baldur's Gate, Bunt was a guest of Nortale's Hostel.
