Bryn Kernwyld was a druid of Chauntea who inhabited the Moonshae Isles. She was known for creating several spells unique to her faith.

History

Although the precise time period during which Bryn lived was unknown, sages agreed that it was likely anterior to 1000 DR. She was known as the author of Earthmother's Weapons, a book containing several unique spells: bone talisman, frostbite, horrible taste and wolfskin.

She created those spells to help her brethren during the wars which opposed the forces of Malar and Bhaal and the civilized people inhabiting the Moonshae Isles. They proved a great help to minimize the damage wars caused to the surrounding nature.
