Jan Buckman was a young beginner mage, son of the village Mayor Lazlo Buckman, from West Harbor in the Mere of Dead Men circa 1374 DR.

Description

Jan was was a nervous young man who found himself unable to properly concentrate of his magic studying and his well-worn spellbook. He was a shy and somewhat awkward individual.

History

Jan Buckman studied under the village mage, Master Tarmas circa the . Jan looked up to his father and was impressed by the Mayor's leadership and the deals he'd made to keep the village safe. When the town and its brewery became threatened by a duo of black dragons, Tarmas was captured by the wyrms, leaving the young apprentice worried for the master's fate and West Harbor's safety.
