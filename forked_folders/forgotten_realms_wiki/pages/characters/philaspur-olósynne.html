King Philaspur was the fourth monarch of the Olósynne Dynasty of Aglarond in the late 12 century DR. He reigned from 1194 DR to his death in battle in 1197 DR.

History

Following the death of his elder brother, King Elthond, in the Battle of the Singing Sands against the Red Wizards of Thay in the , Philaspur succeeded him as king of Aglarond.

He died in the , during the Battle of Brokenhands against the Thayan people.

Relationships

He was the son of Althon "the Old" and the younger brother of Elthond.

His daughters, who would rule the nation after his death, were the twins Thara and Ulae, more commonly known as the Gray Sisters.
