Annicole Meloura was the wife of a trader from the Demiplane of Dread. He cursed her with his dying breath because of her unfaithfulness.

Relationships

She was married to Yassad Meloura.

History

While the trader she was married to, Yassad, went on long trips, Annicole had taken a lover without his knowledge. One day he returned home, he caught the two of them in bed; her lover and husband fought to the death of both, and then her husband pronounced a death curse on her with his last words, which turned her into a werespider. She fled to a nearby cave; the unborn child she carried was turned into a morass of spiders, bearing her curse. At some point, a bear entered the cave, and she fought it to the death.

Legacy

The spiders that had once been her unborn child remained inside the cave, alive; they behaved as normal spiders, but their bite carried lycanthropy. At some later date, they bit Sundian Silgathen and Jebbrie Vokasich, turning the first into a werespider and placing the latter in life-threatening danger.
