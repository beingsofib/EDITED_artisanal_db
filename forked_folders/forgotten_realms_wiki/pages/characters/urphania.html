Urphania was a vile and powerful hag who led the unseelie fey of Citadel Umbra on the Isle of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles as of the late 15 century DR.

Activities

From her citadel deep in the forest of Winterglen, Urphania directed her forces of evil fey, shadow creatures, and undead against the fey kingdom of Sarifal, hoping to seize control of that realm from her hated enemy, the High Lady Ordalf.

History

Once a dreaded inhabitant of the Feywild, Urphania was banished from that plane by the Court of Stars, including High Lady Ordalf. Swearing revenge against Ordalf, Urphania made her way to Gwynneth where she became the mistress of Citadel Umbra sometime before .
