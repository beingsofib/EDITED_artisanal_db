Wrilda Gemfinder was a patron of the Elfsong Tavern in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Wrilda was a rock gnomish woman with a light skin tone, hazel eyes, and long brown hair gathered in a bushy ponytail. Her haughty demeanor and quality clothes suggested Wrilda to be a woman of means.

History

In the , Wrilda was a frequent customer of the Elfsong Tavern in the Eastway neighborhood of Baldur's Gate. She could often be found dining whilst sharing a table with her elvish friend named "Ears" Oohna. Wrilda believed that other visitors of the Elfsong were provincial yokels who could not afford the establishment's patronage.
