Va'aunsh Avithoul was a member of the drow House Avithoul of Sschindylryn in ancient days.

History

Va'aunsh was a son of the Matron Va'arrej Avithoul, and he won a measure of fame and respect by inventing the first of the drow slave collars after the Great Revolt. House Avithoul went on to use derivations of this collar to control and profit off slaves, earning them a place of prominence in the city of Sschindylryn.

The collar that he created was placed on display in the House Avithoul estate, proudly displayed alongside Knee-bender and the skull of his mother Va'arrej. When Cormyrean forces counterattacked during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, they raided the estate and freed their slaves. They may have also destroyed the relic as final jab.
