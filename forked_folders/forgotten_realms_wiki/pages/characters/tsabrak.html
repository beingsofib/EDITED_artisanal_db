Tsabrak was a drider in Menzoberranzan in the late 14 century DR. He was one of Houndaer Tuin'Tarl and Syrzan's rebel group.

History

Tsabrak was part of a drider colony living in tunnels near Menzoberranzan.

During the Silence of Lolth in the , Tsabrak and others driders joined Houndaer's group in the hopes of a better future for their fellows.
