Lyrele Hethcanter was a secretive agent and spy in service of the Crown of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

History

Lyrele Hethcanter's profession ensured that much of her life was shrouded in mystery. One thing was known about the woman – she served the Crown in some undercover fashion. Some speculated that, in reality, she was a Highknight of Cormyr.
