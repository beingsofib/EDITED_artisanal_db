Samuel Thunderburp was a retired adventurer and the innkeep of the Five Flagons in Athkatla, during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Samuel had a reputation as a being one of the greatest fighters in all of Highdale.

Personality

He was a friendly man who offered a warm welcome to those who entered his establishment. He liked to boast that the Five Flagons offered every beverage that could be found throughout the entirety of Faerûn.

Relationships

Samuel ran the Five Flagons with his partner Thalia.

He was well-acquainted with a number of Amnish adventurers. Among his friends the brave Truesword Mazzy Fentan, as well as the actor–adventurers Haer'Dalis and Raelis Shai.

History

Before he settled down in Athkatla, Samuel spent considerable time as an adventurer. In the end, Thalia managed to pull him away from that life and find one that was considerably less dangerous.
