Gariena was a druid at the Scarlet Moon Hall during the Elemental Evil crisis in 1491 DR.

History

Gariena was one of the many druids deceived by the Cult of the Elemental Flame in order to unwittingly help the Rite of the Wicker Giant.

Personality

Gariena sensed that the rite at the Scarlet Moon Hall would be a great event but wondered why it needed such a long preparation time and why she had never heard before about this rite.
