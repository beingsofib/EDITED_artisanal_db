Tohil was a fisher supervisor who worked in the docks of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Pilos Lafrett, a lazy yet industrious fisherman, invented a device of pulleys and balanced weights that would do the fishing on its own, allowing Pilos a stress-free nap. He informed his boss Tohil of the invention and that it would be producing labor-free halibut, but asked him not to touch the invention, while cockily also asking for a raise.
