Kromes was the sahuagin ruler of the underwater city of Vahaxtyl in the empire of Aleaxtis within the Alamber Sea during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Kromes was an absolute tyrant who sunk any ship that dared enter sahuagin waters. He was a bane to Thay, Mulhorand, and Unther alike and conducted frequent raids on Messemprar and Mourktar.

Abilities

He had the powers of a shaman and carried many magic items.
