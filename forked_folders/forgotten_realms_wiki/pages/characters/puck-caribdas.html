Puck Caribdas was an elven acolyte of Tymora and seller of potions in the town of Leilon as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

Puck was physically delicate with a great deal of nervous energy.

Activities

When the Tymora Shrine was erected in Leilon during Dagult Neverember's efforts to rebuild the town, Puck assumed the duties of running the temple. In addtion, he began selling potions to the townsfolk and to visiting adventurers, and was the only source of potions in town. He did not brew his merchandise, but instead received deliveries every tenday, usually of no more than four vials of any given type of potion.
