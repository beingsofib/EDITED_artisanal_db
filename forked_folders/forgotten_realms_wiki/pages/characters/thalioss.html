Thalioss was an author and devotee of Tyr the God of Justice. Based on the conditions of Thalios' writings, it was presumed the author lived long before the 15 century DR.

Activities

One work attributed to Thalios was Moonsea Cults, a book that described numerous cults active in the lands around the Moonsea.
