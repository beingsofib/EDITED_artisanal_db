Yashiira was promising young wizard inhabiting the city of Yhep along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

She was quite rambunctious and wild, and often a strain on her master Zalazel Harauntan.

Activities

Originally from the local Talu-min tribe, Yashiira came to Yhep to apprentice with the skilled wizard Zalazel.
