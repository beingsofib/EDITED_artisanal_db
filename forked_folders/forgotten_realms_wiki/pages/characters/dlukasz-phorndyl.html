Dlukasz Phorndyl

Person
nameDlukasz Phorndyl
homeNorthshield House, Triboar
occupationInnkeeper

Dlukasz Phorndyl was the proprietor of the Northshield House inn in Triboar in the Dessarin Valley in the late 14 century DR.

He was running the inn by 1365 DR and it remained in the Phorndyl family for over a century.
