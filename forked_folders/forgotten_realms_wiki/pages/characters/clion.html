Clion was a young fighter and thief inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Clion joined a group of warrior-thieves who called themselves the Sword Ghosts.

History

He was originally from Luskan.

Relationships

Clion was life-long friends with Raymid. Other close associates of his included Arilyn Moonblade, Olophin, and Tomed Myntion. Ariyln was impressed with his raw skill with a blade and sent him to the Academy of Arms to hone his skill further under Kymil Nimesin. Clion joined the City Watch of Waterdeep to repay Kymil for the training.
