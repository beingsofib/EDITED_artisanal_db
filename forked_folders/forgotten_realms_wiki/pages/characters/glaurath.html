Glaurath, who was nicknamed the Great, was a warrior and the fourth King to rule the city-state of Westgate.

Activities

Glaurath inherited the throne when the previous King, Mather, was slain by black dragons in . He reigned until . He served as Captain of the city guard before his rise in power.

Relationships

Glaurath was a close and personal friend of King Mather.
