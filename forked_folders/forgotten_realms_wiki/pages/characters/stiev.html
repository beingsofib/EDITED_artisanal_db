Stiev was the unfortunate nephew of the druid Rand, who searched for him in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

History

At some point Stiev vanished, prompting the elder uncle Rand to begin traveling far and wide in search of him, armed only with a crude drawing of the young hin. This dogged determination and desperation allowed the unscrupulous master mercenary Dom to lure Rand into his group of sellswords, promising that he could help find Stiev in exchange for Rand's service. Though this was almost certainly a falsity, it was enough to get Rand to participate in the fighting on Greenweald Lane against the Harpers, though Rand was sensible enough to surrender and abandon the cause before dying for it.
