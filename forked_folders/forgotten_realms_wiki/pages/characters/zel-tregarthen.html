Zel Tregarthen was a high-ranking member of the Brotherhood of the Cloak in Mulmaster.

Relationships

Tregarthen was an internal investigator for the Cloaks, tasked with rooting out corruption.

History

In 1491 DR, after discovering a mole inside the Cloaks, Zel started an investigation. During his investigation, he interviewed an adventuring party, but at that moment the cultist assassin Little Wing killed him, framing the adventurers.
