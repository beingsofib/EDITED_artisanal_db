The Winter Werewolf was a peculiar werewolf with the qualities of a winter wolf that prowled the High Road of Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

The werewolf had pure white fur and seemed to emanate coldness.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, the Winter Werewolf openly lead a group of half-orc bandits along the High Road and the eaves of the King's Forest, thanks to the Purple Dragons withdrawing to focus on defending Eveningstar.
