Valon was the military leader of the centaurs of Guard in Bloodstone Barony in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Valon was known for this raw but precise intelligent with a cold and logical mind, making him perfect for the military.

History

As War Chief, Valon served on Guard's Council of Elders alongside Chief Kiros and Elder Shinar.

In 1358 DR, Valon and his fellows met Gareth Dragonsbane's party.
