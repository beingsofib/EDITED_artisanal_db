Beealtimatuche was a pit fiend servant of Asmodeus.

Beealtimatuche was summoned in 1462 DR by the lich Valindra Shadowmantle in Gauntlgrym to fight Drizzt Do'Urden and Bruenor Battlehammer. Bruenor lost his life while destroying the pit fiend.
