Tharden Rockseeker was a dwarf in the late 15 century DR. He was one of the three dwarf brothers who rediscovered Wave Echo Cave.

Possessions

Rockseeker owned a pair of boots of striding and springing.

History

Tharden was one of three Rockseeker brothers attempting to re-open the lost mine near Phandalin.

This dwarf was slain by forces under the command of Nezznar in 1491 DR.

Relations

He was the eldest brother of the Rockseeker Trio, being older than Nundro Rockseeker and Gundren Rockseeker.
