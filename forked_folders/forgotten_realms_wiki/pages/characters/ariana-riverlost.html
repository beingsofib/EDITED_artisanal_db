Ariana Riverlost was a wizard member of the Harpers in the Dessarin Valley during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

In 1491 DR, Ariana and her two half-elven scout companions Elifar and Lorendil were sent to the Halls of the Hunting Axe in order to stop Gargosh Blusterhelm from reclaiming the legendary axe Orcsplitter.
