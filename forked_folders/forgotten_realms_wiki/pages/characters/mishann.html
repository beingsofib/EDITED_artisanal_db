Mishann, also seen as Mithann, was a cleric of Amaunator in Bryn Shander in Icewind Dale in the late 15 century DR.

History

Mishann was an adventurer from Cormyr. When she became too old to fight evil, Mishann retired to Icewind Dale. However, she did all she could for good-hearted people of Icewind Dale.

In the , facing multiple threats to the dale, Mishann recruited adventurers to perform some missions for her.
