Thendara Spearsinger was a shield dwarf of the dwarven city of Earthfast in the Earthfast Mountains of north Faerûn in the 14 century DR.

She was a captain in the hearth guard, and said to be a fierce fighter.

Thendara was the mate of Olam Chistlesmith and the mother of the bard Morgalla the Mirthful. From a young age, she taught her to fight with a staff.
