Clara was an inhabitant of Port Llast in the late 14 century DR.

History

Circa the , Clara worked at the Alliance Arms Inn was a waitress. She was in town when shambling undead creatures started appearing on the streets of Port Llast late at night. However, danger didn't stop locals from drinking, including old Eommar, and Clara needed to make coin.
