Kelvin Blackstaff was a person of unknown race and age who had a statue in memory of him on the second level of Undermountain as of 1372 DR. The inscription on the statue related unflattering facts about him.

His relation to the Blackstaff title is unknown. It is possible that the statue was a joke at the expense of Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun and that Kelvin Blackstaff never existed, being the creation of Halaster Blackcloak, the architect of Undermountain.

Description

According to the inscription on his statue he had cruel intentions, foul breath, and flatulence.
