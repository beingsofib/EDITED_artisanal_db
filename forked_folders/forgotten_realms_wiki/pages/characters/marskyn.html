Marskyn was an inhabitant of Westgate in the mid-8 century DR.

History

In the , Marskyn had doubled the pay offered to mercenaries for stopping Saeraede Lyonora, on behalf of the Duke of Westgate. He still couldn't find any more people in the city willing to take the job, however.
