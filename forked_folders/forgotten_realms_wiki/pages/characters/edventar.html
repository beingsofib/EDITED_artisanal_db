Edventar was a reef hunter-scavenger from South Faerûn, the namesake of Edventar's Gift, who lived in the mid-14 century DR.

History

Edventar gained his notability in history for his help in routing Yenandra Hazm'cri, the Pirate Queen Dambrath in the . This act saved the lives of the local sea elves, Edventar's life-long friends. Edventar's Gift was an enchanted ring that was presented to Edventar after the Queen's fall as a gesture of gratitude by the aquatic elves.
