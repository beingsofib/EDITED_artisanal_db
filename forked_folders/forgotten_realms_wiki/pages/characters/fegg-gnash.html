Fegg Gnash

nameFegg Gnash

Fegg Gnash was a bloodforge commander who operated out of the Realm of Fire in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

From this domain, Fegg Gnash raided the surface lands, forcing peasants to flee their homes in fear of the rumblings in the earth. Coming across a group of refugees, the commander of the Legendary Campaign (which sought to unify the wartorn Utter East through conquest) ventured into the Realm of Fire to put a stop to it in a bloodforge battle.
