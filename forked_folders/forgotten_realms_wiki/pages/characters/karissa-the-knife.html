Master Thief Karissa the Knife was a skilled rogue and senior officer of the Night Brigade gang in Wheloon Prison in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Karissa had pale skin, gray eyes, and short black hair, and wore a suit of black leather armor with a cutout on her chest. She spoke with a rough, informal voice that indicated a lifetime on and under the streets.

Personality

Karissa was aloof and solitary regarding the wider world and even towards the rest of the Night Brigade, and considered her tunnel network and gang of thieves the only important thing in her life. She exceptionally greedy and covetous, and her bedroom was also her treasure vault, and she slept in her bed with gold coins trailed from her main pile of coins. She was happy to lair in her isolated hideout and wait for visitors or invaders to struggle their way in to find her.

Possessions

Karissa's hideout and home was an opulent mansion filled with paintings, fine furniture, sculptures, alcohols, shelves full of books and scrolls, and miscellaneous trinkets and artworks. Inside her bedroom/vault, she kept many treasures including a huge drow tapestry from Sschindylryn, Cult of the Dragon masks, a statue of Amaunator, ornate weapons, and of course massive piles of currency and gemstones.

Among Karissa's treasure were her special goggles, the poniard of pain, echoes of night, tunneler's toolkit, and packrat's rat pack.

History

As the best thief in the Night Brigade, Karissa operated a thieves' guild of sorts in Wheloon Prison. Once secure in her position, she set up an opulent mansion for herself and blocked it off from any regular access. The only way to get in was to traverse a lengthy series of underground tunnels and chambers, filled with traps and monsters that she laid out for visitors. When the Riverside Rats came for their revenge against the Night Brigade, they sent allies ahead to bypass the hazards and face the deadly rogue. Surviving such threats as a ballista and otyugh, the invaders finally found Karissa in her lair and slew her.
