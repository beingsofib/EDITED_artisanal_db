Halbryn Dark Stalker was a drow that served High Priestess Syndrith of the Temple of the Spider within the Northdark. She commanded a squad of assassins that defended her with their lives.

Possessions

She wielded a single longsword in battle, but was known to use throwing daggers to great effect.

History

Halbryn was killed in the , when the Heroes of Sleeping Dragon Bridge stormed the Temple of the Spider to put an end to Priestess Syndrith's plot to reform the Weave under Lolth's control.
