Nabil bin Najib was the heir to the House of Asad tribe in the High Desert of west Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Personality

Nabil seemed to have inherited his mother's wisdom and his father's strength. He was intelligent, friendly, and trusting.

Activities

As of 1367 DR, Nabil began recently assuming his father's duty of taking a yearly trip to Tajar to trade.

Relationships

Nabil was the eldest son of the tribe's leader, Najib bin Kamal al-Asad, and his wife, Alia al-Jamal umm Nabil.
