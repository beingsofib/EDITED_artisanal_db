Rose was a worker at the Herald's Horn in the mid-3 century DR.

History

In the , she was serving at the Herald's Horn tavern the night that Elminster Aumar purchased a wyrmtongue scepter from Surgath Ilder. She dropped a plate of hot tarts on her own toes at the sight of the wealth being traded in the tavern.
