William Ray was a traveler along the High Road between Neverwinter and Luskan during the late 15 century DR.

Description

William was bald and beardless.

History

Some time around , William joined the merchant's caravan of Tollin and his crew going from Neverwinter to Luskan, accompanied by members of the Order of the Burning Dawn. However, his beloved pack horse Sarah went missing during the journey, and after the caravan was attacked by mercenaries, the Champion of the Burning Dawn learned that Sarah had been killed by giant spiders.
