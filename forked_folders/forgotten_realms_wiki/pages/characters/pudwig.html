Pudwig was a grimy but successful beggar with a peculiar outlook on his profession from the city of Westgate in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Pudwig was a scungy human man of middling age. He had pale skin and a blonde beard, and hair. Pudwig was dressed in filthy rags when begging for coin.

Activities

Pudwig was a strange man who eagerly took to begging as a profession and was not forced into it by happenstance. He collected a high amount of coin, more than seven hundred pieces per day. Pudwig did not understand why others would not want to take on begging as a career path. He got to relax all day while folks tossed coin into his hat with no bothersome interactions with people. He thought begging was a dream job as long as one did not mind grime and rags. Luckily, Pudwig always hated hygiene and fancy clothes.

History

In the , Pudwig spent his day collecting coin from passersby on the city's Harbor Loop.
