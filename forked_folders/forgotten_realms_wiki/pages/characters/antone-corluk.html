Antone Corluk was a footman under the command of Belvedere Huntsilver of House Huntsilver, who visited the town of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Antone was a human man with chestnut hair.

Personality

Antone was a loyal servant of his house, but despaired eternally at Oriphaun's shenanigans as he got into disaster after disaster while living as an adventurer.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Antone and his master Belvedere asked adventurers to find and assist their young charge, the nobleman adventurer and relic collector Oriphaun Huntsilver. To their endless consternation and concern, Oriphaun found himself at the heart of a plot to attack the city of Wheloon by the shadowy forces of Returned Netheril. Afterwards, they tracked him as he got entangled with the druids of Druid's Deep, walked the High Road with the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll, and disappeared into the Storm Horns to fight the Shadovar.
