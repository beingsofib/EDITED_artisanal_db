Avgar Filroy was the only innkeeper in Noanar's Hold until sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

Activities

Courageously, Avgar managed the White Hart Inn with the help of some unseen servants for the cleaning. He was a little delusional and always warned customers not to disturb the Hunt Lords or go out of the inn in the night.
