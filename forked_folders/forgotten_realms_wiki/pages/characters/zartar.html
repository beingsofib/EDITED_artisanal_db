Zartar was a chain devil that dwelled in Avernus at least for a time during the Faerûnian year, 1492 DR.

History

At some point, Zartar visited the landmark on Avernus known as Sundered Chains, accompanied by two barbazu bodyguards, Narg and Yangor. The chain devil believed that by performing a certain ritual to Asmodeus at the site, great strength would be rewarded.
