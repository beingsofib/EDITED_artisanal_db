Erian was a soldier in Caer Corwell in service to the royal Kendrick family of Corwell during the early 14 century DR.

Description

Erian was a huge man with strong corded muscles.

Personality

As a veteran of Corwell's guards, Erian despised peacetime and enjoyed the thrill of battle.

History

After arresting Daryth in 1345 DR, Erian entered the Festival of the Spring Equinox to drink Callidyrr mead at the tent of Geoffrey and met the lovely Meridith. Later that evening, Erian laid down with Meridith, who was actually the Darkwalker Kazgoroth in disguise, and as their lips touched, the monster stole Erian's life energy and replaced it with an evil presence. He then became Kazgoroth's unwitting servant.

Under the next full moon, Erian involuntarily transformed into a werewolf and butchered a family of halflings in Lowhill. He awoke the following morning in Corlyth Creek with no memories of the incident but covered in blood and muck. When he finally returned to himself after several days, he remained in the wilderness as a self-imposed exile. He contemplated killing himself, but Kazgoroth's will would not allow it. He thus lived off the land, slowly going mad and giving in to his bestial urges. When he once more assumed his werewolf form at the next full moon (during the Summer Solstice), he chose to remain as a wolf indefinitely.

Acquiescing to the destiny that Kazgoroth wished for him, the wolf Erian sought out The Pack, one of the Earthmother's "children" comprised of thousands of wolves. Finding them, Erian swiftly killed the mighty leader of The Pack and assumed control of it. This continued for many days until he was challenged and slain by Tristan's prized moorhound, Canthus, who was able to injure and eventually kill the werewolf thanks to wearing a torque of the Goddess as a collar.
