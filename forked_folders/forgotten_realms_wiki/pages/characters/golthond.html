Golthond was a merchant from the nation of Thesk during the mid–14 century DR.

History

In Flamerule of 1357 DR, a caravel that Golthond owned, named the Fair Venture, went missing while it was sailing near the Pirate Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. In response, Golthond sought out mercenaries to rid the region of the pirate menace.
