Ormsel Eltunarr was a member of the Harpers in the Eastern Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

He was a caravan-master who specialized in running fast wagons. He was based in Mulhessen, but his activities ranged throughout Sembia and into the Dalelands.

Relationships

He was a friend of Mestrel Hawkmantle, a fellow Harper.

History

In the , Ormsel assisted Mestrel Hawkmantle by communicating the identities of several Zhentarim agents discovered by the latter to Harpers in Shadowdale.
