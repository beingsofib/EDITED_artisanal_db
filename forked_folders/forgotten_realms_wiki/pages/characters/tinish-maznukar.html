Tinish Maznukar was a member of the Gatekeepers in the world of Eberron.

Description

Tinish was a half-orc woman with slate-gray skin and long dark hair.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, Gatekeeper Tinish was one of many who arrived to investigate the Spinner's Prison following the escape of the Spinner of Shadows. They found the Rift Between Worlds, a massive portal to the Demonweb Pits, and struggled to understand its nature while attempting to prevent it from threatening Eberron. She discussed the matter with Jirmisk Zaremikan, a fellow druid.
