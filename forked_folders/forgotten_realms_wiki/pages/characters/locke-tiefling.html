Locke was a tiefling man from Elturel during the late 15 century DR. He was outcast from his city following after it was briefly pulled down into the Nine Hells, and forced to find new home elsewhere.

Description

He had pink-red skin and medium brown hair..

Relationships

Locke and his partner Komira had a daughter named Arabella.

History

In the , the Elturian tieflings were forced out of the city as refugees, forced to find a new home somewhere on the Sword Coast. They were fortunate to find temporary safe haven at the Emerald Grove, by grace of Master Halsin. That security was ended however when a druid named Kagha tried to seal away the grove from the tieflings, by means of a ritual.

While at druidic enclave, Locke and Komira's daughter Arabella tried to steal the idol of Silvanus from the druids, in order to nullify the ritual. Arabella was taken by Kagha and her allies, and forced to answer for her misdeed. Locke, Komira, and several other tieflings tried to take Arabella back, but were unsuccessful, and turned away by druids of the enclave.
