Essafah al-Jamila was a sha'ir and shopkeeper who lived in Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Activities

Essahah's shop, called Items for the Immortals, catered to genies and other sha'irs.

Essafah specialized in the creation of genie prisons, usually in the form of a lamp or bottle, though he claimed he could fashion a genie prison out of any container. He kept three such prisons within his shop. They contained a slayer genie, an efreeti, and a noble dao and sold for 2,000 dinars.

Relationships

A small fire gen served as Essafah's familiar. Since he was terrified that an angry genie would one day track him down and slay him, a pair of guardian genies served as his loyal bodyguards.
