Rarimmon Tlarn, known as the Archwizard of the Castle in the Clouds, was a mage who was active several centuries before the mid–14 century DR.

Rarimmon fought the infamous "spellslinger" Durshult the Doomweaver and was victorious. Afterward, Durshult was polymorphed into the humiliating form of a wingless wonder. For some decades after, it became a fad among mages to trap their defeated foes in wonder form.
