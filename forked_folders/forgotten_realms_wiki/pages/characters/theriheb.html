Theriheb was a paladin of Osiris in Mulhorand in 1357 DR.

History

In 1357 DR, Theriheb went to participate in the tournament held in Mourktar in Threskel to choose the new king. He managed to finish among the tournament's finalists.

Personality

Theriheb was an honorable man and a fierce warrior.

Relationship

Theriheb was a cousin of the current incarnation of Osiris.
