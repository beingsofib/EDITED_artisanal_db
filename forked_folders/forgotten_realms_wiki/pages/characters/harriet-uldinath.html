Harriet Uldinath was a human blacksmith of the town of Triboar in the Dessarin Valley in the late 15 century DR. She was the owner of the Uldinath's Arms smithy.

Activities

She sold fine weapons that were stamped with the Uldinath family glyph, which meant they typically sold for 25% more than the typical price.

Relationships

Harriet was the great-granddaughter of the business's founder, Like him, she maintained a friendly rivalry with fellow smith Ghelryn Foehammer, owner of Foehammer's Forge, which was situated across from the road from Uldinath's Arms. She had known the old dwarf since she was girl.
