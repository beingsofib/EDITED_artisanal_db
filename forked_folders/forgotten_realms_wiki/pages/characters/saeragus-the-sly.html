Saeragus the Sly was an inhabitant of Cormyr some time before 1367 DR.

Declared outlaw by the laws of Cormyr, Saeragus was trapped at Spear Rock by a Purple Dragons patrol. He managed to take down every one of them with his bow and arrows, thanks to the enhanced archery skills afforded him by the shrine of Tempus.
