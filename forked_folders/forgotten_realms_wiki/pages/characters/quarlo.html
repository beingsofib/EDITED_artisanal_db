Quarlo was the innkeeper at the Winsome Repose in Zazesspur in 1366 DR.

Description

Quarlo was a small man, wiry and thin. He had dark hair and beady eyes. He wore an apron.

History

When the civic guard of Zazesspur came to arrest—for a third time—Zaranda Star, who had been staying at Quarlo's inn, he notified the guards that she had left the city.
