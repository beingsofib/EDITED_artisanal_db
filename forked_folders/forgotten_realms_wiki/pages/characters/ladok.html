Ladok was an efreetikin who worked within the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Activities

Ladok assisted his master, the sha'ir Wadi'a bint Sali, in her duties to the Grand Vizier.

Relationships

Ladok was Wadi'a's familiar.
