Fat Gorin was a cook and member of the dwarven Clan Battlehammer that dwelt in Kelvin's Cairn in Icewind Dale in the late 15 century DR.

History

Fat Gorin cooked the feast that was served to celebrate the reunion of the Companions of the Hall in Kelvin's Cairn in the , by request of Stokely Silverstream.
