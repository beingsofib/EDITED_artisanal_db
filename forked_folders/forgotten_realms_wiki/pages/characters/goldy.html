Goldy was one of the horses that belonged to the noble Pharaboldi family of Arrabar circa 1373 DR.

Description

Goldy was a gentle and smaller horse with a palamino coat, a tan-golden color.

History

Goldy and the other Pharaboldi horses were cared for by Turcan, the family's stablemaster. In the , Goldy was saddled up to be ridden by Emriana Matrell, the unwilling object of Denrick Pharaboldi's affections. The young couple was to take a ride around the Pharaboldi family estate and partake in a picnic.
