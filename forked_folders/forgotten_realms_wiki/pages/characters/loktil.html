Loktil was a human warrior of Maztica in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He had shared everything with his cousin Takto since childhood.

Possessions

Like so many other Maztican warriors, he fought with nought but a shield and maca.

Relationships

After training and fighting side-by-side with his cousin and friend, Takto for years, they had developed their fighting style to harmonize with each other, and became quite the formidable team.
