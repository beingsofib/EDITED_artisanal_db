Miba sitt Lelia was in charge of the Gulf Gate of Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Relationships

Miba was the mother of the current imam of the mosque of Selan. She was also a devoted servant to Grand Caliph Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir's father.
