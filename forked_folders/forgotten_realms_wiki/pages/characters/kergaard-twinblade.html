Kergaard Twinblade was the seneschal of the Mulan Dynasty before becoming the King of Westgate.

Activities

Kergaard served as a loyal seneschal for King Ashtukzu II. Following the death of Astukzu II in the , and months of civil strife since no heir was apparent, Kergaard was elevated to the position of King by Westgate's nobility. He ruled until the , when he died of plague.
