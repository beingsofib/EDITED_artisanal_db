Lord Lathamp was a noble based in the village of Elupar in the Vilhon Reach in the late 12 century DR.

History

In the , a thief stole Yornar's Trail Companion, holy Mielikkian spellbook from the ranger Rhighaermon O'Antlers and sold it to Lord Lathamp. In response, Rhighaermon assembled some half-dozen other followers of Mielikki and mounted a daring raid to steal it back from Lathamp's palace. The rangers dueled the Eluphan guards along the battlements and in the galleries as they successfully reclaimed the tome from the palace vault.
