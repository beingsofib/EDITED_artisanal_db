Zillah was the lamia queen of the ruins of Unthalass in Unther in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Zillah was a devoted follower of Graz'zt.

Activities

Zillah ruled over the lamia inhabiting the ruins of Unther's former capital.

History

In the , Gilgeam and his army arrived in the city from the lands of Shyr in the world of Abeir. In the , Gilgeam, thanks to his pact with Graz'zt, reclaimed rulership of the city from the lamias.

Later Gilgeam sent Utu and Zillah to steal Nanna-Sin's divine spark. However, Utu died and Zillah was captured and put in jail.
