Togarr was a member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Togarr was a human woman with a fair skin, and gray eyes. She wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist: a helmet, a set of chainmail, and a tabard adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia. She wielded a halberd.

History

In the , Fist Togarr was stationed in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, where she patrolled the streets near the Blushing Mermaid.
