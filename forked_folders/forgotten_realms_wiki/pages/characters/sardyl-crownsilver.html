Sardyl Crownsilver was an apprentice of Vangerdahast in the mid-14 century DR, also his scribe and messenger.

Description

She was curvaceous but slender, tall and graceful.

Relationships

She trusted Vangerdahast thoroughly, and was in turn trusted by him.

Abilities

She did not have much in the way of guard spells, but she was graceful and quiet.

History

She was Vangerdahast's scribe and messenger in Alturiak of , when Bolifar Geldert went missing. She had given the man the key to the room where he was to write his report, and sought him out for Vangerdahast afterwards, when the latter's patience ran out. She was the one to discover the empty room, with no trace of Bolifar Geldert.
