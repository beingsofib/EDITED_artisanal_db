Stokely Silverstream was the leader of the dwarven Clan Battlehammer that dwelt in Kelvin's Cairn in Icewind Dale.

History

In his position as leader of the Clan Battlehammer dwarves, Stokely sometimes met with various humans, including Darby Snide, at a tavern in Bremen.

Shortly after the spring equinox of 1484 DR, Stokely was one of the first people, beyond the Companions of the Hall themselves, who learned of the rebirth of Bruenor Battlehammer. He threw a feast in Bruenor's honor, and swore to not divulge the secret beyond the halls of Kelvin's Cairn.

Later in 1484 DR, Stokely was apparently killed by Tiago Baenre during the Battle of Kelvin's Cairn when Tiago's forces attacked Clan Battlehammer. In truth he remained a prisoner of House Xorlarrin until 1485 DR when was freed after the dwarven victory in the War of the Silver Marches.
