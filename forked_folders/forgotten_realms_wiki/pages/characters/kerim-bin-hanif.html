Kerim bin Hanif was an ambassador from the House of Hanif living in Huzuz, the City of Delights, around 1367 DR.

Description

Kerim dressed in expensive clothing purchased in the markets of Huzuz. His keffiyeh was fastened with a white gem and adorned with a feather from a peacock.

Relationships

Kerim was the nephew of Sheikh Kaldun bin Hanif of the Haunted Lands. Kerim was intent on courting Alara bint Kira.
