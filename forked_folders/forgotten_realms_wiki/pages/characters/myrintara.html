Myrintara of the Masks was a Harper sorceress of the mid-14 century DR.

Description

She was tall, with large green eyes and glossy black hair.

Abilities

Myrintara had a unique ability to work spells of concealment stronger than most.

Activities

She operated a pleasure house in Zhentil Keep. It was a palatial home.

Relationships

She'd been a lover of Mirt at some point.

History

Myrintara had been a lover of Mirt "before he got fat", in her words, strongly implying it had happened over half a century prior.

In late , Mirt took Shandril Shessair, Belarla and Oalaerone to Myrintara's establishment through the sewers, looking for a way to hide from the Zhentarim, at least temporarily. Though she wasn't all too enthused to see him, she did cast a spell of concealment on Shandril before they parted.
