The Huntress was a demonic humanoid archer who was linked to the fortress of Watcher's Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Abilities

She possessed the inherent resistances and immunities that were typically found among fiends, including a form of natural regeneration.

Activities

The Huntress and her companions served as one of the three groups connected to the fifth level of Watcher's Keep, which held the final seal that kept hidden the Imprisoned One. The group was summoned to the keep when the heart key was used to unlock the seal.

Possessions

She wielded the unique +4 longbow known as Taralash.

Relationships

Her allies included the demons Nalmissra, Xei Win Toh, and Y'tossi, along with the drow Ameralis Zauviir, and an unnamed hive mother.

History

The Huntress was killed in the , when Abdel Adrian and his companions delved into Watcher's Keep at the behest of the Knights of the Vigil.
