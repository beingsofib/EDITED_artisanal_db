Sarndaen Dalabar was the Magister from to . He became known as "The Opener", for his work on documenting portals.

Personality

Sarndaen Dalabar often played the part of a shy, bumbling apprentice with barely any ambition. His greatest passion was exploration.

Abilities

Besides being amongst the most puissant magisters, Sarndaen Dalabar was an excellent actor.

History

Before his challenge of Meldryn Jalensifer, Sarndaen Dalabar had been the apprentice to three wizardly masters in Athkatla, all of which eventually embroiled themselves in mercantile intrigues that became their undoing. After his challenge, he spent a few days in Meldryn Jalensifer's company, prior to finally slaying him with three meteor swarms.

Once the Magister, he worked to keep the most powerful mages opposed to each other, so they wouldn't turn their attention to him; he fulfilled his task to spread magic by giving magic to a few promising apprentices and calling on them to spread magic on his behalf. Then he, personally, devoted himself to exploring and making a map of all the gates he came across. Besides filling up books, he created the Opener's map, in a castle within the Ice Mountains; his work was cut short when he died in an avalanche after crossing through an unrecorded portal.

Rumors & Legends

Sources stress that though there was nothing left down about Sarndaen being responsible for the downfall of his three previous masters, there would be nothing recorded either way.
