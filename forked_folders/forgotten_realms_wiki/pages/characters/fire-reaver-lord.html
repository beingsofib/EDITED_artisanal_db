The Fire Reaver Lord was a powerful fire reaver in the world of Eberron.

History

The Fire Reaver Lord prowled the underworld of Khyber on the road to Tarath Marad near the Spinner's Prison, leading a gang of lesser fire reavers. They preyed upon the Lords of Dust cultists that attempted to gather dragonshards nearby there during the Spinner of Shadows' escape attempt, leaving nothing but charred skeletons behind.
