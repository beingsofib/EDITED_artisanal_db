Filar was a settler in Chult during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Possessions

He had an axe, and a sword, which he ended up using once his axe was lost.

Activities

He was one of the men trying to settle in Chult.

Relationships

Filar had a brother who lived on the coast, who had a run-in with a faerie dragon on the spring of .

History

Filar was one of the men involved in the settling of Chult in the . He persisted in trying to get a camp up near the River Olung, despite a severe, magical storm that scattered his tools and damaged many of his belongings. However, he moved his camp somewhere far from that place after, during a fight with a strange mudman (actually the disguised old mist dragon Cirrothamalan), he discovered a faerie dragon. Remembering that faerie dragons were relentless, tireless pranksters, he reconsidered his choice of camp.

Rumors & Legends

They misidentified Cirrothamalan as a mudman, but once it fell to all fours, they still misidentified as likely a "mudbear".
