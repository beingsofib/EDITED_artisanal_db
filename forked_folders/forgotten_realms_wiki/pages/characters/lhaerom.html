Lhaerom was a guard in the House of the Ladystar, a temple to Mystra, during the mid–8 century DR.

Description

He was a head taller than the Bright Master, high priest of the House of the Ladystar, who was in turn quite tall.

Personality

Lhaerom had great poise.

History

Lhaerom was one of the guards at the House of the Ladystar during the chaos of the Art in the . About two tendays into it, he helped keep a mob away from the temple, and accidentally offended the high priest inside as well. However, he then gave the priest, at the moment over his head with the situation, a good idea on how to proceed. He also gave indications that he had participated in at least one siege.
