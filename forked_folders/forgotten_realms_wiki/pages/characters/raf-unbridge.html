Raf Unbridge was a wizard of the Twelve in the world of Eberron.

Description

Raf was a human man with short light-brown hair.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, Raf came to investigate the Spinner's Prison and Rift Between Worlds after the Spinner of Shadows escaped, working alongside Ygrid Fastheart and Jir Winmyr. He was especially concerned about the size of the Rift and the power it contained.
