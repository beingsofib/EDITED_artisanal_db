Baron Vjurn Blacktower was the head of the clergy of Velsharoon within the Crypt of the Arisen Army in 1367 DR.

Activities

Vjurn Blacktower was a necrophant–a specialty priest of Velsharoon. Blacktower led a group of clergy called the Cryptskulls who haunted Skull Gorge and the neighboring Battle of Bones.
