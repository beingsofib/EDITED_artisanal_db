Ayriisha was a paladin of Corellon that lived in the city of Neverwinter during the late 15 century DR.

History

At some point during or before the , Ayriisha aided the drow ranger Imbross Irri'Karr in defeated a group of assassins sent by his grandmother to kill him. The unlikely couple hit it off while fighting for their lives and found new love in the face of death. They became betrothed to one another and were set to be wed in spring the following year.
