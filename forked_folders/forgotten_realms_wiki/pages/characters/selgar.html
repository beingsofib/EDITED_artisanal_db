Selgar was a merchant-king of Sembia during that nation's past.

History

The city of Chancelgaunt was renamed Selgaunt in Selgar's honor. He was buried within an ornate tomb rumored to be filled with wondrous magic items and treasure. Several ambitious thieves and adventurers discovered that Selgar's tomb was also laden with deadly traps.
