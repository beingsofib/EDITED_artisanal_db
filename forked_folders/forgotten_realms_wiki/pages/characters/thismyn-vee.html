Thismyn Vee was an elfish resident of Eveningstar in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Thismyn had pale skin and brown hair in a long braid.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Thismyn visited the Lonesome Tankard with Olma Westrin and opined that the Old Sage would be a fine ally in such times.
