High Moonmistress Julith Harkspur was the high priestess of Selûne at Moonshadow Hall in the city of Yhaunn in the late 14 century DR. She became High Moonmistress after an adventure with fellow priestess Feena Archwood.

Description

She had pale skin and dark hair.
