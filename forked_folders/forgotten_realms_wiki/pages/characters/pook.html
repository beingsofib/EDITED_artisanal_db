Pook was a pasha and leader of the Pook guild in Calimport, the capital of Calimshan.

History

In the , he ordered Artemis Entreri, "Pasha Pook's prime assassin", to retrieve an enchanted ruby pendant back from the halfling Regis who'd stolen it.

Possessions

Pook was briefly given Drizzt's magic panther, Guenhwyvar, as a token from Artemis Entreri, as he had a love of exotic cats. He also had a dangerous portal to other realms called the Taros Hoop.

Abilities

Pook was deadly with a crossbow.

Relationships

He made many allies to increase his power and influence in Calimport. One example was the wererat guild led by Rassiter. He also had various contacts outside Calimport that he employed for various missions such as the wizard Oberon and the pirate Pinochet.

Home

Pook lived in a villa known as Pook's Palace until his death in 1357 DR. The estate was located in Sarkh Drudach of Sholeh Sabban in the Khanduq Ward of Calimport.

Description

Pasha Pook was tall, muscular, and fancily dressed.
