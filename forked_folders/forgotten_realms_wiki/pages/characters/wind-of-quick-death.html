The Wind of Quick Death was a pirate who sailed the Golden Gulf in 1367 DR.

Activities

The Wind of Quick Death attacked merchant ships traveling along the Southeastern Sea Route until he was successfully defeated by the Admirality of Huzuz.

History

Aided by a pair of djinn, ships from Huzuz attacked and killed the Wind of Quick Death upon the open waters. His head was returned to Huzuz and the Grand Caliph rewarded the captains involved with a magical cutlass.
