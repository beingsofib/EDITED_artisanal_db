Ghengal was a fighter roaming the hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He strove to get the approval of his father, and thus took on near any mission regardless of the dangers involved.

Possessions

He wielded a scimitar of speed and a shield, and wore padded armor, boots of the north, and a ring of regeneration.

Relationships

He was the son of a noyan in the Grand Army of the Tuigan, but the latter never truly approved of his son regardless of his actions.
