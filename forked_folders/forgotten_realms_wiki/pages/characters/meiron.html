Meiron was a hin druid and student of the adventurers' Neverwinter Academy in Neverwinter during the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

Meiron was a classmate and part of the same graduation class as the person who would become the Hero of Neverwinter.

History

Meiron had been one of the Neverwinter Academy students for some time in the , during the deadly outbreak of the Wailing Death. During the graduation ceremony the hin was a part of, alongside the Hero of Neverwinter, the infamous attack on the Academy took place that took the lives of many students and instructors. Meiron was among the defenders who stood against the attackers. It was unknown if Meiron survived the battle or was fell by an enemy sword.
