Malden Col was a follower of Caelar Argent and a spy who operated for the Shining Crusade in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was a devout follower of the Shining Lady's message and a convert of her proselytism.

Possessions

Malden kept a notebook that detailed specifics about the Coalition forces, with particular notes about their strengths and weaknesses.

History

During the war of the Shining Crusade, Malden managed to infiltrate the Coalition's war camp. During his mission, he was able to "borrow" the military code book belonging to Simonetta Twoedged.

While the camp was stationed a short distance away from Dragonspear Castle, the treasure-hunter Safana accidentally pick-pocketed Malden's notebook. Not knowing which of the soldiers from which she stole was the book's owner, Safana was unable to accurately identify the spy.
