Thaerlune was an inhabitant of Ripplestones during the mid–8 century DR.

Relationships

He had a sister.

Rumors & Legends

During the , Thaerlune was mentioned at a tavern in Ripplestones as part of a discussion about The Slayer, a strange entity that was devouring all life near the town. It was brought up that Thaerlune had picked up and moved elsewhere, after claiming he was visiting his sister, who was too sick.
