Phontyr Wonderspell was an archmage who lived in Elturel in the Western Heartlands was long deceased by the mid–14 century DR.

It was said that Phontyr knew a sorceress who was an ally, lifelong friend, and possibly even a lover of his. However, she became polymorphed and stuck in the form of a unicorn forever and Phontyr was unable to free her from magic more powerful than this own. This was but one story given for the mysterious apparition of a unicorn at the place where his house once stood.

After Phontyr's death, his house burned down in circumstances considered mysterious. It later became the site of the inn named for him and his friend, Phontyr's Unicorn, by 1358 DR.
