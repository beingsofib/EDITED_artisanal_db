Gerard Densim was the proprietor of Fine Fabrics shop in the Upper City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Gerard Densim did business with the criminal organization of Little Calimshan – the Guild. He paid for the organization's thugs to stand guard over the shop, protecting it from the dreaded Stone Lord, a new kingpin allied with the Cult of the Absolute. As the guild war grew more severe, The Guild Bursar Uktar raised the protection tithes. If unpaid, the guard would be withdrawn from Fine Fabrics. Gerard wrote a politely pleading letter stating he could not afford the guard to be removed and asked if it would be a good compromise for the Guild to accept a letter of credit from Fine Fabrics in addition to a twenty-percent discount for the Guild's guard (so they could look presentable while standing guard) and a fifty-percent discount to the guildmaster Nine-Fingers Keene, the city's fifth and unforgotten duke.
