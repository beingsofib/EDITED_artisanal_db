Jhessa Brightstar was a priest of Lathander in the town of Idyllglen.

Description

A descendant of Solndor Brightstar, Jhessa was just as dutiful and humble a servant of Lathander.

History

Jhessa was present in Idyllglen when it was nearly entirely destroyed by Yeenoghu and his band of gnolls and demons. She led her congregation in prayer, imploring Lathander to send the angel Zariel to save the town as she did once before.
