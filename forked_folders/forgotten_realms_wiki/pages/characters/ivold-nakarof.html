Ivold Nakarof was a priest of Bane, serving in Zhentil Keep.

Ivold's older brother died in the Battle of Falling Wasps at the hands of the Red Plumes and in 1373 DR he decided to enact his vengeance upon them. After traveling to a cemetery near the road between Hillsfar and Yulash, Ivold started casting animate dead to raise an army of fallen soldiers with the intention to unleash them on the Red Plume forces.
