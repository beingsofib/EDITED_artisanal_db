Velorn was a famed hero of Turmish, active in the nation's early days.

History

Velron made his name as a warrior of Turmish, who loyally protected his homeland from roving orcish warbands that often threatened the Heartland of the Reach. His heroics inspired a Tumishian village's name – Velorn's Valor. The hero's huge and mundane-looking shield was proudly displayed in the village's town hall into the late 14 century DR.
