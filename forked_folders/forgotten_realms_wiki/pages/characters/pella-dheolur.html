Pella Dheolur was a member of the Dheolur noble house of Cormyr. She was the sister of Lord Dheolur.

Description

She was of slight build. Her palms contained fanged mouths with oozing green lips.

History

She was killed in 432 DR by a blue ball of fire cast by Amedahast during the attack on house Dheolur by King Duar Obarskyr.
