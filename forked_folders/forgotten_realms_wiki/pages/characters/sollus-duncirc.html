Sollus Duncirc was a city guard Captain of the Flaming Fist mercenary company in the late 14 century DR.

History

In the late 14 century DR, Sollus Duncirc was stationed in the Upper City, near the Watchful Shield of Helm and the Purple Wyrm Inn and Tavern. He greeted adventurers seeking employment with a welcoming demeanor, offering them directions to the Purple Wyrm, where there were adventuring jobs on offer. On behalf of the city of Baldur's Gate, Sollus Duncirc offered reward for monster extermination jobs in the Sewers of the Gate, where oozes, giant rats, otyughs, giant beetles, and other carrion beasts bread like wildfire.
