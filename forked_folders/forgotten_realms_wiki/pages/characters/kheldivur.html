Kheldivur

Person
nameKheldivur

Kheldivur was a veteran battle-mage. At some point prior to the , he was killed somewhere in Chessenta during a war between wizards in sight of a young Maldiglas Turntower. Glittering rings remained on his hand. The young man snatched what he could from Kheldivur's charred corpse before it cooled down, kickstarting his own career as a wizard.
