Maglor the Apothecary was an agent of the Zhentarim during the mid 14 century DR. He served within the Black Network in Zhentil Keep both before and after the rise of Fzoul Chembryl as the leader of the Black Network. His direct superior within the organization was the Head of Caravans, Meer.

History

He was a spy in Espar in Cormyr, where Maglor was killed in 1355 DR by the Knights of Myth Drannor.

Relationship

In Espar, Maglor was an underling of Whisper. He had a son, Maethlin, who like his father worked as a Zhent spy.
