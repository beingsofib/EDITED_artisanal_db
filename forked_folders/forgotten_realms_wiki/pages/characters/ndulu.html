Ndulu was a balor demon of the Abyss.

Relationships

Ndulu was the greatest of the demon lord Eltab's balor lieutenants.

History

Ndulu forged the Moaning Crown of Ndulu in the 8 century DR in his lair beneath the Earthfast Mountains.

He was general of a demonic army driven back to the Abyss by the Paladin Princes of Impiltur at the Battle of Moaning Gorge in the . Prince Nord, wielding the sword Demonbane, forced Ndulu himself to flee the Prime Material plane.
