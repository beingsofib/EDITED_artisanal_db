Skoona was a surly half-ogre who worked as a bouncer in the Elfsong Tavern during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Despite her severe appearance, Skoona was a lonely soul. She sometimes lamented her job, saying that people often forgot of the hearts of those with huge muscles like hers.

Possessions

Skoona wore a set of chain mail with a maroon tabard. She carried her two scimitar whilst patrolling the Elfsong Tavern.

History

Skoona guarded the Elfsong alongside a suit of animated armor the staff referred to as Klank circa the . The pair never bothered to stop altercations between clientele and only really protected the inn's other staff members.
