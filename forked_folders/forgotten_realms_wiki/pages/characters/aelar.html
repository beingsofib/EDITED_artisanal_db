Aelar was a druid of the Emerald Grove circa the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Aelar served under Archdruid Halsin and for a time, the secret Shadow Druid Kagha.

History

In the , Archdruid Halsin left the Emerald Grove left his people on a journey to investigate the goings on of the cult of the Absolute. When Kagha was put in charge, she immediately tasked Aelar and the other druids with commencement of the Rite of Thorns, a ritual that would hide the grove away from outsiders and protect them from the growing threat of goblinoid raids.
