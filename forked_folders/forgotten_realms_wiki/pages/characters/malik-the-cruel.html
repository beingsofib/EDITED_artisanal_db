Malik the Cruel was an Iron Consul of Bane who served the Cult of the Absolute in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Malik was a human man with a light skin tone. He wore a hellish-looking plate mail and a horned mask, reminiscent of a devil.

History

In the , the Cult of Bane joined forces with the Cult of the Absolute, led by the Chosen of Bane, Lord Enver Gortash. Iron Consul Malik the Cruel was placed in the Steel Watch Foundry, guarding its Gondian slaves in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate.
