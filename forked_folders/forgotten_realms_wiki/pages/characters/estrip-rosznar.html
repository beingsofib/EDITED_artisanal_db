Estrip Rosznar, who was nicknamed the Young Masked Lady, was a noble inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

She resembled Lhestyn—an Open Lord of Waterdeep who was called "the Masked Lady." Estrip enjoyed wearing masks as part of her costume for dances.

Activities

Estrip was part of the Rosznar noble family of Waterdeep. She lived within Rosznar Villa on Thunderstaff Way in Waterdeep's Sea Ward.
