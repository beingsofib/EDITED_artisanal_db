Barker was a war dog living in Marigold's Menagerie in the mid-14 century DR.

Description

This large dog has a black coat of fur.

History

While living in Marigold's Menagerie, Barker was being trained by Marigold Mufflefoot to be a guardian and companion for a nearby elderly soldier who had lost his leg in a battle.
