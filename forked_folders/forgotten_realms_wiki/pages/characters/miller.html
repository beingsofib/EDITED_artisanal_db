Fist Miller was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Miller was a gold dwarf man with pale skin color, shaved face, and light brown hair. He wore a set of chainmail armor decorated with a tabard embroidered with the Flaming Fist insignia and a simple uniform helmet. The dwarf wielded a halberd when on duty.

History

In the , Fist Miller was stationed in the Basilisk Gate's barracks and prison, serving under Blaze Liara Portyr and Manip Seyama. He sternly stood guard at the Basilisk Gate's offices, letting through those who had a crime to report.
