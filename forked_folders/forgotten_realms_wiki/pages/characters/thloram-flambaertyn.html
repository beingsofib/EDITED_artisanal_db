Thloram Flambaertyn was an adventurer of the Brightstar Sash in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was an older man, with a tufted beard.

Personality

He was easy on the jokes.

Abilities

He made an amazing hotspice stew.

Activities

He was an adventurer.

Relationships

He was a member of the Brightstar Sash.

History

Thloram was already a member of the Brightstar Sash when they recruited the sorceress Caladnei, and he cautioned against letting her join too swiftly, as she was young. She was ultimately allowed to join, and during one of their forays together, the Sash recovered a treasure of at least 6,000 gp.

When Vonda and Rimardo attempted to rob a nobleman's house at Waterdeep only to be captured and slain, Thloram sough out Caladnei and warned her to escape. In their getaway, Thloram had to fight a large, enraged mob.
