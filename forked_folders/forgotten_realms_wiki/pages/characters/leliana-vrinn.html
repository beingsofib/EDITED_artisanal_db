Leliana Vrinn was a drow priestess of Eilistraee, a protector of the Promenade of the Dark Maiden and the mother of Rowaan Vrinn, the head priestess of the Misty Forest shrine.

Along with the other followers of Eilistraee and drow of Miyeritari descent without Wendonai's taint, she was transformed into a dark elf during Flamerule in the by Q'arlynd Melarn's High Magic and the grace of Eilistraee. Grief-stricken about the death of Eilistraee at the hands of Halisstra Melarn, she killed the Lady Penitent with her (now silent) singing sword.
