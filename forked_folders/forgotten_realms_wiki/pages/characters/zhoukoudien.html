Zhoukoudien, known as the High One, was a ruler of the batrachi civilization of Merrouroboros during the Days of Thunder ( to ).

The batrachi grew to the height of their power under Zhoukoudien's wise leadership. However, their golden age ended abruptly when the High One was killed in battle by Omo, a titan thane, around . The batrachi lord Bazim-Gorag later called Zhoukoudien a fool who unwisely made war against the Jotunbrud and lost the empire.

The High One shared the same name as the batrachi realm of Zhoukoudien.
