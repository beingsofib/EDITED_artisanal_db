Therfus Handydoer was a wizard in Luskan and the second of Ship Rethnor in the mid–15 century DR.

History

In 1463 DR, he led an ambush against Drizzt and Dahlia just after they entered the city. He hoped to avenge Supreme Captain Borlann of Ship Rethnor, who'd been killed by Dahlia. However, in the end, the ambush failed because of the intervention of men of Ship Kurth. Beniago Kurth threatened to kill Therfus from behind, forcing the wizard to retreat.

Relationship

Therfus served under four different Supreme Captains, but really only liked Borlann.
