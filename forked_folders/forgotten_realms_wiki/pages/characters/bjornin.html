Bjornin was a brave paladin who lived on the Sword Coast in the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

Bjornin wore a plate mail and was armed with an enchanted +1 spear. Among his other possessions, Bjornin carried a +1 enchanted shield.

History

Some time during the , Bjornin was attacked by a band of half-ogres some ways northwest of Nashkel in Amn. He survived the encounter, but was forced to flee to the town of Beregost in order to rest and recover.

While he was stuck in town, Bjornin stayed at the Jovial Juggler and sought out adventurers to bring justice upon his monstrous assailants.
