Nain Keenwhistler was a human wizard of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. Notably, he was a former member of the Company of Crazed Venturers and had resurrected more times than anyone else in the city.

Home

He lived at Naingate, his own personal tower, in the Sea Ward.

Description

Nain had blond hair that later turned white and he had black eyes, and he had a healthy complexion.

Possessions

Nain owned a magical +1 dagger and a decanter of endless water.

Personality

Nain was shy and retiring. He worshiped Mystra and Tymora.

History

Nain was an ex-member of the Company of Crazed Venturers and in their service had died many times, each time being resurrected again. Notably, he fought Iyachtu Xvim, son of the deity Bane. He became friends with Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun and Durnan, and Malchor Harpell was his colleague. He maintained a strong friendship with Sharest Tanthalar after they met at one of Mirt's parties.

After retiring from the company, he used the wealth he had accumulated to construct Naingate, a tower in the Sea Ward, where he resided. He joined up with Shalara Malarkkin to form the Company of Staff and Steel, which lasted for about two years, but later split up.

As of 1368 DR, Nain was a trader and diplomat, advising the Lords of Waterdeep on various issues.

In the , he was among the many powerful mages Arunsun brought together to cleanse part of the High Moor and restore Faer'tel'miir, a lost city of Miyeritar.
