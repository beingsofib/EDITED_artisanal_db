Sarth Baretha was a githyanki warrior who was alive during the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

She was in the possession of a Githyanki slate, a magical map that displayed the location of a crèche on the Sword Coast.

In battle, Baretha wore a suit of Githyanki half plate, and wielded a greatsword.

Relationships

Baretha served under the merciless Kith'rak known as Voss.

History

In the Faerûnian Baretha accompanied Voss and other githyanki warriors in search of a mysterious artifact that they knew to be a powerful weapon in the fight against the illithids. Their search took them to the planet of Toril, and the region of the Sword Coast.
