Zalaren was a priest of Waukeen and an agent of the Lords' Alliance in 1489 DR.

Personality

Zalaren was a dour individual. His main concern was economic stagnation.

History

In 1489 DR, he was the Alliance delegate sent to help a hired adventuring party to defeat the great dragon Claugiyliamatar of Kryptgarden Forest. The Alliance's main objectives were to destroy siege weapons and to recover stolen cargo.
