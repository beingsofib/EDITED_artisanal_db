Dread Wavelord Dumai Arachneie was a Tashalan human cleric and waveservant of Umberlee in Lapaliiya in the mid–14 century DR. Circa 1373 DR, he was the ruler of Ilyaport, a Lapaliiyan town dedicated to Umberlee.
