Sarina Reqnivich was an elven rogue and burglar who led a double life as a wealthy aristocrat in Waterdeep in the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

She owned a pair of boots of elvenkind and a set of leather armor, both of which she used in her cat burglar persona.

Activities

In her guise as a wealthy aristocrat, she would attend all the great balls and parties held by the wealthy elite of Waterdeep. She would use the opportunity to stake out the houses, mansions, and palaces of the idle rich, and then carefully plan her burglaries. She spent most of her time in the different social gatherings held by the elite.
