Buirin Thalshond

Buirin Thalshond was a merchant from the city of Tantras in the Vast in the mid–14 century DR.

Speaking of treasure tales in the Vast in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR, Buirin remarked "Treasure? Aye, lots of it in the Vast, lad; why didn't ye ask earlier? Oh… I see; ye wanted to live a while, first."
