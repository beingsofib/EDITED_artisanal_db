Bizmatec

Person
nameBizmatec
homeAbyss
raceGlabrezu

Bizmatec was the glabrezu that acted as the balor Errtu's second-in-command, and was sent to the Material Plane to help Matron Baenre in her quest to take Mithral Hall, while Errtu was still under his banishment.
