Xei Win Toh was a demonic humanoid who had a connection with the Watcher's Keep in the mid–14 century DR.

Abilities

Xei possessed some inherent resistances and immunities that were typically found among fiends, including a form of natural regeneration.

Activities

Xei Win Toh and her companions served as one of the three groups connected to the fifth level of Watcher's Keep, which held the final seal that kept hidden the Imprisoned One. The group was summoned to the keep when the heart key was used to unlock the seal.

Relationships

Her allies included the demons Nalmissra, Y'tossi, and the Huntress, along with the drow Ameralis Zauviir, and an unnamed hive mother.

History

Xei was killed in the , when Abdel Adrian and his companions delved into Watcher's Keep at the behest of the Knights of the Vigil.
