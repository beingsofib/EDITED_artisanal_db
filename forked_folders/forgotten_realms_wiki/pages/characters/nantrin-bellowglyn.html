Nantrin Bellowglyn was the innkeeper of the Three Old Kegs in Baldur's Gate during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Sometimes, Nantrin helped others to dispose of unwanted bodies and similar matters without asking questions.

History

Nantrin was a retired noble's guard from Tethyr and distrusted all Tethyrians.
