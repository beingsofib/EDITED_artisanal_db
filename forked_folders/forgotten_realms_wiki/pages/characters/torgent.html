Torgent was a guard at the Waterdeep palace in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He was gray-haired and gray-moustached, and not as bulky as he'd once been.

Relationships

He was familiar with Mirt.

History

In the , Torgent was guarding a passage from the home of Shyrrhr to the Palace of Waterdeep when Mirt tried to enter with Asper. Mirt was allowed to pass, and asked for the location of Piergeiron Paladinson, to which Torgent replied by warning him the Open Lord was not himself lately, and had taken up his full armor and helm in the Inner Audience Chamber.
