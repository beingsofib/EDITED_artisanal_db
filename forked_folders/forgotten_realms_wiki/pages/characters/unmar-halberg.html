Unmar Halberg was a dwarf inhabitant of Eveningstar and the town stonemason during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Unmar had light skin and long bright orange-red hair, his beard braided in a fork.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Unmar was distressed to find that the Market Square well was poisoned. While the Purple Dragons and War Wizards were busy in battle, he sent brave adventurers down into the underground caverns that fed the town's water supply. Down there they found yuan-ti agents hired by the drow to poison the town, overcoming maddened dryads, the yuan-ti leaders Saz-sass, Gresl-glis, and Fless-onas, and even their allied green dragon Tharmalos.
