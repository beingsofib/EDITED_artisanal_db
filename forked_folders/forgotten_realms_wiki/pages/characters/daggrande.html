Daggrande was a dwarf and an officer in the Golden Legion.

Personality

Being a warrior his entire life, he met Halloran back when the human was still but an apprentice. They struck up a friendship that lasted for the rest of their lives. Daggrande was a loyal friend and served his comrade with all the commitment he could muster.

Possessions

He wielded an enchanted battleaxe, and wore enchanted field plate and a pair of gauntlets of dexterity.

Activities

He commanded the Legion's contingent of crossbowmen and was known for his bravery and discipline. He was a close friend of Halloran.
