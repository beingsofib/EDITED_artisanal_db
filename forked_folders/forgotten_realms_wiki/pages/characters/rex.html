Rex was a saurial cleric who was separated from his kind by the evil god Moander.

Description

The cleric had green scales, beige chest, white back spines, and dark red eyes. He wore a holy symbol, and bracers of protection +2. Rex carried a mace +2.

History

Rex was a crown prince of his people, until he and several of his kind were taken by the evil god Moander and banished to Faerûn. He traveled to Myth Drannor in search of the Codex of the Planes, a tome he hoped would return him and his people back to their homeworld.
