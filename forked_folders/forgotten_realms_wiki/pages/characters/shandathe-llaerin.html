Shandathe Llaerin, "the Shadow" was a coinlass of Hastarl in the early 3 century DR. She was married to local baker Hannibur through the mid-3 century DR together.

Description

Shandathe was considered the most beautiful woman in all of Hastarl, with deep, dark eyes.

Personality

She was certainly not above helping out in a mean prank.

Abilities

She was able to get in and out of places with uncanny quiet.

Possessions

Of note, she had a rather fancy dagger.

Relationships

She married Hannibur, a local baker.

History

On at The Kissing Wench, a tavern, Shandathe Llaerin propositioned her services to a local thief of Hastarl, Eladar the Dark, who was secretly Prince Elminster Aumar. Before they came to an arrangement, local dandy Jansibal Otharr outbid him and nearly had his guards start a confrontation.

She started seeing Hannibur on the evenings after that, and after a while, they got married. Eladar the Dark brought them a number of coins for their wedding night.

Later in , Shandathe was kidnapped from their bakery by the men of King Belaur Aumar, for the king had caught sight of her face and fancied her. However, the King was prevented from having carnal contact with her because his castle was besieged at that precise point, requiring him to move to a safer location within the castle. She was later rescued by Myrjala Talithyn and reunited with her husband upon the death of King Belaur.

Rumors & Legends

Eladar the Dark noted some time after hurling Shandathe inside Hannibur's home that no word of the meeting between Shandathe and Hannibur had spread, which made him wonder why.

On , Elminster Aumar was mind-called to Druindar's Rock: Delmuth Echorn lured him in with an image of Shandathe bound and tortured, begging him to save her. This was but a ruse unrelated to the real Shandathe.
