Yassilli Vokasich was a woodcarver in the city of Liara in Nova Vaasa of the Domains of Dread.

Abilities

He did quite well for himself as a woodcarver.

Possessions

Of note, he owned rich estates outside the city of Liara where he spent most of his time; his home inside the city proper was actually a second residence atop his shop.

Relationships

He was the husband of Katerina Vokasich and the father of Jebbrie Vokasich.

History

At a time corresponding to the mid-to-late 14 century DR, Yassilli Vokasich's son, Jebbrie, was bitten by spiders carrying the curse of a werespider. Although he did not develop lycanthropy, he suffered a severe allergic reaction that left him feeble and bedridden. The person who rescued him from the spiders, Sundian Silgathen, was discovered publicly to have become a werespider. Yassilli might've had to threaten a mob with his longsword to keep them from killing his son.
