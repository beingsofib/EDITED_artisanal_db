Iskh Lammarask, known as Motherlass Longfingers to her patrons, was an old human inn owner in Blackhold, Fimbrul.

Appareance

Iskh had gold eyes under fierce black brows.

Activities

As of 1479 DR, Iskh managed the Iskh's Rest "inn". She also served as a sort of mother-like figure to the people of Blackhold. In her young days, she was also a dancer.
