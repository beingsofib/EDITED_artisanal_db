Tim was a youth from the town of Eagle Peak in the Storm Horn Mountains of Cormyr in the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the early 1360s DR, Tim was still a fairly young miller's son. Once he learned how to ride a horse, locals started talking about the boy's bright future as one of the Roadwarden Guard, a knightly order that protected the High Road in Cormyr.
