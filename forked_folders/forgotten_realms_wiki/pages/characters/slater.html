Slater was a female human soldier of the Zhentilar Company of the Scorpions during the Time of Troubles in 1358 DR.

History

Slater was one of the first to befriend Cyric when he decided to join the Scorpions. She helped and followed him loyally. She explained to Cyric that she liked him and his vision of power, and she wanted to rise through the ranks as his lieutenant. When in Scardale, the Company was reorganized as an elite force under the god Bane himself. Slater was nominated as captain of one of the ships arranged for the assault against Tantras.
