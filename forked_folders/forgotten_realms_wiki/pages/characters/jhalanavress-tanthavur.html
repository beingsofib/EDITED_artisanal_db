Preceptress Jhalanavress Tanthavur was a priestess of Waukeen, the Merchant's Friend, who served The High Holy House of Coin in Scornubel in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Jhalanavress was a tall and thin woman with long, curly black hair. She wore everyday resplendent garb of the Church of Waukeen clergy and decorated her head with intricate headpieces.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Jhalanavress Tanthavur held the rank of the Preceptress – the priestess who was in charge of daily operations of the temple, maintenance, purchasing, staff, and underpriests. One of her duties was purchasing thael leaf to be used by the clergy with pipeweed and added to incense. To purchase the additive leaf, she did business with Orstrar Galannavruk and Andred Kuroone of Zazesspur. Lurandural Veltree of The Halanthor merchants' guild was known to join her on testing and purchasing ventures through Jhalanavress's invitations.
