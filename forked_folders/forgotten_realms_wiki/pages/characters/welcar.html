Welcar was a member of the Citadel of Assassins in the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

He wielded a poisoned longsword.

Activities

Welcar was a member of the Citadel's bandit army in the late 1450s DR following the Vaasan War. He was first in line to replace Felix as the leader of both the Bloody Swords infantry unit and the Fleet Brigade.
