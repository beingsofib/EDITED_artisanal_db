Lavlaryn was a caravan guard in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Lavlaryn was one of the caravan guards for Orthil Voldovan in the trip from Scornubel to Waterdeep late in the . After an ambush, he was one of the guards searching the wagons for dangerous contraband; in particular, he searched Mhegras and Sabran's wagon but found only peles, large shovels meant for bakery. He still kept a bowgun trained on the contrabandists until Orthil Voldovan took a look at these, to make sure they weren't anything dangerous.
