Jemuril was a dwarven adventurer inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Jemuril owned the House of Jemuril–her rowhouse located on Tower Trail in Waterdeep's Dock Ward. She stored her entire collection of magic items here.

History

She gained fame as an adventurer for slaying a wicked mage from Calimport named Susktar.
