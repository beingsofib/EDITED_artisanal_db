This human was the harbor master of the village of Highcliff on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR.

History

The harbor master tried to convince a ship captain to remain in the village, but failed, and the ship sailed off. When the boat was attacked by lizardfolk, the harbor master and his sailors had to rescue them.
