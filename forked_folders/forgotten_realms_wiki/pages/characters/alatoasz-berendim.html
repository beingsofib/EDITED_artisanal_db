Most Exalted Fallskeeper Alatoasz Berendim was the high priest of Duskwood Dell in Amn.

Activities

Alatoasz was a high priest of Eldath and presided over her most holy site on Faerûn—Duskwood Dell. He lived within a small tree city called Eldathyn, training underpriests of his goddess to prepare them to be sent out into the world to spread Eldath's worship.
