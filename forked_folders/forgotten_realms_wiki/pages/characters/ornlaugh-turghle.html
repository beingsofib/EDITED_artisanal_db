Ornlaugh Turghle was a doppelganger who posed as a merchant in the city of Reddansyr on the Dragon Coast in the late 14 century DR.

Description

In his disguised form, Ornlaugh was a portly man, constantly gasping for air.

History

By the time the doppelganger was discovered, he had impersonated Ornlaugh Turghle for over a decade. As the merchant, Ornlaugh dealt in moneychanging, sold ironmongery, locks, hinges, and fastenings of all types, and ran a merchant fleet. Many of the folk who purchased locking mechanisms from the disguised doppelganger believed that the items were somehow enchanted, allowing doppelgangers to creep into their homes, slay and replace the good folk of Reddansyr. What happened to the real Ornlaugh was unknown, but he was unlikely to have had survived.
