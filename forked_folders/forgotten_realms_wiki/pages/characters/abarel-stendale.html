Lord Abarel Stendale was the general of the military forces of Melvaunt around 1367 DR. Abarel's title of Lord was simply an honorific title; he was not a member of the Council of Lords. By 1373, he had lost his position and was only a mercenary captain.

Description

Abarel was a tough and honorable leader who was unrivaled as a tactician.
