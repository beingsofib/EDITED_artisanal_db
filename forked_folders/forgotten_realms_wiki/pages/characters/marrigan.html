Marrigan was an ancient Uthgardt werewolf who came to serve Selûne many centuries before the 1300s DR.

History

She originated from the Gray Wolf tribe, but left them to serve the Moonmaiden in the New Moon Pact, a holy order of lycanthropes. Her deeds were widely celebrated and remembered in the oral traditions of the Uthgardt for centuries onward, as well as being recorded in transcriptions.
