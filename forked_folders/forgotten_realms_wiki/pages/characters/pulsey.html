Pulsey was a girl that lived in the town of Llorkh during the mid–14 century DR. Some time around the , Pulsey shared a brief romance with the charismatic young rogue named Tiep. Unbeknownst to poor Pulsey, Tiep had other young women waiting for him in settlements across the west from Scornubel to Parnast and beyond.
