Villiam Obarskyr was the younger brother of the first settler to Cormyr, Ondeth Obarskyr.

Relatives

Villiam's wife was Karsha Obarskyr. His older brother was Ondeth Obarskyr. His eldest daughter was Medaly Obarskyr.
