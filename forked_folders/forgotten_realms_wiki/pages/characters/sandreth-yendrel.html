Sandreth Yendrel was an early-14 century DR writer from the city of Neverwinter on the Sword Coast.

History

Sandreth Yendrel penned his renowned chapbook– The Rise of Randral: A Chapbook Adventure, in the . The book described courtly intrigues of the City of Splendors, Waterdeep, and features characters such as Garandal the Chancellor and Largreth the Good Knight.
