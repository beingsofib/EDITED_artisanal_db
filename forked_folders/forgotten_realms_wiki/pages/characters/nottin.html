Nottin was a hedge wizard who lived on the Sword Coast North during the late 15 century DR.

History

While Helm's Hold was under siege, circa the , Nottin set himself up at a small camp located just outside of city on the outskirts of Watcher's Forest, joininh the likes of the ranger Dassar Truebow, the merchant named Kella the Fair-Dealing, and Meryth Heartwood. He aided the Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge with the sabotage of material components used by the Ashmadai conjurers to complete their devil-summoning rituals.
