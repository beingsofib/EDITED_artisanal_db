Noanar was a Netherese arcanist of the Silver Age.

Description

The Fire Warden was a talented arcanist who specialized in fire-based magic.

History

Noanar was born in south of Netheril and became a wanderer of north Faerûn. After much travelling, the arcanist settled in Canlespiere. In , the archwizard Ioulaum bestowed upon Noanar the title of "Fire Warden". Noanar later developed much of the well-known fire-based spells that were used around the Realms. Noanar practiced his spells on ogres around Canlespiere. However, he was killed in a skirmish with these ogres in .
