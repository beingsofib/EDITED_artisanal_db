Ghunayya min Hakiyuh was a high priestess of Hakiyah of the Mosque of Hakiyah the Honest in 1367 DR.

Activities

Ghunayya was a member of the holy slayer organization called the Soft Whisper. The basement of the mosque was their headquarters. Ghunayya was loyal to the Grand Caliph.

Rumors and Legends

It was rumored that if there was a threat to the Grand Caliph's life, the Grand Vizier came to the mosque to pray. The threat to the Grand Caliph was eliminated shortly thereafter.
