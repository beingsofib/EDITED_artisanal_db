Colimpha Twiddle was a patriarch of the Twiddle family at some point prior to the .

Legacy

In the , the gnome inventor Nagreous Twiddle finished a diving bell prototype he named the Colimpha, which was brought along for a mission under Sturge Whitsunday to find Myth Nantar. The gnome named the bell after a past patriarch of the Twiddle family, one almost certainly named Colimpha Twiddle.
