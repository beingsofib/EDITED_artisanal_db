Kehl Skywatcher was an Iliyanbruen elf who lived in New Sharandar in the Feywild during the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa the , the fomorian ruler King Malabog began a campaign of conquest against the elves of New Sharandar that devastated large swaths of land in the Feywild. In response, Kehl led several missions to counteract the malignant effects caused by the fomorian witches in Malabog's army.

With the help of New Sharandar's druids, she developed an alchemical concoction that dissipated the toxic clouds that rose over the waters of the Horrid Mire and helped rescue the elven scouts that were trapped within. She also led the efforts to eradicate the witchcap toadstools that the same coven of witches planted throughout the dark fey enclave.
