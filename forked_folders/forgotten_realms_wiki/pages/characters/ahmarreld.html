Ahmarreld was a once-renowned adventuring sorcerer who lived a long time before the 14 century DR.

History

Ahmarreld's local renown was mostly forgotten by the 14 century DR. However, his name was immortalized as the feminine version of his name, Ahmarreldra, gained popularity. Eventually, it became commonly shortened to Reldra. One of the individuals that bore the name was Reldra Wyntersar of the , another was Reldra, a kitchen worker from the Purple Lady festhall of Westgate.
