Gundul Hulvesper was a breeder of horses in the village of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

His youngest son was Torzhin Hulvesper, an acolyte of Chauntea at the House of Plenty.

Description

He had a stocky build and a hooked nose.

Personality

His humor was broad and he was known for driving a hard bargain.

Home

He dwelled at Hulvesper Farm. It was found just south of the bluff on which Maskyr's Tower once stood, which itself was roughly due south of the village center and on the west of the road.

Activities

One of the most well-respected horse breeders of Maskyr's Eye, he produced the popular blackhair breed of equine.
