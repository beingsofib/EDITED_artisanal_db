Melisende Baybars lived on the island of Kaff in the Djinni's Claws in 1367 DR.

Appearance

Melisende was as near to physical perfection as most mortals could come.

Personality

Her personality was the polar opposite of her striking beauty, filling her with a cold heart and twisted thoughts. Her evil nature was often masked by false courtesy.

Skills

Melisende was a formidable opponent, possessing martial and magical skills.

Possessions

Melisende owned a pair of bracers of defense +5, a magical sword, and a wand of magic missiles.

Relationships

She was the daughter of Alim Baybars. Her mother, and namesake, was from the strange lands far west of the borders of Zakhara. Only her fear of his power kept Melisende from killing her father.
