Yves was a dockworker from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Yves was a human man with a light skin tone, blue eyes, dark wavy hair, and facial hair of the same color.

History

In the , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, Yves worked in the Gate's docks alongside Birta Grougg. They were known to borrow wine bottles from the shipments they worked on and, during breaks, sip the alcohol while pretending to be fanciful sommeliers, claimed that they detected notes, smells, and flavors of various foul things in the wine.
