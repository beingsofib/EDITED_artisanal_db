Lord Sarken Eomane was a member of the Parliament of Peers from the noble patriar Jannath family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

By the , Lord Sarken Eomane was already a member of the Parliament of Peers. When the Absolute crisis arrived to the Gate, Sarken was noted by the Cult of Bane as one of the eight top patriars of the Parliament, vulnerable to bribery, blackmail, or direct threats.
