Fargas Rumblefoot was a lightfoot halfling adventurer in the Silken Paths during the late 15 century DR.

History

The halfling went to the Underdark with his adventuring party in search of the Lost Tomb of Khaem, but they were attacked by mad gnolls. He escaped alone into the Silken Paths and was captured and cocooned by a giant spider.

During the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, a party of adventurers escaping drow slavers of Velkynvelve may have found and freed him.
