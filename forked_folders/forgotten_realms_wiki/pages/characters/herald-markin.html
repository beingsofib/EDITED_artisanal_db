Herald Markin lived in Prastuil in central Dambrath during the 14th century DR.

Activities

Herald served as Warchief of Prastuil, and was the leader of the city by most standards since the city did not have its own Duchess.

Relationships

He was completely loyal to Duchess Merial Luenath.
