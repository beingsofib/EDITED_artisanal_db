Ciara Blank was a laborer and a printer who worked for the Baldur's Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Ciara was a dark-skinned gold dwarven woman. She wore commoner's clothes and kept her face clean-shaven.

History

In the , Ciara lost her printing job Baldur's Mouth after the broadsheet's editor-in-chief, Ettvard Needle, received a gift of an enchanted printing press from Lord Enver Gortash. Ciara lamented the changing times but critiqued the Gazette over its poor paper quality and smudgy ink.
