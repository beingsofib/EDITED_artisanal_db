Angelique Cantaran was the wife of the most wealthy importer of spices in Zhentil Keep circa the mid–14 century DR.

Description

She was described as beautiful but lonely woman.

History

At the onset of the Arrival of the gods, in the , Angelique shared a bed with Trannus Kialton the night he was possessed by Bane.
