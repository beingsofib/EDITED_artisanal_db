Selgauth Cathdeiryn was an inhabitant of Cormanthyr and a house heir in the 3 century DR.

History

Selgauth Cathdeiryn was one of the wizards staying back in the hunt for Elminster Aumar in . He later related the experience unto Earynspieir and the sorceresses in service of the Coronal; the meeting may have turned violent.
