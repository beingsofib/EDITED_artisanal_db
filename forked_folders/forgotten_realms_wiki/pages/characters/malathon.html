Malathon was a half-orc cleric of Bane who helped in the attack on Shadowdale.

Abilities

His cleric domains were Destruction and Evil.

Possessions

The Dark Doom wore +3 full plate that provided him with resistance to spells in battle, and wielded an enchanted +2 morningstar. For additional protection, Malathon carried a ring of counterspells and an amulet of proof against petrification.

History

During the Zhent occupation of Shadowdale in Hammer, 1375 DR, High Priest Malathon was charged with rebuilding the temple of Bane in Castle Krag.
