Bheren was an elven man that lived in the city of Baldur's Gate during the mid—14 century DR.

Personality

He was described by his niece as being real mean. He was not above committing violence to animals.

Possessions

Among his possessions, Bheren owned a +1 cloak of protection.

Relationships

Bheren had a niece named Petrine who had a pet cat named Angel that he could not stand.

History

In the , Petrine ran away from her Bheren's home in the Upper City. Much to his dismay, she did not take her cat with her.
