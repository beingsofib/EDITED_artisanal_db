Hilithiimm Turnstone was the guildmaster of the Cellarers' & Plumbers' Guild of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Hilithiimm was a man of middle age and showing it by his balding pate and ever-widening belly, but was known for his quick laugh.

Relationships

He had a son, Jhalossan Turnstone, who ran the Turnstone Plumbing and Pipefitting store on his behalf. Hilithiimm hoped one day to transfer his position as guildmaster to his son.

For his vital role and discretion, he enjoyed the trust of the Lords of Waterdeep, of the City Watch, and of the nobility of Waterdeep.

Unfortunately, however, unknown to anyone, even his son, Hilithiimm was mentally enslaved by Nexil'Vendyliq, one of the aboleths of the Savants of the Dark Tide. Circa 1372 DR, it permitted Hilithiimm to act normally but had him steer Cellarers away from the aboleth-controlled portions of the sewers and to visit it regularly to renew the enslavement.

Activities

Hilithimm was the guildmaster for the Cellarers' & Plumbers' Guild by 1357 DR and through 1372 DR, by which time he had many years of experience. He held the title of Master Plumber within the guild and worked out of the Old Guildhall–their headquarters on Gaustus Street in the Trades Ward. As guildmaster, he oversaw the maintenance of the city's sewers and the excavation of building foundations. In this role, he and his son had inadvertently unearthed countless secrets of Waterdeep that the Lords, the nobles, and the wealthy merchants would rather stay buried, but thankfully he and his family were renowned for their discretion about the city's secrets.

At the same time, Hilithimm was also owner of the Turnstone Plumbing and Pipefitting in the Dock Ward.
