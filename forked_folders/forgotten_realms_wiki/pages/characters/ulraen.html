Ulraen of Eltabbar was a Red Wizard of Thay during the early 14 century DR.

History

Ulraen slew Veldribold of Calimport in , shortly after the latter finally managed to bring down Phelandern Ghemelt, the Magister. He was mid-gloat when he was savaged by two other challengers' summoned monsters.
