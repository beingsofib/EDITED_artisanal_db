Naga was a yakuza in the town of Aru in Wa.

Personality

Naga recently became known for his many unorthodox ideas, which were not really appreciated by his fellows.

Possessions

Naga carried a magic lajatang as a weapon.

History

Naga was a yakuza with some followers. In 1358 DR, he plotted to kidnap Sono, the servant and lover of Buntaro Ostato, in order to blackmail Buntaro. Despite the veto of his elders, he proceeded with his plan.
