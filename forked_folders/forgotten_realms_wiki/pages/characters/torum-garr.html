Torum Garr was an exiled king that lived in the Realms during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He had a distinct red beard.

History

Torum died some time before the , while fighting in an ongoing war against a population of drow. At some point during the fighting, the mercenary Kelemvor Lyonsbane deserted Torum and left him to his death. The king's death haunted the mortal Kelemvor until his final days on Toril.
