Kung Pu Mok was the High Priest of Shou Lung in 1357 DR and 1358 DR

Activities

Kung Pu Mok plotted to replace the Emperor with a theocracy. He also sought to marry Kai Tsao Shou Chin's third daughter, Princess Pai. In order to fulfill his dream, he forged ties with Black Chung Tao. He had an uneasy truce with Minister of Faith Yui Min Li.

History

In 1358 DR, Kung Pu Mok was busy with a tour of the monasteries in Wa K'an.

Personality

Kung Pu Mok was a vain and stubborn individual.
