Orniiv Fandarfall, also called the Eclipse, was a male human cleric of Mask. He was a member of the Shadow Council of the Shadow Thieves.

Orniiv was responsible for the guild's operation in Baldur's Gate. He was the perfect example of the guild's thieves: always cold and inscrutable. His nickname came from a tattoo of an eclipse on his left cheek.
