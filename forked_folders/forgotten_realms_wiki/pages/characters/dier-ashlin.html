Dier Ashlin was a male human Zhentilar soldier during the Time of Troubles in 1358 DR.

He was guarding the Temple of Bane in Zhentil Keep when the god Bane himself, in the corpse of Fzoul Chembryl, arrived in the city after the disastrous Battle of Shadowdale. Ashlin, believing he was speaking to Fzoul, explained the change in the city's government after the defeat.
