Maree Blimthalloon was an inhabitant of Ieirithymbul in the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

As far as Burlmarr, a local gemcutter's apprentice, knew, his fellow apprentice Ambrythynn fancied her.

Rumors & Legends

In Uktar of the , stray thoughts from a suitor to Lissa Boavartarr were captured by a local apprentice, Burlmarr, who believed he was hearing his fellow apprentice Ambrythynn say those words. Mistakenly assuming Ambrythynn was the one speaking, Burlmarr said he wouldn't tell Maree Blimthalloon about him fancying Lissa. Ambrythynn did not actually have anything to do with Lissa or her suitor.
