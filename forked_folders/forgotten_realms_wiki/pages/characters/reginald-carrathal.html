Reginald Carrathal was High King of Callidyrr in the Moonshae Isles during the early-to-mid 14 century DR. Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles, he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Personality

He liked privacy, and preferred to rule his land isolated within the Palace of the High King. He rarely deigned to meet with even esteemed visitors. The decline and decay of his realm during his reign was sometimes blamed on his inattentiveness.

Relationships

He was the final ruler of the Carrathal Dynasty. He was the third son of his father and predecessor, King Alec IV.

Reginald had a bastard son with an Amnian woman. The son's name was Brynth, and became Reginald's heir.

History

He assumed the throne of Callidyrr in the , after the death of his father.

When Elminster visited Callidyrr in the , he was invited to stay at the Palace of the High King but was never granted an audience with the king himself.

King Reginald's reign lasted fifteen years.
