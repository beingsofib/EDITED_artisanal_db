Halthos Blund, who was nicknamed the Hammer, was a carpenter inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Halthos was the guildmaster for the Carpenters', Roofers', & Plaisterers' Guild circa 1357 DR. He worked out of the Stone House–their headquarters on Telshambra's Street in the Southern Ward.
