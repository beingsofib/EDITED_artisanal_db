Lethchauntos the Black was a powerful and evil mage active on the Sword Coast as of the 10 century DR.

History

At some point prior to 936 DR, Lethchauntos traveled to live among the orc followers of the shaman Wund in Uruth Ukrypt. While there, Lethchauntos crafted the Ettin Axe of Uruth as a gift to the orcish realm's first king, Uruth, and at some point parlayed with the sage Orfidel. From Orfidel's writings of the encounter, Lethchauntos the Black was believed by sages to have been one of the first known owners of The Nathlum, a tome of poisons and evil magic. If so, Lethchauntos apparently lost it during the Orcfastings War in the , as the book was found amongst the booty of one Tymor Threeshields.

Lethchauntos was also credited with authoring the spellbook Doors and Passages of Parallel Worlds, which included such spells as hold portal, knock, jump, and wizard lock.
