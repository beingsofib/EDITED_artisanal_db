Sheila Kree was a pirate and Captain of the ship Bloody Keel active around . Her partners included Jule Pepper, Le'lorinel, Bellany, and many others. She resided in the Golden Cove with her ogre allies. She befriended Chogurugga and Bloog, the two head ogres living in the cove.

Sometimes between and , she took in her crew the moon elf Ellifain Tuuserail.
