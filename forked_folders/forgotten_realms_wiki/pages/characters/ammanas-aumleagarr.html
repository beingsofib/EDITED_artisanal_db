Ammanas Aumleagarr was a warrior and sage inhabiting Yhaunn in the 14 century DR.

Activities

Ammanas was quite wealthy and enjoyed the fine arts, wielding some influence as a patron to artists who caught his fancy. His personal academic studies included the music of human cultures and philosophy.

History

Ammanas was well-known public figure in Yhaunn by 1358 DR, patronizing artists and sharing his scholarly research with those who came to him.

In Eleasis of 1373 DR, Ammanas hosted a party at his mansion with many of Yhaunn's wealthy and powerful attending. Among his distinguished guests were Nessarch Andilal Tharimpar, Moonmistress-Designate Feena Archwood, High Luck Colle Shoondeep, the famed wizard Arthagus of the Miracles, the merchant Diero Mivaldi, Guard Captain Manas, the harpist Betha Horndriver, and the archmages Strasus Thingoleir and Dagnalla Irongard.
