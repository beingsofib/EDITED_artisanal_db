Oliver was an osquip in Marigold's Menagerie in the mid-14 century DR.

Activities

Oliver typically hid under parchment and woodchips whenever visitors came to the Menagerie, leaving some to think of him as a rather boring exhibit. However, Marigold Mufflefoot always managed to entice him out of his hiding spot by offering small gemstones.
