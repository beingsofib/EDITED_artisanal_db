Ajaman was a warrior of the Qahtan tribe who roamed the deserts of Anauroch with his fellow tribesmen during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He had a rugged appearance, with a lean, muscular build and skin that had been weathered by years traveling under At'ar the Merciless. He kept a thick moustache that was common among the mature men of the Bedine.

Personality

Ajaman was a good-natured and measured man with a strong sense of duty and tradition.

Abilities

Having grown up as well-trained warrior, Ajaman was skilled with the bow.

Relationships

Ajaman was the son of the Qahtani sheikh by his second wife.
