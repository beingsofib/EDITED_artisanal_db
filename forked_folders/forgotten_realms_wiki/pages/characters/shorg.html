Shorg was a hobgoblin living near Julkoun around 1485 DR.

Possessions

Dressed in chainmail, Shorg fought with a scimitar, morning star, and javelins.

History

Around 1486 DR, Shorg was the leader of the goblinoids who invaded the village of Julkoun under the suggestion of one of the Red Wizards of Thay. Later, he was defeated by an adventuring party.
