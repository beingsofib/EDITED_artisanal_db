Stablemaster Alark Phlamtryn was the head of the staff of The Redmark inn in the town of Harrowdale in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Circa 1367 DR, due to a spate of suspicious activity of several guests trying to rob the inn's profits as they were transported through town, Alark decided to hire the Stormblades adventuring company to protect his workers.
