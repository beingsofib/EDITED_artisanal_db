Gulian was a bard travelling the land in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Born with both musical talent and wanderlust he enjoyed the life of an adventourer, always seeking new sights and experiences.

Possessions

Wielding his lute and flute instruments, he was protected by a ring of protection +3. He owned a set of Nolzur's marvelous pigments, alongside a pipes of the sewers.

Relationships

He was the son and grandson of travelling minstrels and bards, all caravan travelers who never settled down.
