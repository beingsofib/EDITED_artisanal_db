Ahmad al-Mudill was a Zakharan flame mage who served as a spy and recruit for the Brotherhood of the True Flame in Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Personality

Ahmad was ruthless in his goal to climb the ranks of the Brotherhood. He would remove any obstacle barring him from attaining a higher ranking.

Possessions

Ahmad owned a wand of fire.
