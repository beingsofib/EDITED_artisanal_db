Ahmadora was a young girl living in Easthaven during the 11th century DR.

Description

The blond haired lady liked to wear blue dresses.

Personality

Ahmadora was an fearful young girl who scare about some of the animals found in the Frozenfar.

Activities

Unlike many kids in the small community, Ahmadora was fascinated by magic and loved to see spells cast by wizards.

Relationships

Ahmadora was friends with a few other local children, like Dillsandra, Jeffy, and Chizelo.

History

In Eleint, 1281 DR, Ahmadora and a few other children met a group of mercenaries who decided to head with Hrothgar on his expedition to Kuldahar.
