Firble was a svirfneblin councilor of King Schnicktick in Blingdenstone in the mid–14 century DR. He also had secret dealings with Jarlaxle, who traded information with him.

Description

The deep gnome had stubby fingers and was going bald. Firble stood about tall and weighed around .

Personality

Firble could be impatient, and though he feared Jarlaxle, he was willing to press the mercenary for information, even if the drow pressed back. The deep gnome hated all types of light sources, including torchlight.

Possessions

Instead of wearing metal armor, Firble dressed in long smooth robes.

History

Around 1357 DR, Firble grew more and more suspicious and fearful of the drow creeping around in the Underdark. After "prompting" from Belwar Dissengulp, Firble agreed to escort Catti-brie to the drow city of Menzoberranzan.

Death

After the Battle of Keeper's Dale, Belwar Dissengulp carried Firble's body away from the battlefield.
