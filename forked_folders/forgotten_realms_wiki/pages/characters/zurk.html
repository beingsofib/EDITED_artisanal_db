Beastmaster Zurk was a goblin animal wrangler and devotee of the Absolute that dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Zurk was responsible for caring for the worgs that were kept by within the lower reaches of the ruined temple of Selûne where he and his fellow goblins made camp. Working with Butcher Vrak, he fed them the remains of the people the goblins killed during their raids.

History

In the , Zurk also had to keep imprisoned a mighty bear that was in fact the druid Halsin in his animal form. He performed his duties well, letting goblin children throw rocks at the great beast.
