Shadowhands was a mysterious mage of note who lived some time before the 14 century DR.

History

Shadowhands was a lieutenant of Nelazra who was the guildmaster of the Nighteyes, a thieves' guild in Calimport. Shadowhands was slain during a conflict with the Brothers Six, a rival group of mages who wanted to control trade in Calimport. Before his death Shadowhands penned a spellbook called the Shadowtome.

Relationships

It was rumored that he was also Nelazra's lover.
