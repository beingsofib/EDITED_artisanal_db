Tsabanor Oblodra was the house patron of House Oblodra and the mate of Matron Mother K'yorl Odran circa 1358 DR.

Personality

Tsabanor was a pathetic figure in his house, and lived only with K'yorl's permission. She taunted and tormented him constantly. He often considered hurling himself into the Clawrift to end his suffering.

History

Tsabanor was chosen by Matron Mother K'yorl as patron for his latent psionic powers. He was once a skilled speaker, but K'yorl cut his tongue out because he had spoken to her too often. From that point on he lived a pathetic life.

Powers

He had latent psionic powers.
