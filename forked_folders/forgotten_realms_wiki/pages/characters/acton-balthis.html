Pip was the butler at the Balthis Estate in the City of Coin, Athkatla in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Lord Balthis was a human male who dressed in turquoise finery.

History

By the , Lord Acton Balthis served as the Vice-Regent for the Council of Six, despite not having a seat on the council. However, Acton acted on its behalf and was the de facto ruler when the Council of Six was not in session. Lord Balthis could often be found in his estate in Athkatla's Bridge District. His peace was protected by the loyal hin butler named Pip and home defended by armored bodyguards.
