Shank was an inexperienced assassin who was looking to make a name for himself in the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

At some point, Shank got into heated disagreement with his fellow assassin Carbos, over the affection of Bubbles.

History

In the , Shank took a contract out on the young ward of Gorion, Abdel Adrian. He managed to make his way into Candlekeep but was unsuccessful in killing the young man.

By the following year Shank had made his way to Amnian city of Athkatla.
