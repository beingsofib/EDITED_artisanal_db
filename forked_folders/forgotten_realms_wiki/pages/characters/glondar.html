Glondar was a Zhentilar of the mid-14 century DR.

Relationships

He was part of a squad led by Fimril.

History

In late , Glondar was part of the squad led by Fimril that briefly captured Shandril Shessair at Hullack Forest. He was slain by Mirt, who came to her rescue.
