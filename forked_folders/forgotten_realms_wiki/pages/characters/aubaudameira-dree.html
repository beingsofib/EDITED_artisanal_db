Aubaudameira Dree, usually known as Alais, was a female elf who was the herald of the Coronal Eltargrim of Cormanthyr in the 3 century DR.

History

Alais was a prominent member of Dree House. However, she was mentally enslaved by her fiancé, Glarald Starym, who used her in an attempt to kill the coronal. Glarald's plot failed and Alais was freed and became the new herald of the coronal, becoming one of his most trusted servants. Alais also lent her life-force for the raising of the mythal in 261 DR.

In later years, Alais joined the Harpers at Twilight and fought well in the Weeping War.

Alais was one of the commanders in a decisive battle against the Army of Darkness on Ches 4 and 5 of the , when thanks to the Chosen of Mystra Elminster and Khelben Arunsun the Army suffered a defeat at Elven Wood.

Alais was one of the few Harpers to survive alongside Elminster, Khelben, and Meil Araeln. Alais was at the Gathering of the Gods in the , together with her fellow Harpers.

Relationship

Alais studied magic under one of the founders of the Circle of Flames, Symkalr Vandiir. She was a close ally of Mentor Wintercloak
