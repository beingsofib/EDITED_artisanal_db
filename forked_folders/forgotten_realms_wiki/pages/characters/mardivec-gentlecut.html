Mardivec Gentlecut was an incubus servant of Graz'zt in the Moonsea in the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Mardivec was an old friend of the goblin cambion Agrak. He always had with him his dretch servant Noisome.

History

Circa 1486 DR, Mardivec was forcibly summoned to Faerûn from Azzagrat, together with his master the demon lord Graz'zt. He and Noisome decided to join Lord Agrak. He resided inside the prison of the Skullspike tribe, enjoying the madness spreading among the goblins.

Personality

Mardivec liked his devilish form, unlike his fellows.
