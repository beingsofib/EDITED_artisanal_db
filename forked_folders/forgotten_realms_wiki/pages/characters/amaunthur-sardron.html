Amaunthur Sardron was a human noble from Eskorn, Laerakond, and the main oppositor of the Roaryl dynasty as of 1479 DR.

Background

In 1479 DR, Amaunthur was secretly working to depose the Roaryl dynasty from the throne, and to elevate his son to king of Eskorn. Amaunthur's plan was to use his own son as a puppet-king while he became the real ruler of the land.
