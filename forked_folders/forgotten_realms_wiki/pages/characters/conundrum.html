Conundrum was a Mystran cleric and wizard of Ravens Bluff in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Abilities

Conundrum had superlative wisdom and intelligence, such that he'd mastered not only arcane magic but divine as well.

Relationships

He was seeing a sergeant, and he made a heraldic device for her.

History

Conundrum was seeing the sergeant of the patrol from Ravens Bluff that was sent to Stag Falls one spring after they missed the payment of their taxes. He'd apparently made one heraldic device for her. Curiously, the heraldic device he created for her was identical to the one bore on the arms of a man whom Ferguson, a guard from Stag Falls, blamed for the suicide of his beloved. This heraldic device was green, with a copper colored strip diagonally, between two eagles displayed in white, with three green trees on the stripe, surrounded by a copper bordure.

Provided with proof of her fate after her death, he was willing to cast whatever spells were needed to repay the favor.
