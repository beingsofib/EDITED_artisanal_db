Umberto Noblin was a Neverwintian noble and preeminent historian in the late 15 century DR. His field of expertise was the history of Vecna.

Description

Umberto had a discerning palate and a notable appreciation for magical cuisine. He especially enjoyed food crafted with magical means, such as the berries produced by the goodberry spell.

History

In the late 1490s DR, Umberto and three other nobles were kidnapped by a cult of Vecna and taken to Neverwinter's graveyard, Neverdeath.
