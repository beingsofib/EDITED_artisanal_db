Sheera Goldenleaf was a member of the Council of Seven Burghers in 1368 DR.

History

Sheera was a charismatic adventurer who settled in Harrowdale around 1367 DR. She became a stable-owner, horse-breeder, and trainer.

Activities

Sheera owned and operated Goldenleaf Stables in Harrowdale.
