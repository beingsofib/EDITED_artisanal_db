Suri was an adventurer and wererat active in Waterdeep during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Suri was a girl with brown hair who wore a silver sun-shaped pendant.

History

Circa the , Suri and her sister Perdita adventured through a secret crypt hidden beneath the City of the Dead. Misled by fake treasure maps distributed by Bregan D'aerthe, the young sisters reached the lair of Artor Morlin, a powerful vampire. There, Perdita lost her life and became a ghost, while Suri fought a wererat and subsequently contracted lycanthropy.
