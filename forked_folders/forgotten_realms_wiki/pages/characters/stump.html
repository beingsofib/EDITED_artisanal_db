Stump was a locksmith in Skullport in the late 14 century DR.

Description

He was one-handed and nearsighted.

Activities

Stump, the owner of Stump's Locksmithy, was renowned for his skill in crafting highly sophisticated locks and traps. Despite his physical limitations, Stump’s ingenuity and craftsmanship made his work sought after by those who valued security in Skullport.

He was also an avid chess player.
