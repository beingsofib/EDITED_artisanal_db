Kao Ling was a wealthy merchant of Linshung, Hungtse Province, in Shou Lung circa .

Relationships

He was the father of Kuang Ming Ling; he hoped her dream of being a shukenja would just be a passing interest. Kao Ling also had in his employ two korobokuru bushi: Hsiao Nan Tzu Wen and Ling Yu Pao.

History

When Kuang Ming decided to train as a shukenja and went on pilgrimage to a far-away monastery, Kao Ling discreetly dispatched his two korobokuru retainers to protect her.
