Sthavar was the Lord Magistrate of Suzail as well as the commander of the Purple Dragons of the Citadel of the Purple Dragons in the mid-to-late 14 century DR under King Azoun Obarskyr IV and Regent Alusair Obarskyr.

He was typically accompanied by six veteran bodyguards and his herald, Xorn Hackhand.

Personality

Sthavar was known to be loyal to the Crown, as well as notably confident.

History

In the , he attended a royal feast held by Lord Partic Thistle at Thistleflame Keep.
