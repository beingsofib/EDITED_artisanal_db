Vorryl Farwalker was a well-traveled drow adventurer for hire from the city of Neverwinter, active in the late 15 century DR.

History

By the late 15 century DR, Vorryl Farwalker was considered to be one of the most well-traveled adventurers in all Faerûn and an excellent swordfighter. Eventually Vorryl reached the city of Neverwinter and began entertaining thoughts of settling down. During that time, he made a living by being an adventurer for hire and procurer of various items and resources.
