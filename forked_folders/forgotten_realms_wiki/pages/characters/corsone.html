Corsone was a member of the Shadow Druids who lived on the Sword Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

The druid was not above using deceit and murder to achieve his goals.

Abilities

Like other Shadow Druids, he believed the best way to fight the encroachment of civilization was by might and force.

Relationships

Corsone was an associate of the Malarite druid Osmadi, who maintained a shrine in the forest of Larswood. In fact, he saw his "friend" and his druidic allies as weak, believing them to stand in the way of the progress sought by the Shadow Druids.

History

In the , Corsone poisoned Osmadi, causing him to go mad and slay his fellow druids. He concocted a story about how the group was attacked by bandits.
