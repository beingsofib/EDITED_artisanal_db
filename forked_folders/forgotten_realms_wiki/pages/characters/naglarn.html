Naglarn was a farmer of Skuldaskar in the mid-3 century DR.

Personality

He was scornful of prospectors.

History

In , he was present at the Herald's Horn tavern the night that Elminster Aumar purchased a wyrmtongue scepter from Surgath Ilder, whom Naglarn needled earlier about his job not being honest work.
