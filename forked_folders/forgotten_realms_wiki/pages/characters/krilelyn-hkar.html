Krilelyn H'Kar was an elite warrior from House H'kar and a member of Bregan D'aerthe in Menzoberranzan in the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa 1486 DR, an expedition led by some adventurers, who'd been hired by Bruenor Battlehammer to stop the threat of the demon lords, arrived in Menzoberranzan. Krilelyn was instructed by Jarlaxle to invite them to a meeting with him in the ruins of the House Do'Urden complex.

Personality

Krilelyn was a sarcastic and devilishly charming male .
