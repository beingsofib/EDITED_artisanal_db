Miresella was a scout from Baldur's Gate.

History

Miresella was following a caravan when she happened to discover the secret Cult of the Dragon village of Parnast. She was captured and transported to the Cult's hunting lodge in the Greypeak Mountains, under the care of Talis Kasterel, who deeply scared her.
