Coprates "Cap'n" Bile was a privateer in the Sea of Fallen Stars during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. She was briefly involved in a mission to connect Selgaunt with Myth Nantar militarily.

Description

Coprates Bile was tall and , with brown hair and eyes. Her skin had a ruddy tan.

Personality

People agreed she was rude and obnoxious, but the onus of her efforts was in running a tight ship, not observing the manners of city folk.

Abilities

Notably uncharismatic, she had the makings of a ranger adapted to the sea, and superior heroic strength.

Possessions

Her most notable possessions were a decanter of endless water, a ring of regeneration, and a powerful, enchanted leather armor. She also carried an ointment for sunburns.

Activities

She was the captain of the Emerald Tayar.

Relationships

Her great-uncle was Petrove, a known pirate, and Coprates held a mean grudge against sahuagin ever since they killed him. She prided herself in looking after her crew, including Rondo Marsh, her ship's mage, whose grudge against wealth and power worried her she might lose him to foolish politics. She'd also hired Ilsa Merrymore, a sea elf, to dive for shipwrecks, and turned a blind eye to her constant skimming.

During the mission to contact Serôs, she considered Sturge Whitsunday to be a popinjay who better know his place and do as he was told, and she would make sure to tell him to do things that spoiled his hair and clothes. She viewed his sister Aleine Whitsunday more favorably, taking a liking to her, and pitying that she had such a pompous fool of an older brother. Finally, she viewed Nagreous Twiddle with suspicion, finding him incomprehensible, and keeping an eye to ensure he didn't modify the Emerald Tayar.

History

Born to a middle-class family in Selgaunt, Coprates was raised on her great-uncle Petrove's sea-shanties until at thirteen she became a stowaway, disguising herself for a position as cabin boy. Within three years she'd made first mate, and three years after that, she'd saved up enough money to buy the Emerald Tayar, her ship. About a year after that, her great-uncle was killed by sahuagin, giving her a lasting grudge. She hired Rondo Marsh from the docks in Selgaunt at some point after Mirtul of the , almost a year before her mission to contact Serôs.

She was hired by Sturge Whitsunday in Mirtul of to bring him, his sister, and the gnomish inventor Nagreous Twiddle to Myth Nantar.

Rumors & Legends

She told everyone she was the kid of a pirate queen and a sea wolf, raised by sharks.
