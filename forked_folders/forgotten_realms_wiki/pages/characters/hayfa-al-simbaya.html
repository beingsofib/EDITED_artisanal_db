Hayfa al-Simbaya was a the Sultana of Simbaya in north Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Description

Hayfa was a beautiful woman who possessed long black hair.

Personality

She was incredibly vain.

Relationships

Hayfa formed a symbiotic relationship with a vizier's turban named Saghirah.
