Battle Chaplain Saerlia Ashley was a human cleric of Tempus, god of battle. She tended the shrine of Tempus in the town of Thunderstone in Cormyr around 1369 DR.

She was a scarred woman who inspired attending adventurers and Purple Dragons soldiers with sermons of battle, blood, and fire. She offered her healing only to those wounded in battle, and would confirm the truth of a tale before she started.
