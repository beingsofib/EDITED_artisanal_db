Carngaur was a caravan guard in the Sword Coast during the mid-14 century DR.

Relationships

He was one of Orthil Voldovan's men.

History

During Orthil Voldovan's caravan run from Scornubel to Waterdeep in late , Carngaur was one of the caravan guards. During an ambush by the Cult of the Dragon, he was struck with a lance, and later died; a poisoned lance was suspected.
