Fera was a wild halfling hishnashaper from the deep jungles of Payit in Maztica who wished to travel to Faerûn as to see more of the great world.

Description

She was white-haired where most of her kin were black-haired.

Possessions

She wielded an enchanted knife +3 alongside a spear and club.

History

She was the daughter of the tribal shaman and it was from him she learned the magic of hishna. After the Night of Wailing bands of rampaging nexalan orcs destroyed much including Feras home. Since then she travelled much of Maztica, but once she had seen what she felt as most, she sought to board a ship to Faerûn as to see those foreign lands.
