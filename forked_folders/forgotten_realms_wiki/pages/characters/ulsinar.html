Ulsinar was a carpenter in Highmoon in the mid-14 century DR.

History

During one evening after Mirtul of , while Shandril Shessair was away, Ulsinar patronized the Highmoon Inn and witnessed a brief argument between Yantra and other patrons, on which he whooped and cheered.
