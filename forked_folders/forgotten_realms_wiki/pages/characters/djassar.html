Djassar was a warrior of Mirabar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Djassar served as one of the Hammers, which were a group of four commanders of the personal bodyguard of the Marchion of Mirabar, Elastul Raurym.

Possessions

He wore a armor plated with platinum.
