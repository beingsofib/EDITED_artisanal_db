Pe're was a sea elf sergeant in Myth Nantar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Abilities

His abilities were level with those of other sea elven sergeants.

Possessions

He carried a trident and a conch he could blow on for reinforcements.

Activities

Pe're held the rank of sergeant in Myth Nantar.

History

When in , Sturge Whitsunday was sent to find Myth Nantar by the mayor of Selgaunt, Andeth Ilchammar, if Sturge got far enough, his first contact with the mystical city would be when Pe're captured him for trespassing into the city's territories.

Inside the city, his crew would be interrogated, and then brought to trial.
