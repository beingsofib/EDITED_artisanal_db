Iref Marsk was a guard in Heliogabalus as of the late mid-14 century DR.

Description

He was stocky. His hair was "foppishly" barbered, slicked with oil.

Possessions

As of the time of his death, he'd bought an amethyst and gold earring.

History

Iref Marsk was part of the raid on Kosef Demsk's room the morning he was captured at some point in the 1360s DR.

Nights later, while leaving a brothel, he was followed by Pavel Shemov and Tana Farag, who suspected he had worked to frame Kosef; though he noticed them, they caught up to him. but before they could, he was slain by a barghest.

Rumors & Legends

Though they never actually managed to prove it, Pavel Shemov and Tana Farag had reason to believe that Iref Marsk had employed some device as he entered Kosef's home to dissolve a duplicate of Kosef's signature weapon which had been swapped for the real thing.
