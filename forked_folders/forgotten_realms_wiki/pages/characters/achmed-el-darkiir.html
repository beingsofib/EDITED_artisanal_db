Achmed yn Nuram el Darkiir yi Almraiven was a human thief and mage from Almraiven, Calimshan, and the secret leader of the Many Long Arms, the private thieves' guild and spy network of the Rundeen mercantile organization in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Despite his advancing years, he remaining quite cunning.

Activities

Achmed hid his illicit activities under three or four false identities by means of disguises and aliases. He commanded the Many Long Arms in the guise of a masked man known only as el Chaddalk. He also posed as el Chaddalk's vizar, Harun yn Riikaln, called the Scarlet Sorcerer. Only his superiors in the Yrshelem, the Rundeen leadership, knew his true identity.

He was also a druzir, a ruler of a city subdivision called a drudach in Almraiven.
