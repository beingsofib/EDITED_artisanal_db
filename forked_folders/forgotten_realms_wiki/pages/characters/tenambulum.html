Tenambulum was a mage that had a reputation as a demon-catcher.

History

It was relatively little known that Tenambulum's timely aid allowed the Zarovich family to survive when they were displaced from their ancestral lands. He was more famous for his skills as a demon-catcher, and especially as a spellsmith who created such items as the band of spell enhancement to better leverage his impressive arcane abilities.

Their library in Laothkund was pilfered by the rogue Gage in , who took the Hands of Paymon.
