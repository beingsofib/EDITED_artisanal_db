Karun the Black was a Bhaalspawn mage who fought alongside Gromnir Il-Khan during the Bhaalspawn crisis in Tethyr during the mid–14 century DR.

Abilties

Karun was a particularly powerful spellcaster, capable of casting spells that could emit the horrifying sound of a banshee, or even stop time for a short duration.

History

When Saradush was under siege by Yaga-Shura in the , Karun remained with Gromnir Il-Khan and other Bhaalspawn while they barricaded himself within the city's castle. He fought alongside and against his divine siblings, when Abdel Adrian and his companions stormed the city's castle.
