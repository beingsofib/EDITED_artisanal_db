S'slaar was a troglodyte chieftain of a tribe in the Wormwrithings area in the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa 1486 DR, he had a disagreement with his war chief H'slaat about the ownership of a magic sword. Soon a civil war started between S'slaar's and H'slaat's loyalists.
