Viola Silvertongue was a musician and minstrel, part of the Circus of the Last Days troupe in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Viola was a colorful human woman with fair skin and light pink hair. She wore red metallic cosmetics and was armed with a rapier.

History

In the , Circus of the Last Days returned from the Feywild and opened its gates to the inhabitants of the Rivington district in Baldur's Gate. Viola Silvertongue entertained guests by performing alongside Calliope Blingsdorf, who believed her be a true wonder. Viola was present at the circus when it was revealed that the Cult of the Absolute murdered one of the troupe – the clown called Dribbles, and replaced him with a murderous doppelganger.
