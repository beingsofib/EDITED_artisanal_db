Stonemason Gergerann was a duergar craftsman of Clan Flameshade that took up residence in Grymforge for a time during the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Clan Flameshade were hired by the Cult of the Absolute's Sergeant Thrinn to help Nere with the excavation of Grymforge with the aim of staking a claim on the Adamantine Forge. Gergerann helped Kith in his studies of the ruined architecture of the underground fortress.
