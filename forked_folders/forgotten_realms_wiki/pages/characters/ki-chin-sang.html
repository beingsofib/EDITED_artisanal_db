Ki Chin Sang was a ghostly monk guardian of the Crypt of Durkon in Kara-Tur in the 14 century DR.

Possessions

Ki Chin Sang guarded the Key of the Master's Crypt that unlocked Nan Kung Chi's tomb.

History

Ki Chin Sang died many moons before the late 14 century DR. His undeath was spent guarding the Crypt of Durkon in Shou Lung. In the , the crypt faced an illithid incursion led by Goxxa the alhoon. The mind flayers consumed the ancient preserved minds of the crypt's dead and sought the Claw of the Black Leopard. At the same time, a vampire thief named Hexxat arrived at the tomb on the behest of her master Larloch, who sought the tomb's magical treasure—the Claw of the Black Leopard. Ki Chin Sang stood guard at Nan Kung Chi's tomb and demanded anyone seeking entrance to defeat six of Nan Kung Chi's trusted undead lieutenants.
