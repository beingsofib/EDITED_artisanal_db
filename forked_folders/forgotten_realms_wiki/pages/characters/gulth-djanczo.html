Gulth Djanczo was a tavern owner inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Gulth was a weasley man who spoke in a nasal voice.

Activities

He owned the Full Cup located at the southeast intersection of Coach Street and Carter's Way in Waterdeep's Southern Ward.
