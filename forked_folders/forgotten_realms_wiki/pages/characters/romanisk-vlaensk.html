Romanisk Vlaensk was a vampire that took over Stag Falls in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Abilities

Romanisk Vlaensk had no shortage of spells, certainly including alacrity, burning hands, color spray, cone of cold, disguise undead, dispel magic, dispel silence, feeblemind, flame arrow, flaming sphere, glitterdust, grease, guards and wards, hold portal, Imbue undead with spell ability, invisibility, ironguard, magic missile, minor globe of invulnerability, minor spell turning, mirror image, revenance, stoneskin, watchwere and web.

He may have also possessed other spells such as Bloodstone's frightful joining, charm monster, globe of invulnerability, hold monster, ice storm, ironguard, Mordenkainen's magnificent mansion, screen, spell engine, spell turning, lightning bolt, and even Mordenkainen's disjunction.

Possessions

Notably, the vampire had a crystal parrot. His spellbook could not be found.

Activities

After being trapped underground, but before taking over Stag Falls, Romanisk studied magic in his underground abode for centuries.

Relationships

The vampire had turned Vilhelm von Stag Falls into an undead in his thrall, and also charmed Doreta, a local young woman, for companionship. He was also served by an umber hulk, and a killer mimic. He was not known by name to anyone else, and kept numerous undead in thrall.

History

Centuries before the 14 century DR, Romanisk Vlaensk was a vampire and the master of the castle of the future Stag Falls village; his heraldic device was ermines with a red circle. However, he was defeated, and forced to retreat into the foundations of his castle, which his foes collapsed, sharing the tale so that no one would try and dig in there. He spent centuries underground, merely experimenting with magic, until released by the excavations commissioned by Baron Vilhelm von Stag Falls, who was investigating the history of the region. The baron's line had rebuilt Romanisk's castle and refounded the surrounding village; Vilhelm had the Baron, whom he turned into an undead creature, charm his staff and bring Romanisk live villagers to feed on.

Among other acts, Romanisk summoned bats, stranding the werebat Minsk from the Demiplane of Dread in the Prime Material Plane, had the local church of Torm destroyed, the local blacksmith, Churles, brought to the castle to outfit the army he meant to raise, the local huntmaster, Jeagermeister, killed, along with his dogs, for baying too loudly in his presence, had two other villagers brought to him, driving one of them to suicide, He had animated many others he made meals of.

When adventurers from Ravens Bluff were finally sent to investigate why the village had missed its taxes, about one month into Romanisk's reign, they eventually defeated the undead Vilhelm von Stag Falls, and proceeded into the catacombs to challenge Romanisk.
