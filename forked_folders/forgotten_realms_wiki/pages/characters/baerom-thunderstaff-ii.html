Lord Baerom Thunderstaff II was a Tethyrian noble and practitioner of magic inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

He lived within Thunderstaff Villa located in the North Ward where Thunderstaff Way met Immar Street.

Relationships

Lord Baerom was the patriarch of the Thunderstaff noble family of Waterdeep. His consort was Marle Thunderstaff.
