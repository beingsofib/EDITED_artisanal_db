Byuchyuch was a goblin slave of House Do'Urden in the drow city of Menzoberranzan.

History

Byuchyuch became an "indentured servant" to House Do'Urden circa 1307 DR.

In or around 1317 DR, he was summoned by Maya and Malice Do'Urden to play a part in their plan to force Drizzt Do'Urden to shed blood before entering Melee-Magthere, the drow fighters' academy. Malice magically turned Byuchyuch's appearance into that of a drow and Maya presented him as her champion, challenging Drizzt to a fight. Drizzt easily overcame the poor fighter. While Byuchyuch was helpless, Drizzt was magically compelled by Malice to kill him. Doing so made Byuchyuch the first being Drizzt had killed.
