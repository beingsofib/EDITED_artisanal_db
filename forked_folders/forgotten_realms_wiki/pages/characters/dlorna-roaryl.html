Dlorna Roaryl was the princess of Eskorn, Laerakond, as of 1479 DR. She was a member of the Roaryl dynasty.

Personality

Dlorna was an intelligent, just, and diplomatic woman.

Background

In 1479 DR, Princess Dlorna was very popular among Eskornar nobles, who wanted to elevate her to the throne to replace her father as the monarch of Eskorn.

Relationships

Princess Dlorna was the younger daughter of King Ornan Roaryl, and the youngest sister of Imrar and Flandarr Roaryl.
