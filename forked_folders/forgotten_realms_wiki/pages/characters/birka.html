Brawler Birka was a goblin member of the Cult of the Absolute who dwelled in the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Birka wore a leather harness and loincloth, fortified with chainmail and metal plates. She covered her face in a leather mask and cloth hood, and wore a necklace decorated with what appeared to be animal bones.

History

In the , the druid Halsin—in the form of a bear—and several adventurers attempted to infiltrate the Shattered Sanctum. They were caught by the Cult of the Absolute; while the adventurers managed to get away, Halsin the bear did not. The goblins imprisoned him in the dungeons deep within the Shattered Sanctum.

Birka took several goblin children into the dungeons to ridicule and torture what they believed was just a bear.
