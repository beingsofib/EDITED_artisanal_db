Indith Shalla was a nightcloak of Shar that lived in the city of Elversult during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Beyond her devotion to Shar, Indith was a leader within the Cult of the Dragon and headed their cell in Elversult.

Relationships

She served as the high priestess to Hethcypressarvil, the black dracolich that reigned the Dragon Cultists of Pros and Elversult.

Home

She lived within Blacknight Manor, located just outside the city between the Trader's Way and the Snake Road.
