Sylsinssath Sultlue was a yuan-ti pureblood and member of the Sultlue family of Waterdeep.

History

Once part of House Eselemas in Lushpool, Sylsinssath was forced to flee Lapaliiya during the mid–13 century DR by his cousin and rival. Sylsinssath settled in the City of Splendors where he remade his fortune. He slumbered within Sultlue Vault in the City of the Dead as of the , and was attended by tainted ones and other members of the Sultlue family.
