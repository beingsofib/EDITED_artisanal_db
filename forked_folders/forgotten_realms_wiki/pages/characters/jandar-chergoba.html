Jandar Chergoba was the Head Chef of the Cassalanter family in Waterdeep, who worked in Cassalanter Villa in the Sea Ward circa the late 15 century DR.

Abilities

Jandar possessed spellcasting abilities, appropriate for being a cultist of Asmodeus. With the use of a single command word, "For'zaal," he could animate a dozen of kitchen knives to become animated akin to flying swords.

Possessions

Jandar owned a regalia of Asmodeus, his cultist uniform, which included a golden sacrificial dagger, a golden mask, and crimson robes. He kept it hidden in a lockbox, hidden underneath sacks of potatoes in Cassalanter Villa kitchens.

History

During his tenure as a head chef in the , Jandar oversaw the manse's kitchens and determined the family's daily menus.
