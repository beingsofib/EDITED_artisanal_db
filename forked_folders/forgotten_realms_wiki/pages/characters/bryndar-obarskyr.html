King Bryndar was the third Obarskyr monarch of Cormyr.

He was born in the Year of the Vow Manifest, 44 DR, and was the only son of Imlon Obarskyr, the second king of Cormyr. Bryndar reigned as king himself from the Year of Lamplit Nights, 57 DR, aged only 13, and ended his reign in the Year of Gleaming Frost, 64 DR, at age 20. Bryndar died young without producing an heir.

He was succeeded by his uncle, Eskruis.
