Sable (falcon)

Person
nameSable
homeCaer Callidyrr, Alaron
raceFalcon

Sable was the falcon companion of Keren Donnell in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Sable was a huge black falcon.

History

In 1345 DR, the first time Tristan Kendrick and his companions fought a group of firbolgs, Keren and Sable arrived just in time to save the day, but not before Arlen was slain.

A while later, it was Sable who told Robyn that Keren had been captured by firbolgs and needed their help.
