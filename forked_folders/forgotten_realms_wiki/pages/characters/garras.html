Garras was the elven ghost beloved of Elena whose final resting place in Myth Drannor was disturbed in 1369 DR.

Possessions

Elena's pewter locket was a delicate magical amulet that she gifted to her beloved at some point before their deaths. The item possessed enchantment that increased the wearer's wisdom and intelligence.

Garras' Keen Sight was a unique magic ring in Garras' possession before his death.

Relationships

Garras' lover Elena was haunting Turgild's Square in Dwarven Dungeons' of Myth Drannor.

History

In 1369 DR, Elena encountered the adventurers from Phlan seeking to stop the Cult of the Dragon plan of corrupting the elven city's mythal. She asked them to restore the magical mechanism that powered her favorite fountains by defeating a powerful dwarven statue golem called the Lifespring Guardian in the Deep Halls She also left heroes with a cryptic message that led them to restore the dignity and sanctity of Garras' and her own final resting places.

The lovers' restless spirits lent their strength to the battle with the Guardian. Subsequently, the heroes defeated the golem, sending Elena and Garras into the afterlife.
