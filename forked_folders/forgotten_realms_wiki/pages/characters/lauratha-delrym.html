Lauratha Delrym was a cleric of Chauntea with the rank of Trueseed that resided in the Bounty of the Goddess temple in Voonlar.

__NOTOC__

Description

Lauratha had dark hair and plain features.

Personality

She maintained a prim and proper decorum, choosing to be quiet and observant, which disguised her boundless curiosity about people and their activities.

Relationships

She knew everyone in Voonlar and kept up to date on how they spent their time.
