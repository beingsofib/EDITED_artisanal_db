Harvas Peltsen was one of the leaders of Teziir in 1359 DR.

History

Harvas was one of the founders of Teziir and sat on the Teziir City Council.

In 1359 DR, Harvas knew that a gift from the Lords of Waterdeep to King Azoun Obarskyr IV would pass through his city.
