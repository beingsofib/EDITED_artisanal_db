First Sword Sauvar was an armsman of Hastarl in Athalantar in the mid–3 century DR.

Activities

He guarded Athalgard.

Relationships

Sauvar served under the Magelords of Athalantar.

Possessions

He was armed with a sword.

History

Sauvar was part of the same garrison as the soldier Riol during the siege of Athalgard in the , during the siege led by Prince Elminster Aumar. He was slain by Tharl Bloodbar, a Knight of Athalantar in opposition to the Magelords, who stormed the castle from a secret passage.
