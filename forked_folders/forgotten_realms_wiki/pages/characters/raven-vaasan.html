Raven was a Vaasan man that accompanied Vala Thorsdotter along her journey to the west to seek out Melegaunt Tanthul in early 1370s DR.

Relationships

He was a relative of Sable, a woman that was acquainted with Prince Melegaunt during the late 13 century DR.

History

The Vaasan expedition ended when Vala and her fellow Vaasans arrived at the Greypeak Mountains on Nightal 20 in the . Raven remained with the group during their initial encounter with elves of the Tomb Guard, an attack from a phaerimm behind the Sharn Wall, and onward as they entered into the city of Evereska.
