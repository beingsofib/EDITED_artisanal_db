Toarin Klustoon was a slyblade assassin for the Zhentarim in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a small, agile man.

Personality

Though a killer, he cared about his friends.

Abilities

He executed notably complex assassination maneuvers, even in combat with other stealthy operatives.

Possessions

He carried strangling wire and a dagger.

Relationships

He was friends with Strabbin Stillcorn and Rungo Baerlan, fellow Zhentarim.

History

Toarin Klustoon was one of Calivar Murpeth's people in Warmfires House during a night revel in the . A woman they captured, Talantha, claimed she'd only helped the scribe Maervidal Iloster escape the revel after his poisoning because she was threatened by Blaeragh Ridranus, another Zhentarim slyblade. Toarin slew him after a brief struggle. She then fingered four other operatives as having helped Ridranus. Those were killed by fellow slyblade Taerach Thone; Toarin became restless at that point, and demanded Talantha be more forthcoming, as he affected certainty Strabbin Stillcorn and Rungo Baerlan were not traitors. The woman soon revealed herself to be Storm Silverhand in disguise, and killed him.
