Ucharntar Sendril was the proprietor of Sendril's Silks in the town of Ormath of the Shining Plains in the 14 century DR.

History

As of the 14 century DR, Elminster Aumar's investigated, Ucharntar Sendril and uncovered the shopkeep to be one of the most active agents of the Cult of Tiamat at the time. The old wizard informed the Harpers of his findings and recommended for the agents to watch Sendril's Silks as the cult's shillings indicated inevitable expansion into Sembia and Westgate.
