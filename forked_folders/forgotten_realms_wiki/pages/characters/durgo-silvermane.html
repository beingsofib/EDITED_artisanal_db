Durgo Silvermane is the eldest son of Jhaele Silvermane.

History

As of 1375 DR, Durgo has taken over most of the day to day administrative duties of running The Old Skull Inn from his mother, Jhaele. In addition to his duties as an innkeeper, Durgo farms the family farm.
