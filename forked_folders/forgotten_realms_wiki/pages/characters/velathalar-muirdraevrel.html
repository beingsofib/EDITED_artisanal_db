Velathalar Muirdraevrel was an elf warrior in Myth Drannor.

History

In 1487 DR, during heavy fighting against Sembian mercenaries, Velathalar met again Storm Silverhand, together with Arclath Delcastle and Amarune Whitewave. Storm and Velathalar started considering a retreat from the city. Soon after, the mercenaries managed to break the wall, killing most of the defenders.

Relationship

Velathalar was an acquaintance of Storm Silverhand.
