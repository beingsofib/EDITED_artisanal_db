Ormbras Delzord was a lamplighter inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Ormbras was the guildmaster for the Guild of Chandlers and Lamplighters during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He held the title of "Master of the Flame" within the guild and worked out of the House of Light–their headquarters in the Trades Ward.
