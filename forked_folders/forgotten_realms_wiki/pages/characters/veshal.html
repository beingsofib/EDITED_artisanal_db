Veshal was a dragonsoul of the Cult of the Dragon that operated on the isle of Tuern during the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

He wielded a massive spear in battle.

Relationships

Veshal rode atop an enormous red drake.

History

Some time during the 1480s DR, Veshal was stationed at the entrance to inner reaches of the personal lair of Tuern's red dragon tyrant Lostmauth. He remained there the same time that the Dragon Cult's leader Severin Silrajin ventured within to parlay with Lostmauth for the destruction of the city of Neverwinter.
