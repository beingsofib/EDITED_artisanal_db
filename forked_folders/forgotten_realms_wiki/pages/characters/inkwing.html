Inkwing was a pixie in the Quivering Forest.

Activities

Inkwing was one of the pixies living in the secret village of Thornbud.

History

During the reclamation of Phlan, some adventurers working under the suggestion of the Emerald Enclave asked the help of the pixies and Inkwing acted as the village's spokesman.
