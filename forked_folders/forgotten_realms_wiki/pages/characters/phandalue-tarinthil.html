Phandalue Tarinthil was a human counter clerk for the Aurora's Realms Shop, High Road Catalogue Counter in the North Ward of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

She was known as a stunning beauty.

Personality

Phandalue had a sharp tongue and no patience or time for those she deemed fools. Nevertheless, it seemed half the young noblemen of the city courted her.
