Gabourey D'Vaelan was a cook who lived in Baldur's Gate during the late 15 century DR. She worked for the Vanthampur patriar family at their estate in the city's Temples District circa 1494 DR.

Gabourey was rather fastidious and could not abide anyone in her kitchen while she prepared meals.
