Kymas Nahpret was a vampire Red Wizard of Thay and a necromancer in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , together with Umara Ankhlab, Kymas was tasked by Szass Tam with the mission of finding the young Chosen of Lathander, Stedd Whitehorn. When Umara and the pirate captain Anton Marivaldi finally decided to instead help Stedd in his mission for Lathander, Kymas tried to stop them but was instead killed.
