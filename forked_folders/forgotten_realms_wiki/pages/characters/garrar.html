Garrar the Powerful was a hero who fought agains a hill giant menace some years before the mid-14 century DR.

History

Garrar became a hero after he grew tired of loosing loved ones to hill giant attacks. He somehow acquired an enchanted belt called the Destroyer of the Hills that protected him fro the giants' clubs. There were no historic records that revealed the origins of the belt. But with the item's help, Garrar slew dozens of hill giants, driving them away from the land.
