Currag Blackstone was the son and heir to Earl Angus Blackstone on the Moonshae Isles. He died suddenly in mysterious circumstances in the .

History

Currag was summoned to the gate of his father's manor on a rainy night when an old, grey-haired man approached an inner gate, having inexplicably overcome a palisade. Currag initially reacted with hostility to the old man, setting the family's pack of moorhounds on him. The old man was able to stop the moorhounds with a spoken command, and send them fleeing with another. Currag then took a sword from one of the manor's guards and stabbed the old man, who appeared to fall over and die. The next morning, Currag's body was found lying on the stone paving displaying a terrified expression, apparently having fallen to his death.
