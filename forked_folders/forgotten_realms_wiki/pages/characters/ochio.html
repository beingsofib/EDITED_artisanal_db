Ochio was the myoshu of Myudo village in Kozakura in 1357 DR.

History

In 1357 DR, Myudo was under the control of evil sohei of the Black Temple who terrorized Ochio and other villagers with stories of the dai-oni who led them. They forced Ochio and others to assist in rituals to the dai-oni and to pay heavy tributes.

Personality

Ochio did not like responsibility, being an indecisive individual. Also, in private, he was dominated by his wife.
