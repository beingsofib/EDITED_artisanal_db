Borok Thundercracker was a dwarfish hero who lived long before the 14 century DR.

History

Borok's fame came after he led several successful military campaigns against giants and other "tall vermin" that menaced dwarven communities at the time. His enchanted war hammer faced many opponents, gaining its name – Borok's Fist, the weapon that was called his "little iron fist" by Borok's elven allies behind his back. Many thought the weapon to be blessed. Subsequently, Borok Thundercracker's war hammer left the dwarf's possessions, eventually ending up in the city of Athkatla in Amn.
