Sawaka was a pan lung member of the Celestial Bureaucracy.

Activities

Sawaka was one of the officers of the Qui sub-committee that governed the affairs of the animal population of the Fochu Peninsula under orders of the Ministry of Animal Affairs of the Celestial Bureaucracy.

Sawaka oversaw about the scaly animals of the land. He ruled Qui during the Season of the Dragon. He governed directing 60 human slaves. He did not care for intruders.

History

Sawaka was appointed at the Qui sub-committee after the expulsion of Za-Jikku.
