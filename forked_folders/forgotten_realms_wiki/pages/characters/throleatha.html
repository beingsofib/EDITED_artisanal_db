Throleatha was a gold elf sorceress and the proprietor of Throleatha's Public House in Leuthilspar on the island of Evermeet in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

She was sometimes called into the service of Queen Amlaruil Moonflower to communicate with nobles or with agents in Faerûn.
