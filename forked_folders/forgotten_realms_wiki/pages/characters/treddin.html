Treddin was a commoner human from the City of Coin, Athkatla in Amn who lived in the 14 century DR.

History

Treddin met his demise in the . He, along with several other men, was captured by a drow druid Pai'Na who built a massive spider nest in ancient tombs underneath Athkatla's Grave District. Samar, Bene, Philbert, Treddin were consumed from the inside by Pai'Na's spiders.
