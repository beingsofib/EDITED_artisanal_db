Looxil

Looxil was a conjurer who once served Umberlee, the Sea Queen, years before the 14 century DR.

History

Looxil committed a grave mistake of betraying Umberlee. Years later, the conjurer was assassinated by Dami Darn, a rogue to exacted the Sea Queen's revenge. As the reward for Looxil's slaying, Dami was gifted an enchanted set of leather armor called the Armor of Deep Night.
