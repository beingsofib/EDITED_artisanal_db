Kadokawa Yori was a samurai who assisted in running a posthouse in Kozakura in 1357 DR.

Activities

In a very tranquil posthouse inside a Niwa shoen, located at an equal distance between Tamanokuni and Kuda, Kadokawa assisted Hori Gutsu as his second-in-command.
