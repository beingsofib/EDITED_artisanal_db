Silfy was a tiefling child from Elturel who was kicked out of her home along with all others of her kind in the late 15 century DR.

Description

She had pale white skin and horns that protruded outwards and down.

Activities

Silfy was one of the members of Mol's, exiled tiefling children who found purpose in a life of crime. Silfy's job was to distract passersby so they could more easily be pickpocketed.

History

In the , Elturel was pulled down into the Nine Hells for a brief party. The catastophe, which came to be known as the Descent, led to the mass-exodus of tieflings from the city, an action made against their will. The tieflings rallied under the leadership of Zevlor, a former member of the city's Hellriders. For a time, the Elturian tieflings were able to find temporary safe haven in the druidic stronghold of the Emerald Grove.
