Daudon yn Saibh el Yakan was a nirdey, or a chief collector, for the Rundeen mercantile organization in Memnon, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR.

History

Daudon worked as a bandit, bodyguard, mercenary, and enforcer for the Rundeen for many years until his age made it difficult to do these jobs. Therefore, he asked for a more sedentary job and was appointed as a nirdey of Memnon.

Activities

As well as a collector for the Rundeen, Daudon served as a contact for Rundeen caravans leaving Memnon for the north and for Rundeen-affiliated mercenary companies monitoring the area for non-Rundeen merchants. With his past experiences, he was well-respected by the younger mercenaries and enforcers, so they came to him for help with their professional problems. Daudon also on occasion hired adventurers for some small missions on behalf of the Rundeen.

Description

As of 1370 DR, Daudon was on the cusp of middle age, but remained a tough figure.
