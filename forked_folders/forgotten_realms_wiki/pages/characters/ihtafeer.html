Ihtafeer was a female rakshasa who posed as Adratha, the potion seller at Druid Grove, after killing the original. She was an enemy of the dao of Khan Zahraa.

Eventually, Gorion's Ward became involved with the dao, who asked him to find and kill Ihtafeer. With the help of the Shadow Thief of Amn Itona, Gorion's Ward unveiled Ithafeer's true identify and after fighting killed her. Later, Gorion's Ward took her head to the dao as proof of her death.
