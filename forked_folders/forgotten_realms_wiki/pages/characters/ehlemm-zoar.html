Ehlemm Zoar was a guildmaster inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, in the mid-to-late 13 century DR. He was the guildmaster of the Gemcutters.

Activities

In the , Ehlemm Zoar was one of two guildmasters to survive the brutal Guildwars that occurred in Waterdeep several years after Ahghairon's death. Ehlemm and his fellow surviving guildmaster, Lhorar Gildeggh, declared themselves the "Two Lords Magister" of the city.
