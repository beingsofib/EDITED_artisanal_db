Captain Thardock was a human Purple Dragons officer who was deployed in Tilver's Gap in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Thardock was a huge man with a red mustache.

Possessions

He fought with a bastard sword and short sword, and carried a rod of cancellation for dealing with magically equipped threats.

Activities

Around 1358 DR, Thardock commanded a cavalry patrol on the roads near Tilverton, alongside other squads commanded by Captain Pripyn and Captain Orlberl.
