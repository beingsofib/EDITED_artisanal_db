Mica Foebreaker was a member of the Cult of the Black Earth.

History

In 1491 DR, Mica led a cell of her fellow cultists on a mission to observe a plot by the Cult of the Howling Hatred to kidnap monks from the Monastery of the Yellow Rose, in order to use them in a new plot to access an elemental node. They planned to seize the Howling Hatred's prize at the right time.

However, she was killed with all her companions.
