Yavendel () was a druid of the Emerald Enclave in Mulmaster.

History

In 1491 DR, Yavendel found some of her burrowing mammal friends acting strange. Investigating, she discovered that they had escaped into a tunnel inside the city. Following them inside, Yavendel exited in the Oxpit Tavern, where she met an adventuring party hired to locate some vandals.

Activities

In 1491 DR, Yavendel patrolled just outside the city at the behest of the Emerald Enclave.
