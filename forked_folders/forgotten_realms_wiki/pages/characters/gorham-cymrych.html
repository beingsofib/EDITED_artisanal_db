Gorham Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles at the turn of the 4 century DR.

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the grandson of the dynasty’s legendary namesake, Cymrych Hugh. He was the brother of his predecessor, High King Gwylloch, and his father ruled before that as High King Carrig I. Gorham's successor was his nephew, Markus Cymrych.

History

Gorham assumed the throne of the High King in the , after Gwylloch and his first son, Ketheryll, killed each other and their retainers at the Castle of Skulls.

He ruled for twenty-three years before Gwylloch's second son, Markus, slew him and took the throne in the .
