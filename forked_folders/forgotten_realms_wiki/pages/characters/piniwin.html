Piniwin was an apprentice alchemist who worked in the city of Neverwinter during the late 15 century DR.

Description

The apprentice had blond hair.

Possessions

While in Ebon Downs, Piniwin dressed in a green tunic.

Relationships

Piniwin learned her craft under the supervision of Hazel, who was studying the effects of the Blacklake sludge that appeared in the Blacklake District of Neverwinter.

She had a sister named Penny who also worked with Hazel.

History

Following the siege of Neverwinter in the , Piniwin went with Hazel to the Blacklake District to study the sludge. There, she sold a variety of healing potions to those adventurers who were aiding in the efforts to cleanse and rebuild the city. Later, Piniwin sought out adventurers to help Hazel fight off the sludge-covered zombies that were being summoned by Therzt, the Chosen of Ghaunadaur.

Later, Piniwin traveled to the Ebon Downs to sell her wares to Doomguide Vesper Soulhearth and his fellow priests of Kelemvor in the Temple of Kelemvor.
