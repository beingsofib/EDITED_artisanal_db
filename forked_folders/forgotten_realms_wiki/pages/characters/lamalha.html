Lamalha was a female assassin of the Iron Throne and cleric of Cyric who lived on the Sword Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

She traveled in an all-female party, which included Maneira, Telka, and Zeela.

History

In the , Lamalha and her allies were hired by the Iron Throne to kill Abdel Adrian. The woman ambushed the Bhaalspawn outside the Nashkel mine as he and his companions emerged from a shaft after confronting Mulahey, a servant of the Iron Throne. Presumably, Lamalha did not survive the ambush.
