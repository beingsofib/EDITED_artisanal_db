Helmhorn was a Brigadier of the Cult of the Dragon circa 1369 DR during the occupation of the ruins of Myth Drannor, stationed in the Elven Catacombs underneath Castle Cormanthor.

History

In 1369 DR, Helmhorn was under tremendous pressure from the leader of the Cult of the Dragon, Kya Mordrayn, who wanted the Sisterhood of Silver Fire found, captured, and brought to her. Helmhorn ordered Lieutenant Grudy to get off his lazy ass and find the women by the nightfall or have his bulbous nose cut off.
