Que Nhon

Person
nameQue Nhon
homeBralizzar, Dustwall Mountains, Durpar
occupationGem dealer

Que Nhon was a gem dealer in the town of Bralizzar in the Dustwall Mountains in the mid-14 century DR. Their shop was a large one, just north of an alleyway on the southern end of the western side of the bazaar.
