Marcus Aboy was a half-orcish bodyguard who worked for the Mulman spice merchant Silas Billmore in the late 14 century DR.

History

Marcus began his employment with Silas Billmore at some point before the late 14 century DR. The bodyguard traveled with his master and his mistress – an ex-slave from Thay named Tyvia Velent. The Merchant House of Mossbridges was among business travel destinations for Marcus's boss. Circa the , Marcus, Silas, and Tyvia were guests of the Golden Goblet Inn in Mossbridges. The trio showed little interest in conversing with the locals nor paid any attention to traveling adventurers.
