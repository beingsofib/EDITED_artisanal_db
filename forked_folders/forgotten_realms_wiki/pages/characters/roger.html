Roger was a pirate crew member of the Green Siren that sailed across the Sea of Fallen Stars in the late 14 century DR.

History

In the , the Green Siren came under attack by the forces of Gethshemeth, a kraken, during a rendezvous with the kuo-toa Nogah on Hegruth, a hidden island of the Sea of Fallen Stars. During the attack, he spotted a sea hag amongst the enemies; the hag killed him with a curse within moments.
