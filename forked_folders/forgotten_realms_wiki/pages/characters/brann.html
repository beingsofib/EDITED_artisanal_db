Brann was a shepherd in Thundarlun during the mid-14 century DR.

Personality

He was curious about adventurers, and thought to embark in one himself.

Possessions

He carried a signaling horn.

Activities

He was a shepherd during the mid-14 century DR.

History

In late , Brann witnessed Shandril Shessair being carried towards Thundarlun by Delg Ironstar and Narm Tamaraith. He blew his signal horn so that the town could anticipate their arrival.
