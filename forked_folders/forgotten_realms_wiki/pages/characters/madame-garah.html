Madame Garah was a businessperson who ran a boarding house in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

Madame Garah owned a boarding house named after herself. Her establishment was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter on the south side of Slop Street.
