Swordcaptain Orin Arroway was a Purple Dragon of Cormyr, who was stationed in the town of Eveningstar around the time of the invasion from Sschindylryn in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Orin was a big man with short white hair. He wore a suit of chain mail.

History

In the mid-1480s DR Orin served in the Purple Dragons, and was deployed in Eveningstar. When Storm Horns orcs came down attempted to raid the village, the attack was defeated and exposed Amskar's operation in the process. While others believed it to be a random attack, Orin agreed with the Harpers Kara Patwin and Lorant Tass that something was going on in the mountains and needed to be investigated. While he was forbidden from sending the Purple Dragons to investigate in the wilderness, he did have the ability to hire and organize outsiders to do the same thing, leading to him sending an attack on the Netherese central camp where the core of the Thultanthar-allied army was based.
