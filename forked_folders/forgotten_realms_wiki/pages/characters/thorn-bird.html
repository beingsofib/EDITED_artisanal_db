Thorn (bird)

Person
nameThorn
raceRaven

Thorn was a raven working for Rhespen Ash during the Dawn Age circa .

Personality

For a raven, the creature was quite snarky.

Relationships

Thorn was one of the woodland animals that the elven wizard and commander Rhespen Ash communed with for information.

History

Around , when Rhespen Ash was trying to find the hidden stronghold of the Duskmere elves, he called upon a number of woodland birds to help him. Thorn was the bird who found them; he reported their location to Rhespen Ash.
