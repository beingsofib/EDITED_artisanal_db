Vodos was the great triton architect who planned the Sharksbane Wall.

History

Vodos was a very ancient triton born possibly in . He was old at the time of the rise of Aryselmalyr. A devout priest of Persana, under his god's inspiration, he erected the early garrisons of the Sharksteeth Mounts and planned and possibly built the Sharksbane Wall.

Vodos's death was surrounded by mystery. In , when very old, he went into the Trench of Lopok and nobody saw him again. Many believed he had chosen Lopok as his resting place but this was only speculation.
