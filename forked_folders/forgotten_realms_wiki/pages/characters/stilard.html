Stilard was a Red Wizard of Thay, apprenticed to Azmyrandyr in the mid-14 century DR.

Description

He was gaunt, with a scarred face.

Personality

Stilard was somewhat perverse and the most disloyal of his fellow apprentices.

Relationships

He was apprenticed to Azmyrandyr, and served by one doppleganger.

History

In , the Red Wizard Roeblen learned through trapped crystal balls he spread to rivals and others that Stilard had been asking a doppleganger of his' to assume the form of his master Azmyrandyr, then that of a woman with spectacular curves, and then bedding it. He considered this a sign that Stilard was going to attempt a betrayal soon.
