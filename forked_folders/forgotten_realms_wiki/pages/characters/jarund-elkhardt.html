King Jarund Elkhardt () was the leader of the Reghedmen Tribe of the Elk barbarians during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Physically, King Jarund was an imposing man of great stature with a face hardened by many years. In contrast, he ruled with an exceptional care and wise decision-making.

History

In the , the king's son Jarund Twice-Born was slain by a bear. Jarund the elder struggled to name an heir to his rule.
