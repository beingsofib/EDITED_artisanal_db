Spar was a squirrel that lived in or around the Emerald Grove during the late 15 century DR.

In the , Spar and his fellow squirrel Corinna came upon the tiefling woman Alfira playing her lute. The pain of her singing was so painful, the pair could barely remain in her vicinity and were nearly forced away.
