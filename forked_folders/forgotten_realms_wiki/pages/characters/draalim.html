Draalim was one of the Blades of the Old Man as of . He was a small Calishite, who often posed as a merchant throughout the Sword Coast. He took a vigil on the night of Tarsakh 19 on the orders of the Old Man of the Mountain at the Garden of Winged Horses in Sentinelspire. By the next day, his remains were found on the Star Fountain. Identification was not completely conclusive, but he wasn't seen again.
