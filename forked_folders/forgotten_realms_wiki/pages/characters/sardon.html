Sardon was a sorcerer dying on or before the mid–8 century DR.

History

At some point before , Sardon had a romantic relationship with Saeraede Lyonora, an apparently unique Netherese ghost-like entity. He fed her his own lifeforce for a time, lasting about as long as Brandagaeris lasted, but she'd drained him completely by the end.
