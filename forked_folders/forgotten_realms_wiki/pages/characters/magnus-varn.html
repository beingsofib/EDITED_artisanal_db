Magnus Varn was a Thayan knight within the enclave in the city of Proskur during the 14 century DR. He served Torisk Haut, the Red Wizard master of spies within the enclave, acting as his personal bodyguard. Magnus often acted as an intermediary between Torisk and his various contacts scattered throughout the city.
