Noyle was a patron of the Cutter's Dip in Yhaunn in the late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a lecherous old man, who smoked a clay pipe.

Relationships

Noyle was on good terms with several of the unsavory patrons of Cutter's Dip, including the thugs Stag and Drik, Kor, and Lahumbra Sashtyn.

History

In Eleasis of 1373 DR, he was approached by the incognito Selûnite priestess Feena Archwood, who was attempting to investigate the local temple of Shar. She failed to get any useful information from him and was accosted by Stag and Drik, who turned him and the other patrons against her. The population of the Cutter's Dip had her at their mercy until she was rescued by Keph Thingoleir.
