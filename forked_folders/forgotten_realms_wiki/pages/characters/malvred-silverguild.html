Malvred Silverguild was a wildly successful merchant inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was a shrewd negotiator and was rarely outfoxed when making deals.

Activities

Malvred lived within southern Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter in Silverguild Manor. He owned the vast complex of buildings known as Prosperity House which, in addition to the main manor, included Prosperity Emporium and the Prosperity Caravan Company.

He was, by far, the wealthiest merchant within Zhentil Keep following the city's destruction in the . Though he did not care much for politics, Malvred was considered by the city's rulers to fill a seat on the new Council of Lords.

Relationships

He had two children from his wife Eunice–a son named Meryn and daughter named Emelia.
