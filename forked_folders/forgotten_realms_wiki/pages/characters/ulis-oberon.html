Ulis Oberon was a member of the Oberon patriar family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

During the Absolute crisis of the , Ulis Oberon was found dead, alongside the bodies of Malghar Caldwel and Flaev Linnacker, within the territory controlled by the Guild and guarded by its thugs, near the entrance to the Guildhall in the Lower City. All three patriars were nothing but charred remains after the Guild failed to collect the trio's debts.
