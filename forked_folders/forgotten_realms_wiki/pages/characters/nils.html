Nils was a sailor for Company Yargo stationed aboard the Buccaneer's Tear in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Nils thought the navigator Irrae spent too much time poking her nose in books, and felt she should spend her time drinking with him instead.

History

When the Buccaneer's Tear sailed up to Pirates' Skyhold above the Sea of Swords in the , the ship landed near a dangerous swamp home to frightful bands of lizardfolk. Nils decided to use this occasion to slack off and drink grog.
