Spatula Farggo was an employee of Baldur's Gate's Elfsong Tavern in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Farggo was a dusky-skinned gold dwarf with a clean-shaven face and blue eyes.

History

In the , Spatula Farggo worked in the Elfsong Tavern's kitchens. When the city began being flooded by refugees of the encroaching army of the Absolute, the dwarf began helping out the beggars in the Lower City, by bringing them kitchen scraps and leftovers.
