Cailan was a mercenary who lived in the Realms during the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

He wore a suite of plate mail armor while wielding a small shield and long sword.

History

Cailan joined an company led by the elf known as Vail. Together they and their three other companions, raided Ramazith's tower in Baldur's Gate in search of the Helm of Balduran. Unfortunately, Cailan and his comrades were petrified by the gaze of a basilisk, a fact of which he seemed aware before his transformation.

The stone statues that once were men were sold to the noble art collector Felonius Gist, displayed proudly within his estate in the city.
