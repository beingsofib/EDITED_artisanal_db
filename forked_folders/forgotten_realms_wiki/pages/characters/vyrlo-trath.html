Vyrlo Trath was a member of the Cult of the Crushing Wave in Mulmaster.

Relationships

Trath was a warrior affiliated with the Cult of the Crushing Wave.

History

In 1491 DR, he was chosen to lead an expedition into the sewers of Mulmaster, joined by Xaxan Thavish of the Cult of the Eternal Flame, to find the tomb of an ancient kuo-toa mystic devoted to Olhydra and Bwimb. However, a band of troglodytes surprise-attacked their camp, killing Vyrlo.
