Jessup was a traveler along the High Road between Neverwinter and Luskan during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Jessup had brown hair and a thin mustache.

History

Sometime around , Jessup joined the merchant's caravan of Tollin and his crew going from Neverwinter to Luskan, accompanied by members of the Order of the Burning Dawn. However, during the journey, Jessup claimed to have been bitten by Guen, the girl Jaena's dog, and demanded that the dog be put down. However, it was revealed by the Champion of the Burning Dawn that Jessup had provoked the dog in a drunken state by trying to force-feed it brandy, and the case was dropped.
