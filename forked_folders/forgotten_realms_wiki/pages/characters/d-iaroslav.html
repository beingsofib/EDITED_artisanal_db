D. Iaroslav was a Raumviran tavern owner who lived in Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the .

Activities

Iaroslav owned and operated the Fallow Field Tavern. His tavern was located in the southwest section of Almorel in the shadow of the Moneylender's Guild.
