Xittin Silvertongue was a human adventurer and sorcerer in Cormyr during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Xittin had short black hair and wore a long violet robe.

Personality

Xittin was a very patient, understanding man.

History

Xittin stayed at the ruined Beckside Castle on the High Road around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar. That night, he kept the company of the bard Lyrica Whitestone, who had been driven to an insensible state by her infuriating companion Slade Destinaxe. The interaction consisted mainly of him consoling and agreeing with the bard as she ranted about "smite-for-brains".
