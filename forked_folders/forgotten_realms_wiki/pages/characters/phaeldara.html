Phaeldara was a female human sorceress and wizard living in Velprintalar in Aglarond circa 1372 DR.

Activities

She was an apprentice of the Simbul.

History

In 1374 DR, she acted as regent when the Simbul was briefly indisposed. She dealt with Araevin Teshurr.
