Fayaniz al-Nokar was an employee of the House of Wo in Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Activities

Fayaniz handled the daily workings of the , leaving Wo free to deal with food preparation.

History

Fayaniz was initially attracted to the House of Wo because of a desire to learn foreign magic from his employer. Wo taught Fayaniz a few spells, but the language barrier made progress slow for Fayaniz.
