Sh'aun Darnruel was a drow merchant of Menzoberranzan in the mid–14 century DR. She was one of the more wealthy and influential merchants in the city at the time.

Sh'aun sold various personal fashions, including clothes, body dyes, and augmentations, such as crests, stick-on wigs or manes of hair, body padding, and artificial limbs.

She had her home and shop in the Manyfolk (Duthcloim) area of Menzoberranzan.
