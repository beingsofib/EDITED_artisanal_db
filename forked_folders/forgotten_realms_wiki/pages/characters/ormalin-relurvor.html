Ormalin Relurvor

Ormalin Relurvor was the brother of Xavander the Great. He embezzled a large part of the treasury of Ravens Bluff in the early 14 century DR.

History

During the , Ormalin and Xavander exiled themselves in the Border Kingdoms, using a volcano Xavander found there to melt the treasure Ormalin embezzled.They minted counterfeit Amnian and Calishite coins with the resulting metals, and defined the basics of the future Realm of the Mount.

Ormalin and Xavander disagreed on the direction their newfound kingdom should take: the former wanted a pompous realm of worldwide importance, while the latter wanted to govern over a secretive nation. During a furious argument among brothers, Ormalin died by falling into a lava rift.
