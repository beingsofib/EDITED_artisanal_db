Krastius was a cleric of Cyric who lived on the Sword Coast North in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

At some point, Krastius lost his belief in Kelemvor and decided to follow the teachings of Cyric.

Possessions

The priest was known to wear a green tunic over chainmail, black boots, and breeches.

History

In the 1480s DR, Krastius the Heretic was hired by Samulkin Farcaster, Gromlik Three-Teeth, and Cailyassa Forgedawn to rob a shrine of Kelemvor in the Ebon Downs. Krastius was to break seals of protection to cause undead to attack anyone who came after them, but Krastius was killed when he snuffed the sacred braziers.
