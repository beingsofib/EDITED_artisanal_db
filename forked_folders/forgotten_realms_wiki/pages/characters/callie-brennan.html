Callie Brennan was a resident of Cormyr who lost their life in Lost Souls' Swamp.

Personality

Callie was sweet and kind, even though she was trapped as a cursed soul and had to talk to Evrim Whitehair.

History

While searching for her lost younger brother, Callie drowned while exploring an underground pool. The shadow of Lost Souls' Swamp trapped her soul there for its will-o'-wisps to feed on, along with Kyle Briarbuck and Evrim Whitehair. Though held by the efforts of the shadow's mudmen, vine horrors and wisps, their souls may have been freed by Harpers sent by Eradin Krall and Rina Belostos as they pursued Sshaastess and her Nether Scroll circa 1485 DR. Callie was able to explain that before she died, she had found a key to the ruins deeper in the cave, in which dwelled the predatory shadow. By finding her remains and the key, that would allow the Harpers to delve deeper and destroy the shadow once and for all.
