Thalaumbur was a wealthy merchant in the city of Waterdeep in the 11 century DR.

Personality

Thalaumbur was known be a rather cold and calculating character, what some might describe as villainous.

Activities

Thalaumbur made multiple attempts to earn the title of nobility in Waterdeep, but was repeatedly shunned.

History

In life Thalaumbur owned a block of buildings in Waterdeep that were enclosed by Sulmor Street and (what would later be named) Thalaumbur's Trot. At some point he attempted to acquire lichdom by purchasing it from someone, though his ultimate fate after this was unknown.
