Khaszlokhar was a great draegloth, a champion of Lolth that served her in the Demonweb Pits.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, Khaszlokhar was assigned to guard the Abyss-ward side of the Rift Between Worlds and the orb of Lolth placed there. Seeking to prevent the destruction of Eberron and Toril, the sage Valana sent a team into the Demonweb to repair the hole in reality and sabotage Lolth's plans. They were able to use a sacrifice of life force to shrink the portal, though that meant that Khaszlokhar fell upon them while they were weakened. Despite the disadvantage, the fearsome draegloth was defeated, and the precious orb of Lolth stolen.
