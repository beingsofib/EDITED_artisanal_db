Bohaim was a young mage from Suzail that served as a member of the Company of Dawn during the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the , Bohaim and the rest of the company, led by the indominable warrior Thurbrand, accompanied the outlaw adventurers Kelemvor, Cyric, Adon, and their newest member Midnight through the Spiderhaunt Wood in Shadowdale.

The journey was disastrous for the company, as many members of their group were slain by the monstrous spiders within. Bohaim was killed when one of the spiders pierced its leg through the young mage's chest, much to the horror of onlookers.
