Zeerith Q'Xorlarrin was the matron mother of House Xorlarrin of Menzoberranzan. and remained in that position as of 1484 DR, when House Xorlarrin moved to the city of Q'Xorlarrin.

Personality

She was known for her diplomatic skills and other houses often called upon her to mediate in disputes.

Matron Mother Zeerith was often quietly derided as a baridame, a derogatory term bandied by dark elf women against others who seemed too deferential to males.

History

Zeerith moved her house from Menzoberranzan to the new city of Q'Xorlarrin in the . Her family was deeply involved with the Darkening.

Relationships

Kharza-kzad Xorlarrin

Zeerith's brother was the mage and later lich Kharza-kzad Xorlarrin.

Horroodissomoth Xorlarrin

Her house patron was the mage Horroodissomoth Xorlarrin until his death during the Spellplague.

Tsabrak Xorlarrin

Circa 1485 DR, Tsabrak was Matron Mother Zeerith Xorlarrin's lover and had been elevated by her to a position of near-equal power.

Jarlaxle Baenre

At some point in love, Zeerith had an intimate relationship with the rogue Jarlaxle.

Zeerith had a number of children, including her daughters Berellip, Saribel and Kiriy and her sons Brack'thal and Ravel Xorlarrin. Brack'thal was both brother and father to Ravel after Zeerith took his seed while he was semi-comatose.
