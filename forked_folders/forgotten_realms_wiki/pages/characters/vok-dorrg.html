Vok Dorrg was the vindictive reigning king of Ruathym sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

Activities

Vok ruled from the Hall of Black Waves. He was too old and blind to lead his troops but he was supported by his many sons, daughters and grandchildren to captain his ship.
