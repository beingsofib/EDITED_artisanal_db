Ghauntz, also known as Ghauntz the Cloaked, was a cleric of Gargauth who went on to become the vice chairman of the Knights of the Shield.

Description

Ghauntz was an old man with a scarred face who always dressed in black with a cape.

History

In adulthood, Ghauntz was a worshiper and a cleric of Gargauth, and it was under the guidance and influence of his god that he rose to the upper echelons of the Knights of the Shield.

Most of his peers considered him to be only a crafty old Keczullan merchant who earned his riches with early investments in Maztican businesses.. But his true mission was to assist Inselm Hhune in understanding the wishes of Gargauth and take control the organization. He also proselytized to the rest of the Shield Council and slowly converted the whole order to the worship of the Hidden Lord.
