Kannoth was an eladrin archfey vampire lord who ruled over Cendriane in the Feywild.

He was described as a man of middle height, dark, delicate and slender, and dressed in a jacket of wine-colored velvet. He carried a lily at the end of a long stalk as a weapon.

It was said that he had an army of undead soldiers who worshipped him as a god. Alternatively, that he was the master of a bloodthirsty cult lurking beneath the earth.

History

He once rode with the Wild Hunt, and although he escaped, he retains a bond of loyalty with the Cerunnos. Many of the Undead that participate in the Wild Hunt are loyal to Kannoth.
