Gordryn Haeront

Gordryn Haeront was a priest of the church of Oghma, called a Namer, based in Waterdeep on the Sword Coast North in the late 15 century DR. By around 1480 DR, Gordryn had gained great prosperity from work in an arts or entertainment industry.
