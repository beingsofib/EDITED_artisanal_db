Mahaxara Khal was a human fighter and the Captain of the Royal Guard in service to Prince Andru of the Rock of Bral in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

She was tall and athletic of build. Mahaxara kept her head shaved bald but for a queue of long dark hair at the back, while tattoos of snakes covered both her arms, giving her what some considered a barbaric look.

Personality

She was a reticent warrior and, having no interest in the politics of the royal court, she commonly kept her thoughts to herself.

Abilities

Very difficult to take by surprise, Mahaxara had an almost superhuman alertness.

Possessions

Mahaxara wielded a +3 two-handed sword that she specialized in, but as back-up she also had a +2 short sword and a light crossbow with a half-dozen +3 bolts and three bolts of slaying, two dedicated to illithids and one to beholders. She wore +4 banded mail and a cloak of the bat. Finally, she had a potion of invisibility.

She had private quarters in Starhaven, the palace of Prince Andru in the High City of Bral. It was a spartan and spotlessly tidy place.

Relationships

She was a completely loyal to Prince Andru and House Cozar, and was never far from his side.

Mahaxara saw right through Lord Diadan Cartan's act as a witty fop and saw him for the vicious murderer he really was. In private, she hated the man and waited for the day she could confront him in a fair fight.

History

Hailing from some faraway world, Mahaxara Khal traveled on a spelljammer privateer before she arrived on the Rock of Bral. Already an experienced warrior, she enlisted in the Royal Guard and within eight years had risen to its command.
