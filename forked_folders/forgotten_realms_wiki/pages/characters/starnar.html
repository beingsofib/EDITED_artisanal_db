Starnar was an ex-sailor proprietor of Starnar's Cozy Cove Rest, a ramshackle and run-down inn in the town of Tulmene in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Starnar had cynical and sarcastic personality.

History

After Starnar was crippled, he was forced to retire from making a living as a sailor. He had a large family of nine or ten unruly daughters born from three wives who perished at some point before Starnar resorted to the career of an innkeeper to feed his spawn. Together with an army of his daughters, Starnar had been running Cozy Cove Rest for some time by the late 14 century DR. Starnar's daughters were actively searching for husbands and attracted a large number of sailors, likely pirates, to their father's inn.
