Melinter was a vile mage who inhabited the city of Waterdeep during the 1350's DR.

History

Melinter organized a gang of apprentices and followers to help hatch his nefarious plans. The wizard preferred meeting in a courtyard located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward with his followers to plan their course of actions. Melinter and his apprentices would use their magic to put any innocent bystanders to sleep in order to discuss their matters without leaving witnesses behind. The courtyard was named after Melinter following his death and his followers continued to meet there to further their master's plans.
