Drannor was an elf from Cormanthor known to be the first to marry a dwarf.

Relationships

Drannor married a dwarf lass.

History

They were considered to be ancient history as of to the Coronal Eltargrim Irithyl.

Legacy

The city of Cormanthor was renamed Myth Drannor in the after the laying of its Mythal, in their honor.
