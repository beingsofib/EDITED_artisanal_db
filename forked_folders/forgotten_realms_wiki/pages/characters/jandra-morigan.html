Jandra Morigan was the co-owner and proprietor of the Kettle of Many Things restaurant in Ravens Bluff, during the late 14 century DR. Before they settled down in the city, Jandra and her husband Mijel were daring adventurers. They had a daughter, Jelima, who was known for her bold demeanor as much as her beauty.
