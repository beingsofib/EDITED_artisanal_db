Halcedon Muiryn was an inhabitant of the Dalelands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He had only one arm, the other one being replaced by a prosthetic pincer.

Activities

A mercenary once, Halcedon Muiryn plied a number of trades, including teaching swordplay, making swords, and spying on traders.

History

After retiring as a mercenary, Halcedon had retired to Shadowdale by , where he plied a number of trades. On that year, he was getting a little handsy with Storm Silverhand.
