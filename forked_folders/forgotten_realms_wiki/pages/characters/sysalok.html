Sysalok was a yuan-ti abomination and a servant of Kharantes in 1375 DR.

History

In 1375 DR, Sysalok commanded a patrol of asabi who surveyed the sands of Anauroch. Eventually, they intercepted an adventuring party in search of the Terraseer but were defeated.
