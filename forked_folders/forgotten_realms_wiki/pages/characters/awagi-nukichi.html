Awagi Nukichi was a writer of biographies in Kara-Tur.

A copy of Awagi's biography of Emperor Joon Tsao Choo—an apparent conflation of the ancient emperors Kujawa and Tan Chin—was kept in the biography reference section of the Great Library of Perpustakaan by circa . It covered Tan Chin's corruption and the destruction of Kuo Meilan, as well as made mention of the later destruction of Tempat Larang.
