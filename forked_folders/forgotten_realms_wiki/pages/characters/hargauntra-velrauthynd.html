Hargauntra Velrauthynd, called the Lady Master of the Waves, was a shipbuilder of luxurious warships from the city of Saerloon active in the late 15 century DR.

History

Hargauntra Velrauthynd was renowned for creating warships with "pamper-cabins", fanciful trimmings, and exquisite brass railings. The ships were mounted with mangonels and experimental ballistae. Hargauntra's construction was done slowly and for a lavish price. Hargauntra's warships were used as flagships and vessels of kings and rulers.
