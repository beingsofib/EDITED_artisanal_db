Laethla was a cook at the Eel Revealed in the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

She was known by name to Arclath Delcastle.

History

In Mirtul of the Laethla was testing a new condiment, and she asked Arclath for his opinion. He liked its peppery spiciness, and thought it should result in plenty of drinks being ordered.
