Antara o' Bones was a Shaaran human cleric of Kelemvor in Lapaliiya in the 14 century DR. As of the , she was the ruler of Sheirlantar, the Lapaliiyan city dedicated to Kelemvor, holding the title of Keeper of Crypts, having been appointed by the Overking of Lapaliiya.
