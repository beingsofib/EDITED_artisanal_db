Lord Artificer Khotan was the Emperor of Lower Imaskar during the Middle Kingdoms period. During his rule, the ancestors of the Mulan people were captured and enslaved.

History

On Khotan's orders as Lord Artificer of Imaskar, artificers created the two gates that would be used to abduct the ancestors of the Mulan, searching for a people who had no grasp of the Art to enslave. A spell was used in order to keep their prayers from reaching their gods.
