Drake Elderwood was a human ranger who roamed the wilds of Faerûn in the mid 14 century DR.

Personality

He hated orcs, hated them with with a passion such, that he would abandon allies and ignore other foes if orcs were arrayed against him. His hate was such that he had vowed to eradicate them from Faerûn. While a superb warrior his erstwhile allies were wary of him as they could not truly count on him in battle.

Possessions

He wielded an enchanted longsword in his scourge of the orcish race.
