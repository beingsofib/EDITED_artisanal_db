Red Weaver was a great bebilith demon that served the forces of Lolth in the late 15 century DR.

History

Partway through the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the during the mid-1480s DR, Draya Dun'Robar led a great attack on the town while Red Weaver served as her personal war-mount at the nearby drow field camp. Unfortunately for them, the Cormyrean counterattack drove away the invaders and attacked the camp. Despite the ferocity of the great demon it was defeated in battle, followed shortly by Draya being slain.
