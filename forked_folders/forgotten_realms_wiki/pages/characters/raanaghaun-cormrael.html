Raanaghaun Cormrael was a member of the Dark Diviners of Windsong Tower in the 7 century DR.

History

He was named for a famous Netherese priest of Shar, long since deceased.

After the theft of the Leaves of One Night and the Book of the Black from the Dark Diviners in the , he left the tower and returned to the Fane of Shadows to learn more about the latter tome. He never returned.

Relations

He was the brother of Shar Cormrael.
