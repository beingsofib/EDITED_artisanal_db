Majuvix was a Maztican native and one of the leaders of New Waterdeep in 1370 DR.

History

Majuvix agreed to go to Faerûn and lived two years in Waterdeep. Around 1369 DR, she went back to Maztica as one of the new Lords of the Borderlands thank to her understanding of both worlds.
