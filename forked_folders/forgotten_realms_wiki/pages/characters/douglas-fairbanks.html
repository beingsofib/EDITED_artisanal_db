Doubleway Douglas Fairbanks was a very ambidextrous owner of one particular pair of the gloves of the balanced hands in the late 15 century DR.

History

Doubleway Fairbanks was known for his extreme ambidexterity. It was said that he was so ambidextrous that "his tears ran down the back of his neck." Whether it was due to owning a pair of ranger gloves of the balanced hands from the Moonwood was unknown. By the , Doubleway's magic gloves left his possession and ended up in the hands of a Harper quartermaster named Talli.
