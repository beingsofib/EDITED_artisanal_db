Karazikar was a beholder living in the Wormwrithings as of the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Like all of its kind, Karazikar was arrogant, hateful, and paranoid. It plotted to seize the Maze Engine.

Relationship

Karazikar had a mad but loyal human servant named Shedrak.

History

Karazikar was a typical beholder until it met the last Great Modron March. Karazikar captured a pentadrone modron and discovered that the reality-shifting Maze Engine was somewhere in the Underdark. So its goal became to find and seize the engine. For this, Karazikar tried to capture as many modrons and similar creatures as possible. At one point, it captured an adventuring party, killed many of them. The survivor, Shedrak, became his favorite slave.

Karazikar was killed by an expedition led by some adventurers, hired by Bruenor Battlehammer to investigate the threat of the demon lords. They were in need of the central eye of a beholder for a rite to banish the demon lords.
