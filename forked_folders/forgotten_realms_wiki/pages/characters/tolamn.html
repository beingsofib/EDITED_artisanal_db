Tolamn was a wizard from Shussel during the early 13 century DR. Between and , he went to a Magefair attended by the Magister, Haelvar Ildagh; there, he attempted to attack the other mages, starting a brawl that was stopped by Azuth and Mystra themselves, feebleminding him and a couple other mages, then restoring to life and health all of their victims. This was shocking to the Magister.
