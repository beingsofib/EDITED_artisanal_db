Khoman was a human fighter, and khan of a minor tribe in the Eastern Realms, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Khan Khoman was focused on establishing a secure holdfast for his people, as they had been driven away from their lands thrice over a span of 30 years. He led his tribe in a roaming life until he could establish himself either under a greater khan or conquer an already established settlement.

Possessions

He wore a set of banded mail while wielding an enchanted defending sword.
