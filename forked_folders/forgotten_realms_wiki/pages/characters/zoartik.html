Zoartik was one of the most violent mind flayers of Ch'Chitl in 1370 DR.

Personality

Zoartik suffered from a mental problem: he could not completely sublimate the mind of his original drow host. This caused homicidal reactions every time that Zoartik saw drow or even drow-made items. Unconsciously, Zoartik was trying to finally suppress the remains of the drow personality
