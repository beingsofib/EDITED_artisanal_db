Cagon Fegg

Person
nameCagon Fegg
occupationPlaywright

Cagon Fegg was a playwright who wrote The Folly and the Fall.

Description

Fegg was described to be a great bard.

Activities

Cagon penned plays that were performed in a few theaters, such as the Everlight Theatre of Elturel. Fegg's play, The Folly and the Fall, was performed by the Sharptongue Travelling Troupe.
