Hronkil Elgredesson was an Illuskan paladin inhabiting Waterdeep during the .

Activities

Hronkil held the rank of Righteous Brother within the Order of the Sun Soul, a monastic order originating in ancient Netheril who followed Amaunator and later Lathander, Selûne, and Sune.
