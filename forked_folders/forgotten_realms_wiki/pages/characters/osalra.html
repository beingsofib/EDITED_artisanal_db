Osalra was the queen of Procalith from at most to , having become the wife of Nasana Melnuthquel's previous lover and puppet ruler of the same city. She was married to him at some point after 293 DR, when Nasana left; she was also a mage of minor skill. When Nasana Melnuthquel returned to Procalith in 297 DR, she attacked and quickly slew Osalra, though she died of heartstop moments after.
