Permidion Stark was a thief and burglar who operated on the Sword Coast in the second half of the 14 century DR.

Possessions

Permidion carried a typical thief's gear: a set of leather armor, a short sword, a bow, a couple dozen arrows, and a singular gemstone.

History

In the , Permidion set his sights on the High Hedge northwest of Beregost—the abode of the wizard Thalantyr. The thief salivated, thinking of all the precious magical trinkets just laying around the laboratory, but eventually decided against the heist. The big-time wizard had two powerful flesh golems guarding the place, invulnerable to non-magical damage, and the High Hedge had no shadows to hide in. Eventually, Permidion Stark set off to find a halfling village to harass, leaving High Hedge behind.
