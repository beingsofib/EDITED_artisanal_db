Asmartha was the innkeeper at Myrkiel's Rest in Narthil during the mid-3 century DR.

Description

She was a stout woman.

Activities

Asmartha was the innkeeper at Myrkiel's Rest, an inn in Narthil.

History

At some point between Marpenoth of the and Flamerule of the , Asmartha had the priestess Elmara as a guest. While she was eating at her establishment, she was accosted by agents of the Magelords of Athalantar, who died in combat with her.

Asmartha then witnessed Elmara regenerating from her injuries, which she attributed to a divine miracle.
