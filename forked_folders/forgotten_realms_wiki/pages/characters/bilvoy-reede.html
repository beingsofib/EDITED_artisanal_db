Bilvoy Reede was a Sharran Dark Adept and an agent from the hidden Cloister of Sombre Embrace in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Bilvoy Reede was tasked with spying on the up and coming Lord Enver Gortash on behalf of Mother Superior Viconia DeVir. Bilvoy reported that Lord Gortash, then just a notorious weapons dealer, was a follower of the godling of tyranny Bane and that he was plotting something with a follower of Bhaal with whom Gortash was seen entering Devil's Fee magic shop and spending an entire hour there interacting with its proprietor, diabolist Helsik, known for her ability to open gates to Hells for extravagant fees.
