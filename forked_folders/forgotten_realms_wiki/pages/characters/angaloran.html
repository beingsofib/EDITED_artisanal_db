Angaloran lived in the Desert Mosque in the northern reaches of Zakhara's High Desert in 1367 DR.

Personality

Angaloran was a quiet individual.

Activities

Angaloran was the keeper of the Desert Mosque. Like the Dome Dancers who also worshiped there, Angaloran was a follower of Haku.
