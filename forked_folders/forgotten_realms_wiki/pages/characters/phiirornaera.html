Phiirornaera of Yacla was a mage. She was petrified by the Magister Kurtal at some point prior to and remained in stone form by the late 14 century DR.

History

Phiirornaera was petrified by Kurtal at some point before and put in an embarassing position by his bath to serve as a towel-holder; even in stone form, she somehow co-opted the web of spells he used to query the heads of his rivals for knowledge and advice. She influenced him so into ever bolder actions.

About a century after his death, by , a rockslide covered the cavern home of Kurtal; still petrified, Phiirornaera remained in there until at least the late 14 century DR.
