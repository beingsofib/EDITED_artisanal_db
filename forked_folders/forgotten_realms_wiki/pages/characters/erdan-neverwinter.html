Erdan was a stern human fighter student of the Neverwinter Adventurer's Academy in the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

Erdan attended the same martial combat classes as the person who would become the Hero of Neverwinter. Lamy many fellow students, Erdan looked up to Aribeth de Tylmarande, the Champion of Neverwinter, and the Defender of Tyr's Faith, and hoped to meed the hero face-to-face.

History

Erdan was one of the Neverwinter Academy students in the during the deadly outbreak of the Wailing Death. Although Erdan was capable fighter, his life was tragically cut short in the attack on the Academy.
