Ravenar Andarlis was a Netherese businessman who operated the Grocer Cartel and Andarlis Transport in Netheril before its destruction in .

Activities

Ravenar served as the guildmaster of the Grocer Cartel and owned Andarlis Transport as well. He was a fair dealer in his affairs, but only used favorable contracts whenever possible, and was utterly ruthless when he had the advantage. He was rumored to be the master of the thieves' guild in the Loft, but it was actually controlled by his brother, Silvin Andarlis.
