Ornrim was a fisherman in the mid-14 century DR.

Possessions

He possessed some nets, strongly implying he was a fisherman.

Activities

He fished in the Chionthar.

History

Andor's body was found tangled up in his nets in mid-to-late 1357 DR, a fact known to few other than Belgon Bradasker.

His neck was broken two months prior to the late end of , with a single noblewoman as a witness: Tessaril Winter. He went missing afterwards.
