Blikwik the Beast was a creature of unknown type from the Land of the Beastly Brigands in the Whamite Isles. Together with Badmuddin the Bald, Blikwik represented the Land of the Beastly Brigands in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles
