Hadeshah was head priestess of Chauntea in Daggerford around 1485 DR.

Activities

Hadeshah preferred to take care of the local gardens than speak to people. Her three assistants performed most of the rites.

Personality

Born in Gillian's Hill, Hadeshah considered even Daggerford too great a town for her, full of people and walls.
