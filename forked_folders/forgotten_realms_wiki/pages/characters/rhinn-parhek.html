Rhinn Parhek was an Amnian son of the Parhek dynasty in the late 13 century DR.

Relationships

Rhinn was the eldest son of Prince Malek the Coinmage, and the twin of Savra. Their mother died in childbirth, and they had a younger half-brother Parav by their stepmother.

History

Rhinn grew up in Waterdeep as part of the exiled Parhek family. They were located by his cousin Prince Rohav, and Rhinn was killed in a staged tavern brawl in 1305 DR by his agents.
