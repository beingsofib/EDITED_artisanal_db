Falloor Malthind was a cobbler and retired warrior inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Falloor was the guildmaster for the Order of Cobblers & Corvisers circa the . He held the title of "Senior Merchant" within the guild and worked out of the Cobblers' & Corvisers' House, which was their headquarters located along the Soothsayer's Way in the Trades Ward.
