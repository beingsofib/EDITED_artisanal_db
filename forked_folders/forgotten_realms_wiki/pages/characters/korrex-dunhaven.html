Korrex Dunhaven was a Cormyrean Wizard of War during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Korrex was a big, burly human man who was squeezed into a War Wizard robe.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Korrex assisted the Cormyrean efforts there. He oversaw the post-Battle for Eveningstar operation to find and raid the drow of Sschindylryn in the Underdark, targeting the noble houses of Dun'Robar, Avithoul, and Szind. He worked with Aptem Mews, Traegar Vadamore, Oryne Belamadin, and Jenneth Gosmere to get the job done, and they managed to effectively knock Sschindylryn out of the war.
