Clive the Dandy was an actor from Mulsantir in Rashemen during the late 14 century DR.

Description

Clive was dressed in gaudy but tattered clothes, betraying him as being a low-class bard or a garish actor.

History

As of the , Clive the Dandy could often be found drinking heavily before his so-called performances at the Sloop in Mulsantir.
