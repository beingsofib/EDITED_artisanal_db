Connor the Clever was a bloodforge mage in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

After Princess Roxanna of Edenvale declared she would only marry one who could best her in battle, Connor the Clever was one of the men who took up the challenge in the Matchmaker Mayhem conflict. After her defeat of Bryan the Bold, Roxanna withdrew to the Web Mountains to prevent further assaults on the realm. However, Connor the Clever tracked Roxanna down and insisted she honor her challenge and face him in a bloodforge battle.
