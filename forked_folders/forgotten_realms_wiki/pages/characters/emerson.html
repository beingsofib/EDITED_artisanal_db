Emerson was the foreman of the Nashkel mines during the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

He was armed with a simple club.

History

Emerson was in charge of the mines in the , when the iron ore within was tainted and entirely unusable. Unbeknownst to him they were plagued with kobold minions under the direction of Mulahey, a devotee of Cyric and a pawn of the Iron Throne.
