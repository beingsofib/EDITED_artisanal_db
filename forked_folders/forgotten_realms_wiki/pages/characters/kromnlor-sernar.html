Kromnlor Sernar was a sage and midwife inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Kromnlor rented a room at an inn called Warm Beds located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward where Presper Street met Ship Street. She could typically be found there early in the morning or late at night. She specialized in nature lore, specifically fungi and herbs of the Sword Coast, north Faerûn, and the Heartlands. She wandered the Dock Ward providing services as a midwife to those in need, clashing frequently with the Guild of Apothecaries & Physicians who did not appreciate her effective use of herbal remedies.

History

She was originally from Sespech.
