Magda (Ghars)

Person
nameMagda
homeGhars, Cormyr
sexMale
spousesFendrake

Magda was an inhabitant of the town of Ghars in Cormyr in the 14 century DR.

Relations

She was married to Fendrake. Her nephew was a chandler in the town.

Description

She was known to be a bit homely.
