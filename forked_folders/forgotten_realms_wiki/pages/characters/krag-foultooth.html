Krag Foultooth was the brutal leader of a group of gnolls that allied with the Blackdagger Bandits on the Sword Coast North in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , the Blackdaggers broke their deal with Angelhead Mine owner Melkhor Goldseeker, believing they could find treasure in the mine. Krag Foultooth and his bandits assaulted Angelhead, imprisoned and enslaved the miners, and took Melkhor's niece and the forewoman Mira Thamros hostage to ensure his silence and cooperation. He was visited there by fellow bandit leader Jareth Grim. Finally, Foultooth was killed by an adventurer sent by Yates to Angelhead Mine to free Mira and the other miners.
