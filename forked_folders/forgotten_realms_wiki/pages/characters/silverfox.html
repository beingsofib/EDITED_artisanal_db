Silverfox

Person
nameSilverfox
homeFaerûn
occupationThief

Silverfox was the name of a master thief operating in Faerûn in the mid–14 century DR. By the early 1360s DR, Silverfox was last reported to be in Cormyr and was known to wear a glow ring.
