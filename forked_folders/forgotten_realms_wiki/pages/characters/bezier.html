Bezier was a renowned mathematician working in Waterdeep in the 14 century DR.

He was once student of the great mathematician Furier; though not as great, he was still one of the best in Faerûn. However, at over 80 years of age, he was rumored to have become feebleminded by 1358 DR. He had many scribes and sages working under him.
