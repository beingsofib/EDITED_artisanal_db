Yenward Chorster was a Cormyrean Purple Dragon officer who served in the town of Eveningstar in the late 15 century DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Yenward recruited emergency reinforcements on the spot in the village. He sent them to the ruined keep in the King's Forest, which was a surprisingly vital spot for ritual casting. The reinforcements joined other Purple Dragons in protecting the Wizard of War Arganon Pine from bandits and werewolves, and protecting the Wizard of War Zenyff Bravehand from the murderous forces of nature. The recruits were naturally well-compensated with Cormyrean currency.
