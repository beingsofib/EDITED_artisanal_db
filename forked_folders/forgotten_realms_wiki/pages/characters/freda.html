Freda was a gnome weaver who lived in Phandalin.

Description

Freda frequented the Stonehill Inn in Phandalin. She despised the Redbrands, who had hassled every business in town.

History

In 1491 DR, she noticed that the Redbrands, though they consistently disrupted most trade in the area, left the Phandalin Miner's Exchange as it was. She had information that they apparently did not want to cross the owner, Halia Thornton.
