Roben was a kensai adventurer in Miyama Province in Kozakura in 1357 DR.

History

Roben born to a shipping merchant and lived a comfortable life indulging in harmless pleasures. An old kensai in his village noted Roben's talent and offered to teach him. Unsatisfied with his life, Roben accepted.

In 1357 DR, his master had taught Roben all he knew and so Roben went to Miyama Province following a rumor about a talented master of the naginata.
