Tsabdrar Do'ett was a drow mage and retainer of House Auvryndar in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR, leading the outpost in the Troglodyte Warrens.

History

In 1492 DR, Tsabdrar's soldiers had captured many young troglodyte and he was pondering whether to kill all of them. He was also worried by a rampaging behir and was willing to promise the monster's hoard to any adventurer that could kill it.
