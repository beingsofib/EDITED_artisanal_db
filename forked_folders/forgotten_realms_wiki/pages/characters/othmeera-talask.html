Lord Othmeera Talask was the ruler of Imdolphyn as of 1479 DR.

Activities

Othmeera was the ruler of Imdolphyn in the late years of the 15th century DR, and even if she was a woman, she styled herself as a "lord". As of 1479 DR, she had ambitions to overtake Tarmalune's prominence amongst the Windrise Ports.

Before rising to her position of ruler of Imdolphyn, Othmeera was a famous trader in textiles and scents. Although she retired from this life as she became the ruler of her city, many believed she was still practicing her trade as of 1479 DR.
