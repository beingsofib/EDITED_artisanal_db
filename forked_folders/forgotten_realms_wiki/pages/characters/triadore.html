Triadore was a merchant of the Iron Throne who lived in Baldur's Gate during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Triadore was present in the Baldurian Iron Throne headquarters when it was thrown into chaos in the . Zhalimar Cloudwulfe and other acolytes of Sarevok stormed the building to intercept Emissary Tar before her negotiations began with one of the Throne's leaders, Thaldorn Tenhevich. Triadore came racing out of the building just as Abdel Adrian and his companions arrived inside.
