Burnice was a human child who grew up in the rebuilt town of Leilon in the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Burnice was the daughter of one of Leilon's fishers. Her best friend was Smithwell.

History

Circa 1491 DR, Leilon was attacked by the Cult of Talos and the townsfolk were driven out. Burnice and Smithwell were left behind in town and their parents were still out on the water. Luckily, a group of adventurers arrived just in time to reclaim the town. Both children were left unharmed.
