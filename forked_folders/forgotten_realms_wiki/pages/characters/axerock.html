Axerock was a duergar cleric in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was deceitful and greedy.

Abilities

Axerock was a cleric.

Possessions

He wore chain mail and carried a powerful, enchanted hand axe. He also had a garnet, a topaz and two agates, though he'd been divested of them recently.

History

In the mid-to-late 14 century DR, Axerock came into conflict with two drow who stole his four gemstones and threw him into a pit. Adventurers may have chanced upon him; if they agreed to release him, he'd have turned invisible and fled, stalking them and waiting for the lot to come across these drow and inevitably fight. If they did, he'd attack whoever won that fight; he wouldn't slay the adventurers if they were taken down by the drow, however.
