Tlassian Antar Ludovic was a dragonborn of Tymanther and a member of Clan Tlassian in 1479 DR. He was a renowned master armorer and weaponsmith.

History

Antar was the chief smith of Clan Tlassian in 1479 DR. In his youth, he was trained by a renowned dwarf master smith known as Kvelic Forkbeard. Antar was the only non-dwarf who was recognized as a master smith by Kvelic. His armors were so coveted across Faerûn, that even dwarf smiths, including Kvelic's other students, went to Djerad Thymar to buy them.
