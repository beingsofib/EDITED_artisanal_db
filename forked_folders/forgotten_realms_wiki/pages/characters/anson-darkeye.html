Anson Darkeye

Anson Darkeye was a bloodforge mage in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

The devious Anson harassed and harried the forces of the Legendary Campaign, until its leader pursued Anson into the Web Mountains. The leader quickly became lost in the labyrinthine range, and Darkeye turned the tables and turned back on the Legendary Campaign, forcing them into a defensive bloodforge battle.
