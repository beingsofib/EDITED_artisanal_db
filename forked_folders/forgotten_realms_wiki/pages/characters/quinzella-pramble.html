Quinzella Pramble was a human resident of Eveningstar in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Quinzella had light skin and sandy blonde hair, wearing a white and dark red dress.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Quinzella dined at the Lonesome Tankard with Osbar Mulaban and despaired over how the siege had ruined the food quality.
