Galen Firth was the captain of the Riders of Nesmé, the armed forces of the city of Nesme in the Savage Frontier in the late 14 century DR.

When Nesmé was attacked by trolls in the , he came to Mithral Hall to ask King Bruenor Battlehammer for military support.
