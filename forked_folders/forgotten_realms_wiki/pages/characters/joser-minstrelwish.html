Joser Minstrelwish was an halfling from Arabel and the regional agent of the Moonstars for Cormyr in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR.

History

Joser owned the Minstrelwish Chains, Belts, and Scabbards shop in Arabel. He was a former Harpers and was recruited in the Moonstars for control the region.
