Fergus was a mercenary soldier and a member of the Flaming Fist who lived in Baldur's Gate during the mid−14 century DR.

Activities

At least for a while, Fergus was stationed at High Hall in the Temples District of the Upper City.

Relationships

Fergus had a paramour he referred to has "his lady", and was wholly in love with her.

History

In the , Fergus received a letter from his love saying she would come home to Baldur's Gate very soon. He planned on obtaining an angel skin ring to offer his love upon her return to the city.
