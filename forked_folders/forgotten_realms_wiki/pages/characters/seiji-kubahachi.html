Seiji Kubahachi was a head of the Kubahachi clan and a daimyo of Aru Province in Wa during the late 1600s on the Wa Calendar.

History

Seiji Kubahachi became daimyo of Aru Province in , following the removal of Koji Matsutomo. Seiji governed the province ably.
