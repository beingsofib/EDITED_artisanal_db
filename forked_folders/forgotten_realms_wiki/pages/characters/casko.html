Casko was a mimic that lurked inside the Wheloon Prison in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Like many mimics, Casko had an uncanny resemblance to a large chest full of treasure.

History

During the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, Casko hid inside the Netherese recruitment center, ambushing those that found it.
