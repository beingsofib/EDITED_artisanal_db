Anglatha was a deepspawn who once inhabited the city of Tulmon in the Land of the Lions region of south Faerûn.

Activities

Anglatha was captured by the vile archmage known as the Calavarr who used his powerful magic to change the deepspawn so that all of its offspring were small beholders. The Calavarr then used his magic to take control of the beholders and use as he pleased.

Rumors & Legends

Rumors persisted that Anglatha was successfully cloned, but these were never proven.
