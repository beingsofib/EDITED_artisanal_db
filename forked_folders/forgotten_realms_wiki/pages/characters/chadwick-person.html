Chadwick was a wizard who lived in Waterdeep in late 15th century DR.

Description

Chadwick had a long, blonde hair tied to a ponytail. He was light-skinned, and had green eyes. He wore red clothes.

History

Chadwick was born to a family of smugglers. Although his family was arrested when he was young, Chadwick found himself becoming an apprentice to Barok Clanghammer.

During the Wintershield festivities of 1493 DR, Chadwick was drunkenly boasting about his master's Dragonstaff of Ahghairon. This was overheard by a group belonging to the Cult of the Dragon, and they kidnapped him. Fortunately, Chadwick had managed to create a talking map, which had instructions to guide whoever was using it to Chadwick's grandmother's house outside the city. Chadwick had persuaded the cultists to take him to the house for interrogation, suggesting to them that the staff was held there. A group of adventurers, following the instructions of the talking map they acquired at Waterdeep's marketplace, found the house and rescued Chadwick from the cultists.
