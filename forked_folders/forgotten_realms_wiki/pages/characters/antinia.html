Antinia the Goon was an unsavory element that resided in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Antinia was a human woman with a light skin tone, black eyes, and long straight black hair gathered in a ponytail. She was a proficient fighter who wore a simple breastplate and armed herself with two axes.

History

In the , was part of the same gang with Gal Vharranne, Churg Elvek, Dart Dirtwood, Imprudence, Nobster, and Ormo who could be found in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate near the docks where the Steel Watch Foundry and Water Queen's House stood. Unbeknownst to most, her gang's leader, Churg, was a True Soul, an individual implanted with an illithid tadpole that indoctrinated him into serving the Absolute. Under the Cult of the Absolute's bidding, Antinia and her gang captured the famed writer and traveler Volothamp Geddarm and set to publically execute him for spreading "lies and conspiracies" – talking about the Cult. The gang was confronted by the Absolute Adversaries, who attempted to save the master thespian. It was unknown if Antinia and her gang survived the encounter.
