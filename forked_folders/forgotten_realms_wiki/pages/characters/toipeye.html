Toipeye was a korobokuru bushi adventurer in Kozakura.

History

Toipeye was aways be an outcast even among his tribe for his unattractive aspect and a bad temperament. He on contrary believed to be festinated to great exploits. He abandoned his family after batting a minstrel that showed in comical mode his last adventures.

In 1357 DR, Toipeye was among the candidates values by Ko Mei Kho for the mission in Akari.

In 1358 Toipeye's family finally took pride for his successes, forgiving him for

his outrageous breach of etiquette. Toipeye however was still convinced that they only tolerated him, and was looking for greater actions to justify his abilities when was possibly approached by Ko Ho San

Behind the scenes

Toipeye is a pre-generated PC for use in adventures in Akari. He was presented again upgraded for adventure in T'u Lung.
