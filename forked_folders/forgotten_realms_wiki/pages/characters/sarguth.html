Sarguth was a wainwright and the owner of Sarguth's Wheelworks in Essembra in 1368 DR.

Activities

Sarguth was a fat old man.

Activities

Sarguth's shrewd eye and watchful nature constantly monitored his team of workers ensuring a quick and effective assembly line to perform work for his customers.
