Blademaster Namajj Hru'un was a male warrior drow who served in the Ziggurat below the Temple of Lolth in Sschindylryn during the 1480s DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Namajj oversaw a team of drow inside the Sschindylryn Ziggurat, protecting the ritual spider brooch that High Priestess Hekta Szind used to activate the portal up to the temple. When Elminster and Ana Brabener arrived with a group from Cormyr to put an end to the war, they fought through the defenses to seize this brooch and the ceremonial dagger guarded by Sorntran Arkenep, and used them to access the rooftop temple. Namajj's skills proved insufficient, and he died fighting them.
