Bristol was a child who served aboard the Marderward during the mid—15 century DR.

Relationships

Bristol served under Captain Taraf Predeau, a horrible man who took children into indentured servitude and broke their spirits by pitting them against one another.

History

Some time during or before the , Bristol and the other children aboard Marderward shifted their allegiance from Captain Predeau to a new seaman aboard the ship, Harp Levesque. Harp helped break the system of abuse that was prevalent aboard the vessel, and led both the children and some of the adult crewman in a mutiny.
