Zyx was a faerie dragon of Chult in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

It was about long, with translucent wings.

Personality

The creature was endlessly curious and unreasonably playful, despite being a self-appointed protector of its rainforest and serious about it.

Abilities

Like most other faerie dragons, Zyx was unmatched in its airborne agility, bore a breath weapon that caused joy,

Activities

Zyx's curiosity and concern with its rainforest were endless. When not harassing the local yuan-ti, it helped stray butterflies, and even observed howler monkeys.

Relationships

It was acquainted with the mist dragon Cirrothamalan. It was also endlessly loathed by local yuan-ti, whom it did not consider intelligent creatures.

History

In the , Zyx spotted humans moving into his territory within the jungles of Chult. Knowing they would damage the environment, it decided to take action,

Their disguise was faulty; the humans mistook Cirrothamalan for a mudman. They soon attacked it; fortunately, the disguise was also clumsy, and it failed to kill anyone for long enough that the faerie dragon inserted itself into the fight, and got caught. Zyx was promptly identified as a terrible nuisance, even mid-fight, and the men fled, deciding it was not worth it to settle too close to a faerie dragon's territory.

However, Zyx took its charge as guardian of the jungle seriously. A month later, it decided to move to where the humans were settling to ensure they didn't escape its surveillance.
