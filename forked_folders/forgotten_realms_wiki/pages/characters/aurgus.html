Aurgus

Aurgus was a dwarf hero of the Earthspur Mountains in the Vast sometime before the 14 century DR.

Aurgus once killed a remorhaz single-handedly in the mountain pass on Mount Aergurl. The local dwarves recalled the event in their lore.
