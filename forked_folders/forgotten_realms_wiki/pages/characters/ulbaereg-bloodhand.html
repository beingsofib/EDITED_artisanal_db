Ulbaereg Bloodhand was a warrior who lived during the 5 century DR.

Activities

In the , Ulbaereg Bloodhand conquered the various tribes that settled and farmed the land that would one day become the foundation for Waterdeep. He organized the tribes into the Bloodhand tribe.
