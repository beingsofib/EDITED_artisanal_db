Puck was the homunculus servant of the resurrected Colderan Morn in 1367 DR.

Activities

Puck was the guardian of the growing collection of potions belonging to Colderan. Colderan also sent Puck to travel together with his nightshades to collect information, but he only watched from hiding.

History

In 1367 DR, Puck spied an adventuring party hired to stop the nightshades' attacks.

Powers

Puck's life was linked to Colderan's own and vice versa: if Puck died, Colderan was harmed; if Colderan died, Puck also died. They had a constant telepathic link.
