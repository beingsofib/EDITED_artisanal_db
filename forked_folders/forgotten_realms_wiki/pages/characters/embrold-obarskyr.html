King Embrold was the twelfth Obarskyr monarch and a king of Cormyr. He was born in the Year of the Tusk, 112 DR the third son of Sacrast, taking the throne after his brother Dorglor committed suicide in the Year of the Impassable Chasm, 134 DR. Embrold never married but adopted his older brother's son as his own heir.
