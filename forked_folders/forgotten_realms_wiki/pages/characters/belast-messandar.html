Belast Messandar was an elven adventurer that retired after a successful career, and enjoyed a second life as a merchant of Neverwinter.

Activities

During his life he fought in many military campaigns.

Relationships

Belast Messandar was quite popular among the people of Neverwinter.

He was an adventuring companion of Cruven Gantara, a barbarian warrior from the isle of Tuern.

History

After his death in his 8 century of life, Belast was interred in the Merchant's Landing section of Neverdeath Graveyard. His funeral was attended by many in the city.

Some time in the 1480s DR, agents of the Cult of the Dragon led by an individual named Malthus sought out Belast's resting place in order to find that of Cruven Gantara. They communed with numerous spirits in Neverdeath, from Merchant's Landing to Soldier's Memorial.
