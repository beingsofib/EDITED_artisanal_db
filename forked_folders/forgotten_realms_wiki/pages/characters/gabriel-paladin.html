Gabriel was a human paladin serving king Halvor II in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The prominent champion of Halvors realm, Gabriel and his group travelled all around the kingdom while acting as a font of inspiration to the common people.

Possessions

Wearing a helm of comprehending languages & reading magic and a set of plate mail while riding a warhorse, he wielded either an enchanted battleaxe or a longsword of giant slaying, alongside his heavily enchanted shield.

Relationships

He was friends with his travelling companions three; Ambrose, Barent, & Duncan.
