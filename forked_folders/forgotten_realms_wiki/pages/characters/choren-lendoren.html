Choren Lendoren was a tavern owner of the Dizzy Drake in Skullport.

History

Around 1370 DR, Choren started to drink with his customers. The reason was simple: Nhyris D'Hothek had made his lair in his basement and forced Choren to assist him, by luring some of his patrons down to the lich. He obeyed out of fear.
