Azatlim was a warrior in service to Lord Tessaril Winter in Eveningstar, Cormyr, in the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

He carried a spear for his work.

Activities

He guarded Tessaril's Tower.

Relationships

He worked together with Thrulgar.

History

In the , Azatlim and Thrulgar were protecting Tessaril's Tower the night that Mirt, Shandril Shessair, and Narm Tamaraith arrived seeking her protection. When Shandril threatened them they would not want to cross her, Azatlim asked why; she demonstrated her spellfire in response on a nearby bush. That did not change anyone's mind.
