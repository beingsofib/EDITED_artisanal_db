Barent was a human wizard who served as part of the paladin Gabriel's retinue.

Description

He served under King Halvor II, who trusted him to find magic that could be made to serve the kingdom and the king's ambitions of conquest. As such, he had near-unlimited access to the kingdom's resources, to become ever more mighty in his magical abilities.

Possessions

He wore a robe of the archmagi and a ring of spell turning, while wielding a staff of command. In his possession he also had a figurine of wondrous power.

Relationships

He was friends with both the paladin Gabriel, in whose retinue he served, and his fellow retinue member Ambrose.
