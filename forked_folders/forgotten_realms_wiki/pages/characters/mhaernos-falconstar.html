Mhaernos Falconstar was a human man of the city of Wheloon in Cormyr. He was the owner of Falconstar Wayshipping. He was in this position by 1358 DR, and still held it in 1369 DR.

Mhaernos was a slim man with a black beard. He was cultured and had pretensions to nobility.
