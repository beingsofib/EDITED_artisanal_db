Thoss Balein was Master of Waterdeep's Solemn Order of Recognized Furriers and Woolmen in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was an extraordinarily conservative, uncompromising, and ruthless guildmaster.

Activities

As Master of his guild, Thoss implemented a series of policy changes. These included refusing to accept new members for the entirety of his tenure, and encouraging wealthy guild members to buy out their less-moneyed peers from the organization.

History

Thoss was born in the , and was elected Master of the Solemn Order in the when his predecessor died. Under his leadership, the Order's membership decreased by 25% while revenues grew by 40%.
