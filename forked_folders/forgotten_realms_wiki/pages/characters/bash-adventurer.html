Bash was a half-orc adventurer in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Bash was a huge man with gray skin, and short black hair with muttonchops.

Personality

He didn't care much for his party's inaction, and just wanted them to get somewhere exciting.

History

In between adventures, he visited the Ball and Chain Tavern on the docks of the Wheloon Prison alongside his fellows Irwynne, Gorli, and Marbone.
