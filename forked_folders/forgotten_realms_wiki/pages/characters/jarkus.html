Jarkus was a half-elf thief and scout in the late 14 century DR who died while on an expedition in the Shining Sea.

History

In the , Jarkus was among the members of this group, alongside his thief wife Periscot.

On Marpenoth 11, five velociraptors attacked the expedition's campsite and the team managed to slay them with minimal casualties. The next day Jarkus left with the Chultan Wedonai and the mage Briella Farthing to check on the Sacred Heart of the Beast,

Sometime between then and Marpenoth 23, the two men died from the olive slimes and metamorphosed into slime creatures. Their prone bodies were later discovered by a group of adventurers hired by Briella's father Maskin. The two bodies sprung to life as the adventurers got close to them and had to be fought off, finally ending their existence.
