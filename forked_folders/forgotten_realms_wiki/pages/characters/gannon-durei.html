Oakfather Gannon Durei was a priest of Silvanus inhabiting Mistledale in the Dalelands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Gannon was the head priest of Oakengrove Abbey near Ashabenford. He lead eight other priests in an effort to rebuild the abbey to its former state of glory. He was known to help adventurers who were friends of the forest.
