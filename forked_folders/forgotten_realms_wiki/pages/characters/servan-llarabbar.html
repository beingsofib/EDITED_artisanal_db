Servan Llarabbar was a drow mage in Velkynvelve in the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa 1486 DR, after the demise of Ilvara Mizzrym, Servan was chosen as the next leader of Velkynvelve, but he and most of his minions had become afflicted by the demon lord–spread madness.

Personality

Influenced by madness, Servan started to show heightened paranoia and megalomania. He dreamed of seceding Velkynvelve from Menzoberranzan, to found a land free from the control of the matron mothers.
