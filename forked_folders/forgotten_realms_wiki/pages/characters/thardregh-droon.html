Thardregh Droon was a traveling merchant that dealt in gems, jewelry, ornamental chains, lockets, and other fine metalwork such as cutlery, plates, and coffers with ornate silver detailing. He also sold security hardware such as bolts, hasps, and hinges.

Description

Thardregh was overweight and wore his beard in a goatee.

Possessions

His possessions included his wares and other business property. He wore leather armor +2 and carried a longsword when he felt the need to protect himself.
