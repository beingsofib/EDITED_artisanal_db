Osper Linthalam () was a wizard in Ordulin in 1357 DR.

Activities

Osper devoted more time to political plots and mercantile dealings than to his magical research. He had great riches in his house and always had with him at last three guardian golems.

History

Once in Selgaunt Osper destroyed an attacking pirate ship with his staff of power.

Personality

Osper was very polite, loving fine wine and good feasts.

Possessions

Osper carried a staff of power and many enchanted rings. A trio of stone golems guarded his mansion in Ordulin.
