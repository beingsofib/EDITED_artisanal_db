Murabir Mir was a Coramshite warlord who created and ruled the First Kingdom of Mir, contributing to the rise of the Calimshan Empire and the decline of the dwarves of Shanatar.

History

Though Coramshan and Shanatar had started as allies, Ambril's Bane led to the Shanatar Wars between the humans of the south and the dwarves of High Shanatar. In Murabir Mir led a Coramshite warband from Keltar north between the Calim Desert and Marching Mountains, killing and forcibly recruiting the disparate tribes and families that wandered there. They crossed the River Memnon on rafts, and fell upon the High Shanatar city of Iltakar by surprise, conquering it and seizing it from the dwarves. For the next few decades, Mir continued conquering about Iltakar, taking advantage of the Giant Wars to seize more lands from the dwarves south of the Wurlur, and in he officially founded the Kingdom of Mir as a separate but closely allied realm to Coramshan. After a period of consolidation, the aged Mir launched a new campaign in , seeking to conquer to the east at the Omlarandin Mountains while Coramshan's army protected the Kingdom of Mir. Though he did not live to see it, the Mir territories eventually extended to the Lake of Steam and met the Empire of Jhaamdath, causing the Mir–Jhaamdath War in and eventually the creation of the Calimshan Empire in .
