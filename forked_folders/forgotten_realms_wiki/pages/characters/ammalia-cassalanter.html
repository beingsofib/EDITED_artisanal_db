Ammalia Cassalanter () was a Waterdhavian noble in the late 15 century DR. She was the wife of Victoro Cassalanter.

Activities

Ammalia was schooled in the arcane arts. Like her husband, she was the leader of a Cult of Asmodeus that involved certain nobles in Waterdeep.

During an average day, she normally tended to her garden, smoked on the balcony, dismally watching the ocean, or wept at a transformed Osvaldo's feet in her mansion's attic.

History

In 1489 DR,

In 1492 Ammalia and her husband planned to recover the lost gold of Dagult Neverember and use it to buy back the souls of their children Meantime Ammalia planned to serve poison food during a party ousted during the Founders' Day celebration in order to gain the ninety-nine souls they need.

If Ammalia and Victoro presented in time the gold and the ninety-nine souls to a devil servant of Asmodeus the souls of Terenzio and Elzerina would be safe.
