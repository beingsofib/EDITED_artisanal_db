Cedric Buckenwing was an inhabitant of the town of Ghars in Cormyr in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Cedric was a buddy of Jasper and other young men in Ghars in the . In Eleint, they got drunk on cheap beer and Cedric challenged Jasper to steal something from the retired War Wizard Benelaius. He agreed and they all rode out together to his cottage. Jasper was caught and later reflected that real friends wouldn't've let him be so stupid.
