Lady Ammisyll Veldann was the sun elven governor of Nimlith on the island of Evermeet and a member of the Council of Evermeet.

Personality

Ammisyll had extremely conservative views and opposed the monarchy on Evermeet.

Relationships

Ammisyll was cousin to Tarthas.
