Trannus Kialton was a low-ranking priest of Bane in Zhentil Keep in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Kialton was sharing the bed of Angelique Cantaran the night he was possessed by Bane at the onset of the Time of Troubles. After waking, Kialton rushed out on the sleepy and smiling Angelique with the premonition he would never see her again, navigating the rainy and dismal streets and to arrive at Bane's Temple, the Black Altar. Bane then fell from the sky and crashed into the temple, smiting every inch of the building except his throne and leaving the gauntlet Kialton had seen in his dreams upon it. Kialton believed his prayers for power finally answered, and donned the gauntlet with a great joy allowing evil arcane power to flow into him. Subsequently, his soul was exiled from his body in a small puff of smoke, leaving Bane to take over his form and reshape his flesh into a more godly looking avatar.
