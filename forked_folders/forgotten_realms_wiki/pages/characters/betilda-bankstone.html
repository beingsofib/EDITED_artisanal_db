Betilda Bankstone was a human resident of Tyrluk in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Betilda was an elderly woman with tanned skin and long white hair.

History

Shortly after the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, Betilda witnessed the Shadovar wizards Tarys and Wyle and their hired minions as they invaded the Old Man's Face inn, searching for Oriphaun Huntsilver's Nether Scroll.
