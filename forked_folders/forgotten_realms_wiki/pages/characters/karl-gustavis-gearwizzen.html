Karl Gustavis Gearwizzen was the head priest of Gond in Skullport.

History

Karl was the main proponent of building a water clock with his clergy and the gnome twins Tykkyl Burrwarden and Tohkkal Burrwarden. In 1370 DR, the clock was finished but was so delicate that it always needed the constant supervision of three priests, who rotated their management duties.
