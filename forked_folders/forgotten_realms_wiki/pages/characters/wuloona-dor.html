Wuloona Dor was a gold dwarf commoner from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Wuloona was a clean shaven dwarven woman with dark skin and pale green eyes. Her graying hair was gathered in a braid wrapped around her head.

History

In the , Wuloona Dor was among those citizens of Baldur's Gate who did not trust the new Grand Duke Lord Gortash. She despised Gortash's control of the city's broadsheet – Baldur's Mouth, and mistrusted all of the lies printed on his behalf. She found him despicable.
