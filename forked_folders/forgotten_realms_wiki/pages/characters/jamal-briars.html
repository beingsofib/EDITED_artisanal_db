Jamal Briars was the professor of alchemy who taught at the short-lived Michell Academy in the Sunset Vale, who died long before the early 14 century DR.

Posessions

Jamal worse a set of master's robes – special crimson magely robes created to boost the spellcasting abilities of the members of the Michell Academy.

History

The Michell Academy existed near the town of Berdusk many years before the . Its fall followed an hour-long battle against a nearby temple to Loviatar with whom the Academy had an antagonizing relationship. The temple's mistresses ensured the academics' torture would last for days, if historians were to be believed. Jamal Briars's torture was made ironic. The Loviatans's elementals dragged him across thorned briars until Jamal bled to death.
