Aluena Halacanter was a sorceress and a member of the Harpers who led the community of Serpent's Cowl in the late mid–to–late 14 century DR.

Description

She was known for both her beauty and the influence she wielded in her area of the Western Heartlands.

Activities

Aluena trained pegasi in her Heartwing Estate, which was located north of the crossing of Boareskyr Bridge. She employed staff members who to teach people how to mount the winged horses.

She aided the adventurers Barim Stagwinter and Theskul Mirroreye with overseeing the tented community sprang up around Boareskyr Bridge in the mid–1360s.

Relationships

Expectantly, Aluena was acquainted with Elminster, and was at one point owed a favor by the wizened sage.

History

Some time in the mid–14 century DR, the adventuring company known as the Seekers found one of Aluena's errant pegasi and returned it to her estate. She accompanied the group to look for the steed's missing rider, and found the young man's body in the nest of some giant spiders.

After her adventurer allies left the settlement that former around Boareskyr Bridge, Aluena shifted her priorities. As of the , Aluena was doing all she could to protect her estate at Serpent's Cowl from the growing threat of yuan-ti from the Forest of Wyrms.
