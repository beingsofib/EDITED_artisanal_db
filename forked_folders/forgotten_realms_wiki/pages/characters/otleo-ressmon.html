Otleo Ressmon, usually known as The Fat, was a male thief who was a member of the Shadow Council of the Shadow Thieves during the 14 century DR.

Description

True to his nickname, Otleo was an exceptionally large man.

Activities

Otleo was a career man, rising through the ranks of the guild step by hard step, starting as a common thief. His duty was to control the guild's operations in Tethyr, where he posed as a prospering merchant. He was quite skilled in his dealings, specializing in the trade of exotic goods.

Possessions

His massive size was due to cursed item that had the same powers as a ''+3 ring of protection.
