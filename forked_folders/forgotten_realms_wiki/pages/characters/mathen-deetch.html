Mathen Deetch, was a child who lived in the town of Moonhaven in the late 15 century DR.

History

Sometime before the Moonhaven was attacked and ruined by the goblin raiders of the Cult of the Absolute in the , Mathen was among the four children who had gone missing. Their names: Mathen Deetch, Rochelle Kirk, Marcus, and Maggie Terrens. The missing children's notice was still hanging on a wall as the village laid in ruins in the .
