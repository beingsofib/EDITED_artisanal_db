Zlan Clervish was one of the seven liches who created Crenshinibon, and was later consumed by it.

History

At first, Zlan was an attendant of Shar, the goddess of darkness. In the course of his service, he became one of the very first wizards to investigate the Shadow Weave. Such grand knowledge later became lost to him, and was only discovered again during the rise of the Shadovar.
