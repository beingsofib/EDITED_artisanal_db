Ekim Cyre was a greater werewyvern who served the blue dragon Abazigal during the mid–14 century DR.

Abilities

Ekin could readily change between his wyvern and his humanoid forms.

He had an inherent resistance against the elements and could not be harmed by non-magical weapons.

Activities

Ekim defended the upper levels of Abazigal's enclave, in the caverns above the flooded tunnel system.

History

In the , Ekim was slain by the Bhaalspawn Abdel Adrian and his fellow companions when they stormed the enclave in search of Abazigal.
