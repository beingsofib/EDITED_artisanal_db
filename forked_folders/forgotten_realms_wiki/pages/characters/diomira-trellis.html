Diomira Trellis was an Untaken of Umberlee, a low-ranking member of the Bitch Queen's clergy under Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queen's House in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Diomira was a human woman with long straight black hair. She had heterochromia – Diomira's left eye was bright aquamarine, and her right eye was black in color.

History

Circa the , during the Absolute crisis, Untaken Diomira Trellis was among the low-ranking members of the who were not allowed to perform the ceremonies and sing hymns in praise of the Bitch Queen. She quietly prayed at the temple whilst Flood Tide Allandra Grey and other priestesses sang and performed a fellow priestess's Holli Dylford's last rites. The recently perished priestess died while swimming in Baldur's Gate's Gray Harbor.
