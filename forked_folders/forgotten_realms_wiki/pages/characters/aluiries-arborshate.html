Aluiries Arborshate was a half-elven member of Company Yargo that served during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

She was the type to gather sufficient information to theorize about a situation rather than engage in reckless conjecture.

History

When Company Yargo soared up to the Pirates' Skyhold earthmote aboard the Buccaneer's Tear in the , Aluiries accompanied Captain Shil Yargo to the Market Square above the Pirate King's Retreat hideaway. She worked with an adventurer in the company's employ to study the destruction brought down upon the ruined town, eventually attributing it to the acid breath of a black dragon.
