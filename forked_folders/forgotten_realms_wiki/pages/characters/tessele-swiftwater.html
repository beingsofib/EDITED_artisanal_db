Tessele Swiftwater was the owner of the Dagger's Rest Inn during the mid-to-late 15 century DR.

Description

Tessele was a tall woman with long brown hair, green eyes, and a warm and friendly smile. She was considered middle-aged circa 1479 DR.

Personality

Tessele was a retired rogue, who made a point of helping younger adventurers who frequented her establishment.
