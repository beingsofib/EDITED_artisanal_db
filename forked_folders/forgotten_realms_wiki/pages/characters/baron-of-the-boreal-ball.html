The Baron of the Boreal Ball was a minor noble of the Feywild.

Description

The Baron hosted the Boreal Ball, an unending party in which guests were free to dance, mingle, and jockey for influence in hopes of impressing the Baron and earning his gift, the Charm of the Boreal Ball. He was known to award this boon to whoever gained suitable renown among the attendees.

Possessions

He resided in an ice palace within the Feywild.

History

The Baron was hosting his Ball as of the late 15 century DR.
