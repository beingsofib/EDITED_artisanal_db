Mira Satra owned some boarding houses in Hlath in Chondath in 1358 DR.

Activities

Mira managed the Blue Roan, a complex of four boarding houses in Cormyrean style with well-kept stables.

History

In 1358 DR, Mira Satra was called to a meeting of prominent citizens gathered by Lord Fior Botpur to discuss the menace of a barbarian army from Kara-Tur.

Personality

Mira was an able businessman, his prices were high but his places were comfortable. He hated war but never fled from an enemy.
