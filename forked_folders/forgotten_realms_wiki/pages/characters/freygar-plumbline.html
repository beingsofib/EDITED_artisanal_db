Freygar Plumbline was a dwarven member of Hal Stoutheart's Tethyamar expedition during the 1480s DR.

Description

Freygar had a thick black beard and head of hair.

Relationships

In his aunt Asta's opinion, Freygar had an inflated opinion of himself. Despite that, she cared deeply about him and followed him to the Tethyamar Mining Outpost where he worked as a surveyor, in order to make sure he was safe.

History

Freygar was a surveyor in the expedition, charting the tunnels and the resources within. With the growing fervor around the jewels of ardor, Freygar set about trying to find the Tethyamar Master Manifest, a compilation of maps and ancient mining records that would greatly aid their efforts. Using some surfacer mercenaries, he sent a team down to Ironvale Deep, Tethyamar's library complex. Fighting through monsters and the drow of House Argith, they overcame the leader Kir'tan Melarn and recovered the Manifest. Despite getting what he wanted, Freygar was confused and disappointed that while the records mentioned the jewels of ardor, they were described as low-value crystals not worth mining. He ultimately decided that the Manifest was a fake copy, and the real one was still hidden somewhere in the ruins.
