Pir'Oront Tlabbar was the weapon master of House Faen Tlabbar the fourth house of Menzoberranzan.

History

Around 1358 DR, Pir'Oront was the elderboy of Matron Ghenni'tiroth Tlabbar and was chosen also as weapon master.
