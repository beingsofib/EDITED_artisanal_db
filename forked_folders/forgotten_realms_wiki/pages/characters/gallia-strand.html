Gallia Strand was an smuggler who lived in the Realms during the late 15 century DR.

History

Gallia's smuggling career was stalled when they were convicted of moving some particularly extravagent goods. Gallia was sentenced to a ten-year sentence at Revel's End prison, which lay in the nothern wastes of the Frozenfar.
