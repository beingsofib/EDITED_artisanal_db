Halbrondell was an inhabitant of Ieirithymbul in the late 14 century DR.

History

In Uktar of the , a farmer, possibly Halbrondell's neighbor, got into an argument with Halbrondell over his goat and threatened to have it butchered for getting into his cabbages.

Rumors & Legends

The accuser's stray thoughts were captured by a local gem-cutting apprentice, Burlmarr.
