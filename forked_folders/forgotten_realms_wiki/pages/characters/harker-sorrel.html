Sir Harker Sorrel was an inhabitant of the city of Ravens Bluff and a Knight of the Phoenix in the late 14 century DR.

History

In Marpenoth of the , Leyland - Nightstalker wedding took place. Dame Jenilynn Leyland, a Knight of the Right Hand of Tyr, and Sir Sarren Nightstalker, a Knight of the Griffon, were united in a social gala that was reported on the pages of the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter. Sir Harker Sorrel was one of the attendants to the groom, alongside Lord Farendrell‎ and Alexander Thorin. It was reported that the wedding was scheduled for an earlier date, but Jenilynn Leyland was delayed due to her mission to the Beastlands and being transformed into a Unicorn.
