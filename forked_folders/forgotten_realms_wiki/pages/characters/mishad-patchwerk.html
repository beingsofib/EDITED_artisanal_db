Mishad Patchwerk was the brother of Devlin Patchwerk and a fellow Shadow Thieves guild member who was stationed in Undermountain, the mid–14 century DR.

History

In 1370 DR, The Shadow Thieves established an enclave in one of the top levels of the mega-dungeon underneath Waterdeep. Mishad Patchwerk and his brother were members of that guild branch led by the Guild Master Georges and his right hand, Braakus Sinjen. The word of the guild's return reached the Lords of Waterdeep, and they sent a brave adventurer to discover the guild's hidden stronghold. Mishad was one of the first thieves to fall by the hero's weapons.
