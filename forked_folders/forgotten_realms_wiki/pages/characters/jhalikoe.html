They were on a first name basis with the envoy from Milvarune at the Open Feast, despite them having been recently arrived to Cormyr.

History

Jhalikoe participated in the Open Feast held at Castle Obarskyr on Mirtul 26 of the , where they briefly entertained the envoy from Milvarune, discussing subjects such as the Silent Shadow, Skult, and Vandarl.
