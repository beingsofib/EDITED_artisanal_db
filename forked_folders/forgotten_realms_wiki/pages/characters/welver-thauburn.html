Welver Thauburn was a trader in Scornubel during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Possessions

As a barge merchant, he most likely owned at least one barge.

Activities

Besides his normal barge merchant activities, he liked to carry his wares across the River Chionthar in the night, to foil potential thieves.

History

While ferrying his wares across the river one night in , Welver Thauburn briefly witnessed Qilué Veladorn, one of the Seven Sisters, rise from the bottom of the river and fly naked into the night, bound and blindfolded. The sight made him consider quitting drink, probably after draining dry the keg he had at home.
