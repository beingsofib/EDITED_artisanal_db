Silver Silwyrt was the eldarin captain of a band of fey pirates known as Silver's Blades in the late 15 century DR.

History

Under Silwyrt's leadership, Silver's Blades were among the many capricious fey operating out of the ruins of Caer Corwell. As of , Silwyrt had begun to prioritize the capturing and trading of slaves.
