Par was a villager of Highcliff on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR.

History

Par was a wealthy villager of Highcliff. He asked of Kalach-Cha to go into his house and see what was wrong. The Kalach-Cha found a bunch of sailors, led by Dulahey, who were robbing the place. Once Kalach-Cha defeated the pirates, Par would give some potions as a reward.
