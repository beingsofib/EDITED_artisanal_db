Halvaruth Feirn, nicknamed the "Stone King", was a traveling actor who performed at the famous Crown Aflame theater in Athkatla in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Halvaruth got his royal nickname thanks to his stoic face that always remained inexpressive like chiseled granite. Despite that, he acted with his voice masterfully, showing impressive complexity of character and depth of feeling.

History

Halvaruth Feirn was among several traveling actors who were somewhat popular attractions among posh theatergoers from the Temple District. As of , Halvaruth was still performing at the Crown Aflame.
