Kaddrus was a cambion agent of the Knights of the Shield who acted on behalf of the organization during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

He served as the personal attendant to Lutecia Hhune, who held great trust in his abilities.

Abilities

One tactic of his included masquerading himself as an innocent bystander in order to get near to his quarry.

Relationships

Kaddrus was served by three particularly evil humans, Falar, Zaroud al'Ryshal, and Nulra Blacksaddle.

History

When the Shield of the Hidden Lord was taken from the Hhune family crypt, in the , Kaddrus and his compatriots were tasked with its retrieval.
