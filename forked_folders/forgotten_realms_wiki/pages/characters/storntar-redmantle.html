Thaeram Duskwinter was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

History

In , after becoming a spy for Caladnei, the Royal Magician of Cormyr, the Waterdhavian thief Narnra Shalace's first assignment was to investigate a few War Wizards suspected of untoward activities, amongst which a traitor was suspected. Storntar Redmantle was one of these suspect wizards.
