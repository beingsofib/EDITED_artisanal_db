Darlakhan was a Sharran faithful during the mid–8 century DR.

Personality

He was eager to serve Shar and was ambitious.

Description

He had noticeable ritual scarring on his forearms, and when in ritual, he wore a mantle of shards between his legs, drawing blood with every step.

Activities

He had temple tasks in the House of Holy Night.

History

Darlakhan was called into the heart of the House of Holy Night by Dread Sister Klalaera in what he believed was the prelude to his promotion to a position of power. Instead, he was used as a human sacrifice by the clerics who had Dasumia, an avatar of Mystra, bound.
