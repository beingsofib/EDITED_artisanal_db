Ronglath Knightsbridge was a human warrior of the Zhentarim and a worshiper of Bane in the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the , just before the Time of Troubles, he infiltrated the guards of Arabel to conspire against the city but was unveiled by Kelemvor and Cyric.

Ronglath later became a commander in the Zhentilar army sent by Bane against Shadowdale.
