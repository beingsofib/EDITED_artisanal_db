Toobar Starlight was the innkeeper in the Evening Star Inn of Freedale in the Dalelands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was a pleasant man, who loved stargazing a little too much.

Activities

Together with his wife, he owned and ran the Evening Star Inn. Toobar spent his evening studying the stars.

Relationships

He was married to Katherine Starlight.
