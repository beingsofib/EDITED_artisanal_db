Inyanga Engaruka was a boy wizard in Chult.

Activities

Inyanga resided with his father Ibn Engaruka in Port Castilar, the most hidden port of Chult, studying as wizard and helping in Ibn's depot.

Personality

Inyanga could become very rude if someone made a mess in the store.

Relationship

Inyanga had a pet bat, Zrumya, who helped him and fought for him.
