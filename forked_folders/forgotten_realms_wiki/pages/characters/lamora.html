Lamora was a triton archmage of Myth Nantar who participated in the defense of the fabled city during the assault on the city in the late 15 century DR. She helped a group of adventurers plant and detonate a magical explosive pearl on the death knight-dreadnaught Devourer that was the flagship of the Red Wizards.
