Biff the Understudy was a human male fighter and an actor in Raelis Shai's theater performance at the Five Flagons Inn in Athkatla in 1369 DR. He served as an understudy for Haer'Dalis, who was absent. Biff messed up his lines, prompting booing and causing the play to be abandoned.
