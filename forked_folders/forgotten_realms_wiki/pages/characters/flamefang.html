Flamefang was a large and powerful hell hound in the world of Eberron.

History

Flamefang prowled the underworld of Khyber on the road to Tarath Marad near the Spinner's Prison, leading a large pack of lesser hell hounds. It may have been caught up in the battle between the forces of the Silver Flame when they fought against the Lords of Dust there during the Spinner of Shadows' bid for freedom.
