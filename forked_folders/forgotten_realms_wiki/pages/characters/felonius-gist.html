Felonius Gist was a wealthy nobleman of the Gist patriar family who lived in the city of Baldur's Gate in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

In addition to the usual bluster that was exhibited by many noble-born, Felonious displayed distinct cowardice when presented even the slightest hint of physical harm.

Activities

Felonious earned his wealth at least in part from his transactions as an art dealer. He lived in a posh estate somewhere in the central area of the Gate's Upper City.

History

At some point in the , he purchased some distinct pieces that were added to his private collection: six life-size humanoid statues that closely resembled living individuals. The "statues" were in fact six adventurers led by the elf named Vail, all of whom had been turned to stone by the gaze of a basilisk.
