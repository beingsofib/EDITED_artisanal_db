Invadiah was the most powerful erinyes in the Nine Hells and was the leader of Glasya's elite erinyes guard, the Pradixikai.

Relations

She had fifty-eight erinyes daughters

History

In the , Invadiah sought to carry out Glasya's plot to trick Asmodeus into fighting the Abolethic Sovereignty. Unfortunately for her, the plot unraveled, primarily due to betrayal by the succubus Rohini. As punishment for her failure, Invadiah was "demoted" to succubus herself and her holdings given to her daughter Sairché.
