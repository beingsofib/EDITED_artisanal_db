Umathar Nhalass VI, who was also nicknamed the Grim, was the eleventh Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar.

Activities

Umathar VI reigned for only a few years from to . He inherited the throne from his father Darthen III.

Relationships

Umathar VI's older brother was killed with his father during an invasion of Narfell. He was the secondborn son and had three younger brothers–Rhendar II, Tharen, and Sharath.
