Mikkaalgensis, also known as the Harper Dragon, was an male adult silver dragon who lived in Myth Drannor in Cormanthor in the 8 century DR.

History

Fighting as an ally of Cormanthyr in the Weeping War, the Harper Dragon was slain in the twelfth campaign, known as the Dragons' Last Dive on Kythorn 24 in the . The dragons Mikkaalgensis, Uinnessivar, and Qiminstiir were slain while destroying a gate in Aglarond's Yuirwood to stop an invasion by mezzoloth soldiers of the Army of Darkness.
