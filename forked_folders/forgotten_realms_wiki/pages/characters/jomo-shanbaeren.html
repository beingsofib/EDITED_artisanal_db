Jomo "the Cutlass" Shanbaeren was a retired pirate who owned and managed a tavern around 1359 DR.

History

Jomo stowed away on the Dragon's Claw when he was seventeen years old but it taken by pirates and he was discovered by the female pirate captain, who "offered" him passage. Jomo went with her and became a pirate, and he taught her how to play chess.
