Escobert the Red was a shield dwarf and the castellan of keep of the small town of Greenest, located in the Western Heartlands in west Faerûn.

Personality

As master of the keep, Escobert knew all about the city's defenses and tunnels and the sally port under it.

Description

Escobert was a dwarf with messy and unkempt red hair.

History

In the 1480s DR, during the battle with the adult blue dragon Lennithon and the Cult of the Dragon, Escobert remained in command in the keep with the wounded governor Tarbaw Nighthill.
