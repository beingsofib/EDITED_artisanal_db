Tebora was the saleswoman at the Body Shop in Vaelan in .

Appearance

She was a plangent, sporting the Strong and Tireless configuration, which replaced one of her legs, one of her arms, and her spine; she also sported a plangent crystal eye. When working at the Body Shop, she wore a lavender dress, which matched her prosthetics quite well.

Personality

She was an insistent and tricky seller, eager to demonstrate the capabilities and power of plangents.

Powers

Tebora's eye was capable of some kind of scanning. She was also confident of her ability to wrestle anyone's manservants.

History

In , Tebora was the one answering the questions of Ususi Manaallin and Iahn Qoyllor, who had entered the shop while disguised as Lady Urale and her manservant Alon (Iahn being the manservant). During her conversation, either Shaddon Datharathi or Pandorym looked out of Tebora's body without her notice or knowledge. She offered to wrestle "Alon" to demonstrate the power of her plangent prosthetics; his disguise broke in the violence, and he was forced to knock her out in order to keep their presence a secret.
