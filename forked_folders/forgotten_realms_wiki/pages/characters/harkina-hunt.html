Harkina Hunt was a resident of Elturel during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

She had two twin infant sons, Brask and Ezo.

History

Harkina and her boys were present in Elturel when it was dragged down to the Nine Hells in the . She managed to survive for some time,

Unfortunately for the Hunt family, while searching for supplies, they were eventually intercepted by the barbazus Blass, Nodd and Thunn, who would sell them into slavery at the Wandering Emporium, if not rescued by adventurers. Harkina could tell them what she knew about Elturel's perilous state.

Equipment

Harkina had a longbow and forty arrows, some silvered, which she used to defend her family.
