Rildara Danthree was a half-elven adventurer of the late 9 century DR.

History

In the , she was known to have owned Adriyana's Garter. Rildara later attempted to climb aboard a skyship sailing above the Sea of Fallen Stars. As the vessel moved past Arrabar, she slid off and fell to her apparent death in the sea.
