Anghara Hettix was a gnome merchant in the Moonsea region.

Personality

Anghara was an experienced and well-traveled merchant. She had a pleasant manner and a tendency to use long, multisyllabic words whenever possible.

Activities

Anghara led a caravan from Mulmaster to Tantras.

History

Anghara was a rogue from Icewind Dale. After securing a small amount of wealth, she decided to start a new life as a merchant in Moonsea.

In 1491 DR, she was convinced by Captain Gideon Holke to add to her caravan an adventuring party that, unknown to Anghara, he had hired to clear the area of bandits.
