Mahorustep I was the sixth pharaoh of Mulhorand.

History

Mahorustep I was born in 886 DR. He ascended to the throne of pharaoh of Mulhorand in 921 DR, after the former pharaoh Horuseres II was slain by the green dragon Chathuulandroth.

His reign came to an end a year later, dyeing in battle against the Red Wizards of Thay
