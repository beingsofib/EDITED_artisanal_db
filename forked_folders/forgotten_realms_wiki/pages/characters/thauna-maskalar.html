High Priestess Thauna Maskalar was a cleric of Sune and the head of the House of Firehair, a cathedral of Sune, within the city of Daerlun in Sembia in the mid–14 century DR.

History

By 1358 DR and through 1374 DR, Thauna Maskalar led 48 human and half-elf clerics in the worship of Lady Firehair.
