Jolara Thanterim was the high priestess of the Hall of Luck, a temple dedicated to Tymora, in Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR.

Activities

Jolara was the leader of Tymora's faithful in Urmlaspyr. Her congregation within the temple consisted of 14 other priests and 21 followers.
