Sauvrurn was a nobleman from Marsember during the late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was not enthusiastic about the prospect of bedding Alusair Nacacia Obarskyr, and treason, for all his antipathy regarding the Obarskyrs, was still a prospect he was jittery about.

Appearance

He sounded cultured, and used a strong, musky perfume.

Relationships

He conspired together with Thandro as a member of the Rightful Conspiracy.

Possessions

He'd been able to gather together a comb that slew lice, a head for a walking stick that always knew north, and three more baubles from his family.

History

Sauvrurn and Thandro discussed their involvement in a future assassination attempt on Filfaeril Obarskyr and Alusair, respectively queen and princess regent of Cormyr, in . He was reticent to do much, but Thandro reassured him enough to get him to participate in the planned assassination. He had gathered five minor baubles in advance, and could muster twelve swordsmen; he was instructed to head for a lamppost near Thelvarspike Lane in advance of their attack on Thundaerlyn Hall. Their discussion happened in a warehouse full of Selgauntan glowstones, which prevented them from being scried on, but unknown to them, two merchants and fugitives, Malakar Surth and Bezrar were eavesdropping on them.
