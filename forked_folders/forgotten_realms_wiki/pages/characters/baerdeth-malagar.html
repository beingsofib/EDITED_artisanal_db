Baerdeth Malagar was the High Priest or Huntmaster of the High House of the Hunt, a temple devoted to the worship of Malar in Zhentil Keep in 1358 DR.

Activities

As Huntmaster, Baerdeth supervised 11 other priests of Malar. Their congregation in 1358 numbered just shy of a score of worshipers of the Beastlord. He often supervised hunts in the sewers of Zhentil Keep, the Ride, and hills northeast of the city.

Following the destruction of most of Zhentil Keep in the , Baerdeth took control of the Leathercrafters' Guild in the Foreign Quarter and converted it to a new temple of Malar he called the Lodge of the Great Hunt.
