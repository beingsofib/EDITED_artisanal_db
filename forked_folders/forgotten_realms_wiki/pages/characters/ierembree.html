Ierembree was a priestess of Eilistraee during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

She was tall, with a mistrustful face. Ierembree was known to wear a black robe.

Personality

She was quick to resort to violence, and expected the worst from humans.

Abilities

One of her spells called her favourite dagger to her hand.

Possessions

She had a favorite dagger.

Activities

Ierembree was known to participate in certain rites to Eilistraee within Ardeep Forest.

Relationships

Ierembree served Eilistraee under the guidance of Qilué Veladorn. She later made friends with the Harper Dove Falconhand.

History

During one night of , Ierembree was one of the priestesses dancing under Qilué Veladorn's guidance in an Eilistraeean rite which was interrupted by the arrival of the Harper Dove Falconhand, who sought Qilué for a task in Scornubel. Despite Qilué's protests that as one of the Seven Sisters she was close to Dove and she was not to be harmed, Ierembree and other priestesses took offense to the intrusion, with Ierembree herself attempting to slay Dove outright. In response, Dove employed some manner of magic to have a heart-to-heart contact with her, which both calmed her down and mollified her opinion of the interloper.
