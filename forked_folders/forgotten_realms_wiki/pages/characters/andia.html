Andia was a mage who worked aboard the Crane for a time during the mid-to-late 15 century DR.

Some time after the , Andia joined the crew of the Crane, to serve as the vessel's resident caster. During that time she became involved with the ship's captain, Harp Levesque. The romance did not end well for either party, and eventually Andia left the ship for other prospects.
