Balrud Thal was owned a barn and stable in the city of Zhentil Keep during the late–14 century DR.

Activities

He owned On Four Feet which was located in the Foreign Quarter in between Center Street and Teshwave Street.

Balrud had to fiercely guard his livestock during the destruction of the city in the , as many gained an appreciation for horse flesh as food in the city became scarce.

History

He earned a living as a mercenary before settling in Zhentil Keep.
