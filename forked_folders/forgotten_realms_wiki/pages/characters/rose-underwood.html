Rose Underwood was Hogan Underwood's niece. At the time of Khalitharius' release in , she was visiting her uncle and bartending at his place.

Personality

She was shy of strangers, but would talk once she warmed to the conversation.

Possessions

Rose kept an ornamental comb in her room, worth about a pound of silver.

Rumors

She had been awakened in the middle of the night by strange noises coming from just north of the compound. She considered his uncle to be a honest man and a pillar of the community.; once, while walking behind the smithy, she saw one of the sheik's sons enter the sandvoyagers' guild by a secret way; she could not tell the twins apart.
