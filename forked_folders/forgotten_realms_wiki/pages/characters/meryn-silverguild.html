Meryn Silverguild was the son of a successful merchant inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Meryn was extremely lazy.

Activities

He lived within southern Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter in Silverguild Manor.

Relationships

His father was the successful merchant Malvred Silverguild and his mother was Eunice. His only sibling was his sister Emelia.
