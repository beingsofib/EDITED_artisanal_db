Thran was the leader of the watch guard in Freedale in the Dalelands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was a dedicated soldier.

Activities

Under Thran's leadership, groups of two guardsmen always patrolled the town.

Relationships

Aside from being the leader of the guards, Thran was friends with Beldar the Brave, who could stay over as long as he was available for support when needed.
