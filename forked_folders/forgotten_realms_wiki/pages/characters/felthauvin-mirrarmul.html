Felthauvin Mirrarmul was a weaver inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Felthauvin was the guildmaster for the League of Basketmakers & Wickerworkers circa 1357 DR. He held the title of "Master Worker" within the guild and worked out of the League Office–their headquarters on Wall Way in the Trades Ward. Felthauvin held the position of guildmaster for most of his life, thanks in part to his expert skill at his chosen craft and his ability to train others.
