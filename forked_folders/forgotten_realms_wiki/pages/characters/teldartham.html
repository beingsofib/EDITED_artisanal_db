Teldartham was the new captain of the guard in Skuld in Mulhorand in 1357 DR.

History

In 1357 DR, the clerics of Horus-Re managed to appoint Teldartham, a champion of Horus-Re, as captain of the guard of Skuld despite this being a position usually covered by worshipers of Anhur.
