Flem was the ormyrr lorekeeper of the Sharpstick clan who lived in the ruined city of Myth Drannor circa 1369 DR.

History

In 1369 DR, ormyrr of the Sharpstick clan were divided by the dark mind-affecting magics of the Cult of the Dragon. The usurper chief Pujik allied himself with the cultists, while Retha, the old leader, was trying to stop the evil ormyrr. Flem was uncorrupted and remained loyal to the tribe's true leader. Subsequently, a group of heroes, the Veiled Ones, slew Pujik and freed the tribe from the Cult's corruption. In reward, Flem offered the heroes his expansive knowledge of the old stories and legends that led the heroes to some powerful enchanted items hidden within the ruins of the City of Song.
