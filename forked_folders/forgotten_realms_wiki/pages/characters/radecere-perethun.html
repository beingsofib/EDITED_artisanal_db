Radecere Perethun was a traveler on the Sword Coast just before the Tyranny of Dragons.

Personality

Radecere was usually a loner, but he never refused the opportunity to gamble in a good game, and he was quite good at it.

History

At the start of the Tyranny of Dragons, in 1489 DR Radacere boarded a great caravan that, unbeknownst to him, was full of agents of the Cult of the Dragon transporting treasures for the Hoard of the Dragon Queen.
