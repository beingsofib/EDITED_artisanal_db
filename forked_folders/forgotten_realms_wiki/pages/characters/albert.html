Albert

Person
nameAlbert
homeNashkel
sexMale
racePit fiend

Albert seemed to be a young boy in Nashkel, looking for his dog Rufie. Eventually, Gorion's Ward offered to help him but when Gorion's Ward delivered the dog to Albert, he revealed his true form, a pit fiend. Albert and his dog then plane shifted back to the Nine Hells.
