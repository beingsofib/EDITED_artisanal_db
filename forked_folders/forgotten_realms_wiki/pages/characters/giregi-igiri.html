Giregi Igiri was a merchant who specialized in alchemical supplies and potions in Port Nyanzaru, Chult, during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

He was a difficult man to impress.

Activities

Giregi was a very busy merchant who worked for the merchant princes of Port Nyanzaru to facilitate the import and trade of alchemical reagents as well as finished potions, apothecary supplies, poisons, antidotes, and even certain foods and drinks. He worked alongside the Master of the Market as a point of contact for foreign merchants who sold such goods and were seeking to establish a presence in Port Nyanzaru, and he could be found at the city's Bazaar.

History

During the events of the death curse, Giregi helped to organize a seasonal symposium of Port Nyanzaru merchants and artisans in order to facilitate trade into the city.
