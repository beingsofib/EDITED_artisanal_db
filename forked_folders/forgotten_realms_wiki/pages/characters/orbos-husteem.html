Orbos Husteem was a Chondathan human and the patriarch of the Husteem noble house of Waterdeep.

Personality

He was well aware of his brother Orlpar's illegal activity and attempted to keep him in check.

Relationships

Orbos's younger brother was Orlpar Husteem.
