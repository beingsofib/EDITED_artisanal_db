King Pwylloch was ruler of Snowdown in the Moonshae Isles during the early-to-mid 14 century DR. He was the last king of his line.

Activities

He ruled from his castle of Caer Westphal, where he maintained a fortune worth more than 250,000 gold pieces.

History

In the , King Pwylloch was assassinated by Razfallow on the orders of Cyndre, along with any of his heirs.
