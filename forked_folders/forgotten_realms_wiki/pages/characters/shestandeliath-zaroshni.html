Shestandeliath Zaroshni was a dragonborn member of Clan Shestandeliath in 1486 DR.

Relationships

Zaroshni was the sister of Parvida She was in love with Dumuzi, and tried hard to be chosen as his wife.

History

Zaroshni was one of the few known members of the Liberators of the Steel Sky.

On Nightal 15 of 1486 DR, Parvida was killed, alongside her fellow companions of the Liberators,

Unlike the rest of the Liberators, the maurezhi ate her in order to impersonate her to infiltrate Djerad Thymar.
