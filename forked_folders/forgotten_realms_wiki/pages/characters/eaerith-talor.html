Eaerith Talor was a warlock active in Athkatla during the 15 century DR.

Description

Eaerith was a graceful and handsome man, who regularly pretended to be a woman in order to access the parties held by local nobility or wealthy individuals. Going by the sobriquet "Luthlund of Luskan", he relied on those events to find patrons to sponsor his adventures.

History

Sometime between the and the , Eaerith suffered an attack from the Sword of Savras and consequently lost access to his pact magic. He however retained the ability to wield magic items.
