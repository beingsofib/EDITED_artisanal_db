Elember was a lord of Tavaray on the Sword Coast North in the early 3 century DR.

History

In the , when introduced to someone whom he believed to be Herald Huntinghorn of Tavaray, the Mage Royal of Athalantar, Ubriien Orlyn, assumed him to be an envoy of Lord Elember.
