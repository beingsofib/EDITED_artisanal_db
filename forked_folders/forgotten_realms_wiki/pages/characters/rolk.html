Rolk was a dwarf merchant in Phlan in 1489 DR.

Personality

Rolk was a gregarious individual who recently had a change of fortune. He took his new job very seriously.

Relationship

He was an acquaintance of Gilfron Milon.

History

In 1489 DR, after Vorgansharax's coup, he became the main agent of the local Merchant's Guild. Rolk hired an adventuring party to recover a book from the lair of the black dragon Throstulgrael, which the dragon had raided from a Guild's caravan.
