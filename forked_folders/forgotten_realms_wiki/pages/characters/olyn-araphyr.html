Olyn Araphyr was an elven performer and Harper agent who worked in Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Olyn had long red-brown hair in a braid, and wore a gray and white dress with a choker.

History

After the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon around 1485 DR, Olyn joined Rina Belostos's Harper team in Eveningstar as they tracked Oriphaun Huntsilver and the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll. While acting as a troupe of musical entertainers, Olyn sent agents to meet Girmond Dyle in the Blooded Wood, in order to organize an attack on Granwell Keep and capture the remainder of the Scroll.
