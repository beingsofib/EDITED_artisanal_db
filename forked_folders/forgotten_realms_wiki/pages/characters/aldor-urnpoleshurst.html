Aldor Urnpoleshurst was a lawyer just before the Tyranny of Dragons.

Personality

Aldor was very suspicious of others and made accusations over nothing. He was a lawyer in training but a skunk in inclination.

History

Around 1489 DR, Aldor was forced to escape from Baldur's Gate, probably because he'd been involved in a scandal. He boarded a great caravan, which unknown to him was full of Cult of the Dragon agents transporting treasures for the Hoard of the Dragon Queen.
