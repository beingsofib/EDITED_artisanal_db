Tot Jansen was a child of the numerous gnome Jansen family in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Just like his sister, Tat, Tot had fair skin and light brown hair.

History

In the , Tat and Tot lived in Athkatla's River District slums in the Jansen's family home, along with Ma Jansen, Kylie Jansen, Jan Jansen, and Uncle Gerhardt. Tot had a foul attitude, calling guests ugly to their face.
