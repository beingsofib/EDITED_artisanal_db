Ghondalaath was a blue dragon great wyrm in Anauroch in 1358 DR.

Ghondalaath was a dragon of awesome size and powers, that commanded terrible and unusual spells. He lived in a massive cavern complex in the stone pinnacle called Heroes' Helm.
