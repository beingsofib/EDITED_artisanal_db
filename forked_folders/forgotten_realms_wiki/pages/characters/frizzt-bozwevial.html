Frizzt Boz'Wevial was a drow wizard and a distant relative of the adventurer Drizzt Do'Urden.

Description

Frizzt had night-black skin and a mane of white hair. He had a very pronounced nose, large ears, and a sharp chin. He dressed in a black robe and flame-patterned leather boots, and carried a medallion around his neck.

Possessions

He owned a robe of the archmagi as well as an amulet of life protection.

History

Frizzt was gifted his magical robe and amulet at a young age by his distant, famous relative, Drizzt Do'Urden. Frizzt found the gift irresponsible, feeling he was much too young to be granted such powerful items. Ultimately, he kept the gifts, and later attempted to use them against Drizzt.
