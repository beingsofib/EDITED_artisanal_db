Beomult Ragner was the judge of Eaglton and innkeeper at the Eagle's Eyrie inn of that same town in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Though mostly an innkeeper at the Eagle's Eyrie, of which he was the owner, he acted as a judge in town, with the authority to call for imprisonments and impose fines.

History

During the mid-to-late 14 century DR, during the spring when the vampire wizard Romanisk Vlaensk had been released from under Stag Falls, Beomult Ragner was an innkeep at the Eagle's Eyrie in the nearby town of Eaglton, where he appeared to have been working for at least one season, remembering when traders from Stag Falls usually arrived.

During one evening when agents of Ravens Bluff hired by Marcus Rubicon passed by, he may have shared some information with them regarding the path to Stag Falls, the harsh winter they'd last had, and the patron from Ravens Bluff that had went towards Stag Falls the week prior. He'd also noticed that no one had come from Stag Falls that spring. Through the course of the evening, he might also have had the opportunity to put the thief Louie in lockup, depending on how his attempts at pickpocketing these men went.

He might also fine them the next morning if for whatever reason they damaged the apiary.
