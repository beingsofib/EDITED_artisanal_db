Princess Rasa Parhek was an ill-fated Amnian daughter of the Parhek dynasty.

Relationships

Rasa was the daughter of King Dhanar Parhek, the twin of Prince Dhanar the Younger, and the elder sister of Prince Malek the Coinmage. She married Dornar Steelsar, and they had their children Choris, Rosa, Rohav, and Rhothar.

History

Rasa was moved as a child to Silverymoon after her father was killed and Amn fell into civil war. Living under the protection of Lady Alustriel Silverhand, she married Dornar Steelsar and had four children together. They were eventually found by Amnian assassins, however, and in the , she was forced to flee the city with Rhothar while Dornar and Choris fought to the death, and Rohav was kidnapped. She was believed to have died later in 1302 DR.
