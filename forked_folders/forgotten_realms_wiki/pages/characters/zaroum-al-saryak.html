Zaroum Al-Saryak () was the captain of the pirate ship The Emerald Eye in the late 15 century DR.

Appearance

He was missing his left eye and had an "x-shaped" scar on his face.

Personality

Zaroum was a calculating and ruthless individual. He regularly keel-hauled or even beheaded those in his service that he found displeasing.

He had a particular affinity for gem stones.

Possessions

True to his tastes, Zaroum's coat and his scimitar featured brilliant gems in their decoration. He even had a large gem embedded into his left eye socket. While it did not work for anyone else, this gem allowed Zaroum to see spectral routes that led him to greater caches of treasure.

Relationships

Zaroum's first mate was the Calishite berserker Voltan.

He regularly communication with Liara Portyr, the Commander of Fort Beluarian, by means of a sending stone.
