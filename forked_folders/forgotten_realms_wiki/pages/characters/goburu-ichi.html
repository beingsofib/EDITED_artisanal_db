Goburu Ichi was a shukenja kuei in Ito-jo in 1360 DR.

History

Ichi was a shukenja of the Sun Temple who one day decided to go to Ito-jo to cleanse it of evil. However, he was killed by Ito Murasame and rose as an undead kuei, waiting to possess a new body. In 1360 DR, an adventuring party hired by Michimori Uemon to recover the Seven Swords met Ichi, who possibly decided to help them. Afterward, in any case, he went to the afterlife.
