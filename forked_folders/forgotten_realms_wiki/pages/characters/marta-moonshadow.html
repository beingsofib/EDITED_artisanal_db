Marta Moonshadow was a sun elf wizard in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR.

History

In 1492 DR, after listening to rumors about House Auvryndar's emergence in Undermountain, Marta went to the Sargauth Level and hoped to join their ranks. She found that the drow, however, had no trust in her, and thus they put Marta in prison. She hoped to somehow impress the drow and finally join them.

Personality

Marta had always been fascinated by drow culture.
