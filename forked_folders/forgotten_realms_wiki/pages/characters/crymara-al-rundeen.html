Crymara al Rundeen was a human thief and a founder and leader of the Clenched Fist, a Rundeen-affiliated mercenary and adventuring company based in Schamedar in Calimshan in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

She was a sensual and seductive woman, but carried an explosive temper.

Abilities

She was skilled in thievery and deft conversation, was strong, and had a talent with throwing knives.

Relationships

A fellow Clenched Fist leader, the wereboar Wagym, was unwisely attracted to Crymara.
