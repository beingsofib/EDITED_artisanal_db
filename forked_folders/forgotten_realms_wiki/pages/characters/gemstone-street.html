Gemstone Street

Gemstone Street was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 century DR.

Location

Gemstone Street was found within northwest Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. It branched from Warrior Street in the north and terminated a short distance away in the south at the barrier separating the Inner Quarter from the Common Quarter.

Description

It was one of Zhentil Keep's smaller streets at roughly in length.
