Vonn Dalurieth was a merchant and mage from Calaunt in the Vast.

Description

He was short and graying.

Personality

He really liked his bears. In fact, he favored his bears so much that he was willing to forgive much to recover one. He liked to show off his magic.

Abilities

Vonn Dalurieth could cast a spell similar to ice storm, and he could also cast a spell to serve wine from afar, akin to mage hand.

Possessions

Vonn Dalurieth had two bears, which were emblems of his family's fortune.

Relationships

His daughter was Arveene Dalurieth.

History

One night, the avariel bladesinger Taegan Nightwind attempted to steal away his daughter, Arveene Dalurieth; he desisted as he realized Arveene was not being held against her will. That same night, men working for Falar Aporos killed one of Vonn's bears and stole the other. Not long after, however, Taegan Nightwind defeated Falar's men; his bear was returned soon after. The avariel became a guest of his.

Rumors & Legends

Falar Aporos lied that Vonn was keeping his daughter against her will so that Taegan would steal her away, creating a distraction for him to steal the bear.
