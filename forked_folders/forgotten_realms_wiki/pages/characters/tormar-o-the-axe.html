Tormar o' the Axe was a Tashlutan human ranger of Silvanus in Lapaliiya in the mid–14 century DR. Circa 1373 DR, he was the ruler of the ruler of Malaxer, the Lapaliiyan town holy to Silvanus, holding the title High Lapalyth (baron).
