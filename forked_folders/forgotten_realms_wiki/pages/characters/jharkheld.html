Jharkheld was a magistrate at Luskan's Prisoners' Carnival in the late 1360s DR.

Description

The magistrate was old and thin, and had a nasally voice. He was said to take a lot of pleasure in torturing his victims.

History

In the late 1360s DR, he interrogated four suspects regarding the attempted assassination of Captain Deudermont. Jharkheld tortured the pirate assassins Creeps Sharky and Tee-a-nicknick to death. He was keen to carry on his show and kill Morik and Wulfgar, but Deudermont himself intervened, and the pair were spared.
