Lamia Crytrapper was the head of the Crytrapper family, a major Amnian family, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Lady Crytrapper wielded great influence in Crimmor, as she knew of secrets hidden by many important people. She invested in small businesses, focusing on those run by women, and more particularly those created by widows. She was also known to regularly sponsor minstrels.

Personality

Lamia was a kind and cheerful old lady who loved the countryside.

Relationships

Lady Crytrapper had several influential friends and family members:

  • Her cousin was Corlyn Braen, Mayor of Crimmor.
  • Her sister Chynna Crytrapper was a priestess in the Crimman Church of Lliira.
