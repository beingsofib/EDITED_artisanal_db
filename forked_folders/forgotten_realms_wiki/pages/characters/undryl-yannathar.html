High Loremaster Undryl Yannathar was the leader of the Church of Oghma in Selgaunt in 1358 DR.

Activties

Undryl led a clergy of 16 priests and 14 followers from the Sanctum of the Scroll temple in Selgaunt. In addition to being a high priest of Oghma, Undryl was also a Lorekeeper, or specialty priest of the God of Knowledge.
