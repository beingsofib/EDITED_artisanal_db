Amahl Abd al-Muhlis was a mamluk who lived in the Pantheist city of I'tiraf in Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Activities

Commander Amahl was the leader of the Devoted Mamluk Society, and led them against conflicts with the Exalted for dominance as the region's finest mamluk organization.

Amahl also held the title of Most Respected Warrior.
