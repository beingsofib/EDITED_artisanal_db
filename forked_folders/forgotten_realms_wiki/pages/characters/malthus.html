Malthus was a Dragonsoul of the reborn Cult of the Dragon that arose as a great threat to the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.

Abilities

As a spellcaster, Malthus focused on magic that utilized acids and poisonous gas such as the kind often found in swamps.

Possessions

Malthus wielded a large, highly-decorated staff in battle.

History

During the 1480s DR, Malthus led the cult's efforts to find the grave of the former adventurer Belast Massandar in Neverdeath Graveyard in order converse with his spirit. The cult believe that Massandar's spirit could lead them to the resting place of Cruven Gantara, which in turn would allow them access to a portal leading to the Flame Fault on Tuern.

Malthus and his agents were thwarted by the efforts of the Harpers agent Boward and her ally, the Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge.
