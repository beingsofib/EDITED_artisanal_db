Nikolls was a elven druid serving Silvanus in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a bronze-skinned elf, with golden hair.

Personality

He was a cheerful person with a solid sense of humor. Though dedicated to the service of Silvanus, he found it hard to maintain balance if either his or his friends life were threatened.

Possessions

Wearing a set of leather armor, he would wield a golden sickle and a wooden shield. He also carried a vial of holy water and a potion of flying.
