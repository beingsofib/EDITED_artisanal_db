Tejan ibn-Dhabar was a skilled sha'ir who traveled the elemental planes.

History

Tejan eventually transformed himself into a water elemental when visiting the Elemental Plane of Water.

Possessions

He carried the wicked vorpal scimitar Starzad for a time. Tejan sent the sword to a friend in Sigil before his transformation.
