Sarmeir was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

History

After spring began in the , Harnrim Starangh made a quick raid near Mouth o' Gargoyles on the sanctum of Vangerdahast, then retired from his post of Royal Mage. After he slew three War Wizards and escaped, the War Wizard Huldyl Rauthur ordered Darthym, who'd just reported the losses, to wake Sarmeir and have the two of them stand guard in the armory, where two people had been slain.
