Thurve Thentavva was a cobbler and shopkeeper inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Thurve was a balding man who wore spectacles. He had a calm demeanor.

Activities

He owned a cobbler's shop located on Vellarr's Lane in Waterdeep's Trades Ward called Thentavva's Boots.

Thurve was also a member of the secretive group of vigilantes called the Red Sashes.

Possessions

He owned a set of iron bands of Bilarro.
