Waillan Micanty

Waillan Micanty was a sailor who served aboard the Sea Sprite during the mid–14 century DR. He was quite skilled at manning its ship-mounted ballista.

While he was the youngest member of the Sea Sprite's crew, Waillan was unflinchingly loyal to Captain Deudermont.
