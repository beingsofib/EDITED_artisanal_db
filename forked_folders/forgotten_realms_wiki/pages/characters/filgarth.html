Filgarth was a former warrior from Tethyr who enjoyed retirement on an isolated farm in the kingdom's countryside during the mid–15 century DR.

Description

In his later years, Filgarth rarely if ever spoke. If he did, he would have revealed an entirely toothless mouth. His arms and face were laced with scars, received from his years in battle.

History

For a few months in the , Filgarth watched over Evonne Linde's children, Teague and Ysabel, following the death of their father Garion Linden. He then took them back to their mother's home in Darromar, about a tenday before the coronation of Queen Anais Rhindaun.
