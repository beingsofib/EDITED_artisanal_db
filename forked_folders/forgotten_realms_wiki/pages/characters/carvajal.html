Carvajal was a Netherese arcanist who taught magic at the Variator's Intelligentsia in Eileanar before its destruction in .

Activities

Carvajal was an expert at the transmutation of materials. She taught this at the college under Dean Arrias, particularly focusing on its applications with mythallars.
