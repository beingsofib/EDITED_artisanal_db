Raggnar Redtooth was a tavern owner in the Cult of the Dragon–controlled village of Parnast in the Greypeak Mountains.

History

Raggnar managed the tavern the Golden Tankard in Parnast. Despite not being a cultist, Raggnar was bribed by them to control the villagers and possible visitors. He tried to discourage visitors from remaining in the village for long.
