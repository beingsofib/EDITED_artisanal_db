Lady Sashesh was a powerful and influential merchant, as well as the representative of Sembia in Scardale in 1368 DR.

Activities

Sashesh was a trade liaison that was the principal agent of a cabal of wealthy merchants that wanted to make Scardale part of Sembia. She worked toward this goal by dominating trade and with occasional brides, using the Sembian garrison as her headquarters.
