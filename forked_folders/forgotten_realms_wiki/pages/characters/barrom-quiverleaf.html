Barrom Quiverleaf was a half-elf wizard who restlessly searched for a mysterious woman.

Description

Barrom had chiseled facial features, framed by a mane of wavy, brown hair. His pointed ears hinted at his half-elven heritage. He wore a dark blue cloak over otherwise gray-blue clothes.

Personality

Most attributed Barrom's restless nature to his half-elf blood, but it was actually rooted in a desire for answers.

Possessions

He owned a selection of magical items: a pair of boots of elvenkind, a cloak of the bat, as well as a ring of sustenance given to him by his mysterious benefactor.

Activities

Barrom was approached by an enigmatic woman who promised to reward him with long life and great knowledge if he could find her again.
