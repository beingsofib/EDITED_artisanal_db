Revi was an inhabitant of Vaelan in Durpar in the late 14 century DR. He was one of Zeltaebar Datharathi's men.

Description

He dressed in all gray, sported greasy hair, and wielded a prying bar.

Hitory

In the , he was searching through Eined Datharathi's room in the West Garden district of Vaelan when Warian Datharathi stumbled upon him. Warian nearly removed his leg during the fight; all the combatants broke off when Zeltaebar arrived at the department, thankfully.
