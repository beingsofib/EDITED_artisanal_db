Sorntran Arkenep was a male drow who served as necromancer in the Ziggurat complex atop the city of Sschindylryn in the 1480s DR.

Abilities

Sorntran could cast acid fog, energy drain, and negative energy burst.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Sorntran guarded the Ziggurat, particularly the ceremonial dagger used to activate High Priestess Hekta Szind's portal from the Ziggurat to the rooftop Temple of Lolth. When Elminster and Ana Brabener arrived to stop Hekta, Sorntran fell in battle defending the ceremonial dagger.
