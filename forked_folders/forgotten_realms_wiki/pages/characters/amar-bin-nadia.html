Amar bin Nadia was a member of the House of Nasr tribe in the High Desert of west Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Personality

Amar was bitter and cynical because of the attention and praise heaped upon his sister.

Activities

Overshadowed by his older sister, Amar chose a different path and studied the ways of the sand mage.

Relationships

Amar was the youngest child of Sheikh Nadia umm Fadela. His older sister was Fadela Bloodmane.
