"The Watchspider Wizard" was the epithet given to an otherwise unnamed drow wizard, active some time before the mid–14 century DR.

An angry priestess of Lolth once summoned a yochlol and sent it to kill him. To elude it, he used the wonderform spell to hide in the form of a wingless wonder.
