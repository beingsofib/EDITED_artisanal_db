Junky was a mining boss and member of the dwarven Clan Battlehammer that dwelt in Kelvin's Cairn in Icewind Dale in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Junky was present when the Companions of the Hall first met with Stokely Silverstream after the reunion of their reincarnated forms. He expressed his wariness of trusting Drizzt Do'Urden after the hero went missing for nearly two decades.
