Alshinree was a dancer in the mid–8 century DR.

Description

By the year , Alshinree was getting a bit gaunt due to old age.

Abilities

In her youth, she had been quite an enticing dancer. About once a month, she went into a trance during dances, which allowed her to cast a strange spell that gave directions to caches of unfound magic.

Activities

She danced at a club known as the Stars and Sword.
