Durlan was a moon elven

Activities

Durlan was considered a good and noble person. He spent his time working for the good of all beings.

Hisotry

Some years before the , Durlan met Thazienne Uskevren, a daughter of the Uskevren family of Selgaunt. The noble's daughter spent her childhood on the unsavory streets where she mastered her thieves's skills. The moon elf was found of the child, and after she saved a baby from the midden, Durlan gifted her an emerald ring of projection that one day saved Thazi's life.
