Caulgrith Narueto was a dwarf warrior inhabiting the city of Suldolphor in the far eastern reaches of Calimshan in south Faerûn near the Land of the Lions.

Description

Caulgrith possessed truly unusual breadth, being wider that he was tall. He had heavily muscled arms and calves.

Activities

Caulgirth was a skilled blacksmith and jeweler, specializing in fine detail work. He owned a shop located on Pelaerel Street.

Rumors & Legends

The dwarf appeared to never require sleep, leading some to believe he had a twin brother who stood in for him when he rested.
