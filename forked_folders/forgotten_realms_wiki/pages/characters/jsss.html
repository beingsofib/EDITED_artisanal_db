Jsss

Person

homeHawa, Northwest Zakhara

Jsss was a baby sea wyrm living under the streets of Hawa in 1367 DR.

Activities

Jsss served as a guardian to the secret entrance to lair of the Cult of Istishia.

Relationships

Jsss was the pet of a nereid named Ghaliyah.
