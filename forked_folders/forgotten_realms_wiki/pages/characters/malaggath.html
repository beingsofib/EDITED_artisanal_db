Malagatth was the Slaver Lord of House Xorlarrin in 1484 DR.

History

Malaggath was the leader of the Xorlarrin expedition to the Rothé Valley to capture slaves until Imbross Irri'kar recruited an adventurer to stop the drow raids and kill Malaggath.
