Sorina Parnock was a sage who specialized in the Underdark, living in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Sorina had light skin and brown hair, wearing a purple and white dress.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Sorina was in town lending her expertise on Underdark matters. She got word that House Cay'Vorym was taking captured Purple Dragons and putting them in death matches at the Cay'Vorym Fighting Pit. She sent a team of adventurers down to address the matter, who infiltrated by pretending to be customers before being caught by Feryl Cay'Vorym and dropped into the pit themselves. After a fierce series of battles the arena was destroyed, Feryl was slain, and the particular threat to Purple Dragons ended.
