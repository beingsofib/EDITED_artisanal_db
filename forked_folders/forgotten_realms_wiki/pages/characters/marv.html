Marv was a human bouncer who kept peace at the Black Eye tavern and festhall in the city of Westgate in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Marv was a giant of a man with short chestnut-colored hair. He was tall and was built like an ox. However, he was overshadowed by his truly massive brother and fellow bouncer, Gregory, and the tavern's proprietor, another hulking brother, named Little Davey.

History

In the , Marv's favorite entertainer who worked at the Black Eye, Dimples the Dancer, disappeared and stopped showing up for work. In reality, "Dimples" was a member of Westgate's City Guard and she danced for patrons of the festhall as a hobby. She twisted her ankle in the line of duty and simply wasn't able to perform until she was healed.
