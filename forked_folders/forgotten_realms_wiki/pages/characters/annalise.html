Annalise was a young tavern wench at the Bubbling Bulwark circa 1369 DR.

Description

Annalise was regarded as very pretty, and had bright red hair.

Relationships

Annalise carried on a semi-secret relationship with Lord Partic Thistle, whom she loved dearly. The greatest impediment to their eventual marriage was the high-society scandal that would come from a noble marrying a common barmaid.

Annalise was best friends with her fellow wench Crystyn, and was on good terms with the other staff of the Bulwark.

The dashing young adventurer Garit Strongarm wished to have a relationship with her, but his possessive jealousy, cruel disposition, and fierce temper ruined any chances he might have had.

Annalise had something of a rival in the maid Tami, who tried to court Lord Thistle herself, to no effect.

History

In the , she had the traumatic experiences of having threats made against her by Garit Strongarm, and believing her beloved Partic had been murdered.
