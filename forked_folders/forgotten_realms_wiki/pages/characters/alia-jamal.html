Alia Jamal was a member of the royal family of Medina al-Afyal in 1367 DR.

Personality

Though a conservative moralist, Alia was quite predatory in the pursuit of her goals.

Description

Alia was described as being thin and vulture-like.

Relationships

Alia was the aunt of Padishah Alad bin Alaq bin Alonka on his father's side and was the Empress by her family ties.

Activities

She was focused solely on securing the Elephant Throne of Medina al-Afyal for her son Jamal bin Alia.
