Master Wizard Tsabyrr was a powerful drow mage of the late 15 century DR who served in the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the 1480s DR.

Description

Tsabyrr had long straight white hair and white tattoos, and wore an armored robe. He carried a drow poisoned longsword, a hand crossbow, and throwing daggers.

Abilities

The Master Wizard could cast ball lightning, blur, feeblemind, delayed blast fireball, disintegrate, force missiles, greater shout, Otiluke's freezing sphere, prismatic ray, and see invisibility.

History

During the invasion of the surface, Tsabyrr oversaw the D'Zani outpost, a fortified post on the circuitous route from Sschindylryn to Eveningstar. Following the events in Eberron around the Spinner of Shadows and Lolth's intervention at the Spinner's Prison, a group of Eberronian heroes emerged in the Underdark and fought their way to the surface. They followed the stream of drow reinforcements and caught up to them at the D'Zani outpost, to the confusion and outrage of the defenders who did not expect enemies to appear from behind. Though he fought well and was joined by the outpost's defenders, Tsabyrr fell in battle.
