Ounadar, also called the Blood-Drenched, was a cleric of Garagos who rose to power in the Vale of the Reaver in Yondath.

Activities

Ounadar gathered a congregation consisting of criminals, berserkers, and others not opposed to committing acts of violence. Ounadar and his brethren practiced their slaughter on creatures spawned from several captured deepspawn. He dreamed of one day conquering and ruling the city of Westgate.
