Rolo was an agent of Thylius Caramitru in Mulmaster in 1491 DR.

Activities

In 1491 DR, Rolo and his brother Jott were the main agents of Thylius, running the Yellow Breath Chapter House for him. However, an adventuring party hired to find some missing noblewomen entered the house and defeated and captured Rolo.
