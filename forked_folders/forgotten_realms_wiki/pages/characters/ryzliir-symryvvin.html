Ryzliir Symryvvin was a drow elite warrior in Menzoberranzan in the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa 1486 DR, Ryzliir was commanded to lead a drow battalion to intercept an expedition led by some adventurers, who'd been hired by Bruenor Battlehammer to investigate the threat of a demon lord, but she and her troops were defeated.
