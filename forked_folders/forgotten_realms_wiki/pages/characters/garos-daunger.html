Garos Daunger was a chancellor of Hillsfar in the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa 1486 DR, Garos attended the First Lord's Midsummer Masquerade ball.

Personality

Garos was a shrewd politician willing to do anything to ensure the supremacy of the First Lord over the noble houses. He was very condescending, trying to always show his superiority.

He liked hunting.
