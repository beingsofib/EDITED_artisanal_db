Quartermaster Marcellus was a soldier who served in the Neverwinter Guard during the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

He wore a suit of chain mail armor and a tabard.

History

After the siege of Neverwinter in the , Marcellus was stationed in the city's Tower District. He provided equipment to his fellow soldiers and sold supplies to adventurers acting on behalf of Captain Janros to aid in the effort to take back the area from the occupying force of the Many-Arrows orcs.
