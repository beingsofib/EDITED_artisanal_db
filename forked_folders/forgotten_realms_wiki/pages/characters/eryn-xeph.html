Eryn Xeph () was a young and impressionable elf from Baldur's Gate who joined the cult of Zariel some time during the late 15 century DR.

Description

She had a rather slender form, with pale skin, blue eyes, and silver hair. Eryn had the distinctive characteristic of having an extremely low tolerance for physical pain.

Personality

Eryn had a fervent interest in philosophy.

Activities

Eryn desired to attain true power over the Prime Material plane, and believed her best chances were to join the inner circle of Zariel's cult.

History

In the , Eryn unwittingly offered herself as a sacrifice to the cult of Zariel in the sewers of Baldur's Gate, believing she was being accepted as a full-fledged member.
