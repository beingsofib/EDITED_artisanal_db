Hasan (djinn)

Person
nameHasan
sexMale
raceDjinni

Hasan was the djinni servant of the wizard Amelior Amanitas. Amelior kept Hasan within a golden variant of the iron flask.

History

In 1358 DR, an injured Amelior loaned Hasan to a group of adventurers he had hired in order to stop a rampaging, gargantuan golem he had constructed.
