William was a traveling musician, performer, and a bard on the Sword Coast North circa 1374 DR.

Description

William has a handsome human man with fair complexion, brown hair. He wore his facial hair in a well-groomed manner. William's warm eyes betrayed a slight hint of hardness behind them.

History

In the , William and his tambourine, was a part of a roguish band of traveling performers that included Lila, a flautist, venn a sting musician, and the troupe's leader, a flaming dancer named Azahr. William and the band spent some time performing at the Crossroad Keep as it was being reinvigorated by trade and commerce after a devastating Shadow War. The band's leader was happy to be making coin once again and planned on staying at the keep for a little while. However, that time was cut short after Azahr sole a precious artifact – Gauntlets of Ironfist, from the keep's seneschal. William accompanied his bandmates to Azahr's cabin hideout in the Neverwinter Wood. Khelgar Ironfist sent a group of adventurers to retrieve the precious stolen magic item; a brawl was ensured.
