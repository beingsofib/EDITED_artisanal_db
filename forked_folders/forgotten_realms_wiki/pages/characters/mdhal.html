M'dhal was a Netherese arcanist and experimentalist that specialized in protective spells.

Description

A passionate experimentalist, M'dhal was both wise and hardy.

Activities

The arcanist spent much time involved with his experiments, some of which involved attempts to control creatures from other planes. He worked within Stone Fort, which was located close to the Mines of Dekanter.
