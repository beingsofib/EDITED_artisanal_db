Tregail was a young beekeeper from Eaglton in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

She harvested the hives for breakfast every morning.

Relationships

Her mother sold apiary-related products.

History

Tregail was a witness to the patrol from Ravens Bluff that went to Stag Falls after the latter's taxes went unpaid one spring in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. When adventurers from the same city came after, they may have been able to ask her about the patrol, which she remembered, if they didn't scare her off.
