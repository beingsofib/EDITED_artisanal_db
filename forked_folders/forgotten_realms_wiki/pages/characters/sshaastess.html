Sshaastess was a powerful medusa who came to the Lost Souls' Swamp of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Sshaastess was green, scaly, and crowned with snakes. She was very curvy, as so many medusae seemed to be.

Abilities

Sshaastess was a skilled archer, and by using the captured Nether Scroll could draw on powers to corrupt and control other beings and create magical barriers.

History

When the hermit Seamus hid a piece of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll in Fallen Oak Shrine on behalf of Oriphaun Huntsilver, Sshaastess detected the magical emanations and came to investigate. She found the Scroll and stole it, driving Seamus away and setting up her lair at the Shrine, including a hidden treasure for the Scroll, the ironwood khopesh, and the many other treasures she had accrued in her time. She used her powers to control earth elementals, including her personal bodyguards Rrosp and Gruck, and even drove the local dryads, wood woads, vine horrors, wolves, and bears into a frenzied devotion. She came to an accord of sorts with the Shadow of Lost Souls' Swamp, allowing it to keep its own territory with its will-o'-wisps.

Harper reinforcements were eventually sent by Eradin Krall, finding Seamus and fighting their way through the swamp. Despite Sshaastess's minions and magical defenses, she was overcome and slain.
