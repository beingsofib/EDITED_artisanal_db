Wynneth was a Reghedmen human and the wife of King Jarund Elkhardt from the Tribe of the Elk in the late 15 century DR.

History

After the death of her son, Jarund Twice-Born, almost a decade before , she and King Jarund Elkhardt have not succeeded in conceiving a new child resulting in deliberations about the tribe's future leadership.
