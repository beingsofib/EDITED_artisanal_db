Prand Pebblefoot was a great-nephew of the famed hin hero Pim Pebblefoot, active sometime in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Prand inherited his great uncle's enchanted boots, called The Sturdy Feet of Pim, after the hero's death at the ripe

old age of 135 years. Prand wanted to follow in Pim's footsteps and become an adventurer. Sometime before the , Prand Pebblefoot left his village in the Gray Hills and headed for the Jewel of the North: the city of Neverwinter. What became of Prand was unrecorded, but the Sturdy Feet left his possession, ending up in the inventory of Dayne Lynneth, a Blacklake District merchant.
