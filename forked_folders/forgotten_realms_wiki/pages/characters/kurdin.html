Kurdin was a powerful alaghi druid active in the forests of northwest Faerûn in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Kurdin was a hermit, and was wise and exceptionally intelligent among the alaghi. He was rumored to have played a role in the destruction of Hellgate Keep in the .

Relationships

He was a close ally of the treants of the Woods of Turlang.
