Rendaril was a shopkeeper in Bryn Shander sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

Activities

Rendaril managed the Rendaril Emporium, the largest trade house of the city. Thank to Rendaril ability's learned in the markets of Waterdeep, he made huge business.
