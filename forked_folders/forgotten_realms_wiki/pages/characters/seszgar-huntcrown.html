Seszgar Huntcrown was a heir of House Huntcrown in the late 15 century DR.

Description

When he was decapitated, his features got frozen in terror. His usual face was a sneer.

Personality

He was generally known as a sneering braggart.

Abilities

Seszgar was purportedly confident in his fencing skills.

Relationships

Marlin Stormserpent hated him until the day he died. Seszgar was the heir of House Huntcrown.

History

In his childhood, the Huntcrown heir had bullied a young Marlin Stormserpent, who bore a grudge against him to the end.

In , Seszgar was slain by the Blueflame ghosts Relve Langral and Treth Halonter, sent by Marlin Stormserpent against him. They carried out his head in a bundle, which they presented to Marlin Stormserpent after the fact.

Rumors & Legends

While Ganrahast and Vainrence were briefly informed that Seszgar Huncrown was plotting with Rothglar Illance and Harmond Hawklin to unleash a "ball of Spellplague" in the Council of the Dragon of , this was a plot by Marlin Stormserpent.
