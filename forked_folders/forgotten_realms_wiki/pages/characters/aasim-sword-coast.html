Aasim was a cleric who dedicated himself to the dogma of the Iron Throne leader and Bhaalspawn Sarevok during the mid−14 century DR.

Activities

Unbeknownst to the Iron Throne, Aasim and his companions Gardush, Naaman, Diyab, Alai, along with their leader Zhalimar Cloudwulfe, sought to advance Sarevok's agenda rather than that of the mercantile organization.

Possessions

Aasim wore a suit of +1 enchanted studded leather armor and a +1 mace. He also possessed a single wand of the heavens.

History

In the , Aasim and his allies were sent by the Iron Throne to the city of Baldur's Gate to advise Thaldorn in his negotiations with Emissary Tar and the city's Council of Four. Just prior to when that meeting was supposed to take place, the group was thwarted by Sarevok's half-brother, Abdel Adrian.
