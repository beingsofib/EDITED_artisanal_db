Warryn was a halfling archer that once took a job for the looter Gimblebock.

Description

He had prominent veins that ran underneath his skin. He wore a mask on the lower half of his face, which hid some of these from view.

History

In the , Warryn and the other looters were trying to find their way into a crypt. Warryn stood guard on top of a pillar.
