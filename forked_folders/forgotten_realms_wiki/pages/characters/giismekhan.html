Giismekhan was a powerful and favored draegloth of Lolth in the Demonweb Pits.

History

During the Darkening and the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, Giismekhan stood guard over a portal in the Demonweb Pits that led to the Underdark near Eveningstar. Giismekhan was overcome by adventurers from Eberron who sought a way to Toril after passing through the Spinner's Prison.
