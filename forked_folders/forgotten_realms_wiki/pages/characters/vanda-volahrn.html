Vanda Volahrn was a wizard, owner of the Pearled Harp trading coster, and one of the few female pashas in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

She was the half-Calishite daughter of a courtesan in Suzail, Cormyr, and of Pasha Samiir el Volahrn. She was his sole surviving offspring.

History

Vanda was living with her mother in Suzail and unaware of her birthright when, in the late 1360s DR, agents of Syl-Pasha Ralan el Pesarkhal informed her of her father's passing. Furthermore, they offered her Samiir's status as pasha and ownership of his trading coster, the Pearled Harp, provided she used it to support certain trade alliances beneficial to Calimshan's ruler.

Vanda accepted this and moved to Calimport, becoming suddenly wealthy and one of the only female pashas there. Her takeover of the Pearled Harp blocked rival merchants who coveted it for themselves.

Activities

Now much wealthier and resourced, Vanda was satisfied with focusing on her studies of magic whilst leaving the everyday running of the Pearled Harp, also called Purple Pearls, trading coster to its employees in Calimport and Calimshan. She stayed alert to local politics, despite what others thought, and was well aware of the jealous rival merchants who desired her death as well so they might take control of the business.

Home

Vanda lived in the Pearls, a fine house in Azhaya Drudach, Marekh Sabban, in the city's Emerald Ward. Her house reached three stories and had an outer walled yard.
