Fatima bint Fayiz was a tailor who lived Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Activities

Fatima made clothes for people based on personal orders, as well as bulk orders for larger merchant houses.

She worked her cloth during the day and moved to the Grand Bazaar at night to sell her wares to the large crowds.
