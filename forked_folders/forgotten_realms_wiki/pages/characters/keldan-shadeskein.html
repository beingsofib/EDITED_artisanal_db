Keldan Shadeskein was a Shadovar wizard of Returned Netheril who was deployed to Beckside Castle in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Keldan was a pale man with short brown hair. He wore the daring ensemble of a black and gray robe with the symbol of Shar.

Personality

He was very performative and bombastic in his manner of speech, used a sing-song voice, and was very impressed by his own cleverness. Though loyal to the cause, he felt that he was doing most of the work securing the Nether Scroll while Gaston would end up getting most of the credit.

Abilities

Keldan was a skilled wielder of shadow magic, and could cast ball lightning, black dragon bolt, cyclonic blast, force missiles, mass hold monster, sleet storm, slow, and symbol of stunning.

History

When the Shadovar and Harpers pursued the lost Nether Scroll along the High Road to Beckside Castle, Keldan and Gaston Wyrst led the effort there. They infiltrated past the Weathered Dawn with an escort of mercenaries into the other parts of the ruins, with Gaston going directly for the Scroll while Keldan erected a defensive shield. He enslaved and corrupted a local water elemental with shadow magic as his minion, and bound both him and it to the function of the defenses. To protect himself, he set up several staves that melded into the ground and were imbued with protection from good around the ruins, and used them to teleport about and escape the Harper invaders. Though difficult to handle, it was still possible to break or disenchant the staves, and so Keldan was eventually caught and killed, opening the way to Gaston.
