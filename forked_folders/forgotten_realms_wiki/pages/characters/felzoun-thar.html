Felzoun Thar was a dwarf tavern owner inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid–14 DR.

Personality

His fierce reputation matched his bristling red beard. He appeared to have a limitless amount of energy as he bustled about his tavern.

Activities

Felzoun owned a tavern named after himself called Felzoun's Folly which was located in Waterdeep's Trades Ward.

Possessions

He always carried a pair of throwing axes hidden beneath his apron.
