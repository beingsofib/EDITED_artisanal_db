Urrusk was a slaver in Waterdeep as of the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He had an easy smile.

Activities

Roughly a decade prior to , Urrusk was a slaver, paid to lure destitute people into his hut.

History

Urrusk had attempted to lure the young orphan Narnra Shalace to a hut in the Dock Ward about a decade before 1373 DR. Once inside, he tried to molest her, only to be stopped by a second man claiming he was paying Urrusk to lure slaves rather than ruin them. While the second man was struggling with Narnra, Urrusk strangled him with a chain hanging close by. Narnra escaped while they fought.
