Snake was one of the three ninja in the service of Daisake Mayazato in the town of Aru in Wa.

History

Snake trained in ninja arts inside the Warazama clan together with Moonlight and Smoke, until that clan was destroyed by internal dissent. He and his fellows entered into the service of Daisake Mayazato to to repay him for previous services.
