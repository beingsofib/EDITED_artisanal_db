Lord Gould Wyvernspur was a member of the noble House Wyvernspur of Cormyr who lived in the late 10 and early 11 centuries DR.

History

Gould Wyvernspur was born in the to Paton Wyvernspur and Jasanna Larkspar. Gould's only sibling was his sister Andra Wyvernspur. Later in life, he wed Amalee Winter and fathered two children. The older son, Lord Gerrin Wyvernspur, was born in Gould's and Amalee's younger son was Finder Wyvernspur who would eventually become a renowned bard, one of the founding members of the Harpers, and eventually, a deity.
