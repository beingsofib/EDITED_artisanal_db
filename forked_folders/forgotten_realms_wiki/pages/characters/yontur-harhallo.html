Yontur Harhallo was the owner and manager of Harhallo's Warmhearth House, a quiet inn located in Khôltar, south Faerûn, circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR.

Personality

Yontur accepted his lot with equanimity, rarely getting upset about anything. He was kind and had made many friends over the years.

History

Yontur grew up in the family business and was the third generation of Harhallos to run the establishment.
