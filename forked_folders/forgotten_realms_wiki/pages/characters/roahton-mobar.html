Roahton Mobar was a crime-fighting monk from the port town of Nyanzaru in Chult, active sometime before the late 15 century DR.

History

Roahton was known for his flawless stances that were broad and strong. The monk was one of three fighters, including Strigga Morkoscavich and Kimigha Poji, who opposed the Headless Serpent, Secret Venom gang of Port Nyanzaru some years before the . Each of the monks wore one of the sets of enchanted clothing. Roahton's garb was known as The Graceful Cloth.
