Whitlock Brandish was a member of the Ridesmen of Archendale in the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

Whitlock had a single sibling, his sister Melann Brandish.

History

Circa , Whitlock left the army and went on a quest with his sister to try and lift a deadly curse that was cast on their family by Chare'en.
