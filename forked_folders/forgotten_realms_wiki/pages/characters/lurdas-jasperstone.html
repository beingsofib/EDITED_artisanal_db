Lurdas Jasperstone was a member of the Glass-breakers on the Great Spire of the Ice Sea during the mid–14th century DR.

Activities

Lurdas believed that all of Toril would be immersed in fire, and the greater glory of Gond, if the tower on the island being constructed by a cult of Gond was completed.

Lurdas led missions against the cultists, doing anything within his power to harm the tower or harass their daily work.
