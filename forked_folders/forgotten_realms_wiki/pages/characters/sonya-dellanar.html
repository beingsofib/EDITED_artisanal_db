Sonya Dell'Anar was a human turned vampire of the 13 and 14 centuries DR.

History

Before Sonya became a vampire, she had a quarrel over a man with her cousin, which left them on bad terms. Wanting revenge, her cousin contacted an evil wizard who sent a vampire to kill Sonya but who instead turned her into one.

Description

Sonya appeared as a lady in her thirties, but her age was masked by her vampirism—by the mid-to-late 1300s DR, she was almost 150 years old.

Activities

Sonya would attack anyone who resembled her cousin.

Abilities

As well as her vampiric nature, she was very intelligent.
