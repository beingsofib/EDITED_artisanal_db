The Epic Urus

Person
nameThe Epic Urus
homeUtter East, southeast Faerûn

The Epic Urus was a person in the Utter East.

Regarding the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR), the Epic Urus remarked "There is no satisfaction for the Mage who desires more than enough."
