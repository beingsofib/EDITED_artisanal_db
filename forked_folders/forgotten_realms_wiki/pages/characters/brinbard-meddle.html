Brinbard Meddle was a human inhabitant of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Brinbard had light skin and auburn, shoulder-length hair.

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar and the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon during the mid-1480s DR, Brinbard attended a show by a troupe of traveling performers in Eveningstar—not knowing that they were actually Rina Belostos's Harpers in pursuit of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll.
