Bohemund the Belligerent was a wizard and a street entertainer from the city of Westgate in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Bohemund was a human man with sharp facial features, a light skin tone, short black hair, and a thin black mustache. He wore striking red magely garb.

History

In the , Bohemund the Belligerent entertained passersby on the Market Triangle's central plaza with displays of magic. However, it did not impress the locals, who thought that even a well-trained monkey could do Bohemund's magic. In response, Bohemund challenged the hecklers to perform the same magic, and when the hecklers couldn't, he stated them to be below the level of a well-trained monkey.
