Bedi was a half-orc barbarian worshiper of the Absolute and a guide, stationed in the Moonrise Towers during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Bedi was a half-orcish man with muscled body and a hide of muddy green. He had a gruff and rude personality, often voicing his disrespect for General Thorm.

History

In the , Bedi was among those who joined the new religion that sprang in the Western Heartlands. He was stationed within the Moonrise Towers – the fortress controlled by the Absolute's warlord – Ketheric Thorm. Bedi was among relative new additions to the Cult of the Absolute's guides that helped pilgrims reach the Moonrise Towers amidst the deadly shadow-cursed lands.
