Jolboss Twylar, who was nicknamed Stoutguts, was a skilled human fighter and follower of Lurue during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He was quite pompous. His large girth earned him his nickname.

Activities

Jolboss was one of the original members of the Knights of the Unicorn. He led one of the affiliated groups of the Order of the Unicorn following the Time of Troubles.

History

He was originally from Baldur's Gate on the Sword Coast.
