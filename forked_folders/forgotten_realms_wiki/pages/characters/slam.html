Slam was a powerful earth elemental that served as the bodyguard of the mercenary Dom, who worked in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Abilities

Slam was a capable combatant, and focused on protecting Dom above all else. The pair developed tactics that involved Slam being teleported at short range about the battlefiend, intercepting strikes on Dom and repositioning to flank and overwhelm the enemy.

Relationships

Slam did not seem to be controlled or coerced by Dom, but had a negotiated loyalty or even friendship with the man developed through mutual Terran language.

History

Slam dutifully served Dom as bodyguard, protecting him as they performed dangerous mercenary work together. Their group was eventually hired by Thultanthar to seize a piece of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll from the Harpers, ambushing Bensen Virandson on Greenweald Lane. Slam joined Dom at the Harper Lodge destination, waiting in the basement for the piece to be delivered. After the Harpers refused to give up the artifact a fight ensued; one in which Slam was sent back to the Elemental Plane of Earth in pieces, and Dom was sent to the afterlife.
