Gindrol was a priest of Vhaeraun.

History

In 1377 DR, Gindrol was one of the six nightshadows chosen for an assault led by Kâras on the main temple of Kiaransalee. Arriving near the temple, Kâras decided that he, Gindrol, and Talzir should polymorph into deep gnomes, allies of the Crones of Kiaransalee, and infiltrate the temple.
