Samayan was a boy Chosen of Chauntea.

Activities

Samayan was one of the children in a secret shadovar village for captured Chosen. He always played with Stedd Whitehorn and Vanri, often trailing flowers.

History

In 1486 DR, Samayan met and befriended the disguised Dahl Peredur.
