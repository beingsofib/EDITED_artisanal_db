Savant Sandrew, usually know as Sandrew the Wise, was the leading cleric of Oghma in Waterdeep in 1372 DR.

History

Sandrew hailed from the Silver Marches and in 1358 DR he was working at the Halls of Inspiration in Silverymoon.

He later settled in Waterdeep, circa 1362 DR, with the objective of building a temple and a library worthy of his god and the City of Splendors. As part of this effort he recruited many clerics from his former temple.

In the , on the 17 and 18 of Eleint, Sandrew was officiating the wedding ceremony between the Open Lord of Waterdeep Piergeiron the Paladinson and Eidola of Neverwinter. The event was swept away by a whirlwind of sinister plots around the wedding.

He was later one of four clerics of Oghma among the many powerful mages that Khelben Arunsun brought together in the , in order to cleanse part of the High Moor and restore Faer'tel'miir, a lost city of Miyeritar.
