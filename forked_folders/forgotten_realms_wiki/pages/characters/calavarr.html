The Calavarr was a powerful wizard who once ruled the city of Tulmon in the Land of the Lions region of south Faerûn.

Personality

The Calavarr used his sorcerous might to cow those he ruled.

Activities

Tulmon thrived under the Calavarr's rule, establishing itself as a center of power along the Lake of Steam. He developed and altered a deepspawn called the Anglatha which created its own offspring in the form of small beholders which were also under the Calavarr's control.
