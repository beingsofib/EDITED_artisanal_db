Ravanys Woodsoul was an elf of the King's Forest in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Ravanys had long black hair, tanned skin, and pointy elfy ears.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, Ravanys, Arcton Greenwort and Celemin Ilver represented the interests of the druids and dryads of the King's Forest. Ravanys in particular represented the Grove of the Great Tree, a dryad grove under threat by the forces of Sschindylryn. She implored warriors to take up the righteous fight, promising great riches to those that dared aid the fey.
