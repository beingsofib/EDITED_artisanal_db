Narla Gulmaern was a spellscarred tiefling tavern owner in Gontal City.

Abilities

Narla's spellscar gave her the ability to disrupt arcane magic and rituals with her mere presence. She was also a highly skilled poison maker.

Activities

As of 1479 DR, Narla managed the Night Kiss tavern.
