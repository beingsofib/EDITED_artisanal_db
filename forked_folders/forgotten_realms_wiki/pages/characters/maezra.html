Maezra was the leader of the Thunder Peaks cell of the Cult of the Dragon in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Abilities

She was known to have raised zombies and to be able to cast wizard lock.

Possessions

She had a key to the treasury in the Chantry of St. Sammaster.

Activities

Maezra carried out the numerous responsibilities of a Wearer of Purple as the leader of her cell.

Relationships

Saerin Fierne was her second in command.

History

Maezra was the leader of the Cult of the Dragon cell that returned to the Vale of the Dragon Oracle in the to rebuild their power base. Her cell was joined by a number of necrophants in the , and they built the Chantry of St. Sammaster. She took one room for herself.

By , the Chantry was used as the place to collect and reassemble the bones of the dracolich Aghazstamn. On the same year, Maezra took it upon herself to try and entice the red dragon Nevalarich into joining the Cult. To this end, she gathered vast treasures before setting off, with half of her cell in tow. She left her second-in-command, Saerin Fierne, to watch over the Chantry, which was attacked by Zhentarim in her absence.
