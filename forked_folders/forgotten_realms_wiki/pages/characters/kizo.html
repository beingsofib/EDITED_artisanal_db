Kizo was a flatulent blue ox beast of burden that belonged to an eccentric merchant named Yani Pavani circa 1372 DR.

History

Kizo's entire career consisted of dragging the overeager merchant's wagon across the Sword Coast. The beast accompanied its owner to the hamlet of Liam's Hold in the , where Yani Pavani forced her interior decoration skills on the Hero of Daggerford–the new proud owner of the Stronghold keep. When the duo's work was over, Yani decided to stay in the quaint town for a while, and Kizo was happy to chew on the hamlet's abundant over-ripe greenery.
