Meerdaun Jhavalanko was a traveling acrobatic actor and a rogue who performed at the famous Crown Aflame theater in Athkatla in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Meerdaun had a distinctly moon-shaped face man.

History

Meerdaun Jhavalanko was among several traveling actors who were somewhat popular attractions among posh theatergoers from the Temple District. As of , Halvaruth was still performing at the Crown Aflame.
