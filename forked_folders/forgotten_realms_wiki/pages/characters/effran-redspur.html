Effran Redspur was a duergar merchant in Mantol-Derith.

Personality

Effran started her merchant career in order to travel throughout the Underdark, but Mantol-Derith was her first stop.

History

Around 1479 DR, Effran sold cured meat in Mantol-Derith.
