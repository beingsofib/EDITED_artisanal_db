Dark Stag was the local Herald stationed in Silverymoon, the Gem of the North, in the late 14 century DR. Doing her part to help maintain the city's laws, peace, and quiet, Dark Stag occasionally involved herself even in backstreet affairs.

Description

At some point, the Dark Stag of the late 14 century DR ventured into the Underdark, where she took sick with a strange disease. This devastated her tall body and, ultimately, was the catalyst for her retirement from adventuring. By the days of her tenure in Silverymoon, however, Dark Stag had begun to use a hat of disguise to modify her outward appearance—sometimes seeming an attractive man, but, most often, simply taking a more alluring, younger shape.

Abilities

Dark Stag was highly skilled in deductive reasoning and shrewd observation. So capable was she that the Heralds recognized her abilities, regarding her as one of the organization's best when it came to such matters.

Possessions

The Silvaeren Herald did not only rely on her hat of disguise.

Activities

During the days when fair Alustriel, the Lady Hope of Silverymoon, ruled that great city of the North, Dark Stag was considered one of the High Mage's closest confidantes. For this reason, Dark Stag—and other advisors of similar caliber such as Taern Hornblade—often met and conferred with Alustriel in the morning, soon after dawn.

Even when the mantle of rulership was passed to Taern Hornblade in Alustriel's stead, the working relationship between High Mage and Herald continued; he paid Dark Stag handsomely for any information she gathered and shared with him. To this end, she took to the streets of Silverymoon while using her hat of disguise. Adopting myriad parts amidst the night's revelries, Dark Stag learnt much about strangers and locals in equal measure.

Relationships

Beyond her membership among the Heralds of Faerûn, and even outside of her collaboration with the High Mages of Silverymoon, Dark Stag made many disreputable allies when she was an adventuress. Even after she retired, they continued to communicate, becoming a vast web of contacts.

History

Once in her life, Dark Stag was the target of a deadly ambush. Thanks to the assistance of an amiable wizard, however, she managed to escape; the mage briefly turned her into a sword, which allowed the pair to sneak out.
