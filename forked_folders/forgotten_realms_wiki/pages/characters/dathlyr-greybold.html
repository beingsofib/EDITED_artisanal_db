Dathlyr "the Hammer" Greybold was a dwarf fighter and adventurer based in Elturel in the Western Heartlands in the mid–14 century DR. Circa 1358 DR, he was one of the more important personalities of the city.

Activities

He explored the ruined of the Fallen Kingdom, and acquired many treasures from them.

Dathlyr penned a six-volume series of books about his travels and exploits, entitled My Adventures in the Realms.
