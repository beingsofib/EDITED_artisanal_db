Nul was a high priest of Malar who oversaw the Temple of Malar in the Thayan city of Bezantur in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Nul was deeply racist against elves, and called for their extermination in the name of Malar.

Activities

He was the leader of the temple's congregation and personally led the services. His ceremonial garb consisted of a cloak made of wolfskin topped with the head of a wolf.
