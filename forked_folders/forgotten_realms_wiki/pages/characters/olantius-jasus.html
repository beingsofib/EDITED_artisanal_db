Olantius Jasus was a human warrior, wizard and Captain of the Knights of Myth Drannor, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Olantius had to fight long and hard to be accepted in the order of the Knights of Myth Drannor until he finally was made captain.

Personality

He appreciated both his elven and human heritage and even human culture. This quite unique attitude made him an excellent diplomat for the knight order.

Possessions

Among his magical items he had a pair of bracers of defense and a wand of frost.
