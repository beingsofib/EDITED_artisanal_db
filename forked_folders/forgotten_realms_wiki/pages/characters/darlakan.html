Darlakan was a gofer in the kitchens of Castle Obarskyr in Suzail in the late 14 century DR.

History

During the , in preparation for the Council of the Dragon, the Master of Revels Khaladan Mallowfaer sent Emmur and Darlakan to fetch wagons and bring new chairs and stools from Zorsin's Dock. He later sent Halance Tarandar after them, to make sure they didn't break everything.
