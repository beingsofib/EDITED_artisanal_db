Allis Lhyssich was a master weaponsmith and the proprietor of Allis Lhyssich's Hand Crafted Weapons and Magical Weapons for Noble Causes circa 1358 DR.

Abilities

Lhyssich was famed for being able to work with the rare meteorite ore, and fashion enchanted weapons out of them. He was credited with creating the sword of stonecutting, a magical sword that was exceptional in combat against evil creatures, and those made of stone.
