Thag Slabhead was a ogre of the Sword Coast North in the late 15 century DR.

History

He served Vansi Bloodscar of the Many-Arrows orc kingdom in their occupation of the Tower District of Neverwinter. In the , Hulgen Proudfist asked a half-elf adventurer to kill Thag to prove their strength when considering whether the Proudfist tribe should switch sides.
