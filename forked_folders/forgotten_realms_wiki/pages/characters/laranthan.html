Laranthan was a brigand working for the Cult of the Dragon in the mid-14 century DR.

Relationships

He was Thoadrin's best warrior.

History

In late , Laranthan was part of Thoadrin's forces during their harrying of Orthil Voldovan's caravan in search of Shandril Shessair, attempting to capture her for her spellfire. His duties included scouting ahead,

After the ambush went wrong, he survived, and joined Thoadrin in their trip towards Waterdeep afterwards.
