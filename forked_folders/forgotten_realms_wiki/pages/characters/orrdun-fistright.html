Overblade Orrdun Fistright was the leader of the Stalwart Knights and the head of the Home of the Stalwarts in Velen during the late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was known for being a fearsome man who was not easily angered.

Relationships

Orrdun succeeded Orm Talath as the head of Helm's temple in Velen. Although Talath was beloved throughout the city, local residents felt secure with Orrdun's appointment.
