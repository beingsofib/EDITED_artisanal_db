Larkell Greenspring was a mercenary in Zhent-occupied Daggerdale in 1367 DR.

History

In 1367 DR, Larkell Greenspring and Trover escorted Constable Tren's envoy Marrakt to meet a just-arrived-in-town adventuring party.

Personality

Larkell was a seasoned veteran, not easily intimidated by others.
