Hasantasser Bloodshoulder was a jovial thief and innkeep inhabiting Tilverton in the .

Activities

Hasantasser ran Grimwald's Revenge–a nice but expensive inn that seemed to have a problem with frogs. He was also a member of the Rogues of Tilverton.

Hasantasser actually kept pet frogs. He released one within the inn every time the Rogues pulled off a successful heist. Some of his frogs had yellow markings, alerting fellow Rogues in distress that they could use the small packet of dust of disappearance secured to the frog's belly.
