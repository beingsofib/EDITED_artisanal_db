Anderis was a young servant inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Anderis worked in the House of Comfort, an inn located near the center of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter on the south bank of the River Tesh. He performed general chores for the inn's owner Maradad who occasionally slapped him when he got out of line.
