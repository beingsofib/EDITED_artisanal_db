Rezmir, or Rezmir the Black, was a half–black dragon human woman who was the Black Dragon Wyrmspeaker of the reformed Cult of the Dragon under Severin Silrajin. She descended from House Orogoth.

Personality

She was a fierce and terrible warrior, She was truly devoted to Tiamat.

History

She orchestrated the raid on the town of Greenest.

Later, Rezmir traveled north ahead of the Cult's loot to Castle Naerytar in the Mere of Dead Men, having developed a relationship with the great black dragon Voaraghamanthar. After she went to the Skyreach Castle to deposit the goods that she raided in the hoard of Tiamat but here, it was reported that she was killed by some adventurers recruited to stop the Cult.
