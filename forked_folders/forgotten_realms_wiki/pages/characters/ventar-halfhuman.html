Ventar Halfhuman was a skilled half-elven ranger and follower of Lurue during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Ventar was one of the original members of the Knights of the Unicorn. He led one of the affiliated groups of the Order of the Unicorn following the Time of Troubles.

History

He was originally from Baldur's Gate on the Sword Coast.
