Yvonne Chantel was a paladin of Tyr the Evenhanded from the city of Ravens Bluff, active in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Yvonne Chantel's appearance was described as lovely.

History

Little was recorded of Yvonne Chantel's life and pious deeds; however, the paladin's name graced the pages of the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter broadsheet of Ravens Bluff on at least one occasion. Jacinth "Jackie" Moonspring, the Society Editor, connected handsome warrior of Tyr, Yvonne Chantel, to a notorious womanizer knight named Don Juan Montery. The column named several other women with whom the "Chicken Knight" became entangled in a single month: the half-elven scout named Opal Starlight, Vedina Stareye, and the fellow Knight of the Golden Rooster named Ladyhawke.
