Marten was a toll collector who lived and worked on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.

Description

He had gray-white hair that he worn nealy brushed back.

History

Marten was killed in the , when goblin cultists of the Absolute raided the tollhouse at which he worked. Marten, Vance, and their fellows were abandoned by the tollhouse's guards. They attempted to flee and hide, but were ultimately cut down.
