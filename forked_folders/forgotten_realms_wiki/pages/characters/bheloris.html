Bheloris was an elder malaugrym in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Bheloris was once a member of the elder's council that advised the Shadowmaster High before Dhalgrave decided to dissolve it.

In the , during the hunt for Elminster, Bheloris approached the younger Neleyd and Huerbara. He proposed to hatch a plan together to increase the chance of their success for the hunt.
