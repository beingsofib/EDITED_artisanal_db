Keptus Hunzrin was the House Weapon Master of House Hunzrin, one of the ruling drow Houses of Menzoberranzan in the Northdark in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Xeva was a drow male with obsidian black skin and red color eyes – the only part of his face not hidden behind a mask. His hair was long and snow-white.

Relationships

Keptus Hunzrin was a cousin of Matron Mother Shakti Hunzrin and the 15 century DR master of Melee-Magthere, Syr'daun Hunzrin. Syr'daun's relationship with Keptus allowed the master of Melee-Magthere to lobby with Matron Mother for attacking lower and weaker Houses of Menzoberranzan.
