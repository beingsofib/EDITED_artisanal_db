Jurnau Halbeck was a dwarf auctioneer in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Jurnau had a twin-forked braided russet brown beard. He wore a long vest over a white tunic at work.

History

In the , Jurnau joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their forward camp outside the ruins of Myth Drannor, alongside their drow, human, and gnome allies. Once there, Jurnau set up a simple hut for the storage of goods and went about arranging deals between the various soldiers, pilgrims, and adventurers who passed through the camp.
