Canthan Doolittle was a human wizard and associated with the Citadel of Assassins.

Description

Canthan was a slim, studious-looking man, with beady brown eyes and a long straight nose. His gray-brown hair was thin, with a large bald spot atop his head. He often appeared nervous, rubbing his fingertips together while speaking.

Abilities

Canthan was an adept wizard. Amongst other spells he could cast the telepathy spell.

Relations

Canthan often surrounded himself with other members of the Citadel of Assassins, such as the dwarf warrior Athrogate or the human archmage Knellict, who was his superior.
