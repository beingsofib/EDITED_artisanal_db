Simon was a high priest of Stronmaus for the kingdom of Hartsvale.

Description

Simon was portly and bald and wore lots of gold jewelry. He carried a silver staff shaped like forked lightning.

Personality

Simon of Stronmaus came across as arrogant and domineering.
