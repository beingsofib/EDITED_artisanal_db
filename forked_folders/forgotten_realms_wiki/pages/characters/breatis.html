Breatis was a half-orc barbarian and adventurer in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Braetis was a very large half-orc woman with pale skin and black hair. She wore furs and hide armor and carried a greataxe.

History

One night in the mid-1480s DR, Breatis stayed the night at Beckside Castle, a ruin used as a camping spot. Her night was fairly ruined when the self-appointed campsite guardian Vicas Oakleaf physically blocked her from entering the men's dormitory, on the basis of protecting her virtues. She attempted to dodge around him and reach the second doorway, but the elven crusader was just too fast for her.
