Florentine Dempster was the mayor of Alaghôn and ruler of Turmish in the Vilhon Reach in the mid–2 century DR, reigning soon the nation's founding.

History

The wife of Dempster Turmish, Florentine succeeded him after his death in the . She quickly ended her husband's wars with Hlondeth and sued for peace. Thereafter, she focused on commerce.

However, she was assassinated in the . Thereafter, power changed hands between Turmish's various merchant houses, beginning the hundred-year-long Calmwaters period of Vilhon Reach history.
