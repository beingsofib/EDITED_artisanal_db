Ezra was an inhabitant of Luskan.

History

Ezra loved a person named Toka, but outlived them. Toka was buried in the crypts beneath Luskan sometime before 1487 DR, and had their epitaph engraved with the words "Here lies Toka Filenbar, Forever Ezra's".
