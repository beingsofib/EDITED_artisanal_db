Bethany Carrathal was High Queen of Callidyrr in the early-to-mid 12 century DR. Like her predecessors, although he held the title of High Queen of the Moonshae Isles, she was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

She was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty, and was the first daughter of her predecessor, King Edwin I. Her first son and eventual successor was Edwin II.
