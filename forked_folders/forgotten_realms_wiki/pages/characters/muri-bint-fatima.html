Mur'i bint Fatima was an inhabitant of Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Activities

Mur'i was responsible for collecting taxes from caravans and merchants passing through the Gate of Wealth.

Relationships

New to her post, Mur'i recognized Farim's corrupt ways. Since the mamluks under Farim's command remained loyal to him, there was not much that she could do about it.
