Ha'arlax the Hunter was a powerful draegloth that lived in the Underdark apart from drow society in the late 15 century DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, Ha'arlax roamed about seeking prey in the Underdark near Sschindylryn, having been exiled from the drow.
