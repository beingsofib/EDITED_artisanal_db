Erik was a man who lived in the library-fortress of Candlekeep during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He was a particularly skilled archer.

Relationships

Erik was friends with Jondalar, a mage who also lived in Candlekeep.

History

On the first day of Mirtul, in the , Erik joined Jondalar to help train Abdel Adrian in martial combat, at the request of the sage Gorion.
