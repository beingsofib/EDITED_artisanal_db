Lutha was an ogre around 1485 DR.

Possessions

The ogre wore hide armor and wielded a greatclub.

History

Lutha was beaten with injuries so deep that she became little smarter than a beast. When in that state, she was found by Sezibul, a young orc shaman. The orc decided to heal her and took Lutha as his pet. When he no longer had need her, Sezibul chained Lutha.
