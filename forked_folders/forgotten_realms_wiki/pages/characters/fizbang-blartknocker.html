Fizbang Blartknocker was a famous gnome, at least among the inhabitants of Purfbin Doogrick's home burrows, sometime before 1372 DR.

History

Fizbang Blartknocker participated in the historical event known as the Great War of the Burrow that took place in Purfbin Doogrick's family home, a gnomish community of little significance. Fizbang's actions during the conflict were famously motivated by his empty stomach.
