Roffas Calindor was a man of Tethyr and a member of the Knights of the Shield in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

He was married to Melek, who was the younger sister of Nurih al Suhal, a moneylender of Teshburl and member of the Rundeen in Calimshan by 1370 DR.
