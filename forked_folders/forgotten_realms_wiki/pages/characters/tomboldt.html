Tomboldt the Pallid was a member of the Cult of the Absolute who serviced "special" clients of Felogyr's Fireworks shop in Baldur's Gate in late 15 century DR.

Description

Tomboldt was an ashy-skinned Lolthite drow with snow-white hair and a stern facial features. He wore black and dark red robes.

History

In the , as the Cult of the Absolute planted its roots in Baldur's Gate and ravaged the Western Heartlands, Felogyr's Fireworks shop fell under the cult's sway. Its proprietor, Avery Sonshal, was made into a True Soul and used the shop as a front for the cult's terrorist activities. During that time, shop's second floor, which was only accessible to customers who knew a code phrase, and warned anyone from proceeding to the shop's third floor, which was off-limits to all but the Banite members of the Cult of the Absolute, which included Tomboldt the Pallid.
