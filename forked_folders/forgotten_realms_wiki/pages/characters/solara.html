Solara was a mercenary leader in Chondath in 1358 DR.

Personality

Solara looked to be a confident individual. She expected that all her men followed her orders without questions.

Possessions

Solara was known possess a set of elven chain mail, a +1 shield, a +2 spear, and magical arrows.

History

Solara led the Company of the Singing Dawn. In 1358 DR, she heard rumors of a legion following the tactics of the old Chessentan Empire in Chondath and Solara decided to investigate, together with the Llandrydd's Steel group and Enhanen's men.

She faced the undead army led by the mad cleric of Tempus Theodoric.
