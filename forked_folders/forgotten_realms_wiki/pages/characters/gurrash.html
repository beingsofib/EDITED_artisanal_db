Gurrash was the war chief of the Ear Seekers tribe sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Gurrash led his orcs against the elves of the Ardeep Forest, but they were defeated. An injured Gurrash guided his fellows in retreat to the Nightstone village.
