Seamus was a hermit who dwelled in the Lost Souls' Swamp, near the King's Forest of Cormyr.

Description

After living for decades in the swamp, Seamus was a somewhat ragged elder man.

Personality

Seamus was quite grumpy and preferred his solitude over strangers barging into "his" swamp. He was more than a little selfish, though this sense of self-preservation had kept him alive in dangerous conditions.

History

By the mid-1480s DR, Seamus had become a hermit and lived in Lost Souls' Swamp for over twenty years, learning everything there was to know about surviving there. During this time he became an associate of Oriphaun Huntsilver, helping him find lost relics in the past with his hermit-derived wisdom. This trust led Oriphaun to leave a piece of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll with Seamus, counting on his secretive nature and the desolate swamp itself to keep it safe. While Seamus did as requested and hid it near the Fallen Oak Shrine, the magical power of the Scroll attracted the fell medusa Sshaastess, who took it and started exerting control over the swamp and woods nearby.

When Eradin Krall sent a team to investigate, they found Seamus and got directions to the medusa, meeting him again at the fallen tree that served as the entrance. Seamus pointedly refused to get involved in any combat with magically-enhanced medusa warlords, and waited nearby until the Harpers emerged victorious. He then offered to "clean up" her cave full of treasure.
