Joluv was a merchant specializing in the sale of enchanted weapons, who lived during the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

Within his collection, Joluv had a amassed no less than ten weapons that were distinct to the region of Icewind Dale. Some of these were quite old, dating as far back as the 13 century.

Many of these weapons were the personal armaments of important individuals. Among them was the staff carried by Archdruid Arundel and the Spear of Kuldahar, destined to belong to that village's great defender.

History

As of the , Joluv could be found selling his wares at the Copper Coronet tavern, in the slums of Athkatla.
