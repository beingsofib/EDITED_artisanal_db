Carsa was a mage and adventurer during the 14 century DR.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR, Carsa and her fellow adventurers ventured into the ruins beneath the Firewine Bridge. Within the dungeon, they discovered a magic jar that held the spirit of the oni mage Kahrk. Kahrk drove Carsa's companions mad, until they killed each other in a bloody slaughter, leaving her alone with the jar.

She left the ruins with the jar intact, highly traumatized and with a mind that had been greatly fractured by the oni mage's evil influence.
