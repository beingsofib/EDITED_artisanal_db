Bogohardt Blackmane was a shaman of the Black Lion tribe in the mid to late–14 century DR.

Abilities

Through his shamanic powers Bogohardt was capable of unleashing a deafening roar similar to that of a lion.

History

In 1358 DR, Bogohardt was the only remaining shaman of the Black Lion tribe known to possess the Lion's Roar ability.

By 1370 DR he was the only remaining shaman of the Black Lion totem in the tribe.
