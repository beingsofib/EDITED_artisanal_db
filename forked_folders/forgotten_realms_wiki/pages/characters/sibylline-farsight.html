The Sibylline Farsight was a skilled diviner who lived hidden away within Candlekeep during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

The Sibylline Farsight was a specialty priest of Savras known as a sibylite. She was one of the eight Great Readers and administered a shrine of Savras called the Hall of Pools and Mirrors.
