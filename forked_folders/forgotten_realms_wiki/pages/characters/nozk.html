Sharp-eye Nozk was a goblin cultist of the Absolute who lived in the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

Description

He wore a suit of leather and cloth armor, a leather helm, and a necklace bearing the holy symbol of the Absolute.

Possessions

Nozk carried with him a crude bow.

History

In the , the cult of the Absolute launched a vicious and quite successful raid on the Waukeen's Rest inn. Nozk was left on guard duty at the Shattered Sanctum, while his replacement Mirg got drunk during the celebratory feast.
