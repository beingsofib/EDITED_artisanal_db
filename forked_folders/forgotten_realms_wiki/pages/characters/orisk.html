Orisk was a wizard inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Orisk worked as a judge within the Crypts in Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter on the south bank of the River Tesh. He presided over petty offenses, administering justice quickly which usually came in the form of fines.
