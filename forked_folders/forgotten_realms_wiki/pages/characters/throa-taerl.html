Throa Taerl was the Fourth High Captain of Luskan in the 15 century DR.

Activities

Throa had control over many sailors and workers, and occasionally organized raids when passed on from Ship Suljack.

History

By 1489 DR, Throa was the High Captain of Ship Taerl.
