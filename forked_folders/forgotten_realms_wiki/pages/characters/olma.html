Olma was a streetwalker and criminal in Calaunt.

Description

Olma was blonde, wore thick, visible makeup, with flashy spangles on gauze clothes. She had a sweet perfume; overall, she was rather fleshy.

Personality

Olma was relatively cool-headed even with a weapon to her throat.

Possessions

Besides her makeup, Olma had used some dust to attack Taegan which caused sneezing; it may have been dust of sneezing.

History

Olma lured Taegan Nightwind to an ambush on behalf of Falar Aporos. Later, as Taegan realized that she had done it for Falar, he sought her again, and extracted the plan from her; a day later, he interrogated her as to Falar's location, and used her as a diversion once he reached the criminal.
