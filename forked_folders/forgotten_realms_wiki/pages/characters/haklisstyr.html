Haklisstyr was a wizard from Selgaunt in the early-to-mid 14 century DR.

History

In the , Ilthond carried a scroll of a spell akin to teleport, which he had purloined from Haklisstyr after knifing him with a poisoned dagger in the back, strongly implying he had been his apprentice. Though Ilthond was young, the date of the event was unknown.
