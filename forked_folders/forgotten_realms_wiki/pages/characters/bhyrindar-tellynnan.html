Bhyrindar "Silverhart" Tellynnan was a moon elf werewolf and, as of , was a member of the Vel'Nikeryma and leader of a cell of the Eldreth Veluuthra operating in the north of the High Forest.

The Eldreth Veluuthra failed to ally with Turlang and Lossarwyn, and this was attributed to Bhyrindar's impatience.
