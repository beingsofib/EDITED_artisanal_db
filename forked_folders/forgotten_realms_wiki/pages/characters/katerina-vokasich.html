Katerina Vokasich was a woodcarver's wife in the city of Liara in Nova Vaasa of the Domains of Dread.

Relationships

She was married to Yassilli Vokasich and mother to Jebbrie Vokasich.

History

At a time corresponding to the mid-to-late 14 century DR, Katerina Vokasich's son, Jebbrie, was bitten by spiders carrying the curse of a werespider. Although he did not develop lycanthropy, he suffered a severe allergic reaction that left him feeble and bedridden. After adventurers were hired to investigate a strange series of murders, Katerina might've approached them to inquire regarding why the temple of Bane's spells were not helping her son, if they seemed learned.
