Cleuvus was a swordsman of the Zhentarim in the mid-14 century DR.

Personality

At the time of his death, he was lost in thought, contemplating how swordsmasters were only loved by coinlasses.

Relationships

He was part of a squad that included Othrogh, a half-orc, amongst others.

History

In , Cleuvus was part of a group that tried to ambush Shandril Shessair in the Hullack Forest. He was ambushed and slain by Rathan Thentraver, who then fought the rest of the squad.
