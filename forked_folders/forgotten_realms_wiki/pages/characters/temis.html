Temis was the precept of Mishtan in southeast Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Temis oversaw both Mishtan and the nearby Land of the Dead. The tombs of past pharoahs found within the Land of the Dead were his responsibility. Temis was a priest of Osiris.
