Bagdog was a Fist of Bane who served the Cult of the Absolute in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Bagdog was a dark muddy-green-skinned half-orcish man with long brown hair. He wore a set of cultist chain mails adorned with Bane's black hand and was armed with a simple shield.

History

In the , the Cult of Bane joined forces with the Cult of the Absolute, led by the Chosen of Bane, Lord Enver Gortash. Fist of Bane Bagdog was placed in the Steel Watch Foundry, guarding its Gondian slaves in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate.
