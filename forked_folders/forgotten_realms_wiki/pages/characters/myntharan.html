Myntharan, called the Magus, was a Shoon wizard-king of Westgate.

Activities

Myntharan was a skilled wizard who hailed from the Shoon Imperium. He challenged and killed King Mulsantir II in the , and seized the throne of Westgate for himself. He would rule until the , when he was slain by the androsphinx Lyonarth.
