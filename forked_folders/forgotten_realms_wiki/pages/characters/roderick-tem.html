Roderick Tem was a male human leader of the Fire Knives during the Time of Troubles.

History

Roderick was the leader of the Fire Knives during the Time of Troubles in 1358. Roderick and his men had just occupied Dembling Castle when the god Bane called to him the souls of all assassins and Roderick and his men all died instantly.
