Aurora was an apprentice to Imperial Dyemaster Sigurd in the late 15 century DR.

History

By the , Drake General dye was created exclusively by Sigurd and Aurora. This red and gold dye was considered to be extremely rare and valuable and encountered in the Western Heartlands, West Faerûn, and the city of Baldur's Gate.
