Saj Amog was a lich and the Zulkir of Conjuration in Thay in the late 15th century DR.

Relationships

As a zulkir, Amog was a member of the ruling council of Thay. He was also an ally of Valindra Shadowmantle.

History

After many defeats in Neverwinter and busy with crushing the rebels of the Thayan Resurrection, Zulkir Saj Amog was sent from Thay to help Valindra Shadowmantle in her schemes. Amog led a Thayan army to recapture the Dread Ring but was defeated by an adventurer.
