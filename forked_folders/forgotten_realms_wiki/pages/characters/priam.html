Priam was an officer in the Red Plumes mercenary army of Hillsfar in the early-to-mid 14 century DR.

Description

By 1357 DR, Priam was a middle-aged man of forty-one winters. Sporting a tanned complexion and a trimmed, curly black beard, his armaments were standard for an officer.

Relationships

The only known family member of Priam was his uncle, the High Priest of Tyr in Melvaunt's temple.

History

In the , during the abduction of the Moonsea cities by Bane, Priam gathered the remaining survivors of Melvaunt into a makeshift army. With the fortunate aid of the Heroes of Phlan, they liberated Melvaunt's temple of Tyr. It was not known if he survived the attempt on his life.
