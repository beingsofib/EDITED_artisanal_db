Byron Cymrych I was High King of the Moonshae Isles at the turn of the 9 century DR.

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the son of his predecessor, High King Ardan. His successor was his first son, Kevan Cymrych.

History

Byron I assumed the throne of the High King in the , after the death of his father.

In the twenty-second year of his reign, Byron I fell into a magical sleep that lasted into the following year. During this period, his son and heir, Kevan, ruled in his stead.

Byron I ruled for thirty years before he died of natural causes in the .
