Frell Olsin was a member of the Gondian clergy and a foundry worker from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Frell Olsin was a human man with a light skin tone, hazel eyes, and a shaved head. He wore a foundry worker's uniform and a smith's apron, often covered in soot, dirt, and sweat.

History

In the , the Gondian foundry of Baldur's Gate was usurped by Enver Gortash and his cultists of Bane. Frell Olsin and other Gondians were forced to work on forging the Lord's steel watchers. Their families and loved ones were abducted and held hostage to encourage obedience, and all of the workers were outfitted with Incineration Collars controlled by Prinski's Motivators.

With the Absolute crisis unfolding in Baldur's Gate, the fate of the Gondians remained unconfirmed. It was possible for all of the hostages and foundry workers to perish, or be saved along with their loved ones by the group of heroes known as the Absolute Adversaries.
