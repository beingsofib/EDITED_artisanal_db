Keinen was a paladin in the service of Waterdeep's temple of Torm.

Description

Keinen was an elderly man with long locks of white hair, sporting a significant beard that nearly reached his waist. Underneath his armor he wore a lengthy, green gambeson.

Personality

Although his goal was to become the leader of Torm's Waterdhavian priesthood, Keinen knew deep inside that a quiet clerical life was not for him. His true passion was the art of combat, and he therefore chose to compromise by becoming a paladin.

Possessions

Keinen wore a suit of banded mail. In combat he rode a brown warhorse with chain barding, and wielded a lance and shield.
