Crandall was a member of the Targos Guard, active in the early 14 century DR.

Description

Crandall was a red-headed human man who was clad in Targos Guard's leather armor and was armed with a spear.

History

In the , the town of Targos in Icewind Dale became besieged by the Legion of the Chimera. One of the Legion's surprise attacks herded a troop of goblins by magical means through underground tunnels into the Targos docks, where Crandall was guarding the Dualdon's Wake – one of the more important naval vessels, and a way to escape death were the town to fall. Crandall made an insightful assertion that someone within Targos's walls was conspiring with the Legion.
