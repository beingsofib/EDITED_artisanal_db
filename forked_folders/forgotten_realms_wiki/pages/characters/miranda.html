Miranda was a merchant in Luskan in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Around 1487 DR, Miranda was elderly, with grey hair and sunken skin.

Relationships

She was friendly with the Dead Rats gang and owed them a few favors. Her usual contact in the gang was Titus Minton.

She was also on good terms with Aleema Meddlebrook, an old herbalist friend of hers.

History

Miranda sold a number of items to members of the Order of the Burning Dawn in Luskan.
