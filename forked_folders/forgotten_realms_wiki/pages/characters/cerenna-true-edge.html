Cerenna True-edge was a guildmaster inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the late–14 century DR.

Description

She was 60 years old in the .

Activities

She was the guildmaster of the Way of the Warrior, a fighter's guild located in the Foreign Quarter near the River Trail Gate.

Relationships

She clashed with Ulgrym, the captain of the city guard, because she denied his request that her warriors train with the guard.
