Hnalguk was a gnoll who led her fellow gnoll pack as shock troops in Mrolgyet's Legion in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Hnalguk always led her pack on the frontline owing to a need to run directly into a fight. She embodied the reckless nature and tactics of her forces.

History

Hnalguk led her advance forces in the battle between Mrolgyet's Legion and the Lords' Alliance on Harvestshield Mountain. They were briefly slowed by packs of local ghouls before they began to scour the mountainside for Alliance troops. She was defeated in combat by a group of adventurers from Waterdeep.
