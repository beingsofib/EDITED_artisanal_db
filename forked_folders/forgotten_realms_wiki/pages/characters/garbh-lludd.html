Garbh Lludd was the leader of a large tribe of firbolgs active in The Highlands of the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles as of the late 15 century DR.

Personality

She was a celebrated hunter, boasting considerable skill as a tracker Her favored prey included basilisks, battlebriars, and dire bulettes.

Abilities

Garbh Lludd was a Mistress of the Wild Hunt, and accordingly wielded mighty primal magic that made her a frightful leader among fey of all kinds. She was able to fly (albeit clumsily) and could wield the power of the moon to weaken and inflict grievous wounds against her prey. She could command Wild Hunt hounds.

Activities

She was the mightiest warrior of her tribe. Under her leadership, they were nomadic and hunted along the westward slopes of The Highlands down toward the Shrouded Lands.

Possessions

She fought with a spear that she was able to summon back to herself if thrown. She carried a light shield and wore a stag-antlered helmet. In battle, she rode in a signature chariot pulled by a great elk.
