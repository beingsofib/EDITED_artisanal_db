Baalbisan was a balor who was trapped within Ammon Jerro's haven in the Sword Mountains in the late 14 century DR for the purpose of empowering the warlock. He was in a constant feud with several of the other imprisoned demons and devils, but in the he broke free when he convinced Shandra Jerro to spill her blood to undo the magic that bound the demons to the haven. Baalbisan's matron was named Zaxis.
