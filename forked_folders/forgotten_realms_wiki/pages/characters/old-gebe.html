Old Gebe was a colorful personality of uncertain age from Dlusk, Aglarond, active in the late 14 century DR.

History

Circa the , during the spring festivals in Dlusk, Old Gebe give free hayrides to children and told stories and sang songs about mules, including his own, Sara and Corey. He knew much of local gossip, legends, and was knowledgeable on the subject of the Unapproachable East in general.
