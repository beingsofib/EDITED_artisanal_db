Kalil yi Tulmon was a pirate in the service of the Rundeen in the late 14 century DR.

Personality

Kalil was amiable and outgoing, yet utterly ruthless as a pirate.

Activities

Kalil usually acted as a first mate on Rundeen-affiliated pirate ships; circa 1370 DR, he served aboard one preying on ships out of Almraiven, Suldophor, and Theymarsh. He was loyal first and foremost to the Rundeen, sometimes even over the ship's captain. Sometimes he was assigned to other pirate crews in order to 'teach a lesson' to a particular merchant in the area. Kalil would deceive sailors by convincing them to surrender with promises of being spared, and afterward cruelly cutting them to pieces before the others.

Possessions

He owned a ring of invisibility.
