Lord Harkuldragon was a nobleman of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Perhaps due to bigotry, he believed himself far more capable in hand-to-hand combat than he actually was.

Abilities

He was easily bested in hand-to-hand combat.

Activities

Lord Harkuldragon had been planning some treason in the days leading up to his death.

Possessions

He had a dagger.

Relationships

While listening to King Foril Obarskyr I's speech during the Council of the Dragon, Lord Harkuldragon wore an expression of open contempt, suggesting he was not content with the state of nobility.

Lord Harkuldragon had long hired Sarrak of Westgate as his mage.

History

Lord Harkuldragon was one of the guests to the Council of the Dragon in the .

Later, while discussing with his mage about how to kill Khaladan Mallowfaer for having learned something about his plans for treason, Lord Harkuldragon was interrupted by Cymmarra. As he did not follow her commands, Cymmarra slew him, first attempting magic, which didn't work, and then breaking his neck, which did. This event was observed by Imglor Ironhand, a hired killer and spy, and through him, by Manshoon.

Rumors & Legends

Days prior to his death, Harkuldragon mentioned, at the Three Ravens, that he had "something else" if the usual magics failed. This led the vampire archmage Manshoon to wonder if perhaps he wielded a blueflame item, leading to his eavesdropping.
