Glaicus was a bodyguard who served at Arnise Hold during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

Glaicus was not only under the employ of Lord de'Arnise, but was also those counted among his friends.

History

In the , the Arnise Hold was taken over by a group of yuan-ti and trolls led by TorGal. During the occupation Glaicus was charmed and forced to fight for the malevolent troll.
