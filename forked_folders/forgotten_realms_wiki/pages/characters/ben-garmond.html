Ben Garmond was a trader from Procampur in the Vast in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was huge, and dressed in fine clothes.

Activities

His route took him once a year to meet with Stag Falls traders in Eaglton, where he got quartz and furs he sold at Ravens Bluff and Tantras for a nifty profit.

Relationships

He and his brother Garrold worked together.

History

After Romanisk Vlaensk, a vampire wizard, was released at Stag Falls and took control of the village one year around the , the traders of Stag Falls could no longer go to Eaglton. When Ben and his brother Garrold reached Eaglton, they found none to do business with, and rather than go to Stag Falls, they preferred to wait there. Their patience ran out after two weeks, on the day when investigators from Ravens Bluff, who'd been hired by Marcus Rubicon, arrived whilst headed for Stag Falls. If questioned, the brothers may have said they believed there had been no traders coming because of the muddy ground.
