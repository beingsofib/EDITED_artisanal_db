King Osbrun was the king of the city of Chessagol (later called Tsurlagol) in the Vast in the early 1350s DR.

History

King Osbrun once bought the shield called Thurbrand's protector from the warrior Thurbrand.

In the Year of the Crown, 1351 DR, the king's treasury and armory were robbed—by magic, some said. This included Thurbrand's protector.
