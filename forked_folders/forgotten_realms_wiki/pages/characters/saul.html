Saul () was a halfling in Mulmaster in 1491 DR.

Personality

The halfling was an inquisitive, talkative, and knowledgeable individual. He liked telling strangers all about Mulmaster.

Relationships

Saul was a servant at Astrid Saj's house and hoped to enter into the city's Blade elite.

History

In 1491 DR, he was one of the main servants at Astrid Saj's great party in her villa.
