Malivur was a mage of the Cult of the Dragon in the mid-14 century DR.

Description

Malivur had a black, slick moustache.

Personality

Malivur was impatient and had a terrible temper, but was awed easily by magical power.

Abilities

Malivur was a mage of some power.

Activities

During his attempt to capture Shandril, Malivur disguised himself as a spice seller.

Relationships

Malivur and Krostal were working together to capture Shandril Shessair in late , though they didn't get along too well. Both of them were known to the Zhentarim scriers Hlael Toraunt and Korthauvar Hammantle.

History

Malivur and Krostal were assigned to attempting the capture of Shandril Shessair while she was in a caravan from Scornubel to Waterdeep in late 1357 DR. as well as Red Wizards scrying under the command of Hulrivior in Triel.

Rumors & Legends

Krostal told Malivur he recognized another, especially powerful, fellow mage of the Cult of the Dragon in the caravan. The people scrying on them weren't able to identify that mage; it is possible he was lying.
