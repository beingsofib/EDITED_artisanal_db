Sacrast was the ninth Obarskyr monarch and a king of Cormyr. He was an illegitimate child officially adopted by King Imbre a year before the latter's death. The first sixteen years of his reign was ruled by a Regency of the Four Barons until he came of age in Year of the Quirt, 110 DR. He ruled for 13 years until his death in Year of the Icy Axe, 123 DR.
