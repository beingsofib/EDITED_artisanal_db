Sarlor was a caravan guard in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Sarlor was oddly defiant.

Relationships

He was a guard for Orthil Voldovan's caravan and often worked on tasks together with Tarth and Mulgar.

History

Sarlor was guarding Orthil's caravan in the , when it was joined by Shandril Shessair and Narm Tamaraith. He was assigned briefly to keep watch on Shandril; when she demanded to see Narm after he was injured during a bandit raid, he protested that they didn't follow her orders. The situation would've likely ended in a violent confrontation if it weren't for Arauntar's timely intervention.
