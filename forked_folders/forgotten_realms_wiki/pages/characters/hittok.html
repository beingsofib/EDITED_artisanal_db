Hittok was a Maztican drider. He was one of the Ancient Ones.

History

In 1361 DR, after the Night of Wailing, Hittok was one of the many drow who survived and were transformed into driders by Lolth. He was one of the lieutenants of Darien.

Later he reported to Darien the death of one of their fellow at the hands of the Itza
