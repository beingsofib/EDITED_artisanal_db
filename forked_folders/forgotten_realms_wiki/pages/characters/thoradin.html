Thoradin was a dwarven inhabitant of Luskan in the late 15 century.

Description

Thoradin was a dwarf, with tan skin, a ginger beard and short-shorn ginger hair.

History

Thoradin once befriended Larethar Gulgrin, and eventually saved his life.

Thoradin's wife Hlin died some time prior to 1487 DR, and he had to pawn off her locket amulet soon after, as he couldn't stand to look at it.

In the late 1480s DR, he was working as a drug mule, smuggling Traveler's Dust, a highly illegal substance, for Ship Suljack in Luskan.

Around 1487 DR, he was robbed by bandits while smuggling the drug through the Luskan slums. Having lost the shipment, and failing to recover it in time, he reached out to Larethar after many years apart, asking to meet him in the Cutlass inn in Luskan. There, he asked him for help in recovering the shipment. He asked Larethar and his companions, among them the Champion of the Burning Dawn, to meet him in the slums.
