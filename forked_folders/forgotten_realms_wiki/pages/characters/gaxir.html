G'axir the Seer was a fortune teller and diviner who lived in Baldur's Gate during the mid–14 century DR.

Abilities

While G'axir claimed to see into the lives of others and see their future, his declarations were very vague and unclear.

Possessions

He wore a suit of chain mail armor and regularly carried a quarterstaff.

Activities

G'axir was a regular patron of the Blade and Stars inn found in the Eastway district of Baldur's Gate.

History

In the , G'axir sought out a specific sphene gem found in the "lair of the basilisk", which was in fact a warehouse in the city's docks.
