Rosa Steelsar was an unfortunate child of the exiled Amnian Parhek dynasty.

Relationships

Rosa was the child of Princess Rasa Parhek Dornar Steelsar, and was the sibling of Choris, Rohav, and Rhothar.

History

Rosa was born in Silverymoon, and was only 9 years old in the when the family was attacked by Amnian assassins. While Dornar and Choris fought to the death to give the family the chance to flee, only Rasa and Rhothar escaped while Rosa was killed and Rohav was kidnapped.
