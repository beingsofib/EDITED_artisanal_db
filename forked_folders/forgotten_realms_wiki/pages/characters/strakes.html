Strakes was a domestic servant in the house of Duke Alaric Hembreon in the city of Zazesspur.

Description

Strakes combed his thin black hair over the balding spot of his head. He had long features and a button nose.

Strakes wore a uniform in the duke's official colors, white and blue.

Personality

Strakes commonly bore a sad look on his face. He had a way of speaking in which his lips barely moved.

History

In the , when adventuress Zaranda Star had an audience with Duke Hembreon to discuss her confiscated goods, they were interrupted by Strakes, who announced that the duke's daughter, Tatrina had been stealing money from her father's purse to donate to the cause of the All Friends, who followed Ao.
