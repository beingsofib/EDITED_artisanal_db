Imprudence was an a fighter and a thug from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Imprudence was a woman with a light skin tone and light brown eyes. She wore a simple helmet and a set of ring mail armor. In battle, she wielded a warhammer.

History

In the , Imprudence was a member of a gang assembled by Churg Elvek, secretly a cultist of the Absolute, who sought to strengthen its grasp of Baldur's Gate. When a famous writer Volothamp Geddarm returned to the Gate after misadventures in the Emerald Grove and coming face-to-face with the Absolute's army, Volo became a target for Churg and his gang. The master traveler was captured by Imprudence's gang and tied to a stack of smokepowder barrels, ready to be "brought to justice" for being an alleged agent of the Absolute at the hands of the gang and street gawkers. When a band known as the Absolute Adversaries interrupted the street execution, Imprudence, Churg Elvek, and the rest of the gang attacked the adventurers but were defeated.
