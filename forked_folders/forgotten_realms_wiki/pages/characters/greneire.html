Greneire was the king of an orc tribe located in the Spine of the World mountain range north of Dark Arrow Keep in the .

History

Some days before Midsummer in 1367 DR, King Greneire led an army of 150,000 orcs southward and besieged the kingdom of Many-Arrows at its capital, Citadel of Many Arrows. The siege lasted four months and ended in Uktar of that year when Greneire's forces finally broke through the gates of the citadel, but the battle and the war were lost when King Obould slew Greneire in single combat. when dwarves of Clan Warcrown arrived to rout the survivors.
