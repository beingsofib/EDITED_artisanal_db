Fanegonorom was a revenant that dwelled within ancestral crypts of the Korlasz family as of the mid–14 century DR.

Powers

As a revenant, Fanegonorom could control lesser undead creatures and his touch could inflict disease upon others.

History

During his life as a mortal, he possessed the shield of Egons and cherished it dearly.

When the individual known as Korlasz delved into her family's crypts, her followers and mercenaries desecrated the sanctity of Fanegonorom's resting place. He sought Korlasz's death so that he could rest in peace.
