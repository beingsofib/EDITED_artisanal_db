Bharash was a dragonborn general who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 century DR.

Activities

Bharash was the leader of a company of dragonborn warriors that opposed the Cult of the Dragon and chromatic dragons in general.

Relationships

He was an ally of Sabine, the general in charge of Dagult Neverember's army in the city.

History

When the company's priests divined that a cell of the Cult of the Dragon was working on Neverwinter to free Tiamat from the Nine Hells, Bharash and his warriors went to the city to offer their services to Lord Neverember, in order to foil the plans of the cultists. Although he was unsure at first that his offer would be accepted, Neverember gladly accepted his aid.

Bharash remained in Neverwinter afterwards, considering that helping the Neverwintans to protect their city was a worthy mission.
