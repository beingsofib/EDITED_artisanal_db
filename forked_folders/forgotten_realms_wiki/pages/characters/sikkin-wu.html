Sikkin Wu was an ancient spirit dragon active in Kara-Tur around . He was seen as a symbol of good fortune by humans who encountered him.

Description

Unlike most other shen lung he was able to cast the spells of bless, curse, ice storm, and control weather and used these frequently both in and out of combat.
