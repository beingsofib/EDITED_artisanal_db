Stompin' Borzz the Ratcatcher was a pest exterminator from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

Borzz advertised his exterminator services to the folk of the Lower City via notice board postings. One note was placed on the board across from the Basilisk Gate in the . The advertisement claimed Borzz was highly proficient in stompin' rats of all sizes, including giant rats, but offered rat droppings and corpse disposal for an additional fee. Borzz the Ratcatcher offered referrals upon request.
