Goulart was an adventurer and a member of the Company of the Lynx in Cormyr during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He was a heavy-built man of few words.

History

In the , Goulart and his fellow adventurers recovered a collection of scrolls and documents from the remains of a dragon that had died in battle on the edge of the Wyvernwater. They soon discovered that inscribed upon the scrolls were the true names of quite a number of the Red Wizards of Thay, having been collected by agents of King Azoun IV.
