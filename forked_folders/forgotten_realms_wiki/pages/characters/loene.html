Loene () was a former member of the Company of Crazed Venturers.

Description

Loene had dark blond hair and hazel eyes. She was beautiful and a skilled fighter. She had henna-tipped fingers.

History

Loene was a slave when she was rescued by Nain Keenwhistler, a member of the Company of Crazed Venturers. After she was freed, she adventured with them for a time.

In 1361 DR, Loene was living in a townhouse in the Castle Ward near Castle Waterdeep. She dwelt there with her servant, Elliot Graves, who was killed that year by the Harper Assassin. The house was also guarded by two mastiffs.

Possessions

Loene had a ring of spell storing, which had been given to her by Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun.

Relationships

  • Arilyn Moonblade and Loene were well acquainted.
  • Mirt the Moneylender and Loene were friends and lovers for a time.
