Jinkerton was a logistics clerk who excelled at organizing caravan shipments. He was active in the Moonshae Isles during the late 15 century DR.

Activies

He worshiped Malar and secretly fed information to the Black Blood tribe.

History

After having worked on the island of Alaron, Jinkerton travelled to Langmuir in the south of Moray in the , where young workers were drawn to the lucrative mining industry despite the regions many dangers. He got a job managing caravan logistics for a shipping consortium headed by Maldwyn, and rented an apartment near the village's docks. Over the following months, he excelled at his job at the same time that several other clerks mysteriously went missing. During this time, Maldwyn came to trust him to manage secret shipments of alchemical explosives to Langmuir's mines, making him one of only a couple of people who knew when a caravan was carrying these dangerous substances.

At some point, Jinkerton began feeding information to Bloodeye, a leader among the Black Blood tribe, allowing the tribe to amass an arsenal of mining explosives which they planned to use to topple Langmuir.

After six months in Langmuir, Jankerton hastily and quietly fled the village when Bloodeye began to enact his plan to destroy the village by capturing the nearby lighthouse.
