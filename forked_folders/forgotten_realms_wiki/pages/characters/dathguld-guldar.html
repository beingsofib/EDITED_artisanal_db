Dathguld Guldar was a noble lord of Westgate in the early and mid-14 century DR.

Relationships

He was the head of the Guldar trading family.

He was strongly believed to be the father of Torm, one of the Knights of Myth Drannor, bore to him by a local prostitute, Netha.

He had another illegitimate son, Oswald Guldar. They had no loyalty to each other.
