Kinondra was the witch doctor leader of a fire giant group that resided in the Deepwing Mountains of the Vilhon Reach in the late-14 century DR.

Description

Kinondra was an fire giant man.

Abilities

As a witch doctor, the fire giant had access to both arcane and divine magics.

Possessions

Kinondra protected himself with a ring and a cloak of protection.

History

Circa the , the city of Lachom became menaced by a gang of fire giants of the Deepwing Mountains. Kinondra's band raided several farms around the city but refrained from attacking Lachom itself. In response, the city's Mayor Turien Battlewake put out a call for capable adventurers to resolve the giant threat. Unbeknownst to most, Kinondra was hired by a short-term Lord Simon Dessino of Arrabar who was attempting to show Lachom that the city needed Chondath's protection.
