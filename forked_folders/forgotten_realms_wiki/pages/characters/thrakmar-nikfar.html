Thrakmar Nikfar was a half-orc rogue, living sometime in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was sure that he was better than his relatives, who were all orcs, as he felt he had both superior abilities and look. However, traveling amongst humans had given him an inferiority complex, and he desperately tried to pass himself off as fully human.

Possessions

He owned a dagger, short sword, sling, and a set of thieves' tools.
