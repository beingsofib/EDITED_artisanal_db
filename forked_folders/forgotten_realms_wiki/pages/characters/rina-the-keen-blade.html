Rina the Keen-Blade was a Harper agent and weapons expert who worked in Eveningstar in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Rina had dark skin and long brown hair worn in a ponytail. She wore a suit of fine chain mail.

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar during the 1480s DR, Rina joined Kara Patwin and Lorant Tass in following up on Oriphaun Huntsilver's search of the Storm Horns, where he found Shadovar activity. When the orcs working for Amskar launched a preemptive and failed raid upon Eveningstar, Rina and the hunter Crowler Johns examined the odd armaments the orcs had carried. She quickly came to the conclusion that they were being supplied by Risen Netheril, and put that together with reports from the scout Kyra Armond, who spotted suspicious movement at Tumblerock Pass. Rina dispatched a team to join Kyra there and assault the Netherese caravans, defeating General Grablandurr and ruining their efforts to bring weapons into Cormyr.
