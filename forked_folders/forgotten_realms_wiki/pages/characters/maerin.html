Maerin was an Illuskan arcanist around the time of the Fall of Netheril.

History

In the Year of Seven Spirits, &minus;335 DR, Maerin contacted Immar Fardelver and other dwarven artisans from Delzoun to commission the building of Gauntlgrym. The work was completed in the Year of Hollow Hills, &minus;321 DR.

Maerin was the wizard who rediscovered Maegera after Maegera was lost in a large chamber filled with molten rock. She was put there in by a group of dwarves of the great Northkingdom of Delzoun.
