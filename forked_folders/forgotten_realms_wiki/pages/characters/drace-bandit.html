Drace was a bandit preying on travelers through Skuldaskar in the mid-3 century DR.

History

In , a number of bandits attacked Elminster Aumar while he rode through Skuldaskar. When the bandit captain was overmatched by Elminster's magic, he called on Drace and Algan to shoot arrows at him, which they did, killing his horse.
