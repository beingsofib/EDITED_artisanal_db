Venomfang was a green dragon who made its lair in an old wizard's tower in the ruins of Thundertree village.

Description

Venomfang was a typical young green dragon. Not wanting to give up its amassed wealth, the dragon was protective of its base. However, Venomfang favored survival over death, and planned on flying away if badly injured and at risk of losing a fight. Venomfang spent a lot of time appreciating the contents of its plentiful hoard, containing coins and even an enchanted axe known as Hew.

History

Circa 1491 DR, the green dragon flew over Neverwinter Wood to search for a new lair. When passing over Thundertree, it spotted an abandoned tower, tore through the roof, and killed the giant spiders infesting the tower. Venomfang decided to lie low for a time in this new lair. Soon after, the Cult of the Dragon planned to convene with the dragon.
