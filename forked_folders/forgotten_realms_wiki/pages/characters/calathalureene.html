Calathalureene was a human sorceress who became the namesake of the Calathalureenteir in the Forest of Tethir.

History

Many centuries before the 14 century DR, Calathalureene dwelt somewhere along the banks of the stream that would someday bear her name. Despite being a human, Calathalureene was an ally of the Weldath's elves and lended them her spells against orcish marauders.
