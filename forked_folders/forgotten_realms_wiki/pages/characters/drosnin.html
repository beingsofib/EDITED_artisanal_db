Drosnin was a member of the Cult of the Crushing Wave during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Activities

Drosnin was the priest responsible for Yan-C-Bin's chapel inside Rivergard Keep.

History

In 1491 DR, she managed the chapel at Rivergard Keep. However, Drosnin was confronted and defeated by some adventurers.
