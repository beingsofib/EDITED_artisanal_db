Barroch was said to be the first great bandit lord of the Inner Sea lands. He made his lair at Barroch's Hold, a fabled citadel in the Glacier of the White Worm.

In the Year of the Worm, 1356 DR, a band of adventurers rediscovered Barroch's Hold in the Glacier of the White Worm. However, they soon ran into a number of strange creatures, and were separated or killed.
