Zenyff Bravehand was a human Cormyrean Wizard of War in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Zenyff had a bald head and a full brown beard.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, Zenyff reinforced the Cormyreans at the ruined keep in the nearby King's Forest. Replacing the fellow wizard Arganon Pine in protecting the site, Zenyff was forced to improvise a new spell ward ritual when mysterious and hostile druids attacked with the forces of nature. With the aid of the Purple Dragons and adventurers sent by Yenward Chorster, they held the position despite fierce assaults, including from the wood woad Gordy. At the culmination of the ritual, the area was successfully protected against the primal forces that sought to seize the ruins and the magic within.
