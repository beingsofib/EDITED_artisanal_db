Asshurat was a skilled wizard inhabiting the city of Messemprar in Unther during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Asshurat was a member of the Northern Wizards and a strong advocate for a peaceful solution for the problems in Unther during this time. She was also aware that her brethren needed to be on the lookout for treachery.
