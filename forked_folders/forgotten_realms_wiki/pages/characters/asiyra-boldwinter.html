Asiyra Boldwinter was an innkeep inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

She carried an air of superiority around her.

Activities

Asiyra owned the Blackstar Inn, an expensive establishment located on Gut Alley and Blackstar Lane in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.
