Somi was one of the two bodyguards who worked for the proprietor of Danthelon's Dancing Axe in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Somi was a gruff and tough half-orc with leathery tan skin and long mop of brown hair. He had brown eyes, yellow tusks, and a single nose ring decorated with a tiny skull. Somi wore a breastplate and carrie da massive greataxe.

History

Somi and his brother entered Entharl Danthelon‎'s employ sometime in or before the . Both half-orcs diligently guarded the shop, the proprietor, and the shop's cellar, that was secretly used as a Harper safehouse. Somi made sure that no Harper meeting was interrupted by city guards nor steel watchers.
