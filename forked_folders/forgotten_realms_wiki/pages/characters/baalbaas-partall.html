Baalbaas Partall was a grocer inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid–1360's DR.

Activities

Baalbaas was the main public point of contact for the Council of Farmer-Grocers in Waterdeep. He held the title of "Voice of the Master" within the guild and worked out of the Market Hall, which was their guild headquarters located on Trader's Way in the Castle Ward.
