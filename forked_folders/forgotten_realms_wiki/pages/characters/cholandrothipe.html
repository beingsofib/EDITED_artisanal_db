Cholandrothipe was a skilled wizard who once lived in Feather Falls in Featherdale.

Activities

Cholandrothipe constructed a tower that became Feather Falls' prominent landmark. The tower was a heavily trapped and dangerous place, making some inhabitants of the town wonder about the wizard's kindness.

Cholandrothipe used his magic for a fee to transport cargo safely across the Feather Falls. His fee usually fell into the 25–50 gp range, causing many to believe he had quite the stash of coin hidden somewhere within his tower.
