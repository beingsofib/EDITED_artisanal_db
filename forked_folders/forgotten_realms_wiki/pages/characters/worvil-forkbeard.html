Worvil Forkbeard, mostly known only as the Weevil, was a notorious criminal in Ten Towns sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Worvil led a bandit group that raided caravans heading north to the mines of Mirabar. The Order of the Gauntlet captured most of the bandits but the Weevil escaped. Sir Baric Nylef of the Gauntlet searched for him in Bryn Shander. Worvil in fact was hiding at Xantharl's Keep in the Savage Frontier, posing as a stable hand at the Falling Orc inn.

Personality

Worvil was addicted to Flamebeard's Firebrandy.
