Narg was a barbazu that dwelled on Avernus for a time during the Faerûnian year, 1492 DR.

History

Narg was enlisted alongisde Yangor as a personal bodyguard for the kyton Zartar, during the latter devil's unholy pilgrimage to Sundered Chains on Avernus. The pair protected Zartar during the completion of a ritual beseaching more power from the god Asmodeus.
