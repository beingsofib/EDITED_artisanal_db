Rystia Zav was a spy and a member of the Harpers from Nesme during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Rytsia had gone mad from loneliness, fear, and demon lords' influence. She believed that all beings she met were killers sent after her.

History

In the late 1480s, she was tasked with the mission of finding Mantol-Derith and infiltrating the Zhentarim garrison there. However, Rystia became lost in the Underdark. Circa 1486 DR, she came to the gates of Mantol-Derith without knowing she had arrived or how.
