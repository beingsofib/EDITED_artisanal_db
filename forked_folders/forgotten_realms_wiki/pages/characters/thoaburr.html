Thoaburr was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

History

Thoaburr was a part of the retinue of the Royal Magician of Cormyr, Caladnei, in the early spring of , when they raided the Marsember meeting of the Rightful Conspiracy. He was assigned afterwards to hunt down Beltrar Morgrin.
