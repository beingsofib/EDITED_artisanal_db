Halonya Clayhill was the buxom proprietress of the largest brothel on the waterfront in Lyrabar.

Description

As of , she was described as buxom, wearing heavy makeup and black paper beauty spots.

History

When Taegan Nightwind's fencing academy burned down on Ches 13 of , she was rumormongering amongst the whores on the wreckage, which infuriated the maestro.
