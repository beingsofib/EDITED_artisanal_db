Shiala Torthren was a historian of the Earthome College in the druid stronghold city of Cedarsproke in the Gulthmere Forest, Vilhon Reach, in the 14 century DR.

History

By the late-14 century DR, Shiala Torthren's expertise was renowned. Her works covered the history and politics of Arrabar in Chondath and compared them to the neighboring Sespech. She warned those who supported Sespechan independence of the impotence of Baron Foesmasher. If the leader of Sespech were to fall, other neighbors would surely feel the "gentle caress" of Arrabar next.
