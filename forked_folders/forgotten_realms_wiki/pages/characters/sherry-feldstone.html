Sherry Feldstone was a human druid and adventurer in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Growing up in Mistledale, Sherry was always fascinated by the brooding splendour of the forests, which led her to become a druid.

Possessions

She wore a pair of bracers of defense, a ring of protection, and a ring of animal friendship.

Activities

She had adventured near the ruins of Myth Drannor, and twice she had attempted to prevent gargoyles from moving into the area, both times unsuccessful.
