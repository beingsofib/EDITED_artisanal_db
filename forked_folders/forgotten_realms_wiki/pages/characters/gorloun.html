Gorloun was a dragon of immense size, might, and age, who founded the first draconic empire of Abeir.

History

Gorloun founded its nation some time after the War of Fang and Talon, in the continent of Laerakond. Many other dragons followed Gorloun's example, ushering the Rule of Dragons.
