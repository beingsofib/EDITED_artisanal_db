Bonnie was a doppelganger posing as a barmaid in the Yawning Portal tavern in 1492 DR.

History

Bonnie led a gang of five doppelgängers in Waterdeep in 1491 DR. A year later, she had become the barmaid at the famous Yawning Portal tavern.

Relationships

Mattrim Mereg, a bard and Harper spy, knew her secret and wanted to help Bonnie. However, the Emerald Enclave wanted all doppelgängers out of the city.
