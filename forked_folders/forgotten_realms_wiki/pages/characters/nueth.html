Nueth

Person

Nueth was a shopkeeper in Waterdeep around the 14 century DR.

History

Nueth was the propietor and namesake of the store Nueth's Fine Nets in Waterdeep's Southern Ward. They ran the store until their death, sometime around the 1350's DR.
