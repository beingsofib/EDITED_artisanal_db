Nureh yr Catahra el Pesarkhal, an exceptional spymaster of the Memnon underworld, was the wife of the crime lord Nasim yn Feroz el Pesarkhal and sister-in-law to the syl-pasha in 1370 DR.

Description

She was a beautiful half-elven woman with jet black hair.

Personality

Nureh utilized many guises—bard, barmaid, and jhasina to name a few—and many agents. The syl-pasha considered her the most skilled spymaster he had ever known.
