Laranla Imdalace was an ancient ruler of Ardeep on the Sword Coast North into the early 1 century DR.

Abilities

Imdalace was known to have developed a spell that transformed her into a quietly soaring black blade. By striking down foes in this form, she acquired their memories related to the topic she concentrated upon.

Relationships

She was the grand-niece of Fildaerae, the previous Laranla, and a kinswoman to the next, Embrae Aloevan.

History

After Laranla Fildaerae died in an orc raid during the , she was succeeded by her grand-niece Imdalace, who ruled Ardeep for nearly four centuries. In turn, Imdalace vanished in the , to be succeeded as the Laranla of Ardeep by her kinswoman Embrae Aloevan.
