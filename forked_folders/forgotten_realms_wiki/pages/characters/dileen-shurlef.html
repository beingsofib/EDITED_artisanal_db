Dileen Shurlef was an assassin during the Time of Troubles.

History

Shurlef was an assassin and a devoted worshiper of Bhaal who helped his god when he was cast out into the Realms in 1358 DR.

Dileen died when the god Bane called to him all the souls of the assassins.
