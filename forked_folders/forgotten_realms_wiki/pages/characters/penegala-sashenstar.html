Penegala Sashenstar was a skilled human bard during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

She was one of the original members of the Knights of the Unicorn and a worshiper of Lurue. Following the Time of Troubles, Penegala led one of the affiliated groups of the Order of the Unicorn.

History

Penegala originally hailed from Baldur's Gate on the Sword Coast.
