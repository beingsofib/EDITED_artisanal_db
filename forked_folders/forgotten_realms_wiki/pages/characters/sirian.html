Sirian was one of the male drow, led by the High Priestess of Lolth, Azurna in the mid–14 century DR.

History

In 1370 DR, Azurna was following the will of her goddess to uncover the lost Flame Sword of Lolth in Undermountain and conspiring against Waterdeep. Azurna's drow forces established multiple bases and strongholds in Halaster Blackcloak's mega-dungeon. Sirian was stationed in one of the deeper strongholds and was entrusted with the keys of the base by two higher-ups who were romantically involved with each other.
