The Spirit of the Neverwinter Woods was a fey defender of Neverwinter Wood in the 14 century DR.

History

Sometime shortly before the , the fey inhabitants of Neverwinter Wood faced a crisis that left the Spirit of the Neverwinter Woods wounded. In attempts to defend the forest, a group of dryads and nymphs crafted a powerful weapon – a fey spear, to be given to a hero willing to defend them and the Spirit. Before the fey spear found a hero, the crisis was over, and the weapon was discarded and forgotten.
