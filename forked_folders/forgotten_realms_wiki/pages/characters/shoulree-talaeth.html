Shoulree Talaeth was a wielder of spellfire from the Elven Court at some point prior to .

History

Per Elminster Aumar on , she had been an inhabitant of the Elven Court and a wielder of spellfire in the days of Myth Drannor. When inquired by Lhaeo, he claimed to doubt she yet walked the earth, implying she was most likely dead.

Elminster also owned some of her gowns in that year, which he loaned to Shandril Shessair.
