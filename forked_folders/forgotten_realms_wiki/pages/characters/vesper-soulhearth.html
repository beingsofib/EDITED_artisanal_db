Vesper Soulhearth was a doomguide who lived in the city of Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the 1480s DR, Vesper and a contingent of Kelemvorite priests were tasked with defeating the undead that took control of the village of Ebon Downs.

He later was involved in the defeat of the necromancer Morlanth.
