King Dynnegall was the ruler of the Kingdom of Moray on the island of Moray in the Moonshae Isles during the early-to-mid–14 century DR. He was the last of his line.

History

The village of Dynnegall was named in his honor.

Dynnegall assumed the throne of Moray in the , after the death of his father in battle against Northmen at Trondhelm.

In the , King Dynnegall and his heirs were all slain by assassins led by Razfallow on the orders of Cyndre. His daughter survived just long enough to describe the assassins, whose attack went off-script when they were forced to kill dozens of guards while hunting down an infant member of the family who had been hidden in the attic by a nursemaid.
