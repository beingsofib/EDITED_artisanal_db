Ferguson was a guardsman from Stag Falls in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

The man was underhanded and vindictive.

Possessions

He had access to a nightshade-based poison, somehow.

Activities

Ferguson was one of the guards in Stag Falls' castle.

Relationships

He served the Baron Vilhelm von Stag Falls.

History

His lady love was once swept off her feet, seduced into leaving Ferguson, and then driven to suicide within a week by a man whose arms bore a very specific heraldic device.

Several years later, the vampire Romanisk Vlaensk turned Baron Vilhelm von Stag Falls into an undead under his control; a month after that, adventurers from Ravens Bluff, sent by Marcus Rubicon, came to the castle after looking into a missing patrol. If they bore the shield from the patrol's sergeant, a gift from Conundrum, Ferguson would've noticed the two were identical, and served the man with the shield a slow-acting poisoned wine. Should they survive, complaints would yield nothing but promises to look into it.
