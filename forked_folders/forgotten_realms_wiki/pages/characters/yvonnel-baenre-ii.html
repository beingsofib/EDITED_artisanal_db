Yvonnel Baenre was a drow of House Baenre in Menzoberranzan and the daughter of Minolin Fey-Branche and Gromph Baenre.

Description

Yvonnel was a beautiful drow woman with thick white hair, violet eyes, and a beautiful, melodic voice.

Abilities

Yvonnel could change her true appearance at will,

Yvonnel had spells of healing, enough to save one from the brink of death and return them to good health over several days.

Personality

Yvonnel was a sadistic infant, often tormenting her mother Minolin Fey. She was deeply impacted by her time with Drizzt Do'Urden, and upon studying the history of Menzoberranzan, became disenchanted with Lolth and her evil influence over drow society in the Underdark. Thus, she began on a new path of redemption and revolution.

History

Early Life

During her gestation, Minolin Fey-Branche was subjected to treatment at the hands of the illithid Methil El-Viddenvelp to infuse the as-yet-unborn drow with the memories of the former Matron Mother, Yvonnel Baenre, her name-sake. Born self-aware, Yvonnel was a frightfully powerful priestess and mage from the moment she drew breath. Quickly assuming dominance over her mother, who attempted to assassinate her only to be thwarted by the yochlol Yiccardaria, she was being groomed for eventual succession as matron mother of House Baenre. Quickly growing tired of her infancy, Yvonnel used her implanted memories of her grandmother to cast a much older version of the spell haste, one with the supposed drawback of aging the caster one year each time the spell was cast. Multiple castings of the spell had the desired effect of aging the infant Yvonnel into a shockingly beautiful young woman in very short order. Yvonnel quickly took secret control over House Baenre, allowing Quenthel Baenre to maintain her public seat as Matron Mother.

Defeat of Demogorgon

Yvonnel arranged for the renegade Drizzt Do'Urden and his comrades to be captured in Menzoberranzan during their rescue of Dahlia Sin'felle. Arranging a duel between Drizzt and his long-time rival, Tiago Baenre for the title of "Champion of Lolth," Yvonnel used a risen K'yorl Odran and a ritual involving almost every spellcaster in the city along with support from Lolth and the illithid hive mind to empower Drizzt with a kinetic barrier. Targeting Drizzt's barrier, the combined might of the city of Menzobarranzan provided enough power for Drizzt to destroy the Demon Prince Demogorgon in a single blow.

Infatuation with Drizzt Do'Urden

Yvonnel, believing Drizzt to be in the full favor of the Spider Queen despite his alignment offered him the title of King of Menzoberranzan. Drizzt refused, further fascinating the still-very-young drow. Yvonnel allowed Drizzt, Jarlaxle, Artemis Entreri, and Dahlia to leave the city with their possessions but not before cursing Drizzt. Taking advantage of his budding abyssal madness, Yvonnel arranged to have him hallucinate his love, Catti-brie as a demon when he next saw her, justifying to her aunts his release to be Lolth's will.

Fortunately, Drizzt managing to avoid killing his wife, which surprised Yvonnel, who was scrying him at the time. Yvonnel had come to regret cursing the renegade and was elated that he managed to not kill Catti-brie. She was further surprised by her feelings in the matter; Drizzt's ability to resist her curse triggered the epiphany that later turned Yvonnel to Drizzt's side.

Leaving Menzoberranzan

Infused with the power of a high priestess in the full favor of Lolth and with the inherited arcane might of her father, Yvonnel quickly grew bored with Lolth's plan for her and left Menzoberranzan for the surface.

In Damara outside the Monastery of the Yellow Rose, Yvonnel teamed up with Entreri to put her immense knowledge to the task of curing Drizzt's abyssal madness using the powers of Yiccardaria, whom Yvonnel then betrayed when she had the prearranged Grandmaster Kane banish the yochlol back to the Abyss after the task was complete. Ending Drizzt's madness, Yvonnel then accompanied them on another adventure to defeat the Queen of Succubi, Malcanthet, meeting Wulfgar, Regis, and saving the life of Pikel Bouldershoulder in the process. Utilizing pure guile and an illusion of Graz'zt, Yvonnel tricked Malcanthet into retreating to the Abyss. Drizzt was mortally wounded by the demon in combat. Yvonnel, having turned her back on Lolth, had no power to heal him, instead summoning a yochlol. The yochlol opened a portal and summoned Lolth herself to the Prime Material Plane. Lolth granted a powerful healing spell to Yvonnel, which she quickly used to heal the fallen ranger before being thrown aside like a ragdoll by the Demon Queen of Spiders. Lolth entangled Drizzt's companions in spider webs, torturing them with swarms of biting spiders and demanding Drizzt worship her in exchange for their release, and her love. Drizzt remained defiant even after being lifted and kissed by Lolth, offering him untold pleasures and even the return of his beloved father, Zaknafein Do'Urden. Drizzt again denied her, explaining that to accept would be to lie, as he cannot simply change that which is in his heart. Lolth, apparently satisfied with Drizzt's answer and declaring that he amused her, forced another kiss, then returned to the Demonweb Pits. Yvonnel and Artemis Entreri translated her last benediction: "Drojal zhah obdoluth dorb’d streeak. Lueth dro zhah zhaunau dorb’d ogglin." 'Existence is empty without chaos. And life is boring without enemies."

Seduction of Drizzt Do'Urden

Yvonnel later turned up in Luskan to meet Jarlaxle, joined by Zaknafein Do'Urden, who had been returned to life by unknown means for unknown purpose and demanding to see his son. She accompanied Jarlaxle and Zaknafein to Gauntlgrym via the fiery portal gates beneath the Hosttower of the Arcane and was allowed entry to the dwarven complex once recognized as a known friend of King Bruenor Battlehammer. She joined in on introducing Zaknafein to Cattie-brie and Wulfgar and explained that, after thorough investigation, it was truly Drizzt's father returned from death and no doppelganger.

Yvonnel adjusted her physical appearance a final time, taking on a similar violet eye color to Drizzt's, then revealed the change to Drizzt alone in his home at the hafling town of Bleeding Vines. Drizzt approved of the change in appearance, and Yvonnel explained to Drizzt that she would no longer use illusion magic to appeal to others, and in a similar vein, insisted that Zaknafein's return was real. Soon after, Jarlaxle caught up with her and accused Yvonnel of seducing Drizzt, which she did not deny. Yvonnel then implied that she could wait for Drizzt to become romantically available at the end of Cattie-brie's natural life, to which Jarlaxle suggested Yvonnel may be planning to kill Cattie-brie to take the place of Drizzt's lover. Yvonnel slapped him and stated that she was not Cattie-brie's enemy and would not hurt Drizzt in such a way, though again she did not deny having feelings for Drizzt. The conversation turned to their planned revolution in Menzoberranzan and to the role of Lolth in Zaknafein's return, and Lolth's possible motivations, but the two were left at a loss. Yvonnel revealed a sudden epiphany that she could re-experience the memories of Yvonnel the Eternal vividly as though she was her namesake, including thoughts, emotions, and all five senses.

Befriending More Companions of the Hall

After a brief conversation alone with him, Yvonnel used a spell of windwalking to secretly shadow Drizzt on his journey to catch up with Zaknafein and Jarlaxle at Thornhold to discover the fates of the missing Athrogate and his lover Ambergris, who had ventured there to learn what they may about Clan Stoneshaft's dealings with Lord Neverember. There, Yvonnel healed a broken Athrogate grieving his slain lover and suffering from demonic wounds, saving him from certain death. Yvonnel questioned why Lolth continued to grant her spells to heal the Spider Queen's enemies, even after Yvonnel's treachery of returning Zaknafein to the heretic Drizzt's side.

Soon after saving Athrogate, Yvonnel encountered Regis trying to escape a glabrezu, which she swiftly banishes, saving the imperiled hafling who was meant for Waterdeep. With Regis and the still injured Athrogate in tow, the Yvonnel escorted them the significant distance to the city over the course of a few days, the dwarf sleeping most of the trip riding on Yvonnel's floating disk and being healed by her daily. Regis hesitantly departed the group and entered the city to seek allies against the demons on the surface surrounding Gauntlgrym, wishing Yvonnel would join him. Yvonnel and Athrogate returned the way they had come, hoping to happen upon Drizzt, or to return to Thornhold for revenge on Amber's demonically influenced dwarven executioners. On their journey together, Yvonnel consoled Athrogate through his incredible emotional pain from losing his beloved, expressing her regret in never getting to meet the woman Athrogate and many others held dear. She helped the dwarf find his will to live and carry on, promising to return him to his full strength so they could avenge Amber together.Sometime later, Yvonnel and a fully recovered Athrogate returned to their shelter, a cave where they were also sheltering twelve humans from nearby farms, they had rescued from the demons rampaging in the area. Yvonnel controlled many crystalline bird familiars that scouted the area about them, alerting her of enemies and keeping them all out of danger. Using Regis' pony as bait, they continued to hunt down more demons, encountering one possessing a dwarven women of the Stoneshaft clan via a phylactery in a moonstone necklace. Yvonnel commands Athrogate to spare the dwarf by removing the necklace instead of outright killing her, despite his thirst for vengeance, so that Yvonnel can found out if she can save the possessed dwarf. Yvonnel is successful, and begs Athrogate not to murder the saved dwarf, for his own sake. After, Yvonnel questioned the Stoneshaft dwarf with divination magic to detect lies and found no involvement in the demon invasion and left the dwarf unsupervised with the other refugees. Athrogate seemed in a better mood after sparing the dwarven woman, but their conversation was interrupted by one of Yvonnel's familiars.

Outside, the pair met Drizzt Do'Urden riding Andahar at a quick pace, running from a Retriever pursuing him, one of two set upon himself and Zaknafein by Zhindia Melarn. With her knowledge of the golems, Yvonnel suggested Drizzt continue his run and in the meantime, she would seek out a solution to the nay invulnerable spider and come to his aid. Drizzt agreed with the reasoning and trusted all his equipment to them to give Catti-brie if Drizzt didn't return.
