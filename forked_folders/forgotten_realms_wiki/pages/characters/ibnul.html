Ibnul, who carried the titles of Potentate of the Ebbing Tides, Caliph of the Eastern Currents, and Sovereign of all Sharks, was a marid inhabiting the Elemental Plane of Water in 1367 DR.

Personality

He was a braggart and liar.

Activities

Ibnul found himself outsmarted by a pahari named Busain who grew tired of his constant lies. Under the guise of a fish, Busain tricked Ibnul into entering a genie contract where he granted his voice to the pahari so she could use it until she found her lost mother. Unbenownst to Ibnul, Busain's mother was already dead, granting her the marid's voice for as long as she wished.

Abilities

Ibnul was effectively powerless without the use of his voice.
