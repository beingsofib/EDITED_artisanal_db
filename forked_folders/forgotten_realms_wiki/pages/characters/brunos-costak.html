Brunos Costak was one of the Western Divisional Leaders of the Iron Throne during the mid-14 century DR.

Activities

He worked alongside his colleague Rieltar Anchev, the architect behind the Iron crisis that plagued the Sword Coast.

History

In the , Brunos accompanied Rieltar to Candlekeep. They sought to negotiate a treaty with the Knights of the Shield after the villain Sarevok took control of the Baldurian Iron Throne to his own end.

It was rumored Brunos was killed along with the other members of that meeting, though every other one of them lived for years afterwards.
