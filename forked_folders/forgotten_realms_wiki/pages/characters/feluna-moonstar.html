Feluna Moonstar was a female human werecat cleric of Selûne in Waterdeep in 1372 DR.

Personality

Feluna had many of the traits of felines, like a strong sense of independence, nocturnal habits, and a hunter's mentality.

History

Feluna was the youngest sister of Rober Moonstar. Feluna's feline power appeared during the Time of Troubles when, after being kidnapped and gagged and left in the sewers for several days, she begged the moon goddess for help and was transformed into a cat.
