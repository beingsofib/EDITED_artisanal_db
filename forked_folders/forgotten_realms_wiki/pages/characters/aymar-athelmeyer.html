Aymar Athelmeyer was the assumed identity of a malenti infiltrator on board the Emerald Tayar in the .

Description

He looked like a sailor.

Personality

A fanatic, Aymar Athelmeyer didn't think twice about dying for his mission.

Possessions

Of note, he carried a wand of polymorphing, a short sword, and a suicide capsule he could swallow.

History

The malenti was ordered to hamper the mission of the Emerald Tayar, a ship that was sent to ferry Sturge Whitsunday in his search for Myth Nantar on Mirtul of the . He would first tamper with the Colimpha, and if discovered, use his wand of polymorphing to wreak havoc. He was prepared to leap into the water and escape, but if captured, he'd kill himself with a poison capsule rather than reveal any information.
