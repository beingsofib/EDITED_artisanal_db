Lyrkon was a Zhentilar warrior in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He found victorious mages insufferable.

Abilities

He could tie a good knot.

Relationships

He was part of the Zhentilar squad that accompanied Fimril, alongside Glondar and Duthspurn.

History

Lyrkon joined a squad that accompanied the Zhentarim mageling Fimril in his quest to seize Shandril Shessair. After Fimril managed to get Shandril to surrender at Tethgard, Lyrkon tied her up. While they were doing that, Mirt appeared from hiding and slew Glondar, then fought the rest of his men. After Fimril cast a killing spell at Mirt, and was slain by the reflected magic, the men yet living fled.

Mirt later called on Duskreene to rout the fleeing Zhentilar, which she did.
