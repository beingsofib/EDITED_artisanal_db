Daunt Buirune was a tavern owner inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Daunt owned the Red Gauntlet tavern on the north side of the southern stretch of the Way of the Dragon in the Tween Run of Waterdeep's Southern Ward.

He was a retired master thief, though that was hardly common knowledge.
