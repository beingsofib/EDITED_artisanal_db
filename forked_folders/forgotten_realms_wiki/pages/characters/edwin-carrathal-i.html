Edwin Carrathal I was High King of Callidyrr at the turn of the 12 century DR. Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles, he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty. He was the third son of Queen Yolanda and King Bertrand, and his eventual successor was his first daughter, Bethany. Her first son and heir was named in Edwin's honor.
