Torbit was a bullywug assassin in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR.

Activities

Torbit remained in the temple headquarters of Kuketh, protecting the entrance to the lair of his master.

History

In 1491 DR, Halaster Blackcloak summoned Torbit's bullywug tribe inside the Slitherswamp level and appointed the death slaad Kuketh to act as their king and fight the nagas of Ssethian Scourges.

Personality

Torbit was totally loyal to Kuketh.
