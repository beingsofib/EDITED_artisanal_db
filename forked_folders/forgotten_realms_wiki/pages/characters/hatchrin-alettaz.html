Hatchrin Alet'taz was the disgraced consort of Erelal Freth inside Undermountain in the early 1490s DR.

History

Hatchrin became the consort of Erelal Freth and fathered her unborn eleventh child around the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR. However just after those events, Erelal accused Hatchrin of plotting to poison the unborn child after childbirth (in truth, this was a false accusation planted by Drivvin Freth) and ordered him to be imprisoned and tortured. However, Hatchrin still wanted to regain Erelal's trust. He was tortured together with the Auvryndar spy Varrn Telenna.
