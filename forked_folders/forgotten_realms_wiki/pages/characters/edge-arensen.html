"Edge" Arensen was the city executioner and a member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 century DR.

Description

"Edge" was a middle-aged human man with stern expression and face decorated with several scars. He had fair skin and brown yes. He wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist – a helmet, a set of chainmail, and a tabard adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia. He wielded a halberd in battle.

History

In the , "Edge" Arensen was stationed just outside of the Basilisk Gate in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. He manned gallows built atop a plaza in front of the gate.
