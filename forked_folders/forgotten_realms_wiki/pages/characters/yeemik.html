Yeemik was a goblin leader and second-in-command of Cragmaw Hideout after Klarg.

Description

The ambitious goblin wished to become the first-in-command of the entire hideout. He would even go as far as to consider using force to overthrow him. As well as his ambition, he was very untrustworthy.

Possessions

He carried a pouch of three golden teeth, which were worth about 1 gp each. Yeemik also carried 15 sp, but nothing else of note.
