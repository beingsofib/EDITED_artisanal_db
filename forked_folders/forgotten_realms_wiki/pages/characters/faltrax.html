Faltrax was an incubus of the late 15 century DR.

History

When Elturel was transported to Avernus, first layer of the Nine Hells, in the , Faltrax infiltrated the city on a mission to acquire intelligence on its defenses, planning to sell this to whichever fiend paid the highest. It posed as a male human blacksmith named Orin Ragron, with a shop in the city, but was attacked by two bulezaus who'd also found their way in. Faltrax would try to enlist the aid adventurers, but would flee if found out.
