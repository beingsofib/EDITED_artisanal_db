Xynicynct was the jailer of Vorgansharax in 1489 DR.

History

After Vorgansharax's coup, Xynicynct led a squad of the Tears of Virulence in trying to stop the escape of Madame Freona, the Lord Sage of Phlan, and Jhessail Greycastle from the city, but was killed by an adventuring party hired by the resistance.
