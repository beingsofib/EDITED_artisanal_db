Myrintar Hasantar was a writer and a Sage of Suzail, the capital city of Cormyr, who was active in the late 12 and early 14 centuries DR.

History

Myrintar penned Things a Knight Should Know in the . The book was dedicated to the sage's musings on the topics of adventuring and knighthood.
