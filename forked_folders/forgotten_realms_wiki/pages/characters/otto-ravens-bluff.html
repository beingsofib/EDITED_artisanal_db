Otto "mouse" was a human rogue living in Ravens Bluff in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He knew the harbor area of Ravens Bluff so well that he could disappear into a nook or cranny almost everywhere in that district. This ability gained him the nickname "mouse". From those hiding spots he gained information that was sold on to Catherine Kincaid of the Harbor Patrol.

Possessions

He owned little of note expect a bottle of oil of slipperiness.
