Branley (cooper)

Person
nameBranley
homeMoonhaven, Sword Coast
occupationCooper

Branley was the cooper of the town of Moonhaven sometime during or before the late 15 century DR.

History

At some point, Branley the cooper got a large splinter in their hand, likely while woodworking. Moonhaven's resident healer removed the offending foreign object, cleaned the wood, and treated it with an pointment of balsam.
