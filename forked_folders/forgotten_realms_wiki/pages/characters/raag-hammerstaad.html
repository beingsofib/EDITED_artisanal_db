Raag Hammerstaad was the Northlander king of Norheim in the mid–14 century DR. His insignia was a blue whale.

History

Raag ruled Norheim from his lodge at Hammerstaad. He departed for a voyage to Tethyr circa the , shortly following a visit to his lodge by Elminster.

Alongside Grunnarch the Red, he supported Thelgaar Ironhand in his attempt to invade the southern Moonshae Isles in the .
