Ketta was a hin rogue and an instructor of thieving and roguish skills and the Head of Security, Espionage and Intrigue at the Adventurer's Academy of Neverwinter in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Ketta was a light-skinned halfling woman with brown hair and an intense stare, dressed in black and brown rogue's garb.

History

By the , Ketta was an established rogue, spy, security expert, and master interrogator. Even at the Adventurer's Academy, she had a small network of agents who kept her up to date on students, teachers, and other affairs. When the Adventurer's Academy was attacked by the enemies of Neverwinter in 1372 DR, Ketta was one of the many fallen.
