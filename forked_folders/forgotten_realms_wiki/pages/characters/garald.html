Garald was a Ffolk horse breeder who inhabited Horstall on the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Garald's reputation was so well deserved that even King Bryon Kendrick requested his steeds. Garald was once joined by the mage Elminster of Shadowdale while delivering twelve of his horses to Caer Corwell.
