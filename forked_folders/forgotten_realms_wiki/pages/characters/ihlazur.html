Ihlazur was a glabrezu demon of the Demonweb Pits.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, Ihlazur emerged from a stray portal between the Demonweb Pits and the Underdark near Sschindylryn. He began fighting everything in sight in the hopes of a good challenge, and upon finally being bested happily dissolved back to his Abyssal home.
