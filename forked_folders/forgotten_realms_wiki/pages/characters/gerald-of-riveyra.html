Gerald of Riveyra was a Torilian sage and scholar who specialized in the criminal organizations of the Underdark in the 14 century DR.

Activities

Gerald studied Underdark gangsters such as Gnormahlenvaey Catoneriehn, Vikhrumn Coll, Brikhalna Ipprszhen, Felyndiira Shi'narrvha, and Gray'shivnarien Taliain. His information found use by Sir Elliot of Kness and the Forthright Band of Investigators in Waterdeep.
