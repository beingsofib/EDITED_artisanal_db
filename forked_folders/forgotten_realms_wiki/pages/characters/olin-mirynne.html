Olin Mirynne was an elf fighter in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Olin had blue eyes and light skin. He wore a full suit of silvery chainmail with leafy-styled plates, and carried a fine shield and axe.

History

In the during the Elven Crusade, Olin joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their large camp on the outskirts of Myth Drannor. He served as a trainer for fighters there.
