Kixa To'u was a great wu jen who lived in Koryo several centuries before 1357 DR.

Kixa To'u fashioned a magical staff of eldritch power, the Ky Trencha Ukang. But he came to fear what he had wrought and split the staff apart, transforming it into five magical scroll reels.
