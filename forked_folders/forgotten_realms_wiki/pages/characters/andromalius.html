Earl Andromalius was a devil from Acheron who served Bane. He was involved with, and killed as part of, the early stages of the pixelating curse before it was brought to Toril in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Andromalius looked repulsive.

Abilities

As the ruler of Cube 1717, Andromalius was the only one who had the power to allow teleportation from the cube. When he died, this meant nobody could teleport from the cube.

Activities

In Cube 1717, Andromalius performed magical experiments on those that he had imprisoned.

Relationships

Andromalius was served by Avnas, a derro from Acheron. He considered her a valuable asset for her abilities in engineering. He gave her the book of patterns, which ultimately led to the pixelating curse.

History

While Andromalius ruled Cube 1717, three adventurers tried and failed to overthrow him. They were individually charged and then their heads were cut off and merged together into one body. The three were brought back to life as one being: the skull lord Vargo. Andromalius then imprisoned Vargo in Cube 1717's prisons for 1 million life sentences.

Hundreds of years later, to perform experiments on. This transformed the quadrone into the Archquadrone, who then created the pixelating curse. The curse spread quickly through Cube 1717 and caused a conflict in which Andromalius was slain.

After his death, Vargo, now the ruler of Cube 1717, kept Andromalius's head in a case made of glass. The head was pixelated by the pixelating curse, and rotten.
