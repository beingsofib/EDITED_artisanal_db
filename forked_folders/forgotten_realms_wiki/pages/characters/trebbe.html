Trebbe was an archwizard and one of the most prolific spell creators in Netheril.

Abilities

He was a specialist in magic that protected arcanists, enabling spell duels to last much longer.

History

Trebbe was born in . He established Shadowtop Borough, a Netherese enclave for magical research in . It was here he created the Crown of Horns, an evil artifact.

Death

In , a spellcasting accident triggered while Trebbe was working on the crown led to his death.
