Wyle was a Shadovar wizard of Returned Netheril who raided the Old Man's Face in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Wyle had short white-blond hair.

Abilities

Wyle was a specialist in summoning shadows and umbral rats. He could cast frost lance, mass hold monster, ray of exhaustion, sunburst, symbol of weakness, and thunderstroke.

History

When the Shadovar pursued their stolen Nether Scroll along the High Road, Wyle and Tarys tracked a piece to the Old Man's Face at Tyrluk. Taking their followers and hired mercenaries, they rushed into the village and took over the inn, killing many guests as they searched the premises. While Tarys searched the tavern, Wyle secured the barn, filling it with malevolent shadows. When Harpers sent by Rina Belostos and Kordo Sweetspire caught up, they were directed by Branson Blackboot to get the tavern's spare key from the attic of the barn, sending them through Wyle. Though he summoned many murderous shadows from his perch high up and challenged them to come get him, he proved no match for a small team of righteous heroes.
