Booyagh Benta was a cultist of the Absolute who served her goddess in the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

She wore the crudely-crafted leather-and-cloth straps, and signature headdress that were often worn by goblin spellcasters.

History

As of the , Booyagh Benta remained among the Absolute's devotees who were readied to assail the Emerald Grove, under the command of their general, True Soul Minthara Baenre.
