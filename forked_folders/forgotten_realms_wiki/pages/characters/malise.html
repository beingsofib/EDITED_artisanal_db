Malise was a cleric of Tiamat living in Unther in 1374 DR.

Activities

Malise managed the Altar of Scales temple in Unthalass. He was busy crossbreeding dragons with other creatures like outsiders. He had created a new kind of abishai and some new dragonspawn.

History

Malise gained the collaboration of the young brown dragon blackguard Slavin'krath'magaal, who'd agreed to mate with Malise to give birth to a half-dragon offspring, Braeden.

Personality

Malise dreamed of using his hybrid to gain power in Unther and overthrow Tiglath as high priest of Tiamat.
