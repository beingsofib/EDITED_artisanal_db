Terondarg Del'Armgo was a drow and one of the most able scouts of House Barrison Del'Armgo in Menzoberranzan in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , following his house's attack against the resurrected House Do'Urden, Terondarg conducted a scouting mission. He later made a report to the matron mother Mez'Barris Armgo and high priestess Taayrul Armgo, using Drow Sign Language concealed by his cloak for privacy.
