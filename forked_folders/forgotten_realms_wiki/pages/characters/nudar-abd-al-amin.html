Nudar Abd al-Amin was a platoon commander from Hiyal in Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Personality

Nudar was easy going and well-liked.

Activities

She commanded a platoon within the mamluks of the Faithful, often protecting caravans travelling from Hiyal to Qudra.
