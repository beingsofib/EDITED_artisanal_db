Bardolin was an Amnian soldier and guard who served in Nashkel during the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

Bardolan wore the standard kit of the soldiers in Nashkel: a suit of chain mail and winged helmet, along with a longsword and longbow.

History

Around the , Bardolan was on duty in Nashkel, having to deal with the ongoing iron crisis and the bloody rampage of Captain Brage.
