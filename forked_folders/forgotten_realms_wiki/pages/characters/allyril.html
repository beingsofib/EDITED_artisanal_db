Allyril was a sorceress adventurer and part of the Athan's band that was sent to Myth Drannor by Elminster Aumar in 1369 DR.

History

When the pool of radiance reawakened under Phlan in 1369 DR, Elminster was able to pinpoint it's corrupting influence emanating from the ruins of Myth Drannor. Not losing any precious time, Elminster gathered an adventuring party, led by Athan, and armed them with the Gauntlets of Moander. The band spent some time wishing the ruins, investigating the mysterious magic that spawned the accursed pool, only to be ambushed by the forces of the Cult of the Dragon. Led by Pelendralaar, the cultist slaughtered most of the expedition. Allyril was killed by a direct from a lightning bolt, as later deducted by Ghleanna Stormlake who inspected the gravely burns on the sorceress' body.
