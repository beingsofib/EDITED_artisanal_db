Gastyn was head of the servants in Lord Marsten's hunting lodge.

History

Gastyn was head butler for Lord Marsten until, some time before the Tyranny of Dragons, his master was killed and the lodge occupied by Cult of the Dragon agents under Talis Kasterel. She decide to use Gastyn and other servants as her own.
