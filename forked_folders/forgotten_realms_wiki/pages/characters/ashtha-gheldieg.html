Ashtha Gheldieg was a member of the Amnian House Gheldieg from the city of Eshpurta in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Ashtha Gheldieg was one of the clients who did business with the mummy lord only known as the Mystic Carrion of Baldur's Gate. The mummy procured a doll of the necromancer, a rare magic item for Ashtha to be used for some reanimation purposes. The record of the purchase was kept in Mystic Carrion's lair in Philgrave's Mansion in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate.
