Petrove was a pirate, and the great-uncle of Coprates Bile in the mid-14 century DR.

Activities

He was a pirate.

Relationships

Petrove was great-uncle to Coprates Bile, and told her many tales of the sea.

History

The tales Petrove told as great-uncle to Coprates Bile were formative in her ambitions to become a pirate captain, and were an important part of what made her sneak out of her home at the tender age of 13 years, approximately in the , to become a cabin boy.

He was slain later around , by sahuagin, which made her hate them intensely and forever, never missing the opportunity to kill one.
