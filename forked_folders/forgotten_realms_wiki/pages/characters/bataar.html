Bataar was Ulaan's sister, and a servant in the Fortress of the Old Man. She helped wash and clothe Lewan when he was brought in by Sauk after Tarsakh 16 of . On the night of Tarsakh 25, Ulaan believed her sister had been killed in the fighting that broke out throughout the fortress, but her death was never confirmed.
