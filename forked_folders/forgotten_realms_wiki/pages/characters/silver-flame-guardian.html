The Silver Flame Guardian was a fragment or avatar of the Silver Flame, a deity of Eberron. He was charged with containing the Spinner of Shadows in the Spinner's Prison below Stormreach.

Description

The Guardian appeared to be a blazing mass of silver-blue fire.

History

At some point in the distant past, a fragment of the goddess Lolth was trapped in Khyber, becoming one of the realm's fiendish Overlords. The Guardian was likewise a small piece of the Silver Flame, tasked with maintaining the prison and preventing the Spinner from escaping. He was joined at the Spinner's Prison in this by a series of mortal servants who maintained the wards and protections set upon the prison, ensuring they did not deteriorate or get sabotaged. The latest comrade in this was Brother Aurelius, a scholarly sort who regarded the Guardian as a colleague.

During the Darkening of mid-1480s DR in Faerûn, the Lords of Dust cult hidden in Stormreach sought to release the Spinner of Shadows. Finding their way into the underworld by the road to Tarath Marad, they broke through the Guardian's defenses and set about weakening the prison, including the use of magical spiders that devoured and weakened the Guardian's physical fiery form. Before the breakout could be completed, however, heroes sent by the Templar Orrex arrived and defeated the cultist, meeting with the Guardian before entering the prison themselves to face the Spinner. They destroyed the flame eater spiders that the Spinner was using to devour the Silver Flame and foiled her attempts to escape, only for Lolth to personally intervene. She broke open a Rift Between Worlds from the Demonweb and took the Spinner of Shadows into her domain, and only the efforts of the Guardian prevented the violent conjunction from killing the heroes.

Following this encounter, the Guardian bid the heroes continue their efforts, leading them into the Demonweb, meeting Elminster, and eventually finding the village of Eveningstar in Faerûn.
