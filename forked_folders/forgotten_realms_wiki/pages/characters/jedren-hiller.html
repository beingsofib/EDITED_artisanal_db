Jedren Hiller was a the Bailiff of the Wide in Baldur's Gate during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

He was a wholly corrupt man who fully expected to be offered bribes, which he readily accepted.

Activities

While he carried out his normal duties as bailiff, Hiller gave preferential market space to nobles of the Upper City, and those who offered more generous inducements.
