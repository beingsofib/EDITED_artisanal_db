Vylxildross the Malevolent was a powerful red dragon allied with the Cult of the Dragon and the Red Wizards of Thay in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Vylxildross the Malevolent was present at the Dragonbone Vale, where the Cult of the Dragon, with the help from the Red Wizards of Thay, planned on making the prophecy of the Chronicle of Years to Come come true. When Valindra Shadowmantle's Rite of the Dracolich was interrupted, Vylxildross faced adventurers and heroes of Neverwinter on the Scaleblight Summit.
