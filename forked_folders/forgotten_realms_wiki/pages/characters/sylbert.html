Sylbert was a healer from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Sylbert was a middle-aged hin man with a light skin tone and chestnut eye color. He had light brown facial hair.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Sylbert spent his days at the Harbourview Park in the Lower City, helping recovery of Twitch Broven, a former Flaming Fist who had still long road to recovery ahead.
