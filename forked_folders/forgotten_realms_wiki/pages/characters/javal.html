Javal was a bandit with the Knights of Athalantar in the early 3 century DR.

Description

He had a red beard.

Relationships

He was a rogue, and worked with the Knights of Athalantar.

History

During the , Javal revealed the team he was a part of had slain two armsmen of Athalantar, to which Sargeth, a Knight of Athalantar, responded with approval, but warned him that should be enough, as it ran the risk of revealing they were working together with the lasses at Daera's.
