Rumblebelly was the horse and mount belonging to Regis of the Companions of the Hall.

Description

Rumblebelly was a fat pinto pony. He was well-trained.

History

Regis acquired Rumblebelly during his time with the Grinning Ponies along the Trade Way (1481–1483 DR). He was named for Regis's nickname, "Rumblebelly," given to him by his friend Bruenor Battlehammer.
