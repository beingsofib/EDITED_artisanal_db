Barze was a Netherese arcanist and later a sorcerer-king of his self-titled Kingdom of Barze.

History

In the , the war-weary Barze and his followers fled persecution in Netheril. They left the Negarath Enclave and traveled east for many months. At the start of winter, they discovered a fertile dale, and it was there that Barze founded his kingdom.

The sorcerer-king was happy An assassin killed him stealthily and without a fight, leaving his kingdom to fall to the Twisted Ones.

Barze's tale would be remembered in part within texts later deemed to be heretical by followers of the Dead Three. As of the late 15 century DR, he was the subject of a chapter in a book called The Great Diaspora of Netheril.
