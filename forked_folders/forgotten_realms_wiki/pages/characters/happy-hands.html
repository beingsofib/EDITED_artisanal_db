Happy Hands was a member of Fetcher's Brats and alter, The Guild, from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Happy Hands was a young human boy with a light skin tone, dark blue eyes, and straw-colored hair. He wore a garb dyed with the Guild's green color and was the brains of the group.

History

In the , Happy Hands was a part of a street urchin gang led by Osgur "Fetcher" Hallorn. When the man disappeared following a break out of a guild war between Nine-Fingers Keene and the Stone Lord, Fetcher's Brats were taken in by the Guild and given shelter in the Guildhall. Sticky Dondo took on the role of the leaders among the kids and Happy Hands took on the role of the group's planner and strategist.
