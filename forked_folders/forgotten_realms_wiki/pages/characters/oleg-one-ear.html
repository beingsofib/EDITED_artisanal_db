Oleg One-Ear was a male human and the proprietor of the tavern called The Dragons' Den in the town of Thunderstone in Cormyr in the late 1360s DR.

Although Oleg didn't plan it, the tavern was taken over by the rowdy Purple Dragons garrison in town. However, he was a practical man who knew to mind his own business.
