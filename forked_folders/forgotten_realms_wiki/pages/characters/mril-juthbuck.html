Mril Juthbuck was a half-elf counter clerk for the Aurora's Realms Shop, South High Road Catalogue Counter in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep in the 1360s DR.

Personality

Mril was friendly to everyone and enjoyed jokes, drinking, and dancing.

Description

She was considered pretty.

Rumors

Her surname, Juthbuck, was normally a halfling name, so Volo thought it strange for one of elven heritage like Mril to bear it.
