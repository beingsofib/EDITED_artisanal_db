Sarlisha Kjenth was a truenamer. She led one of the study groups in the House of the Unnamed in the late 15 century DR.

Abilities

Besides her maturing truenamer skills, she had trained as a sorcerer.

Activities

As of , she led a study group on truespeak.

Relationships

She was one of the study group leaders in the House of the Unnamed.
