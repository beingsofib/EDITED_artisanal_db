Razaar Slissin was a vintner inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Razaar was the guildmaster for the Vintners', Distillers', & Brewers' Guild circa 1357 DR. He worked out of the House of Good Spirits—their headquarters on the Rising Ride in the Southern Ward.

History

Razaar sent his spoiled son, Jurmin Slissin, to present the Vintners', Distillers', & Brewers' Guild's zzar at Mossbridges's First Annual Year-End Festival in the . The vintner hoped the trip would humble and mellow his pampered progeny.
