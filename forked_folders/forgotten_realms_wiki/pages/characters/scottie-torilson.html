Scottie Torilson was the leader of the Sons of Light, a knighthood devoted to Torm in the early 8 century DR. He fought with them during the latter days of the Weeping War. He died in the , while defending a strategically unimportant elven village, because his duty demanded it. He was the possessor of the one true enchanted longsword Daystar.
