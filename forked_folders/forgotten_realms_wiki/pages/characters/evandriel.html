Evandriel was a moon elf Harper agent in Neverwinter in the late 15 century.

History

Around 1487 DR, Evandriel led the Harpers' search for the Cult of Elemental Evil. Locating Aerisi Kalinoth's cultists in the cloud giant fortress of Spinward Rise, she worked with the Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge to drive the cult away from Neverwinter.
