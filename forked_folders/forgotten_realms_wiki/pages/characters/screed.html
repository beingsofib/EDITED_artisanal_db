Screed was a sailor aboard the Wicked Wench fishing and cargo vessel that sailed Maer Dualdon and the Shaengarne River in the early 14 century DR.

Description

Screed was a friendly man with pale skin and dark hair. He dined on knucklehead and often flamed his flatulence on the fish.

History

In the , the Wicked Wench was among several ships that carried reinforcements and hired adventurers from the city of Luskan to Targos, to aid the city's defenses against the Legion of the Chimera. Screed was woking aboard the ship at the time and befriended the group of adventurers who would later become the heroes of the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale.
