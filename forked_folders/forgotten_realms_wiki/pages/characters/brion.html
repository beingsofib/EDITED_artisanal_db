Brion was a young thief that served in the Company of Dawn mercenary company during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Brion had very pale skin color, due to albinism.

Personality

Despite his chosen profession in the adventuring lifestyle, Brion had little experience in battle and was believed to have never killed anyone during his life. He a faithful follower of Tymora.

History

In the , Brion and the Company of Dawn traveled alongside acquaintances of their leader Thurbrand across Cormyr

The trek through the woods led to the end of the Company of Dawn, as its members were killed by the monstrous spiders that dwelled within. Brion was impaled upon a giant spider's legs, Cyric looking on unable to help the dying young man. The vision of Brion's last living moments and tragic death haunted the otherwise jaded Cyric for nights to follow.
