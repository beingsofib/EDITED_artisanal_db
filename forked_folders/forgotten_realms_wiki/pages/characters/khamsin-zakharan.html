Khamsin was a skilled sorcerer who traveled the deserts of Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Activities

As the son of a powerful caliph, Khamsin had a life of leisure and pleasure at his fingertips. Instead, he decided to brave the burning sands of the Land of Fate with desert riders who appreciated his useful spells of wind and sand.

Possessions

Khamsin wielded an enchanted jambiya.
