Maglast was a student of sorcery in the mid–8 century DR.

Description

He was handsome.

Personality

He played spell pranks sometimes, but was one of the last to leave class.

Activities

He was one of the students of the scholars Tabarast and Beldrune.

History

In the , Maglast was one of the students of Tabarast and Beldrune in a school on the Westgate side of Ripplestones, almost certainly Westgate itself, up to the point where they were called away by a Harper giving no name who needed them to track down Elminster Aumar, a Chosen of Mystra, for him.
