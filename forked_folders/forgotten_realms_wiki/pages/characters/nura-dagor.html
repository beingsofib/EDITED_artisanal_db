Nura D'Agor was a necromancer who lived in Calimport.

Description

Nura D'Agor was a thin woman with long blond hair. She tended to walk around in a red dress and large black cape. She was also known to wear a tiara of mind shielding and a ring of wizardry.

Personality

Nura D'Agor was known to be very proud and arrogant, with a temper that easily flared up into violence. She often took pleasure in tormenting any stranger who became infatuated with her.

Possessions

Nura D'Agor was known to wield an antique wand of fireballs that allegedly originated from the Cradle Lands, a fabled place that was said to have once resided upon the Plains of Purple Dust. She also possessed a mirror of life trapping.
