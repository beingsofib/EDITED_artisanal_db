Naarhesh was a traveling dragonborn merchant in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Naarhesh had vibrant green scales and wore a suit of leather armor.

History

Naarhesh was seen stopping by Eveningstar, making grand proclamations about who was worthy of his business. Some of his most expensive goods originated from Myth Drannor.
