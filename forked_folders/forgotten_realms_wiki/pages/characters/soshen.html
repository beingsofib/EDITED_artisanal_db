Soshen was a member of the city guard of Port Nyanzaru during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Soshen was the son of the merchant prince Ekene-Afa and the painter Kura. He was named in honor of the River Soshenstar.

Soshen had a twin brother Tiryk, who was a dinosaur-racer.
