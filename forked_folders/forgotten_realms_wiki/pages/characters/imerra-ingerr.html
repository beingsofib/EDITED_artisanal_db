Imerra Ingerr was the wife of a merchant inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Imerra was suspicious of her husband's sudden lack of coin but did not know of the financial trouble he was in. She distracted herself with music and wine, but usually to no avail.

Relationships

She was married to a merchant named Rokkek.
