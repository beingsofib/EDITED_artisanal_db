Miriam was an innkeep who inhabited Caer Corwell on the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Miriam owned the Red Stag inn. Her establishment was nice and quiet, especially compared to the Boar's Tusk which was quite rowdy. Miriam lived in Waterdeep before relocating to the Moonshae Isles.
