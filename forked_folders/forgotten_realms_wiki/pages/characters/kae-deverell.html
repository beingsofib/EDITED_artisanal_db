Lord Commander Kae Deverell was a human member of the Purple Dragons of Cormyr and of the Harpers who was stationed at the stronghold of High Horn in the mid–14 century DR. He was possessed by the god Bhaal, Lord of Murder, and became his new avatar during the Time of Troubles.

History

During the Time of Troubles in the , he helped the then-human Kelemvor and Midnight, who were pursued by Cyric and his men.

However, Deverell's spirit was not destroyed and was left searching for an entrance to the Fugue Plane in Kanaglym. There, he met Midnight and apologized to her for his stupidity.
