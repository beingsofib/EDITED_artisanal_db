Lorant Tass was a Harper agent who worked in Eveningstar during the 1480s DR.

Description

Lorant had tanned skin, short black hair, and wore a light suit of leather armor.

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Lorant and Kara Patwin followed up on Oriphaun Huntsilver's activities in the Storm Horns, where he was investigating Shadovar plots. The pair were unusual for Harpers in that they were known to the officials of Cormyr, allowing them to work together without subterfuge. Unfortunately, most of those they spoke too simply dismissed their reports as overblown reactions to typical orcish activities and not worth pursuing. They were joined in this mission by Nevyle Dunwaite, Rina the Keen-Blade, Renton Finne, and Kyra Armond, and worked with Swordcaptain Orin Arroway and Sherrod Starym to unravel what was happening. After much effort, it was discovered that not only were the Shadovar raising an army in the Storm Horns, they had found an intact ancient mythallar. They planned to use it with Amskar's enclave to attack Cormyr, but Oriphaun and his allies managed to crash the flying fortress before that happened.
