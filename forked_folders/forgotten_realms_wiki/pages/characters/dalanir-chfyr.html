Dalanir Ch'fyr was a drow diviner and information broker who operated in Luskan under the pseudonym Soronil Noonshadow.

History

His past life was a mystery, and his ability to survive in Luskan despite the enemies his profession made him was also a mystery. Fortunately, Dalanir liked mysteries, and he was a fountain of information for anyone who could pay or work for it.
