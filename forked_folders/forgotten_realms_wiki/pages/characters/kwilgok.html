Kwilgok was a tortle who acted as a guide in Port Nyanzaru in Chult in the late 15 century DR.

History

When Kwilgok was young, he visited the ruins of Dangwaru, and was attacked by the shadows that dwelt there, though he was able to flee in a weakened state.

By the time he was 26 years old, Kwilgok had traversed the River Soshenstar, been to the Aldani Basin and its surrounding region, and led expeditions to Orolunga, though he had never encountered the oracle that was supposed to live there.

Possessions

At some point, Kwilgok acquired a pet ankylosaurus named Deadly Treasure, which he used as a mount from a howdah on its back.
