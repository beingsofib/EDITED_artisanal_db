Ko Lei Kho was the shou governor of Akari in 1357 DR.

History

Ko Lei Kho was a member of mercantile Ko clan that led the first colony in Akari. He ruled from Tai Ko town. The colony prospered until in 1357 the settlers started to being terrorized by ochimo. He asked help to his clan and Ko Mei Kho send him an adventuring party to resolve the matter.

Personality

Lei Kho was always suspicious of all, modest and self-effacing.
