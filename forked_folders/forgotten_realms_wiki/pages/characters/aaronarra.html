Aaronarra was a copper dragon living on the western side of the High Moor, near the Misty Forest, at the end of the 14th century DR.

Description

He was around 700 years old in 1368 DR.

Personality

Aaronarra was known for having a bad attitude. The druid Bara described him as both crotchety and sarcastic. For food, Aaronarra selected creatures whose populations could reasonably be reduced, such as goblins.
