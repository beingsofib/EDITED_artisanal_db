Marril was an elven priest who served Corellon at the Temple of Leaves in New Sharandar during the late 15 century DR.

History

When the settlement of New Sharandar was threatened by King Malabog circa the , Marril accompanied Iliyasha Guidingstar during her diplomatic mission to Neverwinter.

Marril worked with adventurers from the city to locate shards of an ancient Illefarn relic that shattered into many pieces when it was stolen from the forest by fomorian giants.
