Estellia Vizen was a potter of Crack'd Pots in Skullport in 1370 DR.

History

Estellia managed a clay laboratory, known to produce unattractive but very durable ceramics. She said she had such a long life thanks to the clay she collected from a certain place in the Sargauth river.
