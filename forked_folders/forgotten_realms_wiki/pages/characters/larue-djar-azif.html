Larue d'jar Azif of Dhaztanar was a known experimenter of psionics.

Rumors & Legends

By , Larue had experimented with psionic powers and made small observations, including that dimensional door psionic power did not involve instantaneous travel, but instead had a small delay. Larue had also hypothesized that the dimension walk power was not across uninhabited dimensions, but that life in those dimensions may have been too different to perceive and identify.
