Sable was a horse belonging to the Moor Eagle clan of Vaasa in the mid-to-late 13 century DR.

History

Sometime after saving the Moor Eagle tribe and building the Granite Tower for them in the , Melegaunt Tanthul met and became quite fond of Sable His fondness for the horse was such that Vala Thorsdotter was familiar with Sable despite the horse having lived in the time of her great-grandfather, Bodvar. he was saddened to see the horse was not with them, and had to be reminded that she had died more than 80 years prior.
