Murtil Dragonsbane was the King of Bloodstone in the mid-to-late 15 century DR, and the last of the Dragonsbane dynasty.

History

He likely ruled from the assassination of Brianne Dragonsbane in the until the , when he died as a childless bachelor. and indeed Murtil had been killed by Yarin's favorite hitman, Rafer Ingot.

Murtil's reign saw the beginning of the end of the unified Kingdom of Bloodstone when the Warlock Knights of Vaasa rebelled in the .
