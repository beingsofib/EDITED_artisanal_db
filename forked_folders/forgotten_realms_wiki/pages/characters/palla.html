Palla the Light was a mercenary recruiter in Turmish around 1358 DR.

Activities

Palla was the leader of the Mercenaries' Guild of Westgate.

History

Palla founded the Mercenaries' Guild of Westgate, but only as a means to send bored and disaffected members of the Guldar merchant family out of the city.
