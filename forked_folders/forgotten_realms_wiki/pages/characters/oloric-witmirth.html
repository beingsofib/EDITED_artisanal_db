Oloric Witmirth was a down-on-his-luck playwright in Baldur's Gate during the late 15 century DR. He was known to patronize the Elfsong Tavern in the Eastway district of the city.

Desription

Oloric was a human man with a square face, ridden with wrinkles and scars. He had a sun-kissed skin, long blone hair, and icy blue eyes.

Posessions

Oloric kept a small book within which he diarized his thoughts and perspectives on what he saw in life.

History

Oloric fell on bad luck sometime before the , when he bacame unable to write to support his livelyhood. Desperate, Oloric turned to the House of Grief in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate where he went through the Mapping of the Heart ritual to cure his "melancholic heart" and remove the memories of hiw writer's career. After the ritual, Oloric Witmirth could be found the same old Elfsong Tavern, not being able to recognize people he knew before but visibly being happier. He claimed that after the memories of being a writer wer taken away, his problems went away too. He no longer was a wreck, a writer who could not write.
