Zeidero was a Priest-Captain of Tempus who served as the representative of the Church of Tempus in Bezantur, Thay during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He oversaw Bezantur's Temple of Tempus.

Personality

Zeidero was a scarred and grizzled military veteran.

History

Prior to his appointment in Bezantur, Zeidero had participated in dozens of military campaigns across dozens of different lands.
