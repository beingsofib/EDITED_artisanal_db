Andwe Cururen was a half-elf agent of the Lords' Alliance in the 15 century DR.

Activities

Andwe was known to journey throughout northwest Faerûn on behalf of the Lords' Alliance. In her travels, she would find out about adventurers who were suitable to be in the service to her superiors. Cururen also sought out information on existing Alliance locations and members.

History

Cururen was born in Silverymoon and became a Knight in Silver. As of the 15 century DR, she was an emissary and, when times called for it, a diligent agent for the Lords' Alliance.
