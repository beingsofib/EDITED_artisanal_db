Nat was a street urchin from Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Nat was tall and skinny for her age. She was deaf and communicated with her friends by using a sign language that she invented.

Possessions

She carried a wooden toy sword.

Relationships

Nat led the group of street urchins that included her friends Jenks and Squiddly.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Nat and her friends were unwittingly entangled in the city-wide hunt for the Stone of Golorr, sometimes innocently helping or hindering the efforts of adventurers in retrieving it.
