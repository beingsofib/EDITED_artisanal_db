Thurl Northmane was a farmer of the village of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the 14 century DR.

History

Circa 1341 DR, Thurl Northmane joined the effort to drive the local cobbler, wizard, and former Elder of the Council Ssuntyr out of the village for his crimes. But the fight turned bloody, seriously injuring two farmers and leaving Thurl slain.
