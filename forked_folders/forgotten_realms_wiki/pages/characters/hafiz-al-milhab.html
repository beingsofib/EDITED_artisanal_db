Hafiz al-Milhab was an efreeti overseer on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1373 DR.

History

Hafiz was the overseer of a mine in the Elemental Plane of Fire, where efreet put enslaved azers to work. In 1373 DR, his mine was attacked by Kaanyr Vhok and his allies, who had forged an agreement with azer leaders. Hafiz was killed by Kaanyr despite his promise of a better agreement.
