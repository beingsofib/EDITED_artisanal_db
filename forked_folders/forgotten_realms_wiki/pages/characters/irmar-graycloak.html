Irmar Graycloak was an adventurer and leader of the Graycloak's Wolves adventuring company in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Irmar Graycloak and Graycloak's Wolves were in the city of Hlondeth in the Vilhon Reach in the , when they were requested to deliver the Tome of Torment—the most holy book of the church of Ilmater—to Master Sufferer Olbedan in Aralent. However, during the harsh winter, Graycloak's Wolves fell prey to doppelgangers and leucrotta preying on travelers. The tome was thought lost in the winter snows.
