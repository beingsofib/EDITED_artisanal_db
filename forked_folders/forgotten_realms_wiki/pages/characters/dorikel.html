Dorikel was an Adept of the Absolute, stationed in the Moonrise Towers during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Dorikel wore the uniform armor of an Adept of the Absolute – a night-blue armored garb and a cowled high-raising helmet. He had drowic dark gray skin and red eyes..

History

In the , Adept Dorikel was among those who joined the new religion that sprang in the Western Heartlands. He was stationed within the Moonrise Towers, along the stairs towards one of the towers' entrances. The fortress was controlled by the Absolute's warlord – General Ketheric Thorm. Like many other members of the cult, Dorikel's fate remained unknown as he could've perished in battle when the High Harper Jaheira and her True Soul allies stormed the Moonrise.
