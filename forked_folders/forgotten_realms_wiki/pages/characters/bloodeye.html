Bloodeye was a werewolf and leader among the lycanthropes of the Black Blood tribe on the island of Moray in the Moonshae Isles during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

He was ambitious, aggressive, viscous, and moved quickly to pursue his goals. In combat, he fought with great savagery and would prioritize attacking lightly-armored opponents. He was known to take prisoners and inflict them with moon frenzy.

Abilities

Bloodeye could freely alter his form between that of a human and that of a gray wolf. His bite could transmit the moon frenzy disease, although he himself was immune to its effects.

Possessions

He dressed only in shredded pants,

History

In the , Bloodeye began terrorizing the village of Langmuir, and over the course of a few months, he became increasingly bold in his strikes against their mining caravans. Bloodeye intended to use these explosives first to destroy the lighthouse critical for Langmuir's supply lines, and then to breach the village's fortified walls.

Putting his plan into action, Bloodeye seized the lighthouse and lured members of Langmuir's militia into a trap where he infected them with moon frenzy, rendering them little more than mindless minions.
