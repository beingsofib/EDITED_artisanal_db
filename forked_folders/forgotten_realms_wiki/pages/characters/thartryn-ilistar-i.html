Thartryn Ilistar I was the third King of the Ilistar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate.

Activities

Thartryn I began his rule in the , when his father, King Belhendar, died. Thartryn ruled until the , when he was deposed and killed by the cambion Iyachtu Xvim who then assumed the role of King.

Relationships

Thartryn I died before siring any offspring. He had one older brother.
