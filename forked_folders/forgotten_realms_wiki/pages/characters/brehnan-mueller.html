Brehnan Mueller was a Cormyrean man that lived in a small house west of Calantar's Way during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

Brehnan was married for a time before his wife passed away circa the late 1340s DR. He raised their daughter Annalee as a single parent for nearly a decade at least.

History

During the Time of Troubles, in the , Brenhan discovered a young magic-user by the name of Midnight alone and unconscious during a terrific storm. He took the young woman to his home and cared for her over two weeks' time.
