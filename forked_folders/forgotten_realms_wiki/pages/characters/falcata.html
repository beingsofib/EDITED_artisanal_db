Falcata was a wood woad that lurked in Uthe Lodge during the late 15 century DR.

History

Falcata defended Uthe Lodge along with the other wood woads Robur and Prinus, around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar. They protected the dryad Sylvie, who was the source of the plant overgrowth and had been subverted by the druid Halsaime through the ranger Peron Uthe.
