Aliss Thondren was a Royal Undertreasurer of Suzail who lived circa 1148 DR.

History

Aliss Thondren's most notable legacy was inventing the term "sneak-coins". The term became a common slang that referred to Sembian cabals, organizations, or even syndicates that were trying to wrestle control over Cormyrean businesses and economy via bribery, blackmail, and other illicit ways of changing minds of government officials or noble houses representatives.

The term was still in use in Cormyr by the royal War Wizards as of 1348 DR.
