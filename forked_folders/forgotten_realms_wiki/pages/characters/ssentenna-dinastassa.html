Ssentenna Di'Nastassa was a beautiful yuan-ti cleric of Sseth in Samarach in 1372 DR.

Activities

Ssentenna was a yuan-ti from the Sauringar tribe who, under orders of the powerful undead jahi, managed the secret cabal of the Harim of the Jahi.
