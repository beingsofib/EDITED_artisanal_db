History

At the Open Feast held at Castle Obarskyr in Mirtul 26 of the , Essard asked why there still was a haunted wing at the Royal Palace of Suzail, and why it hadn't been un-haunted with spells or faith. It was explained to them that it had been tried, but it just wouldn't take.
