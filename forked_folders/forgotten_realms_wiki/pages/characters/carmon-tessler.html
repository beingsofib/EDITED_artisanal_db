Carmon Tessler was a low-ranking priest of Chauntea who served the House of Bountiful Harvest temple dedicated to the The Golden Goddess in Ravens Bluff's Temple District of Ravens Bluff in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Carmon was one of the three lowest-ranking serving priests at the House of Bountiful Harvest under the leadership of High Harvestmistress Arabella Ieruin of Chauntea by the . The other two were Melissa Drover and Penelope Heartbow.
