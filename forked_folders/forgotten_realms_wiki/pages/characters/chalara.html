Chalara was a news hawker who worked for the Baldur's Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Chalara was a very pale-skinned human child with mousy-blonde hair and brown eyes.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Chalara peddled Baldur's Mouth Gazette in front of the Blushing Mermaid tavern in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate.
