Balanta Whiteshield was a skilled warrior and follower of Lurue during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Balanta was one of the original members of the Knights of the Unicorn. She led one of the affiliated groups of the Order of the Unicorn following the Time of Troubles.

History

She was originally from Baldur's Gate on the Sword Coast.
