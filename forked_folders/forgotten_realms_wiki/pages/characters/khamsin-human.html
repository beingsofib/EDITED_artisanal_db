Khamsin was a Zakharan camel-rider and sorcerer who lived in Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Possessions

He wielded an enchanted jambiya, and wore the garments of the desert riders, riding a camel through the harsh deserts of Zakhara.

Relationships

Though the son of a caliph, Khamsin had spent much of his adulthood in the company of desert riders, and they in turn had come to rely on his abilities with wind and water spells.
