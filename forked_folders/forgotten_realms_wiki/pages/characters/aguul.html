Aguul was a ogre printer who lived in the city of Procampur in the Vast in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Oddly for an ogre, Aguul served as an apprentice printer to Forgemaster Inkstain, a dwarf, in the Year of the Helm, 1362 DR. He endured the dwarf's complaints and grouching.

Description

Aguul was thickly muscled and quite hairy, with warty arms.
