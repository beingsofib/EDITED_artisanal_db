Lord Murvyn Eldarr was a noble of Zhentil Keep in the 13 century DR.

Description

In Mirtul of , he had the beginnings of some stubble during a meeting with Lord Chess.

History

In Mirtul of 1334 DR, he joined a meeting with Lord Chess, where he asked him about a maid the latter had bedded. Per Chess' recollection, that was actually the Shadowsil.

Reputation

Per Manshoon's appreciation in the , Lord Murvyn Eldarr was a self-entitled, witless, irresponsible caricature of an old nobleman who could be safely ignored and crushed just as safely.
