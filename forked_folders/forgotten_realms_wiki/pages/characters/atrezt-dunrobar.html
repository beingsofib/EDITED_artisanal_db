Master Scout Atrezt Dun'Robar was a drow soldier that served House Dun'Robar during the 1480s DR.

Description

Atrezt had his white hair in a mohawk, and wore a suit of plate mail.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Atrezt commanded an Underdark patrol close to the portal to the Eveningstar cavern.
