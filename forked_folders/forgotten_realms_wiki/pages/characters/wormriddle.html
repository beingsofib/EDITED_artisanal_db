Wormriddle was a night hag who lived in Undermountain in .

Activities

Wormriddle had a workshop at the magical school at the Dweomercore level of Undermountain.

Wormriddle was also a teacher at Dweomercore. She was second in command of the school, stepping up as headmaster when necessary.

Personality

Wormriddle had a debilitating fear of cats and other feline creatures.

Relationships

Wormriddle commanded a group of four flesh golems. When in dangerous situations, she polymorphed as a young halfling girl named Medley. She kept a collection of magical dolls that resembled each of the students at Dweomercore and contained a sample of each of their bodies, such as hair or other corporeal secretions. The dolls could be used to inflict pain or harm on the students.
