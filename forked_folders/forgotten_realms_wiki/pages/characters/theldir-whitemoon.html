Theldir Whitemoon was a male sun elf psion shaper and an older member of the Kaliesh'erai, an association of psionic elves in Evereska.

History

Around 1374 DR, Theldir Whitemoon had emerged as the leader of a conservative contingent within the Kaliesh'erai. These were older members who preferred seclusion and separation from the outer, non-elven world.
