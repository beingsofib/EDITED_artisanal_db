Fenthaza () was a yuan-ti malison nightmare speaker and high priest of a yuan-ti cult devoted to Dendar the Night Serpent.

Posessions

Fenthaza owned a powerful enchanted item called the Chain of Scales. The broken chain was recovered from her corpse in the late 15 century DR and restored to full power by the adventurers who sought to end the death curse.

History

Fenthaza belonged to a cult of yuan-ti that had founded the Fane of the Night Serpent, a temple hidden beneath the palace of Omu. As high priest of Dendar, Fenthaza performed the ritual to transform Ras Nsi into a yuan-ti malison, though she later began doubting his role as cult leader. This led to Fenthaza planning a coup to end Ras Nsi's rule.
