Melann Brandish was a cleric of Chauntea at the Bounty of the Goddess temple in Archendale in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Melann had long dark hair, tied away from her face. She wore a leather and a brown cloak for protection while traveling. She was never found without her wooden holy symbol to Chauntea around her neck on an amulet.

Relationships

Melann had a single sibling, her brother Whitlock Brandish.

History

Circa , Melann and her brother went on a quest to try and lift a deadly curse that was cast on their family by Chare'en.
