Rand was a druid and sometime mercenary in Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Rand was an elderly halfling man with a thick white beard. He wore an outfit of hides and furs.

Personality

Rand was stubborn, grumpy, and rather unimpressed by those around him.

Abilities

Rand was a druid of some power, able to cast call lightning, creeping doom, enveloping swarm, and ice storm.

Relationships

Rand didn't care for Dom and his goals at all, only focusing on finding his lost nephew Stiev.

History

The old druid spent much time trying to find his nephew Stiev, and was manipulated into joining Dom's mercenary force thanks to the promises of Dom helping find him. This arrangement came to an end when Risen Netheril hired Dom to seize a piece of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll from the Harpers, attacking Bensen Virandson as he transported it down Greenweald Lane. Though he took part in the battle, Rand refused to fight to the death for a cause he didn't care for, and simply surrendered to let the Harpers through.
