Jhoysil Samprava was a courtesan at the Purple Palace festhall in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep and a member of the Red Sashes in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. She was the twin sister of Aletha Samprava. She and her sister were known contacts for the Red Sashes by 1357 DR and through 1367 DR.
