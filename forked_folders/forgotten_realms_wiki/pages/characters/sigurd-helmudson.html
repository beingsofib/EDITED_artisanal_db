King Sigurd Helmudson, known as "the Wise", was ruler of Gnarhelm in the Moonshae Isles during the early-to-mid 14 century DR.

Activities

Sigurd Helmudson ruled over the whole of the realm of Gnarhelm from his capital in the city of Gnarhelm.

History

Circa , King Helmudson and his soldiers unintentionally left the city of Garhelm undefended, leading to a raid by pirates who seemed intent on razing the town. However, the city was saved by the fortunate intervention of King Thelgaar Ironhand of Oman and King Grunnarch the Red of Norland, who arrived by longship while traveling with Elminster just as the raid began. Their berserkers made short work of the pirates, and captured any survivors. The next king to arrive on the scene was Rolf Olafsson of Olafstaad, who rode through the night upon hearing of the pirates and knowing that King Helmudson was at sea.

When King Helmudson died in the , it was Rolf Olafsson who assumed his throne.
