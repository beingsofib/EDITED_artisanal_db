Parise Ulfbinder was a lord of Netheril.

Relationships

Parise Ulfbinder was friends with Draygo Quick, another Netherese noble. For a time Parise wondered if Drizzt Do'Urden was the Chosen of Lolth, although he later rescinded that idea in favor of Dahlia Sin'felle being favored of Lolth.

Parise was also acquainted with Lady Avelyere, with whom he watched the re-acquaintance of the Companions of the Hall on Kelvin's Cairn on 19 Ches 1484 DR.
