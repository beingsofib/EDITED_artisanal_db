Thorst was a caravan drover of the Sword Coast in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was somewhat unfriendly.

Abilities

He struggled with bows.

Relationships

He liked Shandril, though he had his suspicions about her.

History

In the , while working for Orthil Voldovan, Thorst was promoted to caravan drover. Orthil also knew he had been between Shandril Shessair and the burned body of the late guard Storstil, which helped him figure out Shandril hadn't been the killer. He fought alongside Shandril in protecting their wagon afterwards. He was badly injured during the fight, but spoke up in Shandril's defense, as she resorted to spellfire to fight back against his assailants and protect him.
