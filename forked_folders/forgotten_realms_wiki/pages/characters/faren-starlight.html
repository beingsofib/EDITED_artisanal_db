Faren Starlight was a Zhentarim enchanter who was the "keeper" of the black dragon Harondalbar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Faren was a very attractive woman of average height. She had long black hair and emerald green eyes. Her skin was pale to the point of giving her a near ethereal appearance.

Activities

Faren excelled at her role since Harondalbar was somewhat fascinated with her. Given the dragon's often stubborn nature and refusal to rouse to confront any non-Zhentarim caravans moving through Yellow Snake Pass, Faren often resorted to creating her own illusions of Harondalbar to warn off unwanted traffic.

Possessions

Faren carried a staff of power, bracers of defense, a brooch of shielding, and a pair of winged boots.

Abilities

She usually had the following spells memorized – burning hands, charm person, color spray, comprehend languages, detect magic, feather fall, sleep, shocking grasp, spider climb, phantasmal force, alter self, bind, deafness, fog cloud, insatiable thirst, summon swarm, Melf's acid arrow, Tasha's hideous laughter, explosive runes, hold person, lightning bolt, spectral force, and suggestion.

Relationships

Faren was an apprentice of both Manshoon and Sememmon.
