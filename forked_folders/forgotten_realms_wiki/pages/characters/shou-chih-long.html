Shou Chih Long was a korobokuru thief in Shou Lung in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Shou Chih was a cutpurse in marketplaces around 1357 DR, working for the 108 Society Tong and sometimes acting as an informant for the Ministry of Security. If in a good mood, he also occasionally acted as a tour guide.

Personality

Shou Chih never stole from other korobokuru, believing this a dishonorable act.
