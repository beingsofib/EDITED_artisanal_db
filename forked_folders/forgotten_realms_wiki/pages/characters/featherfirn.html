Featherfirn was a notably large and dangerous snowy owlbear dwelling in Dragonscar Gorge as of the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Only those accustomed to the desolate and snowy Dragonscar Gorge could recognize the signs of when Featherfirn was nearby. It acted almost like a natural guardian of the Gorge, and was known to attack intruders. This included members of the Frosthunger Horde as well as any adventurers.
