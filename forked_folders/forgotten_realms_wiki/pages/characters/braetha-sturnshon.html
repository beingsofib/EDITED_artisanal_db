Braetha Sturnshon was the eldest daughter of Hlargrim Sturnshon, who along with her eight sisters worked at the Dryad's Dreams, the only festhall in Mosstone, Tethyr in the mid-to-late-14 century DR.

Description

Like all the Sturnshon sisters, Braetha had long silky brown hair, skin the color of ivory, and large cheerful dark eyes. She wore the Dryad's uniform, a sewn leather gown resembling that of an actual dryad, a dress made out of green-dyed leather leaves.

History

Braetha, Kalaeria, Charethra, Hestroone, and her five other sisters all worked at the Dryad's Dreams with their father. The women were in charge of serving food and drinks to the customers and entertained them, giving rubdown to sore muscles of foresters and rangers at the end of a long workday. The women greeted the clients with cheery attitudes and affectionate body language. On occasion, they perform a limited assortment of medical procedures, such as setting dislocated joints or fingers in for the locals.
