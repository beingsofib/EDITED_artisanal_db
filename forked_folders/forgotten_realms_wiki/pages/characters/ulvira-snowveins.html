Ulvira Snowveins was an elderly half-elf in Skullport in the late 15 century DR.

Circa 1492 DR, Ulvira ran the Feathered Rat pet shop on the Lower Level and was a spy for the Harpers.

She used to be an adventurer, but settled down with adventurer partner Salmarin Bearfriend and held the shop together around 1369 DR. They also had two loyal blink dogs called Yuff and Yapp.
