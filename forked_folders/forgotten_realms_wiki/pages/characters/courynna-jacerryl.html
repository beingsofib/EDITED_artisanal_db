Courynna Jacerryl was a high priestess of Selûne. Within the clergy of Selûne, she held the rank of High Initiate and oversaw seventeen young clerics, including Maera.

She and her followers once performed a "night stalk" ceremony in honor of the goddess, who was pleased and produced moonfire. Courynna called this a blessing and directed her followers to gather the sacred substance.
