Beau was the pseudodragon familiar bonded to Methrammar Aerasumé, a wizard and fighter who served as the Argent Legion's High Marshal, by the late 14 century DR. The two were inseparable, with Beau often accompanying his master into danger. Luckily, the tiny dragon-kin was typically protected by Methrammar's spells and a magic item that Beau kept in his possession.

Personality

Beau's unpredictable actions were somewhat at odds with Methrammar's affection for the creature, with his behavior sometimes baffling even his master.

Abilities

Natural

As both a pseudodragon and a familiar, Beau had traits typical of such creatures. These included, but were not limited to, the ability to speak telepathically, so long as Beau's target spoke either Sylvan or Common, to perpetually see invisibility, and even to maintain an empathic link between him and Methrammar if the two were within of one another. Through his stinger, Beau was also capable of delivering a sleep-inducing poison.

External

Beyond his own, natural defenses, Beau was often protected even further by Methrammar's powerful magic. The specific spell chosen, however, could vary. Beau's master sometimes went as far as casting a magic circle against evil or kept it as simple as a basic protection from arrows.

Possessions

Beau counted on his ring of protection +2 as his sole piece of equipment, which helped keep him safe if ever he found himself in terrible danger.

Relationships

Being Methrammar's familiar, Beau became a kind of constant companion to the half-elf, remaining at his side frequently—even with danger present. Methrammar showed a deep affection for Beau, protecting him with powerful magic and keeping him safe.
