Badiat bint Malin was an influential sha'ir who lived in Kadarasto in 1367 DR.

Activities

Badiat may have been an ally of the Yikarian Empire, working to further the goals of the yak-folk in the Cities of the Ancients. She directly opposed Khedive Aman al-Qasi abu Nari's goals and policies.
