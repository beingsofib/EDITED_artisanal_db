Isbeth was a giant black wolf kept by the Zhentarim that operated within an underground trading post hidden deep within the caverns underneath Waukeen's Rest, during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Isbeth spent much of her time protecting his human Jarg. He often expressed frustration at Jarg's regular pacing and retracing of his steps.

History

Isbeth reluctantly followed Jarg around as he helped clear out the Zhentarim hideout in the , for risk of having their operation upended by the Flaming Fist of Baldur's Gate.
