Duke Folkwain was a portly mercenary commander and Lord Woren's second-in-command in the city of Nimpeth in the Vilhon Reach circa 1370 DR.

History

By the , Folkwain was tasked with the security of his home city, Nimpeth, one of the Free Cities of the Vilhon Reach. The noble fighter oversaw Nimpeth's many hired mercenary companies, used as guards, militia, and law enforcement by the city's government. This included Duke Folkwain's own Company of the Crushed Grapes, a special force under his control that included over 250 footmen and 150 cavaliers.
