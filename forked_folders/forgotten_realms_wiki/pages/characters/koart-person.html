Lord Koart ruled some of the communities within a few hours' ride of Caer Corwell as of the mid–14 century DR, notably the cantrev of Koart.

Description

He was a short and vigorous Ffolk man.

Activities

Lord Koart sat on the council of King Bryon Kendrick of Corwell, alongside Lords Dynnatt and Nowll.

History

During the Darkwalker War of the , Lord Koart participated in the Battle of Caer Corwell. As Corwell Town was evacuated, his troops fled. This forced the remaining defenders to fall back and caused Lord Dynnatt to be mascaraed.
