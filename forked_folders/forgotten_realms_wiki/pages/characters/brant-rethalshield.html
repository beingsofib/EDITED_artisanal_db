Brant Rethalshield was a human priest of Lathander in the late 14 century DR. He was an aspirant to the Order of the Aster and was the squire of Grayth Holmfast.

Description

Brant had sandy-blond hair and wide blue eyes.

Equipment

Brant wore the orange surcoat of an aspirant to the Order of the Aster.

History

Brant was killed on Ches 16, the , when a canoloth bit off his arm. None of the accompanying re-formed Company of the White Star could get to him quickly enough to save him.
