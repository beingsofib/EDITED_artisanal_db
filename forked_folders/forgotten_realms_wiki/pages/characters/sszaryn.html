Sszaryn was a drow from Sshamath that joined the Cult of the Absolute during the late 15 century DR.

History

Sszaryn was among the devotees of the Absolute tasked with abducting Grand Duke Ulder Ravengard from Waukeen's Rest in the . While the cultists burned down the inn and managed to capture Ravengard, Sszaryn and several others in their group were slain by the contingent of Flaming Fist soldiers staying within. Sszaryn was shot down by an archer from the inn's rooftop.
