Kemble "the Great Father" Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid–5 century DR.

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the first son of his predecessor, High King Carrig II. His successor was his own first son, Tristram Cymrych.

History

Kemble assumed the throne of the High King in the , after the death of his father.

In the thirty-sixth year of his reign, a large group of Tethyrian immigrants arrived in the Moonshae Isles. Kemble welcomed them, and they settled among the Ffolk. This introduced mainland religion to the Ffolk as well as Faerûnian methods of architecture and engineering for building strong defensive fortifications."

Kemble ruled for fifty-one years before dying of natural causes in the .
