Lady Isabella Bormul was a silver merchant and a member of the noble patriar Bormul family of Baldur's Gate, active sometime in the 14 or 15 century DR.

History

Lady Isabella Bormul was a supporter and patron of the Counting House in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. She made significant donations to the bank, including seven cartfuls of Amnian silver. Her donations earned Lady Bormul a place in the Counting House's Record of the Honored sometime before the .
