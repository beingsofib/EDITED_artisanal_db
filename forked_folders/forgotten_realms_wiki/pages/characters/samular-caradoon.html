Samular was a human knight of Tyr during the 10 century DR, and the founder of the Holy Order of Samular.

Relationships

Samular was the youngest of three brothers. The oldest was Renwick Caradoon who became a wizard and the middle brother Amphail the Just became a paladin as well.

History

Caradoon founded the Holy Order of Samular after becoming famous for his heroic actions in the Second Trollwar, which ended in 952 DR.
