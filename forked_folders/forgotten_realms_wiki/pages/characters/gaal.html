Gaal was the high priest of the Cult of the Eyeless, who was zealous in his worship of the Unseeing Eye around 1369 DR.

History

Gaal was a wizard and cleric who went to the sewers of Athkatla's Temple District to worship an ancient beholder. From here on, he ruled over the followers of the Cult of the Eyeless. He sung the praises of his "deity", preaching that without his eyes, the beholder was stronger.
