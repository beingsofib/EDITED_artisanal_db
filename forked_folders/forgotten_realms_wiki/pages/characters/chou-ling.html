Chou Ling was a human warrior of Kara-Tur in the 14 century DR.

Activities

He was a faithful servant of the oni and tried to win their favor by any means. He would grow murderously jealous if others got too close to the creatures and threatened to take his place.

Possessions

Chou Ling was known to wield an enchanted +2 long sword and wore padded armor.

Personality

His unwavering loyalty to the oni stemmed from his awe and and fear of the oni's powers.
