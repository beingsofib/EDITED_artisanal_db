Fist Koltz was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Koltz was a human man with dark skin and neatly trimmed bark brown beard. He wore a set of chainmail armor decorated with a tabard embroidered with the Flaming Fist insignia and a simple uniform helmet. Koltz wielded a spear and a large shield when on duty.

History

In the , Fist Koltz was stationed in the Basilisk Gate's barracks and prison, serving under Blaze Liara Portyr and Manip Seyama. His Gauntlet supervisor caught Koltz sleeping on duty, for which he was punished by being forced to train half to death.
