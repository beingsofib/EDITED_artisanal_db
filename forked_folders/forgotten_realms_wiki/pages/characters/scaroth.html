Scaroth was a lich from the world of Oerth and one of the opponents of the hero Scaldulin.

History

Sages of Toril knew about some of Scaldulin's feats and heroics on a distant world of Greyhawk, including the hero's stand against the dreaded lich Scaroth the Warped. Scaldulin used his powerful enchanted sword Hoarfrost Blade to slice the lich's hand and defeating the undead creature.
