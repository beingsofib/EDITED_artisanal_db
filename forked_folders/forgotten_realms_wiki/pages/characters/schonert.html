Schonert was a priest of Ilmater in Waterdeep in 1479 DR.

History

Around 1479 DR, Schonert noticed that many residents of Downshadow had disappeared while rumors had spread of a treasure in the area. He decided to hire an adventuring party to uncover the truth.

Activities

Schonert usually sent food and supplies to the Downshadow folks.
