Gandolar Luckyfoot was the leader of the halfling community of Gullykin circa the late 1360s DR.

Personality

He was a warm and welcoming individual that appreciated good manners and treating others in a polite manner.

Activities

Gandolar and his fellows in Gullykin often lent their aid to those adventurers that sought to prove themselves in the depths of the nearby Durlag's Tower.

History

In the , the folk of Gullykin suffered regular raids by kobolds led by an ogre mage that originated from the ruins of the Firewine Bridge. Gandolar sought out adventurers to keep safe his people and help their village remain free from harm.
