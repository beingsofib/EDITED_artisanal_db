Babadul was a dao fugitive inhabiting the Great Dismal Delve on the Elemental Plane of Earth in 1367 DR.

Activities

Babadul was a wanted man for sabotaging work on the mosque of the Forgotten God. He was pursued relentless by several yikaria and their dao servants.
