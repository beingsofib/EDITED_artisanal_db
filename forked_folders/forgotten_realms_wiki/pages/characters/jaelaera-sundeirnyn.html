Jaelaera Sundeirnyn

Person
nameJaelaera Sundeirnyn

Jaelaera Sundeirnyn was a young sorceress in the mid-to-late 13 century DR. She had used her beauty, charm, and poison to kill more powerful mages and increase her power and influence. At some point prior to , she challenged the Trapmaster directly and slew him. She then fought his apprentice, Ohland Grethgar, who narrowly defeated her.
