Binnefein Simfray was an old scout of Bregan D'aerthe in the early 14 century DR.

History

Binnefein was a very old drow scout and a member of the small House Simfray. One day in a terrible battle his life was saved by Zaknafein Do'Urden but he remained scarred.

After 1018 DR, Binnefein joined Bregan D'aerthe.

In 1313 DR, Binnefein was among the scouts in the Bregan D'aerthe's mission to Ched Nasad headed by Beniago and Zaknafein to follow a House Hunzrin expedition. Binnefein reported the exact numbers of the members of the Hunzrin expedition to Beniago.
