Corladror Silversword was a nobleman from Cormyr. He was one of the heads of the Silversword house during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

He was the head of House Silversword.

History

He attended the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the . While there, he was among those who stood up suddenly at the sight of the ghost of the Regent Alusair Obarskyr.
