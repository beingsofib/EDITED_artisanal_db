Ursala was the name offered by a werebat hunting along the trail near Stag Falls in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Abilities

Ursala possessed normal werebat abilities.

Relationships

She hunted together with the werebat Minsk, posing as his bride; they may or may not have been married.

History

Ursala may have been one of the werebats accompanying Minsk after the latter was accidentally shunted out of Ravenloft by Romanisk Vlaensk's magic. She may have tried to hunt an adventuring party together with him.
