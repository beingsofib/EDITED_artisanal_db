Xog was a mind flayer inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the late–14 century DR.

Activities

Xog lived in secrecy within Sunset House within southern Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of the Foreign Quarter. He served as an advisor of a small contingent of drow of House Mizzrym and reported directly to Kebella Mizzrym.
