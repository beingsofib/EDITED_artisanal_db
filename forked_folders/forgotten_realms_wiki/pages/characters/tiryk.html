Tiryk was a popular dinosaur-racer in Port Nyanzaru during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Tiryk was the son of the merchant prince Ekene-Afa and the painter Kura. He was named in honor of the River Tiryki.

Tiryk had a twin brother Soshen, who was a member of the city guard.
