Alkindus was a Netherese arcanist who performed their research at the Shadow Consortium before its destruction in .

History

Following the disappearance of the institute's founder, Shadow, Alkindus performed experimental research into the planes at the head of a small team. Focusing on the unlikely possibilities of semiplanes and antiplanes, she was aided by Janin, Pringle, Vanderanus, and Shiris Blamira.
