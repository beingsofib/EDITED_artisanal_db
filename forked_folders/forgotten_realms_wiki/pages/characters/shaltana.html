Shaltana was a woman who worked at the Nighthunt inn, which stood on the Dawn Pass Trail a day's ride east of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. She was the wife of the innkeeper, Ildur Arntar.

Relationships

She and Ildur Arntar had four daughters, two of whom were restless and keen to become sword-wielding adventurers themselves. She aided Ildur at the inn in the mid-1360s DR.
