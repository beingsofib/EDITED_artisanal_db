Sequaxion was a treant who lived and worked in the garden of the For-Rest Inn in the city of Ravens Bluff in the 14 century DR.

Description

Sequaxion was an oak type of treant and stood roughly tall.

Personality

Sequaxion was a very cheerful, intelligent, and blissful individual.

Activities

Sequaxion worked as a gardener at the For-Rest Inn, tending the plants around it. He almost never wandered away from the garden, as the city generally did not allow his kind to walk about freely. He also tended to move around at a slow pace, having been told by the inn's owner that humans didn't like trees that moved. As there were no other trees close to him, he sometimes animated the inn itself.

Relationships

Sequaxion was very good friends with the owner of the For-Rest Inn, Thistledew Vine, ensuring that her customers had plenty to eat.
