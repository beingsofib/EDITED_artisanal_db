Lady Kessemer was a noblewoman in the late 14 century DR.

History

Lady Kessemer was known to be hatching some plot in the early 1370s DR. In the , Vangerdahast surprised Queen Filfaeril Obarskyr of Cormyr by mentioning he knew of Lady Kessemer's plot, while discussing another issue.
