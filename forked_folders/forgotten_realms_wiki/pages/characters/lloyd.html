Minister Lloyd was the mayor of Imnesvale circa the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Lloyd was somewhat of a worrisome individual. Whenever the town was presented with a problem he was fearful that the worst possible outcome would arise.

Possessions

Among the mayor's possessions was an enchanted suit of leather armor called the Night's Gift.

History

When a rash of killings and disappearances sprang up around Imnesvale in the , Lloyd sought assistance from outside his little village. At first he hired a group of adventurers led by the brave Mazzy Fentan. When that group went missing he sent a messenger Delon to seek help in the capital city of Athkatla.
