Quysszt was a Zhentarim beholder.

Activities

Usually Quysszt floated in the Zhentarim's headquarters, protected by an invisibility spell, spying upon the councilors under orders of Manshoon.

History

In 1358 DR, after the failure of both Avaerl and Zalarth Bloodbrow, Manshoon decided to go himself and kill Elminster Aumar at Spellgard. So the High Lord of the Zhentarim ordered the wizard Belaghar to take care of Zhentil Keep and ordered Quysszt to control and eventually kill him and others if any treachery or misjudgments occurred during Manshoon's absence.
