Bazelsteen was a cornugon harbormaster in Avernus, first layer of the Nine Hells, in the late 15 century DR.

History

Bazelsteen was appointed by the archdevil Zariel to direct one of her docks on the River Styx. However, by the , he had grown frustrated at not being permitted to do his mechanical experiments with infernal war machines.
