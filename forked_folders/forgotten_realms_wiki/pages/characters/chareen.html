Chare'en

Person
nameChare'en
homeAbyss
raceBalor

Chare'en was a balor of the Abyss. He was part of the Army of Darkness that attacked Myth Drannor. He raised a huge gnoll army in the Thunder Peaks, before being defeated and imprisoned.
