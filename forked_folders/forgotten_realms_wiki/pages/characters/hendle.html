Hendle was a druid from Rashemen and an old member of the Emerald Enclave in 1489 DR.

Personality

Hendle was saddened by the loss of treants in forests.

History

In 1489 DR, he was the Emerald Enclave delegate sent to help a hired adventuring party to defeat the great dragon Claugiyliamatar of Kryptgarden Forest. The Enclave's main objectives were not to destroy the forest in the midst of the battle against the Cult of the Dragon and to ask the help of the fey lords of the forest.
