The Rook was an infamous thief who inhabited the city of Waterdeep during the early 14 century DR..

History

The Rook maintained a hidden headquarters on a small alley that branched off of Kulzar's Alley in the Sea Ward. He eventually perished here while fighting members of the City Watch who discovered the hideout. The alley was later named Rook Alley because of this.
