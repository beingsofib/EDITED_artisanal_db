Lodevin Parkar

Person
nameLodevin Parkar
homeCormyr
occupationScribe, writer

Lodevin Parkar was a scribe and writer of Cormyr who authored the Camber Fosrick detective stories, including The Adventures of Camber Fosrick, in the 1360s DR.
