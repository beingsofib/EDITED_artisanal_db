Kumon was a chieftain of a marauding orc horde that sought to destroy dwarven King Taik's underground kingdom.

History

Kumon lived long before 1372 DR. He led an orcish horde against dwarves of King Taik. His reign of terror however was ended when the King decapitated Kumon with the Blood Frenzy.
