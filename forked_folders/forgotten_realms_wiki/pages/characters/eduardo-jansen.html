Eduardo Jansen was a member of the gnomish Jansen, active in the late 14 century DR.

History

Eduardo was known for his intentions. One of his inventions was proudly displayed in Athkatla's Center District, at the center of Waukeen's Promenade. Eduardo's flying machine was a small device with four wings, big enough to accommodate one person. The winged machine was displayed next to another gnomish flying machine – a boat attached to a big balloon.
