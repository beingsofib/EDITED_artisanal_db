Sklaen Jhavander

Person
nameSklaen Jhavander
homeRed Larch

Sklaen Jhavander was a harness-maker in Red Larch.

Sklaen Jhavander made wares for use with the wagons made at Thelorn's Safe Journeys, or for independent customers.
