Hessperina was a medusa who fought as a gladiator in the Underdark Arena during the 1480s DR.

Abilities

Hessperina preferred to fight at range with her bow, as well as using her petrifying gaze.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Hessperina was a champion at the Underdark Arena controlled by Azena'ar the drider, alongside Oolgnim the mind flayer. She likely perished fighting a team of superior fighters.
