Envir Sykensdottir was a member of the Uthgardt Gray Wolf tribe during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Envir was the daughter of Syken Nightblaze, former Great Chief of the Gray Wolf tribe.

History

Sometime around the , Great Chief Nightblaze was slain by adventurers that hailed from the city of Neverwinter. Envir planned her revenge and sought to assault the city and regain the tribal authority that was taken from her father.
