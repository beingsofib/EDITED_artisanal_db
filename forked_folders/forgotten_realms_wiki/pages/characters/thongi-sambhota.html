Thongi SamBhota was the abbot of Lagiluri during the mid–14th century DR.

Appearance

Thongi wore an orange wool robe and a leopard pendant. Like all monks, his head was shaved.

Activities

The abbot ran a small monastery within the village of Lagiluri.

Abilities

Though he possessed a nice variety of clerical powers, during combat Thongi preferred to use spiritual hammer and spell immunity.
