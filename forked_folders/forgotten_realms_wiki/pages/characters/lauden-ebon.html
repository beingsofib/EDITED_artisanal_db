Lauden Ebon was a Dark Justiciars of Shar and a member of the Gauntlet of Shar in the 10 century DR.

History

Ebon was among hundreds of Dark Justiciars and Selûnites who died in a bitter battle between the followers of the two goddesses in the . Justiciar Ebon was wounded and bled out in battle, and the casualty was recorded in a long log of the dead penned by Malus Thorm of Reithwin.

As of the , Lauden Ebon's death was long forgotten, being just one of an endless list of the dead, stored in Malus Thorm's House of Healing in the shadow-cursed town of Reithwin.
