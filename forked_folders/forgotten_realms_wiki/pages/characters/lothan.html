Lothan was an aquatic dwarf cook who lived in Ravens Bluff Harbor in the 14 century DR. He worked at Sharkey's Bar & Grill, being the only aquatic dwarf on its staff of nine cooks.

Possessions

Lothan wielded a short sword as his weapon of choice.
