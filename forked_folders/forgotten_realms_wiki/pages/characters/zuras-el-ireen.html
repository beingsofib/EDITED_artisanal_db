Zuras el Ireen was a human thief, former pirate, shipping businessmen, and lastly the Grand Yrshelem of Ships of the Rundeen mercantile organization in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

An older man, he was quiet by nature but had a hard appearance about him.

History

Zuras started his career as a pirate in service to the Rundeen. When he became bored of life at sea, he asked his masters for permission to join a shipping business.

In five years, Zuras had risen to become the leader of that shipping business and so, with the blessing of the Rundeen, he started a new shipping business in another city using the experience he'd acquired. With his business skills and his pirate contacts, Zuras was able to dominate much the shipping trade in that city.

It was only natural for the Rundeen leaders in the , to promote Zuras to join them as Grand Yrshelem of Ships when his predecessor died of old age. Slowly, Zuras reduced his involvement in his former company until he was all but retired by 1370 DR, puttin it in the control of his heirs.

Activities

As Grand Yrshelem of Ships, Zuras oversaw all shipping business for the Rundeen, as well as many of their pirate subsidiaries. He had them work in coordination to affect the desires in the competition. He also kept an eye out for new trade routes and new ship designs they could use.
