Rumar was a well-to-do man who lived in Athkatla during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

He was a regular patron of the Copper Coronet tavern located in the River District of Athkatla. He made regular use of the tavern's "back rooms" in order to continue an affair with his lover Priss.

Relationships

Rumar was married to a woman named Tiana while simultaneously maintaining relationship with Priss.

History

In the , Tiana caught wind of Rumar's dalliances and went to the Coronet to confront him over his affair.
