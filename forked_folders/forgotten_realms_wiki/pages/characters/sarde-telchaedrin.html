Sarde Telchaedrin was a nobleman of Halruaa in the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

His uncle was Meleghost Telchaedrin.

History

In the late 14 century DR, Sarde Telchaedrin hired the Brightstar Sash to go after a renegade heir of another house of Halruaa, whom he claimed was creating a bloodtaint spell that would work untold destruction.

Rumors

His uncle, Meleghost, summoned one of the members of the Sash, Caladnei, to come to his house for a talk. She arrived not long after; during their talk he explained that his nephew was tricking them into striking at a rival house and setting them up to take the fall.
