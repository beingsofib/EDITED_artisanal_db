Jathun was a caravan guard of the mid-14 century DR.

History

In late , Jathun was a guard working in Orthil Voldovan's caravan bound for Waterdeep from Scornubel, which Shandril Shessair and Narm Tamaraith joined; during an ambush, Narm appeared to panic, resulting in Orthil having him knocked out and bound. Per Arauntar, Jathun was the one who knocked Narm out when it became necessary to restrain him during an ambush.
