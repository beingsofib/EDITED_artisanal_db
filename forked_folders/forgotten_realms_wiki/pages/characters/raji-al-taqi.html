Raji al-Taqi was an elven moralist who followed the teachings of Najm in Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Activities

Distressed by what he viewed as the fallen state of his pragmatist brethren, Raji believed that his actions would show them the true path to worshiping adventurous Najm.

Possessions

Raji wore armor of the desert evening and a shield of the holy. He wielded a mace in combat.
