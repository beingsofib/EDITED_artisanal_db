Felyndiira T'orgh was the head of the College of Illusion and one of the members of the Conclave of Sshamath in the city of Sshamath in the year 1372 DR.

History

In 1377 DR, Q'arlynd Melarn told the priestess of Eilistraee Miverra that Felyndiira was a slippery and oily individual.

Rumors said Felyndiira worshiped Lolth in secret.

In 1379 DR, she supported Guldor Zauviir's proposal to promote T'lar Mizz'rynturl, Master of Bae'qeshel Magic.
