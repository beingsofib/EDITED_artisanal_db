Badiyah al-Sa'id al-Sahim was an elf and a skilled flame mage living in the Free City of Qadib in north Zakhara in .

Activities

In addition to running the University of Flame Mages, Badiyah was also a member of the Sultan's court.

Rumors & Legends

Badiyah was rumored to be a secret member of the Brotherhood of the True Flame.
