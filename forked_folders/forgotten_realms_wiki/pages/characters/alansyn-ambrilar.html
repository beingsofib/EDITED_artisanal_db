Alansyn Ambrilar was a male human cleric of Lathander, the Morninglord, in Tantras in the Vast around 1358 DR.

Ambrilar was High Morninglord of the Morning Halls temple to Lathander at the time. He led fourteen priests and twenty-one lay-followers.
