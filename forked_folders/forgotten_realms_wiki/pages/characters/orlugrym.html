Orlugrym was a Red Wizard in the mid-14 century DR.

Personality

He was lustful enough to not feel disinclined from his pursuits even after knowing his mind to have been probed.

Abilities

He could feel probes into his mind, and had at least one spell that froze a target, or commanded them.

History

During the mid-14 century DR, Orlugrym was sent on a mission to retrieve something from The Simbul's study. On the way there, after feeling his mind probed, he decided to instead take the robes from one of her female apprentices, whom he had spotted; after enspelling her and commanding her to bare herself in his arms, she turned out to be the Simbul herself. She killed him with silver fire.
