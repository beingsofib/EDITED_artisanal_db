M. V. Zapolsky was a Raumviran who lived in Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the .

Activities

Zapolsky was the proprietor of the Trader's Rest, a caravanserai located near the center of Almorel. His establishment's location along the Golden Way was well-placed to intercept travelers.
