Sorcha MacTire was a woman famed for being the most pragmatic and courageous lover, active sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.

History

By the late 15 century DR, Sorcha was well known for inspiring many sentimental poems comparing her to various geographical features. Another thing that immortalized her in Faerûnean culture was a popular hairstyle named after the woman.
