Vol was a member of the Zhentarim that operating within secluded caves hidden beneath the Risen Road in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

She had a no-nonsense attitude much like her superior Zarys.

Relationships

Vol was often accompanied by the massive wolf Indra.

History

When Zarys ordered the Zhentarim hideout to be demolished in the , Vol was among those members that helped set out explosives and gather all their loot.
