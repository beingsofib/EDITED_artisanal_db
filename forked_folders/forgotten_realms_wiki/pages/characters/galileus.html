Galileus was an astrologer and diviner who performed his work on the Sword Coast in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

As could be expected, Galileus spoke in generalities and offered vague predictions to those who asked. He often got a crick in his neck from starting up at the skies for so long.

Activities

Galileus spent his time studying heavenly bodies, gathering raw data from their positions, and making predictions based on that information.

History

At some point in the , he was seen passing by the town of Beregost.
