Captain Janros was a wood elf officer in the Neverwinter Guard who served his home city in the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

He worked with a number of skilled members of the Guard, including Sergeant Creed, Scout Gaelen, and Private Quickfoot.

History

Janros was a veteran soldier.

Following the Siege of Neverwinter in the , Captain Janros was tasked with defending Neverwinter against the orcish occupiers of the Many-Arrows kingdom under commander Vansi Bloodscar. He led the guardsmen in first containing the orcs and then reclaiming the River District from them. He had soldiers guard the gates and man the walls, and barricaded off parts of the ruined district so they could protect the other districts, but this left the Guard's numbers stretched thin. Thus, Janros regularly recruited adventurers to target important orc leaders and disrupt their war effort.

During that assignment, Sergeant Creed asked an adventurer to find lost dispatches from slain Neverwintan couriers and complete their delivery to Janros. From these, Janros managed to uncover the whereabouts of a holdout Nashers cell led by Karr Nevrim, who colluded with the orcs. Hence, to prevent him undermining the city's security, Janros dispatched the adventurer to invade their sewer base and slay Karr.

Afterward, Janros worked with the same adventurer to check the barricades and district walls to ensure the orcs were not breaking through, recover their stolen supply crates, and eliminate key warband leaders within the Many-Arrows hierarchy, such as their taskmasters and Kartok Paingiver, who'd fortified the old Merchant Guild Hall. Meanwhile, he accepted the Proudfist clan's defection and had half-orc adventurers target spellscarred orcs.

With the Guard and the city secured and the orcs weakened, Janros was ready to strike back. He asked the adventurer to go behind orcish lines to meet Private Quickfoot and Scout Gaelen, who would give them more missions striking the orcs' core. Finally, a team of adventurers penetrated the Cloak Tower, so they could defeat Vansi Bloodscar, and her lieutenants Makred the Foul and Throg the Ravenous, finally ending the war.
