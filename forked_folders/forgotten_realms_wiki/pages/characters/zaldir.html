Zaldir was a drow spy from Menzoberranzan in the late 15 century DR.

History

Around 1479 DR, Zaldir was one of the many spies sent from an alliance of noble houses of Menzoberranzan to observe the elf wizard Etherik, who was plotting to kill every drow of the Underdark using a new kind of construct. However, he was captured by Etherik and subjected to terrible tortures that drove him insane. Thereafter, he mumbled incessantly.
