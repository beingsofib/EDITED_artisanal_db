Zen was an adventuring Evereskan elf monk active in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Zen was a charming, good-natured, and easygoing man.

History

The elf was active across the Sword Coast in the latter 1400s DR. Harper agents who kept an eye on Zen stated that the man used an obvious alias, possibly to conceal his Evereskan heritage and avoid his parents' disapproving gaze and the "rightful destiny" they had prepared for their heir. Free-natured, Zen had his own ideas of what his life should be and instead of settling into the future shaped by the family, he took on a mantle of an adventurer, traveler, eater of good food, and imbiber of sweet wine. Despite the appearances of a careless adventurer, Zen spent his time accumulating contacts within the mercantile and hospitality worlds of the Sword Cost. His other activities during that time included collecting draconic lore and rumors. The Harpers who watched the young elf stated that they witnessed a brief but friendly meeting between Zen and an elven lass who called him a "Dragon Tamer." The significance of the title or nickname was undiscovered.
