Whittlee was a young woman of Phlan that worked as a server at Madame Freona's Tea Kettle during the late 15 century DR. She was one of five daughter's the restaurant's owner and proprietress, Madame Freona.

Description

She had stark white hair that she kept tied back in a braid.

Relationships

Whittlee's other sisters included Blaizette, Briez, Grelinda, and Reece.
