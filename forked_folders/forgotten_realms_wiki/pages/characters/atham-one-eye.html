Atham One-Eye was a blacksmith from Suzail, the capital of Cormyr, active in the early 14 century DR.

History

Atham One-Eye was the proprietor of his smith shop, located to the south of Suzail's Market, on the bank of the Dragonmere. By the , Atham was out of business and his smithy was taken over by the Purple Scar festhall.
