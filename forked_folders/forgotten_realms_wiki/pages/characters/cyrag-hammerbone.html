Cyrag Hammerbone was a dwarf of Illefarn circa 1363 DR.

History

Cyrag was a veteran warrior in the High Moor before joining the reconstruction effort in Illefarn. In 1363 DR, he was chosen to lead the Illefarn troops during the Second Dragonspear War.
