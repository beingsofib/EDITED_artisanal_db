Brawler Rot was a goblin member of the cult of the Absolute who dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Rot had orange skin and long brown hair, that went down to his feet. He wore a leather cuirass and crude bracers adorned with bones.

Possessions

His preferred weapon was a crudely-made goblin scimitar.
