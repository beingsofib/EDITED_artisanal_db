Daegon was a human druid of the Storm Horns during the late 15 century DR.

History

Around the time of the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, the forces of Risen Netheril took over part of the Storm Horns as Archwizard Amskar built his army. They caused a good deal of environmental damage as they went, part of which fell upon the druid Daegon, who lived near Yarrow Glade with his beloved owlbears. He fought off the invaders with mounting fury and eventually began simply attacking anyone who approached, whether they were hostile or not.
