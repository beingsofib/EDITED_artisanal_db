Venger was an ancient half-fiend human sorcerer and archmage from the Realm.

History

Once a goodhearted man, he fell to evil and turned against his father after encountering a force of evil.

He lived to be over a thousand years old.

Description

He was a tall half-fiend human male who wore blood-red robes and a headdress with a single crescent-moon-shaped horn protruding from the right side. His face was pale white with sharp fang-like teeth.

Abilities

He was resistant to acid, cold, electricity, and fire and wholly immune to poison.

Possessions

In his possession were a bead of force, an elemental gem to summon fire elementals, a potion of barkskin, a potion of cat's grace, and a metamagic rod for maximizing spells.

Relationships

He was the son of the Dungeon Master and twin brother of Karina.

He rode a nightmare and employed a 'shadow demon' as a spy.

Venger had an old and bitter rivalry with Tiamat, the queen of chromatic dragons.
