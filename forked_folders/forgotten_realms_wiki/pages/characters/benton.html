Corporal (later Sergeant) Benton was the quartermaster of the Dagult Neverember's City Watch in the 1480 DR Neverwinter.

Description

The solder had his head shaven and thin dark beard.

Possessions

When protecting Neverwinter, Benton dressed in chainmail and carried a longsword. His blue surcoat with the symbol of Neverwinter covered his armor.

History

In the 1480s DR, Benton was made a sergeant. He joined Doomguide Vesper Soulhearth and a contingent of Kelemvorite priests in a task to defeat the undead that took control of the village of Ebon Downs.

Behind the Scenes

Benton gave to the players the first side quest of the game, until it was cancelled.
