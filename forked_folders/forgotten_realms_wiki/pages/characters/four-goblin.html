Four was a goblin child that lived in the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

You think you're hard. Mess with us, and you'' end up like this bleeder. Four, referring to the corpse she was desecrating.

Personality

Four was quite belligerent even for a child, let alone a goblinoid one.

Relationships

She looked up to her mother, believed she was "tough as worg-meat" even before their lives were blessed by the Absolute.

History

Four was present when her companion Eight's parents were killed at their camp's raid of Waukeen's Rest in the . She consoled her friend and avenge his parents' death by helping kick and punch the corpse of the man that killed them.
