Melek was a woman of Calimshan in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

She was the younger sister of Nurih yr Catahra al Suhal, a moneylender of Teshburl and member of the Rundeen in Calimshan.

Melek was married to Roffas Calindor of Tethyr, who was a member of the Knights of the Shield, by 1370 DR.
