High Captain Kayl Moorwalker is both the First Elder of the Council of Elders and the commander of Everlund, as of the .

Personality

Kayl was an intelligent military commander who preferred to look after the interests of the nobility rather than commoners and merchants. As a result of this, he was looked upon favorably by the elves of the Council of Elders.
