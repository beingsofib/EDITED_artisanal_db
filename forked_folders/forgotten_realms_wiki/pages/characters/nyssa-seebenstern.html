Nyssa Seebenstern was a caravan leader in the Dalelands.

Personality

Nyssa was full of enthusiasm and idealism.

History

Nyssa was a woman of Theskian ancestry. She was well known in the Dalelands for her caravans.

In 1479 DR, while traveling with her caravan to Daggerdale, Nyssa was attacked by gnoll raiders.
