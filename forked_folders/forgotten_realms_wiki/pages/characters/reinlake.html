Lord Reinlake was a nobleman of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

He wore a monocle.

Relationships

He was acquainted with Lord Parespur Bloodbright.

History

Lord Reinlake was a guest to the Open Feast in Mirtul 26 of the held at Castle Obarksyr, where he briefly conversed with Lord Parespur Bloodbright and praised the beauty of the lord's escort, the mask dancer and coinlass Amarune Whitewave.
