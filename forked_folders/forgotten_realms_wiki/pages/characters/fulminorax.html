Fulminorax was a blue dragon allied with Valindra Shadowmantle in 1484 DR.

History

To fulfill the terms of their alliance, Valindra agreed to send Fulminorax into the Feywild to help King Malabog to destroy New Sharandar. However, Fulminorax was killed by some adventurers.
