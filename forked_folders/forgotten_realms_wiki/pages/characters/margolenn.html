Margolenn was a gnomish commoner from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Margolenn was a fair-skinned rock gnome with brown eyes and hair. He wore a blue tunic of quality tailoring and a skullcap.

History

In the , as the Cult of the Absolute loomed over the city of Baldur's Gate, Margolenn could often be seen gossiping in the Lower City with his friend – Bigmouth Thimsen. Margolenn's favorite topic was the Baldur's Mouth Gazette and its handsome illustrations.
