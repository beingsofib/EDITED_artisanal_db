Alura Durshavin, also known as Pennae. was a thief and member of the Knights of Myth Drannor in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Alura was a sleek young woman in dark leather. She was short and slender with glossy black hair cut short in a "helm-bob". She had large, liquid dark eyes.

Personality

Pennae was a pragmatic, albeit cynical woman. Quick-witted, she was often sarcastic and blunt.

Relationships

As one of the earliest members of the Knights of Myth Drannor, Pennae held close ties with much of the adventuring group. Before joining them, she was also friends with Martess Ilmra, who also joined the Knights.

History

Alura was born in Arabel to a pastry cook. Her father was unknown.
