Lady Calantar was a noblewoman and matriarch of the Calantar family in the late 14 century DR.

History

As their matriarch, she represented the Calantars at a meeting with the Obarskyrs during the , during the ghazneth crisis and the ensuing Goblin War. Participating in the meeting, she made little comment until she protested Tanalasta Obarskyr's seizing of each house's enchanted items, and the commandeering of three-quarters of their retainers. Immediately, she was executed on Tanalasta's orders for the crime of fostering treason, to her great disbelief.

The meeting would later be known as the Council of Iron.

Relationships

Her nephew was Ravos Calantar.
