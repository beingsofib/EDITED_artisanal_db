Pharaulee was an innkeeper in Scornubel.

Abilities

She apparently had the ability to cast her sight from other people's faces, in the process displaying the face of an old woman on top of theirs.

Description

The old woman's face that peered out of the faces of other people when she looked through them may have been her own. If so, she had old, sad eyes, and a lushly beautiful face, large and gray-haired.

Relationships

Pharaulee was usually contacted through Rildra, and protected by Ulburt. After Rildra's death, she was contacted directly, at least to Marlel.

Activities

She was the innkeeper at the Stormy Tankard in Scornubel, charging three silver pieces per night. She was almost certainly a Harper.

History

Pharaulee was a contact for Marlel when he brought Shandril Shessair and Narm Tamaraith to the Stormy Tankard. He meant to talk through Rildra, but had to deal with Pharaulee directly instead, as Rildra had recently perished. She was also the innkeeper at the Tankard.
