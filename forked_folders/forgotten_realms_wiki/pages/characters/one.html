One was the name of a goblin child that dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Despite his few years on Faerûn, One developed something of a sadistic side and felt glee as other creatures suffered.

Relationships

Along with the other goblin younglings of the sanctum, One was given a number rather than a name to distinguish him from his peers.

History

In the , One and several other goblin children went down to torment a giant bear when it was brought to the dungeons of the Shattered Sanctum. Little did they know that bear was actually a transformed archdruid named Halsin.
