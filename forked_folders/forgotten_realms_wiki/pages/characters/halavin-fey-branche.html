Halavin Fey-Branche was the Matron Mother of House Fey-Branche in the early 14 century DR.

History

The fall of House Do'Urden in the , led to the ascension of House Fey-Branche from the ninth house of Menzoberranzan to the eighth, granting Halavin a seat on the ruling council.
