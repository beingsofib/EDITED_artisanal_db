Rarendon was a priest of Chauntea in the mid–8 century DR.

Description

He was old.

Personality

He was kindly, but confused.

History

Rarendon was evicted from the Shrine of the Sheaf in Buckralam's Starn around , when the bandits of the Iron Fox took over the town. He lived in the old stables behind the local mill since then.

After the Chosen of Mystra Elminster Aumar liberated the town from the Iron Fox, he told a local woman, Immeira, to take Rarendon to Saern Hill.
