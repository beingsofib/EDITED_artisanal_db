Timmis Taern was a ruler of Edenvale and a bloodforge mage in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR). Timmis ruled from Edenvale Castle and was known to be prideful but lacking in tactical ability.

The leader of the Legendary Campaign, who sought to unify and pacify the Utter East through conquest, lured Timmis beyond the castle walls through simple trickery before striking in a bloodforge battle.
