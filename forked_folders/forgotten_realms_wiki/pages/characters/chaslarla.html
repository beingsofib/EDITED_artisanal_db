Chaslarla was a street thief of Athalantar in the early 3 century DR.

Description

She was fat and old.

Activities

Chaslarla was a thief.

Relationships

She was a founding member of the Velvet Hands thieves' gang.

History

Chaslarla was part of the meeting of thieves where the Velvet Hands gang was founded and local thief Farl was selected as its head. Shortly after, the Moonclaws gang decided to slay the new gang. On the first night after the fight, she was accounted for as lost by fellow thief Tassabra, also of the Velvet Hands.

Fellow Velvet Hands member Eladar the Dark, actually young Prince Elminster Aumar, later meant to avenge her, which strongly implied the manner of her loss was a fatality.
