Clancy was a human inhabitant of the City Core of Neverwinter in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Clancy was a muscular human man with a shaved head and his arms tattooed.

Activities

Like so many inhabitants of Neverwinter, Clancy was harried by the Wailing Death plague that ravaged the city in the , despite staying in the City Core district that was relatively unaffected by the disease.
