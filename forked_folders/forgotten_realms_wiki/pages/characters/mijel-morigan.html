Mijel Morigan was a distinguished mage and retired adventurer who lived in the city of Ravens Bluff, during the late 14 century DR, with his wife Jandra and daughter Jelima. Within the city's Swordspoint neighborhood, Mijel and Jandra ran the Kettle of Many Things restaurant, a delightful establishment that specifically catered to wizards and their familiars.

He was known for brewing an excellent mead.

History

For some time, Mijel focused on selling arcane components from a small shop he established in the north end of the Kettle, Morigan's Complete Components. However, competition from Bendekar's Mercantile forced him to refocus his efforts, and instead sell spices to local cooks and housekeepers.
