Embro Cymrych II was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the early-to-mid 7 century DR.

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the son of his predecessor, High King Embro I. His successor was his first son, Alec. His second son, Samuel, would also go on to become High King after the death of Alec and of Alec's son.

History

Embro II assumed the throne of the High King in the , following the death of his father.

During his reign, he managed to establish a temporary peace with his Northmen rivals.
