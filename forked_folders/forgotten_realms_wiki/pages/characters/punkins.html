Punkins was a child from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in late 15 century DR.

Description

Punkins was a human boy with pale skin skin and blue eyes.

History

In the , Punkins spent his days playing with fellow children of the Gate, Cinnamon and Slow-Butt. The kids played steel watchers and the spies of the Absolute. Punkins was assigned the steel watcher's role who was to find the spies and find smuggled candy in the children's pockets. In his role, Punkins loved snitches.
