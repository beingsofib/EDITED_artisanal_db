Dannbo Indigo was a refugee child who found shelter in the Rivington neighborhood of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Dannbo was a human boy with a dark skin tone. He had dark brown hair and eyes. He usually wore pauper's garb and his face was covered in sweat and dirt.

History

In the , an army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldur's Gate, displacing countless refugees in the process along the way. Dannbo Indigo and his father found their way to the Rivington district outside the city walls, but, like all other refugees, they were refused entrance.
