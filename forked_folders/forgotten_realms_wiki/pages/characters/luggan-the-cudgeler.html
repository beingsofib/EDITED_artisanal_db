Luggan the Cudgeler was a hill giant for hire who served the interests of Risen Netheril in the late 15 century DR.

History

Following the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon in 1484/1485 DR, Luggan came to work for Archwizard Amskar, an ambitious Shadovar who found a mythallar in the Storm Horns and used it to create a flying enclave. Luggan served as muscle for this operation, and fought at Yarrow Glade when the Netherese ambushed Oriphaun Huntsilver and his Harper allies. Luggan was aided by a number of gnoll and shadar-kai soldiers, as well as swarms of animated objects, but proved insufficient for the task.
