Laranlor Lathlaeril was the last ruler of Ardeep in the early 7 century DR. He was one of the three kings of Phalorm until his death.

History

Lathlaeril became the Laranlor of Ardeep in the , after the previous Laranlor, Ellatharion, vanished without a trace into the High Forest with his army as they routed a retreating force of orcs after the siege Secomber.

Lathlaeril himself died in the Battle of Firetears in the , which was fought against the Everhorde south of Triboar.

Legacy

After Lathlaeril's death, Ardeep remained an elven kingdom in name only; much of the elven population decided to depart for Evermeet by the .
