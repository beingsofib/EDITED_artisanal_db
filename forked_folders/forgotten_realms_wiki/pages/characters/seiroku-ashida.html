Seiroku Ashida was a fighter in Kara-Tur in the 14 century DR.

Personality

Seiroku desired to destroy many evil creatures in order to please his god, and he followed the lessons of the priests in his village to this end. He was highly determined and dedicated to his training.

Possessions

He wielded a +3 sword and a +2 polearm and wore banded mail and a cloak of displacement. He also had a necklace of missiles and a potion of invulnerability.
