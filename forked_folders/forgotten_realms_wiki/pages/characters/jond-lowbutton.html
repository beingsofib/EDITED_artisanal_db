Jond Lowbutton was a halfling who traveled the Sword Coast North in the late 15 century DR.

History

In 1491 DR, Jond stayed at the Stonehill Inn in Phandalin. During his stay, a swarm of cranium rats assaulted the inn. When the swarm descended upon the establishment, Jond's survival instinct led him to seek refuge in the inn's courtyard privy, where he concealed himself. The halfling witnessed the initial arrival of the vermin. He hid there until a group of adventurers which later gained fame as the Champions of Neverwinter set him free. Jond mentioned that the rats moved and acted with an unnatural, coordinated intelligence, leading him to firmly believe the swarm was guided by dark magic, behaving as if controlled by a malevolent external force.
