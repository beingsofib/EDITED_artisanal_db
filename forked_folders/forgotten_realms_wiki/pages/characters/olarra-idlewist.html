Olarra Idlewist was a Harper agent who, for a time, operated out of the Lonesome Tankard in the town of Eveningstar in the 15 century DR.

Description

Olarra was a human woman with dusky skin, white eyes, and white hair. She wore her Harper pin and carried a lute and heavy crossbow.

History

In the 15 century DR, Olarra Idlewist posed as a minstrel patron of the Lonesome Tankard in Eveningster, scouting out potential heroes who passed through the village and offering them membership in the group.
