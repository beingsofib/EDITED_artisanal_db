Lukan Dargren was a Count of Tethyr in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

He was succeeded as count by his brother, Kelmar Dargren.

History

Lukan Dargren was slain at some point prior to , and his body was found in the woods near the city. His brother inherited the title.

Rumors & Legends

It was rumoured that his brother Kelmar was the one who killed him.
