Rieger Carrathal was High King of Callidyrr for a brief time in the mid-to-late 13 century DR. Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles, he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty and was the second son of his predecessor, King Teldin. His eventual successor was his first son, Benjamin.

History

Rieger assumed the throne in the , after the death of his father. He ruled for only two years before inexplicably disappearing without a trace in the . His subsequent fate and date of death were not known to scholars.
