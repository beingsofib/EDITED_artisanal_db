Landrin was a gnome resident of Beregost during the mid–14 century DR. Her home was located just west of the Jovial Juggler inn.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR Landrin's home became infested with giant spiders. She traveled to Baldur's Gate in order to buy some poison, presumably to kill the arachnids, and stopped off at the Friendly Arm Inn. While there, Bentley Mirrorshade offered her temporary lodging until the problem was dealt with.
