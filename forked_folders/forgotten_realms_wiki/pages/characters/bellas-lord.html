Lord Bellas was an inhabitant of Cormanthyr by the 3 century DR, and a member of House Bellas.

History

On the year , Lord Tassarion was part of a covert meeting against the Coronal Eltargrim. He had concerns about the execution of the Coronal, and who'd do it.
