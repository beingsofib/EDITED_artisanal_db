Suelasta was a collector of exotic animals who lived in Tajar in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara in .

Activities

Suelasta's menagerie was located near Al-Adib River within the city. Though he strove hard to collect an impressive variety of animals and monsters from all over Al-Toril, his safety practices were somewhat lacking, resulting in the occasional breakout of a savage creature within Tajar.
