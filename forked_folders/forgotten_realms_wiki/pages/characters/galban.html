Galban was one of the Blades of the Old Man as of . On the night of Tarsakh 25, he was on guard duty at the Fortress of the Old Man together with Bennig, who was sleeping in fits. After seeing the greenish light of Berun's starstone he woke Bennig up and the two met up with Jerumillis. They pursued the light into the Gallery of Stone Faces, where Jerumilis was attacked, and then they found Lurom's body.
