Indolrere Moravan was a mage of the Zhentarim in the 14 century DR.

History

He was defeated by Onlagar Blurnwood and his apprentice, Talatha Vaerovree, sometime prior to ; they chased him out of the house.

Rumors & Legends

Talatha Vaerovree had reason to believe he'd spied on her since their last encounter.
