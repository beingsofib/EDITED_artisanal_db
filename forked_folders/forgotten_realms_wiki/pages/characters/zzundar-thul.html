Zzundar Thul was a retired warrior inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Zzundar Thul was the guildmaster for the Guild of Watermen during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He held the title "Master of the Harbor," but this was strictly a guild title and not recognized as an official position within the city of Waterdeep.

Relationships

His son Jaster also worked within the Guild of Watermen.
