Xhost was a treacherous glabrezu demon.

Personality

Xhost was happy to deceive even beings who helped him, through offering them cursed items such as the Gift of the Demon.

History

In the , Xhost was discernible using the Spectacles of Spectacle in Avernus, and could potentially be summoned to that infernal realm.
