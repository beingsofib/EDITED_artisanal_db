Frey Silverblade was the leader of the 3 company of High Guards of Waterdeep during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

While she was an artful commander, Frey would readily defer to a more experienced and knowledgeable military leader.

History

In the , Frey and her 3 company of Waterdhavian guardsmen were patrolling the lands south of the city when they heard the call to arms raised by the militia of Daggerford. which culminated at Dragonspear Castle.
