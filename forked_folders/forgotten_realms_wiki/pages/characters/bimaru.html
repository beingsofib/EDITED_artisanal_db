Bimaru was the ambassador of the kingdom of Bertan on the isle of Bawa in 1357 DR.

Activities

Bimaru was a mysterious woman. Rumors said that she was taken from her monastery in Bertan only to fit her current assignation. She was very devoted to her queen and usually remained isolated in her quarters.
