High Priest Inrimmon Othnil was a priest of the Rack of Glory, a temple dedicated to Ilmater, in Yhaunn in 1358 DR.

Activities

Inrimmon was the leader of Ilmater's faithful in Yhaunn. His congregation within the temple consisted of 7 other priests and 11 followers.
