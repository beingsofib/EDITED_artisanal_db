Mujibur was tasked slayer genie who lived within the fortress of Krak al-Mazhar in 1367 DR.

Activities

Muhibur served Nar-Aidiya and the Brotherhood of the True Flame guarding and tending the Great Garden of the fortress. A quartet of copper automatons worked beneath him.
