Eined Datharathi was a member of the Datharathi chaka of Vaelan in Durpar in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

She was the older sister of Warian Datharathi. and Warian lost an arm in a mining accident, Eined encouraged her brother to see the world.

History

In the spring of , she fled her home, suspicious that something was controlling her family members through the crystal in their plangent bodies. During this time, she found Ususi Manaallin and Iahn Qoyllor outside of the Body Shop; she quickly approached them for help in shutting down the crystal's production.

Eined Datharathi was slain by her grandfather, Shaddon Datharathi, in Tarsakh of 1374 DR after allowing the two Deep Imaskari with her inside Shaddon's chambers. Her uncle, Zeltaebar Datharathi, wanted to elevate her to the status of a family saint after her death, while Ususi Manaallin meant for her name to be remembered amongst the greatest heroes of her people.
