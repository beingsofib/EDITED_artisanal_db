Sartrove Kalisskryl

Person
nameSartrove Kalisskryl

Sartrove Kalisskryl was a priest of the church of Oghma, called a Namer, based in the city of Athkatla in Amn in the late 15 century DR. By around 1480 DR, Sartrove had gained great prosperity from work in an arts or entertainment industry.
