Larth was an avowed who frequently guarded the gates of Candlekeep.

Personality

Outwardly he portrayed a cheery and good-natured attitude.

Abilities

He was a skillful linguist, and a keen judge of character.

Description

He had a habit of rubbing his hands.

History

Larth was among the monks that greeted Roablar of Lantan, actually Noumea Cardellith, during their entrance to Candlekeep at some point after spring started in . He was eager to learn of the guest's mysteries.
