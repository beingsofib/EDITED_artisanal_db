Anhurtep was an incarnation of the god Anhur who inhabited Mulhorand centuries before the mid–14 century DR.

History

Anhurtep led Mulhorand through an age of expansion, founding several cities in land that would be later known as Thay including–Bezantur, Tyraturos, Amruthar, Delhumide, and Nethjet.
