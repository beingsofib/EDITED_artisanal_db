Albitur was a pit fiend under Geryon's command in the mid-late 14 century DR.

History

Albitur participated in the counterattack against Halaster while he scoured Avernus, trying to rescue Elminster Aumar in , when he was the youngest pit fiend in service of Geryon (and the most ambitious). He was the head of the first assault, of which none survived.
