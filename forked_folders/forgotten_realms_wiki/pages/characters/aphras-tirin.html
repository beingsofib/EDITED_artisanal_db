Aphras Tirin was a drow merchant in Mantol-Derith.

Relationship

the drow tried to approach Zarell Tahaladar numerous times in order to buy her spellbooks, but she would not collaborate.

History

Around 1479 DR, Aphras sold spell components and alchemical items.
