Frizzerfrazz was a young gold dragon living in the Aphrunn Mountains in Turmish in 1358 DR and 1374 DR.

History

In 1358 DR, Frizzerfrazz was attacked by a group of wyverns controlled by Muktar's army. He was saved by his parents Wizzerrazz and Izzerazz, possibly with the assistance of some adventurers.
