Tharmoraera was a pleasure-lass of Marsember in Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

History

In early spring of the , Tharmoraera attended a secret meeting of the Rightful Conspiracy in Marsember. She was known to the others and was considered low-born. While there, she engaged in amorous activity with a member of the Thorntower family. Others remarked on this to a disguised Elminster.
