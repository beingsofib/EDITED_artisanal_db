Xzand're Hsyl'ran () was a member of Bregan D'aerthe active in Waterdeep during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Xzand're was an clever and creative woman, but suffered from overconfidence.

Possessions

Xzand're possessed a hat of disguise, which looked like a red silk ribbon embroidered with gold thread. She used it to take the appearance of Meggin the innkeeper.

Activities

Xzand're ran a smuggling ring in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep. In addition she managed the Ship's Prow tavern under the guise of Meggin, one of her victims.

Relationships

Xzand're was deeply loyal to Jarlaxle Baenre, but lost his favors. In an attempt to regain his good graces, she distributed false treasure maps to thwart his competitors while he was looking for Dagult Neverember's dragon cache.

History

Circa the , Xzand're assassinated Meggin in order to take over her inn. She kept the young woman's corpse salted, in a barrel, within a hidden room in the the city's sewers below her shop.
