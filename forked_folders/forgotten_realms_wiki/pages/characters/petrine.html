Petrine was an elven girl that lived in the city of Baldur's Gate with her uncle for a time during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

Although the exact circumstances were unknown, Petrine's parents were no longer in her life and she lived with her uncle Bheren, who she found to be quite mean.

Petrine had a cat named Angel that she believed watched over and protected her from harm.

History

In the , Petrine ran away from her uncle's home but lost Angel in the process. The cat had returned to their previous home in the Upper City to retrieve its favorite trinket, an angel skin ring.
