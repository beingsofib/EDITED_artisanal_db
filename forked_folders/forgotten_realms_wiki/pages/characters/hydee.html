Hydee was a member of the Xanathar Thieves' Guild in Skullport around 1370 DR.

Activities

Hydee was a cleric of Loviatar and acted in a supportive role to a Hand's team. Usually her role was to cast silence to the area of action, but often she could not resist entering the fight. Hydee was infatuated with Master Colstan Rhuul, whom she had met a few times, but she was not as beautiful as his usual companions of Colstan. On one occasion, Hydee killed a female wizard whom Colstan chose instead of her.
