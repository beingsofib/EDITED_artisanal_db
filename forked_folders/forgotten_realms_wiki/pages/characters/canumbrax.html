Canumbrax was a shadurakul (shadow canomorph) bounty hunter in 1374 DR.

History

Circa 1374 DR, Canumbrax was hired by Lord Hulraven Irlingstar to help an adventuring party rescue his wife Nael, who'd been kidnapped by some agents of the Jaezred Chaulssin for unknown reasons. Canumbrax's duty was to lead the adventures into the Plane of Shadow.
