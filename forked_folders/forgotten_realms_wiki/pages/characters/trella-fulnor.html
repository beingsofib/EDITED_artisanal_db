Trella Fulnor was a human inhabitant of Eveningstar in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Trella had dark brown skin and long white hair. She wore a long brown dress.

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar and the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, Trella and Brinbard Meddle attended a show by the traveling performers Golo Manks and Vindavere Dyrwool in Eveningstar—not knowing that they were actually Harpers pursuing the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll, under the direction of Rina Belostos.
