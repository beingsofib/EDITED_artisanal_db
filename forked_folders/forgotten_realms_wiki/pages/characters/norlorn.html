Noldorn was an elf wraith guarding the Sacred Vale in Cormanthor in the 3 century DR.

Description

He was a wispy white shadow, trailing off into smoke below the waist.

Personality

Noldorn had grown rather aggressive about protecting the Vale. He showed concern that his fellow, Elaethan, was becoming more like the men by scrying on their minds.

Abilities

He was largely unaffected by spells that destroyed undead, or even chain lightning. Amongst other things, Noldorn could create barriers of force, almost certainly through wall of force, and he could unmake mage-killer creatures.

Activities

He was a guardian of the Sacred Vale.

Relationships

One of his fellow guardians was named Elaethan.

History

In , Noldorn arrived at the scene after Elminster Aumar escaped Heldebran; he mistook Elminster for an elf, due to him being transformed and wearing a kiira. He ultimately left Heldebran at the mercy of his own mage-killer, slaying him and then the mage-killer itself. Later he'd remark that Elaethan was thinking too much like a human.
