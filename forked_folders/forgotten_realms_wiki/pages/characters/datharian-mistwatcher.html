Datharian Mistwatcher was an elf hero who lived millennia before the 14 century DR. He worshiped Sehanine Moonbow.

After his home city was destroyed by dark elves, he was created One Thousand Broken Dreams, a powerful magic longsword. However, he was slain in battle soon after by host of driders.
