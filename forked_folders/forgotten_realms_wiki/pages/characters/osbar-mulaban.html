Osbar Mulaban was a human resident of Eveningstar in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Osbar had short brown hair and wore a long gray gown.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Osbar dined at the Lonesome Tankard with Quinzella Pramble.
