Jhenta Sulpir was a human cleric of Chauntea in the village of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Hailing from Teziir on the Dragon Coast, she had escaped an arranged marriage she was not happy with.

Activities

In the late 1350s DR, she served as an acolyte at the House of Plenty temple under Lharathuel Hesmyr. She was available to provide healing to the farmers.

Description

She was young and relatively well-to-do.
