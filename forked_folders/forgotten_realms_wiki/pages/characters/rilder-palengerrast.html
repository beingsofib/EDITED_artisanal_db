Rilder Palengerrast, Rilder Surtlash, or Rildar was an apprentice Red Wizard of Thay in the mid-14 century DR.

Description

He was gaunt and black-bearded.

Abilities

Rilder was one of Azmyrandyr's four weakest apprentices. Even his disguise spells would not last more than a few hours at a time. His strongest offensive spell was spectral axe.

Relationships

He was apprenticed to Azmyrandyr.

History

In , the Simbul assaulted a temple of Shar somewhere in Thay where the Red Wizard Azmyrandyr was preparing for an intrigue. Her goal was a revenge attack after Azmyrandyr participated in an attack on Silverymoon. During her attack, Azmyrandyr and his apprentices were rehearsing the impersonation of several notables from Nimpeth; Rilder himself was practicing his impersonation of Ilder Taramont.

He employed a spectral axe when fighting her, but aimed at her projected image instead of her real body, and was slain without ever causing any damage on her.
