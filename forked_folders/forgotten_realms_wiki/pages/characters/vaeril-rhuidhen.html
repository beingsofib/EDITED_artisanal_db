Vaeril Rhuidhen was a sun elf and a member of the Council of Elders in Everlund as of 1372 DR and also sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

Activities

Vaeril acted as voice of moderation and reason inside the council. He was also the liaison with the Lords' Alliance.
