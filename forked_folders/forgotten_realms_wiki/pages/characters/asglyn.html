Asglyn was an adventurer and cleric of Tempus during the early-to-mid–3 century DR. He was a member of the Brave Blades.

Description

He was hard-eyed.

Abilities

Asglyn was capable of recognizing some esoteric spells.

Possessions

He carried a warhammer.

History

Asglyn was a member of the Brave Blades and was a part of their expedition to Ondil's Tower in the .

After reaching Ondil's Tomb, they found that all other pathways out of the tower led back to Ondil's tomb; after Dlartarnan died trying to open the door by hand, Asglyn attempted a battleshatter spell that was responded to with a deadly flame, killing him quickly.
