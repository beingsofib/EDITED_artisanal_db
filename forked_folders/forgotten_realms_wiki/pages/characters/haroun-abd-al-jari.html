Haroun Abd al-Jari was a dwarven sergeant stationed in the armory of Sarahin in 1367 DR.

Activities

Haroun rotated his guard duty with two other dwarven farisan.

Possessions

Haroun carried a magic sword that benefited from a continual light spell.
