Hilrad, nicknamed The Patient, was a skilled human wizard and nature lover inhabiting Elventree around 1367 DR.

Activities

Hilrad was an amateur sage who welcomed newcomers to Elventree, listening to their endless questions with patience. He was a useful guide to Elventree. Hilrad did not have any apprentices, nor did he sell spells, but he was known to swap spells with friendly travelers on occasion.
