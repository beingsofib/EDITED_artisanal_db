Cothra was a priestess of Bane active in the Thayan city of Bezantur in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Cothra was the high priestess overseeing the Temple of Bane in Bezantur following Bane's death in the Time of Troubles. As such, her main role was to ensure the temple was kept clean and to lead the remaining priests in services praying for the return of their god.

As the leader of the temple in Bezantur, Cothra was at the top of the hierarchy of the Thayan church of Bane.

History

Cothra assumed control of the temple sometime after the . By the late 1360s DR, although she publicly venerated Bane and prayed for his return, she also worshiped Iyachtu Xvim, who was the source of her spells.
