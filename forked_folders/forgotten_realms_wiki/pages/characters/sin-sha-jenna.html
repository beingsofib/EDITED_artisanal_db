Sin Sha Jenna was a half-elven monk and adventurer wandering Kara-Tur in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Jenna was originally from Faerûn. Her father, a wealthy trader, had plotted a course from the Great Sea overland by portaging their ship, and then onwards on the Segara Sea to Malatra. Traveling with her father and his expedition, they had almost reached their destination when a mighty storm hit the ship. Once the battered ship hit land she had become an orphan. She was adopted by the Sin Shas of Kuo Te' Lung and trained in the path of discipline. After she became, an adult she wandered the lands as an adventurer.
