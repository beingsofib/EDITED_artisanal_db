Brinks was a criminal element who inhabited the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Brinks was active in the criminal underbelly of Baldur's Gate. The criminal worked with the gang called the Dead Eyes – enemies of Astele Keene's Guild. Brinks also gambled and did not joke when the time came to collect the winnings. The unlucky Bruno Indomicus found himself indentured to Captain Grisly and Bosun Gannet of the Blushing Mermaid as well as to Brinks, loosing coin in Baldur's Bones. Brinks demanded Bosun Gannet chop Bruno's finger off as a warning, but the innkeeper turned the request down, offering instead to buy the dwarf's debt out.
