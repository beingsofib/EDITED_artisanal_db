Yannik Johastra was a minor noble in the Monastery of the Precipice in 1479 DR.

Activities

Yannik traveled to Spellgard in 1478 DR seeking Lady Saharel's counsel regarding a feud between his sons. He believed that the key to have the Lady back was reconstructing the mansion.
