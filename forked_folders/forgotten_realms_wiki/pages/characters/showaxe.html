King Showaxe was the ruler of the dwarves who inhabited the Mountains of the Alaoreum circa 227 DR.

History

King Showaxe of Ironfang led his troops into an epic battle with a fearsome red dragon named Stormcrossing in the . King Showaxe emerged victorious in the clash and claimed the dragon's hoard. But the price of victory was steep—Stormcrossing set the dwarven town of Deepgate‎ ablaze. A column of smoke from the pyre alerted humans from the Swordslake Creek of the previously unknown dwarven presence in the Alaoreum.
