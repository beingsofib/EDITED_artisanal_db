Maignith Baenre was a member of House Baenre circa 1373 DR. She was a captain in the guard of the Baenre Compound.

History

Maignith commanded the guards during the Silence of Lolth. In 1373 DR, she died in a Jaezred Chaulssin assassination attempt on Matron mother Triel Baenre.
