Eglendar "Glen" Korr was the proprietor of the Hook, Line, and Sinker tavern in the town of Caer-Konig during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

He had a very welcoming disposition, offering a free half-pint of ale to any person who stepped inside his establishment.

Activities

He purchased the ale served at his tavern from the locals of the Dwarven Valley at the base of Kelvin's Cairn.

Relationships

Glen had a good working relationship with Jarthra Farzassh, who delivered ale from the valley to Caer-Konig.
