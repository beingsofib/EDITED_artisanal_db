Wundrith Parr was a caravanmaster who lived in the 15 century DR.

Activities

Parr was known to lead caravans across Faerûn and took on work hands.

History

Wundrith was born in Waterdeep. At some point within their life, Parr became engaged with Aedyn Graymantle, an experienced moon elf ranger. She told Wundrith that if they were to take a dangerous short cut, them and their workers would surely die.
