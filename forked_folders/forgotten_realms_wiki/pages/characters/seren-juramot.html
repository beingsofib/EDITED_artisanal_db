Seren Juramot was a mercenary war wizard of the ship the Green Siren.

History

Seren was once one of the Red Wizards of Thay and worked with the Thayan enclaves in Raven's Bluff and in Silverymoon.

Physical Description

She wore a bone white sari and wielded a scarlet-glyphed wand.
