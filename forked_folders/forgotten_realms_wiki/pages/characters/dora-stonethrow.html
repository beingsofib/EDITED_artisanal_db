Dora Stonethrow was a dwarf barmaid inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Dora's crimson beard was matched by her fiery personality.

Activities

She worked in the Tesh-Wave, a tavern located on the waterfront in Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter. Dora was the tavern's arm-wrestling champion.
