Sallissi was a morkoth living in an underground river, the Nepenthe River, near Dragonspear Castle around 1363 DR.

History

Sallissi lived as a hermit in the Nepenthe River near Dragonspear Castle. He was possibly disturbed during the Second Dragonspear War when some adventurer members of the allied army crossed his territory.
