Urmon was an adventurer and lore enthusiast.

Description

Urmon kept a journal where they noted down the history of the Lost Mine of Phandelver, and the Forge of Spells. Their journal also recorded that an enchanted mace called Lightbringer, which was made in the mine for a cleric of Lathander.

History

Circa 1491 DR, Iarno Albrek came to own Urmon's journal, and it was placed in his workshop in Phandalin. The book detailed the history of the Wave Echo Cave and related topics.
