Snarl

nameSnarl
homeCragmaw Castle, Neverwinter Wood

Snarl was a wolf and the pet of the Cragmaw tribe chieftain, Grol, around 1491 DR.

Description

Snarl was a powerful wolf, and was much more hardy than others of his species. He often attended King Grol in his personal quarters.

History

In 1491 DR, when the doppelganger disguised as a drow, Vyerith, confronted Grol, Snarl was present.
