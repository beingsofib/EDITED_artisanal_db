Alamarayne Moonray was a priestess of Selûne residing in Elmwood. She assisted Dorn Graybrook and Karasendrieth in repelling the Zhentilar that occupied her village in the .

Description

Alamarayne was a beauty, and she had a pure laugh, described as "like liquid moonlight" in 1367 DR.

Abilities

Alamarayne had employed divinations to determine the capabilities of the Zhentilar forces occupying Elmwood, ascertaining that they may have divinatory powers of their own. When fighting the Zhents, she employed such spells as hold person and dismissal.

Personality

Alamarayne did not flaunt her beauty. She had a mischievous streak. Per Will Turnstone, she was merry and even coquettish, though she could be as cheery as a mass grave sometimes.

Activities

In Alamarayne was the priestess at the Temple of the Half Moon of Elmwood. In that year, she also met once a week with Thoyana Jorgadaul at the Still Waters for conversation, dinner, and a game of chess, but those were different meetings than the ones with Ezril.

Relationships

In , Alamarayne Moonray spent a few pleasant evenings adeptly researchcing the effects of moonlight on romantic situations with Mendryll Belarod. She eventually stood him up just as he was going to tell her about noises he heard coming from the village well.

History

In , she was assisted by Dorn Graybrook's party in ridding Elmwood of the occupying force sent from Zhentil Keep in a conquering bid intended to take advantage of the Rage of Dragons between Tarsakh 11 and Tarsakh 12.
