Harin al-Hayif was the caliph of Liham in 1367 DR.

Personality

Harin was a shy and timid individual who enjoyed writing poetry.

Activities

Harin lived in the Palace of Sly Ravens located within the city near the banks of Al-Sari River. He was confined to the palace most of the time by mamluks of the Faithful Mamluk Society.
