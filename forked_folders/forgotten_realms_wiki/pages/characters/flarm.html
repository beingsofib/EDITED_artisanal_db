Flarm was a chapbook author in the late 14 century DR.

History

Flarm had been responsible for penning a description of the Open Feast of the in Castle Obarskyr, Suzail, in one of his chapbooks.

One of his readers had been Amarune Whitewave, who borrowed his chapbook on that open feast from her employer, Tress.
