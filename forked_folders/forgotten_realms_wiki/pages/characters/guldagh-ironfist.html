Guldagh Ironfist was one of the top independent incense traders in Anauroch.

Activities

Guldagh was a notorious thug. Usually, he recruited criminals in Westgate's dirtiest alleys and marched them over Cormyr and Sembia until they arrived in Anauroch, where they thieved incense from the D'tarig's territory. Afterwards, Guldagh traded the incense with other merchants before returning. If followed by some enemies, Guldagh entered the Stonelands, where rumors said he had a secret fortress.
