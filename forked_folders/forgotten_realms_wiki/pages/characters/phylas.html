Phylas was a sailor in a fishing vessel that offered to carry Taegan Nightwind and Jivex from Elmwood to Thentia; the ship returned to fishing on Mirtul 19 of after their two passengers left it. He was uneasy with dragons, given the Rage of Dragons, but showed some shame after expressing it, once Taegan reminded him of Karasendrieth.
