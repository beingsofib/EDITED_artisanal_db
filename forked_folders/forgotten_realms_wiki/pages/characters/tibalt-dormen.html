Tibalt Dormen was a human performer and Harper agent who worked in Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Tibalt had short brown hair and a light set of muttonchops.

History

After the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon circa 1485 DR, Tibalt joined Rina Belostos's Harpers in Eveningstar as they tracked Oriphaun Huntsilver and the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll. While acting as a troupe of musical entertainers (Tibalt played the lute), he found that fellow agent Bensen Virandson had located a piece, and needed an escort as he moved down Greenweald Lane to the Harper Lodge.
