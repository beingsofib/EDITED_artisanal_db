Yazilliclik was a wood sprite who resided in the Myrloch Vale of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles throughout both the 14 and 15 centuries DR.

Description

He looked like a man with pointed ears, with gossamer wings sprouting from his shoulders and two small insect-like antennae extending from his forehead. He dressed in a green tunic with a matching hat.

Personality

He was less timid than most of his kind, and his courage was an inspiration to his allies. He spoke in a high-pitched, musical tone with a stutter.

Abilities

As a wood sprite, Yazilliclik could fly and turn invisible, two skills that he put to great use.

Possessions

He fought with a miniature bow and wore a quiver of tiny arrows. He also carried a tiny dagger.

Relationships

He was a longtime ally to the druids of the Earthmother, such as Robyn Kendrick. He assisted the circle of Myrloch Vale to defend the region.

If needed, he could readily muster a great swarm of sprites to rain tiny arrows upon his enemies. He was a friend to the faerie dragon Newt.

History

When undead were sent by Bhaal to overrun the Myrloch Vale in the , Yazilliclik warned the druids of the danger and tried to help them fight the threat. He then joined forces with Robyn Kendrick, Tristan Kendrick, and Newt in their quest to defeat Bhaal.

In the , he appeared to Robyn briefly and cryptically warned her about the return of Sarifal. In the following years, people began mysteriously disappearing into the Feywild before the city of Karador finally erupted from the Myrloch in the , fulfilling his warning.

As of the late 15 century DR, he still lived within Myrloch Vale under the fey rule of Sarifal, and remained an ally to the human druids of the isles. He spent much of his time in the woods along the shore of Mal Tarbat in the Myrloch Vale.
