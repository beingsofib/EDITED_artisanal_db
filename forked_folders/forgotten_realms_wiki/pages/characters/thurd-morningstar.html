Thurd Morningstar was a talent scout for adventurers, helping to bring adventuring companies together in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Thurd Morningstar operated out of Golden Hammer Inn. During that time, Piergeiron the Paladinson and the Lords' Alliance made a call to arms seeking aid against the increased criminal and monster activity pouring out of Undermountain onto the streets of Waterdeep. Thurd Morningstar aided the hand-picked adventurers sent by Piergeiron the Paladinson, connecting them with potential new recruits.
