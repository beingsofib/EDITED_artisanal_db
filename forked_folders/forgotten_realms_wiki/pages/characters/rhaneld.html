Rhaneld was a poor child who inhabited the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Rhaneld was a human boy with a light sun-kissed skin tone, green eyes, and light brown hair.

History

In the , the city of Baldur's Gate was suffering from the encroaching war with the Cult of the Absolute, an influx of refugees, scarcity of resources, and an increase in crime. Bastian d'Zinn, a wealthy enough individual, was hit in the head with a pebble by a child playing on a road in the Lower City. The kid, Rhaneld, ended up being yelled at by Bastian, and the kerfuffle attracted a small crowd of Baldurians with varied opinions on the child, including Tylman Sands, who was one of the opinionated gawkers who believed that Rhaneld would end up growing up a criminal. Bastian himself, though, considered the child to be a piece of human garbage and nothing more.
