Brinna Alcott was a merchant living in Bryn Shander in 1485 DR.

History

Brinna was a merchant living in the Ten Towns, but in 1484 DR she became one of the cultists of Auril led by Davrick Fain. Under Fain's instruction, she accused Hengar Aesnvaard of robbery.
