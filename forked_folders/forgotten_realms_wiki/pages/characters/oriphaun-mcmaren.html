Oriphaun McMaren was a Chondathan human swordsage in the city of Procampur in the Vast.

He was a practitioner of blade magic and led an order of crusaders based at a sword-temple in Procampur. They were usually engaged in providing security at major events and noble galas, as well as guarding visiting dignitaries.
