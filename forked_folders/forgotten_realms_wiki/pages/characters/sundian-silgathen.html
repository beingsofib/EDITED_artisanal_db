Sundian Silgathen was a ranger from Sidnar in the Domains of Dread. She contracted a werespider curse in the city of Liara roughly in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

She was tall, and weighed , with almond-shaped green eyes and slender ears. Though more buxom than butch, her frame was still intimidatingly strong.

When in werespider form, she had bristly red fur and glowing yellow eyes.

Personality

Originally, she was a quiet woman. However, since her transformation, her thoughts had turned way darker: she was obsessed with hunting and stalking prey, angered quickly, and couldn't abide anyone intruding her privacy.

Abilities

Since becoming a werespider, Sundian could no longer make use of her ranger abilities, and couldn't stand to use her bow; on the other hand, she possessed a complete suite of lycanthropic abilities.

Possessions

Silgathen wielded a long sword and dagger. She lived in a two-story home, and kept five enchanted arrows there.

Activities

For the last few months, she'd been a caravan guide plying the route to Necropolis.

Relationships

Sundian had earned respected from everyone she had met.

History

Originally from Sidnar in Darkon, Sundian had since become a caravan guide in Liara, going so far as to own a home there; she guided caravans on the route to Necropolis.

About two weeks before her certain death, she'd been bitten by cursed spiders at a catacomb where she rescued a young, innocent boy, Jebbrie Vokasich, from the same spiders; he became bedridden from the bites he received, whie Sundian was afflicted differently, becoming a werespider.

A week afterwards, Sundian changed for the first time. For six days, she killed one person each day, all of them being residents of Liara: they were Alyssa, Anasylle, Cordella, Grodder, Markus, Revans.

After strangers were hired to investigate the murder, she may have been intercepted while carrying out the next killing, where she'd catch Futgar Martovingian, a local smith. If she was spotted, then on the next day, a mob would target the child she rescued, believing them to be the werespider; if she was killed, and her body returned to its own shape, then an even larger mob would. In either case, if she survived, she'd target the local watchtower next night, seize the guards there, and it would perhaps burn down in the ensuing fight, should the adventurers pursue her there.

Rumors & Legends

Due to the bite marks on their necks and exsanguinations, her killings were originally blamed on a vampire.
