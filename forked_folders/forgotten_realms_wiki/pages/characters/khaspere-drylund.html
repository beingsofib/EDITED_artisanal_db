Khaspere Drylund was a noble in Yartar sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

Activities

Khaspere was the proprietor of the Grand Dame, a riverboat housing a gambling hall. He was secretly a member of the Kraken Society and was plotting to become the new water baron of the city.

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Khaspere was a high-ranking member of the Krakenar, and he knew the location where the missing storm giant king Hekaton was chained.

Personality

Khaspere was a little man. If someone cornered and threatened him, he revealed everything about his criminal business.

Powers

He had a telepathic link to Slarkrethel, which was constantly active if they remained on the same plane. The kraken could harm Khaspere and even kill him instantly if it so desired.
