Khulmar Ironfist was a male dwarf and a member of Ironfist Clan on the Sword Coast North in the 1374 DR.

History

Khulmar was a scout of the Ironfist Clan. He went with some dwarves to explore the Ironfist Stronghold, but they were stopped by bugbears.

Gameplay

Khelgar Ironfist and the Kalach-Cha could have encountered Khulmar, but he would refuse their help, as he viewed Khelgar as deserting to pursue his own ideas. Khelgar and the Kalach-Cha could have explored the Stronghold themselves and found the Gloves of Ironfist, then returned to Khulmar to report their findings.
