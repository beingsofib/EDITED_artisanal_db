The Magister was a human wizard who specialized in necromancy sometime in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was obsessed with finding more knowledge about the undead, a obsession that could never be satisfied, no matter how much he already had accumulated.
He had raised a great many undead, including ghasts and wights, and used the more intelligent ones to seek out other types of the undead.

Possessions

In his search for knowledge he had accumulated a great deal of useful magical equipment, and as such he normally wore a cloak of displacement and a set of bracers of defense alongside a pair of winged boots and a necklace of adaptation. He also possessed a very useful, for perusing tombs and crypts, wand of illumination.
