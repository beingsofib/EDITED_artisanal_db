Janath Rendaer was one of the magelords of the kingdom of Athalantar.

History

Janath was a sarcastic individual who was one of the first magelords to join Belaur Aumar. However, he was the only magelord to befriend any of the Athalantan knights or arms-men.

He died in the magelords' assault on the Wyrm Tower in 222 DR.
