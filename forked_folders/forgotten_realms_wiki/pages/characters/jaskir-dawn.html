Jaskir Dawn was a captain of the Hellriders of Elturel during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Jaskir was dedicated to Thavius Kreeg, the High Overseer of Elturel, whom he attributed with summoning the Companion to free Elturel from its vampire rulers. His aspirations were to serve loyally and to raise his children in peace.

History

At some point prior to the , Jaskir was tasked with safely delivering the sage Shandeir to Elturel. They were accompanied on this journey by Falaster Fisk and Sylvira Savikas. They chartered passage with the White Sails Company aboard the Hesperornis. When the ship detoured to Mintarn, Jaskir protested but was overruled by Shandeir.
