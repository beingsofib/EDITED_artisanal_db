Brassons was a rogue neogi restaurateur and owner of the Ocean's Wake bar on Dragon Rock in the Tears of Selûne in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

A former slave trader, he escaped from a mindspider around 1362 DR and became a follower of Torm.

Abilities

He was a magic-user able to cast spells like a priest.

He was a good source of information.

Description

Brassons was tall and very ugly, but exceptionally mild-mannered for a neogi.
