Ophinshtalajiir Sepideh was a dragonborn member of Clan Ophinshtalajiir, and a field commander of the Lance Defenders in 1486 DR.

Description

Sepideh had silver scales.

Relationships

Sepideh was the mother of Rahdia.

History

In her youth, Sepideh was a close friend of Mehen, Uadjit and Arjhani during their service in the Lance Defenders.

In 1486 DR, Sepideh was a field commander of the Lance Defenders, and Clan Ophinshtalajiir's candidate for the elections to chose the new Vanquisher, that were expected to be celebrated in 1487 DR.

Sepideh was killed by a maurezhi servant of the god-king Gilgeam, while investigating a series of murders in the Catacombs of Djerad Thymar.
