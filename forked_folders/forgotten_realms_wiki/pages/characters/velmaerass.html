Velmaerass was a Red Wizard of Thay in the late 14 century DR.

History

When captured and questioned by Elminster in the wake of the raid of the War Wizards on the meeting of the Rightful Conspiracy in Marsember, Cormyr, during the early spring of the , Thauvas Zlorn revealed that he had been praised by Velmaerass for his as-yet-unexecuted plan to use the Company of Crazed Venturers to retrieve magic for him. Elminster jested that Velmaerass might even reward Thauvas with "a tharch or two", if he lived.
