Sharlotta Vespers was a former lieutenant in House Basadoni in Calimport in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

After Jarlaxle and his mercenary band, Bregan D'aerthe, expanded their operations beyond the Underdark and took over House Basadoni, Sharlotta served as figurehead for the guild.

In the , she was killed by Rai-guy Bondalek, a drow wizard/cleric, who was plotting against Jarlaxle to take over Bregan D'aerthe.
