Bridget Cymrych was a Ffolk human princess of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron in the Moonshae Isles in the 7th century DR.

History

In the —during the reign of High King Embro II of Callidyrr, who was known for seeking peace with the Northmen—Princess Bridget of Callidyrr was to marry the Northlander prince Scothgar of Rogarsheim, but she and her whole royal entourage vanished on the voyage to Norland. Ffolk and Northmen each blamed the other for the disappearance, which worsened relations between them.

That same year, Moonshaes Ffolk arrived on the shores of the Utter East, where they chose to settle.
