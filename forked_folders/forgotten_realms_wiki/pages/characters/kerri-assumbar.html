Lady Kerri Assumbar was the matriarch of the Assumbar noble family in Waterdeep during the late 14 century DR.

Activities

She was a devout follower of Siamorphe and active within the Chapel and Chalice of the Divine Right that was housed within the Assumbar Villa.

Relationships

Lady Kerri's mother, Belkerri Gulderhorn, married her off to Lord Laeros Assumbar shortly after they arrived in Waterdeep from their flight from Tethyr. Vrom Gulderhorn was her brother.
