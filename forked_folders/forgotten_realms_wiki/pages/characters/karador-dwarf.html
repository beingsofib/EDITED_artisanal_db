Karador was a dwarven blacksmith and armorer from Dorn's Deep active circa the 10 century DR.

History

Karador helped to craft many wondrous objects in cooperation with the elves of the Hand of the Seldarine. Of note, Karador collaborated with Elameth of Five Trees to create the Black Swan armor for a female elven marshal at the Hand.
