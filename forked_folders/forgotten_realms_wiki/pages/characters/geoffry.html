Geoffry was an apprentice innkeeper that worked for Dauravyn Redbeard at The Way Inn during the mid–14 century DR.

History

During the Second Dragonspear War of 1363 DR, a goblinoid army was seen within a day's travel from The Way Inn. Geoffry rode to Daggerford as fast as he could, in order to enlist the aid of Duke Pwyll "Greatshout" and the Daggerford militia.
