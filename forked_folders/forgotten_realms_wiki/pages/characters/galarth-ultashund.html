Galarth Ultashund, known as "Wizardkiller", was a cleric of Cyric in Luskan on the Sword Coast North and a member of the Arcane Brotherhood in the 1360s DR. He was known to have psionic powers.

Activities

Circa 1365 DR, Galarth's duty was to eliminate any members of the Brotherhood who got too far out of line. He commanded at least seven assassins, who included skilled thieves and two or more doppelgangers .

Relationships

Galarth worked under the direct orders of the Archmage Arcane, Queltar Thaeloon.
