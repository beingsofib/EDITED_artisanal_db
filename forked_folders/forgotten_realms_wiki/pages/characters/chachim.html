Chachim was an assassin of Amn and a member of the Knights of the Shield in 1370 DR.

History

Chachim was born in Amn and he trained at the School of Stealth in Zazesspur. Later, he became an agent of Inselm Hhune, rising to become one of the most trusted men of the First Shield Lord, often anticipating his master's commands. Usually he did the dirty work for the Shield Council.
