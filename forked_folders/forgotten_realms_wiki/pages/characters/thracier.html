Thracier was an early knight of Neverwinter and the fourth of the Nine knights who served Lord Halueth Never. They were the basis for the Neverwinter Nine.

History

Thracier turned traitor before his death. Nevertheless, a statue of Thracier and his sword stood guard in Halueth's tomb, noting that his death did not release him from service.
