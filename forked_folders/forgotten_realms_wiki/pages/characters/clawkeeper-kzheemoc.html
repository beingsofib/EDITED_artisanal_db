Clawkeeper Kzheemoc was a beastmaster of the Batiri Shattered Spear tribe in Chult circa 1374 DR.

Description

Kzheemoc was a larger and stronger specimen who towered over fellow Batiri goblins. His position as a Clawkeeper made Kzheemoc one of the seniors of the tribe.

Possessions

Kzheemoc was armed with a longbow and carried a quiver of mundane arrows. Akkarrak, a powerful deinonychus, loyally accompanied its master until both of their deaths at the hands of merchant Sa'Sani's hirelings.

History

In the , the Shattered Spear tribe discovered the shipwreck of the Vigilant on Omgar's Teeth. The vessel belonged to Sa'Sani of Samargol and her trade company. The Batiri attacked surviving sailors and passengers, including the infamous writer Volothamp Geddarm. Some of the survivors ended up captured by the goblins along with some of the ship's precious cargo. At the same time, another group of survivors was confronted by Ghzermag, Clawkeeper Kzheemoc, and Shoudul of the Skulls. Volo tried to persuade charming locals to let the survivors go, but Volo was not as fluent in the Goblin language as he claimed to be. In the ensuing fight, Clawkeeper Kzheemoc, Akkarrak, and their kin were slain.
