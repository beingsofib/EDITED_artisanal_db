Bralzaer Windriver was the youngest son of Sabrast Windriver in the mid-14 century DR.

Activities

Per his father, Sabrast Windriver, Bralzaer had founded a mercenary company out of Sembia named Bralzaer's Bold Basilisks.

Relationships

In a revel on , Sabrast Windriver explained he wasn't sending his youngest son any money.
