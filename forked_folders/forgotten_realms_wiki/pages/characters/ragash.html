Ragash of the Red Mist was a half-orc hunter operating out of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

She specialized in hunting intelligent prey, namely Batiri goblins, yuan-ti, lizardfolk, and some apes. She was also willing to help other hunters by providing them with lures to bait such creatures.

Relationships

Ragash was a member of the Hunters' Guild of Chult, and could often be found at Hunter's Square in Port Nyanzaru.

History

During the events of the death curse Ragash became acquainted with Volothamp Geddarm. During this time, she was interested in hunting a number of notable individuals within the Chultan jungle, namely the Batiri beast master known as Clawgraze, a lizardfolk druid known as Redfang, and two yuan-ti abominations known as the "Fang of Dendar" and Kutlass.
