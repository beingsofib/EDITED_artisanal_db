Bristlebeard was a dire boar inhabiting the Ashenwood in Rashemen during the late 14 century DR.

Description

Bristlebeard had patches of bright red hair.

Activities

The dire boar was awakened by the telthors of the forest and paid them heed. He aided any Rashemaar lost within the Ashenwood.
