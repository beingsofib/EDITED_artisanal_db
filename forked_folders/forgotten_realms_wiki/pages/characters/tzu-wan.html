Tzu Wan, called the Entrusted, was a hermit and wiseman who lived atop the Marnu Cliffs, near the source of Nam Tso, in Tabot.

Wan was one of only five wisemen recognized by the monks of Tabot around as being a true oracle, being capable of predicting the future and raising heroes from the dead for good causes.
