Meer was the head of caravans within the Zhentarim, during the mid–14 century DR, both before and after the rise to power of Fzoul Chembryl. Several individual caravan masters, along with some particular merchants, worked directly under Meer.

History

For a period, Meer was in possession of the magical scimitar Shazzellim, which was presented to him by his subordinate Lhemako.
