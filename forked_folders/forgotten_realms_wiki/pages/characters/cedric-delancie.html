Torsin Cedric deLancie was a human noble from Waterdeep that served within the City Guard during the mid–14 century DR. He was the youngest to ever be promoted to the rank, despite having never seen a true battle.

Description

Despite his lack of combat experience he was an expert in military history and strategy.

History

Torsin deLancie led the Waterdhavian troops within the Coalition during its war with Caelar Argent's Shining Crusade in the . During the war, deLancie sought a number of ways to give an edge to Coalition forces, including poisoning the crusade's water supply and planting explosives beneath Dragonspear Castle.
