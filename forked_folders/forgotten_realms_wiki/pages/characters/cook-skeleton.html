The creature only known as Cook was a skeleton what haunted the infamous Haunted Halls near the village of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the 15 century DR.

Activities

In the 15 century DR, Cook could be found within the dungeon, still wieilding the the toll of the trade – a massive skillet, as a weapon. It was easily identified by the bleached bones and a dark blue bandana. The skeleton still haunted the dungeon when a group of adventurers that arrived to Toril through a rift from Eberron descended into the Haunted Halls.
