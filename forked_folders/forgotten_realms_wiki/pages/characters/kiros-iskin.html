Kiros Iskin was an inhabitant of Almorel in the Hordelands in 1357 DR.

Activities

Kiros served an important role as the lighthouse keeper of the Lantern-house, guiding smalls boats through the heavy fog of the Lake of Mists to the docks of the city.
