Faerim was a sailor from Waterdeep in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Some time in the 1360s DR, Faerim came to be a captive aboard an infamous pirate ship, Scimitar of Fire, captained by Raurivyl Ornshield. Serving unwillingly as a crewman, he would be a witness to Raurivyl's voyages to the west.

Seeking a desert island he might use as a safe haven and base, Raurivyl took the Scimitar of Fire westward on several voyages beyond the Gull Rocks. However, they found naught but a vast expanse of empty ocean. On three occasions they encountered the naval vessels of Evermeet, which rose out of the sea, hovered in the air, and tilted to direct their strange weaponry at the Scimitar. Via magical speaking horns, the elves ordered the pirates to leave. Sensibly, Captain Ornshield obeyed, but still the flying elven ships followed them until they were long gone.

Following months of such futile searching, Raurivyl returned to the Nelanther Isles. There, Faerim escaped, even taking some pirate treasure with him back to Waterdeep that he spent big with, along with tales of the elven ships. He insisted he was neither making it up, nor had been drinking.
