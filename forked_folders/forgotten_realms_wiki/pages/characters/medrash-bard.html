Medrash was a musician and entertainer, part of the Circus of the Last Days troupe in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Medrash was a dragonborn with a red dragon ancestry. He had flaming red eyes and wore a fanciful bard's garb. He was a proficient lyre player.

Abilities

As a red dragonborn, Medrash could breathe out a burst of flames. He also could cast the faerie fire spell.

History

In the , Circus of the Last Days returned from the Feywild and opened its gates to the inhabitants of the Rivington district in Baldur's Gate. Medrash entertained guests at the circus when it was revealed that the Cult of the Absolute murdered one of the troupe – the clown called Dribbles, and replaced him with a murderous doppelganger.
