Thraka was a half-orc mercenary in Sildëyuir in 1372 DR.

History

Thraka was an orc mercenary captain. He was recruited by the nilshai sorcerer Tolg'byri to conquer the Night Realm from Mourel Duskwalker. He became the second-in-command of the mercenary army after Savera took control of the group and Guerik went mad.

Activities

Thraka posed as the mercenary leader while Savera suggested to him what to say using telepathy.

Personality

Thraka was very nervous about his current situation. He was very scared of Savera but was dominated by her and followed her orders without questions.
