Pumkato was a svirfneblin of Blingdenstone.

Description

The deep gnome had gray eyes.

Possessions

Pumkato wielded a pickaxe in combat and had stones which could summon an earth elemental.

History

In 1357 DR, Pumkato was among a deep gnome patrol who stumbled upon Catti-brie, who used Agatha's mask to disguise herself as a drow female. With the help of Seldig, he stopped the attack after recognizing Guenhwyvar, whom he played with when Drizzt Do'Urden lived in Blingdenstone

Later that year, he become a burrow warden.
