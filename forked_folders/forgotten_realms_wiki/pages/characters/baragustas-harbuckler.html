Baragustas Harbuckler was a retired carpenter in Red Larch and a member of the Believers during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Relationships

Baragustas was an old member of Believers.

History

In 1491 DR, he was quite intimidated by Larrakh of the Cult of the Black Earth, who tried to put the Believers in a more active role. He was not part in the murders committed by Ilmeth Waelvur, Marlandro Gaelkur, and Albaeri Mellikho but knew of the matter and covered for them. He was subdued by some adventurers who were investigating in the village.
