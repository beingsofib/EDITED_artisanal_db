Cheela Flegsteel was one of the Wearers of Purple in the Cult of the Dragon during the late 15 century DR.

History

Some time during the 1480s DR, Cheela was captured by a group of dwarven miners she was torment on behalf of the Cult of the Dragon. Her drake companions were slain and she was marched off as a prisoner by the victorious dwarves.

The cult dispatched a half-dragon agent named Yggran along with four ogres to free Cheela, but their ultimate fate was unknown.
