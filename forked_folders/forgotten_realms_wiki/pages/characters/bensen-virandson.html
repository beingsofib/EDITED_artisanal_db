Bensen Virandson was a wizard and Harper agent who worked in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Bensen had short white hair and muttonchops.

Personality

Bensen was somewhat sardonic and jaded, and hard to impress or surprise.

Abilities

Bensen was a proficient and talented mage, and in particular knew an array of defensive spells that could keep him safe, at the cost of limiting him solely to a defensive posture.

History

Following the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon in the mid-1480s DR, Rina Belostos and her Harpers searched for Oriphaun Huntsilver and the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll that he had taken from the Shadovar at Wheloon Prison. Knowing that the forces of Thultanthar were on his tail, Oriphaun tore the Scroll to pieces and hid them along the High Road to confuse the pursuit and give the Harpers time to catch up. Bensen managed to quickly locate a piece in the eaves of the King's Forest, but his inside source reported on the appearance of Shadovar forces between him and the Harper Lodge where it could be safely contained. He waited for Harper reinforcements at the Greenweald Lane gatehouse, and was displeased when he merely received trustworthy adventurers sent by Rina.

With nothing else to wait for, Bensen carried the Scroll piece, protecting it and himself with powerful magics while his escort fought their way through roadblocks and ambushes of Netherese wizards, hired sellswords, and Dom's team of elite mercenaries. Along the way, the tumult of combat caused the Scroll to randomly summon dretches from the Abyss, adding extra chaos to the series of running battles. When the dust finally settled, he was able to store and hide the artifact piece at the Harper Lodge until it could be safely moved on.
