Sarsor yi Tashluta, called the Rope, was an alias adopted by a yuan-ti pureblood of the Se'Sehen tribe posing as a human enforcer in the service of the Rundeen in the late 14 century DR. His real name was similar in sound to his alias.

Description

Although a yuan-ti, Sarsor fully resembled a human, albeit one who only ever ate meat and boasted incredible flexibility, being able to twist and fold his body in painful-seeming ways.

History

Sarsor hailed from the Black Jungles of Tashalar and was part of a Se'Sehen fact-finding mission about human organizations and trade. With the support of the Grand Yrshelem, the leaders of the Rundeen who knew his true heritage, he infiltrated the Rundeen in the . He adopted the identity of a contortionist from Tashluta and worked as an enforcer.

Activities

He used his racial abilities to twist his body in order to grapple the people he was ordered to restrain or harm, which gave him the nickname "the Rope".

Possessions

He was armed with a dagger +2.
