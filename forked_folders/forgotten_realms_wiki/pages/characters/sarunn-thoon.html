Sarunn Thoon was a sorceress that ruled over Farkeep for a time during the Age of Humanity.

Personality

She was known as an especially cruel and cunning individual.

History

Sarunn took control of the Far Hills' fortress by posing as a weak captive, and entering into the bedchamber of its lord, a man known only as the Wolf Knight. She took over rule of the keep for herself, for a time, proving to be far more malevolent than its previous lords.

The evil sorceress' reign was put to an end however, when she and all of her followers were mentally enslaved by a group of illithids that posed as enigmatic, masked wizards.

Rumors & Legends

Sarunn's story was outlined in the ballad known as The Witch of the Far Cold Hill.
