Orgula Samshroon was a clerk inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

She was a stout and middle-aged human woman.

Personality

She had a motherly or matronly manner to her and was quite easy going.

Abilities

She had an excellent memory, never forgetting a face and able to recall details of a person's appearance and attire.

Activities

Orgula worked as a counter clerk in Aurora's Realms Shop, Street of the Tusks Catalogue Counter within Waterdeep's Trades Ward. Her vast knowledge of Faerûn and of the items carried by Aurora's Emporium made her well known to customers.

Orgula was also a secret contact for the Harpers and served as a set of eyes for Open Lord Piergeiron Paladinson himself.
