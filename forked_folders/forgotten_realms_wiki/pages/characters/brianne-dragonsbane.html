Brianne Dragonsbane was queen of the Kingdom of Bloodstone in the mid–15 century DR.

History

Brianne Dragonsbane was assassinated in the , and the civil unrest caused by this event distracted the Kingdom of Bloodstone from the mustering army of the Warlock Knights in Vaasa.

Shortly before her death, she had an illegitimate son named Kaggen Entreri. The pregnancy was kept a secret from all but her most trusted advisors.
