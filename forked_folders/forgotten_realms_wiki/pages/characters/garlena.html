Garlena, or Sister Garlena, was a cleric of Helm at Watcher's Keep in Amn.

The god Helm gave Garlena and her fellow knights the task of sealing Demogorgon inside the keep. Knowing it was a difficult mission, her leader, Odren, recruited Gorion's Ward to work for him. Garlena helped Gorion's Ward get equipped to enter the keep.
