Thungor Triblane was an gnomish illusionist ex-adventurer and the innkeeper of the Windlord's Rest in Tilverton in the 14 century DR.

Personality

Thungor was generally honest and forthright with others, but was willing to conspire a bit with Filani to extract maximum information and money from strangers. He could be a touch intimidated by people taller than him.

Activities

Thungor managed the Windlord's Rest, an inn of good quality in the town. He treated his guests like adopted children, giving them paternal advice, scoldings, pocket money, and other affectionate treats. Although some were driven away by this behavious, the majority of guests adored it, and made sure to return to the Windlord whenever possible.

Relationship

Thungor was a good friend of the sage Filani, who he had adventured with previously.

History

Thungor adventured with the wizard Filani for a time, eventually retiring to Tilverton and setting up the Windlord's Rest inn by 1358 DR. By the 1360s DR, his business proved too popular and he moved his premises to a larger location nearby, causing much confusion to returning customers who instead found the Jonstyl's Banner.
