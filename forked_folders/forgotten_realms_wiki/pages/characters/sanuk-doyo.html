Sanuk Doyo was a noble of Koryo in the mid–14th century DR.

Born a noble of the former kingdom of Silla, Sanuk became a general and later governor of the city of Tu Pe. As governor, he held the honorary title "Lord of the South Wind"

Sanuk's chief advisor was Xithiaz Jui, a wu jen.
