Saril

Person
nameSaril
titlesSaint, St.

Saril was a saint of the Church of Ilmater. They were the patron and namesake of a priory nestled between Calimshan and Tethyr.

History

A later descendant of Saril, the Revered Father Hamash, would be in charge of St. Saril's Priory.
