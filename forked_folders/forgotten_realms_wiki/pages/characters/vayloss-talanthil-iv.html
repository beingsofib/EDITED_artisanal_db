Vayloss Talanthil IV, who was also called the Flamebringer, was the tenth and final Sorcerer-Arkhan of the Talanthil Dynasty who ruled the nation of Raumathar.

Activities

Vayloss IV inherited the throne in when his older brother Tallos IV was slain in battle during an assault on Dun-Tharos in Narfell. He reigned until and died during Kossuth's final assault of the Great Conflagration that utterly destroyed both Raumathar and Narfell.
