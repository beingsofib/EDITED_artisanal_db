Klav Martilmur

Klav Martilmur was the pastry chef for the Elfsong Tavern during the late 15 century DR. They worked alongside a sous chef Azar, under the joyful halfling Chenna Fatrabbit.

Klav was an unsighted individual. One of the dishes they specialized in baking was bread pudding served with rich syrup.
