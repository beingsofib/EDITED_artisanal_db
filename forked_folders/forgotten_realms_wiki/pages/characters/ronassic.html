Ronassic was a human historian from the famous Outer Planes city of Sigil.

History

At some point Ronassic recorded a legend from the sahuagin of Toril about a mighty megalodon known as Iakhovas, which proclaimed him to be "The One Who Swims With Sekolah," the shark deity that was worshiped by them. Ronassic commited the legend to a book, a copy of which would later be found on Toril in by the malenti Laaqueel while spying on sea elves. The book lead her down a quest to eventually resurrect Iakhovas.
