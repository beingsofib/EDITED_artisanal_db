Istador was an unusual creature that was the offspring of a black dragon and a tyrannosaurus. Favored by Istishia, he guarded the Floating Island, a flying island cursed by Umberlee.

Description

Istador resembled a winged tyrannosaurus with black scales and horns.

Personality

Istador was more intelligent than the other dinosaurs on the Floating Island.

Abilities

As a favored soul, Istador had access to a number of spells from the god Istishia. He could cast the spells create water, cure minor wounds, detect magic, detect poison, guidance, virtue, cure light wounds, divine favor, entropic shield, shield of faith, lesser vigor, bull's strength, cure moderate wounds, lesser restoration, resist energy, bestow curse, contagion, and cure serious wounds.

Istador had the ability to breathe a line of acid. In battle, he could grab opponents smaller than himself and swallow them whole. He was proficient with warhammers.

History

Istador's father was a black dragon who served Istishia. Istishia had sent the dragon to the Floating Island to guard the deadly disease called Umberlee's touch that festered there. To further this goal, the black dragon mated with some of the dinosaurs that lived there, including some deinonychuses and Istador's tyrannosaurus mother.

Continuing to meddle in mortal affairs, Umberlee killed the black dragon before Istador was born. Shortly after his birth, Istador's mother also died. This left Istador as the last tyrannosaurus on the Floating Island, so he practiced fighting on his own. Carrying on his father's mission, he guarded the lake at the center of the island from which the fog of Umberlee's touch billowed.
