Ralzala was a dao in Avernus, first layer of the Nine Hells, in the late 15 century DR.

History

Ralzala made a pact with the archdevil Zariel in order to destroy a rival but found herself in servitude to the devil.

In the , Ralzala was assigned to watch over a demon zapper that imprisoned the unicorn Mooncolor. However, she was tired of servitude and was searching for someone that could help her to break the pact, possibly the former archdevil Bel.
