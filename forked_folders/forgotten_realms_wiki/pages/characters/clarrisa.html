Clarrisa was a bowyer and fletcher in Stag Falls during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Darrold meant much to her, and she would not chance the displeasure of the Baron; she'd betray anyone who tried to rely on her against her liege.

Abilities

She was a competent bowyer and fletcher.

Activities

Clarrisa made bows, and arrows, and quarrels.

Relationships

The bowyer had a close friend named Darrold.

History

After the vampire Romanisk Vlaensk took control of Stag Falls one spring in the mid-to-late 14 century DR, he had her brought to the castle by Baron Vilhelm von Stag Falls, and forced to make arrows and quarrels day and night, by threatening her friend Darrold. Unknown to her, Darrold had already been killed.

Adventurers sent from Ravens Bluff to investigate Stag Falls may have come to her while infiltrating the castle; if they did, she'd have warned the Baron unless they had proof Darrold was indeed dead.
