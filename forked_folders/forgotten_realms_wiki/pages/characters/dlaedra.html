Dlaedra was a woman of Hastarl in the early 3 century DR.

Rumors & Legends

In the , Thelorn Selemban insinuated during the start of an unfriendly meet with Jansibal Otharr at the Kissing Wench, a tavern of Hastarl, that Dlaedra was unimpressed with his "rampant glory", almost certainly an anatomical reference. These insinuations strongly implied Dlaedra was a woman.
