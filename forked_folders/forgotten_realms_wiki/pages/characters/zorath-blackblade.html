Zorath Blackblade was an assassin working for the slaver ring that had taken over the Sandvoyagers' Guild in , at the time of the release of the efreet Khalitarius.

Abilities

Zorath's strength and dexterity were optimal. However, only his climbing skill was good.

Activities

The assassin was under contract with Thurnas Netmaster. though he also wandered the oasis sometimes.

He was also known to Tekuna Chand, who'd caution people not to trust him.
