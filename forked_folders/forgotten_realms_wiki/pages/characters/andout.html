Andout was a messenger who lived in the Sword Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

History

During the iron crisis, in the , Andout traveled from Baldur's Gate to Beregost to deliver a message to Governor Kelddath Ormlyr. It stated that the town was to be garrisoned with Flaming Fist soldiers in case of an invasion from Amn.
