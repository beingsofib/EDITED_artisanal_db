Boon Silverhoarde was a dwarven priest of Grumbar.

Description

He hailed from a wealthy dwarven family noted for their unusual skill at metallurgy. Boon was reddish-brown of hair and fair of skin, clad himself in gilded armor, and wore a lengthy red cape.

Personality

Although he shared his family's interest in metallurgy, Boon devoted himself to the service of Grumbar and the elemental forces of earth.

Abilities

Boon was able to sense the presence of precious metals within 60 feet (18.2 meters).

Possessions

He owned a magical shield +1 and a plate mail of etherealness.
