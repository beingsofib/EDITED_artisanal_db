Trystan P. Shale was a librarian of Candlekeep who served within its hallowed halls during the mid–14 century DR.

History

Throughout the , Trystan helped the sage Elminster correspond to his fellow scholar and member of the Avowed, Gorion, by delivering his incoming letters. The following year, Trystan received one more letter from Elminster, asking about information regarding the Bhaalspawn. Trystan had the unfortunate duty of informing Elminster that Gorion had left Candlekeep shortly before, along with his ward Abdel.
