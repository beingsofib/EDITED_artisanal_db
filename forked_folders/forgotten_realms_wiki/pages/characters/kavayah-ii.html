Novice-Prince Kavayah II was a prince of the Kuong Kingdom in Malatra in the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

Kavayah was the eldest legitimate son of Priest-King Vishnan VII and one of his wives. He was aged 14 around 1357 DR.

Activities

A studious boy, Kavayah was fully absorbed with his studies at the temple of the Lords of Creation to become the next priest-king. With a knack for magic, he learned clerical spells with ease.
