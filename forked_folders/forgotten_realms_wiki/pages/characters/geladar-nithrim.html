Geladar Nithrim was a cordwainer inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Geladar was the guildmaster for the Most Diligent League of Sail-Makers and Cordwainers during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He held the title of "League Master" within the guild and worked out of Full Sails–their headquarters along the Dock Street in the Dock Ward.
