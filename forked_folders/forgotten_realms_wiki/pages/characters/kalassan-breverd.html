Kalassan Breverd was a Talassan Storm Knight, active some years before the late 13 century DR.

History

Years before , Storm Knight Kalassan Breverd fought against the enemies of the Church of Talos in the Moonshae Isles. Kalassan wielded the unique enchanted weapon called the Storm Bow, killing numerous druids and rangers in the Moonshaes, until slain by an enemy in the struggle.
