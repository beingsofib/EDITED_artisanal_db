Ponczer was an individual working at the Firehelm in Marsember in Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

Rumors & Legends

When Durexter Dagohnlar and his wife, Starmara Dagohnlar, were ambushed and captured in their bedroom by the merchants Malakar Surth and Aumun Tholant Bezrar, Malakar threatened to cut off their fingers and have Ponczer at the Firehelm cook them.
