Utheo Myar was a male drow who served as a slavemaster in the Sschindylryn Ziggurat complex below the Temple of Lolth, found atop the city of Sschindylryn, in the 1480s DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Utheo controlled a large number of slaves wearing drow slave collars. When Elminster and Ana Brabener led an attack on the Ziggurat, Utheo may have been caught in the fighting and slain despite using his slaves as living shields.
