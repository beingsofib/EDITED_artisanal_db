Ranger Leokas was a mercenary who worked for Company Yargo aboard the Buccaneer's Tear during the late 15 century DR.

History

When the Buccaneer's Tear soared up to Pirates' Skyhold above the Sea of Swords in the , Leokas was among the first mercenaries to establish a beachhead on the earthmote. He worked with a hired adventurer to eradicate some of the local lizardfolk that threatened the expedition from the nearby Vilebog swamp the swamp.
