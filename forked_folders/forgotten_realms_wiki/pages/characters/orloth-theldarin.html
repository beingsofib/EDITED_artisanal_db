Orloth Theldarin was a counter clerk for the Aurora's Realms Shop, Singing Dolphin Catalogue Counter in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep in the 1360s DR.

Description

He was considered handsome.

Personality

He had effeminate manners that were balanced with courtesy and great tact. He also had a reputation for excellent taste.

Abilities

He had a talent for remembering and identifying colors exactly, even if he'd seen them only once and no matter how long ago.
