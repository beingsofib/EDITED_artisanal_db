Shieldlord Hastor el Khafar ran a fighting school at one of the shrines to Tempus in Memnon.

Activites

In 1370 DR, Hastor took on a sea elf student named Vyaalis, whom he trained in secret, teaching him how to fight on land in exchange for the latter teaching him how to fight below the waves.
