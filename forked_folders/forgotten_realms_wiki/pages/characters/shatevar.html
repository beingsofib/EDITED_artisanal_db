Shatevar was a beholder that lived in the Western Heartlands of Faerûn during the late 14 century DR.

History

Shatevar was coerced into service by Vala Thorsdotter and her Vaasan warriors as they ventured into the depths of the Greycloak Hills in the . Shatevar was forced to use his disintegrating eye beam to carve through rock and stone to reach Melegaunt Tanthul in the area around the Sharn Wall.

When Vala and her men engaged a band of Evereskan tomb guardians, Shatever proved a valuable ally to the humans, nullifying their magic with its antimagic eye beam and even killing the elven sentry Aragath. Shatevar was slain with a single slash of a darksword by one of the Vaasan warriors, its body left limp and lifeless upon the cavern floor.
