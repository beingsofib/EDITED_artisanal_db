Barri Baram was a High Captain of Luskan in the 15 century DR.

Activities

Barri Baram led Ship Baram and was thus a large player in providing food to the city of Luskan.

History

Barri lived in Luskan, and some time in or prior to 1489 DR, became the new captain of Ship Baram.
