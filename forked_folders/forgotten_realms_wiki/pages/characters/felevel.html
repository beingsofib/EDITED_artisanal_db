Felevel the Green was the most powerful druid living in the vicinity of Olostin's Hold, northwest of the High Forest in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Felevel was a half-elven woman, a nature spellcaster of considerable prowess.

Relationships

Felevel was a strong critic of the leadership of Mardan Elthondsson, then-lord of the keep.
