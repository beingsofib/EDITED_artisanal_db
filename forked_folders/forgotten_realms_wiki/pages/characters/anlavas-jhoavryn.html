Anlavas Jhoavryn was a merchant in the mid–8 century DR.

Activities

He dealt in brasswork and cheap sundries, but worked in bulk.

History

The merchant hailed from somewhere south of Nethra, across the sea.

During the late , his throat was cut while at Nethra. Lord Elminster Aumar, then the Court Mage of Galadorna, made inquiries of the local guard captain, Rhoagalow, who admitted he suspected Limmator to be behind his death.
