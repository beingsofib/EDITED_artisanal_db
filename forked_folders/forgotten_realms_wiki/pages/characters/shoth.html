Shoth the Tomb Queen was a giant spider that occupied the abandoned Waterclock Guild's Crypts.

Abilities

She had sharp claws and would swipe at her prey. She could summon her spiderling spawn to fight alongside her against intruders.

History

As of , Shoth had made her lair in the deepest chamber of the crypts, where her webs and egg sacs covered the walls.
