Aghilde was a bog hag that lurked in the Druid's Deep of Thorngap during the late 15 century DR.

History

Aghilde laired in a discrete corner of the Druid's Deep while the nearby druid Halsaime worked to conquer the world, until Harper agents arrived to eliminate the threat. They may have slain Aghilde and taken her ivy wraps.
