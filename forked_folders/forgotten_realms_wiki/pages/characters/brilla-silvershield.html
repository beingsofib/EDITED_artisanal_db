Brilla Silvershield was a patriar of the noble Silvershield family who lived in their familial estate in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Brilla was a spellcaster of some manner. She quite susceptible to charm and gave in to others to some extended when being flattered.

Relationships

She was the wife to Grand Duke Entar Silvershield, as well as mother to their two children, Skie and Eddard.

History

Throughout the , Brilla lost her husband and both of her children at the hands of killers. Fortunately, her husband was resurrected by a noted healer.
