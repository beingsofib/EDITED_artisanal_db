Melisana was a human inhabitant of the island of Ventris in the Korinn Archipelago in the early 14 century DR.

Relationships

She was the daughter of Melkeras, a wealthy merchant and merchant guildmaster.

Description

At age 19, she was a brown-haired and blue-eyed.

Personality

Insightful and good-natured, young Melisana was wise enough to avoid being spoiled by her overindulgent parents, and had decided to take over Melkeras's business whenever he decided to retire from its day-to-day management. She was also well on her way to becoming a good sailor and merchant. Although not violent by nature and tending to stay well away from conflict, the need to survive could prompt her to help her allies if they should require it.

Abilities

A competent sailor and merchant, Melisana was also proficient in use of the dagger.
