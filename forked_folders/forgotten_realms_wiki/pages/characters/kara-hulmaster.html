Kara Hulmaster was a member of the Hulmaster family of Hulburg and the captain of the Shieldsworn in the late 15 century DR. She was the regent of Hulburg as of 1480 DR.

Relationships

She was the niece of Grigor Hulmaster and the cousin of Geran Hulmaster.

Abilities

In addition to her skills as an excellent warrior and ranger, Kara was a competent strategist and battle master.

Description

Kara was touched by the Spellplague and carried a single spellscar. Her eyes burned with an azure fire and she had markings on her body.
