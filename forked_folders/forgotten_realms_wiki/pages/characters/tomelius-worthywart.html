Tolemius Worthywart was a halfling resident of the Realms who lived during the late 14 or 15 centuries DR.

Personality

He believed that the halfling ancestral home of Luiren had sunk beneath the Great Sea.

History

While traveling through the Fireshear Mountains, Tomelius and his brother encountered a wandering dragonborn. The dragonborn was a writer, and recorded the discussion they had regarding the fate of Luiren following the Spellplague. Their discussion was immortalized in the compendium Oral Histories of Faerûn.
