Imbur Waendlar was a chest merchant of the late 14 century DR. He was a participant in the Rightful Conspiracy of .

Description

During the meeting of the Rightful Conspiracy in early spring of , he was red-faced.

Personality

The man was cynical about rulers, and had a strong interest in money.

Abilities

He could continue to pitch his products even in the most thorough state of inebriation.

Activities

Imbur Waendlar was often making pitches about his products, which included coffins, strongchests, and greatchests, almost certainly indicating he was a chest merchant. He claimed to have the best prices in Suzail.

Relationships

His participation in the Rightful Conspiracy was as a financier: the conspiracy needed his money to fund itself. He had no particular loyalty to the crown of Cormyr.

History

Imbur Waendlar was a participant in the meeting of the Rightful Conspiracy in Marsember during the early spring of . He tried to sell a chest to a disguised Elminster Aumar, then claiming to be a member of the Cormaeril family.

Rumors & Legends

While he was accused of being stubborn by a warrior in the meeting, he insisted that the only mark of being stubborn was being unable to change with the times, shift with the deals, and so keep one's coins about oneself. The warrior seemed unimpressed.
