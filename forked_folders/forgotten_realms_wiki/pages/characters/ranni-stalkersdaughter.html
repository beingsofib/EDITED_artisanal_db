Ranni Stalkersdaughter was a human ranger and co-leader of the Ruby Blades adventuring company in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Ranni was a fair young woman, with gray eyes and a long braid of brown hair.

Personality

She was an alert and responsible woman who took her duties very seriously. She felt far more comfortable out in nature than in urban environments.

Abilities

Ranni had a special focus on hunting and killing hill giants.

Possessions

Ranni carried a longbow, spear, and enchanted dagger, but her greatest weapon was a +2 giant slayer short sword. She wore no armor, instead relying on a torc of protection and her natural agility. She had some Keoghtom's ointment in case of emergencies.

Relationships

She was best friends with Pyrra Flamedancer, and together they led the Ruby Blades.

History

In the , the Ruby Blades joined the Wolfmasters company on an excursion to explore and loot Orvaskyte Keep in the Vast Swamp. The Ruby Blades were heavily delayed, and by the time they arrived their allies were already dead. Seeking a new goal, they attempted to hunt a nearby black dragon, only to be overwhelmed and almost killed by it, and were rescued by a band of Cormyrean adventurers. Their fortune waxed once more, and they were joined by two-dozen mercenaries that allowed them to chase away the Cormyreans and secure what was left of Orvaskyte Keep.
