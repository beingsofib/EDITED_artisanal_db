Luxewa was a night hag in the Moonsea in 1373 DR.

Activities

Luxewa laired in some caves in the south of the Galena Mountains, near Mulmaster.

When threatened, she preferred to fight from a distance with spells, but if forced into melee, she would attempt to inflict demon fever on her enemies.

History

In 1373 DR, the area was conquered by the fire giant brothers Soluf and Gharod but as they were not very smart, Luxewa often acted as their advisor.
