Shev'aal was a giant spider that lived in the Ziggurat beneath the Temple of Lolth atop the city of Sschindylryn in the 1480s DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Shev'aal lived in the dedicated spider quarters within the Ziggurat, enjoying a diet of slaves. Despite this lifestyle, Shev'aal was still a morally uncompromised beast.
