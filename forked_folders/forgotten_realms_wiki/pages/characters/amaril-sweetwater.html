Amaril Sweetwater was a half-elf and former adventurer whose travels eventually led her to Elventree. She liked the sylvan community so much that she decided to stay.

Personality

Amaril had a quick temper and took a long time to cool off once angered.

Activities

Amaril owned the Gold Cave—a natural cave in Elventree that she converted into a general shop. As a former adventurer, she had a good idea of the equipment her former brethren required and kept her store stocked accordingly.
