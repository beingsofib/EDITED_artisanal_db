"Bigmouth" Thimsen was a dwarvish commoner from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Thimsen was a gold dwarf with dark skin and wavy black hair. He had blue eyes, large bulbous nose and a clean-shaven face.

History

In the , as the Cult of the Absolute loomed over the city of Baldur's Gate, "Bigmouth" Thimsen could often be seen gossiping in the Lower City with his easily impressed gnomish friend Margolenn.
