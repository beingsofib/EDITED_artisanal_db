Bandaerl, son of Rykos, blood of Melair, High Old One of Dumathoin, and protector of Melairbode's essence was a dwarven archlich of the Melairkyn clan who protected the tomb of King Melair I in the depths of Undermountain, formerly home to this ancient clan.
