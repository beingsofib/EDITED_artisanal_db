Thoven was a mercenary of holy persuasion in Cormyr during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Thoven had pallid skin and thick brown hair tied back in dreadlocks. He carried a morningstar and wore the manacles and metal collar of a slave even as a free man.

Personality

Thoven was extremely loyal and dutiful to a fault.

Abilities

Thoven had divine powers that let him heal, as well as cast searing light and sound burst.

History

After living a life as a slave, Thoven was recruited (and possibly freed) by the mercenary Dom, becoming a key part of his elite forces. They were later hired in the mid-1480s DR by Returned Netheril to take a piece of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll from the Harper Bensen Virandson as he traveled down Greenweald Lane. Part of their ambush involved building roadblocks along the Lane, but this only slowed the Harpers down a little as they fought their way through the attacks, killing Thoven and the others in the process.
