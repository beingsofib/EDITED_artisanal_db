Jaerlethket was a silver dragon paladin of Bahamut and a member of the Talons of Justice.

Lair

Jaerlethket's lair was located somewhere in Damara.

Activities

As of 1374 DR, Jaerlethket was one of the few known members of the Talons. He was also the advisor to King Gareth Dragonsbane.
