Falura was a halfling cook who worked under Chef Marjoram in Lady Jannath's Estate in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Falura was a young, dark-skinned hin woman with curly black hair and dark brown eyes.

History

During her time in Lady Jannath's estate, Falura strived to be a chef. She was supervised by Marjoram, who ran his kitchen with an iron fist, demanding perfection. Falura managed to serve Firelia Jannath's guests raw dishes, struggled with knife-work, and complained over the amount of mouths she had to cook for.
