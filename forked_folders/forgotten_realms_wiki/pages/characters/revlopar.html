Revlopar Twin-tailed was a mutant ixzan of the city of Malydren in the Underdark in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

Circa 1370 DR, Revlopar was a member of the Xendreth, the ruling council of Malydren.

Personality

Revlopar had a secret desire to explore the Realms Above so that he might learn new spells and magic, but until then he waited while he cultivated and developed his current powers.

Abilities

Revlopar's mutation, that is, have two tails, permitted him to cast spells as wizards of other races did, rather than being limited to a fixed set of innate magical abilities like most ixzan. For this reason, he was considered a spellcasting prodigy by most other ixzan.

Relationship

Revlopar studied magic under Erqynak Icebreath, an elderly ixzan magical research.
