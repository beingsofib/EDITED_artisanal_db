Kelnozz was a commoner drow warrior of House Kenafin of Menzoberranzan in the early 14 century DR.

History

Kelnozz was classmates with Drizzt Do'Urden at Melee-Magthere, the fighters' academy. Their class, which began circa 1317 DR was unusually large; it began with 25 students and ended with 22.

At the next Grand Melee, Drizzt gained his revenge on Kelnozz by eliminating him early, reducing him to last in their class.
