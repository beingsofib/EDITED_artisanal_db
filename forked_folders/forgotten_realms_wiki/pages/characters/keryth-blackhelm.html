High Marshal Keryth Blackhelm was a moon elf and high marshal of the island of Evermeet. He was also a member of the Council of Evermeet. He was responsible for the defense of the island kingdom.

Possessions

He wielded a fabulous enchanted spear called Khormalashalal.
