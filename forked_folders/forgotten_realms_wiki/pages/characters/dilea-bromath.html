Dilea Bromath was a paladin of the Silver Flame in the world of Eberron.

Description

Dilea was a human woman with pale skin and a bob of black hair. She wore a full suit of chain mail and a white surcoat.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, Dilea was one of many who arrived to investigate the Spinner's Prison following the escape of the Spinner of Shadows. They found the Rift Between Worlds, a massive portal to the Demonweb Pits, and struggled to understand its nature while attempting to prevent it from threatening Eberron. Dilea correctly speculated that Lolth's domain was a realm of demons, and worked with Geras Pellim and Tanibur Evs to prepare a response.
