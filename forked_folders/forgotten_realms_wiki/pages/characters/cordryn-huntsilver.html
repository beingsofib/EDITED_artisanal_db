Cordryn Huntsilver

Cordryn Huntsilver, was a noble from Cormyr. He was involved with Gaspar Cormaeril and Aunadar Bleth in the Abraxus affair , Cordyn was Maestoon's last surviving son, was exiled and disinherited for his involvement in the Abraxus Affair
