Bonnalurie was a sun elf and priestess of Angharradh in Tintageer. When her home was destroyed in a war, she was the last surviving priestess with enough power to control High Magic. On behalf of Durothil, she began to dance and sing to create a magical gate through the boundaries of the realms. In doing this, she helped fifty survivors and the prince to reach Faerûn, but she was consumed by the power of the magical forces she unleashed and was killed.
