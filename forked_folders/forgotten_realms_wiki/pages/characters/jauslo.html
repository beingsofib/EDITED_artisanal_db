Civilar Jauslo was a member of Waterdeep's City Watch who was trusted by the Open Lord, Laeral Silverhand circa 1490s DR.

Personality

Jauslo was a serious, stern, and brutal woman who has committed her life to the service to the City of Splendors. She was mistrusting and cruel towards criminals, on occasion throwing them into the Castle Waterdeep's dungeons, simply forgetting about them and letting them slowly die of starvation and dehydration. She always prioritized her duty over her morals. Still, she was willing to turn a blind eye to the individuals who performed goodly deeds, like freeing slaves.

Activities

Jauslo was in charge of a small group of loyal blades.

History

Civilar Jauslo was a loyal member of the City Watch member entrusted by Open Lord, Laeral Silverhand with the mission of recovery of her favorite pet, a baby griffin who was stolen from of the Lord's mansion by the Xanathar's Thieves' Guild circa 1490s DR. Jauslo captured the halfling thief who unsuccessfully attempted to pick the Open Lord's pockets and after making sure the thief was not the member of Xanathar's Guild, "requested" the halfling's aid. She outfitted the little thief with disguise and pointed the halfling towards Skullport, the underground city from which the Thieves' Guild operated from.
