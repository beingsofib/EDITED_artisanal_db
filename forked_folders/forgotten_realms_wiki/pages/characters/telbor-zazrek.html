Telbor Zazrek was a mage and the High Lord of Loudwater in the late 15 century DR.

History

Telbor was an ex-adventurer who came to rule Loudwater by the late 1480s DR,

Activities

Telbor was in fact a puppet ruler controlled by the Zhentarim; he enjoyed both the money and the power they granted him. Thanks to Telbor, the Zhents secretly had a tight grip on Loudwater and they kept taxes and the costs of living high.
