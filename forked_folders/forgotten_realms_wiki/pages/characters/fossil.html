Fossil was a remnant of an angel in service to the god Cyric. In 1479 DR, he existed in the form of a sentient mask. He recalled the spirit of Madri from the dead and manipulated her into plotting against the deva Demascus.

Description

Fossil appeared to be a plain mask. As an angel, he was 8 feet (2.4 meters) tall and had black wings.
