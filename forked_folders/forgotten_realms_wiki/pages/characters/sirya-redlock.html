Sirya Redlock was a ranger in the Northern Wastes of Kara-Tur.

Activities

Sirya's mother was a shaman of the Issacort people and her father a ranger from Faerûn. She learned all about her father's profession and started wandering over all the Wastes, and also to south and even visiting her father's place of birth. She never stopping wandering. People often asked her counsel about the Wastes' terrain.

Personality

Sirya was friendly with strangers, loyal to friends, and loved sharing information about her travels.
