Issani was a messanger of Waterdeep.

Biography

Issani was sent from Waterdeep to negotiate with Neverwinter. However, orcs captured this emissary and was tortured. The Luskans wanted Issani to enter Neverwinter and act as a spy.

Gameplay

Whether working with the thieves or the Watch, the Kalach-Cha and the companions had to rescue Issani from the orcs. They would meet an emissary in Bonegnasher Lair, but that emissary is a fraud, who worked with the orcs. Instead, Issani would be found in Eyegouger Lair and would be transported to Neverwinter.
