Paul was a human pirate from the isle of Brynnlaw in the Sea of Swords in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Paul wore standard garb of a buccaneer – a tunic and baggy pants.

History

In the , Paul could be found drinking in the Vulgar Monkey dock-side tavern alongside the rest of the Bleedin' Mary's crew members. Like so many fellow pirate customers of the rowdy watering hole, he was an avid shanty-singer. Fer 'e's a Jolly Good Fellow one one of his favorites.
