Jolene Dander was a dealer in potions, spell components, and various alchemical reagents in the town of Eveningstar in the 15 century DR.

Description

Jolene was a diminutive hin woman with curly red hair and pale skin tone. She wore a leather apron with a dress and choker.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Jolene Dander could be seen at her stall in Eveningstar's Market Square, selling rare ingredients. She ran out of supplies due to the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, and made the decision to travel into the nearby King's Forest to gather herbs and other reagents, hoping to go unnoticed by the invaders. Most unfortunately (but predictably) she was captured by the drow and kept in a cage, but before anything worse could happen she was rescued by Cormyrean heroes. The irrepressible halfling then immediately tried selling her rescuers some reagents.
