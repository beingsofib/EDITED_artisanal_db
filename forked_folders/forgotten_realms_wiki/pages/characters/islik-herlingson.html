Islik Herlingson was a dwarf and leader of the Herlinga Clan in the Hordelands.

Description

Like all Herlinga dwarves, Islik was red-bearded.

Activities

Islik led the Herlinga dwarves from Sossal into the Hordelands where he discovered the Glittering Spires during the mid-1000s DR.
