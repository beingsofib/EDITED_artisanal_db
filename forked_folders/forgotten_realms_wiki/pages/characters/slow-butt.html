Slow-Butt was a precocious child from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in late 15 century DR.

Description

Slow-Butt was a human boy with blush sin-kissed skin, blonde curly hair, and brown eyes.

History

In the , Slow-Butt spent his days playing with fellow children of the Gate, Punkins and Cinnamon. The kids played steel watchers and the spies of the Absolute. Slow-Butt was bored with the game, not getting to be the steel watcher, while his playmate were thoroughly entertained with the candy smuggling and bluffing that were parts of the game.
