Raffaldini was a well-respected and popular halfling tailor that lived in Waterdeep circa 1372 DR.

History

By 1372 DR, Raffaldini was a tailor in high demand among the most wealthy noble houses across Faerûn. Raffaldini's clothes were expensive, well-tailored, and always at the height of fashion. The halfling tailor was famous for the exquisite silk clothes.
