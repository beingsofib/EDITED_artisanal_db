Luar was a loyal servant marid of Ajhuu, who rebelled against Calim and formed the independent Maridlands.

History

After rebelling against Calim's rule around , Luar and Haj served as lieutenants of Ajhuu during the Steam War and fought to defend the new realm. The pair were either destroyed or bound by powerful magics, and the aftereffects of this was claimed to be Hajluar's Falls at the head of the Maridflow river.
