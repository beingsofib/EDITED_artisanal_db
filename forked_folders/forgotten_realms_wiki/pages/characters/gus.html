Gus was a priest of Ilmater from Kuldahar in the Spine of the World.

Description

The priest had light brown hair and wore gray robes.

Relationships

Gus had a twin brother Ferg and helped Sister Calliana at the House of the Bleeding Rose.

History

Around Eleint, 1281 DR, Sister Calliana was aided by Gus and his brother Ferg after Mother Egenia disappeared while seeking missing townsfolk from Kuldahar.
