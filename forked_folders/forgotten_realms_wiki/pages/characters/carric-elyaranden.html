Carric El'Yaranden was an elven minstrel from the island of Gwynneth who traveled the Moonshae Isles during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Carric was a talented musician and storyteller who played the lyre.

Personality

He was raised to believe in the inherent superiority of elven culture, and that it was an inspiration to all other peoples. He naturally assumed that everyone shared this view, and as a result, non-elves perceived Carric as hopelessly naïve about the world and about cultures outside his own. This naïveté made him awkward and unintentionally funny.

History

In the , a young Carric left Gwynneth for the first time and traveled to the town of Warlsbry on the island of Alaron, where he stayed at the Once Dry Toad Inn. There, he performed songs and told stories of elven glories and lost loves, many of which casually reinforced the theme of elven superiority. He was surprised to find that his human audience began to heckle him over these themes, and some even accused him of being a spy for Sarifal.
