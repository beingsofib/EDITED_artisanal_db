Samarkhan

Person

Samarkhan was a pasha in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR. Samarkhan's high-class villa lay in Eyer Drudach, Axash Sabban, in the city's Emerald Ward and comprised multiple two- and four-story buildings.
