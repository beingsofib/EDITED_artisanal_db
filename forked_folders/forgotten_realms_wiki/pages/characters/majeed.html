Majeed was a earth genasi caravanner and guard leader who lived in the land of Turmish during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Both his eyes and the lines that patterned his skin shone a bright gold color.

Abilities

While working with the caravan master Faroz, Majeed was put in charge of commanding their group's guardsmen.

History

In the , Majeed was present on the caravan journey that saw his company pick up four travelers, each of whom was the chosen do a different Faerûnian deity. He helped lead their defense against orc raiders, and shades of Netheril.
