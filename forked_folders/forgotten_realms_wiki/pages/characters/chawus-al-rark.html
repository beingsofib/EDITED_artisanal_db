Chawus al-Rark was a wealthy merchant and smuggler inhabiting the Free City of Utaqa in north Zakhara in .

Activities

Chawus made his fortune in the wilds of far northern Zakhara. He had a deal with the corsairs of the Corsair Domains where he helped them smuggle their goods into Utaqa in exchange for the corsairs agreeing to cease attacking any ships flying Utaqa's colors.

Chawus was also an advisor for Caliph Agara al-Gandar.
