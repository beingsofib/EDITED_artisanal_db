Chief Glorlarkh was the former ruler of the Tornskulls orcs, that dwelled within Dead Orc Pass during the 14 century DR.

Description

He was known as a ferocious chieftain and war-leader.

Relationships

The chief was survived by his wife and mate Baerzel, who was known for her exceptional wisdom.

History

Glorlarkh died sometime around the , after he was caught within a cavern when it collapsed.
