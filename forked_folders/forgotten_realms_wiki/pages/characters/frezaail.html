Frezaail was an old male dragon who dwelled in the vicinity of Ironspur in the Galena Mountains circa 1351 DR.

History

In the , the adventuring mercenary named Skullcrusher, who originally hailed from the city of Phlan, tangled with Frezaail's mate in a memorable event of which he reminisced in his twilight years. By the adventurer's description, Frezaail was an infamous "old bastard."
