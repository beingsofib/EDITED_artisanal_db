Telornym was a human male cleric of Mystra and the Lord of Mystery at the Tower of Mysteries in Saerloon, Sembia. He was the second-in-command of the Tower of Mysteries, assisting the Mage Arcane Tanthala. He was also a senior member of the Moonstars in 1370 DR.

Telornym and his wife Shaal, a priestess of Azuth, lived in a house in Saerloon owned by Khelben Arunsun but rented to Telornym for thirty years.
