Joyella was a gnome and active member of the Zhentarim in the Underdark in the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa 1486 DR, Joyella was a member of a Zhent party in the Underdark. They tried to rob an underground drow caravan but failed, and a bounty was placed on her. She was tracked and captured by two duergar bounty hunters. The duergar later tracked another bounty, the traitorous drow Arrizz, to the Goodroot farm in the Moonsea, but were killed by some adventurers.
