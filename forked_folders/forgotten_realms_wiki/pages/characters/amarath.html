Amarath was a member of the Cult of the Howling Hatred sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Amarath and his fellow N'von led a contingent of cultists on giant vultures in search of a cloud giant willing to ally with their cult. By accident, they found the flying Tower of Zephyros and went to speak with the giant, Zephyros.

Propriety

Amarath possessed a bag of holding with an invisible stalker inside.
