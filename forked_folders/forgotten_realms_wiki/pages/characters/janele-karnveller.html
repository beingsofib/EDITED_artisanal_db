Janele Karnveller was a rancher and owner of the Happy Horse Ranch in Triboar in the Dessarin Valley in the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

A widow, she was the matriarch of the Karnveller family. She had three grown sons: Aldo, Hingo, and Rasko Karnveller.

Personality

She was feisty and would rather deal with horses than people.
