Duddy Dudderken was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Duddy was a human boy with a light olive skin tone, short, curly dark brown hair, and dark brown eyes.

History

In the , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, Duddy Dudderken attended a performance of some showy magics by three spellslingers, on the Murl's Rest plaza in front of Sorcerous Sundries. The boy obnoxiously yelled demands of bigger and louder magics.
