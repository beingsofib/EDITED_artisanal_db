Madame Freona was a halfling businesswoman in Phlan.

Personality

Freona had a keen taste for the finer things in life. She was a good cook, a terrible gossip hound, and a very skilled entrepreneur. She loved her city and hated the dragon usurper.

Activities

Freona managed the Madame Freona's Tea Kettle tea house with the help of her daughters Reece, Briez, Grelinda, Blaizette and Whittlee.

History

Freona's tea house was one of the most famous places in Phlan in 1489 DR.

After Vorgansharax's coup, she hid in her shop many individuals sought after by the Tears of Virulence.

For this, the dragon ordered the tea house to be burned down, but an adventuring party hired by the resistance managed to help Freona to escape from the city.
