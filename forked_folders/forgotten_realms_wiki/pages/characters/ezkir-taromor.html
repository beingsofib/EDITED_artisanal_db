Ezkir Taromor was a Red Wizard of Thay that joined Droic Orgul's raid on Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Abilities

Ezkir was a skilled necromancer and could easily animate skeletons to fight foes and protect him.

History

Ezkir joined Vorinar Gall at the Burial Glen of Myth Drannor as they searched for the remains of Earynspieir Ongluth.
