Baerlos Dunthar was a joiner inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid–to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Baerlos was the main public point of contact for the Fellowship of Salters, Packers, and Joiners in Waterdeep. He worked out of Shippers' Hall, which was their guild headquarters located on Oar Alley in the Dock Ward.
