Garen'thir Szind was a drider exiled from House Szind that lived near Sschindylryn in the 1480s DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Garen'thir had been exiled and lived as a recluse in the caverns south of the city, with only a few spiders for company.
