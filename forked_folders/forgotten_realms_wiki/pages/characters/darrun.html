Darrun was a horse belonging to the adventuress Kendra in the mid–14 century DR. It was trained for battle.

Description

It had a dark gray coat.

History

Kendra rode Darrun through her travels in Cormyr in the , from Wheloon to Ghars on Eleint 16 and even into the Vast Swamp on Eleint 17.
