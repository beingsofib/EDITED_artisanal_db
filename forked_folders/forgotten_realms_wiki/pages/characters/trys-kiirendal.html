Trys Kiirendal was a half-elf ranger and adventurer for hire in the city of Neverwinter, active in the late 15 century DR.

History

Trys Kiirendal spent most of her life sheltered from the world and danger by her father. Eventually, however, she came to pursue an adventurer's career by 1479 DR. Despite her youth and lack of experience, Kiirendal offered her services in adventuring and retrieving crafting components for defenders of Neverwinter.
