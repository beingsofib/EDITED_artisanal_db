Angram Baergil (? – 1333 DR) was a farmer who part-owned a farm near to Ashabenford, Mistledale. He was the father of famous Captain Darrask Baergil of the Riders of Mistledale.

Relatives

Angram was married to Baelaetha Baergil, and they had seven children: three boys and four girls. His first two sons were Athror Baergil and Captain Darrask Baergil.

Death

Angram died in 1333 DR, from a winter fever.
