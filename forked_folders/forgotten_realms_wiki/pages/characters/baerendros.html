Baerendros, known as "the Blade", was a hiresword from Sembia in the mid–14 century DR and a noted supporter of the Lost King Gondegal in his uprising in the city of Arabel in Cormyr of the .

History

Baerendros was already wanted by the Cormyrean authorities for various robberies and killings.

During Gondegal's war, Baerendros was declared an outlaw in a proclamation penned by the Sage Royal Alaphondar on Ches 11, 1352 DR, and was wanted dead or alive by the crown.
