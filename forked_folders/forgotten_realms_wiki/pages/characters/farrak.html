Farrak was an adventurer in the ruin of Spellgard.

Personality

Farrak and Harrak did everything together. They had the same morality as Devlin but kept an eye on their leader to avoid possible betrayals.

History

Farrak and his twin Harrak were evil adventurers and members of the Free Company of the Crescent. In 1479 DR, he went with his fellows to Spellgard hoping to find Lady Saharel's treasures.
