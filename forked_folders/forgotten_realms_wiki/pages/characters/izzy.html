Izzy was a child who lived in the Rivington district of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

She was a spirited human youth with pale skin, chestnut eyes and reddish-brown hair.

History

In the , the famed Circus of the Last Days returned to the city of Baldur's Gate. Izzy and her friend Mavus were curious about the wondrous creatures that the circus brought to Rivington. Izzy cared not for what adults had to say, and she was dead set on getting into the circus. Izzy and Mavus were trying to peep inside and vault over the circus walls to get inside.
