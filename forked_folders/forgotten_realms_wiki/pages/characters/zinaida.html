Zinaida was a half-orcish blacksmith for hire from the city of Neverwinter, active in the late 15 century DR.

History

Unlike many half-orcs, Zinaida did not subscribe to being unnoticed to avoid harassment and discrimination. Instead, she elected to become a master blacksmith, better than most of her peers by the late 15 century DR.
