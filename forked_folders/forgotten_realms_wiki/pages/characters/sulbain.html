Sulbain, also called Steeleye, was a pegasus who was stabled at Sarahin, the great fortress of the Everlasting.

Activites

The Grandfather of the Everlasting trained Sulbain since birth, relying on harsh methods to bend the magical creature to his will.

The Grandfather was the only person allowed to ride Sulbain.
