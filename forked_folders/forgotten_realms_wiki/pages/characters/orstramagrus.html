Orstramagrus was a merchant in the late 15 century DR. He was a guest at the Open Feast of .

Description

Orstramagus was fat, with chubby hands. At the Open Feast, he wore velvets that quickly became wine-stained, and many rings on his hands. To Amarune Whitewave, his voice was like braying.

Personality

During the Open feast, guests found the man coming across as a blowhard, and a smart-mouth. He had a taste for batter-fried prawns.

Abilities

Whether correct or not, he was quite the pedant for undead nomenclature.

Possessions

He wore many rings, and velvets, to the Open Feast.

Activities

Orstramagrus was a merchant.

History

Orstramagrus was a guest at the Open Feast in Mirtul 26 of the in Castle Obarskyr, Suzail. He was quite insistent about the terminology of undead, and got in a jab at Kathkote Dawntard, who took it in good cheer against everyone's expectations.
