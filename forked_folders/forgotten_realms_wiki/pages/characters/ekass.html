Ekass was an enchanter and one of five novice wizards stationed at the enclave run by the young Red Wizard Hinnar.

Description

Ekass' body bore a magical tattoo

Abilities

Ekass was tough, dexterous, and intelligent and was trained in alchemy, spellcraft, and the scribing of scrolls.

Possessions

Ekass carried a dagger and a small crossbow.

Ekass' spellbook contained nineteen different spells.

Activities

Ekass alternated with the other five novice wizards in running the compound's sales booth with two other companions. When sales were slow, Ekass might offer assistance to the wizards responsible for crafting the magic goods that were for sale.
