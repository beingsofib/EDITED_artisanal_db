Huang Chih-Wei was a healer woman of some repute of antiquity from the nation of Shou Lung in Kara-Tur long before the 15 century DR.

History

Many years before the , Huang Chih-Wei was a simple healer who was contemporary to Nan Kung Chi. The man named Nan Kung Chi was also known as the Black Leopard and he held significant amount of power. Huang Chih-Wei was a strong woman and refused to kneel before the Leopard to which the man retaliated. The same thing happened to Huang Chih-Wei that happened to all who threatened men in power. By the late 14 century DR, Huang Chih-Wei's story was still remembered by some, including a vampire treasure hunter named Hexxat.
