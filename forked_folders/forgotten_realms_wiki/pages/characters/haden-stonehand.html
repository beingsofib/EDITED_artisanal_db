Haden Stonehand

Person
nameHaden Stonehand
homeSerpent Valley, Utter East

Haden Stonehand was a bloodforge mage operating out of the Serpent Valley in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

Haden Stonehand was a hardy soul who made a home in the harsh Serpent Valley. The leader of the Legendary Campaign, who aimed to unify the Utter East through conquest, came to challenge Haden in a contest of bloodforge warfare.
