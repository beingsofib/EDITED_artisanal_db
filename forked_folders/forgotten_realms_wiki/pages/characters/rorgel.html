Rorgel, nicknamed Silent, was an apprentice of Maerjanthra Shalace in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Rorgel almost never spoke, hence his monicker.

Relationships

In Narnra Shalace's recollection, her mother, Maerjanthra Shalace, had five apprentices: Goraun, Rorge, Jonczer, Tantheld, and Rivrel. She learned that at least one of them believed her mother to be a literal dragon. She could not confirm that any of them were dead other than Rivrel, knowing only that they disappeared after her mother's death.
