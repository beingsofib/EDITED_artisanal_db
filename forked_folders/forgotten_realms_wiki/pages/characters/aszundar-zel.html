Aszundar Zel was a diplomat of the city of Neverwinter on the Sword Coast North in the 1350s DR.

Around 1354 DR, Aszundar Zel paid a visit to the royal court of Cormyr and met Princess Alusair Obarskyr, later describing her as "impish" and "winsomely beautiful".
