Slicker was one of the many beggars who lived on the streets of the city of Westgate in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Slicker was a rawboned human man with a light, unhealthy skin tone, brown hair, and brown mustache. He wore a beggar's rags. Slicker's eyes darted nervously around the street, and his behavior was reminiscent of that of a cornered animal.

Personality

When Slicker was not begging, his demeanor changed to rude sarcasm, but he still referred to himself in the third person.

History

In the , Slicker was begging on the Market Triangle of Westgate, when a nearby building exploded into a pyre. Unlike the destroyed building, a stack of crates owned by Belub-Zi, a well-connected merchant who manned his stall on the Triangle, was safe, as only a madman would dare to explode Belub-Zi's goods. Slicker decided to stick close to them as that was, in his opinion, the safest place in the area. When a group of adventurers interrogated Slicker about the fiery wreck, he revealed to them that all sorts of shady individuals were going in and out of the building, and recently, a group of new faces went inside, right before the explosion. In reality, the building was used by a group of cultists who worshiped the ancient lich Kokrulobis and the explosion was an attempt to rid the city of the cult's foul presence.
