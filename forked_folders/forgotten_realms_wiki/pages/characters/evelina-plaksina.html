Evelina Plaksina was an earth genasi and a member of the Cult of the Black Earth in the Moonsea.

History

In 1491 DR, Evelina was part of the cell led by Yegor that pursued the dwarf Furgis Boltsmelter to take the elemental codex. She was part of the cultists that attacked Furgis and his adventuring party bodyguards when they were confronted by a Hammer of Moradin squad near Mulmaster.
