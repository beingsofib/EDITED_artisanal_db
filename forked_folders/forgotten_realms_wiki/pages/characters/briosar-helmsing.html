Briosar Helmsing was a high priest ranger of Mielikki in the 14 century DR.

History

Between 1357 - 1367 DR Briosar operated a shrine to Mielikki in Waterdeep's Sea Ward that was known as The Lady's Hands. Around 1367 DR it joined together with the nearby Shrine of Silvanus to form a temple complex known as the Shrines of Nature, which he operated jointly with the other shrine's high priest, Anarakin Iriboar.
