Langdedrosa Cyanwrath was a half–blue dragon member of the reformed Cult of the Dragon.

Description

Cyanwrath was seven feet tall with bright blue scales. He had draconic features and sharp claws.

Personality

While the half-dragon had a strong sense of honor, he could be driven to wrathful rage.

Possessions

In battle, Langdedrosa wore splint mail and fought with greatsword or spear.

Relationships

Cyanwrath was the right hand of Frulam Mondath.

History

Around 1489 DR, Langdedrosa was one of the leaders in the assault on Greenest. During the battle, he challenged Sergeant Markguth, one of the strongest of the defenders of the town, to a duel, promising to spare the lives of Greenest's people in exchange.

A few days later, he was at the Cult's camp near the city in the Greenfields. During an attack on the dragon hatchery, Cyanwrath was killed with a group of his guards in the shrine to Tiamat.
