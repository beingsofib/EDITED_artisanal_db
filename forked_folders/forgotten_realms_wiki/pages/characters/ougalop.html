Ougalop was a kuo-toa in Mantol-Derith during the late 1480s or early 1490s DR.

History

Ougalop hailed from Sloobludop. During the demonic crisis that struck the Underdark in the late 1480s DR, he went near Mantol-Derith to hunt some giant albino cave crickets.
