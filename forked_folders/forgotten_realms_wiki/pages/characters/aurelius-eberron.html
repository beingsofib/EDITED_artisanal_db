Brother Aurelius was a scholarly servant of the Silver Flame in the world of Eberron.

Description

Aurelius was a pale human man with short reddish-brown hair. He wore a white and blue surcoat with the symbol of the Silver Flame.

History

Chosen as a trusted follower of the Silver Flame, Aurelius swore an oath to protect Eberron from the Lords of Dust and was teleported to the Spinner's Prison in order to ensure the integrity of the wards and seals. He spent many years in this duty without issue, living in a makeshift home and working with the Silver Flame Guardian, until the cult of the Spinner of Shadows found the road to Tarath Marad and used a ritual to blast open an entrance from the surface. Unable to stop the small army of cultists and monsters that broke through the defenses, Aurelius could do little but observe and wait until his comrade Orrex sent a team down to find him. After the priestess Sabriz Rinzyn Kho was slain in battle, Aurelius was found and he explained the dire situation at hand. With little time to spare, the heroes proceeded into the Spinner's Prison and faced the demon Overlord herself in a final battle.
