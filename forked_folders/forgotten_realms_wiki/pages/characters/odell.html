O'Dell was the Caravan Master of the Crossroad Keep in the late 14 century DR.

Description

O'Dell was an old and rugged man who spent his entire life as a laborer. His leathery skin was weathered and muscles – lean and tough.

History

By the , O'Dell had spent most of his life working for caravans in the north. When the Crossroad Keep was reclaimed by Neverwinter and subsequently became the the hub for the Mercantile House of merchant princess Sa'Sani of Samarach, O'Dell found himself working as the Caravan Master for the coaster's activities in Northwest Faerûn. Caravans under O'Dell's supervision traveled between the Crossroad Keep, Neverwinter, Leilon, Conyberry, New Leaf, Highcliff, Phandalin, Thundertree, Port Llast, and the West Harbor.
