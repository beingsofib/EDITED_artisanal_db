Sheera bint Tanar was an advisor to the Sheikh of Tajar in the Pearl Cities of Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Activities

As a skilled sha'ir, Sheera served as Sheikh Ali al-Hadd's magical advisor. She maintained Tajar's strong relationships with the jann and monitored all genie activity within the city.

Relationships

Sheera was actually the Sheikh's daughter, though he had not publicly recognized her. Her mother was an elven courtesan in the Sheikh's court.
