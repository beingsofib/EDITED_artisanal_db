Naremos was a Zhentarim bounty hunter sometime after the War of the Silver Marches of 1485 DR.

Appearance

Naeremos was missing his left arm, which he had lost to a troll.

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, posing as officers of Mirabar, Naeremos and his partner Sorelisa Zandra were sent to find the notorious dwarf criminal Worvil "the Weevil" Forkbeard to offer him protection and a recruitment offer.
