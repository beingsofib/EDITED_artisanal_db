Nind was a knight of Athalantar turned to banditry in the early 3 century DR.

Relationships

He was one of the Knights of Athalantar under Helm Stoneblade.

History

Nind was part of the team led by Sargeth, which included Eladar the Dark (actually Prince Elminster Aumar) that prepared an ambush against armsmen of Athalantar in Hammer of the . In the aftermath, he helped butcher the armsmen's horses for meat.
