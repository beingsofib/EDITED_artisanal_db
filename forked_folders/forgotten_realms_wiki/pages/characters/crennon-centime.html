Crennon Centime was a human wizard and jeweler from Amn researching magic in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He was a jeweler before he was a magician, and just dabbled in magic until he developed what he called money magic. His life-goal after becoming a powerful wizard was to create a money-fireball that would explode in a shower of coins instead of flames.

Personality

For Crennon greed was truth, and the only worthwhile magic was that which made him ever richer.

Possessions

A competent and wealthy mage he wore a powerful set of bracers of defense, and had the useful Bucknard's everfull purse and eyes of minute seeing. He also sometimes wielded a wand of metal and mineral detection.
