Tethir the Dragonslayer was an elven hero and the first elven dragonslayer.

Relationships

Tethir was related to the elven hero Keltormir.

History

Tethir sacrificed his own life to slay two ancient red dragons living in the Ridge, allowing most of his tribe to escape to the southern reaches of the forest.
