Lambert was a wainwright of Eaglton in the Vast during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Besides repairing wheels and wagons, Lambert made barrels.

History

During the spring in which the vampire Romanisk Vlaensk was released from under Stag Falls and proceeded to take over that village, Lambert held a job as a wainwright in the nearby town of Eaglton.
