Brathwen was a duergar slaver and a cultist of the Absolute who dwelled Grymforge during the late 15 century DR.

Description

He had dark gray skin and white hair and beard. He wore black padded clothing lined with silver and an amulet bearing the symbol of the Absolute.

Possessions

Brathwen wielded a warhammer in combat.

History

In the , Brathwen, Viss and other duergars of their clan aligned themselves with the cult of the Absolute. After the cult slaughtered and enslaved a community of deep gnomes, Brathen and Viss were assigned the unfortunate task of dumping their corpses into the waters of the Darklake.
