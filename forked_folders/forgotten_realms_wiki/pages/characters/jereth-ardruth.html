Jereth Ardruth

Person
nameJereth Ardruth
aliasesDruth
homeSuzail, Cormyr
occupationWar Wizard

Jereth Ardruth was a War Wizard, contemporary of either Palaghard Obarskyr I or Palaghard Obarskyr II in their youths.

Possessions

They were assigned a closet that terminated at a triple pillar.

History

Jereth Ardruth had been used as a landmark by one of the Palaghards.
