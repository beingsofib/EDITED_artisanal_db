Turik Bloodhelm was a High Sword of Tempus in the 14 century DR.

History

In the mid-to-late 14 century DR, Turik lived in the city of Waterdeep. There they operated as part of the clergy of the Tempuran temple House of Heroes from 1357 DR and through 1368 DR.
