Pratcus was a dwarf priest of Damara in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Pratcus had the typical sturdy build of a dwarf with muscular arms. He wore a sleeveless studded leather vest.

Abilities

Pratcus could enact all kinds of priestly magic, such as casting the bull's strength spell.
