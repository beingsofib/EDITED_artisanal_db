Gulkin Hammerlin was a surly carpenter in High Dale.

History

Gulkin was for a brief time a mercenary hailing from Daerlun before he settled in High Dale. When in 1358 DR a secret Zhent occupation army occupied the dale, Gulkin was a member of the Council of the High Dale. Gulkin hated all usurpers and especially the wizards.
