Acciajuoli was a Netherese arcanist and co-owner of Transgate Industries, who lived in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in .

After the death of her parents Ocelot and Ytartia, Acciajuoli operated Transgate Industries alongside Shander and Thamer. The three siblings managed to do extremely well and greatly increased the success of the company.
