Marcus Rubicon was the field general of Ravens Bluff in the Vast during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

At the time of his retirement, he was a middle-aged man with steel-gray hair and brown eyes.

Personality

The man was responsible, and openly mindful of his reputation.

Possessions

He bore a Marshal's Rod, and his office was covered in enemy standards, presumably from vanquished forces.

Relationships

He was served by two staffers, Malcom and Duncan. The rod he bore marked him as a member of the Knights of the Raven who'd presided over forces in a victorious battle.

History

Marcus Rubicon had once been feted in a festival for a victory over Zhentarim forces. His office was rich in enemy standards that had presumably been taken from defeated enemies.

Years later, before being replaced by Charles Frederik LaVerne Blacktree IV Marcus Rubicon had been responsible for dispatching a patrol to Stag Falls, a nearby town under the protection of Ravens Bluff, after noticing they'd missed the annual taxes. Noticing the patrol had not returned for two weeks, he had adventurers briefed and sent there to investigate.
