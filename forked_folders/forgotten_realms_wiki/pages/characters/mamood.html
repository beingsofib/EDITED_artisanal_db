Mamood lived in the Pilgrims' District of Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Activities

Mamood owned a large tent in the Pilgrims' District which he rented out to travelers and pilgrim's, providing them with a safe place to sleep for only one bit per night. Mamood did not provide food, but did employ a few mercenaries to ensure his guests slept soundly.
