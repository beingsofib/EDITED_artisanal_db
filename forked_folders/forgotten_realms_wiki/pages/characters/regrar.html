Regrar was a Purple Dragon Knight in Azoun IV's personal retinue during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

In Azoun's personal retinue, he served with others including Daervin and Forold.

History

Regrar was leading the effort to dig a grave for Daervin in a gorge near Eveningstar during King Azoun IV's visit to Tessaril Winter, after Highknight Daervin was slain by a malaugrym. While digging, he extensively discussed the subject of Tessaril's exact relationship to the king, explaining to his fellow Highknights that Lord Tessaril was close personally with Azoun, and that he was certain of her loyalty. He later spotted that he was being scried on by Hlael Toraunt and Korthauvar Hammantle, and warned his fellow knights to fetch a War Wizard.
