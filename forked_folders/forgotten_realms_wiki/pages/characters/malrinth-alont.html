Malrinth Alont was a half-elf diviner who specialized in informational magic.

Description

Malrinth had light skin, blue eyes, and blonde hair partially tied into a bun. She had a pug nose, pointed ears, and wore an elaborate piece of hair jewelry.

Personality

A firm believer in knowledge being the most important factor in any venture, Malrinth preferred working out of her study, only taking to the road when a mystery necessitated travel. She was uncommonly fond of the sea and all things related to it.

Possessions

Malrinth owned a powerful ring of protection, in addition to her dagger and spellbook.
