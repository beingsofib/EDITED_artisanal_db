Daitya was the stone giant ruler of the Lodge of Faces in the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa 1486 DR, Daitya and all her fellows succumbed to the faerzress-infected madness caused by the Purple Statue. Daitya became obsessed with carving a perfect replica of the Purple Statue.
