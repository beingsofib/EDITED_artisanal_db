Roldo

Roldo was a member of a mob at the doors of the House of the Ladystar in the mid–8 century DR.

Relationships

The name of Roldo was known to at least one guard at the House of the Ladystar.

History

In the , two tendays into the Chaos of the Art of that year, Roldo was part of a mob that attempted to storm the gates of the House of the Ladystar. While pushing at the gates, Roldo declared that if the guards cast a spell, they'd leave; one of the guards responded to him by name, telling Roldo that guards didn't cast spells.
