Girmond Dyle was an elven Harper agent active in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

He had black hair and pale skin, and wore brown armor.

History

Following the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon circa 1485 DR and Oriphaun Huntsilver's disappearance with a Nether Scroll, Girmond was sent to meet with the Cormyrean-aligned adventurers who had gathered up the Scroll pieces after finding them across the High Road. All of the remaining fragments were in the hands of the Netherese forces occupying Granwell Keep within the Blooded Wood, and so he sent them to assault the Keep and claim them while he safeguarded those the Harpers had taken. However, he was shortly after discovered and killed by the Netherese forces, who claimed the pieces of the Nether Scroll for themselves and put his corpse on display for the Cormyrean forces to find at the entrance of the Keep.
