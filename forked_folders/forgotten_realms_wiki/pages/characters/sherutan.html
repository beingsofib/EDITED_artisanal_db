Sheru'Tan was the constable of the new community of Spiderhaven inside Undermountain in 1375 DR.

Personality

Sheru'Tan was an intelligent but implacable foe for anyone who would disturb the peace of Spiderhaven. Usually she was fair-minded in her dealings, despite her intimidating figure.

Activities

Sheru'Tan was busy patrolling the settlement.
