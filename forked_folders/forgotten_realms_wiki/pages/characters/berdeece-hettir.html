Berdeece Hettir was a local woman in Hastarl in Athalantar during the early 3 century DR.

Description

She was an old woman.

History

In the , Berdeece Hettir was part of a public sharing of news in broad daylight where it was said that a woman had slain a dozen magelords. She said the woman in question had bit off their heads and spat 'em out, to which someone stomped on her foot. She resented that.
