Minn was a forest gnome wizard specialized in illusion magic.

Possessions

Minn supplemented her impressive magical skills with a deck of illusions to produce illusions of whatever (or whoever) she required at a moment's notice.

History

As a child, Minn enjoyed the company of a whole host of imaginary friends. As she grew older, she devoted her life to illusion magic to make her friends real—well, semi-real.
