Udhask was a deep gnome ghost in Blingdenstone in the late 15 century DR.

History

When the drow attacked Blingdenstone in 1371 DR, he tried to reach for his loot when he died of a heart attack. For unknown reasons, he rose as a ghost. Thereafter, every time someone approached his remains he relived his death in a loop, attacking and scaring people.

If someone recovered his remains and put it in the catacombs, he would find peace.
