Fahd yn Sarsor al Nisr was a leader of the Rundeen mercantile organization, holding the position of a First Yrshelem, in the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

Fahd's son was Farid al Nisr, a wizard and member of the Harpers,

History

In the spring of the , there was a surprise wedding between Fahd's son Farid and Syllia Adarbrent, daughter of House Adarbrent of Waterdeep (and also a Harper). Their families did not have long to be shocked before the pair sailed off to Maztica with no clue when they might return. However, House Adabrent were masters of the Adarbrent Shipping Company and the Horizon Armada league of shipowners, yet were struggling financially. Seizing an opportunity to expand and claim these northern shipping fortunes, Fahd contacted Lord Royus Adarbrent with offers of generous loans at very reasonable rates, if they but signed a contract with the Rundeen. Unfamiliar with the southern merchants, Royus considered this reluctantly and cautiously.
