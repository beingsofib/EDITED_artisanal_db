Marrauda Belaskurth was a human waitress at the Yawning Portal inn in Waterdeep in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

She worked as the senior waitress and kitchen maid at the Yawning Portal.

History

Marrauda had worked for Durnan for years by 1375 DR and had come to know all the regular customers.

Personality

Marrauda was jolly, busy, and brisk about her duties. While she didn't mind the occasional spank or grab from amorous customers, if someone took too many liberties, she could toss out even armored warriors.

Description

She was stout, big-shouldered, and plump.
