Evendur Highcastle was an infamous pirate in the Sea of Fallen Stars who rose as an undead Chosen of Umberlee in the late 15 century DR.

History

Before his death, Evendur was a notorious pirate lord. In the wake of the Second Sundering, the goddess Umberlee resurrected him as her Chosen.

Evendur was tasked with capturing the young Chosen of Lathander, Stedd Whitehorn. He was defeated in the , by the combined forces of Anton Marivaldi, Umara Ankhlab, and the Turmish navy.
