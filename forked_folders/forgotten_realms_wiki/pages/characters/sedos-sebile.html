Sedos Sebile was a member of the Neverwinter Nine in 1372 DR.

Appearance

Sedos had light skin and brown hair, and dressed in a green shirt underneath spiked leather armor.

Relationships

Sedos was trusted by Nasher Alagondar, Lord of Neverwinter.

History

Sedos Sebile once was a rowdy youth in the bustling streets of Waterdeep. Later she became a daring adventurer in the Sword Mountains.

Sedos appeared to have finally found her place in Neverwinter as she had become a respected member of Lord Nasher Alagondar's elite guard, the Neverwinter Nine. Sedos carried out her duties with a composure that would astound those who knew her in her earlier days.

During the outbreak of the Wailing Death in the , Sedos was put in charge of Neverwinter Militia operations in the Peninsula District of Neverwinter. The district was plagued with escaped criminals from Neverwinter Prison, having been freed by the Head Gaoler, Alaefin. When the Hero of Neverwinter came to investigate the plague, Sedos informed them of the situation, and directed them to find a way to enter the prison and deal with Alaefin.
