Malygris was a powerful very old blue dragon dracolich that lived in Anauroch.

Relationships

Malygris was a member of the Cult of the Dragon until 1371 DR.

History

Malygris killed Sussethilasis, the suzerain of approximately twenty blue dragons that resided within the borders of the Great Sand Sea, after becoming a dracolich.

In the , Brennus Tanthul and Clariburnus arrived to the lair of Malygris freeing him from bondage to the Cult, and so the dracolich become a new ally for the emerging conflict between Thultanthar and the people of the Heartlands.
