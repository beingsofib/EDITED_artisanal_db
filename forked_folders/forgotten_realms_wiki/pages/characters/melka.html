Melka

Person
nameMelka
occupationWriter, scholar

Melka was a writer and scholar who penned the Mythal Theory, along with Schend, sometime before the fall of the Hand of the Seldarine citadel on the Spine of the World.

History

Melka lived and published his scholarly works sometime after the Hand of the Seldarine's construction circa 900 DR and its fall in the Battle of Seldarine's Hand. The book on the nature of mythals was considered a prolific work and was taught after as late as 1281 DR.
