Sabyna was a ship's mage in 1370 DR.

History

Sabyna lived all her life on the sea. Her father Siann Truesail and two of her brothers were also ship's mages.

Relationships

Thanks to her family, Sabyna had a network of contacts among ship's mages, captains, sailors and merchants.

Personality

Sabyna had great social skills that permitted her to be at home with all kinds of people. She constantly fed half-true gossip. She loved cooking, especially very spicy foods and all kinds of exotic dishes, and entertaining guests. Sabyna loved her ship the Breezerunner. She fell in love with Jherek Wolf's-get but had doubts about their life together.
