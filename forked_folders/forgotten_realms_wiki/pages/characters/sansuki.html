Sansuki was a fighter member of the Shadow Thieves in Athkatla in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Sansuki was a pale-skinned human male who was dressed in gray garb and simple thieves' leathers.

History

In the , the criminal underbelly of Athkatla erupted in an all-out guild wars between the Shadow Thieves and a vampire coven under Bodhi. Sansuki was one of many thieves killed in the war. He was pursued by vampires led by Del across the night streets of Athkatla's Scepter District. Once Del caught up with the man, Sansuki's fate was sealed.
