Stig was a male human fighter and a bouncer at the tavern called the Furniture's Fate in the town of Thunderstone in Cormyr in the late 1360s DR.

He was very strong.

When brawls broken out, Boffo the tavern owner often had Stig lift him up and hurl the gnome into the fray.
