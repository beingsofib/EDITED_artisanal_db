Harmantle was a shady merchant from Suzail in Cormyr, active in the 14 century DR.

History

In the , merchant Harmantle became involved in criminal tax evasion. Tathanter Doarmond, a Wizard of War placed in charge of the investigation, was presented with two massive letters, one from Harmantle and another for a fellow merchant. Both letters heavily contradicted each other. The final discussion in the case was voiced by Vangerdahast Aeiulvana, the Royal Magician of Cormyr. Both merchants lied in their letters, however, Harmantle was the one sent to the dungeons.
