Laeris was a Painbearer of Ilmater and a member of the adventuring company - the Lady's Lament from Waterdeep, active circa 1310 DR.

Description

Laeris was a contemplative and quiet man who rarely talked and asked for very little. He used a mace, a shield, and a sling in battle.

History

Sometime before the , Laeris joined the Lady's Lament, becoming the team's spiritual guiding force. He complimented the group's leader - Sheris Lyricist's charisma with his wisdom.

In 1310 DR, the Lady's Lament, along with many other mercenaries, traveled from Luskan to the besieged city of Targos in the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale.
