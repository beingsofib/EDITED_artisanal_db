The Forgotten One

Person

The Forgotten One, whose name was lost, was a hermit and wiseman who lived on Mount Pojah in Tabot.

The Forgotten One was one of only five wisemen recognized by the monks of Tabot around as being a true oracle, being capable of predicting the future and raising heroes from the dead for good causes.
