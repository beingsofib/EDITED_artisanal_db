Ragnor was an ogrillon fighter and one of the three leaders of the military contingent of Castle Trinity in the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the , Ragnor, with the support of the wizard Dorigen Kel Lamond, guided the Trinity's invasion of Shilmista, causing great damage to the elves there. The tide of the battle changed when Cadderly Bonaduce and his friends arrived, especially the dwarven brothers Ivan and Pikel Bouldershoulder. Ragnor's army was soon defeated
