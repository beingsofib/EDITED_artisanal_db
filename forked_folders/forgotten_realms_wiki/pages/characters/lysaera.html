Lysaera was a succubus servant of the Queen of the Succubi, Malcanthet, in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Lysaera was a cunning and beautiful inhabitant of the lower planes.

Abilities

The succubus possessed magical powers that allowed her to charm and enchant creatures. She could extend a heartfelt barrier to shield her allies and boost their vitality for a limited amount of time. Among her offensive powers was something she called soul rupture, the ability to syphon parts of her enemies' souls and damage them, and, lastly, she could trigger a temptation nova – an explosion of magical power that damaged her enemies and, in turn, healed the Lysaera.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Lysaera was tasked with helping some of the adventurers from Neverwinter by her mistress, Malcanthet. The purpose of such a strange alliance was unknown.
