Ko Mihn Gi was a Shou mayor in Akari in 1357 DR.

History

Ko Mihn Gi was a member of the mercantile Ko clan, the younger brother of Ko Lei Kho. He was mayor of the town of Shu Lan.

Personality

Mihn Gi thought that Lei Kho was confronting the ochimo treat too mildly but family loyalty prevented him from protesting.
