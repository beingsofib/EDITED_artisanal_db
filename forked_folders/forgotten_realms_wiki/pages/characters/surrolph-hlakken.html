Surrolph Hlakken was a horse merchant at the Hlakken Stables in Waterdeep.

Description

Surrolph was a fat, balding man with thick fingers.

Possessions

Surrolph wore a set of fine robes whose sheen had by 1372 DR long since faded.

Activities

While his surface job was that as a horse merchant at the Hlakken Stables, Surrolph was also a contact of the vigilante group Red Sashes. He was considered to be their chief information gatherer.
