Hastarglyrr was copper dragon that lived on the Western Heartlands region of Faerûn circa the .

Relationships

Hastarglyrr was an ally of the Rillyn noble family of Baldur's Gate, and would seek out any enemies that dared attack the family's estate in the city.

History

Hastarglyrr was born sometime between the late 7 and late 14 centuries DR.
