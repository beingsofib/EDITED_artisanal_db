Shestandeliath Ravar was a dragonborn wizard

History

Ravar was one of the few members of the Liberators of the Steel Sky.

For some unknown reasons, Ravar wasn't present the day the Liberators were killed by a maurezhi granted to the God-King Gilgeam. However, on Nightal 19 of 1486 DR, Ravar went to search for the maurezhi in the catacombs of Djerad Thymar and was killed in the ensuing battle, despite the aid of Yrjixtilex Kallan and Ilstan Nyaril.
