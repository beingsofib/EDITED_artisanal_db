Men Lung Chen-Shan-Tien-Kung-Te, or simply Men Lung, was a great lung dragon of the Celestial Empire.

Relationships

Men Lung had at least seven sons; the seventh was Mei Lung Chen-Shan-Tien-Kung-Te, who would be appointed Grand Historian of the Celestial Bureaucracy. Men Lung was the great-grandson of Chih Shih Chen-Shan-Tien-Kung-Te, one of the Nine Immortals and the god of history.
