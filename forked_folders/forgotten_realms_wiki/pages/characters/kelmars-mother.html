Kelmar's mother, name unknown, was a sorceress of the Dargren family of Zazesspur during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Abilities

Kelmar's mother was a soul-sorceress, a necromancer who outstripped her sister.

Relationships

Her twin sister was a fellow necromancer. She had two sons, Lukan Dargren and Kelmar Dargren.

History

Rumors & Legends

When talking to Kelmar Dargren, Kelmar's aunt referred to her twin as "my sister" rather than by name, or as his mother. Kelmar, actually Xexillidaulgrymm in human guise, believed she might be hiding something in her words.
