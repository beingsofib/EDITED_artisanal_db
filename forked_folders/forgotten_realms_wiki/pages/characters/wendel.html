Wendel was a human fighter who was near too timid to be an adventurer.

Personality

A timid man, Wendel had problems as a career adventurer as he stayed out of trouble whenever he wasn't on missions, and as such lost out on many leads a braver more bolder man might have stumbled upon.

Possessions

Wendel wore a suit of scale mail and a set of gloves of missile snaring while he wieding a short sword. He also possessed a rope of climbing.

Relationships

At some point in his dwindling career he happened upon a bard who promised to teach him how to find adventures.
