Kevan Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the early-to-mid 9 century DR.

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the first son of his predecessor, High King Byron I. His successor was his second son, Dolan Cymrych.

History

In the , High King Byron I fell into a magic slumber, and Kevan stepped in to rule while his father was incapacitated. This short regency lasted until his father awoke the following year.

Kevan became the true High King in the , after the real death of his father. He ruled for twenty-four years before he died of natural causes in the .
