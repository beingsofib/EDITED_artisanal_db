Khollynnus Paac was a textile merchant and the leader of the largest Cowled Wizards cell in Amn in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Khollynnus had skin of olive hue and strawberry blonde hair.

Abilities

She was proficient in conjuration magic and had access to such powerful spells as Abi-Dalzim's horrid wilting, chain lightning, circle of death, pierce magic, ruby ray of reversal, spell turning, maze, simulacrum, and many others.

History

Khollynnus led the first cell of the Cowled Wizards in Amn. Her group's secret base was hidden underneath the Jann Lane.

In the , Khollynnus encountered Reyna and Teos–Knights of Solomnia, within the mysterious Planar Sphere that dropped onto slums of the River District of Athkatla. She was hired to return them to the world of Krynn through magical means.
