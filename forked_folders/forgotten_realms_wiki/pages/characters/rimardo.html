Rimardo was an adventurer from Turmish in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

Rimardo was a member of the Brightstar Sash adventuring company alongside Vonda, Thloram Flambaertyn, Bertro, and Caladnei.

History

Rimardo was with the Brightstar Sash when they acquired about 6,000 gp after a quest.

At a later date, he and Vonda tried to rob a nobleman in Waterdeep, but he was captured, interrogated, and tortured to death. During this, he revealed the names of the rest of the Brightstar Sash; the nobleman's forces came after them, forcing the Sash to flee Waterdeep.
