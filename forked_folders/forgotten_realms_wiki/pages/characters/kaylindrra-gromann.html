Kaylindrra Gromann was a gnome of Ieirithymbul in the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

She was married to Froga Gromann, an elder of the town.

History

After a celebration in Uktar of the , Froga Gromann returned home to find his wife Kaylindrra taken hostage by four humans who had taken the guise of local gnomes Merem, Furnis, Sudo, and Drom. They tied her up and left with him, demanding to be taken to the gold dragon, Palarandusk.
