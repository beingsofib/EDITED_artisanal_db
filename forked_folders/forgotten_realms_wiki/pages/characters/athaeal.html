Athaeal, also known as the Witch-Queen of Evermeet, was the author of the Musings of a Witch-Queen in Exile‎ in the 3 century DR.

Personality

Judging by her writing, Athaeal did not hold males in high regard, considering their blind lust for power to be foolish. She believed that only a few foolish women chained themselves in such a manner.

History

By the , Athaeal had already been exiled from her home in Evermeet. Following her expulsion, the Witch-Queen published a collection of her writings.
