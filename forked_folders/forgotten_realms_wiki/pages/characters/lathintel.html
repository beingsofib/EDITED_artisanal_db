Lathintel

Person
nameLathintel
homeAthkatla, Amn
sexMale

Lathintel was a skilled mage from Athkatla who died of winter fever during the late–13 century DR.

Personality

Lathintel was delightful to be around.

History

He penned the Libram of Lathintel, a tome containing several spells.
