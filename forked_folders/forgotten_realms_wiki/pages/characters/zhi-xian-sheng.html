Zhi Xian Sheng was an abbot of the Caves of a Thousand Gods in Kora Shan in the Hordelands during the mid 14 century DR.

Activities

As of the , Zhi Xian Sheng was the eldest and wisest, and by default the most powerful, abbot within the Caves of a Thousand Gods.
