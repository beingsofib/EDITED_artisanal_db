Olam Chistlesmith was a shield dwarf of the dwarven city of Earthfast in the Earthfast Mountains of north Faerûn in the 14 century DR and a member of Clan Chistlesmith.

Olam was the mate of Thendara Spearsinger and the father of the bard Morgalla the Mirthful.
