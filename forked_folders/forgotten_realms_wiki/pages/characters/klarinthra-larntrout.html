Klarinthra Larntrout was an elven winemaker who lived in the 11 century DR.

History

Klarinthra Larntrout was renowned for making a strong type of mushroom wine using morels and mint as main ingredients. The tipple was the Simbul's favorite alcoholic beverage. Klarinthra was also a good friend of Elminster Aumar until her death in the 11 century DR. Following her death, the Simbul was searching for a new drink that could come close to Klarinthra's recipe in taste and potency.
