Galoomp the Bookkeeper was a book merchant from the City of Coin, Athkatla, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Galoomp was a portly man with fair skin.

History

Galoomp's book stall could be found in the grand market known as Waukeen's Promenade in Athkatla's Center District in the . Among more mundane tomes, Galoomp carried a selection of mage's spell scrolls.
