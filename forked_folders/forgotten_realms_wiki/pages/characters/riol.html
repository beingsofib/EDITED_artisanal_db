Riol was a soldier of Hastarl in Athalantar in the mid–3 century DR.

Relationships

He worked under First Blade Sauvar.

History

During the assault on Athalgard in the , Riol was slain by Tharl Bloodbar, a Knight of Athalantar turned to banditry who was participating in the siege, almost as soon as the Magelords of Athalantar struck the alarm.
