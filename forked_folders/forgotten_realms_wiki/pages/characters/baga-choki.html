Baga Choki was a Tuigan warrior and member of the Grand Army of the Tuigan during the mid 14 century DR.

Description

Baga was tall for a Tuigan and possessed a sinister countenance. Keen intelligence was reflected from his hard gaze.

Activities

Baga was the commander of a tumen—a Tuigan military unit of 10,000 soldiers. He served within the portion of the army under Prince Hubadai Khahan's ultimate command.

He was actively involved in the conquest of Semphar.

Possessions

He owned a magical two-handed sword, magical lamellar armor, and a amulet of sound thought.
