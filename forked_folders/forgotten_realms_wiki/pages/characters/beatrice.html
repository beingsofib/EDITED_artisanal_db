Beatrice was a noblewoman of Cormyr in the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

She was a cousin of King Azoun IV and the wife of Grodoveth. However, Grodoveth committed a wanton indiscretion (rumored to involve a prostitute in a Suzail tavern) that not only dishonored himself but embarrassed the throne and saw him exiled from the city as a lowly envoy by 1367 DR, and perhaps fortunately left Beatrice alone.
