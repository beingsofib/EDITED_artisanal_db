Klaphen Fortier was a little known but powerful wizard famed for inventing the mage's battle robes, who lived sometime befroe the late 14 century DR.

History

Klaphen was tired of hearing about the greatness of overpowered archwizards like Elminster Aumar or Khelben Blackstaff, and great arcane organizations such as the infamous Red Wizards of Thay. His creation aimed at bolstering young spellcasters' abilities who were willing to challenge more experienced wizards and make a name for themselves. It was widely rumored that Klaphen Fortier died in his tower when an unlikely accident rained fifty-six lightning bolts with a score of meteors on it.
