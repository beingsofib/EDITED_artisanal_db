Kapanuk Talltree Thuunlakalaga was a young warrior of the Thuunlakalaga clan of goliaths that lived on Wyrmdoom Crag in the Spine of the World during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

He was a brave individual who was ready to take up arms when presented with danger.
