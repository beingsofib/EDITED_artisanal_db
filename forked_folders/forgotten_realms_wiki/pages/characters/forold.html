Forold was a Purple Dragon Knight, part of Azoun IV's retinue in the mid-14 century DR.

Description

Forold's voice was low.

Activities

He served in Azoun IV's personal retinue.

Relationships

In Azoun's personal retinue he served together with others including Daervin and Regrar.

History

Forold was part of Azoun's retinue in late , during the king's visit to Tessaril Winter in Eveningstar after the departure of Shandril Shessair. While burying Daervin after the attack of a malaugrym, Forold related something that a contact of theirs, Delmar, had said about the events in Eveningstar prior to their arrival. He also had suspicions of his own regarding Tessaril.
