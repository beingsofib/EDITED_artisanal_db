Bannor Karralo was a barkeep inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Bannor worked in a small tavern in Waterdeep's Sea Ward called the Wizards' Rest. The tavern closed when his mother died. He lived in a small house on Coach Street in the Southern Ward near the Troll Wall that he attempted to repair for his future wife.
