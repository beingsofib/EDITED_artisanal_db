Uligar was the chieftain of the Bleeding Eye orc tribe that raided the road of Kuldahar Pass in the 11 century DR.

Possessions

Uligar carried a wand of trap detection, an axe, a scroll of blur, and several gold coins.

History

In Eleint of the ,, the Bleeding Eye orcs and a tribe of goblins allied with them raided the traders passing through Kuldahar Pass, between Easthaven and a small town of Kuldahar. During the avalanche in Kuldahar Pass, Uligar and his raiders killed Jermsy's family that owned a mill outside of Kuldahar and used it as a base.
