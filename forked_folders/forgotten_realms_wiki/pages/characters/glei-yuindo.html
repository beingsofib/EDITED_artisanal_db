Glei Yuindo was an adventurer active in Koryo in the 1350s DR.

In 1357 DR, a band of adventurers headed by Glei Yuindo returned to Pan and it was rumored they had discovered the famous lost fortress of Toranu Ganppo. However, before they could tell their story, they died of a mysterious sickness.
