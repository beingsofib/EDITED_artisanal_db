Halder was an archmage of the city of Delzimmer in southeast Faerûn in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Halder was one of those wizards—listed among Omm Hlandrar of Halruaa, Szass Tam of Thay, Elminster of Shadowdale, and the Simbul of Aglarond—who challenged the mortal Velsharoon the Vaunted in his wicked plots. In turn, the arch-necromancer worked to acquire enough power to destroy Halder and all the other mages who'd opposed him. In the late 1350s DR, Velsharoon was working on a means of achieving godhood, and Halder and the others feared for their safety and for the safety and stability of the Realms.
