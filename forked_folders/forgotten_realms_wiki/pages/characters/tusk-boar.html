Tusk (boar)

Tusk was a boar that lived in the Emerald Grove during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Tusk was a rather impatient boar, bordering on boorish even.

Activities

He enjoyed laying out and basking in the warmth of sunlight.

History

Tusk became upset when Master Halsin went missing from the Emerald Grove in the . The kindly archdruid had promised to bring a mated boar for Tusk as a companion.
