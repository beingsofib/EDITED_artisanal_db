Gunderman Brewmaster, also called Horca, was a member of the Council of Seven Burghers in 1368 DR.

Activities

Gunderman was a very able brewer, known for the Old Smoke, a beer much appreciated in the Moonsea area. That made him a wealthy man but he never forgot pleasant manner.

He owned and operated Gunderman Brewery.
