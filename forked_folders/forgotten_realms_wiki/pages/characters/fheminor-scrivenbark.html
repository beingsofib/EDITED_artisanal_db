Fheminor Scrivenbark was one of the eight Great Readers of the Avowed of Candlekeep circa the late 15 century DR.

Great Reader Fheminor was quite knowledgeable about history and the various cultural traditions found across all of Faerûn. This knowledge comprised information about societies and cultures that were current to Fheminor's time, along with those that preceded them, and others that were lost to time.
