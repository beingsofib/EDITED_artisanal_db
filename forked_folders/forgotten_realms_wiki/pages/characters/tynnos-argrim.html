Watchful Skull Tynnos Argrim was the founder of the Flames of the Dark Sun and the head priest of the Cyricist Mountain of Skulls temple in Amn during the late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was a wholly vicious man that held little to no loyalty for others, even within the greater church of Cyric.

Relationships

Argrim held a particular hatred for Blackwill Haarken Akhmelere of the Twin Towers of the Eternal Eclipse. Each of them claimed that their temple was the greatest in all of southwestern Faerûn.

History

In the , Tynnos Argrim secured an invitation for himself to a rival temple of Cyric, the Dark Redoubt under pretense of discussing the Cyricist faith. After entering within, he slew the temple's head priest and brought its clergy under his own influence.

When the Sothillisian War broke out in the , he allied his forces with Amn against the Sothillisisan Empire.
