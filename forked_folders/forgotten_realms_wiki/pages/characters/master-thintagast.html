Master Thintagast was a gnomish jeweler from Ieirithymbul in the late 14 century DR.

Possessions

He had a workshop where jewels were cut.

Activities

Master Thintagast was a gem cutter.

Relationships

He had a number of apprentices which included Ambrythynn and Burlmarr.

History

In Uktar of the , there was an incident in Thintagast's home where one of his apprentices, Burlmarr, briefly passed out and banged his head on the ground. Burlmarr had at the time been an apprentice since the past summer, to his own recollection.
