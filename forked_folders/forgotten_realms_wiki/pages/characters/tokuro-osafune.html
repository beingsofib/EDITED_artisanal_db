Tokuro Osafune was a skilled human blacksmith for hire from the city of Neverwinter, active in the late 15 century DR.

History

By the late 15, Tokuro Osafune traveled far from his home on the continent of Kara-Tur. For reasons known only to the blacksmith, Tokuro settled in the northern city of Neverwinter. There, he gained the reputation of a talented artisan who was a master of forging high quality sharp blades out of the lowest quality metals. Tokuro offered his skills for hire to the heroes and defenders of Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.
