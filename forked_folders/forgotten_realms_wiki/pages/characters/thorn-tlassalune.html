Thorn Tlassalune was an innkeeper in Amphail.

Activities

Thorn owned the Stone Stallion.

Personality

He pretended to be from Amn despite obviously being from Tethyr.

History

In 1370 DR, his wife Khalarra died in childbirth and he started hearing the call of adventure. Thorn sponsored and fenced adventurers thanks to his contacts in Waterdeep.
