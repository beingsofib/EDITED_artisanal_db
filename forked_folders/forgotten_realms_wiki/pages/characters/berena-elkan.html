Berena Elkan was a Bhaalspawn berserker who allied herself with Gromnir Il-Khan during the Bhaalspawn crisis in Tethyr during the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

She wielded a +3 spear in battle, with which she was extremely skilled.

History

When Saradush was under siege by Yaga-Shura in the , Berena remained with Gromnir Il-Khan while he barricaded himself within the city's castle. She fought alongside Gromnir when he was confronted by their divine half-brother, Abdel Adrian and his companions.
