Thorn was a werepegasus who lived near Juniril in Cormyr in 1367 DR.

Activities

Thorn would sometimes fly over the Wyvernwater in pegasus form while his wife rode bareback.

Relationships

He was married to the wizard Iyrytharna Dantras.
