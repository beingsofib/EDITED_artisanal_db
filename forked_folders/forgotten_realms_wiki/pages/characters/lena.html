Lena was a woman who lived on the Sword Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

She was friends with Samuel, a soldier of the Flaming Fist.

History

In the , Lena convinced Samuel to desert the Flaming Fist and travel south to Amn. While on the run from some Fist soldiers, Samuel became gravely injured and Lena did all she could to find help for her dear friend. She wanted to have him transported to Gellana Mirrorshade at the Temple of Wisdom in Friendly Arm Inn.
