Shevron Extaminos was the forebear of House Extaminos of Hlondeth.

History

Shevron was famous for saving the city of Hlondeth from an assault from kobolds of the Tattered Cloth legion in 527 DR. He was a devout follower of Ilmater but held a fascination for snakes.
