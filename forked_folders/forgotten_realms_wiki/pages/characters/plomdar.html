Plomdar, who was nicknamed the Bombastic, was a skilled enchanter during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

He was one of the original members of the Knights of the Unicorn and a worshiper of Lurue. Following the Time of Troubles, Plomdar led one of the affiliated groups of the Order of the Unicorn.

History

Plomdar originally hailed from Baldur's Gate on the Sword Coast.
