Gglaerthus Irymm was a centaur and Harper caretaker around 1368 DR.

Activities

Gglaerthus was an old adventurer who was entrusted with the administration of the Harper Hall in Silverymoon. He was assisted by many venerable but veteran Harper rangers and warriors.
