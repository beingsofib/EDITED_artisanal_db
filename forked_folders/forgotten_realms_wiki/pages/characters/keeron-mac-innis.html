Keeron Mac Innis was a member of the ruling council of Langmuir on the island of Moray in the Moonshae Isles during the late 15 century DR. He was the son of the king, Donald Mac Innis.

History

As of , when Keeron was in his early twenties, he had become respected as a leader among the village councilors. He took the initiative to enlist adventurers from the crew of Ulrik Ragnarsson to strike back against the Black Blood tribe after they seized Langmuir's nearby lighthouse and began mustering to attack the village itself.
