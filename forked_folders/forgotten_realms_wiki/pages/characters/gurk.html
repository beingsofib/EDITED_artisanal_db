Gurk was a warlock that devoted his life to the Absolute, living among the Absolute'sfaithful within the Shattered Sanctum for a time during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Like other fanatics of the cult of the Absolute, Gurk was branded with a distinct brand by High Priestess Gut.
