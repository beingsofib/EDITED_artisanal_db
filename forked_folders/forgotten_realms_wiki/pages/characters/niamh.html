Niamh was a musically-inclined gynosphinx guardian in Myth Drannor, who remained there at least until the late 14 century DR in the service of Oghma.

History

The sphinx protected the inner sanctum of the House of Song when Zelliira and her allies came looking for the Icon of the Humans in 1374 DR. Impressed by their efforts navigating the music-based puzzles and tricks in the temple, Niamh delivered a final challenge on behalf of Oghma—fighting her in direct combat and proving their valor. After a fierce battle, Niamh admitted their strength and allowed them to access the treasures she guarded.
