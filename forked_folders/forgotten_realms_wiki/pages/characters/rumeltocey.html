Rumeltocey was a human priest of Milil renowned for her singing voice.

Description

Clad in flowing blue robes, Rumeltocey had fair skin and blonde hair.

Possessions

Rumeltocey owned a magical shield and flail.

Activities

Her considerable singing talent was known far and wide, and Rumeltocey often found herself invited to meet the rich and powerful of many cities. Occasionally, she would travel with Milil-worshiping bards. Rumeltocey eventually became unsure of her faith, wondering where her true commitment lay.
