Filsaelene Merwyst was a sun elf cleric of Corellon Larethian rescued from captivity by the daemonfey in the ruins beneath Myth Glaurach by Araevin Teshurr and his party.

Possessions

Filsaelene carried a longsword and wore a breastplate bearing the symbol of Corellon Larethian.

History

While traveling to Elvenport, Filsaelene's party was ambushed by fey'ri near Hellgate Keep. The entire company was slain except Filsaelene, who was spared to be used as breeding stock.

Later she joined for a time Araevin's party in their search to destroy the fey'ri. However she joined the Seiveril Miritar's Crusade in the Cormanthor War.
