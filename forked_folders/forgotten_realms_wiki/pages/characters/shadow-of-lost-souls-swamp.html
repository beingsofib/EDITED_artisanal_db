The shadow of Lost Souls' Swamp was a powerful umbral gargoyle—an unliving shadow—which dominated the swamp for a time in the late 15 century DR.

Description

The shadow resembled a true gargoyle while being made of nothing more than shadows.

History

The shadow lurked in an ancient ruin within the swamp for many years, sealed behind locked doors and gaining influence over the local will-o'-wisps and vine horrors. It drew more shadow and umbral creatures to serve it, and used the wisps to catch and feed upon the spirits of the unfortunate travelers who died there—Kyle Briarbuck, Evrim Whitehair, and Callie Brennan—giving the swamp its name.

When the medusa Sshaastess arrived and seized a piece of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll, the two would-be overlords struck a truce of sorts, only for the Harpers sent by Eradin Krall to meet the local hermit Seamus and upend the situation. While the main objective was the Nether Scroll, it's likely that the trapped spirits were freed and the fell shadow found and destroyed, ending its reign of horrors.
