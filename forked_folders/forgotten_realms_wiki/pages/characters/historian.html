The Historian was an enigmatic ape who recorded events from the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars.

Activities

The Historian used a special mechanical device similar to a printing press to chronicle history. From their desk, they wrote a record of a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

By writing history, their device gave them a seemingly magic ability to 'freeze time' and stop conflict on the Whamite Isles.
