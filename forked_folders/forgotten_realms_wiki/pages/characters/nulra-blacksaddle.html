Nulra Blacksaddle was a member of the Knights of the Shield who lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Nulra worked under the cambion Kaddrus, who himself was an aide to lady Lutecia Hhune of Baldur's Gate.

History

In the , Nulra and two other knights of the Shield accompanied Kaddrus on his mission to reclaim the Shield of the Hidden Lord.
