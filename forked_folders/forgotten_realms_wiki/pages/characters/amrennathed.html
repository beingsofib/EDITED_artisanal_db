Amrennathed was an amethyst dragon who was a great wyrm until the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The great wyrm achieved an unlikely state of decrepitude by the end of her life.

Personality

An extremely proud individual, Amrennathed was quite fond of deception and intensely secretive.

Abilities

The dragon had abilities that altered the minds of others, as well as some scrying capabilities, of an unexplained nature. At the time of its death, it demonstrated a unique ability to merge with the mountain where it had ensconced itself, an act that may have eventually been the cause of the guardians that later inhabited its grave.

Possessions

She had some of a hoard left to her by the end of her life.

Activities

Amrennathed had devoted much energy and time to acquiring the secrets of others, even excising them from their memories to keep as her own.

Relationships

Secretly, Amrennathed kept in touch with a local old woman, Diadree, talking to her on occasion.

History

During her life, Amrennathed had stolen secrets from many people, including Iamras Sonmaire and one Zhentarim agent. While she was already a great wyrm, she shifted her lair to somewhere in the Mountains of the Alaoreum. In the , the great wyrm decided to pass on and so deny anyone the ability to target her mind and plumb it for secrets. To do this, she merged with the mountain in which she'd made her lair, creating earthquakes that damaged the nearby village of Orunn. However, her passing created a wave of mental or magical energy that placed some of her memories in a local woman, Diadree.

Rumors & Legends

A dragon known as the Queen of the Mountain was spoken of locally in Orunn, though in that usage, she was mostly a bogeyman to scare children with.
