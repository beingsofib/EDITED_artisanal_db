Jacinth "Jackie" Moonspring was the Society Editor who contributed to the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter broadsheet of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR.

History

Jacinth Moonspring worked for the editor-in-chief Fred Faber. Three months later, Jacinth's column reported on Don Juan Montery, a notorious Golden Rooster knight philanderer.
