John (Elversult)

Person
nameJohn
homeElversult, Dragon Coast
sexMale
raceHuman
occupationFarrier

John was a farrier who worked in Elversult during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

The brawny man, black-bearded man enjoyed eating honeycake and imbibing mead.

Relationships

John had an amorous relationship with Silavia, a cook at Elversult's Moonstorm House.
