Wrint Sprigley was a reporter employed by Baldur's Mouth in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Wrint was a charcoal-skinned deep gnome who wore tunics and hats of quality make.

History

In the , Wrint Sprigley penned The Baldur's Mouth Tavern Review column in the Baldur's Mouth Gazette. During the Absolute crisis, his quill targeted the Blushing Mermaid in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. Wrint wandered the street near the tavern, mumbling the lines for the review. He criticized the establishment for its lack of direction and claimed that the Blushing Mermaid's atmosphere was ruined by street wanderers.
