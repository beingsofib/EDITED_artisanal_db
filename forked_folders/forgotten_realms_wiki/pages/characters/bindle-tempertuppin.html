Bindle "Eye-nock" Tempertuppin was the inventor of Tempertuppin's Ever-Throwers, active sometime before the late 14 century DR.

History

Bindle created his enchanted slings sometime before the . The muses came to the man after he witnessed a fire giant using a giant-sized sling, big enough to launch a donkey 300 paces. However, some questioned the truthfulness of Bindle's story.
