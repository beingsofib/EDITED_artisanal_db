Sinylla Zoranyian was a wizard in service to the House of the Moon during the .

Description

Sinylla had a family resemblance to the sisters Jannatha and Baerimel.

Abilities

Sinylla could cast blade barrier, and she was a mage in service to the Temple of Selûne, suggesting she possessed both arcane and divine magical abilities.

Relationships

She was the cousin of Jannatha Goldenshield and Baerimel Dunnath.

History

Sinylla Zoranyian helped Taegan Nightwind by casting lie-detection spells on the other mages aiding the effort to decrypt Sammaster's notes on the Rage of Dragons in . Sadly, she was slain by the chasme summoned by the sunwyrm impersonating Phourkyn One-Eye.
