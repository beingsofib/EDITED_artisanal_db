Charas of Winterwood was a renowned invoker from the Lake of Steam region, active in the 2 century DR.

History

Charas of Winterwood was the teacher of Riliton Mandleweave, a powerful battle wizard. She used the arcane knowledge taught to her by Charas to defend her home, Hlondeth, from Lord Dempster Turmish's aggression.
