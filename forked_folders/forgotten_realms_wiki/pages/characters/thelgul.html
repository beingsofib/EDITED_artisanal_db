Thelgul was an author on or before the 14 century DR.

History

By , Thelgul's work Do Metals Live? was amongst the works in Candlekeep.

Rumors & Legends

In 1373, Noumea Cardellith, pretending to be a merchant from Lantan, claimed to be interested in Thelgul's Do Metals Live? when seeking admittance in Candlekeep.
