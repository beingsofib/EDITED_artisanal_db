Stedd was a provost of Taegan Nightwind's academy of fencing in Lyrabar as of .

Description

Stedd was a wiry young man with a premature bald spot.

Activities

He was one of the four assistant instructors in the academy.

History

On the night of Ches 12 of the , Stedd was magically put to sleep. When Taegan was rescuing people from the fire, he had to slap the young provost in order to wake him. On the morning after the fire, Maestro Zalan was already trying to recruit him as a provost, to Taegan's concern.
