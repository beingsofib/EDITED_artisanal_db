Jhamil al Tassand was a pirate captain and leader of the Bloody Buccaneers and a member of the Rundeen active on the Sea of Swords and the Shining Sea of west Faerûn in the late 14 century DR.

Possessions

He was captain of the cutter Umberlee's Wake.

Relationships

He had an elder brother, a fellow pirate captain who led the Bloody Buccaneers with him.

Activities

They targeted non-Rundeen-affiliated ships transporting trade goods south into the Shining Sea. What cargoes they took were given to the Rundeen in return for rewards and the goods were then sold on by Rundeen businesses.

History

Since about 1366 DR, the Bloody Buccaneers had successfully attacked and raided over eight ships.

Then, in the , ships owned by the Bladesmile and Bormul merchant houses of Amn fought back against a raid, even killing Jhamil's brother and co-leader of the Bloody Buccaneers. His death left his brother Jhamil disturbed and angry and he and Jafar, his brother's former first mate and now captain of the Water Weird, swore to have their revenge on Bladesmile and Bormul.
