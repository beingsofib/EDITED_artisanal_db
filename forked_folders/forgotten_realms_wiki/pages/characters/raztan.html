Raztan was an armsman of Athalantar during the 3 century DR.

Activities

Raztan was an armsman.

History

In the , Raztan was in the force holding Narthil when Elmara, a priestess of Mystra who was secretly the Prince Elminster Aumar came there. After she frightened a number of armsmen with an illusion, a Magelord of Athalantar arrived. The magelord approached her at Myrkiel's Rest, an inn where she was eating, along with a number of armsmen, including Raztan, one whose name the magelord knew. He ordered Raztan to sword her; though Raztan did, she was under the effects of an ironguard spell, and all Raztan accomplished was hurling himself face-first into a bowl of soup. After she countered them by casting a spell that appeared to turn their swords to snakes, Raztan and the armsmen fled.
