Chalintash was a copper dragon. He became one of Karasendrieth's rogues during the Rage of Dragons of .

Description

When in a human guise, his complexion was ruddy and his hair was rust-colored.

As a dragon, he was larger than Trinculo.

Personality

Though cheery in general, Chalintash entertained some dark thoughts.

Abilities

He was known to bear the ability to turn stone to mud, presumably through the innate transmute rock ability common to copper dragons of a sufficient age.

Activities

The dragon had become one of the Rogues of Karasendrieth, openly defying the King of Justice, Lareth, in the search for a solution to the Rage of Dragons.

Relationships

He was friends with Trinculo.

History

In , Chalintash became one of the dragons to defy the edict of the King of Justice, Lareth, when he decreed all metallic dragons ought to submit to a spell to render themselves asleep through the duration of the Rage. Instead, he became one of the Rogues who sought a way to mitigate or stop the Rages.

After Karasendrieth's group managed to find clues as to the Rage in Northkeep, which stated it was the work of elven High Magic, the Talons of Justice chased him out of his lair, and halfway around the Anauroch, before he managed to shake them off. Chalintash then sent a message of some kind to his friend Trinculo, looking to meet; they met on the Jovial Juggler inn of Beregost. There, he passed on the information to his friend Trinculo, alongside the mission that he should thusly go to Evermeet, as the elves might be able to better investigate and solve the matter. During the conversation, Chalintash pointed angrily at Khalt Laathine, Trinculo's humanoid companion, as an example of an elf, bearing a certain bitterness.

That same night, Chalintash was overwhelmed by the Rage overnight, and attacked Beregost; Trinculo and Khalt, lured him away, and then killed him.
