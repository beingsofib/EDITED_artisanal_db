Bellamy was a halfling who lived in Baldur's Gate for at least a while during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was something of a ne'er-do-well, standoffish and wary about individuals he perceived as being too righteous or good.

Activities

Bellamy frequented the Three Old Kegs inn located in the Temples District of the city.

History

In the , a paladin named Phandalyn stopped by the Three Old Kegs, putting Bellamy on edge.
