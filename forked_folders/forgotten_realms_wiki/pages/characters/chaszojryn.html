Chaszoj'ryn was a drider who served as a guardian of the Temple of Lolth atop the city of Sschindylryn in the 1480s DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Chaszoj'ryn guarded the temple and the High Priestess Hekta Szind. When Elminster and Ana Brabener gained access to the portal leading from the interior of the Sschindylryn Ziggurat to the temple, Chaszoj'ryn immediately crossed from the other side and attacked the invaders. He did not survive.
