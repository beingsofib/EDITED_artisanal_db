Erssler Thamm was a vigilant master of Helm in the House Invincible temple in Silverymoon in the mid–14 century DR.

History

At some point prior to 1372 DR, Erssler died protecting a group of snowbound people making pilgrimage to the House Invincible from a pack of wolves.
