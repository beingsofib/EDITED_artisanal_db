Councilor Florrick was a city official from Baldur's Gate that served the great metropolis of the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Florrick spoke with grace and authority, simultaneously wielding flattery and generosity to elicit aid from others.

Relationships

Florrick held Grand Duke Ulder Ravengard in high esteem, and believed him to be the key to the city's salvation during its most dire times.

History

Councilor Florrick was present at Waukeen's Rest when it was assaulted by drow and goblin raiders belonging to the cult of the Absolute in the . She was trapped on the second floor of the inn as fires raged through the structure.
