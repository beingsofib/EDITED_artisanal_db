Khnarek was a senior member of the Unseen in Waterdeep in 1370 DR.

Personality

The doppelganger was a reluctant ally of Hlaavin, often acting autonomously, such as when it forged an alliance with Priamon Rakesk of the Twisted Rune.

Alias

Khnarek had various identities. It was also known as Terl Fadesmar, a watch-wizard in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep; Ulik Frescem, a member of City Guard patrolling the City of the Dead; Respen, a priest of Selûne; and Enris the Gray, a halfling member of Mask's Conscripts.
