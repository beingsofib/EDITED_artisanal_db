Filbur Royne was a Cormyrean Purple Dragon who served in the town of Eveningstar in the late 15 century DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Filbur tried to get more Amaunatori priests to aid the Purple Dragons in battle. The fellow organizing their efforts, Anazar Narroun, instead put his attention into investigating the outbreaks at the Eveningstar War Hospital, Uthe Lodge, and the druid Halsaime of Thorngap.
