Tolodine was a Netherese arcanist who developed several wind-, fog-, and cloud-based spells.

Description

This proficient arcanist was an astute student of Sadebreth, the priest-arcanist of Mystryl. Unlike his mentor, Tolodine specialized in inventive spells.

History

Tolodine was born in . The inventor concentrated on developing spells from the winds, though the spells he developed were simpler to formulate than those of his tutor, Sadebreth. He lived a short life compared to his mentor, and passed away from old age in .
