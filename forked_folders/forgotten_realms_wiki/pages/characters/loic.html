Loic was a druid that lived in the Emerald Grove during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

He spent much of his time in the grove's library, where many tomes and stone tablets that held a great trove of lore and information.

History

In , Halsin of the Emerald Grove offered safe haven to the tieflings of Elturel who were exiled from their home city following the Descent. When the tiefling child Arabellatried to steal the idol of Silvanus from the grove's altar—thereby ensuring the tieflings could remain within the grove longer—the druids offered mixed responses among their members. Loic wished that the tieflings would leave voluntarily, as he felt the druids could no longer harbor their company.
