Oluubarga was a gelugon in Myth Drannor in 1373 DR.

History

Oluubarga was one of the many devils who'd settled in Myth Drannor.

In 1373 DR, his master the pit fiend Aglaeroch ordered Oluubarga to lead a group of devils to the Abarat's Folly tower in order to gain control of the portal nexus inside and so spread their army over all Faerûn.
