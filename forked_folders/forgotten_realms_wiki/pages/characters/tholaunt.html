Tholaunt was an incarnation of Thoth called a Divine Precept who inhabited the city of Gheldaneth in central Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

As Divine Precept, Tholaunt could overrule the decisions made by Derlaunt, the precept of Gheldaneth, at any time.
