Rizzeryl was a drow wizard in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR.

Activities

Rizzeryl led a hidden base inside the Arcane Chambers managed by eight Zhent wererats.

History

In 1492 DR, some wererat minions of Rizzeryl stole a key from the southern watch post of the Xanathar's Thieves' Guild on the level. Rizzeryl falsely believed it opened a Melairkyn vault. The Xanathar lieutenant Shunn Shurreth wanted to kidnap and ransom a wererat to get the key back.

Personality

Rizzeryl liked to remain invisible when dealing with outsiders. As a Zhent ally, he wanted to destroy the Xanathar's influence in the Arcane Chambers.

Relationship

Rizzeryl worked for two masters: on the surface, he was an ally of the Zhentarim but secretly he worked for the drow House Auvryndar. He had a quasit familiar and had good dealings with the leader of the Zhent wererats.
