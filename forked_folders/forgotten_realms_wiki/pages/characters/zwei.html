Zwei was the first Netherese arcanist to be credited with the creation of new spells. The variator introduced the three successively more powerful extension spells in the years 1280 to 1300 NY of Netheril's Silver Age.

All other facts about Zwei's life (including his or her gender) were lost to history.

Trivia

The Zweihausen were a pair of enormous houses on Netherese territory that were destroyed during the rise of human civilization. It is not known if Zwei was related to the Zweihausen or the namesake Zweihaus River.
