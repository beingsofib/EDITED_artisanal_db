Ulena the Fox was a lillend proxy of the Faerûnian power known as Selûne and was that goddess's principal proxy.

Personality

Ulena was very conscientious when it came to her duty as a guardian of the Gates of the Moon, being suspicious of and questioning all who sought to enter Selûne's divine realm.

Activities

Out of all planar visitors, Ulena particularly sought to keep out the more violent and glory-seeking inhabitants of Ysgard, on account of the fact that when killed her species and Selûne's shards were not reborn in the same way that petitioners were.

Rumors & Legends

Some rumors claimed that Ulena was in particularly vile moods whenever it was a new moon.
