Ulfberht was a virtuoso dwarvish blacksmith for hire from the city of Neverwinter, active in the late 15 century DR.

History

By the late 15 century DR, Ulfberht had already reached the fame and respect of a master bladesmith. Ulfberht's swords were truly remarkable, making many people raise an eyebrow at the fact that such a master liked to work for hire in simple workshops, lending his skills to adventurers of Neverwinter.
