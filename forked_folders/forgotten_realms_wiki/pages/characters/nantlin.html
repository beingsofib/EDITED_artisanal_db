Nantlin was a horse breeder and Harper in the mid–8 century DR.

History

After emerging from the tomb of an old Netherese wizard in the , the Chosen of Mystra, Elminster Aumar, wandered the land using the alias of "Wanlorn". He met the horse breeder Nantlin at some point after that.

At some point prior to the , Elminster liberated Buckralam's Starn from the bandits of the Iron Fox. After that, he told a local woman, Immeira, to look for the horse breeder Nantlin at Saern Hill. He also told her to ask if his harp sounded as sweet as ever, strongly implying the man was a Harper.
