Catharyn was a crinti pirate who lived in Dambrath.

Personality

Much to her mother's pride, Catharyn was cruel and ruthless. It was said that she never took prisoners on the open water.

Activities

Catharyn sailed the Great Sea on Queen's Fury, her personal vessel. She raided the waters of the Great Sea and even ventured as far away as Nimbral on the Trackless Sea where she returned to Dambrath with four shiploads of booty.

Relationships

Catharyn was Queen Yenandra's oldest daughter.
