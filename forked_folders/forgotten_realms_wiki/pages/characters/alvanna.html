Dawnbringer Alvanna was a priest in service of the Church of Lathander of Athkatla's Temple District in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Dawnbringer Alvanna had tanned skin and light brown hair. He preferred to wear purple garb.

Possessions

She was equipped with a set of chain mail and armed with a simple quarterstaff.

History

By the , Dawnbringer Alvanna could be found outside the Temple of Lathander, greeting approaching supplicants. She was a devoted worshipper who was ready to discuss her deity and defend his infamous transgressions of youth. Alvanna denounced all who felt pride and praised hopefulness, creativity, and optimism and believed the cycle of renewal to be her church's most praised virtue.
