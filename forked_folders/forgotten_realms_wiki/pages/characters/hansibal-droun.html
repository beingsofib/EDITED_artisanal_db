Hansibal Droun was an agent of the Zhentarim active within the small village of Xonthal's Tower during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Hansibal masqueraded as a shopkeeper, running a small hardware store and stable. A dozen experienced Zhentilar soldiers fell under his command, allowing him to easily secure Xonthal's Tower for the Black Network. Hansibal and his soldiers fended off sevaral attacks from yuan-ti.
