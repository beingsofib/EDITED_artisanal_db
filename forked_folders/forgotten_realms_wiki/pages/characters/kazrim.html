Kazrim

Person
nameKazrim
sexMale

Kazrim was a hero honored in Vaelan, Durpar. His heroics had been key to defeating the monsters that attacked Old Vaelan in the late 11 century DR. Kazrim's Plunge, the statue in Vaelan, was raised in his honor.
