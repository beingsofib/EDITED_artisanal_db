Belhinn Wavegold was an Amnian prince of the Wavegold dynasty.

Relationships

Belhinn was the son and heir of King Erdhinn I, and father of Belhinn I.

History

Belhinn perished unexpectedly along with his father in the , leaving his very young son Helgrin to inherit the throne. Helgrin was hidden by the Herald Thultar Mornhelm, who acted as his regent until 1127 DR, when he was old enough to rule and took the name Belhinn I.
