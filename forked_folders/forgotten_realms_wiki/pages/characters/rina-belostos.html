Rina Belostos was a human Harper agent who worked in Eveningstar in Cormyr in the the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Rina had dark skin, long black hair, and blue eyes. She wore an unassuming brown outfit and carried a lute for her cover as a musician.

History

Around the time of the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, Rina Belostos led a Harper cell in Cormyr. When Oriphaun Huntsilver stole the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll from the shade lord Tyvrus in Wheloon Prison, she briefly met with Oriphaun and recruited him to the cause—only for him to vanish the next day with the Nether Scroll while pursued by the agents of Returned Netheril. She led her group of Harpers to Eveningstar to look for the Nether Scroll and Oriphaun, finding his journal that described his plan to tear the Scroll to pieces and hide them along the westward High Road that led to the Storm Horns. She and her team posed as traveling musicians while in Eveningstar, gathering information from the local Evenors, Antone Corluk and Belvedere of House Huntsilver, and even Elminster. She was joined in Eveningstar by Harpers including Eradin Krall, Olyn Araphyr, the lutist Tibalt Dormen, the flutist Kordo Sweetspire, and the drummers Freda Gambosi, Golo Manks, and Vindavere Dyrwool. Unfortunately, due to the ongoing Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar the Purple Dragons and War Wizards were unable to assist, and the High Road was overrun with bandits, monsters, and the forces of Thultanthar.

The first piece of the Scroll was found by the wizard Harper Bensen Virandson, who was sent aid by Tibalt Dormen as they transported it down Greenweald Lane to the Harper Lodge hidden in the King's Forest. Along the way they fought Shadovar agents and mercenaries led by the fearsome Dom, receiving unexpected help from the tricky bard Nil.

The next fragment was found by Freda Gambosi at the ruined Beckside Castle, used as a rest stop along the road. Following an investigation they discovered an infiltrating Shadovar force led by Keldan Shadeskein and Gaston Wyrst, who attempted to seize the fragment but were slain in battle.

Eradin Krall located the another bit at Lost Souls' Swamp, where Oriphaun had trusted it with the hermit Seamus. Unfortunately, the medusa Sshaastess detected the magical power of the artifact and invaded the swamp, taking the Scroll fragment for herself until it was taken back by force.

The agent Kordo Sweetspire found another at the Old Man's Face inn at Tyrluk, where Oriphaun had ingeniously hidden it in plain sight. Following a brief battle with the forces of Tarys and Wyle at the inn, the Scroll was found and recovered.

Disaster struck when Olyn Araphyr sent reinforcements to meet Girmond Dyle, who had taken custody of the collected pieces. The Netherese found and killed Girmond first, taking his Scroll pieces and assembling the whole Nether Scroll at Granwell Keep. Worse still, the Netherese accidentally caused a portal to the Abyss to open, inviting the glabrezu Razagnol to perform a minor planar invasion. After a three-way battle between Shadovar, demons, and Harpers, the day was won by the forces of good. Rina arrived a moment late with Elminster via teleportation, apologizing for the tardiness, and had Elminster take the Nether Scroll away for safekeeping, lest some monster or evil empire take it.
