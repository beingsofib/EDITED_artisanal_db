Bok was a powerful flesh golem manufactured by Dendybar the Mottled, a wizard and the guardian of the northern spire in the Hosttower of the Arcane in the seaport city of Luskan.

History

In the , Dendybar sent Bok alongside the wizard Sydney and the soldier Jierdan in search of Drizzt Do'Urden, believing that the drow possessed Crenshinibon or information on the powerful artifact.

Bok was destroyed by Drizzt during a vicious fight near Mithral Hall. Afterward, Dendybar teleported near the golem with the aim of recovering and reconstructing it. However, the spirit of Morkai the Red, a dead foe of Dendybar who was killed and enslaved as a spirit by the wizard, regained control of the remains of the golem and killed Dendybar.
