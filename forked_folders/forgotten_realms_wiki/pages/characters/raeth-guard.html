Raeth was a guard in Shadowdale in the mid-14 century DR.

Personality

He was a late riser.

Relationships

He was teased at times for being a late riser.

History

As of the , he was a young, new guard in the Twisted Tower of Ashaba of Shadowdale, employed at least since some time before the night when Culthar, another guard, was caught trying to kidnap Shandril Shessair.
