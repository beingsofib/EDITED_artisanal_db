Proctiv was a Netherese arcanist, prolific spell inventor, and master of variation.

Description

Proctiv was an extraordinary mage who developed the second most powerful spell in the Empire of Netheril, Proctiv's breach crystal sphere (which was tied with Mavin's worldweave in terms of power).

History

Proctiv developed eight spells during his life. Spectacularly, in , he developed his first spell: the epic move mountain, which became crucial for creating the base of a flying enclave. After this, he invented stone shape in , move earth in , glassteel in , transmute rock to mud in , transmute water to dust in , and dig in . In , Proctiv had an enormous breakthrough and developed the epic Proctiv's breach crystal sphere (and its reverse version).
