Naxene Drathkala was an member of the Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors in Goldenfields sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

Activities

Naxene was usually at the Northfurrow's End inn. She was a member of the Lords' Alliance and an agent for Open Lord Laeral Silverhand.

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR she was the Watchful's liaison to Goldenfields. Naxene was at the inn when giants attacked the city.

Personality

Naxene believed that all can be resolved by magic and to be too smart to mistake something. She supported Laeral as a force of order in a chaotic world.

Behind the scenes

Naxene was one of the special NPCs that could be played by the players during the campaign's arc in their city.
