Jhaam was a drow Harper scout.

History

In the , Jaheira sent Jhaam to the Grymforge to gather information about the Absolute cultists' plans and later get back to the Last Light Inn. Unfortunately, during her mission, Jhaam was caught, interrogated, and later hanged. Her corpse with her orders could be found in a cage near the Grymforge port.

Relationships

Jhaam was a subordinate of Jaheira. In her letter, Jaheira mentioned that the scout hadn't failed her before.
