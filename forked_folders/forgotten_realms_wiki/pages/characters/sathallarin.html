Sathallarin was a moon elven wild mageliving in the mid 14 century DR.

Personality

He was oft described as half crazy and took to wild magic due to his own inner feelings of unpredictability and powerfulness, both concepts that resonated with wild magic itself.

Possessions

He wielded an enchanted +2 dagger, and a wand of wonder which, unlike most wands of that type, would create two or even three different effects whenever used.

Relationships

Whenever he met others in his travels, he was as likely to attack them as to help them, that is, as long as he didn't foresee a great risk upon himself. Even with his capricious mentality, he would avoid situations that might lead to his own death.
