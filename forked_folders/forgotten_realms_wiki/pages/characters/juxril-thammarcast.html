Juxril Thammarcast was a priest of Shaundakul, the Rider of the Winds, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Juxril held religious services at the Plinth in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep. He was one of only a handful of known priests of Shaundakul following the end of the Time of Troubles in the .
