Shak'kral Hunzrin was a young drow and a member of House Hunzrin in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Shak'kral was renowned for her skills in trading beyond Menzoberranzan and even on the surface.

History

In the , Shak'kral accompanied the First Priestess of her House, Charri Hunzrin, to a meeting with a clan of spriggan followers of Malcanthet in Damara arranged by the Hunzrin trader Denderida.
