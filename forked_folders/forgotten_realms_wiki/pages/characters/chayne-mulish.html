Chayne Mulish was a dwarf commander of Citadel Adbar in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , he was part of the doomed Adbar expedition led by King Bromm, which was attacked by orcs of the Kingdom of Many-Arrows. Before battle was joined, Chayne and Oretheo Spikes rallied the dwarves' battle spirit, a task Bromm thought few were better suited to. Chayne died as the main enemy force appeared, his head smashed by a frost giant–thrown boulder while he carried out King Bromm's command for an orderly withdrawal. His last words were "For the love o' ye Ma's hairy back, back and stay tight!"
