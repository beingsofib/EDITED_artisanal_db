Torlyn Wands was a human noble and book collector in Waterdeep.

Description

Torlyn was young (1479 DR) and had mahogany hair and a full beard, as well as a hairy chest.

Personality

Torlyn liked functional clothes.

History

As one of Renaer Neverember's closest friends, Torlyn was present at the Nightal 20, 1479 DR, meeting at Roarke House to reform a group similar to the Moonstars. He was willing to support the group, but only in secret.

Possessions

Torlyn owned a full set of mundane books written by the Seven Sisters. The book he purchased that completed the purchase was Lifelong with Regrets by Laeral Silverhand.

Relationships

Torlyn had a sister named Nharaen Wands.
