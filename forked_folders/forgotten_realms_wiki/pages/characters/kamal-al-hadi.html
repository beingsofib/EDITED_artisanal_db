Sultan Kamal al-Hadi was the ruler of the Free City of Qadib in north Zakhara in .

Description

Kamal was an ancient and skeletal man. He outlived several wives, children, and even grandchildren. Kamal spoke and moved slowly, but this behavior masked the incredibly sharp mind he possessed.

Activities

Kamal was a half-elven sha'ir of great skill. He was a master planner and was usually several steps ahead of anyone looking to gain advantage over him.

All of Kamal's wives were human women, leading to all of his offspring being human as well.
