Smasheye

Person
nameSmasheye
sexMale
raceOrc

Smasheye was an orc leader within the Burntbone Horde in the late 15 century DR.

Abilities

He was able to revitalize his comrades with the strength of his vision for their future. In battle, he attacked with mighty slams of his mace.

History

Smasheye was not as important as Vargor the Fierce in the Horde's hierarchy, but unlike Vargor he survived the attack on Sharpstone Keep by adventurers from Waterdeep.
