Suld was a member of the Cult of the Dragon.

History

In the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR, Suld and Arkuel were servants of the Cult's archmage, Malark Himbruel, on a mission to capture Shandril Shessair inside the Twisted Tower of Ashaba.
