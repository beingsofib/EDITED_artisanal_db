Colombier was a powerful Netherese arcanist and alchemist who taught at the Alchemical Seminary in Eileanar, Netheril, shortly before its destruction in .

Activities

Colombier was an expert alchemist with a focus on magical powders, and taught this skill at the Seminary under the supervision of Quintus. Alongside Delacroix, she was considered one of the more practical and relatable teachers there, as everyone could appreciate the applications of useful potions and powders.
