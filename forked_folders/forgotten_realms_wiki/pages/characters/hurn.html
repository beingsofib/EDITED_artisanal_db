Hurn was a dwarf knight in Phlan in 1489 DR.

Personality

Hurn was an old and unfriendly individual, only wanting to reach his retirement day. Hurn was not corrupt but did not refuse a bribe either.

History

Hurn was a member of the Knights of the Black Fist. He was in charge of a guardpost at one entrance to Sokol Keep.
