Mercer was the chamberlain of Lady Jannath's Estate in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Chamberlain Mercer was an aged rock gnome man with a light skin tone, dark eyes, and black curly hair. He wore a fanciful set of embroidered clothes and a top hat – a chamberlain's uniform of the Jannath Estate.

History

Circa the , Mister Mercer was in charge of the help that served Lady Firelia Jannath in her mansion. When her new husband fell victim of a ghostly possession, Mercer dismissed all servants from the mansion on the mistress's orders, leaving only her loyal butler Tarhun Mnemonis standing guard of the mansion's main entrance. Mercer placed Furbleston, Bailey, Cyrilla, Jooli, Phubbs, and Chef Marjoram on temporary leave with the pay of half-wages and allowance to seek temporary employment somewhere else. The chamberlain issued a statement that this dismissal was done at no imputation of malfeasance and would be ended upon notice.
