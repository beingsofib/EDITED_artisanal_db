Keane was a wizard in the Moonshae Isles and later a High King of the Ffolk.

Personality

Keane was a quiet, observant, and studious individual. Under that facade, however, he was a powerful wizard, always ready to personally fight evil.

History

Keane was the royal tutor of the two young princesses Alicia Kendrick and Deirdre Kendrick, posing as a failed wizard as ordered by King Tristan Kendrick. He followed Alicia in all her adventures during the , helping battle her many foes, destroying a dracolich, fighting the Ityak-Ortheel and traveling to Evermeet. He ultimately fell in love with Alicia and confessed his feelings to her, which seemed to be reciprocated.

They were married in the , and when Alicia succeeded her father, Keene became High King of the Moonshaes by her side.

He suffered terrible injuries when Caer Callidyrr was attacked by the red dragon Hoondarrh in the . He soon succumbed to his wounds, and efforts to resurrect him all failed.
