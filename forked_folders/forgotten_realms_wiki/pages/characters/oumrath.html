Oumrath was a rival of Malark Himbruel in the mid-14 century DR.

History

In , Malark Himbruel contemplated the odds of having to fight other groups to capture Shandril Shessair once she left the Dalelands, and in particular, the likelihood of having to do battle with Oumrath.
