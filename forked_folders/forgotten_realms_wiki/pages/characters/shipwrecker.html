Shipwrecker

Person
nameShipwrecker
homeAl-Zabdiyat, Nada al-Hazan
raceGiant sea snake

Shipwrecker was a giant sea snake living on Al-Zabdiyat in the Crowded Sea in 1367 DR.

Activities

Shipwrecker was the pet of the sha'ir Khadiga, helping her guard the island from intruders.
