Patric Fulgirth was a ruler of the kingdom of Doegan in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

He was an idle ruler who cared not for the needs of the people. Formerly a powerful nation, Doegan fell into decline under his dereliction of duty. The leader of the Legendary Campaign sought to depose Fulgirth, and thought this would be an easy task.
