Rhalaglingalade was a wizard in Neverwinter.

History

In 1370 DR, Rhalaglingalade settled in Neverwinter and announced the creation of a new spell: the sphere of summer. For his creation he was targeted by many Calishite assassins, one the famed Thyruin of the White Flowers, and subject of many kidnapping attempts by merchants of Amn, Luskan, and Thay. Rhalagligalade asked the protection of the Lords' Alliance, who sent an adventuring party to act as his bodyguards.
