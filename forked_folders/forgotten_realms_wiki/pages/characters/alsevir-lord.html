Lord Alsevir was a nobleman of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

As of , together with Lord Huntcrown, Lord Alsevir was largely known to be head of a faction among Cormyrean nobility that wanted war with Sembia to provide the Bleth and Cormaeril families with ample plunder, the reversion of the War Wizards of Cormyr to political and martial irrelevance or their disbanding, a puppet king kept in thrall by the Royal Magician on behalf of the major noble families, and the extermination of the lesser, newer noble families.

Relationships

He planned on the assassination of his contemporary Lord Huntcrown if he ever got what he wanted.
