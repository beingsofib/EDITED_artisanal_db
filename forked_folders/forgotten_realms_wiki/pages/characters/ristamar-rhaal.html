Ristamar Rhaal was a mercenary recruiter in Amn.

Personality

Lord Rhaal was a very dignified man, but he still maintained a sense of humor.

Activities

Ristamar was an Amnian noble and the chairman of the council of Boar's Heads of the Order of the Blue Boar, as well as the owner of Castle Blue. He usually dealt with major government issues, thanks to his connections as a noble.

When in combat, Ristamar wore a suit of full plate armor and led cavalry units.
