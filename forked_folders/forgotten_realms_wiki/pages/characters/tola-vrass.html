Tola Vrass was the beautiful and charismatic leader of the Thayan enclave within Hillsfar.

She served under the Zulkir of Illusion, Mythrellan. Tola was involved in a bit of a courtship with Maalthiir, as the two were mutually attracted to one another and both realized the power and benefits a successful alliance would garner for their power structures.
