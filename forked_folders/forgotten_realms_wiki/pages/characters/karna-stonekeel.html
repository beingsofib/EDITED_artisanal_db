Karna Stonekeel was a shipwright of New Sarshell in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

During the late 14 century DR, Karna Stonekeel was one of the most sought-after shipwrights of New Sarshell; her dwarven crew had a reputation for quick and efficient work, though not one for sailing. The shipwright had a famously long backlog.

History

In , Captain Thoster retained her services to repair the Green Siren after returning from Xxiphu with the ship.

Rumors & Legends

Though after Thoster's return from Xxiphu, Karna Stonekeel told him that the amount he'd paid for repairs would do for the lumber and canvas, Thoster later related to Anusha that he'd paid the shipwright a king's ransom.
