Footy was a precocious human child from Beregost circa mid–14 century DR.

History

In 1368 DR, Footy was still an innocent child when his mother and brother Johnny disappeared, leaving Footy alone and homeless. The child searched for his missing family far and wide by all he could find were zombies. Footy did not realize that his family was murdered and raised by a mad cleric of Cyric in the Red Canyons, named Basillus. After spying on the mad priest, the child witnessed him rambling at this zombie minions, seemingly thinking that they were his family. In response, the under just shuffled about and groaned. Soon after, Footy rushed away to tell his friend Netty about the funny man..
