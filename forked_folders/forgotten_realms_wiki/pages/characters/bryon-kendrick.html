Bryon Kendrick was the king of Corwell in the early-to-mid–14 century DR. He was the father of Tristan Kendrick.

Description

King Bryon was a strong man with dark hair and a long black beard streaked with gray. His face began showing signs of old age even when he was still quite young.

Personality

He was listless and brusque. He was relatively polite to visitors, but cold toward his own son.

Possessions

He wore a steel mail shirt into battle that had also once been worn by his father, and which he in turn gifted to his son.

Relationships

He was a member of the royal Kendrick family.

Bryon was never able to recover from the death of his wife, Addee Kendrick. Adding to Bryon's despair, he was consistently disappointed in the actions of his son, Prince Tristan.

History

King Bryon was a warrior in his youth. He fought against the Northmen His forces helped to relieve King Dagdar of Moray in the 1320s DR when Northmen threatened to overtake his island.

His son Tristan was born in the , which preceded nearly two decades of peace in Corwell. In the , Bryon's young wife died, causing the king to fall into a deep and lasting depression.

He hosted Elminster at his castle of Caer Corwell in the , although the sage found him to be less than pleasant.

By , Bryon's powerful presence had descended into a weakened state of discord. although he was able to have himself strapped into the saddle of his warhorse to lead the final rout of the enemy. He was assassinated the following year by Razfallow on the orders of Cyndre.
