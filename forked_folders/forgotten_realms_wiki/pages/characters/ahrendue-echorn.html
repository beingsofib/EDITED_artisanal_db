Lady Ahrendue Echorn was an inhabitant of Cormanthor in the 3 century DR. She was a high mage involved in the raising of the Mythal.

Abilities

She was a High Mage.

Description

She was wrinkled, walked with a cane, and had a husky voice.

Personality

She was happy to contribute her lifeforce to the mythal, and saw it as a good way to die.

History

In , Lady Ahrendue Echorn was welcome into the inner ring of the mages helping Mythanthor with the laying of the Mythal.

After the city was renamed Myth Drannor, she may have become one of the instructors at Windsong Tower.
