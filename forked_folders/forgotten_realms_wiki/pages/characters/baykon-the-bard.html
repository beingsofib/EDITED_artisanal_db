Baykon the Bard

Person
nameBaykon the Bard
homeUtter East, southeast Faerûn

Baykon the Bard was a person in the Utter East.

Regarding the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR), Baykon the Bard remarked "With a prudent strategy, the Great Mage creates more opportunities than he finds." and "Even a blind Mage can see opportunity if he looks close enough."
