Shontiq A'Lavallier was a drow wizard of Q'Xorlarrin in 1484 DR.

History

Shontiq studied magic under the tutelage of Ravel Xorlarrin whom he idolized.

During the War of the Silver Marches, Shontiq was with the main army on the Redrun river battling the Knights in Silver, and later at the fall of Sundabar. In 1485 DR, he was made leader of a contingent that spied on Silverymoon, a boring mission for him. However Shontiq was killed on surprise when an army led by Regis, Wulfgar and Aleina Brightlance suddenly attacked his emplacement
