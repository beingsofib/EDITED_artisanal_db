Angruin Myrvult was a powerful Zhentarim wizard in High Dale.

History

In 1358 DR Angruin was the commander-in-chief of a secret Zhent occupation army in High Dale; however Elminster Aumar and Sharantyr discovered the Zhent plot about the dale and started an insurrection against the Zhent and thanks also to the coming of the Time of Troubles the Zhent were defeated and Angruin died by a spell going crazy.
