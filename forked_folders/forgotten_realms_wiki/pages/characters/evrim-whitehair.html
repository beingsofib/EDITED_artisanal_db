Evrim Whitehair was an adventurer of Cormyr who met a bad end in Lost Souls' Swamp.

Personality

Evrim was jaded, cynical, and selfish, expecting the worst of everything and everyone. Being turned into a trapped disembodied soul only worsened his outlook.

History

While working as an adventurer, Evrim was betrayed and killed by his "friends", and dumped into a lake in Lost Souls' Swamp (along with his whisperchain armor). To make things worse, the shadow of Lost Souls' Swamp trapped his soul there for its will-o'-wisps to feed on, along with Kyle Briarbuck and Callie Brennan. Though held by the efforts of the shadow's mudmen, vine horrors and wisps, their souls may have been freed by Harpers sent by Eradin Krall and Rina Belostos as they pursued Sshaastess and her Nether Scroll circa 1485 DR. When they arrived, Evrim attempted to encourage their help by offering up his old whisperchain should they be freed.
