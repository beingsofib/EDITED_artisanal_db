Ouldra

Ouldra was a stone golem in Waterdeep in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Ouldra's body had a very feminine shape to it for a golem.

Activities

Ouldra acted as guard within Feldyn's Old Monster Shop in Waterdeep's Southern Ward in the 1360s DR.
