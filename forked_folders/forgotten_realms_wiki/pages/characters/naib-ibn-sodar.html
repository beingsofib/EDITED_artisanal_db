Naib ibn-Sodar was an elven sorcerer who served within the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Activities

Naid was in charge of the wizards who guarded the Grand Gate of Delights leading into the Palace. She organized the shifts of mages, sorcerers, and elemental mages into 8-hour shifts to add magical support to the mamluks guarding the gate.

Possessions

As with all mages stationed at the Grand Gate of Delights, Naib owned a wand of paralyzation.
