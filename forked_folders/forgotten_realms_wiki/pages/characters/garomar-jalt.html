Garomar Jalt was a human merchant of Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Garomar had a brown chinstrap beard and shaven head, wearing a practical set of pants, apron and tunic.

History

Around the time of the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, Garomar set up shop in Eveningstar while picking up curios from Wheloon Prison, where the Purple Dragons and adventurers fought against the shade Tyvrus. He took these trinkets in exchange for Cormyrean weapons and armor, and was joined by Beline Melofora, who collected items from the Storm Horns.
