Hellpin was a thief in the city of Westgate on the Dragon Coast around the 13 century DR.

Personality

Hellpin was among those considered to be rowdy bar patrons.

Possessions

Hellpin owned a number of dogs.

History

One day while at the tavern owned by Righteous Tad, Hellpin got into a brawl and he was one of many that ended up being hit by the owner's special enchanted weapon, Peacekeeper. Taking an offense to this beating, Hellpin went on to attack Righteous Tad with his dogs one foggy night, losing more than half of them in the process. He then plucked Peacekeeper from Tad's corpse, keeping it as a trophy within his safehouse as a warning to anyone else who might think to cross him.
