Yril Mytersaal was a Warlord of Silverymoon during the 9 century DR.

Activities

Yril claimed the title of Warlord after the previous individual to hold the title, Lashtor, was deposed.

Relationships

Yril's sister, Tanalanthara, served as the High Mage of Silverymoon during his time as Warlord.
