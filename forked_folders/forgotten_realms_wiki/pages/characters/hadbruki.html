Hadbruki was a shaman leader and a hunter of the Uthgardt Black Raven tribe who inhabited the Ice Lakes and Cold Marshes area in the Northwest Faerûn in the early 14 century DR.

Possessions

Hadbruki wielded a unique enchanted bludgeon called the Azure Rod.

History

In the , Hadbruki's tribe fell on hard times, forcing Uthgardts to consume their giant ravens. This turned the tribe aggressive to outsiders, especially to those who trespassed into the Black Raven's hunting grounds in the Cold Marshes. In Mirtul of 1312 DR, a group of mercenaries from the town of Targos ended up passing through the Fell Wood and across the tribe's territory. Hadbruki confronted the adventurers and threatened them with violence. Hadbruki and his entire hunting party of barbarians attacked the mercenaries but lost their lives in the process.
