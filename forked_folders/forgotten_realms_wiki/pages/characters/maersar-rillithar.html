Maersar Rillithar was a glassblower inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Maersar was the guildmaster for the Guild of Glassblowers, Glaziers, & Speculum-makers circa 1357 DR. He held the title of "Master Specular" within the guild and worked out of the House of Crystal–their headquarters on Copper Street in the North Ward.
