Chock was an ogre mercenary who worked on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.

Description

The mighty ogre wore a crude loincloth, hide-wrapped bracers, a simple necklace, and makeshift eyepatch.

Abilities

Chock traveled alongside Fank, and their magnificent leader, Lump the Enlightened. They often debated as to whether other creatures they encountered should be classified as friends or food, and argued about what animals that 'food' may taste like.

History

In the , Chock and his allies were hired by the goblin cultists of the Absolute. The trio were directed to remain in the ruins of Moonhaven, to check passersby for anyone bearing the mark of the Absolute. They were given gold by the goblins, along with executive decision making as which individuals could or could not be eaten.
