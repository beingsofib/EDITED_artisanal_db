Lady Darndrella Rondever, born Darndrella Horncastle, and formerly known as Darndrella Mantlepard, was a noblewoman from Cormyr, the second wife of Lord Jarlathin Mantlepard, and, later, the wife of the wealthy Athkatlan man named Ilreth Rondever in the late 14 century DR.

History

Darndrella was born into the Horncastle family of Cormyr. By the 1370s DR, Darndrella have had already been gone from Lord Jarlathin Mantlepard's life for about seventeen years. She became Lord Mantlepard's second wife, but abruptly left him and the Forest Kingdom for Ilreth Rondever. Presumably, she embraced her new life with Ilreth in Athkatla, Amn, with a man of considerable wealth and stunning looks. Shortly after her departure, Jarlathin Mantlepard petitioned the Dragon Throne of Cormyr to have his marriage dissolved. The request was approved, and he was married to Darndrella no more, by royal decree.
