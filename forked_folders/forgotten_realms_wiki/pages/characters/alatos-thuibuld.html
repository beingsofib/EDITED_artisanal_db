Alatos "Ravenscar" Thuibuld was the leader of a thieves' guild in Baldur's Gate during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Ravenscar directed the thieves of the Baldurian guild to aggressively ruin or otherwise suppress any would-be rivals that emerged in the city's underground.

History

In the , Alatos schemed with the Halruaan mage Resar to steal a number of material components used in the construction of Halruaan skyships from the estate of Oberan within the city.
