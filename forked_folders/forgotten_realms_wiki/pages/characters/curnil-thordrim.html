Curnil Thordrim was captain of the guard in Elventree in Cormanthor.

History

In 1372 DR, Curnil introduced the drow outcast Ryld Argith and Halisstra Melarn to his lord Dessaer.

In 1374 DR, he had finished his training in Elventree and had joined the Riders of Mistledale. That year, he investigated the disappearance of two of his fellow Riders.

Later Curnil lead his fellow Raiders against some devils escaped from Myth Drannor. They were saved only by the timely arrive of Felael Springleap and his men, members of the Seiveril Miritar's crusade.
