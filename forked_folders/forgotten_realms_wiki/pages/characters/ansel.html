Ansel was an human cleric student of the Neverwinter Academy in Neverwinter in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Ansel was a human man with a light skin tone and light hair the color of hay. He wore simple robes of an acolyte.

Relationships

Ansel attended the same school as the person who would become the Hero of Neverwinter. He studied divine magic and healing under Elynwyd, the instructor in charge of the school's clerics. Ansel was a deeply devote to his deity and believed only the divine could cure the plague that gripped the city and that Lady Aribeth de Tylmarande's attempts to stop the disease were futile and would only prolong the suffering.

History

When the Wailing Death struck the city of Neverwinter in the , Ansel resided and trained in the Neverwinter Academy, honing his magical and healing skills. However, his graduation was not to be—Ansel's life was cut short in the attack on the Academy that left most of its staff and students dead and the school itself put to the torch.
