The Masked Marquis was a legendary pirate who terrorized the Dragon Reach long before the late 14 century DR.

History

By the , the Masked Marquis was believed to had left a treasure hidden somewhere on the rocky islet of Two Rocks in the harbor of Harrowdale. Despite the stories, not a single coin from the pirate booty had ever been found.
