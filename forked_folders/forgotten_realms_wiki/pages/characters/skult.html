Skult was a thief from Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

Skult was a thief for hire, who avoided stealing from noblemen. Most of the time, he and another were hired by noblemen to steal from creditors, trade rivals, or those for whom they bore a grudge.

Relationships

Skult was often mentioned together with Vandarl, with whom he was teamed up; the duo was sometimes referred to as "Skull and Van".

Rumors & Legends

During the Open Feast of in Castle Obarskyr, Cormyr, the topic of Skult and Vandarl was brought during a reception in which a diplomatic envoy from Melvarune participated. They were compared and contrasted with the Silent Shadow.
