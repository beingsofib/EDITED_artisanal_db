Firehelm was the king of Sundabar in the Silver Marches in the late 15 century DR.

Relations

He was the son of Frosthelm and the grandson of Helm Dwarf-Friend.

History

He was killed by Tiago Baenre when Sundabar was conquered by the orc and drow armies during the Darkening in the .
