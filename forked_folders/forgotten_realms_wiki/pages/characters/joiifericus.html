Joiifericus was a succubus nursemaid serving the demon lord Graz'zt in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Joiifericus' duty was to take care of the many half-demon children of Graz'zt in Maerimydra circa 1486 DR. She would seek escape if attacked and would only fight if someone tried to abduct one of the children, but would not defend them.

Personality

Joiifericus enjoyed her work.
