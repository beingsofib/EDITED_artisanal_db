Borin Woodbracer was a dwarven ex-adventurer who worked for Lord Partic Thistle as a handyman at Thistleflame Keep circa 1369 DR.

Personality

Borin was a gruff and quiet fellow, but also creative and thoughtful. He adored the exterior stonework of Thistleflame, a major influence on his desire to work at the estate.

He had an unfortunate habit of drinking too much at the Bubbling Bulwark and rampaging around the common room, breaking furniture and causing a mess. This would result in Lord Thistle paying for the damages, or Borin performing the repairs himself.

Possessions

He had a magical warhammer from his time as an adventurer.

Relationships

Borin was a good friend to Thistleflame's gardener, Berke Glorata, as he didn't feel threatened by his stunning good looks. He tended to get on less well with elves and half-elves such as Kaleb Fleetwing, who he had a general distrust of.
