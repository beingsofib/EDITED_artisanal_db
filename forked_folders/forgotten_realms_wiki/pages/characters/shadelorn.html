Shadelorn was a Netherese archwizard who lived during the Shadowed Age.

History

The eager Shadelorn inhabited Shadowtop Borough, a enclave that held a friendly community of arcanists and archwizards. Shadelorn achieved much during his life, and even accomplished feats that several other archwizards were unable to achieve. In , he finally rediscovered the Crown of Horns, after others had spent centuries searching for it. Shadelorn even trumped Ioulaum by discovering the key to producing powerful mythallars in .
