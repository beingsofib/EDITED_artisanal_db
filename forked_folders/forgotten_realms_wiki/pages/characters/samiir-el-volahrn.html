Samiir el Volahrn was a Calishite merchant, owner of the Pearled Harp trading coster, and pasha in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the 14 century DR.

Activities

He managed the Pearled Harp trading coster, also called the Purple Pearls, which traded in both Calimport and Cormyr. In this role, he was quite powerful.

Home

Samiir lived in the Pearls, a fine house in Azhaya Drudach, Marekh Sabban, in the city's Emerald Ward. The house reached three stories and had an outer walled yard.

Relationships

He had a daughter, Vanda Volahrn, with a courtesan in Suzail, Cormyr. She would be his sole surviving offspring and heir.

History

When Samiir el Volahrn passed away in the late 1360s DR, he had no heirs to his business. Thus, agents of Syl-Pasha Ralan el Pesarkhal went to Suzail to inform Vanda of her of her father's passing and of her birthright. They offered her Samiir's status as pasha and ownership of his trading coster, provided she used it to support certain trade alliances beneficial to Calimshan's ruler. This made her one of the only female pashas in Calimport and blocked rival merchants who'd coveted her father's coster for themselves, and a target for death.
