Rindee was an apprentice scrivener in Shadowdale during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

To Maervidal Iloster, she was quite fetching.

Abilities

She was quite skilled with the brush.

Activities

She'd become an assistant to the scrivener Maervidal Iloster of Shadowdale.

History

In , Maervidal Iloster had hired her on some time ago. She lived in a farm across town.
