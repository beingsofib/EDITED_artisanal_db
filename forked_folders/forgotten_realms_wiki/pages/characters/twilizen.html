Twilizen was a powerful shadow that served the Shadovar wizard Tarys in the late 15 century DR.

History

Twilizen followed Tarys as he searched for the Nether Scroll piece that Oriphaun Huntsilver had hidden at the Old Man's Face in Tyrluk, circa 1485 DR. Despite their best efforts not only did they not find the piece, but Harpers caught up to them and slew them.
