Xemru Thaal was a high priest of Savras and leader of the House of the All-Seeing Orb in Tashluta during the late 15 century DR.

History

Sometime during the the , or early the following year, Xemru deposited the tome titled The Canopic Being within the shelves of Candlekeep, along with a hand-written note. Shortly thereafter he surrendered himself to the oracle of Tashluta, Valin Sarnaster, helping her achieve immortality as a mummy lord.
