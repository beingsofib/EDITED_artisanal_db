Julani Baenre was a drow wizard of House Baenre in Menzoberranzan and the Master of Evocation at Sorcere in the late 14 century DR.

History

Along with the other mages of House Baenre, Julani aided Gromph Baenre in attacking Nimor Imphraezl through a scrying crystal during the Siege of Menzoberranzan in the . However his magic missile spell was reflected back on Julani, killing him.
