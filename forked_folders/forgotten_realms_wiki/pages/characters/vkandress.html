V'kandress was a mysterious dragonborn follower of Tiamat and a member of Clan Vkriss in 1479 DR.

History

Little was known about V'kandress. Many believed he or she was either a priest of Tiamat or an infernal pact warlock, or an assassin, or all the three things at the same time. V'kandress was also rumored to be the leader of the Knights of the Five-Thorned Rose in the late years of the 15th century DR.
