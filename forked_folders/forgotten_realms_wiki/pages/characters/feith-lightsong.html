Feith Lightsong was a human inhabitant of Eveningstar during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Feith had reddish-brown hair tied into two braids, and wore a brown and white dress with black leggings.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, the House of the Morning was burned down by the drow raiders. She was rather stunned and dismayed by it, and visited the smoking ruins along with Wilkan Drome and Azerie Germond.
