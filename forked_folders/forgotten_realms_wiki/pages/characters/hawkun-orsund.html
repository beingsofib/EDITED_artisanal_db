Hawkun Orsund was a retired warrior and blacksmith inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Hawkun was the guildmaster for the Most Careful Order of Skilled Smiths & Metalforgers circa 1357 DR. He held the title of "Master Hammer" within the guild and worked out of Metalmasters' Hall—their headquarters on the High Road in the Southern Ward.
