Mileirigath was a sorcerer of early Netheril, and the one to turn Palarandusk into the form the gold dragon retained into the 15 century DR.

Possessions

Mileirigath owned much magic, mostly in the form of spellbooks.

Activities

The Netherese mage conducted many experiments, especially on Palarandusk.

Relationships

In life, Mileirigath owned Palarandusk, and had numerous apprentices.

History

Mileirigath was a sorcerer of early Netheril. In life, he and many apprentices experimented on Palarandusk; after his death, the dragon in turn looted his wealth and magic.
