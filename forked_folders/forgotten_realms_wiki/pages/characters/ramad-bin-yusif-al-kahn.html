Captain Ramad bin Yusif al-Kahn was the commander of the House Fajirik army stationed at the military camp near Jamal Oasis in the High Desert of west Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Activities

Renowned as a military commander, Ramad took his job very seriously. The soldiers under his command spent the vast majority of their time guarding the southern border of a qara'a, waiting patiently for the army of House Ashurim to make a move.
