Andalara was a human ranger and member of the Harpers who inhabited Tilverton and the surrounding wilderness in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Andalara usually roamed Tilver's Gap with six half-elven warriors, gathering rare woods, hunting prized game and tracking down dangerous monsters and raiding orcs that weren't caught by the Purple Dragons. She also helped her brother Dundar at Dundar's Fine Blades, where she finished blades and constructed scabbards.
