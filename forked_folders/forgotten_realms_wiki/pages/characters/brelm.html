Brelm was a cobbler from the City of Coin, Athkatla in Amn in the 14 century DR.

History

By the , Brelm was already a good friend of the proprietor of Adventurer Mart in Athkatla's grand Waukeen's Promenade, Ribald Barterman. He could have ten be found alongside his friend, throwing jabs at each other. In 1369 DR, a huge Planar Sphere crash-landed in the vicinity of the Copper Coronet. Brelm, under the influence of nine pitchers of Turmishan whisky, decided to investigate the strange structure but, like many others, failed to find a way in. The dwarf continued gossiping with Ribald about the sphere.
