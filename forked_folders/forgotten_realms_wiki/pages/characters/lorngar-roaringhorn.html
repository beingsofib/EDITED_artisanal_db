Lorngar Roaringhorn was a member of the Roaringhorn noble family of Waterdeep in the early-to-mid 14 century DR.

History

Lorngar Roaringhorn once fought a spontaneous duel with Kelvar Helmfast in the Great Library of the House of the Moon temple to Selûne in the late 1330s DR. They did substantial damage to the library, for which their respective families made reparations at many times the cost of the damage.
