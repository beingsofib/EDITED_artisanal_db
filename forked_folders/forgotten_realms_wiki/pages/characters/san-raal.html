San-Raal was an intelligent dark elf mage who perished in the mysterious Darkmoon temple, in or some time prior to 1358 DR.

Description

The drow wore a dark purple cloak over his ebony skin.

History

Upon their infiltration of Temple Darkmoon in 1358 DR, the Heroes of Waterdeep may have come across the remains of San-Raal. His body was found in the catacombs beneath the main temple, with nothing on it. If resurrected, he offered to join with the heroes.
