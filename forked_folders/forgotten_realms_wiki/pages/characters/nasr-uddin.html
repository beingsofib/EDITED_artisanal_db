Nasr-uddin, called the Lame, was a prophet who traveled the Hordelands in 1367 DR.

Description

Nasr had a long white beard that fit the physical appearance of a wandering prophet. He often wore long flowing robes.

Personality

Patience and precision was evident in all of Nasr's behavior

Activities

Nasr appeared to be spreading the teachings of Muhjari along his travels, though in reality he was a follower of Cyric and a paid assassin in the service of a merchant named Faroud.

Possessions

Nasr owned an enchanted mace, an enchanted suit of plate mail, a potion of sweet water, a potion of levitation, and an iron flask that contained an invisible stalker forced to do his bidding.
