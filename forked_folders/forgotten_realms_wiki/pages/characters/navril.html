Navril was an author some time prior to the late 14 century DR and the writer of the History of the Parsnip.

History

After spring began in the , the monks at the entrance of Candlekeep commented, when seeing the seekers coming after "Roablar of Lantan" (actually Noumea Cardellith), that their entrance-gift might well be Navril's History of the Parsnip. He also said they'd reject it one more time, just as they had previously.
