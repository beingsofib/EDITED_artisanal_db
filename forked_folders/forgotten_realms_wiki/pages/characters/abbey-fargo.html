Abbey Fargo was a soldier in the Neverwinter Guard who served during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

She had a sister named Zoey, who was also a member of Neverwinter's guard force.

History

Following the Siege of Neverwinter in the , Abbey was stationed with her sister in the city's Blacklake district.

Abbey was part of a force that stormed the Tower of Solace in order to rescue Dorothea Linkletter and stop the evil wizard Rhazzad. She fought alongside Zoey and their fellow guardsmen: Sergeant Knox, Dell McCourt, Hawthidon Faalwun, and Dorothea's husband Josef.
