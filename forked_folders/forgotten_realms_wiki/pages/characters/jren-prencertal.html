Sergeant Jren Prencertal was a strongheart halfling warrior of the militia in the village of Shining Falls in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Alongside Nemmet Viccstray and Hethat Deegrell, she served Sheriff Vendal Viccstray.
