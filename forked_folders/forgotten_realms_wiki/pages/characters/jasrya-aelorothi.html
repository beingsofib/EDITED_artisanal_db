Jasrya Aelorothi was a high-ranking daemonfey bladesinger and a member of House Aelorothi of Siluvanede.

In 1374 DR, she was one of the captains of Sarya Dlardrageth in the Cormanthor War. She died during the battle at the Vale of Lost Voices, killed by multiple arrows shot by Dalesfolk archers.
