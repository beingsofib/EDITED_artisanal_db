Carpal was a ghoul servant of Mystic Carrion, a mummy lord who resided in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

Carpal was slain and risen as an undead servant sometime before the . Mystic Carrion, who performed dark magic serviced for the wealthy clients from the Upper City, kept Carpal and other undead as his minions in Philgrave's Mansion in the Lower City. The manse was a mysterious and suspicious landmark that attracted attention due to the putrid stench of the dead that emanated from the building.
