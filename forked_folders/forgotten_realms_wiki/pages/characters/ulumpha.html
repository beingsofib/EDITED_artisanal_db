Ulumpha was a paladin member of the Dead Eyes, a criminal group from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate that named itself for the grim trophies they collected from their enemies in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Ulumpha was a half-orcish woman with handsome features, brown skin, and long brown hair. She was adorned in a simple breastplate and wielded a warhammer in a fight.

History

In the , the Dead Eyes set out to perform a job that would end up setting the gang against the Guild and its leader, Ninefingers. The Guild did not take competition lightly and sent thugs to take care of the Dead Eyes. The band survived and relocated to a new lair. Unbeknownst to Aideen Ni Chuirc, the gang's leader, one of her men was a traitor, informing the Guild of the lair and the hidden stash's location. The traitor bard instructed Ninefingers's men to strike at the hideout quickly and hard. It was unknown if Ulumpha and the Dead Eyes survived an attack from the Guild or other eager adventurers of Baldur's Gate.
