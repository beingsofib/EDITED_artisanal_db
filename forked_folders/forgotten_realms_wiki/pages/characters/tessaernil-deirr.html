Tessaernil Deirr was the star elf lord of House Deirr in the 1370s DR in Sildëyuir.

History

In 1374 DR, Lord Tessaernil welcomed back his nephew Nesterin Deirr, who was accompanied by Jorin Kell Harthan and Araevin Teshurr's party. Nesterin said they'd saved his life.
