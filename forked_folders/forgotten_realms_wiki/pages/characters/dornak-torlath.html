King Dornak Torlath "the Puppet King" was the final Amnian monarch of the Torlath dynasty.

Relationships

Dornak was the distant descendant of King Rhadik II.

History

Dornak usurped the throne of Amn in the , following the rapid assassinations of King Imnel IV, King Niskam, and King Rhor. He won the crown with the assistance of the Villax merchant house, but was himself quickly assassinated as well, putting an end to the Torlath dynasty after 635 years. The same year, the mounting chaos was halted by Erdhinn of the Wavegold family, distant descendants of Jamyll Torlath.
