Iamras Sonmaire was an individual who died in Skullport at some point prior to the .

History

The amethyst dragon Amrennathed remembered, in , that she had at some point deceived Iamras Sonmaire into bleeding himself to death so as to open an enchanted book in Skullport. After she'd tampered with his memory, he still remembered the book's location and power, but was stripped of the memory of how much blood was needed. He tried to open it by offering it his own blood, and bled to death in the process.
