Bevemar was a cleric and a member of the Shadow Thieves around 1370 DR.

Personality

Bevemar tolerated no backtalk from those she worked with, let alone those who worked for her.

Activities

Bevemar worked for both the assassin and the enforcer chapters of the guild in northern Amn. Sometimes, her work forced her to travel to Baldur's Gate. Her moniker derived for her habit of removing the tongues of debtors of the Shadow Thieves.
