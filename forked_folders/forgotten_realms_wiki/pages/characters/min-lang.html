Min Lang was a korobokuru fighter who wandered the lands of Kara-Tur in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He met and befriended the samurai Toraga and, although he could sense his friend's emotional distress, he saw himself as a loyal friend and thus refused to pry.

Possessions

He wandered the land wearing a haramaki-do, fighting with his enchanted kumade, chu-ko-nu, and kyoketsuogi. He had rings of protection and telekinesis both. He often made sure to have a potion of climbing on him.
