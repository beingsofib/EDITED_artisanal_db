Victuusa was a white abishai in Avernus, first layer of the Nine Hells, in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

In order to win the most favor, Victuusa served two masters: Tiamat and Zariel.

History

When the city of Elturel was transported to Avernus in the , Victuusa masterminded the raid on the High Hall cathedral. Afterward, commanding six human cultists of Zariel, it hunted through the cathedral for the leaders of the Elturian resistance, so that it could deliver their heads to Zariel in person.
