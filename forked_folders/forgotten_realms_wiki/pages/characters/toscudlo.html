Toscudlo was a Netherese arcanist, master of illusions, and spell inventor who lived during the Golden Age.

History

This mentalist developed seven spells in total. The spells they invented were illusionary wall in , hallucinatory terrain in , project image in , advanced illusion in , demand in , permanent illusion in , and finally, mass invisibility in .
