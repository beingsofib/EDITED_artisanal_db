Uryuga was an orc and the high shaman of Gruumsh in the Kingdom of Many-Arrows in the late 15 century DR.

History

Uryuga was convinced by Tiago Baenre, Tos'un Armgo, and Ravel Xorlarrin to support the new war against the Silver Marches, giving it the blessing of Gruumsh.
