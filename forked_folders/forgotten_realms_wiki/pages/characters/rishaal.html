Rishaal the Page-Turner () was a dragonborn shopkeeper and wizard in Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Rishaal, a dragonborn of gold dragon ancestry, managed the Book Wyrm's Treasure, a bookshop on Trollskull Alley in the North Ward. He was also a member of the Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors.
