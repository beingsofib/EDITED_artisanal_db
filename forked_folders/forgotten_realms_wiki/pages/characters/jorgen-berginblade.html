Jorgen Berginblade was the general of the Red Plumes. He was the third-ranking authority of the mercenary army after First Lord Maalthiir and his wizard apprentice Mordak Brelliar.

Personality

Jorgen was cruel and ill-tempered, but loyal to Maalthiir so long as he was paid.
