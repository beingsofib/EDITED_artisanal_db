Olma Westrin was a human resident of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Olma had light skin, long bright red hair in twin braids, and wore a long gray dress.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar during the mid-1480s DR, Olma visited the Lonesome Tankard and worried over her mother who lived in Tyrluk, which was actually being menaced by the forces of Thultanthar as they pursued the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll.
