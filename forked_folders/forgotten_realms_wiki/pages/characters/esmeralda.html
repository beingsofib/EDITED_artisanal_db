Esmeralda was an elven warrior and adventurer wandering the lands around the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

A purported descendant of one of the Knights of Myth Drannor, and quite beautiful to boot, she carried her ancestry on her shoulders with pride.

Personality

Esmeralda was very proud of her looks, and tried to not denigrate newer adventurers of lesser ancestry. She thought the ruins of Myth Drannor should be left alone and not picked over by adventurers and others like them.

Possessions

She wore an elven chainmail, and wielded a shield alongside either longsword or barbed dart blowgun.
