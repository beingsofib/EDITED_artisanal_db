Twaine Stone was a jackalwere and member of the Black Blood tribe inhabiting the island of Moray within the Moonshae Isles during the late 15 century DR.

Although capable of subterfuge,

Abilities

He had the ability to daze or even put to sleep up to two individuals with his mere gaze.

As a jackalware, he could assume the form either of a human or of a large jackal. In his human guise, he favored the falchion.

Activities

Twaine was sent to infiltrate the garrison of Caer Moray, and had successfully done so as of . He left secret messages outside of Caer Moray's walls, sharing critical information with the Black Bloods so they could better plan an attack on the town when he deemed it to be most vulnerable. He planned to open the gates and sabotage the defenses from within.
