Jolan was a craftsman within the Fellowship of Bards, Performers and Artists in the city of Ravens Bluff during the late 14 century DR. He served as the assistant to guild's Master Craftsmen Randall Morann, overseeing their workshop within the Ravenian bards' guildhouse.

Before he came to assist Morann in his duties, Jolan served as apprentice under his tutelage. While Jolan's skill was nowhere close to that of the master woodworker, his capabilities as an instrument-maker greatly improved under the senior guild member's tutelage.
