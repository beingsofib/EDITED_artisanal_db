Mausa

Person
nameMausa
sexFemale

Mausa was a woman living in Fullpoint. The farmer Hemish had sought her advice on taking care of the infant Araluen, only to find her to be superstitious and hateful. When by chance, the baby touched her horse, a broken-down nag, it was restored to health; she became fearful of the girl afterwards.
