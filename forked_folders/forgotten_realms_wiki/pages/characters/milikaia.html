Milikaia

Person
nameMilikaia
raceVampire

Milikaia was a vampire who slew Felina Tatch, an undead-hunting priestess, sometime before 1372 DR.

History

Felina Tatch spent her life freeing the tortured spirits bound to their raised bodies on the Prime Material plane by dark magics. Eventually, she set herself against Milikaia, a powerful vampire who killed the priestess and raised her as one of the blood-sucking undead and subjugated her will. However, Felina was later seen traveling across graveyards, killing the undead and seemingly free from Milikaia's control. Milikaia's fate remained unknown.
