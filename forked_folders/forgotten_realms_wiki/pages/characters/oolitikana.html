Oolitikana was an inhabitant of Mitzlato in the Pasocada Basin in Maztica.

Abilities

Oolitikana knew many languages: those of the Dog People, desert dwarves, Poscadar elves, Metahel, Nahopaca, and the Short Ones.

History

In 1362 DR, he acted as a translator to Sozarro's expedition.
