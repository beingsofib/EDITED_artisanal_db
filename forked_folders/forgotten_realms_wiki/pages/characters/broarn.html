Broarn was the innkeeper of the Woodsedge Inn in the early 3 century DR.

Description

He was stout-bodied, with a curling moustache.

Personality

Broarn casually spoke at length, even after killing someone.

Abilities

He could swing an axe in a deadly manner.

Possessions

He was the owner of Woodsedge Inn.

Activities

Broarn was an innkeeper.

Relationships

Helm Stoneblade spoke of him as a "quiet friend to outlaws".

History

In Hammer of the , Elminster headed for the Woodsedge Inn after Lawless Castle was attacked by the forces of the Magelords of Athalantar, trying to shake off their pursuit. On reaching the inn, he snuck into the stable; there, he was confronted by a mageling named Caladar Thearyn. The mageling paralyzed him, but was hewed down by Broarn, who struck him from behind with an axe. The innkepeer warned him that they had armsmen staying over, but brought him food, and told him extensively of the surroundings of Athalantar. He then advised him to become a thief to learn more of the magelords.

During the evening, he also allowed Helm Stoneblade and a number of his outlaws to use the stable to sleep.
