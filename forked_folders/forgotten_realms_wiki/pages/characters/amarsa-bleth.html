Lady Amarsa Bleth was a human woman and a leading member of the Bleth noble family of Cormyr in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Amarsa Bleth was a gentle person.

History

In the , Lady Amarsa and Lord Gruen Bleth attended a birthday feast for King Azoun IV, and were guests of Lord Partic Thistle at his manor, Thistleflame Keep. They knew nothing about Lord Thistle's apparent murder that night.
