Ruinlord Casildar was a leader of the Zhentarim, the second-in-command of Iyachtu Xvim's Heart of the Hand and Tor Blackflame in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR.

History

Casildar was a long time supporter of Fzoul Chembryl. As a cleric of Bane he was peripherally involved in the hunt for Shandril Shessair

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR, Casildar was promoted to be the second to the High Priestess of Xvim Xana the Once Martyred. After his promotion to head priest of the Heart of the Hand, Casildar gained some economic power helping coordinate the Zhent caravan trade.
