Kyrie was a well-known human adventurer who operated along the Sword Coast.

Description

She was clad entirely in black clothing. Her long mane of hair reached down to her legs, framing her symmetrical and light-skinned features.

Possessions

Kyrie owned a very potent magical suit of leather armor. She carried an enchanted short sword, and wore a cloak of displacement.

Activities

Known as a hero among the roguish types of the Sword Coast, Kyrie was celebrated as a thief who preyed on the rich, but also as a daring adventurer who went on solo treks into the Undermountain. She vanished one day, with rumors saying she had fallen in love and eloped with a sailor from Saerloon.
