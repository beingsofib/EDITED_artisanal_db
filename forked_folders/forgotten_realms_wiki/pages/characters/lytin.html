Lytin was a member of the Cult of the Eternal Flame at the Scarlet Moon Hall during the Elemental Evil crisis in 1491 DR.

History

Lytin was one of the many Eternal Flame cultists disguised as druids who waited to take part in the Rite of the Wicker Giant. She waited with her three guardians Biart, Orm, and Malin, but they were confronted and defeated by some adventurers.
