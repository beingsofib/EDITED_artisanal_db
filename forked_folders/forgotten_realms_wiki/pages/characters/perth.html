Perth, also known as Perth the Adept, was a member of the Cowled Wizards of Amn and a resident of Brynnlaw. He owned a wardstone, which could lower the defenses of Spellhold.

Personality

The wizard was very erratic in his speech and appeared to be under some sort of magical domination.

History

He lived in the north-east of Brynnlaw, where he retired from his Cowled Wizard duties. He was killed by Abdel Adrian in order to obtain a wardstone, which was necessary to enter Spellhold.
