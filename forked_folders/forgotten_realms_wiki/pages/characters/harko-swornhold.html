Harko Swornhold was an evil adventurer and bandit active on the Sword Coast North in the late 15 century DR.

History

Harko was exiled from Waterdeep in the , for attempting to bribe a magistrate. Three years later, he returned to the city illegally, where he began recruiting kenku thugs in the Dock Ward and using them to incite violence. He soon came to the attention of the Lords' Alliance, who suspected that he was on the payroll of the Xanathar's Thieves' Guild. Thus, the Alliance's Jalester Silvermane planned to send an agent to quietly eliminate him.
