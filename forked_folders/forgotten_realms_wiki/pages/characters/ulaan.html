Ulaan was and a servant in the Fortress of the Old Man. She helped wash and clothe Lewan when he was brought in by Sauk after Tarsakh 16 of , and eventually became close with him.

Appearance

To Lewan, she was close to his age, maybe a bit older. She had slanted eyes, honey-wax-colored skin, and long raven-eye hair.

Powers

As one of the personal Blades of Lady Talieth, Ulaan was trained in fighting.

History

Ulaan was sold to Lady Talieth in Almorel around . Her mother had been a servant of a merchant trading along the Golden Way.

Ulaan began to get closer to Lewan on the night of Tarsakh 19, during the assassins' vigil; she feigned fear to get into his room, and then seduced him, on the orders of Lady Talieth, who wanted to motivate him to stay in Sentinelspire.
