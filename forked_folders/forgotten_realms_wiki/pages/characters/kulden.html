Kulden was a page in the High Palace of Silverymoon in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Appearance

He was but a boy.

Activities

He was a page.

History

In , Kulden delivered a note someone left on his tray to the merchant Oscalar Maerbree. He was then called on by Alustriel Silverhand to confirm Oscalar's story, as the latter mentioned the note as his alibi for not meeting with a man. Alustriel rewarded him with a feast and a day off.
