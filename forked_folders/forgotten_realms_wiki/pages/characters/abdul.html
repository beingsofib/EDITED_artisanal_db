Abdul was a daolani inhabiting the deserts near the Free City of Muluk in .

Description

He stood tall and was nearly as wide. His skin was tanned and he had stringy dark hair. Abdul usually only wore a small loincloth so he could display the folds of his ample belly.

Personality

Abdul was cruel, dumb, and crude. However, he was fanatically loyal to his master.

Relationships

Abdul served as the familiar of the sha'ir Shafazal.
