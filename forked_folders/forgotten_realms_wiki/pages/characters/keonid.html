Keonid was a Netherese arcanist and very prolific spell inventor who lived during the Golden Age.

History

Keonid developed nine spells during their life, most of which were focused on magic that manipulated the mind. The spells they invented in the 20 century NY were geas in , confusion in , friends in , emotion in , fear in , and forget in .

The spells they invented in the 21 century NY were charm person in , mass charm in , and finally, suggestion in .
