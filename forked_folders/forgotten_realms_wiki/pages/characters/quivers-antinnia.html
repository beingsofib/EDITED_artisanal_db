Quivers Antinnia was an unsavory human archer mercenary from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Quivers was a dark-skinned human male adorned in quality leathers.

History

In the , the opportunistic and amoral mercenary Aradin Beno set out to find the Nightsong for the mage Lorroakan. If the Nightsong, in reality a Selûnite aasimar, was to reach the city of Baldur's Gate, then Beno had a band of mercenaries with questionable morals ready to attack those who sheltered her. The archer named Quivers Antinnia was among those mercenaries, ready to break into the Elfsong Tavern to kill and kidnap.
