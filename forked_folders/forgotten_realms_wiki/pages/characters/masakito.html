Masakito was a human fighter of Kara-Tur in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Having four older brothers, Masakito grew up feeling he had to prove himself and escape from out of their shadows. Thus, to win renown and fortune, he went on many missions and quests, no matter how dangerous or beyond his ability.

History

He once fought with the ninja Takako.

Possessions

Masakito wielded a katana.
