Reinhar IV was a king of the Arkaiuns of Dambrath.

Relationships

The man who would rule as Reinhar IV was nephew to King Reinhar III through a sister. He had at least three sons, two of whom would rule as Reinhar V and Reinhar VI after him.

History

Reinhar IV was born in the Year of Withing Darkness, 572 DR.

When King Reinhar III and his two sons were killed by giants while on a hunting trip in 600 DR, this triggered a short war of succession. At the end of the war, Reinhar IV took the throne of his uncle.

In 617 DR, a plague causing bloating spread throughout Dambrath, killing ten percent of the population, including Reinhar IV. His second son succeeded him as Reinhar V.
