Phoebe was a human commoner inhabitant of Neverwinter's Beggar's Nest in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Phoebe was a human woman with a pale complexion, brown eyes, and blonde hair. She appeared distraught and haggard after losing her home to the plague.

History

During the time the Wailing Death ravaged Neverwinter in the , Phoebe's home district, Beggar's Nest, was hit the hardest. Those who died of the plague were burned on the city streets, and, to make things worse, the district erupted in a zombie infestation. Phoebe managed to evacuate from the district before the Neverwintran militia quarantined it off. Now homeless, Phoebe had to sleep on city streets by a beggars' campfire in the City Core, where she mourned her old home and the fact that she couldn't wait for the plague to take her in the comfort of her own bed.
