Ravos Calantar was a member of the Calantar family, a noble house of Cormyr.

History

Following the execution of his aunt and matriarch of his family, Lady Calantar, Ravos came to hate the Obarskyrs with a passion. By 1372 DR, the Cormyrean rulers expected that he was likely to rebel against them.

Personality

Ravos was bored with life and sought to perform great deeds.

He enjoyed hunting both stags and bandits.
