Nrakka

Nrakka was a spell-slinging pterafolk who menaced Kir Sabal in Chult circa late 15 century DR.

Description

Nrakka had a dark-green leathery skin with lighter aquamarine speckles and striping on the creature's feet.

Abilities

Nrakka was a powerful individual and had knowledge of the arcane, often showering its opponents with fireballs from the sky.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Nrakka took a habit of attacking inhabitants of the Kir Sabal monastery in Chult inhabited by a flock of aarakocra and several your human children. The bird-folk sought help against the cunning and cruel foe among with adventurers who carried to Chult investigating the death curse.
