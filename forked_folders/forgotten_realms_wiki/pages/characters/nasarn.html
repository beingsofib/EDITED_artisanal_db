Nasarn "The Hooded" was a magelord of Athalantar in the 3 century DR.

Appearance

The old man stared without blinking, and wore a black robe, that gave his face the profile of a large vulture.

Activities

His main task as a Magelord was watching for treachery, by presenting himself as a rival to Ithboltar.

Relationships

He was Ithboltar's oldest apprentice, and joined him to become one of the Magelords of Athalantar. He also presented himself as an alternative tutor to Ithboltar.

Possessions

He had a country house near Jander, where he kept the petrified bodies of those he slew.

History

Nasarn the Hooded came to Athalantar together with Ithboltar, hoping for peace and stability. As a Magelord, he was assigned the task of watching for treachery against the Mage Royal.

He was present at a meeting with other Magelords in , during Elmara's time at Narthil. He was not seen in the kingdom after the , though he was not known to have died.
