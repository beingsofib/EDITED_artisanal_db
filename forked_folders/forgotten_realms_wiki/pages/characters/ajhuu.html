Ajhuu was a powerful marid leader during the time of the Calim Empire.

History

Ajhuu was one of Calim's most powerful allies but grew frustrated when Calim capitulated to the elves of Keltormir and the dwarves of Shanatar to fix his borders at the edges of their realms in . Ajhuu was one of several powerful genies who broke from Calim's alliance to establish their own realms, accompanied by his followers Luar and Haj.

By the time Calim returned to his seat of power in Calimport, the city of Ajhuutal had already been built, with the Maridlands taking the eastern portion of his empire. Calim was determined to bring the rogue genies back under his exclusive control and waged war on Ajhuu, starting the Steam Clashes. Calim won the war and destroyed Ajhuu, but the means he deployed—the Shattering—took a heavy toll on both sides and the surrounding land.
