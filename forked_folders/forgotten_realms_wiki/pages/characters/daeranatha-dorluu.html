Daeranatha Dorluu was the olanger, or ruler, of Suldolphor in the far eastern reaches of Calimshan in south Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Daeranatha was an old lady as of the . She preferred to wear silk robes of bright orange coloration.

Activities

In addition to ruling the Golden City, Daeranatha was a high priestess of Waukeen. She became somewhat disillusioned after her goddess' disappearance following the Time of Troubles. She ruled Suldolphor for nearly a century, but her health begun to fade quickly with the loss of her spells following Waukeen's silence.
