Pelloth was a drow mercenary who worked for the infamous Dom in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Pelloth had long flowing white hair, and wore a suit of plate mail while carrying two scimitars coated in poison.

Relationships

Pelloth had an unknown bond with Dom that ensured his loyalty. While on the surface he befriended and tamed a brush creeper giant spider from the King's Forest, keeping it as a companion.

History

Coming up from the Underdark like Udere, Pelloth took up work as a mercenary and joined Dom's team. His last mission took them to Greenweald Lane, where Dom was hired by the Shadovar of Thultanthar to waylay Bensen Virandson as he transported a fragment of a sought-after Nether Scroll to the nearby Harper Lodge. Unfortunately for Pelloth, his unfamiliarity with the surface world caused his ambush to disturb a hungry dire bear, allowing Bensen and his allies to ambush him right back during the confusion, ending his career.
