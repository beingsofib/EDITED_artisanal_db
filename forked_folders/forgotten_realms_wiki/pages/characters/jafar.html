Jafar was a half-orc pirate captain and leader of the Bloody Buccaneers and a member of the Rundeen active on the Sea of Swords and the Shining Sea of west Faerûn in the late 14 century DR.

Possessions

He was captain of the cutter Water Weird.

Activities

They targeted non-Rundeen-affiliated ships transporting trade goods south into the Shining Sea. What cargoes they took were given to the Rundeen in return for rewards and the goods were then sold on by Rundeen businesses.

History

Since about 1366 DR, the Bloody Buccaneers had successfully attacked and raided over eight ships. Jafar was formerly first mate on the Water Weird, serving under its captain and co-leader of the pirates.

Then, in the , ships owned by the Bladesmile and Bormul merchant houses of Amn fought back against a raid, even killing the captain of the Water Weird. Jafar succeeded him to the rank of captain and co-leadership of the pirates, and he and Jhamil al Tassand, his fellow captain and brother to the slain captain, swore to have their revenge on Bladesmile and Bormul.
