Yturn was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the Age of Discovery.

Description

This arcanist focused on spells which manipulated how long one could stay in the air.

History

Yturn developed three spells during their life, each one year after the other. The spells they developed were levitate in , jump in , and finally, feather fall in .
