Zasheena Drethyl was one of the leaders of the Shadowkiss, a criminal organization that operated in the Dusk Ports of Laerakond.

Activities

Unknown for most people, Zasheena was a head member of the criminal organization "Shadowkiss". She usually hired adventurers to fulfill special tasks on her behalf, and even seduced them when she believed was productive for her current goals. Her main goal was to eventually using the drugs sell by the Shadowkiss to control the green dragon Orlarrakh.
