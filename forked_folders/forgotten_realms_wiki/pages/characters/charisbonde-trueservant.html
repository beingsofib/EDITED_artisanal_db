Patriarch Charisbonde Trueservant was the High Priest of the House of the Morning temple in the village of Eveningstar during the late 14 century DR.

Activities

Charisbonde was the leader of Lathander's faithful in Eveningstar. He was assisted by 28 other priests and had a congregation of 170 followers.
