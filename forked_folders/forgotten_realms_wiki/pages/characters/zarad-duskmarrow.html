Zarad Duskmarrow

Person
nameZarad Duskmarrow
sexMale
occupationWizard

Zarad Duskmarrow was a wizard who dwelled in a great clock tower hidden in the depths of the Cloak Wood. According to rumor, he studied necromancy and other strange arts. Ysuran Auondril served him as an apprentice, and wrote his black tome under his tutelage.
