Regg was an abbot of Lathander in Sembia in the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

Regg was a good friend of Abelar Corrinthal, like his father Torar was a good friend of Endren Corrinthal.

History

In the , like many inside the church of Lathander, Regg joined the Risen Sun heresy despite the objections of his friend Abelar. During the Sembian Civil War, Regg provided refuge for Abelar and his men for a time.
