Dablor Zimmulstern was a scribe inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Dablor was the guildmaster for the Scriveners, Scribes, & Clerks Guild circa 1357 DR. He held the title of "High Atlar" within the guild and worked out of the Zoarstar–their headquarters on Quill Alley in the Trades Ward.
