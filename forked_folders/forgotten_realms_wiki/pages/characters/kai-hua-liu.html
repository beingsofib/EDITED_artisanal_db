K'ai Hua Liu was a bushi in Shou Lung in the mid–14 century DR. She was a member of the Liu Clan.

Activities

Going back many generations, the Liu Clan had a feud with the powerful Feng Su clan of Cham Fau. Circa 1357 DR, K'ai was tasked with protecting Liu ships and spying upon Feng Su agents. She believed them to be consorting with pirates of the Hungtse River and would do anything to have proof of that.
