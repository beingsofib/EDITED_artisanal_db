Gorstaf was a duergar baker, who specialized in fungus pies, from the Gracklstugh in the Underdark in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Gorstaf was a duergar man with obsidian black skin. He had a long bushy white beard and wore a chef's toque and was armed with a menacing-looking oversized spoon.

Activities

Gorstaf's bakey was located in the Darklake district of Gracklstugh, an odd place for a bakery due to the district's size and population. Gorstaf's menu included bluecap pies, ripplebark pies, and myconid pies, among others.
