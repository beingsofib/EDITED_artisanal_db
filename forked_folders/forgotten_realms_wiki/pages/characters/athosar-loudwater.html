Prior Athosar was a priest of Lathander in the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

For the authorities and adventurers alike, Athosar was able to raise the dead for a fee of 1000 to 1100 gp and cure lycanthropy, which he did free of charge. He could also with speak with the dead and discern the truth of one's words.
