Adanac Harpell was the elder of the Harpell family and spokesman for the town of Longsaddle in the Savage Frontier in the 14 century DR.

Relationships

The magic-user was the father of Ardanac Harpell.

History

Around 1357 DR, Adanac lived in the Ivy Mansion, the Harpell's ancestral home, on a hill in the center of Longsaddle.
