Oorag was a half-orc fighter leading ambush forces in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was an evil person who had submitted to a more powerful leader, and in turn been put in charge of the scouting and ambushing of other hostile forces. A fearsome sight to behold, he often lost himself in fits of brutality and blood lust.

Possessions

He rode his prized nightmare into battle, wielding an enchanted axe and a short sword, while wearing banded mail, bracers of archery, gauntlets of swimming and climbing, and a ring of regeneration.
