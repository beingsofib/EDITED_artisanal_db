Curse of Irphong

Person
nameCurse of Irphong
homeIrphong, Nelanther Isles

The Curse of Irphong can refer to either of the two creatures that inhabited the lair underneath Irphong island in the Nelanther Isles.

A dragon turtle was the first to be known by this name. It was one of the oldest and largest of its kind. It was slain by a kraken in 1363 DR.

The kraken appropriated the dragon’s name and treasures. It now attack ships that pass through the region, especially those that travel alone and at night.

Lair

Its lair is accessed through an underwater entrance in a sea-cove entrance on the north side of the island. The 1500 foot underwater passage leads to the center of the island, where the treasure is kept.
