Amrythale Goldsheaf was a woman of Heldon in Athalantar in the early 3 century DR. She was the mother of Elminster Aumar.

Relationships

Her father was a forester. She married Elthryn Aumar, a prince of Athalantar.

History

She married Elthryn Aumar in the . They had a child, Narrune Aumar, in the , but she went missing, leaving only blood on her crib. They had another child, Elminster Aumar, who was born in the .

It was said she must've bewitched Prince Elthryn somehow to get him to marry her.
