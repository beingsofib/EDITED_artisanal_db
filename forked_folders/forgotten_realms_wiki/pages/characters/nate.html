Nate was a young boy living in Kuldahar during the 13 century DR.

Description

The blond-haired boy wore a green tabard and brown breeches.

Personality

Nate was an inquisitive young man. Unlike many kids in the small community, Nate liked to watch the surrounding area around the Great Oak.

Relationships

Nate was friends with Archdruid Arundel.

History

In Eleint of the , Nate met a group of mercenaries who decided to help the citizens of Kuldahar and investigate the Vale of Shadows.
