Councilman Ferran Martinez was a member of the Council of Ten in Phlan and a priest of Tyr during the mid—14 century DR.

History

Ferran led a group of citizens to Sokol Keep where they hid during the Dragon Run attack on Phlan. His entire group eventually perished within the keep.
