Samshin was a Zhentarim in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

He mostly operated outside the Dalelands.

Relationships

He used Maervidal Iloster as a contact, and was a superior to Oleir and Rostin.

History

In the , Samshin was posing as a farmhand looking for work. He took some time out of that year to make sure Maervidal Iloster, a local scrivener and Zhentarim spy, went to a revel in Warmfires House where the Zhentarim were planning to kill him for being a double agent.
