Malaggar Xarann was the head of the College of Enchantment and one of the members of the Conclave of Sshamath in the city of Sshamath in the year 1372 DR.

History

In 1377 DR, Q'arlynd Melarn told the priestess of Eilistraee Miverra that the College of Enchantment ruled the city's slave market.
