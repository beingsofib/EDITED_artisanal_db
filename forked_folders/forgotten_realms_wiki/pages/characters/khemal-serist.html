Khemal Serist was a member of the Cult of the Shattered Peak.

Personality

Khemal preferred distracting seekers of Netherese lore rather than killing them outright. He was flexible in his tactics.

Possessions

Khemal wore an enchanted mithral shirt.

History

Khemal rose quickly within the cult after proving his skill and effectiveness over several missions.
