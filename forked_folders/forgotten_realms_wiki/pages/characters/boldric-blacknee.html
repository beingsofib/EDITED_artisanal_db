Boldric Blacknee was a shiftless young halfling who helped run the Blacknee's Halfhappenstance inn circa 1369 DR with his parents Buzzrip Blacknee and Broonella Blacknee, and his elder siblings Breena Blacknee and Billyup Blacknee.
