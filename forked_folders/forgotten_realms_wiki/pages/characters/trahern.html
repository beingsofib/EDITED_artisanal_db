Trahern of Oakvale was an old druid in charge of the Oakvale, a small forest in the Myrloch Vale in the Moonshae Isles in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was proud of his caretaker-ship of the Oakvale.

History

In the , Trahern was corrupted by Kazgoroth in the disguise of a dryad so he could show Grunnarch the Red and his army a safe and fast passage through Myrloch Vale.

After Kazgaroth's defeat, Trahern searched the pond where Kazgaroth fell and recovered a skull-sized black rock—the Heart of Kazgaroth.

A year later, a feral Trahern found his way to the moonwell and Myrloch Vale, which was being tended to by Robyn Kendrick and Genna Moonsinger. The close proximity to the heart caused Genna to fall ill.
