Prince Dhanar Parhek "the Younger" was an unfortunate Amnian son of the Parhek dynasty.

Relationships

Dhanar was the eldest son of King Dhanar Parhek. He was the twin brother of Princess Rasa, and the older brother of Prince Malek the Coinmage.

History

Dhanar the Younger was kept in the royal court of Tethyr after his father was killed and Amn fell into civil war. Unfortunately, in the , he suffered an unfortunate "hunting accident" at the same time as King Errilam I (actually the work of Eldreth Veluuthra assassins).
