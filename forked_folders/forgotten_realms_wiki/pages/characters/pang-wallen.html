Pang Wallen was a member of the Baldur's Gate branch of the Iron Throne during the mid–14 century DR.

History

When Sarevok took control of the Baldurian Iron Throne, following the death of Rieltar Anchev in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR, Pang left the merchant's group and the city for good. He believed the new leader of the organization had run their business into the ground, having no real care for profits or wealth.
