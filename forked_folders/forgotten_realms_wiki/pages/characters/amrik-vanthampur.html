Amrik Vanthampur () was the second youngest son of Thalamra Vanthampur of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

Amrik was born to Thalamra Vanthampur and her second husband.

By the , Amrik ran a moneylending business in the tavern known as the Low Lantern.
