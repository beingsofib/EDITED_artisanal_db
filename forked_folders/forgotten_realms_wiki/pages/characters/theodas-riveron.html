Theodas Riveron was the "Bandit King" of the High Road in Cormyr during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Theodas was notable for his extraordinarily large hat and feather accessory.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, the Purple Dragons pulled back from all non-vital positions in the region to focus on the war. This meant that much of the High Road was left undefended for a time, allowing bandits and monsters to pass without challenge. Theodas and his bandits were thus able to raid and loot as they wished. His gang was largely made up of humans and half-orcs, making use of tamed wolves to support their attacks on locals and travelers.
