Durnibas Uxyndar was a skilled drow arcanist during the 1480s DR.

Description

Durnibas had his white hair worn long and loose.

Abilities

Durnibas was a powerful spellcaster and knew ball lightning, chain lightning, delayed blast fireball, force missiles, Otiluke's freezing sphere, and polar ray.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Durnibas lurked in the ruins of Riz Malag, not too far from Sschindylryn itself. He studied a pool of water in the ruins, from which he could summon two loyal water elementals.
