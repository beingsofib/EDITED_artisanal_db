Dariel Tefoco was a member of the Flaming Fist and one of the older children of Oliver Tefoco, the proprietor of the Beehive Goods and Wares general store in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Dariel was a son to Oliver and Kroyce Tefoco, and had a number of siblings. The oldest of them was Bubbles Tefoco, who lived in the Stonyeyes district of the Gate with her family and the newborn Dorimara. Siblings Crowell and Thegglis traveled as caravan guards with merchants along the Sword Coast North, and the youngest siblings Hemjax, Maggie, and others, still lived with Oliver and Kroyce as of the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , as the Cult of the Absolute threatened Baldur's Gate's safety. The menace appeared around the time Dariel Tefoco decided to join the Flaming Fist in search of adventures. In his letters home, Dariel ensured his father that he was eating well despite the barracks gruel being nowhere as good as Oliver's cooking. Dariel eagerly shared exciting things he got to see as a member of the Flaming Fist, including witnessing one of the newly introduced steel watchers destroying cultists of the Absolute outside of Rivington. The young Fist praised Lord Enver Gortash and the deadly constructs he provided to the city.
