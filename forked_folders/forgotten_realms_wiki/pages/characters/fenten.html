Fenten was a dwarf who lived in Baldur's Gate during the mid–14 century DR.

Possession

He wore a suit of chainmail armor and wielded a battleaxe in combat.

Relationships

He was an acquaintance of the ranger Gerde.

History

In the , Fenten helped Gerde cull the ankheg infestation that sprung up at a farm on the southern bank of the River Chionthar, just beyond Wyrm's Crossing.
