Librarian Kaylie was an elven librarian associate of the electrum dragon Jurin'ildren in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Kaylie had light skin and long brown hair, and wore a white and blue outfit.

History

Around the , Kaylie worked with the dragon Jurin'ildren to protect an intact library in the ruins of Myth Drannor. Unfortunately, just as the Elves of Cormanthor arrived to reclaim the city, planar fluctuations caused rifts to the Elemental Plane of Fire to draw fiery creatures into the great treehouse, turning it into the Burning Library. While Jurin'ildren's abilities could keep the contents of the library safe for a time, Kaylie desperately needed some hands to get rid of the intruders before they annihilated the whole tree. Meeting Jurin'ildren's help at the entrance, she directed them to the different areas of the library to find Nepri, T'Zull, and Thulkaan while guarding the entrance and helping maintain the magical defenses.
