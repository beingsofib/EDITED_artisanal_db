Marren was a monk at the Monastery of the Yellow Rose.

History

In 1491 DR, Marren and some other monks were kidnapped by cultists of the Cult of the Howling Hatred, who planned to use them in a new plot to access an elemental node. In a room of the node's complex under the monastery, thanks to the elemental forces, Marren's body was switched that of a cultist, Luros. Later, he met an adventuring party hired by Grandmaster Perrywinkle Shin to find the missing monks. The adventurers managed to put Marren back in his body.
