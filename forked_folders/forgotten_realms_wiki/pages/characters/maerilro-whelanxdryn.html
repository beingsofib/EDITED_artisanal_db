Maerilro Whelanxdryn was a businessman of Elturel who operated Whelanxdryn's of Elturel in the 15 century DR.

Activities

Maerilro was the latest of the Whelanxdryns to run their scabbardmaking business. Under his influence the business grew in size, and he began making sizable investments in Elturel, Baldur's Gate, and Iriaebor, as well as becoming an important landlord in those places.
