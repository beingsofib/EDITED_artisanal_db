Diaga was a fighter of the mid-to-late 11 century DR.

Her wizard sister Eriel forged the enchanted long sword Draxalon as a gift for her and she used it for many years. However, when Diana went adventuring in the ruins of Myth Drannor, both she and the sword were lost.
