Aurora Brightday was a human proxy of the Faerûnian power known as Lathander.

Activities

Aurora spent most of her time operating the temple of Lathander that stood in the divine realm of Morninglory.

Rumors & Legends

Rumors claimed that Aurora had wed Allain Crimson, a proxy of the Vedic power Ushas, who likewise spent his time in Morninglory operating a temple to his power. One rumor stated that she was even pregnant with his child.
