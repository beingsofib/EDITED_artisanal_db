Khondall Sszundar was a mage of Faerûn.

Circa 1357 DR, Khondall bought the Book of Bats from the merchant Chulu Thall of Ithmong, and was able to catalog the tome's spells. But he had it but briefly before an unknown enemy dispatched demons that demolished his tower, and the tome was lost. Shortly after, the Book of Bats resurfaced in Innarlith, then was swiftly stolen.
