Lerithyn was a drow fighter, mage, thief, and adventurer who wandered the Realms in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He would use cantrips to change the color of his skin, hiding his parentage from those surface dwellers he worked alongside. He would reveal his true nature as a drow if he felt those around him would tolerate him.

Possessions

He wore a set of drow chainmail made from adamantite, a cloak of displacement, and a set of non-magical adamantite swords.

History

Lerithyn was the son of a priestess of Eilistraee.
