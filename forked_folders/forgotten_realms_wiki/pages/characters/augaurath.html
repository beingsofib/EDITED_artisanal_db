Augaurath was a powerful white dragon who lived in the High Ice region.

Description

Augaurath was gigantic in size.

Activities

As the undisputed ruler of all dragons in her region, Augaurath was worshiped as a deity by lesser beings such as yetis, remorhazes, and winter wolves.
