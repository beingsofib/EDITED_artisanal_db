Oinker-Boinker was an ettin sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Oinker-Boinker was one of the minions of the hill giant Chief Guh in Grudd Haug. His duty was to take care of the pigs together with some bugbears.
