Qhelaraxxalarr was a mind flayer.

History

Once Elminster defeated and froze Qhelaraxxalarr.

In 1487 DR, Elminster summoned it to Myth Drannor in order to create chaos among the Sembian mercenaries and then bound Qhelaraxxalarr to that city.
