Canthus was a large moorhound which belonged to Tristan Kendrick, Prince of Corwell in the Moonshae Isles.

Description

The large moorhound was strong with thick brown fur and powerful legs. Canthus was more commanding than others of his breed with teeth the size of a person's little finger.

Possessions

Protected by the Goddess, Canthus wore a torque of the Goddess.

History

In 1345 DR, during the Festival of the Spring Equinox on Gwynneth, Pawldo sold Canthus to Prince Tristan for 50 gp after a dramatic round of bartering.
