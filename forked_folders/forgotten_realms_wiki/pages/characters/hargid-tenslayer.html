Hargid Tenslayer was the Tharchion of Lapendrar in Thay during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Hargid, along with some other Thayan nobles and the Zulkir of Conjuration, hatched a plot in the , where they planned to open a gate to the Elemental Plane of Fire, strike deals with the lords of the plane, and have select cities on the north coast of the Sea of Fallen Stars destroyed in order to rid themselves of competition.
