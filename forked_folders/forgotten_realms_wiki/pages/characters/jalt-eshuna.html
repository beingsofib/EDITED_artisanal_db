Jalt Eshuna

Person

Eshuna was a dragonborn knight and the quartermaster of the Platinum Cadre.

Activities

He was also in charge of the security of the village of Ruinspoke, and as such he was tasked to seek out and recruit trustworthy adventurers when needed.
