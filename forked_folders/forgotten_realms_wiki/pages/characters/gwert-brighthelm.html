Gwert Brighthelm was one of the dwarven citizens of Ironmaster during the 15 century DR.

Relationships

Gwert was a member of Clan Brighthelm and cousin of Augrek Brighthelm of Bryn Shander.

Possessions

He was known to brandish a warhammer.
