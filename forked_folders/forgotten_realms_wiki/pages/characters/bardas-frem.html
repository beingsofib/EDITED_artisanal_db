Bardas Frem was a farm worker in the 14 century DR.

Relationships

Bardas was the cousin of Godfroy Cormas.

History

In 1373 DR, the farm on which Bardas worked was raided by hobgoblins. Bardas was speared to death, and many others were killed alongside him.
