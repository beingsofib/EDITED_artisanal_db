Evren Feray () was a diviner as well as the chief navigator and sailing master of a caravel that operated out of Ayakar in Turmish, and throughout the Sea of Fallen Stars.

Personality

She was a rather warm individual that enjoyed meeting new people and availing them of her services.

Abilities

While at sea, Evren used divination magic to better navigate the open seas and avoid perils during the ships voyages. Interestingly enough, her powers originated from her rigorous studies of nautical navigation and seafaring.

Possessions

Evren kept a number of maps that detailed notable locations across the Sea of Fallen Stars.

Relationships

She was a trusted advisor to Captain Lazlo Sharktooth.

History

Some time during or after the , Captain Lazlo and his crew took on a job to uncover the whereabouts of Zehira Deryan. During their journey, Evren performed a divination ritual in an attempt to discern Zehira's exact location.

Later on, she joined the landing party that ventured onto Hawk's Isle in search of the missing noblewoman. While the party was resting, Evren and her crewmate Samara Elisiar were abducted by the morkoth Axar Xyrl, and taken back to its lair.
