Amon was a mage who adventured along with several companions in Amn during the mid–14 century DR.

He was seemingly a member of the enigmatic Purple Brotherhood.

Relationships

Among the other members of Amon's band was the dwarven warrior Mencar Pebblecrusher, the thief Brennan Risling, as well as the half-orc barbarian Smaeluv Orcslicer. He appeared to barely tolerate the brutish half-orc.

Amon was also accompanied by his imp familiar Pooky, who referred to him as "Papa".

Possessions

Among his possessions was a cloak of non-detection and a wand of lightning.

History

At some point in the , Amon and his group stayed at the Den of the Seven Vales in Athkatla.
