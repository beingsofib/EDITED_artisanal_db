Nikolai Nikopolis was a member of the Unclean in 1367 DR.

Personality

Nikolai was a repugnant pig. As a favored student, he felt he could get away with anything, including pestering the low-ranking holy slayers, guards, and farisan who lived in Sarahin.

Description

Nikolai was a tall man with pale skin. His dirty blond hair contrasted sharply with those of his zakharan neighbors. His eyes were deep blue, appearing violet at times.

History

Fascinated by genies, Nicolai traveled far across Faerûn to Zakhara in order to learn the mystical ways of the sha'ir.

Activities

Nikolai was the lead apprentice of Akim al-Kalaas.
