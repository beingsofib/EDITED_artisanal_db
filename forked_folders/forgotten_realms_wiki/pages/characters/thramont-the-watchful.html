Thramont the Watchful was a senior Red Wizard in Droic Orgul's raid on Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

History

Thramont was assigned to commanding a Red Wizard outpost at the Irithlium, which they used for storing documents and records relating to their overarching schemes in the ruins. He fulfilled his final duty when the Elves of Cormanthor and Maloran Herthana sent a team to search the Irithlium for clues about Droic's plans, and slew him in the process.
