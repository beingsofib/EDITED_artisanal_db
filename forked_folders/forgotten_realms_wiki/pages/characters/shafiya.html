Chief Shafiya was the leader of the Haifami tribe of Jazayir al-Alfar in the Crowded Sea in 1367 DR.

Description

Shafiya was a young man of incredible physical prowess and vigor. He had black skin like all of the members of the Haifami tribe.

History

Shafiya was the third son of the previous chief of the Haifami.
