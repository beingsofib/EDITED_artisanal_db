Golotl the Merchant was a merchant and thief in Nexal.

History

Golotl was one of the renegades living in the human quarter of Nexal in 1362 DR. That year, he met his old friend Hasoctl and an adventuring party hired by Drakosa to find the Cloak of One Plume for the right price.

However, soon after, Candoria and his gang of thieves attacked them with the aim of killing all people present.
