Claive the Carver was a Cormyrean bandit who prowled the King's Forest in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Claive was a hulkingly large man who wore a half-mask over his face and carried a sword.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Claive was part of a werewolf-filled bandit gang that predated upon the King's Forest and High Road. With the collapse of Cormyrean authority in the region due to the war, the bandits went largely uncontested as they raided and robbed. When the drow destroyed a Purple Dragons forest outpost the bandits moved in to use it as their own base, not realizing that it was an important magical site. In response, the Wizard of War Arganon Pine struck back with the Purple Dragons, intent on completing a ritual that would protect the area from dark influences. Claive joined the bandits' counterattack on the Cormyreans, but fell in battle alongside the werewolf Bladeback.
