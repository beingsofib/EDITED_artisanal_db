Scattercloak was a mage in Thentia in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

As of , Scattercloak wore always a gray mantle with the hood up.

History

Scattercloak was one of the mages in Thentia assisting Karasendrieth's party in decoding Sammaster's notes about the Rage of Dragons in , and one of the people who helped slay Phourkyn One-Eye's impersonator. When in that fight, he cast spellstrike to reverse the wyrm's escape.
