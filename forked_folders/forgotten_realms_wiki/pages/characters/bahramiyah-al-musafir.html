Bahramiyah al-Musafir was a caravan master from Hiyal in Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Activities

For over 40 years Bahramiyah oversaw the great caravans that transported fine weapons crafted by the House of Bulad from Hiyal to Qudra. She traveled with each shipment.

Relationships

Her sons and daughters managed most of the business as she aged.
