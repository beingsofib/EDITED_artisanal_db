Lyle was a kid in Stag Falls during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

After the vampire Romanisk Vlaensk turned Baron Vilhelm von Stag Falls into his undead servant, Vilhelm ordered the dogs in his kennels be killed, as their baying upset the vampire. Lyle, a local young boy, heard of this and decided to release them all from their cages, so they would not be senselessly killed. The Baron's men struck him down with a crossbow bolt as he fled the scene.
