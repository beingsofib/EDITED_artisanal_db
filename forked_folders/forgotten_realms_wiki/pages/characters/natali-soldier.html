Natali was a soldier stationed at the Damaran Gate of Bloodstone Pass in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

She was a slender redhead with a sunburned neck.

History

She was the first victim of the traitor Fodel, an infiltrator of the Damaran Gate for the Cult of the Dragon, on the night of Mirtul 2 in the . After she spotted a flight of dragons approaching, she was about to signal with her bugle, but Fodel quickly slew her. Her blood and gore on his chest would later serve as a warning for a local paladin.
