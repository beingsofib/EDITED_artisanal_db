Salid Dhuzma, or the Grand Verser of the South, was a famous bard who lived in Khalab during the mid 14h century DR.

Activities

Students from all over East Faerûn and the Hordelands would travel to the small fishing village of Khalab with the hopes of becoming Salid's next pupil. Sadly for these prospective students, Salid would only take one pupil each year.

Salid lived within Baelzur's Tower. He preferred the comfort of living in a small village to the riches he could have had while living in one of the larger cities located on Gbor Nor.

Relationships

Salim was once the apprentice of Tilmar Wanderfoot.
