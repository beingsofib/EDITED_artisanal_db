Mauza Dalidasis was an innkeeper inhabiting Kadarasto in 1367 DR.

Personality

Mauza was charming and personable—truly rare traits for the citizens of the "City Most Sinister."

Activities

Mauza owned the Broken Wheel inn which, aside from the caravanserai found outside the city gates, was one of a few reputable places to rent a room in the entire city. She was a valuable source of information for anyone willing to spend the coin.

Relationships

Mauzo was married to Cholk min Kado who owned the Shop of Faded Carpets.
