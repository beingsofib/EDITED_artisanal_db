Brawler Rancer was a goblin cultist who followed the Absolute during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Rancer had orange skin, decorated with light blue body point, and black hair. He wore a leather harness and cap.

Possessions

In battle, Rancer wielded a crudely-made goblin scimitar.

History

As of the , Rancer dwelled within the cult's stronghold of the Shattered Sanctum.
