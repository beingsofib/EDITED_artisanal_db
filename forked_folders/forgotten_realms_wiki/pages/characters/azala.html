Azala Fire-eyes () was a wizard in Teziir in 1357 DR.

History

Azala had a great adventuring career, even helping to defeat the Great Worm of Telflamm before retiring to a tower in Teziir. She became a regional member of the Moonstars.

Activities

Azala was usually busy in her magical research and in creating small magic items. However, thanks to her close connection with the city's government, she also helped the Teziir militia.
