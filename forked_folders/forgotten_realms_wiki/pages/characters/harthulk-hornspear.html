Harthulk Hornspear was the chief of the Thunderbeast tribe during the late 15 century DR.

History

Harthulk Hornspear had convinced all of the Thunderbeast clans to hide in the Lurkwood, preparing them for attacks in the Surbrin Hills and the Dessarin Valley.
