Diamssar was a representative of the Triceratops Society in Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 century DR. Secretly, he was actually a spy for a group of yuan-ti.

Description

He had pale skin and dressed in dark clothes.

Personality

Hiss skills with deception hid his true fanatical nature.

Possessions

He was in possession of a potion of healing and a jeweled dagger.

Relationships

Diamssar worked with the Harper agent Soggy Wren, whom he had been manipulating into finding the pieces of a Dreamer's Amulet that had been smuggled into Port Nyanzaru by his yuan-ti masters. He was affiliated with Lekhet, a yuan-ti pureblood.

He had a pet giant poisonous snake.

History

During the events of the death curse around 1490 DR, Diamssar was a double agent for a group of yuan-ti while working as a representative of the Triceratops Society. For the Triceratops Society, he worked outside the public bathhouse. His secret yuan-ti business operated out of the abandoned Northside Market Textile House warehouse in the Market Ward.
