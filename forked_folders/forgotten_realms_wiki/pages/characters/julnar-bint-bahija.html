Julnar bint Bahija was a holy slayer and member of the Gilded Palm in 1367 DR.

Activities

Julnar was selected for a special mission and agreed to have her body permanently polymorphed to appear as a beautiful elf. She was tasked with infiltrating the Mosque of Bountiful Jisan in Huzuz and assassinating Imam Moral, who disagreed with the Gilded Palm's methods.
