Galauntar Hawkhelm was the commander of the forces at the Citadel of the Raven prior to and during the invasion of the Zhentarim. He died from wounds he recieved in a battle with a horde of orcs; his final act was successfully leading the remainder of his forces to safety from these dual dangers. the group would later form the organization known as the Knights of the North.

He originally hailed from Hillsfar before being nominated commander of the Citadel.

History

After his death, Galauntar's ghost haunted the westernmost fortress of the Citadel, Point Dusk.
