Orgelna Highshield was a famed artist of Selgaunt, considered to be the most prestigious sculptor of Sembia by some in the 15 century DR.

Description

Orgelna was a dwarvish woman with a lush beard and surly demeanor. She wore cartoonishly thick spectacles.

History

Orgelna Highshield's work was an impressive sight: a bushy-bearded dwarvish woman scampering and climbing over slabs of rock she was chiseling, often spending nights sleeping atop her sculptures. She was a favored sculptor by many Selgauntian human noble families, including Bowsuntide and Ornwindsea clans.
