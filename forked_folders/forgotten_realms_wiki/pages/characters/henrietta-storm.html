Henrietta Storm was a tiefling cobbler who lived and worked in the village of Wisteria Vale. They ran the shop Silks and Soles with their business partner Josephus Lovett.

Personality

Henrietta was an outgoing individual who loved gathering folks together for events in Wisteria Vale. They were especially fond of crafting decorations from spare fabric and other materials.

History

Some time during or around the , Henrietta's likeness was recreated as an animated, wooden mannequin known as a constructed commoner, which was then placed within the demiplane that housed the famous Harper Arrant Quill.
