Lord Petric Amber was a wood elven patriar of Baldur's Gate and a member of the Parliament of Peers in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Petric Amber was a wood elven man with sharp facial features and a skin tone tinted with floral green-brown. He had long brown hair and wore a monocle over his right eye. He preferred blue finery and wore a resplendent hat decorated with a single massive feather.

History

In the , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldur's Gate, Lord Petric Amber was a Parliamentarian of the Gate who voted in support of crowning lowborn Lord Enver Gortash as the city's Archduke. Lord Amber was present for Gortash's coronation that took place in the audience hall of Wyrm's Rock. Following the coronation, Petric stated that he would support the new Archduke as long as Gortash honored the agreement between them.
