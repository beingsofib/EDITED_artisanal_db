Lab was a she-mule and the constant companion of Saleem the architect in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Relationships

Lab and Saleem were inseparable. Saleem talked constantly with his hoofed companion while walking around the city. Though quite a few citizens of Huzuz thought Saleem was insane, some speculated that Lab was actually his polymorphed lover or even a genie disguised as a mule.

Personality

Lab was a quiet individual.
