Duncan was a human fighter who rode in the company of the paladin Gabriel (paladin).

Personality

Cousin to king Halvor II, he was never comfortable in the royal court, instead he yearned for the wilderness and life on the move. He wished he could have trained as a ranger but his noble lineage prevented this from being an option.

Possessions

He rode astride a heavy warhorse, wearing a set of enchanted chainmail and wielding a flame tongue sword. He possessed a a horn of Valhalla and some eyes of the eagle.

Relationships

He made friends with the kings champion, the paladin Gabriel, and rode along the retinue, coming as close as he was possible to the life of a ranger.
