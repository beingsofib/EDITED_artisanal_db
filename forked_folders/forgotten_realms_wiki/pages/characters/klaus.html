Klaus was the Assistant Sniffer, a gateman, employed by Lucretious, the proprietress of of the Circus of the Last Days in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Klaus was a handsome wood elf with sharp features, slightly tan skin, brown wavy hair streaked with red, and brown eyes. He wore a loud colorful performer's outfit of blues, gold, and green.

History

In the , Klaus and the Principal Sniffer ghoul Benji could be found at the gate of Lucretious's circus in Rivington of Baldur's Gate, where the extra-planar troupe performed for a short period of time, before moving on to another world. In lieu of recently surging murders in the Gate, Benji was tasked with sniffing out blood on every guest entering the circus. The ghoul was supervised by Klaus, the Assistant Sniffer, who allowed patrons through the locked gates. One of the sniffees was the commoner man named Montgomery, who brought his son to see the famed Dribbles the clown. Even though the father and the son both were clean, the boy was terrified of the blood-covered jester ghoul and refused to enter the circus.
