Snapbone was a member of The Guild and a bodyguard of its elected bursar in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Snapbone was a human man with an olive skin tone, brown hair and facial hair, and chestnut eyes. He wore a leather armor dyed in emerald-green – the Guild's telltale colors and was armed with a longbow and a dagger

History

In the , Snapbone, along with Elliot, was placed to guard the Guild's elected bursar, the masked man only known as Uktar. He continued to serve the bursar and the Guild through the Absolute crisis and the conflict with the dreaded Stone Lord, a new kingpin who secretly served the Absolute.
