Dhoran was a human fighter usually working as a bodyguard in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Dhoran worked as a bodyguard only for those of a good nature. He was honest and loyal to his paymasters. He didn't mind the long journeys that his missions often took, and whiled away the long travel time by telling his clients stories of his earlier adventures.

Possessions

He wore a padded leather armor, a pair of bracers of defense, and a ring of warmth, while wielding an enchanted bow.
