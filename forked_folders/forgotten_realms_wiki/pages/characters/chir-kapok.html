Chir Kapok was a halfling monk and trainer in Eveningstar during the 1480s DR.

Description

Very curiously for a monk, Chir wore a suit of plate mail.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Chir was in the village and offered his expertise in training prospective young monks in the finer aspects of the lifestyle.
