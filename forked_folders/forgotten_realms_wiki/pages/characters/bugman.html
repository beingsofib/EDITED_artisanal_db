Bugman

Bugman was a hedge wizard noted for his particularly horrid visage.

Personality

Though he had a loathsome appearance, Bugman's disposition was one of kindness and generosity.

History

At some point in his life Bugman created the spell Bugman's mug. He did so for the sole purpose of aiding a dwarven friend of his, one who was visiting him at the time and presumably suffering the ill affects of inebriation.
