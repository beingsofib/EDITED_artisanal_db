Maerwûn Venedel was a truenamer at the House of the Unnamed in Velprintalar in Aglarond in the late 14 century DR.

Abilities

Her truenaming abilities were full-fledged; she had access to all sorts of utterances, even recitations and those of the lexicon of the perfected map. She could also see the named, an ability through which she used an effect akin to scrying on anyone whose personal true name she knew.

Activities

As of , she led a study group on truespeak at the House of the Unnamed.
