Gullwyn was a sailing merchant-mage who lived centuries before the 13 century DR.

History

Gullwyn was the creator of the famous blade called the Dagger of Safe Harbor, an enchanted blade that protected its wielder from all harm. In his long sailor's career Gullwyn witnessed countless men being swept off the ships during deadly storms all because of a split second of fear. In the years that followed the weapon's creation, the Dagger of Safe Harbor saved Gullwyn's life on at least three occasions. At the end of his career, Gullwyn entrusted the famed dagger to his friend – Valkur of Mintarn.
