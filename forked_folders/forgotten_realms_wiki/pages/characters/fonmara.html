Fonmara was a duergar mind master and a cultist of the Absolute that dwelled in the ruins of Grymforge for a time during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

She was a distrusting individual that remained viligant against any threat that may present itself to her fellow devotess of the Absolute.

Abilities

Like other mind masters, Fonmarra possessed the psionic ability to briefly take over the mind of others and force them to attack their allies in battle.

History

Fonmarra was among the Absolute loyalists that accompanied True Soul Nere and Sergeant Thrinn on their excavation of the Sharran temple within the depths of Grymforge in the .
