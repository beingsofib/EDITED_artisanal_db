Nakata was an ancient spirit of Rashemen, a wolf telthor friend of Okku. She lived in Okku's barrow and served the God of Bears.

History

It was said that Nakata's howling lured an army of beasts from over the Tirulag, and eater of souls slew her. Soon after that she become a telthor and slept in Okku's home for many centuries.

In 1374 DR, Nakata's soul was eaten by the spirit-eater (the Kalach-Cha) and she was the first victim of their curse.
