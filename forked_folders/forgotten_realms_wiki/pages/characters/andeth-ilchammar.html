Andeth Ilchammar was the Hulorn, or ruler, of Selgaunt in 1358 DR.

Personality

Andeth was a bored playboy consider by some to be half-mad. He believed himself to be possessed of divine powers and set on Toril to do a task laid down by the gods themselves. The gods had neglected to reveal this divine task as of 1358 DR.
