Lady Laeyalane Tantorn was a young noble lady of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Laeyalane was a spirited and merry young woman.

Relationships

Laeyalane was a constant companion and lover of Lady Narnathra Barelder, along with Lady Cathone Ravenhill.
