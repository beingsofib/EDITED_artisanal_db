Balaghast Brightlingar was an innkeep inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a determined man who rarely smiled. His gruff exterior hid a kind heart as Balaghast was always willing to help someone in need.

Activities

Balaghast owned the Pilgrim's Rest located at the corner of Satchel Alley and Diamond Street in Waterdeep's Sea Ward.
