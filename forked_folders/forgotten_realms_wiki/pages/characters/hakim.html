Hakim was an efreetikin who lived in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Personality

Perhaps because of his master's numerous negative dealings with genies, Hakim was quite sullen most of the time.

Relationships

Hakim was the gen familiar of the famed sha'ir Adnan al-Raqi.

Activities

Hakim did as his master asked, which usually involved fetching spells from the various elemental planes.
