Brunnt Hallow was a reserved man from Reithwin, once a Selûnite town in the Western Heartlands, sometime before the late 14 century DR.

History

Brunnt Hallow died sometime before the fall of Reithwin and its consumption by the Shadow-cursed Lands, before the . Brunnt was buried in Reithwin's graveyard behind the House of Healing, at the hospital's northwestern walls. His tombstone read: "Here lies Brunnt Hallow, man of few words."
