Lunapine was a languid-seeming moon dog of the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

Lunapine was the closest companion of Naneatha Suaril, the high priestess of Selûne at the House of the Moon temple in Waterdeep around 1370 DR. A regular guest of hers, Lunapine stayed in the antechamber outside her quarters.
