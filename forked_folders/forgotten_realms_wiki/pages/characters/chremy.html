Chremy was a pirate lieutenant, who worked for the pirate king, Desharik, in Brynnlaw.

Description

Chremy was a rude and hardy man. He considered others his property, and did not care for them in the slightest.

History

Chremy worked for Brynnlaw's pirate king, Desharik. For his good service, he was given Ginia as a courtesan, for himself and his fellow pirates. He was a particularly cruel master of Lady Galvena's Festhall, and wore the festhall's medallion for identification.
