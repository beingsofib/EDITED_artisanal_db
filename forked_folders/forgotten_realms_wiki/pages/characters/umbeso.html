Umbeso was one of the arcanists from Estagund that accompanied Aniolon Gruanthe in his exodus on the .

Description

Umbeso had graying hair.

Personality

Umbeso proved to be quite curious, to the point of approaching a dragon and being cautious of their gifts. He was also very decisive.

Relationships

He had once been one of Aniolon Gruanthe's rivals in Estagund.

History

He joined Aniolon's exodus to found the town of Lliiress, looking for a respite from the supremacy of its warrior nobility. When Aniolon Gruanthe secured the help of Gerinvioch, and the dragon in turn employed the Evise Jhontil to create a barrier, Umbeso showed much curiosity about the dragon's exact doings.

The day after that, Umbeso was the first one to publicly figure out the connection between the artifact and the deaths of all who cast a spell in Lliiress. He was quick to propose leaving as a solution, before being informed that there was a barrier; he soon after volunteered to try a wand or other device, employing a brooch of dispelling to try and disable the Evise Jhontil.

Umbeso later witnessed Sohj's entrapment in a blinking state by the artifact corrupting his teleport spell, and Aniolon Gruanthe's final death, after the mage decided to scry on Gerinvioch.
