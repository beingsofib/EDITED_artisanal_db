Url was a dwarven blacksmith and merchant in Mantol-Derith.

Activities

Url usually sold weapons.

Personality

Url disliked drow and duergar. He deeply regretted how his life was ruined by the evil Red Wizard.

History

Around 1479 DR, Url and his lover were captured by a Red Wizard of Thay. With the life of his lover in the wizard's hands, the wizard commanded Url to go to Mantol-Derith to sell weapons and collect information.
