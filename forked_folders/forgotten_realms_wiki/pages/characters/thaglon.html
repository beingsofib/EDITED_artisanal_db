Thaglon was a trader in Waterdeep as of the mid-14 century DR.

Activities

He was a trader.

History

In late , Mirt related unto Asper that Thaglon traded Amn-work of steel and silver, presumably some sort of jewelry or furniture, for half of what he should've asked because of "starvation down there", presumably implying a famine in Amn. Mirt added he was able to unload two warehouses' worth of rotting nut-marrows.
