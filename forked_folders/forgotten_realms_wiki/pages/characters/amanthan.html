Amanthan was a human wizard who resided in Arabel, Cormyr, in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Amanthan was described as a tall, young mage.

Personality

Amanthan was known to be reclusive, preferring to remain within his walled mansion in Arabel, away from the city's bustle. He was protective of his property and privacy, and was not afraid to defend himself with his magic.

According to Khelben Arunsun, Amanthan was "a good man" and "one of his more promising apprentices." The Blackstaff described him as being "too shy and kind to ever be a leader, or have much to do with power or politics." He was content to keep "behind his high walls and work on spells for as long as Faerûn lets him."

Residence

Amanthan lived in a walled mansion in Arabel, complete with gardens, fishponds with statues, and an ivy-covered wall that separated his property from neighboring estates.

History

Amanthan was once an apprentice of Khelben Arunsun, the Blackstaff of Waterdeep, and was considered one of his more promising students.

At some point, he relocated to Arabel in Cormyr, where he established himself as a reclusive wizard.

In the , Amanthan's home was used as a temporary refuge by the Swords of Eveningstar (later known as the Knights of Myth Drannor), who were fleeing from Purple Dragons pursuing them through the city. He allowed them to pass through his property and use a magical portal on his grounds that led to Waterdeep.

Following this encounter, Amanthan was temporarily possessed by the entity known as Old Ghost.

Relationships

Amanthan maintained a connection with his former mentor, Khelben, who spoke well of him. Through this connection, he was involved in some of Khelben's plans regarding the Swords of Eveningstar.
