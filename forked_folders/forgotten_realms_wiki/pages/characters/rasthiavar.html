Rasthiavar of Iriaebor was a sage from Iriaebor, and the author of A Wayfarer's Belt-Book of Advice in the late 13 century DR.

History

In the late 13 century DR, he wrote A Wayfarer's Belt-Book of Advice, which included a small amount of information on balhiir.
