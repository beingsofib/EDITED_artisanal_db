Marane was an exiled archdevil in the mid-late 14 century DR.

Personality

She affected to be almost unthinkingly hungry.

Appearance

She had ruby skin, horse heads for breasts, long nails, like talons, cloven feet, a tail, and wings.

Abilities

During her fight with Elminster Aumar, she only ever acted physically.

Possessions

In her cave, she had stored a number of enchanted items including a wand of lightning, and a rod created by Telamont Tanthul.

History

While Elminster, trapped in Avernus, found a brief respite from Nergal in , he was attacked by Marane, who intended to eat him, explaining she was hungry enough to disregard Nergal's claim over him. Elminster fled into her cave, where he found some magic items; he used them, and his own silver fire, to slay her and prepare an ambush for Nergal afterwards.
