He was a swordsman and guard.

Relationships

He was under the command of Sarltan.

History

In , Dove Falconhand investigated a drow infiltration of Scornubel while disguising herself as the drow Iylinvyx. She ultimately uncovered Sarltan as the man behind it all; when confronted directly, Sarltan called on Velrult, among other soldiers, to attack her. She slew most of them.
