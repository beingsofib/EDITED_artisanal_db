Misha was a vampire spawn Gur child from the city of Baldur's Gate during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Misha was a human boy with a somber facial expression and a scar on his cheek.

History

In the , Ulma led her monster hunter tribe to the city of Baldur's Gate, where they found themselves targeted by vampires. During the Absolute crisis, when the Gurs camped in the Rivington district of the city, along with a small army of refugees displaced by the conflict, the tribe's children were abducted by Astarion Ancunín under orders from Cazador Szarr.

Misha was the fourth child abducted, and posters with charcoal drawings of his somber face were found in the Lower City, promising a reward for any information on the child's fate. Misha, along with other abducted Gur children, was turned into a vampire spawn to be used in a hellish ritual by Cazador. Subsequently, Astarion Ancunín found himself broken out from under Cazador's control and, along with the Absolute Adversaries, sought to infiltrate his old master's home. The outcome of Astarion's infiltration and the fate of the Gur children remained unconfirmed.
