Markos Silverhammer was a dwarf who lived in the Kingdom of Siremun in the Hordelands in 1367 DR.

History

Markos was the king of Siremun in 1367 DR.

Relationships

Markos was a direct descendant of the dwarf who initially discovered the Firepeaks.
