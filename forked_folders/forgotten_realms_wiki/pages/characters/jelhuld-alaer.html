Jelhuld Alaer was a retired warrior and sailmaker inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Jelhuld was the main point of contact for the Most Diligent League of Sail-Makers and Cordwainers during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He worked out of Full Sails–their headquarters along the Dock Street in the Dock Ward where he was the tavernmaster.
