Violir el Cajaan IX was the last syl-pasha of the Cajaan Dynasty of the Third Age of Calimshan.

History

Violir IX was known for the last stand he took against the attacking beholder army of the Alimir Hive in . Believing in the power of his god, the One Sun, to protect his empire, he was turned into stone by the beholders as he stood on the steps of the temple in Calimport, ending his family's rule.
