Bert "The Weasel" Adams was a red cap boatswain of the Blushing Mermaid in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , during the time Captain Grisly was replaced by the green hag Auntie Ethel as the owner of Blushing Mermaid, Bert resided at the Mermaid, alongside other hag's crewmen, spending their days drinking and partying. Bert was masquerading as a human sailor, keeping his fey nature a secret. It was unclear if the Bert "The Weasel" Adams identity was stolen from a slain sailor or was a fabrication.
