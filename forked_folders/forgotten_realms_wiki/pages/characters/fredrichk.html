Fredrichk was a Sentry in the hidden Cloister of Sombre Embrace in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Fredrichk was a full-faced hin with short curly brown hair. He wore a ceremonial set of Sharran medium armor adorned with the dark goddesses' holy symbols. Fredrichk carried a Sharran scimitar and defended himself with a large shield.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Fredrichk served the Mother Superior as one of her sentries underneath the House of Grief in the Cloister of Sombre Embrace. Fredrichk, along with the entirety of the cloister may have confronted its member Shadowheart were she to turn away from Shar and possibly perish in the struggle. By other accounts, Fredrichk and the rest of the cloister helped defend the city of Baldur's Gate from the army of the Absolute, were Shadowheart to remain loyal to her deity, or if Shadowheart was betrayed and surrendered to the Mother Superior for punishment.
