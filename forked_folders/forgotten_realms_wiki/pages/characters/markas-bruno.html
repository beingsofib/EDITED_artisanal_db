Markas Bruno was a suspected leader of the Black Talon gang of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR.

History

In the late 14 century DR, Markas Bruno was among the suspected leaders and lieutenants of thieves' guilds, and gangs found dead in the Crow's End of Ravens Bluff. All were killed during the course of a single tenday. Markas's cause of death was a single dagger in his back.

The occurrence was reported in the Trumpeter chapbook, claiming that Markas Bruno's death was a result of the localized guild war.
