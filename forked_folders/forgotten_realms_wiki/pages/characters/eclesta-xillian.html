Eclesta Xillian was a diviner and scholar of the Twelve in the world of Eberron.

Description

Eclesta had dark brown skin and short black hair. She spoke in a halting, dreamy fashion.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, Eclesta came to investigate the Road to Tarath Marad in Stormreach as the Spinner of Shadows attempted to escape her prison, working alongside Gatekeeper Grazla. Eclesta represented the Twelve as they investigated Lolth's activities, particularly her home in the Demonweb Pits, as well as concurrent events involving the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll.
