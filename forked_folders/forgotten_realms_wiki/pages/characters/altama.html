Altama was a criminal in Westgate around 1374 DR.

History

Altama was the public leader of the Ebon Claws crime organization (the true leader being the vampire Tasheni. Altama was at war with the Night Masks.

Around 1374 DR, Altama helped an adventurer in their fight with the Night Masks. At last, thanks to the adventurer, Altama managed to enter with some minions into the Lair of the Night Masters in order to kill the vampire leaders of the Night Masks. However, Altama found Orbakh ready to fight and in the battle he killed Altama.
