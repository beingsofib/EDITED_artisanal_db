Melinde Agister was an Amaunatori priest, who, for a time, operated out of the Lonesome Tankard Inn and Tavern in Eveningstar in the 15 century DR.

Description

Melinde was a human woman with light skin and red braided hair. She wore a set of Amaunatori chain armor, decorated with the deity's holy symbol.

History

In the 15 century DR during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Melinde Agister stayed at the Lonesome Tankard in Eveningstar, offering her divine healing and restoration to those who needed it.
