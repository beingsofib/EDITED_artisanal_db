Gabe was a commoner son of Emmet from the Rivington district of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Gabe was a human child with raven-black hair and brown eyes.

History

In the , Emmet and his son set out to watch a performance by a beloved clown – Dribbles, of the Circus of the Last Days. Due to the recently skyrocketed crime and unrest, the circus gates were guarded by wood elf Klaus and his trained ghoul partner Benji, who sniffed out blood on the hands of the guests. Gabe, however, was afraid of the undead monster and hesitated to enter.
