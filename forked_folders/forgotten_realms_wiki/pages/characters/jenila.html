Jenila was a noble marid who inhabited Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Activities

Jenila spent a good deal of time within the White Agate's Tower, associating with the White Agate and helping him guard his possessions.

Relationships

She enjoyed spending time with the White Agate because she believed him to be one of the few humans who approached her level of intelligence. They were close friends because of this.

Rumors and Legends

It was widely rumored among the students of the tower that the White Agate and Jenila were lovers.
