Darion Sulmest was a cobbler inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Darion was the main point of contact for the Order of Cobblers & Corvisers circa the . He held the title of "Spokesman of the Order" and worked out of both the Cobblers' & Corvisers' House, which was their headquarters along Soothsayer's Way in the Trades Ward
