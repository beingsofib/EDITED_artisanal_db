Zomith was a half-orc caravan guard during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Activities

Zomith worked as a caravan guard.

History

In 1491 DR, Zomith was one of the guards of a diplomatic caravan from Mirabar passing through the Dessarin Valley. However, she quit the caravan at Beliard and went alone to Red Larch. There, at the Helm at Highsun tavern, Zomith discovered that the caravan had since gone missing, but did not care.
