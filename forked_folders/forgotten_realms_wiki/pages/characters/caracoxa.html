Caracoxa was an ettercap that laired in the basement of the Irithlium of Myth Drannor in the late 14 century DR.

History

The spiderkin lurked in the cellars of the magic school while the Red Wizards of Thay set up base in the ruins around it. It may have been caught in the fighting between the Thayans and the Cormanthor Elves during the Elven Crusade in 1374 DR.
