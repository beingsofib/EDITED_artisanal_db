Boris Elberwelter was a human fighter and the proprietor of the Sign of Thunder inn of Thunderstone in the late 1360s DR. He was aided in its operatation by his wife Tasha Elberwelter.

In the , he overheard the Wolfmasters adventuring company discussing fresh rumors of a great ruin in the Vast Swamp.
