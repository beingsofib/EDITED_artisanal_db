Wadi'a bint Sali was an gnome sha'ir within the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Activities

Wadi'a was an assistant of the Grand Vizier, Alyana al-Azzazi.

Relationships

Her familiar was a fire gen named Ladok.
