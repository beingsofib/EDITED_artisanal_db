Thulla was a gnome of Clan Ironhand who lived in the city of Baldur's Gate during the late 15 century DR. She was part of an unfortunate excursion to the Underdark that led to much of her clan being killed or captured by the Cult of the Absolute.

Description

Thulla had gray skin and vivid white hair. She wore a leather jerkin over a green shirt and leather pants.

Personality

She was an intrepid if a bit secretive individual. The activities she and her clan conducted were held in some secrecy.

History

In the , Thulla and the other deep gnomes of Clan Ironhand journeyed down to the Underdark from Baldur's Gate, on mission to uncover the mythical substance known as runepowder. While searching for the long-lost site known as Grymforge, they were attacked by cultists of the Absolute led by Gekh Coal and other duergar slavers. While most of the gnomes were slain or taken captive by the cult, Thulla managed to get away with mere wounds. Unfortunately, the blade that cut her was laced with duergar poison.

Possessions

At some point during or before their encounter, Thulla managed to steal Gekh's pair of boots of speed.
