Narve Dwarfkin () was a fighter of great renown in the Dragon Reach during the mid-14 century DR.

History

In the end months of the year 1357 DR, Narve and Ryhinn Blackcloak assembled a band of 20 mercenaries and assembled in the city of Selgaunt. They set out to explore the ruins of ancient Myth Drannor for themselves.
