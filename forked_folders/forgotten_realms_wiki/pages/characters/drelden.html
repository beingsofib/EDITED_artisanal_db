Drelden was an innkeeper in the 3 century DR.

Activities

As an innkeeper, Drelden threw out everyone who drew steel in his home.

History

In , Drelden was the innkeeper of the Herald's Horn near Cormyr. He had built the place himself, per his reckoning.
