Shulla Barandai was a retired adventurer who worked at the Dryad's Dreams of Mosstone in Tethyr.

Description

Shulla had a slender body, the product of her adventuring career and acrobatic talents. She was said to be able to avoid a volley of elven arrows unscathed.

History

Shulla Barandai was one of the female escorts who worked at Dryad's Dreams in the late mid-to-late 14 century DR after retiring from adventuring, along with several men and women who offered their services and companionship at the only festhall in Mosstone.
