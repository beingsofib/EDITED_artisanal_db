Pimulpekk was the High Priest of Kurtulmak from the kobold tribe led by Chief Crajbek, who laired in the infamous Haunted Halls near the village of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the 15 century DR.

Abilities

Pimulpekk was a powerful cleric of the kobold patron deity who could sling such spells as unholy blight, greater command, divine punishment, poison, and fire storm''.

Activities

In the 15 century DR, Pimulpekk led a band of priests of Kurtulmak as a part of Chief Crajbek. When a group of adventurers who fell through a rift to Toril from the world of Eberron investigated the haunted depths of the dungeon, they faced High Priest Pimulpekk and Crajbek, possibly slaying both and claiming the kobold tribe's looted treasures.
