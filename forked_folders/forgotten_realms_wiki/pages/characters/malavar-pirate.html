Malavar (pirate)

Person
nameMalavar
homeShoon Imperium, Southwest Faerûn

Malavar was a notorious pirate and slaver of the Shoon Imperium and a known early member of the Rundeen mercantile consortium some time in the 1 to 4 centuries DR.

On the Feast of the Moon in the , while on their way to Malavar's Grasp, Gamalon Idogyr told Raegar Stoneblade of three historical figures named Malavar associated with Shoon, whom Priamon Rakesk might've researched: the most recent a trader of Athalantar; the pirate and slaver of the Rundeen; and the oldest a sorcerer, Malavar of the Three Hands, the legendary creator of the place they were going.
