Burlan Almaether was the acting High Priest of the Temple of Gond Wondermaker in Tilverton in the 1350s–1360s DR.

Activities

Burlan was the leader of Gond's faithful in Tilverton after the disappearance of Gharri in 1357 DR – the temple's previous High Priest. His congregation within the temple consisted of 26 other priests and 39 followers, and he took the role of acting High Priest until Gond delivered a divine sign to officially assign the replacement.
