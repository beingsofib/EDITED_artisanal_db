Rautheene was a human wizard from Oerth and an apprentice of Mordenkainen in the 14 century DR.

Description

Rautheene was slender with long black hair and considered stunningly beautiful. She was "half a head taller" than both Elminster and Mordenkainen, and Ed Greenwood described her laugh as "the merriest tinkling laughter [he'd] ever heard."

Personality

Rautheene was very praiseful of Mordenkainen, insisting that she would call him the greatest archmage of Oerth. She had a tendency to a sarcastic tongue, which tended to not play out well when amongst powerful archmages. She enjoyed many foods of Earth; one of her favorite meals was smoked salmon covered in praline'n'cream ice cream.

Relationships

Rautheene was the apprentice of Mordenkainen. She frequently accompanied him on trips to Earth, and took the place of Dalamar of Krynn in the meetings of the Wizards Three, meeting with Elminster in Ed Greenwood's house.

History

Not much is known of Rautheene's history, except for her apprenticeship to Mordenkainen. She traveled back and forth frequently to House Greenwood to meet with Mordenkainen and Elminster to discuss spells.
