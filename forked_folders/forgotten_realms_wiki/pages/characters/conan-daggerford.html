Duke Conan Daggerford was the ruler and head–of–state of Daggerford during the early 14 century DR.

Possessions

Like the other dukes of Daggerford, Conan wielded the duchy's sword of state, Lawflame.

Relationships

He had a son Pryden who went on to succeed him as duke, and another son Llewellyn, who was born out of marriage. Duke Conan eventually granted his illegitimate son a knighthood.

History

Duke Conan was perhaps most well known for granting Daggerford its own sovereign authority. He formed the Council of Guilds and empowered them to run the town's day–to–day operations.
