Saulter was a human inhabitant of Neverwinter's Peninsula District in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Saulter was a rotund man of middle age with a light skin tone and a bushy brown beard.

History

During the time the Wailing Death ravaged Neverwinter in the , Saulter's home district, Peninsula, was not only hit by the plague, but also by a riot in the district's prison, spilling its inmates out into the streets. Saulter managed to evacuate from the district alongside his wife Maureen, before it was quarantined off by the Neverwintran militia but not before he lost his family to the plague. Now homeless, Saulter had to sleep on city streets by a beggars' campfire in the City Core, where he mourned the loss of his old home and the fact that he couldn't mourn the dead in the comfort of his own home.
