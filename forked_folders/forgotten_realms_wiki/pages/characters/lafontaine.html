LaFontaine was a pious adventuring human, active in the late 14 century DR.

Description

LaFontaine was a tall and muscular human man with a light skin tone and flowing long hair. His armor of choice was scale armor.

History

In Kythorn of the , Waterdeep – the City of Splendors, became once again menaced by the drow from the mega-dungeon of Undermountain. The Masked Lords determined that the danger was coming from underneath the Yawning Portal, and Durnan, the proprietor of the famed establishment, put out a call for adventurers willing to traverse the dungeon's depths and route the evil within.

LaFontaine was among the heroes who answered the call, eager to spread the faith of his goddess and to practice his healing arts. LaFontaine was present at the Yawning Portal's common room, when a squadron of drow assassins sent by "queen" Valsharess attacked. The dark elves slaughtered many of the gathered adventurers. Survivors chased the assassins down into the Undermountain, while those killed were resurrected by the city's clergy, including LaFontaine. LaFontaine thought his skills would be of more use helping hurt and the dead and refused to venture into the Undermountain, leaving the quest in the hands of the hero who would be known as the Slayer of Mephistopheles.
