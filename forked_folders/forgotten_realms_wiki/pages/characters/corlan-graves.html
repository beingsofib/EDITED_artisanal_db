Corlan Graves was a well-to-do man who lived in Helm's Hold during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

He had a brother named Aldwin, who served on the council of Helm's Hold.

History

Corlan lived in Helm's Hold when it was overrun by the Ashmadai and devil allies in the . He tried to warn his brother about leaving the fortress-town for safety, but Aldwin would not listen. After escaping the hold himself, Corlan enlisted the Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge to check on Aldwin and ensure his safety.
