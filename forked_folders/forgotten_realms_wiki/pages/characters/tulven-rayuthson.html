Tulven Rayuthson was a Warlord of Silverymoon during the 10 century DR.

Personality

He was said to be proud and sadistic.

History

Tulven became Warlord in the , upon the death of his father, Warlord Rayuth. He was a sufficiently despicable ruler that the likes of War Captain Aeroth, a hero and champion of the city, left Silverymoon to live elsewhere.

Because of his abuses of power, in the , Tulven was stripped of his position by the High Mage of Silverymoon, Nunivytt Threskaal. In order to prevent a similar incident from occurring, Nunivytt declared that the position of Warlord would only be granted during times of great need.
