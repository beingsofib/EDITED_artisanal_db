Malutt Mauksoun was a retired warrior inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Malutt was the guildmaster for the Guild of Fine Carvers during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He held the title of "First Master Carver" within the guild and worked out of the House of Fine Carvers–their headquarters along the High Road in the Castle Ward.
