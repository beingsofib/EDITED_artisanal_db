Kregos Belizzian was a Red Wizard of Thay who led an outpost in Thesk in the years before , feeding information to the Grand Army of the Tuigan before their invasion of Faerûn.

Personality

He had loyalty only to himself and would work for whomever he felt he needed to in order to secure his own survival.

Abilities

He had learned some of the strange magic of the east.

Possessions

He owned an enchanted +1 dagger and several of eight diagram coins.

Activities

In the time up to Yamun Khahan's invasion of Thesk, he would send information to the Tuigan and recruit various humanoids for his own use once the Tuigan invasion arrived.

History

Kregos was once a member of a wizard circle in Thay. At some point, he was sent to Semphar on an espionage mission to gather intel on the Tiguan but was captured by the selfsame and forced at swordpoint to swear allegiance to Yamun Khahan. Afterward, he was released and went back to Thay, whereupon he managed to wrangle a secondment to an outpost in Thesk.
