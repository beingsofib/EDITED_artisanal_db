Kamon was a short and stocky human mercenary who was killed in a fight with Abdel Adrian in 1368 DR. Kamon had previously worked with Abdel in 1367 DR, guarding a warehouse in Athkatla. Kamon had blue eyes and liked to wield two axes simultaneously.

Death

In combat with Abdel Adrian in 1368 DR, Kamon had been dispossessed of one of his two axes, and swung low at Abdel with his other axe. Abdel dodged a blow from the halberd of Eagus, which struck Kamon on the head, killing him.
