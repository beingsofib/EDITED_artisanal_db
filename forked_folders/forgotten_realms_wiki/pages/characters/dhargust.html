Dhargust was a lecturer in the late 15 century DR.

Description

He had been characterized as "old".

Activities

He gave lectures, some of which were attended by castle guards.

History

In a lecture two summers prior to late Mirtul of , Dhargust lectured on beholderkin, mentioning there were a few beholders yet dwelling in Hullack Forest, waiting for a chance to seize power somewhere in Cormyr. At least one guard in Castle Obarskyr two years later had attended the lecture.
