Hlonsker Belark was a member of the Belark merchant family in Delzimmer circa the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR. His father Oldyle cast such a long shadow that not much was known about Hlonsker except that he was the eldest son and would someday take over the family business. He was much more disciplined than his two younger brothers and often more rigid in this dealings than even his father.
