Dunbold Laraeikan was a retired warrior inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Dunbold was the guildmaster for the Guild of Trusted Pewterers and Casters during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He held the title of "High Artisan" within the guild and worked out of the Pewterers' and Casters' Guildhall–their headquarters in the Castle Ward.
