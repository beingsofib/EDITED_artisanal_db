Patriarch Thyrius Jorman was the head priest of the temple of Oghma in Reddansyr during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Unlike some other Oghmite priests, Patriarch Thyrius was stringent with the knowledge his clergy bestowed upon to others.

Relationships

He was aided in his priestly duties by a group of 14 assistants.
