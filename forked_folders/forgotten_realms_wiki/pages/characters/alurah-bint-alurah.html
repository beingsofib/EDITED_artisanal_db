Alurah bint Alurah was an accomplished sha'ir and princess of Hiyal around 1367 DR.

Personality

Alurah exemplified all of the traits one would expect from spoiled royalty. She was greedy, malicious, and dismissed others "beneath" her status. She held no qualms about attacking an innocent in order to cover her own wrongdoings.

Relationships

Alurah was the daughter of Sultana Alurah bint Asrah and the youngest of all of her siblings. She would turn family members against each other to suit her purposes. Her gen familiar was a djinnling named Pin.
