Voltinabulai, who also went by Voltine, was a druid that lived in Baldur's Gate during the mid–14 century DR.

Abilities

As a shapeshifter druid, Voltine was afflicted with lycanthropy but had complete control over her transformation.

Possessions

She carried on her person a wand of polymorphing.

History

In the , Voltine caught a mage by the name of Gervisse lurking around a building in the Upper City of Baldur's Gate, spying on two twin children, Laerta and Louise. She would transform herself in order to check in on the children and protect them from the old spellcaster.
