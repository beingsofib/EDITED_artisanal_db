Ghaerlin was a guard in Langolos on the Dragon Coast in the mid–8 century DR.

Activities

He had been a horse tamer before becoming a guard in service to High Duke Horostos of Langolos.

History

After winter of the , after the Frostfire Banner was sent to the Dead Place near Starmantle, one of their horses washed up on the coast. Ghaerlin, one of the guards, examined it, concluding it had died from a fall, probably in a panic.
