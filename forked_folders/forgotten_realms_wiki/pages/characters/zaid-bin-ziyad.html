Zaid bin Ziyad was a cook in Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Description

Zaid was a large man.

Personality

Zaid had a friendly personality and loved to ask food-related questions of travelers.

Activities

Zaid was a professonial cook. He sometimes accompanied Bahramiyah al-Musafir when her caravans traveled from Hiyal to Qudra.
