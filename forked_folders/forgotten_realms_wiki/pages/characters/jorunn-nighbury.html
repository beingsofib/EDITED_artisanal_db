Jorunn Nightbury was a rather shy horse breeder that lived and worked in the city of Baldur's Gate during the late 15 century DR. He was employed by the Belt patriar family, whose wealth was attributed to the sale of quality steeds.

By the , Jorunn had accumulated a great amount of debt through excessing gambling.
