Chen Ching Ch'ien was a river spirit folk kensai in Cham Fau in Shou Lung in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Aside from her kensai practices with fellow student Ju Huo Wang at the Wang Ho School, Cheng Ching every morning swam and meditated at the Hungste River, a place that she had ties to as a spirit.

Personality

Chen Ching was very quiet and focused, a trait that helped her channeling her ki.

Relationship

It was rumored that Ju Huo loved Chen Ching Ch'ien, but she was turned off by his temper.
