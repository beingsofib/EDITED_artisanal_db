Dundold Buldharroaz was a baker inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Dundold was the guildmaster for the Baker's Guild circa 1357 DR. He worked out of the Master Bakers' Hall–their headquarters on the Street of the Sword in the Castle Ward.
