Lord Ulbrec Dinnesmore was a man from Neverwinter who became the lord of Targos in Icewind Dale in the early 14 century DR.

Personality

Ulbrec was known for his short temper.

History

Ulbrec was a Neverwintan guard who met Elytharra when he was about 20 years old. They married and, when he retired, the couple moved to Targos, where he took a seat on the ruling council of the Ten Towns. He received the title "Lord" in the .

He was lord of Targos through its assault by goblins of the Legion of the Chimera in the .
