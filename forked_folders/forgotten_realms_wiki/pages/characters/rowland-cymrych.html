Rowland Cymrych, known as "the Boy King" was High King of the Moonshae Isles for a brief time in the mid–7 century DR.

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the son of his predecessor, High King Alec I. His successor was his uncle, Samuel.

History

Rowland assumed the throne of the High King in the , following the death of his father. He was fourteen years old.
