Bilaerus Thongolir II was a member of the Thongolir noble family of Waterdeep in the mid–14 century DR.

Known as the most prominent member of the family by 1357 DR, he was the family patriarch by 1368 DR and through 1372 DR.

His consort was Eva Thongolir by 1368 DR and his eldest son and heir was Dolerphus Thongolir IV.
