Cespenar

Person
nameCespenar
raceImp
occupationButler

Cespenar was an imp who used to serve Bhaal as a "butler". He appeared to have vanished from existence with that deity's death, but reappeared in the Pocket Plane, which Gorion's Ward and party unconsciously created to protect themselves within Bhaal's domain.

Cespenar was able to upgrade and create various enchanted items from the right ingredients. Some of the items Cespenar could improve included:

  • Angurvadal
  • Carsomyr
  • Firetooth
  • Flail of Ages
  • Ixil's Nail
  • Keryvian (Foebane)
  • The Ravager
