Dolthin was a Shadovar soldier turned mercenary archer, who ended up working for the mercenary leader Dom in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Dolthin was pale, heavily scarred, and had a shaven head. He wore a chain shirt and carried a bow.

Relationships

Dolthin's ties and loyalties to Dom were inscrutable from the outside of their group.

History

Dolthin originally served as a soldier in the army of Risen Netheril, which came to an end when he was slain during a disastrous battle. Through mysterious means he was resurrected and came to serve the infamous Dom as a mercenary. Coming full circle, they eventually took a job from Netheril to seize a piece of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll from the Harpers as Bensen Virandson moved it down Greenweald Lane. Working alongside agents from Netheril and a small army of other mercenaries, Dolthin took up a sniping position above the Lane, only to be slain again in the ensuing battle.
