Davyrra was a Rashemaar artist inhabiting Immilmar in Rashemen in the .

Description

She was 50 years old in 1367 DR.

Activities

Davyrra served as the guildmaster of the Carvers' Hall. She enjoyed making beautiful wood and stone carvings.

History

She was a skilled hunter and warrior during her youth.
