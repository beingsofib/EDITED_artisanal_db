Dulahey was a sailor on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR.

History

Par asked of Kalach-Cha to investigate the trouble in his house. The Kalach-Cha would find Dulahey arguing with his sailors, who were robbing the place, then attack. Once Dulahey and the pirates are defeated, Par would reward the Kalach-Cha with some potions.
