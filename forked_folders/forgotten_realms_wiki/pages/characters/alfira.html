Alfira was a tiefling bard who lived among a group of her kin that was exiled from Elturel in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Despite her talent with music, Alfira could be discouraged quite readily.

Abilities

She was a very talented songwriter and singer.

Relationships

While her teacher Lihala was rather hard on her, she was the person who gifted Alfira her lute.

History

In the , Lihala was killed by a pack of gnolls while playing music. Alfira stopped playing music for some time, until she began composition of the Weeping Dawn, dedicated to Lihala's memory.
