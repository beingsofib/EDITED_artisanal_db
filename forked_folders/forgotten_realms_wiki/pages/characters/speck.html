Speck was a half-orc Chosen.

Activities

Speck and his fellow Hamdir acted as guards for Oota in a secret shadovar village for captured Chosen.

History

In 1486 DR, Farideh under the orders of Adolican Rhand, identified Speck as a Chosen and he was taken away by the shadovar.
