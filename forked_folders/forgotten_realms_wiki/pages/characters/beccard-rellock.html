Beccard Rellock was a caravaneer somewhere in the Savage Frontier.

Personality

Beccard was often shortsighted despite his scheming mind. He loved his son.

Activities

Beccard spent most of his time on the road, but returned once a year to a remote logging camp somewhere between the Sword Coast to the west, and the Anauroch to the east.

Relationships

The Harpers considered Beccard to aspire to their status. He had a young son, Nollo Rellock.

History

A caravaneer most of the year, Beccard Rellock had been stopping at a logging camp somewhere between Anauroch and the Sword Coast for years, where he made some exhibits. In , that exhibit had been a "beholder", which was actually made of a sack of potatoes, while in , he'd exhibited a drow who was actually the daughter of a local chimney sweeper, still black with dirt, per the recollection of a local kid. According to his son, the lack of interest in his exhibits frustrated him to no end.

In , Beccard Rellock somehow acquired a brass dragon wyrmling, which he hid in a barn at his logging camp, planning on selling or exhibiting it somewhere, for money. However, the brass dragon attracted the attention of a larger, older, hostile blue dragon, as well as the wyrmling's mother. The blue dragon attacked the lumber camp where Beccard was; it fled after finding the wyrmling in the barn, where it met with the brass dragon's mother. Beccard was reprimanded by the Harpers for this.

The chase between the dragons extended from that barn to a wooden mill; he found his son Nollo under a collapsed wagon in the barn where the dragons' chase started. Despite the damage, his son was quite chipper about having been able to exhibit the dragon to his friends for three copper pieces, one of which was for touching the dragon.

Reputation

One of the local kids thought of Beccard as a liar whose exhibits were underwhelming and invariably fake.
