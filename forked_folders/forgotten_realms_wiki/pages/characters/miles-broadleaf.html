Sir Miles Broadleaf was a knight of Silverymoon who retired to the island of Snowdown in the Moonshae Isles during the first half of the 14 century DR.

Personality

Sir Miles's passion was hunting. He enjoyed the rugged landscape of Snowdown, but he also valued comfort.

History

Originally from the North, Miles was knighted by Alustriel Silverhand of Silverymoon and retired soon after. He relocated to the Moonshae Isles where he constructed a hunting lodge for himself and his guests known as Broadleaf Lodge. He filled the lodge with comfortable furniture
