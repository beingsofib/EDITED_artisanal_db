Mizreal Everdark was a succubus from the Nine Hells that aligned herself with the Ashmadai in Helm's Hold during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

The succubus was guarded by a pair of blazing skeletons.

History

Mizreal was slain in Faerûn during the , by the Heroes of the Sleeping Dragon Bridge, when they ventured beneath the Cathedral of Helm to complete route the devil-worshiping Ashmadai cultists from the city.
