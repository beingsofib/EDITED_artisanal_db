Olgala Zug

Person
nameOlgala Zug
homeWeb Mountains, Utter East

Olgala Zug was a bloodforge mage in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

Olgala Zug brazenly laid claim to the pass in the Web Mountains and extracted a toll from travelers. However, the leader of the Legendary Campaign elected to fight a bloodforge battle against Zug instead.
