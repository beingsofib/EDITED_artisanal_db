Thalaman Harthgroth was the grizzled leader of the nomadic Harthgroth tribe and head of the tribal council that ruled over the land of Narfell.

Personality

He viewed outsiders amiably, preferring to trade with them rather than pursuing endless fights. As such, his influence led the Harthgroth tribe to be far less hostile then the neighboring tribes.
