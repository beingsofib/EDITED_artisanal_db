Miyandra was a "Harper" agent who worked for a man named Teygan against the Zhentarim's grab for power on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Miyandra was a busy and professional brown-haired elven-looking woman. She wore colorful purple clothes.

History

Miyandra and Beowen both used a false Harper identity when they accompanied Teygan, or the infamous Sememmon, the enemy of Fzoul Chembryl of the Zhentarim, to a cave base outside the city of Daggerford. The three briefly aided the Hero of Daggerford in discovering the Black Network's plans for Daggerford. Once "Teygan" delivered invaluable hints and advice to the Hero, all three "Harpers" abandoned the cave base, happy with their involvement in the foiling of Fzoul's plans.
