Garmos Saernclaws was one of the most respected servants of Malar and was responsible for the gospel of Saernclaws, a holy gospel whose words applied to all priests of Malar.

History

Garmos lived and preached circa the 4 century DR. He taught that the church of Malar was to keep prey populations free of disease

His gospel was known and considered binding by the Malarite faithful of Faerûn as of the mid–14 century DR, and only rare learned individuals outside of the faith knew of his words. By the late 15 century DR, the meanings of some of his ancient teachings had come to be disputed between factions of the Black Blood tribe of the Moonshae Isles.
