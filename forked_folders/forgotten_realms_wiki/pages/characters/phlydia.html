Phlydia was a resident of Candlekeep during the mid-14 century DR. She was somewhat absent-minded and couldn't stand being cooped up in the library for too long.

Relationships

Phlydia was friends with the sage Gorion and was quite fond of his adopted child.

Possessions

Phlydia wore a simple gold necklace and was adorned in a mage robe of fire resistance. She was armed with a mundane dagger.

History

In 1368 DR, Phlydia inattentively misplaced her copy of the History of Halruaa and was offering her gratitude and a lynx eye as the reward for the tome's return.
