Marikoth Korianthil was a knight of House Korianthil in Evermeet in 1367 DR.

History

Marikoth had fought in all the elven wars on Faerûn for centuries before deciding to settle in Evermeet. There, he became the informal second-in-command of the Evermeet army, after Keryth Blackhelm.

Personality

Marikoth was a quiet individual with an expression of continual wariness.
