Warlock was a large, strong moorhound owned by Angus Blackstone.

History

In the , Currag Blackstone set Warlock and the pack of moorhounds on an old man who had approached the gate of the manor. With a spoken command, the man was able to stop the pack of moorhounds and with another, they fled, inexplicably jumping a tall palisade.
