Futgar Martovingian was a blacksmith from the city of Liara in Nova Vaasa, one of the Domains of Dread.

Activities

He worked at a smithy

History

After a party from Ravens Bluff arrived at the city of Liara in their travels somehow and were retained to investigate the nightly series of grisly murders that were assumed to have been carried out by a vampire, Futgar Martovingian was the next victim; they may have witnessed the werespider that was actually killing him, if they were swift to arrive upon hearing his scream.

Upon his death, his dead body was prepared by the mortician, Theophelus.
