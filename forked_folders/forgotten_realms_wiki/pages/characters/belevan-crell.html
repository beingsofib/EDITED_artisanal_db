Belevan Crell, who was also later called Belevan the Unliving, was a Nentyarch of Narfell during the time of the Crell Dynasty.

Activities

Belevan ruled from to , far longer than any of his predecessors thanks to his successful transformation into a lich. Belevan's rule, and that of the entire Crell Dynasty, came to an end in -633 DR when he was overthrown by Ulthas and Ulthar Orgolath, a pair of brothers known as the "Demon Twins."
