Glayde was a merchant who lived in Beregost during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

Glayde worked with two other men Faltis and Tessilan.

History

In the , Glayde and his associates were the target of a scam by the bard Silke Rosena. She arranged to meet with them at the Red Sheaf inn under the pretense of purchasing some gems. Silke then tried to have Glayde and his fellows attacked by some locals, pretending as if they were local thugs hired to collect from a poor defenseless bard.
