Daron was a priest of Sharess who joined Aniolon Gruanthe in his exodus from Estagund to build Lliiress in the . After the blue dragon Gerinvioch employed the Evise Jhontil to hide the town from all observation, Daron died the next morning; he cast a spell, possibly intensify sensation, to enhance his pleasure and that of his partner in their morning activities. Unfortunately, he became the first victim of the Evise Jhontil and its curse, as the spell soon killed him.
