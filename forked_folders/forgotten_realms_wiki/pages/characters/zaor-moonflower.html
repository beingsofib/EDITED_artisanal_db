Zaor Moonflower was a moon elf, the patriarch of House Moonflower, the husband of Queen Amlaruil Moonflower, and the king of Evermeet until the early 14 century DR.

Description

Zaor had long dark-blue locks of hair reminiscent of sapphires or stormy seas and the most unusual color among the moon elves, and his eyes were blue, flecked with gold. He was exceedingly tall for an elf, standing over , with a frame as muscular as a human warrior. His height and physique were inherited by his eldest sons Zhoron and Xharlion, as well as his youngest son, Lamruil Moonflower.

History

Zaor Moonflower was born in Semberholme in the , some 237 years before the birth of Amlaruil Moonflower, For a time, he served in Myth Drannor as a ranger

In the , Zaor Moonflower became one of the youngest officers of the Akh'Velahr in Cormanthyr.

In the , Zaor was one of the survivors of Myth Drannor who had sought refuge on Evermeet. In the winter of the , he met Amlaruil in an unknown clearing for the first time. The encounter sparked Zaor to become the king of Evermeet.

Zaor became king of Evermeet in the . Through political maneuvering by Montagor Amarillis, Zaor was pressured to wed Lydi'aleera Amarillis while he had already conceived Ilyrana with Amlaruil. However, upon Lydi'aleera's death many years later because of Prince Rhenalyrr, Zaor wed his true love Amlaruil. They had their youngest child Lamruil Moonflower by the .

Zaor was rumored to have received the Fountain of Stars as a gift by Sehanine Moonbow.

King Zaor was murdered at the end of his 432 year of reign on Ches 2, the , After the Zaor's death, Queen Amlaruil became sole ruler of Evermeet.

Relationships

Zaor and Amlaruil were the parents of their oldest daughter Princess Ilyrana, twins Prince Zhoron and Prince Xharlion (born while they were unwed). Next came Prince Chozzaster, Princess Shandalar, and two more sets of twin girls, Princess Tira'allara and Hhora and then Princesses Lazziar and Gemstarzah. Many years later, they had four more children, first Amnestria, then Prince Zandro, Prince Finufaranell, and lastly Prince Lamruil. Via Princess Amnestria, they were the grandparents of the half-elf Arilyn Moonblade (wife of Danilo Thann) and her secret elder half-brother Elaith Craulnober.

Adamar Alenauth was his look-alike, but lesser in stature, who unknowingly had a son named Rhenalyrr borne by Lydi'aleera. Prince Rhenalyrr was raised by Zaor until his death at the King's Blade bonding.

Possessions

Zaor was the bearer of one of the most powerful elven moonblades, the King's Blade.

He possessed a pendant later worn by his youngest son Lamruil, the next King's Blade bearer, around his neck.
