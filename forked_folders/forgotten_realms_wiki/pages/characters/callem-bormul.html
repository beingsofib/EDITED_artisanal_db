Baron Callem Bormul was a Lord and a senior member of the noble patriar Bormul family of Baldur's Gate and a member of the Parliament of Peers in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Callem was a handsome human man with a light skin tone, dark eyes, long black hair, and a trimmed black short beared. Callem wore an expensive ensemble, gray in color, decorated with golden trim and a golden necklace.

History

In the , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldur's Gate, Baron Callem Bormul was a Parliamentarian of the Gate and voted in support of crowning lowborn Lord Enver Gortash as the city's Archduke. Callem was present for Gortash's coronation that took place in the audience hall of Wyrm's Rock. Following the coronation, he proudly proclaimed his support for Gortash' accession, stating that Balduran himself would be proud of the new Archduke.
