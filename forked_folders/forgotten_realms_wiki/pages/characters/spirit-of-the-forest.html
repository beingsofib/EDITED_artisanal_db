Spirit of the Forest

Person
nameSpirit of the Forest
homeIcewind Dale, northwest Faerûn

The Spirit of the Forest was an entity that became trapped in Icewind Dale.

Description

The Spirit of the Forest appeared much larger than a humanoid. It had huge, stag-like horns and a human-like body.

History

As of the 14 century DR, the Everspring Tree became corrupted by the crystal cultists' foul magic. In its corrupt state, it trapped the Spirit of the Forest in its strong roots.
