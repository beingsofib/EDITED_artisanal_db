Lashyrr Maerdrymm, more commonly known simply as the White Lady, was an aquatic elf baelnorn and the leader of the Dukar Order of Numos in Myth Nantar in Serôs as of the mid-to-late 14 century DR. She was titled the Grand Savant of the First Order and Paragon of the Numosi Order.

Activities

Of all the Dukar paragons, Lashyrr was most public and well known around 1370 DR. Following hundreds of years of isolation in Myth Nantar and Qatoris, she looked forward to rebuilding links with other elves.

High Mage Pharom Ildacer of the Nantarn Council was in secret discussions with Lashyrr to exchange magical knowledge: she would learn a little of elven High Magic and he would learn of Dukar magic and their Coral Hand power.

Relationships

Lashyrr believed herself to be the last of her family, and so had not used her clan name in more than two centuries. In truth, she was a cousin of Khelben Arunsun, the Blackstaff of Waterdeep.

Description

On becoming a baelnorn, Lashyrr transformed her body into living pearl, rather than an undead state. She resembled a statue of pale blue and ivory hue, and looked as beautiful as she did in her younger days, despite being some 975 years of age as of 1370 DR.
