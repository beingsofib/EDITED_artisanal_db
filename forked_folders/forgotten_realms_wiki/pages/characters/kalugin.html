Kalugin was an officer of the City Watch of Mulmaster.

Personality

Kalugin was a rigid applier of the law. He believed himself above good and evil, taking no sides with regard to lawbreakers and offering no leniency.

History

In 1491 DR, Kalugin and his patrol found Zel Tregarthen dead near an adventuring party. Believing them the criminals, he pursued them.
