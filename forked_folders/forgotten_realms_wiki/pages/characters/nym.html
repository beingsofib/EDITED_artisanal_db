Nym was the aged drow proprietor of the Jewel Box tavern in Menzoberranzan in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

He was a retired battle wizard and at some point in his past lost one of his legs and didn't get it magically replaced. It was suspected that he paid a large amount of his profits to the Ruling Council of the city in order for the more blasphemous of the seedier elements of his business to be allowed to pass—keeping female drow slaves for prostitution.
