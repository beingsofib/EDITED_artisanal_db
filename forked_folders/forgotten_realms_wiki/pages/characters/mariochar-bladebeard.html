Mariochar Bladebeard was a shrewd, clanless male dwarf who ruled the dwarven settlement of Eartheart.

History

Mariochar was elected ruler of Eartheart by the Deep Lords in 1346 DR. He then went on to be re-elected by them annually over the next twelve years.
