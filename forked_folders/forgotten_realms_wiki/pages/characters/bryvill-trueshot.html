Bryvill Trueshot was an elf ranger in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Bryvill had long blonde hair and light skin. She wore green leafy clothing including a tunic, breeches, and archer's hat, and had a huge copper bow.

History

In the , Bryvill joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp outside the ruins of Myth Drannor. She provided training and guidance to prospective rangers that passed through the camp.
