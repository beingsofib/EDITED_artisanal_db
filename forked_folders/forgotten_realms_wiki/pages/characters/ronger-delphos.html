Ronger Delphos was a merchant and the founder of an anti-thieves' guild of Waterdeep some years before the late 14 century DR.

History

Sometime before the , Ronger Delphos attempted to create an anti–thieves' guild in the City of Splendors by hiring thieves as security experts for his organization, which involved guarding Waterdhavian merchants. Ronger Delphos's recruits were armed with magic wands that dispensed a hold person spell. To counter this, Waterdeep's thieves' guilds purchased many belts of agility. This led to an escalation of the guild war between the thieves and Ronger Delphos's guards.
