Skith Tsornagar was a High Huntmaster, or high priest, of Malar, the Beastlord, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

High Huntmaster Tsornagar was high priest of the Divine Den, a temple of Malar found in Bezentil in the Great Dale. He lead a congregation of 70 Malarite clergy and many other additional followers.
