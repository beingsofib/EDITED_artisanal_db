Uther Stutgarde was an innkeeper from the city of Liara in Nova Vaasa, one of the Domains of Dread.

Description

He looked like someone's square-jawed, thick-forearmed grandpa.

Personality

Uther was boisterous and jovial.

Possessions

He owned the tavern and inn.

Activities

He kept the tavern and inn.

History

When a number of murders happened in the Merchants' Quarter of Liara in Nova Vaasa at a point roughly matching the mid-to-late 14 century DR, Uther was worried about the decreasing business, but sure that it would pass.
