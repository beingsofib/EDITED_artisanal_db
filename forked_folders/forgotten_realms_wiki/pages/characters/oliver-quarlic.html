Oliver Quarlic was a Gur monster hunter from a tribe led by Ulma that traveled along the Sword Coast and Western Heartlands during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Oliver was a tan-skinned Gur with a thin scar across the bridge of his nose, a brown beard, and a shaved head. He had a ring in his nostril and pierced ears. As an archer, he was dressed in a light set of leather armor and carried a longbow.

History

In the , Oliver Quarlic's monster hunter tribe found itself targeted by the vampires of Baldur's Gate during the Absolute crisis when the Gurs camped in the Rivington district of Baldur's Gate, along with a small army of refugees displaced by the conflict. To the Gurs' horror, the tribe's children were abducted by the vampires under orders from Cazador Szarr. Oliver's superstitious nature though that the gods had abandoned the tribe and that they were likely cursed, despite Ulma's assurance of it not being the case.
