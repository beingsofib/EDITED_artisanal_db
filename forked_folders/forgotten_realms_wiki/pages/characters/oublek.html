Oublek was a bounty office who worked out of Nashkel during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Oublek was a rather large man. He may not have been too keen-minded, as he could readily mistake one bounty hunter for another.

Relationships

He had a working relationship with the bounty hunter known as Greywolf.

History

As of the , he was offering payment for two high-profile bounties: Brage, the town's renegade captain of the guard, and the artist Prism.
