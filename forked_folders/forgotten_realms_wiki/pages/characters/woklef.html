Woklef was an adult black dragon whose lair was in the ruins of Orogoth in the High Moor of the Western Heartlands in the 1360s and 1370s DR.

He was the son of Vilholin and Wastirek and the brother of Aswidorg, Cheleen, and Kasidikal. He was the great-grandson of the black wyrm dracolich Calathanorgoth, "the Old One".
