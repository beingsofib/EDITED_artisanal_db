Winifred was a Ffolk barmaid who inhabited Kingsbay on the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Winifred was quite lovely, in both her physical appearance and personality, according to Elminster during his visit to Kingsbay.

Activities

Winifred worked at the Silver Salmon inn in Kingsbay.
