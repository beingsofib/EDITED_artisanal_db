Ooltheg was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Ooltheg was a hin man with a light skin tone and light brown, almost orange eyes. The halfling was a man of some means, judging by the quality frocks and buffy berets he wore walking the streets of Baldur's Gate.

History

In the , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, Ooltheg attended a performance of some showy magics by three spellslingers, on the Murl's Rest plaza in front of Sorcerous Sundries. The hin man was thoroughly entertained by the sparkling spells that reminded him of the Liar's Night celebrations.
