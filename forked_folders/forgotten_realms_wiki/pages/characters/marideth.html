Marideth was a cultist of the Spinner of Shadows in the world of Eberron.

Abilities

Marideth was a powerful arcane spellcaster, able to call upon acid fog, ball lightning, burning blood, delayed blast fireball, disintegrate, enervation, fear, and meteor swarm.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, Marideth was a senior member of the Lords of Dust cult dedicated to the demonic Spinner of Shadows. When Gnomon, Corin Dryden, and Dectaran's reinforcements assaulted the cultist base, Marideth attempted to defeat the invaders, perishing in the process.
