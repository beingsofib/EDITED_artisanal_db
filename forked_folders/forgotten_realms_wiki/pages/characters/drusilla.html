Drusilla was a young woman in Waterdeep in the late 1350s DR.

One night in a tavern in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR, she and a friend flattered and flirted with the dwarf adventurer Onyx the Invincible. However, this was a distraction so an old woman could get his autograph—on a marriage contract.
