Preani Qin was the ultimate leader of the Diligent Mamluk Society and commander of all mamluks in Huzuz, the City of Delights.

Description

Preani was attractive, so much so that she caught the eye of the Grand Caliph and earned the enmity of his wife, Tanya bint Perijan.

Personality

She was adept in politics and able to keep her varied units working in harmony.

Abilities

Preani was a competent commander and lead the Honored with pride. In addition to the Honored, she commanded units of mamluks from the Dutiful, Faithful, Valiant, and Studious. Each of these units had 1,000 men. Three 300-man units of cavalry from the Dutiful, Valiant, and Dauntless were also under her command.

Activities

Preani lived in an apartment in the Hall of Commanders within the Palace of the Enlightened Throne.
