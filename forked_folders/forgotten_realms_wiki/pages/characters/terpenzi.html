Terpenzi was a ha-naga and the first ruler of Najara, taking this position in . Terpenzi ruled from the capitol city of Ss'thar'tiss'ssun until his death in 90 DR.

History

In 89 DR, Terpenzi foresaw its own death and ordered its servants to search for Netherese artifacts. In the meantime, it searched for a mate. In the next year, it was indeed killed in battle with Verraketh the Shadowking.

After being killed by the Shadowking, it returned more powerful than before in undeath, Terpenzi continued to serve the kings who ruled after it, fighting the relic but still caring about Najara. It resided in the Snake Pit of Ss'khanaja among the treasures of the kingdom.
