Jebidian was an old scholar from the City of Splendors, Waterdeep, established and respected by the late 14 century DR.

History

Jebidian was a scholar from the same Waterdhavian academy as the aged Savant Gumbril. The Savant sought to impress his colleagues and especially Jebidian by uncovering a lost Netherese god from a ruined crypt on the Crab's Claw Coast. Instead Gumbril stumbled upon a prison of an immensely powerful ancient lich.
