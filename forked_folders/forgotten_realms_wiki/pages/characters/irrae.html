Irrae was a navigator for Company Yargo stationed aboard the Buccaneer's Tear as of the late 15 century DR.

Personality

She had an inquisitive mind and always looked out to discover the cause of nature's behavior.

History

When the Buccaneer's Tear sailed up to Pirates' Skyhold above the Sea of Swords in the , the ship landed near a dangerous swamp home to frightful bands of lizardfolk. Irrae set to journeying trough Vilebog swamp, collecting flora specimens, investigating floating motes of light, and even stealing distillation techniques from the local populace.

Irrae worked with Scout Tragold and the Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge to repair the rope bridges that were found throughout the swamp, in order for their expedition to continue further into the searing earthmote.
