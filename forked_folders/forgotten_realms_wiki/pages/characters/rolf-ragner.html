Rolf Ragner was a stablehand at the Eagle's Eyrie in Eaglton during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He said very little, and with great deference.

Activities

Rolf guided and cared for the horses of inn guests.

History

One spring in the mid-to-late 14 century DR, adventurers may have asked him about a passing patrol going from Ravens Bluff to Stag Falls. He was not forthcoming with information, normally, but may have made an exception for people who cared for their own horses or sought friendship.
