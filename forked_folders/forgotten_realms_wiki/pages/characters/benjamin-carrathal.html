Benjamin Carrathal was High King of Callidyrr in the late 13 century DR. Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles, he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty and was the first son of his predecessor, King Rieger. His eventual successor was his own first son, Alec Carrathal IV.

History

Benjamin assumed the throne in the , after the mysterious disappearance of his father.

In the eighteenth year of his reign, an enormous and unexplained explosion rocked the island of Flamsterd, resulting in the devastation of the island and the disappearance of the wizard for whom it was named.

Benjamin ruled for a total of twenty-three years before he was assassinated by nobles from Ogden in the .
