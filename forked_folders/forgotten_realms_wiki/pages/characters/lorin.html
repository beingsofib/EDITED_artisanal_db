Lorin was an elf man who became trapped within the lair of the hag Auntie Ethel, some time during or before the late 15 century DR.

Personality

The unfortunate man was afflicted with seeing horrific illusions. From his perspective, he saw truth where others only saw lies. In actuality, he was forced to witness his own death, seemingly at all times.

Possessions

He carries one Dagger.

Abilities

He can cast Blindness and Shocking Grasp. His melee attacks with his dagger are a regular Main Hand Attack and Piercing Strike.

Relationships

He was both a husband and a father.

History

At some point in his life, Lorin was presented with two paths he could take. He approached Auntie Ethel at her home and requested the power to see his own fate. The hag granted his wish, but with a distinct twist: Lorin was filled with visions of his own death. He remained trapped in the bowels beneath the Gnarled Teahouse as of the . When spoken to by an adventurer at Ethel's house, he panics, seeing them as a Mind Flayer and repeatedly pleading to not be hurt. When spoken to after Ethel is defeated, he says she made him do "vile, disgusting" things.

If talked to using Speak with Dead, he says he regrets making the deal with Ethel, but doesn't regret coming to her house, and there he couldn't see his family dead and rotting.
