Naneethil of the Sword Tattoo was a famous courtesan at the Purple Palace festhall in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep in the mid-1360s DR.

History

Naneethil was once a captive of orc slavers, who cruelly inflicted on her a huge brand while trying to sell her to Thayan slave-buyers. Fortunately, she escaped them years before coming to the Purple Palace.

Description

She was named for a brand resembling a sword running the length of her torso, with the pommel at her throat, the hilt on her chest, and the point at her crotch.
