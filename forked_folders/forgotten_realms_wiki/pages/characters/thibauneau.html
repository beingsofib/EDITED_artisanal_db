Thibauneau was a skilled Netherese arcanist who taught at the Alchemical Seminary in Eileanar until its destruction in .

Activities

Thibauneau was an expert in the area of elemental air and the transmutation of similar substances, such as the conversion of normal air to poisonous gases. She taught the subject at the Seminary under Quintus, from the most basic principles to the most advanced applications.
