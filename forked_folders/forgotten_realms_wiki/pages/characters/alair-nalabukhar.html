Alair Nalabukhar was the fabled owner of an orb of power in the texts studied by Phelandern Ghemelt.

Rumors & Legends

According to the legends, Alair Nalabukhar, one young son of the extinct Nalabukharn family, had merged himself with an orb of power after his body had grown too mangled to live. He was somewhere in Faerûn.
