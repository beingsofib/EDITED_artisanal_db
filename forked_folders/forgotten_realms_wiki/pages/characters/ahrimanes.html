Ahrimanes was a demon lord titled the Chief of the Cacodaemons who presided over the 452 layer of the Abyss, Ahriman-abad. Ahrimanes was the patron of exiles and wanderers.

History

At some point during the mid–14 century DR, Ahrimanes' physical form could be found within an iron fortress in the Abyss. During that time was believed to have been traveling across via the Astral Plane.
