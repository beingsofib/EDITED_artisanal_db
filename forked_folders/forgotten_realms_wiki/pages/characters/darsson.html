Darsson was the captain of the Halruaan skyship Stormsailer owned by the Datharathi as of . He himself was a native of Halruaa, and a wizard of enough experience to control the skyship. He took Warian Datharathi, Sevaera Datharathi and Zeltaebar Datharathi across the Golden Water on that year.
