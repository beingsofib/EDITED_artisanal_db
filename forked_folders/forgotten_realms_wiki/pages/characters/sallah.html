Sallah was an earth gen who babysat an unskilled sha'ir as he travelled Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Description

Sallah resembled a small plump earth elemental.

Activites

Perhaps because of his complete lack of guile and experience, Sallah's halfling sha'ir Rashad al-Anwar always seemed to find himself in tight situations requiring Sallah's skill and experience to smooth things over.
