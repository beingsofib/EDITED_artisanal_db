Padishah Alonka al-Aqil was the ruler of Afyal during the mid 13 century DR.

History

Alonka established the city of Dihliz in the Cities of the Ancients. His purpose behind this was to gain control of magical antiquities flowing through that region, and to establish a base of trade with the rest of the Ruined Kingdoms.
