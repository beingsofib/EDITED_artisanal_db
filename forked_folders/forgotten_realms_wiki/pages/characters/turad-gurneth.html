Turad Gurneth was a restaurateur in the city of Ravens Bluff, the owner and proprietor of the luxurious Gurneth's Goblet, in the 14 century DR.

Activities

Turad was known for encouraging informality among his patrons and made sure to have them seated as far apart as possible so as to ensure feelings of relaxation. These efforts were quite appreciated among the Ravenian nobility, making Gurneth's Goblet a popular site among Ravens Bluff's upper class.
