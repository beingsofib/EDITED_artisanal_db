King Janos Torlath I "the Vengeful" was an Amnian monarch of the Torlath dynasty, known as the first Wizard-King of Amn.

Relationships

Jhanos was the sixth son of of King Brinnaq I. His eldest brother was Esmel IV and his sole sister was Leluia.

History

When his sister's husband Arbas killed much of the royal family and seized the throne in 696 DR, Jhanos was abroad with his family. He returned the next year and slew the Black Usurper, becoming king himself.
