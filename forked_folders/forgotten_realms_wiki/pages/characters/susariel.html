Susariel was a cambion member of the Cult of the Dragon during the Tyranny of Dragons.

History

In 1489 DR, during the Cult's coup in Phlan, Susariel was tasked with the command of the secret Black Fist prison inside Stojanow Gate,, where Ector Brahms was imprisoned. Eventually, an adventuring party aided by Black Fist loyalists led by Aleyd Burral assaulted the prison and defeated the cultists. Susariel was also killed in the fighting.
