Ippen was an early cleric of Gond, who was later canonized by the Church of Gond as a saint of their faith. They were referred to by the Church as being Gond's "first great cleric"

Reputation

  • Ippen was notably the namesake of Ippensheir, the sole calendar-related festival of the Church of Gond. Ippen was said to sometimes appear before Gondar clergy in need during this time.
  • When missionaries from Lantan traveled to Maztica, they named the largest island in the Green Sisters archipelago after them, St. Ippen.
