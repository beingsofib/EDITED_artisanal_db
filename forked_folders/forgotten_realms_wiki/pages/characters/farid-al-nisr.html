Farid yn Fahd al Nisr, or Farid al-Nisr, was a wizard and member of the Harpers in the late 14 century DR.

History

In Calimport, Calimshan, circa 1366 DR, Farid joined the crew of the Hippocampus under Captain Syllia Adarbrent. Within a year, they had become lovers

Around 1369 DR, Farid and Syllia were sent to New Waterdeep in Maztica by High Harper Khelben Arunsun together with fellow Harper Orist Moonbow. There he was chosen as one of the new Lords of the Borderlands for his opposition to slavery, Harper membership, and his connections. After investigating mistreatment of the native Payit, Farid and Syllia returned to Waterdeep, due to arrive in late summer, 1370 DR.

Once Farid and Syllia were back in Waterdeep, they impetuously married in the spring of the , before going back to Maztica and NEw Waterdeep.

Relationship

Known to few, Farid was the son of Fahd yn Sarsor al Nisr, a First Yrshelem of the Rundeen.

Farid was the lover, and later husband, of Lady Syllia Adarbrent of House Adarbrent of Waterdeep. However, her father Royus Adarbrent disapproved of him, since he was trying to arrange a more fitting marriage for his daughter and both he and her brothers were shocked by their wedding.

Personality

Farid hated slavery.
