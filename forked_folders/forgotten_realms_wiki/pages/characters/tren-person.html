Tren was a farmer's son enslaved by the Cult of the Dragon in 1369 DR.

Description

Tren was a homely but strong young man, with a muscular body of a farmer.

History

In 1369 DR, Tren was one of the unfortunate villagers captured by the Cult of the Dragon's slavers. Alongside Wenta, Jorsh, and Ristim, the folk were taken to the Cult's slave pens in the ruined Darnarest's tower.
