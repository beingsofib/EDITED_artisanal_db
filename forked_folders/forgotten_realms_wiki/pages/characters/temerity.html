Temerity was a tiefling descendant of the Toril thirteen.

Activities

Temerity ran a shop in Proskur.

History

In 1478 DR, Farideh and Lorcan went to Temerity's shop. Lorcan asked for Temerity's assistance for his plan. However, Temerity had made a pact with Sairché and informed her of the presence of Farideh and Lorcan in the city.

Powers

Temerity had made a warlock's pact with Lorcan but later transferred to Sairche.
