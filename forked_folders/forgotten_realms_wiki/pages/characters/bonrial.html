King Bonrial was a ruler of Estagund in the Shining South during the 10 century DR.

Activities

Bonrial was the first strong and skilled ruler of Estagund following the country gaining independence from Reinhar I in the 6 century DR. King Bonrial was able to fully unite the various city-states into a single nation.
