Snurd was a goblin devotee of the Absolute who lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Snurd wore a crudely made scale mail cuirass along with a leather hood and mask that obscured his face.

History

In the , the cult of the Absolute's launched a terrible, and yet successful, raid on the Waukeen's Rest. Afterwards many of the goblin cutlists celebrating by drinking, playing games, and enjoying whatever they looted, at the nearby Shattered Sanctum. Snurd unfortunately was left at guard duty, at a small outpost just outside the sanctum. He did not seem to mind however, as his vantage point atop the outpost gave him a lovely view, from which he thought he could see the Absolute herself.
