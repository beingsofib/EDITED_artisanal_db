Akrosz Ulvinhand was a human blacksmith who lived in the village of Amphail in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Akrosz was a very tall man and sported a beard.

Activities

Akrosz lived and worked in an establishment that was known as the Ulvinhand smithy. He only undertook commissions that interested him and local gossip claimed that many of these were blades later enchanted by wizards.

Reputation

He was said to be the equal of any smith in Waterdeep, making him counted among the best human smiths in all of the Sword Coast.
