Iphigen Mohgros was a theologian and a writer, who authored Judge of the Damned sometime in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Iphigen penned his book on the strange occurrences of mortals who reached apotheosis, their mortal lives and divine existence, including the Lords of Death Kelemvor, sometime after the and before the . The book remained in circulation as late as the , with a copy stored in the library of the Stormshore Tabernacle of Baldur's Gate.
