Hulfast was a slaver inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Hulfast worked with Lhund Dhaermos out of the Dhaermos Warehouse located on Belnimbra's Street in Waterdeep's Dock Ward. He acquired slaves and exotic flora and fauna that Lhund sold through the warehouse.
