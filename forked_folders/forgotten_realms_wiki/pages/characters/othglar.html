Othglar was a soldier of Athalantar in the early mid-3 century DR.

History

Othglar was one of nigh-four thousand men that were massed in force for the Magelords of Athalantar in , ready to strike at the elves of the High Forest. He was a lookout on a cliff overlooking the main camp. One night, as he took a moment to relieve himself, he was slain with his own halberd he'd left aside for a minute, by the priestess of Mystra, Elmara.

Rumors & Legends

A soldier later saw Elmara and assumed the reason Othglar hadn't reported in was because he had been fooling around with her, rather than having been just killed.
