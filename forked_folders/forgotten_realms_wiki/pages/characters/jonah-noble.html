Jonah was one of the members of the wealthy high society of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

At some point before the , Jonah set his eyes and amorous ambitions on Lady Firelia Jannath, one of the city's patriars and a notable patroness of the arts. He was among suitors who fought for the lady's hand in 1492 DR, when her lover, Oskar Fevras, was presumed missing or killed, in reality, captured and enslaved by the Zhentarim smugglers.
