Vangol Kuskolt was a dwarven priest of Tyr active in the Sword Coast North in the late 15 century DR.

Description

He walked heavily on an iron peg leg.

Personality

Vangol was fiery yet fair.

Activities

Vangol assumed the duties of presiding over the Tyr Shrine when it was repaired and rebuilt along with the town of Leilon in the late 15 century DR. He soon became the newfounded community's judge, presiding over and adjudicating legal disputes on the steps of his shrine. When he judged a criminal guilty of an awful crime, he would heat his peg leg in a fire and use it to brand the guilty party before driving them out of town.

Shortly after his arrival, he became one of Leilon's five town councilors.
