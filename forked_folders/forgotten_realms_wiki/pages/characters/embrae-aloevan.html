Laranla Embrae Aloevan was a moon elf Chosen of both Mystra and Sehanine. She died in the early 4 century to the power of the Silver fire of Mystra, after being slowly consumed by it.

Description

Queen Aloevan's laughter had become infected by palpable insanity.

Personality

Consumed by madness, the former Laranla only clung to vestigial sanity thanks to the efforts of her ever-present attendants.

Abilities

She possessed Silver fire, though she lost control of it.

Relationships

In life, Embrae Aloevan had been a Laranla, or queen. In death, seven priests served Embrae Aloevan; new priests took their places during solar eclipses.

History

Embrae Aloevan became a Laranla of Ardeep in . However, she responded poorly to the Silver fire she was infused with, and it burnt her out from within; she was unable to keep herself together under the sheer pressure of all she saw and learned with Mystra's power.

Upon her death, the Queen's spirit was trapped by the Silver fire, and she found herself unable to pass onto Arvanaith, caught instead in a limbo made of the same power she could not control. Seven priests of Sehanine joined her in that in-between plane, recreating the Elven Court within. They eased Aloevan's torments and helped her retain some semblance of sanity, though she was reduced to a manner of spectral harpist ghost.

Rumors

According to a chronicle by Volothamp Geddarm, Queen Embrae was only broken by the Silver fire because Bane was trying to possess her and so wrest its power. She sacrificed her sanity and personality to deny the malevolent god.
