Zita was a laborer and a packer of the Baldur's Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Zita was an older, pale-skinned gold dwarf with a clean-shaved face. She had gray hair gathered in a braid and affixed around her head and blue eyes. Zita wore a loose white tunic.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Zita was employed by Baldur's Mouth and could be found outside the broadsheet publisher's office backing issues for delivery. She personally stopped reading the paper due to the sharply declined quality of reporting and Lord Gortash's propaganda. She also was used to hearing complaints about the broadsheet's contents and simply sent everyone into the offices to talk to the editor-in-chief instead.
