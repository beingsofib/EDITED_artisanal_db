Alarashan Tlor was a magelord of Athalantar during the first half of the 3 century DR.

Personality

A careful man with notable self-control, he constantly planned on how to seize power upon the fall of his comrades.

Abilities

He was an extremely powerful wizard.

Activities

As a magelord, his efforts were mostly focused on keeping track of the comings and goings of merchants and adventurers in Athalantar.

Relationships

He was a member of the magelords of Athalantar until his death.

History

Alarashan had been a Magelord since at least some point between the and the .

During the siege of Hastarl by the forces under prince Elminster Aumar in , Alarashan retrieved Nanatha, an apprentice magelord, from the tower of fellow magelord Malanthor Drymm, who had been recently slain. Later during the siege, he heard the death scream of Ortran, a fellow apprentice, who was ambushed by swordsmen infiltrating the palace; Alarashan in turn was disabled by those same men.

He was later summoned to Ithboltar's tower, which collapsed, killing him.
