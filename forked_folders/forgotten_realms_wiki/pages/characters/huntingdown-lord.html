Lord Huntingdown was a nobleman of Cormyr in the 15 century DR.

History

In late Mirtul of the , Elminster Aumar, a mage seeking the blueflame items, found one of his castings returning an alarm for a blueflame ghost attacking Lord Huntingdown; he mentioned the man lived in Huntingdown Hall.
