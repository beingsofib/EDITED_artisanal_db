Helene was a spellcaster from the isle of Norland in the Moonshaes, off the west coast of Faerûn.

Relationships

Growing up, Helene had two close childhood friends, Aiden and Xander.

History

On her Day of Ascension, a milestone that signified she'd reached adulthood, Helene chose to leave her home on Norland for the island of Mintarn, where she planned to join the White Sails company.
