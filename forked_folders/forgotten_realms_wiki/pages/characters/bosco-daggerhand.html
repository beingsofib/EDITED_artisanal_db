Bosco Daggerhand was the proprietor of the tavern Bosco's Bilge in Jahaka Anchorage during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

True to his name, Bosco never made an introduction without keeping his free hand upon the dagger that hung from his hip.

Relationships

Bosco was protected by a partially-domesticated deinonychus called Knuckles. Their relationship was made possible by Bosco's ring of animal influence.
