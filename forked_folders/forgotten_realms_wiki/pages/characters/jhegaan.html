Jhegaan the Maestro was a half-elf bard, a former adventurer, and an employee of Aurora's Emporium in the mid-to-late 14 century DR..

Description

He was handsome and charismatic.

History

Members of an adventuring company together, Jhegaan traveled with Aurora across much of Faerûn, from Icewind Dale to Chult, from the Sword Coast to the fringes of Kara-Tur, as part of their explorations.

When Aurora retired to start her business, Jhegaan agreed to work with her and found that he was good at it too.

Relationships

Once, he and Aurora had been lovers, but they remained very close friends.

Activities

By 1370 DR, he worked as Aurora's assistant to deal with everyday affairs of the company.

From time to time, he traveled via teleport spells to the exotic locales that Emporium agents were active. There, the bard would tell tales of other realms, and often found women to share his company, or his bed.

Possessions

He played a lute and wielded a +2 staff-spear, which he could throw like a javelin of piercing once a day.
