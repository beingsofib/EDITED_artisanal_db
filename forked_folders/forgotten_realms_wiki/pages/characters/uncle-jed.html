Uncle Jed was a fisher uncle of Young Ned in Icewind Dale in the mid-to-late 13 century DR.

History

Uncle Jed was said to had been the best fisher in Maer Dualdon from the town of Lonelywood, who died sometime before the . After his passing, the man's nephew – Young Ned of Lonelywood inherited the title. At least according to Ned's own words.
