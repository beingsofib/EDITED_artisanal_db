Darun Lockmaster was the only dwarven councilor within the Dale Council of High Dale in the late 14 century DR. He was known as a cheerful and intelligent man, as well as an extremely skilled armorsmith and locksmith. He was known to craft the finest locks, as well as accompanying picks, in all of the Dalelands.
