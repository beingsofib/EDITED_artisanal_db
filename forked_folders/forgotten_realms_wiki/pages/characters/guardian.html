The Guardian, or He Who Guards Those That Sleep, was a yak-man inhabiting a secret complex beneath Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Activities

The Guardian was tasked with watching over the helpless bodies of both the Watcher and the Unspeakable while they remained in possession of their victims. He was assisted by a pair of earthservants.
