Merith Strongbow () was an elven fighter mage and a member of the Knights of Myth Drannor. His familiar was a black cat named Shadowclaw.

Description

Merith was tall, standing five feet ten inches. He had dark black hair and one blue colored eye and one green colored eye. He wore both wands and his sword on his belt.

Abilities

In addition to his wizardry, Merith was psionically gifted with the powers detection of good or evil and object reading.

History

Merith was born to Laerune and Lianthorn Strongbow on the banks of the river Duathamper in 1161 DR
