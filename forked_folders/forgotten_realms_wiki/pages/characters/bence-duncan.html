Corporal Bence Duncan was a soldier serving within the Flaming Fist mercenary company during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Corporal Duncan was extremely dutiful individual.

Relationships

He thought quite highly of the Flaming Fist's general, Marshal Kyrill Nederlok.

History

Around Eleint 1, in the , Corporal Duncan was tasked with leading a contingent of Flaming Fists to scout out the rapidly-growing army that was forming up around Dragonspear Castle. By Uktar 3 he had returned from his mission and presented his findings to Marshal Nederlok.

Shortly thereafter, Duke Entar Silvershield tasked the corporal with the protection of his daughter Skie, as he knew she would attempt to discreetly tag along with the newly-formed Coalition army.

Bence Duncan worked closely with the Hero of Baldur's Gate during his involvement with the Coalition and the War of the Shining Crusade. He and his soldiers guarded the entrance to the portal that was opened beneath Dragonspear Castle, during the final battle of the war.

After the portal was closed, Bence discovered the body of the deceased Skie Silvershield and saw that the Hero of Baldur's Gate was covered in her blood. The corporal and his soldiers arrested the Bhaalspawn and took him back to trial in Baldur's Gate, for the crime of murder of a noble.
