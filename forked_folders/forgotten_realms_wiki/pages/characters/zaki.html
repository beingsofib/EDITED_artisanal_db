Zaki was a tiefling child who was among those exiled from Elturel in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Zaki had light red skin and two larger horns that went nearly straight upwards. He wore a simple, blue-grey cloth tunic wrapped in a leather girdle, atop cloth pants.

Activities

He was one of the members of the gang led by another tiefling child, Mol.

History

After the tieflings of Elturel were kicked out of the city in the , many of them journeyed west to Baldur's Gate under the leadership of Zevlor. During the trek, many of the children found safety banding together and fending for themselves. Mol emerged as their leader, and helped them established a little hideaway when the refugees took shelter in the Emerald Grove for a time.
