Vayloss Talanthil III, who was also called the Younger and who was originally named Jharath, was the sixth Sorcerer-Arkhan of the Talanthil Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar.

Vayloss III had one older brother. His son and heir was Mornarth I.
