Ulliam was a cleric of Tempus in the village of Hlath in Chondath in 1358 DR.

Activities

Ulliam took care of Tempus's temple in Hlath together with his acolyte Elspeth.

History

In 1358 DR, Ulliam was worried about his fellow Theodoric and after receiving an omen from his god he hired an adventuring party to investigate what had happened to him.

Some time later, Ulliam participated in a meeting to discuss a barbarian army from Kara-Tur that menaced Hlath. He proposed a sneak attack.

He took part in the defense of the city.
