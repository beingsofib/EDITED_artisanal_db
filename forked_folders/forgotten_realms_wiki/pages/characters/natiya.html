Lady Natiya was the dowager mother of Priest-King Vishnan VII of the Kuong Kingdom in Malatra in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

She remained as the leading member of the nobility and was a significant influence on her son the king. She had imperial ambitions for the Kuong Kingdom, which she encouraged in Vishnan, and this led to the planned invasion of Laothan around 1357 DR.
