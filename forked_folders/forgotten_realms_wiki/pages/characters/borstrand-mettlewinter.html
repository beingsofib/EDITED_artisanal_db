Borstrand Mettlewinter was a banker of sorts in the village of Eveningstar during the 1480s DR.

Description

Borstrand was a large man who had brown hair and a short beard, wearing a long tunic.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Borstrand resolutely continued to provide storage solutions to residents and visitors to the village.
