Ashanda was an evening-lass at The Kissing Wench in Athalantar in the Western Heartlands during the early-to-mid–3 century DR.

Description

She was known to wear a bodice that was cut low and made of silk.

Possessions

She wore knife-boots.

Abilities

Ashanda was deadly with her knife-boots.

Activities

As an evening-lass, she engaged in lascivious activities for money.

History

In the , Ashanda had become an acquaintance of Eladar and Farl, two local underworld characters, who had seen her strike down a sailor with her blade-toe. One early evening, she was drinking with fellow evening-lass Budaera when the two thieves came across her.
