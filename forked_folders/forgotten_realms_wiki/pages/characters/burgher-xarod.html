Burgher Xarod was the original boss of the land that later became the Xiousing district in Marsember.

History

Xarod controlled some of the neighborhoods around Xiousing and when the Sea of Fallen Stars retreated, he assumed control of the newly exposed land. In 1419 DR, King Azoun V granted the Shou Duc-Sum Li regency over the Xiousing district, which infuriated Xarod. Even in his extreme old age, he plotted to regain the land he believed to be rightfully his.
