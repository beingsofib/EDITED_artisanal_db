Ivain Arkenson

nameIvain Arkenson
homeSword Coast, Western Heartlands

Ivain Arkenson was a follower of Oghma who was lived and died in the Realms, presumably somewhere on the Sword Coast. Ivain's obituary was listed among others within the records of an abandoned chapel, that lay above an ancient crypt of Jergal.

While the exact circumstances of Ivain's death was unknown, they were believed to have died as a cause of "internal rot".
