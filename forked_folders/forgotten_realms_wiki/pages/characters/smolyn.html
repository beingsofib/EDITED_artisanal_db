Smolyn was a Netherese arcanist and prolific spell inventor who developed spells that affected one's vision.

History

Smolyn invented eight spells during their life. The spells they developed were blindness and foresight in , true seeing in , blur in , veil in , eyebite in , false vision in , and finally, mirror image in .
