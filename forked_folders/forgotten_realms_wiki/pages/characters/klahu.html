K'lahu was a bookie who accepted bets at Executioner's Run in Port Nyanzaru, Chult during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

If one of her bettors accrued a debt and refused to replay, K'lahu would recruit adventurers to beat the debtor into submission and claim her money. She offered such hirelings 10% of the money which they recovered.

Relationships

A gladiator named Taban came to owe a debt of 500 gp to K'lahu. When he refused to pay, she sought adventurers to collect her money from him.
