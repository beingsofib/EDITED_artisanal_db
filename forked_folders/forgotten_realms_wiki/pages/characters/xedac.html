Xedac was a young vampire agent of the Twisted Rune in 1375 DR.

Activities

Xedac lead a pack of ghouls and ghasts in performing various tasks for the Runemasters.

History

Xedac and his team had performed many missions for the Twisted Rune and he was destroyed several times.

In 1375 DR, the Runemasters sent Xedac and his team via a powerful teleport spell into Undermountain in order to recover the lost runestone created by Halaster Blackcloak just before his sudden death.

Personality

Xedac had become a successful undead and appreciated the powers granted by his condition. He did not care for his destruction, knowing his masters would soon resurrect him if the worst happened. Xedac preferred fighting using his sword.
