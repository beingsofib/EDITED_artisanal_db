"Ears" Oohna was a patron of the Elfsong Tavern in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Oohna was a wood elven woman with brown skin, hazel eyes, and long, straight brown hair gathered in a ponytail. Her haughty demeanor and quality clothes suggested Oohna to be a woman of means.

History

In the , "Ears" Oohna was a frequent customer of the Elfsong Tavern in the Eastway neighborhood of Baldur's Gate. She could often be found dining whilst sharing a table with a gnomish friend named Wrilda Gemfinder. Oohna was surprised to find out a band of adventurers rented out the entirety of Elfsong's upper floor after the infamous murder of Duke Belynne Stelmane. She half-jokingly suggested that the group might awaken the tavern's singing ghost.
