Dida was an apprentice blacksmith who worked in Moonhaven sometime during or before the late 15 century DR. She studied under the guidance of the town's more expert smith, Sampson.

Activities

She worked especially fast and somewhat carelessly, regularly burning her hands and face in the process.

History

Throughout her young career, Dida made regular visits to the healer at Moonhaven's apothecary.
