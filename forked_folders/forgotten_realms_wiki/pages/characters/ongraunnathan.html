Ongraunnathan, also known as Ongraunnathan the Blind, was a court wizard in upland Calimshan of the late 14 century DR. Through magic, he killed and replaced his Pasha, Eiruidin Ondaru.

Personality

Ongraunnathan had such a fondness for whiteworms in wine that he often wound up in stupor lasting for hours after devouring a jar of them.

Abilities

Ongraunnathan had the ability to create flying zombies, presumably from magically competent people. He was also blind.

Possessions

Ongraunnathan possessed a crown that allowed him to see the world out of two wizard eyes.

History

Ongraunnathan the Blind was the court wizard of Pasha Eiruidin Ondaru in upland Calimshan until the year , when he killed the Pasha and employed magic to make him appear to be alive. His deception lasted until .

After slaying his Pasha, Ongraunnathan routinely examined the slaves in his home for magical potential, making them perform various tasks for him. Those he found to bear magical potential, he slew, using them to create flying zombies.

He was killed at some point between and by a sorcerer who dwelt not far away, who employed magic to turn those flying zombies against their master. They rose from the cellars and went to his bedchamber, where they tore him limb from limb.
