Bando the Lame was an old halfling priest of Tymora in Daggerford around 1370 DR.

History

Bando was once injured in a battle with three lizardfolk, which gave him an injured leg and a deep hatred for the scaly fiends. He managed the local shrine to Tymora, the Fairfortune Hall.

His home was located in the northwest corner of Daggerford along Horse Way.
