Al-Warrakh, who was nicknamed the Charmer

Activities

He was a skilled warrior and wizard. Al-Warrakh would use his magical powers to assist Sheik Ar-Rabi on raids in southern Murghôm and on the various tribes of the Plains of Purple Dust.
