Kutlass was a notorious yuan-ti abomination who developed a liking for hunting silverback gorillas of the Jungles of Chult in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Kutlass was a large yuan-ti with a long, sinuous, serpentine body covered in teal scales with a tan underbelly. The monster wielded a massive blood-caked cutlass.

History

In the late 15 century DR, as the death curse ravaged Faerûn, the famed author Volothamp Geddarm found himself in the Hunter's Square in Port Nyanzaru, a city in Chult. As he was working on Volo's Guide to Monsters, Volo enlisted the aid of adventurers from the north to hunt down and slay a collection of powerful beasts of legend and report to him with their experiences and loot. A horn of the ape was used to beckon forth Kutlass near the ruins of Omu deep in the jungles. Kutlass was presumably slain by the adventurers, saving the lives of countless "majestic giants."
