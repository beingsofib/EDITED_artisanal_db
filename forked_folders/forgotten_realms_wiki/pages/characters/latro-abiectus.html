Latro Abiectus was one of the Cult of the Dragon members stationed in the Tower of Bones, Polyandrium of Myth Drannor in 1369 DR.

History

In 1369 DR, Latro Abiectus was under the command of Ryngoth, the High Priest of Velsharoon who commanded the cult's excavation of the Polyandrium of Myth Drannor. Latro had a gambling problem as he wrote an IOU to the cult's female necromancer owing her two hundred gold coins.
