Cyrdemac was a particularly unscrupulous man who lived in Baldur's Gate during the mid−14 century DR.

Personality

He was an unethical man, willing to manipulate others for his own gain.

Possessions

Among Cyrdemac's possessions was an +1 enchanted bastard sword.

Activities

Cyrdemac was a regular patron of the Elfsong.

History

In the , Cyrdemac became aware that the noblewoman Areana was having an extra-marital affair in the city's Undercellar. He began to blackmail the noblewoman, to the amount of hundreds of gold pieces.
