Captain Belars Orhotek was an officer of the Amnian Council Army who commanded the Athkatla garrison in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

In 1370 DR, Captain Belars was responsible for commanding the 4,000-strong garrison of Council soldiers in Athkatla, making it the second-largest garrison in Amn.
