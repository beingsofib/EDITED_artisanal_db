Thalart was a malaugrym in the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the , Thalart was plotting to usurp the position of the Shadowmaster Supreme Dhalgrave. Caught out, he volunteered to kill Elminster in Shadowdale early in the Time of Troubles, but was instead destroyed by Elminster.
