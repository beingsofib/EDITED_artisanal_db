Felix was chief lieutenant in the army of King Virdin Bloodfeathers of Damara when they fought the army of Zhengyi the Witch-King in the mid 14 century DR.

Possessions

Felix wielded an enchanted longsword coated in poison.

History

Felix was a member of the Citadel of Assassins, a group closely allied with Zhengyi the Witch-King of Vaasa.

During the Vaasan War of the 1350s DR, Felix infiltrated the Damaran army and served as chief lieutenant to the young King Virdin during his campaign against Zhengyi. It was widely believed that Felix betrayed Virdin in the , by making him believe that a fake magic wand would enable the Damaran army to cross the Ford of Goliad undetected, and when the king attempted this gambit, Zhengyi wiped out his forces. King Virdin himself was assassinated as he was watching his army's defeat, and it was further believed that Felix was also responsible for this deed.
