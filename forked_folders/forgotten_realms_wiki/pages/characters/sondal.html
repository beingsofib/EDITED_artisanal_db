Sondal was the duel arena owner and organizer in the Ust Natha tavern around 1369 DR.

Description

Sondal hosted a duel arena in the central tavern of Ust Natha. He believed that dueling animals was pitiable, and that only a drow versus drow match was fair. Among his duelists were Lasaonar and Chalinthra.
