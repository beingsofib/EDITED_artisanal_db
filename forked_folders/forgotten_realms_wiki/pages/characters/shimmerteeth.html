Shimmerteeth was an ice mephit residing in Shimmersky Valley in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Shimmerteeth was one of the larger of the many ice mephits that were drawn to protect Shimmersky Valley because of the icy region's pure and elemental cold.

History

When the Frosthunger Horde invade the Shimmersky Valley in the late 15 century DR, they clashed with the local guardians, including Shimmerteeth and its fellow ice mephits. Eventually, adventurers from Waterdeep arrived to investigate the activies of the Horde, and also clashed with the Valley's elemental guardians. Shimmerteeth was defeated by this party, and shattered into a burst of icy shards that bit through their armor.
