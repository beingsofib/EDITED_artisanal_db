Khynree () was a wizard that lived in the town of Ayakar in Turmish during the late 15 century DR. He was known for his skill with. teleportation magic and would charge passengers for translocational voyages across Faerûn.

Personality

He was never one to forget nor forgive a debt, and made practicing of charging three times his normal fee for anyone previously unable to pay.

Activities

Khynree would charge somewhere in the neighborhood of 1,000 gp for his services. and the underwater storm giant fortress of Maelstrom in the Trackless Sea,

Kynree's abilities were not limited to the Prime Material plane. By means of certain spells he could also transport clientele to other planes of existence, including the eternal battlefield of Avernus in the Nine Hells.

Relationships

Due to some unfortunate circumstances, Khynree came to work for the Zulkir of Enchantment Dar'lon Ma. At some point prior he had indebted himself to the Red Wizards of Thay.

He was also a known associate of Zehira Deryan.

History

Some time during or after the , Khynree transported a band of adventurers down to the Nine Hells, as they quested for the soul of the undead Storm King Hekaton.
