Astil adh Saida al Manshaka was a human fighter and a founder and leader of the Clenched Fist, a Rundeen-affiliated mercenary and adventuring company based in Schamedar in Calimshan in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Abilities

He had a natural talent for tactics and strategy, which served him well in battle. He was also strong.

History

Astil was once a slave of Saida al Manshaka in the city of Manshaka. He killed her in the 1340s DR, but chose to keep his slave name.

To keep his freedom he joined the Clenched Fist in Schamedar, then a rowdy gang of mercenaries before it grew into a full-fledged mercenary company.
