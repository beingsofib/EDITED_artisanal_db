Fitzy Relma was an Untaken of Umberlee, a member of the Bitch Queen's clergy under Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queen's House in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Fitzy Relma was a fair-skinned human woman with white hair she wore in bob, decorated with an intricate metal headdress. She wore metallic silver eye-liner that countoured her light brown eyes and her face bore metallic blue markings of paint or tattoos.

Posessions

Fitzy's Untaken uniform was a revealing chainmail dress with light pauldrons and decorative braces. Her weapon of choice was a simple sickle.

History

Circa the , during the Absolute crisis, Untaken Fitzy Relma was placed in charge of stowing tithes on behalf of Allandra Grey. She worked diligently, fearful of the Flood Tide's wrath.
