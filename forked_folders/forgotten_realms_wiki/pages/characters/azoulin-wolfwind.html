Azoulin Wolfwind was a stationer inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Azoulin was the guildmaster for the Stationers' Guild circa 1357 DR. He held the title of "Master Stationer" within the guild and worked out of Stationers' Hall–their headquarters on the Way of the Dragon in the Trades Ward.
