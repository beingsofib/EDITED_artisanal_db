Odd Valros was a coffinmaker from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

By the , Odd Valros's services were in high demand due to the Absolute Crisis. The coffin maker's specialty were deluxe hardwood coffins that, according to the crafter, were thrice as durable as other types of wood. Odd Valros's coffins were said to preserve the bodies for longer than the competitors' and protected the dearly departed from vermin such as rats and maggots. To promote the business, Odd Valros penned The Superior Durability of Hardwood.
