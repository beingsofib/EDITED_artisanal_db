Kordan was a powerful wizard and an infamous archmage who was hired by Amun-Re to build the Maze of Mists within the Pyramid of Amun-Re, sometime before the 4 century DR.

History

Kordan's creation was located on the second floor of the Pyramid of Amun-Re. The archmage filled the maze with monsters and disorienting mists.

As of the , the dopplegangers in the Pyramid of Amun-Re counted wizards that came to the pyramid to discover the secrets of Kordan the Archmage amongst its disguises. Those disguises were based on the forms of previous victims. He may have been the namesake of Kordan's Master Mists
