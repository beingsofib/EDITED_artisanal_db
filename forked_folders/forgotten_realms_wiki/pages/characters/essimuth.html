Essimuth was a shopkeeper inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

He owned Essimuth's Equipment located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter where Weeping Maiden's Run joined Slop Street next door to the Temple of Good Cheer.

Activities

Essimuth sold adventuring gear and general tools.
