Maresa Rost was a tall air genasi-human-crossbreed in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Maresa had very pale skin, large violet eyes, a pointed chin, and a heart-shaped face. She moved gracefully, and she had long silver-white hair. Maresa wore the star-shaped pendant given to her mother by Araevin Teshurr after she left the Company of the White Star.

Relatives

Maresa's mother was Theleda Rost. When Theleda was murdered, Maresa hunted down her assassin and killed him, then killed the assassin's employer.

Equipment

Maresa wore a suit of dark crimson studded leather armor and carried a rapier and a crossbow.
