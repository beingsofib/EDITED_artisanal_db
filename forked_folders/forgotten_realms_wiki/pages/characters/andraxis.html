Andraxis was a great wyrm who ravaged the Shining Plains in the Vilhon Reach during the dracorage of 1018 DR.

History

The saw the Rage of Dragons descend upon Faerûn. Andraxis, the great wyrm, was among dragons who attacked the towns of Lheshayl and Ormath of the Shining Plains. The rage left a heavy toll on the Free Cities, but Andraxis was slain along with three other dragons by the mercenary companies of the Shining Plains.
