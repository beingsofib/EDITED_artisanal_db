Jarvalar Buckman was a tavernkeeper and restaurateur of Swordsfall House, a shraehouse (a small restaurant/tavern), in the city of Silverymoon in the Silver Marches in the late 14 century DR. Master Jarvalar served much food and wine there around 1372 DR.
