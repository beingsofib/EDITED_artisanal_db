Ma Chung-ying was a shou inhabiting the Bitter Well Oasis within the Quoya Desert during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Ma was the only permanent resident of Bitter Well Oasis. He ran a small inn that he would have otherwise abandoned had the Shou government not supplied him with wine and food in return for information on a travelers passing through the small town.
