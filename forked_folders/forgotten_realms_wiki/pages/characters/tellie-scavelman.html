Tellie Scavelman was a trader on behalf of Eveningstar during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Tellie had light skin and dark brown hair in twin braids, wearing a short brown and white dress.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Tellie gave humble material assistance to those that aided the village—refreshing apple cider, the stout oak walking stick, and the bracers, belt, and vestments of the sun soul.
