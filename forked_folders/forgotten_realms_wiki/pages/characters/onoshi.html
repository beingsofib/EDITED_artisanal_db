Onoshi was an inhabitant of the town of Aru in Wa.

Relationships

Onoshi was the son of an Aru fishmonger.

History

Around 1358 DR, he was paid by a stranger to set fire to a peasant's minka in the eta area of town. He complied, but just after the act, Onoshi was discovered by an adventuring party hired by the daimyo Benju Matsutomo and captured.
