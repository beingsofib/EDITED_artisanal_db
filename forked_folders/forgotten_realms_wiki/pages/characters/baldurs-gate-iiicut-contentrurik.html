Rurik was a dwarven man that lived on the Sword Coast for a time during the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

He wielded a pair of daggers in battle and made liberal use of poison on his blades.

Relationships

For a while, Rurik traveled alongside the fallen paladin of Tyr Anders, the wizard Cyrel, and the ranger Trynn.

History

Rurik and his companions spent some time guarding a tollhouse on the Risen Road in the , when it was attacked by a band of vicious gnoll cultists. They managed to turn away the bestial raiders, while Rurik himself killed the largest of their force, but could not save the lives of the innocent toll takers.
