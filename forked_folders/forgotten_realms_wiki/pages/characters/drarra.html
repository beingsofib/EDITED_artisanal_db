Drarra was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Drarra was an elvish man with blue eyes and a greenish-olive skin tone. He wore fine-quality clothes and a big floppy beret decorated with two fanciful feathers.

History

In the , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, Drarra attended a performance of showy magics by three spellslingers, on the Murl's Rest plaza in front of Sorcerous Sundries. The elf eagerly devoured the show with his eyes as the most fun he's had in the Gate, plagues by disappearings, murders, cultists, and corrupt politicians.
