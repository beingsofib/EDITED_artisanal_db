Gnomon was a rakshasa lord and cultist of the Lords of Dust on the world of Eberron, who also had a persona as an Inquisitor of the Silver Flame.

Description

Gnomon had a human disguise that he used to infiltrate wider society as a stern Inquisitor of the Silver Flame.

Abilities

In addition to using his claws and teeth, Gnomon could cast blade barrier, cometfall, destruction, divine punishment, fire storm, and heal.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, Gnomon was a leading figure of the Lords of Dust. He infiltrated the Church of the Silver Flame and used his influence as an Inquisitor to investigate the church's secrets and sabotage their operations. He found the church's records of the Spinner's Prison—the secret and hidden dungeon that contained the Spinner of Shadows in Khyber—and put together a scheme to find and release her. Working with Karas and the cultists of the Stormreach cultist base, they put together a sacrificial ritual that would blast open the road to Tarath Marad and allow access to the ritual. Unfortunately for them, at the last moment the vigilant Brother Dectaran found the cult hideout and put together a team to raid it made up of Inquisitor Gnomon and Novice Corin Dryden, and supported by loyal adventurers. Gnomon led the assault and slew part of his own cult, then attempted to lure the others into danger by fighting the rest of the cult.

Retreating to the temple hidden below the base, Gnomon joined Karas and prepared to finish the ritual. After turning his followers into skeletons Gnomon joined battle himself but was slain, becoming part of the ritual as well. While Karas was amused at seeing Gnomon fall, he too was defeated, and this gave the ritual enough sacrifices to finish and open the way to the Spinner's Prison.
