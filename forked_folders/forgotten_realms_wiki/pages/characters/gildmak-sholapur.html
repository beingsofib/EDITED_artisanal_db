Gildmak Sholapur was a prominent halfling businessman in Luiren in 1367 DR. He was the leader of a group of brewers.

Activities

Gildmak's claim to fame was his brewery in Luiren, which was the largest of its kind throughout the entirety of the Shining South. The brewery specialized in the production of Luiren Stout.
