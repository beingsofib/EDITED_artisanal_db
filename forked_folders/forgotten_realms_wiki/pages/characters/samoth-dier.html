Samoth Dier was a Boar's Head, one of the leaders of the Order of the Blue Boar, mercenary recruitment company in Amn.

Description

Samoth was strong and tall and had a dark complexion. He was a very confident individual.

Samoth usually dressed in his artillery field gear.

Activities

Samoth was a very talented artillery expert. He was the chief logistics officer for the Order.

History

Samoth joined the council to replace Gaurundur Thasz, who died during a campaign in Tethyr.
