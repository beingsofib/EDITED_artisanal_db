Nrog was a goblin warrior cult of the Absolute and a denizen of the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

Description

He had yellow-green skin, decorated with blue body paint, and kept his head bald. He also bore the mark of the Absolute.

Possessions

Nrog wore goblin-made plate armor adorned with bones along with an open-faced helm. Nrog wielded a large battleaxe in combat.
