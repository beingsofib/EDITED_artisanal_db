Marlazander was the bodyguard of Rothglar Illance in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Marlazander was an immense, musclebound man, whose face reflected cold menace. He was also tall.

Personality

He could get somewhat touchy-feely with associates, gloated needlessly, and was a little quick to run away.

Activities

He was the bodyguard of Rothglar Illance.

History

Marlazander accompanied his master, Rothglar Illance, to the Open Feast of on Mirtul 26. He was also spotted later with Verrin, one of rival nobleman's Marlin Stormserpent's men, during the latter's attempt to steal the Wyverntongue Chalice from Castle Obarskyr in Suzail. Before he could do anything to Marlin, the latter unleashed his trained stirge, Thirsty, which slew Verrin immediately; Marlazander tried to flee, but was cut down by the undead knight Targrael before he got very far.
