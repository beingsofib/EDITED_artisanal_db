Isanan was a wizard from then city of Urmlaspyr in Sembia, who lived many years before the late 13 century DR.

History

Isanan was the third person to own a unique enchanted Myrkulyte belt – the Belt of Bones. He purchased the belt from an unnamed grave robber who desecrated and looted the final resting place of the fierce warrior named Proud Keth, the belt's original master. The Belt of Bones served Isanan well for many years after that.
