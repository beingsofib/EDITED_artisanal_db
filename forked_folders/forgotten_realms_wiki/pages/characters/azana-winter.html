Lady Azana Winter was the lord of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR, and a descendent of the previous Lady Tessaril Winter.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the 1480s DR, Lady Azana realized the dire situation her town was in. She sought out champions from adventurers passing through Eveningstar to rid the town of its drow enemies, asking them to work with the Purple Dragons of Constal Adamor Thellik, and War Wizards like Korrex Dunhaven and Xarathas.
