Lorris Niss was a half-elf acolyte at Candlekeep on the Sword Coast in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Lorris was middle aged.

History

In the , Loris was reading The Price of Beauty with her friend, a fellow acolyte named Falthrax Loderr, when he went missing. Lorris grew worried when she hadn't seen him in two days. Encountering some curious adventurers, she explained what had happened and enlisted their help.
