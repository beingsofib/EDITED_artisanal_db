Mrinabnahor was a black dragon who laired in the mountains west of the Moonsea. In 590 DR, he battled and killed Arms-Major Pirphal of Myth Drannor, along with most of his patrol. Pirphal's sword, Dragathil, was added to Mrinabnahor's hoard and remained lost for the next four centuries.
