Zelanz was a mercenary that served for a time as a member of the Company of Dawn adventuring group during the mid–14 century DR.

History

When the Company of Dawn accompanied friends of their leader Thurbrand through Spiderhaunt Wood in the , the group suffered heavily casualties and were nearly all killed. Zelanz fell when a giant red spider dropped down from a tree and killed him alongside his fellow mercenary Welch.
