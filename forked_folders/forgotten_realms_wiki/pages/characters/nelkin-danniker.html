Nelkin Danniker, better known as the Snail, was a member of the Zhentarim in Daggerford sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

Activities

Nelkin was the primary Zhent contact of the succubus Pencheska. Usually he spent his time at the River Shining Tavern where he was well protected.
