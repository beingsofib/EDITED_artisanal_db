Karsh Garmish was a member of the Gatekeepers in the world of Eberron.

Description

Karsh was an orcish man with gray-green skin.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, Gatekeeper Karsh was one of many who arrived to investigate the Spinner's Prison following the escape of the Spinner of Shadows. They found the Rift Between Worlds, a massive portal to the Demonweb Pits, and struggled to understand its nature while attempting to prevent it from threatening Eberron. Karsh and Zumosk Var lent their efforts to trying to magically seal the Rift, but their efforts could barely slow its growth.
