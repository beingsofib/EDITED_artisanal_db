Five Point was the name used by a powerful hybsil druid active in the Silver Marches during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

He was always accompanied by his animal companions: three Dire wolverines and an owl.

History

As of the , Five Point was the leader of the second-largest druid circle in the North. He had become infamous for posting bounties of thirteen gold pieces for the scalps of Zhentarim members in retaliation for their practice of posting bounties on hybasil antlers.
