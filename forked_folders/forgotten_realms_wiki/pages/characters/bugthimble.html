Bugthimble was a grave robbing kobold in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Bugthimble was a kobold with pale orange scales and makeshift armor made of leather, horns, and fangs.

Possessions

Bugthimble wielded the enchanted scimitar called Kurwin's Cauteriser.

History

In the , Bugthimble led a small gang of kobold gave robbers to dig around the graves and coffins of the Lower City Graveyard of Baldur's Gate, including Antunio Bongle's final resting place. Bugthimble operated outside of the other criminal organizations of the Gate and was intimidated by the Guild and its notorious leader, Nine-Fingers Keene.
