Narnorn was mysterious thief who ruled Mreyelundar in 1479 DR.

Background

Narnorn was a thief of legendary status in Laerakond, and its true identity was unknown. It was the true power behind the throne of Mreyelundar, although Trusk Rantyne was in charge of day-to-day government of the city. All the gangs in the city also swore fealty to Narnorn.
