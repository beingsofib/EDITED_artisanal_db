Tekun, who was nicknamed the Warlord, was a kobold chieftain inhabiting the High Country of Rashemen during the late 14 century DR.

Activities

With over two hundred kobolds under his command, Tekun was regarded to be the greatest kobold chieftain in the region. His white dragon lineage lured him to follow Auril, the Frostmaiden.
