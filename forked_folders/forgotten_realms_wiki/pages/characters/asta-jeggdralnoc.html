Asta Jegg'Dralnoc was a powerful Chosen of Lolth of House Jegg'Dralnoc, who wandered the Demonweb Pits during the 15 century DR.

Description

Asta styled her long white hair back in a heavy ponytail and small crown, and wore a daring ensemble of a collar-and-midriff-baring bustenhalt with sabatons and a spiderleg-themed flair that framed her from behind.

Abilities

Asta could call upon a number of divine powers, including heal, death ward, mass, cometfall, and divine punishment. She could also innately see invisibility and sacrifice nearby allies to Lolth to receive a powerful boon from her goddess.

History

During the Darkening and Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Asta wandered the Demonweb Pits, fighting intruders in Lolth's domain.
