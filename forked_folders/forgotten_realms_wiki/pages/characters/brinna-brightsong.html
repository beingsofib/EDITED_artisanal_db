Brinna Brightsong was a halfling bard who lived and performed in Triel during the late 15 century DR.

Description

She had a prominent nose, hazel eyes, and light blonde hair.

Activities

She was known for performing some rather bawdy and ultimately controversial songs.

History

Brinna met her untimely end in the . She was killed while performing at a tavern in Triel, by a cultist of the Absolute. She was among the dozen individuals plucked from the Fugue Plane, chosen by the entity Withers to aid in his campaign against the Dead Three.
