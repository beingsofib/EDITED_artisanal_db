Thrane Mettleworth was a human fighter and adventurer in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Thrane was a very large and strong man, bedecked in chainmail armor.

History

Shortly after the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, Thrane traveled the High Road and had the misfortune of stepping in a bear trap because his rogue was drunk. With his foot firmly bitten and mangled, he was taken to Beckside Castle, a campsite used by wayfarers. Though he was tended to by the cleric Aya Ironhart, her treatment and bedside manner left much to be desired.
