Vlotho the Blue was a half-elf gambler who lived in the city of Harglast, in the Windrise Ports of Laerakond, as of 1479 DR.

History

Vlotho was a known gambler and gossiper in Harglast, Vlotho was a regular customer of the Leaping Hart Tavern.
