Rhangaun was a male lich and a senior member of the Twisted Rune.

Description

Dead for more than a thousand years, he was little more than a skeleton with glowing eyes.

Possessions

He owned a staff of the magi.

History

In his mortal life, Rhangaun of Almraiven was hired to kill Cauln Aumar of Athalantar in 217 DR.

Forced into the Twisted Rune because Rysellan possessed his phylactery, Rhangaun took control after the founder’s death.
