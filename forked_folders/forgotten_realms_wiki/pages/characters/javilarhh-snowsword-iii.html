King Javilarhh "the Dark" Snowsword III was a king of Delimbiyran in the 7 century DR.

Relationships

Javilarhh's ancestor was King Javilarhh Snowsword II, and he had several children, with the later King Davyd Snowsword being his grandson. His youngest son was Erthaer Javilarhhsson.
