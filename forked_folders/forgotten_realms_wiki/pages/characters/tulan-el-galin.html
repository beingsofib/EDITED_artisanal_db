Tulan el Galin was an official in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR. He was a druzir of Khumil Drudach and an important member of the Circle of Architects, Engineers, and Builders. Naturally, his house had three stories with an adjoining four-story minaret. It stood in the heart of Khumil Drudach, Otahl Sabban, in the city's Emerald Ward.
