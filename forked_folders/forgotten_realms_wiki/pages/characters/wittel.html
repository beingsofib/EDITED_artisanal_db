Wittel was a teamster in Hillsfar in the Moonsea in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Wittel was a teamster who worked transferring goods from the farms around Hillsfar to the city.

History

Circa 1486 DR, his wife Dottee announced that she was pregnant and they decided to go for a trip in the Cormanthor forest. There, a family of jackalweres ambushed them: Wittel was left unconscious and Dottee taken.
