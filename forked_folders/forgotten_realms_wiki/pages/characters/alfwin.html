Alfwin was a Shadovar sorcerer serving under the Netherese emissary Herzgo Alegni in the mid–14 century DR.

History

He was part of Herzgo's retinue when, in the , the Shadovar ambushed Dahlia Sin'felle and Drizzt Do'Urden in Gauntlgrym.
