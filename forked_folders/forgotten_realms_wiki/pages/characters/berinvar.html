Berinvar was a fighter who served in the Athkatlan cell of the Harpers in the 1360s DR.

History

As of the , Berinvar and his allies were hunting down the necromancer Prebek, who served under an agent of the Zhentarim.

Relationships

Berinvar operated within the same Harper cell as Rylock and the nobleman Galvarey. They were headquartered at Galvarey's grand estate in Athkatla's docks.
