Abilities

He had proven inexplicably resistant to electricity.

History

In , Orochal was the first guardian to whelm a force against the Simbul when she tried to rescue Elminster Aumar from Avernus. Though her first spell didn't damage him much, and left him laughing in fact, her second spell tore him apart.
