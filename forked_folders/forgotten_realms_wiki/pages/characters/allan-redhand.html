Allan Redhand was an adventurer in the ruin of Spellgard.

Personality

Allan was not bright, and had little patience for social interaction and intrigues. He was happy following Devlin's orders, not caring for the consequences.

History

Allan joined the Free Company of the Crescent after Devlin happened to save Allan's sister. In 1479 DR, he went with his fellow to Spellgard hoping to find Lady Saharel's treasures.
