Huedyn was a traitorous half-elven druid inhabiting the Dernall Forest in Callidyrr in the Moonshae Isles in the late 15 century DR.

Abilities

His magic could empower his beast and goblin allies, and allowed him to move freely through dense woodland. He favored the entangle and thorn whip spells.

Possessions

He wore leather armor and carried a spear.

Activities

Huedyn was part of a group of druids under Great Druid Antola Pilark who were allied with House Kendrick's forces against the dark fey infesting the Dernall Forest.

In truth, by the , Huedryn was actually a spy for the dark fey of Nachtur, and was feeding information to the them while trying to expose the forces of Callidyrr to attack. He had become central to the fey's plans such that losing him would have been a major blow.
