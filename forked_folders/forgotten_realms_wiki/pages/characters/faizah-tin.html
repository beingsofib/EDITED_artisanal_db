Faizah Tin was a mage who lived in the Realms during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was somewhat of a cynic, believing that others would not do good merely for the sake of it.

Relationships

Faizah Tin was an acquaintance of an elven warrior named Vail.

History

At some point, Faizah joined Vail on a venture that saw them and some other mercenaries storming the tower of the famous wizard Ramazith Flamesinger. When they stormed the mage's tower in the Temples District of Baldur's Gate, the entire party was petrified by a basilisk.

Having been turned into stone, the lot of them were sold to an art collector by the name of Felonius Gist. The statues were considered quite the unique pieces of art in luxurious surroundings of the Gist noble estate in the city.
