Thuldra Thuldrin was a dwarf commoner in the late 15 century DR who was displaced by the Absolute and, as a refugee, sought shelter in Baldur's Gate.

Description

Thuldra was a stout clean-shaved dwarven woman with freckled olive skin.

History

In the , the army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldur's Gate, displacing countless refugees along the way in the process. Thuldra Thuldrin found her way to the Rivington district outside the city walls, but, like all other refugees, she was refused entrance. Being a kind soul, Thuldra worked with the Flaming Fist to feed the refugees in the camp, and asked Father Lorgan of the Open Hand Temple for carrots, apples, or anything they could spare for the starving refugees.
