Ruldigul Prestar was an innkeep inhabiting Suldolphor in the far eastern reaches of Calimshan in south Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Ruldigul was the proprietor of the popular Bright Shield Inn located in southwest Suldolphor. He maintained a school to train his extensive staff for the many services the Bright Shield Inn provided.

Rumors & Legends

It was whispered the Ruldigul was actually a disguised mind flayer, or at least was in contact with the illithids.
