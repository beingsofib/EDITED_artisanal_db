Tuloria was a middle class inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 century DR.

Description

Tuloria was a human woman with a light skin tone and green eyes. Her red hair was kept long and she wore quality embroidered clothes of maroon color, decorated with images of firebirds.

History

In the , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, the gates between the Lower City of Baldur's Gate and its more affluent Upper City were closed, monitored by the Flaming Fist mercenaries and allowing only the wealthy and current residents of the Upper City through. Tuloria was among those Baldurians who sought entrance into the Upper City. To gain entrance, Tuloria sold most everything she owned, hoping to be able to find a rental room in the safety of the city's upper-crust neighborhood.
