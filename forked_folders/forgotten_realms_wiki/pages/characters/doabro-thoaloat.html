Doabro Thoaloat

Person
nameDoabro Thoaloat
homeFaerûn

Doabro Thoaloat was a mage at some point prior to the 8 century DR.

Legacy

Thoaloat's Variant was an alteration of some spell the Srinshee, an elven baelnorn, taught Elminster Aumar, a Chosen of Mystra; the spell could duplicate and reshape a tree at the same time. She taught it using a rhyme about Doabro Thoaloat.
