Bransuldyn Mirrortor was a gnome cleric of Oghma in Berdusk in 1359 DR.

Activities

Bransuldyn, a former adventurer, managed the local temple to Oghma. Often he used his collection of disguises and went unnoticed in the city gathering lore and listening. Then, using some personally created spells, Bransuldyn recorded what he had learned.

Rumors

Rumors said that Bransuldyn still adventured and he had found passages to the Underdark, where he had put his treasure, which was protected by a deadly trap.
