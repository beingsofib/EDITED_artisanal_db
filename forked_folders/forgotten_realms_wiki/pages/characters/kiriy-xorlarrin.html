Kiriy Xorlarrin was the eldest daughter of Matron Mother Zeerith Q'Xorlarrin in the mid–14 century DR and First Priestess of House Xorlarrin in 1484 DR.

History

She was killed by Artemis Entreri when Jarlaxle, he and Drizzt came to rescue Dhalia from the House Do'Urden compound.
