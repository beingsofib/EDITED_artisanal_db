Tresset was a bandit lord at some point prior to the mid–8 century DR. He founded Tressardon.

History

After becoming a bandit of some success, Tresset attempted to establish the kingdom of Tressardon to control a territory of his own. He had a slender-spired castle built, the Ringyl, from where he ruled; the town of Tresset's Ringyl was built around it. However, the kingdom came apart shortly after his death.
