Baran al-Amin al-Jinn was a janni who lived in Al-Yabki Mountains in Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Activities

As a priest of Kor, Baran donned the robes of a qadi and began acting as a judge at a sacred site in the mountains near Turab and Sikak. He was popular among Al-Badia, many of whom traveled considerable distances to discuss their problems with him.
