Halbasho the Smart was a common oni inhabiting Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert during the mid–14th century DR.

History

Halbasho led a group of wicked oni in seizing control of the Horseshoe Temple around 1358 DR.

Activities

Halbasho supervised the other nine oni and enslaved goblins as they attempted to convert a temple for the worship of Li Pei, the lord of the underworld.

Possessions

Halbasho owned a bag of dust of illusion, a potion of invisibility, and a magical +3 battleaxe, and set of +2 full plate armor of Shou make. He made his den in the Lesser Hall of Perfect Understanding, the quarters of the former abbot.
