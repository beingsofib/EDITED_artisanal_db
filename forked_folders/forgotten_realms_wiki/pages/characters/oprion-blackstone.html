Oprion Blackstone was a Banite priest in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He had a wintry gaze.

Abilities

He knew how to scry. Whether personally or through others, Manshoon believed him capable of casting resurrection.

Possessions

He claimed mageslayer after Fzoul Chembryl's death.

Activities

He took control of the priestly side of the Zhentarim after the deaths of Elthaulin and Fzoul Chembryl.

History

Oprion Blackstone was one of the priests that Elthaulin called on to seize control of the Citadel after Fzoul engaged in combat with Shandril Shessair.

After Shandril's last frontal assault on Zhentil Keep, causing the death of Fzoul Chembryl, Oprion impersonated Manshoon and sent a speaking stone to Eveningstar, which carried a message from him impersonating Manshoon to Shandril. In the message, he made the claim that the Brotherhood would no longer seek her spellfire. While scrying on the stone, he caught some of a conversation with Elminster. Then the real Manshoon turned up and told Oprion to get on with resurrecting Fzoul.

He later contemplated the possibility of ignoring Manshoon's coming request for parley after Shandril's death, only to be warned by local beholders that they would kill him for that.
