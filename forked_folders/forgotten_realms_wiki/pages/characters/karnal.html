Karnal was a wight working for the Necrophants in the Chantry of St. Sammaster in the early late 14 century DR.

Description

He had burning yellow eyes.

Abilities

Besides his abilities as a wight, he had been endowed with the ability to command the undead dragons Oskurouh and Larghauseth.

Activities

Karnal remained at the Temple of the Dragon Oracle during the early 1370s DR, as a guardian for the dignity of its dead dragons.

Relationships

He served the local necrophants and had as his wards the undead dragons Oskurouh and Larghauseth.

History

At some point on or after , a cell of necrophants encouraged the wight Karnal to remain at the old Temple of the Dragon Oracle, where he would watch over the dignity of the dead dragons from the Cult of the Dragon.
