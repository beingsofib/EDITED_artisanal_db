Toyo Otomoto was a sea captain in Kozakura in 1357 DR.

History

Otomoto was a very experienced captain in service of Shou Ko clan for a long time commanding the "Joyful Spring" ship. He made numerous travels between Kozakura and far Akari island, where Shou Lung was constructing a colony. In 1357 Otomoto transported an adventuring party hired by Ko Mei Kho to stop the Ochimo.
