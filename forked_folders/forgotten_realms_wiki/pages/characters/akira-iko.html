Akira Iko was an adventurer from Shou Lung in Kara-Tur. After serving a defeated feudal lord, he became a ronin adventuring in Ravens Bluff.

Description

He had black hair and brown eyes, with skin tinged slight yellow.

Personality

He was grim and direct, though he tried to curb his usual arrogance.

Abilities

His skill with the greatbow and the katana were superlative; he was also quite effective with the wakizashi, fighting with two swords, and even martial arts. He had notable strength, ideal for a warrior.

Possessions

Of note, he bore a katana and wakizashi that were both enchanted heirlooms, and a regular greatbow. He also carried a pair of potions of healing and a potion of climbing.

Relationships

While a samurai, Akira Iko served Akira Hanko, his uncle.

Akira Iko was friendly with his companions, thinking Tanis Obereck dependable, and believing Taldar Everwind to be somewhat too loose with his sexuality, but deciding not to hold that against him; he even knew that Taldar could raise his spirits sometimes. He considered Crakadan Earthdigger to be a serious, good friend. He respected Serena Worthwood, and found Aliana Meriadeth's demeanor more familiar, closer to the women of his home.

History

Once a samurai, Iko was one of seven such warriors who protected the lord's infant son. But after two years of controversy and political maneuvering, their master was blamed for the razing of two villages, prompting an attack on his castle. They sent the child to live with relatives in T'u Lung in the south. Iko later learned he was a wanted man, and fled along the Golden Spice Road to Faerûn.

Akira Iko reached Ravens Bluff and became an adventurer at the age of twenty-six. Notably, he was the one to introduce Crakadan to the rest of the group.
