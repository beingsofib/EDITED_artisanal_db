Samuel Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid–7 century DR.

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the uncle of his predecessor, High King Rowland the Boy King. His elder brother, Alec I, had been Rowland's predecessor and his father, Embro II, had been High King before that. Samuel's successor was his first son, Warren II.

History
