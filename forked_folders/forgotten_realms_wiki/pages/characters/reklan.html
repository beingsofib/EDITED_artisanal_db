Reklan was a sorcerer guard who worked at the Szarr Palace of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Reklan was a human man with facial tattoos and light skin. He wore robes typical for sorcerers.

History

In the , Reklan was one of three enthralled sentries who stood guard of the entrance to the massive Szarr Palace – home of vampire lord Cazador Szarr located in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. On the day of the accession ritual, the palace's chamberlain Antwun Dufay‎ ordered no visitor nor delivery to be allowed an entrance, promising a "reward" from Cazador upon the ritual's completion.
