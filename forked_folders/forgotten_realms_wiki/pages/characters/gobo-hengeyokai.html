Gobo was a myoshu in Kozakura in 1357 DR.

Activities

Gobo led the village of Ibara; most people suspected he was a cat hengeyokai. He kept his villagers in line thanks to his wu jen powers. Meanwhile, he trying to learn the secrets of Nora-ko.
