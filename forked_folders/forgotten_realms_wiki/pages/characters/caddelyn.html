Caddelyn was a powerful wizard and the author of Caddelyn's Workbook.

History

He inhabited his own tower located on a mountain north of the Great Glacier. Caddelyn was a well-respected wizard who died peacefully while visiting Waterdeep long before the mid–14 century DR. He was entombed within the City of the Dead there.
