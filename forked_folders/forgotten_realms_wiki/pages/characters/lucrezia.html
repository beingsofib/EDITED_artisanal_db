Lucrezia was a mid-ranking cleric of Tiamat living in Unther in 1374 DR.

Activities

Lucrezia was a celebrant at the Altar of Scales temple in Unthalass. However, she was a spy sent by Tiglath from Messemprar to investigate Malise's experiments.

History

Lucrezia arrived in Unthalass from Messemprar in 1374.

Personality

Lucrezia was very cautious in her spying and her cover worked. However she feared that Stendak suspected something.
