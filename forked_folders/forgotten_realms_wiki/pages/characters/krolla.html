Krolla was a goblin that dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.

We already got the greatest art there is – and it ain't no warbling. It's chicken-chasin'.

Description

Like other faithful of the Absolute, Krolla was branded with a mark given to her by Priestess Gut.

Activities

Krolla enjoyed and possibly invented the sadistic game of chicken-chasin', during which a player would have to chase a chicken through a hazardous obstacle course towards a set of goalposts. She would regularly bet on the outcomes of the games and resorted to cheating to ensure her winnings if necessary.

Interestingly enough, she did not the know the difference between an owlbear cub and an oversized chicken.
