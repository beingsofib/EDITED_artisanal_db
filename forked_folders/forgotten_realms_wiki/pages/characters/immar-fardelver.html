Immar Fardelver was an artisan of Delzoun in the 4th century before Dalereckoning.

History

In the Year of Seven Spirits, −335 DR, Immar Fardelver was commissioned by Maerin of Illusk, alongside many other dwarven artisans from Delzoun, for the building of Gauntlgrym. The work was completed in the Year of Hollow Hills, −321 DR.
