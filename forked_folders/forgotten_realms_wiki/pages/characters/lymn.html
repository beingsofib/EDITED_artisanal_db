Lymn was a tiefling Harper who followed the Harper hero Jaheira to the Shadow-cursed lands in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Lymn was a not-very-talkative hellish-looking crimson-skinned female tiefling with burning red eyes. The tiefling wore padded leather armor that proudly displayed her Harper pin..

History

In the , Jaheira was alerted to the mysterious return of Ketheric Thorm, now a leader of a cultist army that worshiped the Absolute. Harper Lymn was one of the agents who accompanied Jaheira into the Shadow-cursed lands surrounding the Moonrise Towers—General Thorm's seat of power.

When a group of True Souls arrived at the Last Light Inn, which the Harpers had claimed as a temporary base, Lymn was among those present for the events that followed. It was unknown if Harper Lymn survived the war with the Absolute; she might've perished after the Last Light Inn was consumed by the darkness or else she survived with the rest, storming the Moonrise Towers to end General Thorm's reign.
