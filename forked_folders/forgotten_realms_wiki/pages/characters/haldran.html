Hadran was a high priest of Sharess who oversaw the Temple of Sharess in the Thayan city of Bezantur in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Haldran led the acolytes of the temple, and personally led some of the daily services. His sermons emphasized the importance of enjoying life and indulging in hedonism.
