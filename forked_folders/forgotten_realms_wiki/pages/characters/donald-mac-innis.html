Donald Mac Innis was king of Langmuir on the island of Moray in the Moonshae Isles during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

He ruled with the aid of his council, which included wealthy merchants, minor nobles, and his own sons, notably Keeron Mac Innis.

History

As of , King Donald was old and sickly, and had come to defer leadership of Langmuir fully to his council.
