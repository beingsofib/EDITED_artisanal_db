Jestra was a human woman of Arabel in Cormyr and an independent wizard specializing in the transmutation school of magic.

History

Around 1369 DR, she resided in Arabel, Cormyr, owned properties in Arabel and utilized her magic to increase the attractiveness of her clients as well as to improve various objects brought to her by enhancing the object's appearance and usefulness.
