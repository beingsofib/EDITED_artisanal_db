Stromm was a Northlander king of the realm of Norland in the Moonshae Isles during the early-to-mid–7 century DR.

History

In the , a political marriage between King Stromm's people and their longstanding enemies, the Ffolk, fell apart when the Ffolk princess and bride-to-be, Bridget of Callidyrr, vanished en route to Rogarsheim. The Ffolk High King Embro Cymrych II blamed the Northlanders while King Stromm blamed the Ffolk. The two would eventually meet in battle in the , and Embro II would lose his life.
