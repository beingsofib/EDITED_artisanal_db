Klaire Gatewood was a famed artist of Cormyr.

A book of sketches by Klaire Gatewood was in the possession of Lady of Mystery Naedaenya Arthas at the Temple of Mystra in Wheloon, Cormyr, in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR. It was worth 200 gp to the right buyer.
