Mystia Hollygrove was an elf adventurer and wizard in Cormyr during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Mystia had long brown hair in a braid, and wore a bright white and blue robe bedecked with books, reagents, potions, and scrolls.

History

Mystia stayed at the ruined Beckside Castle on the High Road around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, and kept Slade Destinaxe company while he complained about the bard Lyrica Whitestone. Mystia gave up on doing anything but agreeing with the dwarf, resigned to the inevitability of his bad mood.
