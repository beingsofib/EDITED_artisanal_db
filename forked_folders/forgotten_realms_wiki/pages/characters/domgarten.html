Domgarten was a young elf scout during the late 15th century DR.

History

Domgarten was one of the elf scouts of the Moonwood during the War of the Silver Marches of 1484 DR–1485 DR. He went with the top scout Myriel to inspect the ground of a battle between dwarves and orcs.
