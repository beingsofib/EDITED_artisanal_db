Sarnjack was an inhabitant of the Dalelands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was recognizable for his weathered face.

Personality

The man was rowdy and lusty.

Activities

He worked as a ring-maker after retiring from farming. At that time, he also became a regular at the Old Skull Inn.

History

Once a farmer from Mistledale, Sarnjack had retired from farming to become a ring-maker in Shadowdale by . In that year, he was getting a little handsy with Storm Silverhand.
