Corlin Cardin () was the apprentice of Vajra Safahr, the seventh Blackstaff of Waterdeep, during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Corlin had a gentle personality. He was proud and honored to be the apprentice of Vajra, and showed a strong loyalty to her.
