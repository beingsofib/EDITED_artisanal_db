Suluq was an enslaved efreeti in Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Activities

Suluq was trapped within a ring of genie summoning and did his master's bidding whenever he was called.

Relationships

Contrary to most enslaved genies, Suluq actually liked his master, Fasim Kaldur. Suluq and Fasim got along quite well since Fasim treated him fairly and frequently allowed him to exercise his thirst for committing violence.
