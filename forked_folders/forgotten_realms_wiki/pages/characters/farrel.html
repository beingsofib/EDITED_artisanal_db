Farrel was a successful shopkeeper inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

He owned Farrel's Fine Jewelry and Apparel, a large jewelry store located in central Daggerford along the Hill Road. Farrel had a keen eye for color and excelled at rematching precious gems acquired through questionable methods.
