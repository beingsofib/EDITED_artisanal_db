Bhuraelea was an elven sorceress of Cormanthyr in the 3 century DR.

History

Bhuraelea was selected to protect Mythanthor by the Srinshee in after the death of the human armathor, Elminster Aumar. While guarding his tower, she was ambushed and felled by a number of elven mages bent on slaying her charge.
