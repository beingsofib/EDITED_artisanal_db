Amanthuras the Benificient was a human proxy of the Faerûnian power known as Ilmater. He was considered to be among the foremost of Ilmater's regular stable of proxies.

Description

Amanthuras was typically dressed in gray robes, along with a blood-red skullcap and blood-red cords wound around his wrists.

Activities

This proxy traveled to wherever his clerical services were most needed. This could include Toril, the world of Ilmater's worshipers, or even the planar city of Sigil.
