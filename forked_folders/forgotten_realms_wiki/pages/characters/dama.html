Dama was a rebel leader in Unther in 1357 DR.

History

Once Dama was a gladiator in Chessenta but was captured by Thayan slavers and sold to a freeholder in Unther.

In 1357 DR, he became one of the main leaders of the slave revolt in Unther. His goal was to destroy the church of Gilgeam and create as much chaos as possible.
