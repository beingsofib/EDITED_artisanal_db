Brynn Osstra was a farmer that lived in Nightstone with his family during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Brynn and his husband Thelbin farmed wheat within the safe confines of the walled village they called home. The couple lived in a cozy cottage with Brynn's mother, sister, and his nephew Broland.

History

Some time during the latter half of the 1480s DR, the Osstra family home was partially destroyed when cloud giants assailed Nightstone with stones from the sky. The village's residents fled for shelter in a nearby cave network but encountered a band of goblins led by Boss Hark. Many of the townsfolk were slain while others were taken as prisoners.

While Brynn, Thelbin, and Broland managed to survive the traumatic ordeal, Brynn's mother and sister were not so fortunate.
