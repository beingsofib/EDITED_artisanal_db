Sudai Calin lived within the Tower of Adnan al-Raqi in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Activities

Sudai was a dwarven sha'ir and instructor for Adnan al-Raqi's students. He also spent time learning the higher powers of the sha'ir with Adnan and the other instructors.
