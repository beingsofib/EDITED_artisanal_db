Scratchy was one of two crawling claw undead pets kept by Lucretious, the proprietress of of the Circus of the Last Days in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Scratchy was an amputated reanimated hand of a humanoid creature with blueish or lavender skin. It had sharp nails and wore a single ring.

Abilities

Like other crawling claws, Scratchy had the usual strengths and vulnerabilities of an undead creature. It was unaffected by poison and possessed a blindsight.

History

In the , Scratchy and another fellow crawling claw named Itchy could be found in Lucretious's tent at the back end of the area in Rivington of Baldur's Gate, where the Circus of the Last Days performed for a short period of time, before moving on across the planes.
