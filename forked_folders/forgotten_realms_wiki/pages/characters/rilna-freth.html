Rilna Freth was the younger sister of Erelal Freth in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR.

Activities

Rilna was the castellan of Spiderwatch Keep, the main Freth headquarters inside the Maze Level. She was in charge of the defense of the keep around the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR.
