Briez was a resident of Phlan that worked as a server at Madame Freona's Tea Kettle during the late 15 century DR. She was one of five daughter's the restaurant's owner and proprietress, Madame Freona.

Description

She had notably long black hair.

Activities

Among their other duties, Briez and her sisters would pick wildberries for jam to be served on biscuits.

Relationships

Briez' other sisters were named Blaizette, Grelinda, Reece, and Whittlee.
