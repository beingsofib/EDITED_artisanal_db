Turcan was the stablemaster of the noble Pharaboldi family of Arrabar circa 1373 DR.

History

Pharaboldi were renowned for their passion for horses. The family kept a collection of beautiful beasts of various colors peacefully grazing on the lawns of the Pharaboldi estate. Turcan, along with a handful of stablehands, was in charge of caring for the horses and outfitting them to be ridden by the noble masters and their guests. Among the steeds Turcan cared for were a palomino named Goldy and a bay named Shert.
