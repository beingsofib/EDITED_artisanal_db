Valdenar Octi was a dragonborn paladin of Bahamut and a platinum knight.

Description

Valdenar had gold scales.

History

Before he underwent the Rite of Rebirth, Valdenar was a Chondathan human. After his transformation, he became an ally of the Sisterhood of Essembra.
