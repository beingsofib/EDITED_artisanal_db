Falstaff was a doomguide who lived in the city of Neverwinter during the late 15 century DR.

History

He was one a member of the contingent of Kelemvorite forces sent to investigate the Neverdeath Graveyard in Neverwinter circa the . He and some of his fellows were lost in an area known as the Rift after an assault by undead and their Red Wizards masters. Falstaff was killed by a volley of arrows.

Some time later Doomguide Volahk hired an adventurer to find his missing comrades in the city's graveyard district. After finding Falstaff's corpse, the adventurer burned it so that it could not be raised as an undead abomination by the Thayan necromancers.
