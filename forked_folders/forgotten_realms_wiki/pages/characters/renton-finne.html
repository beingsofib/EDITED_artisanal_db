Renton Finne was a Harper agent who worked in Eveningstar, Cormyr, in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Renton was notable for his very bald head, and large lute.

History

After the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon during the 1480s DR, Renton joined Kara Patwin and Lorant Tass in investigating the Storm Horns and Amskar's army of monsters. Once it was clear that an enormous army was gathered with fell intent, Renton found that a key frost giant leader, Rungnir, had grown dissatisfied with his treatment by the Shadovar. He immediately sent a team up to Rungnir's glacier to meet the giant, only to find that Rungnir had already been driven into hiding. They then helped him crush Archwizard Tharno in revenge, leading to Rungnir's clan following him in rebellion and further weakening the Netherese forces.
