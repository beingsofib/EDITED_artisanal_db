Lionel Carrathal was heir to the defunct Carrathal Dynasty of Callidyrr in the Moonshae Isles as of the turn of the 15 century DR. He claimed to be the "true king" of the islands as opposed to the ruling Kendrick Dynasty.

Relationships

Lionel was the son and heir of Brynth Carrathal, the bastard son of King Reginald Carrathal and an Amnian woman.

History

After King Reginald's death in the , the Carrathal Dynasty fell out of power, however the line of succession passed to Brynth in Amn. When Brynth died in the , Lionel became the heir.

Following the turn of the 15 century DR, around the same time that Amn was spreading its influence in the Moonshae Isles and was being blamed for stoking civil unrest against the Kendrick family, Lionel was making known his claim to be the "true" High King of the United Moonshae Isles.
