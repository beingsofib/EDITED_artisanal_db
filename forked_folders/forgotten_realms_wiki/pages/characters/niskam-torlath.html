King Niskam Torlath "the Doomed" was an unfortunate Amnian monarch of the Torlath dynasty.

Relationships

Niskam was the great-grandson of King Imnel IV.

History

Niskam took the throne in the , following the assassination of King Imnel IV by the Skeletal Finger thieves' guild. Niskam failed to last the year, and was himself slain by agents of the Braethar merchant house who then installed Rhor Torlath as a puppet.
