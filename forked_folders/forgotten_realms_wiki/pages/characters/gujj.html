Gujj was a Bhaalist doppelganger member of the Murder Tribunal in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

His his human guise, Gujj appeared to be an affluent, dusky man with long black hair. When the disguise was dropped, Gujj looked like the rest of his pale kin.

History

In the , Gujj and several other doppelgangers accompanied a Bhaalist murderer Dolor to assassinate and mutilate the Figaro Pennygood, the proprietor of the Facemaker's Boutique targeted by the church. In case unwanted eyes visited the store, Gujj played the role of an impatient customer who was in line to see the fabulous Figaro. Gujj was presumed killed by a group of adventurers known as the Absolute Adversaries who were investigating the series of murderers perpetrated by the Murder Tribunal.
