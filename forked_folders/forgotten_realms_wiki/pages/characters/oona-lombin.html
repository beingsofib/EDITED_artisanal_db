Oona Lombin was a deep gnome merchant in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Oona was a gray-skinned woman with gray eyes and wavy white hair in a short mullet cut. She wore a simple brown and white dress.

History

In the , Oona joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their forward camp at the outskirts of Myth Drannor, alongside their drow, human, and dwarf allies. Oona quickly set up a simple general store with a small alchemy set in her tent where she sold all sorts of basic supplies and gear to the soldiers, pilgrims, and adventurers who passed through the camp.
