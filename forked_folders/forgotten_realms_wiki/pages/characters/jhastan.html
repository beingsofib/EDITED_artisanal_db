Jhastan was a skilled fighter and member of the Black Gauntlet, an adventuring company based out of Starmantle active during the 13 century DR.

History

Jhastan was with the Black Gauntlet when they slew the mighty green dragon Chathuulandroth in his lair. He met his end later at the hands of a demon that was summoned improperly.
