Boeddu was a human druid, adventurer, and planeswalker of the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He had long brown curly hair.

Possessions

He wore a set of leather armor, and wielded either a sling or a wooden shield and silver scimitar. His most prized possession however was a donkey he called Schmek. He was given the donkey as a reward by a rich merchant after preventing said merchant from being cheated by a local businessman.

History

After meeting Vexter, Master of Illusion, Boeddu became an adventurer and planeswalker

Activities

He joined Vexter in his adventures across the planes and began to realize how many wonders existed outside his native home.
