Rhieldra Zoldaftel was a sorcerer who lived in Crimmor during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. She was the unmarried daughter of the infamous wagon maker Zan Zoldaftel.

Activities

Rhieldra was the business manager of Zoldaftel Wagons. She placed magical dweomers on the wagons built by her father to prevent counterfeits.
