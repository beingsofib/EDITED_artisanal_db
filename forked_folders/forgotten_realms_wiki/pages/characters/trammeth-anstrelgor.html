Trammeth Anstrelgor was a famous cartographer that lived during the 12 century DR. He authored what was considered the most famous single collection of maps, A Manyrealms Gallimaufry.

History

Trammeth Anstrelgor died around the . He was buried with the original copy of his magnum opus.
