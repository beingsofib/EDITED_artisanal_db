Andemel Graeven was a Cormyrean noble in the mid-14 century DR.

Personality

He was quite given to joking about his fellow nobles.

Possessions

His home had a garden, and he owned at least one shop.

Relationships

He was a friend of Sabrast Windriver. He had not stopped shuddering for a tenday after the first time he met Elminster Aumar,

History

During a revel in , Andemel talked to Sabrast Windriver and Raurild Sarpath about his shop. He also considered propositioning a lady in a particularly revealing dress; Sabrast warned him the lady was actually Elminster. He also became a witness to Immult Murauvyn trying to collect back taxes on Sabrast Windriver.
