Daral Yashenti was one of the eight Great Readers of the Avowed that served Candlekeep during the late 15 century DR. As such Daral was partly responsible for the day-to-day operations of the great library.

Daral was considered to be Candlekeep's resident expert on creative arts, such as literature, music and poetry, and was considered a skilled poet.
