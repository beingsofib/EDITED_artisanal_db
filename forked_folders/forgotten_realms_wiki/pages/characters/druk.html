Druk was a goblin warrior and devotee of the Absolute that dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

He was fairly well-mannered for a goblin, at least to those that were favored by the Absolute.

History

In the , Druk remained at the Shattered Sanctum when the leaders of the Shattered Sanctum began the hunt for certain individuals that were previously on the crashed nautiloid that landed near the River Chionthar.
