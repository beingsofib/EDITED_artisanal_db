Hazaufein Oblodra was the House Wizard of House Oblodra and Elderboy of Matron Mother K'yorl Odran circa 1358 DR.

Personality

Hazaufein was often mocked by his brother Drizfryn Oblodra, who said that his ability to craft was the result of being part-dwarf, but Hazaufein always accepted these jests with a wan smile.

Powers

Hazaufein was a very good crafter of magic items.

Activities

The position of house wizard was often given little consideration among the psionically powered Oblodras. Hazaufein's main duty was to create magical arms and armors for the house's warriors.
