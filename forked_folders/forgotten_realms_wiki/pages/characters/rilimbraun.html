Rilimbraun was a cleric of Tyr and one of leaders of both the Knights of Imphras II as well as the Lords of Imphras II that reigned over Impiltur during the 14 century DR.

History

After the crowning of Imbrar II, Rilimbraun and his fellow lord Soargilm retired from public life, acting only as advisers to Queen-Regent Sambryl.
