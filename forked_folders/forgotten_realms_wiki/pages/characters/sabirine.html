Sabirine was a mage and the author of Sabirine's Specular, a spellbook with multiple useful and powerful cantrips.

Activities

Sabirine had an interest in cantrip spells and advocated for the continued use and refinement of them by magic-users, regardless of their power.

Possessions

Sabirine possessed multiple spellbooks hidden around Faerûn.

History

She was instrumental in the exploration of the North and, as such, her adventures became part of the lore of traders and travelers there.

She wrote and concealed spellbooks in various hiding spots around the wilderlands, and the one known as the Specular survived into the mid–14 century DR.

Sabirine learned the secrets of lichdom but chose to die a natural death.
