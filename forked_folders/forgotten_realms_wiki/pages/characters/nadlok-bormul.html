Nadlok Bormul was a member of the Shield Council of the Knights of the Shield in 1370 DR.

History

Nadlok grew up in Crimmor in Amn but sold all his holdings to the Crytrappers and moved to Athkatla in 1366 DR. There, Nadlok made much money, becoming one of the richer tavern and festhall owners in the city. However, bored by money, he greatly enjoyed his role in the Knights of the Shield and soon rose through the ranks to the Shield Council.
