Adom Kimball was a King of Moray in the Moonshae Isles during the early-to-mid 12 century DR.

History

In the , Queen Eleanor of Callidyrr appointed King Adom as regent over her realm following the death of her husband, King Edwin Carrathal II. He served as regent for six years before Edwin II’s sole heir, Adrian Carrathal, was old enough to be crowned king in the .
