Aeriell was a human fighter and commander of her society's cavalry force, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

A female warrior in a matriarchal society, Aeriell supervised the training of the many cavaliers of the mounted forces.
She was an accomplished adventourer in her own right, and had battled many foes, monsters and enemies alike.

Possessions

Riding astride her light warhorse she wore a set of enchanted chainmail, and a girdle of hill giant strength, while wielding an enchanted longsword and shield. Always around her neck hung a medallion of ESP.
