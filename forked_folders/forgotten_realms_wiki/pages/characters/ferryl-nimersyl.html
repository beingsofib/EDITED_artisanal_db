Ferryl Nimersyl was the elf commander of the Moon Knights of Sehanine Moonbow on Evermeet in the 1370s DR.

History

He was known to have fought well in Nimesin's War in 1371 DR.

In 1374 DR, he and his knights joined Seiveril Miritar's crusade.
