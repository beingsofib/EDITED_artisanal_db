Lord Tygarian was a nobleman whose daughter was once taken hostage by a notorious pirate, Baralen the Black.

History

Sometime before 1372 DR, Lord Tygarian's daughter was kidnapped and held hostage by Baralen the Black. The lord paid a ransom for his daughter with a set of enchanted light armor, Fencer's Grace. The pirate however refused to let Lady Tygarian go as promised. When the daughter found out about it, she flew into a fit of rage and struck the pirate dead. She went on to become a pirate herself and she used the Fencer's Grace to achieve that goal.
