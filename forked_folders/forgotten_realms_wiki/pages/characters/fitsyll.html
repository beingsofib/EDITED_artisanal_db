Fitsyll was a retired adventurer who resided in the small village in the vicinity of Darnarest's tower in interior Faerûn circa 1369 DR.

History

Fitsyll had been fully retired and a enjoying quiet life when, in the , her home village experienced disappearances of some of its inhabitants, who, unknown to her, were taken by the slavers of the Cult of the Dragon to labor in the ruins of Myth Drannor. When a group of adventurers volunteered to search for the missing villagers, Fitsyll offered her prized enchanted +2 shortspear as a reward for the villagers' return or at the very least proof of the cause of the disappearance being dealt with.
