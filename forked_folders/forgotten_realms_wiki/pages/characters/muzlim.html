Muzlim was a small lap dog belonging to Mayella Meadowbrock of the town of Ghars in Cormyr in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Mayella took Muzlim with her when she went out with her father, the merchant Barthelm Meadowbrock, to the Bold Bard tavern on Eleint 16 of the . However, Muzlim growled and snapped at Mayor Tobald and Mayella chided the dog. Later, Muzlim was frightened by Dovo the blacksmith's assistant when he came to pester Mayella, when Dovo and Rolf brawled, and Mayella carried the fearful dog out under her arm.

Personality

Although yappy, Muzlim turned out to be a very good judge of character.
