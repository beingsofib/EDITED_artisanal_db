Runt was a commoner child who lived in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Runt was a young human boy with a light skin tone. He had brown eyes and dark hair, hidden underneath a tight green cloth skullcap. His fanciful garb suggested Runt was from a family that had coin.

History

In the , Runt could be found in the Lower City with his two friends, Stinker and Spool, often playing "chase the gelatinous cube" in the alleys or loitering in front of Devil's Fee shop, daring eachother to go into the "devil's house" and accusing eachother of being scaredy-cats.
