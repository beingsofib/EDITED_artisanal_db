Dougan Dubrace was the founder of the settlement of Dougan's Hole, one of the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale.

History

Dougan's founding of his namesake village was fitting enough. The community grew out of the famous fishing spot that Dougan discovered, on the northwestern shore of scenic Redwaters lake.
