Maern was a Ffolk moneylender inhabiting the town of Aithelar in Callidyrr on the Moonshae Isles in .

Activities

Maern provided banking and moneychanging services and lent money. His establishment was located near the center of Aithelar at the intersection of Finn's Alley and Aerynn's Street.
