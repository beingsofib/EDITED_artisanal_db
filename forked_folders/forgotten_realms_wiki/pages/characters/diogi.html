Diogi the Wanderer was a human wu jen from T'u Lung, Kara-Tur, in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Having left his homeland in disgust at its constant strife, Diogi walked the world of Toril while looking for a place of peace. While he would get involved in adventures, he would only choose good causes that spread peace.

He had a taboo against attacking first in a fight, with either his stick or his magic.

Possessions

He fought with a bo stick and wore a robe of protection +2.
