Magreb was a ranger who watched out a small stretch of the Cloud Peak mountains during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

Magreb watched over the land alongside his companion Rilsa.

History

In the , a horde of goblins took up residence with a cave near the home of the old mage Adoy. After they fell under the sway of a hobgoblin named Rogdok, the goblins killed Rilsa, leaving Magreb saddened and a bit paranoid.

Following Rilsa's death, Magreb wanted to recover her remains and take vengeance on her killers.
