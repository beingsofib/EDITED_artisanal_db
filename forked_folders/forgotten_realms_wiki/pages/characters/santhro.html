Santhro was a priest of Garagos and an innkeep active in the Thayan city of Bezantur in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Santhro was a powerful cleric and warrior who served as the High Priest of Bezantur's Temple of Garagos.

Personality

Santhro was devoted to Garagos. In the face of doubts about his god's power, he would point to his powerful spellcasting abilities as evidence of Garagos's greatness. He was charismatic enough to dispel his few followers' doubts as well.

He was pragmatic enough to convert his temple into an inn when money became tight—in which capacity the temple was known as Santhro's Rooming House—although he was proud enough that he refused to cook for his guests unless they asked him nicely.

History

Santhro opened his Rooming House in the mid-to-late 14 century DR in order to make extra money in the face of increasing doubt about his deity in Bezantur.
