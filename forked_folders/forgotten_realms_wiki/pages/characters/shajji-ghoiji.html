Shajji Ghoiji was the leader, or qaghan, of the Tsu-tsu tribe and the city of Jugicha on the Plain of Horses in the early 14 century DR.

Ghoiji's brother, Shajji Hoijarek, abandoned his life as a lama to serve under his brother as apa qaghan. However, Ghoiji's government was corrupt and fell into chaos and finally rebellion circa . Hoijarek stepped in, gathering capable advisors to his side and ascending as the new qaghan.
