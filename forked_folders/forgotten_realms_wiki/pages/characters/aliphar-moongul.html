Aliphar Moongul was a merchant and Zhentarim operative in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He was handsome.

Personality

He worried little.

Activities

He was a trader in cosmetics and medicines.

Relationships

He had more than one wife, and none of them had met each other. He was also a member of the Zhentarim under Calivar Murpeth.

History

Aliphar Moongul was at the revel in Warmfires House in the , in which Maervidal Iloster was to be killed. He was introduced to the scribe Iloster by Calivar just as they poisoned him. He was argumentative about Calivar's concerns, but stopped short of second-guessing him, and was with the operative when they seized a woman, Talantha, whom they suspected of having helped the scribe escape.

The woman was actually a spell-disguised Storm Silverhand, who defeated everyone in the room. Everyone left over was captured or killed by other agents among the staff of Warmfires.
