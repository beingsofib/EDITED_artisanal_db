Annunaki was the ruler of Messemprar in Unther in 1357 DR.

Activities

Annunaki was busy trying to survive the rebel siege on the royal palace.

Personality

Annunaki was one of the most brutal men in Unther. He was also scheming and ambitious with the secret goal to become the ruler of Unther instead of Gilgeam. Annunaki was too proud to accept a negotiation with the rebels or to surrender.
