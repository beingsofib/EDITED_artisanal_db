Meltruil was a half-drow inhabiting Dambrath in the Shining South during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

She was quite ambitious.

Activities

Meltruil was the daughter of Yenandra Hazm'cri, the former Queen of Dambrath.

Relationships

She had two sisters—Hasafir and Luatharyn.
