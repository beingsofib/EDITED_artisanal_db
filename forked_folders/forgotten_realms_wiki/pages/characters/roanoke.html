Roanoke was a patron of the Elfsong Tavern from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Roanoke was a customer of the Elfsong Tavern in the Eastway neighborhood of Baldur's Gate and one of the attendees of the "Laff Riot" show at the tavern. It was a stand-up comedy performance hosted by Harvard Willoughby in Elfsong's side room with a stage. Roanoke "almost pissed himself laughing" and recommended it to his friend, Phibs Brogan.
