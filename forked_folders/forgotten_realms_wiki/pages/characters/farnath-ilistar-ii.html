Farnath Ilistar II was the fifth King of the Ilistar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate.

Activities

Relationships

Farnath II was the great-great-grandson of King Farnath I. He was descended from King Belhendar's younger half-brother. His son and heir was the simpleton Prince Farnath III and his younger brother was Temprith.
