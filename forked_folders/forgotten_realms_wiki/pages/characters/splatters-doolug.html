Splatters Doolug was a mural painter from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Doolug was a half-orcish man of a large frame with a rough orcish face, curly blonde hair, and a muddy light green hide. He often wore an artist's garb and a feathered beret.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Splatters Doolug spent his days painting murals on the Old Wall. His art faced criticism from some passersby Baldurians, like Slatey Marven, who thought Doolug took criticism poorly.
