Turkin Varringore was a militiaman from Liara in Nova Vaasa, one of the Domains of Dread.

Description

He had a well-groomed, waxed mustache that curled upward and shoulder-length black hair. Turkin dressed in silk shirts and trousers, with leather boots.

Personality

He was gruff and a little condescending, arrogant enough to meet hostility in kind even while asking for help.

Abilities

He had superlative agility.

Activities

He was an adventurer with a couple of exploits to his name, but no impressive killings. He had also volunteered to join the militia on a patrol in response to the killings recently plaguing the Merchant's Quarter of Liara.

Relationships

He was the son of a local merchant, and a member of the militia.

History

When a string of grisly murders by exsanguination took place in the city of Liara, Turkin Varringore volunteered to patrol the Merchant's Quarter where the killings had taken place, for the militia. However, he soon realized they'd be overmatched anyhow, and tried to hire a number of adventurers from Ravens Bluff that coincidentally had come to the city. If he succeeded, he'd have asked them to join his evening patrol. After the murder of Futgar Martovingian, if he hadn't hired these adventurers, he'd make one last try.

If it was discovered that the killer was a werespider instead of a vampire, he was at risk of accidentally telling someone about Jebbrie Vokasich, a ten-year-old who had been bitten by mysterious spiders from a catacomb right before the murders, prompting a mob to form and target his home. He'd gather the militia to disperse the mob afterwards, but he might be too late, if going by himself. If shown directly that the killer was Sundian Silgathen as a werespider, however, Turkin would realize exactly how to break the affliction targeting young Jebbrie.
