Tryssia Lanngolyn () was a noblewoman of the Lanngolyn noble family of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

She was the eldest daughter of Lady Tresh Lanngolyn, the family matriarch, and her consort Ormaes Lanngolyn. By 1368 DR, she was also the expected heir to the family, and was still by 1372 DR.
