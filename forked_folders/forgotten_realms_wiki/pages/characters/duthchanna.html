Duthchanna was a bard of Athkatla in Amn.

History

Duthchanna once told a story about the music-loving blue dragon Olothontor, the Minstrel Wyrm. To confirm her account, the sage Velsaert of Baldur's Gate and another bard, Schalalla Irdree of the Harpers, later went on an expedition to meet the dragon.
