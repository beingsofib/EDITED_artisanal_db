Pardulph was an inhabitant of Neverwinter on the Sword Coast North in the late 13 or early 14 century DR.

Relationships

In his time, Pardulph of Neverwinter was an enemy of Stormraven, the Mistress of Port Llast, but later married her.

They had a son, named Rivenhelm, who would grow to be one of the greatest knights in Faerûn by the 1350s DR.
