Maeral

Person
nameMaeral

Maeral was a figure known to elves as of the 3 century DR.

Legacy

Maeral's dream was discussed by Eltargrim and Lady Starym in prior to her assassination attempt on him. Eltargrim spoke well of it, while Lady Ildilyntra Starym thought it a madness born of the grip of elder years.
