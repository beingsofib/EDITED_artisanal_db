Riliton Mandleweave was a powerful invoker who used her arcane prowess to the defend of her home of Hlondeth in 146 DR.

History

Riliton Mandleweave learned her impressive skills to weave spells from a renowned wizard Charas of Winterwood. She earned her place by defending the city of Hlondeth in the siege of . The siege was the second attempt to claim the city by the ambitious and militaristic leader of Turmish, Lord Dempster Turmish. He did not plan the siege accordingly and was stumped by Hlondeth's high walls, and his forces were held at bay by Riliton Mandleweave's barrage of spells. As she handled the enemy's forces upfront, the Company of the Howling Harpoon flanked the army and destroyed Dempster Turmish's siege machinery, food, and other supplies. The siege was a failure, and Turmite armies were turned around.
