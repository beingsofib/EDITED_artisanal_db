Igrayne Elonis was an elven priest of Mystra in the 14 and 15 centuries DR.

Description

She had light skin, gray eyes, and short backswept white hair. She favored a simple dress with a low-cut bodice.

History

Igrayne was once a priestess of Mystra, but after her goddess's death in 1385 DR she was left aimless. Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar during the Darkening of 1484–1485 DR, Igrayne lived in Eveningstar, though still despondent. She could be seen standing on a small cliff near the village, pondering her existence.
