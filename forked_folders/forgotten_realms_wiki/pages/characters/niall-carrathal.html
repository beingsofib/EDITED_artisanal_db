Nial Carrathal, born Niall Cymrych, was a High King of Callidyrr in the mid–10 century DR. He was the first king of the Carrathal Dynasty, and although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles, he only ruled over Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was the son of his predecessor, High King Tanner Cymrych, and he was succeeded by his first son, Erfle Carrathal.

History

Niall was born into the Cymrych Dynasty of High Kings, who traced their lineage to the legendary first High King, Cymrych Hugh. and subsequently lost both control over Norland as well as possession of the Sword of Cymrych Hugh with the death of his father, High King Tanner, the family's legitimacy as well as its connection to its legendary ancestor had been badly weakened.
