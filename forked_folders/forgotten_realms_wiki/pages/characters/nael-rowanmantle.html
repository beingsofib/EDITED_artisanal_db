Nael Rowanmantle was a noble in the early-to-mid 15 century DR.

History

Nael Rowanmantle and Elgrym Stormserpent were assassinated on the orders of the latter's older brother, Nethglas Stormserpent, after Elgrym told Nael of Nethglas' theft of the Wyverntongue Chalice.
