Robyn Kendrick was the ward of the king of Corwell and later High Queen and the Great Druid of the Earthmother in the Moonshae Isles in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Robyn was a beautiful young lady with bright green eyes and thick black hair.

Possessions

Unlike most ladies on Corwell, Robyn wore a rust-colored cape over her dark green leggings.

Relationships

Robyn was friends with the halfling Pawldo and felt a strong connection to Tristan Kendrick.

History

Robyn found out by accident she had druidic powers while fighting a group of firbolgs in Llyrath Forest in the .
