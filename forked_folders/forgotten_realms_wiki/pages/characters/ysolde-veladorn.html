Ysolde Veladorn was the daughter of Qilué Veladorn.

History

Ysolde was born to the Chosen of Eilistraee Qilué Veladorn and her companion Elkantar Iluim in the Promenade of the Dark Maiden in Skullport.

In time, Ysolde also became a priestess of Eilistraee in the Promenade.

In 1361 DR, Qiluè agreed to help Liriel Baenre against the Vhaeraun-worshiping Dragon's Hoard, sending her warriors to attack their headquarters. However, during the fighting, despite their victory, Gorlist killed Elkantar.

Later, Gorlist led the remaining Dragon's Hoard against the Promenade in revenge and in the fighting, where Ysolde was killed by Shakti Hunzrin.
