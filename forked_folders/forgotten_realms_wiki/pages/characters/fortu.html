Fortu was a guard in Freedale during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Possessions

Fortu was armored in chain mail and wielded a short sword.

Activities

He guarded the entrance to the Tower of the High Wizard Netheril.

Relationships

He worked for Netheril, a local wizard, alongside his fellow Deval.

History

During the mid-to-late 14 century DR, Fortu guarded the tower at around the same time that Taran Goldenstar was kidnapped.
