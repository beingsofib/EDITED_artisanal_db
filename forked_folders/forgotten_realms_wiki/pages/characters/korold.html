King Korold was the leader of the Reghedmen Tribe of the Tiger barbarians during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

He was married to Bjornhild Solvigsdottir, who assisted him on the battlefield.

History

King Korold fought in many battles until he was slain by a frost giant. His wife inherited authority over the tribe.
