Mylra was a priestess of Loviatar active in the Thayan city of Bezantur in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

She was the High Priestess of Loviatar's Manor, the largest temple to her goddess in not only Thay but perhaps all of Faerûn.

She was known to be active in all forms of "worship" that transpired within the Manor's walls.

Rumors & Legends

She was said to be especially "skilled" with a whip.
