Zulmark Korathar was a famous adventurer inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a grizzled man with one eye.

Activities

A veteran of both the Blue Mask and Riders of the Night adventuring companies, Zulmark wished to form a new adventuring company to explore a series of dwarven ruins he unearthed beneath the mountains of northwest Faerûn. While in Waterdeep he enjoyed spending his nights at the Bloody Fist tavern in the Dock Ward.
