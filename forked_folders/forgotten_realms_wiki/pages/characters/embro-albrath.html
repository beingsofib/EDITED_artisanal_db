Embro Albrath was the head of the mercantile House Albrath, as well as their council representative, during the late 14 century DR. He was known for his ruthless determination in business and pompous attitude. By the year 1370 he had fathered 16 daughters.

Description

Councilman Albrath was a stout man with a distinct mustache. He also possessed an ostentatious sense of fashion, as evidenced by the many gold chains and large rings he wore.
