Rampah was a destitute man who lived in the city of Athkatla in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

He spent his time begging for coins in the city's bridge district.

History

In the , Rampah became inadvertently involved with the "Skinner murders" that plagued the city. He found a small bit of elephant hide near one of the murder victims.
