Carbos was an assassin who operated on the Sword Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

At some point, Carbos and his fellow assassin Shank got into a duel over Bubbles, the woman with whom they were both in love.

History

In the , Carbos was hired to murder the foster child of the sage Gorion, Abdel Adrian. He managed to gain entrance into the library-fortress of Candlekeep but was unable to kill the young man.

At some point over the next year, Carbos traveled to Athkatla.
