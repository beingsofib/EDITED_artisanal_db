Rudigar was a young man living in the village of Tweyar in Tethyr in the mid-to-late 14 century DR who joined the Tweyar Self-Defense Force.

Relationships

Rudigar was a close friend of Ernico, another member of the Defense Force.

History

In the month of Flamerule, in the , when the village hetman, Osbard, ordered Zaranda arrested for treason, Rudigar and Ernico were also present.
