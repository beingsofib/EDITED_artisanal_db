Maxam was the consort of Storm Silverhand in the mid-14 century DR.

Personality

Storm Silverhand considered Maxam's intelligence and kindness to rival her own, for whom adventuring was in the blood.

Activities

Maxam was an adventurer.

Relationships

He was Storm's man.

History

Maxam was an orphan. After they got together, Maxam went to live with Storm Silverhand in Shadowdale, where he remained for a decade, though they were adventurers throughout that time. He perished in the summer of , during a battle with tanar'ri at a gate opened by illithids.
