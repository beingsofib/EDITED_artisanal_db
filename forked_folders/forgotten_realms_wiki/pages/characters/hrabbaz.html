Hrabbaz () was a bodyguard of House Gralhund in Waterdeep in the early 1490s DR.

Activities

Hrabbaz protected both Yalah Gralhund and her husband Orond Gralhund.

History

In 1492 DR, the Gralhunds were involved in the search for the lost gold of Dagult Neverember under the promise of getting their fair share of the treasure. The Zhents led by Urstul Floxin used Gralhund Villa as their headquarters during their search for the Stone of Golorr.

When a fight started between her guards and the Zhentarim, Hrabbaz was at his mistress Yalah's side and hid with her in her master bedroom. He was with her when she gave the Stone to her nimblewright servant with orders as to where to take it.

Personality

Hrabbaz always showed good manners and his clothes were immaculate.

Relationship

Hrabbaz truly respected Yalah but would like to see Orond humbled.
