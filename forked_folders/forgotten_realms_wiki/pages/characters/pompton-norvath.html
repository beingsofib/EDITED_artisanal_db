Pompton Norvath a wealthy citizen of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Pompton was a human man with a dark skin tone, and light brown hair. Wore a quality coat and a big leather hat.

History

In the , Pompton Norvath lived in the Lower City, strolling the streets near a shady part of the town with abandoned buildings where the notorious Guild had one of their Guildhall entrances guarded by the gang's thugs and snipers. Pompton wished that area was more secure via steel watcher patrols and warned passersby from peeking into the abandoned buildings' windows in fear of sniper arrows. He could often be seen with one of his neighbors – Delyarna.
