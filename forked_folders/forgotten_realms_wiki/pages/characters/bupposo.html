Bupposo was a Winter Sect monk in Nakamaru in Wa in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Bupposo was at the Winter Sect monastery from many years but remained a low-level monk

In Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR), Bupposo and his friend Kogi attempted a grand prank against the monks of the Tsuyoi-no-kami Shrine: they paid Ichi the korobokuru yakuza to find a girl that could seduce the rival monks. Thus, the young maiden O-Shino, acting under the orders of the monks, tempted Anchin and Kaian.

Personality

Bupposo was a stubborn, mischievous, and irreverent individual and also spiteful, remembering slights for a very long time. His only virtue was an incredible patience.

Activities

Bupposo was a good teacher at the monastery. Often he secretly went out with his friend Kogi. Bupposo was a very poor monk.
