Mistress Ada was a ranking red-headed priestess in service of the Church of Talos of Athkatla's Temple District in the late 14 century DR.

Possessions

Ada wore a set of splint mail and carried a hefty club. A heavy necklace of Talos hung from her chest, displaying her affiliation with the Stormlord's church.

History

By the , Mistress Ada could be found in the great Temple of Talos, guarded by Storm Knights while performing daily tasks of a ranking priestess, providing services to visitors, and exchanging enchanted items, protection scrolls, and potions for donations to the temple. The same year, Ada was targeted by Mae'Var, one of the Shadow Thieves of Athkatla. Mae'Var sought to steal the necklace of Talos.
