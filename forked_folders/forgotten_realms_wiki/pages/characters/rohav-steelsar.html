Prince Rohav Steelsar "the Exile" was an ill-fated child of the exiled Amnian Parhek dynasty.

Relationships

Rohav was the second son of Princess Rasa Parhek Dornar Steelsar, and was the sibling of Choris, Rosa, and Rhothar.

History

Rohav was born in Silverymoon, and was only 8 years old in the when the family was found and attacked by Amnian assassins. Dornar and Choris fought to the death, Rosa was killed, and Rasa and Rhothar managed to escape, but Rohav was kidnapped and taken away. He was brainwashed by his captors and led to believe they had actually rescued him, and lived in Baldur's Gate while plotting to take the throne for himself. In 1305 DR he betrayed his uncle Prince Malek the Coinmage, leading to his death and the deaths of his three children. Rohav was poisoned to death by his vizier and controller Arlen Bormul in 1306 DR, putting an end to the last known heir of the Parhek dynasty.
