Gusard was a rawun and the youngest son of Al'ia bint Hazir and Sultan Yusef al-Wadi of Gana in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara.

Personality

Gusard was a natural entertainer and enjoyed joking around.

Description

He had a mole under his right eye in the shape of a butterfly.

History

As with all of his siblings, Gusard's parentage was kept secret from the population of Gana. He was raised in a monastery high in Al-Yabki Mountains.
