Aziza bint Diyab was an foreign psionicist inhabiting Diyab in Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Description

Most of Aziza's features were covered by her dark chador.

Activities

Aziza was the sole survivor of a foreign ship that crashed ashore in Zakhara when she was a child. She was found and adopted by a family in Diyab and raised as their own. Aziza sought training for her recently blossoming psionic powers.

Possessions

Aziza wielded a katar.
