Jeordros was one of the three leaders of the Azatl settlement in Nexal in 1362 DR.

Activities

Jeordros ruled over the largest territory. In his territory's restricted areas, humans were instantly sent to the slave market.

Personality

Jeordros dreamed of one day challenging Hoxitl. He was fanatical about clearing out the ruins in the hopes of gaining control of the sacred square of Azatl.
