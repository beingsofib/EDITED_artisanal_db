Sheldon Thorsdotter was a Vaasan child that lived in the Granite Tower during the late 14 century DR. He was the son of Vala Thorsdotter, leader of the warriors descending from the Moor Eagle clan of Bodvar the Black.

History

Sheldon remained in the Granite Tower when his mother Vala ventured far to the west in the . During that time, Vala's darksword allowed her to "dream walk" to Sheldon's bedchamber in the tower and watch over him from half a continent away.
