Azerie Germond was a cleric of Amaunator in the Cormyrean town of Eveningstar in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Azerie had pale skin and light blonde hair tied back. She wore a resplendent suit of chain mail decorated with the symbols of Amaunator.

History

After the House of the Morning was destroyed by the initial drow raid in the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Azerie rallied the faithful to fight back against the threat. She stood watch over the smoking ruins of the razed temple with Wilkan Drome, directing bold sorts to fight the drow in the King's Forest and rewarding them with potions and holy wargear.
