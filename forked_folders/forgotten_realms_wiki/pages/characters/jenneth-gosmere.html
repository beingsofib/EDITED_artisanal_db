Jenneth Gosmere was a Cormyrean War Wizard who served in Eveningstar in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Jenneth had dark skin and long white hair.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar circa 1484–1485 DR, Jenneth joined the other War Wizards in supporting the defense of Eveningstar and then the raids upon Sschindylryn, which were organized by Aptem Mews, Traegar Vadamore, Oryne Belamadin, and Korrex Dunhaven. Jenneth was then contacted by Elminster, who informed her that the time was right for a strike upon the Sschindylryn Ziggurat, the great structure in the heart of the city from which Hekta Szind was leading the war.

Though the attack was successful in slaying the High Priestess and functionally ending the war in Cormyr, Ana Brabener was captured by Lolth in the process. With the Thread of the Weave under her control, this allowed the Spider Queen to proceed with her ultimate scheme to dominate Mystra's Weave. This forced the Cormyreans and their allies to search the Demonweb Pits for a way to stop Lolth's ambitions once and for all.
