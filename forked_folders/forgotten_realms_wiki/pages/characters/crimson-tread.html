The Crimson Tread was a mysterious haunting that appeared on the streets of Jhaansciim and chilled the blood of inhabitants along the shores of Amn and Tethyr in the late 14 century DR.

History

The Crimson Tread manifested itself as bloody footprints that appeared behind unsuspecting villages or visitors to Jhaansciim. The crimson footprints were not caused by the villagers' bleeding, as they showed no signs of trauma. These bloody prints were the omen of looming doom and the villager's life to be taken by the Crimson Tread within a single tenday. It was unknown whether leaving Jhaanscii could save the doomed from certain death.

Some villagers claimed that the Crimson Tread was the restless spirit of Hathem Gedlaera, a troubled boy who left Jhaansciim for Athkatla and joined the Shadow Thieves. Hathem swore that one day he would pay his tormenters in Jhaansciim back. Hathem died a failure sometime before the late 14 century DR, his severed foot was found in the town of Mosstone, and since then, the Crimson Tread began claiming lives.
