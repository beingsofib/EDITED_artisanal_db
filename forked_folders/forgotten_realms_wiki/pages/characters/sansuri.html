Countess Sansuri was the ruler of the cloud giants sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

History

Sansuri was one of the giant lords at the time of the shattering of the ordning.

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, after the disappearance of storm giant king Hekaton, all giant lords started their path to reshaping the ordning. Sansuri's plan was to rediscover one of the ancient Ostorian markets containing a long-lost trove of dragon magic.

In order to accomplish her project, Sansuri lured the bronze dragon Felgolos into her castle and imprisoned him in order to extract information. Though Felgolos knew nothing, Sansuri did not believe him and tortured him terribly.

Activities

Sansuri was always near Felgolos trying to extract information.
