Barana Talmere was a cleric of Amaunator from the town of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Barana was a stout and muscular dwarven woman with plain brown hair, clean-shaven face, and light skin. She wore a set of chainmail adorn with Amaunator's holy symbol.

History

In the late 15 century DR, in the midst of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Barana Talmere borrowed some adventurers from Constal Adamor Thellik in order to help defend the village. Barana told them about the drow necromancers from House Szind that were using the Deep Glen Graveyard in the King's Forest to bolster their armies in the war against Eveningstar.
