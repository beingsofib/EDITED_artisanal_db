Mev Flintknapper was a deep gnome in Blingdenstone in the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa 1486 DR, Mev was attacked by two gelatinous cubes in one of Blingdestone's grottoes, but was saved by a party of adventurers escaping from drow slavers of Velkynvelve.
