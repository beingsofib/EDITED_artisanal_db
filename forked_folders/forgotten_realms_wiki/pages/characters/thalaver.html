Thalaver was an adventuring mage and member of the Company in Crimson some time before the mid–14 century DR.

History

Thalaver was a native of Suzail and lived in his own tower in Cormyr's capital. He took a young Shandaril as his apprentice. On an excursion into the Stonelands with the Company in Crimson, Shandaril decided to betray her comrades and killed the entire group in order to take their magic.

Possessions

He carried a staff of power and several spellbooks.
