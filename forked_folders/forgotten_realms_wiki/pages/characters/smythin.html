Smythn was a man who lived on the Sword Coast during the 15 century DR.

History

In the , Smythin was captured by the Cult of the Absolute and taken to the Shattered Sanctum. There he was prepared by the goblin warrior Druk, to be thrown into the sanctum's dungeons and fed to their giant spiders.
