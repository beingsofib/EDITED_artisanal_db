Annalee was a young girl that lived in Cormyr with her father Brehnan during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

She spent some of her time caring for her family's horses.

Relationships

Annalee's only known family was her father Brehnan; her mother passed away from illness when she was much younger. Their cottage lay some way's west of Calantar's Way.

History

During the Time of Troubles, in the , Annalee's father found the young magic-user known as Midnight in a field near their home during a wild storm. They took care of the young woman for two weeks, before she regained consciousness and hurriedly continued left for the city of Arabel, with no knowledge she held within her the power of the magic-goddess Mystra.
