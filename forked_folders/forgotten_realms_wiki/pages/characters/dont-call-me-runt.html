Don't-Call-Me-Runt was a member of the Guild from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Runt was a halfling man with brown skin, green eyes, and dark brown hair gathered in cornrows. He wore an archer's leathers dyed in the Guild's green colors and carried a bow.

History

In the , Runt was tasked with standing guard at the secret entrance into Baldur's Gate's Undercellar, where the Guild's Guildhall was hidden. Whilst a half-orcish enforcer Tusgront manned the main entrance, Don't-Call-Me-Runt was ready to dispatch any unwanted guest from a distance.
