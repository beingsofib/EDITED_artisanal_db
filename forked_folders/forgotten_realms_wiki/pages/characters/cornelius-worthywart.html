Cornelius Worthywart was a halfling who lived in the Realms during the late 14 or 15 centuries DR.

Personality

He believed that his ancestral home of Luiren was consumed in the blue fire of the Spellplague.

History

One day, while venturing through the Fireshear Mountains, Cornelius and his brother Tomelius came across a dragonborn author. The writer took the brothers' accounts of the halfling realm of Luiren, including their opposing viewpoints. Their words were immortalized in the compendium Oral Histories of Faerûn.
