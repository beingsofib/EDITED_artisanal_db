Halamar was a mage specializing in fire magic and a member of the Blue Wyverns adventuring company of Ravens Bluff in the Vast in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Halamar and the Blue Wyverns helped Jack Ravenwild recover the Sarkonagael tome from the ancient dwarven city of Sarbreen beneath Ravens Bluff.

Description

Halamar had red hair and a "W"-shaped goatee.
