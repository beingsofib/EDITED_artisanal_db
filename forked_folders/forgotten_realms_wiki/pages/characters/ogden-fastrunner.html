Ogden Fastrunner was a male human commander of a Cormyrean patrol during the Time of Troubles in 1358 DR.

History

Ogden was the captain of a Cormyrean patrol that was patrolling the near the city of Marsember during the Time of Troubles when a strange poisonous fog killed him and his men. He later rose as an undead creation under the order of the god of the dead, Myrkul.

Myrkul ordered the risen Ogden and his men to capture Midnight, but in the end they were all destroyed.
