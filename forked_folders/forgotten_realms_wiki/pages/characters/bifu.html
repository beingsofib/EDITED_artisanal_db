Bifu, whose name translated as "Big Foot," was a warrior of the reformed cannibal Kukalatu tribe of the Malatran Plateau in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Bifu was a strong and nimble warrior who felt at home atop the jungle trees and swinging across its vines.

Possessions

Bifu wore a set of bone armor and was armed with a spear.

History

In The Year of New Peoples of the Malatran calendar, a band of heroes of the Living Jungle was sent to the Kukalatu tribe by Chief Katar of the Koshiva to establish contact with the reformed cannibal and return with a special healing potion for the Chief's wife – Ootoo. A Kukulaturi hunting party was the first contact for the heroes who ran into them not far from the village. Warriors Bifu and eJahi were the ones to meet the group in the open, while others – Gihu, Latu, and a duo of saru, Bubba and Vimu, stayed hidden, in case the strangers brought trouble.
