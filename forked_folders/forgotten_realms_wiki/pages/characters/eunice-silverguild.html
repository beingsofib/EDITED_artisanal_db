Eunice Silverguild was the wife of a successful merchant inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Eunice, despite being quite wealthy, was bored all the time.

Activities

She lived within southern Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter in Silverguild Manor.

Relationships

Eunice was married to Malvred Silverguild, a wildly successful merchant and the owner of Prosperity House. Her two children were Emelia and Meryn.
