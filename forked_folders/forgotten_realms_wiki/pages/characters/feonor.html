Feonor was a human warlord in Avernus in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Feonor was easily recognizable by her milky-white eyes and her parasol made of bones and flesh.

Personality

Feonor's most distinctive personality characteristic was her constant annoyance at all the multiverse around her. Because of this, she spoke only by whispering and using short sentences.

History

Once Feonor lived in an unknown Prime Material plane but was invited to Avernus by Mahadi. However, after arriving in Hell she grew bored of Mahadi's attentions and decided to leave the Wandering Emporium and to become one of the warlords of Avernus by the .

As a warlord, Feonor commanded the Golden Doom, a Demon Grinder she stole from Mahadi. The infernal war machine was crewed by a ghast, several ghouls and ten crawling claws. Two Tormentors piloted by mezzoloths served as her escorts.

Relationship

Mahadi was fascinated by her zombie-like personality and still resented her departure.
