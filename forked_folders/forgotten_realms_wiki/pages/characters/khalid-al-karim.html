Khalid al-Karim was a half-elven rawun and member of the House of Al-Karim in Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Relationships

Khalid was in love with Alia al-Karim, his clan chieftain's daughter.

Activities

The start of a recent blood feud between his clan and a wealthy merchant from Hiyal involving the murder of Alia's brother provided Khalid an opportunity, as he saw it, to prove his worth to her.

Possessions

Khalid wielded a jambiya and sling during combat. He also carried his drum, some bracers of defense AC 6, and a pouch of accessibility.''
