Aleine Whitsunday was a cleric of Eldath from Selgaunt, Sembia, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR, briefly involved in a mission to establish diplomatic relationships with Myth Nantar in .

Description

She weighed and stood , with fair skin, light brown hair and blue eyes.

Personality

She was the picture of patience.

Abilities

Even among her peers in power, Aleine's wisdom was top-notch.

Possessions

Aleine's notable carried possessions were limited to a ring of free action, a tub of Keoghtom's ointment, and two potions of healing.

Relationships

The paladin Sturge Whitsunday was her older brother, and during her holy mission, she wanted him to recognize she wasn't just his baby sister, but an accomplished young woman not to be patronized.

History

At seventeen, presumably in , Aleine entered the priesthood of Eldath through the Path of Silence, a yearlong trial during which she neither spoke nor heard a single word. Around the turn of the , she began dreaming of corpses piled on a ring of standing white marble and coral stones in a place she thought to be the Whamite Isles, which she took to be a sign from the goddess that she was to reconsecrate the ring. At around that time, her brother Sturge was tapped for a mission to find Myth Nantar and enter into an alliance with them; she informed him she'd be joining him, rather than asking to join him.

If she made it far enough to find that holy site in the mission, it would've taken her maybe twenty minutes to clear the dead bodies and reconsecrate the ring, which she'd likely do almost compulsively.
