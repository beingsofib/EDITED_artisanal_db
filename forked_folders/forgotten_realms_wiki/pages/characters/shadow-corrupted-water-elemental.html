The shadow-corrupted water elemental was an unfortunate elemental that dwelled in the ruins of Beckside Castle in the late 15 century DR.

Abilities

The elemental was much more powerful than others of its kind, and could wield the negative energy powers that it had been infused with.

History

When the Shadovar and Harpers pursued a Nether Scroll at Beckside Castle, the dark wizard Keldan Shadeskein bound one of the local water elementals to his will, infused it with shadows, and tied its life and his into a defensive barrier that protected Gaston Wyrst while he worked on obtaining the Scroll. The elemental was imbued with fell energies and left in the flooded basement of the castle, where Harper agents eventually found it and mercifully destroyed it, opening the way forward.
