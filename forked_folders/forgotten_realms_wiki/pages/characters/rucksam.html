Rucksam was a wizard that traveled across the Sword Coast North during the early 15 century DR.

Abilities

In addition to his wizarding ways, Rucksam was fond of traveling to local taverns and making bets with local townsfolk. Whenever passing through Rothé Valley he made sure to stop by its local village.

Relationships

He took on a boy from Rothé Valley Village as his traveling ward, brining him along on his journeys in the North.

History

In the , Rucksam created his own namesake tunnel in Rothé Valley in order to gain quicker passage between its village and some nearby farms. This won him a wager with the old farmer Fergus Gunderson. Local farmers were taken aback at the appearance of a new and mysterious passageway but accepted the traveling wizard's explanation.
