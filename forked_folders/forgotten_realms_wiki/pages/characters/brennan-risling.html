Brennan Risling was an adventurer lived in west Faerûn during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

As an adventurer, Brennan traveled alongside the dwarf Mencar Pebblecrusher, the barbarian Smaeluv Orcslicer, as well as Amon and his imp familiar Pooky.

Possessions

Among Brennan's possessions was the unique ring of invisibility, the sandthief's ring.

History

As of the , Brennan was still living the city of Baldur's Gate. During the iron crisis he believed that Sarevok Anchev would make a fine Grand Duke and prevent war from coming to the city.

At some point during the following year, he traveled south to Amn and joined up with his adventuring band .
