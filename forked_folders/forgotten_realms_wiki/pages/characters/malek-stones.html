Malek Stones was a reporter employed by Baldur's Mouth in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Malek was a beardless male shield dwarf with pudgy features, brown eyes, and short curly brown hair.

History

In the , Malek Stones worked on investigative scoop articles for the Baldur's Mouth Gazette. During the Absolute crisis, the irate dwarf could be found working on his pieces within Baldur's Mouth's offices in Heapside of the Lower City.
