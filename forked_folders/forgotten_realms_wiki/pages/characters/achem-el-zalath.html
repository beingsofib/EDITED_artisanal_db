Achem el Zalath was a Calishite wizard of Almraiven in eastern Calimshan during the late 14 century DR.

Activities

The heir of the Zalath shipbuilding family that were partners of the Rundeen, Achem designed new waterborne vessels.

Although an independent wizard of Almraiven around 1370 DR, by 1372 DR he was a member of the Guild Arcane.

History

Achem was a student of magic who graduated from Blackstaff Tower in Waterdeep.

Circa 1370 DR, Achem gained fame after proposing, with diagrams, a new kind of vessel he dubbed a 'waterwalking ship', which on Earth would be called a in Elminster's understanding.
