Tharmalos was a great green dragon that dwelled in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Tharmalos was hired by the drow to poison the groundwater of Eveningstar. The dragon was joined by a contingent of yuan-ti who helped secure the underground caverns, but when the toxic water was noticed Unmar Halberg allowed a team of adventurers to explore the well. They quickly found the yuan-ti and fought their way through Fless-onas, Gresl-glis, and Saz-sass, before encountering the unexpected dragon. Tharmalos initially thought they were simply a meal delivery, and only realized the error too late before being slain.
