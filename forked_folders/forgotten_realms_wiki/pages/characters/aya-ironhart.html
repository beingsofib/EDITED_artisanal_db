Aya Ironhart was a dwarven cleric of Amaunator and adventurer in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Aya was a stout dwarven woman, dressed in Amaunatori chainmail.

Personality

Aya had a rather terrible bedside manner, though she did take Thrane's welfare very seriously.

History

Shortly after the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, Aya traveled the High Road and came to the aid of Thrane Mettleworth, whose foot had been mangled by a bear trap thanks to an inattentive trapfinding rogue. They stayed at Beckside Castle for the night, but Aya wasn't able to get the trap off his leg, nor properly treat his injury.
