Thunna

Person
nameThunna
homeDock Ward, Waterdeep
occupationFence

Thunna was a fence of stolen goods inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Thunna operated out the Thirsty Throat tavern located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward where Candle Lane and Zastrow Street met the Way of the Dragon. Thunna dealt exclusively in poisons and other alchemical substances.
