Bel Dalemark was a merchant who lived in the city of Athkatla in the mid–14 century DR. He was known to deal in commodities and basic armaments.

Description

He was a knowledgeable man who knew a little bit about various types of market goods; including guril berries, solik berries, elephant hide, and oak trees.
