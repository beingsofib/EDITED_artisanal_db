Terasaka Tadafusa was a human samurai and masterless wanderer who traveled the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was a man with a strong sense of honor.

Possessions

He wore a set of brigandine armor, wielded an enchanted +2 shield, and had a figurine of wondrous power, a stone horse that turned into a destrier.

History

Once a samurai serving a daimyo, he became a ronin when his master died. Critically wounded during a battle with Tuigan skirmishers, he was found by Nariako and healed back to health. Ever since then, he accompanied her in her travels, and the two became inseparable.
