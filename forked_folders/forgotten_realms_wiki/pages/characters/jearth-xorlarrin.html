Jearth Xorlarrin was the weapons master of House Xorlarrin. He was one of the most powerful weapons masters in the drow city of Menzoberranzan in the late 15 century DR.

History

Jearth accompanied the expedition led by Ravel Xorlarrin to Gauntlgrym in the .
