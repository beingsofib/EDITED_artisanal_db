Oskurouh was a skeletal blue dragon that had perished attempting to become a dracolich by the early late 14 century DR.

Description

The dragon's skeleton was long, with an additional long tail.

Personality

It didn't have much of a personality left after being raised as a skeleton.

Abilities

It had roughly the same abilities as it had in life, though of course it didn't breathe anything anymore.

Activities

As a skeletal dragon, it obeyed orders and didn't do much else.

Relationships

Oskurouh obeyed the orders of the wight Karnal.

History

Oskurouh was a juvenile dragon at the time of its death, between 26 and 50 years old. It died during a failed attempt at turning it into a dracolich; its skeleton was then raised as a skeleton dragon, and kept at the Temple of the Dragon Oracle at some point on or before . The wight Karnal was appointed by necrophants aligned with the Cult to order it, and to guard its dignity.
