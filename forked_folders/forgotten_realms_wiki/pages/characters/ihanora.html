Ihanora the Merciful was the chosen of Auril who led a crusade against others in the North in the name of her goddess during the late 11 and early 12 centuries DR.

Activities

Ihanora traveled alongside a savage band of frost giants and yetis, that fought alongside polar worms and even white dragons, striking fear into the hearts of mortals. They assaulted anyone that attempted to seek refuge from the bitter cold of the North or that sought to negate the harsh effects it caused.

History

In the , Ihanora crafted the Snow Maiden's Reaver, that could turn any being it struck into solid ice.

She was finally defeated nearly a decade later, in the , by the Red Mask Brigade led by the infamous Bitter Harley.
