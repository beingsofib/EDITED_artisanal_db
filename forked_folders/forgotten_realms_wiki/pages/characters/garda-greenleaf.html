Garda Greenleaf was a scribe of Mantor's Library in Phlan in 1489 DR.

Description

The librarian was a middle-aged man.

Personality

The scribe was a senior librarian of Mantor's Library with a flawless record of attendance.

Possessions

Garda was known to dress in plain grey robes while working in the library.

History

In 1489 DR, Garda was killed in the Archives room of Mantor's Library by fellow scribe Ellison Berenger, a secret Cult of the Dragon member, in order of acquire a book about Ren o' the Blade.
