Ithym was a thief with the Brave Blades in the 3 century DR.

Personality

He was a little sarcastic, which seemed to be common in the band.

History

During the Brave Blades' delve into Ondil's Tower, Ithym found at least one secret door.

After they made their way into the lich's chamber, Gralkyn smashed one of the windows; in response, a force squeezed him and Ithym out of it, and hurled them into the ground, where they died.
