Phillwyp the Wary was a twisted gnome who staked a claim to the ruins of the castle at Caer Corwell in the late 15 century DR. From there, he aspired to establish his own kingdom of unseelie fey to rival Sarifal.

Relationships

His followers included boggles and other small, capricious fey who had overrun Caer Corwell.

As of , he had formed a tenuous alliance with the Silver's Blades pirates.
