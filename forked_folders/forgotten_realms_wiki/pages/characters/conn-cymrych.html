Conn Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid-to-late 9 century DR. He inherited a High Kingship that was severely weakened, owing to the loss of the Crown of the High King by his father.

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the first son of his predecessor, High King Dolan. His successor was his own first son, Byron Cymrych II.

History

Conn assumed the throne of the High King in the , following the death of his father, who went down in a sea battle against Illuskan invaders and took the Crown of the High King with him.

Conn ruled for thirty-three years before also dying in battle against the Northmen in the .
