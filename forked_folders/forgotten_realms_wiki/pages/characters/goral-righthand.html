Goral Righthand was a cleric of Cyric and a member of the Zhentarim inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Goral worked in the Black Network Headquarters in the eastern section of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter. He served as the assistant priest to his superior Synnda.
