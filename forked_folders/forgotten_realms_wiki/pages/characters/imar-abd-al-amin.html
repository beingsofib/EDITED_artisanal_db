Marshall Imar Abd al-Amin was the Chief Advisor for the Caliph of Liham within the Free Cities of Zakhara in .

Activities

Though he served Caliph Harin al-Hayif, Imar was a mamluk of the Faithful Mamluk Society, and followed their direct orders which were of course influence by nearby Qudra. His primary task was protecting the Caliph from outside forces.
