Saborn Rendel

Person
nameSaborn Rendel
homeNeverwinter
sexMale

Saborn Rendel was a clockmaker in Neverwinter and a co-founder of the Waterclock Guild. He founded it together with Lucan Greenharrow and made clocks that were both accurate and beautiful.
