Thaedras was a guard in Zazesspur during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Thaedras was a member of the Civic Guard of Zazesspur.

Relationships

He was known by name to captain Begg.

History

Thaedras' memories were of growing up in a farm, but he desired to wield a blade in service to people as more than a mere farmer, which eventually led him to becoming a city guard in Zazesspur. During Flamerule of the , he was slain by the dragon Xexillidaulgrymm, who due to ancient magic, became overwhelmed by the man's memories as he extracted his life force. The process almost certainly killed him.
