Bronor was a malaugrym impersonating a merchant in the Sword Coast North during the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

Bronor was using coins from old Athalantar. He owned property in Luskan.

Description

Bronor had a slender mustache with ends oiled to points. During his meeting with Mirt, he wore black Luskanite silks shot with irregular clouds of white, small stars.

History

As of late , Bronor had been impersonated by a malaugrym; Mirt claimed to have known that for six years. During a meeting with the moneylender, Bronor's coins were insulted by Mirt after a negotiation, and he then explained that he knew of the malaugrym impersonating him. He required, in exchange for keeping his secret, that Bronor keep his kin from seeking to kill, capture, or detain himself, Asper, and Shandril Shessair.
