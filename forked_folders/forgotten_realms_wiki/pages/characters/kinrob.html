Kinrob was a oni merchant in the Sargauth Level in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR.

Activities

Posing as an old hobgoblin, Kinrob ran a trade shop inside the hobgoblin settlement ruled by Azrok. In truth, everyone knew Kinrob's true identity and out of fear Azrok sent him newborn goblins to feed on.
