Siliss

Person
nameSiliss
homeLuiren, Shining South

Siliss was a yuan-ti inhabiting the Shining South during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Siliss lived within the Granuin Forest in Luiren, land of the halflings. The yuan-ti led many of the intelligent monsters found within the forest.
