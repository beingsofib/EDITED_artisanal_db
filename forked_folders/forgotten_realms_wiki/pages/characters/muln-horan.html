Muln Horan was a dwarf soldier in Neverwinter.

Activities

Muln was a top advisor to General Sabine, and remained in control of a part of the city when the general was busy in other places.

Personality

Muln hid his feeling for his general with violence.

History

In 1479 DR, he investigated cases of kidnapping. He was with his men at the Beached Leviathan tavern intimidating an adventuring band that came to be involved in the matter when some Ashmadai and their bound devils attacked. It was only thanks to the adventurers that Muln defeated the enemies. However, he died in the chaos of the subsequent battle.
