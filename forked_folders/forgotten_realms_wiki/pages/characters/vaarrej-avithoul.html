Matron Va'arrej Avithoul was the founder of the drow House Avithoul of Sschindylryn in ancient days.

History

Va'arrej was a pioneer among the early drow who inhabited Sschindylryn, and was exceptionally warlike. She led raids through the city's portals to distant places while wielding Knee-bender, and started the tradition of taking slaves to enrich herself. She used this wealth and influence to create the noble house ruled by her daughters. Following the Great Revolt, her son Va'aunsh Avithoul invented the first drow slave collar and cemented their place as masters of slavery.

Following her death her skull was preserved and put on display in the estate of House Avithoul, proudly placed alongside Knee-bender and the first drow slave collar. In the time of her descendent Balbaste Avithoul, her house took part in the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar. When Cormyrean forces counterattacked at the behest of Mirini Elemys they raided the estate and freed Avithoul's slaves, and may have also destroyed Va'arrej's skull as an opportunistic insult.
