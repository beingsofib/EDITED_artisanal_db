Zog was a part of a roaming adventuring troupe that traveled the Sword Coast North during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Zog was a tall and muscular half-orc with long reddish-brown hair and tan skin. His face was decorated with a nose ring and several earrings.

History

In the , Zog was a member of an adventuring band that included the gnome Yonks, the elf Sarafin, and the tiefling Quinara. The group traveled the Sword Coast in search of steady adventuring work. They traveled from Waterdeep to Baldur's Gate during the Absolute crisis and, unable to find work, planned to move out to Neverwinter as the city was searching for adventures.
