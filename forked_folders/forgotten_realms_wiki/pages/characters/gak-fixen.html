Gak Fixen was a surface gnome merchant in Mantol-Derith.

Personality

Gak was bloodthirsty and evil, as his appearance showed. He was a follower of Urdlen.

Activities

Around 1479 DR, Gak usually sold musical instruments, and he could use all of them.
