Pardus was a regular of the Rising Moon in the mid-14 century DR.

Personality

He liked the way Korvan cooked his vegetables, and wasn't above going behind his wife to eat as he pleased.

Activities

He was strongly implied to be a saddle maker.

Relationships

His wife was Yantra.

History

One night during Shandril's initial absence in the , Pardus praised Korvan's cooking in a way that offended his wife, Yantra. Though many in the inn were rooting for a fight, she simply called for a half-ration of boar to her husband, rather than what he preferred. Gorstag, winking, called him to the back under what he believed to be false pretenses, with the likely outcome of getting a full ration of deer.
