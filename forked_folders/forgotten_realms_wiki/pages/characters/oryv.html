Oryv was a businessman inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Oryv ran a fabric shop called Oryv's Cloth Emporium that specialized in importing fine fabrics from all across Faerûn.
