Charming Latham was an evoker wizard member of The Guild crime gang of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Charming was a human man with a pale skin tone, brown eyes, and facial tattoos. He hid his hairless face behind a hooded sorcerer's robe dyed emerald green, the Guild's telltale color. He was armed with a simple dagger.

Abilities

Charming specialized in magic of the evocation school of magic. He would use such spells as conjure water elemental, ice knife, ice storm, and ray of frost.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Charming Latham worked under Farlin, who led a gang of Guild members in dealing with the Stone Lord and his men. The new kingpin secretly served the Absolute and was trying to remove the Guild's influence from across the Gate. Farlin's group faced a band of the Stone Lord's men led by the tiefling Cairos, under the Wyrm's Crossing bridge in Rivington. However, the meeting devolved into a battle and his squad's fate remained uncertain, whether they were killed by the gangsters or survived and returned safely to the Guild's safehouse.
