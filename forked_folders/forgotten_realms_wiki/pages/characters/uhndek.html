Uhndek was the lizardfolk leader of the Khoroshen tribe, based in the marshland of the Evermoors in the 1370s DR.

Relations

Uhndek had a crocodile companion named Dross.

Description

Uhndek was short and stocky.

Possessions

Uhndek owned a suit of well-made crocodile-hide armor and carried a spear, Semuanya's Fang, which had been adorned with human finger bones and carved runes. He also owned a crocodile-tooth necklace.

History

In 1374 DR, Uhndek and the tribe were hired by First Speaker Tessarin Alaurun in defense of Nesmé, but Uhndek betrayed the city and made an agreement with the trolls to work together in attacking Nesmé; the lizardfolk would gain the city and the trolls the surrounding moors. The meeting to agree to this plan was interrupted by Alaurun's aide, Chaver Tegarech, who was captured and taken to mines below Baeniff Tor.
