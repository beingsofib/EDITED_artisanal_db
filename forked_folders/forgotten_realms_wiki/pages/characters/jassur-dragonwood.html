Jassur Dragonwood was a nobleman of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

According to a knowledgeable master of intrigues, Jassur was really nowhere near as smart as he thought.

History

In the lead-up to the Council of the Dragon in the , Jassur was mind-controlled by the evil archmage Manshoon, just as Rorskryn Mreldrake was denounced as a traitor. After the Council degenerated into violence, Jassur Dragonwood was killed.
