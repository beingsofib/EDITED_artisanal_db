Lightspark was a gold dwarf Harper who followed the High Harper Jaheira to the Shadow-cursed lands in the late 15 century DR.

Description

At the time of his death, Lightspark had a bald head and a beardless face. He wore a blue tunic, leather bracers and boots.

History

In the , Jaheira was alerted to the mysterious return of Ketheric Thorm, now a leader of a cultist army that worshiped the Absolute. Harper Lightspark was one of the agents who accompanied Jaheira into the Shadow-cursed lands surrounding the Moonrise Towers—General Thorm's seat of power.

When the Harpers found shelter in the Last Light Inn, protected by Selûne's magic, Lightspark was among the scouts who left the safety of the inn and ventured into the shadow-cursed lands in search of gaps in cultist defenses. He discovered that the cultist caravans that moved to the Moonrise Towers found a way of remaining unaffected by the deathly shadowmagic. However, Harper Lightspark did not get a chance to deliver his findings to the High Harper. He was found dead in the shadow-cursed lands, consumed by the curse.
