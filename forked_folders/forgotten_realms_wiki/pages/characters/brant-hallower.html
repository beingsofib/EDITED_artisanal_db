Brant Hallower was a werewolf servant of Selûne and member of the New Moon Pact in the 8 century DR.

Description

As a werewolf, Brant appeared as a heavily-built white wolf with bright eyes.

History

Brant served Selûne as a werewolf of the New Moon Pact, fighting against the faith's enemies in the darkness. In 757 DR, the High Moonmistress Brima Chintamn of Bright Lady's Tower in Chancelgaunt declared the New Moon Pact a heresy, and Brant and many other members were hunted down and executed.

When Feena Archwood investigated the mystery of the New Moon Pact in 1373 DR, Brant's spirit appeared in her dreams alongside the other members of the Pact to defend and guide her. After she uncovered Velsinore's heresy and they mutually slew each other, Feena's spirit was met by a Shard escort to the afterlife, but the spirits of the Pact members encouraged her to return to life and join the New Moon Pact herself. With the aid of Julith Harkspur's healing, Feena was revived and ultimately continued the legacy of the Pact.
