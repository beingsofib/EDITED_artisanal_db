Lyra was a young girl of Stag Falls in the mid-to-late 14 century DR, who was caught up in the village's takeover by Romanisk Vlaensk, an ancient vampire.

Relationships

Lyra was in love with Kitarno, a fellow youth.

History

When one spring in the mid-to-late 14 century DR the townspeople had to evacuate because of the takeover by the vampire Romanisk Vlaensk, Lyra and Kitarno got lost and went missing one night; during the night, Lyra was injured by the attack of a vampire bat. Blood loss kept her from being fully coherent when Kitarno finally found her at dawn; the puncture wounds on her neck made him suspect the vampire had bit her and she'd turn into another one. He quickly staked her heart, though his inept and bumbling attack failed to kill the injured young woman, and prepared a pyre to burn her body. Adventurers caught him in the middle of that.

The adventurers thankfully saved her.
