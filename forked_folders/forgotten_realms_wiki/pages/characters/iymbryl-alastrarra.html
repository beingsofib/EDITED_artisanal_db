Iymbryl Alastrarra was an elf wizard of House Alastrarra in Cormanthyr.

History

Iymbryl was killed on patrol in 241 DR by hobgoblins, but before he died he succeeded in entrusting his house's tel'kiira to Elminster Aumar.
