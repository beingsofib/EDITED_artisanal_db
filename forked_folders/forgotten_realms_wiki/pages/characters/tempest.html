Tempest was the murdered lover of Ren o' the Blade.

Description

Tempest was a very beautiful and muscular woman with fiery red hair.

Personality

Bawdy and quick as a street courtesan, Tempest was wonderfully talented in all her exploits.

Possessions

When hunting and stealing, Tempest preferred to wear black leather armor.

History

In 1339 DR, Ren and Tempest stole a pair of magical daggers and ioun stones from an assassin's guild in Waterdeep.

Later that year, the killers found Ren and Tempest in their Waterdhavian home and stabbed Tempest through her left breast.
