Gerde was a ranger who lived on the Sword Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

She was incredibly knowledgeable about ankhegs, their habits during mating system, and their relation to the rest of the natural world.

History

In the , Gerde sought to cull the ankheg population some farmlands south of the River Chionthar. While she appreciated their contributions to the ecosystem, she knew their population had to be kept at a reasonable level. She sought so hire some adventurers to help her in her efforts.
