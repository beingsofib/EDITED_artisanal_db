Thraxata the Flamefiend was a young red dragon in the Dalelands in the mid–14 century DR.

In 1370 DR, Thraxata was known to lair on Bloodhorn.

On Eleasias 7 of the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR, Thraxata set many fires in Battledale, consuming a large part of the woodland there.
