Sot was the not so famous innkeeper of the Laughing Goblin Inn though his pork and cabbage soup was renowned throughout Phlan.

History

Around 1290 DR, Sot's grandfather was a cleric of Tyr and guardsman at Sokol Keep on Thorn Island., gave his grandfather's holy symbol of Tyr to Tarl Desanea, which was similar to Tarl's own holy symbol but had different writing on it.

Personality

Though odd for some, Sot collected rare glass liquor bottles and he displayed these treasures behind his bar.

Equipment

Sot's weapon of choice for use at the inn and adjoining tavern was a large cudgel, which was basically a club or short staff. He and the cook would always reach instinctively for their cudgels when trouble was brewing at the inn.
