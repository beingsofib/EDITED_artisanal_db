Surrey was an apprentice blacksmith who lived in the town of Beregost during the mid−14 century DR.

Relationships

For a time, Surrey learned his craft under the tutelage of the famous smith, Taerom Fuiruim.

History

When the iron crisis struck the Sword Coast, in the , Surrey left Beregost in search of better prospects. He traveled north to the Friendly Arm inn, en route to the great metropolis of Waterdeep.
