Sir Voltroy was a member of the Church of Lathander from the city of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR.

History

Sir Voltroy was tasked with healing of young Lord Lorien Darkarrow's acid burns and bite wounds left by a black dragon. Voltroy arranged fellow cleric to cast the regeneration spell on Lord Darkarrow on a request from Mayor Thoden. Sir Voltroy's name was mentioned in the Uktar-Nightal issue of Ravens Bluff Trumpeter, .
