Susklahava Orbryn was a gold elf priestess of Labelas Enoreth from the elven realm of Siluvanede. She died and became an undying baelnorn. By 1370 DR, she had served her god for millennia at the Temple Beyond Time in Mhiilamniir in neighboring Eaerlann.

Alongside Roanmara Neirdre and Phantyni Evanara, she was one of the Timespinners: historians, oracles, sages, and seers whose advice was sought by the faithful on the temple's occasional returns to the normal time-stream.
