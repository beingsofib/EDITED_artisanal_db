Connor Thistledown was the eldest son of Bartholomew Thistledown of the Thistledown family living in the Silver Marches in the mid–14 century DR. He had a brief fight with Drizzt Do'Urden, but later the Ranger failed to save his family from murder by the barghest Ulgulu.
