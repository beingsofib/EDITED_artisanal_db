Darvoro was the owner of Darvoro's Plates, a manufacturing company located in Khôltar, south Faerûn, circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR.

Description

Darvoro was a pleasant man with light-colored hair and considered quite handsome.

Activities

Darvoro's Plates was a very successful business and Darvoro was a very rich man. The only thing his wealth could not buy was membership in the Onsruur (the council of lords that was the real power in Khôltar), and he was determined to become a member. The twenty members of the Onsruur were each from a family that had held their seat on the council for hundreds of years and some of them were adamantly opposed to adding any new members. Darvoro spent most of his time trying to gain leverage with the Onsruur by hiring spies to infiltrate the Onsruur households, paying bribes to clerks and government officials, and scheming to drive a wedge into the impenetrable circle, all to no avail. The Onsruur that were aware of his plots found him amusing, especially the Khaundroves.
