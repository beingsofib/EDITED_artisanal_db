Lhangaern was a wizard with the Brave Blades adventurers in the early-to-mid 3 century DR.

Description

He was old.

Abilities

Besides being a wizard, he could brew potions, perhaps not very well.

Relationships

He was a member of the Brave Blades adventuring band.

History

On Lhangaern tried to brew a potion that would rejuvenate his limbs. Instead of that, he fell into screaming dust on the spot, right before his fellow blades.
