Hes was a merchant from the City of Coin, Athkatla, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Galoomp was a portly man with light bronze skin. He was a pleasant and friendly man who gladly welcomed guests to Athkatla and dispensed directions and establishment recommendations.

History

Hes's stall could be found in the grand market in Athkatla's Center District – Waukeen's Promenade in the , despite reduced stock due the recent political troubles. Hes sold some mundane miscellany including armor, weapons, ammunition, and simple concoctions like antidotes and healing potions. If asked about magic items, Hes declared he has little taste for magic and directed shoppers to the Adventurer Mart.
