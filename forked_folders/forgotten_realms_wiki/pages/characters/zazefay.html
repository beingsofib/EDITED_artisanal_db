Zazefay was an Amnian gnome merchant and owner of the Zazefay Logging and Mining company in Amn's colony at Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the 15 century DR.

Activities

Under Zazefay's leadership, his company focused on extracting Chult's rare natural resources for export to Faerûn, He was known to meet with potential hirelings in person.

History

Zazefay was still leading his company as of 1479 DR, however Amnian companies like his were subsequently expelled from Port Nyanzaru by the Ytepka Society and a local merchant consortium in the late 15 century DR.
