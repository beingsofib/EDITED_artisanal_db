Lhargloroth was a dragon who lived in Oroth, in the realm of Eskorn, Laerakond.

Activities

As of 1479 DR, Lhargloroth was experimenting in Oroth, using living creatures to create strange monsters, most of them gibbering mouthers or rare sterile beasts, although a few of those were able to breed true.

History

Lhargloroth was a former servant who fled from Melauthaur at some point in the past and installed himself in Oroth. He was still there as of 1479 DR.
