Father Jon was a wise half-elf cleric/mage of the mid–14 century DR.

Description

The cleric/mage had white hair and brown eyes. He wore a holy symbol, bracers of protection +1 and a ring of protection +3. Father Jon carried a mace +2, a spellbook, and a wand of acid storm.

History

In 1358 DR, Father Jon entered the overrun town of Myth Drannor in attempt to establish order. His goal was to cleanse the Temple of Lathander and eradicate evil in the area.
