Benjy was a soldier of the Flaming Fist of Baldur's Gate during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

Benjy was an ally of Angelo Dosan, a corrupt officer that temporary took command of the Flaming Fist after aligning himself and his soldiers to Sarevok Anchev.

History

When Abdel Adrian and his allies were imprisoned at the Flaming Fist headquarters in the city, in the , Benjy oversaw the soldiers that guarded their cells.
