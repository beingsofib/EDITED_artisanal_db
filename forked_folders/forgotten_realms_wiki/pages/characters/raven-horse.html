Raven was a horse belonging to Vala Thorsdotter in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Raven was a large and strong black stallion.

History

Raven was descended from Sable, a mare of which Melegaunt Tanthul had apparently been quite fond

In the , Raven was taken to Evereska
