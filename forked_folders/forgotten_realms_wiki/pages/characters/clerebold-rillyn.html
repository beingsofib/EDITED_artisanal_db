Clerebold Rillyn was a member of the noble patriar Rillyn family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Clerebold had an idea he passed along to his cousin, Derque Rillyn, to his usual praise. Clerebold suggested that Nortale's Hostel, a non-profit hostel used to help refugees during the Absolute crisis, should be renamed after Derque's daddy – Hir Rillyn's Hostel, in order to convince him to donate a "good old chunk of change" in aid of the establishment and possibly even a bit of extra coin for Derque herself.
