Janussi was the Keeper of Tomes of Candlekeep during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Keeper Janussi was a devout follower of Deneir, the god of literature and scholars.

Activities

As the Keeper of Tomes, Janussi was responsible for the governance of Candlekeep as well as appointing scholars to various leadership roles within the order of the Avowed.

Relationships

The Keeper of Tomes was served by the eight Great Readers of the Avowed, along with the First Reader Skoda Valanaster.
