Ottolenszillasla, or Otto for short, was a violet faerie dragon living in Maddgoth's Castle in Undermountain in the 14 and 15 centuries DR.

History

Around 1483 DR, Otto was drawn into Undermountain by Halaster Blackcloak's magic, together with a stone giant clan. He enjoyed his new home, especially Maddgoth's Castle, whose magic did not affect the dragon. So Otto decided to settle there playing with the stone giants.

By 1492 DR, Otto was growing tired of the presence of Maddgoth's homunculus and started make plans to remove it.

Personality

Otto was a typical faerie dragon.

Activities

When bored, Otto played pranks on the nearby stone giants or fought with Maddgoth's homunculus as it annoyed him.
