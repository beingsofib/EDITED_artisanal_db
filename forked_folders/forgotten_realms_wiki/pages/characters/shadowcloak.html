Shadowcloak

Person
nameShadowcloak
homeSilverymoon, Luruar

Shadowcloak was a mysterious figure inhabiting Silverymoon in Luruar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Shadowcloak was known for assisting Silverymoon and the Knights in Silver during times of great peril.
