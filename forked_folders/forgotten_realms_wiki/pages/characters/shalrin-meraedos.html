Shalrin Meraedos was the Gentleman Keeper of Waterdeep's Solemn Order of Recognized Furriers and Woolmen and the proprietor of Meraedos Fine Furs in the North Ward in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He always wore a fur half-cloak, under which he concealed a pair of blades.

Personality

Shalrin was known as a careful, soft-spoken, and highly observant man.

Possessions

His favored weapon was a pair of swords of dancing that would fly back to him when called. He was also known to use other magical protection.

Activities

As Gentleman Keeper, Shalrin served as the Order's public contact.

Rumors & Legends

According to local rumor, Shalrin had secretly embarked on a lucrative adventuring career in Undermountain. He was reluctant to speak on the matter.
