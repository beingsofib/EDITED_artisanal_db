The Firemaster was a mysterious mage who traveled the Realms during the 14 century DR. He spent much of his time researching magic and often hired adventurers and mercenaries to aid him in uncovering ancient arcane secrets across the lands.

History

In Flamerule of 1357 DR he traveled to Westgate and Suzail, rumored to be searching for a group to delve into the ruins beneath the King's Forest north of Waymoot.
