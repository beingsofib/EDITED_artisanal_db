Roadrante Haltivur was a famous author in the 14 century DR.

History

Roadrante was famous (and infamous) for writing Tal Tessoran, an erotic adventure novel. After it was published she was inundated with offers to marry, ravish, redeem, or chastise her, depending on the reader's opinions, and promptly went into hiding with a false name.
