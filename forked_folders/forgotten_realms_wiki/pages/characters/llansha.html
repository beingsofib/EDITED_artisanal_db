Llansha was a priestess of Eilistraee in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

She was openly mistrustful of humans.

Activities

She participated in Eilistraeean rites during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

Llansha obeyed the leadership of the priestess Qilué Veladorn.

History

In , during one rite at the glade of the Ladystone, the priestess and Chosen of Mystra Qilué Veladorn was interrupted by the arrival of the Chosen of Mystra Dove Falconhand, who proceeded to request her help regarding the drow infiltration of Scornubel. The other priestesses were not supportive of Qilue helping Dove, with one of them attacking Dove outright and Llansha proclaiming the evils of humans. In the end, Qilue agreed to help, and called on Llansha to guide the rest of them in dance.
