Ivor was a settler in Chult during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

He was one of the men trying to settle in Chult.

Relationships

Ivor apparently held a leadership position over the rest of the settlers; in particular, Filar and Maddock seemed to be close to him.

History

Ivor was one of the men involved in the settling of Chult in the . Though he wasn't among the first to arrive at that site, he persisted in trying to get a camp up near the River Olung, despite a severe, magical storm that scattered his tools and damaged many of his belongings. However, he moved his camp somewhere far from that place after, during a fight with a strange mudman (actually the disguised old mist dragon Cirrothamalan), he caught a faerie dragon. On the advice of Filar, one of his men, he decided that made settling there a poor choice.

Rumors & Legends

They misidentified Cirrothamalan as a mudman, but once it fell to all fours, they still misidentified as likely a "mudbear".
