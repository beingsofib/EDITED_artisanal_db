Gaurldar Ravenhand was an evil adventurer in Myth Drannor in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR.

Activities

Gaurldar directed the Masked Marauders.

Personality

Gaurldar was a cruel and hard-thinking man. He loved wine, women, and killing. He dreamed of becoming lord of a hold and he would do anything to achieve success.
