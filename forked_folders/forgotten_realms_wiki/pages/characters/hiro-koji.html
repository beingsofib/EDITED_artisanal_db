Hiro Koji was a samurai retainer of the Niwa clan and jito of Hiwasa in Kozakura in 1357 DR.

Activities

In the fishing village of Hiwasa, he led a company of ten samurai and ninety bushi, plus an armorer and many servants. He kept an eye on all strangers. Koji was from the Hiro clan and a loyal follower of the Niwa clan and the Takenaka clan.
