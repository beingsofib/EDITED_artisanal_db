T'risstree T'orgh was a drow warrior and priestess of Lolth in Menzoberranzan and an instructor at Arach-Tinilith in the late 14 century DR.

Abilities

She was a good warrior, always wielding a falchion.

History

In the , T'risstree was an instructor at Arach-Tinilith, having risen in power despite her commoner origins. She approached Drisinil Barrison Del'Armgo and her fellow conspirators to offer her help in overthrowing Mistress Quenthel Baenre but Drisinil did not trust T'risstree and killed her.
