The Leviathan, sometimes called The Old One, was a huge whale and child of the Earthmother of the Moonshae Isles. It was regarded as a demipower within the Moonshaes.

Description

The Leviathan was larger than any whale naturally found on Toril at fully long with a tail that was wide. It had dark gray to black skin

Personality

The Leviathan was benevolent and generally peaceful, unless provoked or called upon to defend the Moonshae Isles. It was also known to rush to the aid of any distressed whales within of it, and (if hungry enough) to occasionally sink a fishing boat in order to eat its catch. It was not very intelligent, and could be lazy and slow to act.

Abilities

A creature of tremendous power, It was known to be able to sink ships with a single bite or slam of its tail. Its favored method of attack was to swim up directly beneath a ship and to bite it in half at the midsection, a tactic that could easily result in the accidental swallowing of many sailors. The creature's gullet was vast enough for such unfortunate individuals to survive for some time,

The Leviathan was mortal and could grow old and die like any other natural creature. Even so, its spirit would be reborn in time after it died. A new whale would assume its title a few decades after the death: this beast would begin to grow to tremendous size and any baleen in its mouth would transform to sharp teeth.

Activities

The great whale spent much of its time blissfully asleep in the silt and muck of the pitch black ocean depths, resting so soundly as to enter a state of suspended animation. It sleep like this for many years (possibly centuries) before rousing to surface for air and to hunt for plankton, kelp, and small fish to sate its massive appetite. It hunted not only around the Moonshaes but potentially throughout the full length of the Sea of Swords and even into the Shining Sea to the south. Unless the Earthmother required its service, it would then return to its hibernation.

The Leviathan was highly sensitive to poison and to pollution. It would succumb quickly to poisoned weapons or in contaminated waters.

Relationships

As one of the Earthmother's "children", the Leviathan heeded her calls to protect the Moonshae Isles from any threats, and was one of her most potent weapons. She could direct its actions through the power of the moonwells.

The Ffolk regarded the Leviathan as the protector of the seas of the Moonshae Isles.

History

The Leviathan was said to have been the first of the Earthmother's children, followed by Kamerynn and The Pack. It found its own way to the Isles circa , where it would become the defender of the shorelines and deep waters alike. Ffolk legends would claim that it was the first living creature to inhabit the Moonshaes.

When Kazgaroth first appeared in the Moonshaes circa , the Leviathan and the other children of the Earthmother allied with the Llewyrr elves and the dwarves to drive it off.

The behemoth slumbered for centuries until awakened by the Goddess in the , to combat the renewed rising darkness of Kazgoroth. It traveled to the Strait of the Leviathan to intercept a fleet of longships aboard which was a Northmen army sailing to attack Corwell under the command of Kazgoroth (in disguise as King Thelgaar Ironhand). It began to sink the ships one after another and to terrorize the helpless Northmen sailors. However, Kazgoroth had anticipated the Leviathan's arrival, and had ordered a number of ships to be affixed with battering rams that it then imbued with poison using its magic. As the Leviathan began to tire, Kazgoroth ordered the remaining ships to ram it, killing the massive whale. Although the Leviathan was slain, it had managed to sink a third of the Northmen's ships and to damage so many others that the fleet had to retreat for repairs, providing an important respite for the Ffolk in what would become known as the Darkwalker War. The next Leviathan would not be spotted in the seas of the Moonshaes for almost two decades.
