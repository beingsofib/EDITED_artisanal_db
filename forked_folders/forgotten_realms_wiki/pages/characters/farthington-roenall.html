Lord Farthington Roenall was a nobleman and the patriarch of the Athkatlan Roenall family as of the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was one to look out for his friends and their families and availed himself to those in need.

Relationships

Farthington had a son Isaea who served in the Amnian army. He seemed to not be aware of Isaea's illegal business dealings.

Despite their differences, Farthington thought highly of Lord de'Arnise and made a point to attend his funeral. He was fond of his friend's daughter, Nalia.
