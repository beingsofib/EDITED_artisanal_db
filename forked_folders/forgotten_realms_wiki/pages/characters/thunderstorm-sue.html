Thunderstorm Sue was an unsavory human mage mercenary from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Sue was a woman with brown hair, brown eyes, and fair skin tone. She wore teal-colored mage's robes.

History

In the , the opportunistic and amoral mercenary Aradin Beno set out to find the Nightsong for the mage Lorroakan. If the Nightsong, in reality a Selûnite aasimar, was to reach the city of Baldur's Gate, then Beno had a band of mercenaries with questionable morals ready to attack those who sheltered her. The wizard named Thunderstorm Sue was among those mercenaries, ready to break into the Elfsong Tavern to kill and kidnap.
