Slick Inatra was a half-elven agent of the Zhentarim in the city of Baldur's Gate in the 15 century DR.

Description

Inatra was a bronze-skilled half–high elf with blonde hair and brown eyes. She wore leather armor and wielded a long bow.

History

As of the , Slick Inatra worked under ranking Zhents Roah Moonglow and Friol, forging sticky alliances with the Guild and the Cult of the Absolute alike. The Guild began suffering losses at the hands of the Stone Lord, so Nine-Fingers Keene rolled dice and hired Zhentarim agents as muscle. Slick Inatra was among the Zhents stationed within the Guild's Guildhall in the city's Undercellar. Nine-Fingers Keene, however, did not know that Roah and Friol planned a betrayal. Taking out the Guild meant establishing a Zhentish presence in Baldur's Gate. With the city shuddering under the cult's attack, the struggle's outcome and Inatra's fate remained unrecorded.
