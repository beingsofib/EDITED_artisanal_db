Gavin Oxtundre was a fire giant blacksmith operating in Mount Hotenow in the late 15 century DR. He played an unwitting part in Acquisitions Incorporated's scheme of stealing deep crow eggs.

History

Sometime in the late 15 century DR, Gavin was tricked by an employee of Acquisitions Incorporated into a conflict with a deep crow that nested in Mount Hotenow, leaving the employee an opportunity to steal one of the creature's eggs.
