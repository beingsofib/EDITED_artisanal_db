Jebbrie Vokasich was a young boy of Liara in Nova Vaasa, one of the Domains of Dread.

Possessions

Notably, he carried a holy symbol of Ezra.

Relationships

His parents were Katerina Vokasich and Yassilli Vokasich.

History

At a time roughly matching the mid-to-late 14 century DR, after the caravan he was with stopped near a cavern, Jebbrie Vokasich was dared by some friends to enter the cave. There, he was bitten by strange spiders that bore the curse of werespider lycanthropy. Thanks to the holy symbol he bore, he was not in any risk of becoming a werespider, but his spider allergy coupled with the resulting affliction put his life in jeopardy; after the other kids heard him scream, they called for help. He was rescued by a ranger, Sundian Silgathen. Jebbrie became bedridden, to the horror of his parents, who discovered that the Church of Bane's spells were not enough to save him.

After adventurers were hired to combat the depredations of an apparent vampire in the city, his mother Katerina would go to them, too, and ask them to see if they knew why the spells were not enough to save him. Jebbrie's life would remain in jeopardy until the nature of the killer was revealed, raising the possibility the wrong spells were being used to treat him. Unfortunately, if the creature was discovered to be a werespider, Jebbrie's life would be in jeopardy for a different reason: a mob of people coming after him with torches and pitchforks.

Rumors & Legends

If it became known that the killer was a werespider instead of a vampire, then a mob would have certainly formed targetting the bedridden child in the aftermath of the revelation, convinced that he was the killer after being bitten. This was false, as the killer was Sundian. Ironically, if it was known for certain that Sundian was the killer, if her dead body was available, then an even larger mob would form, its ranks swelled with cowards.
