Rathdaen was a skilled wizard who lived some time during the 7 century DR.

History

Rathdaen traveled extensively, exploring much of Faerûn and aiding others with his magic when possible. Sometimes he was repaid for his kind efforts with other magic, which he collected into a tome later called the Tome of Rathdaen. When it was in pristine condition the tome was believed to hold over 60 potent spells.

Relationships

Upon his death Rathdaen passed the Tome along to his apprentice Narsel.
