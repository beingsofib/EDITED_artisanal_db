Kothar was a male raptor feared as one of the most dangerous predators on the entire Chultan Peninsula during its lifetime.

History

During his life, Kothar was known to the likes of the jungle guide Eku. He eventually died of old age.

By the late 15 century DR, Acererak had raised his corpse to serve as one of his mightiest guardians within the Tomb of the Nine Gods.
