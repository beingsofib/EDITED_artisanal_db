Shaliira Duon was an adventuress in Myth Drannor in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR.

History

Shaliira was born in Deepingdale but in her youth traveled to Sembia with her elven mother in search of her human father. However, they found the man had been assassinated in Ordulin by the Cult of the Dragon. Shaliira's mother was devastated and died soon after.

To make a living, Shaliira started to work in a tavern as a waitress and dancer. She started her training as a wizard in order to one day speak with her dead father and finally get to know him.

In time, she joined the Company of the Catlash.

In 1370 DR, the Company decided to go to Myth Drannor in order to acquire some magic item to empower them.

Activities

Shaliira was a member of the Company of the Catlash.

Personality

Shaliira dreamed of becoming a well-regarded person. She always needed to belong to something, her Company, to a community, or to a group. Despite bad experiences with men in her days at the tavern in Ordulin, Shaliira always hoped to find a new friend in all the people she met.
