Maeve was a half-elven Feloguard that stood watch over the "special" clients of Felogyr's Fireworks shop in Baldur's Gate in late 15 century DR.

Description

Maeve had a blush, fair skin, and hazel eyes. She wore her light brown hair tightly gathered into a bun.

History

In the , as the Cult of the Absolute planted its roots in Baldur's Gate and ravaged the Western Heartlands, Felogyr's Fireworks shop fell under the cult's sway. Its proprietor, Avery Sonshal, was made into a True Soul and used the shop as a front for the cult's terrorist activities. During that time, Maeve was employed as a Feloguard, standing guard of the shop's second floor and its clerk Tamara, who serviced "friends of Felogyr," selling them the shop's more dangerous weaponized stock, like bombs and smokepowder arrows. Maeve knowingly or unknowingly also guarded the shop's third floor that was off-limits to all but the Banite members of the Cult of the Absolute.
