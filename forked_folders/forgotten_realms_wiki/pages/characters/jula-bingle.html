Jula Bingle was a gnome alchemist in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Jula had blue-gray hair styled in a side cut, short on one side and long on the other.

History

In the , Jula joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their base camp at the ruins of Myth Drannor. She served as a trainer for fans of alchemy at the camp.
