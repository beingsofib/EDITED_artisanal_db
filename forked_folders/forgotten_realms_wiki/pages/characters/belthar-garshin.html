Belthar Garshin was a paladin of Torm that lived in Impiltur during the 8 century DR.

History

In the , the Battle of Horned Heads broke out in Impiltur between the nation's forces and the dark army of Soneillon. During the battle, Belthar slew Argosh the Scaled, the half-fiend spawn of the demon lord Eltab and whose consort was Soneillon.
