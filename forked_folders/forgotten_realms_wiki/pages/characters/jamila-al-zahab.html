Jamila al-Zahab was a mercenary who lived within the fortress of Sarahin in 1367 DR.

Personality

Strong and exotic, Jamila's demeanor transcended the norm of Zakharan society.

Activities

Jamila was a werelion who protected the leader of the Loyalists, Yusuf bin Ayyub.

Relationships

It was rumored that Jamila and Yusuf were lovers.
