Fukio

Person
nameFukio
homeShou Lung, Kara-Tur
occupationNinja, assassin

Fukio was a legendary ninja who was active in Shou Lung sometime before the 14 century DR.

Possessions

The ninja assassin possessed an enchanted robe – Fukio's Silks, that aided with stealth and being able to escape danger.

History

Fukio gained historical fame during the Omi War. The ninja was responsible for the assassination of the Emperor of Shou Lung.
