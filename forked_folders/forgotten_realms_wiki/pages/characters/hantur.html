Hantur was a merchant who lived in Marsember during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

He was a rather unscrupulous individual, readily willing to bribe his way to better fortune.

History

Hantur and his wife were caught in Marsember, when the city was being evacuated on Uktar 2 of the . He wound up bribing a member of the Marsember Watch, Kleef Kenric, to clear away a group of priests that were blocking his exit from the city.
