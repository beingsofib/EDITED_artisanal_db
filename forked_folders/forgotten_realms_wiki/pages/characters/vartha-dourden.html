Vartha Do'Urden was the mother and predecessor of Malice Do'Urden, matron mother of House Do'Urden.

History

Vartha Do'Urden was the matron mother of House Do'Urden when she suffered a sudden death in the 10 century DR. Her daughter Malice subsequently became matron mother, even though she was less than 100 years old at the time.

Relationships

Vartha was the mother of Malice Do'Urden and thus the grandmother of Malice's children: Briza, Nalfein, Dinin, Vierna, Maya, and Drizzt Do'Urden.
