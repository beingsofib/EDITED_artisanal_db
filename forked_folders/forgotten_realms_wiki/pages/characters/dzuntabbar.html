Dzuntabbar

Dzuntabbar of Thay was an individual of the mid-14 century DR who briefly required the attention of Elminster Aumar.

History

In late , Elminster Aumar revealed to Storm Silverhand that he needed to deal with Dzuntabbar of Thay before going off to help Shandril Shessair, shortly before her fight with Manshoon.
