Maskalandur Narlur was a sage of the city-state of Ormpur on the edge of Lapaliiya in southwest Faerûn in the 1360s DR. He styled himself as "Lorelord of Ormpur".

He made a special study of dragons, such as the song dragon Taraunramorlamurla who dwelled in Waterdeep. Narlur could be consulted on draconic matters.

He compiled his work in One Sage's Wyrmbook, which was published in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR.
