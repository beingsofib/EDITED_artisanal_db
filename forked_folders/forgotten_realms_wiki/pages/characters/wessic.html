Wessic the Wizened was a member of the reformed Cult of the Dragon under Severin Silrajin.

History

Wessic was an old cultist and, alongside Sorvic and Maelgot, was one of the bodyguards of Talis Kasterel in the Cult's occupied hunting lodge in the Greypeak Mountains. He acted as backup for his two companions.
