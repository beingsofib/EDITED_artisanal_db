Ghanim was a forager from Al Qahara in Raurin in the late 14 century DR.

History

In the , he left to investigate the nearby Palace of the Purple Emperor, which had been returned to Raurin by Pandorym, together with his companions Feraih and Haleem. He entered the structure with Haleem while Feraih remained outside, waiting for them to return; they never did.

Kiril Duskmourn entered the Palace some time after and was attacked by two elves from Al Qahara, now under the influence of Pandorym. She assumed them to be Ghanim and Haleem from the initials engraved in the arrow that one of them shot her with, but was forced to slay one as he kept trying to cut her down with a sword despite her entreaties to talk. The other used an arrow to put his own amulet connecting it to Pandorym inside an imprisoned water elemental, breaking its imprisonment in the process; the separation from its amulet slew it on the spot. The event most likely occurred on Tarsakh 19, the same day Pandorym was resealed.
