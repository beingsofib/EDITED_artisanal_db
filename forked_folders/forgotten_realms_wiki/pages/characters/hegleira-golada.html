Hegleira Golada was a businessperson inhabiting Tulmon in the Land of the Lions region of south Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Hegleira ran a factory called the House of Goloda that she inherited when her husband, and the business' founder, Fflaerin, died. The main product of the House of Golada—Golada's Fish Spread—was quite popular among the populace of the Arnaden.
