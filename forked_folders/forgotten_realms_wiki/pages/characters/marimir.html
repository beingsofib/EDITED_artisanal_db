Marimir was a clerk in Stag Falls during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

She was married to Jurgen, a young clerk from the local general store, and gave birth during a stressful situation.

History

During the evacuation of Stag Falls after it was taken over by the vampire wizard Romanisk Vlaensk, the population, including Marimir, who was pregnant, and her husband took refuge in nearby caves. She went into labor while the local priestess of Chauntea and midwife, Sidone, was not available; he had to get help from adventurers. Their competence on delivery was into question, and may have led to a childbirth just short of disastrous, though she would agree to name the child after the one who assisted with the delivery.
