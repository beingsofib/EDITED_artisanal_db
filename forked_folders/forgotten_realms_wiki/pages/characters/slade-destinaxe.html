Slade Destinaxe was a dwarf paladin of Moradin who adventured in Cormyr during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Slade had long white hair and matching twin-forked beard, wearing a suit of plate armor and carrying a large round shield.

Personality

Slade was very stubborn, simplistic, and aggressive in regards to the grand questions of good versus evil. He believed wholeheartedly that evil must be smote as quickly and efficiently as possible, with no planning or chit-chat beforehand.

History

Slade stayed the night at the ruined Beckside Castle on the High Road around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar. That night, he held Mystia Hollygrove hostage for hours with endless rambling complaints about his bard companion Lyrica Whitestone, who preferred to do more than kill everything they encountered. Mystia was eventually beaten down into just agreeing with whatever he said, in the hopes he would eventually calm down and stop.
