Lehreng was a mythical giant in the folklore of the people of Tulmon in the Land of the Lions region of south Faerûn.

Rumors & Legends

Legend stated that Lehreng was always hungry and lived "a thousand thousand years ago." He lived in the area that was later named the Shoulder of Lehreng.
