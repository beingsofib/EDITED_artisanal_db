Noreyth Harpell was a member of the Harpell family of Longsaddle and the master vampire of Dhusarra yr Fadila el Abhuk.

History

He was attacked in the , by the Company of the Red Dawn, who were sent by the vampire Artor Morlin because Noreyth encroached upon his territory.

Noreyth and Dhusarra escaped from the ambush, but she later murdered Harpell in his crypt.
