Senoon was a gnome sea mage who sailed upon the ''Ebony Queen in 1367 DR.

Activities

She was in the employ of Captain Soot.

Abilties

Spells known by Senoon included charm person, sea sight, wall of fog, ESP, true bearing, and stone hull.
