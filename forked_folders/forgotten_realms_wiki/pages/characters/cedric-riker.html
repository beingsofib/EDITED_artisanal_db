Cedric Riker was regent of Corwell in the Moonshae Isles in the mid-to-late 3 century DR.

Relationships

He was a member of the noble Riker family. He inherited his position from his father, Christopher.

History

Cedric became regent of Corwell after the death of High King Cymrych Hugh and the retirement of his father in the . Cymrych's heir, Warren I was still a child, and this led to disorganization among the kingdoms of the Ffolk. As Warren I established the Cymrych Dynasty from Caer Callidyrr, Cedric continued to rule the family's ancestral seat at Corwell as a steward of the Hugh Dynasty.

Cedric's tenure as regent outlasted both the reign of High King Warren I (which ended in ) and the reign of his successor, High Queen Tamara (), and ended during the time of High King Carrig Cymrych I.
