Crunch (Gondsman)

Person
nameCrunch
homeUndermountain, Waterdeep
raceGondsman
languagesCommon
alignment3eNeutral

Crunch was a Gondsman constructed sometime in the 14 century DR by the inventor Trobriand.

Possessions

Crunch was outfitted with a +1 mithral chain shirt and a +1 greataxe.

Activities

Crunch acted as an assistant, bodyguard, and friend to his creator Trobriand.
