High Priest Manarech Eskwuin is a priest of Tymora in the services of the royalty of Cormyr. In 1369 DR he was attempting to treat the stricken Duke Bhereu Obarskyr when the Duke vomited black viscous bile on him, causing him to flee in terror.

Activites

Manarech was the High Priest in the Towers of Good Fortune.
