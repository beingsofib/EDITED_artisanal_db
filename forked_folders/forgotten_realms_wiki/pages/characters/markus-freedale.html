Markus was a young stablehand in Freedale during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was friendly, and had a romantic view of adventuring, often asking passing adventurers what it was like to slay a monster or rescue a princess.

Activities

He helped his father with the stables.

Relationships

His father was Joben.
