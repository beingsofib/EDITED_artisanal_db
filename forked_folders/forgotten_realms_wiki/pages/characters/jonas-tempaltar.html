Jonas Tempaltar was a cooper that lived in the coastal town of Pros during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

In addition to his occupation crafting and repairing wooden barrels, Jonas was a point-of-contact for the Harpers in the region. If needed, he could meet with those agents that were not familiar with the Dragon Coast region of the Heartlands.
