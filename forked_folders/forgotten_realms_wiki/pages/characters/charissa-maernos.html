Charissa Maernos was a priestess of Tyr in Westgate around 1374 DR.

History

Charissa was a priestess of Tyr and a very strict woman, obsessed with always remarking on other people's actions. She proposed that churches of Tyr and of Lathander should be more direct in fighting evil. In truth, all her anger derived from a man who had ruined her family.

In the , Charissa may have helped an adventurer to fight the Night Masks. During that time, she found the man who had ruined her family and needed to decide whether to kill him or not.
