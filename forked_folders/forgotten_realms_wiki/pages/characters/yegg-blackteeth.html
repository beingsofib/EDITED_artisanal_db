Yegg Blackteeth was an orc shaman inhabiting Tarkhaldale, the Lost Dale, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was a wicked orc.

Activities

Yegg used his dark powers to aid the chieftain of a fortress within the Desertsmouth Mountains called Swordcrag.
