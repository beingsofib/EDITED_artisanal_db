Othglas Aumar was one of the Warring Princes of Athalantar.

History

Othglas was a fat, jolly, and food-loving man, with a penchant for poisoning his enemies. In the Warring Princes' fighting, when the magelords targeted him, Othglas escaped to Dalniir and joined the ranks of Malar's clerics.

However, the magelords turned him into a boar during a hunt, and he was slain by his own men in 220 DR.
