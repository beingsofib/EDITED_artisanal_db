Sim Piang was a young human fighter who had trained for most of his life to defeat the beasts that attacked his village in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He began training at the age of 11, and when he was 21 he was already quite respected for his fighting craft. Having already battled many a foul beast, he longs to fight and slay the evil creatures that haunts his home village.

Possessions

Despite his young age he had obtained a sun blade which he wielded alongside a dagger. He wore a suit of banded mail, and a pair of gauntlets of ogre power alongside a ring of free action. Around his neck hung a stone of good luck.
