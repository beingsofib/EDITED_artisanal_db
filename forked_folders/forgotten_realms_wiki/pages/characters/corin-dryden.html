Novice Corin Dryden was a servant of the Silver Flame in the world of Eberron.

Description

Corin was a young man with short brown hair and large sideburns. He carried a mace and wore a surcoat of the Silver Flame with a suit of chainmail.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, Corin attached himself to senior Inquisitor Gnomon, hoping that he could escape his family's poor legacy with faithful and wise service to the Silver Flame. When Brother Dectaran found the hidden base of the Lords of Dust in Stormreach, Corin followed along as Gnomon burst in on an aggressive assault. Unfortunately for Corin, Gnomon was actually a rakshasa spy and led him into an ambush, letting him be beaten near to death as Gnomon attempted to outrun the Silver Flame's pursuit. It was unknown whether Corin survived the encounter or not.
