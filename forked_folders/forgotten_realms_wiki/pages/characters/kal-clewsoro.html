Kal Clewsoro ran a camp near Spellgard in 1479 DR.

History

Clewsoro started as a soldier, later becoming a teamster and rising to become a merchant prince. In 1477 DR, he traveled to Spellgard seeking Lady Saharel's counsel. He did not trust the Monastery of the Precipice and established a private camp near the ruin.

Personality

Clewsoro considered all fellow seekers possible rivals and did not trust strangers. He loved talking about himself.
