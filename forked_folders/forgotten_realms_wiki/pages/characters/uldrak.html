Uldrak was a former empyrean in Avernus, first layer of the Nine Hells, in the late 15 century DR.

History

Uldrak went to Avernus to kill Tiamat and gain honor for his god Surtur. However, Tiamat defeated Uldrak and laid a curse upon the empyrean, polymorphing him into a spinagon. From that point forward, he plotted to regain his true form. Eventually, he discovered that Tiamat's blood was the key to reverse the curse. Unfortunately for Uldrak, the sample was impossible to obtain until the ,.

Personality

After millennia in isolation, Uldrak begun to lose his mind. He often argued with himself, even in front of strangers.
