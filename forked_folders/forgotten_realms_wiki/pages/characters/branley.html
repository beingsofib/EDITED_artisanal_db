Private Branley was a soldier of the Neverwinter Guard who fought for his city during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

At his post, Branley took to welcoming new adventurers and members of the city guard, briefing them of the situation as they ventured into the beleaguered Blacklake District. He offered particular warning against the group known as the Nashers and their leader Karzov.
