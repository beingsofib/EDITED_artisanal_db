Fist Aggralix was a member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 century DR.

Description

Aggralix was a fair-skinned, blonde, and blue-eyed gold dwarf. She had a clean-shaven face and wore a leather armor uniform of the Flaming Fist, armed with a heavy crossbow.

History

In the , Fist Aggralix was tasked with patrolling the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, near the Basilisk Gate. She was partnered with Fist Elthira Gossen, a zealous woman who sought to protect the city from the agents of the encroaching Absolutist army. Fist Aggralix stood in awe, impressed with Elthira's harassment of common folk. Some of the people harassed by Elthira were Drongo Bixworth and his husband, Florean Bixworth, who were detained and interrogated for the crime of loitering.
