Thaliach Mindogar was a young cleric of Chauntea in Archendale in 1370 DR.

Activities

Thaliach managed the temple of Chauntea in Archendale, the Bounty of the Goddess. He used the temple to manage a network of small businesses.

Personality

Thaliach was a young, energetic, and ambitious priest who wanted to restore the faith of Chauntea in the dale to show his abilities. He had the idea of creating a business to impress the mercantile inhabitants of the dale. Thaliach was rather a sickly and slim fanatic. He liked collecting maps.
