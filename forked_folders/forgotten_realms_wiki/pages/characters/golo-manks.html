Golo Manks was a human performer and Harper agent who worked in Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Golo was a heavyset man with a clean shaven face and head, dark skin, and a white outfit.

History

Around the time of the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon during the mid-1480s DR, Golo was part of Rina Belostos's Harper cell. When Oriphaun Huntsilver stole the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll from the shade lord Tyvrus in Wheloon Prison, Golo joined the pursuit of the Nether Scroll and Oriphaun, hoping to keep both out of the hands of Returned Netheril. The group stopped in Eveningstar for a time during the invasion from Sschindylryn, and searched up and down the High Road for clues while posing as a traveling band of entertainers. Golo helped sell the cover by performing alongside Vindavere Dyrwool as they both played the drums for the locals such as Trella Fulnor and Brinbard Meddle.
