Innanig was an orc shaman in the late 15th century.

History

In 1484 DR, during a new assault by orcs and drow on Silver Marches, Innanig led a contingent of troops against Nesme with his fellow goblin shaman Kllug. However, they were deceived by the reborn Regis.
