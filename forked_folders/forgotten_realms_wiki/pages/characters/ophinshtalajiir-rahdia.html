Ophinshtalajiir Rahdia was a dragonborn member of Clan Ophinshtalajiir, and a member of the Lance Defenders in 1487 DR.

Relationships

Rahdia was the daughter of Sepideh.

History

Rahdia was one of the Lance Defenders that survived the siege of Djerad Kethendi and were taken prisoners by Gilgeam during the First Tymanther-Unther War.

She and her companions were tortured and interrogated by Gilgeam about Djerad Thymar, specifically about the whereabouts of Nanna-Sin's body. When Rahdia refused to yield, she was killed alongside all her companions by the God-King.
