Amalzen was a guard captain of House Dun'Robar in Sschindylryn, who served at the estate during the invasion of Eveningstar in the 1480s DR.

Description

Amalzen wore a suit of plate mail and carried an enormous curved falchion.

History

Amalzen served as a senior officer of the house, but was unable to prevent the escape of Mirini Elemys to the surface. Dun'Robar was similarly unprepared for when she organized a vengeful raid on the estate to free the slaves and destroy the house's prestige, during which Amalzen was slain in combat.
