Thalloviir Vaundruth, the Bard of Beregost, was a musician and songwriter active circa 1344 DR.

History

In the , Thalloviir penned a popular ballad—Ever a Hero Be. The song was popular among adventurers as it featured lyrics about heroes delving into deep and dark dungeons while missing their loved ones and wishing for the safety of a home.
