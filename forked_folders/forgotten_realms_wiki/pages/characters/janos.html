Janos the Lichbreaker was a veteran Knight of the Weave, and the longest-serving member of the order as of 1374 DR. By that year, he had personally trained three other knights of the order.

Janos' was greatly concerned about the plots Shadovar who lived within the returned Netherese enclave, the City of Shade. He patrolled the area Anauroch around the flying city, hoping to stop all possible Shadovar plots before they could disturb the Weave, especially those involved the Shadow Weave.
