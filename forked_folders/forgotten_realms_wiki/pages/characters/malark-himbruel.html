Malark Himbruel was a wizard and a Wearer of the Purple of the Cult of the Dragon in in the mid–14 century DR. He also held the rank of Archmage of the Purple. He was involved in the hunt for Shandril Shessair.

History

In the , Malark tried to capture Shandril in Shadowdale and he also wanted vengeance for the death of Symgharyl Maruel, his underling. But he was ambushed and garrotted by the thief Torm, who soon after slashed his throat, ending his threat.
