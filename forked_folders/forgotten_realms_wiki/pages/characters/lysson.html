Private Lysson was a soldier who served in the Neverwinter Guard during the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

He wore a full suit of plate mail armor and helm that largely obscured his face.

History

After the siege of Neverwinter in the , Lysson was stationed in the city's Tower District. He acted as a liaison for Sergeant Creed, guiding adventurers to aid in the sergeant's efforts to take back the area from the occupying force of the Many-Arrows orcs.
