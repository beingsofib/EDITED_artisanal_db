Xara Tantlor was a wizard and shopkeeper of the Shining Scroll in Silverymoon in 1370 DR.

Activities

Xara worked as a wizard-for-hire but also managed a potion shop. All this was to fund her explorations of ruins and tombs in interior Faerûn.

Relationship

Xara lived with a faerie dragon companion named Villynk, who considered herself the true owner of the shop.

Possessions

Xara wore a mithral shirt that allowed her to move silently.
