Magnol was a firesoul genasi and the Steward of Fire of the Four Stewards of Akanûl in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Magnol was a powerfully built genasi.

Personality

Magnol was considered as a courageous person and he had the well-being and prosperity of Akanûl as one of his priorities.

Activities

As the Steward of Fire, Magnol was the general and second-in-command of the military forces of Akanûl.

History

In the , Magnol was sent by Queen Arathane to Chessenta with a contingent of the Akanûlan army, in order to help Tchazzar in his war against Tymanther.
