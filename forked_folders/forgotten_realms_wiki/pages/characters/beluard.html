Beluard was an apprentice to Manshoon in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was reticent to wade through zombies.

Activities

He was apprenticed to Manshoon and performed a number of important duties and tasks for him. Manshoon expected him to be up to date on his conversations with Sarhthor, implying Beluard was in both their confidences.

History

Beluard accompanied Manshoon late in the , to an encampment when the latter got ready to capture Shandril Shessair. Before Manshoon set off to fight her, he ordered his apprentice to arrange shortages of pork and sugar in Sembia.
