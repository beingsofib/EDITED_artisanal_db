Froga Gromman was an elder gnome of Ieirithymbul in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

He was a village elder in Ieirithymbul.

Relationships

Froga was married to Kaylindrra Gromann and, as an elder, acquainted with Palarandusk.

History

After a celebration in Uktar of the , Froga Gromann returned home to find his wife Kaylindrra taken hostage by four humans who had taken the guise of local gnomes Merem, Furnis, Sudo, and Drom. Under duress, he led them to a rocky outcropping where he had once met with the gold dragon Palarandusk. They knocked him out there, using him as bait for the dragon.
