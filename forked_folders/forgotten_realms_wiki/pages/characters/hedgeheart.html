Hedgeheart was a dwarven thief who fought for Exeter for payment.

Personality

He fought to win, no flashy tricks or other distractions, he was payed to kill Sagrais Bigelow, and so he would kill Sagrais Bigelow.

Possessions

He fought with an enchanted short sword +2, which, if he had time to prepare, would be treated with deadly poison.

History

during Exeter vengeance upon Wemick and the Nellie Thursday Home for Experienced Adventurers retirement home, he would duel Sagrais Bigelow attempting slay the halfling quickly and efficiently.
