Megthanet was a middle-class inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Megthanet was a gold dwarven woman with a clean-shaved face. She had dark bronze skin, brown eyes, and strawberry-brown hair. Her hair was tightly gathered into a bun and her clothes included a fine-quality jacket.

History

In the , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, Megthanet could be found in the Lower City, gossiping with her gnomish friend, Hilario Varota. Megthanet expressed her disgust with the food at a local eatery, the Elfsong tavern, and claimed that Duke Belynne Stelmane, who was murdered in the Elfsong, had her guts displayed in the symbol of the Absolute.
