Luer Dhostar was the Croamarkh, or elected leader, of Westgate in 1358 DR. in 1367 Victor killed and framed his father as the Faceless.

Activities

Luer was serving his second four year term as Croamarkh in 1358 DR.

Relationships

Luer was the head of the Dhostar noble family.
