Skouloun was one of the Nimbral Lords and one of the leading Elders.

Personality

He was a sharp-witted and sharp-tongued figure.

Activities

In the late 15 century DR, in order to gain money for his magical research, Skouloun sold many rival wizards to the Shadovar..

History

Skouloun was planewalking when the Spellplague hit in the , and so escaped the disaster.

He started studying a powerful longevity spell until in the , when he resurfaced and competed with his fellow Elder Yusendre and other archmages for possession of the Lost Spell, a key element for his own spell, during a spellstorm. However, he was killed by Tabra for his dealings with the Shadovar.
