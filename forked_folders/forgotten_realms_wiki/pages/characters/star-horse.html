Star was a horse from the Western Heartlands during the mid–14 century DR.

History

Circa the , Star was kept along with five other horses, Bandy, Cardinal, Ebony, Fowler, and Hopper, by the traveling company of the mages Druhallen, the married couple of Galimer and Rozt'a, while being cared for by their foster son Tiep.
