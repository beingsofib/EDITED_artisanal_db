Tormos Ollday was a human merchant of Tulmene, Tethyr, and the guildmaster of the Guild of Fishmongers and Fishermen there in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Perhaps the most unscrupulous merchant in town, Tormos engaged in all manner of illicit enterprises and dealt with the Rundeen, the Shadow Thieves, and pirates. However, he kept most of this behind various front businesses, so he maintained a reputation as a respectable and legitimate businessman who'd brought prosperity to this guild for three decades by 1370 DR. Should the guildmembers—mostly hardworking and honest folk—discover the truth, they would punish him severely.
