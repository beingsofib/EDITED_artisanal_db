Grand Duke Belt was one of the members of the Council of Four, the rulers of the city of Baldur's Gate. He served in this role during the years following in the Time of Troubles, in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Duke Belt was a human man with a pale complexion and chestnut hair.

Posessions

He wore a set of enchanted +1 chain mail. He wielded a magic +1 bastard sword and protected himself with a +1 shield. A ring of ruby adorned his finger.

Relationships

Belt's combat trainer was named Brevin, and he had a servant by the name of Jospil. Belt fathered Skie Silvershield with Brilla Silvershield.

History

Like his fellow dukes, Belt was taken in by the young and charismatic politician Sarevok Anchev, during the events of the iron crisis in the . However, unlike Dukes Eltan and Entar Silvershield, Belt came out of the events without suffering great personal harm.

In Uktar of that year, the War of the Shining Crusade threatened the entirety of the Sword Coast. Duke Belt and the rest of the Council offered their support to the growing Coalition army, which comprised soldiers from Baldur's Gate, Daggerford, and Waterdeep.

After the war, the young noblewoman Skie Silvershield was murdered in cold blood – a heinous crime that was attributed to the Hero of Baldur's Gate, Abdel Adrian. Duke Belt presided over Adrian's public trial, held in front of the Flaming Fist's city keep.

By the late 15 century DR, Duke Belt's memory lived on with his descendants. They had enjoyed the benefits of being one of the city's wealthy patriar families.
