Mhaumask of Mordulkin was a human wizard from the city of Mordulkin in Chessenta in the mid–14 century DR, and a member of the Zhentarim.

Around 1358 DR, he served as the most powerful mageling to Ruatheene and was based in a Zhentilar camp in the valley of Arntethyl by the river Tesh.
