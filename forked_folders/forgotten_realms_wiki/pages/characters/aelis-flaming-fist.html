Aelis was a solider who fought for the Flaming Fist and the virtues it upheld during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Aelis had fair skin and red hair she wore up in a braid. She wore a suit of scale mail armor with a tabard that boasted emblazoned the crest of the Flaming Fist.

History

Aelis was among the Fist soldiers present at the Waukeen's Rest inn when it was raided, and set ablaze, goblin and drow cultists loyal to the the Absolute. She joined fellow soldiers Ito, Pavel, and Gauntlet Yeva as they tried to break through the front doors of the inn, in order to rescue Grand Duke Ulder Ravengard.
