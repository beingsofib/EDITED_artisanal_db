Regweld Harpell was a member of the Harpell family which lived in Longsaddle in the Savage Frontier.

Description

Regweld was very fat.

Personality

The eccentric wizard loved crossbreeding animals and succeeded in making Puddlejumper, which was a mix of horse and frog.

History

In 1358 DR, Regweld protected Mithral Hall with his family members: Bidderdoo, Bella don Delroy, and Harkle Harpell to help defend Mithral Hall.

While riding Puddlejumper in the Battle of Keeper's Dale, Regweld was hit by three lightning bolts cast by enemy drow wizards as he battled a group of minotaurs.
