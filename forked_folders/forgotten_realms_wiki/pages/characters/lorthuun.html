Lorthuun was a beholder member of the Zhentarim in Mantol-Derith in the late 15 century DR.

History

Lorthuun joined the Zhentarim and was appointed to the crucial garrison of Mantol-Derith. At one point, it battled a behir, which left it greatly injured and missing four of its eyestalks.

Circa 1486 DR, it assisted Ghazrim DuLoc in keeping the situation in the trading post under control.

Relationships

Lorthuun had with Ghazrim DuLoc, the main delegate of the garrison, a relationship like that of a married couple, with them arguing and insulting each other often, but knowing both were loyal to the Black Network.
