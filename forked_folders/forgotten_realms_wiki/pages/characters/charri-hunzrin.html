Charri Hunzrin was a drow of Menzoberranzan and the heir of House Hunzrin in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Charri discussed with her mother, Matron Mother Shakti Hunzrin, about forging an alliance with House Melarn in order to attack the reformed House Do'Urden.

Some months later, Charri went alongside the young Shak'kral Hunzrin to a meeting with a clan of spriggan followers of Malcanthet in Damara arranged by the Hunzrin trader Denderida.
