Crimonel was an human wizard who inhabited the Shining South during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Crimonel was highly intelligent, cruel, and ambitious.

Activities

He lived within the Ajmer Forest in eastern Durpar. He planned to force all of the inhabitants of the forest to bend to his will. He enjoyed setting traps where the victim believed there was a chance to escape.

Rumors & Legends

Crimonel was actually a disguised rakshasa.
