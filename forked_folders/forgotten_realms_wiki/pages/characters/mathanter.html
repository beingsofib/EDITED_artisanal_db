Mathanter of Sembia was a member of the Rightful Conspiracy in the late 14 century DR.

History

During the Marsember meeting of the Rightful Conspiracy in early spring of , Mathanter of Sembia had left an impression on Narnra Shalace by bringing over a half dozen of sellswords with him. This made her remember his name later, when the War Wizards raided the premises; she tried to pass herself off as one of their allies by naming Mathanter as one of the people present earlier.
