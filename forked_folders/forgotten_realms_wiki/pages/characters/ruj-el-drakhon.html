Ruj el Drakhon was a syl-pasha of Calimport during the Third age of Calimshan.

History

Syl-Pasha Ruj was caleph in , when the Battle of the Samesaj Gate occurred. This was the battle against the beholders of the Alimir Hive during which the famous wizard Ali el Samesaj created a gate that transported reinforcements to the garrison at Akkabbel. The resulting victory at Akkabbel saw Ruj elevate Ali to be his Grand Vizar.
