Miriam (Sword Coast)

Miriam was a woman from the Sword Coast who fell in love with the man named Edmund who became a warlock.

History

At some point in his life, Miriam's lover Edmund became indebted to Maggie Two-Fingers. Somehow, Maggie learned of Miriam and began harassing her for the money. Edmund took decisive action. He agreed to an infernal pact with a cambion to gain warlock powers and joined a mercenary group to earn money, and pay back Miriam's debt.
