Rikhard Downriver was a halfling resident of Eveningstar during the 1480s DR.

Description

Rikhard was a hin man with long blond hair. He carried a hoe.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Rikhard stood on a bench in Market Square and exhorted people to face challenges like helping at the nearby Ruined Keep and defending the Grove of the Great Tree.
