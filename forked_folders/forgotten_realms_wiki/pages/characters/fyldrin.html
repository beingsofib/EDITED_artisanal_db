Fyldrin, who was also called Fyldrin of the Eleven Chairs, was a Rashemaar berserker inhabiting the town of Mulsantir in Rashemen during the late 14 century DR.

Activities

He led a small brotherhood of buccaneers who executed attacks on the coast of Thay.

Relationships

Fyldrin was the son of one of Rashemen's previous Iron Lords, Thydrim Yvarrg.
