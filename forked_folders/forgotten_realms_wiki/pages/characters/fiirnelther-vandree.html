Fiirnel'ther Vandree was the matron mother of House Vandree in Menzoberranzan in the late 14 century DR.

Personality

She and her house were considered even by drow standards to be vicious and vengeful, as well as ambitious.

History

She killed her mother and previous matron mother Troken'ther Vandree in the . Under Fiinel'ther's guidance, the Vandree rose quickly to become the eighth house and thus a member of the Ruling Council.

She was still in power by around 1486 DR.
