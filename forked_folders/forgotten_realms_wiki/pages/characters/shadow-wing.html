Shadow Wing was an extra-planar vargouille that came to reside in the ruins of Myth Drannor in the late 14 century DR.

History

Originally from a distant world, Shadow Wing entered Myth Drannor through a portal caused by its unstable mythal. It then laired in the ruins of the Dlabraddath district.
