Darthen Ironwright

Person
nameDarthen Ironwright
homeScornubel, Western Heartlands
sexMale

Baron Darthen Ironwright was a former member of the Company of the White Star. He retired to become lord of his own small hold near Scornubel. He was married and has a number of children.
