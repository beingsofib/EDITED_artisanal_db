Serena Worthwood was an adventurer from Ravens Bluff in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Petite and slender, she didn't look her strength. Serena had dark brown hair and green eyes.

Personality

Serena was curious and obstinate.

Abilities

She was a warrior and a sneak both, with great agility for a thief, and strong enough to gain an advantage in combat. She had specialized in two-weapon fighting.

Possessions

The half-elf bore a cloak of elvenkind and carried a potion of flying. Notably, she had a heirloom silvered long sword.

Activities

By the late 14 century DR, she had become an adventurer in Ravens Bluff.

Relationships

Serena's father was an adventurer.

She was quite comforted by Aliana Meriadeth's quiet wisdom, and had in turn become the only person to learn she'd been a werefox. She thought Akira Iko was not happy amongst them, probably because of something that happened prior. Crakadan Earthdigger reminded her of her dad, and she liked him. She didn't know if Taldar Everwind's feelings for her were serious, and didn't want to return them if they weren't; he'd also taught her thief skills. She found Tanis Obereck's principles interesting, if only in context of his childhood.

History

Serena's human father had met his mother while rescuing her and other elves from evil giants; he worked as a constable throughout her childhood. Both her parents taught her the good of all was more important than how it was achieved.

She met Tanis Obereck at the age of sixteen, and later began adventuring together with him and Taldar Everwind. Three years later, her family moved to Sembia, and she stayed behind to keep adventuring. Financial difficulties had recently required her to sell off her thieves' tools.
