The Rook Widow

Person
nameThe Rook Widow
homeThe Rookery, Nelanther Isles
genderFemale

The Rook Widow was a witch who was said to have known the the secrets of the last Shoon Emperor, Amahl Shoon VII, so she was exiled to the Rookery in the Nelanther Isles, where she made her home. Her cause of death was unknown but as of 1370 DR she still walked on the island as a ghost.
