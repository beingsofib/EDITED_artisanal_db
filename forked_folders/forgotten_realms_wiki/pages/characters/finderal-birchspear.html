Finderal Birchspear was an elven soldier from New Sharandar who was tasked with the protection of his people.

Activities

Finderal led a group of Iliyanbruen elves in an offensive against the forces of the Feydark when they threatened the natural growth and wildlife found in the Feywild.

History

After King Malabog began his campaign against the elves of New Sharandar circa the , Finderal accompanied a few other elves to an outpost near the dark fey enclave in the Feydark. He helped the Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge to cull the scores of dark fey creatures, by wiping out nests of quicklings, as well as slaying fomorian witches and armored troll warriors.
