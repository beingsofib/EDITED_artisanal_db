Mirabel was a Red Wizard diviner and an ally of the Legion of the Chimera in the early 14 century DR.

Possessions

Mirabel wore an intricacy carved enchanted ivory cameo that depicted a bald woman's visage. Its backside was engraved with two letters of the Mulhorandi language – "A.L."

History

In the , Mirabel was sent to the Z'hinda Citadel underneath The Spine of the World by her masters to kill a group of mercenaries from Targos. Her Red Wizard masters were allied with the Legion of the Chimera and sought to stop the adventurers' advancement towards the secluded town of Kuldahar. However, Mirabel and her partner, Majrash, both were killed by the heroes from Targos.
