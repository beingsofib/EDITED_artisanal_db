Murith Garas was a member of the Red Wizards during the 14 century DR who oversaw the sale and export of arcane wares in the city of Proskur.

Murith was the third-most senior Red Wizard within the city's enclave. He headed the Stronghouse, a large complex that housed a number of shops that provided a variety of magical goods and services. He was also personally responsible for the security of the Stronghouse's secret vault, which housed an inventory of a great many exotic and dangerous arcane items held by the Red Wizards of Proskur. As of the late 14 century, the total value of the cache was estimated to be well over 100,000 gp.

Relationships

Among Murith's subordinates were Tarithi, the apothecary and mid-level bureaucrat of the Guild of Foreign Trade; the potion-andscroll merchant, Thazal Tabar; a Red Wizard tattooist named Urhun Kren; the eccentric alchemist Kethin Zurn; and Ramshan Doon of the Arcane Gallery, among others.

Like all the Red Wizards within Proskur, Murith answered to the khazark of the enclave, Chathin Zurn.
