Gorar al-Aksar was a famous barber and owner of the Barber Shop of Gorar al-Aksar in the Grand Bazaar of Huzuz.

Personality

To say Gorar was popular was an understatement. Even his rival barbers loved him.

Activities

This singing barber enjoyed providing customers with horoscope readings, even if they did not want them. His storytelling abilities were renowned, often drawing listeners to his shop.

Possessions

Gorar owned a smoking efreeti bottle that he kept on display in his shop.

History

Gorar was a fixture of the Grand Bazaar for over 40 years.
