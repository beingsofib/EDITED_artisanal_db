Meredin Sandyfoot was the master of the thieves' guild in Bral in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

By the time he headed the guild, Meredin Sandyfoot was fifty winters old, yet had a cheerful positive personality.

Possessions

Meredin wore a set of enchanted +3 leathers, a cloak of displacement, and a ring of invisibility. He was armed with a magic +1 luck blade.

History

As the leader of Bral's thieves' guild, Meredin controlled fences and smuggling in the Middle City, never resorting to bloodshed and always resolving conflicts via rewards and bribery.
