Dreb was a barbed devil in Avernus, first layer of the Nine Hells, in the late 15 century DR.

History

When the city of Elturel was transported to Avernus in the , Dreb led a cast of giant crabs through the High Hall cathedral. Though otherwise normal, these crabs had spiny shells and stank of brimstone.
