Bennik Vandyrmir was an employee of Sa'Sani's Mercantile House that operated in the village of West Harbor in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Bennik was a serious man with short blonde hair, pale skin, blue eyes, and chiseled face.

History

In the , Bennik was one of the local merchants who operated on the Sword Coast North. When merchant princess Sa'Sani expanded her trading coaster, Bennik Vandyrmir was among her hires. The man was put in charge of the Mercantile House's trading post in a remote swamp village of West Harbor, famous for its main export – Harvest Mead. Despite working for Sa'Sani, Bennik never offered any of his stock for free, not even to Sa'Sani's hirelings nor business partners.
