Calahan was a fisherman and sailor who lived in Brynnlaw.

History

Calahan first lived in Ulgoth's Beard. Mistress Mallory believed that Calahan was spreading vicious rumors about her. He admitted that this was indeed the case, but that Mallory had been performing suspicious activities. She hired Scotty McAverstein to beat him up, and sent him a one-way ticket to Brynnlaw.

From there on out, Calahan found his place as a smuggler, where he illegally moved goods such as olive oil.

Rumors

It was rumored that Calahan risked smuggling two unfortunate siblings, Ginia and her brother, from Brynnlaw to Ulgoth's Beard.
