Tylander Roaringhorn was a noble resident of Amphail during the late 15 century DR. He was a member of the Waterdhavian Roaringhorns.

History

On his 63 birthday, Tylander imported the finest libations from Waterdeep and threw a grand, celebratory banquet in Amphail's town square. A group of hill giants were attracted by the smell of the food and briefly crashed the party.
