Entar Silvershield was a nobleman from the wealthy Silvershield family living in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century.

Relationships

Entar was the younger son of Torlin Silvershield and Evelyn Silvershield.

Entar's son Parendarkh left the family after a quarrel, but he still lived in Baldur's Gate in the .

History

Entar was born in the Silvershield family and lived in the Silvershield estate in Baldur's Gate. In the , Entar was six and he was living in the estate's nursery with his sister Alana.

He had children, but some of them left Baldur's Gate in the 1470s DR and there was no information about where they had gone or what had become of them.
