Jalantha Mistmyr was a human priest of Umberlee in Baldur's Gate in the mid–14 century DR.

History

By 1358 DR, Jalantha Mistmyr was the high priestess of the Water Queen's House temple, holding the title of Storm-Priestess. She led eight zealous priestesses and sixteen followers by 1367 DR.
