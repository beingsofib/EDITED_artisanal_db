Tollar Kieres was a sailor of Baldur's Gate and a member of Merchant League.

History

While searching for the sea charts needed by Mendas, Gorion's Ward met Tollar. When found, Tollar exchanged the charts for some of De'Tranion's Baalor Ale from the Blushing Mermaid tavern.
