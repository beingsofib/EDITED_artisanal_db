Tyranis Shagal

Tyranis Shagal was a bloodforge mage who laid claim to Herne's Wood in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

Shagal was known as a great mage with impressive achievements.

An itinerant bard told the leader of the Legendary Campaign about Shagal. Seeking to win glory by defeating a worthy opponent, the leader issued a challenge to Shagal to fight a bloodforge battle.
