Gereth was an aged gray-haired magician from the City of Coin, Athkatla in Amn in the 14 century DR.

History

In the , Gereth was a regular shopper at the Adventurer Mart in Athkatla's grand Waukeen's Promenade. He searched for salamander dust while being wary of the notorious Cowled Wizards. If anyone asked, Gereth claimed to need the substance of soup. The wizard refused to ask the proprietor – Ribald Barterman for help with salamander dust as they were not on speaking terms after Gereth's claim that the shopkeep overcharged him for a bottle of pitch and a bundle of mice tails.
