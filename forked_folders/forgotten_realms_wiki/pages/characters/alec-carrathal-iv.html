Alec IV of the Carrathal Dynasty was High King of Callidyrr at the turn of the 14 century DR. Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles, he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal royal family of Callidyrr and was the first son of his predecessor, King Benjamin. His eventual successor was his third son, Reginald, who would go on to be the last Carrathal to sit the throne.
