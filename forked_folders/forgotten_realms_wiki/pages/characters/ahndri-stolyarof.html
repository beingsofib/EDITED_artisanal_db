Ahndri Stolyarof was a musician and a lute-player of some renown from Faerûn who lived sometime before the late 15 century DR.

History

Ahndri was known for playing a unique heavy deep-voiced lute, later known as Stolyarof's table lute. Notoriously, Ahndri never allowed anyone else to play the instrument. It was unknown if or when Ahndri Stolyarof perished, but by the , Stolyarof's table lute was owned by Thomas C. Quirkilious, the dragonborn proprietor of the Chromatic Scale musical instrument shop located on the second floor of The Glitter Gala gem and jewelry vendor of the Lower City.
