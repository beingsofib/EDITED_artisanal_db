Mikrom Sum was a Purple Dragon of Cormyr, who served in Eveningstar during the invasion from Sschindylryn in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Mikrom was a large and powerful human, with his hair shaved on the sides. He wore a suit of chain mail with a white surcoat.

History

Mikrom oversaw the supply of equipment to Cormyr's forces and their allies, as well as handling repairs and upgrades.
