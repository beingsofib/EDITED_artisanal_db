Toron was a tiefling from Elturel that was forced from his home city along with others of his kind in late 15 century DR.

Activities

He spent his time tending the oxen that accompanied his fellow Elturian tieflings.

History

When Elturel was briefly pulled down to the Nine Hells in the , and subsequently returned to Faerûn, all the tieflings in the city were exiled from its borders. Toron joined a group led by Zevlor and traveled with them west to Baldur's Gate, before being forced to find refuge at the Emerald Grove druidic enclave due to increased goblin attacks.

The compounded instances of bad fortune gave Toron much anxiety and left him feeling as if his fellow tieflings had no good course of action.
