Louie was a thief from Procampur in the Vast during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Abilities

Even in streetclothes, he was quite adept at defending himself, denoting heroic agility.

Possessions

He had some dust of tracelessness.

Relationships

He traveled with a caravan.

History

About a month after the vampire wizard Romanisk Vlaensk took over the town of Stag Falls, Louie was in Eaglton with a caravan. He decided to practice his pickpocketing on one of the guests at the Eagle's Eyrie, selecting entirely at random one of the adventurers sent by Marcus Rubicon to find the patrol headed for Stag Falls, investigate, and report back.

If he was caught, Louie would bargain with his victim, offering them some dust of tracelessness in exchange for not involving the authorities. If the authorities were involved despite his offer, then Beomult Ragner, innkeeper and local judge, would order Louie locked up.
