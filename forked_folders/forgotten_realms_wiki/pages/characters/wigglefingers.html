Wigglefingers was a mage and captain of Morada Topolino, the assassin/thieves' guild run by Pericolo Topolino in Delthuntle.

Description

Wigglefingers wore a mustache.

One of his chief responsibilities in the guild was casting spells to determine the location of oysters that would suitable for the cultivation of pink pearls. He also cast spells of water breathing and furnished Spider Parrafin with potions of water breathing.

Relationships

Wigglefingers was Pericolo Topolino's closest friend and advisor, as well as a captain of Morada Topolino. They grew up together on Delthuntle's streets.
