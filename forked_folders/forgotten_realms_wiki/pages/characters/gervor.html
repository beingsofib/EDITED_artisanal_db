Gervor was an adventurer prisoner of the Cult of the Black Earth at the Temple of the Black Earth in Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

In 1491 DR, Gervor and his adventuring party were exploring the Sumber Hills when they were attacked by Black Earth cultists. Only Gervor survived, despite being greatly injured. He was put in the Temple of the Black Earth's jail until some other adventurers

defeated the jailer cultists and freed him.
