Fel Suparra was a cultist and a member of the Knights of the Black Sword that arose in Caer-Dineval during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Fel was tasked with guarding the gatehouse of the Caer, the eponymous castle of Caer-Dinveal.

Relationships

Fel worked alongside fellow cultist Huarwar Mulphoon.
