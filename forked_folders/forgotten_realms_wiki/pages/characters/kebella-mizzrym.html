Kebella Mizzrym was a drow agent of House Mizzrym who infiltrated the city of Zhentil Keep during the late–14 century DR.

Activities

Kebella operated out of Sunset House within southern Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of the Foreign Quarter. Though the drow's presence in Zhentil Keep was a secret, she planned to insinuate herself into the inner circle of the city in order to use Zhentil Keep as a launching point for attacks on elves to the south and to control the Moonsea coastline.

Relationships

Her consort, who was also with her in Zhentil Keep, was Taybar Rozziss.
