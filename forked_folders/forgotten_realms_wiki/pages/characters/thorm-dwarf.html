Thorm was a dwarf merchant of the late 15 century DR.

History

He partnered with Tatigan Carrlock and another merchant in the , to form a caravan that journeyed to Rashemen. Unknown to Tatigan, Thorm was secretly working with bandits who attacked the caravan along the Golden Way.

Throm was killed in a battle with his former comrades when the witch Ilvani and Daruk the bard used illusion magic to hide a crevasse into which Thorm fell.
