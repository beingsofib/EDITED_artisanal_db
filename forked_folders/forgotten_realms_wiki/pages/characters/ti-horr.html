Ti Horr

Person
nameTi Horr
homeNoko-Ji Peak, Tabot
occupationHermit

Ti Horr was an old hermit and wiseman who lived on Mount Wiz'tcu Tan in Tabot.

Horr was one of only five wisemen recognized by the monks of Tabot around as being a true oracle, being capable of predicting the future and raising heroes from the dead for good causes.
