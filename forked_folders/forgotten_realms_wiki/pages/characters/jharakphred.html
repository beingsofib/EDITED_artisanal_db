Jharakphred was a painter in Suzail during the late 15 century.

Description

He was runty.

Personality

Jharakphred was nervous, whimpering and simpering.

Abilities

The man was perhaps the best painter-for-hire in Suzail.

Activities

He was a painter for hire.

History

Jharakphred was hired by Marlin Stormserpent to paint the likenesses of Draskos Crownsilver and Gariskar Dauntinghorn, whom the painter followed for days until their retinues beat him away. He then painted their likenesses and brought them to Marlin. After being paid, he was quickly skewered by two blueflame ghosts, Relve Langral and Treth Halonter, who threw his body into a furnace, again, on Marlin's orders.
