Esu Kabloona was a human wizard living on the Great Glacier with her husky familiar in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

She was adopted by a native tribe after her parents, who came from the south to preach the word of Sune, died of exposure to the extreme cold. When her wizarding became good enough, she attracted a husky dog familiar.

Possessions

She wore a pair of gloves of missile snaring and a set of boots of the north alongside a cloak of useful items.
