Reetha was a barbarian inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid–1360's DR.

Description

Reetha had blonde hair and was heavily muscled. She stood an imposing tall.

Activities

She worked as a guard within the Blushing Mermaid tavern and festhall in Waterdeep's Dock Ward. She enjoyed wrestling with men who matched her size and strength.
