Soqaxu was a lizardman warrior of a tribe in the Flooded Forest.

Personality

Soqaxu was a smart and experienced warrior. He was willing to use others to do his dirty work.

History

In 1491 DR, after strangers made a camp near the forest, he was sent by his king to lead a patrol on a fact-finding mission. During the mission, he met an adventuring party hired to clear the area of bandits.
