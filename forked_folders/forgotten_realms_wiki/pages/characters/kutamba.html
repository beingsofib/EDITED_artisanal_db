Kutamba was a wandering druid of the Samarach jungles on the Chultan Peninsula in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Kutamba dressed himself in an assortment of black animal furs.

History

Circa 1374 DR, Kutamba was a threat to adventurers foolish enough to wander through the jungles of Samarach. He could be seen wandering through the jungles akin a dark sinister shadow confronting outsiders. The druid enforced the law of jungle on his victims - the lady of "eat or be eaten." Kutamba was always accompanied by his "children" – three deadly black panther.
