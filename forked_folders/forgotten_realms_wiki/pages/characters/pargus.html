Pagrus was an adventurer and a member of Maulers of Undermountain that ventured on the Sword Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

He wore a pair of Bracers of defense, wielded a +1 longsword, and carried on him a number of spell scrolls, including those of lightning bolt, melf's acid arrow, and larloch's minor drain, among others.

History

At some point in the , Pargus and the Maulers were staying at the Helm and Cloak inn found in the city of Baldur's Gate.
