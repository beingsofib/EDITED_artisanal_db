Lanatiramae was an elven cleric and adventurer working near Myth Drannor in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Lanatiramae grew up in the grand elven city of Evereska, and while she was comfortable with staying in her homeland, her sisters prodded her to explore more of the world and hopefully achieve some glory. After enough prodding, she began amassing expertise and nerves to explore the ruins of Myth Drannor.

Possessions

She wore a set of enchanted studded leather armor and a holy symbol, while wielding an ordinary shield and enchanted longsword and longbow.
