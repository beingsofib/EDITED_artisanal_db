Brightstar Risingsun was a foreigner living in Ammathtar in northeast Dambrath during the 14th century DR.

Relationships

Brightstar was the consort of the Duchess of the North, Tralia Falrith. He became Tralia's consort after the death or disappearance of her previous consort, Skield
