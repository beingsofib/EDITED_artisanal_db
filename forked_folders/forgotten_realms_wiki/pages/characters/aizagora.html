Aizagora the Red Death, also known in her human form as Arogazia Firewind, was a female red dragon allied with the Zhentarim who tried to obtain the power of the Onyx Tower in the mid–1370s DR.

Description

In her true form, Aizagora was a massive red dragon. While in her Arogazia Firewind guise, she appeared to be a human woman with a light skin tone and sharp features. She wore a gem-studded circlet and jewelry round her neck. Arogazia's hair was red and short, styled to stick up, evoking the image of flames. She wore a lavish yet sinister-looking red gown.

Possessions

Aizagora owned the Firewind Manor located in the Cloak Wood just south of Baldur's Gate.

Relationships

Aizagora used the services of Luvia Bloodmire, a mad mage who conducted sinister experiments, crafting undead and construct creatures by melding her victims' bodies and various exotic black market parts. Luvia's creations for Aizagora included a dangerous brute called Argesh and the Red Queen, made in Arogazia Firewind's likeness. The Red Queen was tasked with the leadership of the Red Fang Marauders.
