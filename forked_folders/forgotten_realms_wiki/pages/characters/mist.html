Mist was a spy and assassin inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Mist was a member of the Zhentarim. He worked in the Black Network Headquarters in the eastern section of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter, serving as a spy and assassin for the ranking priestess of Cyric, Synnda.
