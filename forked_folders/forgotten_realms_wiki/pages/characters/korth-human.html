Korth was a fighter in Ordulin in 1357 DR.

Activities

Korth mostly worked as a bodyguard-for-hire for people who went to "tavern crawls". He also guarded caravans and valuables or went to find and capture rivals and criminals. He could be found in the The Cap Askew tavern, sometimes indulging in a brawl.

Personality

Korth liked his life. He was a heavy drinker and womanizer. Despite many injures sustained, he never thought to change his life.
