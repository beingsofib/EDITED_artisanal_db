Burnas Tellrot was a shady fortune-seeking adventurer from Daggerford circa 1372 DR.

Description

Burnas Tellrot kept his face hidden in the shadows of his woolen cloak's hood. He claimed he hid his face due to terrible hideous scarring.

History

In 1372 DR he was staying at the Happy Cow inn searching for mercenaries. He asked anyone interested to listen to the tale of Peredus the Great that Jorey Fontalas, the bard entertainer of the Happy Cow gladly performed. Burnas Tellrot was searching for the well, mentioned in Jorey's poem, as it indicated the spot where the hero of legends has perished. Burnas was hoping to find the magic sword that was prominently featured in the legends of Peredus the Great.

Burnas Tellrot was offering to split the loot he was hoping to lift off the hero's long-dead body, with anyone willing to help the shady halfling find the final resting place in the well. Burnas hoped to use the treasures of Peredus to finance his retirement.
