Milon Tillerturn was a Lord and a senior member of the noble patriar Tillerturn family of Baldur's Gate and a member of the Parliament of Peers in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Milon was a handsome human man with a dusky skin tone, dark brown eyes, curly black hair, and a set of bushy muttonchops. Milon wore an expensive ensemble, tan in color, decorated with a teal trim.

History

In the , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldur's Gate, Lord Milon Tillerturn was a Parliamentarian of the Gate and voted in support of crowning lowborn Lord Enver Gortash as the city's Archduke. Milon was willing to move with the times and throw in his support for a commoner, despite it being an unprecedented occasion. Lord Tillerturn was present for Gortash's coronation that took place in the audience hall of Wyrm's Rock. Following the coronation, Milon Tillerturn conversed with Lady Silifrey Sashenstar near a table of smallbites and libations.
