The Lost Liege of Loudwater was a legendary ruler of the settlement of Loudwater in the Savage Frontier, who held the title some hundreds of years before the late 15 century DR.

Description

If the carved tomb's likeness was to be believed, the Lost Liege of Loudwater appeared to be a human man with a thick, full beard. He was interred wearing a crown and a set of ceremonial armor.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Tessalynde Halendria's secret contact provided her with a blank map that led to a long-lost tomb of the fabled Lost Liege of Loudwater and his treasures. After the map's secrets were revealed via Cazrin Varaith's spell, the group of adventurers known as the Fallbacks ventured out into the nearby mountains to find the booty. The tomb's entrance was clouded in sickly green vapors and guarded by roaming skeletons and zombies. In the depths of the tomb, the Fallbacks found the treasure room filled with gold, chests, and the Lost Liege's burial vaults adorned with a carving, presumably, in his likeness.

One of the items stolen by the adventuring graverobbers was an evidently enchanted pendant with a carving of a dragon. In the hands of the group's bard, its magic came to life, releasing an angry white dragon.
