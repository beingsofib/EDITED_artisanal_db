Beldan was a drow elf that lurked in the Low Lantern.

Relationships

Beldan had a twin known as Aerith. They were described to be both awkward and inseparable.

History

Before 1492 DR, Beldan and Aerith escaped from the Underdark to adventure on the surface world.
