Rylveth was a drow Zealot of the Absolute stationed in the Moonrise Towers during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Rylveth wore the uniform armor of a Zealot of the Absolute: a fur-cloaked night-blue armored garb and a metal helmet. She had a lighter skin tone, and her eyes had a lavender color.

History

In the , Rylveth was among those who joined the new religion that sprang up in the Western Heartlands. Rylveth abandoned the Lolthite faith in favor of the Absolute. She was stationed within the Moonrise Towers, a fortress controlled by the Absolute's warlord, General Ketheric Thorm. She stood guard at the Towers' stairs to the second floor, all while maintaining peace and discouraging infighting. Like many other members of the cult, Zealot Rylveth's fate remained unknown as she could've perished in battle when the High Harper of Baldur's Gate Jaheira and her True Soul allies stormed the Moonrise Towers.
