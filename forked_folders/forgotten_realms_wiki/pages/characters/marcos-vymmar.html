Marcos Vymmar was a man of the Vymmar family who served as regent for the Amnian Torlath dynasty in the 7 century DR.

Relationships

Marcos was the father of King Esmel II's wife, and grandfather of Esmel III.

History

Marcos' daughter and eldest grandson disappeared in 600 DR when the realm of Minsorran was destroyed. His son-in-law King Esmel II died of grief the following year, resulting in the young son Esmel III taking the throne. Marcos acted as regent for his grandson until the .
