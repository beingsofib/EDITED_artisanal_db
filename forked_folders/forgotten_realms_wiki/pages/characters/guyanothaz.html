Guyanothaz was a venerable red dragon inhabiting western Unther during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Guyanothaz laired within Mount Temmikant in the western reaches of the Smoky Mountains. He was attacked and blinded by a group of adventurers who called themselves the Dragon's Bane. They also took most of his treasure.
