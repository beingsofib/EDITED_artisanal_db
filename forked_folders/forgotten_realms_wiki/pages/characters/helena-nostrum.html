Helena "Hook" Nostrum was a giantslayer who roamed around the Savage Frontier in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Helena had a strong will and a good sense of leadership, capable of keeping people under her command sharp and focused.

History

Some time after the shattering of the Ordning in 1485 DR, when the Savage Frontier became a hotbed of squabbling giant activity, Helena witnessed the destructive power of giants firsthand. From that day forward she became a giantslayer, dedicating her life to always being one step ahead of the towering humanoids. She eventually joined up with two other adventurers to form a giantslayer organization — Hook, Line, and Sinker. Within the group she acted as the impromptu leader, keeping everyone together.
