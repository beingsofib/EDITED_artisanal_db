King Dalious Croam, who was also called the Errant King, was the third and final King of the Croam Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate.

Activities

Dalious ruled from the , to the , when he abdicated the throne in favor of the first of many Croamarkhs who would rule Westgate for over a century.

Relationships

Dalious was the third son of King Sorentah Croam.
