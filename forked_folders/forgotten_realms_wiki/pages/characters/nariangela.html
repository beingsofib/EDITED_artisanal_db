Nariangela () was an erinyes who served as an officer aboard the flying fortress of Archduchess Zariel.

Activities

Among Nariangela's duties were to guard the command deck of Zariel's flying fortress. She served alongside five other erinyes, one of Zariels' pit fiend generals, and at least a dozen barbazu guardsmen.
