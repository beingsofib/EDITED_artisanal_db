Ra'id bin Kawthar was a sea elf living in Chaos Bay near Hawa in 1367 DR.

Personality

Ra'id was friendly and highly intelligent.

Activities

On occasion, he served as a navigator for ships entering Chaos Bay, assisting their captains to safely pass through the deadly coral maze lurking beneath the waves. Ra'id occasionally participated in the Corsair Council where his useful insight into defense was welcomed.

Possessions

Ra'id used a magical spear or a normal short sword during combat.

Relationships

Ra'id was the son of the leader of the sea elves inhabiting the coral maze of Chaos Bay.
