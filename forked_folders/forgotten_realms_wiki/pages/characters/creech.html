Creech was an evil familiar that was loyal to Ilkor of Thay who resided in Daggerford circa 1372 DR.

Personality

Creech was a sadistic little creature that was infinitely curious about how well things and creatures burn or melt and he liked the taste of humanoid flesh.

History

In 1372 DR, Creech was accompanying his master Ilkor during his nefarious mission in Daggerford. They both reside within an abandoned flophouse in Daggerford's Money Quarter.
