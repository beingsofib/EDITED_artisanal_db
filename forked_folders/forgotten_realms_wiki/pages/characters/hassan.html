Hassan

Person
nameHassan
raceEfreeti
occupationGuard

Hassan was a powerful efreeti who patrolled the halls of the Royal Harim of Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Possessions

Hassan wielded a massive fiery scimitar.

Activities

He was sworn to slay any male—aside from the eunuch mamluks also on guard—found within the Royal Harim.

Relationships

Hassan began his service to the Grand Caliph when he was presented to Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir as a gift from Adnan al-Raqi.

Over the years, Hassan became rather domesticated by the Grand Caliph's wives. They often encouraged him to take long naps during which male visitors could sneak into the harem.
