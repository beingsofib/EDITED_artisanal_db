Calim (Sword Coast)

Person
nameCalim
homeGnarled Teahouse, Sunlit Wetlands
sexMale

Calim was an individual who encountered the hag known as Auntie Ethel.

At some point, his lover made a request to Ethel that Calim's beauty never wane. In response, the hag trapped Calim within a mirror, where his beauty would be visible for all time.
