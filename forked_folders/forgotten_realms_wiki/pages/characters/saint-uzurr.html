Saint Uzurr was a saint of Ilmater.

The walled monastery called the Cloister of St. Uzurr in Lapaliiya was founded by Ilmatari missionaries from Calimshan during the time of the Shoon Imperium (27–450 DR). By 1370 DR, the city of Uzurr had grown up around it and was led by the Order of St. Uzurr.
