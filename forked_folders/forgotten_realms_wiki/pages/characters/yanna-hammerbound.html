Yanna Hammerbound was a dwarf and a high-ranking member of the Ironhanded in Phlan during the Tyranny of Dragons.

History

Yanna worked as high-ranking member of Phlan's Ironworkers' Guild. One day in 1489 DR, she went to the Madame Freona's Tea Kettle to meet a merchant named Trett in order to buy a powerful magic item. When a lightning spell from inside the item hit Trett, Yanna tried to escape with the magic item but was captured by adventurers hired by Buhrell Caah.
