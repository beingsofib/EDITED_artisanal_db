Parda was a human monk and a tutor who dwelt within the library citadel of Candlekeep in the mid-14 century DR.

History

By , Parda was friends with Gorion's ward, Abdel Adrian. Parda was an older man, often draped in yellow robes, who offered kindly advice and cared about the well being of Abdel. After Gorion's ward was attacked by Shank, an assassin sent by Sarevok Anchev, Parda advised the man to get his bearing together and leave Candlekeep as soon as possible, as per Gorion's wishes.
