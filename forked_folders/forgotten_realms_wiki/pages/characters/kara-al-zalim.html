Sultan Kara al-Zalim, also called the Fierce Sword of the Sea, was the ruler of Jumlat in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Personality

Kara was both cunning and ruthless. These traits made him extremely popular with Jumlat's wealthiest citizens.

History

Kara earned his reputation as a ruthless corsair during countless raids against Gana's fleet.

Relationships

Kara was the nephew of Jumlat's previous sultan and was chosen as the sultan's successor over his own children.
