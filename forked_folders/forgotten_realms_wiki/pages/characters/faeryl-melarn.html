Faeryl Melarn was a senior drow priestess of House Melarn in Menzoberranzan.

Personality

Faeryl was willing to risk accusation of heresy and treason in her effort to stop the Demon Weave.

History

Faeryl in 1479 DR agreed to meet Valan Jaelre of the Jaezred Chaulssin in the Braeryn in order to have help to stop the Demon Weave.

Relationship

Faeryl was a relative of matron mother Zhindia Melarn
