Malin Quickarrow was an elven ranger and adventurer in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Malin had chestnut brown hair and wore leather armor.

History

Shortly after the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, Malin traveled along the High Road with his wolf companion and stayed the night at Beckside Castle. He somehow came to a mostly one-sided argument with the druid Ursus over the merits of wolves versus bears. The wolf and bear stayed out of the debate.
