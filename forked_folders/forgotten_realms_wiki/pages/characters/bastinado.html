Bastinado was a ranger, adventurer, and a member of the Annals of Halgren in the early 14 century DR.

Description

Bastinado was a large muscular human male who had an affinity with animals and wielded large swords in battle. He was a relentless optimist.

History

By the , Bastinado has traveled all across the Realms and wore many hats. He's experienced the lives of pirates, fishers, mercenaries, tinkers, south, and foresters. He learned to handle almost all types of weapons and developed his monstrous strength and endurance.

Eventually, Bastinado found himself in the city of Luskan, where he joined the Annals of Halgren. The adventuring troupe searched for mercenaries, and the ranger happily joined. His personality became the cornerstone of the new adventuring group. In battle, Bastinado learned to take the left side of the team's gruff dwarven leader – Halgren Death's Head, becoming the symbolic sword of the group.
