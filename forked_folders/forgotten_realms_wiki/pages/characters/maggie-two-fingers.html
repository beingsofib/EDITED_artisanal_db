Maggie Two-Fingers was a criminal who earned their wealth by extorting others.

History

At some point, a woman named Miriam came to owe a significant amount of money to Maggie. Miriam's lover Edmund managed to pay off Maggie, forming an infernal pact with a cambion and joining up with a mercenary group.
