The Sanjos Irridan was a vampire necromancer and surgeon who sold undead grafts at the Corpse Market in the Shadowfell city of Evernight as of the late 15 century DR.

Personality

He was evil and insane.

Activities

He sold body parts, including those which were magically animated and could be grafted onto a living person. He would also perform the actual grafting of the body part onto a client for an extra fee. This required a complicated and vile ritual that no sane or reasonable person would generally wish for.

History

As of , Sanjos Irridan was the most famous provider of grafts at the Corpse Market. A large banner over his shop proudly displayed the slogan: "an arm and a leg for less than an arm and a leg."
