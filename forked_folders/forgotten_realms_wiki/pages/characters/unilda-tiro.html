Unilda Tiro was a Black Gauntlet of Bane, who posed as a guard working for Baldur's Mouth in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

Unilda was equipped with a chainmail uniform of Baldur's Mouth's guards and armed with a simple mace and wooden shield.

Abilities

As a cleric, Black Gauntlet Unilda could cast such spells as blight, dominate person, inflict wounds, phantasmal force, and summon a spirit guardians imbued with necrotic magics.

History

In the , Unilda Tiro was tasked with guarding the offices of Baldur's Mouth and its editor-in-chief, Ettvard Needle. She was tasked with guarding the elevator that descended into Baldur's Mouth's off-limits basement. None were allowed to pass without Ettvard's permission.
