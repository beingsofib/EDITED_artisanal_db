Sarjak Belszour was a wainwright inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Sarjak was the guildmaster for the Wagonmakers' & Coach Builders' Guild circa the . He held the title of "Guild Master" within the guild and worked out of the Coach & Wagon Hall–their headquarters on the High Road in the Southern Ward.
