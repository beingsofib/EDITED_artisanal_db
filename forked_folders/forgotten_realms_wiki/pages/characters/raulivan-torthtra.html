Raulivan Torthtra was a young playwright from the city of Zazesspur in Tethyr in the late 14 century DR.

History

Raulivan was a young up-and-coming author known for his love of a good-looking woman and his ability to write realistic dialogues that represented real folk speech in its authenticity. His famous grand play was The Gilded Unicorn that in the late 14 century DR was a staple in the Crown Aflame theater in Athkatla.
