Keegan Velryn was a scrimshander who lived in Targos during the late 15 century DR.

Description

He was described as being rather handsome.

Relationships

Keegan was married to the rugged outdoorsman Garret Velryn.

History

In the , Garret Velryn went missing while leading an expedition across Kelvin's Cairn. Over the next few days, Keegan became increasingly worried, and eventually hired some adventurers to uncover his whereabouts.
