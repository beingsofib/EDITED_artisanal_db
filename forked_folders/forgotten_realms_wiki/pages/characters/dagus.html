King Dagus was ruler of the Northlander subkingdom of Olafstaad within Gnarhelm in the Moonshae Isles during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a large and grizzled warrior in his old age, with ice-blue eyes and a snow-white beard. His face was covered in scars and he stood with a crook in his posture. He was missing his left hand, with the arm ending at the elbow.

Personality

He was vengeful and frightening.

Relationships

He was a member of the Olafsson family as elder cousin to King Svenyird Olafsson of Gnarhelm and uncle to Brandon Olafsson.

History

He became ruler of Olaftaad sometime after King Rolf Olafsson left Olaftaad to become king of all Gnarhelm in the .

Dagus lost his arm in the . He led a force of 60 warriors to pillage in the Nelanther Isles, and after initial success, they were confronted by a force of monstrous reavers near Nindrol that forced them to make a tactical retreat. With their superior seafaring skills, the Northlanders navigated their longships through Oboron's Maw, and ultimately prevailed. However, Dagus was grievously wounded and lost many of his men.

In the , an army of Ffolk cavalry bearing the standard of House Kendrick attacked the settlements along the coast near Olafstaad, butchering the Northlanders in their way. Dagus met them in battle, slaying at least one on his own. However, his son was slain in the fighting. With the aid of Princess Alicia Kendrick, his suspicions were confirmed that these attacks were a ruse meant to spark war between Gnarhelm and Callidyrr.
