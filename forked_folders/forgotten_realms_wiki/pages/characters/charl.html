Charl was a halfling who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 century DR.

Activities

Charl was a member of the Dead Rats, although he wasn't committed to their cause. He even wasn't a wererat.

History

Charl was the one who helped to create the alliance between the Harpers and the Sons of Alagondar. He later did the same with for the alliance between the Nashers and the Dead Rats of Neverwinter.

In 1479 DR, Charl instigated a crowd of followers of the "Lost Heir" of Alagondar to attack some adventurers hired by Dagult Neverember to investigate the "Lost Heir". Charl and his followers were defeated, however, and Charl's fate was for the adventurers to decide.
