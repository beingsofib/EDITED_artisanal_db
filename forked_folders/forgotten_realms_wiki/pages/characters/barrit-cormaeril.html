Lord Barrit Cormaeril was a member of the noble Cormaeril family of Cormyr in the 14 century DR.

In the , Lord Barrit attended King Azoun Obarskyr IV's birthday feast, hosted by Lord Partic Thistle at Thistleflame Keep. He was accompanied there by Lady Alise Cormaeril.
