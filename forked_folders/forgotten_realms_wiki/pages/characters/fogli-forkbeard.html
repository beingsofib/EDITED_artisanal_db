Fogli Forkbeard was a gnome in Raurin in the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

Fogli Forkbeard had a room at Happy Hogan's Desert Igloo in the Sandvoyagers Compound at the time of Khalitharius's release in . He did not often actually sleep in his room. Under the bed, there was a chest marked with his name; beneath some clothes, it had 12 pounds of silver and 8 pounds of gold.
