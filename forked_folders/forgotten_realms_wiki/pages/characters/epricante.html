Epricante was a drow warrior of House Xorlarrin living in Q'Xorlarrin in 1484 DR.

History

During the War of the Silver Marches in 1485 DR, he along his old associate Maffizo Vailentarne was part of a Xorlarrin reinforcement army sent to help the orc warlord Hartusk after an orc defeat near Mithril Hall. However, thanks to tge plotting of Gromph Baenre and Kimmuriel Oblodra, Hartusk believed that Epricante and his army were traitors and attacked and destroyed them.
