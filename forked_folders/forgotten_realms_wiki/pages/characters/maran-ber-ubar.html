Princess Maran ber Ubar was the daughter of the Caliph of the Free City of Umara in north Zakhara before he was deposed by barbarians.

Activities

After her father's fall, Maran married the man who claimed his position–Khan Ubar khel Muhif. Maran used her position to peacefully integrate the immigrating Astoks into the city and maintain the rights and status of Umara's nobility.
