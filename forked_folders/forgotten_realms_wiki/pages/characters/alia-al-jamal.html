Alia al-Jamal umm Nabil was a skilled sand mage and chief vizier of the House of Asad tribe in the High Desert of west Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Description

Alia was a beautiful middle-aged woman.

Personality

Though Alia was soft-spoken, her voice was respected as much as that of her husband and sheikh of the tribe.

Activities

Alia often negotiated deals with other desert tribes and made rulings in her husband's absence.

Relationships

Alia was the sole wife of the tribe's leader, Najib bin Kamal al-Asad. Their eldest son was Nabil bin Najib.
