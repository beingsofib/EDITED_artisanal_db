Lesten was a Netherese archwizard.

History

In , Lesten engaged in a spell duel with another archwizard, Buoyance. Over a century prior, Buoyance had already proven his worth in another spell battle, where he slew Kartaklys. Lesten's battle with Buoyance, however, was one of epic proportions. Elemental magic was fired this way and that, until there was only one victor: Buoyance. Lesten had crumbled to ash. Following his demise, the other archwizards of Netheril talked about the grand spell duel until their empire fell.
