Klarynda Ballasdreia, nicknamed The Lady of Masks, was a successful perfume merchant who resided in Athkatla's Temple District in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Klarynda Ballasdreia was a well-known socialite, breaker of hearts, and a schemer of Athkatla's high society.

History

By the late 14 century DR, Klarynda Ballasdreia ran a successful business dealing in perfumes and beauty masks as well as being a savvy investor. Her large mansion was a shrine dedicated to the goddess of fortune, Tymora, before being sacked by the city in the .
