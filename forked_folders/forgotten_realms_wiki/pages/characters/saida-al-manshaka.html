Saida al Manshaka was a woman of Manshaka in Calimshan in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Saida owned the slave Astil, before he killed her in the 1340s DR and escaped. Nevertheless, he chose to keep his slave name: Astil adh Saida al Manshaka.
