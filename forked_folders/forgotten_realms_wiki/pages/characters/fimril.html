Fimril of Westgate was a mageling of the Zhentarim in the mid-14 century DR.

Description

He had dark eyes and wore a black robe.

Personality

To Sarhthor, like most magelings of the Zhentarim, Fimril was dark-hearted and ambitious. Though he bore a spark of self-doubt, he was brave, arrogant, and indeed ambitious, believing himself capable of rising to the top of the Zhentarim, and considering that people who talked back to him in a way he disapproved of could be done without.

Abilities

Fimril was able to use a slaying spell that consumed a crushed black pearl as its material component, almost certainly death spell. His version of the spell, however, was strong enough to kill him and his comrades too. He had also devised a unique spell that took him an entire year to construct, which created a moving barrier of all-devouring darkness, a spell powerful enough to counter spellfire.

Another strange power of Fimril was a very draining spell that called up images from the ashes of a fire, allowing a mage to see what had transcurred while it was burning.

Relationships

He'd been either trained or tested by Sarhthor. Lyrkon, Glondar, and Duthspurn were both part of the squad to which he was assigned.

History

Prior to , Fimril was Fimril of Westgate, strongly implying he'd spent a significant amount of time in that city. He'd spent at least one year, per his own recollection, developing a spell that created an all-devouring shield of darkness, capable of stopping most spells and disintegrating most material that it went through.

He was part of a meeting in late , in which Sarhthor offered several magelings of the Zhentarim the assignment to hunt down Shandril Shessair for her spellfire.

Though he captured Shandril Shessair, he used a killing spell on Mirt when the latter turned up to rescue her; the spell was reflected, slaying him.
