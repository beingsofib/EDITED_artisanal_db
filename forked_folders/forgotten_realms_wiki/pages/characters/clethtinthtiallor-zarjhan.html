Clethtinthtiallor Zarjhan was a dragonborn of Tymanther and the patriarch of Clan Clethtinthtiallor in 1487 DR.

Description

Zarjhan had rust scales.

History

In 1487 DR, Zarjhan was one of the clan elders who supported the decision to allow Namshita's refugees to live in Tymanther, in gratitude for Namshita's role in the liberation of members of Clan Clethtinthtiallor that had being enslaved by Gilgeam after the return of Unther to Toril.
