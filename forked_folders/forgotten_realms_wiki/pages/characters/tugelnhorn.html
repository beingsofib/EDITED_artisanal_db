Tugelnhorn a laborer who worked the docks of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Tugelnhorn was a gold dwarven woman with a bronze skin tone, brown eyes, and brown hair. She kept her face clean-shaven.

History

In the , Tugelnhorn, along with her friend Ormont, were harborhands – laborers who were faced with much work, little pay, and poor work conditions. She was dissatisfied with the job, but, unlike Ormont and other laborers, thought striking in the face of the encroaching army of the Absolute was a bad idea.
