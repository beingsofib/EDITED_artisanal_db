Froedegra was the daughter of a local blacksmith of Halfknot in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

She did not like scars in weird places.

Relationships

She'd once seen a scar somewhere on Wyrmbait Nyx's body which she didn't want to ever see again, strongly implying it was somewhere delicate.

History

In late winter or early spring of the , Froedegra was one of the people in the Dragon Defeated inn during the night when it was destroyed by the sapphire dragon Malaeragoth. Previous to the incident, she'd objected to Wyrmbait Nyx showing a certain scar.
