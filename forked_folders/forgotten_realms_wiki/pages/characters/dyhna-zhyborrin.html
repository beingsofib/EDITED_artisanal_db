High Priestess Dyhna Zhyborrin was a follower of Jergal and the leader of the Crypt of Imminent Death in Bezantur in Thay during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Dyhna was the overseer of the temple's staff. She also led worship services to Jergal and maintained a strong relationship with the clergy and followers of Kelemvor in Bezantur.
