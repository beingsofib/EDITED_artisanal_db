Candalo Windroot was a elven druid in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Candalo had short black hair, and wore a green and brown leaf-themed robe while carrying a long wooden staff.

History

In the , Candalo joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their base camp at the ruins of Myth Drannor. He served as a trainer for prospective druids at the camp.
