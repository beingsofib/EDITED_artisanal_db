Ali Haqim was a masterful Zakharan elephant-rider who lived in Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Activites

Ali began his career as a regular mamluk, but his master quickly discovered his talent for training elephants. Ali was soon given command of a formation of a hundred elephants. Ali went on to train elephants for many princes of Zakhara.

Possessions

Ali wielded a scimitar and wore padded armor.
