Timoshenko was a human fighter and the Grandfather of Assassins, the leader of the Citadel of Assassins, in Damara in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He was previously a commander of its army.

History

In the , Timoshenko was the commander of the bandit army when they attacked Bloodstone Village.

He rose to power after Kane defeated the last Grandfather. Backed by the archmage Knellict, Timoshenko surpassed Banak in power, a once mighty priest of Orcus, who was the other candidate for leader of the Citadel.

Personality

Although Timoshenko was a mighty warrior he was thoughtful, conservative, and took advice from Knellict often.

Activities

Timoshenko played the political face of the organization and let his lieutenants carry out the dirty work.
