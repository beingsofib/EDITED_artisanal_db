Lord Kythyss was the ruler of the coastal town of Kythyss in Callidyrr in the .

Activities

Lord Kythyss maintained an ongoing quarrel with his neighbor to the north, Lord Llewellyn of rival town Llewellyn. Both lords argued over who had rights to fish the fertile waters of Llewellyn Harbor. The matter was even brought before the High King.
