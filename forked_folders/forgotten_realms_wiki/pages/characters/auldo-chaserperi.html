Auldo Chaserperi was a member of the Chaserperi clan of mages of the late mid-14 century DR.

Rumors & Legends

He was said to have found a lich in the cellar of a home he bought in Waterdeep around the , and to have come to an arrangement with it, which included him bringing the lich dead bodies. In exchange, the undead helped protect his properties around the city.
