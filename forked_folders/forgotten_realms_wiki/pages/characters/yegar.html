Lord Yegar was the ruler of Citadel Rashemar during the mid 14 century DR.

Personality

Yegar was arrogant and grossly incompetent. Despite this, he still wielded a great deal of political power within Rashemen.

Activities

Lord Yegar dispatched patrols to monitor the Sunrise Mountains. He spent most of his time pressing Rashemen's rights to the copper mines located in Murghôm. He claimed this because of ancient borders and not present day alignment.
