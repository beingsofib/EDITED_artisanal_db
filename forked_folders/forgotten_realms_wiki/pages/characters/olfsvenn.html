Olfsvenn was a Northlander prince of Norland in the Moonshae Isles in the .

Relationships

Olfsvenn's grandfather was Jarl Rault the Wise. He was the son of Olfgaut, the Jarl's son.

History

Given the advanced age of his father in , Olfsvenn was in a good position to be the next Jarl of Norland. However, the Storm Maiden's rise to power posed a substantial threat to his ascension.

Olfsvenn was killed while battling the Storm Maiden sometime before the .
