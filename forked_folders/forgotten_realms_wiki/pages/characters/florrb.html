Florbb was the mud mephit who "ruled over" the Mud Pits of Florrb on the Snout of Omgar during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Due to the circumstances in which he found himself, Florbb was extremely anxious.

Activities

Florbb spent much of his time attracting and encouraging tortles to bathe in muddy pits, hoping they would trigger some magical effect.

History

Centuries before the Second Sundering, Florbb and two other mud mephits were transported from their home in the Elemental Plane of Earth to the Mud Pits on Toril. While Florbb's companions were able to return home by playing in the mud of their new surroundings, Florbb was not so lucky. He spent decades upon decades trying to return to his friends.
