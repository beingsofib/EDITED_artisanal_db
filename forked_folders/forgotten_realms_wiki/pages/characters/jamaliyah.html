Jamaliyah was a djinni bound to a lamp of the genies in Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Personality

Jamaliyah was exceptionally wily and intelligent with an adventurous side.

Relationships

She was owned by various merchants, healers and viziers over the years. In 1367 DR, she was owned by a courtier in Huzuz, the City of Delights.
