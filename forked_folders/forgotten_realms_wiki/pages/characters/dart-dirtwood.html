Dart Dirtwood was a ranger and thug from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Dart was a gold dwarvish man with a light skin tone and light brown eyes. He kept his face and scalp clean-shaven. As a ranger, he wore simple leather armor and wielded a heavy crossbow.

History

In the , Dart Dirtwood was a member of a gang assembled by Churg Elvek, secretly a cultist of the Absolute, who sought to strengthen its grasp of Baldur's Gate. When the famed writer Volothamp Geddarm returned to the Gate after misadventures in the Emerald Grove and coming face-to-face with the Absolute's army, Volo became a target for Churg and his gang. The master traveler was captured by Dart and the rest of the thugs and tied to a stack of smokepowder barrels, ready to be "brought to justice" for being an alleged agent of the Absolute at the hands of the gang and street gawkers. When a band known as the Absolute Adversaries interrupted the street execution, Dart Dirtwood, Churg Elvek, and the rest of the gang attacked the adventurers but were defeated.
