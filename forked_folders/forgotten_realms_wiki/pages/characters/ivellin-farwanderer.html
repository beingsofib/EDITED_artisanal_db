Ivellin Farwanderer was an elf ranger and arcane archer of Mielikki operating in Cormanthor in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

Ivellin was the second-in-command of the warrior priestess Vishara in her campaign against Nashyim of the Desolated Wood, Ivellin was in charge of scouting missions, which kept track of the movements and plans of Nashyim and his blightspawned creatures.
