Jungle Princess was a deinonychus who competed in the dinosaur races of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 century DR.

History

As of the start of the death curse, bookies were giving Jungle Princess only 1:5 odds of winning at the races, perhaps because of her easily frightened jockey.
