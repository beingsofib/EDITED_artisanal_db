Geoffrey was a brewer in Caer Corwell during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Geoffrey was a huge fat man.

Personality

Being an accomplished aleman, Geoffrey sold light and dark , but his Callidyrr mead was prized by his patrons.

History

In 1345 DR, the guard Erian entered his tent during the Festival of the Spring Equinox and met Kazgoroth disguised as the peasant, "Meridith".
