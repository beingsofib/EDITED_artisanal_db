Nymessa was a tiefling archer who lived in Elturel and later on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Nymessa had red skin and dark hair.

Possessions

She wore a suit of elaborate leather armor and armed herself with a bow and arrows.

History

In the , Nymessa and all the other tieflings within were exiled from Elturel following the city's brief descent into the Nine Hells. The refugees came under the leadership of the exiled Hellrider known as Zevlor. The group traveled west towards the sprawling metropolis of Baldur's Gate, and eventually took refuge at the Emerald Grove after goblin bands harried their march along the road.

After settling into the Emerald Grove the githyanki known as Lae'zel stumbled into a trap just outside the sanctuary's walls, and was soon discovered by Nymessa and her companion Damays. Nymessa exclaimed that the "thing" was dangerous, and that they should just let goblins come and kill her. Damays believed that it might be possible for Lae'zel to escape.
