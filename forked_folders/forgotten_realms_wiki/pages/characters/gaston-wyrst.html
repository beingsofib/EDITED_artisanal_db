Gaston Wyrst was a Shadovar wizard of Returned Netheril who was deployed to Beckside Castle in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Gaston had pale skin, short brown hair, and wore a dark brown and gray robe with the symbol of Shar.

Personality

Though more restrained and professional than his companion Keldan, Gaston was not above arrogantly monologuing at his enemies.

Abilities

Gaston was a shadow wizard and could summon umbral rats en-mass. He could also cast chain lightning, crushing despair, delayed blast fireball, meteor swarm, polar ray, and thunderstroke.

History

When the Shadovar pursued their stolen Nether Scroll along the High Road to Beckside Castle, Gaston and Keldan Shadeskein led the recovery efforts there. They infiltrated past the Weathered Dawn with an escort of mercenaries into the other parts of the ruins, with Gaston going directly for the Scroll while Keldan erected a defensive shield. Finding the Scroll piece in the ruined throne room, Gaston was heavily delayed by a magical barrier that surrounded it, which he struggled to break through. By the time he managed to retrieve the piece, the Harpers had arrived and killed Keldan, then faced him in battle. Though he summoned many umbral rats to his aid, Gaston fell and the Scroll went to the Harpers.
