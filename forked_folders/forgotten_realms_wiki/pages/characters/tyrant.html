The Tyrant was a massive and powerful tyrannosaurus that dwelt within the Jungles of Chult, later, becoming an undead monstrosity that stalked the ruins of Omu in the late 15 century DR.

Description

In its undeath, the Tyrant was a giant thundering creature of insatiable hunger. It had dead white skin that was peeling off, exposing rotting meat underneath.

History

In the late 15 century DR, as the death curse ravaged Faerûn, the famed author Volothamp Geddarm found himself in the Hunter's Square in Port Nyanzaru, a city in Chult. As he was working on Volo's Guide to Monsters, Volo enlisted the aid of adventurers from the north to hunt down and slay a collection of powerful beasts of legend and report to him with their experiences and loot. The tyrannosaurus zombie that was once a living titan of the jungle, called the Tyrant, was among the marks. When the creature was presumably slain, the adventurers responsible for the deep were rewarded with the enchanted boots of the willed.
