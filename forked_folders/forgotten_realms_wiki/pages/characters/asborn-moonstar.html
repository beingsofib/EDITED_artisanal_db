Asborn Moonstar was a young and desperate nobleman from Waterdeep, member of the Moonstar family in the mid–14 century DR.

History

In early 1370 DR, Asborn Moonstar and Elene Ruldegost fell mindlessly in love with each other, despite their families' old animosity. When the youths decided to tie the knot, both families were against even the mere idea of that union, but the Ruldegosts agreed to permit the marriage if Asborn were to retrieve a crystal rose from Undermountain. These roses grew in one place, the Garden of the Drow, thought long abandoned. Asborn ran straight to the Yawning Portal and plunged into the dungeon.

The young Moonstar had been missing for over a week when Khelben Arunsun asked his trusted agent to find the youth in the abandoned drow monastic retreat. When Khelbenite made his way to the cave of the Garden. It became evident that the dark residents of the retreat were back, and it did not bode well for Asborn. Subsequently, the adventurer came across a sealed room with serene-looking springs, plants, mushrooms, and a single crystal rose growing in the center. Next to the delicate flower, the hero found the tattered remains of Asborn Moonstar. He was able to sneak past the drow into the rose chamber but was discovered and sealed inside, and the young man starved to death. There was nothing else to be done; Khelbenite collected the crystal flower and delivered it to Elene Ruldegost so she would have something by which to remember her departed lover.
