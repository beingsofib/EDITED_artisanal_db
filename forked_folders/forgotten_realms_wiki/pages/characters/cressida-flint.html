Cressida Flint was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Cressida was a human woman with a tan skin tone, straight and long dark brown hair, and dark brown eyes. She wore a colorful striped orange and red tunic.

History

In the , Cressida Flint could often be found pacing down the streets of the Lower City, consumed by her thoughts. She reacted hostilely to anyone who dared to interfere with her busy day. She was one of the customers interested in the Honest Vendor's not-so-magical protective trinkets. During that time, she was an avid reader of Baldur's Mouth and eagerly devoured fluff pieces and interviews published in it to uplift Lord Enver Gortash.
