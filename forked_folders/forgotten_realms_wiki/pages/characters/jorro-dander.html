Jorro Dander was a halfling jewelry trader of Eveningstar in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Jorro had a mop of black hair and wore a long gray tunic.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Jorro operated a trinket and jewelry stall in the Market Square of the village, trying to make the best of the situation.
