Dawn MacDare was a bloodforge mage in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

Dawn desired to take advantage of the Legendary Campaign leader's mission to unify and pacify the Utter East through conquest. Thus, Dawn made camp in the Web Mountains, from which they advanced on the Legendary Campaign forces. However, warned of the attack by the army's southern guard, the leader struck at Dawn's forces in a bloodforge battle.
