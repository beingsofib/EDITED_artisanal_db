Baff Elkhorn was an unsavory barbarian gnome merc from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Baff was a light-skinned gnomish woman with brown eyes. She wore barbarian hides and hid her face behind a mask and a hood. The gnome used a massive axe in battle.

History

In the , the opportunistic and immoral mercenary Aradin Beno set out to find the Nightsong for the mage Lorroakan. If the Nightsong, in reality a Selûnite aasimar, was to reach the city of Baldur's Gate, then Beno had a band of mercenaries with questionable morals ready to attack those who sheltered her. Barbarian Baff Elkhorn was among those mercenaries, ready to break into the Elfsong Tavern to kill and kidnap.
