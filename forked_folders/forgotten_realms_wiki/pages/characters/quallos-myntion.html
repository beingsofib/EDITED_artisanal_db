Quallos Myntion was a brewer inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Quallos was an up and coming member of the Vintners', Distillers', & Brewers' Guild. He shared many of his personal recips with the guild which aided his rise. Quallos wished to introduce the citizens of Waterdeep to lagers using some of his knowledge obtained from the Golden Sands Brews.

Relationships

He was married to Miri Myntion
