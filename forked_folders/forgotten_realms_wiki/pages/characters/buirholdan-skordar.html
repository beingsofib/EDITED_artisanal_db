Buirholdan Skordar was a retired warrior and mason inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Buirholdan was the guildmaster for the Guild of Stonecutters, Masons, Potters, & Tile-makers circa 1357 DR. He held the title of "Master Stonewrker" within the guild and worked out of the Builders' Hall–their headquarters on Coach Street in the Southern Ward.
