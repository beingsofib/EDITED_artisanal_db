Shoal was a nereid who dwelled on the Shipwreck's Coast for a time during the mid–14 century DR.

Abilities

Shoal was capable of killing someone with a mere kiss.

History

In the , Shoal was enslaved by the ogre mage Droth. He had her draw in and attract passers so that he could rob and slay them.
