Metedes was a priest of Helm and member of Sir Justin Melenikus's watchers at Iniarv's Tower in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Metedes and his fellow watchers were from the city of Waterdeep. Their services and ventures into the Mere of Dead Men were encouraged via writs that could be changed for coin back in the City of Splendors.
