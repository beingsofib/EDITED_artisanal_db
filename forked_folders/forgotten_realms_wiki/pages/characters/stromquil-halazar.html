Stromquil Halazar was a jewler and shopkeeper inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Stromquil was the guildmaster for the Jewelers' Guild circa 1357 DR. He held the title of "Master Jeweler" within the guild and worked out of the House of Gems–their headquarters on Gem Street in the Castle Ward.
