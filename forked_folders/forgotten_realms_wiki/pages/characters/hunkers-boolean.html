Hunkers Boolean was a human commoner, displaced by The Absolute, and as a refugee, sought shelter in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Hunkers was a brown-skinned human male with short dark brown curly hair. He wore simple commoner's garb, a long traveling jacket and leather trousers.

History

In the , an army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldur's Gate, displacing countless refugees in the process along the way. Hunkers Boolean and his beloved dragonborn Gaheid Slickjaw found their way to the Rivington district outside the city walls, but, like all other refugees, they were refused entrance. The two men had to live in the tent city, only able to gain entrance with proper paperwork. Gaheid thought, somewhat sheepishly, that picking fights with the Flaming Fist mercenaries was a bad idea, to what his partner called him the "most cowardly dragonborn in the Realms." Despite calling Gaheid a blockhead, Hunkers claimed to feel love for the man.
