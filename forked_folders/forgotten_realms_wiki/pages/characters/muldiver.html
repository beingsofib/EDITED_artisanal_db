Muldiver

Person
nameMuldiver
homeTantras, the Vast
occupationShipwright

Muldiver was a master shipwright of the city of Tantras in the Vast in the mid–14 century DR. Muldiver worked out of a shipyard in Tantras's harbor.

During the Time of Troubles of 1358 DR, Muldiver was kept very busy with the city's preparations for war against Zhentil Keep.
