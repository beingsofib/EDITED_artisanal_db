Klutarch was a fighter in Luskan and a member of Ship Kurth in the mid–15 century DR.

History

Klutarch once tried to become the High Captain Kurth but lost the challenge. However he remained as advisor to the new captain.

In 1463 DR, High Captain Kurth spoke with Klutarch about the idea of recruiting Drizzt Do'Urden and Dahlia Sin'felle.
