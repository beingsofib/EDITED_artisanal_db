That Which Lurks was a death knight sentry that guarded the entrance to the Murder Tribunal deep in the Undercellar of Baldur's Gate's Lower City in the late 15 century DR.

Description

That Which Lurks wore a set of intricately decorated plate mail, gauntlets, enchanted pair of blackguard's greaves, and a horsetail helmet. The creature's armor was lines with gold. It carried a longsword.

Abilities

That Which Lurks shared abilities and vulnerabilities of other undead creatures. It could land smiting cleaves that could stagger the targets.

History

In the , That Which Lurks was placed to stand guard of the entrance to the Murder Tribunal alongside That Which Guards and That Which Watches. The creature spoke in rhymes with an archaic turn of phrase.
