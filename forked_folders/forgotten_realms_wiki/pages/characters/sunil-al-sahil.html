Sunil al-Sahil was a tasked administrator genie who inhabited Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Personality

Sunil was prized by the Grand Caliph for her devotion to duty.

Activities

Sunil represented the throne of Huzuz for the three major bureaucratic centers of power within in city. Bureaucrats from the Northwest District, Affluent District, and Central District all answered to her.

Relationships

Sunil was the sister of Sandara al-Sahil.
