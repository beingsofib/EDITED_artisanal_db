Jurgen was a clerk in Stag Falls during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a young man.

Activities

Jurgen was a clerk at the town's general store.

Relationships

He had a wife, Marimir, who gave birth during a stressful situation.

History

During the evacuation of Stag Falls after it was taken over by the vampire wizard Romanisk Vlaensk, the population, including Jurgen and his pregnant wife, took refuge in nearby caves. She went into labor while the local priestess of Chauntea and midwife, Sidone, was not available; he had to get help from adventurers. Their competence on delivery was into question, and may have led to a childbirth just short of disastrous, though he would name his child after the one who assisted with the delivery.
