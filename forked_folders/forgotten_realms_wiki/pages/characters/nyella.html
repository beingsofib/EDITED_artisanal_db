Nyella was the spirit of a deceased canine inhabiting the village of Tinnir in Rashemen in the .

Activities

Nyella protected the local inn called the Lakeside Inn. She was fiercely territorial and often barked at strangers. Nyella was quite fond of the inn's owner, Vrulla.

History

Nyella belonged to the man who constructed the Lakeside Inn during the mid 13 century DR.
