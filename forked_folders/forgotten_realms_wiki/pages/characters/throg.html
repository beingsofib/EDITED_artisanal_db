Throg the Ravenous was an orc warrior of the Many-Arrows kingdom and one of the lieutenants of Vansi Bloodscar in the late 15 century DR.

Abilities

The mighty orc fought in a manner that was quite similar to the ogres that fought in the Many-Arrows forces.

Possessions

Befitting his combat style, Throg favored a magical club of the variety often wielded by ogres. In battle, he wore a suit of leather armor that was unique to the orcs of the Many-Arrows kingdom.

History

During the Many-Arrows orcs' occupation of the River District of Neverwinter, Throg could be found in the Cloak Tower, along with Vansi Bloodscar and her other lieutenants. When adventurers in service to Captain Janros of the Neverwinter Guard finally penetrated the tower in the , they confronted and slew Throg the Ravenous in the Botanical Garden.
