Ragi al-Makruh was an influential merchant inhabiting Jumlat in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Personality

Ragi was exactly what he appeared to be–a hulking angry brute. It was whispered that he had more money than brains and more brains than charm.

Activities

Ragi controlled most of the fleet associated with pearl diving. Many were surprised that he didn't immediately run his family's business into the ground upon gaining control.

Relationships

He spent much of his time paying homage to the Sultan and his Chief Vizier, collecting favors to be used when the moment was right.
