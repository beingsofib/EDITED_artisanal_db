Hassana Alim al-Gaib was the Emira of Dihliz in 1367 DR.

Activities

Hassana was granted the position of Emira based upon the recommendation of the padishah of Afyal. She was appointed to her position by the Grand Caliph himself for a provisional period of five years.

Always planning for retirement, Hassana spent much of her time hoarding her riches. Her interpretation of the Law of the Loregiver was loose and often favored adventurers which in turn favored her.

History

Before becoming the ruler of Dihliz, Hassana was a simple merchant from Afyal.

Relationships

Hassana had four husbands.
