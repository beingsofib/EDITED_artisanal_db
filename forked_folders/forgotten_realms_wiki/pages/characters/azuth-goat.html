Azuth was the beloved pet goat of an eccentric nobleman named Pelp Cadari who lived some years before 1281 DR.

History

Sometime before the , Azuth the goat belonged to Pelp Cadari, known for his eccentricities and wild mood swings. After the nobleman's death, he left Azuth his favorite dining "utensil" – a custom-ordered enchanted halberd called the Long Cleaver. The weapon was commissioned for a small sum of 40,000 gold pieces and was exclusively used by Pelp Cadari to cut poultry at dinner. Because Azuth was a goat, the halberd reminded unused until Azuth's untimely demise.
