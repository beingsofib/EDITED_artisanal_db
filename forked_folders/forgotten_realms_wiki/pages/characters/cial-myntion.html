Cial Myntion was a barmaid inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

She was an outspoken girl who was always on the lookout for fun.

Relationships

Cial's parents were Quallos Myntion and Miri. She had one sister named Branta. Cial was close friends with many guards of the City Watch in her neighborhood.
