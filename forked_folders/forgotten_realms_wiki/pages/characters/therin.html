Therin was the strongman of Pinch's thieves guild.

Personality

Therin was ambitious and had tried unsuccessfully to become the boss of the gang instead of Pinch. Pinch would easily sacrifice him if the situation required.

History

Therin was a Gur in Elturel. He was working as a "stooge for a fortune-teller" when Pinch recruited him. He suffered many scars from a hangman before the gang saved him. Therin was one of the thieves that followed his boss back to Ankhapur to regain his body.

By 1370 DR, Therin had established his own thieves' guild, the Bloody Scarves, within the city.
