Rebecca was the cook and a server at the Mithrest Inn in the City of Coin, Athkatla, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Rebecca was a human woman with sun-kissed bronze skin and blonde hair. She was a diligent worker and polite with the inn's clientele.

History

While Pugney manned his exclusive inn's bar and front desk, Rebecca lorded over its kitchens. Their inn was located in the grand market in Athkatla's Center District – Waukeen's Promenade in the .
