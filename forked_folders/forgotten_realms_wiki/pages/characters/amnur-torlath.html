Prince Amnur Torlath was a legendary general in early Amn during the 5 and 6 centuries DR.

History

Amnur was the younger brother of King Imnel Torlath I, the second king of Amn. During the tumultuous collapse of the Shoon Imperium, Amnur became a general and was credited with many great feats of leadership. Such was his fame, the Amnurflow river and Citadel Amnur were named after him.
