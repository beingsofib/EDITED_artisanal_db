Pruj was a goblin chieftain that led a group of his kin as part of the Hordes of Dragonspear, during the Second Dragonspear War of the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Chief Pruj was well known and respected among his kind. It was speculated that he had delusions of grandeur as he was quite courageous, a trait that was uncommon among the goblinoid races.

History

Pruj led a group of 500 goblins serving in the Marauders contingent of the Dragonspear hordes.
