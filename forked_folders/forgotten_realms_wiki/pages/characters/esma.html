Esma was an elderly woman from Phlan that spent her time gossiping and concocting stories about others circa the 1480s DR.

Relationships

Both Esma and her friend Eve were widows. They met weekly at Madame Freona's Tea Kettle, ceaselessly talking on and on about the possible lives led by passersby.

Possessions

Either Esma or her friend Eve were in possession of a magical, yet still rather mundane, amulet. The amulet's only dweomer kept it clean and free from tarnish.
