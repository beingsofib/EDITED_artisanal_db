Dalabrac Bramblefoot was a halfling member of the Fire Knives in the late 15 century DR.

Relations

Dalabrac was an important leader of the Fire Knives in Westgate and he had many dealings with the pirate Anton Marivaldi.

History

In the , Anton recruited Dalabrac and his men to break into a temple of Amaunator in order to free the Chosen of Lathander, Stedd Whitehorn.
