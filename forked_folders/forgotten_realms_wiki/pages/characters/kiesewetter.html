Kiesewetter was a Netherese cleric who worked as a confectioner at Laclos's Cremes and Candies in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in .

Activities

Kiesewetter was an extremely capable creator of desserts and treats, and alongside Sneyders made much of what was sold at Laclos's chocolate factory. She was extremely well paid by Laclos to ensure her continued loyalty.
