Fear-Lord Vingril, who was called the Obstinate, was a lich inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the late–9 century DR.

Activities

Vingril was a lich of the Night Plague who terrorized Zhentil Keep. He was destroyed by an adventuring company called the Golden Stag when he tried to kill the crew of a cog by shrinking them and their ship.
