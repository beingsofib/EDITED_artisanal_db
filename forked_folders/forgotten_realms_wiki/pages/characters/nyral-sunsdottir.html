Nyral Sunsdottir was a half-elven fighter and wizard in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

She was a half-elf with the bronze complexion of the sun elfs and white curly hair.

Abilities

Nyral's fighting style revolved around using her sword of dancing named Fletis, to hold her enemies at bay, while she threw spells at them.

Possessions

Besides her sword of dancing, she also had an enchanted +1 dagger, a cloak of protection, and a brooch of shielding.

Relationships

She was raised by her mother's relatives in Silverymoon after her mother's death and her father's subsequent abandonment of his infant daughter.
