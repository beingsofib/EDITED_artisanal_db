Lord Malgath was the senior lord of house Malgath of Cormanthyr in the 3 century DR.

History

In , Lord Malgath, alongside Lord Urddusk and Lord Haladavar solicited the Coronal Eltargrim at his court to reconsider the Opening. They made their argument at swordpoint, which Lord Eltargrim later classed as an "error in judgement". He gained their patience by unveiling the project to raise a mythal.
