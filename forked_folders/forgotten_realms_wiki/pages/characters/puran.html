Divine Prince Puran was a prince and general of the Kuong Kingdom in Malatra in the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

Puran was the brother of Priest-King Vishnan VII.

History

In the 1350s DR, Vishnan appointed his brother Puran as general of the "army of conquest" charged with invading the northern nation of Laothan.
