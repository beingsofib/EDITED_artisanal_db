Sabriz Rinzyn Kho was a drow priest of the Spinner of Shadows in the world of Eberron.

Description

Sabriz had gray skin and white hair tied back. She wore an elaborate mask and a cultist outfit that exposed most of her body, and wielded a spiked mace.

Abilities

Sabriz could call on a number of powerful spells, including mass inflict critical wounds, fire storm, cometfall, and unholy blight.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, Sabriz served as a leader in the Lords of Dust cult dedicated to the demonic being known as the Spinner of Shadows. As part of a great plot to free their patron, fellow member Karas fueled a ritual with his life and blasted open a passage into Khyber and the road to Tarath Marad, revealing the way to the Spinner's Prison. Sabriz joined the rakshasas, cultists, and monsters there as they attempted to gain access to the prison, but was slain by forces from the Church of the Silver Flame that arrived to stop the escape attempt.
