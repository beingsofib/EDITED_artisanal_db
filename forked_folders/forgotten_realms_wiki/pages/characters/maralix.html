Maralix was a human fighter working with Exeter during the latter's vengeance upon Wemick and Nellie Thursday Home for Experienced Adventurers.

Personality

A man out to cause trouble, he would fight those weaker than himself, but flee the moment he felt he was in true danger.

Possessions

He wore a set of plate mail and fought with a net and enchanted trident +2.

History

He would fight Matilda the White Lady in a duel for the fate of Nellie Thursday Home for Experienced Adventurers.
