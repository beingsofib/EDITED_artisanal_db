Inder Dunseltree was a merchant of Scornubel during the 14 century DR. He was impersonated by drow during the mid-to-late part of that century.

Description

The drow impersonating him took on his appearance, which included a florid face.

Possessions

He owned a lavish home.

Activities

Inder was a merchant, the owner of Softer Tapestries.

Relationships

He was married to Namra Dunseltree, and employed a number of servants, including a girl named Isryl. His wife was replaced in turn by the drow Anlaervrith Mrantarr, whom his own replacement privately treated with intense disdain. Curiously, they had an arrangement regarding each other's flirtations with other people, as though they had some kind of emotional or legal attachment which did not show otherwise.

History

The owner of Softer Tapestries, Inder Dunseltree was replaced by a drow infiltrator at some point on or before , and sent off to Chult. On that year, the drow infiltrator participated in a mission to get Halonder Eldeglut replaced by another drow. Unknown to him, his partner Anlaervrith Mrantarr had in turn been replaced by a different drow, Qilué Veladorn, one of the Seven Sisters.

During the infiltration, he and his partner (who had in turn been replaced without his knowledge) took care of distracting Halonder and his wife Iyrevven Eldeglut so that their wine could be poisoned. They were successful at this task before the one replacing his partner blew her cover.
