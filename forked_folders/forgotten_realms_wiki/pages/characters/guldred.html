Guldred was a lizardfolk in Cormanthor's Underdark.

History

In 1479 DR, Guldred was an outcast of his tribe living in an Underdark lake under the lair of Dretchroyaster. He may have helped some adventurers that had infiltrated the lair in exchange for killing his treacherous fellows.
