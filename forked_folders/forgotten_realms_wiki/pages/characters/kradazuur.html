Kradazuur was a blue dragon living near the Well of Dragons.

History

When the Cult of the Dragon attempted to summon Tiamat into the world at the Well of Dragons, Kradazuur was one of the chromatic dragon heralds sent to ensure the coming of the Dragon Queen.
