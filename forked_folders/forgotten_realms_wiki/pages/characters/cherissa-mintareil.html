Cherissa Mintareil was a famous fighter who stayed in the Citadel of the Mists under the mysterious Mistmaster.

Possessions

Cherissa carried a ward token that allowed her to avoid the wardmist spell that protected the Citadel of the Mists.

Activities

Before she came to the Citadel of the Mists, Cherissa served the kingdom of Cormyr. She was well-known for her exploits.
