Vaeristhelph Rex

Person
nameVaeristhelph Rex
genderMale
raceLich

Vaeristhelph Rex was one of the seven liches who created Crenshinibon, and was later consumed by it.

History

Rex rose to power as the wizard-emperor of a world beyond Toril. He chose to sacrifice his position for lichdom. Some time after this, he fell into a state of madness.
