Thalto Bresmer was a traveling merchant based in Telflamm, Thesk. He covered in the Dragon Reach area, including the Moonsea and the Dalelands.

Abilities

Thalto was an expert in fine furniture, ornate hardware for doors (locks, handles, and hinges), and hardstones in quantity, used in wall decorations and wood furniture inlays.

History

He was known to stay at the Whistling Wizard inn when passing through Voonlar. He called it "The most comfortable bad inn it has ever been my pleasure to stay in—quiet if shabby comfort, privacy if one desires it, and much entertainment if one does not."
