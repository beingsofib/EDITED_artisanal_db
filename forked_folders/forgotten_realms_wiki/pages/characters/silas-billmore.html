Silas Billmore was a spice merchant from the city of Mulmaster in the Moonsea region in the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

Silas's traveling companion was his mistress Tyvia Velent. The couple was accompanied by the half-orcish bodyguard named Marcus Aboy.

History

At some point before the late 14 century DR, Silas came across a Thayvian slave, Tyvia Velent. Silas purchased her and gifted her freedom, and subsequently, the two became lovers. Tyvia accompanied Silas on his business trips, including when dealing in spices with the Merchant House of Mossbridges in the Vast. Circa the , Silas Billmore, his lover, and his bodyguard, were guests of the Golden Goblet Inn in Mossbridges but showed little interest in conversing with the locals nor traveling adventurers.
