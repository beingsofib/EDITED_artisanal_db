Zaheb al-Zidq was a noble dao inhabiting Ajayib in Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Activities

Despite being a mighty noble among the dao, Zaheb was madly in love with the Calipha of Ajayib, Halima al-Wahsi. He spent most of his time at Halima's side, hoping to win her affections.

Relationships

Aside from driving off other potential suitors, it seemed that all of Zaheb's efforts were wasted since the Calipha viewed him as a favorite pet and nothing more.
