Ridimere Cordwin was the oldest son of General Vandemar Cordwin, an influential Arrabarran priest of Tempus during the late 14 century DR.

History

Sometime shortly before the , Glisena Foesmasher, the daughter of Baron Thuragar Foesmasher of Sespech refused Ridimere Cordwin's advances. Since then, the man's father, Vandemar Cordwin, sought revenge against Baron. Whether this was caused by the failed courting, was unclear. However, that did not stop gossips and rumors on the topic.
