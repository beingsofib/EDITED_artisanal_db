Scintilla was a night hag who found work at the Underdark Arena beneath Cormyr during the late 15 century DR.

Abilities

While still pursuing classic night hag interests, Scintilla also had a particular fondness for fire-related magics, earning her moniker as the Fire Witch.

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Scintilla served as a gladiator in the magma-filled Underdark Arena of the red dragon Aptoryx, alongside the Brimstone Archon and Finnfaran.
