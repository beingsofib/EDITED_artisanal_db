Forsyl Filarfar was an accomplished abjurer living in Yhaunn in 1358 DR.

Personality

Forsyl was a gentle, quiet man.

Activities

Skilled in abjuration magic, it was rumored that Forsyl placed many protective enchantments on his tower so he could pursue his academic studies in peace.
