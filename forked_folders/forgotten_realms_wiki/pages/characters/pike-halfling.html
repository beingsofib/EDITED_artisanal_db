Pike was a member of the Cult of the Crushing Wave during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Activities

Pike was the personal servant of Shoalar Quanderil.

History

In 1491 DR, like his master, Pike was transferred to the Dessarin Valley, where they were involved in an assault on the Mirabar diplomatic delegation. However, at Ironford bridge, they were confronted and defeated by some adventurers hired to find the delegation.
