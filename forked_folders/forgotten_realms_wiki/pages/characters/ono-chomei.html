Ono Chomei was a yakuza ninja of the Ono Kumi clan in Kozakura, circa .

History

In 1433, the clan's oyabun Ono Noburo ordered his lieutenant Ono Arieki to go to Doi No Fugu, an inn run by Arieki's family, and to seize control of the place he knew so well. Once he'd done so, he would call in a second group, which was to be headed by Chomei, and take the Seven Swords from the adventuring party that would shortly arrive. Although Noburo told Arieki that none of his family were to be harmed, he secretly ordered Chomei, Arieki's lieutenant, to kill all the people inside the inn in order to cover their trail, and Arieki too if he caused trouble.

That summer, Arieki led six men of Ono Kumi to the Inn of the Globefish, posing as a traveling merchant and his retainers, guards, and servants, then quickly surprised and subdued the Sumitomo family, imprisoning them. Meanwhile, Chomei led thirteen more ninja and lay in wait outside.

When the adventurers with the relics finally arrived, the Ono Kumi posed as the inn staff and welcomed them in, though male ninja made for poor geisha girls. They planned to distract them with a banquet at which they even performed "The Reward of Tomy", lull them with a biwa of calm and pearl of persuasion, and even have one of their number, a shukenja named Toshiro mislead them by prophesying an ambush after leaving the inn, before ambushing them with thrown shuriken and poison-gas eggshell grenades and attacking, when Chomei and his own men pounced. This would be complicated by the Sumitomo family's attempts to escape and alert the adventurers, the guard Haka Kinnori seizing the chance to strike back, and Yoichi's own crisis of conscience, which would be triggered if he was forced to fight his own family or saw them harmed or even killed, and he would potentially switch sides. The exact outcome of this terrible situation and the fate of the Sumitomos and others involved is unknown.

Abilities

Like other members of the Ono Kumi, Chomei was trained in the Smiling Viper martial arts style, knowing pain touch, stunning touch, paralyzing touch, and distance attacks, as well as choke holds and feints. He was also highly specialized in the ninja-to.

Possessions

He wore leather armor beneath his black fighting clothes and obi and wielded +1 ninja-to and a mariki-gusari. He had concealed three dart-shaped shuriken, and a bag of ten tetsu-bishi.
