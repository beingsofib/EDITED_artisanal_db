Turik Dumark was a bloodforge mage in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

A dangerous mage, Turik Dumark attempted to ambush the Legendary Campaign forces on the road by Old Stone Keep. However, one of Dumark's scouts was captured, and the trap was revealed. The leader of the Legendary Campaign quickly tried to hunt down out Dumark.
