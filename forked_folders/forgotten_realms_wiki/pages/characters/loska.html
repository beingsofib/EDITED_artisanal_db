Loska was a winter wolf that lived in the North during the late 15 century DR. She was the traveling companion of Delaan Winterhound, a ranger of the Emerald Enclave.

History

Loska accompanied Delaan to Waterdeep in the 1480s DR, during the founding on the Council of Waterdeep. She did enter with him into the city however, and remained on patrol beyond its gates.
