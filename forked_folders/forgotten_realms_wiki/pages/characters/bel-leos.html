Bel Leos was a hengeyokai in T'u Lung in 1358 DR.

History

Bel Leos was a hengeyokai working with the Black Leopard Cult in the town of Lenachi in T'u Lung. In 1358 DR, he investigated the many local Dragon Claw followers. Eventually, Bel Leos helped the wu jen Ko La Ko and an adventuring party hired by Ko Ho San to escape from the city.
