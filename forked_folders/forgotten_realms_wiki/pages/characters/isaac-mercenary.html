Isaac was an adventurer that served as a member of the Company of Dawn during the mid–14 century DR.

History

Isaac was among the members of the part that accompanied their fellow adventurers Midnight, Adon, Cyric, and Kelemvor Lyonsbane through the Spiderhaunt Wood to Shadowdale. He was one of three survivors within the Company of Dawn, along with Thurbrand and Vogt.

Devastated by their losses, the trio that remained continued on their travels shortly after their arrival at Shadowdale, back to Arabel in neighboring Cormyr.
