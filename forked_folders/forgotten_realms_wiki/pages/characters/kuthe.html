Kuthe was one of the Riders in Mistledale in the Dalelands in the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the , Kuthe led a patrol of the Riders. He was joined by Jhessail Silvertree, one of the Knights of Myth Drannor, in their effort to stop the Zhentilar armies converging on Shadowdale under the orders of Bane.
