Samuel was a human fighter in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Though he had a few encounters with monsters that saw him victorious, he still spent most of his time training and learning his craft.

Personality

Samuel was a cautious young man that liked to stay in taverns and hear the stories being told there, of adventures going on in the big cities such as Waterdeep.

Possessions

Samuel wore a set of chainmail and a cloak of elvenkind, while wielding a longsword and dagger.
