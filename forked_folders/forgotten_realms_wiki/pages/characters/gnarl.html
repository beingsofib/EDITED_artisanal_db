Gnarl was an ogrillion that dwelled on the Sword Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

Possesions

He carried with him a mundane bastard sword, along with a numerous potions, including one of cold resistance and another of hill giant strength.

History

As of the , Gnarl and his associate Hairtooth could be found extorting "toll payments" from travelers looking to cross the narrow waterway that passed east of a gnoll-controlled stronghold along the Coast.
