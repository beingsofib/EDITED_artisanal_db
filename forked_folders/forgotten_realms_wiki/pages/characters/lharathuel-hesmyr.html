Lharathuel Hesmyr was a human cleric of Chauntea in the village of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

In the late 1350s DR, she led services at the House of Plenty temple and had two acolytes: Torzhin Hulvesper, the youngest son of a local horse-breeder, and Jhenta Sulpir, who'd escaped an arranged marriage in Teziir. They were available to provide healing to the farmers.
