Timon Silvertongue was a human enchanter wandering the lands in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He usually traveled with different groups of adventurers who explored Faerûn.

Personality

Timon Silvertongue wasn't the type to stay in his tower for long periods of time, instead preferring to wander the land and talk with people. He always preferred talking to fighting.

Possessions

He wore a robe of scintillating colors and a pair of bracers of defense. He had a crystal ball and eyes of charming on him.
