Vidad

Person

homeChondath & Chessenta

Vidad was a mercenary and a member of the Sailors of the Crimson Sea based in Airspur in Chessenta and operating in Chondath in the mid–14 century DR.

In the early 1360s DR, Vidad was a lieutenant in command of the archers.
