Sherrill was a Dark Breaker of Umberlee, a member of the Bitch Queen's clergy under Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queen's House in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Sherrill was a human woman with dark skin, dark brown curly hair and watery blue eyes. Her face was decorated with metallic-colored golden and aquamarine tattoos.

Posessions

Sherrill's Dark Breaker uniform was a set of skin-tight heavy chainmail dress. The armor was decorated with golden trim and had decorated metallic pauldrons. Her weapon of choice was a polearm.

History

Circa the , during the Absolute crisis, Dark Breaker Sherrill was among the church's members who performed the faith's ceremonies and sang hymns in praise of the Bitch Queen. She was alongside Flood Tide Allandra Grey and other priestesses during Holli Dylford's last rites.
