Ender Sai was an elven spy who was tasked with investigating the iron crisis that afflicted the Sword Coast in the .

History

In the , Ender began investigating the activities of the Iron Throne. Some time after he began, Endar was apprehended by the bandit leader Tazok. He made the logical conclusion that the two incidents were linked.

During his confinement, Ender learned that Tazok was making regular trips to the Cloak Wood forest and believed that to be the next step in learning the truth behind the iron crisis.
