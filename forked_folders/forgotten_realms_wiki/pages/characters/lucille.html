Lucille was a powerful pit fiend, as well as the most trusted and loyal of Zariel's generals.

Description

She was a fearsome and terrible sight to behold on the battlefield.

Possessions

Lucille wore a frightening helm of devil command and carried a massive battle standard of infernal power while commanding her troops.

Relationships

Among the forces she led were a squad of a dozen spined devils.

History

In the , Lucille led Zariel's forces in the conflict to control the city of Elturel, after it was pulled down into Avernus atop the River Styx.
