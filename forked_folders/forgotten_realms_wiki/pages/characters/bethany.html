Bethany was a well-off human inhabitant of Neverwinter's Peninsula District in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Bethany was an elegant human woman with a light skin tone, brown eyes, and brown hair. She wore fanciful clothing and jewelry.

Relationship

Bethany did not know Head Gaoler Captain Alaefin of the Peninsula District personally, but she believed him to have always been an efficient warden.

History

During the time the Wailing Death ravaged Neverwinter in the , Bethany's home district, the Peninsula, fell to rioting escaped prisoners of the Neverwinter prison. She escaped the district and headed to the City Core to seek help from members of the Neverwinter militia. Sedos Sebile, one of the trusted agents of Nasher Alagondar, was in the rioting district, according to Bethany, but did not have nearly enough people under her command.
