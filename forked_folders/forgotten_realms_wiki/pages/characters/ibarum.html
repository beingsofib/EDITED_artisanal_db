Ibarum was a member of the Neverwinter militia in the City Core of Neverwinter in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Ibarum was a well-built human man with a light skin tone and brown hair. He wore the uniform of the city's militia.

Activities

During the Wailing of the , Ibarum was stationed in the City Core of Neverwinter, standing guard of the city gates, not allowing passage due to quarantine.
