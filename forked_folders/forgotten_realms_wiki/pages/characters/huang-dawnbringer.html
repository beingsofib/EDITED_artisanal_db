Huang was a Dawnbringer of Lathander from the city Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Lady Firelia Jannath hired Dawnbringer Huang to exorcise the restless spirit that took possession of her husband, Oskar Fevras. Huang was unable to fulfill the promise and recommended Lady Jannath to hire a priest of Selûne instead.
