Wheber Ott was a necromancer that hailed from the Sword Coast region of Faerûn for a time during the mid–14 century DR.

Possesions

He wore a robe of cold resistnace, and was armed with a +1 enchanted dagger.

History

In the , Wheber joined the Zhentarim mageNiemain, along with two other spellcasters, Willaim Garst and Oulam, to cast an especiallyparticularly difficult spell on the second flor of the Sorcerous Sundries in Baldur's Gate.
