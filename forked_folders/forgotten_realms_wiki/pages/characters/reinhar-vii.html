Reinhar VII was a king of the Arkaiuns of Dambrath.

Relationships

The man who would rule as Reinhar VII was the fourth son of King Reinhar VI. He was the father of Reinhar VIII.

History

Reinhar VII was born in the Year of the Telling Tome, 668 DR. He took his father's throne and name upon the latter's death in 691 DR. Reinhar VII died in 750 DR, passing the throne to his son.
