Bronzefume was a weak dragon turtle in service to the Cult of the Crushing Wave at the Temple of the Crushing Wave in Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Personality

Bronzefume agreed to help the Cult in exchange for riches. She was a young and impressionable creature. She preferred to destroy boats more than people.

History

In 1491 DR, Gar Shatterkeel convinced Bronzefume to help him in exchange for treasures. She patrolled a part of the temple, waiting for her next meeting with the water prophet.
