Olbert was a mage at some point before the 8 century DR.

History

In the , the sage Beldrune, working at the Moonshorn Tower, opined about a certain Olbert's triple-delayed blast fireball spell. Beldrune noted that Olbert claimed to have created that spell by combining the work of Iliymbrim Sharnult, Lhabbartan, and a third person whose name he could not recall at the moment. This indicated that Olbert was aware of both their works.
