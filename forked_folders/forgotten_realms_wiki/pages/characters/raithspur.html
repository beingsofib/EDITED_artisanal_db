Raithspur was the Captain of the City Guard of Ordulin in Sembia in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Raithspur was a regal individual and a skilled warrior.

Activities

As Captain, circa 1358 DR, Raithspur commanded 2,000 guardsmen in policing Ordulin and sometimes coming to its defense.

Possessions

He was renowned for his collection of powerful protective magic items.

History

During the Sembian Civil War in the , Raithspur managed to maintain control of the streets of the city under orders of the Overmistress Mirabeta Selkirk.
