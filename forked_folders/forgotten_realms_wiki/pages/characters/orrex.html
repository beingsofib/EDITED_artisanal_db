Orrex was a Templar of the Silver Flame in the world of Eberron.

Description

Orrex was a large and powerfully built human man. He wore a suit of plate mail.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, Orrex was one of the leading Silver Flame investigators who pursued the Lords of Dust in Stormreach as they attempted to break into the Spinner's Prison and free the Spinner of Shadows. Despite the best efforts of the Church of the Silver Flame and their allies, the Spinner escaped into the Demonweb Pits thanks to the intervention of Lolth, who broke into the prison herself and opened the Rift Between Worlds. Following this, Orrex called in the Gatekeepers to assist, the experts on planar gateways.
