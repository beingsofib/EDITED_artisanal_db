Avery Schell was a cleric of Deneir and one of the headmasters of the Edificant Library in the mid–14 century DR.

He was the substitute father of Cadderly Bonaduce.

History

Avery became a father figure to Cadderly

In the , Avery was savagely killed by the mage Bogo Rath during an attack by the Night Masks while visiting Cadderly in Carradoon, with his student Kierkan Rufo leading him to his murder.
