Lady Amalee Winter was a member of the noble House Wyvernspur of Cormyr who lived in the late 10 and early 11 centuries DR.

History

Amalee Winter married into House Wyvernspur sometime before the . Her husband was Gould Wyvernspur and with him she sired two sons. The older son, Gerrin Wyvernspur, was born in 980 DR. Gould's and Amalee's younger son was Finder Wyvernspur who would eventually become a renowned bard, one of the founding members of the Harpers, and subsequently, a deity.
