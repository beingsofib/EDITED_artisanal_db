Spool was a commoner child who lived in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Spool was a young human boy of bronze skin. His eyes were chestnut in color, and his light brown hair was trimmed short.

History

In the , Spool could be found in the Lower City with his two friends, Stinker and Runt, often playing "chase the gelatinous cube" in the alleys or loitering in front of Devil's Fee shop, daring eachother to go into the "devil's house" and accusing eachother of being scaredy-cats. Notably, Spool was unenthused by Devil's Fee, thinking the kids' daring of each other to be boring.
