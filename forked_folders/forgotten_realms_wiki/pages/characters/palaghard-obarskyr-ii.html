Palaghard Obarskyr II of the Obarskyr dynasty was a king of Cormyr in the 13 century DR, reigning from the to the .

Relationships

Palaghard was born the second son of King Dhalmass Obarskyr and Queen Jhalass Huntsilver in the . He had two sisters, Pelara and Elliira, as well as an older brother, Proster, who died at the age of 9 in 1222 DR.

Palaghard's queen was Alendue Wyrvar. They had three sons: Lhannas (who died at the age of 22 in 1258 DR), Azoun (who would become King Azoun III), and Salember (who would be regent after Azoun's death).
