Braelen Hatherhand was a boy in Red Larch and a son of a member of the Believers in the 1490s DR.

History

In 1491 DR, his father put him in the Tomb of Moving Stones as punishment for failed to deliver a message to Ilmeth Waelvur. Here he was found by some adventurers who were investigating in the village.
