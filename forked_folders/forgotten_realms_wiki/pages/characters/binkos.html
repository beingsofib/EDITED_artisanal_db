Binkos was a messenger who lived on the Sword Coast during the mid−14 century DR.

Description

Binkos was quite rushed and hurried in his duties, as was befitting for a messenger.

History

When the iron crisis plagued the Sword Coast, in the , Binkos was tasked delivering messages between Beregost and the Council of Four in Baldur's Gate.
