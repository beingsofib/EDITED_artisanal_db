Tlassian Talmar Eldwing was a dragonborn scholar of Tymanther and the second-in-command of Clan Tlassian in 1479 DR.

Description

Talmar was described as a bookish, middle-age dragonborn in 1479 DR. Despite being a scholar, Talmar had an impressive physique, thanks to his long years as a soldier in his youth.

History

Talmar was one of the elders of Clan Tlassian in 1479 DR. He was also considered one of the most knowledgeable scholars in Djerad Thymar, and was widely respected for his intelligence and his encyclopedic knowledge of military history.
