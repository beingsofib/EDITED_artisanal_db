Antola Pilark was a halfling Great Druid and an ally of House Kendrick in the Moonshae Isles in the .

Activities

Antola led troops from House Kendrick as well as the druids of Alaron against the spreading evil of the goblin king Gark's minions within the Dernall Forest near Caer Callidyrr. Unknown to her, one of her followers was a traitor.

She was said to be able to craft weapons that rivaled the sentinel's cudgels made by druids of yore.
