Micahrastir was a powerful red dragon that laired in the Underdark during the 1480s DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Micahrastir lived atop a great pile of coins and other treasures, located on a rocky platform above the trail between the Great Waterfall and Tranquility Grove near Sschindylryn. The great red dragon was rather separate from mundane threats and politics, though the drider Jorenthar Sparskrael strongly desired a sample of the dragon's scales for his experiments.
