X. Scuttermoore

Person
nameX. Scuttermoore
homeBaldur's Gate, Sword Coast

X. Scuttermoore was an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

Scuttermoore owned a wrinkly pet pug dog.

History

During the Absolute crisis of , the Guild of Baldur's Gate targeted X. Scuttermoore, presumably as part of the Guild's protection racket or unpaid debts. When Scuttermoore began running low on coin, the Guild claimed their deposit box key from the Counting House; however, that did not stop harassment and demands of payment by the agents of the Guild.
