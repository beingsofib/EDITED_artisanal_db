Roveer was a cook from Baldur's Gate's Elfsong Tavern, who, apparently, cooked ducks daily, in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Roveer sought exterminators to kill rat vermin in the cellars of the Elfsong Tavern. Despite him carrying a massive axe, and owning two cats, Tambourine and Zambomba, the inhabitants and Alan Alyth's employees could not or were unwilling to resolve the egregious food safety issue.
