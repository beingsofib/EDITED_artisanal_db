Captain Kesmer Red-Eyed, also called the Coast Wolf, was a leader of the Sea Reavers pirate fleet of the Nelanther Isles in the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the 1340s DR, Captain Kesmer Red-Eyed commanded the Sea Reavers through their longest-lasting incarnation to date.

That was until the fateful day he docked at the pirate isle of Skaug and took aboard the adventurer Mintiper Moonsilver. Soon after, a storm drove Kesmer's ship onto rocks, wrecking it. Only one pirate survived, having been hurled well clear of the rocks by the impact. Thus, he was witness to what came next: a great magical flame burst up around Mintiper, then engulfed Kesmer and the whole crew. When the flames faded, all were consumed by the fire and Mintiper had vanished. The pirates believed Mintiper had killed the Coast Wolf and the rest by means of trickery and magical fire. (Mintiper himself escaped, telling acquaintances that an enchanted green gem gifted him by his mother had activated to save him, consuming the pirates and itself in order to teleport him away to the Purple Hills headlands.)

After that, many thought the Sea Reavers had been obliterated for good, but their remnants found a new leader in Toramir Kuurgan.
