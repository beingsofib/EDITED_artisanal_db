Activities

During his years traveling Faerûn, Mhzentul spent much of his time studying arcanespells that involved the conjuring of flames, the creation of magical rings.

He was famed for creating the Seven Lost Rings of Mhzentul, each of which contained extremely powerful magic.

History

Mhzentul died at the Battle of River Rising, after he transformed himself into a pillar of living flame. His legacy lived on his famed book, titled Mhzentul's Runes.
