Thunn, who was also called the Usurper, was the second King of the Mulan Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate.

Activities

Thunn betrayed his uncle, King Ashtukzu I, and claimed the throne for himself in . He ruled until when his cousin, Ashtukzu II, reclaimed his birthright.
