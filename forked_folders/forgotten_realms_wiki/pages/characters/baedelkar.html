Baedelkar the Thaumaturge was a Zhentarim wizard in Ordulin.

History

Baedelkar was a rising hope inside the Zhentarim. On Flamerule 16 of 1358 DR, Baedelkar was at the Winking Will-o'-the-Wisp Pleasure Palace playing with the Lady of the Red Sash. However, his fellow Nentor Thuldoum arrived with orders from Manshoon to depart immediately. The Lady of the Red Sash revealed herself to be a malaugrym and killed Baedelkar before he could act.
