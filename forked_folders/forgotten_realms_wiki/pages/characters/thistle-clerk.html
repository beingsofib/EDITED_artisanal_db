Thistle was a clerk who worked at the largest trading post in Phandalin, Barthen's Provisions, in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Thistle would mostly load and unload wagons full of trade goods. When the owner was not in the trading post, Thistle would attend the needs of the customers.

History

The young clerk and his colleague, Ander, worked for Elmina Barthen in 1490 DR.

During a routine supply delivery to the dwarven Deadknock Mine, Thistle discovered the mine completely abandoned. His calls went unanswered, leading him to conclude Galdibran's dwarves had been forcibly driven out. When he reported his findings, Elmina Barthen decided to halt supplies, making Thistle's delivery the last. A year later, a group of adventurers questioned him about the mine, after they've been tasked by Elmina to find the dwarves.

Not long after the white dragon Cryovain rampaged through the region, Thistle met Velleen Firecrow, a new assistant at the local Lionshield Coster outpost, and took a liking to her. While trying to get to know her, Velleen began talking about her apocalyptic religious beliefs, which scared Thistle off. Unbeknownst to him, Velleen was an operative for the Cult of Talos.
