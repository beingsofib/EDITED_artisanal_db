Helbondel was a drow cleric of Vhaeraun involved in an infiltration of Scornubel during the .

Personality

Helbondel hated Lolthites.

Abilities

Helbondel was a cleric.

Possessions

Notably, Helbondel carried an amulet touched by Vhaeraun himself, which had been twisted and charred by flame in the process. He knew of a manner to employ it to call forth a magic that shattered all magics, but expected that would destroy the relic.

Activities

Helbondel was involved in an infiltration of Scornubel by drow, an effort that lasted until at least 1369 DR.

Relationships

He was an ally of Sarltan.

History

When the drow trader Iylinvyx finally met with Sarltan after trying to find him for most of a night in , he commanded his men to strike her down. She got the upper hand, and while several drow retreated past Helbondel, he decided to involve himself in the fight. However, while he was on the ground, a drow with a sword through his pelvis stumbled back and fell sword-point into his neck, killing him instantly.

Rumors & Legends

During his last moments, Helbondel misjudged Iylinvyx, a newly-arrived drow trader, to be a priestess of Lolth. She was actually Dove Falconhand in disguise.
