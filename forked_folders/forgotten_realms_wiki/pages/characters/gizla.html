Gizla was a scholastic woman in Eveningstar during the 1480s DR.

Description

Gizla was a big half-orc woman, with long black hair and tan skin.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Gizla heard of the strange and terrible events around Lolth and the Rift Between Worlds, which Lolth was using to slowly devour the world of Eberron. She investigated the issue until it culminated with Valana's efforts in the Demonweb Pits.
