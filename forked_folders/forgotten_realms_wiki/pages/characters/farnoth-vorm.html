Farnoth Vorm was a half-orc barbarian in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Farnoth was a huge fellow with green-gray skin, and had black muttonchops and a mohawk. He wore a set of hide armor.

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Farnoth helped train aspiring barbarians there in the village.
