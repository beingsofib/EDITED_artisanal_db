Janszeene Naerhand was the leader of House Naerhand, a powerful merchant family that was active from the Sword Coast to Cormyr as of 1479 DR.

Relationships

Janszeene didn't trust her sister Malarrla who was considered unfit to ever lead the family. She had Malarrla closely watched to prevent a coup attempt.

Abilities

Janszeene could throw poisoned daggers with deadly accuracy and had taken small doses of poison for years to build up immunity.

Possessions

She collected an arsenal of magic items to use in case she was ever attacked by a wizard.
