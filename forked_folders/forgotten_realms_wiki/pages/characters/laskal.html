Laskal was a leader within the Shadow Druids who operated within the Cloak Wood forest during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

As of the late 1360s DR, Laskal was considered rather old for a human.

Personality

He considered himself to be the "protector" of the Cloak Wood forest and carried himself as a diplomat of the organization.

Possessions

Laskal possessed a +2 enchanted quarterstaff.

Relationships

He worked alongside Faldorn, who later became one of the Shadow Druids' most renowned leaders.

History

When the Iron Throne established their mine within the Cloak Wood forest in the , they made made enemies of the forest's Shadow Druids. A mere few years later, Laskal and his companions sought to rid the forest of their corrupting presence.
