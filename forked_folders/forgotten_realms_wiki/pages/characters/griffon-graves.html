Griffon Graves was a human druid of Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Abilities

Griffon preferred to fight in the form of a huge dire bear, eschewing spellcasting in favor of raw power.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Griffon and his brother Leif Graves rampaged about the High Road, acting in a generally hostile manner to everyone.
