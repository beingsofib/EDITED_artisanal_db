Pasi was a tiefling explorer who made her fortune exploring Tier Lathadun in the Underdark sometime before the late 14 century DR.

History

The Ruins of Tier Lathadun held many treasures that were discovered by Pasi, who helmed multiple delves into the cavern, armed with her favorite enchanted bludgeon – Doorknocker. Pasi's life ended some years before the , when she slipped off a rocky edge and fell a thousand feet into the dark depths of the Underdark. Her weapon was believed lost along with Pasi's body until it reappeared in 1372 DR.
