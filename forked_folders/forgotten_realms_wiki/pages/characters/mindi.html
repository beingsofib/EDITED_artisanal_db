Mindi was a child fan of the dancer known as Shimmer from the Blushing Mermaid in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Mindi was a young girl with curly blonde hair.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Shimmer was a regular performer who entertained the customers of the shady Blushing Mermaid tavern in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. Shimmer was a popular performer known for his loud makeup, glittering outfits, and impressive dancing. He had one devoted fan—the young Mindi, who was in fact too young to frequent the Blushing Mermaid, according to her father. The child passionately begged Shimmer to become his apprentice, believing him to be the best dancer she'd ever seen, and she already was a great dancer, in her own words, able to do two cartwheels in a row. She offered the services of a makeup artist, outfit washer, and a hairstylist in exchange for apprenticeship.
