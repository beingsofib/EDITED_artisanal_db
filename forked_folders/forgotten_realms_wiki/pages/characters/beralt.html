Beralt was a boy member of the Emerald Enclave in the Moonsea in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Beralt studied as a druid under a master and was a member of the Emerald Enclave.

History

Circa 1486 DR, his master sent him near Hillsfar to investigate corrupted creatures. There he saved the boy tiefling Pynroth from some wild dogs, with the help of some adventurers who happened to be near the farmhouse.
