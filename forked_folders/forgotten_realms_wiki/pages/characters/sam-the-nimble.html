Sam the Nimble was a tavern keeper and spy from Liara in Nova Vaasa, one of the Domains of Dread.

Possessions

Notably, Sam bore a ring of protection and a powerful enchanted short sword.

Activities

Besides owning The Black Sam Tavern, Sam was a spy.

Relationships

He was a spy for the Bolshniks.

History

Sam the Nimble may have gotten involved in the murders by the werespider Sundian Silgathen in a period matching roughly the mid-to-late 14 century DR, which were investigated by a band of Ravens Bluff adventurers somehow finding themselves in the Domains of Dread. If so, he would've likely been the cause of one or more lycanthropes investigating the catacombs where Jebbrie Vokasich and Sundian Silgathen were bitten by cursed spiders.
