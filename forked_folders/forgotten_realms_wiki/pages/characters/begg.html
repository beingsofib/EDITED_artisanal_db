Begg was a captain of the guard in Zazesspur, Tethyr, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Being a captain of the guard, Begg was known to lead a patrol.

Relationships

At least one of his men was called Thaedras.

History

In Flamerule of the , Captain Begg was among the men involved in the investigation of a series of murders that had drawn the attention of Count Kelmar Dargren, who had gone missing. He remarked on the strangeness of the situation, as the Count had been seen the day prior, the same day they found his corpse hidden in the woods.
