Jergen was a member of a cell of the Cult of Reborn Flame that operated out of Tarmalune in 1480 DR.

Description

Jergen was a bald duergar male with a large tattoo of an erupting volcano superimposed over a flame on his head.

History

In 1480 DR, Jergen was a member of a cell of the Cult of Reborn Flame operating out of Tarmalune. Their job was to awaken the primordial Achazar. Jergen was eventually killed by a group of adventurers sent by Halagothra the Healer to stop the cult.
