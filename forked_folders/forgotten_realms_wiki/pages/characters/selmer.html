Selmer was a tavern owner inhabiting Suldolphor in the far eastern reaches of Calimshan in south Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Selmer owned and operated a tavern named after himself called Selmer's Mark located in southeast Suldolphor. He watered his ales and wines in order to make more profit. Selmer also employed children as servers who worked for room and board.
