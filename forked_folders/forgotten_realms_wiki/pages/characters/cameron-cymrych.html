Cameron "the Wise" Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid–4 century DR.

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty: he was the grandson of the dynasty's founder, High King Warren I, and the great-grandson of the dynasty's legendary namesake, Cymrych Hugh. Cameron was the cousin of his predecessor, High King Markus, and his successor was his second son, Bran Cymrych.
