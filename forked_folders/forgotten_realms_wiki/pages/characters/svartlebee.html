Svartlebee, also known as The Custodian, was a mercenary active some years before the late 15 century DR.

History

Svartlebee was well-known for being the master of the enchanted greatsword, later called Svartlebee's Woundseeker. Sometime before the , Svartlebee used the magic blade to remove individuals causing mess on requests from other and even cleaned up the crime scenes after.
