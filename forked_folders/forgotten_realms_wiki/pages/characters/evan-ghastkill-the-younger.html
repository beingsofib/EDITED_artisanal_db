Evan Ghastkill the Younger was the scion of the prominent Ghastkill family of Nashkel around 1370 DR.

Personality

Evan the Younger was (in the opinion of his family) a headstrong, dull, impatient, and overly arrogant fellow.

Relationships

Evan was the child and heir of Lord Evan Ghastkill and Cara.

History

His father Lord Evan had been very ill for a long time and was not expected to live through 1370 DR. While Evan the Younger was anxious to take up his father's role, both his mother Cara and his uncle Berrun believed that Evan's poor personal traits made him incapable of effectively managing the family's considerable finances.
