Fornault Blacksun was a cleric of Cyric in Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Fornault had a pointed chin and beady eyes. He was always smirking.

Activities

Fornault was the High Priest within Twilight Hall, a temple dedicated to Cyric that was consturcted within the Foreign Quarter following the city's destruction in the . He enjoyed the challenge of rebuilding Cyric's faith in Zhentil Keep. Fornault was served by sixteen underpriests and lay brethren.
