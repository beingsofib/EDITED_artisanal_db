Therry was an individual taking notes for the Watch of Marsember in the late 14 century DR.

History

In the early spring of , after Glarasteer Rhauligan, a Harper, and the quarry he was pursuing, the Waterdhavian thief Narnra Shalace escaped the Watch in Marsember by leaping into a canal, one of his superiors gave Therry the order to record them as drowned lovers.
