Hephis was an enthusiastic tavern-goer who lived in Beregost during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Hephis was a regular patron of Feldepost's Inn and enjoyed singing lengthy tavern songs that went on seemingly forever. He became thoroughly annoyed whenever someone approached him while in the middle of a verse.
