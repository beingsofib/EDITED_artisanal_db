Yonks was a roaming adventure who traveled the Sword Coast North during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Yonks was a diminutive, older gnomish male with pale skin and blue eyes. He wore padded clothing with leather pauldrons and gloves.

History

In the , Yonks was a member of an adventuring band that included high elf Sarafin, half-orc Zog, tiefling Quinara. The group traveled the Sword Coast in search of steady adventuring work. They traveled from Waterdeep to Baldur's Gate during the Absolute crisis and, unable to find work, planned to move out to Neverwinter as the city was searching for adventures. During his stay in Baldur's Gate, Yonks frequented The Velveteen Elixir shop, stocking up on potions.
