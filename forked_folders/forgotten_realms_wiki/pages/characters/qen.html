Warrior Qen was a githyanki guard from Crèche Y'llek, on Toril during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Qen was a gith with gaunt body and leathery yellow skin. He had long brown hair and he was adorned in a set of heavy githyanki armor and armed with a gith greatsword.

History

Warrior Qen, along with other gith of what would become Crèche Y'llek, attacked and claimed the Rosymorn Monastery of Lathander in the Western Heartlands of West Faerûn sometime before the . Soon after, the function of Crèche was changed as the githyanki war against the mind flayers in Faerûn shifted in balance. Qen stood guard in the Crèche's hatchery and showed stern determination to his duty.
