Karsev (pronounced KAR-seff) was a member of the Cult of the Crushing Wave in the Moonsea.

Possessions

In battle, Karsev wore a suit of scalemail and fought with a hand fork and shield.

Relationships

Karsev was the right-hand of fellow cultist, the marel Shelavassa.

History

In 1491 DR, they managed to capture the aquatic elf prince Ulatha Mesavir in order to use him as sacrifice to create a devastation orb to unleash over Mulmaster. However, Karsev and his minions were confronted and defeated by an adventuring party hired to destroy the cultist group for their bandit activities near River Lis.
