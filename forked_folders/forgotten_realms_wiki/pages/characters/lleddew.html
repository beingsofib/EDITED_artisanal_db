Mother Lleddew was a werebear priestess of Selûne and a former adventurer. She was the high priestess of the House of the Lady in Immersea, Cormyr.

History

Originally, she was an adventurer, a companion of Cole Wyvernspur. She adventured with him to gain treasure to complete the building of the House of the Lady temple to Selûne on Spring Hill.
