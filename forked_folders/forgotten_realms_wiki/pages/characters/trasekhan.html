Tras'ekhan was a draegloth berserker that lived in the drow city of Sschindylryn during the 15 century DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Tras'ekhan defended the city from intruders, in particular the Cormyrean agents sent to sow chaos in the city.
