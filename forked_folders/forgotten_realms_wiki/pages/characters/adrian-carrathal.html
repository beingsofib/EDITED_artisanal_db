Adrian "the Twinblade" Carrathal was High King of Callidyrr from the mid–12 through the turn of the 13 century DR. Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles, he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty. He was the sole heir of King Edwin II and Queen Eleanor, and his eventual successor was his grandson, Cedric.

History

Adrian was only ten years old when his father died in the , and so his mother named Adom Kimball, King of Moray, to serve as regent over Callidyrr. Adrian assumed the throne in the , when he was sixteen.

In the seventh year of his reign, agents of the Kraken Society began operating throughout the North and the Moonshaes.

Adrian ruled for sixty-six years before he died of natural causes in the .
