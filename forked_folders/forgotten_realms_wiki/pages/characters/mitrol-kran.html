Mitrol Kran was a famed Sage of Alaghôn, in the nation of Turmish, Vilhon Reach, in the 14 century DR.

History

Mitrol Kran's musings and scholarly works focused on the region of the Vilhon Reach. The Sage of Alaghôn concluded that "Shining Arrabar", the nickname given to the capital of Chondath, was not just its physical description but also referred to the attitude of those who resided in the city. Mitrol Kran warned Arrabarrans that those looking to control neighboring nations in the 14 century Arrabar were destined to repeat the mistakes and failures of Chondath's tumultuous past.
