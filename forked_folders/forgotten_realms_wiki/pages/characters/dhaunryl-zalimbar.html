Dhaunryl Zalimbar was a mage inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Dhaunryl was tall and gangly.

Personality

He had a kind demeanor though he was very shy. He greatly preferred studying and poring over books to putting himself in danger by adventuring.

Activities

He worked as a service-mage at Aurora's Realms Shop, Street of the Tusks Catalogue Counter within Waterdeep's Trades Ward. Dhaunryl enjoyed researching and collecting heraldic devices and sketching other such badges he saw in Waterdeep. He memorized many of the mottoes he came across.
