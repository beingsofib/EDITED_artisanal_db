Kartok Paingiver was an orc warband leader of the kingdom of Many-Arrows in the late 15 century DR.

History

When the Many-Arrows orcs occupied the River District of Neverwinter in the late 1470s DR, Kartok was among the warband leaders behind the takeover of the former Merchant Square. He based himself and his soldiers in the old Merchant Guild Hall, which the orcs repaired and fortified.

In the , when the Neverwinter Guard attempted to retake the district, Captain Janros sent an adventurer to invade the guildhall and slay Kartok.
