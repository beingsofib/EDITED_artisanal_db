Katelyn Lerskin was a Gur monster hunter from a tribe led by Ulma that traveled along the Sword Coast and Western Heartlands during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Katelyn was a tan-skinned Gur with short reddish brown hair and a facial tattoo. She wore a set of leathers and was armed with a war hammer.

History

In the , Katelyn Lerskin's monster hunter tribe found itself targeted by the vampires of Baldur's Gate during the Absolute crisis when the Gurs camped in the Rivington district of the city, along with a small army of refugees displaced by the conflict. To the Gurs' horror, the tribe's children were abducted by the vampires under orders from Cazador Szarr. Katelyn could be found near Ulma as she attended the tribe's funeral bonfire.
