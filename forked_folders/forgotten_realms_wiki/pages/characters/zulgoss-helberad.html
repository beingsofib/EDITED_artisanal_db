Zulgoss Helberad was a dungsweeper and cleric of Lathander inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Zulgoss was the guildmaster for the Dungsweepers' Guild circa the . He worked out of the Muleskull Tavern–their headquarters on Ship Street in the Dock Ward.
