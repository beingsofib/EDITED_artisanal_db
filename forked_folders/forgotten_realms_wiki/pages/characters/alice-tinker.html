Alice Tinker worked at the Curious Past in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep as an assistant to Bronwyn Caradoon.

Activities

Alice was a skilled drawer and appraiser. Her presence at Bronwyn's shop made it a notable meeting place for halflings and gnomes in Waterdeep.
