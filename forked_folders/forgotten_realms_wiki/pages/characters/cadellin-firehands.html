The Lady's Mage Cadellin Firehands was a powerful wizard inhabiting Saerloon, Sembia, in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Circa , Cadellin was the Lady's Mage at the Tower of Mystery temple in Saerloon. He was a devout follower of Mystra and served as the leader of the temple.

History

On Uktar 29 of the , it was suspected that Cadellin and his fellows from the temples of Mystra and of Azuth used teleportation circle to transport the Saerloon army to just outside the rebel city of Selgaunt.
