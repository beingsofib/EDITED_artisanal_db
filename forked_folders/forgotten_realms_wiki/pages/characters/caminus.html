Caminus was a red dragon in Damara who was slain in the late 14 century DR. He laird in the lava-filled crater of a volcano.

History

Caminus took part in the orc attack on the Gemspark Mine in Damara circa the . He obeyed the order of the Slaad lord Ygorl, and served as his mount in an attack on Khelben Arunsun's tower.

Caminus killed the githyanki general Cireka after Ygorl hurled her through a portal to the dragon's lair. Caminus was in turn was killed by Rannek, Zhai and Illius, who had come to his lair in pursuit of Cireka.
