Kegwell was a house guard for the noble Seasilver family of Marsember who served during the late 15 century DR.

History

When two of the god's chosen, Joelle Emmeline, and Malik el Sami were traveling through Marsember, they made their way into the Seasilver estate and were stopped by the lord's majordomo Greymace. Kegwell and his fellow house guards were summoned to the scene, and he was given a parcel wrapped in cloth by Malik. Kegwell was ordered to take it directly to Grand Duke Farnig Seasilver aboard the Wave Wyvern as it would protect the lord.
