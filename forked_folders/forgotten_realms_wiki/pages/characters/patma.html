Patma was a storm giant from the storm giant aerie of Lake Woe mountain.

Description

Patma had pale violet skin and blue-black hair that was very long and unkempt. He was clean-shaven. Patma typically wore a simple tunic with a large amount of silver jewelry. His eyes were silver-colored.

Possessions

Patma bore a rusted sword in battle.

History

In 1369 DR, the then-paramount of the storm giants of Mount Woe, Anastes, and five other storm giants were summoned magically by Lanaxis to defend him when he needed to rest during his kidnapping of Queen Brianna and her son Kaedlaw. Patma was one of those giants. Patma and Sebastion were chosen to find wine and vegetables for Brianna to eat, but during this time, Tavis Burdun and armies of giant-kin attacked. Patma and Sebastion were then ordered by Anastes to deal with the firbolgs. Patma fought with Tavis, until his sword was magically shattered by the firbolg shaman Munairoe. He was then taken down and killed by three firbolg warriors.
