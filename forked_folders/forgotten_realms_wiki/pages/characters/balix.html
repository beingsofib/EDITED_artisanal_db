Balix was a prisoner at the Fane of the Eye under Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Personality

The drow was a wizard and a historian.

History

In 1491 DR, he went with a scout companion to check the Fane but they were ambushed by the Cult of the Howling Hatred. Balix was captured and the scout killed. He remained a prisoner of some air cultists and a nycaloth until some adventurers infiltrated the Fane, killing the cultists, and freed him.
