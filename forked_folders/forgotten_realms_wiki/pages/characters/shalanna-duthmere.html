Shalanna Duthmere was an innkeep inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

She had pale skin and had a generally "worn" appearance.

Activities

Shalanna owned the Splintered Stair, a decent inn located on Gut Alley in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Relationships

She was a widow with six daughters.
