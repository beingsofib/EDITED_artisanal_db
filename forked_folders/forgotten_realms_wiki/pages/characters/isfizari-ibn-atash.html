Isfizari ibn-Atash was an outland priest and fanatic of Kossuth inhabiting Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Description

Frequent violent communes with his god left Isfizari's body horribly burned and disfigured. He hid these injuries behind hand and face wraps.

Activities

Isfizari lived to spread the word of mighty Kossuth. On a weekly basis Isfizari tasked Ayesha al-Rumi and Qirmiz min Hudid with bringing him an enemy of his faith. The victim was burned alive during a special ceremony and their ashes were added to large pile containing the remains of previous victims.
