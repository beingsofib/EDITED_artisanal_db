The Hierophant was a half-elf druid and proxy of the power known as Silvanus.

Personality

The Hierophant's primary passion in life was nature and her deity Silvanus, to a degree that one could call fanatical. There was a gleam in her eyes whenever she spoke of them, which was most of the time.

Abilities

In addition to having druidic powers, Silvanus granted the Hierophant the ability to transform herself into any animal or tree that she's seen within his divine realm of Summeroak.

Activities

The Hierophant spent most of her time wandering the woodlands of the divine realm known as Tir na Og, looking for those who might appreciate the wild beauty of Summeroak.
