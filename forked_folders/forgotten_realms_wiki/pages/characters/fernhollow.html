Fernhollow was a member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Fist Fernhollow was a hin woman with olive skin, and hazel eyes. She wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist: a helmet, a set of chainmail, and a tabard adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia. She wielded a longsword.

History

In the , Fist Fernhollow was stationed in the Outer City of Baldur's Gate, within the Wyrm's Crossing fortress. During the Absolute crisis, Fernhollow and other Fists guarded the new Grand Duke, Enver Gortash—unbeknownst to all one of the men behind the Cult of the Absolute and the strife that befell Baldur's Gate.
