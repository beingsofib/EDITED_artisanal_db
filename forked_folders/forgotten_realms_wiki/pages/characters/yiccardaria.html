Yiccardaria

Yiccardaria was a yochlol, one of many handmaidens of Lolth.

Yiccardaria was appointed to the reborn Yvonnel the Eternal when she became the Chosen of Lloth. Yiccardaria monitored, protected, and served as emissary for Yvonnel, acting as a direct line with the goddess.
