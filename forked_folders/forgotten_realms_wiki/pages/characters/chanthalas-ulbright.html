Chanthalas Ulbright was a human priest of Tymora in Baldur's Gate in the mid–14 century DR.

By 1358 DR, Chanthalas was the high priest of the Lady's Hall temple, holding the title of High Priest of the Lady's Favor. He led twenty-four priests and sixty-nine followers by 1367 DR.
