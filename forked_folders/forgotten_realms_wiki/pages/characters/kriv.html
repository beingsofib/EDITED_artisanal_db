Kriv was one of the few dragonborn wizards of Tymanther.

Description

Kriv had yellow eyes and bronze scales flecked with black.

History

In 1479 DR, Kriv was one of the wizards Vanquisher Tarhun sought to study the dragonspawn-controlling orbs created by Nala.
