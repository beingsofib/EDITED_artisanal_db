Shaeroon Brossfeather was a tavern owner who inhabited the city of Waterdeep during the early parts of the 14 century DR.

Activities

Shaeroon founded a tavern and gambling hall called the Gentle Mermaid in the North Ward by converting an estate that previously housed one of Waterdeep's noble families. She owned and ran the Gentle Mermaid for many years before her death.
