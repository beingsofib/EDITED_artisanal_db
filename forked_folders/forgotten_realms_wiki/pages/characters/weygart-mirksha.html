Weygart Mirksha was the head of the Mirksha family, which operated the eponymous merchant coster in Archendale during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Weygart was the oldest of three brothers, who owned and operated their family business together. He owned trading posts in both cities of Archenbridge and White Ford, and specialized in metalwork, spices, and silk imported from foreign nations.
