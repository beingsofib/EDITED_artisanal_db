Landorl Valadarm was a magelord of Athalantar in the early 3 century DR.

History

In Hammer of the , one of the Magelords of Athalantar, Kadeln Olothstar, agreed with the Swordmaster of Sarn Torel that a team of twenty armsmen and a dozen wizards would be sent to slay some of the rebel Knights of Athalantar. Privately, the swordmaster hoped that Landorl Valadarm was someone who'd offended Kadeln, rather than a lesser or inept mage.
