Rildra was a Harper in Scornubel during the mid-14 century DR.

Abilities

She was a very vicious fighter.

Activities

Rildra was part of Harper activities in or around Scornubel.

Relationships

The Harper was known to Marlel and to Ulburt.

History

In late , Marlel tried to talk to Rildra while guiding Shandril Shessair and Narm Tamaraith through Scornubel. He asked Ulburt to refer them to her, but Ulburt said Rildra had been slain fighting Red Wizards, killing one and hurting his bodyguard while armed with nothing but a hairpin.
