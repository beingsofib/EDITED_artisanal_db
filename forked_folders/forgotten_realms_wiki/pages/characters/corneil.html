Corneil was a conjurer and a member of the Cowled Wizards who lived during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Corneil served as the Cleric Upkeep of Mystical Regulation. He was tasked issuing licenses to spellcasters who wished to freely cast magic in the city of Athkatla.

The fee for one of these licenses was 5,000 gp.
