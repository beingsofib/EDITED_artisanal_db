Suzildara Sharranen was the High Whipmistress at the Resting Place of the Whip in Melvaunt in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

She watched over sixteen other priests and oversaw all the rituals of the temple.

Description

A stunning beauty, Suzildara was slender with dark eyes and hair.
