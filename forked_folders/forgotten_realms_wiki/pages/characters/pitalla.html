Pitalla was a shukenja in the land of Koryo in Kara-Tur.

Pitalla used a wish from the leaping fish to cure the settlement of Mo'ki of the ivory plague. He was believed to have then taken the leaping fish to the island of Su'un and left it there, where it remained long lost by the mid–14th century DR.
