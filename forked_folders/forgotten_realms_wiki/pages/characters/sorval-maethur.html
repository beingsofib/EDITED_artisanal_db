Sorval Maethur was a merchant in the late 14 century DR.

History

Sorval Maethur participated in the Marsember meeting of the Rightful Conspiracy during the early spring of . He was assassinated there while talking to two other people, one of which was injured and the other of which fled; the murder was witnessed by one Narnra Shalace, a thief from Waterdeep that had followed the wizard Elminster Aumar through a portal.
