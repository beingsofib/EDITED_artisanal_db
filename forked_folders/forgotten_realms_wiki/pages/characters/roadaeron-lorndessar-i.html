Roadaeron Lorndessar was the second and fourth King of the Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate.

Activities

Roadaeron became King in the , when his granduncle King Sarvyn "the Old" died. He ruled until the , before his throne was usurped by his Mage Royal Alzurth. Roadaeron lived in exile for over 40 years before reclaiming his rightful throne right before his death in the .

Relationships

He had seven children during his life. His third son Blaervaer would rule as King.
