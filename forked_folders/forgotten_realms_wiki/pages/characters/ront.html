Ront was an orc warrior of the Iceshield tribe in the Dessarin Valley in the late 15 century DR.

History

His war party was slaughtered by a group of dwarves. Only Ront survived by escaping into the Underdark. There, he was captured by drow slavers of Velkynvelve circa 1486 DR.
