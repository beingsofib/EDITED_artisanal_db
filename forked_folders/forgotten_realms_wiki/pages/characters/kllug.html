Kllug was a goblin shaman in the late 15 century DR.

History

In 1484 DR, during a new assault by orc and drow on the Silver Marches, Kllug and his fellow orc shaman Innanig led troops to an assault of Nesmè. However, Kllug and Innanig were deceived by reborn Regis.
