Simon Thrithyn was the innkeeper of the House of Good Sprits In the 14 century DR.

Personality

Simon was a rather calm and timid man, but a dependable fighter.

Relationships

Simon was a member of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Vintners', Distillers', & Brewers' Guild.
