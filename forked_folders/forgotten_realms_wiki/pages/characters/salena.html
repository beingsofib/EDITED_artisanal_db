Salena was a barmaid inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Salena worked in the Mug and Mutton, a tavern located near the center of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter on Zhent Avenue. Unbeknownst to her employer, Salena was an up and coming thief who honed her skills on the tavern's patrons.
