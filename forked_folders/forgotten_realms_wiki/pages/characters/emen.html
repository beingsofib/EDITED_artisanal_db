Emen was an inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Emen was a human child with a light skin tone, long brown hair, and hazel eyes.

History

In the , Emen often played ball with his friend, Conall at the Bloomridge Park. At some point during the Absolute crisis, the children were at the Park, and Conall threw Emen's ball far away, refusing to retrieve it. When a group of assassin worshippers of Bhaal attempted to slaughter the park-goers in the middle of a day to venerate their monstrous deity. It was unknown if the children survived.
