Velsinore was a senior Selûnite priestess responsible for the internal administration and accounting of Moonshadow Hall in the city of Yhaunn. She turned to be a dangerous heretic and traitor in league with the Sharran pirestess Variance Amatick. She was a follower of the Dark Moon heresy, worshipping both Selûne and Shar.
