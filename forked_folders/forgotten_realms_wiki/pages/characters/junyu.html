Junyu was one of the greatest swordsmen in Kuda in Kozakura in 1357 DR.

Activities

Junyu was once one of the most talented duelists in Kozakura, tutoring many students, but sensing a void in his life he decided to become a priest in a Konjo temple. Junyu still accepted a few pupils but now his lessons to them were harder and more philosophical. His current pupil was Miyoshi.
