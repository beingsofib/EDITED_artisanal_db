Oota was a self-styled mayor within the Lost Peaks.

Activities

Oota managed a secret shadovar village for captured Chosen, despite being herself a captive. She had dealings with the maid and Harper spy Tharra.

History

In 1486 DR, Oota agreed to meet the disguised Dahl Peredur, that offer his help to made an escape plan.
