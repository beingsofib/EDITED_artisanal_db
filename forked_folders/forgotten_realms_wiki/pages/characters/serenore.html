Serenore was a female half-sun elf who resided on the island of Alaron in the Moonshae Isles during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

She lived with her husband and daughter.

Her parents were Remallia Haventree and Arthagast Ulbrinter of Waterdeep. She had a brother named Arthius.

History

Circa the early 1470s DR, Serenore (who was a child at the time) posed for an oil painting with her family, which her mother would go on to keep with her.
