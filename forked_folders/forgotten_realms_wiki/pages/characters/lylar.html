Lylar was a member of the Bloodshields Band in the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

Lylar bore a spear.

Activities

They numbered among the Bloodshields Band members.

Relationships

Other members in the same adventuring band included Gaerond, Rornagar Breakblade, Malkym, and Flamdar.

History

Lylar was a member of the Bloodshields Band at the time they were slain in Shadowdale by an ambush of doppelgangers, one of which pretended to be Elminster Aumar, whom they were seeking.
