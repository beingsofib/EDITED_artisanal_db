Briarsting was a thorn who was tasked with guarding the tomb of a long-dead druid in Waterdeep's City of the Dead.

Relationships

The thorn was friends with and often rode a semi-sentient topiary dragon with whom he guarded the druid's tomb.

History

Around 1467–1479 DR, Briarsting helped Sophraea Carver undo the curse that Dorgar Adarbrent placed on tombs in the graveyard.
