Besides a large house, Halonder Eldeglut had widespread business interests.

Activities

Halonder Eldeglut was a glover.

Relationships

He was married to Iyrevven Eldeglut.

History

In , Halonder and Iyrevven Eldeglut were targetted for impersonation by drow invaders, who plotted to carry out the deed in a party the glover was throwing at his home. Their wine goblets were poisoned during a moment of distraction.
