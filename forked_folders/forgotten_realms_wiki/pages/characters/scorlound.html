Scorlound was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 15 century, working with the Watch of Suzail.

History

In the aftermath of the massacre at The Bold Archer in where the nobleman Seszgar Huntcrown was slain among others, and his body carried away, War Wizard Scorlound believed that one loose finger he'd found belonged to that particular nobleman, per the swordcaptain Dralkin.
