Braden Yil was a merchant retainer of House Cadorna of Phlan in 1489 DR.

Personality

Braden was a nervous but meticulous individual. He looked frequently at the sky in order to check his adherence to his time schedule. He spoke curtly and briefly and got exasperated when subjected to long speeches.

History

Braden worked as merchant for House Cadorna. In 1489 DR, after a dramatic increase of bandit attacks around the Iron Route, he hired an adventuring party to solve the matter.
