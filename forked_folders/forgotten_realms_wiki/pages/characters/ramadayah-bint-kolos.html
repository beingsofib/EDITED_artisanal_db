Imam Ramadayah bint Kolos was an ethoist priest of Hajama living in Hiyal around 1367 DR.

Activities

Ramadayah was the Imam, or high priestess, of Hajama's followers within Hiyal's mosque devoted to the god of bravery. She led the religious following of Hajama in Hiyal.
