Lady Thaulindra was a noblewoman of the mid-14 century DR. She had been the possessor of the Tiara of the Eyes.

Possessions

She was the bearer of the Tiara of the Eyes until the mid-14 century DR.

History

Hlael Toraunt, while scrying on the caravan transporting Shandril Shessair in late , commented that Krostal, a thief working for the Cult of the Dragon, had stolen the Tiara of the Eyes off of Lady Thaulindra's head some years prior in Sheirtalar, right under the noses of the Zhentarim.
