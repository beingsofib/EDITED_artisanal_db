Olma Kulenov the Bloody was a powerful priestess of Cyric that riseded within the Twin Towers of the Eternal Eclipse in Amn during the late 14 century DR.

Abilities

She was considered one of the most mighty of Cyric's clerics in all the Twin Towers, second only to the Blackwill himself.

Relationships

Olma served under Blackwill Haarken Akhmelere, head priest of the Twin Towers. While she absolutely detested the man and sought to overthrow him, Olma knew his power was too great to complete the feat alone.
