Rahvin was a mercenary who operated out of the nation of Sembia in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He was a skilled archer and was known to employ arrows of detonation in combat.

Relationships

Rahvin's mercenary companions included the ogre Gorf, two fellow warriors Carston and Haseo, the mage Shaldrissa, and a cleric named Wudei.

History

In the , Rahvin and his allies were dispatched by the Sembian Iron Throne to hunt down and kill Sarevok Anchev, who had caused the collapse of their western branch in Baldur's Gate.

Rahvin's group tracked him down to the subterranean Temple of Bhaal in the Undercity. There they encountered Abdel Adrian and his adventuring party, and attempted to claim the bounty placed upon his head.
