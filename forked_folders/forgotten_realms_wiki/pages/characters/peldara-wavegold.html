Princess Peldara Wavegold was a royal princess of the Amnian Wavegold dynasty in the 13 century DR.

Relationships

Peldara was the daughter of King Gelhinn Wavegold, and married Malek Parhek. They had several children including Derek and Rhothar.

History

At the direction of her father, Peldara married the only decent man to be found in the Amnian royal court—Malek Parhek. On the death of King Gelhinn, Malek became the King of Amn, and their children became the Parhek dynasty.
