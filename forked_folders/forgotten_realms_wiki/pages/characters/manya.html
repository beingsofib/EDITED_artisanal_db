Manya was a young lady that lived in the village of Parnast during the mid–14 century DR. She helped her family's farm by tending their geese.

Relationships

Manya's father was a farmer and her brother was soldier in the local Zhentarim garrison.

She developed a mutual infatuation with the young scoundrel Tiep, Her family had taken a liking to Tiep and had him over on several occasions.
