Cinnamon was a child from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Cinnamon was a human boy with dark skin, black hair, and dark brown eyes.

History

In the , Cinnamon spent his days playing with fellow children of the Gate, Punkins and Slow-Butt. The kids played as steel watchers and spies of the Absolute. Cinnamon was thoroughly entertained with candy smuggling and pretending that were parts of the game. When he played a spy of the Absolute, the child cheerfully proclaimed himself not suspicious.
