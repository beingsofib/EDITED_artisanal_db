Lady Keris Daunt was a human patrician from Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.

Description

By the 1490s DR, Keris Daunt was in her mid-thirties. She was distinguished by her tall stature and long blonde hair, which she wore in a braid adorned with decorative stones.

History

Lady Daunt asserted descent from one of the city's ancient noble houses and presented herself as a potential heir to Neverwinter's rule. This claim contested the authority of the Lord Protector of Neverwinter, Dagult Neverember, and positioned her as his political rival.

In the 1490s DR, Keris Daunt traveled to the town of Phandalin, where she organized a grand tourney. The event was publicly framed as a tribute to the local adventurers which later gained fame as the Champions of Neverwinter, who had rescued her cousin, Ferghul Belabranta, from Zhentarim assailants and a vampire spawn Etterine Tzavari. The festivities, open to all Phandalin residents, included various contests and culminated in a boar hunt in the nearby forest.

Unbeknownst to Keris, her own knight, Sir Bertalot Malcer, was a secret agent in Dagult Neverember's service. Malcer's mission was to assassinate Daunt and eliminate her as a political threat. His plan involved causing a fatal riding accident during the boar hunt by forcing her horse to throw her. This scheme was foiled by the intervention of the same local heroes she had come to honor, who exposed the plot and ensured her safety.
