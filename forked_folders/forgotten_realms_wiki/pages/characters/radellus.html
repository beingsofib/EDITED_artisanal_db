Radellus was a mage at some point before the turn of the 9 century DR.

Personality

By the end of his life, Radellus had wearied of Amn's intrigues.

History

At some point before the turn of the 9 century, Radellus decided to retire to a tower east of Sreve, and slew the previous owner, Justal Iroon, for it. During the battle, a local young boy of the village, Kurtal, snuck in and made out with three of Justal Iroon's spellbooks, which he then used for practice. Radellus flung Justal Iroon's smoking body the length of Sreve's main street; it was then whisked into the millpond, killing all its fish.

At some point afterwards, but still prior to the year , the same young boy, now an experienced mage, came and blasted him to ashes.
