Kepeshkmolik Ashoka was a dragonborn member of Clan Kepeshkmolik. She was the last living daughter of Kepeshkmolik Thymara in 1486 DR. She also was a direct descendant of Shasphur.

Description

Ashoka had one dark eye and one silver eye. Her scales were pale coppery-brown, and she had white plumes.

History

In 1486 DR, Ashoka requested to talk with Farideh, in order to give her the Black Axe of the Moon's Champion, and in doing so, fulfilling the promise her mother had made to Selûne.
