Jay Burn-Hand was a fire genasi proxy of the fire god known as Hastsezini, a favorite among the efreet nobility.

Description

Jay's body was pitch black, comparable to the color of coal, and had the head of a hawk. His eyes had a white-hot glow to them.

Activities

At some point Jay was instructed by Hastsezini to take up residence in the Great Conflagration and observe the creatures that inhabited that elemental plane, though some claimed that his reason for being there was of his own free will.

Rumors & Legends

Some claimed that Jay's gaze could cause any object to erupt into flames.
