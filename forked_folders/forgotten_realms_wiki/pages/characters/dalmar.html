Dalmar was a human abbot and cleric of Chauntea who led the monks of the Green Lady's Keep, a temple to Chauntea in Velprintalar, Aglarond, around 1368 DR.

Personality

The abbot enjoyed making and drinking the strong ale brewed in the abbey.
