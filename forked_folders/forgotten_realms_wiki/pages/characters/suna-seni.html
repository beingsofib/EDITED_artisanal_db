Suna Seni was an elven slaver who worked with Eldarin in the City of Coin, Athkatla in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Suna had a very pale skin tone and platinum-white hair.

Possessions

As a fighter, she relied on dexterity and wore simple leather armor. However, Suna was armed with a deadly enchanted blade – Arbane's Sword of Agility.

History

At some point before the , Suna was romantically involved with ranger Valygar Corthala, however, their eventually cut the ties due to Suna's involvement with slave trade in Amn. In the 1369 DR, Eldarin and Suna Seni worked with a cabal of slavers who operated out of slums in Athkatla. When Bhaalspawn Abdel Adrian interrupted the slaving trade, Eldarin and Suna Seni began hunting the adventurer and his companions across the City of Coin. Suna led a group of slaver warriors and mages but all were believed dead after an unsuccessful attempt to kill their mark.
