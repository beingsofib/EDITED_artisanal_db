Lieutenant Aegisfield was an inspector who served the Athkatlan Magistrate during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was dutiful in his service and genuinely did all he could to ensure citizens were safe on the streets of Athkatla.

Relationships

Aegisfield worked under Chief Inspector Brega.

History

In the , Lieutenant Aegisfield was tasked with stopping the individual behind a series of killings referred to as the skinner murders. While he did his best to protect the poor who lived in the streets at night, he was not entirely successful.
