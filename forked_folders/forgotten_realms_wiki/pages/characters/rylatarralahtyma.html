Rylatar'ralah'tyma, also known as simply Rylatar, was a red dragon dwelling in the Forest of Wyrms in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Rylatar was around 80 feet (24 meters) long. Her scales were crimson-colored and her wings were a deep indigo color.

In her human form, she was a tall, slim, and stunning woman, with fiery hair, curvaceous shape, and muscular frame. Her pale face was lean and sharp, and her eyes were dark.

Personality

Rylatar claimed to not be fond of anything,

During the journey, in her human form, she appeared to be both seductive and irritable.

Possessions

Rylatar had a silver ring in the shape of a winged dragon eating its own tail, which had the power to change her shape into the form of a human or elf.

In her human form, she was dressed in black leather and plate armor in Thayan style complete with spikes like talons, along with a black half-cape falling from her shoulder, a katana on her belt of dark reptile skin, and a gauntlet covering her left.
