Noland Tagart was a guardsman in Ravens Bluff in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a handsome half-elf with long brown luscious hair.

Relationships

He was the son of Bradlie Tagart, the Director of the Special Advisory Council Guard. His father indulged him all throughout his life into adulthood.

Personality

Noland was spoiled by his father from his early childhood and never learned to take care of himself. He grew up to be vain and pompous, but, it was said, "lacked to brains to get out of the rain". His father got him his job in the city guard and even chose what clothes Noland would wear. Still Noland would jump on any chance to appear superior, stroking his own massive ego.

Possessions

As a Special Advisory Council Guard in Ravens Bluff, he would wear a set of leather armor and wield a short sword.
