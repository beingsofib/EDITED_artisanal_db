Kveroslava Rom was a diviner and fortune teller that traveled across Amn with her family during the mid–14 century DR.

Abilities

After merely speaking to someone, Kveroslava was able to discern an aspect of their character, offer them some advice, and even possibly gleam some insight into their future. While the information she gathered was often vague it was generally quite accurate.

She offered a reading to passers by for a mere 10 gp.

Activities

Kveroslava and her family traveled with a band of Amnian merchants.

Relationships

Kveroslava was the matriarch of the Rom family and in her own words its heart. She had a partner in Mastav and traveled with two sons, Rinin and the elder Jidek.

History

In the , Kveroslava and her family were stuck outside the city of Trademeet when it was blockaded by the genie, Khan Zahraa.
