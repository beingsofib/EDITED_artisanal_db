McDonnell was the Ffolk lord of Blackstone in Callidyrr in the mid–14 century DR.

History

McDonnell served as Blackstone's ruler as of the time of Elminster's visit in the 1330s DR. He was quite active with the militia, marching with them on a daily basis. McDonnell enjoyed spending his downtime in one of Blackstone's many taverns.
