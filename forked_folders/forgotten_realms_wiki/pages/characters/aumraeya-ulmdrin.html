High and Most Beautiful Priestess Aumraeya Ulmdrin was the high priestess of the Firehair's House, a temple dedicated to Sune, in Selgaunt, Sembia, in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Circa 1358 DR, Aumraeya was the leader of Sune's faithful in Selgaunt. Her congregation within the temple consisted of 27 other priestesses and 62 followers.

On Uktar 29 of the , during the Sembian Civil War, Prince Rivalen Tanthul summoned a contingent of Sharran priests to help in the upcoming Battle of Selgaunt. However, Aumraeya, together with Ansril Ammhaddan and the city's hight priests of Deneir, Oghma, Lliira, Lathander, Tymora, and Waukeen protested with the hulorn, Thamalon Uskevren II, but he rebuffed them with accusations of cowardice. Aumraeya was the most vehement in opposing the alliance with the Church of Shar.
