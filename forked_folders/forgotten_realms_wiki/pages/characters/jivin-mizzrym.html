Jivin Mizzrym was a warlock member of the Dead Eyes, a criminal group from the Lower City that named itself for the grim trophies they collected from the enemies in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Jivin was a deep gnome male with a charcoal-black skin color, dark eyes, and wavy white hair. He wore a long brigandine coat and handled a simple dagger in battle.

Abilities

As a warlock, Jivin could sling a variety of spells, including acid splash, eldritch blast, misty step, shatter, and fear.

History

In the , the Dead Eyes set out to perform a job that would end up setting the gang against the Guild and its leader, Ninefingers. The Guild did not take competition lightly and sent thugs to take care of the Dead Eyes. The band survived and relocated to a new lair. Unbeknownst to Aideen Ni Chuirc – the gang's leader, one of her men was a traitor, informing the Guild of the lair and the hidden stash's location. The traitor bard instructed Ninefingers's men to strike at the hideout quickly and hard. It was unknown if Jivin Mizzrym and the Dead Eyes survived an attack from the Guild or other eager adventurers of Baldur's Gate.
