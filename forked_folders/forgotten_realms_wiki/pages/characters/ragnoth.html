Ragnoth was a wizard and member of the Zhentarim inhabiting Snowmantle in Daggerdale during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was a cold and methodical individual.

Description

He stood tall and slender. He almost always wore black robes.

Activities

Circa 1369 DR, Ragnoth was in command of the Zhentarim and Zhentilar occupying Snowmantle.

Possessions

He carried a wand of polymorphing and rod of alertness.

Abilities

Ragnoth memorized the following spells—alarm, change self, chill touch, detect magic, friends, light, magic missile, sleep, spider climb, wall of fog, ESP, blur, invisibility, Melf's acid arrow, mirror image, strength, wizard lock, blink, explosive runes, hold person, lightning bolt, protection from good, spectral force, wraithform, dig, hallucinatory terrain, polymorph other, wall of fire, conjure elemental, stone shape, teleport, and transmute mud to rock.
