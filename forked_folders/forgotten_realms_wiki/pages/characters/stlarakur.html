Stlarakur was a priest of Bane in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was unaccustomed to hastening anywhere.

History

Stlarakur traveled with a caravan from Scornubel to Waterdeep in the , which Shandril Shessair joined and he was tasked with retrieving her spellfire. However, while searching for cover during an ambush, he was slain by Marlel.
