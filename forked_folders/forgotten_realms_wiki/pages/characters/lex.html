Lex was a henchwoman, or perhaps pet, to the Talontar blightlord Damanda in . She was a bone-slender woman with a shock of close-cropped hair, purple like dead flowers; she carried a number of tomes, wands, and scrolls on her belt. While fighting Marrec's party, Lex was protected by a spell akin to shield, and she employed a spell that threatened to hold Marrec and paralyzed Elowen, most likely mass hold person, but she was backstabbed by Fallon before she could finish off the ranger she enspelled.

She had been the one to pen her mistress' scroll of escape.
