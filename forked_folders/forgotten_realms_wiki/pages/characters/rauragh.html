Rauragh was an orc king of Uruth Ukrypt in the mid–10 century DR.

History

In the , Rauragh attempted to amass an orc horde out of the Sword Mountains to lead them through the tunnels of the Underdark to attack Waterdeep. His plot was uncovered by the gold dragon Palarandusk, who then prevented the invasion.
