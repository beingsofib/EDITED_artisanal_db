Hinnia Fernshadow was a member of the Cormanthor Elves in the late 14 century DR.

Description

She had long brown hair and pale eyes, wore a chain shirt and carried an elaborate staff.

History

In the , Hinnia joined the Elves of Cormanthor to help reclaim Myth Drannor at their camp near the city. She spent her time coordinating different efforts in the ruins alongside Lorthas Vinewall, largely focused on Aelorin Ungive's rescue of the lost Eilistraee's Wanderers and Tarathiel Miastina's counterattack against the Red Wizards of Droic Orgul.
