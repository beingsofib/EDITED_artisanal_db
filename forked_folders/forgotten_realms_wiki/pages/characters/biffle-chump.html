Biffle Chump was a drow man, who, allegedly, was trapped in the stomach of Lolth's avatar alongside Jan Jansen, an adventurer and a turnip aficianado, who lived in Athkatla during the mid–14 century DR.

History

According to Jan Jansen's highly trustworthy story, he was swallowed by Lolth's avatar sometime before the . He was stuck in the deity's stomach for days alongside a miserable drow named Biffle Chump. Jansen claimed he had to eat Biffle Chump to survive, and he tasted like chicken.

It was likely that Biffle Chump never existed in the first place.
