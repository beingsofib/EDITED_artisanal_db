Kurolaboath Notepirroth was a drow oozemaster and druid who served Ghaunadaur. In the late 14 century DR Ghaunadaur sent him through the Portal of the Dead Goddess on a mission of unknown purpose.

Description

His body had fantastical properties similar to slime.

Personality

Kurolaboath was insane. He had a love for oozes.

Abilities

Kurolaboath could secrete a variety of types of ooze from his hands: he could create ooze that had effects identical to black pudding, green slime, yellow mold, brown mold, and gray ooze. He could strike with them in short range combat, or throw them as globs which splashed upon impact. He could also cast the spell slime wave.

He could squeeze his body into shapes that were as small as wide, and contort his facial features to hide his identity. His slimy body made him more difficult to strike in combat. He could not be harmed by poison, and could not be paralyzed, stunned, or forced into sleep. His body was immune to being polymorphed, and his mind could not be affected by magic.

He was resistant to the magic of fey creatures, and passed through nature without leaving tracks behind. He was an expert at identifying all animals, plants, and drinkable water.

As a druid, Kurolaboath could cast the spells darkseed, detect magic, scarecrow, entangle, faerie fire, obscuring mist, summon nature's ally I, soften earth and stone, summon swarm, wood shape, contagion, meld into stone, spike growth, summon nature's ally III, blight, giant vermin, summon nature's ally IV, animal growth, insect plague, and contagious touch. He could also assume animal forms through wild shape.

As a drow, he could also produce the effects of the spells dancing lights, darkness, and faerie fire.

Possessions

Kurolaboath carried an enchanted quarterstaff, a cloak of resistance, and a set of bracers of armor.

Activities

Kurolaboath used animal friendship to tame animals that he found to become his companions.

Relationships

Kurolaboath was a particularly effective servant of Ghaunadaur. Ghaunadaur shared ancient secrets with Kurolaboath.

History

In the late 14 century DR, Kurolaboath was sent through the Portal of the Dead Goddess in the underdark by Ghaunadaur. Kurolaboath travelled through it to a divine realm in an outer plane. The divine realm once belonged to a long-dead goddess who had some sort of relationship to Ghaunadaur. Kurolaboath was tasked with searching for something in this mysterious domain.

Rumors & Legends

While the true purpose of Kurolaboath's mission remained a secret, some theories had been suggested. It was proposed that Kurolaboath was searching to recover ancient magical treasure left by Ghaunadaur, and the goddess had in life been an ally to That Which Lurks.

Conversely, it was suggested that the goddess had been Ghaunadaur's foe, and she posessed a powerful artifact which had the power destroy Ghaunadaur itself and all slime. This explanation proposed that Kurolaboath was sent to find and destroy this artifact.

A third theory suggested that the goddess was actually only asleep, and Kurolaboath had been sent to vanquish or control her in some way.
