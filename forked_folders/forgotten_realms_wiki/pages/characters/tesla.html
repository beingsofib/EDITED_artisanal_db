Tesla was an individual in the town of Heldon in the early 3 century DR.

History

In the , during the razing of Heldon by Undarl Dragonrider and his steed Anglathammaroth, Prince Elminster Aumar, while escaping, took notice of an escaping fat woman, whom he took to be one of Tesla's seamstresses; this strongly implied Tesla owned some shop.
