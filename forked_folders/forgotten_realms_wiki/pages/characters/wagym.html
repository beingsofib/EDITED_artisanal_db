Wagym was a human wereboar fighter and a founder and leader of the Clenched Fist, a Rundeen-affiliated mercenary and adventuring company based in Schamedar in Calimshan in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He had a bad temper.

History

Wagym was once a gladiator slave raised in the Djen Arenas in Calimport, but escaped amidst the riots of the .

He headed to Schamedar, where he joined the Clenched Fist, then a rowdy gang of mercenaries. The wereboar had little other choice in life, as well as an unwise attraction for the seductive fellow member Crymara. The band soon grew into a full-fledged mercenary company.
