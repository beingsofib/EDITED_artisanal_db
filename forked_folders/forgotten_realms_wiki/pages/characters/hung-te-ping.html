Hung Te Ping was a poet and a great sage of the Path of Enlightenment in Kara-Tur around .

The Dragon Lord Mei Lung maintained a long friendship with Hung Te Ping, whom he consulted and conversed with often.

The Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert was believed to have been a place where Hung Te Ping stopped on one of his journeys between Kara-Tur and the Outer Planes.
