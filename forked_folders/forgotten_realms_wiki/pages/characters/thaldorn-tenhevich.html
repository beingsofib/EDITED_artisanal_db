Thaldorn Tenhevich was one of the leaders of the Baldur's Gate branch of the Iron Throne who oversaw Sword Coast operations along side Brunos Costak, under the leadership Rieltar Anchev, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Thaldorn was incidentally involved in the iron crisis that plagued the Sword Coast during the late 1360s DR.

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR, Rieltar and Brunos traveled to Candlekeep to meet with members of the Knights of the Shield. Thaldorn remained behind in the Baldurian Iron Throne headquarters as he was scheduled to meet with Emissary Tar who represented the Grand Dukes of Baldur's Gate.
