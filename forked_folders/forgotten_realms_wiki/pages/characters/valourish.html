Valourish, who was nicknamed the Younger, was a Calishite wizard inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Valourish owned the Bath House, a spa located in the western section of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter on Teshwave Street. He built the Bath House to help remind him of his warm southern home.

Possessions

His prized possession was a djinni bottle that housed a marid under his command.
