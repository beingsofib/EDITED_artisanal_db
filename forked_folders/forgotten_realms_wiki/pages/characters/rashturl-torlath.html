King Rashturl Torlath "the Watchful" was an early Amnian monarch of the Torlath dynasty who developed a heroic reputation as a general.

Relationships

Rashturl was the third son of King Corl II, and inherited the throne from him. His sole son Brinnaq I later became king himself.

History

In the , the ogre magi called the Horned Mage united the ogres of the Small Teeth and Cloud Peaks, forming an army that sacked the towns of Imnescar and Trademeet. Before they could lay siege to Esmeltaran and claim the Armory of Nedeheim, Rashturl raised an army and led it against the invaders, defeating them and sending the Horned Mage into hiding.

King Rashturl's fame was so great that his name was given to the Citadel of Rashturl in 897 DR,
