Syussa Broar was one of the Stalwart Lions, the masters of Crosswinds Keep as of .

Description

He was slim but muscular, with dark hair and a well-trimmed beard. He always dressed well, measured 5'3" and weighed .

Possessions

His bedchamber in the upper floor of Crosswinds Keep held two golden lion sculptures flanking his bed, gilt curtains, and expensive rugs. He also kept a collection of locks in the room.

Activities

He sometimes traded tales with people at Crosswinds Keep. He was most often called to entertain socially conscious visitors to the keep.

Relationships

He was a member of the Stalwart Lions.

History

Syussa joined the Stalwart Lions while fleeing from a bounty hunter from Cormyr, where he'd stolen something from a wealthy house in Arabel, earning himself a 2,000 gp bounty on his head. After a few months, the bounty hunter abandoned the chase, but Syussa remained with the Lions.
