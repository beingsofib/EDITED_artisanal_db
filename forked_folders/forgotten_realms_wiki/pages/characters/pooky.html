Pooky was an imp who served as the familiar of the mage Amon during the mid–14 century DR.

Powers

Pooky was a competent spellcaster.

Relationships

The imp liked to poke fun at Amon's companion Smaeluv Orcslicer by referring to him as "Smelly". Smaeluv in turn wanted to respond with violence.

Pooky saw Amon as a sort of paternal figure, regularly referring to him as "Papa".
