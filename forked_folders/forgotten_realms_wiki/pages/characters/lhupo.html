Lhupo was a goblin of the Cragmaw tribe of goblins, bugbears, and hobgoblins at Cragmaw Castle in Neverwinter Wood in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Though he was a priest, Lhupo had no divine power, but claimed the god Maglubiyet spoke to him. He commanded two acolytes.
