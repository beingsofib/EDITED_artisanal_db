Thurstwell Vanthampur () was the eldest son of Thalamra Vanthampur of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

Thurstwell was born to Thalamra Vanthampur and her first husband in the 1450s DR.

By the , Thurstwell commanded several imps, which he used to spy upon his brothers. Thurstwell was also a customer of the Sword Coast Couriers and relied on them to receive valuable deliveries, such as special and confidential parcel from the Zhentarim that contained an iron flask with a spectator inside it. However, the parcel never reached Baldur's Gate, the courier who carried it was slaughtered by a gang of gnolls serving The Absolute.
