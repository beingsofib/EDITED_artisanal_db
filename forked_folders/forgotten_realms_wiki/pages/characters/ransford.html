Ransford was an apprentice mage that studied under Khelben Arunsun for a time during the late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was the youngest and most excitable of Khelben's pupils at the time. From Khelben's perspective he was prone to bothering his master with topics as frivolous as three-legged frogs.

Activities

While receiving his education in the arcane arts under the Blackstaff, Ransford served him as a messenger and errand boy.

History

In the , Ransford was forced to once again disturb Khelben along with Lady Laeral Silverhand when High Lord Gervas Imesfor suddenly appeared before Blackstaff Tower fighting off a mind flayer.
