Kelki Lightfingers was a halfling warrior and rogue living in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

She was a young and impetuous woman, that once stole the necklace from a princess while said princess was wearing it. Replacing the necklace with a knotted string, the theft was unplanned and happened because Kelki thought the necklace would look better around her own neck than that of the princess.

Possessions

She wielded an enchanted short sword, while wearing a ring of protection, cloak of protection, and a pair of bracers of defense.
