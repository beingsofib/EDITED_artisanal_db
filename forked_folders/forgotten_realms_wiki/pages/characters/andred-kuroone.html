Andred Kuroone was a merchant from the city of Zazesspur in Tethyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Andred was a tall human man with thick long locks of hair and well-groomed beard and mustache.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Andred made a living my trading in thael leaf – pipeweed flavoring, herbs, spices, and dried foods and fruits, such as apricots. Some of his clients were found in Triboar and Scornubel, including Lurandural Veltree of Scornubel's merchants' guild – The Halanthor. Orstrar Galannavruk was his business associate and the main "coin mountain" behind the enterprise, who prepared quality pipeweed mixes for prospect customers, leaving negotiations to more charismatic Andred.
