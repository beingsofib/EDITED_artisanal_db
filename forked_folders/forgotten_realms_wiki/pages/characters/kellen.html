Kellen was a 10-year-old boy who in 1491 DR was kidnapped from his home in Liam's Hold by werewolves from Barovia, forced to engage in a combat to the death with other children, and subsequently infected with werewolf lycanthropy.

Activities

Kellen usually lay in a small corner of the werewolf den curled up in his wolf form along with Wensencia, a older werewolf of the werewolf pack.

Possessions

Kellen held dear to him a zombie doll with the inscription "Is no fun, Is no Blinsky!" written on it.
