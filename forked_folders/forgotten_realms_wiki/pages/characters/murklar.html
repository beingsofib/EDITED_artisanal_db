Murklar was a hunchback inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

He worked as a janitor at the Wyvern's Rest inn and tavern located in Waterdeep's Sea Ward.

Rumors & Legends

Murklar was actually an agent for the Unseen. He worked until a doppleganger named Ptola.
