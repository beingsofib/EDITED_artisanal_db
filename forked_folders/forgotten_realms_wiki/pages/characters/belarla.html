Belarla was a pleasure queen in Zhentil Keep as of the mid–14 century DR, and also a Harper.

Personality

She knew all the dirty jokes in the business.

Relationships

She was never far from Oelaerone, and she knew Mirt.

History

Belarla's work as a Harper and a pleasure queen of Zhentil Keep continued until the , when Mirt called upon her to aid Shandril Shessair after her first assault on the Citadel of the Raven. They followed her afterwards when she set out to attack the Wizards' Tower.

Myrintara later transported them to the Lonesome Tankard in Eveningstar. There, she argued in favor of Shandril striking again at the Zhents, rather that fleeing to Silverymoon. During the tussle with the forces of Fzoul Chembryl at the Wizards' Tower, Belarla was struck senseless early, taking her out of the fight. She survived the fight, however, and was healed by a kiss from Shandril and her spellfire, getting well enough to leave on her own power soon after.

Later that year, Belarla was drinking with Mirt when he and Asper were called magically to the aid of Shandril in the moments leading up to her death.
