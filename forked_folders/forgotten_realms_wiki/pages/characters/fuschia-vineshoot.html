Fuschia Vineshoot was an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate's Lower City in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Fuschia was a pale-skinned half–high elf with sharp facial features and short golden curls.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Fuschia Vineshoot frequented The Singing Lute alongside his significant other, a fellow half-elf man, Rodondo Logg. Rodondo hated the Lute whilst his darling loved the place and was clueless of Rodondo's true feelings.
