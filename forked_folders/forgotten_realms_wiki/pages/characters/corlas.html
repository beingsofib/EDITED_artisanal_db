Sir Corlas was one of Rangrim's warriors in the incursion against the Cult of the Dragon enclave in the Gray Forest, near Lyrabar on Tarsakh 12. After Rangrim died, he retreated with his men; it took Taegan Nightwind, Jivex, and several transformed elves to help them to regroup.

He wore a lady's scarf tied to his helmet.
