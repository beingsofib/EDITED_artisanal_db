Multon Weny was a magic-user in Hades in 1358 DR.

History

Around 1358, Multon went into Hades in search of his brother Drado Weny but like his brother was enslaved by the arcanaloth Yrkhetep. He was chosen to lead a unit of skeletons and a unit of zombie in Yrkhetep's army.
