Al-Nasr, which was Midani for the Vulture, was a mysterious ajami necromancer who inhabited the city of Hilm in the Pantheist League of interior Zakhara during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

Little was known about Al-Nasr save that he tutored both Ezarhaddon bin Kior and his wife Inanna az-Khunjhati, both of whom would later become the Necromancer King and Queen of the ruined city of Ysawis, in the dark arts of necromancy.
