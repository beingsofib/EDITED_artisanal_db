Gorglak was a duergar guard in Gracklstugh in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Gorglak was well-known as one of the most corrupt members of the Stone Guards. He liked to collect weird weapons.

History

Circa 1486 DR, Gorglak was at the city's main gate and, for a good price, permitted a party of adventurers to escape drow slavers of Velkynvelve and to enter the city.
