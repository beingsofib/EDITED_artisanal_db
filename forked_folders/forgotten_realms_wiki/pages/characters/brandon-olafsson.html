Brandon Olafsson was king of the Northlander kingdom of Gnarhelm in the Moonshae Isles during the mid-to-late-14 century DR.

Relationships

He was the son of King Svenyird Olafsson.

History

Brandon assumed the throne of Gnarhelm when his father died in the .

When High Queen Alicia Kendrick of the Ffolk sought to unify the Ffolk and the Northlanders of the Moonshaes in the mid-to-late-14 century DR, King Brandon threw his weight behind her. He was instrumental in persuading the Northlander kings to swear fealty to the High Queen, forming the United Moonshae Isles.
