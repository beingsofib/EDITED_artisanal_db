Slogg Ogrum was an infamous orc warrior of the Eastern Heartlands and the namesake of a powerful enchanted weapon—Ogrum's Siegebow—who lived in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Slogg Ogrum was a mysterious individual who single-handedly laid siege to the town of Priapurl in the Eastern Heartlands in the . The orc was defeated by the Mindgulph Mercenary Company, but his body was never found, nor could they find any reason for the siege, making Slogg's motivation a complete mystery. The only thing Slogg left behind was an impressive collection of weapons that included the powerful crossbow later known as Ogrum's Siegebow.
