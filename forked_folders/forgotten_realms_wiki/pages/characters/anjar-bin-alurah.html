Anjar bin Alurah was a prince of Hiyal around 1367 DR and the favored son of Sultan Alurah bint Asrah.

Personality

Anjar was intelligent and strong and obeyed his mother's wishes. His greatest fault was his lack of subtlety. Anjar always favored an extreme direct approach.

Activities

Though Anjar had not been officially appointed as the Sultana's heir, he was given the title of Grand Commander of Hiyal's army.

Relationships

Anjar was the eldest of five children.
