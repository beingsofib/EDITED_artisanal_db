Kerit Darkrider was a Sembite adventurer and member of the Gold Coins company in the late 14 century DR.

History

In the , Kerit attended King Azoun IV's birthday party at Thistleflame Keep in Suzail alongside his companion Garit Strongarm. They stayed the night at the Keep and were suspected of being involved in Lord Partic Thistle's apparent murder, but proved innocent.
