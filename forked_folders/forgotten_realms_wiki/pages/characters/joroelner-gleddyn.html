King Joroelner Gleddyn was the ruler of the isolated halfling realm of Delmyr, circa the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR.

Personality

The vast majority of his subjects loved him.

Relationships

He had a wife, Queen Samrasta, and three daughters. The eldest was Barasra, the middle child was Tchalaedra, and the youngest was Esmrela.

Joroelner's rival and main critic was the baron of the Mazron family, Guldin "Strombrow" Mazron.

History

King Joroelner succeeded his father and was the second Gleddyn to sit on the throne after nearly a millennia of rule by the Parradyne family.

Rumors & Legends

Gossips could not resist speculating about a long-lost member of the Parradynes returning to take the throne back from the Gleddyns.
