Zaroud al'Ryshal was a member of the Knights of the Shield, an organization that operated throughout the Sword Coast. He lived in that region of Faerûn during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Zaroud's superior was a cambion by the name of Kaddrus.

History

In the , Zaroud and two of his fellow Knights, Nulra Blacksaddle and Falar, traveled with Kaddrus on a mission to recover the Shield of the Hidden Lord, an item of great importance to their organization.
