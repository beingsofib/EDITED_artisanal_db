Mamoon Klesir was a Zakharan inhabitant of Huzuz, the City of Delights, in .

Activities

Mamoon lived within Huzuz's Craft District in the North Quarter. He ran the prestigious school simply called Mamoon Klesir's University that only admitted Huzuz's nobility for students.
