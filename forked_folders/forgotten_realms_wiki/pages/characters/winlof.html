Winlof was a doomguide who lived in the city of Neverwinter during the late 15 century DR.

History

At some point around the , Winlof was assigned to the post at Neverdeath Graveyard. That year, she and some of her fellows became lost in the Pauper's Field area of the graveyard after being assaulted by the undead and their Red Wizard masters.

She was taken to a the tomb of an unnamed wizard and left in a fearful state of despair. Winlof was the first to be rescued by the adventurer working a senior member of her order Doomguide Volahk.
