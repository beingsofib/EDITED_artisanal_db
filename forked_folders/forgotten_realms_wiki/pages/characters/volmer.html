Lord Volmer was a bone devil from the Nine Hells that ventured to Toril on the Prime Material plane during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

He led a delegation of some seven devil-worshiping cultists.

History

Some time during the 1480s DR, Lord Volmer traveled to Faerûn to intervene against the Cult of the Dragon and their attempts to summon Tiamat from her domain on Avernus. He sought to manipulate the adventurers investigating the cult's activities, by sabotaging their allies in the Red Wizards led by Rath Modar. Volmer was well aware that Thay's ruler Szass Tam would offer a boon for anyone that stood in the way of the Thayan Resurrection movement.
