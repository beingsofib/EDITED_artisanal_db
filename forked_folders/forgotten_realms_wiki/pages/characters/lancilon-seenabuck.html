Lancilon Seenabuck was an adventurer in Maztica.

Personality

Lancilon was totally confused by Maztican customs. He desperately to wanted go home.

Possessions

While in Maztica, Lancilon dressed in breeches, a cap, a good cloth cloak, a tunic, and a vest. They also wore a backpack in which they carried thieves' tools, paper, and ink.

In terms of weaponry, Lancilon possessed a short sword, a shortbow with twenty arrows, and a starwheel pistol with enough smokepowder for six shots.

History

Lancilon was a thief until he tried to steal arquebuses from the temple of Gond in Lantan but was discovered. In order to escape, he hid aboard a ship, not knowing it was sailing to Helmsport. Lancilon found himself in a strange new world where he knew nothing. He needed gold to go back home. Having heard about Nexal, he thought it was a good place where he could find gold.
