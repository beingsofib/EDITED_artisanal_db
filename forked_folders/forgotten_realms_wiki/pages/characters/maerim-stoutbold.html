Maerim Stoutbold was a merchant who ran a shipbuilding business in Cormyr in the 14 century DR.

History

Maerim Stoutbold was the father of Maerun Stoutbold, who later inherited the family business and gained ill repute. Maerim ran Stoutbold Marina, which was located in the central southern part of Suzail, capital of Cormyr. A larger ship-building facility of the family business was located in Marsember.
