Meriba Rahabahne was a travelling human beast-rider from the lands near Anauroch, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Possessions

Mounted on her stallion, she wore leather armor and wielded a spear, shield, and enchanted whip. The whip would force animals struck to either cower or flee unless they resisted its power.

History

She grew up in the wild lands of the Anauroch desert, and bonded with a stallion. At some point a cleric tried to take the horse from her for his own, leading her to flee her homeland. Eversinces that day she wandered the lands, riding her bonded horse.
