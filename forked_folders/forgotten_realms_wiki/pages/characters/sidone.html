Sidone was a druid of Chauntea in Stag Falls during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Abilities

She was a druid.

Possessions

She had a ring of spell storing, with several dispel magic spells on it.

Activities

Sidone was a Chauntean priest in Stag Falls.

History

After the vampire wizard Romanisk Vlaensk turned Baron Vilhelm von Stag Falls into his undead thrall, and in turn the Baron began to terrorize the town with his charmed retinue, Sidone led the population of Stag Falls to nearby caves, where they hid and took refuge. Her fellow priest, a Tormite called Gunther, did not care to join them, and made a last stand; she feared the worst for him, and correctly so.

Help finally arrived as some adventurers were sent from Ravens Bluff to investigate, as the taxes from Stag Falls were late. Welcome in Sidone's camp at the nearby caves after aiding Tommy Fletcher, a shepherd boy of the same town, the adventurers spent a night resting; on that night, Sidone searched the neighboring region for two youths, Kitarno and Lyra, without much success. This caused her to miss the birth of the child of Jurgen and Marimir, two fellow Stag Falls villagers, whose baby had to be midwifed by the same adventurers until Sidone arrived. Before these adventurers finally headed for the castle, she gave them a ring of spell storing and goodberries.

After the destruction of Romanisk Vlaensk, Sidone was the person with the most support to take over Stag Falls, but she was not interested in leadership positions and would only put up with those temporarily.
