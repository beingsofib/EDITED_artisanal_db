Chillclaw was a gelugon summoned by Exeter from a magic scroll.

Personality

When Chillclaw was summoned, Exeter was not powerful enough to control the gelugon, yet Chillclaw went along with Exeter's plan as he was promised to duel and slay Ada the Unbending, a high priestess of Tyr.

Abilities

Like many other baatezu, he could gate in other devils. However, during his duel with Ada, he refrained from doing so, upholding the rules of the duel.

History

During the events of Exeter's vengeance upon Wemick and the Nellie Thursday Home for Experienced Adventurers retirement home, Chillclaw would duel Ada the Unbending, firstly preventing her from fleeing, and then attempting to slay her.
