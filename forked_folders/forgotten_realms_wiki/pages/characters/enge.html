Enge was a fruit merchant from the City of Coin, Athkatla, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Galoomp was a portly man who spoke with a heavy and hard accent.

History

Enge's Shop could be found in the grand market in Athkatla's Center District – Waukeen's Promenade in the . The entirety of Enge's little shop was covered in displays and baskets of fruits of all varieties. Despite his busy green-grocer business, Enge also sold a humble selection of arms, armor, and simple ''healing potions'.
