Jherek was an agent for the Harpers in the mid–14 century DR. He helped in stopping Eldrith the Betrayer, Mordoc SeLanmere, and the Zhentarim (under leadership of Karne) from gaining control of the Onyx Tower in the .

Activities

Jherek was in charge of the Harper activity in Baldur's Gate, the Western Heartlands, and along the Sword Coast.
