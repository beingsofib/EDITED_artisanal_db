Xandra Shobalar was a drow of House Shobalar and the third daughter of Hinkutes'nat Alar Shobalar. She was the house's Mistress of Magic, tasked with training others in the house about wizardry. She trained the young Liriel Baenre.

Relatives

Xandra's mother was Hinkutes'nat Alar Shobalar and she had ten sisters. Five of these sisters were wizards.

History

Xandra became deeply envious of Liriel's magical ability and plotted to kill her, travelling to Mantol-Derith in order to buy an enslaved wizard, Tresk Mulander, to pit against her in the Blooding.
