Gardolf Beorunna was the Thane of Ruathym and was known for being the father of Uthgar. Beorunna had other children, including Morgur, for whom Morgur's Mound was named.

Gardolf met his end fighting a demon in the Druarwood, where it was said in the final moments of the battle, the ground opened beneath their feet, plunging the two of them to their deaths. In his honor, the area was named Beorunna's Well.
