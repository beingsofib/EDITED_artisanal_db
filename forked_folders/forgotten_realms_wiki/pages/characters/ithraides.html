Ithraides was a moon elf wizard and the High Spellstar of Arcorar who inscribed three telkiira, the first of which came into the possession of Araevin Teshurr. His subjects Kaeledhin, Morthil, and Sanathar each took one of the telkiira. After his death and journey to Arvandor he left most of his tomes and treasures to the star elf Morthil.
