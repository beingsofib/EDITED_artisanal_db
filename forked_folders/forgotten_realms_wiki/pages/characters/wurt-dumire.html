Wurt Dumire was a tyrant ruling the goblin realm of the Kingdom of Nix in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

In the aftermath of Redfang the Reaper's reign, Wurt Dumire came to enslave the goblins of Nix, putting them to work in a far-off mine. The leader of the Legendary Campaign felt unexpected sympathy for the goblins' plight and was compelled to try to bring an end to Wurt's operation in a bloodforge battle in Nix itself.
