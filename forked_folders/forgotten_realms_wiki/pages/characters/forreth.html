Gish For'reth was a githyanki warrior who served under Kith'rak Voss as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

He wore a suit of githyanki half plate armor adorned with a red half-cape.

History

In the Faerûnian , For'reth joined his squad on the mission to Toril to capture a gith relic referred to by the locals as the mysterious artifact.
