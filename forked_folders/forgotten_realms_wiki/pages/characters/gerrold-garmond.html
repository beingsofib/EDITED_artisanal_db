Garrold Garmond was a trader from Procampur in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

His route took him once a year to meet with Stag Falls traders in Eaglton, where he got quartz and furs he sold at Ravens Bluff and Tantras for a nifty profit.

Relationships

He and his brother Ben Garmond worked together.

History

After Romanisk Vlaensk, a vampire wizard, was released at Stag Falls and took control of the village one year around the , no Stag Falls traders headed for Eaglton. When Garrold and his brother Ben reached Eaglton, they found no traders, and rather than go to Stag Falls, they preferred to wait there. Their patience ran out after two weeks, on the day when investigators from Ravens Bluff, hired by Marcus Rubicon, arrived headed for Stag Falls. If questioned, the brothers may have said they believed there had been no traders coming because of the muddy ground.
