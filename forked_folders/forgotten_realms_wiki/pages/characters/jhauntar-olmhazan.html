Jhauntar Olmhazan was a jeweler inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Jhauntar was the spokesman for the Jewelers' Guild circa the . He held the title of "Gentleman Speaker for the Jewelers" within the guild and worked out of the House of Gems—the guild headquarters located on Gem Street in the Castle Ward. Jhauntar also owned and operated Olmhazan's Jewels.
