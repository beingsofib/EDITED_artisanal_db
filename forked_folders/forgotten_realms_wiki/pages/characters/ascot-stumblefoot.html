Ascot Stumblefoot was a halfling shopkeeper inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the late 14 century DR.

Personality

Ascot was polite and well-spoken.

Activities

He owned the Spice of Life, a well-stocked spice and herb shop located in the Foreign Quarter near the River Trail Gate. He imported spices from all across Toril including Kozakura, the Moonshae Isles, Maztica, and Zakhara.

He initially decided to settle in the area because of its close proximity to his favorite vintage of wine and tobacco. Ascot sacrificed much during the destruction of Zhentil Keep in the , by cutting down to just three meals a day.
