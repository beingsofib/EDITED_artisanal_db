King Rhothar Parhek was the third Amnian monarch of the Parhek dynasty.

Relationships

Rhothar was the third son of King Malek Parhek and brother of King Derek. His only heir was Dhanar.

History

During his reign, the realm began to suffer from the intrigues of powerful merchant families as they challenged the royal authority.
