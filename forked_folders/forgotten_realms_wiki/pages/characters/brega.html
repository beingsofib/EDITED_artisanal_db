Brega was the chief inspector for the Athkatlan government circa the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was a no-nonsense individual who was very much dedicated to his service of the city.

Relationships

Among the chief inspectors subordinates was one Lieutenant Aegisfield. Brega believed him to be a good and fair-minded man.

History

As of the , Brega was overseeing the investigation of the skinner murders, a series of killings that saw citizens of Athkatla being murdered and having their skin removed.

At that time he was also leading the local manhunt for the nefarious murderer Neb.
