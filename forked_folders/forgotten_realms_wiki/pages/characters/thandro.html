Thandro was a noble of Marsember in the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

He was a participant in the Rightful Conspiracy. When discussing Alusair Obarskyr, he treated her with great disdain.

History

After the War Wizards' raid on the Rightful Conspiracy in Marsember in the , one of the people still involved with it was Thandro. With a fellow conspirator in an apparently empty warehouse, sounding rather amused, he described the place and time for an upcoming ambush at Thundarlyn Hall for Princess Alusair and Queen Filfaeril Obarskyr and expected himself to join it. Unknown to him, he was eavesdropped on by Malakar Surth and Aumun Tholant Bezrar.
