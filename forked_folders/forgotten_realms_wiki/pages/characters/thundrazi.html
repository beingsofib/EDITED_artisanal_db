Thundrazi () was a Red Wizard of Thay and a Harper spy in Mulmaster.

Personality

Thundrazi usually spoke only in brief and curt sentences. She had a soft side for the young of all species. She tried to maintain her cover, but in case of danger to children, she did not care if she was exposed.

Relationships

Thundrazi worked with the Hawks, the secret police of Mulmaster, and infiltrated the local embassy of Thay.

History

In 1491 DR, she was at Astrid Saj's estate during an uprising by Elemental Evil cultists, so Mistress Saj provided her with an adventuring party as an escort to return to the embassy.
