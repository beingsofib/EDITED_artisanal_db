Tharammas was a red wizard, infamous for the strength of his nightmare spell.

Abilities

Tharammas was known by the Simbul to have perfected a stronger version of the nightmare spell.

History

In , the Simbul sought him out to kill him.

Rumors & Legends

In , The Simbul woke up from a nightmare and found herself wounded. She assumed it was a spell of nightmares from Tharammas.
