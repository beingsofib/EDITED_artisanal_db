Nata was the most powerful warrior of the Mtair Dhafir tribe circa the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Nata was a well-built man who moved with purpose and resolve. He was a no-nonsense individual that regularly wore a dour expression upon his face.

Personality

Nata was a man that believed in honoring any pacts that he or his people made, regardless of possible dangers.

Relationships

He had at least one son, who was also a warrior of the tribe.

History

Nata was present at the negotiations conducted by Sheikh Sabkhat and Zarud of the Zhentarim, a meeting that occurred in the .

When the Zhentarim agent was slain that very night, Nata and his son caught the sorceress Ruhaa attempting to flee the Mtairi camp.
