Serdel was a soldier of Dawnfire during the Dawn Age around the year .

Description

He was grizzled and stocky.

Abilities

He was a man-at-arms.

Activities

Serdel was a soldier of Dawnfire, serving under King Orchtrien as a second-in-command under Rhespen Ash.

History

Serdel served under Rhespen Ash during a winter incursion in , in which the elven commander was sent to raid territories of neighboring green dragons by the gold dragon king Orchtrien. One night in the incursion, Serdel was among the troops with Rhespen when a green dragon attacked the elf's forces. Rhespen's magic and planning allowed them to kill the dragon; Serdel congratulated his commander as a dragonslayer after the fact.
