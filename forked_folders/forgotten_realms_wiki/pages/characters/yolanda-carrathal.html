Yolanda Carrathal was High Queen of Callidyrr in the mid-to-late 11 century DR. Like her predecessors, although she held the title of High Queen of the Moonshae Isles, she was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

She was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty and grandniece to King Alec III.

She and her husband, Bertrand, had multiple children. Their third son would go on to rule as Edwin I.

History

Yolanda assumed the throne in the , following the overthrow of her predecessor, King Bayle Carrathal.

Her reign lasted for thirty-seven years before she died of illness in the . She was succeeded by her husband, who ruled for six years before he was in turn succeeded by their son, Edwin I.
