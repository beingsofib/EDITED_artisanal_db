Galentha was a drow merchant who owned a goods store, called Galentha's Goods, in the western region of the Menzoberranzan bazaar.

History

Galentha may have had some valuable information and trade for some adventurers, if they entered his store. He offered to trade his music box and a pair of magical candles for the base of the spider helm the companions received from the tavern keeper in Icewind Dale.
