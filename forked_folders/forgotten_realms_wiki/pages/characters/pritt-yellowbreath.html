Pritt Yellowbreath was an avid puzzle aficionado and a devoted reader of the Baldur's Mouth Gazette from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Pritt Yellowbreath was a golden-scaled dragonborn woman with eyes of orange flames. She was dressed in quality finery.

History

By the , Pritt became a loyal reader of the Baldur's Mouth Gazette and a connoisseur of its puzzle section. When the broadsheet began publishing the overwhelming number of articles in support of the new lord – Enver Gortash and updates on the raging Absolute crisis, Pritt's beloved puzzles had to be dropped. She spent her days walking around the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, ranting about the failing new direction of the Gazette.
