Athis Al-Din was the fiance of Sithal Al-Husn. After Habrauk Al-Nirin kidnapped the princess, he led his troops in numerous attacks against the Al-Alisk clan; he continued to lead the charge after his death.

History

The Sultan of Pazar married Sithal Al-Husn to Athis Al-Din, but the princess was stolen on her wedding night by Habrauk Al-Nirin. Athis Al-Din's family entered into a blood feud with the Al-Alisk clan, to which Habrauk belonged.

The blood feud would continue to play out nightly until , when the Princess' spirit was finally released by adventurers after Martek's crystal minaret. Athis Al-Din joined afterwards his beloved in heaven westward.
