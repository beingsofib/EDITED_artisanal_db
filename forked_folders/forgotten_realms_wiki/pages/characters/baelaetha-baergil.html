Baelaetha Baergil (? – 1337 DR) was a farmer's wife who lived near to Ashabenford, Mistledale. She was the mother of famous Captain Darrask Baergil of the Riders of Mistledale.

Relatives

Baelaetha was married to Angram Baergil, and they had seven children: three boys and four girls. Her first two sons were Athror Baergil and Captain Darrask Baergil.

Death

Baelaetha died in 1337 DR, from heartstop.
