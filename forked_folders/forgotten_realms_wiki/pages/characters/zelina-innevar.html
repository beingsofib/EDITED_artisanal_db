Zelina Innevar was the name assumed by a doppelganger that posed as one of two sisters that traveled the Sword Coast in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

"Zelina" and her sister "Arietta" would pretend to be innocent travelers looking for company while traveling the roads of the Sword Coast. After gaining some level of trust with their companions, they would attempt to lure a single individual away from their group at night and attack them to some unknown end.

History

As of the 1480s DR, "Zelina" and her sister could be found traveling north along the Trade Way.
