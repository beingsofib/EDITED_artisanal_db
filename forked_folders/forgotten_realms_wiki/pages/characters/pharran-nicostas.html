Pharran Nicostas was a drow wight in Blingdenstone.

History

Pharran was a drow warrior who went to Blingdestone. He refused to go home afterwards and somehow after his death became a wight. He remained sitting on the throne of the city's kings until 1479 DR, when an adventuring party hired by Kargien Dissengulp defeated him and his skeleton minions.

Personality

Pharran had in life the ambition to become the conqueror of Blingdenstone and this ambition sustained him even after death.
