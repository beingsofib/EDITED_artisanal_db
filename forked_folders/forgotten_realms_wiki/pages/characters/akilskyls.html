Akilskyls was a pseudodragon of Sembia in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

The Sembian pseudodragon Aeristhax called itself its heir apparent in late Mirtul of the , all but saying that Akilskyls was alive, and that it had spawned other pseudodragons.

History

Its spawn, Aeristhax, was captured by Calishite trappers in Sembia in the year 1372 DR, strongly implying the creature dwelt in Sembia as well.

Reputation

To its heir apparent, Akilskyls was a "Wyrm of Renown".
