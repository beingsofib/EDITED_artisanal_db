Harrafe Orlimbur was a resident of Hultail in Cormyr, active in the late 15 century DR.

History

By the late 15 century DR, Harrafe resided in a red-roofed barn with the spire on Easting Lane – a street on Hultail's southeastern edge that stretched from southwest to northeast. Harrafe's barn stood across the street from seamstress Avarelle Myntdown's home.
