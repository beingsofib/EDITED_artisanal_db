Daidoji Tadako was a samurai that run a posthouse in Kozakura in 1357 DR.

Activities

in a post house very near to Tamanokuni he led three bushi. in truth Daidoji, a old man, was put there as reward for his hard years to live quietly. Tadako know all kind of stories and legends of Miyama

Relationship

Sometimes Tadako was visited by his son Daidoji Kiyomoto.
