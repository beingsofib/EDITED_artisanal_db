Malco was the personal servant of Sozarro in 1362 DR.

History

Malco was a Payit who believed Maztica conquered by the Amnians in 1361 DR and offered his services to them. He became the servant of the legionary Sozarro. Malco escaped with his master from Helmsport but did not know the whole truth about him.

Personality

Malco was so impressed by the magic and might showed by the Golden Legion that he believed the conquest inevitable. He believed that Sozarro had the potential for true greatness. He looked the other way whenever Sozarro acted illegally.
