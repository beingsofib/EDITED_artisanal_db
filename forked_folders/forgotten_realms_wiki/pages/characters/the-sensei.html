The Sensei—the word meant "master" in Wa-an—was the leader of the Hijo clan of ninja in Nakamaru around 1357 DR.

Activities

The Sensei always had his face covered in public. He rarely left the hidden village, preferring to manage all business from there. He was a master of elemental magic centered around earth.

Personality

The Sensei was a brilliant and clever strategist, despite lacking physical prowess.
