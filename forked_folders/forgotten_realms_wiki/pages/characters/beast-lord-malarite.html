Beast Lord (Malarite)

Beast Lords were rare individuals who served Malar, knowingly or not, by creating unnatural monsters.

Culture

Beast Lords were wizards who used their arcane powers to breed unnatural monsters such as owlbears, bulettes, and perytons. Only a handful of Beast Lords served Malar, and most of those were human. It was rumored that several beholder and mind flayer Beast Lords existed as well.
