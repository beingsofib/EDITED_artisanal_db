Ledroc

Person
nameLedroc
occupationWagon driver

Ledroc was a caravan driver in Aglarond as of , alongside Khuldam and Corthandu. They joined Quent's caravan from at least Telflamm to Emmech. They refused a cup of Long Jing tea from Raidon Kane, preferring to share a flask of something that smelled much stronger than tea.
