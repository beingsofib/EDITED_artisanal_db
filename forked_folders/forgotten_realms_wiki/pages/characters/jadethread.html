Jadethread was a human thief who specialized in procuring difficult treasure.

Description

Jadethread was a tan-skinned man with shoulder-length brown hair and blue eyes. He was dressed in all black, and his identity was hidden by a black domino mask.

Personality

Even though he was a successful thief, Jadethread's inclination to spend his riches on parties and women kept him from amassing a fortune.

Possessions

Jadethread owned a rope of climbing.

Activities

When preying on noblemen, Jadethread's signature was leaving a single strand of green thread in place of their valuables.
