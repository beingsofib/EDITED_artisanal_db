Odum

Person
nameOdum
occupationPriest, healer

Odum was a great physician-priest of the Realms. Odum stated something to the effect that what ailed one part of the body might originate in another part.

In 1367 DR, Benelaius used this as justification for asking Mayor Tobald to remove his clothes when examining his big toe for gout.
