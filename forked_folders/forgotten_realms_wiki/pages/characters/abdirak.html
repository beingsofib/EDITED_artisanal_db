Abdirak was a priest of Loviatar, the goddess of pain, in the Sword Coast region of the Western Heartlands in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Abdirak was a human man with fair skin, gray eyes, and silver hair fashioned into a short undercut. His body was riddled with numerous scars, almost certainly a result of his sadomasochistic activities as a worshiper of Loviatar. He could be seen wearing a short, black, high-collared capelet. The garment draped threads across his chest in the shape of the holy symbol of Loviatar: a barbed, nine-stranded whip.

Personality

Abdirak deeply enjoyed pain, both to suffer it and to inflict it. However, his sadomasochism was an act of worship to Loviatar and he did not apply it recklessly. In his words: "Pain without purpose is a terrible thing."

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR, Abdirak was invited by the cult of the Absolute to assist in torturing the prisoner Liam for information. He claimed that he was "thrilled" to accept. In the goblin camp at the Shattered Sanctum, he worked with the torturer Spike under the leadership of the drow True Soul Minthara Baenre. However, he found the goblins to be poor students of his craft, describing their methods as "crude and primitive."

When meeting an adventurer at the Sanctum, he offered them peace and serenity through pain. If accepted and pleased with their performance, he gave them Loviatar's blessing.

Possessions

Abdirak wielded the Loviatar's Scourge mace, a ritual dagger, and a ritual axe. He also owned a scroll of hold person and the book titled On Receiving Her Grace, which describes the practices of Loviatar.
