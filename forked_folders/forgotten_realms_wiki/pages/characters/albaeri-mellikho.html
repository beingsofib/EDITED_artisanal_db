Albaeri Mellikho was a shopkeeper in Red Larch and a member of the Believers during the Elemental Evil crisis in 1491 DR.

Personality

Albaeri was a pot-bellied and jovial woman. She was recently worried about the strange activity of the moving stones in the hidden chambers below Red Larch, but she was reluctant to share her concerns with strangers.

Activities

Albaeri ran a stoneworks shop in the village.

History

In 1491 DR, Larrakh of the Cult of Black Earth used Albaeri's shop as a meeting place with the Believers and she help scare her own stonecutters. Later, under Larrakh's secret suggestions, Albaeri, along with Ilmeth Waelvur and Marlandro Gaelkur, killed some people in order to protect the secret of the Believers. Discovered her crimes by some adventurers she until the end professed her innocence
