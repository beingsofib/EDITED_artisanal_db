Seriadne was a Simbarch of Aglarond in the late 15 century DR. She was secretly a malaugrym who sought to advance her own sinister plans.

Description

She took the form of an elf to disguise her true nature.

History

As of the , Seriadne was one of the fifteen members of the Simbarch Council who ruled Aglarond, making her likely the most influential of the malaugrym active in Faerûn at that time.
