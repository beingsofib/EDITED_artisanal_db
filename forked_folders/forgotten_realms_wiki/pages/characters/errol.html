Errol was a human beggar from the City Core of Neverwinter in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Errol was a human man with brown hair and eyes. He had a light skin tone and wore beggar's rags.

Activities

Like so many inhabitants of Neverwinter, Clancy was harried by the Wailing Death plague that ravaged the city in the . Errol was forced to beg to survive after losing his home in the strife. Despite that, he was helpful to adventurers and was available to give directions and advice for the city's newcomers.
