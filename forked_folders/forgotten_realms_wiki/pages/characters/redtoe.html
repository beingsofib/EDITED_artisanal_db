Redtoe was a verbeeg guard in the Temple of the Forgotten God within the Spine of the World.

Description

The brown-haired giant fought with a halberd and shield.

Personality

Fearful for his own skin, the verbeeg knew when to fight and when to flee.

History

In Eleint, 1281 DR, Redtoe fled the temple to the Forgotten God after a Talonite assassin poisoned members in search of the Heartstone Gem. To avoid his own destruction, Redtoe pretended to be dead then escaped from the temple.
