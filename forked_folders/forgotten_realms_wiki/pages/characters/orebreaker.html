Orebreaker was a dwarven smith in Freedale during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was old, and the oldest person in Freedale at the time.

Personality

Orebreaker was kindly.

Abilities

He was one of the best blacksmiths in the Dalelands.

Activities

Orebreaker made high-quality armor pieces and weaponry that were sold through the the general store, and sometimes customized equipment.

Relationships

He was looking for an apprentice.
