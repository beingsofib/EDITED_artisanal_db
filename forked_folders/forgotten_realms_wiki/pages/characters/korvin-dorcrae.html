Korvin Dor'crae was a male vampire and former lover of Dahlia Sin'felle.

History

Dor'crae was responsible for turning Thibbledorf Pwent into a vampire.

He died in Gauntlgrym in the , when an elemental water flow crashed into him as it rushed towards the fiery primordial Maegera.
