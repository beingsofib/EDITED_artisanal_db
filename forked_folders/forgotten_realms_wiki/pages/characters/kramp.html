Kramp was a warrior who fought for the Absolute during the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

Kramp wore a crudely-made suit of metal armor that vaguely resembled goblin plate mail. He preferred to wield a goblin-sized greataxe in battle.

History

As of the , Kramp was among the Absolute's fervent warriors who remained poised to raid the Emerald Grove, once directed by True Soul Minthara Baenre.
