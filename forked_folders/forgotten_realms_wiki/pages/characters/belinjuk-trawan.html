Belinjuk Trawan, also known as Old Juk, was an inhabitant of the Dalelands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a portly, balding man.

Activities

Once a farmer, he later simply raised chickens. At that time, he also became a regular at the Old Skull Inn.

Relationships

He was married.

History

Once a farmer from Voonlar, he'd moved to Shadowdale to raise chickens instead by . On that year, he was getting a little handsy with Storm Silverhand.
