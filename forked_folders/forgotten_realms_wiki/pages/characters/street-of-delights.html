Street of Delights

The Street of Delights was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Street of Delights was located in the Common Quarter in northern Zhentil Keep. The street travelled east to west roughly parallel to the wall separating the Inner Quarter from the Common Quarter. The Street of Delight originated on Wizards' Way in the west and terminated on Electrum Avenue in the east.

Notable Locations

The Cloven Ogre, which was a tavern popular amoung the Zhentilar, was located on the Street of Delights.
