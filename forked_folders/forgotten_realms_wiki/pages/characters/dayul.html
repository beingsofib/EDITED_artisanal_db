Dayul was one of the Blades of the Old Man as of . On the night of Tarsakh 25, they became aware of an intruder in Sentinelspire. While they were looking out for the intruder, they came across Lewan and Ulaan. On the orders of Weilus, they tried to get them to return to their room; however, this led to a fight. Dayul's wrist was broken by a hammer blow from Lewan; after he was disabled, it was only Ulaan's intervention that kept Dayul from taking revenge on the young man. He was slain by swarming killoren shortly after.
