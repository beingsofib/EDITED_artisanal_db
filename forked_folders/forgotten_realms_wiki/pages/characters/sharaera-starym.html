Sharaera Starym was an inhabitant of Myth Drannor of the 3 century DR.

Description

Her age was only betrayed by the streaks of white and gray at her temples. During the moonrise after the laying of the Mythal, in , she wore jeweled slippers, a low-cut bejeweled gown, and diamonds in the shape of twin falling dragons on her breast.

History

In , after contemplating the remains of Uldreiyn Starym, she vowed vengeance upon Elminster Aumar and the destruction of the Mythal.
