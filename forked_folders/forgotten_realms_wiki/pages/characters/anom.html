Anom was an elf, and a Nentyar hunter in the service of the Nentyarch in . He dressed all in brown cloth, and carried a staff of dark wood. Though the Nentyarch had assigned him to accompany Marrec and Elowen's party in their quest to confront the Rotting Man, Fallon's betrayal forced the party to depart posthaste, without him.
