Hala Myrt was a Tethyrian human who frequented the Grinning Lion tavern in the North Ward of Waterdeep, working for Orlpar Husteem. She found customers for Orlpar and set up meetings. She was arrested by the City Watch on many occasions but was never charged due to the actions of Orlpar's agents, who were responsible for watching her movements while she was carrying Orlpar's money or illegal substances. Hala consumed dreammist, a highly addictive drug, on a regular basis.

Description

In 1372 DR, Hala looked older than her thirty years, with sunken eyes, flat brown hair, and aged skin. She usually wore a stained and threadbare brown frock. She spoke with a soft voice, her smile was usually strained, and she giggled for no discernible reason. In the , she was a fixture in the Grinning Lion, usually sitting on a bar stool in her guise as a grumpy, overweight, male ne'er-do-well.

Activities

Hala acted as a go-between and delivery person for Lord Orlpar. She was constantly watched by other agents in Orlpar's employ whenever she was in possession of drugs or money that belonged to him. It was well-known that she was under protection and nobody bothered her except the Watch. Whenever she was detained, her protectors stepped in to provide alibis and bail.
