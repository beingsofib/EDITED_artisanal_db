Old Meg was a sorceress who lived in the village of Eveningstar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Old Meg lived alone in a hut situated between the Redhand Pool and the farming fields belonging to the House of the Morning. Because of her magic and lifestyle, many natives of Eveningstar believed her to be a hag. Old Meg's was avoided and fell into a state of disrepair after her death. Her spellbook remained buried under one of the blackened flagstones of the hut.
