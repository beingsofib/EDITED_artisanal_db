Lord Skarmaen Alibakkar was a rather unpleasant noble of House Alibakkar who lived in the city of Trademeet during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Skarmaen was well-known for being both selfish and indolent.

Relationships

He was the third brother of Meisarch Erlranther Alibakkar.

History

In the , Skarmaen sought out adventurers to help him in his family's feud against Lady Lilith of House Lurraxol. He wanted them to retrieve the mantle of Waukeen so that he could prove his family was involved with Trademeet's founding.
