Relonor Vrinn was a wizard retainer of House Mizzrym in Menzoberranzan, and a practitioner of illusion magic.

History

In 1372 DR, Relonor accompanied Greyanna Mizzrym in hunting for Greyanna's brother and the Master of Sorcere, Pharaun Mizzrym.
