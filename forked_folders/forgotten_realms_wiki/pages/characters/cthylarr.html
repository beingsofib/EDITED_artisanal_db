Cthylarr, also known as Cthylarr the Illithilich, was an undead mind flayer from Cyrog as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

Cthylarr was an illithilich of epic power.

History

When Orcus arrived in Cyrog in the , Cthylarr ended up serving him. The illithilich travelled with its patron to Castle Never, where it sought the phylactery of the dracolich Azharzel.
