Erdan was a priest of Amaunator in the Abbey of the Rose in Sembia.

History

In 1450 DR, Erdan and Derreg welcomed Varra and helped her to give birth to Vasen Cale, despite being suspicious about the child's heritage.

Relationships

Erdan was a good friend of Derreg.
