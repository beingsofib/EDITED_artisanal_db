Tempus Blackthorne was a human wizard of Zhentil Keep and worshiper of Bane in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Tempus was a powerful wizard of Zhentil Keep and a member of the church of Bane. He was fiercely devoted to his god, aiding him in all matters of mortal life.

Tempus was a childhood friend of Ronglath Knightsbridge and tried to help his friend.

History

In the , Tempus died trying to perform a spell under the orders of his god but the instability of the magic during the Time of Troubles killed him. Strangely for him, Bane fondly recalled the mage after his demise.
