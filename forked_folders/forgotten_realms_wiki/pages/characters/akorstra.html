Akorstra was a giant spider pet of the priestess of Lolth Vicala Szind during the 1480s DR.

Description

Akorstra had a gray-brown body and bright red feet.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Akorstra joined its mistress Vicala on the attack on the Midler farmhouse. While Vicala interrogated the Midler family and Speitar Dun'Robar handled the troops, Akorstra lurked in the attic space waiting to ambush whoever came by.
