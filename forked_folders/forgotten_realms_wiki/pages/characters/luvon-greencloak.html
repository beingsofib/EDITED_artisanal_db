Luvon Greencloak () was the former emissary of the Elven Court in Cormanthyr and the regional agent of the Moonstars for the region in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR. He was an old associate of Khelben Arunsun.

History

He was in 1374 DR among the many powerful mages Arunsun brought together in order to cleanse part of the High Moor and restore Faer'tel'miir, a lost city of Miyeritar.

Personality

Despite his sun elf heritage, Luvon believed in the Moonstars' ideal of unity of humans and elves.
