Phyvvak was an ixilthetocal ruler of history in Seros.

History

Born in 1107 DR, Phyvvak ascended to the position of vitanar in 1185 DR. During his rule, he unleashed many crusades against the other races, claiming the lives of 200,000 merfolk, 2,000 aquatic elves, and nearly 1,000,000 koalinth but at the price of 500,000 of his own subjects. He was the most dogmatic vitanar of all the Xedran Reefs.
