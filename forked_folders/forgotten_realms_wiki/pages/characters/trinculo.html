Trinculo was a mercury dragon pledged to Avachel in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Trinculo's elf guise was a Trunalor, a wild elf, with a shock of white hair, most often dressed in a purple and green robe.

Personality

The dragon was notable for his great zest and cheer.

Activities

Trinculo had taken the pledge of Avachel.

Relationships

He was good friends with Chalintash, a copper dragon, and with Khalt Laathine, a Trunalor of the Forest of Amtar.

History

Trinculo had been born in Evermeet. He had at one point met with Queen Amlaruil on the edge of the Lake of Dreams.

At some point decades before , the restless dragon had taken the pledge of Avachel to give himself something to do away from Evermeet.

During the Rage of Dragons of the , Trinculo warned the elves at the Forest of Amtar to be on the lookout for dragons affected by the Rage; his friend Khalt decided to go with him, to assist him as needed, as part of his own Pledge of Avachel. Khalt accompanied him to Beregost, where Trinculo was meeting with Chalintash at the Jovial Juggler inn. In the meeting, Trinculo learned that the Rage was caused by elven High Magic; he decided he'd go to Evermeet, where together with Khalt, they might find some aid in mitigating or halting it.

Overnight, Chalintash was driven into a mindless berserker by the Rage. Trinculo and Khalt fought him together, finally executing him bitterly. While heading for Evermeet afterwards, Trinculo was overwhelmed by bitterness and grief, and gave hints he openly blamed Khalt's people for the Rage. Believing he was going to be killed, Khalt cut the ropes on his saddle, and fell into the water, not to be seen again. Trinculo regretted it soon after, and searched the water, but proved unable to find him. Finally, he continued on his way to Evermeet.

Despite descending on Evermeet possessed by the Rage, Trinculo survived until at least .
