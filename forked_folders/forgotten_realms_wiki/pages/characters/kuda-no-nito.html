Kuda no Nito was a weapons merchant in Kozakura in 1357 DR.

History

In 1357 DR, while traveling to sell his goods with his two assistants, Nito was attacked by some evil sohei who killed his assistants and robbed him. Only great luck saved his life. Later, Nito told some adventurers what had happened.
