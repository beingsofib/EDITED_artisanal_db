Yonald was the ship steward of the smoke and fire ship in Vaelan as of 1374 DR. In he agreed to take Ususi Manaallin, Iahn Qoyllor and Eined Datharathi to Huorm; though the three of them were attacked by Xaemar Datharathi's men, Ususi intimidated the man into taking them there anyway.

He allowed Ususi and Eined the use of his cabin during the trip. He kept some tins of dried fish in there.
