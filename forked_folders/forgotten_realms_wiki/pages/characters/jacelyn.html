Jacelyn was a half-elven sorcerer who died in the 15 century DR, killed by the Cult of the Absolute.

Description

Jacelyn was a man with fair skin tone, delicate features, green eyes, and long black hair.

History

Jacelyn was born seventh son of a seventh son. He was slain by an agent of the Cult of the Absolute in the . It was believed that Jacelyn's assassin remarked that his parents "probably should've left it at six." Jacelyn's soul yearned for revenge, and he ended up among the dozen individuals plucked from the Fugue Plane, chosen by the entity called Withers to aid in his campaign against the Dead Three and their Cult of the Absolute.
