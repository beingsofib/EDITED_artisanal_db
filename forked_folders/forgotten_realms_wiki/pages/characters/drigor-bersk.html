Drigor Bersk was a cleric of Ilmater who fought by King Gareth Dragonsbane's side in . He was instrumental to ending the Rage of Dragons.

Description

With a scarred face and hulking physique, Drigor Bersk was a very unlikely priest of Ilmater.

Personality

Drigor Bersk was quite fatalistic and pessimistic.

Abilities

Drigor was able to cast many spells, most likely aid, blade barrier, and searing light. He claimed he could cast a restoring spell, likely restoration, and to be more advanced in the mysteries than Pavel Shemov. He participated as one of the circle members of the Grand Divination on the Feast of the Moon during the council of wyrms in Thentia. During the siege of the dracorage mythal on Nightal 6, he cast at least one barrier spell.

History

On the battle near Ostrav of Mirtul 2 when Sergor Marsk stole Gareth Dragonsbane's soul for the Cult of the Dragon, Drigor Bersk was fighting by the King's side.

Drigor Bersk participated in the meeting where Pavel Shemov explained Brimstone's plans to Queen Christine Dragonsbane on Kythorn 25. He later joined the team sent into the Plane of Shadow to recover the King's soul on Kythorn 26. He was also part of King Dragonsbane's offensive against the Vaasan horde once the King was awake, on Flamerule 11.

He was sent to the council of wyrms of the Feast of the Moon in Thentia, on behalf of King Dragonsbane, where he participated in the Grand Divination. Drigor Bersk perished on Nightal 6 of , decapitated by one of Sammaster's guardian ironwyrm golems in the dracorage mythal.
