Emrae Firesky was a Iliyanbruen moon elf and a priestess of Corellon who lived in New Sharandar during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

She was known as an angry upstart among the fey of New Sharandar, often championing the use of violence towards those who lived in the region around the Neverwinter Wood.

Activities

Emrae was the highest-ranking priestess at the Temple of Leaves temple in New Sharandar.

Relationships

Emrae was a close ally of Olirien Mistcrown and his fellow elves who sought revenge against the residents of Neverwinter.

She was aided in her role at the temple by her assistant Addemios Three-Dawn, a secret devotee of Asmodeus who sought to exploit the conflict between the elves of New Sharandar and the residents of Neverwinter.

History

When the fomorian giant king Malabog threatened New Sharandar circa the , Emrae took up temporary residence within the Blighted Forest to act against his destructive army. She ordered some adventurers to gather fey flowers throughout the region in order to preserve them from the blight of the dark fey, and replant new trees that had been magically enchanted.
