High Lady Jasmine Lurian was a noble of House Lurian in the city of Procampur in the Vast in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

As head of House Lurian, he was involved in the family's gold-smithing business.

Relationships

Her husband was Lord Consul Povalo Lurian, the head of the family, and they had some children.

History

She was the High Lady by 1370 DR, when she was aged 52.
