Satiir Thione-Hhune () was a noblewoman of the Hhune patriar family of Baldur's Gate, and a member of the Knights of the Shield during the 15 century DR.

Description

Even in her 70s, Satiir maintained a fit physique and the distinct presence of Baldurian aristocracy.

History

In the , Lady Satiir was kidnapped by agents the Baldurian Vanthampur family. They held her hostage in their estate in the Upper City in an effort to find information the missing Shield of the Hidden Lord.
