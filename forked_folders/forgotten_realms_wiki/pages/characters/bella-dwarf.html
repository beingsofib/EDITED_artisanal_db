Bella was a member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Fist Bella was a fair-skinned gold dwarf woman with light hazel eyes and hair of the same color, tied in a bushy ponytail. She kept her face clean-shaven. Bella wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist: a set of leathers, and a pair of leather gauntlets. Her chest was adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia. Bella wielded a heavy crossbow.

History

In the , Fist Bella was stationed in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, within the Wyrm's Crossing fortress. During he Absolute crisis, Bella, along with other Fists, guarded the new Grand Duke Enver Gortash, unbeknownst to all – one of the men behind the Cult of the Absolute and the strife that befell Baldur's Gate.
