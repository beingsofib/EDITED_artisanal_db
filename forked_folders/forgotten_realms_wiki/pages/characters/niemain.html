Niemain was a wizard and a member of the Zhentarim that operated in the Baldur's Gate region of the Sword Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

Niemain worked alongside his fellow Zhentarim agent, a necromancer by the name of Xzar.

Possesions

The Zhentarim mage wore a robe of fire resistance, a useful set of garments as he also carried with him a wand of fire.

For some reason, he also wore a cursed ring of clumsiness, The Jester's Folly.
