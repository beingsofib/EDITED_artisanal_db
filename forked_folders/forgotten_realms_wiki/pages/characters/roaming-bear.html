Roaming Bear

The Roaming Bear was a monster that was the subject of many legends among the Northlanders of the Moonshae Isles as of the 14 century DR.

Description

The great bear was said to be so large that it could devour whole families in a single bite. The tales generally agreed that it was an unkillable ghostly apparition, and some also claimed that it could assume the shape of a human man.

Activities

The Roaming Bear was said to pace across the highlands of the Moonshaes on misty nights, looking for victims to eat.

History

The legend of the Roaming Bear originated in the realm of Gnarhelm before spreading to other Northlander lands.
