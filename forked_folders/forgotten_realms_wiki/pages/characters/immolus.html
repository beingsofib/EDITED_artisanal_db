Archon Immolus was a fire archon of Mount Hotenow and a member of the cult of Maegera led by the fire giant Gommoth during the late 15 century DR. Immolus commanded a group of lesser fire archons.

History

In the , Immolus and the fire giant Grumthrox captured Firecross Bridge to isolate the dwarves of the Hillborn expedition from their allies on Mount Hotenow.

The pair were defeated by the Heroes of the Sleeping Dragon Bridge that same year, and the bridge was reclaimed by the adventurers and the Hillborn expedition.
