Koleth was a tavernkeeper inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Koleth ran the Tesh-Wave tavern located on the waterfront of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter on the south bank of the River Tesh. He weathered the destruction of Zhentil Keep in the , by boarding up the tavern and waiting in the basement until the violence ceased.
