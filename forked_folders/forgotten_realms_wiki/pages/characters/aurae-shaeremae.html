Lady Aurae Shaeremae was an elf of House Shaeremae in Cormanthor in the 3 century DR.

History

In , she attended a revel where Elminster substituted Coronal Eltargrim Irithyl. Her husband, Qildor Rewen, spoke for the two of them.
