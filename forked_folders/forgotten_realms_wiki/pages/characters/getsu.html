Getsu was an unusually intelligent tigbanua buso in Nakamaru around 1357 DR.

Activities

Somehow, a tigbanua buso gained intelligence and named himself Getsu, plotting to destroy the city of Nakamaru.

History

Getsu was a very ancient creature, a first a servant to humans but at one point he became foul and perverted.

In 1357 DR, it decided to target the Worthless Flower yakuza gang to spread his infection. He would start by infecting the oyabun Uruma.

Personality

Gestu liked to toy with and torture his victims. He was crueler than most of his fellows.
