Beldrigarr Stoneshield was a watchman in Waterdeep during the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

He was familiar with Mirt the Moneylender and his usual doings at the docks.

History

In the , Beldrigarr Stoneshield found the guard detail of Velzraedo Hlaklavarr almost cutting down a serving lad nearly as they crossed the threshold of the Brave Bustard. He entered, explained he was looking for them after they were found to pass counterfeit coins at the Sunset Sail tavern, and then checked up on Mirt at the location, who seemed distressed.
