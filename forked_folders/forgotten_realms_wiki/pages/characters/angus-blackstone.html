Angus Blackstone was Earl of Fairheight in the Kingdom of Callidyrr in the Moonshaes during the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

He kept a pack of moorhounds, one of whom was called Warlock.

Relationships

Blackstone's son and heir, Currag Blackstone, was killed in mysterious circumstances in 1365 DR. Angus was instead succeeded by his son Hanrold Blackstone.

History

Following Currag's death in 1365 DR, Earl Blackstone succumbed to greed and a lust for power, and agreed to betray High King Tristan Kendrick in exchange for favors from an agent of Talos. He would end up paying for their deceit with his life.
