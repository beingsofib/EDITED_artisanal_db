Alysir Ursequarra was a female fey'ri and a member of the daemonfey.

History

As lady of the Ursequarra house in 1374 DR, she attended a meeting of the daemonfey gathered by their leader Sarya Dlardrageth. Sarya considered Alysir very stupid and would kill her if the fey'ri were not irreplaceably few in number.

After the daemonfey's defeat at the Vale of Lost Voices, Alysir questioned Sarya's authority and at last Sarya destroyed Alysir.
