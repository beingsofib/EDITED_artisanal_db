Salene Freig was a broadsheet delivery clerk employed by Baldur's Mouth in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Salene was a human woman with a light tan complexion. The woman had long, dark brown hair, and eyes of the same color. She wore simple garb of a common delivery woman.

History

In the , Salene worked on managing the delivery of freshly printed Baldur's Mouth Gazette bundles. During the Absolute crisis, she could be often be found within Baldur's Mouth's offices in Heapside of the Lower City.
