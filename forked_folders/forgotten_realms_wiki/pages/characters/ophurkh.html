Ophurkh was a quasit servant of Baphomet during the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Ophurkh was one of the many demons in Avernus when Elturel fell into the Nine Hells. He was one of the first to enter the fallen city, acting as scout. Ophurkh managed to infiltrate the Cathedral to Lathander and there waiting the arrival of more demons.
