Claire was a courtesan who was enslaved by Lady Galvena.

History

Claire was to be married to a trader called Sanik, and they planned to elope. However, as her mistress, believed that Claire was her "property", she would not allow for this. Consequently, Claire's husband-to-be was assassinated, and she was ordered to be tortured to death.

Rumors

It was rumored that Claire, whilst undergoing torture and condemnation by Lady Galvena, was rescued by Gorion's Ward. She was informed of Sanik's death, and was deeply saddened. She sought safety under Sanik's captain, Golin.
