Gariskar Dauntinghorn was a nobleman of Cormyr and a head of the Dauntinghorn family in the late 15 century DR.

Description

The handsome, smooth-looking man had a fierce smile. His hair was dark; he gave the impression of an old sea lion.

Relationships

He was the patriarch of the Dauntinghorn family, and a rival noble to Marlin Stormserpent.

History

In Mirtul of the , Marlin Stormserpent paid the artist Jharakphred to follow two people, Lord Gariskar Dauntinghorn and Draskos Crownsilver and then paint a portrait of them. The artist shadowed them for days so closely that he had to be beat off by their retinue with cudgels. Marlin then ordered his two blueflame ghosts, Relve Langral and Treth Halonter, to learn the men's likenesses from their portraits to better identify and kill those men.
