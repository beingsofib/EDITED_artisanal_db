Vogt was a member of the Company of Dawn, an mercenary that operated out of Arabel during the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the , Vogt and the Company of the Dawn took on the job of escorting some friends and acquaintances of their leader Thurbrand through Spiderhaunt Wood. The journey was devastating to their company, leading to many of their deaths, leaving only Vogt, Thurbrand, and Isaac as survivors.

After arriving at their destination in Shadowdale, the three remaining members of the company returned to the city of Arabel in Cormyr.
