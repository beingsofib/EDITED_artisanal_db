Khelvos Dermmen was the Provisional Governor of Scardale in the late 14 century DR. His duties included leading the Scardale Council that ruled over the realm, both of which were stationed a keep in Chandlerscross looking over the Ashaba.

Rulership

Khelvos was elected to replace Myrian Beechwood after the Shaking Plague devastated Scardale Town in 1370 DR.
