Swordcaptain Garwyn Thane was a Purple Dragon of Cormyr, who was stationed in the town of Eveningstar during the invasion from Sschindylryn in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Garwyn was a large human man with brown eyes, black hair, and dark skin. He wore a suit of chain armor and a simple white surcoat, and carried a large longsword.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Garwyn worked under Constal Adamor Thellik in pushing back the drow from the area and defending the town. When the second Battle for Eveningstar was imminent, he organized the Cormyrean side, using his Purple Dragons, War Wizards, the Evenor militia, adventurers, and even Elminster as assets. In particular he had the use of Ana Brabener, a young local girl with an unexpected magical talent, which he used to shore up the defenses further. Garwyn proved to be a skilled commander, as he correctly deduced that the drow would not use the Darkening as cover but instead attack at night. The battle went off as well as anyone could hope, defeating the drow and ending their siege of the village.
