Bassorah was an inhabitant of Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Personality

Bassorah was a shrew of a woman.

Activities

Driven by a desire to be accepted by the elite society of Huzuz, Bassorah spent all of her husband's hard-earned money hosting lavish parties that they could not afford. Her actions drove he husband to abandon her one night after such a party.

Relationships

Bassorah was married to a physician named Mustapha, though she had not seen him in years since he abandoned her with their debts.
