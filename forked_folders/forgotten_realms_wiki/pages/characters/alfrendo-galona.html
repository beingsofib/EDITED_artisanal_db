Alfrendo Galona was a businessman in the Shining Plains in 1358 DR.

Activities

Alfrendo managed Alfrendo's Mythical Menagerie, a circus of mythical creatures, all made inoffensive.

History

In 1358 DR, at the festival of Urml, Alfrendo had a chimera, a gargoyle, a harpy, and a baby copper dragon named Cueperia, which Alfrendo treated as a pet.
