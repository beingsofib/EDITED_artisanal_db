Usta was a half-orc serving girl inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Usta worked in the Pride of Zhentil Keep, an inn located on the west side of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter on the south bank of the River Tesh. She had to put up with the innkeep Berk and his offensive body odor.
