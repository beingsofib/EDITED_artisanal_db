Kneeda Morningchild was a mage who was studying necromancy with Maferil the Gravewalker in Luskan during the late 15 century DR.

History

Studing necromancy in Luskan sometime prior to 1487 DR, Kneeda Morningchild was Maferil's fourth pupil. He described her as "a walking irony", as "her name belied the darkness within."
