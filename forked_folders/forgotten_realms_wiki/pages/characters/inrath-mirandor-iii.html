Inrath Mirandor III was one of the first kings of Impiltur, the 14 ruler of the kingdom's Mirandor Dynasty. He ruled for over thirty years in the early-to-mid–2 century DR.

Relationships

Inrath was born the first son to Baranth I. He had two sons―Inrath IV and Pendarn―with his first wife, who eventually succumbed to the plague. Inrath had one other son, Doraven, born to another woman.

History

Inrath III ruled until the , when he was succeeded by his eldest son Inrath IV.
