Musalim was one of the D'tarig people that lived in the desert of Anauroch during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Like other D'tarig, Musalim was rather diminutive in size, standing about tall with a slender build.

Personality

He was rather subservient to others, readily deferring to others and calling them "Lord", despite their protest.

Relationships

Musalim served as the assistant to Bhadla.

History

In the , Musalim and Bhadla served as a guide for the Harper agent Lander, After a sandstorm separated Lander from the two D'tarig, Musalim went missing, presumably having ridden into an asabi ambush and been slain by one of the reptilian beasts.
