Istine Madrovelle was a Harper agent in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Istine had dark skin, brown eyes, and short black hair. She wore a simple gray dress.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar and the extended Lolth-related situation with Eberron, Istine stayed in Eveningstar to monitor and coordinate efforts against the Spider Queen, including sending teams into Lolth's own domain and striking at her there. She was unusually well-informed about the world of Eberron during these operations.
