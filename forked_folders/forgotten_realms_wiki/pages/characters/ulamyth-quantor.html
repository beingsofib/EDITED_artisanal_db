Ulamyth Quantor served as the High Priest, or High Torturelord, of Loviatar within the Tower of Pain Exalted in Zhentil Keep in 1358 DR.

Activities

High Torturelord Quantor supervised 14 fellow priests of the Maiden of Pain. He preached to a congregation of 26 followers. It was through Ulamyth's own actions that worship of Loviatar occurred within Zhentil Keep to begin with in the . As a gift for his efforts Loviatar rewarded him with extended youth. Ulamyth supported Lord Manshoon's efforts in an attempt to bring more gold to the church.

Following the destruction of most of Zhentil Keep in the , Ulamyth moved Loviatar's church to the newly erected Palace of Sweet Pain located on the southern shore of the River Tesh in the Foreign Quarter.
