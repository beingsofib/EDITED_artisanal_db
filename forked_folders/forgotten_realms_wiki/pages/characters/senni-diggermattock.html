Senni Diggermattock was, along with her husband Dorbo Diggermattock, one of the informal rulers of the svirfneblin settlers in Blingdenstone in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Senni's main task was to find homes for all the new settlers. She also managed the commerce routes to Mithral Hall, Gauntlgrym, and Mantol-Derith, and helped maintain the magical defenses of the city.

Personality

Senni valued and counted everything, to be sure that Dorbo had all he needed. She was devoted to the vision of a rebuilt Blingdenstone.

History

Dorbo and Senni were capable miners, with Senni acting as administrator. With the money they gathered, Dorbo and Senni planned the reclamation of Blingdenstone. They pondered the reclamation mission for ten years, in order to consider all the dangers. Afterward, they led a contingent of settlers to Blingdenstone, slowly reclaiming parts of the city.

Circa 1486 DR, Senni faced many threats.
