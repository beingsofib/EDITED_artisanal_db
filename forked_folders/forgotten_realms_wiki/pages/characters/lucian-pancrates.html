Lucian Pancrates was an apprentice scholar who lived in the city of Neverwinter during the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Lucian studied under the master spellcaster Tualo the Bright.

History

In the , Lucian was present at Tualo's house when the learned master performed a ritual with the use of a tome called Transmogrifications of the Spellplague. The ritual went awry however, as the book had been altered by the malevolent moon elf Rhazzad, opening a portal to the Far Realm. Lucian survived the encounter, but Master Tualo was not so fortunate.
