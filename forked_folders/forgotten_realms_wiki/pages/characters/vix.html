Vix was a dwarf chandler inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

In addition to earning a living selling lamps, torches, and related items, Vix was responsible for lighting Daggerford's street lights along Duke's Way and Hill Road during special celebrations.

Relationships

Vix lived with his wife and two daughters. He also kept a trio of apprentices.
