Aelrue was a moon elf neighbor of Elminster Aumar in the mid-14 century DR.

Description

Aelrue was youthful and slim, with long legs and long waves of silvery-gray hair reaching as far down as her tiny waist. Her eyes were blue-gray, flecked with gold.

History

Aelrue had borrowed a pipe from Elminster Aumar in late Mirtul of , and left it to Tarth Hornwood, who came to her while looking for directions to Elminster's home, to return it.
