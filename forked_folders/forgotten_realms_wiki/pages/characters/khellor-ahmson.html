Khellor Ahmson was a guardsman who served in the Amnian military in the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

He was well very acquainted with Lord Isaea Roenall, who served as an officer in Athkatla. Khellor was aware of Isaea's involvement in smuggling and slaving operations, by means of the pirate Barg.
