Jaqalwar Lansena was one of the Merchant-Lords of Imdolphyn as of 1479 DR.

Activities

Jaqalwar was a well known playboy sailor, who owned a substantial trading fleet. He was also known for his preference to dally with women of exotic races, such as elves, drow or genasi.
