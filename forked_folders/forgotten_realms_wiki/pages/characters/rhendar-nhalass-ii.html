Rhendar Nhalass II was the twelfth Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar.

Activities

Rhendar II inherited the throne when his older brother, Umathar VI, died. His reign was short indeed as he was slain by demons of Narfell during his first year as Arkhan in .

Relationships

Rhendar II was the third son of Darthen Nhalass III. His eldest brother died with their father while invading Narfell. His next oldest brother was Umathar VI, and his younger brothers were Tharren and Sharath.
