Marshal Kyrill Nederlok was a general of the Flaming Fist mercenary company and served the organization since its founding. He was highly respected among his soldiers and superiors, and was well–well trusted by the founder of the Fist, Grand Duke Eltan.

Personality

He preferred Winter Wine over other types.
