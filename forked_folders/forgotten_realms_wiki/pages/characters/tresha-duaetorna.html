Tresha Duaetorna held the office of "officer of the holies" in Tulmon along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Tresha was rotund and motherly.

Activities

She encouraged the sultan's policy of personal unassisted worship among Tulmon's citizens, which helped many of the city's smaller shrines and chapels flourish.

History

Tresha was originally an adventurer from Waterdeep who worshiped Ao at one point in her life.
