Bastion was a powerful dog in the world of Eberron.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, Bastion served as a guard dog for the Lords of Dust at their hidden base in Stormreach. The hound ultimately fell when the forces of the Silver Flame sent by Brother Dectaran raided the hideout, killing the cultists and their dogs too.
