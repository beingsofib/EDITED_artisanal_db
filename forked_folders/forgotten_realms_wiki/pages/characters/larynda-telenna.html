Larynda Telenna was the high priestess of Kiaransalee in the Vault of Gnashing Teeth beneath Vaasa. She was also the leader of Kiaransalee's cult across the entirety of Faerûn.

History

In 1337 DR, Larynda Telenna brought with her a group of acolytes who aided her in constructing the Acropolis of Thanatos in the ruins of the city V'elddrinnsshar. Among those who resided with her in the Vault of Gnashing Teeth was the keening spirit Gurden Myrinn.
