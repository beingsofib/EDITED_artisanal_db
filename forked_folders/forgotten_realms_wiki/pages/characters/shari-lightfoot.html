Shari Lightfoot

Shari Lightfoot was a bloodforge commander in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

Shari Lightfoot's followers were known for their mobility.

The commander of the Legendary Campaign tried to corner Shari's forces against the shores of the Great Sea, but they quickly retreated to the Realm of Tides. However, the commander had laid a trap for them there, and the two clashed in a bloodforge battle.
