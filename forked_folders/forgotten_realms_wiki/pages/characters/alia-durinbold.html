Lady Alia Durinbold was a senior member of the noble patriar Durinbold family of Baldur's Gate, presumably by marriage, and a member of the Parliament of Peers in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Alia Durinbold was a wood elven woman with a pale skin tone and blue eyes. She wore her hair, color of dirty blonde, in two short braids. Alia wore a diadem, a jewelweed coronet, and a long navy blue gown embroidered in gold. Lady Durinbold accentuated her eyes with golden metallic makeup.

History

In the , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldur's Gate, Lady Alia Durinbold was a Parliamentarian of the Gate and voted in support of crowning lowborn Lord Enver Gortash as the city's Archduke. Lady Durinbold was present for Gortash's coronation that took place in the audience hall of Wyrm's Rock. Following the coronation, Lady Durinbold expressed her support of Enver Gortash as she trusted Duke Ulder Ravengard's judgement. Unbeknownst to her, Ravengard's actions were influenced by an altered illithid tadpole and enthrallments by the Cult of the Absolute.
