Colleen was a young woman who inhabited the town of O'Malley on the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Colleen was quite petite.

Activities

Colleen once knocked a pair of firbolg heads free from spikes during an enthusiastic celebration.

Relationships

She was the daughter of O'Malley's lord.
