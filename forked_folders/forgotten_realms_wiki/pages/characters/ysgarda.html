Ysgarda was a page of Candlemas, the of Delia, in the Netherese Empire circa .

Description

The girl had deep blue eyes and short black hair.

Personality

Ysgarda acted very flighty and nervous around powerful figures, such as Candlemas.

Possessions

When working for the High Steward, Ysgarda wore a white and black outfit.

Relationships

Her father managed the in Delia for Lady Polaris.

History

During the Shadowed Age, the nine-year-old page informed Candlemas that members of the Beneficent Traders' Guild of Dalekeva, also known as Oberon's Hold, wished to speak to him about a growing problem.
