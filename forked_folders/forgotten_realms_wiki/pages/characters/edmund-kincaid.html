Edmund Kincaid was King of Snowdown in the Moonshae Isles during the turn of the 6 century DR. He was subsequently named as regent of Callidyrr as well.

Relationships

He was a member of House Kincaid, the royal family of Snowdown and an offshoot of House Cymrych.

History

In the , after High King Tuathal and High King Carrig III were both assassinated in quick succession, Edmund Kincaid was appointed as regent to the throne of High King by Carrig III's wife, Queen Abigail. Edmund held this position until Carrig III's heir, Colin Cymrych, tuned fifteen in the .
