Billyup Blacknee was a responsible young halfling who helped run the Blacknee's Halfhappenstance inn circa 1369 DR with his parents Buzzrip Blacknee and Broonella Blacknee, and his elder sister Breena Blacknee and younger brother Boldric Blacknee.
