Velki was a Reaper of Bhaal who resided in the ruined Temple of Bhaal in the Undercity of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Like many other Reapers of Bhaal, Velki was wrapped in dark cloth, a mask and a hood hid her facial features, leaving only her brown eyes exposed.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Velki killed and skinned her victims in service of Bhaal, the Lord of Murder and his Chosen – Orin the Red. She hold fascination with leather made out of humanoid skin and especially valued drow skin, unblemished by sunlight's glare, that was made into magnificent leather. Thorough servitude to Orin, Reaper of Bhaal Velki supported the Cult of the Absolute that threatened the city of Baldur's Gate.
