Ty Willowbrook was a reporter employed by Baldur's Mouth and a frequent customer of the Elfsong Tavern in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Ty Willowbrook was a human man with a dark skin tone, green eyes, and short, curly, dark brown hair.

History

In the , Ty Willowbrook worked at Baldur's Mouth's offices in Heapside of the Lower City. He regularly struggled with finding words and inspiration, especially for catchy headlines like Open Hand Loses its Grip!, Savage Butchery of Revered Priest!, Templed Turned Slaughterhouse in Shock Horror Priest Murder! and Stabbed and Stabbed Again!
