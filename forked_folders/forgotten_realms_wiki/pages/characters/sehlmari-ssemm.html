Sehlmari Ssemm was a mercenary recruiter in Westgate.

Activities

Sehlmari was the morale officer and quartermaster of the Mercenaries' Guild of Westgate. She always went a day ahead of the troops with a platoon to buy, gather, or commandeer food, drink, and other necessities. She was a master haggler, always taking what she needed for the troops. Sehlmari had also a lovable sense of humor.
