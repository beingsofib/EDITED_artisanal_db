Janthool was a merchant in Athkatla in 1359 DR.

Activities

Janthool traveled Faerûn in order to make money. He tried to damage Aurora's Emporium by hiring orcs and bandits to attack their outlets.

History

Janthool gained wealth thanks to shrewd bartering and careful, covert investments. One time he arranged a stampede against the Aurora's outlet in Waterdeep but had to flee the city hastily when evidence was given against him. In 1367 DR, he found a treasure in Soubar.
