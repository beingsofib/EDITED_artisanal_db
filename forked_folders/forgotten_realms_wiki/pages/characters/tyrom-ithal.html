Tyrom Ithal was a prince of the Ithal Dynasty of Tethyr and the first Lord-Protector of the Dragon's Head Peninsula.

Relationships

Prince Tyrom was the second surviving son of King Silvam Ithal and Queen Alcina and the brother of future king Nishan. He had at least one additional older brother, whom he outlived.

History

During the dreadful and destructive tidal waves of the , the entirety of Clan Tarseth was killed. Their clan lands of the Dragon's Head Peninsula became the property of the crown, and Prince Tyrom was appointed its first Lord-Protector.
