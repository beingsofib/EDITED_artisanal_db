Ivrynxra Hoond was a famous arcanist and adventurer of ancient Netheril.

Personality

Ivrynxra was a lively, humorous and outgoing woman.

History

She was best known for writing Ivrynxra's Exploration of Enchantments, an academically significant record of her adventures that detailed the unusual spell interactions she witnessed and created.
