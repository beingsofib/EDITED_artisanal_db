Commander Lightfeather was a carrier pigeon who worked for the Sword Coast Couriers in the Rivington neighborhood of the Outer City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Commander Lightfeather was an average-looking pigeon with gray feathers and a sheen of greens and purples around the neck. He could not speak as he was just a bird; however, one could communicate with him via magical means.

History

Lightfeather was the delusional self-proclaimed military "Commander," who supervised other carrier pigeons of the Sword Coast Couriers in . The pigeon believed that there was a war going on outside the "headquarters," and the mail he and other birds were delivering was top-secret communiqués attached to them by a "tall one." Commander Lightfeather believed he supervised the "finest aerial communication regiment this side of the Chionthar." When carrier pigeons began disappearing in the city, Lightfeather believed they were casualties of war; in reality, the birds were snatched by Tara, a tressym, that made a nest on a nearby rooftop. The tressym was searching for her friend and master, Gale Dekarios of Waterdeep, who was involved in the Absolute crisis of 1492 DR.
