Shoor Vandree was a drow warrior of House Vandree in Menzoberranzan in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Shoor was a young drow warrior living in the Velkynvelve outpost. Thanks to injuring of Jorlan Duskryn, he became the field commander and lover of the leader of the outpost, Ilvara Mizzrym.

Personality

Shoor was young and quite arrogant. He was still flush about his promotion, showing it by lording over every other male in Velkynvelve. However, Shoor was still insecure about his position and felt the need to show his skill to his mistress and find ways to please her.

Relationship

Shoor became the new lover of Ilvara. He enjoyed showing off his promotion to Jorlan.
