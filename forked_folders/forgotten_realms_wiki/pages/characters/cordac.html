Cordac was a leatherworker in Freedale in the Dalelands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Cordac was middle-aged.

Personality

He was happy to show people the trade, and tell tales of distant places.

Activities

He was a leatherworker, and maker of small jewelry. His goods were sold through the town's general store. He was also a buyer of furs.

Relationships

He lived alone.

History

Cordac had traveled across Faerûn throughout his life. By the mid-to-late 14 century DR, he had been settled in Freedale for years.
