Dilby was a commoner from the town of Nashkel circa mid–14 century DR.

History

Circa 1368 DR, Dilby hated the village's extremely annoying and talkative man. Noober was universally disliked and often was pelted by stones thrown by the locals. Dilby was among the locals who threw rocks at Noober.
