Lendarn was an evil wizard who lived on the Sword Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

History

Lendarn was present in the ruins beneath Firewine Bridge when the village of Gullykin was being terrorized by kobolds in the .

While he was outwardly hostile towards interlopers who ventured into the ruins, it was unknown if he was allied with the oni mage responsible for the kobold assaults.
