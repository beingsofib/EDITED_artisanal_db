Alec III of the Carrathal Dynasty was a High King of Callidyrr in the early 11 century DR. Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles, he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal royal family of Callidyrr and was the grandson of his predecessor, King Erfle. His son and sole heir was Bayle, who would go on to be his successor. Bayle's eventual successor, Yolanda, was Alec III's grandniece.
