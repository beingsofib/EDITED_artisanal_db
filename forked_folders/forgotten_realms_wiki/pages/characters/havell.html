Havell was a merchant in Shussel in 1357 DR.

History

Havell was unveiled as a fraud by a foreign merchant (probably from Durpar).

In 1357 DR, Havell discovered a cache of magic potions that enabled him to turn ethereal for a time while wielding a non-ethereal object. He used those potions to start a killing spree among the foreign merchants of the city.

Personality

Havell hated all foreigners after his humiliation.
