Klavaeron was a priest of Mielikki living in Cedarsproke in the Gulthmere Forest on the Dragon Coast in the late 12 century DR.

History

In the , the ranger Rhighaermon O'Antlers gave the holy spellbook Yornar's Trail Companion to Klavaeron.
