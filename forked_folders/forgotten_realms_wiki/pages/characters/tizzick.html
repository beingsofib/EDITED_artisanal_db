Tizzick was a lizardman guide who lived and worked inside the Wheloon Prison in the late 15 century DR.

History

Tizzick worked in Wheloon on behalf of the forces of Cormyr who operated the prison, using the tunnels and drainage culverts beneath it to get around more easily. He helped their agents traverse the area as they performed missions.
