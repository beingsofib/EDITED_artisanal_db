Myo-Myo was a famous ghost hunter from T'u Lung in Kara-Tur, active sometime before the early 14 century DR.

History

Myo-Myo gained fame by hunting down and slaying ghosts, demons, and many other foul spirits that plagued the good folk of the land. She carried an impressive collection of fantastic magic equipment, including gong – a shield and a gold, enchanted by the monastics of the Seven Stone Monks Temple. By the , Myo-Myo's gong was no longer in her possession and found its way to the frigid Icewind Dale.
