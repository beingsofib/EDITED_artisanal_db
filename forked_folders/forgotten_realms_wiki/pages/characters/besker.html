Besker was an apothecary in the city of Liara of Nova Vaasa, one of the Domains of Dread.

Description

He was an old man.

Personality

Besker was helpful.

Abilities

He knew a few spells, namely identify and mending, and was more helpful due to his knowledge of herbalism.

Activities

He worked at the apothecary shop he owned.

History

In the mid-to-late 14 century DR, a party from Ravens Bluff was for some reason in the Demiplane of Dread. They came to the city of Liara, where they got involved in the search for a vampire who was killing people by draining them of blood; they may have asked Besker for clues at his apothecary.
