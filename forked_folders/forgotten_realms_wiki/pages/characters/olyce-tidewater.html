Olyce Tidewater was a hunter who operated out of Eveningstar in the 15 century DR.

Description

Olyce had dark skin and long black hair, and wore a bright green suit of leather armor.

History

Following the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar and Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, Olyce was hunting in the Storm Horns with Thanbard Krys when they were suddenly ambushed by orcs that secretly served Archwizard Amskar. The pair escaped back to Eveningstar, though Thanbard's leg was injured in the process. They couldn't help but notice the odd behaviour of the orcs, which coincided with the failed raid upon Eveningstar that the Purple Dragons defeated. The two hunters ended up on the Eveningstar bridge, with Olyce bandaging Thanbard's wound while the Harpers Kara Patwin and Lorant Tass organized a full investigation into the strange events.
