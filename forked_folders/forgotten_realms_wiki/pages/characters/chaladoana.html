Chaladoana was a drow infiltrator working in Scornubel during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

Chaladoana took orders directly from Daerdatha, and had three apprentices.

History

Chaladoana was physically present during the ball at Halonder Eldeglut's manor in , where the merchant and his wife Iyrevven Eldeglut were supposed to be subject to a "taking", that is, they were to be taken to Chult as slaves and supplanted by other drow infiltrators. Chaladoana, and their apprentices, helped Daerdratha there with mind reaming Qilué Veladorn, the drow Chosen of Mystra, whose cover was blown during the mission; she had replaced one of their infiltrators to spy on them.
