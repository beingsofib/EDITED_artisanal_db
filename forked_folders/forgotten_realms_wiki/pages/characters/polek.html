Polek was a vanity items merchant in the market district of Luskan in the late 15 century.

Description

Polek had ginger hair and a large handlebar moustache, and wore a feathered bycocket hat.

History

Around 1487 DR, Polek was peddling his wares in the Luskan market district when he encountered the Champion of the Burning Dawn.
