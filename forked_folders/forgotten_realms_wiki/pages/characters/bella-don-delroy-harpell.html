Bella don Delroy Harpell was a member of the Harpell family and daughter of spokesman Delroy Harpell of Longsaddle in the Savage Frontier of Faerûn.

Description

Beautiful Bella was short (barely five feet) with one bright green eye and one dull green eye that looked in different directions.

History

In 1358 DR, Bella journeyed with Harkle, Regweld, and Bidderdoo Harpell to help defend Mithral Hall.
