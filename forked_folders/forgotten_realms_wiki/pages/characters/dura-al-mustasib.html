Dura al-Mustasib lived in Iris Well Spring in the Hordelands during the mid–14th century DR.

Relationships

Dura was the daughter of Atabeg Ali al-Mustasib, an exiled regent of Semphar.

She became a concubine of Prince Hubadai of the Tuigan horde. She bore him two sons.
