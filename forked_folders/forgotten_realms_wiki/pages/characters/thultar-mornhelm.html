Thultar Mornhelm was a High Herald Unicorn who served as the ruling regent of Amn during the Wavegold dynasty.

History

Thultar took the office of regent of Amn in the , when King Erdhinn Wavegold I and his heir Belhinn died suddenly. He worked with King Proster Obarskyr of Cormyr to hide and protect the young prince Helgrin until he was old enough to rule in 1127 DR, whereupon he took the throne and the name Belhinn I.
