Clack was a goblin warrior and cultist of the Absolute who protected the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

Description

She had orange skin and kept her head bald.

Personality

Unlike many of her fellow goblin cultists, Clack had a no-nonsense attitude.

Possessions

Clack wore a suit of crude plate armor and wielded a battleaxe in combat.

History

In the , Clack remained in the Shattered Sanctum while other members of the cult celebrated their successful raid on the Waukeen's Rest inn.
