Mentor Wintercloak was a mysterious human archmage who lived for some time in Myth Drannor in Cormanthyr.

Relationships

He was a close ally of Alais Dree.

History

Mentor was born in the .

As a member of the Incanists' Society, Mentor helped found and direct the Incanistaeum magic school. In the , he was the only human member of the early Council of Twelve.

From 426 DR to 440 DR Mentor tutored the young Khelben Arunsun (at that time still unnamed). In the , Mentor also tutored the young Uthgardt mage Tulrun. During this period, Mentor was believed to have collected new spells created by his apprentices in a tome titled The Wizards' Workbook, which found its way into the possession of the wizard Flamsterd of the Moonshaes, who scribed a copy for his friend Elminster.

However, in the , Mentor, together with the Masked and their four gnomes apprentices, left the city.

In the , he was among the many powerful mages alongside Tulrun as his former student Khleben Arunsun brought them together to cleanse part of the High Moor and restore Faer'tel'miir, a lost city of Miyeritar.
