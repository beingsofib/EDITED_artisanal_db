Tresh Lanngolyn was a weaver inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Tresh was the guildmaster for the Most Excellent Order of Weavers and Dyers circa 1357 DR. She worked out of the House of Textiles, which was their headquarters on Nethpranter's Street in the Trades Ward.

Relationships

Tresh was also the matriarch of the Lanngolyn noble family. Her consort was Ormaes "Seamaster" Lanngolyn and their eldest daughter, Tryssia, was the designated heir.
