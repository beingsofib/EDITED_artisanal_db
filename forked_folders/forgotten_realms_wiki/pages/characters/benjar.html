Benjar was one of the Blades of the Old Man in the fortress of Sentinelspire. He was a part of the team accompanying Sauk to look for Kheil and secure the Erael'len in the Northern Shalhoond in Tarsakh of , conspiring to kill the Old Man of the Mountain. Benjar and Hama were Vaasans.
