Urbone was a troglodyte chieftain in Cormanthor.

Activities

Urbone was the leader of a troglodyte tribe that venerated Dretchroyaster.

History

In 1479 DR, Urbone was ready to destroy the heretical Cult of the Dragon cell led by Silakul when some adventurers infiltrated the lair of Dretchroyaster and they defeated Urbone.

Personality

Urbone and his tribe truly worshiped Dretchroyaster like a true god. Thus he greatly disliked Silakul and his fellows, who saw the dracolich only as an instrument of power. He ruled with cunning and patience.
