Xamus was an efreeti inhabiting Rog'osto in east Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Relationships

Xamus served the sha'ir Othmar bin Kaloth as his loyal genie companion.

Activities

Xamus possessed a shrewd eye for judging a person's character and true intentions, a skill that served his master well. A favorite pastime of Xamus' was collecting magical items from those whom his master had imprisoned. Xamus hoped to gather enough powerful items to impress a beautiful female noble efreeta back home in his native City of Brass.
