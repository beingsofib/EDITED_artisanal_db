Qaland Shurijah was the founder and first qaghan of the Igidujin tribe in the Plain of Horses region.

History

The pitiless Shurijah united the hsing (clans) of the Chigidi Mountains, after almost a century of deadly warring with one another and forged the Igidujin tribe. The cruel and despotic Qaland family reigned over the Igidujin ever since.
