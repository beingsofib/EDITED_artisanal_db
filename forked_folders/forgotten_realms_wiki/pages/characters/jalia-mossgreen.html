Jalia Mossgreen was the most prominent of the Swords of Archendale in 1368 DR.

Activities

As the Black Sword, Jalia supervised the Archendale militia. In public role she owned and managed the Old Stonebows inn.

History

In 1368 Jalia was the oldest of the Swords and was semi-retired from the role.
