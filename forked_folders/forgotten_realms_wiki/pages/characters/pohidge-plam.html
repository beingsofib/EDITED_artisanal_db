Pohidge Plam was the son of a mason in Hlath in Chondath in 1358 DR.

History

For fifteen years, Pohidge chased fortune as a fighter. In 1355 DR, he married and lived a happy domestic life.

In 1358 DR, Pohidge Plam was called to a meeting of prominent citizens gathered by Lord Fior Botpur to discuss the menace of a barbarian army from Kara-Tur. Baron Krondac proposed Pohidge serve as the main commander.

He took part in the defense of the city.

Personality

Pohidge was a good leader, loving his fellow citizens. He was also sensitive and proud of his name. Pohidge loyally followed the Lord's decisions, even if he disliked them.
