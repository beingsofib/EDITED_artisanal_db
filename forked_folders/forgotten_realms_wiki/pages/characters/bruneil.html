Bruneil was a human soldier of Fort Locke near the Mere of Dead Men on the Sword Coast North.

History

Bruneil was one of Fort Locke's newest soldiers (who did not want to become a merchant) that went with Tann and his men to patrol. However, they were captured by the shadow priest. Repko was cornered, as the room he was in was occupied by hostile undead. The Kalach-Cha and Tann could've rescued him.
