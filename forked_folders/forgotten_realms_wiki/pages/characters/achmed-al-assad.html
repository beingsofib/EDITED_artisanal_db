Achmed al-Assad was the father of Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir—the Grand Caliph of Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Activities

Achmed served as the Grand Caliph until his death.

Relationships

Achmed was the eldest brother of Prince Tannous and Prince Cheddah
