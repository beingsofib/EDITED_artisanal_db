Jalasta Kelelder was a skilled warrior inhabiting Suldolphor in the far eastern reaches of Calimshan in south Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

She was middle-aged as of the .

Activities

Jalasta was one of two High Swords who led the Flame Guard–an elite group of warriors who protected Suldolphor's ruling olanger.

History

She earned her stripes serving as a mercenary for many years in Tethyr and beyond.

Possessions

Jalasta wore and enchanted suit of armor and wielded a flame tongue sword.
