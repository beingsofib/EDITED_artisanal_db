Penelope Harpell was a member of the Harpell family and their most powerful member during the late 15 century DR.

Description

She had a spellscar on her side.

History

Penelope was the wife of Dowell Harpell, the family spokesman in 1479 DR. Penelope continued teaching magic to the reborn Catti-brie after she escaped from a Shadovar magic school. In 1484 DR, she was involved with all her family in the war started by drow associated with the Darkening.
