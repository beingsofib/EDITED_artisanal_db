Kaz Hanar was a high-ranking member of the Cult of the Howling Hatred at the Temple of Howling Hatred in Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

In 1491 DR, Kaz Hanar patrolled the plaza outside of the Temple of Howling Hatred in Tyar-Besil with his wyvern mount. While on patrol, he was confronted and defeated by some adventurers.
