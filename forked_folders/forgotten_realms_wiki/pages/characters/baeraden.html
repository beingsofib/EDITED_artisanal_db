Baeraden was one of Elaith Craulnober's men in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a man or a half-elf.

Possessions

He carried a hand crossbow.

Relationships

Baeraden numbered among Elaith Craulnober's men, and did his bidding.

History

In , when Elaith Craulnober followed Alustriel Silverhand into Windpennant Pillars, a number of his men, a mix of human and half-elf goons, trailed behind him for protection. Later, after Alustriel noticed Elaith and teleported away, Elaith directed a pithy remark at Baeraeden, that almost certainly being the name of one of the men with him.
