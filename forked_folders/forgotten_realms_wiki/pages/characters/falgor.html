Falgor was a male human living in Port Llast on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR.

History

Falgor was the innkeeper of Alliance Arms Inn in Port Llast.

Gameplay

The Kalach-Cha could talk with Falgor to learn about the people who visited his tavern.
