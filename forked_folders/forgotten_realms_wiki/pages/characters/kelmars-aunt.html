Kelmar's aunt, name unknown, was a sorceress of Zazesspur during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The decrepit woman had lost all her hair, and an eye had been reduced to a mass of burnt scar tissue. Her voice was a dry whisper.

Personality

Kelmar's aunt was cryptic, and avoided healers.

Abilities

She was known to carry a number of spells, including one that gleaned information out of enchanted objects' properties, and another that gleaned information from people present.

Possessions

She was known to carry charms that granted temporary magical enhancement to armaments. Her home was in the Carpets District of Zazesspur.

Relationships

Her sister was Kelmar's mother, and a greater necromancer than she was. She was not close to her nephew, Kelmar Dargren.

History

Kelmar's aunt had lost the top half of her home in a fire. When her nephew Kelmar became head of the family finances, he offered her a new chair, which she refused.

Kelmar visited her looking for aid in slaying the dragon Xexillidaulgrymm on the . Her spells revealed to her that he was using a suspicious amulet, which he admitted granted him insight into the dragon's attacks; she was suspicious, but gave him charms that would bolster his assassins' attacks.
