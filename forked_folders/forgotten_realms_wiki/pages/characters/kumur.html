Kumur the Skeptic was a sage and the First Speaker of Evernoster. Kumur was considered the most critical of his profession, as his nickname attested.

History

At some point prior to the mid–14 century DR, Kumur the Skeptic undertook a study of history and legendry surrounding the enchanted sword Namarra, and could not even estimate a likely period or locale for its origin, nor uncover any stories alluding to a time "before Namarra was forged".
