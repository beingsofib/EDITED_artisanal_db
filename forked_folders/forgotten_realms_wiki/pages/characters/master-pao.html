Master Pao

Person
nameMaster Pao
homeMount Yei, Spirit World

Master Pao was a Lesser Immortal of the Celestial Empire. Pao dwelled on Mount Yei in the Spirit World.

According to tradition, in ancient times, during the Age of Red Earth, Master Pao crafted the Ivory Chain of Pao. It was gifted to the Nine Emperors as a symbol of their unity and the harmony of the peoples of the empire they ruled jointly.
