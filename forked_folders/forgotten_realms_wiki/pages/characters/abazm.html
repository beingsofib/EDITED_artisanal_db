Abazm was a cultural guide and commercial liaison who operated throughout the Dragon Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He had greasy hair and dressed in a striped cloak.

Personality

Abazm did not offer up great first impressions, often appearing as shifty and untrustworthy, despite his attempted displays of charm and self-importance.

Abilities

Despite his personal shortcomings, Abazm was skilled as a social and commercial middleman.

Relationships

While Abazm had an ongoing working relationship with the Shou royals of the Ginger Palace, he publicly ran afoul of Princess Wei Dao by bringing to her a merchant that sold pearl dust that caused her develop a nasty rash. This was just a superficial gaffe however, as he actually served as Wei Dao's secret messenger, bringing her information for her spies outside the palace.

History

In the , Abazm managed to secure a meeting the novice Harpers agent Ruha, her ally Fowler who posed as her Bedine bodyguard, and the Shou royals of the Ginger Palace.
