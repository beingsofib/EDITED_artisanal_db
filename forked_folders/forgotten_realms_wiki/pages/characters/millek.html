Millek the Thrush was a halfling in the Monastery of the Precipice.

Activities

Millek was a thief who, in 1479 DR, traveled to Spellgard, seeking Lady Saharel's counsel to answer a simple question: where she could find a lost treasure?

Millek resisted the temptation of thieving from the most vulnerable seekers.
