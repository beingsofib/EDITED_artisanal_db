Erevain Blacksheaf was an elven adventurer from Evereska who journeyed to the Icewind Dale town of Easthaven.

Description

The gold-haired elf was of slender build and dressed in hunting leathers.

Personality

Erevain was a kind elf who felt a great desire to see all Faerûn and not take the Retreat to Evermeet.

Possessions

He wielded an enchanted broad sword that was originally forged by the Greycloaks of Evereska.

Relationships

He had a cousin named Xan, who himself was a member of the Greycloaks.

History

In Eleint of the , Erevain stayed at the Snowdrift Inn after traveling from Kuldahar in the Spine of the World. He was killed by spiders in Dragon's Eye later that year.
