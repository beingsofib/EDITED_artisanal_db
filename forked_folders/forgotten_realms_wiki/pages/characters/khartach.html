Khartach was a pit fiend member of Glasya's special guard.

History

On Hammer 10 of 1487 DR, Khartach was sent to Djerad Thymar by Glasya to lead a detachment of the Malbogean forces to help the dragonborn of Tymanther against Gilgeam's demon army, during the last battle of the First Tymanther-Unther War, and in doing so also to help Asmodeus to retain his divinity.
