Sister Cherra was one of the monks of the Monastery of the Precipice.

History

Cherra went from Waterdeep to the Monastery in 1473 DR in search of answers to the disappearance of her brother. She never met Lady Saharel and gave up hope. Instead, Cherra found a mission in helping other seekers, joining Allendi.

In 1479 DR, she took care of the operation of the monastery's distillery.
