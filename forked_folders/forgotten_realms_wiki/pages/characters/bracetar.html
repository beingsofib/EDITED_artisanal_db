Bracetar

Person
nameBracetar
occupationAuthor, scholar

Bracetar was an author and scholar writing in or before the 14 century DR.

History

By the 1370s DR, Bracetar's work Notes On Preservation of Foodstuffs and Oils was amongst the works in Candlekeep.

Rumors & Legends

In the , Noumea Cardellith, pretending to be a merchant from Lantan, claimed to be interested in Bracetar's Notes On Preservation of Foodstuffs and Oils when seeking admittance to Candlekeep.
