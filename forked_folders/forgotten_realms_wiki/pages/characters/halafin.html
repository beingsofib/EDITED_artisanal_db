Halafin was a wealthy merchant inhabiting the Shining South during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Halafin lived in the country of Estagund within the Shining Lands. He was a member of Chaka Seltarir and a member of the Shining Lands' ruling Council of Merchants.
