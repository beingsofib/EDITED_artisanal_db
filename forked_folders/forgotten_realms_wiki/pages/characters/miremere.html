Miremere was a massive mudman that dwelled in the muck of the Blooded Wood in the King's Forest during the late 15 century DR.

History

Miremere lived its mudman life in the swampy pools below Granwell Keep, unconcerned and moisturized while the Harpers and Thultanthar fought over the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll nearby.
