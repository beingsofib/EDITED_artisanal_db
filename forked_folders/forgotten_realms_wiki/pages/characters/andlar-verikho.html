Andlar Verikho was a merchant of Tharsult in the Shining Sea in the 14 century DR.

History

Andlar nicknamed the sage Imbryn Velsaert of Baldur's Gate the "Dragon Sage" for his expertise on dragons and the title stuck by the late 1360s DR.
