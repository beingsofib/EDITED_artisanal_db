Nyriana Greenglade was a elf priest of Amaunator in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Nyriana had long, straight gray hair, gray eyes, and light skin. She wore a neat suit of chain mail decorated with the symbology of Amaunator.

History

In the , Nyriana joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their forward camp outside the ruins of Myth Drannor. Nyriana sought to provide healing and respite for those who arrived at the camp and used it as a base for expeditions into the dangerous ruins, and set up a makeshift shrine to Amaunator in a tent.
