Eadyan Falconmoon was the priest of Chauntea of the small town of Greenest, located in the Western Heartlands in west Faerûn.

Personality

Eadyan remained calm even during the chaos of the attack.

History

During the battle with the Cult of the Dragon, Eadyan gathered as many people as possible inside the temple, one of the most durable buildings, in order to keep them safe from cultist raiders.
