Bonecruncher was a young and ill-tempered allosaurus who competed in the dinosaur races of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 century DR.

History

As of the start of the death curse, Bonecruncher was a favorite in the races, and bookies were giving it 2:1 odds of winning.
