Orrin Glass was a scribe and a member of the Avowed who lived in Candlekeep during the late 15 century DR.

Description

The elderly scholar was completely deaf in one ear.

Personality

Orrin was quite forgetful and became flustered very easily.

Activities

Orrin served as one of the Avowed Adjutants, guides that escorted visitors through Candlekeep's hallowed libraries.
