Lord Terxa was a Shadovar advisor that served the Most High Telamont Tanthul circa the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

He enjoyed the benefit of having the Most High's ear, and his perspective was as trusted as his contemporary Hadrhune.

History

In Mirtul of the , Lord Terxa convinced High Prince Telamont Tanthul to inform their shadow adept agent Galaeron Nihmedu about Shadovar provocation of the phaerimm beneath Myth Drannor, and that the creatures planned to strike back at Thultanthar's mythallar.
