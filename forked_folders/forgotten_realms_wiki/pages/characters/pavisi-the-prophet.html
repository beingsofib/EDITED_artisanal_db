Pavisi the Prophet

Person
namePavisi the Prophet
homeUtter East, southeast Faerûn

Pavisi the Prophet was a person in the Utter East.

Regarding the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR), Pavisi the Prophet remarked "Fate deserves the greatest acclaim. A victory loses its wonder when simple planning is given the credit." and "The Mage who has never known failure may be crushed with a single defeat."
