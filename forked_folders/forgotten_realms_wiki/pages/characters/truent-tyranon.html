Truent Tyranon was a seasoned Cormyrian warrior in Scardale in 1368 DR.

Activities

Truent led the Cormyrean garrison in the city. He found himself trusted in delicate diplomatic matters. The Cormyrean government was starting to consider a retreat but did not want to favor the Zhents by any means.
