Joliet was a fighter travelling alone in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

On a mission to prove his faith, he was forced to travel around on his own, although mages of the faith watched him closely.

Personality

A brash and impulsive young man, he was nicknamed the Rash, and his erratic behavior had members of the clergy question his devotion.

Possessions

On his journey he wielded his sword of wounding while wearing a set of plate mail and a pair of gauntlets of dexterity.
