Indrel was a priest of Bane.

Description

He wore a dark robe.

History

In late , Indrel was scrying Shandril Shessair together with a red wizard during her final immolation. Upon witnessing her death, Indrel revealed that portents from Bane, confirmed by the same portents from Shar, were that the coming year, the would be a peaceful year, adequate to regroup and rebuild their strength. The red wizard told him afterwards to tell Lord Bane that he was willing to give much himself for power such as spellfire.
