Wizards' Way

Wizards' Way was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 century DR.

Location

Wizards' Way was found within northwest Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. It ran from the wall separating the Inner Quarter from the Common Quarter in the south to Zhentil Keep's main exterior wall and Border Street in the north. Wizards' Way was crossed by Warrior Street, Fortress Street, and the Street of Delights along the way.

Notable Locations

The lavish Silver Trumpet Inn was located at the southern end of Wizards' Way.
