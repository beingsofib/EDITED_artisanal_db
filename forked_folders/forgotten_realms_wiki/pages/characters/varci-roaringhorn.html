Varci Roaringhorn was a child who lived in Baldur's Gate during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

While in Baldur's gate, Varci was under the care of Lord Priest Tremain Belde'ar. He was friends with Tremain's son, Casson.

History

In the , Varci and Casson snuck Water Queen's House to cause some mischief for the priestesses of Umberlee. Unfortunately, they were caught and Casson was killed for the transgression. His body was being kept by the the head priestess, Jalantha Mistmyr.

Troubled over the death of his friend, Varci went searching throughout the streets of Baldur's Gate for help to have him resurrected.
