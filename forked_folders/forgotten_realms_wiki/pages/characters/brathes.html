Brathes was the bodyguard of the new pharaoh of Mulhorand in 1357 DR.

Activities

Brathes was trying to act as a bridge between the new pharaoh and the clergy of Anhur.

Relationship

Brathes was a childhood friend of Horustep III. The clerics of Anhur heavily supported him.
