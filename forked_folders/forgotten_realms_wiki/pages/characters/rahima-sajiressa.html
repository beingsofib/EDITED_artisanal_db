Rahima Sajiressa was an astrologer in the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR. Fittingly, they worshiped the deity of fate and divination, Savras.

Rahima was an outgoing individual who loved to gamble. They were known to patronize the Elfsong Tavern in Baldur's Gate.
