Harrington Nethalin

Person
nameHarrington Nethalin
sexMale
occupationAuthor

Harrington Nethalin was the rather curmudgeonly man who penned the book series, Lessons for Sensible Living some time during or before the late 15 century DR.

Personality

He believed that folks of the Realms could live their best lives by making sensible choices, such as avoiding large cities and not taking unnecessary risks.
