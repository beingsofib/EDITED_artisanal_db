Kila Odani was an inhabitant of Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Activities

Kila was in charge of the spice warehouse of the House of Al-Farif within the Warehouse District of Huzuz. She portrayed pure genius in her ability to properly handle and recognize the plethora of spices that passed through the warehouse.
