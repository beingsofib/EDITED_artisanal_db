Thaeram Duskwinter was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

History

In , the Waterdhavian thief Narnra Shalace became a spy for the Royal Magician of Cormyr, Caladnei. Her first assignment was to spy on a number of War Wizards, including Thaeram Duskwinter.
