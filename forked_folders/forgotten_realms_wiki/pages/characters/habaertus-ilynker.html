Habaertus Ilynker was a farmer in the mid–8 century DR.

Activities

He worked at Torntlar's Farm, which involved a lot of hoeing in the stony soil.

Relationships

The guard Bretchimus at Felmorel was his older brother.

History

In the , Habaertus Ilynker was killed by the Slayer while he was working at Torntlar's Farm during a spring day, after swiping at the tinkling, misty entity with his hoe.
