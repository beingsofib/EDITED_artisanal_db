Shandra Mystahr was a mage and bloodforge mage in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

Mystahr was a wary leader but obsessed with discovering the magical secrets rumored to be hidden within Old Stone Keep. Mystahr marched there at the head of a bloodforge troop. However, Mystahr's rival, the leader of the Legendary Campaign, set an ambush here.
