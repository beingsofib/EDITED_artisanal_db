Safsaf was a vishap living in the Furrowed Mountains in 1367 DR.

Personality

Safsaf was a coward, preferring to observe travelers in her land well in advance of encountering them.

Activities

The holy slayers of the Everlasting bribed Safsaf to report any travelers found within her territory.
