Tum bint Nisr was a skilled hakima inhabiting Qudra in north Zakhara in .

Activities

Tum worked within Qudra's slave market where she could use her ability to see the truth in people to ensure that none of the slaves were held there against their or will, or were actually free men or women.

Relationships

Tum's twin sister, Tau'am, was also a hakima and worked alongside her.
