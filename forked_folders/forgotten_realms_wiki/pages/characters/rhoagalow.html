Rhoagalow was a guard captain in Nethrar during the mid–8 century DR.

Relationships

He generally brought three men as his retinue.

History

During the , an exhausted Rhoagalow reported at least two murders to the seneschal of Galadorna, Elminster Aumar. One of those murders, the one of the merchant Anlavas Jhoavryn, he believed to be the work of Limmator. After telling Elminster of a knifing whose victim might live, he received from the seneschal a tankard of Burdym's Best, which he was told to drink in private.
