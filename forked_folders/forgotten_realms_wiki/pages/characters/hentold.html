Hentold was a grave robber who lived on the Sword Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the , Hentold traveled to the Valley of the Tombs and broke into one of the crypts. He stole the Heart of the Golem from one of the tombs within, in the process awakening a revenant of the man once killed by the enchanted dagger. After his grave mistake, Hentold was unable to get restful sleep, believing he would only rest again with the return of the dagger.
