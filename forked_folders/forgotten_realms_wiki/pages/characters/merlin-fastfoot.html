Merlin Fastfoot was a halfling rogue in Cormyr during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Merlin had sandy blond hair and green eyes.

History

Merlin was encamped at the ruins of Beckside Castle on the High Road around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar and Shadovar invasion of Wheloon. He passed the time with the bard Lunasa Eveningwood, singing songs together including The Mermaid, The Crafty Maid's Policy, and Here's a Health to the Company.
