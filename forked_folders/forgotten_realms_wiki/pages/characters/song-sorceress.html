Song, the self-described Mistress of Storms, was a mad sorceress and mercenary who worked in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Song had pale skin and long brown hair. She wore a simple outfit, and carried the wizard's ward to enhance her powers.

Personality

Song was extremely childish and didn't fully understand the consequences of her actions or reality in general, despite being a powerful sorceress, and titled herself the "Mistress of Storms" on a whim. She was erratic in nature and maintained a cheery and innocent mood while engaging in violence, and didn't fully understand the concept of death. While this could have been partly due to being young for an elf, it was ascribed to a form of madness that dominated her thoughts and actions.

Abilities

Though hardly a master of the elements, Song could call upon many destructive spells, including ball lightning, cyclonic blast, delayed blast fireball, disintegrate, horrid wilting, meteor swarm, and polar ray.

Relationships

Song's oldest friend was Dom, and followed his lead without really caring or understanding what was really going on.

History

Song was a longtime friend of Dom since at least one of their childhoods, and together they traveled and fought however Dom wished. They were eventually hired by Thultanthar in the mid-1480s DR to recover a piece of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll from Cormyr, ambushing Bensen Virandson on Greenweald Lane. Despite her magical powers and many allied mercenaries, Song perished in battle, though she didn't seem to quite understand what that meant.
