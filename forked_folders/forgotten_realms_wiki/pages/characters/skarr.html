Skarr was a hobgoblin chieftain that led a part of the Blood Guard within the Hordes of Dragonspear army, during the Second Dragonspear War of the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Skarr was considered a strong leader that commanded loyalty and discipline among his soldiers.

History

Skarr's hobgoblins fought in the final battle of the Second Dragonspear War at Dragonspear Castle.
