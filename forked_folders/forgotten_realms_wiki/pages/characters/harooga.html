Harooga was the greater spirit of the Celestial Bureaucracy governing the island of Akari in the Prioto Island of Kara-Tur. She was the most powerful spirit of all the Prioto Islands.

History

She was captured by the evil Opawang and trapped in a pocket dimension.

In , however, an adventuring party hired by governor Ko Lei Kho to destroy the Ochimo also fought and killed the Opawang, freeing Harooga, who quickly destroyed the lair of the Opawang.
