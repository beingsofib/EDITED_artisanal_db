Kemil Abd al-Jasir was a general and commander of the Dauntless Mamluk Society in Qudra in 1367 DR.

Activities

Kemil usually followed the lead of senior members of the military council since he was only a minor member. Any group or individual supporting exploration or other goals of the Dauntless usually had his vote.
