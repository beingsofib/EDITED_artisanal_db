Constal was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

History

Constal was a part of the retinue of the Royal Magician of Cormyr, Caladnei in early spring of , when they raided the Marsember meeting of the Rightful Conspiracy. He was assigned afterwards to hunt down Regal Lady Mistwind who'd picked the location for her nightly manhunt, but to let her escape in the end.
