Kayid al-Azzazi

Person
nameKayid al-Azzazi
homeHuzuz, Zakhara

Kayid al-Azzazi was the chief tax collector for Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Relationships

Kayid was a nephew of the Grand Vizier, Alyana al-Azzazi.
