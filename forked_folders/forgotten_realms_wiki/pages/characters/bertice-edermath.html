Bertice Edermath was a young half-elf woman living in Phandalin in the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

Her father was Daran Edermath, a retired member of the Order of the Gauntlet who maintained Edermath Orchard in Phandalin.

She was in love with Grovet, a human miner who was her same age.

History

Bertice was born in the .

When she was twenty years old in the , she ran away from home with Grovet without telling her father, although it was an open secret in town. The young lovers headed south to Waterdeep along the High Road, intent on starting a new life together.
