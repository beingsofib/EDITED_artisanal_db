Sanamun was a sage inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the late–14 century DR.

Activities

Sanamun specialized in the history of the Moonsea reagion with special emphasis on Zhentil Keep, Hillsfar, and Phlan. He enjoyed playing competitive chess in the City Square in the Foreign Quarter for 1 gp per game.
