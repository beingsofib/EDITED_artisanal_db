Narm was a large half-orc warrior and the leader of the Blue Wyverns adventuring company of Ravens Bluff in the Vast in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Narm agreed that the Blue Wyverns would help Jack Ravenwild recover a tome of shadow magic, the Sarkonagael, from Sarbreen in exchange for 500 gold pieces per member of the company.
