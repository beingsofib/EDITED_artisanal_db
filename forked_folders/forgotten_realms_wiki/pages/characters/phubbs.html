Phubbs was one of the employees of Lady Jannath's Estate in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa the , Chamberlain Mercer, the man in charge of Lady Firelia Jannath's staff, dismissed all servants from the mansion on the mistress's orders. Mercer placed Phubbs, who was assigned to be a guest greeter, Furbleston, Bailey, Cyrilla, Jooli, and Chef Marjoram on temporary leave with the pay of half-wages and allowance to seek temporary employment somewhere else. The chamberlain issued a statement that this dismissal was done at no imputation of malfeasance and would be ended upon notice.
