Dahrgash

Dahrgash was the savage leader of the orcs who served in the Blood Guard contingent of the Dragonspear army, during the Second Dragonspear War of the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Dahrgash was a ferocious and aggressive personality who kept his fellow orcs working as a unit, despite their constant bickering and strife.

History

Dahrgash led the orcs in the final battle of the Second Dragonspear War at Dragonspear Castle.
