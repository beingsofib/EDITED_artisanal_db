Yassad Meloura was a trader from the Demiplane of Dread. He cursed his wife with his dying breath.

Relationships

He was married to Annicole.

History

A trader, Yassad Meloura returned from a long journey for work one night to find his wife, Annicole, with her lover. He and the lover fought and killed each other; as he died, Yassad pronounced a dying curse on his wife that turned her into a werespider.
