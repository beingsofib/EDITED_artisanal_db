Gardush was a powerful warrior who became a servant of the Bhaalspawn Sarevok in the mid−14 century DR.

Abilities

He possessed exceptional strength.

Activities

Gardush traveled with several similarly-aligned companions in Naaman, Aasim, Diyab, Alai, and Zhalimar Cloudwulfe, the group's leader. While acting under the authority of the Iron Throne, the worked to advance Sarevok's agenda in Baldur's Gate and the Sword Coast.

Possessions

In battle, Gardush wore a suite of plate mail armor and wielded a long sword and shield.

History

In the , Gardush and his companions were sent to Baldur's Gate by the Iron Throne to act as advisors to Thaldorn, to aid in his negotiations with Emissary Tar and the Baldurian Council of Four. Unfortunately for the group, Sarevok's half-brother Abdel Adrian and his companions intercepted them in the Iron Throne headquarters and prevented the meeting from occurring.
