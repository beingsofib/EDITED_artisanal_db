Dazar was a human fighter from the Hordelands, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a noyan, a member of the ruling class, and therefore able to serve his tribe as a commander of their cavalry.

Personality

A somewhat quiet man, he nevertheless commanded a great deal of respect as he was able to come to fair decisions quickly. He cared deeply for his tribesmen and, it was said, would sacrifice his own life to save theirs.

Possessions

Mounted on horseback, he wore just light leather armor and a ring of sustenance, while wielding an enchanted bow and carrying several quivers of arrows, one of which contained arrows enchanted against the undead.
