Bobbish Brankstone was a man who lived along the High Road of Cormyr between Tyrluk and Eveningstar in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Circa 1485 DR, Bobbish was an elderly man with white hair.

History

In the mid-1480s DR, Bobbish was a local who lived near Beckside Castle, and used the bridge over the Starwater tributary as a fishing spot. He was unperturbed by the drama occurring nearby.
