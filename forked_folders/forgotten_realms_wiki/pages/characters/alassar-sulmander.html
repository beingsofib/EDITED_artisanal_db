Alassar Sulmander was a child servant at the keep at Caer-Dineval during the 1490s DR.

Activities

He carried out lowly tasks at the keep for its devil-worshiping cultist residents.

History

In the , Alassar hid himself in a load of supplies headed for the Caer-Dineval keep and snuck his way into the castle. He was taken in by the Knights of the Black Sword as a sort of menial servant.
