Krondorl Waeglossz was the head of the College of Invocation and one of the members of the Conclave of Sshamath in the city of Sshamath in the year 1372 DR.

History

In 1379 DR, he participated in the debate about elevating the College of Ancient Arcana or the College of Bae'qeshel Magic as the new eleventh official college of the city.
