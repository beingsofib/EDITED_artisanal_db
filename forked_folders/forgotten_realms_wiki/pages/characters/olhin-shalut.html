Olhin Shalut was a tavern owner inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Olhin was a cocky old man.

Activities

He owned the Ship's Wheel tavern located in the western section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward on the north side of Diamond Street where it joined Westwall Street near the West Gate.

Olhin used to be an adventurer. He wisely invested his money to make him wealthy upon his retirement from the adventurer's life.

Possessions

He owned a slew of magic items including a ring of blinking, ring of protection, rod of lordly might, iron bands of Bilarro, a shortsword of quickness, and several more.
