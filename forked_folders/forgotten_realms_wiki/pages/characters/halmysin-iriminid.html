Halmysin Iriminid was an elf of Eveningstar in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

She had long auburn hair tied back in a large ponytail, gray eyes, and a pale skin tone. She wore a fashionable long black dress and long gloves.

History

Halmysin lived in Eveningstar during the time of the Sschindylryn invasion, and strongly preferred the times before rampaging arsonous drow.
