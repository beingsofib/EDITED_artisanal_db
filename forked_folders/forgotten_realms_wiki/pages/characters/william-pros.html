William (Pros)

nameWilliam
homePros, Dragon Coast
sexMale

William was a barge pilot who lived in the coastal town of Pros during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He had leathery skin and gray hair that was left messy.

Activities

Like other men in Pros, William made his living transporting people to and from their ships to the city's meager pier.
