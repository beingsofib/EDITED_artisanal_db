Hatar was a sorcerer who served in the court of Tajar in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara in .

Activities

Hatar discovered that Riqqiyah was actually Sultan Kori al-Zafiri, Tajar's previous ruler. He intended to use this information to his advantage.

History

Hatar was originally from I'tiraf across the Golden Gulf.

Relationships

He was served by a loyal dao who would perform any command given by Hatar.
