Krug was a goblin devotee of the Absolute who lived in the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

Description

He wore the attire commonly worn by goblin booyaghs and warlocks.

History

Krug was among the cultists of the Absolute who raided Waukeen's Rest in the . During the assault, he fell and cut his neck on a dagger. He subsequently died from his wounds.
