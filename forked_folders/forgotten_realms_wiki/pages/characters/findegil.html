Findegil was the butler of Andeth Ilchammar, mayor of Selgaunt, Sembia, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

In the , Findegil made a suggestion to Andeth Ilchammar as to how to deflate the electoral prospects of Sturge Whitsunday, a young member of Selgaunt's Diplomatic Corps, whose popularity rivaled his own after a successful renegotiation with the Elven Court: sending the man on a fool's errand to find Myth Nantar and enter a mutual defense agreement with them would probably make him less of a rival. Mayor Andeth agreed to the plan.
