Isyio was a wizard in Teziir in 1359 DR.

History

Once Isyio was an apprentice of the Simbul but after he embarrassed her, the Simbul sent him away in order to mature.

In 1359 DR, he was in Teziir doing magical research and teaching magic to the children of the Teziir City Council's members. He was still hurt by the Simbul's words and his goal was to prove his sincerity and go back to Aglarond.
