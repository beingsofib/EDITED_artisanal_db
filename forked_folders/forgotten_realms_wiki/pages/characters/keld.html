Keld was a stone giant carver at the Lodge of Faces in the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa 1486 DR, Keld and all his fellows succumbed to the faerzress-infected madness caused by the Purple Statue. Keld became very impatient, which was a very strange behavior for a stone giant.
