Rockbottom was a dwarf cleric. He was part of an expedition that accompanied the Companions of the Hall to Shallows in 1370 DR, only to be attacked by orcs and frost giants, and did what he could to heal Withegroo Seian'Doo and Bruenor Battlehammer.
