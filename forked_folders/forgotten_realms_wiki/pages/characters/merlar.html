Merlar was a highknight of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Per the intelligence Storm Silverhand had, Merlar was a widely liked, amiable youngling.

Relationships

He had been trained by Ilvellund Nordroun, who was also his team leader in his last raid. Other members of his team were Daerlund, a fellow highknight, and the War Wizard Hondryn.

History

Merlar participated in the raid on Tethgard on Mirtul 5 in the after the fugitive Elminster Aumar stole the Royal Gorget of Battle from the Royal Palace in Suzail.

He was not named among the known survivors later.
