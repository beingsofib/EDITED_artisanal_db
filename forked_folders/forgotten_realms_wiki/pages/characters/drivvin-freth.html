Drivvin Freth was the drow archmage of House Freth inside Undermountain in the early 1490s DR.

Activities

Drivvin was the main advisor of his sister Erelal Freth in her plans to conquer all Undermountain, especially against House Auvryndar. Usually he resided inside Spiderwatch Keep, the main Freth headquarters in the Maze Level with his sister.

History

Around the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR after a series of very devastating battles, Drivvin was busy with devising a way to summon a goristo in order to impress the minotaurs that were enemies of House Freth, by convincing them that he had summoned an envoy of Baphomet and they must obey him. However he knew he did not have the magical abilities to succeed alone. Tendra Nightblade and Mateen Shadowdusk offered him the help of Halaster Blackcloak himself in the matter in exchange for Drivvin becoming his next assistant. Drivvin was conflicted because he didn't want abandon his House to go with Halaster.
