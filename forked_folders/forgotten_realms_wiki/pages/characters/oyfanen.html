Oyfanen was a dryad living near Julkoun around 1485 DR. Oyfanen was the spirit of an oak tree.

History

Around 1486 DR, Oyfanen was greatly concerned with the invasion of the village by goblinoids in service to one of the Red Wizards of Thay.
