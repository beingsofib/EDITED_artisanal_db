Oulam was a necromancer that lived in the Baldur's Gate region of the Sword Coast for a time during the mid–14 century DR.

Possesions

He wore a robe of electrical resistnace, and carried with him a handful darts of stunning.

History

In the , Oulam joined with the Zhentarim agent Niemain, along with two other mages, Wheber Ott and Willaim Garst, to cast an especially difficult spell on the second floor of Sorcerous Sundries in Baldur's Gate.
