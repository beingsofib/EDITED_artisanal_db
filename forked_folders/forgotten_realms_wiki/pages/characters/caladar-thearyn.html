Caladar Thearyn was a magelord from Hastarl in the early 3 century DR.

Personality

He displayed some sadism.

Abilities

He could cast chain lightning, and another spell that allowed him to fire that spell to locales he was scrying on, across great distances.

Possessions

He possessed some sort of enchanted pendant.

Relationships

He was among the Magelords of Athalantar.

History

In , Caladar Thearyn was part of a force of armsmen trying to root out the Knights of Athalantar that had turned to banditry rather than serve King Belaur Aumar. He attempted to slay a number of them with a chain lightning hurled remotely, but was distracted by a crossbow bolt at the right moment.

He pursued Eladar, one of the bandits who was actually Elminster Aumar, a prince, and caught him asleep at the Woodsedge Inn, where he nearly blinded him with a hay fork. Caladar wanted him alive should they need a heir to the throne, but he was hacked apart from behind by the innkeeper, Broarn, who was secretly against the Magelords himself.
