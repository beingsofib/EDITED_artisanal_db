Phandalyn was a paladin who lived in Baldur's Gate during the mid−14 century DR.

Personality

Phandalyn was extremely judgmental. He would freely comment upon the moral outlook of passers-by. If Phandalyn sensed evilness in anyone, he would readily attack them.

Activities

He frequented a rather unremarkable inn found in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate.

Possessions

Phandalyn owned and wielded a +1 enchanted spear.
