Allanna was a river dweller tam'hi sorceress from the jungles of the Malatran Plateau active in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Allanna was known for her peaceful nature and ability to be a successful peacemaker.

History

In the late 14 century DR, the Tribe of the Wise Ones became aware of a looming threat. The leader of the dreaded spider katanga, the Mother of a Thousand Young was swollen with a thousand children ready to overflow the jungles. In preparation for the birthing, she initiated a ritual that sent deadly poisons into the rivers cascading off the Fire Mountain. Allanna was among the younger and less experienced heroes of the Living Jungle who answered the call. Allanna's group was sent to enter the spider katanga caverns and faced hunting spider katangas who guarded previously captured adventurers.

It was unknown if Allanna survived the battle, but the Mother of a Thousand Young was defeated, and heroes returned to the Village of the Wise Ones for their reward and facilitated the clean-up and nature's healing.
