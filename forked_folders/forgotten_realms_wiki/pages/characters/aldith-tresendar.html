Aldith Tresendar, also known as the Black Hawk, was a knight of great repute in the 10 century DR.

Possessions

The knight owned Tresendar Manor, a large house to the east of Phandalin. He was also in possession of the enchanted longsword Talon.

History

When Phandalin was raided in the , Aldith descended into the caverns beneath his home to fight off the orcs. Unfortunately, he died in this encounter, and his longsword was lost in the cavern complex.
