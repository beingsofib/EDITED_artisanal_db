Crazyface was an adventuring cleric of foul morals who worked with Shagbag and, at one time, with the dwarf berserker Korgan Bloodaxe in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Crazyface was a pale-skinned human male with a head of blonde hair. He wore no armor and was armed with a simple quarterstaff.

Abilities

As a cleric, Crazyface had a variety of divine magic spells at his disposal, including slay living,righteous magic, draw upon holy might, animate dead, and mental domination, among others.

History

In the mid-to-late 14 century DR, Crazyface was part of an adventuring band that consisted of Shagbag, Korgan Bloodaxe, Scrooloose, Stitch, Hogbelly, and Cutter. The band collected valuable tomes for Pimlico of Athkatla in Amn. One day, the group erupted into violence over an especially prized bounty, ending the lives of Hogbelly, Stitch, and Cutter and leading to Korgan parting ways with the group. In the , Crazyface accompanied Shagbag and Scrooloose in confronting the old ally over the Book of Kaza the dwarf was searching. The outcome of the confrontation was unknown; however, all three lives were said to had been cut down by the dwarven berserker and his allies atop the Copper Coronet in Athkatla.
