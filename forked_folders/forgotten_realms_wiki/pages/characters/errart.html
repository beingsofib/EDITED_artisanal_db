Errart was a guard at Castle Felmorel in the mid–8 century DR.

Abilities

He could wield a sword.

Relationships

He was a servant of Lord Esbre Felmorel. As a guard, his fellows included Glavyn, Feiryn, and Bretchimus. To Lady Nasmaerae Felmorel, he was an old, trustworthy man.

History

After the god Azuth put Lady Nasmaerae Felmorel under warning for trying to plunder the mind of one of her guests, Wanlorn in early spring of the , Lord Esbre Felmorel, her husband, came to her with three men-at-arms, and forced her to cast the spell she'd been using to enthrall him again, which Azuth had warned would result in her being forever shorn of her magic. One of those guards was Errart.
