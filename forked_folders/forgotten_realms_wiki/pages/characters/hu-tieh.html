Hu Tieh was a spirit folk monk in Shou Lung in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Hu Tieh was a student of the Dragonfly style of kung fu, his spirit folk heritage helping him learn it. He was in search of a master of that martial art in order to learn the final technique.

Personality

Hu Tieh was a shy and quiet individual and often people underestimated him.
