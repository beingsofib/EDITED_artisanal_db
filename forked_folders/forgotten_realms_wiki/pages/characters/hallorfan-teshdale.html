Hallorfan Teshdale was a male human and one of the four innkeepers of the Rapids inn located in Teshwave in 1367 DR.

He was the original proprietor before the arrival of the Zhentilar. Hallorfan was resigned to living under Zhent rule as he had no place to escape to and, in his late 60s, was too old to fight.
