Audamar Corwin was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company during the 14 century DR.

Relationships

Audamar had a daughter Schael, who followed in his footsteps and became a captain within the Flaming Fist. His granddaughter was named Rohma.

History

Audamar's patience was often tested when Schael was a young woman. Despite her wild ways, he was able to instill in her a strong sense of morality and duty.

In his elder years, he was supported by his Schael and spent his time looking after Rohma.
