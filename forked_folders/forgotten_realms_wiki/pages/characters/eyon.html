Eyon was a member of the Cult of the Crushing Wave at the Temple of the Crushing Wave in Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

Eyon was a callous mercenary who joined the Cult of the Crushing Wave. In 1491 DR, along with some other cultists and her shark mount, she controlled the lake landing near the temple until she and her minions were confronted and defeated by some adventurers.
