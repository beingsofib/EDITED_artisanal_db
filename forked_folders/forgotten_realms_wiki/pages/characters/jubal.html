Doctor Jubal was a businessman in the Moonsea in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Doctor Jubal managed the wandering Doctor Jubal's Carnival of Curiosities.

History

Doctor Jubal had managed his carnival for almost ten years. At one point, he bought a strange mirror maze in Waterdeep.Circa 1486 DR, he hired the doppelganger Tak, promising to help her against the Great Law of Humanity in Hillsfar. However, he did not truly care about it and when Tak discovered this, she subdued Jubal and took his position.

Rumors

Some said that Jubal was a wizard from Hillsfar who had lost his magic or else a member of the Red Plumes.
