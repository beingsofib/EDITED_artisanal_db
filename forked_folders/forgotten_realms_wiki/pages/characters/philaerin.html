Philaerin was a moon elf and the Eldest of the Circle of Reilloch Domayr. He resided in Tower Reilloch on the island of Evermeet.

Description

Despite Philaerin's age, his face was smooth and unlined.

History

Philaerin was slain in early 1374 DR when Tower Reilloch was attacked. He died protecting a vault in the Tower known as Nandiyerron's Armory.
