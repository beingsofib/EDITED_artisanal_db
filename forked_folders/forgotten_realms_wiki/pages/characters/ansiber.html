Abilities

He was of Brother of the Striking Hand rank, or greater.

Relationships

His name was known to Fzoul Chembryl, who considered him to be Brother of the Striking Hand rank or greater.

History

After Shandril Shessair's first escape from the Citadel of the Raven, Fzoul ordered all priests higher in rank than Brother of the Striking Hand to sweep the Citadel, in order to find her, promising instant ascension to the Inner Circle for whoever found her.
