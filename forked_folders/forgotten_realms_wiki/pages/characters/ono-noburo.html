Ono Noburo was a yakuza ninja and oyabun (leader) of the Ono Kumi clan in Kozakura circa .

History

In 1433, Noburo was discreetly contacted by a local lord on behalf of his brother-in-law, Lord Korimori, a daimyo of Maeshi Province far to the north. Korimori sought the Ono Kumi's services in seizing the Seven Swords, a set of relics being recovered by a band of adventurers for Korimori's rival daimyo, Michimori Uemon, and he proposed they intercept the adventurers whilst they stayed at Doi No Fugu—an inn under Imperial protection, and a permanent sentence of death for any who violated it. Despite this risk, Noburo accepted for the chance to gain the support of the two lords and because he had the perfect man for the job: his lieutenant Ono Arieki, his real name Sumitomo Yoichi, was a member of the Sumitomo family that ran the inn and had grown up there. Noburo ordered Arieki to go back to Doi No Fugu and to seize control of the place he knew so well. Once he'd done so, he would call in a second group and take the Seven Swords from the adventuring party that would shortly arrive. Noburo told him that none of the Sumitomos were to be harmed, and Yoichi, although disturbed, reluctantly accepted. Unbeknownst to him, Noburo ordered Yoichi's lieutenant, Ono Chomei, to kill all the people inside the inn in order to cover their trail, and Yoichi too if he caused trouble.
