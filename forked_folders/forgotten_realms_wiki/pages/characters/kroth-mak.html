Kroth Mak was a Cormyrean arms merchant who worked in Eveningstar in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Kroth was a big half-orc man with grayish skin and black hair with a beard.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Kroth sold potions, Cormyrian armors, and weapons to those that defended the village. He could also provide the adamantine cloak of the bear, dragon, and wolf.
