Cosmas Indicopleustes was a notable merchant traveling the Hordelands in 1358 DR.

Personality

Cosmas was honest, but didn't let that particular trait impede his craftiness.

Appearance

Because of his obese frame, Cosmas was constantly sweaty.

Activities

Cosmas invested heavily in a particular caravan, hoping his gamble would pay handsomely. Since he borrowed much of the gold needed to fund his expedition, he worried about anything interfering with the caravan's success.

History

Cosmas was originally from Impiltur.
