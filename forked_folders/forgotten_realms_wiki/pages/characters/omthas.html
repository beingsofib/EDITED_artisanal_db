Omthas was a well-known Prophet of Lathander who was active in the 8 century DR.

History

Omthas was a religious figure renowned by laypeople and the clergy of the Church of Lathander of the Realms. The prophet was considered dead as nothing had been heard about him since the 8 century DR. As of the mid-14 century DR, "Beard of Omthas!" or "by the Beard of Omthas!", or simply "Beard!" were exclamations used by clerics of the church that expressed profane amazement.
