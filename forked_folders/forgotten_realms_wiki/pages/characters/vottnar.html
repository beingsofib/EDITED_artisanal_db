Guardian Vottnar was the cleric of The Watcher, in charge of the Temple of Helm in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Vottnar was a plate armor-clad human male with pale skin. He wore an open-faced horned helmet and was armed with a mace and a shield.

History

In the late 14 century DR, Guardian Vottnar could be found running a small temple dedicated to Helm in Athkatla's Bridge District. He offered services of divine magic and magic potions in exchange for a donation to the Church of Helm. Vottnar also had a small selection of magic scrolls of protection and wooden stakes.
