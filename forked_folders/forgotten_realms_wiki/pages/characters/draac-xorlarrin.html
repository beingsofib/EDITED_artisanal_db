Draac Xorlarrin was a member of House Xorlarrin and an ally of the fire giant Duke Zalto sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

History

Once Draac lived in Q'Xorlarrin.

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, House Xorlarrin allied with the leader of the fire giants, Duke Zalto. Under Zalto's orders, Draac, together with his younger cousin Taal Xorlarrin, led an expedition back to Gauntlgrym using secret tunnels in order to capture the primordial Maegera inside an iron flask and deliver it to Zalto in his fortress of Ironslag.

Personality

Draac was a slippery individual. He did not want to die under any circumstance.
