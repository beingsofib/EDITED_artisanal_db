Bantu was a human pirate sailing the Sea of Fallen Stars sometime in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Bantu was a simple man, not inherently bad, who hoped his crewmates would follow his positive example. However, those selfsame crewmates often made him do immoral things by either fooling him or tempting him with alcohol, knowing he could not resist its lure.

Possessions

He wore a pair of bracers of defense, while dual-wielding a cutlass and a belaying pin.
