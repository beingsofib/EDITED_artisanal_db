Sareene was a Harper during the late 14 century DR.

History

Sareene participated in a meeting alongside Brammagar and others in the , where they discussed their options regarding Vangerdahast's coming scheme to trap and bind dragons to service. Sareene was skeptical of Brammagar's proposal; unknown to them, they were eavesdropped on by Narnra Shalace.
