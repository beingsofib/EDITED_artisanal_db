Jezzy Topps was a smuggler who worked with the infamous Guild in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Jezzy was a dark-skinned human male with dark brown facial hair. He wore a hooded tunic of green and carried a greatclub.

History

In the , Jezzy worked for a gruff hin smuggler named Big Huido, transporting weapons, drugs, and firewine into Baldur's Gate. The shipments were well defended by Jezzy and the rest of the crew – all there released into the hands of the members of the Guild only. One such shipment during the Absolute crisis was a chest containing essence of ether, dream mist, silkroot, and terazul – all illegal substances in the city.
