Mulsantir Illistine II was the second King of the Illistine Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate.

Activities

Mulsantir II inherited the throne when his father, King Mulsantir I, died in the . He ruled until the , when he was slain by a wizard of the Shoon Imperium called Myntharan "the Magus" who claimed the throne and ended the Illistine bloodline in Westgate.
