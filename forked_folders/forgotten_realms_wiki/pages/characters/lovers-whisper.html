Lover's Whisper was a shadar-kai who was part of a group of servants of the Raven Queen in the shadow-cursed lands of Faerûn during the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Of Thine Will led He Who Was and Lover's Whisper into the lands rich with darkness and tragedy. The malignant curse that twisted the shadow-cursed lands into the mockery of darkness and evil was too strong even for the Shadowfell's shadar-kai. They witnessed the land's darkness, making Lover's Whisper strangle her own raven, and the Raven Queen ordered their retreat. Despite Of Thine Will's protestations, He Who Was stayed behind to punish those souls who deserved a post-mortem judgement.
