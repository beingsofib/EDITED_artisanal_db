Yerian was the head necrophant working with the Cult of the Dragon in the Chantry of St. Sammaster as of .

Appearance

He was in his late middle age, though he often kept corpse visage active.

Personality

He knew to strike first and ask questions later when under assault.

Abilities

He was exceptional in his agility, and quite resilient, in addition to his common necrophant abilities. Of note, Velsharoon's death pact was active upon him.

Possessions

Yerian possessed a Staff of the serpent and a robe of defense as protective as scale mail.

Relationships

He was the High Necrophant of the Velsharan faithful in the Chantry of St. Sammaster. Jagdish was one of his underlings.

History

In , a force of necrophants from the Crypt of the Arisen Army came to the Vale of the Dragon Oracle to join with the forces of the Cult of the Dragon. After building the Chantry of St. Sammaster, a part of the Cult moved out to lure in the red dragon Nevalarich; during their absence, the temple was attacked by Zhentarim. Through the hostilities, the temple's high Necrophant, Yerian, remained on the task of reassembling the skeleton of the red dragon Aghazstamn.
