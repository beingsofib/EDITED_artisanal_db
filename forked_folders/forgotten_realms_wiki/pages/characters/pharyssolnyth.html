Pharyssolnyth was a female wyrm silver dragon of the 16th century before Dalereckoning. She was the author of the Tablets of Pharyssolnyth.

History

Pharyssolnyth dwelled in the elven city of Yrlaphon, among the eastern woodlands of Cormanthor, in its first century after its founding in (prior to ).

There she wrote the Tablets of Pharyssolnyth, which detailed how dragons learned and handled magic.

Pharyssolnyth was deceased well before 1373 DR.
