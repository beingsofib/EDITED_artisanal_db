Kimigha Poji was a crime-fighting monk from the port town of Nyanzaru in Chult, active sometime before the late 15 century DR.

History

Kimigha was known for the monk fighting stances – narrow and swift. The monk was among several other fighters who opposed the Headless Serpent, Secret Venom gang of Port Nyanzaru some years before the . Each of the monks were a set of enchanted clothing known as The Mighty Cloth.
