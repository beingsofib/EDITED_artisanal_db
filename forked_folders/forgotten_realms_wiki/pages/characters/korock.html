Korock was an orc warrior during the late 15th century DR.

History

In 1484 DR, during the orc and drow assault on the Silver Marches, Korock was guiding a contingent of troops. Regis used the name of this orc in order to deceive the shamans Kllug and Innanig, who were marching toward Nesmé. He said that Korock had angered their drow allies.
