Akonhorus I was the eleventh pharaoh of Mulhorand.

History

Akonhorus I was born in 919 DR. He ascended to the throne of pharaoh of Mulhorand in 929 DR and his reign lasted until 955 DR.

Later in life, Akonhorus I would die in 976 DR during the Rising of the Orcgates.
