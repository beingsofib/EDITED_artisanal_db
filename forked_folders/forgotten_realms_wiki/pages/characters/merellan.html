Merellan was a veteran amongst the Blades of the Old Man. On the night of Tarsakh 25, , he met up with Valmir at the door of the Tower of the Sun in Sentinelspire. He relayed the situation, including that Talieth had been trapped by the vines, and that the vines were animate.
