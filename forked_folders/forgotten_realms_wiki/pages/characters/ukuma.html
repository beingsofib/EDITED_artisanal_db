Ukuma was a giant walrus who dwelt in the fortress of Grimskalle on the island of Solstice in the Frozenfar in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Ukuma was granted intelligence and speech through a druidic awaken spell. It was not aggressive and did not attack unless provoked, instead spending its days rolling around on the ice and making small talk with anyone who approached. Those who offered fresh fish or other tasty morsels could easily earn its favor (and some valuable information about Grimskalle's layout).

History

Originally awakened and brought to Grimskalle by a group of frost druids, Ukuma was left in the central chamber of the lower level to serve as a guardian as of the winter of the . However, Ukuma had no real interest in guarding anything.
