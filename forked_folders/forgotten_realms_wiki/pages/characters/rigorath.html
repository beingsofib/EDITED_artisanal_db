Rigorath was an cambion of the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Rigorath was one of the many emissaries who, under orders of his master Mephistopheles, tried using the power of the Mirror of Mephistar to bargain an infernal pact with travelers in Avernus. Rigorath's offered knowledge in exchanged for part of the River Styx being damned, as ordered by his master.
