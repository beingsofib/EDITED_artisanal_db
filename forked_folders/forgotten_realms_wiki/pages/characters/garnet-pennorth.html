Garnet Pennorth was the owner of the Silver Scythe inn in the town of Ghars in Cormyr in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Garnet allowed the local merchant Barthelm Meadowbrock to use the Silver Scythe to lodge attendees of the annual meeting of the Merchants' Guild in the . Barthelm also paid for Garnet to add a grand meeting room onto the inn
