Helisa Ithcanter was a cleric of Azuth in Westbridge in 1370 DR.

Activities

Helisa couldn't stay still, always needing something to do. Helisa was thirsty for information about her hometown of Baldur's Gate and her friends there. She was worried by Ghaliver Longstocking's rise to the top, fearing this gave influence to the Zhentarim over Westbridge. Anyone that knew how to damage Ghaliver or the Zhents was welcomed.
