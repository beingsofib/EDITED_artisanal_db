Gauntlet Yeva was an officer within the Flaming Fist of Baldur's Gate that served her city during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

She was a strong-willed leader that readily voiced her concerns to her higher ups.

Relationships

Yeva was the commanding officer of the young nobleman Wyll, before he turned to the life of adventuring. She believed him to be all talk with no skill to back up his boisterous claims.

History

In the , Grand Duke Ulder Ravengard was the target of a kidnapping when he and some others were staying at the Waukeen's Rest inn, during their travels west along the Risen Road form Elturel to Baldur's Gate. After a terrible fight between the soldiers and drow raiders from the cult of the Absolute, Ulder Ravengard was taken west towards Moonrise Towers and the inn was set on fire. Gauntlet Yeva and her soldiers were among the first to come to the Duke's rescue, attempting to break down the door of the inn and pull him to safety.
