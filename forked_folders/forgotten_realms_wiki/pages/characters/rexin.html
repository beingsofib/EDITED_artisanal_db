Rexin was the viper familiar of Handragath, a wizard member of the Cult of the Dragon circa 1369 DR.

Description

Rexin was a tiny magical beast.

Abilities

Rexin could swim and, like other vipers, possessed a venomous bite. Rexin's venom, once in the target's bloodstream, dealt damage to the victim's constitution. Like other familiars, Rexin shared a link with his master. Handragath could channel magic and cast spells through his familiar. Rexin could speak to his master and other snakes.

History

In 1369 DR, Rexin resided in Darnarest's tower alongside Handragath and a small Cult of the Dragon slaver operation. Under Handragath's leadership, the Cult was capturing local villagers and able-bodied adventurers to send them through a portal in the tower's basement to Myth Drannor, where they were doomed to work for the cult or be murdered and turned into undead servants.
