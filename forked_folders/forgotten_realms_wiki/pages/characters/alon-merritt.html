Alon Merritt was the captain of the Marigold, a vessel that sailed to and from Chult during the late 15 century DR

Possessions

Merritt did not keep many personal effects in the captain's cabin, only weather reports and other records taken at sea.

History

During or before the , Captain Merritt's ship was taken over by a mercenary captain and his crew
