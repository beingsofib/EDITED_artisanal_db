Miles Maddoch was a leader of a group of patriots loyal to House Kendrick in Llandrain on the island of Snowdown in the Moonshae Isles in the .

Activities

Miles was a blacksmith of Ffolk heritage. He despised his Amnian and dwarf customers for their exploitation of Snowdown. Miles realized that patience was a virtue when dealing with these outsiders, and bided his time for the perfect time to strike for the High King.
