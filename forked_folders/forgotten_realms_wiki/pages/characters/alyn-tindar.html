Mage Arcane Alyn Tindar, who was nicknamed Wildblade, was a powerful wizard inhabiting Saerloon in the .

Description

Alyn was well-known for her long bone white hair.

Activities

Alyn was once an adventuress. She was a devout follower of Mystra and one of several leaders of the Tower of Mystery temple in Saerloon.
