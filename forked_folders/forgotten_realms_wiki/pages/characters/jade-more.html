Jade More was a talented thief of the mid–14 century DR. She was a more a larcenous version of Alias, with loose morals and a wicked sense of humor. She befriended Olive Ruskettle, whom she worked with for several months before being killed by Flattery Wyvernspur for stealing his purse—he thought that she was his own version of Alias, Cat.
