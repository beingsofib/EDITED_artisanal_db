Darkwater was a guardian water weird that remained in the ruins of Myth Drannor in the late 14 century DR.

History

The elemental was originally a loyal guardian of the city, but over time it was corrupted into a force of random destruction. It roamed the southwestern part of the city, near Tyrintar's Hall.
