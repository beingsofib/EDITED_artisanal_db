Ternen Foxx was a human illusionist, painter, and sculptor from Tethyr who made is artworks in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Forced to become a wizard by his family due to his talents, his passion was always art, and thus he combined his various talents to create paintings that depicted more than usual, and statues that moved.

Possessions

Armed with a wand of illusion and a set of nolzur's marvelous pigments, he went to war against boring and stale art.
