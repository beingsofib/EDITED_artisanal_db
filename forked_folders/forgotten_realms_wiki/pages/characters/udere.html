Udere was a drow assassin who abandoned the Underdark and worked for the infamous Dom as a mercenary in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Udere had long white hair tied back, and wore a suit of plate armor while carrying two scimitars.

Relationships

For reasons not known to outsiders, Udere was fully devoted to the success of Dom's gang.

History

Originating from the Underdark like Pelloth, Udere took up work as a mercenary on the surface and joined Dom's elite team. The final mission took them to Greenweald Lane, where Dom was hired by the Shadovar of Thultanthar to waylay Bensen Virandson as he transported a fragment of a sought-after Nether Scroll to a Harper Lodge. He lead a group of hired mercenaries in an expert ambush that struck near the lodge, but was beaten back and slain nonetheless.
