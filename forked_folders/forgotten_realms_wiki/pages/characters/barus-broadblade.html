Barus Broadblade was the leader of the guards for the slaver ring hidden in the Oasis of the White Palm. Shortly after the release of Khalitharius, during , he had been captured by the dervish cultists of the oasis for interrogation.

History

Barus had been captured by the dervish cultists at least seven days after the princess disappeared; he spent three of those days being interrogated in the dungeon, where he learned the dervishes did not hold the princess. After his release, Barus would remain suspicious, trying to flee his rescuers at the first possible opportunity.

Rumors & Legends

Though he had not been the one making the contact, he knew the dervishes had contacted the slavers, looking to kidnap the princess. He had since learned that the dervish cult had been contacted by an efreet, to whom the princess was probably somehow a threat; he had also learned that the efreet's stronghold was in the Crypt of Badr Al-Mosak.
