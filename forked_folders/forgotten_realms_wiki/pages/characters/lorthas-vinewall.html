Lorthas Vinewall was a member of the Cormanthor Elves in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Lorthas had backswept white hair and wore a nice red outfit. He carried a staff with a large red gemstone.

History

In the , Lorthas joined the Elves of Cormanthor to help reclaim Myth Drannor at their camp near the city. He and Hinnia Fernshadow helped organize the efforts to find the lost Eilistraee's Wanderers and the fighting against the Red Wizards of Droic Orgul, overseeing the efforts of Tarathiel Miastina and Aelorin Ungive.
