Torvel Skydark was a hunter of the village of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

He was the brother of Torst Skydark.

History

Torvel and Torst hailed from Thentia in the Moonsea North, but both had settled in Maskyr's Eye by 1357 DR, where they took up work as hunters.
