Zi Liang was an acolyte of Chauntea at the Harvesthome Abbey in Goldenfields in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Zi Liang often tried to convince Father Ellardin Darovik to not trust the defense of the abbey to incompetent military leaders. Zi was often on patrol.

History

Around 1485 DR, she was at the Harvesthome Abbey when giants attacked the Goldenfields settlement.

Personality

Zi Liang was a true devotee of Chauntea. She believed that Goldenfields was a beacon of hope in the North and she must protect it. Zi didn't trust authority and went with her heart.
