Xena Phobia was a novice priest of Chauntea who served the House of Bountiful Harvest temple dedicated to the The Golden Goddess in Ravens Bluff's Temple District of Ravens Bluff in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Xena was one among five novices who served the House of Bountiful Harvest under the leadership of High Harvestmistress Arabella Ieruin of Chauntea by the . Other novices were Vail Rosehill, Puck, Oliver Tango, and Nicolas Biden.
