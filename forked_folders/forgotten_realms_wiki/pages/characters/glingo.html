Glingo was a bard and the Trapeze-Master of the Circus of the Last Days troupe in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Glingo was a human man with a dark skin tone, hazel eyes, and long braided brown hair, partially dyed in a gleaming blue color. He wore red face paint on his chin and blue mascara.

History

In the , the Circus of the Last Days returned from the Feywild and opened its gates to the inhabitants of the Rivington district of Baldur's Gate. Glingo entertained guests by performing trapeze stunts and aspired to be an opening act for the famous clown, beloved by many, Dribbles. Glingo was present at the circus when it was revealed that the Cult of the Absolute had murdered Dribbles and replaced him with a murderous doppelganger.
