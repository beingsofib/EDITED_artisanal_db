Wulf was a dutiful hireling guard who worked for Baldur's Mouth in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Wulf was a human man with bronze skin, gray eyes, and dark hair.

Possessions

Wulf was equipped with a simple chainmail uniform of Baldur's Mouth's guards and armed with a non-magical halberd.

History

In the , Wulf was employed by Baldur's Mouth and its editor-in-chief, Ettvard Needle. He was tasked with guarding the broadsheet's main entrance from unwanted visitors and critics of the gazette's editorial direction.
