Kaerus Thambadar

Person
nameKaerus Thambadar
homeScornubel, Western Heartlands
sexMale
occupationBlacksmith

Kaerus Thambadar ran a blacksmith shop on the east side of the harbor in the city of Scornubel around 1366 DR. His usual trade was mundane ironmongery, such as wheel rims, hooks, hinges and nails, but he was also capable of producing armor and weaponry.
