Rose Bouquet was a courtesan who lived in the city of Athkatla during the mid—14 century DR.

Activities

Rose worked in an area of Athkatla's bridge district.

History

Rose was a key witness in the "skinner murders" that plagued in the city in the . She saw the killer as they were fleeing the scene of a gruesome murder they committed. While Rose did not get a good look of their face, she noted a distinct scent that reminded her of guril berries.
