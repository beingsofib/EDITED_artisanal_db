Haliim was the first Overking of Lapaliiya, reigning from the mid–12 century DR at the capital city, Sheirtalar.

In the aftermath of the Rage of Wizards, the ruling houses of Sheirtalar and Lushpool formed a union in the Year of Glad Tidings, 1147 DR. They installed their leader, Haliim, as the first Overking of Lapaliiya.
