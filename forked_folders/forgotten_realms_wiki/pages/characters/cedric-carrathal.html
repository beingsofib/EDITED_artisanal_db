Cedric Carrathal was High King of Callidyrr in the early 13 century DR. Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles, he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty and was the grandson of his predecessor, King Adrian. His eventual successor was his son, Vance.

History

Cedric assumed the throne in the , after the death of King Adrian.

In the twenty-first year of his reign, much of the Korinn Archipelago was conquered by Viledel.

Cedric ruled for thirty-six years before he died of natural causes in the .
