Lord Whitburn was the husband of Lady Whitburn, and a member of a patriar family of Baldur's Gate in the 15 century DR.

Description

Lord Whitburn was a fair-skinned bespectacled human man with light chestnut hair and brown eyes. He wore quality firebird-embroidered suits of teal finery.

History

The elfin woman known as Lady Whitburn married into the Whitburn patriar family sometime before the late 15 century DR. By the , Lord Whitburn received a hefty inheritance, presumably after the death of old Lord Whitburn, who was survived by the man's mother and Madeline's mother-in-law – Old Lady Whitburn. During the same period of time, Lord Whitburn frequented the Lower City of the Gate, believing the the nobility needs to keep a finger on the pulse of the city by visiting the common folk and, along the way, to shop and spend his inheritance money.
