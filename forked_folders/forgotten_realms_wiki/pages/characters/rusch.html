Rusch was an officer of the Zhentilar that fought for his god Bane during the mid–14 century DR.

History

Rusch rode alongside Commander Ronglath Knightsbridge during the march on Shadowdale in the . Unlike Knightsbridge and his fellow officer Leetym, Rusch was wholly confident in Bane's assured victory that day.
