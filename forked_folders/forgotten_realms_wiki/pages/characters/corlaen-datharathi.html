Corlaen Datharathi

Person
nameCorlaen Datharathi
homeDurpar, the Shining Lands

Corlaen Datharathi was a member of the Datharathi chaka of Durpar. He was present in the meeting which his third cousin Warian Datharathi attended in Tarsakh of soon after returning to Vaelan. He rubbed his hands in unison with Sevaera Datharathi, Barden Datharathi, and Xaemar Datharathi at one point during the meeting, a gesture owed to the influence of Shaddon Datharathi.
