Noreia was an erinyes in the service of Glasya.

Description

Noreia had woolly black dreadlocks.

Activities

Noreia was the ninth of the Pradixikai, Glasya's elite erinyes guard and the fury leader of her twin sisters, Faventia and Fidentia.

History

In 1486 DR, Noreia was on patrol with Faventia and Fidentia when they met Lorcan. They tried to bully him but Lorcan silenced them.

Later, she was with Sulci and Nisibis when she informed Lorcan that Sairché had gone with Adolican Rhand.

Relationship

Noreia was the daughter of the erinyes Invadiah and the human wizard Ilcanorr of Luthcheq.
