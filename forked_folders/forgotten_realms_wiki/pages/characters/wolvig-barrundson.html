Wolvig Barrundson was a Chieftain within the Reghedmen Tribe of the Bear during the late 15 century DR.

History

Some time around the , Wolvig fell under the influence of the being known as the Ice Witch. Unfortunately, this alliance led to Wolvig's eventual demise.

After his death, Günvald Halraggson remained as the only Chieftain of the Tribe of the Bear and became the new Bear King.
