Larando was an adventuring warrior during the mid–8 century DR.

Description

He had stubble on his chin.

Relationships

He was an adventurer, in a group that also included Klargathan Srior and Ardelnar Trethran; Ardelnar in particular was a friend of his.

History

In the , Larando was part of a group of three different parties that allied together and adventured into Myth Drannor, with seven mages amongst them, only to find themselves exterminated by baatezu. Larando himself was caught by several barbazu, who tore his limbs off with their weapons, and he was ultimately decapitated by an erinyes. His companions ran on.
