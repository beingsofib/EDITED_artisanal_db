Ceryx was a human fire elementalist wandering the lands in search of the Seven Lost Rings of Mhzentul in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a gaunt man with long, reddish hair.

Personality

Fascinated since a young age with fire magic and the seven rings of Mhzentul both, he cared only for magic and the friendship of fellow adventurers.

Possessions

He wielded a copper-cored quarterstaff and wore a set of bracers of defense, and a cloak of the bat alongside his traveling clothes.

Relationships

He made company with the priest of Lathander Magnus Arifek who joined him in his travels.

History

In Ceryx almost recovered two of the rings from the zhentarim wizard Whisper, but the aforementioned was killed by Doust Sulwood who claimed them instead.
