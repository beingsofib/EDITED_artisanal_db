Prince Dunstan was a prince of Hartsvale.

Relationships

Dunstan was the son of King Blod, and he had a twin brother named Camden.

History

When King Blod died, his two twin sons, Dunstan and Camden, fought a war of succession, called the War of the Twins. To win the war, Camden purchased the assistance of a tribe of Ice Spire ogres led by Goboka, their shaman. (Camden used a small ogre band to pull out his brother's forces so that a larger one could attack.)
