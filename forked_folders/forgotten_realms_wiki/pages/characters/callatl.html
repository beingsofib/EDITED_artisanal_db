Callatl was an inhabitant of Kultaka.

History

Callatl was the son of the Eagle Knight Huakal.

However, he decided to become a follower of Zaltec and, later, a Jaguar Knight.

Callatl was a vicious individual, prone to abusing others. He tried many times to sexually approach the young slave Erixitl but with no success.

In 1360 DR, Callatl tried to sexually abuse Erixitl, but she defeated him, and he ended up with heavy injures to his throat and testicles.
