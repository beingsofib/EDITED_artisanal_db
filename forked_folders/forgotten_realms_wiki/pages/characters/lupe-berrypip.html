Lupe Berrypip was a halfling girl in the Earthspur Mountains.

History

In 1491 DR, Lupe lived with her father, Ombul Berrypip, who was sent to the mine in the Earthspurs as a cook. When a medusa and her gargoyle minions took control of the mine, Lupe managed to hide herself until an adventuring party hired to clear the mine found and rescued her.
