Brother Theodosian was a Helmite cleric who lived in Helm's Hold during the late 15 century DR.

History

Unlike so many of his fallen brethren, Theodosian survived the initial occupation and attempted reclamation of Helm's Hold circa the . He accompanied Captain Dunfield to the Last Redoubt war camp formed by the Neverwinter Guard and assisted in the efforts to retake the city. He served as a healer at the camp, and aided in the efforts to bless the remains of his allies who fell on the battlefield.
