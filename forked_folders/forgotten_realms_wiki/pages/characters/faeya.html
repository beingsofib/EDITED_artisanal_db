Faeya was an apprentice wizard in the mid–8 century DR.

Description

She was lushly built. During , she wore at least once a gown of forest green. Her eyes were lively brown. According to Thessamel Arunder, her belly was flat and hard.

Abilities

She was quite handy with a dagger, and with remedies. Her master thought her capable of casting spells.

Activities

Faeya apprenticed under Thessamel Arunder.

Relationships

As well as his student, she was Thessamel Arunder's lover.

History

During the Chaos of the Art in the , Faeya had to kill two men who sought revenge against her master. She then sought out a number of merchants who had offered to buy the lands of Thessamel Arunder and gotten been earlier, then convinced them to repeat their offer. She then got Thessamel Arunder, her master, to sign their offer during a moment of play in bed. She explained the situation to him over the next morning, in a meeting with the aforementioned merchants.
