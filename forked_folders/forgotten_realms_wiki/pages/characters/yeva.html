Yeva was a githyanki from outside Toril.

Description

Yeva's skin was the color of desert sand with dark mottling. Her hair was long and brown layered into braids. Her features were sharp and she had pointed ears. She wore silvery plate armor.

Abilities

Yeva could summon a psionic storm lance that took the form of a lightning bolt.

History

In 1396 DR, she met Anusha Marhana, who was lost within the dreams of the Eldest in her dream form.
