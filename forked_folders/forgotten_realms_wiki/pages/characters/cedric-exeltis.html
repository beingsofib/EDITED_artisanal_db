Cedric Exeltis was the member of the noble Exeltis merchant clan to bring them wealth and power through trade, several generations before the late 14 century DR.

History

Cedric Exeltis was renowned as the first man to develop the recipe for the rare and expensive Exeltis Ice Wine – a rich and potent red wine fermented using a unique type of tiny, fragile grey grapes that grew in the vineries around the Blade Cliffs region of the Sword Coast.
