Kellatarn Nander was a retired warrior and butcher inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Kellatarn was the guildmaster for the Guild of Butchers during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He held the title of "Master Butcher" within the guild and worked out of the Butchers' Guildhall–their headquarters on the Way of the Dragon in the Dock Ward.
