Halamther was a wizard and one of seven High Hands in the Risen Cult of Bane in the mid-14 century DR.

Activities

Halamther spent much of his time creating a variety of awful creatures to serve the Risen Cult of Bane. Out of all creatures he was known to mostly frequently create ju-ju zombies.

Relationships

Halamther was a good friend of the cult's leader Gauntather for much of his life.
