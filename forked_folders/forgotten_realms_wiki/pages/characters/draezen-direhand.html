Draezen Direhand was a monk and the archimandrite of the Dark Ravens' Monastery in the Cloud Peaks of Amn in late 14 century DR.

History

At one point in mid–1370s DR several drow houses of Menzoberranzan hired the assassin monks led by Draezen to destroy House Uoswiir; some time later the drow adventuerer Vhaidra Uoswiir attacked the Dark Raven monastery in order to avenge her house; Vhaidra defeated Draezen in combat.
