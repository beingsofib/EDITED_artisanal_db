Sir Ftucis was the Training Master of the Roadwarden Guard, stationed in the Skull Crag Keep in the Storm Horn Mountains of Cormyr in the 14 century DR.

Description

Ftucis was an older, grizzled male warrior with long gray hair.

History

By the early 1360s DR, Sir Ftucis was already an aging experienced soldier who trained the Roadwarden Guard in the Skull Crag Keep. He offered his martial training sessions to guests of the keep. Shortly after the death of the hero Roadwarden Arelin Starbrow, Sir Ftucis made his services available to the group of caravan guards who aided the keep's soldiers against a monster horde that threatened the High Road.
