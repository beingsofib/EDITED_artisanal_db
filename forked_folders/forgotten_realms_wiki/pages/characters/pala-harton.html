Pala Harton was a sorcerer in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Pala wore a bright orange robe and a light leather skullcap.

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Pala arrived in the village and helped train prospective sorcerers.
