Kybor was a sorcerer member of the Fire Knives in Tilverton in 1357 DR.

Activities

Kybor was a member of the Fire Knives cell refuge in Tilverton and a lieutenant of Radatha.

History

He, together with Radatha, agreed to join the New Alliance in order to finally kill King Azoun Obarskyr IV using the adventuring party possessing the Azure Bonds.

However, Kybor decided to do some research about their new allies and Tyranthraxus decided to kill him.
