Fowler was a horse that was kept at a stable in Parnast for a time during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

In the , Fowler and several other horses accompanied the mage Druhallen, the guardswoman Rozt'a, the thief Tiep, and a goblin named Sheemzher on a trek across Greypeak Mountains.

After the company returned to the surface from the mines of Dekanter, Fowler remained waiting for them along the Dawn Pass Trail. The loyal horse then carried Dru for a ways until they reached the Weathercote Wood, before he refused to continue any longer. The ultimate fate of Fowler the horse is unknown.
