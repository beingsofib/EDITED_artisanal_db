Velicia was an archer gloom stalker member of The Guild crime gang of Baldur's Gate and secretly an undercover Harper in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Velicia was a high elven woman with a fair skin tone and straight red hair gathered in a tight ponytail. She wore a half plate dyed in green colors of the Guild.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Velicia was placed as an undercover spy to work under Farlin, who led a gang of Guild members in dealing with the Stone Lord and his men. The new kingpin secretly served the Absolute and was trying to remove the Guild's influence from across the Gate. Farlin's group faced a band of the Stone Lord's men led by the tiefling Cairos, under the Wyrm's Crossing bridge in Rivington. However, the meeting devolved into a battle and his squad's fate remained uncertain, whether they were killed by the gangsters or survived and returned safely to the Guild's safehouse.
