Tilvara was a skilled archwizard who was influential in the founding of Tilverton.

History

The city of Tilverton sprung up around Tilvara's keep which was called Tilvara's Palace as of the . Humans and elves built a trading settlement around Tilvara's keep and later named the growing town in her honor.
