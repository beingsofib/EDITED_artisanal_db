Sister Iahalae was an elf unicorn rider who lived on Evermeet and was a member of Aerilaya's tribe.

History

In the mid-to-late 1300s DR, Iahalae returned to visit her tribe together with her unicorn. There she met Carreigh Macumail, who was awed by how she and her unicorn looked so impressive. While at first she and the unicorn were distrustful of the human, with Aerilaya's words they later came to accept him, especially once he showed them his otter tattoo. The two talked, and Sister Iahalae became one of his closest friends on the island of Evermeet.

Relationships

Iahalae was a close friend of Carreigh Macumail and came from the same tribe as the druidess Aerilaya.

Possessions

The unicorn rider possessed shining white mail armor and a lance.
