Ibtisam bint Latifa was a clothing designer who lived in Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Activities

Ibtisam's real skill revolved around her ability to pick specific clothing to suit each customer and their particular body shape. Customers of hers included nobles from court. She was even invited to consult with members of the royal harim.

She charged 10 dinars for a consultation.

Ibtisam's small home and shop was located near the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in the Court District.
