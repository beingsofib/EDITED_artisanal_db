Fist Crosse was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Crosse was a human man with tan skin and mousy-colored hair. His face was decorated with black paint or black tattoos. The man's blue eyes shone from under his Fist uniform helmet. He wore a set of chainmail armor decorated with a tabard embroidered with the Flaming Fist insignia. Crosse wielded a halberd when on duty.

History

In the , Fist Crosse was stationed in the Basilisk Gate's barracks and prison, serving under Blaze Liara Portyr and Manip Seyama. His Gauntlet supervisor forced Crosse to train half to death.
