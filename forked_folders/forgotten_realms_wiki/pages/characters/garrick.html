Garrick was a bard who traveled across west Faerûn during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was somewhat naive and too trusting of strangers. He enjoyed traveling and performing his music for others.

History

Garrick honed his musical talents studying at a college in Berdusk.

He was once part of the Dale Wind Troubadours, a traveling acting troupe who performed all along the Sword Coast, from Amn in the south to Neverwinter in the north. Although the Troubadours were a popular act who regularly performed for the higher classes, they also used their performances as distractions for theft. When Garrick learned of this he left the troupe in protest.

By the , Garrick had taken up with actor and ne'er-do-well Silke Rosena in Beregost.

By the Garrick moved south to Athkatla in Amn, where he tried courting a paladin of the Order of the Radiant Heart. Like Lady Alyth, she rebuffed his advances.
