Ursus was a half-orc druid and adventurer in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Ursus was a big and burly man with brown hair who wore furs and hide armor.

History

Shortly after the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, Ursus traveled along the High Road with his bear companion and stayed the night at Beckside Castle. He came to a heated argument with the ranger Malin Quickarrow over the merits of bears versus wolves.
