Renee was a resident of Shadowdale who worked as a server at the Old Skull Inn for a time during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

She had elegant and alluring features, with piercing blue eyes and wild, curly-red hair that was reminiscent of Sune herself.

Activities

Renee spent some of her time cozying up to and flirting with wealthy individuals at the Old Skull.

History

Just prior to the Second battle of Shadowdale, in the , Renee met and startled flirting with Adon, who was too troubled by his own scarred face to accept her advances.
