Porios was a mage-for-hire who operated throughout the Sword Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Porios was something of a pragmatist rather than idealist.

History

During the , Porios was under the employ of the Bhaalspawn Sarevok Anchev during his attempted coup in Baldur's Gate. After Sarevok's death, Porios stole his sword of chaos from the Ducal Palace under the direction of the Bhaalspawn's loyal disciple Korlasz.

Soon after, Porios and his crew were caught by the Hero of Baldur's Gate.
