Bahija bint Tufala was a Zakharan Sa'luk living in Hiyal in Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Personality

Cocky and brash, Bahija was open about her illicit exploits and confident no one would find her secret lair.

Activities

A native of smoky Hiyal, she earned a reputation as a locator of lost objects.

Possessions

Bahija owned an enchanted scimitar and slippers of spider climbing.
