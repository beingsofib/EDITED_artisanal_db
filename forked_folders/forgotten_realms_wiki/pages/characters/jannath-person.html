Jannath was a thief of the Velvet Hands guild in Hastarl during the mid-3 century DR.

History

During the assault on Athalgard concerted under the leadership of Prince Elminster Aumar in the , the prince revealed to his friend Farl, the leader of the Velvet Hands thieves' guild that had involved itself on assaulting the castle to overthrow the Magelords of Athalantar and King Belaur Aumar that, as far as the thieves went, Jannath had sworded a servant too swiftly, but other than that, everything went well.
