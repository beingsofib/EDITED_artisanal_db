Willaim Gurst was a necromancer that stayed in the city of Baldur's Gate for a time during the mid–14 century DR.

Possesions

He wore a robe of fire resistnace kept a couple of spell scrolls on his person, to compliment his array of prepared magic.

History

In the , William joined the Zhentarim mage Niemain, along with two other spellcasters, Wheber Ott and Oulam, to cast what Niemain referred to as an "extremely difficult" casting on the second flor of the Sorcerous Sundries in the Lower City.
