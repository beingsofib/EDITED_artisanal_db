Gurgon was an ogre who served the cult of the Absolute during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Gurgon wore a simple loin cloth, strapped leather bracers, and cloth wraps around his rather large feet.

Personality

He was an ogre of few words. While some could consider him terse, Gurgon could communicate just as effectively with a few words in cases where others may have chosen more.

Activities

Gurgon was tasked with protecting the main doors of the Shattered Sanctum, a ruined Selûnite temple that was taken over by the Absolute's cult.
