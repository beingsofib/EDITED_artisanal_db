Cheth Moonviper was a balding alchemist in High Dale.

History

Cheth was a priest of Bane and a scion of one of the most noble families of Zhentil Keep. Under orders of Fzoul Chembryl, he settled in High Dale working as a physic, pharmacist, and tanner to the Dalesfolk. When in 1358 DR a secret Zhent occupation army occupied the dale, Cheth was a member of the Council of the High Dale.
