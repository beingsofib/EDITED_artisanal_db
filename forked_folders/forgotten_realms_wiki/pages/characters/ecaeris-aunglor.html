Ecaeris Aunglor was the Spell-Captain of the Akh'Faer in Cormanthor in 650 DR.

Relationship

Ecaeris was apprentice to Spell-Major Alea Dahast and the younger sister of Ajaar Aunglor XII, the lord of the Aunglor clan.

Personality

Ecaeris had assimilated all the lessons of Alea, despite not having her experience and secrets, to help oppose the elder Houses. She questioned everything she learned. A perfectionist, Ecaeris hated making even the slightest of mistakes but was very tolerant of other people's mistakes. She was fascinated by humans and this worried her parents.

Rumors

Rumors said Ecaeris studied at the Windsong Tower.
