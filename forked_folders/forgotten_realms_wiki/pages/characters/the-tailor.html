The Tailor was the only known name of a famed and mysterious armorer from Amn in the late 15 century DR.

History

By the , sets of armor made by The Tailor were found outside of Amn, including the city of Baldur's Gate, and the Western Heartlands of the West Faerûn. The Tailor's work had a recognizable specialty style. Some of the armor made by the mysterious person were enchanted +1 breastplates.
