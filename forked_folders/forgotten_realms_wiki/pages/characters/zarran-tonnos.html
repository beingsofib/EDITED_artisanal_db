High Moonmistress Zarran Tonnos was a Selûnite priest and the leader of Moonmaiden's Hall in Murann of Amn during the 8 century DR.

History

In the , during her service at Moonmaiden's Hall, Zarran was informed of the charges laid against the New Moon Pact by High Moonmistress Brima Chintamn of Bright Lady's Tower in distant Chancelgaunt. Believing that the Pact had indeed fallen to the heresy of believing Shar and Selûne the same being, Zarran supported the charges and condoned the extermination of the Pact.
