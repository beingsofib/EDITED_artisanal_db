Shrii Hallek

Shrii Hallek was a hadozee restaurateur and innkeeper of the Man-o'-War in the city of Bral on the Rock of Bral in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. Shrii managed the restaurant and inn on behalf of its unknown owners, who were rumored to be adventurers. Shrii was very respectful to guests.
