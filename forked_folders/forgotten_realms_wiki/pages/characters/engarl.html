Engarl was a knight of Athalantar in the Western Heartlands who turned to banditry during the early to mid–3 century DR.

Abilities

He was known to wield the sling and the lance proficiently enough to kill.

Possessions

He wielded a lance he had looted.

Relationships

He had been a Knight of Athalantar under Helm Stoneblade.

History

By Hammer of the , Engarl had looted a lance after slaying a soldier of the Magelords of Athalantar with a sling. On that date, he participated in an ambush that slew a half-dozen of the Magelords' men. In the aftermath, he'd looted six daggers, and brought horse meat back to Lawless Castle, where the bandits were fortified.
