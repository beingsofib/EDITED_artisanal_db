Indeera Lakhan was the Second Commander of the elven ranger organization called Mist of Shadows that prowled the woods of Cormanthor some time before the mid–14 century DR.

History

Indeera gained acclaim and became a highly decorated member of the Mist of Shadows sometime before the , achieving the rank of an officer and the second-in-command. Under her leadership, the Mist of Shadows protected the great ancestral forest of Cormanthor from monsters and bandits. She famously wore an enchanted set of leather armor, later named Protector of the Second, in her honor. Indeera's armor left her possessions under unknown circumstances.
