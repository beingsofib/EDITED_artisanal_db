Reverend Judge Oleff Uskar was a priest of Tyr who presided over the civil court of Neverwinter known as the Hall of Justice. While practicing what was considered "low justice", Oleff was assisted by Prior Hlam.

Appearance

Oleff Uskar was an older human man with a graying blonde beard. He wore purple chainmail armor of the Neverwintran Tyrran faith. He was a stern individual who was rarely shaken by hardships and crises in Neverwinter.

History

In the , the deadly plague descended on Neverwinter. Oleff Uskar's temple, the Hall of Justice in the City Core had to pivot from the project of artifact recovery to using all its men and magic to help the constantly growing numbers of those afflicted with the Wailing. Even though Reverend Judge's attention was aimed at the plague, he was still willing to involve and adventurer's aid in the artifact recovery from the recently uncovered tomb of Miaslaznia Fairblade, an adventuring companion of Halueth Never, the founder of Neverwinter. Oleff did not have high hopes on the recovery but there was a chance of finding a powerful artifact or even Halueth Never's remains. Oleff Uskar was working with Briley, who was tasked with guarding the tomb in the Peninsula District and would direct adventurers towards him, and, in turn, Briley would escort the adventures to a hose in the eastern side of the district with the uncovered tomb's entrance located in the basement.

The artifact recovery was targeted by the Tyrran faith's enemies–Gilles and the Church of Talona. While Oleff wanted to strengthen the citizens' morale with the recovery of historic artifacts, Gilles would attempt to convince Oleff's adventurers to buy the artifacts instead of them being turned over to the Hall of Justice.
