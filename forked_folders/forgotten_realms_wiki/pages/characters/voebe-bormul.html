Lady Voebe Bormul was a member of the noble patriar Bormul family of Baldur's Gate sometime before or in the late 15 century DR.

History

Some years before the , Lady Allice Bormul became a prisoner of the secret Sharran cloister below the House of Grief of Baldur's Gate. She was incarcerated in the Chamber of Loss and died before ever seeing freedom. Allice died holing her Last Will and Testament, written in her own blood, leaving possessions to her beloved daughter, Voebe Bormul. Her skeletal remains and the will were still within the undergoing cloister during the Absolute crisis.
