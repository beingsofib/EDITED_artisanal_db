Narbuth was a caravan guard of the mid-14 century DR.

Description

He was stouter than Storstil.

Personality

He never stopped cracking jokes, even to himself.

History

Narbuth was one of the guards in Orthil Voldovan's caravan in late , on the route from Scornubel to Waterdeep, which Shandril Shessair joined.

During the ambush, Narm Tamaraith used his spells to save Narbuth's life; to prevent him from running all the way across the caravan to his wife, Shandril, Narbuth and Orthil knocked him out and tied him up. He tended to Narm's injuries afterwards. He bound Narm somewhat tightly, per Arauntar.

He was later slain during another ambush.
