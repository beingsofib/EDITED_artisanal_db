Chevesh was a powerful human wizard who liveed in a marble tower in Airspur. He was widely regarded by the citizens of Airspur to be crazy and most people avoided his tower. Chevesh was suspected of summoning demons and was investigated by Demascus. However, the investigation cleared him of demonic associations. Chevesh would have liked nothing more than to be a firesoul genasi and was researching methods to turn humans into firesouls. His tower was guarded by an azer.
