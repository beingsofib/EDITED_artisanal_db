Bolt was a goblin warlock and a follower of the Absolute in the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

History

Warlock Bolt remained at the Shattered Sanctum in the , when the leaders of the Shattered Sanctum began searching for individuals aboard a crashed nautiloid they believed were True Souls of their goddess. Bolt witnessed the warlord Dror Ragzlin attempt to speak with a dead mind flayer to glean more information about their whereabouts.
