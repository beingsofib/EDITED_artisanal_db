Amsha al-Lanim was in charge of the Gate of the Ghost in Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Personality

Amsha was harsh and strict, relentlessly driving her employees to strive for perfection in their duties.

Activities

Amsha took great pride in running the busiest gate in all of Huzuz—a gate that admitted more people to the city than even the whole harbor.

She took her job very seriously and never missed a day of work in eight years on the post, including the days when she gave birth to her two children.

Relationships

Unable to find an enlightened Zakharan husband who would tolerate her lofty career goals, Amsha married a foreign wizard named Elfinster, who operated a small school.

Possessions

Amsha owned a magical longsword and jambiya. Her wedding ring was actually a ring of regeneration.
