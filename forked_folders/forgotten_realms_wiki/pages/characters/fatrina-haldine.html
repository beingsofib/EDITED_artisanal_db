Fatrina Haldine was a farmer belonging in the Haldine family of Stag Falls in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Fatrina was almost certainly a farmer.

Relationships

She was the eldest granddaughter of Ren Haldine. Vilhelm von Stag Falls fancied her.

History

Fatrina Haldine was one of the inhabitants of the Haldine farm during the mid-to-late 14 century DR, at the same time that baron Vilhelm von Stag Falls was turned undead by the vampire Romanisk Vlaensk. Once undead, Vilhelm's reign became cruel, erratic, and terrible; when he sent his guards to bring him Ren Haldine, patriarch of the Haldine, and Ren's grand-daughter Fatrina, whom he fancied, the entire Haldine farm fled into nearby caves.
