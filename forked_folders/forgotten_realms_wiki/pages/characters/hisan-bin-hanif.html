Hisan bin Hanif was the kennel-master of the Dark Wind Stables in Sarahin in 1367 DR.

Activities

Hisan took care of the horses, ponies, and lone pegasus of the Everlasting. He completely avoided the giant two-headed roc that was also housed within the stables. He was completely terrified of the bird, though his faith in Hajama would not allow him to show it.

History

Hisan originally hailed from the desert tribes of the Haunted Lands.
