Iskra was Auril's roc mount during the years of the Everlasting Rime.

Description

Iskra was white.

Possessions

Stashed throughout the roc's nest were a silver dragon egg, an exotic wooden harp decorated with ivory and zircon, and an electrum chain with a pendant of bloodstone. It also included a chest containing gold pieces, a golden ring with a black pearl, and a spell scroll featuring mass cure wounds.

History

In order to maintain the Everlasting Rime, Auril needed to cast a nightly spell from Iskra's back.

Iskra had a number of hostile encounters with Angajuk, which left the latter scarred.
