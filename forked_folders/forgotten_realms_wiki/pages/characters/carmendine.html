Carmendine

Person
nameCarmendine
sexMale
occupationAdventurer
patron deityDeneir

Carmendine was an adventurer and follower of Deneir who founded the Zealots of the Written Word, an adventuring company that aided the Church of Deneir.

Activities

Carmendine helped clerics of the church raise money by aiding them on holy quests and specific missions.
