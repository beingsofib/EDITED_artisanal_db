Emri Aliniki was an elven guide in the ruins of Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Description

Emri had long brown hair and gray eyes. He wore a bright red outfit and carried a wooden spear.

History

In the , Emri served as a gatekeeper and guide to the eastern ruins of Myth Drannor, starting from the Cormanthor Elves outskirts camp. When the drow raider Niziira Xiltun started kidnapping elves to take into the Underdark, he refused to take hapless travelers with him into the ruins she threatened.
