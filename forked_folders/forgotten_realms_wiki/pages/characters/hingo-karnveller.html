Hingo Karnveller was a militia soldier and a rancher at the Happy Horse Ranch in Triboar in the Dessarin Valley in the late 15 century DR.

Relationships

He was the adult son of Janele Karnveller, owner of the ranch, and brother of Aldo and Rasko.
