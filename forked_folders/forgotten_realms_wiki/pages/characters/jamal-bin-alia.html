Jamal bin Alia was a member of the royal family of Medina al-Afyal in 1367 DR.

Personality

Jamal was easily intimidated by his mother Alia Jamal.

Relationships

Jamal was the nephew of Padishah Alad bin Alaq bin Alonka. Since the padishah's first born son disappeared at sea, Jamal was a legitimate contender to succeed his uncle for the rulership of Medina al-Afyal, much to the delight of his mother.
