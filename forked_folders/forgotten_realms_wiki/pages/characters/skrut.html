Skrut was a goblin boss who led his kind in the cult of the Absolute, within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

Description

He had yellow-green skin and long brown hair he wore back, with small braids in front of his ears.

Personality

Skrut was not particularly fond of the scrying eyes that cult leaders at Moonrise Towers kept in the Shattered Sanctum.

Possessions

The goblin boss wielded a vicious-looking goblin scimitar. He wore a set of goblin-made plate armor that was decorated with various bones and skulls.
