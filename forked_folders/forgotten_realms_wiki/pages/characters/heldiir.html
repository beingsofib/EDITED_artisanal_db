Heldiir was a Zhentarim mage of the mid-14 century DR.

Description

He was only old enough to master his nerves.

Personality

He was somewhat skittish.

History

In late , Manshoon ordered Heldiir to lead a group of twenty mages in keeping a magical watch over Shadowdale, with the purpose of ensuring Elminster and other notables did not undertake secret movements.
