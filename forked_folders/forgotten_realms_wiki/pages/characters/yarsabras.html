Yarsabras was an exiled archdevil in the mid-late 14 century DR.

Appearance

He had many talons, and a hound's head.

History

During the scouring of Avernus by the Simbul in , she moved into a cave that was held by Yarsabras. The exile emerged to attack her, but overshot her and leapt into the forces fighting her; rather than ignore each other, they got into a tussle.
