High Mage Kileontheal was a sun elf and a powerful illusionist, who was over 500 years old by the mid-to-late 14 century DR. She was a member of the Circle of Reilloch Domayr.

During an attack on Tower Reilloch in 1374 DR, she was affected by a spell that caused temporary insanity.
