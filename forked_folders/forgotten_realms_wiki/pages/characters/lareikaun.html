Lareikaun was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Abilities

Presumably, he had abilities to divine something about dead bodies.

Relationships

He was known by name to Glathra Barcantle.

History

In the wake of the violence that erupted during the Council of the Dragon, Glathra Barcantle wanted to make sure Lareikaun and Yarjack Blamreld checked the bodies of the lords that died during the attempt on King Foril Obarskyr I's life.
