Bruno Indomicus was an indentured employee of the Blushing Mermaid in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Bruno was a gold dwarf with a dark brown skin tone, dark brown hair, and blue eyes. He kept his scalp and face clean-shaven.

History

In the , during the time Captain Grisly was replaced by the green hag Auntie Ethel as the owner of the Blushing Mermaid. During that time, Bruno Indomicus found himself in debt to the establishment, unable to pay. Captain Grisly and Bosun Gannet put the dwarf to work on sorting produce for the tavern's kitchen until Bruno's debt was fully paid off. Bruno was also a gambler and owed Baldur's Bones money to Brinks, a criminal element of the Lower City. Brinks demanded Bosun Gannet chop Bruno's finger off as a warning, but the innkeeper turned the request down, offering instead to buy the dwarf's debt out.
