Fadime Zatar was a former Red Wizard in Neverwinter.

History

Fadime was a red wizard in Thay until she decided to defect and join the forces of Neverwinter; she acted in all means to undermine the lich zulkirs. Her main objective was collect all pieces of Thay culture before Szass Tam destroy all culture in her land.
