Launcel was a soldier in the Purple Dragons based in Castle Obarskyr in Suzail, Cormyr, during the late 15 century DR.

History

Launcel was called upon by King Foril Obarskyr I in the wake of the massacre during the Council of the Dragon of the . When the former Royal Magician, Vangerdahast, revealed the place where Vainrence and current Royal Magician Ganrahast were imprisoned, Foril ordered Glathra Barcantle, a high-ranked War Wizard, to take all of his Purple Dragons to save the trapped wizards, leaving him guarded by Launcel and Tarimmon alone.
