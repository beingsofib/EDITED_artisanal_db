Hwesta "breeze" was an elven rogue, wizard, and adventurer in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was an elf with golden hair and fair skin.

Personality

Called breeze by his friends due to calm demeanor and love of birds, he enjoyed nature and all feathered beings within it.

Possessions

He wore a cloak of the bat, a pilfered talisman of memorization, and wielded an lute.

History

Hwesta and his adventuring band were banished to Toril by an angry mage, furious after they stole a talisman of memorization from him, while they all hoped and searched for a way to return home, Hwesta was more at ease in the lands of Toril than the others, as all he really wanted was nature and feathered friends.
