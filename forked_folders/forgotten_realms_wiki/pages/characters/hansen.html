Hansen was an Ilmatari acolyte and child of Brother Hodges.

Personality

The community of the Gate treasured Hansen and others that worked at the shrine.

Relationships

Hansen had a sibling known as Sissa.

History

In 1482 DR, Hansen helped to maintained the Shrine of the Suffering in Baldur's Gate. During this time, Hansen was not old enough to marry, but a decade later, Hansen reached such an age.
