Yonni was a Cashguard in the employ of the Counting House of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Yonni was a human woman with tan skin and gray eyes. As a Cashguard, she wore a set of uniform breastplate armor and a helmet. Yonni was armed with a two-handed heavy crossbow.

History

In the , Cashguard Yonni was tasked with guarding and patrolling the private docks that belonged to the Counting House in the Lower City, alongside a number of fellow Cashguards and the city's steel watchers.
