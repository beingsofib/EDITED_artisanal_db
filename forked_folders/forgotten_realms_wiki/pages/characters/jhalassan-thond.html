Jhalassan Thond was a glassblower and shopkeeper inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

She owned a glass shop located on Sleepers' Walk in Waterdeep's Trades Ward called Thond Glass and Glazing. Jhalassan was also the main point of contact for the Guild of Glassblowers, Glaziers, & Speculum-makers.
