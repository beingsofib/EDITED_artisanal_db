Mirna Dendrar was a resident of Phandalin in the 15 century DR.

Relationships

Mirna had a husband named Thel. They had two children, Nars and Nilsa.

History

Mirna was born in Thundertree where her family ran an herbalism and alchemy shop prior to the town's destruction by the eruption of Mount Hotenow.

Circa 1491 DR, the Redbrand bandits of Phandalin made crude leers at Mirna. Her husband, Thel, had had enough of the group that terrorized the area, and so he stood up to them. He was subsequently killed by them.

After her husband's death, she and her two children were taken to the cellar of Tresendar Manor, and thrown into the slave pen. The Redbrands planned to sell them off as slaves.
