Fender Stouthammer is a young dwarf of the Stouthammer clan. He was among those that followed Torgar when he left Mirabar for Mithral Hall. He was captured by Tos'un Armgo in a sneak attack with a poisoned blade. He managed to warn refugees from Nesmé of an ambush before being skewered by a sharpened branch.
