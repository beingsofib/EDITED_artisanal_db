Nimoar, "the Reaver"

Possessions

Nimoar owned a heavy magic steel shield known as his blazing bulwark. This spiked flaming shield was carried into battle during the First Trollwar of the , where it served well against the regenerating giants.

History

Nimoar was involved in the Orcfastings War, in which the orcs from the Sword Mountains attacked and besieged Nimoar's Hold. Nimoar survived the war, but died of old age in 936 DR.
