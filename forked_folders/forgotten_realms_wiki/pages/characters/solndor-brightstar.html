Solndor Brightstar was a cleric of Lathander that served as a spiritual leader among the people of Idyllglen.

Relationships

Solndor was the ancestor of Jhessa Brightstar, who carried on the tradition of service to the Lathanderian church.

History

When Idyllglen was threatened by a vicious collective of gnoll tribes, Solndor beseeched his god Lathander for aid. Lathander dispatched the solar Zariel to save the people Idyllglen and drive away the bestial marauders.
