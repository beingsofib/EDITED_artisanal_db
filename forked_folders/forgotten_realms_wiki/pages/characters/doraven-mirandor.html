Doraven Mirandor was one of earliest kings of Impiltur and the 16 monarch of the Mirandor Dynasty. His reign lasted a mere three years during the 2 century DR. He was known throughout history as Doraven the Bloody.

Relationships

He was the third son of Inrath Mirandor III and the stepbrother of Inrath IV.

History

Doraven Mirandor staged a coup against his older stepbrother Inrath the IV and seized the throne of Impiltur for himself in the . He ruled over the Kingdom of Wand and Sword for a mere three years, before being slain by another stepbrother, Pendarn Mirandor.
