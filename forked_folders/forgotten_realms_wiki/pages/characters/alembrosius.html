Alembrosius was a child who worked with his mother selling pottery and ceramics in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

In his childhood, Alembrosius was a boy with olive skin, light brown eyes, and curly, shiny black hair. He wore simple commoner's garb and a leather jerkin vest.

History

In the , Alembrosius helped his mother out with her pottery business. The mother ran a shop selling ceramics from her home, offering vase repair services to clients from across the city and the Upper City. When she was out making deliveries or doing other business, Alembrosius was left in charge of the street-side stall, advertising the pottery and asking those interested to return soon once his mother was back to perform transactions.
