Fless-onas was a yuan-ti pureblood priest from the Underdark who worked as a mercenary in the late 15 century DR.

Abilities

Fless-onas could call upon divine punishment, flame strike, and unholy blight.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, Fless-onas and a number of other yuan-ti were hired by Sschindylryn to poison the water supply of Eveningstar. Along with Gresl-glis and Saz-sass, they secured a section of underground caverns that fed the groundwater and guarded the green dragon Tharmalos. Fless-onas was responsible for maintaining a magical barrier to keep intruders out, but was unable to do that while being attacked and was slain.
