Sythrilia

Person
nameSythrilia
homeThindol, Chultan Peninsula

Sythrilia was a famous heroine of ancient Thindol. She was responsible for freeing her people from the domination of the yuan-ti.

The Hills of Sythrilia in Thindol were named for her by the 1370s DR.
