Ydrae Avithoul was a drow of House Avithoul in the late 15 century DR who served in the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the 1480s DR.

Description

Ydrae wore a suit of partial plate mail and carried a large longsword with drow poison and paralytic venom.

History

During the invasion of the surface, Ydrae oversaw the drow encampment in the Eveningstar Cavern, only a short distance from Eveningstar. Following the events in Eberron around the Spinner of Shadows and Lolth's intervention at the Spinner's Prison, a group of Eberronian heroes emerged in the Underdark and fought their way to the surface. They followed the drow reinforcements up through the D'Zani outpost and emerged in Ydrae's posting in the cavern, attacking from behind. At the time she was processing the prisoners taken from Eveningstar, hoping to interrogate them for clues as to the Thread of the Weave and otherwise turning them into slaves. She not only missed the significance of the captured Ana Brabener, but fell in the subsequent battle.
