Carrig Cymrych III was High King of the Moonshae Isles for a year at the turn of the 6 century DR.

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the son of his predecessor, High King Tuathal. His successor was his son, Colin Cymrych. His wife was Queen Abigail.

History

Carrig III assumed the throne of the High King in the , after the assassination of his father. He too was assassinated the next year.

Following his death, Queen Abigail appointed Edmund Kincaid as regent because Carrig III's heir, Colin, was only eight years old. Colin would eventually assume his father's throne in the .
