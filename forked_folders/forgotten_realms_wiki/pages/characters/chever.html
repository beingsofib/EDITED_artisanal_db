Chever was a Netherese archwizard and well-renowned mentalist.

Personality

Chever was an arcanist obsessed with discovering new things and retaining the knowledge of them.

History

Chever was born in and established the Netherese enclave Opus at 39 years old. Upon Opus, he ordered the construction of universities, theatres, and other academic buildings. Chever was also interested in spell creation, and developed contact other plane in and simulacrum 11 years later. Driven by his obsession with acquiring knowledge, he used his first spell to repeatedly contact extraplanar beings.
