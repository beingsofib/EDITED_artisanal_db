Valantajar was a rakshasa who worked as a private investigator in Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

By the , Valantajar went by the name Vincent Trench and managed the Tiger's Eye, a detective business on Trollskull Alley in the North Ward. He could discover any secret in the city in exchange for gold or a service to him.

Personality

Valantajar had lived for many years in Waterdeep, changing identities as necessary. He had grown accustomed to living among mortals and—to his own astonishment—was rather fond of the city and its inhabitants.
