Prellyn Teh'Kinrellz was a drow member of House Teh'Kinrellz in Ched Nasad in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

Prellyn was the main enforcer of the Matron Teh'Kinrellz in the ruins of Ched Nasad. She acted as an overseer to Q'arlynd Melarn, also using him for sex.

History

In the , Q'arlyn subtly learned from Prellyn information about a medallion of Eilistraee he had found while scavenging in the rubble.
