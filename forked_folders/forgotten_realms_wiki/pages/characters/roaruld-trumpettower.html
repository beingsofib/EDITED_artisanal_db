Roaruld Trumpettower, called the Scourge-of-Stirges, was an ancestor of the Trumpettower family before the 3 century DR.

History

A portrait of Roaruld Trumpettower was hung up in the bridal suite where Peeryst Trumpettower and Nanue Glarmeir were to consummate their marriage in the . In it, the Scourge-of-Stirges was depicted with a glass of blood in one hand and a wrung-out stirge with limp wings on the other.
