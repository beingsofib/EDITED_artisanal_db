Alahar Khaumfros was the Waterbaron of Yartar during the mid–14 century DR. During this time, he was also the leader of the Kraken Society.

Relationships

Khaufros employed the doppelganger Semmonemily to do his dirty work and had an elderly steward named Cladence.

History

Around the , four illithids walked into the Waterbaron's Hall and murdered Alahar. He was killed for his fraudulent dealings of the Kraken Society's money.
