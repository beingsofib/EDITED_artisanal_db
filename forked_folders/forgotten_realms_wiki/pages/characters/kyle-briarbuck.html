Kyle Briarbuck was a ranger of Cormyr who met a foul end in Lost Souls' Swamp.

Personality

Kyle was rather steadfast and sensible, even as a disembodied soul.

History

After living a life near Tyrluk as a ranger, Kyle embarked on an adventure that led him to what would become known as Lost Souls' Swamp. He was slain by the will-o'-wisps controlled by the shadow of Lost Souls' Swamp and kept as an imprisoned soul for them to feed on, along with Evrim Whitehair and Callie Brennan. Though held by the efforts of the shadow's mudmen, vine horrors and wisps, their souls may have been freed by Harpers sent by Eradin Krall and Rina Belostos as they pursued Sshaastess and her Nether Scroll circa 1485 DR. When they arrived, Kyle explained the situation to them and how the domineering shadow could be defeated, saving the trio as well as future travelers.
