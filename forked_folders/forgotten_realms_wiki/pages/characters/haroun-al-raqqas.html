Haroun al-Raqqas was the caliph of Wasat in 1367 DR.

Description

Haroun's eyes were always bleary.

Activities

Haroun acquired the position of caliph after the untimely fatal accident of his predecessor. Haroun's quick wit and family tree earned him the title, which he later came to regret.

History

Haroun was also referred to as "Blood of the First Caliph" as he was indeed a descendant of the First Caliph, though his lineage was quite diluted.

Possessions

The caliph wore a magical ring that could summon a powerful sorcerer to his aid at a moment's notice.
