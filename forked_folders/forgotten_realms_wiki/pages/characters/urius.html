Urius was a skilled wizard inhabiting Gheldaneth in central Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Urius was a member of the Brotherhood of Skuld, a group of adventurers who worked to hinder the influence of Set and the god's minions in Mulhorand.
