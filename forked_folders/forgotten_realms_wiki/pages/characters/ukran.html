Ukran the Great was a wizard who lived in Ravens Bluff in the mid–14 century DR.

Home

Ukran lived in a blue two-story house on Magician's Row It was the second of five houses. Written on the wall above the door were the Thorass letters for "Thay".

Description

Ukran had a crow familiar, smoked Dragonfire tobacco, and was primarily a tea drinker.

History

Circa 1357 DR, the City Guard serving Regent of the Exchequer Vernon Condor considered Ukran the Great for the theft of two minor magic items of historical importance from the city vaults, after suspicions and divinations tracked the thieves to Magician's Row. However, Ukran was innocent.
