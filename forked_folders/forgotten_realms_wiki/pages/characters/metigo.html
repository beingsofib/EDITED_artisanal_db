Metigo was a butler who worked in the employ of the noble Arnise family of Amn during the mid–14 century DR.

Relationships

He helped maintain Arnise Hold alongside Olmara the De'Arnise family cook and Talira the maid.

History

Metigo was present in Arnise Hold when it was taken over by TorGal and his band of trolls in the .
