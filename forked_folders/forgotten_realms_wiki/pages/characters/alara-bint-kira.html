Alara bint Kira was a young noble of minor importance living in Huzuz, the City of Delights, around 1367 DR.

Description

Alara enjoyed flaunting her wealth with the finest clothes. Sheer fabric was used to cover her face and arms. She wore a wide variety of golden jewelry and gems.

Relationships

Alara's mother was the second cousin of the Grand Caliph's father.
