Leone was a captain of longbowmen in the Golden Legion mercenary company in the mid-14 century DR.

History

Leone and his longbowmen were part of the expedition that came to Maztica in the . He fought in the Legion's battle against the Payit near Ulatos. Following the battle, he agreed with assessor Kardann's idea that they should send ships back to Amn to request reinforcements.
