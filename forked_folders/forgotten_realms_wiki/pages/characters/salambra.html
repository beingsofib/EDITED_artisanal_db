Salambra the she-wolf of Surthay was a woman from Thay, renowned for her beauty.

History

In , the palace guard Thaergar of Aglarond had a pack of playing cards featuring the likeness of the greatest beauties of Thay. One of these cards featured a picture of Salambra. He gave his belt with those to the Simbul; one of the sorceresses under her command, Phaeldara, returned the belt with its playing cards shortly after.
