Aeark was a human barbarian cleric terrorizing the countryside in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Living only for her faith, she would demand anyone weaker than her to "convert or die". As such she found it hard to gain any friends, but she did manage to gain a small following of a few neophytes who took up her belief (at swordpoint), and fought just as savagely as she.

Possessions

She fought in leather armor, with a bronze chest plate, and a girdle of giant strength, while wielding a footman's flail.
