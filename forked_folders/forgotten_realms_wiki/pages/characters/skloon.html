Lord Skloon was a Red Wizard in the mid–14 century DR.

Abilities

Being a Red Wizard, he presumably cast spells.

Relationships

He was a hated rival of Amalrae, but the two of them formed a cabal under the guidance of Halaster Blackcloak. This cabal also boasted among its ranks two Sharran priests, and the Red Wizards Azmyrandyr, Roeblen, Lord Harkon, Thaltar Glaervar, and more unnamed co-conspirators.

History

Lord Skloon was one of the high-ranked members of Halaster Blackcloak's nameless conspiracy revealed in the by the Seven Sisters. He participated in an emergency meeting to address the slayings of Azmyrandyr and Roeblen, who were spell-linked to Thaltar Glaervar, another Red Wizard. During the meeting, he suggested this connection gave them the opportunity to set an ambush for the Simbul, who'd come for Thaltar Glaervar. Thaltar then revealed he'd been attacked by her earlier that day, but had already slain her; he revealed a wand he'd taken from her body. The wand turned out to be the Simbul in spell-shape, who immediately set about to the task of slayng them all.

In his last moments, he prayed to Mystra and embraced Speaker Amalrae.
