Maggran was a druid who lived in the Emerald Grove during he late 15 century DR.

Description

The powerful druid had tattoos across his right arm, on his chin, and around his eyes.

Abilities

Magran was capable of wild shaping into the form of a mighty bear.

History

In the , tieflings exiled from the city of Elturel were granted refuge at the Emerald Grove by archdruid Halsin. During their stay, a child by the name of Arabella attempted to steal the idol of Silvanus from its altar within the grove. When the child's parents rushed into the grove to try and save her from judgement, Maggran and two fellow druids were forced to intervene.
