Fridrik Hhune was a member of the Hhune family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

Fridrik Hhune perished the , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldur's Gate. Fridrik's tombstone was commissioned to be carved by Candulhallow's Tombstones in the Lower City. His freshly-carved grave marker read: "Hid last connection with this realm, severed."
