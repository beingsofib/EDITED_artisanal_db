Jamal Hsirani was a human proxy of the Faerûnian power known as Azuth.

Description

Jamal was a rather tall man, with dark skin and curly hair. He typically dressed in lionskins.

Personality

This proxy had a fondness for making grand entrances and disappearing sneakily.

Possessions

Jamal carried around a bone staff that was the size of a grown human.

History

Jamal was last seen in Mechanus on the layer of Nemausus, working alongside a proxy named Feryli Krenurum in helping the resistance movement of Arcadian petitioners there to avoid the modron hunters. His aid was considered to be a pivotal factor in the resistance's efforts to live in Nemausus following its shift from Arcadia.
