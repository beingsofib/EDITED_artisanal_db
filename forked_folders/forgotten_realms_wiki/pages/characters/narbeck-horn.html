Narbeck Horn was the shield dwarf commander of Xantharl's Keep sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

Activities

Narbeck commanded the local garrison of 150 veterans and responded directly to the marchion of Mirabar using a sending stone in order to inform the city of any menace.
