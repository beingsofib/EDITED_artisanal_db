Lhabbartan was a mage at some point before the 8 century DR.

History

In the , the sage Beldrune, working at the Moonshorn Tower, opined about a certain Olbert's triple-delayed blast fireball spell. Beldrune noted that Olbert had created that spell by combining the work of Iliymbrim Sharnult, Lhabbartan, and a third person whose name he could not recall at the moment. This strongly implied that Lhabbartan's work predated Olbert's.
