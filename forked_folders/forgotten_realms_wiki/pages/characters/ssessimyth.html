Ssessimyth was a powerful kraken in the Sea of Fallen Stars in the late 15 century DR.

History

Somehow, Ssessimyth entered into symbiosis with the mythal of the ruined Netherese enclave of Sakkors. When the Princes of Shade Rivalen Tanthul and Brennus Tanthul restored the flying city, Rivalen managed to enslave Ssessimyth. On Uktar 11 of the , Rivalen ordered him to attack the docks of Yhaunn in order to distract the city's guards.
