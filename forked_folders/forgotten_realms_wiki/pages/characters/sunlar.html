Sunlar was the high priest of Mystra's temple in Deepingdale during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He was a strikingly handsome man, with strong features. He had wavy brown hair and blue-white eyes that gave off an otherworldly impression.

Relationships

At some point, Sunlar took notable interest in one of his temple's students named Midnight and personally oversaw her instruction in the arcane arts.
