Imlon "the Touched" was the second Obarskyr monarch and king of Cormyr. He was crowned in Year of the Caustic Blood, 55 DR and ruled for two years before his death in Year of Lamp-lit Nights, 57 DR.

Relatives

Imlon's father was the legendary Faerlthann Obarskyr. Imlon also had three younger brothers Emrus, Eskruis and Rhiiman; and a son, Bryndar.
