Nather was a merchant inhabiting Amn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Nather was robbed of a set of six identical statues on a trip to Waterdeep in the . The valuable statues depicted elven males carved from white marble and standing tall. As the statues were stolen from a locked warehouse in Waterdeep's Dock Ward without any sign of forced entry, Nather suspected the use of magic and recruited the help of the Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors to help find his missing property.
