Xylene was a fomorian giant witch and a member of the coven led by Magra Curse-Eye who dwelled in the dark fey enclave within the Feywild.

Abilities

Xylene was a particularly skilled alchemist. She was renowned in her coven for the variety of potions she brewed, which were capable of emitting noxious fumes and vapors.

History

Circa the , Xylene traveled to a quickling den in order to drive the fey creatures from their home and conscript them into the dark fey army of King Malabog.
