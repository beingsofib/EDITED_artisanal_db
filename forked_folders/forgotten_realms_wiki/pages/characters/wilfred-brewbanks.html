Wilfred Brewbanks was a traveler along the High Road between Neverwinter and Luskan during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Wilfred was bald, but had a full, light brown beard.

History

Some time around , Wilfred and his brother Richard joined the merchant's caravan of Tollin and his crew going from Neverwinter to Luskan, accompanied by members of the Order of the Burning Dawn. However, Richard, who was carrying a very valuable ancient elven antique, went missing during the journey, and members of the caravan's group were attacked by mercenaries, an assault that was set up by the treacherous Martumal.
