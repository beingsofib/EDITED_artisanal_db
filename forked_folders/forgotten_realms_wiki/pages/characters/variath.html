Variath was a powerful druid in the Chondalwood in 1358 DR.

Activities

Variath governed a section of Chondalwood.

History

Variath was born and raised in the Chondalwood, only venturing into the outside world once.

In 1358 DR, Variath found ten members of the Sailors of the Crimson Sea mercenary company inside his forest after they had killed a deer, a terrible crime in Variath's eyes. He captured them and put them on trial.

Personality

Variath believed that all outside the Chondalwood was evil and that men were evil. He hated what he had seen in Faerûn. In truth, Variath was full of petty hatred and wanted to vent his ire on someone.
