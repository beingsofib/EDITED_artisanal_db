Azazabus was a wizard and evil overlord in the Dalelands at some point prior to the 14 century DR.

Abilities

He was a wizard.

Personality

He was evil.

Possessions

He had a small home in Mount Dread, and a library there too.

Activities

He ruled over and worked with a number of monsters.

History

Centuries prior to the 14 century DR, Azazabus built himself a home under Mount Dread, attracting many kinds of monsters to work with and serve him. For years, the monsters raided the surface, while he worked underground on secret experiments. Then, at some point, he simply disappeared, leaving the place to the monsters.
