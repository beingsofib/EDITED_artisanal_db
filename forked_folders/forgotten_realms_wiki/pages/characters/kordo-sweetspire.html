Kordo Sweetspire was a halfling performer and Harper agent who worked in Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Kordo was a short fellow with shoulder-length white-blond hair.

History

After the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon circa 1485 DR, Kordo joined Rina Belostos's Harpers in Eveningstar as they tracked Oriphaun Huntsilver and the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll. While acting as a troupe of musical entertainers (Kordo played the flute), he determined that Oriphaun had left a piece with William Windsphere, the bartender at the Old Man's Face in Tyrluk. He sent his agents off on what seemed like an easy mission, only for them to run into the Shadovar wizards Wyle, Tarys, and their mercenary minions.
