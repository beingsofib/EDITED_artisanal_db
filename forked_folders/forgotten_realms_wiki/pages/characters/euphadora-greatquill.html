Euphadora Greatquill was a writer and anthropologist author of One Foot, Two Feet, Well Met, We'll Meet, published some years before the late 15 century DR.

History

Euphadora's book, One Foot, Two Feet, Well Met, We'll Meet, was a volume that collected, recorded, and archived hin children's rhymes and mnemonics. This book was quite popular and by the , its copies were found throughout the Western Heartlands, the Sword Coast, and the city of Baldur's Gate.
