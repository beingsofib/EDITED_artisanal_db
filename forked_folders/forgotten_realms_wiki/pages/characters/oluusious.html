Oluusious was the tribe mother of the grippli village of Urml in the Wetwoods in the Shining Plains around 1370 DR. She was nearly 400 years old.

Personality

Oluusious was afraid of humanoid expeditions into the Wetwoods, worrying that human expansion would mean their small village being added to human lands.
