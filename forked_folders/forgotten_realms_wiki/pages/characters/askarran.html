Askarran was a warrior and sage inhabiting Selgaunt in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Askarran's research focused on theology, cult history, and the nature of undeath.

Of note, he insisted that nothing was known on what would happen when a dracolich would reach such bodily degradation that it reached nigh-demilich status, remarking it was unwise to speculate.
