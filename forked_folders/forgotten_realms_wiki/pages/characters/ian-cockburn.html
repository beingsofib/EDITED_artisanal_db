Ian Cockburn was a shopkeeper in Phlan in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Ian was the owner of the eponymous grocery shop located in the residential district.

Personality

Ian was a welcoming man with a wide smile. He was known for his fair prices, but wasn't one to bargain much.

Possessions

Cockburn was in possession of, and possessed by, a strange artifact which he found in his cellar. When the artifact overtook him, the shopkeeper felt the irresistible impulse to kill. He usually satisfied this need on unsuspecting adventurers—but on one occasion killed his wife, which he was openly grieving.
