Iriskree was an old, reclusive half-elf sorceress of considerable power from the Starmantle area as of the mid-late 13 century DR. She was the first teacher for the Magister Ohland Grethgar.

History

She died at some point prior to the , of a winter fever; before that point, she'd written to her last kinswoman that the young Ohland had a spark for the art that was brighter than most.
