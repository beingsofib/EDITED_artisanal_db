Kusphia was a member of the reformed Cult of the Dragon under Severin Silrajin.

Possessions

In battle, Kusphia wore leather armor and fought with a scimitar.

History

Around 1489 DR, the dragonclaw was a cultist and head assistant of Talis Kasterel, at her master's side in the Cult's occupied hunting lodge in the Greypeak Mountains. She was devoted to Talis to the point of dying for her.
