Chief Tokan was the leader of the dwarven Orothiar Clan in Bloodstone Barony as of the .

Activities

Tokan normally spoke of ancient events like they'd happened yesterday.

Possessions

He carried with him an enchanted dwarven thrower hammer that was revered by his clan.

History

In 1357 DR, Tokan and his tribe met the adventuring party of Gareth Dragonsbane.

Later in the Tokan and his people had joined the Alliance of Belt Watchers alongside a group of local halflings and centaurs.
