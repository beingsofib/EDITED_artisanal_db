Rendal Ironguard was a Harper spy and the cook at Warmfires House in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was grim-faced.

Activities

He was a cook, and a Harper.

Relationships

He saluted Storm Silverhand in the manner of a fellow Harper and followed her orders, rendering it a foregone conclusion he was in fact a Harper.

History

In , Rendal Ironguard was the cook at Warmfires House during the night in which the Zhentarim attempted the assassination of Maervidal Iloster, a local scribe and spy of theirs. Later into the evening, much of the staff revealed themselves to be Harpers, and captured the Zhentarim operatives present.
