Ferremo Magli was an assassin and the chief servant of the green dragon, Andareunarthex in the late 15 century DR.

Description

The blonde-haired Ferremo liked to wear nice clothes because he found that they made him less noticeable than clothing favored by most assassins.

History

In the , Magli helped kidnap Nazra Mrays's young son so Andarenarthex could extort the Dragonstaff from Nazra.

During his rescue of Clytemorrenestrix, Tennora Hedare killed Magli in Andareunarthex's hideout.
