Sharp-eye Nass was a goblin scout and a member of the camp that followed the faithful of the Absolute and dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

As one of her goblin camp's sharp-eyes, Nass acted as a scout working under Skrut.

History

Nass was among the raiders that burned down Waukeen's Rest in the . While Nass endured an injured arm, she managed to make it back to the Shattered Sanctum alive.
