Bollus, nicknamed Copperteeth, was an owner of a caravan company based in Westgate on the Dragon Coast and a member of the Rundeen in the late 14 century DR.

History

Bollus originated from Calimport in Calimshan, before setting up his company in Westgate. He spent so many years there that by 1370 DR he had stopped using his family and house names for ease and instead went by his nickname, Copperteeth.

Activities

Bollus's caravan business in Westgate specialized in going back and forth between the two cities, in which he consolidated the small cargoes of local merchants into larger ones for transport. He had a reputation for fair yet cunning dealing.

Description

Four of Bollus's upper teeth were made of copper.

Possessions

He wore bracers of defense fashioned from copper, as well as much copper-adorned jewelry. He had peculiar predilection for this metal, which he used and wore in all forms.

Rumors & Legends

His nickname, Copperteeth, derived from either his false teeth or his alleged ability to haggle traders down to very last copper piece they had to spare.
