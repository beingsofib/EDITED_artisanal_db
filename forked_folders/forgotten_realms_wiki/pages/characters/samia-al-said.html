Samia al-Sa'id was the elven khedive of Rog'osto in 1367 DR.

Personality

Samia's enlightened progressive rule endeared her to her subjects.

Activities

Samia had been the khedive of Rog'osto for 100 years as of 1367 DR. She spent much of her time settling disputes between the tower lords of the city, as well as serving as a buffer for the greedy representatives from Afyal and the Cities of the Pantheon.

In addition to her political activities, Samia was a skilled sha'ir who collected an impressive array of magic items over the years.
