Hariah Highkin was a ruler of the kingdom of Doegan in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

After previous turmoil, Doegan fell into the safe and steady hands of Hariah Highkin. The capital prospered, the borders held firm, and people were happy. Nevertheless, on a quest to unify the wartorn Utter East through conquest, the leader of the Legendary Campaign returned and tried to conquer Doegan, no matter the cost to the realm.
