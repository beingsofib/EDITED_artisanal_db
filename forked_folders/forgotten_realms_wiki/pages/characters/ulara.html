Ulara was the precept of the city of Jhalhoran in central Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

As precept of Jhalhoran, Ulara was responsible for the city and surrounding lands to the west, particularly the Great Eastern Trade Route. Ulara was a priestess of Nephthys.
