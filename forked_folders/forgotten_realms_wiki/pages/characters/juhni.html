Juhni was a druid of Thorngap during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Juhni had light skin and long white hair.

Personality

Under the influence of Halsaime's staff, Juhni was utterly devoted to his mad cause, though she was still quite a bit less bloodthirsty and aggressive than her fellow victims.

Abilities

Juhni specialized in transforming into a huge dire bear, eschewing other forms of combat.

History

After the corruption of Halsaime and the subversion of his druidic companions, Juhni defended the hidden Druid's Deep, along with the ranger Kuldail Ukien. Though loyal to Halsaime, she did not wish bloodshed, and offered intruders the opportunity to leave unharmed rather than attacking on sight.
