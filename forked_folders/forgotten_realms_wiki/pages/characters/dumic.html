Dumic, who was nicknamed the Red, was a rancher inhabiting the town of Ashabenford in Mistledale during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was a large and boisterous fellow but possessed great wisdom.

Activities

Dumic was a member of the ruling Council of Six in Ashabenford.
