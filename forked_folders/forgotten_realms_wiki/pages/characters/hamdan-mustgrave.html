Hamdan Mustgrave was a martial cleric in Cormyr during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Hamdan had light skin and short black hair. He wore a suit of plate mail and carried a morning star.

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, Hamdan was in the village and helped train prospective clerics there.
