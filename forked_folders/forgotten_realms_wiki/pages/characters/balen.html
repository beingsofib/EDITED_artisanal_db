Balen was a bodyguard who served in the employ of Lord Farnig Seasilver of Marsember during the late 15 century DR.

Description

He had a distinctive red beard.

History

When Lady Elira Seasilver was captured aboard the Wave Wyvern on Uktar 2 in the , Balen was among the guards from House Seasilver who joined the rescue efforts.
