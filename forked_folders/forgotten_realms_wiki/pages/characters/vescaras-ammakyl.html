Vescaras Ammakyl was a half-elf Harper agent of the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Vescaras was a noble of Waterdeep. He used his position in House Ammakyl's business to run a network of spies.

History

In the , Vescaras learned from one of his spies of a smuggling ring trafficking gems, weapons, and people from Vaasa to Sembia. He and his fellow Khochen went on a mission to Westgate to collect clues about it. During the mission, they destroyed a caravan of shadovar trading with drow in the Dalelands.

Afterward, they went to Waterdeep to make a report to Dahl Peredur and Tam Zawad.

Later, he and Khochen were chosen to help, and survey, Mehen in his research into the missing Farideh, whom Vescaras believed to be a shadovar agent.

Relationships

He disliked Dahl Peredur, who believed this was due to his flirting with Vescaras's sister Jadzia Ammakyl. In fact, it was only due to Dahl's abrasive personality. Behind Dahl’s back, he actually spoke fondly of the Oghmanite paladin, and considered him a valued asset to the Harpers, something he admitted to Farideh when Dahl had to leave Cormyr suddenly due to his family being endangered. His fellow agent Khochen once claimed to have had a sexual affair with him, but this ultimately turned out of be false.
