Saint Fanal was a priest and saint of Ilmater and the founder of the Monastery of St. Fanal in the Alimir Mountains.

Personality

St. Fanal, although a priest of Ilmater dedicated to alleviating suffering, believed that knowledge of the past would limit suffering in the present, and that educating oppressed peoples would expand their opportunities and capabilities. Thus he devoted himself to learning, preserving, and teaching history.

History

St. Fanal founded the monastery that bore his name late in the first century before Dalereckoning.
