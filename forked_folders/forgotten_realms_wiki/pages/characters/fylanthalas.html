Fylanthalas

Fylanthalas was allegedly a great mage who lived sometime before 1372 DR.

Possessions

The most notable item in Fylanthalas's possession was his crystal ball, used for scrying.

History

Even though stories claimed his greatness, Fylanthalas was mostly remembered because of his crystal ball that was famously dropped and cracked as the wizard tried to skip on a dinning bill. The cracked crystal ball was kept in Daggerford's curio shop, Stop Hole Abbey in 1372 DR.
