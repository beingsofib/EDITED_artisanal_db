Lady Hawklin was a young member of the Hawklin family in the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the , she was part of a revel in which other guests included Andemel Graeven and Sabrart Windrider. They observed that she had failed to hold her wine, and had to be carried away by servants. Her dressing gown was stained with the red of the wine she had drunk at the time.
