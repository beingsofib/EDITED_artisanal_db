Pavel Tragosk was a Raumviran elected official and inhabitant of Drubiev in the Hordelands in 1375 DR.

Activities

Pavel was a member of the Council of Five. He was an outspoken voice for a portion of the population of Drubiev who supported expanding their city's influence through trade along the Golden Way. He used the lure of wealth and power to help swing votes toward his cause.
