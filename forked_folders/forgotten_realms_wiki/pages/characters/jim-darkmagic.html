James Winifred Darkmagic III, better known by his nickname Jim Darkmagic, was a friend of Omin Dran, as well as an employee of his company, Acquisitions Incorporated.

Relationships

Jim Darkmagic gave an autographed portrait of himself to the wizard Yarek, a student of Dweomercore in Undermountain.
