Halana Shauluth was a baker in the village of Amphail on the Sword Coast North around 1370 DR.

Activities

She baked hard loaves of wheat and meat-filled buns.

History

Halana was formerly a mercenary who was in a group with a dozen female dwarves, but she lost her hand after it became infected with gangrene, She then retired from that career to become a baker.

She claimed to have seen the "Ghost of Amphail", the ghost of Amphail the Just (after whom the village was named) and that the ghost rescued her from thieves who were trying to rob her.
