Draven Millovyr was an innkeeper in Triboar sometime after 1385 DR.

History

Draven tried to join the Arcane Brotherhood of Luskan but was refused. He later became an innkeeper in Triboar by buying the Everwyvern House. He turned it into an overpriced inn that matched the standard of nobles from Waterdeep.
