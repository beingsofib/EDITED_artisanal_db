Lady Winstra Hullhollyn a socialite member of the noble patriar Hullhollyn family of Baldur's Gate and a member of the Parliament of Peers in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Winstra Hullhollyn was a human woman with dusky skin. She wore a diadem atop styled arm drown hair. The lady wore a tight embroidered blue blouse and quality leather pants.

History

In the , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldur's Gate, Lady Winstra Hullhollyn was a Parliamentarian of the Gate and voted in support of crowning lowborn Lord Enver Gortash as the city's Archduke. Lady Hullhollyn was present for Gortash's coronation that took place in the audience hall of Wyrm's Rock. Following the coronation, Lady Winstra Hullhollyn conversed with a fellow Parliamentarian Lord Randolf Vammas, lamenting cancelations of social events due to the armies of the Absolute advancing on the Gate. She believed that the new Archduke would be a more fun leader and a charismatic, unlike boring old Ulder Ravengard.
