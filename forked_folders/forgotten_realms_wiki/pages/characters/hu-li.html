Master Hu Li was a great artificer of Kara-Tur, who was little known in Faerûn of the mid–14th century DR.

Hu Li fashioned the magical katanas known as Master Li's Way. These swords, bearing the maker's mark of Master Hu Li, turned up in northwest Faerûn in the 1360s DR.
