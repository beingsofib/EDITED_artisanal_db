Saeval Calaudra was a moon elf rogue and assassin and, as of 1374 DR, was a member of the Vel'Nikeryma.

Activities

He offered support and expertise to Eldreth Veluuthra cells across Faerûn and introduced people to the Vel'Nikeryma. As of 1374 DR he was the youngest member of the organization.
