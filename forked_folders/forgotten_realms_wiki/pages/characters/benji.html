Benji was a member of the Emerald Enclave who served the organization during the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Benji was a fervent supporter of the Emerald Enclave, believing them to be stalwart allies of the natural world of Faerûn. According to him, they were the best people to bring about balance between nature and the civilized world.

Relationships

Benji had two living parents, and at least one sibling, a sister named Abigail who served at the High House of Wonders in Baldur's Gate.

History

At some point, Benji's sister wrote him a letter asking him to visit her in the city. She also reminded him of their father's upcoming birthday.
