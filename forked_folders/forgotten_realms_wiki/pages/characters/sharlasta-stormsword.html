Sharlasta stormsword was a sea-captain in Waterdeep sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

Activities

Sharlasta was the captain of the "Ravenous". She was an infamous pirate.

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR Sharlasta and her ship were captured by the Lords' Alliance and she was chained in Neverwinter.
