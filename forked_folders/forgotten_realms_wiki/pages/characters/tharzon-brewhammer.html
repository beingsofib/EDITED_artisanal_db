Tharzon Brewhammer was a retired dwarf adventurer and the owner of the Smoke Wyrm tavern in Ravens Bluff in the Vast in the late 15 century DR. He ran it with his son, Kurzen Brewhammer.

History

In the early 1370s DR, Tharzon was an acquaintance of Jack Ravenwild and he was shocked when Jack turned up at his tavern in the , after escaping his one-hundred year imprisonment in the mythal beneath the city. Although he was too old to help Jack with his scheme to recover the Sarkonagael from Sarbreen, he offered Jack advice and even managed to negotiate a cut of the profits from the venture.

Description

Tharzon used a cane and had a grey beard and bushy eyebrows.
