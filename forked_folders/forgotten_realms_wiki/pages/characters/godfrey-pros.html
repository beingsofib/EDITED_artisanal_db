Godfrey was a warrior who lived in Pros and fought for the Dragon Cult that had taken power in the town during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was a burly and confident man who was quite aggressive in his dealings with non-cultists.

Possessions

Along with his fellow warriors, Godfrey wore a steel breastplate and the black cap with the image of a dragon that was so closely associated with their group.

Activities

He and his associates would extort silver from vessels that attempted to moor their boats in Pros, demanding a "harbor tax" from every individual.

Relationships

Godfrey was one of the Black Caps the cult employed as enforcers, alongside his fellow Henry.

History

The over-confident warrior was slain in the by Vaerana Hawklyn, when he attempted to strong arm her Harper associate Ruha.
