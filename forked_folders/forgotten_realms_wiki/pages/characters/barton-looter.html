Barton was a looter who was active in the Western Heartlands during the late 15 century DR. For a time, he was under the employ of Gimblebock.

Possessions

He wore a suit of leather armor and used a bow when in combat.

History

In the 1490s DR, Barton and his fellow looter, Mari, had been trying to break into a crypt for a few days. They had made some progress, and had encountered skeletons. Not wanting to go home empty handed yet again, he was determined to get further in.
