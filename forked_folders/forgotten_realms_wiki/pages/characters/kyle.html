Kyle was a commoner inhabitant of the City Core of Neverwinter in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Kyle was a human man with broad shoulders and a muscled body, a light skin color, and blonde hair. He wore simple commoner's garb, and the stress of the plague was weighing heavily on him.

Activities

During the Wailing of the , Kyle could be found strolling the streets of the City Core, safe away from the dangers of the quarantined and zombie-infested Beggar's Nest and the convict-overrun Peninsula District.
