Jesse was a warrior and guard inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Jesse aided Daggerford's constabulary by setting up watches and training troops.

History

Before coming to Daggerford Jesse was well-liked guard in Amphail until an injury to her shield hand forced her to retire.
