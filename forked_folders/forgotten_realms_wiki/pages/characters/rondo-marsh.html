Rondo Marsh was a ship mage in the mid-to-late 14 century DR, who was briefly involved in a mission to create an alliance between Selgaunt and Myth Nantar.

Description

Rondo Marsh was tanned and freckled, red-haired, green-eyed, and .

Personality

Rondo was a relentlessly hard and loyal worker who bore an intense hatred for the rich and powerful. He was not completely comfortable with the position of ship's mage, because of the implications of wealth and education that it had.

Abilities

In spite of his competence as a wizard, Rondo Marsh was not actually very bright. His hardiness, however, was beyond heroic, and when avenging friends, he had the strength (and recklessness) of a berserker. He also had developed many important seamanship skills, such as fishing, swimming, weather predictions, underwater spellcasting, and even spear wielding.

Most notably, his spellbook contained gust of wind, lightning bolt, and water breathing.

Possessions

Rondo Marsh's spellbook was one of Boccob's blessed books, and he carried a potion of water breathing. His only other possessions of note were an ointment that protected him against sunburns and an oiled canvas poncho.

Activities

He was the ship mage of the Emerald Tayar.

Relationships

Rondo Marsh worked for Coprates Bile, a pirate captain. He was infatuated with Ilsa Merrymore, to the point he was capable of flying into a berserker rage if she was hurt.

During the expedition, he took an intense dislike to Sturge Whitsunday, an important paladin and politician. The paladin's sister Aleine proved an exception to his scorn for upper-class people, on the other hand. The inventor Nagreous Twiddle, he just thought of as a very funny little man with big words.

History

Born in abject poverty in Selgaunt, Rondo Marsh eventually became an oarsman on a Sembian trader. He had made it an habit of working himself like a mule by his mid-teens, a trait only matched by his dislike for the wealthy and influential.

At the age of eighteen, he stole a wizard's spellbook, and realized he could cast some spells, which got him abandoned on shore by his old crew. He signed on to the Emerald Tayar under Captain Coprates Bile. He worked hard to learn more spells as the ship's mage.

During an expedition in Mirtul of the , in which the Emerald Tayar's crew were hired to find a hidden underwater city by the description of Myth Nantar, and bring Sturge Whitsunday there so that a mutual defense agreement could be reached, Rondo Marsh was part of the crew.
