Miranda "the Fair" Cymrych was High Queen of the Moonshae Isles at the turn of the 7 century DR.

Relationships

She was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the daughter of her predecessor, High King Gordon. Her successor was her first son, Embro Cymrych I.
