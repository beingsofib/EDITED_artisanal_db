Gureth the Obedient was an elf warlock in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Gureth had pale orange hair and dark green eyes. He wore an ornate leaf-styled green robe and could be seen floating off the ground in a ring of flickering energy.

History

In the during the Elven Crusade, Gureth joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp on the outskirts of Myth Drannor. He served as a trainer for other warlocks who passed by.
