Leandran was a male drow sorcerer and the Master of Abjuration at Sorcere.

History

He was killed by duergar stonefire pots during the Siege of Menzoberranzan sometime between the , and the . The magic fire hit the sorcerer directly, and left behind nothing but the stench of charred elven meat.
