Tluth Melarn was a drow in Menzoberranzan in the late 14 century DR. He was one of Houndaer Tuin'Tarl and Syrzan's rebel group.

Abilities

He was highly skilled at massage and was favored by Waerva Baenre.

History

During the Silence of Lolth in the , Tluth joined Houndaer's group desiring to start a slave revolt Tluth and Alton Vandree acted as bodyguards of the magical disguised Syrzan at one meeting of goblinoid slaves.
