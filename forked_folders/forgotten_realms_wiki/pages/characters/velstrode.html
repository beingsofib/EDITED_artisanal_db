Velstrode the Venturer was a notable adventurer and successful merchant who lived in the city of Waterdeep in the 14 century DR.

Description

Velstrode was a rather tall and bearded man, who traveled around with defensive magics on his person.

Activities

Velstrode ran an equipment shop out of his home in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep.
