Shaliana Ghend was a merchant in Scardale in 1368 DR.

Activities

Shaliana managed the Scardale Provisioner.

History

Shaliana was the only merchant to survive without damage the rule of Lashan Aumersair. She only relocated to outside the town of Scardale after the Second inter-dale war. In 1368 DR, she decided to send her daughter Talana Ghend to reopen inside the city.
