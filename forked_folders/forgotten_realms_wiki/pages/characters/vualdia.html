Vualdia was a dukar of the Order of Maliirn and a morkoth member of the Nantarn Council.

Personality

Vualdia was a strictly conservative individual, believing that her opinion was fact. Vualdia was arrogant and argumentative, often citing pieces of forgotten lore that only she knew. She had no sense for compromise but despite all this, some councilors were swayed by her arguments.
