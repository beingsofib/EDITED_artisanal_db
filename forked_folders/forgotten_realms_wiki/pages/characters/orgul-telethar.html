Orgul Telethar was a tanner inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Orgul was the guildmaster for the League of Skinners & Tanners circa 1357 DR. He held the title of "Leaguemaster" within the guild and worked out of Telethar Leatherworks and the League Hall—the guild headquarters on the Tower Trail in the Dock Ward.
