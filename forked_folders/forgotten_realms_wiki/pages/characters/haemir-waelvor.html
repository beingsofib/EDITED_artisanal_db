Haemir Waelvor was an inhabitant of Myth Drannor in the 3 century DR, and a scion of house Waelvor.

Appearance

He was cruel-faced.

Abilities

Besides the spells he used to strike at Bhuraelea and Mladris, he meant to consume their lives to power his shield-sundering.

History

In , Haemir Waelvor was part of an attack on Mythanthor's tower. He struck down the two sorceresses guarding the high mage, Bhuraelea and Mladris, in a surprise attack; however, he was thwarted finally by Elminster, who had been present as some sort of phantom. He was finally slain by Mythanthor himself.
