Bruck was a goblin chieftain of an Underdark tribe of three dozen close to Blingdenstone in the 14 century DR.

History

A svirfneblin expedition, among which numbered Brickers, Belwar Dissengulp, and Drizzt Do'Urden, encountered Bruck's tribe. In the hopes of avoiding conflict, they conducted a ruse in which Drizzt claimed drow dominion over the surroundings and the svirfneblin as slaves, and successfully intimidated Bruck to leave. Some time later, the tribe was accosted by the reanimated Zaknafein Do'Urden, who, in his bloodlust, slayed Bruck and some fifteen other goblins.
