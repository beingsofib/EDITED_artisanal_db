The Dancer of Life, who was also known simply as the Grandmother, was the highest ranking priestess among the faithful clergy of Shiallia, the Lady of the Woods.

Activities

The Dancer of Life was a venerable korred who was believed to have been alive to witness the fall of Ascalhorn. The Grandmother lived within the Glade of Life, a sacred location to Shiallia within the High Forest, throughout the year.
