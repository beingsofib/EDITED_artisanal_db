Pheldren was a page in the High Palace of Silverymoon in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Appearance

He was but a boy.

Activities

He was a page.

History

In , Pheldren was a witness to one of the palace guests, someone impersonating Oscalar Maerbree, entering the Red Griffon Room and then leaving, both times accompanied by guards. He also saw the palace steward, Rorild, entering the room and shouting for Taern Hornblade to come, having discovered the murder. Alustriel Silverhand later called on him to testify to that for her, which he did. He got a feast and a day off for being so helpful.
