Korr Odahn

Person
nameKorr Odahn
homeUtter East

Korr Odahn was a person in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

Whilst crossing the high mountains of the Utter East, Legendary Campaign forces found themselves enveloped in a mysterious fog. As it lifted, they found themselves in Phantom Pass and being threatened with a grisly fate by an angry Korr Odahn, whom they faced in bloodforge battle.
