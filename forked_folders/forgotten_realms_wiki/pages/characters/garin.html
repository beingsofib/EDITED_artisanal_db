Garin was a deva in service to Tyr in 1384–1385 DR.

History

In 1384 DR, Garin was assigned by his friend and superior Micus to act as the jailor of Kaanyr Vhok, who had infiltrated the House of the Triad. However, Aliisza and Tauran deceived Garin and freed Kaanyr, believing that they needed his help to capture the more dangerous Zasian Menz, also an infiltrator. For this, Garin was questioned by his superiors.

When the Spellplague hit, Garin was one of the commanders of the celestial armies that tried to repel the evil outsiders that arrived in Celestia.
