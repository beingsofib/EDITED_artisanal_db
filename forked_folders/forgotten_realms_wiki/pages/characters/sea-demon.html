Sea Demon

Person
nameSea Demon
homeThe Whamite Isles, Sea of Fallen Stars

The Sea Demon was a fearsome creature from the waters surrounding the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. While a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of raged on the Whamite Isles, the Sea Demon would appear suddenly and attack sea vessels.
