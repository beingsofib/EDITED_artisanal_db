Salida () was a yuan-ti pureblood posing as a human guide in Port Nyanzaru during the late 15 century DR. She was in fact a spy for Ras Nsi who relayed information about expeditions that ventured south into the jungles of Chult.

Appearance

She was tall and weighed . Salida appeared physically fit and maintained the appearance of an experienced traveler.

Due to her yuan-ti heritage, areas of her skin appeared as scales.

Personality

Salida was adept as creating vivid and fantastic lies to obscure her identity as a spy. She was particularly witty and adroit.

Possessions

Salida possessed a sending stone that she used to communicate with Ras Nsi.
