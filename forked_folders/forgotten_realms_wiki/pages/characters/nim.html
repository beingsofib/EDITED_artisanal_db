Nim was a nimblewright at the House of Inspired Hands in Waterdeep in the early 1490s DR.

Activities

Nim was an avid and capable inventor. Some of its inventions included a partially functional mechanical sparrow, as well as a nimblewright detector. It was also capable of constructing other nimblewrights.

History

Nim was built by a Lantanese wizard who, during a visit to Waterdeep, decided to donate him to the local church of Gond. Out of loneliness and boredom, Nim started creating his own inventions.

In the , Nim created a nimblewright that went rogue and escaped from the temple. When informed about those events, the cleric of Gond Valetta decided to strip Nim of all his construction materials.
