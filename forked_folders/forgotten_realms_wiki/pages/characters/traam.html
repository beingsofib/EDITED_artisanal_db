T'Raam, known by the Dukar as Peaceslayer, was the founder of the sahuagin kingdom of Aleaxtis.

History

In the year , T'Raam founded the kingdom of Aleaxtis. His reign would go on for 297 years, lasting until . Over the course of his reign T'raam would come to slay over a hundred Dukars, leading them to dub him Peaceslayer.
