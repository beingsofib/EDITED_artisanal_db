Perter was a renowned fletcher-merchant from the City of Coin, Athkatla, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Galoomp was a portly man with light skin. He spoke politely in a proper manner and talked about himself in third person.

History

Perter shared a workshop with a fellow merchant and a smith named Arnolinus. Their store could be found in the grand market in Athkatla's Center District – Waukeen's Promenade in the . Some of the enchanted missiles Perter was creating at the time included arrows of biting, arrows of dispelling, arrows of fire, arrows of ice, and bolts of biting. He also carried a stock of mundane missile weapons, bows, and several potions of infravision and potions of strength.
