Vorshk the Worm was a goblin spellcaster who dwelled within the ruins of Citadel Rashemar during the late 14 century DR.

Description

Vorshk was notable among the spellcasters that fought for the Citadel of the Warband in that he was considered the most powerful spellcaster among the group.

Relationships

Vorshk served the annis durthan Chaul, ruler of the ruined citadel.
