Naeryndroth was an adult black dragon and one of the "overdragons" of Melabrauth in Laerakond as of 1479 DR.

Personality

Naeryndroth was cunning and able to deceive many foes with his acting skills.

Equipment

Naeryndroth had a cache of magic items that granted him many illusion and enchantment spells.
