High Priestess Hekta Szind was a powerful priestess of Lolth, who oversaw the Temple of Lolth in Sschindylryn. A prominent member of House Szind, she organized the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar and the hunt for the Thread of the Weave in the 1480s DR, all in the effort of making Lolth the new goddess of magic.

Personality

Like most servants of Lolth, Hekta was willing to do anything for her own power and the glorification of Lolth, but didn't quite understand that she was disposable in the grand scheme of things.

Abilities

Hekta called upon divine spells, including cometfall, divine punishment, enervation, fear, fire storm, hold monster, negative energy burst, and symbol of pain

Possessions

Hekta's blade of the High Priestess was a deadly implement she used in sacrificial rituals.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, High Priestess Hekta received a mission from her mistress Lolth—to find the Thread of the Weave and give her goddess dominion over all magic (part of the greater scheme regarding the Demon Weave). She had the Matron Mothers of Sschindylryn form an alliance together for the purpose of waging war upon the kingdom of Cormyr, all to find and capture the Thread of the Weave. With the Houses of Szind, Avithoul, and Dun'Robar taking the lead, their combined armies fell upon the village of Eveningstar, which only barely survived the first attack.

The Cormyrean counterattack on Sschindylryn targeted the weak points of the three houses, and used misdirection and sabotage to set them against each other. Once the bulk of Sschindylryn's forces were distracted, Elminster led a small team including Ana Brabener into the Sschindylryn Ziggurat with the goal of reaching the Temple of Lolth, where Hekta was performing divinations to find the Thread of the Weave. When the team reached the top of the Ziggurat, the High Priestess finished her scrying and discovered that Ana Brabener was in fact the Thread she had been searching for, and so magically compelled her to walk into a portal to the Demonweb Pits and into the arms of Lolth.

Though she had successfully completed her mission, Hekta was still forced to contend with the forces before her. She called on the guards, priestesses, and wizards of the temple, animated the temple's statues of spiders and driders, and finally summoned dretches and draegloths, but she ultimately fell in battle while crying out for Lolth's aid. In response a goristro stepped from the portal and denounced her weakness, finishing her off.
