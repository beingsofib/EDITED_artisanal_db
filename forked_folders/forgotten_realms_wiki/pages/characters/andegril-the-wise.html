Andegril the Wise was an orc shaman and leader of the armies of Hlundadim.

History

In 111 DR Andegril led an army of orcs and goblinoids to invade Anauria. His campaign was a success and Anauria was destroyed, but the defenders put up such a fight that the orc host was annihilated as well, reducing the orcish population of the Goblin Marches for centuries.
