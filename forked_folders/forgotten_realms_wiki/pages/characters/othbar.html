Othbar was a cleric of Tyche in the early 3 century DR, and an adventurer of the Brave Blades.

Abilities

One of his spells allowed him to somehow thwart undead beings temporarily, at the cost of his life.

Relationships

He was a member of the Brave Blades adventuring band.

Possessions

He carried at least one belt knife.

History

During the Brave Blades' delve of Ondil's Tower in Marpenoth of , Othbar was one of their members. At the door to Ondil's tomb, after realizing that the lich Ondil was still able to cast spells and was slaying them with those from a distance every time they tried to open the door, he volunteered to cast a spell that held that foe at bay. He cast a spell that allowed them to open the door by incapacitating the lich temporarily, but it killed him.
