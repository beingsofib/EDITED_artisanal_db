Auglaer was an inhabitant of Wright's Ferry in Featherdale in the .

Activities

Auglaer worked at Albottle Ferry with his cousins Flender and Dregon Albottle. Given his immense size, he served as muscle when needed if customers complained about the ferry rates. Auglaer was so large that many believed he had ogre blood.
