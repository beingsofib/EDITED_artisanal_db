Thera Goldencorn was a Chauntean high priestess in the city of Saelmur, in the Land of the Lions region of south Faerûn, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Thera served as the high priestess of Saelmur's local Chauntean temple, the Lady's Sheaves. In addition, she served as a member of Saelmur's governing body, the Councilium of Prelates.
