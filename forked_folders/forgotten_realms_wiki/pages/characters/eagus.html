Eagus was a mercenary who was killed by Abdel Adrian in 1368 DR.

Appearance

Eagus had a messy greying brown beard and yellow teeth.

Equipment

Eagus wielded a halberd and wore rusty armour.

Background

Eagus lost a bet to Abdel in Julkoun, eight months prior to Eagus's death, and as a result, he bore a scar on his face.

Eagus was in combat alongside Kamon and others, against Abdel and Gorion, when he was stabbed by Abdel's broadsword. Eagus disarmed the sword from Abdel, but lost a lot of blood from his stab wound, and succumbed to death when Abdel cut his throat with a silver dagger.
