Sandyse Thunderquill was a confident but socially awkward mage and a member of the Harpers in Neverwinter sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

Activities

Sandyse was in training to infiltrate the Arcane Brotherhood. Her duty was to protect one of teleportation circles of Krowen Valharrow's network.
