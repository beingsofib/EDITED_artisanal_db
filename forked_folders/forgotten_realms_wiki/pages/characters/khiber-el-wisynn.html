Khiber yn Makal el Wisynn was a human fighter who served as a guard and mercenary in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR. Formerly, he was a gladiator slave in Manshaka, and was later turned into a vampire.

History

Once a slave and a gladiator in Manshaka,

He went on to be a mercenary in service to Pasha Amahd el Sufontis for over a decade by 1370 DR, slowly but steadily earning his trust and higher positions.

However, circa 1368 DR or earlier, Khiber was transformed into a vampire.

Personality

Khiber kept his emotions tightly reined, but this masked a icy rage.

Relationships

While Khiber was well-respected by Amahd, the pasha's petty elder sons and young wife despised and distrusted him, even without knowing he'd become a vampire, and the feelings were mutual.

As of 1370 DR, Khiber had recently befriended Pasha Alim el Tehibi.

Activities

As well as working as a mercenary for House Sufontis, Khiber was the night-watch commander of the minaret guards at the Great Bazaar in Emerald Ward.

Khiber helped Alim in a plan to buy out his partners in House Sufontis.

Home

Khiber lived in a fine house called Capridah in Gagi Drudach, Axash Sabban, in the city's Emerald Ward. It was a three-story building with a crypt in the cellar.
