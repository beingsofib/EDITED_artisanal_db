Murtlen was an unhappy commoner from the City of Coin, Athkatla, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Murtlen was a fair-skinned human male with red hair.

History

In the , Murtlen could be found shopping in Athkatla's Center District, in Waukeen's Promenade. He frequented shops and stalls opened on the elevated wooden walkways, including stalls run by Maheer yn Kadar el Saddith and Quataris. Murtlen ranted at passersby folk about them surely having been cheated by these merchants and accused their goods of being shoddy and of poor quality.
