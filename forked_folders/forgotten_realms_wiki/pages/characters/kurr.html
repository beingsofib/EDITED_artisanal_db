Kurr was a gnoll fang of Yeenoghu in the Labyrinth in the late 15 century DR.

Personality

Kurr suffered from a peculiar form of demon lord–spread madness: he felt guilty for all the people he had killed. He hoped to find someone who could kill him and his pack.

History

Circa 1486 DR, Kurr led his pack of hyenas into the Labyrinth searching for Yeenoghu. He had them feast on the corpses of his slain victims, transforming them into gnolls. They wandered the area and Kurr succumbed to madness.
