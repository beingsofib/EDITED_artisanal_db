Rayic Gethras was a conjurer and member of the Cowled Wizards in Amn who served during the mid–14 century DR.

Possessions

Rayic's three-story home was located near the docks of Athkatla. He had several protectors within, including a pair of stone golems.

History

In the . Rayic was tasked with looking into the activities of a former Red Wizard by the name of Edwin Odesseiron, had been working with the Shadow Thieves led by guildmaster Mae'Var. The Red Wizard in turn sought out agents to go and kill Rayic.
