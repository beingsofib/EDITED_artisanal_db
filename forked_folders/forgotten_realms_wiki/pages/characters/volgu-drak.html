Volgu Drak was a Fist of Bane who served the Cult of the Absolute in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Volgu was a human man with tan complexion and and curly silver hair. He wore a chainmail adorned with Bane's holy symbols. He was armed with a large wooden shield and a mace.

History

In the , the Cult of Bane joined forces with the Cult of the Absolute, led by the Chosen of Bane, Lord Enver Gortash. Fist of Bane Volgu Drak was a part of the Cult's operations that targeted the countless refugees that flooded Baldur's Gate. His branch of the Cult assembled donations and toys rigged with blackpowder explosives in the Felogyr's Fireworks shop's third floor. Presumably, Volgu perished during or shortly before the Cult attacked Baldur's Gate.
