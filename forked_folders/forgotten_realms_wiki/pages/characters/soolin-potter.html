Soolin Potter was half-elven psionicist and adventurer in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Due to her own experiences with bigotry from being a half-elf in Kara-Tur, she developed mixed feelings about humans, but also compassion towards all victims of bigotry.

Abilities

Her psionic focus was towards the psychoportive and metapsionic disciplines.

Possessions

She owned a stone of good luck.

History

Soolin was born of a traveling elf and a human wu jen. She was ostracized for having mixed blood and forced to leave home at an early age.
