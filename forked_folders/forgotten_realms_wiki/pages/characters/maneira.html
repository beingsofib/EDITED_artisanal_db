Maneira was a female halfling holy slayer who lived on Nada al-Hazan in the Crowded Sea.

Activities

Maneira was a member of the Gilded Palm. She worked for the Ben Ayyub merchant house.

Relationships

Maneira and Kilij formed a matched team of assassins.
