Merem was a gnome from Ieirithymbul in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

He drove the caravan between Leilon and Ieirithymbul in the Sword Mountains in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Relationships

His fellow caravan-driving gnomes were Furnis, Sudo, and Drom.

History

In Uktar of the , a gnomish caravan from Ieirithymbul went to Leilon to sell ores. They discussed their trip, including one of them bringing up that he thought he'd seen a gold dragon during the trip, which he thought to be Palarandusk, the Unseen Protector. Agents of the Arcane Brotherhood led by Daikon slew the gnomes of the caravan during their return, interrogated them for details of their lives, and took their likenesses. Merem was one of these gnomes.

Rumors & Legends

He was replaced by a shapeshifted agent of the Arcane Brotherhood, who returned to Ieirithymbul as part of a plot to seize Palarandusk.
