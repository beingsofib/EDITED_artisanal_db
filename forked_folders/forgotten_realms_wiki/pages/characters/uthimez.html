Uthimez was a drow slavekeeper and taskmaster from Sschindylryn during the 1480s DR.

Description

Uthimez had his white hair long and loose, and wore a breastplate. He carried a staff to hit people with, like slaves and surfacers.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Uthimez got stuck outside the city when his carriage broke a wheel. He had his slaves fix it while he and his fellow drow travelers waited impatiently.
