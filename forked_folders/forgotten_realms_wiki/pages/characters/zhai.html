Zhai was a half-drow, half–wood elf rogue of the late 14 century DR.

Personality

Zhai seemed to be a lone wolf who dismissive toward others and more comfortable with working by herself, something which stemmed from her mixed heritage which made her difficult to fit in among wood elves of Cedarleaf. Even so, she would go out of her way to help her home community without hesitation, even allowing Rannek and Illius to help her defending it from Cireka and Ygorl's raid.

Abilities

She was able to use twin daggers and had skill at hiding herself.

History

Zhai was raised in the wood elf village of Cedarleaf by her drow mother and wood elf father. Feeling insecure in the home of the wood elves, Zhai left them and became a rogue and later gained a reputation in Damara around 1368 DR, with Ellery Dragonsbane hearing tales of her and mentioning them to Jarlaxle Baenre.

In the early 1370s DR, she was captured by orcs attacking a town but was freed by Rannek during the battle. Soon thereafter, Zhai acquired a ring of jumping. Alongside the sorcerer Illius, the trio accidentally released the Cireka and Ygorl from their imprisonment, who went on to cause the deaths of Zhai's parents in a raid against her hometown.

The trio then went on a far-reaching adventure and ultimately defeated Cireka and sealed Ygorl once more. In recognition for their valor in defeating Cireka and Ygorl, Zhai and her companions were granted rulership over Vaasa on behalf of King Gareth Dragonsbane.
