Dauravyn Redbeard was an innkeeper who lived on the Sword Coast in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Dauravyn was an adventurer until his retirement. He spent his later years managing The Way Inn, which was located near Dragonspear Castle.

History

In the , his inn was menaced by the Hordes of Dragonspear, so he joined the Daggerford army. It was later rebuilt thanks to the sponsorship of several merchant families based in Waterdeep.
