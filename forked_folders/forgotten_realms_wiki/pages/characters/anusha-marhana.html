Anusha Marhana was the spoiled daughter of the Marhana merchant family.

History

In 1396 DR, she discovered that she had an uncanny ability to create an astral projection of herself when sleeping (it likely being a form of spellscar). Using her abilities, she stowed away on Captain Thoster's Green Siren. Later she was lost within the dreams of the Eldest in her dream form, where she met Yeva.

Family

Her mother and father were both killed in Sembia. She was the younger half-sister of Behroun Marhana.
