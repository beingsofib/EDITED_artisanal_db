Galfrey Kaarne was a human cleric of Mystra who traveled the North.

Description

Entirely bald save for the sides of his head, Galfrey sported a substantial mustache. He had light skin and wore orange clothing.

Personality

Galfrey was well known as a gentle man who possessed great wit, great faith in Mystra, and had a winning smile.

Possessions

On his person, Galfrey carried a powerful ring of protection, and wore a cloak of reflection, as well as a pair of winged boots.

Activities

Galfrey was a friend and occasional advisor of Alustriel Silverhand, the ruler of Silverymoon. He was interested in clairvoyance, and attempted to push his prophetic abilities.
