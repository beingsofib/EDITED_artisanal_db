Zannastar was a mage in service of the Cult of the Dragon in the mid-14 century DR.

History

In , Salvarad suggested him to Naergoth Bladelord as Malark Himbruel's replacement as a Wearer of Purple. Though they ultimately settled on Thiszult instead, Salvarad viewed Zannastar as competent without being overly headstrong.
