Mandal

Person

Mandal was a taverner in Ylraphon. He was also a wererat who captured Karasendrieth.

Description

He was a gaunt man with spiky, grizzled hair, and shifty eyes.

Activities

Mandal was a taverner.

History

On , on Hammer 16, Mandal captured the song dragon Karasendrieth in her human form. She was later rescued by Dorn Graybrook's party: they slew all of the wererats, including Mandal.
