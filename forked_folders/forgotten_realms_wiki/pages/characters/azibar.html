Azibar, who was nicknamed Azibar of the Seven Skulls, was a vile wizard apprentice who inhabited the city of Waterdeep during the 1350's DR.

History

Azibar was apprenticed to a particularly wicked mage named Melinter. He would meet his master, along with other apprentices and members of their gang, in a small courtyard in Waterdeep's Dock Ward. The courtyard would later become known as Melinter's Court because of these meetings.
