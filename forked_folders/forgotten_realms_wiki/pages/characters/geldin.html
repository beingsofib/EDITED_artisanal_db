Geldin was a half-orc muscle of the adventuring company - the Lady's Lament from Waterdeep, active circa 1310 DR.

Description

Unlike his mate, Geldin respected law and order. He usually wore a set of leather armors, and was armed with a short sword and a club.

History

Several years before the , Geldin, along with his mate Khatja, joined the Lady's Lament and quickly gained respect for their strength. The couple rarely talked about their past, but one thing was known about them - they have been ostracised by both humans and orcs alike.

In 1310 DR, the Lady's Lament, along with many other mercenaries, traveled from Luskan to the besieged city of Targos in the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale.
