Fortress Street

Fortress Street was a large road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 century DR.

Location

Fortress Street was found within north Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. It stretched across the entire expanse of the northern part of the city, running roughly parallel to the exterior city wall. Fortress Street originated at a major intersection in front of the Trail Gate in the west and terminated in front of the Dragon Gate in the east. Along the way it was intersected by many other streets including Wyvern Road, Nool Street, Cobble Road, Warrior Street, Goblin Way, Wizards' Way, Electrum Avenue, Ghost Street, Brass Street, and the Street of Dragons.

Notable Locations

A Zhentilar armory was located on Fortress Street near the Dragon Gate.
