Hurc was a confident kobold who lived in the Silver Marches in the late-14 century DR.

History

In the , Hurc participated in an attack on the village of Hilltop with a small band of his fellow kobolds. During the attack he got separated from the main group and barricaded himself in the kitchen of the Bubbling Cauldron tavern, taking the chef Mara as hostage.
