Jinjivar the Sorcerer was the official leader of the City Council of Zazesspur in 1370 DR.

Description

Jinjivar was a powerful Calishite wizard, who grew rich selling magical goods from Calimshan.

Personality

Jinjivar was a stern man who paid people to brag about his magical talents.

Activities

Within the city of Zazesspur, Jinjivar sold items such as magical hourglasses that flipped themselves back over and brooms that could sweep floors on their own.

As the longest member of the City Council in 1370 DR, Jinjivar was officially its leader; however, he often let Duchess Lucia Thione-Hhune override his decisions, since she was nobility. In addition to his role as Council lord, he also represented the district where he lived, the Carpets District.

For unknown reasons, Jinjivar was often absent from the council and the city.

Relationships

Jinjivar claimed to be the son of a chieftain of nomads living in Calim Desert and a daughter of the pasha by a jhasina. In his adulthood in Tethyr, he continued to maintain many contacts within Calimshan.

History

Jinjivar grew up in the Calim Desert as a nomad.

During the Tethyrian Interregnum, he had fanatic followers who wore colors of blue and purple. These goons often clashed with the thugs of Anakul the Just, another member of the Council at that time when anarchy in the city was rampant.

In the , Jinjivar returned from wherever he had been for the previous year Marquis Enzo had been a Council member along with Jinjivar, but after the Council voted to proclaim Zaranda Star as queen, the marquis secretly sided with Yusuf Jhannivvar during the Second Siege of Myratma. After Zaranda was victorious in the siege, the marquis tried to flee, but Jinjivar captured him and held him until Zaranda and her forces returned to the city. The Marquis Enzo was later beheaded for his crimes against the crown.
