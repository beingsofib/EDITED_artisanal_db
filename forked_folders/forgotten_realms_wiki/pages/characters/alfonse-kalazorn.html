Alfonse Kalazorn, nicknamed "Big Al" for his tall build, was the owner of the Butterskull Ranch in the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

Alfonse Kalazorn owned a suit of mithral chain mail.

History

Alfonse Kalazorn was the sheriff of the town of Triboar until he retired around 1481 DR. As he was bored of retirement, he built up a farm near Conyberry. Big Al's main source of income was the sale of the butter skulls that gave the Butterskull Ranch its name. The butter for these skull-shaped lumps of butter was made from the milk of the cow Petunia.

Circa 1491 DR, the ranch was attacked by a small band of orcs who had been driven from Icespire Hold by the white dragon Cryovain. The orcs freed the pigs and set the barn and the smithy on fire. A few other animals, including the cow Petunia, also escaped. Alfonse and five of his ranch hands tried to defend the ranch but Alfonse was captured and the hands were killed. Only one ranch hand survived and fled on horseback to the village of Phandalin to get help.
