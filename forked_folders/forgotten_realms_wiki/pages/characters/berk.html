Berk was a half-orc innkeep inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Berk was lazy and had a persistant odor that clung to him.

Activities

Berk ran the Pride of Zhentil Keep located on the west side of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter on the south bank of the River Tesh.
