Orm Talath was the head priest of the Home of the Stalwarts and leader of the Stalwart Knights for a time leading up to the mid–to–late 14 century DR.

Personality

He was a celebrated leader and beloved figure among the residents of his home city of Velen.

Relationships

After Orm stepped down as leader of the Stalwart Knights, he chose Orrdun Fistright as his successor.
