Ren Haldine was the patriarch of the Haldine family in Stag Falls during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Possessions

He owned the Haldine farm.

Relationships

His grand-daughter was Fatrina Haldine.

History

Ren Haldine was the owner of the Haldine farm during the mid-to-late 14 century DR, at the same time that Baron Vilhelm von Stag Falls was turned undead by the vampire Romanisk Vlaensk. Once undead, Vilhelm's reign became cruel, erratic, and terrible. When he sent his guards to bring him Ren Haldine, patriarch of the Haldine, and Ren's grand-daughter Fatrina Haldine, whom he fancied, the entire Haldine farm fled into nearby caves.
