Lady Stellephosa was a paladin of Kelemvor in Undermountain in 1479 DR.

History

In 1479 DR, she went into Undermountain after hearing rumors of undead activities there. However, Stellephosa was instead captured by the goblin and kobold followers of the mad half-elf mercenary Zarr. Zarr forced her to fight as a gladiator against the bugbear Smurgle.
