Tezza was a maid at the The Rising Moon in Highmoon in Deepingdale in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Tezza was old but sharp-eyed.

Activities

During spring cleaning each year in the 1350s DR, Tezza would clean the glass and mica windows, fearing they were too valuable for Shandril Shessair's hands.
