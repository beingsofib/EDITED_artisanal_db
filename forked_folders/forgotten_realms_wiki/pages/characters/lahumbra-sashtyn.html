Lahumbra Sashtyn was a crazed warrior and adventurer inhabiting Yhaunn in 1358 DR.

Activities

Lahumbra loved to fight. If she couldn't get her fighting fix on dangerous and reckless adventures, then she went down to the docks to to thrash fellow adventurers or tumbled with the city watch just for the fun of it. Though she had countless injuries from her exploits, all of them had been healed by mysterious magical means.

History

In Eleasis of the , she was arm-wrestling at the Cutter's Dip tavern, and helped Stag and Drik torment the Selûnite werewolf priestess Feena Archwood until she was rescued by the Sharran cultist Keph Thingoleir.
