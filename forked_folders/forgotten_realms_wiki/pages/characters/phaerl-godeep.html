Phaerl Godeep, known as the Rebel Mage, was a male drow mage of House Godeep in Menzoberranzan. He gained notoriety by revolting against the decrees of the priestesses who ruled the city.

He was ultimately captured and polymorphed into the helpless and humiliating form of a wingless wonder. He was presumed to remain trapped like this by the mid–14 century DR.
