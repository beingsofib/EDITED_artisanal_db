Anali Cyl was a noble woman and a famous General of the Luskan-Neverwinter war of 1372 DR.

History

In the , the city of Luskan launched a military campaign against its bitter southern rival – the city of Neverwinter. Anali Cyl, the daughter of a wealthy Cyl family, was betrothed to a merchant family scion of Waterdeep. With the war's advent, Anali was to be shipped away to the City of Splendors. The young woman was outraged over her family's actions. She was rather die defending her home than be wed against her will. Anali's parents sneered at their daughter's words until she grabbed the family heirloom falchion, called Decision off the wall, and stormed away.

Anali Cyl joined the defending forces of Neverwinter and, over the course of the war, became a renowned General. She wielded Decision in every battle since leaving her family estate behind.
