Jaqolya was a human cat burglar pilfering in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

She always dreamt of one day running a thieves' guild, and loved nothing more than crashing parties thrown by wealthy people and purloining their goods.

Possessions

She wielded an enchanted longsword and a rope of climbing while wearing a ring of protection and a necklace of disguise, that worked like a hat of disguise except it altered her clothes to suit the occasion.
