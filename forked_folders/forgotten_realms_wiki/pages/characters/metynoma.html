Metynoma was a female succubus that was a fiend patron for warlocks in the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the , on Flamerule 15, near Bloodstone Pass, the warlock Titus offered to the young tiefling Bysera to make a warlock pact and summoned Metynoma as her patron. Despite some reticence from Bysera, the pact was forged.

Relationships

Metynoma was a follower of the demon lord Graz'zt. She was an old acquaintance of Titus.
