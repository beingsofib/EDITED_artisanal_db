Brossan was a member of the Cult of the Dragon living as a hermit in the mid-14 century DR.

Relationships

He was known to Malark Himbruel.

History

Per Malark, he lived in a hill, as a hermit, near Thunderstone, as of . The mage Malark gave an enchanted ring to Culthar in . The ring was triggered by the name of the first dracolich Culthar had served, and would teleport him and one other person he was in contact with to Brossan's hill, though Malark was not certain Brossan would be there right then.
