Aerith was a drow tavern patron of the Low Lantern.

Relationships

Aerith had a twin called Beldan. They were described to be both awkward and inseparable.

History

Some time prior to 1492 DR, Aerith and their twin sibling escaped from the perils of the Underdark to explore the surface of Faerûn.
