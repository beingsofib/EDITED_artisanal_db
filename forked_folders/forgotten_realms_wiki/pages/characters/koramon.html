Koramon was the precept of the city of Sultim in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

As precept in Mulhorand's fastest-growing port city, Koramon was responsible for the safety and prosperity of the settlement. Koramon was a skilled warrior and a follower of Horus-Re.
