High Moonmaster Bero Falabara was a Selûnite priest and the leader of the Crescent Shrine in Alaghôn during the 8 century DR.

History

In the , during his time overseeing the Crescent Shrine, Bero was informed of the charges laid against the New Moon Pact by High Moonmistress Brima Chintamn of Bright Lady's Tower in Chancelgaunt. Believing that the Pact had indeed fallen to the heresy of believing Shar and Selûne the same being, he supported the charges and condoned the extermination of the Pact.
