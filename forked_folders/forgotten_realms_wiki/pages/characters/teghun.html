Teghun was a bugbear who allied with the cult of the Absolute during the late 15 century DR.

Possessions

He wielded a morningstar in battle.

History

In the , the forces of the Absolute launched a vicious on the nearby Waukeen's Rest situated on the Risen Road. After the assault, the goblinoid cultists retired to the Shattered Sanctum to celebrate their victory with a grand feast. Teghun drank copious amounts of ale and passed out along the upport courtyard of the sanctum.
