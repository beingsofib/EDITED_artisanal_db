Chenraya Xorlarrin was a drow priestess of Lolth and a member of House Xorlarrin in Menzoberranzan in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , the goddess Lolth ordered her minions to collect as many blue fire items and relics of Mystra as possible so that Lolth could become the new goddess of magic. Chenraya conspired with the oni mage Pashra to steal Akanûl's entire supply of arambarium ore. Arambarium was an extremely powerful metal that magnified the effects of magic. Her plans were thwarted by the deva Demascus.
