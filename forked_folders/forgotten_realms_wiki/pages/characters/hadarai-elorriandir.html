Hadarai El’or’riandi was a male eladrin minor lord residing on the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles as of the late 15 century DR.

Personality

He was generous and gracious to his friends and to the friends of his friends.

While he appreciated other cultures, he was fully convinced of the superiority of his high elven culture over all others.

Activities

He was a dealer of elven magic items, notably magic weapons, magic armors (such as eladrin armor), and accessories (such as elven cloaks).

Lord Hadarai was one of a small number of Sarifal eladrin who opposed the isolationist policies of High Lady Ordalf, and in defiance of them, he had traveled among the other Moonshae Isles, befriended and sought humans, and even invited some of these friends for clandestine meetings on Gwynneth's shores.

Relationships

One of his trusted vassals was an eladrin spellcaster named Randall, whom he would send on missions to far off lands.

Haradai had forged a strong friendship with the human Daffyd Mac Lyrr during his travels.

He was generally mistrusting of drow and tieflings.

History

Hadarai's travels took him to the island of Alaron and to the town of Warlsby in particular on multiple occasions, where he became acquainted with the ruling Mac Lyrr family.

By the late 15 century DR, Hadarai sought to establish trade with the Isles beyond Gwynneth, seeking to sell elven jewelry and magic items in exchange for foreign books, lore, magic, and other goods. However, trade with foreign realms was forbidden by the laws of Sarifal. Hadarai did not wish to break these laws, but he believed that as long as he only did business with foreign elves, his business ventures would technically be legitimate.

In the , he arranged for a clandestine meeting with Daffyd on Gwynneth to discuss establishing trade between their realms.

Sometime later, he sent his servant Randall to aid the Feywild town of Baeladar Yaaira in the Cloakwood, which was threatened by fomorians. He instructed Randall to offer rewards for information about portals to and from the Feywild.
