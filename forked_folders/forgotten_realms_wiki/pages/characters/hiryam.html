Hiryam was a shopkeeper inhabiting the city of Waterdeep in the mid-14 century DR.

Activities

Hiryam owned a cloth goods shop simply named after himself called Hiryam's Cloth Goods that was located along Burnt Wagon Way, in the Trades Ward in the City of Splendors.
