Asparagus Mhallie was a dairy goat farmer, displaced by The Absolute, and as a refugee, sought shelter in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Asparagus was an aging human woman with wispy gray hair, dressed in a simple dress with a corset.

History

In the , an army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldur's Gate, displacing countless refugees in the process along the way. Asparagus Mhallie fled to the nearby city of Baldur's Gate with her heard of dairy goats – her girls, her family. Sadly, her girls were taken – confiscated by the Flaming Fist mercenaries upon entry to be slaughtered due to ration shortage in the city. Despite being robbed of her livelihood and beloved beasts, Asparagus was granted entrance to the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, where she was protesting the confiscations along with other farmers near the Basilisk Gate.
