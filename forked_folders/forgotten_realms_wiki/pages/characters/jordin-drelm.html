Jordin Drelm was a ranger in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Jordin had light skin and long brown hair. He wore leather armor with green decoration and carried a bow.

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Jordin arrived in the village and helped train other rangers.
