Kisonraathiisar was a topaz dragon and the first king of Westgate. He founded Westgate in an effort to build a kingdom that would survive the effects of the King-Killer Star.

History

He was slowly destroyed by Saldrinar "of the Seven Spells", a Netherese wizard, in . Saldrinar then became the first human king of Westgate. Some believed that Kisonraathiisar was trapped within an enchanted scepter so that Saldrinar could conjure Kisonraathiisar's spells at will.

Kisonraathiisar's corpse was kept in a secret subbasement below Castle Thalavar in Westgate.

Early in his reign, Orlak took a large chunk of bone from the skeleton of Kisonraathiisar and from it carved a special chalice. With unholy incantations of necromancy, Orlak enchanted this as the Argraal of Orlak.

Long after his death, a large stature (not to scale), was erected in Westgate to commemorate the creation of the city and its first draconic king. The statue depicted Kisonraathiisar as a fierce red dragon and was placed in the Harbor Loop with a plaque that named the creature, his death date, and his slayer – Saldrinar. As of the late 14 century DR, the stature to Kisonraathiisar stood unvandalized.
