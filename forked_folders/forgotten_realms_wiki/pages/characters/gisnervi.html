Gisnervi was a renowned brassworker from the City of Brass in the Elemental Plane of Fire, active before the late 14 century DR.

History

Sometime before the , Gisnervi created a powerful set of enchanted storm armor, intended to be a gift to the son of the City of Brass's sultan. The brassworker's enemies intercepted the armor as it was delivered and placed a curse on the gift. Gisnervi was disgraced as the curse took hold, and the brassworker was banished to the Prime Material plane. Eventually, Gisnervi befriended a blacksmith from the city of Neverwinter, Samir. He was able to break the curse placed upon the storm armor, restoring Gisnervi's honor.
