Jiang Dai was a ghostly kensai lieutenant who served Nan Kung Chi in the Crypt of Durkon in Kara-Tur in the 14 century DR.

Possessions

Dai wore a sturdy set of enchanted +2 leather armor and wielded magic +1 wakizashi and a +1 katana.

History

Jiang Dai died many moons before the late 14 century DR. In life, he was a human kensai warrior and one of six trusted lieutenants who served Nan Kung Chi. He continued to do the same long after death. In the , the crypt faced an illithid incursion led by Goxxa the alhoon. The mind flayers consumed ancient preserved minds of the crypt's dead and sought the Claw of the Black Leopard. At the same time, a vampire thief named Hexxat arrived at the tomb on the behest of her master Larloch, who sought the tomb's magical treasure. A ghostly monk named Ki Chin Sang stood guard of Nan Kung Chi's tomb and demanded anyone seeking entrance to defeat six of Nan Kung Chi's trusted undead lieutenants who all were interred together. When Hexxat and her allies challenged the lieutenants to battle, Jiang Dai was joined by Weimin Min, Yi Niu, Borok-Tosst, and Ming-hua Dai, and, presumably, was defeated.
