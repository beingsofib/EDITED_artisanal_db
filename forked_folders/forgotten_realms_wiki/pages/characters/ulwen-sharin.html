Ulwen Sharin was a prominent landowner inhabiting the town of Ashabenford in Mistledale during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Ulwen's profitable lands were located in the eastern reaches of Mistledale. She was a member of the Council of Six in Ashabenford.
