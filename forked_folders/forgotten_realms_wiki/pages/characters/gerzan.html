Gerzan was a legendary Master Smith of the ancient dwarven nation of Delzoun.

History

By the late 14 century, weapons forged by Gerzan were considered some of the best examples of dwarvish craftsmanship as well as valued for Gerzan's skills as a weapon enchanter. In the , an adventurer and explorer of the Undermountain, Tolem Grandelwood, owned one of the legendary master's swords. He cherished his thrice-enchanted blade and refused to trade it for any treasure from the great dungeon underneath Waterdeep.
