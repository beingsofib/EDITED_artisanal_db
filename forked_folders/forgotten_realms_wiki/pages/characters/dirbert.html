Dirbert was a young man who lived in Imnesvale during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Dirbert was rather naive and impetuous, and did not thoroughly think through his actions. Likes his peers, Dirbert wanted to become a daring adventurer.

Relationships

Dirbert was friends with Imnesvale's other youths, namely Neler and Valsben.

History

In the , Dirbert and his friends went around town looking for anyone that would buy them some bastard swords and a keg of De'Tranion's Baalor ale, so they could begin their adventuring career in earnest.
