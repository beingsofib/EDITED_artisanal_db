Marthin Avathnar was a seneschal in Cormyr.

Activities

Marthin ruled the remote and secret prison stronghold of Castle Irlingstar in the Thunder Peaks on the eastern border of Cormyr, where the king put traitor nobles, murderers, and agents of Sembia and Westgate.

History

Around 1480 DR, he was in his room when suddenly a mysterious individual killed him.

Personality

Marthin was a pompous but diligent individual.
