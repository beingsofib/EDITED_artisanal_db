Nulathoe was a necromancer,

Magic

Most famously, he created the necromantic spell known as Nulathoe's ninemen, He was also credited with inventing the spells Nulathoe's concussive demise, Nulathoe's death ward, and Nulathoe's undead army. Although he was a powerful necromancer, Nulathoe's spells were generally too crude to aid in the preservation of complex organs.

Nulathoe authored many necromantic tomes,

History

Nulathoe had two apprentices, one of whom was the famous wizard and spell inventor Nchaser. After Nulathoe's death, these two apprentices carried on his legacy by proliferating his most famous spell: Nulathoe's ninemen.

Rumors & Legends

Nulathoe was sometimes mentioned alongside infamous liches like Acererak and Vecna as a testament to the corruptive nature of necromancy.
