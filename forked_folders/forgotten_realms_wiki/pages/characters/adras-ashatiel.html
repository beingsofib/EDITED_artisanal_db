Adras Ashatiel was the heir of the Ashatiel noble family of Waterdeep circa the mid–14 century DR. She later served as the right hand to Caelar Argent within the Shining Crusade army.

History

She joined the armed forces of Waterdeep while her brother Arran Ashatiel studied as a businessman. However, Arran was killed in the under the orders of Sarevok Anchev. Totally devastated, Adras joined the crusade of the Shining Lady, in order to take vengeance against Sarevok one day.

After Sarevok was killed by his Bhaalspawn sibling, Adras turned her ire on the surviving godchild.
