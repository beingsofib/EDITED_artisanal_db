Lord Haelwing was a nobleman of Cormyr.

Relationships

Oskrul Meddanthyr was Lord Haelwing's bodyguard wizard.

History

Before the Council of the Dragon on the in Suzail, Lord Haelwing got blackout drunk during mornfeast at the Old King's Favorite, leaving his bodyguard to tattle much of arcane lore to curious fellow nobles.
