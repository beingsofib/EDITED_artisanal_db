Cara Ghastkill was the wife of Lord Evan Ghastkill of Nashkel around 1370 DR.

Relationships

Cara and Evan had their son Evan the Younger.

History

While Lord Evan was very ill and expected to die during 1370 DR, both Cara and her brother-in-law Berrun Ghastkill felt that Evan the Younger would be an incapable heir.
