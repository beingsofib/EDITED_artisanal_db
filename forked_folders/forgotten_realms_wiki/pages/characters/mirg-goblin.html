Sharp-eye Mirg was a goblin scout from the Sword Coast and a member of the camp aligned with the Absolute at the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Mirg enjoyed her drink just as much as any other goblin, if not more so.

Possessions

Mirg had an impeccable sense of fashion, wearing alluring makeup around her eyes and decorating her leather rags with elongated feathers, bones, and animal claws.

History

She was among the raiders that celebrated her camp's raid on Waukeen's Rest in the . She ditched her guard duties, and failed to replace her fellow cultist Nozk.
