Azunt Haelcloak was a herbalist of the city of Arabel in Cormyr in the mid–14 century DR and a noted supporter of the Lost King Gondegal in his uprising of the .

Activities

Azunt Haelcloak ran a business as a "Herbalist for Hire" and once lived at Mother Jessa's on Bottle Alley in Arabel.

History

During Gondegal's war, Azunt Haelcloak was declared an outlaw in a proclamation penned by the Sage Royal Alaphondar on Ches 11, 1352 DR, and was wanted dead or alive by the crown.
