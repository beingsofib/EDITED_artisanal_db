Michil Franzt was a tiefling proxy of the Faerûnian power known as Helm. He was among the few proxies that one could find on the plane of Mechanus.

Description

Michil had reddish skin due to his tiefling heritage and was incredibly tall.

Personality

This proxy was a very proud man, secure in his belief that there had rarely ever been a guardian of Helm as good as himself. He spoke only rarely, for he considered himself bound to his words.

History

The origins of Michil were a mystery to others. Some believed he came from the Prime Material plane, others that he was a planar, and some that he was a petitioner.

Rumors & Legends

Rumors surrounding Michil claimed that he was capable of perceiving ill-will from miles away.
