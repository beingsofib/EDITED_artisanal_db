Myrnath was a high elf that was abducted by a nautiloid in the 1490s DR.

Description

Myrnath had white-yellow skin and weighed . He wore blue trousers and brown leather boots.

History

Some time during the , Myrnath was taken aboard a nautiloid. Myrnath's brain was used to give birth to the intellect devourer known as Us.
