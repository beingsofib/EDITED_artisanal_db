Culthar was a male warrior who was one of the guards of the Twisted Tower in Shadowdale; secretly, he was also a member of the Cult of the Dragon.

History

In 1357, Culthar was involved in the hunt for Shandril Shessair, helping the Cult's archmage Malark Himbruel to enter the Twisted Tower.
