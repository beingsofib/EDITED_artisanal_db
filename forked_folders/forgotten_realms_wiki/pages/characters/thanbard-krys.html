Thanbard Krys was a hunter who operated out of Eveningstar in the 15 century DR.

Description

Thanbard was a ruddy human man with light brown hair and a pointed beard.

History

Following the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar and Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, Thanbard was out hunting in the Storm Horns with Olyce Tidewater when they were ambushed by orcs serving Archwizard Amskar. The pair escaped back to Eveningstar, though Thanbard's leg was injured in the process. They noticed the odd behaviour of the orcs, which coincided with the failed raid upon Eveningstar that the Purple Dragons defeated. They ended up on the Eveningstar bridge, bandaging up Thanbard's wound while the Harpers Kara Patwin and Lorant Tass organized a full investigation into the strange events.
