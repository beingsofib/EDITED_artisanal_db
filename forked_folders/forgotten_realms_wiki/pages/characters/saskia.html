Saskia was an Uthgardt sorceress and warrior of the Great Worm tribe turned mercenary in the Company of the Chimera in the late 14 century DR.

Description

The tall woman had snow-white skin, blue eyes with a crystalline gleam, and raven-black hair down to the middle of her back.

Personality

Saskia thought poorly of guard work, civilization, and people who weren't warriors. However, her scorn for them was weaker than her need for company.

Abilities

Besides being a competent brawler and sword-fighter, Saskia had sorcerous powers that she eventually embraced.

Possessions

The barbarian bore a bastard sword, and was known to wear heavy furs and a sealskin cloak.

Activities

She'd worked in the Company of the Chimera for perhaps a year.

Relationships

After being cast out from the Great Worm tribe, she became a mercenary for the Company of the Chimera, under the command of Tombli, whom she thought needed taking down a peg or two.

After fleeing the Company, she teamed up with the pseudodragon Aeristhax.

History

Saskia was ostracized from the Great Worm tribe for a number of suspicious coincidences that marked her as a witch, but which were signs of budding sorcery. She was offered an ultimatum: to either take the Trial of the Dragon, or leave. The trial called for her to meet with the tribe's spirit at the top of the Uthgardtheim called the Great Worm—actually a white dragon. Unfortunately, two days into her quest, an adventuring band had arrived ahead of her and slain the Great Worm, making it impossible for her to succeed. After eight days of fruitless waiting, she returned to her tribe with a fever; as soon as it broke, she learned of the Great Worm's fate and chose exile, leaving south.

There, she eventually joined with the Company of the Chimera in Sembia; this happened perhaps a year before Mirtul of the . On Mirtul 21 of that year, she clashed with Tombli, captain of the company, who was trying to order her to sit with the others and drink, or fight him. She chose to fight; though she briefly had the upper hand, he had his men pull her off of him. She sustained some wounds, which required treatment by Grummond, the Company's surgeon. Before they were done healing, she was called on by Tombli to identify a creature he was trying to buy. Saskia confirmed it was a pseudodragon, and it mind-spoke to her, asking to be freed. She refused in her heart, and got stung from inside the cage, which made her fall asleep for the rest of the day, having uneasy dreams about her past.

Upon awakening, she decided to free the pseudodragon. She ambushed Tombli, who was alone tormenting the caged creature in the stables at the time. He briefly overpowered her, but Saskia managed to employ some sorcery to free the dragon, which rescued her from the dwarf. She then fled the men from the Company that came after her, stealing a horse
