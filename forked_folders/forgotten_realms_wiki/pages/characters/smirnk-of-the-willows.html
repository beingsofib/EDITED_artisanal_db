Smirnk of the Willows

Person
nameSmirnk of the Willows
homeHokla Mountain, Tabot
occupationHermit

Smirnk of the Willows was a hermit and wiseman who lived on Hokla Mountain in Tabot.

Smirnk was one of only five wisemen recognized by the monks of Tabot around as being a true oracle, being capable of predicting the future and raising heroes from the dead for good causes.
