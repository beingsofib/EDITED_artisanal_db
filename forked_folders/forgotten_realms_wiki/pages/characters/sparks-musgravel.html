Sparks Musgravel was a member of The Guild from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Sparks was a middle-aged human man of light complexion. He had green eyes and wavy blonde hair. Like other grunts of the Guild, Sparks wore green-colored wizardly garb and carried a simple staff.

History

In the , Sparks Musgravel was tasked with standing guard of the secret entrance into Baldur's Gate's Undercellar where The Guild's Guildhall was hidden. Whilst a half-orcish enforcer Tusgront manned the main entrance, Sparks was ready to dispatch of any unwanted guest from a distance.
