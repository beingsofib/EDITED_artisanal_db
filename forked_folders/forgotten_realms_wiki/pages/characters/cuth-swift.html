Cuth Swift was a human man in the small town of Greenest, located in the Western Heartlands in west Faerûn.

History

Around 1489 DR, during a battle with the Cult of the Dragon, Cuth, his wife Linan Swift, and their children were pursued by some kobolds until they were saved by some adventurers.
