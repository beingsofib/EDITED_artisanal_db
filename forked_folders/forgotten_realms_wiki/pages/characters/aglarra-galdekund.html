Aglarra Galdekund is the wife of the merchant Flammos Galdekund of Waterdeep. In 1369 DR, Queen Filfaeril Obarskyr assumed her appearance in order to hide in Waterdeep during the abraxus affair. The change in mannerisms and voice was blamed on an illness, that the couple had to travel to Iritue's Firesprings in southern Amn in order to address. Aglarra is short and masculine in appearance, with a pot belly and a large, pimpled chin.
