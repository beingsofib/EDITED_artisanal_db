Rymel was a bard adventurer, member of the Company of the Bright Spear in the mid-14 century DR.

Description

He had a short beard.

Personality

He took setbacks in stride

Abilities

His skill at telling tales was great, and he commanded much attention at taverns for it.

Possessions

He was known to have owned a lute, a sword, and a dagger.

Relationships

He was a member of the Company of the Bright Spear.

History

At the Rising Moon in Mirtul 6

The following morning, after a discussion in which Rymel had said more than anyone but Thail, Burlane changed the course for the entire company, heading for the Semberflow along the way to Myth Drannor.

The following morning on Mirtul 12,

In his final combat at the Gates of Doom when they were ambushed on Mirtul 20, Delg confirmed his death later that year.
