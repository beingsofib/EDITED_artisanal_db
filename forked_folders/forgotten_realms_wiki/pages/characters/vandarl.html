Vandarl

Vandarl was a thief from Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

Vandarl was a thief for hire, who avoided stealing from noblemen.

Relationships

Vandarl was often mentioned together with Skult. They worked as a team, nicknamed 'Skull and Van'.

Rumors & Legends

During the Open Feast of the in Castle Obarskyr, Cormyr, the topic of Skult and Vandarl was brought up during a reception in which a diplomatic envoy from Milvarune participated. They were contrasted and compared with the Silent Shadow.
