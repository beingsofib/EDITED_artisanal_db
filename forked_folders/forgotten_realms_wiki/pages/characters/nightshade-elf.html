Nightshade was the elven leader of the Silent Rain mercenary company.

Description

Nightshade was reputed to be as beautiful as she was deadly, though her true identity remained a secret.

Relationships

She had a personal hatred of Jadron the Fang and the Teeth ogre mercenaries that he led.
