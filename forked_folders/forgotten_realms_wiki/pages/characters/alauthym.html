Alauthym

Person
nameAlauthym
homeMoonshae Isles
occupationSage

Alauthym was a sage who resided within the Moonshae Isles as of the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Alauthym was known for research into the life and ecology of ixitxachitl.

Relationships

Other sages with similar research interests regarded Alauthym as an esteemed colleague, such as Ramazith Flamesinger and Nenemith.
