Topsy was a deep gnome wererat living in the Underdark with her twin brother Turvy sometime in the late 1480s or early 1490s DR.

Personality

The gnome was very close to her twin brother Turvy. She was the more sociable of the duo. They feared that someone would discover that they were novice wererats who couldn't easily control their instincts.

History

Topsy and Turvy lived in Blingdenstone. Sometime in their past, they were infected with lycanthropy.

Later, during a trip to collect some mushrooms, she and her twin were captured by drow slavers of Velkynvelve.
