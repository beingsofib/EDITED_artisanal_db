Myschanta Halarra was a human wizard who specialized in the school of abjuration and lived in the fortified city of Arabel, within the realm of Cormyr, in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

Myschanta's appearance belied her magical profession. A short and stout woman, she was most often seen in a collection of vests, overtop rumpled shirts of hide, and old britches. Her boots, with their swashed tops, completed an outfit that made her seem more at home tending to horses than wielding arcane magic.

Personality

With a cheerful outlook on life, Myschanta was an energetic, spirited woman. This lively nature was often made apparent through a distinctive vocal habit; she was frequently heard snorting or chuckling with amusement.

Abilities

Beyond her skills as a mage, however, Myschanta also possessed a cunning, tactical mind. These two facets, when combined with her wealth of experience dealing with monsters, made her a tremendously valuable ally.

Possessions

Myschanta was the owner of a wand of armory, a magical wand capable of protecting the sorceress with a field of invisible force, Beyond even these, it seemed Myschanta always had useful spells or magic items at the ready, regardless of the situation.

Activities

Since some point in the 1330s DR, Myschanta began to offer a rare service: the rescue of adventurers, particularly those trapped in difficult situations. Steep, however, were her demands for such treatment; even simple tasks, like freeing them from imprisonment, delivering healing potions, or merely acting as a guide cost 6,000 gp—a price that doubled if she was forced to cast combat magic.

Relationships

Among her allies, Myschanta could count the esteemed High Lady Mage Alustriel of Silverymoon, who owed the Arabellan several favors. Their connection was made even stronger through Myschanta's use of contingency. If she were ever killed or greatly hurt, this particular spell ensured that her body was immediately taken to Alustriel's side, placing it under the Lady Hope's protection. Though it surprised the Silvaeren's court each time this occurred, only twice did her contingency activate in Myschanta's decades long career.

History

In late Flamerule of the , Arabel faced a series of strange storms. Myschanta, who found herself bothered by their oddity, began to track the location of the nearly formulaic lightning strikes. The Night Parade were the perpetrators of these storms, a fact she discovered when some of their members attacked her in her very home. Though the evil Zupar successfully turned one of her walls to water, gaining access, it also activated the wizardess's alarm.

This gave her time to use her wand of fire against these aberrant foes, burning some of the stone wall and Brokus, one of her assailants. Though the latter managed to kill her, she had, luckily, planned even further ahead. Through her contingency, Myschanta's body was swiftly teleported from Arabel, arriving in far-away Silverymoon where she could be tended to by Alustriel. Only a few weeks later, Myschanta was successfully resurrected.
