Felnis was a paladin of the Silver Flame in the world of Eberron.

Description

Felnis was a human man with ruddy skin and short brown hair. He wore a suit of scale mail and carried a round shield, shortsword, and quiver of arrows.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, Felnis was one of the leading Silver Flame investigators who pursued the Lords of Dust in Stormreach. Following the raid on the cult hideout, their ritual reached completion and blasted a hole into Khyber, allowing access to the road to Tarath Marad and the Spinner's Prison. Felnis knew exactly what the enemy goal was, and sent a team underground to find Brother Aurelius and secure the prison before the Spinner of Shadows was freed.
