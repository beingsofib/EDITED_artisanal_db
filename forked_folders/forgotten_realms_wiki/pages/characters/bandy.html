Bandy was a mare stabled in Parnast for a time during the mid–14 century DR.

Description

She was a particularly large animal, well-suited for a role as a pack horse.

History

In the , Bandy and several other horses accompanied the wizard Druhallen, the warrior Rozt'a, her foster son Tiep and the goblin Sheemzher on a trek across Greypeak Mountains.
