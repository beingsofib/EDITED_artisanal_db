Aerich the Steward was a zombie dwarf who existed in Myth Drannor up to the late 14 century DR.

History

The unfortunate post-dwarf remained inside Tyrintar's Hall, along with many other undead like Shenlazeen and Zagath the Feaster. It was encountered when Uhlsdrin Nirveth came looking for the Icon of the Dwarves in 1374 DR.
