Saamark II of the House Jaleh was a pasha of Calimport in Calimshan.

Personality

He was overly indulgent and deeply lazy. He spent his wealth lavishly with little care for what the goods he bought actually did for him, nor the effects of his lifestyle.

History

Naturally, Saamark fell into debt to the Rundeen. To cover it, he cut his guards' pay and was cut down by them in turn.

He was remembered in the name of Jaleh Sabban, also known as Saamark Sabban, in Maker's Ward of Calimport.
