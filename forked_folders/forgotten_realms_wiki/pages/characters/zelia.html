Zelia

Zelia was a powerful yuan-ti telepath psion who planted mind seeds in people all over Toril, who were taken over and became her agents with their own psionic powers, albeit lesser than hers. She used them in a plot of world domination. Her demise started when she became interested in the young psion Arvin, who had powers similar to hers. In the end, Arvin defeatd Zelia.
