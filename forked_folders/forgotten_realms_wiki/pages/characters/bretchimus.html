Bretchimus was a guard at Castle Felmorel in the mid–8 century DR.

Description

He was rather old for a guard.

Possessions

Bretchimus wielded a pike that was in sorry shape.

Activities

He sometimes watched the front gate alone.

Relationships

He served under Lord Esbre Felmorel. His brother was Habaertus Ilynker.

History

On a spring night of the , Wanlorn was present at a feast at Castle Felmorel. When he snuck out, the one guard that watched the gate was Bretchimus. He completely missed not only Wanlorn, but Thessamel Arunder, and luckily for him, even the Slayer.
