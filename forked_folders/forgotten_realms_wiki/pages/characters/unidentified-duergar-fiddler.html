An unidentified duergar fiddler of Zortagra was in Elventree during the late 1480s DR.

History

A duergar fiddler was afflicted by the demon lord's madness in the late 1480s DR. For unknown reasons, she went to the village of Elventree in Cormanthor and there started to play a madness-spreading melody during a celebration in honor of Mielikki. Thanks to some adventurers, she was stopped before too much damage was done.

Later, Elanil Elassidil hired the adventurers that had stopped her to investigate the matter in the Underdark.
