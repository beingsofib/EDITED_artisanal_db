Gethmedra Darmov () was a crime lord in Mulmaster in 1491 DR.

Personality

Her arena made Gethmedra popular among the city's elite, but she knew that, for her poor birth, she would never move among them. Gethmedra liked to display her power and cruelty, by showing to her guests the head of her last enemy.

Activities

Gethmedra was a crime lord in the city. Using the Black Blade and Bloody Boar tavern as cover, she ran a gladiator arena.

History

In 1491 DR, an adventuring party approached her in order to question one of her gladiators, Bhún Ludwakazar the Stone Dwarf, about troubles in a nearby mine.
