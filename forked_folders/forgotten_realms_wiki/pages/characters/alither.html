Alither was a bard, sage, and member of the Harpers from the city of Lyrabar in Impiltur in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Among his works, Alither studied the legends of Adhe Wood in the Vast and believed that the many mutant spiders there were produced by a diseased or magically altered deepspawn dwelling there, dubbed "the Father of Spiders" by 1370 DR.
