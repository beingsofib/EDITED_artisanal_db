Jurck was a lieutenant of the Cult of the Dragon circa 1369 DR during the occupation of the ruins of Myth Drannor, stationed in the Elven Catacombs underneath Castle Cormanthor.

History

In 1369 DR, a fellow commander of the Cult, Galibel communicated with Lieutenant Jurck via messenger-carried letters. In one such communication Galibel expressed concerns over something or someone big tearing through previous night's patrol of soulless drow slaves and taking off with the Cult's wizard's body. Galibel requested to switch duties with Jurck's team in the Elven Catacombs in an attempt to catch the mysterious perpetrator.
