Omskilar Rethwood was an outlaw as well as a member of the Company of the Cloak.

Activities

He loved to kill, often slaying specific individuals with a single crossbow bolt. Failing this, Omskilar would attach a scroll with a message to a bolt's shaft in order to intimidate targets.

Abilities

Along with all of the other members of the Company of the Cloak, Omskilar possessed an immunity to oil of taggit (which caused unconsciousness).
