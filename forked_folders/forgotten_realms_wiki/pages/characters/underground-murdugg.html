Underground Murdugg was a giant spider from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Murdugg, along other giant spiders, drow, and duergar, all were slaughtered in the home of Bloomridge Park groundskeeper, Lavernica. The murder spree was performed by Orin the Red and her Murder Tribunal of Bhaal. It left Murdugg's body scorched my magical flames.
