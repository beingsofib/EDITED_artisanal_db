Erluk was one of the Blades of the Old Man as of . He was a pale-haired man who favored the rapier. On the night of Tarsakh 25, when the intruder alarm was sprung at the Fortress of the Old Man, he and five others sought to see to the Old Man of the Mountain's safety. They came across Talieth and Sauk in the process; protesting that their place was by the Old Man. Sauk, Talieth and her guards killed them in response.
