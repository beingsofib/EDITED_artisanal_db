Kalin Abd al-Bas was the general and commander of the Valiant Mamluk Society in Qudra in 1367 DR.

Description

Kalin had a streak of white running through her hair; a trait that was identical to a hero of the Valiant.

Activities

Kalin was the youngest member of the Mamluk Council, earning her support from many of the younger mamluks of Qudra. She trained the Valiant extensively to perfect their lightning attack techniques.
