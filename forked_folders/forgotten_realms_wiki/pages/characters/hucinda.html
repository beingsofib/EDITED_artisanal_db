Hucinda was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Hucinda was a human woman with a tanned skin tone, dark brown eyes, and short black curly hair.

History

In the , Hucinda and several other citizens of Baldur's Gate were randomly attacked by a gang sahuagin who emerged from the Gray Harbor as Hucinda was shopping for fresh fish at the harbor-side stalls. Serendipitously, a band of adventurers known as the Absolute Adversaries were nearby as the attack commenced. Hucinda wondered if the fishers would chop up and sell the dead sahuagin after the attack.
