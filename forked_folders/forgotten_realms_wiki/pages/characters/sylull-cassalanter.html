Sylull Cassalanter, known as The Lady in White or even The Lady with a Cane, was a noblewoman of the Cassalanter family in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

When Laeral Silverhand disguised herself as Sylull Cassalanter, she employed the disguise of an old woman, with noticeable jowls, in a white robe. She was heavily powdered.

Personality

She was considered to be severe and judgmental, with rigid standards of respectability.

Possessions

Her cane glittered with a variety of gems.

Relationships

She was the wife of Ohrl Cassalanter, mother to Panricon Cassalanter, Erin Cassalanter, and Caladorn Cassalanter.

Rumors & Legends

Lady Cassalanter was known mostly by reputation by ; to the merchant Mrilla Malsander, she was known for her severe and judgmental nature. Laeral Silverhand impersonated the woman on that year to get close enough to Mrilla for casting spells that would drive a wedge between her and Auvrurn Labraster.
