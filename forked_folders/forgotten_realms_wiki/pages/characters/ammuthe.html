Ammuthe was the wife of Bethgarl Nreams, a cheese merchant of Athalantar, in the mid–3 century DR.

Description

Her hair was magnificent.

Activities

She was a homemaker.

History

At some point before the , she told her husband to stop eating certain snails.

In that year, she accompanied her husband to the path between their home and Hastarl, where he'd accidentally dropped a number of cheeses from his cheese cart at the sight of a bizarre, stationary sphere of blue flames. She berated him for the loss of those cheeses, but upon seeing the fireball was still there, she quickly panicked and ran back for their home.
