Vindala Chalanther was a retired adventurer and illusionist who lived in Marsember, Cormyr. She tutored apprentice magicians but would accept hire as a spellcaster, and enjoyed relaxing at the Drowning Flagon inn. She was petite with black hair and dark eyes, although she often disguised herself. She liked to pull pranks.
