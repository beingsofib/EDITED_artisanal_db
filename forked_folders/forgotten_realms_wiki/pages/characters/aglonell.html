Aglonell was a hunter in Icewind Dale in 1485 DR.

History

Aglonell was a trapper and hunter living near Bryn Shander in the Ten Towns. In 1485, he warned of the threat of Hedrun Arnsfirth after finding several traps raided and small animals stolen. In fact, the culprits were wererats of Ship Rethnor. Aglonell spoke of his problems with Mithann, who recruited adventurers to investigate.
