Lhaerhlin Masram was an innkeep who inhabited the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Lhaerlin was tall and well-built.

Activities

He owned and ran the Raging Lion inn located on Lion Street in the northeastern section of Waterdeep's North Ward.

Possessions

He always wore his ring of spell turning and ring of lightning.
