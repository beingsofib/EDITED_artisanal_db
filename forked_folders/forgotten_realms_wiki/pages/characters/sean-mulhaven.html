Sean Mulhaven was a bloodforge mage who camped as a squatter near Herne's Wood in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

Sean harassed and insulted everyone who came past his camp. Finally, the leader of the Legendary Campaign arrived and demanded Sean give it up. When Sean only hurled abuses, the leader decided to teach him courtesy, through bloodforge battle.
