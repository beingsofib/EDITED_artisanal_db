Lynxal was the thief for the Company of the Bright Spear in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

He was thin with short blond hair and a ratlike face.

Possessions

He had a thin knife hidden behind his neck.

History

In Mirtul 6 of the , he was slain by Ghondarrath after trying to pickpocket him at the Rising Moon.
