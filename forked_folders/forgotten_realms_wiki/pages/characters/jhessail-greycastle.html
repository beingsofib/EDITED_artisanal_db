Jhessail Greycastle was the commander of the exiled Knights of the Black Fist.

Personality

Jhessail was despite all loyal to her city and was totally shocked by the Vorgansharax's coup and the betrayal of her fellows. She was a firebrand sworn to reclaim her city or die trying.

History

Jhessail was a high-ranking member of the Knights of the Black Fist.

She exemplified the corruption and cruelty that was synonymous with organization and prospered.

In 1489 DR Jhessail lead a rebellion against Vorgansharax but was betrayed by her fellow officers and captured.

She was freed by an adventuring party hired by the resistance and managed to escape Phlan.

Around 1492 DR, Jhessail led an alliance to reclaim Phlan.
