Lady Polaris was the archwizard leader of Delia in the Netherese Empire during the Shadowed Age.

Description

The Netherese female was attractive with golden skin and snow white hair.

Relationships

The mentalist enjoyed bossing around her , Candlemas, and her , Sysquemalyn.

History

Circa , Lady Polaris was concerned with a blight known as rust appearing on the wheat crops of her people.

Around , Lady Polaris succumbed to fear and perished in her castle during the End of the End.
