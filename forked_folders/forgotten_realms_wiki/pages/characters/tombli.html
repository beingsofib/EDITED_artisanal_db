Tombli was a half-duergar dwarf warcaster of Abbathor, and the captain of the Company of the Chimera in the late 14 century DR.

Description

The dwarf's fingers were blistered as was his forge-hardened face. He had hard, black eyes.

Personality

Tombli was domineering

Abilities

He was a warcaster of Abbathor, a sort of cleric, though his healing was inferior.

One of his spells caused veins to burst through the skin, flaying it; he named it Abbathor's flowering.

Possessions

He bore a bejeweled dagger, likely a dagger of venom, that served also as his holy symbol.

Activities

Tombli led the Company of the Chimera

History

Tombli was the child of an exiled dwarf and a duergar princess; he was born an outcast. He eventually became the leader of the Company of the Chimera.

He'd once skinned a woman alive.

Years later, in Mirtul 21 of the , he got into a fight with Saskia, a recalcitrant Uthgardt woman who had joined his company. He was losing the brawl until his own men stepped in to hold her down. Later that day, he purchased a pseudodragon from a Calishite trader; he had to ask Saskia to identify the creature. The dragon struck her with its venomous stinger in the process.

He had its stinger amputated afterwards, and tormented it in the stables through the day. Come night, he was ambushed by Saskia again. Though he had the upper hand at first, paralyzing her with magic and torturing her, she displayed a previously unseen talent for magic and unlocked the cage telepathically. The furious pseudodragon made him flee into the night. His men then went after Saskia.
