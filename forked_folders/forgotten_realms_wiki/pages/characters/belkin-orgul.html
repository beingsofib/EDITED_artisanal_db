Belkin Orgul was a shopkeeper in Hill's Edge in 1359 DR.

Activities

Belkin managed a weapon shop.

Personality

Belkin was a cynic, always ready to expect the worst situations, believing that the world could turn against him at any moment. He was an able judge of people, however.
