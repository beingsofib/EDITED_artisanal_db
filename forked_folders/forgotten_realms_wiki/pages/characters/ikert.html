Ikert was a halfling merchant from the City of Coin, Athkatla in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Ikert was a hin male who liked to dress in black leathers and carried a short sword.

History

In the , Ikert ran his merchant business from a stall in Athkatla's Wave District. His humble stock included some mundane weapons and armor with an occasional common magic potion. He politely tried to convince clients of the finest quality of his wares, despite poor selection and prevalent thievery in the district.
