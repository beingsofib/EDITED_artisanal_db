Jorin Kell Harthan was a captain of the Simbul's Guard in the late 14 century DR.

History

In the , Jorin went to Araevin Teshurr explaining that he must go to the royal palace escorted by Jorin.

Later, he joined Araevin's party in their search of the Gatekeeper's Crystal.
