Jagdish was a priestess of Velsharoon in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

She cut a dark figure, tall.

Personality

She was fanatical, almost martyr-like.

Abilities

Besides her unique necrophant abilities, Jagdish was notably agile.

Possessions

She was armed with two vials enchanted with Deathmaster's vial.

Activities

Jagdish was an acolyte.

Relationships

She served under Yerian.

History

In , when the Chantry of St. Sammaster was attacked by Zhentarim, she received orders to hole up in the laboratory and ambush anyone who walked in.
