Belgos of Selgaunt was an author on or before the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

Belgos was the author of Draconic Magic: A Study on the Effects on the Nondraconic Mind, a book that had been published on or before the , where one copy of the tome was in the Chantry of St. Sammaster.
