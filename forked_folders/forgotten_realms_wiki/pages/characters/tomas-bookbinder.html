Tomas Bookbinder was a human proxy of the Faerûnian power known as Deneir and one of his most prominent.

Activities

Like other proxies of Deneir, he was occasionally sent from the Library of All Knowledge to carry a book, message, or some news to or from Deneir's master Oghma.
