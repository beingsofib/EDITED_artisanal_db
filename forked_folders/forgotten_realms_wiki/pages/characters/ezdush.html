Ezdush was a sage and a servant of Immurk living in the Pirate Isles during the mid–12 century DR.

Activities

Ezdush researched the strange and gigantic carvings found on the cliff faces of the large mountain on the isle of Paldir. The lack of hand or footholds on the carvings led him to believe them to be made from powerful magic in a single day.
