Theophelus was a mortician from the city of Liara in Nova Vaasa, one of the Domains of Dread.

Activities

He ran a mortuary in the Merchants' Quarter of Liara, embalming dead bodies and preparing them for interring.

History

Around the mid-to-late 14 century DR, when several adventurers from Ravens Bluff came to Liara somehow, they were almost certainly hired to investigate a string of grisly murders. The bodies were taken care of by Theophelus, who allowed the new investigators to examine them, so long as they didn't defile or damage the corpses.
