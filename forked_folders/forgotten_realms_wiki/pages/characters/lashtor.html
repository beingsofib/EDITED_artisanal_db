Lashtor was a Warlord of Silverymoon during the 9 century DR.

Activities

Lashtor seized the title of Warlord by force in the , when High Mage Elué Dualen left the city. He persecuted mages within Silverymoon until he was deposed in the , by Yril and Tanalanthara Mytersaal.
