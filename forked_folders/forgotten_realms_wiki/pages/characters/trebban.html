Trebban was an apiary owner and and brewer from Eaglton in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Possessions

He owned the apiary in Eaglton.

Activities

Trebban made mead out of honey.

Relationships

He employed Tregail as a beekeeper.

History

When adventurers from Ravens Bluff were sent to Stag Falls to see why the taxes were late and the patrol sent to determine why was missing, they passed through Eaglton on the way. They may have damaged the apiary, which would enrage its owner, and get them fined.
