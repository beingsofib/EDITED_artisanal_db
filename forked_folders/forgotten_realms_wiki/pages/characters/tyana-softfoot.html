Tyana Softfoot was the leader of the thieves' guild in Zhentil Keep in 1373 DR.

Activities

Tyana led the guild for mutual survival and propagation of her trade. She was an able strategist and tactician and her primary goal was survival. She was a lifelong resident of Zhentil Keep and knew the lay of the land well.

History

In 1373 DR, Tyana allied her guild with the Handful of Coins merchants, gaining much wealth. However, she was ready to abandon them when the Zhentarim would act against the Coins.
