Gordy was a powerful wood woad in the King's Forest of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, the drow destroyed a keep in the King's Forest that was atop a locus of magic. The site was then taken over by werewolf bandits, who were consequently driven out by Arganon Pine and the Purple Dragons, who created a spell ward to keep the werewolves away. All this activity drew the attention of malevolent druids, who sought to capture the magical site for themselves and use it to control the area. While the War Wizard Zenyff Bravehand created a new ward against the forces of nature, the wood woad Gordy led a final attack on the defenders that ultimately failed.
