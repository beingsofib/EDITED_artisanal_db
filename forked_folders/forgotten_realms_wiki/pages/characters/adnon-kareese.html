Ad'non Kareese was a male drow and a member of a band of four renegades including Donnia Soldou, Kaer'lic Suun Wett, and Tos'un Armgo.

History

Ad'non belonged to House Khareese of Ched Nasad. He was once a scout, thief, and assassin there, but a failed assassination attempt forced him to leave the Underdark.

He was killed in battle in 1370 DR when Drizzt Do'Urden vaulted behind him and sliced his head off.

Relationships

He was Donnia Soldou's lover.
