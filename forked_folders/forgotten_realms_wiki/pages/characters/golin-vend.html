Golin Vend was a resident of Beregost during the mid–14 century DR. He often acted as a guide of sorts to newcomers in town.

He was quite proud that his town boasted the "best smithy in all the realms", Taerom Fuiruim, who ran the Thunderhammer Smithy. Golin considered the Red Sheaf to be a lower-quality inn, when compared to the Jovial Juggler or Burning Wizard.
