Annadas Athlo was a blind monk and a playwright who followed teachings of Ilmater, The Broken God sometime before the late 14 century DR.

History

Annadas Athlo's most well known work was the "grand play" called The Shattered Throne Tumbles. By the late 14 century DR, his play was a part of The Crown Aflame's repertoire – famous theater inside a repurposed temple of Azuth that stood in the Temple District of Athkatla in Amn.
