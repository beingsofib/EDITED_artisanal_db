Fitzallus the Footpad was a Sembian bard, the first owner of Harold, a powerful enchanted heavy crossbow, who died some time before the late 14 century DR.

History

When Fitzallus commissioned his enchanted crossbow, he endlessly praised the new weapon. His excitement over the crossbow was such that his friends began teasing the bard, saying that the weapon was Fitzallus's new "best friend" and he needed to give it an appropriate name. When the enchanted weapon was ready, Fitzallus proudly strolled into a tavern with a bright smile and announced to his friends the weapon's name – Harold.

Several months later, Fitzallus the Footpad perished in a burglary. According to his will, Harold was to be buried with the bard, yet the crossbow disappeared before the funeral.

Sometime later, by the , Fitzallus's crossbow ended up in the hands of Rhynwis, the head of a fledgling thieves guild on Sword Coast North.
