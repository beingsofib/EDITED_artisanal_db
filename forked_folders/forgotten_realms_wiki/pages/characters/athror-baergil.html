Athror Baergil (1301 – 1391 DR) was a wealthy and successful mill owner who resided in northern Sembia.

Relatives

Athror was brother to famous Captain Darrask Baergil, and was one of seven siblings. He was born in 1301 DR on a farm near to Ashabenford in Mistledale. His father was Angram Baergil and his mother was Baelaetha Baergil. The subsequent Captain Emrius Baergil, also of the Riders of Mistledale, was a descendant of Athror.
