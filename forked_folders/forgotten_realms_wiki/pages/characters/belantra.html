Belantra was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

Possessions

Belantra carried a mightywand, a device that allowed her to trigger a number of wands at the same time.

History

Belantra discovered Elminster Aumar attempting to furtively enter a War Wizard barrack in Marsember one early spring night in , and attacked him with all her power. It barely slowed him; by the time he finally made it to Caladnei, whom he sought to converse with, Belantra had fainted.
