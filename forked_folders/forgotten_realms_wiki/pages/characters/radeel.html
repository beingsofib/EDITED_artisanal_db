Radeel was an expatriate from the land of Calimshan, living in the City of Coin, Athkatla, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Radeel was a pale-skinned man with brown hair.

History

Belmin Gergas frequented the the grand market in Athkatla's Center District – Waukeen's Promenade in the . He was not a native of Amn and did not know Athkatla well, sometimes getting lost and asking for directions to his lodgings.
