Omar al-Farid was a holy slayer and member of the Grey Fire in 1367 DR.

Personality

Omar was more a follower than a leader, trusting in his sister's judgement in most matters.

Description

Omar was a chubby man who had a round face. He wore dark clothing to match his hair and eyes.

History

Omar's parents were killed when he was still a child, forcing him and his sister Fadiya to fend for themselves on the streets of Hiyal until they were welcomed by the Everlasting.
