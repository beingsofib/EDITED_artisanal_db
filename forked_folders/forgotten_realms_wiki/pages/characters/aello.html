Lucky Aello was a rather unscrupulous shopkeeper and the proprietor of Lucky Aello's Discount Store in Baldur's Gate during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Aello sold a number of cursed items from his shop, found on the eastern side of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. He primarily dealt in scrolls and potions, but also managed to get his hands on some, less-than-useful magical weapons.
