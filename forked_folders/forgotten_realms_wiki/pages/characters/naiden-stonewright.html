Naiden Stonewright was a dwarven engineer active during the mid–12 century DR.

Abilities

A craftsman of some talent, Naiden enchanted the buttresses of the Arkhen Bridge to improve their strength and durability. Although the bridge was largely destroyed during the due to a severe flood, the engineers who rebuilt it afterwards were able to rely on the stone casements of the original bridge.
