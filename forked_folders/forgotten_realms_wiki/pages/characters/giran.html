Giran was a commoner child of Hannah from the City of Coin, Athkatla, the capital of Amn in the late 14 century DR.

History

in the , Giran and his mother visited a circus performing at the center of Athkatla's Center District, in Waukeen's Promenade. Unfortunately, the circus' tent fell victim to a mad gnome illusionist Kalah, who transformed the tent's interior into a fanciful tower filled with guests and performers, ensorcelled to appear as monsters. Giran's mother was trapped inside, transformed into a giant spider. The child was left outside of the spellbound tent, crying for his missing mother. When the illusion was broken and the gnome defeated, Giran was reunited with Hannah.
