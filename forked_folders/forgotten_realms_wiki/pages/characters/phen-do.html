Baron Phen Do, called the Outlaw, was a rebellious noble of the city of Khampo in Tabot.

History

Wanting to restore the rule of the nobles over Tabot and depose the High Lord, Phen Do launched a rebellion. In response, the worried Mag-chi Le-Khung (the military office) stationed three divisions in Khampo to contain the revolt.

Activities

By that year, Phen Do was training his secret army, nicknamed the Raccoon-dogs, hidden in the mountains east of Nafeen Gompa. They had a base and a form of ninja school. Through gifts and spies, he had influenced both the Nafeen and Zotung Gompas and bribed the Chi-kyap Khempo (the lord chamberlain representing the nobility to the High Lord). Stories spread of Phen Do's exploits and the dzong-pons (sheriffs) of Tabot carried pictures of him. He was in communication with the similarly rebellious Lady Holang of Pim Tung.

Description

He had a strong build and black hair, which he braided and was long enough to hang down his back. He had scars on his left arm and hand, which he concealed by wearing a glove.

Possessions

Phen Do fought with a magical battleaxe that he dipped in poison.
