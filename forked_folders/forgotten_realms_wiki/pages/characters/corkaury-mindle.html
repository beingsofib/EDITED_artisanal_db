Corkaury Mindle was Taegan Nightwind's accountant. He was married to Olpara Mindle. He was small even for a halfling, a wizened, hunched person, who nonetheless commanded the respect of everyone in the academy. After Taegan's academy burned down on the night of Ches 12, , he comforted the maestro and talked him out of several rash decisions. In Greengrass of the same year, Taegan and the faerie dragon Jivex were his guests briefly after a successful raid on the Cult of the Dragon.
