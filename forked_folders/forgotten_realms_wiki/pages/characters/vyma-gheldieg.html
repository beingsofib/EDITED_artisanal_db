Vyma Gheldieg was a noble of House Gheldieg of Amn in the mid–14 century DR. She was the daughter of Namarch Curkon Gheldieg and the wife of Dahaunarch Pehllus Tanislove. She was fun-loving and known informally as the "party-girl priestess".
