Haskrayth was the precept of the city of Klondor in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Haskrayth's duties included protecting the area surrounding the city east all the way to the Plains of Purple Dust due to the increased presence of various monsters. He was assisted by a garrison from Chessenta. Haskrayth was a skilled warrior and chief of the mercenaries serving under Assuran.
