Oratham Hethcanter was a loyal servant of the Crown of Cormyr in the late 13 to the early 14 centuries DR.

History

Lord Oratham Hethcanter of the influential Hethcanter family rose to the rank of the garrison commander of the High Horn military stronghold at the top of High Horn mountain during the reign of King Rhigaerd Obarskyr II. Oratham lost his life battling outlaws in the Stonelands region of Cormyr.
