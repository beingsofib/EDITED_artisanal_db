Feckle Raspude a gnomish swashbuckler, prankster, and heartbreaker, often considered to be one of the most unusual gnomes of his generation, sometime before the 14 century DR.

Description

Feckle was a flamboyant swashbuckler whose flair, impudence, and lopsided grin made him popular among young and impressionable gnomish lasses.

History

Feckle Raspude's reputation attracted many young glory-hunting gnomes to challenge him to a duel, but Feckle wielded a powerful enchanted rapier called Namarra, which allowed him to easily put his opponents to sleep and humbled them with pranks and practical jokes.
