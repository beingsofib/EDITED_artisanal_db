Vilcuum was a mind flayer in Ryxyg in the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa 1486 DR, Vilcuum was one of the mind flayers who joined the allied attack on Maerimydra. With the support of an adventuring party, Vilcuum fought an aboleth named Nevik'Dethig and his orc minions at the Lake of Blood.
