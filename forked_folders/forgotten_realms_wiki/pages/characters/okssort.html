Okssort was a fire giant who, together with his wife Ildmane, led an attack on Triboar sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

History

In the late 15 century DR, under the orders of Duke Zalto, Okssort and Ildmane went in search of pieces of the Vonindod. They led a giant contingent to Triboar, the place of an ancient great battle between giants and dragons. However, they were defeated by some adventurers.
