Prince Ereskas Torlath was a royal son of Amn's Torlath dynasty who founded the city of Mirabar in the 7 century DR.

Relationships

Ereskas was the son of King Esmel Torlath II, and the younger brother of Esmel III. He did not have any children.

History

Ereskas traveled north with the family blade Namarra in the and founded Mirabar atop of the ruins of ancient Gharraghaur. When Ereskas died in the , he was entombed with Namarra within the city.

The Goblin Wars of the , resulted in Mirabar being overrun and looted. Ereskas's tomb was also raided, and Namarra was stolen and carried off by the goblin chief Nethaug.
