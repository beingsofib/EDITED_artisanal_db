Kyra Armond was a Harper agent and druid who worked in the Storm Horns in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Kyra was a pale human woman with short white hair, wearing leather armor with a long skirt.

History

Around the time of the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon during the 1480s DR, Kyra served as a scout for Kara Patwin and Lorant Tass in the Storm Horns, where she was puzzled to see large groups of orcs, hill giants, and gnolls carrying packs through the Tumblerock Pass. When Rina Belostos found orcs attacking Eveningstar with foreign weapons, she realized they were an army supplied through Tumblerock Pass and sent a team to join Kyra. Kyra shared the local situation with them and helped plan the ambush, using traps and suggesting allying with local harpies. Thanks to their teamwork the supply convoys were destroyed and General Grablandurr fell in battle shortly after.
