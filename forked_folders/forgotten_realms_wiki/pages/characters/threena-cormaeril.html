Threena Cormaeril

Lady Threena Cormaeril was a member of the Cormaeril noble house of Cormyr who married Lord Dheolur in order to spy on the house for King Duar Obarskyr. She married King Duar sometime after he slew her husband for treason.

Description

She had blonde hair.
