Frait the Ardent was an elf favored soul in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Frait had gray skin, gray eyes, and short dark gray hair. She wore a sleeveless dress of sorts, which was entirely black and gray.

History

In the during the Elven Crusade, Frait joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp outside the ruins of Myth Drannor. She provided training to prospective favored souls at the camp.
