Ditriv Starkdale was a sage at the Monastery of the Yellow Rose in the late 15th century DR.

Personality

Ditriv was focused only on his research.

History

Ditriv was one of the guests at the monastery in 1491 DR. He needed to use the monastery's libraries for his research into the development of Damaran settlements over the last few centuries and their interaction with the Nars, goblins, and orcs.
