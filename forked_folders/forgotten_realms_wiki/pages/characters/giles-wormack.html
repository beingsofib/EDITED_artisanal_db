Giles Wormack was a human boy during the late 15th century DR.

History

In 1484 DR, during the orc and drow assault on the Silver Marches, Giles lived with his sister Karolina Wormack in Sundabar. They assisted knight-commander Aleina Brightlance with her speech just before the fall of the city.
