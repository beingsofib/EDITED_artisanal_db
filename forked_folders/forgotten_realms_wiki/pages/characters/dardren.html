Dardren

Person
nameDardren

Dardren was a wizard from Memnon in Calimshan, of the 13th century DR.

Dardren was slain by Glaragama Dunrohiyr, a rogue archmage who'd disguised herself as a man. When she killed Dardren, she revealed her true form, then stole all his magic.
