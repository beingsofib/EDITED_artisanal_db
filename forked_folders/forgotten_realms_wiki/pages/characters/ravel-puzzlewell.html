Ravel Puzzlewell was a night hag and the greatest of the Gray Sisters of the Wastes.

Description

Ravel was exceptionally hideous, even for a hag. Her dark-purple skin was covered in warts, scabs, and hairy moles. A blackish tongue lay hidden behind purple lips and yellowed tusks. A few long wisps of black hair hung from her spotted scalp, matching a single furry brow. Her ember eyes were bloodshot with black veins.

Despite a stick-thin, near-starved frame, she possessed a sagging pot belly. Typically hunched over, she moved with a pained skitter.

She was clad in a tattered dress made of white, gummy cobwebs.

Possessions

Before she was exiled by the Lady of Pain, Ravel possessed dozens of magical items such as a charm of blackness, a cube of force, the fiendish eye of Kalem'Darr, a harp of pandemonium, and a ring of human influence.

History

The Nameless one used Kesai-Serris' blood as a portal key to enter the Black-Barbed Maze.

Fragments of Ravel

Ravel was a branching being whose life was like a massive tree extending across the planes. Due to her strange nature, Ravel existed in Sigil and other places simultaneously in multiple forms as different people. The Nameless One encountered several of these manifestations during his journey. Following the hag's death, these fragments of the witch began to unravel, resulting in their lives ending.
