Magnus Arifek was a human priest of Lathander, adventurer, and former blacksmith of the Thunder Peaks wandering the lands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He was a sun-tanned man with a mane of black hair.

Personality

Magnus would, in his youth, run very far very quickly to avoid responsibility, but found that no matter how far or how fast, it would inevitably catch up with him.

Possessions

He wore a set of enchanted chainmail +2, wielded an enchanted flail +3, and an enchanted shield +1. He carried a bag of holding.

Relationships

Fleeing his home after getting a girl pregnant, he fell in with the wandering fire elementalist named Ceryx, and afterwards managed to acquire Garalt a wolf companion and friend.

History

Once the heir to a blacksmith shop, both his father and grandfather were blacksmiths and priests of Lathander, and he was on a similar trajectory. However, he left the blacksmith role at home and fled all responsibility to become an adventurer instead.
