Aelgar the Fierce was a barbarian who took up residence in the city of Neverwinter for a time during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Aelgar venerated Uthgar, the deity of the Uthgardt people.

History

After the Siege of Neverwinter, in the , Aelgar stationed himself at the Neverwinter Guard post in the city's Tower District. While there he led the city's defenders against waves of attacks made upon them by the Many-Arrows orcs. Among the marauding assailants was the enormous ogre, Mog the Crusher.
