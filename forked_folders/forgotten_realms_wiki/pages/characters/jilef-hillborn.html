Jilef Hillborn was an individual that lived in Helm's Hold during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Jilef was a regular patron of the Old Dirty Dwarf inn. She was an outspoken critic of arcane magic, and blamed spellcasters for the rise of cult-activity in her city.

History

She was present in Helm's Hold when the city was taken over the Prophet Rohini and the Ashmadai in the late 1470s DR.
