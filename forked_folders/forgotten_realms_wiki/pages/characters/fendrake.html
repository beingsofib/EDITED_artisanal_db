Fendrake was an inhabitant of the town of Ghars in Cormyr in the 14 century DR.

Relations

He was married to Magda. His nephew was a chandler in the town.

History

Fendrake claimed to have once seen the ghost of the bandit chieftain Fastred.

Years later, in the Bold Bard tavern in early Eleint of the , his nephew the chandler used Fendrake's sighting as proof of the ghost's existence, saying he'd never seen anything that wasn't there. The tailor pointed to Magda's looks to disprove this.
