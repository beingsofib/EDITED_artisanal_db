Ogden Flamebeard was a tavern owner in Bryn Shander sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

Activities

Ogden managed the Kelvin's Comfort tavern, the most known drinking place of the city, famous for his Flamebeard's Firebrandy drink.

History

In his youth Ogden had worked in many mines and had grow a network of contact in all dwarfholds of the North, including Mithral Hall.
