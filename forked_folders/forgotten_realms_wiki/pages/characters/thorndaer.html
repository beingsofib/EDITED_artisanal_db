Thorndaer was the fifth King to rule the city-state of Westgate.

Activities

Thorndaer served as the Captain of the city watch during the reign of King Glaurath. Thorndaer was also a close friend of Glaurath, so close in fact that he actually inherited the throne upon the king's death in . Thorndaer would reign for a few years until when he was slain by a vampire who took the throne.
