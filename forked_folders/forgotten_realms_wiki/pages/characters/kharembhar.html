Kharembhar was Fzoul Chembryl's personal bodyguard, serving from at least 1356 DR until his probable death in 1368 DR.

Description

Kharembhar was extremely muscular and huge, standing over tall.

History

Kharembhar hailed from lands to the south of the Moonsea region.

He was presumed to have perished in the sacking of Zhentil Keep in 1368 DR.

Possessions

Kharembhar owned a pair of bracers of defense and an enchanted sword.
