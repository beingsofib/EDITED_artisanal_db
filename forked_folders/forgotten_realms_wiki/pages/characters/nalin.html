Nalin was a priest of Helm who lived in the town of Nashkel during the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

Nalin was a very understanding man. He interpreted Helm's dogma as justice being an ideal that could be found through penance rather than punishment.

Activities

He served at the town's local temple, offering healing services and certain divine items for sale.

Possessions

Nalin wore a suit of plate mail armor and carried a +1 enchanted mace.

Relationships

He thought highly of Captain Brage, the leader of the town's guardsmen.
