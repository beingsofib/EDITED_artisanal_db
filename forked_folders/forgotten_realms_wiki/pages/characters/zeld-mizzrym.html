Zeld Mizzrym was a drow priestess of House Mizzrym in Menzoberranzan.

History

Around 1361 DR, Zeld was a Mizzrym priestess and an instructor at Arach-Tinilith. She was in charge of teaching first-year students and was the first person to notice the rivalry between Shakti Hunzrin and Liriel Baenre.
