Roquern was the secret ruler of the island of Jacaman in the Korinn Archipelago of the Moonshae Isles as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

He was a devil who used magic to disguise his true nature, choosing to appear as a Ffolk gentleman.

Abilities

Roquern used his infernal powers to enslave mortals, either by offering them infernal deals in which they traded away their freedom (and/or their souls) or by having them consume spices which he had tainted with his power. Consuming even a small amount of these tainted spices rendered a mortal more susceptible to coercion to perform evil acts, while consuming a large amount turned them into a thrall under Roquern's control.

Activities

By the late 15 century DR, Roquern controlled the growing, harvesting, processing, and exporting of a variety of valuable spices which were unique to the island of Jacaman and which he tainted with his infernal powers. His spice plantations were operated by a combination of his slaves as well as evil creatures whom he had recruited.

History

Roquern seized control over Jacaman sometime in the 15 century DR. By the , his schemes were fully operational and he was taking great pains to convince the outside world that Jacaman was just another insignificant island as he exported his tainted spices.
