Garret was a human soldier of Fort Locke near the Mere of Dead Men on the Sword Coast North.

History

Garret was one of Fort Locke's soldiers that went with Tann and his men to patrol. However, they were captured by the shadow priest. Unlike others, Garret was killed by the undead.
