Ineddra was a high elf member of the Lords' Alliance in Cormanthor in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Ineddra was a scholar. She specialized in studying the planes, in particular the threats posed by other planes.

History

Circa 1486 DR, Ineddra discovered that someone in the Allano rothé farm had discovered a secret hideout of House Dlardrageth and went to investigate. There she met an adventuring party.
