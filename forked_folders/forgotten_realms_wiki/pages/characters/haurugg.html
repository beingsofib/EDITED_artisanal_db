Haurugg was a woodsman of the Storm Horns during the late 15 century DR.

Description

Haurugg was a large half-orc fellow with gray skin and a black mohawk and sideburns.

History

Around the time of the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, the forces of Risen Netheril took over part of the Storm Horns as Archwizard Amskar built his army. Haurugg lived in a cave near Yarrow Glade and wisely tried to avoid any interactions with the growing threat, only for them to take over his home while he was out. He might have been lucky enough to run into Harpers who were working with Oriphaun Huntsilver nearby, and then helped clear out the Netherese invaders.
