Timber (squirrel)

Person
nameTimber
homeEmerald Grove, Sword Coast

Timber was a squirrel that lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.

Timber was very protective of the land she claimed as its territory, the breadth of the Emerald Grove. While she was brave in the face of invaders, Timber would at times negotiate passage across her lands.
