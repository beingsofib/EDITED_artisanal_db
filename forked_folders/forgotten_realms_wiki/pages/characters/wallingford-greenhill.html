Wallingford Greenhill was a halfling rogue adventuring in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He loved having fun above everything else, and for Wallingford Greenhill stealing was fun. So was cooking and eating, and so he adventured about mostly seeking new recipes to steal.

Possessions

He owned a dagger, sling, whistle, and a set of thieves' tools, besides his most precious possession, his pet goat from whom he got a steady supply of fresh milk.
