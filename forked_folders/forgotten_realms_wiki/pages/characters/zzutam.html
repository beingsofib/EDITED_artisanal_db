Zzutam was an influential ice para-elemental whose actions directly affected the Fall of Zhentil Keep in the .

History

During the month of Tarsakh in , Zzutam reasserted his control over a group of frost giants who had besieged Zhentil Keep. Zzutam retreated with the frost giants to the north as the weather began to warm.
