Treylo Grassmantle was a hin tailor for hire from the city of Neverwinter, active in the late 15 century DR.

History

Treylo became an orphan early in his life. Forced to take care of himself, Treylo was taken in by a temple. The halfling learned to patch the clerics' robes and in the process discovered his talent. Subsequently, Treylo started offering his seaming services to adventurers and defenders of Neverwinter by the late 15 century DR.
