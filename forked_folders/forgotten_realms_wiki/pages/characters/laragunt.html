Laragunt of Threskel was an archmage.

History

Laragunt was a wizard from Threskel. Around 1487 DR, he went in search of the Lost Spell at the Oldspire mansion, like many other archmages. When a spellstorm hit the place, Laragunt and a Red Wizard of Thay tried to escape but the magic energies made them insane.
