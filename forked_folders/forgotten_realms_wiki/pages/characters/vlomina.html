Vlomina was a dutiful hireling guard who worked for Baldur's Mouth in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Vlomina was a fair-skinned gold dwarven woman with brown eyes and short-trimmed brown hair. She trimmed her facial hair short, in contrast with many other dwarven women who were either clean-shaven to grew their beards out.

Possessions

Guard Vlomina was equipped with a chainmail uniform of Baldur's Mouth's guards and armed with a simple mace and wooden shield.

History

In the , Vlomina was employed by Baldur's Mouth and its editor-in-chief, Ettvard Needle. She was tasked with guarding the elevator that descended into Baldur's Mouth's off-limits basement. None were allowed to pass without Ettvard's permission.
