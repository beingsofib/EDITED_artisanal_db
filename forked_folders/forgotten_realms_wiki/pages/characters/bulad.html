Bulad, also called Bulad the Steelmaker, was a renowned weaponsmith inhabiting in Hiyal around 1367 DR.

Activities

Bulad was considered to be one of the finest weaponsmiths throughout all of Zakhara. He specialized in smithing longswords, broadswords, cutlasses, and of course scimitars.

Bulad was found dead with a dagger buried in his neck, leaving his daughter to continue the family heritage. Though the magistrate declared his death accidental, his daughter was convinced someone connected with powerful friends was to blame.

Relationships

His daughter, Fuladayah bint Bulad, followed in his footsteps.
