Cho Lu-k'u was a wizard who once lived in Koryo.

Cho Lu-k'u died in the Dang Caverns and cursed its tunnels. This may have led to the evil monsters that lurked there.

Meo Cha heard of this story from his old tutor some time before 1350 DR.
