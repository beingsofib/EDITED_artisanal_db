Wessalen was the prelate or leader of the Order of the Radiant Heart in the Amnian city of Athkatla during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

As part of his duties to the order's chapterhouse, Prelate Wessalen would preside over the judgement and induction of new knights that had completed their careers as squires.

Relationships

Wessalen had taken a knight aspiring knight named Cathras to be his squire.
