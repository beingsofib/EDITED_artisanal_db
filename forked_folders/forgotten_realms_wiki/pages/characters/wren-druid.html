Wren was a member of the Shadow Druids of the Cloakwood, who infiltrate the Emerald Grove during the late 15 century DR.

Description

He had dark brown hair and wore his facial hair in a simple goatee.

Activities

While spying upon the Emerald Grove, Olodan took on the form of a large, gray rat.

Possessions

Wren wore a simple leather harness, wood-and-leather greaves, and a cloth hood over his head. He preferred to fight with a large greatclub.

History

As of the , Wren had been dispatched along with Pinta to support Olodan, their superior within the Shadow Druids' hierarchy. The trio remained transformed into their rodent forms, closely following Kagha, who sought to convert druids of the Emerald Grove to the cause of the Shadow Druids.
