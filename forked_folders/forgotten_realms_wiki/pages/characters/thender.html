Thender was someone in service of the Cult of the Dragon in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Thender was the source of the information Holvan and Brasker possessed regarding Shandril Shessair in the , while attempting to capture her during her caravan passage from Scornubel to Waterdeep. The two of them were overheard by her and her husband during a battle, saying amongst other things that Thender had warned them half of Faerûn was after her.
