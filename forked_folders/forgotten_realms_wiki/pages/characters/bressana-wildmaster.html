Bressana Wildmaster was an elf of New Sharandar who lived in the fey settlement during the late 15 century of the Faerûnian calendar.

Activities

She worked under the leadership of Pelendethas Everstride, serving at a camp on the edge of the Sharandan territory near its border with the Realm of Malabog.

History

When Malabog the fomorian king threatened New Sharandar circa the , Bressana and her allies took swift action against the dark fey minions of his domain. When Malabog's redcaps attempted to induce a feral state in the region's portal hounds, Bressana led the efforts to regain the trust of the fey canines. She also worked with some adventurers from Neverwinter to heal dryad trees that had also been harmed by the malevolent redcaps.
