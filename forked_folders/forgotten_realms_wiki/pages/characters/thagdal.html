Thagdal () was non-member of the Zhentarim who operated a small and local wizard's guild within the Tower of the Art in Zhentil Keep. He was a mousy, easily flustered individual who possessed a sharp mind and was always interested about the goings-on within the city. Despite this, he and his colleges within the wizard's guild tried to maintain a low profile, although Fzoul Chembryl always kept a close eye on them.

History

Thagdal started in magic career in the Zhentarim as apprentice of Sememmon and soon became a troubleshooter for Manshoon. Somehow Thagdal managed to exit from the Zhentarim and started his mage guild. He was a loyal supporter of Orgauth until his death.
