Harun el Halid was a wealthy moneylender of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

His son and heir was Husuf el Halid, who lived as a rich playboy in the Emerald Ward. Husuf was renowned for hosting extravagant parties and it was rumored his profligate spending had nearly bankrupted his father, but in truth Harun did not need to fund his son's lifestyle.
