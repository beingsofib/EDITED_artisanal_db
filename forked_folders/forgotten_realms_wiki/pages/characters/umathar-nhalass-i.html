Umathar Nhalass I, who was nicknamed the Horselord, was the first Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule Raumathar.

Activities

In , Umathar I completed his assimilation of the Raumviran tribes of the Endless Wastes of Northeast Faerûn, forming the nation of Raumathor with the capital at Winterkeep. He reigned as Arkahn until his death in .

Relationships

His firstborn son and heir was Umathar II.
