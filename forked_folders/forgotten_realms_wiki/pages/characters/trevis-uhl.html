Trevis Uhl was an innkeep inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

He ran the Defiant Gesture located on the west side of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter on the south bank of the River Tesh as of the .

Possessions

Trevis carried an enchanted claymore and wore a silver necklace that had the magic of a ring of protection.

History

Trevis was a Zhentilar soldier in Zhentil Keep during the destruction of the city in the . He abandoned the fight after seeing holy fire consume much of the city.
