Cora Mulphoon was the proprietor of the Buried Treasures inn, which operated in the town of Bremen during the late 15 century DR.

Description

She was described as being middle-aged as of the mid-1480s DR.

Personality

Cora was exceedingly complaisant, personally seeing to any possible need that her guests may have.

Relationships

Her only known family was her son Huarwar,

History

Circa the , Cora's son Huarwar began acting dramatically different following an excursion to bring back Speaker Dorbulgruf Shalescar after the elder dwarf wandered off on one his "excursions". He started acting cruel, and almost became violent with his mother, before quickly leaving town for the keep at Caer-Dineval.

Cora was devastated by her son's alarming actions, and began looking for adventurers to bring him back safely home.
