Zorind Tulwynd was a wheelwright inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Zorind was the guildmaster for the Wheelwrights' Guild circa the . He held the title of "Master Wright" within the guild and worked out of the Wheel Hall–their headquarters on River Street in the Trades Ward.
