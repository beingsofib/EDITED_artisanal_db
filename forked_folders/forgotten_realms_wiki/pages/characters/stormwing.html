Stormwing was a adventurous giant eagle ranger of Lurue and an agent of the Fangshields during the 14 century DR. With a vast array of contacts and allies across the Realms, Stormwing traveled far and often to help those who requested his aid.

Description

The giant eagle had a brown cloak of feathers over most of his body, which were lighter around the head, neck and chest. He had a keen black eyes, an extremely sharp, yellow-brown beak and mighty, razor-edged talons.

Relationships

Stormwing, third of his name, was an eagle of the Stormwind Aerie. This family of majestic eagles served as the aerial wing of the Fangshields, fighting off predators like wyverns and rescuing allies that were caught in otherwise unreachable terrain. His ancestors that shared his name were famous heroes of Faerûn, a heritage he was more than capable of continuing.

He had a number of friends and contacts among the rangers and druids of the realms, especially among the Harpers and Emerald Enclave. Stormwing had even befriended a particularly progressive Thayan wizard who traveled across the Western Heartlands.

Possessions

Stormwing wore a golden lesser ring of invisibility, which was an heirloom of his family.
