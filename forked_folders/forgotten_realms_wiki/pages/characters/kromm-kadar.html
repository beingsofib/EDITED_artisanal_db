Kromm Kadar was a silent blacksmith in High Dale.

History

Kromm was once a Zhentilar warrior before he settled in High Dale as blacksmith but always collaborated with the Zhents. When in 1358 DR, a secret Zhent occupation army occupied the dale, the wizard Angruin Myrvult killed a member of the Council of the High Dale spying for Sembia and placed Kromm in the empty seat.
