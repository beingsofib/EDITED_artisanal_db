Rhothar Steelsar was an unfortunate child of the exiled Amnian Parhek dynasty.

Relationships

Rhothar was the child of Princess Rasa Parhek Dornar Steelsar, and was the youngest sibling of Choris, Rosa, and Rohav.

History

Rhothar was born in Silverymoon, and was only 7 years old in the when the family was found and attacked by assassins from Amn. Dornar and Choris fought to the death, Rohav was kidnapped, and Rosa was killed, but Rasa and Rhothar managed to escape. He was believed to have died five years later in 1302 DR.
