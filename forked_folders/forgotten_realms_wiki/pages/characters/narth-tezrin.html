Narth Tezrin was an resident of Triboar and member of the Lionshield Coster during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

Narth managed the Lion's Share shop together with Alaestra Ulgar.

Personality

Narth had an adventurous spirit. He sought to become a person of renown, no matter the risks. He adored his colleague Alaestra and this stopped him from running away in search of adventures.
