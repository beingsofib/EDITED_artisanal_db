Baergrim was a mercenary in the late mid-14 century DR.

Relationships

Together with Eltragar and Mheriyam, Baergrim was part of the Blades. He appeared to be familiar with Mhulker, and had concerns that the latter may have been taken over by his "guest".

History

In , Eltragar, Mheriyam, and Baergrim were escorting Mhulker in Undermountain. Mhulker was seeking a particular woman to eat her brain; he found her chained and gagged in an altar, but it turned out to be a trap, which Baergrim spotted by noticing that the woman's gag covered her nose too. After Halaster Blackcloak's trap slew Mhulker and the ancient wizard revealed himself, the mercenaries fled.
