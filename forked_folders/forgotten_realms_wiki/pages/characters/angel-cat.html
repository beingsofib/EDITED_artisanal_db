Angel was a cat and the pet of the young elven girl Petrine.

Description

While Angel was a male cat, both Petrine always referred to it as "she", presumably believing Angel was a female.

Abilities

According to Petrine, Angel had the ability to somehow protect her from harm.

Possessions

Angel the cat had a favorite trinket that it carried around, a specific angel skin ring.

History

In the , Angel accompanied Petrine when she ran away from home. The stubborn cat went back however in order to retrieve its favorite ring. Petrine was distraught without her pet and protector.
