Baerold was a swordsman of Athalantar under Helm Stoneblade during the early-to-mid 3 century DR.

Personality

He favored the task of collecting boots from the dead.

Abilities

Baerold mimicked the call of a wolf rather well.

Activities

As a bandit, he carried out numerous attacks to hamper the Magelords of Athalantar.

Relationships

Arghel, a fellow bandit, was also his sibling. Both of them were under the orders of Helm Stoneblade.

History

Baerold was part of a six-man team including him, Arghel, Engarl, Sargeth, Nind, and Eladar, who was actually the prince in disguise, which ambushed and slew a dozen armsmen under cover of heavy snow in Hammer of . He took care to butcher the horses afterwards.
