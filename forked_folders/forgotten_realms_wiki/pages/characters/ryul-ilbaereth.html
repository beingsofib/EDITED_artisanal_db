Ryul Ilbaereth

Person
nameRyul Ilbaereth
sexMale
raceElf

Ryul Ilbaereth was an elf of House Ilbaereth who, with his followers, was rescued from death on the shores of Lake Eredruie in 360 DR by Arun Maerdrym, father of Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun.
