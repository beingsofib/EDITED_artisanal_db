Sharley was sailor from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in late 15 century DR.

Description

Sharley was a human woman with light skin and Kara-Turan features – black hair, cut short, and black eyes. She wore simple laborer's tunic.

History

In the , Sharley was a bosun who ran a small boat from the harbors of Baldur's Gate. After her last competent shipmate left the employ and joined the Flaming Fist, leaving only Listless and Kadugan to work with, two men who complained more than they loaded the cargo.
