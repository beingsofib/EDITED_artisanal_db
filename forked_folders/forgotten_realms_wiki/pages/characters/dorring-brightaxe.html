Dorring Brightaxe was a dwarf fighter in Daggerdale around 1479 DR.

History

Dorring was a dwarf born from the Brightaxe clan living in Daggerdale.

For a time, he adventured with the Byar's Seven until the group dissolved. He went back to the dale and started working as a scout for the constable Kendar Morn.
