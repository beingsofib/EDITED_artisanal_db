Jhant Daxer was a tavern owner who inhabited the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

He had fox-like features and a ruthless demeanor.

Activities

Jhant assumed ownership of the popular tavern and festhall called the Gentle Mermaid in the North Ward when the previous owner died. Before that he was a momeylender and caravan owner in Baldur's Gate.

Rumors & Legends

Jhant was actually a front for Xanathar. He smuggled, kidnapped, and performed all manner of illicit activities for the beholder, causing him to rarely be seen at the Gentle Mermaid.
