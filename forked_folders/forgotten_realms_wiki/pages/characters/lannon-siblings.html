Lannon siblings were villagers of West Harbor on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR.

History

These teenagers were contestants of the Harvest Fair brawl. The Kalach-Cha, aided by Bevil Starling and Amie Fern, needed to defeat them to pass to next round.
