Catelys Gulchbreul was an illithid of the Absolute from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Catelys Gulchbreul was a duergar or a drow, who was infected by the Absolute's tadpole with their transformation likely taking place at the very end of the creature's life.

History

In the , a large group of duergar, giant spiders, and drow, was slaughtered in the home of a Bloomridge Park groundskeeper, Lavernica. The murder spree was performed by Orin the Red and her Murder Tribunal of Bhaal. Catelys Gulchbreul's body was found already in its aberrant new form in a cellar underneath Lavernica's home, near a butchered drow woman, a grotesque display – an effigy representing Sendai, a drow Bhaalspawn who died a century earlier.
