Hairtooth was an ogrillion that lived on the Sword Coast during the mid–14 century DR.

Possesions

He carried with him a powerful set of magic items, including a pair of bracers of dexterity.

Relationships

Hairtooth was a bit more timid, less forceful, and likely a bit less intelligent than his companion Gnarl.

History

In the , Hairtooth and Gnarl spent their times extorting passers-by as they ventured near the bridge spanning the narrow waterway just east of a gnoll-controlled stronghold.
