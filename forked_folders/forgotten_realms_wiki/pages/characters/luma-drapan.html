Luma Drapan was a human inhabitant of Eveningstar during the late 15 century DR.

Description

She had light skin, long blonde hair, and green eyes. She wore a plain gray dress with black leggings.

History

Luma lost her brother during the first battle of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar. She could be seen with a Purple Dragon grieving at his grave.
