Alsapir was a fisherman of the Sword Coast North.

He was fishing in the Sea of Swords around the Red Rocks north of Waterdeep when a storm smashed his boat against one of the rocky islets, and he died there. That island came to be called Alsapir's Rock by the 1370s DR.
