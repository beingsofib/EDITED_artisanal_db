Triem was a ranger of the Yuirwood in Aglarond in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

He was one of eight Masters of the Yuirwood that survived an attack by assassins on the night of Mirtul 2, . Afterward, that same night, he was the ringleader for the torture and eventual murder of the captured assassin Kheil.
