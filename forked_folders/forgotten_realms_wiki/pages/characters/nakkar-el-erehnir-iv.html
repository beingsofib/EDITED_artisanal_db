Pasha Nakkar el Erehnir IV was the fifth bakkal of the Erehnir Dynasty of the Second Age of Calimshan.

History

Nakkar IV was born in the city of Volothamp in eastern Calimshan. He lived and reigned sometime between the 22 and 24 centuries before the Dalereckoning.

Nakkar IV is most famous for having his image immortalized in stone in the cliffs of Volothamp. Each of the Eight Erehnirs was a different representation of the bakkal—as ruler, priest, warrior, djinni, efreeti, dao, marid, and sun god.
