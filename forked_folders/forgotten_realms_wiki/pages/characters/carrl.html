Carrl was a businessman inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Personality

Though quite offensive, Carrl was also kind.

Activities

Carrl owned the Flying Fish, a bustling fish market located on Water Street in south Daggerford.
