Kavita was a wizard member of the Desai tribe, part of the nomadic Bedine, living in Anauroch, in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Kavita was very beautiful, with smooth brown skin, thick, long raven-black hair, and dark eyes.

History

Kavita was a Bedine wizard, who practiced her Art in secret, as the Netherese forbade the Bedine to practice magic.

She gave birth to her only child, Ruqiah, on the spring equinox, Ches 19, in the .

In the , Ruqiah caught the attention of the Netherese lord Parise Ulfbinder, who sent Shadovar agents Untharis and Alpirs De'Noutess to kidnap her. During the assault, Kavita was gravely wounded as she was stabbed by one of the Netherese, prompting the five-year-old Ruqiah to kill the attackers and heal her mother. Soon after, Ruqiah left to protect her parents from the Netherese.

Kavita would later train Ruqiah in the Art, among them several potent spells to control fire.
