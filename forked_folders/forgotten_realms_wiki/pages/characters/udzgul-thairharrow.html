Udzgul Thairharrow was a farmer in the village of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the late 13 or early 14 century DR.

Personality

He was considered to be half-witted.

History

When the folk of Maskyr's Vale came to investigate the collapse of old Maskyr's Tower, Udzgul claimed to have spotted a silvery doorway appearing for an instant in the air high over the ruins at sunset,

Udzgul was deceased by the late 1350s DR.
