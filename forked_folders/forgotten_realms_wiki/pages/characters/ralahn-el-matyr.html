Pasha Ralahn el Matyr was the guildmaster of the Jhasinnar Sodality in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

Based at The Sultry Siren, a Sodality-controlled festhall in the city's Emerald Ward circa 1370 DR, Ralahn covertly used the establishment for trading goods on the black market and fencing stolen jewelry. He also allowed pashas and sultans a discreet escape from their enemies, or just embarrassment, via the secret passages in the cellars, so they owed Ralahn and the Sodality a favor for their lives or their reputations.

History

In the , the Trades Conflagration blazed across northern and central Calimport. The Sultry Siren was damaged, but calling in some favors, Ralahn had it repaired in less than a month.
