Fieschi was an upstanding Netherese fighter who helped guard the Netheril Mint in Eileanar until its destruction in .

Activities

Fieschi worked under Everdingen alongside Szematseen, with the pair serving as Everdingen's lieutenants. Together they commanded the Mint's guards and maintained extremely tight security, with rigorous devotion to the law.
