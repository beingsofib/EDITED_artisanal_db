Nylerian was a fang dragon that laired in the Spiderhaunt Wood during the late 14 century DR.

History

Around 1374 DR, the time of the Elven Crusade, Nylerian set up a new lair in a cave network in the Spiderhaunt Wood, dominating a group of ettercaps and demanding meals from them. Rather than feed their own to their new master, the ettercaps began actively raiding the nearby areas for captives instead. They caught a number of orcs and elves, many of whom were traveling the roads nearby due to the reclamation of Myth Drannor. Sergeant Thiralen Farstep of the Cormanthor Elves noticed the missing elves, and sent a team to investigate the Spiderhaunt Wood, where they discovered the caves. After fighting through the ettercaps and other creatures, the lazy fang dragon was eventually found and slain.
