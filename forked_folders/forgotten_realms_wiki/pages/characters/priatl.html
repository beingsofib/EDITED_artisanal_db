Priatl was one of the three leaders of the Azatl settlement in Nexal in 1362 DR.

Activities

Priatl ruled over the territory alongside the human quarter and the human market. In his territory's restricted areas, humans must be killed on sign.

Personality

Priatl was a kind of religious fanatic. He believed that they lived in a new world where the Viperhands would rule over humans. He believed Jeordros a fool and Michitl too weak, so in his mind he was the best candidate to be leader of all Azatl.
