Curnetheres was a mage and member of the Wraith of the Inner Sea privateer fleet in the mid–14 century DR. He was outlawed from Luthcheq in Chessenta because of the city's persecution of mages, and joined the company in order to have revenge. Circa 1357 DR, Curnetheres was the highest-ranked mage on the Sea Horse.
