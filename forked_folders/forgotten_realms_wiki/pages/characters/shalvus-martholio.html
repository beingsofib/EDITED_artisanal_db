Shalvus Martholio was was a Zhentarim spy inside Goldenfields sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR posing as a shepherd Shalvus infiltrated Goldenfields. His mission was to find the weakness of the place and reported to Nalaskur Thaelond of Bargewright Inn. He was at the livestock fields when giants attacked the city.

Personality

Shalvus wanted rise the Zhent ranks quickly and hoped that Goldenfield was the right place to show his qualities. He accepted any risks to fed his ambition. However Shalvus truly loved animals.

Behind the scenes

Shalvus was one of the special NPCs that could be played by the players during the campaign's arc in their city.
