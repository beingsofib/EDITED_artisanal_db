Traegar Vadamore was a human Cormyrean Wizard of War during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Traegar had light skin and short reddish hair.

Personality

Traegar was a skilled strategist and planner and came off as rather grand and verbose, even compared to the other War Wizards.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Traegar was deployed to Eveningstar to aid the war effort there. He focused on a way to prevent House Dun'Robar from staying in the war, as they provided the bulk of the soldiers and weapons used by drow armies. To this end, he sent in a team armed with rust monster ichor to infiltrate with illusions and sabotage their weapon stockpiles. Along the way, the team also faced and defeated High Battlemaster Teblyn Dun'Robar, whose favorite weapons had mysteriously fallen apart. This two-part defeat was enough to make them the laughing stock of the Underdark, and they immediately cast their suspicions at their so-called allies Szind and Avithoul.
