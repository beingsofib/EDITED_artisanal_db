Chelnadatilar was a gold dragon and one of the Princes of Murghôm in 1479 DR.

History

Chelnadatilar was one of the princes of Murghôm who participated in Brimstone's xorvintaal.

He was one of the dragons Alasklerbanbastos sought out to attack Luthcheq and destroy the Brotherhood of the Griffon after the dracolich and Brimstone discovered Aoth Fezim attempts to disrupt the xorvintaal.

During the Battle of Luthcheq, Chelnadatilar was freed of the compulsion of the xorvintaal by the dragonborn paladin Medrash, and he turned against Alasklerbanbastos and Tchazzar. In the battle, he killed the black dragon Ferzath.

He later went to Dracowyr alongside the Brotherhood of the Griffon, the dragonborn Medrash and Balasar, and the surviving dragons of the Battle of Luthcheq. Together, they put an end to Brimstone's xorvintaal.
