Komatsu was a yakuza in the town of Aru in Wa.

History

Komatsu was once a yakuza in a large city but became a freelance thief. In , he was in Aru doing dirty jobs for the local yakuza gang. He stayed at The Apple Tree Inn and might've been confronted by adventurers there.
