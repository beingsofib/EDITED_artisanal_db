Kaerasz was a dairy farmer and a former Elder of the village of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the mid–14 century DR.

Personality

He was known for his ill temper and arrogance.

Activities

Kaerasz had once been elected Elder of the village's governing Council, but he made decisions arbitrarily and turned out to be too arrogant for the other councilors' liking, so they voted him. As of 1357 DR, Kaerasz still lived in the village and had few friends.
