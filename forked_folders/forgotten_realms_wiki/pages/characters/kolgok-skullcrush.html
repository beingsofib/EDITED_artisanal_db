Kolgok Skullcrush was the leader of the ogre gang that terrorized the Wild Hills of the Delimbiyr Vale circa 1372 DR.

History

At some point before 1372 DR, Guruk Bonesnap inherited the title and the "crown" from Kolgok Skullcrush, after, ironically, crushing the old chieftain's skull. Under Guruk's leadership and aided by a cunning oni mage, the ogre tribe pushed a gnoll community from the old toll house in the Wild Hills and tried to get rich, collecting 3000 gp per traveler and cooking those who could not pay.
