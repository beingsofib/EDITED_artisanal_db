Asklannan

nameAsklannan

Asklannan was the owner of a spell library that was consulted in the early-to-mid 14 century DR.

Possessions

Of note, Asklannan possessed a library of spellbooks. At least two members of the Zhentarim had accessed his spell library by the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the , the Zhentarim mage Ghaubhan Szaurr put in motion a plan to join his blood with that of Shandril Shessair in an attempt to gain her spellfire powers, an act strongly implied to be carnal in nature. He was stopped by Manshoon, who slew him and his forces before they could move against Shandril; Manshoon explained he'd found Asklannan's book, strongly implying Asklannan had written the book himself.
