Mristin was a member of the Cult of the Dragon in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He served as a guard at the Cult's Moortown outpost.

Possessions

Mristin wore a whistle around his neck that he could use to summon all of the Cult forces stationed in Moortown, including Cultists, dragons, and trolls. He wielded a longsword and a composite shortbow, and had a dagger and twenty arrows on-hand. He kept all of his money with him in a pouch.

History

When two of the Cult's red dragon allies—Wyndfire and Snizzler—hatched a clutch of three wyrmlings early in the , it fell to Mristin to watch over the hatchlings. His duty was to keep them safe by ensuring that they remained in their nursery in the ruined temple of Tempus and to use his whistle to summon backup if they were ever threatened. The baby dragons were instructed by their parents to obey him, but would wander off if they caught an unusual scent. Even so, Mristin was able to shoo them back into their nursery.
