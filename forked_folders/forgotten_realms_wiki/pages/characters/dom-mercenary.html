Dom was a proficient and fearsome mercenary who found work in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Dom appeared a dashing and handsome man with neat dark hair and moustache, wearing a waistcoat.

Personality

Dom was a charming and pleasant man, but could flip to violence and cruelty as needed. He was a master manipulator of people, using these talents on friends and enemies alike, and kept his own personal goals and values secret and hidden. Though very cunning and intelligent, his erratic behaviour led to the idea that he was mad in some way—perhaps explaining his fondness for drinking wine from the skulls of his enemies.

Abilities

Dom possess magical powers, possibly of divine origin. He could call forth flame strike, searing light, poison, and could teleport Slam about tactically in combat.

Relationships

Dom kept a close circle of elite mercenaries by his side, each with ties to him that ideally ensured their loyalty and effectiveness. He maintained a friendship with the earth elemental Slam by speaking Terran, manipulated Rand with promises of finding his nephew Stiev, led the childlike Song along with a lifelong friendship, sheltered the drow renegades Udere and Pelloth, freed Thoven from slavery, and had mysterious ties to "Pep-Pep" Dolthin. His sole miscalculation was trusting in his influence over the bard Nil, who posed as a fool easily led by strong personalities, only to be secretly spying on him and assisting the Harpers.

History

Following the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, the forces of Returned Netheril pursued Oriphaun Huntsilver as he absconded from Wheloon Prison with the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll. As he approached the Storm Horns via Eveningstar, Oriphaun noticed the Shadovar pursuit and tore the Scroll into pieces, hiding them along the High Road in order to confuse the pursuit and buy time for the Harpers. The Netherese drew up a significant force of hired mercenaries to control the road, going largely uncontested as the bulk of Purple Dragons and War Wizards in the area were devoted to fighting against the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar. Dom and his group were assigned to assisting the Netherese in the King's Forest, where one such piece had been detected.

Joined by Netherese wizard supervisors and many more hired sellswords, Dom and his team aimed to intercept the Harper Bensen Virandson on Greenweald Lane, and so set up several blockades on the road and spread out into the surrounding woodlands. Bensen and his escorts managed to fight their way through regardless, slaying most of his forces, with Rand surrendering out of disloyalty and Nil revealed to be a double agent who had reported to the Harpers. With his allies gone, Dom and Slam beat the Harpers to their destination—the hidden Harper Lodge—and awaited in the basement for the Scroll to be delivered to him. Despite the best efforts of man and elemental, his last gambit was unsuccessful and he perished fighting there.
