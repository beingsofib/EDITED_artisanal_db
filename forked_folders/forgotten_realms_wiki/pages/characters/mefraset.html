Mefraset was a wizard member of the cult of Set in Mulhorand in 1357 DR.

History

In 1357 DR, Hodkamset ordered Mefraset and some of her fellows to infiltrate a city in west Faerûn. However, there Mefraset happened to put a ring of reverse alignment on her finger, becoming good. She believed herself to be an enemy of the cult of Set gathering information. However, her fellow cultists understood the situation and decided to kidnap her in order to heal her.

Personality

Mefraset was a loyal agent of Hodkamset and knew much about his plots to conquer Mulhorand.
