Dornar Steelsar was a man of Silverymoon who married into the Amnian Parhek dynasty in the late 13 century DR.

Relationships

Dornar married Princess Rasa Parhek and they had four children–Choris, Rosa, Rohav, and Rhothar.

History

He met and made a family with Rasa while she was hiding in Silverymoon, but they were found by assassins from Amn in the . Dornar and their son Choris died to allow his wife and children to escape, though Rosa was killed and Rohav was captured and manipulated by the hostile agents.
