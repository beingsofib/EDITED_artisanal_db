Thabbys was a necromancer and one of seven High Hands in the Risen Cult of Bane in the mid-14 century DR.

Personality

Thabbys took great delight in researching all manner of necromantic magic.

Abilities

Thabbys was to possess a wide variety of necromantic powers.

Possessions

Thabbys was known to be served by a number of bonebats, which she used largely as messengers.

Relationships

Unlike some members, Thabbys's dedication to the Risen Cult of Bane was rooted solely in their ability to provide her the necessary material components and opportunities to further her goals of becoming more adept in necromancy.
