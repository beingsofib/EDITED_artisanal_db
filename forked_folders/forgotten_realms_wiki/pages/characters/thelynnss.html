Thelynn'ss was a drow captain, who acted under the orders of the Bhaalspawn, Sendai.

Possessions

The drow captain fought with enchanted scimitars and wore an amulet of cheetah speed, as well carrying a healing potion.

History

Thelynn'ss was appointed by Sendai to guard her compound. She was found in the barracks of Sendai's enclave, and commanded several drow fighters. The captain may have been killed by Gorion's Ward's party, upon intruding into the barracks. She was also in possession of the drow guard's key, which opened many areas of the complex.
