Aoln was an adventurer who lived in the Sword Coast during the mid−14 century DR.

Possessions

Aoln wore a leather armor and was armed with a longbow, as well as a short sword.

Relationships

For some time Aoln traveled with an adventuring party, until they were all killed in a battle with an ogre mage.

History

As of the , Aoln could be found traveling alone on the Sword Coast, along the Coast Way road.
