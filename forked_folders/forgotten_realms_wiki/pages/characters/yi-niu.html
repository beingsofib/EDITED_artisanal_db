Yi Niu was a ghostly lieutenant who served Nan Kung Chi in the Crypt of Durkon in Kara-Tur in the 14 century DR.

Possessions

Even in her undead form, Yi Niu wore a simple helmet and a set of enchanted +1 splint mail armor. She carried a magic +1 composite longbow and kept arrows of biting, acid arrows, and mundane arrows in her quiver. In close combat, Yi Niu wielded a +1 magical bastard sword. She wore a ring of duplication on a finger and had a potion of superior healing, a potion of extra healing, and a potion of invulnerability ready.

History

Yi Niu died many moons before the late 14 century DR. In life, she was a ranger and one of six trusted lieutenants who served Nan Kung Chi. She continued to do the same long after death. In the , the crypt faced an illithid incursion led by Goxxa the alhoon. The mind flayers consumed ancient preserved minds of the crypt's dead and sought the Claw of the Black Leopard. At the same time, a vampire thief named Hexxat arrived at the tomb on the behest of her master Larloch, who sought the tomb's magical treasure. A ghostly monk named Ki Chin Sang stood guard of Nan Kung Chi's tomb and demanded anyone seeking entrance to defeat six of Nan Kung Chi's trusted undead lieutenants who all were interred together. When Hexxat and her allies challenged the lieutenants to battle, Yi Niu was joined by Weimin Min, Borok-Tosst, Ming-hua Dai, and Jiang Dai, and, presumably, was defeated.
