Baerin was an adventuring and arrogant fighter from the city of Waterdeep in the mid−14 century DR.

Abilities

Baerin was trained in fighting with spears and preferred to shower his poorness with arrows from afar.

Possessions

The adventurer wore a suit of splint mail and armed with a longbow and a spear. He used enchanted +1 arrows in combat.

History

In the , Baerin's adventuring party, led by Kirian visited the area east of Beregost known as Mutamin's Garden. There, Baerin, Kirian, Lindin, and Peter antagonized travelers, fellow adventurers, and "rustic locals," provoking them into combat.
