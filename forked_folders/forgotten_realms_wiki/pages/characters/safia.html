Safia, also called the Light-Fingered, was a tasked artist genie living among the Jann of the Haunted Lands in the Burning Lands of interior Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Description

Safia was quite beautiful. She stood nearly tall.

Activities

Safia never ceased singing and playing her lute in the presence of the sheik's guests.

Relationships

She was the favorite concubine of Sheikh Ardasir al-Darah of the jann.
