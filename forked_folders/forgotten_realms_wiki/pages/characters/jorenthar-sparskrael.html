Jorenthar Sparskrael was a drider from House Sparskrael that worked near Sschindylryn in the 1480s DR.

Abilities

Among his talents, Jorenthar knew the spells black dragon bolt and acid rain.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar Jorenthar traveled near the city accompanied by two animated armor bodyguards, looking for rare ingredients to use in his experiments. In particular, he took an obsession with the red dragon Micahrastir—or rather, the dragon scales upon the wyrm.
