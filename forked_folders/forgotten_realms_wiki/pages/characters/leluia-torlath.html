Leluia Torlath was a royal daughter of the Amnian Torlath dynasty in the 7 century.

Relationships

Leluia was the only daughter of King Brinnaq I, with at least half a dozen brothers including Esmel IV and Jhanos I. She was married to Arbas, to her family's misfortune.

History

Leluia's marriage to Arbas allowed him access to the royal court, and on the death of King Brinnaq I he proceeded to murder every male family member within reach. Her brother Esmel IV was poisoned shortly after becoming king, followed by his newborn son Esmar. Having cleared the field, Arbas claimed the throne for himself, only to be slain within the year by her vengeful brother Jhanos I.
