Adrian Freeman was a Netherese fighter and arcanist who led the Neth Underground for Freedom Fighters, and owned the Mythomian Warehouse in Eileanar in Netheril until its destruction in .

Activities

Adrian operated the cell of Neth rebels in the enclave, seeking out opportunities to weaken the hold of the archwizards on Netherese society. One of his grandest plans involved potentially assassinating the mighty Karsus himself.
