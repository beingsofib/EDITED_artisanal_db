Ulthas Orgolath, who was also called Ulthas the Darkwing, was the first Nentyarch of Narfell at the beginning of the Orgolath Dynasty in .

Activities

Ulthas and his twin brother Ulthar eradicated the Crell Dynasty when they slew Nentyarch Belevan the Unliving. Ulthas assumed the title over his brother and held it until his death in when he fell in battle while leading an invasion of Unther and Mulhorand.

Relationships

Ulthas' mother, Ayanna Orgolath, gave herself willingly to the demon lord Graz'zt. Ulthas' and his brother's half-fiend blood was a result of this union.
