Nuth Harandal was the leader of the Storm Rune, a guild of merchants and landlords in Eskorn, Laerakond, as of 1479 DR.

Personality

Nuth was considered a wily and calculating person.

Background

Nuth was a poisoner and a merchant who used the Storm Rune to increase his own status and wealth in Eskornar society.
