Nigel Thunderstorm was a dwarf chef of Citadel Adbar in the late 15th century DR.

History

Nigel was the personal chef of King Harbromm.

During the late stages of the War of the Silver Marches in 1485 DR, he was surprised when he discovered that the dwarves' strategy was to counterattack using the closed tunnels under the dwarven strongholds.
