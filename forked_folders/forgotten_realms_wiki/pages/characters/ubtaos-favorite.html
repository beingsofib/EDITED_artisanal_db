Ubtao's Favorite was a young triceratops who competed in the dinosaur races of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 century DR.

History

As of the start of the death curse, Ubtao's Favorite was on a winning streak, and bookies were giving it 5:1 odds of winning. and may have competed in the final leg of that event in which racers rode triceratopses.
