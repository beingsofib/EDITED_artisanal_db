Freda Gambosi was a human performer and Harper agent who worked in Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Freda had shoulder-length brown hair and wore a long gray dress.

History

After the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon around 1485 DR, Freda joined Rina Belostos's Harper team in Eveningstar as they tracked Oriphaun Huntsilver and the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll. While acting as a troupe of musical entertainers (Freda played the drum), she located a piece at the ruins of Beckside Castle, which was used as an overnight camping spot. The agents she sent there arrived on the heels of Keldan Shadeskein, Gaston Wyrst, and their mercenary followers, who had slipped past the Weathered Dawn into the ruins. After a battle through the ruined halls of the castle, the Scroll piece was finally found and recovered.
