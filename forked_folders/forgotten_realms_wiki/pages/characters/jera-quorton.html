Jera Quorton was a human alchemist who operated between Eberron and Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Jera had thick brown hair tied back stylishly, and had bright blue eyes. She wore a full-length protective black and purple robe.

History

Before the schemes involving the Lords of Dust and the Spinner of Shadows, Jera worked as an alchemy trainer at the House Deneith enclave in Stormreach, on the world of Eberron. Shortly afterwards during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar she was seen providing her expertise in the village of Eveningstar, a world away on Toril.
