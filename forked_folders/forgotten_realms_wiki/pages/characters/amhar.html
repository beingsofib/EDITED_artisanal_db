Amhar was a dwarf who served as a royal guardsmen of Tethyr in the late 15 century DR. He was dismissed when he was convicted as perpetrator of the Children's Massacre in 1469 DR, called the Scourge of Tethyr and the Killer of Children and hated throughout all of Tethyr. In his middle years, he traveled with Harp, captain of the ship Crane, around 1479 DR.
