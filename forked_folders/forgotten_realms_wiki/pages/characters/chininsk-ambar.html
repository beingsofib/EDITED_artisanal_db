Chininsk Ambar was a cleric who stayed in Phoenix to the last. He owned a house full of follies, and was served by El-Tarifa.

Overview

Chininsk Ambar may have been amongst the priests that cursed Krinos Pandipolous. However, he stayed to the last in the city of Phoenix.

His house included a number of follies, such as a counter-intuitive door, a fake wall on his library, a fake deathtrap, and a ballroom that forced people traversing it to dance in order to safely cross. He stayed in it to the end, accompanied by his majordomo, El-Tarifa: an aerial servant of exceptional loyalty.
