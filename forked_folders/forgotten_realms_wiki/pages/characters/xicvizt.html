Xicvizt, or Rogue Thought, was a rogue mind flayer from Ryxyg.

History

During the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Xicvizt sensed a strange presence in the Underdark. Investigating, he found the demon lord Graz'zt.

The mind flayer Glorghakt, however, hired an Order of the Gauntlet–affiliated adventuring party to deal with Xicvizt. The adventurers found and killed him.

Personality

Xicvzt fully devoted himself to Graz'zt.

Relationship

Xicvizt and Glorghakt were born from the same pool.
