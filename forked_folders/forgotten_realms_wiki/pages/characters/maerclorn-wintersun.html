Maerclorn Wintersun was the Wintersun heir in the late 15 century DR.

Activities

He frequented The Bold Blazon.

Relationships

Maerclorn was a rival of Arclath Delcastle, and the son of the elder Lord Wintersun.

History

In late Mirtul of , Arclath Delcastle was a rival of Merclorn, who was a regular at The Bold Blazon.
