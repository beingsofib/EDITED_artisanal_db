Handful was Dragonbait's and Copperbloom's the oldest hatchling. He was a finhead saurial and was born in the mid-14 century DR in the Lost Vale of the Dalelands.

Description

Growing up, Handful was, as his given name implied, quite a handful. He was a lot like his father, the heroic paladin Dragonbait. When meeting strangers, Handful was formal and polite.

History

Handful was born after Dragonbait rescued his tribe from under Moander's clutches in the and was already a growing child by the . Tarkhaldale was often visited by Finder Wyvernspur, the savior of the saurials, in the guise of an aged priest Jedidiah. Copperbloom treated him with respect, but Handful had a polite but snarky attitude towards Finder, just like his father, Dragonbait did.
