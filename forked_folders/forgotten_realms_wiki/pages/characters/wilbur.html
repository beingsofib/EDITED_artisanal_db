Wilbur was a Griefguard in the House of Grief temple that hid the Cloister of Sombre Embrace in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Wilbur was a human man with a fair skin tone brown hair, short beard, and blue eyes. He wore a leather armor set.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Wilbur served the Mother Superior as one of her Griefguard inside the House of Grief. Wilbur, along with the entirety of the cloister may have confronted its member Shadowheart were she to turn away from Shar.
