Margrueth was an old Harper sorcerer in Twilight Hall.

History

In 1358 DR, Margrueth was among the Harpers sent by representatives of Berdusk to help fight the Zhents in Archendale. It was here where she met the famed Knights of Myth Drannor.

After the main battle against the main Zhent army in flame rule 17 at Mistledale's borders Margueth helped by Florin Falconhand sacrificed her life essence to resurrect Nelyssa Shendean, captain of the Riders of Mistledale.

Relationship

Margrueth was an old acquaintance of Anastra Syluné Silverhand.
