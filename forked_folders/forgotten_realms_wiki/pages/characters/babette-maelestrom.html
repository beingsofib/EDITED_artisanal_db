Babette Maelestrom

Person
nameBabette Maelestrom
sexFemale
occupationAdventurer

Babette Maelestrom was a female adventurer and scavenger active some time before 1368 DR. She was best known for creating the Eyes of Truth helmet to help her in excavating dark dungeons. She attributed all of the wealth she had gathered to its creation, but her keen eyes were as much responsible.
