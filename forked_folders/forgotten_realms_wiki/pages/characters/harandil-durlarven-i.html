Harandil Durlaven I was the first King of the Durlarven Dynasty to rule over Impiltur.

Activities

Harandil defeated a massive horde of orcs from Vastar in the , at the Battle of Bloody Reeds, earning him a favorable reputation that would eventually lead to his rise to the title of king by acclamation. He reigned until his death in the .

Relationships

He married the daughter of King Sharaun Mirandor, Princess Tayissa. Harandil's son and heir was Harandil II.
