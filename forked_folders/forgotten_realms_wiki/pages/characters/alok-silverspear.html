Alok Silverspear of House Silverspear was a commander in the Elven Court in 1357 DR.

History

Alok remained behind in Faerûn with her troops to protect Luvon Greencloak.

Personality

Alok in her heart truly wanted to go to Evermeet and forget the world but was loyal to her duty.
