Indrila Demaz was the Queen of Velprin and leader of the human army that invaded the Yuirwood in response to attacks from the half-elves living there. She became queen in 1058 DR. A year later, in 1059 DR, she drove the half-elves further back into the forest. Eventually, in 1065 DR, Indrila was defeated by Brindor Olósynne at the Battle of Ingdal's Arm. This triggered the surrender of the humans and the formation of Aglarond.
