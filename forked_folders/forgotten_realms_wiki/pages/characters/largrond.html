Largrond of the Lash was a Red Wizard of Thay in the mid-14 century DR.

Personality

Though still overconfident and reckless, he was willing to take a few moments to discuss strategy before engaging in tough fights.

Abilities

He was known to be able to cast ironguard and antimagic field.

Possessions

He wore numerous rings.

Relationships

At the time of his death, he was a guest in a Sharran temple alongside Ylondan. He was a Red Wizard.

History

In , the Simbul assaulted a temple of Shar somewhere in Thay where the Red Wizard Azmyrandyr was preparing for an intrigue. Her goal was a revenge attack after Azmyrandyr participated in an attack on Silverymoon. After slaying Azmyrandyr and his apprentices, she continued to slay around the temple; the temple master, Maeldur, called on one of his underlings to rouse two visitors, those being Ylondan and Largrond.

The two quickly joined the fight after casting ironguard, and briefly discussed their strategy, right in front of her. Largrond cast an antimagic field, while Ylondan cast a spell to shatter the ceiling on top of her. They were aiming at a projected image of hers instead of the real her, who cast a spell to portal the rubble on top of them, killing them instantly.
