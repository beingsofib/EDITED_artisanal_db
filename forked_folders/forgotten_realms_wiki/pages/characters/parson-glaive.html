Parson Glaive was a dwarf priest of Moradin of Citadel Felbarr in the mid-to-late 15 century DR.

History

Parson was appointed as Steward-in-Waiting of Citadel Felbarr in the .

He was a decisive person during the War of the Silver Marches. In the , Parson confirmed to King Emerus Warcrown that young Reginald Roundshield was in fact the legendary Bruenor Battlehammer reborn.

He was made King Parson Glaive of Citadel Felbarr in the when King Emerus Warcrown abdicated his throne and title to join Bruenor Battlehammer on the march to reclaim Gauntlgrym.
