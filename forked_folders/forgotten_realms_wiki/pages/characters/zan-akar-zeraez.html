Zan-akar Zeraez was a stormsoul genasi who served as the ambassador of Akanûl in the late 1470s DR.

Description

Zan-akar had long and slender silver spikes projecting from his scalp, and his skin color was like the duskiest grapes. The pattern of argent lines etched into his face was so intricate that it looked like it was a mask.

History

In 1479 DR, Zan-akar was the Akanûlan ambassador in Chessenta. when a group of abishai disguised as dragonborn began to kill Chessentan citizens and allied themselves with enemies of Chessenta, Zan-akar was able to convince Shala Karanok to banish the Tymantheran ambassador from the country.
