Jesthren Darakh was the first Nentyarch of the Darakh Dynasty to rule Narfell.

Activities

In , Jesthren slew the previous Nentyarch, Orlathaun of the Orgolath Dynasty, and claimed the Crown of Narfell for himself, establishing the ruling line what would reign until Narfell's destruction during the Great Conflagration in . Jesthren ruled for many years until .

Relationships

Jesthren was the half-fiend son of the human woman Larnaeril and the demon lord Orcus.
