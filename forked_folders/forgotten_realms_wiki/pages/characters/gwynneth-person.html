Gwynneth, known as the First Mother, was a leader of the first Talfir people to settle in the Moonshae Isles. The southern island on which they made their home was named in her honor.

Relationships

She was the second daughter of the Archduke of Ebenfar, Markus Randol.

Her consort was Taylor, and with him she had a son, Deric.

History

In the , she led Talfiric refugees fleeing persecution by the Shadowking of Ebenfar. They travelled across the Sea of Swords to the Moonshae Isles, where they established the first human settlement there.

She died the next year of consumption.
