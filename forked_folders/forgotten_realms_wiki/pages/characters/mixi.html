Mixi () was a half-elf waitress in Mulmaster in 1491 DR.

Personality

Mixi was usually very gentle and professional. She cared deeply for her friends.

History

Mixi was a pirate on the Moonsea until a storm destroyed her ship. She decided to change her life and went to Mulmaster, where she began working as a maid. At the bar called the Black Blade and Bloody Boar, she befriended Ilva, a refugee from Phlan. Mixi became very worried about Ilva's strange behavior.

Activities

In 1491 DR, Mixi worked at the bar called the Black Blade and Bloody Boar in a lower part of the city.
