Hylgaun was a forester active near the Thunder Peaks in the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the , Hylgaun shared with Raunel that he had seen Shandril Shessair along the road. This was shared the next evening with Lendys, who in turn reported within a day to the High Imperceptor of Bane.
