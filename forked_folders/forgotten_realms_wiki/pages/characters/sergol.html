Sergol was a priest of Tyr who served under Reverend Judge Oleff Uskar of the Hall of Justice in the late 14 century DR. Despite him being a priest, Sergol's magic was not of a healing nature.

Description

Sergol was an aged human man of musular physicue and a light skin tone. He had a bushy blonde beard and a full head of blonde hair. He wore a typical purple chainmails of the Tyrran faith of Neverwinter.

History

In the , Sergol was instructed by the Lady Hero of Neverwinter, Aribeth de Tylmarande, to assist the person who would become the Hero of Neverwinter during their quest to stop the Wailing plague that was consuming the city. Sergol's divine magics were empowerinf the Stone of Recall that would allow the Hero and their companions to be teleported back to the Hall of Justice and back at will during the quest. As the true cause of the Wailing Death were revealed, Sergol accompanied Lady Aribeth and the Hero across the Sword Coast North and aided them during the Luskan-Neverwinter war that erupted shortly after.
