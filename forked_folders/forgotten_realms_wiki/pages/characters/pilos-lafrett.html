Pilos Lafrett was a fisher and inventor who worked in the docks of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , Pilos invented a device that used pulleys and balanced weights that would do the fishing for him, while Pilos could nap. He informed his boss, Tohil, of the invention and that it would be producing labor-free halibut, but asked him not to touch the invention, while cockily also asking for a raise.
