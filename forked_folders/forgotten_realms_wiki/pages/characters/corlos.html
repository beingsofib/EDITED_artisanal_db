Fist Corlos was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Corlos was a gold dwarf woman with a clean-shaven face. She had dark skin and dark brown eyes. She wore a set of chainmail armor as a part of her Flaming Fist uniform decorated with a tabard that bore the company's banner, and was armed with a halberd.

History

In the , Fist Corlos was stationed within the barracks of the Heapside district of Baldur's Gate, where she, a steel watcher, and several other Flaming Fists guarded a small dungeon prison.
