Ruelrond Honadarr was the Master Shipwright and builder of Honadarr's Unsinkable Hulks from the city of Hlath in the Vilhon Reach, active in the late 15 century DR.

History

Ruelrond was a famed shipbuilder among sailors for creating large wide-hulled vessels, resistant to capsizing. Honadarr's Unsinkable Hulks were treated with fire-resistant substances and were virtually impossible to sink. Ruelrond Honadarr's critics claimed that the Hulks "sailed sideways" and had "all the grace of a bloated dead ox carcass."
