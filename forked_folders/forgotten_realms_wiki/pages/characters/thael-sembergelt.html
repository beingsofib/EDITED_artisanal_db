Thael Sembergelt was a retired Sembian battle commander in the Dalelands in the mid–14 century DR.

History

Thael retired to the Dalelands and settled in the Low Rythryn Towers with his family and guests.

In the , Storm Silverhand arrived at his house following her escape from the malaugrym. But the malaugrym Ahorga killed and replaced Thael in order to deceive Storm, but she unveiled the shapeshifter and killed him, avenging the death of Thael.
