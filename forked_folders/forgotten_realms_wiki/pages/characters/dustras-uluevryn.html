Dustras Uluevryn was a member the upper class of Suldolphor in the far eastern reaches of Calimshan in south Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Dustras was tall and thin. He often sneered at others. He viewed those with inherited money with disdain.

Activities

He was a wildly successful merchant of self-made means. Dustras established a network of spies within Suldolphor to keep in well-informed of the current events within the city.
