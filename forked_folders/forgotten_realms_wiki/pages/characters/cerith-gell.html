Cerith Gell was a human inhabitant of Eveningstar during the late 15 century DR.

Description

She had light brown hair parted into two small braids, brown eyes, and a light skin tone. She wore a short brown dress, white bodice, and leather corset with her leggings and boots.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, Cerith could be seen outside the Lonesome Tankard, reflecting on the recent horrors she had seen.
