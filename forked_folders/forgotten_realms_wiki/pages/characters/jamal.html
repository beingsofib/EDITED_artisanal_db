Jamal the Thespian was a bard, as well as a member of the Harpers, who lived in Westgate circa the late 14 century DR. She was a champion of the poor and downtrodden. She lived on South Ssemm Spur.

Relationships

Jamal was a strong ally of Lady Thistle Thavalar in her battle for more democracy in Westgate.

History

Jamal was once an adventurer and joined the Harpers because of Finder Wyvernspur before he became a deity. She retired from adventuring sometime before the , when she grew too old for the lifestyle.

Later, she directed a group of streets actors who performed around Westgate for poor people.
