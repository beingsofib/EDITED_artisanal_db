Zarrick the Sorcerer was an evil magic-user member of the Assassins Guild of the Galenas in 1357 DR.

History

Zarrick originally served in Vaasa's army. After the war's end, he started wandering until he was recruited by the Grandfather of Assassins.

In 1357 DR, he was a key member of the punitive army that went to attack Bloodstone Village.

Personality

Zarrick was specialized in battle-magic. He was a boisterous and outgoing individual. He was fast in making friends but also in losing them. Zarrick was greedy and selfish despite his manners and most people quickly abandoned him.
