Allafel was an elf during the late 15th century DR.

History

Allafel was the younger brother of Tarathiel. During the War of the Silver Marches of 1484 DR–1485 DR. He called loudly for elf intervention in the war. After the start of the elves' operations, he led a contingent of Moonwood troops .
