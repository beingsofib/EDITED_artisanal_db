Hull was one of the four Watchers of Candlekeep, as of the .

Possessions

Along with the other Watchers, Hull was armed with an enchanted rod and was given magical rings to aid in his duties. He often carried antidote potions to ward off hangovers.

Relationships

Hull was an acquaintance of Abdel Adrian.
