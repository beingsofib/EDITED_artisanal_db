Welch was a mercenary that fought as a member of the Company of Dawn for a time during the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the , the Company of Dawn took on the task of assisting friends of their leader Thurbrand across a stretch of Cormyr, into the Spiderhaunt Wood and into Shadowdale. The journey was disastrous and nearly the entire company was killed by giant spiders. Welch was killed fighting alongside his fellow mercenary Zelanz, when an enormous red-bodied spider leapt from the trees on top of them.
