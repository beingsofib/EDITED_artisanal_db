Whaul Nightly was a half-orc who worked as a rat-catcher in the city of Baldur's Gate during the late 15 century DR. They were a regular patron of the Elfsong Tavern.

Whaul was rather good-natured individual who had a booming laugh. In contrast to their cheerful disposition, they had a rather strong jaw.
