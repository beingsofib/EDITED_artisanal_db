Zarn was an human cleric and a student of the Neverwinter Academy in Neverwinter during the late 14 century DR.

Relationships

During the graduation ceremonies in 1372 DR, Zarn was chatting with a fellow student named Naiad.

Like almost all the other students of the Neverwinter Academy, Zarn was fond of Lady Aribeth. In her, he found an inspiring figure as she also was a follower of Tyr, and he hoped to receive her blessing.

History

In the , Zarn finished his studies and was one of the eight students, among whom was the future Hero of Neverwinter, present at the graduation ceremony that year. During the ceremony led by Lady Aribeth, the Academy was attacked. He fought against the mysterious mages in theGraduation Chamber. It is not known if Zarn survived the attack.
