Sir Yax of Scornubel was an agent and a True Soul member of the Cult of the Absolute in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , the Cult of the Absolute was attempting to co-opt orcs of the Trielta Hills into its army. The first envoy, Agent Mhoss, was slaughtered on sight as the orcs of the Ringfork Stronghold thought negotiations to be a sign of weakness. Following that failure, General Ketheric Thorm reassessed their approach to dealing with the orcs and Lord Heradd. Sir Yax of Scornubel was decided to be the next envoy as soon as he returned from the Wood of Sharp Teeth. Yax's strategy was to arrive accompanied by two ogres and issue a personal challenge to Lord Heradd for the killing of Agent Mhoss. Yax then would have to defeat the orcish Lord, force him to swear allegiance to the Absolute, and in exchange, the cult would offer Lord Heradd an alliance in conquering the other two orcish communities of the Trielta Hills.
