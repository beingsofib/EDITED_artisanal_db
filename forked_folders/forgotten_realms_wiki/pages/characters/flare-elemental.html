Flare (elemental)

Person
nameFlare
homeTethyamar, Buried Realms
raceMagma para-elemental
alignment3eTrue neutral

Flare was a magma brute that resided in depths of Tethyamar the late 15 century DR.

History

The elemental roamed about in the magma flows near the city while the Argith drow and Hal Stoutheart's expedition fought each other nearby.
