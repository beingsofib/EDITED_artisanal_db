Jir Winmyr was a wizard of the Twelve in the world of Eberron.

Description

Jir was an elven man with lank reddish hair.

History

During the Darkening of Faerûn, Jir came to investigate the Spinner's Prison and Rift Between Worlds after the Spinner of Shadows escaped, working alongside Ygrid Fastheart and Raf Unbridge. Jir focused on the strange webbing bridge that led into the Demonweb Pits, implying the existence of a very large spider in there.
