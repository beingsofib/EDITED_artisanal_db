Rary was a human wizard from Oerth, mostly known to the people of Toril for his spell creations like his telepathic bond or mnemonic enhancer.

Description

Rary looked like an older man with white hair and beard.

Possessions

As a powerful wizard he owned a pair of very powerful bracers of defense, and a powerful ring of protection. He wore a robe of stars, a ring of animal control, and a ring of mind shielding. He also owned a gem of seeing, a helm of brilliance, and a wand of paralyzation.

History

Rary was once part of Mordenkainen's Circle of Ten, but broke with the circle by killing fellow members Bigby, Otiluke, and Tenser thus earning him the moniker of "the Traitor".

In his time, Rary invented many spells including:

  • Rary's mnemonic enhancer
  • Rary's spell enhancer
  • Rary's superior spell enhancer
  • Rary's telepathic bond
  • Rary's urgent utterance
