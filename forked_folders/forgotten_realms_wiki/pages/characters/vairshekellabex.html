Vairshekellabex was a gray dragon who lived on an earthmote above Old Man's Head mountain in the Akanapeaks. He was allied with Tchazzar and with the help of Tiamat's clergy, he made it appear that dragonborn were attacking villages and caravans in Akanûl. This was part of Tchazzar's plot to convince Akanûl to join him in an invasion of Tymanther. Aoth Fezim led an expedition composed of members of the Brotherhood of the Griffon and genasi to the Akanapeaks where they attacked and killed Vairshekellabex.
