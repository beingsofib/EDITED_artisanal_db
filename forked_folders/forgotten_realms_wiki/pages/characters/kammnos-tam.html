Kammnos Tam was a Red Wizard of Thay during the 14 century DR. He was a member of the influential Tam family of Thay, responsible for overseeing the zombies that transported goods along the long portage in the tharch of Surthay.

History

At some point around the year 1368 DR, Tam met with Master Trevin of the Whiteglyph trading coster and enlightened them to the Red Wizards' unique method for conveying goods and products to the top of the Thayan Plateau.
