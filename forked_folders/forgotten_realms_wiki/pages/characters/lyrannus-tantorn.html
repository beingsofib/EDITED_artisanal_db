Lord Lyrannus Tantorn was a noble of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR. He was the head of House Tantorn.

Personality

Lord Tantorn was a man of sophistication.

Description

He was efette.

Relationships

He was the head of House Tantorn. Shortly before the Council of the Dragon, he became controlled by Manshoon.

History

In the lead-up to the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the , Lyrannus Tantorn was mind-controlled by the evil vampire archmage Manshoon. He was later slain during the event.
