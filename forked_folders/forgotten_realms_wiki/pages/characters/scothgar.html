Scothgar was a Northlander human man and a prince of Rogarsheim on the island of Norland in the Moonshae Isles in the 7 century DR.

History

In the , he was due to marry the Ffolk Princess Bridget of Callidyrr, but she and her whole royal entourage vanished on the voyage to Norland. Ffolk and Northmen each blamed the other for the disappearance, which worsened relations between them.
