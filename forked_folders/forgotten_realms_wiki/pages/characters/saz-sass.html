Saz-sass was a yuan-ti pureblood priest from the the Underdark who worked as a mercenary in the late 15 century DR.

Abilities

Saz-sass could call upon divine punishment, obscuring mist, searing light sound burst, and symbol of stunning.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, Saz-sass and a number of other yuan-ti were hired by Sschindylryn to poison the water supply of Eveningstar. Along with Fless-onas and Gresl-glis, they secured a section of underground caverns that fed the groundwater and guarded the green dragon Tharmalos. Saz-sass was responsible for maintaining the last magical barrier before Tharmalos, and was slain while defending it.
