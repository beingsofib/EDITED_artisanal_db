Jeldeth Khondar was a launderer and illusionist inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Jeldeth was the guildmaster for the Launderers' Guild circa 1357 DR. He held the title of "Master Launderer" within the guild and worked out of the House of Cleanliness–their headquarters on Slipstone Street in the Trades Ward.
