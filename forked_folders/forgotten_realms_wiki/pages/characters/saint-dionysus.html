St. Dionysus, or St. Dionysius, was a saint of the god Ilmater and patron saint of wine and other alcoholic spirits.

Bases of Operations

The Order of St. Dionysus was based around St. Dionysus. The Church of Dionysus, a center of the Order and devoted to the saint, began construction in the city of Goliad in Damara in the Year of the Serpent, 1359 DR. As a result, St. Dionysus was honored in Damara.
