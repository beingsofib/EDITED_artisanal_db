May T'ang Lien was a hengeyokai wu jen adventurer in Shou Lung.

Personality

Lien's goal was knowledge, she wanted to know all. This for Lien would be also a demonstration of the equality of men and women, a idea she firmly believed. However, Lien posed as an poor and innocent female to trick her enemies.

History

May T'ang Lien born among the mountain of Arakin province. She first studied wu jen arts alone in Mount Dowmai but in time Lien understood the need of good teachers. She studied with many experience wu jen and for a time she was even student of the ancient and powerful Nan Wu of the Tsui Tong.

In 1358 DR, Lien decided to attent the Komite.
