Unthril Zond was an illusionist and physician inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Unthril was the guildmaster for the Guild of Apothecaries and Physicians during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He held the title of "Master Physic" within the guild and worked out of the House of Healing–their headquarters along the High Road in the North Ward.
