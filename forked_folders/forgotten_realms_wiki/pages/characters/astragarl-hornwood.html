Astragarl Hornwood was a renowned Mage of Elembar, who lived in the 2 century DR.

History

Astragarl hailed from the city of Elembar that was destroyed in the . The city was part of a human kingdom of Delimbiyran, located on the Sword Coast. Astragarl Hornwood became known as the Mage of Elembar by the . As a wizard, Astragarl Hornwood tutored at least one apprentice in 112 DR, to whom he imparted knowledge of dragons. Notably, Astragarl agreed that the great wyrms were indeed magnificent creatures, as long as one observed them on a tapestry or from the great distance of several kingdoms away.
