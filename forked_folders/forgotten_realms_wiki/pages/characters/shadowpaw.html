Shadowpaw was a powerful umbral worg that served the forces of Risen Netheril in the late 15 century DR.

Description

The creature resembled a worg wreathed in shadows.

History

Shadowpaw accompanied Shadovar forces when they secured the Blooded Wood and Granwell Keep and hunted for the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll in the region.
