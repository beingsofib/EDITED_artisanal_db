Thendric was a male human fighter and a temple guard at the new Temple of Mystra in Wheloon, Cormyr.

Activities

Thendric stood guard in the outer courtyard of the temple, and had a bunk in the guard post outside.

In early Eleint of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR, Thendric was questioned by adventurers investigating the temple.

Equipment

The temple guards wielded longbow and longsword, and wore scale mail armor.
