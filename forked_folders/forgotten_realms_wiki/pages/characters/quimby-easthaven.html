Quimby was the owner of the Snowdrift Inn in Easthaven within the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale.

Description

He was a large man who wore a brown outfit.

History

In Eleint, 1281 DR, Quimby provided room and board for the elven adventurer Erevain Blacksheaf, before Hrothgar lead his expedition to Kuldahar.
