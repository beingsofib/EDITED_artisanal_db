Thurduil

Thurduil was a mage instructor in the late 8 century DR.

Relationships

Thurduil had been the first magic instructor of Alustriel.

History

Between and , Thurduil had been Alustriel's instructor for eight years, hired by her uncle, Thamator. Her tutoring proceeded under a different wizard later, Gaerd.
