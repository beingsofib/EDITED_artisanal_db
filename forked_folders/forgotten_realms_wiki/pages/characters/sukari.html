Sukari was a human warrior from the lands of Kara-Tur in the latter part of the 14 century DR.

Description

He was an instructor of younger warriors, expecting and following a strict code of honor in every part of life. He was seen as the best instructor in the arts of fighting in the city he resided in.

Personality

Sukari's dedication to the art of fighting meant that he forswore ever marrying. He focused only on becoming ever greater at combat and training the next generation of students.

Possessions

He wielded an enchanted longsword +3/+4 vs reptiles as his personal weapon and wore leather armor +2. Always around his neck hung an amulet versus undead.
