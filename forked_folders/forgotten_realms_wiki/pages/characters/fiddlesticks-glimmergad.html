Fiddlesticks Glimmergad was a gnome musician and a thief of some infamy from the city of Selgaunt, who lived sometime before the late 15 century DR.

History

Fiddlesticks Glimmergad was a talented bard who used his instrument – Glimmergad's Selgaunt fiddle, to play tear-jerking ballads for bank guards, making them weep so much they never noticed that the gnome's associated were emptying the vault. Little was known of the end of Fiddlesticks's illicit carrier, however, by the , Glimmergad's Selgaunt fiddle was owned by Thomas C. Quirkilious, the dragonborn proprietor of the Chromatic Scale musical instrument shop located on the second floor of The Glitter Gala gem and jewelry vendor of the Lower City.
