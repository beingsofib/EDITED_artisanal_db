Baerdra Monthor was a noblewoman in the late 14 century DR.

History

Baerdra Monthor, a native Marsember inhabitant, was one of the guests of Lady Joysil Ambrur in the mid-to-late spring of . On the day in which Lady Joysil Ambrur departed Marsember to go after Vangerdahast, Baerdra Monthor made no end of venomous comments regarding the Obarskyr family, openly claiming to delight in their misfortune. She impugned Tanalasta Obarskyr's flirtation with converting to the faith of Chauntea, her romance with the traitor Rowen Cormaeril, challenged the parentage of their child, Azoun Obarskyr V, and questioned the honor of the regent Alusair Obarskyr.
