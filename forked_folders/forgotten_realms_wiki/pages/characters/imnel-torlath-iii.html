King Imnel Torlath III "Longbeard" was an Amnian monarch of the Torlath dynasty, known as the third Wizard-King of Amn.

Relationships

Imnel was the first son of of King Brinnaq II, and his own second son was Jhanos II.

History

In 760 DR, following the destruction of the Ring of Eyes by Tashara of the Seven Skulls, Amn expanded into the former territory of Cortryn and achieved the eastern borders that would last for centuries to come.
