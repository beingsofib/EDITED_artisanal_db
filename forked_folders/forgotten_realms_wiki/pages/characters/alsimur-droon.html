Alsimur Droon was a half-orc smuggler and merchant who operated in Urmlaspyr, Sembia, and the Eastern Heartlands in the late 15 century DR. He was a key figure in the Swift Thrust trade route between Urmlaspyr and Westgate, and often orchestrated shortages to increase the value of his wares.

Personality

Alsimur was well-known for his smiling demeanor and charming personality. He was conniving and under-handed, and sought the easiest routes to make the most money.

Activities

In his younger years, Alsimur was the leader of the Swarmers gang of street urchins in Urmlaspyr. He acted as a liaison between the urchins and wealthy traders. Sometimes he hired the Swarmers out as spies and guards, and sometimes he used them as as part of a protection racket.

As the Arranger of Shortages, Alsimur sent his agents throughout the Eastern Heartlands to surreptitiously create artificial scarcity through economic means, social trickery, and vandalism. He would then make a personal appearance to sell his wares exactly when they were needed the most. For wagons, he sold wheels, axles, drover chains, feed for oxen and draft horses, timber, and roof tiles. On the Sword Coast, he created shortages to sell cured pork. In Impiltur, he sold chains, cables, and wiring. In settlements around the Moonsea, he sold textiles. He hired adventurers to bolster his forces and protect his agents.

When in Urmlaspyr, Alsimur frequented disreputable eateries like Fishkillet, Silmur's Haven, and Mindle's Pies.

Relationships

Alsimur had many connections with merchants in Urmlaspyr and beyond. As the leader of the Swarmers, he had a large gang of urchins under his control. When he became the Arranger of Shortages, he put his follower Blarm Faronder in charge of all his Urmlaspyr businesses. His agents included the merchants Hrar Daekyn, Ongoleir Klassam, Marlar Tethnose, and Ulnar Keltur. When Ulnar Keltur was killed, Alsimur replaced him with Jharandarra Relhand and Scaulbrelle Arnketrel, both of whom he had a casual romantic relationship with.

In Urmlaspyr, Alsimur had no shortage of enemies, many of which sought to kill him.

History

Alsimur was born to the merchants Sonshra Longclaw and Ulivar Droon. As a child, he spent much of his time at Urmlaspyr's docks, learning from sailors and thieving. He quickly rose to lead the Swarmers, a small gang of dock urchins. Under Alsimur's command, the Swarmers defeated the Ironside Claw, Kalasper's Hands, and the Slow Smile in a gang war from which they emerged victorious. Having eliminated his competition, Alsimur rose to power as a "Thrust Lord" who controlled the trade between Urmlaspyr and Westgate.

Alsimur survived an assassination attempt, when a rival poisoned nearly all the food and drink at the Fishskillet eatery. More than sixty people died in the disaster, but Alsimur somehow escaped death.

Alsimur met Hrar Daekyn, Ongoleir Klassam, Marlar Tethnose, and Ulnar Keltur when he found them on death's door in one of his warehouses. He supported them back to full health, and with them invented his scheme to orchestrate shortages around Faerûn. They became his subordinates, and he left Urmlaspyr seeking greater profits. He left Blarm Faronder in control of his operations in Urmlaspyr, and Blarm eventually retired and passed the job onto the violent Tharn Olnar. Shortly after the group commenced operations, Ulnar Keltur was murdered in Scornubel. Following that, Alsimur added Jharandarra Relhand and Scaulbrelle Arnketrel to his scheme.

Rumors & Legends

Only a few traders had taken notice of Alsimur's scheme behind the shortages. These included Osilant Lharnree, Ilbert Nornust, and Presper Aulimont. Alsimur had some of these people assassinated to cover his tracks. The wise Elminster was well-aware of the full situation.
