Randal Sweetgrove was the leader of the temple of Amaunator in Westgate in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , he heard rumors about a young prophet of Lathander who had just arrived in the city. Randal instructed his men to bring the boy to him. However, when he met with Stedd Whitehorn, the young Chosen of Lathander, Randal did not believe the boy's claims that, since the world was in need of hope and new beginnings, Amaunator had changed his incarnation and had once again assumed the role of Lathander. He ordered that Stedd be imprisoned as a heretic. However, Stedd's companion, the reaver Anton Marivaldi, recruited Dalabrac Bramblefoot and the Fire Knives to break into the temple of Amaunator to free Stedd. Lathander's prophet escaped and, despite some devas, sent by Randal to pursue him, Stedd and his companions managed to escape the city.
