Juniper Churlgo was an adventuring wizardess, cook, and a cookbook writer, active in the late 14 and late 15 centuries DR.

Description

Juniper was an inquisitive, eccentric, and youthful, in appearance, hin mage with cherubic features and a head of wild red curls.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Juniper authored a cookbook titled Juniper's Companion to Venturesome Cookery. The tome received a pleasantly positive review from Elminster Aumar himself, who, also, penned a foreword for Juniper's work, calling it "the best cook's guide to Realms cuisine penned yet!" The Old Mage of Shadowdale offered his own suggestions, compliments, changes, and improvemets to some of the dishes presented by Juniper, such as Sembite bread pudding, dwarvish leek bread, potato leek soup, Tantran baeranth, black adder stew, lambtails, and hangman's noose pasties from Halruaa.
