Passage Onward

namePassage Onward

deityNephthys

The Passage Onward was a ceremony observed by the Church of Nephthys.

Activities

Preparation

Prior to ceremony, the clergy of Nephthys prepared and protected tombs that would house the fallen. Defensive wards were set up, the bodies were transported to the burial chambers, and the crypts were sealed.

Event

The ceremony consisted of casting several spells, such as sanctify crypt, enduring ward, and major curse. The noble, free people would be granted great places to rest and several protective wards, while slaves and commoners would be granted simple graves or group tombs. Despite one's station in life, each of the resting places would be protected with at least a single ward.
