The Warrior's Plague was a devastating disease that struck the ancient Calimshan Empire circa .

Acquisition

The disease was spread through contact with the blood of infected individuals.

Effects

The Warrior's Plague caused the infected to go mad with rage and attack others wildly, leading to widespread death.

History

The plague was centered on Almraiven and Calimport, although it mostly afflicted members of the military. Among the armed forces, casualties were higher than 40%, while among civilian populations such as in Calimport, casualties were around 14%.
