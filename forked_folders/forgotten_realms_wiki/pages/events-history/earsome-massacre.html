The Earsome Massacre was a fierce battle during the end times of Netheril that saw the orcs of the Hidden Forest overrun and destroy the Netherese city of Earsome.

Events

Started by the Kraal Brook Massacre in , the orcs of the Hidden Forest gradually overtook the defenses of Earsome, including destroying its mythallar and almost taking the city in before being driven off by the enclave of Delia. The orcs returned in great strength in the spring of , and over two months of intense combat they gradually conquered the city and burned it to the ground.
