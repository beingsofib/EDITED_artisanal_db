Mumps

Disease
nameMumps
typeNatural disease

Mumps was a mundane infectious disease, found in the Shining South's hin nation of Luiren.

Treatment

Halflings of Luiren learned a special skill of medicinal cooking that involved making curative foods using Luiren spring cheese. Apprentice cooks knew how to prepare meals that could cure many normal diseases, including mumps.
