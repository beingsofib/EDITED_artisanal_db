Brotherhood of Earth was a a religious ceremony undergone by all earthwalkers, the specialty priests of Grumbar, that celebrated their ability to summon an earth elemental upon reaching a certain level of skill.

Description

This ceremony was always preceded by a full day of the earthwalker fasting and praying. Afterwards, they would summon an earth elemental and bestow upon them gifts in the form of gemstones and gold. These gifts, along with the good wishes of his faithful, were said to be carried away by the earth elemental back to the Great Mountain, the divine realm of Grumbar.
