The Battle of Rocnest was the first major battle in the siege of Evereska by the phaerimm in the . Despite assistance by Khelben Arunsun and a contingent of Waterdhavian and elf soldiers, Aubric Nihmedu, the Blade-Major of Evereska, died during the battle and Rocnest was captured by the phaerimm.
