Five Fevers

The Five Fevers were a group of virulent diseases commonly referenced together by the peoples of Faerûn.

The Five Fevers included:

  • Blacklung fever;
  • Blacktongue;
  • Marsh fever, also called sallar;
  • Shaking fever; and
  • Winterchill fever
