The Binding was a sacred ritual performed daily by the faithful of Oghma, the Lord of Knowledge.

Description

Oghma's devout follwers were required to perform this ritual every morning. It involved writing the symbols of Oghma while uttering a silent prayer of loyalty and praise. The symbols were written in the dirt, in ashes on a stone altar, or simply in the worshiper's mind if they were shackled or otherwise unable to physically write.
