The Day of Freedom was an annual celebration observed in parts of Faerûn, held on Midwinter to mark the death of Amahl Shoon VII, the Shoonach Conflagration, and the destruction of the Shoon Imperium. It was commemorated by the peoples who had once been subjugated by the empire, which met its definitive end on Midwinter of the .
