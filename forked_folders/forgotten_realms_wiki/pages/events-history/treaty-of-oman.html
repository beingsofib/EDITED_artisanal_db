The Treaty of Oman was an agreement of peace between the Northlanders and Ffolk of the Moonshae Isles.

Location

The treaty was signed on Oman's Isle.

Outcome

As a result of the treaty, the Northlanders and Ffolk of the Moonshae Isles joined under the banner of House Kendrick, uniting all of the Moonshae Isles together.
