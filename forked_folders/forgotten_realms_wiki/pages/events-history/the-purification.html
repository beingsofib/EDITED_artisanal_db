The Purification

nameThe Purification

The Purification was a religious event observed by worshipers of Helm, He of the Unsleeping Eyes.

Description

Duing the Purification, individuals who were returning to Helm's embrace, or those atoning for a lapse in vigilance, renewed their faith to the God of Guardians.
