Victory Day was an important holiday in the history of Zhentil Keep.

History

Following the Fall of Zhentil Keep in the , the city continued to be under siege for many months. Finally, on Kythorn 13 in the , the last of the humanoid forces were driven from the city by the Zhentilar. Full reconstruction of the city began in earnest after this event.
