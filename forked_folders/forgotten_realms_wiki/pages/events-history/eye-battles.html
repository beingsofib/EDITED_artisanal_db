The Eye Battles were a long-running series of skirmishes, invasions, and rebellions fought between the beholder tyrants of the Alimir Mountains and their subjects in the Lake of Steam region and the Shoon Imperium in the 5 century DR.

Location

The Eye Battles broadly encompassed all the settlements on the Lake of Steam, which fell under the dominion of beholders originating from the Alimir Hive. It also reached into the area around the Almraiven Peninsula in the later years.

History

During the disastrous reign of Qysara Shaani (367–427 DR), the Shoon Imperium began to fragment and fall to pieces as centralized power declined. In the midst of this, from 409 DR the beholders of the Alimir Mountains once again descended upon the Lake of Steam to conquer the cities there, facing little threat from the Shoon Imperium. This only changed with Shaani's death in 427 DR, when her grandson Amahl yn Shaan yn Shoon raised a fleet and army in the Lake of Steam and set about conquering his way to the royal throne. Along the way, he performed a circuit of the Lake of Steam's ports, liberating them from the gaze of the beholders and their minions as he made his way to Calimport that year. The state of war extended to the south around Almraiven and continued until 430 DR, when the last of the beholders' influence on the surface was eliminated.
