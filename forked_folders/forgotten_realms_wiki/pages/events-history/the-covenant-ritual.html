The Covenant was a sacred ritual performed daily by the faithful of Oghma, the Lord of Knowledge.

Description

This ritual was performed every evening by Oghma's faithful. It involved a brief service where a passage from a work of knowledge was read aloud or recited from memory. A song or poem was then performed for Oghma, followed by mentioning aloud an item of knowledge learned that day.
