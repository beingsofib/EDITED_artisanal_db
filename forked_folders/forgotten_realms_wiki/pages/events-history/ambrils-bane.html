Ambril's Bane was a small battle between a handful of humans and dwarves in the 55 century before Dale Reckoning that caused the Shanatar Wars.

History

Around the year , a group of five Coramshite friends went tomb robbing on the north face of Mount Kellarak, adventurers and treasure hunters led by the heir of the bakkal of Coramshan. They found the burial site of a dwarven merchant's wife and sought to make off with three golden urns and a hammer, only to be caught by four dwarven miners who had no tolerance for their misdeeds. While the tomb was defended, the death of the heir caused the end of the good relations between Coramshan and Shanatar, leading to the millenia-long Shanatar Wars that ultimately destroyed the dwarven civilization and gave rise to the Calimshan Empire.
