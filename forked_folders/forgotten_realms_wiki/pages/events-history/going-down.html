The Going Down was a sacred event observed by worshipers of Lathander, the Morninglord.

History

The Going Down was a solemn ceremony performed under candlelight during a funeral. The ceremony was followed by a wake that lasted until the dawn prayers. The Going Down was never performed on someone who was supposed to be raised from the dead.
