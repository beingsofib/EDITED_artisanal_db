Zenith Aspirant

Event
nameZenith Aspirant
typeRitual
deityHorus-Re
frequencyDaily

The Zenith Aspirant was a ritual observed by worshipers of Horus-Re, the Lord of the Sun within the Mulhorandi pantheon.

Description

This ritual occurred every day at midday. Priests of Horus-Re gave descriptive sermons about their god, often attempting to outdo rival factions by drawing larger crowds. The Zenith Aspirant was one of three daily rituals performed by priests of Horus-Re and was considered to be the most important.
