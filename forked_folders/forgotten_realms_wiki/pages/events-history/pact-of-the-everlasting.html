The Pact of the Everlasting was a sacred unholy ceremony performed by the clergy of Velsharoon.

Description

Priests of Velsharoon underwent the Pact upon attaining a certain rank within the clergy. The Pact involved the casting of Velsharoon's death pact combined with sacrificing sentient creatures and other unholy incantations.

History

Faithful followers believed the Pact of the Everlasting was merely the first of seven rituals provided by the Patron of Evil Liches in order to attain immortality.
