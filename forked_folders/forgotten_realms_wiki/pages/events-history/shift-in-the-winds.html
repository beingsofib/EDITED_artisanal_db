Shift in the Winds

Event
nameShift in the Winds
typeRitual
deityIsis

The Shift in the Winds was a ritual observed by worshipers of Isis, the Lady of All Love within the Mulhorandi pantheon.

Description

The Shift in the Winds ritual involved priestesses of Isis offering their own thanks and chant to their goddess any time the weather changed favorably.
