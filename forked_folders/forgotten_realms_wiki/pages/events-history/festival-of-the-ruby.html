The Festival of the Ruby, or the Festival of the Rubies, was an annual festival held in the svirfneblin city of Blingdenstone in the Underdark, and celebrated in the city of Neverwinter on the Sword Coast North as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

The Festival of the Ruby on the surface included hunts for well-hidden and rare raw eggs that were exchanged for deep rubies, and rubies, in turn, could be exchanged for various mechanical dragon eggs; a clockwork replica of Themberchaud, the Wyrmsmith of Gracklstugh; and delicious traditional deep gnome dishes, such as pho, cha lua, and pork buns.

History

In the late 15 century DR, the Festival of the Ruby was brought to Neverwinter with Zinli Gemcutter acting as an envoy from Blingdenstone. She recruited adventurers from the surface to aid the famous Golden Gourmand of Blingdenstone, Saltlix Potstirrer, in gathering ingredients to prepare the ever-popular 3-Gem Omelette, a.k.a. Encrusted Gem Omelette, with three types of rare eggs. The ingredients were to be delivered to the Feasting Halls of Blingdenstone for a great feast.
