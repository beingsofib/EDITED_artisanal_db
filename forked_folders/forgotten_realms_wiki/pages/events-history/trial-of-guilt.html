Trial of Guilt

Event
nameTrial of Guilt
typeContest
locationZhentil Keep, the Moonsea

A trial of guilt was a judicial proceeding that occurred in Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR before that city's destruction.

Activities

Trials of guilt were decreed by Magistrate Osconivon when the accuser and the accused were unable to outbid one another with bribes. The accused was sent to the Arena where they would fight to the death against a force selected by Osconivon. Sometimes the accuser was forced to step into the arena to fight the accused. The bribe of the "winner" of the trial was accepted after the contest and appropriate judgment was pronounced.
