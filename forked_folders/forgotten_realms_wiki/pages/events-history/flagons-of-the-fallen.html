The Flagons of the Fallen was an important religious event celebrated by followers of Myrkul, the Lord of Bones.

History

The Flagons of the Fallen served as the end point for the Day the Dead are Most With Us observance by Myrkulytes. Occurring at the end of the day on the Feast of the Moon, the ceremony consisted of Myrkulytes using spells to enflame glasses of wine so that any spirits present would be briefly warmed before returning to the chill of the underworld.
