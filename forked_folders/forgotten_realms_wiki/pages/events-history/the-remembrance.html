The Remembrance

Event
nameThe Remembrance
typeCeremony
deityKelemvor

The Remembrance was a religious event observed by worshipers of Kelemvor, the Lord of the Dead.

Activities

The Remembrance was a daily ceremony performed over graves by priests of Kelemvor. The rite involved songs of prayer and was usually attended by relatives of the deceased.
