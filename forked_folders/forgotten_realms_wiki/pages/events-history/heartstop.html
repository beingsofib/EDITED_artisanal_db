Heartstop

Disease
nameHeartstop
typeNatural disease
symptomsHeart failure

Heartstop was a condition that caused the victim's heart to suddenly cease to beat, causing death.

Treatment

Danica Maupoissant devised a technique for restarting the heart of another—by —by adapting internal techniques developed by Grandmaster Penpahg D'Ahn of Tabot for restarting one's own heart after physical suspension.

History

When the tyrannical King Yarin Frostmantle of Damara was assassinated in the late 15 century DR, his advisors decided to cover it up by claiming he had died of heartstop.
