Genies' Day was a celebration that occurred in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in Zakhara.

Description

This annual event, which occurred on the 12 of Nau, was a day devoted to honoring and thanking the various genie races and individual genies for their protection and services.

History

Genies' Day begun the year following , when all genie races rose together to protect golden Huzuz from an invasion attempt.
