Festival of Remembering

Event
nameFestival of Remembering
typeFestival
observedbyDwarves

The Festival of Remembering was a solemn dwarven holy day dedicated to remembrance of the dead.

During the ceremony, loved ones toasted the dead with a glass of ale flavored with honey and bitters to symbolize sweetness and sorrow.
