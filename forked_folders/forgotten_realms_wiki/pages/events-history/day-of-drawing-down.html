The Day of Drawing Down was a solemn ceremony practiced annually by members of the Church of Geb on the last day of Nightal.

Description

This ceremony was meant to mark the deaths of all those who died within mountains or underground over the past months of that year.

Activities

Each year to mark the occasion, at least one abandoned mine was collapsed.
