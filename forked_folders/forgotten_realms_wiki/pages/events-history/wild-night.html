Wild Night

Event
nameWild Night
typeCelebration
deityAzuth

The Wild Night was an occasional celebration performed by followers of Azuth, the High One.

Activities

During a Wild Night, participants would unleash wild magic and dance in its midst just to feel the raw magical power. A handful of wizards and priests were usually standing by in case anyone was harmed by the unleashed magic.
