Battle of the Thousand Dead

Event
nameBattle of the Thousand Dead
typeConflict
locationBlackmoor, Oerth

The Battle of the Thousand Dead was a violent armed conflict that took place in the Archbarony of Blackmoor on the world of Oerth.

Description

The famous hero Scaldulin of Blackmoor wielded his enchanted sword - the Hoarfrost Blade, against a demon army. With the magics sealed within the blade that made it supremely effective agains outsiders, Scaldulin helped to stop the conflict. Many years later, seers and sages of the world of Toril were aware of Scaldulin's adventuring career and of the battle that raged on a far-off world.
