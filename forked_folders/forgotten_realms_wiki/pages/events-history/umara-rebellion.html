Umara Rebellion

Event
nameUmara Rebellion
typeConflict
locationUmara, the Free Cities of Zakhara

The Umara Rebellion occurred in the Free City of Umara.

Activities

Khan Ubar khel Muhif of the Astok tribe offered his service to the Caliph of Umara once he attained enlightenment. Ubar decided to seize an opportunity when the Caliph's forces were busy battling the forces of Muluk in a separate engagement. Ubar staged a coup within the palace and deposed the former Caliph. He married the Caliph's daughter, Princess Maran to solidify his claim. Forces from Qudra were sent to Umara in response to the rebellion, but order had been restored before they arrived. Qudra accepted Ubar's claim as Caliph in order to prevent an extended siege of Umara.

Outcome

Ubar became Caliph of Umara, bringing with him many barbarians of the Astok tribe. Tension between native Umarans and the newly introduced barbarians was extremely high, brewing for a potential clash.
