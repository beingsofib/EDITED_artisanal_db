Returning Day was a holiday celebrated in the city of Baldur's Gate. It commemorated the return of the famed explorer Balduran to his namesake city, after his first expedition to the continent of Anchorome.

History

On Returning Day in the , Grand Duke Abdel Adrian was assaulted while giving a speech in the Wide by Viekang. By the end of the fight both of the Bhaalspawn were gone and the Slayer, an avatar of Bhaal, wreaked havoc in the streets of Baldur's Gate.
