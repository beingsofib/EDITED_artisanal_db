The Ritual of the Waxing was a ritual of the Church of Selûne.

Description

The Ritual of the Waxing could only be performed by a follower of Selûne on the night after the new moon.

History

In the , the goddess Selûne, the Moonmaiden, may have sent Kyriani Agrivar of the Selûne's Smile of Waterdeep a vision, upon which Kyri would recruit a band of adventurers friendly to the church for a quest. She would bid them take the Eye of Selûne artifact to Elah'zad, an oasis in the Sword of Anauroch called the House of the Moon, and perform the Ritual of the Waxing at the center of the Selûnite temple there. This would open a temporary portal to the Gates of the Moon and call back to Toril the ancient Netherese floating city of Selûnnara, which would wage war against the Shadovar of Thultanthar, the rival floating city that lay nearby. On the way, they would be opposed by Shadover and Sharrans. This battle was part of the eternal conflict between Selûne and Shar.
