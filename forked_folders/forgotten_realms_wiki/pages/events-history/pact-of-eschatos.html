The Pact of Eschatos, also known as the Declaration of Eschatos, was an ancient agreement or accord between the drow of the Underdark and the fomorians of the Feydark.

Description

A core tenet of the Pact involved cyclopes loyal to the fomorians allying with and accompanying drow on small-scale raids and evil schemes, although such cooperation rarely lasted for long. The details of the Pact were otherwise unknown to outsiders, although it was rumored that it was meant to safeguard some kind of hidden lore.

History

Whenever drow and cyclopes were seen working together, rumors about the Pact invariably began to resurface.

In the mid-to-late 15 century DR, Prince Araithe of Sarifal and his drow allies held hostage a fomorian princess named Marabaldia for ten years to force concessions relating to the Pact of Eschatos, One such cyclops was Borgol, who gave his life while helping Marabaldia to escape her captivity in the late 1470s DR.
