Eating disease

Disease
nameEating disease
typeNatural disease
symptomsSerpent-like scales
originChult

The so-called eating disease was a natural affliction that affected those who were born in the jungles of Chult.

Effects

The eating disease was quite rare and its main effect was turning skin on the affected areas to form into scale-like calluses that greatly resembled those of a serpent. The disease often affected the forearms, making it fairly noticeable when exposed.

History

In the late 14 century DR, a dark-skinned Chultan human afflicted with the disease was one of the eclectic and multi-ethnic crew of a pirate vessel, the Black Dragon, helmed by the infamous Orim Redbeard.
