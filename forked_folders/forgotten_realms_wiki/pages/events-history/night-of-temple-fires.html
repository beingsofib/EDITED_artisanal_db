The Night of Temple Fires was a conflict that occurred in Waterdeep in 1345 DR.

Following a period of unrest between followers of the churches of Lathander, Selûne, Shar, and Tempus, one night of violence and looting resulted in the destruction of the Spires of the Morning and the House of Heroes, and the sacking of a subterranean temple of Shar near Shadows Alley.

The Dark Army of the Night tried, but failed to set fire to the House of the Moon.
