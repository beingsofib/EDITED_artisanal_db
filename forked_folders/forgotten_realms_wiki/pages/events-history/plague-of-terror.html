The Plague of Terror was a disease that struck Calimport circa .

Effects

Afflicted individuals were driven mad by hallucinations of their fears and enemies.

History

Mysteriously, the plague impacted Calimport and only Calimport. Parts of the inner city as well as the entire waterfront were destroyed in a fire after a number of infected dockworkers hallucinated an attack by giant sea monsters.
