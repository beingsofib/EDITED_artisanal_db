Cancer

nameCancer
typeNatural disease
symptomsVaried

Cancer was an invariably deadly non-magical disease that was present on Toril.

Treatment

Cancers that were not understood as such were often mistaken for curses, and so were treated with arcane spells. Some especially virulent, supernatural cancers could only be treated with spells such as limited wish and wish.

Acquisition

The Color Spray Nebula in Realmspace cured all other diseases, but had a small yet statistically significant chance of inducing an especially persistent cancer, one immune to all magical cures.

Elven metabolism was less susceptible to cancer, though half-drow were strongly susceptible to a particular form of skin cancer due to sunlight exposure.

History

Essemra Ruldegost died of a sickness that would most likely have had a cancer diagnosis had she been diagnosed elsewhere.

Cancer was a point of comparison for many virulent afflictions, and was named in the tale of the Earthmother in the Moonshaes.
