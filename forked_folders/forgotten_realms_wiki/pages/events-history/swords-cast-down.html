Swords Cast Down was a sacred holy ritual observed by worshipers of Lliira, the Goddess of Joy.

History

All of Lliira's holy ceremonies begun with the ritual of Swords Cast Down. At least two weapons were cast to the ground while participants chanted. The weapons were covered with fresh flowers that were either picked, or conjured with magic by a senior member of the clergy if none were available because of the season or location.
