The Festival of Spirits was a religious festival in the Plain of Horses. It was the most commonly observed among the few formal holidays and festivals held in that land.

Activities

This day saw Plainsmen families gather at a local , a structure built of stone and wood reaching in height. They made offerings of foods such as butter and cheese to the local spirits.
