Couralitai

A couralitai was a scheduled meeting of the khans or princes of the people inhabiting the Hordelands.

History

These meetings occurred every fall at the vale of Ejen Horo, located between the Khopet Dag Mountains and the Mountains of Copper.

The couralitai was traditionally used to arrange marriages, settle feuds, determine blood prices for murders, and even to acknowledge a new khan. A khahan could also be elected during these meetings.
