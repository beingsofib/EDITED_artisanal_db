The Fellowship of Flight was a religious ceremony undergone by all airwalkers, the specialty priests of Akadi, that celebrated their ability to summon an air elemental upon reaching a certain level of skill.

Activities

Specialty priests of Akadi, known as airwalkers, prayed for one solemn hour before performing the ceremony. The air elemental was summoned following the prayer. Upon its arrival, the elemental was wafted with incense, sung to, sprinkled with flowers, and gifted poems of Akadi that it would bring back to the Great Funnel, Akadi's divine realm.
