The Impending Doom was a ceremony observed by worshipers of Hoar, the Doombringer, every 11 of Marpenoth.

Activities

This day-long ceremony involved beating drums, making robust oaths, and performing exhaustive acts of purification. The Impending Doom celebrated several aspects of Hoar including acts of both revenge and justice that had yet to be carried out, and good deeds that honored remembered celebrants.
