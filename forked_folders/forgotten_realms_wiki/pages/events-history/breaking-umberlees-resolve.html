Breaking Umberlee's Resolve

Breaking Umberlee's Resolve, designed by Ashley Warren and Chris Lindsay, is an adventure in the Dreams of the Red Wizards storyline published for the Adventurers League. It is the first part of the Sea of Fallen Stars series.
