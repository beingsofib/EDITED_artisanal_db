Prayer of revenance

Event
namePrayer of Revenance
typeRitual
deityHoar
observedbyDoombringers

The prayer of revenance was a special ritual performed by specialty priests of Hoar called doombringers.

Activities

If present at the death of someone who begged for vengeance against their killer, a doombringer could enact the prayer of revenance ritual so that the victim would rise after their death as a revenant, allowing them to seek out the revenge they craved in life.
