The Kume Sa Yeti was a great hunt by packs of yeti on the realm of Tabot in .

History

That year, yeti descended from the Wu Pi Te Shar Mountains and fell upon Frekang Gompa, and they killed or kidnapped 280 lamas out of the total of 400 at the monastery.

Centuries later, in , some Tabotans feared the coming of another Kume Sa Yeti, when the snow men emerged from Wu Pi Te Shar once again to raid Nafeen Gompa, killing 23 and causing great damage. The authorities in Thok organized a hunting expedition against them.
