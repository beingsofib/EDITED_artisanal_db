The Passing (Kelemvor)

Event
nameThe Passing
typeCeremony
deityKelemvor

The Passing was a religious event observed by worshipers of Kelemvor, the Lord of the Dead.

Activities

Priests of Kelemvor performed this ceremony when someone died, offering last rites for the deceased in the form of a chant so Kelemvor would be aware that their soul was on its way to him.
