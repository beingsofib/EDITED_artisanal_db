Omi War

Event
nameOmi War
typeConflict
locationShou Lung, Kara-Tur

The Omi War was a little-known armed conflict that took place in Shou Lung sometime before the 14 century DR.

History

A legendary ninja Fukio was perhaps the most well-known historical figure from the Omi War. The ninja was a master of disguise and became infamous for a successful assassination of the Emperor of Shou Lung of the time. As of the late 14 century DR, only the enchanted set of clothes – Fukio's Silks reminded people of the Omi War.
