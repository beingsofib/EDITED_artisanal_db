Rage of Demons

The Rage of Demons was a Dungeons & Dragons storyline that began in September 2015 with Out of the Abyss and ran through March 2016.

The storyline was primarily set on the Sword Coast and in the Moonsea region.

The storyline was also described this way:

A key part of the storyline was presented in Archmage: the appearance of demons in Menzoberranzan and elsewhere in the Underdark, which were summoned in 1485 DR and 1486 DR by Matron Mother Quenthel Baenre in order to secure her reign over the city. The presence of the demons was according to the plans of Lolth, who arranged for Gromph Baenre to inadvertently summon Demogorgon to the Prime Material Plane in 1486 DR.
