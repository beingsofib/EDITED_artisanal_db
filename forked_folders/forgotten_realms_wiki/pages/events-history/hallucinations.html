Hallucinations

Disease
nameHallucinations
other namesHallucinatory Insanity
typeMental disorder

Hallucinations were perceived aspects of reality that did not actually exist. These delusions could take any form and have any level of detail, and were able to be induced with illusion magic.

Acquisition

Most illusion spells created false aspects of reality perceived by all, such as hallucinatory forest and hallucinatory terrain. Some insidious spells directly affected the senses of specific people, leaving others unaware of what was happening. These personal magical hallucinations were typically phantasms.

  • Mental prison created a powerful and terrifying internal illusion that could kill the victim with its own perception of danger.
  • Phantasmal force and phantasmal killer created an elaborate and believable internal illusion that the victim could be harmed by.

Effects

When hallucinating, a person was witness to sights, sounds, smells, and tactile sensations that did not align with reality. These could be as simple as ordinary objects, as personal as disembodied voices that gave advice or instructions, or as complex as entire people that could be interacted with. They were especially likely to strike during moments of stress like combat, making it exceptionally dangerous for them.

Notable Affected

  • Hagen Silverthumb dissociated and hallucinated a friend after escaping from Egil Hakon.
