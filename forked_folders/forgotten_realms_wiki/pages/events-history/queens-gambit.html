The Queen's Gambit was a religious festival observed by followers of the Red Knight.

Description

The Queen's Gambit occurred on the first day of Tarsakh. The Red Knight's clergy celebrated with a tournament of chess. Prizes included titles, promotions, and occasionally a gift from the temple armory.
