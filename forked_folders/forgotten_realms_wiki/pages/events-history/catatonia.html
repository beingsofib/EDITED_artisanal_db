Catatonia

Disease
nameCatatonia
typeMental disorder

Catatonia was a condition characterized by a complete withdrawal from reality.

Acquisition

Catatonia could appear as a response to a terrible shock or horror, long-term mistreatment, or a magical mental attack. The mind defended itself from this external peril by closing itself off entirely. This could last only a short time or be practically indefinite.

Symbol of sleep sent its victims into a catatonic sleep, which they could not be woken from.

Effects

A catatonic person was unable to interact with the world, as their mind refused to receive stimuli. They would typically slump to the ground or stand completely still, unable to speak, move, or preserve themselves, even in cases of personal peril and injury. They wouldn't be able to eat or drink on their own, and in some cases couldn't even do that with assistance. They might be able to be led around with guidance and even directed to perform simple tasks, but this would end without direct supervision.

When sufficiently agitated, it was possible for a catatonic person to have a sudden outburst of violence against the source of irritation. This would only last a short time before they fell back into an insensible state.

A catatonic person had such strong unconscious mental defenses that their mind was effectively protected by both mind blank and tower of iron will. This made telepathic communication (or further mental injuries) extremely difficult or impossible.

Notable Affected

  • The father of Catlindra Serpentar became catatonic after seeing his daughter slay a doppelganger in the form of her mother.
  • Droth, a Howling Hatred cultist taken prisoner by the rival Black Earth cult.
  • Imoen went into a state of catatonia after her soul was removed in Spellhold by Jon Irenicus and Bodhi.
  • Keletheryl, an elf trapped in Nalavara's hoard demiplane, became increasingly consumed by guilt and remorse, and regularly fell into a catatonic state as he looked inward.
  • Zan, a child, entered a catatonic state after being magically interrogated by Elden Vargas and the Ashmadai. He was only able to mumble warnings about the cult.
