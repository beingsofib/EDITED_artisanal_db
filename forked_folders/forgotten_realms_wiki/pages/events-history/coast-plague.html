The Coast Plague was a fatal disease that killed thousands of people in Amn during the .

History

The plague spread via caravans. It first appeared in Athkatla, Crimmor, Murann, and Purskul, where it was estimated to have killed 20% of the people it infected. It ultimately spread as far as Beregost in the north and Zazesspur in the south.
