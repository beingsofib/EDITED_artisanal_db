The Long Night was an annual druidic festival celebrated in the Vilhon Reach in early Eleasis.

Description

The festival was a celebration of love that lasted for two consecutive nights across all areas of the Vilhon Reach inhabited by druids. The celebration was used to announce and celebrate betrothals and its ending marked the official beginning of the summer season in the Reach.

History

During the , and the raging Godswar, the deity Silvanus's avatar roamed the lush forests of the Vilhon Reach. He visited the isle of Ilighôn during that year's Long Night and gifted a boon of his blessing onto the island.
