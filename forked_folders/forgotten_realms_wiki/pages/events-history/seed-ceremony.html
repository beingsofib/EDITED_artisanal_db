The Seed Ceremony was a ritual performed by followers of Chauntea to wed themselves, completed by planting a seed together and reciting the proper vows.

Description

As long as one had belief in Chauntea and the barest of materials required to sow a seed, this ceremony could be performed anywhere. History

In the , Tanalasta Obarskyr and Rowen Cormaeril performed the ceremony in secret in order to unite themselves against the wishes of the royal family. During their travels, they prepared a small patch of dirt by hand, using water from their waterskin, and a single columbine seed to perform the ceremony.
