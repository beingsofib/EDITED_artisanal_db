The Dragon Run of the leveled the city of Phlan on the Moonsea.

History

During that year, an invasion of dragons swept through the Moonsea region of north Faerûn. The dragon attack occurred at the same time as a human conflict in the region between Mulmaster and an alliance of other cities. Focused on their own struggles, the other powers in the region were unable to send aid to Phlan.

Phlan was completely destroyed during the attack, forcing the survivors to rebuild anew.
