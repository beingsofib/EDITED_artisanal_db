Prayerbane Plague

namePrayerbane Plague

The Prayerbane Plague was a fatal disease that struck Impiltur in the 4 century DR.

History

In the , the wife and son of King Darthorn Mirandor III of Impiltur died tragically from the Prayerbane Plague. In grief, the king drowned himself that year.
