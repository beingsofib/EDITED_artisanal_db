Rite of Respect

Event
nameRite of Respect
typeRitual
deityTiamat

The Rite of Respect was a religious ritual observed by the clergy of Tiamat, the Dragon Queen within the Untheric pantheon.

Description

This complicated ritual of abasement was performed any time a priest of Tiamat approached one of the Dragon Queen's spawns. The Rite of Respect did not protect the priest from the spawn's wrath, but failure to perform the ritual ensured their death at the hands, or fangs, of the spawn.
