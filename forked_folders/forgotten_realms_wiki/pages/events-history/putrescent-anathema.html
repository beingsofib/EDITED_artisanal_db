Putrescent Anathema

Disease
namePutrescent Anathema
originStump Bog

The Putrescent Anathema was a disease present in Waterdeep in the 14 century DR.

History

In 1395 DR, the disease began to spread from Stump Bog. It hit the temple-farm of Goldenfields very hard, and thousands died due to the lack of grain to fed themselves.
