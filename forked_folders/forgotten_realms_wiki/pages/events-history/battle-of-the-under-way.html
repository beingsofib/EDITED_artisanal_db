The Battle of the Under Way, also known as Comragh na Uamh in Dwarvish, was a battle that took place in the Under Way of Gauntlgrym between the dwarves of Mithral Hall and the forces of the drow House Xorlarrin in the , during the reclamation of Gauntlgrym.

History

During the reclamation of Gauntlgrym, Connerad Brawnanvil, with Bungalow Thump at his side, led the dwarven forces to the Under Way. Their army was aided by five young mages of the famous Harpell family, among them Kenneally, Tuckernuck, and Toliver Harpell.

While exploring the upper complex, they found each of their patrols returning to the same central corridor, and the only way down to the deeper levels was by a hole in the roof of a vast cavern. A thick door in the floor closed this hole, and led to a landing suspended on the cavern ceiling. The sheer drop proved a challenge for the dwarven forces, but by utilizing a magical Field of Feather Falling invented by Tuckernuck Harpell, the dwarven battleragers dropped to the bottom in groups of ten, with the spell catching them in the air and letting them float the last or so to the ground. Both Connerad and Bungalow were among the first group, despite the unknown risk of enemies below.

After more than a hundred dwarves had successfully floated to the floor below, however, they were ambushed by the drow forces led by two wizard commanders, Jaemas and Faelas Xorlarrin. They had had an army of slaves, consisting mostly of goblins, orcs, and kobolds hidden in concealed tunnels disguised behind stone slabs.

Knowing that the large host of dwarven battleragers outmatched their own slave forces, and having watched in secret and observed the Harpells installing the Field of Feather Falling, the two drow mages focused on dismantling the enchantment. They cast magical bolts of lightning at the Harpells, striking Toliver and disabling the Feather Fall, so the next three groups of incoming dwarves, thirty in all, fell to their deaths. Frantically, the flying Harpell mages created a new Field of Feather Falling further along the landing, but it was too high, so the dwarves floated the whole way down, delaying their arrival and making them easy targets.

To the horror of the suddenly outnumbered dwarves at the bottom, a small host of demons then joined the drow army. These included flying chasme and they were led by a four-armed glabrezu demon.

With the mages' fireballs exploding around them, setting both goblins and one unlucky dwarf aflame, Connerad Brawnanvil took on the demons, but was soon torn in two by the glabrezu.
