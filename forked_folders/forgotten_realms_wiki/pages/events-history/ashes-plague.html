The Ashes Plague was a disease outbreak that struck the Shoon Imperium in the .

Effects

Afflicted individuals' skin turned a gray color and they suffered from seizures, blindness, and bleeding. Those who died from the disease died of the bleeding.

History

The plague largely struck Keltar, Manshaka, and Volothamp. As much as 9% of the population in these areas died, although many of them did not succumb to the disease but were slain in a vain attempt to prevent the spread of the plague.

The city of Calimport was spared an outbreak of the plague, although it still lost 0.2% of its population because of citizens contracting the illness while travelling.
