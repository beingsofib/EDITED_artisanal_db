The Girding of the Guard was a religeous ceremony observed by worshipers of Horus-Re, the Lord of the Sun within the Mulhorandi pantheon.

Description

The Girding of the Guard was one of three daily rituals performed by priests of Horus-Re. This ceremony occurred every night and priests of Horus-Re pledge to protect Mulhorand by holding their own private ceremony in which ceremonial weapons were girded.
