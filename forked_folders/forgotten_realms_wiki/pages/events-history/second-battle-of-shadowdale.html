Second battle of Shadowdale

Conflict
nameSecond battle of Shadowdale
date1358 DR
placeShadowdale, Dalelands

The second battle of Shadowdale (often referred to as simply the battle of Shadowdale) was a battle fought in Shadowdale within the Dalelands in 1358 DR, between the defending forces of the Dalelands, and troops from Zhentil Keep, led by Bane.
