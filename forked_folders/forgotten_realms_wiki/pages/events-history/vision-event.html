The Vision was a holy day observed by followers of the Savras, the He of the Third Eye.

Description

The Vision occurred during the Feast of the Moon on the day between Uktar and Nightal. Every devout follower of Savras celebrated by strict meditation for the entire day. The form of meditation varied depending on the location. In some temples meditation was done in a sauna or steam room, while others meditated with hazy incense. All who participated were rewarded with a vision from Savras himself.
