The Festival of the Pride was a day of celebration for the faithful of the interloper deity Nobanion. It was considered a time for frolicking and romance, as the followers of Nobanion believed any children or cubs conceived during the festival would grow to to become the ruler of the local church, or in the case of wemics, their pride.

The festival took place during the first week of Ches. It was followed three and a half months later by the Newborn Celebration, with the time in between representation the gestation period of a newborn lion.
