Dance of the Dolphins

The Dance of the Dolphins was a spectacle that occurred in the Bay of Dancing Dolphins in Dambrath. It was the synchronized movement of thousands of dolphins that gave the appearance of a dance.

History

The dance occurred at dusk when tens of thousands of dolphins would gather and play a complicated game that onlookers interpreted as a dance.

Sages believe that the dolphins might be communicating with the deity of dolphins, or that they are simply performing some kind of unknown ritual.

Rumors and Legends

Many believed that viewing the dance on a nightly basis warded off depression and other sicknesses of the mind.
