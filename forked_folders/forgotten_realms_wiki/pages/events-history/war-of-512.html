The War of 512 was an armed conflict between orcs of the Candlekairn clan of the Orsraun Mountains and the human nation of Turmish in the Vilhon Reach.

Description

The War of 512 took place in the and started with swelling numbers of Orsraun Mountains orcs spilling onto the towns of Turmish. The horde ravaged and leveled the cities of Shaus, Marikor, and Dauntshield, and by the end of summer of 512 DR, the Candlekairn clan horde was routed by armies of Turmish in the ruins of Dauntshield. In the aftermath, only one of the three razed cities was rebuilt – Dauntshield.
