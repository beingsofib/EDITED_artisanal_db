The Temples Plague was a disease that struck Calimshan and Tashalar in the .

Acquisition

The disease was spread via touch with an infected individual, even if they were unaware that they were infected.

Effects

It caused fever and accumulation of fluid in the lungs.

History

The disease was concentrated in Almraiven, Calimport, and the cities of Tashalar. It led to the deaths of about 7% of the population across these regions, although Calimport fared slightly better with a casualty rate of 5%.

The disease was unknowingly spread by priests, which was how it gained its moniker of the "Temples Plague".
