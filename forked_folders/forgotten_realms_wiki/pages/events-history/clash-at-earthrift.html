The Clash at Earthrift was a battle of the Giant Wars that took place at what would be known as the Gorge of the Fallen Idol, fought between the giants of Nedeheim and the allied dwarves of Shanatar and elven kingdoms.

History

In order to mutually guard against their giantish foes, Shanatar allied with the nearby elven realms of Tethir and Darthiir, which also served to guard against the expansionist humans of Coramshan. Around to a great battle took place at Earthrift in High Shanatar, which brought the alliance together. A dwarven stratagem to crush the giants with a landslide succeeded in killing twenty of them, but it also slew a number of elves—among which were the two elven princes Tethir, the sons of the elven general. This disaster prompted the elves to leave the battle mid-way and so the giants won a bloody victory over the dwarves.

The alliance with Tethir was broken immediately, and the elves began a vendetta against the Gemcrypt clan of dwarves, eventually eliminating them from the western Starspire Mountains. The other elven realms also broke their alliances over the next century, leaving Shanatar to fight the giants and humans on their own.
