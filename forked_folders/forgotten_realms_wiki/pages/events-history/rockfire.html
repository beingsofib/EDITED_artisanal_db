The Rockfire cataclysm was an event that destroyed a large portion of the Underdark in the late 10 century DR. It permanently closed the Underdark passages that had formerly linked Maztica and Faerûn under the Trackless Sea and forced dwarf and drow survivors to settle in Maztica.

History

Rockfire

In the legends of the desert dwarves, a large-scale war occurred between dwarves and drow across the Underdark. Somewhere in the Underdark, the drow kept a magical focus called the Darkfyre, into which they fed the bodies of their slain enemies, thus causing it to grow in power. The Darkfyre, however, ended up overwhelming those who fed it and grew by its own will into a force of widespread destruction known as the Rockfire. It burned through and destroyed vast areas of the Underdark, consuming entire cities and nations as it spread. Both sides of the conflict, the dwarves and the drow, were almost completely annihilated by the event.

Similar legends existed among the dwarves of Faerûn who remembered a great war being fought between dwarves and drow beneath the Trackless Sea, with the war ending when the drow caused an enormous fire that burned away the rocks in the seafloor and caused a breach that destroyed entire sections of the Underdark.

In the , a group of drow ambushed a dwarven mining expedition traversing tunnels west beneath the Trackless Sea from Denin's Delve in the Moonshae Isles. As dwarves and drow fought in a subterranean gallery, an eruption of a deep core fissure caused magma to spew upwards, before the ocean poured into the collapsing ceiling, killing many on both sides. Divided and thinking the other side obliterated in the catastrophe, the remaining dwarves and drow each fled westward by different routes through the Underdark, on long and perilous journeys.

A small number of survivors of each race successfully escaped to the surface in Maztica and became surface dwellers there, permanently cut off from their brethren in Faerun and the rest of Toril.

Legacy

The event permanently destroyed the Underdark connections between Maztica and Faerûn, as well as much of the Underdark beneath Maztica. Even the caverns that were not destroyed in the fire were no longer inhabitable, because they were filled with poison gas or molten rock. It also altered the landscape of the House of Tezca above, causing new mountains to be formed and also creating the Sunstone, a mysterious lake of liquid silver that could grant visions.

The dwarves who escaped to the surface ended up living in the deserts of the House of Tezca and the Sands of Itzcala, where they became desert dwarfs, living in isolated settlements, far away from other civilizations.

The drow survivors emerged in the foothills of the Axapoztlan Range They disguised their true identities and presented themselves as supernatural beings of power to the native humans of Maztica. They thus became the Ancient Ones who lived in the Mount Zatal volcano next to Nexal and were honored as chosen servants of the Maztican god Zaltec. They exerted great influence and power over the affairs of Nexal, and took the hearts offered at sacrificial altars to Zaltec by the Nexalans and fed them into the Darkfyre, which they preserved and kept inside Mount Zatal. This also meant that they betrayed Lolth in service to another god, but since the Rockfire had permanently separated them from Faerûn and its Underdark, the drow of Faerûn who served Lolth also had little chance to carry out her vengeance on them.

In the , a pair of runestones containing the journal of Varuna Rockjaw, one of the dwarves who experienced the Rockfire disaster, were unearthed in Itzcala by Cragwarden Samiel Tussin.
