The Treaty of Garumn's Gorge, also known as the Many-Arrows treaty, was a treaty signed to ensure peace between an upcoming orc kingdom of the Silver Marches and others of the region.

History

In 1372 DR, both Bruenor Battlehammer and Obould Many-Arrows signed the treaty. It ended the fierce war between the newly recovered dwarven kingdom and the beginnings of the orc realm known as the Kingdom of Many-Arrows.

As of 1409 DR, the two kingdoms were still at peace, although Bruenor had expressed sincere regret on numerous occasions.
