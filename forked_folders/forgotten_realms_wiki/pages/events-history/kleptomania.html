Kleptomania

Disease
nameKleptomania
typeMental disorder

Kleptomania was a mental condition characterized by an irrational compulsion to steal. Unlike typical greed and envy, kleptomania was separate from a person's actual material desires.

Acquisition

  • Bestow minor curse could inflict a state of kleptomania on the victim.
  • The gem of delusion could inflict a random mental illness upon a victim, including a form of kleptomania.
  • Rappers could instill kleptomania on people with the sound of their rhythmic tapping on tunnel walls.

Effects

A person with kleptomania experienced a recurring urge or compulsion (in some cases utterly overwhelming) to steal, regardless of the material worth or utility. The urge was most often felt regarding small, easily pocketed items, and was accompanied by whatever lies and deflections necessary to get away with it. This urge could be at odds with their own values and beliefs, causing feelings of guilt and grief at their own actions.

Kleptomaniacs were not necessarily any good at stealing, and were quite easy to catch in the act. The condition was actually a major detriment to actual professional thieves, as the spontaneous urges interfered with their judgment and ability to work unnoticed.

Notable Affected

  • Billi Sharpe was cursed by a hag with both kleptomania and targeted amnesia regarding her brother.
  • Oscar was the sheriff of Nagarr, and secretly a kleptomaniac responsible for most of the town's thefts.
  • Zilchyn Q'Leptin was an unfortunate drow with many problems, including a strong case of kleptomania.
  • The kenders of Krynn were suspected by many of having an innate form of kleptomania.
