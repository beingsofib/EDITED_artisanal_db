Shattering (Calimshan)

Event
nameThe Shattering
partofthe Steam Clashes
locationThe Maridlands, Calim Empire

The Shattering was the magical destruction of the Maridlands, Ajhuutal, and Ajhuu himself, culminating the Steam War with Calim and the Calim Empire.

Description

After years of war against his rebellious vassal, in Calim brought together his dao allies and his magical artifacts from the Great Arrival (believed to include the scepter of Calim) and performed a great magical ritual that sundered the city of Ajhuutal and sent the surrounding area into the Shining Sea. Though this killed Ajhuu and ended the rebellion, Calim's fury at the rebellion led him to empower the ritual far beyond its needs, causing the destructive earthquakes to spread far beyond Ajhuutal along the coast and inland. Where before the coastline was a near-straight line from the River of Ice to the Alimir Mountains, the Shattering tore it apart and caused the sea to rush in, transforming half the length of the Maridflow from a pleasant river valley to a cliff-lined bay of jagged rocks, and doing the same further south to the foothills of the Alimir Peaks. It also blasted and reshaped the westwards Quag and Volomir Rivers, turning it from the most fertile floodplain in the Calim Empire into a half-bitten bay and a brackish swamp later known as the Spider Swamp.

Though he had destroyed a significant portion of his own empire in the process, the Shattering was enough for Calim to claim victory over the rebellious vassal Ajhuu, who was presumed dead from the Shattering.
