Bog rot

nameBog Rot
typeSupernatural disease
transmissionInjury
carriersBog hag
incubation1–4 days

Bog rot was a disease inflicted by bog hags on their victims.

Acquisition

The disease was contracted from injuries inflicted by the sharp nails of a bog hag. Symptoms would begin to manifest within four days.

Effects

Bog rot caused a steady deterioration of the sufferer's stamina and fortitude. While this could be fatal, a healthy individual could recover in a matter of days from the onset of symptoms.
