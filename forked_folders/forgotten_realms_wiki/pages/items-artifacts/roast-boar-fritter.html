Roast boar fritters was a dwarven game meat dish.

Description

The fritters were cooked using salt, flour, diced apples and dates, served together with thinly sliced roasted boar. This dish was considered a staple of dwarven cuisine and could be found throughout Faerûn's dwarven communities, where the ingredients were available.
