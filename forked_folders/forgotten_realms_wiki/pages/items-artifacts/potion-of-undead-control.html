A potion of undead control was an magic item that granted the imbiber control over a specific forms of undead.

Powers

When consumed, these potions gave the imbiber the ability to charm specific kinds of undead. The undead remained charmed for 5–20 minutes. Different versions of this potion existed with each targeting a specific undead including ghasts, ghosts, ghouls, shadows, skeletons, spectres, wights, wraiths, vampires, and zombies.

Notable Owners

Pizentios, a vile necromancer from Zakhara, carried a potion of undead control.
