Oil of vitriol

aliasesSulfuric acid
typeLiquid
propertiesCaustic

Oil of vitriol was a strong acid distilled from green vitriol.

Properties

The substance was highly corrosive and dangerous to handle. It had an oily, clear appearance.

Usages

  • The acid was used in all manner of trades, such as the dyeing and preparation of some fabrics, and turning cashmere wool into cashmere yarn.
  • Oil of vitriol was used to dissolve tin for coating steel, making tinplate.
  • It could be distilled with alum and nitre to create the stronger acid aqua fortis.
