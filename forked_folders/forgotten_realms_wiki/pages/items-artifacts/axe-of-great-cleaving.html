Axe of great cleaving

Item
nameAxe of Great Cleaving
typeMagic axe
creatorLaeral Silverhand

The axe of great cleaving was a unique enchanted battleaxe crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Tommus Tallstep in the late 15 century DR. It was one of many pieces of so-called "Silverhand gear" crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep.

Description

Silverhand designed the axe to complement Tommus' gift for strategy and his tactics for battling large groups single-handedly.

Powers

As the name suggested, the axe of great cleaving was capable of unleashing a massive cleave that allowed the diminutive Tommus to strike many foes at once.

Notable Owners

  • Tommus Tallstep, a halfling fighter to whom Silverhand also gifted the Captain's Helmet and the seal of patience.
