Rope of constriction was a type of magical rope.

Description

A rope of constriction resembled a rope of climbing or rope of entanglement.

Powers

Once activated, a rope of constriction became a deadly weapon. It coiled around the neck of the wielder and up to four other individuals within . The rope continued to strangle its victims until a dispel magic spell was cast on it. Those entwined in the rope were unable to cast spells or break free by any means.
