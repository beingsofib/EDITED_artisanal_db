The Warder's Signet was a unique ring of protection that originated from the god Ao himself.

Description

It appeared as an unadorned band made of gold.

Powers

The ring held a powerful +3 enchantment.

History

Legends about the ring said it was created by Ao and gifted to Helm for his dutiful service during the Time of Troubles in the . Helm then bequeathed the ring to his devoted followers in Faerûn.

Rumors & Legends

It was possible to manifest the Warder's signet by use of a deck of many things by drawing the Key card.
