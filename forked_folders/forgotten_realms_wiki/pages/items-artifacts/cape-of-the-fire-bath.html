Cape of the fire bath

Item
nameCape of the Fire Bath
typeCloak
locationDaggerford, Sword Coast

Capes of the fire bath were common enchanted cloaks worn by worshipers of Kossuth.

Description

These capes were dyed in brilliant red color and decorated with shimmering embroidered flames.

Powers

Capes of the fire bath shielded their wearers from extreme heat, magical flames of spells, and even fiery dragon breath. Additionally, these cloaks improved the wearers' reaction and defenses against physical and magical threats.

History

These items were created for the use of influential clerics and worshipers of Kossuth, however, by the late 14 century DR, these flaming capes entered a wide rotation among spellcasters of the Realms. In 1372 DR, these capes could be purchased from the Sorceller's Encapsulate of Daggerford.

Notable Owners

  • Marcos Flass, a disgraced Red Wizard of Thay and the creator of tourmaline golems in the late 14 century DR.
