A Mother's Loathing was a variety of potion formulated by the green hag Ethel from her lair beneath the Putrid Bog.

Description

The stopper of the potion was smeared with a small bit of blood. It looked delicious to whomever held the bottle.

Powers

Whomever drank the potion gained the ability to bite out at others in a manner similar to a vampire spawn. This ability remained with them until the next time they laid down to rest.
