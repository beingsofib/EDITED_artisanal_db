Flamefang, the Blazing Bite was a powerful throwing dagger found in the ruins of Myth Drannor in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

Flamefang was an adamantine blade tied to the power of the dracolich Karliath and its deadly breath. It was vorpal and flaming, and eagerly leapt to taste blood. Fortunately for the thrower, it was also returning.

History

The throwing blade was found in the Myth Drannor Cult of the Dragon base, held by the Red Wizards that sought to enslave the dracolich Karliath. The blade may have then been taken by the Cormanthor Elves when they eliminated the Red Wizards.
