A felucca was a small sailing vessel used mainly in Rashemen. They were also used by fishermen in Amn.

Description

These small ships had two masts, which were lateen style. They were similar to keelboats, but usually measured between to in length.

History

These ships were usually operated by a crew of six and could carry up to of cargo.
