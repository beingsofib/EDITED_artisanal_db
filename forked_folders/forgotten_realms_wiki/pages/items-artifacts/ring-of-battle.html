Ring of battle was a variation of a ring of protection that could be found in Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.

Description

A ring of battle was a band of gold with a simple geometrical design of repeating triangle shapes. The ring had an embedded blue-green gem.

Powers

The wearer of this ring was granted magically enhanced battle instincts, effectively increasing their defenses in combat.

Notable Owners

  • Calliope: a bard adventurer owned this ring during her time working with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
