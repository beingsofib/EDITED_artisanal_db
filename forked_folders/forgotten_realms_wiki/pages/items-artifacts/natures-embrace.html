The Nature's Embrace was an enchanted druidic robe, encountered in Chult in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

It was believed that the Nature's Embrace was blessed by nature itself, granting the wearer cat's grace and armor of a turtle, similar to that of a +4 enchantment.

Notable Owners

Qawasha: a druid of the Emerald Enclave from Chult in the late 15 century DR.
