Thorl beldarakul

Item
nameThorl beldarakul
typeAlcohol

Thorl beldarakul, a dwarvish name that translated into Common as "Old Trickster," was a variety of Dwarven alcohol.

Description

This alcohol had a glowing, amber hue to it.

Notable Users

Businesses

  • The Jade Dancer, a festhall and tavern in the city of Waterdeep, sold this alcohol at a price of 5 silver pieces for a tallglass and 1 gold piece for a flagon.
