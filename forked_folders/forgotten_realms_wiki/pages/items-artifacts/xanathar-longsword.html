Xanathar longswords were enchanted blades used by the members of the Xanathar's Thieves' Guild of Waterdeep circa 1372 DR.

Description

These blades bore the hallmarks of the Xanathar's Thieves' Guild making it easily identifiable, often used to intimidate the Guild's marks.

Powers

These longswords were enchanted to make it easier for the user to hit their target and deal more damage upon successful hit. The design of the blades gave them perfect balance and aided the user's quick movements in combat allowing easier tumbles and dodges.

History

Xanathar longswords were in possession of the agents of the infamous thieves' guild circa 1372 DR. One such longsword was owned by Nakoto, a pale-faced agent of Xanathar's in Daggerford extorting the short-termed duke of the city Matagar Bugo.
