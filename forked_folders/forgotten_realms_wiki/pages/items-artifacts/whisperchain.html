Whisperchain was a unique suit of light chain armor found in Lost Souls' Swamp in the mid-1480s DR.

Description

The silvery armor covered most of the body and was made of mithral. While mithral would normally be light, Whisperchain was actually completely weightless.

Powers

The suit of armor was weightless and partially ethereal, and imparted it on the wearer. It let them strike effectively at incorporeal targets that otherwise slid out of material reality. It also made the wearer blurry and harder to hit in turn, and boosted their awareness and reflexes in both dodging and striking.

History

The armor ended up in Lost Souls' Swamp along with Evrim Whitehair. When he was killed by his friends, the shadowy lord of the swamp trapped Evrim as a spectral undead, and the armor (and the rest of his treasures) remained in the same lake Evrim's body was dumped in. It may have been recovered a few years later by adventurers pursuing a piece of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll held by the medusa Sshaastess, who had laired nearby.
