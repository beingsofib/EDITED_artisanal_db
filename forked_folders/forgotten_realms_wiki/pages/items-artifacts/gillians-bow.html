Gillian's bow was the enchanted longbow that belonged to Gillian Cantilar, the defender of the Trade Way and the namesake of the hamlet of Gillian's Hill.

Powers

Gillian's bow possessed a +2 enchantment. The weapon was also fitted with an ingenious sighting mechanism of gnomish creation. It gave Gillian's bow range, dramatically longer than other longbows, and gave the weapon the ability to deal devastating critical damage when its arrows reached their targets dead-center.

History

This enchanted bow faithfully served Gillian Cantilar, the half-elven ranger hero of the Trade Way. By 1372 DR, Gillian's bow was in the hands of Gillian's Hill Gang, a group of bandits who took over the fire cairn tower on the hill's summit and menaced the locals and traveling merchants.
