Noble's cap

Item
nameNoble's Cap

locationNeverwinter, Sword Coast

Noble's caps were minor magic items used by the nobility of large cities such as Neverwinter in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

These hats appeared quite mundane but had minor enchantment weaved into their fabric. Noble's caps subtly affecter the wearer's appearance and charisma making them more attractive and persuasive.

History

Many believed that noble's caps were created by an unnamed enchanted in a rush. The wizard grabbed a first hat they saw and quickly placed enchantments on them to wiggle out of a tight spot.

By the late 14 century DR, noble's caps were in wide use in such cities as Neverwinter of the Sword Coast, and as far south as the city of Samargol in Samarach.
