Spiderbane

Item
nameSpiderbane
typeBattleaxe
level2e+3

Spiderbane was a battleaxe that was the favored weapon of Callarduran Smoothhands, the gnome deity of stone, mining and the deep underground.

Powers

This +3 battleaxe was, as its named implied, a bane to spiders. When either a drow or a spider was struck with an accurate blow, it was slain outright with no chance to resist.
