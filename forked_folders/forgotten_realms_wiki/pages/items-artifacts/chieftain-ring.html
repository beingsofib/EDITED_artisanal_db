Chieftain (ring)

Item
nameChieftain
typeRing
locationTethyamar, Buried Realms

Chieftain was a magical silver ring found in the ruins of Tethyamar in the mid-1480s DR.

Powers

Chieftain gave the wearer better foresight and wisdom, and gave broad protection against magic and mental attacks.

History

The ring was originally worn by an ancient famed dwarf warlord, who left a long legacy that was still remembered in later days. The ring remained a recognizable sign of his influence. It was later found in Tethyamar Mineshaft 214S, following the defeat of the mad priest Egil Hakon.
