Abyss' Alive and Kicking was a type of alcoholic brew found in the city of Zelatar, on the Abyssal plane of Azzagrat.

Description

This intoxicating concoction was a wheat brew served with active living yeast.

History

As of the , Abyss' Alive and Kicking could be purchased from demonic breweries and taverns in Zelatar. Unlike many of the drinks favored by the fiends who inhabited the realm, Abyss' Alive and Kicking was suitable for human or consumption.
