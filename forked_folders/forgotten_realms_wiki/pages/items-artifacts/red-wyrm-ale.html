Red Wyrm Ale was a type of ale concoction found in the fishing town of Tulmene on the Dragon's Head Peninsula in Tethyr.

Description

Red Wyrm Ale was considered to be the cheapest swirl served at the Fast Sails tavern in Tulmene. The drink was a mix of several cheap local brews. Despite its price and ingredients, the drink was smooth to taste and pleasant.
