Muquet was an alcoholic concoction known as a cordial in Khôltar, south Faerûn, circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR.

Description

Cordials in Khôltar, at least the ones available from Munsrum's Ready Ladle (on the North Way just south of Handrornlar, the north gate out of the Iron City), were a mixture of a fiery wine, fruit juice, and a dash of secret herbs. Muquet used the juice, pulp and rind of a fruit similar to a small orange. This cordial was the color of amber and was touted as having medicinal uses by the staff at Munsrum's. A bottle of muquet sold for 3 gold pieces.
