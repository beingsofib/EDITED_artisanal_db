Tufenks were special weapons originating in Zakhara.

Description

These weapons looked like elongated blowpipes.

Uses

Tufenks were used to project over short distances. The Zakharan term for Greek fire was "oil of liquid star" or simply "liquid star." This weapon required the use of both hands to operate safely.

Price

Tufenks cost 21 gp.
