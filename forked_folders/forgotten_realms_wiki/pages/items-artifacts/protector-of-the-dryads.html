The Protector of the Dryads was a magical shortbow that was created by the dryads of the Gulthmere Forest.

Powers

The Protector possessed a +2 enchantment.

History

When a group of orc loggers threatened their home, the dryads of the Gulthmere presented this bow to the great hero Hannable the White. When the orcs nearly chopped down a great oak tree, thus killing the dryad that was linked to it, Hannable confronted the orcs. She slayed the entire band before they could inflict any more harm upon the dryads or their woods.

At some point the bow made its way into the hands of the assassin Marek.
