Mithral bells were a type of enchanted bracelet.

Description

These bracelets consisted of a small mithral chain of eleven mithral bells.

Powers

Each of the elven bells that adorned these bracelets could be thrown as a grenade weapon, detonating with a sound burst effect. The bell would explode after if it did not strike something first.

Additionally, while being worn the wearer would find greater success at attempted artistic performances, provided at least three bells remained on the mithral chain.

Creation

Mithral bells could only be crafted by bards.
