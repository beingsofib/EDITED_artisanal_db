Muffins were a variety of baked goods found across the Realms, sometimes eaten with a cup of tea or served for morningfeast with a side of fruit.

Description

Muffin were small handfoods and some were flavored with blueberries, and even poppy seed. Occasionally, muffins were used to smuggle secret messages or items hidden within.

Availability

Muffins were served with tea at the Royal Arms inn in Luskan and were commonly served as a morningfeast food in Westgate on the Dragon Coast and in Mulmaster on the Moonsea.

In Tegal's Mark in the Dalelands, The Markhouse inn offered its guest complimentary muffins, biscuits, and chilled berry juice. Similarly, the Crescent Moon Inn of Ravens Bluff in the Vast offered its guests muffins free of charge.

Outside of the Prime Material plane, muffins were among the morningfeast bounty served in Finder Wyvernspur's realm of Fermata in Arborea.

History

In the mid–14 century DR, Sheila's Incredible Edibles stall sold the best baked goods in all of Ylraphon in the Vast, including muffins.
