The ring of the frozen falls was an enchanted ring from the cold lands of Rashemen in the Unapproachable East, said to have had been created by the land's telthors.

Powers

The wearer of the ring of the frozen falls received partial immunity to cold, both natural and supernatural. They also could use the hypothermia spell thrice per day as well as polar ray once per day.

History

As it was tradition before embarking on one's dai jemma, one young man camped at the Falls of Erech, a grand waterfall on the northwestern-most edge of Rashemen. All those who set out on their journey of adulthood were to spend at least one night at the Falls of Erech and give an offering to the raging waters. So did the young man before going to sleep. As he slept, a group of Nar barbarians discovered him and began circling the young Raashemaar, intending to murder and rob him. However, before the dark deed could commence, a spirit possessed the young man, and he easily defeated the attackers, showing stunning skill and powerful savagery. Once the attackers were all dead, the spirit left the youth's body. He looked around and found a new ring on his finger. It was a protective gift from the spirit.

In the , one such ring was found witin the Bear God's Barrow north of Mulsantir.
