Swiresy shoes were magical boots that were likely created in tribute to the infamous diminutive vaulting thief named Lupperdiddle Swires.

Powers

The wearer of these boots became an expert vaulter, making their jumps longer and higher. Additionally, they magically improved the wearer's skills in acrobatics.

History

As of the , Swiresy shoes were in possession of Grat, a merchant of Cult of the Absolute who traded at the Shattered Sanctum. The boots were among many other spoils of cultist raids on the communities and locations in the Chionthar Valley.
