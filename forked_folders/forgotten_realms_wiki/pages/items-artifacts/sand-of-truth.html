Sand of truth

Item
nameSand of Truth
typeMagical dust
locationZakhara

Sand of truth was a magical item that could be found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate. They were only carried or used by holy slayers.

Description

This magical dust was created from the remains of a slain sandman.

Powers

Sand of truth could reveal illusions, genie-work, and polymorphed creatures. In essence, it revealed the true nature of any magically altered item or creature. When sprinkled onto such an object or creature, the sand would transform the subject back to its original state.
