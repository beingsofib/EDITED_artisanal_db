Ankh of Life

Item
nameAnkh of Life
typeArtifact
locationOld Empires, east Faerûn

The ankh of life was a powerful artifact that was a holy symbol of the manifestation of Re.

Description

The ankh was worn and covered with dirt. It was made from birch and measured in length.

Powers

This artifact could use the following powers—resurrection twice per day, restoration three times per day, regenerate three times per day, destroy undead, 30' radius three times per day, and continual light at will. However, these great powers came with a steep cost. Using any of these powers, aside from continual light, permanently drained a portion of the wielder's experiences and memories. This was only restorable via a wish.

History

The ankh of life was lost during the Orcgate Wars. Clergy of both Horus-Re and Osiris searched desparately for the artifact so they could place it with a manifestation of Re within the Tower of Eternity in skuld.
