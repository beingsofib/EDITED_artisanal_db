Orosk

Item
nameOrosk
typeFruit
locationEvereska

Orosks were golden-skinned citrus fruits that grew in cold climates during summer months. These elven vine-fruits had a tart flavor akin to a or a tangerine. Orosks were grown only in a few places around Faerûn, such as Evereska. Orosks could be used in making elverquisst.
