Bronzewood cuauhchimalli and their more powerful variant, exalted BBronzewood cuauhchimalli, were a variety of artifact shield created to be used by paladin adventurers in the Chultan city of Port Nyanzaru in the late 15 century DR.

Description

These bronzewood cuauhchimalli were testaments of their owners' strength and the influence of their guilds. These magic items could be created by the most competent weaponscrafters of Chult with the use of living bronzewood timber.

Powers

Bronzewood cuauhchimalli could be enchanted to house various magics customized for each owner, in addition to boosting the owner's battle prowess, their skills to deflect enemy strikes, and strengthening their especially lucky and well-aimed attacks. When a Bronzewood quauhololli was wielded with a bronzewood cuauhchimalli, both items boosted each other's damage output, the wielder's healing capabilities, and absorbed some of the incoming damage, as well as granted the same boons to the wielder's allies.

History

Bronzewood cuauhchimalli were created in the late 15 century DR, and their design suggested the items' origins to be tied to the ancient Lost City of Omu.
