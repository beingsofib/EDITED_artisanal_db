Brooches of safety were enchanted pieces of jewelry from Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.

Description

A small intricately decorated brooch, a brooch of safety was shaped to resemble a shield and had a glowing purple gemstone affixed to its center.

Powers

These enchanted brooches gave the wearer a chance of becoming invisible as if under the effects of the sanctuary spell, allowing them to avoid being targeted by a foe for a short period of time.

Notable Owners

  • Halbenet Soladonel: a moon elf cleric of Corellon Larethian who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
