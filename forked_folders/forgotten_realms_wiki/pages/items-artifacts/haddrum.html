Haddrum was a drug. It was an elixir made out of dragon's blood and other worse components.

History

In , it was smuggled in and out of Huorm, per the elves of Al Qahara.

It was imported from Raurin into Cormyr by the Spice Lords of Marsember as of . That same year, it was banned from Ragheb by its leader, Alzubra, because of the violent fits it could cause.
