Buckled Knot was an adventurers' marine vessel, based in Daggerford and active circa 1372 DR.

History

In 1372 DR, Buckled Knot was stationed in Daggerford's harbor. The ship actively advertising itself to its potential crew members via the River Quarter notice boards, promising adventures and journeys on the high seas.
