Spikevine clubs were enchanted clubs wielded by druids. They could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

True to the name, a spikevine club was a gnarled piece of wood covered in spikes and entwined by a thorny vine.

Powers

Any who were struck by the club risked serious injury, but so too did their nearby allies. At unpredictable intervals, a spikevine club might lash out at nearby foes upon tasting the blood of an enemy.

Notable Owners

  • Pikel Bouldershoulder, a dwarf druid.
