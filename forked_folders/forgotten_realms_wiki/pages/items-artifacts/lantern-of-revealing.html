Lanterns of revealing were enchanted hooded lanterns that allowed the user to dispel invisibility.

Description

Lanterns of revealing were regular hooded lanterns in appearance.

Powers

With a spoken command word, these lanterns were lit with magics that purged invisibility off of all things and creatures that were illuminated. Lanterns of revealing required oil to burn and could sustain its flames up to six hours fro one pint of it. These lanterns illuminated - radius and created dim light for more. Creation

Lanterns of revealing could be created by experienced wondrous item crafters and the use of invisibility purge spell.

Notable Owners

  • Torleth Mindulspeer: the antique and curio dealer from Gillian's Hill kept one of the lanterns of revealing, along with a necklace of fireballs, and the original Greatshout as a Young Man, in his trapped vault under Torleth's Treasures circa 1372 DR.
  • Tchazzar: the Chosen of Tiamat had a lantern of revealing in his horde along with many more precious and rare magical items circa 1374 DR.
