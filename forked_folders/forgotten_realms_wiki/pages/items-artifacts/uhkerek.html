A uhkerek was a type of sled used on the Great Glacier.

Description

The uhkerek, a transportation sled akin to the jakerek, boasted a larger size and employed two caribou as pulling animals. Its platform took on a box-like shape, with sides standing at approximately in height. The bottom of the platform was lined with seal skins, generously coated in blubber to minimize friction. Typically utilized for transporting supplies, the uhkerek also accommodated up to three passengers when necessary.
