Storm bracers

Item
nameStorm Bracers
locationUtter East

Storm bracers were a pair of magical bracers used in the Utter East during the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

These armlets lent power to the wearer's arms, enhancing their ability to make physical attack.
