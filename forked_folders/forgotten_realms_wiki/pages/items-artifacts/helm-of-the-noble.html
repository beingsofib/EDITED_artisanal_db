The helm of the noble was a unique helm of glory that once belonged to a knight of great repute.

Powers

The helm improved the physical defenses of its wearer and granted them an air of allure and magnetism.

History

The helm of the noble once belonged to Sir Tain the Noble a brave knight who fought in a great number of battles. After his time, the helm passed to his squire, who had a less impressive career.

It passed from one owner to another over the years. By the , it came into the possession of Jardak a warrior who lived in the city of Baldur's Gate. Later, the helm was among possessions of the Temple of Helm in Athkatla's Temple District.
