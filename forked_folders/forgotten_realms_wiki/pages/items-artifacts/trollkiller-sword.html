Trollkiller swords were a type of enchanted sword intended for, of course, killing trolls.

Powers

The powers and design of these swords varied; typically, they were ''+1 flaming longswords.

Availability

These swords could be found for purchase in the city of Waterdeep and at Graendel's Fine Dwarven Craft in Targos.

History

These swords were once exclusively associated with the city of Waterdeep, but by they had become a rather common magical item in Faerûn.
