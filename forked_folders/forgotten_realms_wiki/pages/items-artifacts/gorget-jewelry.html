Gorget (jewelry)

Item
nameGorget
typeJewelry
value2e1-3 sp to 50 gp

Gorgets were a collar-like form of jewelry, full and broad in the front, that were worn by some upperclass in the Realms. Both their design and name were derived from the armor of the same name.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe) sold luxury one-size-fits-all gorgets in the "Jewelry" section of its catalogue for 50 gold pieces. These gorgets were filigreed of the finest gold and inset with Kozakuran pearls. They also had a silk fastening band, linen in coloration and of a jacquard weave, that were sewn by women in Sembia.
