The storm rider's pearl was a marid item used by the Great Padisha of the Marid.

Storm Rider's Pearl

The storm rider's pearl was similar to the traveler's pearls but also allowed the Padisha to alter wind, summon storms, and to change the tides. It was not consumed upon use like the traveler's pearls.
