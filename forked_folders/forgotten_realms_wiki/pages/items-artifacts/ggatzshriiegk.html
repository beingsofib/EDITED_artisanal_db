Ggatzshriiegk was a magical obsidian javelin that could pierce a demon and be recalled by its owner across the planes.

Description

Ggatzshriiegk was a shining obsidian javelin, covered in jagged scales. When thrown, it buzzed like a wasp with its scales standing up from its surface, making it spin. The javelin always homed in on its target.

Powers

With the command words "I call on ggatzshriiegk" said out loud or in one's mind, the magical javelin could be summoned into the hands of its owner. The summoning could be done across the room or across the planes with equal ease. The damage done with the javelin was magical, piercing stone and flesh with ease.

History

Ggatzshriiegk was a part of a battle between vrocks led by Shaakat, and the rescue party of paladins led by Aleena Paladinstar, circa 1371 DR. During the combat, the magical javelin easily pierced and killed two barlguras and a simulacrum of Aleena herself in one throw. Eventually, the javelin was broken by one of the paladins and was buried under a collapsed tunnel along with the pyramid and the portal where the battle took place.

Notable Owners

  • Shaakat was a vrock who owned ggatzshriiegk circa 1377 DR.
