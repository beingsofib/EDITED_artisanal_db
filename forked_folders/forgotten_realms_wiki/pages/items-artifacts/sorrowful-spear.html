Sorrowful Spear

Item
nameSorrowful Spear
typeSpear
creatorSebek

The Sorrowful Spear was a unique melee weapon wielded by the avatar of Sebek, the Crocodile God within the Mulhorandi pantheon.

Description

The weapon was a large spear.

Powers

This weapon carried a stout +3 enchantment. Any stick or branch touched by the Sorrowful Spear would be transformed into a vigorous crocodile under the complete control of Sebek.
