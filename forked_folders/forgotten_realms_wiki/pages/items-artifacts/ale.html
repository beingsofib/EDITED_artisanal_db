Ale was a widely available beverage in the Realms.

Notable Brews

Most taverns provided their own supplies from regional brewers. A few ales, however, due to their high quality or ease of transport, were famous and available in wider areas.

Aftermath Abbey Ale : A variety of enchanted and expensive ale from the Yawning Portal.

Avner's Abyssal Ale : type of porter found in the city of Zelatar, on the Abyssal plane of Azzagrat.

Baldur's Gate Pale : A brew from the Cutlass in Luskan.

Baldur's Gate Red Ale : A red ale sold at Port Llast.

Belbuck : A translucent green spearmint beer favored by halflings.

Bitter-ale : A type of dark, thick, and strong foul-tasting duergar ale.

Bitter Black : A dark stout from Arabel.

Bitterroot beer : A smoky beer from Candlekeep and Daggerford made out of bitterroot plant.

Black Bottom : A rough, smoky ale from Cormyr.

Black grog ale: A watered down libation from the Immurk's Hold, Pirate Isles.

Crystal Ale : A type of weak and refreshing ale from the alesmith of Ravens Bluff named Bowser in the late 14 century DR.

Crystal Ale : A completely average in strength and quality ale from Ravens Bluff.

Deep Ale : A frothy reinforced ale from Waterdeep, also known as fire ale.

Darklake stout : A frothy dark ale made by the Duergar of Clan Muzgardt in Gracklstugh.

Darndarr : A nutty-tasting ale from the Sword Coast.

Deadman's Ale : A variety of ale from the alesmith of Ravens Bluff named Bowser in the late 14 century DR, named after a dead taste-tester, whose death was unrelated to the drink.

Dragon's Breath Beer : A strong and harsh brew from Sembia.

Elder Root stout : A rich and flavorful stout from the Running Stag tavern in Berdusk.

Elminster's Choice: A heavy, bitter, dark, and cloudy beer from Immersea, favored by adventurers from around the Wyvernwater.

Firesword : A wide variety of ales of similar recipes brewed across the Moonsea and often reinforced with honey.

Frostkiss Ale : A sour aged variety of ale name after Auril the Frostmaiden.

Golden Sands Brews : Light and effervescent lagers from Calimshan. There were three variations: Golden Sands Basic, Golden Sands Gold and Golden Sands Orange.

Greentart : A thin and fruity gnomish pale ale from Ravens Bluff.

Highwater ale : A minty-flavored cream throat-burning ale from Highcastle.

Iriaeboran North Brew : A dark amber brew with a bitter aftertaste from Iriaebor. The producers called it "the bite of the North winds".

Luiren's Best : A sweet black, thick stout brewed by the Smokardin clan on the coast of Luiren. It was said to be a nice combination with nut cheese.

Mulhorandi dark : quality dark ale from Mulhorand.

Night ale : A strong and dark ale from Voonlar. It was only available at the Swords Meet.

Ogrefire Ale : A variety of enchanted and expensive ale from the Yawning Portal.

Old Black Goblin : A black-colored, extremely valuable, and rare porter from Cormyr and Sembia.

Old One Eye : A light brew with a spicy aftertaste from Llorbauth.

Railur's Red : A "red", or amber ale from the coastal areas of Cormyr and Sembia.

Regal Eagle Ale : A variety of enchanted and expensive ale from the Yawning Portal.

Rockgrit : A variety of very strong gnomish pale ale distilled and brewed from potatoes.

Rock Strained Ale : A type of strong and good-tasting ale from the alesmith of Ravens Bluff named Bowser in the late 14 century DR.

Samman : A bitter brown ale brewed by the dwarves from Gracklstugh.

Sarmsark : A cheap but strong-flavored ale from the Inner Sea.

Shadowdark ale : A cloudy and frothy brown ale from Shadowdale.

Silverymoon Ale : A hearty-bodied ale brewed by a small group of dwarves near Silverymoon.

Sleeping Dragon dark : A nutty brew made of oats and barley and produced at the Sleeping Dragon in Rassalantar.

Slow Spout : A thick and bitter ale produced by Duergar and available in Menzoberranzan.

Spruce beer : A beer made from the spruce tree in Wheloon, Cormyr.

Stint : A variety of gnomish pale ale strong with alcohol and strong in smell from Ravens Bluff.

Suzale : Also known as Purple Dragon Ale. A fine nutty ale originally brewed for the royal household of Cormyr.

Tanagyr's Stout : A heavy black stout with a rich malt flavor from Zhentil Keep.

Wizard's Quaff : A local brew freely available for tenants of the Whistling Wizard in Voonlar.

Wyrmwizz : A muddy ale brewed at Gyudd's Distillery in Skullport. It was sold at The Black Tankard and The Worm's Gullet.
