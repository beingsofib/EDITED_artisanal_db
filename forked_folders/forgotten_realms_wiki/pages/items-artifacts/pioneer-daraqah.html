Pioneer daraqah and its more powerful variant, exalted pioneer daraqah, was a variety of artifact shield created to be used by fighter adventurers in the Chultan city of Port Nyanzaru in the late 15 century DR.

Description

These pioneer daraqah were crafted to help adventuring troupes survive the perils of the Jungles of Chult, being ideal for long expeditions. They were created to be paired with enchanted scimitars called pioneer kilij.

Powers

A pioneer daraqah could be enchanted to hold various unique magics for each owner, in addition to boosting the damage inflicted by the owner in combat, giving the ability to deflect enemy blows, and increasing their physical strength. When a pioneer kilij was wielded with a pioneer daraqah, both items boosted each other's magical powers, scaling in potency, depending on the size of the adventuring group or number of allies. With no allies, the weapons boosted the owner's physical strength; within a party of two, their power and defense were boosted; in a party of three, increased accuracy was added to the list; in a party of four, their luck and well-aimed attacks were empowered; and lastly, with a party of five or more, the weapons gained the ability to penetrate enemy armor.

History

Pioneer daraqah were created in the late 15 century DR, and their design suggested the items' origins to be tied to the ancient Lost City of Omu.
