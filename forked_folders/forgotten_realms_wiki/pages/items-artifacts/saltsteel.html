Saltsteel

Item
nameSaltsteel
typeMagical substance
locationUnther, Old Empires

Saltsteel was a magically hardened substance used in Unther.

History

Saltsteel was used in the creation of a wand of salt. The process for making saltsteel was only known to the wizards of Unther.
