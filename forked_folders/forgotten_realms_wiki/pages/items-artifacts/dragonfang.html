Dragonfang

Item
nameDragonfang
typeDagger

A dragonfang was a magical dagger with a normal appearance.

Powers

Its wielder could cause it to emit a 30-m (90-ft) lightning bolt from its tip, up to four times per day. They were particularly damaging to dragons and dracoliches.
