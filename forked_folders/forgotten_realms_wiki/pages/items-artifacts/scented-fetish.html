Scented fetish

Item
nameScented Fetish
typeWondrous item
creatorNyatar
originNeverwinter, Sword Coast North

A scented fetish was an enchanted druidic trinket created by Nyatar, a druid of Silvanus and Chauntea who operated in the Jewel of the North, Neverwinter, in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

An individual who was entrusted with such a magical poppet received the boon that allowed them to communicate with beasts at will as if they were themselves a druid or a ranger.

History

During the time the Wailing Death plague ravaged Neverwinter in the , Nyatar created a scented fetish to be entrusted to an adventurer willing to help him free captured animals from the Blacklake District Lodge, a zoo in the Blacklake District.
