Acandar was an enchanted bastard sword of unknown origins found on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The sword possessed a +3 enchantment that made it deadlier and guided its blade toward the wielder's target. In addition to cutting the opponent, it also inflicted modest amounts of electrical damage upon each successful hit. It allowed for the casting of the call lightning spell once per day; however only five uses of the spell were available in total as of 1374 DR.

History

The exact origins of the weapon were unknown. In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR, one such blade was found in the Blacklake District of Neverwinter, where it was part of Dayne Lynneth's more exclusive stock.
