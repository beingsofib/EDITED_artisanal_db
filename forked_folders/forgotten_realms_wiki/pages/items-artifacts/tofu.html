Tofu was a type of food that was produced from soybeans.

Description

Specifically, tofu was made up of protein from soybeans that had been congealed.

Tofu came in different forms, such as dried sheets. The versatile protein could also be made into bean curd noodles, which were fine and lengthy; they were also characterized as both transparent and had a satisfyingly chewy texture.

Notable Owners

  • Motoharu Kaijitsu, the yakuza-ninja, kept on his person of tofu sheets.
