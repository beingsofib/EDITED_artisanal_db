The Spear of Halama was an enchanted spear once carried by the elf heroine Halama.

Powers

The spear functioned as a +2 weapon. It also allowed the wielder to fly for up to an hour per day. Additionally, the spear granted the wielder immunity from dragon breath.

Rumors & Legends

The spear was rumored to be a gift to Halama from a gold dragon.
