Glasses of the studious mage

Item
nameGlasses of the Studious Mage
typeGlasses
originMyth Drannor, north Faerûn

The glasses of the studious mage were a set of magical glasses found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Description

These glasses had large round lenses.

Powers

The glasses helped the wearer cast their spells more efficiently and subtly, even aiding their use of metamagic.

History

The glasses were used for centuries by the elven wizards of the city for their studies. They were eventually found when the Cormanthor Elves searched the Speculum of Myth Drannor for Kelgoth Darksong, fighting the demon Zun'thar the Unbound in the process.
