Staves of corrosion were enchanted staves that exuded acid.

Description

Staves of corrosion were simple-looking wooden quarterstaves from to long. They were sometimes carved out of stout oak and impeded with jade stones.

Powers

In battle, staves of corrosion exuded acid when hitting a target. Some stronger versions of the item existed, bearing potent damage and aiming enchantments.

History

In the , staves of corrosion could be found in the Icewind Dale region of northwest Faerûn.
