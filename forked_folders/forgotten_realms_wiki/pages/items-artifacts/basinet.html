The basinet was a style of helmet found in parts of the Realms.

History

This style of helmet could be found in Faerûn as far back as the Netherese Empire, where it first became available in .

Notable Basinets

  • Helms of brilliance were sometimes fashioned in the style of a basinet, forged of brilliant silver and polished steel.

Notable Owners

  • Aubric Nihmedu wore a simple basinet, forged of silvery mithral steel.
  • Maalthiir, First Lord of Hillsfar, once had a polished basinet helm with a brilliant red plume and etched with his signature rune.
