The Silverleaf rod was an enchanted dwarven mace created in the glory days of the City of Song, Myth Drannor.

Description

The Silverleaf rod was created with a distinctly dwarven shape and design. The bludgeon's head was silver-plated.

Powers

The Silverleaf rod carried a simple +2 enchantment, however, when wielded against orcs and orogs, it became functionally an enchanted +3 weapon. Additionally, when battling orcs and orogs, the weapon magically improved the wielder's defenses, similarly to a +1 enchantment.

History

The bludgeon was created by the dwarven smiths and enchanters of Myth Drannor, before the city's destruction in the Weeping War. By the , the Silverleaf rod, along with its sister weapon – Silverleaf hatchet, fell into the clawed hands of lizardfolk, led by a mysterious sorcerer Malgi Hi who laired in tunnels of the Dwarven Dungeons.
