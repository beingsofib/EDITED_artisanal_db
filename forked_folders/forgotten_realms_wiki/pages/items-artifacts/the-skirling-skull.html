The Skirling Skull was unique enchanted flail of svirfneblin make from the vast Underdark.

Description

The Skirling Skull's design was unusual, even for its deep gnome creators. It had a long wooden haft with its head made of carved stone to resemble a skull with impossibly long shards of dark crystal protruding out of it.

Powers

With each strike, the crystal spikes vibrated, emitting a keening wail. This magical harmonics possessed the ability to penetrate and dispel active magical effects and spells from the target and had a chance to deafen them as well as the wielder.

History

As of the , The Skirling Skull was wielded by Harhord Dorm, a duergar bodyguard of Barud Barzam, who was placed in charge of an old duergar stronghold in the River Caves, where the Black Raven River emerged and ran south in Northwest Faerûn.
