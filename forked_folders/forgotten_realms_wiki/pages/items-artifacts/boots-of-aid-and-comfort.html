Boots of aid and comfort were magical boots that bolstered the healing powers of the wearer.

Description

These boots were well-made and provided significant comfort for the wearer.

Powers

When the wearer of the boots of aid and comfort healed another creature, that creature received additional relief.

History

As of the , one pair of these boots remained in possession of Grat, a merchant of Cult of the Absolute who traded at the Shattered Sanctum.
