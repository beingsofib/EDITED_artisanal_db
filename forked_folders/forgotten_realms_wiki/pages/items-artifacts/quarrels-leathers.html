Quarrel's leathers was a set of unique enchanted leather armor created by an obsessive half-drow warlock named Quarrel in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The armor was stitched together from fragments of armor that one was worn by warlock Ammon Jerro, Quarrel's beloved idol. This mismatched armor possessed a +2 enchantment and allowed the wearer to cast the bestow curse spell. The armor could also be only worn by warlocks, half-elves, and those who venerated evil.

History

Quarrel's leathers were the warlock's favorite armor. He wore the leathers into battle during the Quarrel's search for the source of Ammon Jerro's arcane powers. The armor accompanied the half-drow across the Sword Coast North, the Mere of Dead Men, and settled for a while in the Crossroad Keep, before setting out for the land of Thay some years after the .
