Ring of false alignment

Item
nameRing of False Alignment
typeEnchanted ring

A ring of false alignment was an enchanted ring that allowed the wearer to mask their true nature from others.

Powers

These rings made the wearer's true nature appear however they desired to probing magic such as know alignment.

Notable Owners

Therescales, a mage and thief inhabiting Luthcheq, wore a ring of false alignment.
