Haarl's Treatise on Dragonkind was a book of draconic knowledge that contained many hidden secrets of dragons and other draconic creatures, penned by Haarl, sometime before the late 15 century DR.

History

By the 1480s DR, the Cult of the Dragon had been using Haarl's Treatise on Dragonkind, as well as Breyer's Draconic Investigations, to acquire much of the forbidden knowledge hidden in the books' pages. Both books were in the hands of the Cult's leaders when they led a skirmish on the shores of the isle of Tuern. Once the Cult's forces were defeated, both books were retrieved by the forces of Neverwinter.
