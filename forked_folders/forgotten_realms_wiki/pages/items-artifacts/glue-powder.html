Glue powder was a powerful adhesive powdered substance, used in the city-state of Sumbria of the Blade Kingdoms.

Usage

Glue powder powder was often sprinkled on top of battlments of structures as a thivery deterrent and a trap.

History

In the , glue powder was used by House Toporello to secure thieir family palace after a slew of gem and jewelry burglaries that shook Sumbria. The powder saw placed on the top of the hollow-square-spaped palace to trap the infamous cat-burglar. However glue powder was easily avoided by the culprit - the kleptomanic firebird Tekorriikii - "the handsomest and smartest of all the birds."
