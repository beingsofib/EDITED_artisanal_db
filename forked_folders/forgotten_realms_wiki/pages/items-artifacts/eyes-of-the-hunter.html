Eyes of the hunter

Item
nameEyes of the Hunter
typeOptic aid
rarity5eVery Rare

The eyes of the hunter were an enchanted pair of lenses used by those who fought with ranged weapons. They could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Powers

A wearer's vision was clearer through the eyes of the hunter, giving them an edge when targeting enemies' vital points.

Notable Owners

  • Saarvin, a dragonborn ranger and adventurer possessed eyes of the hunter while working with Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
