Shields of the Watch were enchanted shields from the land of Shaar.

Powers

These small shields carried a +1 enchantment and offered additional defense, effectively becoming a +2 shield when wielded against humans.

History

Shields of the Watch were created as a tribute to a wemic group called the Watch. The group's true purpose was unknown; however, many claimed that the wemics were weapon and information dealers. Human nomads learned to trust the Watch. However, by the , the Watch was gone, and the shields created in the wemic name found their from Shaar to the city of Neverwinter
