Philosopher's egg

Item
namePhilosopher's Egg
typeGlassware (Retort)
value2e5,000 gp

A philosopher's egg was an enchanted piece of glassware that aided in the creation of magical fluids such as potions.

Powers

These magical retorts aided in making magical and mundane fluids by halving the time needed to make them. A philosopher's egg was also a material component in the process of creating gold from lead.

Notable Owners

Khenel Barony, a wizard from Waterdeep, owned a philosopher's egg.
