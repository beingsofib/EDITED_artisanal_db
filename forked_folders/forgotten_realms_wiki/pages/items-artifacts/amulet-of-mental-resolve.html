Amulets of mental resolve were a type of mind protecting amulet made of cold iron.

Powers

These amulets enhanced the minds of their wearers, giving them an improved chance to resist charms, illusions, and magical effects that would put one to sleep.

Amulets of mental resolve came in enchanted variants ranging from +1 to +6.

History

Around the late 15 century DR, a group of adventurers operating in Tymanther were sent into an ancient Untheri burial site near Ruinspoke to recover Melnar, a member of the Gem Assayers Union that had gone missing. While exploring a mausoleum in the area that was guarded by a skull enchanted with Skull Watch, the adventurers came across a stone sarcophagus belonging to a Lathanderite named Jagress Dawnlion. Hidden beneath a false bottom in the sarcophagus the adventurers found a number of gold pieces, a +1 amulet of mental resolution, and a +1 symbol of turning.
