Ring of chameleon power

nameRing of Chameleon Power
typeRing
value2e5,000 gp

A ring of chameleon power was a magic item capable of disguising the wearer with their surroundings.

Powers

This item allowed the wearer to blend in with their surroundings to the point where they were nearly invisible. Additionally, the ring could make the wearer appear as another species if viewed from a minimum distance of , though this trickery was discovered quickly if the wearer did not leave the vicinity of those viewing them.

Notable Owners

  • Akim al-Kalaas, a sha'ir of the Unclean in Zakhara, carried a ring of chameleon power.
  • Filarion Filvendorson, an elf inhabiting Daggerford, wore a ring of chameleon power.
  • Nabilanasa, an elven ranger and guide who traveled the deserts of Zakhara.
  • Nkonzi Maru, a ranger from Katashaka and leader of the Anaconda mercenary company and warrior society.
