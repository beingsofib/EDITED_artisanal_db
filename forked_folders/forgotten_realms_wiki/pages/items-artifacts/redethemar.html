Redethemar

Item
nameRedethemar
typeAxe
locationEvermeet, Trackless Sea

Redethemar was a magical axe possessed by Ilianaro Durothil around 1367 DR.

It was the greatest weapon of House Durothil. The axe was said to contain the spirit of an elven goddess.
