Wand of freshness

nameWand of Freshness

value2e7,500 gp

Wands of freshness were a type of wand that operated similarly to the spell freshen.

Description

These wands were always brightly colored and usually decorated with etchings of flowers.

Powers

Expending a charge of this wand would cause any rancid or spoiled food within a to become permanently fresh and delicious once more.

Notable Owners

  • Solimar, a chef and wizard at the Ravens Bluff restaurant The Painted Boat, owned a wand of freshness.
