Lentils were a type of edible legume found in the Realms.

Availability

Lentils could be found in the Savage Frontier and Vilhon Reach. They were also sold in Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue, under its "Aurora's Larder" section.

Notable Uses

  • Lentil stew, served alongside smoked cheese could be found in the City of Splendors, Waterdeep, including the kitchens of the Blackstaff Tower as Khelben Arunsun was quite found of the dish.
  • Beldabar's Rest, an inn beneath the city of Yartar, served lentils in soup alongside onions and rice.
  • In Port Nyanzaru of Chult, lentils featured in both asa wat (sea tuna stewed with lentils, red onions, and spicy berbere spice) and atkilt wat (a milder dish with chard instead of fish).
  • People of Icewind Dale prepared chestnut stews using lentils.
