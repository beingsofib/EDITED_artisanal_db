The Bag of Nails was the enchanted quiver that belonged to its namesake, a tabaxi hunter Bag of Nails in late 15 century DR.

Powers

The Bag of Nails' enchantment increased its owner's speed drastically, being invaluable to rangers and other users of bows.

History

This magic item's original name was unrevealed, but it belonged to an aging hunter of the city of Omu, Bag of Nails. The only thing the tabaxi wanted in his old age was the hunter's death; however, Bag went mad in his advanced years. Eventually, the enchanted quiver found its way into the hands of the immortal bara of Mezro from Cormyr, Artus Cimber. He named this piece of enchanted gear after its previous owner.
