Safeguard shield was an heirloom shield of a wizarding family whose magic became trapped in it over the course of many family dinners, giving it a minor enchantment sometime before the late 15 century DR.

Powers

This shield possessed a minor protective magic that made it a bit easier for the wielder to avoid being affected by various spells; to shrug off poisons, diseases, and other harmful effects; and to dodge deadly spells and traps. It wasn't bad as a shield either.
