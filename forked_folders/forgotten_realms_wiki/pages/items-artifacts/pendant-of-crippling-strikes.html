Pendant of crippling strikes

Item
namePendant of Crippling Strikes
typePendant
originMyth Drannor, north Faerûn

The pendant of crippling strikes was a magical pendant found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Description

The pendant had an intricate design featuring elven blades.

Powers

The pendant of crippling strikes helped the wearer be a much more deadly and unfair fighter. They were superior at tricking, disorienting, and otherwise discombobulating their foes, and all the better at exploiting these moments of weakness and landing the killing blow.

History

The pendant may have been found when the Elves of Cormanthor chased the raiding party of Niziira Xiltun into the Underdark to rescue kidnapped elves.
