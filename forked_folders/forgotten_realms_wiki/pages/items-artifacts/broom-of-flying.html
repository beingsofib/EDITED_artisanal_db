The broom of flying was an enchanted broom that allowed its owner to fly.

Powers

The broom of flying could fly for up to 9 hours every day. Some brooms could fly at a speed of , carrying a load of without slowing down, but with any more weight it would slow at a rate of for every added. Other brooms were able to fly at a faster rate of or even , if the weight was under and would slow down to when above that weight and were able to move at this speed up until when it could no longer fly at all. All brooms could climb or descend at a rather steep rate of 30 degrees.

Every broom had a command word. If the command word was spoken and a known location, including layout, was used it would fly to that place. Otherwise, it would fly to its owner if they were up to from the broom. A broom that could not be sent to other locations could instead be called from as far away as instead.

History

In the Mornhaven Towers deep in the Mere of Dead Men a broom of flying lay hidden within a linen closet, with its command word etched into the handle.

Creation

To create a broom of flying, a grand sum of 17,000 gp of ingredients was needed, this often including roc feathers. The maker would need to able to create wondrous items and wield magic of some sort, as the spells overland flight and permanency were used in the creation.

Notable Owner

  • Diancastra, the giant demigoddess owned and used a, presumably larger and more sturdy, broom of flying.
  • Morda, a witch haunting the village of Woody Glen in the High Forest, used a broom of flying for scouting, combat, and to escape if battle turned against her.
