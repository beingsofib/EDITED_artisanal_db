Treacle tarts were a variety of tart-like pies made with a liquid, uncrystallized sugar syrup.

Description

Treacle tarts were a very sweet variety of dessert with even its breadcrumbs being sticky with molasses.

Availability

The tarts were commonly made and sold across the Chionthar Valley, on the river's north shore, as well as in the eateries of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.
