Murlyn's harp of charming

Item
nameMurlyn's Harp of Charming
typeHarp

Murlyn's harp of charming was a unique harp of charming belonging to the Harper agent Murlyn.

Powers

Whenever Murlyn would use the harp to cast suggestion, there was a slight risk that a surge of wild magic would result alongside the intended magic.
