Broken Promises

nameBroken Promises

locationAncient Abode, Putrid Bog

Broken Promises was a potion created by Auntie Ethel from within her laboratory beneath the gnarled teahouse of the Putrid Bog.

Description

The potion bottle contained a fat, juicy parasite sitting at the bottom of its liquid contents.

Powers

This potion gave an individual a great boost of strength for a few minutes' time, after which they began to feel normal again. Unfortunately their strength was sapped from them after their next night's rest, an affliction that lasted indefinitely.
