Fiendish brand

Item
nameFiendish Brand
typeMagic weapon
rarity5eLegendary

The fiendish brand was a magic rod usable by warlocks. It could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Powers

A warlock could focus their eldritch blasts through the fiendish brand in order to concentrate them into a powerful spear of eldritch energy.

Notable Owners

  • Farideh, a tiefling warlock who worked as an adventurer on behalf of Laeral Silverhand, the Open Lord of Waterdeep, during the late 15 century DR.
