Static dart

Item
nameStatic dart
typeEnchanted dart
level2e+3

Static darts were a type of enchanted dart.

Description

These darts were forged from silver and had tiny runes carved into their surface.

Powers

Static darts possessed a +3 enchantment and would unleash a small burst of electrical energy upon hitting a target.
