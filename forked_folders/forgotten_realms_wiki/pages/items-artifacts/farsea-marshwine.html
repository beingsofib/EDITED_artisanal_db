Farsea Marshwine was a variety of red wine, produced in the Farsea Swamp of Cormyr. It was exported as far as the Western Heartlands, the lands along the River Chionthar, and the city of Baldur's Gate, as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

Farsea Marshwine was a sweet and bitter red. Some described its watery taste as if it's been thrown overboard. This wine was sold in thin and long tan-colored bottles.

Notable Users

  • Danzo Arkwright, the proprietor of the Sword Coast Couriers mail service based out of Rivington district of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.
