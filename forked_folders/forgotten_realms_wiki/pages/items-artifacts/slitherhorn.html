Slitherhorn was a wind instrument from Calimport that produced a peculiar reedy sound.

Description

Slitherhorn was crafted out of snakeskin, shaped, stretched, and dried into a shape of long flute. The instrument was polished to appear sheeny.

History

In the , Cate Quirkilious, daughter of Thomas C. Quirkilious, the proprietor of Chromatic Scale musical instrument shop in Baldur's Gate, encountered slitherhorns during her trip to Calimport. She was impressed by the instrument's peculiar sound and promised to bring it to the Gate and play it for her father.
