Chromatic orb wands were enchanted wands that held multiple charges of destructive spells unleashed upon command.

Description

Chromatic orb wands were on average long scepter-shaped items topped with a glowing orb. They were usually made using ivory, or wood.

Powers

Each of these mages' accessories held several charges of the chromatic orb spell. The magics held within it could be discharged at will using a command word. Upon expenditure of a wand's last charge, it disintegrated.

History

Chromatic orb wands were rare and circa the , sometimes appeared in the hands of spellcasters of Icewind Dale in Northwest Faerûn.
