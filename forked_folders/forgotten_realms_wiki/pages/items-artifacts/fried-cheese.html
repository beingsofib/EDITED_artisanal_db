Fried cheese was a hearty quick meal sometimes served at morningfeast.

Availability

  • In Cormyrean trade town of Waymoot, fried cheeses were served at The Moon and Stars tavern.
  • In Ormpetarr, Barony of Sespech, fried cheese was served on thick-crusted bread as part of a common tavern morningfeast.
  • In Crimmor, the Caravan Capital of Amn, smoked oysters, fried cheese on bread, and pickles were virtually the only meal available at local taverns.
