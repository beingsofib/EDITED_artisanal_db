A Cat's Eye circlet, or a Cat's Eye headband, was a magic circlet that allowed the wearer to see in complete darkness.

Description

These beautiful circlets were often adorned with cat's eye agates, gems that, if enchanted, could bestow darkvision.

Notable Owners

  • Catti-brie, who was gifted this before she went to chase Drizzt Do'Urden, who headed back into the Underdark to his home city of Menzoberranzan. Her circlet was enchanted by Alustriel of Silverymoon.
