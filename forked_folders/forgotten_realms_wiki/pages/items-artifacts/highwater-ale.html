Highwater ale was a cheap curiously-flavored ale that was imported from Highcastle's the Shield and Keep tavern.

Description

Highwater ale was a clear liquid with a very distinct minty flavor that was considered to be an acquired taste

History

In the , Highwater ale was rarely found outside Highcastle, even though by the , Highwater ale's popularity reached the Sword Coast and the ale could be purchased at the inns of Daggerford, such as Happy Cow and Black Stone Inn, as well as Liam's Hold's own Holdfast Inn.

Rumors and Legends

If asked, Highcastle locals eagerly shared their belief that Highwater ale's peculiar flavor came from the bones of monsters that lived deep inside the town's well that was used to brew this libation.
