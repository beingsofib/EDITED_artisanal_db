Centered gear

Item
nameCentered Gear
typeMagic item
rarity5eCommon

The centered gear was an enchanted metal gear or cog that could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The centered gear helped spellcasters to concentrate on their spells. A wild mage could further use this concentration to gain good fortune in the use of their spells.

Notable Owners

  • Coriolis Rillevay, a gnome sorcerer and adventurer during the late 15 century DR.
