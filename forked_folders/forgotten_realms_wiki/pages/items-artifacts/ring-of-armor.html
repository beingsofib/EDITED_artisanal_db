A ring of armor was a magic ring that protected its wearer from physical harm.

Powers

These rings came in three varieties—a ring of light armor, ring of medium armor, and ring of heavy armor. The amount of protection provided to the wearer by such rings increased from the "light" version to the "heavy" version.
