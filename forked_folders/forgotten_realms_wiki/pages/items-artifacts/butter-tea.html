Butter tea

nameButter Tea

locationKhazari, Hordelands

Butter tea was a beverage from Khazari, made from yak butter, salt, and tea.

History

In 1362 DR, the Koja thought of yak-butter tea when he remembered his homeland. He was later presented with a magical feast that included it.
