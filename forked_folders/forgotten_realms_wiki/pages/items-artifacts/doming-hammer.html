Doming hammer

Item
nameDoming Hammer
typeTool
materialMetal, wood

Doming hammers were a type of metalworking tool often used in armor-crafting, resembling a double-headed hammer.

Description

Doming hammers had smooth, round heads that were used to metal sheets and plates, used in conjunction with a raising hammer.
