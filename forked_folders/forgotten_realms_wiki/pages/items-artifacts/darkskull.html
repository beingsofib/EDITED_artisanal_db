A darkskull was a powerful magical item that was wholly evil in nature.

Description

They were carved entirely out of a single piece of ebony.

Powers

A darkskull emanated unholy energy and tainted everything within its vicinity.

It was possible for a darkskull to be 'redeemed'. Upon this occurrence, silver holy symbols would appear upon its surface, and the area around the skull would be marked as hallowed ground.

Notable Owners

  • Szass Tam, zulkir of necromancy and eventual regent of Thay.
