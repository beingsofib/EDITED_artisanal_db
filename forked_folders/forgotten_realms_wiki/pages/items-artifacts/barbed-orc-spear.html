Barbed orc spears were polearms created by orcish smiths.

Description

Barbed orc spears were crude weapons. They had sturdy wooden hafts, topped with a cruel barbed spear head. When willed in battle, these barbed weapons dealt additional piercing damage to their targets.

History

In the , barbed orc spears were among many smuggled orcish-foged weapons provided to the looming army of Gorgomorz - Daggerford's Devil through his alliance with the Zhentarim. Many of these weapons were stockpiled in pirate smuggling sea caves in the Crab's Claw Coast, southwest of Daggerford, shipped there on the Barracuda, through the harbors of Daggerford.
