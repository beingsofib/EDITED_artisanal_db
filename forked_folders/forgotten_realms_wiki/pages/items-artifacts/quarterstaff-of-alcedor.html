The quarterstaff of Alcedor was a unique magic weapon created by Alcedor Kolat of Waterdeep for his own use.

Description

The quarterstaff measured long and weighed . It was made from a gnarled duskwood branch. Both ends of the staff were shod in brass.

Powers

The quarterstaff of Alcedor was imbued with the spellstrike and shattermantle weapon qualities.
