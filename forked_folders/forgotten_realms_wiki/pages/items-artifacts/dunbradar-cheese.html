Dunbradar cheese

Dunbradar cheese was a variety of goat's cheese produced in the town of Dunbridges in the Border Kingdoms of south Faerûn.

Description

Dunbradar cheese was made with goat milk and had a distinct sharp flavor. Another notable feature was the use of various nuts, mixed in with the cheese.
