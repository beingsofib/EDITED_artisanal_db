Cheddar was a type of cheese.

Description

This white-yellow cheese was often formed in the shape of wheels It could be heated up, and emitted a potent smell. Cheddar was sometimes paired and cooked with tomatoes.

Reputation

The great sage, Elminster, often enjoyed eating cheddar, especially when conversing with reputable mages, such as Mordenkainen.

Notable Cheddar

  • Arabellan Cheddar, noted by Volo to be the best cheddar in all Faerûn. It had a sharp taste, and traveled very well. It was served in taverns such as the Hungry Man, and could be brought from Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.
