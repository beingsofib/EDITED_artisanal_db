The Art of Weaponcrafting

Item
nameThe Art of Weaponcrafting

The Art of Weaponcrafting was a highly valued book written by the mage Shraevyn. It was bound in leather and written in gold lettering. The tome detailed the various levels of magic that were stored in a variety of metals and how to remove impurities from metal before forging.
