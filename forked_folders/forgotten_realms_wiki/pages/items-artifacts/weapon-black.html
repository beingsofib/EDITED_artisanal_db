Weapon-black was a useful substance used by some rogues and other nocturnal adventurers.

Uses

When smeared on metal this substance eliminated the chance of light reflecting off of a blade and alerting guards. Weapon-black was not harmful to metal and did nothing but dull a blade. Weapon-black was not flammable.

A vial of weapon-black contained enough of the substance for 25 applications.

Availability

  • In Athkatla, one could find weapon-black for sale in the market Waukeen's Promenade.
  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold vials of weapon-black for 3 gp a piece.
  • In Zhentil Keep, one could find weapon-black for sale at Lita's Equipment.
