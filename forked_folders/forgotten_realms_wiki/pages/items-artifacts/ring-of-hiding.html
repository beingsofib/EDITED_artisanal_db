Rings of hiding were enchanted rings that enhanced the wearer's roguish skills.

Description

They were leather bands that were tanned to be of vaguely the same color as the wearer's finger so as to be unnoticed when worn.

Powers

The ring's magic allowed the wearer to blend into the background with ease, avoid detection, and hide with increased proficiency.

History

By the , rings of hiding were a piece of common equipment worn by rogues, thieves, and individuals of similar professions. Their usefulness also made such rings an increasingly popular piece of equipment worn by the infamous Night Masks of Westgate if the City Watch there was to be believed.

Notable Owners

  • Dela Nereth, a member of the Bloodsailors gang of Neverwinter in 1372 DR.
