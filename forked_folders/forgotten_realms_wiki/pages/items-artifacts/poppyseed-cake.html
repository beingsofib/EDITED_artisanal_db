Poppyseed cakes were a type of fragrant bread made with seeds of the poppy flower from the city of Phlan on the Moonsea.

History

In the mid-14 century DR, poppyseed cakes by baker Celie of Phlan were a favorite treat of a famed hero Shal Desanea. During the final days of her pregnancy, Celie used to slip a poppyseed cake into Shal's husband's bread basket with each purchase.
