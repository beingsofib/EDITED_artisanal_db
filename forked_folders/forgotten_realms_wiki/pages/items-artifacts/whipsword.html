Whipswords were a variety of sword that lashed like whips. They required a great amount of training to be effective, but could be devastating in the hands of an expert.

Description

For the purpose of parrying, the first of blade that emerged from a whipsword's hilt were as sturdy as that of other swords. From there, it transitioned into a thinner shape with a diamond-shaped cross-section, narrowing nearly to the width of a wire or ribbon for at least , after which it widened again into a "barb" that gave way to a more conventional swordpoint. In all, the blade and hilt typically measured in length, with whipswords smithed for taller wielders reaching .

In order to achieve their whip-like lashing without breaking, whipsword blades were made of a special alloy.

Notable Owners

In an old-growth forest in southwestern Mistledale, a complement of elven guardians-turned-ghosts posted at a portal leading to the eastern Glimmerwood wielded long whipswords.
