Gems of curing were enchanted faceted gemstones imbued with healing magic.

Powers

Gems of curing served as a tool of healing. Each gem possessed multiple charges of healing spells.

History

Gems of curing could be found in Waterdeep circa the . They were available for sale in the many wondrous markets of the City of Splendors and often used by adventuring clerics exploring the dangerous depths of Undermountain.
