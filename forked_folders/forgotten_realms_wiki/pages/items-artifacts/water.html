The element of water was one of the four fundamental building blocks of matter and the source of all fluidity, essential for life, liable to transformations but cyclically reverting to its basic liquid nature.

Cosmology

Water manifested its position in the cosmos as the Elemental Plane of Water, one of the Inner Planes. The element was also represented there in living form as water elementals.

The ruler over the portfolio of water in Faerûn and Zakhara was the elemental lord Istishia. The archomental Olhydra also proclaimed her wish to dominate the element by calling herself Princess of Evil Water,

A number Torilian powers were concerned less directly with the elemental aspect of water, but with its various appearances in the Realms: Deep Sashelas, Umberlee, and Valkur Sebek and Shajar with rivers; Aerdrie Faenya, Anhur, Azul and Jisan with rain; and Eldath with springs, pools, and waterfalls.

Magic

School of elemental water

Many magical spells utilized the element of water. Elemental water was recognized as a "school of effect" (rather than one of the eight traditional schools of magic). Wizards specializing in this school were called water elementalists.

Water was opposed to the element of fire, both as a school of magic

Magic-users in Zakhara had a similar but not identical notion of water magic in the form of the province of sea.

Likewise, the wu jen of Kara-Tur divided spells into five elemental groups, including water.

Divine water magic

A number of deities granted divine spells that relied on the element, either in the form of the elemental water sphere, or the water domain.

Anti-water magic

A number of spells were useful against water. Protection from liquids allowed users to shield small objects from the effects of water. Create or destroy water allowed casters to remove remove a quantity of water. Proctiv's water-dust transmutation was an alteration spell that turned all the water in a target area to dust and hard water could transform a body of water, or a section, into a substance that was hard as a rock, both of these spells allowing someone to stop watery bodies that could harm someone. Water Protection was a spell used to negate certain dangers of water, it allowed a creature to breathe normally while submerged, and their clothing and equipment remained dry. Even a torch held by the creature could be protected against waters fire-stopping effects.

Celestial bodies

Spacefarers also used the four elements to distinguish different types of celestial bodies present in the crystal spheres.

Major water bodies in Realmspace were:

  • Karpri
  • Chandos
  • H'Catha
