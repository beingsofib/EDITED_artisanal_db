Tree bed

Item
nameTree Bed
locationAurora's Emporium
value2e8 gp

A tree bed was a simple gnomish contraption consisting of a canvas sheet and wooden frame.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold tree beds for 8 gold pieces and offered a pitched roof for an additional 2 gold pieces.

Uses

Tree beds provided a safe and comfortable place to sleep up off the ground by lashing them to most kinds of trees.
