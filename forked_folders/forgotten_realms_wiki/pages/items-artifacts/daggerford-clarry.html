Daggerford clarry was a blended wine made out of various grapes that were grown by the farmers outside of Daggerford, Liam's Hold, and Gillian's Hill.

History

In the , Daggerford clarry was one of the most popular local wines produced in Daggerford. The clarry was a more expensive option and a staple at the Black Stone Inn.
