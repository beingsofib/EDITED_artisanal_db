Chaos shield (item)

Item
nameChaos Shield
typeShield
locationToril

Chaos shield was a rare enchanted shield that offered its wielder additional magical protection.

Effects

Enchantments weaved into the chaos shield granted the wielder magical protection far greater than that of a mundane shield. The magic's power grew with the number of opponents the wielder faced in battle.
