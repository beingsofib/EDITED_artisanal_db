Tridents of submission were enchanted tridents that damaged both opponents' bodies and their morale.

Description

Often enough, tridents of submission appeared ordinary and completely unremarkable.

Powers

However, these weapons possessed enchantments that, upon a successful strike, had a chance of demoralizing their targets. The feeling of despair and hopelessness that consumed affected creatures caused them to stop fighting and surrender. The duration of this magical effect was between two and eight minutes. Additionally, tridents of submission possessed a +1 enchantment and held between 17 and 20 charges.

Notable Owners

  • Umberlee in her avatar form wielded a powerful enchanted +3 trident of submission that additionally functioned as a trident of fish command.
