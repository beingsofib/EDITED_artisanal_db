Feather leather armor was a type of enchanted light armor, used in the 15 century DR.

Powers

Feather leather armor possessed a +2 protective enchantment and provided the wearer with improved initiative in battle and movement speed. Its magics made the armor tougher than regular leather and practically weightless.

History

In the 15 century DR, feather leather armor could be found worn among the outlaws of the Sword Mountains, and sold in the Mercenary Arms Depot of the city of Neverwinter, as well as recovered from the ruins of the Old Owl Well. The pirates Kajin and her lieutenant Gadain both wore a set of these leathers.
