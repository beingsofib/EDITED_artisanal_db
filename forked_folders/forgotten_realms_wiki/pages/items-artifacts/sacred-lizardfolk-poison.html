Sacred lizardfolk poison

Item
nameSacred lizardfolk poison
typePoison
locationVast Swamp, Cormyr
value3e–

This sacred poison was used by the lizardfolk of the Vast Swamp in Cormyr.

Effect

When poisoning occurred, the victim suffered instant paralysis for two to twelve minutes unless they had the fortitude to resist it.

Users

The Poison Dusk lizardfolk of the Vast Swamp typically used this poison on their arrows to incapacitate foes, such as when they tried to ambush and capture a group of adventurers at the Skull Staff in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR. They typically laughed when a foe was paralyzed by it.

Kessessek, chieftain of the Sharptooth lizardfolk, declined to share it with the adventurers afterwards, saying it was sacred to the god Semuanya.
