An alicorn was the horn of a unicorn.

Usage

Unicorn horns were reputed to have magical and healing properties. They could be used in the production of antidotes and healing potions.

They could also be used as a +1 magical weapon, albeit only whilst still attached to the unicorn.

Availability

They could be sold to alchemists for up to 1,500 gold pieces.
