The seven seedlings of the verdant sages were a group of mythical immature offspring of the most ancient and wisest treants of the North known of in the mid–14 century DR.

History

In 1369 DR, adventurers hired by Celerum the Black disturbed a stone circle in a sacred druidic grove; in reality, it was the crumbled wall of the lost Accursed Tower of Damien Morienus.

The druids confronted the adventurers and tasked them with finding the seven seedlings of the verdant sages and returning them to restore the holiness of the grove and the stone circle.
