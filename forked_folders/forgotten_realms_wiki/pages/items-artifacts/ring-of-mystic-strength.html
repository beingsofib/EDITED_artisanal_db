Ring of mystic strength

Item
nameRing of Mystic Strength
typeRing
originMyth Drannor, north Faerûn

The ring of mystic strength was a magical ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The ring gave to a boon to the physical strength and mental cunning of the bearer, making them more tricky in combat and more adept at spellcraft.

History

The ring was once used by those who passed the Blade-rite of ancient Cormanthyr. Following the destruction of the Weeping War, it was eventually found during the Elven Crusade when the Cormanthor Elves helped reclaim the ruins.
