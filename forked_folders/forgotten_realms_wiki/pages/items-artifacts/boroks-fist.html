Borok's Fist was an enchanted warhammer that once was wielded by a dwarven hero, Borok Thundercracker, long before the 14 century DR.

Powers

Borok's Fist possessed a +2 enchantment that guided the wielder's strikes towards their targets and dealt increased damage. Additionally, upon each strike, Borok's Fist discharged a burst of electricity.

History

Borok's Fist's first known owner was Borok Thundercracker, who led his people in battles against giants, or "tall vermin", as he called them. It was believed Borok's Fist was blessed. Borok's elvish allies jokingly called the hammer his "little iron fist." Circa the , Borok's Fist was available for purchase from Ribald Barterman's Adventurer Mart in Athkatla, the City of Coin.
