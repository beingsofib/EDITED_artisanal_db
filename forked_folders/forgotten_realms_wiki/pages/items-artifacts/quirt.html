Quirt

Item
nameQuirt
typeWhip

A quirt was a short form of whip used to motivate stubborn mounts into moving forward.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold quirts that fit comfortably in the hand under the "Wilderness Gear" section of the catalogue.

Trivia

  • The year 110 DR was referred to as the "Year of the Quirt".
