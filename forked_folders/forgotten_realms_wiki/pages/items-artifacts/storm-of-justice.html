Storm of Justice was a unique enchanted blade found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Description

This sword was a vorpal great scimitar.

History

Storm of Justice was a family heirloom of the House of Hanif tribe of the Haunted Lands. The magnificent weapon was traditionally carried by the Sheikh who, in , was Kaldhun bin Hanif.
