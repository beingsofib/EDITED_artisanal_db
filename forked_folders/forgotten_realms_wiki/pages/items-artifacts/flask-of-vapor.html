Flask of vapor

Item
nameFlask of Vapor
typeBottle
locationThe Sea of Fallen Stars

A flask of vapor was a simple brass bottle that, when opened, released a continuous flow of fog until stoppered.

Powers

The flask would create a large disk of fog with a maximum size of across and high. Once that maximum volume was filled the flask would not release any new fog unless the current fog was blown away by natural or magical means.
