Hacksaws were a type of saw used to cut through metal.

Description

Hacksaws were made by dwarven craftsmen. The blades were made from hardened steel, with a superior version including diamond-tipped blades. One could even find hacksaws made of adamantine.

Availability

  • In Athkatla, one could find hacksaws for sale in the market Waukeen's Promenade.
  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold common hacksaws for 5 gold pieces and hacksaws of superior quality for 20 gp.

Notable Users

  • Hacksaws were sometimes used by thieves or rogues throughout Faerûn,
