The Music Box

Item
nameThe Music Box
typeTorture device
locationDaggerford, Delimbiyr Vale

The Music Box was a devious and exotic torture device.

Description

This mechanism, was facetiously called for its functionality and form. The Music Box was a humanoid-sized cage with its bars created out of tuning forks. Once hit, the cage started reverberating with high-pitched tones, eventually driving the victim locked inside insane and causing profuse ear bleeding.

History

In 1372 DR, one of these devices could be found in Daggerford's curio shop, Stop Hole Abbey. However, at the time, the Music Box was not for sale as per the store owner, Vladmyr.
