A brooch of shielding was a magical protective device. A full-capacity brooch had a base value of 1,500 gp.

Appearance

A brooch of shielding was a small piece of jewelry used to fasten a cloak or cape. They were often made of silver or gold. Most commonly, brooches of shielding were adorned with Mystra's or Azuth's ''holy symbols.

Powers

A brooch of shielding could harmlessly absorb the force darts from a magic missile spell directed at the wearer. A single brooch could only absorb a set amount of energy before melting.

Notable Owners

Individuals

  • Nayeli Goldflower: a paladin from the city of Daggerford and an adventurer who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
  • Nyral Sunsdottir: a half-elven fighter, wizard and adventurer sometime in the mid 14 century DR, whos fighting style revolved around her sword of dancing.

Organizations

  • Guardians of Daggerford: the guard organization from Daggerford who protected the lives of the city's Dukes. Members of the Guardians wore signature brooches of shielding in the late 15 century DR.
