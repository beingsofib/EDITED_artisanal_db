The Crown of Joy and Tears was a Luruean artifact that was found at El'lahana Raikeil in the Moonwood.

Powers

The crown appeared to be able bring delights to those who placed it on their brows.

History

Prior to the Time of Troubles of 1358 DR, the Knights of the Unicorn explored the Moonwood and entered the castle of El'lahana Raikeil. After they overcame a series of puzzles, they found the crown. Each of the knights interacted with the artifact, and the next thing they knew, they were jumping and dancing alongside unicorns and elves close by a stream of silver. The knights decided to leave the crown in the elven castle and began to wander Faerûn freely once again. The leader of the group, Javalar Roaringhorn, announced that none would be able to find the Crown again unless they entered the fortress with great joy and desires. The goddess herself was pleased with the Knights of the Unicorn, and appeared to them with her Silver Herd. After taking them for a grand ride, they pledged their services to the Unicorn Queen, and she blessed them with many great adventures.
