Chestnut stew

Item
nameChestnut Stew
typeFood

Chestnut stew was a rather simple vegetable dish that had been around in the Icewind Dale region for a long time prior to the late-15 century DR.

Description

This dish consisted of roasted chestnuts, lentils, and bread that was a day old.
