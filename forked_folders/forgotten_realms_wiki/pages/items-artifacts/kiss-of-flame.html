Kiss of Flame was an enchanted golden dagger and a helpful weapon against trolls that was created sometime before the late 14 century DR.

Powers

Kiss of Flame's blade glowed with blazing heat and once it connected with a target, it blazed with flames for a mere moment, searing the target. In addition to the fire magic, the dagger also possessed a simple +1 enchantment that guided the wielder's hand towards the target and increased the weapon's damage.

History

In the , a Kiss of Flame dagger belonged to Silvenius Crim and was available for purchase from Icewind Enchantments, his shop in Targos, one of the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale.
