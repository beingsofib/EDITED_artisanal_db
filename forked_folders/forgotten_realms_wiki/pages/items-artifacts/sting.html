Sting

Item
nameSting
typeMagic short sword

Sting was a magical short sword found in the second level of the catacombs of Temple Darkmoon.

Description

The short sword had a +2 enchantment and was located behind a jammed door to the north of the catacombs. It was found beside the magical axe known as The Bait.
