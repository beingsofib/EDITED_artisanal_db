Darkstorm helm

Item
nameDarkstorm Helm
typeHelmet
originEberron

The darkstorm helm was an example of magical drow headgear found in Eberron, used by their spellcasters.

Description

The 'helm' resembled more a partial open-faced mask that sat around the head, and was made of a dark, glittering metal.

Powers

The darkstorm helm greatly boosted spellcasting related to channeling negative energy, lightning, and poison, and as such was commonly used by drow for both arcane and divine magic. Once per day it could be used to summon up a powerful lightning storm.

History

The helm was recovered after battles between the forces of the Church of the Silver Flame and the drow cultists of the Lords of Dust in Stormreach. The cult attempted to free the Spinner of Shadows from the Spinner's Prison, resulting in Lolth breaking through the Rift Between Worlds and Eberronian adventurers finding Faerûn.
