The Fist of Velsharoon was a macabre enchanted mace that was sacred to the Church of Velsharoon.

Description

The mace was crafted to resemble an emaciated arm grabbing a ribbed sphere.

Powers

When wielded by an individual whose heart wasn't evil, the mace behaved as an ordinary mace; only those of black hearts could make its magic operate. It had even more powers when wielded by a priest of Velsharoon, with its combat enchantments growing stronger, and the mace doubling the duration and impact of lich touch spells. Furthermore, those slain by a blow from the mace rose as zombies under the priest's command, and it was impossible to raise or resurrect them without anything short of a wish.
