Skie's locks and bolts

Item
nameSkie's Locks and Bolts
typeLock
value2e5,000 gp

Skie's locks and bolts was a magic item that wizard locked everything nearby.

Description

This item looked like a small ornate lock and silver key.

Powers

When the key was inserted into the lock and turned all doors, windows, and related objects within would slam shut and become wizard locked. These items would remained locked until successfully dispelled, the key was twisted again, or Skie's locks and bolts was moved further than 50 feet away.

History

Specialty priests of Waukeen called goldeyes had a chance of receiving a Skie's locks and bolts as one of the magic items they were given upon their admission to the clergy.
