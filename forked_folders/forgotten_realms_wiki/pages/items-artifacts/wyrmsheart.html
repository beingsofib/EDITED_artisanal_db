Wyrmsheart

Item
nameWyrmsheart
typeEnchanted longsword

Wyrmsheart was an enchanted longsword that belonged to the moon elf warrior Reluraun.

Powers

This weapon had a +2 enchantment and a bane quality geared towards dragons.

History

After Reluraun was buried within his tomb, Wyrmsheart was placed inside in the hands of an extremely lifelike effigy of the warrior.
