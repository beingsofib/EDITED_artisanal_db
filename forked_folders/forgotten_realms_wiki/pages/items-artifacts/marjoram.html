Marjoram was a common type of herb seasoning found in the Realms.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold majoram at a price of 5 copper pieces per in its "Aurora's Larder" section.
  • Marjoram could be found for sale in the markets of Waterdeep.

Notable Marjoram Products

  • Powdered marjoram was commonly used in the elven dish Vaervren to season vegetables.

Notable Users

  • The Painted Boat, a restaurant in the Dragon Reach off the southern edge of the Harbor District of Ravens Bluff, commonly kept majoram stocked in the kitchen's drying rack.
