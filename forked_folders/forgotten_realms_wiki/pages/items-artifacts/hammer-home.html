Hammer Home was an enchanted warhammer, forged out of cold iron and blessed by a cleric of Lathander.

Powers

Hammer Home was specially forged and enchanted in dawn's first light to give it powers against creatures from Outer Planes. It possessed a +1 enchantment, but when wielded against extraplanar targets, the weapon effectively became a +2 warhammer that scorched its targets with Lathander's divine magic.

History

This enchanted warhammer's history was largely mysterious. In 1372 DR, Hammer Home was in the hands of the Gillian's Hill Gang, a group of bandits who took over the fire cairn tower on Gillian's Hill's summit and menaced the locals and traveling merchants.
