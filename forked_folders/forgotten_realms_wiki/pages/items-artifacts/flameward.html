Flameward was magical club found in Eberron during the mid-1480s DR of Faerûn.

Description

The head of the club was shaped like the neck and head of a gold dragon.

Powers

The club gave its wielder strong resistance to fire, and also gave them a confident and glowing personality.

History

The club was first held by someone who sought its fire protection, but it still ended up in the possession of a powerful fire reaver that lurked inside the magma pits of Khyber near the Spinner's Prison.
