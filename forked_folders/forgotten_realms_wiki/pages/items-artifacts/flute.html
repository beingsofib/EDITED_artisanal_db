Flutes were common and portable musical instruments. They were often favored by bards.

Availability

  • Black Dugal's Music Shoppe in the city of Ravens Bluff was known to sell flutes.
  • Several members of the Waterdhavian noble house Majarra produced instruments, including flutes and longhorns.

Varieties

  • Birdpipes
  • Fipple pipe, a variety of flute found in the kingdom of Hartsvale. It was held vertically and played by blowing into its end.
  • Longhorn
  • Songhorn

Notable Flutes

  • Fife of blasting, enchanted flutes that produced an effect similar to sound burst.
  • The Merry Shorthorn, a renowned enchanted flute.
  • Ruehar's flute, a magical silver flute that belonged to the wood elf wizard Ruehar and passed on to his descendants.
  • Sorlyn's Ditty, an enchanted flute that was created for Sorlyn Sheerla.

Notable Users of Flutes

Individuals

  • The drow wizard Dostrealt carried a flute and a piccolo.
  • Sorlyn Sheerla, a cleric of the Faerûnian deity of song, Milil.
  • Taran Goldenstar, a wizard in Freedale, often played a flute outside him home.

Nations, Regions, & Settlements

  • Flutes made from reeds were a common instrument on the Malatran Plateau.

Races & Sentient Creatures

  • Banshrae, a dangerous and musically inclined race of fey, always carried a masterwork flute that doubled as a blowgun.

Religions

  • It was common for clerics of Eilistraee to be proficient with flutes, as well as harps and horns.
