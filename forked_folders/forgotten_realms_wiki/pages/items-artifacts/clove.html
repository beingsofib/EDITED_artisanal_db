Cloves were a type of spice in the Realms noted for their aromatic nature.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold cloves across the reaches of Faerûn under its "Aurora's Larder" and "Priests' Alcove" sections.
  • In the Thayan port city of Priador, one could commonly find imported cloves for sale in the Priador Emporium's spice shop.
  • On the island nation of Wa, the port city of Akkaido often received shipments of cloves.
  • Cloves were cultivated in some parts of Zakhara, such as the city-state of Huzuz.

Price

The price of cloves somewhat varied in Faerûn. Whilst Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold cloves for use by priests at a rate of 1 silver piece per , the cloves sold for culinary use under "Aurora's Larder" were priced at 35 gp per .

Over in the land of Zakhara, a chest of of cloves would be valued at 400 gold pieces.

Usage

Food & Drink

  • Across the various higher-class inns, taverns, and other eateries in the Realms, the "First Meat" course in an eveningfeast meal was typically garnished with hard-boiled eggs that had been spiced with cloves.
  • On the Malatran Plateau, cloves were used as a pickling spice, together with allspice, mustard seeds, black peppercorns, red chiles, coriander, ginger, and mace.
  • Bowl of hares, the specialty dish of the Oak-Father's Boon, used cloves in its rich sauce.
  • Fowl stew, a poultry dish from the Singing Sprite inn of Secomber, was flavored with a variety of spices, including a pinch of either cinnamon or ground cloves.
  • Gravia's hot apple cider included two whole cloves as part of its recipe.
  • Roast gammon pie, a Cormyrian dish, called for a finger of ground cloves, or in other words a heap just large enough to stay on a cook's finger.
  • A typical recipe for mulled brew included whole cloves.
  • Cloves were often a spice of choice when making Shoodra sauce.
  • The spiced wine of Calimshan and Tethyr were flavored with a variety of herbs, spices, and fruits, including cloves. A specifically clove flavored wine could be ordered through Aurora's.
  • Stag sausage, a prized meat good produced by Wyrmkindler Sausage Works, called for 1 thruppinch of cloves.
  • Suulvyk, a rare hard-boiled egg in drow cuisine that was traditionally flavored with cloves imported from the World Above.
  • Vilhon Cider was often said to be enhanced by the addition of cloves.

Other

  • Due to their aromatic nature, cloves were sometimes used in incense, scented oils, perfumes, and pipeweed.
  • Cloves had a wide array of medicinal uses, including as anesthetic, disinfectant, germicide, and aiding in circulation.

Trivia

  • In the land of Zakhara, the fragrance-loving great ghuls and sakinas were known to delight in the aroma of cloves.
