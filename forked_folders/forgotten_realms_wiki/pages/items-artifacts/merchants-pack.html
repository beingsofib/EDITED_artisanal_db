Merchants' pack

Item
nameMerchants' Pack

originTulmene, Tethyr

Merchants' pack was a seafood dish served at Tulmene's Tower inn, located in the town of Tulmene in Tethyr.

Description

Tulmene's Tower was one of the busiest and one of the more expensive establishments in town. It was known for the so-called merchants' pack. The dish was fish baked in a hand loaf of bread, stuffed with onions, mushrooms, and spices.
