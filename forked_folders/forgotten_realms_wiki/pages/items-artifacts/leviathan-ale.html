Leviathan ale

Item
nameLeviathan Ale
typeAlcoholic beverage
locationNeverwinter, Sword Coast North

Leviathan ale was the house ale of the Beached Leviathan tavern in Neverwinter. The amber brew was cloudy in color.

Powers

Leviathan ale possessed some invigorating and energizing properties.

History

In the mid-to-late-15 century DR, Leviathan ale was a cheap and popular libation among the adventuring population of Neverwinter. This alcoholic drink was brewed on sight at the Beached Leviathan.
