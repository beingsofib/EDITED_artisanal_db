Dreamhappy was a medicine.

Powers

According to Orthil Voldovan, dreamhappy was a "painquench", an ointment that acted as a local analgesic. The substance also made folk numb and sluggish, and thus unsuited to tasks that required quick reactions or strength.

History

Dreamhappy was part of Orthil Voldovan's caravan's provisions during its trip late in the .
