Stun dart

Item
nameStun dart
typeDart
value40 gp
weight1 lb (450 g)
rules3rd

A stun dart is a hollow dart that contains poison. When it impacts, it pierces and also releases a puff of poison gas that can stun an opponent for up to five minutes.

Stun darts cost 40 gp each and weigh around 1 lb (450 g).
