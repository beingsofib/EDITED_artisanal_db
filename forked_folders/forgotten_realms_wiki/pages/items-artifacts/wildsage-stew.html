Wildsage stew was a simple meal commonly eaten by farmers of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

The stew was a thick mixture into which game from the forests of the Vast were stirred, including cooked chunks of squirrel, boar, porcupine, and black-masked bear. This was flavored with wildsage, It was often eaten in accompaniment with hardbread slathered in bloodlick, and washed down with beer.

A similar dish was wildsage vegetable stew, which was served at The Wizard's Hand inn.
