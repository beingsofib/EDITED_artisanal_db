Strength (armor quality)

Item
nameStrength
typeArmor quality
casterlevel3e12
value3e+8,000/+32,000 gp

Strength was a quality that could be applied to armor, giving its wearer increased strength. Varying degrees of strength enhancement were available for this armor quality.

Creation

The spell bull's strength was used in the enchantment of armor with this quality.
