Sword of Arvoreen

BGitem
nameSword of Arvoreen
typeWeapon

The Sword of Arvoreen was a special short sword given to Mazzy Fentan by Arvoreen himself.

Powers

It could only be used by halflings, and caused struck opponents to slow down while protecting the wielder from slowing and stun effects.
