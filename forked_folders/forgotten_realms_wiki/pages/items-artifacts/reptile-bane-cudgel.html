The reptile bane cudgel was the traditional weapon of the Lannon family of the Mere of Dead Men on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 century DR.

Description

It was a club made of iron.

Powers

The club possessed a +1 enchantment that made the cudgel deadlier and guided the wielder's hand toward the target. Additionally, it enhanced the user's ability to hit lizardfolk.

History

Some time before the , the Lannon family, swamp dwellers living near the village of West Harbor, came into possession of it. When lizardfolk from the swamp attacked, the Lannons were forced to flee their home. There they met a stranger, whom they asked for help. To support the adventurer (if they agreed to help), Pitney Lannon, the father of the family, gave them the reptile bane cudgel. Afterward, Pitney recalled that, as far as he knew, it had never been used and he wasn't sure if it even was magical, as his father had a reputation as a liar.
