Fured Kiuno of the Bear

Fured Kiuno of the Bear was a set of enchanted hide armor made from the fur of Chultan moonbear and used by the adventurers of Chult in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

Fured Kiuno of the Bear gave the wearer a boost to their offensive powers and abilities, increased their health and constitution, defense, and physical strength, and increased their chances of landing especially powerful and lucky attacks. All the powers that were believed to be a result of adorning the fur of a Chultan moonbear.

History

In the late 15 century DR, a strange and powerful polar bear found its way into the jungles outside the lost city of Omu. The beast, known as Polaris, was considered to be an impressive hunter's trophy to claim offered to various adventurers who ventured into Chult's jungles. As a reward for the beast's demise, the adventures were promised a Fured Kiuno of the Bear.
