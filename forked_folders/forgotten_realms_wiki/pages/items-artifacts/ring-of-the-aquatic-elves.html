Rings of the aquatic elves were magical defensive items created out of coral by the sea elves.

Description

Rings of the aquatic elves appeared to be corals smoothed and pressed into a ring shape.

Powers

These rings were used by the sea elves to grant them magical protection instead of wearing heavy armor underwater. The enchanted coral jewelry was used by aquatic elves both in war and in everyday life.

History

After the Wailing Death of the , these rings could be found in the places along the Sword Coast and islands of the Sea of Swords frequented by pirates and sailors.
