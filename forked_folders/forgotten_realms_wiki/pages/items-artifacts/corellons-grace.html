Corellon's Grace was the name given to a magical staff of elven creation that offered significant protection to its wielder.

Description

The staff was crafted to be light and easy to maneuver in its wielders hands. It was inscribed with arcane sigils of mithral, markings that helped channel the staff's magical properties.

Powers

If the wielder of the staff chose not to encumber themselves with any form of armor, they were granted extraordinary instincts and enhanced inherent defenses. Their physical reflexes, intellectual fortitude, physical constitution, and other capabilities were all enhanced when faced with incoming danger or potential harm.

Notable Owners

  • Auntie Ethel, a hag from the putrid bog of the Sword Coast that somehow came into possession of the staff.
