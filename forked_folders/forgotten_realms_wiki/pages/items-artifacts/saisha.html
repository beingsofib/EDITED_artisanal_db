Saisha, or hammerlock, was a paralyzing ingestion poison used by Zhentarim in the 14 century DR.

Effects

The effects of saisha were such that soon after ingestion, the target became incapable of moving, with their joints tightly locked up; only the lungs and eyes could move, while the jaw hung slack. The joint locks were so effective that they gave the drug the name of "hammerlock", referencing the fact that joints had to be broken with a hammer afterwards. It lasted for about three hours.

Production

It was made out of herbs from Tashalar. The recipe was a secret.

History

Saisha was available to the Zhentarim in the mid-to-late 14 century DR, and in use by Calivar Murpeth on , who employed it on the cook Gustal Sorold, the harlot Talantha, and attempted to use it on the scrivener Maervidal Iloster, all of which he was to kill afterwards.
