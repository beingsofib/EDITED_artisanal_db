A songhorn was a wind musical instrument, similar to a longhorn.

Description

Songhorns were simple recorders made of wood. Their sound was mellower than that of longhorns and fared better on lower ranges.

They were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Notable Users

Individuals

  • Drake Bluenote, a member of the bardic group Adagio Jones and The Goodwinds, played the songhorn for soft songs.
