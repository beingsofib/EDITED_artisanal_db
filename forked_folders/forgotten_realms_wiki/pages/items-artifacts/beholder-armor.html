nameBeholder Armor

materialMetal

Beholder armor was an odd kind of magical device used by beholders to walk around.

Description

The armor very closely resembled full plate mail +1 used by humanoids, but was built specifically for beholders to use. The beholder rested on a seat of sorts atop the shoulders, and was tied in securely with straps and buckles.

Powers

Beholder armor was used by beholders that wished to have a more "hands on" experience in combat. Once attached it allowed the beholder to walk about at a quick pace and use both gauntlet hands to hold items or weapons in addition to their typical beholderish abilities.
