Gloves of the Long Death were enchanted monk gloved used by the monastic Order of the Long Death of Thay.

Powers

Apart from improving the monk's combat prowess and damage dealt by their deadly martial arts, gloves of the Long Death left their targets burnt by magical cold emitting from these items.

History

These gloves were created by the Order of the Long Death, which celebrated change from life to lifelessness in any form. Used in battle, gloves of the Long Death left their opponents feeling like they were touched by death itself, the event that was hard to forget.

In the late 14 century DR, these gloves traveled from Thay and were available in many places along the Sword Coast and the Moonsea region.
