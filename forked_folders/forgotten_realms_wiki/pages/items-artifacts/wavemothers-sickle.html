Wavemother's sickle was an enchanted Umberlant sickle found in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

Wavemother's sickle had a gentle shape that resembled a pitiless crescent moon overlooking a storming sea. The weapon itself resembled an item shaped out of coral or possibly of driftwood. It possessed a +2 enchantment to the damage it dealt and guided the wielder's hand towards the target. Each slash with the sickle could leave bleeding lacerations and harm the targets with magical frost. Additionally, Wavemother's sickle possessed a watery guidance that made all attacks with the sickle highly advantageous against wet targets.

History

As of the , Wavemother's sickle was wielded by Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queen's House in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate.
