A Chondathan steelsword was a broad-bladed, one-handed sword associated with the Chondathan people.

Similar to a well-forged longsword, it was particularly good at breaking other weapons.

As of 1372 DR, they were available in Cormyr, the Dalelands, and Sembia. They were valued at around 315 gp and weighed 5 lb (2.5 kg).
