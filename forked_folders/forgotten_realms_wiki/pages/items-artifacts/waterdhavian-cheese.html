Waterdhavian cheese was pale with holes and came from Waterdeep,

Geography

The cheese was available in most bars in the city and was also commonly eaten by the nobility.

Description

It had a sharp and tangy bite.

Prices

As of 1368 DR, a wheel was around four silver pieces, and a was available for around 35 gold pieces.
