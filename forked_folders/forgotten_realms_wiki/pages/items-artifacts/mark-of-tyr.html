Marks of Tyr were amulets of the Tyrran faith in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Marks of Tyr were round golden disks decorated with Tyr's holy symbol. The gold discs hung off a thong that consisted of multiple segments, tied together.

Powers

A mark of Tyr possessed an enchantment that allowed its owner to compel their opponent to disarm themselves for a short period of time, deterring them from attacking or taking any offensive actions.

Notable Owners

  • Nayeli Goldflower: a paladin from the city of Daggerford and an adventurer who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
