A cauldron of planar travel was a rare and massive enchanted cauldron that had the power to create portals.

Powers

These cauldrons functioned almost identically to a cauldron of gateways. The steps to activating it included bringing fresh water to a steaming boil inside of it, then put in some soil from the desired destination, and finally uttering the command words "I demand a path". Once all steps were carried out a translucent image of the location where the soil originated would manifest in in the steam arising from the cauldron, with he steam acting as a portal. The steam would remain active for around twenty minutes to as long as two bells.

This effect could only be activated once per week. The cauldron was unable to be transported through its own portal. And if an individual were to step through the steam as it was deactivating, there was a chance their body could be bisected.

Where it differed from a cauldron of gateways was in it not being limited to opening portals to locations on the same plane of existence. A cauldron of planar travel could open portals to any plane in the multiverse, so long as soil from the desired plane of existence was placed inside.
