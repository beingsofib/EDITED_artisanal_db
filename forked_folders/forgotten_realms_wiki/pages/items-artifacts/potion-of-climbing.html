Potions of climbing were magical potions that temporarily allowed the user to rapidly climb steep surfaces.

Description

The liquid that composed a potion of climbing appeared to be separated into several layers of gray, silver, and brown colors that resembled bands of sedimentary rock. Agitating the liquid did not mix the layers.

Powers

Upon drinking a potion of climbing, the user gained the power to climb as fast and as effortlessly as walking, for a duration of one hour. Even extremely difficult surfaces became considerably easier to climb while under the effect of the potion.

History

In the late 15 century DR, potions of climbing were sold at Corellon's Crown, an herbalism shop in Trollskull Alley in the North Ward of Waterdeep, run by the wood elf Fala Lefaliir.
