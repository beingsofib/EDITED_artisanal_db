The outfit of sunlight was a fine set of clothes found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The clothes were imbued with the energy of the sun, and helped protect the wearer from physical threats while emboldening their own strikes to be more accurate and powerful.

History

The outfit was found in the House of Song when the Elves of Cormanthor searched for the lost bard Zelliira.
