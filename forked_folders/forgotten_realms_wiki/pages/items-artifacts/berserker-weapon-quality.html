Berserker was a quality that increased the enchantment level of a weapon wielded by someone in a rage. They proved quite useful to barbarians and as expected berserkers.

Crafting

Weapons with this quality were crafted by the vremyonni of Rashemen. The spells involved crafting a berserker weapon were either divine power, rage, or emotion.
