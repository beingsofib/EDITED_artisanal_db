Magic eating was a special quality that could be applied to armor that offered the wearer protection from magic.

Description

Armors enchanted with the magic eating property were often crafted with spiraling decorations and the depiction of many-fanged mouths.

Powers

Magic eating armor offered moderate degree of spell resistance, and could absorb the energy from spells cast directly at the wearer. It even bolstered their lifeforce for a short while.

Creation

Armor with this property were crafted using the spells Simbul's synostodweomer and spell resistance.
