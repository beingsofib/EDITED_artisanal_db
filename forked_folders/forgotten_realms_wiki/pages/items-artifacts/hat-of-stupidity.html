Hat of stupidity

Item
name Hat of Stupidity
typeCursed headwear
value2e1,000 gp

A hat of stupidity was a magical hat that cursed its wearer to become quite dumb.

Description

A hat of stupidity looked just like any other magical hat. Any and all attempts to divine its true nature would fail, still seeming like a beneficial magic hat.

Powers

Once placed upon a head, the hat would curse the wearer with stupidity such that they thought themselves smart, and preferred to wear the hat while thinking, casting spells, or doing any other sort of mental work.
Only with the use of a remove curse spell could the wearer ever be free from the hat's effect.

History

In the a coach traveling from The Way Inn to Waterdeep carried, amongst varied and sundry contents, a hat of stupidity.

Notable Owners

  • Maddgoth was forced to wear a hat of stupidity by the faerie dragon Ottolenszillasla, which made him even more arrogant about his intellect as part of its curse.
