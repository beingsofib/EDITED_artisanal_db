Glowrod

Item
nameGlowrod
typeRod
locationCormyr, Interior Faerûn
rules2nd

A glowrod was a small metal rod that glowed with bright light.

Description

Most glowrods were made of steel and had a cover to block their light when not in use.

Powers

The bright light from a glowrod was normally yellow-white in color.

History

Vangerdahast knew how to create a glowrod.
