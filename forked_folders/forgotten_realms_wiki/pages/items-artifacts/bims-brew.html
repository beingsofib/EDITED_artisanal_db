Bim's Brew was an alcoholic beverage, a variety of hard apple cider created by Pavel of Neverwinter, the owner of Luskan Arms in Targos in the late 14 century DR.

History

Bim's Brew was created in the by Pavel, soon after he purchased the Luskan Arms inn. The brew was created in inspiration after making apple cider on Pavel's family farm and named after Pavel's twin brother, Bim, who died in the attack on the Neverwinter Academy of the .
