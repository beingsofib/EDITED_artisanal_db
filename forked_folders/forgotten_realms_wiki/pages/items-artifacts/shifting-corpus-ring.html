A shifting corpus ring was an enchanted ring that could hide and obscure its wearer.

Description

Shifting corpus ring was a simple polished metallic band designed to look positively common, like something worn by military scouts and pickpockets.

Powers

Through the ring, its wearer gained the ability to cast the invisibility and blur spells, once per day each.

History

In the , the Flaming Fist traitor named Marcus wore a shifting corpus ring. After his death at the hands of the True Souls of Baldur's Gate, who stood against the Cult of the Absolute, Marcus's shifting corpus ring changed owners.
