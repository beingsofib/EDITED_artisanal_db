Yeti-bone hammer was an enchanted maul that belonged to Raika a Reghedmen barbarian adventurer active in the late 15 century DR.

Description

This oversized bludgeon was affixed to a strong yeti bone and reinforced with metal bounds.

Powers

This enchanted hammer had a coin-toss chance of landing an avalanche of blows on a group of targets after landing a successful pummel on a single opponent.

Notable Owners

  • Raika: a half-orc barbarian who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
