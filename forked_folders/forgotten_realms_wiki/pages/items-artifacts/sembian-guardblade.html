A Sembian guardblade was a magical rapier produced in Sembia.

Powers

It was a weapon that possessed a +1 enchantment that guided the wielder's hand toward the target and increased the weapon's damage. It also was enchanted with the defending magic that protected the wielder from some damage.

Users

They were often carried by nobles in Sembia, especially those who wielded twin swords, and they were worth around 8000 gold pieces.

Notable Owners

  • Regis, the "Halfling Hero" of Lonelywood owned a Sembian guardblade and made it available for purchase in the late 14 century DR.
  • Thazienne Uskevren, a Sembian noblewoman and a rogue carried her family's guardblade in the mid to late 14 century DR.
