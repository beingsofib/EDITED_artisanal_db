Proof against transmutation

Item
nameProof Against Transmutation
typeArmor quality
casterlevel3e12th
value3e+6

Proof against transmutation was a quality that could be applied to magical armor.

It made its wearer immune to shape-changing transmutation effects such as disintegration, petrification, or polymorphing.

Bestowing it required spell immunity.
