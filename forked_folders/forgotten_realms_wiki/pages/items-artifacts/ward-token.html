Ward token

Item
nameWard Token
typeMagic item

Ward tokens were small objects with magical properties used to bypass magical wards and traps and gain access to secure areas.

Description

Ward tokens usually had a single rune or symbol inscribed into them.

History

Durlag Trollkiller created various ward tokens as a means of navigating his dangerous, trap-filled home, Durlag's Tower. Due to the adventuring popularity of the tower, many fake ward tokens were created by merchants to sell to adventurers. A few of the original ward tokens were in circulation, but they were indistinguishable from the genuine ones.
