Ring of multiple wishes

Item
nameRing of Multiple Wishes
typeRing
value2e25,000 gp

A ring of multiple wishes was a wondrous magic item that granted wishes.

Powers

A ring of multiple wishes held 2–8 wish spells which could be used to make the wearer's wildest dreams come true.

Notable Owners

  • Shal Desanea, a wizard from Cormyr, wore a ring of multiple wishes.
  • King Halvor II wore such a ring.
