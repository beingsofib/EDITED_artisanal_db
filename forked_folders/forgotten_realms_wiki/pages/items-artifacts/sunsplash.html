Sunsplash was one of the more notable dishes from the city of Velen in the nation of Tethyr.

Description

A salad dish of feathery sharpcheese that was served on a bed of charthoor lettuce leaves. The greens were tossed with a dressing of vinegar and mushroom-fern gravy.

History

As of the late 14 century DR, Sunsplash could be found on Blackthorn's Brew's menu, a festhall on the Captain's Cliff in Velen. The dish was mentioned among other notable foods included in Volothamp Geddarm's published guide to Amn and Tethyr.
