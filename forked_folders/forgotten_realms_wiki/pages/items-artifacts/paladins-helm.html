Paladin's helm

Paladin's helms were, as the name suggested, enchanted helmets favored by paladins. They could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Powers

A paladin's helm had a protective enchantment that rewarded wearers who put themselves in danger to save others. If a paladin drew the attention of their foes away from their allies, the helm gave them the benefits of a shield of faith spell.

Notable Owners

  • Nayeli Goldflower, a human paladin of Tyr who worked as an adventurer on behalf of Laeral Silverhand, the Open Lord of Waterdeep, during the late 15 century DR.
