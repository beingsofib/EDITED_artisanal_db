Tightropes were a type of rope in the Realms popular among bards and acrobats who performed balancing acts upon them.

Description

Most tightropes were made from banded steel cables.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold tightropes under its "Bards' Emporium" section at a price of 6 gold pieces. Their tightropes were banded steel cables that came with a thick ring to ensure they would be sufficiently secured when used.
