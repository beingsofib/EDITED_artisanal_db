The envenomed blade was a deadly magical short sword from Faerûn's Underdark that ended up in Eberron, found in the mid-1480s DR.

Description

The short sword was very thin and sharp, letting it slide into gaps between armor.

Powers

The blade was innately imbued with a deadly and paralytic spider venom that relentlessly weakened, crippled, and killed its victims.

History

The envenomed blade was made for the Lolthite drow assassins of Faerûn's Underdark, and it somehow found its way to a Lords of Dust hideout in Eberron that worshiped the Spinner of Shadows, a fragment of Lolth.
