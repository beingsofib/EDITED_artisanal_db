Goggles of the archer

Item
nameGoggles of the Archer
typeGoggles
originMyth Drannor, north Faerûn

The goggles of the archer were a set of magical goggles found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

These goggles helped make the wearer land shots more accurately and more lethally, even helping aim past magical and mundane defenses that would otherwise make those shots miss.

History

The goggles were used by the ancient elven archers of Myth Drannor to improve their accuracy. They were later found in the Burning Library after the Elves of Cormanthor defeated the genie Nepri.
