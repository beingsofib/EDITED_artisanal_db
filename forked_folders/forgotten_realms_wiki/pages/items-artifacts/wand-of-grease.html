Wands of grease were magic wands that stored and unleashed the grease spell on command.

Description

Wands of grease were utterly mundane in appearance because of the material used in their creation. Each wand resembled a knotted piece of driftwood.

Powers

Upon an utterance of its command word, a wand of grease unleashed a single spell stored within. Each wand held a number of charges and disintegrated upon the use of its last spell.

History

One notable creator of wands of grease was the enchantress Elytharra Dinnesmore of Neverwinter who took residence in the town of Targos sometime before the . She started enchanting wands of grease to slow and frustrate any creatures and savage species of the North that occasionally attacked the Ten Towns.
