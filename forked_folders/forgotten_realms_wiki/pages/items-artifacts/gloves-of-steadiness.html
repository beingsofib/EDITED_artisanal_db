Gloves of steadiness

Item
nameGloves of Steadiness
typeGauntlets
locationDwarven Dungeons, Myth Drannor

Gloves of steadiness were enchanted gauntlets created to be used by archers.

Description

A typical pair of gloves of steadiness were gray and striped.

Powers

The magics woven into these gloves steadied the hand, making it significantly easier to land a hit or send an arrow straight to the target. This made gloves of steadiness a valued commodity among archers and snipers.

History

In the , a pair of gloves of steadiness was worn by one of the orogs who were tasked with prying open the Seal of Mythanthor, a magical portal that connected the Halls of Light of Myth Drannor with Speculum Grounds in the City Heights. The orogs were confronted by a band of adventurers known as the Veiled Ones and the pair of gloves of steadiness changed hands.
