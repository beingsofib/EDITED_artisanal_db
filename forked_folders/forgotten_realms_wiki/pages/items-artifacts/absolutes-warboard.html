The Absolute's warboard was a magical shield bearing the mark of the Absolute.

Description

This large shield was made of wood and had several nails tacked onto it. Despite seeming like a simple rustic shield, it was actually enchanted.

Powers

When this shield was equipped and its powers were invoked, the wearer's armor was magically increased as per the shield of faith spell. However, unlike the spell itself, the shield would cause the wielder to remain imbued with magical armor until they rested (instead of just a few minutes). The powers could be reactivated following a short rest.

Notable Owners

  • High Priestess Gut, a True Soul.
