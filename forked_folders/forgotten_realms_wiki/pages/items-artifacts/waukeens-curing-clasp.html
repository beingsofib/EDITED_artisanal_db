Waukeen's curing clasp was an enchanted cloak clasp created by the Church of Waukeen of Ravens Bluff during the goddess's absence from the Realms.

Description

Waukeen's curing clasp was a finely-crafted coin-shaped clasp of pure gold. The front of the item was decorated with Waukeen's profile.

Powers

The wearer of Waukeen's curing clasp could tap into the item's magic to heal themselves or other creatures. The clasp had a limited amount of healing it could produce per day.
