The pharaoh's mace was an enchanted mace of unknown origins found on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The pharaoh's mace possessed a +2 enchantment that made the weapon deadlier and guided the wielder's hand toward the target. It allowed users adept at turning undead, like clerics, to use these powers more often. Its hits could also slay some undead instantaneously.

History

The exact origins of the mace were unknown, or the leaders of Bakar.

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR, one such weapon was found in the town of Port Llast, where it was sold by Haljal to the friends of the Church of Waukeen.
