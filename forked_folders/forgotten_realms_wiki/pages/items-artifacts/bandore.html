Bandores were bass-range musical instruments similar to citterns.

Description

The sound of the bandore was rich and mellow, and it was capable of reaching lower notes than usual citterns which made them good accompanying instruments for quieter voices. Bandores typically had scalloped sides that were said to enrich their sound. They were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Notable bandores

Fochlucan bandore : One of the seven Instruments of the Bards. Magical bandores that contained nature-based magic, as well as other magical powers.

Notable Users

Individuals

  • Bonnie Harper, a member of the bardic group Adagio Jones and The Goodwinds, had a bandore that she played for mock music and songs that involved the group's string ensemble.
  • Cymon the False, a gnome skald with a signature bandore who wandered the realm of Sarifal in the Moonshae Isles during the late 15 century DR.
