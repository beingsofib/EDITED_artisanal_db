A daikyu () was a type of longbow. It was the largest variety of bow in use in Kara-Tur, but was rarely seen outside of that continent.

Description

A daikyu was a large bow with a bottom grip. This allowed the bow to be easily fired from horseback or while kneeling., that anyone under was unable to use it.

Notable Users of Daikyu

Classes

  • Samurais commonly used daikyu.

Races & Sentient Creatures

  • Some hobgoblins were known to fight with daikyu.
  • The daikyu was a common weapon among Ogre magi.
