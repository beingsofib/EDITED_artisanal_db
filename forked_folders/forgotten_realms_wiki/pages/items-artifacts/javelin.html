Javelins were a type of simple, thrown, ranged weapon, similar to a shortspear.

Description

Javelins were light, flexible spears designed primarily as a throwing weapon, They were usually constructed of wood and tapered to a sharp point at one or both ends. Alternatively, they could be tipped with a small metal spearhead. The shaft was much thinner than a spear intended for melee combat.

Weaponology

The javelin was a ranged weapon, intended to be thrown. Javelins were not sturdy enough for most melee combat and if wielded in this way they would perform poorly. The normal maximum range of a javelin was .

Javelins could easily be used on both foot or mountedback.

Classes

Groups

  • Javelins were a commonly used ranged weapon among members of the Anaconda Company.
  • The Black Darts, a military company of tlincalli slaves in the City of Brass, were highly trained in the use of javelins.

Races & Sentient Creatures

  • Javelins were a commonly used weapon among wild elves.

Regions & Settlements

  • In the High Moor, javelins were a preferred weapon for hunting the swift grazing animals that inhabited the region.
  • Javelins were a commonly used weapon on the Malatran Plateau.

Trivia

Javelins were often associated with deities of hunting or lightning.
