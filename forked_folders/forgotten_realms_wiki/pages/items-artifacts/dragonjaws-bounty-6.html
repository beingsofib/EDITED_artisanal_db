The Dragonjaws' Bounty was a mercantile ship from either east or northeast Faerûn that operated in the Sea of Fallen Stars around 1370 DR.

Activities

This ship primarily dealt in the hauling and trading of ore.

Reputation

The Dragonjaws' Bounty had a somewhat disreputation, being described by others as "rag-tag."
