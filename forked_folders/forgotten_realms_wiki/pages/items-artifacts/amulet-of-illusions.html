Amulet of illusions

Item
nameAmulet of Illusions
typeMagic item
rarity5eCommon

An amulet of illusions was a magic amulet that could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

The amulet was green and had decorations that looked like eyes.

Powers

When the wearer of an amulet of illusions was in danger, it would automatically turn them invisible.

Notable Owners

  • Farideh, a tiefling warlock who worked as an adventurer on behalf of Laeral Silverhand, the Open Lord of Waterdeep, during the late 15 century DR.
