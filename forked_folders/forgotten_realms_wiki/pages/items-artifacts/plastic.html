Plastic

Substance
namePlastic
locationsLantan

Plastic was a peculiar manufactured substance found on the island of Lantan in the 15 century DR.

Availability

Crude plastics were pioneered, designed, and used by the Gondar population of Lantan. The recipes for their creation were closely-guarded secrets held by their creator's families, alongside other production methods for substances such as concrete.
