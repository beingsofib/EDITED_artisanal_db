Reflective shields were enchanted shields that offered protection from elements, popularized in the 15 century DR.

Powers

Reflective shields sheltered the wearer against mundane and magical fire and cold, as well as absorbed additional harm when the wielder of the shield was severely injured.

History

In the 15 century DR, various reflective shields could be found among bandits and heroes of the Sword Mountains, the city of Neverwinter, its environs, the town of Helm's Hold, and ruins of Illefarn.
