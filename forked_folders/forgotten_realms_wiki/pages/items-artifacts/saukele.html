Saukele

Item
nameSaukele
typeClothing
locationthe Hordelands, northeast Faerûn

Saukeles were a type of clothing worn by some members of the tribes of the Hordelands. These tall, conical hats were only worn by unmarried women. During festivals, they were decorated with scarves, silver, and beads.
