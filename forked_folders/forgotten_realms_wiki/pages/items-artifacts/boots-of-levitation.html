Boots of levitation were enchanted footwear that granted the wearer the ability levitate.

Description

These soft boots would expand or contract to fit the wearers feet, from halfling small to giant big.

Powers

When worn, these boots granted the ability to gently climb or descend through air in a vertical movement. There was no limit to the altitude that could be reached while wearing boots of levitation. The maximum carrying capacity of these boots varied.

Notable Owners

  • Haedyll, a cleric of Eldath wore a pair.
  • Qirmiz min Hudid, an agent of the Brotherhood of the True Flame in Huzuz, wore a pair of slippers of levitation.
