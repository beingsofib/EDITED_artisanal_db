A jitte () was a weapon unique to the continent of Kara-Tur.

Description

The jitte was a defensive weapon and lacked a sharp edge. It was a tapered iron bar between in length that incorporated a short hook near its handle. The jitte's main purpose revolved around blocking incoming attacks and seizing weapons. Additionally, it could be utilized for delivering impactful strikes.

Notable Users

Classes

  • Monks were known to utilize the jitte.
  • Shugenja were known to utilize the jitte.
