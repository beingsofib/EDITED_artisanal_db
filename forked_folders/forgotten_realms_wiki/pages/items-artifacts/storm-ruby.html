Storm Ruby was a variety of dessert fortified wine created by Thamalon Uskevren, of the noble Uskevren family of Selgaunt, Sembia, in the 14 century DR.

Description

Storm Ruby was a sweet, tart, and full-bodied wine.

History

By the mid–14 century DR, Thamalon's love for winemaking paid off. He created Storm Ruby, Usk Fine Old, and Thamalon's Own wines. Storm Ruby was in production in the or earlier. In the , Thamalon Uskevren sought to trade with Nuldrevyn Talendar of House Talendar, discussing a deal to ship Storm Ruby and other Uskevren wines to southern Amn, Tethyr, and Calimshan, along with other Sembian trade goods. After tasting Storm Ruby, Nuldrevyn Talendar appreciated its excellent taste.
