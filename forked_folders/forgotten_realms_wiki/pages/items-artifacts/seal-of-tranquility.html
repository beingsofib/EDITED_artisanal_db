Seal of tranquility

Item
nameSeal of Tranquility
typeMagic item
creatorLaeral Silverhand

The seal of tranquility was a unique enchanted disk crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Joppa in the late 15 century DR. It was one of many pieces of so-called "Silverhand gear" crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep.

Description

Silverhand crafted the seal to represent Joppa's monkish discipline.

Powers

The disk was light enough to float, and could neutralize rough waters.

In combat, it had a similarly calming effect on the spirits of Joppa's enemies, making them much more likely to make mistakes.

Notable Owners

  • Joppa, a human monk to whom Silverhand also gifted the harpoon of sudden stillness.
