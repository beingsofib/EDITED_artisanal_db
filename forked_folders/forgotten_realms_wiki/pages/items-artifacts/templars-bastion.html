Templar's bastion was a kind of mithral plate armor found in Eberron, used by the Templars of the Church of the Silver Flame.

Description

The armor covered the body with a mixture of plate and chain.

Powers

Templar's bastion armor was enhanced with extra protection against both physical and magical threats, though unholy weapons cut through it more easily. Those that struck the wearer were hit with blast of divine light in retribution.

History

These suits were said to have been blessed by Tira Miron, and were worn by Templars Silver Flame. This armor was awarded to adventurers who fought the Overlord Spinner of Shadows before her escape from the Spinner's Prison.
