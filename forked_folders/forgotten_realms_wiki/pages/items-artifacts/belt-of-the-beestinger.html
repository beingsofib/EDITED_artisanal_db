The belt of the Beestinger was an enchanted monk's belt that belonged to the famed hin adventurer and a member of Acquisitions Incorporated Rosie Beestinger in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

Rosie's belt of the Beestinger had an empowering effect on its wielder and helped their blows land unerringly.
