The Jansen AdventureWear, or the Jansen-Brand Metal-Repellent AdventureWear, was a unique set of armor that was invented and worn by the gnome inventor Jan Jansen.

Description

The AdventureWear was made from a flexible yet strong material that was not native to Amn. Its appearance was objectively stylish.

It could be cleaned by dry-clean methods only.

Powers

The armor protected Jan from projectiles and partially limited the physical harm inflicted upon him. It did not restrict his spellcasting abilities in any way.

History

As of the , the patent for the AdventureWear was still pending.
