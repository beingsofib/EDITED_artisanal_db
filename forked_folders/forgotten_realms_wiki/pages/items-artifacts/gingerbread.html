Gingerbread was a cake-like bread made with ginger from Shou Lung. It was available for purchase in Faerûn through Aurora's Emporium.

Description

The use of ginger gave a very distinctive flavor to this bread. It was suggested that gingerbread be served topped with whipped cream or rum sauce.

When purchased from Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue, gingerbread came in a reusable tin, whose lid was adorned with Aurora's company logo.
