A boomerang dagger was an enchanted throwing dagger that returned to the owner's hand once thrown.

Powers

These daggers possessed +2 enchantments. They could be wielded as a normal dagger or thrown at a distance of up to 100 feet (30.5 meters).

History

The affluent halfling Fullen Woolspinner commissioned the creation of these daggers. He was inspired by the dwarven throwing axes that famously returned to their owner when thrown in battle.

Notable Owners

The mercenary captain Dennis from Riatavin owned a boomerang dagger.
