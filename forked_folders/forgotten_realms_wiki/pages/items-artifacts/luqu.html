A luqu was a type of trident used by Angulutiun and Iulutiun hunters.

Description

A luqu was a slender trident, measuring at least in length and about in diameter. It was constructed by securing segments of bone or wood together with lashings. Each of the trident's tines was adorned with sharp, curved barbs. The luqu was specifically designed for spearing fish.
