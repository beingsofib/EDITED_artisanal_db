Amulet of the makers

Item
nameAmulet of the Makers
typeAmulet
locationTethyamar, Buried Realms

The amulet of the makers was a type of magical necklace found in the ruins of Tethyamar in the mid-1480s DR.

Powers

These amulets gave the wearer a heightened skill with smithing and crafting, and gave them a desire to create works of art with their skills.

History

These necklaces were typically worn by master dwarven blacksmiths. One of them was later found in Ironvale Deep during fighting between the Tethyamar Mining Outpost and House Argith.
