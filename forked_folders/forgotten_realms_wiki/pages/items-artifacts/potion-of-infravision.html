Potions of infravision were a type of potion that allowed the imbiber see in the dark.

Description

These potions had a brackish taste.

Powers

This potion temporarily gave the imbiber the ability to see in infravision. A human who took this potion would be capable of seeing in infravision with a range. If the potion was taken by a creature who had natural infravision abilities, their sight range increased by .

Creation

An elixir of darkvision could be created by combining the alchemically-extracted essence of crystalline lenses and certain types of salts.

Notable Users

  • Kwanza Maru brewed such potions for members of his mercenary company, the Anaconda.
