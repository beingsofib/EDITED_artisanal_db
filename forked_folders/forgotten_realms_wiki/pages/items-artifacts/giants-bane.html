Giant's bane

Item
nameGiant's Bane
typeBullet
locationNeverwinter, Sword Coast

Giant's bane were enchanted sling bullets created for defense against giants.

Powers

Giant's bane bullets were enchanted to deal additional damage when used against giants.

History

Giant's bane ammunition was created by halflings, defending their homes from giant raids. Over the years, these bullets were the result of many giants' demise. By late 14 century DR, giant's bane bullets were quite often encountered across the Sword Coast as well as the Moonsea regions of Faerûn.
