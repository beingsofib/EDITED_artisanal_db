Fury of the Flame was magical maul found in Eberron during the mid-1480s DR of Faerûn.

Description

The adamantine head of the maul glowed with heat.

Powers

The great hammer struck with immense physical force that stunned and broke those it hit, as well as burning them with both fiery and radiant divine energy.

History

The maul was commissioned by the Church of the Silver Flame and imbued with blazing divine fire. It somehow ended up in the lair of the hellhound Flamefang, who laired in Khyber outside the Spinner's Prison.
