The docent of sunlight was a magical docent-like item found in Myth Drannor in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The docent filled a warforged with the radiance of the sun, making them much more resilient in battle, and made them more adept with weapons.

History

The docent of sunlight was found after agents of the Cormanthor Elves found a lost Temple to Moander hidden in the ruins. They sent a team in to explore and safeguard the site, destroying the works of the church of Moander found within.
