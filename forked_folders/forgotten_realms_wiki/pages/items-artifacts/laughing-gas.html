Laughing gas

Substance
nameLaughing Gas
typeGas
colorGreen

Laughing gas was a substance that, when inhaled, caused the person to contract cackle fever.

Description

The gas was green, thick, and easily noticeable.

Effects

Upon inhalation the individual would contract cackle fever for a short time, making them go through an uncontrollable fit of laughter.

History

In the late 15 century DR, it is possible that the adventurers who slew Orn the fire giant encountered a room filled with laughing gas. Upon exposure, one of the adventurers contracted cackle fever.
