Jumping

Item
nameJumping
typeWeapon quality
casterlevel3e5
value3e+13,750 gp

Jumping was a quality that could be applied to weapons. When grasped, it acted like a ring of feather falling, and once every twelve seconds, the wielder could gain the ability to make an extremely powerful jump, with no limit on distance.

Creation

To forge a jumping weapon, a spellcaster must have jump and mage hand spells available.
