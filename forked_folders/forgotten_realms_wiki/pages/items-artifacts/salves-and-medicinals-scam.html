Salves and medicinals (scam)

Item
nameSalves and Medicinals
locationToril

Scam salves and medicinals referred to a variety of items in the Realms used to cheat others out of money.

Description

These items were available in a variety of containers.

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Notable Users

  • Scam salves and medicinals were often used by thieves or rogues to take advantage of those in need of medical attention. A skill thief could get up to 10 gold pieces per bottle.
