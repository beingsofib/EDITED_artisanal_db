Beakers were glass containers that were used to manipulate liquids and powders. They were typically used in laboratories or by itinerant alchemists to conduct experiments.

Description

Though generally made of glass, some beakers were made of crystal, ceramic, or even lightly hammered metal. They were used to heat liquids, combine solutions, and dissolve watery compounds.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold beakers in a variety of different sizes, along with vials and other laboratory glass containers, all of which were produced by the glass-blowers guild of Urmlaspyr.

Notable Beakers

  • Beaker of plentiful potions

Notable Users of Beakers

  • Beakers of several different sizes were essential in wizards' laboratories.
  • Beakers were an integral components of alchemist's supplies.
