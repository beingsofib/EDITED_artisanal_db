Dragon's bite (beverage)

Item
nameDragon's Bite
typeAlcoholic beverage
locationFaerûn

Dragon's bite was a mixed alcoholic beverage that was served in inns and taverns across the Realms during the 14 century DR, such as the Hidden Lady.

Description

A typical dragon's bite was made using pale translucent green mint wine, pepper brandy, cinnamon powder, and licorice root.

The resulting mixed drink was minty-cool, with a licorice aftertaste. Dragon's bite was not sweet nor bitter. It ended with the "bite", hot spicy cinnamon burn at the back of the drinker's throat.
