A brazier of commanding fire elementals was a powerful magic item that could summon and control fire elementals.

Description

Braziers of commanding fire elementals were small golden containers that were filled with smoldering coals. In order to activate the brazier's powers, the owner needed to simply stir the embers.

Uses

This item could only be used by flame mages and holy slayers of the Flamedeath Fellowship.

History

A brazier of commanding fire elementals was located within the Mosque of Kossuth in the fortress of Krak al-Niraan in the Weeping Desert in 1367 DR.

Braziers of commanding fire elementals sometimes could be found in the rich markets of Hillsfar in the Moonsea region.

Notable Users

  • Marcos Flass, a Red Wizard of Thay who specialized in golemcraft and creation of gemstone golems used in Thay the late 14 century DR.
