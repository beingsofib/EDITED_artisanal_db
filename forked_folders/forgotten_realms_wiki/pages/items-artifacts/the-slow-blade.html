The Slow Blade was an enchanted dagger of unknown origins found on the Sword Coast North in the late 14th century DR.

Powers

The blade possessed a +3 enchantment that made it deadlier and guided it toward the wielder's target. On every hit it had a chance to poison the target.

History

The exact origins of the weapon were unknown. In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR, one such dagger was found in the Blacklake District of Neverwinter, sold by Dayne Lynneth.
