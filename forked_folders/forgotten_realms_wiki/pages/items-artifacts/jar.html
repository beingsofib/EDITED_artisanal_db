Jar was a general term for a style of cylindrical container in the Realms that came in a variety of sizes.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 8 copper pieces to 3 gold pieces depending on the size.

Usages

  • Jars made of ceramic were used across Faerûn as receptacles to store common table wine. Notably, they were known for a satisfying uncorking sound that grew more pleasing the emptier the jar was. Large ceramic jars were used in wine-making in Muluk, a city in Zakhara.
  • Though jars of all types were certainly common household items in the Realms, wizards often used them to store specific ingredients or material components.
  • Through the use of special rituals and alchemical preservative fluids, necromancers could use glass jars to preserve the mind of a creature into a form of undead known as a brain in a jar.
  • Wax-sealed jars could be used to store pickled foods and preserves, such as honeyed figs, whole lemons, and eggs.

Varieties

  • Amphora, a type of jar typically used to hold wine.
  • Canopic jar, a type of jar used to store preserved internal organs.

Notable Jars

  • Jar of preserving, enchanted glass jars that could preserve any type of material indefinitely.

Notable Users

  • Jars were favored by wizards, who often had dozens within their laboratories.
