Yogurt was a fermented milk product, often made alongside cheeses and curds in several regions of Toril.

Usage

In Calimshan, Yogurt was used to served minced pigeon hearts in lambs' bones.

Goat's milk yogurt was a common type of produce in various lands of Zakhara, including Jumlat and the Pearl Cities.

In the secluded Malatran Plateau, some chefs of the Saiyama tribe served succulent roasted goat rubbed with sage, roasted tubers, and goat's milk yogurt.
