Efreeti Firewater was a variety of magical spirits available in the city of Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.

History

The enchanted beverage was on Yawning Portal tavern's menu in Waterdeep with its bottle stashed away in Durnan's cabinet. One serving of Efreeti Firewater was priced at ten gold coins.
