The Protector of the Second was a suit of leather armor that was crafted for, as well as named after, the elven ranger Indeera Lakhan.

Powers

The Protector possessed a +2 enchantment.

History

Indeera Lakhan was the second-in-command leader of the Mist of Shadows, an elite squad of elven rangers that hailed from the forests of Cormanthor. While it was unknown how the armor was seperated from its owner, it came into the possession of the assassin Maneira some time before the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR.
