Portal sherry was a type of sherry, or fortified wine, found in the Western Heartlands, along the River Chionthar, and the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

This sherry was bottled in bottles of distinct design. The bottle's sides were decorated with large overlapping gateways.

Reputation

It was believed that anyone who consumed the entire bottle of portal sherry would awaken in a new and exciting location.
