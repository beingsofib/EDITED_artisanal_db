Pale Jade was a variety of tea from Shou Lung in Kara-Tur. As its name suggested, it was a pale green tea with a subtle flavor.

It was made available to Faerûnians in the early 1360s DR through the Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue. It was the most popular variety on offer and cost 20 gp per pound (453 grams).
