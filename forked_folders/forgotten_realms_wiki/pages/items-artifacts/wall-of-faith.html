The Wall of Faith was a legendary enchanted paladin shield.

Powers

The Wall of Faith enhanced divine powers of paladins that carried it to battle and granted them impenetrable defense against the powers of evil.

Notable Owners

  • Dragonbait: a saurial paladin and Artus Cimber's companion in the late 15 century DR.
