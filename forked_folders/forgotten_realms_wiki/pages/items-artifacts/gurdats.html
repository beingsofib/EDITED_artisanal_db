Gurdats

Item
nameGurdats
typeFood
locationA Pair of Black Antlers, Elturel

Gurdats was a dish made in Faerûn. They were pan-fried mushrooms with a melted cheese batter and spiced with pepper.

History

Gurdats were sold at the A Pair of Black Antlers tavern in Elturel around 1366 DR
