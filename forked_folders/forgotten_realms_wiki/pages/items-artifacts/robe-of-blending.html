A robe of blending was a wondrous item that allowed its wearer to blend in, either by appearing as part of a wall or plant, or by appearing to be a creature of the wearer's choice.

Description

A robe of blending appeared as a completely ordinary robe and could not be detected by any form of magic. However, the moment someone put the robe on they would realize that the robe had special properties and would gain an innate understanding of how to use it.

Powers

The robe could be used to hide, either by being near-invisible or by almost perfectly mimicking any race or gender the wearer wanted. Only those of exceptional intelligence had a minuscule chance of noticing something wrong when looking at the wearer.

History

The god Hermes would on occasion create robes of blending for those he wished to reward.

Notable Owners

  • Aurin, a businessman and conjurer who lived in Skullport in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
  • Karlott the Shaman, a secretive priest of a god unmentioned.
  • Priamon Rakesk, a lich and member of the Twist-ed Rune cabal.
  • Toraga, a samurai who wandered the lands sometime in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
  • Trelia Mornmay, a druid keeping the balance in the Shalhoond.
  • Wesclodat, a cleric of Deneir owned one of these robes.
