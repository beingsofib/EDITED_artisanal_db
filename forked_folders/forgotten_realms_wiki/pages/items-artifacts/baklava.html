Baklava was a kind of sweet dessert found in the Realms.

Description

The dish consisted of a stack of parchment-thin dough layers carefully wrapped around a filling of chopped nuts and honey.

Trivia

Baklavas were also known of (and eaten) in the world of Mystara.
