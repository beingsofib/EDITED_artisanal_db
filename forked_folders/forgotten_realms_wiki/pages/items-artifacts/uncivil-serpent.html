The Uncivil Serpent was a pirate ship that sailed the seas of Faerûn during the late 15 century DR. The ship's reputation was preceded by that of its captain, Lekard "Dead-Eye" Cadavrus, a foul-smelling pirate who was motivated by revenge.

History

Originally captained by Murosko Sessprin, the Serpent experienced a mutiny when its first mate, "Dead-Eye" Cadavrus, took over control of the ship circa the .

After it was discovered that Rhonda Thunderbell stole from the rest of the crew, the Serpent tracked her to Baldur's Gate on the Sword Coast. The ship docked at Gray Harbor while the crew searched the city's bars and taverns.
