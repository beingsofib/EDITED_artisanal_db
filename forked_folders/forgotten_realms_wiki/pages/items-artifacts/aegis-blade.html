An aegis blade was an enchanted sword meant to be wielded by a swordmage.

Description

Any heavy or light blade could be enchanted to become an aegis blade.

Powers

Aegis blades were magically precise and sharp weapons, especially when a target was struck in a vital area. When wielded by a swordmage, it allowed them to utter a word of power to mark all foes within with their aegis of shielding or aegis of assault.

Notable Owners

  • Zaol, a fallen angel of vengeance who dual-wielded an aegis blade longsword and a piercing songblade.
