The frost harpoon was an enchanted spear crafted by the Ulutiun people of the Great Glacier.

Description

The frost harpoon had an inscribing along its haft written in small script. It read: "Until Ulutiu wakes, none will make me sleep."

Powers

Those who touched this polearm, immediately felt a strong sensation of loss as well as a combination of deathless and unending patience. The weapon radiated chill all around it. The weapon possessed a strong +4 enchantment and dealt magical cold damage to its targets. The spear's head was ensorcelled with the keen magics that made it always sharp and capable of dispensing dramatically increased punishment.
