Medium spider venom

Item
nameMedium Spider Venom
typePoison
value3e150 gp per dose

Medium spider venom was a poison extracted from a medium-sized monstrous spider.

Effect

Inflicted via wounding, medium spider venom sapped the strength of a victim, but they could recover from it with time.

Users

A dark stalker operating at the Tomb of Chonis in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR, coated his arrows in medium spider venom.
