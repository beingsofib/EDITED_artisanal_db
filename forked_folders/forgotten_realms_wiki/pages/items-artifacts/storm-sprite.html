The Storm Sprite was a sailing ship that braved the seas of the Dragonmere and Sea of Fallen Stars during the mid–14 century DR. It was captained by the half-orc sea captain named Fowler.

Description

The long cog had sails made of flax.

Defense

The Storm Sprite boasted three ballistae that could be loaded with barbed harpoons, and its crew were armed with boarding axes and spears of their own.

History

While the ship's previous owner was unknown, it was obtained by Captain Fowler by means of theft.

In the , the Harper agent Ruha hired Captain Fowler and the Storm Sprite out of Cormyr, to transport her to town of Pros along the Dragon Coast. During their voyage, Ruha witnessed another ship being attacked at sea by a black dragon, While Ruha was able to use her arcane magic to destroy the creature, the Storm Sprite was splintered apart in the process.
