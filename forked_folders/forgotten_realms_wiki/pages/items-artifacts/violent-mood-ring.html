Violent mood ring

Item
nameViolent Mood Ring
typeRing
locationNeverwinter, Neverwinter Wood, Sword Mountains, The Crags, Sword Coast North

Violent mood rings were enchanted pieces of jewelry that boosted the wearer's spell damage.

Description

These magic rings were set with a single purple gem that flickered is if containing a raging storm within.

Powers

The wearer of a violent mood ring received a boon that boosted the damage dealt with their acid, lightning, and force damage spells.

History

In the late 15 century DR, violent mood rings were sold and used by spellcasters of Neverwinter, including Miloni abd inhabitants of the Neverwinter Wood and Sword Mountains. They were also found in the ruins of Illefarn among the undead controlled by Jack of Thay.
