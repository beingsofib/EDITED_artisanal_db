Bell of Pavlov

Item
nameBell of Pavlov
typeEnchanted bell
value1e3,500 gp

A bell of Pavlov was a type of enchanted bell that when rung caused nearby creatures to salivate uncontrollably.

Description

These bells had the appearance of small, silver handbells.

Powers

When rung a bell of Pavlov affected creatures within a radius, minus the bell ringer themselves. The rate at which the affected salivated impeded their capacity to talk, consequently making it impossible to cast spells that required verbal components.

Additionally, there was a one-in-five chance that creatures affected by a bell of Pavlov would feel compelled to go have a meal, regardless of whatever action they were in the middle of.
