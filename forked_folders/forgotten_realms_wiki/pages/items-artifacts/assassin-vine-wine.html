Assassin vine wine was a wine brewed from the berries of the murderous assassin vine.

Description

The berries were tough and bitter, yet hearty. They made for a heady wine.

History

Five casks of assassin vine wine were kept by the Sharran cultists at the Lost Refuge in the Vast Swamp in the . It was rather cheap.
