Tymora's lucky coin

Item
nameTymora's Lucky Coin
typeWondrous item
locationNeverwinter, Northwest Faerûn

Tymora's lucky coin was an enchanted coin of divine nature granted to adventurers by The Smiling Lady of Good Fortune, goddess Tymora herself.

Description

These coins were adorned with the likeness of Tymora on the obverse side and a shamrock on the reverse side.

Powers

When the coin was flicked, it activated the divine magics of luck within, granting its owner a random beneficial effect. This magic could be tapped into once every ten minutes.
