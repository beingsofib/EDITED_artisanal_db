Hand mirrors were an easily transportable variety of mirror in the Realms that had a variety of uses.

Description

Typical hand mirrors carried by adventurers were usually made of polished steel. Specially constructed ornate hand mirrors made of silvered glass were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue. These special versions had a loop at the handle that allowed them to be attached to belts.

Small mirrors with handles were essential components of thieves' tools.

Powers

A hand mirror was the material component of the wall of light spell.
