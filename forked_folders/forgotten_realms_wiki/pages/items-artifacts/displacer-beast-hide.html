Displacer beast hide was a variety of makeshift cloaks that were just hides of displacer beasts that still retained some of the beast's innate magics.

Description

Creation of displacer beast hide required nothing but a skilled hand and a skinning knife. Pelts of the beasts retained limited amounts of magic, distorting and wrapping light that touched them.

Powers

Once activated, a displacer beast hide released the effects similar to those of the displacement spell, making the wearer that much harder to pinpoint in place and harm in battle. This magical property could be activated once per day.

History

In the , once such a hide belonged to Mintassan, the Sage of Westgate, and was available for purchase from the wizard's curio shop, Mintassan's Mysteris.
