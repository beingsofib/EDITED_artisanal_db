Lunar Crown and an enchanted cleric's circlet from Waterdeep, in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Lunar crowns were metallic silvery circlets of bulky size. They offered protection to the wearer's forehead and with its two side guards, to the wearer's ears. The front of the circlet was decorated with an image of a side-turned crescent.

Powers

Lunar crowns enhanced the wearer's healing magic, turning a simple healing spell into mass cure wounds.

Notable Owners

  • Halbenet Soladonel: a moon elf cleric of Corellon Larethian who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
