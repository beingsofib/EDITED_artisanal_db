Quelaerel

Item
nameQuelaerel
typeSauce
locationLayers 88, 128, and 222 of the Abyss, Calimshan

Quelaerel () was a spicy brown sauce made by boiling threeb, a kind of leech found in jungles and swampy areas of many Abyssal layers, such as Gaping Maw, Slugbed, and Shedaklah.

Description

This thick and fiery delicacy tasted like a lighted tamarind sauce and could greatly improve the taste of meats that had gone bad or fermenting fruits and vegetables. It was prized by Calishite gourmands and was used in other cuisines of Southern Faerûn.
