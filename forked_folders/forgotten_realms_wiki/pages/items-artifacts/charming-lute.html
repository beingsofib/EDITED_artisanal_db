Charming lute was a magical musical instrument from Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Charming lute was a composite lute with a light wooden body. The instrument emanated a purple-hued magical glow.

Powers

The charming lute possessed an enchantment that allowed a bard to cast the dominate monster spell.

Notable Owners

  • Calliope: a bard adventurer who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
