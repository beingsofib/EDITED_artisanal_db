Charqui

Item
nameCharqui
typeFood
locationHordelands, Toril

Charqui was a sun-dried jerky that the tribes of the Hordelands used as a staple of their diet while traveling. Charqui could be made from virtually any kind of meat that was hunted by the inhabitants of the Endless Wastes.
