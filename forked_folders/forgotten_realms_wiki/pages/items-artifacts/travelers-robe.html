A traveler's robe was a type of garment favored by those arcane spellcasters who lived transient lives, moving from one settlement to another.

Powers

This type of robe offered its wearer protection against weapons that pierced the flesh, such as arrows and daggers. It also offered them an extra breath of life when threatened by billowing clouds of noxious gas.
