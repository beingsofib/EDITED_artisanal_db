Mirror masks were enchanted face coverings that protected the wearer from harmful gazes.

Description

Mirror masks were round face masks, decorated with dark lines that radiated from the center point between the masks' eyeholes.

Powers

These enchanted masks granted their wearers significant protection from monsters that used gaze abilities, such as medusas and basilisks. They also shielded the wearer from various spells that affected sight, such as flare, hypnotic pattern, or a vampire's charm. Additionally, these masks reflected the faces of creatures that looked into them, making the wearer appear to have the same face.

Creation

Mirror masks could be created by skilled wondrous item crafters with the use of change self and resistance spells.

Notable Owners

  • Shamoor: one of the high-ranking members of the Cult of the Dragon under Kya Mordrayn's command in 1369 DR.
