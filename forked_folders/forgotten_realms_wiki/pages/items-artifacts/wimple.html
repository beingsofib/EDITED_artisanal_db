Wimple

Item
nameWimple
typeHeadwear

The wimple was a type of headcovering in Faerûn, typically worn by women, and could be considered a female equivalent of a ceremonial coif.

History

In the 14 century DR, wimples were a popular variety of headwear among women in Waterdeep, often worn alongside a veil.

Notable Wearers

  • Fangs of Talona, a variety of specialty priest in the Church of Talona, typically wore a black wimple that covered their face and ears.
  • Priestesses in the Church of Sune sometimes wore an attractive wimple, red in coloration, with a v-shaped crown piece as part of their priestly garments.
