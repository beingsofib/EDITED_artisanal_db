Frost Prince was a piece of enchanted jewelry that possessed offensive ice magic.

Description

The necklace was decorated with dangling sapphire gems, each freezing cold to the touch.

Powers

The wearer of Frost Prince could cast the ice knife spell once per day.

History

In the , Frost Prince could be found locked away in a gilded chest among the remnants of a great battle in the town of Reithwin that was consumed by the shadowcurse in the late 14 century DR.
