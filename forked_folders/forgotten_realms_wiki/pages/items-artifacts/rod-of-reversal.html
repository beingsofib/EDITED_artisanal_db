Rod of reversal

Item
nameRod of Reversal
typeRod
locationNeverwinter, Daggerford, Sword Coast

Rods of reversal were magic rods that proved to be effective and highly sought-after items by spellcasters and those who hunted them.

Powers

Each rod held fifty charges and allowed its wielder to cast dispel magic (three charges) and greater dispel magic (five charges). These wands were effective against friend and foe alike.

History

In the late 14 century DR, rods of reversal gained wide-spread popularity across the Sword Coast. These rods could be in stock of magic goods merchants in cities such as Neverwinter and Daggerford's Sorceller's Encapsulate.
