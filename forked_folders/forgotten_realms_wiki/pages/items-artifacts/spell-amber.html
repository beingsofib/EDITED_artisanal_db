Spell amber

Item
nameSpell Amber
typeSubstance
locationNorthwest Faerûn, Sword Coast North

Spell amber was a magical substance that was formed when a living spell was destroyed.

Description

Shards of spell amber resembled mundane amber or even simple chunks of amber-colored tinted glass.

Powers

Spell amber was a valuable reagent that only the most skilled eyes could distinguish from mundane glass or amber. These shards could be used to restore enchanted lifeforged items to full power.

History

In the late 15 century DR, spell amber fragments could be found scattered across the River District of Neverwinter, during the city's adventurers' and defenders struggle against the rising threat of the Cloaked Ascendancy.
