Screaming was a quality that could be applied to magical weapons.

Description

When active, the weapon made a low and irritating humming noise. It changed to a painful screaming when it struck a target.

Powers

It inflicted sonic damage, even within the area of a silence spell. It had no special effect on creatures with enhanced hearing, but it was especially annoying to them.

If it was applied to ranged weapons, the effects applied to their ammunition.

Creation

It required a shout or sound burst spell to enchant a screaming weapon.
