A samisen () was a stringed instrument common in the land of Wa.

Description

A samisen was similar to a , or lute. It was plucked.

Reputation

Among all the instruments found in Wa, none brought more respect to a musician than the samisen.

Notable Owners

  • Naomi, the favored consort of Benju Matsutomo of Aru.
  • Sono, a household servant of Benju.
