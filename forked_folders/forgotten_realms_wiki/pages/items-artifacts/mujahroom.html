Mujahroom

Item
nameMujahroom
typeAlcoholic beverage
locationDo'dzin Gompa, Tabot

Mujahroom was a potent alcoholic beverage made from figs in Tabot.

The lamas of Do'dzin Gompa brewed mujahroom from the figs that grew at the heart of the monastery compound. They drank it on the eve of Penance Day—penance came the morning after.
