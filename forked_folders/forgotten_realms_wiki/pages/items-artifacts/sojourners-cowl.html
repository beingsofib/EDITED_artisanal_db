The sojourner's cowl was a piece of headgear found in the ruins of Tethyamar during the 1480s DR.

Powers

The cowl made the wearer more cunning and tricksy, and more resistant to tricks and danger in turn.

History

The cowl was once worn by a traveler of the Underdark who underestimated the dangers of the realm. Like many before them, they were overcome by the perils found outside the safety of their home. It was eventually found among the drow forces of Phy'xzar and House Argith, who invaded Tethyamar in search of the jewels of ardor.
