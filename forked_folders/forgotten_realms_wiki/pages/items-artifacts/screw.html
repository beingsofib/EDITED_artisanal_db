Screws and nuts were small but complex metal fasteners rarely found in the Realms.

Availability

Around the 1370s DR, finely-made threaded screws and bolts were treated as "holy secrets of Gond" and used almost exclusively in the temples, furniture, and devices built and controlled by that faith.
