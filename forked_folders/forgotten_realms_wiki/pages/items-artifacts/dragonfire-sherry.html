Dragonfire (sherry)

Item
nameDragonfire
typeAlcohol
locationVelen, Zazesspur

Dragonfire was an expensive sherry exported from the city of Zazesspur in Tethyr.

Description

The alcoholic beverage had a distinctly deep amber color.

History

As of the late 14, Dragonfire sherry could be found in Zazesspur as well as in Morgan's Inn – a large tavern and inn located in the city of Velen. The drink was sold for four silver pieces per bottle. Volothamp Geddarm mentioned the libation in his guide to the Lands of Intrigue as "being worth every coin."
