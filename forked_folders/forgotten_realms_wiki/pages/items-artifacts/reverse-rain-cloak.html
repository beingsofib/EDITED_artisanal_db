Reverse rain cloak was an enchanted cloak of dubious magical properties.

Powers

This clock possessed an enchantment that made its wearer always slightly damp.

History

In the , a reverse rain cloak was displayed among splendorous enchanted rewards for those willing to partake in Akabi's Wheel of Fortune game in the Circus of the Last Days during its stay in the Rivington neighborhood of Baldur's Gate.
