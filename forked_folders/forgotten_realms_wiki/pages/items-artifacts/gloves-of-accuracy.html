Gloves of accuracy

Item
nameGloves of Accuracy
typeMagical gloves
rarity5eRare

Gloves of accuracy were enchanted gloves that made archers more effective in battle. They could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The gloves of accuracy helped to steady an archer's aim, thus increasing their accuracy.

Notable Owners

  • Saarvin, a dragonborn ranger and adventurer possessed a pair of gloves of accuracy while working with Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
