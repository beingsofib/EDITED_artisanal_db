Oxtail soup was a variety of soup found in the Realms, involving the tail of an ox.

Availability

  • This variety of soup could be found in Bloodstone Valley, where Baron Tranth had it prepared at his manor.
  • In some noble houses of Selgaunt in Sembia, oxtail soup was served for eveningfeast, followed by roast beef with carrots and onions, pickled asparagus in a spicy Turmish cream sauce, and fried wheat cakes smothered in jellied pears.
