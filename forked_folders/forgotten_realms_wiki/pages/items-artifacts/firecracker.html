Firecrackers were a small variety of fireworks developed and produced in the nation of Shou Lung.

Description

Like other fireworks, the substance that allowed firecrackers to "pop" were fireseeds, the magically limited kernels of smokepowder.

Availability

  • Some of the Church of Gond's temples produced and sold firecrackers.
  • Shou Lung.

Varities

  • Noisome spirit chasers, enchanted firecrackers that warded off spirits.

Notable Users

  • The Blade Kingdoms imported firecrackers from Shou for use in the Festival of Blades.
