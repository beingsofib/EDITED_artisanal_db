Vidrinath, or Lullaby, was a sword belonging to Tiago Baenre.

Description

Like Orbbcress, the sword was made of translucent glassteel imbued with diamonds. The hilt was a black metal formed like a spider web. Its pommel looked like a spider's head, with two dull emeralds for its eyes.

It was later merged with Drizzt's Twinkle by Catti-brie on the Great Forge in Gauntlgrym, creating a new powerful blade.

Abilities

The sword was imbued with magic by a djinni. It could defeat some magical energy.

History

The legendary weaponsmith Gol'fanin forged Vidrinath in Gauntlgrym for Tiago Baenre in 1463 DR.
