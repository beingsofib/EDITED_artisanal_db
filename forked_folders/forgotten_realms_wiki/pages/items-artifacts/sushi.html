Sushi was a variety of seafood in the Realms, consisting of freshly raw fish served with rice, seaweed, and vegetables.

Availability

  • In Kara-Tur, sushi was popular in Kozakura and in the Hai Yuan Province of Shou Lung, whose people had learned it from the Kozakurans.
  • In Ravens Bluff, one could find sushi being served at Flirin's Sea Morsels.
