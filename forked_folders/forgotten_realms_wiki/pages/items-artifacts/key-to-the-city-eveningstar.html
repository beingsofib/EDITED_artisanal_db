Keys to the city were golden ceremonial keys bestowed by the Purple Dragons upon those who aided Eveningstar. Recipients were also made honorary Purple Dragon Blades.

Powers

Although keys to the city didn't open any doors, they were imbued with the highly specialized muster at Eveningstar spell, which could teleport their holder to Eveningstar's Market Square.

History

As of the , keys to the city were distributed by Dint, a Purple Dragon Battlemaster stationed at Eveningstar.
