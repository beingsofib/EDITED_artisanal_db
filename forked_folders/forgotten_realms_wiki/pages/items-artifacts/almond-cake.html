Almond cakes were a type of sweet baked handfood found in the Western Heartlands, the Sword Coast, and Northwest Faerûn.

Availability

Almond cakes were baked in the City of Splendors, Waterdeep. One of the bakeries that specialized in this sweet treat was Ackrieg's Bakery in the late 14 century DR.

The pastry was consumed as far south as Calimshan and as far east as Thymar, Tymanther.

History

In the late , the Baldur's Mouth Gazette could have published a recipe for the "Almond Cakes of Avernus", the courtesy of a new cafe opened by some of the surviving Elturian refugees, six months after the Absolute crisis' resolution.
