Pizza was a type of baked flatbread commonplace in Baldur's Gate and the Western Heartlands in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Pizza was a type of baked food found throughout the Western Heartlands along the shores of the River Chionthar. It was a savory dish consisting of a flat, round base of dough baked with a topping of no fewer than three different cheeses, typically with added sausage. A pizza usually weighed about and sold for 15 gp as of the late 15 century DR.

History

Apart from Baldurian flatbreads, pizza was a treat found on the world of Earth. The Wizards Three–Elminster, Rautheene, and Mordenkainen, partook in Earth's pizza during their visits to the home of Ed Greenwood. The Old Mage liked his pizza with extra garlic and Ruatheene plotted to spread pralines'n'cream ice cream over the pie.
