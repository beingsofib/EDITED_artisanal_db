Alkaline salt or alkali was a corrosive mineral salt used in a variety of trades.

Availability

  • Alkaline salts were refined from natural water sources and arid soils.
  • Alkaline lakes in Zakhara's deserts such as the Pit of the Ghuls were a source of alkaline salts, bromine salts, and table salt, but were often too remote and dangerous to exploit.
  • Aurora's Emporium sold alkaline salt for 1 sp per .

Usages

  • Alkali was used to neutralize strong vitriols, though was itself corrosive and dangerous when concentrated.
  • Many alchemical recipes used alkali.
  • Alkali was used to make strong red dyes with cinnabar.
  • Certain medicines used alkali, including a treatment for mad monkey fever.
  • Alkali was an ingredient in making a strong putty, along with refined pitch.
  • Enamels were sometimes produced using alkali.
  • The spell airy water could use a small handful of alkaline salts as a material component.
