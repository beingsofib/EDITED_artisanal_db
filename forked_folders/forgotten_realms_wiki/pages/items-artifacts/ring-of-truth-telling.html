A ring of truth telling was an enchanted ring that helped the wearer to detect when someone was lying to them.

Powers

The ring of truth telling did not automatically detect lies (unlike the ring of truth), but instead enhanced the wearer's insight into the honesty of others.

Notable Owners

  • Jarlaxle possessed a ring of truth telling during his visits to Waterdeep the late 15 century DR.
