The Dawn Avenger was a vessel that sailed along the western coast of Faerûn during the late 15 century DR.

Crew

The Dawn Avenger was captained by an aasimar who devoted their life to the god Lathander.

History

In the year before the death curse afflicted the Realms, the Dawn Avenger's captain sailed to Chult, in order to hunt down malicious bands of pirates. Unfortunately, thick plumes of smoke from nearby volcanoes hampered navigation and the ship ran aground.

Only seven members of the crew managed to survive and make it to the shores of the Snout of Omgar. Eventually, they used their lifeboats to transfer over to the mainland jungles of Chult.

The fore section of the Dawn Avenger remained stuck between some rocks on the Snout's coast, while the rest of the ship floated out to sea. The native tortles scavenged everything of value from within its hull.
