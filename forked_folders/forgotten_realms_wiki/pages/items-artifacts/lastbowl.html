Lastbowl was a fruity dessert dish from the city of Velen in the nation of Tethyr.

Description

This dessert was a fruit salad served to finish a meal. It was a mixture of sliced lime, mango, and peach smothered in syrup.

History

As of the late 14 century DR, Lastbowl could be found on Blackthorn's Brew's menu, a festhall on the Captain's Cliff in Velen. The dish was mentioned among other notable foods included in Volothamp Geddarm's published guide to Amn and Tethyr.
