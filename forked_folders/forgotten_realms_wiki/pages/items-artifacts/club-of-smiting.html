Clubs of smiting were enchanted bludgeons created to destroy constructs and found in common use in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

Clubs of smiting bore a simple +1 enchantment that guided the wielder's arm towards the target and increased the weapon's damage output. However, these weapons' true power was in using them to battle constructs. Each successful attack with a club of smiting had a chance of interfering with the magics that animated constructs, instantly and completely destroying them.

History

By the , clubs of smiting could be found in the hands of adventurers in the city of Neverwinter, as well as found in the depths of Undermountain, the massive dungeon underneath the City of Splendors, Waterdeep.
