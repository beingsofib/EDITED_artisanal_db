Seal of fate

Item
nameSeal of Fate
typeSeal
locationZakhara

The Seal of fate was a magical item that could be found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Description

This item appeared as a seal with letters carved deeply into its surface.

Powers

If pressed against someone's palm, the seal would mark them with a magical imprint. The mark was invisible but did radiate magic and could be seen by casting a detect invisibility spell. A person could only be marked once.

With this mark a person could attempt to alter the fate of any outcome to their favor. Each time the seal was used there was a chance that it would disappear forever.
