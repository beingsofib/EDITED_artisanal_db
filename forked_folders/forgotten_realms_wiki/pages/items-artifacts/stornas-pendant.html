Storna's pendant

Item
nameStorna's Pendant
typeNecklace, holy symbol
originWheloon Prison, Cormyr

Storna's pendant was the personal necklace holy symbol of the Sharran priest Storna Harnwood in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The pendant offered a level of magical protection for the wearer and greatly aided divine magic, especially when turning undead. It served as a powerful holy symbol of Shar, but could also be used effectively by followers of other faiths as well.

History

The holy symbol was worn by the priestess Storna Harnwood of the Night Brigade in the Wheloon Prison in Cormyr, until she was slain by the forces of the Riverside Rats.
