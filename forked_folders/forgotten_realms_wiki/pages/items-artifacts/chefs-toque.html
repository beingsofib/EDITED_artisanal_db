Chef's toque

A chef's toque was a variety of hat in the Realms that was commonly worn by chefs, often as part of an ensemble outfit called a "chef's whites."

Description

These hats were white in coloration.

Notable Wearers

Groups

  • The kobold chefs of Xanathar wore such hats.

Individuals

  • Norver
  • Gorstaf, a duergar piemaker from Gracklstugh in the late 15 century DR.
  • Jandar Chergoba, head chef of the Cassalanter family's villa.
  • Zastreva, a cook in the Water Queen's House in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR, wore a chef's toque.

Regions & Settlements

  • Across the Sword Coast these hats were typically worn by only the finest of chefs.
  • Chef's toques were worn by cooks of the Palace of Manniccis in Sumbria, a city-state of the Blade Kingdoms.

Reputation

A chef's toque was widely considered in the Sword Coast to be a great status symbol for chefs.
