Gloves of swimming and climbing, also known as gauntlets of swimming and climbing, were magical gauntlets or gloves that bestowed the wearer with the ability to swim as fast as a triton underwater or as fast as a merman on the surface, and to scale vertical surfaces whether climbing upward or downward. In order for their magic to work both gloves in a pair needed to be worn simultaneously.

Description

These gauntlets appeared normal, but would radiate magic if detected, giving off faint traces of transmutation magic. They fit anyone from a halfling to a large human.

Creation

Creating a pair of gloves of swimming and climbing required a spellcaster with the spells bull's strength and cat's grace.

Notable Owners

  • Charles Frederik LaVerne Blacktree IV, head of house Blacktree, used a set of gauntlets of swimming and climbing when out hunting and fishing, and while not using them often, appreciated the look and craftsmanship of the gloves.
  • Clotho, the human paladin wore a set of gauntlets of swimming and climbing.
  • Jannaxil Serpentil, a Waterdhavian bookseller.
  • Oorag, a half-orc raider possessed a pair of these gauntlets.
