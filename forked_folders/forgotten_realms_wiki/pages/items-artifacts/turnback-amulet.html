Turnback amulet

Item
nameTurnback Amulet
typeAmulet
locationCormanthor

A turnback amulet was an elven amulet that turned spells back upon their caster.

Powers

Per Symrustar Auglamyr, a turnback amulet could turn spells back upon their caster, but needed to draw blood to function.

History

The guards of house Auglamyr in Cormanthor were given turnback amulets as of the mid-3 century DR.
