The mantle of atropal essence was an artifact cloak that was imbued with the unholy power of the destroyed atropal that fueled the Soulmonger in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The cloak granted its unholy power to anyone who wore it, boosting the wearer's vitality, dexterity, combat prowess, and the ability of their attacks, both physical and magical, to pierce enemy armor. Additionally, it increased the wearer's constitution. Lastly, the mantle of atropal essence was a part of a set, alongside the cincture of atropal essence and the decanter of atropal essence. When these artifacts were worn together, they imbued the wearer with great vitality every time the wearer received healing.

History

In the late 15 century DR, demilich Acererak used an atropal to power his Soulmonger that spread the death curse across Faerûn. When a group of adventurers slew the unholy atropal, the cincture of atropal essence, the mantle of atropal essence, and the decanter of atropal essence were pulled out of the pool that was formed out of the atropal's remnants.
