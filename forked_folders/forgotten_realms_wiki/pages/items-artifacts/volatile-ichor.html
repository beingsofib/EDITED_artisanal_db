Volatile ichor

Item
nameVolatile Ichor
typeSubstance
originFar Realm
locationNorthwest Faerûn, Sword Coast North

Volatile ichor was a strange substance imbued with dark magics, harvested from the bodies of dead Far Realm aberration.

Description

The substance possessed a palpable dark magic that pulsed with power. Even when contained within a container, it rattled against its walls. Those mortals who touched a vial of volatile ichor could not help but feel eerie unrest.

Powers

Volatile ichor was invaluable substance used in restoration and re-powering of damaged enchanted items created by aboleths.

History

In the late 15 century DR, volatile ichor was gathered from killed Far Realm monsters that infested the River District of Neverwinter, during the city's adventurers' and defenders' struggle against the rising threat of the Cloaked Ascendancy.
