Wand of sleep

A wand of sleep was a magic item that forced targets to fall asleep.

Description

These wands were made from green willow vines from the Ganathwood. They became as hard as stone once enchanted.

Powers

Once the command word was uttered, the wand would emit a beam of energy long that was wide at the beginning and widened to at the end. Anything caught within this beam would fall asleep for 3–12 minutes.

Notable Owners

  • Harkon Lukas, a one time native of Cormyr, owned a wand of sleep.
  • Ulraunt, the Keeper of the Tomes of Candlekeep owned a wand of sleep circa 1368 DR.
