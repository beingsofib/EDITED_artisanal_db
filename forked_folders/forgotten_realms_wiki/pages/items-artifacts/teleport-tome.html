Teleport tome

Item
nameTeleport Tome
nicknamesSpellbook
locationUtter East

A teleport tome was a type of spellbook used in the Utter East during the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

Used by travelers and bloodforge mages, it let the user teleport to a known, safe location. It was reusable, but required a small expenditure of mana.
