Sure striking

Item
nameSure striking
typeWeapon quality
casterlevel3e6
value3e+1

Sure striking was a magical quality that could be applied to weapons, and enabled the wielder to overcome resistance to injury or damage reduction that an opponent possessed.

Creation

The spell greater magic weapon was used in the creation of sure striking weapons.
