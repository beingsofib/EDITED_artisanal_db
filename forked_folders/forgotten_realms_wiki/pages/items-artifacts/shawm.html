A shawm or shaum was a double-reed instrument similar to an oboe. It was available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Description

Its sound was loud, shrill and buzzing. If played well, the sound of a shawm was clear and melancholic, reminiscent of migrating geese.

Shawms were common among gnomes of Faerûn, They were also used in Zakhara.

Notable Users

  • The Bendekar's Mercantile storehouse in Ravens Bluff had a variety of shawms in their instruments collection.
