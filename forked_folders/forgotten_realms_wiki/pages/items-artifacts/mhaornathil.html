Mhaornathil

Item
nameMhaornathil
typeScimitar

Mhaornathil was a magical scimitar blessed by Rillifane Rallathil. It had the ability to harm ghosts. It was possessed by Malruthiia Chaadren until her death, and was inherited by Tsarra Chaadren.
