Potions of fortitude, also known as elixirs of fortitude, were a type of potion that made the imbiber tougher and harder to injure.

Description

These potions had a viscous consistency and were fuchsia in color.

Powers

Upon imbibing these potions sent a surge of energy through the imbibers body, temporarily increasing their body's constitution.

Availability

  • In the city of Athkatla, circa 1372 DR, one could purchase potions of fortitude from Ribald Barterman in his store Adventurer Mart and in the Bridge district from the potion vendor Mrs. Cragmoon.
  • In the city of Waterdeep, one could find potions of fortitude for sale at Sorcerous Sundries.
