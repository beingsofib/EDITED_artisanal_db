Crackers were crispy tidbits of bread. A selection with herbs from the Dalelands was available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Description

Crackers were produced in several different flavors: onion, garlic, pepper, or savory. Packages contained three dozen crackers, and could be ordered either with a single flavor or in "Gourmet Packs" that contained all four flavors. In the city of Ravens Bluff, crackers were sold during performances of Jack Mooney & Sons Circus.
