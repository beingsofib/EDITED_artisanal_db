The Sword of Chaos was the personal weapon of the Bhaalspawn Deathbringer, Sarevok Anchev.

Description

It was believed the sword was crafted specifically for Sarevok.

Powers

The Sword of Chaos drained the life force of any foe it struck and conferred its wielder.

History

Sarevok wielded this sword in his fateful battle with his half-sibling in the Bhaalist temple beneath Baldur's Gate.

After Sarevok's death the Sword of Chaos was stored within the city's High Hall.

By the following year, the sword came into the possession of Malaaq the genie enslaved by archmage Jon Irenicus who was willing to exchange the sword for his freedom when dealing with Abdel Adrian.

At some point over the next century Sarevok was reunited with his favored blade. It remained in his possession as of the .
