Espruar () was the alphabet of the moon elves and the script used to write the Elven language.

Description

Espruar was a graceful and fluid script It was commonly used to decorate jewelry, monuments, and magic items. It was also used as the writing system for the Dambrathan language, Sylvan, Undercommon, and Aquan.

The script was also used by mortals when writing in Deep Speech, the language of aberrations, as it had no native script of its own.

History

The Espruar script was originally developed by the moon elves, but had been subsequently adopted by sun elves, drow, and other elven peoples as their preferred script, remaining so for several millennia.

Around and by the late 15 century DR, most elves across Faerûn used it to render messages, either in Common or in their own language. The language was also used on Evermeet.

By , a variant of the Espruar script was commonly in use, with characters that differed substantially from the ones in use in previous and subsequent decades.
