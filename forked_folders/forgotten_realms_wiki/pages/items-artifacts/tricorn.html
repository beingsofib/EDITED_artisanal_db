The tricorn was a style of hat in Faerûn that was popular among the captains of ships.

Description

Tricorns were three-cornered hats that were typically made of either felt, leather, or silk. Sometimes they sported feathers.

Notable Wearers

  • Foxilon Cardluck, though he was not the ship's captain, wore a tricorn when the crew of the Realms Master visited the city of Saerloon.
  • Shil Yargo, head of the Company Yargo mercenary outfit, wore a tricorn hat.
  • The avatar of Valkur typically dressed in a blue tricorn hat that was trimmed in a gold braid and featured a large, white plume.
