Fifes of blasting were wind instruments enchanted with offensive sonic magics.

Description

Fifes of blasting appeared as small mundane flutes.

Powers

When blown into, these fifes produced a damaging burst of ultrasonic sound. This effect was similar to the effects of the sound burst spell.

History

As of the late 14 century DR, these instruments were available from vendors who dealt in magic goods. Some could be found in the markets of Neverwinter. One of the vendors who owned a fife of blasting was Tarmas of the Mere of Dead Men.
