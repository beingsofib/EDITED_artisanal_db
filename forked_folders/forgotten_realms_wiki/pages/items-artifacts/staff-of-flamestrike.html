The staff of flamestrike was an enchanted quarterstaff that belonged to a half-elf adventurer Faeril circa 1369 DR.

Description

The Staff of flamestrike appeared to be a simple quarterstaff carved using birch wood.

Powers

Faeril's staff possessed the ability to blast a target with divine fire as if stricken by a flame strike spell.

History

In 1369 DR, the staff of flamestrike was in Faeril's possession when she ventured into the ruins of Myth Drannor, along with many other adventurers, following Elminster's call for aid against the Cult of the Dragon that planned to use the ruins' mythals and the Pools of Radiance to conquer the region.

Consequently, Faeril, wielding her staff of flamestrike, joined the second band of heroes, who arrived from Phlan, on their quest to stop Kya Mordrayn and Pelendralaar.
