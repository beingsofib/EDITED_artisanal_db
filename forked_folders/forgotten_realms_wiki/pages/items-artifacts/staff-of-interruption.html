Staff of interruption was an enchanted quarterstaff used to battle spellcasters and created sometime before the late 15 century DR.

Powers

SStaff of interruption possessed a potent +2 enchantment that strengthened the weapon's blows and aided the wielder's aim. The staff hummed with disruptive magical arcane that could be focused into a daily blast of counterspell magic.

History

In the , a staff of interruption belonged to Mayrina during her time with the Anti-Hag Support Group of Baldur's Gate. She offered the magic weapon as a reward to adventurers brave enough to end the life of malicious green hag Auntie Ethel who laid under the Blushing Maiden Inn.
