The History of the Thistles

Item
nameThe History of the Thistles
typeBook

The History of the Thistles was a massive genealogical tome that contained the history of the Thistle family. Among countless other details, it included the fact that Lord Partic Thistle's maternal grandmother was a Cormaeril.
