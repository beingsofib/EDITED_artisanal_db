In Faerûn, andar was the name for , a semi-precious stone usually found in streambeds as tumbled pebbles or crystals with an average diameter of in (13 mm).

Description

Andar was a durable gemstone that could be facet-cut by a skilled to produce translucent gems with a green-red or brown-red flash. A typical stone had a base value of 50 gp.

Powers

Combining powdered andar with a dragon scale (gold, silver, or amethyst) and boiling produced an alternate ingredient that could be added to the oily base of a potion of treasure finding.
