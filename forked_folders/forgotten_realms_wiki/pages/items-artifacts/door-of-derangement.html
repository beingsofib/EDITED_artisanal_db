Door of derangement

Doors of derangement were dweomered door that inflict make the creatures walking through them blind, deaf and feebleminded as per the spells. They would typically only effect every third creature to pass their threshold, although this could be changed.

Creation

These doors were created with the blindness or deafness and feeblemind spells.
