Dread doorway

A dread doorway was a minor artifact from Halruaa that worked similarly to a dweomered door, in that if a creature interacting with it or passing through it's threshold would trigger a spell or magical effect.

Known Dread Doorways

  • Annihilation doorway
  • Imprisoning doorway
  • Wraith doorway
