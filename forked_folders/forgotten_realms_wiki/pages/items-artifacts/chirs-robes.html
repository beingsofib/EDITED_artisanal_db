Chir's robes were created and enchanted by the deep gnome wizard Chir Darkflame, active in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Chir's robes were dark purple in color and looked more like a quality dress rather than a magician's garb. Upon closer examination, one could see minor alterations and enchantments that Chir weaved into the robes' fabric.

Powers

The dress possessed a +1 enchantment that prevented anyone but gnomes who venerated evil from wearing it. Additionally, the robes allowed the wearer to create magical darkness twice per day.
