Quill of memory

Item
nameQuill of memory
typeMagic item
rarity5eRare

Quills of memory were enchanted quills that could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Powers

A quill of memory could be used to record an encounter with a enemy. This record helped the owner to learn of their foes' weak points after they had faced them at least once.

Notable Owners

  • Vajra Safahr, the Blackstaff of Waterdeep during the late 15 century DR.
