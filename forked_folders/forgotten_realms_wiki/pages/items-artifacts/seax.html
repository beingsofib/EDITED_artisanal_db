Seax

Item
nameSeax
typeBlade

The seax was a type of bladed weapon utilized by some members of the Tribe of the Bear and Tribe of the Elk in Icewind Dale in the 15 century DR.

Description

The blade of a seax could be short like a dagger or longer like a short sword. The last third of the blade gradually tapered towards its point, granting it greater ease of use.
