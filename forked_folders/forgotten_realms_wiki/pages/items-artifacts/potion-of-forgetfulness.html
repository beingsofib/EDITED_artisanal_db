Potion of forgetfulness

Item
namePotion of Forgetfulness
typePotion
value1e800 gp

A potion of forgetfulness was a draught that destroyed the drinker's memories.

Powers

If drank on its own, it caused forgetting of all that was experienced in the last 24 hours, including memorized spells. If split in quarters, adding a quarter of the potion to a non-alcoholic beverage produced a memory lapse of all mundane declarative knowledge only, which lasted only one to four hours half of the time. Adding it to an alcoholic beverage caused nothing but a rage in the drinker and a terrible taste.

History

Such potions could be made out of obliviax.
