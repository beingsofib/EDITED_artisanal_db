A wand of fire was an enchanted wand that contained several flame-based powers.

Powers

These wands replicated the effects of the following wizard spells: burning hands, pyrotechnics, fireball, and wall of fire. A single charge was consumed when burning hands and pyrotechnics were used, and two chargers were consumed when fireball and wall of fire were used. These wands could be recharged.

Notable Owners

  • Ali al-Lazan, a notorious flame mage with the Brotherhood of the True Flame, carried a wand of fire.
  • Ahmad al-Mudill, a Zakharan flame mage who served as a spy and recruit for the Brotherhood of the True Flame in Zakhara.
  • Marcos Flass, a disgraced Red Wizard of Thay and the creator of tourmaline golems in the late 14 century DR.
  • Myschanta Halarra, a renowned abjurer of Arabel, who used her wand against the terrible Night Parade.
  • Xithiss, a mindflayer who worked for Baeloth Barrityl in his coliseum the Black Pits.
  • Some of the crew of the pirate ship Scarlet Picaro carried wands of fire.
