Timestave

nameTimestave

The Timestave was an enchanted quarterstaff wielded by Labelas Enoreth.

Powers

This unique powerfully enchanted quarterstaff could cast a variety of spells when striking a target including age plant, age object, age creature, age dragon and the reverse of those spells.
