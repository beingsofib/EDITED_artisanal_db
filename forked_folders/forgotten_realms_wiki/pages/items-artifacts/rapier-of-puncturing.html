A rapier of puncturing was a magical rapier.

Powers

A rapier of puncturing was a +2 wounding rapier. Its most dangerous ability, which was usable three times per day, was the ability to cause potentially severe blood loss by merely touching an enemy.

History

Rapiers of puncturing were popular weapons among the assassins of the Jaezred Chaulssin in the 14 century DR.

Notable Owners

  • Nimor Imphraezl, an assassin, owned such a rapier.
