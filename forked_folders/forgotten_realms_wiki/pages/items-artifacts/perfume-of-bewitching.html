Perfume of bewitching

Item
namePerfume of Bewitching
typeWondrous item
locationFaerûn
rarity5eCommon

Perfume of bewitching was a magical perfume.

Powers

This perfume was contained in a very small vial, so small it held only enough perfume for a single application. When applied, the individual appeared more charismatic and confident to others for a duration of one hour.
