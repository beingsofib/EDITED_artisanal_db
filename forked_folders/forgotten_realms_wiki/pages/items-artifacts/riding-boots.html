Riding boots were a variety of boots designed to be worn while riding a mount. They were commonly seen in both the lands of Faerûn and Zakhara.

Description

A riding boot was typically constructed from stiffened leather. Some were designed with a break away strap system, allowing one to sheathe a short sword against their calf, while others had a sheath within their cuffs for concealing daggers or stilettos.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold riding boots made of either black or brown leather and with the common weapon sheathing strap system if desired.
  • Cormyr
  • In the city of Raven's Bluff, riding boots could be found for sale at Tym's Supple Leather Shoppe.
  • In the bazaars of Zakhara, riding boots could generally be bought for around five to two gold pieces, depending upon how well one haggled.

History

In the 14 century DR, leather riding boots that reached up to the thighs were fashionable footwear among men in Waterdeep.
