The Ancient Sermon was the name given to enchanted amulets prized among clerics and priests of all denominations.

Powers

The Ancient Sermon amulet offered powerful magical protection to the wearer, similar to an amulet of protection. Additionally, its divine nature granted the wearer a number of additional prayers and miracles they could perform every day.

History

The Ancient Sermon amulets were highly sought after by clerics of all deities. Each church believed that the amulets were bestowed as their deity's favor alongside divine magics and godly protection. One such amulet was in use by the Church of Kelemvor's clerics in the Lord of the Dead's temple in Mulsantir circa .
