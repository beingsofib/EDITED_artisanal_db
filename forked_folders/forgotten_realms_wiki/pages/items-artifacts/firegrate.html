A firegrate was a handy accessory for cooking on fires.

Description

Firegrates were simple iron racks that could be placed over a fire so that a pot or similar device could be used to cook food.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue offered small, portable grates for camping and hiking. They were 6-by-12 inches (15-by-30 centimeters), and had hinged legs that folded in for storage. It came with an iron prod for moving a hot cookpot about, and cost 3 gp.
  • Firegrates and similar fire-related tools and implements could be found at Ember Me More, a dedicated shop in Ravens Bluff.
