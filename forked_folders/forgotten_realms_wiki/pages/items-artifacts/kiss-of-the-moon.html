Kiss of the Moon

Ship
nameKiss of the Moon
typeShip (raker)
locationWaterdeep, Sea of Swords

The Kiss of the Moon was a famous ship of Waterdeep on the Sea of Swords. It was a raker.

In the 1320s DR, it was used to defend Deepwater Harbor against an undead kraken, but was sunk in the effort.

A beautiful and detailed model of the Kiss of the Moon was displayed in the hall of ships in the House of the Moon temple to Selûne in Waterdeep. As of 1370 DR, it had been only recently added to the collection.
