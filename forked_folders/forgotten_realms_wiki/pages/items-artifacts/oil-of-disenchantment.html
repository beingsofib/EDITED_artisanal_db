Oil of disenchantment was a type of oil that could remove enchantments on a person or item for a period of time.

Description

The oil was a type of lotion or salve that one could put of skin or items. These oils were usually made with of disenchanter hair.

Powers

Once the oil was applied, it would remove all enchantments and charms placed upon a creature, or if rubbed on a magic item it would suppress the magic for 2–3 minutes. Once applied. it radiated no magic, thus masking whatever enchantment was on the item it coated. After a couple of minutes had passed, the oil would dry and lose its potency, although a new layer could be reapplied if necessary.
