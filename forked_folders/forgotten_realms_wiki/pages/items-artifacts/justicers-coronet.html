Justicer's coronet

Item
nameJusticer's coronet
typeMagic circlet
rarity5eVery rare

The justicer's coronet was an enchanted crown that could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Powers

A warlock who wore the justicer's coronet was able to cast mass hold monster.

Notable Owners

  • Farideh, a tiefling warlock and adventurer.
