Templar's retribution was a holy cold iron bastard sword of the Church of the Silver Flame found in Eberron during the mid-1480s DR of Faerûn.

Powers

The blessed sword was filled with holy and good energy, and blasted enemies with pure light energy. The cold iron made it more effective against demons and some other types of monsters.

History

Templar's retribution was said to deliver the wrath of Tira Miron, champion of the Silver Flame. It was given as a reward and tool to the brave adventurers who faced the Spinner of Shadows in her prison, and then followed her to Faerûn.
