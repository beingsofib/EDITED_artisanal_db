Glass tubing was a scientific item in the Realms often used by wizards during laboratory experiments and research.

Description

The glass-blowers guild in Urmlaspyr produced a variety of glass tubing in length increments of that could be straight or bent as needed. Additionally, a specialized coiled tube could be made that was perfect for distillation.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold glass tubing for 2 silver pieces per , but also offered coiled tubes for 5 gold pieces.

Notable Users

  • Glass tubing was favored by wizards.
