Northhelm

Item
nameNorthhelm
typeMagic helmet

The Northhelm was a magical helmet that made the wearer resistant to cold and provided them with protection.

Description

This helm had a +2 enchantment. Wearing the helmet made one take less damage from cold-based attacks.

History

In 1358 DR, the magical helmet was owned by the Northlanders of the village known as Icewolf.
