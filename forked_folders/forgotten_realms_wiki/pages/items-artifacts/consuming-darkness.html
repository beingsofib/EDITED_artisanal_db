Consuming darkness

nameConsuming darkness

originAmskar's enclave

Consuming darkness was a magical ring blessed with Shar's "love" found in Cormyr in the mid-1480s DR.

Description

Despite being made of gold, the ring appeared as if a completely black silhouette, shedding or reflecting no light at all.

Powers

Consuming darkness granted the wearer an increased ability in the fields of discombobulating and assassinating their targets.

History

The ring was taken from the treasury of the Shadovar Archwizard Amskar's flying glacier enclave by Cormyrean forces as they attacked it. The battle destroyed the enclave and the army upon it, but consuming darkness was one of the treasures recovered intact.
