Titania (material)

Substance
nameTitania
colorWhite

Titania was a processed mineral sand used for various trades.

Crafting

Titania was made from the mineral ilmenite through an obscure and little-known process.

Usages

  • Titania was used for making bright, white pigment, such as those used in enamel.
