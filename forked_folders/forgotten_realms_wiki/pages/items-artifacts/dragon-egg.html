Dragon egg

Item
nameDragon Egg
typeGemstone
locationNeverwinter, Sword Coast North
Savage Frontier, Northwest Faerûn

Dragon eggs were extremely rare and valuable gemstones, notable for their large size. They bore no particular relation to the actual eggs of dragons.

Usage

Dragon eggs were used as a reagent in the manufacture of extremely valuable and powerful magical items.

History

Fitting for the name, dragon eggs were hoarded at the Temple of Tiamat in the Well of Dragons during the Tyranny of Dragons.
