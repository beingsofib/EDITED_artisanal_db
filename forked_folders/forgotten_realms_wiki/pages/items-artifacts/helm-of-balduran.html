The helm of Balduran was a magical helmet that once belonged to the famous explorer Balduran, before he founded the settlement of Baldur's Gate. It was highly sought after by historians, collectors, and adventurers alike.

Description

Despite its centuries-long history, the helm of Balduran still gleamed as bright the day it was crafted.

Powers

The helm offered significant protections that were magical in nature, but the origin of which was largely unknown. It granted the wearer both the vitality and favor of the famous adventurer.

History

At some point prior to the , Balduran's helm and cloak came into the possession of the elven warrior Vail and his fellow adventurers. Unfortunately, when the group attempted to raid the tower of the wizard Ramazith Flamesinger, they were petrified. However, prior to their raid, Vail hid the helm behind a trapped painting that hung within the second story of the Helm and Cloak inn.

As of the , the helm of Balduran resided in Wyrmway, beneath Wyrm's Rock, safely hidden behind the lifeless body of Ansur, Balduran's once close friend.
