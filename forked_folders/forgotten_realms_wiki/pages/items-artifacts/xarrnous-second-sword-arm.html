Xarrnous' Second Sword Arm, commonly known as the gauntlets of weapon skill, was a set of magical gauntlets that increased the combat prowess of the wearer.

Powers

When worn, these gauntlets allowed an individual to strike at their foes with greater accuracy.

History

These gauntlets were named after their most well-known owner, the smuggler and mercenary, Xarrnous. He often lent them out to any clients that traveled with him as he felt having an extra sword arm often proved handy.

Notable Owners

  • Elan Garaq, a rakshasa trader in the Black Pits in the Underdark.
  • Larze, an orge assassin from Baldur's Gate circa 1368 DR.
