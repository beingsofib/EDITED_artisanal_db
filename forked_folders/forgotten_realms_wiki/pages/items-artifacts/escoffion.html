The escoffion was a type of female headdress in Faerûn related to the style of headwear known as hennin.

Description

The material of an escoffion was shaped into a double-horned configuration and had a veil attached.

History

In the 14 century DR, escoffions were a popular variety of headwear among women in Waterdeep.
