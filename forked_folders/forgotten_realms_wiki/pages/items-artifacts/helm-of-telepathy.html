A helm of telepathy was a magical helm that enabled telepathic communication between the wearer and another creature. It was also capable of issuing limited telepathic commands.

Powers

The wearer of a helm of telepathy could cast the detect thoughts spell at will. While connected to another creature's mind via concentrating on the spell, the helm allowed two-way telepathic communication between the wearer and the connected creature.

Additionally, the helm of telepathy allowed for the wearer to cast the suggestion spell on a connected creature. This power could be used only once per day, as the helm recovered this ability at the next dawn.

Like most other items that allowed for distant communication, any conversation held through a helm of telepathy within the Nine Hells could be overheard by an archdevil.

Notable Owners

  • Alodia Drodeen, a drow cleric and eldest daughter of her house.
  • Allisa of the Mists, a powerful paladin.
  • Chhe Phou, a Kara-Turan warrior.
  • Gauntlet, a battle horror who used its helm of telepathy to command the Doom Brigade's helmed horrors.
  • Priamon Rakesk, a lich who used his helm of telepathy to peer into Halaster Blackcloak's mind.
