Potion of bull's strength was a common magical potion that gave the imbiber great strength.

Description

These potions were usually of a blood-red color and were brewed using the horns and hearts of young bulls.

Potion of bull's strength were dispensed in simple glass potion bottles or decorative glass decanters.

Powers

Consuming a bottle of a potion of bull's strength was similar in effect to the spell of the same name - bull's strength, cast at a highly proficient level.

Notable Owners

  • Karragh - a sadistic and cruel dwarf criminal of Daggerford active circa 1372 DR.
