Datchas was the common name for ' in the Realms. The pink variety was also known as sugar stone'.

Description

Datchas could be found in small stones, which were typically cut into gems, or large warty up to in diameter which were either sliced and polished for use as inlays or cut . The pale yellowish green hue was the most prized for faceted gems; the orange hue was in highest demand for inlays. Other colors, caused by impurities of copper and other minerals, were white to pink to red, and reddish brown. A typical faceted stone had a base value of 50 gp.

Powers

A person under the spider climb enchantment could double the duration of the effect by consuming crushed datchas powder mixed with any non-alcoholic liquid.

Notable Users

  • Lolth sometimes showed her favor to followers by having them discover datchas that had spiders encased in them.
