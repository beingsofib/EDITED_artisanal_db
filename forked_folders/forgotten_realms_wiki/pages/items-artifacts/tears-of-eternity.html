Tears of Eternity was a unique enchanted blade found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Description

Tears of Eternity was a scimitar that functioned as a sword of wounding.

History

This sword was carried by Khaizuran, a warmonger genie who worked for the Brotherhood of the True Flame.
