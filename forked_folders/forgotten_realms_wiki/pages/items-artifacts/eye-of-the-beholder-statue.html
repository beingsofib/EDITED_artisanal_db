The Eye of the Beholder was a large statue located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR before that city's destruction.

Location

The statue sat within Zhentil Keep's Inner Quarter behind the Black Altar.

Description

The statue depicted a beholder sitting atop a spire of black stone. The Eye of the Beholder was carved from a single large piece of stone.

History

The statue stood as a symbol honoring Zhentil Keep's alliance with beholders that helped keep the city safe.
