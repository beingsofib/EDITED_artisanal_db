Callidyrr mead

Item
nameCallidyrr mead
locationCaer Callidyrr, Alaron

Callidyrr mead was a brewed on the island of Alaron in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The mead was served in the tent of Geoffrey the aleman during the Festival of the Spring Equinox in 1345 DR.

Description

It was known for being a very thick beverage.
