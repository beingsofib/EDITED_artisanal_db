One Sage's Wyrmbook was a book on dragons by the sage Maskalandur Narlur of the city-state of Ormpur on the edge of Lapaliiya in southwest Faerûn, published in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR. It told of notable dragons like the song dragon Taraunramorlamurla who dwelled in Waterdeep.
