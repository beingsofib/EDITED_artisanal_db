Three Falcons Volant

Ship
nameThree Falcons Volant
typeShip
locationSembia, Sea of Fallen Stars

Three Falcons Volant was a ship of Sembian ownership on the Sea of Fallen Stars. Like other Sembian mercantile and naval vessels, it was named for a coin, in this case the silver falcon of Cormyr.
