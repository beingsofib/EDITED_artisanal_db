Fagara was a spice from T'u Lung. It was available for purchase in Faerûn through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Description

Fagara was similar to peppercorns in taste and size, but also with an added numbness and a mild tingling sensation. It was very potent, so a small portion was sufficient even for large dishes.
