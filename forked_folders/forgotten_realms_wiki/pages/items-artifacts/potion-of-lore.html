Potion of lore

Item
namePotion of Lore
typePotion
casterlevel3e3

Potions of lore were magic concoctions, the arcane knowledge in a bottle, always in high demand among scholars and adventurers delving deep into magical mysteries-filled dungeons.

Description

Potions of lore were often red

Powers

Drinking a potion of lore was similar in effect to the identify spell.

History

Potions of lore became popular and wide-spread in the areas surrounding Neverwinter, Nelanther Islands, Cormyr, and many other places circa 1372 DR.
