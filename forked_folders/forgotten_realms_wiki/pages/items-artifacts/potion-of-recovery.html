Potion of recovery

Item
namePotion of Recovery
typePotion
locationNeverwinter, The Crags, Sword Coast North

Potions of recovery were rare and powerful curative potions found in northwest Faerûn in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

A single vial of this potion refreshed imbiber's stamina, removing all exhaustion and providing them with a potent dose of healing.

History

By the late 15 century DR, potions of recovery were sometimes sold in the city of Neverwinter on the Sword Coast North and used by groups inhabiting the Crags.
