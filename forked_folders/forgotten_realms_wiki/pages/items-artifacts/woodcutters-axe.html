Woodcutter's axes were enchanted tools of lumberjacks's trade in the Moonsea region.

Powers

These magic woodcutter's axes were created to increase the work efficiency of individual lumberjacks as hands-on logging was the most efficient method. The enchantments placed on woodcutter's axes guided the weapon towards the "sweet spot" on the target's body. This effectively was a +2 enchantment when wielded in battle. The same magic made these aces especially deadly against various shapechanger foes.

History

By the , woodcutter's axes were used by lumberjacks in the Moonsea region and the city of Voonlar. One such weapon could be found stuck in a tree stump on the outskirts of Voonlar circa the Order of the Crippled Fang crisis and a devastating fiend assault on the city.
