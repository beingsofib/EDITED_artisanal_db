Saws were cutting tools used to carve through wood, stone, steel, limbs, and more.

Description

Saws had toothed metal blades, but otherwise varied in size, shape, and use. Types included:

  • Amputation saws, a category of smaller saws used to remove limbs and prevent gangrene.
  • Crosscut saw, the most common type of saw which sold for 3 gp from Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.
  • Folding saw, a portable saw large enough to fell trees
  • Framed jigsaw (8gp), which was sometimes sold with diamond teeth for the purpose of cutting through stone (17 gp).
  • Hacksaw (5 gp), used for cutting through metal with diamond-toothed versions (15 gp) able to cut through stone and even more superior versions (20 gp) able to cut through anything short of diamond itself.
  • Two-person logging saw (15 gp), which could cut through wood five times faster than a common saw.
  • Two-person ripsaw (7 gp).

Saws were ill-suited to be used as weapons.
