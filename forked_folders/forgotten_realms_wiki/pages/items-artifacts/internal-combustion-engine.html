Internal combustion engine

Item
nameInternal combustion engine
typeMachinery
creatorChurch of Gond

Internal combustion engine were a type of massive, experimental machinery. They could only be found in the innermost rooms of major Gond temples, off limits to all but the high-ranking clergy of the church.

Description

These machines required oil to function and were liable to experience explosions.
