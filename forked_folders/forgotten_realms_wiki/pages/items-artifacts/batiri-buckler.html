Batiri bucklers were enchanted Batiri shields created by the crafters and shamans of the Chultan Peninsula.

Description

These shields were created from affixed together, overlapping shells of giant beetles found in the jungles of Chult. Like similar items made of metal, Batiri bucklers were sturdy and lightweight. The bucklers' surface was decorated with Batiri warpaints.

Powers

Batiri bucklers were so light, they offered little detriment to spellcasters. These shields were enchanted with +1 enchantments. However, their organic nature made them vulnerable to both mundane and magical fire.

History

In the , goblins of the Shattered Spear tribe in Samarach used these enchanted beetle carapace bucklers.
