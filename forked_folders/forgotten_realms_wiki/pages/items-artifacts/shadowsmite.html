Shadowsmite

Item
nameShadowsmite
typeMagical mace

Shadowsmite was an enchanted mace wielded by Daelegoth Orndeir, a fire genasi cleric of Amaunator, in the late 14 century DR.

It was a +3 flaming burst, shadowscourge light mace.
