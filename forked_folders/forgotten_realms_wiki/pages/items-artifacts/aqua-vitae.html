Aqua vitae, sometimes called water of life, was a chemical commonly used in alchemy.

Description

It was depicted as an orange liquid.

Creation

It was created with vitriol and solvent.

Usage

It was used in the creation of potions, dyes, primordial goo, and aqua regia.
