Speedy Reply

Speedy Reply was a unique, magical scimitar that enhanced the speed at which the wielder moved.

Description

The sword had a heavy haft and broad blade that made it resemble a well-crafted dueling cutlass.

Powers

The wielder of Speedy Reply moved more deftly in combat. They were able to more-rapidly move around the battlefield whenever they landed a blow upon an opponent with the blade.

History

As of the , Speedy Reply remained in the possession of a Zhentarim agent. That Zhent was slain a group of gnolls cultists at a cave near the Waukeen's Rest inn.
