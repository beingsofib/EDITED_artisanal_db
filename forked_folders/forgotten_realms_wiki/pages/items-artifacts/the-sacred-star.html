The Sacred Star was an enchanted morningstar imbued with radiant divine magics from the city of Baldur's Gate.

Powers

The Sacred Star possessed a +2 enchantment that guided the wielder's hand towards the target and made each strike with the weapon more deadly. The morningstar's attacks were also imbued with radiant energy, dealing addition damage with each attack.

History

Sometime before the late 15 century DR, the The Sacred Star ended up amongst the goods offered for purchase by Vicar Humbletoes of the Stormshore Tabernacle, a non-denominational shrine in Baldur's Gate. In the , the weapon was still in the gnome cleric's hands.
