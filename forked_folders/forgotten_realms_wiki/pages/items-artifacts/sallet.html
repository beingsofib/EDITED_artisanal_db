The sallet was a type of helmet distinguished by its back-end, which curved out into a flange to protect the user's neck. It was primarily found all along the Sword Coast and in the nation of Tethyr.

Description

Sallets varied in their coverage, with some being more like skullcaps and others having a close-faced beak-nose like an armet.

Notable Sallets

  • helms of brilliance were sometimes fashioned in the style of a sallet, forged of brilliant silver and polished steel.
  • Helm of supreme wizardry, an enchanted steel sallet that was part of the hoard of Saryndalaghlothtor.
  • Morthinmar, an artifact created under the reign of Qysar Shoon II of the Shoon Imperium.

Notable Users

Organizations

  • Some members of the Wolves mercenary company wore sallets.

Regions & Settlements

  • The Hawk Guards, the mounted patrols of the Great Dale, wore sallets with a beak-nose.
  • In the mid-14 century DR, sallets were a commonly found form of helmet in Waterdeep.
