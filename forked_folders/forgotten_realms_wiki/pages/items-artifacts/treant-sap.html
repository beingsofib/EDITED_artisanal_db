Treant sap, referred to regionally in Cormyr as larlyn, was the resinous lifeblood of treants.

Regulations

  • According to the Laws of Cormyr, treants were considered to be "of the heart of Cormyr", meaning property of the ruling family. Therefore, only members of the ruling family, or regents, could harvest them for their sap, or direct others to do so, and likewise were the only ones allowed to make use of the substance. It was illegal for anyone else to do so and a monetary charge would be pressed if caught, with the severity depending upon whether the sap was given willingly or obtained through coercion.

  • Treant sap could be used as a metamagic component to empower the spell goodberry.
  • Cormanthor violet fungus live in a symbiotic relationship with treants, protecting them against predators in return for being able to feed off of their sap.
  • In the late 15 century DR, some were known to enchant vials of treant sap that could be injected to fortify a body.
