Permanent gel

Item
namePermenanet gel
typeMagical hair gel

Permanent gel was a type of magical hairstyling gel that indefinitely altered the shape of someone's hair.

Powers

When a hairstyle that had been shaped with permanent gel was reshaped in any way, it would immediately reform into its original shape. The permanent gel could not be washed out without powerful intervention. If this happened, or the hair was cut, the removed gel became a slithering tracker after 1 hour. The tracker would then pursue the one who had once had it in their hair, and attempt to once more attach itself to their hairs. If this was impossible, such as in situations when the victim's hair had been signifcantly cut, the tracker would turn violent and strike to kill them.

One of the known ways to rid someone of permanent gel, was to completely destroy their hair by way of fire or acid.

Notable Owners

The annis Errevastica owned a bottle of permanent gel. She kept it in her underground lair beneath Jooge Nopsmoth's barbershop, in Silverymoon.
