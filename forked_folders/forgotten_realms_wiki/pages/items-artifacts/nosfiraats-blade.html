Nosfiraat's Blade was a golden-hued magical scimitar that originated from Zakhara.

Powers

Nosfiraat's Blade bore a +1 magical enchantment and critical strikes landed with this blade were especially powerful.

History

Nosfiraat's Blade was one of the golden scimitars that belonged to the famous merchant Daavid Ali Fazeer circa 1322 DR. Daavid claimed the swords were found aboard a ghost ship that was sunken shortly after the merchant's excursion onto the vessel. Interestingly enough, Daavid Ali Fazeer refused to say whether the ship's crew became ghosts before or after his involvement.

In 1372 DR the bale was in the possession of its namesake, the Zakharan vampire Nosfiraat.
