Drift disc

Item
nameDrift Disc
typeVessel
locationUtter East

A drift disc was a tiny boat used in the Utter East during the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

It was a small, saucer-shaped craft with fins and colored green. It let a single passenger move freely over water, effectively letting them walk on water.
