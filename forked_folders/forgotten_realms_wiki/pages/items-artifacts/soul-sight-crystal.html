Soul sight crystal

Item
nameSoul Sight Crystal
typeWondrous item
originChult, Chultan Peninsula

Soul sight crystal was an artifact from the Jungles of Chult in southwest Faerûn.

Powers

When glanced through, the soul sight crystal allowed the wielder to see the very essence of things and creatures, revealing all weaknesses of the targets. When used by adventurers, the crystal allowed them to easily target the enemy's weaknesses, greatly boosting their output damage potential.
