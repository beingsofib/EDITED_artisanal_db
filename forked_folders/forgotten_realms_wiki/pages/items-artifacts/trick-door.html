A trick door was a magic door that would open to nothing but a wall unless the correct code-knocks were made before opening the door.

Powers

Trick doors only opened the way through if, before being opened, the user correctly did the specific doors code-knocks. If done incorrectly it would just open into a wall mimicking the surrounding wall where the door was installed.

It was speculated that a spell similar to passwall were used in their creation.

History

Sometime around the Time of Troubles, these doors were known to have been installed in the thieves' guilds of Mulmaster, Hillsfar, Calaunt, and Tantras. Several guilds placed additional false doors around the trick doors in order to further confuse unwelcomed intruders.
