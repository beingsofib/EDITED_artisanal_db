Garlic sauce

Item
nameGarlic Sauce
typeSauce

Garlic sauce was a type of sauce whose primary ingredient was garlic.

Notable Users

Businesses

  • The Blue Jack, a tavern in Waterdeep, offered its patrons garlic sauce.
  • The Moonstone Mask, a festhall in Neverwinter, served mushrooms doused in garlic sauce mixed with herbs.
