Hazelnut oil was a type of vegetable oil in the Realms, produced from the seeds of hazelnut trees.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold hazelnut oil at a standard price of one three gold pieces per in its "Aurora's Larder" section.
