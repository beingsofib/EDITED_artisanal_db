Potion of polish

Item
namePotion of Polish
typePotion

Potions of polish were a type of potion that could improve the quality of equipped items.

Description

The potion appeared as a bottle of shiny silver liquid that clung to the glass edges of its container.

Powers

When drunk, one of the imbiber's equipped items gained a supernatural sheen and became notably more powerful.
