Hellfire weapons were vicious weapons found in the Nine Hells.

Desciption

Hellfire weapons were any weapon made from a special type of iron called infernal iron and then forged in hellfire. As a result of this process, the weapon was suffused with brilliant veins of hellfire. The faint light from the weapon illuminated the surrounding area in a radius.

Powers

When a creature was killed with a hellfire weapon, their soul was instantly sent to the Styx. As a result, the soul was transformed into a lemure.
