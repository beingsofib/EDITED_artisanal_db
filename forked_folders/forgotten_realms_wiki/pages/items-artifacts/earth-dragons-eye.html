Earth Dragon's Eye was a variety of tea from Shou Lung in Kara-Tur. It had a strong taste.

History

It was made available to Faerûnians in the early 1360s DR through the Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue, where it cost 50 gp per pound (453 grams).
