Scimitar of the fool

Item
nameScimitar of the Fool
typeMagic sword
casterlevel3e6
value3e3000 gp

A scimitar of the fool was a magical sword that reduced the wielder's wits.

Abilities

A +1 scimitar, it negatively affected the wielder's morale, reducing their mental defenses.

History

They were often given by tyrants to guards whom they could not completely trust.
