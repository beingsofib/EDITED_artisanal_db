Wand of water-finding

Item
nameWand of Water-Finding
typeWand
value2e10,000 gp

A wand of water-finding was an enchanted wand that would alert the wielder to nearby water.

Powers

When activated, this wand would vibrate if potable water was within of the wielder. The intensity of the wand's vibrations would increase the closer the wielder came to the source of water.
