Scale Cleaver was an enchanted sword originating in Chult.

Description

A very well-forged long sword, it was adorned with intertwining symbols from the language of its elven creators, which told of its origin and purpose.

History

This sword was crafted by a rare elven people living in marshes within the jungles of Chult in order to aid them in their age-old battles against the lizard men.

Powers

Normally a +1 long sword, it became more powerful, rising to +5, against all foes covered in natural scales—not only lizardfolk, but also lizards, snakes, fish, and dragons, and even those wearing armor made of natural scales.
