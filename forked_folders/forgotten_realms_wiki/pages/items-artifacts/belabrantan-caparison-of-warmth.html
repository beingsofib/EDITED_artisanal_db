A Belabrantan caparison of warmth was a magical item unique to Waterdeep.

Description

A Belabrantan caparison of warmth was a large cloth made from red silk and was covered in ornate patterns. Prominently displayed on the cloth was the heraldic symbol of House Belabranta.

Powers

This magical item was used by riders to keep themselves, and their steed, at a comfortable temperature while in either extremely cold or extremely hot climates. This effect was identical to the endure elements spell.
