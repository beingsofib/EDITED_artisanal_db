The Throne of the Coronal was the throne of the Coronal of Myth Drannor.

Description

The throne was high-arched and floated above the circular, glowing Pool of Remembrance.

Powers

The Throne itself hovered over the Pool of Remembrance on its own power.

The Throne of the Coronal had a number of powers, which could be called upon by the mantle of the Coronal when they were close by. Such powers included the ability to bar any liquid or vapor, from rain to green dragon's breath, from anywhere within a small area centered on the throne or mantle, and a few evocation spells such as flame strike.

History

In the , Coronal Eltargrim Irithyl was forced to call upon the lightshock wave to quell the noise in the chamber. It also blocked a dagger thrown at him.
