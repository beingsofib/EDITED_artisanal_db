The Staff of Arundel was a unique enchanted staff from the Ten Towns in Icewind Dale.

Powers

The Staff of Arundel was a lesser version of the staff of the woodlands. It allowed its wielder to memorize additional spells of higher levels of druidic magic. Additionally, the staff possessed a powerful +3 enchantment to aim and punishment dispensed.

History

The staff of Arundel was created by the Archdruid of Kuldahar, Arundel, sometime before his death in the at the claws of the powerful demon Belhifet.

In the , the staff of Arundel, along with several other magic items from Icewind Dale, ended up in the hands of the merchant named Joluv who traveled from Icewind Dale to the City of Coin, Athkatla, to sell the items to wealthy Amnian adventurers. Joluv peeled his wars from the Copper Coronet in Athkatla's River District.
