Firegel was an alchemical coating that protected objects from fire.

Description

Firegel was thick and grainy.

Powers

The alchemical coating could reduce the chances that an object was damaged by flames. It was worthless on creatures.

History

The substance was very challenging to synthesize, requiring a combination of very advanced alchemical and herbal skills; hedge wizards often traded in it. It sold for about 400 gold pieces.
