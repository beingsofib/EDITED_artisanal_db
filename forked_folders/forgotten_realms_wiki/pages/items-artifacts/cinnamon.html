Cinnamon, sometimes referred to as cinnamon sticks when in its pre-ground form, was a type of spice found in the Realms.

Availability

Places in the Realms where cinnamon were known to be found included:

  • Cinnamon was sold in Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.
  • In Loudwater, the local apothecary sold cinnamon.
  • In Ravens Bluff, the noble house of Gultoss prominently sold cinnamon.
  • In interior Faerûn, cinnamon was available in the republic of Turmish due to imports and trade.
  • In south Faerun, cinnamon was common in the town of Innarlith.
  • A species of tea grew on the Chultan Peninsula that was locally brewed with cinnamon, sugar, and milk.
  • Cinnamon was exported as far north as the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale where it was eaten with oatmeal.

Beyond Faerûn

  • In east Zakhara, one could find cinammon being sold in the open bazaar of Dihliz.

History

In 1369 DR, a shipment of exotic foodstuffs that included cinnamon was sent from Luskan to Icewind Dale.

Notable Cinnamon Products

  • Cinnamon possessed some medicinal properties, namely, it was used as a disinfectant agent, and a treatment for nausea.
  • Blood-drops was a dish that cinnamon was regularly used as a spice for.
  • Some were known to use cinnamon in cookies.
  • Dragon's bite, a mixed alcoholic beverage, featured powdered cinnamon.
  • Gravia's hot apple cider, a product of the Maxillian Manor orchards in Daggerford, contained two cinnamon sticks per batch.
  • Kender pak, a type of kender sweetbread that was baked out of six grains, caramel, and cinnamon.
  • Some variations of the mummification process included several preserving and aerobatic plants and substances, such as cinnamon, ginger, rose, myrrh, frankincense, and pomegranate.
  • Spiced wine was sometimes flavored with cinnamon.

Notable Users of Cinnamon

Businesses

  • Harbreeze Bakery sold cinnamon cookies.
  • The Jellied Eel served rice infused with cinnamon.
  • Oak-Father's Boon served bowl of hares, a dish that involved stewing diced hare for half a day in a sauce that featured cinnamon.
  • Salamantha's Sundown served grilled grouse that was marinated in cinammon, rosemary, and sage.
  • The Stone Stallion inn served buttermilk sprinkled with cinnamon.

Regions & Settlements

  • In the Vilhon Reach, the beverage Vilhon Cider was often served with cinnamon.

Trivia

  • A variety of things in the Realms had a scent reminiscent of cinnamon. These included the body odor of burburs, peranox, and the wood of a vunwood tree.
  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold cinnamon-scented bath oil, beeswax, and lamp oil.
  • The deity Nobanion had a peculiar sentimental fondness for cinnamon.
  • The deity Shu's divine realm had the scent of cinnamon wafting through the air.
  • The dandyish priest of Selûne Mifano scented his breath with cinnamon.
