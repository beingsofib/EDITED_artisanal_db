Burners were scientific items in the Realms often used by wizards during laboratory experiments and research with braziers.

Description

A burner was a small slab of wax where linen rags were coiled into wicks and light aflame to provide constant heat within a brazier.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold burners at a price of 5 silver pieces.

Notable Users

  • Burners were favored by wizards.
