Returning was a quality that could be applied to weapons that could be thrown. A weapons with this property would return to the one who'd thrown it.

Creation

To craft a returning weapon, a spellcaster had to have telekinesis available.

Notable Returning Weapons

  • Craftmaster, the personal weapon of the god Gond, was a +4 impact returning seeking throwing warhammer.
  • The followers of some deities received a +1 returning enchantment from casting the spell weapon of the deity and holding that deity's favored weapon. For followers of Mystra and Shar, it would be a chakram. For followers of Abbathor, Brandobaris, and Gargauth it would be a dagger. For followers of Lliira and Tymora it would be a shuriken. And finally, for followers of Gruumsh it would be a shortspear or longspear.
