Daftar

Item
nameDaftar
typeNotebook
locationZakhara

A daftar was a Zakharan sea captain's most treasured item.

Uses

Daftar comprised instructions, tables, charts, etc. compiled over years of sailing the seas of the Land of Fate. A captain without their daftar found himself at a sad disadvantage while at sea. They were often jealously guarded and could never be purchased.
