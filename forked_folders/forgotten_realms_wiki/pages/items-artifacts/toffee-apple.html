Toffee apples were simple dessert treats made with fresh apples, dipped in sweet sauces.

Description

A typical toffee apple was a plump sweet and juicy apple skewered on a wooden stick and dipped in toffee and then honey.

Availability

These small toffee treats were a staple of Nell's Pie Shop of Liam's Hold circa 1372 DR.
