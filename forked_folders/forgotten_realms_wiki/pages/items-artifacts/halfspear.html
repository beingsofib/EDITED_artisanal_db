Halfspear

A halfspear is a spear that demihumans of small stature can use effectively.

Notable halfspears

  • Whisperleaf, favored weapon of Baervan Wildwanderer.

Notable Users

  • Kobolds who dwelt in the sewers of Waterdeep in the 14 century DR were armed with halfspears, among other more common arms.
