A bowl of commanding water elementals was a magical bowl that could summon a water elemental.

Description

A bowl was typically in diameter, deep, and capable of holding of liquid. It generally weighed around , and was usually crafted from semi-precious stones such as lapis lazuli, malachite, jade, azurite, peridot, or turquoise.

If viewed through a detect magic spell, a bowl of commanding water elementals gave off a strong conjuration aura.

Some bowls of commanding water elementals were inscribed with prayers to Istishia.

Powers

When filled with water, the bowl could be activated with a command word to summon a water elemental, identical in function to the conjure elemental spell. This ability could only be used once per day.

If salt water were used to fill the bowl, the resulting water elemental was larger.

Notable Owners

  • The Flaming Fist kept a a bowl of commanding water elementals in the storerooms of Fort Beluarian, their stronghold in Chult.
  • A bowl of commanding water elementals could be found in an underground ruin in the High Forest.
  • Ahri "Golemkin", a gnomish tinker in Samarach, had a bowl of commanding water elementals for sale.
