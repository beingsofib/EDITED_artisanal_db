An Imaskari rod was a magical rod of Imaskari origin that was used to dispel magic, so as to better fight all things divine and druidic.

Powers

These rods were used to cast a powerful greater dispel magic spell three times a day.

History

Despite being in common use by the ancient Imaskari Empire, most of the rods were long lost to the ages by the late 14 century DR. One of Imaskari rods could be found in the ruins underneath the Bear God's Barrow, home to the great spirit Okku in the . This particular rod was damaged and cracked along its length and somehow radiated the feeling of being alive.
