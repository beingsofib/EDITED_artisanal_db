Periapts of wisdom were common magical amulets that were often used by high-ranking clerics.

Description

Upon examination, these periapts appeared to be simple light chains with one hanging pearl.

Powers

Periapts of wisdom varied in its enchantment strength, but all shared one single purpose - they magically enhanced the user's wisdom, improving their perception and insight. Due to this, periapts of wisdom were often sought after by clerics to use as meditative tools.

History

Circa the , during the Wailing Death epidemic in Neverwinter, periapts of wisdom of lower enchantment strength were quite widely-spread. Merchant Husher Clay sold +3 periapts in the trading post in Beorunna's Well. And much stronger +5 amulets were in possession of Oleff Uskar, the priest of Tyr at the Hall of Justice in Neverwinter. Periapts of wisdom were also sold in the Temple of Lathander of Westgate in the .
