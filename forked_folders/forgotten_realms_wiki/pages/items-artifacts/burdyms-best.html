Burdym's Best was a drink in the mid–8 century DR.

Availability

The drink was available in Nethrar in the 760s DR. There was at least one keg at the royal cellars in the House of the Unicorn.

History

In the late , the Chosen of Mystra and Court Mage of Galadorna, Elminster Aumar, gave a keg of Burdym's Best, drawn from the royal cellar, to the Watch Captain Rhoagalow after the latter brought word of yet another knifing, one in which the victim may yet live. He told him to go somewhere safe and out of uniform to drink it, strongly implying he intended to wipe the man's memory.
