Gambit armor was a type of enchanted armor designed for those unafraid to risk their lives in battle.

Description

Cloth or leather armor could be enchanted as gambit armor.

Powers

While this armor provided enhanced protection, its true power lay in granting the wearer advantages in battle when their life was in particular danger. This gave them a greater change of scoring a deadly blow immediately after receiving one, and once per day, the wearer could become more dangerous at the expense of exposing themselves to greater risk of injury.

Notable Owners

As of the , an Executor of the Warlock Knights of Vaasa had enhanced their leather armor into gambit armor. This Executor was part of the team responsible for investigating the rejuvenated Castle Perilous.
