A Waukeen's Defender was a variety of enchanted shield wielded by the Waukeenar clergy across Faerûn.

Description

A Waukeen's Defender was decorated with the golden visage of the goddess Waukeen herself.

Powers

These shields were enchanted with a potent +2 magic that increased the wielder's protection and offered additional protection from missiles. Additionally, it offered the wielder a slight boost to their fortitude, will, and reflexes. The shields' magics were woven in such a manner that they retained the enchantments only as long as the image of Waukeen on the front was uncovered and unaltered. If the owner did not follow these instructions, a Waukeen's Defender lost all of its magic.

History

Waukeen's Defenders first appeared in circulation some years before . They were crafted by temples dedicated to the Merchant's Friend all across Faerûn and sold to clever adventurers, who were instructed to show the image of Waukeen everywhere they went in exchange for the shields' magical protection. One such shield was in the hands of Quinn Silverfinger, a cleric of Waukeen in the Golden Lodge of Lonelywood.
