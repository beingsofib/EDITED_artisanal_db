Port was a variety of sweet red wine found in several locations across Faerûn and beyond.

Availability

Port wines were created and sold at As the Vine Twines hin shop and winery in the city of Ravens Bluff, where it was priced at ten gold pieces per filled carafe.

The Fast Sails tavern in the coastal town of Tulmene had a wide variety of wines, including ports.
