Pea soup was a rustic Faerûnian soup named for its prominence of peas or split peas. It was a common staple for travelers in much wilder lands.

Description

Pea soup was a thick soup that resembled gruel, a dish commonly eaten aboard sailing vessels. It originated from the Dalelands and the recipe included mashed peas, smoked ham, and pepper.

Availability

  • The Painted Boat, a restaurant in the Dragon Reach off the southern edge of the Harbor District of Ravens Bluff, offered patrons pea soup among its menu of soup options at a price of 2 copper pieces.
