Gardener's Spade was an enchanted Chauntean staff with a spade head attached to one end.

Description

Gardener's Spade was a weapon and a tool that had seen the passage of many years. Its willow tree wood was tinted with green, and the shovel head appeared fragile. Despite its flimsy appearance, the Gardener's Spade's magics made it virtually unbreakable.

Powers

Gardener's Spade was touched by the Earthmother herself. Chauntea's blessings gave the staff a +1 enchantment and fortified the wielder's constitution. Additionally, Gardener's Spade could heal with a single casting of the cure moderate wounds once a day.

History

Gardener's Spade belonged to Goodwife Aruna of Wanderfen, who became a refugee in the shortly after the Shadow War scarred the Sword Coast North. She carried her Chauntean weapon to her new home in the village of West Harbor in the Mere of Dead Men. She offered the Spade to any adventurer driven enough to recover her lost shawl from the lair of twin black dragons Voaraghamanthar and Waervaerendor.
