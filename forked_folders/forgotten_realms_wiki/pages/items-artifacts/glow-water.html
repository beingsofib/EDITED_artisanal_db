Glow water was an alchemical substance made by hedge wizards.

Powers

Its effects were unspecified, but it may have possibly glowed. They possibly lasted for up to four hours, or perhaps a full day.

History

The substance was very challenging to synthesize, It sold for about 200 gold pieces.
