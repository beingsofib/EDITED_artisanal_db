Spicy shrimp soup was a specialty dish served at the eateries of Baldur's Gate.

Description

There were many variations on the spicy recipe. Each tavern and each eatery in the city had their own version. However, some bowls were only to be eaten at your own risk.

Availability

As of the late 15 century DR, spicy shrimp soup was cooked not only in Baldur's Gate but also in the Chionthar Valley in the Western Heartlands. The wizard named Cyrel had a bowl of this concoction during her hunt for the tiefling named Karlach on the behest of Zariel in the .
