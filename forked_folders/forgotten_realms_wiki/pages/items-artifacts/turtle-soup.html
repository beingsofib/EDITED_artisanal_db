Turtle soup

Item
nameTurtle soup
typeSoup

Turtle soup was a type of soup in the Realms whose preparation involved the meat of a turtle.

Notable Users

Businesses

  • The Maided of the Moor in Mornbryn's Shield was known to serve this dish.
  • In the port city of Neverwinter, both the Fallen Tower and Moonstone Mask were known to serve this dish.
  • The Singing Sword tavern in Waterdeep was well known for serving turtle soup on a daily basis within upside-down turtle shells.

Individuals

  • Alustriel Silverhand had such a great fondness for this dish that she created the turtle soup spell so that she could always have some on hand. Though the rest of the Seven Sisters did not share her fondness for the dish.

Races & Sentient Creatures

  • Turtle soup was a common dish in Elven cuisine.
