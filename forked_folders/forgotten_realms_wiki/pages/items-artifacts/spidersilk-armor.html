Spidersilk armor was a type of armor originating from the drow of the Underdark.

Description

Spidersilk armor was crafted from treated aranea or drider silk. It was soft and very light but was also surprisingly resilient.

Variations

  • Piwafwi: A piwafwi was another form of clothing made from spider silk. Rather than full armor, piwafwis were cloaks. They often had magical properties.

Usage

Due to its low weight, spidersilk armor was often favored by those with little strength.
