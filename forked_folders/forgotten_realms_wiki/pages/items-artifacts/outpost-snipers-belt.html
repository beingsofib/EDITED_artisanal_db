Outpost sniper's belt

nameOutpost Sniper's Belt
typeBelt
materialLeather
locationTethyamar, Buried Realms

The outpost sniper's belt was a magical belt found in the ruins of Tethyamar during the 1480s DR.

Description

The belt had pockets attached that contained weights, which helped aim shots launched by the wearer.

Powers

The belt helped the wearer shoot faster and more accurately. It also helped them move more quickly.

History

The belt was found in Ironvale Deep by the forces of the Tethyamar Mining Outpost when they arrived to find the Tethyamar Master Manifest.
