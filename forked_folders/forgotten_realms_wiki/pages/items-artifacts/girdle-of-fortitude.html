A girdle of fortitude was a magical belt that improved the wearer's hardiness and vitality for a limited duration.

Powers

Once per day, the wearer of a girdle of fortitude could gain the benefits of its powers for eight hours.

Notable Owners

King Violos wore one of these girdles during his later years when leading his people in battle against a group of trolls.

A girdle of fortitude was owned by Gaal, the leader of a cult dedicated to the Unseeing Eye that operated within the sewers of Athkatla.
