Seared tuna

Item
nameSeared Tuna
typeFood
locationNeverwinter, Savage Frontier

Seared tuna was a lean tuna dish from the lands around Neverwinter near the end of the 15 century DR.

Description

The dish used yellow-fin tuna species of fish. Its steaks were lightly seared and intentionally left undercooked on the inside. Many chefs did not appreciate requests to cook the tuna thoroughly.
