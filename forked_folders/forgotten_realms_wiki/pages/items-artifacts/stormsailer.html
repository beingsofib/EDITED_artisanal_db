The Stormsailer was a Halruaan skyship which had been secured for the use of the Datharathi chaka under Captain Darsson in . That year, Sevaera Datharathi and Warian Datharathi used the ship to reach Adama's Tooth, in order to visit Shaddon Datharathi. Unknown to Sevaera, Zeltaebar Datharathi had snuck onboard before their trip.
