Staff of Travaran

Item
nameStaff of Travaran
typeMagical staff
locationEvermeet

The Staff of Travaran was an ancient unique magic item from the island of Evermeet.

Description

A piece of ashwood composed the body of the staff. A silver horse end was found at the end.

Powers

This powerful item combined all of the powers of a staff of the magi with a staff of wizardry.
