A javelin of piercing was a magical javelin that could be launched via a command word.

Description

These single-use magic weapons were usually found in a collections of two to eight. When thrown, they could take any path up to their maximum range.

Powers

Javelins of piercing were effectively +6 weapons, and could be hurled up to . These weapons only had a single charge each; once hurled, they reverted to normal javelins.

History

In the 14 century DR, javelins of piercing were used all over Toril, particularly in Faerûn, Zakhara, and Kara-Tur.
