A belt of priestly might and warding was a magical defensive item, created by servants of deities in Faerûn.

Powers

These belts granted the same boons as a belt of priestly might as well as the ability to create a magic circle against evil or against good once per day that lasted for 80 minutes.

History

Like the belt of priestly might, this item was first created after the Time of Troubles.

Creation

To create a belt of priestly might and warding, the spells barkskin, bull's strength, and magic circle against good/evil were cast.
