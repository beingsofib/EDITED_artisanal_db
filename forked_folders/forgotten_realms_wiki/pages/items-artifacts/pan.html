Pans and skillets were common pieces of cookware found in Faerûn, alongside pots and cauldrons.

Description

A pan was a shallow dish used for frying or baking food. Magically enhancement for pans was usually a practical property relating to their use in cooking.

They were often crafted from plain iron, and could be easily made by casting freshly smelted iron into a simple clay mold. Along with being polished and finished from the raw metal, they could be fitted with a metal or wooden handle.

Notable Pans

  • Frying pan of perfection, a pan that cooked a perfect meal.
