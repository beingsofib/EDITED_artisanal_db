Choking powder was a substance used as an incapacitating thrown weapon.

Description

This purple powder was mixed using several herbs and packed in throwable pouches.

Powers

Upon impact, choking powder pouches opened up, creating a small stinking cloud that dazed and caused uncontrollable coughing fits in those caught in it. The powder cloud lasted for thirty seconds.

History

These devious items became popular in Faerûn in the late 14 century DR. These pouches were quite common in the Daggerford, Delimbiyr Vale in 1372 DR.
