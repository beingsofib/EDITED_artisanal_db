Oakthorn

Item
nameOakthorn
typeSickle

Oakthorn was a unique weapon that was the favored weapon of Sheela Peryroyl, the halfling deity of agriculture and love.

Description

Oakthorn was a sickle, though its level of enchantment, if it was magical at all, was unknown.
