The Dragon's Flame was the famed flagship of Westgate's navy in the 14 century DR.

Description

The vessel was considered to be one of the most expensive ships that had ever sailed across Dragonmere. The Dragon's Flame was bedecked in gold and silver platings and its sides were decorated with intricate and lavish trimmings.

History

As of the , the Dragon's Flame was renowned for being a moving piece of dazzling propaganda and advertisement for the economic power and wealth of the city of Westgate. A miniature copy of the ship was proudly displayed in the office of the Harbormaster in Westgate's Harbor Loop. The office was open to the public and served as a miniature museum of various symbols of the past conflicts and displays of the present accomplishments.
