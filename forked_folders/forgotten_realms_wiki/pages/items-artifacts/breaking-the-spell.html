Breaking the Spell

Item/Book
nameBreaking the Spell
typeTome
locationVeltalar

Breaking the Spell was one of the books consulted by the warlock Japheth Donard during his attempt to recall the soul of Anusha Marhana and rejoin it with her sleeping body in , after it was stolen away by the Dreamheart. He spent considerable coin renting the tomes from Veltalar's private collections.
