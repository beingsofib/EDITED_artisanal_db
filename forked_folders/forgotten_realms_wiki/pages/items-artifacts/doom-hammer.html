Doom hammer was an enchanted maul, a "bringer of that which it is," found in the Western Heartlands of Faerûn in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

Each successful strike with a doom hammer sent a deathly chill of impending doom down the target's bones. This condition prevented them from healing and stiffened their limbs enough to make it harder to aim and attack.

History

In the , doom hammer was in the hands of Grat the Trader, a goblin of the Cult of the Absolute. The weapon itself was looted from nearby locales targeted by the cult.
