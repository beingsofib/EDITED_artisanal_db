Prisoner's manacles

Item
namePrisoner's Manacles
typeManacles
materialSteel
originWheloon Prison, Cormyr

The prisoner's manacles were a pair of disconnected manacles found in the Wheloon Prison in the mid-1480s DR. They represented the continued suffering of Wheloon's prisoners, who were still victim to greater forces despite their efforts.

Powers

The manacles gave the wearer a certain brutal strength and power, especially when striking with their weighted fists directly.

History

The prisoner's manacles were found by Cormyrean forces that overcame Rantha, the gatekeeper officer of General Marthir. This was part of a greater counterattack at the Netherese Recruiting Center inside the Wheloon Prison, where prisoners were taken in and turned into soldiers for Thultanthar's army.
