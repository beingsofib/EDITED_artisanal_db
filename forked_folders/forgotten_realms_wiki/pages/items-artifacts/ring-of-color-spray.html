A ring of color spray was a magical ring that allowed the wearer to shoot out a vibrant array of colored light towards others.

Description

The central gem of the ring shone with every color of the visual spectrum when hit with light.

Powers

The wearer of the ring could cast the spell color spray.

History

As of the , one of these rings could be found in a harpy nest outside of the Emerald Grove.
