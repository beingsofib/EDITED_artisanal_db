Flame-eye

Item
nameFlame-eye
typeEnchanted mirror

Flame-eye was a magical mirror possessed by the T'u Lung wu-jen Ghutan Dox Ayani, around 1357 DR.

Powers

This mirror allowed one to scry upon others through open flames.
