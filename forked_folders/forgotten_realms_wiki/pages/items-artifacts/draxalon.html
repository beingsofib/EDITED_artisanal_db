Draxalon, also known as Defender of Feminine Virtue, was an enchanted sword that could only be wielded by women.

History

The wizard Eriel forged Draxalon in the mid-to-late 11 century DR as a gift for her fighter sister, Diana. For many years, Diana would use her Defender in battle.

However, when Diana went adventuring in the ruins of Myth Drannor, both she and the sword were lost.

Powers

A long sword +3, the wielder of this sword could gain the benefit of haste once a day. It could only be used by a female, and in fact a male could not even lift the sword.
