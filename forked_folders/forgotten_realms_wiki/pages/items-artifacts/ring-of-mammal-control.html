Ring of mammal control

Item
nameRing of Mammal Control
typeRing
value2e5,000 gp

A ring of mammal control was a magic item that allowed the wearer to control mammals.

Powers

The wearer of this ring had complete control over mammals possessing semi-intelligence or lower. This control was so strong that the mammal could even be commanded to kill itself. Control was maintained as long as the wearer concentrated.

Notable Owners

Minak al-Zafyi, a shopkeeper in the Grand Bazaar of Huzuz, wore a ring of mammal control.
