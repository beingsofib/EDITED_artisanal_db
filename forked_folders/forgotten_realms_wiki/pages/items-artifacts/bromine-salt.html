Bromine salts or bromides were an uncommon mineral salt used in a variety of trades.

Availability

  • Bromine salts were refined from seawater alongside regular salt production.
  • Alkaline lakes in Zakhara's deserts such as the Pit of the Ghuls were a source of alkaline salts, bromine salts, and table salt, but were often too remote and dangerous to exploit.
  • Aurora's Emporium sold bromine salts for 1 gp per .

Usages

  • Bromine salts were used in wizard's laboratories for various processes.
  • Airy water could use a small handful of bromine salts as a material component.
  • Otiluke's bubbling buoyancy used a small cork and a pinch of bromine salt for its material components.
