Opium was a type of drug that was derived from the poppy flower.

History

In 1357 DR, opium dens, establishments where the drug was sold and smoked, were quite common in the hedonistic city of Semmishi.

In 1482 DR, in the Little Calimshan district of Baldur's Gate one could partake in opium at the gambling den Oasis.

Notable Opium Products

Laudanum was a tincture of opium and alcohol.
