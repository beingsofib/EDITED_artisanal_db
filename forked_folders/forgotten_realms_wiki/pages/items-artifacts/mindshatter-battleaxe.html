Mindshatter (battleaxe)

Item
nameMindshatter
typeBattleaxe
level2e+3

Mindshatter was a gargantuan battleaxe that was the weapon wielded by the avatar of Deep Duerra, the duergar deity of psionics and conquest.

Powers

This huge battleaxe had a +3 enchantment. When it struck a creature, it would steal their psionic strength in great proportions.
