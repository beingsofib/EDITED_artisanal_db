A mess kit was a compact collection of essential cutlery and kitchen implements that allowed preparation and consumption of food while traveling.

Description

A typical mess kit consisted of a metal container whose sides worked as a cooking pan and as a plate or bowl. The container stored a cup and basic cutlery while on the road.

Special mess kits were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue, that also included three vials for holding spices.
