A rolling pin was a cooking utensil used to knead dough. and could be used as an improvised weapon.

History

Sometime in the 14 century DR, the cargo ship The Gentle Lady was delivering a shipment of farmer and merchant goods to a small coastal community in the Dragon Reach. Among the goods being carried by the ship was a crate full of cookware that included three rolling pins.

Notable rolling pins

  • Orc rolling pin, a variety of enchanted rolling pins of orcish-make that could be wielded as deadly bludgeoning weapons.

Notable Owners

  • Frieda Doeskin of Ravens Bluff owned one.
  • The crafty tinkerer rock gnomes of Gnomengarde had a special mechanical rolling pin.
  • Ymelda of Deepingdale owned one.
