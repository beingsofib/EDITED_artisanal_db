Puppets were items in the Realms popular among bards and performers who placed them over their hands to create dramatic performances.

Description

Puppets came in a variety of character shapes and sizes.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold a wide variety of puppets, which they marketed as a tool for bards, at a price of 7 gold pieces a piece. The puppets that they sold resembled variously bards, crones, cutpurses, farmers, herdsmen, kings and queens, mages, maidens, merchants, mothers, noblemen, orcs, peasants, priests, princes and princesses, skeletons, slaves, and soldiers. They also sold dragon puppets, made of silk from Shou Lung, that required three hands to operate and cost 15 gp to order.

Varieties

  • Marionettes, a type of puppet that were controlled from above by maneuvering a set of strings.

Notable Users

  • Spunk, an adventuring mage in the 14 century DR who as a lark frequently pretended that his wizard hand puppet Grizzard was actually the one casting spells.
