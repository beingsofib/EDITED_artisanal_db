A wand of smiles was a common magical wand that forced a creature to smile.

Powers

With this wand, a caster was able to target any creature within . It forced the target creature to smile for one minute. The wand had three charges, which were recharged daily at dawn. There was a chance that, after using up the last charge, the wand could spontaneously become a wand of scowls
