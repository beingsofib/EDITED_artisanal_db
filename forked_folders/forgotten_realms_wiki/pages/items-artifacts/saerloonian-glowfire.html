Saerloonian Glowfire was a wine exported from Saerloon. It was also sold through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue,

Description

This renowned wine had a slight luminescence, adding a mystical quality to the wine. Neither sweet nor dry, this aromatic wine conjured images of refreshing summer breezes.

Prices

Saerloonian Glowfire could be purchased in the following containers:

  • Hand keg (5 sp)
  • Cask (26 sp)
  • Barrel (7 gp)
  • Butt (22 gp)
  • Tun (54 gp)

History

In the , A Modest Guide to Sword Coast Wine offered Saerloonian Glowfire as much praise as it showed disdain for mulled wines such as Daggerford clarry and zzar.
