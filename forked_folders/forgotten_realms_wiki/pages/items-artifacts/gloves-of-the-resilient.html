Gloves of the resilient

originMyth Drannor, north Faerûn

The gloves of the resilient were a set of light leather gloves found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The gloves gave the wearer improved physical fortitude and resistance to blows, as well as much greater speed of movement.

History

The gloves were found by the Elves of Cormanthor when they investigated the Speculum, searching for Kelgoth Darksong of Eilistraee's Wanderers.
