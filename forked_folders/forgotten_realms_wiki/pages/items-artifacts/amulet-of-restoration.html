Amulet of restoration

Item
nameAmulet of Restoration
typeAmulet
locationBaldur's Gate, West Faerûn
value5e160 gp

Amulets of restoration were enchanted necklaces of gemstones and delicate chains with their center stone mottled with age and said to had been dyed by the tears of some unnamed martyr.

Powers

The wearer of this amulet could cast the healing word and mass healing word once per day each.

History

Circa the , one of the amulets of restoration belonged to the dwarven herbalist from Baldur's Gate named Derryth Bonecloak. She carried the amulet along with some of her stock while gathering fungi by the Ebonlake Grotto in the Underdark. Another amulet of restoration was stashed away in the Counting House of Baldur's Gate.
