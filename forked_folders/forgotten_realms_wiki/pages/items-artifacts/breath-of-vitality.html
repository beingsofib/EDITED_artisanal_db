Breath of vitality

Item
nameBreath of Vitality
typeNecklace
originMyth Drannor, north Faerûn

The breath of vitality was a magical necklace found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The necklace offered unparalleled vitality and assisted spellcasting in the fields of positive energy and healing magic. Divine magic in particular was boosted as if the priest was a much higher level of skill.

History

The necklace came to light the guardnorn Phathroi sent the Elves of Cormanthor to attack a Cult of the Dragon base in the ruins of Myth Drannor, where the Red Wizards of Thay were attempting to control the dracolich Karliath.
