Potato leek soup was a type of dwarven soup in Faerûn, named for its prominence of leeks and potatos.

Notable Users

Businesses

  • The Flying Stag, an inn in Voonlar.
  • Phontyr's Unicorn, an inn in Elturel.
  • The Whistling Wizard, an inn in Voonlar.
  • Leek and potato soup was on the menu at the Lonesome Tankard of Eveningstar in Cormyr.

Regions & Settlements

  • This dish was a mainstay in Mithral Hall, being served year round.
  • In dwarven cuisine, leek bread was a staple often served alongside potato and leek soup and rothé back steak.
