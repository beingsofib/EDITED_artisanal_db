Cambist's gauntlets were pairs of gauntlets that could occasionally empower the wearer's strikes, at the cost of coin. In the late 14 century DR, a pair of cambist's gauntlets were part of the red dragon Lostmauth's hoard.

Powers

At random, cambist's gauntlets could strengthen an attack made by their wearer. The gauntlets consumed 5 silver pieces every time this power was activated.
