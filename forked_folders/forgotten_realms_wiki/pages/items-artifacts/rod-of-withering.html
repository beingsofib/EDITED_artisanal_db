A rod of withering was a powerful magical rod that weakened those struck by it.

Description

The rod bore a resemblance to an enchanted mace, and could be wielded as such.

Powers

When the rod struck an opponent, it did not inflict physical injuries, but instead sapped the target's strength and stamina if they lacked the fortitude to withstand the magic. Armor did not protect against this magic, and a powerful direct hit could leave the target permanently weakened.
