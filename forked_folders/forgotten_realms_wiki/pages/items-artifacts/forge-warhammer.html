Forge was a magical warhammer and smithing hammer found in the mines of Tethyamar during the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The hammer possessed innate fire and lightning, and blasted whatever it hit with bursts of energy. It proved exceptional at finding weak points and inflicting critical injuries.

History

Forge was part of a line of similar smithing hammers used by the dwarves to create armaments. It was later found after the forces of Hal Stoutheart's expedition defeated Talice Argith and her drow in Tethyamar.
