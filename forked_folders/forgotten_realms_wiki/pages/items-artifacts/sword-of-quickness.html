Sword of quickness

Item
nameSword of Quickness
typeSword (any)
value2e8,000 gp

A sword of quickness was an enchanted sword that enabled the wielder to always strike before their opponent.

Powers

Found more frequently as short swords, these fabulous blades granted the wielder lightning speed to always strike first in every combat exchange.

Notable Owners

  • Cork Renford, a young ambitious adventurer.
  • Jayani al-Jasir, a notorious corsair from Zakhara, wielded a short sword of quickness.
  • Pendleton Merryweather, a well travelled rogue and Viscount of the People of Kathyr wielded a short sword of quickness.
