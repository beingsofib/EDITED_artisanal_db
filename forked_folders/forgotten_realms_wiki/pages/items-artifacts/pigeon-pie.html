Pigeon pie

locationFaerûn

Pigeon pie was a Faerûnian savory game pie made of pigeon meat. It was common in some cities, such as Delzimmer and Khôltar, for the destitute citizenry to sling stones at local pigeons in order to fell them and use their meat in pigeon pies.

Availability

  • Delzimmer.
  • Khôltar.
  • In northwest Faerûn, in the hamlet of Zelbross, the Last Place inn kept its own domesticated pigeons for use in making pigeon pies.

History

The idea of pigeon pie existed in Faerûn as far back as the year , where it could be found eaten by some in the Calim Caliphates.
