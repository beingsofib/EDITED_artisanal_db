Cindermoth cloaks were a variety of enchanted cloaks that retaliated at those attacking its wearer with a searing blast of flame.

Description

Even the lightest rustle of the cloak's fabric released tiny moths of cinder that fluttered around their home and soon disappeared.

Powers

Each successful attack on the cloak's wearer activated its magic – the flaming shroud enchantment that blasted a line of flames at the attacker.

History

As of the , a cindermoth cloak belonged to the mad sewer-dwelling grease-mage named Aelis Siryasius, who inhabited the sewers of Baldur's Gate with a small army of grease mephits, grease elementals, and greaseballs.
