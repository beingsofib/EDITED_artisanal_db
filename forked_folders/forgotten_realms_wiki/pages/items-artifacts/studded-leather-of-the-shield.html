Studded leather of the shield

Item
nameStudded Leather of the Shield
typeStudded leather
locationIcewind Dale, Frozenfar

Studded leather of the shield were types of enchanted light armor imbued with defensive magics.

Powers

These sets of armor proved regular protection on par with mundane studded leathers. Its surface was studded with metal rivets placed close together and forming a flexible layer of metal atop a layer of leather. Additionally, the wearer was able to cast the shield spell twice per day.

History

Some of these sets of magic armor were in use circa the in the Spine of the World and the Frozenfar regions of Northwest Faerûn.
