A waterskin was a bag or pouch of skin that was used to hold water, particularly in hot regions.

Description

Waterskins were often shaped like kidneys and made of goatskin, but they could also come in a variety of other styles. Faerûn,

Powers

Magic waterskins existed throughout the Realms, particularly in Maztica.

Notable Waterskins

  • A notable magical waterskin was the Maztican waterskin of pouring. This magic feathered waterskin was always full and could pour out a gallon of water every minute.
  • The decanter of salt water, which could be found in Undermountain, could turn any liquid poured into it into salt water.

Notable Owners

  • Pikel Bouldershoulder carried a waterskin that had a nozzle attached to it; he loved to fill it with holy water and squirt undead.
