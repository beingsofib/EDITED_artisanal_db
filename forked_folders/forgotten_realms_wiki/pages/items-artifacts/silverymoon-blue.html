Silverymoon Blue was a variety of white wine from the northern city of Silverymoon that gained popularity in Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Silverymoon Blue derived its name from its slight blue tint. It was a light white wine with an excellent taste.

History

Circa the , Silverymoon Blue was imported to the city of Ravens Bluff. It was available from the newly reopened Tavern of the Garden, a tavern and eatery in the Sixstar neighborhood run by Shauna Morden who inherited the business from her late father. Shauna stocked a valuable Silverymoon Blue of a good vintage and offered a case as a reward to a group of adventurers.
