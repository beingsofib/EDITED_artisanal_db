Weaponology

When its trigger was pressed, the pistol's wheel would spin and shower sparks into the enclosed pan where the smokepowder was held.

This weapon required only a single hand to operate and inflicted rather little damage, but compensated with an improved ability to aim accurately.

Availability

  • These weapons were typically rather rare on Toril.
  • Starwheels were sometimes sold or traded across Toril by spelljammer crews, such as the Smiths' Coster trading company, or stolen from those crews by thieves.
  • One particular establishment on Toril where one could purchase starwheels was The Brigadier's Broadside in Skullport.

Prices

On Toril in the mid–14 century DR, a starwheel typically cost around 1,000 gold pieces.

Individuals

  • Lancilon Seenabuck
  • Captain Morgan, a dreaded pirate that sailed the Dragon Reach in the 14 century DR, owned a wheel lock.

Races & Sentient Creatures

  • Wealthy scro often owned and fought with starwheels.

Reputation

Many in Wildspace considered starwheels to be erratic and untrustworthy, on par with muskets.

Trivia

A starwheel over a shield was the symbol of the Smiths' Coster trading company.
