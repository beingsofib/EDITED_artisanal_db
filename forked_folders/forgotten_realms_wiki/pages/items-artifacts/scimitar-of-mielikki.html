Scimitars of Mielikki were enchanted scimitars modeled after the deity Mielikki's favorite weapons, the Hornblades.

Powers

Scimitars of Mielikki possessed an impressively powerful +7 enchantment that made their blade deadlier and guided the wielder's hand towards the target with supernatural ease.

History

As of the , scimitars of Mielikki could be purchased from the cleric of the Three named Katya at the Temple of the Three in Mulsantir.
