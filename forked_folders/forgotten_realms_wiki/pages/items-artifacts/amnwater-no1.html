Amnwater No.1 was a beer exported from the town of Amnwater and could be found throughout North Faerûn and Sword Coast.

History

By 1370s DR this expensive beer from a tavern that grew into a major trade way stop was found all along the Sword Coast and the Trade Way. In Liam's Hold's Holdfast Inn this beer was sold under a dignified name Guest Beer! Amnwater No1.
