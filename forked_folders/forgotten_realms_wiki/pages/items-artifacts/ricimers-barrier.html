The Ricimer's barrier was an enchanted shield, that could be found in the ruins of Myth Drannor.

Description

Ricimer's barrier was simple looking small shield with a sharp spike perturbing from its middle designed to damage its target when shield-bashing.

Powers

Apart from the obvious offensive +3 enchantment to the shield's spike, it served as a small but capable +2 enchanted buckler.

History

In 1369 DR, during the Cult of the Dragon's occupation of Myth Drannor, Ricimer's barrier could be found in possession of hungry margoyles who hunted in Windrider Glade, who plucked the magical item off one of their preys.
