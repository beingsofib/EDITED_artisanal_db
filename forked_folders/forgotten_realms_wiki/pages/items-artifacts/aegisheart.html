Aegisheart

Item
nameAegisheart
typeShort sword
level2e+4

Aegisheart was a magical short sword that was the favored weapon of Arvoreen, the halfling deity of guardians and defense. His avatar wielded it, alongside Hornet, another enchanted short sword.

Powers

Aegisheart was a +4 defending short sword. Arvoreen was able to transform this sword into another form via weapon shift, but was able to bypass the restriction that the weapon must be magical.
