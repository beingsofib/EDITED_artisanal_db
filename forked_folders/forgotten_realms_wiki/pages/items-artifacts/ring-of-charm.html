Ring of charm

Rings of charm were minor enchanted rings that allowed the wearer to bewitch humanoids.

Powers

These bands of precious metal were inlaid with tiny glowing gemstones. The wearer of such ring was able to cast the charm person spell once per day. Additionally, the ring enhanced the wearer's charisma.

History

In the , a ring of charm was worn by Dolon Daemba, an envoy from the Legion of the Chimera to the Black Raven Monastery. Dolon, presumably, used the ring's magic to sway the Aruma Blane's mind on getting involved with the Legion's war against the Ten Towns.
