Sahandrian was an enchanted longsword wielded by Corellon's avatar.

Powers

Sahandrian was a powerfully enchanted longsword that caused immense pain for anyone attempting to hold the blade aside from Corellon. The effect was even more severe for any goblinoids.

History

In the legendary conflict between Gruumsh and Corellon, Sahandrian was destroyed by Gruumsh with the help of Corellon's wife, Araushnee, as one of the first acts of Araushnee's betrayal. It was later restored by Sehanine Moonbow.
