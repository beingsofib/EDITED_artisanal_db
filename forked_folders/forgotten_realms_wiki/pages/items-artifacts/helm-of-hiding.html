Helm of hiding

Item
nameHelm of Hiding
typeHelmet
locationLoudwater, Delimbiyr Vale

The helm of hiding was an enchanted helmet that hid its wearer from magical prying.

Description

It appeared to be a rather plain old war helm.

Powers

Per Laeral Rythkyn, a Harper wizard of note, it prevented all mental scrying on the wearer, hid them from searching magic, and allowed them to cloak themselves in shadow on command. The rest of the armor had other properties, but they were akin to so much shaped metal.

History
