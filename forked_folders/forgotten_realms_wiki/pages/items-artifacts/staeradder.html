Staeradder

Item
nameStaeradder

Staeradder was a deadly poison.

Powers

In high doses, staeradder was instantly deadly. In lower doses, it could be used to build an immunity if taken for years, but caused nausea and weakness.

History

While invading the sanctum of Vangerdahast, the then-retired Royal Mage of Cormyr in the , Harnrim Starangh used darts coated in staeradder as one of his weapons. He had already developed a resistance to this venom.
