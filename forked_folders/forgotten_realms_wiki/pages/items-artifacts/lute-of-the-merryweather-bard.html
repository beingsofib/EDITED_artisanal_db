The lute of the merryweather bard was an enchanted musical instrument from the late 15 century DR.

Powers

This decorated lute hummed with magic, barely containing the voices of sprites and spirits.

History

The lute of the merryweather bard could be found in the Western Heartlands, along the banks of the River Chionthar in the late 15 century DR.
