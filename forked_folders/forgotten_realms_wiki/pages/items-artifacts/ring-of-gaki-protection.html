Ring of gaki-protection

Item
nameRing of Gaki-Protection
typeRing
locationRainbow Monastery, the Hordelands

A ring of gaki-protection was a magic item that could be found within the Rainbow Monastery in the Hordelands.

Description

This item was a plain silver ring.

Powers

When worn by a priest, this ring allowed the wearer to turn all kinds of vile gaki as if they were undead.
