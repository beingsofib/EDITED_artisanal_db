Quickspell gloves were a pair of enchanted gloves once worn by the wizard Bucknard sometime before the late 15 century DR.

Powers

Quickspell gloves boosted the wearer's digit dexterity allowing them to cast cantrip spells with mere moments, greatly improving their casting time. This property could be used by the wearer once per day.

History

The gloves' original owner, mage Bucknard was known for the speed with which he could cast spells. He believed that the casting speed was the most important part of spellcasting because even if one flubbed a casting, they had enough time to run away from danger. In the , quickspell gloves were among the magic items sold by the Sorcerous Sundries shop in the city of Baldur's Gate.
