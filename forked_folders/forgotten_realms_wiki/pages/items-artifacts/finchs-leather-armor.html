Finch's leather armor was a unique set of leather armor worn by the daring half-elven bard Finch in the late 14 century DR.

Description

The armor was designed to be functional and to look stylish. It was dyed dashing red and was ensorcelled to possess a +2 enchantment and to offer significant resistance to enemy spells. However, the leathers' magics made it only wearable by half-elven bards who served the powers of good.

History

In the , Finch wore his red leathers while performing in the city of Neverwinter and adventuring across the Sword Coast North.
