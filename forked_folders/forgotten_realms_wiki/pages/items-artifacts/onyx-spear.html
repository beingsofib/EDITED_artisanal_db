Onyx spear

Onyx spears were enchanted and deadly spears wielded by the Batiri of the Stone Knives tribe in Samarach.

Description

There crude polearms had spearheads carved of razor-sharp onyx, more deadly than those made of steel.

Powers

Onyx spears had an enchanted keen edge that could deliver devastating damage with a luckily aimed strike.

History

By the , Batiri goblins of the Stone Knives tribe wielded such spears. However, it was unknown if the weapons were of their own making or taken from one of their many enemies.
