The Templar's bulwark was an enchanted mithral tower shield found in Eberron, used by the Templars of the Church of the Silver Flame.

Powers

The Templar's bulwark specially protected against acid, fire, electricity, and cold.

History

These shields were said to have been blessed by the champion Tira Miron, and were carried by Templars of the Silver Flame. These shields were awarded to adventurers who fought the Lords of Dust in Stormreach and purified the corrupt altars in their hidden safehouse, before they faced the Spinner of Shadows and Lolth at the Spinner's Prison.
