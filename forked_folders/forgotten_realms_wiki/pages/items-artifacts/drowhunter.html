Drowhunter was a magical dwarven war axe found in the mines of Tethyamar during the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The blade was specially made to destroy drow, but its slaying abilities extended to all elfkind. Even against non-elves, the axe was more than capable of instantly killing a foe with a single devastating blow.

History

Drowhunter originally made to fight the drow, and was later found in the ruins of Ironvale Deep when a dwarven expedition led by Hal Stoutheart searched for the jewels of ardor, running into the drow of House Argith in the process.
