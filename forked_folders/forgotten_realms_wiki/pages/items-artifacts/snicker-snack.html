Snicker-Snack was an enchanted greatsword in the possession of Iggwilv.

Description

It was gold with an ornate hilt and crossguard, purple surfaces on the blade, and a rose-like shape on the hilt.

Powers

This greatsword had a +3 enchantment. In addition, it carried the enchantment of a vorpal blade.

Trivia

The name "Snicker-snack" is probably a reference to Lewis Carroll's poem Jabberwocky. When the protagonist beheads the Jabberwock, Carroll writes "his vorpal blade went snicker-snack." Considering Carroll invented the word vorpal for Jabberwocky (and that vorpal swords have a chance to behead people), it seems likely the name of this sword is meant to reference Carroll's poem.
