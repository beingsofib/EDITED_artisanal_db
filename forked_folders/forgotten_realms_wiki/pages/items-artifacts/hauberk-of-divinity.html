Hauberks of divinity were enchanted suits of paladin chainmail armor, encountered in Chult in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

These heavy chainmails were imbued with a +4 enchantment to the wearer's armor. Additionally, these hauberks improved the wearer's vitality. However, the most notable magical feature was its ability to awaken faith even in the hearts of the most stoic unbelievers.

Notable Owners

Dragonbait: a saurial paladin and a one-time companion of Artus Cimber during their foray into the jungles of Chult in the late 15 century DR.
