Final Arbiter was an enchanted sword wielded by Sheikh Ardasir al-Darah of the Jann of the Haunted Lands.

Description

Final Arbiter was a great scimitar. The hilt was made from pure gold.

Powers

The sword, combined with Ardasir's great strength, caused massive amounts of physical harm to targets.
