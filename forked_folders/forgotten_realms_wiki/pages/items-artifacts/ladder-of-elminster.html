A ladder of Elminster was a special kind of ladder used by some performers.

Uses

Popular with tumblers and acrobats, these ladders were balanced so that a dextrous individual could climb up one side and down the other while the ladder was standing upright without leaning on a solid object. These ladders were classified into various skill levels for beginners at , masters at , and "mages" at .

Prices

A ladder of Elminster could be purchased through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 5 silver pieces—8 gold pieces depending on its size.
