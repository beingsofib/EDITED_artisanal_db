Glittering necklace

Item
nameGlittering Necklace
typeAmulet
locationSword Coast

Glittering necklaces were common enchanted amulets imbued with illusion magic.

Powers

Glittering necklaces came in various designs and different light colors. They were decorated with dull gray stones that glowed with illusory inner light with a radius. The necklace remained cold to the touch when activated.

History

In the late 14 century DR, glittering necklaces were a common commodity, found in inventories of many traders, wizards, rogues, and temples.

Notable Owners

  • Grimbold Tornhelm, a duergar raider and mercenary who operated in the Underdark underneath the Frozenfar region in the late 14 century DR.
  • Kyleena Moonglow: the chieftain of a wild elf tribe who attempted to take the land from Daggerford's farmers in 1372 DR.
