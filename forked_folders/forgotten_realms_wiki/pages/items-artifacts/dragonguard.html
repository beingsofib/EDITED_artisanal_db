Dragonguard

Item
nameDragonguard
typeMagical breastplate

Dragonguard was an enchanted breastplate that protected the wearer from dragon's breath.

Description

The plate of armor was well made. The plate was decorated to show the shape of a golden dragon.

Powers

Dragonguard had a +1 enchantment, and allowed the wearer to have a higher chance of resisting the effects of dragon's breath.

History

Some time in the 10 century DR, the breastplate was specifically crafted for Tergon in the Forge of Spells of the Wave Echo Cave.

Circa 1491 DR, it was guarded by a spectator that was summoned by the original mages that dwelt in the cave.

Notable Owners

  • Tergon, a hero of Neverwinter.
