Natron

Substance
nameNatron
typeMineral salt
colorWhite

Natron was a mineral salt used for a variety of purposes, and a type of soda ash.

Availability

Natron was harvested from mud at the bottom of the sea.

Usages

  • Natron could be used as a soap.
  • Natron was an ingredient in glassmaking.
  • Natron was used to turn woollen fleece into felt.
  • Natron was an ingredient of an ancient Calim potion that would instil long-term sleep or paralysis.
  • Sea mud natron was used in ancient Calim to perform mummification, as it dried out the flesh.
