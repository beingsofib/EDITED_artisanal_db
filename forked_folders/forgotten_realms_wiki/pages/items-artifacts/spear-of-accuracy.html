Spear of accuracy

Item
nameSpear of Accuracy
typeSpear
locationZakhara

Spears of accuracy were enchanted spears that were found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Powers

The magic of these spears made it easier for the wielder to strike and harm their opponent. These effects were multiplied even more if a spear of accuracy was thrown versus being wielded as a melee weapon.
