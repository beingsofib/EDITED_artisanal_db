Smokeless torch

Item
nameSmokeless Torch
typeTorch
locationUnapproachable East, Shining South

A smokeless torch was a form of illumination used in remote areas of Faerûn such as Laothkund in the Unapproachable East or Adama's Tooth in the Shining South. In both cases it was found underground, in wizards' abodes, where no expense was spared. Though bright, they generated uneven light.
