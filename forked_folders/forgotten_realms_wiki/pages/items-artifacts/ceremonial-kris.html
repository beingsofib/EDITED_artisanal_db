A ceremonial kris was an enchanted Rashemi dagger, found in Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.

Description

The hilt of a ceremonial kris was made out of black metal and encrusted with a big purple gemstone in its center. The blade was a typical snaking shape of a kris, and it was made out of a bright green material.

Powers

This green blade allowed the wielder to cast poison spray upon a successful strike.

Notable Owners

  • Shevarith Kendia: a Rashemi wizard who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
