Sharptack

nameSharptack

Short sword
level2e+4

Sharptack was the sword that was wielded by the avatar of Dugmaren Brightmantle, the dwarven deity of invention, scholarship, and discovery.

Powers

This magical sword had a +4 enchantment. It was able to cast the feeblemind spell two times per day.
