Yawning Portal daggers were complimentary enchanted daggers supplied to the guests who stayed at the Yawning Portal as of the late 14 century DR.

Description

Most of the Yawning Portal daggers were masterfully crafted but dulled due to heavy use and time. Their hilts bore markings of the creator, a hidden wizardly cabal from somewhere on the Sword Coast.

Powers

Despite bearing a +2 enchantment that guided the wielder's hand toward the target, Yawning Portal daggers were far from being deadly weapons, dealing less damage due to their dull blades.

History

As of the , Durnan, the proprietor of the Yawning Portal, supplied his guests with these enchanted daggers.
