Lathander's Red was a variety of wine brewed by the priests of Lathander in Dagger Falls.

Description

A valuable red wine, it was typically kept in small casks below Lathander's Light.

History

In the , the temple of Lathander's Light was burnt down by Eragyn the Dark. Despite this, many casks of Lathander's Red survived the fire because they were stored in the wine cellar below ground.
