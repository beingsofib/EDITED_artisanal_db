Sword of stalking

Item
nameSword of Stalking
typeMagic sword

The sword of stalking was a highly enchanted bastard sword.

Description

The magical bastard sword had a +4 enchantment.

History

Many decades prior to 1358 DR, a queen of spiders that lurked in the caverns beneath Waterdeep trapped a valiant fighter. The bastard sword that belonged to the warrior was lost to the passage of time, and was kept amongst rotting armor and refuse.
