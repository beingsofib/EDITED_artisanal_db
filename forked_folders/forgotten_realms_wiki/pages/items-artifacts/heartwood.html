Heartwood was a worked piece of magical wood found in the late 15 century DR. Due to its unique properties, it was able to be used similarly to a docent of Eberron.

Description

The artifact resembled a heart carved from wood. When installed in a warforged, heartwood spread green mossy growth across their body.

Powers

The living wood greatly enhanced the protective and regenerative properties of a warforged's form while it was used.

History

The wood was carved from the heartwood at the center of an ancient tree. Heartwood was found in the treasure pile of the red dragon Daeronnax, the master of Clan Daeronnax in the Stonelands after he kidnapped the adventurer Tristran of Daggerford.
