Facemelter was a unique greatclub imbued with corrosive magics that was wielded by Slabhead, the leader of verbeegs of Icewind Dale in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

Facemelter was a club made of enchanted wood that secreted a type of powerful corrosive acid. Each swing of the weapon resulted in the verbeeg's enemies' faced being melted off with the acid as well as the club's +2 enchantment that aided the wielder's aim and damage potential.

History

in the , Facemelter was wielded by Slabhead when his giants took over the town of Termalaine in Icewind Dale, with the help from his master Iceblood's snow trolls, winter wolves, and winter hags. The club changed hands after Slabhead was killed by the Hero of Neverwinter.
