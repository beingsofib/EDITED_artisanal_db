Sal ammoniac

Substance
nameSal ammoniac
typeMineral Salt
colorYellow-brown
usesAlchemical ingredient

Sal ammoniac was a mineral salt used in alchemy and metalworking.

Usages

  • Sal ammoniac was used to distil aqua fortis into aqua regia, used to purify gold.
  • Sal ammoniac had some use in alloying adamantine, iron, and molybdena together.
