The Botany Bay was a ship that sailed the waters of Maer Dualdon sometime before the early 14 century DR.

History

The Botany Bay's helm was mounted on the wall of The Salty Dog Tavern in the town of Targos in Icewind Dale. The ship's name was inscribed on the wheel's wood. It was displayed by the tavern's proprietor Gohar as early as the .
