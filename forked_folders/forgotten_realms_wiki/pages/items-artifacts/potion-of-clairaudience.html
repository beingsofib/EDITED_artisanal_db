Potion of clairaudience

Item
namePotion of Clairaudience
typePotion
value2e400 gp

A potion of clairaudience was a potion capable of greatly increasing the imbiber's hearing.

Powers

When consumed this potion increased the imbiber's hearing as per the clairaudience spell. The potion also allowed the imbiber to hear within unknown areas up to distant. These effects lasted for twenty minutes.

Availability

  • The Diviner's Guild of Ravens Bluff was known to sell potions of clairaudience for around 375 gp in the 14 century DR.

Creation

The ear drum and canal of a fachan were often used as a material component when brewing potions of clairaudience.

Notable Owners

Ardasir al-Darah, of the Jann of the Haunted Lands, carried a potion of clairaudience.
