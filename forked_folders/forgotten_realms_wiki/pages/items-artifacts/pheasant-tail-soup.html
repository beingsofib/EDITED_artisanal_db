Pheasant tail soup was a soup dish originating from Elturel in the Western Heartlands. It was an Elturian favorite circa 1366 DR.

Description

It was a soup made of a thick simmered meat stock and served hot. In quality establishments like the Phontyr's Unicorn inn in Elturel, this was made from pheasant or other small game fowl depending on availability. In dishonest places, like The Raging Lion in Scornubel, it could well include small rodents or what other meat happened to come to hand.

It was actually named for the three pheasant feathers standing upright beside each bowl as adornment. Volo described it as one of the more adventurous dishes at the Phontyr's Unicorn.
