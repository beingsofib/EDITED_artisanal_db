The theurgy of winter

Item
nameThe Theurgy of Winter
typeRing
originMyth Drannor, north Faerûn

The theurgy of winter was a magical ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The ring embodied the merciless power of the moon and the deadly might of winter. It helped the wearer avoid and survive all sorts of danger, aided spellcasting that channeled the biting cold of winter, and carried a deep chill that inflicted itself upon enemies.

History

The ring was found in the House of Gems when Sharora Leonala and the Elves of Cormanthor searched for the phylactery of the lich Droic Orgul.
