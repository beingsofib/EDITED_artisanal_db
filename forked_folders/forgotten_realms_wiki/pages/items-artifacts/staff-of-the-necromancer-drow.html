The staff of the necromancer was a magical staff used by the drow of Sschindylryn's House Szind in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The staff let the user cast spells as if at a much higher lever of proficiency, and generally improved their effectiveness. It was particularly tied to spells that related to necromancy, negative energy, and poison.

History

The staff was used by the necromancers of House Szind, who made creating the undead the family business. When the forces of Cormyr counterattacked during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, they may have found and stolen the staff of the necromancer during their raid on House Szind.
