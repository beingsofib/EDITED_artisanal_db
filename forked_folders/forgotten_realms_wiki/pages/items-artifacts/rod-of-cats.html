A rod of cats was a magical rod that granted powers associated with felines and thievery.

Description

It was a black wooden staff 7 feet (2.1 meters) in length, with a carved cat's head on a knob at one end.

Powers

It could be wielded as a +1 quarterstaff. When held, the rod of cats greatly improved the wielder's ability to climb, hide, move stealthily, and see in low light conditions.

Once per day, the wielder could use the rod to cast darkness, but only upon the rod. Anyone holding the rod of cats was immune to this spell's effects.

There was a hidden compartment in the rod but it was only large enough to hold a scroll or some thieves' tools.

Creation

Creation of this rod required the spells cat's grace, darkness, darkvision, and spider climb.

Cost

The rod was worth 25,000 gold pieces.
