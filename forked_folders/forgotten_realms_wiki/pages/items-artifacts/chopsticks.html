Chopsticks were paired eating utensils found primarily in Kara-Tur.

Description

Plain bamboo chopsticks were cheap and common, while people of means used lacquered chopsticks.

Usage

Chopsticks were part of the regular kit of any sensible traveler from Kara-Tur.

Broken and whole chopsticks could be used for the "stick magic" of Trigrams divinations.

A pair of tightly-clutched chopsticks could be used as an emergency backup weapon if nothing else was available, though they were of inferior reach and lethality to any other weapon, and utterly terrible at dealing with armor.
