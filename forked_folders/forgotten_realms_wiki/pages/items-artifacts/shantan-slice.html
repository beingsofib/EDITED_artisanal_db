Shantan Slice was the name given to the cheese produced in the Grand Duchy of Shantal in the Border Kingdoms of South Faerûn.

Description

Shantan was sharp in flavor and red in color. It was among several other notable food items produced by the Grand Duchy. Among others were Old Oakey dark beer, vegetables, sausages and hams.
