Thorass alphabet

Item
nameThorass
typeWriting script

Thorass was the name of the alphabet used by the large majority of human languages on Faerûn, including Common.

The alphabet was first developed for use with the Thorass language by a scribe whose name has been lost to history It was influenced by the runes of the Untheric language.

Thorass was the script used in Common, Alzhedo, Chessentan, Chondathan, Durpari, Illuskan, Midani (in Anauroch only), Rasallesian, Tuigan, Turmic, and Uluik.
