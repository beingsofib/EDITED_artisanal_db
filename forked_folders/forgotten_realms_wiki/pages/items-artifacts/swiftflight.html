Swiftflight

Item
nameSwiftflight

Swiftflight was an intelligent magic +3 mighty composite oathbow carried by Methrammar Aerasumé and made for him by his half-brother Tyresia as a parting gift. It contained some of Tyresia's personality, so Methrammar was particularly fond of it.
