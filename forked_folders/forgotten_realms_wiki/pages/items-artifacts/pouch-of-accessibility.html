Pouch of accessibility

Item
namePouch of Accessibility
typeBag
value2e12,500 gp

A pouch of accessibility was a magic item capable of carrying a great amount of weight.

Powers

These mundane looking pouches could carry up to of weight within 30 interior compartments. No more than of weight, or of volume, could be stored in one of the compartments. To retrieve an item inside of the pouch the name of the item simply needed to be spoken aloud, causing the item to appear at the top of the pouch where it could be grabbed.

A pouch of accessibility weighed when empty and only if filled to capacity.

Notable Owners

  • Endrenn Allerendris, an elven acrobat from Tantras.
  • Khalid al-Karim, a half-elven rawun from Zakhara, carried a pouch of accessibility.
