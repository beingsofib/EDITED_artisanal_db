Seal of amusement

Item
nameSeal of Amusement
typeMagic item
creatorLaeral Silverhand

The seal of amusement was a unique enchanted coin crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Jarlaxle in the late 15 century DR. It was one of many pieces of so-called "Silverhand gear" crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep.

Description

The coin could spin eternally, although it was unclear if Silverhand originally gave it any additional properties. Jarlaxe, finding the trinket amusing, sharpened the coin's edges to a razor's edge, and would use it to prank gambling partners and pickpockets. Injuries from the cruelly whimsical weapon left lasting injuries.

Notable Owners

  • Jarlaxle, the drow leader of Bregan D'aerthe and of Luskan in the late 15 century DR, to whom Silverhand also gifted the rapier of derobement.
