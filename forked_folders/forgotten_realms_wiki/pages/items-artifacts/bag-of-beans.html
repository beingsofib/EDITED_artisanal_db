A bag of beans was a rare magical item.

Description

The bag looked like a regular small sack and was made from heavy cloth. On average their dimensions were in width and in length.

Powers

The bag contained dried beans, which looked like regular ordinary beans but they contained powerful magical properties. If a bean was dropped on the ground, it exploded in a brilliant fireball. According to some it was safest to remove them by hand, rather than using any sort of tool or telekinesis.

The owner of the bag had no way of knowing what power a bean held. But if a bean was planted, the magical properties were revealed. The magical effects of the beans ranged from minor to incredibly powerful.

The known magical properties of the beans are listed below:

  • Spawn a treant, although there was a risk it would be evil.
  • Summon an aggressive bulette.
  • Create a patch of toadstool mushrooms.
  • Create a statue that resembled the individual who planted the bean. It was rooted in place but for a day it was animate, and was verbally aggressive towards the individual who planted the bean.
  • Create a tree bearing fruit. The fruit had the effect of various potions.
  • Create a large pyramid, which contained a mummy lord.
  • Sprout a patch of shriekers.
  • Create a campfire of blue flame that burned for a full day.
  • Summon a geyser of liquid. The liquid could be water, beer, juice, tea or even oil.
  • Sprout a gigantic beanstalk.

Prior to the Spellplague, magical properties of the beans included the following:

  • Spawn a cloud of blinding smoke that covers an area of for a few minutes.
  • Create a deep hole in the ground that may contain either a djinni ring or a purple worm.
  • Summon an ice storm to strike the area.
  • Create a poisonous berry bush with animated runners, whose berries were in actuality gemstones that ranged from being worthless to around 100 to 500 gp in value.
  • Summon a slow seepage of poisonous gas from the cloud, forming a radius cloud that persists for one minute. Once dissipated, the ground below where it had floated could be some form of magical dust, such as dust of appearance, sneezing and choking, or vanishing.
  • Sprout a patch of shriekers.
  • Summon an aggressive wyvern whose sting strikes like a javelin of piercing.
  • Summon a whole field of pumpkin plants almost instantaneously. Unfortunately, in a matter of moments they would grow, ripen, rot, and then explode into a stinking cloud , leaving behind one or more evil scarecrows.

Notable Users

  • The avatars of Garl Glittergold always carried a bag of beans with them.
