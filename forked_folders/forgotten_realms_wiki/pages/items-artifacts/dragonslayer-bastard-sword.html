Dragonslayer (bastard sword)

Dragonslayer was the name of an enchanted bastard sword, perhaps one of many such weapons forged throughout the history of Toril, or perhaps the very blade of fables and fireside tales said to have struck down the dragons of countless legends.

Powers

The sword possessed a +1 enchantment that made it deadlier and guided its blade toward the wielder's target. It possessed a +5 enchantment against dragons and dealt more damage to those creatures.

History

The exact origins of this magic weapon were unknown. It was possible that this was the legendary dragon-slaying weapon described in countless tales of a blade, a dragon, a titanic struggle, and a knight saving thousands of lives. Yet it was equally probable that the weapon belonged to a broader tradition of enchanted arms forged for the purpose of slaying dragons.

In the , one such blade surfaced in the town of Port Llast, where it was sold by the dwarven blacksmith Haljal among his over-the-counter wares.
