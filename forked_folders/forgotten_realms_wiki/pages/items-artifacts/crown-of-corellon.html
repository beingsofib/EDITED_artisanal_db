The crown of Corellon was a legendary cleric's circlet from Waterdeep, in the late 15 century DR.

Description

The crown of Corellon was encrusted with glowing magical gems, each represented Halbenet's friend whom they failed to save. Their crown glowed with gentle radiance, reminding them to always defend the friends who were still surrounding them.

Powers

The crown of Corellon enhanced the divine magical abilities of the cleric who wore it and provided some level of defense. The crown's main powers, however, laid in the supremely powerful healing it bestowed on the wearer and their allies.

Notable Owners

  • Halbenet Soladonel: a moon elf cleric of Corellon Larethian who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
