Herbalism kits were sets of tools commonly used by herbalists and apothecaries to prepare and identify medicines and potions and to identify and use medicinal herbs.

Description

A typical herbalism kit contained clippers, a mortar and pestle, gloves, vials, and pouches to manipulate and store herbs and to prepare them for use.

Powers

A herbalism kit was required to brew potions of healing and antitoxins. Its tools also aided the user in identifying edible plants and poisons, and helped in the study of magic that dealt with plants.

Notable Owners

  • Taela Summerhawk and her husband Larlow, halfling apothecaries from Nightstone in the late 15th century DR.
