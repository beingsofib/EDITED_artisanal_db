Whips were a type of exotic, one-handed melee weapon.

Description

The whip was a long, flexible, braided leather cord which tapered gradually from the handle to the tip. The handle was either thick braided leather or leather-covered wood and was in length. When stretched to full length, the whip could be up to in length. An optional tassel of horsehair or twine on the end (called a cracker) amplified the sound of the whip. The average weighed around .

Weaponology

The whip was mostly effective against unarmored creatures. The weapon had an effective range of . Whips could also be used to trip opponents and they were particularly good at disarming. However, it took special training and practice to use a whip effectively without hurting oneself.

Availability

  • In the city of Raven's Bluff, leather whips of assorted lengths and styles could be found for sale at Tym's Supple Leather Shoppe.
