Umber hulk hide heavy shields were shields crafted using umber hulk chitin and imbued with the creature's natural magic.

Powers

These shields were created through stacking together thick umber hulk plates. They offered sturdy physical protection, similar to a +2 enchantment and gave the wielder complete immunity to any mind-affecting abilities and spells.

History

As of the , Umber hulk hide heavy shields were sold by a drow merchant who did business in the Undergate, Westgate's unsavory underbelly.
