Mantle of the Holy Warrior was an enchanted cloak that radiated strengthening aura.

Powers

The wearer of the mantle was granted the ability to cast the crusader's mantle spell once per day, allowing the caster to surrender themselves and all allies within in an aura of holy power, granting all ally attacks additional radiant damage.

History

Sometime in the or before, the Mantle of the Holy Warrior was found after falling into communal steambaths in Baldur's Gate, during "All Saints Morning Retreat and Candlelit Guided Meditation." The mantle was left unclaimed and ended up in possession of the priests in the Gate's Stormshore Tabernacle, available for purchase for devotees with enough coin.
