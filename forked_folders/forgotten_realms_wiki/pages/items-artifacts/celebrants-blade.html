The celebrant's blade was a unique enchanted battleaxe.

Description

This battleaxe was known to be exquisitely beautiful and one could tell its fine craftsmanship just by looking at it.

Powers

This battleaxe possessed a +4 enchantment.

History

The celebrant's blade was forged by the dwarven craftsman Briath Silverhand to celebrate the finished construction of a monument to the dwarven god Moradin in the fortress of Dorn's Deep. He then hid the battleaxe within the monument's stone furnace, with hopes that it would one day be of use to someone seeking to liberate the dwarven fortress from unforeseeable evils.
