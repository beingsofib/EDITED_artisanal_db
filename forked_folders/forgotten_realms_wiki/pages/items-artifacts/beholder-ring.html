Beholder rings were enchanted rings created through through research of beholders by Angwile the Artificer before the early 11 century DR.

Powers

Beholder rings enhanced the wearer's peripheral vision and granted them spell-like abilities of beholders' eye stalks. These rings were created each harnessing one stalk's magic, such as sleep, fear, charming, petrify, etc.

History

Beholder rings were creations of Angwile the Artificer, a svirfneblin inventor and sage. The exact number of rings created was unknown, but they all were created before the gnome's untimely petrification in the .

By the , several of these beholder rings found their way into the Undergate, Westgate's unsavory underbelly, including a beholder ring of sleep.
