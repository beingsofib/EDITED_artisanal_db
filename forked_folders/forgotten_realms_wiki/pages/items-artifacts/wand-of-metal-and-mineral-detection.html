A wand of metal and mineral detection was a magic item that revealed the location of metals.

Powers

The effective range of these wands was . A wand of metal and mineral detection would pulse in the wielder's hand and point toward any detected source of metal or mineral. The wielder could also choose to concentrate on finding only a specific type of metal or mineral such as gold or diamond.

History

A wand of metal and mineral detection lay lost and forgotten deep in the old mines of the Dungeon of Death in as the situation that dark place culminated.

Notable Owners

  • Crennon Centime, a human jeweler and wizard from Amn.
  • Korbus Brightjewel, a gnome jeweler who lived in Daggerford, owned a wand of metal and mineral detection.
