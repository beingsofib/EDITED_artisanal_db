The wand of citrine force was a magical item in Ususi Manaallin's possession as of . It cast a spell similar to ethereal chamber.

Description

The wand of citrine force was a slender rod made of translucent yellow glass.

Powers

The wand of citrine force had two known modes of functioning. One of the modes of functioning cast a spell akin to ethereal chamber on the target it was pointed at, except that unlike the spell, the wand's function could function outside of the ethereal plane. Its second function was its ability to create obstacles; it could be used to block out portals, for instance, in this way.

History

In , Ususi Manaallin held such a wand. It was almost spent; she employed one last charge to distract the Rotting Man.
