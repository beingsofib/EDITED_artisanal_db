Wand of petrification

Item
nameWand of petrification
typeWand

The wand of petrification was a unique wand owned by the half-drow mage Madame Narcosa.

Description

This wand was made of thin, rusty iron. Set at its tip was a fossilized shark's tooth measuring in length.

Powers

When activated, this wand emitted a dark gray beam out to that caused those struck by it to become petrified. The wand was only usable by mages.

Other wielders

The Red Wizard Belathin wielded either the same wand or one like it sometime in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
