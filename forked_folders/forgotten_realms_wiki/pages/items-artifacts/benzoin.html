Benzoin was an aromatic plant resin used in rituals and medical treatments.

Availability

Aurora's Emporium sold benzoin for 1 gp per , intended for use by priests as an aromatic ritual reagent.

Usage

Benzoin was used for a variety of medical purposes, including as an anti-septic, treating wounds and sores, as an expectorant, and as a stimulant.
