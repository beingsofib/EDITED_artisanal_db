Dread helm

Item
nameDread Helm
typeHelm, Wondrous item
rarity5eCommon

A dread helm was a magical helm that made its wearer's eyes glow red.

Description

Dread helms were typically made of steel and had a fearsome appearance.

Powers

The dread helm caused its wearer's eyes to glow red, This power only worked while the user wore it.

Notable Owners

  • Warduke, the evil mercenary of the League of Malevolence.
