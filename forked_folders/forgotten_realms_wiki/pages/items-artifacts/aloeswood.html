Aloeswood was a wood valued for its pleasant smell.

Availability

Aurora's Emporium sold aloeswood chips for 1 gp per , intended for use by priests as an aromatic ritual reagent.

Aloeswood was a regular trade good on the caravans that crossed the Taan region between Faerûn and Kara-Tur.
