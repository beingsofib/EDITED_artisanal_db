Potions of evil protection were magic potions that granted the imbiber protection from creatures of evil and those who venerated the forces of darkness.

Powers

The potions bestowed its protective effects when imbibed, increasing defenses against outsider creatures from planes of evil and from mortals who followed evil creeds and faiths.

History

As of the , potions of evil protection, encased in rustic clay vials could be found in the town of Targos in Icewind Dale, in the hands of magically-inclined citizens of Ten Towns and Reghedmen shamans and traders.
