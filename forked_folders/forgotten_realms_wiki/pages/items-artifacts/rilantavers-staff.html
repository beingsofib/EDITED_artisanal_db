Rilantaver's staff was a type of enchanted staff created by the wizard after which it was named.

Powers

This staff offered +3 protection for up to six creatures who touched it,

Origins

Many of these staves were crafted in the nation of Amn and in the city of Starmantle, where Rilanter operated a business to finance his more dubious travels and endeavors.
