The Rune Hammer was a variety of enchanted warhammers created on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 century DR, with the original and the most powerful variety bing an artifact of the Broodhull Clan of the Vilhon Reach.

Description

This hammer appeared to be etched with glittering silver runes.

Powers

There existed three variants of the Rune Hammers, each possessed a +1, +2, and +8 enchantments what made the bludgeons deadlier and guided the wielder's hand towards the target. All three versions were ensorcelled to offer the wielder spell resistance of various strengths.

A Rune Hammer could be created from an enchanted warhammer and a slab of adamantite.

History

The artifact known as the Rune Hammer was a product of the dwarvish Broodhull Clan that ruled over the mountains of the Vilhon Reach region in Central Faerûn for centuries until its mysterious disappearance. Within a single year, all two hundred members of Clan Broodhull were gone, leaving behind only empty halls. One artifact that the Broodhull Clan left behind was a glittering, rune-carved, mighty war hammer.

More common versions of the Rune Hammer with a simpler +1 enchantment were forged by Master Blacksmith Marrok of Shining Knight Arms and Armor shop of Neverwinter to help the heroes who sought the cure from the dreaded Wailing Death of the . Later the same year, more powerful versions were created by Master Blacksmith Barun of Beorunna's Well to help the forces of Neverwinter stand against Lady Aribeth the Betrayer, who led a marching army of Luskan in the deadly but short-lived Neverwinter-Luskan war.
