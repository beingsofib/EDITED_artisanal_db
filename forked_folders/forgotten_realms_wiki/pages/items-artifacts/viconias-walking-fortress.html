Viconia's Walking Fortress was a unique and powerfully enchanted tower shield used by Mother Superior Viconia DeVir of the Cloister of Sombre Embrace in the late 15 century DR.

Description

This ornate shield's name was derived from the defensive "Walking Fortress" battle technique that aimed to annoy one's opponent by not allowing it to land a single blow.

Powers

Viconia's Walking Fortress possessed a potent defensive +3 enchantment. The wielder of the shield could use its weight and strength to return the attacker's force back at them in a powerful burst of concussive force that had a chance of knocking the opponent prone. Once per hour or a period of short rest and recharge, Viconia's Walking Fortress could surround its wielder in a reflective shell that reflected all missiles that were fired at the wielder, back at their point of origin. Lastly, once per day, the wielder could activate the shield's warding bond enchantment that gave the wielder protection against spells and disadvantaged the attackers' physical, ranged, and magical assaults.

History

Viconia DeVir came to possess her unique tower shield sometime before the , when she was always seen carrying it whilst surrounded by the faithful of the Cloister of Sombre Embrace.
