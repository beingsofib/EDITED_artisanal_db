Frost agate, also called frost stone, was a beautiful member of the agate family found in the Realms. They were sacred to the elven god Erevan Ilesere.

Description

These rare ornamental stones had white markings that resembled frost over the base color of the stone. They were usually tumbled and polished to a beautiful glassy finish. A master could cut the fragile stone in , such as in a cut, so that at each point where facets met, a "snowflake" of white "frost" appeared. A typical stone had a base value of 10 gp.

Powers

Frost agates were often enchanted to be luckstones. Low quality stones were treated like other agates: powdered for use in sleeping draughts and other potions.
