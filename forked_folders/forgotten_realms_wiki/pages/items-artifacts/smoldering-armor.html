Smoldering armor

Item
nameSmoldering Armor
typeArmor
rarity5eCommon

A smoldering armor was a magicallly enchanted armor.

Powers

While worn, smoke emanated from the armor. The smoke had no odor and had no effect on the individual wearing the armor or creatures in the immediate vicinity.
