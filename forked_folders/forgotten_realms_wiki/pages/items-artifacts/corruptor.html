Corruptor, was the favored weapon of Gargauth, the Faerûnian god of political corruption.

Powers

Corruptor was a powerful enchanted +3 returning throwing dagger that could easily be welded in melee as well as as a ranged weapon. When Gargauth manifested an avatar, he carried an infinite supply of these magic daggers. When a Corruptor was thrown, it disappeared and returned to Gargauth's avatar the moment it struck the target.
