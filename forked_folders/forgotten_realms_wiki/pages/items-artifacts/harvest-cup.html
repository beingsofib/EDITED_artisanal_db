The Harvest Cup was the enchanted trophy award annually to the champion of West Harbor's Harvest Fair.

Description

This tarnished bronze trophy cup bore scratched-on names of West Harbor's champions past, as well as some other things carved into it.

Powers

The Harvest Cup had a minor enchantment that allowed casting of the cure light wounds spell twice per day.

History

By 1374 DR, the Harvest Cup had numerous owners. This trophy was awarded to the champion of the Harvest Fair's competitions. The winner held onto the Cup until the following year's Harvest Fair. In 1374 DR, this Cup was claimed by Kalach-Cha and, following a devastating attack on the town of West Harbor, left the Mere of Dead Men for Neverwinter with its new owner.
