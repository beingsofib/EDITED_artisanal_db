Sharpcrumble was a variety of cheese found in northwest Faerûn and the Sword Coast North regions.

Description

Sharpcrumble cheese was white in color and crumbly with a lovely flavor. The cheese was pressed into handwheels for portioning and sale.

History

Famously, sharpcrumble cheese was made on the family farm of Fulbar Hardcheese in Daggerford in the 14 His family's cheese handwheels were sold at The Happy Cow tavern. However, sharpcrumble cheese was not invented by the Hardcheese family and was known back in the days of the ancient realm of Athalantar.
