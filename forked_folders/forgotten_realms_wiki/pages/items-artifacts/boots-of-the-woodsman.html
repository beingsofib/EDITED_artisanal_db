The boots of the woodsman were leather boots found in Cormyr during the 15 century DR.

Description

The boots were made of supple leather.

Powers

The boots of the woodsman let the wearer move with impressive speed, hide more easily in the shadows, and launch more devastating ambushes—making them as swift and silent as the falling night.

History

These boots were granted to the woodsmen who served the Crown of Cormyr and aided the druids of the King's Forest. Arcton Greenwort supplied these to heroes during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar.
