Bow of awareness was an enchanted halfling short bow designed to be wielded by the small folk as a longbow.

Powers

A bow of awareness possessed a simple +1 enchantment that improved the weapon's aim and damage output. Additionally, it ensured that the wielder was seldomly caught unawares, magically boosting their initiative in battle.

History

In the , one such bow belonged to Roah Moonglow, a hin agent of the Zhentarim who did business with the recently emerged Cult of the Absolute in the Western Heartlands of Faerûn.
