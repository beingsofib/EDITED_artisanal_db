Amlath'hana

Item
nameAmlath'hana
typeLongbow
value2e20,000 gp

Amlath'hana was an enchanted longbow wielded by Corellon's avatar.

Powers

This powerfully enchanted bow would never miss a target up to distant. Arrows shot from it caused considerably more physical harm compared to other bows.
