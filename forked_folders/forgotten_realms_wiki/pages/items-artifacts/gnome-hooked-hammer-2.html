Gnome hooked hammers were a type of two-handed martial hammer, specially designed by gnomes for use by members of their race.

Description

Gnome hooked hammer had relatively short shafts, with a hammer on one end and a sharp-pointed hook (curved only slightly more than a pickaxe) on the other. On average they weighed around .

Weaponology

Due to their size, these hammers were well suitable for fights in cramped spaces. The hooked end of these hammers could be utilized to trip opponents.

Notable Users

  • Nadul DaRoni, a renowned gnomish inventor, was skilled in fighting with gnome hooked hammers.
  • Svirfneblin often had familiarity with fighting with gnome hooked hammers.
  • Thistle Thwom, a gnome cleric.
