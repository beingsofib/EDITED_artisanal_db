Mageslayer was an enchanted mace.

Description

The mace was visibly brimming with power.

History

In late , Mageslayer's powers had been explained to Oprion Blackstone by Fzoul Chembryl. The first considered using it to assault a clone of Manshoon when the latter revealed himself to exist after his presumed death, but later decided against it.
