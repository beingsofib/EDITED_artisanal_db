Primal macuahuitl and its more powerful variant, exalted primal macuahuitl, was a variety of artifact sword created to be used by fighter adventurers in the Chultan city of Port Nyanzaru in the late 15 century DR.

Description

These primal macuahuitl were testaments of their owners' strength and the skills of the master weaponcrafters of Chult. These items were infused with the savage energies of the powerful primal jungle beasts, and once equipped by adventurers, they filled them with the rage and strength of the ancestors. Primal macuahuitl were created to be paired with primal mahuizzochimalli – enchanted shields.

Powers

A primal macuahuitl could be enchanted to hold various unique magics for each owner, in addition to boosting the damage inflicted by the owner in combat and increasing their ability to deflect enemy blows. When a primal mahuizzochimalli was wielded with a primal macuahuitl, both items boosted each other's magical powers, increasing the wielder's vitality, damage output, and boosting their ability to heal, magically and via mundane means.

History

Primal macuahuitl were created in the late 15 century DR, and their design suggested the items' origins to be tied to the ancient Lost City of Omu.
