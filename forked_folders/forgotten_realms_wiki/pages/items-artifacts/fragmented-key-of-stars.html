Fragmented Key of Stars

The fragmented Key of Stars was a sliver of a larger and more powerful artifact called the Key of Stars that was created after the Key was destroyed in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The powers of the Far Realm were still imbued in the fragmented Key of Stars despite it being just a shard of a larger and greater item that could pierce reality and open a gate into the horrors of the Far Realm. The fragmented Key of Stars allowed its master to open a smaller gate and temporarily summon forth a far realm horror unto their enemies. It also empowered the wielder, posting their physical power, ability to deal well-aimed and especially deadly hits, as well as giving them the ability to penetrate enemy armor.

History

The fragmented Key of Stars resulted from a struggle between the heroes of Neverwinter and Nostura and the Cloaked Ascendancy in the late 15 century DR.
