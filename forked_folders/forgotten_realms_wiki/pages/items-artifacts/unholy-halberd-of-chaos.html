Unholy halberd of chaos was an enchanted halberd that craved elvish blood. The weapon was of unknown but likely fiendish origins, created sometime before the early 14 century DR.

Powers

This hefty weapon was created to be wielded exclusively by individuals whose hearts were blackened by evil and chaos. The weapons possessed a +2 enchantment that guided its blade towards the target and made its slashes more powerful than similarly made mundane halberds. In addition to that, the weapon was especially deadly to elves, when wielded against them, it effectively became a powerful and deadly +4 halberd. It also dealt additional bludgeoning damage to elves, creatures of good and those who venerated law. And lastly, the unholy halberd sapped the life force from its enemies and healed the wielder with it.

History

Unholy halberd of chaos's history was unknown but it was believed that it must've once belonged to a foul fiend or a powerful undead creature, a vampire or a death knight. The halberd's desire for elvish blood led some to speculate that the unholy halberd of chaos once belonged to a drow weapon master from the Underdark city of Menzoberranzan.

In the , the halberd belonged to Agaza, the night hag who cursed the inhabitants of Lonelywood in Icewind Dale with sleepless nights and nightmares.
