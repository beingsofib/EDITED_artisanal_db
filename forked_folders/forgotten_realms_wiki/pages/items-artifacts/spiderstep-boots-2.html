Spiderstep boots were a variety of magical boots that could be found on the Sword Coast of Faerûn. They allowed the wearer to readily step on and over spider webs, without impediment.

Description

The surface of the boots had a slightly sticky texture.

Powers

The wearer of these boots was immune from being entangled in webs, whether mundane or magical in nature.

History

As of the , a pair of spiderstep boots could be found within the Whispering Depths, a cavern network found beneath the village of Moonhaven on the Sword Coast.

Rumors & Legends

It was speculated by some that these boots were associated with the Lolthite faith.
