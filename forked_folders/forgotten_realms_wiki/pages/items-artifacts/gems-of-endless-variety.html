Gems of endless variety

nameGems of Endless Variety
typeMagic jewelry
rarity5eLegendary

The gems of endless variety were a set of powerful enchanted jewelry. They could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

The set included earrings and a hair cuff, all made of gold and set with a multiplicity of magical red gemstones.

Powers

The gems of endless variety were associated with wild magic and were the essence of controlled chaos. In battle, they could be used to shape wild magic into beneficial spells, and could be used to bestow random boons on the wearer and their allies that made them generally more deadly and resilient. Outside of battle, they could be used to calibrate magical tools and create magical lights.

Notable Owners

  • Coriolis Rillevay, a gnome sorcerer and adventurer from Waterdeep, who used the gems of endless variety both in her career as an adventurer but also in her pursuits as a tinkerer and sculptor. She used the gems both to help her sculpt and to put on light shows around her finished pieces.
