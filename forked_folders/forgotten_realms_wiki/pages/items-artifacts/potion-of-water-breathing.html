}}Potions of water breathing were magical potions that temporarily allowed the user to breathe underwater.

Description

The liquid that composed a potion of water breathing was cloudy green and smelled like seawater. and displayed a single bubble floating on it that resembled a jellyfish.

Powers

Upon drinking a potion of water breathing, the user gained the power to breathe underwater for the duration of one hour,

Additionally, the potion rendered the imbiber immune to the crushing weight of extreme watery depths.

History

In the late 15 century DR, potions of water breathing were sold at Corellon's Crown, an herbalism shop in Trollskull Alley in the North Ward of Waterdeep, run by the wood elf Fala Lefaliir.

Creation

Suggested special ingredients for such potions were the blood of a water naga, the organs of a nixie, or the slime of an aboleth.
