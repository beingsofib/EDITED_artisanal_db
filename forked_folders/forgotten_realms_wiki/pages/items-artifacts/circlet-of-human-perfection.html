A circlet of human perfection was a magical circlet that transformed its wearer into an attractive human.

Description

The circlet was made of gold.

Powers

Upon wearing a circlet of human perfection, an attuned humanoid instantly transformed into an attractive human. The wearer had no control over the gender or physical appearance of the human form, so attributes such as age, skin and hair color, and voice, were determined by the circlet. The height and weight of the human form were not far from the human average.

The transformation had no effects on any traits other than its wearer's appearance. It reversed as soon as the wearer removed the circlet.

Notable Owners

The goblin Yek wore a circlet of human perfection that he found in Undermountain sometime in . The other goblins in Yek's group resented him for it and believed the item to be a curse.
