The Revenge was one of two ships constructed to begin the rebuilding of Zhentil Keep's navy following the Fall of Zhentil Keep in the .

History

Gondsmen were hired for a secret project during the Revenge's construction. The Revenge first sailed in Marpenoth of the . Later in Uktar of that same year it encountered five hostile ships from the Mulman navy. A grand battle ensued on the waters of the Moonsea and the Revenge was able to test the Gondsmen's secret projects. The Gondsmen had outfitted the Revenge with steel armor plating around the exterior of her hull, and a special steam engine to complement her sails. While the engine only worked briefly, it worked long enough to allow the Revenge to easily outmaneuver all five of the Mulman ships and destroy them in turn.
