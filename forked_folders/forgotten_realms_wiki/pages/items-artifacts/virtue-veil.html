Virtue veil

Item
nameVirtue Veil
typeShroud
locationUtter East

A virtue veil was a holy shroud used in the Utter East during the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

These sheets protected the carrier from attacks by basal golem creatures of evil and undeath. Furthermore, they were immune to the fear caused by basal golem wraiths.
