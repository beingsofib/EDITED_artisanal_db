The Medallion of Time was a piece of jewelry with the power to change the time frame of elements within the Mobius Tower.

Description

The Medallion of Time was large, with an intrincate design.

Powers

The Medallion of Time had the power to change the time frame of any object to the wearer's time frame up to three times a week. It was one of the only two ways that such a thing could be achieved in Mobius' Tower.

History

The Medallion of Time was worn by the Guardian of Time inside Mobius' Tower. Adventurers may have used it to remove the crystal minaret required by Martek's trials, but the Guardian would not have given it up without a fight.
