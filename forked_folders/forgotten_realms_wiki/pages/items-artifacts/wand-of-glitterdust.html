Wand of glitterdust

Item
nameWand of Glitterdust
typeWand
locationResurrection Gorge, Amn

Wands of glitterdust were magic wands that were useful when dealing with invisible threats.

Description

The wand of glitterdust found in the Resurrection Gorge in Amn appeared to be a smooth piece of wood with air around it glittering in a strange light.

Powers

Wands of glitterdust possessed a number of charges of the glitterdust spells, discharged at the wielder's command. The spell could remove invisibility from creatures and objects affected by the spells and blind creatures for a short period of time.

History

One wand of glitterdust was found in a crystal growth at the roots of a great oak tree at the center of the Resurrection Gorge. The wand may have been used against an invisible tanar'ri Xachrimos who was bound to the Gorge.
