Spinning wheel

A spinning wheel was a simple device used to spin fibres into thread, the first step in producing fabrics and textiles. By utilizing a spinning wheel, a person could produce thread much faster and more uniformly than performing the work by hand, making it a very useful tool to have.

Notable Owners

  • Kaitlynn.
  • Nib the Miser owned an enchanted spinning wheel that he used to weave gold into various magical items.
  • The Monastery of the Yellow Rose.

Trivia

A spinning wheel was a holy symbol of the Norse goddess Frigga.
