A Sensate amulet was a powerful amulet associated with the Society of Sensation, one of the major power factions in the Outer Planes city of Sigil.

Description

These amulets were offered as gifts to Sensates that demonstrated complete and total loyalty to their organization.

Powers

A Sensate amulet was designed to offered significant boons to any Sensate traveling the planes looking for new and exciting experiences. It bolstered their inherent vitality and guarded them from malevolent forces.

Additionally, it granted the wearer increased aura of personality, allowing them to more easily make friends and influence others.

History

As of the late 14 century DR, a Sensate amulet was seen to have been in the possession of two different individuals in Faerûn: the merchant Deidre, who specialized in extra-planar items and operated out of the Adventurer Mart of Athkatla in Amn, and the enslaved mind flayer Xithiss within the Black Pits.
