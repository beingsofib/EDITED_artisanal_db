The Faceless Eyes of the Zhentarim was a style of hood that completely covered the wearer's face except for their eyes.

Reputation

This hood was immediately recognizable as a symbol of the Zhentarim, and commanded respect accordingly. It was worn by assassins, and the sight of their eyes through the mask could be a source of fear.

Notable Owners

  • Naomlen Girdaur, a dwarven rogue who worked as an adventurer in Waterdeep during the late 15 century DR.
