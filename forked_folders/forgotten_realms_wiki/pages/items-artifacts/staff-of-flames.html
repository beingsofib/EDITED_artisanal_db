Staff of flames

locationZakhara

The staff of flames was a magical item that could be found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Description

These magical items were available in a wide variety.

Powers

These staves granted to following powers to a wizard at the expense of one charge: affect normal fires, flaming sphere, pyrotechnics, sundazzle, and sunfire.

The following powers consumed two charges for regular wizards and only one charge for flame mages: conjure fire elemental, sun stone, and enhance fire creature.

Flame mages gained exclusive access to the conflagration power at the expense of four charges.

Any mage holding the staff gained the advantage of the flameproof spell so long as they maintained contact with the staff.
