Measuring cord

Item
nameMeasuring Cord
typeMeasurement tool

Measuring cords were ropes retrofitted for the purpose of measuring the width, length, and height of objects.

Description

These ropes were knotted at regular intervals to indicate lengths. On average they spanned in length.

History

In the mid–14 century DR, measuring cords were a common variety of tool in the city of Waterdeep.

Notable Users

  • Felsharp, the operator of Felsharp's Furniture in White Ford, kept measuring cords on hand for his work.
