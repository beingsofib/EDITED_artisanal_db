The spring equinox

Item
nameThe Spring Equinox
typeRing
originMyth Drannor, north Faerûn

The spring equinox was a powerful magical ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The ring embodied the equally balanced power of the sun and moon, and channeled the full power of the spring season and spring equinox. It helped the wearer avoid all sorts of danger, and let them strike more rapidly and relentlessly in combat, draining the energy from the foe and exhausting them. A powerful charge of electricity built up in the ring all the while, eventually unleashing a deadly blast of lightning at the unfortunate target.

History

The ring was found in the ruins of Tyrintar's Hall when the Elves of Cormanthor searched for the lost Eilistraee's Wanderer Uhlsdrin Nirveth.
