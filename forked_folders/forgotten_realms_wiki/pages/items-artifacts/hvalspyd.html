The Hvalspyd, meaning Whale Spear in the giant language, was a frost giant long ship captained by Drufi.

Description

The Hvalspyd was 250 feet (76.2 meters) long. Its sails were made from the wings of a white dragon.

Crew

In addition to Drufi, the Hvalspyd had a crew of 20 frost giants.

History

After the Ordning was destroyed, Jarl Storvald tasked Drufi and other frost giant captains with finding the Ice That Never Melts. Drufi and her crew sailed the Hvalspyd down to Chult, arriving around the time the death curse struck Toril.
