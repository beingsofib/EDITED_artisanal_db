Vials, or phials, were small glass containers that were used to hold liquids and powders. They could be used in laboratories to conduct experiments or to transport these substances.

Description

A typical vial could hold up to of liquid. Outside of the laboratory, vials were most commonly used to store and transport acids, antitoxins, perfumes, poisons, and potions.

Availability

A variety of vials were available for sale through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue, along with matching corks and other laboratory glass containers produced in Urmlaspyr by the glass-blowers guild.

Notable Uses

  • In 1357 DR, the markets of Waterdeep sold rare spices within vials made of either gold, silver, or carved jade.
  • Vials were essential in laboratories, and were also integral components of herbalism kits and poisoner's kits.
  • Some healer's bags included several ceramic vials for the purpose of having clean water readily available.
  • Travelers typically kept their spices inside of little glass vials, which were encased in whittled wooden 'shielding shells' to guard against breakage.
