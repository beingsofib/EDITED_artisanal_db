Dumathoin's bracers were a pair of magical bracers from the dwarven city of Thunderholme.

Powers

The bracers offered great protection to the wearer from physical and magical threats, including freezing, burning, acidic, sonic, and shocking assaults. They also improved the wearer's agility and speed quite a bit.

History

The bracers were once used by Dumathoin Thunderstone, Captain of the Guard during an attack upon Thunderholme. The bracers were later found in the lair treasury of the shadow dragon Aurgloroasa in the 1480s DR.
