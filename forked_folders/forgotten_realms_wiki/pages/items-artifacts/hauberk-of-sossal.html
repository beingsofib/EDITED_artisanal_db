The Hauberk of Sossal was the name given to a special suit of chainmail originating from the land of Sossal.

History

The great explorer Dabron Sashenstar recovered the Hauberk on his journey to the fabled land of Sossal in the , and brought it back with him. It went on display in the Hall of Exploration in the House of the Moon temple to Selûne in Waterdeep by 1370 DR.
