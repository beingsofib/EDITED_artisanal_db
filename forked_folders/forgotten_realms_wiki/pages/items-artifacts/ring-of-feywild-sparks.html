A ring of Feywild Sparks was a rare enchanted piece of jewelry that boosted a wild mage's magical abilities.

Powers

Despite the ring's mundane appearance, it connected the wearer with the sporadic bursts of magic of the Feywild. Its Feywild Sparks enchantment allowed a wild magic sorcerer to turn every one of their spells into wild magic as long as their tides of chaos ability was active.

History

In the , a ring of Feywild Sparks belonged to the malicious green hag of Baldur's Gate named Auntie Ethel. The ring was stashed in her underground lair under the Blushing Maiden Inn in the Lower City.
