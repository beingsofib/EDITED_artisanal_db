Conyberry Stout was a type of alcohol brewed and exported out of the village of Conyberry on the edge of the Neverwinter Wood.

Description

The infamous local dark stout was terrible, by all accords, but an extremely potent intoxicant.

History

By the , Conyberry Stout was one of the few things the village was famous, or infamous in this case, for.
