A Shakujo yari was a weapon unique to the continent of Kara-Tur

Description

A shakujo yari was a spear ingeniously disguised within a sheath to give the appearance of a regular staff. When the sheath was intact, it could be utilized as a bo stick. Typically carried during covert missions or when the wielder wished to appear defenseless.
