Cursed Imaskari gloves

Item
namecursed Imaskari gloves
value2e1 gp

The cursed Imaskari gloves were a pair of gloves, an Imaskari relic unearthed from the ruins of Solon by the slaves of Ambuchar Devayam.

Description

These gloves were golden.

Powers

When worn, they slowly constricted. Within two minutes, the wearer's hands were rendered mangled, useless stumps.
