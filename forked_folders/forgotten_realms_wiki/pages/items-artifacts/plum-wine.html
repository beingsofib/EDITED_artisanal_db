Plum wine

namePlum Wine
typeWine

Plum wine was a variety of wine made from plums that could be found in some parts of Faerûn and Kara-Tur.

Availability

  • In the Western Heartlands, plum prosecco, a shimmering variety of wine, found some controversial popularity in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.
  • In the Eastern Heartlands, some farmers in Cormyr and the Dalelands were known to make plum wine.
  • In Kara-Tur, plum wine was made from the plum orchards of Ch'ing Tung Province in Shou Lung.
  • In the Hordelands, plum wine could be found in the nation of Ra-Khati.
  • In the city of Ravens Bluff, Flirin's Sea Morsels sold plum wine, made by an elderly emigrant of Kara-Tur.
  • In the island nation of Kozakura.
  • The Inn of the High Terrace, an establishment in the village of Tegea in the Dragonjaw Mountains, served plum wine.

History

In the , when General Batu Min Ho met Yamun Khahan to offer his services, he gifted him and his men with a hundred casks of quality plum wine from Ch'ing Tung. He offered a toast to the Khahan, though the Tuigan feared it was poisoned, so Yamun had Batu drink first. Chanar Ong Kho scoffed at the idea of wine made from plums and Yamun found it sweeter than the wine the Tuigan were used to.
