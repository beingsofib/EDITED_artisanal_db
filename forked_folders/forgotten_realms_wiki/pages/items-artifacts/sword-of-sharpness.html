A sword of sharpness was a powerful magical sword that dealt devastating damage to its targets.

Description

Only certain types of swords could be enchanted to become swords of sharpness; namely, those that slashed their targets rather than pierce them.

Powers

Whenever a blow from a sword of sharpness connected with an object, it always dealt the maximum possible damage. Particularly precise hits not only dealt even more damage but also incurred a chance of severing a limb or part of the target's body.

In addition, the sword accepted a command word to start shining, illuminating a radius of . This property was deactivated once the command word was spoken again or if the sword was sheathed.

Notable Swords of Sharpness

  • Gershom, a human ranger that tumbled form one world to another.
  • Sharna, a paladin of Helm who was uncommonly strong for her size.
  • Tearulai, a sentient sword of sharpness. By the , it was embedded in the skull of the green dragon Valdemar, who inhabited the Wyllowwood level of Undermountain.

Notable Owners

  • Birdsong: A tabaxi bard from Chult in the late-15 century DR.
