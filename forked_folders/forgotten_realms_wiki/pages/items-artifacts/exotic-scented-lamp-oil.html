Exotic scented lamp oil was a type of lamp oil from Calimshan.

Description

Any oil-burning lamp could be fueled with scented oils. Typically, Calishite lamps were a popular choice, due to their long-burning and aesthetic properties.

Several different scented oils were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue. Imported directly from Calimshan, the options included:

  • Cinnamon
  • Frankincense
  • Lily of the valley
  • Myrrh
  • Patchouli
  • Pine
  • Rose
  • Sandalwood
  • Violet
