Blood sausage, or black pudding, was a somewhat uncommon type of sausage found in Faerûn.

Availability

In the Western Heartlands and the city of Baldur's Gate, now could find platters filled with an assortment of grilled, fried, baked, and boiled blood sausages, served with a ramekin of dipping sauce. These dishes were referred to as Black Pudding Platters.
