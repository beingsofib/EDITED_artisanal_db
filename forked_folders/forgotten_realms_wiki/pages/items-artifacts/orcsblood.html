The Orcsblood was a pirate ship captained by Ping that sailed the Moonsea in the mid–15th century DR.

History

The Orcsblood's captain and crew were known for their brutality, often slaughtering the entire crews of captured ships. In 1460 DR, the Orcsblood was destroyed by the devas Lakini and Lusk.
