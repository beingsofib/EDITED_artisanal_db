Uraeus

Uraeus was a serpentine symbol unique to Mulhorand. Within the culture of Mulhorand, it symbolized wisdom that was otherwordly.

Notable Users

  • Within the Church of Osiris, many high priests wore wigs that were emblazoned with the uraeus.
