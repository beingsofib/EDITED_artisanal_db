Fried bones salad

nameFried Bones Salad

locationPurskul, Amn

Fried bones salad was one of the specialty side dishes served at the ramshackle Owlroost Head Inn in the Amnian town of Purskul as of the late 14 century DR.

Description

Fried bones salad was made using taros and plantains. They were wedged, battered, and deep-fried. Elminster compared the dish to found on Earth. Fried bones salad was one of three types of cloaks served at the Owlroost Head Inn. Others included fried fluffy lemon-flavored mashed potatos and squid-stuffed yams.
