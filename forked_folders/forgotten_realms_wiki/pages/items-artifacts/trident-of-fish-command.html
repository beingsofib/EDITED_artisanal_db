Trident of fish command

Item
nameTrident of Fish Command
typeEnchanted trident
value2e4,000 gp

The tridents of fish command were polearms that could gain control fish and command them in a limited fashion.

Description

These tridents were barbed military forks.

Powers

The wielder of the trident could attempt to gain control of all fish within a radius from the wielder. Those fish unable to resist would become completely under the empathetic command of the wielder, and would not attack the wielder nor anyone else within of the trident. The fish could be commanded to swim as the wielder wished or harass an enemy. Those fish who resisted the power of the trident, would still be unable to come within of it. Besides fish, the power also affected sharks and eels, but mollusks, crustaceans, and other types of non-piscine creatures would remain unaffected.

Notable Tridents of Fish Command

  • The Fisher's trident of fish command was unique in that, unlike other tridents of fish command, it used no charges in its function, and was more heavily enchanted for combat than other such tridents.
