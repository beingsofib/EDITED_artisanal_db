Selune's favor was a Selûnite heavy mace found in Wheloon Prison in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The mace was lit with shining moonlight and struck with supernatural force and weight. It was imbued with strong good energies and could smite evil well, earning Selûne's favor.

History

The moony item was found after the Sharran leader of the Night Brigade, priestess Storna Harnwood, was slain by the Riverside Rats with the blessing of Selûne.
