A potion of human control was an enchanted potion that controlled humans.

Powers

The imbiber of this potion was able to easily control humans, humanoids, and demihumans as if they were charmed. The effects of the potion lasted for 5–30 minutes.

Creation

A recipe for brewing a potion of human control required six ounces of nixie blood or dust from a slain vampire or a succubus.

Notable Owners

The Unspeakable, a yikarian posing as Mu'izzi al-Kamar in Zakhara, carried several potions of human control.
