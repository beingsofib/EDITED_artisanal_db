Brownies, or chocolate brownies, were a variety of baked desserts, sometimes prepared with chocolate, found in several places in Faerûn.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Regis, the halfling hero of Lonelywood, was known to partake in chocolate brownies.

In the late 15 century DR, one of the Right Pashas of Little Calimshan of Baldur's Gate was known to bake brownies using the secret recipe inherited from his grandmother. These Calishite brownies were once used to raise coin for a child's birthday party in the .
