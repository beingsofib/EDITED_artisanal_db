Cloak of battle

Item
nameCloak of battle
typeClothing
value1e12,000 gp

A cloak of battle was a magically enchanted cloak that could entangle any weapon that attempted to strike its wearer. It could prevent an assailant from being able to use their weapon, and keep it if they tried to leave.

They also possessed a command word that, if spoken when held, would transform the cloak into a magical cudgel.
