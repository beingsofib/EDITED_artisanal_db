A globe of purification was a magical trinket that carried a cloud of purification spell.

Description

These devices were six-inches across spheres made of glass, with a swirling, blue gas within.

Powers

Usable only by priests, the globe, when broken, released a cloud of purification spell.

History

A victim of the werebat Minsk thought such globes would be effective against him; they were wrong, and an unbroken globe remained in the lycanthrope's possession during his short stay near Stag Falls.
