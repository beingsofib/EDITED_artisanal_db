Dragon's Tail was a rare variety of enchanted shuriken created in a land to the east of Faerûn.

Powers

Each of these intricately cut sharp thrown disks was enhanced with a +2 enchantment that, upon striking a target, burned the target with magic fire.

History

The creator of Dragon's Tail shurikens remained unknown in the west as even the inscribed symbol was poorly translated by the sages and just as poorly comprehended. By the , some of these enchanted exotic thrown weapons had reached the Sword Coast and the city of Neverwinter. In the , Dragon's Tail were exported from the eastern realms and sold in Okuzo's Imports store in the Harbor Loop district of Westgate.
