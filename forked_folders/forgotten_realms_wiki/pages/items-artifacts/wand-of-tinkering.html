Wands of tinkering were simple metal wands that could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The wand of tinkering was a suitable spell focus for craftsfolk and those working with clockwork components who used magic to facilitate their work.

Notable Owners

  • Coriolis Rillevay, a gnome sorcerer and clockwork artist, began her adventuring career during the late 15 century DR with a wand of tinkering, which she favored for casting the witch bolt spell.
