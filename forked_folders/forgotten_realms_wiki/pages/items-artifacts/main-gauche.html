A main-gauche was a large dagger that had a basket hilt.

History

The main-gauche was used as a defensive tool in the wielder's off hand which was usually their left. The basket hilt of the weapon made it much easier to parry and even disarm opponents.

Notable Main-Gauches

  • Tinris, an ancient enchanted main-gauche made of everbright steel that was wielded by the Smiling Viper.

Notable Users

individuals

  • Artemus Dimartius, a waterdhavian swashbuckler and teller of tall tales.
  • Mordmorgan, a swashbuckler-adventurer from Cormyr in the mid-14 century DR.
  • Losifan Urdo, a mercenary swashbuckler in Westgate.

Groups

  • Loresingers, specialty priests of Milil, favored using the main-gauche in combat.
