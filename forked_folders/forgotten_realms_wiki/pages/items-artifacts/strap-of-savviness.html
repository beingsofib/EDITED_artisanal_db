Strap of savviness

Item
nameStrap of Savviness
typeBelt
originMyth Drannor, north Faerûn

The strap of savviness was a belt found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The belt contained many secret hidden pockets, which held countless arcane formulae, diagrams, recipes, and other notes. The belt itself improved the wearer's charms and physical resilience. Among its more wide-ranging benefits was a specific benefit to healing magic.

History

The belt ended up in Oghma's House of Song in the blasted ruins of Myth Drannor. It may have been found by the Elves of Cormanthor during the Elven Crusade when they searched for Zelliira there.
