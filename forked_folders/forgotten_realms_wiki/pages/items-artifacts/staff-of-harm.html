Staff of harm was an enchanted staff from the ancient elven city of Myth Drannor.

Description

The weapon was a quarterstaff used for battle. Each end of the weapon was bladed.

Powers

This staff possessed a powerful +3 enchantment and allowed the wielder to cast the harm spell. The quarterstaff held five charges of the spell. The weapon's magics made it usable only by rangers, clerics, or paladins.

History

One of the staves of harm was hidden in the deeper levels of the Elven Catacombs occupied by the drow of House Freth in the . The staff was hidden in one of the magically trapped ancient crypts and guarded by a ravenous zombie lord. Another such staff was in possession of House Freth's priests of Lolth who occupied the catacombs. Yet another staff could be found among looted treasures scattered around the floor of the dragonkin aerie in one of the towers of Castle Cormanthor.
