Wreath of flame

Item
nameWreath of flame
typeMagic circlet
rarity5eLegendary

The wreath of flame was a potent enchanted circlet that could inflict both injury and weakness onto any who met the wearer's gaze. It could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

This headpiece was made of hammered yet very reflective gold.

Powers

The wreath of flame allowed the wearer to cast eyebite, and activating the wreath caused the wearer's eyes to turn black.

Notable Owners

  • Farideh, a tiefling warlock and adventurer.
