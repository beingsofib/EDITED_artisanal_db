The Eye of Ranman was a magical amulet intended for a monk's use only. This item was a part of the set of magic items of the Ranman's Meditations.

Description

The Eye of Ranman was a heavy deep green crystal on a chain.

Powers

Along with other artifacts of Ranman's, the Eye of Ranman was usable only by monks and granted the wearer wisdom, along with increased movement speed that exceeded that of an average humanoid.

History

The Eye of Ranman belonged to the dwarves of Harldain Ironbar's House of Gems. Circa 1369 DR, the amulet could be found in the Room of Words of the House of Gems in the ruins of Myth Drannor.
