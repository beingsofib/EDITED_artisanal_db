The drill was a small portable device that allowed the user to bore holes through wood and stone.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold iron hand drills for 3 gp, along with a selection of wood- and stone-drilling bits. For woodworking, needle-sized bits were 1 sp, nail bits were 7 cp, spike-hole bits were 3 sp, and wooden peg bits were 5 sp. Mithral versions were also available for drilling through solid stone, and cost ten times as much.
