Buckley's buckler

BGitem
nameBuckley's Buckler
typeBuckler
value2e800 gp

Buckley's buckler was a target shield that was made of mammoth hide.

Description

The buckler, which was awfully tanned, smelled of decay. Not even the most powerful substances could get rid of the stench.

Powers

As well as providing more defense, the shield seemed to fortify the wielder, making them more vigorous.
