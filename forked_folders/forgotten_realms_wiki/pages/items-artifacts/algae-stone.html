Algae was an ornamental stone found in Faerûn that was used as decoration on baldrics, belts, and as an inlay on fine furniture.

Description

Algae was a variety of quartz similar to banded agate but the bands were wavy and usually found in shades of dark brown. These gemstones were usually cut or sliced, and polished to a glassy finish to bring out the design. A typical stone had a base value of 10 gp.

Powers

A being in direct contact with algae, or wearing it, was more likely to resist polymorphing effects caused by spells, psionic attacks, and other arcane or divine shape-altering enchantments. This resistance was conferred whether the subject was willing or not.
