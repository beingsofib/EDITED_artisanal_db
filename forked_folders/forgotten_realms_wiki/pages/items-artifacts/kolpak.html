Kolpak

Item
nameKolpak
typeClothing
locationthe Hordelands, northeast Faerûn

A kolpak was a type of clothing worn by the tribes of the Hordelands. These caps were tall and pointy. Flaps covered the wearer's ears and neck, offering protection from the wind.
