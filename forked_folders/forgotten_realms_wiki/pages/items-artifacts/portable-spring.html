Portable spring

Item
namePortable Spring
typeMiscellaneous
locationZakhara

The portable spring was a magic item that originated in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Description

Portable springs appeared as blue pieces of cloth.

Powers

When placed upon the ground, this item became a spring of fresh water, providing up to 200 gallons (760 liters) of fresh water per day. The portable spring could be picked up and moved easily, reverting back to a piece of blue cloth whenever it was not resting upon the ground.
