The summer solstice

Item
nameThe Summer Solstice
typeRing
originMyth Drannor, north Faerûn

The summer solstice was a powerful ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The ring embodied the might of the sun, summer, and the solstice. It helped the wearer survive all kinds of danger, and at times erupted in bursts of blinding light and blazing fire against foes. Monks could draw additional benefits from the ring depending on their elemental fighting stance, benefiting from lightning shocks and tearing wounds when aligned with the wind, increased resilience when with the mountain, afflictions of blindness and fear with the sun, and unsteady footing with the tide.

History

The ring was found in the House of Song when the Elves of Cormanthor searched for the lost drow bard Zelliira.
