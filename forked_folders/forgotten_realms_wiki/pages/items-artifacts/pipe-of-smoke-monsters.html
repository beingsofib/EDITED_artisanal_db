Pipe of smoke monsters

Item
namePipe of Smoke Monsters
typeWondrous item
rarity5eCommon

A pipe of smoke monsters was a magical pipe whose smoke could be shaped into the form of a creature.

Powers

The user of a pipe of smoke monsters could concentrate while exhaling to shape the smoke into the likeness of any creature, such as a dragon, a flumph, or a froghemoth. The shape occupied a volume no larger than a cube 1 foot (30 centimeters) on a side for a few seconds, then dissipated.

History

Pipes of smoke monsters were relative common magic items that could be found in various locations, such as Undermountain.
