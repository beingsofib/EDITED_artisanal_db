Catseye brooch

Item
nameCatseye brooch
typeCharm
locationFaerûn, Toril
casterlevel3e5
value3e9,000 gp

A catseye brooch was a small charm that provided luck for its carrier and protected them from disease. They were cast from silver and inset with a green chrysoberyl, also known as a cat's eye.

They were popular in human societies with a significant Chondathan populace.
