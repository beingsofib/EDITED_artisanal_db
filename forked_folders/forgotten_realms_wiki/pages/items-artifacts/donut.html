Donuts or doughnuts, were a type of sweet handfood bread, virtually unknown in the Realms.

Availability

In the city of Ravens Bluff, doughnuts and other small handfood pastries were served in Chemcheaux, a famous magic shop in the Temple District. The store offered several cauldrons of exotic hot teas with a selection of doughnuts and other pastries for the guests to snack on.
