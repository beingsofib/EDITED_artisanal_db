Battleaxes of speed were enchanted axes favored by the dwarvish scouts of Kelvin's Cairn in Icewind Dale in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

Battleaxes of speed carried magic similar to the effects of the haste spell that boosted the wielder's speed.

History

By the , dwarvish scouts of the Dwarven Valley carried such battleaxes while surveying the territories around their mines. Once a threat was spotted, the dwarves would run, sped up by the axes's magics, before drow, umber hulks, or worse enemies could notice.
