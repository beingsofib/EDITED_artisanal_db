The Templar's docent was a kind of docent used by the warforged of Eberron, particularly those Templars of the Silver Flame.

Powers

The docent greatly enhanced the warforged's resilience in the face of magical and physical assaults. Only evilly-aligned weapons could cut through these protections, and any who struck the warforged would be blasted with holy light in return.

History

Said to have been blessed by Tira Miron, these docents were used by the Templars of the Silver Flame. They were also won by their allies, such as the adventurers who fought the Spinner of Shadows (an aspect of Lolth) in the Spinner's Prison before they traveled to Faerûn.
