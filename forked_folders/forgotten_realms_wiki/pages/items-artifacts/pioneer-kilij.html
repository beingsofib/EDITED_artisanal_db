Pioneer kilij and its more powerful variant, exalted pioneer kilij, was a variety of artifact scimitar created to be used by fighter adventurers in the Chultan city of Port Nyanzaru in the late 15 century DR.

Description

These pioneer kilij were crafted to help adventuring troupes survive the perils of the Jungles of Chult, ideal for long expeditions. They were created to be paired with enchanted shields – pioneer daraqahs.

Powers

A pioneer kilij could be enchanted to hold various unique magics for each owner, in addition to boosting the damage inflicted by the owner in combat, the ability to deflect enemy blows, as well as increasing the damage done by their well-aimed and lucky attacks. When a pioneer kilij was wielded with a pioneer daraqah, both items boosted each other's magical powers, scaling in potency, depending on the size of the adventuring group or number of allies. With no allies, the weapons boosted the owner's physical strength, within a party of two – power and defense were boosted, party of three – increased accuracy was added to the list, party of four – their lucky and well-aimed attacks were empowere, and lastly, with a party of five or more – the weapons gained the ability to penetrate enemy armor.

History

Pioneer kilij were created in the late 15 century DR, and their design suggested the items' origins to be tied to the ancient Lost City of Omu.
