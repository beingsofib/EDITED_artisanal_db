The Star of Melos-Pelar was one of five gems, the Star Gems of Martek that the wizard Martek was said to have imbued with an enchantment to retrieve his Sphere of Power, but would actually resurrect him.

Description

The Star of Melos-Pelar was a fist-sized, faceted amethyst of the deepest purple, either ball shaped.

Powers

The Star of Melos-Pelar had the power to point the way to Martek's Tomb, if placed in the monolith in Medinat Muskawoon.

History

The Star of Melos-Pelar was said to have disappeared into the Cursed City of Stone. Actually, Martek hid the Star of Melos-Pelar in the ruins of Medinat Muskawoon. Adventurers retrieved the Star of Melos-Pelar in , using it to restore Martek to life.
