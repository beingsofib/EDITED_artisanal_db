Old Logrin Stout was a stout from the city of Velen in Tethyr.

Description

The beverage had a robust taste.

History

As of the late 14, Old Logrin Stout was offered by Morgan's Inn – a large tavern and inn located in the city of Velen. Volothamp Geddarm mentioned the stout in his guide to the Lands of Intrigue as being preferred to the inn's sweet cordials.
