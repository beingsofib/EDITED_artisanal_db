Gulluk

Item
nameGulluk
typeAlcoholic beverage
locationLaerakond, Abeir

Gulluk was a powerful giant alcoholic beverage brewed by the earth giants of Fimbrul, Laerakond. It was made of fermented cooking broth flavored by much use.
