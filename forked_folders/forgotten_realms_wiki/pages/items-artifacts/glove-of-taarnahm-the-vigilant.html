The glove of Taarnahm the Vigilant was a magical chainmail gauntlet that granted its wearer the ability to throw melee weapons with incredible skill. Any weapon that was cast with the glove would be done as if it had the throwing and returning qualities.

Creation

The spells magic stone and telekinesis were used in the enchantment of this glove.
