The hide of the goristro was a suit of light hide armor found in the Demonweb Pits.

Description

The armor was made of the thick hide of a goristro demon, and maintained a nasty stench.

Powers

The hide of the goristro broadly protected the wearer and made them tougher, and helped them be a little quicker and deadlier in melee combat.

History

A suit of this hide armor was taken from the Domain of Lord Grulemith, either made from Grulemith or some other unfortunate goristro.
