The amulet of Seldarine was a unique amulet gifted to the adventurer Abdel Adrian by Ellesime, the Queen of the elves of Suldanessellar.

Powers

It offered its wearer protection from all manner of spells and magical effects.

History

The amulet was a commemoration of sorts for the rescue of Suldanessellar, when it was besieged by the disgraced elven archmage Jon Irenicus in the . Abdel and his companions helped summon the avatar of Rillifane Rallathil in order to defend the sacred Tree of Life.
