Sugarbread

Item
nameSugarbread
typeFood

Sugarbread was a type of sweet bread including sugar that was eaten in Faerûn.

Usage

It was typically eaten for dessert. For example, The Wizard's Hand inn served a bowl of sugarbread that was soaked in brandy and coated in cream.

Availability

Sugarbread was known to be sold at the following establishments:

  • The Harbreeze Bakery in Baldur's Gate, which was famed for its sugarbread loaves.
  • The Wizard's Hand inn in Maskyr's Eye in the Vast served a bowl of sugarbread that was soaked in brandy and coated in cream.
