Rings of elemental infusion were enchanted rings that absorbed elemental magics.

Description

These rings were golden bands with a single mysterious gemstone set at the center. The stone tended to change its color when the wearer was not looking.

Powers

A ring of elemental infusion made its enchantment evident once the wearer successfully hit a target with a cantrip that dealt acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder damage. It became infused with the spell's elemental magics that could be channeled by the wearer into any successful weapon attack, giving it an elemental edge.

History

In the , one ring of elemental infusion belonged to Gish Umr'a'ac stationed in Crèche Y'llek's infirmary.
