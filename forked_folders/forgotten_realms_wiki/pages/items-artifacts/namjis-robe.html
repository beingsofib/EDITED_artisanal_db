Namji's robe was a unique enchanted wizard's robe that once belonged to a famed mage of Thesk, Namji.

Powers

This red robe possessed meager magical defenses but possessed enchantments that regenerated the wearer's wounds.

History

Namji's robe was created by Namji, a battlemage of Thesk, long before the . The robe was lost until 1281 DR, when it was recovered from an old shipwreck on the island of an undead white dragon Icasaracht, on Lac Dinneshere in Icewind Dale.
