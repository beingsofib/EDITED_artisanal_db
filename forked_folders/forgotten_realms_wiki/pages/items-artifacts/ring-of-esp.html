Ring of ESP

Item
nameRing of ESP
typeRing

A ring of ESP was a magic item that allowed the wearer to hear the surface thoughts of others around them.

Powers

When worn, a ring of ESP allowed the wearer to eavesdrop on the surface thoughts of people near them. Thoughts could be read for several minutes but only worked on a single target at a time.

Notable Owners

Brace Ulmemur, a member of Waterdeep's City Watch, wore a ring of ESP.
