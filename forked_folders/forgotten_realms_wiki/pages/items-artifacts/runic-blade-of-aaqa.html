Runic blades of Aaqa were enchanted ceremonial daggers wielded by the aarakocras of Kir Sabal in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

These runic blades were commonly used in rituals to appease the Wind Dukes of Aaqa or quell their wrath. When wielded in battle, these daggers held enchantments that enhanced the wielder's natural and magical abilities.

Notable Owners

  • Asharra: the aarakocra wizard leader of Kir Sabal in the late 15 century DR.
