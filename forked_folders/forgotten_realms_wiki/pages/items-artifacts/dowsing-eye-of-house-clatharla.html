The Dowsing Eye of House Clatharla was one of the treasures in the Vault of Ages.

Appearance

It was a crystal sphere.

Powers

Looking through the Eye revealed the course of waterflows across a realm, with sufficient resolution to see every handspan and every snag down to the last beaver dam.
