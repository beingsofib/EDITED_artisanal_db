Wand of stick

Item
nameWand of Stick
typeWand

A wand of stick was an unenchanted wand, making it essentially just a stick.

Description

Being non-magical pieces of wood, the wands had no apparent magical qualities or uses. Unlike wands of magic missiles, which were wands that produced the magic missile spell, wands of stick did not produce the stick spell.
