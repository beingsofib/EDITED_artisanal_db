Powder of good taste

Item
namePowder of Good Taste
typeEnchanted powder
value2e200 gp

Powder of good taste was a type of magical powder used for culinary purposes.

Description

This powder had a white hue and it was packaged in packets.

Powers

Placing a single pinch of this powder on any of food, no matter its condition, would cause it to taste delicious. Though the appearance of the food would not be altered.

Notable Owners

  • Solimar, a chef and wizard at the Ravens Bluff restaurant The Painted Boat, owned some powder of good taste.
