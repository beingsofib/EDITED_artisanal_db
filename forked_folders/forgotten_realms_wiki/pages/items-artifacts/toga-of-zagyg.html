Toga of Zagyg

Item
nameToga of Zagyg
typeToga
locationSea of Swords, west Faerûn

The Toga of Zagyg was a bedsheet that gave the impression of having great magical power. In the late 15 century DR, it was in the collection of the morkoth M'warien on his island in the Sea of Swords. It was stored in a glass cylinder and labelled with a metal plaque. M'warien hypnotised a group of four merfolk who became compelled to dance around the toga.
