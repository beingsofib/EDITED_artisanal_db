Mage daggers were relatively simple enchanted daggers that were popular among wizards for their protective capabilities.

Description

These seemingly ordinary daggers had pointed double-edged blades nestled on top of a simple hilt.

Powers

Mage daggers were ensorcelled to be useful to spellcasters. These items were created in several defensive enchantment strengths that ranged from +1 to +4. Additionally, these daggers allowed its mage wielder to memorize additional spells per day, depending on the enchantment's potency.

History

By 1281 DR, mage daggers could be found in various places in Icewind Dale and in the vicinity of the druid village at the Spine of the World known as Kuldahar.
