The Golden Wayfarer was a mercantile sailing ship, originating from either east or northeast Faerûn, in the 14 century DR.

Activities

This ship primarily dealt in the trading of spices.

History

The Golden Wayfarer operated in the Sea of Fallen Stars around 1370 DR.
