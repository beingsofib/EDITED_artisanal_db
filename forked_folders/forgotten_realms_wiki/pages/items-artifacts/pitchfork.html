A pitchfork was a common farming tool found in Faerûn.

Description

Pitchforks had between three and five prongs, and were used to "pitch" materials such as straw and hay

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue offered three-pronged maple pitchforks for 2 gp, and Thayan four-pronged steel-headed pitchforks for 9 gp.
