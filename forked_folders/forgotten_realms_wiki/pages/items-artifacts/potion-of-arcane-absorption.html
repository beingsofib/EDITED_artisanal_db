Potion of arcane absorption

Item
namePotion of Arcane Absorption
typePotion
locationIcewind Dale, Northwest Faerûn

Potions of arcane absorption were a rare type of potion that protected the imbiber from spells and magical effects.

Description

These potions were typically kept in wide-neck ceramic jars in the Northwest Faerûn. Each contained enough liquid for one full dose.

Powers

Potions of arcane absorption gave the imbiber protection against magic via absorption and negating of the offensive spells and magical effects. The effects of the potion were permanent.
