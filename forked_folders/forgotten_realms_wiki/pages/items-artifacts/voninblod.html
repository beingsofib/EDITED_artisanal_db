Voninblod (meaning "titan blood" in Jotun), was a type of magical substance created by giants in the days of Ostoria.

Description

This substance was blue in coloration.

History

Around the late-15 century DR, ancient containers of voninblod were occasionally found around Bryn Shander, Cold Run, and Lonelywood.

Usages

Voninblod was highly valued for use in enchanting items, such as relic armors.
