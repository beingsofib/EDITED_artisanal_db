Wands of cure serious wounds were magic wands that allowed their owners to dispense powerful curative magics, on par with the most experienced clerics.

Description

Wand of cure serious wounds were rods carved out of mahogany wood and was warm to the touch.

Powers

These wands allowed the wielder to cast extremely potent version of the cure serious wounds. Each wand possessed up to ten charges.

History

In 1369 DR, several of these powerful wands were in possession of the drow assassins of House Freth who allied themselves with the Cult of the Dragon that occupied the ruins of Myth Drannor.

Notable Owners

  • Halisstra Melarn: a drow priestess of House Melarn in Ched Nasad in late 14 century DR.
  • Hiflanyl: an elven cleric from the demiplane of Grodd circa 1371 DR.
  • Varlae: a human cleric of Bane, active circa 1372 DR.
