The giant hair crossbow was an enchanted heavy crossbow of unknown origin.

Description

The weapon earned its name from the belief that its bowstring was in fact a single hair taken from a frost giant.

History

As of the , the giant hair crossbow was available for sale at the Adventurer Mart, in the city of Athkatla.
