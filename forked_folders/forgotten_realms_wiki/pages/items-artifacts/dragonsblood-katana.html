The dragonsblood katana was an enchanted katana of unknown origins found on the Sword Coast North in the late 14th century DR.

Powers

The sword possessed a +2 enchantment that made it deadlier and guided its blade toward the wielder’s target. It gave its wielder slight fire resistance and dealt fire damage upon successful hits.

History

The exact origins of the katana were unknown. In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR, one such blade was found in the town of Port Llast, where it was sold by Haljal to friends of the Church of Waukeen.
