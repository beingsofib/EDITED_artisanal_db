Fruitcake was a sweet bread studded with dried fruits and nuts and flavored with rum or brandy made on the Pirate Isles. It was available for purchase across Faerûn through Aurora's Emporium.

Description

Dried fruits used commonly in fruitcakes included dates, citron, currants, raisins, and cherries, as well as nuts such as walnuts, hazelnuts and filberts.

When purchased through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue, fruitcakes were shipped in a decorative tin.

Reputation

It was rumored that, if aged for too long, fruitcakes would become so hard and heavy that they could be used as weapons.
