Sword of Horus

Item
nameSword of Horus
typeSword
creatorHorus-Re

The Sword of Horus was a unique melee weapon wielded by the avatar of Horus-Re, the Lord of the Sun within the Mulhorandi pantheon.

Powers

This weapon carried a powerful +5 enchantment. The Sword of Horus carried the combined enchantments of a holy avenger, luck blade, defender, and a vorpal blade.
