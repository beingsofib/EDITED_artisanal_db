Wand of negation

nameWand of Negation
typeWand
value2e15,000 gp

A wand of negation was a magic item that cancelled the effects of spells and other magic items.

Powers

These wands negated the powers of certain spells and magic items. A pale beam of light would shoot from the wand when pointed at the desired target. There was a 75% chance that the targeted item would fail when used. Each use of a wand of negation expended a single charge. These wands could not be recharged.

Notable Owners

  • Chantos Greybeard owned a wand of negation.
  • Durissa Thornion, an illusionist from Chondath, carried a wand of negation.
  • Vexter, a human illusionist and former apprentice of Delfen Yellowknife Ondabarl.
