Tymora's spinning coin was an artifact coin of divine nature granted to adventurers by The Smiling Lady of Good Fortune, goddess Tymora herself.

Description

These coins were polished and sparkling with gold gleam, believed to had been touched by Tymora. When these coins were placed in a palm, they constantly spun on the rim.

Powers

Tymora's spinning coin could be used in battle. When it was activated, the will of the goddess herself descended onto the battlefield, creating a coin that spun around and knocked the enemies around the wielder prone. Then, the power of fortune determined what happened: either the wielder and their allies received a boon of increased offensive power, or the enemies were weakened and cursed.
