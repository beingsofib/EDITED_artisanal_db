A short sword, (or shortsword), was a light, piercing melee weapon.

Description

This sword was a smaller version of the longsword, but longer than a dagger.

Tactics

The short sword was designed for thrusting and piercing. It was a common weapon for use in the off-hand when dual-wielding.

Varieties

  • Katzbalger, also known as "cat gutter", was a type of short and thick sword from the Blade Kingdoms, sometimes carried by Blade Captain and hippogriff cavalry.
  • Wakizashi, a slightly arched shortsword commonly used in Kara-Tur.
  • Butterfly sword

Notable Short Swords

  • Ilbratha: King Azoun I's short sword, lost in 389 DR.
  • Shadowflash: The short sword of the god Vhaeraun.
  • Hornblade: favored weapon of the goddess Yondalla
