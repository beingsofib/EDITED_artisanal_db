Starborn was a ship that once belonged to Lora Bergauz of Baldur's Gate. It was a Valour-class three-mast, five-gun, sailing vessel.

History

Lora Bergauz retired to the city of Baldur's Gate with her daughter after selling her ship, the Starborn. Despite its damaged condition, with two of the five guns broken and the starboard side requiring moderate repair, Lora received a bid of 10,000 gold pieces from an anonymous buyer.
