nameRaeranthur's Cloak

Raeranthur's Cloak was one of the treasures in the Vault of Ages of the great elven city, Myth Drannor.

Powers

Wrapping the cloak around a tree's trunk banished any blights on the plant. It could also banish blights from other plants it was draped over.
