The belt of the witch king was an enchanted belt that once belonged to Zhengyi the Witch-King.

Description

The belt was a series of small obsidian spheres charged with arcane power, which were each connected to the next by a small silver link of chain.

Powers

The belt fortified the wearer's endurance. If its wearer was an arcane spellcaster, it could leverage their magical power to heal them if they became seriously injured.

History

The belt of the witch king was kept in a secret compartment in the back of Zhengyi's throne within Castle Perilous, alongside other treasures. It remained there for over a century after Zhengyi's defeat at the hands of Gareth Dragonsbane.
