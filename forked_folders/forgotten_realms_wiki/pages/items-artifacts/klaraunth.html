Klaraunth

Item
nameKlaraunth
typeAlcoholic beverage
locationChessenta, Tethyr, Vilhon Reach

Klaraunth was an alcoholic beverage made in Chessenta, Tethyr, and the Vilhon Reach in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Similar to vodka, klaraunth was distilled from barley.

It was a key ingredient in the manyslake known as flaming gullet.
