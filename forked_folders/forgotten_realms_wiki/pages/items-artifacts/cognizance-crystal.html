Cognizance crystals, acting like the psionics equivalent to a pearl of power.

Description

Resembling elaborate jewelry, These crystals gave off a faint glow so long as they were full of psionic energy as well as a faint psychokinetic psionic aura. The glow they emitted could vary in coloration, such as golden or purple. Altogether a set of cognizance crystals weighed around .

Some were known to hang cognizance crystals from belts or necklaces, whilst others were found to wrap them around the ends of rods or staves.

Abilities

A user had to attune to these crystals by holding them or having them on their person for a period of 10 minutes before they could be used. They could be used by both psionics users and psionic creatures. When activated, the wielder was able to use a crystal's stored pool of psionic energy in manifesting any psionic powers that the user knew. They could not, however, be drawn upon to replenish a wielder's own pool of mental energy.

Once depleted of its stored energy, a psionics user could restock a cognizance crystal by using a complex series of asanas, redirecting their own mental energy from the muladhara chakra into it. However, a cognizance crystal could never store more psionic energy than it was originally created to hold.

Value

The value of a cognizance crystal varied depending upon the most powerful psionic power that it could manifest with its stored pool of psionic energy. The lowest possible value of was 1,000 gp, whilst the highest was 81,000 gp.

Notable Users

  • The Kaliesh'erai, a psionic elven organization, kept a number of powerful crystal capacitors within the subbasement vaults of their hall in Evereska.
  • Tanju, a member of the Hlondeth militia in the 14 century DR. His cognizance crystal consisted of a trio of quartz, one clear, one rosy, and one smoky. When active they gave off a faint purple glow.
  • Zelia, a powerful yuan-ti telepath, scorned crystal capacitors as being "crutches" that one could become overly reliant upon.

Trivia

  • Earth nodes functioned similarly to crystal capacitors, though they had twice the storage potential and naturally regenerated any energy drawn from them on a daily basis.
  • Metaminds could make their psicrystals function like a cognizance crystal.
