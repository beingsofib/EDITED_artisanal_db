The Shadow's Blade was a unique enchanted shortsword also known as a short sword of backstabbing.

Powers

The Shadow's Blade possessed a powerful +3 enchantment that made the sword more deadly and helped to guide the wielder's arm towards the target.

History

The Shadow's Blade was a weapon specifically designed to be used by assassins and was in high demand among those who used blades to settle differences. Many of the sword's owners did not keep it for very long. In the , The Shadow's Blade, the original or a replica, was wielded by Purvis, an assassin who was hired by the speaker of Lonelywood.

In the , the weapon belonged to the assassin named Slythe who served the infamous Bhaalspawn Sarevok Anchev in the city of Baldur's Gate. Soon after, the sword fell into the hands of another Bhaalspawn—the thief Viekang in the town of Saradush.
