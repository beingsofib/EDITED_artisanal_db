Stone soup

Item
nameStone Soup
typeSoup

Stone soup was a form of vegetable soup served in the Icewind Dale region and parts of the Sword Coast North.

Description

This dish was known to maintain a warm temperature for extended periods of time due to being cooked with scalding stones.
