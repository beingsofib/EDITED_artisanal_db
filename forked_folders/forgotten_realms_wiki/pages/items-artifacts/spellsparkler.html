The Spellsparlker was a unique quarterstaff crafted by the electric inventor Yrre the Sparkstruck.

Powers

Whenever the wielder of Spellsparkler harmed a person or creature with a magical spell, electricity would course through their body.

History

Yrre the Sparkstruck intended to gift the Spellsparlker to the cleric of Mystra named Lenore, an old lover, only to discover the Mystran's tower abandoned. Eventually, the staff came to be possessed by Counsellor Florrick of Baldur's Gate sometime in or before the .
