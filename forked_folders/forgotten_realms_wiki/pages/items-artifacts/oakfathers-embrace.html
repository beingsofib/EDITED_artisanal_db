The Oakfather's Embrace was a suit of enchanted hide armor assocaited with the faith of Silvanus.

Description

The armor featured a message embroidered into its surface, related to its "order of nature" enchantment. It radiated a subtle amount of divine energy.

Powers

In addition to the magical protection it provided, Oakfather's Embrace possessed an interesting characteristic. Any undead that struck the wearer were blasted with radiant energy, whereas any beasts that did so, had their attacks imbued with the same force.

History

Through unknown circumstances, the armor was lost within the cave of a female owlbear, not far from the Risen Road in the Western Heartlands.
