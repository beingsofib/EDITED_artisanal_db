Leashes were tools used to restrain and guide animals, often through public areas without causing alarm.

Description

A leash was a length of chain fitted to some type of collar.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold chain leash and collar assemblies fit for use on common cats (2 sp) and dogs (4-5 sp), wolves (6 sp), and dire wolves (8 sp).

Notable Users

  • Rangers or woodsmen throughout Faerûn, being people that often interacted with animals, sometimes employed the use of leashes.

History

In the , five bears roused from their annual hibernation by overzealous hunters were led in short leashed muzzles through the streets of Zhentil Keep as the front of a procession of captive beasts, monsters, and sentient beings as part of a Cyricist religious festival.
