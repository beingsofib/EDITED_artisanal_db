Helm of eternal day

Item
nameHelm of Eternal Day
typeHelmet
originMyth Drannor, north Faerûn

The helm of eternal day was a magical helmet found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

When the helm was forged by the elves, a dream of everlasting day was worked into the metal. It gave the wearer an extraordinary resilience against wounds and suffering, allowing them to survive otherwise lethal danger.
