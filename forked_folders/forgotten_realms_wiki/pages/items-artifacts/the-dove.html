The Dove was an enchanted shortbow of unknown origins found on the Sword Coast North in the late 14th century DR.

Powers

The bow possessed a +3 enchantment that made it deadlier and guided its arrows toward the wielder's target. It allowed the user to cast the spells charm person and charm monster, each twice per day. However, charm person could be cast only ten times in total, and charm monster only five times.

History

The exact origins of the weapon were unknown. In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR, one such shortbow was found in the Blacklake District of Neverwinter, sold by Dayne Lynneth.
