Wyrmwizz was a type of ale brewed in Skullport.

Description

It was an extremely low quality, muddy beverage.

History

Wyrmwizz was produced at Gyudd's Distillery in Skullport. As of 1493 DR, this ale was available from the Black Tankard and the Worm's Gullet taverns.

One of the regular buyers of Wyrmwizz from Gyudd's Distillery was Azrok from Undermountain. He had a storage room where he kept it stored.
