Nightscale armor was a type of armor used on Toril.

Description

Nightscale was similar to leather armor, but it was made from dark snakeskin. It was treated with numerous alchemical mixtures making it more supple and formfitting. It was often favored by sorcerers and wizards. Nightscale was typically made in the Underdark.
