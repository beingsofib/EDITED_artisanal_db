Awana (spice)

Item
nameAwana
typeSpice
locationAwana, Kara-Tur

Awana was a herb and spice endemic to the dangerous island of Awana of the coast of Kara-Tur.

Usage

Awana was highly valued for its medicinal applications, purported to being capable of curing many illnesses. It was also said that one could use it extend their life.
