The Firecam full-plate armor was the personal suit of armor of Sir Keldorn Firecam. It was designed specifically for him, so very few others could wear it.

It was a +1 full plate armor that granted the wearer to ability to move freely through hindrances such as webs, as well as boosted resistances to effects that assailed them.
