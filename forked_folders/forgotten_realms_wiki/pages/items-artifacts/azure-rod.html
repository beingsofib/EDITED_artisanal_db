Azure Rods were rare enchanted lapis lazuli maces that were deadly to evil creatures.

Description

Azure Rods were made out of a sturdy metallic alloy of silvery-white color, and the mace's head was carved out of a gently glowing lapis lazuli. The entirety of the weapon was decorated with intricate winding designs. However, they did not indicate the weapon's maker or significance.

Powers

The mace's magical nature was very apparent. It glowed with the magical, slowly drawn power of Goodness itself. When wielded in combat, the weapon's magic dealt additional damage to creatures of evil and malicious nature. The same magic of good invigorated the weapon's owner. Despite Azure Rods' magic, the mace could be wielded by any creature, be they good or evil.

History

Azure Rods' history was a mystery. They were created sometime before the early 14 century DR. One such weapon found its way into the hands of Hadbruki, a shaman leader of the Uthgardt Black Raven tribe, who used the Cold Marshes area of the Ice Lakes as their hunting ground.
