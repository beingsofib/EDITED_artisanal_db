The Sleeping Statue was a large stone carving found within the Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert of the Hordelands during the mid 14 century DR.

Description

The statue was carved into an open clifface on the Great Chamber level of the temple. The Sleeping Statue was massive, measuring long by wide.

History

The Sleeping Statue was a representation of the Enlightened One sleeping in a pose.
