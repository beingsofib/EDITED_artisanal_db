Saravva

Item
nameSaravva
typeAlcoholic drink
locationEsmeltaran, Amn

Saravva was an Amnian wine made from grapes of the vineyards near the halfling city of Esmeltaran. It was tinted green and possessed a particularly tart taste.

Availability

This was one of the few wines available from the Harfin Draether tavern in Athkatla.
