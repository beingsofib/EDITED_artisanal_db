Scroll of protection from magic

Item
nameScroll of Protection from Magic
typeScroll
value2e6,500 gp

A scroll of protection from magic was an enchanted scroll that created a powerful antimagic field.

Powers

After reading this scroll, a sphere of antimagic was created, centered on the reader. The sphere protected anyone inside from all forms of magic. The sphere lasted for 1–5 minutes.

Notable Owners

  • Ratakos, a necromancer wandering the Realms near Cormyr, carried a scroll of protection from magic.
