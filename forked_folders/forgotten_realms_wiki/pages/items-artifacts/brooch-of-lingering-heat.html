Brooch of lingering heat

Item
nameBrooch of Lingering Heat
typeMagic item
locationMenzoberranzan

The brooch of lingering heat was one of the artifacts reserved for the archmage of Menzoberranzan. It was in the possession of Gromph Baenre in the mid–14 century DR.

It granted the wearer the ability to fill Narbondel with twenty-four hours of heat, allowing the city's inhabitants to tell time even in the Underdark.
