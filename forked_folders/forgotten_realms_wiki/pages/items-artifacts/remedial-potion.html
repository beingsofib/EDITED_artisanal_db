Remedial potion was a type of curative potion found in the Western Heartlands and the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Remedial potions were viscous liquid that glittered with magic.

Powers

This potion provided no healing but cured blindness, silence, paralyzation , and poison.
