Battledale Dark was a stout beer from Battledale, popular in the 13 century DR and later in the late 14 century DR.

Description

Battledale Dark was a rich and dark stout. It was a thick, almost chewy drink.

History

The first wave of popularity of Battledale Dark happened in the 13 century DR. However, the drink lost its demand in the century that followed. In the late 14 century DR, a resourceful merchant from Sembia started producing the stout once again, bringing it back to popularity.
