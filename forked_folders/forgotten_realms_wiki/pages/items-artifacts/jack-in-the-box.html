Jack-in-the-box

A jack-in-the-box was a type of children's toy that could be found in both the Realms particularly in the domains of Barovia and Odiare.

Description

This toy outwardly consisted of a music box with a hinged lid

Availability

  • In the Domain of Barovia, the toysmith Gadof Blinsky of Vallaki sold macabre jack-in-the-boxes shaped like coffins that contained a puppet modeled after Strahd von Zarovich.

Notable Owners

  • The Cassalanter family owned and kept a jack-in-the-box within their villa's playroom in the late 15 century DR.
