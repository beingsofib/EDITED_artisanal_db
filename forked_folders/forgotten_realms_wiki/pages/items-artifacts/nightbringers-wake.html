Nightbringer's wake was a powerful greatsword held by Agarta the Breaker, Battlemaster of the Night Brigade in the late 15 century DR. Its name referenced the nightbringer priests of Shar, who the Night Brigade held as their patron.

Powers

The blade cut keenly and inflicted critical wounds with ease, and was innately evil and carried destructive energies. It magically bound and slowed those it struck, and drained the life force of its victims. Pain and suffering followed in its wake.

History

The nightbringer's wake was held by the martial-minded Agarta until she was defeated by the Riverside Rats gang in Wheloon Prison.
