Garlraw

Item
nameGarlraw
typeDrink
locationKhôltar, south Faerûn

Garlraw was a strong, alcoholic dwarven beverage available in Khôltar, south Faerûn, particularly the dwarf-owned tavern known as Phlambror's. Few non-dwarves could tolerate this rich, potent drink.

Description

Garlraw was made by mixing different varieties of ale, whiskey, and sweet syrup together, adding butter, and simmering over low heat to thicken it and enhance the flavor.
