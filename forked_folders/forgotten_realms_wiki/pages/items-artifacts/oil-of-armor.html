Oil of armor was a magic oil that toughened the skin.

Description

Oil of armor was so thin and colorless that it was practically invisible when applied.

Powers

When applied to the skin, oil of armor hardened the user's skin as if they were wearing armor. This effected lasted for seven to thirteen hours.

History

This oil was used in parts of Unther where priests were forbidden to wear traditional armor.
