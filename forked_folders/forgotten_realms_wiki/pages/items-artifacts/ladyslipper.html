Ladyslipper was a slave-ship active on the Lake of Steam.

History

Under a female captain, the Ladyslipper was once due to transport a shipment of slaves out of Derlusk in the Border Kingdoms. However, this captain was not affiliated with the Rundeen. To maintain their grip on the slave trade, the Rundeen gave a tip-off about the Ladyslipper to an anti-slavery organization such as the Harpers or Janessar who would eliminate the competition for them.
