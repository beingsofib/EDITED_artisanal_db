The axe of heavenly fire was an impressive, magical greataxe that was considered sacred to the church of Tyr.

Powers

This weapon was a +3 holy greataxe that emitted a glowing radiance as if it were permanently under the effects of light.

History

Previously held in the possession of the Lord High Justiciar Deren Eriach, of the Abbey of the Just Hammer in Tasseldale, the axe of heavenly fire was bestowed upon Madeiron Sunderstone, the personal bodyguard of Open Lord Piergeiron.
