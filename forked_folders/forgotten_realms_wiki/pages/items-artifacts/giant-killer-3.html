The Giant Killer was an enchanted halfling sling effective against giants.

Powers

The Giant Killer possessed a +1 enchantment and, when wielded against giants and giant-kin, it effectively became a +4 magic weapon.

History

The first known wielder of the Giant Killer was a halfling shire-reeve warrior named Kim Marchwatch. The halfling used the sling against the giants who menaced his home. Marchwatch pelleted them with so many rocks that the giants either fell over or fled the onslaught. Subsequently, Kim was replaced as the shire-reeve and was forced on the road becoming an adventurer. He and his enchanted sling were believed to be consumed by the dreaded Fen Mists of Acallo in 760 DR.

By 1281 DR, the Giant Killer found its way into the stock of Conlan, the proprietor of Kuldahar's blacksmith shop.
