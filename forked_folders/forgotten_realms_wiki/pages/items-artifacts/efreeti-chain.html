Efreeti chain was a set of enchanted chainmail that was resistant to fire and named after the fearsome efreeti.

Powers

Apart from dramatically increasing the wearer's magical and mundane defense, the efreeti chain also protected its wearer from high heat. This magical fire-proofing was potent enough for the wearer to stroll atop molten rocks as if they were cold and solid. Additionally, this set of armor gave the wearer the ability to speak and understand primordial and draconic languages.

Notable Owners

  • Artus Cimber: one of the undying barae of Mezro, Artus owned a set of efreeti chainmail in the late 15 century DR.
