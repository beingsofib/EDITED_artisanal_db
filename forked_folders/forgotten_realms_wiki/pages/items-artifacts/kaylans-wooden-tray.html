Kaylan's wooden tray was a serving tray with an enchantment that would turn it into a temporary campfire, suitable both as a source of warmth and cooking.

Description

This tray was made of wood and its dimensions were x .

Powers

Once per day, by speaking a command word Kaylan's wooden tray would ignite and burn with the flames of a small campfire for a period of eight hours. These flames posed no permanent damage to the tray itself, allowing it to be used indefinitely, and could be extinguished by reuttering the command word.

Upon command this tray will burn as a normal small campfire for eight hours per day, capable of being used for cooking or warmth. It will extinguish on command and can be used indefinitely. Costs 1,500.
