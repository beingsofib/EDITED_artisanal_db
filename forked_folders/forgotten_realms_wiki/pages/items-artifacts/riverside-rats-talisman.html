Riverside Rats talisman

Item
nameRiverside Rats Talisman
typeJewelry
originWheloon Prison, Cormyr

The Riverside Rats talisman was a novel magical accessory in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

When worn, the talisman gave the wearer the ability to shapeshift to and from a rat, not unlike the ability of a wererat. This made it very useful for sneaking about and squeezing through tiny gaps, but not so good for dealing with average- or large-sized cats.

History

The talismans were shared with allies of the Riverside Rats when they ventured into Wheloon Prison to fight the Night Brigade gang. After being organized by Ryndalla Cuttail, the outsiders met the scout Martildae, who provided them with the talismans. The ratty accessories proved vital for infiltrating and defeating the Night Brigade.
