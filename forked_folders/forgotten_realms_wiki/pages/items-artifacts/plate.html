Plates were a piece of commonplace crockery used in the Realms.

Description

Plates were a kind of shallow dish used to serve food, similar to bowls. They could be made from a variety of materials, including ceramics, wood, tin, copper, and even silver.

Availability

Aurora's Emporium sold dining plates, offering porcelain plates for 5 sp each, stoneware plates for 35 cp, and wooden plates for 1 sp each.
