Metal Spellweaving

Item
nameMetal Spellweaving
typeBook
value2e450 gp

Metal Spellweaving was a crafting book written by the mage Shraevyn. It was a dense tome, with a cover made of platinum. The book discussed the best order in which to cast spells into a weapon. The pages were full of columns of percentages, graphs and diagrams expressed with pedantic detail.
