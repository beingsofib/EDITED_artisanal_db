Frost was a weapon quality that could be applied to weapons. Once applied, the wielder could sheathe the weapon in freezing cold.

Powers

A frost weapon dealt extra cold damage to whoever it struck, but the wielder was immune from the effects of the weapon. If firing from afar with a frost weapon, with such as a crossbow or longbow, the ammunition would also have the frost effect.

Creation

A spellcaster needed the chill metal or ice storm spells to create a weapon with this ability.

Uses

Some experienced Thayan gladiators had their horns, claws, and other natural weapons enhanced as weapons of frost.

Trivia

  • The followers of certain deities — Auril, Deep Sashelas, Labelas Enoreth, Mask, Mielikki, and Ulutiu — would receive a +1 frost enchantment from casting the spell weapon of the deity and holding that deity's favored weapon. These weapons were a battleaxe, trident, quarterstaff, longsword, scimitar, and longspear respectively.
  • Winter's Eye was an enchanted longsword of frost that was lit aflame with multiple continual flame spells as a joke and method of misdirection. It belonged to the commander of the Cult of the Dragon named Ellis circa 1369 DR.
