Borax, or tincal, was a type of mineral salt used for a variety of purposes.

Description

Borax was a soft, lusterless stone with a white or off-white color.

Availability

  • Borax was usually obtained from the bottom of lakebeds that had dried up.
  • Borax could be bought from Aurora's Emporium through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue, where it was sold for 1 sp per in paste or stone form.
  • Borax was widely traded, even in remote locations such as Bryn Shander.

Usages

  • Borax was used to treat wood, leather, hide, and fabric to proof it against rotting.
  • Some wizards kept borax within their laboratories as a component for various crafts.
