Rods of surprises were an enchanted variety of rods that could carry messages and transform into a variety of different weapons.

Abilities

A rod of surprises could be extended in length up to and was capable of supporting up to without bending.

Beyond its transformative properties, a rod of surprises could store a message that was up to 25 words and later relay it in a manner similar to the magic mouth spell when a triggering condition set by the wielder was met.

Creation

Creating a rod of surprises required the use of the spells magic mouth, levitate, and warp wood.
