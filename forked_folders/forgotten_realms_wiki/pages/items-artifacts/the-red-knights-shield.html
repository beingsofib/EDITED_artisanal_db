The Red Knight's Shield was an enchanted shield that was once wielded by the Red Knight herself.

Description

The shield appeared simple in design and crafting. Its back rim was decorated with a carved image of a horse's head.

Powers

The Red Knight's Shield possessed a powerful +4 enchantment.

History

When this enchanted item was ensorcelled with divination magics, it became known that the Red Knight's Shield was indeed an authentic item, wielded by the exarch of Tempus – Red Knight, in her mortal life.

The shield's whereabouts were unknown until it reemerged in the , found among many trade goods, treasures, and old enchanted items stashed in the Dragon's Eye of the Spine of the World by minions of a marilith demon Yxunomei.
