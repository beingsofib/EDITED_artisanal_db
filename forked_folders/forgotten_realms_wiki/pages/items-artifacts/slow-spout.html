Slow Spout

Item
nameSlow Spout
typeAle
locationUnderdark

Slow Spout was a duergan ale that was thick and bitter. It was so named because it tended to swirl around in one's insides until it was "spouted" back up.

Availability

In Menzoberranzan, it was mostly enjoyed by goblins and kobolds, but it was not unknown for drow to drink it.
