The Jolly Pelican was a ship stationed out of Luskan that traveled a specific route along the Sword Coast North during the late 15 century DR.

Activities

The Pelican was used to transport individuals to and from the Revel's End prison, which was situated on the coast of the Sea of Moving Ice in the Frozenfar.

When needed, the ship made the voyage from Luskan to Revel's End, transporting prisoners from the coastal city to the isolated prison. Staff members from Revel's End that needed relief from duty were then shuttled along the same route, south to Luskan for leave.

History

Circa the , the vessel was targeted as an asset by Bethra an assocaite of Varrin from Clan Axebreaker.
