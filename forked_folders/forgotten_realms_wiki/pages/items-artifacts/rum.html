Rum was a variety of fragrant distilled alcohol most famously associated with the pirates of the Sea of Fallen Stars, thanks to it being a very common type of drink carried aboard.

Availability

The fortified town and the isle of Skaug in the Nelanther Isles was known as a major producer of rum. Another producer of rum was One-Eyed Jax in Luskan on the Sword Coast North in the late 15 century DR.

Rum was on offer at The Happy Stein and Skully's Bar and Bait dockside tavern in the city of the Ravens Bluff

Throughout Faerûn, rum was used to flavor and moisten fruitcakes, including those sold through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue. Aurora's gingerbread was also sold with rum sauce. Black forest cake decorated with rum-soaked cherries was one of the pastries on the Downunda Patisserie's menu in Ravens Bluff.

Rum was found only on the surface of Toril, making it a valuable export into the Underdark and the drow city of Menzoberranzan. Similarly, in Maztica, rum was rare and found in Ulatos, from where it was sold to the towns of Tezat and Azatl at the dockside drinking establishments.

Notable Rums

  • BG Rum, a harsh concoction from the city of Baldur's Gate, popular among the sailors of the Sea of Swords.
  • Calishite rum, a popular rum exported out of Calimshan and imported into the Moonshae Isles.
  • Chultan Fireswill, a heavily-spiced variety with potent smell and fiery taste, produced in Chult.
  • Grog, a type of watered down rum often sold for cheap.
  • Radagan's Red Rum, a variety of expensive and rare drink found behind the bar at the Yawning Portal in the late 15 century DR.
  • Red-dog rum, a cheap and unpalatable rum from Lathtarl's Lantern.
  • Rolling Deck Rum, a variety of blended sweet rum popular among the sailors.
  • Whalebone Spiced, a popular amber-colored alcohol in the city of Waterdeep.
  • Yggdrasil Garden Rum, a rare variety of rum procured off-world and smuggled onto Toril via wildspace.

Notable Owners

  • Murphy Ironfist, an adventuring dwarvish priest of Moradin from Amn in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
  • Silas Windspray, the captain of the Windspray of Ravens Bluff stocked his ship with rum in the late 14 century DR.
