The Emerald Tayar was a salvage cog captained by Coprates Bile in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. It ferried Sturge Whitsunday in his search for Myth Nantar.

Construction

The Emerald Tayar was a standard, unarmed cog.

Crew

It had 20 sailors in addition to the captain Coprates Bile, the ship's mage, Rondo Marsh, and chief diver Illsa Merrymore. Briefly, one of the sailors, Aymar Athelmeyer, was actually a polymorphed malenti.

History

The Emerald Tayar was bought by Coprates Bile roughly around the , after three years of being a cabin boy and then three more as first mate. She became a salvage vessel under the captain.

In the , Sturge Whitsunday hired Coprates Bile to help him on a journey to find Myth Nantar, coming aboard with his sister Aleine Whitsunday and the gnomish inventor Nagreous Twiddle; the Tayyar towed a barge holding Nagreous' Colimpha, an experimental diving bell.

Unknown to them, one of the seamen was a malenti in disguise, Aymar Athelmeyer.
