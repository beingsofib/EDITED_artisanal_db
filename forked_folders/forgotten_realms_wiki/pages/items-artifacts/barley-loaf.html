Barley loaf was a type of bread made using barley flour, a cheap alternative to wheat bread and a staple of peasant diet. It could be found in Neverwinter and in Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, in the mid-14 century DR.

History

In the , a barley loaf was "served" to a rare sight—a dwarf bard and artist. Morgalla the Mirthful was in the kitchens of Khelben Arunsun's Blackstaff Tower when she ravaged a serving platter of roasted chicken, cheese, and a loaf of barley bread.
