Coastboat

Ship
nameCoastboat
typeSailing ship
locationAglarond

A coastboat was a long craft resembling a canoe with oars and sails. In absence of a formal navy, these craft were used along the coasts and rivers of Aglarond by its elite "Forester" units, for transportaion and raids.
