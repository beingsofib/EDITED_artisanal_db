Mint ale

Item
nameMint Ale
typeAlcohol
locationSembian gnomes

Mint ale was a drink brewed by gnomes in Sembia. It was known to have been brewed around 1350 DR. It was a favorite of Darrask Baergil, captain of the Riders of Mistledale.
