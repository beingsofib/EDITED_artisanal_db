Band of the holy way

nameBand of the Holy Way

originMyth Drannor, north Faerûn

The band of the holy way was a blessed silver ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The ring gave a great aid to divine spellcasters, helping them to smite evil and turn undead more often and more powerfully.

History

The ring was held in the hoard of the fang dragon Goranathanil, who laired in the ruins of Myth Drannor during the brief conflict between the Elves of Cormanthor and Red Wizards of Droic Orgul.
