The Silver slinger was a holy repeating heavy crossbow found in Eberron during the mid-1480s DR off Faerûn.

Description

The crossbow was carved in the form of a beaked, horned beast, with wings outstretched serving as the prods and bolts shooting from its mouth.

Powers

The crossbow was filled with holy and good energy, making it exceptional at smiting evil. It was even more deadly against the undead, and each bolt shot from it was magically enhanced as if they were made of silver, better harming monsters like lycanthropes and vampires, and further altered so that they struck with crushing rather than piercing force, letting them shatter skeletons easily.

History

The crossbow was created for the Templars of the Silver Flame, who desired a tool for smiting the undead. The crossbow was later awarded to adventurers in Stormreach, who helped the paladin Felnis investigate the Lords of Dust when they opened a passage to Khyber and the Spinner's Prison, ultimately leading them to Faerûn.
