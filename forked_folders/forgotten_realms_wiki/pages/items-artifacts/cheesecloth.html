Sieve for cheesemaking

Cheesecloth was a form of textile in the Realms so named for its use in cheesemaking.

Usages

The primary use of cheesecloth across Faerûn was in some styles of cheesemaking, where it was used as a sieve to remove and drain whey from cheese curds. One such style of cheese that utilized cheesecloth in its preparation was farmer's cheese. or as a material to wrap food in.
