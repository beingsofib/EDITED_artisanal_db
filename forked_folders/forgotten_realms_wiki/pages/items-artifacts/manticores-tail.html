Manticore's Tail was an enchanted spear that belonged to Raika a Reghedmen barbarian adventurer active in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Manticore's Tail was a large polearm wielded with two hands. Its severe-looking metallic speartip was shaped like two triangles perched one atop the other.

Powers

The magic spear was enchanted to sap life energy out of its targets and transferring its lion's share back to the wielder.

Notable Owners

  • Raika: a half-orc barbarian who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
