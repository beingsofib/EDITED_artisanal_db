Blue ice

Substance
nameBlue Ice
locationsYellow Sea, Kara-Tur
typeIce
colorBlue

Blue ice was a mysterious magical substance found in the Yellow Sea of Kara-Tur. Blue ice was so cold it burned flesh, yet it sizzled and smoked when immersed in water.
