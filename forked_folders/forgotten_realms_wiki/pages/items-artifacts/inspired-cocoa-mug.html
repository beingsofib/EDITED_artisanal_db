Inspired cocoa mug

Item
nameInspired Cocoa Mug
typeMagic mug
locationIcewind Dale

Inspired cocoa mugs were magical drinking vessels that filled themselves up with cocoa broth. They saw use in Icewind Dale.

Powers

An inspired cocoa mug could fill itself up with cocoa broth multiple times a day. This drink had restorative properties, and aided in recouping energy when drank while relaxing.
