Poisoner's kit were collections of tools and supplies used in the creation and portioning of various toxins, venoms, and poisons.

Description

A typical poisoner's kit was a pouch filled with poisonous substances, plants, dust, and extracts, all to be used in the mixing of potent poisons. The kits also included measuring tools, such as spoons and vials.

Notable Users

Poisoner's kit were commonly used by various rogues and assassins of the Realms.

  • Marliss, a dark naga advisor of the half-fiend Kharantes in 1375 DR.
  • Skrianna Shadowdusk, a human wizard of Undermountain in the late 15 century DR.
  • Undertow, a locathah ranger in the late 15 century DR.
