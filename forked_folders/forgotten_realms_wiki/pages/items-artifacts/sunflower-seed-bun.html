Sunflower seed bun was a variety of bread found in the city of Baldur's Gate, the Western Heartlands, and west Faerûn in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Sunflower seed buns were small fist-sized buns often stuffed with a chunk of cheese and some sausage. This bread was a staple of the strongheart halfling cuisine.
