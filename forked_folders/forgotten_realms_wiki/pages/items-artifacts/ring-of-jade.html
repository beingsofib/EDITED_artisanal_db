Rings of jade were common minor magic items found across the Sword Coast and exported out of Kara-Tur in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

These jade rings were imbued with simple enchantments that made them glow with soft green light, illuminating an area of around them.

History

By the , these common pieces of enchanted jade were found across many pockets, fingers, and merchant stalls of the Sword Coast. They were especially popular among adventurers, freeing their hand for weapons and tools instead of holding lanterns and torches. As of the , rings of jade were sold at Okuzo's Imports, a shop that dealt with exotic Kara-Turan goods, located on the Harbor Loop in Westgate.
