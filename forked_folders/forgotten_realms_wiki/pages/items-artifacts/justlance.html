Justlance was an adamantine returning spear, and the cleric Marrec's favored weapon in .

Description

Justlance was an adamantine spear, with the tip fashioned in the form of a unicorn's horn.

Powers

Justlance always returned to the wielder's hand after being thrown, unable to be parted from its wielder for long. Blessed by a deity, rot did not adhere to the spear.

History

In , Marrec had been the wielder of Justlance for some time. Those who survived combat with him learned to fear its enchantment.

Notable Owners
