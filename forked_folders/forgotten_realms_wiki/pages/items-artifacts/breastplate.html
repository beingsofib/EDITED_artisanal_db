A breastplate was one of the better forms of medium armor.

Description

The armor covered the wearer's front and back with a metal , and included a helmet and greaves. A light suit of studded leather beneath the breastplate finished the armor suit, protecting the wearer's limbs without restricting movement much.

Notable Wearers of Breastplates

Individuals

  • Aviss and the drow magic-user Kilili both typically wore a golden-hued breastplate.
  • Aeark a barbarian cleric wore a bronze version.

Religions

  • Worshipers of Bane typically wore black breastplates with a red fist painted on and below the shoulder blades.
