Ring of plant control

Item
nameRing of Plant Control
typeMagical ring
value2e2,500 gp

A ring of plant control was a magical ring that imbued wearers with an effect similar to a potion of plant control.

Powers

So long as a ring's effect was concentrated upon,

Notable Owners

  • The demon lord Juiblex was said to have rings of plant control among his hordes of treasure in Shedaklah.
  • Marisa Goodfeet, a specialty priest of the nature goddess Sheela Peryroyl, owned a ring of plant control.
  • Mogion, leader of the Survivors of Moander, owned a ring of plant control that he used in 1357 DR to control a pair of algoids, making them fiercely loyal to him.
