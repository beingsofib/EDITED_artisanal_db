Mintar Gold were a variety of wine exported out of the city of Mintar in the Land of the Lions in the late 14 century DR.

History

In the late 14 century DR, a high shipment of Mintar Gold, over over 2,000 bottles, was brought to the Vast by Georborg Fireblade, a wine merchant from Mintar. He sold a bottle for a single gold raven. Mintar Gold was also used in the wine tasting competition during the year end celebration of the in Mossbridges.
