Titan's Maul was a mysterious magical maul that was rumored to have been forged out of a weapon of an ancient titan.

Description

Titan's Maul was an enormous two-handed bludgeoning weapon, one side of its head was a blunt hammer and the other - a sharp polyhedron.

Powers

This enchanted maul granted the wielded the magical effect of titan's boon upon landing a strike with the weapon. The boon increased the wielder's size by fifteen percent as well as raised their strength for thirty seconds.

History

According to stories, the Titan's Maul was found in the middle of nowhere at the foothills of the Spine of the World. No concrete information was known of the weapon's true origins, but there have been claims and theories that th Titan's Maul was created out of melted down weapon of an actual ancient titan.

This maul found it's way to an oni mage circa 1490 DR. The ogre mage, also known as the Troll Matriarch, was encountered in the Evermoors by the Champion of the Burning Dawn and their companions during their rescue mission of recently captured dwarves. After the creature's defeat, the adventurers discovered the Titan's Maul in the oni's treasure chest.
