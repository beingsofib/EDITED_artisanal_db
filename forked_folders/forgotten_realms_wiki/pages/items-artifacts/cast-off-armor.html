Cast-off armor

Item
nameCast-off Armor
typeMagic armor
rarity5eCommon

Cast-off armor was armor that was magically enchanted to become exceedingly quick for its wearer to doff.

Description

Any type of armor could be enchanted to become cast-off armor.

Powers

While normal armor took between 1 and 5 minutes to remove depending on its complexity, cast-off armor could be completely removed within only a few seconds, regardless of type.
