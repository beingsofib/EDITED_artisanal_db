A fang was a weapon unique to the continent of Kara-Tur.

Description

A fang is a robust iron weapon, taking inspiration from the elephant goad. It was in length, similar to that of a short sword. At one end, there was a sharp point, complemented by a sturdy hooked point positioned just below it.
