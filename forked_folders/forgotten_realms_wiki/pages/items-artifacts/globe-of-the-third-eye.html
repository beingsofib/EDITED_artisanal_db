The Globe of the Third Eye was an artifact orb that contained divine powers of Savras the All-Seeing.

Powers

The Globe was a crystal ball with the watchful eyes of the deity Savras floating inside, akin to snowflakes in a snow globe. It was said that the Globe contained Savras's omnipotent power and granted its owner magical powers.

History

In the late 15 century DR, the Globe of the Third Eye was in the hands of Sybella Artis, a seer of Savrasian faith who was on a quest to find the Staff of Savras. During her time in Neverwinter, Sybella ran her operation from the Hall of Justice vaults and offered the artifact for purchase to the adventurers who aided the woman in her quest.
