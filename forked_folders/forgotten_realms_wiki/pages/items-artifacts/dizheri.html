Dizheri

Item
nameDizheri

A dizheri was a musical instrument used by the peoples native to the land of Osse.

Description

It was a wind instrument, often decorated with colorful designs, and was built sturdy enough to be used as a blunt weapon.

Notable Owners

  • Gungarri Ulmarra
