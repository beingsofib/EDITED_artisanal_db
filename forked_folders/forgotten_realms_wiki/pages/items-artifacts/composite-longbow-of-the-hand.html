Composite longbows of the Hand were masterfully crafted longbows found in the Seldarine's Hand citadel on the Spine of the World.

Description

These bows were crafted by joint efforts of elves of the Seldarine's Hand and dwarves of Dorn's Deep.

Powers

Composite longbows of the Hand possessed a +2 enchantment.

History

After completion of construction of Seldarine's Hand circa 900 DR, the inhabitants of the citadel lived in a close alliance with the dwarves of Dorn's Deep. The troops of the elven citadel were outfitted with the best armor and weapons created by the crafters of both races. By 1281 DR, the Seldarine's Hand had been ruined for centuries and came to be known as the Severed Hand, within, composite longbows of the Hand could be found within the Tower of Corellon.
