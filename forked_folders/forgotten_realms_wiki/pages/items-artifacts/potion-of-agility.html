Potions of agility were a type of potion.

Powers

This potion temporarily increased the imbiber's dexterity.

Notable Owners

  • Min Minling, a merchant from the village of Imnesvale in the Umar Hills circa 1368 DR.
  • Oswald Fiddlebender carried a selection of home-made potions, including potions of agility, during his time in Kuldahar circa 1281 DR, and later in Targos of the Ten-Towns circa 1312 DR.
