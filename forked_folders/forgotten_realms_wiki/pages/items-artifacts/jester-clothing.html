Jester clothing was a costume in the Realms popular among bards and, more specifically, jesters.

Description

A typical jester's outfit included white gloves, a three-cornered hat with bells, a star-shaped collar with bells, a poofy satin shirt, bright stockings, large shoes, and baggy pantaloons.

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 25 gold pieces for the complete outfit.

Notable Users

  • Jesters outfits were favored by bards and jesters.
