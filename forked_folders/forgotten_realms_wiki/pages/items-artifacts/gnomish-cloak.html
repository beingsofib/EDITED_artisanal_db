Gnomish cloak

Item
nameGnomish cloak
typeCloak
value10 – 12 gp

Gnomish cloaks were special cloaks worn by some rogues.

Description

These wonderful cloaks were expertly crafted with grey fabrics that created a textured look, allowing a prowling thief to blend into the shadows with more conviction.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold standard gnomish cloaks for 10 gold pieces, as well as ones big enough to completely cover a large individual for 12 gp. The catalogue noted that, while they were not nearly as grand as cloaks of elvenkind, gnomish cloaks made a find addition to any rogue's wardrobe.
