Hair of the Wolf was an alcoholic beverage sold in the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale.

Availability

The libation was sold as early as the at The Salty Dog tavern in Targos. Hair of the Wolf was sold for three gold pieces at the bar by its portly proprietor Gohar who was happy to share the town's latest rumors with paying customers.
