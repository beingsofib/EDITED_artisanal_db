A professor orb was a sentient magical orb imbued with academic knowledge.

Description

A professor orb was a smooth sphere of smoky gray quartz, approximately the size of a grapefruit, and weighing . Pinpricks of silver light shimmered from deep inside, indicating the magical intelligence within.

Powers

A professor orb could speak and read and could understand four languages, and perceived its environment through magical means.

It was able to cast mage hand at will, but only to move itself.

With scholarly personalities, professor orbs provided information and insight on its fields of expertise. Each had four different narrow areas of academic knowledge.

Known Professor Orbs

  • Professor Skant, a lawful good orb owned by Vellynne Harpell and stolen by Nass Lantomir, expert in Netherese history, vampire lore, the tarrasque, and Elverquisst rituals.
  • Professor Bring, a lawful neutral orb found in Dweomercore in Undermountain, expert in Undermountain history, identifying gemstones, xorn, and dwarven grooming habits.
