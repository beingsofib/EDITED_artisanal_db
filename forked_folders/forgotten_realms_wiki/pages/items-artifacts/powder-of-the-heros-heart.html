Powder of the hero's heart

Item
namePowder of the Hero's Heart
typeMagical Substance
value2e1,800 gp

Powder of the hero's heart was an enchanted substance that instilled bravery when inhaled.

Description

This powder was dull red in color.

Powers

Powder of the hero's heart filled the hearts of anyone inhaling it with bravery and negated any magical or nonmagical fear. This effect lasted for 5–20 minutes.

Notable Owners

Batina al-Shaat, a moralist priest from Zakhara, carried some powder of the hero's heart.
