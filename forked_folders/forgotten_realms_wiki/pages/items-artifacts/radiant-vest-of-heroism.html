Radiant vests of heroism were enchanted suits of heavy armor, encountered in Chult in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

These sets of armor were imbued with a +5 enchantment to the wearer's defense as well as improving the wearer's vitality. Radiant vests were also blessed with beneficial magics that filled the allies of the wearer with drive and will for victory, similar to the effects of the heroism spell.

Notable Owners

Dragonbait: a saurial paladin and a one-time companion of Artus Cimber during their foray into the jungles of Chult in the late 15 century DR.
