Unholy weapons were those imbued or otherwise enchanted with evil power that was especially harmful when wielded against morally-upstanding foes.

Powers

Unholy weapons inflicted more grievous wounds whenever they struck a good-aligned creature or being. Similarly, any godly person that attempted to pick up an unholy weapon themselves were severely weakened until they let it go.

Notable Owners

  • Malkizid, wielded a +2 silver unholy bastard sword.

Notable Unholy Weapons

  • Skull blades, enchanted longswords wielded by members of the Cyricist faith.
