Waterdeep News was a newspaper based in Waterdeep on the Sword Coast North, published daily in the early 1370s DR.

It reported on a variety of current political matters, crimes, social events, gossip and unusual happenings in the city. It was one of the largest daily newspapers in Faerûn.
