Lens of detection

Item
nameLens of Detection
typeLense
locationMoonsea

Lenses of detection were enchanted optic aids that could reveal even the most minute details, especially popular among rogues for that reason.

Description

These lenses were circular prisms in diameter, held in place by a decorated frame and a handle.

Powers

Apart from lenses of detection's optical enragement that allowed the user to see the finest details in objects, they also magically revealed all hidden traps within the user's eyesight, as if via the find traps spell.
