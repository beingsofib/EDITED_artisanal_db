Ring of mindcloaking

Item
nameRing of Mindcloaking
typeEnchanted ring

A ring of mindcloaking was a magic item, similar to a ring of mind shielding, that protected the wearer from a variety of effects, no matter if they were psionic or magical in nature. Unlike the ring of mind shielding, however, a ring of mindcloaking was able to project false information in response to the probing, appearing as if the wearer's mind was unguarded.

Powers

A ring of mindcloaking protected the wearer from a greater number of phenomena than a ring of mind shielding. The psionic discipline of ESP was one such example, as were spells that allowed one to know alignment, detect evil, or even detect lies, but this was not necessarily an exhaustive list. A ring of mindcloaking wielded by a sufficiently experienced wearer could then be used to present different thoughts and an altered alignment—this not only blocked, but actively deceived, any attempts to divine the contents of their user's mind.

Notable Owners

Rings of mindcloaking were in frequent use among the more influential members of the Kraken Society, a mysterious information network that operated primarily in the Savage North and nearby Trackless Sea. Prior to his death, Alahar Khaumfros, a member of the group and Waterbaron of Yartar, was one wearer of such a ring.
