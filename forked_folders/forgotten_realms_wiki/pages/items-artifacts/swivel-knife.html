Swivel knife

Item
nameSwivel Knife
typeTool
materialMetal, wood

Swivel knives were a type of leatherworking tool, resembling an oddly-shaped knife.

Description

Swivel knives had a chisel-like blade, guided along by a saddle-shaped hand rest at the end of the handle.
