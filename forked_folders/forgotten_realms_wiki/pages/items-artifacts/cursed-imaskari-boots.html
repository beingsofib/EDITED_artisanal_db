Cursed Imaskari boots

Item
nameCursed Imaskari boots
value2e2 gp

The cursed Imaskari boots were an Imaskari relic unearthed from the ruins of Solon by the slaves of Ambuchar Devayam.

Description

These boots were sized correctly to whoever found them.

Powers

If worn for longer than ten minutes, the wearer would develop a terrible case of athlete's foot.
