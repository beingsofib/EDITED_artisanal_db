Sesame oil was a type of vegetable oil, produced from the seeds of the sesame plant, that was considered very useful in cooking.

Availability

Places in the Realms where sesame was known to be produced or sold included:

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold sesame oil at a standard price of 10 gold pieces per .
  • In the desert region of Anauroch, the Bedine tribes were known to produce sesame oil by placing sesame seeds in large mortars, hollowed out from an acacia tree. They would then crush the seeds with a wooden beam, counter-weighted with stones, by having a camel haul it around in a circle. The Bedine sold this oil in clay jugs for 1 sp.

Notable Users

Bedine tribesmen were known to rub a mixture of the oil and indigo on their chests and legs during the winter season, believing it would keep them warm.
