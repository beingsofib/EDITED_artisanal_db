The Wave Wyvern was the personal galleass of Grand Duke Farnig Seasilver of Marsember.

Description

The ship earned its name from its figurehead, a depiction of a shiny, silver-scaled wyvern with an open mouth.

As were some large sailing vessels, the Wyvern was equipped with a number of longboats.

The ship's tiny brig was referred to as "the Stink".

History

When the city of Marsember was invaded by the Shadovar, on Uktar 2 of the , the grand duke prepared his ship to sail to Elversult. While he planned to bring aboard both his wife and daughter aboard, Grand Duchess Elira and Lady Arietta, the younger Seasilver lady did not make it aboard.

Just after the ship left harbor, the Prince of Shade killed the Grand Duke, took the ship as his own, and kidnapped Duchess Elira. Lady Arietta hired the gnome smuggler Falrinn Greatorm, captain of the Lonely Roamer, to sail after the ship and recover her mother. Under the cover of "gnomish fog" the Lonely Roamer intercepted the Wyvern, and the senior noblewoman was rescued.

A short time later, the Lonely Roamer was destroyed by a massive leviathan, the Wave Wyvern was promised to Captain Greatorm as recompense for his efforts. Greatorm sailed the ship to the city of Westgate, so Duchess Elira could be safely delivered to her family by the Marsember watchman Jang.
