A feydark crystal was a variety of mineral that was unique to the underground Feydark region of the Feywild.

Powers

Each of these crystals possessed a portion of feywild energy.

Usage

Feydark crystals were used by the Iliyanbruen elves of New Sharandar to provide power to their magical defenses and rituals. When placed in the elves' purifying pool a large portion of them were used by the Arcane Reservoir, while some others were released as individual motes of life energy called feywild sparks.

Notable Owners

  • Merrisara Winterwhite, the leader of the Iliyanbruen elves led the efforts to gather feydark crystals in order to power the Arcane Reservoir.
