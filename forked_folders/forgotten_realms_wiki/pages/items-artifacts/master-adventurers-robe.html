Master adventurer's robes were enchanted wizardly robes, famously created by then young and relatively unknown adventurer Vangerdahast Aeiulvana.

Powers

These robes were ensorcelled to provide magical protection to wizards and absorbed the damage that was dealt to their owners.

History

Vangerdahast Aeiulvana was a famous royal magician to King Azoun Obarskyr IV. Before that, however, he was an accomplished spell-slinging adventurer. One of the deeds that brought him fame was creating the master adventurer's robes. With wizards being extremely vulnerable in close combat, these sets of enchanted cloth granted them protection and time to weave a spell against the melee opponents.

By 1372 DR, master adventurer's robes could be found in possession of wizards from Cormyr, the Sword Coast, and Daggerford vicinity.

Notable Owners

  • Boonmoppet: a lonely imp familiar who inherited his master's wizardly paraphernalia that included a master adventurer's robe.
