Potion of love

Item
namePotion of Love
typePotion
value2e300 gp

A potion of love was an enchanted potion used to charm others for an extended period of time.

Powers

This potion charmed whoever drank it for up to a full week.

Notable Users

Wallace, a merchant from Trademeet, carried a potion of love.
