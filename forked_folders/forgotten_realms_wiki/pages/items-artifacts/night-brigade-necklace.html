Night Brigade necklace

Item
nameNight Brigade Necklace
typeNecklace
originWheloon Prison, Cormyr

Night Brigade necklaces were accessories used by the Night Brigade gang members to identify themselves in the late 15 century DR.

Description

These necklaces had crude insignia upon them which the Night Brigade members used to identify their membership.

Powers

A particular magical Night Brigade necklace bore subtle enchantments that improved the marksmanship of the wearer.

History

A powerful example of the necklaces was found on the forces of Agarta the Breaker in her arenas after they were defeated by the Riverside Rats gang in Wheloon Prison.
