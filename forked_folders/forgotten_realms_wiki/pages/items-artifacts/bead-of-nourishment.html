Bead of nourishment

Item
nameBead of Nourishment
typeWondrous item
rarity5eCommon

Beads of nourishment were magical pellets that provided nourishment for extended periods of time.

Description

The bead was spongy and gelatinous in texture. When eaten, it dissolved on the tongue but did not have any taste.

Powers

A single bead of nourishment provided an equivalent amount of nutrition to one day's worth of rations.
