Crossbow of distance

Item
nameCrossbow of Distance
typeWeapon
value2e10,000 gp–12,000 gp

A crossbow of distance was an enchanted crossbow capable of firing bolts to greater distances than their mundane counterparts.

Description

These weapons came in various forms and appeared to be normal crossbows.

Powers

Any bolt fired from a crossbow of distance had its effective range doubled.

Notable Owners

Lord Orgauth, the ruler of Zhentil Keep following the city's fall in the late 14 century DR, carried a crossbow of distance.
