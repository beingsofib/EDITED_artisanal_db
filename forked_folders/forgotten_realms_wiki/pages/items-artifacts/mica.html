Mica

Substance
nameMica
typeMineral

Mica was a type of stone found on Toril.

Mica was sometimes used as substitute for glass in making windows. Experienced stonecrafting dwarf and gnome clans could cut stone in thin sheets, allowing them to be set in window stalls and to let in a small glow when light hit them.
