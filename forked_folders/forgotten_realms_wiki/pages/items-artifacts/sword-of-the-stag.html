The Sword of the Stag was a royal sword of Athalantar created for King Belaur Aumar in the early 3 century DR.

Description

The steel of the sword glittered.

Powers

It was said to have been enchanted by the Magelords of Athalantar.

History

The sword had been newly forged by , when it was wielded by King Belaur in his battle against his nephew, Prince Elminster Aumar, when the latter besieged and finally captured Castle Athalgard of Athalantar. After Belaur was defeated, nearly laying low Elminster in the process, the sword was picked up by a baker, Hannibur, who wielded it to defend a downed Elminster from Belaur's courtiers. Later, Elminster had the sword on his lap during the subsequent coronation.
