Rosto was a gnomish highsunfeat sandwich from the city of Ravens Bluff in the Vast.

Description

Rosto was a rye bread sandwich of sliced roast rothé, drizzled with mushroom gravy or dill sauce. It often served with gnomish pale ales, such as Rockgrit, Stint, or Greentart.

History

Rosto was a staple of a gnomish out-of-the way café in Ravens Bluff called Le PoTeete Café in the late 14 century DR. The only other highsunfeat alternative on the menu was Brick, a type of stuffed and baked bread.
