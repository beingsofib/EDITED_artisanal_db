Potion of plant control

Item
namePotion of Plant Control
typePotion
value2e300 gp

A potion of plant control was a potion capable of influencing plants.

Powers

The imbiber of these potions could influence plants, fungi, molds, and shambling mounds, causing them to perform any of their normal activities with the exception of any self-destructive behavior. The area of control for normal plants was a square with sides.

Notable Owners

Fennis, a winemaker genie in Zakhara, carried several potions of clairaudience that he used to produce fabulous vintages.
