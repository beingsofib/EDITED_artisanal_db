Dagger of life stealing

Item
nameDagger of Life Stealing
typeDagger
value2e25,000 gp

A dagger of life stealing was a a vicious enchanted weapon that stole the life essence of from rended flesh.

Powers

This dagger stole the life essence from a living creature when it pierced their flesh, transferring that energy directly to the wielder in the form of healing power.

Notable Owners

Aida al-Irrahman, the leader of the Winged Bulls in Zakhara, carried a dagger of life stealing.
