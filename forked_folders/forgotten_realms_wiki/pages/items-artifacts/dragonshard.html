Dragonshards were a unique magical stones found in the world of Eberron.

History

When the Lords of Dust found the Spinner's Prison and sought to free her, they were instructed to harvest the dragonshards found along the road to Tarath Marad and deliver them to the Overlord. When she made her escape into the Demonweb Pits thanks to her sister Lolth, she in turn delivered the dragonshards which Lolth had requested.
