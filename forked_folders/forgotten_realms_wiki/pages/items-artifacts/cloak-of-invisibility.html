A cloak of invisibility was a powerful magical cloak that allowed its wearer to become invisible for a cumulative total of 2 hours. It then regains 1 hour of use per 12 continual hours of not being used.

Powers

The wearer activated the cloak's power by pulling up its hood. The cloak of invisibility then turned the wearer and everything being worn or carried invisible for as long as the hood was pulled up.

Notable Cloak of Invisibility

  • Daveak's cloak: the enchanted cloak from Chult, found in the late 15 century DR. This cloak allowed the wearer to turn invisible at will and sages argued about its origins and even how its enchantments were weaved.

Notable Owners

The mercenary Jarlaxle Baenre owned a cloak of invisibility as part of his extensive collection of magic items.
