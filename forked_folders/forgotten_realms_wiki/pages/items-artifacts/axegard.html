Axegard

Item
nameAxegard
typeTwo-handed battleaxe
level2e+4

Axegard was a two-handed battleaxe that was the favored weapon of Gorm Gulthyn, the dwarven deity of vigilance and protection.

Powers

This battleaxe had a +4 enchantment. It had all the properties of a robe of eyes, meaning that whoever wielded the axe could see in all directions, amongst other things.
