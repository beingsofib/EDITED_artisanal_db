Winter wolf scarf

Item
nameWinter Wolf Scarf
typeScarf
locationIcewind Dale, Northwest Faerûn

Winter wolf scarves were enchanted neck wrappings that carried magical properties of winter wolves' pelt they were made of.

Powers

These strips of soft white fur granted the wearers resistance to mundane and magical cold. Additionally, the scarves slightly improved the wearers' charisma.

History

Circa , Oswald Fiddlebender crafted various magic items of clothing using pelts of winter wolves that followed the Cultists of Auril that plagued Icewind Dale. Some of other items included winter wolf cloaks, winter wolf gloves, winter wolf hats, and winter wolf boots.
