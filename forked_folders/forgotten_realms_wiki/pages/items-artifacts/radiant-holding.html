Radiant holding

Item
nameRadiant Holding
typeWeapon quality
casterlevel3e9
value3e+3

Radiant holding was a quality that could be applied to ranged weapon ammunition, such as arrows, bolts, or bullets. When the ammunition struck the target, it immobilized them like the hold monster spell.

Crafting

The spells magic missile and hold monster were used when imbuing ammunition with this quality.

History

This quality was used in the Old Empires by wizards' guards.
