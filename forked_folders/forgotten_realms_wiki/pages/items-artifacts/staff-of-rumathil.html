Staff of Rumathil

Item
nameStaff of Rumathil
typeStaff
locationEvermeet

The staff of Rumathil was a legendary magical staff possessed by the gold elves of House Aelorothi on Evermeet by 1367 DR.

It enabled the bearer—cleric or wizard—to cast their spells at a higher level than they would normally. Furthermore, offensive spells they cast became more damaging.
