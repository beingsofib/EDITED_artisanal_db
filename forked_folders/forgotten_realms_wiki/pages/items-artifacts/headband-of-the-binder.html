A headband of the Binder was a magic item created by bards It was also referred to as a headband of the lorebinder.

Description

This item was a metal circlet of blue-green hue with the symbol of Oghma, a blank scroll, embossed on the front.

Powers

The wearer of a headband of the Binder was able to read magic three times a day. This item was created by bards, and if the wearer was a bard, it also greatly increased the retention and recall of bardic knowledge. It also did the same for loremasters and the lore they studied.
