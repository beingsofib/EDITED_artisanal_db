The Crown of Salt was an artifact from Myth Nantar.

Description

It was a slim, clear-crystal tiara made of salt crystals.

Powers

The device's powers were unknown; though it registered as magical, it displayed no powers.

History

The artifact was stolen by the pirate Vilander at some point.

In the , if the Selgauntan expedition captained by Sturge Whitsunday made contact with Myth Nantar, they'd likely find themselves charged with recovering the Crown from Vilander's hideout in Agenais. If they succeeded, Selgaunt would have a way to call on aid from Myth Nantar, just in time for elections, too, and the crown would be worn by Tu'ola'sara hence.
