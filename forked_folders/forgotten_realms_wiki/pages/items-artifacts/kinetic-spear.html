The kinetic spear was a unique enchanted polearm created by the philosopher wizard named Giddeon the Believer‎ sometime in the late 10 or early 11 century DR.

Powers

Giddeon's kinetic spear was a light spear that possessed a strong +2 enchantment that guided the wielder's arm and increased the weapon's damage. Additionally, the weapon was ensorcelled to tap into lines of latent natural magical power that permeated the world, channeling the magic through the shaft, exploding its tip with a violent crushing force, similarly to the effects of the lance of disruption spell.

History

The kinetic spear was the result of Giddeon's research into "currents" of natural magical energy that existed all throughout Toril. The weapon functioned as a "focus device," allowing a non-mage wielder to harness its destructive power. Giddeon's kinetic spear was one of the very few items to survive a massive explosion of the mage's laboratory in the . The weapon found its way into the frigid lands of Icewind Dale by the . It belonged to a hin cleric Quinn Silverfinger of Waukeen who led The Golden Lodge shrine in Lonelywood.
