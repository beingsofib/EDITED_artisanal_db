Venomous

Item
nameVenomous
typeWeapon quality
casterlevel3e9th
value3e+1

Venomous was a magical quality that could be applied to weapons, allowing it to poison a victim.

Powers

Once per day, it gave the wielder the ability to inflict a poison spell on a target in addition to a normal injury. They could choose this after a successful hit.

Creation

Creation required the poison spell.
