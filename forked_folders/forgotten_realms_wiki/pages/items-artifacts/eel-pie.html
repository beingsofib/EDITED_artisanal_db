Eel pie

Item
nameEel pie
typeFood
locationWaterdeep, Sword Coast

Eel pie was a savory pie made with eel meat as a filling.

Availability

  • The Broken Lance, a cozy tavern in Waterdeep's Sea Ward, had eel pies as its house specialty.
  • In Cormyr, this dish was quite common and popular in the city of Marsember.
  • In Neverwinter, some impoverished folk were known to bake eel pie.
  • In the coastal areas of Turmish, eel pies were a staple of local cuisine, often served along with fishhead soup, chives, and asparagus.
