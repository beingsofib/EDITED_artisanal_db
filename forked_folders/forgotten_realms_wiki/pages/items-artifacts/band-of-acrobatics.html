The band of acrobatics was a magic ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Description

The ring was made of platinum, but was notably lighter than it should have been.

Powers

The band of acrobatics gave a powerful assistance to everything relating to jumping, tumbling, and generally leaping about.

History

The ring was held in the hoard of the fang dragon Goranathanil, who laired in the ruins of Myth Drannor at the time of the fighting between the Elves of Cormanthor and Red Wizards of Droic Orgul.
