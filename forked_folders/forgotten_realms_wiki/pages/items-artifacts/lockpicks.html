Lockpicks

Item
nameLockpicks
typeTools
value30 gp

Lockpicks, or thieves' picks were one of the most indispensible tools for a rogue.

Uses

A complete set of lockpicks included wires, angled wrenches, rods, oil, a small hammer, a magnifying glass, chisels, and acid.

Prices

A good set of lockpicks cost 30 gold, though sets of considerably inferior quality could be purchased for much less.
