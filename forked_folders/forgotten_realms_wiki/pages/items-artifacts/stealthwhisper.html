Stealthwhisper

Item
nameStealthwhisper
typeLongsword
rules3rd edition

Stealthwhisper was one of two weapons possessed by the deity Mask. It was his favored weapon.

It was a magic +4 longsword with the frost, keen, and speed special abilities.
