Nimbleness

Item
nameNimbleness
typeArmor quality
casterlevel3e8
value3e+1

Nimbleness was a quality that could be applied to armor, allowing its wearer to have greater freedom of movement than would normally be available for the type of armor being worn. It felt lighter and less restrictive to wear, but it didn't weigh any less.
