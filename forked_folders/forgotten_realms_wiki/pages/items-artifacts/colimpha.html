The Colimpha was a diving bell built by Nagreous Twiddle in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Construction

It could seat six.

History

After Nagreous Twiddle built an experimental diving bell for the Sembian navy, he built a definitive model, the Colimpha, which he named after a patriarch of his family. That model was then brought along by Sturge Whitsunday alongside Nagreous himself for a mission to discover Myth Nantar,

The diving bell may have been the subject of tampering by Aymar Athelmeyer, a disguised malenti, who wanted to prevent Sturge from improving relationships with Myth Nantar. If so, the diving bell would become a bit less of a forward-thinking invention, and a bit more of a deathtrap, but just a bit, making it impossible to lift the diving bell back from the depths and in fact causing physical damage to its crew during descent.
