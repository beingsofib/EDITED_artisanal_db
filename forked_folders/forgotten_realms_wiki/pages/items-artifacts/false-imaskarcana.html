False Imaskarcana

Item
nameFalse Imaskarcana
typeArtifact

The False Imaskarcana were a collection of seven artifacts created before the collapse of the ancient Imaskar Empire. By their name, they were associated with the genuine Imaskarcana, seven artifacts that recorded the empire's knowledge of magic.

One of the False Imaskarcana was Dhonas's Shroud.

After the empire's fall, in , the surviving artificer Kujawa carried Dhonas's Shroud and claimed the Dragon Throne in the Khati city of Thakos, the former capital of Upper Imaskar. With that, he declared himself Emperor and founded a new realm, Anok-Imaskar.
