The Dreamheart was an ancient aboleth magical artifact.

History

The Dreamheart was first found in modern times by the kuo-toa Nogah in 1395 DR, in a drowned and destroyed illithid city deep under the Sea of Fallen Stars in Serôs.

Description

The Dreamheart was a black stone, not quite spherical,

Abilities

The Dreamheart had great unknown power.

  • Levitate
  • Light
  • Mass charm
  • Black flames that could reduce a being to cinders.
  • Spell resistance
  • Dominate monster
