Infernal spears were enchanted polearms wielded by Iron Consuls of Bane in the late 15 century DR.

Description

These spears' tips were inscribed with filigreed infernal runes that imbued them with hellish magics.

Powers

Infernal spears possessed a simple +1 enchantment that increased their damage output and guided the wielder's hand towards the target. These weapons also dealt additional poison damage with each strike and had a chance of inflicting a longe-lasting poisoning of living targets.

History

These infernal spears could be found in the hands of the Cult of Bane that infiltrated the city of Baldur's Gate in the . The cult's leader, Enver Gortash, outfitted his Iron Consuls with infernal spears. Iron Consuls Mian and Nuff and their magic spears stayed at Lord Gortash's side in the Wyrm's Rock fortress, until his assassination by the Absolute Adversaries.
