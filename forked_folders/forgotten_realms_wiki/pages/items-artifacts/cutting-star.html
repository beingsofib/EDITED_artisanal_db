Cutting Stars were enchanted kukri that originated from beyond the civilized regions of Toril.

Powers

These exotic blades possessed a +2 enchantment and scorched their targets with magical fire. Additionally, Cutting Stars had a chance of blinding the opponents upon successful strike for eighteen seconds.

History

Much information on the origins of these magic blades remained a mystery with them occasionally being brought by adventurers from beyond civilization. Oftentimes these travelers told stories of ancient ruined temples and cults that worshiped sun or stars. In the late 14 century DR, several of Cutting Stars could be found along the Sword Coast, and one in the city of Zhentil Keep, Moonsea.
