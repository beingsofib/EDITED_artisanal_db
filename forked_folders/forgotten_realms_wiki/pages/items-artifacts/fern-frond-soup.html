Fern-frond soup

Item
nameFern-Frond Soup
typeSoup
locationDead Tree Hollow, the Vast

Fern-frond soup was a soup dish made in Faerûn, using the fronds of a fern.

Geography

The village of Dead Tree Hollow in the Vast was famous for its fern-frond soup. It also made a fiddlehead soup from younger plants.
