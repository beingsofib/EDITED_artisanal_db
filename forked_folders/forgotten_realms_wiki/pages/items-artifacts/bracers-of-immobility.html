Bracers of immobility were cursed bracers that rendered the wearer slow and vulnerable to attacks.

Powers

When worn, these magic bracers increased the wearer's magical and mundane resistances while reducing their speed enough to render even the most capable fighter vulnerable in battle. Like other cursed items, bracers of immobility could only be removed via a remove curse spell.

Creation

A skilled crafter of wondrous items could create a pair of bracers of immobility with the use of bestow curse, mage armor, and resistance spells.

History

In 1368 DR, a band of adventurers infiltrated Lyssic's Crypt. One of them, a human male wizard wore a pair of bracers of immobility. The would-be graverobbers perished in the dangerous crypt and the magic bracers remained within the crypt until 1369 DR.
