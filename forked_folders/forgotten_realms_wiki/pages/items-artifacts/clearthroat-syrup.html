Clearthroat syrup was a type of common internal medicine used to relieve cold and similar seasonal ailments.

History

Circa 1348 DR, Maglor the Apothecary concocted medicines for the folk of the town of Eveningstar in Cormyr, including clearthroat syrup. One of his regular clients was Old Mother Naura who used clearthroat syrup to soothe the throats of her sick kids.
