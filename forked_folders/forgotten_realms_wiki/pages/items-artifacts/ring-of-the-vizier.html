The ring of the vizier was a magical item that could be found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Description

This magic item was available in a wide variety of ring types.

Powers

These rings were similar to message rings. When a command word was uttered, the face of the creator of the ring appeared in the center gem. The image of the face was capable of relaying a message that could last for one minute.

The creator of these rings could also store one medium-powered spell within the ring. This spell could be used 10 times before it expired.
