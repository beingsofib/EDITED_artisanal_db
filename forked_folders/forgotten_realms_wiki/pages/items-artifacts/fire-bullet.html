Fire bullets were an enchanted variety of sling bullets that were carved from the knucklebones of salamanders.

Powers

When launched, fire bullets whizzed through the air, igniting with flames of the Elemental Plane of Fire, dealing bludgeoning and searing damage to their targets.

History

In the mid-14 century DR, many enchanted sling bullets were a common sight along the Sword Coast, the Ten Towns, the Moonsea, and used by numerous individuals and factions. In the , Zhentarim-sponsored smugglers that served Gorgomorz, the Devil of Daggerford, supplied the fiend's army of orcs and tanarukka with arms, weapons, and other supplies, including enchanted fire bullets.
