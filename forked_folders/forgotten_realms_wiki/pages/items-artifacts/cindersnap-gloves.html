Cindersnap gloves were rare enchanted gloves imbued with protective magics, found in the Western Heartlands circa the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The wearer of these gloves could cast the protection from arrows spell once between short bouts of rest of at least four hours. This magic made the wearer significantly harder to hit with arrows, bolts, sling bullets, and other mundane missiles. However, if an opponent managed to hit the wearer, they became resistant to that damage.

History

In the , a pair of cindersnap gloves was in stock among the Harper quartermaster Talli's goods during her time accompanying a Harper hero Jaheira into the deadly Shadow-cursed Lands that surrounded the Moonrise Towers.
