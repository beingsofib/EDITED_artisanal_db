Eyes of charming were a set of magical eyes that allowed the wearer to charm others.

Description

Eyes of charming were a pair of crystal lenses (typically made of blue quartz) that fitted over the users' eyes. It was possible to wear only one of the eyes, instead of the pair, though this was far less effective.

Powers

These eyes stored three charges, and these charges would refresh at sunrise. When worn, the wearer could use a charge to charm another person that they could see, provided the other person was within . However, it was possible for the target to avoid being charmed.

History

In the 14 century DR, the lizardmen that inhabited the sixth level of the deep mines of New Verdigris had a set of eyes of charming amongst their stash of treasure.
