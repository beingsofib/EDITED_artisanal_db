Salve of remove paralysis

Item
nameSalve of Remove Paralysis
typeSalve
locationMyth Drannor, Cormanthor

Salve of remove paralysis was a common type of remedy used to remove the effects of paralysis.

Description

Salve of remove paralysis was usually kept in small ceramic jars. The salve itself was a clear goo.

Powers

When applied, salve of remove paralysis acted as a particularly powerful version of the spell of remove paralysis.

History

In 1369 DR, salves of remove paralysis were commonly used in the region of Cormanthor.
