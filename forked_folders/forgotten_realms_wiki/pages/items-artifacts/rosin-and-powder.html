Rosin and powder

Item

locationAurora's Emporium
value2e1 sp (Rosin cube)
5 cp (Powder vial)

Rosin and powder was a useful tool to help provide extra grip on the hands and fingertips.

Uses

This item was used commonly by thieves to aid in picking pockets.

Prices

A cube of rosin cost 1 silver pieces from Aurora's Emporium and a powder vial cost 5 copper pieces.
