The amulet of Alcedor was a unique piece of jewelry enchanted to allow the wearer to cast the hold person spell at will.

Description

The jewel of the amulet was a large sapphire carved into the shape of a fist. The sapphire hung on a chain of platinum links.

Powers

When activated by being touched or with a comnmand word, the amulet activated. The hand opened up, and shot a beam of blue light at its target, enveloping them in Alcedor's magic. The victims held by the magic of the amulet also fell under the influence of a vampiric touch spell, feeling their life force drain away to heal the wearer of the amulet if they so desired.

History

The amulet of Acledor was created by Alcedor Kolat and always worn by him.
