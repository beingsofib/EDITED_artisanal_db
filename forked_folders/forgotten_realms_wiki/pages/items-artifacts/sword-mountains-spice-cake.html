Sword Mountains spice cakes were unassuming handfoods, packed with head-clearing spices and drizzled with sweet glaze.

Powers

These bite-sized bundt-shaped cakes were cooked with such quantity of spices that they put pep in the imbiber's step and increased their initiative and reaction in critical situations.

History

In the mid-to-late-15 century DR, Sword Mountains spice cakes made their way from the villages of the Sword Mountains into the city of Neverwinter, becoming popular among its adventuring population. These invigorating handfoods were also served at the Beached Leviathan tavern of the city's Blacklake District.
