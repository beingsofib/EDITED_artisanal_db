Baldur's Gatecrasher was a variety of whiskey from underneath the city of Waterdeep – the great dungeon of Undermountain in the late 15 century DR.

History

Baldur's Gatecrasher whiskey could be found on offer at the Growling Groghouse, in a secret way-station deep in the infamous dungeon. Drams of this whiskey were stored in the tavern's cellar. The brewer named Woadhl died in a keg-related accident while looking for Baldur's Gatecrasher in the late 15 century DR.
