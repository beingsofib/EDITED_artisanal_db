The bracers of the battlerager were a set of magical bracers marked with the sign of the Eternal Brigade battleragers, found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The bracers gave exceptional protection against strikes that could critically injure the wearer.

History

The bracers may have been found when the guardnorn Phathroi sent the Elves of Cormanthor to strike a Cult of the Dragon base in the ruins of Myth Drannor, where the Red Wizards of Thay were attempting to subvert the dracolich Karliath. A group of undead Eternal Brigade dwarves lurked in a sealed chamber there, fighting whatever entered.
