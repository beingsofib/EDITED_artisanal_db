The Watersparkers were a unique set boots crafted by the electrifying arcane inventor Yrre the Sparkstruck.

Powers

Whenever the wearer of the Watersparkers stepped in a pool of water, that pool became electrified. As they stood on that surface, their body would then conduct electricity for a short time.

History

As of the , the boots remained stashed away in a chest within the Shattered Sanctum.
