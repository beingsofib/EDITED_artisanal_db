Bytopian Stout! was an exotic alcoholic libation from the plane of Bytopia.

History

As of the , Bytopian Stout! could be found among the so-called "Beers of the Planes" served at Imel's Happy Tongue, a casual eatery in the city of Athkatla in Amn. The "Beers of the Planes" were served along with the "Cheeses of the Planes", and the "Fruits of the Planes."
