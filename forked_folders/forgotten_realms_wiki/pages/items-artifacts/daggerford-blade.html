The Daggerford Blade was an enchanted halberd wielded by Nayeli Goldflower, a former member of the Guardians of Daggerford, in the late 15 century DR.

Description

This regal-looking magic weapon had a gilded head, decorated with inlaid purple gemstones.

Powers

The Daggerford Blade's enchantments enveloped the wielder and reflected portion of damage dealt to them back at the opponent.

Notable Owners

  • Nayeli Goldflower: a paladin from the city of Daggerford and an adventurer who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
