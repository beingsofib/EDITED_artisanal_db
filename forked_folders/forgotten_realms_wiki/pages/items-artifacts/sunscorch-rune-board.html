Sunscorch rune boards, or sunscorch gliders, were enchanted items used for recreational purposes during the height of blistering Thayan summers.

Description

A sunscorch glider was a large wooden rune board, ensorcelled to fly. Its surface shimmered like heat haze under a blazing summer sun.

Powers

Apart from a magical radiance and its flying capability, a sunscorch rune board allowed its rider to release a blast of damaging magic around the board. It also enhanced the rider's accuracy with both spells and ranged weapons.

History

In the late 15 century DR, Thayan sunscorch rune boards were seen being ridden by some of the adventurers from Neverwinter, who participated in the festivities celebrating the Sunite Summer Festivals.
