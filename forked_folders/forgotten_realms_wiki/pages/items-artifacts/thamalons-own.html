Thamalon's Own was a pear wine created by Thamalon Uskevren, of the noble Uskevren family of Selgaunt, Sembia, in the 14 century DR.

Description

Thamalon's Own was the color of straw.

History

By the mid-14 century DR, Thamalon's love for winemaking paid off. He created Storm Ruby, Usk Fine Old, and Thamalon's Own wines.
