Tethyrian tanagluth

Item
nameTethyrian Tanagluth
typeWine

Tethyrian tanagluth was a red spiced wine. It was a favorite of Vangerdahast Aeiulvana.

History

By the late 15 century DR, Tethyrian tanagluth was found in the city of Luskan on the Sword Coast North and carried by merchant caravans along the Trade Way.
