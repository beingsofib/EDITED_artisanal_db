The Amulet of the Devout was an enchanted amulet that once belonged to Otto of the Circle of Eight, from the world of Oerth.

Powers

The Amulet of the Devout increased the wearer's protection and resistance against spells and granted them an additinal opportunity to channel divinity.

History

Sometime before the late 15 century DR, the Amulet of the Devout belonged to the mage Otto from Oerth who had a certain fondness for magical jewelry. By some means, possibly left as an offering, the Amulet of the Devout ended up stored in the trapped and secured vault underneath the Stormshore Tabernacle, a non-denominational shrine in Baldur's Gate in the . If anyone was daring or foolish enough to rob the temple and steal Otto's amulet, they were castigated by the divinities of the Realms, sending devas and cambions to end their lives.
