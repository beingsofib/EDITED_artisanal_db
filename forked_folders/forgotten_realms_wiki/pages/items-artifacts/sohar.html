Sohars were a type of ship commonly used by Calishites to ship goods.

Description

On average, from bow to stern these ships measured in length, had a beam (width) of , and required a depth of at least in order to float.

Sohars sported three masts but the average number of crew one could find on a sohar would be around eight individuals. The bare minimum of individuals needed to sail a sohar were four.

History

Circa 1344 DR, a sohar being sailed by Calishite merchants sunk in the Sea of Fallen Stars, leaving a shipwreck.

By 1371 DR, sohars were becoming a more common sight in the Inner Sea.
