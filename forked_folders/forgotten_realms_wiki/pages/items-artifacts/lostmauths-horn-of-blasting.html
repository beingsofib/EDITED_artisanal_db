Lostmauth's horn of blasting was a unique artifact from the horde of the red dragon Lostmauth, who laired on the isle of Tuern, created sometime before the late 15 century DR.

Powers

Lostmauth's horn of blasting possessed all the usual powers of a horn of blasting in addition to giving some empowering effects to the user upon activating its destructive magics. Once used, it greatly but temporarily boosted the owner's power in battle and their ability to penetrate the enemy's armor. Another enchantment that differentiated it from other horns of blasting was the fact that Lostmauth's horn of blasting was a part of a three-item set. Once all three artifacts were wielded by one individual, they added the additional enchantment that allowed the wilder to land an extra attack when they managed to land a lucky, successful, and devastating attack. Other items in the set were: Lostmauth's hoard necklace and the golden belt of puissance.

History

In the 1480s DR, the Cult of the Dragon helmed a skirmish on the shores of the isle of Tuern, standing against the defenders, heroes, and adventurers of Neverwinter and the Lords' Alliance forces. The battle eventually reached the Lair of Lostmauth and the dragon's defeat. Lostmauth's horn of blasting was wielded by the red wyrm's minions, allowing them to summon their master to battle. When the dragon fell, the horn was recovered from the battlefield.
