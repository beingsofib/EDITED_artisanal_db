Kiam Goda's kilat drum was a unique drum that once belonged to Kiam Goda of Alpuk in the Great Glacier sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.

History

Kiam Goda was an Iulutiun musician who was famous for his finger-thumb tarradiddles playing his sealskin hand drum during the koatulit peace festivals. At some point, the drum left Alpuk and by the this unique but mundane instrument was sold at the Chromatic Scale musical instrument shop located on the second floor of The Glitter Gala in Baldur's Gate. Kiam Goda's kilat drum was among several unique instruments in Thomas C. Quirkilious's inventory.
