Türkhaari

Item
nameTürkhaari
typePickle
locationTymanther

Türkhaari was a traditional Thymari pickle dish, made from gourds, pepper, charchuka, lemon, and herbs and spices, and talsh, a resin from the nychaki herb.

Locations

The türkhaari was a traditional delicacy of Tymanther.
