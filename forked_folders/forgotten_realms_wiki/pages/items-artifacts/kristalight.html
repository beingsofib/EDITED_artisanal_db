Kristalight was an enchanted piece of crystal created sometime before the late 14 century DR.

Description

Kristalight was a mostly clear -long and -wide chunk of an elongated hexagonal crystal, seeming of natural origin.

It was a fragile item. A fall from a height of was sure to shatter Kristalight, and it could not be fixed apart from via a powerful casting of the mend spell.

Powers

When the item's command words, "Orthala, Orthala, magic crystal, give light," were uttered, the item began emitting strong strobing light in the area of in diameter. To stop, the user had to say "Orthala, Orthala, make the light cease."

History

Kristalight was one of the more unique items available from the Misti's Moonlight Pawnshop in the Living City, Raven's Bluff, run by Misti Morgan, a proprietor of the establishment and a thief, in the late 14 century DR.
