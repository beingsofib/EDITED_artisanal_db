A scroll of protection from elementals was an enchanted scroll that offered protection from some or all types of elementals.

Powers

After reading this scroll a sphere of protection was centered on the reader. The sphere protected anyone inside from one or all forms of elementals. Elementals in this case included aerial servants, djinn, invisible stalkers, and wind walkers from the air element, xorn from earth, efreeti, and salamanders from fire, and tritons, and water weirds from water. The sphere lasted between 1 and 4 minutes.
