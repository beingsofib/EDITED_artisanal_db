Rose oil

Item
nameRose Oil
typeOil

Rose oil was a variety of fragrant oil in the realms that was often worn by women.

Notable Users

  • Aliisza, an alu-fiend.
  • Halina Sverdej.
  • Nyla, a senator of Innarlith.
  • Thazienne Uskevren, a Sembian thief.

Trivia

Sakinas, air spirits that subsisted on scents, found the scent of rose oil to be quite delectable.
