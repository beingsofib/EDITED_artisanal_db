Slitherstill

Item
nameSlitherstill
typeDrug

Slitherstill was a concoction which helped kill wakefulness.

Powers

Slitherstill was a fast-acting sleeping drug, oft-favored by insomniacs and kidnappers alike. Too much, however, could prove lethal.

Notable Makers

While by no means the only source, even locally, Maelaeve Belvrost was known to trade in it.
