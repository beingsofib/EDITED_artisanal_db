A symbol of Tyr was an enchanted defensive bracelet of the Tyrran faith in the late 15 century DR.

Description

A symbol of Tyr was a metallic piece of jewelry, decorated with red gems and a disk, decorated with Tyr's holy symbol, proudly announcing the wearer's devotion to the God of Justice.

Powers

The enchantments placed on these bracelets magically reflected partial damage dealt to the wearer back onto the attacker.

Notable Owners

  • Nayeli Goldflower: a paladin from the city of Daggerford and an adventurer who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
