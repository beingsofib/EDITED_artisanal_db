Swords of flame were swords that were lit aflame with enchantments, especially useful a gainst trolls and similar creatures venerable to fire.

Powers

Swords of flame were made out of enchanted silver and possessed a +1 magics that made the blade deadlier and guided the wielder's hand towards the target. The words "Meta Infernum" were engraved into their hilt and their blades were burning with magical flames.

Variants

  • Balor's sword of flame was a powerful vorpal Bastard sword of flame typically wielded by the balors of the Abyss.
  • Scimitar of fire was a brass two-handed efreeti scimitar from the Elemental Plane of Fire.

Notable Owners

  • Bernard of the Copper Coronet in Athkatla had a longsword of flame available to well-paying guests in the late 14 century DR.
  • Ribald Barterman of the Adventurer Mart in Waukeen's Promenade carried a longsword of flame among his goods available for sale in the late 14 century DR.
