A sabiki was a Kozakuran fishing device.

Description

A sabiki rig consisted of a set of multiple hooked lines with a single lure and sinker. It was developed by Kozakuran fishermen for catching oceanic baitfish.

History

Originating in Kozakura, the sabiki eventually found its way to northwest Faerûn by the 15 century DR, where it proved especially adept at snaring candlefish and clumps of marimo. Fishing contest organizers found its use unfair, given the amount of hooks involved.
