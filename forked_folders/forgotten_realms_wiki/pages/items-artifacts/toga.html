Togas were a type of light, wraparound outerwear garment.

Description

Togas were woven from a variety of materials, including linen, wool, velvet, and silk.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold both linen and wool togas, as well as ones that were custom-made with concealed pockets for hiding things in, such as stilettos. These were all been imported from the Old Empires region and steadily became extremely popular among inhabitants of the Western Heartlands.

Notable Togas

  • Toga of Zagyg

Notable Users of Togas

Classes

  • Chessentan warriors often preferred to wear a short toga in place of armor, as doing so was less hot and allowed them a greater freedom of movement.

Nations, Regions, and Settlements

  • People in the nations known collectively as the Old Empires—Chessenta, Mulhorand, and Unther—were sometimes known to wear togas.
  • Togas were a common article of clothing among inhabitants of the Malatran Plateau. Among the native Nubari tribe called the Rudra, white togas were reserved for the most prestigious of people. All other members of the tribe could only wear togas if they were of a red or brown color.
  • Throughout the city-states of the Vilhon Reach, it was a common custom for priests, regardless of their faith, to wear togas that were dyed blue. Similarly, wizards in this region were known to often wear togas that had been dyed red.

Races & Sentient Creatures

  • Asuras typically wore loose togas.
  • Togas were a common article of clothing among avariel.

Religions

  • Male priests of Sehanine Moonbow often wore silvery-white togas.
  • Some of the Greater Titans dressed in togas.
