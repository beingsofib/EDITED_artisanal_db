nameBeehive

Beehives were enclosed, artificial structures designed for honey-producing bees to live in.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold beehives shaped like large eggs, with interior sections of nesting eggs circumscribed around their center. This design allowed the tops of their beehives to be removed quickly.
  • In Zhentil Keep, the store Lita's Equipment sold similar beehives to those of Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 12 gold pieces.
