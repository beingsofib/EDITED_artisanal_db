Ring of jumping

Item
nameRing of Jumping
typeMagical ring
value3e2,500 gp

Rings of jumping were magical rings worn by many adventurers across Faerûn.

Description

The ring allowed the wearer to continually jump about, making movement easier, and to access hidden areas.

Notable Owners

  • Derryth Bonecloak
  • Zhai
  • Hm-boye
  • Sherlen Spearslayer
  • Zar-Dandan
  • Sarissa Duncastle
