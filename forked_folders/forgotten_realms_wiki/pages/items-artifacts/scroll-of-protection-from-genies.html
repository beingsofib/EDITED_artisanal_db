A scroll of protection from genies was an enchanted scroll that warded against genie attacks.

Powers

This scroll protected the reader against magical and physical attacks from one or more different kinds of genies, preventing them from directly harming the reader and even from magic that involved an area of effect. The scroll also protected the reader's carried equipment. Different variations of these scrolls existed that offered protection against dao, efreeti, marids, djinn, and tasked genies. Rare versions of these scrolls protected against all geniekind.

Notable Owners

Marwan al-Jabal al-Hiyali, the Grandfather of the Everlasting in Zakhara, carried a scroll of protection from genies.
