Pickaxe of piercing

Item
namePickaxe of Piercing
typePick

A pickaxe of piercing was a rare, enchanted adamantine pickaxe.

Powers

Any creature struck by a pickaxe of piercing would suffer a temporary, minor loss in their magic resistance. This effect was cumulative.

A pickaxe of piercing was also effective in shattering dragonscales and piercing through all manner of magical barriers, including walls, shells, spheres and mantles.
