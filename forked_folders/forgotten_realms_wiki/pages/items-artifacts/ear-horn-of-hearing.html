Ear horn of hearing

Item
nameEar Horn of Hearing
typeWondrous item
rarity5eCommon

An ear horn of hearing was a magical ear horn that temporarily restored hearing to deafened creatures.

Powers

A creature that was magically, temporarily, or permanently deaf needed only to hold up the ear horn of hearing against the ear in order to hear normally.
