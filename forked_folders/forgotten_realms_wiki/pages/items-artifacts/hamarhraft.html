Hamarhraft was an enchanted maul of dwarven origin that had been imbued with the power of thunder.

Description

The great hammer had an inscription, written in dwarvish, that alluded to the importance of making a strong first impression.

Powers

Whenever the wielders of Hamarhraft leapt or jumped onto the group, their landing was followed by a thunderous wave that extended out .

History

As of the , Hamarhraft could be found within a locked chest in one of the upper floors of the Waukeen's Rest inn. It was not entirely clear who remained in possession of the hammer.
