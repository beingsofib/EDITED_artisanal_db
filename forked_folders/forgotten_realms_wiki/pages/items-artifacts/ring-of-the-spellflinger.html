Rings of the spellflinger were a variety of enchanted rings from the Chultan city of Port Nyanzaru in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

Rings of the spellflinger were created with enchantments of various power; however, all of them boosted the wearer's ability to deal well-aimed, devastating magical attacks and boosted their arcane recoveries. Lastly, these rings increased the strength of offensive spells and magical abilities when unleashed from at least away from the target.

History

In the late 15 century DR, as the death curse ravaged Faerûn, the famed author Volothamp Geddarm found himself in the Hunter's Square in Port Nyanzaru, a city in Chult. As he was working on Volo's Guide to Monsters, Volo enlisted the aid of adventurers from the North to hunt down and slay a collection of powerful beasts of legend and report to him with their experiences and loot. A number of these rings were offered as rewards for various marks, such as the Tyrant, Kutlass, and Erjellel.
