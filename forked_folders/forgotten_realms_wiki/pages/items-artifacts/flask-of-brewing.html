Flask of brewing

Item
nameFlask of Brewing
typeWondrous item
originNeverwinter, Sword Coast North

The flask of brewing was an artifact receptacle that belonged to the Acquisitions Incorporated in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

This little metallic flask was engraved with the Acquisitions Incorporated logo. It possessed the magical ability to fill up with any brew that its owner desired or needed to survive. It could be a healing concoction or a highly flammable liquid that could be used in battle.

The members of Acquisitions Incorporated could use the flask of brewing alongside the harness of safety and the badge of identification together to boost all the items' magics and receive physical empowerment in battle, as well as a movement speed increase.
