Ring of disease immunity

Item
nameRing of disease immunity
typeMagical ring
locationFaerûn
value3e8000 gp

A ring of disease immunity protected the wearer against all kinds of disease, including the contagion spell, lycanthropy and mummy rot. As well as curing diseases, it also aided in standard healing. It was worth around 8000 gold pieces.
