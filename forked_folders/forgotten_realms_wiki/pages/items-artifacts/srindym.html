Srindym

Srindym was a poison.

Use

It could be added to moonwine and almost certainly to other drinks, and was deadly to drink.

History

The elf Laurlaethee Shaurlanglar attempted to murder Elminster Aumar with srindym on , in Myth Drannor. It didn't work.
