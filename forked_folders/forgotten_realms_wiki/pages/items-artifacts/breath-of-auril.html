Breath of Auril was a unique enchanted and cursed dagger owned by the Auril worshiper Kontik in the late-13 century DR.

Powers

Whoever wielded the Breath of Auril became slightly more intelligent, though less charismatic. They also became nearly invulnerable to damage brought on by cold, but were more vulnerable to harm by fire. Finally, whoever wielded this weapon was capable of casting the spell cone of cold twice per day.

This weapon also had a +3 enchantment.
