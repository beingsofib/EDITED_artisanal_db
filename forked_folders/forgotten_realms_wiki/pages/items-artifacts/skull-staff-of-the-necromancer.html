Skull Staff of the Necromancer

Item
nameSkull Staff of the Necromancer
typemajor artifact

The Skull Staff of the Necromancer was Velsharoon's weapon of choice.

Powers

This legendary artifact had all the powers of a staff of the magi, a greater staff of skulls, and a ring of shooting stars.

History

The staff was crafted by the greatest archmage of the Imaskari.

Notable Owners

  • Velsharoon
