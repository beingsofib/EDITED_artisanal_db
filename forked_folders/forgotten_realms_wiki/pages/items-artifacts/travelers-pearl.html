Traveler's pearl

Item
nameTraveler's pearl
typePearl
locationElemental Plane of Water

Traveler's pearls were magic items created by marid and given to mortals.

Description

This item appeared as a large pearl with rosy coloration. It gave of a slight hint of alteration magic.

Powers

If given as a gift, the previous owner of the traveler's pearl could speak a command word and teleport themselves and a companion to the pearl's current location, often resulting in the old owner being transported directly into the new owner's treasury. They pearl was destroyed once the previous owner uttered the command word.
