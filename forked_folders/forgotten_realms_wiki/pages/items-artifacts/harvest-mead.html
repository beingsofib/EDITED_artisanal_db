Harvest Mead, later known as Harbor Mead or Dragon Mead, was a traditional alcoholic beverage from the village of West Harbor on the edge of the Mere of Dead Men, served cold annually during the Harvest Festival.

History

In 1374 DR, Lazlo Buckman served his famous Harvest Mead during that year's Harvest Festival, notably to the Kalach-Cha, before the village was attacked under cover of night by duergars and bladelings searching for shards of the Silver Sword of Gith.
