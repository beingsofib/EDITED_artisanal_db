A do-maru was a type of armor used in Kara-Tur.

Description

A do-maru was a corselet, which meant it covered both the front and back of the body. Made of laced metal plates, a do-maru protected the back, chest, shoulders and stomach of an individual. Decorative tassels hung from the lower part of a do-maru. It was often worn with a haidate.
