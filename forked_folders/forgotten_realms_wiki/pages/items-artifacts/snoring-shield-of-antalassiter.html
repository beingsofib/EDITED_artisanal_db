The Snoring Shield of Antalassiter was a magical device, extant by the mid–8 century DR.

Powers

At the very least, none of its powers were activated by bouncing a cork from it.

History

The enigmatic device formed part of the collection that remained in Moonshorn Tower by the year . Much of the collection was Netherese, but many things in it were insignificant.
