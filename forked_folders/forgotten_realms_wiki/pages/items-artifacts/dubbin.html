Dubbin

Substance
nameDubbin
typePaste
propertiesWaxy

Dubbin was a type of waxy paste found in the Realms, particularly the North.

Usages

  • Dubbin was applied to leather products in order to condition and waterproof them.
  • Some utilized dubbin in the restoration of old leather relics.
