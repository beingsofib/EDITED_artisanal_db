Law's banner

Item
nameLaw's Banner
typeMagical item
value2e25,000 gp

A law's banner was a magical standard that raised the morale of troops when seen by a mostly lawful army.

Description

It was a standard of blazing red.

Powers

Once raised, once a day, any soldier in the lawful army who was within a quarter mile (0.4 kilometers) of the banner and could see it would reap its benefits, with bolstered morale. If the standard fell for any reason, its magical effect would be lost almost immediately unless picked up by another soldier. Otherwise, they would suffer great dread, believing their cause to be lost.
