The cloak of the yeti was an enchanted cloak created by Graendel Granitefist of Graendel's Fine Dwarven Craft in Targos in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The cloak of the yeti was created from a magic cloak of protection and a abominable yeti's hide. The cloak granted extremely high resistance to cold. Additionally, it boosted the owner's strength and their skills at hiding.

History

Graendel Granitefist, in his old age, sought to create a collection of masterwork weapons and armor that would immortalize his dwarven craft. One of these items, created in the , was a cloak of the yeti.
