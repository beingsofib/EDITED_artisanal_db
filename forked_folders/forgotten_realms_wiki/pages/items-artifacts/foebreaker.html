Foebreaker was a unique enchanted maul from the city of Baldur's Gate.

Powers

Foebreaker possessed a strong +2 enchantment that guided the wielder's hands towards the target and made the maul's attacks more powerful and deadly. Its enchantments also made Foebreaker into a truly lethal weapon what dealt normal damage even to creatures and objects that otherwise would be immune or resistant to bludgeons.

History

In the , Foebreaker was locked safely away deep within magically sealed, trapped, and protected vaults of Ramazith's Tower. The tower's owner, Lorroakan, kept the weapon locked inside the "Karsus" vault.
