The Celebrant's Blade was a beautifully crafted dwarven battle axe that originated from the stronghold of Dorn's Deep.

Description

The Celebrant's Blade was so finely made that it was set within a monument in the forge of Dorn's Deep.

Powers

The axe held a powerful +4 enchantment.

History

The Celebrant's Blade was forged some time during or before the 13 century DR, by the master smith Briath Silverhand. It was made to honor Briath's patron deity, the dwarven god Moradin.
