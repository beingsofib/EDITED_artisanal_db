Queen Amlaruil's Chariot was a wondrous unique magic item from the island of Evermeet.

Description

The chariot was usually pulled by a pair of unicorns or pegasi.

Powers

The chariot protected any riders by making it much more difficult for incoming attacks to strike them. Additionally, the magic of the chariot increased the likeliness of a rider's ranged attacks striking true and causing more physical harm. The chariot and any riders could teleport without error once per day.

History

Queen Amlaruil Moonflower used this chariot for her personal transportation.
