A beer, or a brew, was a type of alcoholic beverage.

Description

Beer was a very popular beverage that was consumed all over Faerûn., though lager was much rarer than ale. Whilst ale was both flavorful and colorful, lager was light in both weight and color, and was often bland.

Uses

Beer was often consumed for enjoyment, as well as its intoxicative effects. It could be bought at many establishments, where it could either be consumed within, or purchased to take away. In pubs, beer was usually sold in mugs. In other places, such as shops, beer could be found in bottles,

Trivia

  • "Gulletfire" was a slang term in Common for beer or wine of a very bad or poor quality.
