Stalwart shields were enchanted shield created by the best smiths of the House of Gems in the old Myth Drannor.

Description

Stalwart shields usually were simple-looking but decorated curved bronze buckler shields of dwarven make.

Powers

Stalwart shields offered +2 enchantment to the user's defenses as well as improved their fortitude.

History

In 1369 DR, one of the stalwart shields could be found dangling off the arm of a shambling ghast, one of the many warriors who died in the Weeping War but returned to haunt the ruins of the City of Song. This ghast was still living in the dwarven forge it used in life, outside of the once grand keep of the House of Gems.
