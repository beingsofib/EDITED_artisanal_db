The Legacy of the Oni was a unique enchanted katana of Kara-Turan origins.

Powers

This exotic sword was imbued with powerful magics. It's slashing edge was enchanted with a +5 enchantment. Additionally, the Legacy of the Oni granted the wielder magical resistance to slashing and piercing damage and gave them the benefits of rite freedom spell.

History

The Legacy of the Oni originated from the land of Kara-Tur where it was likely crafted by an ambitious oni mage who had certain plans on conquering the lands to the west. However, the history of the katana remained mostly unknown.

Circa 1373 DR, the Legacy of the Oni was in possession of one of the head priestess of the House of the Moon of Thentia, Moonsea.
