The symbol of earth was a powerful artifact imbued with the power of an earth node by the Cult of the Black Earth in the late 15 century DR.

Description

The symbol of earth was a small rock with a carved rune that glowed with magical energy, linking it to the Elemental Plane of Earth.

Powers

This artifact gave its owned the boon of enhanced vitality and greatly boosted their ability to penetrate enemy armor with their attacks. Additionally, it gave them a slight resistance to being controlled, charmed, or dominated by magical means. Lastly, once per a certain period of time, the symbol of earth could be activated to summon forth an earthmote that would crash into the ground, dealing devastating damage and pushing back all creatures caught in the crash zone.

History

This artifact was created sometime in or before the , and was used during the struggle against the cult of the Elder Elemental Eye.
