Swashbuckler's boots

Item
nameSwashbuckler's Boots
typeBoots
locationSword Coast, Sea of Swords

Swashbuckler's Boots were a type of footwear created specifically to complement buccaneers' footwork.

Description

These boots were incredibly comfortable. They augmented the user's dodging ability during their swashbuckling adventures and duels.

History

After the Wailing Death of 1372 DR these boots could be found in the places along the Sword Coast and islands of the Sea of Swords frequented by pirates and sailors.
