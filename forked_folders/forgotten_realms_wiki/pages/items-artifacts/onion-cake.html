Onion cake

Item
nameOnion Cake
typeBread
locationHeartlands, Vilhon Reach, Sword Coast North

Onion cakes were a traditional type of handfood prepared in the Heartlands, the Vilhon Reach, and the Sword Coast North in celebration of Greengrass.

Description

Onion cakes were thick cornmeal fritters prepared with copious finely diced and fried onions. This was a popular food and a staple for the annual Greengrass celebrations.
