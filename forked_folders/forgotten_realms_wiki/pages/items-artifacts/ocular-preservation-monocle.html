Ocular preservation monocles were rare enchanted monocles created using preserved monstrous eyes, first known to have appeared in the city of Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Ocular preservation monocles resembled regular optic aids, but instead of a lens, the frame held the eye of a monster, red in color, like freshly spilled blood. The preserved monstrous eye conferred its vision to the wearer.

History

In the late 15 century DR, several ocular preservation monocles were gifts of Dagult Neverember, Open Lord of Waterdeep and Protector of the City of Neverwinter, to a set of heroes, who aided in the defense of the city against many dangers that plagued the region during that time period. The gifts were announced via a postal courier and given out at Protector's Enclave between the Grand Emporium and the Tarmalune Auction House.
