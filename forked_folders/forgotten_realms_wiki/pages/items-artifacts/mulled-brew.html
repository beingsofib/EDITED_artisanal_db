Mulled brew

nameMulled Brew

locationPrimarily north and northwest Faerûn

Mulled brew was an alcoholic beverage made from beer or ale and served hot. It was popular in many inns and taverns across Faerûn, especially in areas with colder climates.

Recipe

There were many variations of this drink, but a typical recipe started with a strong-flavored beer or ale. Moon Mountain Dark was a favored base for humans in the Savage Frontier, circa the .

  • a small keg of beer or ale
  • a handful of sugar
  • two pinches of nutmeg powder
  • some whole cloves
  • some blackroot
  • a handful of ginger root powder
  • a slice of apple or lime
  • a splash of rum

All ingredients except the rum were heated in a pot to the verge of boiling. When the brew was ready, the rum was added and pot was removed from the heat, stirred, and allowed to cool for a short time (one or two verses of a drinking song was about right).
