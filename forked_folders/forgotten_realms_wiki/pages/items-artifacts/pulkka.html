Pulkka

Item
namePulkka
typeSled
locationHartsvale

A pulkka was a kind of sled pulled by six reindeer.

During coming-of-age ceremonies and other occasions in the kingdom of Hartsvale, it was a sign of honor to be carried behind a hunting party in a specially decorated pulkka.
