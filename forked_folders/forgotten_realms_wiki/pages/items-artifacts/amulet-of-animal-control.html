Amulet of animal control

nameAmulet of Animal Control
typeAmulet
value2e5,000 gp

An amulet of animal control was an enchanted amulet that granted the wearer control over animals.

Powers

These amulets granted the wearer complete control over a single giant animal or up to six smaller ones at a time. The amulet had no effect upon magical beasts or intelligent animals. The wearer's control over the animals lasted as long as they maintained concentration, once the control was broken the animals were free to react or behave as they wished.

Notable Owners

Janci al-Kriman, a camel trader in Huzuz, wore an amulet of animal control.
