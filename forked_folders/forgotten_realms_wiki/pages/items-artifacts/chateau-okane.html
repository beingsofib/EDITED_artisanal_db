Chateau O'Kane was a variety of wine in the Vast in the late-14 century DR that was produced by Charles Oliver O'Kane.

History

Sometime in the early 1370's DR, Charles personally gifted a bottle of this wine to Micah Starfire as thanks for helping him track down some pirates.
