Salarn

Item
nameSalarn
typePoison

Salarn was a sleep-inducing venom.

Effects

Salarn was a very persistent poison, which did not require fresh application on a weapon to remain effective at time of use. It caused its targets to fall unconscious.

History

Salarn was illegal in Silverymoon by . On that year, the merchant Oscalar Maerbree was found out to be using it on his weapons by Alustriel Silverhand along the course of her inquiries into the death of Garthin Muirtree; she advised him discard it by noon on the next day.
