Steel Rain

Item
nameSteel Rain
typeWeapon
locationThe Haunted Lands

Steel Rain was an enchanted longbow wielded by Sheikh Ardasir al-Darah of the Jann of the Haunted Lands.

Powers

The longbow helped Ardasir's sheaf arrows fly true and caused them to inflict more physical harm. Steel Rain was truly a lethal weapon When also combined with Ardasir's great strength.
