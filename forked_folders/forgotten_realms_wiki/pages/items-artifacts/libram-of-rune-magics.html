The Libram of Rune Magics was a rune magic spellbook of the College of Rune Magics.

Powers

This book contained all the rune magic spells and rituals that were taught by the College of Rune Magics, ritual of healing, and ritual of warding with runesticks.

History

In 1358 DR, a group of adventurers from Suzail were seeking out the scattered remains of Awtawmatawn on behalf of Amelior Amanitas. As part of this quest they investigated the ruins of the College of Rune Magics and came across this book within a stone box that had been wizard locked. They found it to be exceedingly valuable, though very few people would actually be willing to pay them for it.
