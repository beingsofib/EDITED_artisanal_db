Lemonade was a variety of drink consumed in several locations in the Realms, created using sugar and lemon juice.

Availability

Lemonade was pale yellow in color and had a strong citrus taste.

History

A unique variety of the drink – Stormreach lemonade, from the city of Stormreach on the world of Eberron, was introduced to Toril and the village of Eveningstar in the 15 century DR, when adventurers from Eberron, in including the warforged adventurers, set out to investigate the happenings in the infamous Haunted Halls.
