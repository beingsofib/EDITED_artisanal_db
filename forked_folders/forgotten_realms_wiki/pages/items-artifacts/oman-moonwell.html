The Oman Moonwell was the magical pool of water found on Oman's Isle within the the Moonshae Isles.

Description

The Moonwell was located within the Old Stone Wood forest near the cairn of Thelgaar Ironhand on the western side of the island.

History

During the late 15 century DR, an orc shaman named Crughth planned to corrupt the moonwell so he could use it as a portal to the other Moonwells scattered throughout the Moonshae Isles, allowing the wicked giants who ruled the island to move their minions about for raids.
