A belt of stone giant strength, also known as a girdle of stone giant strength,

Description

These belts were rather plain-looking in appearance They were considered amongst the less powerful variations of belts of giant strength.

Powers

The belt or girdle would imbue the wearer with the strength, though neither size nor girth, of a stone giant.
