Wine of Eternity

Item
nameWine of Eternity
typeWondrous item
locationToril

Wine of Eternity was a very rare enchanted chalice that possessed incapacitating magics.

Effects

The chalice's magics, or the effects created by the liquid within, could render a single powerful foe incapacitated. The sleeping ensorcellment lasted indefinitely until the affected creature could break free from the magics.
