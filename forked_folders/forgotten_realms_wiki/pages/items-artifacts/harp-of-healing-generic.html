Harp of healing (generic)

Item
nameHarp of Healing
typeMagical Harp

A harp of healing was a variety of harp imbued with restorative magic.

Powers

This harp would gradually heal any wounded creature within of it when played. This could be done for eight consecutive hours before a bard began to seriously risk misplaying it.

If the playing of the harp ceased for whatever reason, it would not continue healing affected creatures until they became wounded again.

Notable Owners

  • Brond Carlow, a bard in Ravens Bluff, owned one of these harps.
