Amberthroat

Item
nameAmberthroat
typeWine
locationTashluta, Tashalar

Amberthroat was an expensive alcoholic beverage originating in the city of Tashluta in Tashalar in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Availability

It was sold in the Dragon's Head Tavern in Waterdeep for 9 gp for a glass.
