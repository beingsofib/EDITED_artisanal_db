Earthcaller was a heavy crystalline rod wielded by the avatar of Segojan Earthcaller.

Description

This rod, which was effectively a heavy mace, was made of crystalline quartz.

Powers

Earthcaller had all the powers of a rod of smiting and had a pair of unique abilities. The heavy mace was able to create a powerful earthquake when it was struck against a surface, but only at the whim of the Lord of the Burrow. Its most unique ability was to summon a stone golem when pressed against a rock of at least . The stone golem would protect and serve Segojan Earthcaller for all of the day. These two powers could only be activated once per day, but seemed to have unlimited charges.
