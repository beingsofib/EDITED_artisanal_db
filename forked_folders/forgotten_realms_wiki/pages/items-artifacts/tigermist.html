Tigermist was a landscape painting created in or before the late 15 century DR.

Description

This serene landscape depicted a mist filling a valley in between improbable peaks. This fog was known as "tigermist" in Faerûnian folklore for its uncanny padding quality.

History

In the , copies of the Tigermist painting were found in certain homes and establishments of the city of Baldur's Gate, the Western Heartlands, and within the ruined apothecary shop in the blighted village of Moonhaven.
