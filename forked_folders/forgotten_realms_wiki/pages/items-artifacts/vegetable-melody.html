Vegetable Melody was a vegetable entrée dish served at the upscale Golden Goblet Inn of Mossbridges in the Vast.

History

In the mid-to-late 14 century DR, Vegetable Melody was present on Golden Goblet's menu amongst its main dishes, priced at five silver pieces. It was the most expensive item on the menu with stuffed devil crab sharing the five silvers price.
