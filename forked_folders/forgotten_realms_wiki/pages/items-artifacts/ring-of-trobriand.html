A ring of Trobriand was a magic ring that allowed the wearer to communicate with scaladar.

Powers

While wearing a ring of Trobriand the wearer was able to issue mental commands to scaladar. The magic of these rings only worked upon scaladar that were associated with the ring during its creation.
