Rope ladders were a type of ladder made largely of rope, with wooden slats between.

Description

The average rope ladder was made from hemp rope, with wooden slats measuring wide.

Notable Users

  • Rangers sometimes carried rope ladders throughout the wilds of Faerûn. Though most were quite fleet-footed and dexterous enough to climb trees without the need of such aid, they were sometimes needed to provide aid to clumsy companions.
