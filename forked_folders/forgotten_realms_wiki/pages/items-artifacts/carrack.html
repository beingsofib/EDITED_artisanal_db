Carrack

Ship
nameCarrack
typeSailing ship

A carrack was a large, multi-masted sailing ship used in Amn and elsewhere on the Sword Coast. It could carry many soldiers and was capable of deep-sea voyages. Carracks had both fore and aft castles on the main deck. Cordell had several carracks in his fleet on his Maztican expedition. A carrack sailed the waters of the Whamite Isles, at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the .
