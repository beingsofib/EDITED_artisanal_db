Phalar Aluve was an enchanted longsword associated with the faith of Eilistraee.

Description

Phalar Aluve was inscribed with a pattern that glowed gold when the sword was drawn. Part of the inscription featured a single line written in the drow language. The sword hummed constantly, eager to be made to sing or shriek.

Powers

It possessed a +1 enchantment and magically enhanced the owner's artistic performances. Wielders could command the sword to either shriek or sing, but only for a short time and only once per while. When it shrieked, the blade damaged foes and weakened both their offense and defense. When it sang, it empowered the wielder’s allies, bolstering their offensive and defensive capabilities.

History

As of the , the sword remained embedded in a stone outcropping in the Underdark, near Ebonlake Grotto.
