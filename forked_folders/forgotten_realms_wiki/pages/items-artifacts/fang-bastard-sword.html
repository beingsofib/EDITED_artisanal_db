Fang (bastard sword)

Item
nameFang
typeBastard sword
locationTargos, Icewind Dale

Fang was a unique bastard sword imbued with corrosive magics, crated sometime before the late 14 century DR.

Powers

Fang was enchanted to produce never-ending acidic venom. This substance was not poisonous but burned anything it touched.

History

In the , Fang was left within one of the guest rooms of the Luskan Arms inn in the town of Targos, in the far-northern region of Icewind Dale.
