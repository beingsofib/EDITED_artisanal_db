Squid Shield

Item
nameSquid Shield
typeMagic shield

Squid Shield was a magical shield that provided the wearer with protection.

Description

This magical shield had a +2 enchantment.

History

In 1358 DR, the Heroes of Ascore rescued the Waterbaron, who was held captive in Triboar. They also destroyed a Kraken Society base in Yartar, and were rewarded with Squid Shield by the Waterbaron for their efforts.

Notable Owners

  • Waterbaron of Yartar
  • Heroes of Ascore
