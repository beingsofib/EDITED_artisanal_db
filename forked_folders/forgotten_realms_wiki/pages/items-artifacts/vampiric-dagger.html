Vampiric daggers were enchanted daggers that drew strength from its victims, created sometime before the late 14 century DR.

Powers

Vampiric daggers had blades of deadly sharpness and tiny serrations. They possessed a powerful +2 enchantment that made the weapon's attacks deadlier and guided the wielder's hand toward their aim. Each successful strike with a vampiric dagger sapped the strength from the target, granting the wielder vampiric ''regeneration.

History

Sometime after the , a vampiric dagger was handled by the leader of the three remnants of the Shadow Cult that followed the King of Shadows. The remnants of the cult holed up in a ruined fort in the Mere of Dead Men, trying to resurrect the King. The priests used a vampiric dagger against a band of adventurers from Crossroad Keep, sent by Khelgar Ironfist to end the cult once and for all.

Notable Users

  • Artemis Entreri, a famed assassin of calimport.
  • Tharchion Aznar Thrul never parted with his vampiric dagger in the 14 century DR.
  • Renho Thane, a transmuter apprentice of Szass Tam's in 1368 DR.
