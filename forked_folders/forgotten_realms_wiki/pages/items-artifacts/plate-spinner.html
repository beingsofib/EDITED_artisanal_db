Plate spinners were tools in the Realms popular among bards and performers who used them to dazzle crowds with balancing tricks.

Description

A plate spinner consisted of a peg and special wooden plates that would not break.

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 3 copper pieces per peg, 2 silver piece per plate, and 1 silver piece for a stand that could hold up to four spinning plates.

Notable Users

  • Plate spinners were favored by bards.
