Flight arrow

Flight arrows were arrows that, as the name implied, were designed with range in mind. They were often used when hunting.

Description

Flight arrows were lighter compared to "common" arrows. They were usually made from birch or ash trees and measured between and in length.

Notable Owners

  • Catti-brie's Quiver of Anariel contained twenty alchemical silver flight arrows.
