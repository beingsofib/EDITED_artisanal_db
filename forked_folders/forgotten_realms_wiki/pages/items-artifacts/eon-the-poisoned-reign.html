Eon, the Poisoned Reign was a powerful longbow that was tied to both dragons and the Feywild.

Powers

Eon was linked to a green dracolich, and was suitably powerful. It shot arrows rapidly and with devastating force, and each arrow was imbued with deadly poisons.

History

The bow was tied to a dracolich who was promised a long and fruitful reign of terror that never happened. The bow came to interact with the Feywild at some point and was lost in time as well, before eventually reappearing in the hands of mortals.

It was tied in some fashion to Aeon, the Blazing Reign, a similar weapon bound to the dracolich Karliath in Myth Drannor.
