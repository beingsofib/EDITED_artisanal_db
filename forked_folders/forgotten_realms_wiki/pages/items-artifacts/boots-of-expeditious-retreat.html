Boots of expeditious retreat were common enchanted boots, popular among beginner thieves.

Powers

When the wearer uttered the boots' command word, these boots boosted the wearer's movement speed, allowing them to runaway with the stolen goods from an irate shopkeep or an angry noble. This was done via the expeditious retreat spell weaved into the boots. They allowed the wearer to cast the spell thrice per day.

Notable Owners

  • Legodia Half-Elven, a mage of Bremen in the late 14 century DR.
