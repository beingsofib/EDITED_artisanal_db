Lover's Avarice

Item
nameLover's Avarice
typePotion
locationAncient Abode, Putrid Bog
value5e1 gp

Lover's Avarice was a potion created by the green hag Auntie Ethel from her underground lair beneath the Putrid Bog.

Description

While the potion smelled sweet like honey with a hint of roses, there was a lingering scent of rot that accompanied it as well.

Powers

The imbiber of Lover's Avarice found themselves feeling weak-willed and less insightful for a matter of a few minutes.

History

As of the , only one bottle of Lover's Avarice could be found in Auntie Ethel's Ancient Abode.
