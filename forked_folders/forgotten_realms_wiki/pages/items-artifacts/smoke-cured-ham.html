Smoke-cured ham, or simply smoked ham, was a variety of ham, cooked and cured by the smoke of hot burning wood.

Description

Smoke-cured ham was sliced to order. It had a distinctly salty and smoky taste.

Usage

Cubes of smoked ham were an ingredient in the rustic split pea soup from the Dalelands in central Faerûn.

Availability

In the late 15 century DR, sliced up smoke-cured ham was served at the Lonesome Tankard of Eveningstar in the Forest Kingdom of Cormyr.
