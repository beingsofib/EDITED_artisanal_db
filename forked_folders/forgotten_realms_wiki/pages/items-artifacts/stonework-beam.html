The stonework beam was a magical staff of sorts found in the mines of Tethyamar during the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The staff was light and strong and surprisingly bouncy despite being a length of stone, and granted the bearer a measure of that toughness. Those struck by the staff found themselves staggered, concussed, and unable to concentrate, and it could even paralyze them.

History

The stonework beam was found in Mineshaft 214S when a dwarven expedition led by Hal Stoutheart searched for the jewels of ardor.
