Ostorian resin was a variety of mysterious, ancient resin found in small batches in only the most well preserved of Ostorian ruins.

History

All artisans and naturalists who studied this resin in the 15 century DR to identify the material.

Usages

Those who found this resin in the Present Age were known to use it in the restoration of Ostorian relics.
