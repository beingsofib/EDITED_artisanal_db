Dispelling

Item
nameDispelling
typeWeapon quality
casterlevel3e10
value3e+1

Dispelling was a weapon quality that could be applied to weapons. Once per day, the wielder could use the weapon to dispel magic on whatever or whoever the weapon next touched.

Creation

In order to forge a dispelling weapon, the caster must have dispel magic available.
