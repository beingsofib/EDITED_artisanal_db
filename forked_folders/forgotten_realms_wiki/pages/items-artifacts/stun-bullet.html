Stun bullets were a type of enchanted sling ammunition that disoriented and stupefied its targets.

Powers

Stun bullets were imbued with magical currents that made them more balanced and gravitate towards the targets more easily. They also possessed a +1 offensive enchantment that, upon reaching a living target, stunned them in a burst of magical energy, rendering them vulnerable for a short period of time.

History

In the , stun bullets were used by Aurilite Stormsisters and postulants who joined the Legion of the Chimera to bring destruction on the Ten Towns and Kuldahar of Icewind Dale.
