Gloves of concentration were magical items that aided the wearer's spellcasting.

Description

Gloves of concentration appeared fairly mundane, well-made gloved oof brown leather.

Powers

Gloves of concentration generally came in three power levels, regular, greater, and epic. All three types had an enchantment that increased the wearer's concentration skill, regular increased it only slightly, as the epic - increased it dramatically.

History

The first pair of the gloves of concentration was created by a wizard Beregost Hamish who noticed his ability to concentrate on complex spells was diminishing with age. He created these gloves as a tool to help relax his mind, allowing him to be able to easily cast spells even in dangerous situations. Eventually, he died of old age, but not before making a fortune by selling the gloves to his fellow spellcasters.

Notable Owners

  • Beregost Hamish, the creator of the original.
