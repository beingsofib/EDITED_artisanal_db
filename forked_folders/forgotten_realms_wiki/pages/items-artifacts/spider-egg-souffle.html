Spider egg souffle

Item
nameSpider Egg Soufflé
typeFood
locationNeverwinter, Sword Coast North
value4e50 gp

Spider egg soufflé was an exquisite and rare culinary indulgence served at the Moonstone Mask inn of Neverwinter.

Powers

This dish was known to stimulate the imbiber's muscles.

History

In the mid-to-late-15 century DR, spider egg soufflé could be found in the city of Neverwinter.
