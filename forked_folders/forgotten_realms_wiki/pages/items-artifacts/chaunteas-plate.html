Chauntea's plate

Item
nameChauntea's plate
typeFood

Chauntea's plate was a type of salad named after the goddess of the earth and growing things. It went well with a spicy vinegar dressing.

Availability

  • It was served as an opening course at a royal feast held by Lord Partic Thistle at Thistleflame Keep.
