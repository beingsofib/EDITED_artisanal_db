Windbiter

Item
nameWindbiter
typeBattleaxe
locationThe Haunted Lands, Zakhara

Windbiter was an enchanted battleaxe wielded by Tebessa al-Khawati, a desert centaur of the Haunted Lands in Zakhara.

Powers

Windbiter made it easier for its wielder to strike their enemy and inflicted more harm than a normal battleaxe.
