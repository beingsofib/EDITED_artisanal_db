An immovable rod was a flat iron rod that could be magically fixed in place.

Powers

An immovable rod had a button on one end that could activate and deactivate the magical effect of the rod. Once activated, an immovable rod was magically fixed in place, even if it was located in midair. The rod could hold up to 8,000 pounds (3,629 kilograms) of weight or withstand an equivalent amount of force.

Common uses for an immovable rod included suspending ropes, barring doors, or creating steps and handholds.
