Docker's quaff was a type of cheap meal commonly available for purchase in the City of Splendors, Waterdeep, for a mere copper piece.

Description

This meal was available to the poorest of the city inhabitants and adventurers down on their luck. This was considered to be the main meal of the day for many Waterdhavians. It consisted of a waterskin of watered-down barley beer and a loaf of dark, crusty bread baked with sausage ends and other types of meat scraps from the local eateries. Docker's quaffs were sold at the City of Splendors's open markets daily, from the early glimpses of the sun to early afternoon.
