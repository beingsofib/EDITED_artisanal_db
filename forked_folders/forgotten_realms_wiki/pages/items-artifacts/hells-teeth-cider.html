Hell's Teeth Cider was a type of alcoholic cider imbued with magics, available in the city of Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.

History

The enchanted beverage was on offer at the Yawning Portal tavern of Waterdeep, with its bottles stashed away in Durnan's cabinet. One serving of Hell's Teeth Cider was priced at ten gold coins.
