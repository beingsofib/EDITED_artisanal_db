The topaz band was an enchanted ring that increased the wearer's defense. It was created in the pre–Weeping War Myth Drannor.

Description

This masterfully crafted band was created by the dwarves of old Myth Drannor. It was inlaid with multiple shimmering topaz gems, appropriately for its name.

Powers

The topaz band bore an enchantment that increased the wearer's defense, similar to that of a +2 ring of protection.

History

In 1369 DR, the topaz band was discovered by a group of adventurers opposing the Cult of the Dragon in the ruins of the Dwarven Dungeons of Myth Drannor. The magic ring was within one of the chests on the Halls of Light level of the dungeon, guarded by restless undead, in the same room with the stairs to the dungeon's lower levels.
