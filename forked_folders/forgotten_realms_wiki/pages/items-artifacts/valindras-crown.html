Valindra's crown was a legendary enchanted crown once worn by Valindra Shadowmantle, the lich elf, who plagued Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Valindra's crown, and, possibly, its copies, was a sinister-looking headpiece decorated with five sharp spikes reaching up to the skies. The crown was made of dark metal and lacked any ornamentations typical for that type of jewelry.

Powers

When worn, or even simply touched, it echoed whisperings of an unknown entity directly into the mind. The crown's true enchantments available to Valindra herself were unknown; however, when the crown was lost after the siege of Neverwinter, its magic allowed the wearers to boost their physical and magical might, their ability to deal devastating, well-aimed, or lucky damage, sped up their recovery time, and slightly increased their physical defenses.

History

In the late 15 century DR, the , the city of Neverwinter was besieged by the armies led by Valindra Shadowmantle. After her defeat at the hands of the Heroes of Sleeping Dragon Bridge, her crown was lost, and, eventually, recovered by the city's defenders and adventurers.
