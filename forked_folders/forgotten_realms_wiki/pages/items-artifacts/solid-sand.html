Solid sand was a magical sand originating in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Powers

This useful sand could be tossed upon any soft surface such as snow, mud, regular sand, and even quicksand, where it would instantly harden into a shell that could be walked upon.

A handful of solid sand could be used to cover 500 square feet or less and lasted for 10-40 minutes.
