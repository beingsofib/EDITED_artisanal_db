Gloves of the Yellow Rose were enchanted gloves used by the monks of Disciples of St. Sollars the Twice-Martyred from the Monastery of the Yellow Rose.

Powers

When worn by a monk, Gloves of the Yellow Rose enhanced their attack power and, when struck, deal electricity damage to the target.

History

In late 14 century DR these gloves could often be found on the hands of monks of the Yellow Rose who traveled alongside paladins of Ilmater as guides and companions. Many of the gloves of the Yellow Rose of various enchantment strength could be purchased from temples and merchants found in Neverwinter, Daggerford, and other cities along the Sword Coast.
