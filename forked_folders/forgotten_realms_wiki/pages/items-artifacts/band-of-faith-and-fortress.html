Band of faith and fortress

The band of faith and fortress was a magical golden ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The ring helped to keep the wearer alive through the greatest of dangers, and even helped focus religious fervor into protection against hostile spells.

History

The ring was found in the tunnels of the purple worm Yegora, who lurked below the ruins of Myth Drannor while the Elves of Cormanthor and Red Wizards of Droic Orgul fought over the city.
