Lamp of Devotion

Item
nameLamp of Devotion
typeFlame, beacon
locationTower of the Dawn, Sarahin

The Lamp of Devotion was a sacred flame for the holy slayers of the Everlasting within the fortress of Sarahin.

History

Located within the Tower of the Dawn, the flame was tended so it burned brightly during all hours of the day, signifying the Everlasting's honor of Hajama.
