Amulets of Lathander were enchanted protective amulets created by high-ranking specialty clerics of Lathander.

Description

Amulet of Lathander were hammered disks of platinum.

Powers

When worn in battle, these magic amulets dispensed light healing to the wearer when they fell but had not yet expired. Amulets of Lathander brought these individuals back from the brink of death. That magic could only be activated not more than thrice per day. The amulet possessed greater powers that were only activated when worn by priests of Lathander. Instead of dispersing minimal healing magics, Amulets of Lathander cast heal spell on the wearer. This power was accessible thrice per day.

The item's divine enchantments made it only wearable by those who revered good. The amulet burned everyone else until it was removed.
