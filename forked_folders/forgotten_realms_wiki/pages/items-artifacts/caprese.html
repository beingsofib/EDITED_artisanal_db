Caprese was a kind of salad.

Description

Caprese was made of slices of red tomato with cheese and basil, seasoned with salt and olive oil.

Reputation

Caprese was especially popular in Neverwinter around Midsummer, when it was served with squash soup and watermelon sorbet.
