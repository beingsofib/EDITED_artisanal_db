Flaming gullet (beverage)

nameFlaming Gullet

Flaming Gullet was a spicy mixed alcoholic beverage that was served in inns and taverns across the Realms during the 14 century DR, such as the Hidden Lady.

Description

Flaming Gullet drink was an amber-hued mixed drink. Its ingredients were carefully measured and consisted of klaraunth (a strong and clear distilled barley alcoholic beverage from Tethyr, Chessenta, or the Vilhon), a distillate of hot red radishes, a distillate of horseradish, and finely crushed and powdered chestnuts.

The drink was never to be served flaming, oftentimes contradicting tall-tales and boasts heard in taverns across Faerûn.

Effects

The name of the drink was a description of what the imbiber was to expect from consuming a flaming gullet. The fiery hot drink caused th sensation of searing fire in the drinker's throat and cleared any and all congestion the drinker could have been suffering from.
