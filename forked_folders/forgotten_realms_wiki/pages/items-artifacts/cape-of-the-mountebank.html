A cape of the mountebank granted its wearer the use of dimension door once per day.

Description

It smelled of brimstone, and created a cloud of smoke when used to teleport, both at the point of departure and arrival.

Notable Owners

  • Jarlaxle, founder and leader of Bregan D'aerthe
  • Laskilar, a pirate captain of Jahaka Anchorage
