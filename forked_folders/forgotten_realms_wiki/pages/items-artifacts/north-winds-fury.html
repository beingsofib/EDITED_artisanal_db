The North Wind's Fury was the pirate vessel of the corsair Makbulla bint Dalmar in 1367 DR.

Description

North Wind's Fury was a 70 ft. (21 m) long sambuk crewed by 40 hardened dwarves.

Activities

Makbulla primarily hunted the waters around the Pearl Cities and the Pantheist League.
