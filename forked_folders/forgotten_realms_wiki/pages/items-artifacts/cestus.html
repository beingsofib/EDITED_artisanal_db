A cestus weas a type of offensive gauntlet found in the Realms, primarily known for being used by gladiators.

Description

These gauntlets were typically made of leather, with spikes and razor edges on the back and across the knuckles.

Some cestuses were also loaded with lead or other heavy filler to give punches more force.

Notable Users of Cestuses

Individuals

  • The avatars of Bahgtru typically fought with cesti studded with steel rivets.
  • Kellack "Killer" Ironfist was a shield dwarf gladiator of Hillsfar, so named for his use of a cestus made of black iron.

Regions & Settlements

  • In the Domains of Dread, some criminals were known to fight with cestuses.
  • Some of Hillsfar's escaped gladiators fought with a cestus.
  • Some of Waterdeep's thugs were known to fight with cestuses.
