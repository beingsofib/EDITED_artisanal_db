Southern Heartlands Cider was a variety of cider produced in the Heartlands of Faerûn and popularized sometime before the late 15 century DR.

Description

Southern Heartlands Cider was a strong alcoholic beverage, mostly used for pickling and cooking.

History

By the late 15 century DR, bottled Southern Heartlands Cider was in wide use, found along the Sword Coast North and consumed as far north as the city of Luskan.
