Date cake was a type of pastry handfood made in Tashalar on the Chultan Peninsula, Calimshan, and Huzuz, a great city in Zakhara.

Description

Date cake were made out of pulverized fruit of the date palm tree, pressed into small hand-sized round cakes.

History

Date cakes from Tashalar were a popular trade commodity, found in the hands of merchants and traders on the Sword Coast, all along the Trade Way, and as far north as the city of Luskan.
