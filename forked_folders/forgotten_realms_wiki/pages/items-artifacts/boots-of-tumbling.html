Boots of tumbling were enchanted boots worn by the Scarlet Mummers of Lliira in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

Boots of tumbling and their weaker version, lesser boots of tumbling, were simple magic boots that boosted the wearer's ability to tumble out of harm's way.

History

Boots of tumbling were created to be used exclusively by the acrobatic militant order of Lliira, the Scarlet Mummers, and worn in their war against the agents of the Maiden of Pain. By the late 14 century DR, these boots found their way into the hands of merchants and adventurers of northwest Faerûn and the Sword Coast North.

Notable Owners

  • Dela Nereth, a black-market merchant of the Bloodsailors of Neverwinter in 1372 DR.
