Amulet of Songs was a variety of enchanted jewelry that empowered bardic songs and offered them protection from harm.

Description

The Amulet of Songs was a dwarven-crafted gold chain and adorned with a crystalized fey teardrop.

Powers

The amulet's powers came from the fey tear, it held fey's natural affinity to magic and their natural charm. These magics were only available for bards to channel, boosting their natural charisma, and offer boosted defense. Additionally, the amulet allowed bards to memorize one additional spell from the first, second, and third circles of magic.

History

Amulets of Songs were created by the dwarvish smith of Icewind Dale, Graendel Granitefist, sometime before the . Since then, such enchanted pieces of jewelry could be found on the necks of many bards, dazzling the stages of many inns and taverns across Faerûn, and inspiring their comrades on the fiends of battle.
