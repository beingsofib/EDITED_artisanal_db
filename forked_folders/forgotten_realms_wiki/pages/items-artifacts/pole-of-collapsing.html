A pole of collapsing was a magical 10-foot pole.

Powers

When a special command word was spoken, this tall pole was able to telescopically collapse into a rod with a length of . The weight of the pole was unchanged. The pole was restored to its original length when the individual spoke another command word. If the individual was in a small space, the pole only lengthened as far the width or height of the space.
