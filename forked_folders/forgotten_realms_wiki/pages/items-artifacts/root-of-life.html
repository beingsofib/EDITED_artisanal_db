Roots of Life were a variety of curative wondrous roots created by the druids of the Emerald Enclave of the Vilhon Reach.

Powers

When a Root of Life was first used, it attached itself to the skin of its owner, boosting their life force in the process. This boon of vitality remained in place as long as a Root of Life remained attached. In dire moments of need, the Root's owner could consume it for its powerful curative qualities, similar to a casting of the greater restoration spell.

History

The Emerald Enclave druids held the methods of Root of Life creation a secret even envied by the elves of Evermeet. One of these items belonged to Liaga Kintree, a member of the Emerald Enclave tasked with improving of the relations between the folk of Westgate and nature.
