Potions of restoration were invaluable magic potions that restored lost life energy, popular among adventurers dealing with powerful undead and clerics of Lathander.

Description

Potions of restoration were usually dispensed in small crystal phials. The liquid itself appeared murky when inside the phial but began to gently glow when brought close to a living creature.

Powers

Potions of restoration came in three power levels, regular, lesser restoration. When consumed, these potions restored the imbiber's life force and ability drained by the creatures of the negative energy.

Notable Owners

  • Nazrym - a minor cleric of Bane involved in the Zhent invasion of Shadowdale in the Year of Risen Elfkin, 1375 DR.
  • Vhazror - an orc champion of Gruumsh in Thar in 1372 DR.
