Vessel of vaporous stones

Item
nameVessel of Vaporous Stones
typeWondrous item
locationToril

Vessel of Misty Passage was a very rare enchanted receptacle that carried strengthening magics.

Effects

When activated, a vessel of vaporous stones produced a cloud of mist that invigorated and toughened the vessel's owner and their allies. The magic was especially useful when they were facing overwhelming odds in battle.
