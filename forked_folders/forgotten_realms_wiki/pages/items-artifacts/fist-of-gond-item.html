Fist of Gond (item)

Item
nameFist of Gond
nicknamesDoohickey

A Fist of Gond,

Creation

Constructing one of these devices required, in part: an axle, cogs, grommets, springs, sprockets, and some form of paper for its three radiating blades.

History

These devices developed out from attempts by Gondsmen to innovate caltrops.
