Paprika was a type of spice on Toril that was considered exotic in many parts of Faerûn, though it was also known to the ancient speakers of Old Common.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold paprika under its "Exotics" food section for 30 gold pieces per .
  • In the city of Procampur, one could find paprika and other spices for sale at the restaurant Drinks O' Toril.

Usage

  • Deviled eggs, a peculiar dish of Earth that the mage Rautheene once partook of, made use of powdered paprika alongside chives.
  • Paprika was commonly utilized in Zakharan cooking, typically being used alongside cinnamon, cumin, and saffron.
  • Medicinally, paprika could be used as a poultice or stimulant.
  • At least one variant of the dish Stumblenoodles utilized paprika as a seasoning.

Notable Users

  • Infernal Rapture, a restaurant in the Wandering Emporium, served roasted wereboar that was liberally seasoned with paprika and pepper.

History

In the , the crew of the Realms Master were being held captive in the underground lair of the Akri. While there, the tribe of werecats were planning to bake hin Foxilon Cardluck into a meat pie seasoned with one cup of paprika.
