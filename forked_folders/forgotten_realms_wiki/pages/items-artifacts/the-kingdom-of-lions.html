The Kingdom of Lions was an ancient Zakharan text written in Old Anwahran.

Description

The cover of the tome was decorated with the symbol of Anwahran kingship–a golden lion displayed on a malachite green field. The book was magically warded to preserve it over the centuries.

History

Written by Azaltin ibn Issad, the Kingdom of Lions chronicled the history of Al-Anwahr up to the revolution led by Azaltin's brother Amakim.

Fearing the tome's discovery by a vizer named Zeenab, Azaltin carefully hid the tome.
