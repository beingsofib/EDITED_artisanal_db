Jar of endless honey

Item
nameJar of Endless Honey
typeEnchanted jar

A jar of endless honey was an enchanted jar that could, presumably, create infinite supplies of honey.

Notable Owners

  • Umberto Thornhart, a druid detective whose preferred wildshape form was that of a brown bear. Whenever he ate from his jar it gave him the energy to strike more often in a battle.
