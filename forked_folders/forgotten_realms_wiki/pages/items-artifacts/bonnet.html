Bonnet

Item
nameBonnet
typeHat

Bonnets were a type of hat common in agricultural communities.

Description

Bonnets had wide brims and were rather flimsy, but were thick enough to shelter their wearer's face and neck from the sun.

Notable Wearers

Individuals

  • Shelaerra Blundfeather, the head of the household servants in Raventree Villa, sometimes styled her long hair into a bun and wore it under a bonnet.

Regions & Settlements

  • In the Blade Kingdoms, fanciful bonnets were common among noble women.
  • Around the mid-14 century DR, one of the many common pieces of headwear for Waterdhavian women included pleated linen bonnets, often embroidered and featuring tassels.
