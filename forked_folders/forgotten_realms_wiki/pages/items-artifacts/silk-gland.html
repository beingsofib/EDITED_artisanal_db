Silk glands were organs extracted from various species of giant spiders and ettercaps for the use in alchemy.

Description

Silk glands were fleshy sacks of bloody pulp spider flesh.

Usage

A skilled alchemist could use raw silk glands to make suspension of spider silk. They could be mixed with special types of alchemical vitriol to create web grenades.
