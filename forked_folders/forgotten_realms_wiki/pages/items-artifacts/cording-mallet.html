Cording mallets, or splitting mauls, were axe-like mauls sometimes used by rangers or woodsmen throughout the wilds of Faerûn.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue offered a cording mallet for 10 gold pieces. It had a solid steel head weighing a hefty that was attached to a steel handle that weighed an additional .

Usage

Cording mallets were used specifically for splitting logs or chopped wood. Though cumbersome and a bit unwieldy, cording mallets could also be used as weapons.
