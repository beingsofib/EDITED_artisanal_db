Twostring

Item
nameTwostring
typeMusical instrument
locationMintarn, west Faerûn

The twostring was a type of stringed musical instrument, noted for having only two strings.

The twostring was particularly associated with the island of Mintarn in the Sea of Swords. Such twostrings were sold from Halambar Lutes & Harps in Waterdeep in the 1360s DR.
