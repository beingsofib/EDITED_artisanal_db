Darstil's gloves

Item
nameDarstil's Gloves
typeGloves
materialLeather
originWheloon Prison, Cormyr

Darstil's gloves were the accoutrements of the Night Brigade wizard Darstil Vurusk, found in Wheloon Prison in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The gloves were built to generally improve the accuracy and power of magical spellcasting.

History

They could have been found in Darstil's Tower in Wheloon Prison, after the mad Darstil was slain by the Riverside Rats.
