Bitterroot beer was a smoky-tasting alcoholic brew made from bitterroot, popularized by residents of Candlekeep

History

Circa 1370 DR, bitterroot beer was served at the Lady Luck Tavern in Daggerford. And by 1372 DR, other taprooms around Daggerford were serving the smoky beverage. It could be found in the Happy Cow in the Money Quarter, and to the south along the Trade Way in the city of Liam's Hold.
