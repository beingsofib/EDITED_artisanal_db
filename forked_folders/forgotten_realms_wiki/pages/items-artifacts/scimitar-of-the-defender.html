Scimitars of the defender were somewhat common enchanted scimitars.

Description

These scimitars were bigger than an average weapon of the type or a saber. They had a curved shape with the outer side sharpened to deal slashing damage. It also had a proposed defensive hilt ending in an elongated sharp point.

Powers

Each scimitar of the defender had its slashing edge sharpened with a +1 enchantment, and its magics allowed the wielder to improve their defenses through magical deflection of attacks. Stronger versions of these weapons existed.
