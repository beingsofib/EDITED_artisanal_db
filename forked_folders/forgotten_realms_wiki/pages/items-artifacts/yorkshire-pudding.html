Yorkshire pudding was a dish eaten in Damara. It was served alongside the main courses of feasts, accompanying fares such as suckling pig, wine pudding, and bread pudding.

History

In the , Baron Tranth of Bloodstone served Yorkshire pudding as part of a grand feast meant to welcome and woo adventurers led by Gareth Dragonsbane in hopes that they might help free Bloodstone Village from the predations of the Bandit Army in service of the Grandfather of Assassins.
