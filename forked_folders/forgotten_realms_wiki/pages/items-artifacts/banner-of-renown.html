Banner of renown

Item
nameBanner of Renown
typeFlag
locationZakhara

The banner of renown was a magical flag that could be found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Description

This flag displayed the moon and trailing stars symbol of the Land of Fate.

Powers

When held during battle, this flag inspired all enlightened persons within with supernatural morale, infusing them with bravery and strength of arm. If the bearer of the flag was killed, the flag dissipated and all magical powers vanished instantly.
