The Skinburster was an enchanted halberd that allowed the wielder to accumulate force energy, created sometime before the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The Skinburster's enchantments made the weapon emit a gentle yellow light in which its curved blade resembled an unnerving smile. The weapon also possessed a simple +1 enchantment that made its blade more deadly, and it guided the wielder's aim towards the target. Lastly, the weapon's skinburster force magic imbued the wielder with a charge of magical force energy upon a successful strike, making them a force conduit for twelve seconds. By itself, this enchantment had little utility but could be synergized with other magic items that allowed for the release of the accumulated force.

History

As of the , The Skinburster belonged to Inquisitor W'wargaz of Crèche Y'llek. The weapon was stored in W'wargaz's quarters in the ruined Rosymorn Monastery, in the chamber that once hid relics of the monastery.
