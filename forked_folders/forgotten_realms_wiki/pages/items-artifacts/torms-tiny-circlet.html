Torm's Tiny Circlet was a unique magic ring, part of the Fading Rings set, that bore the name of Torm, the Hand of Righteousness.

Description

Torm's Tiny Circlet was a thin band with tiny inlaid diamonds.

Powers

This Fading Ring possessed an enchantment that increased the wearer's constitution at the price of reduced initiative. Additionally, the ring had the ability to cast dominate person at high proficiency.

History

By 1369 DR, Torm's Tiny Circlet could be found in the possession of a mysterious monument, the Great Stone Head. The statue was located in the Nightingale Court district of Myth Drannor. In exchange for one of the eidolons, small totem-like statuettes depicting various gods, the monument dispensed powerful magic rings. Torm's Tiny Circlet was one of the rings the monument produced.
