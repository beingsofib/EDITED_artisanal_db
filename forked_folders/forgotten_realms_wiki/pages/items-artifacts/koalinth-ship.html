The Koalinth was a ship captained by the hobgoblin Klarz in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the years following the War of the Silver Marches, circa the , Klarz sailed the Koalinth in search of King Hekaton and the Morkoth. He was hired by the druids of the Emerald Enclave.

Inhabitants

Klarz kept a crew of twenty hobgoblins and five bugbears aboard the Koalinth.
