A quiver was a container for arrows that allowed for quickly drawing them during combat. Besides arrows, some were known to store javelins, quarrels, and similar missile weaponry within them.

Description

Quivers were typically made of leather and had straps on them that allowed it to hang over a user's shoulder.

A typical quiver could hold up to 20 arrows.

Notable quivers

  • Bag of Nails: the enchanted quiver that once belonged to a mad tabaxi treasure hunter of the same name.
  • Quiver of Anariel
  • Quiver of Ehlonna
