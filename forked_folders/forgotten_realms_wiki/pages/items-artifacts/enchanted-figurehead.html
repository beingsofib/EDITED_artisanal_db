Enchanted figurehead

Item
nameEnchanted Figurehead
typeFigurehead
locationEvermeet

Enchanted figureheads were magic items created on the island of Evermeet for use on their sailing vessels.

Description

These items came in a wide variety depending on the creativity of the craftsman.

Powers

All sailors aboard a ship outfitted with an enchanted figurehead were more proficient at any task related to sailing. Furthermore, the ship itself was more hardy with regards to resisting damage from storms and general seaworthiness.
