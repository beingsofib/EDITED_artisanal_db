Maw, the Dracolich's Bite was a magical kukri found in the ruins of Myth Drannor in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

Maw was tied to the dracolich Karliath, and embodied a small part of its power. It excelled at breaking past defenses, staggering the enemy, and paralyzing them.

History

The kukri was found in the Cult of the Dragon base hidden in Myth Drannor, where the Red Wizards of Thay caught the dracolich Karliath. The Elves of Cormanthor then attacked the base, possibly taking the blade for themselves.
