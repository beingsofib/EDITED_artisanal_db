Vodka, or sarrak, was a clear and high-proof distilled alcoholic beverage rarely encountered in the Realms. It was distilled from potatos and originated from Rashemen and Impiltur.

History

Famously, the great wizard Otiluke of Oerth, in his younger days, partook in boastful dares from Bigby, chugging thirteen liters of vodka whilst being protected by a cloak of protection.

In the late 15 century DR, the author of Moxy's Mixology Manual shared their denigrating opinion on mixed drinks made with vodka, vermouth, and rum in their book.
