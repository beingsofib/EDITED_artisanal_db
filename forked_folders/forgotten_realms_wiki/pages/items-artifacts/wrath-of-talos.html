The Wrath of Talos was a unique sacred weapon of the Church of Talos.

Powers

The Wrath possessed a +4 enchantment and granted the wielder immunity to electricity. This shortbow materialized its own arrows made out of lightning. Additionally, it granted the wielder darkvision.

History

Talossan worshipers believed this bow to be manifestation of their god's wraith, showering their targets with unending lightning bolts. It was believed that some of the most devoted worshipers of Talos wandered into raging storms and shot this weapon's lighting upon people scattering for shelter.

In 1373 DR, the Wrath of Talos was in possession of the Zhentarim squad stationed in Iyrauroth's swamps of Thar.
