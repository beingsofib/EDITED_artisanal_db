Autumn's Stand

nameAutumn's Stand

rarity5eLegendary

Autumn's Stand was a pair of enchanted boots worn by druids. They could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

They had thin soles that crackled as the wearer walked, creating a similar sensation to walking on soft fallen leaves.

Powers

Wearers of these boots could move swiftly and confidently across any terrain without distraction, as if under the influence of a find the path spell.

Notable Owners

  • Pikel Bouldershoulder, a dwarven druid, was said to own a pair as of the late 15 century DR.
