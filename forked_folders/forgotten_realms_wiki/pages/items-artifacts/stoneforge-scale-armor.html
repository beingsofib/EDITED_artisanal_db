Stoneforge scale armor was a type of enchanted scale mail, used in the 15 century DR.

Powers

Sets of stoneforge scale armor possessed +1 and +2 defensive enchantments. These sets were as tough as stones of a mountain, granting the wearer protection from elements of fire, cold, and lightning. It's weight, however, made it cumbersome to move in.

History

In the 15 century DR, stoneforge scale armor could be found worn along he mountain paths of the Sword Mountains, and sold in the Mercenary Arms Depot of the city of Neverwinter.
