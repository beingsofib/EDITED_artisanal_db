Reglar's gloves of freedom

Item
nameReglar's Gloves of Freedom
typeGloves
value2e25,000 gp

Reglar's gloves of freedom was a magic item that could break magical charms.

Description

These sturdy leather gloves had silver pearls sewn into the stitching.

Powers

The wearer of Reglar's gloves of freedom could break the enchantment placed upon a charmed individual by shaking their hand. The magic only worked if the target was charmed against their will. Wearing these gloves did not protect the wearer from such enchantments.

History

Specialty priests of Waukeen called goldeyes had a chance of receiving a pair of Reglar's gloves of freedom as one of the magic items they were given upon their admission to the clergy.
