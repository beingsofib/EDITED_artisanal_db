Cursed Imaskari hat

Item
namecursed Imaskari hat
value2e1 cp

The cursed Imaskari hat was an Imaskari relic unearthed from the ruins of Solon by the slaves of Ambuchar Devayam.

Description

This hat was conical, made from iron pyrite, also known as fool's gold.

Powers

Any person who put the hat on would lose a great deal of their intelligence permanently.
