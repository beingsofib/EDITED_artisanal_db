Razorlink Chainmail was a type of enchanted chainmail, used in the 15 century DR.

Powers

Sets of razorlinks possessed +1 and +2 defensive enchantments. These armors were interwoven with razorlink improving their defensive capabilities. Additionally, razorlink chainmail made punishment dealt to those already heavily hurt more severe.Razorlink chainmail could only be worn by fighters and celrics.

History

In the 15 century DR, razorlink chainmail could be found worn by orcs of the Sword Mountains, and sold in the Mercenary Arms Depot of the city of Neverwinter, as well as recovered from the ruins of Illefarn.
