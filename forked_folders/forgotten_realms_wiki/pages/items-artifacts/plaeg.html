Plaeg was an enchanted ancient ornate shortbow imbued with powerful flame magic.

Powers

Plaeg was a powerful weapon that held a +6 enchantment that dealt devastating damage and guided arrows towards their targets. The bow's magic increased the wielder's finesse at the cost of their strength. Lastly, Plaeg held three charges of the flame strike spell that could be released at will.

History

Plaeg's history was a mystery, however, in the , it belonged to the dracolich Pelendralaar. The undead dragon made its lair by the pool of radiance underneath Castle Cormanthor, aiding a Wearer of Purple of the Cult of the Dragon, Kya Mordrayn, in her quest to corrupt Myth Drannor's mythal and harness powers of the pool.
