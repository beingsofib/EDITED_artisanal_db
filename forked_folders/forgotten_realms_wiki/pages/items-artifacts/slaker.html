Slaker was the name of a dark beer brewed in Amphail, a village in northwest Faerûn on the Long Road, circa the , where it was served at the Stag-Horned Flagon. At three copper pieces for a tankard, this heavy, barley-based beer was popular with the locals and visitors alike.
