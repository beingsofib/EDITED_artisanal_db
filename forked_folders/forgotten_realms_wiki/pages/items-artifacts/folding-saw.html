Folding saws were a type of saw carried by rangers throughout the wilds of Faerûn.

Description

The serrated blade of a folding saw was attached to two pieces of wood via a retaining band.

History

Folding saws were compact and used just like regular saws or axes for cutting branches or felling trees. They were ineffective if used as a weapon.

Folding saws were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.
