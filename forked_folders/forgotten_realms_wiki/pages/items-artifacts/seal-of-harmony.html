Seal of harmony

Item
nameSeal of Harmony
typeMagic item
creatorLaeral Silverhand

The seal of harmony was a unique enchanted token crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Jarlaxle in the late 15 century DR. It was one of many pieces of so-called "Silverhand gear" crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep.

Powers

In battle, the seal of harmony served to protect Raika from harm by reflecting incoming attacks back at her foes. Outside of battle, it would pulse steadily to remind Raika to stay on her path and maintain balance in her mission as she transitioned back-and-forth between civilization and the wilderness.

Notable Owners

  • Raika, a half-orc barbarian for whom Silverhand also crafted the maul of focused destruction, the Great Wolf's Mark, and the Great Wolf's Fury.
