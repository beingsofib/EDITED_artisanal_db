Boots of very fast blinking were enchanted boots that harnessed a less useful version of the misty step spell.

Powers

Boots of very fast blinking allowed the individual wearing to blink from one place to another, like with the use of misty step. However, the spell only transported the wearer of the boots, but not their possessions nor clothes.

History

In the , a pair of boots of very fast blinking was displayed among splendorous enchanted rewards for those willing to partake in Akabi's Wheel of Fortune game in the Circus of the Last Days during its stay in the Rivington neighborhood of Baldur's Gate.
