Index (scroll)

Item
nameIndex
typeScroll
materialPaper, parchment, ink

An index was a magical scroll created for the express purpose of holding a spell used in the creation of a magical item.

Description

A wide variety of indexes existed, each able to hold different amounts of magical power and complexity according to the materials and expertise used in their creation. The writing surface could be paper, parchment, or a similar material, and the ink was usually mixed with powdered metal. This ink was used to draw the arcane diagrams and ciphers that contained the spell to be used.

Known indexes included:

  • Simple index, made with cheap paper and ink, able to hold only the weakest of magic.
  • Cypren index, made with copper-based ink.
  • Argent index, made with silver-based ink.
  • Amber index, made with electrum-based ink.
  • Aureus index, made with gold-based ink.
  • Mithrus index, made with mithral-based ink.
