Vauge tea

Item
nameVauge tea
typeBeverage
locationKhôltar, south Faerûn

Vauge tea was a beverage made by steeping parts of the vauge plant in hot water. It was available in Khôltar, south Faerûn, at the popular blurdren (food counter) called Munsrum's Ready Ladle near the north gate of the city, circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR. Vauge tea was free to customers who brought their own cup, or 1 cp if served in one of Munsrum's dented metal cups.
