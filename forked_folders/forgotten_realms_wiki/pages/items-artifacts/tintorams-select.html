Tintoram's Select was a blackberry brandy famous throughout the Realms.

Description

This distilled wine was known for its potency and rich flavor. It had a strong aroma of sweetness, and was dark purple—almost black—in color.

Tintoram's Select was bottled in purple glass flasks with long, narrow necks. Its cork was sealed with lead.

Source

Tintoram's Select was distilled from blackberries by halflings in the Purple Hills.

History

A bottle of Tintoram's Select was used to poison Zaranda Star, when she was staying at the Ith-Side Inn in Ithmong in Tarsakh of the .

Reputation

A flask of Tintoram's Select was considered a valuable gift.
