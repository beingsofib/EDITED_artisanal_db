Ring of reverse alignment

Item
nameRing of Reverse Alignment
typeRing

A ring of reverse alignment was an enchanted ring that completely flipped the wearer's nature.

Powers

These rings caused the wearer's true nature to take on the traits of its polar opposite.

Notable Owners

Mefraset, a Fang of Set, placed a a ring of reverse alignment that prompted her to help a group of adventurers against her previous allies.
