Buskins were a form of footwear found in the Realms.

History

In the 14 century DR, buskins were common among men in the city of Waterdeep and could often be seen worn in place of walking boots.

In 1368 DR, the corpse of Lanig was found wearing laced buskins when it was discovered by Alassra Shentrara and Ebroin.

Notable Wearers

  • When the silver dragon Azhaq shapechanged into the form of a human, he wore a shabby brown robe and buskins.
  • Brom Selwick.
