Crusher's ring was an enchanted ring that belonged to a goblin named Crusher, a member of the Cult of the Absolute circa 1492 DR.

Description

Crusher's ring was a simple golden band with a single green gem affixed to it. While it was in Crusher's possession, the ring was covered with filth and grime, yet managed to exude a faint aroma of lavender.

Powers

Crusher's ring possessed a simple enchantment that increased the wearer's movement speed.

History

In 1492 DR, Crusher's ring was worn on one of Crusher's filthy toes. While the Cult of the Absolute was based in the Shattered Sanctum, a ruined temple of Selûne, Crusher was drunk on alcohol and power, under the leadership of the Absolute. Following successful raids, Crusher felt superior to other races and demanded them to kiss his filthy toes with Crusher's ring on one of them.
