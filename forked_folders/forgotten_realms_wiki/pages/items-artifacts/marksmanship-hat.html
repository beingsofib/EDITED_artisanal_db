Marksmanship hat was a type of enchanted headwear found in the Western Heartlands in the late 15 century DR.

Description

This leather hat was decorated with an image of open silvery wings.

Powers

Marksmanship hat's minor enchantment aided the wearer with their aim and guided the unleashed missiles towards their targets.

History

One such hat belonged to Roah Moonglow, a merchant and an agent of Zhentarim who worked with the members of the Cult of the Absolute in the and visited the cult's lair in the Moonrise Towers to trade.
