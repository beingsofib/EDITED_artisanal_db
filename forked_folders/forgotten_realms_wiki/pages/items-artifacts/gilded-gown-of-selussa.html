Gilded Gown of Selussa

Item
nameGilded Gown of Selussa
typeClothing
locationHellgate Keep, High Forest

The Gilded Gown of Selussa was a tight gauzy dress made of mithral.

Abilities

The garment protected the wearer from wounds and reflected magic missile spells back to the casters.

History

Around 1370 DR, Sarya Dlardrageth wore the gown in the ruins of Hellgate Keep.
