Retching Serpent was a variety of enchanted flute that unleashed deadly magic when played.

Description

Retching Serpent was a wind instrument: a long conical tube made of wood, more than long. The tube was coiled like a snake. The flute had six holes along its body to adjust the produced sound in a manner similar to that of a cornet.

Powers

When Retching Serpent was played, it allowed the owner to discharge a stinking cloud twice per day. The instrument held fifty charges of the spell.

History

In the , one of the Retching Serpent flutes belonged to a merchant of musical instruments and was available for purchase from her stall in the town of Targos.
