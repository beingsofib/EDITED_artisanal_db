Ring of windwarding

Item
nameRing of Windwarding
typeRing
value2e5,000 gp

A ring of windwarding was an enchanted ring that shielded the wearer from all winds.

Powers

This ring created a shield surrounding the wearer for , protecting them from all forms of wind both natural and magical. This also included protection from air elementals and similar creatures.
