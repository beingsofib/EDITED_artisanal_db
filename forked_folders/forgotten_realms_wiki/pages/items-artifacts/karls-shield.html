Karl's shield was an historically important, enchanted shield during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

Karl's shield was decorated with the device of Karl von Stag Falls. After the release of the vampire Romanisk Vlaensk, however, the device was painted over with Vlaensk's own heraldry.

Powers

The shield was unremarkable in its powers, and only its historical and political significance granted it notoriety.

History

The shield had belonged to Karl von Stag Falls, and remained in his home until the times of his great-grandson Vilhelm von Stag Falls, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. After Vilhelm accidentally released the vampire Romanisk, who mind-controlled him and had him twist the minds of his entire estate, the device was painted over with the Vlaensk heraldry.

Notably, even if Baron Vilhelm was slain and his mind-control powers broken, none of his guards would allow anyone to remove Karl's shield, despite its usefulness in battle.
