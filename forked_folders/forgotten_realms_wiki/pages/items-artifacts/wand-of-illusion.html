Wand of illusion

Item
nameWand of Illusion
typeWand
value2e20,000 gp

A wand of illusion was a magic item that enabled the wielder to create visible and audible illusions.

Powers

This wand emitted an invisible ray that replicated the effects of the audible glamer and phantasmal force spells. The wielder needed to concentrate on the illusion to maintain it. A single charge was required for each component of the created illusion. Wands of illusion could be recharged.

Notable Owners

  • Heino, a gnome bard who traveled Faerûn, carried a wand of illusion.
  • Ternen Foxx, a human illusionist, and artist from Tethyr used a wand of illusion in his works.
