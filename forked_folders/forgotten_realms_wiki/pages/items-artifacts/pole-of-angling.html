Pole of angling

Item
namePole of Angling
typeWondrous item
locationFaerûn

A pole of angling was a magical 10-foot pole.

Powers

When a special command word was spoken, this long pole would transform into a fully functional fishing rod. The transformation included a hook, line, and a reel. When the command word was spoken again, the rod transformed back into a pole.
