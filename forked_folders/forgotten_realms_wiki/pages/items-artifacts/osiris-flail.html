Osiris' flail

Item
nameOsiris' Flail
typeFlail
creatorOsiris

Osiris' flail was a unique melee weapon wielded by the avatar of Osiris, the Lord of the Dead within the Mulhorandi pantheon.

Powers

This weapon carried a stout +3 enchantment. The magic of the weapon caused it to do twice as much physical harm as a regular flail. Additonally, Osiris' flail functioned as a mace of disruption.
