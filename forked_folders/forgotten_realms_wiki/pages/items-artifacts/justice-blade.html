A justice blade was a magic longsword made by the church of Tyr. Many of them had been forged.

Description

It appeared to be made from bronze that had been magically hardened. It was inscribed with the holy symbol of Tyr, a hammer and scales.

Powers

It was a +1 holy mighty cleaving longsword. The blade gave off a white light to about 20 ft (6 m) when drawn.
