Potion of animal speaking

Item
namePotion of Animal Speaking
typePotion
value5e50 gp

Potions of animal speaking were magical concoctions that allowed their imbibers to verbally speak with animals for a period of time.

Description

Potions of animal speaking smelled of hay, musk, and manure. The effects of this potion lasted for one day.

Creation

A potion of animal speaking could me created by mixing an essence of acorn truffles and certain types of alchemical salts.

Notable Users

  • Arron, a hin druid merchant from the Emerald Grove in the late 15 century DR.
  • Auntie Ethel, a hag disguised as a healer who lived in the Western Heartlands circa 1492 DR, brewed numerous potions, including potions of animal speaking.
  • Derryth Bonecloak, a dwarven potionmaker and merchant from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.
  • Volothamp Geddarm, a famous, or infamous writer, carried potions of animal speaking during his expedition into the Chionthar Valley and investigation of the Cult of the Absolute in the late 15 century DR.
