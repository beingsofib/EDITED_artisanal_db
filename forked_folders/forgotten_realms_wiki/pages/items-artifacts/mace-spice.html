Mace (spice)

Substance
nameMace
typeSpice
cost25 gp per oz

Mace was a valuable spice found in Faerûn.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold mace spice for 25 gp per , and mace-infused sugar for 35 gp per .

Usage

  • In addition to being used as a spice, mace could also used medicinally as a stimulant.
  • Halruaan hangman's noose breads were dusted with powdered mace, sugar, allspice, and cardamom.
  • On the Malatran Plateau in Kara-Tur, mace was used as a pickling spice, together with mustard seeds, black peppercorns, red chilis, cloves, allspice, coriander, and ginger.
  • Mace was one of the ingredients in the special enchanted ink used to scribe scrolls of shocking grasp. Mace (or masterwort), balm of gilead, ginseng, and asafetida were burnt into ash, mixed with ash of a tree incinerated by lightning, and mixed with the rest of ingredients.
