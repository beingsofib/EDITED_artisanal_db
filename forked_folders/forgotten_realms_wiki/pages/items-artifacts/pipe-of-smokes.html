Pipe of smokes

namePipe of Smokes

locationEvermeet

The pipe of smokes was a magic item from the island of Evermeet.

Description

This item resembled an ordinary set of musical pipes made from alabaster and duskwood.

Powers

A pip of smokes contained between 41–60 charges that could be used to create the following spell-like effects: darkness, 15' radius, wall of fog, sleep, stinking cloud, gust of wind, cloudkill, death fog, and incendiary cloud.
