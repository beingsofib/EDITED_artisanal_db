A starblade was a sword. To Kiril Duskmourn's recollection, Nangulis, a star elf from Sildëyuir, wielded one. She considered such weapons to be more useful than daggers when confronted with incorporeal enemies, and on the same level as flame tongue swords: she also thought to get one at some point.
