Orb of power

Item
nameOrb of Power
typeMagic item

An orb of power was an magic item that allowed identification of other magic items.

Description

The orb was a rare item. When placed in a corresponding position, it identified all of the items a person and their allies carried.

History

In 1358 DR, it could be found in the outer sanctum of Xanathar, an evil beholder crime lord.
