Cave moss was a moss found in the Underdark.

Ecology

Although many creatures, such as rothé and giant vermin, fed on cave moss, it was inedible to humanoids.

Locations

Cave moss grew solely in regions of faerzress in the Underdark.

Use

Drow and duergar learned to use cave moss to distill a potent non-deadly sleeping poison.
