Walnut oil was a type of vegetable oil in the Realms,

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold walnut oil at a standard price of two gold pieces per in its "Aurora's Larder" section, but also at a price of one gold piece per in its "Priest's Alcove" section under material components for divine magic spells.

Usages

Druids used this oil as a material component for the reflecting pool spell.
