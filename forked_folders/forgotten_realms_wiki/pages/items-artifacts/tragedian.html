The Tragedian was an immensely valuable, elegant, enchanted mask created by a master maskcrafter alongside its sister mask–the Comedian, sometime before the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The Tragedian was a powerful mask that significantly enhanced the wearer's oratory skills, such as diplomacy, intimidation, and taunting their enemies. Additionally, the mask allowed the wearer to cast a powerful version of the crushing despair spell.

History

The Tragedian and the Comedian masks were the creation of an unnamed master maskcrafter, during the time he joined a troupe of actors whilst traveling the land. To his dismay, the land was dangerous, and the troupe was in constant danger of being attacked by bandits and brigands, forcing them to take arms in self-defense. The master maskcrafter was a willful man, and he worked day and night, pouring all of his skills and magical prowess into a pair of enchanted masks. The masks were not just beautiful but also possessed great power. One night, the actors were captured by a local warlord to perform for the bandit king as much as the warlord pleased. The troupe's male lead adorned his face with the Tragedian and swayed the bandits with his honeyed words only to plunge them into a fit of cripping sadness. Then, the female lead used her mask, the Comedian, and the actors attacked the bandits, getting away from the kidnappers with enough of the stronghold's gold to consider it their payment. The bandits were left beaten and bruised, wondering if they had just watched the best play of their lives, or the worst.

By the , the Tragedian found its way into the hands of Magda, the proprietress of Mulsantir's Veil Theater.
