Ring of magic missiles

Item
nameRing of Magic Missiles
typeMagic ring
locationFaerûn, Toril

A ring of magic missiles was a magical ring that allowed the wearer to shoot forth arcane projectiles. It functioned in a manner identical to the wand of magic missiles.

In the years around the Time of Troubles, this type of ring possessed 24 charges.

Notable owners

  • Ahbhaer Mhilshoun, a Calishite merchant and business-owner
