A star of Ojy-do was a magical shuriken forged by Ojy of Golden in Koryo in Kara-Tur.

Description

Two distinct versions of the stars of Ojy-do were recorded; one three hooked blades around a circular center and the other had four blades joined at the center.

Powers

These weapons were +1 shuriken that inflicted freezing cold on their targets.

History

When bandits besieged the village of Golden, the villagers were forced to defend themselves. The spiritual guide Ojy was pressed into work as a weaponsmith, though he knew little of metalworking. With every accurate strike of his hammer, he thanked his guiding spirits, and so the stars of Ojy-do were forged from the strength of his faith. They proved just as resilient.

By 1372 DR, stars of Ojy-do circulated around the northwest Faerûn and the Sword Coast North. As of the , Stars of Ojy-do were available for purchase from Okuzo's Imports of Westgate, along with numerous magical items exported from the eastern realms by the immigrant shop owner.
