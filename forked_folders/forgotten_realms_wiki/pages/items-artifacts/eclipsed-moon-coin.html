An eclipsed moon was a coin minted by the city of Silverymoon in the late 15 century DR.

It was crafted by stamping a standard shining blue electrum moon together with a darker silver wedge to complete a round coin. In the late 1480s and early 1490s DR, the eclipsed moon was valued at 5 unicorns (platinum pieces) in Silverymoon, or 2 unicorns elsewhere.
