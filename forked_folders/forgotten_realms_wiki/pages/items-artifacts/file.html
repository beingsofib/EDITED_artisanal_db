Files were tools in the Realms used to cut through metal or wood.

Description

A typical set of files included a variety of shapes and sizes.

History

Files were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Notable Users

  • Files were a common tool among thieves and rogues, often being included in sets of thieves' tools and in keymaking sets. They were also often sewn into the hems of clothing by rogues to aid them in escaping jail if they ever caught.
