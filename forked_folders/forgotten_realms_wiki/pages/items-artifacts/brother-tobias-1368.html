Brother Tobias' 1368 was a popular and good quality wine vintage from Westgate's Brother Tobias Winery.

Description

The 1368 vintage was distributed in big-bottomed glass decanters. The wine itself had a noticeable purple hue.

History

By the late 14 century DR, this vintage was popular in many of Westgate's inns and drinking establishments, including Black Eye Tavern and Bent Mermaid Inn. Most considered 1368 DR to an especially good year for Brother Tobias' wines.
