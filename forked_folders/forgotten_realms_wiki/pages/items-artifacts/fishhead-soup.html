Fishhead soup was a simple type of soup found in Turmish and the city of Velen in Tethyr.

Availability

  • In Turmish, fishhead soup was a staple of humble households in the nation's coastal areas, alongside eel pies, chives and asparagus (locally better known as "greenspears" or "orsauda").
  • Especially nauseating fishhead soup, as per Volothamp Geddarm, was served alongside frybread and firecakes at The Figureheadless tavern in Velen.
