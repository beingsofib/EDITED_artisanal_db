The Sea Horse was a huge dromond and part of the Wraith of the Inner Sea privateer fleet in the mid–14 century DR. Based out of Reth, the fleet prowled the Chessentan coast, the Wizards' Reach, and Inner Sea.

By 1357 DR, it was captained by Morgalshym and its first mate was Brawn, with Curnetheres as the chief mage. It had a complement of two-hundred crewmembers, thirty marines, and five wizards or fifty marines and six other wizards.
