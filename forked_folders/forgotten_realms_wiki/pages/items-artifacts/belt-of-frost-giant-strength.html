A belt of frost giant strength, also known as a girdle of frost giant strength, was a magical belt that granted the wearer tremendous physical strength.

Description

These belts were rather plain-looking in appearance They were considered amongst the middling variations of belts of giant strength.

Powers

The belt or girdle would imbue the wearer with the strength, though neither size nor girth, of a frost giant.
