Safflower oil was a type of vegetable oil in the Realms, derived from the plant of the same name.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold safflower oil at the standard price of two silver pieces per in its "Aurora's Larder" section.

Notable Users

  • Madame Freona's Tea Kettle, a restaurant in Phlan, utilized safflower oil in some of its dishes.
