Melairkyn baldrics were a variety of enchanted baldrics developed by the dwarves of Clan Melairkyn.

Description

These baldrics were made of mithril in a chain mail style and had Clan Melairkyn's symbol as a seal.

Powers

These baldrics granted their wearers a +2 protective enchantment, as well as the ability to understand both Dwarvish and its written form.
