A wild magic stone was a rock with magical properties which presumably related to wild magic.

Description

One wild magic stone was a small, sparkling pebble.

Powers

A stone employed in brought down an antimagic field. It had a further radius of effect; the mage who employed it made sure to remain outside its radius.

History

One wild magic stone was employed by Azmyrandyr of Thay, a Red Wizard, against Alustriel Silverhand in .
