Goblin Hunter was a unique enchanted dagger designed specifically for hunting goblins of Icewind Dale, created by Elytharra Dinnesmore of Targos.

Description

Goblin Hunter had a delicate pearl handle, and its blade looked more like a hunting knife than a weapon.

Powers

The blade's enchantments were especially dangerous to goblins as its edge cut deeper into the creatures' hides. Functionally, the dagger became a +2 enchanted weapon when wielded against goblins.

History

Goblin Hunter was created sometime before the , by the enchantress Elytharra Dinnesmore, the wife of Ulbrec Dinnesmore, the speaker of Targos at the time. She created the magic blade as a way to defend herself from goblin raids that menaced the settlements of Icewind Dale. When asked about Goblin Hunter, Elytharra claimed that the weapon was used only a handful of times. As of 1312 DR, the enchantress was willing to part with Goblin Hunter for the right price.

In the , Elytharra's Goblin Hunter belonged to Graendel Granitefist of Graendel's Fine Dwarven Craft in Targos and was available for sale.
