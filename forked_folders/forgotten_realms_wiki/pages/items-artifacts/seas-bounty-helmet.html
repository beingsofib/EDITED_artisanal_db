Sea's Bounty (helmet)

Item
nameSea's Bounty
typeHelmet
locationNelanther Isles, Sea of Swords

Sea's Bounty was a variety of enchanted helmets imbued with the lost knowledge.

Powers

It was said that when someone wore Sea's Bounty, they received a portion of the ancient secrets and knowledge thought long lost in the sea. The helmet improved the wearer's wisdom and gave them knowledge of appraisal and lore. Additionally, when worn, Sea's Bounty glowed in gentle blue radiance, illuminating the area of around it.

History

As of the late 14 center DR, Sea's Bounty could be found on the Nelanther Isles off the Sword Coast, sold by Sister Tasina of the Temple of Umberlee in Spindrift Town.
