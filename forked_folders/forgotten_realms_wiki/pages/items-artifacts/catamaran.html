Catamarans were a type of watercraft design that used twinned hulls instead of one, and could scale from small boats to large ships.

Description

By using two hulls together, a catamaran boat or ship achieved better stability and speed, though maneuvrability was more limited. It also suffered a lack of space inside the hull or atop the deck, reducing its capacity for passengers or cargo.

Notable Catamarans

  • The naval catamarans of Evermeet were fast boats carried by their larger warships, and were used for scouting and light combat duties.
  • Flying catamarans were delicate craft used as pleasure boats in low orbit or as launches from larger spelljammers.
  • A small catamaran boat was used by Mhair in service of the secret cell of Sharrans at the Temple of Mystra.
