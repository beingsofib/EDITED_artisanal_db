Rings of flinging were enchanted rings that improved the wearer's skills and aim with flinging and throwing.

Powers

Some of these rings were bands inlaid with rubies. The ring made the wearer stronger and more powerful when throwing things at their opponents, dealing increased damage.

History

Historically, rings of flinging were used by organizations like the Harpers, who often preferred to stay out of a blade's reach and often found themselves chasing or being chased across the rooftops during moonlit nights. These rings allowed the Harper agents to end wrongdoers with good aim and a loose roof tile. In the , one such ring was being offered for sale by Arron, a lightfoot halfling druid traders of the Emerald Grove in the Western Heartlands.
