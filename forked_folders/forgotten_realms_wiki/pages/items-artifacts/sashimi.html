Sashimi

Item
nameSashimi
typeSeafood

Sashimi was a variety of seafood in the Realms, consisting of freshly raw fish that had been finely chopped.

Availability

  • In the land of Kara-Tur, one could find this dish on the island nation of Kozakura and in the Hai Yuan Province of Shou Lung, whose people had learned it from the Kozakurans.
  • In Faerûn, one could find sashimi being served in the underwater restaurant Sharkey's Bar & Grill.
