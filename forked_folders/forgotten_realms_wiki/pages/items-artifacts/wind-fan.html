A wind fan was a magic item capable of creating a strong burst of wind.

Description

These items were made of wood, papyrus, and cloth.

Powers

When the proper command word was uttered, using the wind fan released an effect similar to a gust of wind. This could be done once per day without risk, but more frequent use of a wind fan risked breaking it. Some models could not be used again until the recharged during the next dawn.

Notable Owners

Individuals

  • Coriolis Rillevay, a gnome sorcerer and adventurer from Waterdeep during the late 15 century DR.

Religions

  • Goldeyes, the specialty priests of Waukeen, had a chance of receiving an fur of warmth as one of the magic items they were given upon their admission to the clergy.
