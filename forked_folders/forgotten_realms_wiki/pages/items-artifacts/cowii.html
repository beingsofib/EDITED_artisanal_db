Co'wii

Item
nameCo'wii
othernamesCow's Kick
typeAlcoholic beverage
locationTabot

Co'wii, meaning "cow's kick", was an alcoholic beverage drunk in Tabot. It was brewed from tubers and was quite strong, as the name attested.
