Emperor's mail shirt was a masterwork scalemail shirt created by some of the most experienced mailsmiths of Neverwinter, the City of Skilled Hands, in the late 15 century DR.

Description

An emperor's mail shirt was created from a high-quality, ornate scale shirt crafted of mithral rings and scales by Neverwintran mailsmiths. Once forged, the shirt was ensorcelled with the use of an extremely valuable magical ingredient: a dragon egg, which was not an egg, but rather a variety of a massive gemstone. Once the process was done, an emperor's mail shirt was so light and comfortable that the wearer felt like they were wearing nothing at all. Its magics also rendered the shirt almost completely invisible.

History

These masterworks of the mailsmithing art were known to be created by some of the best craftsmen of Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.
