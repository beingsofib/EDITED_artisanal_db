Warhammers of Death were enchanted dwarven two-handed warhammers from the city of Myth Drannor.

Description

The bludgeon was cast out of black steel.

Powers

Warhammer of Death possessed a +2 enchantment that became a dramatically powerful magic weapon when wielded against undead creatures. One such weapon also possessed the power to cast the poison spell at will.

History

After the fall of Myth Drannor in the Weeping War, Warhammers of Death could be found in the Great Hall of the Dwarven Dungeons. In the , one of these black bludgeons was wielded by an orc shaman who served Mol the Great, who led the tribe that claimed part of the Dwarven Dungeons as its territory.
