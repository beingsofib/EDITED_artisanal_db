A ruby pendant of beguiling was a ruby pendant with the powers of a rod of beguiling.

Effects

The pendant was very powerful. Any creature within 30 feet (9 meters) of the pendant would feel the effects of a charm monster spell. The enchantment remained as long as the creature was within sight of the pendant.

Notable examples

Pasha Pook once owned a ruby pendant, and it was a key to his move to the top of the Calimport criminal world. The gem was stolen by Regis, who left the criminal world for Icewind Dale, where he used the ruby to gain the upper hand in the negotiation in Ten Towns. Artemis Entreri was sent to find the gem and the hin and get them back to Pook.
