Lady Scepter

Item
nameLady Scepter
typeScepter
locationMoonshorn Tower

The Lady Scepter was one of the possessions of the Guardian of Moonshorn Tower, a temple to Mystra.

Description

The scepter could flash, and be worn on the head somehow.

Powers

The last person to hurl the scepter had been disintegrated on the spot.

History

The mage Mardasper Oblyndrin wielded the Lady Scepter during his guardianship of Moonshorn Tower. He'd seen the last wielder be disintegrated for hurling the scepter. It could also flash, sometimes on command, and sometimes as a sign from the goddess Mystra.
