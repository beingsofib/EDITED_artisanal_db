Sacred urn

Item
nameSacred Urn
typeMagical item
locationUtter East

A sacred urn was a holy vessel used in the Utter East during the Bloodforge Wars (648–657 DR).

When carried by a creature such as a basal golem who was subsequently slain, their essence was saved and transported back to their controlling bloodforge mage in the form of mana.
