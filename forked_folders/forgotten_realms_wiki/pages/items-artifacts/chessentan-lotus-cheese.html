Chessentan Lotus Cheese was a popular cheese crafted in Chessenta. It was also sold through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Description

This novelty cheese was mixed with petals from many flowers before the Chessentans finally settled on lotus for its exotic qualities. It was particularly popular among the Old Empires, not only in Chessenta but in Mulhorand and Unther as well.
