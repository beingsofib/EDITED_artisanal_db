Goldengulp

Item
nameGoldengulp
typeBeer
locationGoldenfields, Sword Coast North

Goldengulp was a local beer made within a brewery in the settlement of Goldenfields on the Sword Coast North in the late 15 century DR. It was made with the finest local barley and hops.
