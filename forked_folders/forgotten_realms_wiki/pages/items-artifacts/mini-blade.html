Mini-blade

Item
nameMini-blade
typeBlade, tool
value1 sp

The mini-blade was a tiny blade that was easily concealed between the knuckles.

Uses

Mini-blades practically gave the cutpurse their name, allowing a brash thief to slice the strings of an unwary victim's coin purse with ease.

Prices

Mini-blades could be purchased for 1 Silver.
