The banner of the krig rune was a rare magical banner.

Description

The banner was made from a thick fabric that was dyed red. It was large, with dimensions of in height and in width. In the middle of the banner was a prominently displayed the krig rune, which stood for "war". The banner was often mounted on a pole and used as a standard.

Powers

When unfurled, the banner had several magical properties. It provided a temporary immunity to fear for the bearer and any ally within of the banner. It also allowed the bearer to see invisible creatures and also protected the bearer and their allies from ranged attacks. Additionally, the banner's ability to provide immunity to fear could be permanently affixed to an area. The bearer drew a krig rune in the desired location. The process was lengthy, up to eight hours, and required the banner to be within of the rune. The banner was destroyed during the process.
