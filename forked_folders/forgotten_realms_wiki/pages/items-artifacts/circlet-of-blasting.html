A circlet of blasting was a delicate magical circlet that had the power to sear and burn a number of targets.

Powers

The wearer of a circlet of blasting could use its power to cast the scorching ray spell, once per day. The circlet regained its power at the following dawn. Pre-Spellplague versions unleashed the searing light spell instead.

History

A weaker variation of these items, minor circlets of blasting, were recovered from agents of the Zhentarim sometime shortly before the . These circlets were taken from the Bedine nomads of the Anauroch, suggesting that the items' true origins were the ancient Netherese empire.

Notable Owners

  • Blurg: A bugbear member of the Society of Brilliance who resided in a peaceful myconid colony in the Underdark circa 1492 DR.
  • Raggadragga: The wereboar warlord of the Goreguts Gang wore a circlet of blasting.
