Mattock

Item
nameMattock

A mattock was used primarily as a tool for digging although it could be used as a weapon. The head of a mattock was a combination of a pick and an adze.

Variants

Hand tock: Small mattocks with short handles. Were common around Delzimmer, where adolescents used them to harvest braskh roots.

Notable Owners of Mattocks

Individuals

  • The goliath warrior Tobin used a huge mattock as his primary weapon.

Religions

  • Dumathoin owned a great, two-handed mattock with a +5 enchantment that was made of solidified magma.
