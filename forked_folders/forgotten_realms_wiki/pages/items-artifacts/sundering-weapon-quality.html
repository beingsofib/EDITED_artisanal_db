Sundering (weapon quality)

Item
nameSundering
typeWeapon quality
casterlevel3e10th
value3e+1

Sundering was a weapon quality that could be applied to bludgeoning or slashing weapons.

Power

A weapon imbued with the sundering property caused additional harm as the magic attempted to shatter whatever it came in contact with.

Creation

A cleric used the shatter spell to add the sundering effect to a weapon.
