Boar dumplings

Item
nameBoar Dumplings
typeFood
locationArnise Hold, Amn

Boar dumplings was one of the staples served at the Arnise Hold to its guests in the late 14 century DR.

Description

These dumplings were made using wild boar meat. The dish was considered the menu's mainstay due to its low price and popularity among those of humble means. Boar dumplings could be found in the Arnise Hold's rustic guesthouse, seven along other surprisingly good foods.
