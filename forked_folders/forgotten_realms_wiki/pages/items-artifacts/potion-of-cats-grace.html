Potions of cat's grace were common magical concoctions that increased the imbiber's dexterity.

Powers

Potions of cat's grace greatly increased the drinker's dexterity, similarly to the effects of the cat's grace spell.

History

Circa 1369 DR, the ruins of Myth Drannor still held magical potions left behind by the remnants of the city. Potions of cat's grace in delicate crystal decanter were among them., and as far as the Nelanther Isles.

Circa 1373 DR, potions of cat's grace were part of a standard equipment for the wizards and agents of Thay.

Notable Owners

  • Thraka, a half-orc mercenary in Sildëyuir in 1372 DR.
  • Varzek, a drow cleric of Vhaeraun who operated in Cormanthor circa 1373 DR.
