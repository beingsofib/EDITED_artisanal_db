Velveteen

Substance
nameVelveteen
typeFabric
usesClothing

Velveteen was a type of fabric found in the Realms, often used in the crafting of clothing.

Usage

Articles of clothing velveteen was used in crafting included armwraps, headdresses, hoods, jackets, pants, sashes, and shirts.

Notable Users

The wizard Jazrac owned robes that were made of velveteen.
