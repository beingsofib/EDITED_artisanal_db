Arundoon sauce

Item
nameArundoon Sauce
typeSauce
locationLost Peaks, High Forest

Arundoon sauce was a condiment which paired well with people.

Use

Arundoon sauce was to be applied prior to cooking people, preferably alive.

History

The lich Thalon had a preserved decanter of arundoon sauce in his lair as of the . He poured it on Laeral Rythkyn, a Harper wizard, before cooking her.
