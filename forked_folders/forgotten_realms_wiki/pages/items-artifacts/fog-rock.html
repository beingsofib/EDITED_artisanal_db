Fog rocks were small, naturally magical stones that were known to wash up on the western shores of islands in the Sea of Swords.

Description

These rocks apparently manifested in the Trackless Sea, and could be found along the western coasts of the Moonshae Isles and the Nelanther Isles. Sages theorized that they collected magic directly from the Weave via some unknown but natural process.

Powers

When dropped into fresh water, the stones produced a cloud of thick mist, which then dispersed normally. They did not produce this effect in salt water, and they had to be kept damp and salty to maintain their magic.
