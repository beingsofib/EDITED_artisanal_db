Billhooks were a type of agricultural tool in the Realms that could double as an improvised weapon.

Description

Billhooks had wooden shafts and iron heads that consisted of a hook and a sharp, barbed tip.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold billhooks, The catalogue advertised the tool as being useful for removing the loose thatch off of a roof in the case of a fire and for attacking vermin that nested in thatched roofs.
  • The Iron Bull Smithy in Ravens Bluff sold billhooks for 5 gold pieces.
  • The Moonlight Pawnshop, a pawnbrokerage in Ravens Bluff, sold billhooks alongside other common farming tools.

History

The combination polearm known as the bill-guisarme was developed from this tool.

Notable Users

  • Harginn were often known to fight with long billhooks.
  • Some in the Blade Kingdoms were known to use their billhooks as weapons.

Trivia

A billhook featured prominently in the story, "The Adventure of the Battledale Billhook".
