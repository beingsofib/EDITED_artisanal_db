Boots of the willed

Item
nameBoots of the Willed
typeBoots
originChult, Chultan Peninsula

The boots of the willed was a pair of enchanted boots from the Chultan city of Port Nyanzaru ceated in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The wearer of these boots received the boon of a slight boost to their physical power, defense, and vitality. Additionally, the boots empowered all magical and mundane abilities available to the adventurers at will.

History

The boots of the willed were created as a reward for the adventurers who managed to slay the great and fearsome Tyrant of Omu, one of the powerful beasts that dwelt within the Jungles of Chult.
