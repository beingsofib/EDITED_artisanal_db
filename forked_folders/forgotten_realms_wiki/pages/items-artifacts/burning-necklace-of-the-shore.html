Burning necklace of the shore

Item
nameBurning Necklace of the Shore
typeAmulet
locationNeverwinter, Sword Coast North

Burning necklaces of the shore were powerful enchanted amulets crafted for the survivors of the battle on the shores of the isle of Tuern in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

These pieces of magical jewelry were imbued with various enchantments that boosted the wearer's vitality, physical power, as well as the time of their natural recovery and regeneration. When a burning necklace of the shore was worn together with a draconic ring of the shore and a barbaric ring of the shore, all three items boosted each other's enchantments, granting the wearer a bigger boost to their physical power, as well and enhanced their physical defense and speed of movement.

History

These items were created as a reward for the defenders and adventurers of Neverwinter, who risked their lives in a battle that took place in the 1480s DR. The Cult of the Dragon on the isle of Tuern was met with Lords' Alliance forces as well as volunteer adventurers. The survivors were rewarded with burning necklaces, draconic rings, and barbaric rings.
