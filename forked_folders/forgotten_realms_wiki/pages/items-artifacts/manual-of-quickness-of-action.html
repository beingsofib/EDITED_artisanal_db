Manual of quickness of action

Item
nameManual of Quickness of Action
typeMagic book
value2e50,000 gp

A manual of quickness of action was a powerful magical tome that could make the attentive reader more agile and dexterous.

Powers

One who studied the text found detailed instructions for physical exercises and recipes for medicinal unguents that could be read and memorized in three days, at which point it disappeared. If these practices were applied dutifully for one month, the user found their quickness and dexterity much improved.

History

  • The book-collecting lich Adzerak held a manual in his library collection at Barrenstone.
