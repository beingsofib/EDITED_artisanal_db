Gohar's Galloping Grog was an alcoholic drink brewed in the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale.

Availability

Gohar's Galloping Grog was named after its creator – Gohar, the portly proprietor of the The Salty Dog tavern in the town of Targos circa the . Gohar served his namesake specialty concoction to his guests for two gold pieces per customer.
