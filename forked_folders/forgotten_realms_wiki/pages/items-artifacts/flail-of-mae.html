The Flail of Mae was a unique enchanted morning star wielded by Eled the Desecrator sometime before 1281 DR.

Description

The Flail of Mae appeared to be an ordinary morning star, however, unlike other similar weapons, it was made out of solid granite.

Powers

This weapon possessed a powerful +3 enchantment and had a small chance of petrifying its targets upon a successful strike.

History

The Flail of Mae was created by a group of evil medusae and maedar as the payment to Eled the Desecrator, a warrior who was a crusader and defender of all malicious creatures. Eled used the morning star with terrifying efficiency until the end of his life sometime before the . Eventually, the weapon found its way into the hands of Quinn Silverfinger, a cleric of Waukeen in the Golden Lodge of Lonelywood.
