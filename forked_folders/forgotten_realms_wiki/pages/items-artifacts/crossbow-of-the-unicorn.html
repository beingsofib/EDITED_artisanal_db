Crossbows of the unicorn were enchanted light crossbows created in the town of New Mardale.

Powers

Crossbows of the unicorn possessed powerful +5 enchantments, and while a person held one of these weapons, they could move with a breakneck speed as if affected by the haste spell.

History

These crossbows were created sometime long before the late 14 century DR by the ingenious inhabitants of New Mardale to be used against an evil enchanter. The story claimed that the woman who weaved magics into these crossbows was inspired after witnessing a beautiful unicorn at the edge of town. She created spells that represented the celestial creature's purity and defense and enchanted the first crossbows of the unicorn.

As of the , some of these powerful items could be found in the hands of merchants and adventurers along the Sword Coast North and the Moonsea region.
