Nullscent was an alchemical concoction that masked scents.

Powers

It removed any lingering scents the user would ordinarily leave behind. It had to be applied liberally to the user's body, covering it completely to work.

History

The synthesis of this reagent was complex, requiring both chemical and herbal knowledge in order to brew correctly. Hedge wizards often traded in it. It sold for about 1,200 gold pieces.

Reputation

It was popular with thieves.
