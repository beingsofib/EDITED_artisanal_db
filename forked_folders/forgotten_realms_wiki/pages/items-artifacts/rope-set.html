A rope set was a tool in the Realms used to climb surfaces.

Description

Rope sets included a variety of grapples and pulleys with of elven-made triple-stranded silk rope. A set weighed only .

History

Rope sets were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 40 gold pieces and included a satchel.

Notable Users

  • Rope sets were favored by thieves or rogues to aid them in scaling walls.
