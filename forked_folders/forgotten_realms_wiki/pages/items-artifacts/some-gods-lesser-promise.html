Some God's Lesser Promise was a unique and enigmatic enchanted short sword from the citadel of Seldarine's Hand on the Spine of the World.

Description

Some God's Lesser Promise appeared to be a simple sword, basically a double-edged dagger with a blade of and a pointed tip. The blade's surface was etched with arcane symbols that radiated power.

Powers

This ordinary-looking blade possessed a +2 enchantment and upon a successful strike, it had a chance of dispensing small amount of magical healing onto its owner.

History

Most of Some God's Lesser Promise's remained a mystery. However, the sword first appeared in 1281 DR, when it could be found in the ruins of an ancient elven citadel of Seldarine's Hand, more commonly known in that time period as the Severed Hand.
