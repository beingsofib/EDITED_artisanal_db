The Heavenly Bow was an exotic enchanted longbow once wielded by solars of the Celestial planes.

Description

Everything about the Heavenly Bow was perfection. It's mere creation was unachievable by any of the most skilled mortal hands. The materials used for its creation were unknown to anyone on the Prime Material plane. Because of these things, it was believed that this powerful bow is of Upper planes origins.

Powers

This huge longbow was imbued with a potent +6 enchantment. This weapon dramatically increased damage done with critical lucky hits, allowed even an unexperienced wielder to land perfect point-blank shots, and rapidly shower the targets with arrows. History

Long before 1374 DR, the Heavenly Bow was sealed underneath a chasm-ridden plane in Thar, not far from the swamps of Iyrauroth. This celestial bow was wielded by the unnamed fallen solar who was the reason for the chasms that scarred the land around her magical prison.
