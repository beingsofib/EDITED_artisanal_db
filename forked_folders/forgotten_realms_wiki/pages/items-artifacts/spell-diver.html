Spell Diver was a unique enchanted short sword especially useful against spellcasters.

Description

The sword's ricasso was stamped with the image of a kingfisher.

Powers

Spell Diver possessed a +2 enchantment and granted the wielder resistance to magic. Additionally, the sword's targets had a coin-toss of a chance to lose their ability to use magic for 12 seconds.

History

Unlike many other magic items, Spell Diver did not have a long history veiled in mystery and grim happenstances. It belonged to Naradon, a mercenary from Thesk who earned his fame for being able to quickly and efficiently dispatch magic-users even when faced with overwhelming odds. Naradon retired due to old age due to excessive use of potions of speed, at the age of thirty-two. He died of old age four years later, and his only heir had no use for Spell Diver. The magic sword was sold to an infamous cat burglar. By the , Spell Diver was stored in the snaking chambers of marilith Yxunomei, inside the Dragon's Eye caverns on the Spine of the World.
