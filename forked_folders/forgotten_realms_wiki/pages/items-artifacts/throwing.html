Throwing was a quality that could be applied to any melee weapon, enabling it to be thrown.

Power

A weapon with this property could be hurled effectively at enemies up to if the wielder was proficient with it normally.

Creation

To craft a throwing weapon, a spellcaster had to have magic stone available.

Examples

  • Craftmaster, the personal weapon of the craftsman god Gond, was a +4 impact returning seeking throwing warhammer.
