Harvest cloaks were enchanted cloaks, the prize awarded annually to the winning team of West Harbor's Harvest Fair competition.

Description

Harvest cloaks were masterfully crafted, simple but beautiful green cloaks.

Powers

These clothing items were imbued with a simple charisma boosting enchantment that accentuated Harvest cloaks' beauty, drew the eye, and complimented any outfit paired with them.

History

In 1374 DR, a Harvest cloak and the Harvest Cup were awarded the competition's winners, Amie Fern, Bevil Starling, and Kalach-Cha. Following a devastating attack on the town of West Harbor, Kalach-Cha took the Harvest cloak out of the Mere of Dead Men and headed to Neverwinter.
