Belth

Item
nameBelth
typeFood
originMoonsea North, North Faerûn

Belths were cooked trail foods, popular in the Moonsea North, alongside sausages and hardloaves.

Description

A belth was a large pickle, wrapped in a jacket of crumbly dry pastry, made with copious amounts of lard or suet. Belths then were baked, sealing the juices in and keeping the pickles from spoiling.
