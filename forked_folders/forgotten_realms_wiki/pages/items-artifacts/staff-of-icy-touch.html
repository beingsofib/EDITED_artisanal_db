Staff of icy touch

Item
nameStaff of Icy Touch
typeMagic staff
rarity5eUncommon

The staff of icy touch was an enchanted staff used in combat. It could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The staff of icy touch fired a ray of frost, but had a good chance of spontaneously releasing two additional rays, each targeting a different enemy of the wielder.

Notable Owners

  • Shevarith Kendia, a rashemi wizard who worked as an adventurer on behalf of Laeral Silverhand, the Open Lord of Waterdeep, during the late 15 century DR.
