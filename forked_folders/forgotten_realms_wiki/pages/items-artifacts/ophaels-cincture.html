Ophael's cincture was the personal belt of the marilith Commander Ophael, left in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

The belt helped with cunning and deceptive speech in conversation, and landing deep and deadly blows in combat.

History

After a period of enjoying her fashionwear, Ophael lost her cincture when she was banished back to the Abyss after facing the Cormanthor Elves in battle. She had been summoned by the Red Wizards of Thay under Kathul Sep to defend an old Cult of the Dragon base in Myth Drannor, where they attempted to subvert and control the half-born dracolich Karliath.
