Breathstoppers and their more powerful variants, exalted Breathstoppers, were a variety of artifact shields created to be used by fighter adventurers in the Chultan city of Port Nyanzaru in the late 15 century DR.

Description

These Breathstoppers were testaments of their owners' strength and the skills of the master weaponcrafters of Chult. Those heroes who survived the deadly dangers of the jungles were honored with these enchanted shields, paired with Breathtakers – enchanted swords.

Powers

A Breathstopper could be enchanted to hold various unique magics for each owner, in addition to boosting the damage inflicted by the owner in combat as well as increasing their ability to deflect enemy strikes. When a Breathtaker was wielded with a Breathstopper, both items boosted each other's magical powers, increasing the wielder's damage output, resistance to all incoming damage, and boosting their healing capabilities.

History

Breathstoppers were created in the late 15 century DR, and their design suggested the items' origins to be tied to the ancient Lost City of Omu.
