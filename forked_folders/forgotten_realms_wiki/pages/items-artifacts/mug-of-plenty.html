A mug of plenty, also known as a tankard of plenty, was a large, magical drinking vessel used for consuming ale and other potent potables.

Powers

With the proper command word, the mug would fill to the brim with light ale, dark ale, or mead as the user desired. Speaking the word again after emptying the mug's contents rewarded the drinker with free refills, the only apparent limitation on use being equal to the imbiber's tolerance level.

Reputation

This was perhaps the most sought after magical object in the Moonshaes.

Notables Owners

  • Raggnar Redtooth, owner of the Golden Tankard tavern in Parnast.
