Auquhol's hourglass

Item
nameAuquhol's Hourglass
creatorAuquhol
weight2eType I, II: 10,000 gp
Type III: 40,000 gp

Auquhol's hourglass was a type of magical hourglass that extended the duration of spells, as with extension.

They used powdered nilbog bones as their sands.
