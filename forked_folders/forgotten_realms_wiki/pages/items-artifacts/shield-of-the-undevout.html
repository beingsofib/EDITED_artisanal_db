The Shield of the Undevout was an ornate enchanted shield that was once wielded by a blackguard and later by a death knight they became.

Powers

The Shield of the Undevout possessed a +2 protective enchantment. Additionally, it had the dreadful hewer enchantment that made the wielder's spells and abilities that caused fear dramatically easy to take hold of the opponents' hearts. Lastly, the shield also expanded the wielder's magical capabilities, allowing them to memorize one additional simple spell of an arcane of divine nature.

History

In the , blackguard's greaves were worn by the guardian death knight called That Which Guards, who, along with That Which Guards and That Which Lurks, guarded the entrance to the chamber of the Bhaalist Murder Tribunal in the Undercellar of Baldur's Gate.
