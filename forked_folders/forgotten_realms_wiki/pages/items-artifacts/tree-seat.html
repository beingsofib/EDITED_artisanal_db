Tree seat

Item
nameTree seat
typeFurniture
value15 gp

Tree seats were handy items used by rangers and others who spent time travelling through the wilds of Faerûn.

Uses

With a tree seat, a ranger could turn any sturdy tree into his own fortress or watchtower. Using a tree seat almost always guaranteed that the user was not surprised by an enemy. These items were made from duskwood and had steel claws that never slipped.

DISCLAIMER: Use of this item on treants voided the manufacturer's warranty.

Prices

A tree seat cost 15 gold pieces from Aurora's Emporium.
