Chopped sauce, known on Earth as chutney, was a variety of spicy sauce in the Realms.

It was typically made of a mix of fruits, vegetables, and spices, such as sour apple and spiced lime or tomato, onion, and hot spices.

Notable Chopped Sauces

  • The Last Jack, an inn in the city of Ravens Bluff, was known for its fiery tomato and onion chutney.
