The Heart's Splinter was an enchanted rapier found in Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.

Description

The Heart's Splinter had an intricately decorated hilt and guard that had an gem stone at the design's center.

Powers

The Heart's Splinter's magic allowed the owner to attack their opponents at a distance as well as in melee range. Additionally, upon a successful hit, the target could be affected by magical paralyzing fear, incapacitating them for a short period of time.

Notable Owners

  • Calliope: a bard adventurer owned this rapier during her time working with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
