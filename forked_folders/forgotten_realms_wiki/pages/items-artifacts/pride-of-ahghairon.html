Pride of Ahghairon

Ship
namePride of Ahghairon
typeShip
locationWaterdeep, Sea of Swords

The Pride of Ahghairon was a famous ship of Waterdeep on the Sea of Swords from the city's early maritime history.

By the early 14 century DR, a beautiful and detailed model of the Pride of Ahghairon was displayed in the hall of ships in the House of the Moon temple to Selûne in Waterdeep.
