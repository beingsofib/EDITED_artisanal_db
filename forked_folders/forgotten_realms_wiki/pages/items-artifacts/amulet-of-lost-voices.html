The amulet of lost voices allowed one to commune with the dead.

Description

The amulet featured the holy symbol of Jergal set upon a beaded chain.

Powers

The amulet granted its wearer with the power to converse with the dead multiple times each day and ask a handful of questions of their subject. This power would only work when directed at the remains of intelligent creatures that were recently deceased.

Interestingly enough, the recently deceased often refused to have conversations with their killers.

History

As of the , one amulet of lost voices was found interred with the undead being known as Withers.
