Just Reward was the personal weapon of the avatars of Osiris, the Mulhorandi deity of death.

Description

This weapon resembled a ceremonial flail. Accounts varied on whether it was equivalent to a light horseman's flail or a heavy footman's flail, though the harm it caused was far greater than both.

Powers

Just Reward carried a +3 enchantment and had the same powers as a mace of disruption.
