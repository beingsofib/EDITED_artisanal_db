Wand of shatter

Item
nameWand of Shatter
typeWand
locationMyth Drannor, Cormanthor

Wands of shatter were aptly-named magic wands that allowed the wielder to cast shatter spell.

Powers

Wands of shatter held up to ten charges of the spell and could discharge it at will.
