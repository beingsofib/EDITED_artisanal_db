Sapphire pendants of mage armor were enchanted pieces of jewelry worn by the aarakocra elders of Kir Sabal in the late 15 century DR.

Description

These enchanted amulets were inlaid with a single deep-blue sapphire.

Powers

Sapphire pendants of mage armor protected the wearer from magical attacks and enhanced the wearer's defensive spellcasting abilities. Additionally, the sapphire pendants allowed the wearer to cast the mage armor spell.

Notable Owners

  • Asharra: the aarakocra wizard leader of Kir Sabal in the late 15 century DR.
