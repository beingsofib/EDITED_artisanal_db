Querns were scientific items in the Realms often used by wizards to grind substances into fine powder at a much faster rate than if they used a mortar and pestle.

Description

A quern resembled a much smaller version of a miller's wheel that could be operated by hand via a small handle located at the top.

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 20 gold pieces.

Notable Users

  • Querns were favored by wizards.
