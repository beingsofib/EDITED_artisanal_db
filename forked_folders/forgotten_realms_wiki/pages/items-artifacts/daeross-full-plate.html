Daeros's full plate was a dwarven-crafted suit of full plate armor worn by Daeros Dragonspear, builder of Dragonspear Castle.

Description

While it was crafted from heavy steel the breastplate was notable light. The front of the armor bore a detailed relief of flying copper dragon, presumably Daeros's companion Halatathlaer.

It was believed to have been made by the dwarves of Kanaglym.

Powers

The suit of +1 full plate mail protected the wearer from fire and the devastating breath of dragons.
