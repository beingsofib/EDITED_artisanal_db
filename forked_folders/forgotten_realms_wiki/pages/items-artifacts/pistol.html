Pistols were small, single-shot firearms that only required one hand to operate.

Varieties

  • Gunpowder wand
  • Lantanese pistol: Pistols manufactured in Lantan.
  • Starwheel pistol: Pistols from Wildspace.

Trivia

Those unfamiliar with firearms might refer to pistols as "strange wands" or "strange stringless hand crossbows".
