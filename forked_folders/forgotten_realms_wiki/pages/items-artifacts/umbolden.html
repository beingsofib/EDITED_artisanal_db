The Umbolden was a full-rigged cog that sailed the waters of the Sea of Fallen Stars during the 14 century DR.

History

In Ches of 1357 DR, the Umbolden left the docks of Telpir, stocked with dried fish and cheese, headed to the Moonsea city of Melvaunt. Before arriving, it was due to make a stop at a port in the Dragon Coast—either Scardale or a city south of Scardale—some 10 days after embarking on its journey. However, it was never heard from again.
