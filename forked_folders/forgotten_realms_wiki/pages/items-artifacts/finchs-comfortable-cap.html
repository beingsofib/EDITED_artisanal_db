Finch's comfortable cap

Item
nameFinch's Comfortable Cap
typeHat
locationNeverwinter, Sword Coast North

Finch's comfortable cap was just one of many enchanted hats worn by the daring half-elven bard Finch in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

Despite the headwear being nowhere as well-ensorcelled as his previous hats, Finch still developed an attachment to this fanciful floppy-sided cap. It did possess a minor enchantment that slightly boosted the wearer's physical health and constitution and improved their evasion. The hat's custom enchantments made it impossible to be worn by anyone who was not a half-elven bard.

History

In the , Finch wore his comfortable cap while performing in the city of Neverwinter and adventuring across the Sword Coast North.
