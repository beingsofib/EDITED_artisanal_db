The Shield of Harmony was an enchanted small shield that offered its wielder significant protection from mind-altering effects.

Powers

When held, this shield offered an individual protection from spells and other affects that attempted to charm, befuddle, hold, paralyze, or dominate them, as well as those that tried to make them act erratically or without purpose.

History

The shield of harmony once belonged to the Knight-Champion Audn of the Field, who served the wizard Kekkim.

After being recovered from Kekkim's mage tower, it found its way into the hands of a Guildmistress Busya of Trademeet by 1369 DR.
