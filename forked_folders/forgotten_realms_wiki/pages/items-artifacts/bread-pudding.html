Bread pudding was a simple dessert from the cold lands of the Barony of Bloodstone.

Availability

  • Bread pudding was served in the Bloodstone Village of the Barony of Bloodstone in the mid–14 century DR, where it was good enough food to be put on the Baron's table.
  • In Sembia, bread pudding was considered to be a simple and boring food.
