Philter of persuasiveness

Item
namePhilter of Persuasiveness
typePhilter
value2e850 gp

A philter of persuasiveness was a magical substance that boosted charisma.

Powers

When imbibed, this philter boosted the imbiber's charisma and ability to persuade others. Additionally, the imbiber could make a suggestion to all creatures within once every ten minutes.

Notable Owners

Shahpesh, a moralist from Zakhara, carried a philtre of persuasiveness.
