An ekaa was a type of arrow used by Angulutiun and Iulutiun hunters

Description

An ekaa was a specialized arrow utilized for hunting caribou and other herd animals. The arrowhead, expertly crafted from the antlers of caribou or deer, was affixed to a bone shaft. Both the arrowhead and shaft were adorned were covered with hooked barbs for enhanced efficacy.

Uses

These arrows were used primarily to hunt caribou and deer.

Availability

Though relatively rare outside of the Great Glacier, six elkaa arrows could be purchased for 3 sp.
