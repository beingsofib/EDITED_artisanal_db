Enduring spellbook

Item
nameEnduring Spellbook
typeSpellbook, wondrous item
rarity5eCommon

An enduring spellbook was a spellbook that had been magically enchanted for extra durability.

Powers

All pages and writing within an enduring spellbook did not deteriorate with the passing of time. The book was also immune to damage caused by fire and immersion in water.
