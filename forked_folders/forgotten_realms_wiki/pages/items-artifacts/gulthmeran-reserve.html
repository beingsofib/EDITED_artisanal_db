Gulthmeran Reserve was a variety of wine, produced on the southern Dragon Coast and the northern Vilhon Reach. It was exported as far as the Western Heartlands, the lands along the River Chionthar, and the city of Baldur's Gate, as of the late 15 century DR.

Description

This high quality yet harsh-flavored wine was in high demand among those of discriminating taste palettes. It was sold in small round green glass corked bottles.
