Chromatic breastplates were a variety of enchanted medium armor created on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 century DR.

Powers

Chromatic breastplates were made out of shimmering metal that changed color. They possessed a simple +1 or a stronger +3 defensive enchantment. Additionally, they offered the wearer a slight resistance to electricity, cold, as well as fire.

A chromatic breastplate could be created from an enchanted suit of armor and a precious diamond.

History

Chromatic breastplates with a simpler +1 enchantments were forged by Master Blacksmith Marrok of Shining Knight Arms and Armor shop of Neverwinter to help the heroes who sought the cure from the dreaded Wailing Death of the . Later the same year, more powerful versions were created by Master Blacksmith Barun of Beorunna's Well to help the forces of Neverwinter stand against Lady Aribeth the Betrayer, who led a marching army of Luskan in the deadly but short-lived Neverwinter-Luskan war.
