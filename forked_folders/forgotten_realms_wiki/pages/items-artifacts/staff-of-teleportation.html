A staff of teleportation was a staff with the power to teleport small objects. One of them was in the Mobius Tower.

Powers

These staves could teleport objects of up to , simply by touching the staff to the object while picturing where it would go.

Notable Staves

An efreet guarded one that hung on a wall in the Mobius Tower as of . That particular staff had only five charges left.
