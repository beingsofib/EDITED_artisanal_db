Spiced wine was wine with added herbs produced in Calimshan and Tethyr. A variety was available for sale through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Description

Spiced wine was said to be an effective remedy against nausea, cough and sore throat.

Each individual spice was used to make a different flavor of spiced wine. Typical flavors which could be ordered through Aurora's included raisins, cinnamon, fennel, anise, nutmeg, and clove.

Spiced wine or mulled wine was especially popular in north Faerûn for its warming taste and potency. This northern type of mulled wine was made from cheap red wine steeped with citrus rinds and spices.
