Winged boots were enchanted footwear that allowed the wearer to fly through the air.

Powers

These boots would sprout wings if the wearer concentrated on flying. Flight could be maintained without concentrating for up to two hours per day. If the boot's energy depleted, the wearer would be gently lowered to the ground. Winged boots came in a variety of kinds with varying degrees of speed and aerial maneuverability.

Notable Owners

  • Elistar Thistlebuck, a halfling adventurer that came from humble beginnings as a farmer's son in Luiren.
  • Galfrey Kaarne, human cleric of Mystra and sometime advisor to Alustriel Silverhand.
  • Hermes, the Olympian deity of travelers, wore a pair of winged sandals.
  • Larloch, the powerful and ancient lich wore a pair of winged boots.
  • Little Wing, a kenku assassin of the Cult of the Howling Hatred.
  • The Magister a self-aggrandizing necromancer.
  • Zahara the Shark, a famous swashbuckler-thief folk hero and captain of a small privateer fleet on the Sword Coast called Zahara's Krakens.
