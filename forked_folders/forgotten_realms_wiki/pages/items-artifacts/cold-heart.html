Cold Heart

Item
nameCold Heart
typeDagger
rules2nd edition

Cold Heart was the favored weapon of Kiaransalee, the drow goddess of undeath.

Description

It was a curved dagger that constantly dripped acid.

Abilities

A +4 dagger, those struck by it were also harmed by ongoing acid burns.
