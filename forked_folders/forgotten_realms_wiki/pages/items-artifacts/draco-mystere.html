The Draco Mystere was an artifact book of knowledge on dragons.

Powers

While lesser translations, transcriptions, and copies existed, studying the original Draco Mystere full-time for a month uninterrupted granted the reader peerless, inherent knowledge of arcane matters related to dragonkind. Furthermore, it granted them the ability to see further in low light, and rendered them forevermore immune to paralyzing enchantments as well as those that would make them fall asleep.

Reputation

Draco Mystere was considered to be something of which wizards spoke. The original text was known to be quite ancient, at least centuries old. Some attributed it to the high priest of the Cult of Ashardalon, but not all sages agreed on the matter.
