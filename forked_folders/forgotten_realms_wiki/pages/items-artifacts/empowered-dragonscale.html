Empowered dragonscale

Item
nameEmpowered Dragonscale
materialDragonscale
originDaeronnax's lair, the Stonelands

The empowered dragonscale was a red dragonscale found in the late 15 century DR, taken from the wyrm Daeronnax.

Powers

The dragonscale glowed with power and greatly improved the bearer's health, fortitude, and resistance to fire.

History

The dragonscale was taken from the red dragon Daeronnax, the master of Clan Daeronnax in the Stonelands, after he kidnapped the adventurer Tristran of Daggerford.
