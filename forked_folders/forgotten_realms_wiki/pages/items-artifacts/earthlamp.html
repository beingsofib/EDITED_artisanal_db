Earthlamp

Item
nameEarthlamp
typeLamp
locationThe Finger Defiant, Mulhorand

An earthlamp was a magical light source that gave off a bright amber glow. It was small enough to be brought while traveling. When there was no one in the room, its glow shut off on its own. The dwarf Thormud Horn used those in his abode.
