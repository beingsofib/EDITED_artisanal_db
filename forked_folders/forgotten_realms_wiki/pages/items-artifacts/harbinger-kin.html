Harbinger Kin was a variety of enchanted great swords created on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 century DR, with the original and the most powerful variety being the artifact that once was sought after by Kwukgixrin, an illithid brain.

Powers

There existed three variants of the Harbinger Kin, each possessed a +1, +3, and +8 enchantments what made the sword's edge deadlier and guided the wielder's hand towards the target. All three versions were powered by magical flames that burned the target upon a successful slash.

A Harbinger Kin could be created from an enchanted great sword and a slab of adamantite.

History

The original Harbinger Kin was an artifact that belonged to a powerful adventurer who delved into the dangers of the Underdark sometime before the late 14 century DR. When the adventurer fell victim to Kwukgixrin and his mind was consumed, the elder brain became obsessed with finding the artifact sword. It sent out its mind flayers to scour the Underdark in search of the greatsword that was hidden be the adventurer before his death. However, adventurer's twin brother recovered Harbinger Kin before Kwukgixrin's thralls were able to find it. The twin was strong in his own right and slaughtered the elder brain's thralls and its illithids before leaving the Underdark.

More common versions of Harbinger Kin with a simpler +1 enchantment were forged by Master Blacksmith Marrok of Shining Knight Arms and Armor shop of Neverwinter to help the heroes who sought the cure from the dreaded Wailing Death of the . Later the same year, more powerful versions were created by Master Blacksmith Barun of Beorunna's Well to help the forces of Neverwinter stand against Lady Aribeth the Betrayer, who led a marching army of Luskan in the deadly but short-lived Neverwinter-Luskan war.
