Potions of eagle's splendor were common magical potions that increased the imbiber's charisma.

Powers

Potion of eagle's splendor greatly increased the drinker's charisma and affability, similarly to the effects of the eagle's splendor spell.

History

By the , these potions could be found in wide use in many places across the Sword Coast, such as Neverwinter, and as far as the Nelanther Isles and Icewind Dale.

Notable Owners

  • Lucia Calefar, a drow rogue of the Night Masks in the late 14 century DR.
