Cashew soup was a thick, nut-based soup in the Realms whose primary seasoning was its namesake ingredient, cashew nuts.

History

In the , when Tennora Hedare's Aunt Aowena and Uncle Eckhart came to visit her home in Waterdeep's Castle Ward, a tenement building called the God Catcher, she spent most of the morning preparing a pot of cashew soup for them. This was accompanied by morsels of bread and salty ham, which took Tennora an hour to assemble and were largely made so that her aunt wouldn't notice that she was out of butter. However, Aunt Aowena was so displeased with the local conditions compared to their home back in the North Ward that she barely touched her bowl of soup.
