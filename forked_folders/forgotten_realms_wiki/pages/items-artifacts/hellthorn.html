Hellthorn

Hellthorn was an exotic variant of iron associated with the Nine Hells.

Description

It had a red hue in both its raw ore and pure metallic form.

Usage

Hellthorn took Hellish enchantments especially easily, making it an ideal medium for creating delightfully devilish tools of destruction and tyranny like staves, swords, flails, and spears. The fiendish aura of the weapon let the bearer benefit from effects that would normally only affect devils, and heightened any existing resistance to fire (such as that of a tiefling, or a ring of fire resistance). Impacts could produce blasts of flame that set the target alight, and on a failed attack it emboldened nearby devils (which included other hellthorn-wielders) to follow up immediately and attack as well. It could also be used to make a variety of iron golem fittingly called the hellthorn golem.

Availability

It was exclusively mined in the Underdark and was found near Gauntlgrym, in the form of hellthorn-infused boulders.

History

It was believed that hellthorn was not a typically occurring underground mineral, but the remnants of an ancient meteorite that struck Toril and deposited the hellthorn upon impact somewhere around Gauntlgrym. It was further speculated that its infernal powers were the result of the meteorite containing the essence of some sort of great fiendish entity, possibly even a piece of Asmodeus or a prior incarnation of him. If true, the main body of the meteorite might remain intact somewhere underground and still contain the greater whole of this fiendish power.

After taking over Gauntlgrym from the previous derro inhabitants, the duergar began extensively mining the area, recovering good amounts of iron and silver, and even a little mithral and hellthorn. This inspired leaders like Kholzourl to intensify the mining in search of ever greater hauls.

Around 1479 DR, the Ashmadai of Neverwinter sought to find a source of the metal somewhere in the region. Their leader Mordai Vell ended up dealing with the deceptive duergar merchant Nimor Ironvice, who seemed to know where to find it in the Underdark and was willing to provide it. but was captured by his fellow duergar before he could get away with it.

During a battle to reclaim Gauntlgrym from the duergar, hellthorn boulders were hurled with catapults and hellthorn golems were a threat.
