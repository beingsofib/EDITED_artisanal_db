Revenant's Necrotic Tonic was a variety of magical spirits available in the city of Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.

History

The enchanted drink was on offer at the Yawning Portal tavern in Waterdeep, stashed away in Durnan's cabinet. One serving of Revenant's Necrotic Tonic was priced at ten gold coins.
