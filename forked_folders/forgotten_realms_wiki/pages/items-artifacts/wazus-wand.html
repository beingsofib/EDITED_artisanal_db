Wazu's Wand was a huge "wand" that possessed mysteries magical properties.

Description

This "wand" was effectively an oversized chunk of yellow crystal, shaped like an icicle with both ends sharp. It was unknown if it was wielded as a wand by some mysterious giant creature or simply the name given to the item by Torleth Mindulspeer. Wazu's Wand was as thick as an adult human and as tall as two.

History

In 1372 DR, Wazu's Wand could be found in Torleth's Treasures, a curio vendor of Gillian's Hill. The crystal regularly pulsed and rippled with arcane energies. Torleth had a white tag attached to this unique item, asking for 500 gold pieces for it.
