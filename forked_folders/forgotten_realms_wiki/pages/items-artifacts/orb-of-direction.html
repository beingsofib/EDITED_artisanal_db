Orb of direction was a magical orb.

Powers

When held, this orb acted like a compass. It always showed in which direction true north was. The orb only functioned on the Prime Material plane.

Notable Owners

  • Jarûk the Prismatic, a dao musician that dwelled within the Caverns of Ooze in Undermountain.
