Pickled rice was a side dish from the Red Wizards' nation of Thay and the eastern land of Kara-Tur.

Description

In Priador's capital – Bezantur, pickled rice was often served as a side and a bed for the jellied egg dish, while in Wang Kuo province in Shou Lung, pickled rice and raw fish were used in making sushi, a food tradition learned from the Kozakuran peoples of Kara-Tur.

History

As of the , Aznar Thrul, the Zulkir of Evocation and the Tharchion of Priador, favored jellied eggs with pickled rice. He enjoyed the dish's blood-red yolk oozing out of the dish like real blood, mixing with the rice.
