Trapmaster's hoods were a type of enchanted thieves' headwear, in the 15 century DR.

Powers

These hoods possessed magics that enhanced the wearer's sight, allowing them to see the most intricate details of any traps or locks, resulting in drastic improvements of their thieving and roguish skills.

History

In the 15 century DR, trapmaster's hoods could be found among various inhabitants of the Sword Mountains, areas surrounding the city of Neverwinter, its markets, and the ancient ruins of Illefarn.
