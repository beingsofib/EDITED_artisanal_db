Umoja's armor was a set of enchanted hide armor worn by a druid of Ubtao named Umoja circa 1374 DR.

Powers

This hide armor was a surprisingly tough garment made of a hide of some unknown jungle beast. Apart from giving its wearer protection from the elements, Umoja's armor possessed a +1 enchantment. Due to its Ubtaoan origins, this armor could only be worn by a druid.

History

By the late 14 century DR, the armor belonged to Umoja‎ of Chult. Guided by his deity's commands, Umoja traveled to the nation of Samarach where he was attempting to uncover a yuan-ti conspiracy.
