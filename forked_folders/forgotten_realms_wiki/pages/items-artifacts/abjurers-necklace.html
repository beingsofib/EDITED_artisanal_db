Abjurer's necklace

Item
nameAbjurer's Necklace
typeMagic jewelry
rarity5eCommon

An abjurer's necklace was a protective enchanted amulet worn by wizards. It could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The wearer of an abjurer's necklace could use it to cast the shield spell when they came under attack.

Notable Owners

  • Shevarith Kendia, a rashemi wizard who worked as an adventurer on behalf of Laeral Silverhand, the Open Lord of Waterdeep, during the late 15 century DR.
