Staff of mighty sweeping

Item
nameStaff of Mighty Sweeping
typeQuarterstaff
casterlevel3e5
value3e29,000 gp

A staff of mighty sweeping was a magical +2 quarterstaff with the sweeping weapon quality, so the wielder was able to trip opponents while wielding it. It was made from strong ash. It was worth around 28,600 gold pieces.
