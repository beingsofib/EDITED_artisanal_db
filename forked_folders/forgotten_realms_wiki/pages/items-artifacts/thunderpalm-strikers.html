Thunderpalm Strikers were enchanted monk gloves that discharged a thunderclap with each strike.

Powers

When used in unarmed combat, each successful attack with Thunderpalm Strikers produced a thunderous damage that sounded like a "rumbling boom of a thousand demons' ravenous stomachs." Additionally, the wearer could cast the thunderwave spell once per day.

History

In the , a pair of Thunderpalm Strikers was sold by merchant Bumpnagel of the Clan Ironhand, a deep gnome clan of artificers from Baldur's Gate.
