Hungry Knight

Item
nameHungry Knight
locationSinging Sprite, Secomber, North Faerûn

The Hungry Knight was a signature dish from the Singing Sprite inn of Secomber.

Description

The Hungry Knight was a disgusting-looking platter of flatbread rolls stuffed with chicken livers that had been fried in garlic butter, garnished with diced pepper potatoes and dill. Despite the appearance, it was a delicious savory dish.

Availability

The Hungry Knight was a signature dish served at the Singing Sprite inn, alongside Three Crabs and A Trio of Trumpets. The dish sold for 7 gp, though this also included two tallglasses of mead, milk, wine or tankards of ale, as well as plentiful hot buttered bread rolls and biscuits that could sate additional guests.
