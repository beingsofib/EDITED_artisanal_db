Glasses of the Night Brigade

Item
nameGlasses of the Night Brigade
typeGlasses
originWheloon Prison, Cormyr

The glasses of the Night Brigade were special glasses used by the Night Brigade gang in the late 15 century DR.

Powers

The glasses improved the confidence and verve of the wearer, as well as providing some protection against magic.

History

These fine glasses were found after the defeat of the Night Brigade leader Storna Harnwood in her Temple of Shar at the hands of the Riverside Rats gang.
