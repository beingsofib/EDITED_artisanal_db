Restoration flask

Item
nameRestoration Flask
typePotion
rarity5eCommon

Restoration flasks were restorative potions that could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.

Powers

Quaffing a restoration flask cured the imbiber of ailments and certain negative magical effects.

Notable Owners

  • Tommus Tallstep, a halfling fighter who worked as an adventurer on behalf of Laeral Silverhand, the Open Lord of Waterdeep, during the late 15 century DR.
