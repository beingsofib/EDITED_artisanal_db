Whip of frost, fire, and fear

The whip of frost, fire, and fear was a type rechargeable, enchanted wand that was favored by evil clerics.

Description

This wand was the size of a rod and constructed of steel. Nine flexible electrum tentacles, each in length, were attached to its shaft.

Powers

This wand's effect active whenever it was unsheathed or wielded. When struck by its tendrils a victim would either suffer severe cold, severe burns, or an effect equivalent to that of the wand of fear.

Notable Owners

  • Sespetralee, a cleric of Loviatar in the 14 century DR.
