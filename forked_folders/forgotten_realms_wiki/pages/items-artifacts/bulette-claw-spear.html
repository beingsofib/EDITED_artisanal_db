Bulette-claw spears were dangerous types of spears, one of which belonged to Raika a Reghedmen barbarian adventurer active in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Bulette-claw spears were large two-handed polearms with slender and sharp edges.

Powers

These spears rendered and tore its targets, leaving them with debilitating bleeding wounds.

Notable Owners

  • Raika: a half-orc barbarian who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
