Acid dart

Item
nameAcid dart
typeDart
value20 gp
weight1 lb (450 g)
rules3rd

An acid dart was a hollow dart that contained acid. When striking something, the dart broke and released the acid in a splashing fashion, as well as the usual piercing action associated with darts.

Acid darts cost 20 gp each and weighed around 1 lb (450 g).
