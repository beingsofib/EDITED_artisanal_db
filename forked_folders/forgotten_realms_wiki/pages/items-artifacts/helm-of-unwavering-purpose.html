The Helm of Unwavering Purpose was an ancient magical helmet of unknown origin.

Description

The helm was made of pitted bronze. It featured flaring crest that encircled the rear of the helm starting at the temples.

Powers

In addition to protecting the wearer from physical harm, the helm rendered them immune to spells and spell-like effects that attempted to confuse and addle the mind.

History

As of the , the Helm of Unwavering Purpose was hidden away in the familial crypts of the dark Korlasz family.
