Celestial maces were the favored weapons of astral devas.

Description

It was described as a huge mace-like weapon.

Powers

It had the power of a +3 mace of disruption or a +3 disruption heavy mace.

It constantly glowed with the light of a torch.

Creation

Forging one required the spell disrupting weapon.

Notable Owners

These maces were conventionally carried by astral devas,
