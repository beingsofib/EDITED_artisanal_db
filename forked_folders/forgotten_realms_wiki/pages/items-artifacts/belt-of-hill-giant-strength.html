A belt of hill giant strength, also known as a girdle of hill giant strength,

Description

These belts were rather plain-looking in appearance, made of thick leather and metal studs. They were considered among the least powerful of belts of giant strength.

Powers

When worn, the belt imbued the wearer with the strength—albeit not the size or the girth—of a massive hill giant.

History

As of the , one of these belts could be found in Ribald Barterman's Adventurer Mart in Athkatla.

Notable Owners

  • Angel Rockford, a Ravenian thief and wizard.
  • Aeriell, cavalry commander and adventurer.
  • Culak, the spider-tamer.
  • Elan Garaq, a rakshasa right-hand of Baeloth Barrityl's in the Black Pits.
