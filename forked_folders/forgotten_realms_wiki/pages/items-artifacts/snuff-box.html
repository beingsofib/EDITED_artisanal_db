A snuff box was a container used to store snuff for its owner.

Description

A typical snuff box held about of snuff powder or another similar fine material, and was small enough to be easily carried about. They could be made from any number of materials, from the durable to the decorative.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue supplied mail-order snuff boxes with Calishite designs, built from hardwood, steel, silver, or gold.
