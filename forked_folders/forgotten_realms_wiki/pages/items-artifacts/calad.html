Calad

Item
nameCalad
typePoison

Calad was a poison that was available in many places across Faerûn.

Description

This opaque fluid had a hue that resembled liquid gold. It was distilled from the brain juices of basilisks, catoblepas, and disenchanters.

Effects

Upon ingesting, the victim experienced a disorienting degradation in vision and hearing. This poison also corroded the victim's internal organs. Both effects did not last long, but rendered the victim vulnerable and damaged.
