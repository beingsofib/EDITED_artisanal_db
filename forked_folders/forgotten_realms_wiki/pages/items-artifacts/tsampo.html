Tsampo

Item
nameTsampo
locationKhazari, Hordelands

Tsampo was a staple foodstuff of Khazari, made from roasted barley flour. It could be prepared as a porridge.

History

The lama Koja thought of tsampo porridge when he remembered his homeland. He was later presented with a magical feast that included tsampo porridge.
