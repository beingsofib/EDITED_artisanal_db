A naginata (), also known as a footman's glaive

Description

A naginata was a sword-like blade mounted on a shaft between in length.

Weaponology

There existed one unique form of martial art, titled the Mad Monkey school, that was centered around the naginata.

Notable Users

Classes

  • Samurais, particulaly female samurais, were known to wield naginatas.
  • Geisha.
  • Sohei.

Individuals

  • Nrakk, a gitzherai kensai.
  • Okuma, a human fighter from Kara-Tur, wielded an enchanted naginata.

Organizations

  • The militia of Toro-dai-be were armed with naginatas.

Races & Sentient Creatures

  • Some hobgoblins were known to fight with naginatas.
  • Among the oni, go-zu oni and me-zu oni were often skilled in the use of naginatas. The related ogre magi

Regions & Settlements
