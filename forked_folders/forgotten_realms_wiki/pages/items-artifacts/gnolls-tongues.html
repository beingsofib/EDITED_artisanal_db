Gnolls' tongues was a peppered delicacy dish from the Red Wizards' nation of Thay.

Description

Gnolls' tongues were cooked, peppered, and served encased in molded gelatinous aspic. In Priador's capital – Bezantur, gnolls' tongues in aspic was served as a main dish, accompanied by jellied eggs and pickled rice. The dish was served in a mound on a plate and had to be eaten quickly before it melted and lost its shape.

History

As of the , Aznar Thrul, the Zulkir of Evocation and the Tharchion of Priador considered peppered gnolls' tongues to be a favorite but not worth eating once warm and shapeless.
