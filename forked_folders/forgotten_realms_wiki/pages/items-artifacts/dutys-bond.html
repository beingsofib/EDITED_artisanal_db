Duty's Bond was the favored weapon of Torm, the Faerûnian god of loyalty. Besides his sword, Torm is an expert of all weapons and sometimes uses his gauntleted fists.

Powers

It was a +5 holy avenger two-handed greatsword with all the powers of a sun blade.
