Corwin's bow was the personal longbow of Captain Schael Corwin, an officer that served in the Flaming Fist during the mid–14 century DR.

Powers

Corwin's bow possessed a +1 enchantment and significantly increased the accuracy with which it could shoot arrows.

The bow possessed a unique magical boon. About one out of every ten arrows fired by it would splinter open after impact, with each fragment sprouting wooden tendrils that would wrap around the person it struck.
