Star Sticks were enchanted items that could illuminate drakes with starlight.

Description

Star Sticks were small glass tubes, according to the manufacturer, filled with captured starlight.

Powers

These tubes need to be shaken and snapped to be activated. Once the glass was snapped, it magically returned to its original shape and illuminated the surroundings similarly to the effects of the continual flame spell. Star Sticks were single-use items.

Notable Users

  • Xanda Pennywell: a weaver from the hamlet of Liam's Hold kept a Star Stick in her business papers-littered desk at home circa 1372 DR.
