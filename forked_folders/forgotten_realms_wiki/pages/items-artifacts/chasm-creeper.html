Chasm creepers were rare and valuable purple crystals. They could only be found growing on vertical surfaces of the deepest chasms.

Description

These beautiful crystals glowed with gentle purple radiation. The crystal base was of a brighter lavender hue, and it got darker towards the crystals' edges, becoming deep purple.

Availability

Chasm creepers could be found in the area of Underdark just below the Western Heartlands. And growing on rocky moats in the Astral Sea.

Usages

Chasm creepers could be processed via alchemy, extracting salts chasm creeper. This substance could be mixed with a special variety of alchemical suspension to create an elixir of the colossus.
