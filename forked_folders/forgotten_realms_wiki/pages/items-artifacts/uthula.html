Uthula was a magical axe wielded by Lord Maiele Nierdre the leader of the gold elven House Nierdre.

History

As of the , Uthula could be found in Leuthilspar, on the isle of Evermeet. However, before the the fall of Myth Drannor, centuries earlier, the enchanted axe was kept with House Nierdre in the City of Song of Cormanthor.
