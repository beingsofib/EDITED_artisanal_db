Biscuits were small buttered flaky bite-sized breads found throughout the Realms,

Availability

  • Waterdhavian biscuits were sometimes served topped with spiced silverfin.
  • In Shadowdale, buttered biscuits were served with melted cheese and mustard as dawnfry, along with sausages and sauced roast pheasant.
  • In Cormyr's capital city Suzail, cubed redruth goat cheese was served with a herbed paste of oil and crushed roasted vegetables to be spread on buttery biscuits with dull paddle-shaped knives.
  • Bisquits were a somewhat common food found in Phlan on the Moonsea North, where they were served with chowder, porridge, or simply with a glass of milk.
  • The Harvest Inn in Westbridge, a town along the Long Road in the Savage Frontier, offered its guests mutton accompanied by butter biscuits.
  • Tea and biscuits was a common midday treat among nobles of Arrabar in Chondath.
  • In the city of Undumor in Aglarond, freshly baked biscuits were a menu staple at the Green Man, alongside boiled mushrooms, fresh berries, and roasted venison.
