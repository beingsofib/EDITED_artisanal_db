Golden pear pies were sweet baked pastry dishes made with ripened pears.

History

During the Absolute crisis of the , golden pear pies were served at the art show titled "Strange Tides: Self-Portraits from the Sword Coast," hosted by Lady Jannath in her Lower City estate, along with wild mushroom soup, steak medallions with asparagus, and best wine procured from the Manorborn district of the Upper City.
