Brooch of dispelling

Item
nameBrooch of Dispelling
typeBrooch

A brooch of dispelling was a magical brooch which contained powerful dispelling magic. In the , the mage Umbeso attempted to use such a brooch, made of fine silver over onyx, which he had acquired from a Calishite magic dealer many years before, to cancel the magic of the Evise Jhontil. unsuccessfully; though the backlash did not kill him, it destroyed the brooch.
