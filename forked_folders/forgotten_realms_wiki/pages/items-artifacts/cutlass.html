Cutlasses were a type of a short and heavy sword.

Description

Cutlasses had slightly curved blades that could either be used in a stabbing or slashing manner. They had heavy basket hilts, making it is more difficult for the wielder of a cutlass to be disarmed.

Notable Cutlasses

  • Blackjammer's Cutlass

Notable Owners

Individuals

Cutlasses were often considered a favored weapon of the god Valkur.

Groups

Cutlasses were known to be especially popular among pirates.
