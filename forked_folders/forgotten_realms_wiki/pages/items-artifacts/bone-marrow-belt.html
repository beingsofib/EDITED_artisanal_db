The Bone Marrow Belt was a strange enchanted belt that granted defensive boons to wearers at a price.

Appearance

Aptly named Bone Marrow Belt was crafted out of bone and was of the unappealing color of bone marrow.

Powers

Like many other magical items, the Bone Marrow Belt adjusted its size to fit snugly around any humanoid's waist. However, once the belt was on, its enchantments gave the wearer a sickly unhealthy appearance that negatively affected their charisma. In exchange for the unappealing frail appearance, the Bone Marrow Belt increased the wearer's defense and made them resistant to slashing damage and somewhat vulnerable to blunt weapons.

The belt's enchantments did not allow good-aligned creatures to wear it.

History

In 1281 DR, the Bone Marrow Belt was worn by one of the monstrous giant skeletons with serrated blades for hands, that guarded the third floor of the ruined Hand of the Seldarine.
