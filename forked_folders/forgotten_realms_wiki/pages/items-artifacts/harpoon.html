Harpoons were spear-like weapons used to hunt marine animals.

Description

The metal head of a harpoon was fixed to a wooden shaft. The head was often pointed and hooked to increase the weapon's grip in flesh and cause more harm. The harpoon was connected to a rope which the thrower could release or increase tension on to reel a target in or throw it off balance. A typical harpoon weighed and could be purchased for 20 gp.

Notable Users

Religions

  • In the Church of Umberlee, harpoons were favored as weapons by the specialty priests known as anchors. and waveservants.
  • Shamans often favored the use of harpoons.
  • Waterwalkers, a variety of specialty priest in the Church of Istishia, favored the use of harpoons as weapons.
