Explosive bulb

Item
nameExplosive bulb
typeAlchemical explosive

An explosive bulb was an alchemical device known in the late 14 century DR. It was a bulb, warm to the touch, and if dashed against a hard surface, it would break and then explode. They were powerful enough explosives to hurl a careless user far away if they were too close, and generated enough heat to set alight flammables in the area. They were considerably more expensive than a tenday's stay at a festhall. The thief Gage from Laothkund was known to use them, as of .
