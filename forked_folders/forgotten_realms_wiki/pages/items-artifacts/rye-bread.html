Rye bread

Item
nameRye bread
typeBread

Rye bread was a type of dense, darkly colored bread made from rye. It was often served alongside Dragon's Breath Beer.

Availability

Rye bread was known to be served at the Bowels of the Earth tavern in Waterdeep and the restaurant The Painted Boat near Ravens Bluff.

Reputation

Rye was a popular type of bread among many travelers in the Sword Coast due to maintaining freshness for a prolonged period of time.It was often served alongside Dragon's Breath Beer.
