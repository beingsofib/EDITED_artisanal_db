Shadow armor was a variety of enchanted studded leather armor associated with the Shadow Thieves.

Description

The armor was black in color. Its form actually melded into the shadows that surrounded its wearer.

Powers

In addition to grating its wearer protection of a +3 enchantment, shadow armor enhanced their natural skill and ability to remain hidden from the sight of others.

Notable Owners

  • Mae'Var, guildmaster of the Shadow Thieves of Athkatla

Reputation

Suits of shadow armor were made specifically for the Shadow Thieves' Shadowmasters.
