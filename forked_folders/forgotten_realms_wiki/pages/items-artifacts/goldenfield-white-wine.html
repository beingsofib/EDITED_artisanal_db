Goldenfield White Wine

Item
nameGoldenfield White Wine
typeWine
locationMistledale, Liam's Hold
value4 gp

Goldenfields white wine was a light white wine exported from Mistledale and could be found throughout North Faerûn and Sword Coast.

Description

Goldenfields white was made using skinless grapes which gave it the light yellow color.
