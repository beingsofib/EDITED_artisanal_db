Water Weird was a pirate cutter operating on the Sea of Swords and the Shining Sea of west Faerûn in the late 14 century DR. Captained by Jafar, it was part of the Bloody Buccaneers' small fleet and an asset of the Rundeen alongside the Umberlee's Wake.

Inhabitants

It had a crew of corsairs, all sponsored by the Rundeen. Formerly, it was captained by the brother of Jhamil al Tassand, but after his death, the first mate Jafar took over.

Activities

The Bloody Buccaneers targeted non-Rundeen-affiliated ships transporting trade goods south into the Shining Sea. Although they worked hard to make good the "bloody" part of their name, they struggled to kill enough people to get a reputation, as many ships they attacked and raided managed to escape. Nevertheless, they were relatively successful in their piracy and served the Rundeen's ends.

History

Since about 1366 DR, the Bloody Buccaneers had successfully attacked and raided over eight ships.

Then, in the , ships owned by the Bladesmile and Bormul merchant houses of Amn fought back against a raid, even killing the captain of the Water Weird. Jafar succeeded him to the rank of captain and co-leadership of the pirates, and he and Jhamil, his fellow captain, swore to have their revenge on Bladesmile and Bormul.
