Shortrib was a simple and favored by many dish, a slowly cooked pig's, hog's, or boar's ribs.

Description

Shortribs, when properly cooked, were tender, delicious, and falling off the bone. They often were served glazed. This dish was favorite among soldiers and their war dogs.

History

Tender shortrib were commonly found all along the Sword Coast, both, in taverns, and travelers' camps in the late 15 century DR.
