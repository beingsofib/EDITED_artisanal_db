Prestigious chromatic vestments were enchanted robes that belonged to the aarakocra people of Kir Sabal in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Chromatic vestments were made out of uncannily light material of shivering colors that moved as if underwater.

Powers

The enchantments placed on this set of robes made the wearer hard to strike, with the physical impact of hits dispersed as if fighting a water elemental.

Notable Owners

  • Asharra: the aarakocra wizard leader of Kir Sabal in the late 15 century DR.
