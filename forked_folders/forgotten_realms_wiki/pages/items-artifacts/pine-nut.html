Pine nuts were hard-shelled edible seeds of the pine tree sometimes consumed in the land of Calimshan.

Availability

Pine nuts were among travel rations eaten in Calimshan, alongside dried dates and scorchmeat.

In the rest of the Sword Coast and the City of Splendor, Waterdeep, pine nuts were sold in Aurora's Emporium and via Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue, valued at ten gold pieces per .

Outside the Prime Material plane, pine nuts were among items treasured by the ratatosks of Yggdrasil in Arborea. Pine nuts among other nuts were infused with magics to function in a similar fashion to potions.
