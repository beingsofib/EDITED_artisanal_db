Heavy plate armor was a form of full plate armor designed for maximum strength at the cost of immense weight.

Description

The armor was an heavily reinforced version of full plate, combining the complete personal coverage of an expertly-crafted suit of armor with unusually thick plating. This made it extremely heavy and thus it was only made by very strong humanoids such as orogs. The advantage of this over-building was unparalleled physical protection, though at the cost of reduced agility.
