Smelling salts were overwhelmingly pungent ammonia crystals valued for their mind-waking power.

Availability

Smelling salts were among the minor herbal and alchemical medicinal products created by alchemists, apothecaries, and hedge wizards.

The salts could be obtained from deer antlers via an alchemical process that concentrated the ammonia.

Usage

  • Smelling salts were useful for waking up those who had been knocked unconscious or put to sleep, magically or otherwise. It could even break some mental enchantments.
  • Brightmantle used smelling salts or similar ammonia-based substances as a material component.
  • Modify memory could use smelling salts as a metamagic component.
