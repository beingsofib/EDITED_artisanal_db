Ravensong was a unique powerful enchanted sword from the ancient elven city of Myth Drannor.

Description

Ravensong was a longsword with a blade made of a type of dark-hued metal, and its handle was crafted in a shape of a raven.

Powers

The weapons possessed a mighty +5 enchantment, increased the wielder's dexterity, and honed their reflexes. This magic came at a price, however, as Ravensong reduced their willpower. Additionally, the sword allowed the wielder to cast feeblemind at will, and it held three charges of the spell.

History

As of the , Ravensong belonged to a brigadier of the Cult of the Dragon that occupied the ruins of the City of Song on the orders of Kya Mordrayn, a Wearer of Purple.
