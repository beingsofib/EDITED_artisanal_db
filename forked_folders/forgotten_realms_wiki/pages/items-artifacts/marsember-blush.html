Marsember Blush was wine of good vintage exported from Marsember as far as Ten Towns of Icewind Dale in the 13 century DR and cities like Baldur's Gate and Athkatla of the Sword Coast in the 14 century DR.

Description

Marsember Blush was a popular and widely consumed wine that tasted of a fruit blend and spices with a fragrance of dew-flowers. The unique and popular flavor of the wine came from the firm grapes of Marsember vineyards that carried the region's fragrant salty air of the Sea of Fallen Stars.
