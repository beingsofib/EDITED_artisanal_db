Serpent ring

Item
nameSerpent Ring
typeRing

Serpent rings were magic items worn only by the most trusted servants of Tiamat, Set, Sseth, and Varae.

Description

These rings were made from brass and designed to resemble a snake biting its own tail.

Powers

A serpent ring allowed serpentfolk, scalykind, and cultists of Tiamat, Set, Sseth, and Varae to safely pass through wards set within their temples. The magic of the ring also identified the wearer so that serpent blades would not attack and gave the wearer immunity to all serpent venom, while also making them more resistant to other poisons and acids.
