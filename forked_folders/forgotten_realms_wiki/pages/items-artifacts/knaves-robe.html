Knave's robes were common enchanted robes worn by many wizards across Faerûn in the 14 century DR.

Powers

Knave's robes were defensive robes often used by shady wizards and thieves who dabbled in the Art. The robes' enchantments shielded the wearer from poisons, blades, and death spells.

Notable Owners

  • Elan Garaq: a rakshasa trader and Baeloth Barrityl's right-hand from the Black Pits of Underdark.
  • Yago: a wizard from Baldur's Gate in the mid−14 century DR.
  • Zordral: a witch hunter wizard from Nashkel's carnival in 1368 DR.
