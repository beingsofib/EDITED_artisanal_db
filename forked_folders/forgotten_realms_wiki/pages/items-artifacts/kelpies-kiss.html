The Kelpie's Kiss was a ship captained by the half-elf Zaldar Floshin in the late 15 century DR.

History

Circa 1485 DR, Zaldar and his ship were docked in Waterdeep when they were hired by the Harpers to set sail in search of the ship Morkoth and its hostage King Hekaton. While on this mission, a Harper agent by the name of Ilkara Levari served as the ship's bosun.

Inhabitants

Zaldar kept a crew of 15 Tethyrian humans. Additionally, he had a sprite first mate by the name of Arrow.
