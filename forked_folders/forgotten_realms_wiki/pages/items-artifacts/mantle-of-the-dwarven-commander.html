The mantle of the dwarven commander was a clothing accessory found in the ruins of Tethyamar during the 1480s DR.

Powers

The mantle imbued the wearer with greater reflexes, cunning, and strategy, as well as enhanced their general intelligence.

History

The cloak was once worn by a renowned dwarven commander, who won every battle before him with his tactical brilliance. The only battle he ever lost was his last one.

The accessory was later found in Mineshaft 214S after the dwarf Egil Hakon went mad for the jewels of ardor.
