Hamarfae

Item
nameHamarfae
typeWriting script

Hamarfae was an ancient Elven script, distinct from Espruar, used to write the (defunct by 1372 DR) Seldruin language of Elven High Magic.

History

It was practically forgotten by the late 15th century DR.
