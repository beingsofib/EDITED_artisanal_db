The Harebrained Harpy was a ship, of a type known as a raker, based in Waterdeep and operating in the Sea of Swords to the north in the mid–14 century DR.

Crew

Among its crew was the ship's navigator Respen Moongleam, a priest of Selûne.

History

In the , the Harebrained Harpy was attacked by pirates flying the colors of Luskan, led by the enigmatic Black Admiral. The Harpy was sunk with all hands on board, but Respen was rescued from the freezing waters to face a far worse fate—Captain Kulleras.
