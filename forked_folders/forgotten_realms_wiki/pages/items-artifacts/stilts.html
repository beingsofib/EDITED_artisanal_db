Stilts were tools in the Realms popular among bards and performers who stood on them to greatly increase their height during a performance.

Description

Stilts were usually made from wood and were avaible in various lengths.

Availability

  • Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold stilts for around 2 to 12 gold pieces per pair, depending upon their length.

Notable Users

  • Stilts were favored by bards.
