The Tyr's Justice was an enchanted greatsword wielded by Nayeli Goldflower in the late 15 century DR.

Description

Nayeli's most prized blade was crafted out of devil-tainted gold and encrusted with gemstones.

Powers

Apart from being a fierce enchanted blade, Tyr's Justice allowed the wielder to cast the searing smite spell on one enemy at a time.

History

Following the death of Maldwyn Daggerford in 1485 DR, Nayeli Goldflower, the member of the Guardians of Daggerford, commissioned the creation of Tyr's Justice out of devil-tainted gold and gems that were on Duke Daggerford body during his assassination. The jewelry was melted and fashioned into the hilt of the blade that was to become Nayeli's instrument of vengeance.

Notable Owners

  • Nayeli Goldflower: a paladin from the city of Daggerford and an adventurer who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
