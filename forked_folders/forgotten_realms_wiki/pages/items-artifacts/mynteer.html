Mynteer, also known as '', was a hard, clear, and rare gemstone found in the Realms.

Description

These semi-precious stones occurred in crystals, usually with inclusions so that it yielded only small gems. A typical specimen had a base value of 50 gp.

Powers

This stone was powdered and mixed to make magical ink to inscribe scrolls for casting spells related to the movement of objects via levitation, telekinesis, or magical force, such as unseen servant, Tenser's floating disc, etc.
