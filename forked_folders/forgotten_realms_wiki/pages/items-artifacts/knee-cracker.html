Knee-Cracker was a cider crafted in Daggerford, Faerûn. It was also sold through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Description

Made from windfall apples, Knee-Cracker cider possessed heavy sediment. It was suggested to let a container of Knee-Cracker sit for three days upon receipt to allow the sediment to settle.

This cider was a flavorful cloudy beverage that was stronger than ale but weaker than wine.

Prices

Knee-Cracker could be purchased in the following containers:

  • Hand keg (1 sp)
  • Cask (4 sp)
  • Barrel (1 gp)
  • Butt (35 sp)
  • Tun (8 gp)
