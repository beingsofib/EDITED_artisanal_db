Kita

Item
nameKita
typeBread
locationPort Nyanzaru, Chult

Kita was a type of unleavened bread, unique to the region of Chult, prepared with salt and a variety of herbs in a hot pan. It was primarily available for purchase around Port Nyanzaru.
