Potions of ironguts were magical concoctions that increased imbiber's protection against poisons.

Powers

These potions functioned similarly to the ironguts spell, magically increasing the drinker's fortitude and their defenses against all types of poisons.

History

Circa 1372 DR, potions of ironguts could be found sold in the Hellbreath Tavern in Cania of the Nine Hells. The same year, a single bottle of this potion could be found among the "junk" available for sale at Torleth's Treasures in Gillian's Hill.

Circa 1373 DR, the Zhentarim Enclave created and distributed several minor magical items, including potions of ironguts.

Notable Owners

  • Mordak Brelliar: one of the apprentices of First Lord Maalthiir of Hillsfar, aligned with the Zhentarim circa 1373 DR.
