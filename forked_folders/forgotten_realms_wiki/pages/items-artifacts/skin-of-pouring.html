Skin of pouring

Item
nameSkin of Pouring
typeEnchanted waterskin
locationMaztica

A skin of pouring was a waterskin enchanted with the pluma spell create talisman.

Description

The exterior of these waterskins was feathered.

Powers

The enchantment placed upon a skin of pouring ensured that it was always full of clean drinking water. Water could be poured from the skin at a rate of per minute.

Notable Users

  • Sozarro, a deserter from the Golden Legion, traded a magic sword for a skin of pouring so he wouldn't die of thirst whilst crossing the Pasocada Basin.
