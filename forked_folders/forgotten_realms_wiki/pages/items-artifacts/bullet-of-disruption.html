Bullet of disruption were a type of enchanted sling bullet deadly to the undead.

Powers

Cores of the bullets of disruption were ensorcelled with magical currents that improved their aim and balance. These bullets also obsessed enchantments that disrupted magics that kept the undead reanimated and planar creatures anchored to the Prime Material plane. As such, bullets of disruption were especially useful against those two types of creatures. Each strike for these bullets had a chance of instantly destroying an outsider or an undead creature. Additionally, they were imbued with a simple +1 enchantment.

History

In the , these bullets were used by the Legion of the Chimera to bring destruction on the Ten Towns and Kuldahar of Icewind Dale.
