Stone of mixed luck

Item
nameStone of Mixed Luck
typeStone
value2e12,500 gp

A stone of mixed luck was an enchanted stone that provided good luck most of the time.

Powers

These magical stone provided good luck to whoever carried them for most of the time. However, every now and then they did the exact opposite.

Notable Owners

Myste, an illusionist from Toril, owned a stone of mixed luck.
