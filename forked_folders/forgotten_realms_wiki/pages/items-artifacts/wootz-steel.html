Wootz steel

Substance
nameWootz Steel
typeMetal

Wootz steel was a naturally occurring steel alloy found in Chult.

Properties

Wootz steel had a natural distinctive wavy patterning that came through however it was used. It was strong and durable like typical steel and was used for much the same purposes.

Usages

The raw ore could be refined into a pure metal and then used for making traditional Chultan weapons and armor (including with plates, rings, and scales). It was also useful for making durable tools like wire, hatchets, bolts, pickaxes, and files.
