Dye remover was a commonly used substance that stripped all dyes from metals, leather, and cloth, used in the Western Heartlands in the late 15 century DR.

History

Commercially sold dye remover found throughout the Chionthar Valley and the city of Baldur's Gate was a secret recipe that included a universal solvent. The substance was first created by a certain hedge witch long before the .

Notable Users

  • Araj Oblodra, a drow alchemist, allied with the Cult of the Absolute in the late 15 century DR.
  • Arron, a halfling druid trader from the Emerald Grove in the late 15 century DR.
  • Barcus Wroot, a deep gnome who was on a quest to find his friend Wulbren in the late 15 century DR.
  • Figaro Pennygood, a merchant from Facemaker's Boutique of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.
