Leomund's rod of shelter was a magic rod created by the Archmage Leomund from the world of Oerth.

Powers

This enchanted rod opened a portal to an impenetrable by any other means pocket dimension allowing the wielder to safely rest and recuperate even in the most dangerous of locations.

History

Leomund's rod of shelter was crafted by the creator of Leomund's tiny hut, Leomund of Oerth, who wanted an easier access to secure areas to rest in following exhausting battles. By 1372 DR, the rod was in possession of the Sorceller's Encapsulate of Daggerford.
