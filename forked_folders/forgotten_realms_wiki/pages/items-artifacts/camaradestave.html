Camaradestave

Item
nameCamaradestave
typeClub (quarterstaff)
level2e+1

Camaradestave was a unique magical club that was wielded as a quarterstaff by the avatar of Cyrrollalee, the halfling deity of hospitality of hospitality and friendship.

Powers

Camaradestave was a +1 weapon. It was able to charm any creature that it struck, providing that it was unable to resist.
