Lampblack

Lampblack was a type of black pigment found in the Realms, made from the soot of oil lamps.

History

Lampblack existed on Toril as far back as the Netherese Empire.

Usages

  • Some used lampblack for the purpose of disguising themselves.
  • Older versions of the spell friends required the caster to coat their face in lampblack as a material component.

Notable Users

  • Sigird Sigirdson, a thief in the Vast in the 14 century DR, was known to sometimes use lampblack in order to disguise himself.
