Fang (sword)

Item
nameThe Fang
typeShort sword
locationChult, Chultan Peninsula
rarity5eLegendary

The Fang was an enchanted short sword from Chult, said to be made out of Shagambi's tooth.

Powers

The magic of the blade filled the wielder with primal power of Shagambi, inducing feral rage when defending their kin.

Notable Owners

  • Birdsong: A tabaxi bard from Chult in the late 15 century DR.
