Boots of fleet and fortune

Item
nameBoots of Fleet and Fortune
typeBoots
originMyth Drannor, north Faerûn

The boots of fleet and fortune were a pair of boots found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 century DR.

Powers

Accurate to their name, the boots made the wearer extremely nimble, dextrous, and above all lucky.

History

The boots were originally brought to Myth Drannor by an adventurer who thought it a source of easy treasure, only to fall to a terrible fate. It may have been found by the Cormanthor Elves during the Elven Crusade when they put out the fires at the Burning Library at the behest of the dragon Jurin'ildren and the elf Kaylie.
