The Hall of Gold was a temple dedicated to the goddess Waukeen in the city of Port Nyanzaru, as well as one of the city's most affluent banks.

Location

This temple was located in Port Nyanzaru's Market Ward, situated atop Mount Sibasa.

Structure

The Hall of Gold was named for shining, golden roof, which many in the city believed was really sheathed in pure burnished gold. In actuality, the roof was merely painted golden, but whenever the sun was shining its light would glint off the roof for miles out to sea.

Activities

This temple arranged charitable events and projects that helped improved conditions within Port Nyanzaru.

Inhabitants

In the 15 century DR, the chief priest of this temple was Sibonseni, Mother of Prosperity, one of the most influential residents of Port Nyanzaru next to the merchant princes.
