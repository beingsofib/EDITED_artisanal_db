Why Is There Air?/Storm Streak

Storm Streak, round one of Why Is There Air?, focuses on the players traveling to an uncharted island in the Sea of Fallen Stars that's been determined to be the source of a recent string of storms. The story is continued in round two, Air Everywhere.
