The Trades District of Athkatla was home to a great number of the city's merchants.

Notable Locations

  • The Antlers of Andurl: a festhall and hunter's dining club connected with the Tessaerlee cult.
  • The Atrium, a financial hub through which most of Athkatla's paperwork flowed.
  • Dunstul's Pump: this literal watering hole was a popular meeting place for Athkatlans.
  • The Black Frog: a popular local tavern.
  • The North Side, a neighborhood populated by Athkatlans with plenty of wealth but little influence within the city.
  • The Waft, a street lined with shops selling aromatic goods, from perfume to spice.
