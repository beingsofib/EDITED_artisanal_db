Bridge of the Ancients

Location
nameBridge of the Ancients
typeBridge
regionPasocada Basin, Maztica

The Bridge of the Ancients was a stone bridge over the Pasocada River in Maztica. It stood near the ruins of Esh Alakar in the Long Canyon.

It offered a useful crossing point over the Pasocada River. There were no other safe crossing points for miles in either direction.
