Chelor's Alley

Chelor's Alley was a short alleyway of hard-packed dirt and gravel in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Geography

This alleyway ran due west off of the Court of the White Bull, terminating on Deloun Alley.

Notable Locations

House of Chelor: the residence of Chelor, the alleyway's namesake. The home had a great deal of lanterns set in its upstairs windows, providing illumination to the alley below. Because of this, some used Chelor's Alleyway as a night time rendezvous when in need of light, including nightly patrols of the City Guard and Watch when needing to investigate evidence.
