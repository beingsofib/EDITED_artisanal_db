The Palace of the Chief Examiner was the headquarters for the city guard of Dhaztanar in Semphar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The palace was located within the Furdah Rustauk District in southwest Dhaztanar. The Palace of the Chief Examiner was actually constructed along the shore of Gbor Nor at the base of the Caliph's Pier.
