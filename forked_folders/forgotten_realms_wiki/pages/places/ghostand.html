Ghostand was the small home village of the Tree Ghost tribe of Uthgardt barbarians.

Geography

This village was located in the High Forest, close to the Grandfather Tree. Constructed in a similar manner to the various wild elf cities in the High Forest, the village stood high above the forest floor atop a stand of towering oak trees.

History

This village was founded sometime in the , after the Tree Ghost tribe had finally rediscovered the Grandfather Tree after six decades of searching for it. Their efforts to establish the village received aid from wild elves that lived in the region as well as backing by Alustriel, the ruler of Silverymoon.

By the late 14 century DR, the village emerged as a trading center for the inhabitants of the surrounding forest.

Inhabitants

By the late 14 century DR, the village was home to over 350 Uthgardt barbarians. Additionally, wild elves, wood elves, and half-elves could often be found around it.
