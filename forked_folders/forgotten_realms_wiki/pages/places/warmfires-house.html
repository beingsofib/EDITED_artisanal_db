Warmfires House was a rented farmhouse in Shadowdale during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

Warmfires House lay in a bend of the River Ashaba, on the northern edge of central Shadowdale; the surrounding area had come to be called "Northend".

Structure

It was a roomy, sprawling farmhouse.

Interior

The place had a dance floor and feast hall, rooms with bathing pools, and luxury bedchambers. The upper story had several meeting rooms.

Services

Warmfires house could be rented for periods of up to a tenday at a time.

Defenses

There was a guard post nearby.

Staff

Most of the other staff were undercover Harper forces.

History

Warmfires House was a venture by Sembian investors. It received a number of visitors, including local scribe Maervidal Iloster, on tours during its grand opening. After Lord Mourngrym Amcathra had a guard post installed nearby, the surrounding area came to be called "Northend", and a new cluster of shops and houses had risen around it.

The Zhentarim rented the place for a revel at some point in the . During the revel, they planned on slaying Maervidal Iloster, but he never came, being substituted by a disguised Storm Silverhand. By the end of the night, many of the Zhentarim were slain by her, with the rest of them captured by the staff.
