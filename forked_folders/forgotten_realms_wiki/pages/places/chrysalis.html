Chrysalis was a wondrous elven city located within the heart of the Kingdom of Synnoria on the isle of Gwynneth on the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Chrysalis was secluded within the small Synnorian valley found between the Cambro Mountains and the Highlands near central Gwynneth. The city rose up a rounded hill upon a small island in the center of a circular lake, the largest of several lakes found in the valley. Chrysalis was accessible by land via two natural bridges: one in from the northeast and the other from the southwest.
