Kraanfhaor's Door

Building
nameKraanfhaor's Door
locationTalthalaran, High Moor

Kraanfhaor's Door was an ancient elven magic college in Talthalaran on the High Moor. It was one of the most ancient colleges, dating to millennia before the Descent of the drow. In the , Q'arlynd Melarn found a clue to its location inside a kiira found in the High Moor.
