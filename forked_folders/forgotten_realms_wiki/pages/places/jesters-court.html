Jester's Court

Jester's Court was a courtyard that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Jester's Court was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward. It was enclosed by Selduth Street to the north, the Street of Silks to the west, the Street of Silver to the east, and Howling Cat Court to the south.
