The Sheltering Hand was a temple to Tymora in Waymoot, Cormyr.

Activities

The temple provided healing services to those who took risks even if they weren't followers of Tymora. The clergy believed that helping members of other faiths might one day lead them to follow Tymora. In addition to healing, patrons often received hot meals and a purse full of coins when they departed the temple.

Inhabitants

The Chancepriest of the temple was a dignified man named Gothric.

Defenses

Because of their affluent leadership, the temple housed a guardian golem that would defend the temple against any attack.
