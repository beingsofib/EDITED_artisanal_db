The Temple of Ishistu was a temple dedicated to the living idol Ishistu loacted in the ruined city of Moradask on the Sea of Salt in the Haunted Lands of interior Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Location

The temple lay within the catacombs beneath Moradask.

Description

Four vertical tombs for long-dead priests of Ishistu were arranged around a central structure that contained an altar and pit.
