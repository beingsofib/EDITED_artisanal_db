The Wailing Wench Tavern was a spacious tavern and inn built in the Tyr's Eye plaza of the Blacklake District in the late 14 century DR.

Structure

The Wench a large horseshoe-shaped three-storied building with steeply pitched roofs and timber-framed walls. Its entrance was located at the inner side of the horseshoe's curve, the building's wings looming over it, forming a small courtyard. The building had multiple gabled roofs and dormer windows on all three floors. The tavern was easy to notice thanks to its external walls of plaster and wooden beams. The entrance was under a small awning with a sign displaying the tavern's name dangling above.

The tavern's front side was fenced with benches lining its walls.

Interior

The tavern's taproom was typical for the region. It featured stone-laden floors and walls. Several load-bearing stone columns were placed around the room with dinner tables, chairs, couches, and private booths. The walls were draped in tapestries depicting the Neverwintran Eye & Tears design.

History

Following the Shadow War of the , the Tyr's Eye plaza suffered a fire that destroyed several buildings that stood there as well as the city's mage school. The Wailing Wench Tavern was built in place of the torched businesses on the northeastern side of Tyr's Eye.

Inhabitants

Half-orcish paladin Grykk Bannersworn was one of the Wench's guests in 1374 DR. Some of the performers who graced the tavern with their shows included Finch the bard as well as Shymon and Scarbunkel.
