Stall Street

Stall Street was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Stall Street was located in Zhentil Keep's Common Quarter in the eastern section of the city. The road originated on the Street of Sorrow, branching southwest for just a block before ending at the wall of the Inner Quarter.
