Mezekth Isle was an island in the Sea of Fallen Stars off the coast of Saerloon in Sembia.

History

In the , the heart of Borem of the Lake of Boiling Mud was brought here and buried with the Jathiman Dagger piercing it by the adventurers Bane, Bhaal and Myrkul after killing Borem on his home plane during their quest for godhood..

In the , by the request of Lady Merelith, a lighthouse was built on Mezeketh Isle. Zembrath Klun was put in charge. Klun found a menhir inscribed with ancient Jhaamdathan script.
