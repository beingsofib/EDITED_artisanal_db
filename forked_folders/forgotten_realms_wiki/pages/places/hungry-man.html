The Hungry Man, also known as the Hungry Man Inn, was a restaurant located in the city of Arabel.

Inhabitants

The proprietor was the elderly woman Zehla.

Food and Drink

The establishment sold watered down ale and wine known as bullock juice.

Prices

  • All you can eat for 2 sp
  • Tankard of ale or wine for 1 cp
