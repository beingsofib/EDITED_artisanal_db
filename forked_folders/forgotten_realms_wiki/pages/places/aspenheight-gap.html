The Aspenheight Gap was a low mountain pass located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The pass separated the Aspenheight range in the north from the Highlands in the south. It was approximately long and connected the central Myrloch Vale with the Shrouded Lands to the west. It lay six days' ride north of Caer Corwell, and provided an easy if roundabout route from Corwell into Myrloch Vale.

The pass entered Myrloch Vale to the north of the Myrloch. This entrance was marked by a ring of stones that served as a natural windbreak and thus a perfect campsite.
