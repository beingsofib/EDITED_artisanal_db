The Temple Bell

Building
nameThe Temple Bell
typeShop
locationTemple District, Athkatla

The Temple Bell was a shop in Athkatla's rich Temple District in the 14 century DR.

Location

The Temple Bell stood on Aundle's Walk in the Temple District, three buildings east of the busy Harfin Draether tavern.

Services

The shop did business selling goods of religious nature. It offered accessories, holy symbols, vestments, and religious texts, all dedicated to various churches and deities of the Realms.

History

Before the Temple Bell took ownership of the building, it was the mansion of the Flamaerdown family of Athkatla. By the late 14 century DR, Flamaerdowns were presumed to have gone extinct and the Temple Bell shop occupied the estate.
