Raven Street

Raven Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 century DR.

Location

Located in the Inner Quarter in central Zhentil Keep, Raven Street cut north to south to touch both the northern wall of the Common Quarter and southern wall of the Harbor District. Along the way Raven Street crossed Elephstron Way.
