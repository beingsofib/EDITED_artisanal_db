The Watchful Knight was popular inn located in the town of Beliard. It earned its name from the centerpiece of its main hall, a suit of armor that sprang to life to guard the inn.

It was run by Arachar Calatharr who, at his insistence was not related to the famous adventurer of the same name.

Structure

The inn was built of logs and as such was deathly cold in the winter. It was warmed, as much as possible, by two chimneys located on either end.

Interior

The Watchful Knight comprised a vast central hall, atop which sat two additional floors of 16 guest rooms. Each of these rooms had an interior balcony that overlooked the inn's first floor.
