Golden Road

The Golden Road was a trade road running from Arrabar in Chondath on the Vilhon Reach through Ormpetarr to Innarlith on the Lake of Steam and then on to Shaarmid and Kormul in the Shaar.

The Golden Road connected with the Dunsel Trail at Shaarmid and with the Trader's Way at Kormul.

History

In 1385 DR the spellplague created an area of extreme magical chaos known as the Plaguewrought Land over much of the northern area formerly traversed by the road.
