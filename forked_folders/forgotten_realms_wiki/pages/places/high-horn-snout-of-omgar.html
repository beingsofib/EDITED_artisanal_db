High Horn was an enormous statue of a revered tortle that was built atop the isle known as the Snout of Omgar in Chult.

Description

The statue featured the likeness of the tortle Gumdarr, blowing into a large stone sounding horn.

Geography

Geographical Features

Gumdar's statue was built atop a sand-covered beach, dotted with enormous black rocks that were covered in moss.

History

Nearly a century before the time of the Second Sundering, the Bitch Queen crashed near the statue of High Horn. Over the course of the following decades, several other vessels crashed or otherwise washed ashore at the beach beneath High Horn, including the Dawn Avenger and the Carcerius, among others.

Inhabitants

A two-headed plesiosaurus roamed in the waters near High Horn.
