The Hall of the Courtiers was part of the complex of the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Activities

This area served as both the living quarters and workplace of the hundreds of scribes, courtiers, and bureaucrats who ran the daily operations of the Palace.

The quarters here were good and the food was excellent. Most of the inhabitants of the Hall of the Courtiers felt that they were overworked, which they were.
