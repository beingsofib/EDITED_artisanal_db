Ewin's Trinkets, originally known as Earvin's Treasures,

Services

Under the ownership of its founder, the shop sold small items of a magical nature. Once it changed hands, Ewin's Trinkets sold locally found treasures, gems, and jewelry.

History

The shop was founded by a wizard of the Arcane Brotherhood, Earvin Fastul, who fled Luskan following the destruction of the Host Tower of the Arcane.
