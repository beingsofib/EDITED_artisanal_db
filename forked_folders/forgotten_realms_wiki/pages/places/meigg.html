Meigg was an enclave of Netheril that was notable for its involvement in the Seven Sigils War.

History

The flying citadel was created by the Netherese domain of Rdiuz in concert with their attempted invasion of the Outlands through the Cat's Gate in , leading to the Seven Sigils War and a century of conflict in both planes.
