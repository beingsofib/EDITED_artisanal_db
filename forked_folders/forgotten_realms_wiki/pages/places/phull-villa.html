Phull Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Phull.

Location

The Phull Villa was located in the North Ward of Waterdeep, on the corner of Phull Lane and Trollkill Street. It stood across from the Snome Villa and to the back of it was Anteos Villa.

Structure

Like many Waterdhavian villas in the North Ward, this was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden with elaborate adornments.

Inhabitants

Besides the members of House Phull, this estate was inhabited by five watchspiders who guarded the family's vault. Whenever there was an intruder the watchspiders would attack in two pairs, while the fifth would ring a bell on the wall near the entrance.
