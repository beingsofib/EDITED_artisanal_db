Hathlock's Rest was an abandoned settlement, once home of foresters, located on the Bowshot road in the Border Kingdoms.

Description

Hathlock's Rest was located north of the Trollhead Bridge that crossed a boggy area of the Owlhold region in the Border Kingdoms. The Bowshot road ran north of the Trollhead Bridge and eventually rose up and away from the swamps and to the site of Hathlock's Rest. The settlement's only amenity was the Hathlock House, a rustic inn that offered humble lodgings and plain food.

History

By the late 14 century DR, Hathlock's Rest was abandoned, and the only sign of life in the area that it had once been a settlement was the Hathlock House inn that catered to travelers across the Bowshot. Apart from guests, the inn served as home to seven hunters and furriers.
