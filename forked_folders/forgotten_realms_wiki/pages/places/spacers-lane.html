The Spacer's Lane was a rough-and-tumble tavern in Yeoman's Loft, Netheril, in the 4 century before Dalereckoning.

Location

The tavern was situated near the spelljamming docks in Helm Harbor.

Inhabitants

The Lane was a favorite hangout for sailors and other unsavory visitors to the city. The establishment was run by an old captain known as Eleron Mistif.

History

Around , Karsus tried to steal all the magic from Mystryl and caused the downfall of Netheril. When this happened, four spelljamming vessels exploded and crashed into the docking district, killing thousands of people, and possible destroying the Spacer's Lane tavern.
