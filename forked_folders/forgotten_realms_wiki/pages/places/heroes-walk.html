Heroes' Walk

Heroes' Walk was a cobblestone, U-shaped street that encircled Heroes' Garden in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep.

Geography

This street both began and terminated at Trollkill Street, forming with it a ring around Heroes' Garden.

On its eastern side, Heroes' Walk was a terminus to Phastal Street and Staghunter's Way. The road then went on to act as a terminus on its southwestern end to Ivory Street, on its southern end to Mendever Street, and on its southeastern end to Skulls Street.

Activities

Every year during the summer, Heroes' Garden and Heroes' Walk were both overtaken by the nobles of the Church of Siamorphe on their holy day, the Divine Pageantry.

Notable Locations

  • Heroes' Garden
  • Tower of Yintros, a school of wizardry that from 1066 DR to 1306 DR occupied the space that would later be taken by the Heroes' Garden.
