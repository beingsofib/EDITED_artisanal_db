The Golden Forge was a secret temple dedicated to Geb, the Father Under the Skies and Sands within the Mulhorandi pantheon.

Location

The Golden Forge was located within a volcano on the island known as the Ship of the Gods in the Alamber Sea. Only Geb's most faithful followers knew the location of this temple.

Activities

Geb's clergy within the Golden Forge spent the vast majority of their time attempting to stop the volcano from erupting, as it threatened to do since the Time of Troubles. Unbeknownst to the outside world, Geb's clergy saved the coasts of Unther from being buried by ash had the volcano erupted.
