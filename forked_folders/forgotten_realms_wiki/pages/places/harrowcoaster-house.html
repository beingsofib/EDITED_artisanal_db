Harrowcoaster House was the main office and warehouse of the Harrowcoaster Coster in Harrowdale where fine textiles, glassware and spices were imported into the Dalelands.

Locations

Harrowcoaster House was located near the shoreline on Moonside Lane.

Inhabitants

The warehouse and coster were run my Captain Durana Shaleel.
