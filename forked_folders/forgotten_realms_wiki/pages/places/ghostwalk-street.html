Ghostwalk (street)

The Ghostwalk was a street in Silverymoon.

Description

The Ghostwalk was located in Northbank. Running west to east for under 500 feet, it began at Dancer's Mask Lane in the west—across from the Golden Oak inn—crossed Bowshot Ride and the Moonway, and ended at the Old Wall.
