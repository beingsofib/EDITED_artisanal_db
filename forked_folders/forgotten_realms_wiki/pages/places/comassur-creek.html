Comassur Creek

Comassur Creek was a small tributary of the Amnurflow in Amn.

Description

Comassur Creek was known as a wonderful area for fishing. Salmon spawned there annually.

Geography

This tiny creek flowed into the Amnurflow five miles northeast of Lake Weng.
