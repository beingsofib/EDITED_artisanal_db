Ongul's Water was a small community in a wooded valley just northwest of Hermit's Wood.

Geography

Ongul's Water was named for a small lake that was filled by a spring that rose there. The lake drained westward into Starwater River near a small forested island known as Hunters' Isle.

Trade

The only significant industry in Ongul's Water was a gristmill run by Durkin, a descendant of the namesake founder Ongul.

Inhabitants

The people of Ongul's Water primarily grew vegetables and raised ducks. Some of the braver ones hunted game in Hermit's Wood, or hired themselves out as guides to visitors despite the fact that the "guides" actually knew very little about the forest itself.
