Yinazzil was a small town found along the coast within the Cities of the Ancients region of east Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

Yinazzil was found approximately southwest of Rog'osto along the coast of Bahr al-Ajami. It lay at the edge of the jungle but was not close to any of the mighty rivers of the Ruined Kingdoms, making the purpose of its location somewhat puzzling.
