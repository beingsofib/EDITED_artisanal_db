Agmoth Road

Agmoth Road was a short road that connected the small town of Moranay with the Royal Road of Impiltur that spanned the length of the Easting Coast.

Traffic along the road was not very busy. However the road was used to convey shipments of gold and silver ore, from the mines of the Earthfast Mountains to the royal mint in the city of Hlammach.
