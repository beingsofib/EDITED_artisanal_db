The Crafts District was a walled section of the capital city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Crafts District was located in the east section of Skuld.

Description

Walls surrounded the Crafts District. A pair of gates allowed access to this area of Skuld–one on the western wall allowing access to the Central Ward, and another on the southern wall near the East Gate.
