Zarach was a small farming community found in the southern region of Ostel in Damara.

Geography

Zarach's location at the convergence of the Great Imphras River and the Sidewinder River made it a prime trading hub for the barony.

Activities

A constant steam of wagons and caravans from outlying communities made Zarach a bustling place to live.

Inhabitants

The largest problem the inhabitants of Zarach faced was deciding which specific market to focus their trade upon. They preferred to stay out of politics so long as the coin continued to flow into their pockets.
