Odd Street

Odd Street was secondary avenue of cobblestone in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Location

This road ran from northeast to southwest, extending off of the intersection of Fish Street and Ship Street until terminating at Dock Street.

Notable Locations

  • Halfway down the street, near its northern edge, stood a fish-shaped stone that if activated would act as a portal to Maldiglas’s Refuge.
  • Alkul's Ice Storage.
  • The Angry Coxswain tavern stood on the intersection of Odd Street and Dock Street.
