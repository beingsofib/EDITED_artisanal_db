Desperately Seeking Selûne is an adventure in the Living City organized play campaign for 2 edition published in 1999. Written by Chris Ruester, it is the finale of the Red Threats series and features two rounds in total; one per difficulty level. Each of the rounds is separately labeled as a chapter, with their events taking place concurrently with one another.

Index

This adventure contains two rounds in a singular document, which are indexed separately on these subpages:

  • Another Rescue (Low Tier; Chapter 3)
  • Dealing With Darkness (High Tier; Chapter 4)
