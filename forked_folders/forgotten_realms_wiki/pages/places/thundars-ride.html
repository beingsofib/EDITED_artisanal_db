Thundar's Ride

Thundar's Ride was a moderately traveled trade road that crossed the Western Heartlands in west Faerûn. It branched southwest off of the Trade Way just south of Boareskyr Bridge and cut across the Fields of the Dead, meeting Skuldask Road at the village of Tempus's Tears. It continued southwest and became lest rustic as it approached the Coast Way. It merged with the Coast Way a few miles north of Baldur's Gate.
