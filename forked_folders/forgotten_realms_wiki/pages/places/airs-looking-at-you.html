Air's Looking at You

Air's Looking at You is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the Living City organized play program and part three of the Elementally Speaking series of modules. It was published in 1998 and is intended for a party of adventurers of any levels. It includes a special issue of the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter.
