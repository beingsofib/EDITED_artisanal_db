Smuggler's Run

Smuggler's Run was a minor avenue of hard packed dirt and gravel in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Location

This alleyway ran from northeast to southwest, extending off of the Southyard and terminating at Sambril Lane.

Notable Locations

  • Gelfuril the Trader, a shop that stood at the intersection of Sambril Lane and Smuggler's Run.
