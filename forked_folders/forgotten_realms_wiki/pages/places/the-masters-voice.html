The Master's Voice was a tavern that once stood in the town of Yûlash in the Moonsea region, ruined by 1357 DR.

Location

The Master's Voice stood at the heart of the ruined Yûlash with an occupying force of the Red Plumes' checkpoint to the northwest, stables across the street to the north, and a bakery next door to the east.

History

After the ruination of Yûlash, The Master's Voice only suggested its previous use through remnants of a bar and copious broken glass and pottery across its taproom. A sign outside read: "The Master's Voice, A Drinking Establishment of Quality." In the , an occasional Red Plume mercenary from the nearby checkpoint snuck into the Master's Voice for an unauthorized nap.
