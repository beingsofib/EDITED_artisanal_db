Tibold was a mining town in the Kuldin Peaks east of the Swimstar Creek.

Layout

Tibold was built on top of an ancient dwarven city. It was said a third of the city was underground in caverns.

History

Tibold was founded as an outpost for High Shanatar. The dwarves and humans banded together to fight off Calimshan's advances after the fall of High Shanatar in .
