The Vampire's Tooth

Building
nameThe Vampire's Tooth

locationLuthcheq, Chessenta

The Vampire's Tooth was a tavern located in the capital city of Luthcheq in Chessenta during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The tavern was located in northeast Luthcheq along the coast of the Bay of Chessenta across the street from the Bloody Roger inn.
