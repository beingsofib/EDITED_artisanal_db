Darchar was an ancient giant-ruled kingdom in Faerûn during the Time of Giants of the Dawn Age ( to ).

Geography

Darchar was roughly located in the eastern portion of the region that later became known as Amn, around the Cloud Peaks mountains north of Keltormir.

History

Darchar was one of the giant kingdoms that warred with the ancient dragon kingdoms during the Dawn Age.

Darchar managed to survive beyond the Thousand Year War, existing well into .
