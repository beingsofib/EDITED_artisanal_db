Fields Lane

Fields Lane was a small road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

Fields Lane was located in the northwest section of Daggerford. It branched south from Wall Street near the Farmer's Gate and terminated at Daggerford's market. Horse Way connected with Fields Lane's northern stretch.

Notable Locations

Notable buildings located along Fields Lane included:

  • The Dragonback Inn
