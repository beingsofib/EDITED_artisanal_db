Wright's Ferry was a small village located in Featherdale.

Location

The hamlet was located along the River Ashaba about halfway between Blackfeather Bridge and Feather Falls

History

Wright's Ferry was home to 30 individuals in the . The hamlet included several orchards and pig farms, the horse ferry for which the village was named, a small pond, and a single tavern. A bizarre ring of stones called the Chessmen of Valsprendar was believed to be linked to some magic ritual.
