The Wary Warrior was a weapon shop in the city of Arabel.

Location

The store was located in the center of the city, on the north side of the High Road before it intersected with the East Way and Calantar's Way. It was west of Szantel's Ropes, Cords, Chains, and Mesh and across the street from the shop of Dazniir Relharphin, the wheelwright.

Services

The shop sold all manner of weapons.
