Al-Naqus River

Al-Naqus River, also called the River of Peril, was a waterway found in the northwest region of the Cities of the Pantheon in Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

The river originated in the foothills of Al-Sayaj Mountains. It flowed west then northwest before meeting the Golden Gulf. The city of Fahhas was founded along its western bank on the gulf.
