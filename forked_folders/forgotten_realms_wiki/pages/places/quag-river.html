Quag River

The Quag River was a small waterway found in the far eastern corner of Calimshan.

Geography

The river flowed through the Spider Swamp. It originated on the northern end of the swamp near the edge of the Forest of Mir and flowed south and southwest for approximately until it reached the coast and the Shining Sea.
