Tchazzam Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Tchazzam.

Location

The villa was located in the western section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward on the northeast corner of where Diamond Street and Seawatch Street joined.

Description

The walled compound contained two buildings. The larger building stood on the north side of the estate. Each building stood between one and two stories tall.
