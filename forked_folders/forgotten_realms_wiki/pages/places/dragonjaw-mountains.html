Dragonjaw Mountains

The Dragonjaw Mountains were mountains in Thesk inhabited by gnomes and copper dragons. The gnomes were frequent visitors to the human city of Milvarune where they traded their crafts. The dragons were known to enter annual joke-telling competitions with the gnomes.
