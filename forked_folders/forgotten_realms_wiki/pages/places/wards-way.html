Ward's Way

Ward's Way was a small road located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

The road was found in southern Waterdeep within the Dock Ward and Castle Ward. Ward's Way ran north to south, originating on Soothsayer's Way just south of Castle Waterdeep and branching south to end at Belnimbra's Street. Along its length Ward's Way was intersected by Lackpurse Lane, Rainrun Street, and Arun's Alley.
