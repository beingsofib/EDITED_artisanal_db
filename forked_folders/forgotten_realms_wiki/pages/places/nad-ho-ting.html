Nad Ho Ting

Nad Ho Ting was a lake in Tabot, sometimes called Nad Ho Ting Tso; "tso" meant "lake" in the Tabotan language.

Geography

Nad Ho Ting lay amongst the mountains of the Kun-Yen Shan in northwestern Tabot and was fed by three tributaries. The towns of Nad Ho and Fij were situated to the south and M'tsin Gompa stood in the northeast.

Description

Nad Ho Ting remained frozen for all but a few months in a year.
