Impresk Lake

Impresk Lake was a lake in Erlkazar.

Geography

The water of Impresk Lake was ice cold. Impresk Lake drained into the Deepwash The lake formed part of the eastern border of the barony of Impresk.

Notable Location

The town of Carradoon, the birthplace of Cadderly Bonaduce, was located on the southern shore of the lake on a small peninsula.
