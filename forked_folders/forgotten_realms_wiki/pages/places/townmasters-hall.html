The Townmaster's Hall, later called the Town Council Hall, was the local government building in Phandalin.

Location

The town hall was located just south of the center of the village.

Structure

The hall was a single story building made of good quality stone and was well built. The roof was made of wood with a bell tower rising from the back.

Interior

The hall contained office space for the leader(s) of Phandalin as well as a small jail with two cells.

History

When Phandalin was first resettled in the late 15 century DR, the Townmaster's Hall was the office of Townmaster Harbin Wester, although he was known to also conduct official business from his personal home nearby.

After only a few years, confidence in the Townmaster had plummeted, and the position was dissolved and replaced with a Town Council consisting of Harbin Wester, Sildar Hallwinter, and Halia Thornton. While their meeting place continued to be known as the Townmaster's Hall for some time, it was eventually renamed to the "Town Council Hall." By the , the town was undergoing a new political reorganization, and plans had been made to expand the hall to include a mayor's office.
