Goblintide River

The Goblintide River, also known as Gaurlynlur to the dwarves, traveled through the eastern Lurkwood where it joined with Shining Creek before emptying into the River Surbrin. The matriarchal Urlbluk tribe of kobolds lived near the Goblintide's headwaters.
