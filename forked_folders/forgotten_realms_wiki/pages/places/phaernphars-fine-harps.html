Phaernphar's Fine Harps was an instrument shop specializing in harps in the city of Silverymoon in the Silver Marches in the late 14 century DR. It stood on Candlestar Lane in the city's Northbank. It was owned by Altymo Phaernphar and used strings made by Chesmyr Morrowynd circa 1372 DR.
