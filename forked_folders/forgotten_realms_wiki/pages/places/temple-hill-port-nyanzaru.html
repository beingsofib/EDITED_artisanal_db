Temple Hill (Port Nyanzaru)

Temple Hill was the westernmost of the four hills in Chult atop which Port Nyanzaru was built. It was located on the northern side of the Merchants' Ward.

Description

The top of Temple Hill was dominated by the Temple of Savras, which was also the hill's namesake. The hill was quite steep, and the upper area was completely walled-off from the lower ground, making it generally an exclusive area for the city's wealthy.
