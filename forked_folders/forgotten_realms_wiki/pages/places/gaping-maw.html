Gaping Maw

The Gaping Maw, was the 88 level of the Abyss and the home of Demogorgon, Prince of Demons.

Reflecting the dual personality of its master, the Gaping Maw was a vast continent covered in a jungle and surrounded an equally vast ocean with brine flats. Rising from the sea were twin towers, topped with enormous fanged skulls that marked the fortress Abysm, a labyrinth of dark and cold underwater caves. Few could enter without succumbing to madness.
