Cantrev Llewellyn was a small coastal city located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Description

It was a plain and prototypical Ffolk settlement. Several hundred cottages filled the town, surrounding a few larger structures including several inns and taverns.

Geography

Llewellyn was located along the High King's Road in southern Callidyrr in the county of Stirgewick. The town sat on Alaron's west coast along the Strait of Alaron on the north end of Llewellyn Harbor within Bregonshire.

Government

As of the , Llewellyn was led by a Lord Llewellyn.

Defenses

A stone tower located about inland from the shore provided a degree of protection against raiding Northlanders.

Trade

The village relied on the rich fishing in Llewellyn Harbor, and clashed over rights to the waters with Kythyss to the south.

History

During the reign of High King Reginald Carrathal, he failed to intervene in the disputes between Llewellyn and Kythyss, leading to Kythyss hiring mercenaries that threatened both Llewellyn's fishers and perhaps the town's very autonomy.

The cantrev grew from a large fishing village in the early-to-mid 14 century DR (around which time it was visited by Elminster) to the size of a city by the late 15 century DR.

Inhabitants

In addition to the Ffolk, a large community of halflings resided within the town and had additionally excavated burrows in the hills near the defensive tower. These halflings operated many of the inns and taverns catering to humans in the town.

A band of doppelgangers were known to live in and around Llewellyn, and were known to impersonate travelers in hopes of ambushing unsuspecting victims on the roads and in the inns.
