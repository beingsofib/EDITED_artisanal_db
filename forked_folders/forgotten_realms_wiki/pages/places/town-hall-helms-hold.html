The Town Hall of Helm's Hold, formerly the Dragon's Gauntlet inn, was a public meeting place for the city's residents and its governing body.

Location

It was located in the center of the city, not far from the Heartward marketplace and a short ways away from the Venturer's Rest inn.

Activities

The hall was the official statehouse for the Hold's council of Speakers, eight individuals who were elected among the city's residents. It also served as a public meeting place for the non-political citizens of Helm's Hold. It was often the site of loud shouting matches that exemplified the uncompromising and individualistic nature of the populace.
