The Ruined Outpost was a former fortification of the Purple Dragons of Cormyr.

Geography

The outpost was located on the north side of the High Road as it entered the foothills of the Storm Horns west of Tyrluk, placed to overlook the road as the land grew rougher and harder to travel.

History

By the mid-1480s DR, the outpost was long abandoned and fallen to ruin, being little more than crumbled stone and rotted wood. During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, the withdrawal of the Purple Dragons from the High Road allowed the enemies of Cormyr to use the outpost as a base of operations in plain sight, including the bandits of Theodas Riveron and the Shadovar of Thultanthar who sought the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll.
