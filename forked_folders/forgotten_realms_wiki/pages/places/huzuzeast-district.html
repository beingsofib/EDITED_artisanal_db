The East District was a district of Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Description

The East District was found in the Palace Quarter and made-up most of the eastern section of the city along the city walls. This district was bordered by the Affluent District in the south, the Court District in the west, and the Craft District in the north. The Avenue of Wealth rang along much of the East District's western border, while the Ghost's Route framed it from the north.

Inhabitants

A portion of Huzuz's military was quartered within the East District. Though many caravanserai could be found around all of the gates of Huzuz, the East District was one of only two districts within the city walls to house a caravanserai neighborhood.
