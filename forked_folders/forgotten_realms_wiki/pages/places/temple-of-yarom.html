The Temple of Yarom was a temple in Telflamm in the late 14 century DR.

History

In the , Chun of the Nine Golden Swords ordered Raidon Kane to slay everyone in the Temple of Yarom so as to prove his intent to join the organization. Per Chun, the temple preached salvation which lay beyond life and the gods, doubting even their divinity.
