Sahhar Canal

The Sahhar Canal was a prominent avenue located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR.

Location

The canal was located in the western section of the city within the Rookery and Iskalat.

Description

The canal originated on the north end of the City of Brass' only harbor. It ran due north through the western section of Iskalat and eventually terminated in the north section of the Rookery at Maboul Fennic Square. It was bridged by the Street of the Vine near its midpoint.

The Sahhar Canal was filled with lava from the Sea of Fire.
