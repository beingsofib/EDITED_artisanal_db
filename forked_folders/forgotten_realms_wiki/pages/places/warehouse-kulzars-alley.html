This unnamed warehouse was a warehouse located on Kulzar's Alley in Waterdeep.

Location

This building was adjoined to the buildings known as the Gildenfires and Roarke House, being situated right between them.

History

In the late 15 century DR, this warehouse and the buildings adjoining it were all owned by noble family Neverember. Around this time it was leased longterm to the noble families Ammakyl and Gralleth.

In Eleint of 1479 DR, an inspection of the warehouse found that half of it was filled with older furniture and decorations from three separate instances in which Jadzia Ammakyl decided that her mansion wasn't up to the latest Waterdhavians fashions and needed updating.

That autumn inspection also found that the other half of the warehouse was filled by the Gralleths with the belongings of noble villas the family had obtained after absorbing the interests and estates of the noble families Bladesemmer and Markarl.
