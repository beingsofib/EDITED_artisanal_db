Changeland

A changeland was a generic term adopted in the 15 century DR to refer to any magical geographic area or feature that did not fit with the surrounding terrain, generally as a leftover of the cataclysmic effects of the Spellplague.

Notable Changelands

Examples of changelands included lands torn to pieces by the Spellplague—producing both earthmotes and chasms. More broadly, any geographic change brought about by the Spellplague could be called a changeland. This included:

  • The elevated Thayan Plateau
  • The Underchasm
  • The ruination of Unther
  • The Bonedark Hills on the border of Thesk and Thay
