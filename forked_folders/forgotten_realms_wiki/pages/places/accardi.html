Accardi was a small coastal village located on the Dragon Coast in interior Faerûn around 1479 DR.

Geography

Accardi was located on the shore of the Neck, the strait between the Dragonmere and the Sea of Fallen Stars, and lay opposite Dawngleam in Cormyr. It was connected to the city of Westgate by a trail.
