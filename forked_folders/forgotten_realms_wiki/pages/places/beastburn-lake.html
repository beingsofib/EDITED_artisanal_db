Beastburn Lake

Beastburn Lake was a small body of water found in northern Dambrath.

Geography

Beastburn Lake sat at the halfway point between the Gnollwatch Mountains and the southern Lluirwood along the west to east stretch of the River Ammath where it flowed just north of the Hills of the Dead Kings. The only settlement of note on this small lake was North Post on its northwest shore.
