Royal Docks (Port Nyanzaru)

Location
nameRoyal Docks

regionPort Nyanzaru, Chult

The Royal Docks of Port Nyanzaru in Chult were an enclosed section of the city's Harbor Ward that contained docks reserved for the exclusive use of the city's merchant princes or their honored guests.

Description

The Royal Docks were only used by the merchant princes or those whom they wished to impress, generally meaning foreign dignitaries, ambassadors, or other powerful merchants.

The name "Royal Docks" was derived from a time when Chult had been ruled by literal kings and queens as opposed to Merchant Princes.

Geography

The Royal Docks were located at one end of the harbor, separated from the public docks by the drydocks and Warehouse District.
