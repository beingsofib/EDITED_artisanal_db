Cholandrothipe's Tower was the prominent landmark found in the village of Feather Falls in Featherdale in the .

Description

The tower was a fire-blackened ruin that resembled a large slender cylinder as of 1367 DR. A single porch-like entrance allowed access to the tower. The tower appeared small from the outside but was rumored to plunge deep into the earth and cross beneath the River Ashaba to emerge on the other side of the river. Some of the tower's lowest chambers were rumored to lead to the Deep Realms.

Inhabitants

The tower was once the home of the wizard Cholandrothipe.

History

The tower was ruined by its own defenses when a group of mages from Thay attempted to break into the tower to steal Cholandrothipe's gold and magic items.

Defenses

Several golems, xorn, and a ghost served as guardians for the tower. A living wall formed part of the tower's structure as well. Cholandrothipe also enchanted several traps that triggered wands of withering and polymorphing. Some of Cholandrothipe's treasure chests concealed deadly oozes that were released as soon as the chests were opened. Gate traps transported unwanted visitors to sealed rooms that contained other deadly monsters. These rooms were only escapable by using different gates that transported the individual to the ceiling of a different room and dropped them for a long fall.

Adventurers who explored the tower reported that they discovered several invisible doors along the tower's stairwells. Other doors opened from the outer walls into other dimensional regions where treasure was kept safe behind deadly spell webs.
