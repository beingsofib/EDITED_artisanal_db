Downybeard Tobacconist was a shop that specialized in the sale of tobacco products located in the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The shop was situated in the south section of Waterdeep's North Ward on the southeast corner of Undal's Lane where it joined Golden Serpent Street.
