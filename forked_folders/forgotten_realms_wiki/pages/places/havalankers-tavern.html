Havalanker's Tavern

Building
nameHavalanker's Tavern
typeTavern
locationTulmon, Land of the Lions

Havalanker's Tavern was located in the town of Tulmon on the Lake of Steam in the late 14 century DR.

Description

The tavern was aging and poorly lit, making all of the booths dark. The layout was confusing to patrons, but the tankards were generously large.

Activities

Havalanker's Tavern was a favorite for travelers and locals alike. They served meals that were usually overcooked and loaded with gravy. The dim atmosphere made it a great place for cover meetings.
