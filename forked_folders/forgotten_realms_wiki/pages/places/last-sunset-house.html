Last Sunset House

Building
nameThe Last Sunset House
typeInn, tavern
locationYhaunn, Sembia

The Last Sunset House was an inn and tavern found in Yhaunn in 1358 DR.

Services

Although the establishment wasn't counted among Yhaunn's finer inns, it provided surprisingly good service for customers while being very cheap.
