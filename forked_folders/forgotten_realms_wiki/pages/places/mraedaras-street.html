Mraedara's Street

Mraedara's Street was a road that crossed the entirety of The Shutters neighborhood in the Crow's End District of Ravens Bluff from north to south, and ended in the Pumpside neighborhood.

Geography

Mraedara's Street was only three city blocks in length. It began at Karen Court in The Shutters in the north, and moved straight south, crossing Hammerstone Street, Drover's Run, and ended on Conant Court.

Notable Locations

  • Fellowship of Exalted Adventurers, a young boys' club formed in the late 14 century DR.
