Tyraturos was city located in the country of Thay during the mid–14 century DR.

Geography

The city in the central region of the tharch of Tyraturos north of Bezantur.

History

Tyraturos was founded during an age of expansion of Mulhorand under the leadership of Anhurtep. The city was eventually claimed by Thay and was part of that country as of the .
