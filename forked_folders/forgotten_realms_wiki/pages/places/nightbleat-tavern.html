Nightbleat Tavern

Building
namethe Nightbleat Tavern
typeTavern
locationBospir, Cormyr

The Nightbleat Tavern was the lone tavern found in the small village of Bospir in Cormyr in the 1360s DR.

Activity

Brawls were common within this establishment as this was the only logical place in Bospir where the inebriated sheepshearers could unload on each other.
