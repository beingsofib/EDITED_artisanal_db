Bitter Well was an oasis located in the desert of Anauroch, in north Faerûn.

Geography

The oasis was located on the eastern side of the region known as the Sword, south of El Ma'ra and the Bowl of Loneliness.

History

In the , the oasis was taken over by the Zhentarim spellcaster Yhekal, and a contingent of the Zhentilar army led the warrior known as Zarud. Their presence troubled some tribesmen of the Bedine people, and even the great place spirit of Rahalat.
