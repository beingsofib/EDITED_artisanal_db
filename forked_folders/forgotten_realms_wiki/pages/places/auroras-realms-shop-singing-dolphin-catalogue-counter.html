Aurora's Realms Shop, Singing Dolphin Catalogue Counter was an outlet of the Aurora's Emporium chain in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. It was one of six such outlets in the city.

Location

It lay on the Street of the Singing Dolphin, on the western side and three shops north of the corner with Grimwald's Way.

Description

It was quite opulent and displayed all the finest and priciest items in the catalogue.

Services

This shop sold the various goods found in Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Staff

In the mid-1360s DR, the handsome Orloth Theldarin worked here as a counter clerk with a reputation for taste and tact alike, and Saerghon "the Magnificent" Alir was the service-mage, and quite arrogant for his mundane trade. There were also six guards who worked shifts of three on and three off and were noted for their shiny weapons and fine uniforms.
