Misbahd was a village on the sparsely populated island of Sahu in the Crowded Sea of east Zakhara in the early 1400s DR.. The village was located on the northern coast below the plateau dominating the island's interior.

Inhabitants

The majority of the inhabitants were concerned with piracy and dealing with goods gained that way.

Defenses

Misbahd was also protected by fortifications and a number of allied ships.
