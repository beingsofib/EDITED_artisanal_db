The Bear and Black Buckler was an inn inside of the fortified village Xantharl's Keep.

Interior

The interior of the inn was dimly lit and its beds were covered by bear pelts.

Services

The inn offered an assortment of mixed-meat stews as well as potato dishes that were seasoned with a variety of spices.
