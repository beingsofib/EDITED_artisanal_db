Launtok was a town in Athalantar during the 3 century DR.

Description

Launtok was a large town.

History

In Eleasias of the , the prince Elminster Aumar and the sorceress Myrjala Talithyn decided to begin their plan to besiege Athalgard and overthrow King Belaur while at a tavern in Launtok.

Inhabitants

  • Urdan of Launtok, a writer in the 3 century DR
