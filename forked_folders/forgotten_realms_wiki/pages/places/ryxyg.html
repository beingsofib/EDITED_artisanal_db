Ryxyg was a mind flayer enclave in the Deep Wastes, located a few miles beneath Sporedome.

Trade

The mind flayers of Ryxyg engaged in peaceful trade with the myconids of Sporedome.

History

In the late 15 century DR, the mind flayers of Ryxyg allied with the myconids of Sporedome and with a group of adventurers to repel an invading force of fire giants from Szith Morcane.

Inhabitants

The inhabitants of Ryxyg were not as aggressive as mind flayers of other colonies, and did not actively pursue slaves. However, they did not refrain from enslaving attackers to the city.

In the late 1480s DR, the ambassador of Ryxyg was the mind flayer Glorghakt.
