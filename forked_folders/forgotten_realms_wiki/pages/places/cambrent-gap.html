The Cambrent Gap was a mountain pass located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The Cambrent Gap split the Cambro Mountains toward their northern end. The pass connected the eastern edge of Myrloch Vale with eastern Gwynneth.
