Glorianus was one of the twelve moons of Garden in Realmspace.

Description

Glorianus was a very strange celestial object shaped like a crescent about by . The moon was covered with a soft, green grass.

Inhabitants

The moon was inhabited by a single human, a druid named Griffon Broadleaf.
