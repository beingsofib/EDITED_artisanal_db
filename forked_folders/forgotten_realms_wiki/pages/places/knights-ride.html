Knights' Ride

Knights' Ride was a main avenue in Tilverton.

Description

Knights' Ride was in the Old Town of Tilverton, a short length of road that connected a number of important locations. At its western end it met the Cormyr Stairs and the ruins of Tilver's Palace, before it ran along the northern curve of the Old Town walls and intersected with Gateguard Road, before ending at the Street of the Sorceress near the Market Stairs.
