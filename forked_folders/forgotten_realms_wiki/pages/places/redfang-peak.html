Redfang Peak

Redfang Peak was a mountain located in the southern reaches of the Giantspire Mountains.

History

In the , King Lashilmbrar commissioned a mercenary band known as the Gauntlet of Doom to explore the area around Redfang Peak as part of a longstanding effort to find any trace of King Imbrar I, who disappeared somewhere in the area during the . While the mercenaries never returned to Impiltur, their captain, Delros Parander, was subsequently seen wielding the famous royal Impilturan sword known as Malagar, which he presumably discovered while on that expedition.
