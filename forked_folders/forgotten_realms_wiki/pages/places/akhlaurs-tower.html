Akhlaur's Tower

Building
nameAkhlaur's Tower
typeTower
locationAkhlaur Swamp, Halruaa
ownerAkhlaur

Akhlaur's tower was created by the eponymous wizard in 12th-century DR.

The tower resided in the middle of the swamp that bore the spellcaster's name and had long since been abandoned since he called it home. As the waters of the swamp grew, the tower sunk, and became partially submerged. In the Year of Wild Magic the tower stood out enough to still be entered and explored.

Considering how powerful a wizard Akhlaur was, there was likely a wealth of magical items and arcane secrets inside.
