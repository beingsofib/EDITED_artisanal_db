Dragon Rock was one of the twelve moons of Garden in Realmspace.

Description

Like most of Garden's moons, Dragon Rock was larger than its parent, being about and wide. It was bizarrely shaped like a white dragon and was composed entirely of white jade.

The slight atmosphere of Dragon Rock smelled like pipe smoke.

Inhabitants

The moon was inhabited by an evil wizard known as Darkon of the Earth's Depths, who was also responsible for the creation of the moon. He built the massive structure to observe and study Garden.
