Warrior Street

Warrior Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 century DR.

Location

Located in the Common Quarter in northwest Zhentil Keep, Warrior Street ran between Border Street in the west and Wizards' Way in the east. It was intersected by Goblin Way, Gemstone Street, and Fortress Street.

Notable Locations

A Zhentilar barracks was found at the intersection of Goblin Way with Warrior Street, and a large armory sat next to the barracks. The Silver Trumpet Inn was found at the eastern end of Warrior Street.
