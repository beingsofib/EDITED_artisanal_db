The Undercity, also known as the Eldercity, was a ruined, underground city located in vast cavern under Baldur's Gate.

Description

The remains of an older city that once stood there, the Undercity consisted mostly of broken-down shells of buildings haunted by undead.

Geography

An entrance to the Undercity was found through the thieves' maze located underneath the connected safe houses used by Baldur's Gate's thieves' guild.

Notable Locations

Within the Undercity was an abandoned Temple of Bhaal. During the iron crisis of the mid–14 century DR, it was used by Sarevok Anchev as a secret lair and hideaway.

Inhabitants

The various undead that walked the ruined streets included skeletons, skeleton warriors, ghouls and ghasts.
