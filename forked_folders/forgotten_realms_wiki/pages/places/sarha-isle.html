Sarha Isle was a small island found just off the southwest shore of Afyal in the Cities of the Ancients region in east Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

The island was located just offshore from the mainland of Afyal. It was also due east of the Horn of Sahu, and northeast from the mainland of Sahu.

Activities

Afyal kept soldiers stationed in a qal'at on the north side of Sarha Isle, perhaps to keep a close watch on Sahu.
