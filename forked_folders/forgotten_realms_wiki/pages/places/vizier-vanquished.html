The Vizier Vanquished was an inn in northern Calimshan and a favourite of Almer Galarthund during his time as the Magister. It was destroyed during the Night of Fourteen Magisters.

Location

The inn was somewhere in northern Calimshan.

Defenses

The inn had hired Belmer of the Six Thunders as its "firestop" mage.

History

The inn was the location of the Night of Fourteen Magisters in Eleasis 4 of the , where twelve clones of Zunroun Ilber ambushed Almer Galarthund; he slew three of them before being slain, and the nine remaining turned on each other, bringing the building down.
