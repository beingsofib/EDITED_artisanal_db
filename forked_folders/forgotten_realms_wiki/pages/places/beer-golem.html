The Beer Golem was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The building stood two stories tall.

Location

The Beer Golem was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter. The front of the tavern sat along Kerrigan's Court while the west side of the building touched Snake Alley.
