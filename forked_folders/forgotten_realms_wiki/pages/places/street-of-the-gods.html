Street of the Gods

The Street of the Gods was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Located in the Inner Quarter in central Zhentil Keep, the Street of the Gods cut north to south, touching the wall of the Common Quarter and ending south Elephstron Way. Along the way, the Street of the Gods was crossed by Gold Street and the Lord's Way.

Notable Locations

The residences of Lord Phandymm, Lord Ssorg and Lord Halaster were located on the Street of the Gods. Zhentil Keep's Arena, a Zhentarim warehouse, and a Zhentilar guardpost were other notable locations.
