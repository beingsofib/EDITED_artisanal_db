The Boar's Tusk was a tavern in the city of Liara in Nova Vaasa, one of the Domains of Dread.

Location

It was in the Merchant's Quarter of Liara.

Structure

It was a two-story structure, with the inn registry and an adjoining tavern on the first floor and twenty guest rooms on the upper story.

Interior

Each guest room had a comfortable bed, a work table, a wash basin, and a bathtub.

Services

The Boar's tusk had drinks and beds.

History

In the mid-to-late 14 century DR, adventurers from Ravens Bluff somehow came to the Domains of Dread, finding their way to Liara at some point. There, the directions they asked for got them to the Boar's Tusk in the Merchant's Quarter, where they were most likely hired by Turkin Varringore to catch a suspected vampire who had killed six people so far. Among the payment they were offered, there were five nights at the tavern/inn itself.
