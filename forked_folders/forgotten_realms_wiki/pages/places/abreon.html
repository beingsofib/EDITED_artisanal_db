Abreon was a large town in Lapaliiya in southwest Faerûn in the mid–14th century DR.

Geography

This inland town lay amidst low rolling hills. These were strewn with fields, vineyards, and olive groves.

Government & Religion

Around 1373 DR, it was governed by a council of wealthy farmers.

The civic deity of Abreon was Lliira the Joybringer.

History

The area was once the location of a great battle fought between the besieged defenders of Lapaliiya and the invading forces of Serpentes, which finally conquered the land in the Year of Sunned Serpents, –189 DR.

Locations

Barrows dating back to the last battle between Lapaliiya and Serpentes still lay hidden in the fields around Abreon in the 14th century DR. Occasionally, undead humanoids and serpentfolk creeped out to terrorize the people of the area.
