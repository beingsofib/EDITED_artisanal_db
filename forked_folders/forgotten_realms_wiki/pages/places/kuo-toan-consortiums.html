The Kuo-Toan Consortiums were some kind of ancient kuo-toa nation in Serôs beneath the Sea of Fallen Stars.

Geography

The Kuo-Toan Consortiums consisted of a number of cavernous cities along the Plains of Duna.

Culture

The kuo-toans of this nation widely engaged in the practice of slavery, primarily of the locathahs.

History

The Second Serôs War began in , with the war's combatants being the Kuo-Toan Consortiums and the sea elf empire of Aryselmalyr. The elves would rally the enslaved locathah against their masters, as well as various crabmen and merfolk. A great deal of the surviving kuo-toa of the nation would flee into the Underdark through the the Tunnels of Iratis, eventually founding the city of Leshnymul. Others were said to have escaped into the Underdark by entering through the depths surrounding the Aglarondan Peninsula.
