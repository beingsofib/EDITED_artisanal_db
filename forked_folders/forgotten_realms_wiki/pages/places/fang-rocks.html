The Fang Rocks,

Geography

The Fang Rocks were situated near the halfway point in the waters between Kelthann and Dragonisle,

Description

Most of the reef was only visible at low tide, making it particularly dangerous to incoming vessels unaware of the region's geography. They covered an area of sea roughly in radius.

History

A fleet of twenty warships from Sembia was sent to destroy Immurk and his band of followers in the Pirate Isles in 1180 DR. Unaware of the hidden Fang Rocks, all but three of the warships were destroyed by the reef.
