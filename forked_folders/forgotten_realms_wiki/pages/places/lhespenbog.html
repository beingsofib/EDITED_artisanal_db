The Lhespenbog was a small wetland found in the western Shaar near the border of Lapaliiya.

Geography

The bog was located along the southeastern shore of Lake Lhespen at the northeastern edge of the Shaareach. The Lhespenbog was bordered to south and east by the Rathgaunt Hills.
