High Hillock Lane

High Hillock Lane was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

High Hillock Lane was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward. It ran north to south in between Niles Way in the north and Soldier's Street in the south. High Hillock Lane also connected with Crossbow Lane and Guards Court.
