Staerngates was the estate of the wealthy Thonok family of Purskul in Amn.

History

Staerngates was a fine manse built by Staernel Thonok, the family's founder, who was renowned for his financial acumen yet infamous for dabbling in necromancy. Staerngates was still the Thonok seat of power, long after Staernel's "death," in the late 14 century DR.
