Anauril, also known as the City of Swords was the one of the cities of ancient Anauria.

Geography

The city was located northeast of Amazandar the capital of Anauria, just due west of the Twisted Tower of Ashaba.

Trade

Anauril was famous for the great quality steels swords crafted by the finest smiths in all the lands north of the Moonsea. They were often granted enchantments by the mages of House Nyntynel and House Olyrnn.

History

The city was founded some time after Netheril's fall in , as the enclave of Anauria grew into a grand kingdom.

In the , goblinoid and orcish forces led by the shaman Andegril the Wise, caused the collapse of the kingdom of Anauria and all of the cities within. Nearly all of the orcs besieging the city were slain at the hands of Anauril's skilled warriors, their corpses splain by the city's famed blades.

Inhabitants

Centuries after the city's fall, it remained protected by the many sword wraiths that haunted its ruins.
