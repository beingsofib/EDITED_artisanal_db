Dathantar’s Crawl

Dathantar's Crawl was an alleyway of hard-packed dirt and gravel in the North Ward of Waterdeep.

Location

This curving alley ran north off of the Beaconmarch, cut through Selvur Alley, and terminated in a dead-end.

History

Dathantar's Crawl was named for Dathantar, a famous nightsoil wagon operator in the 1200s DR whose wagon was said to "crawl" along the street, as Dathantar often stopped at home to eat and left the stench of the nightsoil in his wagon to linger.
