Shaleel Warehouses were a trio of busy and well-guarded warehouses owned by the Harrowcoaster Coster company in the 14 century DR, located in Harrowdale Town in the Dalelands.

Location

Shaleel Warehouses stood on Cart Street, just north of Moonside Lane, and west of The Harrow Plow pier.

Structure

The three all-stone warehouses were decorated with the Harrowcoaster Coster insignia, the same image that decorated all of their shipping crates and packages: a circle of gold coins encircling a ship with its sail up high.

Services

When Harrowcoaster Coster imported goods into Harrowdale, they were repackaged and stored in Shaleel Warehouses until the coaster received sale orders for the goods to be shipped. The goods were imported without corresponding pre-orders from the buyers—a risky strategy that often paid off in the speed of order fulfillment. The selection of stored goods was wide, including such exotics as silk dancer's costumes from Calimshan.
