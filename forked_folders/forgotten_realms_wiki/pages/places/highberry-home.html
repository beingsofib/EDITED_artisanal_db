Highberry Home was a large manse located in the Lower City of Baldur's Gatein the late 15 century DR.

Location

Highberry Home stood next-door to Bonecloak's Apothecary, north of the Sorcerous Sundries shop in the Heapside neighborhood of Baldur's Gate.

Structure

Highberry Home was a spacious three-story building with a big open space used for festivals. Its first floor was a large wine cellar.

History

In the , Highberry Home hosted a fashionable wine festival filled with guests, servers, and gossip. The hosts – gnome spouses Cora Highberry and Roger Highberry, wow busy mingling with the guests, their wards – orphans Molly and Timmy, inside the manse, all unaware that mistress Cora was marked for death by a Bhaalist serial killer Dolor and his special poison, Karabasan's Gift. It was unrecorded if the assassination attempt on Cora Highberry was successful and if the festival was ruined by Dolor and his doppelganger murderers.
