The Smoke Wyrm was a tavern in Ravens Bluff in the Vast run by the dwarf Tharzon Brewhammer and his son Kurzen Brewhammer in the late 15 century DR. The tavern was best known for its excellent stout known as Old Smokey, which was shipped all over the Vast.

Structure

The tavern had a sign that depicted a sleeping dragon blowing smoke out of his nose.
