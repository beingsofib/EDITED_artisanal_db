Mielikki's Grove was a woodland holy site in the King's Forest of Cormyr, dedicated to the goddess of nature and forests Mielikki.

Description

The grove was a picturesque stretch of woodlands, and included a few artificial shrines. The hag Granny Gertrude lived nearby in her hut.

History

Not long after the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the late 15 century DR, the Red Wizard Dhovras invaded the grove. He slew a unicorn upon the stones of Mielikki's shrine, then took its remains to be reanimated as a skeletal beast loyal to him. He also began animating the ancient remains of everyone who had perished within the forest, creating a small but growing army of undead. Mielikki felt this outrageous profanity in her very essence and raised up a number of blights to destroy the wizard, but he proved stronger than them. The hag Granny Gertrude was likewise powerless to stop him, as to fight him meant fighting the unicorn, and she had sworn not to harm any creatures of Mielikki's forest. The situation was ultimately resolved when the Evenor forester Jarra Morand noticed the rampaging blights and sent some folks to investigate, upon which they discovered the horrid situation unfolding.

The local heroes found the hag and were willing to believe she was innocent, and then found the shrine soaked in unicorn blood, where Mielikki herself spoke to encourage them onwards. After perhaps cleaning up the bloody scene, they proceeded on and found the Red Wizard working on his unicorn project. They managed to destroy the unfortunate creature and put it to rest, which gave Granny Gertrude the opportunity to strike at Dhovras next. He was captured alive by her magics and taken away to an unknown but undoubtedly nasty fate.
