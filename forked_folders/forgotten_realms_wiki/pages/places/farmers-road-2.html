Farmer's Road

The Farmer's Road was a road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The Farmer's Road in northcentral Daggerford left the northern arm of Wall Street at the Farmer's Gate and cut south, then east between the Happy Cow and River Shining Tavern, and south once more to Duke's Way. Its segment between Market Way and the Farmer's Gate was sometimes referred to as Field's Lane.

Notable Locations

Notable buildings located along the Farmer's Road included:

  • The Grand Playhouse
  • The Harvest House
  • Farmers' Society
  • The Happy Cow
