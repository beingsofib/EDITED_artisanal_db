The Twin Towers of the Eternal Eclipse were a pair of towers hidden high in the Small Teeth mountains in the Lands of Intrigue, one on each side of the Trade Way. Formerly temples to Bane, they were seized and rededicated as temples to Cyric in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The towers were about 5 miles (8 kilometers) apart, deep in the highest peaks of the Small Teeth Mountains between Amn and Muranndin. The towers were almost completely obscured from view, even from above.

Structure

Each tower was five stories tall and they were connected by a twisting underground passage.

Inhabitants

As of 1370 DR, Blackwill Haarken Akhmelere was in charge of the church of Cyric congregation.

History

Originally temples dedicated to Bane, Cyricists conquered the towers in the , and rededicated them to the worship of Cyric.

In the , the Sojourner destroyed the western tower to get at the magical granite it was made out of. He moved it to atop the Wayrock in the Sea of Fallen Stars for his great ritual. After his defeat, this tower was later made a temple to Mask by the assassin and priest Drasek Riven.

During the Rage of Dragons that same year, Iryklathagra badly damaged the eastern tower but on Uktar 13 of the same year, Akhmelere used the Golden Lamp of Samesaj to rebuild the western tower and repair the eastern tower.

On Flamerule 12 of the , after a fifty-day siege, the Army of Three Stars successfully conquered the western tower, handing it over to Cyricist members of the crusade.
