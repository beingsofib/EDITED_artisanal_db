The Hall of the Enlightened One was a shrine and part of the Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert of the Hordelands during the mid 14 century DR.

Description

The Hall was located on the ground level of the temple on the right side of the Grand Stairs. The Hall itself was roughly long while the chamber at the end of the Hall measured wide by long.

A trio of statues of the Enlightened One were found within the chamber, though the heads of each statue had been shattered by the . Frescoes adorned the walls of the chamber, but these had been defaced. A single small altar to Li Pei sat upon the chamber's central dais.
