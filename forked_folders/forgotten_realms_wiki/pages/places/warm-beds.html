Warm Beds was an inn located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The inn stood three storys tall.

Location

Warm Beds was located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward where Presper Street met Ship Street.

Inhabitants

A sage named Kromnlor Sernar lived within the inn.
