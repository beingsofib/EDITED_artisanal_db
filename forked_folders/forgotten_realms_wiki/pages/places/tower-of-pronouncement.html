The Tower of Pronouncement was a sacred tower located in the capital city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Tower was located at the entrance into the City of the Gods in the northcentral part of the city. It was flanked on both sides by the Temple of Horus-Re.

History

The Tower of Pronouncement was used by Mulhorand's God-Kings to make announcements, though this rare occurrence usually only happened once a century.
