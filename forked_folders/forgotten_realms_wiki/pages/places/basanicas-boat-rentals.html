Basanicas Boat Rentals

nameBasanicas Boat Rentals

Basanicas Boat Rentals was a rental shop found in the southwestern corner of Nesmé.

Description

The rental shop lent out boats to travelers for about 25 gp, but required a 20 gp deposit. A huge sign was set above the door to the shop, and was carved into the shape of a longboat, where the name was printed.
