The mound of the First Shrine of the Queen of Air was the holiest site for followers of Akadi, the Lady of the Winds.

Location

The shrine was located in the ruins of Blaskaltar on the open plains of the western Shaar.

Description

The shrine was a neglected ruin as of the .

Activities

Akadi's faithful gathered at the shrine on a yearly basis during Midsummer to remember their church's history and those who served it. They did this in order to avoid repeating the mistakes of the past so as not to doom their future. New names were added to the roll at this time commemorating those of the clergy who passed on during the year.
