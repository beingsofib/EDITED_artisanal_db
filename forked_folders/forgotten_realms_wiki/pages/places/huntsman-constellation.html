Huntsman (constellation)

The Huntsman—as it was called in the North—was a constellation visible in Faerûn in the spring. It was known as the Hourglass in the Lands of Intrigue. To the Tuigan of the Endless Wastes, it was the Horse-Bowman. The group of stars making up the constellation looked something like a lopsided hourglass with three bright stars at the middle.
