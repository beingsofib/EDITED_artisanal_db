The Execution Yard was a site for public execution located located in the city of Luthcheq in Chessenta during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Execution Yard was located in the southwest perimeter of the city just northwest of the south gate.

Description

This area was a separate walled section of Luthcheq accessible by a single gate that opened near the stables. The yard was triangular in shape.
