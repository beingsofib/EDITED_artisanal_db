Raudilauth was a valley located within Anauroch.

Geography

Raudilauth was a long and narrow valley, its course carved from the river that once flowed in its place. The valley was found in the south-eastern edge of Anauroch, south of Alauthwaerd in the Desertsmouth Mountains and connected with the Shadow Gap.

Description

Due to its location, the valley was used by merchants to reach the territories of the D'tarig.
