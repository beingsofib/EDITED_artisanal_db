Flask Circle was a small shopping district in the Anvil neighborhood of Ravens Bluff, referred to by some as a "boutique district." It was originally a hidden cul-de-sac, but by late 1370 DR it had opened up and gained direct ingresses from two streets.

Description

Paved in cobblestone, this cul-de-sac

By 1370 DR, the cul-de-sac housing Flask Circle was opened up into a proper alleyway, with ingresses from Blacktree Boulevard South and Red Wyrm Ride.

Notable Locations

From counterclockwise, the shops that lined the Flask Circle were:

  • Flora's Flower Shop was, as the name implied, a flower shop It was later bought out by the Ampners, who demolished the building and in its place built a four-story apartment building that retained a flower shop on the ground floor.
  • Healthier Living, formerly the site of a laboratory that was run by the potion maker Wendell, was an apothecary shop and clinic run by the cleric Sister Maltornis.
  • Material Assistance, a shop that sold spell components.
  • Modern Ointments, a shop that sold a variety of aromatic oils, candles, incense, and lotions.
  • Ollie's Discount Potions, a store that specialized in the buying, selling, and bartering of a wide range of potions.
  • Practical Potions, another store that specialized in selling potions. Its owner typically ran his prices alongside Ollie of Discount Potions to ensure that the two businesses minimized any arbitrage.
  • Supreme Supplies, a shop that sold all manner of alchemy supplies.
  • Wendell's laboratory, a potion store that was shut down after its owner was kicked out of the market for having one too many accidents occur within.
  • Winn's Ice House, an icehouse business that later occupied the building that once housed Healthier Living.
