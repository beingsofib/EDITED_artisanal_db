Burdag Lane

Burdag Lane was a hard-packed dirt and gravel alleyway in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Geography

This road ran south off of Blackhorn Alley and terminated on Hunters' Alley. Along the way it acted as the eastern terminus of Tsarnen Alley and the western terminus of Lathin's Cut.

Notable Locations

The Plinth: An all-faiths temple and the only temple in all of the Trades Ward.

Links to Sewers

An entrance to the sewers of Waterdeep could be found on the intersection of Burdag Lane and Tsarnen Alley.
