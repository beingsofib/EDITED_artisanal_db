Shrine of Lliira was a small temple dedicated to the worship of Lliira, the Mistress of Revels, located in the city of Suzail, the capital of Cormyr, the Forest Kingdom.

Location

The Shrine to Lliira stood just two city blocks away from the splendorous Promenade, sandwiched between Swordstars Lane and Blade Lane.

History

On Mirtul 7 of the , the amnesiac swordswoman Alias visited the Shrine of Lliira and the Silent Room of Deneir in attempts to remove her azure bonds, which she thought to be a mysterious curse. However, both temples were closed at the time.
