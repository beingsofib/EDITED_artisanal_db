South Road (Zakhara)

The South Road was located in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Description

The South Road encircled the City of Delights from the south, providing access to all of the major gates before connecting with the River Road on the north side of the city. The South Road was a viable option for those who wished to get around Huzuz but did not want to deal with crowded gate traffic or the congested streets within the city walls.
