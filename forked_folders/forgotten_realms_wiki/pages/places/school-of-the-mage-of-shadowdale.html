The School of the Mage of Shadowdale was a small school in Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Activities

This school was run by the foreign wizard Elfinster. Students accepted at the school could learn magical spells, or, if they lacked magical ability, they could learn to recognize a specific spell being cast based upon the caster's gestures and speech.

Though Elfinster could be quite eccentric, his prices were reasonable and he was a proficient teacher.

Scrolls could also be purchased here.

Inhabitants

Elfinster owned and operated this school.
