The Axe and Hammer, nicknamed The Tomb, was a spacious and calm inn found in the city of Elversult during the mid–14 century DR.

Interior

Atmosphere

It had a reputation as a peaceful place to get lots of rest after an exhausting journey, though not one that was particularly lively or fun. Its rather subdued atmosphere led to the nickname of The Tomb.

Services

The Axe and Hammer was run by an all-dwarven staff and made every effort to protect its patrons and keep the peace.
