The Magelings' Manor was the nicknamed given to a particular boarding house that operated in the great city of Myth Drannor.

Location

The rowhouse was located at the intersection between Scatterhorn Street and Moonshadows Street, in the Kerradunath district of the city.

Services

The Magelings' Manor offered private rooms for those students of the Irithlium that chose not to live on-campus, often of a desire for more privacy. In addition to extra space to hone their craft, the young spellcasters also received a tastier variety of food, prepared by a Calishite cook named Usaagar.
