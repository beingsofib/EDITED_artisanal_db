Sullerton Shipbuilders was a shipyard that operated in the town of Daggerford during the late 15 century DR.

Location

The Sullerton family business was established within a large warehouse located near Daggerford's River Gate. The gate was wide enough that ships could be moved down to the town's docks.

Like the businesses that came before it, Sullerton Shipbuilders was solely dedicated on making ships that would traverse the Delimbiyr River.
