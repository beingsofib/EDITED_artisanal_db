River Haqar

The River Haqar was a small waterway found Semphar in east Faerûn and the Hordelands.

Geography

The River Haqar was formed of several unnamed tributaries and originated within the central region of the Shalhoond as wells as the Khopet-Dag mountains. It flowed southwest through the mountains and forest to empty into the northern banks of Gbor Nor.
