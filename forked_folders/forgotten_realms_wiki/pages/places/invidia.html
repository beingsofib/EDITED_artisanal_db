Invidia was one of the Domains of Dread. It was a land of largely unspoiled wilderness and rivers.

Description

The domain was mostly wilderness. Invidia's largest city, Karina, was home to only two thousand people, and was surrounded by vineyards. The people were for some reason especially prone to crimes of passion.
