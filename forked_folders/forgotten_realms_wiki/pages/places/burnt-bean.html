The Burnt Bean was a famous coffee house located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR.

Location

The coffee house was located in the north of the city within Avencina. The building sat in the southeastern section of Avencina near the border of the Plume just north of Ahsinat Bazaar.
