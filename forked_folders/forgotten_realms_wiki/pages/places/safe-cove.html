Safe Cove was a small coastal fishing village found on the Island of Firethorns in the Corsair Domains of northwest Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Geography

Safe Cove was found along a natural bay on the western side of the island. The village was approximately southwest of Hawa. It sat in the forested region of the island between the northern and southern land rises.
