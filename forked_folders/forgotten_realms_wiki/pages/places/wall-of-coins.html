The Wall of Coins was a temple devoted to Waukeen, the Coinmaiden, located in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan in the late 14 century DR.

Location

The temple was located in central Suldolphor near Emerald Dragon Way.

Description

Since Waukeen's silence following the Time of Troubles in 1358 DR, the Wall of Coins was practically abandoned, filling with dust and darkness. A famed floating altar made from pure gold lay within the temple.

Inhabitants

The temple lacked a high priest circa . Members of the Flame Guard protected the temple.

Defenses

Anyone attempting to steal or disturb the floating altar would be struck by chain lightning.
