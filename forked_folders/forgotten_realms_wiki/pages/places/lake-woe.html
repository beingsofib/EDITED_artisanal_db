Lake Woe

Lake Woe was a large lake in the North.

Description

The lake was full of ice and covered in a thick mist that drifted down from the massive mountain at its center.

Geography

The lake was located south of the Ice Spires South

Mount Woe, one of the tallest mountains in northern Faerûn, was at its center.
