Sorlmar Pass, also known as Sormarl Pass was a mountain pass through the Sanrach Mountains in Samarach on the Chultan Peninsula of southwest Faerûn.

Geography

Sorlmar Pass was the only documented way through the eastern side of the mountains. The pass connected Sorl in Samarach and Samagard in Thindol.

History

Boat traffic and commerce along the River Gur was quite busy in the days before Samarach and Thindol separated. Goods were unloaded from the river essels and loaded onto wagons which traversed Sormarl Pass.
