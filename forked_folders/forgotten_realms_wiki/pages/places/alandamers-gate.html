Alandamer's Gate was the northern gate of the city of Athkatla in Amn.

Location

Alandamer's Gate stood on the border of two districts of Athkatla: the Wave District to the west and the Temple District to the east. The road that passed Alandamer's Gate ran north towards the grand temple complex of the Goldspires and the monastery Arbalest's House.

Activities

All non-citizens who passed Alandamer's Gate had to pay an "any one coin" toll, of gold or silver, to be allowed into Athkatla.
