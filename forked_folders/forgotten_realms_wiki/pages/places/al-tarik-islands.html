Al-Tarik Islands were a large archipelago found in the Golden Gulf and Crowded Sea within west Zakhara.

Geography

Most of the islands of the archipelago were surrounded by coral reefs. The archipelago sat in between the mainland of the southwestern tip of the continent of Zakhara and Al-Qirmiza Islands. Jazayir al-Sartan was located directly south of Al-Tarik Islands. Most of the islands in the chain sat roughly off the coast from the Realm of Coffee in the west, stretching north through the Golden Gulf for about and nearing Ajayib.

Fourteen individual islands were part of the archipelago. Several of the islands were named including:

  • Tarikat Island
  • Ma'mur Island
  • Azim Island
