Nam River

The Nam River was a river in the Haraki Ridge in Tabot.

Geography

The Nam River emerged from the foot of the Marnu Cliffs before it rushed westward to fill Nam Tso. The lake was also fed by the Wohani River, which emerged from the range further to the north.
