Thalaumbur's Trot

Thalaumbur's Trot was a curving alley in the North Ward of Waterdeep.

Geography

The alley was situated directly north of Sulmor Street and crossed through Brondar's Way.

History

This alley was named for the wealthy merchant Thalaumbur, who in the 11 century DR had owned the block of buildings that were enclosed by the alley and Sulmor Street.

Notable Locations

The front gates of both the Maerklos and Nandar Villas stood on this alleyway.
