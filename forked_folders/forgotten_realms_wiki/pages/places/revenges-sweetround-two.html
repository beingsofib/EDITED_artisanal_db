Revenge's Sweet/Round Two

Round Two of Revenge's Sweet focuses on the player characters sailing to the Pirate Isles to rescue their kidnapped prisoner, the dread pirate Captain Morgan, so he may stand trial in Yhaunn. The story is continued from Round One.
