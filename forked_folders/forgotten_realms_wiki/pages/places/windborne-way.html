Windborne Way

Windborne Way was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Windborne Way was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's North Ward. It ran north to south in between Thunderstaff Way in the north and Delzorin Street in the south. Windborne Way was intersected by Sashtar Street, Tower March, and Saerdoun Street.
