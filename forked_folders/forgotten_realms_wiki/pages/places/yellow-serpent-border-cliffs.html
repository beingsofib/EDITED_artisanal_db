Yellow Serpent Border Cliffs

The Yellow Serpent Border Cliffs was a long stretch of sheer rock that served as a natural boundary between part of the eastern Hordelands and western Shou Lung, specifically the provinces of Mai Yuan and Ch'ing Tung.

Geography

The cliffs ran for hundreds of miles from the northernmost region of the Yehimal mountains near Nas Trango Shan to the peak of Shan Trango in the south.

Description

Much of the cliff face was a sheer drop, including a dizzying plunge at the Yellow Thunder Falls.
