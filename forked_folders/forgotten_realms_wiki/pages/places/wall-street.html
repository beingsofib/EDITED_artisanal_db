Wall Street

Wall Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Wall Street was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward. The road ran parallel to the eastern outer city wall in the Southern Ward, originating in the north in the Trades Ward on River Street, and passing south to eventually end on Coach Street. Along the way Wall Street connected with Belzer's Walk, Olaim's Cut, Ruid's Stroll, the Specterwalk, Tilman's Lane, and the Wagonrace.

Notable Locations

The Redbridle Stables was located along Wall Street.
