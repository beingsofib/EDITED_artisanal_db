The waymoot of Assamboryl was a landmark in Cormanthor.

Geography

It was approximately two days' north of Druindar's Rock and one day south of Six Thorns.

History

In , Galan Goadulphyn left for the waymoot of Assamboryl after being injured from stray spells from the mage duel between Delmuth Echorn and Elminster in Druindar's rock. He expected the trip to take two days.
