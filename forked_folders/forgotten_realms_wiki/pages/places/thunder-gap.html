Thunder Gap, also known as Thunder Pass, was an east-west pass in the Thunder Peaks mountain range that separated Cormyr in the west from neighboring Sembia and the Dalelands in the east. The East Way, a major road from Arabel to Ordulin, ran through it.

History

Thunder Gap was officially declared as the border between Cormyr and Sembia by mutual agreement.
