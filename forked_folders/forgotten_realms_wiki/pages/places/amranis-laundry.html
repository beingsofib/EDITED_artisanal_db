Amrani's Laundry was a business located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The building stood one story tall.

Location

Amrani's Laundry was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter. The front of the building lay along the west side of Slop Street, while the rear of the building faced Kolat Towers on Weeping Maiden's Run.
