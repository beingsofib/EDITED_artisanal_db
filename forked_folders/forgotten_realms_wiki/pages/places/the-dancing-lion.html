The Dancing Lion

Building
nameThe Dancing Lion
typeInn
locationYhep, Lake of Steam

The Dancing Lion was an inn located in the town of Yhep.

Services

The Dancing Lion offered travelers cheap lodging and decent food. The common room had a pleasant atmosphere that was cool and dark.
