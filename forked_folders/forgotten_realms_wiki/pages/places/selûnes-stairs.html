Selûne's Stairs

Selûne's Stairs was a small waterfall or cascade located in the town of Immersea in Cormyr during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Geography

The cascade originated on the eastern side of Spring Hill. Its waters fed the Immer Stream which also flowed east before emptying into an arm of the Wyvernwater.
