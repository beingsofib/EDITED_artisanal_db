Darselune Street

Darselune Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Darselune Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. Darselune Street branched southwest off of a major intersection that included Feather Street, Zarimtar Street, the Sutherlane, and Haltovar Street. It continued southwest to eventually end on Julthoon Street at the border with the Castle Ward. Other roads intersecting Darselune Street included Gulzindar Street, Dob's Loss, and the Street of the Singing Dolphin.
