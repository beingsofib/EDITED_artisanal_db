Bolgor was a tiny nation of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. It participated in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Description

Bolgor had a variety of colorful political figures that vied for control of the nation and of the Whamite Isles at large. Known individuals were the Czar Ferdinand, the Princess Annya, the Prince Nicholas, and Disputin. They were served by the Imperial Army, the Imperial Air Corps, and the Gruusboatmen. The population of Bolgor included the Serfs, who although humble, participated in the wars of the Whamite isles.

Bolgor was landlocked by the Cold Desert, Veldergauttland, Penbroc, Graubünden, and Inter Mierionydd. The River Gruus ran from the mountain range which formed the border with the Cold Desert and Veldergauttland, itself forming the border with Penbroc. The historic fortress of Gruuscastle stood on the border between Bolgor, Penbroc, and Graubünden, and the three nations often fought over it.

Bolgor was the only of nations of the Whamites to have no sea border. It compensated for this with the Imperial Air Corps, which included the only airships of the archipelago.
