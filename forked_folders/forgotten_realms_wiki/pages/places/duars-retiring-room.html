Duar's Retiring Room was a room in Castle Obarskyr in Suzail, Cormyr, in the late 15 century DR.

History

In , during the aftermath of the massacre during the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the , the undead Highknight Targrael borrowed an enchanted gemstone from Duar's Retiring Room, which she believed would be eventually spotted. This implied the room was patrolled with some regularity.
