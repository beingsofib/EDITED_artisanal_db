Eggs of the Dragon

Location
nameEggs of the Dragon
typeRock formation
regionSouth of Nashkel

The Eggs of the Dragon was a rock formation used as a landmark south of Nashkel.

Description

They were three black boulders.

Geography

It lay south of Nashkel's settled area. Between it and the Staring Ranger, the Magister Maldiglas Turntower had placed a refuge.
