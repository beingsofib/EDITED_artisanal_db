Bellister's House was a storage warehouse in Waterdeep that was privately owned by the business Bellister's Hand in the mid–14 century DR.

Location

This warehouse was located on the major intersection Waymoot, close to Bellister's Hand, in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This warehouse stood three stories tall.
