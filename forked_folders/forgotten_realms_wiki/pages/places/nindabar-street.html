Nindabar Street

Nindabar Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Nindabar Street was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's North Ward. The road spanned much of the ward from north to south, connect Saerdoun Street in the north with Zendulth Street in the south. Nindabar Street was also intersected by Hassantyr's Street, Endcliff Lane, Sulmor Street, Tarser's Street, Suldown Street, Brahiir's Street, Golden Serpent Street, and the Passar.
