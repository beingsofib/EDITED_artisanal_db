A Smuggler's Life for Me

A Smuggler's Life for Me is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the Living City organized play program. It debuted at Con Man '94 and was later reprinted in Polyhedron #113 under the title A Pirate's Life for Me, though with heavy edits to remove all connections to the Forgotten Realms setting.
