Stormshore Street Dock was a dock in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

Location

It was a shorter dock located on the eastern side of the Gray Harbor at the end of Stormshore Street in the Brampton neighborhood of Baldur's Gate.

Description

The Low Lantern tavern was housed within a ship permanently docked at Stormshore Street Dock. The dock itself was built between the larger dock to the north, Waendel's Wharf, and a larger dock to the south, the Stormwynd Dock.
