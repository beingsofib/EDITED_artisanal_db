The Dark Road

The Dark Road was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Dark Road was located in central Zhentil Keep within the Inner Quarter. The street touched two sections of the wall of the Common Quarter, curving around the northeast corner of the Inner Quarter. Along its path the Dark Road was intersected by the Lord's Way and Gold Street.

Notable Locations

Several prominent landmarks of Zhentil Keep were located along the Dark Road including the Tower High, the Last Stop, and the residence of Magistrate Osconivon.
