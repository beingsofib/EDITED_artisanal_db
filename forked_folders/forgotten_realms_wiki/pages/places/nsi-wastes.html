Nsi Wastes

Location
nameNsi Wastes
typeWasteland
regionChult, southwest Faerûn

The Nsi Wastes was a stretch of blighted jungle found in the wilds of Chult. The plant life found within were diseased and poisonous to any who ate from them.

Notable Locations

  • Palace of Ras Nsi: This stone keep was taken over by the warlord Ras Nsi and his undead horde.
