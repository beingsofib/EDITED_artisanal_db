The Blue Cow was a rowdy inn frequented by adventurers found in the city of Saerloon in the .

Location

The Blue Cow was located in the northwest section of the city.

Activities

Famous for its chaotic brawls and bawdy tales of high adventure, the Blue Cow drew the line with magic, enforcing a strict policy against casting spells or waving wands about. Anyone breaking these rules would have their magic items confiscated and were charged double the normal rates during their stay.
