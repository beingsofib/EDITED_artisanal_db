Kaya's House of Repose

Kaya's House of Repose was a prominent inn in Port Nyanzaru, Chult. Visiting adventurers were often referred there if they wanted a quiet and restful night.

Location

The inn was located in Port Nyanzaru's Market Ward just south of the Red Bazaar.

Services

Kaya's House of Repose was known to be relatively opulent and quiet compared to the average inn in Port Nyanzaru, and as a result, charged a full gold piece for a night's stay.

History

During the events of the death curse in the late 15 century DR, Kaya's House of Repose was a favorite drinking and meeting spot for the Harper spymaster known as Soggy Wren.
