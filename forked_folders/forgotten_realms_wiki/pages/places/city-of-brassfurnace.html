The Furnace was a district within the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire.

Description

This section of the City of Brass formed its small central core.

Inhabitants

The Furnace was the home of the noble efreet within the City of Brass.

Notable Locations

  • The Barracks of the Ring of Fire, the residence of the Sultan's personal guard.
  • Breath of the Sultan, an ornate fountain.
  • The Charcoal Palace, the residence of the Sultan that arose from the district's center.
  • The Red Pillar Halls, government offices that surrounded the Sultan's residence and accompanying areas.
  • The Eternal Flame Pavilion
  • The Flamesight Mosque
  • The Mosque of Blistering Atonement
  • The Mosque of Kossuth
  • The Mosque of the Irreducible
