Haraki Ridge

The Haraki Ridge was a mountain range in Tabot, and an arm of the Po Yul Dzayul Range.

Geography

Branching from the southern side of the Po Yul Dzayul Range, it divided the forests to the south from It was the site of the Marnu Cliffs and the headwaters of the Nam River and the Wohani River, which together flowed into Nam Tso.

Significance

The Haraki Ridge formed a geographic and political/religious demarcation in Tabot, dividing the northern and southern monasteries in the government.
