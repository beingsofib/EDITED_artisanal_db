Pansemil was a small village in Tethyr.

Geography

Pansemil was located east of the village of Tweyar. Both villages sat along the Sulduskoon River.

History

In the spring of the , Star Protective Services offered its training to the villagers of Pansemil.

Rumors

The people of the village of Tweyar, downriver of Pansemil, believed that the villagers of Pansemil practiced incest and bestiality. The people of Pansemil happened to believe the same rumors about the villagers of Tweyar.
