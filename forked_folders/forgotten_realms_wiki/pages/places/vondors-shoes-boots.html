Vondor's Shoes & Boots

Building
nameVondor's Shoes & Boots
typeShoe Store
locationArabel, Cormyr

Vondor's Shoes & Boots was a footwear shop operating in the city of Arabel in the late 14 century DR.

Location

The store was located in the center of the city across the street from the Elhazir's Exotica.
