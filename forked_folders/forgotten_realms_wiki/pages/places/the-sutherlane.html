The Sutherlane

The Sutherlane was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

The Sutherlane was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward and Castle Ward. It originated in the Sea Ward in the north on Darselune Street and Feather Street where it branched southeast toward the Market. The Sutherlane crossed into the Castle Ward at Julthoon Street and continued before ending at Trader's Way just northwest of the Market. Other streets connecting with the Sutherlane included Whim Street, Zarimtar Street, Haltovar Street, and Runer's Alley.
