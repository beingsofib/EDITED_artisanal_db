The Thomm Warehouse was a storage warehouse in Waterdeep that was privately owned by Haerlit Thomm in the mid–14 century DR.

Location

This warehouse was located along Sambril Lane,

Structure

This warehouse was four stories tall.

Services

Within his warehouse, Haerlit Thomm would fence furniture and distinctive furnishings that he had obtained from his contact Felzoun Thar.
