Gondeth's Mageries was a shop located in the city of Westgate in 1358 DR.

Inhabitants

The shop was owned and operated by the skilled wizard Gondeth who sold scrolls and would cast spells for a fee.

Location

Gondeth's Mageries was found near the center of the city at the intersection of Soutgate Market Street and Westgate Market Street.
