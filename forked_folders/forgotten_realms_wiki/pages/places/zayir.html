Zayir was a small town located on the Pilgrims' Route in Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Description

Nestled between the Golden Gulf to the south and Al-Akara Mountains to the north, Zayir was one of the few stops along the Pilgrim's Route heading south into the Cities of the Pantheon.
