Old Temple Lot

The Old Temple Lot was a small courtyard located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

The courtyard was found in southern Waterdeep within the Castle Ward near the southern end of Mount Waterdeep. The Old Temple Lot was bordered by Tarnished Silver Alley to the north and east, Coin Alley to the west, and Sail Street to the south.
