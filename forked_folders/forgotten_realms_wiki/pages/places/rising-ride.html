Rising Ride

The Rising Ride was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

The Rising Ride was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward. The road originated in the southwest corner of Caravan Court where it branched southwest and eventually turned west to end on the High Road. The Rising Ride was also intersected by Tulmaster's Street, Robin's Way, Juth Alley, and Tornsar Alley.

Notable Locations

Coach & Wagon Hall and Saddlers' & Harness-Makers' Hall sat at opposite sides of the intersection of Tulmaster's Street with the Rising Ride. The House of Good Spirits sat along the Rising Ride where it was joined by Robin's Way.

History

The road earned its named from a small knoll that sat along its length, causing the road to elevate. The House of Good Spirits was founded at the top of this rise.
