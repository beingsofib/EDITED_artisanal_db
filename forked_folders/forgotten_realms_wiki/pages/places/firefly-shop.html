The Firefly was a mysterious and rather nondescript shop found in the Temple District of Ravens Bluff during the late 14 century DR. Not much was known about the building, its services or owner; in fact, it could only be identified by its name appearing on tax rolls within the city records.

The shop was located on the west side of O'Kane Court within the district's Swordspoint neighborhood.
