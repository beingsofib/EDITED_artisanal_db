Ohlo's Mystic Devices was a small shop that dealt in magical miscellany in the city of Phlan of the Moonsea region in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

As of the , Ohlo's Mystic Devices had a signage over its door inviting customers inside. At the time, the proprietor of the establishment was a young, yet distinguished woman. Her inventory consisted of cleric and wizard scrolls, potions of healing, potions of speed, a precious wand of magic missiles, rings and cloaks of protection, and a set of bracers of defense.
