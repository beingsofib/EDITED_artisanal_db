Snowdown Ride

Snowdown Ride was a well-maintained road found on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles.

Description

Snowdown Ride cut through the heart of Snowdown for nearly . It began in the north at the town of Llandrain and continued south and then southwest through the island to end at Pengram. Along the way were Harloch—where the Ride intersected with the muddy road east to Brannoch—and the capital of Caer Westphal, as well as all three of Snowdown's lakes.

History

Snowdown Ride was built during the height of the Kingdom of Snowdown with the help of the realm's high taxes.

As of the late 15 century DR, the road was well-kept and well-patrolled by mercenaries of Amn, who had come to occupy the island of Snowdown. Trade goods and raw materials traveling south along the road were catalogued at Harloch.
