River Sarren

The River Sarren was located in Durpar in the Shining Lands as of the mid–14 century DR.

Geography

The River Ssarren originated in the Giant's Belt Mountains within Veldorn, flowing briefly south into Durpar before emptying into the Golden Water.

Notable Locations

Two Durpari cities were found at the mouth of the river, Turelve on the west shore and Orpher on the east.
