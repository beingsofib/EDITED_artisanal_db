The Nine Swords, also known as the Inn of the Nine Swords was a high-quality inn found in the village of Swords Pool in the Vast. It stood on the western side of the road, between the North Road and the pond of Swords Pool.

History

The inn was named for the nine swords of the Nine Swords Company. These were hidden in Swords Pool, the body of water that gave its name to the village.

In the mid-1370s DR, several young blade magicians of the Vast re-founded the Nine Swords Company. They used the Inn of the Nine Swords.

Owners

The Nine Swords inn was owned by the three Mellicot sisters: Melicent, Meliors, and Melusine.
