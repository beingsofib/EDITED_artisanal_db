Maldridge House was a stately old mansion located on MacIntyre Path in Ravens Bluff in the late 15 century DR. The mansion was best known as Jack Ravenwild's temporary residence after he rescued Seila Norwood from enslavement by drow in the . The mansion burned to the ground that same year during a drow attempt to recapture Jack.
