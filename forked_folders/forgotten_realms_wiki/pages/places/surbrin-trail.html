Surbrin Trail

The Surbrin Trail was a long trail that passed through the farmlands around the fortified city of Yartar, in the Dessarin Valley region.

Geography

The trail went north from Yartar, around the western border of the Evermoors, and extended to Mornbryn's Shield and further to the city of Nesmé.

Activities

The southern stretch of the Surbrin Trail was well-protected by Yartarran patrols.
