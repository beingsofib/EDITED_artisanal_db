The Ugly Stick

The Ugly Stick is a one round adventure module set in the Living City organized play program. It was published in August 1992 as the tenth Living City tournament module and is intended for adventurers of any level. The module received a reprint in Polyhedron 89 in 1993.

Synopsis

A group of adventurers of the Living City set out to investigate customers of the famous Poston Plateau Resort mysteriously loosing their attractiveness. they discovered that a resort employee named Fawn, an unnaturally beautiful cleric, came to possess a duo of interconnected enchanted wands. The wand of ugliness, when used, made its target unattractive – an act of evil that shifted its wielder's personality towards maliciousness and evil. After being used, the wand of ugliness allowed the wielder to use the wand of beauty to beautify a target. Eventually, the adventurers reassembled special containers that neutralized both wands and their effects, and confronted Fawn.
