The Spice of Life was an herb and spice shop located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the late 14 century DR.

Location

The shop was located in south Zhentil Keep in the western section of the Foreign Quarter in front of the River Trail Gate on Slingshot Way and Twilight Street.

Activities

The shop's selection of herbs and spices was truly impressive. In addition to common items, the shop carried exotic wares from Kozakura, the Moonshae Isles, Maztica, and Zakhara. Furthermore, anything not in stock could be ordered.

Inhabitants

The Spice of Life was owned by a halfling named Ascot Stumblefoot in the .
