Rauvinwatch Keep was the headquarters for the Argent Legion, the grand army that comprised soldiers from the member-cities of the Confederacy of the Silver Marches.

Geography

The keep was situated just outside of Silverymoon.

History

As of the , Rauvinwatch was overseen by High Marshal Methrammar Aerasumé.

Inhabitants

While most of the Argent Legion served in locations throughout the Silver Marches, a contingent was left within the keep's walls.
