The Roaring Lion Inn was an inn found in the city of Tantras in the Vast. It was located just off Whistleturn Way, near the Market.

It was known as the best inn in the city around 1358 DR, with excellent accommodations but a high price to match. However, it was commandeered by the Guard of Tantras for use as an army barracks during the Time of Troubles of that year.
