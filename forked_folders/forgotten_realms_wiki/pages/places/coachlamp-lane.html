Coachlamp Lane

Coachlamp Lane was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Coachlamp Lane was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward. The road originated on the southern end of the High Road where it branched east, crossed Carter's Way, then turned southeast to end within a neighborhood at a deadend.

Notable Locations

Hlakken Stables and Tehmak's Coaches were both located along Coachlamp Lane.
