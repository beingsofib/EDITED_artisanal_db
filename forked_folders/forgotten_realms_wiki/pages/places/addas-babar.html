Addas Babar was a sprawling settlement in Anauroch that was home to many of the desert's D'tarig people. The town was a spacious camping grounds for those travelers who dared to cross the great desert of northern Faerûn.

Description

The town comprised mud and wood structures, mostly built for living accommodations, scattered across a handful of peaks and troughs of hilly landscape. Due to designation as the premiere trading post of the D'tarig, Addas Babar held a spacious marketplace, where outlanders could resupply and hire local experts and guides.

Geography

It was located in a hilly range just beyond the northern slopes of the Tethyamar Mountains.

Trade

In addition to local shopkeepers, Addas Babar attracted all manner of foreign merchants who peddled their wares on nearly every street in town.
