Dragon's Dip was a small lake located at the heart of Icewind Dale, named after its frequent visitor throughout several centuries: the ancient white dragon Arveiaturace.

Description

The Dip was a small frigid lake with one of its shores being an elevated cliff. The lake was located south of Fallen Crags and east of Bryn Shander, along the Eastway road that connected Bryn Shander and Easthaven.

History

In the , Arveiaturace befriended the wizard named Blizzard, an aptly named ice-mage, who took to meeting the dragon at the Dragon's Dip to converse. The two females bonded over the loss of their loved ones.
