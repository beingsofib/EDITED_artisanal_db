Fairwater Coster (Archenbridge)

The Zhentarim

The Fairwater Coster was a small shops selling inexpensive goods in the city of Archenbridge during the 14 century DR.

This shop was secretly a a Zhentarim outpost, and sold goods at a loss in order to destroy the competition.
