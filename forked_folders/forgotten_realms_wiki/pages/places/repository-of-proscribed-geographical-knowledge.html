The Repository of Proscribed Geographical Knowledge was located in the Potala in Saikhoi in the mid–14 century DR.

Description

All of the volumes and scrolls found within this library were related to geography. A monk controlled access.

History

In 1360 DR, adventurers seeking information on the city of Solon were directed here. They had to convince the duty monk they had a real need before being permitted to examine a book about it.
