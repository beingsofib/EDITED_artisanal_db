Seawolf was a village and later city in Norheim in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The village was located on the east coast of Giantspine, the largest of the Norheim Isles.

History

By tradition, the local Tempuran clergy was entirely made up of men, and women were barred from becoming priests of the Lord of Battles.

Notable Inhabitants

  • Branwen, a priestess of Tempus, left her native Seawolf at a young age due to the village's stance on female Tempuran priests. Despite being reviled by the populace, she held no resentment for her former home, seeing her treatment there as a challenge she had overcome.
