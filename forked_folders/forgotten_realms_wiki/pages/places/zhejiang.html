Zhejiang

Location
nameZhejiang
typeProvince
exportsLong Jing

The Zhejiang province was the claimed location of provenance of West Lake Dragon Well tea, also known as Long Jing, according to the merchants in Telflamm. It may have been actually mythical instead of real.
