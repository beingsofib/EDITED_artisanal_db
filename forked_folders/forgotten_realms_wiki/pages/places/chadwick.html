Chadwick was a small island within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Chadwick was located in the west of the Korinn Archipelago, just off the southwestern tip of the much larger isle of Ventris and due north of the northern tip of Caftenor.

Inhabitants

The isle's Northlander humans were quite poor. The southern coast of Chadwick was home to the largest community of gnomes in the Korinn Archipelago, and this was the isle's largest claim to fame.
