The Northend Tower was a fortification located in Scardale Town, used by the criminal Aldavius Darkhand during the late 14 century DR. Darkhand defended his tower with a number of thugs and hired swordhands. It was rumored to be filled with a small horde of ill-gotten goods collected by the bandit-merchant.
