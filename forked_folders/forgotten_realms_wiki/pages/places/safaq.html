Safaq was a small isolated town found in the southeastern reaches of the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara.

Geography

Safaq rested along the coast of the Golden Gulf and Crowded Sea at the foot of the southern end of Al-Yabki Mountains in a fertile grassland. It lay on the west bank of an unnamed river that emptied into the sea.
