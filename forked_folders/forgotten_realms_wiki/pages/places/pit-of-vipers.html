The Pit of Vipers was a sprawling stronghold and temple dedicated to Sseth, the World Serpent.

Location

The Pit of Vipers was found deep within the overgrown Black Jungles near the northwestern shore of the Lapal Sea.

Inhabitants

The complex was filled with histachii who guarded the brood chambers. Yuan-ti eagerly explored the vast ruins of the temple which also served as the entire capital of Serpentes during Sseth's rise to power.

Rumors & Legends

A vast network of subterranean tunnels was rumored to run beneath the Pit of Vipers, connecting it with locations as far south as Mhairhetel, as far north as Lapalgard, and as far west as Delselar.
