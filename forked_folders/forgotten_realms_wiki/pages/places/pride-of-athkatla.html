The Pride of Athkatla was an enclosed marketplace within the Temple District of Athkatla full of local vendors, well-versed in city's politics and local customs, who sold goods and wares for traveling merchants.

Description

The Pride was comprised of rows upon rows of curtained boutiques and mercantile stalls. They were run vendors peddling goods from all around Faerûn, anything from Tashalaran cheese to locksmiths' hardware. The marketplace was set within the entrance hall of a former temple of Savras.

One of the more memorable shopkeepers in the Pride was Sulrajack, a gregarious and silver-tongued covert informant who spied on Athkatlans for the Cowled Wizards.
