Naingate was the tower of the wizard Nain Keenwhistler who was a former member of the Company of Crazed Venturers.

Location

The tower was located in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep at the intersection of Grimwald's Way and Seawatch Street. It was used by Nain as a home, as well as a base for his research.
