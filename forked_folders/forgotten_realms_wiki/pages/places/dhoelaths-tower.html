Dhoelath's Tower was a green marble tower in Leuthilspar, Evermeet.

Description

The black and white striped tower had balconies and a reflective black marble roof.

History

Around 1367 DR, the tower was inhabited by the wizard Dhoelath.

Rumors and legends

Hidden within the halls of the tower, Dhoelath was said to hold the secret to powerful high magic.
