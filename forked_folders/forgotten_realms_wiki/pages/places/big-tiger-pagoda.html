The Big Tiger Pagoda was a large structure found in the city of Manass in Khazari.

Structure

The pagoda was composed entirely of brick and stood seven stories tall, making it an impressive landmark in the city.

Activities

Funds needed to maintain the building were raised by the governor of Manass through a special head tax.

History

Big Tiger Pagoda was originally constructed to honor the "King-Who-Slew-Bambalan", an ancient hero of Khazari.
