Dalton Drive

Dalton Drive was a small street in the Torchtown neighborhood of Ravens Bluff.

Location

Considered rather hidden by some, Dalton Drive spanned only a single block in length, connecting Sylgarth Street and Broken Bit Lane.

Notable Locations

  • The Cup of Coins, guildhall of the city's Merchants Guild, stood on the corner of where Dalton Drive met Broken Bit Lane.
