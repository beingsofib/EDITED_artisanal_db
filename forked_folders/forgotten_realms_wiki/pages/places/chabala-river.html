Chabala River

Location/Body of water
nameChabala River
typeRiver
regionSildëyuir, Aglarond

The Chabala River was a body of flowing water in Sildëyuir which fed the Chabala Mere, on top of which sat the Causeway of Stardeep. Its mouth into the lake was the mouth of a narrow ravine by an old birch tree.
