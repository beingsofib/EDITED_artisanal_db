Shadowdale Trail

The Shadowdale Trail was a road in the Dalelands that ran north and south out of Shadowdale. Followed north out of Shadowdale it dwindled away to open country and became little more than a trail when followed south from Shadowdale along the River Ashaba to Mistledale.

Shadowdale Trail intersected with the North Ride in the heart of Shadowdale village.
