Windsummer Street

Windsummer Street was one of the roads in the city of Urmlaspyr in Sembia.

History

In the mid-14 century DR, the adventuring wizard named Pendara the Slim was one of the inhabitants of Windsummer Street in Urmlaspyr. In the , she rode an untamed griffon and crashed it in front of her house.
