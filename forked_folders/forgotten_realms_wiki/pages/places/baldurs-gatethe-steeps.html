The Steeps was a district in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, located between the Old Wall and eastern side of Gray Harbor.

Notable Locations

Businesses

  • Counting House: For centuries, this building served as the central hub for trade, commerce and currency exchange within Baldur's Gate.
  • Felogyr's Fireworks

Statues

  • Seaserpent Tamed by Umberlee
  • Faithful Shopkeeper Meets the Honest Trader
  • The Queen's Favor: This marble fountain depicted a life-size sailing ship that appeared as if it were sinking beneath waves of the ocean.

Temples

  • Water Queen's House: This temple to Umberlee was a popular destination for the great many Baldurians whose livelihoods depended on sailing, fishing or otherwise required the blessing of the "fickle mother" of the sea.
