Rangfjell (meaning "Honor Mountain" in the giant language) was an ancient giant-ruled kingdom in Faerûn during the Time of Giants of the Dawn Age ( to ).

History

Rangfjell was one of the giant kingdoms that warred with the ancient dragon kingdoms during the Dawn Age.
