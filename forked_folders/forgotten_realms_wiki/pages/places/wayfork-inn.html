The Wayfork Inn was the only inn and a tavern in the Wayfork Village that stood on the Trade Way north of Baldur's Gate.

Interior

The inn's taproom was a large simple square room filled with tables and seating. A single fireplace blazed on the opposite wall from the entrance and to the entrance's left side stood a curved bar stand.

History

In the late 14 century DR, the Wayfork Village was raided by the Red Fang marauder goblin gang, secretly allied with the Zhentarim. Hobgoblin Harnak the Butcher lead the assault and began slaughtering villages. He took a stand against a group of six adventurers inside the Wayfork Inn, where the scared townsfolk found shelter. Subsequently, the village was saved and Harnak – slain. The innkeeper at the time – Larani, a human woman, saw present during the attack but survived the experience. Despite the fact that adventurers saved the village, Larani the innkeeper disliked those who took to adventuring, believing them be rough and dangerous, but still welcome at the Wayfork Inn.
