Al-Kufr River

Al-Kufr River was a small river found in the Free Cities of Zakhara.

Geography

Al-Kufr originated in the northern expanse of the Furrowed Mountains. The river flowed northwest on a brief course before meeting the Great Sea.

Description

Though small compared to other rivers in Zakhara, Al-Kufr's fertile banks were vital for the survival of Hafayah, which was found on the east bank.
