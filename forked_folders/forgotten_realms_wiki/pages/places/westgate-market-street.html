Westgate Market Street

Westgate Market Street was a major avenue found in the city of Westgate.

Location

Westgate Market Street entered the city through Mulsantir's Gate. It passed southeast through the center of the city before terminating with Woodside Way in front of Castle Thorsar in the south section of the city. Along the way Westgate Market Street intersected with Westlight Walk, Wagonwheel Ride, Sword Lane, Brewhouse Lane, Silverpiece Way, Southgate Market Street, and East Market Street.

Inhabitants

Notable people who lived on Westgate Market Street included the wizard Gondeth, High Priest Hunn Irblast, and High Articifer Ashuntira Elhorn.
