Borth was a small coastal town located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Borth was found along the coast of the Strait of Alaron in the northeastern region of the kingdom of Corwell in southern Gwynneth. Borth sat just north of Kingsbay.

History

Borth fell into ruin following the conquest of Gwynneth and expulsion of the humans by the fey of Sarifal.
