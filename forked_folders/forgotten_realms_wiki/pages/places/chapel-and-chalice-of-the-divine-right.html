The Chapel and Chalice of the Divine Right was a temple dedicated to Siamorphe, the Divine Right, located in Waterdeep during the late 14 century DR.

Location

The Chapel was located within the residence of the Assumbar family along the Street of the Singing Dolphin in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep.

Activities

Though relatively small, the Chapel was the center of Siamorphe's worship within Faerûn. Lord Laeros Assumbar funded the Chapel out of his own pockets.

Inhabitants

First High Lady Scion of Siamorphe Belkerri Gulderhorn oversaw the Chapel and Chalice of the Divine Right. Lady Gulderhorn was assisted by two other ranking priests—her niece Harlaa and youngest son Vrom. Additionally, the local nobility was represented within the Chapel by six acolytes and a dozen lesser priests.
