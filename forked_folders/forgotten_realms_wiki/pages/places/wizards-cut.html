Wizard's Cut

The Wizard's Cut was a small street found in the city of Westgate.

Location

The Wizard's Cut branched off of Silverpiece Way toward the east side of the city in between Woodside Way and East Market Street. It ended along the Harbor Loop. The Ssemm family dock was found at the end of the Wizard's Cut on Westgate's harbor.
