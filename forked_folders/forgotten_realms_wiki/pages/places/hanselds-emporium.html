Hanseld's Emporium was a trading company headquartered in Highmoon within Deepingdale during the late 14 century DR. Travelers from all over Faerûn came to browse Hanseld's wares, which included exotic spices, finished goods and curious items from Selgaunt and Marsember.

Hanseld also ran a smuggling and fencing operation out of his shop and would arrange for the transference of stolen goods.
