Casting ground

A casting ground was a landmark with unique properties, to be called up by those who knew the method.

Notable Examples

One casting ground created by Aralagath Tarsil boosted certain spells, and anyone who knew the methods could cast protection from evil or a floating ring of mauve-white spheres akin to faerie fire.
