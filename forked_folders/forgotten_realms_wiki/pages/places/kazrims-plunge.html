Kazrim's Plunge

Location
nameKazrim's Plunge
typeStatue
regionVaelan, Durpar

Kazrim's Plunge was a statue near West Gardens in the Durpari city of Vaelan. It commemorated Kazrim, whose heroics had been instrumental in freeing Vaelantar from the monsters in the late 11 century DR. The statue could serve as a recognizable navigational landmark.
