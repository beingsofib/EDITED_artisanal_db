Murkendraw

Location
nameMurkendraw
typeSwamp
regionFeywild

Murkendraw was a swamp in the Feywild and the realm of Baba Yaga, a hag archfey.

When not traveling around, Baba Yaga and her dancing hut resided within Murkendraw. Many types of hags were also known to inhabit this swamp.
