Mermaid and Stars

Building
nameMermaid and Stars
typeGambling den
locationCopper Street, North Ward, Waterdeep

Mermaid and Stars was a gambling den in the city of Waterdeep in the 15 century DR.

Location

This gambling hall fronted the eastern side of Copper Street, three doors north of Sulmor Street, in the North Ward of the city.
