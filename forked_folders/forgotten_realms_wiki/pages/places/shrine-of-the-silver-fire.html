The Shrine of the Silver Fire was a small shrine dedicated to Mystra, built in the depths of the Elven Catacombs of Myth Drannor and maintained by the Sisterhood of the Silver Fire, before the city's fall during the Weeping War.

Location

The Shrine of the Silver Fire was built on the second level of the sprawling tunnel system of Elven Catacombs. The Shrine was lit with Mystra's Silver Fire of pure magic.

History

The Mystran sect, known as the Sisterhood of the Silver Fire was founded Anorrweyn Evensong. She was inspired by the actions of Elminster Aumar during his stay in the City of Song, that eventually led to Myth Drannor opening its gates to members of other races and its renaissance. The Sisterhood build several shrines, including the ghostly Temple of Mystra in Nightingale Court and the Shrine of the Silver Fire among the tombs of the Elven Catacombs.
