Sarn Torel was a large fortified town of Athalantar in the early 3 century DR.

Description

Sarn Torel had a castle along the Unicorn Run.

Defenses

The city had castle walls overlooking the Unicorn Run.

History

In Hammer of the , a group of twenty armsmen and twelve mages were sent out of Sarn Torel by mutual agreement between the local swordmaster and the Magelord Kadeln Olothstar.

Ildryn Thallin had a tower in Sarn Torel, where contingencies went haywire upon his death in the , causing much chaos.
