Tsenviilyq was a derro city in the Underdark beneath the High Moor.

Geography

Tsenviilyq was located in the Lowerdark region of the Underdark, in a series of caverns known as the Sharnlands.

Inhabitants

The people of this city did not venture out onto the surface too often, but when they did it was usually on expeditions to acquire slaves.

Rumors & Legends

  • Adventurers who had seen the city claimed that the derro waged a war against the local illithids for control of the area.
  • Vincin the Gray Druid theorized that the derro of this city had likely descended from the former inhabitants of the Halls of the Hammer.
