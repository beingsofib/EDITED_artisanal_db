Arush Vayem was a small, isolated walled village located on the frontier of Tymanther in an unmapped region of the Smoking Mountains.

History

Arush Vayem was a remote village unknown to most people. It was a home for misfits, exiles and outcasts, and had a number of tieflings among its population.

Notable Residents

  • Farideh
  • Havilar
  • Mehen
  • Caisys the Vicelord, a warlock of the Toril thirteen
