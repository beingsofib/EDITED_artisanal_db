The Gate of Eagles was found in the city of Muluk in the Free Cities of Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Location

The Gate of Eagles was located on the eastern wall of the city. The Mount of Forgiveness was found just beyond the Gate of Eagles outside of Muluk.

Description

A pair of eagles were carved into the framework of the gate, giving the portal its name.

Inhabitants

A garrison of 200 soldiers was stationed at the Gate of Eagles, making the gate a fortress as much as it was an entryway into Muluk.
