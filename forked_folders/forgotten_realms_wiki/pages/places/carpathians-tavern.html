Carpathian's Tavern was one of the taverns in Menzoberranzan in the mid–14 century DR. It sold food and beverages.

Adventurers could find Rizzen Do'Urden here. Rizzen offered to make them a map of House Do'Urden, including where the prisoners of their village were being kept, in exchange for the Helm of Spiders. He explained that the helmet had three parts: the base, the crest, and the gem. With it, a drow house could gain a large advantage over others. He later sent them to meet with Jarlaxle, to acquire an insignia of House Do'Urden, without which the house glyphs would destroy them upon entry.
