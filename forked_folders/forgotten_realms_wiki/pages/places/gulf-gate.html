The Gulf Gate was one of the entrances into Huzuz, the City of Delights in 1367 DR.

Activities

Located south of the Clothiers' District, this gate was largely used by traders and suppliers. It was also the closest gate to the Grand Bazaar. In addition to the craft goods, most of Huzuz's food supply entered through this gate.

The Gulf Gate was closed during the night.
