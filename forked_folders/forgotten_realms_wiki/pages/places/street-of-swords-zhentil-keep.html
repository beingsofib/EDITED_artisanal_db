Street of Swords (Zhentil Keep)

The Street of Swords was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Street of Swords was located in northeast Zhentil Keep within the Common Quarter. Originating along Border Street at the outer wall of the city, the Street of Swords passed southwest for several blocks until terminating at the barrier to the Inner Quarter. The Street of Swords was intersected by Beholder Street and Black Street.
