The Downed Falcon was a tavern in Suzail in Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

History

In early spring of , Caladnei pointed out the Downed Falcon to her newest spy, the thief Narnra Shalace from Waterdeep, as a regular watering hole for the War Wizards Bowsar Ostramarr and Bathtar Flamegallow, whom she was going to spy on for her first assignment.
