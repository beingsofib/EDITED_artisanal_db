Hasbeen Road

Hasbeen Road was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Hasbeen Road was located in southeast Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. The road branched south then west off of the midpoint of Moonsea Avenue and terminated several blocks later at the wall of the Harbor District. Hasbeen Road connected with Alley Way.
