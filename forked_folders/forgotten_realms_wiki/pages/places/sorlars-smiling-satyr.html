Sorlar's Smiling Satyr was a bright and lively tavern in Silverymoon, a maze of stained glass windows and drapery, and still a new establishment as of the late 14 century DR.

Location

The tavern was a large building just past the Blacklar Gate on Auchtareen Lane in Silverymoon's Southbank.

Interior

The tavern's labyrinthine taproom was a collection of colorful glass windows, private booths, silken curtains, and many stairs, all of which proved to be challenging for drunken customers to navigate.

Services

Sorlar's Smiling Satyr's clientele was almost exclusively females and their male escorts, who tended to be dignified or timid.

History

As of the , Sorlar's was a relatively new business run by a skillful sorcerer who abhorred bar brawls.
