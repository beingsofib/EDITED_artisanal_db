Silverpiece Way

Silverpiece Way was a major thoroughfare found in the city of Westgate.

Location

Silverpiece Way began at the River Bridge in the east section of the city. It ran straight through Westgate, bisecting the north and south sections of the city, to end at the city walls along the western side. Many of Westgate's other streets intersected Silverpiece Way.
