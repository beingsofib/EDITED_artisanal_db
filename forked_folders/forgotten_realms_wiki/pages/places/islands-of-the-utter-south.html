The Islands of the Utter South, also simply called the Utter South, was a series of unexplored islands found south of the known regions of the Crowded Sea.

Geography

Situated south of the Djinni's Claws, the Steaming Isles, and Bariya, the Islands of the Utter South sprawled east to west for over a thousand miles across waters that most sailing vessels wisely avoided.

Description

The various islands were clumped into a roughly linear chain. Many of the islands were forested, but as of 1367 DR no Zakharan had dared explore the region.
