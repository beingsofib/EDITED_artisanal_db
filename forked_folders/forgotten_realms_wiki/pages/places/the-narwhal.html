The Narwhal was a tavern in the Central Port of Skullport in the late 14 century DR.

Structure

The Narwhal was a one-story building in poor condition.

Interior

The tavern's only notable feature was a horn mounted above the mantelpiece. The owner claimed that the horn originated from a sea creature of the Shining Sea, though in truth it was the horn of a unicorn.

Services

Regarded by locals as a seedy dive, the cheap ale served was diluted, the food was of low quality and often infested with insects, and service was largely absent under the inattentive and frequently drunken management of its proprietor.

Inhabitants

Circa 1370 DR, the owner of the Narwhal was Hugo Littlestaff. He purchased day-old mushroom bread from Orthon Calliar, owner of the Tumbling Bugbear.
