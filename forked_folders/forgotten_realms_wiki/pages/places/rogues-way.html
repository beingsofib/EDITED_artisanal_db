Rogues' Way

Rogues' Way was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 century DR.

Location

Rogues' Way was located in central Zhentil Keep within the Inner Quarter. The street branched west from the midpoint of the Street of Bones to terminate at the wall to the Common Quarter

Notable Locations

The residence of Lord Hael was located on Rogues' Way.
