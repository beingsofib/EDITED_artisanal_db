The Belching Dragon was a tavern located in the town of Nashkel during the mid–14 century DR. It was rather run-of-the-mill and offered a standard variety of drinks at reasonable prices.

Services

The Belching Dragon had the following drinks:

  • Dragon's Breath Beer: 1 gp
  • Mead: 4 gp
  • Westgate Ruby: 1 gp
  • Arabellan Dry: 2 gp
  • Saerloonian Topaz: 1 gp
