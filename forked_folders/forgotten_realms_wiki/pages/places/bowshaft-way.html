Bowshaft Way

The Bowshaft Way was a large road found in the Moonsea region just south of the great lake.

Description

The roadway originated in the west, branching east from the North Ride. It passed through the ruins of Yûlash before reaching Hillsfar along the coast of the Moonsea.
