Anechu was a village in the Ausa Province of T'u Lung. According to Tan Hikao Nong, it was a prosperous village whose inhabitants enjoyed a high quality of life unusual within T'u Lung, and it was for that reason that he considered it one of his favorite places.

Geography

It was situated along the northern bank of the upper Akensai River, where said river converged with an unnamed tributary. It was connected by road to the city of Chempka upstream and by trail to the villages of Honan and Chin as well as the city of Keeling.

Government

As of , Anechu was led by Ho Kui Dung, a principled and trustworthy man who was a member of the same Ho clan that ruled over Ausa Province as a whole.

Notable Locations

The Hearth Warmth, an inn which Tan Hikao Nong considered to serve the finest food within T'u Lung, was situated within Anechu.

Anechu was also home to a temple which housed a golden statue of Ju Shou, the god of the west. As Ju Shou had aided him before, Tan Hikao Nong frequently made offerings to him at this temple.
