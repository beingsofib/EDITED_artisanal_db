The commons was a large open area located in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The commons dominated the southeast corner of Daggerford south of the Ducal Castle.

Activities

This area was intentionally kept clear to use as an emergency pasture for cattle and livestock. The horses of the duke and Daggerford militia grazed here during times of peace.
