River Chionthar

The River Chionthar was a large river that ran through the Western Heartlands. The river started high up in the Far Hills and flowed west into the Trackless Sea on the Sword Coast.

Tributaries

  • River Reaching

Settlements on the river

  • Baldur's Gate, which stood around the estuary of the river, granting a tidal nature to its harbor.
  • Anga Vled
  • Berdusk
  • Elturel
  • Iriaebor
  • Scornubel
  • Ulgoth's Beard
  • Reithwin

Trade

Much trade flowed up and down this waterway, with barges traveling as far inland as Iriaebor.

The river's estuary in Baldur's Gate was crossed on a daily basis by many ferries and barges, particularly before its Upper City expanded and bridges were built.
