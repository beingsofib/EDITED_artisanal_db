The Garrulous Grocer was a grocery store located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The complex consisted of three buildings standing one, two, and three stories tall.

Location

The Garrulous Grocer was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter on the north side of Grocer's Lane across from where it was joined by Snake Alley.

Inhabitants

The Garrulous Grocer was founded by Nindil Jalbuck, a Masked Lord of Waterdeep.
