Kryptgarden Scrolls was the sole library located in Daggerford. It was said to contain over a thousand score books, scrolls, and other individual documents.

Location

It was located on the north side of the town's Money Quarter.

Structure

The three-story building was protected by a magical ward that shielded the structure, and everything within, from any manner of fire and lightning.

Activities

The library catered to the scribes and scholars that were sanctioned by the Scribes' Friendship guild. They spent much of their time copying, chronicling, and categorizing all of the works contained within its shelves.

While anyone could spend time within the library's entrance hall, only guild scribes were allowed to access its archives. Members of the Thinkers' Fraternity were allowed to access its books free of charge.
