Al-Fatir River

Al-Fatir River, also called the Tepid River, was a slow-flowing river found in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

Originating in Al-Yabki Mountains north of Gogal Pass, the Tepid River flowed eastward from the mountain range to the city of Jumlat on the coast of the Golden Gulf. The Tepid River measured in length.
