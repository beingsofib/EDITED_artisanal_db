Runer's Alley

Runer's Alley was a small backalley that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Runer's Alley was located in the southeast section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. It connected the southern ends of the Sutherlane and Haltovar Street, passing through a small neighborhood just north of Julthoon Street near the border with the Castle Ward.

History

The alley was named for a minstrel called Runer who slept here for many years.
