Sevenlamps Cut

Sevenlamps Cut was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Sevenlamps Cut was located in the western section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward. It ran east to west between Swords Street and the Street of Silks southeast of Turnback Court.

History

The road was named for the enchanted everburning lamps placed by Ahghairon. It was known to be a good place to hire spellcasters or priests for healing.
