The Eagles' Eyrie (inn)

Building
nameEagles' Eyrie
typeInn
locationHighcastle, High Dale
servicesRooms for rent

The Eagles' Eyrie was a smaller, less-than-clean inn found in Highcastle in late 14 century DR. It was popular among travelers in the area who were looking for a place to stay while on a budget.

Prices

A room for a night only cost 5 sp.
