The School of Stealth was the training grounds of an assassins' guild in Zazesspur, Tethyr.

Location

The School of Stealth was within a district of the Low City of Zazesspur, known as the Fortress.

Services

The School trained assassins, which had been in high demand locally until approximately .

History

Around , the School of Stealth was keeping a lower profile because of the recent strengthening of the law.

Some of the assassins were sent afterwards to a small school under the control of Chaks of the Shadow Thieves under a different identity.
