Cape Raeth was a sparsely-populated peninsula to the southwest of the Cloakwood that formed the western border of Cloak Bay.

Description

Cape Raeth was a cliffy peninsula that housed three fishing settlements of Orthul's Notch almost on the cape's tip, Calyaun on the cape's base, and Eldelorr at its northernmost point where Cloakwood almost reached the Cloak Bay.

History

By the late 14 century DR, the hamlets of Cape Raeth were almost deserted, inhabited by very few hardy folks who lived on fishing and ship-salvage.
