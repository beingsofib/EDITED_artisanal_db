Stabbed Sailor Alley

Stabbed Sailor Alley, which was also simply called the Stabbed Sailor, was a small alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Stabbed Sailor Alley was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's North Ward. The alley branched east from Vhezoar Street, joining Shattercrock Alley after passing Margaster Villa, and finally ending on Whaelgond Way. Little Street and Sprangalar's Swordcut connected with the north side of Stabbed Sailor Alley.

Description

A cluster of large shadowtop trees towering to nearly ensured the alley was always shaded.

History

This alley was the scene of a violent clash between Waterdeep's citizens and members of the Cult of the Dancing Bear.
