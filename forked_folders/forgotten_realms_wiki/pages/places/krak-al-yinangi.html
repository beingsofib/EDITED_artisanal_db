Krak al-Yinangi, also called the Castle of Conveyance, was a fortress located in Muluk, the City of Kings, in 1367 DR.

Description

This beautiful but stout bridge-like fortress was designed to appear to float upon the lagoon where it sat.

Activities

Anyone wishing to visit the grand palace needed to first pass through the Castle of Conveyance.
