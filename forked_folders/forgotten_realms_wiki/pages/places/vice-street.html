Vice Street

Vice Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Vice Street was located in the Common Quarter in the southeast section of Zhentil Keep. The road curved through the far southeast corner of the city, branching south from Battle Way and joining with Alley Way and Border Street near the North Tesh Gate.

Notable Locations

A Zhentilar guardpost was located on Vice Street.
