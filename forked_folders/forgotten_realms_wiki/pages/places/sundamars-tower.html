Sundamar's Tower was a mage tower found in Myth Drannor, and the personal residence of the eccentric wizard Sundamar.

Location

The tower was located high above Eldansyr's Ride, in the city's Sheshyrinnam district.

Structure

The four-story stone tower was not built atop the ground, or even as an addition to one of the city's massive shadowtops, but rather appeared to grow out of the prolific and appropriately-named Towersclutch Tree.

The foundation of the tower rest some above ground level, offering little protection against winds that regularly shook the branches of the tree, and the tower with them.

Rumors & Legends

Some people believed that the tower's unique construction was not entirely intentional, and may have been a result of one of Sundamar's failed experiments, perhaps one involving spelljamming-related magics.
