The Bath House was a public bathing site located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Bath House was located in south Zhentil Keep in the western section of the Foreign Quarter along Mace Way, Slingshot Way, and Teshwave Street.

Description

The ornate structure was made from marble and constructed over a natural hot spring.

Interior

The Bath House had both large communal pools and smaller private pools to use.

Activities

Chilled wine was available to any bathers who did not wish to get too hot. Massages were available as well for a completely relaxing experience. Admission to the Bath House cost 5 gp for a communal pool and 7 gp plus 5 sp for a private pool. Massages were 2 gp. Each pool session lasted for two hours and a massage was fifteen minutes.

Inhabitants

The Bath House was owned by a Calishite wizard named Valourish in the .

Defenses

A quartet of eunuch warriors armed with enchanted scimitars kept guard within the Bath House.
