Tummarlin's farmstead was a farm on the outskirts of Eveningstar in the realm of Cormyr during the late 15 century DR.

Description

The farm comprised a group of buildings and fields on the southwestern side of Eveningstar, near the King's Forest.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar, the farmstead was taken by the drow forces as they overran the outlying village in their first attack. It remained abandoned but intact until the ultimate Battle for Eveningstar, when the drow assault was turned back and Cormyrean forces retook the whole village, killing the commander Draya Dun'Robar and scattering her forces.
