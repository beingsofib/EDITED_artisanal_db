Copper Canyon

Location
titleCanyon
nameCopper Canyon
areaEvereska

The Copper Canyon was a high-walled ravine leading into the shadows of the High Shaeradim. It was a dead end for invading armies as the canyon wound its way to high cliff walls in the shadows of the highest mountain peaks.
