Alley Way

Alley Way was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Alley Way was located in southeast Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. The road branched south then southwest from the midpoint of Hasbeen Road to terminate along Vice Street and Border Street near the southern wall of the city.
