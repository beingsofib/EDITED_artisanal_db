Sossar Bay

Sossar Bay, which was also referred to as Sosser Bay, was a small bay found in northeast Faerûn.

Geography

Sossar Bay was located on the eastern shore of Sossal in the Cold Lands. The bay was a small extension of Yal Tengri, the Great Ice Sea.

Locations

Naupau, one of Sossal's few trading settlements, was located along Sossar Bay.
