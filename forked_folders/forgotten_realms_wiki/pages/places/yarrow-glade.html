Yarrow Glade was an area of woodlands in the foothills of the Storm Horns in Cormyr.

Geography

The glade was located in an area of dense forests somewhere in the northwestern Storm Horns on the Cormyr side of the mountains.

History

In the 1480s DR, following the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, the Archwizard Amskar found a lost ancient mythallar in a mountaintop glacier, aiming to create a flying enclave and fill it with an army. Though he did his best to keep his operation secret, the Harper and noble adventurer Oriphaun Huntsilver was already on his way to investigate, having stolen the Nether Scroll from Archwizard Tyvrus in Wheloon Prison and followed clues that indicated Shadovar operations in the Storm Horns. Once Oriphaun confirmed that the Netherese were assembling an army in the mountains, he sent a message back to Nevyle Dunwaite in Eveningstar and requested a meeting at Yarrow Glade to share what he had learned.

Nevyle sent a team to aid Oriphaun, only for Amskar and Shai to ambush the meeting and abduct Oriphaun. The remaining Harpers then fought their way out of the woods, overcoming orc, gnoll, and shadar-kai soldiers, the hill giant Luggan the Cudgeler, and finally Shai herself as she blocked their exit. They might also have aided the local hermit Haurugg, and fell afoul of the maddened druid Daegon as they went. The information they brought back was then used by Kara Patwin and Lorant Tass to organize further operations against the Shadovar in the region.
