The Latimer farm was a holding near Stag Falls during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The farm occupied both sides of the road.

Geography

The farm was about two miles from town. There was a well, and notably, a smoke house.

History

One spring in the mid-to-late 14 century DR, the inhabitants of the Latimer farm took all their prized possessions and the best blades from their tools, including axes and scythes, fleeing to the nearby caves in response to Baron Vilhelm von Stag Falls turning into an undead.
