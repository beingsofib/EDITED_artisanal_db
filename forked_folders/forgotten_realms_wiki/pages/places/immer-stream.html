Immer Stream

The Immer Stream was a small stream located in the town of Immersea in Cormyr during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Geography

The stream originated from Spring Hill on the southwest side of Immersea at a small waterfall called Selûne's Stairs. The stream flowed due east along the south side of Immersea, eventually connecting with the Wyvernwater. It crossed Calantar's Way along its way.
