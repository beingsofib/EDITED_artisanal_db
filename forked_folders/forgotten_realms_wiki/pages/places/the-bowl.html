The Bowl

The Bowl was an open area of deep sea found in the center of the Pirate Isles on the Sea of Fallen Stars.

Geography

This area of sea was roughly encircled by Mirg Isle in the south, Bulta in the east, Ilthan in the north, and the reefs of Sumbar in the west.

Description

The Bowl was the largest area of free sailing found within the Pirate Isles.
