The Tower of Nephthys was a sacred tower located in the capital city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Tower was located in the northern central part of the City of the Gods in the northern central part of the city. It sat just north of the grand Pyramid of Re.

History

The Tower of Nephthys served as a home for the incarnation of Nephthys, the Mulhorandi protector of the dead. The Tower of Nephthys was built by slaves and was thousands of years old as of the .
