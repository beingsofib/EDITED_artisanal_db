The Walled City of Al-Ubaid was a tiny city-state of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. It participated in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Description

Al-Ubaid had a variety of colorful political figures that vied for control of the nation and the Whamite Isles. Known individuals were Anndoquat the Sardar, Felquish the Merchant, Dandarnab the Trader, and Count Clannano. It was served by twin legions: the Kardic Legion and the Neddic Legion. Anndoquat also kept a private bodyguard force. The common people of Al-Ubaid, loyal to their home, also joined in the wars of the Whamite isles.

Al-Ubaid was located in the mountain range that formed the border between Klocckbarn and Zagrus. Although smaller than its neighbors, it boasted impressive fortifications like the Gate of Odendog and the South Citadel.

Among the nations of the Whamites, Al-Ubaid was the only known city-state.
