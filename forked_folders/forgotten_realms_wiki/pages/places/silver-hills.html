The Silver Hills were a prominent geographical feature located on the elven island homeland of Evermeet.

Geography

The Silver Hills were located on the western banks of the River Ardulith. They stretched from Evermeet's northwest coast through the center of the island along the river before terminating just upriver from where the River Shaelyn joined the Ardulith. The hills stretched for approximately .

Description

The hills stood over the river valley. Large conifers covered the slopes of the Silver Hills.

Inhabitants

The Silver Hills were home to group of pegasi and giant eagles

History

Sunstones could be found within the Silver Hills.
