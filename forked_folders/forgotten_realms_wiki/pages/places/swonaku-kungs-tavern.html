Swonaku Kung's Tavern

Building
nameSwonaku Kung's Tavern
typeTavern
locationTu Pe, Silla, Koryo

Swonaku Kung's Tavern was a tavern in the city of Tu Pe in Koryo around 1357 DR. It lay in the floating city, close to the second of the stone bastions that guarded the Tenasu Ferry route.
