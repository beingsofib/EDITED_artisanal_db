The Divine Den was a temple of Malar in the small village of Bezentil, considered to be the most holy sites of worship for the Beastlord in all the Realms. It was considered the center of the Malarite faith for the entirety of the Eastern Heartlands and eastern lands north of Thay.

Activities

Clergy of the Divine Den would lead great hunting expeditions to the more exotic and dangerous locales of Faerûn in order to hunt down its most exotic prey.

Despite the belief there were few skilled smiths in Bezentil, twelve Malarite clergy members in the Divine Den were among the few that could forge the True Talons of the God, specialized Claws of Malar that were enchanted and doused in the blood of beasts that were killed during their hunts.
