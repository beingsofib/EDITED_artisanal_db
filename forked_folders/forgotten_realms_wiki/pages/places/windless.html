Windless was a town in Bloodstone Valley in Damara founded in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Geography

The town lay within Bloodstone Valley several miles southeast of Bloodstone Village. It was set well off the main road.

Government

Windless lacked a formal government, leaving all major decisions in the hands of small council.

History

The town was founded in the , following the Bloodstone Wars, and was largely made up of folk looking to get away from the turmoil that had been invited upon Bloodstone Village first by the arrival of Zhengyi the Witch-King and later by Gareth Dragonsbane.

Inhabitants

The folk of Windless considered their town to be a suburb of Bloodstone Village, and preferred to lead quieter lives away from the bustle of that settlement. In the mid-to-late 14 century, they were largely loyal to Gareth Dragonsbane, and many served in the Bloodstone army and helped to garrison the nearby Damaran Gate.
