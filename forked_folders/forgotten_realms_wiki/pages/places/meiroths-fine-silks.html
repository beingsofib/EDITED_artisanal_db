Meiroth's Fine Silks was a clothing shop in the city of Waterdeep

Location

This shop fronted the east side of Snail Street, in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This shop stood three stories tall.

Services

Meiroth's Fine Silks sold silks and clothing.
