River Orlaphar

The River Orlaphar flowed from the north out of the Glaur Lake and into the Trackless Sea, across the lands of the kingdom of Gontal.

Features

The valley surrounding River Orlaphar was wild and trackless, full of strange monsters that defied description such as the anaxims.
