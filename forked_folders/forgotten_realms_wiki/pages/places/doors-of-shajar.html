The Doors of Shajar were a pair of gargantuan stone statues named for Shajar that stood in the eastern mouth of east Zakhara's Grey Jungle.

Geography

The Temple of the Serpentine Empress was located roughly southwest from this landmark.

History

The Doors of Shajar were once the ends of a huge stone dam that was built to force the River Abdo into a nearby city's large resevoir. Over time the dam fell apart, leaving the statues the only remaining parts of it.
