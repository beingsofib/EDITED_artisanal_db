The Embassy of Procampur was an embassy of the city of Procampur located in the city of Ravens Bluff in the Vast around 1370 DR.

Description

The embassy was found at the south end of the Foreign District in Ravens Bluff, where a looping side-lane provided access. It was surrounded by a wooded garden illuminated by lamps of ruby and emerald colors on dry evenings, in which visitors could wander freely.

Activities

The Embassy of Procampur held frequent parties all through the year, drawing a large number of visitors and guests. These were mainly merchants and veteran adventurers considering moving to Procampur. They often explored the gardens, talked, and made various deals.
