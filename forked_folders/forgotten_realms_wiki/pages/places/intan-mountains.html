Intan Mountains

The Intan Mountains were a large range of mountains in Kara-Tur.

Geography

The Intan Mountains were bordered in the south by the deep jungles of Malatra, the nation of Petan to the north, and by the forests of Tertava in the north. These set of mountains were south of the several clustered towns and lakes.
