Gulzindar Street

Gulzindar Street was a cobblestone paved avenue

Locations

The right end of Gulzindar Street terminated on Haltovar Street. Up until the mid-14 century DR, its left end terminated on the Lions road. By 1368 DR, that section of road was re-designated as part of Jelzar's Stride.

At its midsection, Gulzindar Street was fully intersected by Darselune Street. The road also acted as the northern terminus of Caldwell Street and the southern terminus of Seawatch Street.

Notable Locations

  • The Field of Triumph's famed Lion Gate entrance stood at the left end of Gulzindar Street.
