Uthgardtheim

Uthgardtheim was the divine realm of Uthgar. It was located on the first layer of Ysgard according to the Great Wheel cosmology, and in Warrior's Rest according to the World Tree cosmology.

Description

The realm was cold, but not inhospitable. The climate was similar to the North during winter.

Cosmography

According to the World Tree cosmology, the realm of Uthgardtheim was located in the colder reaches of Warrior's Rest.

Inhabitants

The petitioners of Uthgardtheim enjoyed hunting and feasting in Uthgar's halls. The god himself shared the realm's pleasures with his fellow warriors.
