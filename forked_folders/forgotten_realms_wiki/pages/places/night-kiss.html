Night Kiss

Building
nameNight Kiss
typeTavern
locationGontal City, Gontal

The Night Kiss was the most notorious tavern in Gontal City.

Background

Its owner was a spellscarred tiefling named Narla Gulmaern, who had the ability to disrupt arcane magic and rituals with her mere presence, making the tavern a safe and popular place for important meetings and illegal activities alike.
