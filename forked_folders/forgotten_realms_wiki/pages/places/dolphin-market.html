The Dolphin Market was an open-air market and landmark found in Saerloon in the .

Location

This roughly circular area was located in the southeast corner of the city near the docks.

Description

The Dolphin Market was crammed to the brim with stalls. A heavy scent of fish permeated the area. The Dophin Market was usually quiet loud with all of the hustle and bustle of activity.
