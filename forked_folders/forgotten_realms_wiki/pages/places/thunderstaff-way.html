Thunderstaff Way

Thunderstaff Way was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Thunderstaff Way was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's North Ward. The road branched southeast from Immar Street, turned due south at the intersection with Tower March, and ended at Trollskull Alley. Thunderstaff Way was intersected by Sashtar Street, Windborne Way, Saerdoun Street, and Horn Street.
