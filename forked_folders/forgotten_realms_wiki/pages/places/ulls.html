Ulls

Ulls was a small lake in the Highlands of the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles.

Description

The waters of the Ulls were calm and dark, appearing black in the evening light. At low tide, a narrow strip of sand allowed for passage along the shore to Crane Point, a small peninsula that was the site of a temple to the moon during the reign of the Kendricks and of a hunting lodge during the reign of the leShay.

Geography

The lake lay below Ulls Peak. It was very near to another small lake, Innes.

Notable Locations

  • An old wall and gatehouse, built during the height of the kingdom of Corwell, which lay between Ulls and Innes. It was later used as a guardhouse by the fey of Sarifal.
  • Corwell's Steeple, a former temple that became a roost for hippogriffs by the mid-to-late 15 century DR.
