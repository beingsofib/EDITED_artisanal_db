Tern's Shipwright Service was a business located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR before that city's destruction.

Location

Tern's Shipwright Service was located in south Zhentil Keep in the Harbor District along Baron Street near the docks and waterfront.

Activities

The business constructed and repaired ships for customers.
