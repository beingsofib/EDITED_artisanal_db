The Flurmastyr residence was a row house and the home of Kappiyan Flurmastyr located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The home stood two stories tall.

Location

The Flurmastyr residence was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward on Anchoret's Court in between Anchoret's Alley and Caravan Street.
