The Quarter of No Hope was the name given to the slums of the city of Ormpe in Durpar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The slums were located outside of Ormpe's stone walls.

Inhabitants

The majority of the nearly 5,000 inhabitants of the Quarter of No Hope scraped by as grain workers.
