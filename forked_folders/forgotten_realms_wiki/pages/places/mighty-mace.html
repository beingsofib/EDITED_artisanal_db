The Mighty Mace was a popular dining and drinking establishment in Daerlun, interior Faerûn, circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR.

Location

This building was on the southeast side of the Westmarket, a large, open marketplace on the Street of the Dragon in west Daerlun.

Structure

Of the buildings that formed the perimeter of the Westmarket, the Mighty Mace was the largest.

Services

The Mighty Mace was open all night and served good food and drink in a cheerful atmosphere of revelry. There were also rooms for rent for those that wanted a comfortable stay or a place to recover from carousing.
