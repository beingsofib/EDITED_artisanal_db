The Crying Witch was a tavern that stood on Suzail's Market, a large open-air plaza on Garth Street.

Location

The tavern stood on the Market's northern end where Garth Street met the plaza, across the street from the Hardshutters House. it was one of the closest drinking establishments to Suzail's western Field-Gates.
