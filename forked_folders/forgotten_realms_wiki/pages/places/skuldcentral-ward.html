The Central Ward was a walled section of the capital city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Central Ward was sat in the middle of Skuld. The district's borders touched those of every other ward and district in the city, and its southern border ran along the north bank of the River of Shadows.

Description

The Central Ward was surrounded by walls. Gates provided access to every other part of Skuld including the Warehouse District, Merchant District, and City of the Gods.
