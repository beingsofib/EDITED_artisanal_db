The Winsome Repose was a riverfront inn in Zazesspur and one of the largest inns within that city.

Location

The inn sat in the western side of the Ships' District of the High City and overlooked the Sulduskoon River.

Structure

The building was four stories tall, making it stand out from other locations on its street.

Interior

The inn was kept in fine condition.

Services

The Winsome Repose surpassed many other inns in quality and served decent meals, making it a favorite among adventurers.

Staff

The innkeeper was a man named Quarlo.

History

Future-queen Zaranda Star once stayed at this inn with her companions Farlorn Half-Elven, Vander Stillhawk, and Shield of Innocence—although the latter two slept in the stable with Zaranda's talking mare.
