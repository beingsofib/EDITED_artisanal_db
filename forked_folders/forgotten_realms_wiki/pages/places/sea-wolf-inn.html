Sea Wolf Inn

Building
nameSea Wolf Inn
typeInn

The Sea Wolf Inn was an inn located in the coastal city of Bezantur in Thay during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The Sea Wolf Inn was located in southern Bezantur on the waterfront with the Alamber Sea near the docks.
