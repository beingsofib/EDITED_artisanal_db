Cold Dolor Gate was one of the the southern gates in the city of Athkatla in Amn.

Location

Cold Dolor Gate was located on the border of the Bridge District and the Center Districts of Athkatla, separating the main city from its Grave District.

Activities

All non-citizens who passed Cold Dolor Gate had to pay an "any one coin" toll, of gold or silver, to be allowed into the main body of Athkatla, however, passage through Sparandar Gate was unrestricted by such means, allowing guests access to Athkatla's cemetery.
