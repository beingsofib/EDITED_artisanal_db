Evening Star Inn (Loudwater)

nameEvening Star Inn
typeInn
locationLoudwater, Delimbiyr Vale

The Evening Star Inn was an inn in the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

It stood in the High Town area of the city.

Description

It was a quite high-quality inn, with windows that could be shuttered and private rooms that could be used to host meetings.

Services

It could put on lavish meals for large gatherings, including wide varieties of meats, cheeses, breads, and stewed vegetables, as wine. It was favored by the elven nobility of the city—Ovendal, matriarch of the Beutaleen'dal family, held meetings there.
