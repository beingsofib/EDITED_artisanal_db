Galaglavur Street

Galaglavur Street was a street in Khôltar, the Iron City, circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR.

Description

This cobblestone street began at Hael Way just a short walk east-southeast of Dubrinlar, the western gate through the walls of Khôltar. On the western corner was Vandanamer's, a noisy and popular luthdren (restaurant). On the eastern corner was another loud establishment, Krostur's Forge. Opposite the mouth of Galaglavur Street, the view was mostly of the back side of Manycoins Hall. Galaglavur ran off in a southerly direction from Hael Way.
