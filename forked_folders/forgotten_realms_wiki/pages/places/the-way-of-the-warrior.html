The Way of the Warrior was the guildhall of a fighter's guild in the city of Zhentil Keep during the late 14 century DR.

Location

The guildhall was located in south Zhentil Keep in the western section of the Foreign Quarter in front of the River Trail Gate in between Mace Way and Slingshot Way.

Description

The hall was long and sat low to the ground. A shrine to Tempus could be found within.

Inhabitants

The guildmaster in the was Cerenna True-edge.
