The Scarlet Slipper was an inn in Waterdeep

Structure

The old inn was a rambling building. It had stairs, indicating more than one floor.

Interior

The place was kept dim.

Services

The inn was renowned for the number of low-price coin-lasses that flocked to it, and it had a less exclusive reputation than other places.

History

Mirt the Merciless was a regular at the inn even as far back as . He met Nalitheen there on Eleint 6.
