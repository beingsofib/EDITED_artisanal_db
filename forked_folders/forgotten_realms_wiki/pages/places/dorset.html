Dorset was a small town located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Dorset was found in southern Callidyrr in the county of Stirgewick. It sat within the southern tip of the island of Alaron which was known as Bregonshire. Dorset was a coastal town on the Sea of Swords just south of the Rushdown Wood on Alaron's eastern coast.
