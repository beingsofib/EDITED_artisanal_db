The Mlembryn Lands were a territory of the Western Heartlands in the 3 century DR.

Description

Settlements in the region were sparse, with steadings and walled villages most common.

Geography

The Mlembryn stretched from the western end of Siluvanede to the southeast of Yarlith. At its south end lay Ladyhouse Falls.

Government

The only settlement of any size in the region was Baerlith, on its western end.

History
