The High Hall of Swords was the local temple of Tempus in the city of Mulmaster, as of the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The temple was located in the eastern region of the city, near the Southroad Keep.

Services

Throughout the 1350s and '60s, the High Hall of Swords was overseen by High Priest-Captain Ghallas "Foesmiter" Kheinstar and tended by 16 war-priests.
