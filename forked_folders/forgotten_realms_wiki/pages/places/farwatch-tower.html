Farwatch Tower was one of the major torchtowers of Waterdeep's Trollwall, located in the city's North Ward.

Location

This tower was located at the end of Tower March, just north of Watch Alley.

Structure

Farwatch Tower stood five stories tall and was directly connected to the Trollwall that bounded the city and separated North Ward from the Field Ward.

Interior

Being a torchtower, Additionally, being a major torchtower, it had six small holding cells that could hold up to ten people, which were used in times of trouble.

Behind an open doorway in the tower's southeastern wall, was a tall yet tiny bare sentry post with walls several feet thick, that was regularly monitored by the City Watch. This was because the sentry post was both haunted and contained a secret portal to Maldiglas’s Refuge. Despite their best efforts, it was often utilized by conspirators, lovers, and beggars seeking shelter.

Activities

Like all other torchtowers, this structure had three beacon-fires that were constantly kept on standby.

Inhabitants

Farwatch Tower, like all torchtowers, was manned by members of the City Watch.
