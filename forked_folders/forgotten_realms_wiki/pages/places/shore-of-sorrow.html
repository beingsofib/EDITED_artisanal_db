The Shore of Sorrow was the border shoreline between the Farsea Marshes and the High Moors in the edge of the Goblin Marches.

Geography

The Shore of Sorrow was located roughly in the foothills of the central part of the Storm Horns. The shoreline separated the swamp from the Shore Horns' northern side, west of the Tempest Valley and northeast from the High Horn.
