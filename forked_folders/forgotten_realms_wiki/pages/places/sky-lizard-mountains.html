Sky Lizard Mountains

The Sky Lizard Mountains, also known as the Luo Peaks, was a branch of the Peaks of Flame that jutted toward Thindol on the Chultan Peninsula.

Description

The towering peaks showed signs of wind erosion.

Geography

These mountains were found in southeastern Chult near the border of Thindol and Samarach. Along with the Sanrach Mountains to the south, they formed the mountainous border of the Chultengar and the Valley of Dread,
