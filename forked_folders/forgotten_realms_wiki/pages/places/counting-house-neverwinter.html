Counting House was a bank and one of the financial centers in the city of Neverwinter in the mid-14 century DR.

Location

The Neverwintran Counting House was located in the city's Warehouse District and was used by the district's tax collector's office located nearby.

History

In the , a band of ne'er-do-wells broke into the Counting House and got away with a fair share of gold coins. The tax collector's office hired a band of adventurers to track the burglars down and return the stolen coin. The adventurers were greeted with the bank's front doors busted open and barely hanging off the hinges. The bandits in question were a gang of goblins led by a menacing-looking hobgoblin.
