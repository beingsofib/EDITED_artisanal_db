Tier Lathadun was a little-known location in the Underdark that hid treasure-filled undead-infested ruins.

History

Some years before the , a tiefling explorer named Pasi discovered the ruins in Tier Lathadun and quickly enriched herself. However, despite her successes, Tier Lathadun became Pasi's tomb. She died plummeting a thousand feed off a slippery edge along with her favorite enchanted weapon called Doorknocker.
