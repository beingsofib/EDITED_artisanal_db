The Hall of the Sages was a tomb located within the City of the Dead in Waterdeep.

Location

The Hall of the Sages was located in the southern section of the City of the Dead.

Description

The tomb was small and dusty.

History

As its name implied, the Hall of the Sages was reserved specifically for sages. Approximately 80 sages were interred within as of the .

Rumors & Legends

A rumor persisted that some of the sages were buried with some of their rare and valuable tomes, making the place an enticing target for thieves. The rumor was spread by Laeral Silverhand and some of her allies in order to flush out black market dealers in forbidden lore.
