Warlsbry was a small village located in the kingdom of Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Warlsbry was located on the coast of the Sea of Moonshae in southwest Gnarhelm near the border of Callidyrr, making it the southernmost settlement in Gnarhelm. The village sat at the end of an unnamed bay at the mouth of the Ironflow near the Hughloch.

History

In the , a young elf named Carric El'Yaranden visited Warsbry and caused a minor stir when he publicly expressed the belief in the superiority of his culture over all others. Some accused him of being a spy for Sarifal.
