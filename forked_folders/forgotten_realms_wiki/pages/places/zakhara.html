Zakhara (), also known as the Land of Fate, was a giant peninsula of the same super-continent that hosted Faerûn and Kara-Tur on the planet Abeir-Toril.

Description

The land was full of secretive cities, unwelcoming to travelers, huge deserts, lush oases and powerful genies who meddled in the affairs of humans frequently.

Zakhara was a land of many types of exotic magic and powerful warriors. Faerûnian tales told of demon-infested cities and godless sorcerers, like the genie-binding sha'irs wielding strange magic.

Geography

The land was located south and east of Faerûn, beyond the region of the Utter East, with the closest Faerûnian lands to Zakhara being Dambrath and Var the Golden (by sea) and Ulgarth or arguably Konigheim (by land). Zakhara was mostly isolated from the rest of the world, as the peninsula was separated from the main mass by the treacherous World Pillar Mountains.

The seas around Zakhara were plagued with pirates and corsairs who charged traders tolls to cross their seas. Such traders willingly paid these exorbitant fees, as Zakhara's exotic trading goods tended to be well worth the price back in Faerûn. Occasionally the pirates decided to completely cut off Zakhara from Faerûn.

Inhabitants

The Land of Fate was home to a diversity of non-human inhabitants, but the majority comprised human Zakharans with brown skin, dark hair, and relatively small height.

Only a few Zakharans ventured outside their homeland, usually as traders, and could typically be found only in the very south of Faerûn. and Calishites came from the Land of Fate to Faerûn in the ancient past with the help of portals.
