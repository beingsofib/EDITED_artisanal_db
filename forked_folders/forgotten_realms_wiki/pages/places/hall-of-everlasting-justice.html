The Hall of Everlasting Justice was a stronghold of a temple dedicated to the deities Tyr and Torm within the city of Sundabar, during the 14 century DR. It was located in the center of town, facing into the Circle, opposite the Master's Hall.

Services

Clerics of the hall served within the Shieldsar, the army of Sundabar, as both combat medics and divine casters of battle-magic.

History

Sometime shortly before Eleasis 1372 DR, the temple's lead clergyman, Defender of Justice Triandial Truthammer disappeared while out on adventure. He was succeeded by "Lady Stone", Lathkiera Morlund.
