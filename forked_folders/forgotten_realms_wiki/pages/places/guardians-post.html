The Guardian's Post was a temple dedicated to Helm found along the Lake of Steam in the coastal city of Yhep.

Inhabitants

The temple was led by a paladin of Helm named Imingo Deiros during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

Much of the Guardian's Post's time and resources were spent monitoring for any beholder activity near Yhep which plagued the city during its past.
