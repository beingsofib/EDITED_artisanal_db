Fireshear Inn was an inn located in the city of Fireshear in the early 14 century DR.

Location

This inn was located in the north-eastern side of Fireshear, across from Bahriit's General Equipment Shop.

History

In the , after having finally cleared their names to the Lords' Alliance, the Heroes of Ascore traveled to Fireshear in search of the legendary hoard of Freezefire. While there they were able to find refuge within the walls of the Fireshear Inn.

Sometime between 1358 and 1365 DR the inn presumably shut down, for while there Volothamp Geddarm remarked in his book Volo's Guide to the North that the mining settlement was completely lacking in any sort of inn.
