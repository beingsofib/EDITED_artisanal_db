Vaelan/Gold District

Location
nameGold District
typeDistrict
regionVaelan, Durpar

The Gold District was a district in Vaelan, Durpar. It held most of the chaka towers in Vaelan, and enjoyed the protection of flimsy-looking yet strong bleached stone walls. Its borders were well-patrolled.
