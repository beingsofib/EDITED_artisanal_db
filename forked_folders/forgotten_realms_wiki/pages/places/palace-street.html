Palace Street

Palace Street was a road located in the city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Palace Street was found in the central section of Skuld in the Central Ward. The street originated at the entrance to the Palace District where it ran southeast through the Central Ward before ending near the East Gate.
