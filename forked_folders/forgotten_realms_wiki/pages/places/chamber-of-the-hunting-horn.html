Chamber of the Hunting Horn

Building
nameChamber of the Hunting Horn
typeRoom

The Chamber of the Hunting Horn was a luxurious chamber in the High Palace of Silverymoon during the 14 century DR.

Location

It was one of the rooms in the High Palace of Silverymoon.

Structure

The doorknob to the chamber was shaped like an upswept horn. There was a balcony, with pillars and overhangs carved in wood; the top of it was not visible when there wasn't enough light.

Interior

The room was richly furnished, with tapestries and paintings, their subjects including elven hunts, swanmays rising from a pond in a forest and transforming into swans, a flight of pegasi. Rich rugs were strewn around the floor. The room could be lit by its lamps and hanging sconces, but a single hunting horn hung from a chain above all, glowing a soft amber.

It boasted many chairs, tables, and a lounge.
