The Jade Dancer was a festhall and tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The building stood three stories tall.

Location

The Jade Dancer was located in the southern section of the Southern Ward on the Dancing Court just off of the southern end of Slop Street.
