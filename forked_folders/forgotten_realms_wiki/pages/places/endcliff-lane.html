Endcliff Lane

Endcliff Lane was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Endcliff Lane was located in the east section of Waterdeep's North Ward. Endcliff Lane branched due east from Nindabar Street and ended on Saerdoun Street a short distance away from the outer wall in front of Endcliff Tower.
