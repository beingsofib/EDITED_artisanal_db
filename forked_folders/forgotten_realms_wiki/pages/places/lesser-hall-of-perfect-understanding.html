The Lesser Hall of Perfect Understanding was a large dormitory cave found within the Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert of the Hordelands during the mid 14 century DR.

Description

The cave was located on the far left side of the great chamber level of the temple. The dorms measured wide by long. Three passages served as entryways into the cave.

History

During the temple's heyday, the hall was used as personal quarters for all of the abbots who lived within.
