Goewin Wood was a remote and wild woodland in the southwest of the isle of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

It was a deciduous forest that continued from the undead-haunted western reaches of the largely evergreen Llyrath Forest on the southern coast of Gwynneth. It lay south of Kimball Moor and east of Mere Adammair and the Dwyvach Salt Flats.

Inhabitants

The forest and surrounding wilderness south of Kimball Moor were home to nomadic tribes of shifters. These tribes venerated a being known as the Cat Lord who dwelt within the heart of Goewin Wood.

Notable Locations

  • Glade of the Cat Lord, a meeting place for the shifter followers of the Cat Lord to perform bloody rites in his honor.
  • Tetauctaa, a village just inside the southern edge of the woods.
  • A moonwell lay in the far south of the forest just east of Tetauctaa.
