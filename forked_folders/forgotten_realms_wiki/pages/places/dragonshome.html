Dragonshome, sometimes called Dragonhome, was a moderately sized island within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Dragonshome was one of the northernmost of the Moonshaes. It was northeast of the Norheim Isles and west of the Korinn Archipelago, and its closest notable neighbor was Grayrock to the southwest.

Inhabitants

The isle was under the control of the Northlanders of the Moonshaes, although it was notably the only major island in its area that was uninhabited.

As of the mid-to-late 14 century DR, Dragonshome lay within the territory claimed by Arveiaturace, a mighty white dragon. It was one of only a few places where she allowed other dragons to reside within her hunting grounds, although this privilege was offered only to young dragons that would obey any commands she might make. If these dragons defied her, she would tear them apart.
