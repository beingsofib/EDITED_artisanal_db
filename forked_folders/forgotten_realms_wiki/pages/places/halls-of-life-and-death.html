The Halls of Life and Death were storage rooms for religious items found within the Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert of the Hordelands during the mid 14 century DR.

Description

The Halls were located in the upper galleries level of the temple. They were connected to both the Hall of the Water Buffalo and the Cave of Tranquil Peace. Two chambers were found within. The smaller measured long by wide, while the larger measured long by wide.

History

The chambers within the Hall were used to store religious artifacts such as ceremonial masks and other priestly garbs used on holy days.
