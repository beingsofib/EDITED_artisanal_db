The Captain's Suq was a chandler's shop located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR.

Location

The shop lay in the north central part of Iskalat in the southwest region of the City of Brass. It was situated between the Albrazi Canal to the north and the Street of Stelae to the south.
