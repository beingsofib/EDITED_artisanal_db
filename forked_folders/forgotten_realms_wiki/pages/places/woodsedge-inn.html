Woodsedge Inn was an inn of Athalantar in the 3 century DR.

Location

The inn was due north of Jander.

Structure

It had a stable.

Services

Among other foodstuffs, the inn was known to have served at least once turkey soup, and cold fowl in a pinch.

History

Prince Elminster Aumar, while an outlaw in Horn Hills, headed for the Woodsedge Inn in winter of , on the advice of the knight Helm Stoneblade, when they were looking for a place to hide. While sleeping in the stables, he was captured briefly by the Magelord Caladar Thearyn, from whom he was in turn saved by Broarn, the innkeep. They later had an unplanned rendezvous with Helm Stoneblade, where they decided the outlaws could spend a season hiding out in Hastarl, where the Magelords wouldn't be searching for them.
