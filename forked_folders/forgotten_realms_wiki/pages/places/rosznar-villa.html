Rosznar Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Rosznar.

Location

The villa was located in the northeast section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward in between Copper Street and Shield Street on Thunderstaff Way at the eastern end of Wrightstone Street

Description

Four buildings were found within the walled estate with gardens separating them. Each building stood two or five stories tall.
