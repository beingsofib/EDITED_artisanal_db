The Waiting Stone was a ryokan (inn) in Aru in Wa in the 1770s on the Wa Calendar. It stood in the northern Mixed District. It was run by a woman known as a .

Description

Rooms were divided by screens and were warmed by braziers on which aromatic woods were burned. Each had futon beds.

History

In high summer of , a group of adventurers newly arrived in Aru were staying at The Waiting Stone. It was here, late at night, that a troop of samurai led by Tsukku woke and summoned them to a midnight meeting with the daimyo, Benju Matsutomo. with the monk Yaemon possibly sending them a message, and any blow-back from their investigations and associations potentially finding them here. On a later night, samurai once again woke and summoned the adventurers to a meeting, this time in secrecy with Benju's consort Naomi. At one point, their rooms may have been ransacked by a thief in black looking for valuables to steal; the mama-san saw the thief leave but thought it was just another odd visitor.
