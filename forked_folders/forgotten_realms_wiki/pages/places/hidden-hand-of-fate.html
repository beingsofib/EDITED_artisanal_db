The Hidden Hand of Fate was a temple complex dedicated to Hoar, the Doombringer.

Location

The temple was located within the Arch Wood in Archendale in the Dalelands.

Activities

The temple's influence rose with the decline of Hoar's following in Chessenta. The temple supported numerous bards, bounty hunters, assassins and vigilante groups who were active throughout the Heartlands.
