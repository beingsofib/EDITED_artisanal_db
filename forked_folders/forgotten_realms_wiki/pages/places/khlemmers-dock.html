Khlemmer's dock was a dock along the Chionthar in Scornubel during the 14 century DR.

Location

It lay along the Chionthar.

Activities

There were weights at the dock.

History

In , several drow under the command of the drow sorceress Daerdatha hurled a blindfolded and tied-up Qilué Veladorn into the Chionthar at Khlemmer's dock, after loading her with weights to make her stay down.
