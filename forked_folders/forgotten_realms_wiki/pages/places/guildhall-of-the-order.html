The Guildhall of the Order was the headquarters of the Solemn Order of Recognized Furriers and Woolmen in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep.

Location

The guildhall was situated on Waterdeep Way between the intersections of the High Road and the Street of Bells.

Structure

The building itself was large, prosperous-looking, and made of stone. Outside of it stood a large statue of a beaver with a wooden placard that indicated it was the "Guildhall of the Order."

For much of its existence this guildhall had only one floor, and 1479 DR a second story was added to the structure.
