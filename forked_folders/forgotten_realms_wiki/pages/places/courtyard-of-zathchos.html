The Courtyard of Zathchos was a plaza in Suzail, named after once of the most influential merchant families in the city – Zathchos.

Description

The Courtyard of Zathchos was at the center of a block between Stonebow Street to the north, to the south, the Promenade to the west, and Torch Street to the east. The entrance to the plaza was a wide opening by Blackgorgons, the tower home of wizard named Baskor on the Stonebow Street.
