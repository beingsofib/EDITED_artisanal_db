Scirkhel Wands residence was a row house and the home of Scirkhel Wands located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The home stood three stories tall.

Location

Scirkhel Wands residence was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward on the southern side of River Street near the River Gate.
