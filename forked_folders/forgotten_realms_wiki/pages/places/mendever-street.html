Mendever Street

Mendever Street was a large road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Mendever Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward with a small portion extending into the Castle Ward. It ran north to south, nearly through the entire length of the ward. Mendever Street originated on Heroes' Walk where it branched south, eventually terminating on Trader's Way just north of the Market. It was intersected by many other roads including Wrightstone Street, Chasso's Trot, Vondil Street, Delzorin Street, the Rough Road, Zarimtar Street, Whim Street, Julthoon Street, and Heard Lane.
