The Plain of Stone Spiders was a wasteland between the Calim Desert and Almraiven in Calimshan. Its most notable inhabitant was the demon called the Spider that Waits, who was bound by the magic of Almraiven's many wizards.

History

By the , the wasteland was formed from the dried up Spider Swamp caused by the expansion of the Calim Desert. At this time, the local creatures had been changed by some power, be it elemental magic or perhaps plaguechanged, which granted them elemental powers limited over the rocky wasteland.
