P'karnis

P'Karnis was a merrow kingdom in Serôs, under the Sea of Fallen Stars.

History

P'Karnis was one of the many merrow kingdoms that united alongside the koalinths of Serôs in the Third Serôs War. Two years after the war began, in , the kingdom of P'Karnis fell.
