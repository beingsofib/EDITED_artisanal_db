Hammerlar's Fine Floors and Housework was the woodshop of Olen Hammerlar, the highly skilled house carpenter found within the city of Sundabar during the mid–14 century DR.

He would work throughout the Sundabar Vale, taking his wagon-drawn, horse-driven sawmill anywhere within a six-day ride from the city.

Hammerlar prided himself on fast, efficient work in and around the city of Sundabar. Unless ordered during the winter months, Olen swore he only needed a single day to build the porch of a house, or a tenday for a palisade or entire cabin.
