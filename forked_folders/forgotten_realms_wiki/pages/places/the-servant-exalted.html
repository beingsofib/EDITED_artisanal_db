The Servant Exalted was an upscale tavern in Suzail, Cormyr, in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , prior to the Council of the Dragon, the lords Broryn Windstag, Kathkote Dawntard, and Delasko Sornstern had offered much money at taverns frequented by servants of noblemen, including the Servant Exalted, for any enchanted largish hand axes belonging to a noble under the assumption such weapons were likely to be blueflame items.
