Dancing Druid Lane

The Dancing Druid Lane was a short curved street in Suzail to the northeast of the Bolliver's Wharf and the location of the Osculatory.

Description

The Dancing Druid Lane began on the crossing of the Court Close and Silverscales Street, just two buildings away from the waters of Dragonmere. The street ran east whilst forming a gentle curve southwards and ended just past the Osculatory.

Notable Locations

  • The Osculatory, the establishment sometimes referred to as the kissing club – a meeting place for single folk in search of companionship.
