Crypt of the Moondark

nameCrypt of the Moondark

locationWestgate, Dragon Coast

The Crypt of the Moondark was the family crypt of the Moondark family in Westgate.

Structure

There was a sign posted outside of the crypt, until Elminster Aumar defaced it with a spell that made it crumble to dust. Guards could rest along one corner of the crypt, while the opposite path was lined with stones.

Interior

There were stacked stone coffers in a room inside, which had a funeral slab in the center. Older coffins had the likeness of the departed on their lids, while the newer coffins were plain, some even nameless.

Defenses

Inside, there were a number of traps, including door wards, a falling beam trap, and a number of jutting blades.

Outside, there were guards posted on a corner.

History

In the , about a hundred and sixty years after the Moondark family had all perished, Elminster entered the crypt and placed an enchanted wand, an everbright dagger that glowed upon command, and spell notes in the late Eaergladden Moondark's coffin.

Rumors & Legends

Elminster knew that Eaergladden Moondark was no wizard, and that he'd in fact died penniless, but counted on the fact that no one would know this if they came looking for hidden magic.
