On Four Feet was a barn located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the late 14 century DR.

Location

The barn was located in south Zhentil Keep in the western section of the Foreign Quarter in between Teshwave Street and Center Street.

Activities

The barn housed a number of livestock, and customers could use the structure as a stable for their horses or other animals as well. The rates were 10 gp per night for regular mounts and 25 gp for exotic mounts. These prices included grooming and feeding.
