Highpeak

Highpeak was one of the peaks of the Orcskull Mountains, on the island of Moray in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

This particular mountain was on the northwestern side of the Orcskull Mountains.

History

In the mid–14 century, Elminster visited King Dagdar, and they explored Moray. During one of the nights, a frightening cry was heard by both men and their hunting party. The king explained that it was the maiden of Highpeak, who cried out for her children's lost souls. He then explained the maiden's tale to the Old Mage.

During the Time of Troubles in 1358 DR, a soul upon the Fugue Plane claimed to have killed Astolpho of Highpeak with their blade.

Notable Locations

  • A castle of glass where the maiden of Highpeak was said to reside.
