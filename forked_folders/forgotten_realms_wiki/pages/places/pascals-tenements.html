Pascal's Tenements was a boarding house and den of thieves located in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

This larger four story building was located at the intersection of Duke's Way and the Farmer's Road in central Daggerford.

Activities

Several thieves worked together within the tenement to avoid the attention of Daggerford's militia. Each flat within the building had its own stove, privy, kitchenette, and small front room. The building was owned by Pascal.
