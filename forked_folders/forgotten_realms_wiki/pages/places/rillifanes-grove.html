Rillifane's Grove

Location
nameRillifane's Grove
typeGrove
regionLeuthilspar, Evermeet
religionSeldarine

Rillifane's Grove was a grove of old oak trees. It was used by moon and sun elf priests to contact the Seldarine. It was also used for ceremonies, funerals, and marriages by wood and wild elves who traveled to Leuthilspar.
