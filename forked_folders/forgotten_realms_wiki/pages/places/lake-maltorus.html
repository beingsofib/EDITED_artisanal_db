Lake Maltorus

Lake Maltorus was a lake found in the Ride, in the cold lands of north Faerûn.

Geography

Lake Maltorus lay in the center of the plains situated between the White Peaks and the Dragonspine Mountains. It was fed by the waters of Stoneshoulder Creek, that flowed north from the Dragonspines.

A stretch of the Rogan's Sprint road passed along the northern shore of the lake.
