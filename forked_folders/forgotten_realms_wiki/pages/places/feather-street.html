Feather Street

Feather Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Feather Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. It originated on Delzorin Street where it branched southwest and ended at a major intersection that included Haltovar Street, Zarimtar Street, the Sutherlane, and Darselune Street. Feather Street was also intersected by the Rough Road.
