Saden was an independent, small thorp located in southern Chessenta, on the bank of the Adder River.

Places of interest

Saden was well known for its ancient temple of Marduk, which was located in its central plaza. As of 1480 DR, the temple had been repurposed for the use of the faithful of Bahamut.
