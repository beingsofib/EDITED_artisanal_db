The Inn of the Seven Silvers was an inn alongside the Dawnpost road in Sembia during the late 14 century DR.

Location

The inn lay alongside the Dawnpost highway.

Structure

The building had double doors for entry

Interior

There was a hearth in the common room.

Services

There were rooms for hire.

History

The Inn was used as quarters by the Company of the Chimera during Mirtul of the , when they were guarding a waystation and a stretch of the Dawnpost.
