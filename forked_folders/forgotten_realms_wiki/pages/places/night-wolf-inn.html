Night Wolf Inn

Building
nameNight Wolf Inn
typeInn
locationArabel, Cormyr

The Night Wolf Inn was an inn located in the city of Arabel in Cormyr. It was considered an inn of lesser quality

Location

The inn was located outside the city walls north of the High Road just before it passed through High Horn Gate into the city. It was across a path from Mother Lahamma's House, a boarding house.
