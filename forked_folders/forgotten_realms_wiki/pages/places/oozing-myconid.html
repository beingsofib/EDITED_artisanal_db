Oozing Myconid

Building
nameOozing Myconid
typeTavern
locationBraeryn (the Stenchstreets), Menzoberranzan

The Oozing Myconid was a tavern in Braeryn (the Stenchstreets) in Menzoberranzan. It was known for having the most disgusting fare in the city. It was frequented by Jarlaxle and Zaknafein.
