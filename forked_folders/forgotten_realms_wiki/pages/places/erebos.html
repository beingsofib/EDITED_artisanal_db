Erebos was a large city on the Sea of Fallen Stars. The city could call up 8,000 troops and had a large fleet of ships with 5,000 marine soldiers. An ancient temple of Tiamat stood near the sea.

History

In the 15 century DR, Erebos had trade relationships with the nation of Sebakar, with whom they did business via the small trading post of Thurik.

Sometime during the Era of Upheaval, a new incarnation of Tchazzar appeared in the Realms and claimed Erebos as his own domain.
