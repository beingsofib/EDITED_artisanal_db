Coin Alley

Coin Alley was a small road located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Coin Alley was found in southern Waterdeep within the Castle Ward. The alley originated on Sail Street near the waterfront. It branched north and merged with Tarnished Silver Alley just west of the Old Temple Lot near the base of Mount Waterdeep.

Notable Locations

The mansion of Mirt the Moneylender sat at the junction of Coin Alley and Tarnished Silver Alley.
