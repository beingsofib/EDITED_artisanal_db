Toadstool Thicket was a small forest located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Toadstool Thicket sat in the shadow of the southern slopes of Aspenheight in western Gwynneth just west of the Aspenheight Gap. Two tributaries of an unnamed river cut through the forest on their way down from the mountain to the Strait of the Leviathan. Toadstool Thicket was approximately long.

Notable Locations

A single Moonwell was found within the forest.
