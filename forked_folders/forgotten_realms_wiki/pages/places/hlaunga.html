Hlaunga was a secret, Zhentarim-manufactured oasis located within the Anauroch desert. During the mid-14 century DR it served as a way point for Zhent caravans that traveled across Great Grand Sea via the Black Road.

Geography

Hlaunga was located in the southern stretch of the Sword, just below the southern peaks of the Scimitar Spires mountain range.

Inhabitants

The Zhentarim kept a contingent of mages and the Zhentilar a garrison of soldiers in Hlaunga, to protect the collective groups' interests from the Bedine.
