Trader's Road

The Trader's Road was a road traveling from the Sunset Vale through the Dragon Coast.

Geography

The road ran from the town of Easting in the Sunset Vale, across the Dragon Reach through Elversult, across the Horse Prairie, to the town of Teziir, then turns south and goes through the town of Smuggler's Bank and Reddansyr, then crosses the river Reddan and ends at the city of Starmantle on the Sea of Fallen Stars.
