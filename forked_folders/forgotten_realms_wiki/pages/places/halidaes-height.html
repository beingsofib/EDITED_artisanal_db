Halidae's Height was a forest-covered hilltop south and a little west of the city of Myth Drannor in Cormanthor.

History

In the , Halidae's Height was warded against all types of fiends by the god Azuth, for "as long as Azuth's power endures". This ward may have collapsed with the destruction of Azuth in the .
