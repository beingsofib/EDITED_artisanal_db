Aveen Street

Aveen Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Aveen Street was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's North Ward. The road ran in a straight line diagonally between Shando Street in the southwest and Gelthoon Street to the northeast. Several small unnamed alleys branched off both sides of Aveen Street.
