Rosestone Abbey

Rosestone Abbey is a partially ruined abbey located in Eastern Thar dedicated to the worship of Amaunator.

Description

Much of the abbey is ruined with many gaps in the outer curtain wall. Some of the inner buildings remain intact and these are occupied by a small number of priests.

History

In the , Rosestone Abbey was the site of a battle between forces loyal to the rightful Harmach of Hulburg under the command of Kara Hulmaster and those of the shadow mage Rhovann and his puppet Maroth Marstel. The loyalist army won the battle after Geran Hulmaster destroyed a magical device that animated dozens of golem soldiers in Rhovann's army.
