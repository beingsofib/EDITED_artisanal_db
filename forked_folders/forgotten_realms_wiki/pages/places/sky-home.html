Sky Home

Sky Home was a plane in the Elemental Chaos and was home to the primordial Akadi. The plane was a broad expanse of sky, clouds and storms. In some places the winds coalesced to form structures which the planes inhabitants resided in. Sky Home had connections to Arvandor in the Astral Sea.
