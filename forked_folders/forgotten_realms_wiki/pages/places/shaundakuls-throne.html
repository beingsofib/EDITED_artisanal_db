Shaundakul's Throne was a temple dedicated to Shaundakul, the Rider of the Winds.

Location

The shrine was located within in Myth Drannor before the fall of the city.

Description

A pair of towers were linked by walls forming an enclosed courtyard. The large central building contained the altar and an undercroft where Shaundakul's clergy lived. The actual throne sat upon a raised dais and was exosed to the sky.

History

The ruins of the temple stood long after the fall of Myth Drannor. Shaundakul's avatar often stood guard over the ruins.
