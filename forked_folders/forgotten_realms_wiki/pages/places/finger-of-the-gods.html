The Finger of the Gods was a lighthouse enchanted with a continual light spell in Melvaunt around 1367 DR.

Location

The lighthouse was located at the western end of the docks.

Description

The Finger of the Gods stood tall and was made from cast-iron.

Inhabitants

Jered Wavetamer manned the lighthouse in 1367 DR.
