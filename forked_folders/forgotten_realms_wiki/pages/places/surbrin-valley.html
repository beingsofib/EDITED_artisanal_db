The Surbrin Valley was the rocky vale formed by the River Surbrin in the Savage Frontier of northwest Faerûn. It extended from the Spine of the World in the north to the Evermoors in the south, and between the Moonwood in the east and the Frost Hills in the west.

History

As of 1371 DR, the whole of the Surbrin Valley was taken up by the Kingdom of Many-Arrows.
