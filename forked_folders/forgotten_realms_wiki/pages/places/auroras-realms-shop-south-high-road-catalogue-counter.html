Aurora's Realms Shop, South High Road Catalogue Counter was an outlet of the Aurora's Emporium retail chain in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. It was one of six such outlets in the city.

Location

It was located along the Way of the Dragon, next to the Red Gauntlet tavern.

Description

It was situated in a four-story tenement building of decaying appearance.

Services

This shop sold the various items found in Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Staff

In the mid-1360s DR, Mril Juthbuck worked here as a counter clerk and Logros Hlandarr as a service-mage. In addition, there were four guards who worked shifts of two and two.
