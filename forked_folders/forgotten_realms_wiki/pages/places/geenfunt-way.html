Geenfunt Way

Geenfunt Way was a road in the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the 14 century DR.

Description

Geenfunt Way led into the Funt Market. At the end of the road there stood the Geenfunt Armory, which was a station of the Loudwater Guard commanded by Fist Captain Nesrin circa 1372 DR.

Elsewhere on Geenfunt Way was an entrance to the Duelists' Grotto, which connected to Pool's Walk.
