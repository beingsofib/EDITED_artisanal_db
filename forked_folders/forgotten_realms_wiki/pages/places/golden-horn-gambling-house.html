The Golden Horn Gambling House, which was also simply called the Golden Horn, was a festhall located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The festhall stood three stories tall.

Location

The Golden Horn Gambling House was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward on the north end of Snail Street where it was met by Niles Way.

Services

Drugs could also be purchased within the Golden Horn.

Inhabitants

A doppelganger named Ptola posed as a courtesan at the Golden Horn Gambling House.
