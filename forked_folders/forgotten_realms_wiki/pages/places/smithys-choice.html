Smithy's Choice was a small blacksmiths' shop in the western part of the city of Phlan in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

As of the , the Smithy's Choice operated out of the western part of Phlan, not far from the Traitor's Gate that led to the eastern Phlan. The smith was happy to sell several mundane sets of armor, including more exotic elfin chain.
