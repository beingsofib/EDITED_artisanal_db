Redrun

The Redrun was a stream located in the Ten Towns region of Icewind Dale. It drained water from Lac Dinneshere and carried over to Redwaters lake.

It was a common destination for fishermen who preferred to stay off boats.

Features

During most of the year, the Redrun was little more than a steady stream. When the ice from Kelvin's Cairn melted in the spring, the rush of fresh ice water transformed it into raging whitewater rapids.

History

In the , two fishermen traveling along the Redrun disappeared within the span of two tendays.
