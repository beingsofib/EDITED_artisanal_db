Wolmad's Camp was an Uthgardt thorp located in the Silver Marches.

Geography

The Camp was located in a vale of the northern flanks of the Nether Mountains not far from Citadel Felbarr.

Organization

A branch of the Sky Pony Tribe led by Wolmad Eaglewing lived here. They practiced banditry in the guise of "civilized" rule over the surrounding land.

Notable Inhabitants

  • War-leader Wolmad Eaglewing
  • Chief lieutenant Morgwan Fellfoot
  • Fara Icehewer
  • Numa the Speaker
