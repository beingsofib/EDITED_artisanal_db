The Saprach's Fine Wines was a shop in the city of Tantras in the Vast around 1358 DR, dedicated to selling wine.

Location

The store was located in the east side of the city, on Whistleturn Way near the Market.

Services

The store had the finest selection of wines in Tantras. Furthermore, the proprietor, Saprach, had a sideline of fencing stolen property and also specialized in the discreet disposal of dead bodies.
