Agenais was a tiny empire of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. It participated in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Description

Agenais was on the southernmost island of the Whamite Islands. It shared major land borders with the Land of the Beastly Brigands and Wolfenbutte, and a minor one with Swil. A distinctive mountain range marked Agenais's border with the Land of the Beastly Brigands and along its coastline that overlooked the Swil Passage.

Perhaps the only settlement on Agenais was a small village by the side of a hill. It had a white lighthouse and a dock. The other buildings there were divided between common and more wealthy sections. The streets were narrow and cobbled. In the water, there was a circle of standing stones sacred to Eldath. The stones were made from white marble and coral.

History

When the kelpie summoned by Iakhovas as part of the Twelfth Serôs War caused the people of the Whamites to drown themselves en masse, Agenais was desolated. In mid-Mirtul of the , an adventuring party sent by Andeth Ilchammar to establish diplomatic relations with Myth Nantar arrived in Agenais. They were sent there through a portal at the request of the Nantarn Council, who demanded that they return the Crown of Salt from Agenais. After avoiding the song of the kelpie, and the undead that prowled Agenais, they came to the lighthouse. There, they faced off against Vilander and his traps to recover the crown from him.

Inhabitants

Before they all drowned themselves, Agenais had a variety of colorful political figures that vied for control of the nation and of the Whamite Isles at large. Known individuals were the Emperor Inwhar III, the Empress Zelina, and the Grand Wizir Necchamin. They were served by the Imperial Guard of Agenais and the simply named Fleet. It was also the home of the Monks of Nabbach and the Monastary Nabbach. The people of Agenais were sailors and villagers.

By the , the mad, elderly, deaf pirate Vilander the Fierce had barricaded himself inside Agenais's lighthouse.
