Syòrpiir was an ancient kingdom of moon elves and sun elves created during the First Flowering in .

History

Syòrpiir was drawn into the War of Three Leaves against Thearnytaar and Eiellûr by the actions of assassins and spies from Ilythiir in .

In , the kingdom was destroyed by the dark elf kingdom of Ilythiir during the second war of the Crown Wars. Over the years, the ruins of the elven civilization were taken over by the Chondalwood and the Ankhwood.
