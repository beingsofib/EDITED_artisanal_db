Lathander's Open Hand was a shrine dedicated to the Morninglord Lathander, found in the village of Hap in Battledale. It remained standing throughout the 14 and 15 centuries DR.

Structure

The Open Hand was in fact once a simple barn built from wood and stone.

Interior

A simple chapel with an altar lay in the center of Lathander's shrine. Floating some to above the alter was the Blood of Lathander, drop's of the god's blood encased in a chunk of amber that dated back centuries before the Era of Upheaval.

Located in the rear of the shrine were the simple living quarters of its attending priestess. Set atop a disused hay loft was a single bell, that was rung when local townsfolk needed to be alerted.

Staff

As of the mid–14 century, the shrine was overseen by Mumfrey Mimly, a rather timid priest of the Morninglord. Mimly was later replaced by the forthright and altruistic Dawnmaster Cathalandra Dovaer.

Circa 1479 DR, Lathander's Open Hand was overseen by Sister Tressa, one of the few Lathanderians that remained in the Dales for a time following the return of Amaunator to the Realms.

Services

Both Dawnmaster Dovaer and Sister Tressa provided aid to morally true adventurers in the form of healing.
