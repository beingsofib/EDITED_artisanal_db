River Arran

The River Arran was a river system found in Chondath and Sespech.

Geography

The beginning tributaries of the river originated in the Chondalwood in southern Chondath. The tributaries joined together near Elbulder and continued to flow northwest for approximately before emptying into the Vilhon Reach. The river flowed past the Golden Plains and formed some of the border between Sespech and Chondath.

Notable Locations

Fort Arran was located on the western bank of the River Arran near the Vilhon Reach. The river was crossed by both the Golden Road and the Old Road.
