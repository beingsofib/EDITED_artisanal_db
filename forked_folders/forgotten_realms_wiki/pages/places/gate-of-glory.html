The Gate of Glory was located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire.

This gate was found in the Foundry ward of the city.

Description

The Gate of Glory was the entrance to the Glory Mines–a maze of tunnels running beneath the City of Brass that was inhabited by salamanders.
