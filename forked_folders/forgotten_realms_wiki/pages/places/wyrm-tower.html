Wyrm Tower was the tower of the archmage Shandrath in the early 3 century DR.

Location

The tower lay in Athalantar.

Defenses

The building was defended by the power of its archmage, Shandrath.

History

Shandrath took the tower for his own at some point in his old age. The tower itself was destroyed in the , during a combined attack by many of the Magelords of Athalantar.
