The Shigatse Glacier was a monstrous glacier that sat just south of Ra-Khati in the Katakoro Shan mountain range.

Geography

The glacier measured in length and was at its widest point. The Shigatse's yearly runoff fed the Muren Tso on the Katakoro Plateau to the east.
