Castle Klocck

Building
nameCastle Klocck

locationWolfenbutte, the Whamite Isles

Castle Klocck was a castle in Wolfenbutte in the Whamite Isles. It was a key defense of Wolfenbutte in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Although Castle Klocck was a oart of Wolfenbutte, its name was curiously similar to that of the opposing country Klocckbarn.
