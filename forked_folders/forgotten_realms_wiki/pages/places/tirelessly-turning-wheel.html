The Tirelessly Turning Wheel was a shop in the city of Waterdeep in 1370 DR that sold various curios from all across Faerûn.

Location

This shop was located along Julthoon Street, in the North Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

The building housing this shop stood three stories tall.

Services

This shop sold a variety of different curios, some of them quite fragile and irreplaceable.

Inhabitants

This business was owned by the elven couple Jhanathe and Ormsiir Mrhulaedir. They lived in the business with their son, Oenel Mrhulaedir, who sometimes accidentally broke curios with his stocky build's passing.
