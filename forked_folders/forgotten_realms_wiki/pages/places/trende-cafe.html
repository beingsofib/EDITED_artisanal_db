Trende Cafe was an eatery located in the affluent Merchant's District of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR.

History

In the , the wizard named Samman was swingled by a group of criminals. These people, Marella, Elizza, Moamar, and Rundell, were members of the warlord Myrkyssa Jelan's army that waged war on the Vast just a year prior. Samman posted a bill that he sought a special magic wand. The criminals arranged a meeting and sealed the deal at the Trende Cafe.
