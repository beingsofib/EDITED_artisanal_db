Lake Way

The Lake Way was a major highway in Amn that formed the route between Crimmor and Amnwater.

Geography

The road was built along the wide east–west ridgeline that rose above Lake Weng and Minsor Vale. It ran mostly parallel to the South Fork, and passed north of the Seventeen Sentries standing stones that lay between the two roads.
