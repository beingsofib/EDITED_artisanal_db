River Shaelyn

The River Shaelyn was an important waterway found on the elven island homeland of Evermeet.

Geography

The river originated from the Lake of Dreams near the center of the island. It branched from the lake's eastern shore, traveling toward the Silver Hills before joining the larger River Ardulith just north of Leuthilspar.
