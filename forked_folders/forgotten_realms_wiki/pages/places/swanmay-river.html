Swanmay River

The Swanmay River was a prominent river located within the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron in the Moonshae Isles.

Description

The river was largely pleasant to travel down via coracle.

Geography

The river originated in far northern Callidyrr in the Fairheight Range just south of the border with Gnarhelm. The river flowed south into the tangles of the Dernall Forest, and wound its way through the wood to exit in the forest's southeast corner where it continued to flow east to empty into the Sea of Swords between Torriage Wood and the Tearglade. In all the Swanmay River stretched for over through Callidyrr.

Notable Locations

  • Blackstone
  • Doncastle
  • Horsa

Inhabitants

The bandits who hid at Doncastle in the Dernall Forest were known to lay traps along the river to ensnare boats which they suspected of carrying treasure belonging to the High King.

As with other freshwater streams in the Moonshaes, swanmays could be encountered in the river.
