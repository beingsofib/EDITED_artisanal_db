Kasal was a small town found along the coast of Afyal within the Cities of the Ancients region of east Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

Kasal was found toward the middle of the island on Afyal's western shore with the Crowded Sea. It was approximately from Medina al-Afyal on the opposite side of an unnamed bay.
