The Citadel of Carifar was the seat of power in Nimpeth, one of the Free Cities of the Vilhon Reach.

Location

The Citadel of Carifar stood at the center of Nimpeth.

History

The palace was constructed circa 970 DR via cruel slave labor. The Citadel was considered to be the second-most impressive product of unwilling labor in the city, after the Woren Vineyards, built in the Broken Hills outside the city walls.

Around 1370 DR, the Citadel of Carifar belonged to the Lord of Nimpeth, Woren, who also owned the the Woren Vineyards.
