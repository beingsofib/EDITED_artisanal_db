Ventris was an island within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Ventris was found near the center of the Korinn Archipelago. It was the largest single island among the group, covering approximately . It was mountainous, and thick pine forests covered much of the island, especially along both the eastern and western coasts. Several open plains were well-suited to agriculture.

Trade

The isle was the wealthiest among the Korinn Archipelago, and commanded a large share of the islands' maritime trade as of the early 14 century DR.

Inhabitants

The island was home to Northlander humans, including a number of quite wealthy families. It also hosted sizable populations of dwarves and halflings lived on the island, alongside a few elves from the Faerûnian mainland. Several prosperous farming communities had been founded on the isle, primarily on cleared land within the forests.
