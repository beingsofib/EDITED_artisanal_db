The Settler Camp outside Leilon was a temporary encampment during the town's reconstruction in the late 15 century DR. It was managed by Mazira Shae.

Description

The vast settler camp outside of Leilon consisted of circled wagons, tents, cooking fires, and supply containers. The town council met there on a regular basis to plan the rebuilding and address any risks in the area, and the majority of the town's business was conducted there.

Geography

The Settler Camp was situated under the trees along the High Road just south of Leilon.

History

By the early 1490s DR, great strides had been made to rebuild Leilon at the behest of Dagult Neverember, Lord Protector of Neverwinter. During the town's reconstruction, the Settler Camp was the settler's temoprary home.
