Robin's Way

Robin's Way was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Robin's Way was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward. The road branched southeast from the Rising Ride and ended on Caravan Street a few blocks away. Juth Alley and Tezambril's Street also connected with Robin's Way.
