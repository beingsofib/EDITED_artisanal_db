River Dolsel

The River Dolsel was a river in the Guthland on the Chultan Peninsula.

Geography

The river started in the Dolsel Gap and drained into the Thingulph.

Trade

The river was the heart of trade in the region. Merchants and fishermen used to transport fruit along the smooth river to the markets of Delselar.

Inhabitants

Snakes, living and undead, slithered from the dark regions of the Black Jungles to hunt for prey, under the guidence of the yuan-ti.
