T'o na'Chee was a town in the kingdom of Koryo in Kara-Tur, lying north of the Vorkani Mountains. The mountains separated T'o Na'chee from the rest of Koryo.

Around 1357 DR, the town was the base of the powerful yakuza clan known as the Yio Dufong, or "the bears".
