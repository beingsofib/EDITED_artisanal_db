Barth was an islet among the tiny archipelago known as "the Teeth" in the middle of the Trackless Sea.

Description

Barth was a crag that rose to the highest elevation among "the Teeth".

History

As of the mid-to-late 14 century DR, the white dragon Arveiaturace was known to alight and rest atop Barth as she hunted across the open seas.
