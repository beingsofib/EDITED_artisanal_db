Evermelt

The Evermelt was a subterranean hot spring found within a cave at the base of the Reghed Glacier. It was considered a holy place by the Reghedmen barbarians of nearby Icewind Dale.

Geographical Features

The warm, tranquil waters of Evermelt appeared as a serene turquoise color that glittered like gold.

Despite its mostly still waters, Evermelt had a small undercurrent that could pull individuals beneath the surface.

Inhabitants

As of the , the spring was guarded by members of the Tribe of the Bear, at the behest of the Ice Witch Hedrun Arnsfirth.
