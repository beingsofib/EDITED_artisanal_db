Shield Street

Shield Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Shield Street was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward and formed part of the western border of the North Ward. The road branched south from Skulls Street and continued due south to eventually end on Trader's Way at the border with the Castle Ward. Other streets intersecting Shield Street included Immar Street, Chasso's Trot, Vondil Street, Delzorin Street, Rough Road, Sulmor Street, Hassantyr's Street, Wrightstone Street, Julthoon Street, and Manycats Alley.
