The Spinner's Prison was the cell and lair of the Eberronian Overlord known as the Spinner of Shadows, a grossly powerful demon from ages past. Overseen by the church of the Silver Flame, it was guarded by a small part of the Flame itself, the Silver Flame Guardian.

Location

The prison was located below the city of Stormreach within the underground domain of Khyber in Eberron, not far from Tarath Marad. It could not normally be accessed from the surface until the Lords of Dust blasted a passageway that opened the road to Tarath Marad.

Structure

The underground approach to the prison was blocked by numerous magical wards that blocked fiends and other fell creatures, but mortals were able to bypass them much more easily. Nearby, the home of Brother Aurelius was used to keep an eye on the prison and maintain the wards in person.

The prison itself was a singular large chamber ringed with braziers filled with the essence of the Silver Flame. This kept the Spinner trapped in the center in a great pillar of flame, unable to act.

Two sections of wall were incomplete and connected onto rough tunnels and caverns, some filled with magma, which allowed the native demons of that realm to enter when called.

Following the departure of the Spinner, an entire wall of the prison was destroyed and pulled into the Demonweb Pits, becoming a massive and unstable Rift Between Worlds that threatened to consume the whole of Eberron.

History

The prison was formed in ages past when the demon Overlords of Eberron were defeated. While the Silver Flame Guardian held the Spinner tight, the prison was further guarded and maintained by a chosen member of the faith who swore an oath of devotion to the cause. This person was then teleported to the prison to spend many years underground maintaining the wards and communing with the physical manifestation of their god.

Around the time of the Darkening and the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, the Lords of Dust rakshasa Gnomon used his infiltration of the Silver Flame church to locate the Spinner's Prison, and put together a plan to break in and free the trapped Overlord. Using the local cultists in Stormreach, they put together a great ritual to blast open the road to Tarath Marad and gain access to the sealed prison. The investigators of the Silver Flame discovered the conspiracy too late and the cult managed to work their way through the wards, using the Spinner's flame eater spiders to devour and weaken the Silver Flame Guardian. The agents of the Silver Flame arrived shortly after and engaged in battle with the partially-free Spinner, with only the efforts of the Silver Flame Guardian keeping the demonic lord from simply destroying them. Even with the Spinner calling forth wild demons from Khyber the wards were relit, trapping her once more, only for Lolth herself to take notice and reach into Eberron from the Demonweb Pits. She broke open the Rift Between Worlds and beckoned the Spinner through, taking the amassed dragonshards with her and leaving Eberron behind.

The forces of the Silver Flame, the Gatekeepers, and the Twelve arrived at the location afterwards. They were relieved to find that the Spinner had not stayed to conquer Eberron, but were aghast at the enormous portal. Tavnar Kronnak understood that holding their ground would just lead to the Demonweb devouring Eberron, and so assembled a team of heroes to plunge into the Demonweb first and find a solution. These brave sorts ventured into the cursed domain of Lolth, luckily meeting Elminster and finding a path to the Underdark below Eveningstar, where they helped undo Lolth's schemes and saved Eberron in the process.
