Kerrigan's Court

Kerrigan's Court was a courtyard located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

Kerrigan's Court was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter. The court connected with Slop Street along the southern edge of the Adventurers' Quarter.
