The Baliaka Hall was a guildhall located in the city of Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The hall was located in the southern section of Almorel near the sprawling residence of Mechnik Nikitsky.

Activities

The Baliaka Hall served as the guildhall for bards and other entertainers within Almorel.
