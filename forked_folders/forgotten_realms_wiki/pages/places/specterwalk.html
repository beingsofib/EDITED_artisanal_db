Specterwalk

The Specterwalk was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

The Specterwalk was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward. The road ran east to west, originating in the west on the High Road and cutting east to end on Wall Street near the outer city wall. Other roads connecting with the Specterwalk included Caravan Street, Carter's Way, and several smaller unnamed alleys.
