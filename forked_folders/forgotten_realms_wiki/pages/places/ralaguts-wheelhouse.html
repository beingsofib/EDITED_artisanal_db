Ralagut's Wheelhouse

Building
nameRalagut's Wheelhouse
typeGeneral store
locationSnail Street, Dock Ward, Waterdeep

Ralagut's Wheelhouse was a general store in the Dock Ward of the city of Waterdeep in the 15 century DR.

Location

This shop fronted the western side of Snail Street and was located a block south of Shesstra's Street.
