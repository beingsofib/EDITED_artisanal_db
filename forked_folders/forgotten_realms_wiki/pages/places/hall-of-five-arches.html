The Hall of Five Arches, also referred to as the Tall Hall to its enormous size, was the guildhall of the Builders' Guild in the city of Ravens Bluff.

Location

This building was located along Moorland Ride, in the Sixstar neighborhood of the city's Uptown District.

Interior
