River Tamthew

The River Tamthew, also called the Lluirflow, was a small river found in southern Luiren in the Shining South.

Geography

The river originated near the center of the southern section of the Southern Lluirwood. It flowed almost due south for approximately before reaching the Great Sea near Luiren's far southwest corner.
