Yarlith was a kingdom in the Sword Coast North between the 2 and the 6 centuries DR.

Geography

The area where Yarlith was located later became known as the Mere of Dead Men; it was a little south of the later position of Neverwinter. Mlembryn was later established to its east.

Government

Its first king was the twin of the king of Uthtower.

Trade

It was a land renowned for its shepherds and woodcarvers.

Defenses

The people of that land were known to be tireless at orc-slaying, by necessity.

History

The area was settled around by the migrating Illuskans. Around the turn of the 3 century DR, settlers from Yarlith established a number of holds along the Dessarin, forming what would be Mlembryn. In the , they were also part of the recolonization of Illusk.

In the , the armies of Phalorm were the defenders of that city while the orcs under the frost giant Hrothgar attacked it. The city fell later, in the , to the orcs of the Everhorde from the Spine of the World.
