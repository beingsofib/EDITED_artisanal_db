Riz Malag was a destroyed settlement in the Underdark beneath Cormyr, somewhere near the drow city of Sschindylryn.

History

Long ago, Riz Malag was destroyed by forces from Sschindylryn, who attacked by opening a portal into the middle of the settlement. By the 15 century DR, Riz Malag was a crumbling ruin inhabited only by forces from Sschindylryn, with the old portal still active and offering a direct route into the drow city. Mind flayer allies of the drow made up part of the defenders, along with the magister Durnibas Uxyndar who studied water elementals. The hezrou Chorzan also found an opportunity to pass into the Prime Material plane there, appearing on an overlook above the ruins.
