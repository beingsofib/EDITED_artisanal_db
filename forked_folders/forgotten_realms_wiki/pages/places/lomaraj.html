Lomaraj was a human settlement in the Pasocada Basin in Maztica.

Geography

The town was located on the Pasocada River a few miles downriver from the Raven Falls and Great Raven Pueblo, an Azuposi settlement. Lomaraj was a center of trade between the two peoples.

Inhabitants

Lomaraj was the largest Metahel settlement with a population of around one thousand people.
