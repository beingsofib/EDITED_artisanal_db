Kagune was an islet located in Kozakura.

Geography

Located in the Inland Sea, Kagune was found near Eburishima and Tenmei. Kagune was barren, lacking any freshwater or terrestrial plants. The only vegetation found on the islet were aquatic plants that thrived in saltwater.

Inhabitants

Beneath the islet was a system of caverns in which young lung wang made their homes. At a given time, one could expect to find between five to ten lung wang making a home in the caves.
