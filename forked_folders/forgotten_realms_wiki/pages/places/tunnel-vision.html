Tunnel Vision

Tunnel Vision is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the Living City organized play program, Part V of the Destiny of the Dwarves series. It was published in 2001, and intended for adventurers of all levels.

Summary

In 1374 DR, heroes of the Living City were summoned back to the Crimson Lagoon, where the last of the series of expeditions took place. The Dwarven Clan Elders revealed the previous adventures' goal РђЊ to reforge the legendary Hammer of Thanes. The Adventurers descended into the Underdark and were faced with The Test of Bravery, The Test of Belief, The Test of Avarice, The Test of Truth, and The Test of Blood, ancient trials set up by the dwarven gods and dwarven ancestors to determine who would become the new Thane. In the end, the adventurers faced a familiar face who orchestrated the expedition РђЊ Citadel Urn, who attempted to claim the title and the artifact but was slain in the process.
