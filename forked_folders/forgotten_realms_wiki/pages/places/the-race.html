The Race

The Race was a stretch of water located off the southern edge of the Tethyr Peninsula in west Faerûn. The sea was named for the strong winds through it

Usage

In the 14 century DR, the Race and Asavir's Channel were used to reach the wealthy ports of the south, but such shipping was harassed by pirates based in the nearby Nelanther Isles. Once through the Race, merchant ships came under the protection of the Calimshan navy.

History

In the , the Companions of the Hall traveled through the Race after escaping the pirate Pinochet's fleet and landed in Memnon.
