Wall Street (Daggerford)

Wall Street was a road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR..

Location

Wall Street ran along the inside of Daggerford's city walls, fully encircling the entire city.

Notable Locations

Many of Daggerford's roads connected with Wall Street including Duke's Way, the Hill Road, Horse Way, Fields Lane, Tymora Alley, Rogar Alley, Kaoth Alley, and the Farmer's Road. Since Wall Street encircled the perimeter of all of Daggerford, many buildings were located along its length. The most notable of these are listed below:

  • Dragonback Inn
  • Daggerford's barracks
  • The Table of the Sword
  • Farmers' Society
  • The Caravan Gate
