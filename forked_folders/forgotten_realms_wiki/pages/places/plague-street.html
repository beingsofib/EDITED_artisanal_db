Plague Street

Plague Street was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 century DR.

Location

Plague Street was found within northwest Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. It originated on Nool Street in the west and terminated in the east at the wall separating the Common Quarter from the Inner Quarter. It intersected Cobble Road along the way.

Description

It was a small road measuring roughly in length.
