The Woodlands Inn was an inn in Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid–14 century DR.

Location

It stood near one of the gates out of Loudwater.

History

It was a place where the Heroes of Ascore could find refuge when the Zhentarim and Zhentilar commanded by Vaalgamon invaded the streets of Loudwater in the .
