Xaryxispace was a small star system in wildspace that was home to the Xaryxian Empire of astral elves.

Description

The system consisted of the star Xaryxis and three natural satellites, the planets Rivus, Vaerduran, and Xaryndar. Also situated within the system was the Imperial Citadel, constructed by the astral elves of the Xaryxian Empire, who constitute the system's main inhabitants. The system itself served as the core territory and home system of the Xaryxian Empire.

The system has a diameter of 800 million miles across.

The Solar System

Xaryxis

A white-hot fire body that lies at the center of Xaryxispace, and serves as the literal and figure heart of the Xaryxian Empire. The astral elves discovered how to harness the star's power, using it to fuel their magic. After thousands of years of exploitation, Xaryxis's light began to dim, the astral elves began harvesting astral seeds from the star to tap the energy of other planets and use it to replenish the strength of the star.

Vaerduran

An irregular and rocky earth body that once supported life, but its ecosystem was diminished when it became the astral elves' first testing ground for their newly harvested astral seeds.

Rivus

A spherical and rocky earth body, second from Xaryxis, known for its rich platinum deposits, mined by the astral elves. Despite appearing desolate on the surface, the planet bustles with activity underground, where populations of umber hulks, purple worms, and other burrowing creatures make their lairs.

Xaryndar

A spherical, lush, and green earth body teeming with life. The flora of the world grow to extraordinary size thanks to the light of Xaryxis, and many of the planets most formidable are various kinds of intelligent plants. It is implied to be the homeworld of the astral elves. Most of the planet's elvian population left long ago to become closer to their gods on the Astral Plane, but the planets still supports a few settlements that cultivate and harvest the crystal-leaf trees used in the construction of star moths.

Imperial Citadel

The floating citadel, constructed by the astral elves, that serves as the administrative heart of the Xaryxian Empire and imperial family. The floating platform the city rests on is a mile-wide and constructed of hewn stone shaped in the form of a moth. The location is home to approximately 10,000 inhabitants, most of them astral elves. The citadel has the ability to travel across the Astral Sea and Wildspace through the addition of twelve spelljamming helms. The citadel siphoned power from the star Xaryxis directly, by means of a beam of light.

Inhabitiants

A number of notable inhabitants originated from Xaryxispace, including:

  • General Caila Eanor, Commader of the Mutineer Underground.
  • Prince Xeleth, heir to the Xaryxian Empire and member of the Imperial Family.
  • Princess Xedalli, banished member of the Imperial Family.
