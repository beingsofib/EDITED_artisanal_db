Bone Brambles

Location
nameBone Brambles
typeForest
regionAvernus, Feywild

The Bone Brambles was a cursed forest located in the first layer of the Nine Hells, Avernus. It was the home of the night hag Red Ruth. The forest was made up of twisted trees and bone-like vines mixed with calcified corpses and fungal pods.
