Purskul was a city in Amn.

Demographics

Circa 1370 DR, Purskul was recorded as having a population of around 12,000 people, comprising 40% humans, 30% halflings, 29% half-orcs, and 1% full-blooded orcs.

The large half-orc population dated back to 1235 DR, when the armies of Amn, while battling with humanoid forces, captured many orcs and forced them to work at labor camps in Purskul. Those who worked the hardest were granted their freedom. Their descendants made up the later orcish population.

Industry

It was a granary city and an important caravan stop. The half-orc inhabitants generally worked as caravan guards and mercenaries.

Locations

There was a temple to Chauntea, but it was left empty after its clerics fell sick and died around 1370 DR.
