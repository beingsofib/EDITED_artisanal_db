Barge Street

Barge Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Barge Street was located in the Zhentil Keep's Harbor District in the south section of the city. The road was located in the northwest corner of the Harbor District. It originated at the east gate to the Common Quarter and passed southwest.

Notable Locations

Oparil's Tower, and dusty inn, was located on Barge Street.
