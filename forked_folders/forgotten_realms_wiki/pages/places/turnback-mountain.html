Turnback Mountain

Turnback Mountain was a single peak located among the Abbey Mountains in the Moonsea North region of Faerûn. Its name originated from the Turnback Mountains a nickname given to its home range.

Geography

Turnback Mountain was the southernmost peak in the Abbey Mountains, and was located in the east the range.

Notable Locations

The House of Gold lay deep beneath the mountain.
