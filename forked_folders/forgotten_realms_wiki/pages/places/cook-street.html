Cook Street

Cook Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Cook Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward. Cook Street originated on Lackpurse Lane at the border between the Castle Ward and Dock Ward, branching north before terminating at a dead end just shy of Soldier's Street. Other roads connecting with Cook Street included Soothsayer's Way and Rainrun Street.
