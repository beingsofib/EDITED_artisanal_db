Cairnpur was a village located in Impiltur in the Northeast Faerûn, off the coast of the Easting Reach.

Geography

Cairnpur was known as one of several overnight stops for caravans that traveled along the Merchant's Run, part of the Herald's Road that stretched between Impiltur and the Cold Lands. Cairnpur was the first caravan stop northwest of Ilmwatch, west of Even's Gulf, the northernmost part of the Easting Reach. Maracrath was a caravan stop village to the northwest of Cairnpur.

Defenses

Cairnpur received protection from Ilmwatch's garrison contingents that traveled with merchant caravans between the Cold Lands and the Easting Reach.
