Surbroar was a city located in the country of Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Geography

Surbroar lay along the north shore of the northern branch of the River of Spears in central Mulhorand in the Great Vale. It sat on the Great South Road due north of Ulzel.

Inhabitants

The precept of Surbroar in the , was a warrior of Isis named Kesia.
