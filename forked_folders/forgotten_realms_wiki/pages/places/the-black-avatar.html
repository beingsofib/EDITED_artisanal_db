The Black Avatar was a tavern with a sinister reputation found in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR before that city's destruction.

Location

The Black Avatar was located in Zhentil Keep's Inner Quarter in the north section of the quarter in between Raven Street and the Street of the Gods.

Activities

Disciples of Bane used the Black Avatar as a place of relaxation. It was rumored that many wicked deals were struck within the Black Avatar.
