The Prince and Pauper was a discreet gambling establishment in the city of Iriaebor. It was owned by a man named Cormik.

Location

The building laid on the Street of Lanterns, in the Old City.

Building

The dark storefront looked like it had been abandoned for years, but inside was a large room.

There were backrooms in the building that were used for more important or private matters that Cormik wanted to attend to.
