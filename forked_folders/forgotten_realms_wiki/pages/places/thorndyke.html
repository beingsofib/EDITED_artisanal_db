Thorndyke was a small town located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles in the mid–14 and late 15 centuries DR.

Geography

Thorndyke was found in eastern Gwynneth north of the kingdom of Corwell. The town sat among the eastern foothills of the Cambro Mountains near the center of the mountain range.
