The Night's Rest Inn was an inn located along the Triboar Trail in the Sword Coast North region during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The Night's Rest Inn was located on the trail, on the way to the town of Phandalin.

History

In the , a band of adventurers visited the Night's Rest Inn and were attacked by yuan-ti followers of Zehir.
