Bountyh was a small village located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The village was located along the High King's Road on the northeastern fringes of the Dernall Forest. Bounty was located between Lehigh to the north and Ogden to the south in the county of Talffolk.
