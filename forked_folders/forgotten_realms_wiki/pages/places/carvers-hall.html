The Carvers' Hall was the guildhouse for a group of artisans in the capital city of Immilmar in Rashemen in the .

Location

The guildhall was located in the center of the city just north of the Huhrong's Citadel.

Description

The Carvers' Hall was a single storey stone building. The interior of the guildhall contained dormitories that housed visiting artists.

Activities

Several workrooms were outfitted with a wide variety of artistic tools and implements, allowing guildmembers to create works of art in comfort.

Inhabitants

The guildmaster as of 1367 DR was Davyrra.
