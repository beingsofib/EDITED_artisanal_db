The Bonaku Boarding House was a boarding house in the T'u Lung city of Balanzia around the .

Location

This boarding house was located in the eastern reaches of Balanzia, down the street from the city's market and the observatory of Minkra the Sage.

History

Around , the boarding house was one of many buildings that were damaged in Balanzia as the city fell at the hands of the Shou Lung invasion of T'u Lung.
