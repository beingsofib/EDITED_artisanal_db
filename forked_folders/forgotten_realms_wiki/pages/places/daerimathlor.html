Daerimathlor was an archipelago nation consisting of the islands surrounding the Race, off the coast of Tethyr.

Government

Daerimathlor was ruled over by the self-styled Mage-King Thaerdimor.

History

In 141 DR, Thaerdimor was defeated in battle by the mercenary Flaerivus Grevauldyn.
