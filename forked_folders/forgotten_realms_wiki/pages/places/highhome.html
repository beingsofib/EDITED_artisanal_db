Highhome was a dwarven fastness

Description

This dwarven stronghold was naturally protected by the surrounding mountains.

History

Highhome was founded by dwarves circa –7000 DR by the settlers of Dwarvenhome.

Some time prior to –201 DR, the Sword of Cymrych Hugh was forged by Highhome dwarves.

Following the return of Sarifal in the , the dwarves of Gwynneth would eventually be driven from their homes by evil fey, notably fomorians and spriggans. By the 15 century DR, there were only a handful of defenders (known as cragwardens) left to protect Highhome. Around the same time, a ki-rin named Daiwon fought off a red dragon and took refuge in Highhome.

Notable Inhabitants

  • Finellen, a shield dwarf hero of the Darkwalker War
