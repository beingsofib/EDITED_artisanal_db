Vahaxtyl was a sahuagin city within the empire of Aleaxtis within the Alamber Sea.

Location

Though its precise location was unknown, Vahaxtyl was believed to be north of the volcanic island called the Ship of the Gods.

Inhabitants

Vahaxtyl was home to an estimated 13,000 sahuagin during the mid–14 century DR. The sahuagin leader during that time was a tyrant named Kromes.
