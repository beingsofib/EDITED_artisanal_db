The Shrine to Chauntea was a small place of worship devoted to Chauntea, the Great Mother, located in the town of Ashabenford in Mistledale.

Location

The shrine sat along the northern side of the Moonsea Ride near the center of Ashabenford.

Inhabitants

The shrine was maintained by a young priestess named Jhanira Barasstan circa the .
