The Eagle's Eyrie was a tavern and inn within Eaglton, a town of the Vast, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Structure

The wooden building was three stories tall, with a large stable.

Interior

Save for the common room, most rooms were really small.

Defenses

The local innkeeper, Beomult Ragner, had the ability to order people locked up, by acting on occasion as a judge.

Services

Rooms with up to four beds were available for between 10 and 20 sp, but one could sleep on the common room floor for 1 sp. Local meads were very cheap.

History

In one springtime of the mid-to-late 14 century DR, a patrol from Ravens Bluff stopped there, leaving an impression on the innkeeper and his stablehand, Rolf Ragner; both remembered them even after a fortnight, if interrogated by adventurers. Rolf even remembered a couple of dwarves who headed towards Stag Falls three or four days prior to questioning, and that they had a mule.

Inhabitants

Besides the owner, Beomult Ragner, the only other known resident staffer was a young boy, Rolf Ragner, who tended to the stables.
