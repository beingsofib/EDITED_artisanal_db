The Palace of the Steelbearers was a headquarters for the mercenaries' guild in the city of Dhaztanar in Semphar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The Palace of the Steelbearers was located within the Shari-Souk District in north-central Dhaztanar. It sat along an arm of the Tarkhold River.
