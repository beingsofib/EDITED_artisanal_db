Shannyth River

The Shannyth River was the only named river found on the isle of Moray within the Moonshae Isles.

Description

It was a placid and winding river that was the source of the great fertility of the Shannyth River Valley. It was a deep blue at its banks, but shimmered silver from afar.

Geography

The Shannyth River originated near the center of the Orcskull Mountains in southern Moray. If flowed north for before reaching the Breasal Marsh. The river formed the western border of the marsh for before joining Lac Dynnegall on its southern shore. The river exited the lake on its north shore, joined an unnamed river from the Trollclaw Range, and continued to flow northeast before reaching the coast of the Sea of Moonshae near Caer Moray.

Notable Locations

  • Horst
  • Dynnegall
  • Caer Moray

Inhabitants

Water sprites were said to play along the banks of the river.
