The Dawn of Any Day was an instrument shop in Berdusk in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR, which also carried other goods that had minor enchantments placed upon them. The shop had a distinctive sigil: the rays of the sun rising over a harp standing atop a lute.

Description

The Dawn of Any Day was a small, dim building with simple wooden doors leading inside, which were only decorated by the store's sigil.

The wares inside were often in used or damaged conditions and were kept safe within floating orbs of glowing air, which could only be consistently opened by the shop's proprietor, Darthleene. Customers who tried to grab the orbs themselves would find them passing through their bodies harmlessly, item and all.

Location

The store was in Berdusk, nestled between the homes of wealthy merchants and the temples to Milil, Oghma, and Helm. It fronted many different streets in the city, including Lute Street, the Minstrelride, Danathar's Street, and Amble Lane. The shop appeared to be open at all times.

Wards placed upon the shop allowed its many entrances to change, appearing as though they led into small private domiciles rather than the shop itself as protection against thieves.

Services

The shop specialized in the sale of musical instruments but also carried a number of items bearing minor enchantments. There was a persistent rumor around Berdusk that these magical items had spells imbued into them that allowed the Harpers to track the items' locations.

Aside from instruments, healing potions, and items with glow-on-command enchantments, the store's stock changed constantly. The rarer items that passed through the store were often found to be more costly, as well as to have all manner of curses, conditions, or spells upon them.
