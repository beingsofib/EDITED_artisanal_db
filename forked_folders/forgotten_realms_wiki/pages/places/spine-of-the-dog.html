Spine of the Dog

The Spine of the Dog was a long, thin stretch of mountains that formed much of the border with West Zakhara and the shores of Suq Bay and the Golden Gulf.

Geography

The Spine of the Dog appeared to break from the Mountains of the Lizard's Tongue near the Sea of Caravans, running east along Al-Tariq Channel and turning south along both quarters of Suq Bay before turning back west toward the High Desert as it entered the territory of the Pearl Cities.

Description

Though relatively thin, the Spine of the Dog ran for over a thousand miles along the eastern perimeter of the sub-continent that was West Zakhara.
