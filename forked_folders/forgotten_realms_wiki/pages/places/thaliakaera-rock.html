Thaliakaera Rock

Thaliakaera Rock was the highest peak found among the Eagle Hills of the elven homeland of Evermeet.

Description

The view afforded to one standing atop Thaliakaera Rock allowed them to not only view all of Evermeet and the surrounding seas, but the island fortress of Sumbrar as well.
