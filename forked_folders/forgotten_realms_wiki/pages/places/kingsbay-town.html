Cantrev Kingsbay was a small port town located on the eastern coast of the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles. It was sometimes called Cantrev Kingfish.

Description

Kingsbay comprised about one hundred cottages clustered around the coast at the base of a steep, rocky bluff. The harbor featured a large crane for unloading fishing boats, as well as a smokehouse for curing the local catch.

The town was a quintessential Ffolk community during its height.

Geography

Kingsbay was found at the end of Corwell Road in the kingdom of Corwell in southeast Gwynneth. The town sat along the coast of the Strait of Alaron on the Kingsbay, for which it was named.

Trade

Kingsbay was one of the two most important ports for maritime trade in all of Corwell, with the other being Caer Corwell on the western coast. Fishing was a major industry, and the whole town smelled of fish. It boasted approximately two dozen fishing boats operating from the harbor. Many of these fishing boats were more similar to Northlander longships than to a typical Ffolk coracle.

History

Kingsbay fell into ruin following the conquest of Gwynneth and expulsion of the humans by the fey of Sarifal.

Notable Locations

As of the mid–14 century DR, notable locations in Kingsbay included:

  • The harbor and smokehouse.
  • The lodge of the local lord.
  • The Silver Salmon, a hospitable inn.

Inhabitants

As of the mid–14 century DR, notable inhabitants of Kingsbay included:

  • Fergus, the Lord of Kingsbay as of the Darkwalker War.
  • Rodger, a fisherman and captain of The Lucky Duckling.
  • Winifred, a barmaid at the Silver Salmon.
