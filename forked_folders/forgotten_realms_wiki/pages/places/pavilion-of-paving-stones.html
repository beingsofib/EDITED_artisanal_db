The Pavilion of Paving Stones was a guildhall located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The guildhall stood three stories tall.

Location

The Pavilion of Paving Stones was located in the western section of Waterdeep's Dock Ward in a neighborhood bordered by Lackpurse Lane to the north, Belnimbra's Street to the south, Eel Street to the east, and Net Street to the west.
