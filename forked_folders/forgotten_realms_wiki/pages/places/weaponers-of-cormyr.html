Weaponers of Cormyr was a simple arms shop in Tilverton in the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Weaponers of Cormyr neighbored a rowdy tavern called The Curse, and stood not far from the Windlord's Rest inn.

History

In the , Weaponers of Cormyr was in business dealing in simple weapons. The shopkeep proudly announced that the store carried the finest steel from Cormyr: axes, swords, bardiches, bec de corbins, guisarmes, fauchards, voulges, lucerne hammers, bo sticks, jo sticks, javelins, and others.
