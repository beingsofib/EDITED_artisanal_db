Norfen Lake

Norfen Lake was the only freshwater body of water located on the island of Norland within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Norfen Lake was located in northern Norland on the northern edge of the Frostfenn. It emptied into the Sea of Moonshae via a small unnamed river. The lake covered approximately .
