Helena Gap was a small mountain pass found in the nation of Narfell.

Geography

Helena Gap was located in southeastern Narfell, separating the Firward Mountains to the east from the edge of the Rawlinswood to the west near Lake Ashane. A trail crossed through Helena Gap connecting N'Jast in the north with Krant in the south.

Notable Locations

Bildoobaris was located in Helena Gap.
