Thraotor

Thraotor was the divine realm of the giant god Memnor.

Description

In the World Tree cosmology, it was said to be a massive thunderhead cloud that roamed across the lands of Jotunheim, spreading violent storms in its wake. There was also said to be a palace of adamantine atop it.
