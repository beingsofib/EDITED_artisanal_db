Serpents' Holding

Location
nameSerpents' Holding

The Serpent's Holding was the grassland located west of the Nagawater in the country of Sespech in the Vilhon Reach.

Inhabitants

A large variety of spitting serpents lived there and made travel dangerous in the mid–14th century DR.
