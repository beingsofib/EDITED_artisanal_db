The Ssan, which was also called the Daughters, was a small archipelago near the Mother-Of-Mists island on the Trackless Sea.

Geography

The Ssan was located just off the coast of the northeast corner of the Mother-Of-Mists. The archipelago was also approximately southwest of Baelrah on Suj's southern coast.

Description

Several small islands composed the archipelago.
