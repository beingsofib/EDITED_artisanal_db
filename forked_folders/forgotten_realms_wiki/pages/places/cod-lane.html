Cod Lane

Cod Lane was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Cod Lane originated on Dock Street on the waterfront within Waterdeep's Dock Ward. It branched northeast toward Ship Street was terminated at a dead end before connecting with the larger avenue.
