The main Westfront holding cell was a jail cell in Castle Obarskyr.

Description

It was clean, dry, and well-lit. There were chains from which people could be dangled.

History

After Storm Silverhand, Marchioness of Immerdusk, Arclath Delcastle, noble heir of House Delcastle, and Amarune Whitewave, a mask dancer, were finally captured by Glathra, they were left in the Westfront holding cell. They were then rescued by a hidden Elminster Aumar, then in the form of a pile of ashes, controlling the War Wizard Welwyn Tracegar and having Mirt procure a carriage to hide the three of them.
