Urmlaspyr Keep was an aging fortress located in the city of Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR.

Location

The Keep sat on the southern end of ridge that rose out of the eastern sections of the city.

Activities

The encrusted battlements of Urmlaspyr Keep were used as a barracks for the Helms and a jail.
