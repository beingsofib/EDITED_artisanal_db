Sahtyra's Lane

Sahtyra's Lane was a road paved in hard-packed dirt and gravel, that stood on the boundary line of the city of Waterdeep's Southern Ward and Trades Ward.

Geography

This road stretched northwest out of Caravan Court, curved westward into a straight lane, and then terminated on the east side of the High Road.
