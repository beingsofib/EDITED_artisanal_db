Nuthkhal's Way

Nuthkhal's Way was a curved street that ran from the Eastway district of Baldur's Gate to its southernmost city gate – the Cliffgate.

Location

Nuthkhal's Way began just north of the Manycoins House, passing to the west from it, curving south, crossing Stormshore Street and eventually reaching the Cliffgate.

Notable Locations

  • Eastway Expeditions on the crossing of Hammaskarl's Ride and Nuthkhal's Way, a shop that specialized in exploration equipment, particularly sold to adventurers en route to the Chultan Peninsula in the late 15 century DR.
  • Manycoins House, the office of the Merchant's League and a temple dedicated to Waukeen.
  • Garmult's House of Mastery, a martial arts and combat skills academy and a social club in the late 15 century DR.
  • Harborside Hospital, in the 14 century DR, the building was known as Scarbradro's Haven.
