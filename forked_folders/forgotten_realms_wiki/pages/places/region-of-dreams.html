Region of Dreams

The Region of Dreams was a plane coterminous with the Prime Material plane, to which mortal beings traveled out-of-body whenever they dreamed. while others considered it an astral dominion.

The Region of Dreams was said to be coterminous with the Demiplane of Nightmares.
