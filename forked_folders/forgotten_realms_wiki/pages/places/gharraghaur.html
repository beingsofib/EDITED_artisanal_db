Gharraghur was a small but wealthy dwarven kingdom located in the Sword Coast North during the Age of the Proud People. It had one of the most abundant and easily accessed ore mines in all of the North.

Gharraghur fell to marauding orcs and became abandoned, until the mines were repurposed by Prince Ereskas of Amn to create Mirabar.

Geography

The borders of the kingdom stretched from the coastline, where the Neverwinter River emptied out into the Sea of Swords, northeast to the Goblintide River and north to the Valley of Khedrun.
