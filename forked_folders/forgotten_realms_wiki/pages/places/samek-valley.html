The Samek Valley was an isolated valley in northern Damara, close to the border of the Great Glacier.

Geography

Flora & Fauna

Much of the landscape in the Samek Valley was made up of pine trees.

Inhabitants

One of the main inhabitants of the valley were a tribal group of gnomes known as the Vani.
