The Hills of Apion was the hilly valley on the south-eastern side of the Akanapeaks of the Vilhon Reach.

Description

The Hills of Apion stretched from the slopes of the Akanapeaks and to the shores of Akanamere. The city-states of Zutria and Sumbria sere situated on the lush green rolling hills.

History

All through the history of the Blade Kingdoms, the glimmer of the summer sun in the Hills of Apion signaled the start of annual "white wars," the bloodless military competitions of strategy that ruled and resolved all internal conflicts of the city-stated of the Blade Kingdoms.
