The Garden District of Zazesspur was the central district of the High City in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The district was at the center of Zazesspur, adjacent to all other districts in the Old City save for the Ships' District.

Government

In the , the local representative in the council was Brother Hikar Bartaen.
