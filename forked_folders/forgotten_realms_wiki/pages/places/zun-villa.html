Zun Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Zun.

Location

The villa was located in the east section of Waterdeep's North Ward. It sat on the eastern end of Tarsar's Street on Saerdoun Street. The villa also touched the Cliffwatch just south of Endcliff Tower.

Description

The walled compound contained two buildings located adjacent to each other. The buildings were two and four storys tall.
