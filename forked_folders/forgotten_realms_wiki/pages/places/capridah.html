Capridah was a private house in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR. It was the home of Khiber el Wisynn, night-watch commander of the Great Bazaar, circa 1370 DR.

It was located on the edge of the city's Emerald Ward, in Gagi Drudach, Axash Sabban. The house reached three stories and had a crypt in the cellar.
