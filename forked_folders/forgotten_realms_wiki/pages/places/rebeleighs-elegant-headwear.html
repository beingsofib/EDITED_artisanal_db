Rebeleigh's Elegant Headwear was a clothing shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The shop stood a single story tall.

Location

Rebeleigh's Elegant Headwear was located in the southeast section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward on the west side of the Street of the Sword just north of Waterdeep Way. The rear of the shop sat on Zeldan's Alley.

Services

The shop sold clothing and specialized in fabulous headwear.
