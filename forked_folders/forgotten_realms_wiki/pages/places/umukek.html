Umukek was the northernmost named island in Faerûn.

Geography

Umukek was located to the northwest of the Ice Peak, and to the west of the Sea of Moving Ice. Thus, the island was located at a similar latitude to the Reghed Glacier.

History

Between circa −1000 and −500 DR, the Illuskans of Ruathym colonized Umukek, and other islands, such as Tuern.

Inhabitants

Though Northmen once colonized the isle, there were no known inhabitants of Umukek by the 14 century DR.
