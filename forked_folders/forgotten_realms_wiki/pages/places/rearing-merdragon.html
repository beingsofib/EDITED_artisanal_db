The Rearing Merdragon was a club in Suzail during the late 15 century DR.

Location

The club was in Suzail.

Interior

There was more than one table at the club's most expensive and overpriced room.

History

In the lead-up to the Council of the Dragon in the late the vampire archmage Manshoon mind-controlled Lord Melder Crownrood while the latter was eating at the club's most expensive and overpriced room.

Services

To Manshoon's knowledge, pets were not allowed. Meals included skewered roast quail.

Reputation

The place numbered among the most overpriced and haughtiest in the city.
