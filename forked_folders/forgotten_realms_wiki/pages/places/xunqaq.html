Xunqaq was the capital city of the beholder realm of Xun'Qoroth.

Notable Locations

A two-way portal leading to Ooltul was located somewhere in Xunqaq. This gateway was used nightly to ferry slaves bearing scrolls, gems, minor artifacts of ancient Netheril and new beholder configurations. Once the Sharn Wall was compromised in the , the portal functioned as an emergency escape for the phaerimms that secretly ruled the realm.

Inhabitants

  • Rilathdool, elder orb puppet ruler of Xun'Qoroth.
