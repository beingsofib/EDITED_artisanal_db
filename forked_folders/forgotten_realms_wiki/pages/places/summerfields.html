Summerfields was a farm belonging to the Klaeverdar clan as of the late mid-14 century DR.

Location

The farm was east of Amnwater.

History

Olbrinhannar Klaeverdar headed one of the Klaeverdar branches from that farm. One of the potential successors to Gueladdeth Klaeverdar in , Meldrethe Klaeverdar, lived there.
