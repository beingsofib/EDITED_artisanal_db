Akarav's Bay

Akarav's Bay was the northernmost bay of Lake Esmel in Amn.

Description

The bay contained Lake Esmel's largest concentration of mineral springs, which kept the waters warm and provided health benefits to those bathing within.

Geography

In addition to many springs, Akarav's Bay was fed by the Splendarrllur.

History

The bay was named after a priest of Waukeen who discovered the springs and founded the first resort to take advantage of the waters,
