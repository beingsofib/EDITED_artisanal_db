Awana was an island in the Celestial Sea, part of the island chain known as the Three Sisters.

Geography

Awana was located north of the Joi Chang Peninsula and the Ausa province of T'u Lung.

Flora & Fauna

Awana was the only place where the spice of the same name, awana, was known to grow.

History

Around the mid–13 century DR, Awana and the other islands that made up the Three Sisters were disputed territory between the nations of Shou Lung and T'u Lung.

In 1357 DR, Tan Wui Locca, member of the royal Tan family of T'u Lung whose jurisdiction included the Three Sisters, had a fleet of war ships anchor off the coast of Awana after being told by an apparition that the Shou were going to attempt to take the island chain.

Inhabitants

The island was said to be inhabited by dangerous monsters and cannibal humans.
