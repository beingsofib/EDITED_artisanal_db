Gondalim's was an inn located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The inn stood three stories tall.

Location

Gondalim's was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward where the Winter Path and Burnt Wagon Way joined just east of the High Road.
