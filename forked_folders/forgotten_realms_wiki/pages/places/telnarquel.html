Telnarquel, also called The Hidden City, was a city in the Underdark that was so well concealed that some drow explorers refused to believe that it actually existed. In truth, it did indeed exist, deep below Yallasch in the Border Kingdoms.

Rumors & Legends

Dove Falconhand once claimed to be from Telnarquel for a cover identity while polymorphed as a drow infiltrating a large group of Vhaeraun-worshipers who were covertly invading Scornubel in 1369 DR.
