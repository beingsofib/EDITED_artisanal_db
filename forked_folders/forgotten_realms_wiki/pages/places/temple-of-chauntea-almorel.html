The Temple of Chauntea was a house of worship dedicated to the Goddess of Agriculture located in the city of Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands.

Location

The Temple was located in the northwest section of Almorel near the shore of the Lake of Mists. It was one of the larger single buildings found in the city.
