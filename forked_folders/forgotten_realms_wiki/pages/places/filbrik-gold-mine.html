Filbrik Gold Mine

Building
nameThe Filbrik Gold Mine
typeGold mine
locationGraubünden, the Whamite Isles

The Filbrik Gold Mine was a gold mine in Graubünden in the Whamite Isles. It was a key part of Graubünden's economy during a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles
