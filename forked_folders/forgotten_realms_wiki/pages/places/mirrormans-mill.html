Mirrorman's Mill was an old millhouse in Shadowdale.

Geography

The mill was located south of the village at the confluence of the Duckrace and the Millpond.

History

In the years leading up to 1375 DR, Lord Mourngrym used the mill to house troops from the Twisted Tower of Ashaba as a service to the community during harvest time.
