The South Great Head was a peninsula located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The South Great Head formed the southern tip of Alaron. It jutted into the Sea of Swords and Strait of Alaron for approximately . Much of the peninsula was covered by the plains of Bregonshire with the Rushdown Wood covering a small section in the north.
