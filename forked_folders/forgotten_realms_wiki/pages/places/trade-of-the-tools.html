Trade of the Tools was a shop in Daggerford that specialized in selling all manner of tools and household items in the late 15 century DR. It was owned by the individualistic man known as Old Ander.

Location

The shop was located in Daggerford's Money Quarter.

Interior

Trade of the Tools was nothing short of an absolute mess.

Services

The massive building was full of all manner of items, from broken and near-useless furniture, to functional and practical hand tools. Old Ander bought all manner of items, reconditioned and repaired them if necessary, and rented or sold them to his customers.
