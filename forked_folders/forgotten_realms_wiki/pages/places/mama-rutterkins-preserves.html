Mama Rutterkin's Preserves was a food store on the lowest level of the underground city of Skullport.

Location

The shop was located next to the Dark Harvest Market and the Thrown Gauntlet. It was directly across from the Sanguine Hall and west of Shradin's Excellent Zombies.

Interior

Atmosphere

The interior of this shop , as well as the street outside of it, smelled strongly of vinegar. This was often a source of complaint from the business's neighbors.

Services

This shop specialized in selling a variety of preserves and pickled goods, with prices ranging from 5 cp to 5 sp.

Inhabitants

The shop was owned and operated by a human woman named Gwylleth Rutterkin.
