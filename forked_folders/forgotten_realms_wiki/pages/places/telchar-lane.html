Telchar Lane

Telchar Lane was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Telchar Lane was located in the northwestern section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. The road cut diagonally in between the Street of Glances and the Street of the Singing Dolphin.
