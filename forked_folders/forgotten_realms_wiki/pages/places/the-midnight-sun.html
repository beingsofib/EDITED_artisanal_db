The Midnight Sun was a tavern located in the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The tavern lay at the end of a smaller western branch of Curtain Alley within a section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward that lay in between the Way of the Dragon and the High Road. The locals called this branch the Tween Run.
