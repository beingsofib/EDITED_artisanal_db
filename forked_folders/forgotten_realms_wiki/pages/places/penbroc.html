Penbroc was a tiny kingdom of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. It participated in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Description

Penbroc had a variety of colorful political figures that vied for control of the nation and the Whamite Isles. Known individuals were the kings Finerb, Tmosic III, Ralph MCVI. Other royalty included the Queen Sweetsia and Prince Ralph MCVII. They were served by the Palace Guard.

Penbroc had a complex structure of nobility - some wealthy, some poor. A third group of nobles were known to be missing, and only revealed their true nature in the heat of conflict. The people of Penbroc, while humble, participated in the wars of the Whamite isles along with the royalty and nobility.

Penbroc shared land borders with Veldergauttland, Bolgor, and Graubünden. The River Gruus seperated Penbroc from Bolgor and Graubunden, while the border with Veldergauttland was a range of mountains. The historic fortress of Gruuscastle stood on the border between Penbroc, Bolgor, and Graubünden, and the three nations often fought over it. This was in contrast to Castle Tmos, which remained firmly within Penbroc's grasp.

Among the nations of the Whamites, Penbroc was unique for its overabundance of kings.
