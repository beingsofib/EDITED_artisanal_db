Stormreach was a city in the world of Eberron, located on the continent of Xen'drik. It was the main port of the continent, and dominated by the political and mercantile interests of the great Houses Jorasco, Phiarlan, Kundarak, and Deneith, while also being home to the devout priests of the Silver Flame, the druidic Gatekeepers, and the arcane sages of the Twelve.

History

Around the time of Faerûn's Darkening in the 1480s DR, the Lords of Dust hidden in the city enacted a scheme to find and free the Spinner of Shadows, a powerful demonic Overlord trapped in Khyber beneath the city. Led by figures such as Gnomon, Karas, and Sabriz Rinzyn Kho, they opened the road to Tarath Marad and broke into the Spinner's Prison, weakening the Silver Flame Guardian enough that the Spinner was able to call out to Lolth. Instead of rampaging into Eberron to free the other Overlords and take over the world, the Spinner escaped through the Rift Between Worlds and delivered a large quantity of dragonshards to Lolth before being voluntarily consumed by the goddess.

The Church of the Silver Flame, the Gatekeepers, and the Twelve all came to address the open portal that led into the Demonweb Pits, leading to them sending a team of heroes through it to find a solution. Those heroes went on to meet Elminster and found their way to Eveningstar in Cormyr, helping to defeat Lolth's plans to conquer Mystra's Weave and saving Eberron in the process.
