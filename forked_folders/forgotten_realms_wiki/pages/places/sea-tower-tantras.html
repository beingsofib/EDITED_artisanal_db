Sea Tower (Tantras)

Building
nameSea Tower
typeFortrress
locationTantras, the Vast

The Sea Tower was a fortress in the city of Tantras in the Vast. It was the base of the Guard of Tantras.

Location

The Sea Tower stood on the city's coast, on the southern side of the opening of the bay. It lay at the end of Whistleturn Way.

Activities

The Sea Tower was the city's main fortress, and held armories and training areas for soldiers.
