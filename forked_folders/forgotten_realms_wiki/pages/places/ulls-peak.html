Ulls Peak

Ulls Peak was a mountain in the Highlands of the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The base of the peak's cliffs sat above two lakes: Ulls at its base and Innes a mere away. The area was a full, hard day's ride from Myrloch and Karador.
