Faenya-Dail was a settlement of the avariel located somewhere within the Realms. It was one of the last remaining homes of the 'winged elves' in existence as of the mid–14 century DR.

Notable Inhabitants

  • Aerie, a resident of Faenya-Dail who was taken into slavery when she descended too far beneath her home while attempting to rescue an innocent child.
