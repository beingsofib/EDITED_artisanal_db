Long Lane

Long Lane was a street, located on the border between Brampton and Eastway districts of Baldur's Gate.

Location

Long Lane was one of the two streets that crossed eachother on the moot called Fox Bottom. The other street being Stormshore Street, as to curved towards the Stormshore Street Dock. Long Lane was located two blocks south of the Manycoins House and one block southeast of Garmult's House of Mastery.
