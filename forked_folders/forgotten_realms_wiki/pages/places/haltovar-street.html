Haltovar Street

Haltovar Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Haltovar Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. It branched south from Darselune Street and Feather Street at a major intersection with the Sutherlane and Zarimtar Street. Haltovar Street terminated on Julthoon Street at the border with the Castle Ward. Gulzindar Street intersected Haltovar Street on its west side near its midpoint.
