Woodside Way

Woodside Way was a small avenue found in the city of Westgate.

Location

Woodside way was located in the south section of the city. It branched from Silverpiece Way and met with Westgate Market Street near the Lord's Water pool. Several prominent castles were found along Woodside Way.

Notable Locations

  • House of Silks
  • Castle Thorsar
  • Castle Athagdal
  • Castle Urdo
  • Castle Guldar
