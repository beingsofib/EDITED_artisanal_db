Mirksha, Mirksha, and Mirksha was an imports shop in the city of Archenbridge.

Services

This business sold metalwork, spices, and silk imported from the nations of Cormyr and Sembia.

Inhabitants

This shop was operated by three brothers, with the oldest, Weygart Mirksha, being the primary owner. The shop also owned a warehouse and trading post in White Ford.
