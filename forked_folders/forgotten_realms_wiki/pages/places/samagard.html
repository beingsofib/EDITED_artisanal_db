Samagard was a large town found in western Thindol on the Chultan Peninsula.

Geography

Samagard was located on the River Gur between the Sanrach Mountains and the coast of the Thingulph.

Activities

The town of Samagard was constructed across the river from a large fortress that guarded the eastern end of Sormarl Pass. The town was the only location within Thindol where Samarachan merchants were permitted to trade. Agents of the yuan-ti had secretly infiltrated the local trade consortiums in order to gather information on Samagard's weaknesses. The yuan-ti also did what they could to heighten tension between Samarach and Thindol.
