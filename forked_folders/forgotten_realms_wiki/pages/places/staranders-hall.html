Starander's Hall was a great hall in Castle Obarskyr.

Location

It was the way to the Room of the Watchful Sentinel, which was as an antechamber to it.

History

Starander's Hall was a reference landmark for Storm Silverhand, one of the Seven Sisters, and her comrade the fugitive wizard Elminster Aumar, when they sought the Dalestride Portal in Castle Obarskyr in .
