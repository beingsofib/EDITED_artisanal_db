Trade Way (Daggerford)

The Trade Way was a road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The Trade Way was found in the southern section of Daggerford, running east to west in between the drill fields and the Hill Road. Both the High Road and Water Street connected to the Trade Way.

Notable Locations

Notable buildings located along the Trade Way included:

  • Korbus's Jewelry and Fine Ornaments
  • The Blacksmiths' Guildhouse
  • Chateau Elite
  • Daggerford's jail
