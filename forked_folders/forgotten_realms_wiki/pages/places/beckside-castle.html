Beckside Castle was a fortification located along the High Road in the kingdom of Cormyr, between Tyrluk and Eveningstar. After being destroyed, it was used as a camping spot for travelers.

Structure

The castle was set on a Starwater tributary, with a stone bridge crossing it to the gated entrance. Within the walls were many chambers, including a wine cellar, library, dining hall, and throne room, and a tall remaining tower that served as a beacon for travelers. By the late 15 century DR much of the castle was collapsed and inaccessible, and lower levels flooded, though ways through it did exist.

History

The castle had lain in ruins since the end of the Goblin War in the .

By 1485 DR, travelers and adventurers used the castle ruins as a sheltered campsite when traveling down the High Road, with Breagar Coppervein setting up a small shop to service them. Shortly after the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, the Weathered Dawn group set up in the ruins, with Tanya Brock and Vicas Oakleaf taking the lead on managing and civilizing the place. Unbeknownst to them, Oriphaun Huntsilver had hidden a scrap of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll in the ruins as he was pursued by the forces of Thultanthar. The Shadovars Keldan Shadeskein and Gaston Wyrst infiltrated the ruins with a team of mercenaries, bringing shadows and umbral creatures with them and corrupting a local water elemental. They were shortly followed by Freda Gambosi's Harper agents, who spoke with the campers and found their way into the secret areas of the ruins by activating a hidden passage in an old fireplace. The two groups fought in the dilapidated halls of the castle, with the Harpers eventually coming out on top and taking the Nether Scroll. They might also have found the old war relics forgotten light and the roadwatch bow there.
