The Platter of Plenty was a restaurant in Marsember, Cormyr, owned by Blentra Whaelbuckler.

Location

The Platter was built at the very center of Dawnturtle Isle in Marsember.

Services

It was a busy, alcohol-free establishment that opened around dusk and remained open until dawn. Notable dishes included Blentra's oysters and wild rice in mushroom soup, and Blentra's cider and vinegar oyster sauce served with oysters, but the restaurant served various roasts and platters and steamed vegetables with assorted sauces.
