Gap of Reth

The Gap of Reth was the only passage across the Akanapeaks that connected the city of Reth to the west and Airspur to the east.

Location

The Gap of Reth was located in the northern portion of Akanapeaks and was part of Chessenta. The mountain pass connected the nation's main territory with its westernmost land at the city-state Reth.

History

At some point before the , the Gap of Reth was the site of a fierce Chessentan battle that left many of its survivors crippled and bitter drunks. Bashar and Morka were among the soldiers who participated in the war and survived the bloodshed.
