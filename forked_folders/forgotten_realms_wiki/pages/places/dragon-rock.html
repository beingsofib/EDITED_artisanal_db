Dragon Rock was a spelljamming port and the most active asteroid in the Tears of Selûne.

Geography

The asteroid had a surface about 10 mi (16 km) wide and was located near the edge of the Tears. It featured hundreds of docking ports.

Government

The entire asteroid was owned by twelve honest and trustworthy traders.

Trade

The surface of Dragon Rock was entirely dedicated to trade. Docking vessels paid a fee of 1 sp per ton and could freely conduct trade across the asteroid. If they did not wish to stay long, traders had a choice of leaving their merchandise in the hands of the asteroid's owners to be sold later on commission.

Notable Locations

  • The Ocean's Wake, a bar and restaurant owned by the rogue neogi Brassons.

Inhabitants

  • Brassons, a former neogi slave trader who later became a follower of Torm. He owned the Ocean's Wake bar and was a good source of information.
