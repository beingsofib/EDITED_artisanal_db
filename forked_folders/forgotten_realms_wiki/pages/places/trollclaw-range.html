Trollclaw Range

The Trollclaw Range was a mountain range located on the island of Moray within the Moonshae Isles.

Description

A rocky wasteland, the tallest peaks of the Trollclaw Range rarely exceeded , but the many sheer cliffs, loose rocks, narrow ravines, and deep lakes made traversing the region very dangerous. The mountains were completely impassable by horses.

Geography

The mountains were located in the northwest corner of Moray. They jutted north into the Trackless Strait and were wedged between the Sea of Moonshae in the east and the Trackless Sea in the west. An river flowed southeast down from the mountain to drain into the Sea of Moonshae.

Inhabitants

The mountains were populated with tribes of orcs and goblins. During the 15 century DR, the region's coast also came to be home to Northlanders.

Notable Locations

  • Dennin's Delve, an ancient dwarven stronghold that sat beneath the mountains.
