The Fishscale Smithy was a blacksmith shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The smithy stood two stories tall.

Location

The Fishscale Smithy was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Dock Ward where Adder Lane met Gut Alley.
