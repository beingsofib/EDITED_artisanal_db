Ertyky Valley was a valley located in the Keryjek Ridge of the Great Glacier.

Geography

Ertyky Valley was a wide valley.

History

In , the final battle of the fifty-year long Keryjek Wars occurred within the valley. The valley was now home to a massive cemetery to honor the fallen. The valley floor was filled with hundreds of equkoku totems.
