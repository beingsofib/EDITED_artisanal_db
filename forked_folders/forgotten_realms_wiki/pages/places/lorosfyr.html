Lorosfyr was a bitterly cold and unexplored chasm located in the lowerdark of the Underdark.

Description

Lorosfyr was eerily quiet and still. Its depths remained unexplored because individuals who spend too much time within the chasm began to hear faint whispers of madness.

Geography

Lorosfyr was a massive chasm located beneath the Tortured Lands, outside the Sharnwall. Lorosfyr ran for from east to west and had an average width of . Lorosfyr was very deep, at some points reaching up to in depth. The chasm was connected to the caves of the Buried Realms.

On the western side of the chasm, a wide and well-made thoroughfare circled Lorosfyr. At intervals, massive staircases punctuated the road, leading down into the depths of the chasm. It was unknown who constructed both the road and the stairs.
