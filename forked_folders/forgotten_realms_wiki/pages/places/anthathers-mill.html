Anthather's Mill was a water mill somewhere in Faerûn.

Location

Anthather's Mill stood over a fork in the road. The land around it was largely farmlands, broken up by large stones.

Structure

The building was made of fieldstone, and had a pool where the stream that turned its wheel was dammed. The pool continued along one of the roads.

Services

Flour was made there.

History

A Chosen of Mystra, Elminster Aumar, asked for directions at Anthather's Mill when looking for the Riven Stone, learning it was just down the road from there.
