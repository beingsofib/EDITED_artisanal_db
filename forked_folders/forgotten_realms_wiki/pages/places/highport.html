Highport was an island within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Highport was located just northeast of the island of Caftenor and lay less than off the coast of the far northern tip of Gnarhelm on Alaron. A large mountain peak dominated much of the southern half of the island while a small forest was found in the northern half.

Trade

As of the early 14 century DR, the island was a rich trading hub that boasted several port cities.

Inhabitants

The isle boasted a large population of elves and half-elves
