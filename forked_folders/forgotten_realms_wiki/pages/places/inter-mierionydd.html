Inter Mierionydd was a tiny nation of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. It participated in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Description

Inter Mierionydd had a variety of colorful political figures that vied for control of the nation and of the Whamite Isles at large. Known individuals were the Archbishop Bucket, the Bishop Ziplocbagration, and the Cardinal Canot. It was also home to the First Order as well as the Monks of the Mount. The people of Inter Mierionydd were also represented by the clans of Nordor and Mier.

Inter Mierionydd shared land borders with Graubünden, Bolgor, and the Cold Desert. The border with the Cold Desert was marked by a mountain range. Inter Mierionydd was also the location of the well-defended Castle on the Mount

Among the nations of the Whamites, Inter Mierionydd was significant for the religious hierarchy that led it.
