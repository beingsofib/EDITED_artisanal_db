Peaceon was one of the twelve moons of Garden in Realmspace.

Description

Peaceon was a tiny planar object long and only wide.

The moon contained a solitary building in perfect condition.

History

At one time, Peaceon served as an outpost for wildspace elves. It is not known why they abandoned it.
