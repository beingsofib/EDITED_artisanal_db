The Harper Lodge was a hidden Harper base in the King's Forest of Cormyr used in the 15 century DR.

Location

The lodge was within the King's Forest, and best accessed by following along Greenweald Lane from the northern edge before going off the road and traveling through the woods for a time.

Description

The lodge was a two-story structure with several outbuildings, a small grain field, and a vineyard. The interior was largely a single chamber that served as a meeting and dining area, with the second floor as a mezzanine around it. A basement level was used as a wine cellar and secure repository.

History

Following the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon in the mid-1480s DR, the Harpers raced to track down the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll that Oriphaun Huntsilver had stolen from the Shadovar and scattered in pieces along the High Road. One such piece was found by Bensen Virandson, who noted that the artifact was radiating an aura of energy that made it extremely easy to track, and so needed to be carefully contained and hidden with a special strongbox found at the Harper Lodge. He was joined by reinforcements sent by Rina Belostos from Eveningstar, who escorted him along Greenweald Lane as they fought through the Netherese wizards and their hired mercenaries, including the elite forces of Dom. The lodge was eventually found by enemy forces thanks to the woodsy wisdom of the druid Rand, letting Dom and Slam beat Bensen there, and causing a short battle in the basement as both sides struggled for the Nether Scroll piece, ending in the Harpers' favor.
