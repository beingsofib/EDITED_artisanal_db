The Doffed Cloak was the sole festhall in Halfhap.

Services

The Doffed Cloak had a dance floor and adjoining bar, as well as many comfortable private rooms. The Captain of Halfhap's Garrison, Perrin Clovenshield, gave strict orders to his Purple Dragons not to overindulge or cause a ruckus, so it was surprisingly free of off-duty soldiers. It was instead mainly attended by locals, traveling merchants, and adventurers.
