Levast River

Levast River was a river in eastern Tethyr that was a tributary of the Idolflow.

Etymology

The river was believed to have been named after a dwarven clansman.

Geography

Levast River fed the shorter Idolflow.

Rumors & Legends

Some dwarves believed that an offshoot of the river broke off and became subterranean, perhaps even entering the ancient realm of Shanatar.
