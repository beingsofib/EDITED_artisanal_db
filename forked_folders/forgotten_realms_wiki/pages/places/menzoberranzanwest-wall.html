West Wall was a district of the drow city of Menzoberranzan.

Geography

West Wall encompassed the area next to the entire western wall of the cavern where Menzoberranzan was located, from Tier Breche in the north to Narbondellyn in the south.

Inhabitants

West Wall was home to many of the older noble families.

Locations

Geographical features

Lolth's Web : A network of webs suspended hundreds of feet off the ground; home to House Melarn.

Westrift : A deep chasm leading to the western wall where it begins to curve east.

Houses

House Do'Urden : Rubble remaining along the southwest part of the cavern wall;

House Duskryn : Located in a narrow compound just north of the Westrift.

House Druu'giir : Located in a square compound southeast of the Westrift.

House Melarn : Located in the network of webs hanging from the ceiling that make up Lolth's Web.

House Symryvvin : Located in a five-sided compound northeast of the tip of the Westrift.

House Teken'duis: A noble house destroyed in the early 14th century DR.

House Vandree : Located in a large walled compound south of the ruins of House Do'Urden.

Other

Elstearn's Escorts : An escort and hireling service.

Fane of Quarvelsharess : A public cathedral dedicated to Lolth, completed in 1475 DR.

Barracks of House Barrison Del'Armgo : Training grounds.

Ruilath's Rental Coaches : Rental business.

Six Black Rings : A tavern.

Tolkar's Copper Door: A massage house.

Warmfires: Rentable mansion.

Xeva's Den : A tavern and gaming hall.

Notable inhabitants

Drizzt Do'Urden : A famous rogue drow who lived on the surface.
