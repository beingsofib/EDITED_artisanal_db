Codfin was a small village located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Codfin sat along the northern end of the Strait of Alaron on the Sea of Moonshae on Alaron's western coast southwest of the Drowned Forest. The Tunnels of Dwarvenhome passed directly beneath the village.
