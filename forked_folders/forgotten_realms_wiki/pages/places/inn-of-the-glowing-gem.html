The Inn of the Glowing Gem was an establishment in Yartar.

Description

The inn was located very close to the western gate of Yartar.

Services

Circa 1358 DR, the humble establishment, owned by a brunette that was adorned with flowers and wore a white dress. She offered places for travellers to rest.

History

Around 1358 DR, a group of adventurers, later known as the Heroes of Ascore, awoke in the morning to find thieves from the Kraken Society had stolen all their possessions.
