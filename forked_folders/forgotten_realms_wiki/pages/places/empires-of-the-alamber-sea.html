Empires of the Alamber Sea

The Empires of the Alamber Sea was a collective term used in the late 14th century and through most of the 15th century DR to describe the empires of High Imaskar and Okoth. Although not technically an empire, people also usually associated the nation of Tymanther with this term as well.
