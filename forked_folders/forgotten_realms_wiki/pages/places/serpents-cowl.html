Serpent's Cowl was a small village upstream of Boareskyr Bridge on the east bank of the Winding Water. It was very close to the Forest of Wyrms.

Geography

Located nearby was the estate of Heartwing owned by Aluena Halacanter.

History

The village was named for a yuan-ti city of Ss'thar'tiss'ssun, and its temple that was once found on this location.

Locations

The village had a ferry crossing.

Defenses

The village itself was warded against dragons to prevent them from digging up the destroyed temple.

The village was also protected by Netherese wards designed to keep out serpentfolk.

The human inhabitants of the village were killed when its Netherese wards failed shortly after the Spellplague of 1385 DR.
