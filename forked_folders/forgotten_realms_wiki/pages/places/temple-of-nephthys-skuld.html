The Temple of Nephthys was a temple dedicated to the worship of Nephthys, the god of commerce within the Mulhorandi pantheon.

Location

The temple was located in the southwest section of the city of Skuld in the Merchant District near the waterfront of the Alamber Sea and north of the River of Shadows. The temple sat just west of Bridge Street.
