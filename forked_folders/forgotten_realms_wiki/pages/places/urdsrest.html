Urdsrest

Urdsrest was the divine realm of Kuraulyek.

Location

In the Great Wheel cosmology, Urdsrest was located in a cavern inside a hill on Oinos in Hades.

In the World Tree cosmology, Kuraulyek's realm was located in the Underdark of the Barrens of Doom and Despair.

Features

The place was described as being gloomy.

Purpose

Urdsrest's purpose was to be Kuraulyek's hiding spot. He never set a foot outside of it because he feared Kurtulmak's revenge for putting the entire kobold race at risk of extinction with his cowardice.
