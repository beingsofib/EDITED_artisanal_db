Whitegates was a white stone manor located in Temple District of Ravens Bluff. The modest home was located on the corner of Dragonscale Street and Moorland Ride, within the district's Swordspoint neighborhood.

During the 1300s DR, Whitegates served as the home of "Oldwrinkles" Minstrelwish, the patriarch of the halfling Minstrelwish noble family.
