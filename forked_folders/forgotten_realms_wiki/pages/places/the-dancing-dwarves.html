The Dancing Dwarves

Building
nameThe Dancing Dwarves
typeStone circle
locationCalimshan, West Faerûn

The Dancing Dwarves was a mysterious stone circle that stood on the edge of a cliff in Calimshan.

Location

The Dancing Dwarves stood, or more precisely, leaned off a cliff just south of the Seminary of St. Ihrvyn and south of the Spider Swamp and to the northwest from the sunken ruins of the marid city of Ajhuutal.

Structure

The Dwarves were a stone circle of four massive blocks of granite. Their sides were carved with images of squat figures with no pattern or writing that could explain the significance of the structure, carvings, or the location of the Dancing Dwarves. The monoliths could not be damaged in any way. For example, if stricken with a hammer and chisel, the person attempting to do the damage received a wound as if someone had stricken them with a hammer and chisel.

History

The Dancing Dwarves were in existence during the days of the Shoon Imperium in Calimshan and possibly predated it. During that time, the Dancing Dwarves were often chiseled to no success.
