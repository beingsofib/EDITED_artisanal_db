Walltower Walk

Walltower Walk was a street of hard-packed dirt and gravel in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Geography

This street stretched from northwest to southeast, curving northwest off of the Wall Way and terminating at the Court of the White Bull.

Notable Locations

Guild Paddock: The guildhall of the Stablemasters' & Farriers' Guild, situated at the corner of where Walltower Walk met the Court of the White Bull.
