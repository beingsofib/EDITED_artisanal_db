Edive was a small fishing village on Lake Weng in northern Amn.

Description

Edive was one of the many small, unimportant, nondescript fishing villages around the edge of Lake Weng. It had a population of fewer than 200 people, and its only notable feature was its mysterious glowing bell tower.

Geography

Edive was located on the northern shore of Lake Weng in the foothills of the Cloud Peaks at an altitude of , between Ideepton and Shatar, and opposite Minsor Vale. It lay close to the Amnurflow as it descended out of the mountains,

Trade

Edive folk performed lake fishing and farmed root vegetables, and sold the surplus to travelers and nearby military sites such as Citadel Amnur and Citadel Rashturl.
