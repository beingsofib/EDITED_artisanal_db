The Redbridle Stables were a stables in the city of Waterdeep that was owned by Rhazbos Redbridle.

Location

These stables were located along Caravan Street and Wall Street, in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

The Redbridle Stables was a two-story-tall building.

Services

Mounts and gear related to them, such as bits, bridles, and saddles, could be purchased here.

Reputation

The Redbridle Stables were considered to be a fairly successful business.
