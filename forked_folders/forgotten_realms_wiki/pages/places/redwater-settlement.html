Redwater was a notable settlement found in the Dragon Coast region of the Eastern Heartlands.

Geography

It was situated on the northern shore of its namesake body of water, Redwater lake.

History

Redwater became a destination for Helmite pilgrims following the mid–14 century DR, after the Guardtower of the God was sacked by followers of the Gods of Fury.

Some time later, the Helm of Helm rose from the rubble of the destroyed temple and presented itself to Rolor, who believed himself to be the new Sword of Helm.

Notable Locations

  • Bloody Antlers, a tavern owned by Arbleth Golhund.
  • Guardtower of the God, the local temple to Helm.
