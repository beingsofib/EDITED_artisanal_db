River Morgur

The River Morgur was the northernmost tributary of the Neverwinter River, located in the Neverwinter Wood.

Description

The river's mouth was located in the northeastern part of the Neverwinter River, south of Morgur's Mound. From there, the River Morgur weaved south, where it merged with the Neverwinter River.
