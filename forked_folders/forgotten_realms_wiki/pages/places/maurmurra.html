Maurmurra was a kingdom in the Vilhon Reach.

Geography

It comprised the lands between the 14 century DR locations of Arranford and Nagarr.

Government

Maurmurra had a king until , at which point, King Uemer Vordryn was slain by Kalgrathur Daycloaks, who used magic to make the courtiers bow to him, and then crowned himself High Lord.

History

In , Maurmurra's king, Uemer Vordryn, was made to abdicate in favor of Kalgrathur Daycloaks with magic; shortly after, the same spell made the king slit his own throat, dying, and the courtiers of the realm bow to Kalgrathur Daycloaks. The latter continued to rule until his death in .

Inhabitants
