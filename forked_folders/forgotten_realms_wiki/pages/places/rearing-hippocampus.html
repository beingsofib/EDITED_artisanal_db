The Rearing Hippocampus was an inn located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The inn stood two storys tall.

Location

The Rearing Hippocampus was located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward on Adder Lane to the northwest of Gut Alley.
