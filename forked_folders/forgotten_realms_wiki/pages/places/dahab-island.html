Dahab Island was found on the Great Sea off the coast of north Zakhara.

Geography

Dahab Island was located approximately off the coast of mainland Zakhara and southwest from the coastal town of Mina. The northwestern half of the island was lightly forested while the southeastern half was mostly covered in sandy dunes. Dahab Island was approximately long and wide.
