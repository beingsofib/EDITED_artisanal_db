The Abbey of Highsong was a temple to Milil located northwest of the town of Nashkel.

History

The temple was built by the Sorlyn priest Velmos Sonder after he acquired Wythyndle's Round Book, circa 1360 DR. It was built in an old country villa that once belonged to the Tharthyn family.

The abbey began to flourish a couple years after its opening, attracting many wealthy young folks from Amn and Baldur's Gate. It was the last known location of the Round Book that disappeared three years after the place's opening, seemingly stolen during Velmos' assassination.
