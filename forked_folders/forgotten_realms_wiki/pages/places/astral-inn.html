Astral Inn

Building
nameAstral Inn
typeInn
locationElchul, Halruaa

The Astral Inn was an inn located in the farming village of Elchul in Halruaa during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Activities

The inn served fine wine and great food.

Defenses

An iron golem served as the inn's bouncer to dissuade any would-be troublemakers.
