The Three Roldons was an exclusive, expensive, and opulent tavern in Eshpurta in early 1370 DR, the Year of the Tankard. The proprietor was a halfling with impeccable taste who provided a quiet place for couples and small groups to gather and partake of good quality fare. The booths had heavy curtains and there were private rooms available for the utmost in discretion. Only those with a name in good social standing in Eshpurta were allowed to enter.
