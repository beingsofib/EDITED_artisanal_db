Phorn's Lane

Phorn's Lane was a small street in Tilverton.

Description

Phorn's Lane was a small dead-end spur in the Old Town of Tilverton that split off the Street of the Sorceress west of the Tilver Stairs. The most notable building on the Lane was the Flagon Held High, a festive and popular tavern that regularly sent crowds of disoriented patrons out into the Lane every night.
