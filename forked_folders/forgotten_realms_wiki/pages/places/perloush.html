The Perloush was a great valley in Turmish in the Vilhon Reach.

Geography

Lying in the western reaches of Turmish, the Perloush cut westward into the great Orsraun Mountains. The Mountains of the Alaoreum rose upward on the northern side of the valley.

At the 'mouth' of the Perloush in the east, the land opened into some of the few plains to be found in Turmish

Flora & Fauna

Wild Turmish ponies once roamed the plains in the mouth of the Perloush, but by the mid–14 century DR they were found only in captive herds bred by farmers.
