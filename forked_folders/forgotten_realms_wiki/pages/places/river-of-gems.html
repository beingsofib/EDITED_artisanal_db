River of Gems

The River of Gems was a clearwater river that flowed the length of the Plain of Standing Stones in Anauroch.

Geography

The River of Gems ran from the southern edge of the High Ice, an area known as the Ice Wall, south through the Plain of Standing Stones to a large sinkhole known as the Throat. The river itself was located at the bottom of a deep, gemstone-studded gorge.

Flora & Fauna

Giant bats were known to lair within caves pocketed throughout the gorge.
