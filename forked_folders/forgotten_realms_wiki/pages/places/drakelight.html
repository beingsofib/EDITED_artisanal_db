The Drakelight was a lighthouse located in Threskel in the Old Empires during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Drakelight stood on the outward tip of Watchers' Cape overlooking the Sea of Fallen Stars in northwest Threskel.

Activities

The lighthouse was lit whenever pirates were spotted entering the Bay of Chessenta. This massive lighthouse stood tall.

Defenses

In order to protect the Drakelight from pirate retaliation, Soorenar, Luthcheq, and Mordulkin each sent a detachment of one hundred soldiers to garrison the lighthouse. The soldiers stationed here were sworn to work together even if their parent cities were at war with each other.
