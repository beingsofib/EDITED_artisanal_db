The Wainwrights' District was one of the six districts of the High city of Zazesspur in Tethyr.

Description

The district was north of the Sulduskoon River and bordered the Garden District.

As suggests, the district was home to wagonmakers.

Government

Exar Civulteq, a cleric of Shar, was the council member who was lord over this district.
