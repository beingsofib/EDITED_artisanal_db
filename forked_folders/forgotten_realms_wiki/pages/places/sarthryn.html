Sarthryn was a city purported to export swords in the mid-14 century DR.

Rumors & Legends

In the , the Knights of Athalantar under Helm Stoneblade passed themselves off as traders in a plaza of Hastarl in preparation for a sneak attack on Athalgard coordinated under Elminster Aumar, prince of Athalantar. One of them pretended to be selling swords from Sarthryn while in the plaza.
