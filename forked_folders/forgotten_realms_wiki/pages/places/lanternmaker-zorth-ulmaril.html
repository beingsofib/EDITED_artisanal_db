Lanternmaker Zorth Ulmaril's shop was located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The building stood two storys tall.

Location

The shop was located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward on Presper Street just south of the end of Bitter Alley.
