The Mystic Cornucopia was the most significant temple of Isis in all of Mulhorand.

Location

This temple was located near the center of the Great Vale, surrounded by a network of lush farms around where the tributaries of Mishtan and Klondor joined to form the River of Spears.

Structure

The Mystic Cornucopia was a sprawling complex. It stood out from the surroundings by its lush gardens, wide-open terraces, towering pillars, and a great deal of pools and streams. The temple also had vast storehouses containing grain and seeds.

Activities

The priestesses of this temple administered most of the great farms in the valley.
