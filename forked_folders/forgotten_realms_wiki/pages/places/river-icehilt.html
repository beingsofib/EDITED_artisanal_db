River Icehilt

The River Icehilt was small river found in northern Impiltur.

Geography

The Icehilt originated from Lake Icemelt in the Earthspur Mountains. It flowed south out of the mountains via the High Pass, and then flowed west to east across the base of the mountains before merging with the Great Imphras River just north of the Bluefang Water.
