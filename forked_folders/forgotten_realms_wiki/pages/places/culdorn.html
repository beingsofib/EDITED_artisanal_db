Culdorn

regionThesk

Culdorn was a small village in Thesk in , along the Golden Way, maybe fifteen miles from Fullpoint. The party led by the Nentyar hunter Elowen passed through there on the way to Two Stars. There was an inn; they also bought horses and a pony there.
