The Magma Gate was one of the entrances into the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire.

Location

The gate was located in the southwest corner of the city.

Description

A lone lighthouse stood on the eastern side of the gate.

Activities

The Magma Gate served as the only point of entry into Iskalat, the City of Brass' main harbor from the Sea of Fire.
