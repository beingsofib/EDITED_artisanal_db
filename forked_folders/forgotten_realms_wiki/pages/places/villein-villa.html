The Villein Villa was a small castle located in the capital city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Villein Villa was located in the eastern section of Skuld in the Crafts District along the outer wall next to a market.

Activities

Anyone with the coin to spend, and the desire to sleep in a castle, could rent the Villein Villa for their needs.
