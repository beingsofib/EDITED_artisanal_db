Shey Chorten was the name given to a section of the Katakoro Plateau where a large collection of burial mounds could be found.

Geography

Shey Chorten was located due west of the Glacier of the Lost King. The Old Caravan Road passed by to the south.

Description

The presence of so many burial mounds created a hilly section on the relatively flat Katakoro Plateau.
