Stormy Bay

Stormy Bay was a broad inlet that formed along the norther coast of the Moonsea lake, in the region that shared its name in northern Faerûn.

Description

Stormy Bay earned its name from the horrendous weather that wracked its coast. The storms disrupted activities along the northern coast of the sea, muddying the trail of the Iron Route.

Geography

It was adjacent the coast of the Moonsea between the cities of Zhentil Keep and Phlan.
