The Wave District of Athkatla housed many of the city's maritime businesses, such as shipwrights, cartographers, and nautical traders. Interestingly enough, it was home to many scribes and scholars. It was located in the northwest portion of the city, along the Sea of Swords and northern bank of the Alandor River. Alamander's Gate led northward out of the city.

Notable locations

  • The Great Griffon: A massive, seven-ton bell that was used to alert captains who sailed into Athkatla during storms.
  • Moonhall: A temple of Selûne
