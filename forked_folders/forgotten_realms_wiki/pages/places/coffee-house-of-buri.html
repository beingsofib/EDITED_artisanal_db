The Coffee House of Buri was a small shop in Bandar al-Sa'adat in 1367 DR.

Location

The establishment was located near Usamah the Carpet Dealer in the Bandar al-Sa'adat marketplace.

Structure

Near the carpet dealer's stand, the structure was buried behind a large line of stacked fruit.

Interior

The interior of the Coffee House of Buri was furnished with carpets and ragged cushions. Customers enjoyed a pleasant variety of sweet and overly spiced coffee.

Inhabitants

Both Alix and Karbuqa were known to frequent the coffee house.
