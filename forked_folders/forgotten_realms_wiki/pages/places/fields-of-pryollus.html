The Fields of Pryollus was a stretch of land used by the people of Threskel, and thus the nation of Unther, as a venue for sporting events and grand athletic competitions.

Geography

The fields were situated at the base of Mount Thulbane in northern Threskel.

Activities

Regular athletic games were held on the field every two years.

History

During the , the ailing King Theris of Mourktar held what came to be known as the King's Tournament to decide who would become the new ruler of the city after his impending death. No clear victor emerged from the games however,
