The Barrowfield was a large, misty glade within Mistledale that had over a dozen mounds dotting its landscape. The origin of the Barrowfield was a mystery, but it was speculated to be the burial grounds for Dalesmen who lived before the signing of the Dales Compact. Local rumors suggested the area was haunted.

The Barrowfield was located about 30 mi (48.2 km) east of Peldan's Helm.
