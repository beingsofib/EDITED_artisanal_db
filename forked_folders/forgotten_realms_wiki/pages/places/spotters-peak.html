Spotter's Peak

Spotter's Peak was a tall mountain in the Storm Horns of Cormyr, the Forest Kingdom.

Geography

Spotter's Peak loomed over the fortress of Castle Crag and Gnoll Pass. The mountain stood directly to the northeast of the castle on the eastern side of Gnoll Pass. The Cliff of Lost Lovers stood on the opposite side of the mountain range to Spotter's Peak's northeast. The closest road to the mountain was the Dragon Trail to the south, stretching from the Gnoll Pass, past the Castle Crag, and ending in the mining village of Hillmarch.
