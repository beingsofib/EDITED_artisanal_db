River Square

River Square was a hard-packed dirt and gravel courtyard in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Geography

This courtyard opened from the east off of Salabar Street and opened from the north on to an unnamed alley.

Notable Locations

Felzoun's Folly: a tavern that stood on the northeastern corner where River Square opened onto Salabar Street.
