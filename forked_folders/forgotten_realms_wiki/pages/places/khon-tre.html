Khon Tre was a village in Laothan in Malatra.

Geography

It lay amidst the northern jungles of Laothan.

Description

In the mid–14 century DR, Khon Tre was an unremarkable village notable only as the adoptive home of the weapon master Loh Thieu. Adventurers and fighters came from all around to receive training from him.
