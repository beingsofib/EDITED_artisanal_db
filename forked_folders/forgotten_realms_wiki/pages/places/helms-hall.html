Helm's Hall was a temple dedicated to Helm, He of the Unsleeping Eyes, located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The temple stood three stories tall.

Location

Helm's Hall was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter. The temple sat on Snake Alley in the southwest section of the Adventurers' Quarter next door and north of Tymora's Blessing.
