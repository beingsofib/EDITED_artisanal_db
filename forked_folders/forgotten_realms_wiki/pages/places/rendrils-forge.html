Rendril's Forge was a metalworking smithy in the city of Procampur in the Vast in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

It was found on Shards Street in the Port District.

Staff

By 1358 DR, the business was owned by Opara Rendril, a master metalsmith who, in particular, crafted high-quality longswords and helms, while her bracelets and belt-daggers were famous around the Inner Sea. Rendril's swords were the most highly desired in Procampur. She was still running the forge through 1367 DR.
