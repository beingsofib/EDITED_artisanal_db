Twilit Land

Location
nameTwilit Land
regionSword Coast North, northwest Faerûn
racesHuman (Northmen)

The Twilit Land was the name given to the coastal area of a stretch of the Sword Coast North, lying between what would be in the 14th century DR Neverwinter and Waterdeep.

This area of land began to be settled around the Year of the Boisterous Orc, 64 DR, by the Northmen.
