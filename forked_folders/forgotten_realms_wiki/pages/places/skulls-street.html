Skulls Street

Skulls Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Skulls Street was located in the north section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. Skulls Street ran east to west, originating on the High Road at the border with the North Ward and ending on Heroes' Walk. Skulls Street was intersected by Copper Street, Shield Street, the Fanebar, and Sul Street.
