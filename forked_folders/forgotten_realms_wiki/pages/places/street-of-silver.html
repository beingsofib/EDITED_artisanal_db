Street of Silver

The Street of Silver was a major road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

The Street of Silver spanned much of Waterdeep's Castle Ward. It ran north to south, running parallel with the Street of Silks to the west and Warrior's Way to the east. The street originated in the north on Bazaar Street where it branched south to end on Waterdeep Way. Other roads connecting with the Street of Silver along its path included Keltarn Street, Selduth Street, Palfrey Lane, and Cage Street.
