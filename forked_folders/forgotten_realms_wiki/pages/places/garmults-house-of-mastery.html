Garmult's House of Mastery

Garmult's House of Mastery was the name of at least two establishments, both found in Baldur's Gate:

  • Garmult's House of Mastery on Longwind Lane, active in the 14 century DR;
  • Garmult's House of Mastery on Nuthkhal's Way, active in the 15 century DR.
