Iskalat was a district within the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR.

Description

Iskalat lay in the southwest corner of the city along the harbor and Sea of Fire. It bordered the Rookery to the north, the Pyraculum to the northeast, and the Foundry to the east. Several main thoroughfares passed through Iskalat, including the Street of Stelae, Street of the Vine, Albrazi Canal, Sahhar Canal, and the Great Canal.
