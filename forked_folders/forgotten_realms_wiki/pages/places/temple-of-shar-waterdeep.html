The Temple of Shar was a temple dedicated to the goddess Shar in the city of Waterdeep,

Location

This temple was located in the North Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This temple was constructed of black stone and its domed roof was dotted with sharp steeples.

Interior

The interior of the temple was dimly lit by torchlight. Its main chamber of this measured by wide, while its domed ceiling was . The main chamber also had stairs leading down to the temple's basement.

Activities

The temple's adherents were known to produce pamphlets for the public that lauded their goddess.

History

Around 1035 DR, the Sharran clergy fell under the sway of a vampire who hid themselves within the temple's basement. Also around this time, the normally irreligious Hultmarks became frequent visitors to the temple, falling under the sway of its vampire.

Eventually, the vampire lord Artor Morlin noticed that vampire activity around the city had increased, This mission eventually lead them to the Hultmark Manor, where Joy Hultmark informed them she suspects the Temple of Shar as being a vampire den. When the adventurers made their way to the temple they found the Hultmarks inside, debating whether or not to remain loyal to their master.
