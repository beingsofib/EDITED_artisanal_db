Soot Alley

Soot Alley was a small alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 century DR.

Location

Soot Alley was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Field Ward. The alley meandered between Nanze Street in the north and the Fieldway in the south, connecting to both roads at several points.
