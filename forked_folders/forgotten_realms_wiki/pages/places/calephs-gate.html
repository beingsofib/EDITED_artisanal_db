Caleph's Gate was a gate that guarded passage into the Pasha's Sabban, the seat of power in the city of Calimport. Though there were some secret entrances into the sabban, as far as the vast majority of Calimpanni knew, it was the only entrance.

Structure

This fortified structure stood two stories tall, or roughly . It had a set of three portcullises built into it, which could be dropped down at any time to deter unwanted entry. And both atop and aside the entryway were a number of for the defenders to shoot through.

As a last line of defense against unwavering resistance, gates had a trapdoor floor. By drawing away the supports below, the hinged floor inside could be opened up by, unveiling a drop into a pit lined with stakes.

Defenses

At any given time there were eight guards stationed at the Caleph's Gate. Four were posted atop it, two outside of it, and two just inside its walls.
