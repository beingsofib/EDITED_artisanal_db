The Plaza of the Ancients was an open courtyard found in Muluk, the City of Kings, in 1367 DR.

Description

This public square featured two extraordinary monuments honoring two of Muluk's greatest historical rulers—Amakim ibn Issad and Aswar al-Mutiq.

Activities

Many pilgrims traveling from Muluk to Huzuz would begin their arduous journey from the Plaza of the Ancients.
