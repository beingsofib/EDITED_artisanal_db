Wyrm Bay

Wyrm Bay was found within the Pirate Isles on the Sea of Fallen Stars.

Geography

This roughly circular bay formed much of the eastern edge of Dragonisle

Description

The waters of Wyrm Bay were quite clear.

Inhabitants

Wyrm Bay was so named because of a large population of territorial dragon turtles that lived beneath the waves. The dragon turtles were fiercely protective and could easily capsize ships if they weren't busy fighting each other. Safe passage through Wyrm Bay was often directly related to the quality of tribute or treasure offered to the dragon turtles.
