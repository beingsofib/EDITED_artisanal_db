The Conaluil range was a strip of land located in central Dambrath.

Geography

Nestled between the Great Swamp of Rethild to the west and the Bay of Dancing Dolphins to the east, the Conaluil range ran north to south.

History

Arkaiun raiders from the Swagdar preyed upon ranchos located in the northern hills of this area.
