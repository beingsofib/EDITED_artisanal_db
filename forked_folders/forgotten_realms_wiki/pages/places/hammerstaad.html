Hammerstaad was the Northlander capital of Norheim, situated on the island of Giantspine in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The city was located on the southern end of the island on a natural bay just west of Northwind Strait that ran through the Norheim Isles.

Government

Hammerstaad was the seat of power for the king of Norheim. The city was protected by the nearby fortress where the king and his family lived, which was a wooden lodge built around an old fortified tower of Ffolk construction.

Trade

Hammerstaad was a major port in the maritime trade of the Northlanders.

History

Hammerstaad was once the site of a small Ffolk kingdom.

In the mid–14 century DR, King Raag Hammerstaad ruled Norheim from Hammerstaad.
