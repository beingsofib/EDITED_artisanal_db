Pirate of Hearts

Pirate of Hearts is a one-round tournament adventure module in the Living City organized play program. It was published in 1997 and is intended for adventurers of up to 8 level in 2-edition . Set in Procampur, it introduces an expansion of the Living City program and is the first in the 11-part Cracked Jewel Saga.
