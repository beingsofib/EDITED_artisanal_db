Ironflow

The Ironflow was a small river located in western Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The Ironflow originated in the southern Hardangheight, flowing south from the mountain range's southwestern slopes. The river widened to form the Hughloch near the coast of the Sea of Moonshae. The Ironflow was approximately long.
