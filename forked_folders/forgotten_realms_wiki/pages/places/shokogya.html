Shokogya was a small Azuposi pueblo found within the Pasocada Basin of northern Maztica.

Geography

Shokogya sat in the far northwestern corner of the Azuposi lands. It sat along the banks of a seasonal tributary of the Danozi River along the base of an unnamed series of hills.
