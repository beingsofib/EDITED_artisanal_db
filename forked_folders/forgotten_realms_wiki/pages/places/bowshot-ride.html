Bowshot Ride

Bowshot Ride was a street in Silverymoon.

Description

Bowshot Ride was a north–south street in Northbank that ran from the Docks to the Old Wall, passing through the former gatehouse of Helmer's Wall, for a total distance of about 350 feet. It intersected the Ghostwalk along the way.
