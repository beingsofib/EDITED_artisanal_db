Hill Road

The Hill Road was a road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The Hill Road originated from Duke's Way near Morninglow Tower near the center of Daggerford. The road passed southeast through the city along the base of the landrise that separated the Ducal Castle from the rest of Daggerford. The Hill road terminated at the southern part of Wall Street at the River Gate. The Trade Way connected to the Hill Road near its midpoint.

Notable Locations

Notable buildings located along the Hill Road included:

  • Farrel's Fine Jewelry and Apparel
  • Chateau Elite
  • Taverners' Confederation
  • Daggerford's jail
  • Mikitan Shipyards
