Caladorn Cassalanter's residence was a row house and the home of Caladorn Cassalanter located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The home stood three stories tall.

Location

Caladorn Cassalanter's residence was located in the west section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward just north of Piergeiron's Palace on the the eastern side of Swords Street.
