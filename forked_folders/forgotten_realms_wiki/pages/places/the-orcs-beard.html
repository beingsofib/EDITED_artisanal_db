The Orc's Beard

Building
nameThe Orc's Beard
typeTavern

The Orc's Beard was a tavern located in the city of Eltabbar in Thay during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The Orc's Beard was located in the northwest section of the city adjacent to the entrance of the Wizard's Gate. A single building separated the Orc's Beard from the River Eltar.
