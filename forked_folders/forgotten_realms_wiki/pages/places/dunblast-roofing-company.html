Dunblast Roofing Company was a carpentry business in the city of Waterdeep that was owned by Elemos Dunblast in the 14 century DR.

Location

This shop was located along the corner of Ironpost Street and Zendulth Street, This lasted until the late 14 century DR, at which point the business moved from Ironpost Street to Coffinmarch.

Structure

This shop's building stood two stories tall.

Services

The Dunblast Roofing Company that offered carpentry and roofing services to customers.
