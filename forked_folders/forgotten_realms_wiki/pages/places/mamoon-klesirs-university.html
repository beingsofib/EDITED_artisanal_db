Mamoon Klesir's University was a school located in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in .

Location

The university was found in Huzuz's Craft District in the northeast corner of the city.

Activities

The school was run and owned by Mamoon Klesir. He only admitted member of Huzuz's nobility into his prestigious school.
