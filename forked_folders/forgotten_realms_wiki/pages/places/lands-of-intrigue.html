The Lands of Intrigue was a collective term used to describe the nations of Amn, Calimshan, Muranndin, Erlkazar, Velen and Tethyr.

An older term for the same territory was the Empires of the Sands, though that term was a misnomer, as out of all the nations that made up the region only Calimshan had any deserts to speak of.
