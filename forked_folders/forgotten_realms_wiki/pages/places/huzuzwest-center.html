The West Center was a ward of Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Activities

Found in the Central District, this ward controlled all activity through the Suq Gate, Harbor Gate, and the Grand Bazaar.

Inhabitants

Many of Huzuz's tax collectors were based in the West Center. Hosan Khalid was in charge of the bureaucrats here.
