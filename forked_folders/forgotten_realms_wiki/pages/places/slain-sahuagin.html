The Slain Sahuagin was a tavern located in the coastal city of Bezantur in Thay during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The Slain Sahuagin was located in the far southwest corner of Bezantur in the warehouse district on the waterfront with the Alamber Sea.
