Cloudcrown Palace was a palace in the elven city of Evereska. It was a place of mighty defensive magics and ancient towers.

Location

It was located in the northwestern area of the city, just a bit ways east of Cloudcrown Hill.

Structure

The palace was one of the finest example of Evereska's "naturist" architecture. Although built of stone, the palace was fashioned to resembled a stand of huge bluetop trees, standing so close together that their boles had grown into one another. Carvings on the walls to give the impression of bark were so accurate that a trained sculptor could not guess it to be stone.

History

In the , Cloudcrown Palace was damaged during the phaerimm attack on Evereska. Despite the damage done, the palace remained standing for well over a century and a half later.
