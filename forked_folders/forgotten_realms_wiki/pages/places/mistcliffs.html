Mistcliffs

The Mistcliffs, also known as the Mistcliff Mountains, was a mountain range that spanned the northwestern coast of Chult.

Inhabitants

As of the , the Mistcliffs were home to one of only four major colonies of aarakocra in all of Faerûn.

By the late 15 century DR, the aarakocra population had grown several large settlements across the coastal Mistcliff. Pteranodons and quetzalcoatluses were also known to make their home within the range's jagged peaks.

Notable Locations

  • Mistcliff: The eponymous cliffside that faced the sea.
