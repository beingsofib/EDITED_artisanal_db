Fullpoint was a small village in Thesk in , placed along a detour from the Golden Way. The farmer Hemish and his adopted daughter Araluen lived there. On the spring of , the town was attacked by blightspawned volodni, only to be saved by the timely intervention of Gunggari Ulmarra and the cleric Marrec, who sought the child of light in Hemish's care. The town's treasury had been spent in seed earlier that year, so they could not be rewarded for their help.
