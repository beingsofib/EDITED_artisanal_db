Isle Broddick was a tiny island nation of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. It participated in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Description

Isle Broddick was led by the Wizard of Broddick as well as the sorcerer Silidon and his apprentice. It was the home of the mysterious Nebelungen, the huge giant Fafnolt, as well as trolls, and shephereds from the western slope of the island. All of them took part in the wars of the Whamite Isles. Broddick had a fleet of its own, the Faire Fleet.

Isle Broddick was a volcano, the crater of which acted as a fortification in times of war. It rose out of the sea and was positioned in between the other Whamite Isles. Broddick was also defended by the simply-named Tower.

Among the nations of the Whamites, Isle Broddick was by far the most magical. One of its treasures was a particular crystal ball.
