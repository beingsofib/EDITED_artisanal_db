Orsabbas's Fine Imports was a shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The shop stood three stories tall.

Location

Orsabbas's Fine Imports was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward on the northeast side of Vellarr's Lane.

Services

In addition to their normal business, Orsabbas's Fine Imports also dealt in stolen property.

Inhabitants

The shop was owned by Ildar Orsabbas in the .
