Ransar's Ride

Ransar's Ride, also known as Sunset Boulevard, was the main thoroughfare in the city of Innarlith on the Lake of Steam.

Description

It was a wide avenue that cut the city in half. It extended from the east gate to the harbor and ran through the second quarter. It was also known as "Sunset Boulevard" because it lined up perfectly with the Midsummer sunset.

Trade

The street was often covered with merchant carts, carriages of the wealthy, and the foot traffic of peasants.
