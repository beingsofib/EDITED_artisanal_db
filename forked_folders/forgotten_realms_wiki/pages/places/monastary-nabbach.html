Monastary Nabbach

Building
nameMonastary Nabbach
typeMonastery
locationAgenais, the Whamite Isles

Monastary Nabbach was a monastery in Agenais in the Whamite Isles. It was the home of the Monks of Nabbach and a key defense of Inter Agenais in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles
