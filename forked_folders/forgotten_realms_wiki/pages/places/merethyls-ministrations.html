Merethyl's Ministrations was the most expensive and luxurious public bath house found in the city of Myth Drannor circa the mid–7 century DR.

Location

The establishment was built in the Kerradunath district of the city, where the Street of Song intersected with Srinshee's Stroll.

Structure

This exquisite structure was built as an adapted tree-dwelling, utilizing the boughs and branches as the form for its open-air ivory bathing pools. It stood three-stories tall, with the first level built some above street level, with a floating disc that carried patrons to a main entrance nearly nearly above that.

Services

Entering into the main structure from above, patrons received an experience that was the epitome of decadence and extravagant pleasure.
