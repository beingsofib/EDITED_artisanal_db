The High Hall, which was also called the High Hall of the Lords' Council, was the meeting place for the Council of Lords who were the ruling government in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The High Hall was located in Zhentil Keep's Inner Quarter on Gold Street and the Dark Road.

Description

A series of connected buildings formed the structure of the High Hall.

Activities

The Lords of Zhentil Keep met here to discuss issues critical to the city, and many minor bureaucrats worked here on more mundane tasks to ensure the smooth operation of daily activities.

Defenses

In addition to a squad of at least 36 Zhentilar soldiers, a pair of stone giant bailiffs guarded the Hall at all times. Security was doubled any time the Lords conducted a meeting.
