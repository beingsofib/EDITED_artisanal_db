Azuth's Mug, formerly known by the name Crow's Nest, was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

Azuth's Mug was located just off Soothsayer's Way, in the southeastern corner of the city's Castle Ward.

Description

This building stood just a single story tall.

History

Some years after moving to Waterdeep in the , tutoring or performing spells for anyone who was willing to pay him, the wizard Mistmyr Iroan befriended the owner of Crow's Nest. When the tavernkeeper passed away, they bequeathed the ownership of their tavern over to Mistmyr. Using his arcane magic and whatever funds he could scrape together, Mistmyr conducted repairs on the tavern and set up some lightning. Eventually, in the , the business would reopen under new name Azuth's Mug. Under the new management, the tavern thrived, attracting far greater crowds after one year of operation than it ever did in its previous incarnation.
