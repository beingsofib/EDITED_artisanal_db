The Temple of the Lady of Mercy was a temple located in Hammergate dedicated to the dwarven goddess Sharindlar. The temple had a tall statue of the goddess carved from a single huge fire opal as well as a ritual cleansing pool. In the , the temple inadvertently spread the Stoneplague by accepting offerings of tainted gold from worshipers.
