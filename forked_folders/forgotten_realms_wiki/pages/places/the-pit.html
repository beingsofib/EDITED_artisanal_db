The Pit was a derelict gambling den found in Teshwave around 1367 DR.

Activities

The Pit also served as a tavern and festhall for the occupants of Teshwave.

Inhabitants

The establishment was run by off-duty soldiers and was a coveted assignment given the lascivious nature of the building. Zhentilar, mercenaries, and nonhuman troops visited the Pit for entertainment.
