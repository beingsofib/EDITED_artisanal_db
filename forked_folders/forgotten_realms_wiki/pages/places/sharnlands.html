The Sharnlands was a series of caves located in Lowerdark.

Description

The Sharnlands was a large complex of interconnected caverns and was home to the largest population of sharn in Faerûn. However, it was still a rare thing to encounter a sharn within the caverns. The caverns were also home to several species of dangerous predators.

Geography

The Sharnlands were found about beneath the Delimbiyr Vale and stretched throughout the Northdark. Several caverns were comprised purely of crystal and were often home to an earth node.
