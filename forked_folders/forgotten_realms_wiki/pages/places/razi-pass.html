Razi Pass

Location
nameRazi Pass
typePass
regionWu Pi Te Shao Mountains, Tabot

The Razi Pass was a pass through the Wu Pi Te Shao Mountains at southern border of Tabot. It led to the Kera Valley and Salju Mountains, and ran near a shrine and the Gua Caves. The village of Dalam stood at the southern end.
