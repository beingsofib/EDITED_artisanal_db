Cantrev Koart was a small town located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Koart was located near the heart of the kingdom of Corwell along the Corwell Road just a day's ride east of Caer Corwell. The town sat in the shadow of the Highlands to the north and the Llyrath Forest to the south.

Government

The Lord Koart was a member of the council held by the King of Corwell in Caer Corwell, alongside the Lords Dynnatt and Nowll.

History

During the Darkwalker War, forces from Koart aided in the defense of Caer Corwell during the Battle of Caer Corwell, but fled in the middle of the fighting.

Koart fell into ruin following the conquest of Gwynneth and expulsion of the humans by the fey of Sarifal at the turn of the 15 century DR, and remained in ruins as of the late 15 century DR.

Notable Locations

  • A cozy inn along the Corwell Road where patrons told stories about the legendary Castle of Skulls.
