The Frozen Sea was one of the regions of Anauroch, the great desert of Faerûn, located on its western edge.

Description

The Frozen Sea was the part of Anauroch lying west of the High Ice. Here, where that massive glacier ended gave rise instead to the hard ground of frozen sand. The place was a maze of natural sand sculptures, shaped by the wind and frozen by the cold. The area was void of any water or ice.

Flora & Fauna

In springtime, life briefly appeared in the Frozen Sea, as low-growing ground plants briefly grew. During this time many varieties of birds would fly here to breed. Outside this narrow time, little life could be found here.

Notable Locations

Anarath : The ruins of this ancient Isstosseffifil city located near the High Ice were occupied by humans of Netheril, and later became the lair of the blue dragon Iymrith

Ascore : The ruins of this dwarven port still contained ships of stone.

Hlaungadath : This was one of the fallen cities of ancient Netheril.

Oreme : This was once a Netherese port on the edge of the Narrow Sea.

Spellgard : A grand castle located on a rocky ridge that was the abode of Lady Saharel, High Mage of Netheril.

Notable Inhabitants

Jolarr : The Bedine sheik and his tribe of 300 kept lamias confined to the area around Hlaungadath.

History

In , the Isstosseffifil empire's wizards rerouted the Narrow Sea in an effort to drown the phaerimm inhabiting Phaerlin. This resulted in a massive ecological change that devastated their empire, and shifted the position of the waters. The basin that remained behind eventually became the Frozen Sea.

During the golden age of Netheril the area not still part of the Narrow Sea was a grassy plain known as the Rengarth Ancestral Lands for the Rengarth that lived in and patrolled the area..

In , the phaerimm started casting spells in the land of Netheril that eventually caused the creation of Anauroch. By Karsus's Folly the area was already turning to sand.
