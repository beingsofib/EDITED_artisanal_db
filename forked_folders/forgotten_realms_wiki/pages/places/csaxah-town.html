C'Saxah () was one of the nine towns of the sahuagin kingdom of Kurrimal and its predecessor Aleaxtis.

Geography

This town was located within the Alamber Sea, in the Pythan Trench.

Government

Like all nine of the towns of Kurrimal, this town was governed by a prince of the same name, C'Saxah, who was the son of the last king of Aleaxtis.

History

C'Saxah was found in the , though under a different name. this town's name was changed to that of the prince put in charge of governing it.
