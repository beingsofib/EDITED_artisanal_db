Shovel of Sparkling Stones

Building
nameShovel of Sparkling Stones
typeInn
locationHillmarch, Cormyr

The Shovel of Sparkling Stones was the sole inn of Hillmarch. It was built out of a converted smelter, and was quite undesirable as far as inns went.
