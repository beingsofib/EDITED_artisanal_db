The Old King's Favorite was a resplendent and glittering lounge that stood on the grand Promenade of the Cormyrean capital city of Suzail in 15 century DR.

Location

The club stood on the Promenade, just west of Castle Obarskyr. The Court Close hooked around the Old King's Favorite's city block, starting to the west and crossing the Promenade to the east.

Services

The Old King's Favorite was exuberantly expensive even for the nobility of Suzail. It was renowned for being the most magnificent establishment on the Promenade. The price of the lounge meant status–the place to be seen at. The Favorite also offered exquisite food and only the best wines.

History

As of the late 15 century DR, Marlin Stormserpent, a scion of the Stormserpent family, considered Old King's Favorite one of his favorite places in the city for socializing and finest dining. Lord Stormserpent tried to partake in highsunfeast at the Old King's Favorite on the daily.
