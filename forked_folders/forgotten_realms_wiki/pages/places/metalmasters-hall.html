The Metalmasters' Hall was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Most Careful Order of Skilled Smiths & Metalforgers.

Location

The Metalmasters' Hall was located along the High Road,

Structure

This guildhall stood three-stories tall. It was built of granite in a blockhouse style and one entire end of the building was dominated by a smoky forge.

Services

A variety of tools and kits could be purchased here.

Reputation

The Metalmasters' Hall was among some of the more notable and recognizable guildhalls in the Southern Ward. Like other guildhalls in the South Ward, it was considered by locals to be a landmark.
