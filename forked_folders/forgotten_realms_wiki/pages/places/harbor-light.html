Harbor Light

Building
nameHarbor Light
typeLighthouse
architectureStone
locationVeltalar, Aglarond

The Harbor Light was a grey stone-built lighthouse in Velprintalar (as of 1368 DR). Its light was produced by a magical fire that was maintained by Vuraz, a wizard.
