The Halls of Fortune was a temple to Tymora in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR.

It was located on the eastern side of Farix Drudach, Otahl Sabban, in the city's Emerald Ward. It comprised two five-story buildings of high-quality.
