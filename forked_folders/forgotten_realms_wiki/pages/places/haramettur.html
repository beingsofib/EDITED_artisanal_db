Haramettur was a settlement somewhere in Faerûn.

Description

The town had a temple to Mystra, known as Mystra's Mantle.

History

The sage Tabarast had been to Mystra's Mantle in Haramettur sometime in the , where he learned that the One Who Walks, a Chosen of Mystra, was to be left alone and his identity left unacknowledged by the faithful of Mystra per her divine orders.
