Tethyr Hardware was a hardware and general goods store located in the village of Eveningstar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The hardware store was located along the High Road as it entered Eveningstar from the west.

Inhabitants

Tethyr Hardware was owned by Arbold Tethyr and run by his three daughters.
