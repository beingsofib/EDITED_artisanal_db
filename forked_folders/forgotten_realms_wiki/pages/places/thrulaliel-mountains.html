Thrulaliel Mountains

The Thrulaliel Mountains were sat within Luiren and Estagund in the Shining Lands.

Geography

The Thrulaliel Mountains sat at the far eastern edge of the Toadsquat Mountains in Luiren, overlapping the border with Estagund. A portion of the mountain range also sat within the mighty grasslands of the Eastern Shaar. The mountains were the source of the Liontongue River
