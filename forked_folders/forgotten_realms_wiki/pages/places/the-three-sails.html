The Three Sails

nameThe Three Sails

The Three Sails was a tavern located in the city of Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR.

Activities

The Three Sails was open round the clock and catered to sailors who often usually only had enough coin for drinks. The sailors often slept the night away wherever they were tossed into the alley when their coin ran dry.
