Munhani Canal

The Munhani Canal was a prominent canal located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR.

Location

Though it formed the entire southern border of the Pyraculum, the canal originated in the center of the Rookery, where it branched east from the Sahhar Canal. It terminated on the southern side of the Naranj Canal on the northern border of the Foundry. Along its path, the canal crossed beneath a section of the Street of Stelae and was intersected by the Great Canal.
