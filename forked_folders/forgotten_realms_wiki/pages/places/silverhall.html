Silverhall was the traditional home of the Silver family of half-elves in Velprintalar (now known as Old Velprintalar), Aglarond.

Description

Silverhall was a low airy building and one of the oldest in the city. It featured towers and large lawned grounds that contained numerous guest houses and gazebos.
