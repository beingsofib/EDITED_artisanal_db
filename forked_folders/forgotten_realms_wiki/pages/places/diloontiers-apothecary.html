Diloontier's Apothecary was located in the heart of the Castle Ward of Waterdeep around 1364 DR.

Staff

The shop was owned and run by Jerome Diloontier.

Goods & Services

This store specialized in herbal and magical balms, potions, and perfumes.

Patronage

This upscale store catered to the elite of Waterdhavian society. Those who had the right credentials and money for it could quietly purchase poisons and more nefarious potions from the proprietor.
