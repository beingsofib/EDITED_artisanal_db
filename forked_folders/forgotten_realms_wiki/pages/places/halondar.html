Halondar

The Halondar, originally called the Turmway, was a road in the Vilhon Reach that stretched from Alaghôn to Hlondeth.

Geography

The Halondar ran through the Halondar Valley between the Orsraun Mountains and the Aphrunn Mountains before nearly bisecting Turmish. Xorhun was a major stopping point at the end of a trail a few miles off the road.

History

The road was originally named the Turmway. In 37 DR, the foundation of Hlondeth marked its westernmost end.
