Jhalhoran was a city located in the country of Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Geography

Jhalhoran was located on the north bank of the River of Shadows in central Mulhorand. It sat on the Great South Road in between Surbroar to the south and Maerlar to the north.

Inhabitants

The precept of Jhalhoran in the , was a priest of Nephthys named Ulara. Nephita, the incarnation of Nephthys, lived here at this time as well.

Activities

Jhalhoran was a major center of worship for Osiris.
