Sorkryn's Soft Hides of Scornubel was a trader and dealer in hides and animals in Scornubel in the 15 century DR.

Services

The business sourced hides and living beasts from eastern Amn and Tethyr. It was linked by merchants' map to Samril's Fine Blades in Secomber, who used the cured hides for the hilt wrappings for weapons such longswords.
