Fortress Eyegad was a re-claimed fortress, located in the Spine of the World, that was inhabited by the Skortchclaw tribe during the 14 century DR.

Activities

The Skortchclaw orcs used a group of enslaved goblins to mine mithral beneath its structure.

History

It was originally constructed and occupied by dwarves, but by the it had been taken by the orcs.
