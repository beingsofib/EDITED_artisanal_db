Wyrm's Hamlet was a settlement located in the south of the Horse Prairie on the Dragon Coast in the late 14 century DR.

Geography

Wyrm's Hamlet laid on the southern end of the Snake Road that connected the hamlet with the city of Elversult to the northwest. The town was built on the northern shores of the Lake of the Snake, east of the Giant's Run Mountains. Just to the east from the town, downstream along the River Reddan that flowed out of the Lake of the Snake, stood the closest notable settlement to Wyrm's Hamlet, known as Smuggler's Bank.
