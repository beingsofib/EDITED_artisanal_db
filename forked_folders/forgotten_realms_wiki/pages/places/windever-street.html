Windever Street

Windever Street was a short street in Suzail, the capital of Cormyr, that stretched from the Horngate on the the Promenade to Mayhap Lane.

Description

Windever Street was the western edge of the triangular city block that The Six Candles inn on its southeastern corner and the Wailing Wheel inn on the northern end where Windever Street met the Promenade. Many shop and residential entranced for buildings on the block faced Windever Street.
