Rathgaunt Hills

The Rathgaunt Hills was a rocky outcropping located along the southern edge of the Shaar.

Geography

The Rathgaunt Hills were located just east of Lake Lhespen and south of the River Shaar. The Trader's Way passed through the hills near their midpoint near the town of Sebben. The hills ran west to east for approximately before levelling out near the Landrise.

History

The mage Phelpar had a hidden cache of magic items stored in a cave within the Rathgaunt Hills.
