Spider hatchery

nameSpider Hatchery

regionDeep Wastes, Underdark

The spider hatchery was a spider-filled cavern that served as a nursery for the drow city of Sschindylryn.

Description

The cave was filled with webbing, cocoons, and immature spiders. The hatchery was set up by the Church of Lolth from Sschindylryn to breed spiders en masse for the service of Lolth, making the whole area a kind of religious site. The area was directly defended and supervised by the priestesses and their servants.

Geography

The cavern was located not too far from the Great Waterfall and Tranquility Grove.
