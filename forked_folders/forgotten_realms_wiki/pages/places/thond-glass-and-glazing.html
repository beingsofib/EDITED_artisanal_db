Thond Glass and Glazing was a glasswares shop in the city of Waterdeep, owned by Jhalassan Thond in the 14 century DR.

Location

This shop was located along Sleepers' Walk,

Structure

This shop stood two stories tall.

Services

This shop sold various glassware goods.
