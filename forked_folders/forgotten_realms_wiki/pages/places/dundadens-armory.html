Dundaden's Armory was a renowned smithy and armor shop in Eileanar until its destruction in .

Location

The Armory lay on the eastern edge of the city, between the Netheril Mint and Constabulary Branch Three.

Services

Dundaden was reputed to be the finest armorer in Karsus Enclave and potentially all of Netheril. She and her employees Macchi and Lucio worked together to make exceptional armor, working from raw metal to finished products.
