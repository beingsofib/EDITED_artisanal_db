Jesters' Clubhouse was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Jesters' Guild.

Location

Jesters' Clubhouse was located at the corner of Pressbow Lane and Shrimp Alley, in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This guildhall stood only a single story tall.

History

In 1357 DR, the group that would later be known as the Jester's Guild robbed the corpse of a jester from the past century, Myyrth, from the Anteos family crypt in the City of the Dead and brought it to the clubhouse as part of a plot planted in their minds by the Musicians' Guild. A plot that their then-guildmaster Bazzle referred to as the greatest "practical joke" the city had ever seen.

On that same night the jesters went on to confront and knock out the heroes of Selûne's Smile in the City of the Dead, as they had been looking into their graverobbing on behalf of the ghost of Myyrth. They then brought the unconscious adventurers back to the Jesters' Clubhouse.

In front of the adventurers, the jesters relayed their plans before proceeding to cast a combination of spells on Myyrth's corpse, This process reanimated his skeleton, imbued it with a number of offensive spells — flame breath, frost breath, and withering gaze — and expanded it to such a gargantuan size that it broke through the clubhouse.
