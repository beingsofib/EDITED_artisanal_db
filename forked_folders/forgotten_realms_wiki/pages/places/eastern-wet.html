Eastern Wet

The Eastern Wet was the sister road of the Western Wet, both located in the Baldur's Gate/Lower City of Baldur's Gate.

Location

The Eastern Wet began where two boatbuilding drydocks stood, just to the east from the Counting House overlooking the Gray Harbor. It ran alongside the city's docks, touching Stormshore Street, encircling Athcaulyr's Stand, and culminated at Waendel's Wharf.

Notable Locations

  • Baldur's Mouth, a print shop that employed reporters
  • Athcaulyr's Stand, a large legless wharf in Eastway.
  • Fisher's Wharf, a small wharf of fishing boats and houseboats.
  • Harbormaster's Office, the center of the Baldur's Gate naval operations and naval trade.
  • Sailor's Crossing, a small dockside plaza.
  • Waendel's Wharf, smaller wharf at the northern end of Admiral's Way.
