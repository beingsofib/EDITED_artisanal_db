Watermen's Circle was the guildhall for ferrymen and other watermen in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The guildhall was located along Kaoth Alley in the southern section of Daggerford near the River Gate. It was one of the larger buildings found along Kaoth Alley.
