Barret's Quay was a small island in the eastern reaches of the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles.

Inhabitants

This island lacked any major port cities. Its population consisted primarily of humans, along with a few dwarves. Residents of the island were noted by outsiders for their taciturn nature.
