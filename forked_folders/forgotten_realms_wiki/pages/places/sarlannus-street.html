Sarlannus Street

Sarlannus Street was a street running north–south through the Temple District in the city of Athkatla.

Geography

The southern end of the street connected to wide road called Godsmoot. On the corner was the Crown Aflame theater.

Notable locations

  • The Crown Aflame: A popular Athkatlan theater.
  • House of Thammas Thoul: Home of the spice merchant and art collector.
  • Society of the Lost Ingot: This private club catered to spectacularly wealthy, male Athkatlan merchant princes.
