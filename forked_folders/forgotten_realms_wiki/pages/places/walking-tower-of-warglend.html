The Walking Tower of Warglend was a motile tower during the mid–8 century DR.

Location

As of , the Walking Tower was located near Tower Tor.

History

In the , the sage Tabarast of Moonshorn Tower reported that, amongst other signs from the Gods, the Walking Tower of had Warglend moved for the first time in a thousand years, since approximately , and left its place in Tower Tor, moving instead to the middle of a nearby lake.
