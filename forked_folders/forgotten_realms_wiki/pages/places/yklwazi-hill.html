Yklwazi Hill

Yklwazi Hill (), meaning "hill of blades", was one of the four hills in Chult atop which Port Nyanzaru was built.

Description

The top of Yklwazi Hill was dominated by the city's Grand Coliseum. Its slopes were characterized by terraces and steep, stepped streets.
