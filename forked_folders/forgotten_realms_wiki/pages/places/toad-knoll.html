Toad Knoll

Location
nameToad Knoll
regionShadowdale, Dalelands

Toad Knoll was a hill in the Dalelands just north of the North Ride.

Geography

Toad Knoll was located less than west of Shadowdale's bridge that crossed over the River Ashaba.

Flora & Fauna

The hill took its name from the bumper crop of frogs and toads that occurred in the spring months, living and breeding in the marshy ground at the base, caused by the upwelling of an intermittent natural spring..
