Felgontar's Firehelm

Building
nameThe Firehelm
typeTavern
locationMarsember

The Firehelm was a dining house in Marsember in Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

Location

The tavern was at the intersection of Gelguld Lane and Tarnsar Lane.

Services

The large dining house was popular, but respectable.

Rumors & Legends

In early spring of the , when Aumun Tholant Bezrar and Malakar Surth threatened their fellow merchant Durexter Dagohnlar and his wife, Starmara Dagohnlar, Malakar threatened cutting off Starmara's toes and giving them to Ponczer at the Firehelm to cook something with them.
