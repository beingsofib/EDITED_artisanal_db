The Gatereach Inn

locationWestgate, the Dragon Coast

The Gatereach Inn was an expensive inn found just outside the city of Westgate in 1367 DR.

Description

The inn was surrounded by a palisade.

Location

The inn was located outside of Mulsantir’s Gate along the Trader's Road.

Activities

This inn catered to wealthy merchants who cherished the inn's fine accommodations and could afford the prices. The inn withstood several raids by bandits and roving monsters over the years. All of the staff was vetted to ensure they had no connections to the criminal underworld of Westgate.
