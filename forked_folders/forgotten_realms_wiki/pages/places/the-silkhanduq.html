The Silkhanduq was a guildhall and khanduq in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR. It was the headquarters of the Jhasinnar Sodality.

A quality four-story building, it stood on the northern side of Nar'asr Drudach, Khatrev Sabban, in the city's Palace Ward.
