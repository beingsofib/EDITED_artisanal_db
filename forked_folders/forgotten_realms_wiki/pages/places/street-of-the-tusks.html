Street of the Tusks

Street of the Tusks was a street of hard-packed earth and gravel

Geography

This road began at Burnt Wagon Way and ran south to the High Road, crossing Spendthrift Alley and Vellarr's Lane along the way.

Notable Locations

  • Aurora's Realms Shop, the Trade Ward's outlet of the retail chain Aurora's Emporium, was located in the northwest corner of where this street met the High Road.

Links to Sewers

An entrance to the sewers of Waterdeep was located at the mouth of a dead-end alley opening that was just east off this street.
