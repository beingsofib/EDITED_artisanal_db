Hinjaz

Location/Mountain
nameThe Hinjaz
typeRock formation
regionRaurin

The Hinjaz was a stone formation in Raurin, not tall enough to be mountains, but taller than any other hills. They were impassable from the ground.

Overview

The black rock grew too hot, even for the best of climbers, and the wind continuously scoured the stone peaks with sand. The Durpari airlancers' pegasi often used the updrafts created by the excessive heat to gain altitude around the Hinjaz.
