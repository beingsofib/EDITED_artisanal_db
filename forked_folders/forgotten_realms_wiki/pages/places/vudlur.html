Vudlur

The Vudlur was one of the watercourses of the Esmelflow in Amn.

Geography

This creek began at a spring in the western Snakewood. It joined with the Golden Creek before flowing into the Splendarrllur. The Star Bridge spanned its waters.

History

The creek was named by the dwarves; vudlur means "woods stream" in Dwarvish.
