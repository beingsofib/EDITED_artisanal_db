Firefist Alley

Firefist Alley was an alley located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR.

Location

The alley was located with the Castings on the eastern side of the city. It lay just east of an embassy of the Brotherhood of the True Flame.

Activities

Firefist Alley was a notorious spot for brawling.
