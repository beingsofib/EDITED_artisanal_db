The Wave Rocks were a small cluster of islands in the Trackless Sea.

Geography

The isles were located about midway between the Moonshae Isles to the east and Evermeet to the west. They lay south of the Purple Rocks and north of the Gull Rocks.

History

Circa -1000 DR, the Wave Rocks, along with the Gull Rocks, Gundarlun, Mintarn, the Purple Rocks, the Teeth, Tuern, Umukek, and the Whalebones, were colonized by the Illuskan people of Ruathym, later becoming known as the Northmen.

As of the mid-to-late 14 century DR, the Wave Rocks marked the western boundary of the territory nominally claimed as hunting grounds by the white dragon Arveiaturace, which encompassed most of the Sea of Swords. At the same time, the isles served as the northeastern boundary of the seas patrolled by the navy of Evermeet, who hunted the seas for potential threats from pirates and Northmen raiders.
