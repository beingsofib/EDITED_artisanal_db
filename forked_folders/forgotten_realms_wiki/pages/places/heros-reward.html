The Hero's Reward was tavern found in the small village of Newfort in the Silver Marches during the 14 and 15 centuries DR.

Interior

Atmosphere

The taproom of the tavern was described as being rather cozy.

Services

Throughout its history, the tavern provided tasty drinks and delicious food to its patrons.

History

Hero's Reward was first established by Newfort's founder and first mayor, Trevis Uhl, in the .

In the years following the War of the Silver Marches, the tavern was run by the well-liked half-elf Delf Dereldar.

Notable Patrons

15 Century

  • Jorok Narm, the constable of Newfort who played regular games of chess with Delf.

Rumors & Legends

The tavern's name was a wink and a nod to the ex-soldiers of Zhentil Keep that resettled in Newfort in the years following the keep's destruction, many of whom were patrons at Trevis' previous establishment within the Moonsea city.
