Sagat was a small town found within the Cities of the Ancients region of east Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

Sagat was the only populated settlement found on Dawihi Island in Bahr al-Ajami. Sagat was also the only island settlement in the region not bound to the larger islands of Afyal and Sahu. Sagat was found on the far northern coast of Dawihi Island approximately due east of mighty Rog'osto.
