Ningtang Road

The Ningtang Road was a road in Ch'ing Tung Province in Shou Lung. It ran from the city of Shou'Kuan westward to the city of Yenching in Ma' Yuan Province.

History

In late or early , increasing bandit activity along the Ningtang Road east of Yenching made travel dangerous and expensive. Thus, a local merchants guild hired the chanshi Zarwu Koi to protect their caravans. Exploring the western hills of Ching Tung and posing as a bandit himself, Zarwu found and joined the group, learning they were from Chukei Province and were journeying south to prey on travelers and tourists visiting the Komite in Suijeng. He killed them as they slept and took their heads back to the merchants guild.
