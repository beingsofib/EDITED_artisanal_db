Sorn Street

Sorn Street was a road of hard-packed dirt and gravel in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Geography

This road stretched from east to west, spanning between Salabar Street in the west and Wall Way in the east. Between these two roads, it acted as the southern terminus of Urcandle Alley and the opening to Brindul Alley.

Notable Locations

Undiber's: A private club.

Uza Solizeph's Bookstore: A three-story bookstore that was owned and operated by Uza Solizeph.
