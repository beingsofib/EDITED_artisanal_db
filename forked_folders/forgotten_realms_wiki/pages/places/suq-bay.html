Suq Bay

Suq Bay was a vital body of water located in the heart of Zakhara.

Geography

The bay was literally found in the center of the continent of Zakhara. It was bordered to the south by the Golden Gulf, to the north by the Furrowed Mountains, to the east by the Haunted Lands, and to the west by the High Desert.

Description

Suq Bay was divided into a North Quarter and South Quarter, with a narrow strait connecting the two. Rocky mountainous terrain or desert nearly encircled the entire bay.
