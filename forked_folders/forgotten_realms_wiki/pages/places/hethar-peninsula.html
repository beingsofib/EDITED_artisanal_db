The Hethar Peninsula was a stretch of land located in southern Dambrath.

Geography

Located between the Great Sea to the south and the Bay of Dancing Dolphins to the north, the Hethar Peninsula formed a good portion of Dambrath's southern border.

Notable Locations

The following settlements were located on the Hethar Peninsula:

  • Darmesh
  • Far Outpost
  • Hethar
  • Purl
