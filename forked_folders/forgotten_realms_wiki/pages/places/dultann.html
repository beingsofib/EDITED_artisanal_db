Dultann was a small village on the island of Gwynneth.

Description

Dultann consisted of a pond owned collectively by the residents, a woodcarver's shop, and a tavern that were clustered among a few sheep farms.

Geography

This village was located on the eastern edge of the Myrloch Vale.

History

In 1359 DR, the inhabitants of this village were wracked with fear over tales of the Risen Cult of Bane and their leader the "Dark Druid".

Notable Locations

  • Dultann's Beard, a tavern run by Paedrac Rollstone in the 14 century DR.
