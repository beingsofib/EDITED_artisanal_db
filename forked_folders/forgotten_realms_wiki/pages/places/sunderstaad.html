Sunderstaad was a Northlander town and kingdom located in the realm of Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Sunderstaad was located on the coast of the Sea of Swords in northeast Gnarhelm near Lake Sunder. It was the northernmost settlement found in Gnarhelm. The town was situated on a small unnamed bay.

Defenses

The kingdom could muster an army of 400 foot soldiers and 120 cavalry riders.
