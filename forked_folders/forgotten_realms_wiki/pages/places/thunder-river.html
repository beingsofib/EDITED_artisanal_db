Thunder River

The Thunder River, also known as the Thunderflow, was a river in northeastern Cormyr that flowed out from the Thunder Peaks.

Geography

Thunder River flowed west out of the Thunder Peaks, passing through the Eastern Plains and by the Hullack Forest, On the Eastern Plains side, the towns of Hultail

Trade

A lot of merchantile ships traveled along the Thunder River between Cormyr, the village of Hultail, and the Dalelands. The town of Ghars, for example, had ships navigate the river in order to export goods to Hultail.

History

Prior to the 14 century DR, the name Thunderflow could be seen on maps of the region. By the 14 century DR, the name had fallen out of favor.
