Canter Street

Canter Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Canter Street was located in the Common Quarter in the southwest corner of Zhentil Keep. The road originated at the entrance to the Harbor District and passed west through the city to end at the intersection of Beggars' Way and Fish Street. Canter street was crossed by Brigand Way and Dixon Street.

Notable Locations

A Zhentilar guardpost was located along Canter Street at the entrance to the Harbor District.
