The Goose of Doom

Building
nameThe Goose of Doom
typeTavern
locationSuzail

The Goose of Doom was a dockside tavern in Suzail.

Reputation

The place attracted a crowd that had little love for the nobility of Cormyr, or for the Purple Dragons.

Patrons

A regular was Brorn Roril, retired swordcaptain.
