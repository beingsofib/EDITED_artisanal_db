Achazar's Throne

Achazar's Throne was a mountain located in the Nightmare's Hoof mountain range, near the city of Sambral, one of the Windrise Ports of Laerakond.

Background

The mountain had a depression in its southern face beneath its peak. Legends claimed this mountain was used by the primordial Achazar as a throne.
