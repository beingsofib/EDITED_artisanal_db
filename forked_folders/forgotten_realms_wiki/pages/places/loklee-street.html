Loklee Street

Loklee Street was a street in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate that began in Heapside and curved to Eastway.

History

Loklee Street got its name from from one of the earliest names of Baldur's Gate sometime before 0 DR. Circa the summer of the , the adventuring detective named Tav lived on Loklee Street.
