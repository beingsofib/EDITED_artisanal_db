Nor Tso

Nor Tso, or the Sky Lake, was a lake in the Hordelands found near the center of the Katakoro Plateau.

Geography

The lake sat north of the mighty Hungtse River, forming part of the boundary of the Wild Yak Meadows to the west. It was fed by the Muren Tso.
