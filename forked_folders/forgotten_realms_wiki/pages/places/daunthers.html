Daunthers was a fortress in Cormyr that housed disabled Purple and Blue Dragons.

Description

The site was a large hospital, residence, and farm that cared for the realm's soldiers who could not live on their own in retirement.

Geography

Daunthers stood east of Gladehap on the north side of the Way of the Manticore, at the point where the road turned east-south-east to Wheloon.
