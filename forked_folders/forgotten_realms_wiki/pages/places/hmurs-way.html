Hmur's Way

Hmur's Way was the sole major clear roadway in the underwater city of Myth Nantar.

Etymology

Hmur's Way was most likely named for Hmur,

Location

This road was paved in blaze coral and depicted mosaics that over time broke down in appearance. Like the other major roadways of Myth Nantar, it measured in width. It spanned west from the lowest slope of Mount Halaath to the Fire Fountain at the city's central square.
