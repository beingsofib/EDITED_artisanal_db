Adder Lane

Adder Lane was minor avenue of hard-packed earth and gravel in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Location

This road ran from northwest to southeast, extending off of the Fishgut Court and terminating at an intersection with Gut Alley. Not far from its opening at Fishgut Court, the lane was intersected by Wastrel Alley.
