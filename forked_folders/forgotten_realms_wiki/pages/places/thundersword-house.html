Thundersword House was the castle and the seat of power of the noble Thundersword family of Cormyr.

Structure

The Thundersword family was the third major noble family in the city of Immersea, and the castle they erected was the newest and largest among their peers: the families of Wyvernspur, and Cormaeril. Unlike them, however, Thundersword House was built outside the borders of Immersea, to the southwest of the Wyvernspurs' Redstone Castle and their farmlands. Thundersword House stood on a small wooded knoll in its own small valley.
