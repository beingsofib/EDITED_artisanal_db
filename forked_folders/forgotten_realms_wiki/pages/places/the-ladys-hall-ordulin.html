The Lady's Hall, which was also called Luck House by the non-devout, was a temple dedicated to Tymora located in the city of Ordulin in the .

Inhabitants

The temple was run by High Priestess Duatha Lindar. Duatha was assisted by 16 other priests and 24 followers.
