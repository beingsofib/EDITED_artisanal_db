Karen Court

Karen Court was a major street that crossed the entirety of The Shutters neighborhood in the Crow's End District of Ravens Bluff from east to west.

Geography

Karen Court ran parallel to Hammerstone Street and Red Sails Way, that formed The Shutters's southern border. It began at Mraedara's Street in the east and crossed Stonekeep Concourse, Burning Shield Lane, Landleen Lane, Helhavryn's Walk, Castles Way, and Deepaxe Way, reaching its end at the muddy bank of the Fire River.

Notable Locations

  • Fish Tails, a fried fishcake factory, two doors west from The Last Jack near the Ravensbridge.
  • Fruits & More, a grocer located next-door to Muldryn's Chainworks on Karen Court. It specialized in sugars, jams, and compotes, as well as crude berry-infused cordials.
  • The Last Jack, a quiet inn with a safe taproom located on the riverside where Stonekeep Concourse and Mraedara's Street crossed.
