The Prosperity Caravan Company was part of the Prosperity House complex located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The complex was located in south Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of the Foreign Quarter on Zhent Avenue.

Activities

Owner Malvred Silverguild assembled caravans here that traveled the breadth of Faerûn. Incoming caravans alos stopped here to unload their cargo. Malvred's skill in assembling near perfect caravans prompted many merchants to seek his services for their own goods. Mercenaries and adventurers were also hired here as caravan guards.
