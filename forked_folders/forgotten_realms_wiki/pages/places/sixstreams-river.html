Sixstreams River

Sixstreams River, also known as the arm, was a river in the Sword region of Anauroch.

Geography

This river ran west from the Desertsmouth Mountains into Tel Badir, a D'tarig village whose streets winded along it and were cascaded through by it. It was situated between two large streams and four smaller ones, which also came from the Desertsmouth Mountains. A bit west of Tel Badir, the six streams all coaleseced into one large river, hence the name Sixstreams River.

The Sixstreams ran out westward from Tel Badir into the desert.
