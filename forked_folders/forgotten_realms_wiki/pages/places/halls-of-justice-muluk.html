The Halls of Justice was a government building located in Muluk, the City of Kings, in 1367 DR.

Description

The Halls of Justice were found near the center of Muluk.

Activities

This location served as the court of law, center for tax collection, center for all other governmental decisions for the city. Legal decisions made within the Halls of Justice could be appealed to the Caliph in the grand palace where she met with officials once per week.
