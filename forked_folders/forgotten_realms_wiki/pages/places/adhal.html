Adhal was a small town in North Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

Adhal was located along several caravan routes in between Qudra and the Sea of Caravans. Its position at the northern end of the Sea of Caravans provided the town with a constant supply of commercial and pilgrim traffic.
