The Lonely Lake

The Lonely Lake was an oasis found on the outskirts of the Plains of Purple Dust in east Faerûn and the Hordelands.

Geography

The oasis was nestled in the foothills on the eastern slopes of the Sword Mountains just north of Fuirgar, the Land of the Stone Giants. Its location made it the only source of fresh water for hundreds of miles.
