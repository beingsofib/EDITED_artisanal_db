Shelves of Honor were special graves found beneath Battlehall in Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter during the mid-to-late 14 century DR..

History

The Shelves of Honor were carved into the solid rock walls beneath Battehall, a temple dedicated to Tempus. Faithful heroes of Tempus who died heroically in battle were interred here as an honor. It was rumored that anyone buried on a Shelf of Honor served as a favorite of Tempus in the afterlife.
