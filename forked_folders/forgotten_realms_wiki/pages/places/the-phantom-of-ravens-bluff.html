The Phantom of Ravens Bluff

The Phantom of Ravens Bluff is a one-round adventure module set in the Living City organized play program. It was published in blank and is intended for a group of six adventurers of any levels.

Categories: Living City adventures · 2nd edition adventures · Published in 1993 · Works by Shawn F. Carnes
