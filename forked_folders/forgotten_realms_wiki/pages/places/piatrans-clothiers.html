Piatran's Clothiers was a shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The building stood a single story tall.

Location

Piatran's Clothiers was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter on the east side of Weeping Maiden's Run directly behind Madame Garah's Boarding House and Amrani's Laundry.

Inhabitants

As its name implied, the shop was owned and run by Piatran in the .
