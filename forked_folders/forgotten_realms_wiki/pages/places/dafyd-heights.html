Dafyd Heights

Dafyd Heights was a small mountain range located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Dafyd Heights was found enveloped by the Llyrath Forest in southern Gwynneth. The mountains ran east to west through the forest parallel to the nearby coast of the Sea of Swords for . A small unnamed river flowed south from the mountains to empty into the sea.
