The Cascade of Coins was the temple of Waukeen within the third quarter of the city of Innarlith.

Location

It was located in the third quarter among the tradesmen and workshops.

Structure

It had a triangular roof supported by eight columns.

Interior

A deep well was in the middle of the temple that people threw coins down.

History

In 1369 DR Senator Pristoleph married Phyrea, the daughter of Senator Inthelph, at the temple in a simple ceremony without guests.
