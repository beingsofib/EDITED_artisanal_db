Jacaman was a small island within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Jacaman was the furthest east of the notable islands in the Korinn Archipelago, located among a cluster of smaller isles due east of Dennik.

Trade

Jacaman produced exotic spices for export, and was the premier location among the Korinn islands for the growing of such cash crops. However, trade for these spices was risky owing to the great interest in them shown by pirates.

History

As of the late 15 century DR, control over the island was seized by Roquern, who took over control of cultivation and export of many of the isle's unique spices. He secretly tainted his spices with infernal power that made imbibers more susceptible to coercive magic, and any who consumed large amounts of these spices would become Roquern's thralls. To conceal his operations, he took great pains to convince the outside world that Jacaman was insignificant.

Inhabitants

The isle's Northlander human settlements were known to be weak and unfortunate due to the great deal of pirate activity around the island and its shipping routes. It was not very populous even compared to other islands in the Korinn Archipelago.

By the late 15 century DR, the isle was also home to a large number of evil creatures and slaves in service to Roquern.
