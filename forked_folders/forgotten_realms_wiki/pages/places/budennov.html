Budennov was a small town located along the shores of the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Geography

Budennov was found along the southern shore of the Lake of Mists northwest of Strelsi. The Clearflow River and Dead Dwarf Bridge were located due west of Budennov. It was located approximately north of the Golden Way.
