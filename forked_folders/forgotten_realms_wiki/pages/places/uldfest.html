Uldfest was a small settlement found in Sossal in the Cold Lands of northeast Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Geography

Uldfest lay along the northwestern slopes of the Armridge Mountains, making it the westernmost settlement in Sossal. It was connected to Shannath to the southeast by an unnamed road, and the Reach trail connected with Uldfest from the southwest.
