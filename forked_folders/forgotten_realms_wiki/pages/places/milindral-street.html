Milindral Street

Milindral Street was a road found within the city of Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan.

Location

Milindral Street ran through the central section of Suldolphor near the harbor.

Notable Locations

An underground temple to Beshaba called the Deep House of Doom was located beneath Milindral Street.
