Aurora's Realms Shop, Street of the Tusks Catalogue Counter was an outlet of the Aurora's Emporium retail chain in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 and late 15 centuries DR. It was one of six such outlets in the city.

Location

This shop was situated on the northwest corner of the intersection of the Street of the Tusks and the High Road.

Description

It was a four-story building.

Services

This shop sold the various items found in Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue.

Staff

In the mid-1360s DR, the counter clerk was Orgula Samshroon, a matronly woman known for her knowledge and memory, and the service mage was Dhaunryl Zalimbar, a shy but kindly fellow. In addition, there were six guards who worked shifts of three on and three off.
