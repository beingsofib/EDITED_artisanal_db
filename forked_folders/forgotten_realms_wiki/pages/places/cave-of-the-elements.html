Cave of the Elements

Location
nameCave of the Elements
typeCavern
regionDeep Wastes, Underdark

The Cave of the Elements was a peculiar cavern near the drow city of Sschindylryn that was home to a great many elemental creatures.

Description

The Cave of the Elements was a large chamber that was taller than it was wide, with pools of water at the base and irregular formations of stone reaching up to the ceiling. A perilous network of bridge-like stone let an explorer traverse the vertical extent of the chamber (assuming they survived the elementals), finding hidden side passages leading elsewhere in the Underdark. At the very top of the cavern, guarded by a small army of stony elementals was a massive daycrystal formation, lighting the top of the chamber in a warm orange glow as if the sun itself was peeking into the Underdark.

Geography

The cavern was located not far from the Boneyard and a portal to Sschindylryn.

Inhabitants

The cavern was home to a large number of elemental beings, including earth, water, and air elementals, as well as salamanders. They all co-existed in a strange harmony, not struggling against each other but instead helping and defending each other. This combined threat made the cavern extremely dangerous to interlopers.
