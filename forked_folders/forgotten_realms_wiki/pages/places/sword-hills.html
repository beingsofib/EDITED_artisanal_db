The Sword Hills were a hilly region south of the Delimbiyr River and east of Daggerford.

Geography

The Sword Hills were a territory south of the Delimbiyr River.

History

The lord of Morlin Castle in the early 3 century DR had a title of "Lord Protector of the Sword Hills".

There had been a short-lived silver rush after new mines were discovered in the area in the mid-15 century DR.

Notable Locations

A notable location marked the southern border of the Sword Hills near the Misty Forest, the Tombholes.
