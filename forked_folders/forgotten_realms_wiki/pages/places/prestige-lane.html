Prestige Lane

Prestige Lane was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 century DR.

Location

Prestige Lane was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Field Ward. It originated on the eastern end of the Fieldway, branching southwest through a neighborhood and crossing Saltpork Street to end at Twelvedog Court.
