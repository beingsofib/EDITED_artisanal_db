The Watchful Lynx

Building
nameThe Watchful Lynx
typeInn
locationArabel, Cormyr

The Watchful Lynx was an inn in the city of Arabel in Cormyr around 1367 DR.

It stood right by Eastgate, the eastern entrance of the city, and faced the Bazaar.

It catered to customers of lesser means.
