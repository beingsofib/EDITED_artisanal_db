Orbrekh

The Orbrekh was a slim branch of the Orsraun Mountains in Turmish.

Inhabitants

The Orbrekh was inhabited by a mountain giant clan called the "community of the high brows". Their relative peacefulness made the Orbrekh the safest part of the Orsrauns for travelers.
