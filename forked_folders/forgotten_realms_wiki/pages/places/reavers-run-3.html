Reavers' Run

Reavers' Run was the main street in Luskan's South Bank.

Description

Reavers' Run was a wide boulevard—and the slums to the southwest.

Geography

The road ran from the South Gate northwest to the city market before terminating at the Short Span, a bridge leading to Blood Island It was also the quickest route to get from the South Gate to the bridge leading to Closeguard Island.

Notable Locations

  • Captains' Close, a walled park on the western side of the street that contained the palatial homes for the High Captains of Ship Taerl and Ship Suljack.
