The Temple of the Dawn was a temple to Lathander in Heliogabalus.

Staff

Pavel Shemov had been an initiate from at least the early 1360s. The patriarch was Kovor Gemetsk from at least that time, until at least .

Structure

The place had archives, and cloisters. It was a place of learning, if not the biggest temple in Damara.
