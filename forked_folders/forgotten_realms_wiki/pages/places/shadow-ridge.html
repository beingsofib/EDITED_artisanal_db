Shadow Ridge

Location
nameShadow Ridge
typeRidge
regionShadowdale, Dalelands

Shadow Ridge was a rise of ground on the west side of the Ashaba in Shadowdale.

Geography

The ridge was north of the Shrine to Mystra and east of Luth Mlennon's farm.

Inhabitants

Children occasionally swam the river to pick berries on Shadow Ridge.
