Caftenor was a small port settlement on the island of Caftenor within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The village sat along the island's eastern coast of the Sea of Swords toward the center of the island.

History

During the 14 century DR, Craftenor was the most common stopover for ships traversing through the Korinn Archipelago. However, by the late 15 century DR, only Westhaven offered any safe haven for sailors.

Inhabitants

The town was occupied largely by humans and halflings.
