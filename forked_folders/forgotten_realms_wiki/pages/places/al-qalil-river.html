Al-Qalil River

Al-Qalil River, also called the Insufficient Flow perhaps because of its small size, was a waterway found in the northern region of the Cities of the Heart in Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

Al-Qalil River originated in the foothills of the southernmost tier of the Ghost Mountains. The river flowed west briefly before reaching the northern reaches of Suq Bay. The city of Wasat was found along its banks at its mouth.
