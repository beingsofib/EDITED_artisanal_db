Street of the Ajami

The Street of the Ajami, or Ajami Street, was located in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Description

The Street of the Ajami was adjacent to the Caravan Boulevard, branching off to head northwest to exit Huzuz near the Caliph's Harbor where it met the River Road. The Street of the Ajami formed much of the southwest boundary for the Craft District, bisected the Warehouse District, and formed a small portion of the northeast borders of both the Central District and Pilgrims' District. The street also lined a portion of the Court District where it bulged up into the southern Craft District.
