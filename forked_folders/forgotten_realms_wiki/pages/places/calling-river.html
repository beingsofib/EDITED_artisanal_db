Calling River

The Calling River was the river that flowed through and separated the Grandlore and the Great Forests on the southern part of Ilighôn.

Description

The Calling River was one of the three waterways that started as waterfalls plummeting from a plateau at the center of Ilighôn, forming mist-covered rivers. The Elder River ran north, passing Archentree, the Springbrook ran west until it emptied into the Springbrook Shallows, and lastly, the Calling River flowed south into the Lake Bluesprings, through it, and culminating in the river mouth delta that fed into the Sea of Fallen Stars.

History

In the early ventures, before Ilighôn was settled, the Calling River served as a path for pilgrims who followed Silvanus's call up to the plateau that later became the Oakenhall, or the House of Silvanus.

In the , the shores of the Calling River were a meeting spot of Shinthala Deepcrest, the Amazon Queen of Ilighôn and a member of the Elder Circle of the Emerald Enclave, and Ashenford Torinbow a druid from Shadowdale. The druids met to discuss the looming danger of undead warlord Borran Kiosk.
