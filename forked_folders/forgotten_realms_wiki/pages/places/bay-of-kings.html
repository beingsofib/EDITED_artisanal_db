Bay of Kings

The Bay of Kings was located on the coast of Estagund in the Shining Lands in 1367 DR.

Geography

The Bay of Kings offered natural protection for the city of Klionna, sheltering many of their warships and fishing boats from the fierce winter storms that blew in from the Great Sea and wracked the region.
