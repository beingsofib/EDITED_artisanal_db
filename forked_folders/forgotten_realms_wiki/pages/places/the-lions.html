The Lions

The Lions was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

The Lions was located in the southwest section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. The Lions branched off of Diamond Street just east of that road's intersection with Westwall Street. It passed southeast through the Sea Ward, terminating at Julthoon Street after passing the Field of Triumph where it formed its eastern border. Other roads connecting with the Lions included Dob's Loss, Gulzindar Street, Seawatch Street, and Jelzar's Stride.
