Rabin's Reef was a small island within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Rabin's Reef was rocky It was located in the center of the Korinn Archipelago, just north of the northern tip of the much larger island of Ventris.

Inhabitants

The isle was mostly known as a place where wealthy Northlander families (especially from nearby Ventris) would spend the summer.
