Giant's Belt Mountains

The Giant's Belt Mountains marked the northeastern border of the Beastlands and Durpar. The Aerilpar Forest lay at the foot of this mountain range, as well.

The monsters who destroyed Veldorn came from the Giant's Belt Mountains, making them the origin point of the Beastlands themselves.
