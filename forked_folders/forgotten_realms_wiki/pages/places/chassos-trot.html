Chasso's Trot

Chasso's Trot was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Chasso's Trot was located in the northeastern section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. It ran east to west, originating on the High Road at the border with the North Ward and passing west to eventually end on Diamond Street just south of Ivory Street. Along the way Vondil Street was intersected by Mendever Street, Sul Street, Shield Street, and Copper Street.
