Wyrm River

Wyrm River in Hartsvale was a tributary of the Clear Whirl River.

Description

Wyrm River froze solid in the winter months.

Geography

The river flowed from the Ice Spires South, through the Gorge of the Silver Wyrm, and fed the Clear Whirl.
