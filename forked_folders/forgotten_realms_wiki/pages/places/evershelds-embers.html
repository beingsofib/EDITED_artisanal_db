Eversheld's Embers

Building
nameEversheld's Embers
typeTavern
locationHalfhap, Cormyr
ownerAngryn Eversheld

Eversheld's Embers was a pleasant tavern in Halfhap operated by Angryn Eversheld around 1369 DR. The tavern was named for its huge fireplace, the mantle of which was decorated with a rotating collection of trophies brought in by visitors.
