Shining Sea

The Shining Sea was an arm of the Trackless Sea that was one of the great seas of Faerûn.

Geography

The sea stretched along the coastline of southwest Faerûn, with the Chultan Peninsula to the south and the shoreline of Calimshan to the north. It was quite large, although it was not as large as the Sea of Swords. In the northeast was a massive enclosed bay known as the Lake of Steam, owing to its high volcanic activity.

Inhabitants

The Shining Sea had a large amount of trade and traffic across its surface. It was a much busier region than other seas in Faerûn's west.

The sea was home to aquatic elves.

A rare beautiful species of intelligent firebirds dwelt across the Shining Sea, in seclusion, away from prying eyes. These birds were legendary among Chultans for their perfect beauty, but they were also infamous for their horrible shrieks the birds themselves called "singing."
