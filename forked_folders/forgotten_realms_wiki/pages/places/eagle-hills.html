The Eagle Hills were a geographical feature located on the elven homeland of Evermeet.

Geography

The Eagle Hills were located on the eastern banks of the River Ardulith. They stretched from Evermeet's northwest coast through the center of the island along the river before terminating just upriver from where the River Shaelyn joined the Ardulith. The hills stretched for approximately . The Eagle Hills highest peak was Thaliakaera Rock.

Description

The hills stood over the river valley. Large conifers covered the slopes of the Eagle Hills.

Inhabitants

The Eagle Hills were home to group of pegasi and giant eagles

History

Sunstones could be found within the Eagle Hills.
