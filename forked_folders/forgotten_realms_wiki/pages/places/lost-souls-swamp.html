Lost Souls' Swamp was a small but dismal wetland in Cormyr.

Description

The swamp was full of harsh terrain, containing heavily wooded areas, rocky spurs, cliffs and caves, and deep, wide pools of stagnant and flowing water. It also contained several sets of ruins long abandoned, including the remnants of the Fallen Oak Shrine, which a visiting wizard had helpfully left a magically illuminated path to through the swamp.

Geography

The swamp was located in northwestern Cormyr, south of Tyrluk, between the depths of the King's Forest and the foothills of the Storm Horns.

Flora & Fauna

The swamp was home to woodland beasts such as bears and wolves, and even had dryads, but the depredations of evil made it a hostile and lifeless place. Travelers were at risk of being attacked by will-o'-wisps—and having their souls trapped by them.

History

By the late 15 century DR, the swamp had gained its name from the shadow of Lost Souls' Swamp, which used wisps to trap the souls of lost travelers, and gathered up a force of subservient shadows and umbral creatures. These included the unfortunate Kyle Briarbuck, Callie Brennan, and Evrim Whitehair, who perished from malice and misadventure and were forced to remain at the sites of their deaths. The hermit Seamus later arrived and learned to navigate the hostile terrain and survive the malevolent creatures of the swamp, enjoying decades of solitude there.

In the aftermath of the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon in the mid-1480s DR, Oriphaun Huntsilver deposited a scrap of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll with Seamus, trusting him to keep it hidden. Despite the excellent hiding place, the medusa Sshaastess invaded the swamp, took the Scroll piece, and made the ruins near Fallen Oak Shrine her lair. She proceeded to use her magical powers to dominate much of the swamp's life, corrupting the local dryads into maddened service and pulling up earth elementals as her personal guards. At least for the moment she let the shadow of the Swamp control its own domain, but her long-term plans were ended when Rina Belostos's Harper reinforcements arrived and fought their way through the gauntlet of threats, ending the reign of Sshaastess and likely the shadow as well.
