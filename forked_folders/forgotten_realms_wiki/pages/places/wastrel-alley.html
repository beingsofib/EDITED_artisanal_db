Wastrel Alley

Wastrel Alley was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Wastrel Alley was found in Waterdeep's Dock Ward. The road originated on Belnimbra's Street in the north where it branched south and passed southwest to terminate at Fish Street just southeast of Fishgut Court. Other roads that intersected Wastrel Alley included Adder Lane and Nelnuk's Walk.

Notable Locations

A tavern called Darth's Dolphyntyde was located on the east side of Wastrel Alley.
