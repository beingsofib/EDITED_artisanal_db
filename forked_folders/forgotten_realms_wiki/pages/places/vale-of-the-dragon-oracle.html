The Vale of the Dragon Oracle was a valley on Mount Drakkor Rouge.

Geography

In the , the valley was flooded with a shallow swamp. Recent volcanic activity, caused by a small change in the magma pressure, had caused a rockslide and collapsed parts of some buildings by , and the valley was covered in scrub instead of a swamp.

History

Starting in , the Cult of the Dragon built a new temple into a north-facing slope of the Vale, opposite the Temple of the Dragon Oracle, with help from the Church of Velsharoon, after their old temple was damaged in the rockslide at some point after .
