Loudwater Hall of Training was a school in Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid–14 century DR.

Location

It stood near the bank of the Delimbiyr River, conveniently located beside Armoghh's Armoire of Arms and Armor.

Services

In business by 1358 DR, the store presumably provided training in martial techniques.
