Katakoro Glacier

Location
nameKatakoro Glacier
typeGlacier
regionKatakoro Shan, Yehimal

The Katakoro Glacier was a large glacier located within Katakoro Shan, bordering both Ra-Khati and Khazari.

Geography

This large glacier covered hundreds of square miles.

Inhabitants

Many dangerous creatures lived on the glacier, but most were kept out of the neighboring kingdoms by the steep mountains of Katakoro Shan.
