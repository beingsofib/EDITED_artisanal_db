The Pearl-Handled Pipe was an exquisite inn that was considered by some to be the finest establishment in all of Yartar. As of the 14 century DR, it was run by Elladuth Myristar, who loved buying new decorations for her ever-expanding establishment.

Location

It was located just west of the Fishyard, Yartar's central marketplace.

Structure

The inn was large and spacious. It could accommodate over 600 guests in good comfort. Every time Elladuth purchased new furniture or decorations, she renovated the inn and had new rooms built.

Interior

Each of the inn's rooms was furnished with finely crafted chairs, desks and other pieces of furniture. Amenities were provided for every guest, among them quills, ink, and parchment for writing.

Services

Beyond the lush accommodations, the Pearl-Handled Pipe served three meals to its guests each and every day. These were nothing short of actual feasts, fit for any lord or wealthy merchant. Complementary meals for guests were made available in the common dining room; they were also served to non-guests for the price of 1 gp each.
