Treefall Pass

The Treefall Pass was a narrow path through the Mountains of the Alaoreum.

Description

The humble mountain path of the Treefall Pass separated the Orsraun Mountains from their northernmost peak cluster—the Alaoreum Mountains. The pass ran from Turmish to the south, through the mountains, and reached the Gulthmere Forest to the north.
