Lancegallop Lane

Lancegallop Lane was the continuation of the Harrow Ride when it entered the town of Harrowdale.

Description

The road entered Harrowdale in the northwest, splitting Goldenleaf Stables before continuing to the heart of the town near the shores Dragon Reach.

Notable Locations

  • Goldenleaf Stables
  • Willowman Trading Post
  • Gunderman Brewery
