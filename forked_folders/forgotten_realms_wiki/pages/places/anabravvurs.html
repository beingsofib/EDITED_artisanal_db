Anabravvur's was the best and most luxurious shipwright workshop in the Sea of Fallen Stars, based in Selgaunt of Sembia in 1490s DR.

History

By the late 15 century DR, Anabravvur's shipbuilders were run by Alpharra Anabravvur, the third generation of her family to helm the business. Alpharra's ships were huge and luxurious, elegantly designed out of superb materials. Anabravvur's received a lot of business but could afford to spend time on building, unaffected financially by the loss of one or more clients.
