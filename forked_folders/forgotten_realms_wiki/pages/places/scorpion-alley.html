Scorpion Alley

Scorpion Alley was an alley located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR.

Location

The alley was located with the Marlgate district near the center of the city just east of Krak al-Tawil.

Activities

As its name implied, the alley was known for its infestation of sparktail scorpions.
