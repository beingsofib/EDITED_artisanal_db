Bane's Mistress was a gambling hall and festhall in the Central Port of Skullport in the late 14 century DR.

Structure

Bane's Mistress was a two-story building in fair to poor condition.

Services

The festhall's gambling operations are conducted at a copper-ante level, while its other services were regarded as mediocre in quality, though offered at average prices.

History

According to local accounts, the festhall was originally constructed by a former priestess of Bane, in the aftermath of the Time of Troubles. However, due to its frequent change of ownership over the years, the precise origins of the establishment remained uncertain.

Inhabitants

In 1370 DR, the owner of Bane's Mistress was Mirabon.
