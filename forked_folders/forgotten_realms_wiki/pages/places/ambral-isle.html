Ambral Isle was an island in the Sea of Fallen Stars.

History

At some point before the founding of Waterdeep, Princess Elvandaruil's bracelet washed ashore after a flight spell failed on her.

Rumors & Legends

Jokes at the expense of the intelligence of the inhabitants were common among the inhabitants of the Dragonmere, the Dales, and the Vast.

Inhabitants

  • Arguth, a mage of the 3 century DR.
