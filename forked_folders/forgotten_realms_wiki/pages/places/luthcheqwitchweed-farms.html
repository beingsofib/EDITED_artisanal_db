The Witchweed Farms was a large site dedicated to growing witchweed located in the city of Luthcheq in Chessenta during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Witchweed Farms occupied a large walled enclosure of land approximately a quarter of the size of the rest of Luthcheq.

Description

The plot of land contained many separate buildings. The plot was accessible only by a single gate situated near the the Burning Mage inn.
