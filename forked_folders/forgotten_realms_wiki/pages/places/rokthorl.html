Rokthorl was a dragonborn settlement and the capital of Relmaur, Laerakond.

Background

Rokthorl was a "mobile" city. Whichever cavern the High Crown of Relmaur was inhabiting at any given moment was named Rokthorl. The Relmorn always tried to maintain the cave-settlements of Harone Thar and Aranghthal near the current location of Rokthorl.
