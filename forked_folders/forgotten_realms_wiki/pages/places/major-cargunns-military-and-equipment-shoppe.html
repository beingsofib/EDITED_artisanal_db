Major Cargunn's Military and Equipment Shoppe was a general store in the southern quarter of Eshpurta run by Cargunn, a retired member of the Shield Militia.

Location

The Shoppe was located two doors north of the Hamstrung Giant.

Services

The Shoppe carried all manner of adventuring gear, weapons, armor, and supplies up to a top price of 500 gp;
