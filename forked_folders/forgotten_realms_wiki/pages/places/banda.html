Banda was a town and fortress in the Kuong Kingdom in Malatra, standing just south of the northern border with T'u Lung.

Activities

The fortress was built as a show of strength, but T'u Lung displayed little interest in marching over the border. It had a garrison of 1,000 soldiers, who conducted patrols of the surrounding jungle and provided escorts for the merchant caravans.
