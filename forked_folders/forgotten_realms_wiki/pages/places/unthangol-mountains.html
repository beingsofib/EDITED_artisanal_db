Unthangol Mountains

The Unthangol Mountains were a range of mountains located in the Old Empires area of Faerûn, between southern Unther and the Eastern Shaar.

Geography

The Rivers Angol and Alamber originated from the Unthangol Mountains. The Unthangol Mountains formed a portion of Unther's, and later Tymanther's, southern border, separating it from the northern reaches of the Shaar. Unthangol Pass, found in the center of the mountain range, allowed for passage through the peaks.

Notable inhabitants

  • Furifax, a bandit leader in the 14th century DR.
