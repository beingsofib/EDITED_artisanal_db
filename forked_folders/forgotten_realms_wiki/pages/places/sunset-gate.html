Sunset Gate

Building
nameSunset Gate
typeGatehouse
locationEvereska, Western Heartlands

The Sunset Gate was a gate separating the West Cwm and the Vine Vale on the outskirts of Evereska. There was a stone guardhouse nearby that used to serve as a watch post for a dozen Evereskan soliders.
