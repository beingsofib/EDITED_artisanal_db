Fire Way

Fire Way was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Fire Way was located in the southwest corner of Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. The road ran west to east, beginning near the Tower of Pain Exalted in the west and passing east to end at the wall of the Harbor District. Fire Way connected with Brigand Way.
