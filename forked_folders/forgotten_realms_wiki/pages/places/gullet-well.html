Gullet Well or the Well of the Bonedance was a watering hole somewhere in or near Maraeda in the mid–8 century DR.

History

In the , Gullet Well dried up. Tabarast, a sage of Moonshorn Tower, witnessed horses being laid low by the lack of water, and also heard from priests that such a thing was in fact a divine sign.
