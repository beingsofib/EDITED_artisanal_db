Tymora's Blessing was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The tavern stood a single story tall.

Location

Tymora's Blessing was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter. The tavern was located along Snake Alley in the southwest section of the Adventurers' Quarter next to Helm's Hall.
