Westhaven was a small port located on the island of Pandira within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles. It was a known as a thriving base for pirates who plied the Sea of Moonshae and the Sea of Swords.

Geography

Westhaven was found along the northern side of Pandira along the coast of the Sea of Swords.

Government

The city was governed by a single law: violence was not tolerated, and any disturbance of the peace rendered the offender fair game for the aggression of the entire city's pirates and outlaws. This meant that Westhaven was renowned as a "neutral ground" for anyone, regardless of their criminality or history.

Trade

The port was a thriving center of illicit commerce catering mostly to pirates. Black market goods as well as information was readily available to the highest bidder.

History

Westhaven was established as a pirate haven in the early 14 century DR. Prior to this, it had been a normal port like any other, but after unending raids by pirates and Northlander marauders, the inhabitants decided that the only way to survive was to cater to these criminals rather than to fight them. Thus, they began to welcome anyone, whether they were honest traders, pirates, soldiers, etc.

Within a few years, Westhaven was said to be "the greatest pirate city in the world" among the criminals who knew about it, and while authorities knew that such a pirate haven had sprung up, the location at Westhaven was initially kept secret. The pirates' enemies were said to be willing to pay handsomely for a map showing the way to this secret pirate city.

By the late 15 century DR, every realm in both the Moonshae Isles and from along the Sword Coast maintained a spy in the city.

Inhabitants

Most of Westhaven's population was composed of Northlander buccaneers, ruffians, and other dangerous individuals. Even so, as of the late 15 century DR, it was said to be the only port in the whole of the Korinn Archipelago that welcomed for Northlander sailors who needed a place to resupply or wait out a storm.
