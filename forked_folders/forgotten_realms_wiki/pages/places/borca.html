Borca was one of the Domains of Dread, a land of valleys ruled by a robber baroness.

Geography

Borca was mostly in valleys.

Flora & Fauna

The wildlife was quite poisonous.

Government

The local aristocracy were all tenants of the local Lady, Ivana Boritsi. She charged outrageous taxes to all, and poisoned all she fancied.
