Bay of Balduran

The Bay of Balduran was a large bay in southeast Anchorome. The bay was named after the explorer Balduran, who was known as the first Faerûnian to set foot on the continent.

Geography

The bay was located at the mouth of a river, along the coast of Anchorome north of Kultaka. It opened into the Trackless Sea.

The only settlement along the bay was Fort Flame, a small colony of Flaming Fist mercenaries commanded by Bellan.

History

The bay was named in the by the survivors of a Flaming Fist expedition commissioned by Eltan with the objective of exploring Anchorome. The explorers established the settlement of Flaming Fort and also named the surrounding region Balduran.
