Cliffgate

Location
nameCliffgate
typeLandmark
regionLower City, Baldur's Gate

The Cliffgate was a landmark located along the southeastern portion of the outer wall of Baldur's Gate. It served as a passageway connecting the Lower City to the isolated district of Tumbledown in the Outer City.

The gate also allowed access to the cliffs that overlooked the northern shore of the River Chionthar, along with the statue known as Balduran Looks out to Sea.
