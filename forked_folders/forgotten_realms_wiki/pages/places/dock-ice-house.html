Dock Ice House

Building
nameDock Ice House
typeCold-storage warehouse
locationSilverscales, Ravens Bluff

The Dock Ice House was a cold-storage business located on the docks of Ravens Bluff within the city's Harbor District, during the 14 century DR. Its operation was critically important to the local fisheries and seafood vendors, such as Flirin's Sea Morsels, as they allowed their goods to be safely transported throughout the city and beyond.

Location

The Ice House was centrally located within the neighborhood's docks, just a ways north of Gulp Lane in the Silverscales neighborhood.
