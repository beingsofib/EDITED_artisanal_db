The Emerald Dolphin was a quality bar located in the city of Iljack, Chondath.

Services

Located near Iljack's Warehouse District, the Emerald Dolphin offered its guests quality and reasonably-priced drinks.

History

At some point before the , Adamar Session, Bard of Starmantle, visited the Emerald Dolphin during his twelve-year adventuring trek across the Reach. The drinking establishment was praised in the bard's guide to the Vilhon Reach.
