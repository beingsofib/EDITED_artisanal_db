Shalass Road

Shalass Road was a small roadway found in western Sembia that connected Daerlun in the north with Urmlaspyr in the south.

Description

Shalass Road originated in Urmlaspyr on the Sea of Fallen Stars, passing northwest from the city to connect with Daerlun on the Way of the Manticore.
