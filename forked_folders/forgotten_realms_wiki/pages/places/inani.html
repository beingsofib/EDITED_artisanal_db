In'ani was a city on the island of Saishu in the kingdom of Koryo in Kara-Tur.

Geography

In'ani stood on the northern tip of Saishu, facing the Yellow Sea.

History

Around 1357 DR, a pride of sea lions harassed shipping around In'ani. Dan Zor, the leader of Saishu, sought adventurers who would destroy the beasts.

Inhabitants

The fishermen of In'ani, along with those of Ansong, were claimed to be the best in the world, having done such things as eliminate the prides of sea lions in the Straits of Koryo and catch the enormous eel Ghartii Nung.
