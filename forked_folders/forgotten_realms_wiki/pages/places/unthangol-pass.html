Unthangol Pass

Unthangol Pass was a mountain pass located in southern Unther. After the Spellplague, the pass became part of the dragonborn nation of Tymanther.

Geography

Unthangol Pass cut through the middle of the Unthangol Mountains, allowing for passage between southern Unther, later Tymanther, and the Shaar.
