The High Harvest Home was a temple to Chauntea, the Grain Goddess, situated in the city of Elturel in the Western Heartlands in the mid–14 century DR.

Circa 1358 DR and through 1367 DR, it was managed by High Priest and Harvestmaster Raulauvin Oregh, with eighteen priests and forty-two followers.

It provided aid and shelter to the faithful.
