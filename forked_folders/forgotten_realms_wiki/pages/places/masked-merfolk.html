The Masked Merfolk was a club in Marsember, Cormyr near to Starwater Keep on Thelvarspike Lane.

Structure

The door of the establishment had a carving of a mermaid and a merman embracing one another.

Staff

The club was known for having doppelganger employees who could be paid for information.

Activities

Typical entertainment consisted of minstrels, acrobats, singers and tasting of rare wines.

Notable Inhabitants

  • Elestra Blaebur

Prices

  • Information sold for 5 sp per message
  • Exotic wine sold for 6 gp per glass
