River Lhair

The River Lhair was a small waterway found in the far southwestern region of Halruaa.

Geography

The River Lhair originated in the southern stretch of the Lhairghal Mountains near the Mhair Jungles. It flowed southeast for approximately before reaching the Great Sea. The town of Lhair was located on its eastern bank where it met the sea.
