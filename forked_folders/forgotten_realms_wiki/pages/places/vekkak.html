Vekkak was an Iulutiun village on the shore of Lake Risuak. Its claim to fame was that its iquemelum (council of elders) was particularly adept at predicting the weather.

Geography

Vekkak was found in Alpuk, northwest of the Lugalpgotak Sea and north of the village of Gotokok.
