Jelzar's Stride

Jelzar's Stride was a cobblestone avenue

Location

Up to the mid-14 century DR, this road stretched from northeast to southwest, extending off of Seaeye's March and terminating at the Lions.

By 1368 DR, Jelzar's Stride had its cobblestone street extended, overtaking where the Lions once stood alongside the Field of Triumph, making it terminate at Julthoon Street. Along the way it was met from the east by Dob's Loss and Gulzindar Street.

Notable Locations

  • The Field of Triumph, a large outdoor stadium, had northern and eastern walls that spanned the entirety of Jelzar's Stride.
  • The House of Heroes, a temple of Tempus that stood on the street's corner with the Lions.
