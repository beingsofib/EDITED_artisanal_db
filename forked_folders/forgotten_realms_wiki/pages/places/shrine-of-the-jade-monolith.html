The Shrine of the Jade Monolith was a temple dedicated to the yikarian Faceless God in 1367 DR.

Location

The temple was located in the city of Lipo along the coast of the Great Sea in the Sun's Eye province of the Yikarian Empire.

Activity

Since the yikarians of Lipo were more tolerant of outsiders compared to others of their race, many pilgrims came to the Shrine of the Jade Monolith with the hopes of having one of the yak-folk plead to the Faceless God on their behalf.
