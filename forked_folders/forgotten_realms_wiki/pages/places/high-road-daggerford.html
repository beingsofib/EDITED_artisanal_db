High Road (Daggerford)

The High Road, which was also called Tanner's Way

Location

The High Road was found in the center of Daggerford in between Duke's Way in the north and the Trade Way in the south. The smaller Rogar Alley also connected to the High Road.

Notable Locations

Notable buildings located along the High Road included:

  • The Grand Playhouse
  • Korbus's Jewelry and Fine Ornaments
  • Nartan's Fine Foods
  • Scribes' Friendship
  • Lady Luck Tavern
  • Kryptgarden Scrolls
  • Merchants' Guild
  • Derval's Bright Blade
