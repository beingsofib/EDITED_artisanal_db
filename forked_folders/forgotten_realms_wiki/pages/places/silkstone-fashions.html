Silkstone Fashions

Building
nameSilkstone Fashions

locationTemple District, Athkatla

Silkstone Fashions was a tailor's shop in Athkatla's rich Temple District in the 14 century DR.

Location

Silkstone Fashions was a large shop on the Godsmoot–the street that ended at the famed The Crown Aflame theater in the Temple District. Meir Lane ran along the shop's eastern side.

Services

The shop offered services of a tailor and a wide selection of fashionable accessories for purchase.
