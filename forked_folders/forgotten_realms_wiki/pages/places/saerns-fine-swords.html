Saern's Fine Swords was a business located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The building stood two stories tall.

Location

Saern's Fine Swords was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward on the east side of the High Road where the Winter Path and Burnt Wagon Way joined.

Services

The shop dealt in weaponry to include the occasional magical variety.
