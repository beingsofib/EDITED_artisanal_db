Brondar's Way

Brondar's Way was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Brondar's Way was located in the central section of Waterdeep's North Ward. The alley ran north to south, branching from Delzorin Street and passing south to end on Hassantyr's Street. Brondar's Street was intersected by Sulmor Street near its midpoint and Thalaumbur's Trot connected with the southern end.
