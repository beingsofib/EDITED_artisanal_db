The Golden Bridle was a caravanserai found in the city of Almorel along the shore of the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands.

Description

The Golden Bridle sat outside of the city walls of Almorel just beyond the western-facing gate. The building was strategically found along the Golden Way where it entered the city.
