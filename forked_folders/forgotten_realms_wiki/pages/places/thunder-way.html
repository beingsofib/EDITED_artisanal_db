Thunder Way

The Thunder Way was a wagon track passing through High Dale, connecting Saerb in Sembia to Thunderstone in Cormyr.

Route

From Thunderstone to Saerb:

  • Thunderstone
  • Twoswords Bridge, where it crosses Baerast's Stream
  • Saerb
