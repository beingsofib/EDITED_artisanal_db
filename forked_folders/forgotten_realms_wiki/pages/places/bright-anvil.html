The Bright Anvil was a small temple dedicated to Gond within the fortified city of Yeshpek in the late 14 century DR.

Activities

Activity in this temple was relatively quiet, but it maintained an impressive degree of steady income through charitable donations of coins that were made by almost all of Yeshpek's craftworkers. The temple divided this income evenly between two different avenues of charity. The first was to the less fortunate, needy and sick of Yeshpek. Their other use of this income was to encourage in the community new designs, inventions, and methods of working.
