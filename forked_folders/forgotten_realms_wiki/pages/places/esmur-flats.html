Esmur Flats

Location
nameEsmur Flats
typeRegion
regionDusk Ports, Laerakond

The Esmur Flats was an area of land in the southwestern lands of Laerakond. It lay between the Dusk Ports and Gontal. Due to the River Esmur, the area was a busy fishing center in the region.
