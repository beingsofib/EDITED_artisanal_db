Dragon Head was a rocky peninsula located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Dragon Head jutted from the southern shore of Gwynneth into the Sea of Swords for approximately . The vast majority of the peninsula was composed of rocky mountainous terrain. The region of the peninsula closest to the mainland was covered by part of the Llyrath Forest.
