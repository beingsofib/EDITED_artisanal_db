Bhaerynden

Location
nameBhaerynden
regionGreat Bhaerynden, Underdark
racesDwarves

Bhaerynden was the first great kingdom of the dwarves of Faerûn, centered in the great cavern deep beneath the Shaar. Bhaerynden fell to the drow after the Crown Wars and the descent of the drow.
