Tower March

Tower March was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Tower March was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's North Ward. It ran in between the High Road at the border with the Sea Ward and the Trollmarch, passing roughly northeast through the ward. Tower March merged with Horn Street near its midpoint and was also intersected by Windborne Way, Thunderstaff Way, Whaelgond Way, Immar Street, and Watch Alley.
