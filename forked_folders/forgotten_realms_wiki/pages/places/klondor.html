Klondor was a town located in the country of Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Geography

Klondor lay along the north shore of the northern branch of the River of Spears in western Mulhorand in the Great Vale at the foot of the Sword Mountains.

Inhabitants

The precept of Klondor in the , was a warrior of Assuran named Haskrayth. A group of mercenaries from Chessenta worked out of Klondor to provide aid against the threat of monsters.
