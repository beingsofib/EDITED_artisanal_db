Silly Satyr

Building
nameSilly Satyr
typeTavern
locationTantras, the Vast

The Silly Satyr was a tavern in the city of Tantras in the Vast. It was located by the western gate out of the city, on the Street of Shadows.

Around 1358 DR, the Silly Satyr was notorious as being very wild and had a bad reputation.
