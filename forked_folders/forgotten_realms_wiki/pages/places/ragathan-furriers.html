Ragathan Furriers was a furrier shop in the North Ward of Waterdeep in the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The shop lay on the eastern end of Tarsar's Street near the intersection with Saerdoun Street.

Structure

The building was a typical two-story Waterdhavian row house.

Inhabitants

Around the , the rooms above Ragathan Furriers were rented by the Netherese sorcerer Telbran Nelarn, who had disguised himself as a quiet book collector.
