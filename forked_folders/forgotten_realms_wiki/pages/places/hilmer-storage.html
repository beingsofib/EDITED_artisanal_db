Hilmer Storage, were two storage warehouses in Waterdeep that were privately owned by Hilmer in the mid–14 century DR.

Location

These two warehouses were located in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep,

Structure

Both warehouses were two stories tall.

Activities

Hilmer used these warehouses to store the extra metals
