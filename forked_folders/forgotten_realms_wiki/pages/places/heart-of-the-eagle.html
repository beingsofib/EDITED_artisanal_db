The Heart of the Eagle was a treasury located within the fortress of Sarahin in the Haunted Lands.

Description

Carved from the rock of the ridge upon which Sarahin stood, the Heart of the Eagle was a tight chamber decorated with several tapestries. Windows provided a splendid view of the river gorge below.

History

One of the tapestries was actually a tapestry of folded existence housing the entire treasure horde of the Everlasting.
