The North Cape was a portion of the island of Norland that jutted into the Sea of Moonshae within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The cape was located on the eastern coast of central Norland. It formed the southern portion of the Bay of Norland.

Description

The land of the North Cape was largely composed of open plains.
