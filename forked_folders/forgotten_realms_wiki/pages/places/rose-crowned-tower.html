The Rose-Crowned Tower was a temple devoted to Lathander, the Morninglord, located in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan in the late 14 century DR.

Location

The temple was located in northeast Suldolphor near the outer city wall.

Description

The Rose-Crowned Tower was small.

Inhabitants

Elurthron Belsurd was the Most High Mornmaster, or high priest, of the temple in the . The clergy within the temple were almost exclusively men.

Activities

Supplicants were allowed to pray within the temple based upon the size of their donation, with larger donations earning them spots higher within the building. Those prayer on the top floor and drinking wine from a holy chalice were believed to be heard directly by Lathander.
