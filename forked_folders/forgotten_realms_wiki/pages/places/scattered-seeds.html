The Scattered Seeds was a small shrine dedicated to Chauntea within Tegal's Mark, the capital of Tasseldale. It was open to open to any who sought shelter.

Description

This marble shrine featured a domed roof made of colored glass. When the sunlight shone through, radiant patterns moved along the floor. The focal point within was a fountain covered with a well-tended garden that spread outward.

History

The temple was only once vandalized in all its history. It was believed to done be followers of Talos that were passing through town.
