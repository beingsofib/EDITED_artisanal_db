Zhaelun's Stockyard

Zhaelun's Stockyard was one of feedlots that kept livestock and poultry in Suzail in the 14 century DR.

Location

Zhaelun's Stockyard stood on the Court Close. The feedlot could be found two city blocks south of the Promenade and two blocks north of the intersection of the Dancing Druid Lane and Silverscales Street.
