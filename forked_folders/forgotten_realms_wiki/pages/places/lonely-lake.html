Lonely Lake

The Lonely Lake was a small oasis found within the Plains of Purple Dust in east Faerûn and the Hordelands.

Geography

The Lonely Lake lay on the eastern side of the Sword Mountains in the southern fringes of Mulhorand on the Plains of Purple Dust north of Fuirgar.

Inhabitants

Descendants of the mighty wizard Nezram held and protected the oasis.
