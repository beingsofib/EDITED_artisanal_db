Rainbow Falls

The Rainbow Falls were located in Ra-Khati.

Geopgraphy

The mighty chasm of the Rainbow Falls was located where the Akundi River plummeted over a thousand feet into the Gogrus River within the Katakoro Shan mountains.

Description

Mist from the waterfalls and prevalent low-hanging clouds regularly created beautiful rainbows but masked much of the mountain walls bordering the falls.

Rumors and Legends

Natives of Ra-Khati viewed any absence of the common rainbows as a bad omen.

Notable Locations

Buildings of the Rainbow Monastery could be found along the descending path along the chasm of Rainbow Falls.
