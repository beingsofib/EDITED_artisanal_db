The Black Sam tavern was a gambling den in the city of Liara within the Domains of Dread.

Structure

It was a rather large building.

Interior

The ground floor was a kitchen and tavern, with numerous booths for privacy. The first floor was a gambling den, and the last floor was Sam's residence.

Atmosphere

The ground floor had a fire pit at the center; the second floor was smoky and poorly lit.

Services

Besides the drinks, there was gambling. Interestingly, the tavern remained open at night, unlike most of Liara's establishments.

Defenses

Sam employed four enforcers, all of them experienced fighters.

History

Though the merchants had fought hard to keep gamblers out of their quarter in Liara, they had met with only limited success. They were able to nip all gambling dens in the bud, but Sam the Nimble somehow managed to establish his own.

At some point in the mid-to-late 14 century DR, adventurers from Ravens Bluff were drawn into the Mists, and came to Liara in Nova Vaasa, where a werespider had been killing people. They were almost certainly impressed into helping discover the killer, suspected to be a vampire; the first victim once they'd accepted might happen while they were at the Black Sam tavern.
