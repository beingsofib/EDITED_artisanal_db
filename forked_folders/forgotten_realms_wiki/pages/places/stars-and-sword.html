The Stars and Sword was a tavern in Westgate during the mid–8 century DR.

Structure

The sign was enspelled to be bright, animated, and sparkly.

Services

There were stables, meals, drinks, and such fare. Additionally, there was a dancer with supernatural powers of prophecy, Alshinree, who sometimes gave directions to hidden magic to passing wizards.

Defenses

People who sought Alshinree outside the tavern met with unfortunate ends.

History

Ilbryn Starym visited the tavern in the , giving a bit of a fright to the locals.
