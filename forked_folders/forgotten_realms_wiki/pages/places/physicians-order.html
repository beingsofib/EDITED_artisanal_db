The Physician's Order was a guildhall in Daggerford that served as a meeting place for the town's apothecaries, healers, and morticians.

Location

The hall was located in the center of town within the Caravan Quarter, quite close to the Clean Chin barber shop/mortuary

Services

Visitors to the Physician's Order were granted access to a number of goods and services. They could purchase medicinal herbs and substances, employ the work of healers and surgeons, and even seek out magical cures to their ailments.
