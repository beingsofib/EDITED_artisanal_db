The Naga Plains are a grouping of tall grasslands located between the Golden Road and the Nagawater, in the nation of Sespech.

Recent History

There are persistant rumors that nagas live among the grasses. Though there has never been a reported attack, travelers to the region still tend to avoid the plains.
