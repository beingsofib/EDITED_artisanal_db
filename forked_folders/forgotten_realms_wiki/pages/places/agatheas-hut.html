Agathea's hut was a hut in the Underdark near the drow city of Sschindylryn—the home of the night hag Agathea.

Description

The hut was built into the end of a cavern, and was peculiarly built in the manner of a wooden house with a thatched roof, resembling a surface structure. The interior was warm and cozy, with a large cage for keeping guests in, and a large kitchen and dining table for serving them.

Geography

The hut was near to Nessaleesa's Garden and the Great Waterfall. The tunnel to the hut was lined with large traps that caught travelers, right up to the front door.
