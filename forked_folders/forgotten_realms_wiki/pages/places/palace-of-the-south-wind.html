Palace of the South Wind

Building
namePalace of the South Wind
typePalace
locationTu Pe, Silla, Koryo

The Palace of the South Wind was a palace in the city of Tu Pe in Koryo around 1357 DR. It stood in the upper-class district, near the eastern wall of the city. It was the official residence of the governor, Sanuk Doyo, who bore the honorary title "Lord of the South Wind".
