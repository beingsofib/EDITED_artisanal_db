The cultist base was a secret lair of the Lords of Dust on the world of Eberron devoted to the Spinner of Shadows.

Location

The hidden base was found in the Harbor district of Stormreach, amid other shops and warehouses on the waterfront.

Structure

On the ground level, the hideout was essentially a repurposed warehouse, filled with supply crates and makeshift beds to house the cultists, and several hidden demonic shrines. On the lower levels it took on a different architectural style and became a dark and forbidding underground complex, culminating in a temple devoted to the Spinner of Shadows.

History

Around the time of the Darkening of Faerûn, the rakshasa Gnomon infiltrated the Church of the Silver Flame and used his position as an Inquisitor to both spy on the church and set them upon false and destructive investigations. He eventually came across information regarding the Spinner's Prison they had hidden in Khyber, and devised a plan to break it open and free the demonic Overlord within. Joining his forces with that of Karas, the cult assembled in the hidden base and worked on a great ritual that would blast open a hole in the surface of the city and open up a way to the prison.

At the last moment, Brother Dectaran found the cult and organized a raid upon it. Gnomon attempted to betray and delay the attacking forces, but they managed to fight their way through the cultists and entered the lower levels. When they reached the temple at the bottom of the hideout they slew Gnomon and Karas, only to find that their deaths had been the final ingredient for fueling the ritual. The remaining cultists charged into the breach and entered the road to Tarath Marad, breaking through the defenses of the prison while sacrificing themselves to slow the pursuing Silver Flame forces.

Inhabitants

A number of rakshasa led the cult, including Gnomon and Tahmael. They were joined by mortal cultists like Karas and Marideth, a number of attack dogs including Bastion, and some jarilith demons. The lower levels were also guarded by fierce death knights devoted to the cult.
