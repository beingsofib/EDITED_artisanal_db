Buftooroo's Dry Goods was a shop located in the Harbor District of Ravens Bluff during the late 14 century DR. It was somewhat hidden, tucked into the southwest corner of Fishleap Lane in the Silverscales neighborhood of the city. Buftooroo's specialized in selling everyday necessities.
