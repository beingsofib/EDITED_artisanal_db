The Shipwreck's Coast was a narrow peninsula and a part of the Sword Coast.

Geography

Shipwreck's Coast was located to the west from High Hedge.

Flora & Fauna

The area along the coast was inhabited by ogres, half-ogres, and ogrillons, as well as small groups of kobolds.

The local forests were home to carrion crawlers and different types of wolves, including the dire and the dread ones.

Ghasts and ghouls were spotted from time to time lurking among the shipwrecks. Some travelers mentioned meeting sirines close to the abandoned vessels.

Inhabitants

  • Droth, an ogre mage.
  • Shoal, a nereid enslaved by Droth.
  • Arcand, a sage whose ship crashed on the Shipwreck's Coast.
  • The Surgeon, a healer and Davaeorn's brother.
