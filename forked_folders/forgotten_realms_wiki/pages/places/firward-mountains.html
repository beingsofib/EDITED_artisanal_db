Firward Mountains

The Firward Mountains was a small mountain range found in the nation of Narfell.

Geography

The mountain range sat in southeastern Narfell just north of the shores of Lake Ashane, wedged in between the larger Dunwood to the west and the smaller Erech Forest to the east. The collection of small lakes known as the Teardrops sat to the north of the Firward Mountains, and Helena Gap separated the mountains from the fringes of the Dunwood.
