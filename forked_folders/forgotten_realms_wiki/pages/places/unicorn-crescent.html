The Unicorn & Crescent was an inn located in Evereska.

Description

This inn welcomed many goodly organizations, such as Harpers, the Heralds of Faerûn, and even the Chosen of Mystra.

History

In the 1360s DR, Volothamp Geddarm could not visit such an establishment, and thus could not give it score it on his famed rating system.
