Cantrev Brannoch was a small town located on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Brannoch was located in eastern Snowdown along the coast of the Sea of Swords. Brannoch was unique among Snowdown's settlments in that it was the only one not connected to Snowdown Ride. This was largely because the marsh of Maloren Sods separated the town from the rest of the island.

History

During the height of the Kingdom of Snowdown, Brannoch was one of the larger and more influential cantrevs in regards to influencing the monarch's policies.

Notable Locations

  • Broadleaf Lodge, a bardic college outside of town.

Inhabitants

Brannoch was a Ffolk settlement.
