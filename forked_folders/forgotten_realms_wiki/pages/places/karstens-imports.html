Karsten's Imports was a business that operated in the city of Luskan in the late 15 century DR.

Location

Karsten's Imports was located in the Market District of Luskan, right next to the northern gate.

Structure

Karsten's Imports was a sparsely decorated single room with a table and two chairs.

History

Around the , the Champion of the Burning Dawn encountered Ridrien Karsten here.
