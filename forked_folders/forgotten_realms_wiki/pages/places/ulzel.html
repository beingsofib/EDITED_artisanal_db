Ulzel was a town located in the country of Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Geography

Ulzel was found within the Great Vale on the Great South Road in central Mulhorand along the southern arm of the southern tributary of the River of Spears.

Inhabitants

The precept of Ulzel in the , was a priest of Isis named Nessisi.
