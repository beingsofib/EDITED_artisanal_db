The Thayan Embassy was an Thayan enclave located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The building stood four stories tall.

Location

The Thayan Embassy was located in the northwest section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward at the intersection of Trader's Way, the Sutherlane, Mendever Street, Heard Lane, and Bazaar Street.

Services

Various suits of armor, weapons, magic items, potions, and scrolls could be purchased here.
