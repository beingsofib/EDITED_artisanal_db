Abbai Abbathor was a dwarven temple dedicated to the Morndinsamman deity of greed, Abbathor, in the independent city of Teziir during the 14 century DR.

Description

The holy house was actually a series of short, stone buildings of dwarven architecture, with tall, spiked iron fences.

Within the abbai was a trading post where the dwarves dealt their precious gems and magic items. The friendly and amiable priests of the Trove Lord were also unscrupulous merchants, who preyed upon the gullible and naive patrons who visited their mercantile house of worship.
