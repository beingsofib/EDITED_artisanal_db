The Pipe & Unicorn was an enormous inn found in the city of Myth Drannor. It was run by the energetic master brewer, Vulph Giftankard.

Location

The inn was located on the Street of the Evening Star, in the district of Dlabraddath on the eastern side of the city.

Structure

Constructed at ground level and extending upward three stories, the Pipe & Unicorn was a massive boarding house. It could provide accommodations for nearly one hundred people, with more than enough drinks from its great brewery located in the cellar.

Services

The Pipe & Unicorn brewed a unique ale that shared the name of the inn itself. The distinct and robust scent of the brew permeated not just the taproom but the rest of the inn as well.
