Dharvil was a small settlement found in Sossal in the Cold Lands of northeast Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Geography

Sundice was located in western Sossal along the southeastern foothills of the Armridge Mountains. Dharvil was connected to Sundice in the east and Shannath in the west by an unnamed road.
