Bay of Balefires

The Bay of Balefires was a small inlet found within the Lake of Steam of south Faerûn.

Geography

The bay sat along the southwestern corner of the Lake of Steam near Talagath's Gauntlet.

Description

The bay was relatively shallow and muddy. Large plumes of smoke rose from the sulphorous waters several times a year courtesy of underwater volcanic activity, giving the bay its name.

Notable Locations

The only notable location along the Bay of Balefires was the town of Tulmon.
