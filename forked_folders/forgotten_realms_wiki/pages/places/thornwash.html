Thornwash

The Thornwash, also called the Thornwash River, was a small river located in the Vilhon Reach and Lake of Steam region.

Geography

The Thornwash originated in the western branch of the Cloven Mountains as well as in the heart of the Winterwood. It flowed south for approximately before emptying into the Lake of Steam.
