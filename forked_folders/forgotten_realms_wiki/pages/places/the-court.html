The Court

The Court was one of the divine realms of Tyr, the God of Law.

Description

The Court included a massive marble hall from which Tyr ruled his realm much like a court of law. It was impossible to lie in Tyr's realm.

Location

The Court was on the summit of one of three great mountains surrounding the base of Mount Celestia, making it—from the point of view of the Great Wheel cosmology—a part of Lunia or—from the point of view of the World Tree cosmology—a part of the House of the Triad. The realm was adjacent to that of Siamorphe, whose Alabaster Palace was located in a lush vale between the Court and Celestia.
