Tharos was a kingdom that existed within the Great Dale from to .

History

Tharos was established when Nentyarch Thargaun Crell of the Crell Dynasty rose to power, naming the kingdom in honor of his father Tharos Crell. The fortress, and seat of power, of Dun-Tharos was established within the kingdom in . Tharos later became Narfell when Thargaun assimilated all of the surrounding Nar tribes in -946 DR.
