Big Edna's was a cheap bar in the city of Westgate in 1367 DR.

Location

The bar was found in the northwest section of the city near Mulsantir’s Gate along a branch of the King's March.

Activities

Big Edna's was a tiny ramshackle establishment that catered to fishermen and dock workers who appreciated the silence.
