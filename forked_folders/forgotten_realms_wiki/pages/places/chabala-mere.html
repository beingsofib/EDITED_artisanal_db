Chabala Mere

Location/Body of water
nameChabala Mere
typeLake
regionSildëyuir, Aglarond

The Chabala Mere was a body of water in Sildëyuir on top of which sat the Causeway of Stardeep. It was fed by the Chabala River. The water was nearly freezing, and the local star elves weren't sure it had a bottom. When the land-bridge was closed by the magical defenses in Stardeep, the Chabala Mere became covered in mist.
