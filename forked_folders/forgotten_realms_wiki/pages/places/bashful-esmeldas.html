Bashful Esmelda's

Bashful Esmelda's was a festhall and tavern in the Amnian city of Esmeltaran during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The festhall was built in northern Esmeltaran.

Services

The place provided fairly good services at cheap prices. Many male customers were lured in by the illusion-enhanced signpost that showed the indistinct form of a woman bathing in a lake.
