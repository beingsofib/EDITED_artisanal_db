Fhaoralusyolkiir () was an Aryselmalyn garrison city, a primary garrison for the empire's capital city Coryselmal, that stood for centuries until eventually being abandoned and left to ruins in -80 DR. In the late 6 century DR, members of the Church of Deep Sashelas moved into what remained of Fhaoralusyolkiir, repurposing it into a temple to Deep Sashelas.

Geography

This city was nestled along and built into the subsea cliffs of Turmish, between Alaghôn and the Aprhunn Mountain, Its city gates and grandiose guardposts, with gleaming tridents made of blaze coral, were set into the edge of the underwater valley of the Vilhon Reach.

Inhabitants

By 1371 DR, about two hundred sea elves lived in Fhaoralusyolkiir, though most were tied to its temple.

History

Fhaoralusyolkiir was founded in the year . It would continue to stand following Aryselmalyn Empire's demise not becoming entirely abandoned and left to ruin until . As the years passed, much of the city built into the cliffside would be buried by collapsing caverns.

In the , the Church of Deep Sashelas took up residence in the palace once lived in by the garrison city's commanders, turning it into a temple to their god. This state of affairs would continue well into the 14 century DR, with the city still retaining some vestiges of its grandeur.
