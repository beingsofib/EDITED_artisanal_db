Weeping Princess Pool

Location
nameWeeping Princess Pool
regionCrimmor, Zehoarastria, Amn

The Weeping Princess Pool was a rainwater pool located near the city of Crimmor in Amn.

Description

The pool was located four hills southwest of the city. Its fancy name was a marketing ploy used by local merchants, who sold its water to snobbish folks.

Trade

of clearwater from the pool was sold for about 6 gp in ornate, stoppered flagons.
