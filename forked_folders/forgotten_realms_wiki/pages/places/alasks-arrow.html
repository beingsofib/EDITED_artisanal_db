Alask's Arrow was an inn and tavern located in Daerlun, Sembia, circa the . It was owned and operated by Alask "Eagle-eyes" who, before he retired, held the reputation as the best archer in the lands around the Dragon Reach.

Services

The typical price for room and board was 1 gp per person per night. Guests could expect to be charged twice or thrice this much if they caused damage or got violent.
