Winter Edge was a hamlet in the Silver Marches of northwest Faerûn.

Geography

The hamlet lay west of the Moonwood and east of the River Surbrin, near to Mithral Hall where it lay in the Frost Hills.

History

Winter Edge gained importance in the autumn of the after King Obould's orc army threatened the dwarves of Mithral Hall. Their allies at Citadel Felbarr marched an army to Winter Edge to aid them, making camp there, and King Bruenor Battlehammer planned to link up with them at the Subrin. Later, in a meeting with Bruenor, Alustriel Silverhand of Silverymoon declared to turn the settlement into a military encampment and channel supplies and soldiers through Silverymoon and Winter's Edge to aid Mithral Hall, and to have Silvermoon's forces relieve Felbarr's.

During the War of the Silver Marches in the , the town came under attack by orcs from the Spine of the World.
