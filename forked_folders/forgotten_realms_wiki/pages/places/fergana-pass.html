Fergana Pass

Location
nameFergana Pass
typeMountain pass
areaTeyla Shan, the Hordelands

Fergana Pass was a well-used route through the northern reaches of Teyla Shan in the Hordelands.

Geography

Caravans heading north from Semphar into the vast steppes often chose this route since Iris Well Spring was found within a short distance where the pass exited the mountains.
