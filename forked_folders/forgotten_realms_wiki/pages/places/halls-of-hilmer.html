The Halls of Hilmer was an armorer's shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The shop stood a single story tall.

Location

The Halls of Hilmer was located in the southeast section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward on the west side of the Street of Bells south of Buckle Alley. The rear of the shop faced Duir's Alley.

Services

Magical and normal armor could be acquired within the shop.
