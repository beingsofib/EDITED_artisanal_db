The Ten Tapestries Chamber was a room in the palace of Silverymoon during the 14 century DR.

Location

The hall was within the palace of Silverymoon.

Interior

Besides the entrance, there were four doors on it, each leading to a different room for lodging. There was a secret passage on one wall, at least two cloak stands, and ten tapestries, presumably giving the room its name.

History

During the , the trader Auvrurn Labraster took one of the rooms reachable through the Ten Tapestries Chamber as his lodgings throughout the stay. After Alustriel Silverhand challenged him, he quickly called in an umber hulk and several red wizards; their battle with Alustriel quickly turned the room to so much debris.
