Aladiam's Imports was a shop in Tegal's Mark that sold spices and silks imported from Sembia during the mid-14 century DR. The establishment was run by a retired Calishite adventurer named Aladiam. He was a known smuggler and fence

The shop was defended by some sort of invisible guardian. On one occasion it ripped through three Sembians who confronted Aladiam about some stolen goods.
