Maloren Sods was a swamp located on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Maloren Sods was found on the eastern side of the island separating the coastal town of Brannoch from the rest of the island. The Maloren Sods covered approximately of the island.

A winding and muddy road ran through the Sods from Brannoch in the east to Harloch in the west.
