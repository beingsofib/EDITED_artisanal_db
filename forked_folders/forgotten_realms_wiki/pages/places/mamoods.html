Mamood's was a large tent located in Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Location

Mamood's could be found in the Pilgrims' District of the City of Delights.

Inhabitants

Mamood's was owned and operated by Mamood. He offered the tent as a place to sleep for pilgrim's visiting the city for a mere one bit per night. Several mercenaries stood guard to allow his guests to sleep soundly.
