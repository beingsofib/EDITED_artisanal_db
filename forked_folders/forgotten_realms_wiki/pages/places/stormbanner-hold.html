Stormbanner Hold, also known as Rogarsheim, was a stronghold at the Northlander capital of Rogarsheim on the island of Norland in the Moonshae Isles. It was the seat of power for the Kingdom of Norland.

Structure

Stormbanner Hold was a huge, rectangular wooden lodge that was constructed around a previously existing circular stone tower of Ffolk construction. The walls were made of sturdy timbers, and averaged three tree trunks in thickness crossed by perpendicular logs for reinforced strength. From the exterior, these walls were intricately decorated with ornamental carvings, most of which depicted the heads of bears, boars, walruses, wolves, and other fearsome beasts. It had a high, peaked roof. It was an overall magnificent structure that clearly served as a mighty fortress and a royal abode.

Interior

Visitors to the lodge were greeted by the hearth.
