Enclave Architectural was an architectural design company that operated in (and built much of) the enclave of Eileanar.

Services

The company was a collection of some of the finest architects in the city, and they were responsible for the construction, expansion, and remodelling of the complex and outlandish magically-enabled structures in the city. In addition to drawing up the initial plans, the experts of Enclave Architectural also worked directly with the builders and personally oversaw the work process so as to ensure it was done accurately and efficiently.
