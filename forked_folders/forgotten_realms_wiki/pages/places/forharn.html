Forharn was an underground city of gnomes found within the White Peaks mountain range of the Moonsea North.

Geography

The city was hidden within the deepest depths beneath Kossuth's Ire, a volcano found among the eastern peaks of the mountains.

It was one of the many settlements of gnomes linked by the magical transportation network known as the Trail of Mists.

Government

Forharn was governed by the Hammers of Flandal, a council that comprised city elders and experienced craftspeople.

The city's sigil was an upturned hammer set over a mountain.

History

In the , Kossuth's Ire erupted after a long period of dormancy. While the gnomes of Forharn were fortunate that their home was not damaged during the eruption, passages to their underground home were cut off by magma flows for a time.

Inhabitants

In addition to its gnomish residents, Forharn was home to two dragons that were raised and cared for by the city's clergy, an adult red dragon named Rauklarnemis and an elder blue dragon named Trakaulorr.
