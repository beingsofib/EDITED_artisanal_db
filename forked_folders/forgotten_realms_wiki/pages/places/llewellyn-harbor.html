Llewellyn Harbor

Llewellyn Harbor was a small natural bay located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Llewellyn Harbor was located along the southwestern coast of Alaron on the Strait of Alaron.

Notable Locations

The town of Llewellyn was the only settlement found along Llewellyn Harbor.
