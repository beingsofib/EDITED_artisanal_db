Pelmuth's Draw

Pelmuth's Draw was the name given to an alley in Scornubel in the 14 century DR.

Location

Pelmuth's Draw branched off from Delsart's Drive. It led to a courtyard, where one of the doors was blue, leading to a warehouse.

History

In the , Dove Falconhand went through that street when looking for the trader Sarltan during her investigation of a drow infiltration on that year.
