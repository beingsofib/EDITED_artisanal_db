Selmalyr was an ancient sea elf kingdom in Serôs, the realms beneath the Sea of Fallen Stars, that gave rise to the Aryselmalyr Empire in or 1 AC (the first year in the Aryselmalyn calendar).

Geography

This kingdom was located in the Selmal Basin portion of the Inner Sea,

History

Circa , a number of rogue marids of the Calim Empire trespassed into Selmalyr, gradually sparking a war between them and the sea elves of the Selmal Basin.

Notable Locations

  • Coryselmal, one of the grandest cities of Selmalyr and the future capital of Aryselmalyr.
