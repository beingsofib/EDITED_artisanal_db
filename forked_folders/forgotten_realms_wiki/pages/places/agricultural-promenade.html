Agricultural Promenade

The Agricultural Promenade, or Farmers' Road, was located in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Description

The Farmers' Road separated the Clothiers' District and Peasants' District, branching off from the Road to Enlightenment near the Gulf Gate to run north roughly parallel to Clothiers' Way before terminating at the Grand Bazaar in the Merchants' District.
