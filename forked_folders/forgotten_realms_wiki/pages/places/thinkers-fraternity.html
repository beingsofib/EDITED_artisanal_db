Thinkers' Fraternity

Building
nameThinkers' Fraternity
typeGuildhall
locationHigh Road, Daggerford

The Thinkers' Fraternity was the guildhall for the scholars and theorists in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The guildhall was located in central Daggerford along the High Road near the drill field and Trade Way.
