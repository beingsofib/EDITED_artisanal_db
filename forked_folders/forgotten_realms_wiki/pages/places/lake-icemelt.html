Lake Icemelt

Lake Icemelt was found high in the peaks of the Earthspur Mountains in southern Damara.

Geography

The lake was found near the High Pass toward the middle of the mountain range just south of the Earthwood. The River Icehilt flowed from the lake's southern shore into Impiltur.
