The Swordmaiden was one of the colossal Walking Statues of Waterdeep and its remains could be found in the North Ward of Waterdeep.

Description

This statue resembled a female version of the Honorable Knight walking statue.

Locations

By 1492 DR, the statue's head was a shop known as Thort's Findings, located in a city square between Hassantyr's Street, Tarsar's Street, Whaelgond Way, and Ussilbran Street.

History

During the Spellplague, in the , the walking statue appeared and rampaged through the city. When stopped, the residents of Waterdeep's North Ward dismantled it and used its pieces for construction.
