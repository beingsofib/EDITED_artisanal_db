Sullen Street

Sullen Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Sullen Street was located in south Zhentil Keep within the Harbor District The road was located on the eastern side of the Harbor District. It originated at the wall to the Common Quarter then passed west to connect with Ocean Way near the docks.

Notable Locations

A Zhentarim warehouse was located along Sullen Street.
