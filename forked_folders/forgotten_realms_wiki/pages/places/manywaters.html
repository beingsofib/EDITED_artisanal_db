Manywaters was a small independent town in the southern Border Kingdoms in the mid–14 century DR.

Geography

The town was located to the southwest of the Grand Duchy of Shantal. Manywaters was so close that the borders of the Grand Duchy lied a mere two hunded and forty paces from the town's walls.

Trade

Manywaters would just be a small, sleepy village if were not for the Riven Rock Pool and the Riven Healing House, a small temple dedicated to Ilmater.
