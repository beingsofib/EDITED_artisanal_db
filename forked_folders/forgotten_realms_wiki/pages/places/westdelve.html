Westdelve was the most westerly hold of the dwarven kingdom of Ammarindar in the 3 century DR.

Geography

Westdelve was at the west end of Ammarindar.

Government

In the early 3 century, the axelord of Westdelve was Arthlach. Axelords at that time were determined to stay in good terms with humans.

Trade

Westdelve dwarves bought food from places along the Delimbiyr, and sold a number of crafted products such as stone coffers, caskets, gems, and a variety of wines such as dragonsblood (a red) and moonbubble wine.

History

On Eleint 11 of the , axelord Arthlach of Westdelve spent a night at Morlin Castle in a feast held by Lord Breiyr.
