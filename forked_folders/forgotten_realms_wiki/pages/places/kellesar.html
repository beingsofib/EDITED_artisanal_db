Kellesar

Location
nameKellesar
typeSettlement
regionUlgarth, southeast Faerûn

Kellesar was a settlement near the kingdom of Ulgarth in southeast Faerûn. It lay at the southern end of Barbarians' Road, which formed the border between Ulgarth and the barbarian lands of the east, amidst the Stony Waste.
