Canthrell was an elongated island within the kingdom of Norheim in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Canthrell was part of the Norheim Isles, located near to Norland. It lay between the Sea of Moonshae on its southeastern shores and the Trackless Sea to the northwest. Canthrell was approximately long, but only wide at its widest point.
