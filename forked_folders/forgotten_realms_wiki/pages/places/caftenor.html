Caftenor was an island within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Caftenor was the southernmost island of any noteworthy size within the Korinn Archipelago, sitting just off the coast of northern Gnarhelm near the Lake of the Leech. It boasted some of the most temperate weather in all of the Archipelago.

The village of Caftenor was located near center of the island on the eastern side of the isle. Several small unnamed islands were found just off the island's coast.

Trade

The island was the most resource-rich of the Korinn Archipelago, and was surrounded by bountiful fishing grounds.

Inhabitants

A large population of halflings made their home on Caftenor in addition to the Northlander human inhabitants.
