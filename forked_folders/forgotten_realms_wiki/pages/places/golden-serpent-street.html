Golden Serpent Street

Golden Serpent Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Golden Serpent Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's North Ward. The road branched west off of Saerdoun Street south of the Cliffwatch, winding through the southern North Ward and curving south to end on Sammarin's Street near the City of the Dead. Along its path Golden Serpent Street was intersected by Nindabar Street, Catchthief Alley, Mhalsymber's Way, Gelthoon Street, Shando Street, Undal's Lane, and Black Cat Way.
