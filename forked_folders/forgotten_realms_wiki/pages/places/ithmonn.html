County Ithmonn was an agricultural region in central Tethyr.

Geography

Ithmonn was located in the Crown Lands north of the River Ith and bordering the Purple Marches in the west.

The county was the agricultural center of Tethyr and was blanketed with crops of all varieties and farms containing all manner of livestock.

Government

The Count of Ithmonn in 1370 DR was Malcor Grannox, who was also the Law Chancellor of Tethyr. He ruled from Loranse, a manor just west of Darromar along the river.
