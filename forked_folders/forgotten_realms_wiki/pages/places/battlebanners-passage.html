Battlebanners Passage was a hallway in Castle Obarskyr.

Description

The passage held a succession of faded trophies along its length. There were doors along its length; at least two doors, one on each side, led to narrow, dimly lit passageways.

History

In the aftermath of the massacre during the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the , the War Wizards Welwyn Tracegar and Yarjack Blamreld used it to go towards Glathra Barcantle; they took no great notice of Mirt and Storm Silverhand moving across the hallway, perpendicular to them.
