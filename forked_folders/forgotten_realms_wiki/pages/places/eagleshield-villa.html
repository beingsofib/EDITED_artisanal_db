Eagleshield Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Eagleshield.

Location

The Eagleshield Villa was located in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep,

Structure

This building complex consisted of two high-quality buildings, one that was four stories tall and another that stood two stories tall, one being the mansion proper and the other a guesthouse. They featured many spires.

Like many Waterdhavian villas, it was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden.
