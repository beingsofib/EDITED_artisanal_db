The Menori Trading Compound

Building
nameThe Menori Trading Compound
typeMarket
locationTu Pe, Silla, Koryo

The Menori Trading Compound was a cooperative market in the city of Tu Pe in Koryo around 1357 DR. It lay in the very center of the city.
