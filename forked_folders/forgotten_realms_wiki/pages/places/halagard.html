Halagard was a city located in central Halruaa.

Description

At 7,500 inhabitants, Halagard sported a large population when compared to other Halruaan cities.

The city was located on the Bay of Halruaa at the mouth of the Halagard Channel.

Inhabitants

Halagard was governed by the wizard Basel Indoulur.

History

Up until the mid 1200's DR, Halagard was the capital city of Halruaa.
