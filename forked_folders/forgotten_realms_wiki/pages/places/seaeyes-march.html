Seaeye's March

Seaeye's March was a partially dirt & gravel paved, partially cobblestoned paved,

Location

This road stretched from northwest to south, extending off of Westwall Street and terminating at Julthoon Street. Along the way, it was the western terminus of Jelzar's Stride.
