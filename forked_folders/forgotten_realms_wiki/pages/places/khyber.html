Khyber was a vast underground region of tunnels and caverns beneath the surface in the world of Eberron, roughly analogous to the Underdark of Toril. It was most notable for being where the fiendish Overlords of Eberron were imprisoned and hidden away so that they could never threaten the world again.

History

Around the time of Faerûn's Darkening in the 1480s DR, the Spinner of Shadows, a powerful Overlord who specialized in deceit and manipulation, orchestrated her escape from Khyber by using the Lords of Dust to break her bonds. She had them blast open a path from the surface in Stormreach into the road to Tarath Marad, and from there the Spinner's Prison was accessed. She had them gather up the dragonshards embedded in the cavern walls and eventually escaped into the Demonweb with Lolth, aiding her plans to seize Mystra's Weave. The local demons were not all helpful to this cause, with the Fire Reaver Lord taking pleasure in hunting the drow cultists.
