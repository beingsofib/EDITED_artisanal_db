The Daffyd Heights, also known as the Llyrath Highlands, was a rugged ridge of mountains rising from the middle of the Llyrath Forest on the south side of the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles.

Description

The crest of the mountains was sometimes described as the spine of the Llyrath Forest, with the peaks rising above the trees. The steep climb up the mountains took travelers from the dense oak and pine groves at their base through a region where outcroppings of stone began to outnumber the trees until the steep and invisible trails upward began to follow the winding crest of the mountains. Here the craggy path was barely wide enough for horses to travel in single file and sheer precipices dropped away on either side, making the journey treacherous and leaving travelers exposed and visible from miles away. The path would sometimes dip between rocky peaks, creating narrow notches lined with clumps of rock and small pines that were ideal for ambushes.

Geography

The mountains dominated the eastern reaches of the Llyrath Forest. At the base of the foothills were sheltered and pleasant vales.

History

When Kazgaroth returned to the Moonshae Isles in the , he emboldened the firbolgs of Gwynneth, promoting a group to establish themselves in the Daffyd Heights from where they attacked travelers within the forest. They were discovered and tracked by a hunting party led by Tristan Kendrick, but the firbolgs would ambush the hunters in the highlands: they killed Tristan's protector, Arlen, and forced the remaining hunters to flee.

Inhabitants

Some isolated cottages of lumberjacks and their families could be found around the base of the mountains.
