The Camel's Hump was a large island found in the Corsair Domains of northwest Zakhara around 1367 DR. The island was part of an archipelago called the Desert's Virtues.

This island was named for its gradual elevation which rose to a peak that resembled a camel's hump.

Geography

The Camel's Hump was located approximately due west of the Island of Firethorns. The island itself measured in length and in width. An elevated northeast to southwest swath of arid land split the forested coasts of the island. The town of Ravenwing sat on the island's western coast.
