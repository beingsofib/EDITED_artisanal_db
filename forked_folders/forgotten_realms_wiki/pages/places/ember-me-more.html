Ember Me More was a specialty shop within Swordspoint in the city of Ravens Bluff that specialized in any and all items that were related to fireplace use and maintenance. The store was run by the Sweep brothers, Ashley and Woodruff, who built, repaired, and maintained fireplaces throughout the city.

Services

The Sweeps had a great selection of fire screens, grates, bellows, pokers, tongs, coal shovels, available in brass, bronze, or iron, as well as other implements such as brooms, bellows, tinderboxes, and strikers. They also sold slow-burning logs to provide longer-lasting warmth and comfort for their customers.
