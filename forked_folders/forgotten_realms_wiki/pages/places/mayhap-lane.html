Mayhap Lane

The Hunt Bide was a short street in Suzail, the capital of Cormyr, that stretched from the Promenade to Doloph Street.

Description

The The Six Candles stood on the Hunt Bide's northern end that merged into Suzail's grand Promenade. From there, the Bide arched north as it moved west, passing Windever Street and ending on Doloph Street, within an alleyway from the Trueshield Trading Priakos yards on Suzail's western city wall.
