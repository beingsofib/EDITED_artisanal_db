Shamph, also known as the Crossroads City, was a city in the nation of Chondath in the Vilhon Reach. Merchants from the south stopped at Shamph to sell their goods to avoid the war zone between the cities of Iljak and Hlath.

Government

The city was ruled fairly and justly by mayor Tian Redown.

Geography

Shamph sat at the junction of the Emerald Way and the Old Road.

History

Shamph was destroyed during the Spellplague.
