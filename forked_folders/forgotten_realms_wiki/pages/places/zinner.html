Zinner was a small, quiet village in southern Amn.

Geography

The village lay on the South Road on the west side of the Hyrzashyr river crossing, south of Lake Esmel, between the Small Teeth and Tejarn Hills. It lay across the river from the Citadel of the Banner Raised Anew, and was about halfway between Hillfort Ishla and Hillfort Torbold.

Trade

Zinner was a fishing village and busy trade post on the way between Amn and Tethyr. They kept a good relationship with the people of Brost.
