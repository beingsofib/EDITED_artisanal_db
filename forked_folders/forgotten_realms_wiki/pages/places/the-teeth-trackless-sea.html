"The Teeth" was the same given to a tiny cluster of islets in the middle of the Trackless Sea.

Geography

The isles were located about midway between the Nelanther Isles to the east and Evermeet to the west. They lay south of the Gullrocks.

History

Circa −1000 DR, the Teeth, along with the Gull Rocks, Gundarlun, Mintarn, the Purple Rocks, Tuern, Umukek, the Wave Rocks, and the Whalebones, were colonized by the Illuskan people of Ruathym, later becoming known as the Northmen.

As of the mid-to-late 14 century DR, the Teeth fell along the westernmost reaches of the territory of the white dragon Arveiaturace.

Notable Locations

  • Barth, the tallest of "the Teeth".
