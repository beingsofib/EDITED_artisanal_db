The People's Quarter was one of the four quarters of Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Description

The People's Quarter encompassed the Peasants' District, Pilgrims' District, and South District. The People's Quarter was the religious epicenter of Huzuz, as well as the entire Land of Fate. Pilgrims from all over the continent traveled here to worship in the historical Golden Mosque.
