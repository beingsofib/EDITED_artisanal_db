Zabbasz was a small town located in the nation of Estagund in the Shining Lands during the 14 century DR.

Geography

Zabbasaz was a coastal town located in southern Estagund on the coast of the Great Sea. The town sat directly southeast of Klionna and east of the Bay of Kings.
