The Wet Wizard was a tavern in the town of Llorkh in the 1360s DR.

History

It opened some time after the Zhentarim takeover of Llorkh in 1357 DR. Townsfolk were pressed into the construction of two new but ugly taverns. The Wet Wizard was in business by 1366 DR and through 1370 DR.

Description

The travelogue writer Volothamp Geddarm presumed it to be a pretty poor place, but hadn't been able to visit to judge.

Lord Geildarr, a mage and ruler of Llorkh, was uncertain if the name "Wet Wizard" was a mocking reference to him or not.
