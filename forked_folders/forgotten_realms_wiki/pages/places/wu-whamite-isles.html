Wu (Whamite Isles)

Location
nameWu
regionWhamite Isles, the Sea of Fallen Stars

Wu was a tiny island nation of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. Along with its neighbors Chi and Chu, Wu sent privateers as mercenaries to participate in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles In these wars, Wu was at its strongest when it allied with Chi and Chu.
