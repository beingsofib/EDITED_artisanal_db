Setting Sun Street

Setting Sun Street was a street in Luskan.

Geography

The street ran southwest from the city walls to the market (where it turned south and continued as Aldever's Street).

Notable Locations

  • The Needle, a water tower was on the north side of this street.
  • The Seven Sails Inn, the only inn in the city, was on the south side of this street two blocks east of the Needle.
  • The Winter Palace, a temple to Auril, was on the north side of the street close to the walls.

History

Setting Sun Street was the site of "wet parades", religious spectacles held by the Church of Auril that saw supplicants wearing clothes packed with ice process down the streets.
