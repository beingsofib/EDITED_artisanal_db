Caer Westphal was a fortified castle that overlooked the city of the same name on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Caer Westphal was located on the southern coast on Snowdown Ride along the Sea of Swords

Structure

The castle was a stone fortress similar in layout to Caer Callidyrr on a smaller scale, with high walls and many towers. A sealed dungeon was located beneath the castle.

History

Caer Westphal was the only true castle on Snowdown, and was the seat of the monarchs of the Kingdom of Snowdown. During the reign of King Pwylloch in the mid–14 century DR, he maintained one of the biggest hoards of wealth in the Moonshaes beneath the castle, estimated to contain at least a quarter-million gold pieces.

Inhabitants

Following the annexation of Snowdown by Amn in the early 15 century DR, the remaining members of the Kincaid royal family were kept imprisoned within the castle.
