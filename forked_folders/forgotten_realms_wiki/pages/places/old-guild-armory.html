Old Guild Armory was a small shop that dealt in arms and armor in the city of Phlan of the Moonsea region in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

History

As of the , the Old Guild Armory could be found in the western part of Phan, not far from the entrance to the eastern part of the city through the Parkside Gate. The shop's inventory included a vast array of mundane weapons and armor, including some clothing items, such as boots, cloaks, and robes.
