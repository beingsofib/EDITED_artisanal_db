Tymora's Holy Hall of Good Fortune and Casino was a gambling establishment located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR before that city's destruction. The hall also served as a shrine to Tymora, the goddess of luck.

Location

The casino was located in Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter in the section of the city located on the south bank of the River Tesh. It was located near the center of the quarter along Zhent Avenue.

Activities

Rare among the various gambling dens of Zhentil Keep, Tymora's Holy Hall of Good Fortune and Casino always played fair. The business did not extend any lines of credit and any payouts were usually modest.

Lucklady Demetria Fortunato ran the temple and casino as of the .

Defenses

The temple was warded with protective spells including protection from evil and protection from undead. A werebear named Graddis monitored the halls for security.
