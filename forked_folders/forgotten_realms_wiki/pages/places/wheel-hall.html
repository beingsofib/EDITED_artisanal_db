Wheel Hall was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Wheelwrights' Guild.

Location

Wheel Hall was located along River Street, in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This guildhall stood two-stories tall.

Reputation

Like other guildhalls in the Trades Ward, Wheel Hall was considered by locals to be a landmark.
