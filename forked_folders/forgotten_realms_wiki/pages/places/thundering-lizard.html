The Thundering Lizard was a notable inn and tavern in Port Nyanzaru, Chult. Visiting adventurers were often referred there if they wanted a place to go for a raucous time.

Location

The inn occupied a street corner in Port Nyanzaru's Market Ward near the Red Bazaar on the slope of Mount Sibasa.

Structure

The Thundering Lizard was recognizable by the tables and benches spilling out onto the street, and by the painted tyrannosaurus skull that hung above the main entrance.

Interior

The tavern had low, ceilings that were stained with tobacco and a floor coated in sawdust. It was well-lit with oil lamps A kitchen and pantry sat in a backroom behind the bar, with a staircase leading down into a dimly lit cellar supported by four columns and filled with numbered casks.

The simple guestrooms had sawdust floors.

Atmosphere

The Thundering Lizard attracted a raucous and rough clientele seeking hard drinks, bar fights, and sex. It was nevertheless an inviting place, full of loud laughter and off-key music. It smelled faintly of vomit and stale beer.

Services

In addition to tavern services, the Thundering Lizard offered rooms for five silver pieces per night.

History

At some point in the late 15 century DR, a smuggler named Pelican Jake stole a cursed treasure from the dreaded pirate Idrianne Smoke. Terrified of retribution, he hid it behind a secret door inside the Thundering Lizard's cellar. When Smoke's infernal forces stormed the Market Ward years later to seek Jake and the treasure, one of her champions—a minotaur known as Captain Walharrow—violently seized control of the inn.

During the events of the death curse, the Thundering Lizard was a favorite drinking and meeting spot for the Harper spymaster known as Soggy Wren.
