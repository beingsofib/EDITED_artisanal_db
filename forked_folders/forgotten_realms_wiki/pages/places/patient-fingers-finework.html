Patient Fingers Finework was a woodcraft shop in the city of Waterdeep that was owned by Dannath Lisosar in the 14 century DR.

Location

This shop was located along Sleepers' Walk,

Structure

Patient Fingers Finework was a two-story-tall building.

Services

This business sold both wood carvings and wooden toys.
