Sleighs and Skis was a shop found in the capital city of Immilmar in Rashemen in the .

Location

The shop was located near the center of Immilmar southeast of the Huhrong's Citadel.

Activities

Dana Grayrunner was the shop's proprieter in 1367 DR. She often had Tholli Ironweaver craft the runners for her sleighs. Wychlaran commissioned various sleighs and sleds from Dana that were eventually enchanted.
