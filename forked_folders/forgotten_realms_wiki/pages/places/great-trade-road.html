Great Trade Road

The Great Trade Road was a trail in Anauroch.

Description

The road headed west from Bitter Well Oasis, and continued for roughly five days, maybe a little less, until it reached a pillar at which the trail turned north, towards the Well of the Chasm.

History

At some point around , a number of caravans went missing trying to find another way across the mountains. Those turned west instead of north at the pillar.
