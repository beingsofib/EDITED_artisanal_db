Shark Street

Shark Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Shark Street was located in the western section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. The road passed east to west in between the Street of the Singing Dolphin and Seawatch Street. It was intersected by the Street of Whispers near its midpoint.
