The Tower of the Order was the headquarters and seat of the Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors in Waterdeep.

Location

The Tower was situated in the Castle Ward on the Street of Bells, near the middle of the block between Selduth Street and Buckle Alley.

Description

Stretching four stories tall, As of the 1360s DR, it was considered the most spectacular guildhall in the Castle Ward.

The Tower's cellars were heavily guarded by golems and magical traps.

Services

The Watchful Order provided spellcasting for a fee, Members of the Order could purchase spell components and magic items from the Tower's cellars.

Inhabitants

  • Mhair Szeltune, Lady Master.
  • Orlar Thammas, Speaker for the Order.
