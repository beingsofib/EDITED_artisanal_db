Rocnest

Rocnest was a high bowl-shaped outcrop in the Western Heartlands, standing between Evereska and the Greycloak Hills.

History

A natural citadel, Rocnest was used to garrison elf soldiers during the Crown Wars, for a period lasting a thousand years. Following the fall of Aryvandaar, it was abandoned.

In the late 14 century DR, the Evereskan elves proposed reusing Rocnest as a watch post.

In the , on Nightal 30, Rocnest was lost when forces from Waterdeep led by Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun were too late to turn the tide against the phaerimm in the Battle of Rocnest.
