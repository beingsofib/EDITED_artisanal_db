The Watchful Eye was the only inn in Hluthvar around 1368 DR.

Structure

The building had a roof, shutters, and not much else.

Interior

The rooms were plain and the beds were very hard.

Atmosphere

The Watchful Eye of Hluthvar was a dour place that was at least quiet, almost unnerving, like Volothamp Geddarm stated in Volo's Guide to the Sword Coast, "calm before a vicious storm".

Services

The inn served served simple food and diluted beer.
