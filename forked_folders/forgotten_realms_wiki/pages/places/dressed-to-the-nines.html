Dressed to the Nines was a men's clothing store and haberdashery that was found in Swordspoint neighborhood of the Temple District within the city of Ravens Bluff. They maintained a high exclusivity and the store's owner, the haughty Jordan Whipt, preferred to cater to the nobility of the city.

The store specialized in the sale of custom-ordered uniforms. Many adventurers and rogues would use the store's services as a source of disguises, much to Jordan's irritation.

Description

The store featured a signboard that displayed a Ravenian soldier standing at full attention. The Nines' doorman wore matching attire.
