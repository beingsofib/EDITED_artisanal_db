Llorgh was a large island within the kingdom of Norheim in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Llorgh was located in the southeast region of the Norheim Isles approximately off the coast of Gnarhelm. It was second in size only to Giantspine among the islands of Norheim. Northwind Strait passed between Llorgh and Giantspine. Llorgh was long and about wide on average.
