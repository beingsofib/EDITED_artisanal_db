Harkstag was a village located northeast of Serpent's Cowl during the 13 century DR.

History

The village of Harkstag was known to be the home of the Vigilant Riders, who protected the region against armies of orcs and goblinoids circa 1265 DR. Sages believed that the destruction of the village by a dragon living in the nearby Forest of Wyrms in the was due to it lacking the protective wards of Ss'thar'tiss'ssun.

During the mid-to-late 14 century DR, the ruins of the village served as a hideout for yuan-ti of Najara.

Inhabitants

  • Garshond: A priest of Helm active during the mid–13 century DR, and one of the most prominent Helmite priests of his generation.
