The Seven Masks Theater was a theater in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep that catered to the masses typically found in the neighborhood. Ship captains and sailors were admitted for free. In the , the owner was a man from Shou Lung named Rongquan Mystere but in reality it was the famed rogue Jarlaxle in disguise.

Structure

The building was approximately wide by deep. The auditorium had a ceiling with bare rafters. The lobby, ticket booths, offices, dressing rooms, etc. had ceilings. Passageways between rooms were high with doorways.
