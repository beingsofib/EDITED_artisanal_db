Tormel River

The Tormel River was a waterway found in the land of Thar in the Moonsea North region of north Faerûn.

Geography

The river originated in the body of water referred to as the Bloodmuck, that formed in the wetlands of Thar. It flowed southwest, passing by the city of Melvaunt before emptying into the Moonsea lake.
