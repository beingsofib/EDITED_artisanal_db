Turnell's Provisions was a general store located in Tegal's Mark in Tasseldale during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

It was found on the north side of town, a few buildings over from a shrine dedicated to the goddess Chauntea.

Services

The shop sold general goods and supplies at a 10% markup.
