Iron Trail

The Iron Trail was a snowy route between the Ten Trail and the town of Ironmaster.

Geography

The trail was constantly hit by strong winds as it crossed the hills and the tundra. It almost disappeared in the winter due to snow and there was very little shelter from the cold temperatures.
