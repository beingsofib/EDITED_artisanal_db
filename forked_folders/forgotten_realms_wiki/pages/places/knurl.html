Knurl was one of the twelve moons of Garden in Realmspace.

Description

Like most of Garden's moons, Knurl was larger than its parent, with a diameter of . Knurl was a sphere composed of marble with swirls of color. In places, chunks of the surface were broken, and molten limestone oozed from the core.

History

Some had discovered a way of harvesting the "liquid marble" and molding it into sculptures.
