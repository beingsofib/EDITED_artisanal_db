The Char was a district in the City of Brass dedicated to metalworking businesses.

Description

This walled section held slave barracks, tenements, The smell of molten metal was prevalent throughout the district.

Notable Locations

  • Forge of Manacles, an establishment that was dedicated to making manacles and shackles for the city's slaves.
  • Locksmith's Row, an establishment that was dedicated to making the fasteners for the manacles and shackles used on the city's slaves.
  • Red Wyrm Smelter, an area with several slave-operated workshops that refined the great quantities of ore brought into the city.

Inhabitants

As property of the Sultan, the slaves here worked for the smelters, smithies, and assorted crafts shops.
