Gleskur's Finemetals of Secomber was a metalworking business in Secomber in the 15 century DR.

Services

Gleskur's Finemetals was a dwarf-operated workshop that crafted finely-made products, using metal sourced from secret dwarven suppliers who were presumed to make deliveries at night or underground to avoid detection. It was linked by merchants' map to Samril's Fine Blades in Secomber, who used their braided copper wire for securing the leather hilt wrappings on weapons such longswords.
