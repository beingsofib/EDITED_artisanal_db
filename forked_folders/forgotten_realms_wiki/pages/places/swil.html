Swil was a tiny pirate nation of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. It participated in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Description

Swil was controlled by a group of pirates known as the Swilissaire Swipers. Their numbers included the strong Swil Swillis and the sharp-minded Smee. Their numbers included the Swilissaire Slicers and fleets known as the Squat Squadron and the Second Sail. Swil was also the site of the sturdy Swilissaire Schloss.

Swil shared land borders with Agenais and Wolfenbutte. Both of these borders was marked by a mountain range. Part of its coast overlooked the aptly named Swil Passage.

Although Swil was one of the smaller Whamite nations, it was significant for its naval prowess.
