Helm Rift

Location
nameHelm Rift
typeRift
regionThe Plain of Standing Stones, Anauroch

Helm Rift was one of the many rifts within Anauroch in the Plain of Standing Stones. It was located east of Dry Rift and the isolated peak known as the Tail.
