Otta was a small village located in the kingdom of Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Otta was located in western Gnarhelm near the coast of the Sea of Moonshae. The village sat upon the northern shore of Lake Otta in the leeward shadow of Hardangheight.
