The Sea Towers were twin lighthouses on the islands of Irphong and Nemessor in the Nelanther Isles to mark Asavir's Channel and the Race.

Structure

Of almost identical design and quality construction, the towers stood high and in diameter. Accessed by a simple door, each had winding stair along the inside of the outer wall that gave access to the three levels. The Sea Tower of Nemessor stood atop a cliff on the island's northeastern shore, while the one on Irphong stood on the northern shore at a height of above sea level.

The topmost levels were encased with windows. Inside, the roof was illuminated by a continual light, bright enough to be seen for miles around thanks to their height. This spell was permanent and could not be dimmed or dispelled without magic as strong as a wish.

History

Built by the Shoon Empire during Emperor Shoon IV’s reign, the Sea Towers allowed imperial vessels to travel safely through the Race at night.

The Sea Towers were likely manned to serve as watch posts, but by the mid-to-late 14 century DR they were left empty, with only rotting wooden furniture marking their former occupation. However, their lights still glowed brightly. Nemessor children like to play in the Sea Tower on their island.
