Rimlost was a fortress located on the southern shore of Glaur Lake, on the kingdom of Gontal.

Description

Rimlost was a small trading town and a popular stop for explorers and adventurers seeking a place to use as a base of operations while exploring the Glaur Barrens.

History

While on the world of Abeir, Rimlost was founded in 1385 DR by Prince Nathur Hethkantan to serve as a base of operations during his ill-fated expedition to the Glaur Barrens. It was named in honor of a tower of the same name located in Fimbrul.
