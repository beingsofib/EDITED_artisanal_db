Burnt Wagon Way

Burnt Wagon Way was a corduroy-log paved road in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Geography

This road ran west off of an intersection of Zendulth Street and the Wall Way, then terminated on the High road alongside the Winter Path. Along the way, it acted as the northern terminus of Vellarr's Lane and the Street of the Tusks.

Notable Locations

  • The Citadel of the Arrow.
  • Gondalim's, an inn that stood at the corner of Burnt Wagon Way and the Winter Path's intersection.
  • Hiryam's Cloth Goods.
  • Saern's Fine Swords.

Links to Sewers

An entrance to the sewers of Waterdeep was located at the mouth of a dead-end alley opening that was just south off this street.
