Ashakar's Accessories was a showroom-style shop in the city of Ravens Bluff that sold all manner of small trinkets, accessories, and other unique aquatic tchotchkes during the late 14 century DR.

Location

The shop was located on the southern end of the Front, within the Silverscales neighborhood of Ravens Bluff's Harbor District.

Interior

The showroom of the business featured quality decor and dim lighting.

Services

Ashakar's catered to discerning travelers from throughout Faerûn. It specialized in items found from the depths of the Sea of Fallen Stars and offered many items created by the underwater merfolk. The business maintained steep prices for these wares.

Inhabitants

The proprietor of the business was a rather strikingly-handsome mage named Ashkar.
