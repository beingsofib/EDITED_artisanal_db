Avenue of Wealth

The Avenue of Wealth was located in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Description

This street entered Huzuz through the Gate of Wealth. It served as the border between the East District, Affluent District, and Court District. The Avenue of Wealth ran southeast to northwest through the city until it terminated at the Caravan Boulevard near the middle of Huzuz.
