Mamlukkar, the Slave's Village was a town inside Shoonach, capital complex of the Shoon Imperium.

Geography

Mamlukkar was in western Shoonach, attached to the Imperial City and surrounded by the Fedhiyajar lands. It was a relatively short distance from the port of Agis on the River Agis.

Description

In its time the town was devoted to housing and containing the slave population of Shoonach. Even after thousands of years, ancient dwarven water pumps from High Shanatar continued to pump fresh water to the surface which was then used in fountains. It was walled in with its initial construction, and following the destruction of the city the walls were expanded and extended again to 15 feet high in order to contain the danger within.

History

Mamlukkar was built after when the construction efforts of Shoonach began. It was created alongside the Imperial City, serving as the home for Shoonach's slaves.

In 450 DR Prince Strohm I slew Emperor Amahl Shoon VII and destroyed the city in the Great Fires of Shoonach. Mamlukkar was abandoned, only receiving expanded walls in order to contain a deadly plague that emerged in the slave village, then was carried by its undead victims.

Inhabitants

By the mid-14 century DR, Mamlukkar was still home to large amounts of moaning and rotting undead, carrying a plague that the walls had contained for centuries. It was also home to peculiar remnant's of Shoon's slave-containment measures, such as living walls. The ruins were also inhabited by orc zombies and skeletons from the Black Horde.
