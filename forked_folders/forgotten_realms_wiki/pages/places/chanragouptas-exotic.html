Chanragoupta's Exotic was a clothing and fabrics shop in Eileanar, an enclave of Netheril.

Services

Chanragoupta obtained fine and exotic textiles from around Toril, and even from the reaches of Realmspace on rare occasions via the brave spelljammers of Yeoman's Loft.
