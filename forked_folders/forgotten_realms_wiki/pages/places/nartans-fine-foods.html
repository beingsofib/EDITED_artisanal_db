Nartan's Fine Foods was an upscale market located in the town of Daggerford during the late 14 century DR. It catered to both wealthy Daggerford residents and those visitors who had enough gold to afford Nartan's steep prices.

Location

It was located on the western side of Daggerford's Money Quarter in central Daggerford along the High Road.

Interior

Nartan's shop was protected by a ward that preserved all the food within and prevented it from going bad.

Services

The shop offered a wide variety of high-quality foods and spices from all across Faerûn, as far away as Chult or even the Ice Mountains in the North.
