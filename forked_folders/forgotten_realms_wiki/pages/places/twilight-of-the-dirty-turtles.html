Twilight of the Dirty Turtles

Twilight of the Dirty Turtles is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the Living City organized play program and the final installment of Gang Wars, an informal three-part series of modules. It was published in 2000 and is intended for a party of adventurers of any level.
