Southgate Market Street

Southgate Market Street was a small branched street found in the city of Westgate.

Location

Southgate Market Street was found in the south section of the city. The street passed through the South Gate to connect with Westgate Market Street after branching several times.

Inhabitants

Mintassan, Tersonm and Gondeth, all powerful wizardss, lived on Southgate Market Street.
