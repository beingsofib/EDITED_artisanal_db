Harone Thar was a dragonborn settlement in Relmaur, Laerakond.

Background

Harone Thar was one of the two "mobile" settlements the Relmorn dragonborn always maintained near the city of Rokthorl. Every time the location of Rokthorl changed, the dragonborn also moved Harone Thar to an eastern cavern near the capital's current location.
