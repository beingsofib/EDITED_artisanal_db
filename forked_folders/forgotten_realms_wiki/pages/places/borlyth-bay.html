Borlyth Bay

Borlyth Bay was a small bay south of the Cloakwood on the Sword Coast North.

Description

Borlyth Bay was named after the monster-infested village of Borlyth with had its sheltered anchorage and derelict shipyards on the bay's shores. It was connected to the larger Cloak Bay with Fang forming its western edge. The hamlet of Ausabbason stood on Borlyth Bay's northernmost end, to the south from the rocky plateau that formed the bay's eastern border.
