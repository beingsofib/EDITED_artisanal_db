Rig-A-Mortice was a famous locksmith business in the city of Mirabar sometime in the 14 century DR.

Inhabitants

The business was owned and operated by the dwarf Glengar Silverpalm. His brother, the fence Durdlan Silverpalm, had a share in the business.
