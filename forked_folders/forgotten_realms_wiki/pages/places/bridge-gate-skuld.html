The Bridge Gate was a point of entry into the city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Bridge Gate stood in the southwest corner of the city, separating part of Skuld on the north side of the River of Shadows from the Naval Yard and the small section of the city located on the south side of the river.
