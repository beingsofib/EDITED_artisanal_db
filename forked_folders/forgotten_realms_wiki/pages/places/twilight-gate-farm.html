Twilight Gate Farm was a turnip farm in Sembia.

Description

Twilight Gate Farm was a ramshackle turnip farm. After seemingly being abandoned for a long time, the woods reclaimed the area, and a hunting lodge was erected. The farm also had a fishpond.

History

In the 14 century DR, Gormstadd purchased the allotment.

Inhabitants

Aldamair Galashar was hired as a steward to oversee the area, though he used the lodge to stash stolen goods.
