The Lockup was a prison owned by the Crown of Cormyr and the Purple Dragons of Suzail in the Forest Kingdom of Cormyr.

Location

The Lockup stood just outside the Citadel of the Purple Dragons's gate in the southeastern part of Suzail. It was located on the street that stretched from the Market Hall on the Coachwheels Way and the city's Market Yard.

Description

The prison was a notably unfriendly, almost square stone building that loomed over the street. Its edifice was decorated with carved statues of Purple Dragon Knights. The Lockup was usually used to keep suspects and criminals as they waited for a trial as well as convicted wrongdoers.

Rumors & Legends

  • Some said that Purple Dragon Knight statues could be animated by the Lockup's warden with a single command in case of riots and unrest.
