Palter was a floating city of ancient Netheril. Even among the enclaves of Netheril, the city ruled by archwizard Halavar was a bastion of magical innovation and progress.

After the fall of Netheril in the , the city crash-landed on rocks to the north of the Netheril River. The few survivors journeyed south in search of the other Netherese enclaves, but suffered attacks by orcs, ravenous monsters, and more catastrophes.
