The Inner Audience Chamber was a location within Castle Waterdeep.

Location

The Inner Audience Chamber was within Castle Waterdeep.

Structure

There was at least one hidden tunnel leading out.

History

Mirt the Moneylender visited the Inner Audience Chamber in early Flamerule of to warn Piergeiron that someone was slaying the Lords of Waterdeep, only to find the Open Lord's daughter, Aleena, impersonating him. After she explained Piergeiron had already been attacked and nearly slain, they fled through a secret passage.
