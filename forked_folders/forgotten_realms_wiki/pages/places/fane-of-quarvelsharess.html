The Fane of Quarvelsharess, also called the Fane of the Goddess, was a public cathedral dedicated to Lolth in the district of West Wall in Menzoberranzan.

History

The Fane of the Quarvelsharess was a "gift" of House Baenre, providing, for the first time, a place for the general public to worship. Its construction out of stone was an "epic union of artistry and engineering" that was completed in 1475 DR.

Staff

Sos'Umptu Baenre was a high priestess at the Fane.
