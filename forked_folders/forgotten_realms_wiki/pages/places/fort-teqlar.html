Fort Teqlar was a Tethyrian military outpost established inside the ruins of Shoonach, former capital of the Shoon Imperium.

Geography

Fort Teqlar was on the northwestern edge of Shoonach in the Fedhiyajar, just inside the city's curtain wall west of Iltakar and the gate to Darromar. It counted as being inside County Monteshi and Duchy Ankaram.

Defenses

The fort was staffed by a garrison of 150 Tethyrian soldiers, and three patrols of 25 soldiers that ranged out into the ruins to destroy threats, one in the day and two at night.

History

The fort was restored from a ruined structure after the Tethyrian Interregnum, overseen by Count Oxsal Keeneyes of Monteshi. He named the fort after a comrade killed in the Storm Siege of Myratma, and effectively moved his court there from his actual home at Pelmarin. He saw his primary duty as maintaining the guard over the ruins of Shoonach, and preventing its dangers from emerging and threatening Tethyr, especially the nearby capital of Darromar.
