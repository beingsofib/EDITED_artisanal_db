The Old Beard was a tavern found in the city of Westgate in 1367 DR.

Location

The tavern was located on Eastgate Street in the far eastern section of the city along the shore of the River Thunn where it met the harbor.

Activities

The Old Beard catered mostly to retired sailors, many of whom enjoyed telling lengthy tales. The accommodations within the tavern were fair and the prices were cheap.
