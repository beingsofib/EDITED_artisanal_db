The Faraway was a great forest in Turmish in the Vilhon Reach.

Geography

A vast wood, it spread over much of central western Turmish, at the southeastern foot of the Orsraun Mountains. A steep slope ran down from the Allascar valley to the Faraway. The forest's eastern fringe ran close to the village of Illowwood, with its own dense stand of shadowtop trees.

History

During the Ten Black Days of Eleint in the , several members of the royal family escaped Tethyr and somehow made it to The Five Lions inn. However, they were followed by rebels, who killed them in the Faraway woods.
