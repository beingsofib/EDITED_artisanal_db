Fort Locke was a garrison located at the southern end of the Mere of Dead Men along the Sword Coast.

Geography

Located between Highcliff and West Harbor, Fort Locke was built as a garrison. It was a point where the patrols would report for duty.

History

It was the place where the King of Shadows fought his first and second battle. In the first battle, the defenders of the fort pushed his forces to West Harbor. The dead soldiers were buried in a graveyard near the fort.

Later that year, the undead armies of the shadow reavers took the fort and destroyed it.

Inhabitants

Most of inhabitants of Fort Locke were Greycloak soldiers, though the fort also accepted refugees and those willing to help.

Notable inhabitants

  • Tann, a Greycloak commander of the fort;
  • Vallis Anton, a Greycloak lieutenant and the temporary commander of Fort Locke in 1374 DR;
  • Tor Millows, a priest of Ilmater;
  • Jacoby, Fort Locke's weaponsmith;
  • Liza, a leader of refugees;
  • Bruneil, a Greycloak soldier.
