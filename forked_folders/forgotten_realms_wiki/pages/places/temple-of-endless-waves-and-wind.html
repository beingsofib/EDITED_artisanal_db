The Temple of Endless Waves and Wind was a temple dedicated to Valkur, the Captain of the Waves.

Location

The Temple was contructed on the back of a massive Zaratan that swam the southern waters of the Sea of Fallen Stars. The zaratan seemed content to follow the directions of High Captain Orbachnar.

Inhabitants

The temple's high priest, or High Captain, in the was Orbachnar.

Rumors & Legends

The presence of the zaratan in the Sea of Fallen Stars was something of a mystery considering the creatures were native to distant Zakhara. However, one tale from the Time of Troubles told of a small army of reef giants that left Zakhara with a large turtle strapped to their backs.
