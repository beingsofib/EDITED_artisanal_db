The Marid States was a realm or group of realms that was a great power of Serôs for over a thousand years, ruled by marids and janni originating from the Calim Empire.

Geography

The States were based in the depths below the Hmur Plateau, and largely spread throughout the deeper reaches of the surrounding waters were shallow-dwellers could not reach.

History

Around a number of marids and janni rebelled and fled the Calim Empire, splashing into the Vilhon Reach and the underwater Selmalyr, causing a brief conflict with the native sea elves. Within the year they traveled into wider Serôs, settling in the depths below the Hmur Plateau beyond the reach of their neighbors. They proceeded to expand in the harder to reach depths of the sea, formed diplomatic ties with many realms and taught magic to their allies, becoming a dominant power in Serôs. In a similar move shortly after , the marid Ajhuu rebelled from Calim and founded the Maridlands in the eastern empire.

Peace accords between Aryselmalyr and the States ended their long-running conflicts in , and some janni joined the Dukar orders.

The Marid States came to an end in when the majority of the marids and janni either vanished or were killed by krakens. The remainder continued to disappear over the following sixteen years, leaving Aryselmalyr as the dominant power of Serôs.
