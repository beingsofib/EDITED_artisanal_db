Lutemaker Street

Lutemaker Street was a street in Lyrabar.

Description

Showing little of Lyrabar's weal, the district around Lutemaker street bore crumbling brick tenements and rookeries.

Notable Locations

The pawnbroker Hezza had a shop on that street.

History

On Hammer 16 of , Gorstag Helder stole an emerald pendant from Hezza's pawn shop. The pawnbroker caught him in the act, and after a fight, the thief escaped.
