The Warehouse District was a walled section of the capital city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Warehouse District was located in the southwest part of Skuld along the north bank of the River of Shadows.

Description

The entire district was walled. Two gateways were found in the northwest corner of the Warehouse District, allowing access to the Central Ward to the north, and the Merchant District to the west.
