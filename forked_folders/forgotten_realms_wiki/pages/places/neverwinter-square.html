Neverwinter Square or The Great Plaza of Neverwinter, was one of the oldest features of the City of Skilled Hands, Neverwinter, with an ancient arbor at its center in the mid-14 century DR.

Location

Neverwinter Square was a plaza, or a strake, in the area that would be later known as the Protector's Enclave, near Castle Never. It was the location of two of the city's temples, including the Temple of Tyr – the Hall of Justice, inns, taverns, shops, and other businesses lined the plaza's edges. Its most notable feature was an ancient public garden that was as old as the city itself. Neverwinter Square was a popular meeting spot for adventurers who were planning to set out on a journey.
