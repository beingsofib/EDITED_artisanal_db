The Harim Gate was supposedly the only entrance into the Royal Harim within the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Activities

This gate was constantly guarded by a contingent of the Honored Mamluk Society. Each member of the Honored assigned here was a eunuch.
