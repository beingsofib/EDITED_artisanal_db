The Tavern O'er the Waters was a tavern in Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Unusually, the tavern was built as a bridge over the Delimbiyr River itself, with entrances at each end. This made it a handy way of getting around. One end lay near The Woodlands Inn.

History

When the Zhentarim and Zhentilar commanded by Vaalgamon invaded the streets of Loudwater in the , the Heroes of Ascore were able to use The Tavern O'er the Waters to get around where they'd blocked the street for an ambush.
