This structure was a large temple in the city of Unthalass, in the mid–14 century DR, dedicated to the Untheric god Ramman.

History

During the tyrannical reign of the god-king Gilgeam, this and other temples of Ishtar were mandated to turn over half of their funds to him. The priesthood of the temple was ambivalent towards the growing rebellion against Gilgeam's reign, feeling certain that Gilgeam's forces would inevitably crush the rebels.
