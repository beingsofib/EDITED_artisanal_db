Sternpost Street

Sternpost Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Sternpost Street ran from the southwest on the waterfront with Dock Street to the northeast to end on Ship Street within Waterdeep's Dock Ward. Asteril's Way merged with Sternpost Street just a few buildings south of its intersection with Ship Street.
