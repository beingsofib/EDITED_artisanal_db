Reliable Guides was a shop located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The shop was located on the Throat outside of the Throat Gate of the Foreign Quarter.

Activities

The shop hired out guides to take adventurers through the ruins of Zhentil Keep following the city's destruction in the . Guides cost 1 gp per day of use and usually consisted of children.

Inhabitants

The shop was owned a pair of brothers named Goartet and Jenzar.
