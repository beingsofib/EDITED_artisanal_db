Gildengloop was a svirfneblin city in the deadly Underdark, long-abandoned by 1370 DR.

History

Gildengloop was an insignificant settlement apart from one detail; it was one of the thirteen destinations of the teleport without error spell ward that protected the hidden lair of a wizard who resided in Nonthal circa the . The wizard's lair - Nonthal's Hold, was protected by thirteen gates that, in not deactivated with the correct command word, spirited the intruder away. Gildengloop was the destination of the ninth gate.
