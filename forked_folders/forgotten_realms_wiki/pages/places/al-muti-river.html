Al-Muti River

Al-Muti River, also called the River of Generosity, was found along the border of the Cities of the Heart and the Cities of the Pantheon in interior Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

Al-Muti River formed a rough border between the northern Cities of the Heart and southern Cities of the Pantheon regions. The river originated in the foothills found between Al-Sayaj and Al-Akara mountains. Al-Muti flowed west to empty into the Golden Gulf.

The settlement of Talab was found near its origin point while the great city of Hilm lay on its southern bank where it met the Golden Gulf.
