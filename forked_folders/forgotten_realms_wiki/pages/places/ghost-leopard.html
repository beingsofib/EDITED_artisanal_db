The Ghost Leopard province was found in the northwest region of the Land of the Yak-Men in 1367 DR.

Geography

This province of the Yikarian Empire was dominated by the World Pillar Mountains on its eastern side and foothills and rugged terrain along the River Ruin in the west and north.

Inhabitants

Aside from the yak-folk who controlled the region, the Ghost Leopard province was home to a large population of marrash whose eyries were scattered throughout the mountain peaks. These dangerous creatures were close allies with the yak-folk and served as aerial scouts for the area.
