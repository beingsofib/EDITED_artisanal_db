The Hall of the Fountain was located within the complex of the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Description

The Hall of the Fountain was filled with enchanted water that remained within the room when any door was opened.

Activities

This hall housed the marids who served the Grand Caliph.

Inhabitants

Nereids and water weirds assisted the marids in their duties.
