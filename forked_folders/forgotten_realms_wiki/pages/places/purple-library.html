The Purple Library was a library in Deep Imaskar. It dedicated to the memory of the ancient Imaskari.

Location

The Purple Library was located in Deep Imaskar.

Structure

The Library had a number of extradimensional vaults.

Services

Besides holding the tomes of ancient Imaskari history commissioned by Lord Ilphemon, the library kept a number of artifacts and a great deal of arcane writings, including some esoteric lore about Pandorym. It had tomes on anatomy and monsters, though they were sadly out of date.

History

The Purple Library was built to hold the tome of Imaskari history commissioned by Lord Ilphemon.
