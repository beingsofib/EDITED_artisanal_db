The Swordsmith's House

Building
nameThe Swordsmith's House
typeInn
locationHighcastle, High Dale
servicesRooms for rent

The Swordsmith's House was a finer quality inn found in the village of Highcastle in late 14 century DR. Although they were open for most of the year, their doors would close during winter months for lack of business.

Prices

A room for rent at the Swordsmith's House cost 12 sp per night.
