Cobh was a small town located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Cobh was found in the county of Talffolk on Callidyrr's eastern coast on the Sea of Swords. The village was located north of Torriage Wood and south of Whitefish Bay.
