The Cave of Wickedness was a cavern located in the southeastern Storm Horns in the Forest Kingdom of Cormyr.

Location

The entrance into this colorfully named cave was located just north of Castle Kilgrave in the body of a Storm Horns peak that loomed over the keep. The Cave of Wickedness was accessible via the portion of the Moonsea Ride called the Mountain Ride that crossed the Gnoll Pass with the Helmlands to its north and northwest.
