Citadel Firequench was the headquarters of the Firequench Order, a cabal of spellcasters that operated out of Tarmalune, one of the Windrise Ports of Laerakond.

Background

Citadel Firequench was a massive basalt citadel west of the Raging Flame's location, surrounded by the ruins of buildings destroyed by the fires of the Raging Flame.

Citadel Firequench was originally the property of a pirate baron and the previous city's jail before the Firequench Order was founded.
