Orcskull Mountains

The Orcskull Mountains were a mountain range located on the island of Moray within the Moonshae Isles.

Description

The Orcskulls were a curved line of jagged peaks and glaciers rising up to . The valleys were barren but the uplands consisted of dry meadows and green tundra ideal for pastureland. The mountains were crossed by the occasional pass, and were not quite so rugged as the Trollclaw Range to the north .

Geography

The mountains covered the southern tip of Moray. They were bordered on the east by the Strait of the Leviathan, and by the Trackless Sea to the south and west. The Shannyth River flowed north from the center of the Orcskull Mountains into central Moray.

Flora & Fauna

The upland meadows were full of wildflowers—including buttercups, columbines, and lavender—while the slopes were covered in gorse and juniper.

Inhabitants

Most of the mountain vales hosted dens and holdfasts of orcs and giants. and while they were known to occasionally wipe out whole towns, their goal was generally to capture provisions, treasure, or slaves. The largest and most powerful orcish stronghold was a community of about 500 within a network of caves and mineshafts nestled in the heights of the mountains.

Legends & Rumors

On particularly hot summer days or particularly cold winter days, a loud wail could be heard echoing from the Orcskull Mountains, which was believed to belong to a grieving mother known as the maiden of Highpeak who dwelt in a castle of glass.
