Kimball Moor was a large moor located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Kimball Moor was a windswept coastal plain found along the eastern coast with the Trackless Sea and Corwell Firth. It bordered Goewin Wood and the western end of the Llyrath Forest to the south and the Mere Adammair to the southwest. Kimball Moor covered approximately .

Flora and Fauna

The moor was a heathland covered in dwarf-shrubs, but was further home to a diverse population of plants and animals. Each autumn before the first snows, displacer beasts from the nearby woodlands were known to congregate on the moor for bloody hunts.

History

The moor got its name after , when Lord Kimball established Cantrev Graystone.

Notable Locations

The town of Graystone was located in the southwest section of the moor.
