Uvaeren's Legacy, also known as the Legacy Library, was a unique repository of knowledge found in Myth Drannor that offered tremendous amount of lore to knowledge-seekers, albeit in unusual ways that seldom involved the reading of books or other documents.

Location

Uvaeren's Legacy was situated on the western side of the Street of Sorrows in the Dlabraddath district of the city.

Structure

The grand stone library comprised four stories, and four different sides, with wide-set stairs leading up to an entrance on each side.

Interior

The first floor of the library held an open-air space lined with statues of knowledgeable sages and scholars, in the form of the various races that called Myth Drannor Home. Rooms found throughout this floor included the Hall of Scents, the Lorespirits' Room, the Hall of Crystal,

The top three floors of the library's Legacy Tower composed the Memory Chamber, a massive domed chamber whose walls were embedded with the same empathic crystals found in the Hall of Crystal. Within these tel'kiira-like crystals were thousands of years of collected emotions, nightmares, aspirations, and accumulated knowledge. Within the chamber was a seat that resembled spelljamming helm in its function, allowing elves to access the collected information of the crystals, or instilling madness into the minds of other races.

Services

Knowledge could be ingested in ways that were not possible in other libraries or houses of knowledge. Bottled fragrances in the Hall of Scents could be used to bring up memories and help identify certain pungent plants and substances; singing along with the library's statues would reveal new lyrics and notes; patterns of twinkling flashes of light could reveal hidden lore during meditations sessions; and empathic crystals that evoked feelings that uttered specific command words when standing before a mural or sculpture, or while accessing the Memory Chamber.

Defenses

The four entrances of the library were guarded by stone guardians that occasionally offered information about where to access information within the library.

The Memory Chamber was always watched over by two members of the Akh'Faer and at least one amrathor.
