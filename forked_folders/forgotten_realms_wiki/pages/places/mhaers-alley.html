Mhaer's Alley

Mhaer's Alley was a hard-packed dirt and gravel paved,

Geography

This street ran north off of Spendthrift Alley and terminated on Wall Way.

Notable Locations

Mhaer's Alley was host to a great variety of shops across both of its sides, all along its length. Including one of the greatest concentrations in Waterdeep of shops that sold cord, rope, thread, wire, and trimmings for clothing. Some of the known stores that stood along Mhaer's Alley included the following:

  • Caer Bannog
  • Chuki's Moneylenders
  • Diabel's Potions & Curatives
  • Huer's Sundries
  • Marie's Dry Goods
  • The Old Druid's Herbs & Seeds Shoppe
  • Salavant's Scriveners
