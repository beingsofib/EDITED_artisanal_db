Klocckbarn was a tiny pirate kingdom of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. It participated in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Description

Klocckbarn was led by Black Dan and Brown Dort, who commanded the Swabbies and the ships The Sea Grover and Der Fliegende Dingbat. It was also the home of the ticking Klocckodyle.

Klocckbarn shared a land border with Zagrus, which was marked by a range of mountains. The walled city-state of Al-Ubaid was nestled within these mountains, and opposed both Klocckbarn and Zagrus. Across the Lennahc Hsilgne from Kloccbarn was the nation of Wolfenbutte. Wolfenbutte was the location of Castle Klockk, which curiously shared etymology with the name Klocckbarn.

Among the nations of the Whamites, Penbroc was significant for its two powerful ships.
