The Fishery was a store of the fishing industry in Leilon in the late 15 century DR. It was also temporarily used as the town hall in circa 1491 DR. Town councilor Valdi Estapaar was in charge of the Fishery.

Location

The Fishery was located at Quayside north of the barge yard.

Services

The Fishery was where local fisherfolk sold their catches and most business relating to selling or repairing fishing equipment took place. During the reconstruction efforts, the Fishery also served as a makeshift town hall in the evenings. Outside the Fishery, there was a job board with adverts for adventurers searching for employment.
