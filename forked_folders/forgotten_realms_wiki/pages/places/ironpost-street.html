Ironpost Street

Ironpost Street was a lesser avenue, paved in corduroy-logs, in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Geography

This street ran from east to west, running roughly parallel to the more southerly Burnt Wagon Way, coming off of Winter Path and terminating on Zendulth Street.

Notable Locations

  • Dunblast Roofing Company
  • Rejviik's Mortuary
  • One could find weapon merchants on this street that sold their equipment as discount prices, though the quality of the equipment was dubious.
  • On the east front of Ironpost Street's southern edge, right against the wall of a corner building, was set a huge cracked paving stone. If that stone were activated, it would act as a portal to Maldiglas’s Refuge.
