The Bold Archer was a club on the Promenade in Suzail, the capital city of Cormyr in the 15 century DR.

Location

The entrance to the Bold Archer was marked with a carved hanging sign depicting a calm woman on a forest rock wielding a bow against a pack of wolves. The club's two sets of heavy doors were made out of copper-sheathed duskwood.

History

In the , the Bold Archer became a site of a murder. Two ghostly warriors clad in blue flames murdered Lord Seszgar Huntcrown It was later discovered by Storm Silverhand, the Marchioness of Immerdusk, that these blueflame ghosts were under the control of Lord Marlin Stormserpent.
