Red Sail Tavern, also known as the Sail, was a tavern in the city of Ravens Bluff's island neighborhood of Ladyrock in the late 14 century DR.

Exterior

The building was constructed out of various dark-paneled, salvaged fittings and timbers, from scrapped or wrecked ships.

Atmosphere

The Sail was a dim and smoky tavern, catering primarily to sailors. The authorities and "landlubbers" were often not welcomed by the clientele.

Reputation

The Sail was the busiest business establishment in the whole of Ladyrock.
