Aina was a small town located in the country of Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Geography

Aina was a coastal town located in western Mulhorand along the southeastern coast of the Alamber Sea west of the mouth of the River of Spears.

Inhabitants

The precept of Aina in the , was a priest of Thoth named Alakin.
