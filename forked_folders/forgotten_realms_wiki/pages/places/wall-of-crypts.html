The Wall of Crypts was the defensive wall of Surcross in Thay as of the late 15 century DR.

Structure

The wall was thick, at least twice as tall, and surrounded the city. It was made of heavy stone and appeared solid from the outside.

Interior

The wall contained hundreds of crypts located behind bronze doors bolted to the stone. Within these crypts were corpses that served as an emergency reserve force of zombies to be deployed if the wall was ever breached. Any Thayan officer could speak the command word and cause the corpses to rise and immediately begin marching towards the greatest concentration of enemy forces.

History

As of 1479 DR, only a relatively small proportion of the Thayan undead forces in Surcross were housed within the Wall of Crypts, even as the city came under siege by the Netherese enclave Kolthunral. The remainder served as soldiers and laborers.
