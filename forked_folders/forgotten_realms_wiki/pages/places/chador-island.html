The Chador was an island found in the Corsair Domains of northwest Zakhara around 1367 DR. The island was part of an archipelago called the Desert's Virtues.

This island was named for a pair of circular elevations that resembled the eye holes of a moral woman's chador.

Geography

The Chador was roughly the same size as the Camel's Hump located just southeast. The island was long and — wide. The eastern side of the island was arid while the western side was heavily forested. An unnamed town was found in the center of a natural bay on the west coast.
