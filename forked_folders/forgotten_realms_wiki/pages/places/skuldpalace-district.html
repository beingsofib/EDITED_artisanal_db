The Palace District was a walled section of the capital city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Palace District was situated in the northeast corner of Skuld. It was bordered on the west by the Alamber Sea.

Description

Aside from the nearby City of the Gods, the Palace District was lavish and orderly when compared to the rest of the cappital. This district was only accessible via a single gate found on the south wall.
