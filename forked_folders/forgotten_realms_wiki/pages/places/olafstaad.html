Olafstaad was a Northlander town and kingdom located in the realm of Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Olafstaad was a small coastal town located on the Sea of Moonshae on the northern end of Prince's Sound on the western coast of Gnarhelm. It was the westernmost settlement in the kingdom. The small cottages spread out neatly along the shore around work buildings for farmers and fishers, with the large wooden lodge of the king situated in the middle of town.

Defenses

The kingdom could muster an army of 400 foot soldiers and 120 cavalry riders. Their horses were swift and strong enough to traverse half of Gnarhelm in a single day and night.

Government

Olafstead was the original seat of the Olafsson kings of Gnarhelm.

History

In the , King Rolf Olafsson of Olafstaad was named king of all Gnarhelm.

A large force of sixty raiders set out on longships from Olafstaad in the , but suffered terrible losses in the whirlpools of Oboron's Maw.

In the , an army masquerading as the forces of Callidyrr attacked Olafstaad in an effort to provoke a war between the Northlanders and the Ffolk.

Notable Locations

The grand lodge of the king was adorned with a great many luxuries and pieces of art acquired through raiding, including sculptures, tapestries, plush rugs, and dinnerware of gold and crystal.
