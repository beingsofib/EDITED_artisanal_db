Athanar was a major port of the planet Coliar in Realmspace,

Description

Athanar was an aarakocra spelljamming port. Like all land masses within the atmosphere of Coliar, Athanar was a floating island, one of many thousands.

The port contained several structures. A stream flowed through the town and poured off the edge to fall to unfathomable depths.

Government

The aarakocra of Coliar maintained democractic governments. On Athanar, that government's business was centralized at the Congress building.

Trade

Athanar charged a moorage fee of 125 gold pieces and an exorbitant air tax of 200 gold pieces. An additional cargo tax was also charged, which was based on the amount of cargo being carried by mooring spelljammers.

Religion

The islanders of Athanar were known to revere the god Syranita, and the port contained at least one shrine dedicated to her.
