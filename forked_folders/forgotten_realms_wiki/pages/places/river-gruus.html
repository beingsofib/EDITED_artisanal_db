River Gruus

The River Gruus was a river that flowed from the westernmost of the Whamite Isles into the Sea of Fallen Stars. The river was a key location in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

The River Gruus had its source in the mountains that seperated Veldergauttland from the rest of the island. It flowed down along the isle, forming a border between Bolgor and Penbroc. It then turned sharply away from Bolgor, and formed Penbroc's border with Graubünden, then emptied into the Sea of Fallen Stars. The historic fortress Gruuscastle overlooked the river, and was its most significant landmark. It was built on the spot where the borders of Graubünden, Penbroc and Bolgor met.

The Gruusboatmen of Bolgor were known to row up and down the river's waters.
