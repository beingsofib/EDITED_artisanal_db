Stormwinds Towers

Building
nameStormwinds Towers
typeCastle
locationMarsember, Cormyr
ownerSzwentil Illeon

Stormwinds Towers was the private home of Szwentil Illeon, the wealthiest man in Marsember, Cormyr.

It was a small fortified castle surrounded by a moss garden, but Szwentil was not known to allow anyone inside.
