Giantspine Range

The Giantspine Range was a mountain range found on the island of Giantspine in the Norheim Isles in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The mountain was a dominant feature of the island, stretching from end to end for over . Aside from the shoreline, and a small forested region in the northwest, the Giantspine Range covered the entire island. The village of Seawolf was found at the mountain's northern edge, and the capital of Hammerstaad was found at its southern edge.

History

The mountains were once home to Ualintharghar, a red dragon known in Ffolk legends as "the Devouring Flame". He was long-dead as of the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
