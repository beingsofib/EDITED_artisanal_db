Crypt of the Six

Building
nameCrypt of the Six
typeCrypt
locationThe Serpent Hills, Najara

The Crypt of the Six was one of many Anaurian tombs littered throughout the Serpent Hills. It housed the bodies of multiple counselors and their families and contained a number of magical guardians, along with a path forcing travelers to wander the Abyss before delving deeper. Because of these defenses, it was one of the few tombs in the area to never be looted.
