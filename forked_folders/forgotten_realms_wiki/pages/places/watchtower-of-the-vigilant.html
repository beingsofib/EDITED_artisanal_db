The Watchtower of the Vigilant was a temple dedicated to Helm and located in Harkstag during the 13 century DR.

History

The Watchtower was a small temple located in a small village near Serpent's Cowl. It came to prominence during the , for hosting the Council of Helm that resulted in the creation of the Vigilant Riders.

The temple was destroyed alongside the rest of the village during a green dragon attack in the . During the mid-to-late 14 century DR, its ruins served as a hideout for yuan-ti of Najara.
