The D'Zani outpost was a fortification of Sschindylryn in the Underdark beneath Cormyr.

Location

The outpost was found in a cavern accessed by portals set up by the Sschindylrynyr. One set of portals eventually led to the Demonweb Pits, another to Sschindylryn, and another up to the Eveningstar Cavern at the surface.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, the outpost was used by drow forces as a midpoint on the march from Sschindylryn up to Eveningstar, and overseen by Master Wizard Tsabyrr. Although they made use of many portals to move around, there were still lengthy sections of regular marching through the Underdark along the route. When Eberronian heroes arrived through the Demonweb after encountering Lolth at the Spinner's Prison, they followed the drow forces up through the outpost and attacked them from the rear. Despite the Master Wizard and his garrison, the drow were unable to stop the lost but deadly Eberronians.
