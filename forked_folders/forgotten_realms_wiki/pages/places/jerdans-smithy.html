Jerdan's Smithy was a blacksmith's shop found in Daggerford during the late 15 century DR.

Location

It could be found in the Farmers' Quarter in the north side of town.

Services

While the Jerdan wasn't quite as skilled as Daggerford's two dwarven blacksmiths, he found his niche in the Daggerfordian economy. He forged all manner of items that his customers requested, much of which the other smiths could not be bothered to work on.
