Cromach's Smithy was a forge that operated within the town of Daggerford for well over a century.

History

The shop was originally founded by Cromach, a human blacksmith who knew better than to compete with the local weaponsmith at Derval's Bright Blade. Cromach had something of a flair for artistry, as his wares were highly valued for their aesthetics as well as their functionality.

Around the , the smithy was being run by the dwarf called Tholvar Cragjaw. He continued Cromach's legacy by crafting utilitarian metal items for Daggerford's citizens.

Lore

There was a saying during Cromach's times that his work was "as shaky as Wayfel's best," referring to the the proprietor of Wayfel's Smithy.
