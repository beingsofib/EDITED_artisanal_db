Ladyhouse Falls

Location/Body of water
nameLadyhouse Falls
typeWaterfall
regionSavage Frontier

Ladyhouse Falls was a waterfall with an eponymous temple to Mystra in the 3 century DR.

Description

The roar of water in the falls was audible from a distance.

Geography

Ladyhouse Falls was fed by the River Dessarin. It was broadly considered the southern border of the Mlembryn lands.

History

By the 14 century DR, the tributary of the Dessarin River that fed Ladyhouse Falls had vanished.
