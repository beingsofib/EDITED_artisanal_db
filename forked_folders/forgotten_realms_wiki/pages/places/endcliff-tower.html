Endcliff Tower was one of the major torchtowers of Waterdeep's Trollwall, located in the city's North Ward.

Location

This tower stood on Saerdoun Street, directly across from Endcliff Lane and just north of Zun Villa.

Structure

Endcliff Tower stood three stories tall. It was directly connected to the eastern Trollwall, right where it ended before Cliffwatch as a gap, a sheer drop that presented a grand view of the countryside east and south of the city.

Interior

Being a torchtower, Additionally, being a major torchtower, it had six small holding cells that could hold up to ten people, which were used in times of trouble.

Activities

Like all other torchtowers, this structure had three beacon-fires that were constantly kept on standby.

Inhabitants

Endcliff Tower, like all torchtowers, was manned by members of the City Watch, who carefully watched over the edge of Cliffwatch for any sign of invasion.
