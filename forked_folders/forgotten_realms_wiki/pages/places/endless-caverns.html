Endless Caverns

The Endless Caverns were a series of deep-reaching cavern and tunnel complexes located to the south of the Star Mounts. Inside the Endless Caverns lived many powerful and dangerous creatures, such as Grimnoshtasdrano, the Riddling Dragon, and the lich Aumvor The Undying.
