Darkhope's Road

Darkhope's Road, formerly known as Peakskirt Trail, was a trail found in eastern Tethyr and the Lake of Steam region of south Faerûn. It was rarely ever featured on maps made by northerners.

Description

Darkhope's Road was paved and narrower than the more prominent Golden Road.

Geography

The trail originated in Kzelter. It stretched southeast for approximately before meeting the Tyrant's Road at the crossing at the River Mintar.

Notable Locations

Mintar was found along Darkhope's Road where the River Mintar emptied into the Lake of Steam. The Narrows Trail branched south from Darkhope's Road northwest of Mintar.
