Best Harps Hall was a guildhall in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR and the headquarters of the Innkeepers Society.

A quality five-story building, it was located in the heart of Maz'ahl Drudach, Marekh Sabban, in the city's Emerald Ward.
