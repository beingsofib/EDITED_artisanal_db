Lo'S'sem Gompa was a monastery (a gompa in the Tabotan language) of the Way of Enlightenment in Tabot.

Geography

It was found in the south of Tabot.

Description

Pang of Pokarr Gompa dismissed Lo'S'sem, Jo'nai, and Mafensea Gompas as "all alike", with "primitive and gaudy" ceremonies.

History

It was founded some time between and , when fifteen groups of monks and temple guardians escaped persecution in Shou Lung and journeyed southwest in search of a holy land that would remain pure and lasting, ideal for isolated hermitages. One of these groups built the beginnings of Lo'S'sem.

Circa , Lo'S'sem was among those southern monasteries opposing the northern monasteries in the yig-tsang (monk council) of Tabot.
