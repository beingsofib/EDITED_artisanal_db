Nova Vaasa was one of the Domains of Dread, a rural, beautiful grassland roamed by herds of horses, with sparse, impoverished cities.

Geography

Warm and temperate all year round, Nova Vaasa was largely grassland with a bit of rocky ground to the west.

Government

Five aristocracies ruled the domain, with the Bolshniks being first among their peers. Confiscatory taxes were the norm.

Trade

Native lumber was not durable, rotting within a few years; all good lumber had to be transported in at great expense. The domain was famous for its excellent horses.

Defenses

Prince Othmar of the Bolshniks had secretly hired a number of lycanthropes to augment his own sizable cavalry.
