Reluraun's Tomb was a tomb in the Ardeep Forest as of 1370 DR. It was the final resting place of Reluraun, an elven warrior who was a hero to the elves. Reluraun's body was said to be clad in magic chain mail and lying with a magical dragon slayer sword.

Inhabitants

As of 1372 DR, Reluraun was a mad and aggressive flying pair of skeletal hands and eyeballs, attacking anyone who ventured too near. His tomb was guarded by three baelnorns.
