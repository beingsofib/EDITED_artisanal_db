The Inn of the Golden Palm was an inn in Bralizzar in the Dustwall Mountains.

Location

The Inn of the Golden Palm was at the intersection of the Boulevard and the Bazaar, opposite the latter.

Structure

The inn was a building of brilliant white stone, with crossed palms growing in stone tubs to provide shade on the entrance; it had once been a temple, but a sign had been added when it was turned into an inn. The inside had a single common area with a stage.

Staff

Tekuna Chand was the proprietor.

Services

The House of Mirth was of middling quality between it and the Swayback Camel; given the latter's description, the Inn of the Golden Palm probably compared favorably to the others. Its stage could be used for singing and dancing.

Inhabitants
