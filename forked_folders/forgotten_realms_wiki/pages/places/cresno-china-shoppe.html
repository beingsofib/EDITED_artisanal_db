Cresno China Shoppe was a store in the city of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR.

Location

Cresno China Shoppe stood next-door to the Sleepy Dwarf Tavern

History

In Hammer of the , the street in front of Cresno China Shoppe and the Sleepy Dwarf Tavern erupted in violence. Clergy of Cyric, the Mad God, clashed in a vicious battle with priests of Bane. To make matters worse, Banites were joined by devotees of Iyachtu Xvim halfway through. The battle resumed with numerous civilian casualties and massive property damage for both Cresno China Shoppe and the Sleepy Dwarf. Only one competent was found still alive by the Ravens Bluff's guardsmen. One notable injured individual was Meegan, an employee of the Sleepy Dwarf Tavern. The incident was reported by Silva Sinderan in that month's Ravens Bluff Trumpeter broadsheet.
