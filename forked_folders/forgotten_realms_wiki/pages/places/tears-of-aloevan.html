The Tears of Aloevan was the name of a magical pool of water within a glen of the northern Ardeep Forest called the Glen of Aloevan, and the hidden demiplane, a cloud of magic, accessible from this pond.

Description

The pond itself was clear.

The cloud of magic boasted a replica of the long-lost Court of Silver Fire,

Geography

The Tears of Aloevan lay within a glen

Government

Embrae Aloevan ruled the demiplane, though her madness meant she could do very little without her seven attendants.

Defenses

The portal that led into the hidden realm would only open during solar eclipses. Besides Embrae herself, her seven attendants were powerful priests of Sehanine.

History

After the Laranla, Embrae Aloevan, passed away and was consumed by silver fire, her spirit was prevented from passing, remaining trapped within a nimbus of the silver fire. Her death also created a mystical pool. At a later date, seven clerics of Sehanine learned of this, and created a pathway to the otherworldly cloud of magic where her spirit was trapped. Once there, they took it upon themselves to recreate the Court of Silver Fire, or the Court of Ardeep, and ease the former queen's mental torment.

By the late 14 century DR, many outsiders had sought audience with the queen; none had returned.
