Intibah was a small town found in the western reaches of the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara.

Geography

Intibah was located near the source of Al-Malja River upstream approximately from Ajayib in the shadows of Al-Suqut Mountains. Though somewhat isolated from other civilization, an occupied qal'at nearby provided military protection.
