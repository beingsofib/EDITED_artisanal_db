Narthil Keep was a fortress near Narthil in the 3 century DR.

Location

It was north of Narthil.

Structure

The fortress could be described as "a pile of rocks", but had a gate.

Activities

In the , Aunsiber, the Lord Steward that oversaw the town during the occupation by Athalantar, lived in that keep.

Defenses

There was at least a nominal amount of forces at the keep.

History

Already by , the keep was described as a pile of rocks.
