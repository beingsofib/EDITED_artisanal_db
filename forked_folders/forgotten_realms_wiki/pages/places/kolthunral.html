Kolthunral was a Shadovar flying fortress enclave used to threaten Thay in as part of the Shadovar–Thay War.

Description

Kolthunral was a fortress, and small for a floating city, with built-in shadow features that mimicked the Shadowfell. It normally moved between 5 and 10 miles (8–16 kilometers) an hour, but could accelerate up to 40 miles (64 kilometers) per hour for half an hour at a time.

Government

Kolthunral was run by the spirit devourer Duchess Antethriss, formerly an assassin under Clariburnus Tanthul, as a military base for the Shadovar. Shade and shadar-kai soldiers manned the garrison.

Defenses

  • Outer guard towers equipped with siege weapons extended above and below the city, and reinforced bridges connected them to the keep and other strongholds.
  • The interiors of the guard towers and other important structures were partially made of walls of shadowstuff that allowed creatures from the Shadowfell to phase through, but created a maze for others.
  • Meteor Tubes, arcane cannons that launched boulders through the Shadowfell to accelerate them past terminal velocity before launching them at targets, were the most damaging, though imprecise, weapons.
  • The Tower of the Mythallar was disguised as an inner guard tower and the most heavily guarded building.

History

During the Shadovar–Thay War, in the late 1470s DR, Kolthunral launched a surprise attack on Thay, while most the action was far to the west, and destroyed a number of communities near Surcross, before they settled in to besiege the city and block access to Shadowfell roads..
