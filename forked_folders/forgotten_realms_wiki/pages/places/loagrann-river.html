Loagrann River

The Loagrann River was formed by the joining of three tributaries—two of which were named Skull Creek and Whitewater—flowing from the northern part of the Greypeak Mountains. Flowing southwest it joined the Greyflow river about halfway between Llorkh and the junction of the Greyflow and the Delimbiyr rivers.

Geographical Features

  • Deadstone Cleft: This deep canyon, through which the Loagrann flowed, housed a cave-complex inhabited by a clan of stone giants.
