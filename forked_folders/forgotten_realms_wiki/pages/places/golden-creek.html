Golden Creek

The Golden Creek was one of the watercourses of the Esmelflow in Amn.

Description

The waters in this stream were good for fishing.

Geography

This creek began in the Ridge before joining with the Vudlur and flowing into the Splendarrllur.

History

The people of Keczulla used to pan this stream for gold.
