Ulshantir was a small settlement in the Lost Kingdom of Asram.

Trade

It was notable for its community of skilled smiths that worked in soft metals such as copper and brass.

History

The ruins of Ulshantir remained lost in the desert of Anauroch centuries after the fall of Asram.
