The Lady's College was the first open school for mages in Silverymoon. It was considered by many to be the heart of the Conclave of Silverymoon.

Services

The education system of Lady's College was unique, as unlike the other schools at the time, it didn’t utilize the traditional apprentice-master dynamic, in which a master took on several apprentices, and slowly taught them magic in exchange for servitude. The Lady's College took on aspiring mages as students, not as servants.

The radically different teaching philosophy of Lady's College made the school a polarizing institution, though even its detractors agreed that its graduates were not only talented wizards, but also well rounded scholars.
