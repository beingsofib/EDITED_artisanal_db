The Tying-Place was a large pen for livestock located in the city of Dhaztanar in Semphar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The Tying-Place was located within the Quarters of the Unclean district in east Dhaztanar. It sat in the central part of the district along the Silk Road near Tarkhold River.

Description

A wall surrounded the livestock pens.
