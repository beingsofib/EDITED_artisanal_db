The Gate of the People was the main entrance for the Public Gardens in Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Activities

Commoners used this gate the enter the beautiful gardens. Though mamluks were stationed here, they did not bar entrance to the gardens to any unless specifically instructed to do so.
