Kristen's Circle

Kristen's Circle was a circular intersection in the Holyhouses neighborhood of Ravens Bluff.

Location

This junction was intersected at its northeastern and southwestern corners by Fire Lane, whilst intersected from the parallel directions by Evensong Ride. From its northern and southern ends, it was intersected by DeVillar's Ride. From the east it was connected to a small, unnamed path that led to Manycoins Way, whilst from the west another unnamed path connected it to Blacktree Boulevard South.

Notable Locations

  • Bhuklyn's Merchant House, the head offices of the Bhuklyn merchant house. It fronted the Circle between the northern leg of DeVillar's Ride and the northwestern run of Evensong Ride.
  • Firestorm House, the head offices of the Firestorm merchant house. It fronted the southwestern corner, where the southern run of DeVillar's Ride left the Circle.
  • The Silver Halls, the city's dedicated temple to Tyr. Standing in the very center of Kristen's Circle, it was a massive stone structure that dominated the area.

History

Sometime in the late 14 century DR, Kristen's Circle was the startling site of a murder. Early one morning, two City Guardsmen discovered the corpses of four priests chained together at the junction's center. They were identified as each being acolytes of a different faith: Helm, Lathander, Tempus, and Tyr. Pinned to one of the bodies was a note that read, "Gods sleep while Checkmate stirs". The incident was reported the next day by the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter broadsheet, though the City Watch were withholding details at that time.
