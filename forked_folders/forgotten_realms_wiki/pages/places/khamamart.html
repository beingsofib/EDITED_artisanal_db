Khamamart, also known as the Tomb of Fire, was the fiery marble-walled final resting place of the Grand Vizar Samesaj of Calimport.

Location

Khamamart was built to the east from the city walls of Calimport.

History

In , the construction of Khamamart was completed. Soon after the Grand Vizar walked through one of the tomb's flaming walls never to be seen again.
