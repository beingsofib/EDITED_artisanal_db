Lennahc Hsilgne

Lennahc Hsilgne was a strait that seperated two of the Whamite Isles. The nations of Klocckbarn, Zagrus, and Wolfenbutte all had coastlines on Lennahc Hsilgne. The body of water was a key location in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

It was a shipping route for many vessels, including the Carrack, the Boat of Moltar, the Smardese Fleet, and Phoenetian ships. On occasion, a fleet from Vandmeer or even the Great Khan himself passed through Lennahc Hsilgne.

The fortress of Ratlarbig overlooked Lennahc Hsilgne from Zagrus's coast, and took a tax of gold from all traders who sailed the strait. Pirates from Klocckbarn also raided the ships for gold.
