The Land of the Beastly Brigands was a tiny nation of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. It participated in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Description

The Land of the Beastly Brigands was led by Badmuddin the Bald and Blikwik the Beast. As well as an abundance of thugs, the Beastly Brigands included the Bloody Buggers and the Beastlboats fleet.

The Land of the Beastly Brigands shared a land border with Agenais, which was marked by a mountain range. A part of its coastline looked out onto the Swil Passage, not too far from the smaller nation of Swil. One of the most notable landmarks of the land was the Black Cave.
