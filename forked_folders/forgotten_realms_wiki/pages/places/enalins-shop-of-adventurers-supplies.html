Enalin's Shop of Adventurers' Supplies was a shop in Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid–14 century DR.

Location

It lay near one of the gates out of Loudwater.

Services

In business by 1358 DR, the store sold whatever an adventuring company might require.
