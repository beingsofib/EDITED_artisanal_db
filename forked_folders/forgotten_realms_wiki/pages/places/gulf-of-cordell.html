Gulf of Cordell

The Gulf of Cordell was a large body of water that lay east of Maztica and north of Payit.

Information

The Gulf of Cordell was an oceanic gulf the lay just of Payit along Maztica's eastern coast. The bay of the Twin Visages opened into it, along with the nearby Helmsport. In the gulf itself were the Green Sisters (a chain of islands), Zilhatec Island, and Maztapan Island.
