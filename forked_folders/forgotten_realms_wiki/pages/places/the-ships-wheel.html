The Ship's Wheel was a tavern located in the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The tavern was located in the western section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward on the north side of Diamond Street where it joined Westwall Street near the West Gate.

Description

The front of the tavern was adorned with a shining ship's wheel sized to be used by a titan. The Ship's Wheel was a two story building.

Activities

The Ship's Wheel had a reputation for being one of the safest taverns around. Most activity within the tavern never got more exciting than the old patrons watching each others hair fall out.

Inhabitants

The tavern was owned by a man named Olhin Shalut.
