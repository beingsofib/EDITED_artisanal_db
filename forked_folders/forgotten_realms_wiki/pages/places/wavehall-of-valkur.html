The Wavehall of Valkur was a temple to Valkur located in Waterdeep during the late 15 century DR.

Location

The temple was located in the Dock Ward, on Net Street.

History

The temple was built during the late 15 century DR.

During the , the Wavemaster of the temple was killed by Mirt the Moneylender over a dispute involving the priest's refusal to bring a deceased ally back to life.

Inhabitants

The temple was inhabited by over a dozen priests from the Church of Valkur, led by a Wavemaster.
