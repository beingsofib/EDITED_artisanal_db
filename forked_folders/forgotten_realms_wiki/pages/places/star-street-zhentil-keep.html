Star Street (Zhentil Keep)

Star Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Star Street was located in east Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. The road ran north to south, in between Dusk Street in the north and Realm Street in the south. Star Street was intesected by Traders' Way near its midpoint.

Notable Locations

Joseph's Locks was the only prominent location along Star Street.
