Zehoarastria was the formal name of the region of Amn north of the Small Teeth and west of the Trade Way.

Geography

Encompassing the lands of Amn between the Sea of Swords and the Trade Way, Zehoarastria was named for the swampy floodplains found within its territory. The infamous traveler and author Volothamp Geddarm postulated that Zehoarastria would have been one enormous city were it not for the presence of the swamp.
