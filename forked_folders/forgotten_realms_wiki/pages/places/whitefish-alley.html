Whitefish Alley

Whitefish Alley was a small street found in the coastal town of Aithelar in Callidyrr on the Moonshae Isles in the .

Location

Whitefish Alley began at Bottle Lane and ended in an alley on the east side of Aithelar. The street crossed Aithe High Street near its center.
