Thurdan's Tree was a landmark in Cormanthor.

Geography

It lay at the southern end of Cormanthor.

History

In , Glarald Starym hid at Thurdan's tree after a confrontation with some unicorns and Aubaudameira Dree after they broke free of his domination.
