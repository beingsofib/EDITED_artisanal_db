Ravenwing was a small town found on the Camel's Hump island in the Corsair Domains of northwest Zakhara around 1367 DR.

Geography

Ravenwing sat along the coast of the Great Sea on the western side of the island. It was the sole settlement on the entire island as of 1367 DR.
