Sammarin's Street

Sammarin's Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Sammarin's Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's North Ward. The road followed originated near where Andamaar's Street entered the City of the Dead, passing north the curving to the east and ending on Mhalsymber's Way. Golden Serpent Street also intersected Sammarin's Street.

Notable Locations

Hriiat Fine Pastries sat near the midpoint of Sammarin's Street.
