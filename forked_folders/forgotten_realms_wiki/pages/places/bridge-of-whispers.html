Bridge of Whispers

Location
nameBridge of Whispers
typeBridge
regionUwaji, Tsukishima, Wa

The Bridge of Whispers was a magical bridge in the city of Uwaji in Wa. It was one of the most famous landmarks in the metropolis city and was located in the Financial District. If someone spoke words to the bridge, it would echo back what was said.
