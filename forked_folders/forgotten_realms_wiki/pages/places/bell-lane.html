Bell Lane

Bell Lane was a street in the built-up area of Hultail in Cormyr.

Geography

Bell Lane was a curved street that moved from north to south in the southwestern part of Hultail. In the 15 century DR, Bell Lane was where the cobbler shop of Arlburt Nammond was located.
