Keep to Yourself was a tavern located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the late 14 century DR following the city's destruction in the .

Location

The tavern was located in south Zhentil Keep outside of the River Trail Gate in the Foreign Quarter on the Tesh Trail.

History

Keep to Yourself was constructed for travelers who did not wish to enter the city but still wanted a place to rest for the night. The tavern was an excellent source for wild rumors.
