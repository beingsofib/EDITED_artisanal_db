Vathal, which was also spelled Vathel, was a small island located on the Golden Water in the Shining Lands of southeast Faerûn during the 14 century DR.

Geography

The island was located in the southern region of the Golden Water, just north of Var and the small bay called the Throat.

Activities

There were no permanent settlements on Vathal during the 14 century DR.
