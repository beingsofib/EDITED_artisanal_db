Staghunter's Way

Staghunter's Way was a small corner road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Staghunter's Way was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. It cut the corner between the Street of Glances and Gorl Street, passing through Sighing Maiden's Walk along the way. Wrightstone Street connected with the east side of Staghunter's Way.
