The Cold Desert was a tiny nation of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. It participated in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Description

The Cold Desert had a variety of colorful political figures that vied for control of the nation and the Whamite Isles. Known figures were Nonook (known as the Whale Killer), Umgook (known as the Tusk Eater), and Ma-Onya (known as the Large Lady). The common people of the Cold Desert, also participated in the wars of the Whamite isles. The Desert was inhabited by a significant population of reindeer, which somehow also joined in on the war.

The Cold Desert shared land borders with Inter Mierionydd, Bolgor, and Veldergauttland. Each of these borders was marked by a mountain range. The mountain regions were home to huts.

Among the nations of the Whamites, the Cold Desert was significant for its snowy climate.
