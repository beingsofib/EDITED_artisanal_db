The Palace of the Hulorn was the ancestral home of the Hulorn–Selgaunt's merchant-mayor ruler.

Location

The Palace was located in the northeast region of the city next to the Hunting Gardens of the Hulorn.

Inhabitants

Andeth Ilchammar was the Hulorn of Selgaunt in 1358 DR.
