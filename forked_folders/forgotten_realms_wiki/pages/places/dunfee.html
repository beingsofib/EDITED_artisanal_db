Dunfee was a small but vital mining town found in Impiltur near the border of Damara nestled within the Earthspur Mountains.

Activities

The inhabitants of Dunfee maintained quality relationships with all of their neighbors including fellow miners from Sudrav, nobles from Ravensburg, and the farmers of Zarach.

Given the town's close proximity to Damara, agents of the Lords of Imphras II were usually gathering information in Dunfree.

Inhabitants

Dunfree supported a population of 1,100 in 1357 DR.
