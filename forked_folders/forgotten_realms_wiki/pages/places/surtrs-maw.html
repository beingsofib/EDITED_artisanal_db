Surtr's Maw

Surtr's Maw was a mountain found on the island of Llorgh in Norheim in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Surtr's Maw dominated the southern half of Llorgh, touching both Northwind Strait and the Sea of Moonshae. The mountain covered approximately of land.
