Southflow Marsh was a small marshland found near Aithelar in the kingdom of Callidyrr on Alaron in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Southflow Marsh was located just south of Aithelar along the coast of the Sea of Moonshae. The marsh was fed from the east by the Southflow Stream. The Three Tors borderded the marsh's southern edge.
