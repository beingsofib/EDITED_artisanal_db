Stersen's Paddock

Building
nameStersen's Paddock
typeStables
locationArchenbridge, Archendale
servicesHorses, for purchase or rent; harnesses and saddles

Stersen's Paddock was the only corral and equestrian shop in Archenbridge as of 1369 DR. It was run by a horse trader named Stersen. His services included the purchasing or hiring of horses, as well as the sale of harnesses and saddles.
