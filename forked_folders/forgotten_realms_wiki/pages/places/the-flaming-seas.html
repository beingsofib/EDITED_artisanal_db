The flaming seas

Ganra ye-do, or "the flaming seas" was an area of sea lying south of the southwestern tip of the Koryo Peninsula in Koryo, Kara-Tur.

The Muradi Lighthouse at the end of the peninsula was maintained to warn of ganra ye-do.
