Caldreth's Cobbling was a shop in the town of Mornbryn's Shield

Services

Caldreth Wyvernlyng repaired shoes, boots, and cloaks. He also produced high, heavy leather boots that were waxed and waterproof, suitable for wading in streams, rivers, and lakes.
