Kork Bay

Kork Bay was a south-facing bay on the east coast of Moray in the Moonshae Isles.

Description

The bay was deep and surprisingly cold, with sandy beaches along the coast.

Geography

It sat below Kork Head at the narrowest point of the Strait of the Leviathan.

History

As of the late 15 century DR, the approach to Moray via Kork Bay was said to be cursed, owing to the presence of fire nagas in the water, although many dismissed this as rumors. One captain known for spreading tales of his harrowing escape from these monsters was Captain Blau of Callidyrr. In the late 1470s DR, the Sphinx attempted to cross the narrows despite its captain having heard Blau's tales, and it was attacked and sunk by the nagas.

Notable Locations

  • Kork

Inhabitants

Kork Bay was home to fire nagas as well as tentacled predators that dwelt in the depths.
