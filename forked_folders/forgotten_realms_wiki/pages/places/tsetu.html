Tsetu was a town in Tabot.

Geography

It stood amongst the southern foothills of the Po Yul Dzayul Range in central Tabot, at the end of a road from the road between Ji and Chophon.

Government

Tsetu had a dzong-pon (sheriff or district official) whom Pag Hou Ling served as assistant deputy for a decade.
