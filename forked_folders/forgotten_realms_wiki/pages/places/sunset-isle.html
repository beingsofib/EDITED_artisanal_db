Sunset Isle, or sometimes simply Sunset, was a small island in the Moonshae Isles archipelago.

Geography

Sunset was the westernmost of the Moonshaes, laying in the Trackless Sea due west of Moray. The terrain was largely mountainous but included forested hillocks and glens.

Flora & Fauna

The forests of Sunset included weir trees and ferns. These were home to families of tressym, which had become more rare by the late 14 century DR.

History

As of the mid-to-late 14 century DR, there were no notable settlements on Sunset, As of this time, the white dragon Arveiaturace was sometimes known to rest on the island while out on her sea-spanning hunting trips.

When the Spellplague struck in the , one of the first clear signs of the coming conjunction between Toril and Abeir was the sudden retreat of the Trackless Sea from the Moonshaes, which the Earthmother first detected at Sunset Isle when the waterline dropped by .

Notable Locations

  • A moonwell overlooked the coast and sat beside a particularly old and gnarled weir tree.
