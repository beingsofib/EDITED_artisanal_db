Evenstar Lake

Evenstar Lake was a lake in northern Turmish.

Geography

Fed by the Alaoreum River, it lay west of Alaghôn, southeast of Ironcloak, and northeast of Nonthal. A trail ran from the lake to Nonthal. It was surrounded by dense forests.

In these forests lived small birds called bloodybeaks. These fed on the mosquitoes that were common around the lake.

Activities

Evenstar Vale was a popular meeting place for lovers on the night of the local Feast of the Moon celebration.
