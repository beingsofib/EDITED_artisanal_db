Red Stag Inn (Dynnegall)

nameRed Stag Inn

The Red Stag Inn was an inn located in the town of Dynnegall on the island of Moray in the Moonshae Isles as of the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

The Red Stag was an oasis of hospitality in the otherwise squalid village. It made for a good stopover after the three-day ride from Caer Moray to Dynnegall.

History

When Elminster visited Moray in the early 14 century DR, he stayed at the Red Stag Inn while traveling with King Dagdar of Moray.
