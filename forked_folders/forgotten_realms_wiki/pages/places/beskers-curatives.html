Besker's Curatives was an apothecary in Nova Vaasa within the Domains of Dread.

Location

It was located in the Merchant's Quarter of the city of Liara in Nova Vaasa.

Structure

Besker's store was about by .

Services

The store sold small, minor material components, herbal remedies, charms, talismans, and first-aid products.

History

Besker's Curatives was staffed by its owner. He was the source for some of the remedies used by his parents to help the health of Jebbrie Vokasich after he was bitten by cursed spiders.
