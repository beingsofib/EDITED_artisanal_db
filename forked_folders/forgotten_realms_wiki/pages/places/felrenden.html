Felrenden was a valley on the western end of the the Sword Mountains, where Ieirithymbul was.

Geography

Felrenden was sited between three peaks: Mount Stemhelm to the north, Mount Ardabad to the east, and Mount Pheldaer to the west. The valley had an eponymous stream flowing north-to-south across it, only to vanish into a sinkhole, Braeder's Pit. Ieirithymbul, a gnomish small city, lay on that valley.

There were rich iron and copper lodes, as well as a huge coal seam.

Inhabitants

Of note, Felrenden boasted Palarandusk, a largely unknown entity who was actually a gold dragon.

Rumors & Legends

Palarandusk, the Unseen Protector, was not widely known to be a gold dragon, and there were many who suspected him to be a mage of sorts.
