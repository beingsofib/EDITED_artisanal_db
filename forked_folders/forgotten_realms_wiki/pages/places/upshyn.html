Upshyn

Location
nameUpshyn
regionAthalantar, Western Heartlands

Upshyn was a landmark of Athalantar, almost certainly a town, in the early 3 century DR.

Geography

Upshyn was close to a crossing of sorts, almost certainly a bridge, for the Unicorn Run. It was also a high-traffic area, where guards could conceivably be posted.

History

In the , Elminster had his doubts as to how to cross the Unicorn Run. Helm Stoneblade advised him on how.
