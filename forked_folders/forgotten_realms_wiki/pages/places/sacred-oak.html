The Sacred Oak was an oak tree in Leuthilspar on the island of Evermeet.

Inhabitants

At night, the tree was frequented by lovers who make their vows in secrecy. wild and wood elves claimed that the Sacred Oak was the first tree on Evermeet.

History

The tree was believed to have originated in Arvandor and was looked upon with great respect by the elves on Evermeet. Once per year each Agelong, Queen Amlaruil Moonflower visited the tree to assure the island's independence and freedom and to show her dedication to the elven gods.
