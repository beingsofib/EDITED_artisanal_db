Cloudtop Tower was a tower in the elf city of Evereska. It was home to the High Magi of the Cloudtop Magi Circle, advisers to the Council of Hill Elders and commanders of the Spellguard.

History

It played a crucial role in the initital skirmishes between the elves of Evereska and the malevolent phaerimm that broke out in the . The high mages of Cloudtop divined information about the movements of the phaerimm, granting insight to the elves on the ground, such as those led by Zharilee.
