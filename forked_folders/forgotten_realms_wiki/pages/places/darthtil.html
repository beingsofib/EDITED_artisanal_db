Darthtil

Location
nameThe Darthtil
typeLandmark

The Darthtil was a landmark of Athalantar.

Geography

The closest point of it to the Haunted Vale was about half a day's walk away.

History

In the or after, the sorceress Myrjala Talithyn commanded Elmara, a priestess of Mystra who was secretly the Prince Elminster Aumar, to restore nature from the Haunted Vale to the lands near Darthtil. She warned the priestess that it would take at least a year.
