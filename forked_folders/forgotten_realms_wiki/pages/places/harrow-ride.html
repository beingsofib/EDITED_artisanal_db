Harrow Ride

Harrow Ride was the name given to two separate roads leading through Harrowdale, in the Dalelands, one on either side of the dried river bed.

At the south east end of the route lay New Velar, and the north west part of the route went through Velarsburg before becoming the Halfaxe Trail at Velartree.
