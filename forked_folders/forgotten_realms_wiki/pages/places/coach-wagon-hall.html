The Coach & Wagon Hall was the headquarters of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Wagonmakers' and Coach Builders' Guild.

Location

This guildhall was located along the High Road,

Structure

This guildhall stood three stories tall.

Reputation

Like other guildhalls in the Southern Ward, this building was used as a landmark by locals.
