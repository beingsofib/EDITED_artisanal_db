Mal Sul

Mal Sul was a lake located on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Mal Sul was found in the north central part of the island along the eastern slopes of Andover Heights. A small unnamed river connected Mal Sul with Harloch to the south.

Notable Locations

A Moonwell was located near Mal Sul's southern shore in the forest.
