Poison Sea

Location
namePoison Sea
typeAquifer
areaRelmaur, Laerakond

The Poison Sea was an aquifer located beneath the Howling Mountains in Relmaur, Laerakond.

Inhabitants

A tribe of genasi known as the Saqr lived on an island made of crystal located in the center of the aquifer.
