The Pilgrim's Rest, which was also seen as the Pilgrims' Rest, was an inn that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

The inn was located in the western section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward on the west side of Satchel Alley where it met Diamond Street.

Description

The Pilgrim's Rest was a three-story building.

Activities

This comfortable inn was moderately priced, at least by Waterdeep standards. Many of the patrons stayed here while visiting Waterdeep's temples. A private room could be had for 9 gp per night and included stabling and a meal. 'Two-share' rooms with a pair of double beds ran 6 gp per night, and a communal room with eight or more beds was 4 gp per night.

Inhabitants

The inn was owned by Balaghast Brightlingar.
