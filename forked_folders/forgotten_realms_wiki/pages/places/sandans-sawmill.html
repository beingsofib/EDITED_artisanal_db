Sandan's Sawmill was a lumber mill found in Archenbridge in late 14 century DR. The mill was used to strip and saw timber felled from the nearby Arch Wood. It was powered by the strong currents of the River Arkhen that ran through the city, and under its eponymous bridge.

Owner

The mill was owned and operated by a former captain of the Archenriders named Sandan,
