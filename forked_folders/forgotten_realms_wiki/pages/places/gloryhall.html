Gloryhall was a shrine of Tempus in Halfhap, on the edge of Cormyr and the Stonelands.

It was a squat, bunkerlike building. Circa 1367 DR, it was overseen by Battlelord Boneblaster, who accepted worship from anyone who was sufficiently violent and warlike.
