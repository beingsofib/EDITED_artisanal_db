Beland was a town located in the far eastern stretches of the Eastern Shaar during the mid–14 century DR.

Geography

Beland sat near the western foothills of the Giant's Belt Mountains south of Rolling Stone Gap. It was situated along a trail crossroads est of Buldamar and the Lake of Salt.
