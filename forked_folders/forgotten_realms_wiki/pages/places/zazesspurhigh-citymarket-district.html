The Market District (or Merchant District) of Zazesspur was one of the districts of the city.

Description

The district was broadly rectangular, stretching east to west.

Government

The market's representative in the council as of the was Dolam Coincatcher.

Notable Locations (1370 DR)

  • The Hanging Garden, tavern
  • House of Tears, temple of Ilmater
  • House of the Steadfast, a temple of Helm turned courthouse
