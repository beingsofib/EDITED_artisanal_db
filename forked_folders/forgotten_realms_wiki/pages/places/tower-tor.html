Tower Tor was a hill in the mid–8 century DR.

Geography

Until the Walking Tower of Warglend was in Tower Tor. There was at least one lake close by.

History

In the , the sage Tabarast of Moonshorn Tower reported that, amongst other signs from the gods, the Walking Tower of Warglend had moved for the first time in a thousand years, approximately since , and left its place in Tower Tor for the middle of a nearby lake.
