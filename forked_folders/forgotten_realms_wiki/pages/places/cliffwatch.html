Cliffwatch

The Cliffwatch was a public walkway in the North Ward of Waterdeep that partially spanned the length of the edge of the steep cliff of the same name.

Description

There were no rails or walls separating this walkway from the steep drop of the Cliffwatch.

Location

This street stretched from north to south, running parallel to Saerdoun Street and Zendulth Street — as well as meeting the two of them at an intersection — until terminating alongside Malkur's Ride at the Beaconmarch, right before the Watchway Tower.
