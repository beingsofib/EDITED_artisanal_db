Mr. Peabody's Pit

Building
nameMr. Peabody's Pit
typeGold mine
locationGraubünden, the Whamite Isles

Mr. Peabody's Pit was a gold mine in Graubünden in the Whamite Isles. It was a key part of Graubünden's economy during a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles
