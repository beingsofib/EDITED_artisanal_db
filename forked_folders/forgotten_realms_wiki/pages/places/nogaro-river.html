Nogaro River

The Nogaro River was a mighty waterway found in the Cities of the Ancients in east Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

The Nogaro River originated in the far southern reaches of the Haunted Lands. It flowed south for over a thousand miles through thick jungles in the heart of the dead kingdom of Nog, emptying into the Foreigners' Sea in an area called the Gateway.

The river flooded each summer with the onset of heavy monsoons strong enough to wash an entire city away overnight.

History

The Zakharan inhabitants of the surrounding jungles would wash newborn babies in the waters of the Nogaro during their naming ceremony. The dead were placed on small rafts to be carried downstream, entrusted to Shajar's care in the watery underworld.
