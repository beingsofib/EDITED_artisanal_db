Ascloakan's Fine Inn was a large inn located in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the 15 century DR.

Location

Ascloakan's Fine Inn stood at the beginning of the curving Talankar's Lane and Hammaskarl's Ride, just across from the city's easternmost gate – the Basilisk Gate. It was located next-door to The Fool's Altar, a shrine to Finder Wyvernspur.
