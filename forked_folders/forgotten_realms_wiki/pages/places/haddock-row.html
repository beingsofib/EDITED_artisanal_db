Haddock Row

Haddock Row was a small street in Tilverton.

Description

Haddock Row was a dead-end side-street that ran off the main Street of the Sorceress as it curved through the Old Town of Tilverton. It was notable for being the home of the Whispering Witch, a pleasant inn with an odd reputation.
