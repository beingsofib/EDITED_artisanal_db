The Throne of Thought was an abandoned temple of Labelas Enoreth found in the city of Myth Drannor.

Location

It was located on Eldansyr's Ride, on the northwestern border of Myth Drannor's Sheshyrinnam district.

Structure

The five-story temples was built and as adapted tree-dwelling, a common form of architecture among the Cormanthan elves. Beneath it was an underground crypt-complex, with a tunnel that led to the Warrior's Gate tomb.

History

The Throne of Thought was abandoned, made to be left secretive, or outright ignored by the city's residents some time during or before the mid–7 century DR.
