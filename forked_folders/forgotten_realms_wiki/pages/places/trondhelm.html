Trondhelm was a small village located on Oman's Isle within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The village was located within the heart of the Old Stone Wood on the western coast of the island.

History

Thelgaar Ironhand, the King of Oman for the first half of the 14 century DR, was born in Trondhelm.

The castle of Caer Windlauer was once located along the coast at Trondhelm until sometime before the mid-14 century DR when its occupant, Omar McDauphin, used a ring of three wishes to wish for total isolation so that he and his family would never be robbed or harmed. Among other unintended consequences, the spell caused the entire castle and estate to be thrown into Realmspace. Once the estate was launched into wildspace, the ocean water filled the space left behind, creating Iron Keep Bay.

In the , King Dagdar of Moray was slain when he led an assault against Trondhelm.

Inhabitants

The village was initially a stronghold of the Northmen, but like the rest of the settlements on Oman's Isle, Trondhelm was overrun and ruled by giants by the later part of the 15 century DR.
