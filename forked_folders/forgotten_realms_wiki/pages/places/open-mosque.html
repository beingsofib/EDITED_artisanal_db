The Open Mosque was a temple devoted to the Zakharan pantheon located in Qudra in north Zakhara.

Description

The blue tiles of the Open Mosque stood out in stark contrast to the muted grays seen throughout the city. The mosque attracted worshipers within Qudra who were not devoted to Vataqatal.

History

In honor of the valiant service of the mamluks. the eighth Grand Caliph of Zakhara sent his finest architects and engineers to Qudra to construct a mosque as their reward.
