The Watchful Turtle was a warehouse business in the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale. Owned by Agrath Dundai, it was in operation by 1366 DR and through 1370 DR.

Location

The warehouses stood beside the bridge over the Delimbiyr. The carvings of dragon turtle heads on the bridge, watching over the warehouses, gave the business its name.

Services

The Watchful Turtled rented out secure storage space, protected by guards but unheated. The cost was 1 silver piece per crate per month. Time less than a month still incurred the full cost, but if the goods were removed within a tenday, there was a refund of 4 copper pieces.
