The Whispering Maiden was an inn of Zazesspur in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The inn was sited in the Garden District of the city.

Interior

There were at least two storeys to the building.

Atmosphere

The place was luxurious and discreet.

Services

An example breakfast served there consisted of quail's eggs and bacon with spiced ale; The inn had become famous for that drink.
