The Basilisk Gate was a gate and local landmark in Baldur's Gate, situated in the outer city wall, that separated the Lower City district of Eastway from the Outer City district of Stonyeyes.

The gate acted as the eastern passageway in and out of the city along the Coast Way, which passed through the Outer City and eventually connected to the nation of Elturgard to the east, and the Lands of Intrigue to the south.

Description

Basilisk Gate was distinctive in that it was lined with a number of statues,

Within the gate were offices of the Flaming Fist, along with a few single-occupant prison cells.

Notable Inhabitants

  • Flame Daryn Falburn, an officer of the Flaming Fist who made her offices within the second story of the Basilisk Gate.
