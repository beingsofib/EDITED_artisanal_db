The Bare Bones Hill was a hilly plot of land nestled between the Halondar and the Aphrunn Mountains in the southwestern part of the Halondar Valley, Turmish.

Description

The Bare Bones Hill was a scenic hill favored by the lovers seeking a tryst during annual celebrations of the Feast of the Moon. The area received increased patrols following the Time of Troubles and increased activity of Malarite cults seeking to slay unwitting lovers. Despite its popularity for romantic encounters, the Bare Bones Hill was an allegedly haunted spot.
