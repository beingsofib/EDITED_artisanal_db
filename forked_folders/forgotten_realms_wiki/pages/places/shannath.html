Shannath was a small settlement found in Sossal in the Cold Lands of northeast Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Geography

Shannath was located in the far western reaches of Sossal along the southwestern slopes of the Armridge Mountains. The settlement was connected to both Dharvil to the east, and Uldfest to the northwest, by an unnamed road.
