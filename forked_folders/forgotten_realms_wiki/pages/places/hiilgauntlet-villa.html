Hiilgauntlet Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Hiilgauntlet.

Location

The villa was located in the north section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. Portions of the villa were located on Gorl Street, Sighing Maiden's Walk, Ivory Street, and the Street of the Singing Dolphin.

Description

The walled estate contained four separate buildings that were all three stories tall. Two of the buildings were much larger than the others. These were located in the southeast and southwest corners of the villa.
