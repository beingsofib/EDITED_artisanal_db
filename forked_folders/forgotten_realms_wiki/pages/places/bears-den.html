The Bear's Den was an inn in the town of Thunderstone in Cormyr in the late 1360s DR, run by Derdek Deepingdell.

It was a large and comfortable establishment, where noise was frowned upon.

Rooms in the Bear's Den cost 7 gp a night, while a meal and drink were 3 gp. A bath could be had for 5 sp, and a horse could be stabled overnight for 1 gp.
