Fort Calim, also known as "Shoonach's Sunset", was one of the main border forts of County Calimmon in Tethyr, together known as the Calimmon Garrisons.

Location

This fort was located in eastern Calimmon, north of the River Agis and very close to the border of County Monteshi, the ruins of Shoonach, and Fort Teqlar.

Structure

Like most of the Calimmon Garrisons, this fortress had a simple but effective stone construction. Its walls were ten-to-fifteen-feet thick (3.0–4.6 meters) and 20 feet high (6.1 meters) with a deep, empty trench around them.

Defenses

Fort Calim was defended by around 200 Tethyrian soldiers, overseen by 10 officers and the fort commander.

History

The Fort was built on the ancient foundations of a long-lost dwarven fortress, As of the 14 century DR, the fort was approximately 600 years old. Due to its position close to Shoonach, it served equally as a bulwark against Calishite aggression and monsters emerging from the Shoonach ruins.
