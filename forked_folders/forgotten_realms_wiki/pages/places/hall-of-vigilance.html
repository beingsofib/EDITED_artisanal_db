The Hall of Vigilance was a temple of Helm, within the city of Sundabar, that possessed an imposing grandeur.

Geography

This temple was located on the west side of Sundabar, just within the Rivergate.

Activities

Suspicious individuals were often brought before the priests of this temple for questioning under the effects of a detect lie spell.

Inhabitants

In the mid-14 century DR, the Hall of Vigilance were governed by High Guardian Ruthard Fourl. In the late 14 century DR, he was succeeded by Mraskin Thoelaunth.
