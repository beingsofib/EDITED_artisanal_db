The Temple of Bountiful Joy was a temple dedicated to Isis, the Mulhorandi goddess of agriculture and fertility.

Location

This temple was located in Skuld, the capital city of Mulhorand,

History

As of the 14 century DR, it was considered to be among the most prominent temples of Isis, along with the Mystic Cornucopia.
