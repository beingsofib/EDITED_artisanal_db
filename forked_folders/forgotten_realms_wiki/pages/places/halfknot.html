Halfknot was a small village somewhere in Sembia.

Geography

During the winter, the roads got really muddy.

Trade

Halfknot depended on travelers' money to supplement its local industry. There was a local foundry.

Demographics

The village was mixed, with humans, dwarves, and gnomes.

Government

They had a part-time magistrate, Grangy Guerner, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Notable Locations

  • The Dragon Defeated, an inn which later rebranded as the Royal Rescue
