The Hammer and Shield Inn stood at the central square of Thundertree, a logging community at the edge of the Neverwinter Wood in the late 14 century DR.

Interior

The establishment was frequented by Thundertree woodsmen and barge captains who transported lumber down the Neverwinter River.

Services

The Hammer and Shield Inn offered a good meal and a good night's rest.
