The Temple of Finder Wyvernspur was a rather modest temple to the deity of the same name in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This building stood roughly three stories tall.

History

Operating as a commonplace street-level shop up until the , this building was then purchased and converted into a temple. However, the building was largely unaltered from its prior state.
