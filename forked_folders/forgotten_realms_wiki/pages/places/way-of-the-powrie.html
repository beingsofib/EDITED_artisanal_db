Way of the Powrie

The Way of the Powrie was a trail that ran though the Moonsea North.

Geography

The road started at the village of Ilinvur (where it connected to the Ilinvur Trail), and stretched north over , skirting the eastern edge of the Ride before it crossed into the Tortured Lands. The trail ended at the gnoll community of Frozen Flindyke where a bridge crossed the River Pelauvir, but the way continued northwards along the Remorhaz Ride.
