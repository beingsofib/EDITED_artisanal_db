Drik Hargunen was a duergar city in the Underdark region of Faerûn.

Geography

The city was built around a natural rock chimney and iron stairways connected the various levels.

Inhabitants

The duergar of Drik Hargunen appeared to be more tolerant than many of their kin and allowed some human slavers and traders to live in the city.

History

In 1480 DR, the duergar of Drik Hargunen devised a rune magic ritual that infected the dwarven god Moradin's River of Gold, his tangible link to Toril, and caused the Stoneplague.
