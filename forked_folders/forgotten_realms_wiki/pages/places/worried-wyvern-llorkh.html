Worried Wyvern (Llorkh)

Building
nameWorried Wyvern
typeInn
locationLlorkh, Savage Frontier
ownerPhintarn's brother

The Worried Wyvern was an inn in the town of Llorkh in the 1350s DR, owned by the brother of Lord Phintarn "Redblade".

Not long after Phintarn was murdered in a Zhentarim takeover shortly before the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR, his brother died mysteriously one night. The Worried Wyvern was razed the very next day.
