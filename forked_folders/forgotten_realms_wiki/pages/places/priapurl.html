Priapurl () was a small town located on the Trader's Road.

Government

As of 1372 DR, the ruler of Priapurl was Tar Hurara, who had descended from bandits but was often away from the town spending her time in the court of Cormyr.

History

In the , Priapurl became besieged by a one-orc army Slogg Ogrum. Slogg by his lonesome managed to almost bring the town to its knees if not for the Mindulgulph Mercenary Company, who eventually slew the threat. Disturbingly enough, no-one even uncovered the reason for the orc's attack. His impressive stash of powerful weapons was the only thing recovered from the siege, including the powerful crossbow – Ogrum's Siegebow.

The Mindulgulph Mercenary Company was a mercenary company of monstrous humanoids and other monsters who maintained Mindulgulph Castle overlooking the town in the hills to the south.
