Flipcoin Alley

Flipcoin Alley was a back alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 century DR.

Location

Flipcoin Alley was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Field Ward. It was wedged between Burnt Inn Street and the wall of the Sea Ward. Flipcoin Alley branched south from Burnt Inn Street and branched several more times within its neighborhood.
