Stationers' Hall was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild of the same name.

Location

Stationers' Hall was located along the High Road and the Way of the Dragon, in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This guildhall stood two-stories tall.

Services

Parchment was produced within this building. It was supplied to members of the Stationers' Guild, along with other products of their trade, at a discounted price.

Reputation

Like other guildhalls, Stationers' Hall was considered by locals to be a landmark.

History

Jalaerho Kloskurmrundur was the Guildmaster Emeritus of the guild in the late 14 century DR. In the , Jalaerho published a book called A Seat That Draws Blood: Ten Years a Guildmaster, recording a decade of his career and personal musings.
