The Maridlands were a short-lived realm belonging to the marid Ajhuu and his followers, former vassals of the great genie Calim.

Description

Though also an imperial conqueror like Calim, Ajhuu was noticably less tyrannical towards his subjects, and allowed them greater freedoms than in the Calim Empire.

Geography

The Maridlands split from the Calim Empire, taking the lands west of the Alimir Mountains centered on the newly-built coastal city of Ajhuutal.

History

When Calim paused his expansionism in in order to avoid the ire of the local Shanatar dwarves and the elves of Tethir and Darthiir, some of his vassals took this as a sign of weakness. When he turned his efforts to exterminating the giants and dragons of the Marching Mountains, the marid Ajhuu seized the eastern extents of the Calim Empire for himself, forming the Maridlands. By the time Calim noticed the treachery and reoriented his forces in , Ajhuu was well-established and fought to maintain his independence, leading to the Steam Clashes. Unable to bear the appearance of fallibility any longer (an unacceptable state for a tyrannical overlord), in Calim caused the Shattering, which obliterated Ajhuu, his capital, the Maridlands, and a good chunk of the Calim Empire with it.
