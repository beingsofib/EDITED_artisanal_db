Dukars' Academy

Dukars' Academy was an academy of magic in the underwater city of Myth Nantar, comparable to a bards' college.

It comprised four college buildings, one for each order, and a fifth octagonal building known as the Paragon's College.

The buildings appeared disconnected on the surface, but underneath the city they were connected with tunnels to allow easy access.
