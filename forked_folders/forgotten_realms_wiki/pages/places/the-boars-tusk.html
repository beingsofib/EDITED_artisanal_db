The Boar's Tusk was an inn located in the town of Caer Corwell on the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles during the mid–14 century DR.

Inhabitants

The Boar's Tusk was owned and operated by an old hunter named Garek. The majority of people found within the inn were men.
