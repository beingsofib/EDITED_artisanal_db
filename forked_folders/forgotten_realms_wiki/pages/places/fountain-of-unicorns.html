Fountain of Unicorns

The Fountain of Unicorns was a small spring located within the Glade of Life in the High Forest of northwest Faerûn.

Geography

The spring was located near the center of the Glade of Life which was a holy site of Shiallia. The waters from the spring fed the Unicorn Run.
