Guards Court

Guards Court was a hard-packed dirt and gravel paved cul-de-sac in the southern section of the Castle Ward of Waterdeep.

Geography

This cul-de-sac originally stretched south off of Niles Way, ending in a dead-end block of buildings that were bounded by Crossbow Lane, Niles Way, and Snail Street.
