Oar Alley

Oar Alley was a minor avenue of hard-packed earth and gravel in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Location

This alleyway ran from southwest to northeast, extending off of Ship Street and terminating into a dead end.

Notable Locations

  • The Blue Mermaid tavern stood at the southwest corner of this alley.
  • Shippers' Hall.
