Tulach was a small town in the land of Chessenta during the 15 century DR.

Geography

It rested between the hills of the Maerwatch and the Adder River on the western edge of the Fields of Oriam, roughly centered between Luthcheq, Pandrick, Maerchlin, and Oslin.

Government

Despite its small population of 1,100, Tulach was ruled by a self-proclaimed King. He was known for sending mercenaries to eliminate rust monsters in the mines of the Maerwatch, which were rich in metals like copper and silver. The rust monsters inevitably returned, so he would send more hirelings every decade to clear them out again.
