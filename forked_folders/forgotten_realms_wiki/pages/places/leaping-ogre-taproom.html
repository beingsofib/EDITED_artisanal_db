The Leaping Ogre Taproom was an eatery on the outskirts of Telflamm found just outside the legal city limits.

Description

It was a rambling edifice; services for traders such as stables, warehouses, carts sprang all around it. According to some, caravans headed for the Golden Way used the construction as a touchstone; every morning at dawn, as of the , work was assigned to arriving mercenaries. Raidon Kane found out as much that year.

Private rooms were available.
