Withermeet was a small village found in the Barony of Polten in Damara.

Geography

Withermeet sat along the eastern bank of Lake Mogador about halfway between Trailsend and Daleport on the Merchant's Run.

Description

The village proper consisted of several dozen structures catering mostly to travelers passing through on their way north. Viable land surrounded the village, earning the attention of those who sought to develop the area but the townsfolk refused all offers.

Inhabitants

The folk of Withermeet were highly conservative individuals who resisted change over the few centuries since the town's founding.
