The Dungeon (Tavern)

Building
nameThe Dungeon
typeTavern
locationSkuld, Mulhorand

The Dungeon was a tavern located in the capital city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Dungeon was located in the eastern section of Skuld in the Crafts District in the southwest section of the district near the gateway to the Central Ward.
