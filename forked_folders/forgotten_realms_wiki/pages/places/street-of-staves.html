Street of Staves

The Street of Staves was a wide city street that started at the Promenade and ended in a junction with Blade Lane.

Description

The Dragon's Jaws, one of the more famous drinking establishments in Suzail, stood on the southwestern corner of the grand Promenade and the Street of Staves. It curved southeast and ran in almost a straight line further east, crossing Torch Street and ending in a T-junction with the curved Blade Lane.

Notable Locations

  • The Wedding Knight, a store that dealt in luxurious clothes and fabrics, frequented by Suzail's nobility.
