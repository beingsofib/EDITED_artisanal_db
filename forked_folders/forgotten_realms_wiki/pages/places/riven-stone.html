The Riven Stone was a landmark in Faerûn.

Description

The landmark was an immense pitch-black helm-like conical stone, cloven down the middle, through which the road went.

Geography

The Riven Stone was down a road from Anthather's Mill, in the midst of a forest.

History

In the , Elminster Aumar, a Chosen of Mystra, sought out the Riven Stone as part of a quest commanded by the goddess Mystra. There, he met Dasumia, whom he was commanded by the goddess to serve.

Rumors & Legends

Curiously, people around the stone remarked on how there was nothing there but a stone, though Elminster remarked someone would probably say it was haunted.
