Crown & Raven Scriveners was a scrivener's shop in Shadowdale during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The shop stood within view of the Twisted Tower.

Services

Almost certainly a scriptorium, people sometimes ordered signs made there. There was also brushwork; some of the staff rendered coats of arms, or penned letters for customers.

History

The shop was in business as of , when the owner, Maervidal Iloster, was discovered by the Zhentarim to be working for the Harpers too. He had at the moment at least one hire, a young woman named Rindee.
