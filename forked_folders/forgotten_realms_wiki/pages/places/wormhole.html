The Wormhole was a secret location within the City of Brass in 1367 DR.

Activities

By an incredible stroke of luck, or genius, the salamander population of the City of Brass was able to keep the location of this hideout a secret from the efreet for ages.

The Wormhole was used as a meeting place by salamanders where they helped escaped slaves and planned to move stolen weapons outside of the city to their salamander kin to help in their constant fight with the efreet.
