Redhand Pool

Redhand Pool was a small lake found near the village of Eveningstar in Cormyr.

Geography

Redhand Pool was located just northwest of Eveningstar, within the Eveningstar Gorge. The Starwater River entered along its northwestern shore and continued through Eveningstar, exiting the lake on its southeastern shore.
