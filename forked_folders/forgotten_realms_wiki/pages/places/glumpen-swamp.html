Glumpen Swamp was a swamp found in the greater marshlands of Thar, in the Moonsea North region of Faerûn.

Geography

It was found in the western area of Thar, just northwest of Citadel Ankhalus.

Geographical Features

The waters of the swamp originated from Lake Longreach found to the northwest in the Ride.
