The Royal Mint was the location where all production of Halruaa's currency occurred.

Location

The Royal Mint was located in Aluarim in the center of Halruaa.

History

Halruaa's unique electrum coins, called skie, were produced in the Royal Mint from electrum mined to the east in Maeruhal.
