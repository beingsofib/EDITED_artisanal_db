Ironhar was the biggest of the "Royal Keeps" of Eskorn, Laerakond, as of 1479 DR.

Background

Ironhar was home to several wealthy collectors, who often hired adventurers to explore old ruins in eastern Eskorn, and to retrieve old relics for them.

Notable inhabitants

  • Dramar Lornen, a collector obsessed with the Dawn Titan's Spire.
