Matilda's Boarding House

nameMatilda's Boarding House
typeInn
locationMossbridges, the Vast
ownerMatilda circa 1371 DR
servicesLodgings

Matilda's Boarding House was one of few businesses that offered lodgings in the town of Mossbridges in the late 14 century DR.

Servies

Matilda's Boarding House offered apartments priced at two silver pieces per night per person; morningfeast included in the price.

History

In the late 14 century DR, the Boarding House was run by the woman named Matilda.
