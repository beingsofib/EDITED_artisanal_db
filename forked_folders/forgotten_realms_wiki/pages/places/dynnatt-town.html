Dynnatt was a small town located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Dynnatt was located in central Corwell in southern Gwynneth just southwest of Caer Corwell. The town was located among the moors in between the Corwell Road and Llyrath Forest.
