Tyrant's Road

The Tyrant's Road, formerly known as the Coast Road up until 1370 DR, was a trail found along the north banks of the the Lake of Steam in south Faerûn. It was rarely ever featured on maps made by northerners.

Description

Tyrant's Road was paved and narrower than the more prominent Golden Road.

Geography

The trail began where Darkhope's Road ended in the west at the River Mintar. The Tyrant's Road cut east along the shore of the Lake of Steam, terminating in Saelmur.

History

In the , the name of the road changed to "Tyrant's Road" in reference to Teldorn Darkhope.
