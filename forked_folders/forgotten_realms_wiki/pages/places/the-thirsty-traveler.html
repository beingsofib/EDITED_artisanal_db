The Thirsty Traveler was a popular tavern and an eatery in the hamlet of Skull Crag in the Storm Horn Mountains of Cormyr in the 14 century DR.

Location

The tavern was located next-door to the east to caravan stables wafting a delightful aroma of fresh manure.

Services

The Thirsty Traveler catered to caravaners and their hireling offers them well-deserved slake and grub after a long and dangerous road across the mountains. The tavern's kitchens were ran by a singe haggish old woman who did not tolerate anyone invading her domain.

History

In the mid-14 century DR, the Thirsty Traveler was a well-renowned establishment, popular among the travelers of the High Road. During that time, the tavern was visited by a group pf caravan guards who would later become the heroes of Skull Crag.
