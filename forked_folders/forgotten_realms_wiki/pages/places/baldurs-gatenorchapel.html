Norchapel was a district in the Outer City of Baldur's Gate.

The neighborhood was located some distance beyond the Basilisk Gate, sharing poorly defined borders with the Stonyeyes and Whitkeep neighborhoods. Its tenement buildings surrounded the walled district of Little Calimshan. It was known as the quietest of the Outer City districts.

Inhabitants

As with many Outer City neighborhoods, Norchapel was home to many laborers and criminal gang members. As of 1482 DR, Stepan Groat, a Flaming Fist manip, lived in one of the tenements.
