Sonnmorndin was a fortress located on the island of Ruathym.

History

The fortress was constructed in on Arauwurbarak which would later become known as Ruathym. Dwarven marines from the Sailors of the Mountainous Waves from Haunghdannar built Sonnmorndin to be used as a naval base for their excursions into the sea.
