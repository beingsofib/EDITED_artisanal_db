Twilight Tor was a small dwarf village in the Starmetal Hills in the 1480s DR. It lay on the shore of a small mountain lake, Lake Glorfindar.

The village was made up mainly of worshipers of Marthammor Duin. It was largely unknown, but the villagers were welcoming of friendly travelers who visited. There were excellent conditions for stargazing .

The villagers threw a Simril festival most years, with stargazing at night and various games and celebrations.
