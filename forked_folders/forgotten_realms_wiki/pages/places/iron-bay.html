Iron Bay

Iron Bay was a large bay located on the northern coast of Oman's Isle within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Iron Bay was found on the north shore of Oman's Isle.

Description

The waters of Iron Bay were steel gray in color. The town of Iron Keep was located along the bay's eastern shore with the massive Iron Keep itself towering above on a rocky hill. Iron Bay was considered the best natural harbor available to the northmen, with a wide anchorage and smooth beaches for parking longships. The bay's mouth was less than wide and was flanked by rocky mountains on either side, which shielded the inner the bay from the worst of the storms coming in from the sea.

History

As of the mid–14 century DR, Iron Bay was under the control of the Northman kingdom of Oman. After they were expelled in the , the fomorians who came to rule Oman's Isle filled the bay with traps in order to prevent any large ships from approaching the ruins of the Iron Keep.
