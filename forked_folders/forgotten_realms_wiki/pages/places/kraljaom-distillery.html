Kraljaom Distillery

Building
nameKraljaom Distillery

locationMyratma, Tethyr

The Kraljaom Distillery was a famous producer of ales, beers, ciders, wines, and other alcohol, located in the western half of the city of Myratma.

Notable Products

Many of the distillery's wares were known throughout Faerûn, as they were sold both locally and through Aurora's Emporium. Among them were Purple Hills Cider, Shanale lagers, and wines made from grapes found only in County Calimmon.
