Sambril Lane

Sambril Lane was a minor avenue of hard packed dirt and gravel in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Location

This alleyway ran from north to south, extending off from the Way of the Dragon and terminating at Smuggler's Run.

Notable Locations

  • Gelfuril the Trader, a shop that stood at the intersection of Sambril Lane and Smuggler's Run.
  • Thomm Warehouse.
