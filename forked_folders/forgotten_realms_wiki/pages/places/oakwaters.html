Oakwaters

Oakwaters was a bathing pool open to the public located in the city of Daerlun in Sembia in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The pool was located in the north section of the city near the House of Firehair.

Description

At roughly in diameter, Oakwaters was quite large and rivaled the pond found in the nearby Wild Wood in size.
