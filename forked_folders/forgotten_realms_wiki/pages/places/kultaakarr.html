Kultaakarr, also called the Throne of the Titan Lords, was a realm of giants in the Cold Lands founded in the 15 century DR.

Geography

The realm was located in northern Vaasa. It was bordered to the north by the Great Glacier and the Surykyk Range mountains, and to the south by the Lugsaas Chain mountains. It encompassed the Sea of Frozen Tears.

Government

Kultaakarr was ruled by titan lords from their provisional capital of Baunilorotha on the northern coast of the Sea of Frozen Tears.

Defenses

In the late 15 century DR, the titans had begun raising an army that had the potential to destabilize the region. Their frost giant warriors and berserkers favored greataxes while their spellcasters favored flails and ice magic, and could summon mighty frost titans.

History

In the distant past, Kultaakarr was a subkingdom of Ostoria and was lost beneath the ice with the advance of the Great Glacier around . Once the Glacier began to retreat after 1038 DR, giants began returning to the region in increasing numbers.

From Kultaakarr, frost giants explored the Great Glacier and sought the ruins of Iyraclea’s palace. In the , a band of Auril-worshiping frost giants were called to these ruins by a Zhengyian artifact, and slew a band of Banite Warlock Knights who were likewise seeking this artifact.

Tensions remained between the Warlock Knights and Kultaakarr, although this had yet to escalate into war as of the . By this time, the titans were also opposed by the gold dragon Tamarand, the King of Justice.

A decade or so later, Annam All-Father broke the Ordning, thus throwing the hierarchies of all giant communities in Faerûn into chaos.
