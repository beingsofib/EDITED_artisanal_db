Jolly Warrior Inn

locationWestgate, Dragon Coast

The Jolly Warrior Inn was a posh and expensive inn located in Westgate.

Location

The Jolly Warrior Inn was located just inside the West Gate on Westgate Market Street.

Services

The Jolly Warrior catered mostly to visiting nobles in search of a relaxing atmosphere.

History

In 1357 DR, Giogioni Wyvernspur, a nobleman on a mission for Azoun Obarskyr IV, stayed at the Jolly Warrior Inn. Even though the city was on lockdown after an explosion that lit up the skies that caused the deaths of Mistinarperadnacles Hai Draco and the Abomination of Moander, and another explosion the day later that signaled the death of the evil wizard Cassana, Giogioni was able to enter the city and reach the Inn.
