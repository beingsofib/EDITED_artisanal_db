Codscove was a small village located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The village was located along the northeastern coast of the island on the south side of the Strait of Oman on the Codsbay just northwest of the Codsbay Mire. The town was surrounded by the trees of the Winterglen.

History

During the height of the Northlander kingdom of Oman in the 14 century DR, Codscove fell within Oman's territory on Gwynneth.

By the late 15 century DR, following the rise of Sarifal, Codscove lay in ruins.
