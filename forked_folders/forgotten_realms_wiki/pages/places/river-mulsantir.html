River Mulsantir

The River Mulsantir was a prominent river found between Rashemen and Thay in the Unapproachable East.

Geography

The northern section of the River Mulsantir flowed north into Lake Ashane

while the southern section flowed west from the Sunrise Mountains to form Lake Mulsantir in southern Rashemen. The river formed part of the border between Rashemen and Thay as it passed through the Gorge of Gauros.

Description

The neck of the river found between Lake Ashane and Lake Mulsantir was considerably wider when compared to the section of the river that flowed south from Lake Mulsantir.

Notable Locations

Mulsantir was located along the river's north shore where it flowed out of Lake Ashane and into Mulsantir Lake.
