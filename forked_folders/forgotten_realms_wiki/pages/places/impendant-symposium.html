Impendant Symposium

Impendant Symposium is a two round adventure module set in the Living City organized play program. It was published in October 1998, and is intended exclusively for wizard adventurers of all levels.

Synopsis

The Symposium organized by the trans-dimensional mages guild called the Order of the Eternals began on the 14 of the month, taking place at the humble Glittering Star Inn of Ravens Bluff with a number of wizard adventurers from the Living City invited to participate at no cost.
