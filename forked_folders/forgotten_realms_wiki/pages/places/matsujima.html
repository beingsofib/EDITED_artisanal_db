Matsujima was an island located in Kozakura.

Description

Located northeast of Gizan Province, Matsujima stands out with its towering cliffs and prominently north-facing slopes. This distinctive geography made it easily recognizable. The passage that stretched between the Gizan coast and Matsujima represented the deepest and most secure channel in the southern part of the Inland Sea. While the island hosted a handful of fishing villages, its primary claim to fame lied in serving as a notable landmark denoting the presence of this channel.
