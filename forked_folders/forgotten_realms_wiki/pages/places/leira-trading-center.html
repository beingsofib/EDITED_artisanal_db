The Leira Trading Center was the largest and most beautiful building on Selûne. It contained over fifty spelljamming docks and was the center of commerce of the entire moon.

Location

The Trading Center was located on the far side of Selûne, in the center of the equatorial region. It was surrounded by thousands of smaller colorful residential and commercial buildings, such as theaters and other businesses.

Structure

The enormous building was over 300 ft (91 m) tall. Its numerous spelljamming dry docks were the dominant feature of the structure.

Interior

The spelljamming docks were crewed by a dedicated staff that made sure that all ships were safely maneuvered in and out of the port.

Atmosphere

All working hands and inhabitants were very welcoming to spelljamming crews, who were generally free to roam anywhere except for a few off-limits secret locations.

This cordiality did not extend to Torilians, however. Since Selûnians were paranoid about spies from Toril and extremely intolerant of anyone trying to pry into their military secrets, any visitors claiming to be from Toril were likely to be arrested and tried for espionage. Conviction meant immediate decapitation.

Services

Trade at the Trading Center, like elsewhere on Selûne, was very active. Selûnians enjoyed importing items of great beauty and comfort, as well as deadly magic items in case an assault from Toril were to happen, and they consumed all forms of art and entertainment.

Inhabitants

The dry docks of the Leira Trading Center were supervised by the human Icon Drowmaster, who was married to a halfling woman. He was friendly to humanoid crews, except for lizardfolk, tolerated beholder crews if they wanted to trade, and disliked illithids and neogi. His refusal to allow a neogi ship to dock earned him a scar on his face.
