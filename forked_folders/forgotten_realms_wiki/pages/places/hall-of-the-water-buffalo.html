The Hall of the Water Buffalo was a shrine dedicated to Yampa found within the Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert of the Hordelands during the mid 14 century DR.

Description

The Hall was located in the center of the upper galleries level of the temple. The Hall was roughly long. A pair of chambers were found along the hall, each measuring wide by long. The rearmost chamber contained a statue of Yampa the water buffalo.
