The Pool of Tefnut was an oasis that would appear and disappear in Raurin. It was named for the goddess Tefnut, one of the members of the Pharaonic pantheon who was forced to stay behind instead of traveling to Realmspace to become part of the Mulhorandi pantheon.

Overview

The pool of Tefnut would remain in its location until a good-aligned person looked into its waters. They would see two almond-shaped eyes, which would volunteer to answer their questions. Once they had asked three questions, the oasis would vanish. All travelers who had witnessed the oasis would find their water reserves refilled, and their thirst quenched.
