The Gaol Tower was a prison located in the capital city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The Gaol Tower stood in the eastern section of Skuld in the Crafts District in the northwest section of the district.

Description

A quartet of guard towers were arranged around the center of the Gaol Tower.
