Shippers' Storage was a warehouse in Waterdeep that was owned by the Waterdhavian guild known as the Fellowship of Salters, Packers, and Joiners.

Location

This warehouse was located in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This warehouse was three stories tall.
