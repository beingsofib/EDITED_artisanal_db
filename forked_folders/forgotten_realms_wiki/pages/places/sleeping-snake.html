The Sleeping Snake was a tavern in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Location

This store stood on Dock Street, across from the shop Nestaur's Ropes.

Structure

The Sleeping Snake's structure stood only a single story tall.

Interior

This tavern was roughly furnished with furniture that were all hastily mended.

Atmosphere

The Sleeping Snake had a rather rowdy atmosphere to it, being frequented by dockhands patrons who often broke the establishment's furniture over each others' heads.

Services

In addition to the standard tavern fare, the Sleeping Snake employed human, half-elf, and half-orc dancers that performed atop of tables. Sometimes even singing. These dancers were willing to do activities besides dancing for a negotiable price. Anyone who harmed the dancers was charged a fine of a single silver piece.

Rumors & Legends

Rumors around the Sleeping Snake suggested that one could encounter a fence for stolen goods within the establishment. In truth this rumor was false, but utilized by the Red Sashes member, to spy on those engaging in shady business.
