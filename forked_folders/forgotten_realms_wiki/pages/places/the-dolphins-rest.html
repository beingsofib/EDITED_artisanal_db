The Dolphin's Rest

Building
nameThe Dolphin's Rest
typeTavern
locationTu Pe, Silla, Koryo

The Dolphin's Rest was a tavern in the city of Tu Pe in Koryo around 1357 DR. It stood on the waterfront, near the middle of the city.
