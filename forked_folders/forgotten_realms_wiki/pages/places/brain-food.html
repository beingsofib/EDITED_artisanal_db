Brain Food

Building
nameBrain Food
typeRestaurant
locationShip Street, Dock Ward, Waterdeep

Brain Food was an eatery in Waterdeep in the late 14 century DR. It was one in a chain of eateries and was primarily, infamously, known for being ran by a mindflayer.

Location

This eatery stood along Ship Street, next to the festhall Velvet Tentacles, in the Dock Ward district of Waterdeep.

Services

Brain Food was primarily known for serving fish balls.
