The Hall of Heroes was a tomb located within the City of the Dead in Waterdeep.

Location

The Hall of Heroes was located in the southern section of the City of the Dead.

Description

Blue marble was used for the tomb's walls. The interior was decorated with trophies of famous fallen warriors including the sword hilt of Raurlor. One of Anacaster's magical portals within the tomb lead to a large grassy plane where the dead were interred in rows.

History

The tomb was used to honor those fighters and warriors of Waterdeep who died in battle.
