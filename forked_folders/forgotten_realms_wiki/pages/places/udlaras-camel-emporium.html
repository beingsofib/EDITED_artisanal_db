Udlara's Camel Emporium was a camel-selling and rental business owned by Udlara Thaerintauhn in the 14 century DR.

Location

Udlara's Emporium was across the bazaar in the Bitter Well oasis.

Structure

The tent appeared rather worn down, but it was actually an enchanted tent, called Udlara's tent.

Interior

The interior was actually clean, spacious, and well-lit.

Services

Udlara sold camels and camel-related services. Depending on their opinion of the buyer, the asking price for a camel could go from 45 to 80 gp.

Defenses

The magical properties of the tent made it difficult to damage without using magical weapons; even those had to be stronger than the bare minimum in order to deal damage.

History

Udlara's Camel Emporium had made arrangements with Cormyr to supply some of their adventurers with camels and guides, but Udlara renegotiated with the agents as they arrived.
