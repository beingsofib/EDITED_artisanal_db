Trank River

The Trank River was a river found in the Moonsea North region of North Faerûn.

Description

It was a calm and easily-navigable river with seldom-encountered rushing rapids.

Geography

It was located north of the Moonsea proper, in the northernmost reaches of the Eastern Heartlands.

Geographical Features

The Trank River originated in the Vanishing Hills and flowed southward for approximately .

As the waters continued streaming southward they flowed around the archipelago known as the Ringcurrent Islands, forming a circular eddy around them.

The Phlan Path crossed over the Trank River at the Trank River Bridge.

History

In the late 15 century DR, the lizardfolk of the Twilight Marsh carried out a series of raids against the farms nestled along the shores of the river, abducting farmers to appease their black dragon master, Throstulgrael.

Notable Locations

There were three notable farmsteads along the eastern bank of the Trank River, the Renon Farm, Alyin Farm, and the Tralio Farm.

The Crossing Inn was also situated at the east bank of the river at the point where it crossed the Phlan Path.
