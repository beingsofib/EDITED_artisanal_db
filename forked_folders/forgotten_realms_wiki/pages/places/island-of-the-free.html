The Island of the Free was a small settlement located on an earthmote in eastern Tethyr near the Omlarandin Mountains.

History

It was founded in approximately 1462 DR by the escaped slave and former gladiator Azad the Free. The settlement's primary source of income and only claim to fame was the gladiatorial arena Azad constructed. When Cephas returned to the island he freed the gladiators, many of whom decided to leave the settlement.
