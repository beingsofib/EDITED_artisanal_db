Cantrev O'Malley was a small town located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

O'Malley was located in the kingdom of Corwell in southern Gwynneth. The town was located along the halfway point on Corwell Road between Caer Corwell in the west and Kingsbay in the east. O'Malley sat among the foothills where the Highlands and Cambro Mountains met in the shadow of the hidden kingdom of Synnoria.

Defenses

As of the mid—14 century DR, O'Malley's ragtag militia was outfitted mostly with pitchforks, with only a few possessing swords or longbows. They were led by a ranger who was known to conscript travelers (or their mounts) into his service.

History

O'Malley fell into ruin following the conquest of Gwynneth and expulsion of the humans by the fey of Sarifal at the turn of the 15 century DR, and remained in ruins as of the late 15 century DR.

Inhabitants

The Ffolk of O'Malley kept cows and sheep.

Natives of O'Malley were known for their raucous celebrations. They had been known to celebrate victories over firbolg raiders from the Highlands by placing their heads upon spikes and dancing around them.

As of the mid–14 century DR, notable inhabitants included:

  • Colleen, the daughter of the Lord of O'Malley.
