Lasdur was an independant city located just south of the country of Aglarond during the mid–14 century DR.

Geography

The city lay on the coast of the Wizards' Reach on the Sea of Fallen Stars just south of the Yuirwood.

History

Lasdur was initially founded by the empire of Unther following the great wars between Narfell and Raumathar, but over the passage of time Unther lacked the strength to hold it and Lasdur eventually declared independence. Lasdur was destroyed in the Salamander War in a plot hatched by Hargid Tenslayer and his allies in the .
