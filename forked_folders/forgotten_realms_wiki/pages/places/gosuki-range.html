The Gosuki Range was a mountain range located in Kozakura.

Description

The Gosuki Range was located on the island of Tenmei. The heavily forested mountain range was located on the northeast part of the island.

Inhabitants

The Gosuki Range was home to the largest population of korobokuru in Kozakura.
