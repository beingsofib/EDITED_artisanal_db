Earth Mother's Bounty was a shop located in the Chauntean temple-farm of Goldenfields in the Dessarin Valley.

Location

It was located near the main gates of Goldenfields, visible to all who passed within.

Description

The shop was a stone building with an open facade. It featured a sign that proudly displayed the shop's name: Earth Mother's Bounty.

In front of the shop was a well-trodden roundabout decorated with a 20-foot-tall (6.1 meters) wooden statue of Chauntea. She was depicted as a rotund woman standing in a field of wheat.

Services

The shop sold various types of seeds as well as decorative cornucopias to the abbey's visitors.
