Snake Alley

Snake Alley was a small street located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

Snake Alley was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter. The alley meandered through the southern section of the Adventurers' Quarter, branching several times and connnecting with Grocer's Lane, Slop Street, and Kerrigan's Court.
