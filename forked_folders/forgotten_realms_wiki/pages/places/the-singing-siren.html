The Singing Siren was a rowdy and dangerous tavern found in Scardale Town within Scardale during the mid–14 century DR.

This establishment served as headquarters for the merchant-criminal Aldavius Darkhand and as such served as a haven for mercenaries and black market suppliers. Its proprietor was the thug known as Ulfgang Halfhand, a lieutenant within Darkhand's organization.
