Sulduskoon River

The Sulduskoon River was a major river in the kingdom of Tethyr.

Description

The river was wide and slow-moving.

Etymology

The river received its name from the tribe of Suldusk elves living within the Forest of Tethir.

History

Circa , the last survivors of High Shanatar were slain by Calishites on the shore of the Sulduskoon.
