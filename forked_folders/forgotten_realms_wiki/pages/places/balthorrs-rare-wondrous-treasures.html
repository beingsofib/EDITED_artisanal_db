Balthorr's Rare & Wondrous Treasures was a shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The shop stood a single story tall.

Location

Balthorr's Rare & Wondrous Treasures was located in the southeast section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward on the west side of the Street of the Sword just north of Buckle Alley.

Inhabitants

The shop was owned by its namesake, Balthorr Olaskos.

Services

Magic items and stolen property could be acquired within the shop.
