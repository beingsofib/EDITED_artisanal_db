The Tesh-Wave was a tavern located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The tavern was located in south Zhentil Keep near the waterfront of the Foreign Quarter. Its location outside of the main defensive wall provided it with a fantastic view of the River Tesh and the ruins of Zhentil Keep on the north bank.

Description

The signboard for the Tesh-Wave depicted a large wave on a river.

Activities

Adventuring groups eager to explore the ruins of the city launched their ventures from the Tesh-Wave. Captains of river boats and larger vessels from the Moonsea frequented the tavern, making it a great place to book passage.

Inhabitants

The Tesh-Wave was owned by a man named Koleth in the . A trio of serving wenches worked for him.
