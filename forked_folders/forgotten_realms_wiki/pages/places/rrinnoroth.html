Rrinnoroth was a settlement located within the dwarven realm of Shanatar. It was a ghost town by .

Geography

The ruins of Rrinnoroth were located beneath the Kuldin Peaks which were located in the surface realm of Tethyr. One of the only access points to the ruins of Drakkalor was down a tunnel which began in Rrinnoroth.

Inhabitants

The former dwarven settlement was now devoid of all life. It's sole occupant was the mighty red dragon Charvekkanathor. He used the ruins of Rrinnoroth as his lair and where he stored his treasure hoard. As a result, the ruins where heavily guarded with explosive traps and teleportation wards.

Trivia

Rrinnoroth, in the Dwarven language, meant "place over enemy land".
