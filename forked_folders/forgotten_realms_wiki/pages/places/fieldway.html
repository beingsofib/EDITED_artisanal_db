Fieldway

The Fieldway was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 century DR.

Location

The Fieldway was located in the western and central section of Waterdeep's Field Ward. It ran east to west in between the High Road and the Trollyard, spanning more than half of the Field Ward. The Fieldway was intersected by Nanze Street, Boltspring Alley, Burnt Inn Street, Firewine Alley, Soot Alley, Saltpork Street, and Prestige Lane.
