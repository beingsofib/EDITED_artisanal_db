The Crescent Court was the elven temple of Sehanine Moonbow within the fabled city of Myth Drannor.

Location

The temple was located on the Stroll of Minstrels, in the city's Temple Ward.

Structure

The temple itself was six stories tall, with one level that extended underground. Each of its towers were even taller, at eight stories tall each.

History

The temple was constructed in the , under direction of Miirphys Irithyl, the fourth Coronal of Cormanthyr.

Rumors & Legends

There persisted rumors that Coronal Miirphys remained within the temple grounds long after his death, as either some manner of ghost, baelnorn, or even as one of the Reverend Ones.
