Dormythyrr's Ride

Dormythyrr's Ride was the name given to a road in central Damara as of the late 15 century DR. It connected the Duchy of Brandiar and the Duchy of Carmathan.

Geography

The road connected Goliad, the capital city of Brandiar, on its northern end to Ravensburg, the capital city of Carmathan, on its southern end. It passed through the village of Canipe in Carmathan and crossed the Beaumaris River at the town of Brahms.

History

The road was named for Dormythyrr, a hero and regent of Brandiar following the defeat of Zhengyi the Witch-King in the Bloodstone Wars of 1359 DR.
