Rook Alley

Rook Alley was a small alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Rook Alley was located in the north section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. It branched off of the southern end of Kulzar's Alley, passing through a small neighborhood in between Heroes' Walk and the Fanebar.

History

The alley earned its name as it was the headquarters of an infamous thief called the Rook.
