Naranj Canal

The Naranj Canal was a prominent canal located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR.

Location

The Naranj Canal originated near the center of the Pyraculum amid a collection of bazaars, branching southeast from the Great Canal where it bisected the Foundry before connecting with the Sea of Fire near Krak al-Zinad. Along its path, Naranj Canal connected with the Riqaq Canal and the Munhani Canal.
