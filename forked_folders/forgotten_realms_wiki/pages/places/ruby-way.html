Ruby Way

Ruby Way was a reddish crystal road in Leuthilspar on the island of Evermeet.

Structure

Despite its name, the road was not made from ruby. Like the Diamond Road, the Ruby Way had a smooth, shiny appearance but was not slippery underfoot.
