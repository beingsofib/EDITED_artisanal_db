Snome Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Snome.

Location

The Snome Villa was located in the North Ward of Waterdeep, on the corner of Phull Lane and Trollkill Street. It stood across from the Phull Villa.

Structure

Like many Waterdhavian villas in the North Ward, this was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden with elaborate adornments. Inside the walls of the estate were two high-quality buildings that both stood two stories tall, one being a mansion and the other a guesthouse.
