The River Trail Gate was the western entry point into lower half of the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The River Trail Gate sat on the western side of the Foreign Quarter on the southern bank of the River Tesh. It was situated on the Tesh Trail where the road entered the Foreign Quarter to connect with Slingshot Way.

Defenses

The gate was flanked by a pair of guard towers.

Activities

The River Trail Gate provided access to the Foreign Quarter from the west for land-based traffic.

History

The gate was completely sealed during Cyric's attack on the city in the , to protect the Foreign Quarter from attack.
