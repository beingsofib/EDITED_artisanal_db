The Stone Crab Coster was a shop selling seafood in the city of Archenbridge during the 14 century DR.

Location

The shop was located on the northern part of town, near Pelter's Street.

Services

The shop specialized in selling freshwater crabs and crayfish, as well as various seafood from the Sea of Fallen Stars.
