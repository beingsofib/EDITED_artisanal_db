Mytela Halvasyn's house

Lady Mytela Halvasyn's house was a palace-like house in Velprintalar, Aglarond, once occupied by Lady Mytela Halvasyn, but, as of 1368 DR, was occupied by her descendants, who formed an anti-human organization called the Society of the Verdant arrow.
