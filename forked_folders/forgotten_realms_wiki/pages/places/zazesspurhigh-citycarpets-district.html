The Carpets District was a district of Zazesspur.

Description

The district was on the eastern side of the city, marked on its northeast end by the Dung Gate of Zazesspur.

Government

The district's representative to the city council was Jinjivar in .

History

During the Ten Black Days of Eleint, two temples, the Hall of Doom and the House of the Dawn Lord, were burnt down. The district became especially run down afterwards, though by the , Major Kantis became a major rallying figure for rebuilding efforts.
