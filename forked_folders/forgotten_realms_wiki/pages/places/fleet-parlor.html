Fleet Parlor

Building
nameFleet Parlor
typeReceiving Room
locationMalsander residence, Waterdeep

The Fleet Parlor was a room in the residence of the Malsanders in the 14 century DR.

Interior

The receiving room was overfull of gold details and portraits of the ships that the Cassalanters owned.

History

Lady Sylull Cassalanter (actually Laeral Silverhand in disguise) was asked to hold at the Fleet Parlor when she dropped in on Mrilla Cassalanter in the mid-14 century DR.
