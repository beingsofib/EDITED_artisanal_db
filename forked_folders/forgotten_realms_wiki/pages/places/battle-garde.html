Battle Garde

Battle Garde was the divine realm of Garagos, a Faerûnian demigod of war.

Cosmography

To those who subscribed to the Great Wheel cosmology, Battle Garde was said to be originally located in the plane known as Limbo. But following defeat at the hands of Tempus, Garagos's disposition changed and he no longer felt comfortable in Limbo, so moved his realm to the second layer of Pandemonium.

According to the World Tree cosmology, Garagos nomadically roamed the celestial plane of Warrior's Rest and declared whatever region he happened to be in as Battle Garde.
