Stonebow Street

Stonebow Street was a street that stretched from the Promenade to the curved Blade Lane in the eastern part of Suzail in Cormyr.

Description

The street crossed Torch Street to the south and Swordstars Lane to the north.

Notable Locations

  • Bindle's, a private tavern and club popular among merchants.
  • Blackgorgons, the tower home of the wizard Baskor in the late 14 century DR.
  • The Shrine to Lliira stood between the Swordstars Lane and the Blade Lane, on Stonebow Street's northern side.
