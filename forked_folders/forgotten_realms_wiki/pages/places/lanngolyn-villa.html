Lanngolyn Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Lanngolyn.

Location

The villa was located in the North Ward on the southeast corner of the intersection of Delzorin Street with the High Road. The east side of their property was bounded by Ilzantil Street.

Structure

The Lanngolyn Villa was a walled compound of one- and three-story buildings.
