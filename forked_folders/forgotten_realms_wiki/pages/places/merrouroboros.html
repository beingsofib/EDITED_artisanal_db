Merrouroboros was a super-continent in existence on the planet Abeir-Toril during the Days of Thunder and before the Sundering of Abeir from Toril.

Geography

Merrouroboros was bordered on the west by the Silver Sea and on the south by the Great Blue.

Inhabitants

The land of Merrouroboros was inhabited by the legendary creator races and the early jotunbrud and human tribes.

History

After the creation of the planet Abeir-Toril, that world had a great Ice Age, which ended in approximately . At this time, the continent of Faerûn did not yet exist; instead, Faerûn was joined with Kara-Tur, Maztica, and Zakhara as part of Merrouroboros.

The sarrukh were the first to establish an empire on Merrouroboros, ruling over most of the land that would later be Faerûn. Third came the batrachi empires, coming to prominence some 500 years later and peaking around . Lastly, the aearee empires emerged.

Merrouroboros ceased to exist around in the great cataclysm known as the First Sundering, which caused the super-continent to break apart into Faerûn, Maztica, and Katashaka, and the appearance of Evermeet.
