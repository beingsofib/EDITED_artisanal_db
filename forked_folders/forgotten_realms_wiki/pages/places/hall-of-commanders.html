The Hall of Commanders was part of the complex of the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Activities

The Hall of Commanders was a large area dedicated to the housing of various leaders of the mamluks, guards, and admirality. Each apartment was complimented by a host of servants.

Inhabitants

Notable inhabitants of the Hall of Commanders included Thokkor, Preani Qin, and Sedji al-Sanje.
