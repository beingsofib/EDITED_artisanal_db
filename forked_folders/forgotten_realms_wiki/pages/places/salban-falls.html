Salban Falls

Salban Falls was a massive waterfall found in western Thindol on the Chultan Peninsula of southwest Faerûn.

Geography

The waterfall was part of the River Salban just east of the Sanrach Mountains.

Description

Salban Falls originated atop a cliff face overlooking the grasslands below.
