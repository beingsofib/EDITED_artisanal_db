The Defiant Gesture was an inn located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

The inn was located in south Zhentil Keep in the western section of the Foreign Quarter just off of Twilight Street.

Description

The Defiant Gesture stood three storys high and was well-maintained. A signboard depicting a human hand with thumb and pinky touching adorned the entrance. This was considered to be a rude gesture in Zhentil Keep.

Activities

The inn rented 24 rooms at a rate of 10 gp per night. Stables found behind the inn could be used for 5 gp per night. Baths were 1 gp, meals 4 gp, and a mug of pathetic ale was 4 sp. The steep prices reflected the difficulty in importing quality food into the city following its destruction in the .

Inhabitants

A retired Zhentilar warrior named Trevis Uhl ran the inn in the . A trio of young serving girls worked at the inn as well.
