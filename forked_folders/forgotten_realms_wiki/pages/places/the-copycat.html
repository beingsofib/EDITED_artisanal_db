The Copycat

The Copycat is a one-round adventure set in the Living City organized play program. It was released in April of 1998 and is intended for adventurers of all levels.

Summary

Takes place after the war and after A Walk on the Wildside and Elementally Speaking: Air's Looking at You adventures.
