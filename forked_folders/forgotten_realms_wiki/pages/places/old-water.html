Old Water

Old Water, sometimes called Cold Water, was a large lake found in eastern Impiltur.

Geography

The lake sat downstream beyond Bluefang Water along Great Imphras River. This waterway was collectively referred to as Traders Bay, and Old Water was the last step on its way to the Easting Reach.
