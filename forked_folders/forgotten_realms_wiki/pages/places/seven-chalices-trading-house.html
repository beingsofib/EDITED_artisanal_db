Seven Chalices Trading House was a guildhall in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR and the headquarters of the Kindly Order of Silversmiths.

A quality four-story building, it was located on the edge of Farix Drudach, Otahl Sabban, in the city's Emerald Ward.
