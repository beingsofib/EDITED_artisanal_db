The Haldine farm was a holding belonging to the Haldine family of Stag Falls during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The main house on the property was stone, and had a device of a lion atop the front door.

History

When a party sent by Marcus Rubicon came to Stag Falls, they found the Haldine farm to be emptied of people; unlike a different farm, farm implements were not shorn of their metal parts. Unknown to these adventurers, the property's owner, Ren Haldine, had fled the place with his family recently, and the Lord's guards ransacked the property looking for them.

Inhabitants

  • Ren Haldine
  • Fatrina Haldine
