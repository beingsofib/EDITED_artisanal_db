Ransaran's Sergeant-at-Arms was a weapon's shop located in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The shop was located along Duke's Way in central Daggerford abutting the landrise.

Activities

The shop's owner, Ransaran, was a highly skilled weaponsmith who forged quality pieces. His prices were steep but most customers thought them fair considering the weapon quality.
