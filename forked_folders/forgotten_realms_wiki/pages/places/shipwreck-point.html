Shipwreck Point was a section of the River Tesh that was home to a collection of ruined ships following the destruction of Zhentil Keep in the .

Location

Shipwreck Point lay on the south coast of the River Tesh just east of the Foreign Quarter.

History

During Zhentil Keep's razing, the majority of the city's navy was assaulted by white dragons, resulting in the vessels being encased in giant blocks of ice. The miniature icebergs eventually came to rest naturally in the spot that would be later known as Shipwreck Point. In the years following Zhentil Keep's destruction, the clergy of Umberlee declared Shipwreck Point to be consecrated by their goddess, proclaiming that any who dared defile the place would risk the Bitch Queen's curse.

Rumors & Legends

The dead sailors caught within the frozen ships were believed to haunt the region as zombies, skeletons, and ghouls.
