Felder's Pawnshop

Building
nameFelder's Pawnshop
typePawnshop
ownerCallie Felder (1479 DR)

Felder's Pawnshop was a pawnshop located in Waterdeep. Its shelves and displays were well maintained, and packed to the brim with merchandise.
