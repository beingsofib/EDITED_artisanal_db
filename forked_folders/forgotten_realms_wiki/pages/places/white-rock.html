White Rock was a small mining town located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

White Rock was found at the northern end of the eastern branch of the High King's Road on the northern side of Whitefish Bay. The town was located in central Fairheight county on the southern slopes of the Fairheight Range.

Defenses

The town maintained a militia of hundreds of fighters to protect itself from the constant threat of raids by the goblins and orcs of the Fairheight Range.

Trade

As with other settlements in and around the dangerous mountains, the town was founded for the purpose of mining for metals.
