The Meirig Peatlands was a large swamp located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles. It was a fetid swamp of sloshing and acidic mud.

Geography

The swamp was found in western Gwynneth along the coast of the Trackless Sea and Corwell Firth, where it was fed by several rivers flowing down from the Highlands to the east. Much of the swamp's north and eastern border abutted the Shrouded Lands, where the ground became increasingly drier and more fertile as one climbed up into those grasslands.

Flora & Fauna

The Peatlands were infested with vine horrors, In addition, the occasional roaming bog hag, feymire crocodile, or troll made it an especially dangerous place.
