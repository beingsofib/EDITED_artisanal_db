Shan Skar

Shan Skar was a prominent peak of Katakoro Shan found in Khazari.

Geography

The peak sat high in the northwestern corner of the country near Manass.

Description

Given the narrow breadth of Katakoro Shan in that region, Shan Skar's altitude of gave it an impressive view of Khazari as well as the Quoya Desert.
