Oak and Spear

Building
nameOak and Spear
typeTavern
locationHighmoon, Deepingdale
servicesAle

The Oak and Spear was an alehouse in Highmoon, Deepingdale in late 14 century DR. It was predominantly patroned by travelers and minstrels, and was famed for its home-brewed ale.

A draft of ale cost 8 cp or a recital of a good song.
