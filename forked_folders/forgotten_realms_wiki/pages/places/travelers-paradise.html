Traveler's Paradise was a travel agency located in the town of Mossbridges in the late 14 century DR.

Servies

Traveler's Paradise was a sales office that organized vacations and travels to the nearby vacation chalet known as the Resort, a magical place that had a reserved climate from the rest of the Vast – bringing the cold of winter in the summer and vise vera. A week's vacation package was sold at 500 gold pieces per person.

History

Traveler's Paradise was opened in the late 14 century DR.
