Caer Westphal, also known as Westphal, was the largest city on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles, as well as the seat of power for the Kingdom of Snowdown and subsequently the seat of Amn's military occupation of Snowdown after its seizure in the early 15 century DR.

Geography

Caer Westphal was located in a small cove that served as a natural harbor on the southern coast of Snowdown along the Sea of Swords. It lay on Snowdown Ride.

Description

The style of buildings in Westphal varied overtime due to Snowdown's military occupation by Amn. Prior to the occupation, buildings were entirely of the Ffolk style, being round towers and low houses. In the eighty years following Amnian occupation, structures were designed and erected in the Amnian style, being square and functional. The castle, also known as Caer Westphal, overlooked the city.

Trade

Under Amnian occupation, Westphal and the rest of Snowdown only did business with that nation. Because of this, its harbor was typically full of ships flying the colors of Amn. Each day, a small squadron of these ships left Westphal for Athkatla, loaded with goods and resources.

Defenses

Westphal was more heavily defended than the Moonshae settlements of Caer Callidyrr or Moray. Amnian troops and a number of foreign mercenaries served as its city guard. On average, the Amnian forces tended to be fairer on the native Ffolk than the mercenaries, with many of the latter making it a point to ensure locals crossed them so as to give them cause for making mischief or soliciting a bribe. Though not all the mercenaries were like this.

Whenever outsiders made trouble with the city guard, locals would lock up and bar their homes and businesses. And if such a troublemaker tried to force their way in, the occupant would fight them with whatever they had on hand.

Religion

Prior to Amnian occupation, the Church of Chauntea was the major faith of Caer Westphal. The Chauntean faith was gradually overtaken by others over the course of Amn's occupation and their temple fell into a dilapidated state. Of the faiths that had overtaken it, the biggest temples in the city belonged to the Church of Bane, Church of Gruumsh, Church of Tempus, and Church of Waukeen. All of these temples were noted for being well-kept and clean.

Notable Locations

Westphal was host to a number of seedy taverns and festhalls, catering to the needs of visiting Amnian sailors.

  • Caer Westphal, a fortified castle inhabited by the city's rulers.
  • Singing Fisherman, a rundown Ffolk style inn.

Inhabitants

Westphal was more densly populated than other settlements in the Moonshaes, being more akin to cities like Baldur's Gate or Waterdeep. In terms of demographics, the number of inhabitants from foreign lands outnumbered the population of native Ffolk, These natives were downtrotten under Amnian occupation, being compliant to the mercenary guard but harboring a hatred for them.

Foreign creatures that took up residence in the city as mercenaries included drow, goblins, hobgoblins, orcs and half-orcs, some ogres, even some cambions,
