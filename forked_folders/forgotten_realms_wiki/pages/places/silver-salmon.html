The Silver Salmon was an inn located in the coastal town of Kingsbay on the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles during the mid–14 century DR. The inn was named for the massive run of salmon that traversed the Strait of Alaron each year.

Inhabitants

As of the early-to-mid 14 century DR, the barmaid was a lovely Ffolk lass named Winifred.

History

Elminster stayed at the Silver Salmon when he visited Gwynneth in the .

Tristan Kendrick and his entourage stayed in the Silver Salmon while passing through Kingsbay on their way to Caer Callidyrr on Eleasis 18 of the . It was there that the Bhaalist cleric Hobarth overheard his plans to travel across the Strait of Alaron, and used this information to sabotage the boat he was to take, The Lucky Duckling.
