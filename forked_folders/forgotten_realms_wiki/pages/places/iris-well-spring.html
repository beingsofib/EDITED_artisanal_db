Iris Well Spring

Iris Well Spring was a small oasis and village located just east of Semphar in the Hordelands.

Description

This area was a dreary place for much of the year, but each spring the dormant dwarf irises bloomed, surrounding the area with beautiful blue flowers.

Inhabitants

Atabeg Ali al-Mustasib and his daughter Dura lived on a small farm here.
