Thunder Trail

The Thunder Trail was a short overland route in eastern Cormyr that began at the East Way just east of Masoner's Bridge and before it entered Hullack Forest. The trail headed north between the Immerflow and the forest's edge and climbed into the Thunder Peaks north of the forest.
