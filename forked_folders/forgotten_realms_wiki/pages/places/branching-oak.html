The Branching Oak was a small inn in Essembra in the mid–14 century DR.

History

In the , the Branching Oak offered lodging to travelers who passed through the town. The Heroes of the Bonds passed through the town and the inn when traveling to the town of Hap, whilst on a quest to destroy the New Alliance of the Bonds.
