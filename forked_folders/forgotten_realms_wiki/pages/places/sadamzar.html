Sadamzar was a town located in the country of Unther in the Old Empires during the mid–14 century DR.

Geography

Sadamzar sat along the eastern shore of the northeast section of the Methmere in northern Unther. An unnamed trail connected the town to Shussel to the southeast along the coast of the Alamber Sea.
