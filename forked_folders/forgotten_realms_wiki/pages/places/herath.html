Herath was a city located in central Dambrath.

Geography

Located along the Dolphin's Climb river just south of the Gnollwatch Mountains, Herath was a bustling city.

History

Easily the richest city in all of Dambrath, Herath held the nation's state mints for electrum and silver coins.

Defense

The city sported an army composed of 500 cavalry and 2,500 infantry. They spent much of their time guarding the precious mines that were the lifeblood of the city. In addition to these troops, Duchess Selanith L'baros had a personal guard of 1,000 gnolls and 40 hill giants.

Inhabitants

Nearly all of the dwarves in Dambrath lived in Herath.
