Latif Island was a small island within the Crowded Sea of interior Zakhara.

Geography

Latif Island was located southwest of the Cities of the Pantheon region of Zakhara. It formed a portion of the southern tail of Al-Faraq Islands. The island was small, measuring only long and wide. Much of the northeast section of the island was covered in rock, while the southwest section supported scattered grasslands. Coral reefs were found offshore of the north and east parts of Latif Island. The island was uninhabited as of 1367 DR.
