Canary Lane

Canary Lane was a short and unwelcoming street just one block away from The Eastern Wet in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate.

Description

Canary Lane ran one block, from south to north, one block southwest from the Sailor's Crossing plaza decorated with a small fountain,

Canary Lane was a shaded and covered lane, crossed by dark alleyways that did manholes and grate passages into the city's sewers.

History

In the summer of the , on Kythorn 19, the Sailor's Crossing plaza became a scene of a grizzly murder. An adventuring detective named Tav conducted the investigation when a cloaked figure of a thief darted past, carrying a coin purse stolen from the murder victim. On Kythorn 22, Tav ventured to Canary Lane in an attempt to track down the thief, who was considered a prime suspect. The investigator chased down a young urchin nicknamed Magpie who ended up slipping past drain grates into the sewers, marked with the symbol of The Guild.
