Trader's Way (Waterdeep)

Trader's Way was a prominent road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Trader's Way originated on Julthoon Street at the border of the Dock Ward and Sea Ward. It branched southeast then east, passed by the north side of the Market, then merged with the High Road on the far east side of the Market at a large intersection that also included Bazaar Street and Suldown Street. Other roads connectin with Trader's Way included Gothal Street, Melshar's Street, Tchozal's Race, the Sutherlane, Mendever Street, Sul Street, Shield Street, and Copper Street.
