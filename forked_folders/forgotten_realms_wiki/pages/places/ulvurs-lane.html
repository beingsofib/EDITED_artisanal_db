Ulvur's Lane

Ulvur's Lane was a small street found in the Bloomridge district of Baldur's Gate that ran along the Old Wall's southern side.

Description

Ulvur's Lane began at the Gond Gate in Bloomridge, which connected the Lower and Upper Cities, and ran west, along the Old Wall for two blocks and ending at the House of the Lute. The House of the Lute was a kaeth house inside the building that stood against the Old Wall, cutting the shaded lane short.
