The Cave was a small asteroid in the Tears of Selûne. It was the lair and base of the Batship.

Geography

The Cave was located near the center of the cluster. The entire surface of the asteroid was covered in a grass-like vegetation. In the underside of the rock there was a large cavern that served as a docking port for the Batship, as well as a base where plunder collected by the ship's crew was stored before being sold.

Defenses

The Batship would promptly jump to protect its lair and prevent it from being located.
