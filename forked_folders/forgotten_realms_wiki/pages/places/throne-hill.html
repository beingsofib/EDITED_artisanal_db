Throne Hill

Throne Hill was one of the four hills in Chult atop which Port Nyanzaru was built. It was located on the southern side of the Merchants' Ward.

Description

Throne Hill was steep, and its top was dominated by the palace of Goldenthrone, as well as a handful of villas. A wide stone bridge atop arched columns connected the top of Throne Hill to the top of neighboring Temple Hill to the north, while a second bridge spanned above the Grand Souk to connect the hilltop to the southern slopes overlooking the city, where several merchant princes' villas were located.

The city's Jewel Market was located around the northern base of Throne Hill.
