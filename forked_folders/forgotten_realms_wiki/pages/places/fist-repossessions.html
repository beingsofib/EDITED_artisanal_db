Fist Repossessions was a collection agency from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 century DR.

History

In the , during the Absolute crisis, Pammella Nortale of Nortale's Hostel fell behind on paying for deliveries from Durinbold & Oathoon. Martnev Oathoon, Valued Customer Disciplinarian, mailed Pammella a notice that unless she made a minimum payment for the previous month's shipment, she would be turned over to Fist Repossessions, despite the hostel being a non-profit.
