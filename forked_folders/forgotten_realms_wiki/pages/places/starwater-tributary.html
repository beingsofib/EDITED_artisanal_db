Starwater tributary

The Starwater tributary was a small river in Cormyr. It flowed from the Storm Horns range, eventually meeting with the Starwater River.

Geography

The river originated in the peaks of the Storm Horns northwest of Tyrluk, flowing south from glacial melts high up in the clouds near Tumblerock Pass, Rungnir's glacier, and the lair of the white dragon Nexxylplyx. It then flowed south and east past Tyrluk, the High Road and Beckside Castle before joining with the Starwater River. It had many waterfalls, cataracts and rapids along its course as it descended, and the lower reaches were dotted with farms and homesteads.
