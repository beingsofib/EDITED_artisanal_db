The Plume was a district within the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire.

Description

This district of the City of Brass was jammed full of kaeth houses, shops, and stalls. It had many magnificent buildings that overlooked open courts that were architecturally impressive.

Inhabitants

Many dao and merchants stayed within this ward while traveling to the City of Brass on business. Though only efreet were allowed to own property here.

Notable Locations

  • The Street of the Last Houses
  • Krak al-Nayyiran, also known as the Castle of the Sun and Moon, was where the finest legions of the city's sultan lived and trained.
