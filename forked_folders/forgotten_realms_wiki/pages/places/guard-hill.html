Guard Hill

Guard Hill was a barracks and defensive position located in the capital city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Guard Hill stood in the northwest section of Skuld in the Port District near the northern outer wall. It sat near the shore of the Alamber Sea and the merchant yards.

Description

The walled structure included a trio of guard towers.
