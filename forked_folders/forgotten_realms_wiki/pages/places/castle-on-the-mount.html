Castle on the Mount

Building
nameThe Castle on the Mount
typeCastle
locationInter Mierionydd, the Whamite Isles

The Castle on the Mount was a castle in Inter Mierionydd in the Whamite Isles. It was a key defense of Inter Mierionydd in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles The Monks of the Mount appeared to have some relationship to the castle.
