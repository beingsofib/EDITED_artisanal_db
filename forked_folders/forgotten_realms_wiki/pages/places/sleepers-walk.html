Sleepers' Walk

Sleepers' Walk was a street of corduroy-logs in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Geography

This street ran southwest off of Wall Way, running roughly parallel to Slipstone Street, which it later terminated on. Along the way it acted as the northwestern terminus of Nethpranter's Street.

Notable Locations

Patient Fingers Finework: A woodcraft shop owned by Dannath Lisosar.

Thond Glass and Glazing: A glass shop owned by Jhalassan Thond.
