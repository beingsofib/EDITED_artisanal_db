Harrowdale Harbor was found in the town of Harrowdale on the Dragon Reach.

Description

The harbor had two named piers, the Heart of the Harbor and Harrow Plow Pier, and another unnamed pier on its north side at the end of Lancegallop Lane. Several trading warehouses and offices were located on the harbor including the Shaleel Warehouses, Treskeden House, and Harrowcoaster House.
