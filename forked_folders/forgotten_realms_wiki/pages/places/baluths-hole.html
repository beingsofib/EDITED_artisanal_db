Baluth's Hole was an urban path in Scornubel during the mid-14 century DR.

Geography

Baluth's Hole was one of the paths leading to Belgon Bradaskor's home in Scornubel.

History

In , Sharantyr forced Besmer Altuth to take him to Belgon Bradaskor's home, after the latter failed to mug her. He offered her a choice of paths, between Rat Stair and Baluth's Hole. She chose the latter.
