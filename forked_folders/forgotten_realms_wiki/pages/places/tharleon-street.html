Tharleon Street

Tharleon Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Tharleon Street ran east to west within the northern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward. The road originated in the east at the intersection of Bazaar Street and the Street of Silver where it branched west to terminate near Blackstaff Tower on Swords Street after several blocks. Other streets connecting with Tharleon Street included the Street of Silks, Siren Lane, and Aka Way.
