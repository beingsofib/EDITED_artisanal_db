Cede Pool

Cede Pool was a body of water in the Shinantra Mountains that was a holy site of Jannath during the time of ancient Netheril.

Description

The pool was the source of the Cede Run, an unusually pure and clear river. Jannathans believed that the pool was a blessed site and that it provided prophetic dreams and visions to those who visited, and a great many pilgrims arrived every summer solstice after passing through the friendly Amaunator-aligned town of Trinity. Skeptics maintained that the visions were mere delusions brought on by a waterborne taint, even though the water source was renowned for its purity.
