Coastwatch

The Coastwatch was a small road found in Sembia that connected Yhaunn in the north with the smaller towns of Surd and Tulbegh in the south along the Sea of Fallen Stars.

Description

The Coastwatch ran roughly north to south, bending westward to pass through Surd near its halfway point.
