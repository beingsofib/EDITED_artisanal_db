Jajam was a small village in the Nakvaligach region within the Great Glacier.

Geography

Jajam was located in western Nakvaligach near the Tuutsaas Chain.

Layout

The villages minikitak were constructed with slabs of turquoise.

Inhabitants

The inhabitants of Jajam were under a kaiurit which commanded them to kill anyone who was caught tampering with the villages minikitak.
