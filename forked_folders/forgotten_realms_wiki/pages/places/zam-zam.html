Zam-Zam was a coffee house located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR.

Location

The establishment sat in the northwest section of the City of Brass in the south central part of Ashlarks near the border of the Rookery. Zam-Zam lay due west of Krak al-Tawil.

Activities

Zam-Zam was a favored spot for nobles wishing to slum it up.
