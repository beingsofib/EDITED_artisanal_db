Razack's Tears

Razack's Tears was a long stream located in the western region of the Ride on the Moonsea North.

Geography

The stream flowed north from the Dragonspines, wound across the Ride before turning northeast and joining with Telstang Brook.

Geographical Features

The Verdigris Trail intersected the stream at a bridge crossing just north of Verdigris.

Rumors & Legends

According to legend, Razack was a titan lord that used his great axe to cut a scar into the face of Toril itself.
