Torch Lane

Torch Lane was a small road in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep near the House of Wonder.

Geography

The road ran north to south from Ivory Street to Pharra's Alley.

Notable Locations

  • This road spanned the western length of the Eltorchul Villa.
