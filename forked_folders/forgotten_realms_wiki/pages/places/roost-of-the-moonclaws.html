The Roost of the Moonclaws was the base of the Moonclaws gang of Hastarl in the early 3 century DR.

Location

The Roost of the Moonclaws was in the zone of the city with upscale houses, but which were not yet so wealthy as to have their own baths.

Structure

The place had a main hall with a number of tubs.

Services

Customers could get baths at tubs, with scented soaps.

Activities

Junior members of the Moonclaws gang scrubbed bathtubs there, in addition to their usual gang activities.

History

In , shortly after the elimination of the Velvet Hands rival thieves' gang, the Moonclaws were attacked by the last survivor of the Velvet Hands, Elminster Aumar, who slew a great number of theirs in an ambush at a warehouse. They set the warehouse on fire with him inside, and he responded by escaping and torching the home of Isparla, leader of the Moonclaws. She retreated to the Roost, where he followed her forces.

At the Roost, he ambushed the thieves, killing one. Before he managed another kill, the Magister of Mystra, Dorgon Heamiiolothtar, intervened and froze time for most of them, biding him flee and attack no other foe if he'd see Farl, the leader of the Velvet Hands, and Tassabra, another member, alive. Elminster took his offer and left while they were frozen.
