East Market Street

East Market Street was a small branched street found in the city of Westgate.

Location

East Market Street was found in the south section of the city. It connected with Silverpiece Way and Westgate Market Street then branched once before ending at the city's perimeter wall. A city watch barracks was located on East Market Street.
