Galath's Roost was a fortress located in Mistledale during the 14 century DR.

Location

Galath's Roost was located in the eastern region of Mistledale.

History

The fortress was occupied by its namesake–a bandit captain named Galath during the 10 century DR who used the keep as a base to launch raids throughout Mistledale and even Sembia. A large band of Sembian soldiers eventually retaliated and killed everyone within the keep.

Rumors & Legends

Galath's ghost was rumored to haunt his fortress long after his death.
