Fallenstar Fine Gems

Fallenstar Fine Gems was a jewelry shop in Athkatla's rich Gem District in the 14 century DR.

Location

Fallenstar Fine Gems stood across from the Blevynsoar House in the easterly Gem District, clustered in the chaos of buildings north of the Ladycloak Lane and west of Sarbront's Walk.

History

By the late 14 century DR, Fallenstar, along with a dozen other expensive jewelry stores "made Gem District glitter."
