Anteos Lane

Anteos Lane was a street of hard-packed dirt and gravel in the North Ward of Waterdeep.

Geography

This street ran from east to west, coming southwest off of Trollkill Street and curving eastward. It eventually turned northeast and terminated into Brassfeather Lane.

Notable Locations

  • Anteos Villa, the family estate of the noble house Anteos, stood on the corner of Anteos Lane and Trollkill Street.
  • Brossfeather Villa, the family estate of the noble house Brossfeather, stood on the corner of Anteos Lane and Brassfeather Lane.
  • Phull Villa, the family estate of the noble house Phull, stood directly between Anteos Lane and Phull Lane.
