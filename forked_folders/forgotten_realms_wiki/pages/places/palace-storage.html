The Palace Storage building was a warehouse located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The building stood two stories tall.

Location

The Palace Storage warehouse was located in the western section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward just north of Piergeiron's Palace and south of Fetlock Court.
