Duke's Way

Duke's Way was a road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

Duke's Way began at the Caravan Gate entering Daggerford from the west. It continued east through the center of the city before terminating at the Ducal Castle which covered most of eastern Daggerford.

Notable Locations

The Hill Road intersected Duke's Way at the base of the landrise the separated the ducal estate from the rest of Daggerford, while the High Road branched south from the Hill Road near the drill field. Notable buildings located along Duke's Way included:

  • The Ducal Castle
  • Daggerford Inn
  • The Grand Playhouse
  • Kryptgarden Scrolls
  • Morninglow Tower
  • Oryv's Cloth Emporium
