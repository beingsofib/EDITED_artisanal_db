Fireleap Way

Fireleap Way was a long and straight street in Ravens Bluff that crossed the city almost completely, from east to west, beginning in the Foreign District and ending in the Tentowers neighborhood.

Geography

Fireleap Way's western end was located in the Foreign District, by the Coker Wharf Company's docks. From there the street ran east, turning into Daren's Ring circle with the Ring Park in the middle, turning back into a straight line just before the gate out of the Foreign District. Next, Fireleap Way passed the moot with Westwall Way North and Fire Lane, further east it crossed Raphiel Road, Blacktree Boulevard North, DeVillars Ride, Manycoins Way, the moot with Macintyre Path and Evensong Ride, followed by crossings with O'Kane Court, Waelstar Way, and Lamorgyr's Ride. Then, Fireleap Way crossed DeVillars Creek via the Old Dragonbridge, and continued on east past Torngel's Ride, Stonekeep Concourse, and finally ended on Bouldergrinder's Way.

Notable Locations

  • Embassy of Tantras, just where Fireleap Way began and turned into Daren's Ring.
  • Embassy of Thay, on the southern side of the street, across from the Embassy of Tantras.
  • Home of Hark the Herald, on Daren's Ring's southeast.
  • Hangman's Courthouse, a large plaza, south of where Westwall Way North and Fire Lane crossed Fireleap Way.
  • Littleate & Sons, a moneylender located on the crossing of Fireleap Way and Blacktree Boulevard North.
  • Ondrel Spires, a large luxury apartments complex located just past Littleate & Sons.
  • The Stout Staff, a vendor of canes, crutches, and peglegs located on the crossing with Manycoins Way.
  • Tower of Holy Revelry, a temple dedicated to Lliira, located across Fireleap Way from The Stout Staff.
  • Home of Regora Ravensworth, on the moot with Macintyre Path and Evensong Ride.
  • Ravengard House, the manse home of the Therogeon family, facing Lamorgyr's Ride.
  • Stane Opera House, across the street from the Ravengard House, facing Lamorgyr's Ride.
  • House of the Rose, a merchant house located on the crossing with Torngel's Ride.
  • Gurneth's Goblet eatery on Bouldergrinder's Way.
