The Tower of Luck was a temple of Tymora in Waterdeep.

Location

The temple stood in the Sea Ward of the city, on the east side of the Street of the Singing Dolphin between Ivory Street and Sighing Maiden's Walk.

Inhabitants

Circa 1372 DR it was led by Honorable Mistress Seenroas Halvinhar.
