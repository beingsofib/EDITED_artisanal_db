The Mists Gate was one of three terrestrial entry points into the city of Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan.

Location

The gate was located along the eastern side of Suldolphor's exterior wall south of the Golden Gate, facing the harbor and Lake of Steam.

Activities

The Mists Gate remained open from dawn until dusk.
