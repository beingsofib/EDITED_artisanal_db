Khampo was a city in Tabot.

Geography

It stood amongst the forest in southern Tabot, north of the Wu Pi Te Shao Mountains, on the road between Thok and Pabex and at the end of the road from Bidnop So. It was roughly a day's ride from Thok.

History

In , Baron Phen Do of Khampo rebelled against the government of Tabot. In response, the worried Mag-chi Le-Khung (the military office) stationed three divisions in Khampo to contain the revolt. In , a tortoise-shell divination at U'Chan Gompa suggested burning rocks would fall on Khampo during Qwa'chein Goh celebrations; they elected not to warn the city, as punishment for its rebellion. Despite this, that same year, in response to a Phutanese army assembling at the border, Tabot went on a war footing, with the Mag-chi Le-Khung mustering soldiers in the area. They ordered conscription in Bidnop So, Darka, Thok, and Khampo. In short, it was not a good year for the people of Khampo.

Inhabitants

  • Baron Phen Do, a rebellious aristocrat of Khampo
