Wolfenbutte was a tiny nation of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. It participated in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Description

Wolfenbutte was led by Rolf the Rough and Richard the Wrinkled. It was home to the avaricious group known only as The Guild. Even the wolves and farmers of Wolfenbutte participated in the wars of the Whamite Isles.

Wolfenbutte shared land borders with the Agenais and Swil. The border with Swil was marked by a mountain range. Part of Wolfenbutte's coast lay on the Lennahc Hsilgne, which separated it from Klocckbarn and Zagrus. Wolfenbutte was well-defended by famous structures including the Wearwall and Castle Klockk, the latter of which curiously shared etymology with the name of neighboring Klockkbarn.

Among the nations of the Whamites, Wolfenbutte was significant for the esteem to which its society held wolves.
