Portith was a medium-sized town found in the Barony of Ostel in Damara.

Portith's population in 1357 DR was 1,200.

Activities

Situated on the western bank of Lake Mogador between the larger cities of Praka and Heliogabalus, Portith was a popular stopping point for travelers weary of the long road. A reputation for being the home of many fine inns and skilled craftsmen also helped attract travelers.

In addition to the income provided from travelers, Portith operated many successful farming and fishing ventures, sustaining them with comfort during even the most trying of times.
