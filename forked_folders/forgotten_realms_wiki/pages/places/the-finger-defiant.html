The Finger Defiant

The Finger Defiant was a mesa in southern Mulhorand, excavated by the dwarf geomancer Thormud Horn over long years. He used to call it simply "the mesa", until he took Kiril Duskmourn as his bodyguard; she insisted on the name until he took it up too. The mesa contained storerooms, libraries, halls, galleries, and even balconies. There was an exposed spiral staircase outside the mesa.

Inhabitants

  • Thormud Horn
  • Xet
  • Kiril Duskmourn
