Sarango's was a jewelry shop in Athkatla's rich Gem District in the 14 century DR.

Location

Sarango's shop was located on the northern side of Sarbront's Walk, in a chaotic and clustered easterly Gem District. Across the street from Sarango's stood merchant Darastiir Thalongal's abode.

History

By the late 14 century DR, Sarango's, along with a dozen other expensive jewelry stores "made Gem District glitter."
