Roonsundyr's Warren was a private residence in the Central Port of Skullport in the late 14 century DR.

Structure

The entrance to the warren consisted of an ornately sculpted balcony situated within the cavern, fronted by a permanent prismatic wall that barred the archway beyond.

Defenses

The chambers beyond the entrance were reputed to be heavily fortified with an array of mechanical and magical traps of exceptional complexity, said to rival the skills of even a grandmaster thief. The internal layout and exact nature of the residence remained undocumented, as no known individual had successfully entered the warren and returned.

Inhabitants

Roonsundyr's Warren was the private residence of one of Skullport's most reclusive figures, the eccentric archmage Othur Roonsundyr. Roonsundyr did not receive visitors under any circumstances. He typically emerged from his sanctum no more than once or twice per month. On such occasions, he frequented the Trade Lanes, conducting transactions with gemstones as currency. Upon completion of his business, he returned to his residence, transporting his purchases through the air by magical means.
