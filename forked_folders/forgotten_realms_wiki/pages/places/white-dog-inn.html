The White Dog Inn was an inn in the T'u Lung city of Balanzia around .

Location

This inn was located in the southern portion of Balanzia, down the road from the homes and warehouse that were owned by a man named Xu.

History

Around , the White Dog was one of many buildings that were left intact in Balanzia as the city fell at the hands of the Shou Lung invasion of T'u Lung. Centuries later, around , the building that once housed the White Dog Inn still stood within the abandoned cityand was recorded as such on a map made of the city in that year.
