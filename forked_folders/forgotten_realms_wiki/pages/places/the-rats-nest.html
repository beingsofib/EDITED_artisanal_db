The Rat's Nest was a tavern in Hillsfar and popular hangout for off-duty Red Plumes.

Activities

The combination of cheap ale and brawny occupants often led to military brawls breaking out on a regular basis in the Rat's Nest. Visitors of the tavern who weren't associated with the Red Plumes needed to be wary as criminals in Hillsfar often ended up in the Arena.
