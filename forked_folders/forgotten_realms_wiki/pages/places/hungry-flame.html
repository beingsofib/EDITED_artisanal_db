The Hungry Flame was a run down tavern found in the city of Helm's Hold circa the late 15 century DR.

Services

The staff of the Hungry Flame were particularly discourteous to any patrons who did not bear the spellscars that were common among so many the Hold's residents.

History

As of the 1470s DR, the tavern was the meeting place and headquarters for the Heirs of Azure, the group of spellscarred political activists that fought out against the "pureblood" citizens of Helm's Hold.
