The Watchful Eye was the only inn in Espar, Cormyr.

Structure

The building's name came from the sign outside which showed the symbol for the god Helm. Inside, the establishment was simple and bland with no decorations or elaborate furniture.

Atmosphere

The inn was calm and efficient, and was not dissimilar in its atmosphere to an actual temple of Helm.

Inhabitants

Mostly farmers stayed at the Watchful Eye of Espar.

Services

Although not a restaurant, the inn served mediocre food in generous portions. It took pride in its superior stable service.
