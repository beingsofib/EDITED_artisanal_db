The Brave Bustard was a tavern in Waterdeep.

Environment

The jacks for wine were made out of clay.

History

In , Velzraedo Hlaklavarr got laid flat by Mirt the Moneylender at the Brave Bustard after trying to bully him out of the best seat. His bodyguards later carried him out.
