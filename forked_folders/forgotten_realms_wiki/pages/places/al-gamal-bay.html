Al-Gamal Bay

Al-Gamal Bay was a small naturally occurring bay found in north Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

Al-Gamal Bay was located approximately southwest of mighty Qudra along the coast of the Great Sea.

History

The bay was the site for an unnamed battle in Zakhara's history.
