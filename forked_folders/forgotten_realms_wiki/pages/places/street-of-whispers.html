Street of Whispers

The Street of Whispers was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

The Street of Whispers was located in the western section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. It ran roughly north to south, originating on the Street of Glances where it branched south and eventually ended with Seawatch Street on Dob's Loss. The Street of Whispers was also intersected by Diamond Street, Grimwald's Way, Shark Street, Gondwatch Lane, Ivory Street, Moonstar Alley, and Sharra's Flight.
