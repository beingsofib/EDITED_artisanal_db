River Gundar

The River Gundar (alternatively the River Gundan) was a waterway found in Estagund in the Shining Lands.

Geography

The River Gundar originated in the Thrulaliel Mountains in northwest Estagund. It flowed southeast for approximately , practically bisecting the country in a diagonal line, before reaching the vast expanse of the Great Sea southeast of the Gundar Forest.
