The Gate of Ashes was one of the entrances into the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR.

Location

The Gate of Ashes was found in the north wall of the city, leading into the Avencina district east of the northern arm of the Great Canal.

Description

The gate resembled a permanent fortress complete with a garrison of soldiers.
