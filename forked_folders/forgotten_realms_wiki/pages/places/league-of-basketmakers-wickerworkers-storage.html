League of Basketmakers & Wickerworkers Storage also known simply as the Warehouse of the League, was a warehouse in Waterdeep that was owned by the Waterdhavian guild of the same name.

Location

This warehouse was located in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This warehouse was two-stories tall.
