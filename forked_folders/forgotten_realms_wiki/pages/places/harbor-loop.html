Harbor Loop

The Harbor Loop was a small rounded court found in the city of Westgate.

Location

The Harbor Loop was found in the eastern section of the city. It began and ended along Silverpiece Way, looping toward Westgate's harbor. The small street known as the Wizard's Cut connected to the middle of the Harbor Loop. Several prominent families, including the Cormaeril's and Ssemm's, owned docks touching the street.

Notable Locations

  • Okuzo's Imports, established in the 14 century DR, shop that traded in exotic goods from the realms to the east of Faerûn.
  • The Rosebud, an outdoor ale garden favored by merchants and young nobles.
  • Statue of Kisonraathiisar, the dragon king of Westgate.
  • Statue of Verovan Lorndessar, the last king of Westgate. The statue was decapitated before 1374 DR.
  • Thayan enclave of Westgate stood on the southwestern end of the street.
