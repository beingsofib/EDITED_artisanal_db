The Tarnished Trumpet was a tavern that faced the Mayor's Tower in Hill's Edge in the Western Heartlands in the 1360s DR. It was alleged to be the best drinking location in Hill's Edge.

It was a noisy, raucous tavern with tapestries and battered weapons on the walls. Each table in the tavern had old treasure maps magically embedded in glass planes in the tables. Copies were available for sale.
