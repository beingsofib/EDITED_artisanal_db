The Golden Cutlass was one of the finest inns in Tajar in the Pearl Cities of Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Activities

Guests of the Golden Cutlass enjoyed a night's rest on smooth silken sheets and pillows stuffed to the brim with duck down.
