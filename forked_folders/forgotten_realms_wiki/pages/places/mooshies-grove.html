Mooshie's Grove was the home of Montolio Debrouchee ("Mooshie") and was a diamond-shaped grove of trees and piled stone walls in the Rauvin Mountains near the Coldwood.

Structure

The grove featured elevated walkways and beautifully designed huts. It was surrounded by tall pines and emitted a magical aura.

History

Drizzt

In 1340 DR, Mooshie's grove was the site of Drizzt Do'Urden's ranger training. At one point, the grove was visited by Roddy McGristle, who was hunting Drizzt, but Montolio sent him on a false trail to Morueme's Cave. When McGristle later discerned Montolio's deceit, he allied himself with the orc king, Graul, and led an assault on the grove. Drizzt and Montolio defended the grove against the attackers, secretly aided by Kellindil and his elven cousins. Some time after the assault, Montolio passed away and Drizzt buried him within the grove's soil. He would later spent the winter in the grove before fulfilling his promise to his mentor to go and find his place in the world, taking some of Montolio's books with him.

Drizzt would later return to Mooshie's grove in 1357 DR, on his way back to the Underdark entrance he emerged from, accompanied by the elf Tarathiel, where he discovered a unicorn in the grove. The creature allowed the drow to stroke his neck before vanishing

Pikel

In 1370 DR, as a test, Tarathiel took Pikel Bouldershoulder, the dwarf druid from the Spirit Soaring, to the grove, where they encountered a unicorn, symbolic of the nature goddess Mielikki. Pikel befriended the unicorn, and Tarathiel considered him to have passed the test.
