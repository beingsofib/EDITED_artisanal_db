Lumbe was the smallest and farthest moon of H'Catha, the farthest planet from the Sun in Realmspace.

Geography

The moon was cylindrical in shape, with a length of and a diameter of . Like Turnbetl, it orbited H'Catha along the planet's disc in a highly eccentric trajectory, with a perigee distance of and an apogee of . In its orbit, the moon rotated around an axis perpendicular to its length, similarly to a twirling baton.

The exterior of the moon had no atmosphere, but its surface had two doors that led inside. With the doors closed, noises could be heard coming from the inside, but they all ceased as soon as any of the doors were opened. The sounds resumed once all visitors left the moon and closed all the doors again.

Interior

The moon's interior was a seemingly uninhabited wizard's laboratory filled with thousands of extremely powerful magic items, such as bracers of invulnerability, that were protected by powerful illusions.

The laboratory's supply of breathable air was maintained by a constant flow coming out of magical jars. Other bottles scattered throughout the interior exuded a fresh, flowery scent that could be felt throughout the laboratory.

History
