Brassberg

Brassberg was one of the divine realms of the draconic deity Hlal the Jester, who was usually known as Aasterinian in the Outer Planes.

Cosmography

In the Great Wheel cosmology, Brassberg was Hlal's lair deep within Ysgard's third layer, Nidavellir (her other realm was a secret lair in Arborea).

In the World Tree cosmology, the realm covered part of the lower slopes of the great Dragon Eyrie.

Description

The lair of Hlal consisted of a number of caverns, with just the space to contain the riches collected by the dragon goddess. The surrounding area was dry and warm, as befitted the patron of brass and copper dragons.

Features

A portal linked Brassberg to Erevan Ilesere's realm on the plane of Arvandor.
