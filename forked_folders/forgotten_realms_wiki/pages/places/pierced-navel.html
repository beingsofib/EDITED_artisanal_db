The Pierced Naval was a tattoo shop in the Central Port of Skullport in the late 14 century DR.

Structure

The Pierced Naval was a one-story building in squalid condition.

Services

The Pierced Naval provided tattoos and piercings to paying customers. However, it was widely regarded as the poorest-quality business of its kind in Skullport, with an unhygienic and plague-ridden reputation. Each piercing cost 1 gp, while the rates for a tattoo were charged by the square inch, usually 1 gp per square inch (6.5 cm2). This rate doubled if the tattoo required intricate detailing.

Inhabitants

Circa 1370 DR, the owners of The Pierced Naval were the Scabaros sisters, "Stabbem" and "Doodles". Doodles was known for her tattoo work, though she had a tendency to add unsolicited embellishments to her designs. Stabbem attempted to promote an unusual form of piercing that involved the use of large nails.
