River Amarast

The River Amarast wound through the jungles of Tashalar on the Chultan Peninsula of southwest Faerûn.

Geography

Originating in the Delphin Mountains, the River Amarast flowed southeast for approximately before emptying into the Lapal Sea in the heart of the jungle. It was the largest river in the jungle feeding the Lapal Sea.
