Dalniir was an outlying settlement of Athalantar in the 3 century DR.

Geography

Dalniir was near the edge of Athalantar, in a position that would be northwest-by-north of the future Secomber.

Flora & Fauna

The forests around Dalniir provided for good hunting opportunities.

Government

The Magelord Taraj Hurlymm took over governance of the territory personally until his death.

History

In the , the prince Othglas Aumar joined the local Church of Malar, abandoning Hastarl. In the , he was killed by the Magelords of Athalantar, who transformed him into a boar and had his own underpriests hunt him down.

Later, Magelord Taraj Hurlymm took personal governance of the territory, until his death in the , when he was ambushed and slain by Prince Elminster Aumar and the sorceress Myrjala Talithyn.
