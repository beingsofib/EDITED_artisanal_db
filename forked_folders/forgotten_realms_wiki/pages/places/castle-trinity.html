Castle Trinity was the headquarters of a sect worshiping the goddess Talona. It was located in the Snowflake Mountains of Erlkazar in the mid–14 century DR.

Architecture

Located high in the Snowflake Mountains and mainly built underground, Castle Trinity was shaped to look like a rocky outcropping,

Inhabitants

The castle was ruled by a triumvirate comprising the leaders of the orders of wizards, clerics, and warriors in the castle.

Barjin, a cleric, was head of the clerical order as well as of the triumvirate. When Barjin died, Aballister Bonaduce took over the leadership.

Aballister Bonaduce was the head of the wizards. Another notable member was the mage Dorigen.

The ogrillon Ragnor was the leader of the sect's warriors
