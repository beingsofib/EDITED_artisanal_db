Duir's Alley

Duir's Alley was a small alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Duir's Alley was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward. It connected the Street of Bells in the west with the Street of the Sword in the east via passing through a neighborhood of buildings. Lhoril's Alley branched off of the southern side of Duir's Alley.

Activities

Patrons of the Elfstone Tavern often exited the building's rear onto Duir's Alley where they displayed their magical prowess.
