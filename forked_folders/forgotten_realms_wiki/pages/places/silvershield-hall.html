Silvershield Hall was a dwarven-owned and operated tavern found in Myth Drannor.

Location

The tavern was located in the far-western side of the city, in the district of Sheshyrinnam.

Structure

It was a massive two-story structure built atop a two-level cellar. The ground level housed the main taproom, with offices and storage on the floor above and the brewery built into the converted cellar.

Services

Arrak Mithrose was the owner of Silvershield Hall, one of the few dwarves who ran a business within the city. He employed a staff made up entirely of other dwarves along with some humans.
