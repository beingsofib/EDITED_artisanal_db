Muirenon Lake

Muirenon Lake was the second-largest lake forming the Teardrops in Narfell.

Geography

Muirenon Lake was the center-most and longest of the lakes forming the Teardrops. It was connected to the lakes Mereth and Muskar by small rivers.

Inhabitants

The Ragnor tribe lived along the northern Teardrop lakes of Muirenon and Muskar.
