Yellow Sea

The Yellow Sea washed the northern shores of Kara-Tur and the haunted island of Talmaruk, as well as the west coast of Koryo.

Geography

Some in Shou Lung considered the Yellow Sea to be the same as the Celestial Sea, which lay in the south.

Inhabitants

Many tribal settlements dotted the shores of the Yellow Sea. These were inhabited by Wu-haltai, who believed in the Lord of the Sea.

Legends

The Koryoans knew the Yellow Sea as a home to sea serpents and strange magical phenomena, such as a green mist that transformed people into fish and a blue ice so cold it burned flesh, yet sizzled and smoked when immersed in water.

The Wu-Haltai told that the worst winter storms were breathed out by the evil spirits of the isle of Talmaruk.
