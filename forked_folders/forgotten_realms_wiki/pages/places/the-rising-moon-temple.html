The Rising Moon was a temple of Selûne in the city of Tsurlagol in the Vast.

By 1367 DR, the temple was led by High Priest Orlathon Lunemast, who was still in charge by 1370 DR. Beneath him were nine sub-priests of differing ranks.

The Rising Moon was the wealthiest of Tsurlagol's temples.
