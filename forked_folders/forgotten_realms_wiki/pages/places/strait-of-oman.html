Strait of Oman

The Strait of Oman was a narrow channel of the Sea of Moonshae that separated the islands of Oman's Isle and Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The strait measured approximately from end to end. Several settlements were located along its length. The Codsrun Creek emptied into the strait at the Codsbay on Gwynneth's north shore.
