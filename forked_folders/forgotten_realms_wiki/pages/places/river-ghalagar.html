River Ghalagar

The River Ghalagar was a small waterway found in western Halruaa.

Geography

The River Ghalagar originated in the Lhairghal Mountains on Halruaa's western border. It flowed southeast through the foothills of the mountains where it bisected the Akhlaur Swamp before ultimately reaching Lake Halruaa. The river had a length of approximately .
