Undal's Lane

Undal's Lane was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Undal's Lane was located in the south section of Waterdeep's North Ward. The road branched east off of the north side of Andamaar's Street, connecting with Golden Serpent Street just north of Black Cat Way. An unnamed alley branched off the north side of Undal's Lane leading to the Magecourt.

Notable Locations

Downybeard Tobacconist sat on the eastern side of Undal's Lane where it joined Golden Serpent Street.
