Sweepaway Ford

Sweepaway Ford was a ford in Northknife Pass, across the Dustwall Mountains.

Description

The ford was about wide and several feet deep, near the start of the pass on its Bralizzar side. Its ice cold waters were very quick, and perilous to cross.
