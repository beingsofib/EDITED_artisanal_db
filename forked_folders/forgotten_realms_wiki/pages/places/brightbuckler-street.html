Brightbuckler Street

Brightbuckler Street was a street in Silverymoon.

Description

Brightbuckler Street was located in Northbank. It was a relatively short street, running a full length of only about 100 yards. It began at the River Rauvin, east of the Moonbridge, and headed north. It then bent to the west and merged into the Moonway.
