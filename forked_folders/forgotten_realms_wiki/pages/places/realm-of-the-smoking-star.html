Realm of the Smoking Star

Location
nameRealm of the Smoking Star
regionBorder Kingdoms, south Faerûn

The Realm of the Smoking Star was a bucolic forest permeated with wild magic, located in the northeastern Border Kingdoms. It was considered one of the most magic-strong lands in the Kingdoms.
