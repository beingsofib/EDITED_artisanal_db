Bruilhaven was the opulent mansion and home home of the House Bruil in Melvaunt around 1367 DR.

Location

The mansion was located in the northwest section of Melvaunt.

Description

Ivy covered the exterior of the mansion. The interior was decorated with tastefully lavish furnishings.

Defenses

Fifteen men-at-arms were on duty at all times to guard the mansion.
