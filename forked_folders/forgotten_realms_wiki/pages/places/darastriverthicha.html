Darastriverthicha ("Dragon Mountain", in Draconic) was an ancient dragon kingdom during the Dawn Ages ( to ). It warred with the giant kingdoms of the day. It was ruled by Caesinsjach, a mighty green dragon.

Among its vassal states was Darastrixhurthi, a nation of kobolds ruled by Kurtulmak, located in what is now the Shoal of Thirst.

History

During the first Rage of Dragons, circa , Caesinsjach and her fellow dragons destroyed Darastriverthicha and all its vassal states.
