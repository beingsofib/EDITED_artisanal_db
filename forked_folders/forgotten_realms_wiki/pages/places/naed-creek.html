Naed Creek

Naed Creek () was a flowing stream of water found in the Moonsea North region of north Faerûn.

Etymology

The creek's name "Naed", originated from the word "naeth", that was an epithet used in the place of the word dung.

Geography

It flowed east from the Galenas from the Valley of the Falls, snaked its way between the steppes of the Ride and the wetlands of Thar, before emptying into Lake Longreach.

Geographical Features

The creek was crossed by two major trails, the Ilinvur Trail to the west and the North Glister Path to the east.
