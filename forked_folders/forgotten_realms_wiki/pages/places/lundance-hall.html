Lundance Hall

Building
nameLundance Hall
typeResidence
locationUptown District, Ravens Bluff
ownerLeorduin family

Lundance Hall was an eye-catching residence located in the Uptown District of Ravens Bluff during the 14 century DR. The summer home of the noble Leorduin family featured a row of pillars along its front side, highlighted by extremely realistic statues of crouching harpies.

The residence was located on Torngel's Ride in the Tentowers neighborhood of the city.
