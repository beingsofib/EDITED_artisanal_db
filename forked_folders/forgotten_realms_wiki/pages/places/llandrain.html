Llandrain was a small village located on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Llandrain sat between the southern shore of Mal Feargal and the coast of the Sea of Swords, making it the northernmost settlement in Snowdown. The northern end of Snowdown Ride began here. The harbor here was dangerous because of hidden coral reefs and frequent raids from Northlanders.

History

During the height of the Kingdom of Snowdown, Llandrain was one of the larger and more influential cantrevs in regards to influencing the monarch's policies.

As of the late 15 century DR, the town was tightly controlled by Amn and was home to many miner operations which extracted valuable metals from the nearby Andover Heights. The locals sought the aid of adventurers to rid them of their occupiers.

Inhabitants

As of the late 15 century DR, the village, Dwarven mercenaries hired by Amn resided in the town to ensure that mineral resources were mined and protected efficiently. At the same time, Llandrain was home to the largest population of Ffolk patriots loyal to House Kendrick who despised Snowdown's Amnian occupiers. The leader of this group was the blacksmith Miles Maddoch.
