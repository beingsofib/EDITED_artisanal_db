Hlethvagi's Coins was a moneychanging house located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The building stood two stories tall.

Location

Hlethvagi's Coins was located in the far southern section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward near the South Gate where the Way of the Dragon and Waymoot joined. The rear of the building sat on Carter's Way.
