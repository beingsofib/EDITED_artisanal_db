Belzound Street

Belzound Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Belzound Street was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's North Ward. The road branched south from Tarsar's Street and joined with Suldown Street after a short distance.

Notable Locations

A surface shaft connecting to Waterdeep's sewers was located along the midpoint of Belzound Street. The noble villa for the Roaringhorn family was located on the west side of the street.
