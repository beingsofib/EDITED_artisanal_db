Items for the Immortals was a shop located in the Court District of Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Location

Items for the Immortals was located within the Court District since only the residents of that lavish section of Huzuz could afford the price of most of the items sold by the shop.

Inhabitants

Essafah al-Jamila owned and operated this shop, selling goods needed by both genies and sha'irs.
