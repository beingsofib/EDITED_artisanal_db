Dallabad Oasis was an oasis and nearby palatial fortress in Calimshan in the Lands of Intrigue in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

It lay in the Calim Desert, a short distance outside of Calimport along the northwestern trails.

Description

The oasis itself was but a collection of small ponds surrounded by a few palm trees and large boulders. Passing caravans set up their tents and pavilions close by.

From the outside, the fortress seemed equally unremarkable, appearing to be a simple square-walled fortress built of brown stone appeared to blend in with the surrounding desert sands and dunes. It was surrounded by an open field, almost a wide, which separated it from the oasis and made it hard for infiltrators to get close unobserved.

Structure

The outer walls stood some high, and were constructed of brown sandstone and gray-hued granite with polished white marble joints. They were quite sheer to all but the most expert climbers. The space between the walls and inner keep formed a courtyard.

The square outer walls concealed a roughly circular keep within. Its corridors wound inward in ever tighter circles, leading ultimately to its master's treasury rooms and private chambers. These halls were secured with heavy, metal-banded doors comprising layers of wood and stone that would withstand physical and even magical attack. Sturdy locks, alarms, and traps further enhanced security.

Deep tunnels ran beneath the fortress

Interior

Within, the keep was decorated with floor-to-ceiling tapestries and doors adorned with gold leaf.

Defenses

At first glance, the fortress did not appear to be very strong, nor well-defended. The robed guards patrolling the walls seemed bored by their quiet duty. In fact, the fortress's defenses were designed more to thwart thieves and protect its master's treasures. The guards were actually courageous, highly trained, and well-equipped with chainmail, bucklers, and enchanted swords. Kohrin relied on the power of Charon's Claw and of Yharaskrik to counter any magical attack.

Government

In the mid-1300s DR, Dallabad Oasis was ruled with an iron fist by Kohrin Soulez, who exploited his power over the enclosed oasis and the caravans that stopped by and slew many who displeased him. Jealously guarding his treasures and especially the evil sentient sword Charon's Claw, he remained isolated,, keeping himself locked up in his fortress. The fortress was independent of Calimport's guilds as none of them had the power to take the fortress, even if two or three came together in alliance.

In order for Kohrin Soulez to run Dallabad Oasis, he had five trustworthy lieutenants with him at all times, three of which were his own children, including his daughter Ahdahnia Soulez, a fourth was a talented military commander from Memnon, and the other was a thief from Calimport named Preelio. A sixth lieutenant that only Soulez's closest commanders knew about was a mind flayer by the name of Yharaskrik, who aided in interrogation and torture but otherwise stayed apart.

After Kohrin's death in 1366 DR, it was commanded by Ahdahnia Soulez, on behalf of House Basadoni.

Activities

The Soulez clan collected tolls from caravans passing through the area.

Dallabad Oasis also served as a relay point for communication between Pasha Da'Daclan of Calimport and his more powerful allies far away, and Da'Daclan paid Soulez for this, whilst remaining independent.

History

At some point, the illithid known as Yharaskrik had come to Dallabad Oasis seeking to study Charon's Claw. It fought with Kohrin and pretended to be defeated by him and Charon's Claw's power, before Kohrin offered it a deal. Yharaskrik studied the Charon's Claw artifact while living in the deep tunnels below Kohrin Soulez's throne room.

For decades, Dallabad Oasis had been held by the Soulez clan. It was said that nothing had changed and nothing of note had happened at Dallabad Oasis in twenty years, as of the mid-1360s DR. That was until the , when the assassin Artemis Entreri took an interest in Dallabad Oasis and especially in Charon's Claw. Entreri's friends Jarlaxle and Sharlotta Vespers in House Basadoni sought to disrupt Pasha Da'Daclan's line of communication to his allies, and so sought to take Dallabad for themselves. and Jarlaxle and Rai-guy Bondalek led a force of Bregan D'aerthe drow mercenaries to overwhelm the stunned defenders. Meanwhile, Entreri used the distraction and confusion to get over the walls and get behind Kohrin as he led a hasty defense, infiltrating the keep ahead of him. The drow used sleep poison to knock out the defenders and accepted surrenders, taking the majority alive. Having already been contacted by Kimmurel Oblodra, Yharaskrik abandoned its servitude to Kohrin as it much preferred drow to humans, and departed. Then Entreri surprised Kohrin and Ahdahnia in the throne room and the two men fought. During the battle Entreri disarmed Kohrin of Charon's Claw and the protective glove he wore, and deftly changed it for a copy. When Kohrin donned the false glove and again picked up the cursed sword, he was killed. Artemis Entreri took Charon's Claw from Kohrin Soulez's lifeless hand.

In the aftermath, Jarlaxle and Bregan D'aerthe kept Dallabad Oasis for themselves, now using it as a base and relay point for the Basadoni Guild. Ahdahnia Soulez and the many surviving guards agreed to stay on and actually run the place—the psychic power of Crenshinibon helped sway their minds and obscure their memories. This maintained appearances of a near-seamless transition of power to the outside world. Back in the tunnels, Yharaskrik allied himself with Kimmuriel Oblodra. The creature taught Kimmuriel how to prevent Crenshinibon from controlling him, thereby making Kimmuriel an ally of necessity.

Days later, rumor in the criminal underworld of Calimport was that Ahdahnia had slain her father in a coup triggered by the cursed sword, and she had sold Charon's Claw to Entreri, as he'd earlier offered to buy it from Kohrin. Nevertheless, many of those in the know were skeptical of this story and the new crystalline tower and suspected the truth. Pasha Broucalle of Memnon sent spies Lipke, Trulbul, and Rolmanet to Dallabad to observe. Jarlaxle experimented with the power the Crystal Shard even as it gained influence over him, together mind-controlling and slaying the spies. Later, Jarlaxle even erected a second crystalline tower within the fortress.

Finally, House Basadoni's covert control of Dallabad Oasis was an open secret in Calimport and Kimmuriel and Rai-guy took control of Bregan D'aerthe, owing to the Crystal Shard's influence over Jarlaxle, forcing he and Entreri to flee Dallabad Oasis whilst the crystalline towers collapsed. Dallabad Oasis remained in the clutches of House Basadoni and Bregan D'aerthe, at least until Kimmuriel and Rai-guy took their drow mercenary force back to the Underdark and killed Sharlotta Vespers, leaving the Basadoni guild in turmoil and Dallabad in the command of Ahdahnia Soulez.

Notable Inhabitants

  • Kohrin Soulez, commander of Dallabad Oasis and wielder of Charon's Claw and its gauntlet.
  • Ahdahnia Soulez, his daughter and successor, serving Bregan D'aerthe.
  • Tonakin Ta'salz, a courageous solder amongst the guards.
  • Yharaskrik, a mind flayer dwelling below ground.
