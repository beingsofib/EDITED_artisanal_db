The Long Passage was a hallway in Castle Obarskyr.

History

In , during the aftermath of the massacre during the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the , the Marchioness Immerdusk, Storm Silverhand, while trying to furtively leave, was seeking the Long Passage when along the way she overheard the War Wizard Surgol Velard in the Mages' Dutychamber and took a detour to free her own comrade, mask dancer Amarune Whitewave.
