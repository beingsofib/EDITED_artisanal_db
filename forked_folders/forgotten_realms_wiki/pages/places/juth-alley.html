Juth Alley

Juth Alley was a small side alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Juth Alley was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward. The alley was found just south of Caravan Court, branching southeast from the Rising Ride and branching north and south within a neighborhood a short distance away. The northern branch cut back to connect with Caravan Court, while the southern branch ending on Robin's Way.
