Viledel's Isle, also known as the Island of Viledel, was an island in the Korinn Archipelago of the Moonshae Isles.

Description

It was said to be one of the least hospitable islands in the archipelago.

Geography

The isle lay in the western edge of the Korinn Archipelago, near Pandira.

History

The island was claimed by the "Sea King" Viledel in the , who chose it because of its proximity to pirates with whom he was at war to claim control over the Korinn Archipelago. Forty years later, pirates sacked the island, killed Viledel, and scattered his followers. In the decades that followed, the location of the island was lost, and inhabitants of the Korinn Archipelago knew only that it lay somewhere in the west of the isles.

Inhabitants

By the mid–14 century DR, the island's only inhabitant was Keestake.
