West Wind Stables were stables in Freedale in the Dalelands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The stables were along the way to the Tower of the High Wizard Netheril, next to the Temple of Lathander.

Structure

There was an exercise field in the back.

Interior

The building had an office and living quarters attached. It had a hayloft.

Services

The owner, Joben, and his son Markus looked after horses, and sometimes traded horses, too. They allowed some adventurers to sleep on the hayloft.
