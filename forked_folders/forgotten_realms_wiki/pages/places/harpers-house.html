The Harper's House was the name given to the personal residence of Chesslyn Onaubra, the local guardswoman that offered her home as a safehouse to her fellow members of the Harpers.

Location

It was located in the southeast side of Mulmaster, not too far away from the city's docks.

Structure

Perfect for spies and information-gatherers, the home was a rather non-descript brick-and-wood building that did not attract unneeded attention to Chesslyn's comings and goings.

Interior

Atmosphere

With its cozy and welcoming atmosphere, the Harper's House was a welcome refuge for agents operating in the harsh city of Mulmaster.
