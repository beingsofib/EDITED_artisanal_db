Chawdik was a poor island that was part of the Korinn Archipelago in the Sea of Swords

Description

Chawdik was a small and humble island located to the west of its much larger neighbor, the isle of Ventris. The island's large gnomish population resided on the island's southern coast.

Notable Inhabitants

  • Gofin, or Gofus, a gnomish fisher from the isle of Chawdik in the 14 century DR.
