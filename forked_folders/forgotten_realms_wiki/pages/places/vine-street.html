Vine Street

Vine Street was a road in the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the 14 century DR.

Location

It lay in the western part of Loudwater.

Description

It was a major thoroughfare, taking over from Pool's Way where it turned toward the docks and receiving much traffic of pedestrians, horses, and wagons. It was dense with the shops that attracted them.
