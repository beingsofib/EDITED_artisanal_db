The Heart of the Hand was a temple devoted to Iyachtu Xvim, the Godson of Bane.

Location

The temple was located beneath the ruins of Zhentil Keep.

Inhabitants

High Tyrannar Fzoul Chembryl and Ruinlord Xana the Once-Martyred ran the Church of Iyachtu Xvim from within the temple.
