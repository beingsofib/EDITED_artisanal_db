Huhrong's Road

The Huhrong's Road was a prominent roadway found in northern Rashemen.

Geography

The Huhrong's Road originated in Rashemen's capital city of Immilmar near Lake Ashane. The road passed east just north of the Ashenwood before connecting with Mulptan and continued northeast along the outskirts of the Endless Wastes until reaching Nathoud.
