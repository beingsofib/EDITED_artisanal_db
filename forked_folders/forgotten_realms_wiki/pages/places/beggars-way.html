Beggars' Way

Beggars' Way was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 century DR.

Location

Beggars' Way was located in the southwest corner of Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. The road ran north to south, beginning along Nool Street in the north and passing south to end at Merchant Street near the outer wall. Beggars' Way was intersected by Fish Street and Pride Street.

Notable Locations

A warehouse used exclusively by the Zhentarim and a Zhentilar barracks were located on Beggars' Way.
