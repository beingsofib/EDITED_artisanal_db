Griffon's Roost

Griffon's Roost was a towering mountain peak among the hills surrounding the city of Ravens Bluff in the Vast, and other flying mounts that were dropping off important visitors, noblemen, costly goods for merchants, or important messages. By the late 14 century DR, it was being used by the Griffon Ride,

Description

The top of the spiraling peak was the exit point of a one-way portal in Wyllowwood, a sublevel of the legendary Sword Coast North dungeon known as Undermountain.

History

In spring of the , notorious con artist Mortimer Mittlemer was expecting the delivery of six ceramic cylinders, one of which concealed the stolen emerald collection of a wealthy Dalefolk lord near Shadowdale. He couldn't ask his courier Warden Jameson to land his griffon mount just anywhere near Ravens Bluff, as it could attract unwanted attention, so Mortimer requested he land at Griffon's Roost. However, being a wanted conman, he couldn't travel through several neighborhoods and pick up the package himself.

Mortimer then came up with a scheme, wherein he would use his illusion magic to take on the appearance of Charles Frederik LaVerne Blacktree IV, but go by the alias of "Lord Merriwether Winston Sheffield-Trublood the Third". Elmerth managed to find a willing group of six adventurers in Volodar's Stardust Inn.

At the peak they were met by griffon rider Warden Jameson, dressed in a long forest green robe with white embroidery and a tall, off-white turban with a silver & onyx brooch pinned to its front. Warden was initially hesitant, as he had only been anticipating Mortimer sending at most two couriers, and rebuffed them when they tried to inquire as to what was inside the cylinders. It was unknown whether or not the adventurers stuck to their original arrangement and delivered the cylinders to Mortimer.

In the , a Dwarven citadel in Realmspace was skyjacked by a illusionist trickster named Tim Tumbleweed, who refused to leave until its denizens could best him in joke telling. They fanned out over the planet in search of someone that could aid them in ridding their citadel of Tim's menace. The pair Dilwa Dundab & Thorin Bucklebrand traveled to the Vast, landing atop Griffon's Roost in a wasp. At that time the region was in the midst of the Three Women War and a group of giant eagles on loan from Procampur were roosting on the peak.

The pair luckily managed to find a group of adventurers in Ravens Bluff willing to help and escorted them to their spelljammer.
