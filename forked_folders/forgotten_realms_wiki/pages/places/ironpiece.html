Ironpiece

Celestial body
nameIronpiece
typeWorldlet.

Ironpiece was one of the "worldlets" that existed in the same crystal sphere as the Rock of Bral.

It was inhabited by gnomes and had at least one lake, called Lake Crashsplash. The worldlet was also, at least partially, covered in forests.
