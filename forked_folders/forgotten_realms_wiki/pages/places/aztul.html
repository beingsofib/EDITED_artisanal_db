Aztul was a large town found in southern Thindol on the mouth of the Thingulph.

Geography

Aztul was located approximately due south of Delselar. The town was located on the coast of the Great Sea wedged in between two portions of the Guth Mountains.

Description

Aztul was a rather unremarkable fishing town.

Activities

Many of the wealthy Thindolese flocked to Aztul with dreams of hunting a blood ape, whose pelts became a status symbol in Thindol.

Rumors & Legends

A "City of Apes" was rumored to exist somewhere on the forested slopes of the Gath Mountains near Aztul.
