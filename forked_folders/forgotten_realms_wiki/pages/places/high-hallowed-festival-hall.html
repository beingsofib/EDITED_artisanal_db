The High Hallowed Festival Hall was a temple and hall dedicated to the worship of Lliira in Yhaunn in 1358 DR.

Inhabitants

Joybringer Endress Halatar was the High Mistress of Revels for the temple. She was served by 22 other priestesses. Together they attended a congregation of 49 worshipers of Lliira.
