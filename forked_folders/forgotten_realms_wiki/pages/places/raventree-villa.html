Raventree Villa, also known as Raventowers, was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Raventree.

Location

The villa was located at the corner of Sul Street and Chasso's Trot in the northeast section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward.

Description

Raventree Villa was composed of several structures reaching two and three storeys in height. The walled estate dominated much of the city block where is was located.
