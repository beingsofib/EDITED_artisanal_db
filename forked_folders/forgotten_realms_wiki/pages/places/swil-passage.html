Swil Passage

The Swil Passage was a small strait of the Sea of Fallen Stars, situated somewhat south of the seafaring state of Swil in the Whamite Isles. It shared shores with Swil, as well as the Land of the Beastly Brigands and Agenais.

The passage was a key trade route during a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles. Notable ships that sailed the passage were the Carrack, the Smardese Fleet, the Zomeze Merchant, and the Teclar Trader. As the traders passed, the pirates of the Land of the Beastly Brigands and Swil took gold from their ships. Privateers from the islands of Chu, Chi, and Wu also plundered from the passage.
