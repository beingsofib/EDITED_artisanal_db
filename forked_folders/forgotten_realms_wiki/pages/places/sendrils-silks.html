Sendril's Silks was a silk merchant's shop in the town of Ormath of the Shining Plains in the 14 century DR.

History

As of the 14 century DR, Sendril's Silks was ran by Ucharntar Sendril. According to Elminster Aumar's investigation, Ucharntar was one of the most active agents of the Cult of Tiamat at the time. The old wizard informed the Harpers of his findings and recommended for the agents to watch Sendril's Silks as the cult's shillings indicated inevitable expansion into Sembia and Westgate.
