The Dancing Coin was a moneychanging house located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The house was located in south Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of the Foreign Quarter on Zhent Avenue.

Inhabitants

The Dancing Coin was owned by a man named Andalias in the .

Defenses

Since upwards of 75,000 gp worth of currency was kept in the Dancing Coin at any given time, Andalias took security seriously. He had a wizard create a pair of clay golems that were under his control thanks to an enchanted ring he wore at all times. Zhentilar patrols made it a point to stay close to the Dancing Coin as well, perhaps because of well-placed bribes.
