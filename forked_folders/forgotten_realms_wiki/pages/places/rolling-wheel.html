The Rolling Wheel was an establishment of Scornubel in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Interior

The common room of the inn held a large space for dancers, which were visible from the threshold of the entrance.

Atmosphere

At least one night in 1369 DR, the place was full of roaring men and scrawny dancers.

Services

The place had dancers.

History

While disguised as the peddler "Tarthan", Dove Falconhand determined at a glance that the place was not to her interest
