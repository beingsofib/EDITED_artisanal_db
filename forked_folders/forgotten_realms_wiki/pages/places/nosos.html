Nosos was a Domain of Dread focused near-entirely around its eponymous city.

Description

The domain was just about completely taken up by a city of six thousand inhabitants, which was so constantly choked in stench, filth and smog that the Mists were almost unnoticeable. Decaying garbage took up the rest of the countryside; its nobility was constantly preoccupied with avoiding disease, which they believed were carried by the lower class.
