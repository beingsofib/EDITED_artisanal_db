The Smokehouse was a smokehouse in Waterdeep that was owned by the Waterdhavian guild known as the Fishmongers' Fellowship.

Location

This smokehouse was located along Dock Street, It was located near the Fish Warehouse, which was also owned by the Fishmongers' Fellowship.

Structure

This building was one-story tall and constructed of brick. It almost always smelled of curing fish and other meats. By 1372 DR, a second story was added to the building.

Services

Besides the Fishmongers' Fellowship, this smokehouse was used by butchers. Both those unassociated, and associated, with the Waterdhavian Guild of Butchers. Though they had to pay a fee to the Fishmongers' Fellowship.

Reputation

This smokehouse was among some of the more notable and recognizable shops in the Dock Ward.
