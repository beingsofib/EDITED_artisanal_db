Tresset's Ringyl was a town in Tressardon, long-destroyed by the mid–8 century DR.

Description

Long overgrown, Tresset's Ringyl was full of trees. Only forty buildings were visible over their treetops. There was a castle in the middle, the Ringyl.

Geography

The Ringyl sat inside a valley. It was east of Felmorel, and further east was the Moonshorn Tower.

Government

Once governed by the bandit Tresset, Tresset's Ringyl had been the seat of the short-lived kingdom of Tressardon that he declared. However, the kingdom just about died with him, surviving him by onluy a few days.

History

In the , Elminster Aumar, a Chosen of Mystra, crossed Tresset's Ringyl while seeking Dasumia; he found the streets strewn with bones, and much ruin. He also gave the Ringyl a cursory exploration before spending the night in a nearby hilltop. That sorceress was in fact the Dasumia he sought.
