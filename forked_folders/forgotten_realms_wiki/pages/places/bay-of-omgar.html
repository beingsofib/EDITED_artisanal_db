Bay of Omgar

The Bay of Omgar was located on the southern coast of Samarach on the Chultan Peninsula of southwest Faerûn.

Geography

The Snout of Omgar formed most of the western boundary of the bay, with the eastern side curving around past the city of Samargol and the far southeastern edge of the Sanrach Mountains.
