Wet River

The Wet River was a river found in the Vilhon Reach region of central Faerûn.

Geography

The Wet River originated in the southwestern slopes of the Orsraun Mountains and flowed westward across the Shining Plains. The river passed through the Wetwoods at the base of the mountains, contributing to the bog-like conditions that gave them their name, and wound north before emptying into the Long Arm Lake on the Dragon Coast.

Notable Locations

  • Assam, a small city that was governed by the two city-states of Lheshayl and Ormath.
