Pandira was an island within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Pandira was one of the larger of the many isles in the Korinn Archipelago. It was found in the northern region of the archipelago surrounded by many smaller islands. Pandira was rather unremarkable save for the settlement of Westhaven.

Trade

Westhaven was a hub for black market trade, and Pandira's businesses thrived off of this reputation.

Inhabitants

The isle's residents, centered around the port at Westhaven, were almost exclusively pirates and other ruffians who plied the northern seas. Notably, the Northlanders were on unfriendly terms with their kin elsewhere in the Moonshaes. The island hosted a large number of outlaws, many of whom were hostile even to each other, and as such was governed by a single rule prohibiting aggression or violence on the island's shores. Breaking this rule meant that any pirate or raider was in turn free to inflict violence on the rule-breaker.
