Paektu-san

Location
namePaektu-san
areaKoguryo, Koryo, Kara-Tur

Paektu-san was a settlement in the kingdom of Koryo in Kara-Tur.

Geography

Paektu-san lay on the southern coast of the western peninsula, north of the Dragon's Teeth, a series of straits that were impossible to navigate.
