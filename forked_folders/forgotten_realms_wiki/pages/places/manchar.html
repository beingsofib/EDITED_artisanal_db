Manchar was a town in the kingdom of Koryo in Kara-Tur, lying in the eastern arm of the Vorkani Mountains.

Around 1357 DR, the town was the home of the wandering holy man Korax of Manchar. It was also the base of the Vi'oontu ninja clan.
