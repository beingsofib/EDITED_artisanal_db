The Slithering Plains was a plot of land that laid immediately to the west from the freshwater lake of Nagawater in the Golden Plains.

Description

The Slithering Plains bordered Nagawater's west, while the Naga Plains bordered its sough-eastern shores. The plains were frequented by nagas – the lake's namesake, who dwelled in the southern portion of Nagawater.
