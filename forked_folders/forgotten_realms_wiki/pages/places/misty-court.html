Misty Court

Misty Court was a humble and perpetually foggy street in the Tumbledown district of Baldur's Gate's Outer City.

History

In the summer of the , on Kythorn 28, was visited by Tav, an adventuring detective who was investigating a series of grizzly murders. One of the dead bodies was fished out of River Chionthar, wrapped in a tarp taken from one of the workshops on Misty Court. Tav found the open-air stonemason's workshop called Alabaster Stonecrafts. The twins who ran the workshop told the investigator that strange happenings had been plaguing the nearby Cliffside Cemetery, claiming that the gravedigger – Grom Skullsmash, was responsible, or the ghosts.

Notable Locations

  • Alabaster Stonecrafts, the workshop of "Sister" and "Brother Alabaster" in the late 15 century DR.
