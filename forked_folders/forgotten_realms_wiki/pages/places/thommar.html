Thommar

Location
nameThommar
areasoutheast Faerûn

Thommar was a location lying somewhere near Durpar, south of the Dustwall Mountains in southeast Faerûn. It lay south of Bralizzar, a town nestled in the Dustwall.

The mountains shielded Thommar from the Raurin desert.

Notable Inhabitants

The noted astrologer and mage Aman Al-Raqib of Bralizzar in the 14th century DR hailed from Thommar.
