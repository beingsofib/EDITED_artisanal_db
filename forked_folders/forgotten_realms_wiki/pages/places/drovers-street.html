Drovers' Street

Drovers' Street was a hard-packed dirt and gravel paved road in the city of Waterdeep that ran through the Trades Ward and into the Southern Ward.

Geography

This road ran from north to south, running south off of the southern half of Salabar Street (sometimes known as Moorewalk) in the Trades Ward, in a manner roughly parallel to Buckle Street, and passed through Sahtyra's Lane as it moved into the Southern Ward. Along the way, Shoor Street ran west off of Drovers' Street.

An unnamed cul-de-sac opened east off the north end of this street.
