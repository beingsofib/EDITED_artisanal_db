The Temple of Chauntea was a house of worship for Chauntea found in the capital city of Immilmar in Rashemen in the .

Location

The building was located in the southwest section of the city along the shore of Lake Ashane.

Activities

Bhalla, as Chauntea was known in Rasheman, worship was closely monitored by the faithful within the temple to ensure that nothing strayed too far from official church doctrines, fearing that Rashemen's isolation could lead to a cult developing.

Inhabitants

The clerics of the temple were supervised by Brother Shad.
