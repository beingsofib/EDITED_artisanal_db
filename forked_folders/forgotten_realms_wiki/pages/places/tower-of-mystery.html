The Tower of Mystery or Tower of Mysteries was a temple to Mystra, the Lady of Mysteries, in Saerloon in Sembia.

Leadership

Around 1370 DR, the temple was led by Mage Arcane Tanthala, who was assisted by Lord of Mystery Telornym.

History

In Eleint of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR, Tunaster Dranik, an itinerant cleric of Mystra, journeyed to the Tower of Mystery in search of an explanation or assistance regarding the mysterious Temple of Mystra in Wheloon in neighboring Cormyr. He believed someone there would be able to teleport him back to Wheloon if needed.
