Cragdyk's Arms & Armor was a weapon and armor shop in the city of Neverwinter in the mid-14 century DR.

Location

Cragdyk's smithy stood not far from the Neverwinter Square, one of the oldest locations in the Jewel of North.

History

Cragdyk's Arms & Armor was established sometime before the . The proprietor had a wide selection of mundane weapons and armor available for a reasonable price.
