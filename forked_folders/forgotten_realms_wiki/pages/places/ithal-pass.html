Ithal Pass was a fortified town in the Iltkazar Highlands in Tethyr, in the southern region of County Surkazar, in the late 14 century DR.

Inhabitants

Once a small crossroads town supported by local dairy farmers, the city swelled in size with the influx of soldiers from the duke of Suretmarch's army. The garrison worked to fortify the town to repel the forces of the Knights of the Black Gauntlet, who had invaded nearby Kzelter.

Locations

  • Vales' Rest: This neglected taproom was the oldest and only inn in town, offering a warm atmosphere to its clientele.
  • Soldiers' Slake: A converted barn served the military soldiers and officers while the town's wall was being built.
