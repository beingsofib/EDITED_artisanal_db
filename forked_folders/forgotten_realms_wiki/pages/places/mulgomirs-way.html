Mulgomir's Way

Mulgomir's Way was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Mulgomir's Way was located in Waterdeep's Castle Ward. It ran north to south in between two larger avenues—the Street of Silks to the west and the Street of Silver to the east. Mulgomir's Way originated in the north on Selduth Street where it branched south then passed through Jester's Court and Howling Cat Court before terminating on Cage Street.
