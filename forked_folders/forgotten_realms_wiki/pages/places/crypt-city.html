Crypt City referred to a specific section of the coastal town of Tulmon on the Lake of Steam that was haunted by undead.

Description

Crypt City covered the entire western half of Tulmon. Aside from the roaming undead, this ruined area was abandoned with the exception of some brave criminals who used it as a base. A set of walls separated the rebuilt section of Tulmon from the dangerous ruins.
