The Butterbisk Inn was an inn in Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, run by Harold Butterbisk sometime before the late 15 century DR.

History

At some point before the , Harold Butterbisk ran the Butterbisk Inn and used the enchanted heavy crossbow that was named after himself – Harold. The namesake crossbow had an engraving on its stock that read "Quaffing is a shootable offence." At some point after the Butterbisk Inn changed owners, its name was changed.
