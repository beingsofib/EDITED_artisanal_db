Jeradin was a town located in the Eastern Shaar during the mid–14 century DR.

Geography

Jeradin sat along the southern foothills of the Unthangol Mountains just south of the source of the River Angol. The town sat along a trail in between the Council Hills to the east and the town of Hardcastle to the west.
