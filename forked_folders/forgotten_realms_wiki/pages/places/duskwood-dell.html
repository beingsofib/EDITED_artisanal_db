Duskwood Dell was the most sacred site of Eldath located in Faerûn.

Location

Duskwood Dell was nestled within the Snakewood in eastern Amn.

Description

The River Rimril plunged down Eldath's Mount on the eastern face of the Troll Mountains, creating the Green Goddess Falls that fell for over to a series of smaller waterfalls and pools.

Inhabitants

Most Exalted Fallskeeper Alatoasz Berendim lived within the small tree city of Eldathyn. Here he instructed new priests in ways to spread worship of their goddess throughout the continent.
