nameThe Naughty Nixie

The Naughty Nixie was an inn and tavern found in Yhaunn in the 14 century DR.

Services

The Naughty Nixie was one of the newer establishments in Yhaunn as of 1358 DR. The adventurers and caravan masters who favored the Naughty Nixie could expect excellent accommodations and moderate prices, especially when compared to the exorbitant Firedrinkers' Inn.
