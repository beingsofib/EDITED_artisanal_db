Rejviik's Mortuary was a business located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The building stood three stories tall.

Location

Rejviik's Mortuary was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward near the outer wall of the City of the Dead where Ironpost Street meet Zendulth Street.
