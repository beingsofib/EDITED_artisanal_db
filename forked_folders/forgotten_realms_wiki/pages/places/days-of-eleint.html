Days of Eleint was a tavern in the Amnian city of Esmeltaran during the 14 century DR.

Location

The tavern was built on the western edge of Esmeltaran near Lake Esmel.

Services

The Days of Eleint provided fair services for expensive prices. The place was decorated with the luxuries of Tethyrian nobles taken during the Tethyrian Interregnum.
