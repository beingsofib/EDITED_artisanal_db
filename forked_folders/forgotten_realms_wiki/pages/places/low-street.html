Low Street

Low Street was a shady street in Khaerbaal, a port city of Halruaa.

History

Low Street of Khaerbaal was known for its rogues and thieves. Tzigone, then known as Sindra, a hero of Halruaa, was trained by Gwillon on Low Street in her youth. In the , Tzigone caught a young urchin who attempted to pickpocket her. Instead of turning him in, she gave his an electrum skie and and instructed him to find her old teacher who was always in need of apprentices.
