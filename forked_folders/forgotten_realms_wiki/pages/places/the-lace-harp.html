The Lace Harp was an exclusively female drinking and dining social club that catered to women of all races, and later, dominated by crossdressing Nobles of Waterdeep, in the late 14 century DR.

Location

The Lace Harp occupied rentable upper rooms on the third floor above Hammarak's Fine Laces and Adornments, the shop that traded in feminine garments, located in the third building west of Tchozal's Race, on Calamastyr Lane's southern side.

Services

The Lace Harp offered its guests quality dining and libations, as well as the company of fellow women. The club also hosted individuals with artistic proclivities who put up plays, including a popular operetta called Two Masked Dalliances and a Misunderstanding.{{Cite web/Forging the Realms/Lord Mantlepard's Homecoming

}}

History

In the period of time between the 1350s to 1370s DR, The Lace Harp's clientele consisted of females – human, elven, half-elven, hin, dwarvish, gnomish, and other races. Lady Dethelle Mantlepard and Nymara "Kitten" Scheiron, one of the Masked Lords of Waterdeep, were active members. By the late 1370s DR, the clientele largely shifted to be pampered noble and wanna-be-noble men of Waterdeep who were into crossdressing.
