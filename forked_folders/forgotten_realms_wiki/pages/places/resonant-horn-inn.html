The Resonant Horn Inn was an inn in the aquatic elf city of Myth Nantar, operated by the shalarin couple of Po'ioa'nam and Tu'ara'thal.

Location

This inn stood in the Dukar Quarter of Myth Nantar.

Structure

This building stood two stories tall.
