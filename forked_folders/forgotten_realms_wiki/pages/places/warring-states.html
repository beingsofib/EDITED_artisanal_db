The Warring States (or simply the warring states) were a group of countries in the south of Kara-Tur known for their conflicts.

Location

They lay in the wooded lands south of T'u Lung and between Petan in the west and Malatra in the east.

Relations

The Warring States were often at war with T'u Lung, ever since that empire was established in . Around , the Fengnao Province of T'u Lung had some political connections to the Warring States. By that time, Nu'sazto Ichi, the son of a captured general of the Warring States, had been brought to the imperial court in Wai, kept as an amusement despite his clumsy attempts to assassinate Emperor Wai Gada Sinzu. The emperor believed Nu'sazto to be a crude barbarian.
