Gaustus Street

Gaustus Street was a street of hard-packed dirt and gravel in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Geography

This street stretched from north to south, curving off of the Wall Way and terminating at the Court of the White Bull.

Notable Locations

Old Guildhall: the guildhall of the Cellarers' & Plumbers' Guild.
