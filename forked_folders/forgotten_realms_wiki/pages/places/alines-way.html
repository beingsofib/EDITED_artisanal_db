Aline's Way

Aline's Way was minor avenue of hard-packed earth and gravel in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Location

This alleyway ran southeast, extending off of Ship Street, until eventually turning northeast and terminating into Keel Alley.

Notable Locations

  • This alleyway ran along the backside of the shipwright shed of Arnagus.
  • At the mouth of Aline's Way, where it met with Keel Alley, there was a moon-shaped stone set into the ground that if activated would act as a portal to Maldiglas's Refuge.
