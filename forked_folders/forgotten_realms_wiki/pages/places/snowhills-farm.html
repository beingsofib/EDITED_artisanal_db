Snowhills Farm was a farm belonging to the Klaeverdar clan as of the late mid-14 century DR.

Location

The farm was northwest of Secomber.

History

Keldeln Klaeverdar headed one of the Klaeverdar branches from that farm. One of the potential successors to Gueladdeth Klaeverdar in , Andrath Klaeverdar, lived there.
