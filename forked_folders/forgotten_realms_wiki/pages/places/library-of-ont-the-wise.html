The Library of Ont the Wise was a library in the T'u Lung city of Balanzia, around the , that was either owned by or named for an individual known as Ont the Wise.

Location

This library was located in the western portion of Balanzia, across from the Cotnui Park and Zoo.

History

Around , this library was one of many buildings that were left intact in Balanzia as the city fell at the hands of the Shou Lung invasion of T'u Lung. Centuries later, around , the building that once housed the Library of Ont the Wise still stood within the abandoned city and was recorded as such on a map made of the city in that year.
