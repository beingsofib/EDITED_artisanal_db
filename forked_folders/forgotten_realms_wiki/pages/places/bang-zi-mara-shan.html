Bang Zi Mara Shan

Bang Zi Mara Shan was a range of mountains in northern Kara-Tur.

Geography

The mountain range was found to the east of the larger Koryaz Mountains, bordering the lands of Chukei and the Yellow Sea. To the north lay the Mountains of Amara and the Valley of Nanayok, and to the east in the ocean were the Talmaruk Islands.
