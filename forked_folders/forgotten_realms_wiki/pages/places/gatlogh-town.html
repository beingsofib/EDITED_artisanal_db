Gatloghwas one of the nine towns of the sahuagin kingdom of Kurrimal and its predecessor Aleaxtis.

Geography

This town was located within the Alamber Sea.

Government

Like all nine of the towns of Kurrimal, this town was governed by a prince of the same name, Gatlogh.

History

In the , when the kingdom of Kurrimal was founded as the successor of Aleaxtis, this town's name was changed to that of the prince put in charge of governing it.
