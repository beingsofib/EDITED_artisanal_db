Roguerun Alley

Roguerun Alley was a small back alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Roguerun Alley was located in the northwest section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward. The alley was situated in a small neighborhood located in between the Street of Glances and Breeze's Cut, branching from Breeze's Cut in the north, passing through the neighborhood, and exiting to join Westwall Street.
