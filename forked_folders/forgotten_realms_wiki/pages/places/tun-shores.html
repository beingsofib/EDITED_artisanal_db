Tun Shores

Location
nameTun Shores
regionCormyr, Interior Faerûn

The Tun Shores was a stretch of land in southwestern Cormyr administered by House Barelder from their estate of Windcrag House.

Geography

The Tun Shores lay along the north shore of the Tun River, in the foothills of the Storm Horns in the West Reaches of Cormyr.

Trade

The area was primarily populated by herders, ranchers, and smallcrofters.
