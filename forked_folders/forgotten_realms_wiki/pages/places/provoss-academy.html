The Provoss Academy was a wealth private school owned by the Provoss patriar family of Baldur's Gate's Upper City.

History

As of the , the Provoss Academy offered schooling to the wealthy, costing less well-off folk "two arms and a leg." One of the school's students during the Absolute crisis was Lily Chazman, who used the services of the Sword Coast Couriers to exchange letters with her family residing in Rivington.
