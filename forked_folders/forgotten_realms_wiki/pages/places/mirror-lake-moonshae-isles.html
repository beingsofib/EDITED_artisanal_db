Mirror Lake (Moonshae Isles)

Mirror Lake was a small body of freshwater located within the elven kingdom of Synnoria on the isle of Gwynneth on the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The lake was situated directly northwest of the small island upon which the elven city of Chrysalis sat. It was fed by several small unnamed rivers descending from the surrounding Cambro Mountains and the Highlands.

Description

Mirror Lake was relatively small, covering only of area.
