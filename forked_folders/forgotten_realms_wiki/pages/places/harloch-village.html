Harloch was a small village located on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The village was located near the center of the island on the eastern shore of Harloch for which it was named. Snowdown's main road, Snowdown Ride, passed through Harloch.

Travelers to Brannoch followed a muddy road east from Harloch through the Maloren Sods.

History

During the height of the Kingdom of Snowdown, Harloch was one of the larger and more influential cantrevs in regards to influencing the monarch's policies. Following the occupation of Snowdown by Amn in the 15 century DR, the village's mayor's office maintained records of goods and raw materials that passed through the village along the Snowdown Ride.
