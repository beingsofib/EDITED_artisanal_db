The Leaping Hart Tavern was the most popular tavern in the city of Harglast, in the Windrise Ports of Laerakond. It also served as an inn and a gambling hall, and was the center of gossip in the city.

Services

The tavern served fine drinks and foods, and also allowed patrons to engage in gambling games. The most popular game was named Liar's Dice.

Inhabitants

The proprietor of the Leaping Hart was an affable woman named Thelia Ondertos.
