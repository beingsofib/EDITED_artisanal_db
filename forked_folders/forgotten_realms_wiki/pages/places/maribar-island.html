Maribar Island was located off of the eastern coast of the Pearl Cities region of Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Description

Maribar Island covered roughly of land. The town of Samak was the only settlement of any considerable size found on the island, though numerous pirate hideouts were probably scattered across the island's coast as well.

Geography

The island was located in the Golden Gulf approximately northeast of the city of Sikak on the mainland. The Bountiful Shoals separated Maribar Island from the coast of West Zakhara.
