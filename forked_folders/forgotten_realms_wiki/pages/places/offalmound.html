Offalmound

Offalmound, sometimes named interchangeably with the name of its Abyssal layer–Rarandreth, was the 223 layer of the Abyss,

History

Many scholars argued that after the perceived death of its ancient master Moander in the , the layer ceased to exist. Another school of thought on the matter was that along with the deity's body, their realm was ejected beyond the Astral Plane, where it floated adrift. These theories seem to have been false or Moander's demise was not as complete as many thought. As of the late-14 century DR, Offalmound remained a layer of the Abyss, with its lordship contested.
