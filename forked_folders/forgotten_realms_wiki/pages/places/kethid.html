Kethid was the nation of Lizardfolk from the great swamp. They were ruled by King Ghassis around the year 1372 DR. He was in turn served by a state-sponsored company of mercenaries known as the Servants of the Royal Egg. Their ranks were available for hire all across Faerûn.
