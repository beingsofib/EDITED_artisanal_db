The Shaareach was a small plain found in the western Shaar near the border of Lapaliiya.

Geography

The open plains of the Shaareach were surrounded by the Misty Vale to the west, the Shaareach Forest to the south, the Wormbones to the north, and the Rathgaunt Hills to the east. The River Talar flowed through the southern region of the plains and the River Shaar flowed in from the north.
