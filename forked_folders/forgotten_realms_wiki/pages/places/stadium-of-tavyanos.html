The Stadium of Tavynos was a stadium in the underwater city of Myth Nantar.

Location

This stadium stood along Chamal Avenue, in Myth Nantar's Trade Quarter. It was one of the few structures in the quarter that was untouched by the overgrowth of the Three Gates Reef by 1371 DR.

Structure

This stadium stood three stories tall and had a sublevel, containing a number of cells and staging areas.

History

During the Twelfth Serôs War, the forces of Iakhovas forced the merfolk of Eadraal to abandon their city of Voalidru and a number of other towns,

In 1371 DR, a High Merpriest of Eadro named Narvolas joined the refugees, using his expertise to tend to the injured. Narvolas was approached by Prince-Regent Mirol III of Eadraal, having been told by a mysterious fish that the priest knew of a way to reclaim Voalidru. Narvolas then informed Mirol III about his experiences during the siege on Voalidru and of an undead repelling relic known as the Akriloth before sending him away.
