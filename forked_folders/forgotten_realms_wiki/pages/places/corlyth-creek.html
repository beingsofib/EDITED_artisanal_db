Corlyth Creek

Corlyth Creek was a small river located on the isle of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The creek originated in the Highlands mountains, flowing southeast to empty into Corwell Firth. Corlyth Creek flowed past the north side of Caer Corwell through an unnamed swamp.
