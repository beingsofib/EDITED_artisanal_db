Eaglton was a really small village between Ravens Bluff and Stag Falls in the Vast during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Description

The village was minimal, composed only of the Eagle's Eyrie inn (and its attached stables) alongside a smithy, wainwright, and apiary.

Geography

The village was about from Stag Falls to its west-north-west, and about that far from Ravens Bluff to its north.

Flora & Fauna

The apiary industry in the village called for raising bees.

Government

The innkeeper, Beomult Ragner, was also a local judge.

Trade

Notably, there was an apiary at the village; most of the honey was used locally to make cheap mead.
