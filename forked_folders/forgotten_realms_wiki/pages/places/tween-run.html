The Tween Run was a group of alleys and buildings that were located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

The Tween Run was what locals of Waterdeep's Southern Ward collectively called the small alleys and buildings that ran between the High Road and the Way of the Dragon. The area of the Tween Run south of Telshambra's Street lay within the Southern Ward, while the area north lay in the Trades Ward.
