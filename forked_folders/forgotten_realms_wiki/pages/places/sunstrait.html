Sunstrait

The Sunstrait was the "mouth" that linked the Golden Water with the Great Sea.

Geography

The Sunstrait was located in the Shining Lands of Southeast Faerûn. On the strait's western shores belonged to Var the Golden and the port-city of Myrmyr was built on its waters.
