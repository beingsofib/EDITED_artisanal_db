Ansildabar's Last Rest was a crypt in Hastarl, Athalantar, during the 3 century DR.

Location

The tomb was in a walled-off burial ground across the city from the Wanton Wench, a tavern.

Structure

The crypt had a sloping roof, which was shaded by two trees. The burial ground was largely in disrepair, with trees growing through some of the buildings.

Interior

There was a coffin inside the crypt.

Defenses

Other than being walled off, the burial ground was left completely unguarded.

History

At some point, the crypt had held the mortal remains of Ansildabar, a famous explorer.

Ansildabar's Last Rest was already extant by the , when Prince Elminster Aumar took the alias of Eladar the Dark to become a thief of Hastarl. The prince, and his fellow thief Farl, a local criminal, used the tomb to hide away from the local militia after they greatly offended Thelorn Selemban and Jansibal Otharr, two local noble heirs.

At a later date, Elminster used the tomb for the remains of Tassabra, another fellow thief, evicting Ansildabar's remains to do so.
