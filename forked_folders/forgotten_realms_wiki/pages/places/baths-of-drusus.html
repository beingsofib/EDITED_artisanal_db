The Baths of Drusus were a ruined bathhouse in Medinat Muskawoon.

Structure

A greenish stone building by , it was now open save for the walls and vaulted ceiling on the northeastern end.

Inhabitants

Two wraiths remained in the building; there were four golden links hidden in the drains.
