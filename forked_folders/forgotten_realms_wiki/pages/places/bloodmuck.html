Bloodmuck

The Bloodmuck was a lake in the land of Thar in the Moonsea North region of north Faerûn.

Geography

The Bloodmuck served as the source of a short winding Tormel River that flowed from the lake towards the Moonsea to the south, where it met the sea at the city of Melvaunt.
