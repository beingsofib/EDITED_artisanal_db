The Tower of the Lord Protector was a two-story keep in the center of Triboar in the mid-to-late 14 and late 15 centuries DR. It was the residence of the ruler of the town, titled the Lord Protector.

Structure

The keep was made of stone, was of simple design, and leaned noticeably eastward. It got quite dusty.
