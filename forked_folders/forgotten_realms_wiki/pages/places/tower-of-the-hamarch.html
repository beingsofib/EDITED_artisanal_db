The Tower of the Hamarch was located in the city of Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR.

Location

The Tower sat near the base of a rocky outcropping found in the northeast section of the city.

Activities

The Tower of the Hamarch was the home of Urmlaspyr's elected mayor–the Hamarch. This was Aluin Sumbrar in 1358 DR.
