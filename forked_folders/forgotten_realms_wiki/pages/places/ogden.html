Ogden was a small town located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Ogden was located along the High King's Road near the center of the Dernall Forest's eastern edge. Ogden was just due south of Caer Callidyrr in the county of Talffolk.
