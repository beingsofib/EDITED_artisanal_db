Unglur was the middle of the three islands located west of Thelve and the the fortress library of Candlekeep.

Description

Unlike its sister isles, Unglur had no surface ruins, and there were only a few sea caves that could be used for shelter or harborage. The island also lacked any freshwater springs. Vegetation was scarce and only consisted of thin shrubbery. Unglur had no harbors and just one anchorage at the center of the western shore, where the curved shore met with a snout. It was the only spot for boats to reach the beach despite strong blistering winds that pelted the shore. The winds made it almost impossible to un-beach any boats without the use of magic.

The island's southwestern shore was long, and the short snout on the southeast had a long shore. Continuing around the snout, its eastern part was long until curving to the northeast for and continuing curving to the northwest for more.

Geography

Unglur was located to the west of Thelve, with two sister islands to the northwest – Sklorn to the east and Raerest to the west. Together Sklorn, Unglur, and Raerest were known as the Crab Reach, or simply the Reach among locals.

Unglur was separated from the isle of Sklorn by a stretch of water and from Unglur's eastern neighbor – Raerest.

Flora & Fauna

The island had a negligent population of seals, few monsters, and even fewer castaway inhabitants. The only creatures of note here were abundant gray coasters and rock gulls that left their white marks on the island's rocks.

History

By the late 14 century DR, Unglur was used by pirates as a harbor, and its shores were littered with derelict ships and wrecks. For many years there existed stories of an undead creature haunting the land. the Spirit of Unglur was a long-dead bloodthirsty berserker pirate turned a strange monstrous creature with long claws.
