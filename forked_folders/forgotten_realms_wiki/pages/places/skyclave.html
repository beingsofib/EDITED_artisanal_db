Skyclave was a city contained predominantly within a huge, hollow tower approximately a mile (1.6 kilometers) in height. It was the capital of High Imaskar in the 15 century DR.

Description

Exterior

Protruding from the vast and imposing tower were hundreds of windows, spires, balconies, verandas, staircases, and doorways. The lowest balconies were over above the ground.

The base of the tower was surrounded by farms, fisheries, and other industries that provided for the city.

Interior

The inside of the hollow tower was magically bigger than the outside. It was filled with galleries that seemed to go on endlessly and which contained shops, inns, theaters, libraries, and other interesting sites. Some levels of the tower were abandoned and inhabited only by magical lights and plant-like growths.

Geography

Scyclave was located on an inlet off the Sea of Fallen Stars in the broken land of Mulhorand, known as High Imaskar for much of the 15 century DR.

Government

Skyclave was the seat of High Imaskar's ruler, Empress Ususi Manaallin, as well as its Body of Artificers, Planners, and Apprehenders.

The city's nonmagical and nonmilitary day-to-day needs and provisioning were overseen by a "mayor" known as Lord Planner Yanay.

Scyclave also hosted a political faction known as the Collectors, who were responsible for the acquisition of knowledge and resources from outside the city.

Trade

As of 1479 DR, Scyclave had few visitors and little trade with the outside world aside from the occasional ship arriving from the Sea of Fallen Stars to exchange goods.

Defenses

A military force called the Vengeance Riders stabled their redwings in magnificent roosts on balconies near the top of the tower. A patrol of at least four mounted redwings would constantly circle the tower.

History

The tower was erected sometime following the devastation of the Spellplague of the , when the descendants of the ancient Imaskari emerged to reclaim the shattered land of Mulhorand for their own.

As of the , a cell of devil worshipers led by a self-proclaimed "child of Mephistopheles" known as the Son of the Eighth was active somewhere within Skyclave despite efforts by the Empress to have them eliminated. However, all of her agents had either failed to find them or had failed to return alive.

Notable Locations

  • Academy of Imaskar, a school of wizardry.
  • Ayar's Table, a tavern that served mushroom ale and other fare common in Deep Imaskar.
  • The Edge, a tavern that occupied several balconies on the outside of Skyclave. The establishment was famed for its views.
  • Palace of the Purple Emperor, a partly-restored magically mobile palace from which Empress Ususi ruled which had been relocated into Scyclave.

Inhabitants

In 1479 DR, Skyclave was inhabited by both nobles and commonfolk of High Imaskar. At this time, the city was home to more than half of the High Imaskar's total population, of whom half lived within the tower and the other half around its base. The inhabitants were predominantly humans descended from the Deep Imaskari.
