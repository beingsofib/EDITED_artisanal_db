Stone Trail

The Stone Trail was a rough dirt road that ran east—west through a stretch of the Dessarin Valley. The dwarven landmark of Stone Bridge allowed for passageway along the road over the River Dessarin.

Geography

The road connected the settlement of Westbridge, fittingly enough on the west end of the road, to Beliard, on the eastern side.
