Skinwalking

Skinwalking was a unique magical form of non-lycanthropic shapechanging that relied on hides and other animal parts to assume an animal form. The only known practitioners of this art were the green elven priests known as skinwalkers. It was taught by elder priests to those who had lived in solitary priestly existence long enough, far removed from other green elves. When skinwalking, a priest would take on the form of their totem animal, and the duration of the transformation depended on the skill and experience of the practitioner. If struck down while skinwalking, the priest could sometimes avoid death, instead falling into unconsciousness for several days. While skinwalking, the priest retained their intelligence.
