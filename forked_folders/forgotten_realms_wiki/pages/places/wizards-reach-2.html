The Wizard's Reach was a small island found in the Great Sea in northwest Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

The island was located off the coast of mainland Zakhara, approximately due north of Qadib and due east of the Island of Gardens in the Corsair Domains. The island itself measured roughly in length and wide.

Description

The southern half of the island was dominated by sandy dunes, while the north half was lightly forested. A wizard's tower was found toward the center of the north half of the island at the end of a long and narrow natural bay.

Activities

The official ownership of the island was disputed between the corsairs of the Corsair Domains and the Free Cities of Zakhara.
