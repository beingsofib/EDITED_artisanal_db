Ambrara was the name of an inhabited settlement somewhere in Faerûn during the early-to-mid 8 century DR.

History

During the , the sage Beldrune told his fellow Tabarast that he had once been an actor in the original play version of The Stormy Knight, as the Second Undergardener, in Ambrara.
