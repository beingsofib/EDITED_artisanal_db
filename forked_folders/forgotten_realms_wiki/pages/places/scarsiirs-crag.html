Scarsiir's Crag

Scarsiir's Crag was a mountain in the Cloud Peaks of Amn, once home to the old silver dragon Eacoathildarandus.

Fauna

After the killing of Eacoathildarandus, the Crag was taken over by a variety of monsters. Perytons and wyverns vied for the high ledges to use as perches, while giant slugs and worse things overtook the dark interior of the mountain.

Geography

The Crag sat in the northern Cloud Peaks, overlooking the Neck near Citadel Amnur.

History

The mountain was controlled by the ancient silver dragon Eacoathildarandus, who laired atop it. He was slain in the by the great red dragon Balagos in order to remove any potential competition from other old dragons in the region. Having fulfilled his mission, Balagos did not use the lair and simply left it abandoned.
