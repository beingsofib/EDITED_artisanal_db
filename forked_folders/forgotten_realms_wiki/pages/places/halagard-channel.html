Halagard Channel

The Halagard Channel was a waterway located in Halruaa.

Geography

The Halagard Channel connected Lake Halruaa with the Great Sea. The channel was 3 miles (4.8 km) wide for most of its length.

Cities and towns lining the Halagard Channel included:

  • Halagard
  • Mithel
  • Kithtym
