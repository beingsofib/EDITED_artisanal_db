The Royal Harim of Huzuz, or simply the Royal Harim, was part of the complex of the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz in 1367 DR.

Description

The Royal Harim was one of the largest sections of the palace. It contained dormitories, beautiful gardens, and opulent suites.

Inhabitants

The Royal Harim was the home to the Grand Caliph's many wives and concubines as well as their slaves, servants, and guards

Rumors and Legends

A secret tunnel, known only to the inhabitants of the harim, was used to escort men into the harim. The men were usually dressed as women.
