The Jumpa River Gorge was one of the largest canyons in all of Toril.

Geography

Formed from the churning waters of the mighty Jumpa River, the gorge stretched along Katakoro Shan passed the countries of Khazari and Ra-Khati before entering the lands of East Faerûn.
