The Lonely Vale, also known as the Forsaken Dale, was the valley in the Savage Frontier that formed between the Greypeak Mountains and Greycloak Hills.

Description

Befitting its name, the valley did not appear on nearly, if any maps at all.

Geography

The Forgotten Forest in the adjacent Backlands were situated just south of the valley.
