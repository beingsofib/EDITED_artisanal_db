The Hall of Spurs was a room in Castle Obarskyr, Suzail, in the late 15 century DR.

History

After his theft of the Royal Gorget of Battle, the fugitive mage Elminster Aumar was pursued by a team, led by the War Wizard Kelgantor. Prior to deployment, they were assembled at the Hall of Spurs by order of Vainrence.
