Darkul Tower

Building
nameDarkul Tower
typeMage's Tower
locationBezantur, Thay
ownerNusair Darkul

Darkul Tower was the residence of Nusair Darkul in Bezantur. a room devoted to a crystal ball, and a library. The latter was perhaps the largest room in the tower, with a fireplace, a rug, an oaken table, and red tapestries covering every wall that had no bookshelves.
