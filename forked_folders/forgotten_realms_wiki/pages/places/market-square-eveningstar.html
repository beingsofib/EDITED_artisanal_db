The Market Square of Eveningstar was the center of commerce in the small village during the late 14 century DR.

Location

The Market Square was located directly in the center of Eveningstar along the High Road.

Activity

Ladian Ruldo's stables were found here, as was a fine clothing shop and Ebbard Highsong's butcher shop.
