Al-Harir River

Al-Harir River, also called the River of Gold, was a beautiful waterway located on the Island of Gardens within the Corsair Domains off the coast of northwest Zakhara.

Description

The river originated atop a high cliff on the island, splitting to the south into two majestic waterfalls. The eastern portion of the river flowed smoothly through jungles as it traveled to the southern regions of the island.

Inhabitants

The River of Gold was inhabited by a zin named Zthyz al-Zin who would confront anyone trying to take water.
