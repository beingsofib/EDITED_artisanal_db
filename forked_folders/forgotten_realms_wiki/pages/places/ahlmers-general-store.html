Ahlmer's General Store was a shop in the city of Procampur in the Vast in the late 14 century DR. It was owned and run by Ahlmer.

Location

It stood in the Port District.

Services

The prices were low and among its goods were fresh-baked biscuits for which the store was famed.

In Alturiak in the , a strange well-dressed gentleman found wandering the streets of Procampur identified himself as "Lord Sellwin Wagglebottom, Deputy Assistant Commander in charge of Perishable Supply Requisition for the Armies of Ravens Bluff" and asked for directions to Ahlmer's General Store.
