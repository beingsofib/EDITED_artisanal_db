Palace Lane

The Palace Lane was a street located in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.

Description

This was a small street that branched off of the Street of the Inns; leading travelers northeast toward the Palace of the Enlightened Throne, the Court of Enlightenment, and the beautiful Public Gardens of Huzuz.
