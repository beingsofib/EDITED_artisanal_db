The Oakengrove Abbey was a human temple stronghold dedicated to the worship of the deity Silvanus.

History

It was founded in 591 DR. It stood about west of the Standing Stone in Cormanthor.

Inhabitants

The abbey was lead by Oakfather Gannon Durei in the . He supervised eight other priests.
