The Guns of Azzagrat

The Guns of Azzagrat is an adventure in the Living City organized play campaign for 2 edition published in 1999. Written by Erik Mona, it is the finale of the Ebon Tendrils series and features four rounds in total; two per difficulty level.

Index

This adventure contains four rounds in three separate documents, which are indexed separately on these subpages:

  • The Brigadier (Low Tier Round 1)
  • A Celestial Crown (Low Tier Round 2)
  • The Quarry of Ebulon (High Tier Rounds 1 & 2)
