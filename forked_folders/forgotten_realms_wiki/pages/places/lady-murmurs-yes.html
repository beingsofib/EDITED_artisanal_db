The Lady Murmurs Yes was a club where people of means could hire companionship for a night, located in Suzail, the capital of Cormyr in the 15 century DR.

Description

The Lady Murmurs Yes stood two blocks east of the Horngate, along Suzail's grand Promenade. The brothel was known for its ladies "not sporting a face like an old boot" whose companionship was priced at ten lions and twenty more for a night-long affair and a choice of activities that did not include flogging.
