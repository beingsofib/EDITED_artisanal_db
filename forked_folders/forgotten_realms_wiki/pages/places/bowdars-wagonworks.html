Bowdar's Wagonworks

nameBowdar's Wagonworks

Bowdar's Wagonworks was a business in the city of Tantras in the Vast circa 1358 DR. It was located in the southwestern side of the city and was owned by Bowdar.

It was dedicated to the construction and repair of wagons.
