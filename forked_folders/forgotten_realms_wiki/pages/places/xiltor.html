Xiltor was a small city located in Veldorn in the Shining South during the 14 century DR.

Geography

Xiltor was located directly southeast of the Aerilpar Forest at the junction of the Liontongue River and the River Madras.

Inhabitants

The city was ruled by a pair of beholder twins named Xaoch and Veoyh. Several tribes of kobolds, a large group of ogres, and a black dragon named Kelpacitus also lived within the city.
