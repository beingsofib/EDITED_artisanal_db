The University of Suzail was an institution of learning in the city of Suzail, the capital of the kingdom of Cormyr, in the mid–14 century DR.

Staff

The academic staff engaged in research, teaching, and writing, and usually writing when they wearied of teaching. The professors were well-respected experts in their respective fields who wrote books of their own. The majority of masters at the university had a job for life.

Notable Academics

  • Professor Linnaeus Gozzling, author of The Internal Structure of the Brachiopod;
  • Tobald, later Mayor of Ghars, dismissed from his academic position for unknown reasons.

Notable Alumni

  • Grodoveth, later envoy of King Azoun IV
