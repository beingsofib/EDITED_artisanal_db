Northkeep was the first settlement built on the Moonsea by humans.

Features

The Bell in the Depths: This bell sat in one of the tallest of the shattered towers of the sunken city. At times, nearby ships could hear the ghostly bell ring. The sunken city was reputed to be haunted.

History

The city was founded in , on an island near the lake's southern shore, about midway between the present towns of Elventree and Elmwood. The city was a beacon of civilization and a jumping off point for merchants.

The power of Northkeep made it a target for orcs, giants, and other evil beings, who formed the Dark Alliance to battle and contain Northkeep.

Northkeep appeared safe on its island, but in the , a huge force mounted on dragonback, commanded by the black dragon Iyrauroth, swept over the city while ships landed an army that overran and sacked the city. To make their victory complete, a force of nonhuman mages and clerics (forty thousand strong, some said) gathered on the north shore of the Moonsea and brought the vengeance of Gruumsh down upon the human interlopers. The city sank beneath the purple waves.

Notable Inhabitants

It was said that the mysterious ghost called the Lady of the North inhabited the ruins. She wielded the artifact called the Astral Blade and was guarded by an army of ghostly defenders of Northkeep.
