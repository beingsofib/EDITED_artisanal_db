Frozenfar Expeditions was a shop in Caer-Konig that catered to adventurers, outdoorsmen, and wilderness explorers. It was run by the elderly Atenas Swift.

Location

The shop was located on the north side of town, a bit off its main road.

Services

Frozenfar Expeditions offered all manner of survival equipment and gear, including maps of surrounding areas.

In addition to these goods, Atenas offered transportation across Icewind Dale by means of dogsled, as well as the services of the experienced guide Jarthra Farzassh.

Atenas allowed adventurers and explorers to put up postings in his shop, looking to hire folks for their respective expeditions across the North.
