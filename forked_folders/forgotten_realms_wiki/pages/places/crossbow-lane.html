Crossbow Lane

Crossbow Lane was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Crossbow Lane was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward. The road branched west off of Snail Street, terminating on Watchmen's Way near the base of Castle Waterdeep.

Notable Locations

Several guard barracks sat on Crossbow Lane.
