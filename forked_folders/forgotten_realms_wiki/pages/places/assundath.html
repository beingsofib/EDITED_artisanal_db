Assundath was a small town located in the nation of Halruaa during the 14 century DR.

Geography

Assundath was a coastal town located in southeast Halruaa in the shadow of the Muaraghal Mountains. The town was located on the eastern side of the Bay of Azuth where it met the Great Sea.
