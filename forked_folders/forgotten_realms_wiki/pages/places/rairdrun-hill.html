Rairdrun Hill was the name of a hill near the Dragon Coast in the mid–8 century DR.

History

In the , the stretch between Rairdrun Hill and Oggle's Stream was known as the Dead Place, because of a prowling phantom known as the Slayer, which had killed almost everything larger than a mouse.
