The Weather Eye was a tavern of some repute in Suzail, the capital of Cormyr.

Location

The tavern stood two blocks north of the Dragonmere and Suzail's public docks – Bolliver's Wharf. It was located on the street that ran parallel the Dancing Druid Lane, to its south.
