Magecourt

The Magecourt was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

The Magecourt was located in the southwest section of Waterdeep's North Ward. The Magecourt branched southwest from Shando Street, branching further several more times forming unnamed alleys within a small neighborhood.
