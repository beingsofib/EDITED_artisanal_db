The Central Plains was an open plain located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The Central Plains covered much of the open land within the county of Talffolk in central Callidyrr. They were bordered in the west by the Dernall Forest, and in the east by the coast with the Sea of Swords and Torriage Wood. The Swanmay River formed the plains' southern edge while the cut of land formed by Whitefish Bay formed the northern edge. The Central Plains covered roughly of land in Callidyrr.
