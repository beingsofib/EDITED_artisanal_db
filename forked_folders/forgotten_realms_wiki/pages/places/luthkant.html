Luthkant was a city in Calimshan during the 3 century DR.

History

In the , Taraj Hurlymm, a Magelord of Athalantar, had a Luthkantan peddler seized and released near Dalniir to hunt him for sport. That man was a Calishite from far away, strongly implying the city of Luthkant was somewhere in Calimshan.
