Claw Peak was a peak known by dragons in the late 14 century DR.

History

Ammaratha Cyndusk kept four thousand-odd speaking gems in the spire of a ruined keep atop Claw Peak; in , when she needed a number of dragons to spare Vangerdahast after discovering he had been crafting a spell to bind them, she told them about her stash, and gave them leave to split it.
