Safe Harbor

Safe Harbor was the divine realm of the Faerûnian demigod Valkur the Mighty.

Cosmography

In the Great Wheel cosmology, Safe Harbor could be found in Ysgard, the first layer of the chaotic good plane of the same name.

In the World Tree cosmology, the realm was located on Tempus' plane of Warrior's Rest. There, it lay between the large sea area on which naval battles took place and the mainland desert where the petitioners of the other deities of war gloried in their constant struggles.

Description

Safe Harbor itself was a large stretch of calm sea.
