Coach Street

Coach Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Coach Street was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward. The road ran east to west, originating in the west in the center of a neighborhood, crossing the High Road, and ending on Wall Street at the Trollwall. Coach Street was also intersected by Carter's Way.
