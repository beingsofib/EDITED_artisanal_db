The Shrines of Nature was a temple complex in Waterdeep dedicated to the worship of the deities Mielikki and Silvanus, as well as a garden. The shrine dedicated to Mielikki was commonly known as the Lady's Hands.

Location

This temple complex was located in Waterdeep's Sea Ward, between Bliss Street, Ivory Street, and the Street of Whispers.

Structure

This temple complex garden was walled-in, surrounded by an iron fence, and was very forested. It had a number of beautiful fountains, statues, and shrines within.

Two buildings stood inside the complex. For much of their existence, both buildings stood only a single story tall, but by 1372 DR they had each gotten an additional floor.

History

These shrines to Mielikki and Silvanus were once separate, unaffiliated structures, but sometime around 1367 DR they merged to become a temple complex and took up the name "Shrines of Nature".

In 1368 DR, the Shrines of Nature was one of the many temples within Waterdeep to receive an endowment from the nobleman Ultas Maernos.

By the 15 century DR, the scope of the temple complex grew to honoring other nature deities in addition to Mielikki and Silvanus. By this time they also began to deny entry to those who weren't residents of the Sea Ward.

Inhabitants

Prior to becoming a temple complex, the high priests Anarakin Iriboar and Briosar Helmsing ran their respective shrines to Silvanus and Mielikki. In addition, the shrine to Mielikki had a ranger-priest by the name of Tehtira Bellsilver.
