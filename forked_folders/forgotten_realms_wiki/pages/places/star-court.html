The Star Court was a government building in Silverymoon used for holding courts and assemblies in the mid–14 century DR.

To be accepted as a citizen, every person intending to reside in Silverymoon (whether as a resident or just for study) must attend the Star Court to register their name, occupation, and names of family members.

Here also were applications processed for purchasing real estate, conducting trade negotiations with other cities, and to create magic fields inside the city.
