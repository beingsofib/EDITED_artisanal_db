Pengram was a small town located on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Pengram was located in southwest Snowdown at the end of Snowdown Ride. The town was situated in the middle of an unnamed open plain at the foothills of Andover Heights.

History

During the height of the Kingdom of Snowdown, Pengram was one of the larger and more influential cantrevs in regards to influencing the monarch's policies.
