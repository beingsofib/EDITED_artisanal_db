Zashuma was a small town in Lapaliiya in southwest Faerûn in the mid–14th century DR.

Geography

It sat beside the Lapal Way, in the shadow the city of Ithmong in the north.

Government & Religion

Around 1373 DR, it was led by Clanlaird Burinn Rivenstone.

The civic deity of Zashuma was Garl Glittergold.

Economy

It was a farming town focused on growing olives and producing wine.

Population

Unusually, Zashuma was mostly populated by rock gnomes, unlike the predominantly human communities of Lapaliiya.
