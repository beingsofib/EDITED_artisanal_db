Rodanar was a town located in the country of Chessenta during the mid–14 century DR.

Geography

The town lay on the northwest shore of the Akanamere in western Chessenta, just south of the Akanapeaks.

Inhabitants

Some 5,000 people lived in Rodanar in the , and 1,000 of these belonged to the garrison. Rodanar was led by a warrior named Hyurkes.

History

The war god Tchazzar founded Rodanar to serve as a fortress to thwart orc incursions from the Akanapeaks. Rodanar used to be a large city, but a war with Akanax during the 12 century DR left it in ruins. The new Rodanar that sprung up since was much smaller.
