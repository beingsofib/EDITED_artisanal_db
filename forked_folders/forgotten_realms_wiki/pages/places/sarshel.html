Sarshel was a walled port city of Impiltur. As of the late 15 century DR, it was better known as New Sarshel.

Notable Locations

  • Marhymbel House Shipways, one of the fastest ship-builders in the Sea of Fallen Stars, led by brothers Ilund and Vromur Marhymbel in the 1490s DR.
