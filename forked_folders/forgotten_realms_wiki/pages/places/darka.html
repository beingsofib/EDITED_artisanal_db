Darka was a city in Tabot.

Geography

It stood amongst the forest in southern Tabot, at the northern edge of the Wu Pi Te Shao Mountains. Roads from here led northeast to Bidnop So and southeast to Thok. It was roughly a day's ride from Thok.

History

In , in response to a Phutanese army assembling at the border, Tabot went on a war footing, with the Mag-chi Le-Khung mustering soldiers in the area. They ordered conscription in Bidnop So, Darka, Thok, and Khampo.
