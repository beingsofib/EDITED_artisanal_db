High King's Road

The High King's Road was the main thoroughfare in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Description

The road was paved with the same signature white granite of which the Palace of the High King in Caer Callidyrr was constructed.

Geography

The High King's Road stretched from one end of Callidyrr to the other. The road was branched in the north within the Fairheight Range, beginning in the mining settlements of Blackstone and White Rock and traveling nearly to the south through Caer Callidyrr to terminate at Kythyss on the southern coast. The road crossed the Swanmay River near its midpoint within the Dernall Forest at the town of Horsa.
