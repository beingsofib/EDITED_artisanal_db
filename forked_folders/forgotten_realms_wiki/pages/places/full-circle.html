Full Circle

Full Circle is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the Living City organized play program and the final installment of the Elementally Speaking series of modules. It was published in 2001 and is intended for a party of adventurers of level 5 and up. A special issue of the Trumpeter is included with this module.
