The Palace of the Watchmen was a barracks for the city watch located in the city of Dhaztanar in Semphar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

The Palace of the Watchmen was located within the Quarters of the Unclean district in east Dhaztanar. It sat near the far eastern edge of the city along the Silk Road and Tarkhold River.

Description

The compound was quite large and surrounded by a defensive wall.
