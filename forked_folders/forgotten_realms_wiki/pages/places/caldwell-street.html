Caldwell Street

Caldwell Street was a dirt and gravel paved minor avenue in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep.

Locations

Caldwell stretched from north to south, with the northern end terminating on Gulzindar Street and the southern end terminating on Julthoon Street.
