Maunator, also known as Sunrest, was a flying Netherese enclave that existed during the Golden Age of Netheril.

History

It was disintegrated in in an accident caused by poorly planned spell research. The spell that led to the enclave's destruction was Sunrest sunshock, a significantly more powerful version of the spell meteor swarm.

After its destruction, the abandoned mines of Dekanter became the premiere site of Netherese arcane research.
