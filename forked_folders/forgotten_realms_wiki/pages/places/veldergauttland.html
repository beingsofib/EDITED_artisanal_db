Veldergauttland was a tiny nation of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. It participated in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Description

Veldergauttland had a variety of colorful political figures that vied for control of the nation and the Whamite Isles. Known figures were King Faelrud, Queen Nata, Prince Firshner and Edgar the Pretender. They were served by the Brothers of the Sword.

Veldergauttland shared land borders with the Cold Desert, Bolgor, and Penbroc. Each of these borders was marked by a mountain range.

Among the nations of the Whamites, Veldergauttland was significant for its powerful navy. Known fleets were those belonging to the Phaelbart Nobles, Oblart Nobles, the tribes of Quintax and Flebert, as well as the simply-named Great Fleet.
