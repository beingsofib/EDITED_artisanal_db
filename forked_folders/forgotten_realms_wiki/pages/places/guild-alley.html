Guild Alley

Guild Alley was a small road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

Guild Alley was located in southwest Daggerford. It originated from the southern part of Rogar Alley and cut south until ending at Daggerford's drill field.

Notable Locations

Notable buildings located along Guild Alley included:

  • Oleg's Weaponforge
  • Headquarters for the Taverner's Association
