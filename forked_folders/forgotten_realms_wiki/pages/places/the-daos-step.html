The Dao's Step was a guildhall in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR and the headquarters of the Cobblers' Society.

A mudbrick rowhouse, it was located on the western edge of Wahir Drudach, Axash Sabban, in the city's Emerald Ward.

It was named after a tale of the legendary First Trader of Calimshan, when he created the first slippers for a dao.
