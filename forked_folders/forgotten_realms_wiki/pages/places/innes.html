Innes

Innes was a small lake in the Highlands of the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles.

Description

The waters of the Innes were green. An old wall and gatehouse—built during the height of the kingdom of Corwell and later used as a guardhouse by the fey of Sarifal—sat near the shore between the Innes and its twin, the Ulls.

Geography

The lake lay very near to its twin, the Ulls, and only from the cliffs of Ulls Peak. Reaching Crane Point on the Ulls required passing between the two lakes.
