Eye of the Sky was a small asteroid in the Tears of Selûne. It was a beholder base, inhabited by hundreds of those creatures.

Geography

The asteroid itself was two miles (3.2 km) wide. The interior of the asteroid was a labyrinth of caves and tunnels, which were used by its inhabitants as living and sleeping quarters.

Defenses

By the mid-14 century DR, no successful attack had been led against Eye of the Sky.

Inhabitants

Several hundreds of beholders inhabited the asteroid.
