Castle Dasaajk was a castle situated on the eastern edge of the Purple Hills in Tethyr.

History

In the 3rd and 4th centuries DR, the location was the site of a fortress and the base of the Holy Warriors of Suffering, a paladin order devoted to Ilmater.

By 1374 DR, the site was occupied by Castle Dasaajk.

In the Year of the Hunted Elk, 841 DR, King Samyte of Tethyr was assassinated in Castle Dasaajk by Uthaedeol the Blooddrenched, a priest of Bhaal, along with his sons.
