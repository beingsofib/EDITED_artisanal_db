Goldenrod Street

Goldenrod Street was a road in the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the 14 century DR.

Location

It lay in the northern High Town area of Loudwater.

Description

The site of a number of elven homes, it was a place of simple yet pleasant houses, with flower boxes and weeping willows.

Notable Locations

  • The Grotto of Serene Blossoms, which lay just to the south.
  • The shop of Verith Oakbow, a local herbalist, lay at the end of the street.

Notable Inhabitants

  • Aduniron Phelaniityr, a musician of the noble sun elf Phelaniityr family.
  • Jaylor and Melindra, an elven couple.
