Weeping Maiden's Run

Weeping Maiden's Run was a small street located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Location

Weeping Maiden's Run was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter. The street cut through the Adventurers' Quarter, entering from the northwest where it branched southeast from Slop Street, before ending on Grocer's Lane at Kolat Towers.
