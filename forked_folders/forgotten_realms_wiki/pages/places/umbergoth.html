Umbergoth

Location/Mountain
nameUmbergoth
typeMountain
regionTannath Mountains, Unapproachable East

Umbergoth was the tallest mountain in Aglarond. As of , it had never been scaled.

Geography

It lay north of the Watchwall and south of Shyvar Pass. The mountain was so tall that its peak was always covered in snow.

Flora & Fauna

There had been sightings of griffons and hippogriffs around the peak. By , Nartheling had scared off most of them, alongside the giant eagles, out of the mountain peak. There were asperii on the mountain, in fact.

History

In , Nartheling, an ancient fang dragon, moved into the mountain.

As of , the Cult of the Dragon regularly scaled the mountain to seduce Nartheling with the tranformation into a dracolich. The copper dragon Filauxerimos preferred to instead avoid the location. Nartheling himself was known to raid nearby caravans.

In , Nartherling, while succumbing to the rage, got into a number of skirmishes with the beholders, which spilled into the surrounding area.

Rumors & Legends

Asperii were a rumor in .

Notable Locations

The dragon Nartheling had made his lair in a labyrinthine complex of caves inside the peak, surrounded by the nests of griffins and asperii. As of , he had discovered a network of hidden portals in the tunnels.

Inhabitants

Galeb duhr were known to inhabit the mountain. The mountain's fauna included wyverns, and in fact, beholders lived in hidden tunnels.

Nartheling inhabited the mountain from to at least .
