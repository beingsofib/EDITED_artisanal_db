Thesk Mountains

The Thesk Mountains were bare, inhospitable mountains in Thesk that reached as high as 7,000 feet (2,100 meters). They featured extremely difficult terrain, and offered a limited amount of iron ore which was enough to supply the nation and provide extra for export.
