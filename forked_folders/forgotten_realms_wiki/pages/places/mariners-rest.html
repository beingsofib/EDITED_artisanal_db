Mariners' Rest was a tomb located within the City of the Dead in Waterdeep.

Location

Mariners' Rest was located in the far southeast corner of the City of the Dead near a guard watch tower.

History

As its name implied, the tomb was reserved sailors and seamen who prepaid to be interred within.
