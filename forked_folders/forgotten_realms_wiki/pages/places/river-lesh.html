River Lesh

The River Lesh was located in Durpar in the Shining Lands in 1367 DR.

Geography

The River Lesh originated in the Dustwall Mountains of northeast Durpar. It flowed south through the Ajmer Forest and a stretch of flat farmland before emptying into the Golden Water. The River Lesh was used heavily to float logs harvested from the Ajmer down to Morvar.
