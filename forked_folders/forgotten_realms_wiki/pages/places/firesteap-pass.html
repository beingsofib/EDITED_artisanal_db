Firesteap Pass

Firesteap Pass was the aptly-named part of the Golden Road that crossed the Firesteap Mountains on the border of the Vilhon Reach and the Land of the Lions.

Location

Firesteap Pass was located in the western end of the Firesteap Mountains, south of the city of Innarlith, beginning just past the Firesteap Citadel. There existed at least one cavern with its entrance located in the Firesteap Pass. A magic gate within the cave was connected to a ruin on one of the islands of Marsember in Cormyr. The gate could be activated via singing, playing, or humming a certain tune.
