Tchozal's Race

Tchozal's Race was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Tchozal's Race was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward. The road originated in the north on the border with the Waterdeep/Sea Ward, branching south and west from Julthoon Street before curving west of the Market to end on Swords Street. Other roads crossing Tchozal's Race included Trader's Way and Calamastyr Lane.
