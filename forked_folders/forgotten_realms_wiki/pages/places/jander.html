Jander was a hamlet in Athalantar in the 3 century DR.

Description

Jander was a small, forlorn hamlet.

Geography

It sat near the northern edge of Athalantar, north-by-northeast of the site of the future Secomber.

Landmarks

The Woodsedge Inn was due north of Jander.

History

Jander was flourishing in some ways by .

In the , locally named the Year of Frosts, King Uthgrael Aumar perished near Jander in battle against orcs.

By , the mage Nasarn had a country house near Jander.

It was long-gone by the 14 century DR.
