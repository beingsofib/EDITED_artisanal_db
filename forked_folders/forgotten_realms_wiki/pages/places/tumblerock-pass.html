Tumblestone Pass was a mountain pass in the Storm Horns, which served as a battleground in the 15 century DR.

Geography

The pass was located somewhere in the northwestern Storm Horns, and served as a passageway across the mountain range between Netheril and Cormyr.

History

In the 1480s DR, at the time of the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon, the Archwizard Amskar conducted his own shadowy conspiracy in the Storm Horns. He found a lost ancient mythallar in a mountaintop glacier and used it to create a flying enclave, and assembled an army to fill it. In order to equip his local auxiliaries to a high standard he had General Grablandurr command Tumblerock Pass as convoys passed through it from Netheril.

The convoys were noticed by the Harper scout Kyra Armond, who couldn't figure out who they were working for or what they were transporting. It was only when Rina the Keen-Blade examined the armaments found on orcs killed at Eveningstar that the Harpers realized that Tumblerock Pass was being used to supply an entire army being assembled in secret. A team was then sent to the pass and made contact with Kyra, then ambushed the porters and their guards (possibly with the assistance of local harpies. The Harpers utterly ruined the shipments and enraged Grablandurr until he arrived in person to deal with the threat, only to fall in battle himself.
