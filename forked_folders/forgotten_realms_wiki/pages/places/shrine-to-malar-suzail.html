The Shrine to Malar, was a small temple dedicated to the worship of Malar, the Beastlord, located in the city of Suzail, the capital of Cormyr, the Forest Kingdom.

Location

The Malarite shrine was located on the Royal Ride, across from the Thistleflame Manor, two doors east from the bridge crossing the Jaws of Lake Azoun, south-east of Wyvernspur House. The Shrine to Tyr and the Shrine of Tempus stood on the same side of the Royal Ride, two doors east of the Shrine to Malar.
