River Heldar

The River Heldar was a small river found in the middle of Durpar in the Shining Lands of the Shining South.

Geography

The river originated within the Dustwall Mountains, flowing south on a brief course before meeting the Golden Water.

Description

Durpar's capital, Heldapan, was found along its eastern bank.
