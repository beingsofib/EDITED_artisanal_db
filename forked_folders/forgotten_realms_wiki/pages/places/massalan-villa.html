Massalan Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Massalan.

Location

The villa was prominently located on the northeast corner of the High Road and Delzorin Street in the North Ward.

Structure

It consisted of a few unique one- and two-story buildings in a walled compound, with the main manor facing the Lanngolyn Villa across Delzorin Street.
