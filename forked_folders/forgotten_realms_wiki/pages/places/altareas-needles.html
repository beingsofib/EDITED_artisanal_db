Altarea's Needles

nameAltarea's Needles

Altarea's Needles was a seamstressing and waterproofing house in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep in the mid–14 century DR.

Activities

Tessaril Winter expected someone, almost certainly Harpers, to be operating there.

Rumors & Legends

In the , Narm Tamaraith and Shandril Shessair were instructed by Tessaril Winter to ask for "the little lady that does the pearls" at the shop when they arrived in Waterdeep.
