The Sword and Sextant was a map shop located in the subterranean city of Skullport deep beneath the grand metropolis of Waterdeep. It could be found within the south-eastern region of the city's lowest level, known as the Dredge.

History

During the late 14 century DR, the Sword and Sextant was overseen by its owner, the Tethyrian map-maker Aekyl Dafyre.

By the , it was being run by a pair of halfling brothers, Oleander and Will. They sought to procure any accurate maps that portrayed a precise layout of Undermountain.
