Gazzeth, or simply the ruined orc city, was a long-abandoned ruin of a city along the coast of the Moonsea.

Geography

It lay between the city of Thentia and the Seawave ruins, and between the Ruined Trail and the shore of the Moonsea.

History

The name 'Gazzeth' was lost to history and, given the legend surrounding it, few in the Moonsea were eager to find it. By the mid-1300s DR, the ruins of Gazzeth were known simply as "ruined orc city".

Orcs from the ruined city destroyed Seawave, a nearby colony of Thentia.

Inhabitants

Giant forms of many kinds of vermin infested the ruins as of the late 1360s DR, which kept people away. At one point, it was also inhabited by orcs.

Rumors & Legends

A legend formed around the city's forgotten name, claiming that if the name of the city was ever discovered, then its long-dead inhabitants would rise, gather into an army, and march on Thentia.

There were rumors in Thentia that dragons and beholders might be establishing lairs in some of the ruined cities to the east, which would include Gazzeth.
