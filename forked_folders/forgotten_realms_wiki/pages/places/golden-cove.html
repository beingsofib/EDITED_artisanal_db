Golden Cove was a series of caverns and fjords on the very western tip of the Spine of the World.

It was the base of the pirate Sheila Kree and the winter home of her ship Bloody Keel.

It was shared with Clan Thump, a matriarchal ogre clan led by Chogurugga.
