The Breasal Marsh, also called the Breasal Swamp, was a marshland located on the isle of Moray within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The Breasal Marsh was located toward the center of the island. It covered approximately , making it one of the prominent geographical features of Moray. The marsh was fed by the Shannyth River flowing in from the south, which also exited the marsh's northern edge at Lac Dynnegall.

Notable Locations

  • The village of Horst sat on the southeast edge of the Breasal Marsh.
  • A portal to Citadel Umbra on Gwynneth was located within a grove of beech trees deep within the marsh. It was a shallow pool of water ringed by six hexagonal basalt blocks inscribed with demonic sigils, and was activated by the blood of lycanthropes. It was rumored to have been part of a network of portals used by the High Kings and Queens of House Kendrick prior to the Spellplague.

Inhabitants

Tribes of lizardfolk lived within the marsh. Some of the tribes wished to reawaken Kazgaroth, while others were more peaceful and followed Silvanus.
