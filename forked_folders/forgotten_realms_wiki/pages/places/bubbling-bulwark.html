The Bubbling Bulwark was a tavern and inn attached to Thistleflame Keep, the estate of the noble Thistle family in Suzail. It was owned by the ex-Purple Dragon Haverish Hallefon.

Description

The Bulwark was a pleasant and lively place, which drew in customers from within the Keep and from wider Suzail. It was a popular place to relax for the domestic staff of the Manor, and especially the Purple Dragons garrison who appreciated Haverish's service.

Location

The Bulwark was built into the Keep's wall, thus the name, and could be accessed from both sides of the wall.

Structure

The Bulwark was a three-floor construction, with a tavern and common room on the ground floor and two floors of rented rooms above. The inn's rooms were comfortable, and had beds for two people with enough space for two more on the floor. An underground storage cellar was also accessed from the ground floor. The tavern had an entrance from the outside of the Keep, which was how most public patrons entered. This door was attended by the bouncer Dwak. Opposite the public entry, a door led to the interior of the Keep, which was guarded by Louella Bonecracker

Inhabitants

The Bulwark had a total of 10 staff.

  • Haverish Hallefon, the proprietor.
  • Dwak and Louella Bonecracker, the bouncers.
  • Annalise, Caryn, Crystyn, Heather, Raewyn, and Sara, the barmaids.
  • Gram, the handyman.
