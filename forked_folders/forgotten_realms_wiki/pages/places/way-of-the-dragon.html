Way of the Dragon

The Way of the Dragon was western road in Cormyr connecting the capital of Suzail with other towns along Cormyr's western region bordering the Storm Horns.

Description

Originating in Suzail, the Way of the Dragon passed north to reach the town of Waymoot in the King's Forest before connecting Espar and terminating at Tyrluk on the East Way.
