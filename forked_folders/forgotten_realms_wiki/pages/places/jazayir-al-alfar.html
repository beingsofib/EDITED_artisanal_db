Jazayir al-Alfar, also known as the Islands of the Claws, were a string of small islands in Jazayir al-Sartan.

Geography

The islands were split into two clusters—one branching out from the northeastern shores of a larger central island called Jazirat al-Sadaf, and the other off of the southwestern shores.
