Keel Alley

Keel Alley was minor avenue of hard-packed earth and gravel in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Location

This alleyway ran from southwest to northeast, extending off of Ship Street and terminating into Dock Street. Along the way it was intersected by the alleyway Aline's Way.

Notable Locations

  • The Blue Mermaid tavern stood at the southwest corner of this alley.
