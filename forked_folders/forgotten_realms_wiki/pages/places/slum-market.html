The Slum Market in Phlan around 1367 DR was a marketplace where unbelievable bargains could be had as long as the buyer was fine with items of dubious quality.

Locations

The Slum Market was found in the southwest section of Phlan.

Activities

It was said about the Slum Market that you would be unlikely to see the same merchant two days in a row. In addition to the questionable commerce that thrived here, pickpockets infested the area looking for easy targets.
