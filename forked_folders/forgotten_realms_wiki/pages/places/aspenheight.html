Aspenheight

Aspenheight was a mountain range located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The mountain range was located in northwestern Gwynneth along the coast of the Strait of the Leviathan. Aspenheight continued from the Black Mountains to the north to form part of the northwestern border of Myrloch Vale. It was separated from the Highlands to the south by the Aspenheight Gap. Several rivers flowed into the Strait of the Leviathan from the mountains.

Inhabitants

As of the mid–14 century DR, the mountains were overseen by the druid Eileen.
