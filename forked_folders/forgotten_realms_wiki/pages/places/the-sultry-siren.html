The Sultry Siren was a festhall in the city of Calimport, Calimshan, in the late 14 century DR. It was fully controlled by the Jhasinnar Sodality guild.

Location

It was located on the eastern edge of Gagi Drudach, Axash Sabban, in the city's Emerald Ward.

Activities

A lavish building, it offered services by the female concubines known as jhasinnar. It was well known to be controlled by the Jhasinnar Sodality so most customers were only interested in its pleasures. However, this was a cover: guild leader Ralahn el Matyr used it for trading goods on the black market and fencing stolen jewelry.

Interior

From the cellars, secret passages connected to the Muzad, the undercity of Calimport. These allowed pashas and sultans a discreet escape from their enemies, or just embarrassment, so they owed Ralahn and the Sodality a favor for their lives or their reputations.

History

In the , the Trades Conflagration blazed across northern and central Calimport. The Sultry Siren was damaged, but calling in some favors, Ralahn had it repaired in less than a month.
