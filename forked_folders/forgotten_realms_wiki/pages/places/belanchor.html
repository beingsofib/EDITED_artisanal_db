Belanchor

nameBelanchor

regionWizards' Reach, Sea of Fallen Stars

Belanchor was a port town of the Sea of Fallen Stars, completely wiped out by the 14 century DR.

Geography

Belanchor was located in the eastern Sea of Fallen Stars,

Trade

It was a trading port of some importance.

History

The port was still extant in the early 3 century DR, but had been wiped out after a dragon took it for its lair, and then battled a rival in a fight that turned the entire town into so much rubble to be swallowed by the sea.

Inhabitants

  • Dlartarnan, an adventurer of the Brave Blades
