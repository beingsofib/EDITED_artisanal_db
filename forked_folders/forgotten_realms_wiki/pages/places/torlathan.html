Torlathan was a city in eastern Amn that served as an important trade stop with the Dragon Coast, named after the royal Torlath family.

Geography

The city was built on the north bank of the Valashar River, upstream of its confluence with the Khalieshyr.

History

Torlathan was an important part of the overland caravans between Amn and Westgate over the Giant's Plain. It was destroyed during the early Crusade of Slaughter in the , as the Bhaalist crusaders traveled from eastern Amn to Westgate along the trade route, crippling the east–west trade for decades. Attempts to rebuild the city and re-establish the trade routes proceeded slowly until the fall of the Amnian monarchy in the , when it was abandoned completely.
