Pearl Banks

Location
namePearl Banks
typeCoral reef
regionThe Pearl Cities, west Zakhara

The Pearl Banks, named for their abundance of treasured pearls, were a large coral reef system found off the coast of the south and southeast region of the Pearl Cities of west Zakhara.

Geography

The reef stretched for nearly around the coast of the Pearl Cities, beginning in the west near Ajayib and continuing all the way to Jumlat in the northeast.
