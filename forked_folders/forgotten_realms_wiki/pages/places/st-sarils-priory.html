St. Saril's Priory was an isolated Ilmatari monastery in Calimshan, one of many in the wilderness of that land.

Location

St. Saril's was found in a far-flung region of northern Calimshan, caught between the Marching Mountains, River Memnon, and Calim Desert, not too far from Qian Hillfort and Shoonach to the north in Tethyr's Monrativi Teshy Mir. The desolate, rough landscape was scarcely inhabited by people and practically forgotten about by both Calimshan and Tethyr.

History

During the 14 century DR the region around the monastery lacked any sort of oversight from Calimshan, which led to the Revered Father Hamash el Saril el Ilmater and the monks functionally ruling the area as an independent theocracy. They aided and protected those that lived in the harsh terrain, and resisted any attempts by outsiders to interfere.
