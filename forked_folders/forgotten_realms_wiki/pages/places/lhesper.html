Lhesper was a ruined town on the shore of Lake Lhespen in the western region of the Shaar.

History

Lhesper was founded in the , by refugees from Lapaliiya who were fleeing plague. The city remained independent until th , when the army of Qysara Shoon V marched across the Shaar conquering everything in their path. The city would not regain its independence until the , and again in the .

The town was destroyed by a pack of rampaging gnolls in 7th century DR. Around the year of Wild Magic Yuan-ti began to move into the area, searching for some mysterious buried object.

Activities

As of the , Lhesper was taken over by members from the Coiled Cabal who were searching for the Emeralds of Merrshaulk.
