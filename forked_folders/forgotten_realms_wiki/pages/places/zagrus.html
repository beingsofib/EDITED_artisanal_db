Zagrus was a tiny nation of the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars. It participated in a series of wild conflicts against the other nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles

Description

Zagrus was led by Caliph Oznan (who was known as The Great) and Prince Mehtmid. It was home to the Camel Corps, the Council of Tribal Chieftains, the tribes of Zagrusti and Jewhar. Also living in Zagrus were the Dark Wanderers, who were nominally aligned with Zagrus but threatened to secede and take the fortress known as the Quattary Depression as their hideout, planning to pillage gold from the caravans of Zagrus. Zagrus also commanded a powerful fleet known as The Ships of War.

Zagrus shared a land border with Klocckbarn, which was marked by a range of mountains. The walled city-state of Al-Ubaid was nestled within these mountains, and opposed both Zagrus and Klocckbarn. The mighty fortress of Ratlarbig stood on Zagrus's coast, overlooking the Lennahc Hsilgne which seperated Zagrus from Wolfenbutte. During the wars of the Whamite isles, Zagrus fought for control of Ratlarbig, which sometimes acted independently.

Among the nations of the Whamites, Zagrus was significant for its caravans, which included the Caravan of Nesde that passed between it and Al-Ubaid. Other merchant vessels, such as The Carrack, the fleet from Vandmeer, and the Smardese Fleet often passed by Zagrus, offering much wealth. Even the Great Khan was known to favor routes around Zagrus. Zagrus was also home to camels.
