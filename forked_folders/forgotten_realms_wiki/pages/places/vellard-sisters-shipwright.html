Vellard Sisters Shipwright was a shipbuilding business that operated out of the city of Tsurlagol in the Vast, circa the late 14 century DR.

History

By the late 15 century DR, Vellard Sisters Shipwright was the main competitor of Selvard Haeleophar, Master Shipbuilder of Telflamm. Vellard Sisters assembled three vessels at the time, delivering "bare-timbers but navigable" rakers or battle-caravels per, one per eighteen days.
