Tír faoi Thoinn, also known as the Land Beneath the Waves, was a massive kelp forest just off the southeastern coast of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Tír faoi Thoinn spanned about across the mouth of the strait between the southeast corner of Gwynneth and the southwest corner of Snowdown. On the Gwynneth side, it lay just off the coast from one of Llyrath Forest's moonwells and the location known as Siren's Grotto. Here, barren shallows gave way into the forest of kelp growing out from a rocky seascape marked by stone spires.

Flora & Fauna

In addition to the magnificent kelp, which gave Tír faoi Thoinn the feeling of an idyllic forest, the region featured reed-like plants covering the rocky outcroppings. These seaweeds served as habitats for a wide variety of bioluminescent sea creatures.

History

Tír faoi Thoinn was rumored to be the Torilian demesne of Deep Sashelas in his capacity as patron of the sea elves.

As of the late 15 century DR, Tír faoi Thoinn was nominally considered to be part of the fey kingdom of Sarifal.
