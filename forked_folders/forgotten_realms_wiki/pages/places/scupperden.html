Scupperden was an eatery located in the Dredge, the lowest level of Skullport.

Services

The establishment was known primarily for its stew made of fish, mushrooms, and vegetable scraps that sold for 1 cp per bowl. The stew was described as somewhat alarming but unlikely to kill the eater.

Description

Scupperden was a single room built from scavenged wood, sailcloth, and rope. Inside, patrons lined up to a serving trough.
