Zestra's Hot Soups was an eatery in the city of Waterdeep in the 14 century DR.

Location

Zestra's was located down on Carter's Way in Waterdeep's Southern Ward.

Services

The shop offered its customers servings of salted sprouts, various soups, and spiced sausages in gravy.

History

Circa the , Fylgard "the Fat" Onister of South Ward, a merchant of ropes, fastenings, and trail-leathers, routinely visited Zestra's after sunset and the closing of his stone, along with his sons.
