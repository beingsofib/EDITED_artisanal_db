Dark Mere was a swamp in the Spine of the World mountains in northwest Faerûn, north of the small town of Blasingdell.

Geography

This swamp stood at the foot of the Stone Tooth, a peak of the southern Spine of the World range.

Inhabitants

Kobolds from the Sourscale Warrens were known to leave slain creatures in its brackish waters to "pickle" them for a while before eating.
