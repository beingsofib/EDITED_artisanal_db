Calantar's Way

Calantar's Way () was a long road running north to south in the nation of Cormyr. The southernmost point of Calantar's Way was the city of Suzail.

Geography

Calantar's Way crossed the Starwater River at Calantar's Bridge, just south of the town of Hilp.

Description

Calantar's Way had been well-cared-for over the years by those who used it.

History

Calantar's Way was surveyed and built by the Calantar noble family in the late 900s DR as a cart road.
