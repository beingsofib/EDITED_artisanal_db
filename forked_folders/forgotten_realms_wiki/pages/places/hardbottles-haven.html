Hardbottle's Haven

Building
nameHardbottle's Haven

Hardbottle's Haven was a tavern in the town of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid–14 century DR. It was managed by the halfling Hardbottle family.

Location

It lay in the far southeastern end of Loudwater.

Atmosphere

It was a relatively well lit, subdued, and family-friendly establishment.
