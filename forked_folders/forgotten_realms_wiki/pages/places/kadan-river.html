Kadan River

The Kadan River was a central river in the Cities of the Ancients in east Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

The Kadan River flowed roughly north to south for hundreds of miles, originating in the unnamed mountains forming the border with the Haunted Lands. It flowed through the heart of the jungles that dominated the Ruined Kingdoms before finally emptying into the Foreigners' Sea.

Description

Once its several unnamed tributaries joined together, the Kadan River wound from side to side like a snake as it made its way south to the sea.
