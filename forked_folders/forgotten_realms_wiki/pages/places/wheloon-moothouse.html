The Wheloon Moothouse was a meeting hall located in the city of Wheloon in Cormyr. It stood on Wheloon Way, opposite Oldstone Hall.

The moothouse served as Wheloon's courthouse and a hall for meetings. It also contained apartments for visiting court messengers and high-ranking Purple Dragons.

The building was constructed of timber and stone. It was built wide and with few internal walls or furniture to allow as many people as possible to gather inside.
