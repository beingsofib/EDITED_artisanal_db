Al-Zulm Islands was a large archipelago found in the Crowded Sea within west Zakhara.

Geography

The archipelago was one of several prominent island groups congesting the Crowded Sea. The northernmost island in al-Zulm Islands was located south of the Pearl Banks, which were close to the southeastern peninsula of the Pearl Cities.

Thirteen individual islands were part of the archipelago. The Zakharans named some of these uninhabited islands as listed below:

  • Anklis Island
  • Manhus Island
  • Qusur Island
  • Saliam Island
