Zill was a small settlement found on the eastern shores of the South Quarter of Suq Bay in the Cities of the Heart region of interior Zakhara.

Geography

Zill was located near the midway point between Huzuz and Wasat. The town was founded along the banks of an unnamed river that flowed south from the southern tip of the Ghost Mountains. It was also located near the caravan route that connected Halwa and Wasat.
