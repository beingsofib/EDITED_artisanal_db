Phaesha's

Building
namePhaesha's
typeBoarding House
locationArabel, Cormyr

Phaesha's was a boarding house in the city of Arabel in Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.

Location

The building was located on the southwest corner of a block in the center of the city opposite the Ivory Jack tavern. It also sat across the street from Elhazir's Exotica.
