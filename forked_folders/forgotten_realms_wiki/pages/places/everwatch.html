Everwatch

Everwatch was the divine realm of Helm, the Faerûnian God of Guardians.

Description

It was said to manifest as an aerial watchtower that apparated every time a portal was opened to its home plane.

Cosmography

When viewing the multiverse through the Great Wheel cosmological model, Everwatch was observed to reside on the Outer Plane of Mechanus, the place of supreme order.

Utilizing the World Tree model, Everwatch could be found in the House of the Triad.

Inhabitants

Everwatch was inhabited by a lone, unnamed guardian, appearing as an armored figure in a suit of mithral full plate mail. It was empowered with the ability to summon any of Helm's divine servants at a moment's notice.
