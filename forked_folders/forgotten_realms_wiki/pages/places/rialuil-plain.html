The Rialuil Plain, which was also called the Rialuil Range, was an open grassland located in the nation of Dambrath in the Shining South during the 14 century DR.

Geography

The Rialuil Plain covered much of central Dambrath. It was bordered to the north by the Hills of the Dead Kings, to the south by the Bay of Dancing Dolphins, to the east by Melanith Bay, and to the west by the River Caraguir.
