The Green Chapel was an inn found in the city of Urling in Rashemen in the .

Activities

Though visiting the nearby Urlingwood was strictly forbidden upon pain of death, foreigners were allowed to travel through Urling. The Green Chapel catered to these travelers, though they were discouraged from staying longer than a few days.
