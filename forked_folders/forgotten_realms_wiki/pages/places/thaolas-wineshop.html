Thaola's Wineshop was a wine shop in Leuthilspar on the island of Evermeet.

Geography

Thaola's shop was located east of Dhoelath's Tower and north of the Shrine of the Black Sword.

Inhabitants

The shop was owned by Thaola who sold vintage elven wines.

History

Around 1367 DR, Thaola had a single bottle of wine from Myth Drannor.
