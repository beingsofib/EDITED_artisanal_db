The Glowing Goblet

Building
nameThe Glowing Goblet
typeTavern
ownerMaerpir Tchar

The Glowing Goblet was a tavern in the city of Elturel in the Western Heartlands in the mid–14 century DR. It stood on Baerth Lane.

Circa 1367 DR, the tavernmaster of the Glowing Goblet was Maerpir Tchar.
