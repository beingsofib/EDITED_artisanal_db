Shee Pool

The Shee Pool was a small lake found on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

The Shee Pool was located near the center of Myrloch Vale. It was fed from the northwest by the Deepglen Stream flowing down from the Black Mountains. The Deepglen Stream continued south to connect with the Myrloch. A branch of the Codsrun Creek exited the lake from its northeast shore.

Notable Locations

A Moonwell was located along the Shee Pool's northern shore.
