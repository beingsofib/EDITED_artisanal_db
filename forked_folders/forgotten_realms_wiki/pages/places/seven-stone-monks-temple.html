The Seven Stone Monks Temple was a monastery in T'u Lung, Kara Tur, established at some point before the early 14 century DR.

History

The Seven Stone Monks Temple was a well-known monastic organization, already in existence some years before the . The monastery's inhabitants were the seven stone monks who possessed powers of divine nature. One notable creation of the seven monks was Myo-Myo's gong, an enchanted shield and a gong wielded by a famous ghost hunter Myo-Myo. The seven stone monks etched the gong with geometric patterns and imbued it with the sacred power to perform ''holy smite'.
