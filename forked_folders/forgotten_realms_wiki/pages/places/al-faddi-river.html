Al-Faddi River

Al-Faddi River was a split river system found in north and west Zakhara.

Geography

The northern branch of al-Faddi River originated in the southern tip of the Furrowed Mountains, flowing east then south before reaching Qudra's bay on the coast of the Great Sea. The southern branch of the river originated where the Mountains of the Lizard's Tongue and Spine of the Dog merged. It flowed northeast before reaching Qudra.

Description

Once past the foothills of the mountains, the river turned the plains into fertile grassland.
