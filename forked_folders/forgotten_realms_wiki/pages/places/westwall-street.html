Westwall Street

Westwall Street was a large road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Westwall Street was located in the western section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward, fully spanning the entire western boundary of the ward with the exterior wall in that portion of the city. The road originated in the north, branching south off of the Street of Lances near the Trolltower and continuing south along the exterior wall until it terminated at the junction of Julthoon Street and the Cliffride at the border with the Castle Ward. Along its way Westwall Street connected with Aureenar Street, Breeze's Cut, the Street of Glances, the Skulkway, Bliss Street, Ignorance Alley, Grimwald's Way, Satchel Alley, Diamond Street, Seaeye's March, and Wagonslide Alley.
