Fairheight was a county located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.

Geography

Fairheight was the smallest of Callidyrr's counties. It was located along the far northern border with Gnarhelm. The vast majority of the county was filled by the Fairheight Range and the mountain's foothills.

Government

The Masters of Fairheight were the Earls of the Blackstone family. They were among the most powerful nobles in Callidyrr as of the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Trade

The county was known as the center of mining and smelting in Callidyrr, making it a critical backbone of Callidyrr's economy.

History

In the , Earl Angus Blackstone betrayed High King Tristan Kendrick in exchange for favors from an agent of Talos. When his betrayal was discovered, he was slain and replaced as Earl by his son, Hanrold Blackstone.

Notable Locations

Several mining towns, including Blackstone, White Rock, and Highrock, were found in Fairheight.
