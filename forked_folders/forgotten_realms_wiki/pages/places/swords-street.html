Swords Street

Swords Street was a major road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Swords Street was located on the western side of Waterdeep's Castle Ward. It ran north to south, running parallel with the Street of Silks to the east. Swords Street originated in the north on Calamastyr Lane, branching south then turning southeast west of Bazaar Street before turning due south again. The road ended where it met Waterdeep Way in front of Piergeiron's Palace. Other roads crossing Swords Street included Keltarn Street, Tharleon Street, Selduth Street, Tchozal's Race, Sevenlamps Cut, and Turnback Court.
