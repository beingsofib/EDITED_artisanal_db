Al-Hadi River

Al-Hadi River, also called the River of Inspiration, was a small winding river found in the northern region of the Cities of the Pantheon in interior Zakhara in 1367 DR.

Geography

Al-Hadi River originated in Al-Sayaj Mountains. It flowed west down from the mountains before emptying into the southern reaches of the Golden Gulf at the site of I'tiraf.
