Watchrun Alley

Watchrun Alley was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.

Location

Watchrun Alley was located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward. The alley ran between Gut Alley in the northeast and Nelnuk's Walk in the southwest. It originated on Gut Alley, branching southwest to end on Nelnuk's Walk. Along its path Watchrun Alley was intersected by Redcloak Lane and Drawn Sword Alley.

History

The ally's name was derived from the City Watch to hurry across this section of the Dock Ward. Thieves and pranksters were known to string trip wires across the alley to cause havoc.
