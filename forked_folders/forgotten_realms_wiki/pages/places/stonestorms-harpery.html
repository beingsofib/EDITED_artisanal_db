Stonestorm's Harpery was an instrument shop specializing in harps in the city of Silverymoon in the Silver Marches in the late 14 century DR. It stood on Goblinwood Rise in the city's Southbank. It was owned by Markovel Stonestorm and used strings made by Chesmyr Morrowynd circa 1372 DR.
