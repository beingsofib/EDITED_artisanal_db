Shadowtop Cathedral

Location
nameShadowtop Cathedral
typeGrove

Shadowtop Cathedral was a stand of tall shadowtop trees in the High Forest and an important meeting place for the Emerald Enclave after 1485 DR.

Inhabitants

Moon elf druids occasionally visited the Cathedral.
