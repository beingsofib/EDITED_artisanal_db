Melt metal was a common spell used by wu jen in Kara-Tur.

Effects

When cast, the spell allowed a wu jen to heat up and melt metal. During casting, the wu jen touched a piece of metal and magical energy flowed into it. The spell only affected metal objects. A wu jen's total focus was required to cast this spell.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, a gem valued at least 20 ch'ien.
