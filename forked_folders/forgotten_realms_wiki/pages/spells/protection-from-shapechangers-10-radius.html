Protection from shapechangers, 10' radius was a spell used by the Red Wizards of Thay.

Effects

This spell was very similar to Protection from all lycanthropes, 10' radius. In addition to protecting the caster from all forms of lycanthropes, it also protected against other shapechangers, such as doppelgangers, jackalweres, druids, some dragons, those under the influence of polymorph spells, It created a small area of protection around the caster, 10 feet (3 meters) in radius. This protected area moved with the caster.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component.

Notable Users

  • Lui-hsia Chih, a wu-jen in Shou Lung, carried a scroll of protection from shapechangers.
