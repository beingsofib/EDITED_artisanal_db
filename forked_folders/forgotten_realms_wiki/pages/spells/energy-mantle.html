Energy mantle

The Energy mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concept of energy types–such as acid, cold, electricity, fire, and sonic.

Abilities

Those who followed the energy mantle could grant themselves a minute resistance towards one energy type by expending a psionic focus.

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the fate mantle granted greater insight, allowing the manifester and nearby allies to alter the probability of their luck one or more times each, depending upon the the expertise of the divine mind.

Powers

Novice manifesters gained the power to fire energy types as psionic rays and to unleash psychokinetically pushing blasts of energy. Manifesters of some experience learned progressively stronger versions of both powers in the form of energy bolt and energy burst, as well as the power to create a wall of the chosen energy. As a manifester progressed further they obtained the power to manipulate sources of energy with energy manipulation. Masters of this mantle obtained the power of energy wave, allowing them to unleash the most devastating psionic blasts of an energy type of their choice.
