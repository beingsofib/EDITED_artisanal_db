Searing light was a divine evocation spell that created a holy light that burned a cleric's foes, especially undead.

Effects

The cleric fired a beam of divine power, focused like a ray of the sun, from their open palm, to a distance of or more for more experienced casters. Those it struck were harmed according to the power of the caster. Undead creatures were injured more, especially those vulnerable to bright light, while constructs and objects were damaged less.

Components

It required verbal and somatic components.
