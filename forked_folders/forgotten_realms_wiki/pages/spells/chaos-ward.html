Chaos ward

Spell
nameChaos Ward
school2eAbjuration
spheres2eChaos
level2eChaos sphere Chaos 2 Cleric 2 Crusader 2

Chaos ward was an abjuration spell that surrounded a creature with a swirling light that reduced the chance of being hit in combat and even had a slight chance of deflecting or reflecting ranged attacks, physical or magical.
