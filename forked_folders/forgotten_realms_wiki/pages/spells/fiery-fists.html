Fiery fists

Spell
nameFiery fists
school2eAlteration, Flame
level2eWizard 3

Fiery fists was a flame spell used commonly in Zakhara, the Land of Fate. This spell was only usable by wizards.

Effects

Casting this spell caused the caster's hands to become engulfed in blue flames that were useful as weapons. Simple touching any opponent caused a fiery explosion that not only burned the target, but also released noxious smoke that caused them to cough uncontrollably for several minutes. Only a small amount of the smoke was released with each hit, causing only the single target to begin coughing.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a lump of coal to cast.
