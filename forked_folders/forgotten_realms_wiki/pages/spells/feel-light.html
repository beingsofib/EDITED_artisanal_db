Feel light was a psionic devotion of the clairsentience

Effects

This power made a manifester's entire body act as surrogate eyes, becoming a transceiver for light waves, allowing them to see whatever their eyes would with light for a duration of 10 or more minutes. This had the added benefit of making the manifester more capable of resisting gaze-based abilities or effects, such as the petrifying gaze of a medusa. If their eyes were functioning normally, the manifester also became noticeably better at searching and spotting minute details. This became even more pronounced when used in conjunction with hear light.

This power granted no wider field of vision and did not function within darkness, not even if the manifester had darkvision. Nor could it counter areas of magically induced darkness, as such darkness outright destroyed or blocked light waves.

If a manifestation backfired, the manifester would be blinded and overly sensitive to light for the next one to ten minutes, with exposure to light causing harm.

Displays

When used, this power manifested a material display.

Users

This power was knowable by psionicists,

Feel light was found to be exhibited as an innate psionic ability by wall-walkers.

Known/Notable Users

  • Teliene Coloara, a drow psychic warrior and illithid slayer.
