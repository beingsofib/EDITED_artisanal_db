Sympathy was an enchantment spell that compelled a creature to approach another.

Effects

The spell caused a specified creature within to feel an intense urge to be near the target. If the creature would be attacked by the target, then it would have to try to resist the spell to defend itself.

Components

The material component was a drop of honey.
