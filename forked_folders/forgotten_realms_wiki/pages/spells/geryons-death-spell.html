Geryon's death spell

Spell
nameGeryon's Death Spell
inventorGeryon

Geryon's death spell was an extremely dangerous and forbidden spell, created by Geryon, sometime before his demise in the early 14 century DR.

Effects

Geryon's death spell started with complex patterns of colored light that opened a swirling color rift in the sky. At the center of the colorful storm, a rift the size of a coin opened and grew to eclipse the sun. An enormous eye peeked out of the rift, only to disappear moments later, before the rift began to suck unsecured things from the earth in whilst creating whirlwinds of dust and sand on the ground.

History

During the Time of Troubles in 1358 DR, shortly after the death of Mystra at the hands of Helm, the mage adventurer Midnight used diamond dust to cast a wall of force. However, with the goddess's death, magic was turned into chaos. Instead of creating a wall of force, Midnight unleashed Geryon's death spell on the battlefield. Luckily, Elminster of Shadowdale stepped in just in time to counter the spell. Once he was sure that Geryon's death spell was an accidental conjuring and that Midnight did not have the forbidden spell written down, Elminster departed.
