Metaphysical claw

Psionic Power
nameMetaphysical claw
discipline3ePsychometabolism
mantles3eNatural World
level3eArdent 1 Divine mind 1 Psychic warrior 1
type3eStandard power

Metaphysical claw was a psionic power of the psychometabolism discipline that allowed a manifester to enhance their own natural weapons.

Effects

This power could make natural weapons such as claws and jaws, as well as those provided by powers such as claws of the beast or bite of the wolf, act as though they were weapon with a +1 or greater enchantment. Normally this power would last a period of minutes, but through the focusing of psionic powers a psychic warrior could extend the duration to hours.

Displays

Manifesting this power produced a standard auditory display.

Users

This power was knowable by psychic warriors, as well as ardents and divine minds that followed the Natural World mantle.

Metaphysical claw was an innate psionic ability for thri-kreen. Normally they could only use it once per day, but through practice and training some managed to manifest the power multiple times per day.
