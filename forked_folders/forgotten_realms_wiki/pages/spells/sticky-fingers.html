Sticky fingers was a spell that made the caster better at pilfering things using sleights of hand.

Effects

On casting sticky fingers, the caster's fingers secreted a sticky substance while the hands became more agile. The effect lasted for at least ten minutes (longer for more experienced casters) and could be dispelled at will.

Components

One version of this spell required both verbal and somatic components. An improved version only required silent, somatic gestures.
