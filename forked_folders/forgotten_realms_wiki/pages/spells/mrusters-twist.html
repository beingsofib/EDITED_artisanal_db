Mruster's twist was a spell.

Powers

The spell changed incoming spells into other spells, and allowed its caster to redirect the latter.

History

By , Jhalavan's fond return had been modified to create Mruster's Twist. Elminster Aumar, a new human armathor of Cormanthor, used the spell in his duel against Delmuth Echorn. He used it to convert a spell akin to acid rain into a dispel magic to strike at his opponent's mantle.
