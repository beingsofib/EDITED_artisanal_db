Javelin (spell)

Spell
nameJavelin
school2eEvocation
level2eWizard 6

Javelin was an uncommon evocation spell of the southern magic tradition.

Effects

When cast, the spell caused a javelin to become filled with energy. When thrown, the javelin struck a target without error within the spells range, which was 80 yard (73 meter).

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component, an ordinary javelin.
