Pierce magic

Spell
namePierce Magic
school2eAbjuration
level2eWizard 6

Pierce magic was a spell used by wizards. The spell was used to penetrate a creature's magic resistance.

Effects

This spells lowered a creature's magical resistance. Additionally, the spell removed one protection-type spell that was cast upon the targeted creature.
