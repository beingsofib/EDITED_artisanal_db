Weather summoning was a conjuration spell that could bring forth any kind of weather that might normally occur for the location, climate, and season at the time of casting.

History

In the month of Eleasis, of the , the city of Ravens Bluff declared it illegal to cast weather summoning within city limits due to a raging unnatural storm at the time called the Soulstorm, threatening arrest for those that broke this decree.
