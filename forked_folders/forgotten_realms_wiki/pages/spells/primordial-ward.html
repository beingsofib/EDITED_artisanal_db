Primordial ward

Spell
namePrimordial Ward
school5eAbjuration
level5eDruid 6

Primordial ward was an abjuration spell that conferred protection from a variety of elements on its caster.

Effects

The spell granted the caster magical resistance against acid, cold, fire, lightning, and thunder for up to 1 minute, as long as the caster kept concentrating. Additionally, the spell's energy could be concentrated on a single element, completely blocking any damage of the chosen type for a few seconds, after which the spell ended.

Components

The spell only required verbal and somatic components.
