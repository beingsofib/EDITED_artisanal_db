Summon nature's ally V

Spell
nameSummon Nature's Ally V
school3eConjuration (Summoning)
level3eDruid 5

Summon nature's ally V was one of the series of summon nature's ally spells available to druidic spellcasters.

Effects

This spell functioned just like all summon nature's ally spells, which in turn resembled the summon monster spells. It could summon a single adult arrowhawk, polar bear, dire lion, elasmosaurus, large elemental of any type, griffon, janni,rhinoceros, satyr, giant constrictor snake, nixie, adult tojanida, or orca. It could also be used to summon a larger number of monsters from a lower level of a spell in the series.

Components

The spell required a divine focus and both verbal and somatic components.
