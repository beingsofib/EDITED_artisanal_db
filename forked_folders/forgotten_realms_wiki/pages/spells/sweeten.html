Sweeten

Spell
nameSweeten
school1eEvocation
level1eMagic-user 0
type1eUseful

Sweeten was a useful cantrip that acolyte wizards could learn. It summoned a sprinkling of sugar or a drizzle of honey or syrup above a target creature or object.

Effects

This cantrip created a small quantity of a sweet-tasting condiment such as sugar, honey, or syrup. It appeared just above the target and fell as a fine powder or as a slow trickle of liquid. The amount was typically enough to sweeten food or drink for about six people. The target of this spell had to be within of the caster.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster made a buzzing sound while pantomiming stirring.
