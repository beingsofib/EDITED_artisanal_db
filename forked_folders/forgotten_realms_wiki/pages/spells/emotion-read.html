Emotion read

Emotion read was a simple divine spell that allowed a cleric to receive an instant read on a target's emotional state.

Effects

The spell was limited to reading the emotions of only one individual within or greater—and only the strongest of that individual's emotions. Targets with powerful strength of will could resist the read or even cause the spell to provide an opposite read from the true one. The spell gave no indication about the cause or topic of the emotion felt.

Components

The spell required verbal and somatic components and an unmarked square of white wax.
