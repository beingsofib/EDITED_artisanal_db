Sharpen

Spell
nameSharpen
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 1

Sharpen was an uncommon spell known to hedge wizards and artificers.

Effects

The spell sharpened and straightened a blade, removing nicks and dents along the edge. It did not provide any additional combat advantage beyond that of regular maintenance.

Components

The spell called for verbal and somatic components only.

History

An enterprising hedge mage had developed the spell.
