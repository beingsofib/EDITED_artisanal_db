Protection from magic energy

Spell
nameProtection From Magic Energy
school2eAbjuration
level2eWizard 6

Protection from magic energy was a defensive spell used by wizards.

Effects

When cast, the caster was protected from all attack-based spells. While the spell did not offer total immunity to damage from spells, the caster received only half the damage they normally would.
