Sphere of wonder was a wizard spell from the schools of alteration and evocation.

Usage

This spell created a stationary sphere of magical light measuring in diameter. The sphere blocked all spells and magic items from functioning within its borders. However, at the time of casting the caster could choose to allow certain types of spell or magic items to work properly. The caster could select fire-based spells to function for example.

Components

This spell only required verbal and somatic components to cast.
