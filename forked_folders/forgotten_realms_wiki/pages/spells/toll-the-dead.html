Toll the dead

Spell
nameToll the Dead
school5eNecromancy
level5eCleric 0 Warlock 0 Wizard 0
type5eCantrip

Toll the dead was a necromancy cantrip that drained the life force of creatures, especially ones who had been recently hurt.

Effects

By pointing at a target within a range of , the spell filled the air around the creature with the sound of a slowly sounding bell in a funeral toll. A creature unable to resist the spell's effect suffered an immediate drain in life force that increased as the caster grew more powerful. Although fully healthy creatures could be damaged by toll the dead, the spell was considerably more effective on injured targets.

Components

The spell required only verbal and somatic components.
