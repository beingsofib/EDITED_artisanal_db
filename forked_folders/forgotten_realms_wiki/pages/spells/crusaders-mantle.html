Crusader's mantle

level5ePaladin 3 War domain War 3

Crusader's mantle was an evocation spell that created a aura that radiated holy energies.

Effects

The aura made non-hostiles feel a holy an awakened boldness and also made them cause radiant damage. The aura traveled with the caster and only lasted one minute.

Components

Only a verbal component was needed to cast this spell.
