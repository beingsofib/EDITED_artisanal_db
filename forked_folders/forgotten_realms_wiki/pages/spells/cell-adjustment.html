Cell adjustment was a restorative psionic power of the psychometabolism discipline.

Effects

If successful, those touched by the user would have their wounds healed and any non-magical diseases cured, though it could only do one or the other. In addition, unnatural diseases such as mummy rot and lycanthropy would not be cured.

Users

This power was knowable by psionicists.

Cell adjustment was innately exhibited as a power by amethyst dragons, baku, beljuril dragons, and brain coral.

In psionics terms, it could be known by phoenixes and yochlol, though the latter could only use it while in their humanoid form.

Known/Notable Users

  • Ralayn, an outcast alhoon.
  • Vhondryl, a psionicist in Skullport and head of the Hall of Mental Splendor
  • Elminster Aumar, prince of Athalantar, had this power from birth.

Trivia

Tessadyle robes conferred cell adjustment upon their wearers for use once per day on themselves.
