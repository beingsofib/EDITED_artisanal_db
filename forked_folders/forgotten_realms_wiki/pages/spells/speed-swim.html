Speed swim

nameSpeed Swim
school3eTransmutation
level3eDruid 1 Ranger 1 Sorcerer 1 Wizard 1

Speed swim was a simple spell that improved a creature's swimming ability.

Effects

This spell increased ability and speed while swimming. The creature's swimming speed was the same as if they were running on dry land. The spell only functioned if swimming in a single direction. The targeted creature had to be within of the caster while the spell was cast. This range increased as the caster's skill level grew.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a focus: a small paddle made from wood.
