Immunity to spider venom

Spell
nameImmunity to Spider Venom
school2eAbjuration
level2eWizard 2

Immunity to spider venom was an arcane abjuration spell, used in the Underdark, which would allow the target to become immune to all effects of arachnid venom, digestive secretions or internal fluids.

Effects

The spell would work on any creature that the caster touched, including themselves. Once cast, the recipient was immune to all venom from arachnids. The recipient would also become slightly more resistant to other types of venom, acid, or poison.

Components

As well as verbal and somatic components, the material component consisted of a fully intact spider, of any size, alive or dead.
