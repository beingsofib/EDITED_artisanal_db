Minor disguise was a transmutation spell used by bards.

Effects

This spell was used to perform minor changes to the caster's appearance. Examples included changing hair color, adding a scar or wrinkles, and staining the teeth. Changes made with this spell last for an hour.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
