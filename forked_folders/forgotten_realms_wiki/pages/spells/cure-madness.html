Cure madness was a divine abjuration spell from the necromantic sphere that removed the harmful effects of diseases that targeted the mind.

Effects

In order for the spell's magic to work the priest needed to place their hands upon the target's brow. The negative effects of madness dissipated within several minutes to several days depending on the severity of the disease. This spell also worked to combat the feeblemind spell and the aftereffects of psionics that harmed the mind.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.

History

This spell was only granted to followers of the elven goddess Sehanine Moonbow.
