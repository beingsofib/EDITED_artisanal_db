Warlock's leap

Spell
nameWarlock's Leap
level4eWarlock 10
type4eDaily

Warlock's leap was a spell known to warlocks with a fey pact.

Effects

The spell enabled the warlock to teleport up to by "leaping" through the veil of the Feywild. The caster could attempt to leap to a location which they could not see, and the spell failed if there was not space for them to reappear at their destination.

History

Among the feywardens of Sarifal were those who embraced the trickery of the Feywild and could gain the ability to replicate this spell.
