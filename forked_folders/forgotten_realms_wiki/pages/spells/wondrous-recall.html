Wondrous recall was a common spell used by clerics.

Effects

By means of this spell, the caster was able to recall into their memory the last spell they used, then duplicated it. These two spells were stored in the caster's memory along with any other spell they had memorized. Each spell could be cast silently and without required material components. The caster was only able to cast one of the stored spells at a time. Additionally, the caster was only able to use one wondrous recall spell at a time. If the caster attempted to cast two one after another, they were struck with effects similar to the feeblemind spell.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components
