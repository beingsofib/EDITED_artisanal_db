Dreamwhisper (spell)

Spell
nameDreamwhisper
deityMystra

Dreamwhisper was a spell granted to wielders of Mystra's magics.

Effects

When dreamwhisper was cast, it flashed blue fire in the caster's mind for a mere moment as a sign of Mystra's attention.

History

In the , Aumlar Chaunthoun, a companion of Shandril Shessair, unwillingly cast dreamwhisper on himself. He later believed the spell to be sent by Mystra as a test.
