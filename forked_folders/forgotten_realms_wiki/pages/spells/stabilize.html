Stabilize was a wild magic abjuration spell used by wild mages.

Effects

This spell negated the chaotic effects within wild magic regions. With the caster's immense concentration during casting, stabilize allowed the caster and all creatures within to use magic normally without fear of triggering a wild surge. The safe area followed the caster if they moved around. Furthermore, a wild surge triggered outside of range of this spell would not pass into the safe area.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
