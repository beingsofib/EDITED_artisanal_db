Cough

Spell
nameCough
school1eEvocation
level1eMagic-user 0
type1ePerson-affecting

Cough was a person-affecting cantrip that acolyte wizards could learn. It caused a target creature to suffer a brief fit of coughing.

Effects

This cantrip had a range of and could target a single person or creature. If the target did not resist the spell, then it experienced a loud bout of coughing for a duration of one to three seconds. If the spell was resisted, the target's breathing was only momentarily disrupted by a small cough that, in most circumstances (other than singing, perhaps), did not adversely affect any activity.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster made a gasping noise while placing a hand on their throat as if they were choking.
