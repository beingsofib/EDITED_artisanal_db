Combat readiness

Spell
nameCombat Readiness
school3eDivination
level3eAssassin 1 Bard 1 Sorcerer 1 Wizard 1

Combat readiness was a spell that enabled the subject to act more rapidly in combat.

Effect

When the spell was cast, the subject's eyes widened and briefly shone white. Thereafter, until the spell's duration ended in a minute or more, the subject gained an increased reaction time at the beginning of a combat encounter.

The subject was also better able to defend against adversaries in flanking positions.

Components

It only required a verbal component.
