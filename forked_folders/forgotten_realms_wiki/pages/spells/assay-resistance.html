Assay resistance was a spell used by clerics, sorcerers, and wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell allowed a caster to determine the type and weaknesses of a magical creature's defenses. The caster had to be within visual range of the targeted creature. The spell only targeted a single creature per casting.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
