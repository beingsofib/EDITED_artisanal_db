Ritual strength

Spell
nameRitual Strength
school2eAlteration, Universal
level2eWizard 3

Ritual strength was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate. This spell was only used by sha'irs.

Effects

Sha'irs used this spell to prepare themselves for the arduous task of enhancing the power of their gen. With the spell active, sha'irs did not suffer from exhaustion due to the ritual.

Components

This spell did not require any material components to cast.
