Disentangle was a spell used by clerics of Thard Harr.

Effects

With this spell, the caster or a targeted creature was capable of escaping being physically entangled or trapped. The spell was effective against glues, the jaws of an animal or creature, plants, webs and even being held in a hold.

Components

This spell required only a verbal component.
