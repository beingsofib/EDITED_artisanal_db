Sacrifice soul

Sacrifice soul was a unique empowering spell from the necromancy school of magic that was granted to the most devoted servants of the githyanki Lich Queen Vlaakith CLVII.

Effects

The sacrifice soul spell was cast upon the corpse of a recently killed humanoid within a hundred feet away from the caster. The spell offered the dead being's soul to Vlaakith, and in exchange, she temporarily made the caster Vlaakith's undying aspect, greatly bolstering their protection as well as all physical and mental abilities. Lastly, the spell allowed the undying aspect to discharge six powerful beams of magic, similar in effect to the eldritch blast spell. When the spell was cast upon a still-living humanoid, they became afflicted by bad luck and weakened, while the opposite was the boon granted to the caster. Once the target creature was killed, the caster temporarily became Vlaakith's undying aspect.

History

In the , Tska'an, a gith assassin, was granted that spell by Vlaakith to hunt down and slay individuals marked for death for displeasing the Lich Queen. The group known as the Absolut Adversaries may have had been such targets during their travels across the blighted town of Reithwin.
