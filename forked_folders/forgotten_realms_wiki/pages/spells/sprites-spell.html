Sprites (spell)

Spell
nameSprites
school2eConjuration
level2eWizard 7

Sprites was an elven wizard spell from the school of conjuration that summoned a group of sprites.

Effects

When cast this spell instantly summoned 2–20 sprites. It remained unclear where the sprites were summoned from, as the sprite community swore they were not teleported away from their homes. Many believed the sprites were the spirits of sprite warriors who perished while serving the elves in the past.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components a piece of sprite clothing and a small silver bell to cast.

History

The origins of this spell resulted from an ancient pact made between elves and sprites.
