Speed of the zephyr

Speed of the zephyr was an utterance from the Lexicon of the Evolving Mind that used truespeak that granted agility and speed without match for a moment, or in its reversed form, reduced speed.

Usage

In its straight form, it granted a great boost to speed and the ability to walk on liquids or through even the most obstructed terrain with no challenge, even up walls for the seconds it lasted. In its reversed form, it reduced speed somewhat, making their feet feel as though they stuck to the ground.

History

The utterance was known to garblers, in its reversed form, and loquasphinxes.
