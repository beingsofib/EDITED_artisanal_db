Mass teleport was a very rare spell used by sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

This spell was identical to the teleport spell, just more powerful. It allowed the caster to teleport up to twelve creatures or objects instantly between two points. Teleportation between two separate planes was not possible.

Components

After the , this spell required only a verbal component, but previously also required a somatic component.
