Clutch ward

Spell
nameClutch Ward
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 3

Clutch ward was a spell that could only be cast by dragons. It was used to protect their eggs.

Effects

The spell was cast onto the nest itself, with the dragon handling each egg separately as the spell was cast. The ward only activated when it was triggered. This occurred when a creature either got too close to the eggs or touched one. When activated, the spell instantly transported the eggs to a predetermined location with a teleport without error spell. The dragon knew immediately that the ward was triggered, even from a great distance or while on another plane. If the spell was still in place after the eggs began to hatch, the hatch-lings did not trigger the spell, but they were not protected by it. Only a limited wish spell could deactivate the ward.

Casting

This spell required only a verbal component.
