Spirit guardians was a spell used by clerics.

Effects

When cast, this spell summoned several spirits who protected the caster. They remained within of the caster. The spirits matched the alignment of the caster. During casting, the caster could choose which creatures within the spell's area of affect would be unaffected by the spell. Otherwise, creatures within the area of effect slowed down and also suffered radiant or necrotic energy.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, the caster's holy symbol.
