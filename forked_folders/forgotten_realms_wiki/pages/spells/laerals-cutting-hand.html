Laeral's cutting hand was a spell used by sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell modified the structure of the caster's hand so it acted like a bladed weapon. It caused the same damage as a regular bladed weapon. Although the caster was unable to use this hand for casting spells that used a somatic component, they were able to use it normally otherwise.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
