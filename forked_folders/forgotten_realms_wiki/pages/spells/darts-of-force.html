Darts of force

Spell
nameDarts of force

Darts of force was a spell that sent magical projectiles towards a foe.

Description

The darts took the form of shafts of azure light that streaked towards an opponent.

Components

Darts of force only required somatic and verbal components, with no need for a spell focus.

History

In the , Pharaun Mizzrym often used such a spell. He tried to use it against the alhoon Syrzan, but it failed—the bolts stopped in the air, fell to the ground, and turned into leech- or tadpole-like things that crawled into a pool. This was because they were actually inside the illithid's mind.
