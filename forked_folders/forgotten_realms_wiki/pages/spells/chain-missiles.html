Chain missiles

Spell
nameChain Missiles
school3eEvocation
level3eSorcerer 3 Wild mage 3 Wizard 3

Chain missiles was an arcane evocation spell that blasted foes with magical darts.

Effects

The spell created magic missiles that shot off to away, unerringly striking the target. After impact, a number of smaller magic missiles burst from the target, hitting nearby foes as well. The more skilled the caster, the more missiles they could evoke at once.

Components

The spell required verbal and somatic components to cast.
