Know bloodline

Spell
nameKnow bloodline
schoolDivination
levelCleric 3
rules3.5

Know bloodline is a divination spell available to clerics. By casting this spell a cleric was able to determine the ancestry and racial type of the target.

History

Know bloodline was commonly used by Mulhorandi priests to determine the worthiness of Mulan humans following the path to becoming a glorious servitor.
