Embalm

Spell
nameEmbalm
school2eNecromancy, Alteration
level2eWizard 2

Embalm was a spell that preserve corpses for superior animation as undead.

Effects

The spell preserved the corpse permanently, making sure it always had the same odor and decayed no further, though it could not reverse putrefaction. Undead and golems made of such corpses were notably hardier, though not impossibly so.

Components

Besides verbal and somatic components, the spell called for a drop of vinegar as a material component, or in some later versions, a special unguent that cost around 100 gp to prepare.

History

The spell was in use by the Necrophant Yerian during his time at the Chantry of St. Sammaster, in the . It was already commonly used in the Cult of the Dragon.
