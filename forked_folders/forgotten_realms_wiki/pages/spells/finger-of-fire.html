Finger of Fire was a basic fire-based psionic power of the Metacreativity discipline that allowed one to manifest a mote of flames.

Effects

This power manifest motes of unstable flaring ectoplasm that explosively dissipates in flames at an area of choice, setting fire to anything flammable it touches in the process. The maximum targeting range of this power was .

Display

Manifesting this power produced a standard visual display.

Notable Users

Creatures & Sentient Races

  • Blue goblins, a psionic subrace of goblins, all had an innate ability to unleash Finger of Fire.

Individuals

  • Gaulauntyr, an adult copper dragon.
