Shark charm was a spell used by clerics of Deep Sashelas.

Effects

When cast, this spell hypnotized one or more sharks within of the caster. In addition, the spell could hypnotize creatures related to sharks such as bunyips and weresharks.

Components

This spell required verbal and somatic components.
