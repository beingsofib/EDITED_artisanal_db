Benign projection

Spell
nameBenign Projection
school3eIllusion (Shadow)
level3eSanctified 6

Benign projection was a type of sanctified spell.

Effects

This spell was almost identical to the project image spell but with some key differences. The projected image could only cast spells from specific schools of magic: abjuration, conjuration and divination.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a divine focus and a sacrifice of the caster's strength.
