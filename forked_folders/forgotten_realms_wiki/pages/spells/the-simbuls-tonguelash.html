The Simbul's tonguelash

Spell
nameThe Simbul's Tonguelash
inventorThe Simbul

The Simbul's tonguelash was a curious spell created by Alassra Shentrantra Silverhand, the Witch-Queen of Aglarond, better known as the Simbul.

Effects

When cast, the spell created a magical spectral extension of the caster's tongue. The said prehensile extension was fully controlled by the caster's will. It could perform any actions the caster could imagine with some physical limitations. The tongue could avoid danger; move akin a snake; wrap itself around something; pick up, manipulate, or throw a small object; or restrain a small object or an appendage. The Simbul herself liked to use the tonguelash to slap people's eyeballs with it, giving them a ringing headache and compromised vision due to tears. If the spectral tongue was damaged or destroyed, it disappeared without damaging the caster. The distance of the spell was the caster's eyesight.
