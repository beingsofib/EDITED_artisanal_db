Dweomerbanish

Spell
nameDweomerbanish
school2eAbjuration, Alteration, Enchantment
level2eWizard 9

Dweomerbanish was a spell that broke all ongoing magic within its area.

Effects

Within a radius, all ongoing effects were ceased, even those of scouring magic such as dispel magic, spell engine, or Mordenkainen's disjunction. Though it only struck effects longer than instantaneous, it was effective even against prismatic sphere and sphere of wonder. However, only the area intersected by the dweomerbanish's area was cleaned; this could leave some effects reduced in area without being outright eliminated. Some permanent, multifold effects, wardmists and mythals for instance, were only suppressed for about an hour instead.

The spell was ineffective against any magic cast directly by deities. Furthermore, it dealt some damage to the caster- of which, a certain amount was irreversible.

Components

Besides verbal and somatic components, the spell required two finger bones from archmages. They could belong to the same archmage.

History

Azuth made this spell available through Elminster sometime prior to .
