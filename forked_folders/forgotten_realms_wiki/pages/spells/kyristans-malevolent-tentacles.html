Kyristan's malevolent tentacles

Spell
nameKyristan's Malevolent Tentacles
inventorKyristan
school3eNecromancy
level3eSorcerer 6 Wizard 6

Kyristan's malevolent tentacles was an arcane necromancy spell that summoned negative-attuned tentacles, similar to the spell Evard's black tentacles. Like all magic whose power came from the negative energy plane, they could drain the life force from living and heal and bolster the undead.
