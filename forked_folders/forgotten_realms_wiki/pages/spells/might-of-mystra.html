Might of Mystra was a multi-school divine spell that allowed a cleric to replicate the effects of another spell.

Effects

Once cast, the magic of might of Mystra remained in effect for a single day. If the caster saw another spell being cast during this day, they could choose to replicate its effects immediately afterwards.

History

The specifications of this spell could be displayed by the relic known as the Scepter of Mystra.

Components

This spell required verbal and somatic components in order to be cast.
