Wall against noise

Spell
nameWall against noise
school2eAlteration, Wind
level2eWizard 2

Wall against noise was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Effects

This spell created a wall of air that prevented sound from passing through. The wall blocked any sound-based attacks, such as a harpy's cry or a banshee's wail.

Components

This spell required a white feather to cast.

Rumors & Legends

Some ancient tales in Zakhara spoke of great cities ruled by marids that used walls against noise to protect their citadels against auditory-based magical items, such as drums of panic and horns of blasting.
