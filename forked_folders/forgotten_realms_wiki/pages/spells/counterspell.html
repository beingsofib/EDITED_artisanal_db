Counterspell was an abjuration spell that attempted to stop another spell from being cast.

Effects

If a creature within 60 feet (18.3 meters) of the caster attempted to cast a spell, then the caster could make an instant reaction to attempt to counter it. If the spell was of the same spell level as counterspell, then it would end, but if it was more powerful, it had a chance of failing. If the caster wanted to interrupt a spell of higher level she could cast counterspell as a higher level spell, if the targeted spell had the same level or lower then it would be stopped.

Components

Only a somatic component was needed.
