Sun bolt

Spell
nameSun Bolt
school3eEvocation
level3eCleric 2 Sorcerer 2 Wizard 2

Sun bolt was a spell that caused a ray of burning sunlight to shoot forth from the caster's hands. It caused temporary blindness and was especially potent against the undead, oozes, slimes and other creatures vulnerable to sunlight.
