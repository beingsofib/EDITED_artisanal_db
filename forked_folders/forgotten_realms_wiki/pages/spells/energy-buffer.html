Energy buffer was a spell that protected a caster from elementals.

Effects

When cast, this spell protected a caster and their equipment from energy-based attacks used by elementals. The spell absorbed damage from acid, cold, electricity, fire or sonic based spells. When an energy-based attack struck the caster, a multi-colored aura was visible around them. This spell was able to be used in conjunction with other elemental defense spells. The spell could only by cast on the caster.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
