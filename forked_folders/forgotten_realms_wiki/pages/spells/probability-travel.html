Probability travel was a major psionic power of the psychoportation discipline that allowed the manifester to travel to other planes of reality.

Effects

Early records suggested that users of probability travel were able to cross into other realities, some of them alternative, and some reachable through the ethereal plane. Novice manifesters were only able to transport themselves, and went off-course about one in five times. With more progress, they became more adept, being able to bring first two people with themselves, and ultimately eight at the peak of their psionic practice.

The earliest records also asserted that, when used at the moment when one was struck by a sphere of annihilation, this science allowed the manifester to destroy the sphere harmlessly and gain an additional power—if they survived.

The power could also be used to arrive in the astral plane bodily, and find one's way to alternate Prime Material planes that had color pools in the astral plane. As there was no projection, no silver cord was manifested, and the risks were commensurate.

Records post-dating the Time of Troubles referred to powers such as this as being unable to access the ethereal plane.

Display

This power had no psionic display when manifested.

Users

This power was knowable to all psions

Probability travel was exhibited as an innate psionic ability by baku, cerebral parasites, dhours, opinicuses, and shedu.

In psionics terms, it could be known by githzerai, mind flayers and alhoons, ki-rin, and phoenixes, as well as nabassu and yochlol among the tanar'ri. When exhibited by nabassu, they were noted as being able to bring others with them.

A staff of the magi was said to use similar powers, per some early sources.

History

The illithid that attacked the Emerald Dragon in the planned to escape with probability travel if things got too dicey for it.

Known/Notable Users

  • Charon
  • Lolth
  • Lugribossk, a proxy of the illithid god Ilsensine.

Rumors & Legends

Some ancient stories claimed that the rutterkin were descended from a race of humans from a distant corner of the Prime Material plane, who discovered the Abyss and its tanar'ri as a result of experimenting with planar travel and probability travel.
