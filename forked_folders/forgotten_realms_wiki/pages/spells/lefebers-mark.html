Lefeber's mark was an inventive arcanist spell of small power. It was developed by inventor Lefeber in 1725 NY (−2134 DR) during Netheril's Golden Age.

Knowledge of its effect was lost in later ages, but given its name and comparable power it is possible that Lefeber's mark was the Netherese name for the spell wizard mark.
