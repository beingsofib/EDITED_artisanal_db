Thump (cantrip)

Spell
nameThump
school1eIllusion
level1eMagic-user 0
type1eHaunting

Thump was a haunting cantrip that acolyte wizards could learn. It created an auditory illusion of a heavy object falling a short distance.

Effects

This cantrip was a variation of groan that simulated the sound of a weighty object falling a distance of about . The range of this spell was from the caster and the noise could appear to come from behind, under, or inside other things. All creatures in close proximity that could be expected to hear the sound had a chance to resist the spell. If successful, they not aware of the illusion.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster imagined the desired sound in their head, then swallowed loudly and pointed at the target location.
