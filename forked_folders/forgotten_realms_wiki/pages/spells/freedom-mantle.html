Freedom mantle

The Freedom mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concept or philosophy of freedom.

Abilities

Those who followed the freedom mantle were faster and fleeter of foot while psionically focused. Additionally, they could expend a psionic focus to enhance their ability to escape or outright evade a case of grappling or constriction.

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the freedom mantle similarly made the manifester and all allies within the radius of the aura more skilled escape artists, better capable of escaping bondage and avoiding it outright.

Powers

The powers granted by the Freedom mantle were largely of the psychoportation discipline, owing to its focus evading imprisonment, as well as two from the psychometabolism discipline.

Novice manifesters were granted greater movement with the psychometabolism power hustle and minor teleportation with dimension hop. Manifesters of some experience gained the power of psionic flight and rendering themselves incapable of being immobilized with psionic freedom of movement. As a manifester progressed further they gained the more potent teleporting power psionic teleport and psychometabolic power evade burst. Masters of this mantle reached the pinnacle of teleportation power psionic greater teleport.
