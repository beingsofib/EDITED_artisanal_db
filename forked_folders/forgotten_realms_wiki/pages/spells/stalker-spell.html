Stalker was a special divine conjuration quest spell from the creation, guardian, and plant spheres that essentially summoned a group of shambling mounds to serve the caster.

Effects

When this spell was cast 3–7 plant creatures resembling shambling mounds were conjured to aid the caster. The conjured plant creatures would remain with the priest for a full week unless dismissed early. If summoned specifically for guard duty, the stalkers would stand guard for up to seven months provided their duties only included guarding a specific location.

Components

This spell did not require spell components of any kind.

History

This spell was used by druids who served Moander during the height of ancient Netheril's power.
