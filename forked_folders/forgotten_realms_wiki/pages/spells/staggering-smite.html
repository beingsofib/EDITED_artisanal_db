Staggering smite

Spell
nameStaggering Smite
school5eEvocation
level5ePaladin 4

Staggering smite was a spell used by paladins.

Effects

This spell was cast while the caster wielded a melee weapon. The weapon became temporarily enhanced and inflicted psychic damage to a target when used immediately after the spell was cast.

Components

This spell required only a verbal component.
