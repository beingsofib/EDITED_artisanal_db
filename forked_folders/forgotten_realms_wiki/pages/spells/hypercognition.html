Standard power

Metafaculty was a psionic power of the clairsentience discipline that allowed a manifester to enhance the logic centers of their mind to generate rapid-fire deductions concerning any conundrum.

Effects

This power forced the user's mind into undergoing rigorous logic process, combing through their memories for the most minute scraps of information regarding a topic and correlating them to form reasonable conclusions. It could potentially even extract echoes of knowledge from the Astral Plane. Through this a psionics user could do such things as determine the path out of a maze, the answer to a riddle, or come to a conclusion regarding a dilemma that their conscious mind was unable to find.

Displays

When used this power manifested a mental display.

Users

This power could be known by seers, as well as ardents and divine minds that followed the Mental Power mantle.

Known/Notable Users

  • The Mind Shard of Pandorym, the imprisoned form of the eponymous elder evil.
