Ignite flame

Ignite flame was a common evocation spell of the southern magic tradition.

Effects

The spell created a small, hot flame. The maximum size of the flame was 3 square-inch (7.6 square-centimeter) and could be cast in a 10 yd (9 m) radius from the caster. The spell would automatically destroy certain flammable materials, such as paper. A creature targeted by the spell could smother the fire before the flame spread. The fire produced by the spell was not magical.

Components

The spell required only verbal and somatic components.
