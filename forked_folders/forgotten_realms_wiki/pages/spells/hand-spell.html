Hand (spell)

Spell
nameHand
school2eInvocation/Evocation
level2eWizard 2

Hand was a spell that could only be cast by dragons. It allowed a dragon who was unable to polymorph to perform tasks requiring precise movements or manipulations.

Effects

When cast, the spell created a human-sized hand that glowed softly. The hand was able to lift up to in weight. The hand had to remain within sight of the dragon, if the line of sight was blocked by an object, the spell expired. The hand was unable to throw an object or use a weapon.

Casting

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
