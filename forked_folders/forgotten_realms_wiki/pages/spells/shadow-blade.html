Shadow blade

Spell
nameShadow Blade
school5eIllusion
level5eSorcerer 2 Warlock 2 Wizard 2

Shadow blade was an illusion spell that created a sword made of solidified shadow.

Effects

The spell condensed the shadows to create a lightweight sword that inflicted damage upon the minds of the creatures it hit. The sword lasted for up to 1 minute, as long as the caster kept concentrating. It was especially accurate in poorly lit environments and became deadlier the more powerful the caster became. If thrown toward an opponent, the blade magically reappeared in the caster's hand at a command.

Components

The spell required only verbal and somatic components.
