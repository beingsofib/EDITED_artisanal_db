Sandspray

Spell
nameSandspray
school2eAlteration, Sand
level2eWizard 3

Sandspray was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Effects

With this spell the caster caused pillar of sand to explode upward beneath a target. The pillar rose to high, blinding the target for several minutes and causing abrasive damage based on the magical might of the caster.

This spell could only be cast in an area of plentiful sand.

Components

This spell required sand to cast.
