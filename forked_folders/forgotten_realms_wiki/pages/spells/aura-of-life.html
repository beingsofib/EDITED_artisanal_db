Aura of life was a divine abjuration spell that generated an aura of life preservation.

Effects

The caster created an aura with a radius that preserved the life of the caster and all allies within it. The aura granted resistance to necrotic energies and restored unconscious non-hostiles. The aura traveled with the caster.

Components

The spell required only a verbal component.
