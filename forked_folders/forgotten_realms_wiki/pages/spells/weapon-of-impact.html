Weapon of impact was a spell used on weapons.

Effects

This spell was cast onto a blunt weapon, such as a hammer or staff. It could also be cast on blunt missiles such as stones used with a sling. The spell significantly increased the impact strength of the weapon or projectile. The spell could not be cast on missiles or weapons that pierced or slashed with a sharp edge.

Components

It required verbal and somatic components.
