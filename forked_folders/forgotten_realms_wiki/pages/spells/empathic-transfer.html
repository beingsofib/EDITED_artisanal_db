Empathic transfer was a psionic power of the psychometabolism discipline that allowed a manifester to take on the injuries of another.

Effects

By touching a willing creature, the manifester empathically transferred a set amount of injuries and pain from the target unto themselves. Due to the manner in which this power worked, neither powers nor abilities with regenerative or harm reductive effects could dampen these injuries during transfer. such as body adjustment.

Alternatively, empathic transfer could be used by a manifester to transfer to themselves a single form of disease or poison the target creature was afflicted with.

Displays

When used this power manifested an auditory and material display.

Users

This power was knowable by egoists, psychic warriors, as well as ardents and divine minds that followed either the Good or Repose psionic mantles.
