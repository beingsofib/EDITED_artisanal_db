Mouse (cantrip)

Spell
nameMouse
school1eSummoning
level1eMagic-user 0
type1ePersonal

Mouse was a personal cantrip that initiate wizards could learn. It summoned a common, normal-sized mouse to a nearby location chosen by the caster.

Effects

This cantrip conjured a single common field mouse or a closely related species from the surrounding area. It appeared at a spot within of the caster. The caster had no control over the rodent, and it behaved as a creature was inclined to do in the circumstances in which it found itself.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster placed both hands on the head, angled to mimic mouse ears, and spoke the name of a food item preferred by mice.
