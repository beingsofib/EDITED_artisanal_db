Fire trap was both an abjuration and evocation spell that could be used on any closeable item, installing a trap that caused a small fiery explosion upon unauthorized entry.

History

This spell was invented by the arcanist and Fire Warden of Netheril Noanar in . It was the first of his many fire-based spells, and originally called Noanar's trap.
