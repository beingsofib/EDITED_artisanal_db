Dragon knight

nameDragon Knight

type3eEpic

Dragon knight was a rare epic spell that allowed for the summoning of an adult red dragon at a location chosen by the spell caster.

Effects

As of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR, casting the spell led to the summoning of an adult chaotic evil red dragon. The dragon had to be summoned within 8 meters (26 feet) of the caster. The dragon fought alongside the caster.

The presence of the dragon lasted around two minutes, after which it vanished. The spell could not be countered, interrupted, or dispelled using normal spells.

Development

Before it could be cast, dragon knight had to be developed. Both divine and arcane casters could develop this spell. Developing an epic spell was different from learning normal spells. Once an epic spell was developed, the caster knew the spell. A developed epic spell became a part of the caster and could even be prepared without a spellbook (if a wizard was the caster). Only those well-versed in spellcraft could develop dragon knight.

Components

The spell required verbal and somatic components.
