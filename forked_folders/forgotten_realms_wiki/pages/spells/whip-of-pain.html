Whip of pain

Whip of pain was a divine necromancy spell used by priests devoted to Loviatar, enabling them to create a whiplike line of force.

Effects

This spell created a thin lines of force extending from the caster's fingertips. The whips crackled with purple energy and could strike targets up to distant. The whips made loud cracking sounds each time they struck and harmed a target, wracking them with intense pain.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components a drop of unholy water and one of the caster's hairs to cast.
