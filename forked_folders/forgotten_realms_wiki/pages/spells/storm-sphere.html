Storm sphere

Spell
nameStorm Sphere
school5eEvocation
level5eSorcerer 4 Wizard 4

Storm sphere was an evocation spell that created a ferocious spherical storm of loud winds and lightning.

Effects

The spell created a storm inside a radius of centered at at point within of the caster that lasted for up to one minute, or as long as the caster kept concentrating. Anyone caught within the storm was immediately bashed by violent winds that grew stronger the more powerful the caster. In addition, the storm generated bolts of lightning at the caster's command, directed at creatures as far as from the storm's center.

Anyone nearby was partially deafened by the storm's loud noise.

Components

The spell required only verbal and somatic components.
