Force mantle

The Force mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concept of force energy.

Abilities

Those who followed the force mantle slightly took on the deflective qualities of force energy while psionically focused.

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the force mantle required the expenditure of a psionic focus to activate. Once done, the manifester and all allies within the radius of the aura became capable of striking at incorporeal creatures.

Powers

All of the powers granted by this mantle were of the psychokinesis discipline.
