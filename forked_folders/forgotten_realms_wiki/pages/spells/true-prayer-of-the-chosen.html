True prayer of the chosen

Spell
nametrue prayer of the chosen
school3eTransmutation
level3eCleric 3 Paladin 4

True prayer of the chosen was a spell that used truespeak to shield one creature with divine insight.

Effects

Those protected with the true prayer of the chosen were more difficult to strike or to affect due to a golden coruscation around them, through which a deity gave them divine insight into the dangers they faced.

Components

Besides a holy symbol and a verbal component, the caster needed to speak their own personal true name, a task involving great truespeak skill.

History

This spell was known to all brimstone speakers.
