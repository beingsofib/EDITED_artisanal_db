Knowledge mantle

The Knowledge mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionic powers and abilities derived from the universal concept of knowledge.

Abilities

Those who followed the Knowledge mantle became well-versed in a wide variety of fields of study while psionically focused, allowing them to conceptualize information from their mind on topics they otherwise weren't trained in, Additionally, by expending a psionic focus, a manifester could drive their power of the mind to obtain some fact or another on a particular topic of study.

Divine minds of the Knowledge mantle exuded a slightly similar effect with their psychic aura, making them and any allies within the radius of their aura minutely more capable of realizing and conceptualizing information on various topics.

Powers

Novice manifesters had access to a wide variety of powers. They could enhance their already impressive knowledge of fields of study with call to mind, determine where they were with know direction and location, or recognize the presence of psionics and teleportation. Manifesters of some experience could draw pyschic impressions with object reading, recognize hostile beings with detect hostile intent, and discern the properties of psionic items with psionic identify. As a manifester progressed their powers of detection grew ever stronger, learning detect remote viewing, touchsight, and trace teleport. Masters of this mantle obtained psychometry, allowing them to draw psychic impressions of the past from entire locations.
