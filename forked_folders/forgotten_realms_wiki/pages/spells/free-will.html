Free will

nameFree Will
deityUbtao

level2eCleric 2

Free will was a divine enchantment spell used only by shamans devoted to Ubtao called spiritlords.

Effects

This spell freed the target's mind from any magical charms, enchantments, or psionic intrusions affecting the will of the mind. Spiritlords who prayed for and received this spell, but had yet to cast it could do so immediately in response of an incoming attack on their mind.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
