Mestil's acid breath was an arcane spell of the conjuration school of magic practiced by wizards and sorcerers after the .

Effects

The caster breathed out acid in the form of a cone of droplets to a distance of at least 30 feet (9.1 meters), burning all caught within who could not avoid it. It increased in range and potency with the prowess of the caster.

Components

It required verbal, somatic, and material components. The latter was a handful of fire ants, whether dead or living.
