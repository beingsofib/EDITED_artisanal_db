Minor malison

Spell
nameMinor Malison
school2eEnchantment
level2eWizard 3

Minor malison was an enchantment spell used by wizards.

Effects

This spell allowed the caster to hinder a target's chance of resisting spell effects from a particular school of magic.

Components

This spell only required verbal components to cast.

History

This spell was originally developed in the Underdark, but later on found usage by some on the surface.
