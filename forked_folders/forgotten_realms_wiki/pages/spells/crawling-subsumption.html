Crawling subsumption

Spell
nameCrawling Subsumption
inventorMasked One

Crawling subsumption was a frightful, unique spell that assimilated the target's powers.

Effects

If successful, the spell would have stolen the target's powers, and probably subsumed its mind, too.

History

This spell was used by a Red Wizard known only as the Masked One in the lead-up to the Time of Troubles in the . He developed the power to dissolve individuals and subsume their power, his only real accomplishment.
