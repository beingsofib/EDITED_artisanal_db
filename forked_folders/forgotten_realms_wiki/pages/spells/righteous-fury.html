Righteous fury

Spell
nameRighteous Fury
school3eTransmutation
level3ePaladin 3

Righteous fury was a transmutation spell used by paladins..

Effects

Paladins used this spell fill their body with positive energy granted from their deity. The positive energy provided temporary boosts to health, strength, dexterity and physical hardiness. Additionally, any undead striking a paladin protected by righteous fury was harmed when the positive energy lashed out at them.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
