Inflict minor wounds

Spell
nameInflict Minor Wounds
school3eNecromancy
level3eCleric 0

Inflict minor wounds was a necromancy spell that generally evil clerics learned as an orison. It delivered the minimum noticeable amount of damage to a living creature.

Effects

This spell function much like inflict light wounds, requiring the caster to touch the creature being harmed. This spell could be used to do the minimum amount of healing to undead.
