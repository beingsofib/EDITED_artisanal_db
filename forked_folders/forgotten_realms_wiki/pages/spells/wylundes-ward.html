Wylunde's ward was a defensive wizard spell created by the adventurer Wylunde.

Effects

At the utterance of a single word, this spell erected an shimmering energy barrier around the caster, protecting them from the brunt of physical or magical harm for several minutes before expiring.

If cast again within 24 hours of the first casting, Wylunde's ward would have the opposite effect and actually increase any harm befalling the caster. If cast a third time, the caster suffered wracking pain that could prove fatal.

Components

This spell only required verbal components to cast.
