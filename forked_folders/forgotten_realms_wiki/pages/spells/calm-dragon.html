Calm – dragon

Spell
nameCalm – Dragon
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 2

Calm was an arcane dragon spell from the alteration school that allowed the casting dragon to cancel the effects of their fear aura.

Effects

Calm lasted as long as the dragon concentrated on maintaining the spell's magic.

Components

This spell required only verbal components to cast.
