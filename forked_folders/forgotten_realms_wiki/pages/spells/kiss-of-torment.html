Kiss of torment was a divine necromancy spell used by priests devoted to Loviatar, enabling them to attack a target's nervous system with a touch.

Effects

This spell allowed the caster to attack a target's nervous system by touching their skin, overwhelming them with phantom pain that caused a great deal of physical harm and caused them to convulse uncontrollably.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
