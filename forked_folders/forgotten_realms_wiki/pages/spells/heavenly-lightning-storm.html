Heavenly lightning storm was an evocation spell that shocked targets with divine lightning. It was a more powerful version of heavenly lightning.

Effect

Functioning like heavenly lightning, this created a storm of arcs of radiant lightning at a more distant range and over a greater area, and target twice as many creatures. They were shocked with a unique celestial electricity.
