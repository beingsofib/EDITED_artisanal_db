Sheela's entangle was a spell used by clerics of Sheela Peryroyl.

Effects

This spell had the same effects of the entangle spell. It could be cast up to from the caster and affected an area of . What distinguished this spell from entangle was that the effects of this spell were cumulative, therefore could be cast several times within the same area.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required material components, the holy symbol of Sheela Peryroyl and a piece of vine.
