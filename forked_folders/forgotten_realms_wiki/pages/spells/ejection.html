Ejection

Psionic Power
nameEjection
discipline2eTelepathy
level2eMajor (Science)
type2eStandard power

Ejection was a psionic science of the telepathy discipline. This major power was often considered to be a psionicist's final defensive measure against telepathic intrusion, allowing them to eject another psion from their mind.

Effects

Though a pivotal tool of defense in the arsenal of many psionicists, use of this power was not without risk. Among the possible side-effects, a psionicist might find themselves unable to a single psionic devotion, or any of their psionic sciences, for 1 to 10 hours. A user might even risk causing permanent damage to their physical health.

All potential side-effects of ejection could be circumvented through use of the spell mind mantle.

Users

Ejection was innately known by emerald dragons.

Known/Notable Users

  • Arelisa, a githzerai psion and elite spy.
