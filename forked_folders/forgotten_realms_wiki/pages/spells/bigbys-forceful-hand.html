Bigby's forceful hand was an evocation spell similar to Bigby's interposing hand. To Zakharan mages, always reluctant to acknowledge ajami names and accomplishments, the spell was known simply as forceful hand, and counted among the spells of the universal province.

Effects

When cast, this spell created a hand of force. This hand followed and shoved at a targeted creature, even a very strong one. The hand would push the targeted creature right to the distance limit of the spell. The spell was strong enough to slow a moving spelljammer.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a strong glove made from either cloth or leather.
