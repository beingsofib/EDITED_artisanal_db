Absolute immunity

Spell
nameAbsolute Immunity
school2eAbjuration
level2eWizard 9

Absolute immunity was an incredibly powerful spell used by wizards for defense.

Effects

When cast, this spell protected the caster from all mundane and magical weapons. Only powerful enchantments on a magical weapon were able to defeat this spell. Both the casting time and the duration of this spell were incredibly short.
