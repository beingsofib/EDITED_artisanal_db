Improved haste was a spell used by wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell increased a creature's movement speed and stamina. The caster could cast it on themselves or another creature. The spell did not alter the speed of casting another spell. After the spell expired, the affected creature felt no fatigue. This spell removed the affects of a slow spell. It could not be combined with other movement-enhancing spells.
