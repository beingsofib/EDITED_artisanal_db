Vampiric embrace

typeencounter
nameVampiric Embrace
level4eWarlock 1
type4eEncounter

Vampiric embrace is a simple but deadly spell used most often by infernal pact warlocks, though other warlocks may also learn it. When cast vampiric embrace allows its caster to extend a strand of necrotic energy into the life force of a creature within twenty-five feet, sapping its vitality to temporarily fuel the warlock's own. The potency of the spell is largely determined by the warlock's regular health, and those of the infernal pact use an even deadlier version of the spell. The spell does not come easily, however, and a warlock must rest for a few minutes after using it if he or she wishes to cast it again. Vampiric embrace is similar, but not identical, to the devour magic spell.
