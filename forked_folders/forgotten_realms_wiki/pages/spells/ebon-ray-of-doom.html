Ebon ray of doom

Spell
nameEbon Ray of Doom
levelAssassin 3 Sorcerer 4 Wizard 4
schoolNecromancy
rules3rd

When ebon ray of doom was cast, a ray of black energy leaped from the caster's hand to the target. The victim's ability to heal was immensely reduced by the spell.
