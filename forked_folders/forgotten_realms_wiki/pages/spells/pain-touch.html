Pain touch

Spell
namePain Touch
school2eNecromancy
level2eWizard 3

Pain touch was an arcane spell from the school of necromancy that allowed a wizard to induce extreme pain with a touch.

Powers

The caster could affect a single target once they touched an exposed part of their skin. The wracking pain made the victim more clumsy and easier to strike in combat.

Components

In addition to verbal components, this spell required as material components a needle and the finger from a burned glove to cast.

History

This spell was found within the Book of Shadows, a tome based upon the oration of the necromancer Nebt Bhakau of Zakhara.
