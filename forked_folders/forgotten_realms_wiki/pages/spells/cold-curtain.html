Cold curtain

Spell
nameCold Curtain
school2eNecromancy
level2eWizard 8

Cold curtain was a powerful arcane necromancy spell that warded portals with deadly magic.

Effects

When cast upon any portal, cold curtain would attack any creature passing through who did not speak the proper pass phrase with energy drain magic. Casting this spell upon a portal would noticeable lower the temperature in the general area. Targets caught within the portal would continue to suffer the ill effects of the energy drain for as long as they remained in the area.

When casting this spell the caster could choose whether they wanted the warding magic of the spell to be visible or not on the portal.

Components

This spell required only verbal components to cast.

History

This was a rare spell used only by dragons in Faerûn.
