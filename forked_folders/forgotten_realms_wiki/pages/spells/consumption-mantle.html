Consumption mantle

The Consumption mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concept or philosophy of consumption, themed around parasitically drawing power from other creatures.

Abilities

Those who followed the consumption mantle gained the unique psionic power to expend a psionic focus to moderately heal their own wounds when within of a wounded creature.

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the consumption mantle allowed the manifester to expend a psionic focus whenever a power was manifested within the radius of their aura. When done, this leeched psionic energy away from the manifestation and added it to the divine mind's mental reserve.

Powers

Novice manifesters gained a deleterious, self-healing touch with hungry touch and the power to temporarily exhibit the unique psionic abilities of others with feat leech. Manifesters of some experience could steal the psychic energy of other manifesters with power leech. As a manifester progressed further they could temporarily steal another manifester's power with power thief and cause significant harm to a creature's brain with decerebrate. Masters of this mantle obtained the power apopsi, allowing them to outright remove the powers ingrained within a psionics user's mind.
