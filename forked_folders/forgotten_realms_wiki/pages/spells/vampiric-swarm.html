Vampiric swarm

Spell
nameVampiric Swarm
school5eConjuration

Vampiric swarm was a powerful conjuration spell from the 6 circle of magic used by the vampire lord Cazador Szarr.

Effects

When the spell was cast, it conjured forth a swarm of vampire bats that were sicked on a target within of the caster, and the swarm attacked all creatures within the -cube. The swarm health significant damage to all creatures that failed to dodge the critters and pushed them away from the caster. Some creatures trapped within the spell's area of effect could be swarmed by the summoned vampire bats. Such swarmed creatures became temporarily vulnerable to piercing vampire bat bites, and the caster received healing from the swarm through the life force being sapped from the victims.

The spell required no somatic components and could be cast when silenced.

History

Circa the , this powerful spell was known to Cazador Szarr of Baldur's Gate, who sought to perform an infernal ritual to become a vampire ascendant.
