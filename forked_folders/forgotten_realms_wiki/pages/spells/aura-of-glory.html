Aura of glory

Spell
nameAura of Glory
school3eTransmutation
level3ePaladin 2

Aura of glory was a spell used by paladins.

Effects

When cast, this spell allowed a caster increase their magical abilities and physical presence. The spell achieved this by channelling divine energy into the caster. Allies of the caster within were healed, the equivalent of a cure minor wounds spell.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a divine focus.
