G'elsewhere chant

Spell
nameG'elsewhere Chant
school3eTransmutation
level3eBard 3

G'elsewhere chant was a spell used by bards.

Effects

This spell allowed the caster to teleport to another location. The spell randomly selected a safe location to teleport to. This location was up to from the caster and had to be within visual range.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
