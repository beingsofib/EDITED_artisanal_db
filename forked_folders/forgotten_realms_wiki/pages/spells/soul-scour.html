Soul scour

Spell
nameSoul Scour
school3eNecromancy
level3eCleric 5

Soul scour was a necromancy spell that allowed the caster to corrupt the soul of another living being on physical contact.

Effects

Upon casting the spell, the caster's mere touch temporarily drained the essence of any living person or creature. After contact, the mental faculties and individualism of that person were drained from them over the course of a minute.

Casting

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required the use of a pinch of dust comprising the ground bones of a lich.
