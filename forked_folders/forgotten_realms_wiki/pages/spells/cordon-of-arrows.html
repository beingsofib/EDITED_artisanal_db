Cordon of arrows

Cordon of arrows was a transmutation spell that made a number of missiles lie in wait for a triggering target.

Effect

The caster placed four non-magical bolts or arrows in the ground. These waited until a target moved into a range of 30 feet (9.1 meters) before striking the target. When casting the spell, the caster could designate what type of creatures the spell would not attack. The spell would last for 8 hours. Missiles used would be destroyed after being fired at a target and the spell ended when no missiles remained.

If used with a higher level, more missiles could be used.

Components

This spell needed verbal and somatic components and four or more bolts or arrows.
