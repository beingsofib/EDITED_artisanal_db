Chill was a southern magic divine spell available to worshipers of Osiris.

Effects

When cast, the spell saturated an area with chilling, cold air. The spell affected an area with a maximum radius. The spell also allowed to focus the cold air to surround a single creature.

Components

In addition to somatic and verbal components, this spell required a material component, a feather.
