Nature's beauty was a spell used by clerics.

Effects

When cast, this spell altered the caster's appearance to become the very idea of beauty. The illusion lasted only for a few seconds. Every creature within of the caster were struck with temporary blindness. There was also a chance an affected creature would yearn for the illusion after the spell expired, leading to their death.
