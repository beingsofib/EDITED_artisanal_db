Far hand was a basic psionic power of the psychokinesis discipline that enabled a minor form of telekinesis.

Effects

For a period of up to a minute of concentration, this power allowed a psion to mentally lift, move, and propel mundane objects, weighing around , anywhere within . This power would cease if the distance between the psion and object ever exceeded this range. Through exerting themselves mentally, psions were capable of expanding this power's range in increments of and the power's weight limit in increments of .

Display

Manifesting this power produced a standard visual display.

Users

Far hand was found to be an innate psionic ability for blue goblins, githyanki, and tsucora quori.
