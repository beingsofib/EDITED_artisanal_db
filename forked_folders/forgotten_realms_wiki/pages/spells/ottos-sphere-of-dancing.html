Otto's sphere of dancing was an arcane enchantment spell that produced a spherical area of compulsive dancing.

Effects

The spell created a glowing point in the air, from which emanated a mind-affecting sphere of magical energy that compelled all within it to dance, akin to Otto's irresistible dance. The sphere of dancing could be blown away and dispersed by the effects of spells like cyclonic blast and gust of wind.

Components

The spell required only a verbal component to cast.
