Songbird (spell)

Spell
nameSongbird
school3eTransmutation
level3eBard 0

Songbird was a simple spell used by bards.

Effects

When cast, this spell temporarily increased the caster's charisma while performing. Anyone who heard or saw the caster's performance was affected by the spell. The effect lasted for up to one hour after the spell expired.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
