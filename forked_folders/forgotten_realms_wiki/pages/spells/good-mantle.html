Good mantle

The Good mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concept of good.

Abilities

Manifesters of this mantle gained the unique psionic ability to expend their psionic focus to cause greater harm to any creature that wasn't good-aligned with their next attack using a melee weapon, ranged weapon, or spell.

Powers

Manifesters were able to guard against evil creatures with psionic protection from evil, take on the burdens of others with empathic transfer, and temporarily make themselves celestial with planar apotheosis. Manifesters of some experience obtained the power to launch positive energy-tinged ectoplasm with celestial conduit and temporarily become half-celestial with planar embrace. Masters of this mantle obtained the power of greater glory, giving good-aligned creatures the upper hand in battle against evil-aligned creatures.
