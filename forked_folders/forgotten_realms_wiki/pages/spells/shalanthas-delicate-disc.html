Shalantha's delicate disc

Spell
nameShalantha's delicate disc
schoolConjuration (Creation)
inventorShalantha
levelSorcerer 6 Wizard 6
rules3.5

Shalantha's delicate disc was a conjuration spell that caused a small disk to appear that looked like it was made of rose-colored eggshell. A second spell was then cast which the disk absorbed. This spell was released if the disk was ever broken.

History

It was presumably developed by Shalantha.
