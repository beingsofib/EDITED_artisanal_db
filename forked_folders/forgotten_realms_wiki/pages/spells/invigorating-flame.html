Invigorating flame was a unique and strangely specific conjuration spell that empowered defeated grease elementals, created by Aelis Siryasius the mad made of the Baldurian sewers sometime in the late 15 century DR.

Effects

This spell could be cast upon the corpses of defeated grease elementals, such as grease mephits and grease elementals, within 60 ft. and visible to the caster. When the spell was cast, it ignited grease-filled corpses with revitalising fires, returning them to life as more powerful flame elemental creatures. Grease mephit were resurrected as magma mephits and grease elementals as lava elementals. The more powerful the caster was, the more powerful the elementals became.
