Dragonblood affinity

Spell
nameDragonblood Affinity
school3eTransmutation
level3eBard 3 Sorcerer 3

Dragonblood affinity was a spell that was used by dragons.

Effects

When cast, the spell significantly increased the strength of a targeted dragonic creature. This spell could only be cast by dragons or other draconic creatures.

Components

This spell required verbal and somatic components.
