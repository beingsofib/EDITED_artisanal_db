Cometfall

Spell
nameCometfall
school3eConjuration (Creation)
level3eCleric 6 Favored soul 6

Cometfall was a divine conjuration spell that created a large celestial missile to impact the target.

Effects

Upon casting, the spell created brightly-glowing comet in the air—a ball of rock and ice—and immediately sent it hurtling down at the victim. It crashed into the ground and shattered on impact, sending debris everywhere and likely crushing or knocking down anyone there.

Components

The spell required verbal and somatic components to cast.
