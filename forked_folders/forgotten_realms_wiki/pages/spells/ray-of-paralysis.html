Ray of paralysis

Spell
nameRay of Paralysis
school2eNecromancy
level2eWizard 3

Ray of paralysis was a necromancy spell that materialized a narrow beam of energy that caused paralysis within a single target creature or person.

Effects

The ray emanated from the caster, appearing as a green beam of light the width of an arrow shaft that shot forth in a straight path towards the spell's target. If the target dodged the ray of paralysis,

Casting

The casting of this spell required verbal and somatic components.

History

This spell was known to be used by wizards of the Cult of the Dragon.
