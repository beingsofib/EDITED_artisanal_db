Tempestcone

Spell
nameTempestcone
school2eAlteration
Evocation
level2eWizard 9

Tempestcone was a powerful alteration and evocation spell used by wizards.

Effects

This spell created and upright cone of shadowy force surrounding either the caster or a target they touched. The tempestcone drank all incoming magical energy for any source, converting the energy into magic missiles. The magic missiles would be hurled unerringly at any target within . A quick mental command was all that was needed to hurl the missiles. This could be done even if the being inside of the tempestcone was not a spellcaster. The number of magic missiles created depended on the strength of the spell they absorbed, but even the weakest spell would create a pair of magic missiles.

If on solid ground, the tempestcone would come to a point a few feet about the caster's head and terminate at the ground. If the caster was flying or not standing on solid ground, the tempestcone would end just below the lowest part of their body.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
