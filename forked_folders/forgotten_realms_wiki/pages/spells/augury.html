Augury () was a divination spell that told if a proposed action would have a positive or negative outcome.

Effects

After the Second Sundering, the spell was altered little: after casting a second time or more before a long rest the spell became about 25% more unreliable. When the wrong answer was to be given, the spell gave any one of the answers.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required material components, a set of marked sticks, bones, or similar tokens of at least 25 gp value. The shugenja version of this spell required a pair of straight sticks from a willow or carved bones.
