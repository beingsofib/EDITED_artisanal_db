Curse of ill fortune was a spell used by clerics of Beshaba.

Effects

When cast, this spell temporarily cursed a targeted creature. The curse generally weakened the target. The curse could be lifted by any spell that also removed bestow curse.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a divine focus.
