Distort speech

Spell
nameDistort Speech
school3eTransmutation
level3eBard 1

Distort speech was a bard transmutation spell that warped a creature's voice.

Effects

It distorted the voice of any target creature to the point of incomprehensibility. Spellcasters affected by this spell could not cast spells that required speech.
