Dread seizure

Spell
nameDread Seizure
level3eWarlock 2
type3eLesser invocation

Entropic warding was a defensive warlock invocation designed to slow an opponent and weaken their attacks.

Effect

When the spell was invoked, targeting a single creature within from the caster, it exploded in pain within the target's muscles. The pain caused seizures, causing the creature to move slowly and rendering their attacks weaker and harder to land. The invocation lasted for around 35 seconds. Creatures of good fortitude could resist the spell's effects.
