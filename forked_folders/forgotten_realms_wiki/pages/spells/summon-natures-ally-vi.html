Summon nature's ally VI

Spell
nameSummon Nature's Ally VI
school3eConjuration (Summoning)
level3eDruid 6

Summon nature's ally VI was one of the series of summon nature's ally spells available to druidic spellcasters.

Effects

This spell functioned just like all summon nature's ally spells, which in turn resembled the summon monster spells. It could summon a single dire bear, huge elemental of any type, elephant, girallon, megaraptor, giant octopus, pixie (which favored casting Otto's irresistible dance), salamander, baleen whale, or xorn. It could also be used to summon a larger number of monsters from a lower level of a spell in the series.

Components

The spell required a divine focus and both verbal and somatic components.
