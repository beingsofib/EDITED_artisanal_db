Resist turning

Spell
nameResist Turning
school2eAbjuration
level2eWizard 2

Resist turning was a rare spell used by necromancers.

Effects

When cast, the spell protected undead creatures from being commanded or turned by clerics and paladins. The spell protected all undead creatures in a area, this area was either a set location or mobile as the caster moved. The caster was able to cast this spell on themselves.

Components

This spell required only a verbal and somatic component.

History

This spell was known to be used by wizards of the Cult of the Dragon.
