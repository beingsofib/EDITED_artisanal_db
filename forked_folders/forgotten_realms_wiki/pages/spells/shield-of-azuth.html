Shield of Azuth

Spell
nameShield of Azuth
inventorDove Falconhand

Shield of Azuth was a spell that buffered against one dispelling attempt.

Effects

The shield of Azuth responded to a dispel magic by buffering against the spell at the cost of itself, preserving all other magic on the subject of the casting.

History

The spell had been devised by Dove Falconhand, who employed it during the . The spell preserved numerous wards she'd cast on herself prior, despite a cautious foe casting dispelling magics on her.
