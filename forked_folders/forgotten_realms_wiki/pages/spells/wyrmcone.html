Wyrmcone

Spell
nameWyrmcone
school3eEvocation
level3eSorcerer 6 Wizard 6

Wyrmcone was an arcane spell that dealt damage depending on the material component used to cast it.

Effects

Wyrmcone, when cast, created a cone of in length starting at the wizard's hands. The come was filled with wildly whirling transparent dragon claws and sharp-toothed maws. The cone sliced and shredded everyone within its bounds and dealt damage dependent on the material component used.

Components

The spell required verbal, somatic, and material components. The two material components for the spell were: a tiny glass or crystal cone and a small sliver of a dragon's scale, tooth. bone, or claw. Different types of dragons changed the energy damage dealt with the spell: black, copper, and green dragons created a cone of acid; blue and bronze dragons - electricity; brass, gold, and red dragons - fire; silver and white dragons - the cone of cold.

History

Wyrmcone was among the spells that were exchanged between Elminster Aumar, Mordenkainen, and Rautheene, during one of the regular meetings of the Wizards Three in the home of Ed Greenwood of Earth. The topic of discussion of that particular assembly was "battle spells."
