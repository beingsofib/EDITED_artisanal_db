Launch item was a spell used by sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell allowed the caster to fling an item at a target with incredible speed. The item was protected from damage during flight but suffered normal damage when it struck its target. The maximum weight of the item could not exceed .

Components

This spell required only a somatic component.
