Nightmare lullaby

Spell
nameNightmare Lullaby
school3eEnchantment (Compulsion)
level3eBard 2

Nightmare lullaby was a spell used by bards.

Effects

This spell allowed the caster to place a targeted creature into a sleepwalking nightmare. The targeted creature became confused. The creature had to be within of the caster for the spell to be effective. This range grew as the caster's skill level increased.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.

History

This spell was found in Volume III of A Harper's Companion.
