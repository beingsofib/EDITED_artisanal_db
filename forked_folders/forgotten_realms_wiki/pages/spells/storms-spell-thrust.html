Storm's spell thrust was an enchantment wizard spell created by Storm Silverhand.

Effects

This spell protected the caster from up to two hostile spells targeted at them at a time by rebounding the spells back to their source. Each casting of spell thrust granted the caster this protection for two consecutive minutes. This rebounding was automatic so the caster was still protected even if unaware of the attack.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component a small curved piece of glass or metal to cast.
