Undead bane weapon was a transmutation spell used by paladins. This spell originated from Kelemvor, the god of the dead.

Effects

This spell enchanted a weapon to cause additional harm against the undead. This spell worked on melee weapons and up to 50 arrows or other projectile weapons.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a weapon to cast.
