Insulation

Spell
nameInsulation
school2eAbjuration, Alteration
level2eWizard 1

Insulation was a spell unique to Halruaa.

Effects

Insulation provided temporary resistance to electrical damage to the caster.

Components

This spell required 1" (2.54 cm) square piece of rubber to cast. The rubber needed to be placed between the caster's foot and the ground and remain in contact with the caster's skin at all times for the spell to work.
