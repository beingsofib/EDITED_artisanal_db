Dragon scales (spell)

Spell
nameDragon Scales
school2eAbjuration
level2eWizard 6

Dragon scales was an arcane abjuration spell that gave any person or creature touched a protective skin of dragon scales.

Effects

The dragon scales gave the recipient natural armor in addition to whatever armor they were already wearing. The color of the dragon scale used as the spell component also affected the color of the resulting scales. While under the effects of this spell, the scaly one was somewhat less charismatic than normal.

Components

As well as the standard verbal and somatic components, the required material component was the scale of any type of dragon.
