Spirit mask was a divine alteration spell used only by shamans devoted to Ubtao called spiritlords.

Effects

This spell allowed the spiritlord to entwine their spirit essence with that of a major or minor spirit. The spell diverted divination spells targeted on the caster to the entwined spirit for the spell's duration, allowing the caster to misdirect any such intrusion for information.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components the spiritlord's holy symbol to cast.
