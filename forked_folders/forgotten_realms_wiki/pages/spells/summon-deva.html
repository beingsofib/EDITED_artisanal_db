Summon deva

Spell
nameSummon Deva
school2eConjuration/Summoning
level2eCleric 7 Druid 7 Paladin 7

Summon deva was a powerful spell used by clerics, druids and paladins.

Effects

When cast, this spell opened a gateway to the celestial planes and summoned a deva. The summoned deva fought for the caster against their enemies until they were killed or the spell expired.
