Loviatar's caress was a divine alteration spell used by priests devoted to Loviatar, enabling them to succeed with a touch spell.

Effects

This spell ensured that the following touch-based spell would always succeed. The target spell needed to be cast immediately after Loviatar's caress. This spell could also be used with the priest's pain touch ability.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
