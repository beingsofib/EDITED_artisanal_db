Foesight was a divine divination spell from the divination sphere that alterted the caster to the presence of enemies.

Effects

This spell informed the caster when enemies approached and their approximate numbers. The spell's magic extended outward from the caster for several miles depening on their skill.

The reverse of this spell, hidden hatred, was centered on the caster and masked their approach to an enemy until they were within of them.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component the caster's holy symbol to cast.

History

Kyri carried several scrolls with the foesight spell.
