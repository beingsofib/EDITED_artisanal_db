Enveloping swarm

Spell
nameEnveloping Swarm
school3eConjuration
level3eDruid 4

Enveloping swarm was a druidic conjuration spell that created a swarm of vicious insects.

Effects

The spell reached to about , summoning magical flying insects that harried the target for some time. Their bites and stings were imbued with corrosive acid, making it a deadly threat.

Components

The spell required verbal and somatic components to cast.
