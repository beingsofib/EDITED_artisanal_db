Favor of Tymora, known also as Tymora's smile, was a divine abjuration spell used by priests devoted to Tymora, enabling them to confer a protection on a single target.

Effects

Once cast, the favor of Tymora could not be dispelled and would last until the target's death or the spell's energy had been expended. This spell would cancel the bane of Beshaba spell. The protection offered from this spell made it easier for the target to resist the effects of spells, magic items, poisons, etc. That resistance became slightly weaker with each use, slowly draining the spell's magic until none remained. This spell could not be used on the same target more than once per day.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
