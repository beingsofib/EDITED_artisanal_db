Mystic Carrion's explosive cloudkill was a necromancy spell from the 5 circle of magic, created by Mystic Carrion, a mysterious mummy lord of Baldur's Gate.

Effects

Mystic Carrion's explosive cloudkill was a variant of the more common cloudkill. It dealt heavy poison damage to everyone who was within the spell's area of effect. The cloud itself also obscured all objects and creatures within it. Lastly, Mystic Carrion's cloud was flammable and could explode from a single spark.
