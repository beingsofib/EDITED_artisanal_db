Know vulnerabilities

Spell
nameKnow Vulnerabilities
school3eDivination
level3eBard 4 Cleric 4

Know vulnerabilities was a spell used by bards and clerics.

Effects

This spell allowed a caster to ascertain the resistances and vulnerabilities of a targeted creature. The targeted creature had to be within of the caster for the spell to function properly. This distance increased as the caster's skill level grew.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
