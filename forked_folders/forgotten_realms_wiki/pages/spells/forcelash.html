Forcelash

Spell
nameForcelash
school2eEvocation
level2eWizard 4

Forcelash was a spell that was first developed by the phaerimms.

Effects

When cast, a long whip of energy was created at the end of the caster's arm or other limb chosen during casting. This powerful whip was controlled by the caster's willpower and was able to attack a target within of the caster. The whip was strong enough to penetrate some barrier or protection spells like shield. When struck, the targeted creature became immobile and was wracked with painful spasm as energy coursed through their body.

Components

This spell required verbal and somatic components.
