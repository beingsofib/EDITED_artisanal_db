Shadow gauntlet

Spell
nameShadow Gauntlet
inventorJume
school2eEvocation, Illusion/phantasm, Shadow
level2eWizard 6

Shadow gauntlet was a spell crafted by the wizard Jume that acted as an enhanced version of shadow hand.

Effects

This spell created a hand made of shadows that could manipulate objects, shield the caster, or wield weapons. It could be commanded by the caster, without concentration, to travel, strike opponents, or raise weapons against them. It could be attacked and destroyed by magical or non-magical means.
