Aura fire was an alteration spell that outlined a target creature or person in a red, glowing light.

Effects

The spell worked in a similar manner to the druidic spell faerie fire.

Casting

When casting aura fire, the spellcaster motioned their hands in a manner that outlined the body of the spell's recipient.
