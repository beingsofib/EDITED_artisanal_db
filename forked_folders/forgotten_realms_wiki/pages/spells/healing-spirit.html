Healing spirit

Spell
nameHealing Spirit
school5eConjuration
level5eDruid 2 Ranger 2

Healing spirit was a spell that created an area that healed creatures within it.

Effects

A caster used this spell to request assistance from a nature spirit. If successful, the spirit appeared before the caster. It was transparent and in the form of an animal or fey. For the duration of the spell, the area around the spirit provided healing to the caster or another creature. The spell had no effect on constructs or the undead.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
