Stoneview was an alteration spell that allowed wizards to see through a mass of stone.

Effects

This spell allowed a caster to turn a mass of stone temporarily transparent, allowing the caster to see through it as if it wasn't there. The mass of stone that could be affected varied with the skill of the spellcaster, with the lowest possible being . The distance at which it could be cast and the spell's duration also varied with the skill of the spellcaster, with the lowest possible being a distance of and a duration of 5 minutes.

Components

In addition to requiring somatic and verbal components, casting stoneview required a material component in the form of the stone that was to be the target of the spell.

History

In the mid–14 century DR, the earth elementalist Maurice Stone had this spell activate within his lair when a group of adventurers confronted him over the kidnapping of Lauren DeVillars.
