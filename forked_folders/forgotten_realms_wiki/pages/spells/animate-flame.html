Animate flame

Spell
nameAnimate Flame
school2eAlteration
spheres2eElemental Fire
level2eDruid 5 Elemental sphere Elemental 5 Elemental fire sphere Elemental Fire 5

Animate flame was an alteration spell that caused natural flames to move at the command of the caster, leaving their fuel behind, possibly with devastating effect. With luck, experienced priests could control even magical fires.
