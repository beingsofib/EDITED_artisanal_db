Mists of Ghaunadaur was a spell used by clerics of Ghaunadaur.

Effects

When cast, the caster was cloaked in a flowing violet mist. The mist effectively rendered the caster invisible. The mist protected the caster from attacks that required sight, and made them invisible to not only natural vision but also infravision and any abilities that detected heat. The true seeing spell was capable of penetrating the mist.

Components

This spell required verbal and somatic components.
