Jester's jest

Spell
nameJester's Jest
inventorLendolin
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 2

Jester's jest was a wizardly alteration spell from the 2 circle of magic that could render even the happiest laughing creature serious in an instant.

Effects

When cast upon a creature, jester's jest stripped the target from all happy and jovial inclinations, rendering them serious, unless the target was able to resist the spell's effect. While jester's jest remained active, all of the target's words came out sad, depressing, and sorrowful, spreading their demoralizing influence to all who listened, slowing their reactions and mental sharpness. The duration of the spell increased with the caster's proficiencies with the Art.

Components

The spell required verbal, somatic, and material components to be cast.

History

The jester's jest spell's creation was attributed to a curiously named jester who served King Azoun Obarskyr I of Cormyr, Lendolin the Laughless.
