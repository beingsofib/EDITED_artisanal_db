Summon nishruu

Spell
nameSummon Nishruu
school2eConjuration/Summoning
level2eWizard 6

Summon nishruu was a spell used by wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell summoned a nishruu, a powerful creature from another plane that fed on magical energy. Despite its desire to do so, the nishruu did not attack the caster as they were under their control.
