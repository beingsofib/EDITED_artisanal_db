Astral rift

Spell
nameAstral Rift
school5eConjuration

Astral rift was a conjuration spell from the 3 circle of magic, unique to githyanki and githzerai of the Astral Plane.

Effects

When astral rift was cast, it ripped open the planes, creating a gate that temporarily connected the Prime Material plane with the Astral. The gate allowed githyanki and githzerai reinforcements to arrive at Prime and return back with deadly swiftness. The caster had to put all their efforts into maintaining their astral rift, rendering them unable to move or do anything apart from concentrating on the gate or closing it.

History

Circa the , the warring factions of githyanki and githzerai used the astral rift spell the assault the adventuring band known as the Absolute Adversaries and the illithid calling itself the Emperor.
