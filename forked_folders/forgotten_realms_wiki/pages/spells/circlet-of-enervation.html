Circlet of enervation

Spell
nameCirclet of Enervation
school3eNecromancy
level3eSorcerer 5 Wizard 5

Circlet of enervation was a spell that created a small, 6"-in-diameter ring of negative energy in the hand of the caster. The spellcaster could then launch the ring at an opponent nearby, whereupon another would appear in their hand, and would continuously replenish until the duration of the spell expired. These rings would harm the living as per the spell they were named after as well as heal and bolster the undead.

The material component of this spell was a bracelet made of jet, worth at least 100gp.
