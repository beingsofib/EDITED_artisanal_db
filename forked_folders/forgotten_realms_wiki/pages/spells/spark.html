Spark

Spell
nameSpark
inventorSabirine
school1eEvocation
level1eMagic-user 0
type1ePersonal

Spark was a personal cantrip that beginning wizards could learn. It created a big blue bolt of electricity at a location chosen by the caster.

Effects

The caster could choose any point within for the spark to appear. Creatures targeted by spark had a chance to resist the magic of the spell and, if successful, received only an uncomfortable shock, otherwise, they suffered minimal damage. Metals and other conductive materials could transmit the energy released, therefore, a person wearing metal armor was slightly more likely to be shocked. However, if the target creature was very near death, this cantrip could not finish them off. Atmospheric conditions, such as dry air, humidity, or a nearby storm, had no effect on the size or puissance of the spark.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster simultaneously snapped their fingers and made a popping sound.

History

This cantrip was invented by Sabirine, a mage who supported the use and improvement of cantrips. Spark was recorded in the tome known as Sabirine's Specular, along with catfeet and snatch.
