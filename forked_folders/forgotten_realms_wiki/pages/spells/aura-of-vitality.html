Aura of vitality was an evocation spell for druids and paladins that created an aura that could heal creatures close by.

Effects

The caster created an aura with a radius that was centered on the caster. The caster could pick a creature within the aura to regain some lost health. The aura only lasted for 1 minute.

Components

This spell required only a verbal component.
