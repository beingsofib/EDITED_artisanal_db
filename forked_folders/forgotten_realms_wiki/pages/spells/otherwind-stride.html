Otherwind stride was a spell of the conjuration school used by some warlocks, particularly those of the fey pact.

Effects

The caster of otherwind stride created a maelstrom of arcane Feywild power. Any creatures caught in the spell's roughly effect around the warlock were injured by its energy. If the warlock wished, they could step into the chaos unharmed and teleport themselves a distance of up to , or potentially more if they had made a fey pact.

Components

Otherwind stride was not overly complex, and the ability to cast it could be regained with a rest of a few minutes or more.
