Malavon's rage was an evocation spell that shot darts of magical energy out from the caster in all directions.

Description

When cast, Malavon's rage generated small wedges of magic energy, which were then instantly issued forth in all directions within a radius of to , piercing anyone caught in their path. The spell was noted as being particularly useful when surrounded by enemies.

History

Malavon's rage was created by the drow sorcerer Malavon Despana prior to 1281 DR.
