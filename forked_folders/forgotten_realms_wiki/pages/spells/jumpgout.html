Jumpgout was an evocation spell that created a massive pillar of flame that jumped from one location to another, either at the direction of the caster or by random chance.

Effects

When this spell was cast, the soaring fire engulfed an area in diameter and high. Any person or creature caught within the inferno was horribly burned. A few moments later, the fires vanished for a second, then reappeared in an area within of the original location.

If the caster concentrated on directing the flames, they could choose were the reappearance occurred; otherwise, it was completely random. Following the second conflagration, the jumpgout vanished in a swirl of cinders.

Components

This spell only required verbal components to cast.

History

Since at least the , the bronze dragon Nymmurh passed knowledge of jumpgout down to various members of the Silmerhelve family over the generations. And at some point during the 14 century DR, Volothamp Geddarm managed to learn of the spell.
