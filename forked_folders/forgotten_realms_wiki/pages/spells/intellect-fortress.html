Intellect fortress

Psionic Power
nameIntellect Fortress
level4ePsion 2
type4eEncounter
discipline2eTelepathy
level2eMinor (Devotion)
type2eStandard power

Intellect fortress was a psionic devotion of the telepathy discipline that allowed the psionicist to envelop their mind in protective psychic energy.

Effects

An intellect fortress excelled at protecting the mind against an ego whip attack but was susceptible to a psionic blast.

Notable Users

Individuals

  • Arvin, a human psion.
  • Vhondryl, a psionicist in Skullport and head of the Hall of Mental Splendor
