Low-light vision (spell)

nameLow-light Vision
school3eTransmutation
level3eAssassin 1 Harper agent 1 Hathran 1 Ranger 1 Sorcerer 1 Wizard 1

Low-light vision was a transmutation spell that granted a target creature the type of vision after which it was named.

Effects

By casting this spell, the affected creature could see twice as far as a human in poorly lit areas such as those illuminated by starlight or candlelight. The recipient of this effect could more effectively distinguish and better discern details within its field of vision.

Casting

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a small candle in its casting.
