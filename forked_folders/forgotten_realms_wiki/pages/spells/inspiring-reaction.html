Inspiring reaction

Inspiring reaction was a warlord exploit that brought immediate aid to a wounded ally.

Effects

Any wound received by the warlord or an ally could initiate this exploit. Immediately after receiving injury, the warlord could reach out and touch a close ally and heal some of his or her wounds. The warlord could choose to heal himself or herself instead.
