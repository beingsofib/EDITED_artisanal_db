Promise of wealth was a divination spell from the 6 circle of magic granted to the warlocks who had their pacts forged with Mammon, the King of Greed.

Effects

When the spell was cast, it infected a creature of the caster's choice with infectious greed and another creature with an aura that promised wealth. This granted the greedy creatures a vicious advantage in battle, dramatically increasing the chances of them landing attacks against the wealthy individuals.

The spell did not get empowered if the caster was a more powerful warlock. Its targets had to be visible to the caster and be within of them. Lastly, the spell lasted for 12 seconds, and the caster had to maintain concentration.

History

Circa the , the promise of wealth spell was known to Helsik, the diabolist of Baldur's Gate, the Latchkeeper of the Nine Hells, the mistress of Devil's Fee shop in the Lower City.
