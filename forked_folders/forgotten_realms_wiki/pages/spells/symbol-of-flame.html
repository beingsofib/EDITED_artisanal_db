Symbol of flame

Spell
nameSymbol of Flame
school3eEvocation
level3eCleric 4 Favored soul 4 Sorcerer 4 Wild mage 4 Wizard 4

Symbol of flame was an evocation spell that created a fiery rune to be triggered by a victim.

Effects

When cast, the spell created a rune in the air. It waited for several minutes until a creature came close, upon which it blasted them with a gout of fire, then dispersed.

Components

The spell required verbal, somatic, and material components to cast.
