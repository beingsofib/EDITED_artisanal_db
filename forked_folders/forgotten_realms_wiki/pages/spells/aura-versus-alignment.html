Aura versus alignment

nameAura Versus Alignment
school3eAbjuration
level3eCleric 8

Aura versus alignment was a spell used by clerics.

Effects

When cast, this spell allowed a caster to protect themselves from spells by evil or good alignment creatures. It protected against mind-altering abilities or spells, and also provided some magical resistance.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
