Speed rot was a divine abjuration spell used by priests devoted to Moander, enabling them to cause rot in fruit and vegetables.

Effects

This spell caused fruit, vegetables, and grain to immediately ripen and rot. Up to of plant material could be affected with a single casting. This spell could also be used to cause physical harm in plant-based creatures.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component a bit of mold to cast.
