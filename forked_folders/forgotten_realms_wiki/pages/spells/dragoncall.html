Dragoncall

Spell
nameDragoncall
school3eEnchantment (Compulsion)
level3eSorcerer 6

Dragoncall was a spell that was used by dragons.

Effects

This spell was used by dragons to both communicate and compel a nearby sentient creature to hurry to the casting dragon's location. The creature rushed to the location as fast and as safely as possible—the spell could not force a creature to harm itself unnecessarily. Dragoncall could only be cast by dragons.

Components

This spell required only a verbal component.
