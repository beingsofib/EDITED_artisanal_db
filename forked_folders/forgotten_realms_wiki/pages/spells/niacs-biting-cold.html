Niac's biting cold

Spell
nameNiac's Biting Cold
inventorNiac
school3eEvocation
level3eSorcerer 5 Wild mage 5 Wizard 5

Niac's biting cold was an arcane evocation spell that imbued a target with a deep and deadly chill.

Effects

The spell reached and filled the target with a pervasive and chilling cold, freezing them from the inside out. Biting cold could be cast repeatedly on the same target, magnifying the effect each time and turning a slow death into a quick one.

Components

The spell required verbal, somatic, and material components to cast.
