Trobriand's baleful teleport

Spell
nameTrobriand's Baleful Teleport
inventorTrobriand
school3eConjuration
level3eSorcerer 6 Wizard 6

Trobriand's baleful teleport was an arcane conjuration spell that transported a target to a designated location.

Effects

A clear mental image of the target destination was vital to ensure the spell worked as intended.

Components

Trobriand's baleful teleport required verbal spell components to cast.

History

This spell was developed by Trobriand of Waterdeep. A staff of rejecting was able to cast this spell.
