Inflict moderate wounds, also known as cause moderate wounds was a necromancy spell that harmed the living and healed the undead.

Effects

On a successful touch, this spell revitalized undead creatures or had a withering effect on the living. This spell was about twice as powerful as inflict light wounds but otherwise operated in the same manner.

The older version of this spell was not as potent as the newer version but could knock back foes with possibly critical results on a successful attack. Plus, it was reversible (called cure moderate wounds) for use in healing the living.
