Insatiable thirst was an enchantment spell used by wizards.

Effects

The victim of this spell believed they were about to die of thirst and was struck with an uncontrollable urge to drink, driving them to seek potable fluids to consume with the utmost haste. The victim's thirst was not satisfied, regardless of how much fluid they drank, until the spell ended.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
