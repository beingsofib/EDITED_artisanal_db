Drunken inhale

Spell
nameDrunken Inhale
school5eEvocation

Drunken inhale was a spell of the evocation school of magic used by the djinni.

Effects

This spell had multiple effects. It dealt damage to creatures, intoxicated them, and pulled them towards the spellcaster. Drunken inhale intoxicated and pulled all creatures caught in a cone in front of the caster and the drunkenness lasted for the next six seconds, giving the creatures momentary vulnerability in battle.
