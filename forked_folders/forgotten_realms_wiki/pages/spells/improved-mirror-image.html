Improved mirror image

Spell
nameImproved Mirror Image
school2eIllusion/Phantasm
level2eNinja 4

Improved mirror image was a common spell used by ninjas. The spell was very rare outside Kara-Tur.

Effects

A ninja used this spell to create a mirror image of themselves. The illusion could move up to from the ninja. The illusion was required to face the same target as the ninja, but acted independently and able to use different weapons. If a target struck the illusion, it acted as would a person injured or killed in combat, and felt solid. Similar to the mirror image spell, the illusion caused no damage to the target. A ninja was able to summon more than one illusion, depending on their skill level.

Components

The spell required only a somatic component, kuji-kiri hand gestures.
