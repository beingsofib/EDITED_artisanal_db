Venom immunity was a necromancy spell used in the Underdark.

Effects

This spell rendered either the caster or recipient immune to all toxins for the duration of the spell. Unlike an antidote or the higher-level neutralize poison spell, venom immunity did not cure the target of their existing toxins.

Components

The spell required only verbal and somatic components.
