Corruption and Madness mantle

The Corruption and Madness mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concept and philosophy of insanity.

Abilities

Those who followed the Corruption and Madness mantle gained terrible insights into its associated concept, making them impervious to being confused or driven mad while psionically focused.

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the Corruption and Madness mantle wrought disturbance in the minds of all who opposed them, making it more difficult for enemies within the radius of the aura to cast spells or manifest psionic powers, as well as increased the difficulty of using spell-like and psionic-like abilities.

Powers

Novice manifesters were able to modestly disquiet the minds of their foes with brain lock, primal fear, and psionic daze. Manifesters of some experience learned how to further hinder a creature's capacity to function with mental turmoil and mindwipe. As a manifester progressed further they obtained decerebrate and insanity, allowing them to cause permanent damage to a creature's mind. Masters of this mantle obtained the power to significantly warp the consciousnesses of others with microcosm, allowing them to trap creatures within their own mindscape.
