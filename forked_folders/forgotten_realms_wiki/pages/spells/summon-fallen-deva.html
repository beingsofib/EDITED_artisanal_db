Summon fallen deva

Spell
nameSummon Fallen Deva
school2eConjuration/Summoning
level2eCleric 7

Summon fallen deva was a powerful spell used by evil clerics.

Effects

When cast, this spell opened a gateway to the abyss and summoned a deva who has fallen from grace. The summoned evil deva fought for the caster against their enemies until they were killed or the spell expired.
