Spellseeking worm

Spell
namespellseeking worm

The spellseeking worm was a spell used against Tsjancy Yildree during his time as the Magister.

Effects

The spell performed or enabled some form of tracking which bypassed many ways of hiding.

History

The spell was used by Urboltar Highymhelver to track Tsjancy Yildree, the Magister, whom he later slew in . The spell was successful, in spite of Tsjancy's grasp of magic being excellent and his powers being boosted by the office of the Magister.
