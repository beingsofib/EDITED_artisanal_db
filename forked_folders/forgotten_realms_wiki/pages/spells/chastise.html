Chastise

Spell
nameChastise
school2eEnchantment, Universal
level2eWizard 3

Chastise was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Effects

Wizards used this spell on those who offended them. This spell did nothing if a wizard used it on someone who did not offend them.

Targets of this spell felt an overwhelming compulsion to abase themselves before the caster, performing menial tasks for the duration of the spell.

Components

This spell did not require physical components to cast.
