Transmute water to dust was an alteration spell that turned all the water in the target area to dust. The reverse of this spell, improved create water, converted a volume of dust or dirt into pure water.

History

The arcane version of this spell was developed by Netherese arcanist Proctiv in under the name Proctiv's water-dust transmutation.
