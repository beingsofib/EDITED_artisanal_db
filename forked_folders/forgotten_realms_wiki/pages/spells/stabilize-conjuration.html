Stabilize (conjuration)

Spell
nameStabilize
school3eConjuration
level3eCleric 2 Paladin 2

Stabilize was a divine conjuration spell that pulled creatures from the brink of death in the heat of battle. The spell was granted to clerics and paladins by their patron deities.

Effects

When the spell was uttered, it could affect all creatures within from the caster. It produced a wave of positive energy that stabilized all downed creatures on the brink of death, ensuring their survival. The energy also dealt minuscule damage to all undead creatures within the same radius.

Components

The spell required only a somatic and verbal components.
