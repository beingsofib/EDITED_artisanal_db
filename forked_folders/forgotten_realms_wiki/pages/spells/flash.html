Flash

Spell
nameFlash
school1eEvocation
level1eMagic-user 2

Flash was an evocation spell that allowed the caster to create a blinding flash of light that hampered the eyesight of anyone caught within the blast.

Effects

The partial blindness caused by flash lasted for anywhere from a matter of seconds to a couple minutes. Those that resisted the spell's effects recovered in a shorter amount of time.
