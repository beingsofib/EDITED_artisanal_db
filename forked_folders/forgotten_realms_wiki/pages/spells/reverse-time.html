Reverse time

Spell
nameReverse Time
school2eAlteration
spheres2eTime
level2eCleric 6

Repeat time was a divine magic spell available to priests.

Effects

When cast, the spell stopped time in an area with diameter centered around the caster. Everything within this area became motionless. Creatures or objects that entered this area while the spell was in effect also became frozen in time. The spell reversed time up to a certain point determined by the caster then froze.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component, an etched arrow made from silver that was bent into a circle. The arrow could be longer than nor cost more than 500 gp.

History

This spell was inscribed within the Kelemvorite relic the Testament of Vraer.
