Augment psionics was a spell used by clerics of Deep Duerra.

Effects

When cast, this spell increased the psionic abilities of the caster or targeted creature. If cast on a creature with no psionic abilities, the spell had no effect.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component, the holy symbol of Deep Duerra.
