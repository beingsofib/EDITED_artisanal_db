Bombardment was a spell used by druids.

Effects

When cast, this spell bombarded an area with a radius. The bombardment was caused by rocks falling from the sky. Any creature within this area was harmed. The amount of damage caused by the falling rocks was determined by the caster's skill level.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a divine focus, a rock or stone with quartz embedded in it.
