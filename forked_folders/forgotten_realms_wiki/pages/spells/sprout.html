Sprout

Spell
nameSprout
school1eEvocation
level1eMagic-user 0
type1eUseful

Sprout was a useful cantrip that neophyte wizards could learn. It caused live seeds or plants to experience an immediate growth spurt, or caused vegetable matter to ripen or age.

Effects

The target of this spell had to be within of the caster and affected plant matter in a volume no bigger than . This cantrip accelerated the growth of plants. Seeds sprouted to seedlings, seedlings grew about , flower buds bloomed, and so on. If used on fruit or vegetables, they aged, advancing from unripe toward ripe, or possibly overripe to spoiled.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster pantomimed hoeing while making a whispery rustling sound.
