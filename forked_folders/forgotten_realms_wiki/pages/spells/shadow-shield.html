Shadow shield

Spell
nameShadow shield
school3eIllusion
level3eSorcerer 7 Wizard 7

Shadow shield was a powerful spell used by sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell created a temporary cloak of shadow for the caster. The cloak protected the caster from damage, necromantic spells, negative energy and death-based magic.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
