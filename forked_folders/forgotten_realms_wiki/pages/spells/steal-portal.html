Steal portal

Spell
nameSteal Portal
school3eTransmutation
level3eWizard 9 Sorcerer 9

Steal portal was a transmutation spell which would allow the caster to steal a portal.

Effects

The caster was able to steal any portal they had traveled through and relocate it to a new location within of the existing portal. The new location needed a portal frame in place or the spell would not work. The spell would not change a portal's properties and took approximately one hour to cast.

Components

The spell required verbal, somatic and material components.
