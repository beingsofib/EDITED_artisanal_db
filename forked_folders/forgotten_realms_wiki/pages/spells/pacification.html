Pacification

Spell
namePacification
school3eEnchantment (Compulsion)
level3eBard 4 Cleric 5

Pacification was a spell used to calm a creature.

Effects

When cast, the caster breathed out a calming, gentle wind. A single creature within of the caster was then calmed, as if the subject of a calm emotions spell. The spell did not function on creatures more powerful than the caster.

Components

This spell required verbal and somatic components. If, during casting, the caster tossed blue spinel dust into the calming breeze, the spell last two minutes longer, with an additional two minutes per skill level.
