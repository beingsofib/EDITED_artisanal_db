Call of the crustacean

Spell
nameCall of the Crustacean
school5eConjuration

Call of the crustacean was a conjuration spell from the 3 circle of magic, granted to her servants by Auntie Ethel, a green hag of Baldur's Gate.

Effects

When cast, the spell summoned a cast of armored crabs, under the spellcaster's control. The spell's target was a visible area within sixty feet of the caster.

History

Circa the , the call of the crustacean spell was known to Jatlo, a red cap spy, who, on behest of Auntie Ethel, infiltrated the Anti-Hag Support Group in a guise of a green-scaled dragonborn. If his charade were to be uncovered, Jatlo would cast the spell, unleashing an army of hermit crabs onto the group of anti-hag activists.
