Towering oak

Spell
nameTowering Oak
school3eIllusion (Glamer)
level3eRanger 1

Towering oak was an illusion spell used by rangers.

Effects

When cast, this spell allowed the caster to increase their ability to intimidate their opponents. It functioned by using the strength of a nearby oak tree and drawing it into the caster.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
