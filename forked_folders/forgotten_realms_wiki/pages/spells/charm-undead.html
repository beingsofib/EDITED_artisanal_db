Charm undead

Spell
nameCharm undead
school1eEnchantment/necromancy
level1eMagic-user 3

Charm undead was an enchantment/necromancy spell that allowed the user limited control over undead creatures.

Effects

The spell worked in a similar manner to charm monster, but only affected the undead, even though that were typically immune to charm spells and effects.

Casting

The casting of this spell required verbal and somatic components.
