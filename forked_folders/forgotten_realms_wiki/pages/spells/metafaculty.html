Standard power

Metafaculty was a psionic power of the clairsentience discipline that allowed a manifester to receive a remarkably accurate vision of a creature and its whereabouts.

Effects

Those who employed metafaculty raised their minds to a near-universal level of consciousness in the process, allowing them to meditate over numerous impressions and predictions regarding a certain target. This target creature had to be someone the psionic user had seen prior, either in person or remotely through another power, such as remote viewing. The power took around an hour to manifest and had an average duration of one minute, extended by one or more minutes depending upon how skilled the psionic user was.

Once metafaculty was fully manifested, the user would receive various visions regarding the target's personal nature, location, and activities. For the remaining duration of the power, the user would also receive a mental view of the creature, as though they were using the remote reviewing power.

Details on the target's personal nature included such things as their name, health, race, class, alignment, and any significant items they had on their person.

Locational premonitions included the target's place of residence, what town or country they were currently in, to as far out as what world in the Prime Material plane they were currently on or what plane in the multiverse they were in.

Activities included anything significant that the target had done within the prior 8 hours – what places they had traveled through, the names or races of creatures they fought, what (if any) spells they had exist, and items they had acquired or left behind, as well as the location of said items.

What was considered "significant" by these premonitions could vary depending upon an individual manifester's own biases. For example, a powerful seer would likely overlook a routine meal or the presence of a lowly potion of cure light wounds on their target, instead finding attendance of a banquet or the presence of a potion of supreme healing to be significant.

Metafaculty could potentially overcome most psionic powers, spells, and abilities that normally obscured clairsentience powers. This even included such things as mind blank, screen, and wish. However, this power could not overcome epic spells or epic powers that obscured clairsentience powers and divinations.

Displays

When used this power manifested mental, olfactory, and visual displays.

Users

This power was knowable by seers, as well as ardents and divine minds that followed the psionic mantles of communication or fate.

Known/Notable Users

  • Raulothim, an emerald great wyrm.
