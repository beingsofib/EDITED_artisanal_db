Nondetection (or non-detection

Effects

Other than casting on themselves, a caster was able to cast this spell on an creature or an object. After casting, it became very difficult for detection and divination spells to detect the targeted individual or object. The targeted creature's equipment was also protected. The spell also successfully prevented magical items such as crystal balls from targeting the subject of the spell.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, some diamond dust with a value of at least 50 gp.

History

The spell was attributed to Netherese arcanist Ptack in and was originally called Ptack's nonscryance.
