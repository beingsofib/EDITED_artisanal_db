Lesser mind blank

Spell
nameLesser Mind Blank
school3eAbjuration
level3eSorcerer 5 Wizard 5

Lesser mind blank was a spell used by sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell granted immunity to some mind-affecting effects and spells. The spell also removed their negative affects if cast on a creature already affected by such spells.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
