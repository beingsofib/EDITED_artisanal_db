Enlarge skeleton

Spell
nameEnlarge Skeleton
school2eNecromancy
level2eWizard 1

Enlarge skeleton was a necromancy spell that increased the size of either a portion, or the entirety of, a human-sized or smaller skeleton.

Effects

Upon casting this spell, the affected skeletal remains were extended or otherwise enlarged as per the caster's choosing. If used on an animated skeleton, the undead creature grew twice in size; re-animated animals grew to the size of monsters, and humanoid skeletons grew to giant proportions.

If used on non-animated remains, the bones grew in proportions and weight. Enlarge skeleton could not be used in this way on collected bones of multiple individuals.

Enlarge skeleton had no effect when cast upon living beings.

Casting

This spell only required verbal and somatic components to be cast.

History

This spell was known to be used by wizards of the Cult of the Dragon.
