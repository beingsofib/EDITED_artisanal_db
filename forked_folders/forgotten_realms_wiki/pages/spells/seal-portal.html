Seal portal

Spell
nameSeal Portal
school3eAbjuration
level3eSorcerer 6 Wizard 6

Seal portal was an arcane abjuration spell that permanently closed an interplanar portal or a gate.

History

Elminster Aumar knew this spell around 759 DR and was taught it by Mystra herself.
