Conflict mantle

The Conflict mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concept or philosophy of conflict.

Abilities

Those who followed the conflict mantle became more in tune with martial concepts,

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the conflict mantle made the manifester and their nearby allies more proficient at landing devastating strikes in combat.

Powers

Novice manifesters gained the power to infuse weapons with psionic energy with metaphysical weapon and gain brief glimpses of the future of a conflict with offensive prescience. Manifesters of some experience could push themselves to the head of a battlefield with psionic lion's charge, glide around it with dimension slide, and make themselves unbudging bastions with immovability. As a manifester progressed further they obtained the power to brutally strike the enemy's mind with psychic crush and to mold weapons to their flesh with graft weapon. Masters of this mantle obtained the power spirit of war, allowing them to exude a martial aura that improved their battle prowess.
