Solitude was a spell used by clerics of Fenmarel Mestarine.

Effects

This spell was used by casters to create a zone of solitude. While within this zone, which had a radius of , the chances the caster was disturbed by a non-sentient creature was significantly reduced. This zone increased in size as the caster's skill level increased. If the caster left the perimeter, the spell expired. The spell also ended if a sentient creature entered the zone of solitude.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component: a handful of soil that was sprinkled to mark the perimeter of the spell's area of effect.
