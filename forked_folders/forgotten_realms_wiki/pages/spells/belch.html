Belch

Spell
nameBelch
school1eEvocation
level1eMagic-user 0
type1ePerson-affecting

Belch was a person-affecting cantrip that beginning wizards could learn. It caused a target creature to involuntarily burp.

Effects

This cantrip could target a creature within of the caster and cause it to burp. The target had a chance to resist the spell. If successful, they were able to stifle the reaction so it amounted to a relatively quiet hiccup. Failure meant a loud, very noticeable belch.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster pressed a hand to their own diaphragm while emitting a tiny, quiet burp.
