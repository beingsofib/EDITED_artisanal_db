Call woodland beings was a conjuration spell that allowed the caster three chances to name a type of forest-dwelling creature, such as brownies, centaurs, dryads, pixies, satyrs, sprites, treants, unicorns, etc., and summon one or more of them to aid the caster.

History

It was recorded in Yornar's Trail Companion, a holy text of the church of Mielikki.

In the month of Eleasis, of the , Chief Prelate Dayspring of the Clerical Circle of Ravens Bluff declared it illegal for priests to cast call woodland beings within city limits due to a raging unnatural storm at the time called the Soulstorm, threatening arrest for those that broke this decree.
