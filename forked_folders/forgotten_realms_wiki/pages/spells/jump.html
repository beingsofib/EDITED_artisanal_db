Jump was a transmutation spell used by young or inexperienced wizards that enhanced the capacity of its target to jump.

Effects

When jump was cast on a creature, it became more likely to succeed in any jump.

History

The spell was attributed to Netherese arcanist Yturn in and was originally called Yturn's long jump.
