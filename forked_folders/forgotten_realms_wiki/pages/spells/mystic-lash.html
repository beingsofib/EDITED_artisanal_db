Mystic lash was an evocation spell used by clerics. This spell originated from Bane.

Effects

This spell created a long whip of red energy extending from the caster's hand. The caster could use the magical whip as a weapon in which they were proficient. The mystic lash stunned targets by harming them with electrical energy.

Once a target was struck, the caster could release to lash from their hand and it would continue to harm the target. The lash would return to the caster's hand if the target was rendered unconscious or died.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
