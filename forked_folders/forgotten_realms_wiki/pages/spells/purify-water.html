Purify water was an arcane alteration spell that allowed the caster to cleanse water and make it safe to drink.

Effects

This spell purified dirty or contaminated water making it safe to drink. The reverse of this spell, contaminate water, had the opposite effect.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.

History

This spell was used commonly by noble marids.
