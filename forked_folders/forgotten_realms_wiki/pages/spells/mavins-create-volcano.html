Mavin's create volcano was an arcanist spell of epic power created in the days of Netheril.

Effects

Casting Mavin's create volcano caused a minor earthquake to erupt in the desired area. After a week’s time, ash and smoke exploded from the earth and, within a month, the mountain site would erupt into a powerful volcano.

Drawbacks

The spell was never cast in Netheril‚ but every arcanist sought the knowledge to conjure a devastating volcano, but they were stilted due to the terrifying effects the spell would have had on the ground-dwelling creatures, such as dwarves and elves.

Components

The components needed for this spell were a mantle of sewn diamonds which acted like a ring of fire resistance, a helm of brilliance, and a mythallar, which was placed at the location of the volcano.

History

The spell was created by Mavin around (3258 NY). This spell could no longer be cast due to the restrictions placed on magic by Mystra after the near cataclysmic destruction of the Weave by the casting of Karsus's avatar in (3520 NY).
