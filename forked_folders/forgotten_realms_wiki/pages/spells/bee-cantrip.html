Bee (cantrip)

Spell
nameBee
school1eSummoning
level1eMagic-user 0
type1ePersonal

Bee was a personal cantrip that wizards-in-training could learn. It summoned an angry honey bee to a spot chosen by the caster.

Effects

This cantrip conjured a single honey bee that appeared at a spot within and visible to the caster (that is, it could not be inside a pouch or under clothing, for example). The insect was irritated by the summoning and was highly likely to sting if it appeared on or very close to a living creature.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster focused their vision on the desired target spot, made a soft buzzing sound, and traced a wandering path in the air similar to the flight of a bee.
