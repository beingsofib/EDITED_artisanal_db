Heaven's trumpet

Spell
nameHeaven's Trumpet
school3eEvocation
level3eBard 6 Cleric 7 Emissary of Barachiel 4

Heaven's trumpet was an evocation spell that sounded music that could paralyze a celestial archon's enemies.

Users

This spell could be cast only by archons who were bards, clerics, or emissaries of Barachiel.

Effect

The caster emitted a blast of music of such perfect beauty and clarity it struck those who heard with awe. Enemies up to 120 feet (36 meters) who not withstand it could be paralyzed for up to 24 seconds.

Components

It required a somatic component, the caster's own archon nature, and a focus in the form of a well-made trumpet, which was blown during casting.
