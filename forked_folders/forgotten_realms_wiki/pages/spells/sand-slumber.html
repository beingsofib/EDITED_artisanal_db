Sand slumber

Spell
nameSand slumber
school2eEnchantment/Charm, Sand
level2eWizard 1

Sand slumber was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate. This spell was only usable by wizards.

Effects

This spell was used to cause sleep to fall upon any creature that normally required sleep, including genies. This spell lasted from a few minutes to an hour or two depending on the caster's power. Targets instantly fell asleep, collapsing and dropping anything they were holding. They would remain asleep for the duration of the spell unless physically disturbed. Loud noises would not wake them.

Components

This spell required a pinch of fine sand to cast.
