Joyful noise

Spell
nameJoyful Noise
school3eAbjuration
level3eBard 1

Joyful noise was a spell used by bards.

Effects

A caster used this spell to remove any magical silence that was cast within an area. It was effective up to from the caster. The spell did not dispel the magical silence, merely suspended it within it's area of effect, outside of this area the magical silence was still active.

Components

This spell required only a verbal component.
