Alert vigil was a divine spell that caused an individual to remain focused and attentive for a number of minutes.

Effects

Despite their level of fatigue or exhaustion, an individual affected by alert vigil remained mentally sharp and vigilant for the spell's duration. The effects of the spell lasted for at least an hour, but was extended longer depending the power of the cleric.

History

The specifications of this spell were inscribed upon one of the metal plates found on the reverse side of the Veloghon of Vigilance.

Components

This spell required verbal and somatic components in order to be cast.
