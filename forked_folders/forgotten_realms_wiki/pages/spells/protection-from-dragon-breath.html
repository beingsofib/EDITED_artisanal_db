Protection from dragon breath

Protection from dragon breath was a spell often embedded in enchanted scrolls for protecting against dragon breath.

Effects

The spell protected one creature from the breath of any dragon, no matter the variety of the dragon or the breath, for anywhere from six to a dozen minutes.

Components

The Red Wizards knew a version of the spell that could be cast as any other. Its material component was a gold coin, destroyed by the casting. It also required verbal and somatic components. The spell, however, was mostly employed in the form of scrolls that would activate upon reading them aloud.

History

The spell was known to the Red Wizards of Thay, but had been abandoned by . Its use was far more common in the form of scrolls of protection by that time.
