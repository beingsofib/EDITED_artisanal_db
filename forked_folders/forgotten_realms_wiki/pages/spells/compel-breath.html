Compel breath

Spell
nameCompel Breath
school3eEnchantment (Compulsion)
level3eBard 3 Sorcerer 3 Wizard 3

Compel breath was a spell that targeted dragons.

Effects

When cast, this compelled a targeted dragon to use its breath weapon, whether it wanted to or not.

Components

This spell required only a verbal component.
