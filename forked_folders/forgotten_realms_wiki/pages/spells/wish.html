Wish was arguably the most powerful spell that a sorcerer or wizard could hope to learn.

Effects

When cast, it allowed its caster to accomplish multiple, various effects ranging from duplicating other spell effects to altering reality to suit the caster's wish.

Components

The spell required only a verbal component to cast.

History

The wish spell was not crafted until a hundred years after the Fall of Netheril.
