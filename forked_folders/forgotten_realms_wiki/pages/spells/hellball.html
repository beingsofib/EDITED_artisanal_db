Hellball

A hellball was a rare epic evocation spell that produced a mighty explosive ball of energy.

Effects

As of the , the caster quickly created a ball of energy that did enormous acid, fire, electrical, and sonic damage to everything in its blast radius upon detonation, by pointing their finger at the desired target. The hellball had a range of 300 feet (91.44 meters) and a 40-foot (12.19 meters) radius spread upon impact.

The spell could not be countered, interrupted, or dispelled using normal spells. To prevent the spell from being cast, one had to, for example, use an epic version of counterspell. Fully avoiding the damage of the spell using even the most amazing physical feats of dexterity was almost impossible.

Casting such epic magic could sometimes significantly hurt the caster, The spell was, however, very difficult to control.

Development

Before it could be cast, hellball had to be developed. Both divine and arcane casters could develop this spell. Developing an epic spell was different from learning normal spells. Once an epic spell was developed, the caster knew the spell. A developed epic spell became a part of the caster and could even be prepared without a spellbook (if a wizard was the caster).

To develop the spell, 810,000 gp and 17 days were needed along with precise arcane procedures. Only spellcasters well-versed in spellcraft could develop hellball.

Components

The spell required verbal and somatic components. The spell could also harm the caster's knowledge, but it depended on the variation.

Variations

Some variations inflicted damage upon the user for casting it, while others didn't. Depending on the version, the spell could also harm the mind of the user. In all cases however, friendly damage and self-damage were very much a possibility.
