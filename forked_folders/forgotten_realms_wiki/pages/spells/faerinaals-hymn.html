Faerinaal's hymn

Spell
nameFaerinaal's Hymn
school3eEnchantment (Compulsion)
level3eBard 2

Named for the consort to the eladrin queen Morwel, Faerinaal's hymn was an enchantment spell for bards that prevented opportunistic attacks by evil creatures.

Effect

By playing a gentle hymn, the spell clouded the minds of evil beings in close range who heard it, making them forget to attack when an opportunity presented itself. It lasted as long as the bard concentrated and continued playing it.

Components

It required only a verbal component, being the hymn itself.
