Cloud of bewilderment

Spell
nameCloud of Bewilderment
school3eEvocation
level3eBard 2 Sorcerer 2 Wizard 2

Cloud of bewilderment was a spell used by bards, sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell allowed the caster to exhale a cloud of rancid air. This noxious gas was invisible and any creature coming into contact with it would be temporarily blinded and stunned.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
