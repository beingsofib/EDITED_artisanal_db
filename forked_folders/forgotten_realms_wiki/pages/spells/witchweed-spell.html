Witchweed (spell)

Spell
nameWitchweed
school2eEvocation
level2eWizard 3

Witchweed was an uncommon evocation spell of the southern magic tradition.

Effects

The spell created an anti-magic cloud of smoke with a radius of 40 feet (12 meters). Any wizard within the cloud was unable to cast spells. The cloud also inflicted damage. The cloud did not move; only a gust of wind spell was able to move the cloud.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component, tobacco, which was burned during casting.
