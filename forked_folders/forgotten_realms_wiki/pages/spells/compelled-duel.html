Compelled duel was an enchantment spell that by divine command compelled a duel with a target.

Effect

The caster would compel a duel with a target within . If the target failed to resist, then it would have to try and engage with the caster. The target was at a disadvantage when attacking others and if the caster attacked someone else the spell would end.

Components

Only a vocal component was needed for the casting.

History

Some time during or before the late 1490s DR, the Thayan paladin Xenk Yendar compelled the elder brain Zuphelithid to a duel to the death. Xenk emerged as the victor.
