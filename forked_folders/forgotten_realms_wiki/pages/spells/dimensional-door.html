Dimensional door

Psionic Power
nameDimensional Door
discipline2ePsychoportation
level2eMinor (Devotion)
type2eStandard power

Dimensional door was a psionic devotion of the psychoportation discipline that allowed the psionicist to open a portal leading to another dimension.

Effects

The human-sized shimmering portal created by this power allowed the psionicist to step through and appear anywhere within through another portal. The portals would remain as long as the psionicist expended psychic energy. The portals could only be used from a single side, allowing up to five individuals to step through at a time.

Stepping through a dimensional door was disorienting, dazing travelers and preventing them from performing complex tasks for a minute after traveling.

Notable Users

Individuals

  • Vhondryl, a psionicist in Skullport and head of the Hall of Mental Splendor
