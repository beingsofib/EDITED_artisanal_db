Ghost rigging was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Effects

This spell created a ghostly rigging on a sailing vessel that functioned just as normal rigging did. This spell was often used to replace damaged rigging while at sea. The ghostly rigging matched the vessel's needs.

This spell would not function when cast in the phlogiston.

Components

This spell required a bit of canvass and string to cast.
