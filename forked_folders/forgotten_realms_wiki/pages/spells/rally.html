Rally was a spell used by clerics.

Effects

When cast, this spell rallied a unit of up to 300 individuals. The unit had to be within sight of the caster and no farther than away.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component, a duplicate of the targeted unit's banner or pendant, in miniature. This could include the flag of the unit's nation or liege lord.
