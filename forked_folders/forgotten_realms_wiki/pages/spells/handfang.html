Handfang was an necromancy/transmutation spell that conjured a fanged mouth within the caster's hand.

Effects

The serpentine mouth would take the place of the caster's hand for at least half a minute. The caster could in effect bite down with the jaws of their snake-like appendage and hold on to them with its fangs until they broke free, or the spell's duration finished.

Notable Users

Some time in the early 14 century DR, an apprentice of Halaster created a living spell that was a combination of handfang and magic mouth, known later as the Hand That Sings.
