Seeking mote was an evocation spell that conjured a bolt of light that streaked towards a target.

Effects

When this spell was cast, the mote appeared from the caster's body and streaked towards a targeted person or creature. If the mote wasn't destroyed or dispelled en route to its target, it struck them and inflicted damage.

History

The specifications of seeking mote were inscribed upon one of the "inner petals" of the Chauntean relic, the Glarathra.

Components

This spell required verbal, somatic and two material components: a bit of dust and a speck of translucent gemstone, both of which had to be exposed to direct sunlight.
