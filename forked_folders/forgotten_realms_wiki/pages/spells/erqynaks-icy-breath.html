Erqynak's icy breath

Spell
nameErqynak's Icy Breath
inventorErqynak
school2eEvocation

Erqynak's icy breath was an arcane evocation spell that rapidly chilled water without freezing it.

Effects

A submerged spellcaster used this spell to chill a cone of water in front of them below the water's actual freezing point without forming ice. It was a particularly useful offensive spell when underwater.

History

This spell was developed by the ixzan Erqynak.
