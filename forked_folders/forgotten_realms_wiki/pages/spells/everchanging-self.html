Everchanging self was a multi-school spell that allowed a cleric of Mask to continually change their form for a limited time.

Effects

When this spell was cast, the Maskarran cleric's appearance changed significantly and repeatedly for some time. The types of alterations included increased or decreased size, different shades and textures of skin and the addition of superfluous limbs. The types of changes were beyond their control and left a great toll on their body.

This spell did not confer increased strength or improved senses, nor did it confer additional means of locomotion.

Components

This spell required verbal and somatic components in order to be cast.
