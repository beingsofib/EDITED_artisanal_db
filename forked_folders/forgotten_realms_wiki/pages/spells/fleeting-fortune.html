Fleeting fortune

Spell
nameFleeting Fortune
deityTymora
school3eTransmutation
level3eInitiate of Tymora 1

Fleeting fortune was a spell used by clerics of Tymora.

Effects

When cast, this spell imbued a targeted creature with a small bit of the luck of Tymora.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a divine focus.
