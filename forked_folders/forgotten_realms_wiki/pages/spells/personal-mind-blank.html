Personal mind blank

Personal Mind blank, and devotion of the telepathy discipline that allowed a manifester to hide their mind from psionic attacks.

Effects

Mind blank created a vast featureless void in the psionicist's mind, making it extremely difficult for others to target their mind. Though highly effective against an id insinuation attack, mind blank was susceptible to a mind thrust.

Display

When this power was used it manifested an olfactory display.

Users

This power was learnable by psionicists, psychic warriors, wilders, and lurks, as well as ardents and divine minds that followed the repose mantle.

Mind blank was innately exhibited as a power by amethyst dragons, brain coral, brain moles, braxat, crystal dragons, emerald dragons, hook spiders, ikiryo, adult intellect devourers and their larval form, ustilagor, observers, ruby dragons, sapphire dragons, thought eaters, and topaz dragons.

On the desert world of Athas, this power was commonly exhibited by muls.

In psionics terms, it could be known by couatl, duergar, psionically gifted gray oozes, and ki-rin, mind flayers, githyanki and githzerai, and yuan-ti, as well as by astral devas, planetars, and solars among aasimon; by amnizu, cornugons, and gelugons among baatezu; by balors, babau, hezrou, mariliths, nalfeshnee, succubi, and only by arcanaloths among yugoloths.

The duergar goddess Deep Duerra was sometimes known to manifest her influence as a crown-like nimbus of silver light around a creature's head. For the next ten minutes, the target's reservoir of psionic power would quadruple and they would have some kind of psionic defense surrounding them. Mind blank was among the many defense modes this could be.

Known/Notable Users

  • Awtawmatawn, a unique stone golem that was possessed by the horned devil Rotbite.
  • Elyse, a street urchin in Ravens Bluff in the late 1350s DR.
  • Illistyl Elventree, a member of the Knights of Myth Drannor.
  • Talib the Lich-Killer, a wily old Zakharan necromancer of the deathslayer variety.
  • Vhondryl, a psionicist in Skullport and head of the Hall of Mental Splendor

Mind blank was a commonly exhibited power among the avatars of deities, though a select few stood out for having a far more limited selection of psionic defense modes. The deities who counted mind blank among their three psionic defenses were as follows: Gruumsh, the Furies, Osiris, Prometheus, Ratri, Skerrit, Yama.
