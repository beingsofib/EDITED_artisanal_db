Wall of light

Spell
nameWall of Light
school5eEvocation
level5eSorcerer 5 Warlock 5 Wizard 5

Wall of light was an evocation spell that created an opaque wall of extremely bright light.

Effects

The spell created a straight wall up to long, tall and thick out to a range of . The wall could appear in any orientation and did not need to rest on any surface, so floating walls of light in vertical, horizontal, or diagonal positions were possible.

The wall was so bright that any creature caught within it risked getting burned and being afflicted with temporary blindness. The damage inflicted by the spell increased as the caster grew more powerful.

Additionally, the wall could consume part of itself to shoot beams of burning light at the caster's command, that had a range of . Each beam consumed of wall. The spell lasted for up to 10 minutes, as long as the caster kept concentrating, or until it was entirely converted into fired beams.

Components

The spell required verbal, somatic, and material components The material required was a small hand mirror.
