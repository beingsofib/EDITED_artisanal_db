Comet (spell)

Spell
nameComet
school2eUniversal
level2eSorcerer 9 Wizard 9

Comet was a powerful spell used by sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

The caster was able to use this spell to reign destruction on a target. When cast, a large comet or meteor would impact the targeted area, causing massive destruction and shock waves. The caster could choose a target within . The caster and their allies were not harmed by this spell.
