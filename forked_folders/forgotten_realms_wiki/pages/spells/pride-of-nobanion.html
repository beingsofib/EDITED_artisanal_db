Pride of Nobanion

Pride of Nobanion was a divine conjuration spell used by specialty priests devoted to Nobanion called Firemanes, enabling them to summon feline aid.

Effects

This spell allowed the caster to call a number of great cats within to their side, drawing the strongest specimens available. The great cats would aid the caster in their predicament and then wander away back into the wild.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
