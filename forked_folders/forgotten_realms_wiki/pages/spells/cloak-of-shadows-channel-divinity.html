Cloak of shadows (channel divinity)

Cloak of shadows was a channel divinity power granted to clerics of the Trickery domain.

Effects

A cleric with the Trickery domain could turn invisible until he or she attacked or cast a spell.

Components

Only the cleric's skill was needed to evoke this power.
