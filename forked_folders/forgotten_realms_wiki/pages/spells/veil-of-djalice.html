Veil of Djalice, also called the glamour glamer, was a divine illusion spell used by priests devoted to Sune, enabling them to temporarily enhance a target's beauty to otherworldly levels.

This spell was named after the priestess Djalice who developed it.

Effects

This spell altered the target's beauty and grace to unimaginable levels. Individuals of the same race as the target were completely smitten with them for the spell's duration provided they were not deaf or blind. The veil of Djalice lasted for six minutes.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
