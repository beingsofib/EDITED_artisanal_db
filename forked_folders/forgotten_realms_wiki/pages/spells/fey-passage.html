Fey passage was a ritual that activated or forced open a fey crossing, thus allowing for travel between the Prime Material plane and the Feywild. Some fey crossings could only be traversed by casting fey passage.

Effects

Upon completing this ritual, a nearby fey crossing was opened and the caster (alongside up to eight companions) was instantly transported from the Prime Material plane to the Feywild or vice versa, arriving at the site that corresponded to the other side of the fey crossing.

A sufficiently powerful fey could prevent fey passage from functioning normally if they opposed its use or wished to stop the caster from escaping their domain.

Components

The ritual entailed 140 gp worth of material components and took ten minutes to enact.

Because of their innate and special connection to the Feywild, eladrin could cast fey passage without any material cost.

History

As of the late 15 century DR, scrolls of fey passage sold for 360 gp.
