Turtle soup (spell)

Spell
nameTurtle Soup
inventorAlustriel Silverhand
school2eAlteration, Evocation
level2eWizard 1

Turtle soup was a wizard spell created by Alustriel Silverhand. In fact, it was the first spell she developed by herself.

Effects

This spell created a bowl of thick, peppery turtle soup. The volume of the soup matched the caster's head. The soup was held in place by the spell's dweomer, matching a bowl shape. The soup would endure after the spell expired, though it would behave like a normal liquid at that point and spill if not positioned into a normal container.

This nourishing soup matched whatever temperature the caster desired.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required three drops of water and a piece of turtle shell or skin to cast.

Notable Users

  • Both Elminster and Khelben Arunsun knew this spell. Though they most often would cast it to drench the head of an unexpecting foe, rather than using it to gain a meal.
