Ritual of renaming

Ritual of renaming was a spell that forever transformed the creature's true name.

Effects

A successful ritual of renaming completely removed all connection between the target and its prior true name, giving it a new true name in its stead. This new truename was known only to the caster, the target, and those attending.

Components

Alongside verbal components and somatic components, the ritual called for a loss to the caster's self, their energy, their experience, and it demanded the subject's true name be spoken correctly, which was often a taxing truespeak task. If the true name was not spoken correctly, the spell failed.

History

This spell was necessary in order to become a bereft.
