Jergal's mind probe was a divine necromancy and divination spell used by specialty priests devoted to Jergal called Doomscribes.

Effects

This bizarre spell combined the ESP spell with the paralyze spell, allowing the caster to attack a single target by touching them. The target would be paralyzed for several minutes, allowing the priest to probe every corner of their mind for information regarding the target's faith, adherence to that faith, how they dealt with their strengths and weaknesses, and their biggest failures and accomplishments. The spell would end if the priest attempted to cast another spell or stopped concentration on probing the target's mind. The casting of this spell was viewed as a violation significant enough for the victim to develop a strong hatred for the caster.

This spell had no effect upon creatures immune to paralysis, mindless creatures such as some undead and golems, and nonintelligent creatures.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components the priest's holy symbol to cast.
