Communication mantle

The Communication mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concept and philosophy of communication.

Abilities

Those who followed the communication mantle became far more eloquent and skilled communicators when psionically focused, making them more better equipped to resolve matters diplomatically.

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the communication mantle created an effect similar to mindlink between the manifester and their nearby allies, so long as they remained within the aura's radius.

Powers

Novice manifesters were able to send telepathic messages with missive and subtly alter the behaviors of others with attraction, psionic charm, and telempathic projection. Manifesters of some experience gained greater telepathic abilities with mass missive and psionic tongues, as well as greater manipulation with psionic suggestion. As a manifester progressed further they learned how to cooperatively use their psionics with metaconcert and telepathically talk from great distances with correspond. Masters of this mantle obtained the power of metafaculty, allowing them to discern information on any subject.
