Protection from the elements

Spell
nameProtection from the Elements
school2eAbjuration, Flame, Sea, Sand, Wind
level2eWizard 4

Protection from the elements was a spell used commonly in Zakhara, the Land of Fate. This spell was only usable by wizards.

This spell was used primarily by genies upon their servants.

Effects

The target of this spell was immune to most harmful effects of a single elemental plane. The caster would need to either touch the target on the forehead, or kiss them on the lips, hand or cheek, while sprinkling the material components.

Protection from the elements lasted for a full week if cast by a genie. The duration was severely limited to several hours when cast by any other creature.

Components

This spell required different material components depending on the desired elemental protection required:

  • Air: perfume
  • Earth: chalk
  • Water: salt
  • Fire: sulphur
