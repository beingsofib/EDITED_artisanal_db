Force screen

Force screen was a psionic power of the psychokinesis discipline that granted its user a protective shield of pure force.

Effects

When manifested, this created a invisible disk of force that followed the manifester, always hovering in front of them. This provided the manfiester creator protection from attacks, including incorporeal ones due to it being force-based power. The power lasted for a duration of one minute, extended by one or more additional minutes depending upon how skilled the psionics user was.

Displays

When used this power manifested an auditory display.

Users

This power was knowable by psions, psychic warriors, and wilders, as well as ardents and divine minds who followed the force mantle.

Force screen was innately possessed by thoon hulks, who had a force screen around their bodies at all times.

Known/Notable Users

  • Sysalok, a yuan-ti abomination psychic warrior.
