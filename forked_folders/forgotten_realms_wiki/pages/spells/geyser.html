Geyser was a very rare spell used by druids.

Effects

When cast on a target, the spell created a geyser in the targeted location. The geyser erupted, shooting steam and scalding water to a height of . The column of water could be up to in diameter.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required material components.
