Ysgard (rune)

Spell
nameYsgard
type2eRune

Ysgard was a type of giant rune and was considered one of the most powerful.

Effects

Once activated, this rune was said to create in place of a doorway a temporary portal between Toril and Annam All-Father's crystal palace Gudheim in Jotunheim. Once closed, this portal could not be re-opened from the Outer Planes.

The number of minutes for which this portal remained open was equivalent to the level of the runecaster that activated it.

Components

Like most giant runes, it required no material components to cast, though the caster needed to know the giant rune Annam's unblinking eye. The process of shaping this rune could take anywhere from one to six weeks and it needed to be inscribed upon a closed doorway.
