Razorfangs was a spell that could only be cast by dragons.

Effects

This spell was an improved version of the sharptooth spell. When cast, this allowed a dragon to alter their teeth to make them incredibly hard, sharp and strong. It was cast before battle or when a dragon was trying to eat a rather hard-to-chew meal, like a knight.

Casting

This spell required only a verbal component.
