Healing sting

Spell
nameHealing Sting
school3eNecromancy
level3eDruid 3

Healing sting was a spell used by druids.

Effects

When cast, the spell allowed the caster to absorb the life force of another creature. When the caster inflicted damage onto another creature, they absorbed the equivalent amount of life energy.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, five dead and dried wasps.
