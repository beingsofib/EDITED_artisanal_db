Divine word

Spell
nameDivine word
schoolEvocation
school5eEvocation
level5eCleric 7

Divine word was an evocation spell where the caster spoke a word imbued with the power of the first creation.

Effect

Casting this spell the caster would chose creatures within 30 feet whom had to attempt to resist the spellpower of the caster. If the chosen are celestials, elementals, feys or fiends and cant resist the spell are sent to there natural plane and cant return for 24 hours unless they cast wish.

The chosen will be affected dependent on how healthy they individually are in 4 levels. The healthiest were deafened for a minute. The level below are deafened and blinded for 10 minutes. Below that are blinded, stunned, deafened for 10 minutes. The last are instantly killed.

Components

Only a verbal component are needed for the casting of this spell.
