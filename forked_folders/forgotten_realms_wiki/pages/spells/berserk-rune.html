Berserk (rune)

Spell
nameBerserk
type2eRune

Berserk was a type of giant rune that allowed its caster to enter a berserk state.

Effects

During battle the wielder of a weapon inscribed with this rune could call upon its power to enter into a state of berserk fury. While in this state, the wielder's strength and accuracy was enhanced. However, until this effect concluded they would be compelled to fight, even after their intended enemies had been vanquished. Driving one to go so far as to attack their own allies.

Components

Like most giant runes, it required no material components to cast. The process of shaping this rune could take anywhere from one to four hours and it needed to be inscribed upon the haft or hilt of a weapon.
