Waukeen's silver tongue

Waukeen's silver tongue was a divine spell, or Channel Divinity, given to worshipers of Waukeen that granted them a second chance at a more favorable outcome in situations requiring diplomacy or when attempting to bluff or intimidate. Better or worse, the second outcome was final.
