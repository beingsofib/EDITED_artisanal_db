Hedge enchantment

Spell
nameHedge Enchantment
school2eEnchantment, Invocation
level2eWizard 5

Hedge enchantment was a lesser spell akin to enchant an item, which hedge wizards employed to make baubles.

Effects

The spell allowed the caster to labor for up to a week, weaving magic into the item. Only objects of little combat use could be created; the typical example involved embedding a minimal cantrip into the object, something that could be accomplished with a cantrip spell.

Components

Besides verbal and somatic components, the item to be enchanted was considered a material component.

History

The spell was only known by hedge wizards; it was common amongst their profession.
