Preserve wood

Spell
namePreserve Wood
school2eAbjuration, Artifice
level2eWizard 3

Preserve wood was an arcane abjuration spell that permanently protected a quantity of wood from magical alteration.

Effects

This spell could be cast on a wooden structure, such as a ship, to prevent the wood from being magically deformed by warp wood and similar spells. It could permanently protect up to 100 lb (45 kg) of wood per level of the caster, but could only be used on one structure per casting. For example, a portion of a ship's hull could be protected, but a separate casting would be required to protect a cargo crate aboard the ship.

It was possible for casters of higher level to overcome the protection provided by this spell.

Components

Only verbal and somatic components were required to cast this spell.

History

This spell was associated with the pirates roaming the Sea of Fallen Stars.
