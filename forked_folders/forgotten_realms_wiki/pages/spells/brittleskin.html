Brittleskin

Spell
nameBrittleskin
school3eTransmutation
level3eDruid 3 Ranger 3 Sorcerer 3 Wizard 3

Brittleskin was a spell that caused the natural armor of a creature to become hard and brittle. If that creature were struck in combat, the crystallized tissue (such as hide, bone, chitin, scales, etc) would crack and shatter, causing more permanent and often lethal damage.
