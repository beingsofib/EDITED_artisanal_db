Energy containment

Psionic Power
nameEnergy Containment
discipline2ePsychometabolism
level2eMajor (Science)
type2eStandard power

Energy containment was a psionic science of the psychometabolism discipline that allowed the psionicist to draw harmful energy into their body.

Effects

This powerful science allowed the psionicist to absorb harmful energy from fire, cold, heat, sound, or electricity and redirect it with a release of light. The psionicist would radiate light for several minutes depending on how much energy was absorbed. The light emitted was soft and only illuminated the area within of the psionicist.

Notable Users

Individuals

  • Vhondryl, a psionicist in Skullport and head of the Hall of Mental Splendor
