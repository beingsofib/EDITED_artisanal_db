Burst

Psionic Power
nameBurst
discipline3ePsychoportation
level3eLurk 1 Nomad 1 Psychic warrior 1
type3eStandard power
Talent

Burst was a psionic power of the psychoportation discipline that had the simple effect of granting a temporary boost to a targeted creature's land speed.

Effects

Some claimed that the power could be targeted on other creatures, out to a range of plus additional increments of depending upon the manifester's skills, while others attested that the power could only target the manifester. The power could be manifested rather quickly and lasted for no more than a duration of 1 minute.

Displays

When used burst manifested an auditory display.

Users

This power was knowable by lurks, psychic warriors, and psions who specialized as nomads.

Known/Notable Users

  • Raulothim, a great wyrm emerald dragon.
