Reflected image

Spell
nameReflected Image
school2eIllusion/Phantasm
level2eWizard 1

Reflected image was a spell used by wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell created a duplicate of the caster. The duplicate remained close to the caster and mimicked the movement of the caster precisely. If the duplicate was attacked, it vanished. This spell was vulnerable to dispel magic.
