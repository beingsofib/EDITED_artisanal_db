Protection from magic, 5' radius

Spell
nameProtection From Magic, 5' Radius
school1eAbjuration
level1eMagic-user 6

Protection from magic, 5' radius was a powerful spell used by the Red Wizards of Thay.

Effects

This spell protected the caster from all forms of magic. It created a small area of protection around the caster, 5 feet (1.5 meters) in radius. This protected area moved with the caster. If a magical item entered the protected area, it was drained of its magical properties.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
