Infratorch

Spell
nameInfratorch
school2eAlteration, Artifice
level2eWizard 2

Infratorch was an alteration spell that allowed the caster, along with anyone nearby, to see as if they possessed infravision.

Effects

When this spell was cast, a mage imbued a piece a wood with magic that cause it to act as if it were a magical torch that conferred infravision upon others. It did not radiate light or heat but did grant its beneficial effects to anyone within 15' (4.6 m).

Components

In addition to the verbal component, this spell required a single piece of wood, such as a staff, stick or even a snuffed-out torch.
