Repose mantle

The Repose mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concepts of rest and tranquility.

Abilities

Those who followed the repose mantle embodied the concept, finding it easier than others to relax their mind and concentrate. By expending a psionic focus, they could greatly improve their ability to concentrate.

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the repose mantle suppressed the negative effects of fatigue in both the manifester and all allies within the radius of the aura. If an ally of the manifester were to fall victim to exhaustion while within the radius of their aura, the manifester would take the burden upon themselves.

Powers

Novice manifesters could calm themselves and others through empty mind and serenity. Manifesters of some experience learned solicit psicrystal and could take on the wounds of others with empathic transfer. As a manifester progressed further they could make themselves immune to illusions with steadfast perception and suspend their bodies with suspend life. Masters of this mantle obtained personal mind blank, allowing them to become impervious to scrying and mental effects.
