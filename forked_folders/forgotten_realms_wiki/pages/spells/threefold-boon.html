Threefold boon

nameThreefold Boon

Threefold boon was a divine alteration spell that increased the chances an individual was successful in their actions, for a brief period of time.

Effects

When this spell was cast, a single recipient gained a higher chance of success with their actions. The boon affected their combat prowess, their ability to perform skilled tasks and their resilience towards certain spells, spell-like abilities, and other harmful effects.

History

The specifications of this spell appeared upon the Flame of the Spirit when it assumed its tablet form.

Components

This spell only required verbal and somatic components in order to be cast.
