Protection form poison was an abjuration spell that conveyed to the caster complete immunity to a single type of poison.

Effects

Upon casting protection from poison, If cast on another individual who was already suffering from poisoning, the spell eliminated the poison. The caster had to physically touch the individual and know what poison was causing the damage.

Casting

The spell required verbal, somatic and material components in its casting,
