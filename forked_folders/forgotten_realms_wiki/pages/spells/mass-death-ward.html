Mass death ward

Spell
nameMass Death Ward
school3eNecromancy
level3eCleric 8 Druid 9

Mass death ward was a divine necromancy spell that performed the effects of death ward for a group of creatures.

Effects

As with death ward, the spell protected its targets from effects that would instantly slay them or degrade their innate vitality in some way, such as with negative energy.

Components

The spell required verbal and somatic components to cast, in addition to a divine focus.
