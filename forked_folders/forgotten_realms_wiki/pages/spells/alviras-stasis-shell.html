Alvira's Stasis Shell was a very rare spell that Alvira created to protect objects.

Effects

The spell placed a faint blue glow upon the object that it was cast upon, which had to be between the sizes of a potion vial and a normal spellbook; the object became impossible to affect with anything short of disintegrate or a successful dispel magic spell. Though the object could be moved normally, unlike in stronger stasis spells, it was impossible to affect it much. Most attacks, even dragon breath, would simply slide off around the protected item. Though it could not affect living tissue directly, there had been reports of it working on living tissue inside an inanimate container. For all intents and purposes, time had stopped for the object within the field.

Components

Besides verbal and somatic components, the spell involved the protected object in its magic.

History

The spell had been developed by Alvira, a hedge wizard of Raven's Bluff;
