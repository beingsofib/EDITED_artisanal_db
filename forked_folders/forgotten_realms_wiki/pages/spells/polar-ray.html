Polar ray was a spell used by sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

When cast, a ray of freezing cold air and ice was released from the caster's hands towards a target. The caster could attack an opponent up to distant. The range and damage caused by the spell was determined by the caster's skill level.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a divine focus, either a small cone made from ceramic that was painted white or a prism.
