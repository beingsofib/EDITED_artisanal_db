Poison spray was a conjuration spell that created a cloud of poisonous gas.

Effects

When cast, a small cloud of poisonous gas was created from the caster's palm. This cloud enveloped a target creature. The spell was only effective as a short-range attack, as the target creature was required to be within 10 feet (3 meters) of the caster.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
