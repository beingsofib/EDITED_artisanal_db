Thought shield

Psionic Power
nameThought Shield

type1eDefense mode

Thought shield was a psionic defense mode and devotion of the telepathy discipline that allowed a manifester to create a glowing shield that repelled psionic attacks.

Effects

A thought shield excelled at protecting the mind against a psychic crush attack, but was susceptible to an ego whip.

Display

When used this power manifested an auditory display.

Users

This power was knowable by psionicists, psychic warriors, wilders, and lurks.

Thought shield was innately exhibited as a power by amethyst dragons, blue slaad, brain coral, braxat, crystal dragons, emerald dragons, dhours, grippli, hollyphants, ikiryo, adult intellect devourers, observers, obsidian dragons, ruby dragons, shedu, thought eaters, thri-kreen, and topaz dragons.

In psionics terms, it could be known by aboleths, duergar, couatl, githyanki and githzerai, as well as by astral devas, planetars, and solars among aasimon; by amnizu, cornugons, gelugons, and pit fiends among baatezu; by balors, mariliths, nalfeshnee, succubi, among tanar'ri; and only by arcanaloths among yugoloths.

On the desert world of Athas, this power was commonly exhibited by kirres, muls, and pyreen.

The duergar goddess Deep Duerra was sometimes known to manifest her influence as a crown-like nimbus of silver light around a creature's head. For the next ten minutes, the target's reservoir of psionic power would quadruple and they would have some kind of psionic defense surrounding them. Thought shield was among the many defense modes this could be.

Known/Notable Users

  • Awtawmatawn, a unique stone golem that was possessed by the horned devil Rotbite.
  • Elyse, a street urchin in Ravens Bluff in the late 1350s DR.
  • Gaulauntyr, an adult copper dragon.
  • Illistyl Elventree, a member of the Knights of Myth Drannor.
  • Raulothim, a great wyrm emerald dragon.
  • Vhondryl, a psionicist in Skullport and head of the Hall of Mental Splendor

Thought shield was a commonly exhibited power among the avatars of deities, though a select few stood out for having a far more limited selection of psionic defense modes. The deities who counted thought shield among their three psionic defenses were as follows: Gruumsh, the Furies, Osiris, Prometheus, Ratri, Skerrit, Yama.
