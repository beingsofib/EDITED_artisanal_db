Protection from paralysis

nameProtection From Paralysis
school2eAbjuration
level2eWizard 2

Protection from paralysis was an abjuration spell used by wizards.

Effects

This spell offered complete protection to the recipient from all forms of magical paralysis. In addition to protecting from spells such as hold person, the spell also protected against similar attacks from monsters such as a ghoul's paralyzing touch. The spell offered no protection from physical harm from monster attacks.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a bit of cloth from a cleric's robes to cast.

History

This spell was originally developed in the Underdark, but later on found usage by some on the surface.
