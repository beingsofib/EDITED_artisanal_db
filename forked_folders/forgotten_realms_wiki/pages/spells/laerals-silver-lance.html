Laeral's silver lance was an arcane evocation spell that created a column of force resembling a silver lance.

Effects

The caster pantomimed a throw and the lance streaked toward a single target, striking with a violent blast of force that often knocked the target off their feet in addition to causing physical harm. The range of the lance depended on the caster's skill, but could reach a minimum distance of .

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component a miniature silver lance engraved with runes and worth at least 250 gp to cast. The silver lance was used as the spell focus.

History

This spell was developed by Laeral Silverhand of Waterdeep.
