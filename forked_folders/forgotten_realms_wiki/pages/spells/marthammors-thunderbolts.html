Marthammor's thunderbolts was a spell used by clerics of Marthammor Duin.

Effects

When cast, this spell unleashed bolts of lightning from the eyes and hands of the caster. The caster was capable of striking a target up to away. The lightning had no effect on objects and could be blocked by barriers. They also did not fork nor rebound off of a surface.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required material components, the holy symbol of Marthammor Duin.
