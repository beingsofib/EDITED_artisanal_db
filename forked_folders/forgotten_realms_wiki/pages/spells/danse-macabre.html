Danse macabre

Spell
nameDanse Macabre
school5eNecromancy
level5eWarlock 5 Wizard 5

Danse macabre was a spell used by warlocks and wizards to raise the dead.

Effects

When cast, up to five corpses within were reanimated as undead. The caster was able to command the creatures. If no command was given, the undead only defended themselves if attacked. The undead followed the caster's command until it was fulfilled. When the spell expired, the spell was lifted and the undead collapsed, becoming corpses once more.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
