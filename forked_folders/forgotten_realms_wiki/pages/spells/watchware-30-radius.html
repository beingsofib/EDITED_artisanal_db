Watchware, 30' radius

Spell
nameWatchware, 30' Radius
school2eEvocation
level2eWizard 6

Watchware, 30' radius was an arcane evocation spell that was an improvement over the basic watchware spell.

Effects

When placed upon a fixed location, the magic of this spell extended for in every direction, alerting the caster to anything entering that area.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components a spider web, a small bell made from brass or silver, and a pinch of powdered glass to cast.

History

This was a rare spell in Faerûn.
