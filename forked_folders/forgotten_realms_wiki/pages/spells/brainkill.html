Brainkill

Spell
nameBrainkill
school2eNecromancy
level2eWizard 4

Brainkill was an arcane spell from the school of necromancy that allowed a wizard to erase a memory from the target's mind by destroying a portion of their brain.

Powers

This spell could be used to remove all memory of a person, place, or period of time of up to one year from the target's mind. If the target was able to resist the spell they would only experience a painful headache that lasted for several hours.

Components

This spell required only verbal components to cast.

History

This spell was found within the Book of Shadows, a tome based upon the oration of the necromancer Nebt Bhakau of Zakhara.
