Protection from breath weapon, non-dragon was a spell used by the Red Wizards of Thay.

Effects

This spell protected the caster from all forms of breath weapons, regardless of the alignment of the creature. This spell did not protect against a dragon's breath weapon.

Casting

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, a platinum piece. The coin was destroyed during casting.
