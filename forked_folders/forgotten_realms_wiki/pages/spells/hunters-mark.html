Hunter's mark was a simple ranger spell, which could also be cast by paladins who had taken the Oath of Vengeance.

Effects

The caster could select a target from up to away, and cast hunter's mark upon them. The target was then more susceptible to regular weapon attacks of the caster, hurting them more than normal. The spell lasted for up to an hour, but only if the caster was able to maintain concentration for the long time period. Very experienced spellcasters could maintain the concentration required for the spell for up to a day.

Components

The spell only required a verbal component.

History

As of the late 15 century DR, the ability to use this power was a prerequisite to be trained as an Oman giantkiller of the Moonshae Isles.
