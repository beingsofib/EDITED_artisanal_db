Natural World mantle

The Natural World mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concept of nature and the wilderness.

Abilities

Those who followed the Natural World mantle exhibited an empathy towards nature comparable to druids,

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the Natural World mantle had a minute restorative effect, healing any animals, plants, or fey within its radius.

Powers

Novice manifesters gained the power to blend into their surroundings with chameleon power, to psionically enhance their body's natural weaponry with metaphysical claw, and enhance their searching abilities in nature with stone mind. Manifesters of some experience gained psionic scent and the power to enhance one of their natural traits with animal affinity. As a manifester progressed further they could mimic a druid's wild shape abilities with metamorphosis, harden their flesh with oak body, and psionically animate plants to fight for him. Masters of this mantle obtained psionic earthquake, allowing them to unleash intense tremors in the earth.
