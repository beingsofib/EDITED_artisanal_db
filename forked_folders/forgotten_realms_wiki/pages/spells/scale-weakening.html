Scale weakening

Spell
nameScale Weakening
school3eTransmutation
level3eSorcerer 2 Wizard 2

Scale weakening was a spell used to weaken the natural armor of creatures.

Effects

The spell generated a dull gray ray out of the caster's fingertips, which could be used to attack a creature in relatively close range. This creature's natural armor would be mitigated for several minutes. While it could dampen the impact of its natural armor down to none, it could not create new weaknesses, and neither did it have any effect on the resilience of its flesh granted due to spells that improved it.

Components

In addition to somatic and verbal components, the spell called for a shed snake skin.
