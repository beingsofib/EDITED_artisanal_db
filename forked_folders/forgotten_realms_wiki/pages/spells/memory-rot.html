Memory rot

Spell
nameMemory rot
school3eEvocation
level3eDruid 5

Memory rot was an evocation spell used by druids.

Effects

This spell created a cloud of spores that invaded the brain of the target creature, destroying their mind in the process. The spores permanently damaged the creature's brain.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
