Remove magic

Spell
nameRemove Magic
school2eAbjuration
level2eWizard 3

Remove magic was a spell used by wizards.

Effects

This spell worked in the same way as dispel magic but was only effective against the enemies of the caster. The spell did not function against magical items. The effectiveness of the spell was determined by the caster's spell level.
