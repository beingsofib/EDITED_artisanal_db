Golden dragonmail

Golden dragonmail was a spell used by sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell created a suit of of radiant golden plate mail on the caster. Sketched on the plates were patterns of dragonscale, mimicking a gold dragon. The caster could also choose to have the symbol of Torm sketched on the plate. If the caster removed the suit of armor, or it was destroyed, the spell expired. If the caster was already wearing armor, the spell had no effect.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required material components, a coin of solid gold and mithral in the shape of a dragonscale.
