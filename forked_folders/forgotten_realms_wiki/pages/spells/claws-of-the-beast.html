Claws of the beast

Spell
nameClaws of the Beast
school3eTransmutation
level3eDruid 1 Ranger 2

Claws of the beast was a spell used by druids and rangers.

Effects

When cast, this spell caused razor-sharp claws to grow from the caster's fingertips. They caused considerable damage during melee combat.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
