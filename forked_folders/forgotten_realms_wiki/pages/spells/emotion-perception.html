Emotion perception

nameEmotion Perception
school2eDivination
spheres2eWar sphere
level2eCleric 2

Emotion perception was a divine divination spell from the war sphere that allowed the caster to get a sense of the emotional state and morale of a military unit.

Effects

In order for the spell to function the caster needed to have an uninterrupted line of sight to the military unit.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component the priest's holy symbol to cast.

History

Watcher Tonorak Winthrax, a priest of Helm, could cast emotion perception.
