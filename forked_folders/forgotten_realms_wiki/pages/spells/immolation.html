Immolation

Spell
nameImmolation
school5eEvocation
level5eSorcerer 5 Wizard 5

Immolation was a cruel evocation spell that consumed a creature in flames for a long period of time.

Effects

The spell targeted a creature within a range of . If unable to dodge the effect, the creature was immediately enveloped by bright, magical flames that could not be quenched by any non-magical means. The fire continued to burn for as long as 1 minute, as long as the caster kept concentrating, or until the victim managed to successfully evade the flames. A creature killed by this spell was completely turned to ashes, leaving no body behind.

Components

The spell only required verbal components.

History

Immolation was favored by some members of the Cult of the Eternal Flame in the late 15 century DR.
