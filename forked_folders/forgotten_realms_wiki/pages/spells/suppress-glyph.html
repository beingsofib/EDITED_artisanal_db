Suppress glyph was a transmutation spell used by clerics. The spell originated from Deneir, the Lord of All Glyphs.

Effects

This spell enhanced the caster's awareness to magical writings, bathing such writings in pale blue light. The caster could read magical writings without triggering any harmful effects for the spell's duration.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
