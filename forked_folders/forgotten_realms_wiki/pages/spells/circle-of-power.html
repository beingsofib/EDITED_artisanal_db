Circle of power was an abjuration spell for paladins that created a sphere of divine radiance centered on the caster.

Effects

The sphere gave all allies of the caster within it an advantage to resist spells cast against them. An affected ally who was able to withstand a spell and suffer only partial damage would instead take no damage from the spell.

Components

Only a verbal component was needed.
