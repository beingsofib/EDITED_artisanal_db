Right of might was a divine alteration and enchantment spell used only by specialty priests devoted to Gilgeam called Martinets.

Effects

This spell empowered the caster by combining three other spells–enlarge, command, and strength. Casters influenced by this spell were larger, stronger, and their voice was capable of subduing others. The size of the caster's equipment also increased proportionately.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component the Martinet's holy symbol to cast.
