Phobia amplification

Psionic Power

Phobia amplification was a psionic devotion of the telepathy discipline that allowed a psionicist to amplify one of a target's greatest fears into an irrational phobia.

Effects

Able to affect people from any distance, phobia amplification caused a target to believe that they were imminently in danger of their greatest fear, even if they were in a situation where it was impossible for that fear to be present. For example, a target with a fear of falling would be made to feel they were imminently in danger of falling over a cliff, even if they were standing in the middle of a prairie.

Reactions to this effect varied amongst individuals, but their actions would uniformly be centered around ensuring their protection from whatever object or situation they were fearful of. Some might still defend themselves when attacked (unless defending was somehow tied in to the phobia), others might freeze in place or flee. In the case of magic users like wizards, some would resort to using their spells to guarantee their safety or try to teleport away from the danger.

The effect of phobia amplification lasted for as long as the psionicist maintained it. Upon being ceased, the target's fear would fade back to whatever its base degree of fearfulness was.

Prerequisite Powers

In order to learn and make use of phobia amplification, a manifester first had to know and use the powers contact and mindlink in order to access the fears buried within a target's mind.

Users

This power was exhibited as an innate psionic ability amongst dhours and yochlol.

In psionics terms, phobia amplification was innately known by yuan-ti.

Notable Users

  • Ryvvik Dlardrageth, a cambion psionicist.
  • The Watchers, a group of illithids that served as the early-warning system for the Underdark community of Nar'Vheen, included phobia amplification among their repertoire of powers to sus out the greatest fears of intruders.
