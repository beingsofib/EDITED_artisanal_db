Brilliant aura

nameBrilliant Aura
school3eTransmutation
level3eDruid 7

Brilliant aura was a spell used by druids.

Effects

When cast, the spell outlined the caster's allies in a luminous vapor. As a result, their melee and ranged weapons temporarily became energy weapons. The energy weapons were strong enough to penetrate most armor but had no effect on constructs, objects or undead creatures. The aura illuminated the surrounding area up to around each ally.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
