Time mantle

The Time mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionic powers and abilities derived from the universal concept of time.

Abilities

Those who followed the Time mantle became more in tune with the flow of time, allowing them to react to things faster. Additionally, by expending a psionic focus, a manifester could delay the onset of a magical effect, psionic effect, or the pain and wounding of an attack. This would eventually affect the manifester as it would have at the moment it struck, meaning a a manifester could not obtain immunity in the interim to circumvent harm entirely.

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the Time mantle granted the manifester and any allies within the radius of their aura brief glimpses into the future, allowing them to react to things much faster.

Powers

Novice manifesters gained the power to slow down time with decelerate and slow their own bodily processes with sustenance. Manifesters of some experience could rapidly react with anticipatory strike and send themselves minutes into the future with time hop. As a manifester progressed further they learned how to speed up the passage of time with temporal acceleration and how to send a group of people minutes into the future with mass time hop. Masters of this mantle obtained time regression, allowing them to turn back time.
