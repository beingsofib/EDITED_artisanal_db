Vicious mockery

nameVicious Mockery

level5eBard 0
type5eCantrip

Vicious mockery was a cantrip used by bards to hurl insults at a creature.

Effects

A bard used this spell to hurl enchantment-powered insults at a target creature. The creature had to be within of the bard. For the spell to be effective, the creature had to be able to hear the bard, but did not need to speak the same language. As a result of this spell, the target creature suffered minor psychic damage.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component: an eye of a newt, which had to be petrified.
