Strength (rune)

Spell
nameStrength
type2eRune

Strength was a type of giant rune that improved upon the physical skills of whoever wielded its inscribed object.

Effects

Those who wore the inscribed helmet or girdle would be granted a mild improvement to their strength. The duration of this effect was equal to the caster's level. Upon removal of the runed object this effect would instantly cease.

If a caster extensively studied this rune a second time, then the casted effect would improve a wearer's strength by twice as much.

Components

Like most giant runes, it required no material components to cast, though the caster needed to know the giant rune healing. The process of shaping this rune could take anywhere from one to twelve hours and it needed to be inscribed upon either a helmet or girdle.
