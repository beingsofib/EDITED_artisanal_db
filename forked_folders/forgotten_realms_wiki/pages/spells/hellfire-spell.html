Hellfire (spell)

Spell
nameHellfire
school3eEvocation
domains3eDiabolic
level3eDiabolic domain Diabolic 4

Hellfire was an evocation spell.

Effects

By casting hellfire, the caster caused an explosion with a radius of 10 foot (3 meters) of brimstone and fire. This fire bypassed every kind of magical and other defenses against fire.

Components

Hellfire required only verbal and somatic components.

History

Hellfire was a spell that was only available to people who had access to the Diabolic domain.
