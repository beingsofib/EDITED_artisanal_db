Bursting sinew was a necromancy cantrip spell from both arcane and divine magics as well as death domain clerics.

Effects

Bursting sinew's target could be any visible corpse of a living creature within sixty feet of the caster. When cast, the target corpse exploded, impaling everyone around it. The cantrip's damage increased with the spellcaster's magical prowess and experience.
