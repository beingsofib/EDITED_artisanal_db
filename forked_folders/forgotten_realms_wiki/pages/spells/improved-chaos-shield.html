Improved chaos shield

Spell
nameImproved Chaos Shield
school2eAbjuration
level2eWizard 7

Improved chaos shield was a spell used by wild mages.

Effects

A wild mage used this spell to improve their casting results during a surge of magical energy. It was often cast after the Nahal's reckless dweomer spell.
