Choose future

Spell
nameChoose Future
school2eDivination
spheres2eTime sphere
level2eCleric 3

Choose future was a divine divination spell from the time sphere that granted the target the best outcome for a situation.

Effects

The target of this spell was allowed to perform a single act twice and pick whichever act had the more desirable outcome. The chosen act would then become reality.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components two grains of sand and a rose petal to cast.

History

The Tome of Torment, a holy book of the Church of Ilmater, contained the spell choose future.
