Sacred strike was a divine necromancy spell that created a whip-like lash of force energy within the hand of the caster.

Effects

The lash appeared as chain of twinkling, purple motes of light.

When wielded by the casting cleric the lash possessed the properties of an enchanted weapon. When it struck a living being it inflicted great pain upon them and healed the wielder. It was not very effective when used against the undead.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, sacred strike required the material components of a single hair taken from the caster and two drops of unholy water.
