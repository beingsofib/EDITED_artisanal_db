Ghastly hands

Spell
nameGhastly hands
school2eNecromancy
level2eWizard 3

Ghastly hands was a necromancy spell that transformed the spellcaster's hands to those of a ghast.

Effects

For the duration of the spell, the caster's mere touch was capable of paralyzation. They emitted a distinct, foul odor that was recognizable to anyone who had previous encountered a ghast.

The caster could end the effects of the spell at will, but any paralyzation they caused could not abated, and ran its course.

Casting

The casting of this spell required verbal and somatic components.

History

This spell was known to be used by wizards of the Cult of the Dragon.
