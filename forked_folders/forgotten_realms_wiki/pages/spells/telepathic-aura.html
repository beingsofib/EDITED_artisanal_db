Telepathic aura

Spell
nameTelepathic Aura
school3eDivination
level3ePaladin 4

Telepathic aura was a divine divination spell used by paladins that allowed the caster to connect with their companions telepathically.

Effects

Telepathic aura only allowed one-way communication where the caster could send thoughts to companions within range, but the companions could not reply with their own thoughts.

Components

This spell required only somatic components to cast.

History

Telepathic aura was found within a spellbook from the Church of the Red Knight called Master Tactician.
