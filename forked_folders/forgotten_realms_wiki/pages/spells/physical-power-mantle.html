Physical Power mantle

The Physical Power mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionic powers and abilities derived from the universal concept of raw physical might, centered around enhancing the manifester's own physical capabilities.

Abilities

Those who followed the Physical Power mantle gained the unique psionic ability to expend a psionic focus to temporarily enhance their physical capabilities, such as their strength or hardiness.

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the Physical Power mantle granted the manifester and any allies within the radius of their aura the phyiscal power to overcome aggressive acts of might, such as attempts to restrain them, trip them, or disarm them of their weapons.

Powers

Novice manifesters obtained adrenaline boost and vigor. Manifesters of some experience gained animal affinity and the power to meld weapons to their body with graft weapon. As a manifester progressed further they learned how to readjust their body with psychofeedback and harden their flesh like a tree with oak body. Masters of this mantle obtained true metabolism, enhancing their bodies to such a point that they became incredibly difficult to kill.
