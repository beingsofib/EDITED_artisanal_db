Spell shear

A Spell shear was an advanced elven practice of magic.

Powers

It prevailed against antimagic field.

History

In , Mythanthor survived an assassination attempt by Llombaerth Starym, in which he was joined by Elminster Aumar, by using a spell shear, which overcame Elminster's antimagic field, even after it was bolstered with a lifequench spell.
