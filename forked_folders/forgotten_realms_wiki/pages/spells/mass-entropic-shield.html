Mass entropic shield

Spell
nameMass Entropic Shield
school3eAbjuration
level3eCleric 3

Mass entropic shield was a divine abjuration spell that protected the caster and allies from ranged attacks. It worked just like the spell entropic shield but had an area of effect that extended beyond the spellcaster.
