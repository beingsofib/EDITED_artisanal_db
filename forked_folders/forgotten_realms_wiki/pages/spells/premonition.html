Premonition

Spell
namePremonition
school3eDivination
level3eDruid 8 Sorcerer 8 Wizard 8

Premonition was a powerful spell that allowed a caster to predict an attack.

Effects

This spell allowed the caster to gain a brief glimpse into the immediate future. By doing so the caster was given a reduction in the damage they would take.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
