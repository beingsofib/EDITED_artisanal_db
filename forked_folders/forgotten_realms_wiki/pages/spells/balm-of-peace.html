Balm of peace

Balm of peace was a channel divinity power granted to clerics who chose the Peace domain.

Effects

When a cleric of the peace domain channeled divinity whilst retreating, they could move out of the way of attacks without getting hit. If the caster was to move within another creature, the creature would be soothed with a healing balm. The wiser the cleric became, the more the creature would be healed.
