Spell

A spell was an ability or effect achieved through magic, and was cast by direct or indirect usage of the Weave.

Description

There were several types of spells in the Realms, though many of their definitions changed throughout the span of time, though they often pertained to formulas and techniques capable of producing various effects. The innate supernatural abilities that creatures had, even those that were related to the Weave, were not spells. Generally, magic-users manipulated the Weave directly to cast arcane spells, whilst priests relied on their deities to cast divine spells.

Spells, however, could be cast by using magic items, such as wands, and could be copied onto scrolls or into spellbooks.

Someone who could cast a spell of any kind was known as a spellcaster.

Notable Spells

  • Magic missile, a very well-known basic spell that was developed by General Matick in .
  • Extension I, developed in , was the first Netherese spell ever invented.
  • Karsus's avatar, a spell of immense power that momentarily made a mortal a god. This spell caused the Fall of Netheril.

Notable Spellcasters

  • Elminster, the Sage of Shadowdale, was one of the most powerful arcane spellcasters of the Realms, and even developed his own spells.
  • Gromph Baenre, potentially the most powerful arcane spellcaster of the Underdark.
  • Yvonnel Baenre, an extraordinarily powerful divine caster of Lolth.
  • Fzoul Chembryl, an exceptional divine caster of Bane.
