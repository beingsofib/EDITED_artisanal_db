Swordward

Spell
nameSwordward
deityTorm
school2eAlteration, Evocation, Necromancy
spheres2eProtection sphere, Necromantic
level2eCleric 6

Swordward was a powerful divine alteration, evocation, and necromancy spell used by priests devoted to Torm, enabling them to create a unique guardian effect.

Effects

All harm inflicted upon enemies within the swordward's area of effect would be transferred as healing magic to the caster or their allies. Furthermore, attacks from enemies within the area were less likely to strike and caused less physical harm. This spell could not coexist with a blade barrier.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components an amethyst worth at least 5 gp, a piece of a lodestone, and an edged weapon, or piece of one, that has drawn blood in battle to cast.
