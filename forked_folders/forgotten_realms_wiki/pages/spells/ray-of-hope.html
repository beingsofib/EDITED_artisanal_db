Ray of hope

Spell
nameRay of Hope
school3eEnchantment (Compulsion)
level3eBard 1 Cleric 1

Ray of hope was a simple enchantment spell that granted hope to a nearby target.

Effects

This spell granted a burst of hope and a boost of morale to a nearby creature. (Despite the name of this spell, it did not need to be aimed in order to take effect; no visible emanation came from the caster.)

This spell could counter the effects of the sorrow enchantment.

Components

This spell required verbal and somatic components.
