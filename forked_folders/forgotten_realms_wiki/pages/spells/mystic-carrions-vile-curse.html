Mystic Carrion's vile curse was a necromancy spell from the sixth circle of magic, created by Mystic Carrion, a mysterious mummy lord of Baldur's Gate.

Effects

When Mystic Carrion's vile curse was cast, it targeted a single target creature within sixty feet of the caster. If the target creature did not possess strong enough will, they fell victim to Mystic Carrion's blinding curse. This deadly curse poisoned, restrained, and blinded the creature, dealing large amounts of necrotic damage every six seconds the target remained affected by the spell and failed to break out from its foul magics.
