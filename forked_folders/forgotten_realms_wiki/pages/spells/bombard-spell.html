Bombard (spell)

Spell
nameBombard
school2eEvocation
level2eWizard 8

Bombard was a rare evocation spell of the southern magic tradition that bombarded a target with giant stones.

Effects

The caster was able to bombard a single target within a radius. The frequency of the bombardment was dependant on the caster's skill level. The spell expired if the target moved out of the range of the spell. Dispel magic would negate the spell, and creatures with magical resistance were somewhat protected from the effects of the spell.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component, a single piece of meteoric iron.
