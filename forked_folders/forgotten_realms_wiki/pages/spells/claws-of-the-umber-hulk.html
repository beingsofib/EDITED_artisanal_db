Claws of the umber hulk

Spell
nameClaws of the Umber Hulk
school2eAlteration, Earth
Universal (province)
level2eWizard 6

Claws of the umber hulk was an arcane alteration spell that increased the size and power of the target's hands, akin to that of an umber hulk. The spell effects were incredibly painful as the hands grew and reformed, but allowed the target to cut through rock, or burrow underground. The caster had to touch the target of this spell in order to cast it.

Effects

If the target's stamina allowed for it, the target could cut up to pure rock or six times that amount of dirt in ten minutes, without resting. To create a passable tunnel in soil, the digger had to proceed at half the maximum rate, taking care that the roof didn't collapse. The claws could also be used in combat, dealing significant additional damage beyond what their normal strength could inflict.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required the claw of an umber hulk.

History

This spell was originally developed in the Underdark, but later on could be found in use by some on the surface.
