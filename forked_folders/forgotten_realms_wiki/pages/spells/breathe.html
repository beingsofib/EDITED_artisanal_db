Breathe

Spell
nameBreathe
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 1

Breathe was an alteration spell of the southern magic tradition.

Effects

When cast, the spell allowed the caster or the target creature to breathe normally in a location or substance it would normally suffocate.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component. A cloth of any material was held over the caster's mouth while reciting the verbal component of the spell.
