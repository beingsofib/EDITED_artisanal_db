Hidden truth

Hidden truth was an utterance from the Lexicon of the Evolving Mind that used truespeak to connect a target to a reserve of knowledge, or, if reversed, made their words ring true while unfettered to actual truth.

Effects

In its straight form, the utterance granted great insight into truths and research, allowing even the untrained to tap into knowledge they never possessed, if for a moment. In its reversed form, the utterance improved their ability at lying and making their words ring true to listeners.

Known Users

The utterance was known to logokron devils.
