Tyrant's bindings was a powerful conjuration spell from the 6 circle of magic, granted to some of his followers by the deity of tyranny Bane.

Effects

When the spell was cast, heavy chains materialized out of thin air and ensnared the spell's targets, binding them to the caster. While the chains were wrapped around the target, its movement speed was dramatically slowed down. The chain remained attached to the targets for six seconds, and unless the spell was disabled, the chains exploded, dealing a significant amount of concussive damage to the targets. Those individuals with steeled minds could avoid being entangled by these magical chains, but were still damaged by their force.

History

This unique spell was used by Enver Gortash, the Chosen of Bane, during the Absolute crisis of the .
