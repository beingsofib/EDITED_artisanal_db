Rockburst was an evocation spell that cause a boulder or pile or rubble to explode.

Effects

This spell caused an amount of stone to explode, possibly damaging any creatures within of the center of the blast. Stone-made possessions of others and magic objects made of stone had a chance to resist the spell. Additionally, creatures that were made of stone were typically immune to rockburst.

Components

This spell only required verbal and somatic components to cast.

Usage

Dwarven priests of Clanggedin Silverbeard were typically known to have this spell bestowed upon them.
