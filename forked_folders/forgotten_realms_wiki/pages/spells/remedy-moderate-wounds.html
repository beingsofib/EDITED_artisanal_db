Remedy moderate wounds

nameRemedy Moderate Wounds

Remedy moderate wounds was a divine healing spell employed by druids and clerics of the Realms.

Effects

Upon laying hands on a single living creature, the target began to heal slowly over the next minute at minimum and perhaps longer, if the caster was more powerful. Unlike other healing spells, such as cure moderate wounds, the healing was gradual and not instantaneous, but it had the extra advantage of being able to work even on someone who had not yet been injured, beginning to heal as soon as any damage was dealt.

The power of the spell could not quench thirst or hunger or cause lost limbs to regenerate, but it could stabilize a dying individual.

Components

The spell required only verbal and somatic components.
