Hammer of the gods

Spell
nameHammer of the Gods
school3eEvocation
level3eCleric 4

Hammer of the gods was a spell used by clerics.

Effects

When cast, this spell covered an area with a radius in divine light. The divine light was used by the caster to smite any opponent within the affected area.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
