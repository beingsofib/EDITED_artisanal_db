Instant regeneration was a powerful spell commonly used by wu jen in Kara-Tur. The spell was very rare outside Kara-Tur.

Effects

Using this spell, a wu jen was able to instantly regenerate a severed limp. The spell was only able to regenerate an existing appendage, it was unable to create a new one. The wu jen experienced no discomfort from the effects of the spell.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, a piece of yellow paper with red writing.
