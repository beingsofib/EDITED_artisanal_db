Nimodes' unseen butler

Spell
nameNimodes' Unseen Butler
inventorNimodes
school2eConjuration
level2eWizard 2

Nimodes' unseen butler was a stronger version of the unseen servant spell.

Effects

The spell created a more powerful unseen servant that raced like a whirlwind. The spell straightened, swept, buffed, polished, cleaned and waxed along the area of effect, about at the very least. The spell was so powerful that it usually finished the tasks within three or four minutes.

Components

The spell called for verbal and somatic components, and as a material component, it called for a bit of string and a drop of oil.

History

After creating the spell, Nimodes sold it to enterprising hedge wizards far and wide; the spell was now found all across Faerûn, from Cormyr to Kara-Tur.
