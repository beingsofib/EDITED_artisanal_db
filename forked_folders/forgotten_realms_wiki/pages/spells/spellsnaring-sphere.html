Spellsnaring sphere

Spell
nameSpellsnaring Sphere
inventorThe Simbul

Spellsnaring sphere was one of the Simbul's personal spells during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Effects

The spellsnaring sphere created an area where the caster was able to wrest control of others' spells away from their casters, keeping them from effectively ending their own spells, and prevented even other quelling spells from affecting the targeted magics. For spells that spared the caster from their effects, it also made them subject to its effects too. The spell also allowed even ordinarily fixed or stationary spell effects to be moved around.

History

The Simbul used this spell, one of her newer ones, while assaulting the stronghold of the Red Wizard Azmyrandyr in the , after she was attacked by Sharran priests during her raid. She employed this spell to hijack a blade barrier, which she proceeded to send forth in pursuit of other foes, also preventing its dismissal by the priest who cast it.
