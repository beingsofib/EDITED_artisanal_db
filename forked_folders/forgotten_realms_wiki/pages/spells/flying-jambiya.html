Flying jambiya

Spell
nameFlying Jambiya
school2eAlteration, Wind
level2eWizard 2

Flying jambiya was a spell used by wizards in Anauroch and Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Effects

With this spell the caster could send a small blade flying after a target like a hawk chasing a rat. The caster could changes targets for the duration of the spell. The caster needed to be able to see the target for the spell to function properly. This spell would fail if the caster was interrupted in any manner.

Components

This spell required a teardrop from the caster to cast.
