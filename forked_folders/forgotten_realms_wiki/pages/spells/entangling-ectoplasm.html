Entangling ectoplasm

Psionic Power
nameEntangling Ectoplasm
discipline3eMetacreativity (Creation)
level3ePsion 1 Wilder 1

Entangling ectoplasm was a psionic power of the metacreativity discipline that allowed a psion or wilder to create a simple slimy projectile.

Effects

This power conjured forth a glob of ectoplasm from the Astral Plane, which would then be hurled at a target within or more of the psionicist, the range being greater depending upon a psionic user's skills. This target was typically a creature, as upon contact the sticky ectoplasm would entangle a creature hit with it. After a period of one minute, the ectoplasm would evaporate.

By pushing the extent of this power, a psionics user could draw forth greater amounts of ectoplasm, allowing them to entagle larger foes.

Displays

Entangling ectoplasm exhibited a material and visual display in the form of the ectoplasmic goo that the psionics user conjures forth.

Users

This psionic power was innately known by ssvaklors, as well as yuan-ti abominations, halfbloods, and purebloods.

Known/Notable Users

  • Iltharagh, a very old topaz dragon that later became a dracolich.
