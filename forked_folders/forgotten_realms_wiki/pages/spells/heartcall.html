Heartcall was a spell developed by Majinhime. It found the target's true love.

Effects

The spell indicated approximate direction and distance to the target's true love, if it existed. If the true love of the subject was in a different plane, only indications as to the nature of the plane (Transitive, Inner, Outer, demiplane) would be obtained. Sometimes, it simply failed, perhaps because it was not yet the time for them to meet.

Components

Besides verbal and somatic components, the potion required a glass disc and a spool of red thread.

History

The spell was developed by Majinhime, inspired by her travels through Kozakura. It was very rare outside of soothsaying businesses.
