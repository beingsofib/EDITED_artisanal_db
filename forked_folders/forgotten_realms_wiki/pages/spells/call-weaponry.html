Call weaponry was a psionic power of the psychoportation discipline and its teleportation subdiscipline that manifested a weapon into the hands of the psionics user.

Effects

Though to the eyes it appeared to manifest something out of thin air, call weaponry actually teleported a random simple, martial, or exotic weapon from elsewhere in the multiverse, or even from another time period, into the waiting hands of the manifester. The manifester could not call upon a specific weapon, only a broad kind, such as a longsword, a warhammer, etc. If they called upon a ranged weapon, such as a crossbow or sling, the weapon would be accompanied by 3 to 18 appropriate, non-magical projectiles. All weapons summoned by the power were made of common materials, were considered magic weapons, and emitted a low hum.

Once summoned, a weapon remained for a period of one or more hours, but would prematurely disappear back from whence it came if the manifester relinquished their grip on it fore more than 12 seconds. Additionally, a manifester could focus their reserve of psychic energy into further augmenting the weapon summoned by call weaponry, granting them +1 or greater enchantment bonuses.

Older versions of the power relied more upon the manifester's skills in psionics to determine what kind of weapons they could manifest, with greater skill corresponding to greater weaponry, but also greater costs of psychic energy.

Display

This power exhibited an auditory and material display.

Users

This power was knowable by psionicists

Known/Notable Users

  • Teliene Coloara, a drow illithid slayer and psychic warrior.
