Alahandra's questing call was a spell that Alahandra created to call adventurers to a quest.

Effects

The spell called forth the vague shadowy images of adventurers within who were not on a quest at the moment, as well as their general location for at least 15 minutes, more for more advanced mages. The spellcaster had little control over which adventurers the spell showed; about three out of every ten times, the spell showed nothing, or scenes from other planes.

Components

Besides verbal and somatic components, the spell called for a silver mirror, a candle made of beeswax, and a small bell. Of these three material components, only the mirror remained after casting.

History

The spell had been developed by Alahandra, a hedge wizard of Waterdeep. The spell was quite rare, and less than one in thirty wizards outside of hedge wizards bothered to learn it.
