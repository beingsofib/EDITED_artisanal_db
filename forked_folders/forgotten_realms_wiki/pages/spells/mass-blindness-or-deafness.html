Mass blindness or deafness

Spell
nameMass Blindness or Deafness
school3eIllusion
level3eSorcerer 8 Wizard 8

Mass blindness or deafness was a spell used by sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell affected an area with a radius. All creatures within this area were rendered blind and deaf until the spell expired.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
