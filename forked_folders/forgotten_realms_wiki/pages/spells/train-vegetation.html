Train vegetation

Spell
nameTrain Vegetation
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 2

Train vegetation was a spell that was used to repair spelljammers.

Effects

The spell allowed the caster to sculpt wood. The caster was also able to cultivate, prune, and train living plants for up to from the caster. The spell manipulated wood by bending, lengthening, shortening, thinning, thickening, and twisting it. The spell did not allow the caster to alter the actual matter of a plant.

Casting

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required material components: an acorn, a small branch with a leaf still attached, and a green gem with a value of at least 500 gp.

History

This spell was created by wizards of the Elven Imperial Fleet. The goal was to create a spell that allowed for rapid repairs to elven spelljammers.
