Verve

Psionic Power
nameVerve
discipline3ePsychometabolism
level3ePsion 0 Psychic warrior 0
type3eTalent

Verve was a basic psionic power of the psychometabolism discipline that granted its users a negligible boost to their health.

Displays

Manifesting this power produced a standard material and olfactory display.

Known/Notable Users

  • Eldenser, an amethyst great wyrm.
  • Gaulauntyr, an adult copper dragon.
  • Malaeragoth, a sapphire dragon.
  • Raulothim, an emerald great wyrm.
