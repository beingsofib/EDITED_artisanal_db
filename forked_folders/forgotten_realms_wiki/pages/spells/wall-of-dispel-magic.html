Wall of dispel magic

nameWall of Dispel Magic
school3eAbjuration

Wall of dispel magic was an abjuration spell that dissipated magic.

Effects

On casting wall of dispel magic, a wall was created. This wall was invisible; so invisible in fact that it was impossible to see with a see invisibility spell. It was also impossible to feel and one could easily pass through it as though nothing was there. However, people who passed through this wall came under the effect of a targeted dispel magic effect. A creature that was summoned could be dissipated by this spell. True seeing allowed a person to see both the invisible wall and its capacities.

Components

Wall of dispel magic required somatic and verbal components and a divine focus if cast as a divine spell.
