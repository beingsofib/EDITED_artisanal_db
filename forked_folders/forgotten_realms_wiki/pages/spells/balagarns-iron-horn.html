Balagarn's iron horn

Spell
nameBalagarn's Iron Horn
school3eTransmutation
level3eBard 1 Sorcerer 2 Wizard 2

Balagarn's iron horn was a spell used by bards, sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell created a sonic vibration strong enough to cause a creature to stumble or trip. The strength of the resonant vibration was determined by the caster's skill level.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
