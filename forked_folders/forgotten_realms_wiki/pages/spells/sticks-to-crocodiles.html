Sticks to crocodiles was a spell granted by the Mulhorandi god Sebek to his werecrocodile priests.

Effects

The spell functioned identically to sticks to snakes, except that it transformed sticks into crocodiles rather than snakes.

History

In the mid-to-late 14 century DR, the spell was popular in the City of the Werecrocodiles among Sebek's specialty priests,
