Summon gilded hellsboar was a conjuration spell from the 1 circle of magic granted to the warlocks who had their pacts forged with Mammon, the King of Greed.

Effects

When cast, the spell summoned a sounder of three hellsboars, covered in gold. Despite their appearance, these creatures were fiends like their common kin. The spell's target was a visible area within of the caster.

History

Circa the , the summon gilded hellsboar spell was known to Helsik, the diabolist of Baldur's Gate, the Latchkeeper of the Nine Hells, the mistress of Devil's Fee shop in the Lower City.
