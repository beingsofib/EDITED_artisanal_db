Magic mantle

The Magic mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concept of magic.

Abilities

Those who followed the Magic mantle became equally in tune with the Art and the Invisible Art, treating the two as if they were but one field of power. The psionic powers of a follower of this mantle could affect magic effects, items, and spells as if they were psionic in nature. Additionally, followers became skilled in the use of magical devices.

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the Magic mantle made any weapons wielded by the manifester, as well as weapons wielded by any allies within the radius of the aura, be treated as though they were magic weapons for the purposes of overcoming defenses against non-magical weaponry.

Powers

Novice manifesters could grant weapons a +1 enchantment with metaphysical weapon, as well as cancel both magical and psionic effects and powers with dispel psionics. Manifesters of some experience learned how to enhance their resistance towards both powers and spells with power resistance. As a manifester progressed further they gained null psionics field, which they alone could use to create a field that functioned as both a dead-magic zone and dead-psionic zone, as well as psionic analyze dweomer, allowing them to reveal the magical history of a subject.

Followers of the Magic mantle were uniquely capable of augmenting psionic analyze dweomer, allowing the power to analyze more than just one creature or object. Masters of this mantle were granted psionic protection from spells, allowing them to make any creature more resistant to the effects of spells and spell-like abilities.
