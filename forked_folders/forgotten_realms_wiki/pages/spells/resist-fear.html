Resist fear

nameResist Fear

level2eWizard 2

Resist fear was a wizard spell that largely raised the morale of the targets.

Effects

The abjuration spell had to be cast at a specific position or a single target. Those within a 30 foot (9.1 meter) radius of the target would become instilled with great courage and morale. Even magical fear could not scare off those under the effect of resist fear.

The spell lasted for an hour, and the recipients' morale gradually lowered to standard levels as the spell reached the end of its duration.
