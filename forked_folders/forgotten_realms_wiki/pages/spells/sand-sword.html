Sand sword

Spell
nameSand sword
school2eEvocation, Sand
level2eWizard 3

Sand sword was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Effects

Similar to the sand jambiya spell, this spell created a sharp blade out of sand. The blade could be as long as the caster's arm. It was able to harm creatures that could only be injured by magical weapons.

Components

This spell required enough sand to create the sword to cast.
