Skate

Psionic Power
nameSkate
discipline3ePsychoportation
level3eLurk 1 Psion 1 Psychic warrior 1 Wilder 1

Skate was a psionic power of the psychoportation discipline that allowed a psion, psychic warrior, lurk, or wilder to move a creature or object across solid ground as though the surface they were moving upon was smooth ice.

Effects

If manifested upon the psionics user themselves or another willing creature, the subject's body retained a sense of equilibrium that was driven solely by their own mental desire. Under these effects, skate allowed a creature to gracefully slide along any solid ground incline or decline that could normally be traversed without issue, make turns, and sudden stops as they desired. Moving up an incline would decrease the speed at which the skater moved, whilst descending would accelerate their movement.

Besides creatures, skate could be manifested upon an object. As its most basic, only objects weighing up to could be effected, though manifestation upon heavier objects could be achieved depending upon a psionic user's skills. Once skate was manifested upon an object, it could be dragged along any solid surface as though it weighed only one-tenth of its original weight.

The basic duration of skate was a single minute, extended by one or more additional minutes depending upon how skilled a psionics user was. The manifester of the power could also will the power into prematurely ending.

Displays

Skate manifested both a material and visual display on use.

Users

This psionic power was innately known by sapphire dragons, who could use it three times per day.

Known/Notable Users

  • Malaeragoth, a very old sapphire dragon that almost always had skate active.
