Synaptic static

Spell
nameSynaptic Static
school5eEnchantment
level5eBard 5 Sorcerer 5 Warlock 5 Wizard 5

Synaptic static was an enchantment spell that caused an explosion of psychic energy, scrambling the thoughts of everyone caught in the blast.

Effects

The spell created a -radius blast centered on a point of the caster's choice within a range of . All creatures within the blast radius who possessed even moderate animal intelligence suffered massive mental damage, and those who could not resist the spell had their thoughts jumbled for up to one minute, considerably impairing all their attempts to perform activities or even concentrate on spells.

Components

The spell required only verbal and somatic components.
