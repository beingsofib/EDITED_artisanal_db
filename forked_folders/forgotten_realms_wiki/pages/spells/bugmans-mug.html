Bugman's mug was a spell developed by Bugman to combat hangovers.

Effects

The spell converted the water inside a pewter mug into a potion that suppressed hangovers, drunkenness, and most other alcohol-related impairments if imbibed within the duration. The duration was dependent on the caster's experience: it would almost certainly last two minutes at least.

Components

Besides verbal and somatic components, the potion required common water as the material component, served in a pewter mug.

History

The spell was developed by Bugman for a dwarven friend of his. It was a rare spell, barely known outside of hedge mages.
