Cone of euphoria was a spell that affected the mind.

Effects

When cast, a cone of sparkling motes was exhaled by the caster. This cone extended up to from the caster and had an area of up to . Any creature within this cone was struck by an intense sensation of euphoria.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component, a small amount of gem dust with a value of at least 10 gold pieces.
