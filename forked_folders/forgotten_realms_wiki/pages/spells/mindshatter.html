Mindshatter was a divine enchantment spell from the thought sphere that created a form of insanity within the target.

Effects

The casting priest could inflict one of five different forms of insanity upon the target as detailed below:

  • Schizophrenia, characterized by a loss of personality.
  • Dementia, characterized by a lack of interest in doing anything.
  • Delusional insanity, characterized by the conviction that the target was someone of great importance.
  • Paranoia, characterized by a strong belief that the target was being spied upon.
  • Hallucinatory insanity, characterized by sensing things that did not exist.

The duration of the spell was based upon the target's intelligence and mental fortitude, but could last anywhere from one day to one month. Insanity caused by mindshatter could be negated by a wish, limited wish, or a heal spell cast for that specific purpose.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component a small bust of a human head made from fine porcelain to cast. The bust was shattered during the casting.

History

Mindshatter was found within Gorothir's Girdle, a holy book from the Church of Shar.
