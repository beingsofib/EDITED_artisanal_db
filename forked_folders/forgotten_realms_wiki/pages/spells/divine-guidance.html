Divine guidance

Divine guidance was an ability of avengers to channel divinity of a god they served.

Effects

This ability allowed the avenger to to guide the attack of an ally within who was engaged in combat with a foe against whom the avenger had sworn an oath of emnity. The guidance effectively allowed that ally to benefit from the increased accuracy that the avenger themself received thanks to their oath.
