Thought capture

Spell
nameThought Capture
school2eDivination
spheres2eThought sphere
level2eCleric 1

Thought capture was a divine divination spell from the thought sphere that allowed the caster to grab the lost thoughts of others.

Effects

A casting priest was able to grab temporarily forgotten thoughts from others via thought capture, making the priest's brain a magnet for such thoughts. Only a single thought could be grabbed per casting.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.

History

The Scepter of Mystra, a relic of the Mystran faith, could cast the thought capture spell.
