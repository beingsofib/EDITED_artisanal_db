Find the gap

Spell
nameFind the Gap
school3eDivination
level3eAssassin 3 Paladin 3 Ranger 3

Find the gap was a spell used to bypass armor and shields of many kinds.

Effects

For at least half a minute, the one who employed the spell became so cognizant of their foes' weak points that some of their attacks completely bypassed all armor, shields, and even bodily toughness. They were able to loose roughly ten attacks of such precision in any given minute.

Components

The spell called only for verbal components.
