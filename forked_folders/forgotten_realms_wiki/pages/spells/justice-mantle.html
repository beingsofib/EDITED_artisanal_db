Justice mantle

The Justice mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concept or philosophy of justice.

Abilities

Those who followed the justice mantle could expend a psionic focus to gain the upperhand against a creature that had prior injured an ally of the manifester.

Similarly, the psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the justice mantle made the manifester and all allies within the radius of the aura have a better chance at striking against those who had injured the manifester or another ally.

Powers

Starting out, manifesters had only the power to create a temporary weapon through their psionic will. Novice manifesters gained the power to do the same with armor, to make themselves and allies fearless with incite bravery, and command the truth of creatures with psionic zone of truth. Manifesters of some experience could discern the alignment of nearly anything with aura sight and reveal falsehoods with psionic discern lies. As a manifester progressed further they could gild themselves against illusions with psionic true seeing and strike back at unlucky opponents with perfect riposte. Masters of this mantle obtained reddopsi, allowing them to reflect psionic powers back at their manifesters.
