Touch of death

Touch of death was a channel divinity power granted to clerics who chose the Death domain.

Effects

When the cleric attacked a creature with a melee weapon, part of the creature's life force would be destroyed. This attack hurt the struck creature more than a typical melee attack, due to the necrotic energy. The more powerful a caster became, the more life force was destroyed.
