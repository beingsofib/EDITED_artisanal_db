Airy water was an arcane alteration spell that allowed the caster to transform liquids into breathable substances.

Effects

This spell transformed liquids such as water into breathable substances. The affected area was usually a bubble of airy water around the caster that moved with them. Water-breathing creatures could not breath or swim within the bubble.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component a handful of alkaline salts or bromine salts to cast.

History

Airy water was invented in by the Netherese arcanist Fjord with the name Fjord's distortion.
