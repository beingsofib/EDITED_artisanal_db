Mental prison

Spell
nameMental Prison
school5eIllusion
level5eSorcerer 6 Warlock 6 Wizard 6

Mental prison was an illusion spell that bound a creature within a cell of its own mind's creation.

Effects

The spell targeted a visible creature within a range of , inflicting damage to its mind. If unable to resist the spell, the target perceived the surrounding terrain as extremely dangerous, such as covered in fire, blades, or drooling maws, and could perceive nothing else but the illusion. The target remained confined to this prison for up to 1 minute, as long as the target kept concentrating. If the target attempted to touch or attack the illusory prison, the spell dissipated, inflicting copious damage to the creature's mind.

Creatures who were immune to being charmed could not be affected by this spell.

Components

The spell only required a somatic component.
