Ability rip

Spell
nameAbility Rip
school3eTransmutation
level3eWizard 7 Sorcerer 7

Ability rip was an arcane transmutation spell that allowed the caster to strip away a supernatural or spell-like ability from one creature and grant it to another.

Effects

For a number of hours, the first creature targeted by the spell would lose an ability that was specified by the caster. During the same amount of time, a second creature of the caster's choosing gained this power but lost one that they possessed.

Casting

The material component for this spell was a caterpillar within a cocoon.
