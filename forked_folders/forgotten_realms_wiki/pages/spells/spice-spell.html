Spice (spell)

Spell
nameSpice
school1eEvocation
level1eMagic-user 0
type1eUseful

Spice was a useful cantrip that initiate wizards could learn. It conjured a sprinkling of a particular spice or herb above a target creature or object.

Effects

This cantrip conjured a small quantity of a spice known to the caster. It appeared just above the target and fell as a fine powder or small flakes. The amount was typically enough to treat food or drink for about six people. The target of this spell had to be within of the caster.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster hummed a little tune while pretending to crumble and sprinkle a substance with one hand.
