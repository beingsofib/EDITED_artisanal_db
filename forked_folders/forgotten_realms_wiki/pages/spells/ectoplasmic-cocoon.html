Ectoplasmic cocoon

Psionic Power
nameEctoplasmic cocoon
level3ePsion
discipline3eMetacreativity
type3eStandard power

Ectoplasmic cocoon was a psionic power of the metacreativity discipline that captured one creature with strands of ectoplasm.

Effects

The power drew on the astral plane for strands of ectoplasm that captured a roughly man-sized target in their writhing coils. These regenerated too solidly to break free, and were roughly as solid as stone; they rendered the target blurry and muffled sounds, making them unable to speak as well as move, effectively rendering them helpless. The cocoon could be escaped transdimensionally, but not through the ethereal plane, as it extended into it. Furthermore, it took specialized powers to dismiss such constructs, such as dismiss ectoplasm.

The power could be manifested to wrap larger targets, but it was costlier.

Display

Besides material, the display was auditory.
