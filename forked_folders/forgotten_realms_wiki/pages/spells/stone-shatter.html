Stone shatter

Spell
nameStone Shatter
school3eEvocation
level3eBard 4

Stone shatter was a sonic evocation spell used by bards.

Effects

With this spell a bard could shatter a stone object or creature with a single well-placed note of perfect pitch. The size of the target that could be affected by this spell depended on the bard's skill level. Flying stone from the shattering effect could harm those standing nearby.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
