Plague carrier

Plague carrier was a devious necromancy spell that acted in a similar manner to the contagion but allowed for the target to unwittingly spread the a horrendous disease around.

Effect

Just like the contagion spell, plague carrier infected a touched target with a deadly disease. But unlike contagion, the target was not stricken with the sickness right away, instead, the target served as a carrier, spreading it as the disease incubated. While the incubation period was ongoing, the target showed no effects nor symptoms of the deadly ailment. A remove disease spell could be cast to neutralize plague carrier.

Components

Only verbal and somatic components were needed for the casting.
