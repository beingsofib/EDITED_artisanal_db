Remove fatigue

Spell
nameRemove Fatigue
school3eTransmutation
level3eCleric 4 Painbearer 2

Remove fatigue was a restorative divine transmutation spell granted to clerics and the Painbearers of Ilmater.

Effects

With a single touch, the caster restored stamina to a single creature, making it feel as if just awoken from a night of perfect restful sleep. However, it did not restore the exhausted spells of the tired spellcasters.
