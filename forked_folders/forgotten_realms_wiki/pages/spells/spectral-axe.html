Spectral axe

Spell
nameSpectral Axe

Spectral axe was a spell known to Rilder Palengerrast in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.

Effects

The spell formed an axe made of force that struck at the caster's direction.

Components

The spell was known to have verbal components; it may have had others.

History

In the , the apprentice Red Wizard Rilder Palengerrast had this as his strongest battle spell. He tried to use it against the Simbul, expecting it may work where magic missile didn't work, but was not able to confirm its effectiveness, because he used it to strike her projected image instead.
