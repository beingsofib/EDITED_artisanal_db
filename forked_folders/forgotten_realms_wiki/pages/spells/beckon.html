Beckon

deityOsiris

level2eAnimal sphere Animal 1 Cleric 1

Beckon was a divine magic spell of the southern magic tradition that affected one semi-intelligent or less intelligent creature. It was usually used on a domesticated creature of the caster, or a creature who was susceptible to the animal friendship spell.

Effects

The creature heard the call of the caster from up to away, and was compelled to rush to the caster as quickly as possible.

Components

This spell required a verbal call to the creature and a piece of the animal's favorite food as a material component.
