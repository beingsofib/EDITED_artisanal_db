Binding chain

Spell
nameBinding Chain
school3eEvocation
level3eWarlock (fiend patron) 4

Binding chain was an evocation spell that trapped the target in encumbering bonds. It was acquired by warlocks who took up with a fiend patron.

Effects

The spell reached up to away and created temporary magical chains that wrapped around the victim, slowing them down and impeding their every action. It made it harder for them to fight and made them far easier to hit.

Components

The spell required no verbal, somatic, or material components.
