Feel sound was a psionic devotion of the clairsentience

Effects

This power made a manifester's entire body act as surrogate ears, becoming a transceiver for light waves, allowing them to perceive whatever their ears would normally "hear" even when deaf, for a duration of 10 or more minutes. This had the added benefit of making the manifester more capable of resisting auditory abilities or effects, such as the song of a sirine, as well as better at listening overall if their ears were functioning normally. This enhanced reception was even greater when used in conjunction with see sound.

Feel sound granted no greater range hearing. It did not function within areas of magically induced silence. And if a manifestation backfired, the manifester would become overly sensitive to sounds for the the next one to four minutes, perceiving any sound as a painful garbled mess.

Displays

When used this power manifested a material display.

Users

This power was knowable by psionicists,

Feel sound was found to be exhibited as an innate psionic ability by rautym and wall-walkers.
