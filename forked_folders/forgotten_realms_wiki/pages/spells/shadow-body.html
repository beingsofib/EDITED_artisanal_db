Shadow body

Psionic Power
nameShadow Body
discipline3ePsychometabolism
level3ePsion 8 Wilder 8
type3eStandard power

Shadow body was a psionic power of the psychometabolism discipline that allowed psions and wilders to become their own shadow.

Effects

Through the use of this power, a psion and everything on their person would be transformed into a living shadow, unattached to anything, allowing them to blend into areas of darkness. While the power was active, the psion was capable of moving along walls, ceilings, and even liquid surfaces such as waterfalls, with the same ease as walking on solid ground. However, this power did not make one's body malleable like a shadow—they could not squeeze through any enclosed spaces they wouldn't normally be able to fit through, such as a keyhole.

Additional benefits provided by shadow body included darkvision, increased protection from most forms attack, and immunities to drowning, diseases, and poison. However, while in shadow form, a psion could still be partially harmed by all manner of acids, electricity, and fire. They also were incapable of physically manipulating or interacting with objects, though could still use their other psionic powers as normal.

Those using shadow body could be detected by any spells or psionic powers that detected thoughts, life, or presences, such as true seeing.

Displays

Manifesting this power produced a standard auditory display.

Users

Shadow body was an innate psionic ability for shadow efts.

Known/Notable Users

  • Eldenser, an amethyst great wyrm.
