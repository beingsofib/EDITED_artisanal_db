Lutzaen's frequent jaunt was a teleportation spell used by sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

This spell acted exactly as the dimension door spell, but only over short distances. The spell was able to teleport the caster over a distance of or less. This distance grew as the caster's skill level increased.

Components

This spell required only a verbal component.
