Control undead (rune)

Spell
nameControl Undead
type2eRune

Control undead was a type of giant rune.

Effects

Once activated this rune allowed the caster to control nearby undead creatures.

Components

Like most giant runes, it required no material components, though the caster needed to know the rune blinding light in order to cast it. The process of shaping this rune could take anywhere from one to eight hours.
