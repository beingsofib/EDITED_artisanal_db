Ladder (spell)

Spell
nameLadder
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 2

Ladder was an arcane magic spell of the southern magic tradition that created a ladder of force.

Effects

The ladder created was 1 ft (30 cm) wide and a minimum of 20 ft (6.1 m) long. More experienced casters could extend the length up to 60 ft (18 m). The ladder was firmly set when cast and could not be moved. The ladder of force lasted for 10 minutes or until dispelled, whichever came first.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a knot of any kind of wood as a material component.
