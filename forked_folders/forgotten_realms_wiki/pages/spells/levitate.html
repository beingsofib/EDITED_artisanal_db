Levitate was a transmutation spell or supernatural ability that allowed the caster to move a creature or object vertically as desired.

Effects

The caster could choose to levitate him- or herself or a creature or object within a close range. The weight of the creature or object lifted depended on the power of the caster but at a minimum, the spell could lift anything under . Only a willing creature could be lifted. Levitated persons or objects could be moved mentally at a speed of , but only in the up or down direction.

It was difficult to maintain one's balance if attempting to engage in combat while levitating.

The effects of this spell lasted for several minutes, depending on the power of the caster.

Components

Proper casting of this spell required verbal and somatic components and the use of a spell focus. The focus could be either a piece of golden wire shaped into the form of a small cup with a shank or a small loop of leather.

History

The spell was attributed to Netherese arcanist Yturn in and was originally called Yturn's levitation.

Users

Some races native to Faerûn, such as the air genasi and the drow (while in the Underdark), had the innate ability to levitate.

During the religious ceremony of the Dance of the Swirling Winds, the elven goddess Aerdrie Faenya would sometimes grant her worshipers who could not fly the power to levitate.

Levitation played a key role in the drow game khaless.
