Teleport field

Spell
nameTeleport Field
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 4

Teleport field was a spell used by wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spells teleported creatures within an area at random. The caster was able to cast this spell in a location that was within visual range. Once a location was chosen and the spell cast, any creature within a radius was affected by this spell.
