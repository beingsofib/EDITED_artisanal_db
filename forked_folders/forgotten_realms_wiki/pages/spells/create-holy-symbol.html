Create holy symbol

Spell
nameCreate Holy Symbol
school2eConjuration
spheres2eCreation
level2eCreation sphere Creation 2 Cleric 2

Create holy symbol was a conjuration spell that created a permanent holy item appropriate for a representative of the caster's deity. It could be used as a spell component or divine focus for all priestly duties and functions.
