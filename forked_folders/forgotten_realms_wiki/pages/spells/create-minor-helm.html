Create minor helm was a spell that created a minor spelljamming helm.

Effects

When cast, the caster altered a regular chair into a major spelljamming helm. This effect was temporary and was often reserved for emergencies. The spell was also infrequently used as it aged the caster by a whole year. The size of the spelljammer the helm could power was two times the caster's skill level.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component, any seat suitable for use as a helm.
