Locate remains

Spell
nameLocate Remains
school2eDivination, Necromancy
level2eWizard 1

Locate remains was a spell that the identified the locations of corpses.

Effects

After casting, the user could, for a matter of minutes, determine whether there were any corpses within at least straight ahead, with more powerful mages searching further. It also detected corporeal undead. Unlike similar spells, lead and other barriers did not outright block it, just limited it to , with mightier mages managing twice, or perhaps three times that. If a possession of a specific person was used as a material component, the spell could be adjusted to only respond to their remains.

Components

Besides verbal and somatic components, the spell called for a small piece of bone from a human cadaver, or a piece of cloth or strand of hair belonging to the subject sought when cast in the targeted version.

History

The spell was in common use by the Cult of the Dragon, and it was known to the necrophant Yerian.
