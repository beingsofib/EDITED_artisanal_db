Rhino's rush

Spell
nameRhino's Rush
school3eEvocation, Transmutation
domains3eWrath
level3eCleric 1 Druid 1 Paladin 1 Ranger 1 Wrath domain Wrath 1

Rhino's rush was a transmutation or evocation spell that allowed the caster to make a devastating charge.

Effects

Rhino's rush instilled a murderous rage inside the caster for a brief time, allowing the caster to make one doubly effective charge attack.

Components

Rhino's rush required only somatic and verbal components.
