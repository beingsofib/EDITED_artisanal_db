Spellslayer arrow

Spellslayer arrow was a transmutation spell that altered an arrow of masterwork quality, bestowing upon it extra lethality to those who were affected by magical effects or abilities.

Effects

The enhanced arrow became more harmful to people and creatures that had been enchanted, protected or otherwise affected by magical spells.

In order for the effects of the spell to apply, the arrow had to be launched within a few seconds of spellslayer arrow's casting. The arrow itself was destroyed whether or not it struck its intended target.

Casting

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required the material component of a single masterwork arrow.
