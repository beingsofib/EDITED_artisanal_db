Stone hull

Spell
nameStone hull
school2eAlteration, Sea
level2eWizard 3

Stone hull was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Effects

This spell strengthened a ship's hull with a magical aura, helping it withstand attacks from sea monsters, hidden reefs, or bombardment. This spell countered the effects of warp wood and turn wood. It did not offer additional protection from fire.

Any ship under the influence of this spell had its movement speed cut in half.

Components

This spell required a bit of pumice to cast.
