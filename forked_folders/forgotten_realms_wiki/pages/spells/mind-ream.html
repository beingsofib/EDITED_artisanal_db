Mind ream

Spell
nameMind Ream
typeSpell

Mind ream was a spell unique to the War Wizards of Cormyr that allowed a mage to extract information from a person's mind. The spell often left the victim's mind in ruins.

With the changes to the Weave of magic after the Spellplague, the spell often destroyed the mind of the caster as well as that of the victim.
