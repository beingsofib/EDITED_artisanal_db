Faith healing wand

Spell
nameFaith Healing Wand
school3eTransmutation
level3eCleric 3

Faith healing wand was a spell used by clerics.

Effects

This spell was identical to convert wand with one key difference. When cast, this spell turned any wand into a wand of faith healing.

Components

This spell required verbal and somatic components.
