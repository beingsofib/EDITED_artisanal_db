Concussion burst, also known as concussion blast, was a psionic power of the psychokinesis discipline that allowed a manifester to unleash a harmful blast of telekinetic force at an opponent.

Effects

The manifester of concussion blast could choose a single target, either a creature or an object, anywhere within a range of to unleash a blast of telekinetic force. This range was further extended in increments of based upon how skilled the manifester was. Creatures or objects that were fully concealed were invalid targets. And while any object could be targeted by the power, only non-magical objects could be harmed by the blast.

By focusing their psychic energy, a manifester could augment concussion blast to be more adverse, to target one or more additional creatures or objects, or both. In the case of the latter augmentation, any additional target had to be within of another.

Following the Spellplague and the nature of psionics changing once more, the blast of telekinetic force became more harmful and manifested outward as a wave, effecting all creatures in its path, and caused those affected by it to fall over.

Displays

When used this power manifested an auditory display.

Users

This power was knowable by psions and wilders, as well as ardents and divine minds who followed the force mantle.

Concussion blast was innately known by embracs and psurlons.

It was also commonly found in the psionic repertoire of githzerai.

Known/Notable Users

  • Arvin, a human psion.
  • The Mind Shard of Pandorym, the imprisoned form of the eponymous elder evil.
  • Raulothim, a great wyrm emerald dragon.
