Wrath of the just was an Elven High Magic wizard spell.

Effects

The scale of this spell meant it was only to be used if the very existence of the elves was threatened. Wrath of the just created largescale natural disasters such as floods, earthquakes, lightning storms, etc.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
