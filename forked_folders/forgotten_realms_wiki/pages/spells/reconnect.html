Reconnect

Spell
nameReconnect
school2eEvocation, Universal
level2eWizard 5

Reconnect was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate. This spell was a closely guarded secret weapon used by members of the Brotherhood of the True Flame.

Effects

Wizards used this spell to re-attune themselves to their magical powers after spellslaying. The reconnection lasted for the spell's duration. If the wizard performed a spellslaying that kept them from casting spells longer than the reconnection spell's duration, then the wizard would not be able to cast spells after the reconnection spell ended.

Components

This spell did not require physical components to cast.
