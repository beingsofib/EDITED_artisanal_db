Abi-Dalzim's horrid wilting, also known as horrid wilting, was a necromancy spell that damaged a creature via dehydration.

Effect

Horrid wilting caused moisture to evaporate from bodies of living creatures, causing injury. This was particularly harmful to water elementals and plant creatures.
