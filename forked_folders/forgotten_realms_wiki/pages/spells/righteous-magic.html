Righteous magic

Spell
nameRighteous Magic
school2eAlteration
level2eCleric 5

Righteous magic was a spell used by clerics.

Effects

When cast, this spell greatly increased the physical strength of the caster. The spell's effects were temporary. The spell was vulnerable to dispel magic.
