Mass cure

Spell
nameMass Cure
school2eNecromancy
level2eCleric 5

Mass cure was a healing spell used by clerics.

Effects

A caster used this spell to heal a small group of individuals, such as an adventuring party. During casting, the caster had to face the group for the spell's effects to be successful.
