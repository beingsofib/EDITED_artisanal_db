Crown of stars is a spell used most frequently by warlocks of the star pact, though other warlocks may learn its power. Crown of stars, when cast, conjures a circle of small, star-like orbs that circle the head of the caster. At the warlock's command, the glowing spheres fire a beam of burning, radiant energy powered largely by the warlock's charisma at a target within fifty feet or, with a little more concentration on the warlock's part, two targets. If one of the creatures is badly injured it is even possible that the attack may momentarily blind them. After this moment, however, the crown of stars fades away and cannot be regained for at least another day.

Components

The spell required verbal and somatic components.
