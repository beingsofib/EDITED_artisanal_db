Quillfire was a spell used by druids.

Effects

When cast, the caster's hand became covered in quills that contained poison. The caster was able to use these quills in either melee or ranged combat against an opponent. The amount of damage the poison inflicted was determined by the caster's skill level.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
