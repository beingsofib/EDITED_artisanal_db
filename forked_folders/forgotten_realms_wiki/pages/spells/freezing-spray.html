Freezing spray

Spell
nameFreezing Spray
school3eEvocation
level3eDruid 7

Freezing spray was a druidic evocation spell that blasted the target with frigid water and chilled them to the bone. Water elementals used a form of this ability to weaken their foes.

Effects

The spell soaked the target in magically chilling water, reaching up to away. While it did not directly harm the victim, it allowed subsequent spells that used the power of cold to inflict deeper and more lethal harm upon them.

Components

The spell required verbal and somatic components to cast.
