Create crossroads and backroad was a spell used by druids.

Effects

When cast, this spell created two fey crossroads and a backroad to connect them. For the spell to be cast successfully, the caster had to have visited the end locations at the end of each crossroad. Additionally, the spell summoned a crossroads guardian. During casting, the spell required the caster's full attention and dedication for a day.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a divine focus.
