Lesser confusion, also known as random action, was an enchantment spell that made a target being act randomly for a brief period. It was a lesser form of the longer-running confusion spell.

Users

The spell could be cast by beginner bards and clerics with the Mentalism domain.

Effects

Cast against one living creature nearby whose mind could be affected, this spell compelled them to become confused and act at random for several seconds. They might attack the caster, attack the nearest creature even if it was an ally, run away, stand and simple babble, or even act as they would have normally.

Components

It required verbal and somatic components, while clerics also required a divine focus.
