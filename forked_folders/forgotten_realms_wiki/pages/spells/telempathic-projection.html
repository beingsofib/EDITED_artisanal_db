Telempathic projection was a psionic power of the telepathy discipline that allowed a manifester to project emotions into the minds of other creatures and thereby subtly alter their moods.

Effects

Telempathic projection allowed a manifester to project emotions upon anyone within a diameter of them whose minds had been previously contacted. Typically this power could not radically change an emotional state, such as making an angry individual happy or turning love into hate, only adjusting them by a degree. However, on rare occasions, manifestations of the power did exhibit this capacity for drastic alteration. If a manifestation of telempathic projection backfired, all affected individuals would be stricken with strong negative emotions towards the manifester for the following 1 to 6 minutes.

Prior to the Time of Troubles, the power operated in a much shorter directional path, targeting those whom the manifester was facing.

In the late 14 century DR, telempathic projection could only target one creature at a time and had a targeting range of , plus or more depending upon the skill of the manifester. It was still limited to altering a target's emotional state by one step. It primarily used for the purpose of adjusting a creatures' attitudes towards the manifester in a positive direction,

Prerequisite Powers

Older versions of telempathic projection required knowing the powers contact and mindlink.

Displays

When used this power manifested a visual display.

Users

This power was knowable by psions

Telempathic projection was exhibited as an innate psionic ability by adult intellect devourers, lythlyx, and ustilagors.

In psionics terms, telempathic projection was also known by agathinon.

Known/Notable Users

  • Raulothim, a great wyrm emerald dragon.
