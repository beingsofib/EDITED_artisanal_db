Dark way was a spell used by clerics of Mask.

Effects

When cast, this spell created an unbreakable bridge. The bridge looked like a ribbon and was weightless. It could be at any angle but it had to be solidly anchored at both ends and continuous. The bridge could support up to . This maximum weight increased as the caster's skill level grew.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a divine focus.
