One with the land

Spell
nameOne With the Land
school3eTransmutation
level3eDruid 2 Hathran 2 Ranger 2

One with the land was a spell commonly used by druids, hathrans and rangers.

Effects

When cast, this spell allowed the caster to have a greater perceptiveness with nature. It increased the caster's ability in empathy with animals, hiding and stealthy movement, searching and increased directional awareness, wilderness lore and handling animals.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
