Gnats

Spell
nameGnats
school1eSummoning
level1eMagic-user 0
type1ePersonal

Gnats was a personal cantrip that neophyte wizards could learn. It summoned a swarm of gnats to bother and distract a nearby creature.

Effects

This cantrip conjured a small swarm of tiny flying insects that occupied a volume of . It appeared at a spot within and immediately formed a cloud around the head of the creature nearest to the summoning point. If the target did not resist the magic of this spell, they were distracted by the gnats for a few seconds before the bugs flew off. If the target successfully resisted, then they were essentially unaffected.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster pointed at the target while making a high-pitched sound, like the buzzing of tiny wings close to one's ear, using the tongue and upper palate.
