Shadow well

Spell
nameShadow Well
school3eIllusion
level3eSorcerer 4 Wizard 4

Shadow well was a spell used by sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell caused a targeted creature's shadow to become a temporary gateway to a pocket realm within the Plane of Shadow. The targeted creature then fell into this pocket realm. Within, it was a desolate place filled with phantasms made from shadow. These phantasms would pursue and mock the creature, but did not cause any physical harm. The only way for a creature to escape the pocket realm was with a plane shift spell. Once the spell expired, the targeted creature was returned to the location they were in when the spell was cast, filled with fear and dread.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
