Quintessence, also known as collapse time, was a rare psionic devotion of the psychokinesis or metacreativity discipline. Originally developed by mind flayers, it allowed a manifester to create the rare substance quintessence.

Effects

This power allowed a manifester to collapse of small fraction of time from the greater continuum, crystallizing it into of quintessence, a substance that could be used to protect things from the effects of time and, in large quantities, disrupt psionic activity.

Displays

When used this power produced a material display.

Significance

This power was of great importance to the mind flayer worshipers of Thoon in the Underdark, who used quintessence to create a variety of creatures.

Users

This power was knowable by psion shapers, mind flayers, ulitharids and elder brains. The psionic masters of the race originally the elder brains to safeguard the knowledge of this power, who then passed it down to future generations.

Known/Notable Users

  • Lugribossk, a proxy of the mind flayer god Ilsensine.
