The closed fist of Bane

nameThe Closed Fist of Bane
deityBane
school5eConjuration

The closed fist of Bane was a powerful conjuration spell that was somewhat similar to Bigby's crushing hand. The spell was from the 6 circle of magic, granted to some of his followers by the deity of tyranny Bane.

Effects

When the spell was cast, it conjured forth a large black gauntleted fist – the divine aspect of Bane the Accursed. This fist materialized within 60 ft. of the caster and hung in the air for six seconds, then it descended down on the targets, crushing everyone underneath, dealing devastating force damage in the process. The caster had to maintain concentration on Bane's fist until it descended or the spell failed and the fist disappeared.

History

This unique spell was used by Enver Gortash, the Chosen of Bane, during the Absolute crisis of the .
