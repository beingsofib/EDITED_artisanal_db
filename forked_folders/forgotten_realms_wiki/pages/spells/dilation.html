Dilation

nameDilation
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 4Wizard 6

The dilation group of spells were alteration spells used by wizards.

Effects

Dilation I and dilation II allowed the caster to increase the area of effect for the spell cast immediately after. If more than a minute passed before the second spell was cast then the dilation spell was wasted.

Dilation I increased the area of effect of basic spells by a quarter. Dilation II increased the area of effect of basic spells by half and intermediate spells by a quarter.

Components

These spells only required verbal components to cast.
