Feylights

Spell
nameFeylights
level4eEvermeet warlock 12
type4eDaily

Feylights was an Evermeet warlock spell that allowed the caster to create spheres of silver flame dancing around them, driving back shadows and revealing hidden objects and creatures.

Effects

This arcane spell created a zone of bright light around the casting warlock. All insubstantial creatures lost their light-creating qualities while in that zone. All effects of concealment (including invisibility) were negated within that zone,

This zone shrank or expanded, based on the caster's choice and lasted for a short time.
