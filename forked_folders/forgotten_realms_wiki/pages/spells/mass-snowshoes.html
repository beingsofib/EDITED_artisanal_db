Mass snowshoes

Spell
nameMass Snowshoes
deityAuril
school3eTransmutation
level3eCleric 3 Druid 2 Ranger 3

Mass snowshoes was a divine magic spell available to priests and rangers of Auril, the Frostmaiden. It cast the snowshoes spell on a group of creatures.

Effects

This spell could be cast at a creature or group of creatures up to 30 ft (9.1 m) away (higher level casters could extend this range). It affected a number of creatures determined by the caster's level, and that fit within a 30 ft (9.1 m) diameter circle or other regular shape as long as no two individuals were more than 30 ft (9.1 m) apart. The recipients were able to travel unhindered over icy or snowy terrain for a few hours as per the snowshoes spell.

Components

Only verbal and somatic components were required to cast this spell.

History

This spell was believed to be recorded in a holy book of Auril called Revelations of the Icedawn and was granted to her clerics, druids, and rangers.
