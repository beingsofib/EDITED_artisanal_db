Water blast

Spell
nameWater blast
school2eEvocation, Sea
level2eWizard 5

Water blast was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Effects

This spell turned a regular bottle, or vial, of water into a deadly weapon. The caster opened the bottle and aimed the opening at a target, striking one target at a time as a magic missile would.

The water would always strike the target's face unless otherwise prevented from doing so. In addition to causing physical damage, the water blast blinded a target unless they did not have eyes.

A bottle of holy water used this way caused additional damage to the undead.

Components

This spell required a bottle of water to cast.
