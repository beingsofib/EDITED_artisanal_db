Fireward was a divine abjuration spell used by priests devoted to Silvanus, enabling them to render an area fireproof.

Effects

This spell allowed the caster to render a spherical area fireproof while also extinguishing any current fires. The magic worked by consuming fires as soon as they started. The sphere was centered on the caster but remained stationary once cast. A fireward protected against all kinds of normal and magical fire including the breath of a red dragon. The sphere also prevented smoke from entering within.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components a pinch of sand and drop of water to cast.
