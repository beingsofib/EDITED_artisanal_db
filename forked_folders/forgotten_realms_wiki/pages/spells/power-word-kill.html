Power word, kill, formerly known as Xanad's killer, was a powerful conjuration spell that instantly killed one target creature.

Effects

After the , the spell was able to instantly slay a single living creature in close range, under a certain, high degree of health, whether they could hear it not. Creatures more robust were unharmed.

The post–Second Sundering version of the spell could still kill a single creature instantly, provided it was not extremely powerful.

Components

The spell required only a verbal component, the uttering of the power word itself., however, like with all spells, once a spellcaster understood how to cast it they could modify the word they used to be almost anything.

History

The spell was attributed to the Netherese arcanist Xanad in the and was originally called Xanad's killer.
