Tenser's lift was a ritual that allowed the caster to conjure a large magical platform that they could move with mental commands.

Effects

The spell summoned a pearlescent white hovering square platform that was on a side. The caster could command the platform to move with their thoughts, although it was significantly faster at moving vertically than horizontally. If the caster dismissed the spell, it would not dissipate until it had gently descended to the nearest solid ground.

Components

Tenser's lift entailed material components worth 125 gold pieces.

History

Circa the , the Bregan D'aerthe captain Xalbyn carried at least one scroll of Tenser's lift, as well as the material components necessary to cast it, during his assignment in Neverwinter. This was likely always intended to be used to reach and infiltrate the Shard of Night, a mysterious hovering tower about which Bregan D'aerthe wanted to learn more.
