school2eAbjuration

Storm shell was a divine abjuration spell that protected the caster from elemental damage.

Effects

When cast, a dark shell of storms surrounded the caster. This shell of disruptive energy made the caster resistant to fire-, cold-, and electrical-based damage. The effect of storm shell lasted for ten minutes.
