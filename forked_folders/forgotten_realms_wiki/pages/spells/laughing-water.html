Laughing water was a divine alteration spell that transformed a small amount of water into laughing water.

Effects

When this spell was cast, the cleric transformed a small volume of mostly-water liquid into a substances known as laughing water.

Laughing water had a number of curative and helpful benefits. Tasting a drop of the liquid completely reversed any exhaustion an individual felt. A single mouthful cleansed the individual from diseases, parasites, reversed effects of insanity and cure blindness.

Laughing water served as a substitute for holy water in rituals of the Eldathyn faith, albeit at double the required volume. It was harmful to the undead and even negated the effects of the spell Tasha's hideous laughter, when splashed on an affected individual.

History

The specifications of this spell were inscribed upon the Eldathyn relic, the Crystrum of Tranquility.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required an amount of water, a pinch of diamond dust, and a seed taken from a young, green plant.
