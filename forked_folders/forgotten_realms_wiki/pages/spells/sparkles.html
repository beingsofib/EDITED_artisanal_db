Sparkles

Spell
nameSparkles
school3eConjuration (creation)
level3eSorcerer 4 Wizard 4

Sparkles was a conjuration spell that created a cloud of glowing, multicolored particles that would rain outward from the caster. The sparkling motes would attach and outline any living creature they landed upon and could not be removed except my magical means. They would continue to twinkle and shine for the spell's duration, illuminating any invisible creatures or persons they landed upon.

This spell was extremely popular during Halruaan festivals, where the sparkles would be released from the skyships over the celebrants in the cities.

Components

As well as having verbal and somatic components, a pinch of ground mica was required in the casting of sparkles.
