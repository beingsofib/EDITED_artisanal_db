Create chosen one was a cruel alteration spell that consisted of numerous rituals of torture infected upon a willing or unwilling human. This spell was only used by Red Wizards of Thay, and they never shared it with outsiders.

Components

The spell required a bound, restrained, and helpless human to be subjected to an hour-long series of ritualistic torture sessions. Apart from the prolonged agony of the target, the spell had somatic and vocal components.

This ritual spell was available to evil-aligned wizards.

Effects

Once the ritual of torture was complete and the last syllables of the spell were spoken, the victim was engulfed in the death magic energies that twisted, disfigured, and mutated the target into a gangly vicious creature known as chosen one under full control of its master creator. Nothing short of a wish spell could reverse this transformation.
