Thornbush arrows was a spell used by wu jen in Kara-Tur. The spell was very rare outside Kara-Tur.

Effects

When cast, the spell turned regular thorns into magical missiles. A wu jen gathered thorns into their palm during casting, and shot the thorns out all at once. They could be spread out to strike multiple targets. The thorns were only effective against targets in the visual range of the caster, they could not follow or track a target.

Components

The spell required verbal and material components. To cast the spell, a wu jen needed thorns from a plant such as a rose and the feather of a phoenix. During casting, the wu jen oscillated the feather over the thorns. The feather was destroyed during casting and rendered useless.
