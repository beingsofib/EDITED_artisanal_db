Breach

Spell
nameBreach
school2eAbjuration
level2eWizard 5

Breach was a spell used by wizards.

Effects

This spell was used to both penetrate and remove any protection spells that were cast upon a creature. If the creature had an innate resistance to magic, it did not prevent the spells from breaching their cast protection spells.
