Spell reflection

Spell
nameSpell Reflection
school2eIncantation

Spell reflection was an obscure incantation spell that caused incoming spells to bounce back on their casters. It was known mostly only by flameskulls.

Effects

Spell reflection caused all spells reaching the target to be returned to their caster. If the spell did damage, the caster would suffer it; otherwise, the spell was negated.

Components

It required only a verbal component, making it useful for flameskulls.

History

Spell reflection was lost or long forgotten to most mages, and only remembered by old flameskulls in the mid–14 century DR.

Notable Users

  • The Lichlord, a lich who sought to avert Karsus's Folly by conquering Netheril, had his clothing enchanted with spell reflection.
