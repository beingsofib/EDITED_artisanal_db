Battering ram was an uncommon evocation spell used by sorcerers and wizards that duplicated the effects of a ring of the ram.

Effects

For a matter of seconds, the caster's arm was surrounded with a translucent arcane force that appeared in the form of a ram's head over their hand. For the spell's duration the caster possessed great strength, comparable to that of a hill giant and could readily smash, break or otherwise damage people and creatures with their punch.

Alternately, they could blast the force energy up to away in a direction of their choosing.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a focus, a carved horn from a ram.

Notable Users

This spell was readily available to guild members of the Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors as well as members of the City Watch of Waterdeep.
