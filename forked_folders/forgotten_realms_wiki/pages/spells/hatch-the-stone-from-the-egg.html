Hatch the stone from the egg was a bizarre spell combining alteration, enchantment, and evocation arcane magic that was used by wizards.

Effects

This spell was used to create a magic item known as a philosopher's stone. After researching the required alchemical formula and gathering the necessary ingredients, the caster enchanted the mixture with the enchant an item spell followed by the hatch the stone from the egg spell which slowly transformed the mixture into the philosopher's stone. A permanency spell completed the process.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a philosopher's egg to cast. The egg was not destroyed when casting the spell.
