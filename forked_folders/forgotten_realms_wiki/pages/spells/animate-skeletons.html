Animate skeletons

nameAnimate Skeletons

Animate skeletons was a necromancy spell that created a number of skeletons from nearby available remains.

Effects

Similar to the spell animate dead, this spell reanimated the remains of a number of humanoids, depending on the power of the caster. The skeletal undead obeyed simple verbal commands as given by the caster until they were turned by a priest, or otherwise destroyed. The magical effect of animate skeletons could not be dispelled.

Casting

This spell required verbal, somatic and material components in order to be cast. The material component consisted of a mixture comprising a drop of blood and a pinch of bone powder (or bone shard) that was combined with a specific salve that cost at least 10 gp.
