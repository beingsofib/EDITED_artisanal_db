Daunting light

Daunting light was an encounter prayer available to clerics during the post-Spellplague era that would hurt and hinder opponents with radiant light.

Effects

Casting daunting light upon a target creature caused a fiery column of light to engulf them, burning the opponent with radiant energy and causing their movements to be hindered for a short time. This allowed one of the caster's nearby allies a more advantageous position in combat when striking against the target.

Components

Casting this spell required an implement of some kind, likely taking the form of the cleric's holy symbol.

History

In the , a party of four Daggerdalesmen adventurers were summoned to the mines of Tethyamar by the Banite priestess Lorin-Aria to kill the Zhent wizard Rezlus and put an end to his plans to conquer Daggerdale and the greater Dalelands region. Among this party of adventurers was a dwarf cleric who knew the prayer daunting light.
