Know time

Spell
nameKnow Time
school2eDivination
spheres2eTime sphere
level2eCleric 1

Know time was a divine divination spell from the time sphere that enabled the caster to determine the precise time of day.

Effects

Know time was particularly useful if the caster had lost time from a bout of unconsiousness or similar situations. Time determined was accurated to the exact year, month, day, and hour.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.

History

The Scepter of Mystra, a relic of the Mystran faith, could cast the know time spell.
