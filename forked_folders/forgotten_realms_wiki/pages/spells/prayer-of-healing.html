Prayer of healing

Spell
namePrayer of Healing
school5eEvocation
level5eCleric 2

Prayer of healing was a common spell used by clerics.

Effects

When cast, the spell healed up to six creatures in a 30 foot (9 meter) radius from the caster. Constructs and undead were immune to this spell.

Components

This spell required only a verbal component.
