Break limb was an alteration spell that allowed a cleric to break the limb of another creature by mere touch alone. The reverse of this spell was called mend limb and caused the bones of a broken appendage to successfully bind back together.

Effects

While in effect, the caster gained the ability to potentially fracture, snap, or break the limb of the next being they touched. While it was possible they could resist the effects, the touch was still harmful nonetheless.

Depending on which bone was broken, the recipient of the broken limb suffered reduced capabilities until the bone was properly healed. This could be accomplished by a month of bedrest, a successful casting of mend limb, or heal.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a small broken bone taken from any animal.
