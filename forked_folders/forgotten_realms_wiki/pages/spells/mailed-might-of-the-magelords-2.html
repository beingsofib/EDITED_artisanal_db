Mailed might of the magelords

Spell
nameMailed might of the magelords
schoolConjuration (Creation)
levelSorcerer 5 Wizard 5
rules3.5

Mailed might of the magelords was a conjuration spell that caused a barely seen bubble of force to form around the caster that acted like armor and protected against force type spells.
