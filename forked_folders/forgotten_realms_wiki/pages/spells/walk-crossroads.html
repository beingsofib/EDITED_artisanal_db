Walk crossroads was a ritual that activated a fey crossroads, thus allowing for travel across the Prime Material plane or the Feywild.

Effects

Upon completing this ritual, a nearby fey crossroads of the caster's choice was revealed and opened, enabling the caster and their allies to freely enter it.

If using the crossroads to travel across the Prime Material plane, the caster's skill and understanding of the natural world dictated how far they could travel and how close to their intended destination they would arrive. The ritual had a tendency to send travelers to notably unusual or dangerous locales near their destination.

Components

Casting walk crossroads entailed 850 gp worth of material components, as well as holly and mistletoe that was to be wrapped around the fingers of the caster's upraised hand. The ritual took thirty minutes to cast and involved singing a song intended to appease the crossroads guardian.

History

As of the late 15 century DR, some fey crossroads could only be found by casting walk crossroads. At this time, scrolls of walk crossroads sold for 4,250 gp.
