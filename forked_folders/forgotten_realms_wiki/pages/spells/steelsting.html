Steelsting

Spell
nameSteelsting
school3eEvocation
level3eSorcerer 4 Wizard 4

Steelsting was an arcane evocation spell that created a maelstrom of dangerous flying darts.

Effects

Steelsting affected an area surrounding the caster based upon their skill level, attacking any targets within the area and slicing them with tiny darts.

Components

This spell required only verbal components to cast.
