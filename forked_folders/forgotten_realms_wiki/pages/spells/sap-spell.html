Sap was a spell used by clerics of Rillifane Rallathil.

Effects

When cast, this spell covered an area, per side, with tree sap. Any creature within the area of effect was subsequently covered in the sticky sap and their movement slowed considerably. The sap was thick enough to prevent physical attacks and the casting of spells that required movement. An individual could free themselves with a free action spell or similar effect. Pouring Copious amounts of wine onto an individual could also work.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required material components, the holy symbol of Rillifane Rallathil and a small amount of sap from a tree.
