Catapult was an arcane alteration spell in general use throughout the Realms.

Effects

This spell caused a loose item roughly the weight of a double-fist sized stone to move in the direction indicated by the spell caster. The item moved in a straight line up to the range and/or for the duration of the spell and then dropped in place, unless it came in contact with something solid first. Depending on the nature of the missile and the target, and the speed at which it was hurled, damage could result.

Objects hurled by this spell were not capable of damaging the hulls of spelljammers.

Components

No material components were required for this spell; only verbal and somatic, including pointing in the desired direction. The post-Second Sundering version of the spell required only somatic components.
