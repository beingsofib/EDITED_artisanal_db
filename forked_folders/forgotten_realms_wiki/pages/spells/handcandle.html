Handcandle was a divine alteration spell that allowed a caster to conjure a red or white flame within their hand.

Effects

When cast, this spell manifested one of two types of light within the cleric's open palm. The red flame was incendiary and damaging, in a manner similar to the spell produce flame. The white flame was non-harmful and often used to simply illuminate a given area.

Neither of the handcandle flames could be extinguished by strong gusts of air, even if they were magical in nature.

History

The specifications of this spell could be revealed upon a surface by the Oghman relic, the Key of Faith.

Components

This spell required verbal and somatic components in order to be cast.
