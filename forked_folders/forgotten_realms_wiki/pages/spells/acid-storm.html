Acid storm was a spell that caused acid to rain down in an area 20 feet (6 meters) in diameter.

Components

The material component of this spell was a flask of acid.

Crafting

This spell was one of several that could be used in the creation of acidic burst weapons.

History

Acid storm was created by the Red Wizards, who called it acid rain. By 1372 DR, the spell was known throughout the Realms.
