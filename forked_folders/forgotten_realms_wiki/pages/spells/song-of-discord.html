Song of discord

Spell
nameSong of Discord
school3eEnchantment (Compulsion)
domains3eWrath
level3eBard 5 Wrath domain Wrath 6

Song of discord was an enchantment spell that caused the targets to fight each other.

Effects

On casting song of discord, creatures within a 20 ft (6 m) radius area of the caster's choice started to fight each other. There was a one in two chance every six seconds that a victim of this spell attacked the nearest creature. When this happened, the victim was compelled by the spell to use its most deadly course of action.

Components

Song of discord required only somatic and verbal components.
