Groan

Spell
nameGroan
school1eIllusion
level1eMagic-user 0
type1eHaunting

Groan was a haunting cantrip that wizards-in-training could learn. It could create the illusory sound of a tortured cry that emanated from close by.

Effects

This cantrip produced the auditory illusion of a disembodied expression of grief or pain within a radius of from the caster. The spot chosen for the emanation of the sound could be anywhere within that range, including behind doors, under floors, or inside a chest of drawers. Creatures in the vicinity that could be expected to hear the sound had a chance to resist the spell. If successful, they did not notice the illusion.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster surreptitiously imitated a groaning sound while pointing at the location from which the sound was to emanate.
