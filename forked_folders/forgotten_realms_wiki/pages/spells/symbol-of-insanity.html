Symbol of insanity

Spell
nameSymbol of Insanity
school3eEnchantment (Compulsion)
level3eCleric 8 Sorcerer 8 Wizard 8 Wu jen 8

Symbol of insanity was a necromancy spell that created a powerful rune that could drive one or more creatures insane when triggered.

Notable Users

Inside the Jade Palace of Ysawis, the treasury room's thresold was warded with symbols of insanity that would discharge upon being approached within .
