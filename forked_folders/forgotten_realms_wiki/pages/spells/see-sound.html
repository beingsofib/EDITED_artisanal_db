See sound was a psionic devotion of the clairsentience

Effects

This power allowed a manifester to visually see sound waves, translating them into light impulses, for a duration of 10 or more minutes. This allowed a manifester to see even in darkness, so long as there were was some noise present to give their surroundings shape. If their ears were functioning normally, the manifester also became noticeably better at listening in on things. This expanded reception became even more pronounced when used in conjunction with feel light.

See sound would not work if the manifester lacked functioning eyes or an active power that could grant them sight, such as feel light. Anything that could induce silence on the manifester would effectively "blind" them. If a manifestation backfired, the manifester would become overly sensitive to loud noises, becoming "blinded" by them, and would perceive other sounds as disturbing bright lights.

Displays

When used this power manifested a visual display.

Users

This power was knowable by psionicists,

See sound was also commonly known by couatl.

Known/Notable Users

  • Ka'cha, an Athasian thri-kreen that had see sound as a wild talent.
