Sharptooth (spell)

Spell
nameSharptooth
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 3

Sharptooth was a spell that could only be cast by dragons.

Effects

This spell allowed a dragon to alter their teeth to make them both sharper and stronger. It was cast before battle or when a dragon was trying to eat a rather hard-to-chew meal, like a knight.

Casting

The spell required only a verbal component.
