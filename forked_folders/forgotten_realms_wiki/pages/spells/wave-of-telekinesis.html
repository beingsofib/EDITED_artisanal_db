Wave of telekinesis was a spell used by clerics of Callarduran Smoothhands.

Effects

When cast, this spell allowed the caster to move one or more objects in a violent action. Targeted objects within from the caster, weighing up to , were thrown up to away. This distance increased as the caster increased their skill level.

Components

This spell required verbal and somatic components.
