Pain and Suffering mantle

The Pain and Suffering mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionic powers and abilities derived from the universal concepts of pain and suffering.

Abilities

Those who followed Pain and Suffering mantle gained the unique psionic ability to expend inflict half the pain dealt to them by an attack to their attacker. Doing so required the expenditure of a psionic focus.

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the Pain and Suffering mantle made the manifester and any allies within the radius of their aura deal more grevious wounds to the injured.

Powers

Novice manifesters gained the powers disable, inflict pain, and recall agony. Manifesters of some experience could transfer or share their pain to others with empathic feedback, forced share pain, and hostile empathic transfer. As a manifester progressed further they could send people into a frenzy with psychotic break and turn creatures into suffering masses with fuse flesh. Masters of this mantle obtained pain affinity field, allowing them to create an affinity feedback loop of pain with nearby creatures.
