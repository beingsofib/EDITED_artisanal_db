Henley's digit of disruption was a divine evocation spell from the creation sphere that smited a target with holy energy.

Effects

This spell was used specifically against the undead and creatures of the Negative Energy plane. The casting priest gathered holy positive energy and released it through their fingertip, destroying the target creature. This spell did not harm demipowers.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components the caster's holy symbol and a powdered sunstone worth at least 500 gp to cast.

History

This rare spell was known to be found within the Demonomicon of Iggwilv.
