Shar's aegis was a unique version of the shield of faith divine spell available to some of the members of the Church of Shar. It was an abjuration spell from the first circle of magic.

Effects

Similarly to the shield of faith spell, Shar's aegis enveloped the caster in a shimmering protective shell that boosted their defenses. Additionally, and dissimilarly to shield of faith, it also slightly reduced incoming damage to the caster and dispensed retaliatory necrotic damage to the attacker.

Shar's aegis spell could be granted by Dark Justiciar half-plate sets of armor, enchanted for the use of Shar's Dark Justiciars.
