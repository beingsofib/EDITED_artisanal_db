Knowledge of the ages

Knowledge of the ages was a channel divinity power granted to clerics of the Knowledge domain.

Effects

Knowledge of the ages gave the cleric knowledge about doing or using something for ten minutes. During this time, the caster was effectively trained in the use of the tool or in conducting the action they were to do.

Components

Only the cleric's skill was needed to evoke this power.
