Proctiv's move mountain was a legendary Netherese spell that sheared off a mountaintop, allowing the creation of the famous Netherese enclaves.

Effects

The spell was extremely direct in its applications. With the arcanist's guidance, it severed the section of mountain desired, rotated it so that the flat side faced upward, and maintained a levitation effect that kept it aloft until more permanent arrangements could be made.

Components

The material components for this spell were a mythallar (which needed to be placed in the new city within two weeks), a magical device that allowed flying or levitation, and a crystal ball.

History

The spell was created by the arcanist Proctiv in , and without it the construction of Netheril's flying enclaves would have been near-impossible. As an epic spell, it could no longer be cast due to the restrictions placed on magic by Mystra after Karsus's Folly in . Later ages remembered the spell simply as move mountain.
