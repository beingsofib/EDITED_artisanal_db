Thunderstroke was a divine evocation spell used only by specialty priests devoted to Anhur called Militars.

Effects

The spell summoned the wrath of Anhur in the form of a bolt of lightning to strike a single target.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components the Militar's holy symbol to cast.
