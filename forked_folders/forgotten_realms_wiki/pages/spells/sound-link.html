Sound link

Psionic Power
nameSound Link
discipline2eTelepathy
level2eMinor (Devotion)

wild2eYes

Sound link was a minor psionic devotion of the telepathy discipline that allowed a psionicist to connect another creature's sense of hearing to their own.

Effects

This power tapped into the auditory system of another creature from an unlimited distance away, making the manifester hear whatever the linked creature heard. In some cases, this also established a link with the target's sense of sight. If the manifestation backfired, the manifester would be deafened for 1 to 4 hours.

If the linked creature was subjected to an auditory-based ability or effect, like the song of a sirine, the manifester could also be affected.

Prerequisite Powers

In order to learn sound link a manifester first had to know the powers contact and mindlink.

Users

Sound link was found to be manifested as an innate psionic ability by emerald dragons. And while not typically a wild talent, sound link sometimes manifested as such for necromancers, who were known for developing unusual mental abilities.

Known/Notable Users

  • The Watchers, illithids that served as the early warning system for the Underdark community of Nar'Vheen, included sound link among their repertoire of powers and used it to eavesdrop on distant conversations.
