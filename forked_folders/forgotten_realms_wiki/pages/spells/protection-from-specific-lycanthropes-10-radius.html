Protection from specific lycanthropes, 10' radius was a spell that protected the recipient from a specific type of lycanthrope.

Effects

This spell protected the caster or another individual from only a specified type of lycanthrope. The specified lycanthrope was unable to get within 10 feet (3 meters) of the recipient. The caster had to learn the spell for each separate type of lycanthrope. Lycanthropes of a type not targeted by this spell could cross the protection barrier.

Casting

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, wolfsbane. During casting, the wolfsbane was crushed and then cast in a circle around the caster.
