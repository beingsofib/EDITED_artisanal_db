Enhance maneuverability was an alteration wizard spell that altered the maneuverability of a single spelljammer.

Effects

Within of the caster, this spell could either enhance or decrease by a single degree the maneuverability of a spelljammer. Only one spelljammer, with a spatial tonnage scaling with the power of the caster), could be affected for up to four or more minutes. This effect was not cumulative with other castings of the spell.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component to cast. This took the form of a vial of wind from an air-based world,
