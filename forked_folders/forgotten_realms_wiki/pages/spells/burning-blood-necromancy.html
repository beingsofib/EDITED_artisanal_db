Burning blood (necromancy)

Spell
nameBurning Blood
school3eNecromancy
level3eSorcerer 4 Wizard 4

Burning blood was a necromancy spell that infused the target's blood with red-hot corrosive properties.

Effects

On casting the spell, for about a minute or two, the target suffered damage from their own blood being infused with a red-hot corrosive. Even if they managed to hold off the spell for a moment, that didn't guarantee the damage wouldn't return shortly after. It failed to work on constructs, plants, undead, elementals, or oozes.

Components

The spell called for verbal and somatic components. The material components were a pinch of saltpeter and a drop of blood.

History

The spell or one much like it was used by the gold dragon king Orchtrien against the elven wizard Rhespen Ash in their battle during the Time of Dragons.
