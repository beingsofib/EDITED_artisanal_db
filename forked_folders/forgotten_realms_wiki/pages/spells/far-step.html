Far step

Spell
nameFar Step
school5eConjuration
level5eSorcerer 5 Warlock 5 Wizard 5

Far step was a spell that allowed for short-distance teleportation.

Effects

A caster used this spell to teleport to a point within sight. This location had to be unoccupied and not father than from the caster.

Components

This spell required only a verbal component.
