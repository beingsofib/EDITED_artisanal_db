Dimensional folding

Spell
nameDimensional Folding
school2eAlteration
spheres2eNumbers sphere
level2eCleric 4

Dimensional folding was a divine alteration spell from the numbers sphere that allowed the caster to warp the fabric of space.

Effects

This spell folded the space around the caster, allowing for instantaneous travel to a selected location within range by stepping through a created gateway. This spell was best used only if the casting priest was familiar with the selected destination, otherwise the act of stepping through folded space had a chance to age the caster, and anyone traveling with them, by several years.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component a sheet of platinum worth at least 15 gp to cast. The sheet was intricately folded and consumed during the casting.

History

The Scepter of Mystra, a relic of the Mystran faith, could cast the dimensional folding spell.
