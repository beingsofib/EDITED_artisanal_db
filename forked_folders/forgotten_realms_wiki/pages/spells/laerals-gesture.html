Laeral's gesture was an alteration wizard spell developed by Laeral Silverhand.

Effects

This spell allowed the caster to cast a previously memorized medium- to lower-power spell by performing a complex physical gesture. It was quite useful if a wizard found themselves without their spell components.

Components

This spell only required verbal and somatic components to cast.

Rumors & Legends

Laeral created this spell after she was attacked while bathing and not in the vicinity of her spell components.
