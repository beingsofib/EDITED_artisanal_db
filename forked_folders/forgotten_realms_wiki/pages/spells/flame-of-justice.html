Flame of justice

Spell
nameFlame of Justice
school2eNecromancy, Flame
level2eWizard 6

Flame of justice was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Effects

This spell was a more powerful version of the fire truth spell and was cast in the same manner using a special lit candle.

When the target of this spell spoke falsely, part of their body would burst into flame.

For this spell, the idea of truth is based on the caster's ideal, not the target's. If something is incorrect yet the caster believed it to be the truth, then the target would suffer accordingly if they "lied."

This spell expired after ten questions were asked.

Components

This spell required a candle made from a rare form of desert bee's wax to cast.
