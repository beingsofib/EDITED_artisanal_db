Lesser shavecat

Lesser shavecat was an arcane cantrip or novice spell for journeyman mages, in the late 15 century DR, that allowed a spellcaster to remove the hair from a feline to some unspecified limited degree.

History

Sometime in the late 15 century DR, a Waterdhavian lowlife by the name of Garuskus Mrandrivurr got a hold of seven spell scrolls that included one for lesser shavecat. However, not being a dabbler in the Art himself, Garuskus was unable to test the spells.

Around winter time, the wizard Hornadar came to Garuskus's home, a tallhouse in the Dock Ward, in search of spells. Garuskus offered him a deal of 800 dragons for the whole lot of scrolls, but being a shrewd mage, Hornadar engaged in some haggling with him. Eventually, Hornadar talked him down to taking 700 dragons and a talking chastity belt as payment.
