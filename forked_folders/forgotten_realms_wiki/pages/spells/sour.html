Sour was a reversed cantrip that initiate wizards could learn. It created a small quantity of vinegar in a target food, drink, open container, or other object.

Effects

This cantrip had a range of and could target one item of food or drink that was not in a closed container, or could be cast at a point just above an object, similar to the salt or spice cantrip. The quantity of vinegar created was limited to a maximum of .

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster clenched one hand into a fist while making a whoosh sound through pursed lips.
