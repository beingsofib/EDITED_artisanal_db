Silent portal

Spell
nameSilent Portal
school3eIllusion
level3eAssassin 1 Sorcerer 0 Wizard 0

Silent portal was an illusion spell or glamer used by wizards, sorcerers, and assassins.

Effects

This spell negated any sound made by opening or closing a single non-magical portal by normal means. Smashing a window would still make noise for example. The portal could be a door, window, gate, chest lid, etc.

Components

This spell required only somatic components to cast.
