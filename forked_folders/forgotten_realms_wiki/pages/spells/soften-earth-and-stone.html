Soften earth and stone was a spell commonly used by clerics of Urdlen.

Effects

When cast, the spell transformed stone into clay and packed dirt into sand or mud.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required material components, the holy symbol of Urdlen and wet clay from the wheel of a talented potter.
