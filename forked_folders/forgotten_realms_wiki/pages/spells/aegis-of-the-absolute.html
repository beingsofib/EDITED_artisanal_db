Aegis of the Absolute

Spell
nameAegis of the Absolute
school5eEvocation

The Aegis of the Absolute was a unique evocation spell from the ninth circle of magic used in the late 15 century DR.

Effects

When cast, the Aegis of the Absolute made the caster immune to all types of damage they received within the last six seconds. The effects of the spell lasted for the six seconds that followed.

History

This unique sell was known and was used by the netherbrain during the culmination of the Absolute crisis of the – the battle of Baldur's Gate, in which the Absolute Adversaries stood against the cult in the Upper City.
