Steal psionic strength was a spell used by clerics of Deep Duerra.

Effects

When cast, this spell stole the psionic abilities of a targeted creature and transferred them to the caster.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component, the holy symbol of Deep Duerra.
