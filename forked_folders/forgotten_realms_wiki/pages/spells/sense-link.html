Sense link

Psionic Power
nameSense Link
discipline3eTelepathy
level3ePsion 1 Wilder 1
type3eStandard power

Sense link was a psionic power of the telepathy discipline that allowed a manifester to connect any one of a willing creature's senses to their own.

Effects

This power tapped into the sensory system of another creature – such as hearing, sight, smell, taste – allowing the manifester to perceive whatever the linked creature perceived. Alternatively, a manifester could channel their psychic energy to augment the power, making the target perceive one of their senses. Each manifestation of sense link could only link to a single sense at a time and could not switch between them, though a manifester could augment it to allow for linking to two senses at once.

Manifesters were subject to all abilities or effects that the linked sense was subject to, such as the gaze attacks of a beholder or the auditory effect of a sirine's singing. Loss or inability to access a senses, such as being deafened, did not prevent a manifester from perceiving that sense through the linked creature. And if the linked creature's sense was enhanced by any means, the manifester would be tapping into that enhancement.

Sense link could had a maximum targeting distance of , though depending upon how skilled the manifester was this range was extended in increments of . The operational range of the sense link was unlimited, but didn't work across planes. And it had a duration of one or more minutes, depending upon the skill of the manifester, and required concentration.

Displays

When used sense link manifested a visual display.

Users

This power was knowable by psions and wilders.

Known/Notable Users
