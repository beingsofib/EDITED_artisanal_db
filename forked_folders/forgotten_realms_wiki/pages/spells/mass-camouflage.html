Mass camouflage was a mobile version of the camouflage spell.

Effects

When cast, this spell allowed a group of creatures within of the caster to blend in with the surrounding area. If any creature within the group moved more than this distance from another, they became visible.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
