Lasting breath

Spell
nameLasting Breath
school2eAlteration, Chronomancy
level2eChronomancer 1 Wizard 1

Lasting breath was an alteration spell used by wizards.

Effects

The spell allowed the caster to drastically increase the time they could hold their breath.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
