Sustain was a common spell used by shugenja in Kara-Tur. The spell was very rare outside Kara-Tur.

Effects

A shugenja was able to cast this spell on themselves or another creature. Until the spell expired, the individual was able to go without food or water. They did not feel hunger or thirst. After the spell expired, there was no ill effects. If this spell was cast on a starving creature, they suddenly felt as if they had been eating a regular meal every six hours.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required material components, a rice cake and a flask of warm sake.
