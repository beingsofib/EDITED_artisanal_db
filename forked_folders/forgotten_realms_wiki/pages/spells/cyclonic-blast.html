Cyclonic blast

Spell
nameCyclonic Blast
school3eEvocation
level3eSorcerer 5 Wild mage 5 Wizard 5

Cyclonic blast was an arcane evocation spell that created a powerful windstorm to blast enemies away with.

Effects

When cast, the spell reached more than and created a horizontal tornado-like vortex of blasting wind from the caster's fingertips. The sheer strength of the wind was enough to injure and maim people in its path, staggering them backwards and sending flying creatures spinning away. Other physical barriers in the face of the winds were likely to be destroyed too if not sturdy enough. Lingering magical effects like stinking cloud, wall of fire, and Otto's dancing sphere would be forcibly dispersed as the wind swept through.

Components

The spell required verbal, somatic, and material components to cast. It used a child's spinning top as a focus.
