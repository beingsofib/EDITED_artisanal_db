Tyranny was a divine enchantment spell used only by specialty priests devoted to Gilgeam called Martinets.

Effects

This spell enabled to caster to command obedience from anyone within a radius. Any creature unable to resist the spell's magic would obey the caster as if he were their king provided the caster did not command them to perform actions against their nature. A Martinet using this spell against Gilgeam's wishes would be punished severely.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component the Martinet's holy symbol and a golden circlet worth at least 250 gp to cast. The circlet was placed upon the Martinet's head during the casting and vanished upon the spell's completion.
