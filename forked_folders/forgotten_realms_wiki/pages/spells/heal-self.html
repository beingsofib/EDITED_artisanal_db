Heal self

Spell
nameHeal Self
school2eNecromancy
level2eWizard 6

Heal self was a spell that was first developed by the phaerimms.

Effects

When cast, this powerful spell fully healed the caster. Broken bones and damaged organs were fully restored. The spell also healed illnesses, parasitic infestations, and conditions such as lycanthropy and mummy rot.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required several material components: a single drop of the caster's blood, two drops of water, and a gemstone worth at least 100 gp. During casting, the caster placed the material components in their hand and slapped them to their body.

History

This spell was originally created by the phaerimm of Myth Drannor.
