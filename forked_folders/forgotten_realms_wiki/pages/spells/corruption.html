Corruption

Spell
nameCorruption
school2eNecromancy
level2eWizard 2

Corruption was an arcane necromancy spell that temporarily corrupted a target's soul with greed and ambition.

Effects

The target of corruption was overwhelmed with dark power and halted in their steps, unable to move as they dreamed of delusions of grandeur. When the spell ended there was a chance the target would look favorably upon the caster.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
