Anticipation was a wizard spell from the school of divination.

Effects

This useful spell allowed the caster to foresee the actions of all creatures within a radius, allowing the caster to act accordingly. This spell did not work against the undead or creatures native to other planes. By default, anticipation revealed the presence and location of any invisible creatures.

Components

This spell only required somatic components to cast.
