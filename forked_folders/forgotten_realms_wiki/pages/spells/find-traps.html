Find traps was a divination spell that allowed the caster to detect traps.

Effect

The oldest version of this spell functioned across an area and revealed all traps in a specific area around the caster. A newer version allowed the caster to search for traps like a rogue might within or more, depending on skill, but it would not make the caster any better at finding them.

The post Second Sundering version functioned by letting the caster know that a trap such as glyph of warding or alarm was within his or her line of sight out to a range of .
