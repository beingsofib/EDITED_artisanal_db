Chameleon power, also known simply as chameleon, was a minor psionic devotion of the psychometabolism discipline that allowed a manifester to blend in with their surroundings in a manner akin to a chameleon.

Effects

When used, chameleon would change the texture and coloration of the psion's skin, as well as any clothing and equipment on their person, to match the surroundings to the back of them, including floors and walls. The psion had no control over the change in coloration or appearance. As the psionicist moved while the power was active, they and their equipment would shift in coloration to match the changed surroundings. By remaining stationary, a psionicist could use this effect to evade detection.

This power was found to be most effective in natural environments, but of middling effectiveness within urban environments or areas without any cover in broad daylight. If the power backfired on the psionicist, it would cause their skin and any equipment on their person to shift into a coloration that strongly clashed with their surroundings, making them stand out.

The average duration of chameleon was ten minutes, though would be prolonged depending upon the psionicist's skill.

Displays

When used this power manifested an olfactory display.

Users

While once available to all psionicists, in the decades following the Time of Troubles it became exclusive to egoists, psionicists who specialized in psychometabolism. It could also be learned by psychic warriors and lurks, as well as ardents and divine minds who followed the Natural World mantle.

Chameleon power was exhibited as an innate psionic ability by dhours, hook spiders, adult intellect devourers, observers, rautym, temporal filchers, thri-kreen, and wall-walkers.

In psionics terms, it could be known by yuan-ti, particularly yuan-ti abominations, halfbloods, holy guardians, and mageslayers, as well as jaculi.

On the desert world of Athas, an ancient and mysterious humanoid race of psionicists called the pyreen exhibited chameleon as an innate power.

Notable/Known Users

  • Iltharagh, a topaz dracolich.
  • Selinah Varhast, a human psion and assassin.
