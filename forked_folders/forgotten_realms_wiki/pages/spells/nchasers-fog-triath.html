Nchaser's fog triath

Spell
nameNchaser's Fog Triath
othernamesNFT
inventorNchaser

Nchaser's fog triath, or NFT, was a spell, invented presumably by Nchaser, that conjured the image of a lord or other leader (that is, a triath). It was formed from fog but appeared quite solid. Moreover, it moved and signaled in time with the real triath, regardless of their separation, provided they remained on the same plane. The subject had to be a real leader who was familiar to the caster.
