Dry was a useful cantrip that initiate wizards could learn. It removed a moderate amount of moisture from a small area.

Effects

With this cantrip, one could remove dampness from cloth, a surface, or use it to dry a bundle of herbs. The maximum area of effect was .

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components. The caster made a wringing motion with both hands while humming a tune.

History

This cantrip was described on the second page of Bowgentle's Book, penned by the famous mage from Silverymoon.
