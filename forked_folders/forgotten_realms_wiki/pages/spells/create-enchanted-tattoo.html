Create enchanted tattoo

Spell
nameCreate Enchanted Tattoo
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 2

Create enchanted tattoo was an arcane alteration spell that allowed the caster to place enchanted tattoos on the target's body.

Effects

Each casting of this spell placed a single enchanted tattoo on the target's body, but up to three tattoos could be created on a single target provided they bestowed different enchantments. The enchantments bestowed by the tattoo were determined by the caster's skill. Each tattoo lasted for several days depending on the caster's skill as well.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component a target body to cast. Additionally, the caster needed to possess artistic skill to draw the tattoo.

History

This spell was used exclusively by Red Wizards from Thay.
