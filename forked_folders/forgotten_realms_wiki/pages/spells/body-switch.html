Body switch

Spell
nameBody Switch
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 8

Body switch was a spell that translocated the target with another target in range.

Effects

The spell swapped the positions of the user and another target within which the caster must have touched at some previous point in time. Alternatively, it could be used to translocate a willing target touched by the caster with another creature within range.

Components

The spell called for only verbal and somatic components.

History

In the , when assaulted by Symrustar Auglamyr, a Chosen of Mystra, Elminster employed this spell to escape and bring a foe of hers, Elandorr Waelvor, to his position.
