Sarcophagus of death was a divine evocation spell used only by specialty priests devoted to Set called Nighthunters.

Effects

This spell created a cube of force in the shape of a coffin around a target chosen by the Nighthunter. The coffin was airtight and impervious to magic save for a dispel magic spell. The only way to escape the coffin from the inside was via a teleportation spell. Simply being inside the coffin caused physical harm as well as drained the target's overall constitution. Targets trapped within the sarcophagus of death stood a likely chance of death by asphyxiation.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components the priest's holy symbol and fragment of a sarcophagus consecrated by a priest of Osiris or Nephthys to cast.
