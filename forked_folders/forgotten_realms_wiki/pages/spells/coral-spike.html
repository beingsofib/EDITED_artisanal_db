Coral spike

Spell
nameCoral Spike
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 6

Coral spike was a formidable spell known for its ability to harness the internal coral skeleton of a Dukar and bring it to life.

Effects

When cast, this spell triggered the growth of menacing, barbed spikes within the Dukar's body. These spikes delved deep, targeting vital organs and inflicting substantial damage while temporarily incapacitating the Dukar. The excruciating pain endured during the spell's effects rendered the Dukar unable to take any actions, reducing them to a state of vulnerability.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component.
