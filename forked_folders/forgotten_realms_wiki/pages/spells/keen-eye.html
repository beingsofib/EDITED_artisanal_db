Keen eye, also known as bull's eye, was a spell used by clerics of Solonor Thelandira.

Effects

When cast, the caster or targeted creatures accuracy when firing a ranged missile weapon, such as a bow, increased significantly.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required material components, the holy symbol of Solonor Thelandira and a single feather from a hawk.
