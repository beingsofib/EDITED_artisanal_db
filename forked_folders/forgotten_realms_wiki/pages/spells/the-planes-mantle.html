The Planes mantle

The Planes mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the fundamental concept of the planes of existence.

Abilities

Those who followed The Planes mantle could expend a psionic focus to more easily overcome the power resistances of an outsider.

Divine minds of The Planes mantle could pinpoint the location of an outsider or extraplanar creature that was within range of their psychic aura. Additionally, the manifester and allies within the radius of their aura were more capable of overcoming spell and power resistances of outsiders.

Powers

Novice manifesters had greater ease traversing the Astral Plane, gaining the powers astral caravan and astral traveler. Manifesters of some experience could restrict planar movement with psionic dimensional anchor, force creatures back to their native plane with psionic dismissal, or travel to any plane with psionic plane shift. As a manifester progressed further they could enter the ethereal plane with psionic ethereal jaunt and summon a cerebrilith or sibyllic guardian to their aid with planar champion. Masters of this mantle obtained psionic etherealness, allowing them to bring other creatures with them into the ethereal.
