Wind at back

Spell
nameWind At Back
school3eEvocation
level3eDruid 4

Wind at back was a spell used by druids.

Effects

When cast, this spell increased the travelling speed of overland travel for 12 hours. It affected multiple creatures as well as their mounts or pack animals. All targeted creatures had to be travelling in the same direction. The spell did not affect items or objects. The amount of creatures affected by this spell was determined by the caster's skill level.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
