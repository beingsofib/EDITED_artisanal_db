Incarnation of angels

Incarnation of angels was an advanced utterance from the Lexicon of the Evolving Mind that used truespeak to connect a target to the upper planes, or to the lower planes, if reversed.

Usage

In its straight form, the silver grace and golden might of the Upper Planes suffused the target, granting them the abilities of a celestial being. In the reversed form, the target gained cunning and strength from the depths of Hell, granting it the abilities of a fiendish creature. Both lasted for about half a minute.

History

The utterance was known to word archons.
