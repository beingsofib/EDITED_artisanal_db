Swirling leaves of steel

Swirling leaves of steel was an exploit that allowed a ranger wielding two melee weapons to attack all adjacent enemies in a spinning dance of blades.

Effects

Once per day, a ranger who learned this exploit could spin like a top with blades held out and strike all adjacent enemies that he or she could see in the immediate surrounding circle.
