Summon undead II, also known as Undead summoning II, was one of the series of summon undead spells available to arcane casters in the Realms.

Effects

This spell could summon a heucuva, skeleton or zombie, or it could summon one to three medium-sized skeletons or the same number of small zombies, provided they were within of each other. Usually, the skeleton of an owlbear or a bugbear zombie were chosen. The summoned undead creatures obeyed the commands of the caster and were able to perform simple tasks.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, a piece of bone taken from a ghast, mummy or a wight.

History

This spell was known to be used by wizards of the Cult of the Dragon.
