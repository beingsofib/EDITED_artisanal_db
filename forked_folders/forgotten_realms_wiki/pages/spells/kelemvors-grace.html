Kelemvor's grace was a divine abjuration spell that protected an individual from all non-physical attacks of the undead.

Effects

When cast, the caster conferred the spell's protections upon themself or a single person they touched. Kelemvor's grace made them immune to life-draining effects, certain diseases such as lycanthropy or mummy rot, magical aging effects and supernatural attacks such as the wail of a banshee.

If cast directly upon an undead creature, this spell temporarily negated some of their inherent powers. The effects of spells they cast were also delayed until the end of the spell's duration.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required the cleric's holy symbol.

History

This spell was inscribed within the Kelemvorite relic the Testament of Vraer.
