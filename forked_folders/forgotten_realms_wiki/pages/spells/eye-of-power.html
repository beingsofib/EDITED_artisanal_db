Eye of power was a wizard spell from the schools of alteration and evocation.

Effects

This spell was the same as the wizard eye spell with the major exception that it could appear at any distance from the caster, allowing the caster to cast other spells through the eye.

The caster needed to concentrate to move the eye around. The spell would end if the eye was destroyed.

Components

This spell only required verbal and somatic components to cast.
