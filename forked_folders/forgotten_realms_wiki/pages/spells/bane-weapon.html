Bane weapon

Spell
nameBane Weapon
school3eTransmutation
level3eCleric 4 Sorcerer 4 Wizard 4

Bane weapon was a transmutation spell that applied the bane quality to a given weapon or bundle of projectiles, causing them to strike with greater precision and inflict more damage upon a certain type of creatures. The types of beings chosen in this manner included broad groups such as fey, aberrations, specific humanoids, magical beasts or undead, among many others.

Components

The casting of this spell required verbal and somatic components.
