Sun stone

Spell
nameSun stone
school2eEvocation, Flame
level2eWizard 7

Sun stone was a spell used by wizards in Anauroch and Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Effects

This spell turned a small stone into a magical incendiary missile. When held in the sunlight, a regular stone became so hot that it glowed with heat but did not harm the caster.

The sun stone was aimed at a target like a magic missile. If the stone missed its target then it fell to the ground where it was still hot enough to damage anything touching it for several minutes. Wizards were capable of casting multiple sun stones at once depending on their magical might. Each sun stone had its own target.

Components

This spell required a small stone to cast.
