Thunder step

Spell
nameThunder Step
school5eConjuration
level5eSorcerer 3 Warlock 3 Wizard 3

Thunder step was a conjuration spell that teleported the caster over a short distance, leaving behind a deafening shockwave of sonic energy.

Effects

Upon casting the spell, the caster could teleport to a visible location within a distance of . It was possible to take objects or a nearby willing creature along, provided the creature was willing and that there was enough room at the destination.

As soon as the caster disappeared from the original location, a loud sonic boom detonated in the area, injuring anyone within of the origin. The boom could be heard from as far as away.

Components

The spell required only a verbal component.
