Animal summoning III was an improved version of animal summoning II.

Effects

The spell operated in the same way as animal summoning I but could summon up to sixteen animals of the same size as animal summoning I, or up to eight animals of the larger size from animal summoning II, or up to four animals approximately twice the size of the larger ones from animal summoning II.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component.
