Galkyn's bolt

Spell
nameGalkyn's Bolt
inventorGalkyn
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 4

Galkyn's bolt was a wizard spell often employed by Harpers who expected a battle but needed to carry passive spells.

Effects

This spell converted medium to lowered powered spells from the caster's memory into magical bolts that could be used as an attack in a pinch. Each spell converted in this manner became a single bolt that the caster could will to attack anything they wished. This spell caused more harm to undead and creatures from the Negative Energy Plane.

Components

This spell only required verbal and somatic components to cast.
