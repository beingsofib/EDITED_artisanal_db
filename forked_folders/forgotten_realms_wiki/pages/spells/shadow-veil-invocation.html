Shadow veil, sometimes known as darkness, was an invocation used by some warlocks, particularly inexperienced ones, that allowed them to cloak themselves in magical darkness for a brief moment.

Effects

This spell made the warlock more difficult to detect, allowing them to hide or vanish from sight. The spell was relatively easy to use and warlocks required only a short rest between castings in order to regain the power necessary to cast it.
