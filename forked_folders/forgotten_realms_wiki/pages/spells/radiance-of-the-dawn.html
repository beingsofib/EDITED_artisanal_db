Radiance of the dawn

Radiance of the dawn was a channel divinity power used by clerics of the Light domain.

Effects

When invoked using their holy symbol, this spell-like ability dispelled any magical darkness out to a range of 30 ft (9.1 m). In addition, any enemy creatures exposed to the light were dealt radiant damage.
