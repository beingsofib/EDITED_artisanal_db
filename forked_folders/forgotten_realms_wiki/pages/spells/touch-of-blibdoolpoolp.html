Touch of Blibdoolpoolp

nameTouch of Blibdoolpoolp
schoolTransmutation

rules3.5

Touch of Blibdoolpoolp was a cleric and druid spell in the transmutation school granted to the faithful of the kuo-toan deity Blibdoolpoolp.

Powers

The hand of the caster of this spell would turn into a large claw, resembling that of a lobster.
