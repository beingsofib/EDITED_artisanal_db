Trobriand's glassee

Spell
nameTrobriand's Glassee
inventorTrobriand
school3eTransmutation
level3eSorcerer 6 Wizard 6

Trobriand's glassee was an arcane transmutation spell that made an object as transparent as glass.

Effects

The caster's skill determined the size of the object that could be affected. The effects of Trobriand's glassee were permanent once the spell was cast.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component a sliver of glass to cast.

History

This spell was developed by Trobriand of Waterdeep.
