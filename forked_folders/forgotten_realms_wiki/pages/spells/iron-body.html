Iron body was a transmutation or alteration spell that turned the subject's body to living iron which, in turn, greatly enhanced some resistances and granted some new abilities.

Effects

While under the effects of this spell, the target experienced the following effects:

  • Increased strength
  • Decreased speed, increased weight
  • Decreased agility
  • Lose the ability to consume liquids
  • Cast all spells as if wearing a suite of plate mail
  • Resistance to physical harm
  • Immunity to all forms of deafness, blindness, disease, drowning, poison, electricity, and stun attacks
  • Resistance to fire and acid

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell needed as a material component a small piece of iron that had once been part of an iron golem, armor worn by a hero, or a war machine to cast.
