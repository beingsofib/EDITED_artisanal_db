Harp of war

Harp of war was a powerful divine alteration and evocation spell used by priests devoted to Milil, enabling them to create a harp of glowing force.

Effects

The weightless magical harp created by this spell hung in the air near the caster, leaving them free to cast other spells if desired. The harp could only be played by the caster and each pluck of the strings triggered a power. The harp of war had the following powers:

  • shatter nonmagical metal items within
  • teleport all magic items within to a random location in Faerûn
  • shoot a lightning bolt at a single target
  • teleport without error targets selected by the caster within to a desired location
  • unleash a flame strike on a single target within

Variations in the harp of war's powers depended on the incantation. Each temple of Milil had their own unique versions of this spell.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component a thin strand of silver wire to cast.
