Twilight sanctuary

Twilight sanctuary was a channel divinity power granted to clerics who chose the Twilight domain.

Effects

As a cleric of this domain channeled divinity by displaying their holy symbol, a sphere of soothing twilight surrounded them. The dimly-lit sphere had a diameter of and moved with the direction of the caster for a minute. Every 6 seconds, anyone in the sphere could gain additional fortitude or stop being frightened or charmed. The sphere would fade should the cleric become incapacitated.
