Sleep (rune)

Spell
nameSleep
type2eRune

Sleep was a type of giant rune that functioned similarly to the sleep spell.

History

In 1366 DR, seeking a way to delay the interference of Morten and the earls of Hartsvale in his group's rescue of Princess Brianna, the runecaster Basil suggested and began work on a sleep rune.
