Enemies abound

Spell
nameEnemies Abound
school5eEnchantment
level5eBard 3 Sorcerer 3 Warlock 3 Wizard 3

Enemies abound was a spell that confused an enemy.

Effects

When cast, this spell caused a target creature to become confused and frightened, and subsequently unable to distinguish friend or foe. The creature randomly attacked anyone nearby. A strong-enough creature or a creature with a natural immunity to fear was unaffected by this spell.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
