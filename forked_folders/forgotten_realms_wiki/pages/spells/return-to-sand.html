Return to sand

Spell
nameReturn to sand
school2eAlteration, Sand
level2eWizard 7

Return to sand was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate.

Effects

This spell caused items made from metal, stone, or earth to crumple into sand. This spell targeted a single item or an entire area up to by by .

Items affected by this spell returned to their normal form after the spell ended.

This spell had no effect on magical items or creatures made from stone or earth, such as golems.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
