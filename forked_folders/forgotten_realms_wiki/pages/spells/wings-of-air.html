Wings of air

Spell
nameWings of Air
school3eTransmutation
level3eDruid 2 Sorcerer 2 Wizard 2

Wings of Air was an arcane transmutation spell used by dragons and other creatures with wings.

Effects

The spell improved the aerial maneauverability of the touched creature for a matter of minutes, provided it used its wings to fly.

Components

The spell had only a verbal component.
