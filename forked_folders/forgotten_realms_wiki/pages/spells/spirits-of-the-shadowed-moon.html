Spirits of the shadowed moon

Spell
nameSpirits of the Shadowed Moon
level4eShaman 10
type4eDaily

Spirits of the shadowed moon was a spell known to shamans that allowed them to summon a primal spirit of the moon.

Effects

The moon spirit burst forth from the summoner and appeared as a creature of mist, shadow, and glimmering lights. The spirit concealed the summoner and any allies within , and could move freely if the summoner willed it.

A spirit could be summoned only once per day, and would aid the summoner for the duration of a battle.

History

Among the feywardens of Sarifal were those who embraced the trickery of the Feywild and could gain the ability to replicate this spell.
