Immunity to adherence

Spell
nameImmunity to Adherence
school2eAbjuration, Alteration
level2eWizard 1

Immunity to adherence was an arcane spell used in the Underdark, which would allow the target to become temporarily immune to both natural and magical web-like materials.

Effects

The spell would work on any creature that the caster touched, including themselves. Once the spell was cast, the recipient could move through the web-like material, as if there was no material clinging to them at all.

The types of materials affected by the spell were natural and magical webs, mimic pseudopods, cave fisher filaments, quicksand, and viscid globs.

Components

As well as verbal and somatic components, the material components consisted of a windborn seed and a drop of either alcohol, water, tears, or saliva.
