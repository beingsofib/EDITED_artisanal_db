Wrathful smite

Spell
nameWrathful Smite
school5eEvocation
level5ePaladin 1

Wrathful smite was a spell used by paladins.

Effects

When cast, this spell allowed the caster to inflict psychic damage when attacking a target with their melee weapon. The psychic damage caused a target creature to be overcome with fear.

Components

This spell required only a verbal component.
