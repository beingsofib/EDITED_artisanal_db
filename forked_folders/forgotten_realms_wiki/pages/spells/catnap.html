Catnap

Spell
nameCatnap
school5eEnchantment
level5eArtificer 3 Bard 3 Sorcerer 3 Wizard 3

Catnap was a spell used by bards, sorcerers, and wizards that calmed multiple creatures into a restorative sleep.

Effects

A caster used this spell on up to three willing creatures to render them unconscious for a short time. The target creatures had to be within of the caster. After the spell expired, the creatures felt slightly rejuvenated, as if they had a short nap. If the creature was disturbed or hurt during the spell's duration, the spell expired and the creature regained consciousness. A caster could target more creatures at once with this spell; the amount of creatures was determined by the caster's skill level.

Components

In addition to a somatic component, the spell required a material component, a pinch of salt.
