Theskyn's hearty heave

nameTheskyn's hearty heave

level3eSorcerer 2 Wizard 2

Theskyn's hearty heave was an evocation spell that created a blast of force.

Effects

Casting this spell caused a blast of force to project from the caster, pushing away creatures that may have surrounded them.

History

This spell was created by Theskyn, a court mage who served the Stag King of Athalantar.
