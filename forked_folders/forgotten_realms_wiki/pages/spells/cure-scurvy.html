Cure scurvy was a divine abjuration spell used by specialty priests devoted to Valkur, enabling them to cure scurvy.

Effects

This spell allowed the caster to instantly cure scurvy–a common disease among malnourished sailors.

Inflict scurvy could be cast to inflict a single target with the same disease if they were within of the caster. The effects of scurvy could be negated by a cure disease spell or by including fresh fruit in the diet.

Components

In addition to verbal components and somatic components, this spell required as material components the priest's holy symbol and a salt-encrusted pit of a fruit to cast.
