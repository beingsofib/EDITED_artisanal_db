Muster at Eveningstar was a spell from the school of conjuration.

Effects

When cast, the spell teleported the caster to the Market Square in Eveningstar.

This spell could only be cast once every half hour.

History

Ceremonial keys to the city imbued with Muster at Eveningstar were distributed by Evenor Purple Dragons to those who aided the city.
