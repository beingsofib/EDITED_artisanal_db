Trebbe's scry magic was a weak arcanist spell from the Field of mentalism. It was developed in Netheril by the archwizard Trebbe in 1589 NY (−2270 DR).

Knowledge of its effect was lost in later ages, so that its relation to the spell of similar name and equally small power, detect magic, remained unclear.
