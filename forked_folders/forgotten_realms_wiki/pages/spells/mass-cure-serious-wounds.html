Mass cure serious wounds was a conjuration spell that applied an enhanced cure serious wounds to a small group of creatures.

Effects

Like other cure spells, the caster channeled positive energy that healed the living or harmed the undead. No more than one creature per level of the caster could receive this spell and they all had to be within 30 ft (9.1 m) of each other, but the caster was not required to touch the recipients.
