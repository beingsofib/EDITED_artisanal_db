Nightshield

Spell
nameNightshield
school3eAbjuration
level3eCleric 1 Sorcerer 1 Wizard 1

Nightshield was a protective arcane and divine abjuration spell from the first circle of magic. The spell could be used by clerics, wizards, and sorcerers.

Effects

When the spell was cast, it enveloped the caster in a protective nighshield that boosted their resistance to harmful effects and increased their chances of avoiding harm or shrugging off harmful magics and effects. Additionally, the shield completely negated damage from all magic missiles. The spell's protective properties increased with the caster's magical proficiencies.

Components

The spell required only a somatic and verbal components.
