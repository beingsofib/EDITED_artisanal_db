Resist magic was a southern magic divine spell available to worshipers of Thoth.

Effects

When cast, this spell imbued the caster with a measure of magical resistance. The strength of the magical resistance was determined by the caster's skill level. The caster could only use this spell on themselves.

Components

This spell required only somatic and verbal components.
