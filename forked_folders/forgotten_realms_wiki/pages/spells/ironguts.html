Ironguts was a spell used by sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

This spell allowed a caster to increase a targeted creature's resistance to poison. The spell gave resistance to all poison regardless of method of ingestion.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, a vial that contained the diluted poison from four different poisonous creatures.
