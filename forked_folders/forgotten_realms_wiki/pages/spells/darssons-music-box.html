Darsson's music box

Spell
nameDarsson's Music Box
inventorDarsson Spellmaker
school2eEnchantment, Song
level2eWizard 5

Darsson's music box was a spell unique to Halruaa.

Effects

With this spell the caster could enchant some kind of container so that it would play music or recorded messages. The recorded message or song could not be longer than 10 minutes.

The recording was played when a specific condition, or set of conditions, was met as specified by the caster.

This spell was permanent when cast.

Components

This spell required as material components a pearl or small brass cube to cast.
