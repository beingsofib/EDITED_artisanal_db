Abbathor's flowering

Spell
nameAbbathor's Flowering
deityAbbathor
inventorTombli

Abbathor's flowering was a divine spell used by the Abbathorian priest Tombli that caused gruesome wounds.

Effects

The spell caused veins throughout the body to pulse twice, then burst through the skin. The effect was incredibly painful.

History

Tombli, an Abbathorian cleric and leader of the Company of the Chimera, employed this spell and knew it by that name. He favored using it on held targets, as he did against the Uthgardt woman Saskia in Mirtul of the .
