Fire burst

Spell
nameFire Burst
school2eAlteration, Evocation
level2eWizard 1

Fire burst was an alteration and evocation spell used by wizards.

Effects

When cast upon any nonmagical flame, this spell caused a single burst of small fire arrows to lance from the flame, striking any creatures found within of the flame.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
