Malec-Keth's flame fist

Malec-Keth's flame fist was an evocation spell that was used by wizards. This spell was developed by Malec-Keth.

Effects

Upon its completion, this spell caused one of the caster's hands to burst into flame. The caster decided which hand would be wreathed in flame. The flames did not harm the caster and cast as much light as a torch. If the caster was able to touch a creature there was a high chance the flames would transfer from the caster's hand to completely envelop the creature in agonizing fire. The caster could also choose to transfer the flame to any touched object which would react accordingly to the fire.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
