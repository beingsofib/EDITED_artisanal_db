Palarandusk's fire breath

Spell
namePalarandusk's Fire Breath
inventorPalarandusk
school3eEvocation
level3eSorcerer 2 Wizard 2

Palarandusk's fire breath was an arcane evocation fire spell that allowed the caster to spit a gout of flame.

Effects

The gout of flame was a cone measuring in length. The flames burned anything caught within the cone with increasing intensity based upon the caster's skill. The caster experienced a nasty case of heartburn after casting this spell.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.

History

This spell was developed by the gold dragon Palarandusk.
