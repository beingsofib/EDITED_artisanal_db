Nature's balance

Spell
nameNature's balance
school3eTransmutation
level3eDruid 8

Nature's balance was a powerful spell used by druidss.

Effects

When cast, this spell lowered the spell resistance of all opponents within a radius. Additionally, all allies of the caster within the same area were healed. The amount of healing and spell resistance removed was determined by the caster's skill level.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
