Athelzedar's rest

Spell
nameAthelzedar's rest
inventorImrul Athelzedar
level2eWizard 9

Athelzedar's rest was a powerful arcane spell that cloaked the caster from all manner of seeking.

Effects

The ward employed layers of misdirection and nullification to hide a wizard from all seeking.

The caster could also employ his own energies to boost its duration, or those of a different wizard.

History

The Magister Imrul Athelzedar perfected this spell over five summers, starting in . After he was found anyway by Eron Imgrym and attacked, he cast a version of this spell that employed his own energies, and those of his attacker, to cast an even more powerful version of the spell on his apprentice, Nouméa Drathchuld, before commanding her to kill him and take the mantle of the Magister.
