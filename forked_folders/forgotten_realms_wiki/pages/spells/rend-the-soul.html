Rend the soul

Spell
nameRend the Soul
school3eNecromancy
level3eSorcerer 9 Warlock (fiend patron) 6 Wild mage 9 Wizard 9

Rend the soul was a powerful necromantic spell that whelmed the target with negative energy.

Effects

The spell wrapped around the target, slowly destroying them with unholy and entropic energies .

Components

The spell required verbal and somatic components to cast.
