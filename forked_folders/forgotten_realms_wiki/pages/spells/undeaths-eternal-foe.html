Undeath's eternal foe

Spell
nameUndeath's Eternal Foe
school3eAbjuration
level3eCleric 9

Undeath's eternal foe was a spell that protected against negative energy attacks.

Effects

When cast, this spell granted immunity to all negative energy based attacks as well as disease and poison. It affected any ally of the caster within .

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
