Lesser dispel

Spell
nameLesser Dispel
school3eAbjuration
level3eBard 1 Cleric 2 Druid 2 Sorcerer 2 Wizard 2

Lesser dispel was a spell that removed magical effects from a creature.

Effects

A caster was able to use this spell against either a single target or multiple targets. If used on a single creature, the spell removed all magical effects used by the creature. If used on a group of creatures, the spell removed the most powerful effect or spell from each creature.

Components

This spell required only a somatic component.
