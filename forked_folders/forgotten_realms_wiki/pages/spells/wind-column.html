Wind column

Spell
nameWind Column
school2eEvocation
spheres2eElemental Air
level2eDruid 1 Elemental sphere Elemental 1 Elemental air sphere Elemental Air 1

Wind column was an evocation spell that used existing winds to catch the caster during a fall. If the winds were strong enough, they could even buoy the caster upwards.
