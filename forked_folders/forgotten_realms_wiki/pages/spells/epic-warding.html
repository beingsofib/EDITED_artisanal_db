Epic warding

Spell
nameEpic Warding
level3eEpic
type3eEpic

Epic warding was a rare epic spell that conjured a powerful magical ward around the caster.

Effects

As of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR, the caster quickly created a powerful magical ward around themselves that protected against physical damage. More specifically, the spell reduced and absorbed damage to the user.

The effect lasted around 6 seconds. The spell could not be countered, interrupted, or dispelled using normal spells. To prevent the spell from being cast, one had to, for example, use an epic version of counterspell.

Development

Before it could be cast, epic warding had to be developed. Both divine and arcane casters could develop this spell. Developing an epic spell was different from learning normal spells. Once an epic spell was developed, the caster knew the spell. A developed epic spell became a part of the caster and could even be prepared without a spellbook (if a wizard was the caster). Only the most well-versed in spellcraft could develop epic warding.

Components

The spell required verbal and somatic components.
