Word of faith

Spell
nameWord of Faith
school3eEvocation
level3eCleric 7

Word of faith was a powerful divine evocation spell from the 7 circle of magic that blasted or killed creatures around the spellcasting cleric.

Effects

When the spell was unleashed, it released a blast of powerful divine magic that affected all enemies within a certain area. All enemies within the blast were stunned, confused, and blinded; each effect was dependent on the creature's power, with the strongest being only blinded and the weakest being instantly killed. Summoned creatures were instantly banished and sent back to their home planes.

Components

The spell required verbal and somatic components to be cast.
