Wingbind was a very rare combat spell. It was originally useable only by dragons.

Effects

This spell was only effective against other dragons. When cast, a web of force wrapped around the target dragon. The spell prevented the dragon from flying. If cast on a dragon already in flight, the targeted dragon fell and slammed into the ground.

Casting

This spell required only a verbal component.

History

A version available in could only be cast by dragons: supposedly, it would be akin to the highest magic if cast by anyone else. However, by , a version with no such limitations was circulating.
