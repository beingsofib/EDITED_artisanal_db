Psychoportation

The psychoportation discipline of psionics revolved around the propelling or displacement of objects, the manifester, and other creatures across space and time. plane shift, teleport, and teleport trigger. Unlike many disciplines, psychoportation had no equivalent among the schools of magic.

Subdisciplines

Teleportation: Powers that instantaneously transported creatures or objects great distances by shuttling them through the Astral Plane. The most potent powers of this subdiscipline allowed a manifester to cross planar boundaries. The process of psionic teleporting was usually instantaneous and one-way, though some powers allowed for two-way travel. Powers under this subdiscipline could be obstructed by anything that could block astral travel,

Notable Users

  • Nomads, psions who chose to specialize in mastery of telepathy over all other disciplines. Their specialization in the discipline lead them to accessing psychoportive powers that were largely unobtainable by other psionics users.
  • Charisma, the sapphire dragon thane of Sardior, whose area of focus was psychoportation.
