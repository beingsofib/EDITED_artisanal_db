Scatter was a conjuration spell that teleported several creatures to different nearby locations.

Effects

The spell targeted up to five creatures within a range of and teleported those who failed to resist the effect each to a visible location of the caster's choice within a range of . The spell could not teleport anyone to a point in mid-air, however; it was necessary that a surface existed under every destination spot.

Components

The spell required only a verbal component.
