Spirit worm

Spell
nameSpirit Worm
school3eNecromancy
level3eSorcerer 1 Wizard 1

Spirit worm was a necromancy spell used by wizards and sorcerers.

Effects

This spell created a persistent decay in both the body and spirt of the target. This effect manifested as either a small amount of physical harm or a slight weakening of the target's overall constitution.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a piece of fire-blackened ivory or bone carved in the shape of a worm to cast.
