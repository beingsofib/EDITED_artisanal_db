Chromatic ray

Spell
nameChromatic Ray
school3eEvocation
level3eBlackguard 4 Cleric 5

Chromatic ray was a spell used by blackguards and evil clerics.

Effects

This spell was mainly used against good-aligned dragons and other dragonkind. When cast, the spell unleashed a bright, powerful multicolored ray at a target. If the targeted dragon failed to defend itself, it temporarily lost the ability to use its innate magical abilities.

Components

This spell required only a verbal and somatic component.
