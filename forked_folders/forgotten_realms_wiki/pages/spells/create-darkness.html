Create darkness

Spell
nameCreate Darkness
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 3

Create darkness was an arcane alteration spell that extinguished all nonmagical light.

Effects

This spell could also extinguish light given off from magical sources if the source was unable to resist the spell's magic. A normal light source affected by create darkness could not be relit for one hour, while magical sources stood a chance of being relit every ten minutes.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.

History

This was an uncommon spell used by drow and other spellcasters of the Underdark.
