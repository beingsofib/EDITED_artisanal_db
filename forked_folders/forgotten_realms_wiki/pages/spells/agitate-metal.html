Agitate metal

Spell
nameagitate metal
level3eTruenamer 2
lexicon3eCrafted Tool

Agitate metal was an utterance from the Lexicon of the Crafted Tool that used truespeak to violently shift the temperature of a given item.

Usage

The utterance functioned similarly to the spells heat metal or chill metal, but its chilled form generated a blue glow around the object.

History

The recitation was known to loquasphinxes.
