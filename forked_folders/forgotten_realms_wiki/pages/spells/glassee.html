Glassee

Spell
nameGlassee
school2eAlteration
level2eWizard 6

Glassee was an arcane alteration spell that make other material as transparent as glass.

Effects

This spell temporarily made a portion of flat material as transparent as glass to the caster's gaze. This spell only worked on wood, stone, iron, or steel. The transparent area measured by and the caster could make it fully transparent or so only they could see through.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component a small piece of glass or crystal to cast.

History

Zrie Prakis, a lich from Westgate, used this spell.
