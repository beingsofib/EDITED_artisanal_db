Boon of Lathander was a spell that was commonly used by clerics of Lathander.

Effects

This spell temporarily augmented the abilities of the caster or a targeted individual. When the spell was in effect, the recipient glowed as if surrounded by faerie fire.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.

History

In the month of Eleasis, of the , Chief Prelate Dayspring of the Clerical Circle of Ravens Bluff declared it illegal for priests to cast Boon of Lathander within city limits due to a raging unnatural storm at the time called the Soulstorm, threatening arrest for those that broke this decree.
