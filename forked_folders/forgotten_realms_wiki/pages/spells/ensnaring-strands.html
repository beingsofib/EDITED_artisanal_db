Ensnaring strands

Spell
nameEnsnaring Strands
school5eConjuration

Ensnaring strands was a conjuration spell from the 1 circle of magic that restrained the target with a weapon's strike.

Effects

The ensnaring strands spell was a variant of the common ensnaring strike magic. The spell was cast with the caster's weapon being the material component. With a successful strike, the target became enwebbed in spider's webs, unlike ensnaring strike that restrained the target with plant matter. Just like the effects of the web spell, the spider's webs were flammable, and the target could dodge the attack or break out with sheer physical strength. The spell lasted for 60 seconds.

History

In the , the ensnaring strands spell was woven into the enchanted sword called Cruel Sting. It was wielded by Kar'niss, a mad follower of the Absolute, who acted as a guide to the Absolutists crossing the shadow-cursed lands on their way to the Moonrise Towers in the town of Reithwin.
