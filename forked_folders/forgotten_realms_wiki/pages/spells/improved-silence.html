Improved silence

Spell
nameImproved Silence
school2eAlteration

Improved silence was an alteration spell that created a permanent zone of complete silence.

Effects

When cast, improved silence created a wave of silence that rendered the area around the target completely soundless, similar to how the silence spell functioned. All those who were affected could not cast any spells that required vocal components. The effects of improved silence were permanent unless removed or dispelled by magical means.

The spell required verbal and somatic components and had a range of . The spell's targets could be creatures or objects. When cast on an unwilling creature, the spell had a chance of being shrugged off.

History

In the late 14 century DR, vampire servants of Kimber Gallen of Ravens Bluff carried coins ensorcelled with the improved silence spell to aid them in stealth and sneak.
