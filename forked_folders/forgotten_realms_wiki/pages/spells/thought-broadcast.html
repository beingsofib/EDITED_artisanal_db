Thought broadcast

Thought broadcast was a divine alteration spell from the thought sphere that allowed a subject's thoughts to be experienced by others.

Effects

While this spell was active the subject's thoughts were broadcasted to others within . This could be extremely useful or hindering depending on whether or not the subject was a willing participant. The spell's magic crossed any language barriers so the thoughts could be understood by all races.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material components a small balloon to cast. The balloon was inflated during the casting and then consumed.

History

The Scepter of Mystra, a relic of the Mystran faith, could cast the thought broadcast spell.
