Law mantle

The Law mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concept of law.

Abilities

Manifesters of this mantle gained the unique psionic ability to expend their psionic focus to cause greater harm to any creature that wasn't lawfully-aligned with their next attack using a melee weapon, ranged weapon, or spell.

Powers

Novice manifesters were able to improve their martial abilities with grip of iron and hammer, as well as secure any container or gateway with psionic lock. Manifesters of some experience could alter gravity in an area with heavy earth and make themselves impossible to budge with immovability. As a manifester progressed further they learned how to steel their minds against the effects of others with steadfast perception and tower of iron will. Masters of this mantle obtained the powers of psionic iron body and timeless body.
