Weirdshield

Spell
nameWeirdshield
school2eEvocation
Incantation (Shielding)
level2eWizard 7

Weirdshield was a spell that was first developed by the phaerimms.

Effects

When cast, this spell created a powerful magical barrier in a location of the caster's choosing. This barrier was triangular in shape and had tentacles along the edge, each with a humanoid hand at the end. Once cast, the caster was able to move the barrier. Swords created from magic, including swords of dancing within were instantly drawn to this barrier, which then destroyed them. When the barrier was within of the caster, it protected them from magical attacks, reflecting the spell back to the creature who cast it. The barrier was able to block incorporeal creatures. It was also immune to all physical attacks and nearly all magical attacks. If this barrier came into contact with another type of barrier spell, both spells instantly expired. The only other way the spell could be defeated was either by a dispel magic, limited wish, or wish spell, or if the caster was killed.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required several material components: the caster's saliva, a small shield, and an arm.

History

This spell was originally created by the phaerimms of Myth Drannor.
