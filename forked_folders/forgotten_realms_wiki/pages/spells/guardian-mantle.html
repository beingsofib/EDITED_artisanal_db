Guardian mantle

The Guardian mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionics powers and abilities derived from the universal concept or philosophy of the guardian, being themed around bolstering defenses and protection.

Abilities

Those who followed the guardian mantle gained the unique psionic power to take on the harm that would have been suffered by a nearby ally from an attack. Doing so required the expenditure of a psionic focus.

The psychic aura exuded by divine minds of the guardian mantle made all allies within the radius of the aura suffer less harm in battle so long as the manifester was psionically focused.

Powers

Novice manifesters could enhance the defensive qualities of their body with thicken skin, make their weapons less lethal with deaden blow, and minimize the baleful spell and psionic effects with damp power. Manifesters of some experience could outright negate psionic powers and effects with dispel psionics and erect a protective wall of ectoplasm. As a manifester progressed further they could protect the mind itself with protection from psionics and mentally reinforce living tissue against energy types with mind over energy. Masters of this mantle obtained psionic iron body, allowing them to use pure psionic will to transform their tissue into living iron.
