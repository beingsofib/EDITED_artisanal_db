Tie

Spell
nameTie
school1eAlteration
level1eMagic-user 0
type1eUseful

Tie was a useful cantrip that beginning wizards could learn. It caused the end of a rope-like item to form a knot around another object of similar shape.

Effects

This spell worked on rope-like objects from as small as a thread to as large as a cable. The object to be connected had to be within of the rope (with enough slack to form a knot) and had to resemble a rope in some fashion (a ring, loop, rod, another rope, or the end of the same rope) for the knot to be completed. Any common knot (square, half-hitch, bowline etc.) familiar to the caster could be tied.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The name of the knot was spoken as the caster held up three fingers.

History

The untitled book known as Briel's Book of Shadows described this cantrip along with exterminate, wilt, and smokepuff.
