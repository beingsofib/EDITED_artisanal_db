Rend shadow weave

Spell
nameRend Shadow Weave
school3eAbjuration
level3eCleric 3 Sorcerer 3 Wizard 3

Rend shadow weave was an abjuration spell that affected the Shadow Weave.

Effects

When cast, this spell created an area of dead magic measuring within the Shadow Weave. The spell had no effect on the Weave.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component, a moonstone with a minimum value of 50 gp.
