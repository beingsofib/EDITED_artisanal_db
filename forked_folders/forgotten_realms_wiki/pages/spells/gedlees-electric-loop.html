Gedlee's electric loop

Gedlee's electric loop was a spell used by sorcerers and wizards.

Effects

When cast, this spell caused a small strobe of lightning to cycle through all creatures within of the caster. Creatures struck with the lightning were hurt by the electrical surge and were temporarily stunned. The strength of the lightning was determined by the caster's skill level.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required material components—a small magnet and some copper wire.
