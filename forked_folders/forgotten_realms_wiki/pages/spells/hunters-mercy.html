Hunter's mercy

Spell
nameHunter's Mercy
school3eTransmutation
level3eRanger 1

Hunter's mercy was a transmutation spell granted uniquely to rangers by their deities.

Effects

After a prayer for mercy, a ranger's next shot with a bow, if taken quickly, would not miss and had a good chance of inflicting critical damage to its target.

Components

To cast this spell required verbal and somatic components.
