Spider (cantrip)

Spell
nameSpider
school1eSummoning
level1eMagic-user 0
type1ePersonal

Spider was a personal cantrip that acolyte wizards could learn. It summoned a common, normal-sized spider to a nearby location chosen by the caster.

Effects

This cantrip conjured a single common spider. It appeared at a spot within of the caster and arrived in an irritated state. If summoned upon a living creature, the spider immediately tried to bite, distracting the target for a few seconds. There was a slight chance that the conjured spider was venomous, in which case the target took minimal damage and had a chance to resist, or else fall ill for a few days. The effects of spider venom could be reduced with slow poison or removed with neutralize poison.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster spoke the word "arachnid" while wiggling their fingers like the legs of a crawling spider.
