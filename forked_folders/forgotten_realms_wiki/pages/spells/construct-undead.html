Construct undead

Spell
nameConstruct undead
school2eNecromancy, Universal
level2eWizard 6

Construct undead was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate. This spell was used by ghul lords.

Effects

Ghul lords used this spell to construct undead servants from spare body parts, both human and inhuman.

Once the caster determined the type of servant needed, the spell could be cast. These servants could only perform basic tasks that did not require thought. Physical labor tasks were common selections.

These undead servants made for poor combatants, being physically weaker than the weakest zombie or skeleton.

This spell lasted for many days depending on the power of the caster.

Components

This spell required a corpse or skeleton to cast.
