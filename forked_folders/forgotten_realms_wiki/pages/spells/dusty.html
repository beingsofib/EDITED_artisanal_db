Dusty

Spell
nameDusty
school1eEvocation
level1eMagic-user 0
type1eReversed

Dusty was a reversed cantrip that neophyte wizards could learn. It caused a thin layer of grimy dust to cover an area.

Effects

This cantrip had a range of and could affect a circular area across. When cast, the area was covered in a film of dust and grime as if it had been left unoccupied and undisturbed for a long time.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster made a quiet humming sound while moving both hands as if shaking or sprinkling dust over something.
