Hurl thunderbolt was a divine evocation spell used only by specialty priests devoted to Gilgeam called Martinets.

Effects

This spell allowed a Martinet to hurl a lightning bolt from their upraised holy symbol toward a single target. In addition to electrifying the target, the bolt unleashed a clap of thunder upon striking the target, temporarily deafening anyone within of the target.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component the Martinet's holy symbol to cast.
