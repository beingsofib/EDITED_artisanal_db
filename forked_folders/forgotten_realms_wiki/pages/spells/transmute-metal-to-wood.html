Transmute metal to wood was a transmutation spell that permanently transformed any metal within a 40 foot (12 m) radius into wood. Older versions of this spell only operated on a single object.

Effects

All objects made of metal were affected. Magical items had a chance to resist being transformed. Wooden weapons and armor were less effective and had a chance to splinter and break. The effect was permanent unless a limited wish or the equivalent was used to transform items back to their original state. When this spell was used only on a single object, it took a full wish to return it to metallic form again.

Components

Verbal and somatic components, as well as the caster's holy symbol or divine focus were required to cast this spell.
