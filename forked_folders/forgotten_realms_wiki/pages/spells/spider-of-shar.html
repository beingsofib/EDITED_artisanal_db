Spider of Shar

Spell
nameSpider of Shar
deityShar

Spider of Shar was a divine spell used by priests of Shar.

Effects

The spider of Shar spell created a cloud of darkness overhead, which projected tendrils down. Those tendrils whipped around, bringing stinging pain; they could also twist limbs, or even heads.

Components

The spell involved motions, and thus had a somatic component. Other components may have been involved.

History

A casting of spider of Shar was employed against the Simbul by the Sharran priest Staenyn in during her raid on the stronghold of Azmyrandyr, a Red Wizard. He aimed it at her projected image, however, and only managed one slaying by friendly fire: the Red Wizard Marlus Belraeblood, whose head was twisted off by the spell.
