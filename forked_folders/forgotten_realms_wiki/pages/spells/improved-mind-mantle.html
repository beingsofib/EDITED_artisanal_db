Improved mind mantle

Spell
nameImproved Mind Mantle
school2eAbjuration
level2eWizard 3

Improved mind mantle was a spell that was commonly used by alhoons.

Effects

This was a more powerful version of mind mantle. This spell protected the caster's or targeted creature's mind against charm and mind-reading spells. It worked by covering the thoughts of the creature with random thoughts. The spell also protected against magical items and psionic influences. While protected, the targeted creature was able to cast spells. The spell was vulnerable to dispel magic.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
