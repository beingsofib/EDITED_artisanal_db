Flock of birds

Flock of birds was a divine conjuration spell used only by specialty priests devoted to Thoth called Scriveners.

Effects

This spell would summon a large flock of birds to a sphere where they would normally be found. The birds were so densely packed that vision was limited to and spellcasting within the sphere was impossible.

The scrivener could direct the birds to fanatically attack a single target within the sphere. The birds were not able to harm creatures immune to normal weapons. Nothing would drive the birds away before the spell ended, but they could be killed by normal methods.

Druids and nature priests viewed this spell as an affront to nature and would make the caster atone for its use if witnessed by the druid.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components a handful of feathers and the priest's holy symbol to cast.
