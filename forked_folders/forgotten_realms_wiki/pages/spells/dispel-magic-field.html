Dispel magic field

Spell
namedispel magic field
schoolAbjuration

The dispel magic field was a spell used against Tsjancy Yildree during his time as the Magister.

Effects

This variant of dispel magic created a field of similar effects to it, but which instead "waited", and could not be removed with anything less than a limited wish. Its range, however, was limited.

History

The spell was used to disable Tsjancy Yildree, and then to keep him captive in a cell. The Magister finally bypassed the spell by severing the hand trapped in the manacle that kept him in place, then staggering out of the spell's range.
