Storm tower

Spell
nameStorm Tower
school3eAbjuration
level3eDruid 7

Storm tower was a powerful abjuration spell used by druids.

Effects

This spell formed a dark tower of swirling storm clouds that negated many forms of magic. The high speed of the swirling winds of the tower prevented any sort of ranged attack to pass through. Man-sized creatures were simply not strong enough to pass through the barrier of the storm tower, though giant-sized creatures could.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
