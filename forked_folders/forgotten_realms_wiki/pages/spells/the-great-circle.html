The great circle

Spell

spheres2eCreation, Sun

level2eCleric 6 Creation sphere Creation 6 Druid 6 Sun sphere Sun 6

The great circle was a cooperative abjuration spell that four to twenty priests could use to cleanse an area of evil. The reverse of this spell, the black circle, could purge an area of good.
