Warm was a useful cantrip that apprentice wizards could learn. It raised the temperature of a quantity of liquid or solid matter.

Effects

This cantrip had a range of . liquids and solids that amounted to no more than in volume. The rise in temperature was about 40 ℉ ( ℃) but could not go above . The cantrip and temperature change were nearly instantaneous but thereafter the heated material acted normally in the conditions surrounding it, slowly cooling back down to the ambient temperature.

Components

This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster vigorously rubbed both hands together and said aah.
