Divine punishment

Spell
nameDivine Punishment
school3eEvocation
level3eCleric 5 Favored soul 5

Divine punishment was a divine evocation spell that smote a target with heavenly power.

Effects

The spell struck the target with a continuous burning divine light, slowly annihilating the victim. The cleric or favored soul channelling it could repeat the casting and further intensify the power of the smiting.

Components

The spell required verbal, somatic, and material components to cast.
