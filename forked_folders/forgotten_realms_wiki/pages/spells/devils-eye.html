Devil's eye was a divination spell that gave the caster a devil's eyesight.

Effects

On casting devil's eye, the caster's eyes gained the eyesight of a devil. However, it was a downgraded version of it. This spell's recipient could see through normal darkness, but only to a range of 30 ft (9.14 m) through magical darkness.

Components

The casting of devil's eye required somatic and verbal components.
