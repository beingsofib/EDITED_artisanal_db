nameCandle

Candle was a conjuration orison.

Effects

This spell was cast while the caster was holding a candle. Once cast, the spell caused the candle to light. The candle flame could not be extinguished while the candle was held by the caster. The candle burned normally. The flame could be extinguished with water or smothering.
