Foresight was a divination spell that granted the caster powerful warnings about the future.

Components

The spell required verbal, somatic, and material components. The material component was a hummingbird's feather.

History

The Netherese arcanist Smolyn was credited with the creation of this spell in . It was originally called Smolyn's foreseer.

Foresight was an uncommon but somewhat widely accessible spell in the Realms circa 1358 DR. Only the most powerful spellcasters could use this spell.
