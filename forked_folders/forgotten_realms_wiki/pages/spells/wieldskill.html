Wieldskill was a divination spell used by clerics. This spell originated from Gond the Wonderbringer.

Effects

When cast, this spell granted the caster a hint of divine guidance. The effect typically manifested by either temporarily enhancing the caster's proficiency with a known skill, or by granting them rudimentary knowledge of an unknown skill. This also applied to the use of weapons.

Components

This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
