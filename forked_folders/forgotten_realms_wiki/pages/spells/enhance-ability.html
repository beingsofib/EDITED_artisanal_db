Enhance ability

Enhance ability was a transmutation spell that would enhance a target's abilities for up to a hour with effects generalized from the six famous improvement spells used by adventurers.

Effects

The caster would pick an effect that would grant improvements to the target in a predetermined way:

  • Bear's Endurance: The target was granted improved constitution and health.
  • Bull's Strength: The target gained an improvement to its strength and also was able to carry twice as much as before.
  • Cat's Grace: The target gained an improvement to its dexterity and could fall from a height of 20 feet without getting hurt.
  • Eagle's Splendor: The target would gain an increased advantage from its charisma.
  • Fox's Cunning: The target gained advantages to attempts that used its intelligence.
  • Owl's Wisdom: The target would gain benefits to using wisdom.

For every spell level higher then the normal spell level used, another target could gain a benefit of this spell.

Components

Verbal and somatic components were needed, as well as a feather or fur from a beast as a material component.
