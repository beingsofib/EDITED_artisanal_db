Angharradh's favor

Angharradh's favor was a divine spell, or Channel Divinity, granted to worshipers of Angharradh that granted one ally a burst of skill or luck against various types of attacks. This power could only be invoked after the caster or an ally demonstrated skill or luck on their own.
