Frost lance

Spell
nameFrost Lance
school3eEvocation
level3eSorcerer 3 Wild mage 3 Wizard 3

Frost lance was an arcane evocation spell that struck the target with beams of freezing energy.

Effects

The spell shot off a lance of pure cold energy at the target that inflicted injury and death upon them. The lance reached about away, and the more skilled the caster, the more lances they could create at once.

Components

The spell required verbal and somatic components to cast.
