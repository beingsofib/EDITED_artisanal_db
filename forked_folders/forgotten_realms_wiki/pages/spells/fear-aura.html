Fear aura

Spell
nameFear Aura
school2eAbjuration
level2eWizard 5

Fear aura was a very rare spell.

Effects

When cast, this spell caused all creatures within who looked at the caster to be stricken with terror. The terrified creatures would either flee the area or become immobilized in fear. Creatures who were either stronger than the caster, had a natural immunity to fear, or were mindless were not affected by this spell.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component: a piece of cloth cut from the shroud of a lich.

History

This spell was known to be used by wizards of the Cult of the Dragon.
