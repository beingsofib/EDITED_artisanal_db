Thundaerl's universal taster

Spell
nameThundaerl's Universal Taster
inventorThundaerl
school2eDivination, Illusion
level2eWizard 2

Thundaerl's universal taster was a bespoke spell that detected and announced the presence of poison in food.

Effects

Any food within of the caster, when he looked at it, would register in his sight with luminous purple flames where substances harmful to them were present in the meal. At will, the caster could make hissing black illusory serpents rise from any such poisoned foodstuffs. The spell's effect could even penetrate sauces, solid foods, and food container lids.

Components

Besides verbal and somatic components, the spell called for a spark of flame and a piece of snakeskin.

History

The spell was Thundaerl's latest as of approximately .
