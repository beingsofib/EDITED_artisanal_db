Leonal's roar

Spell
nameLeonal's Roar
school3eEvocation
level3eDruid 8

Named for leonal guardinals, leonal's roar was an evocation spell for druids that injured, debilitated, and even slew creatures that were not good.

Effect

To non-good beings within 40 feet (12 meters) who lacked the fortitude to resist, leonal's roar harmed them and inflicted a holy word, which could daze, weaken, paralyze, or even slay them.

Components

It required only a verbal component.
