Mystra's unraveling

Spell
nameMystra's Unraveling
school2eAlteration, Enchantment
level2eWizard 9

Mystra's unraveling was an arcane spell that unmade magic items and enchantments.

Effects

The spell could be targeted against ongoing spells alone, or against spells and enchanted objects. In either case, it affected all targets within that weren't touching the caster. These were unfailingly broken immediately, with the caster learning of all the powers of items so destroyed. It also broke planar linkages, and caused objects held within extradimensional spaces to appear immediately. Against artifacts, it had no other effect but to reveal their powers.

Components

The spell called only for verbal and somatic components.

History

This spell was used by Elminster against Delmuth Echorn in the . He destroyed a sword with it.
