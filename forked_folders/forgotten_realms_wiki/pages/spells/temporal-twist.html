Temporal twist

Temporal twist was an utterance from the Lexicon of the Evolving Mind that used truespeak that granted extra precious fractions of a second, or in its reversed form, it drew away time.

Usage

In its straight form, it reweaved time to grant just enough extra slack for an additional melee attack. It would not add up with other sources. In the reversed form, the target lost time, becoming dazed and unable to act for a few seconds.

History

The utterance was known to garblers, in its reversed form.
