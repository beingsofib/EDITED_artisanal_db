Disk of solar vengeance

Spell
nameDisk of Solar Vengeance
deityHorus-Re
school3eEvocation
level3eInitiate of Horus-Re 1

Disk of solar vengeance was a spell used by clerics of Horus-Re.

Effects

When cast, this spell created a brilliant disk of flame behind the caster's head. The spell gave the caster an increased resistance to cold.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a divine focus
