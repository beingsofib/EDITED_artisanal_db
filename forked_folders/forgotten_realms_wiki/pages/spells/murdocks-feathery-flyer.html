Murdock's feathery flyer was an alteration spell used by wizards. This spell was invented by Murdock.

Effects

This spell created a feathery membrane between the caster's arms and extending to their feet. With arms and legs spread the caster could jump from a height and glide through the air. The membrane would disappear instantly when the spell ended.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required an eagle's feather to cast.
