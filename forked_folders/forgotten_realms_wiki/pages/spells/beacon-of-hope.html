Beacon of hope was an abjuration spell that granted health and feelings of hope.

Effects

Any targeted person or creature within gained an advantage for surviving death effects and divine spells, like those of clerics, druids, and rangers. The targets also gained the most out of healing spells cast on them.

Components

Casting this spell required verbal and somatic components.
