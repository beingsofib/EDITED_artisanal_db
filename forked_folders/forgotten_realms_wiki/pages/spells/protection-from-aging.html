Protection from aging was a spell used by clerics of Labelas Enoreth.

Effects

When cast, this spell protected the caster or a targeted creature from unnatural aging. This included the effects of time-based spells and the effect of seeing a ghost.

Components

In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required material components, the holy symbol of Labelas Enoreth and a black sapphire with a value of at least 50 gp, crushed into a powder.
