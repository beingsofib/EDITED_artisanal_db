Mental Power mantle

The Mental Power mantle was a psionic mantle that granted ardents and divine minds an array of psionic powers and abilities derived from the cosmic philosophy of the power of the mind.

Abilities

Those who followed the Mental Power mantle gained a greater capacity for reserving psychic energy within their mind.

Divine minds of the Mental Power mantle exuded a psychic aura that made them and any allies within the radius of their aura more resistant to mind-affecting spells and abilities.

Powers

Novice manifesters could disrupt the minds of others with id insinuation and mental disruption, as well as assault the mind with mind thrust. Manifesters of some experience could protect the mind with intellect fortress and strike back at telepathic attacks with mind trap. As a manifester progressed further they learned how to crush mental essence with psychic crush
