Steel wind strike

Spell
nameSteel Wind Strike
school5eConjuration
level5eRanger 5 Wizard 5

Steel wind strike was a conjuration spell that caused a weapon and its wielder to meld with the wind and strike multiple opponents within seconds.

Effects

The spell infused the caster's weapon with magic. The caster and the weapon then disappeared, striking up to five different opponents within a range of for considerable damage. The caster and the weapon then reappeared next to one of the opponents that were attacked.

Components

The spell required only somatic components and a melee weapon worth at least 1 sp as a material component.
