Redirect spell

Spell
nameRedirect Spell
school3eAbjuration
level3eCleric 3 Paladin 3

When Redirect spell was cast by a divine spellcaster, it created a connection between them and an ally whereupon any spells cast at the ally would be directed to the caster. For this redirection to work, both participants must remain within range of one another for the spell's duration. If this range was broken, the protective connection would end.
