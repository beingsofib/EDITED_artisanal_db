The feeblemind field was an especially vicious spell developed by Tsjancy Yildree during his time as the Magister, between and . After his death, it became a lost spell, by the will of Mystra.

Effects

The spell disabled anyone who was maneuvered into it, likely through an effect akin to the feeblemind spell.

History

After Tsjancy's death, the spell became a lost spell, by the will of Mystra.
