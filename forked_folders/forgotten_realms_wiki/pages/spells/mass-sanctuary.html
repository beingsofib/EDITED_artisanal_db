Mass sanctuary was an abjuration spell.

Effects

Mass sanctuary provided those benefitting from it the same effect as the sanctuary spell. The difference between that spell and mass sanctuary was the number of affected people. while mass sanctuary affected several touched creatures the number of which increased with the caster's skill.

Components

Apart from the aforementioned difference, mass sanctuary worked like sanctuary and required somatic and verbal components as well as a divine focus to cast.
