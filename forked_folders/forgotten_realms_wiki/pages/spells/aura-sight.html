Aura sight, formerly known as detection of good or evil, was a psionic power of the clairsentience discipline that allowed a manifester to view the invisible auras given off by all creatures, objects, powers, and spells.

Effects

This power granted a manifester the power to read the invisible auras of living creatures and objects. Auras appeared as glowing halos or envelopes of colored light surrounding creatures and objects. Contrary to its former name, throughout the ages aura sight could identify any alignment on the spectrum, though later versions of the power could only focus on one axis of the alignment spectrum at a time, meaning good and evil auras or chaotic and lawful auras.

Prior to the Time of Troubles, the rate of successfully identifying an aura could vary depending upon one's mastery of psionics. And reading the aura of a creature was easier than identifying the aura of an object.

Following the Time of Troubles, An aura could potentially be read completely or slightly incorrectly, such as only identifying the chaotic portion of a chaotic neutral individual's alignment. If aura sight backfired on the manifester, they would simply be unable to use the power again for the next 24 hours.

The even later versions of this power that cropped up in the late 14 century DR lacked this specific backfiring side-effect, had a maximum range of , and a duration of 10 or more minutes, though it still required the manifester to maintain concentration. A manifester could further augment the power's range in increments of by focusing their psychic energy into the manifestation.

This later version provided greater information the longer a manifester concentrated on the power. At first a manifester could only identify that an aura was in the area, seeing it suffuse the surroundings as a colored haze, unable to pinpoint its origin. Next a manifester could identify the number of auras within an area. The final examination step revealed the originator of each aura within the manifester's line of sight. However, greater that were more powerful than the manifester could overwhelm their senses, temporarily dazing them and prematurely ending aura sight's manifestation.

Signifigance

Aura sight could be used to identify cerebral parasites and latent instances of the implanted suggestion power.

A powerful psionics user could render the effects of aura sight permanent through use of the power incarnate.

Users

This power was knowable by lurks, It was particularly common as a wild talent among necromancers, whom were known for developing unusual mental abilities.

Aura sight was exhibited as an innate psionic ability among baku, emerald dragons, greater shedu, and pyreen.

In psionics terms, aura sight was known by arcanadaemons, charonadaemons, and couatl.

Known/Notable Users

  • Aleithilithos, a legendary amethyst dragon.
  • Anthraxus, a unique ruler among the yugoloths.
  • Madame Iyvdanya, a fortuneteller in Skullport, possessed aura sight as a wild talent.
  • Merith Strongbow, a member of the Knights of Myth Drannor.
  • Phaedrus, a hollyphant.
  • Raulothim, a great wyrm emerald dragon.
  • Tanith, an innkeeper and psionicist.
