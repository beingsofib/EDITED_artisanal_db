Matter agitation,

Effects

This power could be used to target one item, weighing no more than , within of the psion. Early instances of this power found that all effects occurred after a period of ten minutes. Later documentation found that molecular agitation's effects were actually evident as early as one minute, but that their severity increased the longer the power was active for.

For the first minute of active use: readily flammable material would ignite and burn brightly, such as paper, parchment, straw, dry grass, etc. Skin would become red and tender. And wood would darken.

After two minutes of active use: wood would begin to smoke and smolder, metal would become hot to the touch (an effect some described as being similar to the spell heat metal), skin would begin to blister, hair would begin to smolder, paint would shrivel, and water would boil.

After three minutes of active use: wood would properly become aflame, metal would be scorching, skin would be burning away, water would be boiling, and lead would melt.

After four minutes, molecular agitation would cause even steel to grow soft, and after five minutes of active use steel would melt.

Display

When used this power manifested an auditory and material display.

Significance

This power was one of the prerequisites for learning the psychokinesis discipline detonate.

Users

This power was knowable by psions and wilders, ardents and divine minds who followed the chaos mantle, and could be gained as a wild talent.

Molecular agitation was exhibited as an innate psionic ability by amethyst dragons, aratha, beljuril dragons, greater shedu, and lythlyx.

In psionics terms, it could be known by duergar, phoenixes, and planetars.

Thonots, psionic equivalents of shamans within quaggoth society, possessed molecular agitation as one of their psionic powers.

Known/Notable Users

  • K'yorl Odran, a drow psionicist and the matron mother of House Oblodra.
