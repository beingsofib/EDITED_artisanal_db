Burning talons

Spell
nameBurning Talons

Burning talons was a spell known to the archdevil Harhoring.

Effect

Disembodied talons would swirl into existence at the beginning of this spell.

History

The spell was used by Harhoring against the Simbul in , during her scouring of Avernus in search of Elminster. He employed it to augment his own talons.
