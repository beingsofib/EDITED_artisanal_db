# Wildroot
*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 25 gp

Introducing the juice of a wildroot into a [poisoned](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) creature's bloodstream (for example, by rubbing it on an open wound) rids the creature of the [poisoned](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition. Once used in this way, a wildroot loses this property.

*Source: Tomb of Annihilation p. 205*
