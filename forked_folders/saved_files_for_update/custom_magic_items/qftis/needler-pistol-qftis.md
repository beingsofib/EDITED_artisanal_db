# Needler Pistol
*Adventuring gear*  
![Left (Top to Bottom): Need...](file://C:\Users\cgray\Desktop\clihome\5etools-img/items/QftIS/Technology.webp#right "Left (Top to Bottom): Needler Pistol, Paralysis Pistol, Bandolier of Grenades. Middle: Antigravity Belt. Right (Top to Bottom): Laser Pistol, Robot Controller, Laser Rifle")


This strange pistol resembles a flask with a honeycomb of tubes sticking out its front. The weapon is powered by an [energy cell](/3-Mechanics.CLI/items/energy-cell-xdmg.md) stored at the base of the flask. Placing a full [energy cell](/3-Mechanics.CLI/items/energy-cell-xdmg.md) in the pistol gives the pistol 10 charges.

As an action while holding this pistol, you can expend 1 of its charges to fire a burst of glowing, needlelike darts from the pistol in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking `8d4` piercing damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

## Replacing the Energy Cell

While the pistol has charges remaining, its [energy cell](/3-Mechanics.CLI/items/energy-cell-xdmg.md) can't be removed. Once the pistol has 0 charges, you can replace the [energy cell](/3-Mechanics.CLI/items/energy-cell-xdmg.md) with a new cell by using an action or a bonus action.

*Source: Quests from the Infinite Staircase p. 192*
