# Sleep Grenade
*Explosive*  
![Left (Top to Bottom): Need...](file://C:\Users\cgray\Desktop\clihome\5etools-img/items/QftIS/Technology.webp#right "Left (Top to Bottom): Needler Pistol, Paralysis Pistol, Bandolier of Grenades. Middle: Antigravity Belt. Right (Top to Bottom): Laser Pistol, Robot Controller, Laser Rifle")


As an action, a character can throw a grenade at a point up to 60 feet away. With a grenade launcher, the character can propel the grenade up to 120 feet away.

This grenade releases a cloud of soporific mist that fills a 20-foot-radius sphere, then dissipates. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or have the [unconscious](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Unconscious) condition for 1 hour. The condition ends on a creature early if the creature takes damage or if another creature uses an action to shake it awake.
