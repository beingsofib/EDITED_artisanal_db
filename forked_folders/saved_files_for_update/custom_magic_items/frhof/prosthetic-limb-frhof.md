# Prosthetic Limb
*Wondrous item, common*  
![](file://C:\Users\cgray\Desktop\clihome\5etools-img/items/XDMG/Prosthetic Limb.webp#right)


*A perfect illustration of the notion that magic should make our lives easier. Thanks to the generosity of Sultana Songal, anyone who requires a Prosthetic Limb can have the device for free. Speak to one of our vendors in the Aurora's Emporium nearest you.*

This magic item replaces a lost limb—a hand, an arm, a foot, a leg, or a similar body part. While the prosthetic is attached, it functions identically to the part it replaces. You can detach or reattach it as a [Magic](/3-Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Magic) action, and it can't be removed against your will while you are alive.

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 132*
