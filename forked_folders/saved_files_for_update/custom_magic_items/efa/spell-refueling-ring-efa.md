# Spell-Refueling Ring
*Ring, uncommon (requires attunement by a spellcaster)*  



While wearing this ring, you can recover one expended spell slot as a [Bonus Action](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md). The recovered slot can be of level 3 or lower. Once used, the ring can't be used again until the next dawn.

*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 112*
