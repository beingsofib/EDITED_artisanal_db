# Manifold Tool
*Wondrous item, common (requires attunement)*  



This tool takes the form of a wrench, a screwdriver, or another basic tool. As a [Magic](/3-Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Magic) action, you can touch the item and transform it into a type of [Artisan's Tools](/3-Mechanics.CLI/items/artisans-tools-xphb.md) of your choice. Whatever form the tool takes, you have proficiency with it when you use it.

*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 112*
