# Mind Sharpener
*Ring, uncommon (requires attunement)*  



The item has 4 charges. When you fail a Constitution saving throw to maintain [Concentration](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Concentration), you can take a [Reaction](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) and expend 1 of the item's charges to succeed instead. The item regains `1d4` expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 112*
