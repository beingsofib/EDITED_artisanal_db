# Medallion of Thoughts
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  
![](file://C:\Users\cgray\Desktop\clihome\5etools-img/items/XDMG/Medallion of Thoughts.webp#right)

- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

The medallion has 5 charges. While wearing it, you can expend 1 charge to cast [Detect Thoughts](/3-Mechanics.CLI/spells/detect-thoughts-xphb.md) (save DC 13) from it. The medallion regains `1d4` expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 278. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
