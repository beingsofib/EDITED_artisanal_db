# Potion of Superior Healing
*Potion, rare*  


- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

You regain `8d4 + 8` [Hit Points](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) when you drink this potion. The potion's red liquid glimmers when agitated.
