# Yellow Death
*Adventuring gear, poison (ingested)*  


- **Properties**: [Poison](/3-Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 150 gp

A creature subjected to Yellow Death makes a DC 13 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the creature takes 14 (`4d6`) Poison damage and has the [Poisoned](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for 1 minute. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage only.

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Adventures in Faerûn p. 237*
