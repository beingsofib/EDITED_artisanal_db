# Ulcrun
*Adventuring gear, poison (injury)*  


- **Properties**: [Poison](/3-Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 1,000 gp

A creature subjected to Ulcrun must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or take 21 (`6d6`) Poison damage and gain 1 [Exhaustion](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Exhaustion) level.

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Adventures in Faerûn p. 237*
