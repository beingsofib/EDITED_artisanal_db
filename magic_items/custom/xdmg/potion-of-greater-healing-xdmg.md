# Potion of Greater Healing
*Potion, uncommon*  


- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

You regain `4d4 + 4` [Hit Points](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) when you drink this potion. The potion's red liquid glimmers when agitated.
