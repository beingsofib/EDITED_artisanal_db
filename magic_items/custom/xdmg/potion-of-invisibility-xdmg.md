# Potion of Invisibility
*Potion, rare*  
![](file://C:\Users\cgray\Desktop\clihome\5etools-img/items/XDMG/Potion of Invisibility.webp#right)

- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

This potion's container looks empty but feels as though it holds liquid. When you drink the potion, you have the [Invisible](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Invisible) condition for 1 hour. The effect ends early if you make an attack roll, deal damage, or cast a spell.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 288. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
