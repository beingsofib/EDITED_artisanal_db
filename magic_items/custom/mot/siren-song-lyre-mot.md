# Siren Song Lyre
*Wondrous item, instrument ([lyre](/3-Mechanics.CLI/items/lyre-xphb.md)), rare (requires attunement)*  



You can use an action to play this lyre and cast one of the following spells from it: [animal friendship](/3-Mechanics.CLI/spells/animal-friendship-xphb.md), [charm person](/3-Mechanics.CLI/spells/charm-person-xphb.md), [enthrall](/3-Mechanics.CLI/spells/enthrall-xphb.md), [suggestion](/3-Mechanics.CLI/spells/suggestion-xphb.md). If the spell requires a saving throw, the spell save DC is 13.

Once the instrument has been used to cast a spell, it can't be used to cast that spell again until the next dawn.

*Source: Mythic Odysseys of Theros p. 198*
