# Paralysis Pistol
*Adventuring gear*  
![Left (Top to Bottom): Need...](file://C:\Users\cgray\Desktop\clihome\5etools-img/items/QftIS/Technology.webp#right "Left (Top to Bottom): Needler Pistol, Paralysis Pistol, Bandolier of Grenades. Middle: Antigravity Belt. Right (Top to Bottom): Laser Pistol, Robot Controller, Laser Rifle")


This curious-looking pistol is shaped like a large, glass bulb with a handle on the bottom and brass prongs protruding from its front. The weapon is powered by an [energy cell](/3-Mechanics.CLI/items/energy-cell-xdmg.md) stored in its grip. Placing a full [energy cell](/3-Mechanics.CLI/items/energy-cell-xdmg.md) in the pistol gives the pistol 6 charges.

As an action while holding this pistol, you can expend 1 of its charges to fire a ray of crackling energy at a creature you can see within 60 feet of yourself. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or have the [paralyzed](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Paralyzed) condition for 1 minute. At the end of each of the target's turns, it can repeat the saving throw, ending the effect on itself on a success.

## Replacing the Energy Cell

While the pistol has charges remaining, its [energy cell](/3-Mechanics.CLI/items/energy-cell-xdmg.md) can't be removed. Once the pistol has 0 charges, you can replace the [energy cell](/3-Mechanics.CLI/items/energy-cell-xdmg.md) with a new cell by using an action or a bonus action.
