# Crampons
*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 2 gp
- **Weight**: 0.25 lbs.

A crampon is a metal plate with spikes that is strapped to the sole of a boot. A creature wearing crampons can't fall [prone](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Prone) while moving across [slippery ice](/3-Mechanics.CLI/traps-hazards/slippery-ice-xdmg.md).

*Source: Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden p. 20*
