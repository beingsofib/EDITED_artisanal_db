# Garb of Light and Shadow
*Adventuring gear*  
![](file://C:\Users\cgray\Desktop\clihome\5etools-img/items/FRHoF/Garb of Light and Shadow.webp#right)

- **Cost**: 50 gp
- **Weight**: 6.0 lbs.

*These outfits are fashionable among fey courtiers in the Moonshae Isles. After winning a riddle game and parting with a lock of hair, I secured enough samples to re-create them in my workshop.*

This garb appeals to Fey from one Domain of Delight, such as the Gloaming Court or the Summer Court. While wearing the garb, you have [Advantage](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on ability checks to influence Fey associated with that Domain of Delight.

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 132*
