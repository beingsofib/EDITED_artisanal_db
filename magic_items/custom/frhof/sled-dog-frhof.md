# Sled Dog
*Mount*  


- **Cost**: 50 gp

*We recommend a team of these sled dogs to ease the burden on individual animals. Rotate your dogs through the lead positions to give weary animals a rest.*

A sled dog uses the [Wolf](/3-Mechanics.CLI/bestiary/beast/wolf-xmm.md) stat block (see the Monster Manual). A sled dog pulling a sled alone must take a [Short Rest](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) after pulling a sled for 1 hour; otherwise, it gains 1 [Exhaustion](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Exhaustion) level.

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 132*
