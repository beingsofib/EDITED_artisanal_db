# Genie Robe
*Adventuring gear*  
![](file://C:\Users\cgray\Desktop\clihome\5etools-img/items/FRHoF/Genie Robe.webp#right)

- **Cost**: 50 gp
- **Weight**: 6.0 lbs.

*Channel the authority of genies with these elegant Calishite fashions.*

This robe appeals to Elementals associated with a particular Elemental Plane (Air, Earth, Fire, Water). While wearing a Genie Robe, you have [Advantage](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on ability checks made to influence Elementals associated with that plane.

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 132*
