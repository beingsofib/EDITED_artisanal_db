# Locking Spellbook
*Adventuring gear*  
![](file://C:\Users\cgray\Desktop\clihome\5etools-img/items/FRHoF/Locking Spellbook.webp#right)

- **Cost**: 35 gp
- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

*The wise wizard keeps a copy of all their spells safely tucked away in case of disaster. We supplement our spellbook with a trusty lock secure enough to dissuade curious goblins.*

This 100-page leather-bound tome can be used as a Spellbook. It is closed with a lock that comes with a key. As a [Utilize](/3-Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Utilize) action, a creature can try to pick the lock using [Thieves' Tools](/3-Mechanics.CLI/items/thieves-tools-xphb.md), doing so with a successful DC 15 Dexterity ([Sleight of Hand](/3-Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Sleight%20of%20Hand)) check.

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 132*
