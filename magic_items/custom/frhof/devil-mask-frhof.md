# Devil Mask
*Adventuring gear*  
![](file://C:\Users\cgray\Desktop\clihome\5etools-img/items/FRHoF/Devil Mask.webp#right)

- **Cost**: 25 gp

*Grant your sins plausible deniability by wearing one of these diabolical masks inspired by fiends from the Nine Hells. Also suitable for patriar galas in the city of Baldur's Gate.*

While you are wearing a Devil Mask, other creatures have [Disadvantage](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on Intelligence ([Investigation](/3-Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Investigation)) and Wisdom ([Insight](/3-Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Insight)) checks made to discern your true identity or intentions.

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 132*
