# Eyescratch
*Adventuring gear, poison (contact)*  


- **Properties**: [Poison](/3-Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Poison)

A creature that comes into contact with this poison must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or be [poisoned](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) for 1 hour and [blinded](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Blinded) while [poisoned](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) in this way. A [lesser restoration](/3-Mechanics.CLI/spells/lesser-restoration-xphb.md) spell or similar magic ends the effect.

*Source: Waterdeep: Dragon Heist p. 211*
